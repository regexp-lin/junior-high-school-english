# 中考英语完形填空：学做元宵汤圆

## 完形填空原文

In Chinese culture, eating tangyuan (sweet rice balls) is a tradition that represents family ___1___. This winter, my mom decided to make tangyuan at home instead of buying them from the store. She invited me to help.

First, we prepared the filling. Mom mixed crushed peanuts, sesame seeds, and sugar together in a bowl. The ___2___ smelled amazing. Then she scooped small amounts of the mixture and rolled them into tiny balls. I tried to do the same, but my balls were either too big or not ___3___ enough.

Next came the dough. Mom poured glutinous rice flour into a large bowl and added warm water little by little. She kneaded the flour ___4___ until it became smooth and soft. "The dough should feel like your earlobe," she said. I touched my ear and laughed. She was right—the texture was very ___5___.

Then Mom showed me how to wrap the filling. She took a small piece of dough, flattened it in her palm, placed a filling ball in the ___6___, and carefully closed the edges. She made it look easy, but when I tried, the dough kept breaking and the filling ___7___ out. After several attempts, I finally made one that held together.

We boiled the tangyuan in a big pot of water. When they ___8___ to the surface, Mom said they were ready. She served them in sweet ginger soup. I picked one up with a spoon and took a bite. The outside was chewy, and the inside was warm and ___9___.

As we sat together eating tangyuan, I felt a deep sense of happiness. Making food with family is not just about the result—it is about the ___10___ you share along the way.

## 选项

1. A. unity &emsp;&emsp; B. wealth &emsp;&emsp; C. power &emsp;&emsp; D. freedom
2. A. color &emsp;&emsp; B. shape &emsp;&emsp; C. mixture &emsp;&emsp; D. picture
3. A. flat &emsp;&emsp; B. round &emsp;&emsp; C. heavy &emsp;&emsp; D. hard
4. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. lazily &emsp;&emsp; C. patiently &emsp;&emsp; D. nervously
5. A. different &emsp;&emsp; B. similar &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. terrible
6. A. bottom &emsp;&emsp; B. edge &emsp;&emsp; C. middle &emsp;&emsp; D. corner
7. A. fell &emsp;&emsp; B. jumped &emsp;&emsp; C. leaked &emsp;&emsp; D. flew
8. A. sank &emsp;&emsp; B. floated &emsp;&emsp; C. dropped &emsp;&emsp; D. rolled
9. A. empty &emsp;&emsp; B. cold &emsp;&emsp; C. sweet &emsp;&emsp; D. sour
10. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. gifts &emsp;&emsp; D. tests

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：吃汤圆代表家庭团圆。A 项意为"团结/团圆"；B 项意为"财富"；C 项意为"力量"；D 项意为"自由"。family unity 意为"家庭团圆"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个混合料闻起来很香。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"形状"；C 项意为"混合物"；D 项意为"图片"。花生、芝麻和糖的 mixture。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我搓的球要么太大要么不够圆。A 项意为"平的"；B 项意为"圆的"；C 项意为"重的"；D 项意为"硬的"。搓馅料球要 round。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈耐心地揉面直到光滑柔软。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"懒洋洋地"；C 项意为"耐心地"；D 项意为"紧张地"。揉面需要 patiently。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：面团的质感和耳垂非常相似。A 项意为"不同的"；B 项意为"相似的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。妈妈说像耳垂，摸了确实 similar。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把馅料放在中间。A 项意为"底部"；B 项意为"边缘"；C 项意为"中间"；D 项意为"角落"。包汤圆把馅放在 middle。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：面皮总是破，馅料漏出来。A 项意为"掉落"；B 项意为"跳"；C 项意为"漏出"；D 项意为"飞"。leaked out 表示馅料漏出。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当汤圆浮到水面上就说明熟了。A 项意为"沉下"；B 项意为"浮起"；C 项意为"掉落"；D 项意为"滚动"。煮汤圆浮起来表示 floated to the surface。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：外面有嚼劲，里面温暖香甜。A 项意为"空的"；B 项意为"冷的"；C 项意为"甜的"；D 项意为"酸的"。花生芝麻糖馅是 sweet。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做美食不只是结果，更是你一路分享的时光。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"考试"。the time you share 意为"共度的时光"。故选 B。
