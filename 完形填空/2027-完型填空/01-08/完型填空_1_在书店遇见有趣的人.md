# 中考英语完形填空：在书店遇见有趣的人

## 完形填空原文

One afternoon during winter vacation, I went to a small bookstore near my home. It was a quiet place with wooden shelves and the ___1___ of old books. I loved spending time there because it always felt like a different world.

I was looking for a science fiction novel when I noticed an old man sitting in the corner. He was reading a thick book and making notes in a small ___2___. He looked very focused, as if nothing else in the world existed. I was curious about what he was reading, so I walked closer.

"Excuse me, sir. What book are you reading?" I asked ___3___. He looked up and smiled. "It's a book about the history of space exploration," he said. "I've been ___4___ in space since I was your age."

We started talking, and I learned that the old man was a retired science teacher. He had spent forty years teaching physics at a middle school. ___5___ he was retired, he still came to the bookstore every day to read and learn new things. "Learning never ___6___," he said. "There's always something new to discover."

He recommended several books to me and wrote down the titles on a piece of paper. One of them was about a young astronaut's journey to Mars. "This one will change the way you ___7___ about the universe," he said with bright eyes.

Before I left, he gave me a piece of ___8___: "Read as much as you can while you're young. Books are the best teachers you'll ever have." I thanked him and promised to come back next week.

Walking home with two new books in my bag, I felt ___9___. A simple visit to the bookstore had turned into an unforgettable meeting. I realized that interesting people can be found in the most ___10___ places—you just need to keep your eyes and mind open.

## 选项

1. A. taste &emsp;&emsp; B. smell &emsp;&emsp; C. sound &emsp;&emsp; D. touch
2. A. bag &emsp;&emsp; B. phone &emsp;&emsp; C. notebook &emsp;&emsp; D. camera
3. A. angrily &emsp;&emsp; B. politely &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. coldly
4. A. interested &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. disappointed &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
5. A. Unless &emsp;&emsp; B. Because &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Until
6. A. begins &emsp;&emsp; B. stops &emsp;&emsp; C. hurts &emsp;&emsp; D. fails
7. A. worry &emsp;&emsp; B. complain &emsp;&emsp; C. think &emsp;&emsp; D. talk
8. A. candy &emsp;&emsp; B. advice &emsp;&emsp; C. bread &emsp;&emsp; D. cake
9. A. hopeless &emsp;&emsp; B. inspired &emsp;&emsp; C. frightened &emsp;&emsp; D. exhausted
10. A. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; B. crowded &emsp;&emsp; C. ordinary &emsp;&emsp; D. faraway

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：书店里有木质书架和旧书的气味。A 项意为"味道"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"声音"；D 项意为"触感"。书店里闻到的是 smell of old books。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他在一个小笔记本上做笔记。A 项意为"包"；B 项意为"手机"；C 项意为"笔记本"；D 项意为"相机"。making notes in a notebook。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我礼貌地问他。A 项意为"生气地"；B 项意为"礼貌地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"冷淡地"。向陌生老人搭话用 politely。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从你这么大的时候起我就对太空感兴趣。A 项意为"感兴趣的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"失望的"；D 项意为"迷路的"。interested in space。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然退休了，他仍然每天来书店读书学习。A 项意为"除非"；B 项意为"因为"；C 项意为"虽然"；D 项意为"直到"。Although...still 表让步。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学习永不停止。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"伤害"；D 项意为"失败"。Learning never stops 意为"学无止境"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这本书会改变你对宇宙的看法。A 项意为"担心"；B 项意为"抱怨"；C 项意为"思考"；D 项意为"谈论"。the way you think about 意为"你的看法"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他给了我一条建议。A 项意为"糖果"；B 项意为"建议"；C 项意为"面包"；D 项意为"蛋糕"。a piece of advice 意为"一条建议"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家路上我感到备受鼓舞。A 项意为"绝望的"；B 项意为"受启发的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"精疲力竭的"。遇见有趣的人后 inspired。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有趣的人可以在最普通的地方找到。A 项意为"危险的"；B 项意为"拥挤的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"遥远的"。ordinary places 与 interesting people 形成对比。故选 C。
