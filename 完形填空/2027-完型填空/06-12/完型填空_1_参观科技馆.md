# 中考英语完形填空：参观科技馆

## 完形填空原文

Last Thursday, our class went to the city science museum ___1___ a field trip. We were all very excited because most of us ___2___ been there before.

The museum was much ___3___ than I expected. There were four floors, and each floor had a different theme. Our guide led us to the space exhibition first, ___4___ we saw models of rockets and satellites.

I was most interested ___5___ the robot area on the third floor. A small robot could answer questions ___6___ different languages. When I asked it a question in English, it replied ___7___ a clear voice.

The most amazing part was the 4D movie theater. We wore special glasses and ___8___ as if we were really flying through the sky. Some students screamed because the experience was too ___9___ for them.

Before leaving, we visited the gift shop. I bought a small telescope ___10___ my own money. I decided to watch the stars every clear night from now on.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. for
2. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. has &emsp;&emsp; C. hadn't &emsp;&emsp; D. haven't
3. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. bigger &emsp;&emsp; C. biggest &emsp;&emsp; D. the biggest
4. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. what
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
6. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. felt &emsp;&emsp; C. feeling &emsp;&emsp; D. feels
9. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. really &emsp;&emsp; C. reality &emsp;&emsp; D. realize
10. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. in

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（on a field trip）
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周四我们班去市科技馆参加实地考察。"on a field trip"为固定搭配。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 过去完成时（hadn't + 过去分词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都很兴奋因为我们大多数人之前没去过。过去的过去用过去完成时 hadn't been。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（much + 比较级 + than）
**【解析】** 本句意为：博物馆比我预期的大多了。than 提示用比较级，much 修饰 bigger。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句（where 指代地点）
**【解析】** 本句意为：导游先带我们去了太空展览，在那里我们看到了火箭和卫星模型。先行词为 the space exhibition（地点），用 where。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be interested in）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对三楼的机器人区域最感兴趣。"be interested in"表示"对……感兴趣"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（in + 语言）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小机器人可以用不同的语言回答问题。"in different languages"表示"用不同语言"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（in a...voice）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它用清晰的声音回答了我。"in a clear voice"为固定搭配，表示"用清晰的声音"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时（and 连接并列谓语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们戴上特殊眼镜，感觉好像真的在天上飞。wore 和 felt 并列，用一般过去时。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词（too + adj.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些同学尖叫因为体验太逼真了。too 后接形容词 real。really 为副词。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（with 表使用）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用自己的钱买了一个小望远镜。"with my own money"表示"用我自己的钱"。故选 C。
