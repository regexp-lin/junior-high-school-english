# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工风车

## 完形填空原文

On a windy afternoon, my little sister asked me ___1___ make a pinwheel for her. I remembered ___2___ our teacher once showed us how to make one in class.

I found a square piece of colored paper and ___3___ it from each corner toward the center. But I was careful not to cut all the way ___4___ — I stopped about two centimeters from the middle.

Then I folded every ___5___ corner to the center and pushed a pin through all the layers. This was the ___6___ part because the pin was sharp. My mother helped me press the pin into a wooden stick.

When we took it outside, the wind ___7___ the pinwheel spin fast. My sister held the stick and ran around the garden ___8___. The colorful paper turned into a beautiful circle.

She was so happy ___9___ she didn't want to go back inside. I promised to make her ___10___ one in a different color next weekend.

## 选项

1. A. / &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. where
3. A. cut &emsp;&emsp; B. cuts &emsp;&emsp; C. cutting &emsp;&emsp; D. to cut
4. A. over &emsp;&emsp; B. through &emsp;&emsp; C. across &emsp;&emsp; D. along
5. A. another &emsp;&emsp; B. other &emsp;&emsp; C. the other &emsp;&emsp; D. others
6. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. hardest &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
7. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. makes &emsp;&emsp; C. made &emsp;&emsp; D. making
8. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. happily &emsp;&emsp; C. happier &emsp;&emsp; D. happiness
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. who
10. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. the other &emsp;&emsp; C. another &emsp;&emsp; D. others

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹让我给她做一个风车。"ask sb. to do sth."为固定结构。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 宾语从句（that 引导）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我记得老师曾经在课上教过我们怎么做。remember 后接 that 引导的宾语从句。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 一般过去时（and 连接并列谓语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我找了一张正方形彩纸，从每个角向中心剪开。found 和 cut 并列，cut 的过去式仍为 cut。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（all the way through 完全穿过）
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我小心不要完全剪穿。through 表示"穿过"，all the way through 表示"完全剪透"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 不定代词（every other 每隔一个）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我把每隔一个角折向中心。"every other"表示"每隔一个"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（the + 最高级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是最难的部分。the hardest 为最高级。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 使役动词（make + 宾语 + 动词原形）
**【解析】** 本句意为：风使风车快速旋转。"make + 宾语 + 动词原形"。上下文为过去时，用 made。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹握着棍子开心地在花园里跑来跑去。修饰动词 ran 用副词 happily。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构（so...that... 如此……以至于）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她太开心了以至于不想回屋。"so + adj. + that"为固定结构。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定代词（another 另一个）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我答应下周末给她再做一个不同颜色的。another 表示"另一个（三者以上中的又一个）"。故选 C。
