# 中考英语完形填空：参加社区义卖活动

## 完形填空原文

When our community organized ___1___ charity sale last Saturday, I decided to join ___2___ a volunteer. My mom helped me choose old books and toys ___3___ were still in good condition. Many neighbors came, and ___4___ bought something to support poor kids in the mountain area. My friends brought handmade cards, and a warm-hearted baker donated fresh cookies so more people would stop at our tables. ___5___ the weather was cold, the square was warm with smiles and laughter. I stood ___6___ a small table and introduced each item patiently. A little boy bought a storybook with his pocket money, and his smile made my voice braver than before. At first, I was shy to speak ___7___, but after ten minutes I spoke more loudly and clearly. ___8___ the end of the afternoon, we raised more money than we ___9___ expected. I left the square feeling ___10___ useful because my small effort really helped others.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
4. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. they &emsp;&emsp; D. them  
5. A. Because &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. If &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
6. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
7. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. aloud &emsp;&emsp; D. louder  
8. A. In &emsp;&emsp; B. On &emsp;&emsp; C. At &emsp;&emsp; D. By  
9. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. has &emsp;&emsp; C. had &emsp;&emsp; D. having  
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. such  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指“一场义卖活动”）  
**【解析】** charity 以辅音音素开头，泛指一场活动用 a charity sale；the 表特指但此处首次提到活动；an 用于元音音素开头；零冠词不符合“组织一场活动”的表达习惯。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词（join as 表示“以……身份加入/参加”）  
**【解析】** join as a volunteer 表示“作为志愿者参加”；like 表“像”，for/with 与 join 的常见搭配不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词（先行词为物）  
**【解析】** 先行词是 books and toys，指物且在从句中作主语，用 which 或 that；who/whom 指人；what 不引导定语从句。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 人称代词主格（作主语）  
**【解析】** 指代前文的 Many neighbors，且在 and 后作主语，用 they；them 为宾格；we/us 指代错误。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词（让步状语从句）  
**【解析】** “天气冷”与“广场很温暖”形成让步对比，用 Although；Because 表原因会逻辑不通；If/Unless 表条件不符合。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词（地点：站在小桌子旁/摊位处）  
**【解析】** stand at a table 表示在摊位桌子处；on 表“在桌面上”，in/by 不如 at 自然表达“值守摊位”。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词（speak aloud 表示“大声说出来”）  
**【解析】** speak aloud 强调“出声、公开讲”，符合害羞到敢开口的语境；loud 多作形容词；loudly 偏重“吵闹”；louder 是比较级，此处无比较对象。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（at the end of...）  
**【解析】** at the end of the afternoon 表示“在下午结束时”；in/on/by the end 搭配不标准或语义不对。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 动词时态（过去完成时：过去的过去）  
**【解析】** “筹到的钱比我们之前预料的更多”，expect 发生在 raised 之前，用 had expected；have/has/having 与语境时态不符。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词（修饰形容词 useful）  
**【解析】** feeling very useful 表示“觉得很有用”；too useful 常含“过分”或否定语气不符；so 常接 that 结构；such 后接名词短语。故选 A。
