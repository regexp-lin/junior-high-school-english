# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸花篮

## 完形填空原文

In our art class, Ms. Wang showed us ___1___ to make a paper flower basket step by step. The classroom smelled like fresh paper, and everyone whispered ideas about what flowers to fold first. She brought colored paper, scissors, and glue, ___2___ told us to work in pairs. My partner was Lucy, ___3___ hands were always neat and quick. First, we folded the paper ___4___ small squares and cut the edges carefully. ___5___ we tried to glue the pieces together, the basket kept falling apart. Ms. Wang smiled and said, "Press ___6___ for five seconds and wait until it sticks." Following her advice, we succeeded ___7___ the second try. When I carried the basket home, my little sister ran ___8___ me and asked, "Did you make ___9___ yourself?" I nodded proudly. That night, I put some candy ___10___ the basket and placed it on the desk like a small gift.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
3. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. which  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
5. A. When &emsp;&emsp; B. While &emsp;&emsp; C. Before &emsp;&emsp; D. After  
6. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gentler &emsp;&emsp; C. gently &emsp;&emsp; D. gentlest  
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
8. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
9. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. its &emsp;&emsp; C. this &emsp;&emsp; D. that  
10. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构（how to do）  
**【解析】** show us how to make... 表示“演示如何做”；what to make 也可说，但后文已有宾语 a paper flower basket，强调方式步骤用 how 更贴切；why/when 与 step by step 的“做法演示”不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（顺承并列）  
**【解析】** “带了材料，并且让我们两人一组”为顺承，用 and；but 转折，or 选择，so 表结果，均不如 and 自然。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词（whose 表所属）  
**【解析】** Lucy, whose hands... 表示“露西的手”；who/whom 不能在名词 hands 前表所属；which 指物。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词（fold...into... 折成……）  
**【解析】** fold the paper into small squares 表示“把纸折成小方块”；in/on/at 不能准确表达“折成某种形状”。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词（时间状语从句：当……时）  
**【解析】** 句意为“当我们试图把各部分粘在一起时，篮子总是散架”，强调时间点/过程，用 When；While 多接延续性动词，glue 的动作可接受但此处更常见 When；Before/After 与语境“一边尝试一边掉”不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词（修饰动词 Press）  
**【解析】** press gently 表示“轻轻地按压”；gentle 为形容词；gentler/gentlest 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较范围。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（succeed on the second try 第二次尝试成功）  
**【解析】** on the second try 表示“在第二次尝试时”，与 succeed 搭配自然；in/at/to the second try 不符合英语习惯搭配。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词（run to sb. 跑向某人）  
**【解析】** run to me 表示跑向我；run at 常带有“朝某人冲去”的攻击性意味，for/with 不符合此处语境。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 代词（指代 basket，作 make 的宾语）  
**【解析】** Did you make it yourself? 中 it 指代上文 the basket；its 是所有格，后需名词；this/that 虽偶见口语，但此处最自然的是 it。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词（put...in the basket 放进篮子里）  
**【解析】** put some candy in the basket 表示把糖果放进篮子内部；on 强调表面，at/by 不符合“装入容器”的空间关系。故选 B。
