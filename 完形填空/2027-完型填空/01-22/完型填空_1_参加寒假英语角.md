# 中考英语完形填空：参加寒假英语角

## 完形填空原文

Every Tuesday evening, volunteers organize a free English corner ___1___ the community library. I went there for the first time last week. When I opened the door, several teenagers were already talking ___2___ a volunteer from Canada. Bright posters on the wall listed useful phrases for daily conversation, and a bookshelf stood in the corner for anyone who wanted to read.

I stood near the wall, too shy ___3___ a word. My heart beat fast, yet I told myself that making mistakes was part of learning a language. A girl named Tina smiled ___4___ me and invited me to join their group. We played a word game ___5___ guessing jobs from short descriptions. I answered two questions ___6___ and won a small notebook.

___7___ the activity ended, we exchanged social media accounts and promised ___8___ each other online. Someone suggested practicing short speeches next week, and several hands went up at once. Walking home, I realized speaking English is not ___9___ grammar rules only; it is also ___10___ confidence built step by step.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. for
3. A. to say &emsp;&emsp; B. saying &emsp;&emsp; C. say &emsp;&emsp; D. said
4. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. from
5. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. under
6. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. nervously &emsp;&emsp; C. correctly &emsp;&emsp; D. angrily
7. A. When &emsp;&emsp; B. Unless &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Until
8. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. to help &emsp;&emsp; C. helping &emsp;&emsp; D. helped
9. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. against &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
10. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. through &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. without

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：志愿者在社区图书馆“里”组织免费英语角。in the library 表示在图书馆内。A 项 on 在……上；B 项 at 也可表地点，但 in 更强调在空间内部；D 项 by 在……旁。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：几名青少年正与一位来自加拿大的志愿者交谈。talk with/to sb. 均可；选项中 with 最贴切。A 项 to 也可 talk to，但此处选项 B 符合常见表达。C 项 about 关于；D 项 for 为了。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定结构）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太害羞而说不出话。too...to... 为固定结构，表示“太……而不能……”，后接不定式。B 项 saying；C 项 say；D 项 said 均不符合结构。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：Tina 对我微笑。smile at sb. 为固定搭配。A 项 on；B 项 for；D 项 from 不搭配 smile。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们通过猜职业描述来玩文字游戏。by + doing 表示方式“通过……”。B 项 on；C 项 with；D 项 under。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我“正确地”回答了两个问题并赢得小本子。A 项 carelessly 粗心；B 项 nervously 紧张；C 项 correctly 正确地；D 项 angrily 生气。与 win 呼应。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当活动结束时，我们交换了社交账号。When 引导时间状语从句。B 项 Unless 除非；C 项 Although 尽管；D 项 Until 直到。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：承诺在网上互相帮助。promise to do sth. 为固定搭配。A 项 help 原形不能直接跟在 promised 后；C 项 helping；D 项 helped。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：说英语并不只“关于”语法规则。be about 表示“关于；在于”。B 项 against 反对；C 项 without 没有；D 项 beyond 超出。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它也“关于”一步步建立的自信。与前文 not only about grammar 并列，用 about confidence。B 项 through 通过；C 项 against；D 项 without。故选 A。
