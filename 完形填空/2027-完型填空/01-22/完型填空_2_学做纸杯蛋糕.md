# 中考英语完形填空：学做纸杯蛋糕

## 完形填空原文

My aunt owns a tiny bakery on our street. Yesterday she let me ___1___ the kitchen as her helper. We measured flour, sugar, and butter ___2___ a kitchen scale, then mixed them ___3___ an egg beater until the batter looked smooth. Customers walked past the glass window from time to time, waving at my aunt with friendly smiles.

I poured the batter ___4___ paper cups carefully. She reminded me to leave a little space at the top so the cakes would have room to rise in the heat. Aunt Lin put the tray ___5___ the oven and set a timer. While waiting, I cleaned the bowls and wiped the table ___6___ a wet cloth.

When a sweet smell spread through the room, I could not wait ___7___ the oven door. The cupcakes rose ___8___ little golden hats. Aunt Lin taught me how to pipe cream ___9___ top and add a strawberry on each one.

We shared the cakes ___10___ neighbors. Their warm smiles made the cold winter afternoon taste sweeter.

## 选项

1. A. enter &emsp;&emsp; B. paint &emsp;&emsp; C. lock &emsp;&emsp; D. repair
2. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. about
3. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. without
4. A. above &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. below
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. under
6. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. open &emsp;&emsp; B. to open &emsp;&emsp; C. opening &emsp;&emsp; D. opened
8. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. among

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：昨天她让我进入厨房当帮手。A 项 enter 进入；B 项 paint 粉刷；C 项 lock 锁上；D 项 repair 修理。let me enter the kitchen 符合语境。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们用厨房秤称面粉、糖和黄油。with 表示“用（工具）”。A 项 on 在……上；C 项 from 从；D 项 about 关于。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后用打蛋器搅拌。mix...with... 或 mix them with a beater 表示用工具搅拌。A 项 for；B 项 by 虽可表方式，但 mix them by an egg beater 不如 with 自然；D 项 without 没有。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把面糊小心倒进纸杯里。pour...into... 为固定搭配。A 项 above；C 项 against；D 项 below。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把烤盘放进烤箱。put...into... 表示放入内部。A 项 in put the tray in the oven 也可，但选项中 into 更强调“放入里面”；B 项 on 放在烤箱上不合理；D 项 under 在下面。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用湿抹布擦桌子。with a wet cloth 表示使用工具。A 项 by；C 项 for；D 项 at。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我迫不及待想打开烤箱门。can't wait to do sth. 为固定搭配。A 项 open 原形不能直接接在 wait 后；C 项 opening；D 项 opened。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：纸杯蛋糕胀起来，像小金色帽子。like 作介词，表示“像”。B 项 for；C 项 with；D 项 about。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在顶部挤奶油。on top 为固定短语，表示在顶部。A 项 in；C 项 at；D 项 by。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把蛋糕与邻居分享。share sth. with sb. 为固定搭配。A 项 to；B 项 for；D 项 among 在……之中，不搭配 share。故选 C。
