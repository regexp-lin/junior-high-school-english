# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工铅笔盒

## 完形填空原文

In our school's "Reduce, Reuse, Recycle" week, the teacher asked us to make ___1___ useful from waste materials. I decided to make a pencil case from an old pair of jeans.

I found a pair of jeans that no longer ___2___ me in the closet. My mother said I could cut them up for my project. I felt a little sad ___3___ they had been my favorite jeans, but I was excited to give them a new life.

First, I cut one leg of the jeans into a rectangular piece. The denim was thick and ___4___, which was perfect for a pencil case. Then I folded the fabric in half and sewed the two ___5___ together with blue thread.

The most creative part was making the zipper opening. I didn't have a sewing machine, so I had to sew the zipper ___6___ hand. It was slow work, but I enjoyed the process. Each stitch was like a small victory.

When the pencil case was finished, I decorated it with a button from the original jeans. My classmates were ___7___ when they saw it. "It doesn't even look handmade!" one of them said. That was probably the ___8___ compliment I could receive.

The teacher said our projects showed that with a little creativity, old items don't have to end up ___9___ the trash. She reminded us that every small action ___10___ and we should all try to reduce waste in our daily lives.

## 选项

1. A. something &emsp;&emsp; B. anything &emsp;&emsp; C. nothing &emsp;&emsp; D. everything  
2. A. fit &emsp;&emsp; B. fits &emsp;&emsp; C. fitted &emsp;&emsp; D. fitting  
3. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
4. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. strongly &emsp;&emsp; C. strength &emsp;&emsp; D. stronger  
5. A. side &emsp;&emsp; B. sides &emsp;&emsp; C. line &emsp;&emsp; D. lines  
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
7. A. amaze &emsp;&emsp; B. amazed &emsp;&emsp; C. amazing &emsp;&emsp; D. amazement  
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
10. A. count &emsp;&emsp; B. counts &emsp;&emsp; C. counted &emsp;&emsp; D. counting  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 something  
**【解析】** make something useful 意为"做一些有用的东西"，肯定句中用 something。anything 用于否定/疑问句；nothing 意为"没有东西"；everything 意为"所有东西"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** no longer fitted me 意为"不再适合我了"，全文为过去叙述用过去式。fit 为原形；fits 为三单现在时；fitting 为现在分词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "感到难过因为它们曾是我最喜欢的牛仔裤"，because 引导原因状语从句。so 表结果；but 表转折；although 表让步。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** thick and strong 为两个并列的形容词作表语，strong 意为"结实的"。strongly 为副词；strength 为名词；stronger 为比较级。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词复数  
**【解析】** the two sides 意为"两边"，two 后接可数名词复数 sides。side 为单数；line/lines 意为"线条"，不符合缝合两边的语境。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 by hand  
**【解析】** by hand 意为"手工地"，为固定搭配。in hand 意为"在手中/进行中"；on hand 意为"在手边"；with hand 不常见。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）  
**【解析】** were amazed 意为"感到惊讶"，amazed 修饰人的感受。amaze 为动词；amazing 意为"令人惊讶的"修饰事物；amazement 为名词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** the best compliment I could receive 意为"我能收到的最好的赞美"。good 为原级；well 作形容词表"身体好"；better 为比较级。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 end up in the trash  
**【解析】** end up in the trash 意为"最终进入垃圾桶"，in 表示"在……里面"。on/at/to 不与 trash 构成此搭配。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 主谓一致（一般现在时）  
**【解析】** every small action counts 意为"每个小行动都很重要"，every + 单数名词为第三人称单数，用 counts。count 缺 s；counted 为过去式（此处为老师的general truth）；counting 为现在分词。故选 B。
