# 中考英语完形填空：参观美术馆

## 完形填空原文

Our art teacher organized a visit to the city art gallery last Friday. It was the first time that many of us ___1___ been to a real art exhibition.

The gallery was a large white building with glass windows. When we walked inside, the first thing I noticed was how ___2___ it was. Everyone spoke in whispers, as if they were afraid of disturbing the paintings.

Our guide was a young woman ___3___ had studied art in France. She led us through three exhibition halls. In the first hall, we saw traditional Chinese ink paintings. She explained that these paintings were usually created ___4___ a few simple brushstrokes, but they could express deep emotions.

I was especially attracted by a painting of bamboo. The artist used only black ink, ___5___ the bamboo looked so real that I could almost feel the wind blowing through it. The guide told us that bamboo ___6___ strength and modesty in Chinese culture.

The second hall displayed modern art. Some paintings were just colorful shapes and lines. I couldn't understand ___7___ the artists wanted to express. My friend whispered, "I think my little brother could paint ___8___ than this!" We both tried hard not to laugh.

Before leaving, the guide said, "Art doesn't have to be understood. It just needs to make you ___9___." Her words changed the way I thought ___10___ art. Now I want to visit art galleries more often.

## 选项

1. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. has &emsp;&emsp; C. had &emsp;&emsp; D. having  
2. A. noise &emsp;&emsp; B. noisy &emsp;&emsp; C. noisily &emsp;&emsp; D. quiet  
3. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
6. A. represent &emsp;&emsp; B. represents &emsp;&emsp; C. represented &emsp;&emsp; D. representing  
7. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
9. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. felt &emsp;&emsp; C. feeling &emsp;&emsp; D. to feel  
10. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. about &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时（It was the first time + had done）  
**【解析】** "It was the first time that + 主语 + had done" 为固定句型，表"这是某人第一次做某事"。have/has 用于现在完成时；having 为现在分词。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语（语境推理）  
**【解析】** 后文说大家都低声说话，说明画廊很安静，用 quiet（安静的）。noise 为名词；noisy 意为"吵闹的"与语境矛盾；noisily 为副词。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词（指人）  
**【解析】** 先行词 a young woman 为人，在从句中作主语，用 who。which 指物；whom 作宾语；what 不引导定语从句。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表使用  
**【解析】** created with a few simple brushstrokes 意为"用几笔简单的笔触创作"，with 表使用的工具。in 表在……里；by 后接动名词或施事者；on 表在……上。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折）  
**【解析】** "只用了黑墨"与"看起来如此逼真"为转折关系（出乎意料），用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表因果。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般现在时表客观事实  
**【解析】** 竹子在中国文化中象征力量和谦虚，为客观事实用一般现在时。主语 bamboo 为不可数名词第三人称单数，用 represents。represent 缺 s；represented 为过去式；representing 为现在分词。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连词  
**【解析】** what the artists wanted to express 意为"艺术家们想表达什么"，what 在从句中作 express 的宾语。that 在从句中不作成分；which 需有选择范围；how 不作宾语。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级  
**【解析】** paint better than this 意为"画得比这更好"，than 需用比较级，修饰动词 paint 用 better（well 的比较级）。good 为形容词；well 为原级；best 为最高级。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 使役动词 make + 宾语 + 动词原形  
**【解析】** make you feel 意为"让你有感觉"，make 后接宾语 + 动词原形。felt 为过去式；feeling 为现在分词；to feel 为不定式（make 不接 to do）。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 think about  
**【解析】** the way I thought about art 意为"我对艺术的看法"，think about 意为"思考关于"。of 也可但 about 更常见于此语境；for/with 不与 think 构成此义。故选 B。
