# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工棉拖鞋

## 完形填空原文

Aunt May runs a small sewing class ___1___ the neighborhood. Yesterday she showed me ___2___ to sew a pair of cotton slippers. We started ___3___ measuring my feet on thick fabric. Aunt May used pins ___4___ hold the layers together so they would not move easily. “Always keep the needle ___5___ from your fingers,” she warned. I tried to push the needle slowly, ___6___ it still broke once because I pulled too hard. Aunt May laughed and gave me ___7___ new needle without blaming me. When the soft cotton sole was finished, it looked ___8___ comfortable than I imagined. She said practice would make the stitches ___9___ neat day by day. Before I left, I thanked her ___10___ her patience and useful tips.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
3. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
4. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
5. A. safe &emsp;&emsp; B. safer &emsp;&emsp; C. safest &emsp;&emsp; D. safely  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
8. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too  
9. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 in the neighborhood  
**【解析】** “在附近社区”常用 in the neighborhood。on/at/to 与 neighborhood 搭配不表示此义。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + to do”作宾语  
**【解析】** show sb. how to do sth. 教某人如何做。此处缺方式，用 how to sew。what/why/when 与“演示缝纫步骤”不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 by 表“通过某种方式”  
**【解析】** start by doing 表示“从做……开始”。from/with/for 与 start 搭配不表达“以测量为第一步”。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 use sth. to do  
**【解析】** use pins to hold... 表示“用别针来固定”。help/helping/helped 不能直接作 use pins 的目的状语结构。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作宾补 keep + n. + adj.  
**【解析】** keep the needle safe from your fingers 表示“让针尖远离手指、保持安全状态”，此处无比较对象，用形容词原级 safe。safer/safely 词性或比较级不必要；safest 缺范围。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 but 表转折  
**【解析】** “我尽量慢慢推针”与“还是断了一次”为转折，用 but。and 顺承；or 选择；so 结果，均不符合。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 a 表泛指  
**【解析】** new 以辅音音素开头，一根新针用 a new needle。an 用于元音音素开头；the 特指不足；零冠词可数名词单数一般不单独出现。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** more + 形容词原级 + than  
**【解析】** comfortable 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more comfortable than。most 最高级；very/too 不接 than。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（暗含与现在比较）  
**【解析】** day by day 暗示“一天比一天更整齐”，用比较级 more neat（也可用 neater，但选项给 more）。much/very 不接隐含比较；most 为最高级。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 thank sb. for sth.  
**【解析】** “因……感谢某人”用 thank...for...。thank to 非标准搭配；of/with 不与 thank 构成此义。故选 A。
