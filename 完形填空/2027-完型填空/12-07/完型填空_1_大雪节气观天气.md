# 中考英语完形填空：大雪节气观天气

## 完形填空原文

December 7th is “Major Snow”, one of ___1___ twenty-four solar terms in China. Our science teacher asked us ___2___ the weather for a week and write a short report. Every morning, I recorded the temperature ___3___ a notebook and took photos of the sky. On Wednesday, dark clouds appeared ___4___ it rained for an hour. I wondered ___5___ the temperature would drop below zero soon. My grandpa, ___6___ has farmed for forty years, said heavy snow might come ___7___ the wind turned north. By Friday, cold air really arrived, ___8___ felt like a thin knife on my face. I learned ___9___ ancient Chinese people read nature’s signs without apps. This experience made science ___10___ interesting for me than before.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. observe &emsp;&emsp; B. to observe &emsp;&emsp; C. observing &emsp;&emsp; D. observed  
3. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
4. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
5. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. which  
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
7. A. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
8. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. they &emsp;&emsp; C. this &emsp;&emsp; D. those  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
10. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定冠词 the 与固定表达  
**【解析】** “二十四节气”为特定文化概念，常用 the twenty-four solar terms 表特指这一类体系。a/an 表泛指单数名词，不适合与 twenty-four 搭配表整体概念。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** ask sb. to do sth.  
**【解析】** ask 后接带 to 的不定式作宾补，即 ask us to observe。observe 原形不可直接作宾补；observing 非 ask 的常见宾补结构；observed 表被动完成，语义不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 in 与 notebook 搭配  
**【解析】** “在笔记本里记录”用 in a notebook。on 多指表面；at 表地点点；with 表工具，均不如 in 自然。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and 表顺承  
**【解析】** “乌云出现”与“下了一小时雨”为顺承并列，用 and。but 转折；so 结果；or 选择，均不符合天气连续变化。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句 whether 表“是否”  
**【解析】** wondered 后接宾语从句，询问“气温是否会很快降到零度以下”，用 whether。that 无疑问语气；what 需在从句中充当成分，此处从句结构完整；which 表选择范围，不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句关系代词 who 指人  
**【解析】** 先行词 My grandpa 指人，从句缺主语，用 who。which 指物；whom 作宾语；whose 表所属，后需名词。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 as soon as  
**【解析】** 句意为“北风一转，大雪可能就来”，as soon as 表“一……就……”。even though 让步；so that 目的/结果；unless 除非，均不符合农谚逻辑。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 人称代词 it 指代天气/冷空气  
**【解析】** 英语中描述天气感受常用 it 作主语，it felt like... they/this/those 指代不明或与 cold air 不一致。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接副词 how 表方式  
**【解析】** learned 后为宾语从句，表示“古人如何不靠应用软件读懂自然迹象”，强调方式，用 how。what 缺宾语成分；why 原因；when 时间，语境侧重“方法”。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 more interesting than  
**【解析】** 后有 than before，interesting 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more interesting。much/very 不直接接 than；most 为最高级。故选 B。
