# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工雪花窗贴

## 完形填空原文

Art club started a winter project ___1___ making paper snowflakes for classroom windows. Our teacher handed out white paper and scissors, ___2___ told us symmetry was the key. I folded the paper ___3___ half again and again until it looked like a thin triangle. Then I cut small shapes along the edges ___4___ care. When I opened it, a large snowflake appeared, ___5___ delicate than I expected. Some classmates’ designs were even ___6___ creative than mine. Instead of feeling upset, I studied ___7___ lines and tried a new pattern. By the end of the lesson, thirty snowflakes ___8___ on the windows, shining under the lights. The teacher praised us ___9___ working so carefully. I realized sharing ideas could make everyone’s work ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
5. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too  
6. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
7. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs  
8. A. hang &emsp;&emsp; B. hung &emsp;&emsp; C. were hung &emsp;&emsp; D. were hanging  
9. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 about 表“关于/围绕”  
**【解析】** a project about making... 表示“关于制作……的项目”。on/at/in 与 project 搭配不如 about 贴切表达主题。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and 连接并列谓语  
**【解析】** “老师分发纸和剪刀”与“告诉我们对称是关键”为顺承并列，用 and。but 转折；so 结果；or 选择，均不符合。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 fold...in half  
**【解析】** “对折”常用 fold the paper in half。on/at/by 均不构成该固定搭配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表方式 with care  
**【解析】** with care 表示“小心地”，作状语修饰 cut。in care 多表“在照料中”；by care 不自然；for care 语义不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 more delicate than  
**【解析】** delicate 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more delicate。most 最高级；very/too 不接 than。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 more creative than  
**【解析】** even 可修饰比较级，且后有 than，用 more creative。much 修饰比较级但不直接接 creative；most/very 不与 than 搭配。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词 their  
**【解析】** 空后为名词 lines，指同学们的线条，用 their。they/them 不能作定语；theirs 为名词性物主代词，后不接名词。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** 雪花窗贴被贴在窗户上，主语 snowflakes 与“悬挂/张贴”为被动关系，且叙述课程结束时的结果，用 were hung。hang 原形；hung 虽为 hang 的过去式，但此处强调被张贴，被动更准确；were hanging 为过去进行时，不如被动表结果贴切。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 praise sb. for (doing) sth.  
**【解析】** “因……表扬某人”用 praise us for working...。of/with/to 不与 praise 构成此固定搭配。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 make sth. + adj.  
**【解析】** make everyone’s work better 表示“让每个人的作品更好”，暗含分享想法前后的对比，用比较级 better。good 原级；best 缺范围；well 作形容词多表“身体好”，此处指作品质量。故选 B。
