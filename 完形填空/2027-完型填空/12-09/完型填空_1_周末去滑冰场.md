# 中考英语完形填空：周末去滑冰场

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, my brother and I went ___1___ an indoor skating rink for the first time. Dad bought tickets online ___2___ we could avoid long lines. When we put on skates, my legs shook ___3___ I was afraid of falling. A coach smiled and told me ___4___ knees slightly bent. “Look forward, not ___5___ your feet all the time,” he added. I moved ___6___ step at a time along the handrail. Twenty minutes later, I could glide ___7___ slowly without holding it. My brother, ___8___ had taken lessons before, showed off a small turn and cheered for me. On the way home, Dad said courage grows ___9___ you keep trying new things. I agreed and hoped we ___10___ visit the rink again next month.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. until  
4. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. keeping &emsp;&emsp; C. to keep &emsp;&emsp; D. kept  
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. down  
6. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
7. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. really &emsp;&emsp; C. more real &emsp;&emsp; D. most real  
8. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. before  
10. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. could &emsp;&emsp; C. should &emsp;&emsp; D. must  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** go to + 地点  
**【解析】** “去滑冰场”用 go to a skating rink。go for 表目的；go at/in 搭配不符合“前往某地”。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** so that 引导目的状语从句  
**【解析】** 爸爸网上买票的目的是避免排长队，用 so that。because 表原因；although 让步；unless 除非，均不符合目的关系。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** because 引导原因状语从句  
**【解析】** “腿发抖”是因为“害怕摔倒”，表原因用 because。if 条件；unless 除非；until 直到，均不符合。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** tell sb. to do sth.  
**【解析】** tell me to keep knees slightly bent 表示“告诉我保持膝盖微弯”。keep/keeping/kept 不能直接作 tell 的宾补。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** look at 固定搭配  
**【解析】** “不要一直盯着脚看”用 look at your feet。look in 往里面看；look on 旁观；look down 俯视，虽可接 at，但此处教练强调“注视脚”，用 look at 最直接。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定短语 one step at a time  
**【解析】** one step at a time 表示“一步一步来”，one 以辅音音素开头用 a。an 不适用；the 特指不符；零冠词不能构成该固定表达。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词 glide  
**【解析】** glide 为动词，需用副词 really 修饰 slowly 的程度或修饰 glide：glide really slowly。real 为形容词；more real/most real 语义不对。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句 who 指人作主语  
**【解析】** 先行词 My brother 指人，从句缺主语，用 who。which 指物；whom 宾语；whose 表所属。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** when 引导时间状语从句  
**【解析】** 句意为“当你不断尝试新事物时，勇气就会增长”，when 表“当……时候”。if/whether 表条件/是否；before 在……之前，语气不如 when 贴切。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去将来时 could  
**【解析】** hoped 为过去时，从句“下个月再来”为从过去看的将来可能性，用 could visit 更自然（委婉希望）。can 现在能力语气；should/must 义务，不如 could 贴合 hoped 的委婉。故选 B。
