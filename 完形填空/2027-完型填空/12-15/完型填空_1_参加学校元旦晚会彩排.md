# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校元旦晚会彩排

## 完形填空原文

Two weeks before New Year’s Day, our class began ___1___ for the school evening party. We chose an English song and designed simple dance moves. ___2___ we were not professional dancers, everyone practiced ___3___ after school.

During the first rehearsal in the hall, we felt shy ___4___ the bright lights and many chairs. Our music teacher, Ms. Yang, clapped her hands and said, “Look at ___5___ other, not only at your feet.” Her advice helped ___6___ lot. We learned ___7___ listen to the beat and move together.

The second rehearsal was ___8___ than the first one. ___9___ a few students forgot their positions, we corrected the mistakes quickly. We even added a short ending pose so that the audience could clap on time. When Ms. Yang nodded with a smile, we knew we were on the ___10___ track.

## 选项

1. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. preparing &emsp;&emsp; C. to prepare &emsp;&emsp; D. prepared  
2. A. Because &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. If &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
3. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. hardest &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. under &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
5. A. each &emsp;&emsp; B. every &emsp;&emsp; C. both &emsp;&emsp; D. all  
6. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where  
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
9. A. If &emsp;&emsp; B. Because &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
10. A. right &emsp;&emsp; B. rightly &emsp;&emsp; C. rightest &emsp;&emsp; D. more right  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*begin to do / begin doing*）  
**【解析】** *begin to prepare* 与 *begin preparing* 均可，选项中给出不定式 *to prepare*，为常见正确形式。*prepare/preparing/prepared* 不能直接作 *begin* 的宾语（无连接词情况下）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 从属连词表让步  
**【解析】** “不是专业舞者”与“大家仍努力练习”为让步关系，用 *Although*。*Because* 表原因；*If/Unless* 表条件。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词 *hard* 与 *hardly* 辨析  
**【解析】** *practice hard* 表示“努力练习”，*hard* 为副词。*harder* 无比较对象；*hardly* 意为“几乎不”，语义相反；*hardest* 无三者比较语境。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *under* 表“在……之下”  
**【解析】** *under the bright lights* 表示在聚光灯下。*in/on/at* 不如 *under* 贴切表达“灯光笼罩”。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *each other*  
**【解析】** *look at each other* 表示“互相对视/彼此配合”。*every other* 表“每隔一个”；*both/all* 不与 *other* 构成此义。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *a lot*  
**【解析】** *helped a lot* 表示“帮助很大”。*a lot* 为固定短语。*an lot* 错误；*the lot* 语义不对。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** *learn how to listen to the beat* 表示“学会如何听节拍”。*what/when/where* 与“听节拍、同步动作”的方式不符。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（*than* 提示）  
**【解析】** *better than the first one*，用比较级 *better*。*good/well* 无比较；*best* 为最高级。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 从属连词表让步  
**【解析】** “少数同学忘位”与“我们迅速纠正”为让步：尽管有小失误，仍及时处理，用 *Although*。*If/Because/Unless* 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *on the right track*  
**【解析】** *on the right track* 表示“走上正轨”，*right* 为形容词作定语。*rightly* 为副词；*rightest/more right* 不符合英语习惯搭配。故选 A。
