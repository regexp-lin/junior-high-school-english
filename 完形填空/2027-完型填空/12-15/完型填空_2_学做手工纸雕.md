# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸雕

## 完形填空原文

In the school art week, I joined ___1___ paper-carving workshop in the library. The instructor explained ___2___ paper carving is popular in many places because it is cheap, light, and easy to carry. She showed us several traditional patterns and told us ___3___ attention to small curves on the design.

Then she gave each student ___4___ black paper, a cutting mat, and a small craft knife. “Never cut ___5___ fast,” she reminded us. “Move the knife ___6___ along the lines.” I chose a pattern of a rabbit and started ___7___ the outline carefully. At the beginning, my hands shook ___8___ little, so I had to stop and take a rest.

Two hours passed ___9___. When I removed the extra paper, a delicate rabbit appeared. My classmates said it looked ___10___ real than theirs. I knew that was a joke, but their words still made me feel warm and confident.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. why &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
3. A. pay &emsp;&emsp; B. to pay &emsp;&emsp; C. paying &emsp;&emsp; D. paid  
4. A. piece &emsp;&emsp; B. a piece of &emsp;&emsp; C. a piece &emsp;&emsp; D. pieces of  
5. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. much  
6. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slower &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowest  
7. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. following &emsp;&emsp; C. to follow &emsp;&emsp; D. followed  
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
9. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quicker &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. quickest  
10. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词表泛指  
**【解析】** *join a paper-carving workshop* 表示“参加一场纸雕工作坊”，泛指用 *a*。*paper* 以辅音音素 /p/ 开头，不用 *an*；*the* 无特指依据；零冠词不自然。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 *why*  
**【解析】** 后文 *because it is cheap...* 解释原因，前文用 *explained why...*。*what/how/when* 与 *because* 的原因解释对应不如 *why* 紧密。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*tell sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *told us to pay attention* 为 *tell sb. to do sth.* 结构，用不定式 *to pay*。*pay/paying/paid* 不能直接作 *told us* 的宾补。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不可数名词的量表达 *a piece of*  
**【解析】** *paper* 不可数，“一张纸”用 *a piece of black paper*。*piece*、*a piece*（缺 *of*）、*pieces of*（与 *each student* 单配不当）均不规范。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词 *too* 表“过于”  
**【解析】** *Never cut too fast* 提醒不要切得太快，*too* 表程度过头。*very/so* 也可修饰副词，但此处强调“过快危险”，*too* 最贴切；*much* 修饰 *fast* 不如 *too* 自然。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 *move*，用 *slowly*。*slow/slower/slowest* 为形容词级，不能修饰动词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*start doing sth.*）  
**【解析】** *started following the outline* 表示开始沿线刻画，动名词作宾语符合持续动作。*start to follow* 也可，但选项提供 *following* 时选动名词更贴切。*follow/to follow/followed* 形式不对。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *a little*（程度）  
**【解析】** *shook a little* 表示“有一点发抖”。*little*（不加 *a*）常表“很少”，语义不同；*the little* 不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** *passed quickly* 表时间过得快，用副词 *quickly*。*quick/quicker/quickest* 为形容词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（*than* 提示）  
**【解析】** *more real than theirs*，*than* 提示比较级用 *more real*。*as* 需 *as...as*；*like* 不构成比较级；*most* 为最高级。故选 C。
