# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸花

## 完形填空原文

My neighbor, Grandma Liu, is very good at making paper flowers. She ___1___ this skill for over forty years. One afternoon, she invited me to learn from her.

She had prepared colorful tissue paper, scissors and thin wire. "Paper flowers can last much ___2___ than real ones," she said with a smile.

First, she cut the tissue paper into rectangles and ___3___ five pieces together. Then she folded them back and forth like a fan. ___4___ step needed to be neat and even.

After folding, she tied a piece of wire ___5___ around the center. Then came the exciting part — I carefully pulled each layer of paper ___6___, one by one. Slowly, a beautiful flower appeared.

I was ___7___ at how real it looked. Grandma Liu told me to add some green paper ___8___ leaves. When I finished, my flower looked almost as beautiful ___9___ hers.

She put all the flowers into a vase. The room was immediately ___10___ with color and joy. I thanked her for teaching me such a wonderful craft.

## 选项

1. A. practice &emsp;&emsp; B. practiced &emsp;&emsp; C. has practiced &emsp;&emsp; D. is practicing
2. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. longer &emsp;&emsp; C. longest &emsp;&emsp; D. the longest
3. A. stack &emsp;&emsp; B. stacks &emsp;&emsp; C. stacked &emsp;&emsp; D. stacking
4. A. Both &emsp;&emsp; B. All &emsp;&emsp; C. Each &emsp;&emsp; D. Neither
5. A. tight &emsp;&emsp; B. tightly &emsp;&emsp; C. tighter &emsp;&emsp; D. tightest
6. A. down &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. away &emsp;&emsp; D. up
7. A. amaze &emsp;&emsp; B. amazing &emsp;&emsp; C. amazed &emsp;&emsp; D. amazement
8. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
9. A. than &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. as &emsp;&emsp; D. like
10. A. fill &emsp;&emsp; B. filling &emsp;&emsp; C. fills &emsp;&emsp; D. filled

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 现在完成时（for + 时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她练习这门技艺已经超过四十年了。"for over forty years"搭配现在完成时 has practiced。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（much + 比较级 + than）
**【解析】** 本句意为：纸花能比真花保存更长时间。than 提示用比较级，much 修饰 longer。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时（and 连接并列谓语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把薄纸裁成长方形并把五张叠在一起。cut 和 stacked 并列，用过去时。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定代词（each 每一个）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一步都需要整齐均匀。Each 强调"每一个"，后接单数名词 step。Both 用于两者；All 用于三者以上（但接复数）。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她用一根铁丝在中间紧紧绑住。修饰动词 tied 用副词 tightly。tight 可作副词但 tightly 更规范。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词短语（pull up 拉起）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我小心地把每一层纸向上拉开。pull up 表示"向上拉/拉起"。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很惊讶它看起来多么逼真。amazed 表示人的感受"感到惊讶的"。amazing 修饰物。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（as 作为）
**【解析】** 本句意为：刘奶奶让我加一些绿色的纸作为叶子。"as leaves"表示"作为叶子"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构（as + adj. + as 同级比较）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的花看起来几乎和她的一样漂亮。"almost as beautiful as"为同级比较结构。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 被动语态（was filled with）
**【解析】** 本句意为：房间立刻充满了色彩和欢乐。"was filled with"表示"被……充满"。故选 D。
