# 中考英语完形填空：图书馆做志愿者

## 完形填空原文

During the summer vacation, I decided to work ___1___ a volunteer at the community library. It was my first time doing volunteer work, and I felt both nervous and excited.

The librarian, Mrs. Chen, showed me around ___2___ the first day. She taught me how to sort books and put them back on the ___3___ shelves. It seemed simple, but there were thousands of books in the library.

My main job was to help children find the books ___4___ they wanted. Some children were too ___5___ to reach the top shelves, so I would get books down for them. Their happy smiles made me feel ___6___.

One afternoon, a little boy asked me to ___7___ him a story. I sat down with him and read a picture book about animals. He listened so ___8___ that he forgot about his mother waiting outside.

After two weeks of volunteering, I learned a lot. I became more ___9___ than before and understood the importance of helping ___10___. I plan to continue volunteering next summer.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. as &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. right &emsp;&emsp; B. rightly &emsp;&emsp; C. wrong &emsp;&emsp; D. rights
4. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. that
5. A. tall &emsp;&emsp; B. short &emsp;&emsp; C. long &emsp;&emsp; D. wide
6. A. worth &emsp;&emsp; B. worthy &emsp;&emsp; C. worthwhile &emsp;&emsp; D. valued
7. A. tell &emsp;&emsp; B. say &emsp;&emsp; C. speak &emsp;&emsp; D. read
8. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. careless &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
9. A. confident &emsp;&emsp; B. confidently &emsp;&emsp; C. confidence &emsp;&emsp; D. unconfident
10. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. the other &emsp;&emsp; C. another &emsp;&emsp; D. others

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（as 作为）
**【解析】** 本句意为：暑假期间，我决定在社区图书馆做志愿者。"work as"表示"担任、作为"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（on the first day）
**【解析】** 本句意为：图书馆员陈老师第一天带我参观了一圈。"on the first day"表示"在第一天"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（right 正确的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我怎样分类图书并放回正确的书架。right 表示"正确的"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 定语从句（that 指代物）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的主要工作是帮孩子们找到他们想要的书。先行词 books 为物，that 在从句中作宾语。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（short 矮的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些小孩太矮了够不到最高的书架。too short to reach 表示"太矮而够不到"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（worthwhile 值得的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们开心的笑容让我觉得很值得。worthwhile 表示"值得的"，可作表语。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（read sb. a story 给某人读故事）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一天下午，一个小男孩让我给他读故事。"read sb. a story"表示"给某人读故事"。tell 也可以说故事，但下文"I read a picture book"提示用 read。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他听得很认真以至于忘了妈妈在外面等他。修饰动词 listened 用副词 carefully。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（more + 多音节形容词 + than）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我变得比以前更自信了。"more confident than"为比较级结构。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 不定代词（others 其他人）
**【解析】** 本句意为：理解了帮助他人的重要性。others 泛指"其他人"。故选 D。
