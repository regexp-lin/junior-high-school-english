# 中考英语完形填空：消防安全演练

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, our school organized a fire safety drill. Before it started, our headteacher told us ___1___ run toward the playground when we heard the alarm.

When the loud bell rang, we covered ___2___ mouths and noses with wet towels and lined up quickly. Some students felt scared, ___3___ nobody pushed or shouted.

On the playground, a firefighter explained ___4___ to use a fire extinguisher. He also reminded us ___5___ play with lighters at home.

Then we watched a short video about real accidents. The stories were serious, ___6___ they helped us understand why small mistakes ___7___ cause big problems.

At the end of the activity, each group ___8___ had made a safety poster was asked to share one useful tip. Our monitor said we should check the smoke alarm ___9___ month.

I learned that staying calm is ___10___ important than running fast during an emergency.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. not to &emsp;&emsp; C. / &emsp;&emsp; D. of  
2. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
5. A. to not &emsp;&emsp; B. not to &emsp;&emsp; C. don’t &emsp;&emsp; D. not  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. should &emsp;&emsp; C. must &emsp;&emsp; D. need  
8. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
9. A. each &emsp;&emsp; B. every &emsp;&emsp; C. both &emsp;&emsp; D. all  
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell us to run... 不定式作宾补，肯定式用 to。not to 表否定，与“听到警报跑向操场”的指令相反。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词 our  
**【解析】** covered our mouths and noses，名词前用 our。we/us 不能作定语；ours 后不接名词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “有人害怕”与“没人推搡喊叫”转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 how（how to use）  
**【解析】** explained how to use a fire extinguisher 表示“讲解如何使用”。what/which/when 不如 how 贴切。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. not to do）  
**【解析】** remind us not to play... 不定式否定式 not to do。to not 语序不规范；don’t/not 不能直接作 remind 的宾补。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “故事很严肃”与“帮助我们理解”转折，用 but。and 并列；or 选择；so 结果，不如 but 贴切“严肃但有教育意义”。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词 can（可能）  
**【解析】** mistakes can cause big problems 表示“小错也可能导致大问题”，can 表可能性。should 应该；must 必须；need 需要，语气过强或搭配不顺。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句 that（指物/组，作主语）  
**【解析】** each group that had made a safety poster 中先行词为 group，从句缺主语且表“已经做好海报的小组”，用 that。which 在部分语法规则中与 each 连用不如 that 稳；who/whose 指人或表所属，此处从句需主语且语义为“完成了海报”。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** every + 单数名词（every month）  
**【解析】** every month 表示“每个月”。each month 也可，但选项中 every 更常见于“定期检查”。both/all 后接复数；each 虽可 each month，本题选 every 与习惯表达 check... every month 一致。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 more important than  
**【解析】** than 提示比较级，important 多音节用 more important。very/too 无比较；most 为最高级。故选 C。
