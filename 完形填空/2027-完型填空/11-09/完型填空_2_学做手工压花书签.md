# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工压花书签

## 完形填空原文

In science class, Ms. Wu taught us ___1___ pressed-flower bookmarks. She brought dried flowers, clear tape, and thick paper ___2___ a plastic box.

We cut the paper into narrow strips and arranged the flowers ___3___ we liked. Then we covered them ___4___ tape carefully to keep air out.

While we were working, Tom broke ___5___ strip by accident. Ms. Wu gave him ___6___ piece of paper and said mistakes were part of learning.

When my bookmark dried, it looked almost ___7___ a tiny painting. I wrote a short poem on the back and planned ___8___ it to my sister as a gift.

My classmates showed ___9___ projects proudly. Ms. Wu told us that patience matters ___10___ than perfect tools if we want to finish a delicate craft.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
4. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
5. A. he &emsp;&emsp; B. him &emsp;&emsp; C. his &emsp;&emsp; D. himself  
6. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. another &emsp;&emsp; C. others &emsp;&emsp; D. the others  
7. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. likes &emsp;&emsp; C. likely &emsp;&emsp; D. liking  
8. A. send &emsp;&emsp; B. sending &emsp;&emsp; C. to send &emsp;&emsp; D. sent  
9. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs  
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（teach sb. to do）  
**【解析】** teach us to make... 不定式作宾补。make/making/made 不符合 teach 的常见结构。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 in（在盒子里）  
**【解析】** in a plastic box 表示装在塑料盒里带来。on/at/by 不合适。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句/方式状语从句 how we liked  
**【解析】** arranged the flowers how we liked 表示“按我们喜欢的方式摆放”。what/which/when 与 arrange 搭配不如 how 自然。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with（用胶带覆盖）  
**【解析】** covered them with tape 表示“用胶带覆盖”。of/for/by 搭配不当。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词 his  
**【解析】** broke his strip，strip 为名词，用 his 表所属。he/him 不能作定语；himself 反身代词不符“他的纸条”。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 another（又一、再一）  
**【解析】** another piece of paper 表示“另一张纸”。other 后常接复数名词；others/the others 后不接 piece。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 like（像……一样）  
**【解析】** looked almost like a tiny painting 中 like 为介词“像”。likes 为动词第三人称；likely 形容词；liking 动名词，均不对。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（plan to do）  
**【解析】** planned to send it... plan 后接不定式。send/sending/sent 不符合 plan 搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词 their  
**【解析】** showed their projects，名词 projects 前用 their。they/them 不能作定语；theirs 后不接名词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级 more than  
**【解析】** matters more than perfect tools，than 提示比较级，用 more。very/too 无比较；most 为最高级。故选 C。
