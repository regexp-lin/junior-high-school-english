# 中考英语完形填空：勇敢参加班长竞选

## 完形填空原文

When our teacher said we could run for class monitor, my heart beat fast. I had never given a speech ___1___ front of so many people.

My best friend Anna encouraged me. She said I was responsible and good ___2___ organizing small group work. Her words made me feel ___3___ confident than before.

I spent two evenings writing my speech. I didn't promise fancy things; ___4___, I listed three practical plans: a reading corner, a study group, and a class diary ___5___ good deeds.

On election day, my hands were cold, ___6___ my voice was clear. After I finished, classmates clapped politely. I didn't know ___7___ I would win.

In the end, another student got ___8___ votes, but the teacher thanked everyone ___9___ joining bravely. I learned that trying something new is ___10___ valuable than the result itself.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
3. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most
4. A. however &emsp;&emsp; B. instead &emsp;&emsp; C. therefore &emsp;&emsp; D. besides
5. A. record &emsp;&emsp; B. to record &emsp;&emsp; C. recording &emsp;&emsp; D. recorded
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. why
8. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of
10. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（in front of）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未在这么多人面前演讲过。in front of 在……前面。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be good at）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说我很有责任心，擅长组织小组活动。be good at 擅长。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的话让我比以前更自信。后有 than，confident 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more confident。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我没有许诺花哨的事；相反，我列出了三个切实可行的计划。instead 表示“相反；而是”。however 然而；therefore 因此；besides 此外。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作定语表用途）
**【解析】** 本句意为：以及用来记录好人好事的班级日记。a class diary to record good deeds 中不定式 to record 作后置定语，表示日记的用途“用来记录”。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：竞选那天我的手很凉，但声音很清晰。前后转折用 but。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词（whether）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不知道自己是否会赢。whether 表示“是否”，常与 know 搭配。what 什么；how 如何；why 为什么。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后另一位同学得到了更多选票。暗含与我比较，votes 可数，用 more votes。many/much 原级；most 最高级需范围。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（thank sb. for doing sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师感谢每个人都勇敢地参与。thank sb. for (doing) sth. 因……感谢某人。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了尝试新事物比结果本身更有价值。后有 than，valuable 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more valuable。故选 C。
