# 中考英语完形填空：在家看纪录片

## 完形填空原文

One rainy afternoon, I stayed at home ___1___ my brother. Mom was busy with work, ___2___ she suggested we watch a nature documentary together. I turned on the TV and chose a program ___3___ oceans and whales. Rain tapped against the windows, and the living room smelled like the oranges Mom had peeled earlier.

At first, my brother kept checking his phone. I worried he would leave halfway, so I offered him the better seat closer to the screen. ___4___ ten minutes, he put it down and leaned closer to the screen. The film showed how whales travel long distances ___5___ find food. I was amazed ___6___ the clear underwater photography.

When the narrator talked about pollution, the room became ___7___. I looked at the plastic bottles on our table and felt a little ___8___. After the show, we decided to carry cloth bags ___9___ shopping and use fewer disposable cups.

That day, a simple TV program changed our daily habits ___10___. Small actions can protect the big blue home we share.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. above &emsp;&emsp; B. below &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. about
4. A. After &emsp;&emsp; B. Before &emsp;&emsp; C. During &emsp;&emsp; D. Without
5. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. thanks to &emsp;&emsp; C. instead of &emsp;&emsp; D. as soon as
6. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
7. A. noisy &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. bright &emsp;&emsp; D. dirty
8. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; C. uncomfortable &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
9. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. before &emsp;&emsp; D. after
10. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. suddenly &emsp;&emsp; D. safely

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和弟弟待在家里。stay at home with sb. 表示和某人一起在家。A 项 for 为了；B 项 of ……的；D 项 about 关于。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈忙于工作，“所以”建议我们一起看自然纪录片。结果/顺承用 so。A 项 because 因为（因果颠倒）；C 项 although 尽管；D 项 unless 除非。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了一部“关于”海洋与鲸鱼的节目。a program about... 表示关于……。A 项 above 在上方；B 项 below 在下方；C 项 against 反对。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：十分钟后，他放下手机凑近屏幕。After ten minutes 表示过了十分钟。B 项 Before 之前；C 项 During 在期间；D 项 Without 没有。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：鲸鱼长途迁徙是为了寻找食物。in order to 表目的。B 项 thanks to 多亏；C 项 instead of 代替；D 项 as soon as 一……就……。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对清晰的水下摄影感到惊叹。be amazed by/at 为固定搭配，此处用 by。A 项 for；C 项 at 也可，但选项中 B 为 by；D 项 with 不搭配 amazed。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：讲到污染时，房间变得安静。严肃话题让人静下来。A 项 noisy 吵闹；B 项 quiet 安静；C 项 bright 明亮；D 项 dirty 脏。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到桌上塑料瓶，我有点不自在。A 项 proud 自豪；B 项 relaxed 放松；C 项 uncomfortable 不舒服、不自在；D 项 bored 无聊。与环保反思呼应。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：购物“之前”带布袋。before shopping 作时间状语，表示提前准备。A 项 when；B 项 while；D 项 after 之后，不符合“提前带袋”习惯。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一部简单的电视节目“慢慢地”改变了我们的日常习惯。习惯改变多为渐进过程，slowly 最贴切。B 项 loudly 大声；C 项 suddenly 突然，与“日常习惯”形成过程略不符；D 项 safely 安全。故选 A。
