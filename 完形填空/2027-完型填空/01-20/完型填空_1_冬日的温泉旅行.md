# 中考英语完形填空：冬日的温泉旅行

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, my family drove to a small town ___1___ for its hot springs. The road was wet because of light snow, ___2___ Dad drove slowly and carefully. I sat in the back seat and watched the mountains ___3___ in the fog. My sister played soft music on her phone, and the car heater kept our feet warm.

When we arrived, warm steam rose ___4___ the outdoor pool. A friendly worker handed us clean towels and reminded us to drink water after bathing for a long time. I changed into my swimsuit and tested the water ___5___ my toes first. The temperature felt just right, neither too hot ___6___ too cold.

That night, we stayed in a wooden hotel and ate local noodles. My sister said the trip was expensive, ___7___ I thought it was worth the money. We talked ___8___ and laughed until late. On the way back, I wrote a short diary ___9___ the beautiful lights along the river.

Winter can be long and gray, ___10___ such a warm memory makes it brighter.

## 选项

1. A. famous &emsp;&emsp; B. sorry &emsp;&emsp; C. ready &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. although
3. A. disappear &emsp;&emsp; B. shine &emsp;&emsp; C. sleep &emsp;&emsp; D. rise
4. A. under &emsp;&emsp; B. above &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
5. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. about
6. A. nor &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. but
7. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. if
8. A. softly &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. politely &emsp;&emsp; D. angrily
9. A. below &emsp;&emsp; B. about &emsp;&emsp; C. across &emsp;&emsp; D. inside
10. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. but

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们开车去一个因温泉而“闻名”的小镇。be famous for 为固定搭配。B 项 sorry 抱歉；C 项 ready 准备好；D 项 afraid 害怕。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：路面因小雪潮湿，“所以”爸爸开得很慢很小心。因果关系用 so。A 项 but 转折；C 项 or 选择；D 项 although 尽管。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（感官动词用法）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看着群山在雾中“消失”。watch sb./sth. do sth. 表示看到动作过程；disappear in the fog 符合冬景。B 项 shine 照耀；C 项 sleep 拟人不当；D 项 rise 升起与雾中景象不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：热气从室外池子上方升起。above the pool 表示在池面上方。A 项 under 在下面；C 项 against 靠着；D 项 beyond 超出。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：先用脚趾“试探”水温。test...with... 表示用……测试。A 项 for 为了；C 项 by 通过；D 项 about 关于。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：既不烫也不冷。neither...nor... 为固定搭配。B 项 or 常与 either 搭配；C 项 and；D 项 but。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：姐姐说旅行很贵，“但”我觉得物有所值。转折用 but。A 项 because；B 项 so；D 项 if。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们轻声聊天，笑到很晚。家庭温馨场景用 softly（轻声地）最贴切。B 项 loudly 大声；C 项 politely 礼貌；D 项 angrily 生气。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我写下一篇关于河边美丽灯光的短日记。a diary about... 表示“关于……的日记”。A 项 below 在下面；C 项 across 穿过；D 项 inside 在里面。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冬天可能又长又灰蒙蒙，“但”这样的温暖回忆让它更明亮。转折用 but。A 项 if；B 项 unless；C 项 and 顺承稍弱。故选 D。
