# 中考英语完形填空：收到期末成绩单

## 完形填空原文

The report cards were given out today. I was very nervous ___1___ the teacher handed mine to me. My hands were shaking when I opened the envelope.

To my surprise, my math score was ___2___ higher than last time. I used to be weak in math, but this semester I worked really ___3___. I did extra exercises every night and asked my teacher questions ___4___ I didn't understand something.

My English score was the ___5___ in the class. The teacher wrote a comment saying that my reading and writing had improved ___6___. This made me feel very proud.

However, my science score ___7___ a little. The teacher pointed out that I needed to spend more time ___8___ experiments and reviewing notes. I knew she was right.

When I got home, I showed the report card to my parents. They were pleased ___9___ my overall progress. Father said, "Results matter, but the effort you put in matters ___10___."

I promised to keep working hard and improve my weak subjects during the summer.

## 选项

1. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
2. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. most
3. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. harder &emsp;&emsp; D. hardest
4. A. whatever &emsp;&emsp; B. whenever &emsp;&emsp; C. wherever &emsp;&emsp; D. however
5. A. high &emsp;&emsp; B. higher &emsp;&emsp; C. highest &emsp;&emsp; D. highly
6. A. great &emsp;&emsp; B. greatly &emsp;&emsp; C. greater &emsp;&emsp; D. greatest
7. A. drop &emsp;&emsp; B. drops &emsp;&emsp; C. dropped &emsp;&emsp; D. dropping
8. A. do &emsp;&emsp; B. to do &emsp;&emsp; C. doing &emsp;&emsp; D. done
9. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for
10. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（when 当……时候）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师把成绩单递给我时我非常紧张。when 表示"当……时候"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰比较级（much + 比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：令我惊讶的是，数学成绩比上次高了很多。much 修饰比较级 higher。many 不修饰比较级。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（hard 努力地）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这学期我真的很努力。hard 作副词意为"努力地"。hardly 意为"几乎不"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（whenever 每当）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每当我不理解的时候就问老师。whenever 表示"每当"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（the + 最高级 + in）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的英语成绩是全班最高的。"the highest in the class"为最高级结构。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师评价说我的阅读和写作进步很大。修饰动词 improved 用副词 greatly。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：但是我的科学成绩下降了一点。上下文为过去时态，用 dropped。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（spend time doing sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师指出我需要花更多时间做实验和复习笔记。"spend time doing sth."为固定结构。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be pleased with 对……满意）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们对我的整体进步感到高兴。"be pleased with"表示"对……满意"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词比较级（more 更多）
**【解析】** 本句意为：结果很重要，但你付出的努力更重要。more 为比较级，暗含比较。故选 C。
