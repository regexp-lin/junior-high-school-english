# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工蜡烛

## 完形填空原文

My mother bought a candle-making kit online. She thought it would be ___1___ interesting activity for us to do together during the weekend.

The kit included soy wax, wicks, fragrance oil and ___2___ small glass jars. The instructions were easy to follow. First, we melted the wax in a pot ___3___ hot water.

While the wax was ___4___, I placed a wick in the center of each jar. I used a stick across the top to keep the wick ___5___. This was important because a crooked wick would not burn evenly.

After the wax melted completely, Mother added some rose fragrance oil. The whole kitchen ___6___ wonderful immediately. Then she carefully poured the hot wax ___7___ each jar.

We had to wait for the wax to cool down and ___8___ solid. This took about four hours. When I touched the candle, it felt smooth and ___9___.

That evening, we lit the candles. The soft light and gentle rose scent made the room feel warm and cozy. Father said our handmade candles were nicer ___10___ the store-bought ones.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. a few &emsp;&emsp; B. few &emsp;&emsp; C. a little &emsp;&emsp; D. little
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. under
4. A. melt &emsp;&emsp; B. melts &emsp;&emsp; C. melted &emsp;&emsp; D. melting
5. A. straightly &emsp;&emsp; B. straight &emsp;&emsp; C. straighter &emsp;&emsp; D. straightest
6. A. smell &emsp;&emsp; B. smells &emsp;&emsp; C. smelled &emsp;&emsp; D. smelling
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. become &emsp;&emsp; B. becomes &emsp;&emsp; C. became &emsp;&emsp; D. becoming
9. A. coolly &emsp;&emsp; B. cool &emsp;&emsp; C. cooler &emsp;&emsp; D. coolest
10. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. than

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她觉得这会是一个有趣的周末活动。interesting 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，泛指用 an。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 不定代词（a few + 可数名词复数）
**【解析】** 本句意为：套装包括大豆蜡、灯芯、香精油和几个小玻璃罐。jars 为可数名词复数，"a few"表示"几个"。few 表示"几乎没有"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（over 在……上方）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在热水锅上融化蜡。over hot water 表示"在热水上方"（隔水加热）。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 过去进行时（was/were + doing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：蜡在融化的时候。"was melting"为过去进行时，表示正在进行。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词作宾补（keep + 宾语 + adj.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用一根棍子横在顶部来保持灯芯笔直。"keep...straight"中 straight 为形容词作宾补。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：整个厨房立刻闻起来很棒。上下文为过去时态，用 smelled。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（pour...into... 倒入）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后她小心地把热蜡倒入每个玻璃罐。"pour...into..."表示"倒入"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词原形（and 连接并列不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们要等蜡冷却并变成固体。to cool down and become solid 中 become 与 cool 并列用原形。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 系动词 + 形容词（feel + adj.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我摸蜡烛时，感觉光滑凉爽。feel 为系动词，后接形容词 cool。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（nicer + than）
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说我们手工做的蜡烛比店里买的更漂亮。nicer 为比较级，后接 than。故选 D。
