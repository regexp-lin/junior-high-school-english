# 中考英语完形填空：暑假回忆录

## 完形填空原文

On the last evening of the holiday, I opened my photo album ___1___ looked through the pictures slowly. There was the photo ___2___ was taken at the beach, and another one ___3___ showed my friends and me volunteering in July.

Each picture reminded me ___4___ a different story. I wrote a short paragraph ___5___ each photo so that I would not forget the feelings behind them. Mom read my notes and said, "Your summer seems ___6___ than last year's because you tried many new things."

I nodded and realized time had passed ___7___ quickly. I promised ___8___ I would keep writing short reflections every month. When I closed the album, I felt thankful ___9___ the people who had stayed with me. I also hoped ___10___ new term would bring more growth.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
4. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. beside
6. A. rich &emsp;&emsp; B. richer &emsp;&emsp; C. richest &emsp;&emsp; D. the richest
7. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. too &emsp;&emsp; D. very
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我打开相册，慢慢翻看照片。opened 与 looked 为顺承并列，用 and。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：有一张在海边拍的照片。先行词 photo 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：还有一张展示我和朋友们七月做志愿活动的照片。先行词 one 代指 photo，为物，用 which。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：每张照片让我想起不同的故事。remind sb. of sth. 使某人想起某事。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在每张照片旁边写了一段话。beside 表示“在……旁边”。in/on/at 不符合空间关系。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：你的暑假似乎比去年更丰富。有 than，用 richer（也可用 more colorful，此处选项给 richer 表“更充实”）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到时间过得太快了。too quickly 表示“太快”，带一点感叹语气。more/most 需比较对象；very 语气较弱。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我保证每个月都写短反思。promise 后接 that 从句。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很感谢陪伴我的人。be thankful to sb. 感谢某人。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也希望新学期带来更多成长。特指即将到来的新学期，用 the new term。故选 C。
