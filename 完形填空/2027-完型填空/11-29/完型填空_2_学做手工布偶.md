# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工布偶

## 完形填空原文

In the handicraft club, we learned ___1___ a cloth doll for Children’s Day. The materials were soft fabric, buttons, cotton, and thread. Our teacher said the project ___2___ into three clear steps.

Step one was ___3___ a paper pattern on the cloth. I moved the pencil too fast, ___4___ the ears looked uneven. My partner lent me ___5___ eraser and encouraged me to try again.

Step two was sewing the edges ___6___ a needle. I pricked my finger once, ___7___ I was more careful after that. Step three was filling the doll ___8___ cotton and adding two black eyes.

When I held up my little bear, I couldn’t believe I ___9___ it by myself. The teacher took a photo of our group and said teamwork made the work ___10___ than we expected.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. divides &emsp;&emsp; B. divided &emsp;&emsp; C. was divided &emsp;&emsp; D. has divided  
3. A. draw &emsp;&emsp; B. drawing &emsp;&emsp; C. to draw &emsp;&emsp; D. drew  
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. though &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
7. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
8. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
9. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. had made &emsp;&emsp; D. have made  
10. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. easiest &emsp;&emsp; D. the easiest  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** learn to do sth.  
**【解析】** learn to make 表示学习去做某事。learn doing 非典型搭配；learn make 缺少 to。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** project 与 divide 为被动关系，且叙述过去课堂介绍，用 was divided into。主动 divides/divided/has divided 主语为 project 时不合理。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 不定式作表语说明步骤内容  
**【解析】** Step one was to draw… 用不定式表将要做的具体步骤内容。drawing 也可作表语，但步骤列举常用 to do 表“要做的是……”；draw/drew 谓语形式不能直接作表语。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表结果  
**【解析】** “铅笔移动太快”导致“耳朵看起来不齐”，用 so。because 表原因应放在结果前逻辑不同；though/unless 不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 an 用于元音音素开头  
**【解析】** eraser 以元音音素开头，用 an eraser。a 用于辅音音素开头；the 表特指；零冠词不适用可数单数。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表示“用（工具）”  
**【解析】** sewing the edges with a needle 表示用针缝合。in/on/by 与 needle 搭配不贴切。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “扎到手指一次”与“之后更小心”为转折，用 but。if/until/because 语义不符。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** fill … with … 固定搭配  
**【解析】** filling the doll with cotton 为固定短语。fill of 错误；fill for/by 不搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** couldn’t believe 为过去，制作布偶发生在其之前，强调“已经做出”，用 had made。made 仅过去；have made 与过去主句时态不一致。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than）  
**【解析】** easier than we expected 与 than 搭配。原级/最高级无 than 支撑。故选 B。
