# 中考英语完形填空：冬日里的暖心故事

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, a sudden snowstorm made the roads icy. ___1___ I was waiting for the bus, I saw an old man ___2___ slowly with a heavy bag.

A young driver stopped his car and rolled down the window. “Sir, ___3___ you like a ride home?” he asked politely. The old man hesitated, ___4___ the driver smiled and opened the door for him.

I watched the car disappear around the corner. ___5___ that moment, I felt the winter wind ___6___ cold at all. On the bus, I thought about ___7___ small kindness could change a stranger’s day.

When I got home, I told my parents ___8___ had happened. My father said, “When you grow up, remember ___9___ others the way you hope to be treated.” I nodded and wrote one sentence in my diary: “Warmth is not only sunshine — ___10___ it is also a helping hand.”

## 选项

1. A. Before &emsp;&emsp; B. While &emsp;&emsp; C. Until &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
2. A. walk &emsp;&emsp; B. walks &emsp;&emsp; C. walking &emsp;&emsp; D. walked  
3. A. would &emsp;&emsp; B. should &emsp;&emsp; C. could &emsp;&emsp; D. must  
4. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
5. A. In &emsp;&emsp; B. On &emsp;&emsp; C. At &emsp;&emsp; D. For  
6. A. isn’t &emsp;&emsp; B. wasn’t &emsp;&emsp; C. doesn’t &emsp;&emsp; D. don’t  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
9. A. treat &emsp;&emsp; B. to treat &emsp;&emsp; C. treating &emsp;&emsp; D. treated  
10. A. / &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. or  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** while 引导时间状语从句  
**【解析】** While I was waiting 表示“在我等车期间”，while 常接进行时。Before/Until 表先后；Unless 表条件。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** see sb. doing sth.  
**【解析】** see an old man walking slowly 表示看见老人正缓慢地走，强调进行。see sb. do 表全过程，此处亦可，但选项中 walking 更符合“当时映入眼帘”的场景；walk/walked 不能直接作宾补结构。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词 would 表委婉请求  
**【解析】** Would you like…? 为固定礼貌用语。should/could/must 与 you like a ride 搭配不如 would 自然。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** 老人犹豫，但司机微笑开门，转折用 but。so 表结果；or 表选择；for 表原因（书面）。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 at 与 moment 搭配  
**【解析】** At that moment 为固定短语“在那一刻”。in/on/for 与 that moment 搭配不如 at 常见。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时与否定  
**【解析】** 叙述过去感受，felt 已过去，wind 为单数，否定用 wasn’t cold at all（口语中常接 any，此处选项为 wasn’t）。isn’t 现在时；don’t/doesn’t 与形容词 cold 不搭配（需 be 动词）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接副词 how  
**【解析】** thought about how small kindness could change… 表示思考“小小的善意如何能改变……”。what 需在从句中作宾语/主语；why/when 与句意侧重不符。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接代词 what  
**【解析】** told my parents what had happened，what 作 happened 的主语。that 引导从句时从句需完整陈述且无疑问含义，happened 缺宾语；which 表选择；how 表方式。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** remember to do sth.  
**【解析】** remember to treat others 表示“记得要去善待他人（将来行为）”。remember doing 表“记得做过”，与父亲叮嘱不符。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** not only … but (also) … 结构  
**【解析】** not only sunshine — but it is also… 为并列递进。and/or 无法与 not only 构成固定搭配；零冠词不成立。故选 B。
