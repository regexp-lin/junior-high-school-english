# 中考英语完形填空：期末复习计划

## 完形填空原文

The final exams are coming next month, ___1___ my classmates and I are making a study plan together. We agreed ___2___ we should start early instead of staying up late at the last minute.

Every weekday evening, I spend an hour ___3___ my notes for math and science. On weekends, I read English articles aloud ___4___ I can improve my pronunciation. If I feel sleepy, I take a short walk ___5___ drinking too much coffee.

My parents promised ___6___ they would keep the living room quiet while I studied. They also reminded me ___7___ breaks between long study periods. I wrote my goals on a card and put it ___8___ the desk to encourage myself.

I know exams are only one part of school life, ___9___ I still want to do my best. With a clear plan, I believe I will feel ___10___ nervous on the exam days.

## 选项

1. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. though  
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  
3. A. review &emsp;&emsp; B. reviewing &emsp;&emsp; C. to review &emsp;&emsp; D. reviewed  
4. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. instead of &emsp;&emsp; B. because of &emsp;&emsp; C. thanks to &emsp;&emsp; D. as for  
6. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
7. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. to take &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. took  
8. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. under  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
10. A. little &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. least &emsp;&emsp; D. fewer  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表结果  
**【解析】** “期末考试将至”与“一起制定计划”为因果顺承，用 so。but 表转折；or 表选择；though 表让步。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** agree 后的宾语从句引导词  
**【解析】** agreed that we should… 陈述共识内容，用 that。what 需在从句中作成分；if/whether 表是否，与“应早点开始”不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** spend … (in) doing sth.  
**【解析】** spend an hour reviewing my notes 为固定搭配。spend … to review 错误。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** so that 引导目的状语从句  
**【解析】** read articles aloud so that I can improve… 表目的。in order to 后接不定式，不接从句；although/unless 语义不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 短语辨析（instead of）  
**【解析】** instead of drinking too much coffee 表示“而不是喝太多咖啡”，符合选择散步替代咖啡提神的做法。because of/thanks to 表原因；as for 表“至于”，语义不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** promise 后的宾语从句  
**【解析】** promised that they would… 用 that 引导陈述性从句。what/which/how 均与从句结构不符。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** remind sb. to do sth.  
**【解析】** remind me to take breaks 为固定结构“提醒我去休息”。remind doing 非标准；remind take 缺少 to。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 on 表示“在……表面上”  
**【解析】** put it on the desk 表示把目标卡片放在书桌上，符合自我提醒的日常场景。in the desk 表抽屉里，可见性差；at the desk 多指人在位；under the desk 不符合激励自己时常看见的目的。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “考试只是校园生活一部分”与“仍想尽力”为转折，用 but。and 表并列；because/so 表因果，逻辑不当。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（与有计划相比更不紧张）  
**【解析】** feel less nervous 表示“没那么紧张”，less 修饰形容词 nervous。little 不直接修饰形容词表比较；least 为最高级；fewer 修饰可数名词复数。故选 B。
