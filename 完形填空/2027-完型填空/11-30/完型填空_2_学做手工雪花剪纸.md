# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工雪花剪纸

## 完形填空原文

Winter arrived, and our art teacher brought square red paper ___1___ teach us how to cut snowflakes. She folded the paper several times ___2___ it looked like a small triangle.

“You must cut slowly along the lines,” she said. “___3___ you rush, the paper may tear.” I followed her advice, ___4___ my first snowflake still looked uneven. ___5___ classmates laughed kindly and shared their skills with me.

After three tries, I ___6___ a snowflake with six neat corners. We taped the best works ___7___ the classroom windows. When the light came through, the patterns looked ___8___ tiny crystals.

This activity made me love traditional paper cutting ___9___. I also learned that practice makes perfect, ___10___ I want to try more difficult designs next time.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. though &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
3. A. If &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. Until  
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
5. A. My &emsp;&emsp; B. Mine &emsp;&emsp; C. Me &emsp;&emsp; D. Myself  
6. A. cut &emsp;&emsp; B. cuts &emsp;&emsp; C. was cutting &emsp;&emsp; D. had cut  
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
8. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
9. A. deep &emsp;&emsp; B. deeper &emsp;&emsp; C. deeply &emsp;&emsp; D. deepest  
10. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. though  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 不定式表目的  
**【解析】** brought square red paper to teach us 表示“带来红纸是为了教我们”，不定式作目的状语。and/but/or 表并列/转折/选择，不能准确表达目的。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** until 引导时间状语从句  
**【解析】** folded… several times until it looked like… 表示“一直折直到看起来像小三角形”。if 表条件；though 表让步；unless 表否定条件。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** if 引导条件状语从句  
**【解析】** If you rush, the paper may tear 表示“如果着急，纸可能会撕破”。Although 表让步；Unless 表“除非”，语义变为“除非着急”，逻辑反了；Until 表直到。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “听从建议”与“第一片雪花仍不齐”为转折，用 but。and 表并列；so 表结果；or 表选择。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** My classmates 需要形容词性物主代词修饰名词。mine 为名词性物主代词；me 为宾格；myself 为反身代词。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** After three tries 叙述过去完成的动作，用一般过去时 cut（cut 的过去式与原形同形）。第三人称单数 cuts 时态错误；was cutting 强调当时正在剪，不突出“尝试三次后完成”的结果；had cut 可表示“过去的过去”，但此处无更晚的过去参照点，一般过去更简洁自然。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 on 表示“贴在……表面”  
**【解析】** taped the works on the classroom windows 表示贴在窗户玻璃表面。in the windows 多指窗框内；at/to 搭配不自然。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 like 表示“像”  
**【解析】** looked like tiny crystals 表示“看起来像小晶体”。look as 后需接从句；look for 表寻找；look with 不搭配。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** love … deeply 用副词 deeply 修饰动词 love。deep 为形容词；deeper/deepest 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较对象与范围。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表结果  
**【解析】** “熟能生巧”与“下次想尝试更难图案”为因果关系，用 so。but 表转折；or 表选择；though 表让步。故选 B。
