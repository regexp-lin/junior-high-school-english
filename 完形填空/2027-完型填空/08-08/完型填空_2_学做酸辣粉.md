# 中考英语完形填空：学做酸辣粉

## 完形填空原文

Hot and sour noodles are famous ___1___ their strong taste. Aunt Wang invited me ___2___ her kitchen and taught me the basic steps. The sweet potato noodles ___3___ soaked in warm water before cooking.

She heated oil and added chili, then poured hot soup ___4___ the bowl. "You can adjust the vinegar ___5___ you like it more sour," she said. I tasted a spoonful and coughed ___6___ because it was spicier than I expected.

After a few tries, the flavor became ___7___ balanced. My cousin said it was ___8___ delicious bowl of noodles she had tasted that week. I wrote the recipe in my notebook ___9___ I could cook it again. Learning street food at home feels ___10___ exciting adventure.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on
3. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. should be &emsp;&emsp; D. have been
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until
6. A. sudden &emsp;&emsp; B. suddenly &emsp;&emsp; C. loud &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
7. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the most &emsp;&emsp; D. more
9. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be famous for
**【解析】** 本句意为：酸辣粉因其浓烈的味道而闻名。be famous for 因……而闻名。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王阿姨邀请我进她的厨房并教我基本步骤。invite sb. into... 邀请某人进入……。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：红薯粉在煮之前先在温水里浸泡。noodles 与 soak 为被动，叙述过去，用 were soaked。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：……然后把热汤倒进碗里。pour...into... 把……倒入……。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你喜欢更酸，可以调整醋的量。if 如果，引导条件状语从句。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尝了一勺，突然咳嗽起来，因为比我想的更辣。修饰动词 coughed，用 suddenly。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：试了几次后，味道变得更平衡了。暗含与之前相比，用 more balanced。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐说这是她那一周吃过的最美味的粉之一。that week 作范围，用 the most delicious。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把配方记在笔记本上以便能再做一次。so that 以便。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：在家学做街头小吃就像一场刺激的冒险。exciting 以元音音素开头，用 an。故选 B。
