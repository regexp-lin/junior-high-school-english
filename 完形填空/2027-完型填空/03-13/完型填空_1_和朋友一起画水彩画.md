# 中考英语完形填空：和朋友一起画水彩画

## 完形填空原文

On a sunny Sunday, my friend Nina invited me ___1___ her balcony to paint with watercolors. I had never tried it before, ___2___ I felt a little nervous.

Nina set out brushes, paints, and thick paper. "Don't worry about being ___3___," she said. "Watercolors like slow breathing."

We started with simple shapes: a blue cup, a yellow lemon, and a green leaf. The colors spread ___4___ the wet paper like tiny rivers. I laughed when my lemon looked ___5___ a small sun.

When I made a mistake, Nina passed me a clean brush. "You can lift extra paint ___6___ soft taps," she explained. Little ___7___ little, I learned to control the water.

By afternoon, our table was bright with small paintings. I realized art is not only about skill, ___8___ about patience and friendship.

Before I left, Nina gave me ___9___ extra set of paints. I thanked her and promised ___10___ practice every weekend.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
2. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. and
3. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. noisy &emsp;&emsp; C. lazy &emsp;&emsp; D. shy
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. across &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. as &emsp;&emsp; D. of
6. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. in
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：朋友邀请我到她的阳台一起画画。invite sb. to + 地点 表示“邀请某人去某地”，为常见搭配。on/at/for 与 invite 搭配不符合此结构。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前从没试过，所以有点紧张。因果关系用 so。but 表转折；or 表选择；and 表并列。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：别担心画得太完美。Don't worry about being perfect 符合学画语境。noisy/lazy/shy 与“水彩入门”劝慰不匹配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：颜色像小河一样在湿纸上蔓延开来。spread across 表示“铺开；蔓延过”。spread in/on/at 不如 across 能体现“在纸面上扩散”。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的柠檬画得像小太阳。look like 表示“看起来像”。look as 需接从句；for/of 不搭配 look。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你可以用轻柔的点按带走多余的颜料。with soft taps 表示“用轻柔的点击/点按方式”。by 虽可表方式，但 with + 具体工具/动作更自然。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：渐渐地，我学会了控制水分。little by little 为固定短语，意为“逐渐地”。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列结构 not only...but (also)...
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到艺术不仅关于技巧，还关于耐心与友谊。not only...but (also)... 为固定搭配。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：Nina 多送了我一套颜料。extra 以元音音素开头，用 an extra set。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我向她道谢并承诺每个周末练习。promise to do sth. 为固定搭配。故选 A。
