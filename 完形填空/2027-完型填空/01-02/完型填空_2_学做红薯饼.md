# 中考英语完形填空：学做红薯饼

## 完形填空原文

Last winter, my grandmother came to stay with us. One cold afternoon, she said, "Let's make sweet potato cakes together." I had never cooked before, so I was both ___1___ and nervous.

Grandma asked me to wash three big sweet potatoes first. Then she boiled them in a pot until they were ___2___ enough to mash. I used a fork to press them into a smooth paste. It took some effort, but Grandma said I was doing a ___3___ job.

Next, we added some flour, a little sugar, and one egg to the sweet potato paste. Grandma taught me how to ___4___ everything together with my hands. The mixture felt sticky at first, but after adding more flour, it became easier to handle. I shaped the mixture into small, ___5___ cakes and placed them on a plate.

Then came the most exciting part—frying. Grandma heated some oil in a pan and carefully put the cakes in one by one. She told me to stay ___6___ from the hot oil. I watched as the cakes slowly turned golden brown on each side. The ___7___ coming from the pan was wonderful. My mouth started watering.

After about ten minutes, all the cakes were done. Grandma put them on a paper towel to ___8___ the extra oil. I could not resist picking one up right away. It was crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. The taste was ___9___ sweet, just the way I liked it.

We shared the sweet potato cakes with the whole family that evening. Everyone said they were delicious. I felt very ___10___ of myself. Grandma patted my head and said, "You are a natural cook." That was one of the happiest afternoons I have ever had.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从没做过饭，既兴奋又紧张。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"伤心的"。excited and nervous 表示既兴奋又紧张。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：煮到足够软可以捣碎。A 项意为"硬的"；B 项意为"冷的"；C 项意为"软的"；D 项意为"干的"。soft enough to mash 表示软到可以捣碎。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶说我做得很好。A 项意为"很好的"；B 项意为"差的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"错误的"。doing a great job 意为"做得好"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶教我用手把所有材料混合在一起。A 项意为"切"；B 项意为"混合"；C 项意为"倒"；D 项意为"扔"。mix everything together 意为"混合所有材料"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把混合物做成圆形小饼。A 项意为"方形的"；B 项意为"圆形的"；C 项意为"长的"；D 项意为"高的"。饼通常是 round 的形状。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她让我远离热油。A 项 stay close 意为"靠近"；B 项 stay away 意为"远离"；C 项 stay next 搭配不当；D 项 stay near 意为"靠近"。stay away from 表示远离。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：锅里传出的香味太好了。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"声音"；C 项意为"气味"；D 项意为"光"。煎饼散发出的是 smell。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶把饼放在纸巾上去除多余的油。A 项意为"添加"；B 项意为"保留"；C 项意为"铺开"；D 项意为"去除"。remove the extra oil 意为"去除多余油脂"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道恰到好处地甜。A 项意为"非常（贬义）"；B 项意为"稍微"；C 项意为"几乎不"；D 项意为"完美地"。perfectly sweet 表示甜度恰好。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我为自己感到非常骄傲。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"生病的"。proud of myself 意为"为自己自豪"。故选 C。
