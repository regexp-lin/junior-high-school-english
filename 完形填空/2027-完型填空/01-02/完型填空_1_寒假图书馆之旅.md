# 中考英语完形填空：寒假图书馆之旅

## 完形填空原文

During the winter vacation, my mother suggested that I go to the city library. At first, I was not ___1___ because I thought the library would be boring. But when I walked through the front door, I changed my mind completely.

The library was much ___2___ than I had imagined. There were thousands of books on tall wooden shelves, and soft music was playing in the background. A friendly librarian welcomed me and asked what kind of books I ___3___. I told her I liked adventure stories. She led me to a section and recommended a book called "The Lost Island."

I found a comfortable seat by the window and started ___4___. The story was about a young boy who sailed across the ocean to find a mysterious island. The writing was so vivid that I felt like I was right there ___5___ him. I could almost hear the waves and smell the salty air. Before I knew it, two hours had ___6___.

When I looked up from the book, I noticed that the library was full of people—students, old men, and even small children. Everyone was reading ___7___, lost in their own worlds. It was amazing to see so many people who loved reading just like me.

Before leaving, I ___8___ the book and two more from the same series. The librarian stamped my library card and said, "Welcome back anytime." I smiled and promised I would ___9___ the next week.

On the way home, I thought about how wrong I had been. The library was not boring at all. It was a place full of ___10___ and knowledge. I decided to visit it every week during the vacation.

## 选项

1. A. interested &emsp;&emsp; B. surprised &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. frightened
2. A. smaller &emsp;&emsp; B. darker &emsp;&emsp; C. bigger &emsp;&emsp; D. noisier
3. A. wrote &emsp;&emsp; B. liked &emsp;&emsp; C. sold &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
4. A. writing &emsp;&emsp; B. drawing &emsp;&emsp; C. singing &emsp;&emsp; D. reading
5. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. against &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. behind
6. A. stopped &emsp;&emsp; B. passed &emsp;&emsp; C. arrived &emsp;&emsp; D. waited
7. A. loudly &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
8. A. returned &emsp;&emsp; B. borrowed &emsp;&emsp; C. bought &emsp;&emsp; D. copied
9. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. return &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
10. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. danger &emsp;&emsp; C. wonder &emsp;&emsp; D. noise

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始我对去图书馆没有兴趣。A 项意为"感兴趣的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。not interested 表示不感兴趣。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：图书馆比我想象的大得多。A 项意为"更小"；B 项意为"更暗"；C 项意为"更大"；D 项意为"更吵"。后文提到成千上万的书，说明比想象中 bigger。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：图书管理员问我喜欢什么类型的书。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"喜欢"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"丢失"。询问读者喜好用 liked。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我找到窗边舒适的座位开始阅读。A 项意为"写作"；B 项意为"画画"；C 项意为"唱歌"；D 项意为"阅读"。在图书馆借了书当然是 reading。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感觉好像就在他身边。A 项意为"和……一起"；B 项意为"反对"；C 项意为"没有"；D 项意为"在……后面"。right there with him 表示和主人公在一起。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不知不觉两个小时就过去了。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"过去/流逝"；C 项意为"到达"；D 项意为"等待"。时间 passed 表示流逝。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都在安静地阅读。A 项意为"大声地"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"安静地"；D 项意为"快速地"。图书馆里 quietly reading 合理。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：离开前我借了这本书和同系列的另外两本。A 项意为"归还"；B 项意为"借"；C 项意为"买"；D 项意为"抄写"。图书馆用 borrowed。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我微笑着承诺下周还会回来。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"返回"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"休息"。return 与 welcome back 呼应。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：图书馆是一个充满奇妙和知识的地方。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"危险"；C 项意为"奇妙/奇迹"；D 项意为"噪音"。full of wonder and knowledge 搭配合理。故选 C。
