# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校心理健康讲座

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday afternoon, our grade attended ___1___ school talk about mental health. The speaker was a young psychologist ___2___ shared simple ways to manage stress before exams. At first, some students whispered ___3___ they thought the topic sounded boring.

However, when she asked us ___4___ close our eyes and breathe slowly for one minute, the hall became quiet. She explained ___5___ we should talk to trusted adults when feelings grow heavy, instead of keeping everything inside. She also showed a short video ___6___ students described how sports and music helped them relax.

After the lecture, I realized ___7___ important it is to care for our minds as well as our grades. My friend Tom said he would try ___8___ journal every night to organize his thoughts. Our class teacher added that asking for help is ___9___ a weakness but a wise choice. On the way home, we agreed ___10___ we would use the tips during the busy month ahead.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
3. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. who  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. such  
8. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. keeping &emsp;&emsp; C. to keep &emsp;&emsp; D. kept  
9. A. no &emsp;&emsp; B. not &emsp;&emsp; C. never &emsp;&emsp; D. nor  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词表泛指（辅音音素开头）  
**【解析】** *school* 以辅音音素 /s/ 开头，泛指“一场关于心理健康的校讲座”，用 *a school talk*。*an* 用于元音音素开头；此处非特指某场已知的唯一讲座，一般不用 *the*；零冠词不符合“一场活动”的表达习惯。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词（指人作主语）  
**【解析】** 先行词 *psychologist* 指人，从句缺主语，用 *who*。*which* 指物；*what* 不引导定语从句；*whose* 表所属，后需接名词，此处不适用。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 从属连词表原因  
**【解析】** “一些同学窃窃私语”是因为“他们觉得话题无聊”，因果关系用 *because*。*although* 表让步；*unless* 表条件；*until* 表时间，语义不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*ask sb. to do*）  
**【解析】** *ask us to close our eyes* 为固定结构“要求某人做某事”，用不定式 *to close*。*for/of/with* 不能与 *ask sb.* 构成此搭配。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词（表原因/理由）  
**【解析】** 句意为解释“我们为什么要在情绪沉重时与信任的成年人交谈”，*why* 引导宾语从句说明原因。*what* 在从句中需充当成分，此处从句结构完整；*whether* 表是否；*how* 表方式，不如 *why* 贴切。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系副词（抽象地点）  
**【解析】** 先行词 *video*，从句 *students described...* 表示“在视频里学生们描述……”，用关系副词 *where*（相当于 *in which*）。*which* 作主语/宾语，从句不缺主宾；*when* 表时间；*who* 指人，先行词为物。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 感叹句结构 *how + 形容词*  
**【解析】** *how important it is* 为感叹句，*how* 修饰形容词 *important*。*what* 后应接名词短语（如 *what an important thing*）；*so/such* 不用于此感叹结构。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*try to do* 表“努力做”）  
**【解析】** *try to keep a journal* 表示“努力坚持写日记”。*try doing* 表“试着做（看看效果）”，此处语境更强调“打算努力去做”，中考常考 *try to do* 与 *try doing* 辨析，结合“养成习惯”用 *to keep* 更自然。*keep/keeping/kept* 不能直接接在 *try* 后作宾语。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 否定副词 *not...but...*  
**【解析】** *not a weakness but a wise choice* 为“不是……而是……”，在 *is* 后用 *not*。*no* 后多接名词但结构常为 *no weakness*，不如 *not* 与 *but* 并列自然；*never/nor* 不符合该并列对比结构。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 *that*（陈述事实，无是否含义）  
**【解析】** *agreed that...* 后接陈述内容的从句，用 *that* 引导（可省略，但选项中需选 *that*）。*what* 在从句中需充当成分；*if/whether* 表“是否”，从句内容为确定的事实，不适用。故选 B。
