# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工热缩片挂件

## 完形填空原文

During the winter craft club, Ms. Lin taught us ___1___ make shrink-plastic charms. She handed out sheets ___2___ looked like thin plastic and told us to draw small pictures with colored pencils.

___3___ I drew a tiny penguin, my classmate Lily chose a star. Ms. Lin warned us ___4___ the lines should be thick enough, or the colors might disappear after heating. We cut out the shapes carefully and punched holes ___5___ string could go through later.

When the oven was ready, we watched the plastic curl and then flatten ___6___ it cooled down. The pieces became harder and ___7___ than before, which felt like magic. I tied a ribbon through the hole and attached the charm ___8___ my pencil case.

Lily’s star turned ___9___ a little crooked, but she laughed and said it was unique. Before we left, Ms. Lin reminded us ___10___ put the tools back in the box. I decided to make another charm for my cousin next week.

## 选项

1. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to  
2. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
3. A. While &emsp;&emsp; B. Unless &emsp;&emsp; C. Until &emsp;&emsp; D. Although  
4. A. this &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  
5. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. as if &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as  
6. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since  
7. A. small &emsp;&emsp; B. smaller &emsp;&emsp; C. smallest &emsp;&emsp; D. more smaller  
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
9. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. off  
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** *teach us how to make...* 表示“教我们如何制作”。*what to make* 虽语法可行，但后文已有宾语 *shrink-plastic charms*，用 *how to make* 更贴切“教制作方法”。*when/where to make* 与语境重点不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词（指物作主语）  
**【解析】** 先行词 *sheets* 为物，从句缺主语，用 *which*。*who/whom* 指人；*whose* 表所属。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 从属连词 *while* 表“当……时/与此同时”  
**【解析】** 描述两人同时做不同事，用 *While I drew..., Lily chose...*。*Unless* 表条件；*Until* 表直到；*Although* 表让步，均不符合并列对比的时间关系。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 *that*（陈述内容）  
**【解析】** *warned us that the lines should be thick enough*，*that* 引导宾语从句。*this* 不能引导从句；*what* 需在从句中充当成分；*whether* 表是否，此处为确定提醒内容。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 *so that*  
**【解析】** 打孔的目的是“以便线能穿过”，表目的用 *so that*。*even though* 让步；*as if* 好像；*as long as* 只要，逻辑不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 *when*  
**【解析】** “当它冷却下来时变平”，*when it cooled down*。*before* 与“先卷曲再变平”的过程若用 *before* 易歧义；*while* 多与进行时连用表“在……期间”；*since* 表自从/因为，此处不如 *when* 自然。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（*than* 提示）  
**【解析】** *harder and smaller than before*，与 *than* 搭配用比较级 *smaller*。*more smaller* 为错误双重比较；原级/最高级不匹配 *than*。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *attach...to...*  
**【解析】** *attach the charm to my pencil case* 表示“把挂件系在笔袋上”。*in/on/at* 不与 *attach* 构成此固定搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 短语动词 *turn out*  
**【解析】** *turn out a little crooked* 表示“结果是/看起来有点歪”。*turn into* 变成；*turn up* 出现/调大；*turn off* 关掉，语义不符。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*remind sb. to do*）  
**【解析】** *remind us to put the tools back* 为“提醒某人去做”，用不定式 *to put*。*for/of/with* 不能与 *remind sb.* 构成标准搭配。故选 A。
