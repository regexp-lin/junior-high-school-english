# 中考英语完形填空：校园植物角

## 完形填空原文

Our school decided to set up a small plant corner in every classroom this term. Each class was given several potted plants to ___1___ care of. Our class received two green plants and a pot of sunflowers.

The teacher put me and Li Na ___2___ charge of watering the plants. We made a schedule — I watered them on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, ___3___ Li Na did it on Tuesday and Thursday.

At first, the sunflower looked a little weak. Some leaves were turning ___4___ and falling off. Li Na was worried and searched online for ___5___. She found out that sunflowers need plenty of sunlight, so we moved the pot to the windowsill ___6___ it could get more light.

After a week, the sunflower began to grow taller. New leaves appeared, and the stem became ___7___ than before. Everyone in the class was excited to see ___8___ change. Even the students who sat far from the window would come over to check on it.

By the end of the month, the sunflower ___9___ beautifully. Its bright yellow petals made our classroom look much ___10___. Taking care of plants taught us patience and responsibility.

## 选项

1. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. make &emsp;&emsp; C. have &emsp;&emsp; D. take
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
3. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
4. A. green &emsp;&emsp; B. red &emsp;&emsp; C. yellow &emsp;&emsp; D. blue
5. A. advice &emsp;&emsp; B. advise &emsp;&emsp; C. advices &emsp;&emsp; D. advised
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. because
7. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. stronger &emsp;&emsp; C. strongest &emsp;&emsp; D. strongly
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
9. A. bloom &emsp;&emsp; B. blooms &emsp;&emsp; C. blooming &emsp;&emsp; D. bloomed
10. A. live &emsp;&emsp; B. lively &emsp;&emsp; C. alive &emsp;&emsp; D. living

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个班分到几盆植物来照顾。take care of 表示"照顾"。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我和李娜负责浇水。put sb. in charge of 表示"让某人负责"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在周一、三、五浇水，而李娜在周二和周四浇。while 表示对比，"而"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 常识与语境
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些叶子在变黄并掉落。植物生病时叶子变黄，用 yellow。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 词性辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：李娜很担心，在网上搜索建议。for 后接名词，advice 为不可数名词，表示"建议"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系副词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把花盆移到窗台上，那里能得到更多光照。先行词为 the windowsill（地点），在从句中作地点状语，用 where。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：茎比之前更粗壮了。than 提示用比较级，strong 的比较级为 stronger。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 冠词（特指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：全班同学都兴奋地看到了这个变化。特指向日葵的变化，用定冠词 the。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：到月底，向日葵开出了美丽的花。叙述过去发生的事，用一般过去时 bloomed。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：明亮的黄色花瓣让教室看起来更有生气。look 为系动词，后接形容词；lively 表示"生动的、有活力的"，可作表语。故选 B。
