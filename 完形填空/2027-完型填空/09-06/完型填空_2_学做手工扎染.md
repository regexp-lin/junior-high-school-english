# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工扎染

## 完形填空原文

During the weekend, our community center offered a free tie-dye workshop. I had never tried tie-dye before and was ___1___ to learn something new.

The instructor gave each of us a white cotton T-shirt and several bottles of ___2___ dye. She told us to fold, twist, or tie the fabric in different ways ___3___ unique patterns.

I decided to make a spiral pattern. First, I pinched the center of the shirt and ___4___ it tightly. Then I wrapped rubber bands around it. "The tighter you wrap, the ___5___ the white lines will be," the instructor explained.

Next, we applied dye to different sections. I chose blue and green because they ___6___ me of the ocean. The colors mixed beautifully ___7___ the edges, creating a gradient effect.

After dyeing, we wrapped our shirts in plastic bags and had to wait ___8___ twenty-four hours. This was the ___9___ part because I was so curious about the result. The next day, I unwrapped my shirt, rinsed it under cold water, and spread it out to dry.

The pattern was even more beautiful ___10___ I had imagined! I wore it to school the following Monday, and many classmates asked where I bought it.

## 选项

1. A. excite &emsp;&emsp; B. exciting &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. excitement
2. A. color &emsp;&emsp; B. colors &emsp;&emsp; C. colorful &emsp;&emsp; D. colorfully
3. A. create &emsp;&emsp; B. created &emsp;&emsp; C. creating &emsp;&emsp; D. to create
4. A. twist &emsp;&emsp; B. twisted &emsp;&emsp; C. twisting &emsp;&emsp; D. to twist
5. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. clearer &emsp;&emsp; C. clearest &emsp;&emsp; D. clearly
6. A. remind &emsp;&emsp; B. reminded &emsp;&emsp; C. reminding &emsp;&emsp; D. reminds
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. hardest &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
10. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. then

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（-ed 与 -ing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很兴奋去学习新东西。be excited to do sth. 表示"对做某事感到兴奋"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师给我们每人一件白色纯棉 T 恤和几瓶彩色染料。colorful 为形容词，修饰名词 dye。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她让我们用不同的方式折叠、扭曲或系紧布料，以创造出独特的图案。to create 表示目的。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：我捏住衬衫中心并把它紧紧拧起来。pinched 和 twisted 为并列过去时动作。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（the + 比较级, the + 比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：你包得越紧，白线就越清晰。"the + 比较级, the + 比较级"结构表示"越……越……"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了蓝色和绿色，因为它们让我想起了大海。叙述过去的事，用一般过去时 reminded。remind sb. of sth. 表示"使某人想起"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：颜色在边缘处漂亮地融合。at the edges 表示"在边缘处"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们必须等二十四个小时。for + 时间段表示等待的持续时间。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是最难的部分。the + 最高级，hard 的最高级为 hardest。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 比较结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：图案比我想象的还要漂亮！more beautiful 为比较级，后接 than。故选 C。
