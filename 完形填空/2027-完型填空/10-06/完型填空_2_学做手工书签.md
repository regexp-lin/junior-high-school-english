# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工书签

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, our class held a creative workshop. The teacher asked each student ___1___ a handmade bookmark for the school library.

I chose a piece of thick blue paper and began ___2___ a pattern on it. My idea was to draw a small tree with colorful leaves. However, the tree I drew didn't look as ___3___ as I expected. My friend Lily noticed my trouble and offered to help.

"Why ___4___ you try cutting leaf shapes from different colored paper?" she suggested. I thought her idea was ___5___ than mine, so I followed her advice immediately.

We worked together for about forty minutes. Lily was good ___6___ cutting neat shapes, while I was responsible for gluing them onto the bookmark. ___7___ we finished, the bookmark looked like a real autumn scene.

The teacher praised our teamwork and said, "This is ___8___ beautiful bookmark I have seen today." She put it on display at the front desk of the library. I felt very proud ___9___ I saw other students admiring it.

This experience taught ___10___ that working with friends can turn a difficult task into a joyful one.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. draw &emsp;&emsp; B. drew &emsp;&emsp; C. drawing &emsp;&emsp; D. to draw  
3. A. nice &emsp;&emsp; B. nicely &emsp;&emsp; C. nicer &emsp;&emsp; D. nicest  
4. A. not &emsp;&emsp; B. don't &emsp;&emsp; C. won't &emsp;&emsp; D. aren't  
5. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
6. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
7. A. When &emsp;&emsp; B. Before &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. Although  
8. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. the more &emsp;&emsp; D. the most  
9. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
10. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. me &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do）  
**【解析】** ask sb. to do sth. 意为"要求某人做某事"，不定式作宾补。make 为原形；making 为现在分词；made 为过去式，均不能直接用于 ask sb. 后。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（begin doing）  
**【解析】** begin doing sth. 意为"开始做某事"，后接动名词。draw 为原形；drew 为过去式；to draw 虽也可接在 begin 后，但此处强调"开始画的过程"，用 drawing 更自然。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** as... as 同级比较  
**【解析】** as + 形容词原级 + as 构成同级比较，意为"和……一样……"。look 为系动词后接形容词，nice 为原级。nicely 为副词；nicer 为比较级；nicest 为最高级，均不用于 as...as 结构。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 特殊疑问句中的否定（Why don't you...?）  
**【解析】** "Why don't you + 动词原形？"为固定句型，用于提建议，意为"你为什么不……？"。not 不能单独用于主语前；won't 表将来时否定；aren't 用于 be 动词否定句。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 句中有 than，需用比较级形式。good 的比较级为 better。good 为原级；well 作形容词表"身体好"；best 为最高级，均不与 than 搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be good at  
**【解析】** be good at (doing) sth. 意为"擅长做某事"，at 为固定介词。be good for 意为"对……有益"；be good in 不常见；be good with 意为"善于与……打交道"，不符语境。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词  
**【解析】** 完成后书签看起来像真正的秋景，表示"当……时"，用 when 引导时间状语从句。before 表"在……之前"；unless 表条件"除非"；although 表让步"虽然"，均不符合语义。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** 句意为"这是我今天见过的最美丽的书签"，需用最高级 the most beautiful。more 为比较级标志；most 缺冠词 the；the more 为比较级，均不正确。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "当我看到其他同学欣赏它时感到很自豪"，用 when 表"当……时候"。before 表"在……之前"；until 表"直到"；unless 表"除非"，均不符合语境。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** teach 为及物动词，后接宾语须用宾格 me。I 为主格；my 为形容词性物主代词；mine 为名词性物主代词，不能作 teach 的直接宾语。故选 C。
