# 中考英语完形填空：假期去动物园

## 完形填空原文

During the National Day holiday, my cousin invited ___1___ to visit the city zoo together. I was so excited that I could hardly fall ___2___ the night before.

We arrived at the zoo early in the morning. The first animals we saw were the pandas. They were eating bamboo ___3___ and looked very cute. My cousin took several photos ___4___ his new phone. I asked him to send the best one to me later.

Then we walked to the monkey area. A baby monkey was hanging from a tree and ___5___ funny faces at visitors. Everyone around laughed happily. I had never seen ___6___ a clever little animal before.

At noon, we sat under a big tree and shared the lunch that my aunt ___7___ for us. She made sandwiches and fruit salad. Everything tasted ___8___.

In the afternoon, we watched a dolphin show. The dolphins jumped ___9___ of the water and did amazing tricks. The whole audience cheered loudly. My cousin said it was the ___10___ wonderful show he had ever seen. I totally agreed.

## 选项

1. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. me &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
2. A. asleep &emsp;&emsp; B. sleep &emsp;&emsp; C. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; D. slept  
3. A. quiet &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. noisy &emsp;&emsp; D. noisily  
4. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
5. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. to make  
6. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. such &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too  
7. A. prepares &emsp;&emsp; B. prepared &emsp;&emsp; C. has prepared &emsp;&emsp; D. had prepared  
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. delicious &emsp;&emsp; D. deliciously  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. up  
10. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** invite 为及物动词，后接宾语应用人称代词宾格 me。I 为主格；my 为形容词性物主代词；mine 为名词性物主代词，均不能作 invite 的宾语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 fall asleep  
**【解析】** fall asleep 为固定搭配，意为"入睡"，asleep 为形容词作系动词 fall 的表语。sleep 为动词/名词；sleeping 为现在分词；slept 为过去式，均不能构成此搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 eating 应用副词。quietly（安静地）符合大熊猫安静吃竹子的场景。quiet 为形容词；noisy/noisily 意为"吵闹"，不符合语境。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（工具）  
**【解析】** 表示"用某种工具"用介词 with。by 后常接交通方式或被动语态的施事者；in 表示在……里面；on 表示在……上面，均不如 with 准确表达"用手机拍照"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作伴随状语  
**【解析】** 主语 monkey 与 make 之间为主动关系，and 连接两个并列的动作，was hanging 和 making 均为进行时态的并列谓语。make 为原形；made 为过去式；to make 为不定式，均与 was hanging 不平行。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** such 与 so 的用法区别  
**【解析】** such + a/an + adj. + n. 为固定结构，"such a clever little animal"。so 后直接加形容词/副词（so clever an animal 也可但不常见于口语）；very/too 不与冠词搭配构成此感叹结构。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** 阿姨准备午餐的动作发生在"我们吃午餐"之前，即"过去的过去"，需用过去完成时 had prepared。prepares 为一般现在时；prepared 为一般过去时；has prepared 为现在完成时，均不能体现两个过去动作的先后关系。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 系动词后接形容词  
**【解析】** taste 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。delicious（美味的）最符合描述食物味道的语境。good 虽语法正确但 delicious 更具体；well 作形容词时仅表"身体好"；deliciously 为副词，不能作表语。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 jump out of  
**【解析】** jump out of the water 意为"跳出水面"，out of 表示"从……出来"。in 表示在里面；off 表示离开表面；up 不与 of 搭配表此义。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** "the + 最高级 + n. + sb. have/has ever seen" 为固定最高级句型。wonderful 为多音节词，最高级用 most wonderful。more 为比较级标志；much 修饰比较级；very 不用于该句型。故选 C。
