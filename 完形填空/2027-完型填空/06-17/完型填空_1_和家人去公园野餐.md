# 中考英语完形填空：和家人去公园野餐

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday was Father's Day. Our family went to the park for ___1___ picnic. The weather was sunny and warm — perfect for an outdoor meal.

Mother prepared sandwiches, fruit and juice the night ___2___. Father carried the big basket while my sister and I brought the blanket. We found a ___3___ spot under a tall tree.

After we spread out the blanket, I helped Mother set ___4___ the food. Everything looked and smelled ___5___. Father said the food tasted even better outdoors than at home.

After eating, Father taught us ___6___ to fly a kite. The wind was just right. My sister ran with the kite string and it flew ___7___ into the sky. We took turns holding it.

Later, we ___8___ by the lake and watched the ducks swimming. It was so peaceful that I almost fell ___9___. Father said he really enjoyed this simple day with us. I think the best gift for him was our ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. ago &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. later &emsp;&emsp; D. after
3. A. shade &emsp;&emsp; B. shady &emsp;&emsp; C. shadow &emsp;&emsp; D. shading
4. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. down &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. off
5. A. well &emsp;&emsp; B. good &emsp;&emsp; C. great &emsp;&emsp; D. greatly
6. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. how
7. A. high &emsp;&emsp; B. highly &emsp;&emsp; C. higher &emsp;&emsp; D. highest
8. A. sit &emsp;&emsp; B. sits &emsp;&emsp; C. sat &emsp;&emsp; D. sitting
9. A. sleep &emsp;&emsp; B. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; C. slept &emsp;&emsp; D. asleep
10. A. company &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. gift &emsp;&emsp; D. food

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一家去公园野餐。picnic 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（the night before 前一晚）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈前一天晚上准备了三明治、水果和果汁。"the night before"表示"前一天晚上"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在一棵大树下找到了一个阴凉的地方。shady 为形容词"阴凉的"，修饰名词 spot。shade 为名词；shadow 为影子。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语（set up 摆放/布置）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我帮妈妈摆放食物。"set up"表示"摆放、布置"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 系动词 + 形容词
**【解析】** 本句意为：一切看起来闻起来都很棒。looked 和 smelled 为系动词，后接形容词 great。greatly 为副词。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式（how to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：饭后爸爸教我们怎样放风筝。"how to do sth."表示"如何做某事"。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（high 高高地）
**【解析】** 本句意为：风筝高高地飞入天空。high 作副词表示"高高地"。highly 表示"高度地、非常"，用于抽象含义。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：后来我们坐在湖边看鸭子游泳。上下文为过去时态，用 sat。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（fall asleep 入睡）
**【解析】** 本句意为：太安静了以至于我差点睡着。"fall asleep"表示"入睡"，asleep 为表语形容词。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（company 陪伴）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得给他最好的礼物就是我们的陪伴。company 表示"陪伴"。故选 A。
