# 中考英语完形填空：学做三明治

## 完形填空原文

Making sandwiches is one of the ___1___ things to learn in the kitchen. My older brother taught me his special recipe last weekend.

We needed bread, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese and ham. Brother told me to wash the vegetables ___2___ and dry them with a clean towel. Then I sliced the tomatoes as ___3___ as possible.

First, he spread butter ___4___ two pieces of bread. "The butter keeps the bread from getting wet," he explained. Then we put a piece of lettuce, two slices of tomato and a piece of ham ___5___ the bread.

The cheese was ___6___ on top of the ham. My brother pressed the sandwich down gently and cut it into two ___7___. He said cutting it diagonally made it look more professional.

I took a big bite and the flavors mixed ___8___ in my mouth. It tasted ___9___ better than the ones from the convenience store. I was surprised that something so simple could be so delicious.

Now I make sandwiches for ___10___ almost every morning. It saves time and money.

## 选项

1. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. easiest &emsp;&emsp; D. easily
2. A. thorough &emsp;&emsp; B. thoroughly &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. though
3. A. thin &emsp;&emsp; B. thinly &emsp;&emsp; C. thinner &emsp;&emsp; D. thinnest
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. to
5. A. between &emsp;&emsp; B. among &emsp;&emsp; C. under &emsp;&emsp; D. above
6. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. placing &emsp;&emsp; C. placed &emsp;&emsp; D. places
7. A. half &emsp;&emsp; B. halves &emsp;&emsp; C. piece &emsp;&emsp; D. parts
8. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. perfecting &emsp;&emsp; C. perfected &emsp;&emsp; D. perfectly
9. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. more
10. A. me &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. myself &emsp;&emsp; D. mine

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（one of the + 最高级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：做三明治是厨房里最简单的事情之一。"one of the + 最高级"结构，用 easiest。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：哥哥让我把蔬菜彻底洗干净。修饰动词 wash 用副词 thoroughly。thorough 为形容词；through 为介词。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词（as + adv.原级 + as possible）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尽可能薄地切西红柿。"as thinly as possible"中 thinly 为副词修饰动词 sliced。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（on 在……上面）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他在两片面包上涂黄油。on 表示"在……表面上"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（between 在两者之间）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把生菜、番茄片和火腿放在两片面包之间。between 表示"在两片面包之间"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 被动语态（was placed 被放置）
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶酪被放在火腿上面。cheese 是被放置的，用被动语态 was placed。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词复数（halves 是 half 的复数）
**【解析】** 本句意为：哥哥轻轻按下三明治并切成两半。two halves 为"两半"。halves 是 half 的复数形式。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：各种味道在嘴里完美地融合。修饰动词 mixed 用副词 perfectly。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰比较级（much + 比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：比便利店的好吃得多。much 修饰比较级 better。very/too 不修饰比较级。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 反身代词（for myself 为自己）
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我几乎每天早上为自己做三明治。for myself 表示"为我自己"。故选 C。
