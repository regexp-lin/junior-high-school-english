# 中考英语完形填空：白色情人节班级活动

## 完形填空原文

On March 14th, our class planned a simple "White Day" activity ___1___ show kindness to each other. It was not about expensive gifts, ___2___ about warm words and small surprises.

Our monitor brought white paper cards and colored pens. We wrote thank-you notes ___3___ classmates who had helped us during the term. I wrote one ___4___ Leo because he always shared his notes with me.

Some students folded paper stars ___5___ put them into a glass jar for the teachers. The classroom felt busy ___6___ happy, like a small festival.

When the teacher walked in, we shouted "Surprise!" She smiled and said, "Kindness is a language ___7___ everyone understands."

That afternoon, I walked home ___8___ a light heart. I learned that care does not need a ___9___ price tag; a sincere sentence can be ___10___ enough.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
4. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. of
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. yet
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. that
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. but
9. A. low &emsp;&emsp; B. small &emsp;&emsp; C. high &emsp;&emsp; D. little
10. A. rich &emsp;&emsp; B. poor &emsp;&emsp; C. heavy &emsp;&emsp; D. loud

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/不定式表目的
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班计划了一次简单的“白色情人节”活动，以向彼此表达善意。不定式 to show 表目的。for show 不成立；of/with 不符合结构。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构 not...but...
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的不是昂贵礼物，而是温暖的话语和小惊喜。not...but... 表示“不是……而是……”。and/or/so 不构成该结构。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们给本学期帮助过我们的同学写感谢卡。write notes to classmates 表示“给同学写（便条）”。for/at/on 搭配不如 to 自然。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我给 Leo 写了一张（感谢卡）。write a note/card to sb. 表示“给某人写便条/卡片”。at/for/of 搭配不如 to 自然准确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些同学折纸星星，并把它们放进玻璃罐送给老师。folded 与 put 为顺承并列，用 and。or/but/so 不符合语境。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：教室既忙碌又快乐。busy 与 happy 为并列形容词，用 and。or/yet 表转折或选择；but 表转折，与“像节日一样”氛围不符。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：善意是一种人人都能理解的语言。先行词 language 指物，关系词在从句中作 understands 的宾语，用 that/which。此处选项中 that 正确。what 不引导定语从句；who 指人。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我心情轻松地走回家。with a light heart 表示“怀着轻松的心情”，为固定表达。and/or/but 无法连接 walked home 与 a light heart。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关心并不需要“高昂”的价格标签。a high price tag 表示“高昂价签”，与 expensive gifts 呼应。low/small/little 与“昂贵”语境相反或搭配弱。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一句真诚的话就足够“丰富/厚重”（很有分量）。rich enough 可表示“内涵丰富；足够珍贵”。poor/heavy/loud 与 sincere sentence 的情感色彩不符。故选 A。
