# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工圣诞老人贴画

## 完形填空原文

During the English week, we made Santa paper collages ___1___ recycled magazines. Ms. Yang brought scissors, glue, and red paper ___2___ the classroom and said we should work in pairs.

I cut out a round face ___3___ a white beard, while my partner Tom chose a bright hat. We needed ___4___ time because the pieces were small, ___5___ we did not feel bored. Ms. Yang walked around and praised students ___6___ tried creative ideas.

When we finished, we put our Santas ___7___ the wall near the door. They looked funny ___8___ each one had a different smile. Ms. Yang took pictures ___9___ our parents could see our work online. She also told us ___10___ the scraps into the recycling bin.

## 选项

1. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. used &emsp;&emsp; C. using &emsp;&emsp; D. to use  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. a lot &emsp;&emsp; D. lot of  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
8. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
9. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until  
10. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. putting &emsp;&emsp; C. to put &emsp;&emsp; D. puts  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（现在分词表伴随）  
**【解析】** *made Santa paper collages using recycled magazines* 用现在分词 *using* 表方式/伴随，相当于 *by using*。*use/used/to use* 不能直接作伴随状语而不加连接词。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *into* 表“进入”  
**【解析】** *brought... into the classroom* “把……带进教室”。*bring into* 强调带入内部。*in/on/at* 不如 *into* 与 *brought* 搭配自然。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *and*  
**【解析】** “圆脸”和“白胡子”为并列关系，用 *and*。*but/or/so* 语义不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词 *much* 修饰不可数名词  
**【解析】** *time* 不可数，用 *much time*。*many* 修饰可数复数；*a lot* 需加 *of*；*lot of* 表达不完整（应为 *a lot of/lots of*）。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “需要很多时间”与“并不觉得无聊”为轻微转折/对比，用 *but* 更自然。*and* 顺承；*so* 结果；*or* 选择。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词（指人）  
**【解析】** *students who tried creative ideas* 先行词 *students* 指人，从句缺主语用 *who*。*which* 指物；*what* 不引导定语从句；*whose* 表所属。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *on the wall*  
**【解析】** 贴画挂在墙表面，用 *on the wall*。*in* 在内部；*at/by* 搭配不当。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句（*because*）  
**【解析】** “看起来有趣”是因为“每个笑容不同”，表原因用 *because*。*if/unless* 条件；*although* 让步。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句（*so that*）  
**【解析】** 拍照是为了“以便家长在线看到作品”，*so that our parents could see*。*unless/although/until* 不符。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*tell sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *told us to put the scraps...* “告诉我们把碎纸放进回收箱”。*tell sb. to do* 固定。*put/putting/puts* 不能直接作宾补。故选 C。
