# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校英语话剧表演

## 完形填空原文

Last month our class chose an English play ___1___ the school art festival. I was nervous ___2___ I had never spoken English on a real stage before. Our English teacher, Mr. Lin, told us ___3___ worry too much and said teamwork would help.

We practiced ___4___ school for three weeks. At first I forgot my lines easily, ___5___ my classmates never laughed at me. They repeated the dialogue slowly ___6___ I could follow. On the day of the show, my hands were cold ___7___ my voice was clear when I spoke.

The audience clapped loudly ___8___ the play ended. I realized ___9___ brave moments often begin with small steps. Mr. Lin took a photo ___10___ we could remember this special afternoon forever.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
3. A. not &emsp;&emsp; B. not to &emsp;&emsp; C. don't &emsp;&emsp; D. didn't  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. after  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
8. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
10. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. if  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *for* 表目的/对象  
**【解析】** *chose an English play for the school art festival* 表示“为学校艺术节选了一部英语话剧”。*for* 引出活动对象。*to* 表方向或不定式符号，此处不接动词原形；*of/with* 搭配不当。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句（*because*）  
**【解析】** “紧张”是因为“从未在舞台上说过英语”，因果关系用 *because*。*but* 转折；*although* 让步；*unless* 条件。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*tell sb. not to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *told us not to worry* 为“告诉某人不要做某事”，否定不定式用 *not to do*。*not worry* 结构不完整；*don't/didn't* 不能直接作 *tell us* 的补足语。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *at school*  
**【解析】** *practiced at school* “在学校练习”。固定地点搭配用 *at school*。*in/on/after* 不如 *at* 常见准确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “我容易忘词”与“同学从不嘲笑我”为转折，用 *but*。*and/so/or* 语义不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*until*）  
**【解析】** *repeated... until I could follow* 表示反复到我能够跟上。*if/because/unless* 逻辑不对。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “手冷”与“声音清晰”形成对比转折，用 *but*。*and* 表并列；*or* 选择；*so* 结果。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*when*）  
**【解析】** *clapped loudly when the play ended* “话剧结束时观众热烈鼓掌”。*before* 在之前；*until* 直到；*unless* 除非。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 *that*  
**【解析】** *realized that brave moments...* *that* 引导宾语从句陈述事实。*what* 需在从句中充当成分；*whether* 是否；*how* 方式，此处从句结构完整。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句（*so that*）  
**【解析】** 拍照是为了“以便永远记住这个下午”，*so that we could remember*。*unless/although/if* 语义不符。故选 A。
