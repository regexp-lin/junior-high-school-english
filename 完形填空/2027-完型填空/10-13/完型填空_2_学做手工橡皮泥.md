# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工橡皮泥

## 完形填空原文

Have you ever made your own clay at home? Last weekend, my little brother and I tried making homemade clay, and it turned out to be a lot of ___1___.

We found a recipe online. The ingredients were simple: flour, salt, water, and food coloring. My brother was so ___2___ that he wanted to start right away. I told him to be patient and follow the steps one ___3___ one.

First, we mixed the flour and salt together in a big bowl. Then we ___4___ added warm water while stirring the mixture. This part required teamwork — I poured the water, and my brother stirred ___5___ a wooden spoon.

When the dough became smooth, we divided it into four parts and added different food coloring to ___6___ part. We kneaded the dough until the color was spread ___7___. Our hands became very colorful, which made us laugh.

Then came the creative part. I made a small cat, ___8___ my brother made a dinosaur. His dinosaur looked a bit strange, but he was very proud of it. He said it was the ___9___ dinosaur in the world.

We put our clay works on the windowsill to dry. Mom came home and was ___10___ to see what we had created. She took photos and sent them to our grandparents. It was truly a wonderful afternoon.

## 选项

1. A. fun &emsp;&emsp; B. funny &emsp;&emsp; C. funs &emsp;&emsp; D. funnily  
2. A. excite &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. exciting &emsp;&emsp; D. excitement  
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
4. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slower &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowest  
5. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
6. A. each &emsp;&emsp; B. every &emsp;&emsp; C. all &emsp;&emsp; D. both  
7. A. even &emsp;&emsp; B. evenly &emsp;&emsp; C. more even &emsp;&emsp; D. most even  
8. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. while  
9. A. cool &emsp;&emsp; B. cooler &emsp;&emsp; C. coolest &emsp;&emsp; D. coolly  
10. A. surprise &emsp;&emsp; B. surprised &emsp;&emsp; C. surprising &emsp;&emsp; D. surprisingly  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 a lot of fun  
**【解析】** a lot of fun 意为"很多乐趣"，fun 为不可数名词。funny 为形容词"滑稽的"；funs 不存在（fun 不可数）；funnily 为副词。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）  
**【解析】** was 为系动词，后接描述人感受的形容词。excited（兴奋的）修饰人。excite 为动词；exciting 意为"令人兴奋的"修饰事物；excitement 为名词。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 one by one  
**【解析】** one by one 意为"一个接一个地"，为固定搭配。one to one 意为"一对一"；one in one/one on one 不为常见固定搭配。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 added 需用副词 slowly（慢慢地）。slow 为形容词；slower 为比较级形容词；slowest 为最高级形容词。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表工具  
**【解析】** stir with a wooden spoon 意为"用木勺搅拌"，with 表使用工具。for 表目的；by 表方式后多接动名词；in 表在……里。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 each  
**【解析】** each part 意为"每一部分"，each 后接单数名词。every 也接单数但前面有 to 构成 "to each part" 更自然；all 接复数或不可数名词；both 指两者。此处分为四部分，用 each 最恰当。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 was spread 需用副词 evenly（均匀地）。even 为形容词/副词（但作"均匀"义时用 evenly）；more even/most even 为比较级/最高级形式，此处无比较。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 连词 while 表对比  
**【解析】** "我做了猫，而弟弟做了恐龙"，while 表对比两个同时发生或对照的动作。so 表因果；or 表选择；but 表转折（此处无明显转折，而是对比并列）。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** "the + 最高级 + n. + in the world" 为最高级固定句型。cool 的最高级为 coolest。cool 为原级；cooler 为比较级；coolly 为副词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）  
**【解析】** was surprised 意为"感到惊喜"，surprised 修饰人的感受。surprise 为名词/动词；surprising 意为"令人惊讶的"修饰事物；surprisingly 为副词。故选 B。
