# 中考英语完形填空：假期晨跑

## 完形填空原文

Before the holiday started, I made a promise to ___1___ that I would go jogging every morning. I wanted to become healthier and prepare for the school sports meeting.

On the first morning, my alarm rang at six o'clock. It was still dark outside, and the bed felt so ___2___ that I almost changed my mind. But I told myself, "A promise is a promise," and got up ___3___.

The air in the park was fresh and cool. A few elderly people were doing tai chi on the grass. I started running slowly along the path. After about ten minutes, I was already out of ___4___. I realized how weak my body had become after months of sitting at a desk.

I didn't run very ___5___ on the first day — only two kilometers. But I kept going every morning without ___6___ a single day. By the end of the first week, I could run three kilometers ___7___.

The biggest change was not just physical. I found that morning exercise made me feel more ___8___ throughout the day. My mind was clearer, and I could concentrate ___9___ in class.

My father noticed the change and said he was proud of my persistence. He decided ___10___ me from then on. Now morning jogging has become a precious habit for both of us.

## 选项

1. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. myself  
2. A. comfort &emsp;&emsp; B. comfortable &emsp;&emsp; C. comfortably &emsp;&emsp; D. comforted  
3. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. quicker &emsp;&emsp; D. quickest  
4. A. breathe &emsp;&emsp; B. breathing &emsp;&emsp; C. breath &emsp;&emsp; D. breathed  
5. A. far &emsp;&emsp; B. long &emsp;&emsp; C. near &emsp;&emsp; D. short  
6. A. miss &emsp;&emsp; B. missed &emsp;&emsp; C. missing &emsp;&emsp; D. to miss  
7. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easily &emsp;&emsp; C. easier &emsp;&emsp; D. easiest  
8. A. energy &emsp;&emsp; B. energetic &emsp;&emsp; C. energetically &emsp;&emsp; D. energize  
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
10. A. join &emsp;&emsp; B. joined &emsp;&emsp; C. joining &emsp;&emsp; D. to join  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 反身代词  
**【解析】** make a promise to oneself 意为"对自己许下承诺"，主语和宾语为同一人，用反身代词 myself。I 为主格；me 为宾格（但此处强调"自己"）；my 为物主代词。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 系动词 feel + 形容词  
**【解析】** feel 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。comfortable（舒适的）修饰 bed 的感觉。comfort 为名词/动词；comfortably 为副词；comforted 意为"被安慰的"，不修饰 bed。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 got up 需用副词 quickly（快速地）。quick 为形容词；quicker/quickest 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 out of breath  
**【解析】** out of breath 意为"上气不接下气"，breath 为名词。breathe 为动词；breathing 为动名词/现在分词；breathed 为过去式。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词辨析  
**【解析】** run far 意为"跑很远"，far 修饰 run 表距离。long 多修饰时间；near 意为"近"，与下文"只有两公里"语义不一致；short 不修饰 run 表距离。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 without + 动名词  
**【解析】** without missing 意为"没有错过"，without 为介词后接动名词。miss 为原形；missed 为过去式；to miss 为不定式，均不能跟在介词后。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 run 需用副词 easily（轻松地）。easy 为形容词；easier/easiest 为比较级/最高级形容词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语（more + adj.）  
**【解析】** feel more energetic 意为"感觉更有活力"，feel 为系动词后接形容词。energy 为名词；energetically 为副词；energize 为动词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级  
**【解析】** concentrate better 意为"更好地集中注意力"，better 为 well 的比较级，修饰动词 concentrate。good 为形容词；well 为原级；best 为最高级，此处有隐含比较（比以前更好）。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide to do sth. 意为"决定做某事"，后接不定式。join 为原形；joined 为过去式；joining 为动名词，均不用于 decide 后。故选 D。
