# 中考英语完形填空：假期图书馆读书

## 完形填空原文

On the third day of the holiday, I decided to spend some time in the city library. My mother was pleased ___1___ my plan and drove me there after breakfast.

The library was much ___2___ than I had imagined. Rows of bookshelves stood neatly, and soft music played in the background. I found a seat near the window and picked up a novel ___3___ was recommended by my English teacher.

The story was about a boy who traveled around the world ___4___ himself. At first, I read slowly because some words were new to me. But as the plot became more exciting, I couldn't stop ___5___. Two hours passed without my noticing.

A librarian came over and reminded me ___6___ softly. She said, "The reading room will close for lunch in ten minutes." I thanked her and decided to borrow the book so that I ___7___ finish it at home.

While waiting in line, I met my classmate Wang Lei. He was ___8___ surprised to see me as I was to see him. We agreed to come back together the next day and share ___9___ favorite books with each other.

That afternoon, I read two more chapters. I truly believe that reading can open a door ___10___ a bigger world.

## 选项

1. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. about  
2. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. bigger &emsp;&emsp; C. biggest &emsp;&emsp; D. the biggest  
3. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
4. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
5. A. read &emsp;&emsp; B. reads &emsp;&emsp; C. to read &emsp;&emsp; D. reading  
6. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
7. A. could &emsp;&emsp; B. must &emsp;&emsp; C. should &emsp;&emsp; D. need  
8. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. such &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
9. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. in  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be pleased with  
**【解析】** be pleased with 意为"对……感到满意/高兴"。be pleased of 不存在；be pleased for 多表为他人高兴；be pleased about 也可表高兴，但 with 更常接具体事物。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 句中有 than，需用比较级。big 的比较级为 bigger。big 为原级；biggest 为最高级且缺 the；the biggest 为最高级，不与 than 搭配。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词  
**【解析】** 先行词 a novel 为物，在从句中作主语，用 which 或 that 引导定语从句。who 指人；what 不引导定语从句；whom 指人且作宾语。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 by oneself  
**【解析】** by oneself 意为"独自地"，即 by himself 表示男孩独自环游世界。of oneself 不常见；for oneself 意为"为自己"；with oneself 不成立。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（stop doing）  
**【解析】** stop doing sth. 意为"停止做某事"，couldn't stop reading 表示"忍不住一直读"。stop to do 意为"停下来去做另一件事"，不符语境。read/reads 为谓语动词形式，不能跟在 stop 后。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** remind 为及物动词，后接宾语用宾格 me。I 为主格；my 为形容词性物主代词；mine 为名词性物主代词，均不能作 remind 的宾语。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词（could 表能力）  
**【解析】** so that I could finish 表示"以便我能完成"，could 表目的从句中的能力。must 表必须；should 表应该；need 表需要，均不如 could 自然。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** as... as 同级比较  
**【解析】** "as surprised... as I was" 构成同级比较结构，意为"和我一样惊讶"。so 不与后面的 as 搭配；such 修饰名词；very 不用于该比较结构。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** 修饰名词 favorite books 需用形容词性物主代词 our。we 为主格；us 为宾格；ours 为名词性物主代词，后不能再接名词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词搭配（a door to）  
**【解析】** a door to 意为"通往……的门"，to 表方向。of 表所属；for 表目的；in 表在……里面，均不如 to 贴切。故选 A。
