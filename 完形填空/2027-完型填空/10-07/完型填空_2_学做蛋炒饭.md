# 中考英语完形填空：学做蛋炒饭

## 完形填空原文

My father is a great cook, and he ___1___ to teach me how to make egg fried rice last Saturday. I was both excited and nervous because it was my first time ___2___ alone in the kitchen.

First, he told me ___3___ two eggs into a bowl and mix them well. Then I needed to chop some green onions into small pieces. I was very ___4___ while using the knife because I didn't want to cut my fingers.

When the oil in the pan became hot ___5___, my father asked me to pour the egg mixture in. The eggs turned golden quickly. "Now add the rice," he said. I ___6___ the cold rice from the fridge and put it into the pan.

The most difficult part was stir-frying. I had to keep ___7___ the rice so that it wouldn't stick to the bottom. My arm got tired after a few minutes, ___8___ I didn't give up.

Finally, I added salt, soy sauce, and the green onions. The kitchen was filled ___9___ a wonderful smell. My father tasted a spoonful and gave me a big smile. "Well done! It's even tastier than ___10___!" he said. That compliment made my whole day.

## 选项

1. A. offer &emsp;&emsp; B. offers &emsp;&emsp; C. offered &emsp;&emsp; D. offering  
2. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. cooked &emsp;&emsp; C. cooking &emsp;&emsp; D. to cook  
3. A. break &emsp;&emsp; B. broke &emsp;&emsp; C. to break &emsp;&emsp; D. breaking  
4. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. careless  
5. A. enough &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too  
6. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. took &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. taken  
7. A. stir &emsp;&emsp; B. stirred &emsp;&emsp; C. to stir &emsp;&emsp; D. stirring  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
9. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
10. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 根据时间状语 last Saturday，全文为过去时态，offer 应用过去式 offered。offer 为原形；offers 为三单；offering 为现在分词，均不符合时态要求。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定句型 It's one's first time doing  
**【解析】** "It's one's first time doing sth." 为固定句型，意为"某人第一次做某事"，doing 为动名词。cook 为原形；cooked 为过去式；to do 虽有 "It's time to do" 结构但与 first time 搭配应用 doing。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell sb. to do sth. 意为"告诉某人做某事"，不定式作宾补。break 为原形；broke 为过去式；breaking 为现在分词，均不能用于 tell sb. 后。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** was 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。careful（小心的）符合语境。care 为名词/动词；carefully 为副词；careless 意为"粗心的"，与语境相反。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** enough 的用法  
**【解析】** enough 修饰形容词时放在形容词之后，hot enough 意为"足够热"。much 不修饰 hot 的程度；very hot 虽正确但不表示"足够"的概念；too hot 意为"太热"，含消极义。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为叙述过去事件，动词应用过去式 took。take 为原形；taking 为现在分词；taken 为过去分词，均不适合作谓语。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（keep doing）  
**【解析】** keep doing sth. 意为"一直做某事"，后接动名词。stir 为原形；stirred 为过去式；to stir 为不定式，keep 后不用不定式。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折）  
**【解析】** "手臂累了"与"没有放弃"为转折关系，用 but。and 表并列；so 表因果；or 表选择，均不符合语义。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be filled with  
**【解析】** be filled with 意为"充满"，为固定被动搭配。be filled of 不存在；be filled by 强调动作施行者；be filled in 意为"填写"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 名词性物主代词  
**【解析】** 此处比较的是"我做的蛋炒饭"和"他（爸爸）做的蛋炒饭"，than 后省略了 rice，用名词性物主代词 mine 代替 my (rice)。I 为主格；me 为宾格；my 为形容词性物主代词，后需接名词。故选 D。
