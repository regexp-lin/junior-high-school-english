# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工陶瓷杯

## 完形填空原文

On Saturday, my aunt took me ___1___ a pottery studio in our neighborhood. “You will make ___2___ own cup today,” she smiled. The teacher showed us ___3___ to shape the clay on a wheel. At first, the clay kept ___4___ off my hands, and I almost gave up. “Be patient,” the teacher said. “Try ___5___ hold it gently.” I took a deep breath and followed her advice. An hour later, my cup ___6___ looked like a real one! After it was dried, it ___7___ in a kiln for a long time. When I got it back, I painted flowers on ___8___ carefully. My aunt said the cup could be used ___9___ drinking tea, but I decided to keep it ___10___ a gift for my mom.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. you &emsp;&emsp; B. your &emsp;&emsp; C. yours &emsp;&emsp; D. yourself  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
4. A. slip &emsp;&emsp; B. slipping &emsp;&emsp; C. slipped &emsp;&emsp; D. to slip  
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
6. A. final &emsp;&emsp; B. finally &emsp;&emsp; C. finalize &emsp;&emsp; D. finalized  
7. A. fires &emsp;&emsp; B. fired &emsp;&emsp; C. was fired &emsp;&emsp; D. is firing  
8. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. its &emsp;&emsp; C. itself &emsp;&emsp; D. them  
9. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
10. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 take sb. to sp.  
**【解析】** “带某人去某地” 用 take sb. to + 地点。for 表目的或原因；at 表在小地点但不与 take 构成此义；with 表伴随。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** own 前须用形容词性物主代词 your，构成 your own cup，表示 “你自己的杯子”。you 为人称代词主格/宾格；yours 为名词性物主代词，后不接名词；yourself 为反身代词，不与 own 这样搭配。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式” 结构  
**【解析】** show sb. how to do sth. 表示 “向某人展示如何做某事”。what to shape 语义不通；which to shape 缺语境；when 表时间，不如 how 贴合 “塑形方法”。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（keep doing）  
**【解析】** keep 后接动名词 doing，表示 “持续不断做某事”，the clay kept slipping 表示 “泥一直从手中滑掉”。slip 为原形；slipped 为过去式；to slip 为不定式，均不与 keep 搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 try to do sth.  
**【解析】** try to hold 表示 “尽量轻轻地握住”。try doing 表 “试着做（看效果）” 也可，但选项为 to；for/of/with 不能与 try 构成 “尽力做” 的固定结构。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** looked 为动词，须用副词 finally 修饰，表示 “最终看起来像一个真正的杯子”。final 为形容词；finalize 为动词 “定稿”，词性与句意不符；finalized 为过去分词。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** it（the cup）与 fire（烧制）为被动关系，且叙述过去经历，用 was fired in a kiln。fires 为主动第三人称单数；fired 缺助动词 be；is firing 为主动进行时，均不符合 “被放入窑中烧制” 的含义。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** paint sth. on it 中 it 指代上文的 cup（单数）。its 为物主代词，后需名词；itself 强调自身，此处仅需宾语 “在杯子上画”；them 为复数，与 cup 不一致。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（be used for doing）  
**【解析】** be used for drinking tea 表示 “被用来喝茶”，for 后接动名词表用途。be used by 后接人；be used with 表 “与……一起使用”；be used to 表 “习惯于” 时 to 为介词，或 “被用来做” 时为 used to do（不定式），此处 drinking 为动名词，应用 for。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 as 表 “作为”  
**【解析】** keep it as a gift 表示 “把它作为礼物留着”。like 作介词表 “像”，不如 as 准确表达 “身份/用途”；for/with 与 keep...a gift 搭配不自然。故选 A。
