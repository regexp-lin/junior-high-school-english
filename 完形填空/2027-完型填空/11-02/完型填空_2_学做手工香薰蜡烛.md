# 中考英语完形填空：学做香薰蜡烛

## 完形填空原文

My sister loves sweet smells, so we joined a DIY workshop ___1___ scented candles last month. The teacher told us ___2___ hot wax could be dangerous if we were careless.

First, we melted the wax slowly in a pot. While we ___3___ for it to become liquid, the teacher showed us ___4___ to mix in a few drops of lavender oil. The smell in the room grew ___5___ than at the beginning.

When the wax cooled a little, we poured it into small glass jars. I tried ___6___ decorate the surface with dried flowers, ___7___ they floated away at first. Later, I learned ___8___ them gently on top after pouring half of the wax.

At the end of the class, everyone ___9___ a candle home. I felt proud because this was the ___10___ handmade gift I had ever made for my sister.

## 选项

1. A. learn &emsp;&emsp; B. learning &emsp;&emsp; C. to learn &emsp;&emsp; D. learned  
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
3. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. waited &emsp;&emsp; C. were waiting &emsp;&emsp; D. have waited  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
5. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. stronger &emsp;&emsp; C. strongest &emsp;&emsp; D. the strongest  
6. A. decorate &emsp;&emsp; B. decorating &emsp;&emsp; C. to decorate &emsp;&emsp; D. decorated  
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
8. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. to put &emsp;&emsp; C. putting &emsp;&emsp; D. puts  
9. A. took &emsp;&emsp; B. takes &emsp;&emsp; C. was taken &emsp;&emsp; D. has taken  
10. A. meaningful &emsp;&emsp; B. more meaningful &emsp;&emsp; C. most meaningful &emsp;&emsp; D. the most meaningful  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）  
**【解析】** joined a workshop 的目的是学习制作香薰蜡烛，用不定式 to learn 表目的。learn/learning/learned 不能直接表目的状语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 that  
**【解析】** told us 后为陈述句内容的宾语从句，成分完整，用 that。what/whether/how 在从句中含义或成分不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去进行时  
**【解析】** While 引导的时间状语从句强调“在等待蜡融化的过程中”，与主句 showed 同时进行的背景动作用过去进行时 were waiting。wait/waited 无进行含义；have waited 为完成时，与 last month 叙述不协调。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构  
**【解析】** show sb. how to do sth. 表示“演示如何做某事”，how to mix in... 作直接宾语。what to mix 缺宾语不明确；which/when 与 mix in oil 搭配不如 how 自然。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 有 than at the beginning，用比较级 stronger smell（grew stronger）。strong 原级；strongest/the strongest 为最高级。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（try to do）  
**【解析】** try to do sth. 表示“努力/试图做某事”，try decorating 为 try doing（尝试做），语境为努力装饰表面，用 to decorate 更贴切“试图装饰”。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词  
**【解析】** “花一开始漂走了”与上文尝试装饰形成转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（learn to do）  
**【解析】** learn to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示“学会做某事”，用 to put。learn put 语法错误；putting 不典型；puts 为谓语形式。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 叙述上个月课堂结束时的动作，用一般过去时 took。takes 为现在时；was taken 被动不必要；has taken 与整体过去叙事不一致。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（ever 提示）  
**【解析】** I had ever made 提示范围“曾经做过的所有礼物中”，用 the most meaningful。meaningful/more meaningful 无最高级含义；most meaningful 缺定冠词 the 不严谨（中考常考 the + most）。故选 D。
