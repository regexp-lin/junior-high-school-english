# 中考英语完形填空：敬老院志愿行

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, I went to a nursing home ___1___ three classmates. We brought some fruit and ___2___ storybooks that we had read many times. The old people there welcomed ___3___ with warm smiles.

A grandma told us about ___4___ life when she was young. Her stories were so interesting ___5___ we forgot about the time. Then we helped clean the windows and ___6___ the chairs for them.

Before we left, a grandpa said, “You are kind children. I hope more students ___7___ here next month.” We all nodded and promised we ___8___ again.

On the bus home, I thought about ___9___ I had learned that day: respecting the elderly is not ___10___ saying polite words, but doing small things with patience.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. but  
2. A. a few &emsp;&emsp; B. few &emsp;&emsp; C. little &emsp;&emsp; D. a little  
3. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
4. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself  
5. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
6. A. fix &emsp;&emsp; B. fixing &emsp;&emsp; C. to fix &emsp;&emsp; D. fixed  
7. A. visit &emsp;&emsp; B. will visit &emsp;&emsp; C. visited &emsp;&emsp; D. have visited  
8. A. come &emsp;&emsp; B. came &emsp;&emsp; C. would come &emsp;&emsp; D. have come  
9. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
10. A. only about &emsp;&emsp; B. about only &emsp;&emsp; C. just about &emsp;&emsp; D. about just  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（with 表伴随）  
**【解析】** “与三位同学一起”用介词 with。and 为连词，连接并列成分，不能直接接 three classmates 表伴随；or/but 不符合语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** a few / few / little 辨析  
**【解析】** storybooks 为可数名词复数，且句意为肯定“带了一些”，用 a few。few 表否定“几乎没有”；little/a little 修饰不可数名词。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** welcomed 为及物动词，后接宾语，指“我们”，用宾格 us。we 为主格；our/ours 为物主代词，不能作宾语。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** 修饰名词 life，表示“她的生活”，用形容词性物主代词 her。she 为主格；hers 为名词性物主代词；herself 为反身代词。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定结构 so... that...  
**【解析】** “如此有趣以至于我们忘了时间”，用 so... that...。what/which/how 不能构成该结果状语从句结构。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列谓语与动词形式一致  
**【解析】** and 连接与 helped 并列的谓语，help (to) do 结构中 clean 与 fix 并列，均用动词原形 fix。fixing/to fix/fixed 与 helped clean 并列形式不一致。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句中的一般将来时  
**【解析】** hope 后宾语从句表“下个月更多学生会来参观”，从时间 next month 可知用一般将来时 will visit。visit 原形无时态变化；visited 为过去时；have visited 为现在完成时，均不贴切。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去将来时  
**【解析】** 主句 promised 为过去时，从句“我们会再来”在过去作出的承诺，用过去将来时 would come。came 为过去时，与“承诺将来”不符；come/have come 时态不当。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词后宾语从句（what 作 learned 的宾语）  
**【解析】** think about 后为宾语从句，从句中 learned 缺宾语，表示“所学到的东西”，用 what。that 无成分意义；which 需选择范围；how 表方式，与 learned 搭配不当。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定结构 is not only about... but...  
**【解析】** 空格填 only about，与前面的 is not 构成 is not only about saying...，与后文 but doing... 呼应，表示“敬老不仅仅在于说客气话，还在于耐心做小事”。B、D 词序错误；just about 虽可表“大约”，但在此与 but 的逻辑衔接不如 only about 贴切。故选 A。
