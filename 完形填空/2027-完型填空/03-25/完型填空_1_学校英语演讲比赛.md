# 中考英语完形填空：学校英语演讲比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last Thursday, our school held an English speech contest. I was chosen ___1___ our class to take part in it. I felt both excited ___2___ nervous.

I spent three days ___3___ my speech. The topic was "My Dream." I practiced in front of the mirror again ___4___ again until I could speak without looking at the paper.

On the day of the contest, there were twelve ___5___ from different classes. When it was my turn, my heart beat fast. I walked onto the stage ___6___ took a deep breath.

As I began speaking, I noticed my teacher ___7___ at me with an encouraging smile. Gradually, I became ___8___ relaxed and my voice grew stronger.

In the end, I won second place. I was so happy ___9___ I almost cried. My classmates clapped and cheered for me.

The contest taught me that practice is the key ___10___ success. I will keep working hard on my English.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. as
2. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. prepared &emsp;&emsp; C. to prepare &emsp;&emsp; D. preparing
4. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. then
5. A. speakers &emsp;&emsp; B. listeners &emsp;&emsp; C. writers &emsp;&emsp; D. readers
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. look &emsp;&emsp; B. looked &emsp;&emsp; C. looking &emsp;&emsp; D. to look
8. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very
9. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. if
10. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. in

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/被动语态搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被我们班选中参加比赛。be chosen by... 表示"被……选中"。for/with/as 不符合被动语态的施动者搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词/both...and 结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：我既兴奋又紧张。both...and... 表示"既……又……"。or/but/so 不能与 both 搭配。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/spend...doing
**【解析】** 本句意为：我花了三天准备演讲稿。spend time doing sth. 为固定搭配。prepare/prepared/to prepare 不符合 spend 的搭配。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一遍又一遍地在镜子前练习。again and again 为固定搭配，表示"一次又一次"。or/but/then 不符合此搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有来自不同班级的十二位演讲者。演讲比赛的参与者为 speakers。listeners 为听众；writers 为作者；readers 为读者。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我走上舞台并深呼吸了一下。walked 与 took 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/现在分词作宾补
**【解析】** 本句意为：我注意到老师正带着鼓励的微笑看着我。notice sb. doing sth. 表示"注意到某人正在做某事"。look/looked/to look 不符合 notice 的搭配。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我渐渐变得更加放松了。became 后接比较级 more relaxed 表示"变得更放松"。much 不能直接修饰形容词原级作表语；most 为最高级；very 不表示变化趋势。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** so...that 结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太高兴了以至于差点哭了。so...that... 表示"如此……以至于……"。when/because/if 不符合 so 引导的结果状语从句。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：练习是成功的关键。the key to... 为固定搭配，表示"……的关键"。of/for/in 不符合 key 的搭配。故选 B。
