# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工明信片

## 完形填空原文

Graduation is coming soon. I wanted to give each of my close friends ___1___ handmade postcard. My art teacher taught me the basic steps.

I prepared thick white cards, watercolor paints and some ___2___ tape. First, I painted a small picture on the front of each card. One had a sunset, ___3___ had flowers, and another had a little cat.

The pictures were simple ___4___ full of feeling. I waited for the paint to dry ___5___ before moving to the next step. If the paint was still wet, the colors would run and ___6___ messy.

On the back, I wrote a short message to ___7___ friend. For my best friend Lily, I wrote: "Thank you for always ___8___ by my side." It took me two evenings ___9___ finish all the postcards.

When I gave them out on the last day of school, my friends were very surprised. Lily said she would keep the postcard ___10___ and treasure it forever.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. color &emsp;&emsp; B. colorful &emsp;&emsp; C. colorfully &emsp;&emsp; D. colors
3. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. another &emsp;&emsp; C. the other &emsp;&emsp; D. others
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. complete &emsp;&emsp; B. completely &emsp;&emsp; C. completed &emsp;&emsp; D. completing
6. A. become &emsp;&emsp; B. becomes &emsp;&emsp; C. became &emsp;&emsp; D. becoming
7. A. every &emsp;&emsp; B. all &emsp;&emsp; C. both &emsp;&emsp; D. each
8. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. been &emsp;&emsp; C. being &emsp;&emsp; D. is
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. / &emsp;&emsp; D. of
10. A. safe &emsp;&emsp; B. safely &emsp;&emsp; C. safety &emsp;&emsp; D. save

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想给每个好朋友一张手工明信片。handmade 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我准备了厚白卡纸、水彩颜料和一些彩色胶带。colorful 为形容词修饰名词 tape。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 不定代词（one...another...another... 一个……另一个……又一个）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一张是日落，另一张是花，还有一张是小猫。三者中的"另一个"用 another。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（but 转折）
**【解析】** 本句意为：图画很简单但充满感情。simple 和 full of feeling 为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我等颜料完全干了才进行下一步。修饰动词 dry 用副词 completely。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 情态动词 + 动词原形（would + 动词原形）
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果颜料还是湿的，颜色会晕染变得凌乱。would 后接动词原形 run 和 become 并列。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 不定代词（each 每一个）
**【解析】** 本句意为：在背面，我给每位朋友写了一段短留言。each 强调"每一个"个体。every 也可，但 each friend 更强调个体。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（thank sb. for doing sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：谢谢你一直在我身边。"for"为介词，后接动名词 being。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定句型（It takes sb. + 时间 + to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我花了两个晚上才完成所有明信片。"It takes sb. time to do sth."为固定句型。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：Lily 说她会安全地保存明信片并永远珍藏。修饰动词 keep 用副词 safely。safe 为形容词。故选 B。
