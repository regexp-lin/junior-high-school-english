# 中考英语完形填空：给老师写感谢信

## 完形填空原文

Before the semester ended, I decided to write a thank-you letter ___1___ my English teacher, Ms. Wang. She has been teaching us ___2___ three years and helped me fall in love with English.

I sat at my desk and thought ___3___ what to write. There were so many things I wanted to say. I remembered the time when I ___4___ badly in a test and almost gave up. Ms. Wang talked to me after class and encouraged me ___5___ trying.

She also organized many fun activities, ___6___ English corner and drama club. These activities made learning English much ___7___ interesting than just reading textbooks.

I wrote three pages in total. At the end, I drew a small heart and signed my ___8___. The next morning, I put the letter on her desk before she ___9___.

Later that day, Ms. Wang found me in the hallway. Her eyes were a little red. She gave me a warm hug and said my letter was one of the ___10___ things she had ever read.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. about &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. over
4. A. do &emsp;&emsp; B. does &emsp;&emsp; C. did &emsp;&emsp; D. done
5. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. keeping &emsp;&emsp; C. to keep &emsp;&emsp; D. kept
6. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. with
7. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. much
8. A. name &emsp;&emsp; B. address &emsp;&emsp; C. age &emsp;&emsp; D. phone
9. A. arrive &emsp;&emsp; B. arrives &emsp;&emsp; C. arrived &emsp;&emsp; D. arriving
10. A. nice &emsp;&emsp; B. nicer &emsp;&emsp; C. nicest &emsp;&emsp; D. most nice

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（write...to... 写信给……）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定给英语老师王老师写一封感谢信。"write a letter to sb."表示"给某人写信"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（for + 时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教了我们三年。for 后接时间段，搭配现在完成进行时。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（think about 考虑）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在桌前想该写什么。"think about"表示"考虑、思考"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：我记得有次考试考得很差。when 引导的从句描述过去事件，用一般过去时 did。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（encourage sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师课后找我谈话并鼓励我继续努力。"encourage sb. to do sth."为固定结构。"to keep trying"继续尝试。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（like 例如）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她还组织了很多有趣的活动，比如英语角和戏剧社。like 表示"例如"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（much more + 多音节形容词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些活动使学英语比只读课本有趣得多。"much more interesting than"为比较级结构。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（sign one's name 签名）
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后我画了一颗小心并签上了我的名字。"sign one's name"表示"签名"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时（before 引导的时间状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二天早上我在她来之前把信放在了她桌上。before 引导时间状语从句，用一般过去时 arrived。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说我的信是她读过的最温暖的东西之一。"one of the + 最高级"结构，用 nicest。故选 C。
