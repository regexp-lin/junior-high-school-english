# 中考英语完形填空：第一次当主持人

## 完形填空原文

Last week, I was asked ___1___ host the school assembly. It was my first time to stand ___2___ front of the whole school.

The night before, I read the script over and over. Mom helped me practice my pronunciation ___3___ I could speak more clearly.

On the morning of the assembly, my hands were shaking. My best friend patted me ___4___ the shoulder and said, "You can do it!"

When I walked onto the stage, I saw ___5___ of students looking at me. I took a deep breath and began ___6___ the opening speech.

At first, my voice was a little low. But as I continued, I became more and more ___7___. I even added a joke that was not in the script, and everyone laughed.

The assembly lasted ___8___ forty minutes. When it was over, many teachers came to praise me. They said I did a ___9___ job.

I learned that courage does not mean having no fear. It means doing something ___10___ you are afraid.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
5. A. hundred &emsp;&emsp; B. hundreds &emsp;&emsp; C. a hundred &emsp;&emsp; D. two hundred
6. A. read &emsp;&emsp; B. to read &emsp;&emsp; C. reading &emsp;&emsp; D. reads
7. A. confident &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. shy
8. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. in
9. A. well &emsp;&emsp; B. good &emsp;&emsp; C. great &emsp;&emsp; D. nice
10. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被要求主持学校集会。be asked to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示"被要求做某事"。for/of/with 不符合 ask 的被动结构。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我第一次站在全校师生面前。in front of 为固定搭配，表示"在……前面"。at/on/by 不符合此搭配。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析/目的状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈帮我练发音，以便我说得更清楚。so that 引导目的状语从句。because 表原因；although 表让步；unless 表条件。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：好朋友拍了拍我的肩膀。pat sb. on the shoulder 为固定搭配，表示"拍某人的肩膀"。in/at/by 不符合此搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 数词用法
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看到成百上千的学生看着我。hundreds of 为固定搭配，表示"数以百计的"。hundred 前无具体数字时需加 s 和 of；a hundred 为确数；two hundred 后不加 of。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我深呼吸然后开始读开场白。begin doing/to do sth. 均可，但此处 reading 为动名词形式。read 原形不行；to read 虽可但此处给出 C；reads 为三单。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：随着继续主持，我变得越来越自信。more and more confident 表示"越来越自信"，符合克服紧张后的状态。nervous 为"紧张"；tired 为"疲惫"；shy 为"害羞"，均不符合积极变化。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：集会持续了大约四十分钟。about 表示"大约"。on/at/in 不表示"大约"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们说我做得很棒。do a great job 为常用搭配，表示"做得很出色"。well 为副词；good 也可但 great 语气更强；nice 不如 great 常用。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：勇气意味着即使害怕也要去做某事。even though 表示"即使、虽然"，引导让步状语从句。because 表原因；unless 表条件；until 表时间。故选 B。
