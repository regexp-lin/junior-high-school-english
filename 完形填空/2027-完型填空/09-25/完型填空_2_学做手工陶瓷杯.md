# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工陶瓷杯

## 完形填空原文

During the Mid-Autumn holiday, I signed up for a pottery class ___1___ learn how to make a ceramic cup by hand. The studio was quiet and smelled ___2___ earth and fresh paint. Soft music played in the background, which helped me stay calm. The teacher first showed us ___3___ to shape the clay on a spinning wheel.

I tried to follow her steps, ___4___ my cup looked uneven at the bottom. She told me ___5___ to press more gently and keep my hands steady. ___6___ about an hour of practice, I finally made a small cup that could stand straight.

After that, we waited for the cups ___7___ dry. The next day, I painted simple patterns ___8___ blue and white. The teacher said the colors would look brighter ___9___ the cups were fired in the kiln. When I held the finished cup at home, I felt ___10___ I had created something truly my own.

## 选项

1. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; D. even though
2. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. from
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. why &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. how
6. A. Before &emsp;&emsp; B. After &emsp;&emsp; C. Until &emsp;&emsp; D. During
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. after &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
10. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【考点】** 非谓语/目的表达（in order to）

**【解析】** 报名陶艺课的目的是学习做杯子，表**目的**用 **in order to + 动词原形**。so that 后接从句，不接动词原形原形 learn；as soon as 表“一……就……”；even though 表让步，语义不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【考点】** 固定搭配（smell of）

**【解析】** **smell of** 表示“有……的气味；闻起来像……”，后接气味来源（earth and fresh paint）。smell like 也可表“闻起来像”，但选项中 like 不如 of 与“泥土与颜料混合气味来源”这一搭配常见且贴切（教材中也常见 smell of）。smell with/from 搭配不典型。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构（how to）

**【解析】** show sb. **how to do sth.** 表示“向某人展示如何做某事”。此处指在拉坯机上**如何**塑形，用 **how**。what to 需接宾语成分；why/when 与“示范操作步骤”不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【考点】** 并列连词（转折）

**【解析】** “我努力跟着做”与“杯子底部不平”为**转折**关系，用 **but**。and 表顺承；or 表选择；so 表结果，均不合适。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【考点】** 宾语从句/“疑问词 + 不定式”（how to）

**【解析】** tell sb. **how to** press more gently 表示告诉对方**如何**更轻地按压并保持稳定。why 问原因；when 问时间；where 问地点，都不如 how 贴合“操作方法指导”。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**

**【考点】** 介词/时间表达（After + 时间段）

**【解析】** “经过大约一小时的练习之后，我终于……”用 **After about an hour of practice**。Before 与“终于成功”逻辑相反；Until 多与 not 搭配或强调持续到某时；During 后一般接名词短语但不如 After 自然表达“练习结束后的结果”。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**

**【考点】** 非谓语动词（wait for…to do）

**【解析】** **wait for + 宾语 + to do** 为固定结构，表示“等待……去做/变得……”。此处指等待杯子变干：*wait for the cups to dry*。for/of/with 不能这样接宾补表达“变干”。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**

**【考点】** 介词（in + 颜色）

**【解析】** 表示“用某种颜色（绘制）”常用 **in + color**：*in blue and white*（青花配色）。on 多表在表面上（虽可说 paint on the cup，但此处紧跟颜色）；at/by 不用于此结构。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**

**【考点】** 时间状语从句（after）

**【解析】** 老师说颜色会在杯子**进窑烧制之后**看起来更鲜艳，**after** 引导时间状语从句，符合工艺顺序。if 表条件；until 表“直到”；unless 表“除非”，均不如 after 准确描述“烧制后变化”。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**

**【考点】** 宾语从句（that 引导）

**【解析】** feel 后可接 **that** 引导的宾语从句，陈述一种感受或判断：*I felt that I had created something truly my own*（我觉得自己仿佛/确实创造出了真正属于自己的东西）。whether 表示“是否”，与陈述语气不符；unless/although 不能这样连接从句表达感受。故选 A。
