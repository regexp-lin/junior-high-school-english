# 中考英语完形填空：冬日放风筝

## 完形填空原文

On a sunny winter afternoon, Dad drove me to a wide riverside park ___1___ the wind was steady. We carried a kite we had built ___2___ bamboo sticks and thin paper.

At first the kite dropped ___3___ a stone, and I felt discouraged. Dad told me ___4___ the string slowly when a gust came. I tried his advice, ___5___ the colorful tail suddenly lifted and danced in the blue sky.

A group of younger kids ran ___6___ us, pointing and cheering. Their voices mixed ___7___ the soft flapping sound above our heads. I laughed, forgetting how cold my fingers ___8___.

Before sunset, we rolled the string ___9___ and walked home slowly. That simple afternoon taught me that patience works ___10___ the wind, not against it.

## 选项

1. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. when
2. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. pull &emsp;&emsp; B. pulling &emsp;&emsp; C. to pull &emsp;&emsp; D. pulled
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. across &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. over
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
8. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. have been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be
9. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. off
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. beyond &emsp;&emsp; D. without

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父亲开车带我去河滨公园，那里风很稳。先行词 park 表地点，从句缺状语用 where。A 项 which；C 项 what；D 项 when。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用竹条和薄纸做的风筝。built from 用……材料制成。A 项 of；B 项 with 也可表工具，但 from 更强调材料来源；D 项 by。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：风筝像石头一样掉下来。like a stone 像石头。B 项 as 作“像”时常接从句或 as a...；C 项 for；D 项 by。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：告诉我阵风来时慢慢放线。tell sb. to do sth.。A 项 pull；B 项 pulling；D 项 pulled。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我试了建议，彩色尾巴突然升起。顺承并列用 and。B 项 but；C 项 or；D 项 so。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一群更小的孩子从我们身边跑过。run past 跑过。B 项 across 横穿；C 项 through 穿过；D 项 over 越过。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的声音与头顶轻拍声混在一起。mix with。A 项 mix to；B 项 for；D 项 on。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：忘了手指有多冷。主句过去时，从句描述当时状态用 were。A 项 are 时态不一致；C 项 have been；D 项 will be。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：把线收起来。roll up 卷起；收起。B 项 roll in；C 项 roll on；D 项 roll off。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：耐心要顺着风，而不是逆风硬来。work with 与……配合；顺应。A 项 for；C 项 beyond；D 项 without。故选 B。
