# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校科学实验比赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a science fair ___1___ March, and my partner Tom and I joined the experiment competition. Our project was about ___2___ plants grow toward light.

We designed a simple box ___3___ a small window on one side. Every day we measured the angle of the stems and recorded the numbers ___4___ a chart.

Two days before the show, our sensor broke. Tom wanted ___5___, but I said we could use a ruler ___6___. We worked late and fixed the problem.

On the judging day, I explained our steps ___7___ and answered questions confidently. The judges nodded and wrote notes ___8___.

In the end, we won the second prize. Tom hugged me and said teamwork was ___9___ important than any tool. I agreed, thinking science is not only about facts, ___10___ about trying again.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
2. A. why &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
5. A. give up &emsp;&emsp; B. giving up &emsp;&emsp; C. to give up &emsp;&emsp; D. gave up
6. A. instead &emsp;&emsp; B. instead of &emsp;&emsp; C. instead for &emsp;&emsp; D. instead to
7. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. clearly &emsp;&emsp; C. more clear &emsp;&emsp; D. clearest
8. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. more careful &emsp;&emsp; D. most carefully
9. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学校在三月举办了科学展。月份前用介词 in。on 用于具体日期；at 用于具体时刻。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的项目研究植物如何向光生长。about 后接宾语从句，表示方式用 how。why 原因；when 时间；where 地点。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们设计了一个一侧有小窗的简单盒子。with 表示“具有；带有”。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：并把数据记录在表格上。on a chart 表示“在图表上”。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：汤姆想放弃。want to do sth 为固定搭配，用 to give up。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我说我们可以改用尺子。instead 为副词，表示“反而；代替”，常放句末或用逗号隔开。instead of 后须接宾语，此处无宾语，用 instead。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我清楚地解释我们的步骤。修饰动词 explained 用副词 clearly。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：评委点头并认真做记录。修饰动词 wrote 用 carefully。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：团队合作比任何工具都更重要。important 的比较级为 more important，后有 than。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得科学不仅关于事实，也关于再次尝试。not only...but (also)... 为固定搭配，此处用 but about。故选 C。
