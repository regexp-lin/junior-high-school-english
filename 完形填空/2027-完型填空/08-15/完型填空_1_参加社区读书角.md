# 中考英语完形填空：参加社区读书角

## 完形填空原文

Every Friday evening, volunteers open ___1___ small reading corner in our neighborhood center. Last week, I went there ___2___ my cousin and borrowed a book about space. A volunteer ___3___ name was Ms. Lin helped us find the right shelf.

While I was reading at the table, several children listened to a story ___4___ was read aloud by a university student. The room was quiet ___5___ you could hear pages turning. I finished two chapters ___6___ I realized it was already eight o'clock.

Before leaving, I signed up ___7___ a summer reading challenge. Ms. Lin said the books ___8___ were returned on time could be borrowed again for free. I promised ___9___ I would come back next week and bring a notebook ___10___ down new words.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. but
3. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. which
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
5. A. enough &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. such &emsp;&emsp; D. too
6. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. after &emsp;&emsp; D. before
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how
10. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. writing &emsp;&emsp; C. to write &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：志愿者们在社区中心开办一个小型读书角。small 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和表弟一起去了那里。with 表示“和……一起”。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位名叫林老师的志愿者帮我们找书架。先行词 volunteer 与 name 为所属关系，用 whose。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：几个孩子在听一位大学生朗读的故事。先行词 story 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定句型
**【解析】** 本句意为：房间安静到你能听见翻页声。so…that… 如此……以至于……。enough 位置不同；such 后接名词；too…to… 表“太……而不能”，与句意不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我读了两章才意识到已经八点了。before 表示“在……之前/才”，体现“没注意到时间流逝直到……”。until 也可表“直到才”，常与 not 连用；此处 before 更自然。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我报名参加暑期阅读挑战。sign up for 报名参加。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：按时归还的书可以再次免费借阅。先行词 books 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我保证下周还会来。promise 后接 that 引导的陈述从句。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：带上笔记本以便记下生词。用不定式 to write 表目的。故选 C。
