# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工莲花灯

## 完形填空原文

The lotus lantern looks beautiful ___1___ the Mid-Autumn Festival is coming, so our craft teacher decided to teach us ___2___ to make one. She prepared pink paper, glue, and a small LED light ___3___ could be put inside the flower.

First, we cut the petals carefully. "If you cut too fast, the edges ___4___ rough," she reminded us. I slowed down and tried to fold each layer ___5___ than before. When the petals were glued together, the flower opened ___6___ it was blooming in water.

We fixed the LED light at the bottom and tied a string ___7___ we could hang the lantern on the balcony. Looking at the soft light, I thought handmade work was ___8___ difficult than it looked, ___9___ it was worth the effort. I showed a photo ___10___ my parents and they said it looked professional.

## 选项

1. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when
3. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
4. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. will be &emsp;&emsp; D. have been
5. A. neat &emsp;&emsp; B. neater &emsp;&emsp; C. more neatly &emsp;&emsp; D. most neatly
6. A. as if &emsp;&emsp; B. even if &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as
7. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
8. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：莲花灯很漂亮，因为中秋节快到了。表原因用 because。if 条件；although 让步；unless 除非。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师决定教我们如何做一盏。teach sb. how to do sth. 故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：准备了一盏可放进花里的小 LED 灯。先行词 light 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态（主将从现）
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你剪得太快，边缘就会很粗糙。if 引导条件状语从句，主句常用一般将来时表结果。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我放慢速度，尽量把每一层折得比以前更整齐。修饰动词 fold，用副词；有 than，用 more neatly。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：花瓣粘好后，花朵张开，仿佛在水中绽放。as if 好像、仿佛。even if 即使；so that 以便；as long as 只要。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：系上一根绳子，以便能把灯笼挂在阳台上。so that 表目的。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工作品比看起来更难。有 than，difficult 多音节，用 more difficult。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但很值得付出努力。与前文“更难”形成转折，用 but。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把照片给父母看。show sth. to sb. 向某人展示某物。故选 B。
