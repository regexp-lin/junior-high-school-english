# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工石膏模型

## 完形填空原文

During the art festival last month, our school club decided ___1___ a DIY plaster model workshop. I had never tried anything ___2___ than paper cutting, so I was excited but worried.

The instructor first showed us ___3___ to mix water and plaster powder safely. She warned us ___4___ touch the mixture with bare hands for too long. While the plaster ___5___ hard, we used small tools to smooth the surface. My hands shook at the beginning, ___6___ I broke one corner by accident.

___7___ of giving up, I asked the teacher for help. She taught me ___8___ to fix tiny cracks with a little extra paste. Two hours later, my little rabbit model ___9___ on the shelf to dry. When I took it home, my parents said it looked ___10___ a real toy from a shop.

## 选项

1. A. hold &emsp;&emsp; B. to hold &emsp;&emsp; C. holding &emsp;&emsp; D. held  
2. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. more difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. most difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. the most difficult  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
4. A. not to &emsp;&emsp; B. to not &emsp;&emsp; C. don't &emsp;&emsp; D. not  
5. A. gets &emsp;&emsp; B. got &emsp;&emsp; C. was getting &emsp;&emsp; D. has got  
6. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until  
7. A. Instead &emsp;&emsp; B. Because &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. where  
9. A. places &emsp;&emsp; B. was placed &emsp;&emsp; C. is placing &emsp;&emsp; D. has placed  
10. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide 后接动词不定式作宾语，decide to hold 表示“决定举办”。decide doing 非标准搭配；decide hold 缺少 to。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than）  
**【解析】** 句中有 than paper cutting，应用比较级 more difficult。原级 difficult 不能与 than 搭配；最高级需有范围或 the。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”  
**【解析】** show us how to mix 表示“向我们演示如何混合”。what to mix 偏重“混合什么”；why/when 与演示操作步骤不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** warn 的用法（warn sb. not to do）  
**【解析】** warn sb. not to do sth. 为固定结构，意为“警告某人不要做某事”。to not touch 语序错误；don't touch 不能作 warned 的直接宾语补足语结构。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去进行时  
**【解析】** While 引导时间状语从句，强调“在石膏正在变硬的过程中”，主从句均为过去背景，用 was getting 表示过去进行。一般现在时 gets 时态不符；一般过去 got 不强调过程。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 状语从句连词（因果关系）  
**【解析】** “手抖”导致“不小心弄破一角”，用 because 引导原因状语从句。though 表让步；unless 表条件；until 表“直到”。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定短语 instead of  
**【解析】** Instead of giving up 意为“没有放弃而是……”，符合语境。Because/Although/Unless 后接从句，不接 of doing。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** teach me how to fix 表示“教我如何修补”。what to fix 若选则偏重“修什么”；which/where 与修补方法不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** 模型“被放在架子上晾干”，主语 model 与 place 为被动关系，且为过去发生的事，用 was placed。主动语态 places/is placing/has placed 均不合理。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 like 的用法  
**【解析】** look like 意为“看起来像”。look as 需接从句；look for 表寻找；look with 搭配不当。故选 B。
