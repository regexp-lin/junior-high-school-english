# 中考英语完形填空：假期骑自行车

## 完形填空原文

My neighbor, Uncle Zhang, is a cycling fan. During the National Day holiday, he suggested ___1___ a bike ride along the river with him. I immediately said yes.

We set off at seven in the morning. The air was fresh and the road was ___2___ empty because most people were still sleeping. Uncle Zhang rode ahead and I ___3___ closely behind. He told me to pay attention to the traffic lights and always stay on the right side.

After riding for about half an hour, we reached a beautiful park ___4___ the river. The autumn leaves were red and golden, and the view was breathtaking. We stopped to rest and took many photos. I ___5___ so happy that I posted some pictures online right away.

On the way back, the wind became ___6___. It was difficult to ride against it. Uncle Zhang encouraged me by saying, "Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your ___7___, you must keep moving." I remembered this was a famous quote ___8___ Albert Einstein.

We returned home at about ten o'clock. ___9___ I was tired, I felt full of energy inside. I made a plan to ride ___10___ Uncle Zhang every weekend from then on.

## 选项

1. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. took &emsp;&emsp; C. to take &emsp;&emsp; D. taking  
2. A. almost &emsp;&emsp; B. already &emsp;&emsp; C. always &emsp;&emsp; D. also  
3. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. follows &emsp;&emsp; C. followed &emsp;&emsp; D. following  
4. A. between &emsp;&emsp; B. beside &emsp;&emsp; C. behind &emsp;&emsp; D. below  
5. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. felt &emsp;&emsp; C. feeling &emsp;&emsp; D. to feel  
6. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. strongly &emsp;&emsp; C. stronger &emsp;&emsp; D. strongest  
7. A. health &emsp;&emsp; B. promise &emsp;&emsp; C. balance &emsp;&emsp; D. interest  
8. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
9. A. Because &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. If  
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（suggest doing）  
**【解析】** suggest doing sth. 意为"建议做某事"，后接动名词。take 为原形；took 为过去式；to take 为不定式，suggest 后不能接不定式。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词辨析  
**【解析】** almost empty 意为"几乎空无一人"，almost 修饰形容词 empty 表程度。already（已经）表时间；always（总是）表频率；also（也）表补充，均不修饰 empty 的程度。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，and 连接两个并列谓语 rode 和 followed，时态应一致。follow 为原形；follows 为三单现在时；following 为现在分词，不能单独作谓语。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（方位）  
**【解析】** a beautiful park beside the river 意为"河边一个美丽的公园"，beside 表"在旁边"。between 意为"在两者之间"；behind 意为"在后面"；below 意为"在下方"，均不符合公园在河边的位置关系。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，此处应用过去式 felt。feel 为原形；feeling 为现在分词；to feel 为不定式，均不能作主句谓语。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 系动词 become + 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** became 为系动词，表示风变得"更强了"（与之前对比），用比较级 stronger。strong 为原级，不体现变化；strongly 为副词；strongest 为最高级，无比较范围。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 名词辨析（语境推理）  
**【解析】** 这是爱因斯坦的名言"骑自行车要保持平衡就必须不断前进"，balance（平衡）符合原义。health（健康）、promise（承诺）、interest（兴趣）均不符合原话含义。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 by 表作者  
**【解析】** a famous quote by Albert Einstein 意为"爱因斯坦的名言"，by 表示作品或言论的作者。in/of/at 均不表示此义。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 让步状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "虽然很累，但内心充满能量"，前后为让步关系，用 although。because 表原因；unless 表条件"除非"；if 表条件"如果"，均不符合。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表伴随  
**【解析】** ride with Uncle Zhang 意为"和张叔叔一起骑车"，with 表伴随。to 表方向；for 表目的或时间段；of 表所属，均不如 with 自然。故选 C。
