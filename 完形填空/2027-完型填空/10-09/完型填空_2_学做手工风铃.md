# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工风铃

## 完形填空原文

Our neighbor Mrs. Wang is good at making handicrafts. Last Sunday, she taught me and my sister ___1___ to make a wind chime using old keys, buttons, and strings.

She said the first step was ___2___ the materials by size. Big keys would hang at the bottom, and small buttons would go at the top. I found it interesting because I had never thought old things could ___3___ so beautifully.

My sister chose a wooden stick as the frame. She tied several strings to it and ___4___ them hang down at different lengths. I was responsible for tying keys and buttons ___5___ each string. It took great patience because the knots had to be tight enough.

While ___6___, Mrs. Wang told us stories about how she learned this skill from her grandmother years ago. She said making things by hand could help people relax and forget ___7___ worries for a while.

When a soft breeze came through the window, our wind chime began to sing. The sound was ___8___ gentle that everyone in the room smiled. My sister was so excited that she wanted to make ___9___ one for her best friend.

Mrs. Wang praised us and said, "You two are the ___10___ students I've ever taught." We all laughed happily.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
2. A. sort &emsp;&emsp; B. sorted &emsp;&emsp; C. sorting &emsp;&emsp; D. to sort  
3. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. used &emsp;&emsp; C. be using &emsp;&emsp; D. be used  
4. A. let &emsp;&emsp; B. lets &emsp;&emsp; C. letting &emsp;&emsp; D. to let  
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
6. A. work &emsp;&emsp; B. worked &emsp;&emsp; C. working &emsp;&emsp; D. to work  
7. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs  
8. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. quite &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. another &emsp;&emsp; C. the other &emsp;&emsp; D. others  
10. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quicker &emsp;&emsp; C. quickest &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** "疑问词 + 不定式"作宾语  
**【解析】** teach sb. how to do sth. 意为"教某人怎样做某事"，how to make 作 taught 的直接宾语。what to make 侧重"做什么"；where/when 与教授制作方法的语境不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 不定式作表语  
**【解析】** "the first step was to sort" 中不定式 to sort 作表语，说明第一步的内容。sort 为原形；sorted 为过去式；sorting 虽可作表语但此处强调"要做的动作"，用不定式更恰当。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 被动语态（情态动词 + be + 过去分词）  
**【解析】** old things 与 use 之间为被动关系，意为"旧东西能被使用得如此漂亮"，用 could be used。use 为主动；used 缺 be；be using 为主动进行时。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 使役动词 let  
**【解析】** let sb./sth. do 意为"让……做"，let them hang down 让绳子垂下。and 连接 tied 和 let，均为过去式（let 的过去式与原形同形）。lets 为三单现在时；letting 为现在分词；to let 为不定式，均不平行。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 to 表方向  
**【解析】** tie...to... 意为"把……绑到……上"，to 表示目标方向。in 表在里面；on 表在表面上但 tie on 不如 tie to 常用于绑到绳子上；at 表在某点。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 省略主语的时间状语从句（while doing）  
**【解析】** while working 为省略了主语和 be 动词的时间状语从句（= while we were working）。work 为原形；worked 为过去式，不能跟在 while 后构成省略结构；to work 为不定式。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** 修饰名词 worries 需用形容词性物主代词 their。they 为主格；them 为宾格；theirs 为名词性物主代词，后不能再接名词。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** so...that... 结果状语从句  
**【解析】** so + adj. + that 引导结果状语从句，意为"如此……以至于"。very/quite 不与 that 从句搭配；too 常与 to 连用（too...to...）。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 another  
**【解析】** another one 意为"另一个"，表在已有基础上再做一个。other 需接复数名词或用 the other 表特指另一个；the other 指两者中的另一个（此处无明确两者）；others 为代词不与 one 连用。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** "the + 最高级 + n. + I've ever taught" 为固定最高级句型。quickest 为 quick 的最高级形式。quick 为原级；quicker 为比较级；quickly 为副词，不修饰名词 students。故选 C。
