# 中考英语完形填空：检查暑假作业

## 完形填空原文

The night before school started, I spread all my homework ___1___ the desk and checked each page. Math problems ___2___ I thought were easy still had two careless mistakes. English exercises ___3___ were copied from the workbook needed neater handwriting.

Dad sat beside me and said, "It is better to find mistakes ___4___ than in class." His words made me less worried. I corrected the errors and put the papers ___5___ a folder ___6___ I would not lose them. Then I packed my schoolbag ___7___ the timetable Mom printed for me.

When everything was ready, I took a deep breath and told myself ___8___ I had done my best. I also set an alarm clock ___9___ I could get up early. That night I fell asleep ___10___ than usual because my mind was finally quiet.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
3. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
4. A. now &emsp;&emsp; B. late &emsp;&emsp; C. later &emsp;&emsp; D. early
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
6. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. but
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how
9. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
10. A. earlier &emsp;&emsp; B. later &emsp;&emsp; C. more slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. worst

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把所有作业摊在书桌上。on the desk 在桌面上。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以为很容易的数学题仍有两处粗心错误。先行词 problems 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：从练习册抄写的英语练习需要更工整的书写。先行词 exercises 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在发现错误总比在课堂上发现好。与 in class 对比，用 now。later/early 不如 now 与 in class 形成直接对照；late 词性不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把试卷放进文件夹。in a folder 在文件夹里。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：以免弄丢。so that 表目的“以便”。although/unless/until 不符合。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我把书包连同妈妈打印的课程表一起收拾好。with 表示“带着、连同”。and 为连词，连接结构不如 with 自然。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我告诉自己我已经尽力了。tell oneself that… 故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我设了闹钟以便早起。so that 表目的。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：那晚我比平时更早入睡，因为心里终于踏实了。暗含与往常对比，用 earlier。later 相反；more slowly 与语境矛盾；worst 为最高级且搭配不当。故选 A。
