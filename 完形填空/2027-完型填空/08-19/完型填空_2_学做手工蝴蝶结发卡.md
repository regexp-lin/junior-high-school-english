# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工蝴蝶结发卡

## 完形填空原文

My cousin gave me a DIY kit ___1___ could be used to make bow hair clips. The instructions were simple, ___2___ my hands shook a little when I tied the ribbon. "Pull it tight, ___3___ the bow will fall apart," she said patiently.

We cut two pieces of ribbon and crossed them ___4___ the middle. A small rubber band held the center ___5___ the glue dried. When I clipped it onto my hair, the mirror showed a girl ___6___ looked happier than an hour before.

The bow was not perfect, ___7___ it was made by me. I decided ___8___ give the second clip to my mom as a small gift. She hugged me and said handmade things ___9___ always remembered because they carry love. That night I practiced tying bows again ___10___ I could improve my skills.

2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
5. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. what
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
8. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. will be &emsp;&emsp; D. have been
10. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. unless

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐送我一个可用来做蝴蝶结发卡的 DIY 套装。先行词 kit 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：说明很简单，但我系丝带时手有点抖。转折用 but。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词（否则）
**【解析】** 本句意为：拉紧点，否则会散开。or 表“否则”。and 并列；but 转折；so 结果。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在中间交叉。in the middle 在中间。on/at/by 不如 in 常见。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词（until）
**【解析】** 本句意为：用小皮筋固定中心直到胶水干透。until 表示“直到……为止”。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：镜子里映出一个比一小时前更快乐的女孩。先行词 girl 指人，从句缺主语，用 who。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：蝴蝶结并不完美，但它是我做的。转折用 but。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定把第二个发卡送给妈妈。decide to do sth. 决定做某事。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 被动语态与时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工作品总会被记住，因为它们带着爱。表示一种普遍看法，用一般将来时 will be remembered 表“终将被铭记”。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：那晚我又练习系蝴蝶结，以便提高技巧。so that 表目的。故选 C。
