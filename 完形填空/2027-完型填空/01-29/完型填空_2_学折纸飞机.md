# 中考英语完形填空：学折纸飞机

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, my older brother decided to ___1___ me how to fold a paper plane that could fly far. We cleared the dining table and ___2___ some colorful sheets from my art box.

The first few models crashed ___3___ they left my hand. I almost ___4___, but my brother said small changes in the wings could make a big ___5___. We tried again and again ___6___ lunch.

He told me to press the creases ___7___ and to keep the nose sharp. When a plane finally sailed across the room and landed ___8___ on the sofa, we shouted with excitement. That afternoon I kept practicing ___9___ the sky outside turned grey. I wrote down each successful ___10___ in my notebook like a tiny engineer.

## 选项

1. A. ask &emsp;&emsp; B. teach &emsp;&emsp; C. send &emsp;&emsp; D. invite
2. A. bought &emsp;&emsp; B. borrowed &emsp;&emsp; C. took &emsp;&emsp; D. made
3. A. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. gave up &emsp;&emsp; B. woke up &emsp;&emsp; C. looked up &emsp;&emsp; D. grew up
5. A. mistake &emsp;&emsp; B. difference &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. surprise
6. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. during &emsp;&emsp; D. until
7. A. loudly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. heavily
8. A. hardly &emsp;&emsp; B. softly &emsp;&emsp; C. nearly &emsp;&emsp; D. badly
9. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. because
10. A. step &emsp;&emsp; B. design &emsp;&emsp; C. dream &emsp;&emsp; D. joke

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：哥哥决定教我折能飞得很远的纸飞机。teach sb. how to do sth. 符合语境。A 项 ask 询问；C 项 send 派遣；D 项 invite 邀请。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们从我的美术盒里拿出几张彩纸。take...from... 表示“从……取出”。A 项 bought 购买；B 项 borrowed 借；D 项 made 制作，与 from my art box 不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：前几架飞机一离手就坠毁。as soon as 表示“一……就……”。B 项 until 直到；C 项 although 尽管；D 项 unless 除非。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我差点放弃。give up 放弃。B 项 wake up 醒来；C 项 look up 查阅/抬头；D 项 grow up 长大。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：机翼的微小改动也能带来很大不同。make a big difference 为固定搭配。A 项 mistake 错误；C 项 problem 问题；D 项 surprise 惊喜。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一遍又一遍尝试直到午饭时间。until 表示“直到……为止”。A 项 before 在……之前；B 项 after 在……之后；C 项 during 在……期间，不如 until 能体现持续练习到某个时间点。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他让我把折痕压紧。press...carefully 表示“小心地按压”。A 项 loudly 大声地；B 项 quickly 快速地；D 项 heavily 重重地，均不如 carefully 符合折纸细致操作。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：飞机终于飞过房间并轻轻落在沙发上。softly 表示“轻轻地”。A 项 hardly 几乎不；C 项 nearly 几乎；D 项 badly 糟糕地。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直练习直到外面的天色变灰。until 引导时间状语从句。A 项 when 当……时；B 项 if 如果；D 项 because 因为。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我像小工程师一样把每一次成功的折法设计记下来。design 可指“设计方案/折法”。A 项 step 步骤，虽可通，但 each successful design 更概括一次完整尝试；C 项 dream 梦想；D 项 joke 玩笑。故选 B。
