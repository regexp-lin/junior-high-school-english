# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工树叶画

## 完形填空原文

Autumn is the best season for collecting fallen leaves. Our biology teacher ___1___ us to use them to create beautiful leaf paintings.

First, she took us to the school garden to gather leaves of different shapes and ___2___. I picked maple leaves in red and orange, ginkgo leaves in bright yellow, and some small oval leaves in dark green.

Back in the classroom, she showed us how to press the leaves flat. She put them between two sheets of newspaper and placed heavy books ___3___ top. "We need to wait for at least two days," she said. "If the leaves are not completely ___4___, they will curl up and your painting won't look good."

Two days later, we started our artwork. The teacher gave us white cardboard as the background. She suggested that we plan our design ___5___ gluing anything down.

I decided to make a picture of a girl ___6___ under a tree. I used a large green leaf as the tree crown and a thin stick as the trunk. For the girl, I ___7___ small leaves into the shape of a dress and added a tiny round leaf as her face.

The final step was gluing everything onto the cardboard. I was very careful ___8___ each leaf. When I held up my finished work, the teacher said it told a beautiful story ___9___ any words.

She encouraged us to frame our leaf paintings and hang them on the wall. "Nature gives us the ___10___ materials," she said. "We just need to use our imagination."

## 选项

1. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. teaching &emsp;&emsp; D. teaches  
2. A. color &emsp;&emsp; B. colors &emsp;&emsp; C. colorful &emsp;&emsp; D. colorfully  
3. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
4. A. dry &emsp;&emsp; B. drier &emsp;&emsp; C. driest &emsp;&emsp; D. drying  
5. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. since  
6. A. stand &emsp;&emsp; B. stood &emsp;&emsp; C. standing &emsp;&emsp; D. stands  
7. A. cut &emsp;&emsp; B. cuts &emsp;&emsp; C. cutting &emsp;&emsp; D. to cut  
8. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of  
9. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. without &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，taught 为 teach 的过去式。teach 为原形；teaching 为现在分词；teaches 为三单。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词复数  
**【解析】** different shapes and colors 意为"不同的形状和颜色"，shapes 为复数，并列的 colors 也用复数。color 为单数；colorful 为形容词；colorfully 为副词。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 on top  
**【解析】** placed heavy books on top 意为"把重书放在上面"，on top 为固定搭配表"在顶部"。at/in/by 不构成此搭配。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** completely dry 意为"完全干透"，dry 为形容词作表语。drier 为比较级；driest 为最高级；drying 为现在分词。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 before + 动名词  
**【解析】** plan our design before gluing 意为"在粘贴之前先规划设计"，before 表时间先后。after 意为"之后"语序不对；when 表"当……时"；since 表"自从"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作后置定语  
**【解析】** a girl standing under a tree 意为"一个站在树下的女孩"，standing 为现在分词作后置定语。stand 为原形；stood 为过去式；stands 为三单。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，cut 的过去式与原形同形。此处 cut...into the shape 为过去式。cuts 为三单现在时；cutting 为现在分词；to cut 为不定式。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be careful with  
**【解析】** was very careful with each leaf 意为"对每片叶子都很小心"，careful with 表"小心对待"。about 表"关于"；for 表"为了"；of 不与 careful 搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 without 表否定  
**【解析】** told a beautiful story without any words 意为"不用任何文字就讲述了美丽的故事"，without 表"没有"。with 意为"用"；by 表方式；for 表目的。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** the best materials 意为"最好的材料"，the + best 为固定最高级形式。good 为原级；well 作形容词表"身体好"；better 为比较级。故选 D。
