# 中考英语完形填空：写日记记录秋天

## 完形填空原文

Our Chinese teacher once said, "The best way to remember life is to write it down." ___1___ by her words, I started keeping a diary this autumn.

Every evening after finishing my homework, I would spend about fifteen minutes ___2___ about what happened during the day. At first, I didn't know what to write. My daily life seemed too ___3___ to be interesting.

But gradually, I discovered beauty in small things. One day, I wrote about a golden leaf that ___4___ onto my shoulder on the way to school. Another day, I described the smell of roasted sweet potatoes from a street vendor. These little moments made my diary ___5___ and vivid.

My favorite entry was about a rainy afternoon. I sat by the window and watched the raindrops ___6___ down the glass. The world outside looked blurry and dreamlike. I wrote, "Rain is nature's way of washing the world clean so that everything can start ___7___."

After one month, I was surprised to find that I had written over thirty entries. Reading them again, I ___8___ that autumn was not just a season of falling leaves. It was also a season full of warmth, beauty, and quiet joy.

My teacher read some of my entries and said they were ___9___ written. She encouraged me to keep going. I plan to continue this habit ___10___ I grow up. A diary is like a time machine that can take you back to any moment you want to revisit.

## 选项

1. A. Inspire &emsp;&emsp; B. Inspired &emsp;&emsp; C. Inspiring &emsp;&emsp; D. Inspires  
2. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. wrote &emsp;&emsp; C. writing &emsp;&emsp; D. written  
3. A. ordinary &emsp;&emsp; B. ordinarily &emsp;&emsp; C. order &emsp;&emsp; D. ordered  
4. A. fall &emsp;&emsp; B. fell &emsp;&emsp; C. fallen &emsp;&emsp; D. falls  
5. A. color &emsp;&emsp; B. colorful &emsp;&emsp; C. colorfully &emsp;&emsp; D. colors  
6. A. slide &emsp;&emsp; B. slid &emsp;&emsp; C. sliding &emsp;&emsp; D. to slide  
7. A. fresh &emsp;&emsp; B. freshly &emsp;&emsp; C. fresher &emsp;&emsp; D. freshest  
8. A. realize &emsp;&emsp; B. realized &emsp;&emsp; C. realizing &emsp;&emsp; D. realizes  
9. A. beauty &emsp;&emsp; B. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. beautifully &emsp;&emsp; D. more beautiful  
10. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作状语  
**【解析】** Inspired by her words 意为"受到她的话启发"，主语 I 与 inspire 为被动关系，用过去分词作原因状语。Inspire 为原形；Inspiring 为现在分词（主动）；Inspires 为三单。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定句型 spend time doing  
**【解析】** spend fifteen minutes writing 为固定搭配。write 为原形；wrote 为过去式；written 为过去分词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** too ordinary to be interesting 意为"太普通了以至于没什么趣味"，ordinary 为形容词。ordinarily 为副词；order 为名词/动词；ordered 为"有秩序的"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** that fell onto my shoulder 为定语从句中的谓语，全文为过去叙述用过去式。fall 为原形；fallen 为过去分词；falls 为三单现在时。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词作宾补  
**【解析】** made my diary colorful and vivid 意为"使我的日记丰富多彩而生动"，colorful 为形容词作宾补。color 为名词；colorfully 为副词；colors 为名词复数。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 感官动词 watch + 宾语 + 动词原形  
**【解析】** watched the raindrops slide down 意为"看着雨滴滑落"，watch 后接宾语 + 动词原形表完整过程。slid 为过去式；sliding 为现在分词（强调正在进行也可）；to slide 不用于感官动词后。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作副词用法/start fresh  
**【解析】** start fresh 意为"重新开始"，fresh 在此为形容词作结果补语。freshly 为副词；fresher/freshest 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，realized 为过去式。realize 为原形；realizing 为现在分词；realizes 为三单。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰过去分词  
**【解析】** beautifully written 意为"写得很优美"，beautifully 为副词修饰过去分词 written。beauty 为名词；beautiful 为形容词；more beautiful 为比较级。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词 until  
**【解析】** continue this habit until I grow up 意为"持续这个习惯直到我长大"，until 表"直到"。after 表"之后"；before 表"之前"；unless 表"除非"。故选 C。
