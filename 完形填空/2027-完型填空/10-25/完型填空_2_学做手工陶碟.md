# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工陶碟

## 完形填空原文

During the school's cultural week, a pottery master named Uncle Chen came to teach us how to make ceramic dishes. I had always been ___1___ in pottery, so I was thrilled when I heard the news.

Uncle Chen set up a small pottery wheel in the art room. He placed a lump of wet clay on it and started spinning. His hands moved ___2___ over the clay, and within minutes, a beautiful little dish appeared. We all watched in amazement.

"The secret is to let your hands feel the clay," he told us. "Don't think too much. Just ___3___ your hands guide you." His words sounded simple, but I soon found out that doing it was ___4___ different story.

When it was my turn, I sat at the wheel and placed my hands on the spinning clay. It felt cold and slippery. I tried to push it into a dish shape, but the clay kept ___5___ to one side. Uncle Chen came over and gently ___6___ my hands to the right position.

With his help, I ___7___ shaped a small dish. It wasn't perfectly round, but Uncle Chen said that handmade items don't need to be perfect. "The small imperfections are ___8___ make each piece unique," he explained.

After the dishes were shaped, Uncle Chen told us they needed to dry for two days ___9___ they could be baked. He promised to bring the finished dishes back to us next week.

I can't wait to see my dish after ___10___ baked. This experience opened my eyes to the beauty of traditional crafts.

## 选项

1. A. interest &emsp;&emsp; B. interested &emsp;&emsp; C. interesting &emsp;&emsp; D. interests  
2. A. skill &emsp;&emsp; B. skillful &emsp;&emsp; C. skillfully &emsp;&emsp; D. skills  
3. A. let &emsp;&emsp; B. lets &emsp;&emsp; C. letting &emsp;&emsp; D. to let  
4. A. a completely &emsp;&emsp; B. completely a &emsp;&emsp; C. complete a &emsp;&emsp; D. a complete  
5. A. lean &emsp;&emsp; B. leaned &emsp;&emsp; C. leaning &emsp;&emsp; D. to lean  
6. A. adjust &emsp;&emsp; B. adjusts &emsp;&emsp; C. adjusted &emsp;&emsp; D. adjusting  
7. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. succeed &emsp;&emsp; C. successful &emsp;&emsp; D. successfully  
8. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
9. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. since  
10. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. being &emsp;&emsp; C. been &emsp;&emsp; D. is  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be interested in  
**【解析】** be interested in 意为"对……感兴趣"，interested 修饰人的感受。interest 为名词/动词；interesting 意为"有趣的"修饰事物；interests 为名词复数。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** moved skillfully 意为"熟练地移动"，修饰动词 moved 用副词。skill 为名词；skillful 为形容词；skills 为名词复数。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 使役动词 let  
**【解析】** let your hands guide you 为祈使句，let + 宾语 + 动词原形。lets 为三单；letting 为现在分词；to let 为不定式。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 冠词与副词的位置  
**【解析】** a completely different story 意为"一个完全不同的故事"，正确语序为"a + 副词 + 形容词 + 名词"。completely a 语序错误；complete a 中 complete 为形容词不修饰 different；a complete 缺副词。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（keep doing）  
**【解析】** kept leaning 意为"一直往一边倾斜"，keep doing sth. 表"持续做某事"。lean 为原形；leaned 为过去式；to lean 为不定式。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** and 连接 came over 和 adjusted，时态一致用过去式。adjust 为原形；adjusts 为三单；adjusting 为现在分词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** successfully shaped 意为"成功地做出了形状"，修饰动词 shaped 用副词。success 为名词；succeed 为动词；successful 为形容词。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 表语从句连接词  
**【解析】** are what make each piece unique 意为"是使每件作品独特的（东西）"，what 引导表语从句并在从句中作主语。that 在表语从句中不作成分；which 需有选择范围；how 不作主语。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "干两天后才能烧制"，before they could be baked 意为"在它们被烧制之前"。after 意为"之后"语义相反；when 表"当……时"；since 表"自从"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 after + 动名词被动  
**【解析】** after being baked 意为"被烧制之后"，after 为介词后接动名词，dishes 与 bake 为被动关系用 being baked。be 为原形；been 为过去分词；is 为现在时。故选 B。
