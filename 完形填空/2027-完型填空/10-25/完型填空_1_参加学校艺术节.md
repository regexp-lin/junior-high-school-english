# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校艺术节

## 完形填空原文

Every October, our school holds an art festival. It is one of the events that students ___1___ forward to the most. This year, my class decided to perform a short play.

We spent two weeks ___2___ for the performance. Our Chinese teacher helped us choose a classic story and adapted it into a script. I was given the role of a wise old man, ___3___ surprised me because I had never acted before.

Rehearsals were held every afternoon after school. At first, I found it ___4___ to remember all my lines. I kept forgetting the words at critical moments. My classmate, Liu Yang, who played the main character, was very patient. He practiced with me again and again ___5___ I got it right.

On the day of the festival, the auditorium was packed with students and teachers. When the curtain rose, my heart was beating ___6___. But once I stepped onto the stage, something magical happened — I became the character.

Our play lasted about fifteen minutes. When it ended, the audience gave us a standing ovation. I felt so proud that tears almost ___7___ down my face.

___8___ of our hard work, we won the first prize. The head teacher said, "Your performance has shown that teamwork can create ___9___." I will never forget this experience because it taught me to be ___10___ and try new things.

## 选项

1. A. look &emsp;&emsp; B. looks &emsp;&emsp; C. looked &emsp;&emsp; D. looking  
2. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. prepared &emsp;&emsp; C. preparing &emsp;&emsp; D. to prepare  
3. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
4. A. difficulty &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. difficultly &emsp;&emsp; D. difficulties  
5. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. since &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
6. A. fast &emsp;&emsp; B. slow &emsp;&emsp; C. quick &emsp;&emsp; D. quiet  
7. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. ran &emsp;&emsp; C. running &emsp;&emsp; D. runs  
8. A. Instead &emsp;&emsp; B. Because &emsp;&emsp; C. In spite &emsp;&emsp; D. As a result  
9. A. miracle &emsp;&emsp; B. miracles &emsp;&emsp; C. miraculous &emsp;&emsp; D. miraculously  
10. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. braver &emsp;&emsp; C. bravely &emsp;&emsp; D. bravest  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 一般现在时（定语从句中）  
**【解析】** 描述每年的常规事件用一般现在时，students look forward to 为习惯性动作。looks 为三单但 students 为复数；looked 为过去式；looking 为现在分词。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定句型 spend...doing  
**【解析】** spent two weeks preparing 为 spend time doing sth. 结构。prepare 为原形；prepared 为过去式；to prepare 为不定式。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句  
**【解析】** 先行词为前面整个句子"给我分配了智者的角色"，从句缺主语，用 which。that 不引导非限制性定语从句；who 指人；what 不引导定语从句。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** find it + adj. + to do  
**【解析】** found it difficult to remember 为固定句型，it 为形式宾语，difficult 为形容词。difficulty 为名词；difficultly 不存在；difficulties 为名词复数。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词 until  
**【解析】** "一直练到我做对为止"，until 意为"直到"。after 意为"之后"；since 意为"自从"；unless 意为"除非"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词 fast 修饰动词  
**【解析】** beating fast 意为"心跳加速"，fast 既可作形容词也可作副词修饰 beating。slow 意为"慢的"与紧张不符；quick 多作形容词；quiet 意为"安静的"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，tears almost ran down 为过去式。run 为原形；running 为现在分词；runs 为三单。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 短语辨析  
**【解析】** As a result of our hard work 意为"由于我们的努力"，as a result of 表原因。instead 意为"相反"；because 后接从句不接 of；in spite 需接 of 表"尽管"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词复数  
**【解析】** create miracles 意为"创造奇迹"，miracles 为可数名词复数。miracle 为单数缺冠词；miraculous 为形容词；miraculously 为副词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** to be brave 意为"勇敢的"，be 后接形容词。braver 为比较级（无比较对象）；bravely 为副词；bravest 为最高级。故选 A。
