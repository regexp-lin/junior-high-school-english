# 中考英语完形填空：旧物改造手工课

## 完形填空原文

Our craft teacher brought a box of "waste treasures" to class: empty bottles, old jeans, and broken buttons. She said we could ___1___ them into useful things if we used imagination ___2___ care.

My partner and I chose a plastic bottle. We cut the ___3___ part off and painted the bottom with blue waves. Then we punched small holes ___4___ the edge and wove string through them to make a simple pen holder.

Another group turned an old T-shirt ___5___ a cloth bag. The classroom was filled ___6___ the sound of scissors and laughter. The teacher walked around, giving tips ___7___ of grades.

At the end of the lesson, we displayed our work on the windowsill. I felt surprised ___8___ how colorful rubbish could look with a second life. Mom said my pen holder was ___9___ than the ones in shops because it carried my effort.

That project changed my idea ___10___ waste. Now I think twice before throwing things away.

## 选项

1. A. throw &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. divide &emsp;&emsp; D. hide
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. bottom &emsp;&emsp; B. top &emsp;&emsp; C. middle &emsp;&emsp; D. side
4. A. along &emsp;&emsp; B. against &emsp;&emsp; C. beyond &emsp;&emsp; D. through
5. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. off
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. about
7. A. instead &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
8. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. about
9. A. cheaper &emsp;&emsp; B. more special &emsp;&emsp; C. heavier &emsp;&emsp; D. louder
10. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. across &emsp;&emsp; D. against

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说只要我们用心想象并细心制作，就能把这些东西变成有用的物品。change ... into ... 把……变成……；throw 扔；divide 分开；hide 藏。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：……如果我们同时运用想象力与细心。imagination 与 care 为并列积极因素，用 and 连接。or 表选择；but 转折；so 结果。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们切掉瓶子的上部，并把底部涂成蓝色波浪。做笔筒通常保留底部，切掉上部 top。bottom 底部；middle 中部；side 侧面。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们在边缘沿边打了一些小孔并用线穿过。along the edge 沿着边缘；against 靠着；beyond 超过；through 穿过（孔内穿过可用 through，但 punch holes along edge 更常见）。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：另一组把旧 T 恤改成了布袋。turn ... into ... 把……变成……。turn up/over/off 不符合语境。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：教室里充满了剪刀声和笑声。be filled with 为固定搭配，意为"充满……"。of/from/about 不与 be filled 构成此义。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师在教室里走动，给技巧指导而不是打分。instead of 代替、而不是；because of 因为；although 尽管；unless 除非。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我惊讶地发现垃圾经过第二次生命可以变得如此多彩。be surprised at ... 对……感到惊讶。surprised by 也可，选项给 at。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说我的笔筒比商店里的更特别，因为它承载了我的努力。A 项 cheaper 更便宜；B 项 more special 更特别；C 项 heavier 更重；D 项 louder 更响。强调亲手制作的意义。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个项目改变了我对废品的看法。idea about ... 关于……的想法；idea into/across/against 不自然。故选 A。
