# 中考英语完形填空：帮邻居铲雪

## 完形填空原文

After a night of heavy snow, our neighborhood looked like a quiet white painting. My dad asked me to help ___1___ the steps in front of our building. While we worked, I noticed Mrs. Lin, an elderly neighbor, struggling ___2___ her shovel.

Dad walked over and offered help ___3___. Mrs. Lin's shoulders relaxed, and she thanked us with a warm smile. Dad said neighbors should look ___4___ one another, especially on cold days.

We cleared her doorway first so she could walk ___5___ safely. The wind made my fingers numb, ___6___ sharing the work made time pass faster. Mrs. Lin brought out hot tea and told stories ___7___ her student years in the north.

When the path was finally ___8___, she insisted on taking a photo with us. That picture is still ___9___ my phone, reminding me that small kindness can melt winter's ___10___ edges.

## 选项

1. A. draw &emsp;&emsp; B. clear &emsp;&emsp; C. paint &emsp;&emsp; D. lock
2. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
3. A. politely &emsp;&emsp; B. secretly &emsp;&emsp; C. angrily &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
4. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. through
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. up
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. if
7. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. across &emsp;&emsp; D. against
8. A. dirty &emsp;&emsp; B. empty &emsp;&emsp; C. wide &emsp;&emsp; D. clean
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
10. A. soft &emsp;&emsp; B. sharp &emsp;&emsp; C. low &emsp;&emsp; D. free

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸让我帮忙清理楼前的台阶。A 项 draw 画；B 项 clear 清理、清除；C 项 paint 涂；D 项 lock 锁。铲雪清理通道用 clear。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看见林奶奶用铲子铲得很吃力。struggle with sth. 表示"做某事很吃力、与……搏斗"。struggle for 为……奋斗；against 对抗；beyond 超出。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸走过去礼貌地提出帮忙。A 项 politely 礼貌地；B 项 secretly 秘密地；C 项 angrily 生气地；D 项 carelessly 粗心地。邻里互助应礼貌。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说邻居之间应该互相关照。look after 照顾；look like 看起来像；look without 非固定；look through 浏览。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们先清理她家门口，让她能安全进出。walk in 走进；walk out 走出、外出；walk on 继续走；walk up 走上前。清理门口便于进出，out/in 都可能，先清门口让她能走出来也合理 - "so she could walk out safely" 出门安全。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：寒风让我的手指发麻，但一起干活让时间过得更快。前后转折：寒冷不适 vs 合作愉快。but 表转折；or 选择；so 结果；if 条件。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：林奶奶端出热茶，讲起她在北方求学年代的故事。tell stories about ... 讲述关于……的故事。into/across/against 不搭配 stories。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当小路终于清理干净时，她坚持要和我们合影。A 项 dirty 脏；B 项 empty 空；C 项 wide 宽；D 项 clean 干净。铲雪目标是把路弄干净。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那张照片仍保存在我的手机里。on my phone 在手机上（常用表达）；in my phone 也可用但 on 更常见于照片存储展示语境，初中题常用 on my phone。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：提醒我小小的善意也能融化冬天刺骨的棱角。sharp edges 比喻寒冷刺骨、尖锐的感受；soft 柔软；low 低；free 自由。与 winter 搭配，sharp 可表示凛冽。故选 B。
