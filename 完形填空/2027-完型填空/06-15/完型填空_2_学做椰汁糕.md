# 中考英语完形填空：学做椰汁糕

## 完形填空原文

During the weekend, I learned to make coconut milk cake with my aunt. It is ___1___ traditional dessert in Guangdong province.

We prepared coconut milk, sugar, cornstarch and water. My aunt ___2___ me to measure each ingredient carefully. "The right amount is the key ___3___ success," she said.

First, we divided the mixture ___4___ two parts. One part was white and the other was mixed with brown sugar to become darker. We heated each part on the stove ___5___ it became thick.

Then came the ___6___ interesting part. We poured one layer of white mixture into a square dish, waited for it to set, and then added a layer of brown mixture ___7___ top. We repeated this process until there were six layers.

After the cake cooled down ___8___, we cut it into diamond shapes. Each piece showed beautiful white and brown stripes. The cake felt ___9___ and bouncy in my mouth.

I was proud that my first try turned ___10___ so well. My aunt said I was a quick learner.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. tell &emsp;&emsp; B. tells &emsp;&emsp; C. told &emsp;&emsp; D. telling
3. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. in
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. after
6. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. to
8. A. complete &emsp;&emsp; B. completed &emsp;&emsp; C. completely &emsp;&emsp; D. completing
9. A. softly &emsp;&emsp; B. soft &emsp;&emsp; C. softer &emsp;&emsp; D. softest
10. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. out &emsp;&emsp; D. off

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它是广东省的一种传统甜点。traditional 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：阿姨让我仔细量好每种配料。上下文为过去时态，用 told。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（the key to 关键）
**【解析】** 本句意为：精确的量是成功的关键。"the key to"表示"……的关键"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（divide...into... 分成）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把混合物分成两部分。"divide...into..."表示"把……分成……"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（until 直到）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在炉子上加热每一部分直到它变浓稠。until 表示"直到"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（the most + 多音节形容词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后到了最有趣的部分。"the most interesting"为最高级。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（on top 在上面）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后在上面加一层棕色混合物。"on top"表示"在上面"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：糕完全冷却后。修饰动词 cooled down 用副词 completely。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 系动词 + 形容词（feel + adj.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：糕在嘴里感觉又软又弹。feel 为系动词，后接形容词 soft。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语（turn out 结果是）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很自豪第一次尝试结果这么好。"turn out"表示"结果是"。故选 C。
