# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校朗读比赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school held an English reading competition last Friday. Students from each class ___1___ invited to take part. I signed up because I wanted to challenge ___2___.

I chose a short story about friendship. Every evening, I practiced reading it ___3___ in my room. My mother listened and gave me some useful ___4___. She suggested that I should read more slowly and use more emotion.

On the day of the competition, I was the ___5___ student to go on stage. My heart was beating fast when I stood in front of the microphone. But as soon ___6___ I started reading, I forgot about the audience.

I read with feeling and even ___7___ my voice for different characters. The judges nodded their heads while ___8___. After I finished, the hall was quiet for a moment, and then everyone clapped.

I didn't win first prize, ___9___ I got a "Most Expressive Reader" award. I was very happy with the result. The experience taught me that practice really ___10___ a difference.

## 选项

1. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. are &emsp;&emsp; C. was &emsp;&emsp; D. were
2. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. myself
3. A. aloud &emsp;&emsp; B. loud &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. louder
4. A. advise &emsp;&emsp; B. advice &emsp;&emsp; C. advises &emsp;&emsp; D. advised
5. A. five &emsp;&emsp; B. fiveth &emsp;&emsp; C. fifth &emsp;&emsp; D. fively
6. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. for
7. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. changes &emsp;&emsp; C. changed &emsp;&emsp; D. changing
8. A. listen &emsp;&emsp; B. listened &emsp;&emsp; C. to listen &emsp;&emsp; D. listening
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
10. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. makes &emsp;&emsp; C. made &emsp;&emsp; D. making

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 被动语态（一般过去时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个班的学生都被邀请参加。主语 Students 为复数，last Friday 提示过去时，被动语态用 were invited。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 反身代词（challenge myself 挑战自己）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我报名因为我想挑战自己。主语为 I，用反身代词 myself。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（aloud 出声地）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天晚上我在房间里大声朗读。read aloud 表示"出声朗读"。loud 更强调音量大；aloud 强调出声。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词（advice 不可数名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈听了并给了我一些有用的建议。advice 为不可数名词，前可用 some 修饰。advise 为动词。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 序数词（fifth 第五）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我是第五个上台的学生。"the fifth"为序数词。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（as soon as 一……就……）
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我一开始朗读就忘了观众。"as soon as"表示"一……就……"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时（and 连接并列谓语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有感情地朗读，甚至为不同角色变换了声音。read 和 changed 并列，用过去时。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（while + doing 省略主语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：评委们一边听一边点头。"while listening"为省略主语的现在分词结构。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（but 转折）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我没有赢得一等奖，但获得了"最有表现力的朗读者"奖。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 主谓一致（第三人称单数）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次经历教会我练习真的有用。practice 为第三人称单数主语，谓语用 makes。一般现在时表真理。故选 B。
