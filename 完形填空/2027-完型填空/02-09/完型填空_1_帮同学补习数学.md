# 中考英语完形填空：帮同学补习数学

## 完形填空原文

My classmate Eric struggled with word problems in math. He asked ___1___ I could help him twice a week after school.

I agreed, ___2___ I was not the top student. I told him we would learn together. We started ___3___ easy questions about speed and time.

Sometimes Eric forgot a formula. Instead of telling him the answer directly, I guided him ___4___ the steps on scratch paper. Slowly, he learned ___5___ to translate sentences into equations.

Once he solved a difficult problem by himself. His eyes shone, and he said it felt ___6___ winning a small game. That made me happy, ___7___.

Before the monthly test, we reviewed mistakes in his notebook. Eric was still nervous, ___8___ he finished all the questions calmly.

His score improved by ten points. He thanked me with a note, ___9___ I thought the bigger change was his confidence. Helping others also made my own understanding ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. why
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. in
4. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. writing &emsp;&emsp; C. to write &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
6. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
7. A. also &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. either &emsp;&emsp; D. neither
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. deep &emsp;&emsp; B. deeper &emsp;&emsp; C. deepest &emsp;&emsp; D. deeply

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词（whether）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他问我能否每周放学后帮他两次。ask if/whether 表示“是否”。what 什么；how 如何；why 为什么。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（让步状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我同意了，尽管我不是尖子生。although 尽管。because 因为；if 如果；unless 除非。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（start with）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们从关于速度时间的简单题开始。start with 以……开始。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（guide sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我引导他在草稿纸上写下步骤。guide sb. to do sth. 用 to write。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他慢慢学会如何把文字叙述变成方程式。表示方式用 how。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说那感觉就像赢了一场小游戏。feel like 感觉像。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（too 用于肯定句句末）
**【解析】** 本句意为：那也让我很开心。肯定句句末表示“也”用 too。also 常放句中；either 用于否定；neither 表两者都不。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：Eric 仍紧张，但他冷静地做完所有题。转折用 but。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他写了便条感谢我，但我认为更大的变化是他的自信。转折用 but。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助别人也让我自己的理解更深。暗含与过去比较，用 deeper。deeply 为副词，make understanding deeply 结构不如 deeper 自然作宾补。故选 B。
