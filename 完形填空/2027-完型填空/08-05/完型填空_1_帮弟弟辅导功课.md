# 中考英语完形填空：帮弟弟辅导功课

## 完形填空原文

My brother Tom is two years ___1___ than me, but his math homework always looks ___2___ difficult. Last week, Mom asked me ___3___ him after dinner. I agreed, ___4___ I wanted to watch a cartoon.

At the desk, Tom couldn't understand the word problems ___5___ were printed in small letters. I read them ___6___ and drew simple pictures. Slowly, his eyes became ___7___ than before.

On Friday, Tom's teacher praised him ___8___ class. He ran home ___9___ told me the news excitedly. I learned ___10___ teaching others also helps me review old knowledge.

## 选项

1. A. young &emsp;&emsp; B. younger &emsp;&emsp; C. youngest &emsp;&emsp; D. the youngest
2. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too
3. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
6. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. more loud &emsp;&emsp; D. aloud
7. A. bright &emsp;&emsp; B. brighter &emsp;&emsp; C. brightest &emsp;&emsp; D. brightly
8. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
9. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. how

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我弟弟汤姆比我小两岁……。than 提示用比较级 younger。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：……但他的数学作业看起来总是更难。暗含与我的作业相比，用 more difficult。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周妈妈让我晚饭后辅导他。ask sb. to do sth. 让某人做某事。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我同意了，尽管我想看动画片。表让步用 although。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：汤姆看不懂用小号字体印刷的应用题。先行词 the word problems 指物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我大声朗读并画了简单的示意图。read aloud 朗读，强调出声阅读；loudly 偏重“吵闹”。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：慢慢地，他的眼睛比以前更亮了。than 提示用 brighter。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：周五汤姆的老师在课堂上表扬了他。in class 在课堂上。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他跑回家并兴奋地告诉我这个消息。ran 与 told 顺承并列，用 and。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了教别人也有助于我复习旧知识。learned 后接 that 引导的宾语从句，陈述事实。故选 B。
