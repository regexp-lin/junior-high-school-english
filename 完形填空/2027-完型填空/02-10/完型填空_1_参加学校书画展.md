# 中考英语完形填空：校园书画展的一幅画

## 完形填空原文

Our school art festival included a calligraphy and painting show. I decided to enter ___1___ ink painting of bamboo that I had practiced for weeks.

On the submission day, I carried the scroll carefully ___2___ I didn't want any ink to smudge. The hall was full of amazing works, ___3___ I felt a bit unsure about mine.

An art teacher hung my painting on the board ___4___ other students' pieces. She said my lines were confident, though I could work ___5___ spacing next time.

During the exhibition, several visitors stopped ___6___ my picture. A little girl asked ___7___ bamboo stayed green in winter. I explained in simple English and Chinese.

On the closing day, I received a certificate of participation. It was not a gold prize, ___8___ it meant a lot to me. I learned to show my work ___9___ fear.

At home, I rolled up the scroll ___10___ put it in a tube for safekeeping.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. between &emsp;&emsp; B. among &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. besides
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. beside &emsp;&emsp; C. below &emsp;&emsp; D. behind
7. A. why &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. when
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. without &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. by
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定送展一幅我练习数周的墨竹图。ink 以元音音素开头，泛指“一幅”用 an。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：交稿那天我小心捧着卷轴，因为我不想墨迹被蹭糊。表原因用 because。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：展厅里满是精彩作品，所以我对自己的画有点没把握。表结果用 so。and 并列；but 转折；or 选择。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（among / between）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位美术老师把我的画挂在展板上，与其他同学的作品放在一起。三者及以上“在……之中”用 among；between 多用于两者之间。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（work on）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说我的线条很有力，不过下次可以在留白间距上再下功夫。work on sth. 在某方面继续改进。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：展览期间，几位参观者在画前驻足。stop before 表示停在……前面（观赏）。beside 在旁；below 在下；behind 在后。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小女孩问为什么竹子在冬天仍保持绿色。问原因用 why。how 如何；what 什么；when 何时。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这不是金奖，但对我意义重大。转折用 but。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了不再害怕地展示作品。without fear 毫不畏惧。with fear 带着恐惧，与 learned to show 的积极语境相反。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在家卷起画轴并放进画筒妥善保存。并列动作 rolled 与 put 用 and。故选 A。
