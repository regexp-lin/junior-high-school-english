# 中考英语完形填空：课间交换小零食

## 完形填空原文

After the long winter holiday, many students brought snacks ___1___ share during the break. Our teacher reminded us ___2___ eat too much sweet food.

I packed dried plums and small crackers. My desk mate Lily brought seaweed rolls ___3___ her hometown. We agreed ___4___ trade a little of each.

In the hallway, we met Leo, who offered chocolate cookies. I hesitated ___5___ I was worried about crumbs in the classroom. He promised ___6___ eat them outside only.

We made a simple rule: keep voices low and clean up wrappers. ___7___ way, we could enjoy food without disturbing others.

That afternoon, I tasted Lily's rolls. They were a bit salty, ___8___ very fresh. I wrote a thank-you note ___9___ show how much I liked the rolls.

This small exchange made the new term feel ___10___ friendly from the very first week.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of
2. A. not to &emsp;&emsp; B. don't &emsp;&emsp; C. not &emsp;&emsp; D. didn't
3. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. trade &emsp;&emsp; B. trading &emsp;&emsp; C. to trade &emsp;&emsp; D. traded
5. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of
7. A. In this &emsp;&emsp; B. On this &emsp;&emsp; C. At this &emsp;&emsp; D. By this
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多同学带了零食想在课间分享。表目的用 to share。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. not to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师提醒我们不要吃过多的甜食。remind sb. not to do sth. 用 not to eat。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同桌 Lily 带了来自她家乡的海苔卷。from 表示来源。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（agree to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们同意互相交换一点。agree to do sth. 用 to trade。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我犹豫了，因为我担心教室里有饼干屑。表原因用 because。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（promise to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他承诺只在外面吃。promise to do sth. 用 to eat。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（in this way）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这样，我们就能享受食物而不打扰别人。in this way 这样；用这种方法。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有点咸，但很新鲜。转折用 but。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作定语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我写了一张感谢便条，以表达我有多喜欢这些海苔卷。a note to show... 中 to 与动词原形 show 构成不定式，作后置定语表目的。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次小交换让新学期从第一周起就显得更友好。暗含与没有交换时比较，friendly 的比较级用 more friendly。故选 B。
