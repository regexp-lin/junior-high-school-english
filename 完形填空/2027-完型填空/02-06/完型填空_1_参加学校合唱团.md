# 中考英语完形填空：加入学校合唱团

## 完形填空原文

Last term, I passed the audition and joined the school choir. I loved singing, ___1___ I had never performed on a real stage.

Our conductor, Mr. Hu, was strict ___2___ kind. He asked us to arrive on time and warm up our voices ___3___ gentle exercises.

At the beginning, I could not hit the high notes. I felt embarrassed ___4___ the older students still waited patiently. Mr. Hu told me ___5___ rush and focus on breathing.

Week after week, we practiced the same song until every part sounded balanced. I learned ___6___ to listen to others while singing my own line.

In December, we sang at the winter concert. The lights were bright, ___7___ I heard my parents clapping in the audience. That moment felt ___8___ a dream.

After the show, Mr. Hu said, "Music is not ___9___ being perfect. It is about sharing feelings together." I nodded and promised ___10___ keep practicing.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
5. A. not to &emsp;&emsp; B. don't &emsp;&emsp; C. not &emsp;&emsp; D. didn't
6. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
9. A. only about &emsp;&emsp; B. only &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. all
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我喜欢唱歌，但我从未在真正的舞台上表演过。转折用 but。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：指挥胡老师严格但善良。strict but kind 表转折并列。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他要求我们准时到达，并通过轻柔的练习热身声带。with 表示“用；通过”。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到尴尬，但学长学姐们仍然耐心等待。转折用 but。if 如果；because 因为；unless 除非。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. not to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：胡老师告诉我不要着急，把注意力放在呼吸上。tell sb. not to do sth. 告诉某人不要做某事。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了在唱自己声部时如何倾听他人。表示方式用 how。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：灯光明亮，我听到父母在观众席鼓掌。并列用 and。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那一刻感觉像一场梦。feel like 感觉像。as 作“像”多用于 as if 结构；此处 like 更自然。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：音乐并不仅仅是关于“唱得完美”。about 表示“关于”，与 being perfect 搭配自然。only 单独填入语法不通；only about 易与 not only 结构混淆。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（promise to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我点头并承诺继续练习。promise to do sth. 用不定式 to keep。故选 A。
