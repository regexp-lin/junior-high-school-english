# 中考英语完形填空：为名著制作读书卡

## 完形填空原文

Our Chinese teacher asked each student to make a reading card ___1___ a classic novel. I chose *The Adventures of Tom Sawyer* because the story was lively.

First, I wrote down the writer's name and a short introduction. ___2___, I picked two favorite scenes and explained ___3___ I liked them.

Finding suitable pictures took a long time. I searched online, ___4___ I also drew a small fence ___5___ white card to match the famous painting scene.

When I showed the card in class, some classmates said the colors were too bright. I felt a little upset, ___6___ the teacher praised my clear notes and honest feelings.

She suggested I choose a ___7___ background next time, but she also said creativity was welcome. I understood that feedback could help me do ___8___.

At home, I added a thin blue border. The card looked ___9___ and tidy. I put it on my desk to remind me ___10___ reading carefully.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. However &emsp;&emsp; B. Instead &emsp;&emsp; C. Then &emsp;&emsp; D. Although
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. why &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. when
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. softly &emsp;&emsp; B. softer &emsp;&emsp; C. soft &emsp;&emsp; D. pale
8. A. well &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. good
9. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. simpler &emsp;&emsp; C. simplest &emsp;&emsp; D. simply
10. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. keeping &emsp;&emsp; C. to keep &emsp;&emsp; D. kept

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：语文老师要求每位学生为一部经典小说制作读书卡。for 表示“为了；针对”。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连接副词/顺序词
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后，我挑选了两个最喜欢的场景。Then 表示顺序“然后”。However 然而；Instead 相反；Although 尽管。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：并解释我为什么喜欢它们。explain 后接 why 引导的从句表原因。what 什么；how 如何；when 何时。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在网上搜索，并且还在白卡上画了一道小栅栏。并列补充用 and。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在白卡上画栅栏。on white card 在卡片表面上。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点沮丧，但老师表扬了我的清晰批注和真诚感受。转折用 but。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她建议我下次选一个更柔和的背景。暗含与当前过亮的颜色比较，用比较级 softer；soft 为原级；pale 偏“苍白”，不如 softer 贴切；softly 为副词不能修饰名词 background。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白反馈能帮助我做得更好。do better 做得更好，暗含进步。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：读书卡看起来简洁又整齐。and 连接并列形容词，tidy 为原级，simple 用原级并列更自然；simply 为副词。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把它放在书桌上，提醒自己要认真阅读。remind sb. to do sth. 提醒某人做某事。故选 C。
