# 中考英语完形填空：学做手抄报

## 完形填空原文

Our teacher asked each group ___1___ a hand-copied newspaper about traditional festivals. I had never done one before, ___2___ I felt both excited and worried.

My teammates and I decided ___3___ the topic “The Mid-Autumn Festival”. Lily collected facts ___4___ the Internet, while Tom designed the borders. My job was ___5___ short English introductions beside each picture.

We worked ___6___ school for three afternoons. Sometimes we argued ___7___ which color to use, but we always found a solution in the end. Miss Chen told us ___8___ hurry—quality mattered more than speed.

When we finished, our work looked ___9___ than the first draft. On Monday, our hand-copied newspaper won ___10___ prize in the class show. Teamwork really made the difference.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. from
5. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. writing &emsp;&emsp; C. to write &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote
6. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. during &emsp;&emsp; D. until
7. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for
8. A. not to &emsp;&emsp; B. don't &emsp;&emsp; C. not &emsp;&emsp; D. to not
9. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. clearer &emsp;&emsp; C. clearest &emsp;&emsp; D. the clearest
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the first &emsp;&emsp; D. first

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师要求每个小组制作一份关于传统节日的手抄报。ask sb. to do sth. 用不定式作宾补。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前从未做过手抄报，所以我既兴奋又担心。前后为因果关系，用 so。because 表原因放在结果前；though 表让步；unless 表条件。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和队友决定以“中秋节”为主题。decide on 表示“选定、决定”，后接话题或方案。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：莉莉从网上搜集资料。from the Internet 表示“从网上”，符合表达习惯。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的任务是在每幅图片旁写简短的英语介绍。主语为 My job，表语用不定式 to write。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们放学后又花了三个下午继续制作。after school 表示“放学后”。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们会为使用哪种颜色争论，但最后总能找到解决办法。argue about 表示“就……争论”。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 否定不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：陈老师告诉我们不要着急——质量比速度更重要。tell sb. not to do sth. 否定不定式 not 要放在 to 之前。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：完成时，我们的作品看起来比初稿更清晰。than 提示比较级，用 clearer。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 序数词 / 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：周一，我们的手抄报在班级展示中获得了一等奖。序数词 first 前通常加定冠词 the，the first prize 表示“一等奖”。故选 C。
