# 中考英语完形填空：寒假书法练习

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, Grandma gave me a brush, ink, and rice paper. She said practicing calligraphy could help me stay ___1___ and improve my handwriting for schoolwork as well.

On the first day, my strokes looked ___2___ and uneven. The ink was either too thick ___3___ too light. I wanted to give up, but Grandma told me to breathe slowly and try ___4___.

She placed her warm hand over mine and showed me how to press the brush ___5___ at the beginning of each stroke and lift it gently at the end. "Control comes from practice, not from ___6___," she smiled.

Every morning, I spent twenty minutes copying simple characters. Little ___7___ little, the lines became smoother. When I wrote the character for "peace" neatly, I felt a quiet sense of ___8___.

Before school started again, I framed my best work and hung it ___9___ my desk. Now, whenever homework feels stressful, I look ___10___ at the characters and remember to slow down.

## 选项

1. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. patient &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. careless
2. A. tidy &emsp;&emsp; B. straight &emsp;&emsp; C. shaky &emsp;&emsp; D. colorful
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. again &emsp;&emsp; B. alone &emsp;&emsp; C. abroad &emsp;&emsp; D. online
5. A. softly &emsp;&emsp; B. heavily &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. politely
6. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. hurry &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. noise
7. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
8. A. shame &emsp;&emsp; B. pride &emsp;&emsp; C. doubt &emsp;&emsp; D. regret
9. A. under &emsp;&emsp; B. behind &emsp;&emsp; C. above &emsp;&emsp; D. inside
10. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. down &emsp;&emsp; C. back &emsp;&emsp; D. away

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶说练习书法能帮助我保持耐心，也有助于改善作业书写。A 项 nervous 紧张；B 项 patient 耐心；C 项 lonely 孤独；D 项 careless 粗心。书法练习强调耐心。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一天，我的笔画看起来颤抖且不匀。A 项 tidy 整洁；B 项 straight 笔直；C 项 shaky 颤抖的；D 项 colorful 多彩。初学者笔画不稳用 shaky。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：墨不是太浓就是太淡。either ... or ... 为固定搭配，意为"要么……要么……"。either ... and 不搭配；but/so 不符合结构。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想放弃，但奶奶让我慢慢呼吸，再试一次。try again 再试一次；try alone 独自试；abroad/online 不合语境。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我每一笔开头要稍重按笔，结尾轻轻提笔。书法起笔常需用力按压，press heavily 与后文 lift gently 形成对比。softly 轻按与技法常识相反；quietly/politely 不修饰 press brush。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：控制力来自练习，而不是来自急躁。A 项 luck 运气；B 项 hurry 匆忙；C 项 fear 恐惧；D 项 noise 噪音。与 patience 相对的是 hurry。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：渐渐地，线条变得更流畅。little by little 为固定搭配，意为"一点一点地、逐渐地"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我把"平安"写得工整时，我感到一种安静的自豪。A 项 shame 羞愧；B 项 pride 自豪；C 项 doubt 怀疑；D 项 regret 后悔。取得进步应感到 pride。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：开学前，我把最满意的作品装裱好挂在书桌上方。above the desk 书桌上方；under 下方；behind 后面；inside 里面。装饰常挂上方。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：每当作业有压力时，我抬头看看那些字，提醒自己慢下来。look up at 抬头看；look down 俯视；look back 回顾；look away 移开视线。挂在书桌上方需抬头看。故选 A。
