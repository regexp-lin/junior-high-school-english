# 中考英语完形填空：第一次独自做饭

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my parents had to work late. They left a note saying I could heat up soup, ___1___ I wanted to surprise them with a simple dinner.

I chose tomato and egg, a dish Mom often cooks ___2___ weekends. I washed the tomatoes, cracked the eggs, and turned on the stove. The oil in the pan soon became ___3___ enough, so I poured in the eggs.

The kitchen filled with a ___4___ smell, and for a moment I felt proud. Then I turned to chop tomatoes ___5___ the eggs turned brown too quickly. Smoke rose, and I hurried to turn down the heat.

When my parents walked in, the plate looked a little ___6___, but the taste was not bad. Dad took a bite and said, "This is your first step ___7___ becoming independent."

Mom hugged me and reminded me ___8___ safety rules next time. That night, I wrote a short ___9___ in my diary about the small accident and the warm praise. Cooking alone was scary ___10___ exciting.

## 选项

1. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. wet &emsp;&emsp; C. hot &emsp;&emsp; D. full
4. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. strange &emsp;&emsp; C. delicious &emsp;&emsp; D. salty
5. A. while &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although
6. A. dark &emsp;&emsp; B. empty &emsp;&emsp; C. cheap &emsp;&emsp; D. dry
7. A. beyond &emsp;&emsp; B. toward &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. among
8. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. across &emsp;&emsp; D. below
9. A. lie &emsp;&emsp; B. joke &emsp;&emsp; C. report &emsp;&emsp; D. note
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们留了纸条说我可以热汤，但我想给父母一个简单的惊喜晚餐。前后为转折：本可简单热汤 vs 想做饭。because 原因；though 尽管；but 但是；unless 除非。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了西红柿炒鸡蛋——妈妈常在周末做的菜。on weekends 在周末，为固定搭配。in/at/by weekends 不常用或不符合习惯。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：锅里的油很快够热了，我便倒入鸡蛋。炒菜前需油热，hot enough 足够热。cold/wet/full 不符合倒蛋入锅的前提。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房里飘满香味，那一刻我很自豪。A 项 terrible 糟糕的；B 项 strange 奇怪的；C 项 delicious 美味的；D 项 salty 咸的。鸡蛋炒香用 delicious smell。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我转身去切西红柿时，鸡蛋却过快变焦。while 表示"当……时"，强调同时发生；until 直到；unless 除非；although 尽管。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母回来时，菜看起来有点焦，但味道还不错。A 项 dark（颜色）发暗、焦；B 项 empty 空；C 项 cheap 便宜；D 项 dry 干。鸡蛋炒过头会变 dark。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说这是我走向独立的第一步。step toward 朝向……的一步；beyond 超过；against 反对；among 在……之中。toward becoming independent 符合搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈拥抱我，并提醒我下次要注意安全规则。remind sb. about sth. 提醒某人关于某事；remind into/across/below 不搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当晚我在日记里写了一条简短的记录。A 项 lie 谎言；B 项 joke 玩笑；C 项 report 报告；D 项 note 笔记、记录。日记中简短记录用 note。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：独自做饭既吓人又令人兴奋。scary 与 exciting 形成转折对比：害怕却刺激。but 表转折；and 并列顺承；or 选择；so 结果。故选 D。
