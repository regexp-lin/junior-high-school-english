# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸盒

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a DIY activity last Friday. A volunteer teacher showed us ___1___ to make a paper box step by step. At first, I thought it would be easy, ___2___ I failed twice because the lines were not straight.

The teacher smiled and told me ___3___ up. “Practice makes perfect,” she said. I took a deep breath and tried ___4___ third time. This time, I used a ruler and folded the paper ___5___ slowly. Finally, the box looked tidy and strong.

When I carried it home, my little brother was ___6___ excited that he wanted to put his toys in it at once. My mother praised me and said I had done a ___7___ job than she expected. That night, I wrote a short diary ___8___ English about the experience.

Now I understand that ___9___ is not only about talent, but also about patience and careful work. I have decided ___10___ more handmade things in my free time.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
3. A. not give &emsp;&emsp; B. don’t give &emsp;&emsp; C. not to give &emsp;&emsp; D. to not give  
4. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
5. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. much  
6. A. such &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. too &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
8. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
9. A. succeed &emsp;&emsp; B. success &emsp;&emsp; C. successful &emsp;&emsp; D. successfully  
10. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构  
**【解析】** show sb. how to do sth. 表示“向某人展示如何做某事”。此处指老师一步步演示“如何”做纸盒，用 how 引导不定式短语。what to make 偏重“做什么”；which / when 与“步骤演示”语境不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折关系）  
**【解析】** 前句“我原以为会很简单”与后句“我失败了两次”形成转折，应用 but。and 表并列或递进；or 表选择；so 表结果。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** tell 的用法及动词不定式的否定形式  
**【解析】** tell sb. (not) to do sth. 为固定结构，否定词 not 应放在不定式符号 to 之前，即 not to give up，表示“告诉我不要放弃”。don’t give 不能作 tell 的宾补结构中的标准形式；to not give 为错误语序。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词与序数词连用（a + 序数词）  
**【解析】** “a third time” 表示“再一次、又一次（第三次尝试）”，强调又一次尝试，并非特指顺序上的“第三次”必须用 the。此处语境为“我又试了一次”，用 a third time 很自然。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰副词（very + slowly）  
**【解析】** slowly 为副词，可用 very 修饰，表示“非常慢地”。more slowly 为比较级，句中无比较对象；most slowly 为最高级，语境不适用；much 多修饰比较级或不可数程度，一般不说 much slowly。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 固定句型 so…that…（如此……以至于……）  
**【解析】** 句型为“so + 形容词/副词 + that 从句”。excited 为形容词，用 so excited that… 表示“如此兴奋以至于……”。such 后接名词短语 such + (a/an) + adj. + n. + that；very / too 不能与 that 构成该结果从句句型。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than 提示）  
**【解析】** 句中出现 than，提示用比较级。better 是 good 的比较级，done a better job than… 表示“做得比她预期更好”。good / well 为原级，不能与 than 搭配表达比较；best 为最高级，需有范围或 the。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（in English）  
**【解析】** “用英语”固定表达为 in English。on / at / by 均不与 English 构成此义。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 词性辨析（名词作主语）  
**【解析】** 从句主语位置需要名词。success 为名词，表示“成功”。succeed 为动词；successful 为形容词；successfully 为副词，均不能直接作主语中心词（此处结构为 ___ is not only about…）。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** decide 的固定搭配（decide to do sth.）  
**【解析】** decide 后接动词不定式作宾语，即 decide to do sth.，表示“决定做某事”。make / making / made 均不符合该搭配。故选 B。
