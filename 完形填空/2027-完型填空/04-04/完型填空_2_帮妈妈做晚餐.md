# 中考英语完形填空：帮妈妈做晚餐

## 完形填空原文

Mom came home late yesterday, ___1___ she looked very tired. I decided ___2___ dinner with her instead of only doing homework. We opened the fridge and found tomatoes, eggs, ___3___ a little beef. Mom taught me ___4___ slice vegetables safely and how ___5___ control the heat on the stove. While the soup was simmering, I set the table ___6___ neat chopsticks and bowls. I almost added ___7___ much salt, but Mom stopped me in time. The noodles tasted ___8___ than I expected, and Dad said the kitchen smelled ___9___ a small restaurant. After we washed the dishes together, Mom hugged me and said I had been ___10___ really helpful helper that evening.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. to cook &emsp;&emsp; C. cooking &emsp;&emsp; D. cooked
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. quite
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well
9. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. alike
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. some

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈昨天很晚回家，而且她看起来很累。并列补充情况，用 and，故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定和她一起做晚饭，而不是只做作业。decide to do sth. 为固定用法，故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们打开冰箱，发现了西红柿、鸡蛋和一点牛肉。并列列举用 and，故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + to do”结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈教我如何安全地切菜…… teach sb. how to do sth.，故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + to do”结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：……以及如何控制炉火。how to control 与前面 how to slice 并列，故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：汤在慢炖时，我用整齐的筷子和碗摆好餐桌。with 表示“用；带着”，故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（too much）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我差点加了太多盐。too much 修饰不可数名词 salt，故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：面条尝起来比我想象的更好。than 提示用比较级 better，故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说厨房闻起来像一家小餐馆。smell like 表示“闻起来像……”，故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：……说我那天晚上真是一个很有帮助的小帮手。really 以辅音音素开头，泛指“一个”，用不定冠词 a，故选 A。
