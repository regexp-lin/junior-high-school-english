# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工皮革钥匙包

## 完形填空原文

In our school’s craft club, Mr. Zhou taught us ___1___ make a simple leather key pouch. He said leather was strong ___2___ could protect keys from scratches. Before we started, he checked ___3___ tool on the table to make sure everything was safe.

We used a soft leather piece, a metal button, and strong thread. Mr. Zhou showed us ___4___ to fold the leather and punch holes along the edge. I had to push the needle ___5___ slowly because the leather was thick. ___6___ I made a wrong hole at first, Mr. Zhou helped me cover it with a small decoration.

When I finished sewing, I tried ___7___ put the button on. It was harder ___8___ I thought, but I kept trying. At last, my key pouch looked tidy, and I put my house key ___9___ it happily. Mr. Zhou smiled and said, “Good job! Practice ___10___ make perfect.”

## 选项

1. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
3. A. each &emsp;&emsp; B. every &emsp;&emsp; C. both &emsp;&emsp; D. all  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. where  
5. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gentler &emsp;&emsp; C. gently &emsp;&emsp; D. most gently  
6. A. If &emsp;&emsp; B. Because &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. that  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
10. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. makes &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构（*teach sb. how to do*）  
**【解析】** *taught us how to make* 表示“教我们如何制作”。*what to make* 偏重“做什么东西”，此处强调方法步骤，用 *how* 更合适。*why/when* 与教学内容不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表递进/并列  
**【解析】** “皮革结实”与“能保护钥匙”为并列顺承关系，用 *and*。*but* 表转折；*or* 表选择；*so* 表结果，语气不如 *and* 自然。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** *each* 与 *every* 辨析  
**【解析】** *each tool* 强调“每一件工具”（个体），常与可数名词单数搭配，适合检查清单。*every* 也可接单数，但 *each* 更突出逐个检查；*both/all* 需接复数或不可数语境，且 *tool* 为单数。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** *showed us how to fold* 表示“示范如何折叠”。*what/which/where* 不如 *how* 贴合“折叠、打孔”的方法。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 *push*，用副词 *gently*。*gentle/gentler* 为形容词；*most gently* 无比较范围，不必用最高级。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 从属连词表让步  
**【解析】** “起初打错孔”与“老师帮我遮盖”为让步关系：尽管出错，仍得到帮助，用 *Although*。*If* 表条件；*Because* 表原因；*Unless* 表除非。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *try to do sth.*  
**【解析】** 此处表示“试图把扣子装上”，用 *try to put*。*try doing* 表“试着做看看”，语义也可，但选项若只有 *to* 结构，选 *to* 最稳妥；且后文 *kept trying* 强调努力去完成，*try to* 更贴切。*for/of/with* 不构成 *try* 的正确搭配。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 比较级结构 *比较级 + than*  
**【解析】** *harder than I thought* 为“比我想的更难”。*as* 需构成 *as...as*；*so/that* 不构成比较。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 *in* 表“在……里面”  
**【解析】** 把钥匙放进钥匙包，用 *put...in it*。*on/at/to* 不符合“装入”之义。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 谚语中的语法一致（主谓一致）  
**【解析】** *Practice makes perfect.* 为固定谚语，主语 *Practice* 为不可数概念，谓语用第三人称单数 *makes*。原形/动名词/过去式均不符合该谚语固定形式。故选 B。
