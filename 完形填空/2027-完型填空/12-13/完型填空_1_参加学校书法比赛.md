# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校书法比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school organized ___1___ Chinese calligraphy competition for students in Grades Seven and Eight. When I saw the poster, I hesitated ___2___ I was not confident about my handwriting. My Chinese teacher noticed my worry and encouraged ___3___ to have a try.

From that day on, I practiced ___4___ school every afternoon. I copied classic poems and paid attention ___5___ each stroke. At first, my brush moved ___6___ than I wanted, and ink dropped onto the paper. ___7___, I didn’t give up. Little by little, my characters looked neater and more balanced.

On the day of the competition, I took ___8___ deep breath before writing. I focused on ___9___ task instead of thinking about prizes. When the judges walked past my desk, I stayed calm. I didn’t win ___10___ first prize, but I won a special award for progress.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
3. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. me &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
4. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. after &emsp;&emsp; D. before  
5. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
6. A. fast &emsp;&emsp; B. faster &emsp;&emsp; C. fastest &emsp;&emsp; D. more fast  
7. A. However &emsp;&emsp; B. Therefore &emsp;&emsp; C. Besides &emsp;&emsp; D. Instead  
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
9. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词表泛指  
**【解析】** “一场书法比赛”为泛指，*Chinese* 以辅音音素 /tʃ/ 开头，用 *a*。*an* 不适用；*the* 首次出现不宜特指；零冠词不符合习惯。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 从属连词表原因  
**【解析】** “犹豫”是因为不自信，后句解释原因，用 *because*。*if* 表条件；*although* 表让步，与句意矛盾；*unless* 表“除非”。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** *encourage* 后接宾语，用宾格 *me*。*I* 主格；*my/mine* 为物主代词。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *after* 表时间  
**【解析】** *after school* 表示“放学后”。*at school* 强调在校内但不突出“放学后练习”的时间点；*on/before* 与语境不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *pay attention to*  
**【解析】** *pay attention to each stroke*，介词用 *to*。其余介词不构成搭配。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级（*than* 提示）  
**【解析】** *than I wanted* 提示比较级，*faster* 正确。*more fast* 为错误形式；原级/最高级不符合 *than* 结构。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词表转折关系  
**【解析】** 前文写困难，后文写不放弃，为转折，用 *However*（然而）。*Therefore* 表因此；*Besides* 表此外；*Instead* 表代替，均不如 *However* 贴切。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *take a deep breath*  
**【解析】** *take a deep breath* 为固定短语，用不定冠词 *a*。*deep* 以辅音音素开头，不用 *an*。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** 修饰名词 *task*，用 *my task*。*I/me* 不能作定语；*mine* 后不接名词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 序数词前用定冠词 *the*  
**【解析】** *the first prize* 表示“一等奖”，序数词 *first* 前常用定冠词 *the*。*a/an* 或零冠词不符合序数词表名次的常见用法。故选 C。
