# 中考英语完形填空：假期爬山

## 完形填空原文

During the holiday, my uncle invited our family ___1___ climb a nearby mountain. We set off early ___2___ the sun was still soft, carrying water, fruit, and a simple first-aid kit.

The path at the beginning was wide and easy, ___3___ it became steeper after an hour. My legs felt heavy, ___4___ my father kept encouraging me. “Look at the birds above the trees,” he said. “They are flying ___5___ they are not tired at all.”

When we reached a small platform halfway up, we took a short break. My mother handed me a banana and reminded me ___6___ drink slowly. From there, we could already see part of the town ___7___ looked like a toy model in the morning mist.

The last part of the climb was the hardest, ___8___ the view from the top made every step worthwhile. Standing on the rock, I took a deep breath and felt ___9___ than I had expected. On the way down, I promised myself ___10___ give up easily next time life felt difficult.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
5. A. as if &emsp;&emsp; B. even if &emsp;&emsp; C. as though &emsp;&emsp; D. so that
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
7. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. stronger &emsp;&emsp; C. strongly &emsp;&emsp; D. strongest
10. A. to not &emsp;&emsp; B. not to &emsp;&emsp; C. don’t &emsp;&emsp; D. not

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（invite sb. to do sth.）  
**【解析】** invite 后接“人 + 不定式”，构成 invite sb. to do sth.，表示“邀请某人做某事”。此处 invite our family to climb 符合结构。for/of/with 不能与 invite 构成该固定搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词辨析（时间状语从句）  
**【解析】** 句意为“我们早早出发，那时太阳还很柔和”。when 引导时间状语从句，说明出发的时刻背景。if 表条件，until 表“直到”，unless 表“除非”，均不如 when 贴合“清晨出发”的画面。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折关系）  
**【解析】** 前半句说路又宽又好走，后半句说一小时后变陡，前后为转折关系，用 but。and 表并列递进，so 表结果，or 表选择，均不符合语境。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词辨析（让步关系）  
**【解析】** “腿很沉”与“爸爸一直鼓励我”形成让步：尽管累，仍得到鼓励。though 引导让步，相当于 although。because 表原因，与后文鼓励的逻辑重复；if/unless 表条件，不符合。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 短语辨析（as if）  
**【解析】** 父亲用比喻安慰：“鸟儿好像一点也不累”。as if / as though 都可表“仿佛、好像”，选项中 as if 正确。even if 表“即使”，so that 表目的或结果，均与比喻语气不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do sth.）  
**【解析】** remind sb. to do sth. 表示“提醒某人去做某事”。remind sb. of sth. 表“使某人想起某事”，与 drink slowly 不搭配；for/with 不构成 remind 的常见宾补结构。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词  
**【解析】** 先行词为 part of the town（物/景象），从句中 looked like 前缺主语，用 which。who/whom 指人；whose 表所属，此处不适用。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折关系）  
**【解析】** “最后一段最难”与“山顶风景让每一步都值得”为转折，用 but。and 表顺承，or 表选择，so 表结果，均弱于转折语气。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** feel 为系动词，后接形容词；句中有 than，需用比较级 stronger，表示“比预想的更坚强/更有力量感”。strong 为原级；strongly 为副词；strongest 为最高级且无范围提示。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定式的否定式（promise not to do）  
**【解析】** promise 后接不定式，否定形式为 promise not to do sth.，not 置于 to 之前。to not 语序不规范；don’t 不能紧跟在 promised myself 后作宾语；单独 not 无法构成不定式结构。故选 B。
