# 中考英语完形填空：学做水彩画

## 完形填空原文

My art teacher encouraged me ___1___ try watercolor painting. I had never used watercolors before, so I was a little ___2___.

She gave me a set of paints, a brush ___3___ a cup of water. "Start with light colors and add dark ones ___4___," she said.

I dipped the brush in blue paint and drew a sky on the paper. The color spread quickly ___5___ the wet surface. It looked soft, just ___6___ a real cloud.

Then I tried to paint a tree. ___7___, the green and brown mixed together and became messy. I was about to give up ___8___ the teacher came over.

"Mistakes are part of learning," she said gently. She showed me how ___9___ control the amount of water on the brush.

I tried again and this time the tree looked much ___10___. I smiled and decided to keep practicing every week.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. on
2. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. happy &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. later &emsp;&emsp; C. latest &emsp;&emsp; D. lately
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. into
6. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
7. A. However &emsp;&emsp; B. Besides &emsp;&emsp; C. Therefore &emsp;&emsp; D. Moreover
8. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. until
9. A. what to &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. where to &emsp;&emsp; D. when to
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：美术老师鼓励我尝试水彩画。encourage sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示"鼓励某人做某事"。for/of/on 不符合 encourage 的搭配。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前从未用过水彩，所以有点担心。从未接触过新事物，自然感到 worried（担心）。happy 为"开心"；angry 为"生气"；bored 为"无聊"，不符合初次尝试的心态。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她给了我一套颜料、一支画笔和一杯水。三者并列列举，用 and。or/but/so 不符合列举。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：先从浅色开始，之后再加深色。later 表示"之后、稍后"。late 为"迟到"；latest 为"最新的"；lately 为"最近"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：颜色在湿纸面上迅速扩散。on the surface 表示"在表面上"。in/at/into 不如 on 贴切表示"在平面上"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看起来柔和，就像真正的云朵。like 为介词表比喻，"像……一样"。as 多接从句；for/of 不搭配此比喻。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连接副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然而，绿色和棕色混在一起变得很杂乱。前后为转折关系，However 表示"然而"。Besides 为"此外"；Therefore 为"因此"；Moreover 为"而且"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析/be about to...when
**【解析】** 本句意为：我正要放弃时老师走了过来。be about to...when... 为固定句型，表示"正要……这时……"。after/before/until 不符合此句型。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** "疑问词 + 不定式"结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我如何控制画笔上的水量。how to do 表示"怎样做"。what/where/when 不符合"方法"语境。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次树看起来好多了。much + 比较级，表示"……得多"。good 为原级；well 为副词；best 为最高级。故选 C。
