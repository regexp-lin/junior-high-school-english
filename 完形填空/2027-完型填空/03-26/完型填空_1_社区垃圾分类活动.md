# 中考英语完形填空：社区垃圾分类活动

## 完形填空原文

Our community organized a waste-sorting event last Saturday. Many volunteers, ___1___ students and adults, came to help.

A worker first taught us the four types ___2___ waste: recyclable, kitchen, harmful and other waste. He showed us colored bins ___3___ asked us to practice sorting.

I was surprised to learn that used batteries should go ___4___ the red bin. Before this, I always threw them into the wrong one. Many neighbors made the ___5___ mistake.

After the training, we went door ___6___ door to hand out leaflets. Most people were friendly and thanked us ___7___ the information.

An old lady asked me ___8___ plastic bags belonged to recyclable waste. I told her they did not. She nodded and said she would sort more ___9___ in the future.

By the end of the day, we had visited over fifty ___10___. I felt tired but very proud of what we did.

## 选项

1. A. both &emsp;&emsp; B. either &emsp;&emsp; C. neither &emsp;&emsp; D. all
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. different &emsp;&emsp; B. same &emsp;&emsp; C. new &emsp;&emsp; D. old
6. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. with
7. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
8. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how
9. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. careless &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
10. A. families &emsp;&emsp; B. schools &emsp;&emsp; C. shops &emsp;&emsp; D. parks

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 不定代词/both...and
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多志愿者来帮忙，包括学生和成年人。both...and... 表示"既……又……"，连接两类人。either 用于"二选一"；neither 为"两者都不"；all 用于三者及以上。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位工作人员先教我们四种类型的垃圾。types of... 表示"……的种类"。in/for/at 不符合"种类"的表达。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他给我们看了彩色垃圾桶并让我们练习分类。showed 与 asked 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：废旧电池应该扔进红色垃圾桶里。go into 表示"进入……里面"，强调从外到内的动作。in 表示已在里面；on/at 不表示"投入"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多邻居也犯了同样的错误。the same mistake 表示"同样的错误"。different 为"不同的"；new/old 不符合上下文。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们挨家挨户分发传单。door to door 为固定搭配，表示"挨家挨户"。by/after/with 不符合此搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：大部分人很友好并感谢我们提供的信息。thank sb. for sth. 为固定搭配，表示"因……而感谢某人"。of/with/about 不符合 thank 的搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位老奶奶问我塑料袋是否属于可回收垃圾。if 引导一般疑问句转变的宾语从句，表示"是否"。that 不表疑问；what/how 不符合句意。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说以后会更仔细地分类。修饰动词 sort 需用副词 carefully。careful 为形容词；careless/carelessly 意为"粗心"，语义相反。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到一天结束时我们已经走访了五十多户家庭。上文提到"挨家挨户发传单"，走访对象为 families。schools/shops/parks 不符合社区入户宣传语境。故选 A。
