# 中考英语完形填空：参加社区志愿活动

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, our class teacher asked us to ___1___ a community volunteer activity. We were divided into groups, and each group was given a different task.

My group was asked to help clean up the small garden near the community center. When we arrived, the garden was in a ___2___ condition. Fallen leaves covered the paths, and weeds grew everywhere. It was clear that nobody ___3___ care of it for a long time.

We started working ___4___. Some of us swept the leaves while others pulled out the weeds. I was in charge of ___5___ the flower beds. The soil was hard, so I had to use a small shovel to loosen it first.

After two hours of hard work, the garden looked completely ___6___. The paths were clean, and the flower beds were neat. An old man living nearby came out and thanked us ___7___. He said the garden used to be beautiful, but after the old gardener moved away, no one took care of it.

We promised to come back ___8___ month to keep the garden tidy. The old man was so moved that he almost cried. Our teacher said this experience taught us an important ___9___: small actions can make a big difference.

I felt that volunteering not only helps ___10___ but also brings joy to ourselves. I will keep doing it.

## 选项

1. A. join &emsp;&emsp; B. join in &emsp;&emsp; C. attend &emsp;&emsp; D. take part  
2. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. bad &emsp;&emsp; D. badly  
3. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. takes &emsp;&emsp; C. took &emsp;&emsp; D. had taken  
4. A. immediate &emsp;&emsp; B. immediately &emsp;&emsp; C. more immediate &emsp;&emsp; D. most immediate  
5. A. tidy &emsp;&emsp; B. tidied &emsp;&emsp; C. to tidy &emsp;&emsp; D. tidying  
6. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. similar &emsp;&emsp; C. different &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult  
7. A. warm &emsp;&emsp; B. warming &emsp;&emsp; C. warmly &emsp;&emsp; D. warmer  
8. A. every &emsp;&emsp; B. each &emsp;&emsp; C. some &emsp;&emsp; D. any  
9. A. lesson &emsp;&emsp; B. class &emsp;&emsp; C. subject &emsp;&emsp; D. course  
10. A. another &emsp;&emsp; B. other &emsp;&emsp; C. the other &emsp;&emsp; D. others  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析  
**【解析】** join in 意为"参加（活动）"，后接活动名词。join 后直接加组织或人（如 join the army）；attend 侧重出席正式场合；take part 需加 in（take part in）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** in a bad condition 意为"处于糟糕的状态"，后文描述落叶和杂草说明花园状况不好。good 意为"好的"与语境矛盾；well 作形容词表"身体好"；badly 为副词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** "很长时间以来没有人照顾过"，for a long time 与完成时搭配，且"没人照顾"发生在"我们到达"（过去）之前，用过去完成时 had taken。take/takes 为一般现在时；took 为一般过去时，不与 for a long time 自然搭配。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 started working 需用副词 immediately（立即）。immediate 为形容词；more/most immediate 为比较级/最高级形容词。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词 of 后接动名词  
**【解析】** be in charge of doing sth. 意为"负责做某事"，of 为介词后接动名词 tidying。tidy 为原形；tidied 为过去式；to tidy 为不定式，均不能跟在介词 of 后。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（语境推理）  
**【解析】** 经过清理后花园"完全不同了"，different 表示变化。same 意为"相同的"与变化语境矛盾；similar 意为"相似的"；difficult 意为"困难的"不修饰花园外观。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 thanked 需用副词 warmly（热情地）。warm 为形容词；warming 意为"变暖的"；warmer 为比较级形容词。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** every + 时间名词  
**【解析】** every month 意为"每个月"，every 后接单数名词表频率。each 也可但 every 更强调周期性；some month 不表频率；any month 用于否定/疑问句。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 名词辨析  
**【解析】** an important lesson 意为"一个重要的教训/道理"，lesson 有"经验教训"之义。class 指课堂；subject 指学科；course 指课程，均无"人生道理"之义。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 others  
**【解析】** others 意为"其他人"（泛指），helps others 表"帮助他人"。another 指"另一个"（单数）；other 为形容词需接名词；the other 指两者中的另一个。故选 D。
