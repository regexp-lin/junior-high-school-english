# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工花瓶

## 完形填空原文

My art teacher, Ms. Zhao, believes that old items can ___1___ into beautiful things. Last week, she taught us to turn empty glass bottles into decorative vases.

She asked each student to bring an empty bottle from home. I brought a tall juice bottle that had been ___2___ in the kitchen for weeks. I washed it ___3___ and brought it to school the next day.

In class, Ms. Zhao prepared acrylic paint, brushes, ribbons, and some small beads. She said, "Today, you are not just students — you are ___4___!" Everyone laughed, but we were also excited.

First, we painted the bottles with a base color. I chose white because it looked clean and ___5___. Then we waited for the paint to dry. Ms. Zhao reminded us to be patient. "Good art takes ___6___," she said.

After the paint dried, we started adding patterns. I painted green leaves and small red flowers on my bottle. Some classmates drew stars and moons, ___7___ others wrote short poems. Each vase was unique.

Finally, we tied colorful ribbons around the ___8___ of the bottles and glued beads on them. When I put a fresh flower ___9___ my finished vase, it looked stunning.

Ms. Zhao said she was very ___10___ with our creativity. She displayed all the vases in the school hallway. Many teachers and students stopped to admire them.

## 选项

1. A. turn &emsp;&emsp; B. be turned &emsp;&emsp; C. turning &emsp;&emsp; D. turned  
2. A. sit &emsp;&emsp; B. sits &emsp;&emsp; C. sat &emsp;&emsp; D. sitting  
3. A. clean &emsp;&emsp; B. cleanly &emsp;&emsp; C. cleaning &emsp;&emsp; D. cleaned  
4. A. art &emsp;&emsp; B. artist &emsp;&emsp; C. artists &emsp;&emsp; D. artistic  
5. A. simply &emsp;&emsp; B. simple &emsp;&emsp; C. simpler &emsp;&emsp; D. simplest  
6. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. times &emsp;&emsp; C. timing &emsp;&emsp; D. timely  
7. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
8. A. top &emsp;&emsp; B. bottom &emsp;&emsp; C. neck &emsp;&emsp; D. side  
9. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. onto &emsp;&emsp; C. out of &emsp;&emsp; D. away from  
10. A. please &emsp;&emsp; B. pleasant &emsp;&emsp; C. pleased &emsp;&emsp; D. pleasure  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 被动语态（情态动词 + be + 过去分词）  
**【解析】** old items 与 turn into 之间为被动关系，"旧物品能被变成美丽的东西"，用 can be turned into。turn 为主动原形；turning 为现在分词；turned 缺 be。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作后置定语/补语  
**【解析】** had been sitting 构成过去完成进行时，表示"一直放着"，sitting 与 had been 搭配。sit 为原形；sits 为三单；sat 为过去式但此处需进行时态体现"一直"。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作结果补语  
**【解析】** washed it clean 意为"把它洗干净"，clean 为形容词作宾语补足语（wash sth. clean）。cleanly 为副词；cleaning/cleaned 不用于此补语结构。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 名词单复数  
**【解析】** 主语 you 指全班同学（复数），表语应用复数 artists（艺术家们）。art 为不可数名词"艺术"；artist 为单数；artistic 为形容词"有艺术感的"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** looked 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。simple（简洁的）与 clean 并列。simply 为副词；simpler/simplest 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不可数名词 time（时间）  
**【解析】** takes time 意为"需要时间"，time 表"时间"为不可数名词。times 意为"次数/时代"；timing 意为"时机"；timely 为形容词"及时的"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词 while 表对比  
**【解析】** "一些人画星星和月亮，而另一些人写短诗"，while 表对比两类人的不同做法。when 表时间；because 表原因；if 表条件，均不适合。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 名词辨析（常识推理）  
**【解析】** the neck of the bottles 意为"瓶颈处"，系丝带常系在瓶颈。top（顶部）、bottom（底部）、side（侧面）虽可但系丝带通常在瓶颈处。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 into 表放入  
**【解析】** put a flower into my vase 意为"把花放入花瓶里"，into 表从外到内。onto 意为"到……上面"；out of 意为"从……出来"；away from 意为"远离"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be pleased with  
**【解析】** be pleased with 意为"对……感到满意"，pleased 为形容词。please 为动词；pleasant 意为"令人愉快的"修饰事物；pleasure 为名词。故选 C。
