# 中考英语完形填空：第一次做番茄炒蛋

## 完形填空原文

My parents were busy last Sunday, so I decided to cook lunch ___1___. I chose tomato and egg stir-fry because it looked simple on a video.

I washed tomatoes and cut them ___2___ small pieces. When I cracked eggs into a bowl, a bit of shell fell in. I used a spoon to take it out ___3___.

Then I heated oil in a pan. The oil popped ___4___ I put the eggs in too quickly. I stepped back and turned the heat down. Mom had told me safety should come ___5___.

After the eggs were done, I added tomatoes and a little sugar. The kitchen smelled ___6___. I tasted the dish and added a pinch of salt.

When my parents came home, they were surprised ___7___ the meal on the table. Dad said it was not restaurant-level, ___8___ it was full of love.

That afternoon, Mom taught me ___9___ to hold the knife correctly. I promised I would practice again ___10___ following weekend until I could cook it smoothly.

## 选项

1. A. me &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. mine &emsp;&emsp; D. myself
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. more careful &emsp;&emsp; D. care
4. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. after &emsp;&emsp; D. until
5. A. one &emsp;&emsp; B. first &emsp;&emsp; C. last &emsp;&emsp; D. second
6. A. well &emsp;&emsp; B. good &emsp;&emsp; C. nicely &emsp;&emsp; D. wonderfully
7. A. see &emsp;&emsp; B. saw &emsp;&emsp; C. to see &emsp;&emsp; D. seeing
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 代词辨析（反身代词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：于是我决定自己做午饭。强调“独自、亲自”用反身代词 myself。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把番茄洗净并切成小块。cut...into... 把……切成……。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用勺子小心地把蛋壳取出来。修饰动词 take out 用副词 carefully。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（时间状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我太快把鸡蛋倒进锅里时，油溅了起来。when 当……时。before 在……之前；after 在……之后；until 直到。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（come first）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说过安全应该放在第一位。come first 表示“优先；最重要”。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 系动词后接形容词
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房闻起来很香。smell 作系动词，后接形容词 good 表气味好。well 作形容词表“健康的”，不符。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be surprised to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母回家看到桌上的饭菜很惊讶。be surprised to do sth. 惊讶地做某事，用不定式 to see。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说味道达不到餐厅水平，但充满了爱。转折用 but。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天下午妈妈教我如何正确握刀。表示方式用 how。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析（定冠词表特指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我答应在接下来的那个周末再练习。the following weekend 表示“接下来的那个周末”，following 前常用定冠词 the 表特指。故选 C。
