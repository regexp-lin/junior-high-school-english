# 中考英语完形填空：年级课间操比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our grade held a break-time exercise competition. Each class had to show a routine ___1___ music in front of judges. Our PE teacher said teamwork mattered ___2___ than perfect moves.

At first, a few students were shy and ___3___ to practice after class. The monitor organized short sessions every day and recorded videos ___4___ we could see our problems clearly.

Two weeks later, we could follow the beat ___5___ and keep the same pace. On the practice field, we shouted our class number ___6___ and felt like one big family.

On the competition day, I dropped my hat by accident. ___7___ I picked it up quickly and returned to my line. My classmates smiled at me, and I didn't feel embarrassed ___8___.

In the end, we didn't win first prize, ___9___ we got third and a certificate of teamwork. I learned that showing up and ___10___ together is also a kind of success.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. but
2. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most
3. A. refuse &emsp;&emsp; B. refused &emsp;&emsp; C. refusing &emsp;&emsp; D. refuses
4. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
5. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. easily &emsp;&emsp; D. easiest
6. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. louder &emsp;&emsp; D. loudest
7. A. Luckily &emsp;&emsp; B. Lucky &emsp;&emsp; C. Luck &emsp;&emsp; D. Unluckily
8. A. no more &emsp;&emsp; B. not any more &emsp;&emsp; C. any more &emsp;&emsp; D. not more
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. tried &emsp;&emsp; C. trying &emsp;&emsp; D. tries

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个班级都要在评委前伴着音乐展示一套动作。with music 表示“伴随着音乐”。and 连词连接并列成分，此处需介词短语。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：体育老师说团队合作比动作完美更重要。句中有 than，matter 为动词，用 much 的比较级 more 修饰。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态（一般过去时）及并列结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初有些同学害羞，拒绝放学后加练。and 连接 were shy 与空白处，时态一致，用一般过去时 refused。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：班长每天组织短训并录视频，以便我们能清楚看到问题。so that 引导目的状语从句，表示“以便”。although 尽管；unless 除非；until 直到。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两周后我们能轻松地跟上节拍。修饰动词 follow 用副词 easily。easy 为形容词。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在操场上大声喊出班级口号。修饰动词 shouted 用副词 loudly。loud 也可作副词但多用在口语；此处选项更规范用 loudly。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 词性辨析（副词作评注性状语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：幸运的是，我很快捡起帽子回到队伍。修饰整个句子用副词 Luckily。Lucky 形容词；Luck 名词；Unluckily 不幸地，与语境不符。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（not...any more）
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们对我微笑，我也不再感到尴尬。否定句中“不再”常用 not...any more。此处已有 not（didn't），用 any more。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后我们没有得一等奖，但获得了三等奖和团队合作证书。表转折用 but。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词与 showing 并列）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了坚持出现并一起努力也是一种成功。and 连接 showing up 与空白处，介词 of 后接动名词，用 trying。故选 C。
