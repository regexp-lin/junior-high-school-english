# 中考英语完形填空：和爸爸钓鱼

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday morning, Dad woke me up earlier than usual. He said we would spend a quiet day ___1___ the small lake in the countryside. I was sleepy, ___2___ I still packed a sandwich and a bottle of water.

The road was long, but the fields looked ___3___ in the soft winter light. When we arrived, Dad helped me put bait on the hook and taught me how to ___4___ the line into the water gently.

For an hour, nothing happened. I started to feel ___5___ and wanted to play games on my phone. Dad whispered, "Fishing is about waiting ___6___ patience, not about speed."

Just then, my float moved ___7___. I pulled too hard at first, ___8___ Dad guided my hands. A small fish appeared, shining like silver. We laughed and ___9___ it back into the lake.

On the way home, I realized I did not ___10___ many fish, but I had learned to stay calm and enjoy time with my father.

## 选项

1. A. above &emsp;&emsp; B. below &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. through
2. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. and
3. A. noisy &emsp;&emsp; B. peaceful &emsp;&emsp; C. crowded &emsp;&emsp; D. dirty
4. A. throw &emsp;&emsp; B. tie &emsp;&emsp; C. break &emsp;&emsp; D. hide
5. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. surprised &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. nervous
6. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. about
7. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. clearly &emsp;&emsp; C. suddenly &emsp;&emsp; D. nearly
8. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. though &emsp;&emsp; D. because
9. A. threw &emsp;&emsp; B. sold &emsp;&emsp; C. cut &emsp;&emsp; D. followed
10. A. catch &emsp;&emsp; B. cook &emsp;&emsp; C. buy &emsp;&emsp; D. feed

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说我们要在乡下小湖边度过安静的一天。A 项 above 在……上方；B 项 below 在……下方；C 项 beside 在……旁边；D 项 through 穿过。钓鱼应在湖边 beside the lake。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很困，但我还是装了三明治和一瓶水。前后为转折关系：困倦 vs 仍然准备出行。but 表转折；or 表选择；so 表结果；and 表并列。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：路很长，但田野在柔和的冬日光线下显得很宁静。A 项 noisy 吵闹；B 项 peaceful 宁静的；C 项 crowded 拥挤；D 项 dirty 脏。乡村清晨氛围用 peaceful。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸教我把鱼线轻轻抛入水中。A 项 throw 扔、抛；B 项 tie 系；C 项 break 折断；D 项 hide 藏。throw the line into the water 抛竿入水。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一小时后仍无动静，我开始感到无聊，想玩手机游戏。A 项 bored 无聊的；B 项 surprised 惊讶；C 项 proud 自豪；D 项 nervous 紧张。长时间等待易无聊。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：钓鱼需要的是耐心等待，而不是比速度。with patience 表示"带着耐心"；waiting for patience 不通；without 与句意相反；about 不贴切。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：就在这时，我的浮漂突然动了。A 项 slowly 缓慢；B 项 clearly 清楚地；C 项 suddenly 突然；D 项 nearly 几乎。鱼咬钩常很突然。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始我拉得太猛，直到爸爸扶着我的手引导我。until 表示"直到……才"；if 如果；though 尽管；because 因为。表示在爸爸帮助前一直拉得太猛。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把小鱼放生回湖里。throw back 有"扔回"之意，此处指放生；sell 卖；cut 切；follow 跟随。保护小鱼、放生用 threw it back。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家的路上我意识到我并没有钓到很多鱼，但我学会了保持冷静并享受与爸爸相处的时光。catch fish 钓鱼、捕获鱼；cook 烹饪；buy 购买；feed 喂养。故选 A。
