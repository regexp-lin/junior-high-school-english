# 中考英语完形填空：冬天的第一场大雪

## 完形填空原文

When I woke up early on Saturday, the world outside my window had turned ___1___ white. The first heavy snow of winter had arrived ___2___ the night, covering roofs, cars, and the playground in a soft blanket.

I could not wait to go ___3___. After breakfast, my brother and I put on thick coats, hats, and gloves. The cold air felt ___4___ on my face, but my heart felt warm with excitement.

We built a short snow wall and rolled a huge snowball for the ___5___ of a snowman. My brother found two stones for eyes, ___6___ I used a carrot for the nose. A red scarf from Mom made the snowman look ___7___.

Neighbors came out with shovels, smiling and greeting each other. Even ___8___ work felt easier when people helped one another. Before noon, the path in front of our building was ___9___ enough for walking.

That evening, Dad made hot soup and said winter is not only about cold weather, ___10___ about small joys shared with family. I agreed completely.

## 选项

1. A. suddenly &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. nearly
2. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. during &emsp;&emsp; C. after &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
3. A. outside &emsp;&emsp; B. abroad &emsp;&emsp; C. online &emsp;&emsp; D. upstairs
4. A. fresh &emsp;&emsp; B. sharp &emsp;&emsp; C. sweet &emsp;&emsp; D. deep
5. A. top &emsp;&emsp; B. bottom &emsp;&emsp; C. middle &emsp;&emsp; D. side
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. because
7. A. lonely &emsp;&emsp; B. lively &emsp;&emsp; C. sleepy &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
8. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; C. secret &emsp;&emsp; D. heavy
9. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. wide &emsp;&emsp; C. dark &emsp;&emsp; D. low
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：窗外的世界几乎全白了。A 项 suddenly 突然；B 项 hardly 几乎不；C 项 quietly 安静地；D 项 nearly 几乎、差不多。描述被雪覆盖后的视觉效果，nearly white 表示"近乎一片白"。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冬天的第一场大雪在夜间降临。A 项 before 在……之前；B 项 during 在……期间；C 项 after 在……之后；D 项 beyond 超过。雪在夜里下用 during the night。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我迫不及待想出去。go outside 出去；go abroad 出国；go online 上网；go upstairs 上楼。玩雪应到户外。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冷空气吹在脸上有些刺痛，但我的心里因兴奋而暖暖的。A 项 fresh 清新的；B 项 sharp（寒冷）刺骨的、尖锐的；C 项 sweet 甜的；D 项 deep 深的。形容寒风拂面常用 sharp。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们堆了一堵矮雪墙，并滚了一个大雪球做雪人的中间部分。堆雪人通常分底部、中部、顶部，大雪球常作身体即 middle。top 顶部；bottom 底部；side 侧面。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：哥哥找了两块石头做眼睛，而我用胡萝卜做鼻子。while 可表示对比"而、却"。or 选择；but 转折语气更强；because 原因。并列对比用 while 自然。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈的红围巾让雪人看起来很生动。A 项 lonely 孤独的；B 项 lively 活泼的、生动的；C 项 sleepy 困倦的；D 项 angry 生气的。红围巾点缀使雪人更有生气。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：即使繁重的劳动在人们互相帮助时也变得更容易。A 项 boring 无聊的；B 项 dangerous 危险的；C 项 secret 秘密的；D 项 heavy 繁重的。铲雪劳动用 heavy work。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：中午前，楼前的小路已清理得足够畅通，可以行走。A 项 clear 畅通的、清理干净的；B 项 wide 宽的；C 项 dark 暗的；D 项 low 低的。路被铲雪后变 clear。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说冬天不只是寒冷的天气，更是与家人分享的小快乐。not only ... but (also) ... 为固定结构，意为"不但……而且……"。故选 C。
