# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校合唱团排练

## 完形填空原文

This term, I joined the school choir ___1___ Tuesday and Thursday afternoons. On the first day, I felt shy ___2___ my voice shook when I sang alone. Our conductor, Ms. Han, paired me ___3___ a friendly alto named Lily. She taught me ___4___ breathe before long notes. At first, I couldn’t follow the beat, ___5___ I practiced with a metronome at home. Two weeks later, I could blend my sound ___6___ the group more smoothly. Ms. Han said music is not about being perfect ___7___ about listening to each other. Last Friday, we performed ___8___ song about friendship on stage. The audience clapped loudly, ___9___ made us feel proud. I know I still have much ___10___ learn, but I love every rehearsal.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
2. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间介词 on + 星期  
**【解析】** on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons 表示 “在周二与周四下午”，星期前用 on。in 用于月/年/季节；at 用于具体钟点；to 表方向。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句（because）  
**【解析】** “害羞是因为独唱时声音发抖”，because 表原因。so 表结果；although 让步；if 条件，均不符合。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 pair sb. with sb.  
**【解析】** paired me with a friendly alto 表示 “把我与一位友善的女低音配对”。pair to/for/by 不是此义。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”  
**【解析】** teach me how to breathe 表示 “教我如何呼吸”。what to breathe 不通；when/where 与发声技巧不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词  
**【解析】** “一开始跟不上拍” 与 “在家用节拍器练习” 为转折，用 but。and 顺承；or 选择；so 结果，此处更强调克服问题。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词 with（与……一起）  
**【解析】** blend my sound with the group 表示 “与合唱团的声音融合在一起”。in/on/into 不搭配 blend...group 表达 “与集体融合”。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** not...but... 结构  
**【解析】** not about being perfect but about listening 表示 “不是……而是……”，用 but。and/or/so 不能构成 not...but...。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 a  
**【解析】** perform a song about friendship 表示 “表演一首关于友谊的歌”，泛指用 a。an 用于元音音素开头的词前；the 表特指需上下文已明确该曲；零冠词不与可数单数 song 这样搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句（which 指代整句）  
**【解析】** which made us feel proud 中 which 指代前面整句 “观众热烈鼓掌” 这件事。what 不引导此类定语从句；who/whom 指人。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（have much to do）  
**【解析】** have much to learn 表示 “还有很多要学”，不定式作后置定语。have much for/of/with learn 结构错误。故选 A。
