# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工毛线帽

## 完形填空原文

Winter is coming, so Aunt Judy offered ___1___ teach me to knit a wool hat. She bought soft blue yarn and two needles ___2___ me. At the beginning, my stitches were too tight, ___3___ the hat looked smaller than it should. Aunt Judy told me ___4___ relax my fingers and count stitches quietly. Every evening, I practiced ___5___ half an hour while watching a slow video. After five days, the hat grew ___6___ a bowl, and I felt excited. On the last day, Aunt Judy helped me ___7___ off the yarn and hide the loose ends. When I tried the hat on, it fit ___8___ though it was not perfect. I decided ___9___ it to my cousin because he walks ___10___ school every cold morning.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
6. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
7. A. cut &emsp;&emsp; B. cuts &emsp;&emsp; C. cutting &emsp;&emsp; D. to cut  
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
9. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. to give &emsp;&emsp; C. giving &emsp;&emsp; D. gave  
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（offer to do）  
**【解析】** offer to teach 表示 “主动提出教我”，不定式作宾语。offer for/of/with teach 结构不成立。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 for 表对象  
**【解析】** buy sth. for sb. 表示 “为某人买某物”。buy to me 不自然；with/by 不表达 “为我买”。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（因果）  
**【解析】** 针脚太紧，所以帽子看起来偏小，so 表结果。and 仅并列；but 转折；or 选择，均不符合。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell me to relax 为固定结构。tell for/of/with 不接不定式作宾补。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词 for + 时间段  
**【解析】** practiced for half an hour 表示 “练习了半小时”。in/on/at 不与持续时长 half an hour 这样搭配表 “长达多久”。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 like 表 “像”  
**【解析】** grew like a bowl 表示 “长成像碗的形状”，口语化比喻。grow as 需接从句或身份；for/with 不贴切。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（help sb. (to) do）  
**【解析】** help me to cut off / help me cut off 均可，选项中只有不定式 to cut。cut 原形无 to 时也可，但选项设置选 to cut 符合 help sb. to do。cuts/cutting 不能作 help 后宾补结构。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词（fit well）  
**【解析】** fit well 表示 “很合身”，well 为副词修饰动词 fit。good 为形容词；better/best 为比较级/最高级，句中无比较对象。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide to give 为固定搭配。decide give/giving/gave 结构错误。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 walk to school  
**【解析】** walk to school 表示 “步行上学”。walk in/on/at school 语义不同。故选 C。
