# 中考英语完形填空：班级联欢会

## 完形填空原文

Our class held a farewell party yesterday ___1___ the end of the school year was near. Everyone brought food and drinks to ___2___.

The classroom was decorated ___3___ balloons and ribbons. Some students even made a big banner that read "We Will Miss You." The monitor ___4___ the party and introduced each program.

First, three girls sang a beautiful song together. Their voices were so ___5___ that many students were moved to tears. Then some boys performed a funny skit ___6___ made the whole class laugh.

When it was my turn, I read a letter I had ___7___ to all my classmates. I thanked them for three years of friendship. My voice shook a little, but I managed ___8___ it till the end.

At last, the teacher gave ___9___ of us a small card with a personal message. She said she was proud of our class and wished us a ___10___ future. We took a group photo together — the last one of our middle school life.

## 选项

1. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. although
2. A. share &emsp;&emsp; B. sharing &emsp;&emsp; C. shared &emsp;&emsp; D. shares
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with
4. A. host &emsp;&emsp; B. hosted &emsp;&emsp; C. hosting &emsp;&emsp; D. hosts
5. A. beauty &emsp;&emsp; B. beautifully &emsp;&emsp; C. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. more beautiful
6. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
7. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. wrote &emsp;&emsp; C. writing &emsp;&emsp; D. written
8. A. finish &emsp;&emsp; B. to finish &emsp;&emsp; C. finishing &emsp;&emsp; D. finished
9. A. every &emsp;&emsp; B. all &emsp;&emsp; C. both &emsp;&emsp; D. each
10. A. bright &emsp;&emsp; B. brighter &emsp;&emsp; C. brightest &emsp;&emsp; D. brightly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（because 表原因）
**【解析】** 本句意为：昨天我们班开了一场欢送会因为学年快结束了。because 表原因。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词原形（to + 动词原形表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都带了食物和饮料来分享。to share 中 share 用原形。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be decorated with）
**【解析】** 本句意为：教室用气球和丝带装饰。"be decorated with"表示"用……装饰"。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：班长主持了派对。上下文为过去时，用 hosted。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构（so + adj. + that）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她们的声音如此优美以至于很多同学被感动得流泪。so 后接形容词 beautiful。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句（which 指代物）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些男生表演了一个有趣的小品，逗得全班大笑。先行词 skit 为物，在从句中作主语，用 which。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 过去分词（had + 过去分词 过去完成时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我朗读了一封我写给所有同学的信。"had written"为过去完成时，表示在朗读之前已经写好。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（manage to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的声音有点颤抖，但我设法读完了。"manage to do sth."表示"设法做成某事"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 不定代词（each of 每一个）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师给我们每个人一张写有个人留言的小卡片。"each of us"表示"我们每个人"。every 不与 of 搭配。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她祝愿我们有一个光明的未来。bright 为形容词修饰名词 future。不用比较级，因为无比较对象。故选 A。
