# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工书签

## 完形填空原文

Reading has always been one of my favorite hobbies. To make reading even ___1___ enjoyable, I decided to create some handmade bookmarks for myself and my friends.

I prepared watercolor paper, colored pens, and thin ribbons. First, I cut the paper ___2___ long, narrow strips. Each strip was about fifteen centimeters long and four centimeters ___3___.

Then I drew different pictures on each bookmark. One had a sleeping cat, ___4___ had a little boat sailing on the sea. I painted them ___5___ so that the colors looked bright and lively. On the back of each bookmark, I wrote an encouraging sentence ___6___ "Keep reading, keep growing."

After the paint dried, I punched a small hole at the top and tied a ribbon ___7___ it. The bookmarks looked quite professional. I was ___8___ to see the final results.

The next day, I gave one to each of my close friends. They were all ___9___ and said they would use them every day. Making things with my own hands always gives me a strong sense of ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. many
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. from
3. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. tall &emsp;&emsp; C. wide &emsp;&emsp; D. high
4. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. the other &emsp;&emsp; C. another &emsp;&emsp; D. others
5. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. careless
6. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
7. A. across &emsp;&emsp; B. over &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. along
8. A. pleased &emsp;&emsp; B. pleasant &emsp;&emsp; C. pleasure &emsp;&emsp; D. please
9. A. surprise &emsp;&emsp; B. surprising &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. surprisingly
10. A. achieve &emsp;&emsp; B. achieves &emsp;&emsp; C. achieved &emsp;&emsp; D. achievement

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：为了让阅读更加愉快。even 修饰比较级，enjoyable 为多音节词，比较级用 more enjoyable。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把纸裁成细长的条状。cut ... into ... 表示"把……切成……"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每条大约十五厘米长、四厘米宽。描述纸条的宽度，用 wide。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个画了一只睡着的猫，另一个画了一只在海上航行的小船。one ... another ... 表示"一个……另一个……"（泛指多个中的又一个）。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我仔细地上色。修饰动词 painted，需用副词 carefully。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我写了一句鼓励的话，比如"继续阅读，继续成长"。like 作介词表示"比如、例如"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：穿一条丝带过小孔。through 表示"穿过"（从内部穿过孔洞）。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到最终成品我很高兴。pleased 表示"（人）感到高兴的"，作表语。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（-ed 与 -ing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们都很惊喜。surprised 表示"（人）感到惊讶的"，描述人的感受。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：亲手做东西总是给我一种强烈的成就感。a sense of 后接名词，achievement 为名词，表示"成就"。故选 D。
