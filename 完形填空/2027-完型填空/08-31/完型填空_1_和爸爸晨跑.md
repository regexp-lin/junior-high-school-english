# 中考英语完形填空：和爸爸晨跑

## 完形填空原文

My father has a habit of ___1___ every morning. Last Sunday, he invited me to join him. I was not a morning person, so getting up at six o'clock was really ___2___ for me.

We ran along the river near our home. The air was fresh and ___3___ birds were singing in the trees. At first, I could keep up with my father, but after ten minutes I started to feel ___4___.

"Take it easy. We don't need to run ___5___," Father said, slowing down his pace. He told me that the key to running was not speed ___6___ persistence. We jogged side by side and talked about school life.

When we finished the run, I was ___7___ with sweat, but I felt great inside. Father bought two cups of soy milk ___8___ the way home. We sat on a bench and enjoyed the quiet morning together.

"Can we do this ___9___ next week?" I asked. Father looked at me with surprise and happiness. "Of course!" he replied. Since then, morning jogging ___10___ become our weekend tradition.

## 选项

1. A. jog &emsp;&emsp; B. jogging &emsp;&emsp; C. jogged &emsp;&emsp; D. to jog
2. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. possible &emsp;&emsp; D. wonderful
3. A. a few &emsp;&emsp; B. few &emsp;&emsp; C. a little &emsp;&emsp; D. little
4. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. tiring &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxing
5. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. slow &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. cover &emsp;&emsp; B. covering &emsp;&emsp; C. covered &emsp;&emsp; D. covers
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. again &emsp;&emsp; C. also &emsp;&emsp; D. either
10. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. had &emsp;&emsp; D. has

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我爸爸有每天早上慢跑的习惯。a habit of doing sth.，of 为介词，后接动名词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：六点钟起床对我来说真的很难。由前文"不是早起的人"可知起早很困难，用 difficult。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 数量词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：几只鸟在树上歌唱。birds 为可数名词复数；a few 表示"一些（肯定）"，few 表示"几乎没有（否定）"。语境积极，用 a few。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（-ed 与 -ing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：十分钟后我开始感到累了。feel 为系动词，后接形容词；tired 表示"（人）感到累的"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不需要跑得快。修饰动词 run，需用副词 quickly。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：跑步的关键不是速度而是坚持。not ... but ... 表示"不是……而是……"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语
**【解析】** 本句意为：跑完时我满身是汗。be covered with 表示"被……覆盖"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸在回家的路上买了两杯豆浆。on the way home 表示"在回家路上"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下周我们还能再这样做吗？again 表示"再次"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词时态（现在完成时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那以后，晨跑已经成为我们的周末传统。since then 与现在完成时搭配；morning jogging 为单数，用 has。故选 D。
