# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工毛线球

## 完形填空原文

In the DIY class, our teacher asked us to make wool pom-poms ___1___ old yarn. She gave each group a pair of scissors, cardboard rings, and colorful string. "Wrap the yarn around the rings ___2___ you get a thick ball," she explained.

My partner Tina was ___3___ careful than I was, so her yarn never tangled. ___4___ I wrapped too fast, the threads knotted and I had to start again. Tina didn't laugh at me. Instead, she helped me cut the loops ___5___ tied them tightly in the middle.

When the pom-pom was fluffed up, it looked ___6___ a soft cloud. I pinned it ___7___ my schoolbag as a decoration. The teacher praised our teamwork and said handmade things are often ___8___ valuable because time and love are put into them.

I used to think only expensive gifts ___9___ good, ___10___ now I know a small wool ball can also warm someone's heart.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since
3. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very
4. A. Because &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. If &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless
5. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
8. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
9. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. are &emsp;&emsp; C. was &emsp;&emsp; D. were
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们用旧毛线做毛线球。with 表示“用（材料、工具）”。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词（until）
**【解析】** 本句意为：把线绕在纸环上，直到绕成厚厚一球。until 表示“直到……为止”。when/while/since 不符合步骤逻辑。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的搭档蒂娜比我更细心。有 than，用 more careful。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为我绕得太快，线打结了。表原因用 Because。Although 表让步；If/Unless 表条件。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她帮我在中间剪开线圈并扎紧。cut 与 tied 为顺承并列，用 and。but 转折；or 选择；so 结果。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：蓬松后像一朵软云。look like 看起来像。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把它别在书包上当装饰。on the schoolbag 表示表面。in 表内部；at/to 不自然。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工作品往往更珍贵，因为投入了时间与爱。暗含与流水线商品对比，valuable 多音节，用 more valuable。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 时态与主谓一致
**【解析】** 本句意为：我过去认为只有昂贵的礼物才好。used to think 后接过去看法，宾语从句主语 gifts 为复数，用 were。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但现在我知道一颗小毛线球也能温暖人心。与前文过去想法对比，转折用 but。故选 B。
