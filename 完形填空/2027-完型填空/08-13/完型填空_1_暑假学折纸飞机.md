# 中考英语完形填空：暑假学折纸飞机

## 完形填空原文

During the summer holiday, my brother showed me a video ___1___ a boy made a paper plane that could fly very far. I thought it looked easy, ___2___ I decided to learn it the same afternoon. I found some A4 paper ___3___ was left from last term's project and started to fold it step by step.

The first plane I made ___4___ only two meters before it fell. I felt a little disappointed, ___5___ my brother said, "Try again. The tip must be sharp ___6___ it can cut the air." I followed his advice and tested the second plane on the balcony. To my surprise, it flew ___7___ across the yard than the first one.

That day I learned ___8___ useful lesson: small details can change the result. Now I keep several planes ___9___ I made on my bookshelf, and I plan to teach my classmates ___10___ to fold them after school starts.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. that
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
4. A. fly &emsp;&emsp; B. flew &emsp;&emsp; C. flies &emsp;&emsp; D. flying
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. as if &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as
7. A. far &emsp;&emsp; B. farther &emsp;&emsp; C. farthest &emsp;&emsp; D. the farthest
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. when
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系副词
**【解析】** 本句意为：哥哥给我看了一段视频，视频里一个男孩折了纸飞机。先行词 video 表地点/场景，从句结构完整，用 where 引导定语从句（也可用 in which）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得看起来很简单，所以决定当天下午就学。表因果关系，结果用 so。because 表原因在前；although 让步；unless 条件。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我找到上学期项目剩下的 A4 纸。先行词 paper 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：我做的第一架飞机只飞了两米就掉了。叙述过去发生的事，用一般过去时 flew。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点失望，但哥哥说……。转折用 but。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：机头必须尖，这样才能“切开”空气（飞得更稳更远）。so that 引导目的状语从句，表示“以便”。even though 尽管；as if 好像；as long as 只要。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：它飞得比第一架更远地穿过院子。有 than，用比较级 farther（指距离）。far 原级；farthest/the farthest 为最高级。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天我学到了有用的一课。useful 以辅音音素 /j/ 开头，用 a。an 用于元音音素开头。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我把几架我做的飞机放在书架上。先行词 planes 为物，从句缺宾语，用 that/which。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”/宾语从句简化
**【解析】** 本句意为：开学后打算教同学如何折。teach sb. how to do sth. 教某人如何做。故选 B。
