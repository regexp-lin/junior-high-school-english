# 中考英语完形填空：学做凉面

## 完形填空原文

When the weather turned hot, Dad promised ___1___ me his cold noodle recipe. "The secret is in the sauce," he said ___2___ a smile.

We boiled the noodles until they were just tender, then rinsed ___3___ under cold running water. ___4___ doing that, the noodles would stay chewy instead ___5___ turning soft and sticky.

Dad mixed soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, and a little sugar in a bowl. He let me taste the sauce and asked ___6___ it needed more salt. I nodded because it was already ___7___ than the noodles sold at the shop near our school.

Finally, we topped the noodles ___8___ cucumber strips and roasted peanuts. The dish looked simple, ___9___ every bite was full of flavor. I took a photo and sent ___10___ to my cousin immediately.

## 选项

1. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. to teach &emsp;&emsp; C. teaching &emsp;&emsp; D. taught
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
3. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. they
4. A. With &emsp;&emsp; B. Without &emsp;&emsp; C. By &emsp;&emsp; D. Through
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. /
6. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how
7. A. tasty &emsp;&emsp; B. tastier &emsp;&emsp; C. tastiest &emsp;&emsp; D. more tastier
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. one

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（promise to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸答应把他做凉面的秘方教给我。promise to do sth. 为固定搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（with a smile）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他微笑着说。with a smile 表示"带着微笑"。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（指代 noodles）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后在流动的冷水下冲洗它们。指代复数 noodles 用 them。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（Without 没有……就）
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果不这样做，面条就会变得软黏。Without doing that 表示否定条件。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（instead of）
**【解析】** 本句意为：从而保持筋道，而不会变得软黏。instead of 后接动名词。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词（if 是否）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他让我尝酱汁并问是否需要再加盐。asked 后接是否，用 if。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为已经比学校附近店里卖的更有味。than 提示用比较级 tastier。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（top…with…）
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后我们在面条上放上黄瓜丝和烤花生。top…with… 表示"在……上放配料"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（转折）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这道菜看起来简单，但每一口都很有味道。转折用 but。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词（指代 photo）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我立刻把照片发给表姐。指代单数 photo 用 it。故选 A。
