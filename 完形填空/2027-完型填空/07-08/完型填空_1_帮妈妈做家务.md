# 中考英语完形填空：帮妈妈做家务

## 完形填空原文

Since the summer vacation started, Mom has been busier ___1___ usual at her office. I wanted to share the housework ___2___ she could rest a little after work.

Every morning, I made the bed and swept the floor ___3___ breakfast. In the afternoon, I watered the plants on the balcony ___4___ the hottest hours had passed.

Yesterday, I tried ___5___ for the first time. Mom taught me how to separate the whites from the colors. Some shirts ___6___ carefully because they were made of silk.

When Mom came home, she noticed the clean floor and the fresh smell in the air. She hugged me and said I was ___7___ helpful than she had expected. Her smile made me feel ___8___ all the tiredness was worth it.

I know I still have ___9___ to learn, ___10___ I am willing to keep trying.

## 选项

1. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. to
2. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even if &emsp;&emsp; C. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; D. although
3. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. while
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. though
5. A. wash &emsp;&emsp; B. washing &emsp;&emsp; C. to wash &emsp;&emsp; D. washed
6. A. handle &emsp;&emsp; B. handled &emsp;&emsp; C. were handled &emsp;&emsp; D. were handling
7. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. many
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. whether
9. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. a lot &emsp;&emsp; D. lots
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（比较级 + than）
**【解析】** 本句意为：暑假开始后，妈妈在公司比平时更忙。busier than usual 为固定比较结构。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（so that 表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想分担家务，以便她下班后能休息一下。so that 引导目的状语从句。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（时间先后）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天早饭后整理床铺并扫地。after breakfast 符合日常顺序。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（when 当……时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：下午最热时段过去后，我给阳台上的植物浇水。when 引导时间状语从句。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（try to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：昨天我第一次尝试洗衣服。try to do 表示"尝试做某事"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些衬衫需要小心处理，因为是丝绸的。shirts 与 handle 为被动关系，用 were handled。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说我比她预想的更有用。than 提示用比较级 more helpful。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的微笑让我觉得一切辛苦都值得。feel 后接 that 引导的宾语从句。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词/名词修饰（much to learn）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道我还有很多要学。have much to learn 中 much 修饰不可数概念。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（转折）
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我愿意继续尝试。与前文"还有很多要学"形成转折，用 but。故选 C。
