# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工衍纸

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, I went to a community center ___1___ a DIY workshop about quilling. Quilling means rolling thin paper strips ___2___ beautiful shapes like flowers and leaves.

At first, I thought it would be easy, ___3___ my fingers were too stiff. The teacher told me ___4___ patiently, “Press gently and turn the tool slowly.” After practicing for twenty minutes, I could make a simple ___5___.

Then we moved on ___6___ a small card. I glued the paper patterns onto the card ___7___ they wouldn’t fall off. When I showed my work to Mom, her eyes lit up ___8___ happiness.

This experience taught me ___9___ skill needs time and practice. I plan to make more quilling gifts ___10___ my friends on their birthdays.

## 选项

1. A. join &emsp;&emsp; B. to join &emsp;&emsp; C. joining &emsp;&emsp; D. joined
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. me &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. mine &emsp;&emsp; D. myself
5. A. circle &emsp;&emsp; B. circles &emsp;&emsp; C. a circle &emsp;&emsp; D. the circles
6. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made
7. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as
8. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
9. A. some &emsp;&emsp; B. any &emsp;&emsp; C. each &emsp;&emsp; D. every
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）  
**【解析】** “went to a community center” 的目的是参加手工坊，常用不定式表目的：went ... to join ...。join 为动词原形不能直接跟在 center 后；joining 多表伴随；joined 是过去式，不能作目的状语。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析与固定搭配  
**【解析】** roll ... into ... 表示“把……卷成……”，into 引出结果或形状。in/on/at 与 “beautiful shapes” 搭配不当。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词辨析  
**【解析】** 前句以为简单，后句手指僵硬，语义转折，用 but。and 表并列，or 表选择，so 表结果，均不符合 “原以为……实际上……” 的对比。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** tell 后接间接宾语用人称代词宾格：told me。my/mine 不能单独作 told 的宾语；myself 用于强调“自己”，不符合师生对话语境。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 可数名词与冠词  
**【解析】** “make a simple circle” 表示“做一个简单的圆形”，可数名词单数首次出现，用 a simple circle。单独用 circle 缺冠词；circles 与 a simple 矛盾；the circles 无特指依据。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）与动词短语  
**【解析】** 填入 to make 后全句为 “Then we moved on to make a small card.”，即接着进入“制作小卡片”的环节，不定式 to make 作状语，表示接下来的动作，语义与结构均成立。make/making/made 直接接在 moved on 后均无法构成正确句型。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词辨析（目的状语从句）  
**【解析】** 把图案粘在卡片上，目的是它们不会掉下来。so that 引导目的状语从句，意为“以便，为了”。even though 尽管；as soon as 一……就……；as long as 只要，均不符合。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表伴随  
**【解析】** eyes light up with happiness 表示“眼里因喜悦而发亮”。with 引出情绪或状态。of/for/about 搭配不如 with 自然。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 限定词辨析（some / any / each / every）  
**【解析】** 此处总结普遍道理：“每一项技能都需要时间和练习”，every skill 表示“任何一种技能都……”，语气概括性强。some/any 与单数 skill 搭配不贴切；each 强调“各自”，多用于已知若干个体，every 更合谚语式概括。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 for 表对象  
**【解析】** make sth. for sb. 表示“为某人做某物”。为朋友在生日时准备礼物，用 for。to/from/with 不如 for 准确。故选 A。
