# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校读书分享会

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, our school held a reading ___1___ in the hall. I was nervous ___2___ I had never spoken in front of so many people before. My English teacher encouraged me ___3___ and said, “Just share what you truly feel about the book.”

I chose *Charlotte’s Web* because it was the first English story ___4___ made me cry. While waiting for my turn, my hands were shaking ___5___. When I finally walked onto the stage, I took a deep breath and started slowly. To my surprise, the audience listened ___6___ and nodded from time to time.

After my speech, there was warm applause. A girl even came up ___7___ me a small notebook as a gift. “Your ideas are clear and honest,” she smiled. That moment, I realized ___8___ important confidence is.

On the way home, I thought about ___9___ I could read next. Maybe I will join the school book club ___10___ and recommend more stories to my classmates.

## 选项

1. A. meeting &emsp;&emsp; B. party &emsp;&emsp; C. sharing &emsp;&emsp; D. competition
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
3. A. hardly &emsp;&emsp; B. warmly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. sadly
4. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
5. A. heavy &emsp;&emsp; B. heavily &emsp;&emsp; C. heavier &emsp;&emsp; D. heaviest
6. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. more careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. most carefully
7. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. to give &emsp;&emsp; C. giving &emsp;&emsp; D. gave
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
9. A. what book &emsp;&emsp; B. what books &emsp;&emsp; C. which book &emsp;&emsp; D. how book
10. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. later &emsp;&emsp; C. latest &emsp;&emsp; D. lately

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 名词辨析与语境理解  
**【解析】** 下文提到 “share what you truly feel about the book” 以及在台上发言、观众鼓掌等，说明活动核心是“分享阅读感受”。meeting（会议）、party（聚会）、competition（比赛）均不如 sharing（分享会）贴切。reading sharing 表示“读书分享会”。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词辨析（原因状语从句）  
**【解析】** 前句 “I was nervous” 与后句 “I had never spoken…” 之间是因果关系：紧张是因为从未在这么多人面前讲过话。because 引导原因状语从句。although 表让步，unless 表条件（除非），until 表时间（直到），均不符合逻辑。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词辨析  
**【解析】** encourage sb. 需要积极、支持性的方式修饰。warmly（热情地）符合老师鼓励学生上台的语境。hardly（几乎不）、loudly（大声地，易含干扰）、sadly（悲伤地）均不合适。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词  
**【解析】** 先行词是 “the first English story”，指物，且从句 “___ made me cry” 缺主语，应用关系代词 which/that。此处选项给出 which。who 先行词为人；what 不引导定语从句；whose 表所属。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 shaking（发抖）要用副词。heavily 表示“厉害地、剧烈地”，hands were shaking heavily 意为“手抖得厉害”。heavy 是形容词；heavier/heaviest 是比较级和最高级，此处无比较含义。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析  
**【解析】** listened 是动词，需用副词修饰。carefully（认真地）说明观众认真听。careful 是形容词；more careful/most carefully 在此无比较对象或结构不当。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）  
**【解析】** “A girl even came up ___ me a notebook” 表示女孩走上来的目的是“给我”本子，用不定式 to give 作目的状语最自然，相当于 came up to give me...。单纯并列两个谓语需加 and；giving 表伴随不如表目的贴切；gave 会造成双谓语错误。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词  
**【解析】** “I realized ___ important confidence is” 中，从句强调形容词 important 的程度，用 how：how important confidence is（自信有多么重要）。what 后常接名词短语；why/when 与句意不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句中的疑问词 + 名词  
**【解析】** think about 后接宾语从句，表示“接下来读哪一本书”，用 what book 很自然。what books 侧重多本，与 “next” 的一次性选择略不贴合；which book 在特定范围内更常用，此处无明确范围；how book 结构错误。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词 late / later / lately 辨析  
**【解析】** join... later 表示“之后再加入”，符合“回家路上想接下来读什么，也许稍后加入读书俱乐部”的时间先后。lately 意为“近来”；late（晚地）、latest（最新的）均不适合修饰 join。故选 B。
