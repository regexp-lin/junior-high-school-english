# 中考英语完形填空：放鞭炮烟花

## 完形填空原文

In our town, fireworks are allowed only ___1___ special zones on certain holidays. My father took me to an open square ___2___ many families had already gathered. The cold wind bit my cheeks, ___3___ the excitement in the air kept me warm.

A volunteer explained safety rules ___4___ we lit anything: stand back, wear gloves, and never point sparklers ___5___ people. I listened carefully, knowing one careless move could cause ___6___ trouble.

When the first rocket rose, gold flowers burst ___7___ the dark sky. Children covered their ears ___8___ still stared upward. My heart jumped ___9___ each loud boom, yet I felt brave standing beside Dad.

On the way home, he said beauty needs limits, ___10___ respect for rules protects everyone. I agreed, carrying the memory of light safely shared.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
2. A. there &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. which
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since
5. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. serious &emsp;&emsp; B. seriously &emsp;&emsp; C. more serious &emsp;&emsp; D. most serious
7. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. across &emsp;&emsp; D. against
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：烟花只允许在特定区域的特定假日燃放。in special zones 在特定区域内。A 项 on 在……上；B 项 at 在某点；D 项 by 凭借。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父亲带我去一个开阔广场，许多家庭已聚集在那里。先行词 square 表地点，从句缺状语用 where。A 项 there 不能引导定语从句；B 项 when 表时间；D 项 which 多作主语/宾语。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：寒风刺脸，但空气中的兴奋让我暖和。转折用 but。A 项 and；C 项 or；D 项 so。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在我们点燃任何东西之前说明安全规则。before 在……之前。A 项 after；C 项 while；D 项 since。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要把烟花棒指向人。point at 指向。B 项 point to 指向（方向）；C、D 不搭配。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个粗心举动可能造成严重麻烦。trouble 为名词，用形容词 serious 修饰。B 项 seriously 副词；C、D 比较级/最高级此处不必要。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：金色礼花在夜空绽开。across the sky 划过/遍布天空。A 项 on；B 项 in；D 项 against。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：孩子们捂耳却仍抬头看。转折用 but。A 项 and；C 项 or；D 项 so。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一次巨响我都心一跳。with each boom 表伴随。A 项 jump to 不符；B 项 for；C 项 at。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：美需要界限，因为对规则的尊重保护每个人。表原因用 because。B 项 although；C 项 unless；D 项 if。故选 A。
