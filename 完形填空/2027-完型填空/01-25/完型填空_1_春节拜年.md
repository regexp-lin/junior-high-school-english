# 中考英语完形填空：春节拜年

## 完形填空原文

On the first morning of the lunar new year, I put ___1___ my new jacket and followed my parents to visit relatives. We carried gift boxes ___2___ fruit and nuts, walking along streets decorated ___3___ red lanterns.

At Uncle Li's home, I bowed slightly and said the polite greeting I had practiced ___4___ week. He smiled, praised my manners, ___5___ handed me a red envelope. I thanked him twice because I was a little nervous.

We drank sweet tea and talked ___6___ everyone's health and school life. My cousin showed me her new skateboard, ___7___ we promised to practice together later. Before leaving, my mother reminded me ___8___ shoes at the door and not to rush upstairs with dirty soles.

Walking home, snow began ___9___ fall lightly. I thought visiting relatives was not ___10___ about gifts; it was about respect and warm connections.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on
4. A. all &emsp;&emsp; B. whole &emsp;&emsp; C. every &emsp;&emsp; D. each
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. for
7. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. to change &emsp;&emsp; C. changing &emsp;&emsp; D. changed
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for
10. A. only &emsp;&emsp; B. almost &emsp;&emsp; C. nearly &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我穿上新外套。put on 穿上。A 项 put in 投入；C 项 put at 估计；D 项 put with 忍受（非 put up with）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：提着装有水果和坚果的礼盒。with 表伴随、装有。A 项 of；C 项 for；D 项 by。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：街道用红灯笼装饰。be decorated with 用……装饰。A 项 by；C 项 in；D 项 on。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：练习了一整周。all week 整个星期。B 项 whole 需 the whole week；C 项 every 后接单数名词；D 项 each 不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他笑着夸我有礼貌，并递给我红包。smiled, praised 与 handed 并列。A 项 or；B 项 but；D 项 so。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：谈论大家的健康与学业。talk about 谈论。B 项 talk with 与某人交谈；C、D 不搭配。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐给我看了新滑板，我们约定之后一起练。并列顺承用 and。A 项 but；C 项 or；D 项 so。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：提醒我在门口换鞋。remind sb. to do sth.。A 项原形；C 项 doing；D 项过去式。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：雪开始轻轻落下。begin to do sth. 开始做。B 项 from；C 项 with；D 项 for 均不能与 begin 构成 begin...fall 的正确结构。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：拜年不只是关于礼物。not only about 与后文 it was about 呼应。B、C、D 不符语境。故选 A。
