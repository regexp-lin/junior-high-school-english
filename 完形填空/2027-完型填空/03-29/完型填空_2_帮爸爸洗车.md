# 中考英语完形填空：帮爸爸洗车

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday morning, Dad asked me ___1___ help him wash the car. I agreed happily because I wanted to earn some pocket ___2___.

We carried a bucket of water, a sponge ___3___ some soap outside. Dad showed me how to wet the car first and then rub it ___4___ the soapy sponge.

I started with the roof. It was ___5___ than I thought because the car was quite tall. I had to stand on tiptoe to reach the top.

"Don't forget the wheels," Dad said. The wheels were the ___6___ part. There was dried mud everywhere. I spent a long time ___7___ them clean.

After rinsing off all the soap, we dried the car ___8___ soft towels. The car looked brand new under the sunshine.

Dad was pleased ___9___ my work. He gave me twenty yuan and said, "You did a great job." I felt proud because I earned the money ___10___ my own effort.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. on
2. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. food &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. water
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
5. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. hard &emsp;&emsp; D. harder
6. A. cleanest &emsp;&emsp; B. easiest &emsp;&emsp; C. dirtiest &emsp;&emsp; D. newest
7. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. to make
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
9. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸让我帮他洗车。ask sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示"要求某人做某事"。for/of/on 不符合 ask 的结构。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我高兴地答应了因为我想赚点零花钱。pocket money 为固定搭配，表示"零花钱"。food/time/water 不符合"赚取"的语境。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把一桶水、一块海绵和一些肥皂搬到外面。三者并列列举，用 and。or/but/so 不符合列举。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用打了肥皂的海绵擦拭车身。with the soapy sponge 表示"用海绵"，介词 with 表工具。in/on/by 不如 with 自然表示使用工具。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这比我想象的更难。than 提示比较级，且后文说"车很高要踮脚"，说明有难度，用 harder。easy/easier 与"困难"语境相反；hard 为原级。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮子是最脏的部分。后文提到"到处是干泥巴"，说明轮子最脏，用 dirtiest。cleanest 为"最干净"；easiest 为"最容易"；newest 为"最新"，均不符合。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/spend...doing
**【解析】** 本句意为：我花了很长时间把它们弄干净。spend time doing sth. 为固定搭配。make/made/to make 不符合 spend 的搭配。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们用柔软的毛巾擦干了车。with soft towels 表示"用毛巾"，with 表工具。in/on/by 不如 with 自然。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸对我的工作很满意。be pleased with... 为固定搭配，表示"对……满意"。at/for/of 不符合 pleased 的搭配。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我靠自己的努力赚到了钱。by my own effort 表示"通过自己的努力"，by 表方式手段。in/on/with 不如 by 贴切表示"凭借"。故选 A。
