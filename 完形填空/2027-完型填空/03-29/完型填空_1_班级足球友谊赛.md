# 中考英语完形填空：班级足球友谊赛

## 完形填空原文

Our class had a friendly football match ___1___ Class Three after school on Wednesday. The weather was perfect — sunny ___2___ not too hot.

Before the match, our PE teacher reminded us ___3___ fair play. "Winning is not everything," he said. "Enjoy the game and respect your opponents."

The first half was very intense. Neither team could score ___4___ both sides defended well. I played as a midfielder and ran back ___5___ forth across the field.

In the second half, Li Wei received a long pass ___6___ kicked the ball into the goal. Our team jumped with joy. However, Class Three equalized ten minutes ___7___.

With five minutes left, I saw an opening. My teammate passed me the ball. I aimed carefully ___8___ shot. The ball flew past the goalkeeper and hit the net!

We won 2 to 1. After the match, we shook ___9___ with the other team. Everyone was smiling.

This match taught me that teamwork is more important ___10___ individual skill.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. with
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. then
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. ago &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. later &emsp;&emsp; D. after
8. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. heads &emsp;&emsp; B. hands &emsp;&emsp; C. arms &emsp;&emsp; D. feet
10. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. from

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班和三班进行了一场友谊足球赛。a match against... 表示"与……的比赛"。to/for/with 不如 against 准确表示"对阵"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：天气很好——阳光明媚但不太热。sunny 与 not too hot 之间有轻微转折（晴天通常热，但这次不热），用 but。or/and/so 不如 but 表达对比。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：体育老师提醒我们关于公平竞赛的事。remind sb. about sth. 表示"提醒某人注意某事"。of 也可搭配 remind，但此处 about fair play 更自然表示"关于"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两队都无法得分因为双方防守都很好。because 引导原因状语从句，解释为什么无法得分。if 为"如果"；although 为"虽然"；unless 为"除非"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在场上来回跑。back and forth 为固定搭配，表示"来回地"。or/but/then 不符合此搭配。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：李伟接到一个长传并将球踢进球门。received 与 kicked 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：三班在十分钟后扳平了比分。later 表示"之后"，ten minutes later 为"十分钟之后"。ago 用于一般过去时但不接在"数字+时间+后"；before 为"之前"；after 为介词/连词，不直接修饰时间段。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我仔细瞄准并射门。aimed 与 shot 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：赛后我们和对方握手。shake hands with... 为固定搭配，表示"与……握手"。heads/arms/feet 不符合"握手"的表达。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 比较级结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：团队合作比个人技术更重要。more important 后接 than 表比较。as/like/from 不符合比较级结构。故选 C。
