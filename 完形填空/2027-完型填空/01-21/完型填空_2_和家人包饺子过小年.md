# 中考英语完形填空：和家人包饺子过小年

## 完形填空原文

The Little New Year evening was cold, ___1___ our kitchen felt warm and busy. Dad rolled the dough into thin circles ___2___ Mom mixed pork and cabbage filling. My job was to press the edges ___3___ so the dumplings would not open while boiling. Steam rose from the pot on the stove, and the radio played a cheerful song in the background.

My little sister tried to make a funny shape and ___4___ flour on her nose. Dad pretended to be a strict judge and scored each dumpling shape from one to ten, which made Grandma laugh loudly. Everyone laughed, ___5___ nobody minded the mess. Grandma told an old story ___6___ we worked, and the smell of ginger filled the air.

When the dumplings floated in the pot, Mom scooped them ___7___ a big spoon. We sat around the table, dipped them ___8___ vinegar, and wished each other good luck. I felt lucky ___9___ I could share such a simple moment ___10___ my family.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. tightly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
4. A. got &emsp;&emsp; B. kept &emsp;&emsp; C. left &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot
5. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. if
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. though &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
7. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. about
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
9. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小年夜很冷，“但”厨房温暖又热闹。室外冷与室内暖形成转折。A 项 and 并列；C 项 or 选择；D 项 so 结果。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸擀面皮，“同时”妈妈拌馅。while 表两个动作同时进行。A 项 after 之后；C 项 until 直到；D 项 unless 除非。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的任务是把边捏“紧”，以免煮的时候开口。A 项 carelessly 粗心；B 项 tightly 紧；C 项 loudly 大声；D 项 slowly 慢。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹想捏个有趣的形状，结果面粉弄到鼻子上。get sth. on... 表示“把……弄到……上”。B 项 kept 保持；C 项 left 留下；D 项 forgot 忘记。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都笑了，“但”没人介意乱。转折：笑归笑，不介意。A 项 because；B 项 so；D 项 if。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一边干活，奶奶一边讲故事。as 表示“当……时/一边……一边”。A 项 if；C 项 though 尽管；D 项 unless 除非。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈用大汤勺把饺子捞起来。with a big spoon 表示使用工具。A 项 by 通过；C 项 from 从；D 项 about 关于。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：蘸醋吃。dip...into... 表示把……蘸进……里。A 项 in 不如 into 强调“浸入”；C 项 with 表伴随；D 项 on 在……上。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（宾语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得自己很幸运，因为我能分享这样的时刻。feel lucky that... 后接 that 引导的宾语从句。B 项 whether 是否；C 项 when 何时；D 项 where 何地。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：和家人分享这样的简单时刻。share sth. with sb. 为固定搭配。A 项 for；B 项 to；C 项 of 均不搭配 share 表对象。故选 D。
