# 中考英语完形填空：学做风筝

## 完形填空原文

Uncle Wang runs a small workshop near the river park. Last Sunday, he taught me ___1___ a simple kite. He cut bamboo sticks into thin strips and tied them ___2___ strong string. I ___3___ him the tape whenever he asked for it. Tools hung neatly on the wall, and the smell of glue reminded me of art class at school.

When we covered the frame ___4___ colorful paper, the kite looked like a bright bird. Uncle Wang checked every corner twice to make sure the paper would not tear in strong wind. Uncle Wang added a long tail ___5___ balance. "Wait for a windy day," he said, "___6___ the kite will fly higher."

On Wednesday, the wind finally came. I ran ___7___ the grass and let the string go little by little. The kite shook at first, ___8___ then it rose steadily into the sky. My classmates cheered ___9___ they saw it from the playground.

Making something with my own hands felt ___10___ than buying a toy from a shop.

## 选项

1. A. to fly &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. to buy &emsp;&emsp; D. to paint
2. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
3. A. passed &emsp;&emsp; B. threw &emsp;&emsp; C. lent &emsp;&emsp; D. sold
4. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
5. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. against &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. across &emsp;&emsp; B. above &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. below
8. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. though &emsp;&emsp; D. until
10. A. stranger &emsp;&emsp; B. more special &emsp;&emsp; C. cheaper &emsp;&emsp; D. easier

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他教我“制作”一只简单的风筝。teach sb. to do sth. 为固定结构。A 项 to fly 飞；B 项 to make 制作；C 项 to buy 购买；D 项 to paint 涂色。与 workshop、竹条、纸面制作流程一致。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用结实的线把它们绑起来。with 表示“用（工具/材料）”。A 项 of ……的；B 项 for 为了；D 项 about 关于。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每当他要，我就把胶带递给他。A 项 passed 传递；B 项 threw 扔，不礼貌且不安全；C 项 lent 借出，tape 不是借出语境；D 项 sold 出售。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们用彩纸蒙在骨架上。cover...with... 为固定搭配，表示“用……覆盖”。A 项 of；B 项 in；D 项 by。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：加一条长尾以保持平衡。for balance 表示目的“为了平衡”。B 项 against 对抗；C 项 without 没有；D 项 beyond 超出。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：“等刮风的日子，风筝会飞得更高。”此处为“祈使句 + and + 结果分句”结构，表示顺承结果，用 and 最自然。B 项 or 否则/选择；C 项 but 转折；D 项 so 表“所以”时多与 because 呼应，此处不如 and 常见。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我跑过草地。run across the grass 表示横穿草地。B 项 above 在上方；C 项 against 靠着；D 项 below 在下面。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：风筝起初摇晃，“但”随后稳稳升空。转折用 but。A 项 because；B 项 so；D 项 unless。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们“当”他们从操场看见时欢呼起来。when 引导时间状语从句。A 项 if 如果；C 项 though 尽管；D 项 until 直到。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：亲手做的东西比店里买的玩具感觉更特别。A 项 stranger 更奇怪；B 项 more special 更特别；C 项 cheaper 更便宜；D 项 easier 更容易。情感升华用 more special 最合适。故选 B。
