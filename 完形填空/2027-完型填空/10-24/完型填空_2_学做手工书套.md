# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工书套

## 完形填空原文

To protect our textbooks from getting dirty, our class teacher suggested that we ___1___ book covers for them. Instead of buying plastic covers, she encouraged us to make handmade ones from cloth.

She said cloth covers were not only prettier ___2___ also more environmentally friendly. This idea got everyone excited. Some students had never sewn before, so the teacher ___3___ to give us a quick lesson.

She brought different kinds of cloth to class. I chose a piece with small blue flowers on it because it ___4___ me of spring. The teacher measured my textbook and helped me cut the cloth to the right ___5___.

Folding the edges and sewing them was the ___6___ step. I had to be very patient because the needle kept slipping out of my fingers. The boy next to me was having even more trouble — he accidentally ___7___ his finger twice!

After about forty minutes, I finished my first book cover. I slipped my English textbook inside and was ___8___ to see how well it fitted. The blue flower pattern made the book look much more ___9___.

Our teacher displayed several students' book covers and said, "I hope you'll keep these covers ___10___ the whole school year. They will remind you that small efforts can make everyday things special."

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. to make  
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
3. A. offer &emsp;&emsp; B. offers &emsp;&emsp; C. offered &emsp;&emsp; D. offering  
4. A. remind &emsp;&emsp; B. reminded &emsp;&emsp; C. reminding &emsp;&emsp; D. reminds  
5. A. size &emsp;&emsp; B. color &emsp;&emsp; C. shape &emsp;&emsp; D. weight  
6. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. hard &emsp;&emsp; D. hardest  
7. A. prick &emsp;&emsp; B. pricked &emsp;&emsp; C. pricking &emsp;&emsp; D. pricks  
8. A. please &emsp;&emsp; B. pleased &emsp;&emsp; C. pleasant &emsp;&emsp; D. pleasure  
9. A. attract &emsp;&emsp; B. attracted &emsp;&emsp; C. attractive &emsp;&emsp; D. attractively  
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 虚拟语气（suggest + 动词原形）  
**【解析】** suggest that we (should) make，should 省略后用动词原形 make。made 为过去式；making 为动名词；to make 为不定式。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** not only...but also... 结构  
**【解析】** not only prettier but also more environmentally friendly，but 与 not only 搭配构成固定结构。and/or/so 不与 not only 搭配。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，动词用过去式 offered。offer 为原形；offers 为三单现在时；offering 为现在分词。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，reminded 为过去式。remind 为原形；reminding 为现在分词；reminds 为三单现在时。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 名词辨析  
**【解析】** the right size 意为"合适的尺寸"，裁剪布料需要量好尺寸。color 意为"颜色"；shape 意为"形状"；weight 意为"重量"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（语境推理）  
**【解析】** 后文说"需要非常耐心""针一直滑落"，说明这一步很难，用 hard（困难的）。easy 意为"容易的"与语境矛盾；easier/hardest 为比较级/最高级（此处非比较结构）。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，accidentally pricked 为过去式。prick 为原形；pricking 为现在分词；pricks 为三单。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）  
**【解析】** was pleased to see 意为"很高兴看到"，pleased 修饰人的感受。please 为动词；pleasant 意为"令人愉快的"修饰事物；pleasure 为名词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语（more + adj.）  
**【解析】** much more attractive 意为"吸引人得多"，look 为系动词后接形容词。attract 为动词；attracted 意为"被吸引的"修饰人；attractively 为副词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 for + 时间段  
**【解析】** for the whole school year 意为"整个学年"，for 表时间段。in/by/at 不表持续时间段。故选 A。
