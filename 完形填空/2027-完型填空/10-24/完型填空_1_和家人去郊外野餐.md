# 中考英语完形填空：和家人去郊外野餐

## 完形填空原文

The autumn weather was so pleasant ___1___ my family decided to have a picnic in the countryside. We got up early on Sunday and packed everything we needed: sandwiches, fruit, juice, and a big blanket.

We drove for about forty minutes and arrived at a quiet spot beside a small river. The scenery was breathtaking — golden leaves ___2___ the hillsides, and the river sparkled in the sunlight.

My father set up the blanket under a large tree ___3___ my mother arranged the food. My little brother ran straight to the river and started throwing stones into the water. Each stone made a splash, and he laughed every time it ___4___.

After eating, my mother suggested that we ___5___ a walk along the river. The path was narrow but beautiful. Wild flowers grew on ___6___ sides, and butterflies danced around them. I took many photos because I wanted ___7___ this beautiful moment.

Suddenly, my brother spotted a small rabbit behind a bush. He wanted to catch it, but my father stopped him and said, "Wild animals should be free. We should respect ___8___ home." The rabbit looked at us for a second and then hopped away.

On the way home, everyone was tired but ___9___. My mother said we should do this more often because spending time in nature is good ___10___ both our body and mind.

## 选项

1. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
2. A. cover &emsp;&emsp; B. covers &emsp;&emsp; C. covered &emsp;&emsp; D. covering  
3. A. while &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
4. A. happen &emsp;&emsp; B. happens &emsp;&emsp; C. happened &emsp;&emsp; D. happening  
5. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. took &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. taken  
6. A. all &emsp;&emsp; B. each &emsp;&emsp; C. every &emsp;&emsp; D. both  
7. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. remembered &emsp;&emsp; C. remembering &emsp;&emsp; D. to remember  
8. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs  
9. A. happily &emsp;&emsp; B. happier &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. happiness  
10. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** so...that... 结果状语从句  
**【解析】** so pleasant that 构成结果状语从句，意为"如此宜人以至于"。which/when/what 不与 so 搭配引导结果从句。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作后置定语  
**【解析】** golden leaves covered the hillsides 可理解为独立主谓结构，但此处为描写场景，covered 为过去式动词作谓语。cover 为原形；covers 为三单现在时（此处 leaves 为复数且为过去叙述）；covering 为现在分词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词 while 表同时  
**【解析】** "爸爸铺毯子的同时妈妈整理食物"，while 表两个动作同时进行。until 表"直到"；unless 表"除非"；although 表"虽然"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** every time it happened 为一般过去时，描述过去反复发生的事件。happen 为原形；happens 为三单现在时；happening 为现在分词。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 虚拟语气（suggest + 动词原形）  
**【解析】** suggest that we (should) take a walk，should 省略后用动词原形。took 为过去式；taking 为动名词；taken 为过去分词。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** both + 名词复数  
**【解析】** on both sides 意为"在两边"，both 表两者都。all 表三者以上；each/every 后接单数名词。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（want to do）  
**【解析】** wanted to remember 意为"想要记住"，want 后接不定式。remember 缺 to；remembered 为过去式；remembering 为动名词。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** respect their home 意为"尊重它们的家"，修饰名词 home 用形容词性物主代词 their。they 为主格；them 为宾格；theirs 为名词性物主代词。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** was tired but happy，but 连接两个并列的形容词作表语。happily 为副词；happier 为比较级（无比较对象）；happiness 为名词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be good for  
**【解析】** be good for 意为"对……有益"。be good at 意为"擅长"；be good in/to 不表"有益"。故选 B。
