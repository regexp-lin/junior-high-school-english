# 中考英语完形填空：班级英语话剧排练

## 完形填空原文

Our class chose a short English play for the school art festival. I played the role of a doctor, ___1___ I had only five lines.

At the first rehearsal, everyone read their lines ___2___. The play sounded boring. Our teacher suggested adding gestures and changing our ___3___ on stage.

I practiced my lines at home in front of a mirror. I also searched for videos ___4___ how doctors speak calmly. During the next rehearsal, I spoke more ___5___ and made eye contact with the "patient."

Not everything went smoothly. One actor forgot his hat, ___6___ we had to pause. Another scene was too long, ___7___ we cut two sentences. Teamwork meant solving problems together ___8___ blaming each other.

On performance day, the lights felt bright, ___9___ I remembered my teacher's advice: "Tell the story, don't just read words." When the audience clapped, I knew we had grown ___10___ a real team.

## 选项

1. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
2. A. loudly &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. angrily &emsp;&emsp; D. happily
3. A. positions &emsp;&emsp; B. prices &emsp;&emsp; C. grades &emsp;&emsp; D. scores
4. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
5. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. nervously &emsp;&emsp; C. confidently &emsp;&emsp; D. rudely
6. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. and
7. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
8. A. beside &emsp;&emsp; B. inside &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. through
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. against

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我扮演医生，虽然只有五句台词。although 尽管，表让步。because 因为；if 如果；unless 除非。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一次排练时，大家只是小声读台词。quietly 轻声地，与后文 boring 呼应。loudly 大声地；angrily 生气地；happily 高兴地。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师建议加手势并改变台上的站位。positions 位置、站位。prices 价格；grades 成绩；scores 分数。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还搜索视频，了解医生如何冷静说话。search for information on... 搜索关于……的资料。about 关于也可，但 videos on how... 很常见。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下一次排练我说得更自信并与“病人”眼神交流。confidently 自信地。slowly 慢慢地；nervously 紧张地；rudely 粗鲁地。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位演员忘了帽子，所以我们不得不暂停。因果关系，用 so。but 但是；or 或者；and 并且。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：另一场戏太长，所以我们删掉两句。so 所以。because 因为；although 尽管；unless 除非。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：团队合作意味着一起解决问题而不是互相指责。without 没有、不。beside 在……旁边；inside 在……里面；through 通过。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：演出那天灯光很亮，但我记得老师的建议。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当观众鼓掌时，我知道我们已成长为一个真正的团队。grow into 成长为。from 从；over 超过；against 反对。故选 A。
