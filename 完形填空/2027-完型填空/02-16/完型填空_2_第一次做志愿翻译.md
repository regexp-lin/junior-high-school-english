# 中考英语完形填空：第一次做志愿翻译

## 完形填空原文

Our community center held a culture day for foreign visitors. The organizer needed student volunteers ___1___ could speak English and Chinese. I raised my hand, though my hands were shaking.

My job was to help a visitor from Canada understand a paper-cutting show. The artist used many special ___2___. At first, I translated word by word, ___3___ the visitor looked confused.

I took a breath and tried a different way. I explained the ___4___ of each pattern in simple sentences and pointed to the scissors when the artist cut the paper. The visitor nodded and smiled ___5___.

After the show, she thanked me and said my explanation helped her "see the story behind the art." I felt shy ___6___ proud at the same time.

That evening, I looked up new words about folk art. I realized translation is not only about language ___7___ about culture and kindness. I want to join ___8___ volunteer activities and practice more.

My mom said experience is ___9___ best teacher. I agree. The first step is always the ___10___, but it opens a new door.

## 选项

1. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
2. A. tools &emsp;&emsp; B. toys &emsp;&emsp; C. words &emsp;&emsp; D. jokes
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. meaning &emsp;&emsp; B. weight &emsp;&emsp; C. price &emsp;&emsp; D. speed
5. A. sadly &emsp;&emsp; B. coldly &emsp;&emsp; C. warmly &emsp;&emsp; D. angrily
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. others &emsp;&emsp; C. another &emsp;&emsp; D. the other
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
10. A. easiest &emsp;&emsp; B. hardest &emsp;&emsp; C. happiest &emsp;&emsp; D. latest

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：组织者需要会说英汉两种语言的学生志愿者。先行词 volunteers 指人，从句缺主语，用 who。which 指物；what 不引导定语从句；whose 表所属。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：艺术家使用许多特殊工具。tools 工具。toys 玩具；words 词语；jokes 笑话。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我逐字翻译，但访客看起来很困惑。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用简单句解释每种图案的含义。meaning 含义。weight 重量；price 价格；speed 速度。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：访客点头并热情地微笑。warmly 热情地。sadly 悲伤地；coldly 冷淡地；angrily 生气地。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我既害羞又骄傲。shy 与 proud 有轻微转折，用 but 连接 felt shy but proud 很自然。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列结构（not only... but...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：翻译不仅是语言，更是文化与善意。not only... but (also)... 不仅……而且……。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 限定词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想参加更多志愿活动并多练习。other + 复数名词 其他的。others 后不接名词；another 后接单数；the other 两者中的另一个。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说经验是最好的老师。形容词最高级 best 前用定冠词 the。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一步总是最难的，但它会打开一扇新门。hardest 最难的。easiest 最容易；happiest 最快乐；latest 最晚的。故选 B。
