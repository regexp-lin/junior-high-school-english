# 中考英语完形填空：帮爷爷修剪盆栽

## 完形填空原文

Grandpa loves his balcony garden. On Sunday morning, he asked me ___1___ help him trim the potted plants.

He handed me small scissors and said, "Cut ___2___ the yellow leaves and keep the healthy ones." I moved ___3___ because I was afraid of hurting the stems.

"Plants need air and light," Grandpa explained. "If branches crowd together, new buds ___4___ grow well."

He showed me ___5___ to cut at an angle near the node. I followed his example and ___6___ placed the cuttings into a plastic bag.

When we finished, the pots looked neater, ___7___ the green leaves seemed brighter in the sun. Grandpa patted my shoulder ___8___ pride.

I washed my hands and drank tea with him. Working with soil made me ___9___ than playing games all morning.

Before lunch, I promised ___10___ visit him next week to water the plants together.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. off &emsp;&emsp; B. up &emsp;&emsp; C. out &emsp;&emsp; D. down
3. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quicker &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. slow
4. A. can't &emsp;&emsp; B. shouldn't &emsp;&emsp; C. needn't &emsp;&emsp; D. mustn't
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
6. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. more careful &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
7. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. happier &emsp;&emsp; C. happiest &emsp;&emsp; D. happily
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷让我帮他修剪盆栽。ask sb. to help... 为固定搭配。for/of/with 不符合 ask 结构。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：剪掉黄叶，留下健康的叶子。cut off 表示“剪掉；切掉”，符合修剪语境。cut up 切碎；cut out 剪掉（中间挖空/停止）；cut down 砍倒，均不如 off 贴切。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我动作很慢，因为怕伤到茎。修饰动词 moved 用副词 slowly。quick/quicker/slow 为形容词，不能直接修饰 moved。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 情态动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果枝条挤在一起，新芽就长不好。表示“不能”用 can't。shouldn't 表不该；needn't 表不必；mustn't 表禁止。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他示范如何在芽眼附近斜剪。show sb. how to cut... 表示“演示如何剪”。what/why/when 不符合“方法示范”。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我照着做，并把剪下的枝条小心放进塑料袋。修饰动词 placed 用 carefully。careful 为形容词；more careful 不如原级副词自然；carelessly 为否定义。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：花盆更整洁了，绿叶在阳光下也显得更亮。并列顺承用 and。or/but/so 不符合并列描写。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷自豪地拍拍我的肩。with pride 表示“带着自豪”。in/on/at pride 搭配不典型。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：和泥土打交道让我比玩一上午游戏更快乐。than 提示比较级 happier。happy 原级；happiest 缺范围；happily 为副词，make sb. + 形容词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我承诺下周再来一起浇水。promise to do sth. 为固定搭配。for/of/with 不符合 promise 结构。故选 A。
