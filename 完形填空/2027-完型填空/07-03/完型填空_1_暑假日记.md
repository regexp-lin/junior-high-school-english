# 中考英语完形填空：暑假日记

## 完形填空原文

July 3rd, Sunny

This morning I woke up ___1___ than usual because I wanted to finish my English reading before breakfast. The birds outside my window sang ___2___ loudly that I couldn't fall asleep again.

After lunch, I rode my bike ___3___ the river bank. The wind felt cool ___4___ my face, and I stopped several times ___5___ photos of the clouds.

Grandma called and asked ___6___ I would visit her next weekend. I said yes right away. She always cooks the ___7___ dishes in our family, and I miss her stories.

Before going to bed, I wrote down three goals ___8___ I hope to achieve this summer: read six books, learn swimming, and help Dad repair ___9___ old bookshelf in the study. I believe small steps will make ___10___ big difference.

## 选项

1. A. early &emsp;&emsp; B. earlier &emsp;&emsp; C. earliest &emsp;&emsp; D. more early
2. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. such
3. A. along &emsp;&emsp; B. across &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. over
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. to take &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. took
6. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how
7. A. delicious &emsp;&emsp; B. more delicious &emsp;&emsp; C. most delicious &emsp;&emsp; D. the most delicious
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. who
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：今天早上我比平时醒得更早。than 提示用比较级 earlier。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构（so…that…）
**【解析】** 本句意为：窗外的鸟叫得如此大声，以至于我无法再睡着。so…that… 表示"如此……以至于……"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（along 沿着）
**【解析】** 本句意为：午饭后，我沿着河岸骑车。along the river bank 表示"沿着河岸"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（against 贴着）
**【解析】** 本句意为：风贴着我的脸，感觉很凉爽。against one's face 表示"贴着脸"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（stop to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我停下来好几次去拍云的照片。stop to do 表示"停下来去做另一件事"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词（if 表是否）
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶打电话问我下周末是否会去看她。asked 后接是否，用 if。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她总是做全家最好吃的菜。in our family 表范围，用 the most delicious。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句（that 指物）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我写下三个希望在这个夏天实现的目标。先行词 goals 为物，从句缺宾语，可用 that。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 冠词（特指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮爸爸修理书房里那个旧书架。特指家中的旧书架，用 the。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（make a difference）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信小步也会带来大不同。make a difference 为固定短语。故选 A。
