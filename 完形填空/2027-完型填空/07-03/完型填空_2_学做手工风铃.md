# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工风铃

## 完形填空原文

In our craft lesson, we were asked ___1___ wind chimes with simple materials: strings, small bells, and colorful beads. Our teacher showed us ___2___ video first so that we could understand the steps clearly.

I chose blue and white beads ___3___ they reminded me of the sky. ___4___ threading them onto the string, I tied each knot ___5___ than my partner did at the beginning.

When the wind chime ___6___ finished, I hung it near the window in my room. Every time the breeze comes in, the bells ring ___7___ and cheer me up after a long day of study.

My cousin saw it online ___8___ I shared a short clip, and she asked me ___9___ teach her next Sunday. Making something beautiful ___10___ hand makes me proud.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although
4. A. While &emsp;&emsp; B. Before &emsp;&emsp; C. Until &emsp;&emsp; D. Since
5. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slower &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. more slowly
6. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. had been
7. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gentler &emsp;&emsp; C. gently &emsp;&emsp; D. most gently
8. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（be asked to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们被要求用手工材料制作风铃。be asked to do sth. 为被动结构中的不定式。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师先给我们看了一段视频。video 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（because 表原因）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了蓝白珠子，因为它们让我想起天空。表原因用 because。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（while 当……时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：在把它们穿到绳子上时。While 引导时间状语从句，强调同时进行。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我打的每个结起初比我同伴的更慢。修饰动词 tied 用副词；than 提示比较级，用 more slowly。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：当风铃被做好时。wind chime 与 finish 为被动关系，叙述过去用 was finished。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（修饰动词 ring）
**【解析】** 本句意为：铃铛轻柔地响起。修饰动词 ring 用副词 gently。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（when 当……时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我分享了一段短视频后，表姐在网上看到了它。when 引导时间状语从句。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（ask sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她请我下周日教她。ask sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（by hand）
**【解析】** 本句意为：亲手做出漂亮的东西让我自豪。by hand 表示"用手工/亲手"。故选 C。
