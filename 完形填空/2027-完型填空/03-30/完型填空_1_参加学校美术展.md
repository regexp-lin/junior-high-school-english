# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校美术展

## 完形填空原文

Our school held an art exhibition in the library last week. Students were invited ___1___ display their best works.

I submitted a landscape painting that I ___2___ during the winter holiday. It showed a quiet village covered ___3___ snow. My art teacher said the colors were warm and the composition was well-balanced.

On the opening day, many parents and students came to ___4___ the exhibition. I stood beside my painting nervously, waiting ___5___ people's reactions.

A little girl stopped in front of my painting and said, "Look, Mom! It looks like Grandma's village!" Her words made me feel ___6___ proud.

The judges chose the ___7___ ten works for special awards. I did not win, but a senior student told me, "Your painting has a ___8___ feeling. Keep going!"

Before leaving, I walked around and enjoyed ___9___ students' works. Some used oil paints, while others used pencils or digital tools. Each piece told a different ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. finish &emsp;&emsp; B. finished &emsp;&emsp; C. finishing &emsp;&emsp; D. finishes
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
4. A. join &emsp;&emsp; B. visit &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. on
6. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. really &emsp;&emsp; C. true &emsp;&emsp; D. truly
7. A. top &emsp;&emsp; B. last &emsp;&emsp; C. first &emsp;&emsp; D. next
8. A. cool &emsp;&emsp; B. cold &emsp;&emsp; C. warm &emsp;&emsp; D. hot
9. A. another &emsp;&emsp; B. other &emsp;&emsp; C. others &emsp;&emsp; D. the other
10. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. joke &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. secret

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生们被邀请展示最好的作品。be invited to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示"被邀请做某事"。for/of/with 不符合 invite 的结构。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态/定语从句中的时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：我提交了一幅寒假期间完成的风景画。定语从句中描述过去完成的动作，用一般过去时 finished。finish 原形不行；finishing 为分词；finishes 为三单。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：画中是一个被雪覆盖的安静村庄。covered with snow 为固定搭配，表示"被雪覆盖"。in/on/at 不符合 covered 的搭配。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多家长和学生来参观展览。visit the exhibition 表示"参观展览"。join 为"加入"；leave 为"离开"；miss 为"错过"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我紧张地站在画旁等待人们的反应。wait for... 为固定搭配，表示"等待……"。to/at/on 不符合 wait 的搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰形容词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的话让我感到非常骄傲。修饰形容词 proud 需用副词 really。real 为形容词；true/truly 虽可表"真正地"，但 really 更自然。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：评委选出了前十名的作品获得特别奖。top ten 表示"前十名"。last 为"最后的"；first/next 不符合"排名前十"的表达。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你的画有一种温暖的感觉。warm feeling 表示"温暖的感觉"，与前文"colors were warm"呼应。cool 为"酷的"；cold 为"冷的"；hot 为"热的"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我四处走走欣赏其他学生的作品。other + 名词复数表示"其他的"。another 接单数；others 后不接名词；the other 指"另一个/另一些（特指）"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每幅作品讲述了一个不同的故事。tell a story 为常用搭配，表示"讲述故事"。joke 为"笑话"；lesson 为"课程"；secret 为"秘密"。故选 A。
