# 中考英语完形填空：跨年夜写新年愿望

## 完形填空原文

On New Year’s Eve, our family stayed up late ___1___ watch the countdown on TV. Before midnight, Mom gave each of us a small card ___2___ write down our wishes. I thought for a long time ___3___ I picked up the pen. I wanted my wish ___4___ clear and honest, not just fancy words. I wrote that I hoped ___5___ healthy and brave in the coming year. My little sister asked me ___6___ she should write about grades or hobbies. I told her ___7___ could choose both if she liked. When the clock struck twelve, fireworks lit up the sky outside. Dad raised his cup and said, “Let’s cheer ___8___ the new year!” I smiled and felt warm, knowing I was lucky ___9___ have a family like this. I folded the card carefully and put it ___10___ my diary for memory.

## 选项

1. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
3. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. before &emsp;&emsp; D. after  
4. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. to be &emsp;&emsp; D. being  
5. A. stay &emsp;&emsp; B. to stay &emsp;&emsp; C. staying &emsp;&emsp; D. stayed  
6. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
7. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself  
8. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
9. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. had &emsp;&emsp; C. to have &emsp;&emsp; D. having  
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定式表目的 in order to do  
**【解析】** “熬夜是为了看电视倒计时”，表目的用 in order to watch。so that 后接从句；because/although 不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 动词不定式作定语（名词 + to do）  
**【解析】** a small card to write down our wishes 表示“用来写下愿望的小卡片”，不定式 to write 作后置定语修饰 card，说明卡片的用途。in/on/at 不能接动词原形构成此结构。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 连词 before（在……之前）  
**【解析】** “想了很久才拿起笔”，before 表先后顺序。when/while 表同时；after 与句意相反。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** want + 宾语 + to do（want my wish to be clear）  
**【解析】** want my wish to be clear 为复合宾语结构。want…is/was/being 结构错误。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** hope to do sth.  
**【解析】** hoped to stay healthy… hope 后接不定式。hope stay/staying/stayed 不正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** whether 引导宾语从句表选择“是否”  
**【解析】** asked whether she should write about… 表示在两者间犹豫。that 无疑问语气；what/how 与 should write about 搭配不如 whether 贴切。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 人称代词主格 she 作主语  
**【解析】** I told her she could choose both 中从句主语用 she。her/hers/herself 不能作主语。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 cheer for…（为……欢呼）  
**【解析】** cheer for the new year 表示为新年欢呼。cheer to/with/at 搭配不如 for 常见。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词 lucky 后接不定式 lucky to do  
**【解析】** lucky to have a family… 形容词后用不定式说明原因。have/had/having 不能直接跟在 lucky 后。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 in（放进日记本里）  
**【解析】** put it in my diary 表示夹在/放进日记本里。on/at/to 搭配不当。故选 A。
