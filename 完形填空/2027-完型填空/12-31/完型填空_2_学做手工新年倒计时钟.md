# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工新年倒计时钟

## 完形填空原文

Our science teacher asked us ___1___ a DIY countdown clock for the New Year project. I worked with Leo, ___2___ is good at drawing numbers. We used a paper plate as the face and colored markers ___3___ the hours from ten to one. The hardest part was attaching the hands ___4___ they could move smoothly. Leo suggested ___5___ a small pin, and it worked perfectly. When we showed our clock in class, everyone clapped ___6___ loudly that the teacher laughed. She said our work was ___7___ creative than she expected. After school, I carried the clock home ___8___ my parents could put it on the living room wall. At midnight, we turned it to twelve together. I thought this simple clock was ___9___ valuable than any expensive toy ___10___ I had received before.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
3. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. to write &emsp;&emsp; C. writing &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote  
4. A. such that &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. even if &emsp;&emsp; D. as if  
5. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. using &emsp;&emsp; C. to use &emsp;&emsp; D. used  
6. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. quite  
7. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. much  
8. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
9. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. the most  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. who  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** ask sb. to do sth.  
**【解析】** asked us to make… 不定式作宾补。ask make/making/made 结构错误。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句 who 指人（Leo）作主语  
**【解析】** Leo, who is good at… 先行词为人，从句缺主语用 who。which 指物；whom 作宾语；whose 表所属。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** use sth. to do（用记号笔来写数字）  
**【解析】** used markers to write the hours 不定式表目的。use…write 缺 to；writing/wrote 结构不对应 use 的常见表达。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 so that（以便）  
**【解析】** attaching the hands so that they could move… 表目的。such that 多表结果；even if/as if 不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** suggest doing sth.  
**【解析】** suggested using a small pin 动名词作宾语。suggest to do 在标准书面语中较少用；suggest use/used 结构错误。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定结构 so…that…（如此……以至于……）  
**【解析】** clapped so loudly that… 与结果从句连用。very/too/quite 不能与 that 构成该结构。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 more creative than  
**【解析】** than 提示比较级，creative 为多音节形容词，用 more creative。very/most/the most 不与 than 搭配成比较级。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 so that  
**【解析】** “带回家以便父母能挂在客厅墙上”，表目的用 so that。because 原因；although 让步；unless 条件。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 more valuable than  
**【解析】** more valuable than any expensive toy 比较级结构。very 不能构成比较；most/the most 为最高级，与 than 不搭配。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句 that 指代先行词 toy（先行词被 any 修饰时常用 that）  
**【解析】** any expensive toy that I had received 先行词为物，关系词在从句中作宾语，that 可引导。what 不引导定语从句；which 在先行词含 any 时不如 that 常见；who 指人。故选 C。
