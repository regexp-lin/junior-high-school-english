# 中考英语完形填空：暑假学画画

## 完形填空原文

This summer, I decided ___1___ something new. I signed up for a painting class ___2___ is held every Tuesday and Thursday at the community center.

The teacher, Ms. Lin, is patient and kind. On the first day, she showed us how ___3___ light and shadow on paper. I found it difficult ___4___ first, but she encouraged me ___5___ keep trying.

Last week, we went outdoors ___6___ paint the old bridge near the river. While we were working, a little boy stopped ___7___ watch us. He said my picture was ___8___ most colorful one he had seen that day.

When I got home, I hung the painting on the wall. My parents said it ___9___ by many visitors in the future. I feel proud ___10___ I have learned to express myself with colors.

## 选项

1. A. learn &emsp;&emsp; B. learning &emsp;&emsp; C. to learn &emsp;&emsp; D. learned
2. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
3. A. draw &emsp;&emsp; B. drawing &emsp;&emsp; C. to draw &emsp;&emsp; D. drawn
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
5. A. / &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. but
7. A. watch &emsp;&emsp; B. watched &emsp;&emsp; C. to watch &emsp;&emsp; D. watching
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
9. A. will admire &emsp;&emsp; B. admires &emsp;&emsp; C. is admired &emsp;&emsp; D. will be admired
10. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：今年夏天，我决定学点新东西。"decide to do sth." 表示"决定做某事"，用不定式。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系代词 which）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我报名参加了一个绘画班，它在社区中心每周二、周四上课。先行词 class 指物，从句缺主语，用 which。who/whom 指人；whose 表所属。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（疑问词 + to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一天，她向我们展示如何在纸上表现明暗。"how to draw" 作宾语。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（at first）
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我觉得很难。"at first" 表示"起初"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（encourage sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她鼓励我继续尝试。"encourage sb. to do sth." 中 to 与 keep 构成不定式。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周，我们到户外去画河边的老桥。不定式表目的，用 to。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（stop to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小男孩停下来看我们。"stop to do" 表示停下来去做另一件事。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 冠词（形容词最高级前用 the）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说我的画是他那天见过的最有色彩的一幅。"the most colorful" 为最高级结构。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 被动语态与一般将来时
**【解析】** 本句意为：我父母说将来会有很多访客欣赏它。it 指画，与 admire 为被动关系，in the future 表将来，用 will be admired。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（because 表原因）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到自豪，因为我学会了用色彩表达自己。前后为因果关系。故选 A。
