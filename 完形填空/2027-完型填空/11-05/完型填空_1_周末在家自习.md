# 中考英语完形填空：周末在家自习

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, I stayed at home ___1___ for the coming mid-term exams. On Saturday morning, I made a timetable and stuck ___2___ on the wall beside my desk.

Math was harder than I expected, ___3___ I spent two hours on exercises. After lunch, I felt sleepy, ___4___ I decided to take a short nap instead of ___5___ with my phone.

In the afternoon, I reviewed English grammar notes ___6___ my teacher had shared online. There were still a few questions ___7___ I could not work out by myself, so I called my classmate Lin Tao. He explained the rules ___8___ clearly than the examples in the book.

By Sunday evening, I was tired, ___9___ I was also satisfied. I learned that good results don’t come from luck ___10___ from steady plans and healthy habits.

## 选项

1. A. study &emsp;&emsp; B. studying &emsp;&emsp; C. to study &emsp;&emsp; D. studied  
2. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. its &emsp;&emsp; C. itself &emsp;&emsp; D. one  
3. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. play &emsp;&emsp; B. playing &emsp;&emsp; C. to play &emsp;&emsp; D. played  
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
8. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. /  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）  
**【解析】** stayed at home to study 表目的“为了备考而待在家”。study/studying/studied 不能直接作目的状语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 人称代词 it（指代 timetable）  
**【解析】** stuck 后宾语指代前文 a timetable，用 it。its/itself 分别为物主代词和反身代词；one 表泛指同类不同物，此处特指那张时间表。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 因果连词 so  
**【解析】** “数学比预想的难”与“花两小时做题”为因果关系，用 so“所以”。but 表转折；or 表选择；although 表让步。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 因果连词 so  
**【解析】** “感到困”与“决定小睡”为结果关系，用 so。and 表并列；but 表转折；unless 表除非。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（instead of doing）  
**【解析】** instead of 为介词短语，后接动名词 playing。play/to play/played 不符合介词后用法。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词（指物）  
**【解析】** 先行词为 notes，从句缺宾语，指物可用 which。who/whom 指人；what 不引导定语从句。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词 that（先行词含 few）  
**【解析】** 先行词为 questions，前有 a few 修饰，从句中 work out 缺宾语，关系代词用 that 或 which；选项中 that 正确。what 不引导定语从句；how/when 为关系副词，从句不缺状语。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级  
**【解析】** than the examples 提示比较级 more clearly。very/too 无比较；most 为最高级。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “很累”与“也很满足”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 not... but...  
**【解析】** don’t come from luck but from... 表示“不是来自运气而是来自……”，用 but。and/or 不能构成 not... but...；斜杠不选。故选 C。
