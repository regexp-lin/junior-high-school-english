# 中考英语完形填空：学绕毛线球

## 完形填空原文

Our school club decided ___1___ colorful yarn balls for the charity sale. I had never rolled yarn neatly before, ___2___ the leader showed us a simple skill step by step.

We cut long pieces of yarn and wrapped them around two cardboard circles. At first, my circles were ___3___ than the samples, so my ball looked uneven. I unwound the yarn and tried again ___4___.

After practicing for an hour, I could roll ___5___ ball than my first one. The leader praised me and said my work might ___6___ sold at a good price if we labeled ___7___ clearly.

Before going home, we packed the finished balls into boxes. I kept one ___8___ myself as a souvenir. Looking at it, I thought handmade things might ___9___ simple, ___10___ they carried warm hearts.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
3. A. loose &emsp;&emsp; B. looser &emsp;&emsp; C. loosest &emsp;&emsp; D. the loosest  
4. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. more careful &emsp;&emsp; D. more carefully  
5. A. nice &emsp;&emsp; B. nicer &emsp;&emsp; C. nicest &emsp;&emsp; D. the nicest  
6. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. is &emsp;&emsp; C. was &emsp;&emsp; D. been  
7. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. it &emsp;&emsp; D. its  
8. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of  
9. A. look &emsp;&emsp; B. looks &emsp;&emsp; C. looked &emsp;&emsp; D. looking  
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide to do sth. 用不定式 to make。make/making/made 不符合 decide 搭配。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “从未绕整齐过”与“队长一步步演示”为转折，用 but。because 表原因会重复；unless/if 为条件。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** than the samples 提示比较级 looser“更松”。loose 原级；loosest/the loosest 为最高级。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级修饰动词  
**【解析】** tried again more carefully 表示“更仔细地再试”，修饰动词 tried 用副词 carefully 的比较级 more carefully。careful/more careful 为形容词，不能修饰动词 tried；仅用 carefully 无比较含义。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** than my first one 提示 nicer ball。nice 原级；nicest/the nicest 为最高级。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词 might + 动词原形 + 被动语态  
**【解析】** might be sold 为情态动词后接 be + 过去分词，表示“可能被卖掉”。is/was 不能与 might 直接构成被动；been 缺少 be。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** label 为及物动词，后接宾语指代毛线球（复数），用宾格 them；clearly 为副词修饰 labeled。they 为主格；it/its 与复数指代不一致。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 for（keep sth. for oneself）  
**【解析】** keep one for myself 表示“给自己留一个”，用介词 for。to/with/of 不符合 keep sth. for sb. 的常见搭配。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词 might + 动词原形  
**【解析】** might 后接动词原形，things might look simple 表示“手工作品也许看起来简单”。looks 与 might 后原形规则冲突；looked/looking 形式错误。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “看起来简单”与“承载温暖的心”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 C。
