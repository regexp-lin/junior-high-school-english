# 中考英语完形填空：新年第一天的愿望清单

## 完形填空原文

January 1st is always a special day for me. Every year on this day, my family sits together and writes a wish list for the ___1___ year. This tradition started when I was only five years old.

This morning, Mom prepared a big breakfast for us. While we were eating, Dad gave each of us a blank card and a ___2___. He said, "Write down three things you want to achieve this year." My little sister looked confused because she was too ___3___ to write, so Mom helped her.

I thought for a long time before writing. Last year, I wrote that I wanted to learn to play the guitar, but I ___4___ gave up after two months. This year, I decided to be more realistic. My first wish was to read at least twenty books. Reading has always been my favorite hobby, and I wanted to ___5___ myself to read more.

My second wish was to be ___6___ to my classmates. Sometimes I get angry easily and say things I don't mean. I know that kind words can make a big difference in someone's day. My third wish was to help Mom with housework more often, because she always looks ___7___ after a long day at work.

After everyone finished writing, we put our cards into a glass jar and ___8___ it on the bookshelf. Dad said we would open it on December 31st to see how many wishes had come ___9___. I felt excited and also a little nervous.

I believe that writing down your goals is the first step to ___10___ them. A new year means a new beginning, and I am ready for it.

## 选项

1. A. old &emsp;&emsp; B. new &emsp;&emsp; C. last &emsp;&emsp; D. same
2. A. pen &emsp;&emsp; B. book &emsp;&emsp; C. toy &emsp;&emsp; D. key
3. A. shy &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. young &emsp;&emsp; D. lazy
4. A. never &emsp;&emsp; B. already &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. actually
5. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. teach &emsp;&emsp; C. push &emsp;&emsp; D. allow
6. A. kinder &emsp;&emsp; B. closer &emsp;&emsp; C. funnier &emsp;&emsp; D. cooler
7. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. scared &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
8. A. hid &emsp;&emsp; B. placed &emsp;&emsp; C. threw &emsp;&emsp; D. dropped
9. A. back &emsp;&emsp; B. true &emsp;&emsp; C. out &emsp;&emsp; D. down
10. A. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; B. achieving &emsp;&emsp; C. changing &emsp;&emsp; D. losing

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每年这一天，家人坐在一起为新的一年写愿望清单。A 项意为"旧的"；B 项意为"新的"；C 项意为"上一个"；D 项意为"相同的"。新年写的是 new year 的愿望。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸给每人一张空白卡片和一支笔。A 项意为"笔"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"玩具"；D 项意为"钥匙"。写愿望需要 pen。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹太小了不会写字，所以妈妈帮她。A 项意为"害羞的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"年幼的"；D 项意为"懒的"。too young to write 表示太小不会写。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：去年我写了想学吉他，但实际上两个月后就放弃了。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"已经"；C 项意为"几乎不"；D 项意为"实际上"。actually 表示事实上放弃了。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想督促自己多读书。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"推动/督促"；D 项意为"允许"。push myself 意为"督促自己"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二个愿望是对同学更友善。A 项意为"更友善"；B 项意为"更亲近"；C 项意为"更有趣"；D 项意为"更酷"。后文提到容易生气、说不好听的话，所以希望更 kind。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈工作一整天后总是看起来很累。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"疲倦的"；D 项意为"担心的"。工作一天后 tired 最合语境。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把卡片放进玻璃罐，放在书架上。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"放置"；C 项意为"扔"；D 项意为"掉落"。placed it on the bookshelf 表示放在书架上。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：看看有多少愿望实现了。A 项 come back 意为"回来"；B 项 come true 意为"实现"；C 项 come out 意为"出来"；D 项 come down 意为"下来"。wishes come true 意为"愿望成真"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：写下目标是实现它们的第一步。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"实现"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"失去"。achieving them 与写下目标形成逻辑关系。故选 B。
