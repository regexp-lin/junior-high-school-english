# 中考英语完形填空：学做饺子馅

## 完形填空原文

Learning to make dumpling filling is harder ___1___ it looks. Yesterday, Dad stood next to me ___2___ mixed minced pork with chopped cabbage in a big bowl. “Add a little salt slowly,” he said. I was ___3___ careful that I almost forgot to stir. Dad laughed and told me not ___4___ worry. After ten minutes, the filling smelled delicious, and I couldn’t wait ___5___ it. We wrapped dumplings together, and Mom took photos ___6___ us happily. Later, when the dumplings were served, everyone said the filling tasted ___7___ than last year’s. I felt proud ___8___ I had helped a little. Dad encouraged me ___9___ more cooking skills during the vacation. I nodded and thought cooking was ___10___ useful life skill for teenagers like me.

## 选项

1. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. that  
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
3. A. such &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of  
5. A. taste &emsp;&emsp; B. tasting &emsp;&emsp; C. to taste &emsp;&emsp; D. tasted  
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about  
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
8. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
9. A. learn &emsp;&emsp; B. learning &emsp;&emsp; C. to learn &emsp;&emsp; D. learned  
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 比较级结构 harder than…  
**【解析】** 句中出现比较级 harder，需用 than 引出比较对象。as 多与原级 as 连用；so/that 与 harder 不构成比较结构。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and 连接并列谓语  
**【解析】** Dad stood… and mixed… 两个动作并列，用 and。but/or/so 表转折、选择、结果，不符合并列描述。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** so…that…（如此……以至于……）  
**【解析】** so careful that… 修饰形容词 careful。such 后接名词短语；very/too 不与 that 构成该固定结果结构。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** tell sb. not to do sth.  
**【解析】** tell me not to worry 为不定式的否定式作宾补。not for/with/of worry 结构错误。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** can’t wait to do sth.  
**【解析】** can’t wait to taste 表示“迫不及待想尝”。wait 后接不定式，不能接动词原形或动名词单独作宾语（此处）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 take photos of sb.  
**【解析】** take photos of us 表示“给我们拍照”。take photos for/with/about 搭配不如 of 准确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 + than  
**【解析】** tasted better than last year’s 中有 than，用比较级 better。good/well 为原级；best 为最高级。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句 because  
**【解析】** “我感到自豪，因为我帮了一点忙”，表原因用 because。although 让步；unless/if 条件，不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** encourage sb. to do sth.  
**【解析】** encourage me to learn… 不定式作宾补。encourage 后不接动词原形或动名词作宾补。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 a 表示泛指“一项技能”  
**【解析】** a useful life skill 中 useful 以辅音音素 /j/ 开头，用 a。an 用于元音音素开头；the 表特指；零冠词不适合可数单数首次泛指。故选 A。
