# 中考英语完形填空：帮奶奶准备年夜饭食材

## 完形填空原文

The Spring Festival is coming soon, and my family started getting ready last Sunday. My grandma asked me ___1___ her buy ingredients for our New Year’s Eve dinner. We went to the market early ___2___ it was crowded later in the morning. Grandma taught me ___3___ to choose fresh vegetables and fish. She said, “Smell the fish and look at ___4___ eyes. Clear eyes mean it is fresh.” I listened carefully and took notes on my phone. ___5___ we were walking home, Grandma told stories about her childhood. I carried two heavy bags, ___6___ I didn’t feel tired at all because I enjoyed the time with her. When we arrived, Mom was surprised ___7___ how much we had bought. Dad helped put everything ___8___ the fridge. That evening, I told my cousin ___9___ I had learned at the market. He said he wanted ___10___ us next year. I smiled and said yes.

## 选项

1. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
2. A. although &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
4. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. its &emsp;&emsp; C. it’s &emsp;&emsp; D. itself  
5. A. Before &emsp;&emsp; B. After &emsp;&emsp; C. While &emsp;&emsp; D. Until  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
9. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  
10. A. join &emsp;&emsp; B. joining &emsp;&emsp; C. to join &emsp;&emsp; D. joined  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** ask sb. to do sth.（要求/让某人做某事）  
**【解析】** ask 后接宾语 + 不定式，即 asked me to help。help/helping/helped 不符合 ask 的结构。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句 because  
**【解析】** “很早就去市场，因为上午晚些时候会很挤”，表原因用 because。although 表让步；unless/if 表条件，与句意不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式” teach / tell sb. how to do  
**【解析】** teach me how to choose… 表示“教我如何挑选”。what 需在不定式中缺宾语时才常用；which/when 与“挑选方法”不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词 its 修饰名词 eyes  
**【解析】** 指鱼的“它的眼睛”，用 its。it 为人称代词；it’s 为 it is 缩写；itself 为反身代词，不修饰名词 eyes。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 while（当……时；在……期间）  
**【解析】** “在我们走回家的路上”，while 引导延续性动作。before/after 表先后；until 表直到，均不如 while 贴切。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 but 表转折  
**【解析】** “袋子很重，但我一点也不累”，转折关系用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表结果。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be surprised at…（对……感到惊讶）  
**【解析】** be surprised at how much… 为常见搭配。surprised for/by/with 不如 at 与“惊讶内容”搭配自然。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 in（放进冰箱里面）  
**【解析】** put everything in the fridge 表示放进冰箱内部。on/at/to 与 put…fridge 搭配不当。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 what（在从句中作 learned 的宾语）  
**【解析】** told my cousin what I had learned 中 what 充当 learned 的宾语。that 在从句中不充当成分，若用 that 则从句缺宾语；if/whether 表是否，不符。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** want to do sth.  
**【解析】** want 后接不定式 to join。want join/joining/joined 结构错误。故选 C。
