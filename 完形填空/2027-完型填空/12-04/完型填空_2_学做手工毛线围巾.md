# 中考英语完形填空：学织一条暖围巾

## 完形填空原文

Winter was coming, ___1___ I wanted to knit a scarf for my dad. Aunt Sue promised ___2___ me on weekends. She said, “Knitting needs patience ___3___ practice.”

On Saturday morning, we went ___4___ a shop to choose soft wool. Aunt Sue taught me ___5___ hold the needles correctly. At first, my stitches were loose, ___6___ she didn’t laugh at me. “It’s normal ___7___ beginners,” she said.

Two weeks passed, and the scarf became ___8___ than before. When I gave it to Dad, he wrapped it ___9___ his neck immediately. His eyes looked ___10___ warm and proud.

## 选项

1. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. though  
2. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
5. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. what to &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. neat &emsp;&emsp; B. neater &emsp;&emsp; C. neatest &emsp;&emsp; D. more neat  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. around &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
10. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. such &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表结果  
**【解析】** “冬天要来了，所以我想给爸爸织围巾”，用 so 表结果。but/or/though 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（promise to do）  
**【解析】** promise to help 为固定搭配。help/helping/helped 不能直接跟在 promise 后。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and 连接并列成分  
**【解析】** patience and practice 表示“耐心与练习”，并列关系用 and。or/but/so 不符合并列名词结构。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 to 与 go 搭配表去向  
**【解析】** go to a shop 表示“去商店”。go for/at/in a shop 不表“前往”。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** teach me how to hold 表示“教我如何握针”。how 单独或 what/what to 均不完整或语义不对。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “针脚松”与“她没有嘲笑我”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 不合适。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 for 表对象  
**【解析】** It’s normal for beginners 表示“对初学者来说很正常”。of 用于 It’s kind of you 这类结构；to/with 不搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than 提示）  
**【解析】** than before 提示比较级，neat 的比较级为 neater。neat/neatest/more neat（错误形式）均不合适。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 around 表“围绕”  
**【解析】** wrap it around his neck 表示“围在脖子上”。in/on/to 不如 around 准确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰形容词  
**【解析】** looked very warm and proud 表示“看起来非常温暖又自豪”，very 修饰形容词 warm。such 后接名词短语；so 虽可修饰形容词，但与 proud 并列时常用 very 更自然；too 含“过分”之意，语境不符。故选 C。
