# 中考英语完形填空：老照片里的时光

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, it rained heavily, ___1___ we stayed at home. Mom brought out ___2___ old photo album and said, “Let’s look at these pictures together.” I sat ___3___ her on the sofa, feeling curious.

The first photo ___4___ my parents’ wedding day. Dad looked a little nervous, ___5___ Mom was smiling brightly. I pointed ___6___ a small house behind them and asked, “Is that our hometown?” Mom nodded and told me many stories ___7___ happened long ago.

Some photos were yellow, ___8___ they were still clear enough. I realized ___9___ happy my grandparents had been when they were young. That rainy afternoon became ___10___ unforgettable memory for me.

## 选项

1. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
3. A. beside &emsp;&emsp; B. besides &emsp;&emsp; C. below &emsp;&emsp; D. between  
4. A. was taken &emsp;&emsp; B. took &emsp;&emsp; C. was taking &emsp;&emsp; D. taken  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表结果  
**【解析】** “雨下得很大，所以我们待在家里”，因果关系用 so。but 表转折；or 表选择；because 引导原因从句，若用 because 应说明原因，此处前句已是原因，用 so 更顺。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词与元音音素  
**【解析】** old 以元音音素开头，用 an old photo album。a 用于辅音音素开头；the 表特指但此处为首次提到“一本相册”。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 beside 表“在……旁边”  
**【解析】** sit beside her 表示“坐在她旁边”。besides 意为“除……之外还”；below/between 语义不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** 照片“被拍摄”，主语 photo 与 take 为被动关系，用 was taken。took 为主动；was taking 过去进行被动不自然；taken 不能单独作谓语。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “爸爸有点紧张”与“妈妈笑得很灿烂”形成对比，用 but。and/so/because 逻辑不如转折贴切。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（point at）  
**【解析】** point at 表示“指向（某物）”。point to 也可表指向，但 point at 更强调对准目标；point on/for 非常见搭配。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词（指物作主语）  
**【解析】** 先行词 stories 为物，从句缺主语，用 which/that。what 不引导定语从句；who 指人；whose 表所属。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “有些照片发黄”与“仍然足够清晰”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 不符合语义。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 感叹句中的连接词 how  
**【解析】** I realized how happy... 为宾语从句，how 修饰形容词 happy，表示“多么快乐”。what 后多接名词短语；why/when 语义不符。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词与元音音素  
**【解析】** unforgettable 以元音音素开头，用 an unforgettable memory。a/the/零冠词不符合可数单数泛指搭配。故选 B。
