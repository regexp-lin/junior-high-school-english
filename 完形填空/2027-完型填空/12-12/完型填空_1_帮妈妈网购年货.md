# 中考英语完形填空：帮妈妈网购年货

## 完形填空原文

The Spring Festival was only three weeks away, ___1___ my mother was still busy at work. One evening, she asked me ___2___ her buy some New Year goods online. I felt excited because I had never helped ___3___ such an important family task before.

We sat at the computer and made ___4___ shopping list first: nuts, candies, red envelopes, and some snacks for guests. My mother told me ___5___ compare prices and read the comments carefully. If a product had many bad reviews, we should avoid ___6___ it.

Sometimes the pictures looked wonderful, ___7___ the real quality might be poor. I learned ___8___ to tell useful information from advertisements. After two hours, we finally placed an order. My mother thanked me and said I was ___9___ helpful than she had expected. That night, I went to bed ___10___ than usual, but I felt proud of myself.

## 选项

1. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
2. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
3. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
4. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
6. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. buying &emsp;&emsp; C. to buy &emsp;&emsp; D. bought  
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where  
9. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
10. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. later &emsp;&emsp; C. latest &emsp;&emsp; D. lately  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** 前句说春节临近，后句说妈妈仍忙于工作，语义转折，用 *but*。*or* 表选择；*so* 表结果；*because* 表原因，均不符合逻辑。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*ask sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *ask* 后接带 *to* 的不定式作宾补，*asked me to help her*。其余形式不符合 *ask* 的用法。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *help with sth.*  
**【解析】** *help with (such) a task* 表示“在某项任务上帮忙”。*help for/in/on* 不是此处常见搭配。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词与固定短语 *make a list*  
**【解析】** *make a shopping list* 表示“列一张购物清单”，用不定冠词 *a*。*the* 表特指，此处首次提到清单；*an* 用于元音音素开头词前；零冠词不符合习惯。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定式符号 *to*（*tell sb. to do*）  
**【解析】** *tell me to compare* 为 *tell sb. to do sth.* 结构，用 *to*。*for/of/with* 不能构成该宾补结构。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动名词作宾语（*avoid doing*）  
**【解析】** *avoid* 后接动名词作宾语，*avoid buying*。不定式、原形或过去式均不符合 *avoid* 的用法。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “图片好看”与“真实质量可能差”为转折关系，用 *but*。*and* 表并列；*or* 表选择；*so* 表结果。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构  
**【解析】** *learn how to tell...* 表示“学会如何区分……”，*how* 表方式。*what/when/where* 与“辨别信息”的方式语义不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（*than* 提示）  
**【解析】** 句中有 *than she had expected*，用比较级 *more helpful*。*much/very* 不能直接与 *than* 搭配；*most* 为最高级。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级（隐含与平时相比）  
**【解析】** *later than usual* 表示“比平常更晚（睡）”，用比较级 *later*。*late* 无比较；*latest* 表“最晚/最新”；*lately* 意为“近来”，为副词但语义不对。故选 B。
