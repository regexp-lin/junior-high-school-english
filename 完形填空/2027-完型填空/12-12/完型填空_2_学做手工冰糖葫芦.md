# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工冰糖葫芦

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, my cousin came to visit ___1___ family. She said she could teach me ___2___ make candied hawthorns at home. I thought it would be difficult, ___3___ she told me we would use strawberries instead of real hawthorns for safety.

First, we washed the fruit and dried ___4___ with paper towels. Then we put wooden sticks ___5___ the strawberries carefully. My cousin heated sugar and water in a pan ___6___ the syrup turned golden. She warned me ___7___ touch the hot syrup because it could burn my fingers.

When everything was ready, she let me ___8___ the strawberries into the syrup quickly and place them on a plate ___9___ oil paper. The sweet smell filled the kitchen, and I couldn’t wait ___10___ taste our handmade “tanghulu.”

## 选项

1. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. me &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
2. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. they &emsp;&emsp; D. their  
5. A. through &emsp;&emsp; B. across &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. over  
6. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
7. A. don’t &emsp;&emsp; B. not to &emsp;&emsp; C. not &emsp;&emsp; D. didn’t  
8. A. dip &emsp;&emsp; B. dipping &emsp;&emsp; C. to dip &emsp;&emsp; D. dipped  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** 修饰名词 *family*，用 *my family*。*I/me* 不能作定语；*mine* 后不接名词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** *teach me how to make* 表示“教我如何做”，*how* 表方式。*what to make* 语义可通但此处强调制作方法；*when/where* 与制作过程关系较弱。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “我以为会很难”与“她说用草莓代替”形成转折，用 *but*。*and/or/so* 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（指代复数名词）  
**【解析】** *dried* 的宾语指代 *the fruit*（此处水果为复数概念/多个草莓），用宾格 *them*。*it* 单数；*they* 主格；*their* 为物主代词。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（*put...into*）  
**【解析】** 把木棍“插进”草莓里，用 *into*。*through* 强调穿过；*across* 表横穿；*over* 表在上方，均不符合。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词 *until*（直到……为止）  
**【解析】** 加热糖和水“直到”糖浆变金黄，用 *until*。*when* 仅表“当……时”，不如 *until* 准确表达持续加热到某状态；*if/because* 逻辑不对。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*warn sb. not to do*）  
**【解析】** *warn sb. not to do sth.* 为固定结构，用 *not to touch*。*don’t/didn't* 不能作 *warn* 的宾补结构；单独 *not* 不完整。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 使役动词 *let* 后接动词原形  
**【解析】** *let me dip*，*let sb. do sth.* 用动词原形。动名词、不定式或过去式均不符合 *let* 的用法。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *with* 表“带有/铺有”  
**【解析】** *a plate with oil paper* 表示“铺了油纸的盘子”。*in/on/for* 不如 *with* 自然表达“配有油纸”。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *can’t wait to do sth.*  
**【解析】** *can’t wait to taste* 为固定表达，意为“迫不及待要品尝”。*for/of/with* 不构成该搭配。故选 A。
