# 中考英语完形填空：学做紫薯饼

## 完形填空原文

Aunt Lin invited me ___1___ her kitchen to learn how to make purple sweet potato cakes. The steamed potatoes ___2___ mashed until they became soft purple dough.

We added a little sugar and rice flour, then rolled the dough ___3___ small round pieces. Each piece ___4___ pressed flat before being cooked in a pan with a little oil.

"Keep the heat low," Aunt Lin said, ___5___ the cakes would turn brown too quickly. I stood beside her and flipped them ___6___ when I saw tiny bubbles.

The kitchen ___7___ wonderful when the cakes were almost done. They tasted sweet ___8___ not too heavy, so I ate three at once.

Aunt Lin laughed and told me ___9___ I could take some home for my parents. I thanked her and promised ___10___ the recipe in my notebook that night.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. were &emsp;&emsp; B. are &emsp;&emsp; C. was &emsp;&emsp; D. is
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. was being
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gentler &emsp;&emsp; C. gently &emsp;&emsp; D. most gently
7. A. smell &emsp;&emsp; B. smells &emsp;&emsp; C. smelled &emsp;&emsp; D. was smelled
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how
10. A. copy &emsp;&emsp; B. copying &emsp;&emsp; C. to copy &emsp;&emsp; D. copied

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（invite sb. into）
**【解析】** 本句意为：林阿姨邀请我进她的厨房。invite sb. into 表示"邀请某人进入"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：蒸熟的紫薯被捣成泥。potatoes 与 mash 为被动关系，叙述过去用 were mashed。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（roll…into…）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后把面团搓成小圆块。roll…into… 表示"把……搓成……"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一块在被煎之前都被压扁。piece 与 press 为被动，叙述过去用 was pressed。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（or 否则）
**【解析】** 本句意为：保持小火，否则饼会很快变焦。or 表示"否则"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（修饰动词 flipped）
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到小气泡时我轻轻翻面。修饰动词 flipped 用副词 gently。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时（系动词 smell）
**【解析】** 本句意为：快做好时厨房闻起来很香。smell 作系动词无被动；叙述过去用 smelled。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（转折）
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道甜但不会太腻。sweet 与 not too heavy 为转折，用 but。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她告诉我可以把一些带回家给父母。told me that… 引导陈述性宾语从句。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（promise to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我答应当晚把做法抄在笔记本上。promise to do sth. 为固定搭配。故选 C。
