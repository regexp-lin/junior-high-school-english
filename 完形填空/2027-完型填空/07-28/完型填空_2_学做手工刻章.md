# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工刻章

## 完形填空原文

In the art room, Noah learned ___1___ carve a small rubber stamp. The teacher handed him ___2___ carving knife and a piece of soft rubber.

Noah drew a simple flower ___3___ copied the outline onto the rubber. He cut away the extra parts ___4___ the pattern could stand out.

His hands were tired, ___5___ he enjoyed every minute. When the stamp ___6___ pressed onto ink and paper, the flower looked clear ___7___ beautiful.

Noah thought handmade stamps were ___8___ personal than printed stickers. He planned ___9___ a set of stamps for his friends. He understood ___10___ art takes time and focus.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
3. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. or
6. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be
7. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. and
8. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most
9. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. how

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词+不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：诺亚学习如何雕刻一枚小橡皮章。learn how to do sth. 学习如何做某事。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师递给他一把刻刀。carving 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：诺亚画了一朵简单的花，并把轮廓描到橡皮上。动作顺承并列，用 and。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词 / 目的状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：他削去多余部分，以便图案能凸显出来。so that 表目的。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：手很酸，但他享受每一分钟。转折用 but。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 被动语态 / 时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：当印章被按在印泥和纸上时。stamp 与 press 为被动关系，叙述过去用 was pressed。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：花朵看起来又清晰又漂亮。并列形容词用 and。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工印章比印刷贴纸更有个性。than 提示用 more personal。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他计划为朋友们做一套印章。plan to do sth. 计划做某事。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：他明白艺术需要时间和专注。understood that… 陈述性宾语从句用 that。故选 B。
