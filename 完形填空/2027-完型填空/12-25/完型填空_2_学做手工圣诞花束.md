# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工圣诞花束

## 完形填空原文

The school club taught us ___1___ make a small Christmas bouquet with pine branches and dried oranges. The instructor tied a ribbon ___2___ my wrist so I would not lose the scissors.

We cut the branches ___3___ same length and wrapped them ___4___ brown paper. I added two silver bells ___5___ the bouquet would look brighter in photos. My desk partner Mia suggested ___6___ a tiny card between the twigs.

When we carried the bouquets to the office, the teachers smiled ___7___. I thought the gift looked simple, ___8___ it carried a warm holiday message. The instructor told us ___9___ the bouquets in cool water for a longer life.

I planned ___10___ one to my aunt after school.

## 选项

1. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. why to  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. around  
3. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
6. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. putting &emsp;&emsp; C. to put &emsp;&emsp; D. puts  
7. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. happier &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. happiest  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. keeping &emsp;&emsp; C. to keep &emsp;&emsp; D. kept  
10. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. giving &emsp;&emsp; C. to give &emsp;&emsp; D. gave  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构  
**【解析】** *taught us how to make a small Christmas bouquet* 表方式“如何做小花束”。*how to do* 最贴切。*what to* 需缺宾语结构；*when/why to* 不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词 *around*  
**【解析】** *tied a ribbon around my wrist* “在手腕上系丝带”。*around* 表环绕。*in/on/at* 不如 *around* 准确表达“绕手腕”。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定冠词 *the same*  
**【解析】** *the same length* 为固定搭配“相同长度”，*same* 前通常加 *the*。*a/an* 或零冠词均不正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *with* 表材料  
**【解析】** *wrapped them with brown paper* “用牛皮纸包裹”。*with* 表使用材料。*in/on/by* 搭配不自然。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句（*so that*）  
**【解析】** *added two silver bells so that the bouquet would look brighter in photos* 表示目的“以便花束在照片里更亮眼”。*if/unless* 表条件；*although* 表让步。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*suggest doing sth.*）  
**【解析】** *suggested putting a tiny card* “建议放一张小卡片”。*suggest doing* 为常见考点。*suggest to put* 非主流；*put/puts* 不能直接作宾语。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** *smiled happily* 用副词 *happily* 修饰动词 *smiled*。*happy/happier/happiest* 为形容词，不能修饰动词。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “看起来简单”与“承载温暖祝福”为转折，用 *but*。*and/or/so* 不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*tell sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *told us to keep the bouquets in cool water* “告诉我们要把花束放在凉水里”。*tell sb. to do* 固定。*keep/keeping/kept* 不能直接作宾补。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*plan to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *planned to give one to my aunt* “计划放学后送姑姑一束”。*plan to do* 固定。*give/giving/gave* 不能直接跟在 *planned* 后。故选 C。
