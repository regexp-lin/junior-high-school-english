# 中考英语完形填空：圣诞节学校庆祝活动

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a Christmas celebration ___1___ the hall on December 25. Students sang English carols ___2___ played short skits about winter kindness. I was excited ___3___ I could join the choir for the first time.

Before the show started, volunteers handed out paper hats ___4___ everyone. The lights turned soft ___5___ the music began, and the whole room felt warm. When our class performed, I looked at the audience ___6___ forgot my nervousness.

After the performance, we took a group photo ___7___ the stage. Our head teacher thanked all the helpers ___8___ their hard work. She also said the best gift was not candy, ___9___ the smiles we shared.

On the way home, I told my parents ___10___ proud I felt to be part of the team.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
3. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
4. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
10. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. that  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 *in the hall*  
**【解析】** 活动在大厅内部举行，用 *in the hall*。*on* 在表面；*at* 多接具体点；*by* 在旁。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *and*  
**【解析】** “唱英文颂歌”与“表演短小品”为并列顺承，用 *and*。*but* 转折；*or* 选择；*so* 结果。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句（*because*）  
**【解析】** “兴奋”是因为“第一次加入合唱团”，因果关系用 *because*。*but/although/unless* 不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *hand out... to...*  
**【解析】** *handed out paper hats to everyone* “给每个人分发纸帽”。*hand sth. to sb.* 常用 *to*。*for/of/with* 搭配不如 *to* 准确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*when*）  
**【解析】** *The lights turned soft when the music began* “音乐开始时灯光变柔和”。*if/unless* 条件；*until* 直到。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *and* 表顺承  
**【解析】** *looked at the audience and forgot my nervousness* 两个动作顺承，用 *and*。*but/or/so* 语义不符。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *on the stage*  
**【解析】** *on the stage* “在舞台上”。*in/at/by* 不如 *on* 常见。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *thank sb. for sth.*  
**【解析】** *thanked all the helpers for their hard work* “感谢志愿者的辛苦付出”。*thank... for...* 固定。*in/on/with* 不搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *not... but...*  
**【解析】** *not candy, but the smiles* “不是糖果，而是我们分享的笑容”。*and/or/so* 不能构成此对比。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 感叹宾语从句引导词 *how*  
**【解析】** *told my parents how proud I felt* 中 *how* 修饰形容词 *proud*，表示程度“多么自豪”。*what* 修饰名词；*whether* 是否；*that* 无感叹含义。故选 A。
