# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工草编

## 完形填空原文

Grass weaving is one of the oldest crafts in China. Last week, our school invited a village artisan, Grandma Liu, to teach us ___1___ art.

Grandma Liu was over seventy years old, but her fingers were still amazingly ___2___. She brought bundles of dried rice straw and palm leaves. "Nature gives us the best materials," she said ___3___ a warm smile.

She began by showing us how to weave a small grasshopper. Her hands moved so ___4___ that we could barely follow. She twisted, folded, and tied the straw, and within two minutes, a lifelike grasshopper sat on her palm.

"Now it's your turn," she said. She handed ___5___ of us several pieces of straw and guided us step by step. The straw was harder to bend than I ___6___. My fingers hurt after just a few tries.

I was about to give up when Grandma Liu sat down beside me. She held my hands and moved them gently. "Feel the straw," she whispered. "It will tell you ___7___ it wants to go." With her help, I finally made a simple dragonfly.

___8___ it was far from perfect, I was proud of my first grass-weaving work. Grandma Liu told us that fewer and fewer young people were learning this craft. She hoped we could help keep it ___9___.

I promised myself that I ___10___ practice more and learn this beautiful art. Grandma Liu's patience and love for her craft deeply inspired me.

## 选项

1. A. this &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. these &emsp;&emsp; D. those  
2. A. skill &emsp;&emsp; B. skillful &emsp;&emsp; C. skillfully &emsp;&emsp; D. skills  
3. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
4. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. quicker &emsp;&emsp; D. quickest  
5. A. every &emsp;&emsp; B. all &emsp;&emsp; C. both &emsp;&emsp; D. each  
6. A. expect &emsp;&emsp; B. expects &emsp;&emsp; C. expected &emsp;&emsp; D. expecting  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
8. A. Although &emsp;&emsp; B. Because &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. If  
9. A. live &emsp;&emsp; B. alive &emsp;&emsp; C. lively &emsp;&emsp; D. living  
10. A. will &emsp;&emsp; B. would &emsp;&emsp; C. can &emsp;&emsp; D. may  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 指示代词 this  
**【解析】** this art 意为"这门艺术"，指代前面提到的草编艺术（近指）。that 为远指；these/those 为复数。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** were still amazingly skillful 意为"仍然惊人地灵巧"，skillful 为形容词作表语。skill 为名词；skillfully 为副词；skills 为名词复数。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** with + 名词表伴随  
**【解析】** with a warm smile 意为"带着温暖的微笑"，with 表伴随状态。by 表方式；in 表穿着；for 表目的。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** so + 副词 + that  
**【解析】** so quickly that 构成结果状语从句，修饰动词 moved 用副词。quick 为形容词；quicker/quickest 为比较级/最高级。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** each of + 代词  
**【解析】** each of us 意为"我们每个人"，each of + 代词为固定结构。every 后不接 of + 代词（every one of 可以但此处为 every 单独使用）；all of us 表全部但文中强调"每人几根"；both 指两者。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** harder than I expected 意为"比我预料的更难"，全文过去叙述用过去式。expect 为原形；expects 为三单；expecting 为现在分词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连词  
**【解析】** where it wants to go 意为"它想去哪里"，where 在从句中作地点状语。what 作主语/宾语；which 需有选择范围；when 表时间。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 让步状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "虽然远非完美，但我很自豪"，although 引导让步状语从句。because 表原因；unless 表条件；if 表条件。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词 alive  
**【解析】** keep it alive 意为"保持它存活/传承"，alive 为形容词作宾补。live 为动词/形容词（live 作形容词意为"现场的"）；lively 意为"活泼的"；living 意为"活着的"但 alive 更常用于 keep...alive 结构。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句时态呼应  
**【解析】** promised that I would practice 中主句为过去式，从句表将来用过去将来时 would。will 为一般将来时与过去式不呼应；can/may 不表将来计划。故选 B。
