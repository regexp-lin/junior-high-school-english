# 中考英语完形填空：帮同学辅导功课

## 完形填空原文

My classmate, Zhang Fang, has always been weak in English. One day, she came to me and asked ___1___ I could help her with grammar. I agreed without hesitation.

We started meeting in the school library every Thursday after class. I prepared some exercises and explained the grammar rules ___2___ simple words. Zhang Fang was a slow learner, but she was very hardworking. She never gave ___3___ even when she made mistakes.

The first topic I taught her was the present perfect tense. She found it ___4___ to understand the difference between "have done" and "did." I used real-life examples to explain. "Have you ever been to Beijing?" means the experience matters, not ___5___ you went there.

After three weeks of practice, Zhang Fang could use the present perfect tense ___6___. She was so happy that she bought me a small gift — a notebook with a cute cat on the cover.

What surprised me was that helping her also improved ___7___ own English. When I explained grammar rules to her, I had to think more ___8___ about them. This made my understanding much deeper.

Our English teacher noticed Zhang Fang's ___9___ and praised her in front of the class. She thanked me publicly, which made me feel a little embarrassed but ___10___ proud. I realized that helping others is the best way to help ourselves.

## 选项

1. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
3. A. away &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. out  
4. A. confuse &emsp;&emsp; B. confused &emsp;&emsp; C. confusing &emsp;&emsp; D. confusion  
5. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
6. A. correct &emsp;&emsp; B. corrected &emsp;&emsp; C. correctly &emsp;&emsp; D. correction  
7. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
8. A. deep &emsp;&emsp; B. deeply &emsp;&emsp; C. deeper &emsp;&emsp; D. deepest  
9. A. progress &emsp;&emsp; B. project &emsp;&emsp; C. promise &emsp;&emsp; D. problem  
10. A. main &emsp;&emsp; B. mainly &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. mostly  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连词（是否）  
**【解析】** asked if I could help 意为"问我是否能帮忙"，if 表"是否"。that 引导陈述从句；what/how 为疑问词不表"是否"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 in simple words  
**【解析】** in simple words 意为"用简单的话"，in 表使用的语言方式。on/by/at 不与 words 构成此搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 give up  
**【解析】** give up 意为"放弃"。give away 意为"赠送/泄露"；give in 意为"屈服"；give out 意为"分发/耗尽"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** find it + adj. + to do（-ing 形容词修饰物）  
**【解析】** found it confusing 意为"觉得它令人困惑"，confusing 修饰事物（it 指语法知识）。confuse 为动词；confused 修饰人的感受；confusion 为名词。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连词  
**【解析】** not when you went there 意为"而不是你何时去的"，when 表时间。where 表地点；why 表原因；how 表方式，均不如 when 符合语境。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** use...correctly 意为"正确地使用"，修饰动词 use 用副词。correct 为形容词/动词；corrected 为过去式；correction 为名词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** my own English 意为"我自己的英语"，修饰名词 English 用形容词性物主代词。I 为主格；me 为宾格；mine 后不接名词。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** think more deeply 意为"更深入地思考"，deeply 为副词修饰 think。deep 作副词时多指物理深度（dig deep）；deeper 为形容词比较级；deepest 为最高级。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 名词辨析  
**【解析】** Zhang Fang's progress 意为"张芳的进步"，progress 指学习上的进步。project 意为"项目"；promise 意为"承诺"；problem 意为"问题"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 副词辨析  
**【解析】** mostly proud 意为"主要是自豪"，mostly 表"主要地、大部分"。main 为形容词；mainly 也可但 mostly 与情感搭配更自然；most 为最高级。故选 D。
