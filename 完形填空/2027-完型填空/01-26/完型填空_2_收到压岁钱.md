# 中考英语完形填空：收到压岁钱

## 完形填空原文

When relatives visited during the holiday, I stood beside my parents and greeted everyone politely. ___1___ each elder smiled at me, a red envelope often appeared ___2___ a warm palm.

At first I felt awkward ___3___ money as a gift. My mother whispered that accepting it was not ___4___ being greedy; it carried blessings for health and study. She also told me ___5___ a polite thank-you and both hands.

That evening, we counted the envelopes ___6___ the dining table. My father suggested saving most ___7___ the bank for my future books and trips. I agreed, ___8___ I kept a small part to buy a science magazine I had wanted.

Writing down the plan in my notebook, I felt ___9___ than simply rich—I felt trusted. I promised myself ___10___ use the money wisely and remember the love behind every red paper fold.

## 选项

1. A. While &emsp;&emsp; B. Until &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. Although
2. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
3. A. receive &emsp;&emsp; B. receiving &emsp;&emsp; C. to receive &emsp;&emsp; D. received
4. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. by
5. A. say &emsp;&emsp; B. saying &emsp;&emsp; C. to say &emsp;&emsp; D. said
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. many
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每当长辈对我微笑时，红包常从温暖的手掌递出。While 在此表“当……时；每当”。B 项 Until；C 项 Unless；D 项 Although。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从温暖的手掌中出现。from 从……。B 项 to；C 项 for；D 项 with。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定结构）
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我对收钱作礼物感到别扭。feel awkward to do / about doing，选项中 to receive 符合不定式结构。A 项原形；B 项 receiving 若用需 about receiving；D 项过去式。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：接受并不代表贪婪。be about 表示“关于；意味着”。B 项 with；C 项 for；D 项 by。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她还告诉我要礼貌道谢并用双手接。tell sb. to do sth.。A 项 say；B 项 saying；D 项 said。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在餐桌上数红包。on the table 在桌上。A 项 in；C 项 at；D 项 by。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：把大部分存进银行。in the bank 在银行（账户里）。A 项 on；C 项 at；D 项 to。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我同意了，但我留了一小部分买杂志。转折用 but。A 项 and；C 项 or；D 项 so。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得自己不只是富有——更是被信任。more than 超过；不仅仅，与 simply rich 呼应。A 项 much；C 项 most；D 项 many。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我承诺要明智用钱。promise oneself to do sth.。B 项 of；C 项 for；D 项 with 均不能直接接动词原形 use。故选 A。
