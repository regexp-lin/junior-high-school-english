# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校科技节

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, our school science festival opened on the playground. My class had worked on ___1___ project about recycling for two weeks. In the morning, we set up ___2___ table and displayed bottles turned ___3___ small flowerpots. Many students came ___4___ asked how we made them. I explained the steps ___5___ English because we wanted more visitors to understand. At noon, a judge walked over and said our idea was ___6___ of the best in Grade 8. In the afternoon, we watched a robot show ___7___ was controlled by senior students. I took notes ___8___ I could try something similar at home. Before leaving, our teacher told us ___9___ we should keep asking questions about the world. That day, I realized science is not only in books ___10___ in daily life.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
4. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
5. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
6. A. one &emsp;&emsp; B. ones &emsp;&emsp; C. first &emsp;&emsp; D. once  
7. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
8. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but also &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 a/an  
**【解析】** project 以辅音音素开头，泛指 “一个关于回收的项目” 用 a project。an 用于元音音素前；the 表特指时此处无明确限定；零冠词不用于可数单数名词表泛指。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词  
**【解析】** set up a table 表示 “摆一张桌子”，泛指用 a。the table 需特指某张已知桌子；零冠词不适用。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（turn into）  
**【解析】** turn...into... 表示 “把……变成……”，bottles turned into small flowerpots 为 “瓶子变成小花盆”。in/on/at 不能与 turn 构成此义。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词  
**【解析】** came 与 asked 为顺承并列，用 and。but 表转折；or 表选择；so 表结果，均不符合。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 in + 语言  
**【解析】** in English 表示 “用英语”。with/by/at 不与语言名搭配表示 “用某种语言”。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定结构 “one of + the + 最高级 + 复数名词”  
**【解析】** one of the best 表示 “最……之一”。ones 不能这样接；first/once 与结构不符。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词  
**【解析】** 先行词 a robot show 为物，从句缺主语，用 which 引导。who 指人；what 不引导定语从句；whose 表所属。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句（so that）  
**【解析】** “记笔记以便回家能尝试类似的东西”，so that 表目的。if 表条件；unless 表除非；although 表让步，均不符合。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 that  
**【解析】** tell sb. that + 陈述句，从句 “we should keep asking questions...” 结构完整，用 that。what/how 须在从句中充当成分或与语义不符；whether 表是否。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** not only...but also...  
**【解析】** 与前文 not only in books 呼应，须用 but also in daily life。单独 and/or/so 无法与 not only 构成固定搭配。故选 B。
