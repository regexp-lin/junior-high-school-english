# 中考英语完形填空：世界森林日主题活动

## 完形填空原文

On World Forests Day, our school held a theme activity ___1___ raise awareness of tree protection. Posters with green leaves covered the bulletin board ___2___ the hallway.

In the lecture, a forester showed photos ___3___ wildlife living in old forests. "Trees are not only wood," he said. "They are homes ___4___ clean air factories."

We played a guessing game about tree names. When my team said "ginkgo," the teacher praised us ___5___ quick thinking.

After class, we collected used paper boxes ___6___ sent them for recycling. I also promised ___7___ use both sides of my exercise paper.

On the way home, I looked at the street trees ___8___ fresh eyes. They stood there quietly, ___9___ they had always been protecting us.

That night, I searched online ___10___ volunteer tree-care programs near my community.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. along
3. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. for
4. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. as if &emsp;&emsp; D. although
10. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. on

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/不定式表目的
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校举办主题活动以提高保护树木的意识。不定式 to raise 表目的。for raise 不成立；of/with 不符合结构。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：绿叶海报贴满走廊里的布告栏。along the hallway 表示“沿着走廊”，描写张贴范围。in/on/at 不如 along 能体现“走廊沿线”。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：护林员展示老林中野生动物的照片。photos of wildlife 表示“关于野生动物的照片”。with/about/for 不如 of 贴切名词修饰。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们既是家园，也是清洁空气的“工厂”。homes 与 clean air factories 并列，用 and。or/but/so 不符合并列定义。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师表扬我们反应快。praise sb. for... 表示“因……表扬某人”。of/with/on 不符合 praise 搭配。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们收集废旧纸箱并送去回收。collected 与 sent 并列顺承，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还承诺练习纸双面使用。promise to do sth. 为固定搭配。for/of/with 不符合 promise 结构。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用全新的眼光看待行道树。with fresh eyes 表示“以新的眼光”。in/on/at eyes 不自然。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们静静站在那里，仿佛一直在保护我们。as if 引导方式状语从句，表示“好像”。if/because/although 不符合比喻语气。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在网上搜索社区附近的志愿护树活动。search for... 表示“搜索寻找”。search of/about/on 搭配不如 for 标准。故选 B。
