# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工香皂

## 完形填空原文

In a DIY workshop, I learned ___1___ make handmade soap with my cousin. The teacher gave us gloves ___2___ goggles because the soap base was hot.

We melted the clear base in a pot ___3___ stirred it slowly. When it turned into liquid, we added a few drops of lavender oil. The smell spread ___4___ sweet rain in the room.

"Pour it into the mold ___5___ wait until it gets firm," the teacher said. My hands trembled a little, ___6___ the liquid slid in smoothly.

After twenty minutes, we turned the molds upside down. The soap came out ___7___ small purple bricks. I wrapped it ___8___ kraft paper and tied a string bow.

My cousin laughed, "Now washing hands feels ___9___ opening a gift." I agreed, thinking small creations can make daily habits ___10___ interesting.

## 选项

1. A. what to &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
2. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
10. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. many

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：在手工坊里我学会如何做手工皂。learn how to make... 表示“学习制作方法”。what/when/where 与“步骤学习”语境不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师给我们手套和护目镜。gloves 与 goggles 并列，用 and。or/but/so 不符合并列列举。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在锅里融化透明皂基并慢慢搅拌。melted 与 stirred 并列顺承，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：香味像甜甜的雨在房间里扩散。like sweet rain 为介词短语表比喻。as 多接从句；for/of 不自然。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：把它倒进模具并等它凝固。Pour 与 wait 为顺承祈使句并列，用 and。or/but/so 不符合操作步骤。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手有点抖，但液体还是顺利滑入。转折关系用 but。and/or/so 不符合“紧张但顺利”的对比。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：肥皂像小紫砖一样脱模出来。like small purple bricks 表比喻。as/for/of 不如 like 贴切。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用牛皮纸把它包起来。wrap...in paper 表示“用纸包裹”。on/with/at 不如 in 贴切“包在纸里”。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在洗手就像拆礼物。feel like doing/opening 中 like 为介词，后接动名词短语。feel as 需接从句；for/of 不搭配。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：让小创作让日常习惯更有趣。暗含“比以前更有趣”，用 more interesting。much/most/many 不能直接修饰 interesting 表比较。故选 B。
