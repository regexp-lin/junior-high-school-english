# 中考英语完形填空：学编程入门

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, I decided to learn something new. My cousin Tom is good at coding, so he ___1___ to teach me the basics. We started with a simple online course for beginners.

On the first day, I couldn't understand the symbols on the screen. Everything looked like a foreign ___2___. Tom explained slowly and drew pictures to help me. He told me not to worry ___3___ mistakes because even experts were beginners once.

We wrote a small program to print "Hello, world!" When it finally worked, I jumped up with ___4___. Then we tried a mini game: guessing a number. I had to think about ___5___ the computer should respond when the answer was wrong. It was like solving puzzles.

Sometimes the program didn't run. I felt ___6___ and wanted to quit. Tom taught me to read the error messages ___7___ and search for solutions. Step by step, I learned to fix bugs by myself.

After two weeks, I finished my first project—a digital clock. I showed it to my parents, and they were ___8___ surprised. This experience taught me that learning coding is not only about typing codes ___9___ about logical thinking and patience. I plan to join the school computer club ___10___ the new term starts.

## 选项

1. A. refused &emsp;&emsp; B. agreed &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. failed
2. A. language &emsp;&emsp; B. country &emsp;&emsp; C. song &emsp;&emsp; D. story
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. with
4. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. shame &emsp;&emsp; D. doubt
5. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. who
6. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. sleepy
7. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. politely &emsp;&emsp; D. quietly
8. A. quite &emsp;&emsp; B. never &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. almost
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的表哥汤姆擅长编程，所以他同意教我入门。agree to do sth. 同意做某事。refuse 拒绝；forget 忘记；fail 失败。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：屏幕上的一切看起来像一门外语。foreign language 外语，比喻代码难懂。country 国家；song 歌曲；story 故事。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他告诉我不要担心错误。worry about 担心，为固定搭配。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当它终于运行时，我高兴得跳了起来。with joy 高兴地。fear 恐惧；shame 羞愧；doubt 怀疑。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我必须思考当答案错误时电脑应如何回应。how 如何，表方式。what 什么；where 哪里；who 谁。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时程序不能运行。我感到生气并想放弃。angry 生气的。happy 开心的；relaxed 放松的；sleepy 困倦的。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：汤姆教我仔细阅读错误信息并查找解决办法。carefully 仔细地。carelessly 粗心地；politely 礼貌地；quietly 安静地。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把它给父母看，他们相当惊讶。quite 相当、很，修饰 surprised 表示程度。never 从不；hardly 几乎不；almost 几乎。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词（not only... but (also)...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这段经历让我明白，学编程不仅是敲代码，更是逻辑思考与耐心。not only... but (also)... 不仅……而且……，also 可省略。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我打算在新学期开始之后参加学校的计算机社团。after 在……之后。before 在……之前；while 当……时；since 自从。故选 A。
