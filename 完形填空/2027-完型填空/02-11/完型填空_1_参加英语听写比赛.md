# 中考英语完形填空：参加英语听写比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our English teacher told us about a school dictation contest. At first, I felt ___1___ because I often made spelling mistakes in class. However, my teacher said, "Practice makes perfect. You should ___2___ yourself a chance." Her words made me think twice.

Every evening after dinner, I spent twenty minutes ___3___ new words from our textbook. I also listened to short recordings and wrote down what I heard. Sometimes I got tired, ___4___ I didn't give up. My best friend Lily practiced with me. We checked each other's lists and ___5___ mistakes together.

On the day of the contest, my heart beat fast. When the judge read the first word, I took a deep breath and wrote ___6___. The room was quiet except for the sound of pens. After ten rounds, only eight students were left. I couldn't believe I was still ___7___!

In the final round, I spelled a long word wrong and came third. I felt a little sad, ___8___ I was proud of my progress. My teacher smiled and said, "You have learned something more important ___9___ a prize—confidence and persistence." Now I know that ___10___ I try my best, I can improve step by step.

## 选项

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
2. A. lend &emsp;&emsp; B. send &emsp;&emsp; C. give &emsp;&emsp; D. pass
3. A. reviewing &emsp;&emsp; B. drawing &emsp;&emsp; C. selling &emsp;&emsp; D. throwing
4. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. because
5. A. forgot &emsp;&emsp; B. corrected &emsp;&emsp; C. repeated &emsp;&emsp; D. copied
6. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
7. A. there &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. away &emsp;&emsp; D. behind
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but
9. A. over &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
10. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，我感到紧张，因为我经常在课上拼写错误。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。由"often made spelling mistakes"可知应选紧张。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你应该给自己一个机会。give sb. a chance 为固定搭配，意为"给某人机会"。lend 借出；send 发送；pass 传递。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词-ing 形式辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我每天晚饭后花二十分钟复习课本里的生词。spend time doing sth. 花时间做某事。review 复习；draw 画；sell 卖；throw 扔。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我很累，但我没有放弃。前后为转折关系，用 but。or 否则/或者；so 所以；because 因为。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们互相检查清单并一起改正错误。correct mistakes 改正错误。forget 忘记；repeat 重复；copy 抄写。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当裁判读出第一个词时，我深呼吸并认真地写下来。carefully 认真地。carelessly 粗心地；quickly 快速地；loudly 大声地。比赛听写应认真书写。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词/语境理解
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不敢相信自己还在那里（仍在比赛中）。be still there 表示仍在场上/仍在比赛中。out 出局；away 离开；behind 落后。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点难过，但我为自己的进步感到骄傲。前后转折，用 but。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：你学到了比奖品更重要的东西——自信与坚持。more important than... 比……更重要。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我明白，如果我尽最大努力，就能一步步提高。if 如果，引导条件状语从句。unless 除非；although 尽管；until 直到。故选 A。
