# 中考英语完形填空：班级春游去植物园

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, our class went to the Botanical Garden ___1___ a spring outing. The weather was warm and sunny, perfect ___2___ an outdoor trip.

When we arrived, the guide led us ___3___ a path lined with cherry trees. The pink flowers were so beautiful ___4___ everyone took out their phones to take photos.

In the greenhouse, we saw many tropical plants. Some leaves were ___5___ than my hand. The guide explained that these plants need a lot of water ___6___ sunlight to grow.

After lunch, we joined a plant quiz. My group answered seven questions ___7___ of ten. We didn't win the first prize, ___8___ we still got a small cactus as a gift.

On the bus home, I looked ___9___ my photos and felt happy. Nature has so much to teach us ___10___ we pay attention.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
3. A. along &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
4. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. if
5. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. bigger &emsp;&emsp; C. biggest &emsp;&emsp; D. as big
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. out &emsp;&emsp; B. up &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. in
8. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. after
10. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. although

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班去植物园进行春游。for a spring outing 表示"为了春游"，说明出行目的。in/at/with 不符合表目的的搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：天气很适合户外旅行。perfect for... 表示"非常适合……"。in/at/with 不符合 perfect 的搭配。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：导游带我们沿着一条两旁种满樱花树的小路走。along a path 表示"沿着小路"。in/at/on 不如 along 贴切表示"沿着"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** so...that 结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：粉色花朵如此美丽以至于每个人都拍照。so...that... 引导结果状语从句。when/because/if 不符合 so 引导的结构。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些叶子比我的手还大。than 提示用比较级 bigger。big 为原级；biggest 为最高级；as big 需搭配 as。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些植物需要大量的水和阳光来生长。water 与 sunlight 并列，用 and。or/but/so 不符合列举关系。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们组答对了十道题中的七道。... out of ten 表示"十个中的（七个）"。up/off/in 不符合"在总数中"的表达。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们没赢得一等奖，但仍然得到了一个小仙人掌作礼物。前后为转折关系，用 but。or/and/so 不符合。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在回家的公交车上翻看照片。look through 表示"浏览、翻阅"。look at 为"看"；look for 为"寻找"；look after 为"照顾"，不符合语境。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果我们留心观察，大自然有很多东西可以教我们。if 引导条件状语从句，表示"如果"。before/unless/although 不符合"条件"语境。故选 C。
