# 中考英语完形填空：第一次骑车上学

## 完形填空原文

This Monday, I rode my bike to school ___1___ the first time. Dad checked the tires and brakes for me the night ___2___.

The road was busy in the morning. I rode ___3___ along the bike lane and kept my eyes on the traffic lights. When a red light came, I stopped ___4___ waited patiently.

Near the school gate, I saw my friend Li Hua. He waved ___5___ me and shouted, "You made it!" I felt proud ___6___ myself.

Parking the bike was harder ___7___ I expected. The bike rack was almost full. I squeezed my bike between two others ___8___ locked it carefully.

In class, I told my deskmate about the ride. She said she also wanted ___9___ try cycling to school.

After school, the ride home was even ___10___. The evening breeze felt cool on my face. I decided to ride my bike every day from then on.

## 选项

1. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. on
2. A. ago &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. later &emsp;&emsp; D. after
3. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. quick &emsp;&emsp; D. fast
4. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. in
7. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. from
8. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. on
10. A. enjoyable &emsp;&emsp; B. more enjoyable &emsp;&emsp; C. most enjoyable &emsp;&emsp; D. less enjoyable

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个周一我第一次骑车上学。for the first time 为固定搭配，表示"第一次"。at/in/on 不符合该短语搭配。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸前一天晚上帮我检查了轮胎和刹车。the night before 表示"前一天晚上"。ago 不能与 the 连用；later/after 不表示"之前"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我沿着自行车道慢慢骑。修饰动词 rode 需用副词 slowly。slow 为形容词；quick 为形容词；fast 虽可作副词但不符合"小心骑行"的语境。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：红灯亮时我停下来并耐心等待。stopped 与 waited 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他向我挥手。wave to sb. 为固定搭配，表示"向某人挥手"。at/for/with 不符合 wave 的搭配。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我为自己感到骄傲。be proud of... 为固定搭配，表示"为……感到骄傲"。for/with/in 不符合 proud 的搭配。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：停放自行车比我预期的更难。harder 为比较级，后接 than 表比较。as/like/from 不符合比较级结构。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把自行车挤进两辆车之间并仔细锁好。squeezed 与 locked 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说她也想试试骑车上学。want to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示"想要做某事"。for/of/on 不符合 want 的结构。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：放学骑回家更加愉快。even + 比较级表示"甚至更……"，用 more enjoyable。enjoyable 为原级；most enjoyable 为最高级；less enjoyable 意为"不那么愉快"，不符合积极语境。故选 B。
