# 中考英语完形填空：学校运动会开幕

## 完形填空原文

The sports meet opened ___1___ a bright Wednesday morning. Students lined up on the playground, ___2___ the class flags were waving in the wind. The headmaster gave a short speech ___3___ encouraged everyone to play fairly and cheer for one another. When the athletes marched past the podium, our class shouted ___4___ loudly than any other team. My friend Leo carried the torch ___5___ he had practiced the route many times. During the oath, we promised ___6___ respect rules and help teammates. A flock of doves was released, ___7___ the sky looked wide and hopeful. I used to think opening ceremonies were only formal walks, ___8___ today I felt real excitement in every step. After the first whistle blew, I ran ___9___ the stand to save seats for my classmates. This event reminded me ___10___ teamwork begins long before the race starts.

## 选项

1. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
4. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. many
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. toward &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. over
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. how

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（时间）
**【解析】** 本句意为：运动会在一个晴朗的周三上午开幕。在具体某天上午用 on，故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词与并列结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们在操场上列队，班旗在风中飘扬。前后为两个并列分句，用 and 连接，故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：校长发表了简短讲话，鼓励大家公平竞赛并相互加油。先行词 speech 指物，从句缺主语，用 which，故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：……我们班比其他任何队伍喊得都更大声。than 提示用比较级 more loudly，故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的朋友 Leo 举着火炬，因为他已经练习过很多次路线。说明原因用 because，故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（promise to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：宣誓时，我们承诺尊重规则并帮助队友。promise to do sth. 为固定搭配，故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一群白鸽被放飞，天空看起来开阔而充满希望。并列顺承，用 and，故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我过去总以为开幕式只是走过场，但今天我在每一步里都感受到真正的兴奋。转折用 but，故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一声哨响后，我朝看台跑去给同学占座。toward 表示“朝；向”，故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：这件事提醒我：团队合作在比赛开始之前就已经开始了。remind sb. that... 引导宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 that，故选 B。
