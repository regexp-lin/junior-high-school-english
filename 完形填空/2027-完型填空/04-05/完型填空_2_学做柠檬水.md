# 中考英语完形填空：学做柠檬水

## 完形填空原文

Summer is coming, ___1___ my sister taught me to make lemonade at home. We washed fresh lemons and rolled them ___2___ the cutting board to get more juice. She told me ___3___ squeeze gently so the seeds would not fall in. I added cold water and a spoonful of honey ___4___ of white sugar because Mom prefers a healthier taste. The first glass was ___5___ sour for my cousin, ___6___ we added a little more honey. After tasting it again, he smiled and said it tasted ___7___ than drinks from the shop. I wrote the steps ___8___ small cards and stuck them on the fridge. Making drinks at home is ___9___ expensive, and it also helps us reduce plastic waste. Next time, I will try ___10___ a few mint leaves on top for a cooler smell.

## 选项

1. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. although
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
4. A. instead &emsp;&emsp; B. instead of &emsp;&emsp; C. rather &emsp;&emsp; D. without
5. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. quite
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
7. A. fresh &emsp;&emsp; B. fresher &emsp;&emsp; C. freshest &emsp;&emsp; D. freshly
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. little &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. least &emsp;&emsp; D. fewer
10. A. add &emsp;&emsp; B. adding &emsp;&emsp; C. to add &emsp;&emsp; D. added

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：夏天快到了，所以姐姐在家教我做柠檬水。因果关系用 so，故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们洗净柠檬，并在砧板上滚动以榨出更多汁。on the cutting board 表示“在砧板上”，故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她告诉我要轻轻挤压，以免籽掉进汁里。tell sb. to do sth.，故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（instead of）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我加了冷水和一勺蜂蜜，而不是白砂糖。instead of 表示“而不是”，故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（too...to...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一杯对我表弟来说太酸了。too...for sb. 表示“对某人来说太……”，故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：所以我们又加了一点蜂蜜。表结果用 so，故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：……他说味道比店里的饮料更清爽。than 提示用比较级 fresher，故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把步骤写在小卡片上。on small cards 表示“在卡片上”，故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：在家做饮料不那么贵…… expensive 的比较级为 less expensive，故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（try doing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次，我会试着在顶部加几片薄荷叶…… try doing 表示“试着做某事（尝试一种做法）”，符合语境；try to do 侧重“努力去做”。此处尝试加薄荷，用 trying/adding 结构：will try adding，故选 B。
