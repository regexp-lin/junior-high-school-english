# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校冬季运动会

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, our school held ___1___ big winter sports meet on the playground behind our teaching building. Although it was a cold morning, the playground was full ___2___ energy and happy shouts. This year's meet was ___3___ popular than last year's, and several new fun races ___4___ planned for Grade Eight students only.

My best friend Tom and I got there ___5___ than our classmates because we wanted extra practice. I finally chose ___6___ the 4×100-meter relay with Tom, Lily, and Jack, though I had worried that I was not good ___7___ running short distances. Our PE teacher, Mr. Hu, encouraged ___8___ and asked us to warm up slowly and carefully.

When it was our turn, I felt my legs shaking and my heart beating ___9___. The starting gun sounded, and I tried ___10___ best to run my part smoothly and hand the baton to Lily without dropping it. We came third, but the smiles on our faces were brighter than any medal.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
3. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
4. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. are &emsp;&emsp; C. was &emsp;&emsp; D. were  
5. A. early &emsp;&emsp; B. earlier &emsp;&emsp; C. earliest &emsp;&emsp; D. more early  
6. A. join &emsp;&emsp; B. joining &emsp;&emsp; C. to join &emsp;&emsp; D. joined  
7. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
9. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quicker &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. quickest  
10. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. me &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词表示泛指  
**【解析】** “一场大型的冬季运动会”表示泛指，且 *big* 以辅音音素开头，用 *a*。*an* 用于元音音素开头的词前；*the* 表特指，此处首次提到活动，不宜用定冠词；零冠词不符合“一场运动会”的表达。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *be full of*  
**【解析】** *be full of* 为固定短语，意为“充满……”。*with* 常构成 *be filled with*；*for/in* 与 *full* 不构成此义。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（*than* 提示）  
**【解析】** 句中有 *than last year's*，需用比较级 *more popular*。*most* 为最高级；*much/very* 不能直接与 *than* 搭配构成比较。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** *races* 与 *plan* 之间是被动关系，且叙述过去事件，用一般过去时被动 *were planned*（主语复数）。*is/are* 时态或语态不对；*was* 与复数主语不一致。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级  
**【解析】** 后有 *than our classmates*，表示“比同学更早到”，用 *earlier*。*early* 无比较含义；*earliest* 为最高级，语境无三者以上“最”；*more early* 为错误形式。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*choose to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *choose* 后接不定式作宾语，*choose to join* 表示“选择参加”。动名词、原形或过去式均不符合 *choose* 的常见搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *be good at*  
**【解析】** *be good at doing sth.* 表示“擅长做某事”。*for* 常构成 *be good for*；*on/with* 不与 *good* 构成此义。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** *encourage* 为及物动词，后接宾语，用人称代词宾格 *us*。*we* 为主格；*our* 为形容词性物主代词；*ours* 为名词性物主代词，均不能单独作宾语。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 *beating*（心脏跳动），用副词 *quickly*。*quick/quickest* 为形容词；*quicker* 为比较级形容词，不能修饰动词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *try one's best*  
**【解析】** *try one's best* 中 *one's* 需与主语 *I* 一致，用形容词性物主代词 *my*。其余代词形式不能构成该固定搭配。故选 B。
