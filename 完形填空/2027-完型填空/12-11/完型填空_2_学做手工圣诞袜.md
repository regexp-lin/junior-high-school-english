# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工圣诞袜

## 完形填空原文

Christmas was coming, and our art teacher, Ms. Lin, decided ___1___ us make small Christmas stockings in class. She brought red felt, white cotton, scissors, and glue. First, she showed ___2___ a simple sock pattern on the screen and explained each step ___3___.

We worked in groups ___4___ four. I was a little nervous because I am not good ___5___ using scissors. My partner Anna smiled and said, “Don’t worry. We can help ___6___ other.” We cut two sock shapes, glued the edges, and left ___7___ top open for “gifts.”

While we were working, Ms. Lin walked around the classroom ___8___ questions. When I made a mistake, she didn’t laugh ___9___ me. Instead, she taught me how to fix it patiently. At the end of the lesson, ___10___ stocking looked cute with a little golden bell on it.

## 选项

1. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
2. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
3. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. clearer &emsp;&emsp; C. clearly &emsp;&emsp; D. clearest  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
5. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
6. A. each &emsp;&emsp; B. every &emsp;&emsp; C. both &emsp;&emsp; D. all  
7. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
8. A. answer &emsp;&emsp; B. answering &emsp;&emsp; C. answered &emsp;&emsp; D. to answer  
9. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
10. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*decide to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *decide* 后接动词不定式作宾语，*decided to help us*。*help/helping/helped* 均不能直接跟在 *decide* 后作宾语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（双宾语 *show sb. sth.*）  
**【解析】** *show* 后接间接宾语，用人称代词宾格 *us*。*we* 为主格；*our/ours* 为物主代词，不作间接宾语。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 *explained*，用副词 *clearly*。*clear/clearer/clearest* 为形容词，不能修饰动词。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *of* 表示组成  
**【解析】** *in groups of four* 表示“四人一组”，*of* 表数量构成。*in/on/at* 与 *groups of* 的常见搭配不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *be good at*  
**【解析】** *be good at* 后接名词或动名词，表示“擅长……”，*be good at using scissors* 符合句意。*be good with* 多表示“善于应付/与人相处或与工具打交道”，此处考查最典型搭配 *at*。*for/on* 不构成此义。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 *each other*  
**【解析】** *help each other* 表示“互相帮助”。*every* 不与 *other* 构成固定搭配；*both* 指两者都，后不接 *other* 表相互；*all other* 语义不通。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定冠词表特指  
**【解析】** *the top* 特指袜子的开口那一端，双方已知所指。*a/an* 表泛指此处不贴切；零冠词不能清楚表达“袜口”。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作伴随状语  
**【解析】** *walked around... answering questions* 表示边走边回答问题，用现在分词作伴随状语。不定式多表目的，过去式、原形不能作伴随状语。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *laugh at*  
**【解析】** *laugh at sb.* 意为“嘲笑某人”。*laugh to/for/with* 不构成“嘲笑”之义。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** 修饰名词 *stocking*，用形容词性物主代词 *my*。*I/me* 不能作定语；*mine* 后不接名词。故选 C。
