# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸灯笼

## 完形填空原文

The Chinese New Year is near, so Mom signed me up ___1___ a paper lantern workshop at the culture center. The instructor first showed us ___2___ to fold red paper evenly. I followed each step, ___3___ my first lantern looked uneven. “Try again ___4___ pressing the creases harder,” the instructor advised. I took another sheet ___5___ paper and worked more slowly. This time the frame stood straight, ___6___ I painted a golden rabbit on the sides. Mom came ___7___ pick me up and took photos immediately. She said the lantern looked ___8___ than the ones in the shop window. I hung it ___9___ our living room and turned on the small LED light inside. Making something by hand made the holiday feel ___10___ real to me.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
5. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. lovely &emsp;&emsp; B. lovelier &emsp;&emsp; C. loveliest &emsp;&emsp; D. more lovely  
9. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
10. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** sign sb. up for sth. 固定搭配  
**【解析】** “为某人报名参加……”用 sign me up for a workshop。to/with/at 不构成该固定搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + to do”作宾语 show sb. how to do  
**【解析】** 老师示范“如何把红纸折得均匀”，强调方式，用 how to fold。what/why/when 与示范折纸步骤不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 but 表转折  
**【解析】** “我跟着每一步做”与“第一个灯笼仍不均匀”为转折，用 but。and 顺承；or 选择；so 结果，均不符合。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 by 表方式 by doing  
**【解析】** try again by pressing... 表示“通过更用力压折痕再试一次”。in/on/at 不表“通过某种方式”。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** another sheet of paper 固定量词搭配  
**【解析】** a sheet of paper 表示“一张纸”，another sheet of paper 为固定搭配。for/in/with 不与 sheet 构成此结构。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and 表顺承  
**【解析】** “框架立得很正”与“我在侧面画了金兔”为顺承并列，用 and。if 条件；because 原因；although 让步，均不符合。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 动词不定式表目的 come to pick sb. up  
**【解析】** come to pick me up 表示“来接我”，不定式 to pick 表目的。for/of/with 不构成 come...pick 的目的结构。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 lovelier than  
**【解析】** 后有 than，用比较级 lovelier。lovely 为原级；loveliest 为最高级缺范围；more lovely 虽偶见，但中学阶段更规范、更常见的比较级为 lovelier。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 in our living room  
**【解析】** “挂在客厅里”用 in our living room。on 多指表面；at 表点；to 方向，均不如 in 自然。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** more + 形容词原级（暗含对比）  
**【解析】** feel more real 表示“感觉更真实”，暗含与“只停留在口头/商业氛围的节日感受”对比。many 修饰可数名词；much 修饰不可数名词或比较级但不直接接原级 real；most 为最高级。故选 C。
