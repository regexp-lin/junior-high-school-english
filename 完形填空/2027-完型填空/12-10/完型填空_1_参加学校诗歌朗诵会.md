# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校诗歌朗诵会

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a poetry reading ___1___ Friday afternoon. Each class could choose ___2___ English poem and perform it on stage. Our group picked a short piece ___3___ nature and peace. We practiced pronunciation after school, ___4___ our English teacher helped us record a demo. On the big day, my hands were cold ___5___ I still spoke clearly into the microphone. When we finished, the hall was quiet ___6___ a second, and then applause broke out. The judges said our teamwork was ___7___ of all the Grade 8 teams. I felt ___8___ excited than nervous at that moment. After the event, I bought a small book of poems ___9___ I wanted to read more at home. This experience taught me ___10___ beautiful words can connect hearts.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well  
8. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. which  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间介词 on + 星期/具体某天下午  
**【解析】** “在周五下午”用 on Friday afternoon。in 多用于月份/季节；at 多用于具体钟点；by 表“到……为止”，不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 an 用于元音音素开头的单词前  
**【解析】** English 以元音音素开头，一首英语诗用 an English poem。a 用于辅音音素开头；the 特指不足；零冠词可数单数一般不单独使用。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 about 表“关于”  
**【解析】** a short piece about nature 表示“关于自然与和平的短诗”。in/on/at 与 piece 搭配不如 about 表达主题贴切。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and 连接两个并列分句  
**【解析】** 前半句讲“放学后练习发音”，后半句讲“英语老师帮我们录了示范音频”，两者为顺承并列关系，用 and。if 表条件；although 表让步，与语境不符；unless 表“除非”，逻辑不通。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 but 表转折  
**【解析】** “手很冷”与“仍然读得很清楚”为转折，用 but。and 顺承；or 否则；so 结果，均不符合。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 for + 一段时间 for a second  
**【解析】** “安静了一秒钟”用 for a second。of/in/on 不与 a second 表持续时间搭配。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级 best of all...  
**【解析】** 句中有范围 of all the Grade 8 teams，表示“最好的”，用 best。good 原级；better 缺两者比较对象；well 为副词。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** more + 形容词原级 + than  
**【解析】** excited 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more excited than。most 最高级；much/very 不接 than。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** because 引导原因状语从句  
**【解析】** “买书”是因为“想在家多读”，表原因用 because。if/unless 条件；although 让步，均不符合。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接副词 how 表程度/方式  
**【解析】** taught me 后为宾语从句，表示“美好的词句如何连接心灵”，强调方式/程度，用 how。what 缺成分；whether 是否；which 选择。故选 C。
