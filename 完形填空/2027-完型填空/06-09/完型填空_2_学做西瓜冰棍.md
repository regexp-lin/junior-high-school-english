# 中考英语完形填空：学做西瓜冰棍

## 完形填空原文

It was a very hot day. My brother and I decided ___1___ make watermelon popsicles to cool down. Mom bought us a big, round watermelon from the market.

First, I cut the watermelon in half ___2___ a big knife. Then my brother helped me remove all the ___3___. This took quite a long time because there were so many.

After that, we put the watermelon pieces into the blender and added ___4___ little sugar. We pressed the button and waited. In less than a minute, the juice was ___5___.

We poured the pink juice into popsicle molds ___6___. My brother was so careless ___7___ he spilled some on the table. I wiped it clean and told him to be more ___8___.

We put the molds into the freezer and waited for four hours. ___9___ the popsicles were finally done, we each ate two. They were the ___10___ delicious popsicles I had ever tasted.

## 选项

1. A. / &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. on
2. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. in
3. A. seed &emsp;&emsp; B. seeds &emsp;&emsp; C. skin &emsp;&emsp; D. leaf
4. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
5. A. finish &emsp;&emsp; B. finished &emsp;&emsp; C. finishing &emsp;&emsp; D. ready
6. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. careless &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. who
8. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. careless
9. A. When &emsp;&emsp; B. Before &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. Although
10. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. much

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和弟弟决定做西瓜冰棍来降温。"decide to do sth."表示"决定做某事"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（with 表工具）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用一把大刀把西瓜切成两半。with 表示"用……工具"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词单复数
**【解析】** 本句意为：弟弟帮我去掉所有的籽。all 后接可数名词复数 seeds。下文"so many"也提示用复数。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（a little + 不可数名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：加了一点点糖。"a little"表示"一点点"，修饰不可数名词 sugar。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词作表语（ready 准备好的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：不到一分钟，果汁就做好了。ready 表示"准备好的/完成的"。finished 也可，但 ready 更自然。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们小心地把粉色果汁倒入冰棍模具。修饰动词 poured 用副词 carefully。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构（so...that... 如此……以至于）
**【解析】** 本句意为：弟弟太不小心以至于洒了一些在桌上。"so + adj. + that"为固定结构。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 系动词 + 形容词（be more careful）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我让他更小心一些。"be + more + adj."中用形容词 careful。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（when 当……时候）
**【解析】** 本句意为：当冰棍终于做好时，我们每人吃了两根。When 表示"当……时"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（the most + 多音节形容词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是我吃过的最好吃的冰棍。"the most delicious"为最高级，配合 ever。故选 B。
