# 中考英语完形填空：参加社区义卖活动

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, our community held a charity sale. My mother encouraged me ___1___ part in it. I decided to sell some old books and toys ___2___ I no longer needed.

We set up a small table early in the morning. I put price tags ___3___ every item. Most things were very cheap — just one or two yuan each. My mother said the purpose was not ___4___ money but to help others.

A little girl stopped at my table and picked ___5___ a picture book. She looked at it ___6___ a long time but didn't have enough money. I decided to give it to her for free. Her mother thanked me ___7___ and the girl smiled brightly.

By noon, I had sold ___8___ everything on the table. The organizer told us all the money ___9___ be donated to children in poor areas. I felt very proud ___10___ I had done something meaningful.

## 选项

1. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. took &emsp;&emsp; C. to take &emsp;&emsp; D. taking
2. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. where
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
4. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made
5. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. on
6. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on
7. A. warm &emsp;&emsp; B. warmer &emsp;&emsp; C. warmly &emsp;&emsp; D. warmth
8. A. near &emsp;&emsp; B. nearly &emsp;&emsp; C. next &emsp;&emsp; D. nearby
9. A. will &emsp;&emsp; B. would &emsp;&emsp; C. can &emsp;&emsp; D. must
10. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（encourage sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈鼓励我参加。"encourage sb. to do sth."为固定结构。to take part in 参加。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句（that 指代物）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定卖掉一些我不再需要的旧书和玩具。先行词为 books and toys，that 在从句中作宾语。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（on 在表面上）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在每件物品上贴了价签。on 表示"在……上面"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作表语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：目的不是赚钱而是帮助他人。"The purpose was not to... but to..."结构，不定式作表语。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语（pick up 拿起）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小女孩在我的摊位前停下，拿起一本图画书。"pick up"表示"拿起"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（for + 时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她看了很长时间。"for a long time"表示"很长时间"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她妈妈热情地感谢我。修饰动词 thanked 用副词 warmly。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（nearly 几乎）
**【解析】** 本句意为：到中午我几乎卖完了桌上所有东西。nearly 表示"几乎"。near 为形容词/介词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 情态动词（would 过去将来时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：组织者告诉我们所有的钱将会捐给贫困地区的孩子。主句用过去时 told，从句用 would 表过去将来。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（because 表原因）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到很自豪因为我做了有意义的事。because 表原因。故选 B。
