# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工南瓜灯

## 完形填空原文

To celebrate Halloween, our class decided to make jack-o'-lanterns. Our English teacher brought several small pumpkins and the ___1___ tools to class.

She first explained the tradition behind jack-o'-lanterns. In old Irish legends, people carved scary faces on turnips ___2___ scare away evil spirits. Later, when Irish immigrants went to America, they started ___3___ pumpkins instead because pumpkins were bigger and easier to carve.

We worked in pairs. My partner was Chen Yu, ___4___ is good at drawing. She designed the face on paper first — two triangle eyes, a nose, and a big smiling mouth.

I was responsible for the carving. The teacher gave me a small knife and told me to be ___5___ careful. First, I cut a circle on the top and scooped out the seeds and pulp inside. It was messier ___6___ I had expected!

Then I carefully followed Chen Yu's design and carved the face. The hardest part was the mouth because it had many ___7___. I went slowly and managed to finish without breaking anything.

When all the pumpkins were done, the teacher placed candles ___8___ them. She turned off the lights, and the classroom was filled with warm orange glows. The carved faces looked both funny and mysterious.

Everyone took photos with their pumpkins. The teacher said this activity not only taught us about foreign culture ___9___ also improved our teamwork skills. I agreed. It was ___10___ of the most enjoyable afternoons I had ever spent at school.

## 选项

1. A. need &emsp;&emsp; B. needed &emsp;&emsp; C. needing &emsp;&emsp; D. needs  
2. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. even though &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as  
3. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. used &emsp;&emsp; C. using &emsp;&emsp; D. to use  
4. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. that  
5. A. extreme &emsp;&emsp; B. extremely &emsp;&emsp; C. more extreme &emsp;&emsp; D. most extreme  
6. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. from  
7. A. curve &emsp;&emsp; B. curves &emsp;&emsp; C. curving &emsp;&emsp; D. curved  
8. A. beside &emsp;&emsp; B. behind &emsp;&emsp; C. between &emsp;&emsp; D. inside  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
10. A. some &emsp;&emsp; B. any &emsp;&emsp; C. one &emsp;&emsp; D. every  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作定语  
**【解析】** the needed tools 意为"需要的工具"，needed 为过去分词作前置定语。need 为动词原形；needing 为现在分词；needs 为三单。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语  
**【解析】** in order to scare away 意为"为了吓走"，in order to + 动词原形表目的。so that 后接从句；even though 表让步；as long as 表条件。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（start doing）  
**【解析】** started using pumpkins 意为"开始使用南瓜"，start 后接动名词。use 为原形；used 为过去式；to use 虽可但 using 更自然表"开始的持续动作"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句（指人）  
**【解析】** 先行词 Chen Yu 为人，在从句中作主语，用 who 引导非限制性定语从句。which 指物；whom 作宾语；that 不引导非限制性定语从句。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰形容词  
**【解析】** extremely careful 意为"极其小心"，extremely 为副词修饰形容词 careful。extreme 为形容词；more/most extreme 为比较级/最高级。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 比较级搭配 than  
**【解析】** messier than I had expected 意为"比我预期的更脏乱"，比较级后接 than。as 用于同级比较；like 意为"像"；from 意为"从"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词复数  
**【解析】** many curves 意为"很多弯曲处"，many 后接可数名词复数。curve 为单数；curving 为动名词/现在分词；curved 为形容词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（方位）  
**【解析】** placed candles inside them 意为"在南瓜里面放蜡烛"，inside 表"在里面"。beside 意为"旁边"；behind 意为"后面"；between 意为"之间"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** not only...but also... 结构  
**【解析】** not only taught us...but also improved 构成"不仅……而且……"结构，but 与 not only 搭配。and/or/so 不与 not only 搭配。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** one of + the + 最高级 + 名词复数  
**【解析】** one of the most enjoyable afternoons 意为"最愉快的下午之一"，one of 为固定结构。some/any/every 不构成此最高级结构。故选 C。
