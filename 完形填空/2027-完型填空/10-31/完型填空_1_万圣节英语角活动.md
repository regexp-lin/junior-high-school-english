# 中考英语完形填空：万圣节英语角活动

## 完形填空原文

October 31st is Halloween, a traditional Western holiday. Our English teacher decided to hold a special English Corner ___1___ this occasion.

She asked each student to come to class ___2___ a costume or a Halloween-themed prop. Some students wore witch hats, some drew spider webs on their faces, and one boy even dressed up ___3___ a vampire. The classroom was decorated with paper bats and pumpkin lanterns.

The activity started with a storytelling session. The teacher turned off the lights and used a flashlight to create ___4___ spooky atmosphere. She told us an old English ghost story in a low voice. Even though we knew it was just a story, several students still felt ___5___.

After that, we played "Trick or Treat" in English. We had to knock on different groups' desks and say the phrase correctly. If the pronunciation was good, we would ___6___ a piece of candy. If not, we had to answer an English question as a "trick."

I visited five groups and ___7___ four candies. The only group that tricked me asked me to spell "Halloween" backward. I tried my best but ___8___. Everyone laughed, including me.

The most popular part was the costume contest. Each student introduced ___9___ costume in English. The boy dressed as a vampire won first place because his English was fluent and his costume was the most impressive.

The teacher said she hoped this activity would make us more interested ___10___ English and Western culture. I think she succeeded.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
2. A. wear &emsp;&emsp; B. wore &emsp;&emsp; C. wearing &emsp;&emsp; D. worn  
3. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. into  
4. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
5. A. scare &emsp;&emsp; B. scared &emsp;&emsp; C. scary &emsp;&emsp; D. scaring  
6. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. gave &emsp;&emsp; C. be given &emsp;&emsp; D. giving  
7. A. collect &emsp;&emsp; B. collects &emsp;&emsp; C. collected &emsp;&emsp; D. collecting  
8. A. fail &emsp;&emsp; B. failed &emsp;&emsp; C. failing &emsp;&emsp; D. fails  
9. A. his &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. their  
10. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. for  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 for 表目的/原因  
**【解析】** for this occasion 意为"为了这个场合"，for 表目的。in/at/on 不如 for 贴切表达"为了庆祝这个节日"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作伴随状语  
**【解析】** come to class wearing a costume 意为"穿着服装来上课"，wearing 为现在分词表伴随状态。wear 为原形；wore 为过去式（不能与 come 并列）；worn 为过去分词（被动）。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 dress up as  
**【解析】** dress up as a vampire 意为"装扮成吸血鬼"，as 表"作为"。like 意为"像"但不如 as 精确表装扮成某角色；for/into 不构成此搭配。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词  
**【解析】** a spooky atmosphere 意为"一种恐怖的氛围"，spooky 以辅音音素 /s/ 开头，用 a。an 用于元音音素前；the 为定冠词；零冠词此处不合适。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）  
**【解析】** felt scared 意为"感到害怕"，scared 修饰人的感受。scare 为动词；scary 意为"吓人的"修饰事物；scaring 为现在分词"正在吓人"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 被动语态（would + be + 过去分词）  
**【解析】** would be given a piece of candy 意为"会被给一块糖"，we 与 give 为被动关系。give 为主动原形；gave 为主动过去式；giving 为现在分词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（并列谓语）  
**【解析】** and 连接 visited 和 collected，时态一致用过去式。collect 为原形；collects 为三单；collecting 为现在分词。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** but failed 意为"但失败了"，全文为过去叙述用过去式。fail 为原形；failing 为现在分词；fails 为三单。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** each student introduced their costume 中 student 为泛指，用 their 指代。his/her 只指代单一性别；its 指物。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be interested in  
**【解析】** interested in English and Western culture 意为"对英语和西方文化感兴趣"，in 为固定介词。at/on/for 不与 interested 构成此搭配。故选 B。
