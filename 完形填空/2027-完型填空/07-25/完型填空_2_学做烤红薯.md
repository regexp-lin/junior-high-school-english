# 中考英语完形填空：学做烤红薯

## 完形填空原文

At the campsite, Uncle Wang taught the kids ___1___ roast sweet potatoes. They wrapped each potato ___2___ foil and buried them ___3___ hot ashes.

While they ___4___, Uncle explained ___5___ turning the potatoes helped them cook evenly. The smell grew ___6___ and made everyone hungry.

When the potatoes ___7___ taken out, they were soft and sweet. Lily said they tasted ___8___ better than those from the oven at home. She asked Uncle ___9___ show her the trick again next month. Cooking outdoors seemed ___10___ more exciting than staying indoors.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. under &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. waited &emsp;&emsp; C. were waiting &emsp;&emsp; D. have waited
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. how
6. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. stronger &emsp;&emsp; C. strongly &emsp;&emsp; D. strongest
7. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. have been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be
8. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. too &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. quite &emsp;&emsp; C. even &emsp;&emsp; D. too

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词+不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：王叔叔教孩子们如何烤红薯。teach sb. how to do sth. 教某人如何做某事。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们用锡纸把每个红薯包起来。wrap…with/in… 用……包裹，with foil 常见。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：把它们埋在热灰烬下面。under hot ashes 在热灰下面，符合露营烤法。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：当他们正在等待时，叔叔解释……。while 引导的时间段内用过去进行时 were waiting。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：叔叔解释翻动红薯有助于受热均匀。explain that… 后接陈述性宾语从句，不缺成分用 that。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级 / 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：香味越来越浓。grow 作系动词后接形容词，暗含“越来越”，用 stronger（比较级表示更浓）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 被动语态 / 时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：当红薯被取出时。叙述过去，被动语态 were taken out。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：比家里烤箱烤的好吃得多。修饰比较级 better 用 much。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她请叔叔下个月再给她演示诀窍。ask sb. to do sth. 请求某人做某事。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：户外烹饪似乎比待在室内更令人兴奋。even 可修饰比较级 more exciting，加强语气。故选 C。
