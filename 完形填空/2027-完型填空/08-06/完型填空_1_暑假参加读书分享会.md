# 中考英语完形填空：暑假参加读书分享会

## 完形填空原文

Our community library organized a reading club ___1___ July. Each member was asked ___2___ a book and share three ideas. I chose a science fiction story ___3___ was translated into Chinese last year.

On the sharing day, I arrived ten minutes ___4___ than the starting time. A volunteer handed me a name card ___5___ I could sit in the front row. When my turn came, my voice shook ___6___ at the beginning.

Luckily, the listeners nodded ___7___ and smiled. After I finished, a girl asked a question ___8___ made me think deeper. "Reading is ___9___ bridge between us and the unknown world," the host said finally. I left the hall feeling ___10___ confident than when I entered.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to
2. A. introduce &emsp;&emsp; B. introducing &emsp;&emsp; C. to introduce &emsp;&emsp; D. introduced
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
4. A. early &emsp;&emsp; B. earlier &emsp;&emsp; C. earliest &emsp;&emsp; D. more early
5. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if
6. A. slight &emsp;&emsp; B. slightly &emsp;&emsp; C. more slight &emsp;&emsp; D. slightest
7. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gently &emsp;&emsp; C. gentler &emsp;&emsp; D. most gently
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. where
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
10. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词（月份）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们社区图书馆在七月组织了一个读书俱乐部。在某月用 in July。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每位成员被要求介绍一本书并分享三个观点。be asked to do sth. 被要求做某事。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了一部去年被译成中文的科幻故事。先行词 story 指物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：分享那天我比开始时间早到了十分钟。than 提示用 earlier。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位志愿者递给我姓名卡，以便我能坐在前排。so that 以便。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮到我时，起初我的声音微微发抖。修饰动词 shook，用副词 slightly。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：幸运的是听众轻轻点头并微笑。修饰动词 nodded，用 gently。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我讲完后，一个女孩问了一个让我思考得更深的问题。先行词 a question 指物，从句缺主语，用 that。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：阅读是我们与未知世界之间的一座桥梁。泛指用 a。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我离开会场时比进来时更自信了。than 提示用 more confident。故选 B。
