# 中考英语完形填空：学做冰糖葫芦

## 完形填空原文

Tanghulu looks like a small red lantern ___1___ shines in winter markets. My uncle promised ___2___ me how to make it if I finished my chores. We chose fresh hawthorns ___3___ were not too sour.

The fruits ___4___ washed and dried first. Then Uncle heated sugar and water ___5___ a shiny syrup formed. "Be careful, ___6___ the syrup is very hot," he said.

I skewered the hawthorns ___7___ slowly dipped them into the pot. When the tanghulu cooled down, it became ___8___ than I expected—sweet outside ___9___ a little sour inside. Uncle took a bite and said it tasted ___10___ good as the ones he ate in his childhood.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
2. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. teaching &emsp;&emsp; C. to teach &emsp;&emsp; D. taught
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
4. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. have been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be
5. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. though
6. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. or
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. very

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：冰糖葫芦像冬日集市里闪亮的小红灯笼。先行词 lantern 指物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：叔叔答应如果我做完家务就教我怎么做。promise to do sth. 答应做某事。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们挑选不太酸的新鲜山楂。先行词 hawthorns 指物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：水果先被洗净晾干。fruits 与 wash 为被动，叙述过去，用 were washed。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后叔叔加热糖和水直到形成亮晶晶的糖浆。until 直到……为止。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：小心，因为糖浆很烫。表原因用 because。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把山楂串起来并慢慢浸入锅中。skewered 与 dipped 并列，用 and。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：冰糖葫芦冷却后变得比我预想的更好……。than 提示用 better。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：外甜但里面略酸。表转折用 but。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定结构 as...as...
**【解析】** 本句意为：叔叔咬了一口说味道和他小时候吃的一样好。as + 形容词原级 + as 和……一样……。故选 B。
