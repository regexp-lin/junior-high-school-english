# 中考英语完形填空：青年节主题演讲

## 完形填空原文

On Youth Day, our grade held ___1___ speech competition in the school hall. The topic was “What Youth Means ___2___ Me.” I had never spoken in front of ___3___ many students before, so my hands were shaking while I waited backstage.

My English teacher patted me ___4___ the shoulder and said, “Speak slowly and make eye contact ___5___ the audience.” Her calm voice helped me take ___6___ deep breath.

When my name was called, I walked onto the stage ___7___. I started ___8___ a short story about volunteering in my community. To my surprise, the hall became quiet, and everyone listened ___9___.

After I finished, loud applause broke out. That experience taught me ___10___ courage grows when we face our fears step by step.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of
3. A. such &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
6. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
7. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. bravely &emsp;&emsp; C. braver &emsp;&emsp; D. more brave
8. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. in
9. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. more careful &emsp;&emsp; D. most careful
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：青年节那天，我们年级在学校礼堂举办了一场演讲比赛。泛指“一场”，speech 以辅音音素开头，用 a。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：主题是“青春对我意味着什么”。mean…to sb. 表示“对某人意味着……”，用 to。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前从未在这么多学生面前演讲过。so many 修饰可数名词复数 students。such 后接名词短语 such many 不成立；very/too 不直接修饰 many。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：英语老师拍了拍我的肩膀鼓励我。pat sb. on the shoulder 为固定搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：说慢一点，并与观众进行眼神交流。make eye contact with… 为固定搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她平静的声音帮我深吸了一口气。take a deep breath 为固定搭配，deep 以辅音音素开头，用 a。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 词性转换（形容词 → 副词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：当叫到我的名字时，我勇敢地走上舞台。修饰动词 walked，用副词 bravely。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以一个在社区做志愿者的短故事开头。start with 表示“以……开始”。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 词性转换（形容词 → 副词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都认真地听。修饰动词 listened，用副词 carefully。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：那次经历让我明白：当我们一步步面对恐惧时，勇气会如何成长。强调方式，用 how 引导宾语从句。故选 B。
