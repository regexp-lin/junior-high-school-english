# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工织布杯套

## 完形填空原文

During the winter craft lesson, Ms. Liu taught us ___1___ weave a simple cup sleeve on a small loom. Colorful yarn was piled on her desk like a small rainbow waiting for our hands. She handed each student ___2___ ball of thick yarn and a plastic needle. I watched her fingers move ___3___ a dance, but when I tried, my threads became messy at once. "Pull ___4___ tight enough, ___5___ not too hard," she reminded me. I took a deep breath and followed her rhythm. ___6___ ten minutes, the fabric started to look even, and I could see the shape of a sleeve. Ms. Liu praised me and said I was much ___7___ careful than before. At last, I sewed the ends together ___8___ a needle and thread. The cup sleeve fit my mug ___9___, and it kept my hands from getting hot. I sent a photo ___10___ my cousin, and she asked me to make one for her, too.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
3. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
4. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. they  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
6. A. After &emsp;&emsp; B. Before &emsp;&emsp; C. Until &emsp;&emsp; D. Since  
7. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. quite  
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
9. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. perfectly &emsp;&emsp; C. more perfect &emsp;&emsp; D. most perfect  
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”（how to weave）  
**【解析】** teach us how to weave 表示教我们如何编织；what to weave 偏重织什么，此处强调方法与步骤；why/when 不符合教学重点。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 冠词（a ball of yarn 一团线）  
**【解析】** a ball of thick yarn 为固定搭配；an 用于元音音素开头；the 表特指但此处为分发材料；零冠词不符合 a ball of 结构。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词（like 表“像……一样”）  
**【解析】** move like a dance 表示手指动作像跳舞一样流畅；as 作“像”时常接从句或特定结构，不如 like + 名词直观；for/with 语义不对。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（指代复数 threads，作 pull 的宾语）  
**【解析】** 上文提到 my threads became messy，老师提醒“把这些线拉得够紧”，threads 为复数，作宾语用 them；it 指代单数，they 为主格不能作宾语，its 是所有格。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折：够紧，但别太用力）  
**【解析】** tight enough 与 not too hard 形成转折提醒，用 but；and/or/so 不符合“既要…又要防止过度…”的语气。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词（After + 时间段：过了十分钟之后）  
**【解析】** After ten minutes 表示十分钟后出现变化；Before 与结果相反；Until/Since 不符合“十分钟之后才开始变整齐”的时间推进。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级（more careful than...）  
**【解析】** than 提示比较级，多音节形容词 careful 用 more careful；most 为最高级；very/quite 不能接 than。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词（with 表示“用……工具”）  
**【解析】** sewed...with a needle and thread 表示用针线缝合；in/by/on 不符合工具搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词（修饰动词 fit，用副词）  
**【解析】** fit my mug perfectly 中 fit 为动词，用副词 perfectly 修饰；perfect 为形容词不能直接修饰动词；more perfect/most perfect 在英语里通常避免（绝对形容词），且此处无比较结构。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词（send sth. to sb.）  
**【解析】** send a photo to my cousin 表示把照片发给堂姐/表妹；send for 表“派人去请”，at/with 不符合 send 与对象搭配。故选 B。
