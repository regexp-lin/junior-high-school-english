# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校跳绳比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school held ___1___ rope-skipping competition on the playground. Each class could send ___2___ team of ten students, and I was lucky to be chosen. ___3___ we had practiced for only two weeks, we were not very confident at first. Our PE teacher told us ___4___ mistakes were normal and encouraged us to keep the same rhythm. We practiced during lunch breaks, and the rope marks on our hands became a quiet joke between friends. On the day of the competition, my heart beat ___5___ fast that I could hear it in my ears. ___6___ the whistle blew, we jumped together and counted silently in our minds. ___7___ teammate fell at the ninth jump, but she stood up quickly and returned to the line. ___8___ we didn't break the school record, we still won ___9___ second prize. Standing on the stage, I learned ___10___ teamwork could turn nervousness into strength.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
3. A. Because &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. If &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
5. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. such  
6. A. While &emsp;&emsp; B. When &emsp;&emsp; C. Before &emsp;&emsp; D. After  
7. A. We &emsp;&emsp; B. Our &emsp;&emsp; C. Us &emsp;&emsp; D. Ours  
8. A. Because &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. If &emsp;&emsp; D. When  
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指“一场跳绳比赛”）  
**【解析】** 此处首次提到学校举办的活动，泛指一场比赛，用 a rope-skipping competition；the 多用于双方已知或独一无二的对象；an 用于元音音素开头的词前；零冠词不符合可数名词单数在此语境中的用法。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 冠词（a team of... 一支十人队伍）  
**【解析】** each class could send a team 表示每班派一支队伍，泛指用 a；the team 会暗示唯一特定队伍，不如 a 自然；零冠词不搭配 team of ten。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词（让步：尽管只练了两周）  
**【解析】** “只练两周”与“起初不自信”为让步关系，用 Although；Because 表原因会逻辑重复；If/Unless 表条件不符合。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词（tell sb. that...）  
**【解析】** told us that mistakes were normal 陈述老师的话；what/if/how 不符合直接陈述劝告内容的结构。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定结构（so...that... 如此……以至于……）  
**【解析】** so fast that I could hear... 为结果状语从句；very/too/such 不能直接与 that 构成该结构（such 后接名词）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词（when 表时间点：当哨声响起时）  
**【解析】** When the whistle blew 表示哨响那一刻开始跳；While 多接延续性动词，blow 为短暂动作更常用 when；Before/After 与“一起跳”同时开始不符。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 物主代词（our teammate 我们的队友）  
**【解析】** teammate 为名词，需用形容词性物主代词 our；We/Us 不能作定语；Ours 后不接名词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词（让步：尽管没破纪录）  
**【解析】** “没破纪录”与“仍获二等奖”为让步，用 Although；Because 表原因不当；If/When 不符合逻辑。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 冠词（序数词前用 the：the second prize）  
**【解析】** the second prize 表示二等奖，序数词前通常用 the；a second prize 会理解为“又一个奖”，不符合比赛名次表达。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句（how 表方式/程度）  
**【解析】** learned how teamwork could turn nervousness into strength 表示明白团队合作如何把紧张变成力量；what 需带名词性成分；why/when 偏离句意重点。故选 B。
