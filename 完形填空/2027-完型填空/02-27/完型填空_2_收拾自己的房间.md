# 中考英语完形填空：收拾自己的房间

## 完形填空原文

My room looked messy ___1___ books, clothes, and empty snack bags covered the desk and chair. Mom said I could invite friends over ___2___ I cleaned everything by Saturday.

I started ___3___ putting dirty clothes into a laundry basket. Then I stacked textbooks ___4___ size and put pens back into a pencil case.

Under the bed, I found a lost earphone ___5___ a birthday card from last year. I smiled ___6___ I remembered that small party.

I wiped the desk ___7___ a cloth and opened the window ___8___ fresh air could come in. The sunlight made the floor look warmer ___9___ cleaner.

When Mom checked my room, she hugged me ___10___ said, "This space finally matches your careful heart."

## 选项

1. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. if
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
8. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. as if &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的房间看起来很乱，因为书、衣服和空零食袋堆满了桌椅。because 因为。unless 除非；although 尽管；if 如果。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说如果我周六前收拾干净，就可以请朋友来玩。if 如果，引导条件状语从句。unless 除非；although 尽管；until 直到。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我先把脏衣服放进洗衣篮。start by doing sth. 以做某事开始。in 在……里；on 在……上；with 用。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我按大小把课本摞好。by size 按大小。in 在……里；on 在……上；at 在。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在床下找到一副丢失的耳机和一张去年的生日贺卡。earphone 与 card 并列，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想起那场小聚会时笑了。when 当……时。before 在……之前；until 直到；unless 除非。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用抹布擦桌子。with a cloth 用一块布。in 在……里；on 在……上；by 通过。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我打开窗户以便新鲜空气进来。so that 以便、为了。even though 尽管；as if 好像；as long as 只要。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阳光让地板看起来更温暖也更干净。warmer 与 cleaner 并列，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈检查房间后拥抱我并说话。hugged 与 said 并列，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。
