# 中考英语完形填空：第一次体育课测试

## 完形填空原文

This week, our PE teacher announced that there would be a fitness test. We ___1___ run 800 meters, do sit-ups, and jump rope. Many students looked worried ___2___ they heard the news.

I was especially afraid of the 800-meter run. During summer, I had spent most of my time ___3___ indoors and barely exercised. My friend Xiao Ming suggested that we ___4___ together after school every day to prepare.

On the day of the test, the weather was sunny and cool — ___5___ for running. When the whistle blew, I started running at a steady pace. By the third lap, my legs felt as heavy ___6___ stones. I almost wanted to stop.

Just then, I heard Xiao Ming shouting, "Come on! You ___7___ do it!" His encouragement gave me strength. I kept running and ___8___ crossed the finish line.

The result showed I finished in four minutes and twenty seconds. It was not the ___9___ score, but it was my personal best. I learned that with enough preparation, we can overcome any ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. may &emsp;&emsp; C. had to &emsp;&emsp; D. might
2. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. stay &emsp;&emsp; B. stayed &emsp;&emsp; C. to stay &emsp;&emsp; D. staying
4. A. practice &emsp;&emsp; B. practiced &emsp;&emsp; C. practicing &emsp;&emsp; D. to practice
5. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. common &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
6. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. as
7. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. must &emsp;&emsp; C. need &emsp;&emsp; D. should
8. A. final &emsp;&emsp; B. finally &emsp;&emsp; C. near &emsp;&emsp; D. nearly
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well
10. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. difficulty &emsp;&emsp; C. different &emsp;&emsp; D. difference

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 情态动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们必须跑 800 米、做仰卧起坐和跳绳。had to 表示客观上"不得不、必须"（过去时）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多同学听到消息时看起来很担心。when 引导时间状语从句，表示"当……时候"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：暑假期间我大部分时间都待在室内。spend time doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 虚拟语气（suggest 从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：小明建议我们每天放学后一起练习。suggest that sb. (should) do sth.，从句用动词原形。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：天气晴朗凉爽——非常适合跑步。perfect 表示"完美的"，符合语境。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（as ... as）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的腿感觉像石头一样重。as heavy as stones，as ... as 表示"和……一样"。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 情态动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你能做到的！can 表示"能够"，用于鼓励。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坚持跑下去，最终冲过了终点线。修饰动词 crossed，需用副词 finally。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这不是最好的成绩。the 后接最高级 best。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学到了只要准备充分，就能克服任何困难。any 后接名词，difficulty 为名词，表示"困难"。故选 B。
