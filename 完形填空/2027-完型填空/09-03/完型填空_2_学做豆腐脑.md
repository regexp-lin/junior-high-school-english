# 中考英语完形填空：学做豆腐脑

## 完形填空原文

My grandfather makes the best tofu pudding in the neighborhood. Last weekend, he finally agreed ___1___ me his secret recipe. I was so excited that I got up at five in the morning.

Grandfather had already soaked the soybeans ___2___ the night before. He showed me how to grind them into a smooth liquid ___3___ a stone mill. "This is the traditional way," he said proudly. "Machines are faster, ___4___ the taste is different."

After grinding, we filtered the soy milk through a cloth bag. Grandfather heated it in a big pot and asked me ___5___ stirring. "If it sticks to the bottom, the whole pot ___6___ be ruined," he warned.

The most magical moment was adding the coagulant. Grandfather poured it in slowly and told me ___7___ to stir at that point. Within minutes, the liquid started to thicken. It was ___8___ watching a science experiment!

When the tofu pudding was done, Grandfather topped it ___9___ brown sugar syrup. I took a spoonful — it was smooth, warm, and slightly sweet. "This tastes much ___10___ than anything from the shop!" I said. Grandfather laughed happily.

3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by

7. A. don't &emsp;&emsp; B. not &emsp;&emsp; C. no &emsp;&emsp; D. never

9. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷终于同意教我他的秘方。agree to do sth. 表示"同意做某事"。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷前一天晚上就已经把黄豆泡好了。since the night before 表示"从前一天晚上开始"，与过去完成时搭配。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他教我怎样用石磨把豆子磨成光滑的浆。with 表示"用（工具）"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：机器更快，但味道不一样。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷加热豆浆，让我一直搅拌。ask sb. to do sth. 表示"要求某人做某事"。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 情态动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果粘锅了，整锅就毁了。if 条件句中主句用一般将来时，will 表示"将会"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 不定式的否定形式
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷告诉我那时不要搅拌。tell sb. not to do sth. 表示"告诉某人不要做某事"，不定式的否定在 to 前加 not。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：就像在看一个科学实验！like 作介词表示"像"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷在上面浇上红糖水。top ... with ... 表示"在……上面加上……"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这比店里买的好吃多了！than 提示用比较级；much 修饰比较级加强语气，good 的比较级为 better。故选 C。
