# 中考英语完形填空：和朋友去游乐场

## 完形填空原文

Lucy called me last Friday and asked ___1___ I was free the next day. She wanted to go ___2___ the new amusement park that had just opened near the lake.

We met at the gate at nine. The roller coaster line was already long, ___3___ we chose a gentle ride first. While waiting, we shared an ice cream ___4___ tasted like fresh strawberries.

At noon, we watched ___5___ parade. Actors in bright costumes danced ___6___ the music, and children waved their hands happily.

Lucy dared me ___7___ try the Ferris wheel with her. From the top, the whole city looked ___8___ a colorful map. I took many photos, hoping ___9___ remember every moment.

When we said goodbye, we agreed ___10___ come again before the summer ended.

## 选项

1. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
5. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. / &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词（if 是否）
**【解析】** 本句意为：露西打电话问我第二天是否有空。asked 后接是否，用 if。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（go to）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她想去湖边新开的游乐场。go to the park 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（so 表结果）
**【解析】** 本句意为：过山车排队已经很长了，所以我们先选了温和的项目。表结果用 so。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句（which 指物）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们分享了一支尝起来像新鲜草莓的冰淇淋。先行词 ice cream 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：中午我们观看了一场游行。parade 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（dance to the music）
**【解析】** 本句意为：演员们随着音乐跳舞。dance to the music 为固定搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（dare sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：露西激我一起去坐摩天轮。dare sb. to do sth. 表示"激某人做某事"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（look like）
**【解析】** 本句意为：从高处看，整座城市像一张彩色地图。look like 表示"看起来像"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（hope to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我拍了很多照片，希望能记住每一刻。hope to do sth. 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（agree to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们约定在暑假结束前再来一次。agree to do sth. 为固定搭配。故选 A。
