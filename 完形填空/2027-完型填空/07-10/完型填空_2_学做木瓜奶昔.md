# 中考英语完形填空：学做木瓜奶昔

## 完形填空原文

My sister runs ___1___ small milk-tea shop, and she taught me ___2___ make papaya milk shake yesterday. She said ripe papaya ___3___ sweet enough, so we didn't need much sugar.

We cut the fruit into cubes and put ___4___ into the blender with cold milk. While the machine ___5___ working, the kitchen filled ___6___ a fruity smell that made me hungry.

She reminded me ___7___ add ice too early, or the shake would become watery. After blending for about forty seconds, we poured the drink ___8___ tall glasses.

The color looked like soft orange light. It tasted ___9___ than the drinks I usually buy with my pocket money. My sister laughed and said I could help ___10___ customers on busy weekends if I wanted.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
3. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be
4. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. they
5. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. not to &emsp;&emsp; B. don't &emsp;&emsp; C. not &emsp;&emsp; D. to not
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. fresh &emsp;&emsp; B. fresher &emsp;&emsp; C. freshest &emsp;&emsp; D. more fresher
10. A. serve &emsp;&emsp; B. serving &emsp;&emsp; C. to serve &emsp;&emsp; D. served

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我姐姐经营一家小奶茶店。small 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：她昨天教我如何做木瓜奶昔。teach sb. how to do 表示"教某人如何做"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说熟透的木瓜已经足够甜。叙述当时她的话，用一般过去时 was（主语 papaya 不可数，谓语单数）。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（指代 cubes）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把木瓜块放进搅拌机。指代复数 cubes 用 them。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 过去进行时
**【解析】** 本句意为：当机器正在运转时。While 强调过程，叙述过去用 was working。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（fill with）
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房充满了果香。fill with 表示"充满"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. not to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她提醒我不要过早加冰。remind sb. not to do sth. 为固定结构。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（pour…into…）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把饮品倒入高杯。pour…into… 表示"倒入"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：它比我平时用零花钱买的饮料更新鲜。than 提示用比较级 fresher。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（help to do / help (to) do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果愿意，我可以在忙碌的周末帮忙招待顾客。help (to) do sth.，选项中 to serve 正确。故选 C。
