# 中考英语完形填空：课堂上的小组讨论

## 完形填空原文

In our English class, Mrs. Wang often asks us to work in groups. Last Friday, our topic was "How to protect the environment in daily life." Each group had four members, and we had only fifteen minutes to get ready.

At the beginning, we didn't agree with each other. Tom wanted to talk ___1___ plastic bags, but Lucy preferred to discuss saving water. The air in our corner felt ___2___. I suggested writing down everyone's ideas first, ___3___ choosing the best two.

___4___ we did that, the discussion became smoother. I learned that listening is as ___5___ as speaking. When Tom shared his examples, I nodded to show I was paying attention. Lucy also ___6___ her tone and tried to understand us.

Finally, we presented our plan: reuse shopping bags, turn off the tap while brushing teeth, and ride bikes for short trips. Mrs. Wang praised us ___7___ working as a team. She said, "A good discussion is not about winning an argument, ___8___ about finding better answers together."

After class, I realized that group work is ___9___ a small society. We need respect, patience, and clear roles. I hope we can have ___10___ discussions like this more often.

## 选项

1. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. fresh &emsp;&emsp; B. heavy &emsp;&emsp; C. sweet &emsp;&emsp; D. cool
3. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. Although &emsp;&emsp; B. Until &emsp;&emsp; C. Once &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless
5. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. dangerous
6. A. raised &emsp;&emsp; B. softened &emsp;&emsp; C. lost &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot
7. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. against &emsp;&emsp; C. beyond &emsp;&emsp; D. without
10. A. fewer &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. less &emsp;&emsp; D. little

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：汤姆想谈论塑料袋。talk about 谈论某事，为固定搭配。talk with 与某人交谈。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们小组角落里的气氛变得沉重。heavy 可形容气氛压抑。fresh 清新；sweet 甜蜜；cool 凉爽。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我建议先写下每个人的想法，再挑选最好的两个。before 在……之前，表顺序。after 在……之后；if 如果；unless 除非。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一旦我们那样做了，讨论就变得更顺畅。Once 一旦，引导时间/条件。Although 尽管；Until 直到；Unless 除非。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词原级比较（as... as）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我懂得倾听和表达一样重要。as important as 和……一样重要。difficult 困难的；boring 无聊的；dangerous 危险的。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：露西也放软了语气并试着理解我们。soften one's tone 缓和语气。raise 提高；lose 失去；forget 忘记。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师表扬我们团队合作。praise sb. for (doing) sth. 因……表扬某人。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列结构（not... but...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：好的讨论不是为了争赢，而是一起找到更好的答案。not... but... 不是……而是……。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：课后我意识到小组合作就像一个小社会。like 像。against 反对；beyond 超出；without 没有。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望我们能更经常有像这样的讨论。more 与 often 构成 more often 更经常。fewer 更少（修饰可数）；less 更少（修饰不可数）；little 少。故选 B。
