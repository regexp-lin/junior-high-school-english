# 中考英语完形填空：学做三明治

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday morning, my dad showed me how to make a simple sandwich. He said it was a useful life skill, especially ___1___ Mom was away on business trips.

We cleared the table and washed our hands ___2___. Dad placed bread, lettuce, ham, and cheese on a clean plate. He told me to cut the vegetables ___3___ so they would be easy to bite.

The first sandwich I made looked messy. The bread was ___4___ and the filling fell out. Dad laughed and said, "Don't be afraid of ___5___ mistakes. Cooking is practice."

On my second try, I spread a thin layer of salad dressing ___6___ and added fewer ingredients. It looked much ___7___. When I took a bite, the taste was fresh and balanced. Dad gave me a thumbs-up.

We packed an extra sandwich for my little sister. She said it was ___8___ than the ones from the store. That made my day.

Now I understand that food connects people. A sandwich is small, ___9___ it carries love and care. I have decided to prepare breakfast for my family ___10___ weekend.

## 选项

1. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. before
2. A. first &emsp;&emsp; B. late &emsp;&emsp; C. again &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
3. A. widely &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. thinly &emsp;&emsp; D. heavily
4. A. dry &emsp;&emsp; B. wet &emsp;&emsp; C. thick &emsp;&emsp; D. broken
5. A. making &emsp;&emsp; B. breaking &emsp;&emsp; C. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; D. keeping
6. A. evenly &emsp;&emsp; B. heavily &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. freely
7. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. cheaper &emsp;&emsp; D. older
8. A. more delicious &emsp;&emsp; B. more difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. more expensive &emsp;&emsp; D. more boring
9. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
10. A. some &emsp;&emsp; B. any &emsp;&emsp; C. each &emsp;&emsp; D. every

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说这是很有用的生活技能，尤其是当妈妈外出出差时。when 当……时，引导时间状语从句。unless 除非；although 尽管；before 在……之前。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们清理桌子并先洗手。first 首先，符合做饭卫生步骤。late 晚；again 再次；slowly 慢慢地。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他告诉我把蔬菜切薄，这样更容易咬。thinly 薄薄地。widely 广泛地；loudly 大声地；heavily 重重地。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：面包碎了，馅料掉了出来。broken 破碎的。dry 干的；wet 湿的；thick 厚的。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要害怕犯错。make mistakes 犯错。break 打破；forget 忘记；keep 保持。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二次我均匀地涂了薄薄一层沙拉酱。evenly 均匀地。heavily 大量地；hardly 几乎不；freely 自由地。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：它看起来好多了。much better 好得多。worse 更差；cheaper 更便宜；older 更旧。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说它比店里的更好吃。more delicious 更美味。more difficult 更难；more expensive 更贵；more boring 更无聊。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：三明治很小，但它承载着爱与关心。前后转折，用 but。because 因为；if 如果；unless 除非。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 限定词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定每个周末为家人准备早餐。every weekend 每个周末。some 一些；any 任何；each 每个（常接可数单数名词，each weekend 也可，但 every weekend 更自然泛指）。故选 D。
