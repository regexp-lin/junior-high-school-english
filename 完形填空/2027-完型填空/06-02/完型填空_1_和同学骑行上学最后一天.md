# 中考英语完形填空：和同学骑行上学最后一天

## 完形填空原文

Today was the last day of riding to school ___1___ my best friend Tom. We have been cycling together ___2___ we were in Grade Seven.

Every morning, he waited for me at the corner of the street. No matter how bad the weather was, he ___3___ gave up. Even on rainy days, we rode side ___4___ side with our raincoats on.

This morning, the sun was shining ___5___. We rode slowly because neither of us wanted the ride ___6___ end. Tom suddenly said, "I will miss this." I nodded and felt something warm ___7___ my heart.

When we arrived at school, we parked our bikes in the ___8___ place as always. I looked at Tom and said, "Let's keep ___9___ together even after graduation." He smiled and gave me ___10___ high five.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
2. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. since &emsp;&emsp; C. after &emsp;&emsp; D. before
3. A. always &emsp;&emsp; B. never &emsp;&emsp; C. often &emsp;&emsp; D. sometimes
4. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. to
5. A. bright &emsp;&emsp; B. brightly &emsp;&emsp; C. brighter &emsp;&emsp; D. brightest
6. A. / &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
8. A. different &emsp;&emsp; B. same &emsp;&emsp; C. other &emsp;&emsp; D. another
9. A. ride &emsp;&emsp; B. to ride &emsp;&emsp; C. riding &emsp;&emsp; D. rode
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（with 表伴随）
**【解析】** 本句意为：今天是我和好朋友 Tom 最后一天一起骑车上学。with 表示"和……一起"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（since 自从）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们从七年级就开始一起骑车了。since 表示"自从"，常与现在完成时连用。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 频率副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：无论天气多糟糕，他从不放弃。根据语境"no matter how bad"，后文应表示"从不"放弃，用 never。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（side by side 并肩）
**【解析】** 本句意为：即使在雨天，我们也穿着雨衣并肩骑行。"side by side"为固定搭配，表示"并肩"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：今天早上，太阳明亮地照耀着。修饰动词 shining 用副词 brightly。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（want sth. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们骑得很慢，因为我们都不想让这次骑行结束。"want...to do"结构。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（in one's heart）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我点点头，心里感到一阵温暖。"in one's heart"为固定搭配，表示"在心里"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（the same...as）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把自行车停在和平时一样的地方。"the same...as..."表示"和……一样的"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（keep doing sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：让我们毕业后继续一起骑车。"keep doing sth."表示"继续做某事"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他微笑着和我击了个掌。"give sb. a high five"为固定表达，a 表泛指。故选 A。
