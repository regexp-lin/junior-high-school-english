# 中考英语完形填空：学做南瓜饼

## 完形填空原文

My grandma is famous ___1___ her pumpkin pies in our family. This time, she let me watch ___2___ she mixed the filling.

She steamed the pumpkin until it was soft enough ___3___. Then she added sugar, a little salt, and some milk ___4___ the bowl. The kitchen ___5___ wonderful while she was cooking.

When the filling cooled down, we put it ___6___ thin pastry circles and folded them carefully. Grandma reminded me ___7___ the edges tightly, or the pies would open in the pan.

The pies ___8___ in the oven for twenty minutes. They tasted ___9___ than any pumpkin pies I had bought from stores. I learned that food ___10___ with patience always smells sweeter.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. where
3. A. mash &emsp;&emsp; B. mashing &emsp;&emsp; C. to mash &emsp;&emsp; D. mashed
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
5. A. smell &emsp;&emsp; B. smells &emsp;&emsp; C. smelled &emsp;&emsp; D. was smelling
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. seal &emsp;&emsp; B. sealing &emsp;&emsp; C. to seal &emsp;&emsp; D. sealed
8. A. bake &emsp;&emsp; B. baked &emsp;&emsp; C. were baked &emsp;&emsp; D. are baking
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
10. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. preparing &emsp;&emsp; C. prepared &emsp;&emsp; D. prepares

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be famous for）
**【解析】** 本句意为：在我们家，外婆因她的南瓜饼而出名。"be famous for" 表示"因……而著名"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词（how）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次，她让我看她如何搅拌馅料。watch 后接宾语从句，表示方式用 how。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（形容词 + enough + to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把南瓜蒸到足够软以便捣碎。"adj. + enough + to do" 用不定式。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（add...to...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后她往碗里加入糖、少许盐和牛奶。"add...to..." 表示"把……加入……"。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态（一般过去时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她做饭时，厨房里闻起来很香。叙述过去，主语 kitchen 视为整体，用 smell 的过去式 smelled。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（into 表进入内部）
**【解析】** 本句意为：馅料冷却后，我们把它放进薄面皮圆片里。put...into... 表示"把……放进……里"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：外婆提醒我要把边捏紧。"remind sb. to do sth." 用不定式。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 被动语态与一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：饼在烤箱里烤了二十分钟。pies 与 bake 为被动关系，叙述过去用 were baked。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们比我在店里买的任何南瓜饼都好吃。tasted 为系动词，后接形容词，than 提示用比较级 better。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（过去分词作定语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了，用心准备的食物闻起来总是更香。food prepared with patience 中 prepared 作后置定语，表被动完成。故选 C。
