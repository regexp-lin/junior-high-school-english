# 中考英语完形填空：和朋友一起排话剧

## 完形填空原文

Our English club decided to put on a play for the school's English Culture Week. Five of us formed a team and began ___1___ immediately.

We chose the story "The Gift of the Magi" by O. Henry because it was touching and not too long. Our club advisor helped us adapt it ___2___ a short script. I was given the role of the narrator, which I was happy about because I didn't have to ___3___ too many lines.

Rehearsals started the ___4___ week. At first, nobody could remember their lines, and we kept laughing in the middle of serious scenes. Our advisor was patient but strict. She said, "If you want the audience to take you ___5___, you must first take yourselves seriously."

Her words ___6___ us. From that day on, we practiced much harder. Liu Wei, who played the husband, even practiced his lines while ___7___. I helped paint the background scenery using old cardboard boxes.

On the performance day, the classroom was turned into a small theater. When the lights went down and I spoke the first line, ___8___ in the room became quiet. I could feel the audience's attention.

The play went ___9___ without any big mistakes. When the last scene ended, there was a moment of silence before the audience burst into applause. Our advisor wiped a tear and said, "I'm so proud of every ___10___ of you."

## 选项

1. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. prepared &emsp;&emsp; C. preparing &emsp;&emsp; D. to prepare  
2. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. memorize &emsp;&emsp; B. memorized &emsp;&emsp; C. memorizing &emsp;&emsp; D. memorizes  
4. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. follows &emsp;&emsp; C. following &emsp;&emsp; D. followed  
5. A. serious &emsp;&emsp; B. seriously &emsp;&emsp; C. more serious &emsp;&emsp; D. most serious  
6. A. wake up &emsp;&emsp; B. woke up &emsp;&emsp; C. woken up &emsp;&emsp; D. waking up  
7. A. eat &emsp;&emsp; B. ate &emsp;&emsp; C. eating &emsp;&emsp; D. to eat  
8. A. nobody &emsp;&emsp; B. somebody &emsp;&emsp; C. everybody &emsp;&emsp; D. anybody  
9. A. smooth &emsp;&emsp; B. smoothly &emsp;&emsp; C. smoother &emsp;&emsp; D. smoothest  
10. A. one &emsp;&emsp; B. ones &emsp;&emsp; C. first &emsp;&emsp; D. two  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（begin doing）  
**【解析】** began preparing 意为"开始准备"，begin 后接动名词。prepare 为原形；prepared 为过去式；to prepare 虽可但此处强调立即开始的动作，用 doing 更自然。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 adapt...into  
**【解析】** adapt it into a short script 意为"把它改编成短剧本"，into 表示变化。from 表来源；with 表伴随；by 表方式。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** have to + 动词原形  
**【解析】** didn't have to memorize 意为"不必记忆"，have to 后接动词原形。memorized 为过去式；memorizing 为现在分词；memorizes 为三单。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词 following 表"接下来的"  
**【解析】** the following week 意为"接下来的一周"，following 作形容词。follow 为动词；follows 为三单；followed 为过去分词"被跟随的"不合此义。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 take sb. seriously  
**【解析】** take you seriously 意为"认真对待你们"，seriously 为副词修饰动词 take。serious 为形容词；more/most serious 为比较级/最高级形容词。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（动词短语）  
**【解析】** woke up 意为"唤醒了"，全文为过去叙述。wake up 为原形；woken up 为过去分词；waking up 为现在分词。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** while + 动名词  
**【解析】** while eating 为省略了主语和 be 动词的时间状语从句。eat 为原形；ate 为过去式不能跟 while；to eat 为不定式。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 everybody  
**【解析】** everybody in the room became quiet 意为"房间里每个人都安静了"，everybody 用于肯定句表"每个人"。nobody 意为"没有人"；somebody 意为"某人"；anybody 用于否定/疑问句。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** went smoothly 意为"进行顺利"，修饰动词 went 用副词。smooth 为形容词；smoother/smoothest 为比较级/最高级形容词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** every one of  
**【解析】** every one of you 意为"你们中的每一个人"，one 为单数代词。ones 为复数不与 every 搭配；first/two 不符合语义。故选 A。
