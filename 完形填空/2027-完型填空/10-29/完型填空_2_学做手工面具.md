# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工面具

## 完形填空原文

Halloween was approaching, and our English teacher decided to hold a mask-making activity. She said it was a good way ___1___ about Western culture while having fun.

She prepared white plastic masks, acrylic paint, brushes, feathers, and glitter. Each student could design ___2___ own mask in any style they liked. Some chose scary themes, while others preferred beautiful or funny ones.

I decided ___3___ a phoenix mask because the phoenix is a symbol of power and rebirth in many cultures. I first painted the base with bright red and gold colors. ___4___ I waited for the paint to dry, I drew my design plan on a piece of paper.

After the base dried, I carefully painted feather patterns around the eyes. My hands were a little ___5___, so some lines were not very straight. But the teacher said the slight imperfections actually made the mask look ___6___ natural and artistic.

The most fun part was adding real feathers and glitter. I glued orange and red feathers along the top edge and ___7___ golden glitter around the eye area. The mask sparkled under the classroom lights.

When everyone was ___8___, we held a small fashion show. Each student put on their mask and walked in front of the class. My phoenix mask ___9___ a lot of attention. One classmate said it was the ___10___ creative one in the whole class.

## 选项

1. A. learn &emsp;&emsp; B. learning &emsp;&emsp; C. to learn &emsp;&emsp; D. learned  
2. A. his &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. their  
3. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
4. A. While &emsp;&emsp; B. Until &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. Although  
5. A. shake &emsp;&emsp; B. shook &emsp;&emsp; C. shaky &emsp;&emsp; D. shakily  
6. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
7. A. spread &emsp;&emsp; B. spreads &emsp;&emsp; C. spreading &emsp;&emsp; D. to spread  
8. A. do &emsp;&emsp; B. did &emsp;&emsp; C. done &emsp;&emsp; D. doing  
9. A. attract &emsp;&emsp; B. attracts &emsp;&emsp; C. attracted &emsp;&emsp; D. attracting  
10. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. most  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 不定式作后置定语  
**【解析】** a good way to learn 意为"一种学习的好方法"，to learn 为不定式作后置定语修饰 way。learn 缺 to；learning 为动名词；learned 为过去式。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** Each student could design their own mask，student 为泛指（不确定性别），用 their。his/her 只指代单一性别；its 指物。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decided to make 意为"决定制作"，decide 后接不定式。make 缺 to；making 为动名词；made 为过去式。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词 while  
**【解析】** "在等油漆干的同时画设计图"，while 表两个动作同时进行。until 表"直到"；unless 表"除非"；although 表"虽然"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** were a little shaky 意为"有点发抖"，shaky 为形容词作表语。shake 为动词；shook 为过去式；shakily 为副词。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** look more natural and artistic 意为"看起来更自然更有艺术感"，比较级暗含与"完美"相比更自然。much 修饰比较级但不作比较级标志；most 为最高级；very 修饰原级。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（并列谓语）  
**【解析】** and 连接 glued 和 spread，时态一致用过去式。spread 过去式与原形同形。spreads 为三单；spreading 为现在分词；to spread 为不定式。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语  
**【解析】** everyone was done 意为"每个人都完成了"，done 为形容词（完成的）作表语。do 为原形；did 为过去式；doing 为现在分词。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** attracted a lot of attention 意为"吸引了很多注意力"，全文为过去叙述用过去式。attract 为原形；attracts 为三单现在时；attracting 为现在分词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** the most creative one 意为"最有创意的一个"，most creative 为最高级。more 为比较级；much 修饰比较级；very 修饰原级。故选 D。
