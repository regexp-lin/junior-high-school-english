# 中考英语完形填空：开学典礼

## 完形填空原文

September 1st is a special day for students in China because it marks the ___1___ of a new school year. Our school held a grand opening ceremony on the playground that morning.

All the students stood in neat rows. The national flag was ___2___ slowly while everyone sang the national anthem. I felt very proud ___3___ that moment. After that, the principal gave a speech. He spoke about the ___4___ of setting goals and working hard.

A student from the senior grade was invited ___5___ her experience. She said that she used to be ___6___ at math, but she never gave up. Through constant practice, she ___7___ improved her grades. Her story inspired all of us greatly.

The ceremony lasted about one hour. Before it ended, each class received a new flag ___8___ the school's motto printed on it. Our head teacher told us to hang it in the classroom ___9___ we could see it every day.

Walking back to the classroom, I ___10___ excited about the new term. I believe this year will be full of challenges and achievements.

## 选项

1. A. begin &emsp;&emsp; B. begins &emsp;&emsp; C. beginning &emsp;&emsp; D. began
2. A. raise &emsp;&emsp; B. raising &emsp;&emsp; C. raised &emsp;&emsp; D. rises
3. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. for
4. A. important &emsp;&emsp; B. importance &emsp;&emsp; C. impossible &emsp;&emsp; D. impolite
5. A. share &emsp;&emsp; B. shared &emsp;&emsp; C. sharing &emsp;&emsp; D. to share
6. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. weak &emsp;&emsp; D. strong
7. A. gradual &emsp;&emsp; B. gradually &emsp;&emsp; C. sudden &emsp;&emsp; D. suddenly
8. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
9. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. even though &emsp;&emsp; D. as soon as
10. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. feels &emsp;&emsp; C. feeling &emsp;&emsp; D. felt

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为它标志着新学年的开始。the 后接名词，beginning 为名词，表示"开始"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：国旗被缓缓升起。flag 与 raise 构成被动关系（国旗被升起），用 was raised。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在那一刻我感到非常自豪。at that moment 表示"在那一刻"，为固定搭配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：他讲了设定目标和努力学习的重要性。the 后接名词，importance 为名词，表示"重要性"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位高年级学生被邀请分享她的经验。be invited to do sth. 表示"被邀请做某事"。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她以前数学很差。be weak at 表示"在某方面薄弱"。由后文"从不放弃""提高成绩"可知原来较差。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：通过不断练习，她逐渐提高了成绩。修饰动词 improved，需用副词 gradually，表示"逐渐地"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词（with 复合结构）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个班收到了一面印有校训的新旗帜。with ... printed on it 为 with 复合结构，表示"上面印着……"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任让我们把它挂在教室里，以便我们每天都能看到。so that 引导目的状语从句。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：走回教室的路上，我对新学期感到兴奋。叙述过去的感受，用一般过去时 felt。故选 D。
