# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工风车

## 完形填空原文

On the first day of the new term, our art teacher gave us a fun task — making paper pinwheels. She said it was a good way to ___1___ the new school year.

She handed out square pieces of colored paper and showed us how ___2___ them step by step. First, we folded the paper along the two diagonals. Then we cut from each corner ___3___ the center, but stopped about two centimeters away.

The ___4___ part was folding every other point to the middle. I had to be very patient ___5___ the paper was thin and tore easily. My friend Wang Lei finished his pinwheel ___6___ than anyone else in the class. It looked really beautiful.

After folding, we pushed a pin ___7___ the center and attached it to a small stick. When I held the pinwheel outside the window, it started spinning ___8___ in the autumn breeze. All the students laughed and cheered.

Our teacher took a photo of us ___9___ our colorful pinwheels. She said, "Just like these pinwheels, I hope you will spin with ___10___ this year." It was a perfect start to the new term.

## 选项

1. A. celebrate &emsp;&emsp; B. celebrated &emsp;&emsp; C. celebrating &emsp;&emsp; D. celebration
2. A. fold &emsp;&emsp; B. folded &emsp;&emsp; C. folding &emsp;&emsp; D. to fold
3. A. toward &emsp;&emsp; B. away &emsp;&emsp; C. between &emsp;&emsp; D. around
4. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. hard &emsp;&emsp; D. hardest
5. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
6. A. fast &emsp;&emsp; B. faster &emsp;&emsp; C. fastest &emsp;&emsp; D. the fastest
7. A. across &emsp;&emsp; B. over &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. along
8. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quicker &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. quickest
9. A. hold &emsp;&emsp; B. held &emsp;&emsp; C. to hold &emsp;&emsp; D. holding
10. A. energy &emsp;&emsp; B. energies &emsp;&emsp; C. energetic &emsp;&emsp; D. energetically

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式中的动词原形）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说这是庆祝新学年的好方法。a way to do sth.，to 后接动词原形 celebrate。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 特殊疑问词 + 不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：她给我们展示了如何一步步折叠。how to do sth. 为"疑问词 + 不定式"结构。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后从每个角向中心剪开。toward 表示"朝向"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：困难的部分是把每隔一个角折到中间。由后文"需要非常耐心"可知这一步有难度，用 hard。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我必须非常耐心，因为纸很薄容易撕破。because 引导原因状语从句。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的朋友王磊比班里其他任何人都做得快。than 提示用比较级，fast 的比较级为 faster。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把图钉穿过中心。through 表示"穿过"（从一端到另一端穿透）。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：风车在秋风中快速旋转起来。修饰动词 spinning，需用副词 quickly。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（现在分词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师给我们拿着彩色风车拍了张照。holding 为现在分词作宾语补足语，表示伴随状态。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望你们今年也充满活力地旋转。with 后接名词，energy 为不可数名词，表示"活力"。故选 A。
