# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工圣诞花环

## 完形填空原文

Our art room smelled ___1___ pine after school because we were making Christmas wreaths. Ms. Hu placed a wire ring, ribbons, and bells ___2___ the table and showed us a finished sample.

First, we wrapped green paper strips around the ring ___3___ it looked full. I used glue carefully ___4___ the paper would not fall off easily. ___5___ my hands were cold, I still enjoyed shaping the “leaves”.

Next, we added small red bows. Tom wanted ___6___ many bells as possible, but Ms. Hu said too many bells might make the wreath heavy. She suggested ___7___ three bells on one side only.

When we hung the wreaths on the classroom door, everyone cheered. I felt proud ___8___ my wreath was not perfect but made with patience. Ms. Hu took photos ___9___ we could share them with our parents. Before the bell rang, she told us ___10___ tidy up the scraps on the floor.

## 选项

1. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
4. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. as if &emsp;&emsp; C. even though &emsp;&emsp; D. before  
5. A. Because &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. If  
6. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. such &emsp;&emsp; C. as &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
7. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. putting &emsp;&emsp; C. to put &emsp;&emsp; D. puts  
8. A. whether &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
9. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until  
10. A. clean &emsp;&emsp; B. cleaning &emsp;&emsp; C. to clean &emsp;&emsp; D. cleaned  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *smell of*  
**【解析】** *smelled of pine* 表示“有松木的气味”。*smell like* 也可表“闻起来像”，但选项中 *of* 与 *smell of* 为常见考点搭配。*as/with* 不用于此结构。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *on the table*  
**【解析】** 物品摆放在桌面，用 *on the table*。*in* 在……里；*at* 多接地点；*by* 在旁，不如 *on* 准确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 *until*  
**【解析】** *wrapped... until it looked full* 表示一直包到看起来饱满。*if/because/unless* 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 *so that*  
**【解析】** 小心涂胶的目的是“让纸不容易掉”，*so that the paper would not fall off*。*as if* 好像；*even though* 让步；*before* 在……之前。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 从属连词表让步  
**【解析】** “手冷”与“仍享受制作”为让步关系，用 *Although*。*Because* 表原因；*Unless/If* 表条件。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定结构 *as many...as possible*  
**【解析】** *as many bells as possible* 为固定表达“尽可能多的铃铛”。*so many* 后不接 *as possible* 的这种结构需 *as...as possible*；*such* 修饰名词短语结构不同；*very* 不适用。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*suggest doing / suggest (that)...*）  
**【解析】** 中考常考 *suggest doing* “建议做某事”，*suggested putting three bells*。*suggest to do* 非主流搭配（英式常用 *suggest doing*）；*put/puts* 不能直接作宾语。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 *that*  
**【解析】** *felt proud that my wreath was not perfect...*，*that* 引导宾语从句说明原因内容。*whether/if* 表是否；*what* 需在从句中充当成分。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 *so that*  
**【解析】** 拍照的目的是“以便和父母分享”，*so that we could share*。*unless/although/until* 语义不符。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*tell sb. to do*）  
**【解析】** *told us to tidy up* 为固定结构“告诉某人做某事”。*clean/cleaning/cleaned* 不能直接作 *tell us* 的补足语。故选 C。
