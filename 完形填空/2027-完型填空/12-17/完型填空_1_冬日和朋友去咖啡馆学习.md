# 中考英语完形填空：冬日和朋友去咖啡馆学习

## 完形填空原文

On a freezing Saturday morning, Anna texted me ___1___ meet her at a small café near the library. I put on my scarf and gloves ___2___ I would not catch a cold on the way.

The café smelled ___3___ fresh bread and cocoa. We chose a table by the window ___4___ we could see thin snow on the branches. Because the music was soft, we could focus ___5___ our English reading without shouting.

Anna brought flashcards, ___6___ I prepared grammar notes. Whenever I felt sleepy, I ordered a cup of hot lemon tea ___7___ myself awake. The owner smiled and said we were ___8___ polite group of students she had met that week.

Two hours passed quickly. We finished a whole unit and planned ___9___ back next weekend. Walking home in the cold wind, I thought studying with a friend was ___10___ boring than studying alone in a silent room.

## 选项

1. A. meet &emsp;&emsp; B. meeting &emsp;&emsp; C. to meet &emsp;&emsp; D. met  
2. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even if &emsp;&emsp; C. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
3. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about  
4. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. who  
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
6. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. to keep &emsp;&emsp; C. keeping &emsp;&emsp; D. kept  
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
9. A. come &emsp;&emsp; B. coming &emsp;&emsp; C. to come &emsp;&emsp; D. came  
10. A. little &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. least &emsp;&emsp; D. fewer  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*text sb. to do*）  
**【解析】** *texted me to meet her* 表示“发短信让我去咖啡馆见她”，用不定式 *to meet* 作宾补。*meet/meeting/met* 不能直接跟在 *text me* 后作补足语（需 *to*）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 *so that*  
**【解析】** 戴围巾手套的目的是“以免在路上感冒”，*so that I would not catch a cold* 表目的。*even if* 让步；*as soon as* 时间；*although* 让步，语义不符。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *smell of*  
**【解析】** *smelled of fresh bread* 表示“闻起来有……的气味”。*smell like* 也可表“像……”，但 *smell of* 更常见于“带有某种气味”；选项中 *of* 与教材常见搭配一致。*with/about* 不搭配 *smell* 表气味来源。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系副词（地点）  
**【解析】** 先行词 *table*，从句 *we could see thin snow* 表“在那张桌子所在的位置能看到雪”，用 *where*。*when* 表时间；*which/who* 关系代词，从句结构不缺少主宾时用副词更合适。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *focus on*  
**【解析】** *focus on our English reading* 为固定短语。*focus in/at/to* 非标准搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *while* 表对比  
**【解析】** “Anna带了单词卡，而我准备了语法笔记”，并列对比用 *while*。*but* 表转折语气过强；*or* 选择；*so* 结果，不符。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）  
**【解析】** *ordered tea to keep myself awake*，不定式 *to keep* 表目的。*keep/keeping/kept* 不能直接说明点单的目的。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词与可数名词单数（*a group of*）  
**【解析】** *we were a polite group of students* 表示“我们是一群有礼貌的学生”，泛指“其中一群”，用 *a*。*an* 用于元音音素开头；*the* 需明确特指双方已知对象，此处首次评价更像泛指；零冠词不能直接与单数可数名词 *group* 搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*plan to do*）  
**【解析】** *planned to come back next weekend* 为“计划做某事”，用不定式 *to come*。*come/coming/came* 不能直接作 *planned* 的宾语。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（*than* 提示）  
**【解析】** *less boring than studying alone* 表示“不如独自学习那么无聊”，*than* 提示比较级，用 *less* 修饰形容词 *boring*。*little/least* 不与 *than* 搭配；*fewer* 修饰可数名词。故选 B。
