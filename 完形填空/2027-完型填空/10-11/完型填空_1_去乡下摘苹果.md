# 中考英语完形填空：去乡下摘苹果

## 完形填空原文

Last autumn, my parents took me to a farm in the countryside to pick apples. It was a bright and cool day, ___1___ for outdoor activities.

The farm owner, a friendly old man, welcomed us warmly. He gave ___2___ a big basket and told us which trees had the ripest apples. I was surprised to see ___3___ many apple trees in one place. The red apples hanging on the branches looked like little lanterns.

I reached for the nearest apple and pulled it gently. It came off the branch ___4___. "Good job!" my father said. He taught me to twist the apple slightly ___5___ pulling, which made it easier to pick without damaging the stem.

After about an hour, our basket was almost full. My mother picked the most apples among ___6___ three of us. She smiled and said it was because she grew up in the countryside and ___7___ used to farm work.

Before leaving, the farm owner invited us ___8___ some fresh apple juice. It tasted much ___9___ than any juice I had bought from the store. I told him it was the most delicious juice I had ___10___ tasted. He laughed and said, "That's because nature gives us the best flavor."

## 选项

1. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. perfectly &emsp;&emsp; C. more perfect &emsp;&emsp; D. most perfect  
2. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. our &emsp;&emsp; C. us &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
3. A. such &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. quite  
4. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. easily &emsp;&emsp; D. easiest  
5. A. until &emsp;&emsp; B. during &emsp;&emsp; C. before &emsp;&emsp; D. after  
6. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
7. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. are &emsp;&emsp; D. were  
8. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. tried &emsp;&emsp; C. trying &emsp;&emsp; D. to try  
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
10. A. never &emsp;&emsp; B. already &emsp;&emsp; C. ever &emsp;&emsp; D. just  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作后置定语/补语  
**【解析】** perfect 作形容词修饰 day 的状态，意为"非常适合户外活动的（日子）"，此处为形容词作补充说明。perfectly 为副词；perfect 本身已表绝对概念，不常用 more/most 形式。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** gave 为及物动词，后接间接宾语用宾格 us（give sb. sth.）。we 为主格；our 为形容词性物主代词；ours 为名词性物主代词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** so many + 可数名词复数  
**【解析】** so many apple trees 意为"如此多的苹果树"。so + many/much/few/little + 名词为固定结构。such 后接 a/an + adj. + 单数名词或直接接复数名词（such many 不成立）；very/quite 不与 many 构成此感叹结构。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词短语 came off 需用副词 easily（轻松地）。easy 为形容词；easier 为比较级形容词；easiest 为最高级形容词，均不能修饰动词。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 before 表时间先后  
**【解析】** "拉之前先轻轻拧一下"，before pulling 意为"在拉之前"。until 意为"直到"；during 意为"在……期间"接名词；after 意为"在……之后"，均不符合先拧后拉的顺序。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定冠词 the  
**【解析】** among the three of us 意为"在我们三人中"，the 特指"我们这三个人"。a/an 为不定冠词不与数词搭配；零冠词不能用于特指的三人。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时与 be used to  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，she was used to farm work 意为"她过去习惯农活"。is 为现在时；are/were 主谓不一致（主语为 she）。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（invite sb. to do）  
**【解析】** invite sb. to do sth. 意为"邀请某人做某事"。try 为原形；tried 为过去式；trying 为现在分词，均不能用于 invite sb. 后。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 句中有 than，需用比较级。taste 为系动词后接形容词，good 的比较级为 better。good 为原级；well 作形容词时多表健康；best 为最高级。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词 ever 用于最高级句型  
**【解析】** "the most delicious juice I had ever tasted" 为最高级句型中常见搭配，ever 意为"曾经"。never（从不）与句意矛盾；already（已经）不用于此结构；just（刚刚）不符合语义。故选 C。
