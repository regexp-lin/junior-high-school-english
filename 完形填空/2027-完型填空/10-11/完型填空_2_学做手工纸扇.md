# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸扇

## 完形填空原文

During our school's art week, the teacher brought some bamboo sticks and rice paper. She planned to teach us ___1___ traditional Chinese paper fans.

She first explained the history of paper fans. She said they ___2___ in China more than two thousand years ago and were once symbols of elegance. We were all amazed by this ___3___ of knowledge.

The teacher demonstrated each step ___4___. She cut the rice paper into a semi-circle, then glued it onto the bamboo frame. After that, she drew a plum blossom on the fan ___5___ a fine brush.

When it was our turn, I found ___6___ quite challenging to keep the paper smooth. My paper kept wrinkling, and I had to start over twice. The girl sitting next to me was ___7___ at art, so her fan looked beautiful from the very beginning.

I didn't feel upset. Instead, I asked her ___8___ she could give me some tips. She kindly showed me how to apply glue evenly. With her help, my fan finally turned out ___9___.

At the end of class, we displayed all our fans on the wall. The teacher said she was happy to see that everyone ___10___ their best. She believed that patience and practice were more important than talent.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. to make  
2. A. invent &emsp;&emsp; B. invented &emsp;&emsp; C. are invented &emsp;&emsp; D. were invented  
3. A. piece &emsp;&emsp; B. pair &emsp;&emsp; C. set &emsp;&emsp; D. group  
4. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. quick &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly  
5. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
6. A. this &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. it &emsp;&emsp; D. one  
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
8. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
9. A. nice &emsp;&emsp; B. nicely &emsp;&emsp; C. nicer &emsp;&emsp; D. nicest  
10. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. tried &emsp;&emsp; C. has tried &emsp;&emsp; D. had tried  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（teach sb. to do）  
**【解析】** teach sb. to do sth. 意为"教某人做某事"，不定式作宾补。make 为原形；made 为过去式；making 为现在分词，均不能用于 teach sb. 后。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 被动语态（一般过去时）  
**【解析】** 纸扇"被发明"，主语 they 与 invent 为被动关系，且时间为两千多年前，用一般过去时被动语态 were invented。invent 为主动原形；invented 为主动过去式；are invented 时态不对。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 a piece of knowledge  
**【解析】** a piece of knowledge 意为"一条知识"，piece 为量词搭配不可数名词 knowledge。pair（双/对）搭配成对物品；set（套）搭配成套物品；group（组/群）搭配人或物，均不与 knowledge 搭配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 demonstrated 需用副词。slowly（慢慢地）符合老师耐心演示的语境。slow 为形容词；quick/quickly 意为"快速地"，与"演示步骤"时的耐心氛围不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表工具  
**【解析】** with a fine brush 意为"用一支细毛笔"，with 表示使用的工具。of 表所属；by 表方式但后多接动名词或抽象名词；in 表在……里。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** it 作形式宾语  
**【解析】** find it + adj. + to do 为固定句型，it 作形式宾语，不定式为真正宾语。this/that 不作形式宾语；one 表泛指某一个，不用于此结构。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be good at  
**【解析】** be good at art 意为"擅长美术"。well 作形容词表"身体好"，不与 at 搭配；better/best 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较对象或范围。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连词（是否）  
**【解析】** "我问她是否能给我一些建议"，表"是否"用 if 引导宾语从句。that 引导陈述句不表疑问；what 意为"什么"；how 意为"怎样"，均不表"是否"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 系动词 turn out + 形容词  
**【解析】** turn out 意为"结果是"，后接形容词作表语，nice（漂亮的）合适。nicely 为副词；nicer 为比较级但无比较对象；nicest 为最高级但无范围限定。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** 主句 said 为过去时，宾语从句中描述的"尽力"发生在"说"之前（展示作品后总结），用过去完成时 had tried。try 为原形；tried 为一般过去时（也可接受但 had tried 强调先发生）；has tried 为现在完成时。故选 D。
