# 中考英语完形填空：社区志愿者打扫公园

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, our community organized a volunteer activity to clean up the nearby park. My mom signed both of us up. I was not very ___1___ at first because I wanted to sleep in, but Mom said it would be a meaningful experience.

When we arrived at the park at eight in the morning, about thirty volunteers were already there. The organizer gave each of us a pair of gloves and a large plastic ___2___. He divided us into small groups and assigned different areas. My group was responsible for the area around the lake.

I was shocked to see how much ___3___ there was. There were empty bottles, food wrappers, and even old shoes lying on the grass. It was hard to believe that people could be so careless about the ___4___. We picked up trash piece by piece and put it into the bags.

After two hours of hard work, the park looked completely ___5___. The grass was green and clean, and the lake reflected the blue sky like a mirror. I felt a great sense of achievement. Mom smiled and said, "See? A small ___6___ can make a big difference."

During the break, I talked with an elderly volunteer named Mr. Chen. He told me that he came to clean the park every ___7___. "I've been doing this for ten years," he said proudly. "This park is like my second home." His ___8___ deeply moved me.

On the way back, I thought about what I could do to help protect the environment. I decided to carry a small bag with me and pick up any litter I saw on the ___9___. It might seem like a tiny thing, but if everyone does it, our city will become much ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. willing &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. able &emsp;&emsp; D. lucky
2. A. cup &emsp;&emsp; B. box &emsp;&emsp; C. bag &emsp;&emsp; D. bowl
3. A. water &emsp;&emsp; B. trash &emsp;&emsp; C. food &emsp;&emsp; D. grass
4. A. weather &emsp;&emsp; B. traffic &emsp;&emsp; C. environment &emsp;&emsp; D. education
5. A. empty &emsp;&emsp; B. dark &emsp;&emsp; C. noisy &emsp;&emsp; D. different
6. A. action &emsp;&emsp; B. mistake &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. accident
7. A. year &emsp;&emsp; B. month &emsp;&emsp; C. weekend &emsp;&emsp; D. holiday
8. A. wealth &emsp;&emsp; B. humor &emsp;&emsp; C. dedication &emsp;&emsp; D. talent
9. A. road &emsp;&emsp; B. roof &emsp;&emsp; C. wall &emsp;&emsp; D. desk
10. A. bigger &emsp;&emsp; B. busier &emsp;&emsp; C. quieter &emsp;&emsp; D. cleaner

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始我不太愿意，因为想睡懒觉。A 项意为"愿意的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"能够的"；D 项意为"幸运的"。not willing 表示不太情愿。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：组织者给每人一副手套和一个大塑料袋。A 项意为"杯子"；B 项意为"盒子"；C 项意为"袋子"；D 项意为"碗"。捡垃圾用 plastic bag。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我震惊于有这么多垃圾。A 项意为"水"；B 项意为"垃圾"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"草"。后文列举空瓶、食品包装等，是 trash。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很难相信人们对环境如此不在意。A 项意为"天气"；B 项意为"交通"；C 项意为"环境"；D 项意为"教育"。乱扔垃圾是不爱护 environment。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两小时的辛苦工作后，公园看起来完全不同了。A 项意为"空的"；B 项意为"黑暗的"；C 项意为"吵闹的"；D 项意为"不同的"。清理后变得 different。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小举动也能带来大变化。A 项意为"行动"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"事故"。a small action can make a big difference。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他每个周末都来清理公园。A 项意为"年"；B 项意为"月"；C 项意为"周末"；D 项意为"假期"。活动在周日进行，every weekend 最合理。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的奉献精神深深打动了我。A 项意为"财富"；B 项意为"幽默"；C 项意为"奉献"；D 项意为"才华"。坚持十年打扫体现 dedication。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：捡起路上看到的任何垃圾。A 项意为"路"；B 项意为"屋顶"；C 项意为"墙"；D 项意为"桌子"。日常在 road 上捡垃圾。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果每个人都这样做，城市会变得更干净。A 项意为"更大"；B 项意为"更忙"；C 项意为"更安静"；D 项意为"更干净"。文章主题是清洁环境，cleaner 最合适。故选 D。
