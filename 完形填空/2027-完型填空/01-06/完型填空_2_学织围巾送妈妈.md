# 中考英语完形填空：学织围巾送妈妈

## 完形填空原文

My mother's birthday was coming in January, and I wanted to give her something ___1___. After thinking for a long time, I decided to knit a scarf for her by hand. I had never knitted before, but I thought it would be the most meaningful gift.

I went to a craft store and bought some soft pink yarn and a pair of knitting needles. The shopkeeper showed me a ___2___ video on knitting basics. It looked easy in the video, but when I tried it at home, I realized it was much harder than I ___3___.

At first, my stitches were uneven—some were too tight and some were too ___4___. The scarf looked more like a fishing net. I almost gave up, but then I thought about how Mom always encouraged me to keep trying. So I pulled out the messy rows and started ___5___.

I practiced every evening after finishing my homework. Slowly, my ___6___ improved. The stitches became neat and even, and the scarf began to look like a real one. My fingers often hurt from holding the needles for too long, but I did not ___7___.

After two weeks, I finally finished the scarf. It was not perfect—there were still a few small ___8___ here and there. But I wrapped it in a nice box and tied a ribbon on top. On Mom's birthday morning, I handed her the gift with a big smile.

When Mom opened the box and saw the scarf, her eyes filled with ___9___. She put it around her neck and hugged me tightly. "This is the best gift I have ever received," she whispered. Seeing her so happy made all the hard work completely ___10___. I learned that the value of a gift is not in its price, but in the love behind it.

## 选项

1. A. cheap &emsp;&emsp; B. common &emsp;&emsp; C. special &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
2. A. funny &emsp;&emsp; B. short &emsp;&emsp; C. scary &emsp;&emsp; D. sad
3. A. expected &emsp;&emsp; B. remembered &emsp;&emsp; C. promised &emsp;&emsp; D. noticed
4. A. small &emsp;&emsp; B. loose &emsp;&emsp; C. clean &emsp;&emsp; D. dark
5. A. again &emsp;&emsp; B. outside &emsp;&emsp; C. away &emsp;&emsp; D. late
6. A. grades &emsp;&emsp; B. health &emsp;&emsp; C. skills &emsp;&emsp; D. mood
7. A. agree &emsp;&emsp; B. move &emsp;&emsp; C. mind &emsp;&emsp; D. speak
8. A. holes &emsp;&emsp; B. flowers &emsp;&emsp; C. buttons &emsp;&emsp; D. pockets
9. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. anger &emsp;&emsp; C. tears &emsp;&emsp; D. doubt
10. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. worth it &emsp;&emsp; C. wasted &emsp;&emsp; D. forgotten

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想送她一些特别的东西。A 项意为"便宜的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"简单的"。给妈妈的礼物要 special。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：店员给我看了一个关于编织基础的短视频。A 项意为"有趣的"；B 项意为"短的"；C 项意为"可怕的"；D 项意为"伤心的"。店里快速演示用 short video。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比我预想的难得多。A 项意为"预想"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"承诺"；D 项意为"注意到"。harder than expected 意为"比预期更难"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些太紧有些太松。A 项意为"小的"；B 项意为"松的"；C 项意为"干净的"；D 项意为"暗的"。tight 和 loose 形成对比。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我拆掉乱七八糟的行然后重新开始。A 项意为"重新"；B 项意为"外面"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"迟"。started again 意为"重新开始"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：慢慢地，我的技术提高了。A 项意为"成绩"；B 项意为"健康"；C 项意为"技能"；D 项意为"心情"。编织的 skills improved。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手指经常疼，但我不在意。A 项意为"同意"；B 项意为"移动"；C 项意为"介意"；D 项意为"说话"。did not mind 表示不在乎。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：还有一些小漏洞。A 项意为"洞/漏洞"；B 项意为"花"；C 项意为"纽扣"；D 项意为"口袋"。编织不完美会有小 holes。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈眼里充满了泪水。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"泪水"；D 项意为"怀疑"。感动时 eyes filled with tears。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到她这么开心让所有辛苦都值得了。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"值得的"；C 项意为"浪费的"；D 项意为"被遗忘的"。worth it 表示一切都值得。故选 B。
