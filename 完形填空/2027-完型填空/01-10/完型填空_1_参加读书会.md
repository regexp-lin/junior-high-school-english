# 中考英语完形填空：参加读书会

## 完形填空原文

After school on Fridays, our English teacher runs a small reading club in the classroom. Last month, I almost gave it ___1___ because I was busy with math homework and felt too tired to read extra pages.

Then my desk mate said the club was a quiet place to slow ___2___ and enjoy stories. I decided to give it one more ___3___. That Friday, we read a short story about a boy who ___4___ his fear of speaking in public.

The teacher asked us to share one sentence we liked ___5___. I stayed silent at first, but when the room became ___6___, I read my favorite line in a low voice. Everyone listened ___7___ and nodded with smiles.

After the meeting, the teacher told me that reading is not a race. Those words stayed ___8___ me like a warm light. Now I go to the club ___9___ a week, and I even joined a city reading event last weekend. Books have become my ___10___ friends who never judge me.

## 选项

1. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. away &emsp;&emsp; C. out &emsp;&emsp; D. back
2. A. down &emsp;&emsp; B. off &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. through
3. A. chance &emsp;&emsp; B. prize &emsp;&emsp; C. excuse &emsp;&emsp; D. joke
4. A. forgot &emsp;&emsp; B. overcame &emsp;&emsp; C. kept &emsp;&emsp; D. followed
5. A. aloud &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. alone &emsp;&emsp; D. online
6. A. noisy &emsp;&emsp; B. bright &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. empty
7. A. patiently &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. secretly
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
9. A. once &emsp;&emsp; B. never &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. almost
10. A. noisy &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. lazy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：上个月我差点放弃参加读书会。give up 为固定搭配，意为"放弃"。give away 赠送；give out 分发；give back 归还。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：同桌说读书会是个能让人慢下来、享受故事的地方。slow down 为固定搭配，意为"放慢速度、冷静下来"。slow off 非固定搭配；slow over/through 不用于此语境。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定再给它一次机会。A 项意为"机会"；B 项意为"奖品"；C 项意为"借口"；D 项意为"玩笑"。give it one more chance 表示"再试一次"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们读了一个关于男孩克服在公众场合发言恐惧的短篇故事。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"克服"；C 项意为"保持"；D 项意为"跟随"。fear 与 overcome 搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们大声分享自己喜欢的一句话。A 项 aloud 强调"出声地"；B 项 quickly 快地；C 项 alone 独自；D 项 online 在线。read/share aloud 表示朗读、出声分享。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我保持沉默，但当教室安静下来时，我用很低的声音读了我最喜欢的那一句。A 项 noisy 吵闹；B 项 bright 明亮；C 项 quiet 安静；D 项 empty 空。敢于开口需要安静氛围。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都耐心地听着，并微笑着点头。A 项意为"耐心地"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"秘密地"。鼓励性场景用 patiently。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：那些话像温暖的光一样一直留在我心里。stay with sb. 可表示（话语、记忆）留在某人心中。stay for 为……停留；stay against/beyond 不符合搭配习惯。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我每周去一次读书会。once a week 表示频率"每周一次"。never/hardly/almost 与语境不符。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：书籍成了从不评判我的安静朋友。A 项 noisy 吵闹；B 项 quiet 安静的；C 项 strange 奇怪的；D 项 lazy 懒惰的。与书的特点及 never judge 呼应，quiet friends 为常见表达。故选 B。
