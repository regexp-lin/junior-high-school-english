# 中考英语完形填空：帮邻居照看小孩

## 完形填空原文

Mrs. Wang lives next door ___1___ us. Last Saturday she had ___2___ important meeting, so she asked ___3___ I could look after her five-year-old son Leo for two hours. I agreed at once because Leo is polite and easy ___4___.

Leo brought his toy cars and picture books. We built a small “city” on the floor ___5___ used blocks. When he felt thirsty, I gave him warm water ___6___ of cold juice because his mom asked me to do so.

Later Leo wanted ___7___ cartoons, but I said we should keep the volume low. He listened carefully ___8___ he was young. Before Mrs. Wang returned, we tidied the toys together. She thanked me ___9___ a small box of cookies and said I was ___10___ helpful neighbor.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
3. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
4. A. to get on with &emsp;&emsp; B. get on with &emsp;&emsp; C. getting on with &emsp;&emsp; D. gets on with  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
6. A. instead &emsp;&emsp; B. instead of &emsp;&emsp; C. rather &emsp;&emsp; D. without  
7. A. watch &emsp;&emsp; B. watching &emsp;&emsp; C. to watch &emsp;&emsp; D. watched  
8. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
10. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. really &emsp;&emsp; C. more real &emsp;&emsp; D. most real  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 *next door to*  
**【解析】** *lives next door to us* 为固定表达“住在我们隔壁”。*for/of/with* 不构成此搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 *an*  
**【解析】** *important* 以元音音素开头，用 *an important meeting*。*a* 用于辅音音素开头；此处非特指。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 *whether*  
**【解析】** *asked whether I could look after...* 表示“问我是否能照看……”，表“是否”用 *whether*（*if* 也可表是否，但选项给 *whether* 更典型）。*that* 无“是否”含义；*what/how* 语义不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词后接不定式（*easy to do*）  
**【解析】** *easy to get on with* “容易相处”，*easy* 后常用不定式主动形式表被动意义。*get on with* 原形或 *-ing* 不能直接跟在 *easy* 后作补足语。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *and*  
**【解析】** “搭城市”与“用积木”为并列顺承，用 *and*。*but/or/so* 不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定短语 *instead of*  
**【解析】** *warm water instead of cold juice* “温水而不是冰果汁”。*instead* 为副词，不能直接接名词短语；*rather* 需 *rather than*；*without* 语义不对。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*want to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *wanted to watch cartoons* 固定 *want to do*。*watch/watching/watched* 不能直接跟在 *wanted* 后。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 让步状语从句（*although*）  
**【解析】** “他认真听（把音量调低）”与“年纪小”可理解为让步：虽然年纪小，仍然认真听。*although* 合适。*because* 若选会变成“因为小所以认真听”，不如让步自然；*if/unless* 条件。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *with* 表伴随/工具  
**【解析】** *thanked me with a small box of cookies* “用一小盒饼干感谢我”。*thank sb. with sth.* 表方式。*in/on/for* 搭配不如 *with* 自然。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰形容词  
**【解析】** *really helpful* 用副词 *really* 修饰形容词 *helpful*。*real* 为形容词；*more real/most real* 与 *helpful* 搭配不当且语义不对。故选 B。
