# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工棉花糖

## 完形填空原文

Our science teacher showed us ___1___ homemade cotton candy looks like clouds made of sugar. We wore gloves and stood ___2___ from the hot machine, listening to safety rules carefully.

First, we poured sugar into the small bowl ___3___ the teacher checked the heat. The machine spun quickly, ___4___ thin sugar threads appeared in the air. I moved a paper cone slowly ___5___ the threads could wrap around it evenly.

The candy tasted sweet, ___6___ it was lighter than I expected. My classmate Anna laughed ___7___ her candy stuck to her hair by accident. Before the class ended, the teacher let us ___8___ one photo with our cones.

She said science is not only about books, ___9___ also about careful observation. I wrote down the steps ___10___ I would not forget them at home.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
2. A. far &emsp;&emsp; B. long &emsp;&emsp; C. high &emsp;&emsp; D. wide  
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. after  
4. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
5. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. as if &emsp;&emsp; C. even though &emsp;&emsp; D. before  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
7. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
8. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. taking &emsp;&emsp; C. to take &emsp;&emsp; D. took  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
10. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. because  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 *how*  
**【解析】** *showed us how homemade cotton candy looks...* 中 *how* 表示方式，意为“手工棉花糖看起来怎样像糖做的云”。*what* 需在从句中作主语/宾语等；*why/when* 与语境不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词 *far* 与 *far from*  
**【解析】** *stood far from the hot machine* 表示与高温机器保持安全距离。*far from* 表“离……远”。*long/high/wide* 不用于此搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*after*）  
**【解析】** *poured sugar into the small bowl after the teacher checked the heat* 表示“在老师检查好温度之后再倒糖”，符合安全操作顺序。*if* 条件；*until* 与“先倒糖再等检查”顺序矛盾；*when* 可表“当……时”，但此处强调先后顺序 *after* 更准确。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *and*  
**【解析】** 机器快速旋转，并且糖丝出现，为顺承并列，用 *and*。*but/or/so* 语义不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句（*so that*）  
**【解析】** 慢慢移动纸筒的目的是让糖丝均匀缠绕，*so that the threads could wrap*。*as if* 好像；*even though* 让步；*before* 在……之前。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “很甜”与“比预想的更轻”形成轻微转折，用 *but*。*and/so/or* 不符。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*when*）  
**【解析】** *laughed when her candy stuck to her hair* “当糖粘到头发上时她笑了”。*if* 条件；*until* 直到；*unless* 除非。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 使役动词 *let* 后接动词原形  
**【解析】** *let us take one photo* 为 *let sb. do sth.*，用动词原形 *take*。*taking/to take/took* 不符合 *let* 的用法。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词固定结构 *not only... but also...*  
**【解析】** *not only about books, but also about careful observation* 为固定搭配“不仅……而且……”。*and/or/so* 不能构成 *not only... also* 结构。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句（*so that*）  
**【解析】** 写下步骤是为了“以便回家不忘”，*so that I would not forget*。*unless/although/because* 语义不符。故选 A。
