# 中考英语完形填空：试穿新校服

## 完形填空原文

The day before the opening ceremony, our class ___1___ to try on new school uniforms. The jackets were made ___2___ soft cotton, and the trousers felt comfortable ___3___ we walked around the classroom.

Lily’s size was ___4___ small, so she had to change ___5___ a larger one at the office. Tom complained that the sleeves were ___6___ long, ___7___ the teacher measured his arms patiently and suggested tailoring.

Standing in front of the mirror, I thought the uniform made everyone look ___8___ than usual, yet still like ___9___ team. “Wear it proudly,” the teacher said, “because it shows ___10___ you belong to this school.”

## 选项

1. A. invite &emsp;&emsp; B. invited &emsp;&emsp; C. was invited &emsp;&emsp; D. has invited
2. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. little &emsp;&emsp; B. a little &emsp;&emsp; C. few &emsp;&emsp; D. a few
5. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. by
6. A. much too &emsp;&emsp; B. too much &emsp;&emsp; C. very much &emsp;&emsp; D. so many
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. serious &emsp;&emsp; B. more serious &emsp;&emsp; C. most serious &emsp;&emsp; D. the most serious
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. where

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 被动语态与时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：开学典礼前一天，我们班被安排试穿新校服。class 与 invite 为被动关系；叙述过去事件，用一般过去时的被动 was invited。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：外套由柔软的棉制成。能看出原材料，常用（be）made of。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：……我们在教室里走动时，裤子感觉很舒服。while 引导时间状语从句，表示“当……时候”。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：莉莉的尺码有点小…… small 为形容词，用 a little 修饰表示“有点儿”。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：……她不得不到办公室换一件更大的。change into 表示“换成、改成”。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：汤姆抱怨袖子太长。修饰形容词 long，用 much too 表示“实在太……”。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：……但老师耐心地量了他的手臂并建议修改。前后转折，用 but。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得校服让大家看起来比平时更严肃…… than 提示用比较级 more serious。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：……但仍像一个团队。team 可数，此处泛指“一个团队”，用 a。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：……因为它表明你属于这所学校。show 后接宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 that 引导。故选 B。
