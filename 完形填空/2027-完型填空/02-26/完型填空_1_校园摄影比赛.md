# 中考英语完形填空：校园摄影比赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school announced a photo competition ___1___ the theme "Light and Shadow on Campus". Students could use phones ___2___ cameras, but editing software was not allowed.

I walked around after class ___3___ interesting corners: sunlight on the library windows, raindrops on bike seats, and friends laughing ___4___ the basketball court.

On the last day, I still felt unsure ___5___ my best shot. My art teacher suggested I choose the picture ___6___ showed both people and trees clearly.

I sent the file ___7___ the school email address before midnight. The next week, my photo won third prize. I was surprised ___8___ happy because I had never won an art-related award.

The judge said my work looked honest ___9___ full of daily warmth. I learned that beauty is often close ___10___ us if we pay attention.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. find &emsp;&emsp; B. finding &emsp;&emsp; C. to find &emsp;&emsp; D. found
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
5. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. to
6. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校宣布举办以“校园光影”为主题的摄影比赛。with the theme... 以……为主题。in 在……里；on 在……上；at 在。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生可以使用手机或相机。or 表选择。and 并列；but 但是；so 所以。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：下课后我在校园里走动，寻找有趣的角落。用不定式表目的 to find。find 原形；finding 现在分词；found 过去式。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：朋友们在篮球场上欢笑。on the basketball court 在球场上。in 在……里；at 在；to 向。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后一天我仍对自己最满意的一张照片没把握。be unsure about... 对……不确定。of 常接名词表所属；for 为了；to 向。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：美术老师建议我选那张人与树都清晰的照片。先行词 picture 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。who 指人；what 不引导定语从句；whose 表所属。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在午夜前把文件发到学校邮箱地址。send...to... 把……发送到……。in 在……里；on 在……上；at 在。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我又惊又喜，因为我从未得过与美术相关的奖。surprised 与 happy 为转折兼递进，常用 but 连接 surprised but happy。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：评委说我的作品看起来真诚且充满日常温度。honest 与 full 并列，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：美常常离我们很近，只要我们留心。close to sb. 离某人很近。in/on/at 不与 close 构成此义。故选 D。
