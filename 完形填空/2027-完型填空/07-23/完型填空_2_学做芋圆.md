# 中考英语完形填空：学做芋圆

## 完形填空原文

Jason's aunt taught him ___1___ make taro balls, ___2___ popular dessert in Taiwan. They peeled the taro, steamed it, ___3___ mashed it into a smooth paste.

Then they added sweet potato starch ___4___ the paste could be shaped ___5___ small balls. Jason rolled them ___6___ his hands carefully.

The balls ___7___ in boiling water until they floated. Jason found them chewier ___8___ he had imagined. He decided ___9___ some for his friends the next day. Cooking taught him ___10___ patience is important.

## 选项

1. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
3. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. cooked &emsp;&emsp; C. were cooked &emsp;&emsp; D. are cooking
8. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. that
9. A. bring &emsp;&emsp; B. to bring &emsp;&emsp; C. bringing &emsp;&emsp; D. brought
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. how

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 疑问词+不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：杰森的阿姨教他如何做芋圆。teach sb. how to do sth. 教某人如何做某事。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：一种在台湾很受欢迎的甜品。popular 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们削皮、蒸熟，再捣成泥。三个动作顺承并列，用 and。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词 / 目的状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后加入红薯淀粉，以便面团能被搓成小球。so that 引导目的状语从句，表示“以便”。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：被塑形成小球。shape…into… 把……塑造成……。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：杰森用手小心地搓圆。with one's hands 用手。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：圆子在沸水中被煮到浮起。balls 与 cook 为被动关系，叙述过去用 were cooked。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：他发现它们比自己想象的更有嚼劲。chewier 为比较级，后用 than。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他决定第二天带一些给朋友。decide to do sth. 决定做某事。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句 / 连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：烹饪让他明白耐心很重要。taught him that… 后接陈述意义的宾语从句，不缺成分用 that。故选 B。
