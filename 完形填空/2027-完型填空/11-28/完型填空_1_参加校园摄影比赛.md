# 中考英语完形填空：参加校园摄影比赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school ___1___ a photography competition with the theme “Light and Shadow” last week. I love taking pictures, ___2___ I decided to join it at once.

I spent two afternoons ___3___ around the playground and the library. I wanted ___4___ photo that could tell a warm story about friendship. Finally, I chose a picture of two classmates ___5___ books under a tree.

Before handing it in, I asked my English teacher ___6___ she thought of the title. She suggested ___7___ a short sentence under the photo to explain the idea. Her advice was ___8___ helpful that I changed a few words and felt much better about my work.

___9___ the result came out, I won the third prize. It was not the highest award, ___10___ it meant a lot to me because it was my first competition.

## 选项

1. A. holds &emsp;&emsp; B. held &emsp;&emsp; C. has held &emsp;&emsp; D. was holding  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. though  
3. A. walk &emsp;&emsp; B. walking &emsp;&emsp; C. to walk &emsp;&emsp; D. walked  
4. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
5. A. read &emsp;&emsp; B. reads &emsp;&emsp; C. reading &emsp;&emsp; D. to read  
6. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. where  
7. A. add &emsp;&emsp; B. adding &emsp;&emsp; C. to add &emsp;&emsp; D. added  
8. A. such &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too  
9. A. When &emsp;&emsp; B. While &emsp;&emsp; C. Since &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. if  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 句末 last week 为明确的过去时间状语，谓语用一般过去时 held。现在完成时与单纯 last week 搭配不如一般过去自然；过去进行时常表背景。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表结果  
**【解析】** “喜欢拍照”与“立刻决定参加”为因果关系，用 so。but 表转折；or 表选择；though 表让步。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** spend … (in) doing sth.  
**【解析】** spend two afternoons walking 为固定结构。spend … to walk 错误；spend … walk 不成立。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词表泛指  
**【解析】** 此处表示“我想要一张能讲述友谊故事的相片”，首次泛指且 photo 以辅音音素开头，用 a photo。the 表双方已知的特定事物，语境尚未选定作品；an 用于元音音素开头。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作后置定语  
**【解析】** two classmates reading books 表示正在读书的同学，现在分词表主动进行。read/reads 谓语形式不能作定语；to read 表将来/目的，不符照片场景。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定句型 What do you think of …? 的宾语从句形式  
**【解析】** ask … what she thought of the title，what 作 think of 的宾语。how she thought of 搭配错误；which/where 与 think of 的对象不符。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** suggest 后接动名词（suggest doing）  
**【解析】** suggest 表示“建议做某事”时，后接动名词作宾语，即 suggest adding…。suggest to do 为常见错误搭配；suggest 后接宾语从句时常用 suggest (that) sb. (should) do，但本题选项为动词形式，故选 adding。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** so…that… 引导结果状语从句  
**【解析】** 形容词 helpful 前用 so 构成 so…that…“如此……以至于……”。such 后接名词短语 such helpful advice；very/too 不能与 that 构成该结果结构。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** when 引导时间状语从句  
**【解析】** When the result came out 表示“当结果公布时”，when 可接短暂性动词。came out 为短暂动作，while 常接延续性动词；since/unless 语义不符。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 not… but…  
**【解析】** “不是最高奖，但它对我意义重大”，表转折用 but。and 表并列；because 表原因；if 表条件。故选 B。
