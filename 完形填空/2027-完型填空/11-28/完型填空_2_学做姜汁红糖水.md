# 中考英语完形填空：学做姜汁红糖水

## 完形填空原文

When my sister caught a cold, my grandma taught me ___1___ a simple drink — ginger brown sugar water. She said it ___2___ by many families in winter for hundreds of years.

First, we washed fresh ginger and cut it ___3___ thin pieces. Grandma told me ___4___ the fire should be low when we boiled the water. ___5___ the sweet smell filled the kitchen, she added brown sugar and stirred slowly.

The drink tasted a little spicy, ___6___ it warmed my throat at once. My sister drank a small cup and said she ___7___ better soon. I wrote the steps in my notebook ___8___ I would not forget them.

That evening, I understood ___9___ love can be shown in such an ordinary way. I also learned that following instructions carefully is ___10___ important than rushing in the kitchen.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. uses &emsp;&emsp; B. used &emsp;&emsp; C. has been used &emsp;&emsp; D. is used  
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
5. A. As soon as &emsp;&emsp; B. Even though &emsp;&emsp; C. As long as &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. will feel &emsp;&emsp; B. feels &emsp;&emsp; C. would feel &emsp;&emsp; D. has felt  
8. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. quite &emsp;&emsp; D. too  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** teach sb. to do sth.  
**【解析】** teach me to make 为不定式作宾补。teach doing 非标准搭配；teach make 缺少 to。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 现在完成时的被动语态  
**【解析】** 时间状语 for hundreds of years 表示从过去持续到现在的很长一段时间，常用现在完成时；it 与 use 为被动关系，故用 has been used。一般现在时 is used 虽可表习惯，但与 for hundreds of years 搭配时，现在完成时更贴切；used/uses 不能单独构成被动。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** cut … into … 固定搭配  
**【解析】** cut it into thin pieces 表示“切成薄片”。cut in 表插话/参与；cut on 不搭配；cut with 表用某物切，不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 that  
**【解析】** told me that the fire should be low 为陈述性宾语从句。what/if/how 与从句结构不匹配（从句不缺成分且非是否/方式疑问）。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** as soon as 引导时间状语从句  
**【解析】** As soon as the sweet smell filled… 表示“香味一弥漫就……”。Even though 表让步；As long as 表条件；Unless 表否定条件。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “有点辣”与“立刻暖到喉咙”为转折，用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表结果。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去将来时  
**【解析】** 主句 said 为过去时，从句“很快就会好些”表从过去看将来，用 would feel。will feel 与过去主句时态不一致；feels 现在时；has felt 现在完成时不符。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** so that 引导目的状语从句  
**【解析】** wrote the steps… so that I would not forget 表示“以便不忘”。in order to 后接不定式，不接从句；because/although 语义不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接副词 how  
**【解析】** understood how love can be shown 表示“理解爱如何以平凡方式表达”。what 需在从句中作名词性成分；why/when 与“方式”不符。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than）  
**【解析】** more important than rushing 与 than 搭配用比较级。very/quite/too 不能直接接 than。故选 B。
