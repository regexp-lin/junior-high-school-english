# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校辩论赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school held an English debate ___1___ the topic of “Phones in Class”. I was nervous ___2___ I had never spoken in front of so many students before. The hall was bright, and microphones stood on the stage like thin silver trees.

My partner Lily and I practiced ___3___ school for a week. She helped me organize ___4___ ideas and use polite language. We recorded short videos to check our speed and eye contact. On the match day, when our names ___5___ called, my heart beat fast.

During the free-debate part, I answered a question ___6___ than I expected. The judges smiled, ___7___ I knew we did well. In the end, ___8___ team won second place. We hugged each other and took a photo with our certificates.

This experience taught me ___9___ courage is built step by step. I am thankful ___10___ my teachers and teammates who trusted me. On the way home, I kept thinking about how one clear sentence can change a whole room.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
3. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. during &emsp;&emsp; D. between  
4. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
5. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. have been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be  
6. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. clearer &emsp;&emsp; C. clearly &emsp;&emsp; D. more clearly  
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
8. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 on 与 topic 搭配  
**【解析】** on the topic of... 为固定搭配，表示“关于……主题”。in/at/with 与 topic 搭配不自然。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句连词 because  
**【解析】** “我很紧张”是因为“从未在这么多人前发言”，表原因用 because。if/unless 表条件；although 表让步。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 after 表时间  
**【解析】** practice after school 表示“放学后练习”，符合学生场景。before/during/between 与语境不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** ideas 为名词，需用 my ideas。I/me 不能作定语；mine 后不接名词。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** names 与 call 为被动关系，且 on the match day 为过去时间，用 were called。are 现在时；have been/will be 时态不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级  
**【解析】** answered 为动词，需用副词修饰；than 提示比较级，用 more clearly。clear 为形容词；clearer 修饰名词；clearly 为原级副词，不接 than。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 结果连词 so  
**【解析】** “评委微笑”与“我知道我们表现不错”为因果顺承的结果，用 so。and 仅并列；or/but 逻辑不符。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词与名词搭配  
**【解析】** team 为名词，前用 our team。we/us 为人称代词主格/宾格；ours 后不接名词。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 that  
**【解析】** taught me that courage is... 中 that 引导宾语从句陈述观点。what 需在从句中作成分；which 表选择；how 表方式，均不如 that 贴切。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be thankful to sb.  
**【解析】** be thankful to sb. 表示“感谢某人”。thankful for 后多接事物；of/with 不构成常见搭配。故选 B。
