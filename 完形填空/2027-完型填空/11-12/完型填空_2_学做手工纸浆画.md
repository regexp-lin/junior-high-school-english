# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸浆画

## 完形填空原文

In our labor class, we recycled old newspapers ___1___ colorful pulp paintings. First, we tore the paper into tiny pieces and soaked ___2___ in warm water. The classroom sounded like soft rain as everyone ripped pages gently, and our desks slowly turned gray with tiny paper dots.

When the mixture became soft, we had to ___3___ it with glue. That step was sticky, ___4___ nobody complained. Our teacher explained ___5___ we should press the pulp flat on a board. We used old rulers to keep the edges neat.

After the surface dried, I painted a small bird ___6___ blue and yellow. The work was not as smooth ___7___ the teacher's example, ___8___ I still liked it. My friends said the bird looked ready to fly.

Looking at ___9___ painting on the wall, I realized waste paper could become ___10___ useful if we treat it with care. Our teacher pinned it next to the window so the morning light could touch the bird's wings gently.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. they  
3. A. mix &emsp;&emsp; B. mixing &emsp;&emsp; C. to mix &emsp;&emsp; D. mixed  
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. why  
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
7. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
10. A. something &emsp;&emsp; B. anything &emsp;&emsp; C. nothing &emsp;&emsp; D. everything  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）  
**【解析】** recycle... to make... 用不定式表目的“为了制作纸浆画”。make/making/made 不能直接表目的状语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格与数的一致  
**【解析】** 指代复数 pieces，作 soaked 的宾语，用 them。it/its 表单数；they 为主格。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词后接动词原形  
**【解析】** had to 后接动词原形 mix。mixing/to mix/mixed 不符合 had to do 结构。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “步骤很黏”与“没人抱怨”为轻微转折，用 but。and 顺承弱化对比；or/so 不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 how  
**【解析】** explain how we should press... 表示解释“应该如何按压”，how 表方式。what 缺宾语成分不符；whether 表是否；why 表原因，此处强调做法。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 in 表颜色  
**【解析】** paint... in blue and yellow 表示“用蓝黄配色（去画）”，in + 颜色为常见表达。of/with/by 搭配不如 in 自然。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原级比较结构 as... as...  
**【解析】** not as smooth as... 表示“不如……光滑”，第二个 as 不可少。like/than/to 不构成该结构。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “不如老师范例光滑”与“我仍然喜欢”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** painting 为名词，用 my painting。I/me 不能作定语；mine 后不接名词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 something  
**【解析】** 肯定句中表示“某种有用的东西”，用 something useful。anything 多用于疑问/否定；nothing 表否定；everything 语义过重。故选 A。
