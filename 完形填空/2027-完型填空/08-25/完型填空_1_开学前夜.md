# 中考英语完形填空：开学前夜

## 完形填空原文

The night before school started, I could not fall ___1___ easily. My mind was full of questions ___2___ I lay in bed staring at the ceiling. Would the new teachers be strict? Could I still keep up ___3___ my classmates?

I got up and opened the window ___4___ fresh air came in quietly. Downstairs, my parents were packing fruit ___5___ snacks for my lunch box. Their low voices sounded warm, ___6___ I felt less worried.

I took out my timetable and read it ___7___ again. Each subject seemed challenging, ___8___ I told myself that worry would not solve anything. “Tomorrow is ___9___ new page,” I whispered. After drinking a glass of milk, I returned to bed and finally fell asleep, dreaming ___10___ a sunny classroom.

## 选项

1. A. sleep &emsp;&emsp; B. asleep &emsp;&emsp; C. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; D. sleepy
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
10. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. about &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：开学前夜，我很难入睡。fall asleep 为固定搭配，表示“入睡”。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我躺在床上盯着天花板时，脑子里满是问题。while 引导时间状语从句，表示“当……时候”。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还能跟上班里同学吗？keep up with 表示“跟上、不落后”。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我打开窗户，以便新鲜空气悄悄进来。so that 引导目的状语从句，表示“以便”。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母在楼下为我的饭盒装水果和零食。fruit 与 snacks 为并列，用 and。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们低沉的声音听起来很温暖，所以我没那么焦虑了。表因果关系，用 so。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我拿出课程表又认真地读了一遍。修饰动词 read，用副词 carefully。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每门课似乎都有挑战，但我告诉自己担心解决不了任何问题。转折用 but。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词与固定表达
**【解析】** 本句意为：明天是崭新的一页。a new page 表示“新的一页”，new 以辅音音素开头，用 a。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：……最后睡着了，梦见一间洒满阳光的教室。dream about/of 表示“梦见、梦想”。故选 B。
