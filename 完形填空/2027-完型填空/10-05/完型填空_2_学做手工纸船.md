# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸船

## 完形填空原文

Our art teacher, Mr. Lin, told us that folding paper boats was not only fun ___1___ could also improve our patience. He brought colorful paper to class and showed us ___2___ to fold step by step.

At first, my boat looked uneven, ___3___ made me feel a little embarrassed. Mr. Lin walked over, smiled, and said, “Practice makes perfect. Try ___4___ more time.” His words sounded simple, ___5___ they gave me courage.

I watched the video he shared ___6___ and copied each movement slowly. Half an hour later, the paper in my hands turned ___7___ a neat little boat. My deskmate cheered, “Wow, it looks even ___8___ than the sample on the screen!”

When the bell rang, we still didn’t want ___9___ away our paper boats. Mr. Lin encouraged us ___10___ our works in the classroom display corner. That afternoon, I realized that small handmade projects could bring big happiness.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
4. A. one &emsp;&emsp; B. first &emsp;&emsp; C. once &emsp;&emsp; D. the first  
5. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
6. A. careless &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. careful  
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. into  
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
9. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. putting &emsp;&emsp; C. to put &emsp;&emsp; D. puts  
10. A. post &emsp;&emsp; B. posting &emsp;&emsp; C. to post &emsp;&emsp; D. posted  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（not only... but (also)...）  
**【解析】** not only... but (also)... 为固定并列结构，意为“不仅……而且……”。also 可省略，第二分句前用 but。单纯用 and 不能体现该套语的规范搭配；or 表选择；so 表结果，均错误。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** show sb. how to fold 表示“演示如何折叠”，how to do 作 showed 的直接宾语。what to fold 偏重“折什么”；why/when 与“一步步演示动作”的语境不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句关系词  
**【解析】** 先行词为前面整句话“我的船看起来不平整”这一情况，从句缺主语，用 which 引导非限制性定语从句。what 不能引导定语从句；who/whom 指人。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 数词与固定表达  
**【解析】** “one more time” 意为“再一次”，为常见口语表达。first more time 不成立；once more 虽可表“再一次”，但空格后有 more time，结构重复；the first more time 语法不通。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折）  
**【解析】** “话很简单”与“给了我勇气”为轻微转折，用 but。because 引导原因从句，与后句不构成因果；if/unless 表条件，不符。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 watched 须用副词，排除形容词 careless 与 careful。carelessly（粗心地）与后文“一步步模仿”矛盾；carefully（认真地）符合学习折纸的语境。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 turn into  
**【解析】** turn into 意为“变成”，强调形态或性质的变化，纸折成小船用 turned into 最贴切。turn in 上交；turn on 打开；turn at 非典型搭配。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 句中有 than，需用比较级；looks 为系动词，后接形容词，good 的比较级为 better。well 作形容词时多表“身体好”；good 为原级；best 为最高级。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（want to do）  
**【解析】** want 后接不定式 want to do sth.，want to put away 表示“不想把纸船收起来”。put/puts 不能直接跟在 want 后；putting 不用于 want 后表“想要”。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（encourage sb. to do）  
**【解析】** encourage sb. to do sth. 意为“鼓励某人做某事”，不定式作宾补。post 为原形；posting 不能单独作 encourage 的宾补；posted 为过去式。故选 C。
