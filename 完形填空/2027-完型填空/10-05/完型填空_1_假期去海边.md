# 中考英语完形填空：假期去海边

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, my parents promised ___1___ a trip to the seaside if I finished my summer homework on time. I worked hard every morning, and two weeks later, we finally set ___2___ on a sunny Friday.

The beach was more beautiful than I had ___3___. The waves rolled gently, and children were building sand castles ___4___ their parents. I ran toward the water at once, but my mother stopped me and reminded me ___5___ a life jacket first.

In the afternoon, we joined a beach clean-up activity. Many volunteers picked up plastic bottles and paper bags ___6___ they could protect marine animals. I felt tired, ___7___ I was proud because I did something useful for the environment.

Before leaving, my father bought me a small shell necklace ___8___ a souvenir. On the way home, I looked out of the window and thought this holiday would be one of ___9___ unforgettable memories in my junior high years. I thanked my parents ___10___ giving me such a meaningful experience.

## 选项

1. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
2. A. out &emsp;&emsp; B. off &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. down  
3. A. imagine &emsp;&emsp; B. imagined &emsp;&emsp; C. imagining &emsp;&emsp; D. to imagine  
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
5. A. wear &emsp;&emsp; B. wearing &emsp;&emsp; C. to wear &emsp;&emsp; D. wore  
6. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as  
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but  
8. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. as &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
9. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. the most &emsp;&emsp; D. much  
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** promise 为及物动词，后接宾语，指“向我承诺”，应用人称代词宾格 me。I 为主格；my 为形容词性物主代词；mine 为名词性物主代词，均不能单独作 promise 的宾语。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析  
**【解析】** set off 为固定搭配，意为“出发、启程”，符合全家动身去海边的语境。set out 也可表“出发”，但常与 for + 地点连用（set out for...）；set up 意为“建立”；set down 意为“写下、放下”。题干无 for，set off 最自然。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** “than I had ___” 表示“比我（过去）曾想象的更……”，had 后需用过去分词构成过去完成时，imagined 正确。imagine 为原形；imagining 为现在分词；to imagine 为不定式，均不能与 had 构成该结构。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析  
**【解析】** 句意为孩子们正和父母一起堆沙堡，表示“和……一起”用介词 with。and 为并列连词，连接并列成分，此处 parents 前若用 and 易与前面结构不平行；but 表转折；or 表选择，均不符合。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do）  
**【解析】** remind sb. to do sth. 意为“提醒某人去做某事”，不定式作宾补。wear 为原形；wearing 常用于 keep doing 等结构；wore 为过去式，不能直接跟在 remind me 后。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 状语从句连词（目的）  
**【解析】** 志愿者捡拾垃圾的目的是保护海洋动物，so that 引导目的状语从句，意为“以便、为了”。even though 表让步“尽管”；as soon as 表时间“一……就……”；as long as 表条件“只要”，均与“捡拾—保护”的目的关系不符。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折）  
**【解析】** “很累”与“很自豪”为转折关系，用 but。and 表并列递进；or 表选择；so 表结果，均不符合前后语义对比。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 as 表“作为”  
**【解析】** as a souvenir 意为“作为纪念品”。like 多表“像”；for 表目的或原因时不如 as 与 souvenir 搭配固定；by 表方式或施事者，不适用。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** “one of + the + 形容词最高级 + 可数名词复数”为固定结构，表示“最……的……之一”。unforgettable 为多音节词，最高级为 the most unforgettable。more 为比较级；most 缺 the；much 修饰比较级或不可数名词，不用于该结构。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 thank sb. for doing  
**【解析】** thank sb. for (doing) sth. 意为“因……感谢某人”，for 后接名词或动名词。thank sb. to do 非常见结构；of/with 不与 thank 构成此义。故选 B。
