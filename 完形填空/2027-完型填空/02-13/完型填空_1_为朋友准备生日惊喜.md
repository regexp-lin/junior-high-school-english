# 中考英语完形填空：为朋友准备生日惊喜

## 完形填空原文

My best friend Anna's birthday was coming, and I wanted to give her a surprise. She loves reading, ___1___ I decided to decorate our classroom reading corner with balloons and handmade cards.

I told our classmates the plan ___2___ a secret meeting after school. Everyone was ___3___ to help. We collected colorful paper and cut out stars. Lily brought a small cake ___4___ her mom's permission.

On the birthday morning, we arrived early. While Anna was still at home, we hung the decorations ___5___ and wrote warm messages on the board. My hands were shaking because I was ___6___ she might find out.

When Anna walked in, we shouted, "Happy birthday!" Her eyes opened wide, and she almost ___7___. She hugged each of us and said it was the best gift she had ever ___8___.

That day, I learned that surprises are not about expensive presents. They are about ___9___ you remember small details about a friend. Kindness makes ordinary days ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. for
2. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. lazy &emsp;&emsp; D. nervous
4. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. without &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. about
5. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
6. A. sure &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. glad &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
7. A. cried &emsp;&emsp; B. slept &emsp;&emsp; C. left &emsp;&emsp; D. shouted
8. A. bought &emsp;&emsp; B. sold &emsp;&emsp; C. received &emsp;&emsp; D. borrowed
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. why
10. A. shorter &emsp;&emsp; B. brighter &emsp;&emsp; C. colder &emsp;&emsp; D. busier

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她喜欢阅读，所以我决定装饰教室的阅读角。前后因果关系，用 so。or 或者；but 但是；for 因为（表原因时常放句中）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：放学后的秘密会议上我告诉同学们计划。at a meeting 在会议上。on 在……上；in 在……里；by 通过。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都很乐意帮忙。excited 兴奋的、积极的。angry 生气的；lazy 懒惰的；nervous 紧张的。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：莉莉在得到妈妈允许后带了一个小蛋糕。with one's permission 经某人允许。without 没有。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：安娜还在家时，我们悄悄挂上装饰。quietly 安静地、悄悄地，以免被发现。carelessly 粗心地；quickly 快速地；loudly 大声地。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手在抖，因为我担心她会发现。afraid 担心、害怕。be afraid (that)... sure 确信；glad 高兴；proud 骄傲。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她眼睛睁得很大，几乎哭了（感动落泪）。cried 哭。slept 睡；left 离开；shouted 喊叫。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说这是她收到过的最好的礼物。received 收到。bought 买；sold 卖；borrowed 借。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们关乎你如何记住朋友的小细节。how 如何、怎样。what 什么；whether 是否；why 为什么。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：善意让平凡的日子更明亮。brighter 更明亮的、更美好的。shorter 更短；colder 更冷；busier 更忙。故选 B。
