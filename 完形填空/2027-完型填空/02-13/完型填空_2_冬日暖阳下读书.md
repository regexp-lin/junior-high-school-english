# 中考英语完形填空：冬日暖阳下读书

## 完形填空原文

It was a cold Saturday, but the sun came out after lunch. I carried a blanket and a novel to the balcony. My cat Mimi followed me ___1___ she also enjoyed warm sunshine.

I opened the book and read the first chapter ___2___. The story was about a boy who learned to face fear. Outside, the wind blew gently, ___3___ the pages turned by themselves once. I laughed and used a clip to keep them ___4___.

Half an hour later, my mom brought hot tea. She reminded me ___5___ a coat if I stayed too long. I nodded and thanked her. The tea smelled ___6___, and my fingers felt warm again.

Reading in winter is different from reading in summer. The world feels ___7___, yet the words inside the book feel closer. I wrote down two sentences I liked ___8___ my notebook.

When the sun moved lower, I closed the book and stretched. I felt relaxed and ___9___. That simple afternoon taught me that happiness doesn't always need a big plan—sometimes it is just a book, a cat, ___10___ a patch of sunlight.

## 选项

1. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
2. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. angrily
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. but
4. A. open &emsp;&emsp; B. closed &emsp;&emsp; C. still &emsp;&emsp; D. clean
5. A. wear &emsp;&emsp; B. to wear &emsp;&emsp; C. wearing &emsp;&emsp; D. wore
6. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. strange &emsp;&emsp; C. sweet &emsp;&emsp; D. salty
7. A. louder &emsp;&emsp; B. wider &emsp;&emsp; C. quieter &emsp;&emsp; D. heavier
8. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
9. A. lonely &emsp;&emsp; B. sleepy &emsp;&emsp; C. peaceful &emsp;&emsp; D. careless
10. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的猫咪咪跟着我，因为她也喜欢温暖的阳光。because 因为，表原因。although 尽管；unless 除非；until 直到。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我打开书并认真阅读第一章。carefully 认真地。carelessly 粗心地；loudly 大声地；angrily 生气地。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：外面微风轻吹，书页有一次自己翻动了。前后并列顺承，用 and。or 或者；so 所以；but 但是。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我笑着用夹子把书页固定住不让再动。keep sth. still 使……保持不动。open 开着的；closed 关着的；clean 干净的。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她提醒如果待太久要穿外套。remind sb. to do sth. 提醒某人做某事。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：茶闻起来香甜。sweet 香的、甘甜的（形容气味/味道）。terrible 糟糕的；strange 奇怪的；salty 咸的。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：世界感觉更安静，但书里的文字却感觉更近。quieter 更安静的，与冬日阳台氛围相符。louder 更大声；wider 更宽；heavier 更重。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把喜欢的两个句子记在笔记本里。in my notebook 在笔记本里。on 在……上；at 在；by 通过。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到放松而平和。peaceful 平和的。lonely 孤独的；sleepy 困倦的；careless 粗心的。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时幸福只需要一本书、一只猫和一片阳光。三者并列，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 B。
