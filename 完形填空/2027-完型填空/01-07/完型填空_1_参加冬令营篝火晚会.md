# 中考英语完形填空：参加冬令营篝火晚会

## 完形填空原文

This winter, I attended a five-day winter camp in the mountains. The best part was the campfire party on the last ___1___. As the sun went down, the camp leaders lit a big fire in the center of the open field. The orange flames danced in the cold air, making everyone feel warm and ___2___.

We sat in a big circle around the fire. The leaders asked each group to ___3___ a performance. Some groups sang songs, and some told funny stories. My group decided to act out a short play about friendship. I played the role of a shy student who finally found the ___4___ to speak up in class. Even though I forgot a few lines, everyone clapped and laughed.

Between performances, we roasted marshmallows on long sticks. I had to hold mine just the right ___5___ from the fire—too close and it would burn, too far and it would stay cold. When I finally got a perfectly golden marshmallow, I felt so ___6___. It was soft, sweet, and delicious.

As the evening went on, the camp leader brought out a guitar. He played some popular songs and everyone ___7___ along. I did not know all the words, but I hummed the melody quietly. Looking up at the starry sky, I felt a deep connection with ___8___ around me.

Before the fire died out, the leader asked us to share one thing we had ___9___ during the camp. Many students said they had learned to be more independent and to get along with new people. I said I had learned that stepping out of my comfort zone was not as scary as I thought.

That night, lying in my sleeping bag, I could still smell the smoke from the campfire. I knew I would ___10___ this experience for a very long time.

## 选项

1. A. morning &emsp;&emsp; B. night &emsp;&emsp; C. afternoon &emsp;&emsp; D. week
2. A. lonely &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. cozy &emsp;&emsp; D. sleepy
3. A. cancel &emsp;&emsp; B. prepare &emsp;&emsp; C. refuse &emsp;&emsp; D. record
4. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. courage &emsp;&emsp; D. ticket
5. A. distance &emsp;&emsp; B. speed &emsp;&emsp; C. weight &emsp;&emsp; D. size
6. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. sorry &emsp;&emsp; D. confused
7. A. ran &emsp;&emsp; B. walked &emsp;&emsp; C. sang &emsp;&emsp; D. drove
8. A. nobody &emsp;&emsp; B. everyone &emsp;&emsp; C. something &emsp;&emsp; D. nothing
9. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. spent &emsp;&emsp; C. wasted &emsp;&emsp; D. learned
10. A. regret &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. remember &emsp;&emsp; D. change

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最棒的是最后一晚的篝火晚会。A 项意为"早上"；B 项意为"晚上"；C 项意为"下午"；D 项意为"周"。campfire party 在 night 举行。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：火焰让每个人感到温暖和舒适。A 项意为"孤独的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"温馨舒适的"；D 项意为"困倦的"。warm and cozy 搭配合理。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：营长让每个小组准备一个表演。A 项意为"取消"；B 项意为"准备"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"录制"。prepare a performance 意为"准备表演"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个害羞的学生终于找到勇气在课堂上发言。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"勇气"；D 项意为"票"。courage to speak up 意为"勇气发言"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要和火保持恰到好处的距离。A 项意为"距离"；B 项意为"速度"；C 项意为"重量"；D 项意为"大小"。the right distance from the fire。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：烤出完美金色棉花糖时我感到很自豪。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"抱歉的"；D 项意为"困惑的"。成功后感到 proud。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他弹流行歌曲，大家一起唱。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"走"；C 项意为"唱"；D 项意为"开车"。sang along 意为"跟着唱"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感受到与周围每个人的深层联系。A 项意为"没人"；B 项意为"每个人"；C 项意为"某物"；D 项意为"没什么"。connection with everyone。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：分享在营中学到的一件事。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"花费"；C 项意为"浪费"；D 项意为"学到"。后文都在说学到了什么，用 learned。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道我会长久记住这次经历。A 项意为"后悔"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"记住"；D 项意为"改变"。remember this experience 意为"铭记这段经历"。故选 C。
