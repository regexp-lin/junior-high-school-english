# 中考英语完形填空：第一次学下象棋

## 完形填空原文

My grandfather is a big fan of Chinese chess. Every weekend, he plays with his friends in the park. Last winter holiday, I asked him to teach me how to ___1___. He was very pleased and said, "I've been waiting for you to ask!"

Grandpa set up the chessboard on the dining table. He explained the rules one by one. Each piece has its own way of ___2___. The general can only move within a small area, while the horse can jump in an L-shape. I tried hard to remember everything, but there were so many ___3___ that I felt confused at first.

We played our first game that afternoon. Grandpa went easy on me and let me take back several bad ___4___. Even so, I lost quickly. I was a little disappointed, but Grandpa said, "Don't worry. Nobody wins on their first try. The key is to think before you ___5___."

Over the next few days, I played with Grandpa every afternoon. Each time, I lasted a little ___6___ before losing. I started to understand the importance of strategy—you have to think not only about your next move but also about what your ___7___ might do. It was like solving a puzzle in real time.

On the last day of the holiday, I almost beat Grandpa. He had to think very hard before each move. ___8___ I still lost in the end, Grandpa praised me for my improvement. "You have a good ___9___ for chess," he said with a big smile.

Now I understand why Grandpa loves this game so much. Chess teaches you patience, planning, and how to stay ___10___ under pressure. I plan to keep practicing so that one day I can finally win a game against him.

1. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. swim &emsp;&emsp; C. play &emsp;&emsp; D. sing
2. A. looking &emsp;&emsp; B. moving &emsp;&emsp; C. speaking &emsp;&emsp; D. growing
3. A. stories &emsp;&emsp; B. colors &emsp;&emsp; C. rules &emsp;&emsp; D. songs
4. A. habits &emsp;&emsp; B. moves &emsp;&emsp; C. grades &emsp;&emsp; D. jokes
5. A. eat &emsp;&emsp; B. sleep &emsp;&emsp; C. act &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
6. A. shorter &emsp;&emsp; B. longer &emsp;&emsp; C. earlier &emsp;&emsp; D. worse
7. A. teacher &emsp;&emsp; B. partner &emsp;&emsp; C. opponent &emsp;&emsp; D. parent
8. A. Because &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. Until
9. A. mind &emsp;&emsp; B. voice &emsp;&emsp; C. body &emsp;&emsp; D. face
10. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. calm &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. sad

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我请爷爷教我怎么下棋。A 项意为"做饭"；B 项意为"游泳"；C 项意为"下/玩"；D 项意为"唱歌"。play chess 意为"下象棋"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个棋子有自己的走法。A 项意为"看"；B 项意为"移动"；C 项意为"说话"；D 项意为"生长"。way of moving 表示移动方式。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：规则太多让我一开始很困惑。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"颜色"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"歌曲"。棋类有很多 rules。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷让我悔掉好几步坏棋。A 项意为"习惯"；B 项意为"步/着"；C 项意为"成绩"；D 项意为"玩笑"。bad moves 意为"坏棋步"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关键是在行动之前先思考。A 项意为"吃"；B 项意为"睡"；C 项意为"行动"；D 项意为"离开"。think before you act 意为"三思而后行"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次输之前坚持得更久一点。A 项意为"更短"；B 项意为"更长"；C 项意为"更早"；D 项意为"更差"。lasted longer 表示进步了。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不仅要想自己的下一步，还要想对手可能怎么做。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"伙伴"；C 项意为"对手"；D 项意为"父母"。下棋时要考虑 opponent 的策略。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然最后我还是输了，但爷爷表扬了我的进步。A 项意为"因为"；B 项意为"虽然"；C 项意为"除非"；D 项意为"直到"。Although...praised 表示让步。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你有下棋的好头脑。A 项意为"头脑"；B 项意为"声音"；C 项意为"身体"；D 项意为"脸"。a good mind for chess 意为"下棋有头脑"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：象棋教你耐心、计划和在压力下保持冷静。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"冷静的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"伤心的"。stay calm under pressure 意为"压力下保持冷静"。故选 B。
