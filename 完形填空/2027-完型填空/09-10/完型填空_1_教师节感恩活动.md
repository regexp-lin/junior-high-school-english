# 中考英语完形填空：教师节感恩活动

## 完形填空原文

September 10th is Teacher's Day in China. Our school organized a special event ___1___ teachers for their hard work. Every class prepared a short performance or a surprise.

Our class chose to sing a song together. We practiced it ___2___ for a whole week without letting our teachers know. The song was called "You Raise Me Up," and we picked it because its words perfectly ___3___ our feelings.

During the morning assembly, the principal invited all the teachers to the front. One by one, each class ___4___ their program. Some danced, some recited poems, and some acted out funny scenes from classroom life. The teachers laughed and clapped ___5___.

When our turn came, we stood up and began to sing. I could see Ms. Li, our head teacher, ___6___ us with wide eyes. As the song went on, her eyes became red. She was ___7___ moved by our performance.

After the event, students rushed to give flowers and cards ___8___ their teachers. I gave Ms. Li a handmade card and a small bouquet. She held my hands and said, "Having students like you makes all the hard work ___9___."

That day, I truly understood ___10___ important teachers are in our lives.

## 选项

1. A. thank &emsp;&emsp; B. thanked &emsp;&emsp; C. thanking &emsp;&emsp; D. to thank
2. A. secret &emsp;&emsp; B. secrete &emsp;&emsp; C. secretly &emsp;&emsp; D. secrets
3. A. express &emsp;&emsp; B. expressed &emsp;&emsp; C. expressing &emsp;&emsp; D. expression
4. A. present &emsp;&emsp; B. presented &emsp;&emsp; C. presenting &emsp;&emsp; D. presents
5. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. happier &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. happiness
6. A. look at &emsp;&emsp; B. looking at &emsp;&emsp; C. looked at &emsp;&emsp; D. looks at
7. A. deep &emsp;&emsp; B. deeply &emsp;&emsp; C. depth &emsp;&emsp; D. deepen
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. worth &emsp;&emsp; B. worthy &emsp;&emsp; C. worthwhile &emsp;&emsp; D. worthless
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. that

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校组织了一个特别活动来感谢老师们的辛勤工作。to thank 为不定式表目的。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 词性辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们偷偷地练习了整整一周。修饰动词 practiced，需用副词 secretly。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们选这首歌是因为歌词完美地表达了我们的感受。叙述过去的事件，用一般过去时 expressed。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个班逐一呈现了自己的节目。叙述过去的事，用一般过去时 presented。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师们开心地笑着鼓掌。修饰动词 clapped，需用副词 happily。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（现在分词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能看到李老师睁大眼睛看着我们。see sb. doing sth. 表示"看到某人正在做某事"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她被我们的表演深深感动了。修饰过去分词 moved，需用副词 deeply。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们争着把鲜花和贺卡送给他们的老师。give sth. to sb. 表示"把某物给某人"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有像你们这样的学生，让所有的辛苦都值得。make + 宾语 + adj.（补语），worthwhile 表示"值得的"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句（感叹句作宾语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天我真正理解了老师在我们生活中有多重要。how + adj. 引导宾语从句，表示感叹程度。故选 B。
