# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工编绳

## 完形填空原文

My aunt is very good at making braided bracelets. She promised to teach me ___1___ I visited her last weekend. I was happy because I wanted to make one as a gift for my teacher.

She took out several rolls of thin colored cord and asked me ___2___ three colors. I picked red, gold, and white. "These colors stand ___3___ good luck, wisdom, and purity," she said.

The first step was to cut three cords of equal ___4___ and tie them together at one end. Then I taped the knot to the table so it wouldn't ___5___. My aunt showed me how to braid the cords by crossing them over each other in a specific order.

It sounded simple, but my fingers kept getting ___6___. The cords tangled several times, and I had to start over. "Don't worry. Everyone is slow ___7___ the beginning," my aunt comforted me.

After practicing for half an hour, I ___8___ got the hang of it. My hands moved more smoothly, and the pattern became neat. By the end of the afternoon, I had finished a beautiful bracelet.

I was so ___9___ of it that I showed it to everyone at home. The next Monday, I gave it to Ms. Li. She wore it on her wrist ___10___ and said she loved it. I felt warm inside.

## 选项

1. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. until
2. A. choose &emsp;&emsp; B. chose &emsp;&emsp; C. to choose &emsp;&emsp; D. choosing
3. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. length &emsp;&emsp; C. wide &emsp;&emsp; D. width
5. A. move &emsp;&emsp; B. moved &emsp;&emsp; C. moving &emsp;&emsp; D. moves
6. A. confuse &emsp;&emsp; B. confusing &emsp;&emsp; C. confused &emsp;&emsp; D. confusion
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. for
8. A. final &emsp;&emsp; B. finals &emsp;&emsp; C. finalize &emsp;&emsp; D. finally
9. A. pride &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. proudly &emsp;&emsp; D. prides
10. A. right away &emsp;&emsp; B. right now &emsp;&emsp; C. just now &emsp;&emsp; D. from now on

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她答应在我上周末去拜访她时教我。when 引导时间状语从句，表示"当……时候"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她拿出几卷细彩绳让我选三种颜色。ask sb. to do sth. 表示"要求某人做某事"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些颜色代表好运、智慧和纯洁。stand for 表示"代表、象征"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一步是剪三根等长的绳子。equal 为形容词，后接名词 length，表示"等长度"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 情态动词后接动词原形
**【解析】** 本句意为：把绳结贴在桌上以免它移动。wouldn't 后接动词原形 move。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（-ed 与 -ing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我的手指一直弄混。get confused 表示"（人/手指）变得混乱"，confused 描述感受/状态。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人在开始的时候都很慢。at the beginning 表示"在开始时"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 词性辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：练习了半小时后，我终于掌握了窍门。修饰动词 got，需用副词 finally。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常自豪。be proud of 表示"为……感到自豪"；so 后接形容词 proud。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她立刻戴在手腕上。right away 表示"立刻、马上"，叙述过去的动作。故选 A。
