# 中考英语完形填空：暑假学书法

## 完形填空原文

Linda chose calligraphy ___1___ her summer hobby because she wanted to write ___2___ beautifully. Her grandfather, ___3___ has practiced for forty years, became her first teacher.

Every morning, she ground the ink ___4___ and held the brush ___5___. At first, her strokes were shaky, ___6___ she did not give up. Her grandfather praised her ___7___ being careful with each character.

Two weeks later, Linda could copy a short poem ___8___ than before. She hung ___9___ best work on the wall. She believes practice makes ___10___ perfect.

## 选项

1. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. to
2. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. quite
3. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
4. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slower &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowest
5. A. correct &emsp;&emsp; B. correctly &emsp;&emsp; C. correction &emsp;&emsp; D. correcting
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. or
7. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of
8. A. well &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. good
9. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：琳达把书法选作暑假爱好。choose…as… 把……选作……。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 比较级 / 副词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她想写得更漂亮。暗含与现在比较，修饰动词 write 用副词比较级 more beautifully（选项用 more 与 beautifully 搭配在句中即 more beautifully）。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的祖父已练习四十年。先行词 grandfather 指人，非限制性定语从句作主语，用 who。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她慢慢地研墨。修饰动词 ground，用副词 slowly。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：并正确地握笔。修饰动词 held，用副词 correctly。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初笔画发抖，但她没有放弃。转折关系用 but。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：祖父因她对每个字都很认真而称赞她。praise sb. for (doing) sth. 因……称赞某人。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：两周后她能比以前更好地临摹一首短诗。后有 than，用比较级 better。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词 / 物主代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把自己最好的作品挂在墙上。修饰名词 work，用形容词性物主代词 her。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 冠词 / 固定谚语
**【解析】** 本句意为：她相信熟能生巧。practice makes perfect 为固定谚语，perfect 前不用冠词。故选 D。
