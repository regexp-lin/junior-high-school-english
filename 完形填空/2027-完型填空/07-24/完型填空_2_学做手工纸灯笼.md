# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸灯笼

## 完形填空原文

In the craft workshop, students were shown ___1___ make a paper lantern step by step. Daniel cut red paper ___2___ a pair of scissors ___3___ his teacher had lent him.

He folded the paper ___4___ and glued the edges together. ___5___ little light was put inside, the lantern looked warm and bright. Daniel thought this project was ___6___ interesting of all the activities.

He promised ___7___ the lantern home safely ___8___ his little sister could see it. His classmates said his lantern was ___9___ beautiful than theirs. Daniel smiled ___10___ said he would help them next time.

## 选项

1. A. what to &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
4. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. careless
5. A. Before &emsp;&emsp; B. After &emsp;&emsp; C. Until &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless
6. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. the most &emsp;&emsp; D. very
7. A. bring &emsp;&emsp; B. to bring &emsp;&emsp; C. bringing &emsp;&emsp; D. brought
8. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although
9. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most
10. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词+不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生们被示范如何一步步做纸灯笼。show sb. how to do sth. 向某人展示如何做某事。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：丹尼尔用一把剪刀剪红纸。with 表示“用（工具）”。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师借给他的那把剪刀。先行词 scissors 指物，在从句中作宾语，用 which。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他仔细地把纸对折。修饰动词 folded，用副词 carefully。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：放进一盏小灯之后，灯笼看起来温暖明亮。after 表示“在……之后”。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：丹尼尔认为这是所有活动中最有趣的一个。of all the activities 表范围，用 the most interesting。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他答应把灯笼安全带回家。promise to do sth. 承诺做某事。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词 / 目的状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：以便妹妹能看到它。so that 引导目的状语从句。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学说他的灯笼比他们的更漂亮。后有 than，用 more beautiful。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：丹尼尔微笑着说下次会帮他们。smiled 与 said 并列，用 and。故选 C。
