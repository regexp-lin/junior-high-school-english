# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工木刻印章

## 完形填空原文

In the culture workshop, Mr. Zhou gave each of us ___1___ small piece of soft wood and a carving knife. Wood shavings gathered on the table like light snow while we listened to his safety tips. He warned us ___2___ the blade was sharp, so we had to work slowly. I drew a simple Chinese character ___3___ means "luck" and copied it onto the wood with a pencil. ___4___ I started to cut along the lines, my hands shook a little. Mr. Zhou stood ___5___ me and showed me how to control the angle. After practicing for an hour, the lines looked ___6___ than my first try. When I pressed the stamp onto red ink and paper, the character appeared clearly. I couldn't help ___7___ loudly because it really looked like a real seal. Mr. Zhou said, "Skills come ___8___ practice, not from hurry." I promised ___9___ I would keep practicing at home. Carving taught me ___10___ important patience is in learning something new.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
4. A. When &emsp;&emsp; B. Before &emsp;&emsp; C. Until &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
5. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. above &emsp;&emsp; C. below &emsp;&emsp; D. across  
6. A. neat &emsp;&emsp; B. neater &emsp;&emsp; C. neatest &emsp;&emsp; D. neatly  
7. A. cheer &emsp;&emsp; B. cheered &emsp;&emsp; C. cheering &emsp;&emsp; D. to cheer  
8. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 冠词（a piece of...）  
**【解析】** a small piece of soft wood 为固定量词结构，用不定冠词 a；an 用于元音音素开头；the 表特指但此处首次分发材料；零冠词不符合 a piece of 结构。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词（that 引导陈述内容）  
**【解析】** warned us that the blade was sharp 中 that 引导宾语从句；if 表“是否”，how/what 语义不符警告内容。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词（先行词为物，作主语）  
**【解析】** character which/that means "luck" 指“意思是‘运/福’的那个汉字”；what 不引导定语从句；who/whom 指人。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词（时间状语从句：当……时）  
**【解析】** When I started to cut... 表示当我开始沿线条刻时；Before/Until/Unless 与“手抖”的同时性情境不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词（方位：站在我身后指导）  
**【解析】** stood behind me 符合老师站在学生身后示范的常见场景；above/below/across 不符合语境。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than 提示）  
**【解析】** looked neater than my first try 表示比第一次更整齐；neat 为原级，neatest 为最高级，neatly 为副词不能作 looked 的表语形容词位置（此处需要形容词作表语）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（can't help doing 忍不住做某事）  
**【解析】** couldn't help cheering 为固定搭配；help 后不接动词原形 cheered/to cheer 表此义；cheer 原形不对。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词（come from 来自/源于）  
**【解析】** Skills come from practice 表示技艺来自练习；come by 表“获得”不自然，come in/on 语义不对。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词（promise that...）  
**【解析】** promised that I would... 陈述承诺内容；promise what/if/how 均不如 that 贴合“承诺会继续练习”的陈述语气。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句（how + 形容词，表程度）  
**【解析】** taught me how important patience is 表示让我明白耐心多么重要；what important 结构错误；why/when 与强调程度不符。故选 B。
