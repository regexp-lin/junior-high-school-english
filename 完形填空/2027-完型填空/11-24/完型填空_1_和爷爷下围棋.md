# 中考英语完形填空：和爷爷下围棋

## 完形填空原文

Every Sunday afternoon, my grandpa invites me ___1___ play Go with him in the living room. The soft click of stones on the board has become the sound I associate most with weekends at home. At first, I always lost ___2___ five minutes because I didn't think carefully. Grandpa never laughed at me. ___3___, he taught me how to read the board and plan two steps ahead. "Go is not only about winning," he said. "It is also about ___4___ patient when you are behind." Slowly, I learned ___5___ calm down after a bad move. Last week, for the first time, I won ___6___ half a point. Grandpa clapped ___7___ happily than anyone else. After the game, he made tea and told stories ___8___ his own teacher in the 1970s. I realized ___9___ precious these afternoons were. Now I look forward ___10___ Sundays not because I want to win, but because I love staying with him.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. However &emsp;&emsp; B. Instead &emsp;&emsp; C. Though &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless  
4. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. being &emsp;&emsp; C. been &emsp;&emsp; D. to be  
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. / &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
7. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. quite  
8. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. such &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. on  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（invite sb. to do sth.）  
**【解析】** invite me to play 为不定式作宾补；for/of/with 不构成 invite 的标准用法。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词（in + 时间段：在……之内）  
**【解析】** lost in five minutes 表示“五分钟内就输了”；on/at/by 与“在几分钟内结束”不搭配。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词（Instead 表“相反/而是”，承接上下文）  
**【解析】** 上文“爷爷从不嘲笑我”，下文“他教我……”，用 Instead 表示转折性的替代行为；However 常放句首且多逗号隔开，语气偏“然而”；Though/Unless 词性与语义不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词后接动名词（about + doing）  
**【解析】** about being patient 中 about 为介词，后接 being；be/been/to be 不能直接跟在介词 about 后作宾语。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（learn to do sth.）  
**【解析】** learn to calm down 表示学会冷静下来；learn 后接不定式更常见；直接接动词原形不符合语法。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词（by 表示“以……差距”）  
**【解析】** win by half a point 表示“以半目获胜”；in/with/for 不用于表达比分/差距的“以……计”。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级（more happily than...）  
**【解析】** than 提示比较级，happily 为多音节副词，用 more happily；most 为最高级；very/quite 不能接 than。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词（tell stories about... 讲关于……的故事）  
**【解析】** stories about his own teacher 表示关于他老师的故事；tell stories to sb. 才用 to，此处宾语是 stories 的内容，用 about；with/for 不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句（how + 形容词）  
**【解析】** realized how precious these afternoons were 表示意识到这些下午多么珍贵；what precious 结构不对；such/very 不能这样引导从句。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（look forward to 中的 to 为介词，后接名词）  
**【解析】** look forward to Sundays 中 to 是介词，后接名词 Sundays；look forward for/at/on 均为错误搭配。故选 C。
