# 中考英语完形填空：校园音乐节

## 完形填空原文

Our school held its first Music Festival last Friday. Students ___1___ different grades performed on the outdoor stage.

The show started ___2___ a piano solo by a girl from Class Two. Her fingers moved quickly over the keys. Everyone listened ___3___ and the playground became very quiet.

Then a rock band came on stage. They played so ___4___ that some students stood up and danced. The lead singer's voice was powerful ___5___ full of energy.

My friend Chen Wei and I sang a folk song together. We had practiced it ___6___ two weeks. When we finished, the audience gave us warm applause. I was moved ___7___ their support.

The most surprising act was a teacher who played the violin. Nobody ___8___ he could play so well. The whole crowd cheered for him.

At the end, all performers went back on stage ___9___ sang the school song together. It was one of the ___10___ memories of this school year.

## 选项

1. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. quiet &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. quick &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
4. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. louder &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. loudest
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. knows &emsp;&emsp; B. knew &emsp;&emsp; C. is knowing &emsp;&emsp; D. has known
9. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. happier &emsp;&emsp; C. happiest &emsp;&emsp; D. happily

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：来自不同年级的学生在室外舞台上表演。from different grades 表示"来自不同年级"。in/at/to 不如 from 贴切表示"来自"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：演出以一位女生的钢琴独奏开场。start with... 表示"以……开始"。to/for/by 不符合 start 的搭配。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都静静地听着。修饰动词 listened 需用副词 quietly。quiet 为形容词；quick/quickly 为"快速"，不符合语境。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词/so...that 结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们演奏得如此大声以至于有些同学站起来跳舞。so + 副词 + that，修饰动词 played 用副词 loudly。loud 为形容词；louder/loudest 为比较级/最高级，不符合 so...that 结构。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：主唱的嗓音有力且充满活力。powerful 与 full of energy 并列修饰 voice，用 and。or/but/so 不符合并列关系。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/时间介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们练习了两周。for + 时间段，表示持续时间。since 后接时间点；in/at 不表示持续时间段。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/被动搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被他们的支持所感动。be moved by... 表示"被……感动"。of/for/with 不符合 moved 的搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：没人知道他能演奏得这么好。全文用一般过去时叙述过去事件，此处也用过去式 knew。knows 为现在时；is knowing 不合语法；has known 为现在完成时，不符合过去叙事。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：所有表演者回到舞台上并一起唱校歌。went 与 sang 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是本学年最快乐的记忆之一。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数为固定结构。happy 为原级；happier 为比较级；happily 为副词。故选 C。
