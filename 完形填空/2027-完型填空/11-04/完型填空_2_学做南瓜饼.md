# 中考英语完形填空：学做南瓜饼

## 完形填空原文

Autumn is the season when pumpkins taste ___1___ in our town. Grandma taught me ___2___ pumpkin pies last Sunday, and every step felt like a small adventure.

We steamed the pumpkin until it ___3___ soft enough to mash. While I ___4___ it with a spoon, Grandma mixed flour and eggs in ___5___ bowl. She explained ___6___ we should not add too much water at once.

Then we shaped the dough into round cakes. Some of ___7___ were thicker than the others, ___8___ Grandma said home cooking did not need to be perfect. When the cakes ___9___ in the pan, the kitchen was filled with a sweet smell.

At dinner, my brother ate two pieces quickly. He said they were ___10___ delicious pies he had ever tasted. I knew part of the secret was sharing the work with Grandma.

## 选项

1. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. the best  
2. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
3. A. get &emsp;&emsp; B. got &emsp;&emsp; C. gets &emsp;&emsp; D. was getting  
4. A. mash &emsp;&emsp; B. mashed &emsp;&emsp; C. was mashing &emsp;&emsp; D. have mashed  
5. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. others &emsp;&emsp; C. another &emsp;&emsp; D. the other  
6. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. why  
7. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. fry &emsp;&emsp; B. fried &emsp;&emsp; C. were frying &emsp;&emsp; D. were being fried  
10. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. the most &emsp;&emsp; D. much  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** taste the best 表示“尝起来最好”，in our town 给出范围，用 the best。good/better 程度不足；best 缺 the 不够规范。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（teach sb. to do）  
**【解析】** teach sb. to do sth. 用不定式 to make。make/making/made 不符合 teach 的常见结构。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** until 引导的从句叙述过去，南瓜“变得”够软，用 got。get 原形；gets 现在时；was getting 进行时不必要。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去进行时  
**【解析】** While 强调“正在用勺子捣碎时奶奶在另一处和面”，用 was mashing。mash/mashed/have mashed 不能突出同时进行的画面感。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** another / the other / other 辨析  
**【解析】** in another bowl 表示“在另一个碗里”，泛指又一个碗。other 后需名词复数；others 后不接名词；the other 常表两者中的另一个，此处无两者限定。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 why  
**【解析】** explained 后为宾语从句，说明原因“为什么不要一次加太多水”，用 why。what/that/whether 不能表达原因解释。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（介词后）  
**【解析】** Some of 后接宾格 them，指代 the cakes。they 为主格；their/theirs 为物主代词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词  
**【解析】** “有些饼更厚”与“奶奶说家常菜不必完美”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 过去进行时的被动语态  
**【解析】** cakes 在锅里被煎，强调过程用 were being fried；were frying 多为主语自己煎，主语为 cakes 应用被动。fry/fried 时态或进行体不当。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（the most + 形容词）  
**【解析】** he had ever tasted 提示最高级，the most delicious。more 为比较级；most 缺 the；much 修饰原级形容词不当。故选 C。
