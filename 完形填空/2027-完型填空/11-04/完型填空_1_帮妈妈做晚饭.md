# 中考英语完形填空：帮妈妈做晚饭

## 完形填空原文

Yesterday was busy for Mom, so I offered ___1___ dinner with her in the kitchen. She smiled and handed ___2___ an apron, then told me ___3___ the vegetables first.

I washed the tomatoes carefully, ___4___ I almost dropped one on the floor. Mom laughed and said, “Take it slowly. Cooking is ___5___ a race.” While the rice ___6___ in the pot, we started to cut the chicken into small pieces.

Later, Mom let me ___7___ the sauce by myself. It smelled wonderful, ___8___ I added a little too much salt. Mom tasted it and said we could put in ___9___ sugar to balance the flavor.

When Dad came home, he said the meal tasted ___10___ than usual because we made it together. That night, I understood love could be a simple dinner, too.

## 选项

1. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. cooking &emsp;&emsp; C. to cook &emsp;&emsp; D. cooked  
2. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
3. A. wash &emsp;&emsp; B. washing &emsp;&emsp; C. to wash &emsp;&emsp; D. washed  
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
5. A. no &emsp;&emsp; B. not &emsp;&emsp; C. none &emsp;&emsp; D. neither  
6. A. cooks &emsp;&emsp; B. cooked &emsp;&emsp; C. was cooking &emsp;&emsp; D. has cooked  
7. A. stir &emsp;&emsp; B. stirring &emsp;&emsp; C. to stir &emsp;&emsp; D. stirred  
8. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
9. A. a few &emsp;&emsp; B. few &emsp;&emsp; C. a little &emsp;&emsp; D. little  
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. the best  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（offer to do）  
**【解析】** offer to do sth. 表示“主动提出做某事”，用不定式 to cook。cook/cooking/cooked 不符合 offer 的搭配。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** handed 后接双宾语，间接宾语用人称代词宾格 me。I 为主格；my/mine 为物主代词。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell me to wash the vegetables 为 tell sb. to do sth.。wash/washing/washed 不符合 tell 结构。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 转折连词  
**【解析】** “认真洗番茄”与“差点掉地上”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 否定词 not 与名词搭配  
**【解析】** Cooking is not a race. 用 not 否定后面的名词短语。no 后一般不接冠词 + 可数名词单数的这种表语结构（多用 not a）；none/neither 语义不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去进行时  
**【解析】** While 引导时间状语从句，强调“米饭正在锅里煮的同时我们开始切鸡肉”，用 was cooking。cooks/cooked/has cooked 不能体现同时进行。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 使役动词 let 后接动词原形  
**【解析】** let sb. do sth. 用动词原形 stir。stirring/to stir/stirred 均不符合 let 的用法。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 让步连词 although  
**【解析】** “闻起来很香”与“盐加多了一点”为让步关系，用 although。because 表原因会因果颠倒；unless/if 为条件。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** a little / little 修饰不可数名词  
**【解析】** sugar 为不可数名词，且句意为肯定“加一点糖”，用 a little。a few/few 修饰可数名词；little 表否定“几乎没有”。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** than usual 提示比较级 better。tasted good 无比较；best/the best 为最高级。故选 B。
