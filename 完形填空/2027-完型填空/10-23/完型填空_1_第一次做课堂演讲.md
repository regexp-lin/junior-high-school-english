# 中考英语完形填空：第一次做课堂演讲

## 完形填空原文

Our English teacher announced that each student had to give a five-minute speech in class. When I heard the news, my heart started ___1___ fast. Public speaking had always been my biggest fear.

I spent the whole weekend ___2___ my speech. The topic I chose was "My Dream." I wrote it out word by word and practiced in front of the mirror at least ten ___3___.

On Monday morning, I was so nervous that I could ___4___ eat breakfast. My mother noticed and held my hand. "Just be yourself," she said. "If you forget a word, take a deep breath and ___5___."

When my name was called, I walked to the front of the class. My legs felt like they were made of jelly. I looked at my classmates and saw their encouraging ___6___. That gave me a little courage.

I started speaking. At first, my voice was too low for anyone ___7___. The teacher reminded me gently to speak up. I took a breath and raised my voice. As I talked about my dream of becoming a doctor, I gradually forgot my fear.

When I finished, the class gave me a round of applause. I could hardly ___8___ it. My best friend gave me a thumbs-up and whispered, "You did ___9___ than you think!"

That experience taught me that courage doesn't mean having no fear. It means ___10___ forward even when you are afraid.

## 选项

1. A. beat &emsp;&emsp; B. beating &emsp;&emsp; C. to beat &emsp;&emsp; D. beaten  
2. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. prepared &emsp;&emsp; C. to prepare &emsp;&emsp; D. preparing  
3. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. times &emsp;&emsp; C. timing &emsp;&emsp; D. timely  
4. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. hardest  
5. A. continue &emsp;&emsp; B. continues &emsp;&emsp; C. continued &emsp;&emsp; D. continuing  
6. A. smile &emsp;&emsp; B. smiles &emsp;&emsp; C. smiled &emsp;&emsp; D. smiling  
7. A. hear &emsp;&emsp; B. hears &emsp;&emsp; C. hearing &emsp;&emsp; D. to hear  
8. A. believe &emsp;&emsp; B. believed &emsp;&emsp; C. believing &emsp;&emsp; D. to believe  
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
10. A. move &emsp;&emsp; B. moved &emsp;&emsp; C. to move &emsp;&emsp; D. moving  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（start doing）  
**【解析】** start beating 意为"开始跳动"，start 后接动名词表已开始的动作。beat 为原形；to beat 虽可但 beating 更强调心跳正在发生；beaten 为过去分词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 固定句型 spend...doing  
**【解析】** spend time doing sth. 意为"花时间做某事"，后接动名词。prepare 为原形；prepared 为过去式；to prepare 为不定式。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词 times 表"次数"  
**【解析】** ten times 意为"十次"，times 表次数为可数名词复数。time 为不可数（时间）或单数（一次）；timing 意为"时机"；timely 为形容词"及时的"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词 hardly 表否定  
**【解析】** could hardly eat 意为"几乎吃不下"，hardly 意为"几乎不"。hard 意为"努力地"；harder/hardest 为比较级/最高级。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 祈使句（动词原形）  
**【解析】** 祈使句用动词原形，take a deep breath and continue 为两个并列的祈使句。continues 为三单；continued 为过去式；continuing 为现在分词。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词复数  
**【解析】** their encouraging smiles 意为"他们鼓励的微笑"，classmates 为复数，对应多个微笑用 smiles。smile 为单数；smiled 为过去式动词；smiling 为现在分词。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** too...for sb. to do  
**【解析】** too low for anyone to hear 意为"太低了以至于没人能听到"，too + adj. + for sb. + to do 为固定结构。hear/hears 缺 to；hearing 为动名词。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词 + 动词原形  
**【解析】** could hardly believe it 意为"几乎不敢相信"，could 后接动词原形。believed/believing/to believe 均不跟在情态动词后。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级  
**【解析】** did better than 意为"做得比……更好"，better 为 well 的比较级修饰动词 did。good 为形容词；well 为原级；best 为最高级。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 动名词作表语  
**【解析】** It means moving forward 意为"它意味着向前迈进"，means 后接动名词 moving。move 为原形；moved 为过去式；to move 虽可但 mean doing 侧重"意味着"而 mean to do 侧重"打算"。故选 D。
