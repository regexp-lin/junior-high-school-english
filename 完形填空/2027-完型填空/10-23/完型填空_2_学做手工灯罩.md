# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工灯罩

## 完形填空原文

Our physics teacher wanted us to combine science ___1___ art. He designed a special project: making a lampshade that could create interesting shadow patterns on the wall.

He explained the basic ___2___ behind it. "Light travels in straight lines. If you cut patterns in the shade, the light will pass through and form shapes on the wall." I thought it was a ___3___ idea.

Each of us received a piece of thick paper and a small LED light. First, we rolled the paper into a cylinder and ___4___ the edges with tape. This would be the main body of our lampshade.

Then came the fun part — designing the patterns. I used a small knife to carefully cut out shapes of trees and birds. The teacher reminded us ___5___ too fast because one wrong cut could ruin the whole piece.

After ___6___ our cutting, we placed the LED light inside the cylinder. When the teacher turned off the classroom lights, something ___7___ happened. Beautiful patterns appeared on the walls and ceiling! It looked like a starry forest.

Everyone was excited. We walked around the classroom to admire each ___8___ work. Some classmates had cut flowers, some had cut animals, and one student even created a whole city skyline.

The teacher said this project showed that science and art are not ___9___ separate things. They can work together ___10___ create beauty. I will always remember this wonderful class.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
2. A. principle &emsp;&emsp; B. principal &emsp;&emsp; C. practice &emsp;&emsp; D. problem  
3. A. wonder &emsp;&emsp; B. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; C. wonderfully &emsp;&emsp; D. wondered  
4. A. fix &emsp;&emsp; B. fixing &emsp;&emsp; C. fixes &emsp;&emsp; D. fixed  
5. A. not cut &emsp;&emsp; B. not to cut &emsp;&emsp; C. to not cut &emsp;&emsp; D. cutting not  
6. A. finish &emsp;&emsp; B. finishing &emsp;&emsp; C. finished &emsp;&emsp; D. finishes  
7. A. magic &emsp;&emsp; B. magical &emsp;&emsp; C. magically &emsp;&emsp; D. magician  
8. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. others &emsp;&emsp; C. other's &emsp;&emsp; D. others'  
9. A. complete &emsp;&emsp; B. completely &emsp;&emsp; C. completing &emsp;&emsp; D. completed  
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. by  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 combine...with...  
**【解析】** combine science with art 意为"把科学与艺术结合"，with 为固定介词。to/from/for 不与 combine 构成此搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 名词辨析  
**【解析】** the basic principle 意为"基本原理"，principle 指科学原理。principal 意为"校长/主要的"；practice 意为"练习"；problem 意为"问题"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词  
**【解析】** a wonderful idea 意为"一个极好的想法"，wonderful 为形容词修饰 idea。wonder 为名词/动词；wonderfully 为副词；wondered 为过去式。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（并列谓语）  
**【解析】** and 连接 rolled 和 fixed，时态一致用过去式。fix 为原形；fixing 为现在分词；fixes 为三单。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. not to do）  
**【解析】** remind sb. not to do sth. 意为"提醒某人不要做某事"，not 置于 to 之前。not cut 缺 to；to not cut 语序错误；cutting not 语序错误。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 after + 动名词  
**【解析】** after finishing 意为"完成之后"，after 为介词后接动名词。finish 为原形；finished 为过去式；finishes 为三单。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词  
**【解析】** something magical 意为"神奇的事情"，形容词修饰不定代词时后置。magic 多作名词/形容词（但 magical 更常用于修饰名词/代词）；magically 为副词；magician 为名词"魔术师"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 名词性物主代词/所有格  
**【解析】** each others' work 意为"彼此的作品"，others' 为复数所有格。other 缺所有格；others 无所有格标志；other's 为单数所有格但此处为多人互看。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰形容词  
**【解析】** not completely separate 意为"并非完全分开的"，completely 为副词修饰形容词 separate。complete 为形容词/动词；completing 为现在分词；completed 为过去分词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 不定式表目的  
**【解析】** work together to create beauty 意为"共同合作来创造美"，to create 为不定式表目的。for 后接名词或动名词；with 表方式；by 表手段。故选 C。
