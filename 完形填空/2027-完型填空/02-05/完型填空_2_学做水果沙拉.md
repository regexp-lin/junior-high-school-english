# 中考英语完形填空：周末学做水果沙拉

## 完形填空原文

On Saturday morning, my cousin visited us with a basket of fruit. Mom suggested we make a fruit salad ___1___ lunch.

We washed apples, bananas, and grapes. Mom taught us ___2___ the apples into even pieces so that they would look neat on the plate.

Then we mixed yogurt ___3___ a little honey. I wanted to add much sugar, ___4___ Mom shook her head. She said natural sweetness was ___5___ for our health.

My cousin squeezed lemon juice carefully. The smell was fresh, ___6___ it made everyone in the kitchen feel awake.

When we finally tasted the salad, the flavors balanced well. Dad took a photo ___7___ posted it in our family group chat.

I used to think cooking was ___8___ parents' job. That day I realized teenagers could also create something healthy and ___9___. I decided ___10___ a fruit salad for my friends next time they came over.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. cut &emsp;&emsp; B. cutting &emsp;&emsp; C. to cut &emsp;&emsp; D. cuts
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. only &emsp;&emsp; B. almost &emsp;&emsp; C. even &emsp;&emsp; D. still
9. A. fun &emsp;&emsp; B. funny &emsp;&emsp; C. funnier &emsp;&emsp; D. funniest
10. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈建议我们做水果沙拉当午餐。for lunch 作为午餐。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（teach sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈教我们如何把苹果切成大小均匀的块。teach sb. to do sth. 用不定式 to cut。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（mix A with B）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们把酸奶和少许蜂蜜混合。mix...with... 把……与……混合。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想加很多糖，但妈妈摇了摇头。转折用 but。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说天然的甜味对我们的健康更好。暗含与加糖比较，用 better。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：气味很清新，而且让厨房里每个人都精神一振。并列补充用 and。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸拍了照并发到家庭群。并列动作 took 与 posted 用 and。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我过去以为做饭只是父母的事。only 表示“仅仅”。almost 几乎；even 甚至；still 仍然。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天我意识到青少年也能做出健康又有趣的东西。something 后接形容词作定语后置，fun 表示“有趣的”。funny 多指“滑稽的”。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定下次朋友来家里时再给他们做水果沙拉。decide to do sth. 决定做某事。故选 C。
