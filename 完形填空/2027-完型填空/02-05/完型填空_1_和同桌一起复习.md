# 中考英语完形填空：与同桌的复习小计划

## 完形填空原文

Before the midterm exam, my desk mate Leo and I made a simple study plan. We decided to review English vocabulary ___1___ 7:30 p.m. every Tuesday and Thursday.

At first, we just read words aloud. It was boring, ___2___ we added a game: one person said the Chinese meaning, and the other spelled the word ___3___.

Sometimes we disagreed ___4___ a difficult phrase. Instead of arguing for long, we looked it up in the dictionary ___5___ wrote an example sentence together.

Leo was weak in listening, ___6___ I was worried about grammar mistakes. We traded tips and checked each other's short paragraphs. Little ___7___ little, we felt less stressed.

On exam day, I still felt nervous, ___8___ I remembered our practice. When I saw a word we had reviewed, I smiled ___9___ myself.

After the results came out, both of us improved. Leo said teamwork made studying ___10___ lonely and more effective.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
2. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. because
3. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quicker &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. quickest
4. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. to
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. while
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. little &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. least &emsp;&emsp; D. fewer

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（时间点前用 at）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们决定每周二、四晚上七点半复习英语词汇。具体时刻前用 at。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我们只是大声读单词，很无聊，于是我们加了一个游戏。表结果用 so。but 转折；or 选择；because 原因。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一人说中文意思，另一人快速拼出单词。修饰动词 spelled 用副词 quickly。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（disagree about）
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们会为一个难短语意见不一。disagree about/on sth. 在某事上有分歧。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们查字典并一起写例句。并列动作用 and。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（while 表对比）
**【解析】** 本句意为：Leo 听力较弱，而我担心语法错误。while 可连接两个并列句表对比。and 单纯并列；but 强转折；or 选择。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（little by little）
**【解析】** 本句意为：渐渐地，我们感到压力小了。little by little 渐渐地。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：考试那天我仍紧张，但我想起了我们的练习。转折用 but。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（smile at）
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我看到一个我们复习过的词时，我对自己笑了笑。smile at sb. 对某人微笑。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：Leo 说团队合作让学习不那么孤单、更有效。后有 more effective 并列比较，lonely 的比较级用 less lonely。故选 B。
