# 中考英语完形填空：学做酸奶

## 完形填空原文

My science teacher said yogurt is rich ___1___ helpful bacteria, so I wanted to try making ___2___ at home. I bought plain milk, ___3___ small bottle of yogurt as a starter, and a clean glass jar.

First, I heated the milk ___4___ it became warm but not boiling. After it cooled a little, I mixed in two spoons of yogurt and stirred ___5___. Then I covered the jar with a cloth and left ___6___ in a warm corner.

Six hours later, the milk turned thicker ___7___ I had imagined. It tasted a little sour, ___8___ the texture was smooth. I added ___9___ honey and some fruit, and my parents said it was ___10___ than the yogurt from the store.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. its &emsp;&emsp; C. itself &emsp;&emsp; D. they
3. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although
5. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gentler &emsp;&emsp; C. gently &emsp;&emsp; D. gentlest
6. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. they
7. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. a little &emsp;&emsp; B. little &emsp;&emsp; C. few &emsp;&emsp; D. a few
10. A. fresh &emsp;&emsp; B. fresher &emsp;&emsp; C. freshest &emsp;&emsp; D. the freshest

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：科学老师说酸奶富含有益细菌。be rich in… 表示“富含……”。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：所以我想在家试着做酸奶。指代上文的 yogurt，用 it。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我买了纯牛奶和一小瓶酸奶作为发酵引子。small 以辅音音素开头，泛指“一小瓶”，用 a。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把牛奶加热到温热但不沸腾。until 表示“直到……为止”，符合加热过程描述。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 词性转换（形容词与副词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：加入两勺酸奶后轻轻搅拌。修饰动词 stirred，用副词 gently。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后用布盖住罐子，把它放在温暖的角落。指代 the jar，用 it。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析 / 形容词比较级结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：六小时后，牛奶变得比我想象的更浓稠。thicker 为比较级，后接 than。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尝起来有点酸，但口感很顺滑。前后转折，用 but。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 限定词辨析 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我加了一点蜂蜜和一些水果。honey 不可数，表示“少量”，用 a little。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母说它比店里的酸奶更新鲜。than 提示比较级，用 fresher。故选 B。
