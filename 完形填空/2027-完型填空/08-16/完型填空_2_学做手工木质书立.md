# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工木质书立

## 完形填空原文

My desk was always messy ___1___ heavy books kept falling over. Dad suggested we make wooden bookends together. He bought two small boards and sandpaper ___2___ was very fine.

First, we measured the size and drew a simple pattern ___3___ a pencil. Dad used a saw ___4___ I was not allowed to touch for safety. After the pieces were cut, we painted them blue and white ___5___ they looked like waves.

When the paint dried, Dad helped me fix a metal angle at the back ___6___ the bookends could stand steadily. I placed my favorite novels between them and found my desk ___7___ tidy than yesterday. Dad smiled and said, "Tools should ___8___ used carefully, but practice makes skills ___9___."

I thanked him and promised ___10___ I would keep the desk clean in the new term.

## 选项

1. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
2. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although
6. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until
7. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
8. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. being &emsp;&emsp; C. been &emsp;&emsp; D. to be
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的书桌总是很乱，因为厚书总倒下来。表原因用 because。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他买了两块小木板和很细的砂纸。先行词 sandpaper 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用铅笔画出简单图案。with a pencil 用铅笔。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸用了锯子，出于安全我不准碰它。先行词 saw（锯）为物，从句缺宾语，用 which。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词（目的/结果）
**【解析】** 本句意为：把它们漆成蓝白色，让它们看起来像波浪。so that 表目的“以便、为了”。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：在后面固定金属角码，以便书立站得更稳。so that 表目的。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：发现书桌比昨天更整洁。有 than，用 more tidy（也可用 tidier，选项给 more）。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 被动语态（情态动词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：工具应该小心使用。should 后接动词原形，被动为 should be used。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级与固定表达
**【解析】** 本句意为：但熟能生巧（练习让技能更好）。make + 宾语 + 形容词作宾补，此处暗含进步，用 better。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感谢他并保证新学期会保持桌面整洁。promise 后接 that 从句。故选 B。
