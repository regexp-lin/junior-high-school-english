# 中考英语完形填空：暑假最后的旅行

## 完形填空原文

Three days before school started, my family took ___1___ short trip to a lakeside town. Dad drove ___2___ we could enjoy the mountain views along the highway. When we arrived, the air smelled ___3___ grass and rain, fresher than ___4___ in the city.

We rented bikes and rode around the lake. Mom took photos of the white birds ___5___ were standing in shallow water. At noon, we ate local fish ___6___ was cooked with ginger and green onions. It tasted ___7___ than any dish I had tried before.

In the evening, we sat on the balcony and watched the sunset. I knew this might be ___8___ most relaxing moment of the whole summer, ___9___ I tried to remember every color in the sky. On the way home, I wrote a few lines ___10___ my diary to keep the memory alive.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
3. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
4. A. this &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. it &emsp;&emsp; D. one
5. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what
6. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
7. A. well &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. good
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. the &emsp;&emsp; C. an &emsp;&emsp; D. /
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
10. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们家去湖边小镇短途旅行。short 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸开车，以便我们能在高速路上欣赏山景。so that 表目的。although 让步；unless 条件；until 直到。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：空气闻起来有青草和雨水的味道。smell of 表示“有……的气味”。like 虽可表“像”，但 smell of 更贴切“散发……气味”。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比城里的空气更清新。比较结构中常用 that 指代前文不可数名词 air，避免重复。this/it/one 不如 that 常用于比较。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈给站在浅水里的白鹭拍照。先行词 birds 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们吃了用姜葱烹制的当地鱼。先行词 fish 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道比我以前尝过的任何菜都好。taste 为系动词，后接形容词；有 than，用 better。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 冠词与最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这可能是整个暑假最放松的时刻。形容词最高级 most relaxing 前需加定冠词 the。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：所以我努力记住天空的每一种颜色。表结果用 so。and 并列；but 转折；or 选择。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在自己的日记里写了几行字。表示“写在日记里”用介词 in。on/at/to 不符合搭配。故选 C。
