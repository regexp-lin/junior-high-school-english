# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工新年贺卡

## 完形填空原文

In art class, we made New Year cards ___1___ our families. Ms. Bai gave each student two pieces of thick paper ___2___ different colors.

I folded one sheet ___3___ half and drew a rabbit holding a lantern. ___4___ I was coloring the ears, I accidentally used a brown pencil. Ms. Bai laughed and said mistakes could become ___5___ special part of the design.

She taught us ___6___ cut a small window on the cover so a surprise picture could show inside. I wrote “Happy New Year” in both Chinese ___7___ English, trying ___8___ keep the letters neat.

When I gave the card ___9___ my parents, they hugged me and put it on the shelf. That night, I promised myself ___10___ send a handmade card to my grandparents, too.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
4. A. Until &emsp;&emsp; B. While &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. Although  
5. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
6. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to  
7. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
8. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
10. A. send &emsp;&emsp; B. sending &emsp;&emsp; C. to send &emsp;&emsp; D. sent  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *for* 表对象（为某人）  
**【解析】** *made New Year cards for our families* 表示“给家人做贺卡”。*to* 虽偶见 *make sth. to sb.*，但中考更常见 *for sb.* 表受益对象；*of/with* 不贴切。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *in* 表“以……颜色”  
**【解析】** *paper in different colors* 表示“不同颜色的纸”。*on/at/by* 不用于描述颜色的常见搭配。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *fold...in half*  
**【解析】** *folded one sheet in half* 表示“把一张纸对折”，为常见表达。*into half* 非地道搭配；*on/at* 不与 *fold* 构成此义。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 *while*（当……时）  
**【解析】** *While I was coloring the ears* 表示在给兔子上色期间发生意外，用 *while* 与进行时搭配自然。*Until* 直到；*Unless* 除非；*Although* 让步，语义不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词与可数名词单数  
**【解析】** *become a special part of the design* 表示“成为设计里一个特别的部分”，泛指用 *a*。*special* 以辅音音素开头，不用 *an*；*the* 特指不足；零冠词不搭配单数可数名词 *part*。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** *taught us how to cut a small window* 表示“教我们如何开窗洞”。*what to cut* 语义不清；*when/where to cut* 与操作重点不符。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *both...and...*  
**【解析】** *in both Chinese and English* 为固定搭配。*or/but/so* 不能与 *both* 构成此结构。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*try to do*）  
**【解析】** *trying to keep the letters neat* 表示努力把字写整齐，用不定式 *to keep*。*for/of/with* 不构成 *try to do*。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *give sth. to sb.*  
**【解析】** *gave the card to my parents* 为双宾语结构的常见介词搭配。*for* 虽可见 *buy sth. for sb.*，但 *give* 与 *to* 更标准。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*promise oneself to do*）  
**【解析】** *promised myself to send a handmade card* 用不定式表承诺将要做的事。*send/sending/sent* 不能直接作 *promised myself* 的补足语。故选 C。
