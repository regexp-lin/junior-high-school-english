# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校跨年倒计时准备

## 完形填空原文

The student union decided ___1___ a simple countdown show on the last day of December. My job was ___2___ design the digital clock that would appear on the screen behind the singers.

At first, I worried ___3___ the numbers might not match the music. The tech teacher told me ___4___ test the animation at least three times. ___5___ I stayed after school, he checked my file and pointed out a spelling mistake quietly.

On the rehearsal night, the hall was decorated ___6___ golden balloons. When the practice countdown reached zero, everyone cheered ___7___ loudly that our ears rang a little. The host smiled and said we were ready ___8___ the real midnight moment.

I went home late, ___9___ I could not sleep because I was excited. I told myself ___10___ stay calm and double-check everything the next morning.

## 选项

1. A. hold &emsp;&emsp; B. holding &emsp;&emsp; C. to hold &emsp;&emsp; D. held  
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. whether &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
5. A. While &emsp;&emsp; B. Unless &emsp;&emsp; C. Until &emsp;&emsp; D. Although  
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
7. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. such  
8. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*decide to do*）  
**【解析】** *decided to hold a countdown show* 为固定搭配“决定举办……”，用不定式 *to hold*。*hold/holding/held* 不能直接作 *decide* 的宾语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定式作表语（*My job was to...*）  
**【解析】** *My job was to design the digital clock*，用 *to do* 说明工作内容。*for/of/with* 不能作表语说明职责内容。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 *whether*（是否/会不会）  
**【解析】** *worried whether the numbers might not match* 表示担心“是否对不上”，*whether* 引导宾语从句表不确定性。*that* 无“是否”含义；*what* 需在从句中充当成分；*how* 表方式，不如 *whether* 贴切“担心出差错”。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*tell sb. to do*）  
**【解析】** *told me to test the animation* 为“告诉某人做某事”，用不定式 *to test*。*for/of/with* 不构成此结构。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 *while*  
**【解析】** *While I stayed after school, he checked my file* 表示“在我留校期间，他检查了我的文件”。*Unless* 除非；*Until* 直到；*Although* 让步，语义不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *with* 表“带有、用……装饰”  
**【解析】** *decorated with golden balloons* 表示用金色气球装饰。*decorate in/on/at* 不如 *with* 表示装饰材料常见。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定结构 *so...that...*  
**【解析】** *so loudly that our ears rang* 为结果状语从句。*very/too* 不直接引导 *that* 结果从句；*such* 后应接名词短语（*such a loud cheer that...*）。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *be ready for*  
**【解析】** *ready for the real midnight moment* 表示“为真正的午夜时刻做好准备”。*ready to* 后多接动词；*ready of/with* 非标准搭配。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “回家很晚”与“睡不着因为兴奋”为转折，用 *but*。*and* 顺承；*or* 选择；*so* 结果，逻辑不如 *but*。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*tell oneself to do*）  
**【解析】** *told myself to stay calm* 用不定式表“告诉自己去做”。*for/of/with* 不构成 *tell oneself* 的补足语结构。故选 A。
