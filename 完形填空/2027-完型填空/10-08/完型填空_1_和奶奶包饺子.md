# 中考英语完形填空：和奶奶包饺子

## 完形填空原文

Every time I visit my grandma, she likes ___1___ dumplings for the whole family. This holiday, she invited me to join her in the kitchen for the first time.

Grandma had already prepared the filling — pork mixed ___2___ Chinese cabbage and some seasoning. She showed me how to hold the wrapper in one hand and put a spoonful of filling in the ___3___. Then she folded it carefully and pressed the edges together.

My first dumpling looked quite ugly. It was too fat and couldn't even stand ___4___ on the plate. Grandma laughed and said, "Don't worry. ___5___ you practice more, you'll get better." Her words made me feel ___6___.

After making about twenty dumplings, I finally got the hang of it. My dumplings became neater and ___7___ beautiful. Grandma nodded with satisfaction and said she was proud ___8___ me.

When the dumplings ___9___ ready, the whole family sat around the table. Everyone agreed that homemade dumplings tasted much better ___10___ store-bought ones. I decided to visit Grandma more often and learn more traditional recipes from her.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. makes &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
3. A. middle &emsp;&emsp; B. side &emsp;&emsp; C. bottom &emsp;&emsp; D. top  
4. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. down  
5. A. If &emsp;&emsp; B. Unless &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Until  
6. A. encourage &emsp;&emsp; B. encouraged &emsp;&emsp; C. encouraging &emsp;&emsp; D. to encourage  
7. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. the most  
8. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about  
9. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. were &emsp;&emsp; D. is  
10. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. than  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（like doing）  
**【解析】** like doing sth. 表示"喜欢做某事"（侧重经常性习惯）。此处描述奶奶的习惯，用 making 最合适。make 为原形；makes 为三单；made 为过去式，均不能直接跟在 likes 后作宾语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 mix with  
**【解析】** mix...with... 意为"把……和……混合"，pork mixed with Chinese cabbage 即猪肉和白菜混合的馅。to 表方向；by 表方式；for 表目的，均不构成此搭配。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 名词辨析（常识推理）  
**【解析】** 包饺子时将馅放在饺子皮的"中间"（middle），符合常识。side（旁边）、bottom（底部）、top（顶部）均不符合包饺子的实际操作。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 stand up  
**【解析】** stand up 意为"立起来、站住"，饺子太胖"甚至都不能在盘子上立住"。stand out 意为"突出"；stand off 意为"远离"；stand down 意为"退下"，均不符合语境。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 条件状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "如果你多练习，你会做得更好"，if 引导条件状语从句表假设。unless 意为"除非"，语义相反；although 表让步"虽然"；until 表"直到"，均不符合。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）  
**【解析】** feel 为系动词，后接描述人感受的形容词。encouraged 表"受到鼓舞的"，修饰人的感受。encourage 为动词原形；encouraging 意为"令人鼓舞的"修饰事物；to encourage 为不定式。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 前有 neater（更整齐的），and 连接并列成分，后面也应为比较级 more beautiful。much 修饰比较级但不能单独用于此位置；most 为最高级；the most 需要最高级语境。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be proud of  
**【解析】** be proud of sb. 意为"为某人感到骄傲"，of 为固定介词。be proud for/with/about 均非标准搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 主谓一致与时态  
**【解析】** dumplings 为复数名词，全文为过去时态，be 动词应用 were。are 为现在时复数；was 为过去时单数；is 为现在时单数，均不匹配。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 比较级搭配 than  
**【解析】** much better 为比较级，后接 than 引出比较对象。as 用于同级比较 as...as；like 意为"像"；from 意为"从"，均不与比较级搭配。故选 D。
