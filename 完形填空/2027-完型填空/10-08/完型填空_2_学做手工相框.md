# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工相框

## 完形填空原文

In our art club, the teacher announced that we would make photo frames ___1___ recycled materials. Everyone looked at each other with curiosity because none of us ___2___ done this before.

The teacher handed out cardboard, colorful tape, glue, and small ___3___ of dried flowers. She explained each step patiently and reminded us ___4___ the edges smooth so that we wouldn't cut ourselves.

I decided to make a frame for my family photo. First, I cut the cardboard ___5___ the shape of a rectangle. It was harder than I ___6___ because the cardboard was thick. My classmate Tom lent me his stronger scissors, which helped a lot.

Next, I decorated the frame with blue tape and stuck tiny flowers on the corners. The teacher walked around and gave advice. She told me ___7___ another layer of tape on the back to make it stronger.

After an hour of hard work, I held up my finished frame proudly. It wasn't ___8___, but it was full of love. I planned to give it to my parents ___9___ a holiday gift.

The teacher smiled and said, "The best gifts are ___10___ that come from the heart." Everyone in the class agreed.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
2. A. has &emsp;&emsp; B. have &emsp;&emsp; C. had &emsp;&emsp; D. having  
3. A. piece &emsp;&emsp; B. pieces &emsp;&emsp; C. pair &emsp;&emsp; D. pairs  
4. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. kept &emsp;&emsp; C. keeping &emsp;&emsp; D. to keep  
5. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
6. A. think &emsp;&emsp; B. thought &emsp;&emsp; C. thinking &emsp;&emsp; D. to think  
7. A. add &emsp;&emsp; B. added &emsp;&emsp; C. adding &emsp;&emsp; D. to add  
8. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. perfectly &emsp;&emsp; C. more perfect &emsp;&emsp; D. most perfect  
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. of  
10. A. this &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. these &emsp;&emsp; D. those  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（材料来源）  
**【解析】** from recycled materials 意为"用回收材料（制作）"，from 表示材料来源。in 表在……里；by 表方式或被动施事者；on 表在……上，均不如 from 贴切。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** none of us had done 表示在"老师宣布"之前（过去的过去）"我们都没做过"，需用过去完成时。has 用于第三人称单数现在完成时；have 用于复数现在完成时；having 为现在分词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词单复数  
**【解析】** small pieces of dried flowers 意为"一些小块干花"，pieces 为复数表数量。piece 为单数，前缺冠词；pair（双/对）和 pairs 通常修饰成对的物品如鞋、袜等，不修饰花。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do）  
**【解析】** remind sb. to do sth. 意为"提醒某人做某事"。keep 为原形；kept 为过去式；keeping 为现在分词，均不能用于 remind sb. 后。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 cut...into  
**【解析】** cut...into the shape of 意为"剪成……的形状"，into 表示状态的变化。from 表来源；with 表工具；for 表目的，均不表示"变成"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** than I thought 表示"比我（之前）想的"，全文为过去叙述，thought 为一般过去时。think 为原形；thinking 为现在分词；to think 为不定式，均不能作从句谓语。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell sb. to do sth. 意为"告诉某人做某事"，不定式作宾补。add 为原形；added 为过去式；adding 为现在分词，均不能用于 told me 后。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** wasn't 为系动词否定形式，后接形容词作表语，perfect（完美的）符合语义。perfectly 为副词；more perfect 和 most perfect 用法不当，perfect 本身已表绝对概念，不常用比较级和最高级。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 as 表"作为"  
**【解析】** as a holiday gift 意为"作为假日礼物"。for 可表目的但不如 as 精确表"当作"；like 意为"像"；of 表所属。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 指示代词 those 指代复数名词  
**【解析】** those 在此指代前文的 gifts（复数），意为"那些来自内心的（礼物）"。this/that 为单数；these 指近处事物，此处为一般性陈述用 those 更恰当。故选 D。
