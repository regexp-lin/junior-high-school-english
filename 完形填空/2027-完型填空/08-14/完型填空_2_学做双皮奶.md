# 中考英语完形填空：学做双皮奶

## 完形填空原文

Double-skin milk pudding is ___1___ popular dessert in Guangdong. My aunt taught me ___2___ to make it at home last Sunday. We heated fresh milk ___3___ it began to steam, then poured it into bowls and waited until a thin skin formed on top.

"Don't rush," Aunt said. "Good pudding ___4___ slowly." After we cooled the milk and added beaten egg whites with sugar, the mixture ___5___ steamed over gentle heat. While waiting, I kept asking questions ___6___ I wanted every step to be clear.

Twenty minutes later, the pudding looked smooth ___7___ silk. We tasted it with red beans, ___8___ were cooked the day before. "It's ___9___ than the one I bought last week," I said proudly. Aunt laughed and told me practice would make it ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when
3. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since
4. A. need &emsp;&emsp; B. needs &emsp;&emsp; C. needing &emsp;&emsp; D. needed
5. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. be &emsp;&emsp; D. been
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
7. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
8. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what
9. A. nice &emsp;&emsp; B. nicer &emsp;&emsp; C. nicest &emsp;&emsp; D. the nicest
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. the best

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：双皮奶是广东一种很受欢迎的甜品。popular 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”
**【解析】** 本句意为：姑姑教我在家如何做。teach sb. how to do sth. 故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词（until）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把鲜奶加热直到开始冒蒸汽。until 表示“直到……为止”。when 也可，但 until 更突出过程终点；选项中 until 更贴合“加热到开始冒气”。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 主谓一致与时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：好布丁需要慢慢来。主语 pudding 为不可数名词，一般现在时谓语用 needs。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 被动语态与时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：混合物被小火蒸。叙述过去，被动 was steamed。选项只有 was 可作助动词构成被动（steamed 被省略在理解中）— 等等，我写的是 "the mixture ___5___ steamed" - 需要 was。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直问问题，因为我想把每一步都弄清楚。表原因用 because。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：布丁看起来像丝绸一样顺滑。smooth like silk。as 需接从句；for/of 不搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们配上前一天煮好的红豆。先行词 beans 为物，非限制性定语从句用 which。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：比我上周买的更好吃。有 than，用 nicer。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级与谚语
**【解析】** 本句意为：姑姑笑着说练习会让你做得更好。Practice makes… 此处接比较级 better 表示“越来越好/更出色”。故选 B。
