# 中考英语完形填空：和同学约打羽毛球

## 完形填空原文

Two days before school started, I sent a message ___1___ my classmate Tom. "Would you like to play badminton ___2___ the park tomorrow morning?" I asked. He replied ___3___ once that he would love to, but he needed to buy ___4___ new racket because the old one was broken.

We met at eight o'clock, ___5___ the weather was cool enough for sports. Tom's racket looked light, ___6___ he said it cost less than a movie ticket. While we were playing, a little boy watched us ___7___ and clapped when Tom made a difficult save.

After an hour, we sat on a bench and talked ___8___ our plans for the new term. I realized that simple activities like this could make the end of summer ___9___ meaningful for me. Before leaving, we promised ___10___ each other again on the first weekend of September.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
4. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. more careful &emsp;&emsp; D. more carefully
8. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
10. A. meet &emsp;&emsp; B. meeting &emsp;&emsp; C. to meet &emsp;&emsp; D. met

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我给同学汤姆发了条信息。send a message to sb. 给某人发信息。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：明天早上要不要在公园打羽毛球？in the park 在公园里。at 多指小地点门口；on the park 不常用。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他立刻回复说很愿意。at once 立刻、马上。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他需要买一副新球拍。racket 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们八点见面，因为那时天气够凉爽适合运动。表原因用 because。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：汤姆的球拍看起来很轻，而且他说价格比一张电影票还便宜。补充说明，并列用 and。but 转折；or 选择；so 结果。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小男孩认真地看着我们。修饰动词 watched，用副词 carefully。无比较对象，不用比较级。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们坐在长椅上谈论新学期的计划。talk about 谈论。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：让暑假的结尾对我来说更有意义。暗含与“若不做这些事”对比，meaningful 为多音节形容词，用 more meaningful。most 为最高级；much/very 不直接构成该比较结构。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们约定九月的第一个周末再见面。promise to do sth. 承诺做某事。故选 C。
