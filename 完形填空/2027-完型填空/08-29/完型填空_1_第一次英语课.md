# 中考英语完形填空：第一次英语课

## 完形填空原文

I was looking forward ___1___ the first English class this term. Our new teacher, Mr. Wang, walked in with a bright smile and greeted us ___2___ English. Some students looked confused because he spoke quite ___3___.

He asked us to introduce ___4___ in English one by one. When it was my turn, my face turned red and my voice was so ___5___ that only the front row could hear me. But Mr. Wang nodded kindly and said, "Good job! Don't be afraid ___6___ making mistakes."

Then he played a short English movie clip for us. It was ___7___ a funny story that everyone laughed. He told us that learning English should be ___8___ and interesting, not just about memorizing words.

Before the class ___9___, he gave each of us a small English notebook. "Write down one new word every day," he said. "Small steps ___10___ to big progress." I decided to follow his advice from that day on.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. in
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. faster
4. A. us &emsp;&emsp; B. we &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ourselves
5. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. low &emsp;&emsp; C. high &emsp;&emsp; D. clear
6. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. on
7. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. such &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too
8. A. fun &emsp;&emsp; B. funny &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
9. A. end &emsp;&emsp; B. ends &emsp;&emsp; C. ended &emsp;&emsp; D. ending
10. A. lead &emsp;&emsp; B. leads &emsp;&emsp; C. led &emsp;&emsp; D. leading

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我期待着这学期的第一节英语课。look forward to 表示"期待"，to 为介词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他用英语和我们打招呼。in English 表示"用英语"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些同学看起来很困惑，因为他说得很快。修饰动词 spoke，用副词 fast；由后文"困惑"可知说得快。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 反身代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他让我们逐一用英语做自我介绍。introduce oneself 表示"自我介绍"，主语为 us（宾格对应 ourselves）。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的声音低得只有前排能听到。low 表示"（声音）低的"，符合"声音小、别人听不到"的语境。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要害怕犯错。be afraid of doing sth. 表示"害怕做某事"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** such 与 so 辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是一个非常有趣的故事，以至于每个人都笑了。such + a/an + adj. + n. + that 引导结果状语从句。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学英语应该是有趣的，不只是记单词。fun 表示"有趣的、好玩的"；与 interesting 并列，语义一致。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：在下课之前。before 引导时间状语从句，叙述过去的事件，用一般过去时 ended。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 主谓一致与时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的进步会带来大的进步。此处为一般性陈述（名言），用一般现在时；steps 为复数主语，谓语用原形 lead。故选 A。
