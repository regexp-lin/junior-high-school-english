# 中考英语完形填空：学做酸梅汤

## 完形填空原文

The summer heat was still strong in late August. My grandmother taught me ___1___ traditional sour plum soup to cool down. She said this recipe ___2___ passed down in our family for over fifty years.

We needed dried plums, hawthorn, rock sugar, and a little licorice. Grandmother asked me ___3___ all the ingredients in water for thirty minutes first. "This step is ___4___ important because it helps bring out the flavor," she explained.

After soaking, we boiled everything in a big pot. The kitchen was soon filled ___5___ a sweet and sour smell. I kept ___6___ the pot because I didn't want the soup to boil over. Grandmother told me to turn the heat ___7___ and let it simmer slowly.

An hour later, the soup was ready. I poured it into a glass bottle and put it in the fridge. When my family drank the ___8___ soup that evening, everyone said it tasted ___9___ than the ones sold in shops. I felt ___10___ proud of myself for learning this old family recipe.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made
2. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. had been &emsp;&emsp; D. has been
3. A. soak &emsp;&emsp; B. soaking &emsp;&emsp; C. soaked &emsp;&emsp; D. to soak
4. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. really &emsp;&emsp; C. true &emsp;&emsp; D. truly
5. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. in
6. A. watch &emsp;&emsp; B. watched &emsp;&emsp; C. watching &emsp;&emsp; D. to watch
7. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. off &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. down
8. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. hot &emsp;&emsp; C. warm &emsp;&emsp; D. cool
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
10. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. really &emsp;&emsp; C. true &emsp;&emsp; D. truly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶教我做传统酸梅汤来消暑。teach sb. to do sth. 表示"教某人做某事"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态（过去完成时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说这个配方在我们家已经传了五十多年了。said 为过去时，配方传承从更早以前开始，用过去完成时 had been。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶让我先把所有材料在水里泡三十分钟。ask sb. to do sth. 表示"要求某人做某事"。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这一步非常重要。修饰形容词 important，需用副词 really。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房很快充满了酸甜的气味。be filled with 表示"充满"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直看着锅。keep doing sth. 表示"持续做某事"，后接动名词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶叫我把火调小，慢慢炖。turn down 表示"调小（火/音量）"。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天晚上家人喝了冰凉的汤。由前文放入冰箱可知汤是冰凉的，cold 最合适。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都说比店里卖的好喝。than 提示用比较级，good 的比较级为 better。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我真的为自己学会了这个老配方而自豪。修饰形容词 proud，需用副词 really。故选 B。
