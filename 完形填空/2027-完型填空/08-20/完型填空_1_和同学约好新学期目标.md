# 中考英语完形填空：和同学约好新学期目标

## 完形填空原文

On the first morning of the new term, my friend Leo and I walked to school together. We talked ___1___ the goals we hoped to reach this semester. "I want to improve my physics grades," he said, "___2___ I plan to ask questions bravely in class."

I told him I would read English aloud ___3___ twenty minutes every day. We wrote our promises on ___4___ small card and put it in our pencil cases. "If we feel lazy, the card ___5___ remind us," Leo laughed.

During break time, we met two more classmates ___6___ shared similar plans. We decided to form ___7___ study group that would meet twice a week. I believe teamwork can make goals ___8___ to achieve than working alone.

Before the bell rang, we high-fived each other and said, "No giving up ___9___ we reach the midterm." That promise sounded simple, ___10___ it gave me strength for the busy days ahead.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
5. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. should &emsp;&emsp; C. must &emsp;&emsp; D. will
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. what
7. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
8. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. easiest &emsp;&emsp; D. the easiest
9. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. after &emsp;&emsp; D. before
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们谈论希望这学期达成的目标。talk about 谈论。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想提高物理成绩，所以我计划课上勇敢提问。表结果用 so。because 表原因在前；although 让步；unless 条件。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词（时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我每天大声读英语二十分钟。for + 时间段表示持续。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把承诺写在一张小卡片上。small 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 情态动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果我们偷懒，卡片会提醒我们。表示将会发生的作用，用 will。can 可能；should 应该；must 必须。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：课间我们又遇到两个有类似计划的同学。先行词 classmates 指人，从句缺主语，用 who。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们决定成立一个学习小组。study 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：团队合作让目标比单打独斗更容易实现。有 than，用 easier。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词（until）
**【解析】** 本句意为：期中之前不放弃。until 表示“直到……才/为止”。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那句承诺听起来简单，但它给了我力量。转折用 but。故选 B。
