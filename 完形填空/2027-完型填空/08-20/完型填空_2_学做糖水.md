# 中考英语完形填空：学做糖水

## 完形填空原文

Sweet soup is a traditional dessert ___1___ can cool you down in hot weather. Grandma taught me ___2___ cook red bean soup with dried tangerine peel. First, we soaked the beans overnight ___3___ they became softer.

The next day, we boiled them over low heat ___4___ about an hour. Grandma stirred the pot from time to time ___5___ the beans would not stick to the bottom. When rock sugar was added, the kitchen ___6___ by a sweet smell.

I tasted a spoonful and said it was ___7___ than the dessert shop's version because it was less sweet. Grandma nodded and told me good food ___8___ made with care, not hurry. I promised ___9___ I would learn one new sweet soup each month. That evening, we shared the soup ___10___ the whole family.

## 选项

1. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
5. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. as if &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as
6. A. filled &emsp;&emsp; B. was filled &emsp;&emsp; C. fills &emsp;&emsp; D. is filling
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well
8. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. be &emsp;&emsp; D. is being
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：糖水是传统甜品，能在炎热天气里解暑。先行词 dessert 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶教我做陈皮红豆沙。teach sb. to do sth. 教某人做某事。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词（until）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把豆子泡一夜直到变软。until 表示“直到……为止”。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词（时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二天用小火煮了大约一小时。for + 时间段。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶不时搅拌，以便豆子不会粘锅底。so that 引导目的状语从句，后接否定表示“以免/防止”。even though 尽管；as if 好像；as long as 只要。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 被动语态与时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房充满了甜香。be filled with 充满；叙述过去用 was filled。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：比甜品店的版本更好，因为不那么甜。有 than，用 better。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 被动语态与时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：好食物应用心慢慢做，而不是赶工。主语 food 与 make 为被动；陈述道理用一般现在时 is made。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我保证每个月学一款新糖水。promise 后接 that 从句。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：那晚我们全家一起喝糖水。share sth. with sb. 与某人分享某物。故选 C。
