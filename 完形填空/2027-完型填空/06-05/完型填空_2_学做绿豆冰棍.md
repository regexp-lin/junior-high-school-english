# 中考英语完形填空：学做绿豆冰棍

## 完形填空原文

The weather has become ___1___ and hotter these days. My grandmother decided to teach me to make green bean popsicles at home. She said homemade ones were ___2___ than the ones in shops.

We soaked green beans in water ___3___ one night. The next morning, I found the beans had become much ___4___. Grandma put them into a pot and added enough water.

She boiled the beans ___5___ they became very soft. Then she added some sugar and stirred the mixture ___6___. "Don't add too ___7___ sugar," she reminded me. "A little sweetness is just right."

We poured the green bean soup into popsicle molds and put ___8___ in the freezer. I checked the freezer every hour because I was too excited ___9___ wait.

Six hours later, the popsicles were ___10___ ready. They tasted cool and sweet — the perfect summer treat!

## 选项

1. A. hot &emsp;&emsp; B. hotter &emsp;&emsp; C. hottest &emsp;&emsp; D. the hottest
2. A. healthy &emsp;&emsp; B. healthier &emsp;&emsp; C. healthiest &emsp;&emsp; D. the healthiest
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. on
4. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. bigger &emsp;&emsp; C. biggest &emsp;&emsp; D. the biggest
5. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. because
6. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. slower &emsp;&emsp; D. slowest
7. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. few &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. little
8. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. they &emsp;&emsp; D. its
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. at
10. A. finally &emsp;&emsp; B. final &emsp;&emsp; C. near &emsp;&emsp; D. already

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（and 连接两个比较级表渐变）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这几天天气越来越热。"比较级 + and + 比较级"表示"越来越……"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than 提示）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说自制的比店里卖的更健康。than 提示用比较级 healthier。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（for + 时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把绿豆在水中浸泡了一晚上。for 后接时间段，表持续时间。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（much + 比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二天早上，我发现豆子变大了很多。much 修饰比较级 bigger。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（until 直到）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她煮绿豆直到它们变得很软。"until"表示"直到"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后她加了一些糖，慢慢搅拌。修饰动词 stirred 用副词 slowly。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定代词（too much + 不可数名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要加太多糖。sugar 为不可数名词，用 much 修饰。"too much"表示"太多"。many/few 修饰可数名词。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（them 指代复数名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把绿豆汤倒入冰棍模具并放入冰箱。them 指代复数名词 popsicle molds（里面的内容物）。it 指单数；they 为主格。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（too...to... 太……以至于不能）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我每小时都去查看冰箱因为太兴奋了等不了。"too + adj. + to do"表示"太……而不能……"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（finally 终于）
**【解析】** 本句意为：六小时后，冰棍终于做好了。finally 为副词表示"终于"，修饰形容词 ready。final 为形容词。故选 A。
