# 中考英语完形填空：帮班主任搬新书

## 完形填空原文

On Monday morning, boxes of new workbooks arrived at the office. Our head teacher, Ms. Lin, needed help ___1___ them to the classroom on the third floor.

I volunteered with two other boys. The boxes were heavier ___2___ we thought, so we carried one box together at first.

In the stairway, my foot slipped a little, ___3___ I didn't drop anything. Ms. Lin walked behind us and reminded us ___4___ the steps slowly.

When we finally placed the books on the desk, we were out of breath ___5___ happy. Ms. Lin thanked us and offered ___6___ some warm water.

Later, she asked me ___7___ distribute the books. I called names clearly and tried not to make ___8___ mistakes.

That afternoon, my arms ached, ___9___ I felt proud. Helping teachers is not ___10___ about getting praise; it is also about responsibility.

## 选项

1. A. bring &emsp;&emsp; B. bringing &emsp;&emsp; C. to bring &emsp;&emsp; D. brought
2. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. then
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. climb &emsp;&emsp; B. climbing &emsp;&emsp; C. to climb &emsp;&emsp; D. climbed
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours
7. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped
8. A. some &emsp;&emsp; B. any &emsp;&emsp; C. many &emsp;&emsp; D. much
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. only &emsp;&emsp; B. almost &emsp;&emsp; C. even &emsp;&emsp; D. still

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（need sth. to do / need to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：林老师需要人帮忙把书搬到三楼教室。need help to do sth. 需要帮忙去做，用不定式 to bring。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级结构（more...than / -er than）
**【解析】** 本句意为：箱子比我们想象的更重。heavy 的比较级 heavier 与 than 搭配。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：楼梯上我的脚滑了一下，但我没掉东西。转折用 but。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：林老师走在后面，提醒我们慢慢爬楼梯。remind sb. to do sth. 用 to climb。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们上气不接下气，但很开心。转折用 but。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析（人称代词宾格）
**【解析】** 本句意为：林老师向我们道谢并给我们倒了温水。offer sb. sth. 中 sb. 用宾格 us。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：后来她让我帮忙发书。ask sb. to do sth. 用 to help。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 限定词辨析（否定句中的 any）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我清楚地点名，尽量不犯任何错误。否定句中常用 any mistakes。some 多用于肯定；many/much 与语境不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天下午我的胳膊酸痛，但我很自豪。转折用 but。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助老师不仅仅是为了得到表扬。not only about 与后文 also about 呼应。故选 A。
