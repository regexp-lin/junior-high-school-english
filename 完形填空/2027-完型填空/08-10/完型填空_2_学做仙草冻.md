# 中考英语完形填空：学做仙草冻

## 完形填空原文

Grass jelly is a cool dessert ___1___ many people enjoy on summer nights. Grandma let me watch ___2___ she cooked the dark liquid from herbs. The mixture ___3___ stirred slowly until it became thick.

After it cooled, we cut it ___4___ small cubes and added honey. My brother said the taste was ___5___ than the canned jelly, ___6___ it was fresher. I helped wash the pots ___7___ wiped the table.

Grandma packed a box for our neighbor ___8___ daughter loves sweet snacks. "Sharing makes food ___9___ sweeter," she smiled. I think family recipes are ___10___ valuable gifts than anything bought from shops.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
2. A. while &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. if
3. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. should be &emsp;&emsp; D. has been
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well
6. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. which
9. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. felt &emsp;&emsp; C. feeling &emsp;&emsp; D. feels
10. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：仙草冻是很多人在夏夜喜欢的清凉甜品。先行词 dessert 指物，从句缺宾语，用 which。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶让我看着她如何把草药煮成深色液体。while 当……时候，引导时间状语从句。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：混合物被慢慢搅拌直到变稠。mixture 与 stir 为被动，叙述过去，用 was stirred。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配 cut...into...
**【解析】** 本句意为：冷却后我们把它切成小块并加蜂蜜。cut...into... 把……切成……。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我哥哥说味道比罐装果冻更好……。than 提示用 better。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：……因为它更新鲜。表原因用 because。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我帮忙洗锅并擦桌子。helped 与 wiped 并列，用 and。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶给邻居装了一盒，邻居的女儿爱吃甜食。先行词 neighbor，从句中 daughter 需用 whose。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 使役动词 make 的用法
**【解析】** 本句意为：分享让食物尝起来更甜。make + 宾语 + 动词原形作宾补，即 make food feel sweeter，此处用 feel。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我认为家传食谱比商店里买的任何东西都更有价值。than 提示用 more valuable。故选 B。
