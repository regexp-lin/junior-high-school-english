# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工灯笼

## 完形填空原文

To celebrate the Mid-Autumn Festival, our class decided ___1___ traditional lanterns. The teacher brought red paper, bamboo sticks, glue, and string. Everyone was excited to start.

The teacher drew the steps on the blackboard and explained them ___2___. First, we cut the red paper into a large rectangle. Then we folded it in half and made a series of cuts along the folded edge. "Be careful not to cut all the way ___3___," the teacher warned.

When we unfolded the paper, it had many parallel slits. I rolled it into a cylinder and glued the edges ___4___. A bamboo stick was placed inside as a handle. My lantern took shape quickly, ___5___ it didn't look very round at first.

Li Wei, who sat beside me, showed me a trick. He pressed the top gently so that the middle part would ___6___ outward. I tried it, and the lantern immediately looked ___7___!

Finally, we tied a short piece of string at the bottom and attached a small LED light ___8___ it. When we turned ___9___ the lights in the classroom, all the lanterns glowed softly in the dark. The scene was so beautiful that even the teacher took out her phone to take ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made
2. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. clearer &emsp;&emsp; C. clearly &emsp;&emsp; D. clearest
3. A. across &emsp;&emsp; B. along &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. over
4. A. apart &emsp;&emsp; B. together &emsp;&emsp; C. away &emsp;&emsp; D. alone
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. push &emsp;&emsp; B. pushed &emsp;&emsp; C. pushes &emsp;&emsp; D. pushing
7. A. much good &emsp;&emsp; B. much well &emsp;&emsp; C. much better &emsp;&emsp; D. more good
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. off &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. down
10. A. photo &emsp;&emsp; B. photos &emsp;&emsp; C. photoes &emsp;&emsp; D. a photo

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班决定制作传统灯笼。decide to do sth. 表示"决定做某事"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师在黑板上画了步骤并清楚地解释了它们。修饰动词 explained，需用副词 clearly。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：注意不要把纸完全剪穿。cut all the way through 表示"完全剪穿"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把它卷成圆柱形，把边缘粘在一起。glue ... together 表示"把……粘在一起"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的灯笼很快成形了，但一开始看起来不太圆。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 情态动词后接动词原形
**【解析】** 本句意为：这样中间部分就会向外鼓出来。would 后接动词原形 push。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：灯笼立刻好看多了！much 修饰比较级 better，good 的比较级为 better。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：在底部系一根短绳并在上面装上小 LED 灯。attach ... to ... 表示"把……系在/附在……上"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们关掉教室的灯时。turn off 表示"关掉"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词单复数
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师拿出手机拍照。take photos 为固定搭配，表示"拍照"，用复数。故选 B。
