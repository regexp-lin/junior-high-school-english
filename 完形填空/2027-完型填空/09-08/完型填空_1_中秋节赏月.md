# 中考英语完形填空：中秋节赏月

## 完形填空原文

The Mid-Autumn Festival is one of the most important ___1___ festivals in China. This year, our family celebrated it at my grandparents' house in the countryside.

After dinner, we carried a small table and several chairs to the yard. Grandmother had prepared mooncakes, fruit, and tea ___2___ the table. The night was clear, and we could see ___3___ of stars in the sky.

As the moon rose ___4___ above the rooftops, everyone became quiet. It was so round and bright that it seemed close enough to ___5___. My little brother pointed at it and asked, "Does Chang'e ___6___ live there?" We all laughed.

Grandfather told us the legend of Chang'e and Hou Yi. I had heard the story many times before, ___7___ I still enjoyed listening to it under the moonlight. His voice was gentle, and the story sounded even more ___8___ than usual.

Later, we ate mooncakes and shared our wishes. Mother said she hoped everyone in the family would stay ___9___ and happy. I silently wished to do ___10___ in my studies this year.

That night, the moon followed us all the way home through the car window. It was truly a memorable festival.

## 选项

1. A. tradition &emsp;&emsp; B. traditions &emsp;&emsp; C. traditional &emsp;&emsp; D. traditionally
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. under
3. A. thousand &emsp;&emsp; B. thousands &emsp;&emsp; C. a thousand &emsp;&emsp; D. two thousands
4. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. quick &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
5. A. touch &emsp;&emsp; B. touched &emsp;&emsp; C. touching &emsp;&emsp; D. touches
6. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. reality &emsp;&emsp; C. really &emsp;&emsp; D. realize
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. beauty &emsp;&emsp; B. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. beautifully &emsp;&emsp; D. beautify
9. A. health &emsp;&emsp; B. healthy &emsp;&emsp; C. healthily &emsp;&emsp; D. healthier
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：中秋节是中国最重要的传统节日之一。修饰名词 festivals，需用形容词 traditional。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶在桌上准备了月饼、水果和茶。on the table 表示"在桌子上"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 数量表达
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们可以看到天空中成千上万的星星。thousands of 表示"数千的"，为固定搭配。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当月亮慢慢升上屋顶。修饰动词 rose，需用副词 slowly。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它又圆又亮，好像近得可以触摸到。enough to do sth. 表示"足够……去做"，to 后接动词原形。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：嫦娥真的住在上面吗？修饰动词 live，需用副词 really。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前听过很多遍这个故事，但在月光下听仍然很享受。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：故事听起来比平时更美。sounded 为系动词，后接形容词；than 提示用比较级，beautiful 的比较级为 more beautiful。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说她希望家里每个人都保持健康快乐。stay 为系动词，后接形容词作表语；healthy 表示"健康的"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词/副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我默默许愿今年学习上做得更好。暗含"比之前更好"的比较，well 的比较级为 better。故选 C。
