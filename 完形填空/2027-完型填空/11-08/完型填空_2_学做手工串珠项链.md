# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工串珠项链

## 完形填空原文

In our school craft club, the leader showed us ___1___ to make a simple beaded necklace. She placed tiny glass beads, a string, and a clasp ___2___ the table.

“Thread the string ___3___ the needle first,” she said. I tried several times, ___4___ the needle was too thin for my shaking hands.

My partner Anna lent me ___5___ needle with a bigger eye. With her help, I finally passed the string through the beads one ___6___ one.

When the necklace was long enough, we tied a strong knot and fixed the clasp. I put it around my neck and looked at ___7___ in the mirror. The colors looked even ___8___ under the classroom lights.

Our teacher took a photo of ___9___ work and said it could be displayed ___10___ the school art festival next week.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. through &emsp;&emsp; B. across &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. along  
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
5. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself  
6. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
7. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. myself  
8. A. bright &emsp;&emsp; B. brighter &emsp;&emsp; C. brightest &emsp;&emsp; D. more brighter  
9. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”（how to make）  
**【解析】** showed us how to make... 中 how 填入空白，与后文 to make 构成 how to make，表示“如何做”。what to make 偏重“做什么”；which/when 与制作步骤语境不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 on（在桌面上）  
**【解析】** on the table 表示平放在桌上。in/at/by 不符合常见摆放描述。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 through（穿过内部）  
**【解析】** thread the string through the needle 表示“线穿过针眼”，用 through。across 横穿表面；over 越过；along 沿着。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 因果连词 because  
**【解析】** “试了好几次”是因为“针太细手又抖”，因果关系用 because。although 让步；unless 除非；if 条件。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词 her  
**【解析】** lent me her needle，needle 为名词，前用形容词性物主代词 her。she 主格；hers 名词性物主代词后不接名词；herself 反身代词。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 one by one  
**【解析】** one by one 表示“一个接一个”。with/for/to 不构成此短语。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 反身代词 myself  
**【解析】** looked at myself in the mirror 表示照镜子看自己。I/me/my 不能表达“自己”。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（even 修饰比较级）  
**【解析】** even brighter 表示“甚至更亮”，even 常修饰比较级。bright 原级；brightest 最高级缺 the/范围；more brighter 重复比较级，错误。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词 our  
**【解析】** our work 修饰名词 work。we/us 不能作定语；ours 后不接名词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 at（at the festival）  
**【解析】** at the school art festival 表示“在艺术节上/活动期间”，常用 at。in/on/to 搭配不如 at 常见。故选 C。
