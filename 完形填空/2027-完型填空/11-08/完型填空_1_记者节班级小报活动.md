# 中考英语完形填空：记者节班级小报活动

## 完形填空原文

November 8th is Journalists’ Day in China. This year, our class decided ___1___ a mini English newspaper to practice writing and interviewing skills.

Our teacher divided us into groups. I was responsible ___2___ interviewing two students about their reading habits. I felt nervous, ___3___ my teammates promised to help me.

Before the interview, I wrote down my questions ___4___ a notebook. While I was talking to my classmates, I recorded their answers ___5___ my phone so that I would not miss any details.

Later, we designed the layout on the computer. Some articles ___6___ by Li Ming, and the photos ___7___ by our art monitor. When everything was ready, we printed ___8___ copies for each student in our grade.

During the break, many students stopped ___9___ our newspaper and asked when the next issue ___10___. I realized that good news stories come from careful listening and teamwork.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
6. A. types &emsp;&emsp; B. typed &emsp;&emsp; C. were typed &emsp;&emsp; D. was typed  
7. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. took &emsp;&emsp; C. were taken &emsp;&emsp; D. were taking  
8. A. hundred &emsp;&emsp; B. hundreds &emsp;&emsp; C. hundreds of &emsp;&emsp; D. hundred of  
9. A. read &emsp;&emsp; B. reading &emsp;&emsp; C. to read &emsp;&emsp; D. reads  
10. A. comes out &emsp;&emsp; B. came out &emsp;&emsp; C. would come out &emsp;&emsp; D. has come out  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide to make... 不定式作宾语。make/making/made 不符合 decide 搭配。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be responsible for  
**【解析】** be responsible for... 表示“对……负责”。of/with/about 不构成此固定搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “紧张”与“队友答应帮忙”为转折。and 并列；or 选择；so 结果，均不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 in（在笔记本里）  
**【解析】** write questions in a notebook。on 多指写在封面/表面；at/by 不合适。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with（用……工具）  
**【解析】** recorded their answers with my phone 表示“用手机录”。in/on/for 搭配不自然。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态（主谓一致）  
**【解析】** articles 为复数，与 type 为被动关系，用 were typed。types 为主动错误；typed 缺 be；was typed 单复数不符。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** photos 与 take 为被动“被拍摄”，过去时用 were taken。take/took 为主动；were taking 为过去进行主动。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定表达 hundreds of  
**【解析】** hundreds of copies 表示“数百份”。hundred 前无具体数词时不用单数表概数；hundred of 错误；hundreds 需加 of。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（stop to do 停下来去做）  
**【解析】** stop to read 表示“停下来去读我们的报纸”。stop doing 表停止正在做的事，与“驻足阅读”语境不符。read/reads 形式不对。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句时态（过去将来时）  
**【解析】** 主句为 asked（过去时），宾语从句询问“下一期何时出版”，从过去看将来，用 would come out。comes out 为一般现在时，时态不呼应；came out 表已发生，与“下一期”不符；has come out 为现在完成，也不符合间接引语中的将来含义。故选 C。
