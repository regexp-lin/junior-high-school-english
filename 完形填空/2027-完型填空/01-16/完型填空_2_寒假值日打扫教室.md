# 中考英语完形填空：寒假值日打扫教室

## 完形填空原文

Last Monday was our first day back at school after the winter holiday. Our class had ___1___ duty in the afternoon. The classroom felt ___2___ because nobody had used it for two weeks. Dust covered the desks, and some old papers ___3___ on the floor near the door. Outside in the hallway, students' voices sounded cheerful because everyone was happy to see friends again.

Our monitor divided us ___4___ four groups. We borrowed tools from the office and opened the windows to let fresh air in, though the winter wind made our fingers a little cold. I was in Group 3, ___5___ for cleaning the windows. I carried a bucket of warm water and a cloth, but the glass was ___6___ than I expected. Jamie climbed ___7___ a chair carefully and reached the top corners.

While working, we talked about our vacation stories. The ___8___ in the room became warmer, not only because of the sunshine, ___9___ because of our teamwork. When we finished, the teacher praised us and said, "You ___10___ set a good example for the younger students."

## 选项

1. A. cleaning &emsp;&emsp; B. cooking &emsp;&emsp; C. reading &emsp;&emsp; D. shopping
2. A. empty &emsp;&emsp; B. noisy &emsp;&emsp; C. crowded &emsp;&emsp; D. modern
3. A. flew &emsp;&emsp; B. lay &emsp;&emsp; C. grew &emsp;&emsp; D. hid
4. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. ready &emsp;&emsp; C. responsible &emsp;&emsp; D. famous
6. A. cleaner &emsp;&emsp; B. dirtier &emsp;&emsp; C. newer &emsp;&emsp; D. wider
7. A. onto &emsp;&emsp; B. across &emsp;&emsp; C. below &emsp;&emsp; D. beside
8. A. weather &emsp;&emsp; B. atmosphere &emsp;&emsp; C. habit &emsp;&emsp; D. tradition
9. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. if
10. A. never &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. truly &emsp;&emsp; D. suddenly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词/形容词搭配辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下午我们班有“大扫除/值日”任务。A 项 cleaning duty 表示“卫生值日”；B 项 cooking 与教室场景不符；C、D 项与语境无关。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：教室感觉很“空荡/冷清”，因为两周没人用。A 项 empty 可指空间与气氛上的“空”；B 项 noisy 与无人使用矛盾；C 项 crowded 相反；D 项 modern 与后文灰尘多不呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些旧纸“躺/散落”在门边地板上。A 项 flew 过于夸张；B 项 lay 是 lie（位于）的过去式，表示平放；C 项 grew 不合理；D 项 hid 与“在地板上显眼处”不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：班长把我们分成四组。divide...into... 为固定搭配，表示“把……分成……”。A、B、D 项不能与 divide 构成此义。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在第三组，“负责”擦窗户。be responsible for 表示“对……负责”。A 项 late for 迟到；B 项 ready for 准备好；D 项 famous for 因……著名。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但玻璃比我想象的更“脏”。A 项 cleaner 与擦窗努力矛盾；B 项 dirtier 符合久未打扫；C、D 项与擦拭难度关联弱。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：Jamie 小心地爬到椅子上。climb onto 表示“爬到……上面”。B 项 across 横穿；C 项 below 在下面；D 项 beside 在旁边。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：房间里的“气氛”变得更温暖。A 项 weather 指天气，通常不说房间里 weather；B 项 atmosphere 可指氛围；C 项 habit 习惯；D 项 tradition 传统。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（固定结构）
**【解析】** 本句意为：不仅因为阳光，“而且”因为团队合作。not only...but (also)... 为固定搭配。A、B、D 项不能与此结构搭配。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们“确实”给学弟学妹树立了好榜样。A 项 never 从不；B 项 hardly 几乎不；C 项 truly 真正地、确实；D 项 suddenly 突然。表扬语气用 truly 最贴切。故选 C。
