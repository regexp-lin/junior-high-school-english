# 中考英语完形填空：学做姜饼人

## 完形填空原文

My aunt runs a small bakery, and last Saturday she invited me ___1___ her kitchen to learn how to bake gingerbread men. She had already prepared flour, brown sugar, butter, eggs, and ginger powder. “Follow my steps ___2___,” she said with a smile.

First, we mixed the dry ingredients ___3___ a big bowl. Then we added butter and eggs ___4___ the mixture became smooth. The dough smelled sweet and a little spicy, ___5___ made me hungry. After we chilled the dough for thirty minutes, my aunt let me ___6___ it flat with a rolling pin.

The most interesting part was cutting ___7___ little people shapes with cookie cutters. We placed them on a baking tray and put the tray ___8___ the oven. While we were waiting, my aunt told me ___9___ stories about her first year in the bakery. When a bell rang, I jumped up ___10___ opened the oven door carefully.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
2. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. more careful &emsp;&emsp; D. most carefully  
3. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
4. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
6. A. roll &emsp;&emsp; B. rolling &emsp;&emsp; C. to roll &emsp;&emsp; D. rolled  
7. A. off &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. down  
8. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
9. A. funny &emsp;&emsp; B. funnier &emsp;&emsp; C. fun &emsp;&emsp; D. funniest  
10. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. and  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *into* 表“进入……里面”  
**【解析】** *invite me into her kitchen* 表示“邀请我进入她的厨房”。*in* 少“进入”的动态感；*invite sb. to* 后常接地点时需 *to the kitchen* 也可，但 *into* 更强调“走进厨房内部”；*for* 不符合搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 *Follow*，用副词 *carefully*。*careful* 为形容词；比较级/最高级无比较对象。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *in* 与容器搭配  
**【解析】** *in a big bowl* 表示“在大碗里”。*on the bowl* 表在碗上；*at/by* 不自然。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词 *until*  
**【解析】** 持续加入黄油和鸡蛋“直到”混合物变顺滑，用 *until*。*when* 不如 *until* 准确；*if/because* 逻辑不对。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 关系代词引导非限制性定语从句  
**【解析】** 指代前面整句话内容，用 *which*。*what* 不引导此类定语从句；*who/whose* 指人，不适用。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 使役动词 *let* 后接动词原形  
**【解析】** *let me roll it flat*，*let sb. do sth.*。其余形式不符合 *let* 的用法。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 *cut out*  
**【解析】** *cut out little people shapes* 表示“剪/切出人形”。*cut off* 表切断；*cut up* 表切碎；*cut down* 表砍倒，均不符合。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *in the oven*  
**【解析】** *put...in the oven* 为固定说法。*on/at/to* 不符合“放入烤箱烘烤”。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词作定语（*fun stories*）  
**【解析】** *fun stories* 表示“有趣的故事”，*fun* 作形容词用。*funny* 偏重“滑稽”；比较级/最高级无比较范围。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表顺承  
**【解析】** *jumped up* 与 *opened the door* 为连续动作，用 *and*。*so/or/but* 逻辑不符。故选 D。
