# 中考英语完形填空：和同学打雪仗

## 完形填空原文

After a heavy snow, our schoolyard turned ___1___ a soft white playground. As soon as the PE teacher allowed us ___2___ go outside, my classmates and I ran ___3___ the snow cheering. Someone suggested ___4___ a snowball fight, and everyone agreed excitedly.

We divided ___5___ into two teams and built small snow walls for cover. The rules were simple: if ___6___ was hit on the arm or leg, that player had to sit out for one minute. I tried to hide ___7___ a pine tree, but Tom still found me. A snowball flew past my ear ___8___ landed on the ground behind me.

We laughed until our faces turned red, ___9___ we didn’t feel cold at all. On the way back to the classroom, we promised ___10___ help each other clean our wet gloves after class.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. through &emsp;&emsp; B. across &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. over  
4. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. having &emsp;&emsp; C. to have &emsp;&emsp; D. had  
5. A. us &emsp;&emsp; B. ourselves &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
6. A. anyone &emsp;&emsp; B. someone &emsp;&emsp; C. everyone &emsp;&emsp; D. no one  
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. behind &emsp;&emsp; D. under  
8. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
10. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *turn into*  
**【解析】** *turn into* 表示“变成……”。*turn in/on/at* 无此义。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*allow sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *allow us to go* 为不定式作宾补。*allow* 不接 *for/of/with* 此类结构表达“允许做某事”。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 *through*（从内部穿过）  
**【解析】** *run through the snow* 表示在雪中穿行。*across* 强调表面横穿；*into* 表进入；*over* 表越过，均不如 *through* 贴切。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动名词作宾语（*suggest doing*）  
**【解析】** *suggest* 后接动名词作宾语，*suggested having a snowball fight*。不定式、原形或过去式均不符合 *suggest* 的常见用法。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 反身代词  
**【解析】** *divided ourselves into two teams* 表示“把我们（自己）分成两队”，主语与宾语为同一群体，用反身代词 *ourselves*。*us* 虽可理解，但标准表达常用 *ourselves* 表“自我分组”。*our/ours* 不能作宾语。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析（*if anyone...*）  
**【解析】** *if anyone was hit...* 表示“如果有人（任何人）被击中……”，*anyone* 常用于条件句中表泛指。*someone* 偏指“某个人”，规则表述不如 *anyone* 严谨；*everyone* 表示“每个人都”，逻辑不通；*no one* 语义相反。句首 *if* 已给出，小写 *anyone* 作从句主语。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词表方位  
**【解析】** *hide behind a pine tree* 表示躲在松树后面。*in/on/under* 与“躲藏观察敌情”的常见画面相比，*behind* 最合理。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表顺承  
**【解析】** “雪球飞过耳边”与“落在身后地上”为顺承并列，用 *and*。*but* 表转折；*or* 表选择；*so* 表结果。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “笑到脸红”与“一点也不觉得冷”为转折对比，用 *but*。*because* 表原因，与后句逻辑颠倒；*if/although* 不合适。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*promise to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *promise to help* 为不定式作宾语。其余形式不符合 *promise* 的用法。故选 C。
