# 中考英语完形填空：新学期班会

## 完形填空原文

Our class held the first meeting of the new term on Thursday afternoon. The head teacher asked each of us ___1___ about our goals for the coming year. I felt a little ___2___ when it was my turn to speak.

I stood up and said, "I want to improve my English, ___3___ in speaking." Some classmates nodded because they had the same wish. The teacher ___4___ us to write down three goals and stick them on the wall.

Li Hua, the class monitor, ___5___ a plan for group study. He suggested that we ___6___ study groups so that everyone could help each other. Most students agreed ___7___ his idea right away.

Before the meeting ended, Ms. Wang said, "Remember, a goal without a plan is just ___8___ wish. Work hard and you ___9___ achieve anything." Her words ___10___ us deeply. We left the classroom feeling full of energy and hope.

## 选项

1. A. think &emsp;&emsp; B. thinking &emsp;&emsp; C. thought &emsp;&emsp; D. to think
2. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. nervously &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
3. A. simply &emsp;&emsp; B. especially &emsp;&emsp; C. luckily &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
4. A. refused &emsp;&emsp; B. warned &emsp;&emsp; C. encouraged &emsp;&emsp; D. prevented
5. A. put up &emsp;&emsp; B. gave up &emsp;&emsp; C. set up &emsp;&emsp; D. put forward
6. A. form &emsp;&emsp; B. formed &emsp;&emsp; C. forming &emsp;&emsp; D. to form
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
9. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. need &emsp;&emsp; C. must &emsp;&emsp; D. should
10. A. touch &emsp;&emsp; B. touching &emsp;&emsp; C. touched &emsp;&emsp; D. touches

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任让我们每个人想想新一年的目标。ask sb. to do sth. 表示"要求某人做某事"。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮到我发言时，我感到有点紧张。felt 为系动词，后接形容词作表语；nervous 表示"紧张的"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想提高英语水平，尤其是口语。especially 表示"尤其、特别"，用于进一步强调。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师鼓励我们写下三个目标贴在墙上。encourage sb. to do sth. 表示"鼓励某人做某事"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班长李华提出了一个小组学习计划。put forward 表示"提出（建议/计划）"。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 虚拟语气（suggest 从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他建议我们组建学习小组。suggest that sb. (should) do sth.，that 从句中用动词原形。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：大多数同学立刻同意了他的想法。agree with sb./one's idea 表示"同意某人/某人的想法"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有计划的目标只是一个愿望。wish 为可数名词单数，以辅音音素开头，用 a。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 情态动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：努力学习，你就能实现一切。"祈使句 + and + 陈述句"结构，表示"如果你……就能……"；can 表示"能够"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的话深深触动了我们。叙述过去发生的事，用一般过去时 touched。故选 C。
