# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工笔筒

## 完形填空原文

The new term had just started, and my desk was already ___1___ of pens and pencils. I thought it would be great to have a pen holder, so I decided to make ___2___ by myself.

I found an empty milk carton in the kitchen and washed it ___3___. Then I cut the top part off with scissors. My mother reminded me to be ___4___ with the sharp edges.

After that, I covered the carton ___5___ colorful paper and drew some small patterns on it. I also used old buttons to make it look ___6___ special. The whole process took me about an hour, ___7___ I enjoyed every minute of it.

When I put my pens into the new holder, it ___8___ perfectly on the corner of my desk. My father saw it and asked, "Did you really make this ___9___?" I nodded proudly. He said that recycling materials ___10___ make wonderful things was a great idea.

## 选项

1. A. fill &emsp;&emsp; B. full &emsp;&emsp; C. filled &emsp;&emsp; D. filling
2. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. one &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. this
3. A. clean &emsp;&emsp; B. clearly &emsp;&emsp; C. cleaning &emsp;&emsp; D. cleaned
4. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. careless &emsp;&emsp; D. carefully
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. most
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. fit &emsp;&emsp; B. fits &emsp;&emsp; C. fitted &emsp;&emsp; D. fitting
9. A. you &emsp;&emsp; B. your &emsp;&emsp; C. yours &emsp;&emsp; D. yourself
10. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. on

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的桌子已经满是钢笔和铅笔了。be full of 为固定搭配，表示"充满"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定自己做一个。one 用来替代前面提到的同类事物中的"一个"（a pen holder），泛指。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词作补语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把它洗干净了。wash sth. clean 为"动词 + 宾语 + 形容词补语"结构，clean 作宾语补足语。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈提醒我小心锋利的边缘。be careful with 表示"小心对待……"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用彩色纸包住了牛奶盒。cover ... with ... 表示"用……覆盖"。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还用旧纽扣让它看起来更特别。此处暗含"比之前更……"的比较，用 more 修饰 special。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：整个过程花了我大约一个小时，但我享受每一分钟。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：把笔放进去后，它完美地放在了桌角。叙述过去的动作，用一般过去时 fitted。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 反身代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：你真的是自己做的吗？强调"你自己"，用反身代词 yourself。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定式（目的/用途）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说回收材料来做出美妙的东西是个好主意。to make 为不定式表目的。故选 C。
