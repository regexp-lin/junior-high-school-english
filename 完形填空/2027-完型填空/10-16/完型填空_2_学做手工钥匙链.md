# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工钥匙链

## 完形填空原文

Our school's craft club meets every Friday afternoon. This week, the club leader taught us to make keychains ___1___ colorful beads and wire.

She first showed us a finished keychain. It was shaped like a little star, and the beads ___2___ beautifully in the light. We were all eager to make one for ___3___.

The materials were laid out on the table. I picked some blue and silver beads because they reminded me ___4___ the night sky. The club leader told us to thread the beads onto the wire in any pattern we liked.

I started threading the beads ___5___. At first, my fingers were clumsy, and some beads fell off. But after a few tries, I became ___6___ at handling the tiny wire. My pattern was a simple wave design.

The girl sitting across from me made a keychain with a heart shape. Hers was ___7___ beautiful than mine. I told her that her design was really creative, and she smiled ___8___.

At the end of the session, we attached metal rings to our keychains. I decided to give ___9___ to my mother as a small gift. When she received it that evening, her eyes lit up with joy.

"You made this yourself?" she asked in surprise. I nodded proudly. She said it was the ___10___ gift she had ever received.

## 选项

1. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. uses &emsp;&emsp; C. used &emsp;&emsp; D. using  
2. A. shine &emsp;&emsp; B. shined &emsp;&emsp; C. shining &emsp;&emsp; D. shone  
3. A. us &emsp;&emsp; B. our &emsp;&emsp; C. ours &emsp;&emsp; D. ourselves  
4. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
5. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. careless  
6. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
7. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  
8. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. happier &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. happiness  
9. A. one &emsp;&emsp; B. it &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作方式状语  
**【解析】** using colorful beads and wire 为现在分词短语作方式状语，表"用彩色珠子和铁丝"。use 为原形；uses 为三单；used 为过去式（也可理解为省略的被动，但此处主动更自然）。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（不规则动词）  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，shine 的过去式为 shone。shine 为原形；shined 虽在美式英语中可用但 shone 更标准；shining 为现在分词。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 反身代词  
**【解析】** make one for ourselves 意为"为我们自己做一个"，主语 we 与宾语为同一群人，用反身代词 ourselves。us 为宾格但不强调"自己"；our 为物主代词；ours 为名词性物主代词。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 remind sb. of  
**【解析】** remind sb. of sth. 意为"使某人想起某事"，of 为固定介词。about/for/with 不与 remind 构成此搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 threading 需用副词 carefully（仔细地）。care 为名词/动词；careful 为形容词；careless 意为"粗心的"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（隐含比较）  
**【解析】** became better at 意为"变得更擅长"，暗含与之前对比（从笨拙到熟练），用比较级。good 为原级；well 为原级；best 为最高级。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** more beautiful than mine 意为"比我的更漂亮"，beautiful 为多音节词，比较级用 more。much 修饰比较级但不能单独充当比较级；very 不与 than 搭配；most 为最高级。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 smiled 需用副词 happily（快乐地）。happy 为形容词；happier 为比较级形容词；happiness 为名词。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 名词性物主代词  
**【解析】** give mine to my mother 意为"把我的（钥匙链）给妈妈"，mine 代替 my keychain。one 泛指一个；it 虽可指代但 mine 更强调"我亲手做的那个"；that 为指示代词。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** "the + 最高级 + n. + she had ever received" 为固定最高级句型，best 为 good 的最高级。good 为原级；well 作形容词表"身体好"；better 为比较级。故选 D。
