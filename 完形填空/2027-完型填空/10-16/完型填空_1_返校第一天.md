# 中考英语完形填空：返校第一天

## 完形填空原文

After the long National Day holiday, I felt a little ___1___ about going back to school. However, when I walked into the classroom, all my worries disappeared.

My classmates were chatting ___2___ about what they had done during the holiday. Some went traveling, some visited relatives, and others stayed at home reading books. Everyone had ___3___ own special experience to share.

Our head teacher, Mr. Liu, came in with a big smile. He asked us to write a short paragraph about the most ___4___ thing we did during the holiday. I wrote about the bike ride with my neighbor Uncle Zhang. Mr. Liu read a few paragraphs aloud and said he was ___5___ by our colorful holiday life.

In the afternoon, we had our first math class. The teacher reviewed ___6___ we had learned before the holiday. I was glad to find that I still ___7___ most of the formulas. It seemed that my holiday studying had paid off.

After school, my best friend Wang Mei walked home with me. She said she ___8___ me very much during the holiday. I felt the same way. We made a plan ___9___ together every weekend so that we would never feel lonely again.

That evening, I wrote in my diary: "School is not just a place ___10___ learning. It is also where friendships grow."

## 选项

1. A. worry &emsp;&emsp; B. worrying &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. worries  
2. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. excitedly &emsp;&emsp; C. exciting &emsp;&emsp; D. excitement  
3. A. his &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. their  
4. A. interest &emsp;&emsp; B. interested &emsp;&emsp; C. interesting &emsp;&emsp; D. interestingly  
5. A. impress &emsp;&emsp; B. impressed &emsp;&emsp; C. impressing &emsp;&emsp; D. impression  
6. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
7. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. remembered &emsp;&emsp; C. remembering &emsp;&emsp; D. to remember  
8. A. miss &emsp;&emsp; B. missed &emsp;&emsp; C. missing &emsp;&emsp; D. has missed  
9. A. meet &emsp;&emsp; B. met &emsp;&emsp; C. to meet &emsp;&emsp; D. meeting  
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. at  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）  
**【解析】** felt 为系动词，后接描述人感受的形容词。worried（担忧的）修饰人。worry 为动词/名词；worrying 意为"令人担忧的"修饰事物；worries 为名词复数。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 chatting 需用副词 excitedly（兴奋地）。excited 为形容词修饰人；exciting 为形容词修饰物；excitement 为名词。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** 主语为 Everyone（泛指所有人），对应的物主代词用 their（语法上 everyone 为单数，但现代英语中常用 their 指代不特定性别的人）。his/her 只指代单一性别；its 指物。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 现在分词形容词修饰物  
**【解析】** the most interesting thing 意为"最有趣的事情"，interesting 修饰事物。interest 为名词/动词；interested 修饰人；interestingly 为副词。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 被动语态/过去分词作表语  
**【解析】** was impressed by 意为"被……所打动"，impressed 可理解为被动语态或形容词作表语。impress 为动词原形；impressing 为现在分词；impression 为名词。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词  
**【解析】** reviewed what we had learned 意为"复习我们学过的内容"，what 引导宾语从句并在从句中作 learned 的宾语。that 不作成分；which 需有选择范围；how 不作宾语。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，was glad to find that I still remembered 为一致的过去时态。remember 为原形；remembering 为现在分词；to remember 为不定式。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句时态呼应  
**【解析】** 主句 said 为过去时，宾语从句应用相应的过去时态。missed（一般过去时）表示假期中想念我。miss 为原形；missing 为现在分词；has missed 为现在完成时，均不与主句时态一致。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（plan to do）  
**【解析】** make a plan to do sth. 意为"制定做某事的计划"，不定式作后置定语修饰 plan。meet 为原形；met 为过去式；meeting 为动名词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 for 表目的/用途  
**【解析】** a place for learning 意为"一个用于学习的地方"，for 表用途。to 也可（a place to learn）但此处 learning 为动名词，用 for 更合适；of 表所属；at 表地点。故选 B。
