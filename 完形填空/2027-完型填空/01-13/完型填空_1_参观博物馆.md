# 中考英语完形填空：参观博物馆

## 完形填空原文

Our school organized a trip to the city history museum last Friday. I had never been there ___1___, so I felt curious about everything on the bus ride.

At the entrance, a guide gave us small earphones so we could hear her ___2___ without shouting in the hall. The first exhibition showed old tools and pottery ___3___ hundreds of years ago.

I stopped in front of a bronze mirror and read the English label ___4___. The guide explained how people ___5___ used mirrors in daily life for more than beauty. Her voice was calm, ___6___ her words painted clear pictures in my mind.

In the interactive area, we tried a digital game about rebuilding an ancient street. My team worked ___7___ and won a small paper badge as a prize. I pinned it ___8___ my bag immediately.

Before leaving, I bought a postcard ___9___ my grandparents. That visit made history feel close and ___10___ instead of distant dates in a textbook.

## 选项

1. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. already &emsp;&emsp; C. almost &emsp;&emsp; D. perhaps
2. A. clearly &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. secretly &emsp;&emsp; D. cheaply
3. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. toward
4. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. twice &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
5. A. never &emsp;&emsp; B. seldom &emsp;&emsp; C. sometimes &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. if
7. A. alone &emsp;&emsp; B. together &emsp;&emsp; C. away &emsp;&emsp; D. abroad
8. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. under &emsp;&emsp; D. below
9. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. like
10. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. silent &emsp;&emsp; D. empty

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前从未去过那里，所以在公交车上对一切都很好奇。never ... before 表示"以前从未"。already 已经；almost 几乎；perhaps 也许。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：导游发给我们小耳机，这样我们就能听清楚她讲解，而不必在大厅里大喊。A 项 clearly 清楚地；B 项 angrily 生气地；C 项 secretly 秘密地；D 项 cheaply 便宜地。耳机用于听清讲解。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一个展厅展出数百年前的古老工具和陶器。from hundreds of years ago 表示"来自数百年前"；after 之后；beside 旁边；toward 朝向。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在一面铜镜前停下，仔细阅读英文说明牌。A 项 carelessly 粗心地；B 项 twice 两次；C 项 carefully 仔细地；D 项 loudly 大声地。阅读标签应仔细。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：导游解释过去人们在日常生活中有时会用镜子，而且不只是为了美观。A 项 never 从不；B 项 seldom 很少；C 项 sometimes 有时；D 项 hardly 几乎不。说明历史用途的频率，sometimes 语气客观、符合讲解语境。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的声音很平静，但她的话语却在我脑海里勾勒出清晰的画面。calm 与 painted clear pictures 形成转折，用 but。or 表选择；so 表结果；if 表条件。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的队友们齐心协力，赢得了一枚小纸徽章作为奖品。work together 齐心协力；work alone 独自；away/abroad 不符合搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我立刻把它别在书包上。pin ... on ... 把……别在……上；pin in/under/below 不自然。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：离开前我给爷爷奶奶买了一张明信片。buy sth. for sb. 为某人买某物；of/about/like 不用于此结构。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那次参观让历史变得贴近而真实，而不再是课本里遥远的日期。A 项 real 真实的；B 项 boring 无聊的；C 项 silent 沉默的；D 项 empty 空洞的。与 distant dates 对比，real 恰当。故选 A。
