# 中考英语完形填空：学做酸奶

## 完形填空原文

In our life skills class, the teacher showed us how to make yogurt at home with simple tools. I wrote the steps ___1___ because I wanted to try the recipe with my sister on Sunday.

First, we heated milk in a pot ___2___ it was warm but not boiling. After it cooled a little, we added a spoonful of store-bought yogurt as a ___3___ to help the bacteria grow.

We poured the mixture into clean glass jars and covered them ___4___ a thick towel to keep the temperature steady. My sister set a timer ___5___ we would not forget to check them.

Six hours later, the yogurt looked thick and smooth. It tasted a little ___6___ than the sweet kind from the supermarket, but we liked its natural flavor. Mom said we should ___7___ it in the fridge and eat it within two days.

That evening, we added fresh fruit ___8___ top and shared a small bowl with Dad. He joked that our kitchen had turned ___9___ a tiny science lab. I learned that healthy food does not need to be ___10___ if you follow the steps carefully.

## 选项

1. A. down &emsp;&emsp; B. away &emsp;&emsp; C. back &emsp;&emsp; D. off
2. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although
3. A. starter &emsp;&emsp; B. prize &emsp;&emsp; C. joke &emsp;&emsp; D. excuse
4. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
5. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. though
6. A. sweeter &emsp;&emsp; B. saltier &emsp;&emsp; C. sourer &emsp;&emsp; D. hotter
7. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. store &emsp;&emsp; D. fry
8. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. in
9. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. down &emsp;&emsp; D. over
10. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. free &emsp;&emsp; C. empty &emsp;&emsp; D. silent

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把步骤记下来，因为想和姐姐周日一起试做。write down 记下、写下；write away 写信索取；write back 回信；write off 勾销。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们先把牛奶在锅里加热到温热但不沸腾。until 表示"直到……为止"；when 当……时；unless 除非；although 尽管。加热到某一程度用 until。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：牛奶稍冷却后，我们加入一勺市售酸奶作为发酵引子以帮助菌群生长。A 项 starter 发酵剂、引子；B 项 prize 奖品；C 项 joke 玩笑；D 项 excuse 借口。自制酸奶常用 starter。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把混合物倒进干净的玻璃罐，并用厚毛巾盖住以保持温度稳定。cover ... with ... 用……盖住……。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：姐姐设了定时器，这样我们就不会忘记检查。so 表结果"以便、所以"；because 因为；if 如果；though 尽管。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道比超市里的甜味酸奶略酸一点，但我们喜欢这种天然风味。自制酸奶相对更酸，sourer 与 natural flavor 呼应。sweeter 更甜；saltier 更咸；hotter 更烫。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说我们应该把它冷藏并在两天内吃完。store in the fridge 冷藏储存；hide 隐藏；sell 出售；fry 油炸。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：当晚我们在上面加了新鲜水果。on top 在顶部、在上面，为固定搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他开玩笑说我们的厨房变成了一个小型科学实验室。turn into 变成；turn up 出现；turn down 调低；turn over 翻转。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了只要按步骤操作，健康食物并不一定很难做。A 项 difficult 困难的；B 项 free 免费的；C 项 empty 空的；D 项 silent 沉默的。与 carefully follow steps 对应。故选 A。
