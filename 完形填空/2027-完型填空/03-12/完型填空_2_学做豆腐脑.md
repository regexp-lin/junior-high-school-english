# 中考英语完形填空：学做豆腐脑

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my mom decided ___1___ teach me how to make tofu pudding at home. I was excited ___2___ I had only tasted it in breakfast shops before.

First, we soaked soybeans overnight. The next morning, we blended them ___3___ water until the liquid looked milky white. Mom reminded me ___4___ hot steam could burn my fingers, so I stood a little farther away.

After filtering the soy milk, we heated it slowly. "Stir gently, ___5___ it may stick to the pot," Mom said. When the smell filled the kitchen, I felt calm ___6___ relaxed.

Then she added a little brine ___7___ turned off the fire. We waited quietly. Minutes later, the surface became smooth ___8___ soft like silk.

We added a spoonful of light soy sauce and chopped scallions. The first bite was warm and silky. I smiled ___9___ Mom, "It's not perfect, ___10___ it's full of love."

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. or
4. A. this &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. which
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. yet
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈决定在家教我做豆腐脑。decide to do sth. 为固定搭配，意为“决定做某事”。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很兴奋，因为我以前只在早餐店吃过。后半句解释兴奋的原因，用 because。although 表让步；unless 表条件；until 表时间。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把黄豆和水一起打成浆。blend...with water 表示“与水一起搅拌打碎”。and/or 为连词，不能直接连接宾语与介词短语结构此处不如 with 自然。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈提醒我热蒸汽可能会烫伤手指。remind sb. that... 后接陈述句作宾语，用 that 引导（口语中可省略，但选项中应选 that）。this/what/which 不用于该结构。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轻轻搅拌，否则会粘锅。or 在此表示“否则”，相当于 otherwise。and 表并列；so 表结果；but 表转折。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到平静又放松。calm 与 relaxed 为并列形容词，用 and 连接。or 表选择；but 表转折；so 表结果。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后她加入少量卤水并关火。added 与 turned off 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合语境。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：表面变得光滑柔软，像丝绸一样。smooth 与 soft 为并列形容词，用 and。or 表选择；but/yet 表转折。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对着妈妈笑了。smile at sb. 表示“对某人微笑”。smile to 偶见，但初中阶段更常见考点为 smile at。for/on 搭配不当。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不算完美，但充满了爱。前后为转折关系，用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表结果。故选 C。
