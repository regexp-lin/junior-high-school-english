# 中考英语完形填空：新学期的第一天

## 完形填空原文

On the first day of the new term, I woke up early ___1___ I didn't want to be late. After breakfast, I checked my schoolbag twice ___2___ sure I had my student ID.

When I got to the classroom, some classmates were already there. They talked ___3___ their winter holidays happily. Our homeroom teacher came in ___4___ a smile and welcomed everyone back.

In the morning meeting, she told us ___5___ new class rules and encouraged us to set small goals. I wrote down two goals: read English for fifteen minutes every day and help others ___6___ I can.

At lunch, I sat with Tom. He said he felt ___7___ about math because the topics would be harder. I shared my plan to study together after school, and he looked ___8___.

In the afternoon, we received new textbooks. Carrying them home, I felt the bag was heavy, ___9___ my heart was light. I knew a new journey ___10___ begun.

## 选项

1. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
2. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made
3. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
7. A. worry &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. worrying &emsp;&emsp; D. worries
8. A. relax &emsp;&emsp; B. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxing &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxation
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. has &emsp;&emsp; B. have &emsp;&emsp; C. had &emsp;&emsp; D. is

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：新学期第一天我很早起床，因为我不想迟到。前后为因果关系，后句表原因用 because。but 转折；so 结果；or 选择。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：早饭后我把书包检查了两遍，以确保带了学生证。表示目的用不定式 to make，make sure 为固定搭配“确保”。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们开心地谈论寒假。talk about 谈论某事；talk with/to 与某人交谈。此处接 their winter holidays，用 about。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任微笑着走进来。with a smile 表示伴随“带着微笑”。in/on/at 均不如 with 贴切。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（tell sb. about sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她告诉我们新的班规。tell sb. about sth. 告诉某人关于某事。tell sb. to do sth. 表命令，此处为介绍内容，用 about。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（条件状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：尽我所能帮助别人。if I can 表示“如果我能够”，作条件/让步含义时常用“if I can”表达力所能及。until 直到；although 尽管；unless 除非。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（feel + adj.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说他对数学感到担心。feel 为系动词，后接形容词 worried（感到担忧的）。worry 动词/名词；worrying 令人担忧的，多修饰物。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** -ed / -ing 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他看起来放松了。look 为系动词，主语是人 he，表“感到放松”用 relaxed。relaxing 多修饰物。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：书包很重，但心里很轻松。前后转折用 but。and 并列；or 选择；so 结果。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词时态（现在完成时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道一段新的旅程已经开始。强调对现在的影响，用现在完成时；主语 a new journey 为单数，用 has begun。故选 A。
