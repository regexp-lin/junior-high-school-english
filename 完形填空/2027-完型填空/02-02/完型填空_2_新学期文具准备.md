# 中考英语完形填空：为新学期准备文具

## 完形填空原文

Before school started, Mom took me to a stationery store. She said good tools could help me study ___1___ efficiently, but the most important thing was still hard work.

I needed a new pencil case ___2___ my old one was broken. There were many styles on the shelf. I liked a blue one ___3___ a small rocket on it, ___4___ it was a little expensive.

Mom asked, "Do you really need all these fancy pens?" I thought for a while and put back two ___5___ I didn't need them for class. I kept a black pen, a red pen, and a highlighter ___6___ marking key points.

We also bought sticky notes and index cards. The shop assistant was kind and ___7___ us how to choose paper that was easy to write on.

On the way home, I made a list of ___8___ I would use each item. Mom praised me ___9___ being practical. That night, I organized my desk and felt ready ___10___ the new term.

## 选项

1. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. many
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. though
3. A. has &emsp;&emsp; B. had &emsp;&emsp; C. having &emsp;&emsp; D. have
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. although
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. tell &emsp;&emsp; B. tells &emsp;&emsp; C. told &emsp;&emsp; D. telling
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：好工具能帮我更高效地学习。efficiently 为多音节副词，比较级用 more efficiently。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我需要一个新笔袋，因为旧的坏了。表原因用 because。if 如果；unless 除非；though 尽管。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（现在分词作后置定语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我喜欢一个上面有火箭的蓝色笔袋。one 与 have 为主动关系，用 having 作后置定语修饰 one。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但它有点贵。前后转折用 but。and 并列；or 选择；so 因此。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（原因状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想了一会儿，把两支笔放回去，因为上课并不需要它们。表原因用 because。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我留了一支黑笔、一支红笔和一支用来标重点的荧光笔。表示用途“为了”用 for。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态（一般过去时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：店员很友好，并告诉我们如何挑选好写的纸。and 连接并列谓语 was 与空白处，全文叙述过去，用 told。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家的路上，我列了清单，写下每件物品我将如何使用。宾语从句中不缺主宾，表示方式用 how。what 什么；when 何时；where 何地。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（praise sb. for sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈表扬我很务实。praise sb. for (doing) sth. 因……表扬某人。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be ready for）
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天晚上我整理了书桌，感觉为新学期做好了准备。be ready for 为……做好准备。be ready to do 准备去做某事，后接动词，此处接名词短语 the new term，用 for。故选 B。
