# 中考英语完形填空：学做奶茶

## 完形填空原文

Bubble tea shops are popular, ___1___ my sister wanted to learn a healthier version at home. We watched a short video ___2___ taught us how to brew strong black tea first. Then we heated milk ___3___ it became warm but not boiling.

We mixed the tea and milk ___4___ a 3:1 ratio as the video suggested. "Too much sugar is not good ___5___ you," Mom reminded us, so we added only a little honey. The pearls we bought were cooked ___6___ ten minutes until they turned soft and chewy.

When everything was combined, the drink smelled ___7___ than I expected. I took a sip and smiled, "It tastes ___8___ the one from the shop, but it is lighter." My sister said she would practice ___9___ so that she could treat her friends ___10___ the weekend.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
3. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
7. A. sweet &emsp;&emsp; B. sweeter &emsp;&emsp; C. sweetest &emsp;&emsp; D. the sweetest
8. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
9. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶茶店很流行，所以我姐姐想在家学做更健康版本。表结果用 so。and 并列；but 转折；or 选择。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们看了一段短视频，它教我们如何先泡浓茶。先行词 video 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词（until）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后加热牛奶直到变温但不沸腾。until 表示“直到……为止”。when 也可，但 until 更突出加热终点。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：按 3:1 的比例混合茶与奶。in a…ratio 以……比例。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：糖太多对你不好。be good for 对……有好处；否定结构 not good for 对……不好。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词（时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：珍珠煮了十分钟直到变软糯。for + 时间段表示持续。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：闻起来比我想象的更香甜。有 than，用 sweeter。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道像店里的那杯。taste like 尝起来像。as 需接从句。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词比较级/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她会多练习。practice more 多练习，暗含比以前更多。most/much/very 不如 more 贴合“再多练”。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：周末请朋友喝。on the weekend 在周末（美式常用）。故选 B。
