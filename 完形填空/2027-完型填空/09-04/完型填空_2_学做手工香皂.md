# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工香皂

## 完形填空原文

Teacher's Day was just around the corner. I wanted to make something special for my teachers, so I chose ___1___ handmade soap as a gift. I found a simple tutorial online and started preparing.

The materials I needed ___2___ a soap base, essential oils, dried flowers, and silicone molds. I bought them from a craft shop ___3___ was near my home.

First, I cut the soap base into small pieces and melted it in a pot over ___4___ heat. It was important not to let the temperature get too ___5___, or the soap would lose its smoothness. After melting, I added a few drops of lavender oil. The kitchen was immediately filled ___6___ a wonderful smell.

Then I placed some dried flowers at the bottom of each mold and ___7___ poured the liquid soap in. The hardest part was waiting — the soap needed at least four hours ___8___.

The next morning, I carefully took the soaps ___9___ of the molds. They looked like little works of art! I wrapped each one in tissue paper and tied it with a ribbon. When I gave them to my teachers, they were ___10___ touched. "You made these yourself?" they asked in surprise. I nodded and smiled.

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made
2. A. include &emsp;&emsp; B. included &emsp;&emsp; C. including &emsp;&emsp; D. includes
3. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. what
4. A. low &emsp;&emsp; B. high &emsp;&emsp; C. big &emsp;&emsp; D. strong
5. A. low &emsp;&emsp; B. high &emsp;&emsp; C. cold &emsp;&emsp; D. warm
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slower &emsp;&emsp; C. slowest &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
8. A. cool &emsp;&emsp; B. cooled &emsp;&emsp; C. cooling &emsp;&emsp; D. to cool
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. off
10. A. deep &emsp;&emsp; B. deeper &emsp;&emsp; C. deeply &emsp;&emsp; D. depth

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选择制作手工香皂作为礼物。choose to do sth. 表示"选择做某事"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：我需要的材料包括皂基、精油、干花和硅胶模具。叙述过去的事件，用一般过去时 included。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系代词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在家附近的一家手工店买的。先行词为 a craft shop（物），在从句中作主语，用 which。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把皂基切成小块，用小火在锅里融化。low heat 表示"小火/低温"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的是不要让温度太高。too high 表示"太高"，由后文"否则会失去光滑度"可知温度不能过高。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房立刻充满了美妙的气味。be filled with 表示"充满"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后慢慢地把液态皂倒入。修饰动词 poured，需用副词 slowly。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：香皂需要至少四个小时来冷却。need + 时间 + to do sth. 表示"需要多长时间做某事"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（动词短语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我小心地把香皂从模具里取出来。take ... out of ... 表示"从……中取出"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们深深地被感动了。修饰过去分词 touched，需用副词 deeply。故选 C。
