# 中考英语完形填空：竞选班干部

## 完形填空原文

At the beginning of this term, our class needed to elect new class leaders. I always wanted to be the study monitor, ___1___ I was not sure if I was good enough. My mother encouraged me to give it ___2___ try.

I spent two evenings ___3___ a short speech. In it, I talked about my plan to organize study groups and help classmates ___4___ had trouble with homework. I also promised to be fair and ___5___ to everyone.

On election day, five students stood in front of the class to give speeches. I was the ___6___ one to speak. My heart was beating fast, but I tried to keep my voice ___7___. When I finished, the classroom was quiet for a second, and then everyone clapped.

The teacher counted the votes. I won ___8___ thirty-two out of forty-five votes. I could ___9___ believe it! My classmates trusted me, and I was determined not to let them ___10___.

That day, I learned that courage and preparation can help you achieve your dreams.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
3. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. prepared &emsp;&emsp; C. preparing &emsp;&emsp; D. to prepare
4. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
5. A. friend &emsp;&emsp; B. friends &emsp;&emsp; C. friendly &emsp;&emsp; D. friendship
6. A. three &emsp;&emsp; B. third &emsp;&emsp; C. thirty &emsp;&emsp; D. thirtieth
7. A. steady &emsp;&emsp; B. steadily &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. nervously
8. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. easy &emsp;&emsp; D. easily
10. A. out &emsp;&emsp; B. down &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. up

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直想当学习委员，但不确定自己是否够好。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈鼓励我试一试。give it a try 为固定搭配，表示"试一试"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我花了两个晚上准备一个简短的演讲。spend time doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系代词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助在作业上有困难的同学。先行词为 classmates（人），在从句中作主语，用 who。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：我承诺对每个人公平友善。be 后接形容词作表语；friendly 为形容词，表示"友好的"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 序数词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我是第三个发言的。the + 序数词 + to do，three 的序数词为 third。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尽量保持声音平稳。keep + 宾语 + 形容词（补语），steady 表示"稳定的"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以四十五票中的三十二票获胜。win with ... votes 表示"以……票赢得"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我几乎不敢相信！hardly 表示"几乎不"，表达惊喜之情。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（动词短语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决心不让他们失望。let sb. down 表示"让某人失望"。故选 B。
