# 中考英语完形填空：双十一帮妈妈拆快递

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday was ___1___ busiest online shopping day in our family. Packages kept arriving, and our living room looked ___2___ a small warehouse. The hallway was noisy with tearing tape, and even our cat hid under the sofa because it felt a little nervous. Mom smiled ___3___ said, “Could you help me open them after school?”

I washed ___4___ hands, put on gloves, and started right away. I tried ___5___ each box carefully ___6___ some glass cups inside might break. I also stacked the empty boxes near the door so we could recycle them later. Mom told me ___7___ check the order list before throwing away any plastic bags.

The work was ___8___ tiring than I expected, but I didn't mind ___9___ I saw Mom finally sit down and drink tea. She said the house felt lighter, and I agreed because the floor was clear again. By evening, everything ___10___ put away neatly on the shelves. Dad joked that our living room finally looked like a home again, not a delivery station.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
5. A. open &emsp;&emsp; B. opening &emsp;&emsp; C. opened &emsp;&emsp; D. to open  
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. much  
9. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. though  
10. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定冠词 the 与形容词最高级  
**【解析】** busiest 为 bad 的最高级形式，前面通常用定冠词 the 表示“最……的”。a/an 用于泛指或元音前；零冠词不能修饰最高级前的限定。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 like 表“像……一样”  
**【解析】** look like 为固定搭配，意为“看起来像”。as 多作“作为/当……时”；for/with 与 look 搭配语义不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and  
**【解析】** smiled 与 said 为并列谓语，用 and 连接。or 表选择；but 表转折；so 表结果，均不符合语境。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** hands 为名词，前面需用形容词性物主代词 my 修饰。I 为主格；me 为宾格；mine 为名词性物主代词，后不接名词。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（try to do）  
**【解析】** try to do sth. 表示“努力/尽量去做某事”，符合“尽量小心拆箱”。try doing 多表示“试着做（看效果）”，此处语境更强调目的性的小心。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句连词 because  
**【解析】** “小心拆箱”与“里面有玻璃杯可能碎”为因果关系，用 because。if/unless 为条件；although 为让步。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语/tell 结构（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell me to check... 为 tell sb. to do sth.，需用不定式符号 to。for/of/with 不能构成 tell 的正确结构。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（more + 多音节形容词）  
**【解析】** than I expected 提示比较级；tiring 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more tiring。very/much 不直接接 than；most 为最高级标志但句中无范围比较。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词 when  
**【解析】** “我不介意”与“看到妈妈坐下喝茶”为时间关系：当我看到……时，用 when。unless/if 条件；though 让步，语义不如 when 贴切。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** everything 与 put away 为被动关系，且全文叙述过去发生的事，用 was put away。is 为现在时；has been 强调对现在影响但语境为当晚已完成；will be 将来时。故选 B。
