# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工编织杯垫

## 完形填空原文

Our art teacher started a new club ___1___ teaches students simple handicrafts. The classroom smelled of cotton string, and sunlight fell across the long tables. Last Friday, I learned ___2___ make a woven coaster with colorful strings.

At first, my fingers felt stiff, ___3___ I could not remember the pattern. The teacher came over and said, “Watch ___4___ slowly, and copy my steps.” She showed me ___5___ to cross the strings without making knots. I breathed in deeply and tried to keep my hands steady.

After twenty minutes of practice, I made ___6___ mistakes than before. The coaster was not perfect, ___7___ I felt proud because it was made by ___8___. My classmates said it looked ___9___ of all the coasters in our group. Someone even asked if I could teach them next week.

That afternoon, I understood ___10___ patience could turn string into something useful and beautiful. I wrapped the coaster in paper and planned to use it under my study mug every morning.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
2. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. what to &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where  
6. A. few &emsp;&emsp; B. fewer &emsp;&emsp; C. little &emsp;&emsp; D. less  
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
8. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. myself  
9. A. nice &emsp;&emsp; B. nicer &emsp;&emsp; C. nicest &emsp;&emsp; D. the nicest  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词 which  
**【解析】** 先行词 a new club 为物，从句缺主语，用 which 引导定语从句。what 不引导定语从句；who 指人；whose 表所属，后接名词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构  
**【解析】** learn how to make... 表示“学习如何做……”，how to do 作 learn 的宾语。单独 how 或 what 不能直接接 make 的结构作宾语；what to make 语义偏重“做什么东西”，此处强调方法。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and  
**【解析】** “手指发僵”与“记不住花样”为并列的困难，语义顺承，用 and。or 表选择；but 表转折（此处并非转折）；so 表结果。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** watch 为动词，后接宾语用人称代词宾格 me。I 为主格；my/mine 为物主代词，不作 watch 的宾语。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语（how to do）  
**【解析】** show sb. how to cross... 表示“示范如何交叉……”，用 how。what to cross 语义不通；when/where 与“交叉绳子”的方法不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** few / fewer 修饰可数名词与比较级  
**【解析】** mistakes 为可数名词复数，且有 than，用 fewer mistakes 表示“更少的错误”。few 无比较；little/less 修饰不可数名词。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “杯垫并不完美”与“我很自豪”为转折，用 but。and 表并列顺承会弱化转折；or/so 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 反身代词  
**【解析】** by myself 表示“靠我自己/亲自”，强调独立完成。I/me/my 不能与 by 构成此义。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** of all the coasters in our group 表示范围，用最高级 the nicest。nice 原级；nicer 比较级缺 than；nicest 缺定冠词 the（形容词最高级前通常加 the）。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 that  
**【解析】** understood 后接宾语从句，陈述事实用 that 引导（口语中可省略，但选项中需选 that）。what 需在从句中作成分；how 表方式；whether 表“是否”，语义不符。故选 C。
