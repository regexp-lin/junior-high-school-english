# 中考英语完形填空：校园里的第一只蝴蝶

## 完形填空原文

Spring arrived quietly on our campus. On Tuesday morning, I saw ___1___ orange butterfly near the lilac bushes. It was the first one this year, ___2___ my classmates crowded around with phones.

The butterfly opened and closed its wings ___3___ a small fan. I held my breath ___4___ I was afraid of scaring it away.

Our biology teacher walked over and whispered, "Look at the patterns. They help it hide ___5___ enemies." I nodded, feeling science was suddenly ___6___ to life.

Later, we searched online ___7___ its name and learned it likes warm flowers. We decided ___8___ plant more nectar plants in the class garden.

When the bell rang, the butterfly flew ___9___ the fence. I smiled and thought spring is not only a season ___10___ a promise of new beginnings.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. though
3. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. close &emsp;&emsp; B. closer &emsp;&emsp; C. closest &emsp;&emsp; D. closely
7. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. on
8. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. at
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在紫丁香灌木旁看到一只橙色蝴蝶。orange 以元音音素开头，用 an。a 用于辅音音素开头；the 表特指，此处首次出现用不定冠词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是今年第一只，所以同学们都围过来用手机拍。因果关系用 so。or/but/though 不符合语境。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：蝴蝶开合翅膀像小扇子。like a small fan 为介词短语作状语，表比喻。as 作“像”多接从句；for/of 不表比喻。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我屏住呼吸，因为我怕把它吓跑。表原因用 because。although/unless/until 不符合逻辑。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：花纹帮助它躲避敌人。hide from enemies 表示“躲避敌人”。hide to/for/with 搭配不当。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我突然觉得科学更贴近生活了。close to life 常见；此处暗含“比以前更近”，用 closer。close 原级也可说，但选项有 closer 且与 suddenly 感受呼应更自然；closest 缺范围；closely 表“紧密地”，与 to life 搭配不如 closer natural（be close to）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在网上查找它的名字。search online for... 表示“在网上搜索……”。search of/about/on 搭配不如 for 标准。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们决定在班级花园里种更多蜜源植物。decide to do sth. 为固定搭配。for/with/at 不符合 decide 结构。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：蝴蝶飞过围栏。fly over 表示“飞过”。fly in/on/at 不如 over 贴切“越过”。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构 not only...but (also)...
**【解析】** 本句意为：春天不仅是季节，更是新开始的承诺。not only...but... 为固定搭配。and/or/so 不构成该结构。故选 C。
