# 中考英语完形填空：收到一封感谢信

## 完形填空原文

Two weeks ago, I helped an elderly neighbor carry groceries upstairs. I didn't think much about it ___1___ it was just a small favor.

Yesterday, I found a letter in my mailbox. The handwriting was neat ___2___ a little shaky. It was from Mr. Zhou. He wrote that his knee hurt that day, and my help saved him ___3___ trouble.

Reading the letter, I felt warm inside. Mr. Zhou also said he enjoyed ___4___ me practice the violin on weekends. He hoped I would ___5___ practicing music because it made the building feel lively.

I showed the letter to my parents. My dad said, "Good actions often come back ___6___ unexpected ways." I put the letter ___7___ my desk drawer as a reminder.

Now I greet Mr. Zhou ___8___ I see him. Sometimes we chat for a few minutes ___9___ the weather. I have learned that kindness builds a bridge ___10___ people, even when words are few.

## 选项

1. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. lot &emsp;&emsp; B. few &emsp;&emsp; C. many &emsp;&emsp; D. much
4. A. hear &emsp;&emsp; B. hearing &emsp;&emsp; C. heard &emsp;&emsp; D. to hear
5. A. keep on &emsp;&emsp; B. give up &emsp;&emsp; C. put off &emsp;&emsp; D. worry about
6. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. for
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. under &emsp;&emsp; D. behind
8. A. whenever &emsp;&emsp; B. however &emsp;&emsp; C. whatever &emsp;&emsp; D. wherever
9. A. above &emsp;&emsp; B. below &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. against
10. A. between &emsp;&emsp; B. among &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我没想太多，因为那只是个小忙。because 因为。unless 除非；although 尽管；until 直到。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：字迹工整但有点颤抖。neat 与 shaky 转折，用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 限定词与名词搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的帮助为他省去很多麻烦。trouble 作不可数名词，用 much 修饰；few 修饰可数名词且表否定含义；many 修饰可数名词复数；a lot 需加 of 才接名词。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（enjoy doing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：周先生说他也喜欢听我周末练小提琴。enjoy doing sth. 喜欢做某事。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他希望我坚持练习音乐。keep on doing sth. 继续做、坚持做。give up 放弃；put off 推迟；worry about 担心。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好的行为常常以意想不到的方式回报你。in... ways 以……方式。on 在……上；by 通过；for 为了。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把信放在书桌抽屉里作为提醒。in my desk drawer 在抽屉里。on 在……上；under 在……下；behind 在……后。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每当我见到周先生，我都会打招呼。whenever 无论何时、每当。however 然而；whatever 无论什么；wherever 无论哪里。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们聊几分钟天气。chat about the weather 聊天气。above 在……上方；below 在……下方；against 反对。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：善意在人与人之间架起桥梁。between 在（两者）之间，people 泛指人与人之间常用 between。among 在三者或以上之中；against 反对；beyond 超出。故选 A。
