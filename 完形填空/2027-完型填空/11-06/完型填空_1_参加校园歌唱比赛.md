# 中考英语完形填空：参加校园歌唱比赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a singing competition last month. At first, I was afraid ___1___ in front of so many students, but my music teacher encouraged me ___2___.

I chose a song ___3___ lyrics were easy for me to remember. Every afternoon, I practiced ___4___ the classroom was empty. My deskmate Lily even offered ___5___ me record my voice on her phone.

On the day of the competition, my legs were shaking ___6___ I stepped onto the stage. However, when the music started, I took a deep breath and sang as ___7___ as I could. The audience clapped warmly when I finished.

To my surprise, I won the third prize. My parents were proud ___8___ me, and they said many successful singers ___9___ similar feelings when they were young.

Now I believe that courage doesn’t mean having no fear. It means ___10___ what you are afraid of and still trying your best.

## 选项

1. A. sing &emsp;&emsp; B. singing &emsp;&emsp; C. to sing &emsp;&emsp; D. sang  
2. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. trying &emsp;&emsp; C. tried &emsp;&emsp; D. to try  
3. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. after &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
5. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
6. A. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; B. as long as &emsp;&emsp; C. even though &emsp;&emsp; D. so that  
7. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. clearer &emsp;&emsp; C. clearly &emsp;&emsp; D. more clearly  
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
9. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. has &emsp;&emsp; C. had &emsp;&emsp; D. are having  
10. A. face &emsp;&emsp; B. facing &emsp;&emsp; C. to face &emsp;&emsp; D. faced  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作宾语）  
**【解析】** be afraid to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示“害怕去做某事”。be afraid of doing 也常见，但此处选项为不定式结构，且语义为“不敢在众人面前唱”。sing/singing/sang 不能直接跟在 was afraid 后作标准宾语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（encourage sb. to do）  
**【解析】** encourage sb. to do sth. 为固定结构“鼓励某人做某事”，用不定式 to try。try/trying/tried 均不符合该动词搭配。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词（whose 表所属）  
**【解析】** 先行词为 a song，从句 lyrics were easy 中 lyrics 与 song 为所属关系，用 whose lyrics“这首歌的歌词”。which/who/whom 不能表达“……的”。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 连词 when（当……时）  
**【解析】** “教室空着的时候练习”，when 引导时间状语从句。if 表条件；until 表直到；after 表在……之后，均不如 when 贴合“趁空练习”的日常表达。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（offer to do）  
**【解析】** offer to do sth. 表示“主动提出做某事”，用不定式 to help。offer 后不接动名词 helping 作宾补的常见结构；help/helped 形式不对。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词短语 as soon as  
**【解析】** “一踏上舞台腿就发抖”，as soon as 表“一……就……”。as long as 只要/和……一样长；even though 尽管；so that 以便/结果，逻辑不符。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词原级（as... as 中间用副词修饰动词）  
**【解析】** sang 为动词，需用副词 clearly 修饰；as clearly as I could 表示“尽可能清楚地唱”。clear 为形容词；clearer/more clearly 用于比较级结构，此处为原级比较 as... as。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be proud of  
**【解析】** be proud of sb. 为固定短语“为某人感到骄傲”。for/with/to 均不与 proud 构成此义。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 动词时态（一般过去时）  
**【解析】** when they were young 提示过去，主语 many successful singers 为复数，谓语用 had similar feelings 表示“也曾有过相似感受”。have/has 时态与 were young 不呼应；are having 进行时与语境不符。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（mean doing）  
**【解析】** 此处 mean 表“意味着”，后接动名词，mean facing what you are afraid of 表示“意味着去面对所害怕的事”。mean to do 表“打算做”，语义不符。face/to face/faced 形式不对。故选 B。
