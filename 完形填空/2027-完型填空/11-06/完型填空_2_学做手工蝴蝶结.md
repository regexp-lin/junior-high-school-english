# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工蝴蝶结

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, Aunt Tina taught my cousin and ___1___ how to make ribbon bows for gift boxes. She brought colorful ribbons, scissors, and glue ___2___ a small basket.

“First, cut two pieces of ribbon,” she said. “Make sure they are ___3___ same length, or the bow will look strange.” I measured carefully, ___4___ I still made one side shorter than the other.

Aunt Tina smiled and told me ___5___ again. This time, I used a ruler and wrote the numbers ___6___ a piece of paper so that I would not forget them.

When the two pieces were ready, she showed us ___7___ to tie a neat knot in the middle. After ten minutes of practice, I could do it ___8___ myself. My cousin laughed and said my bow looked ___9___ beautiful than hers.

We put our bows on the boxes ___10___ we had prepared for our grandparents. It was a small project, but it filled the afternoon with warmth.

## 选项

1. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
2. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
5. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. trying &emsp;&emsp; C. to try &emsp;&emsp; D. tried  
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
8. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
9. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** taught 为动词，后接双宾语 taught my cousin and me，用人称代词宾格 me。I 为主格；my 为形容词性物主代词；mine 为名词性物主代词，均不能作 taught 的直接宾语。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 in（在……里面）  
**【解析】** “胶水等放在小篮子里”，用 in a small basket。on 表在表面上；at 表地点/时刻；by 表方式/在旁，均不合适。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定冠词 the（same 前固定用 the）  
**【解析】** the same 为固定搭配“相同的”。same 前一般不用 a/an 或零冠词。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “我仔细量了”与“还是一边短了”为转折，用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表结果，逻辑不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell sb. to do sth. 用不定式 to try。try/trying/tried 不符合 tell 的常见宾补结构。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 on（在纸/页上）  
**【解析】** write numbers on a piece of paper 表示“写在纸上”，用 on。in 多指写在书内/字里行间；at/for 搭配不当。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 how  
**【解析】** showed us how to tie... 表示“演示如何打结”，how + 不定式作宾语。what/which/when 与 tie a knot 的搭配不如 how 自然。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 by（by oneself 独自）  
**【解析】** by myself 为固定短语“独自、靠自己”。of/for/with 不构成此义。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** than hers 提示比较级，beautiful 为多音节形容词，用 more beautiful。very/too 无比较；most 为最高级。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词 which（指物）  
**【解析】** 先行词为 the boxes，从句 we had prepared 缺宾语，指物用 which。what 不引导定语从句；who/whom 指人。故选 B。
