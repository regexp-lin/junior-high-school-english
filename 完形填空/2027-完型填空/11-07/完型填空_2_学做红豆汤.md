# 中考英语完形填空：学做红豆汤

## 完形填空原文

Winter evenings in our city are cold, so my grandmother often cooks sweet red bean soup. Yesterday, she asked me ___1___ her in the kitchen because she wanted me to learn a useful skill.

She had ___2___ washed the beans and soaked them in water for hours. “If the beans are soft enough, the soup ___3___ taste better,” she explained.

We put the beans into a pot and added enough water. While we were waiting, she told me ___4___ stir the soup from time to time ___5___ it didn’t stick to the bottom.

When the beans became soft, we put in some rock sugar. I wanted ___6___ more sugar, but she stopped me. “Too much sugar isn’t good ___7___ your health,” she said gently.

At dinner, my parents tasted the soup and praised ___8___ loudly. I felt shy, ___9___ I was truly happy. Cooking is not only about food. It is also about love ___10___ is passed down in a family.

## 选项

1. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
2. A. already &emsp;&emsp; B. yet &emsp;&emsp; C. ever &emsp;&emsp; D. still  
3. A. will &emsp;&emsp; B. would &emsp;&emsp; C. can &emsp;&emsp; D. must  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. not to &emsp;&emsp; C. tell &emsp;&emsp; D. telling  
5. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
6. A. add &emsp;&emsp; B. adding &emsp;&emsp; C. to add &emsp;&emsp; D. added  
7. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
8. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
10. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do）  
**【解析】** ask me to help her 为 ask sb. to do sth.。help/helping/helped 不能直接作 ask 的宾补。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词 already（已经）  
**【解析】** 肯定句中表示“已经洗好并浸泡”，用 already。yet 多用于疑问/否定；ever 曾经；still 仍然，语义不符。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词 will（表将来/结果）  
**【解析】** If 引导的条件句，主句常用 will 表结果“就会更好喝”。would 多表过去习惯或虚拟；can/must 语气不如 will 贴合条件结果。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell me to stir... 不定式作宾补。not to 表否定，与“要搅拌”矛盾；tell/telling 形式错误。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词 so that（以便）  
**【解析】** “不时搅拌以便不粘锅”，so that 引导目的状语从句。although 尽管；unless 除非；if 如果，逻辑不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（want to do）  
**【解析】** want to add more sugar，want 后接不定式。add/adding/added 不符合 want 的搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be good for  
**【解析】** be good for your health 表示“对健康有益”，否定为 isn’t good for。be good to 对人友好；good with 善于应付；good at 擅长。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（作 praised 的宾语）  
**【解析】** praised me loudly，动词后用宾格 me。I 主格；my/mine 为物主代词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “害羞”与“真开心”转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不对。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 定语从句 which（指物 love）  
**【解析】** 先行词 love 指抽象事物，从句 is passed down 缺主语，用 which。who/whom 指人；what 不引导定语从句。故选 A。
