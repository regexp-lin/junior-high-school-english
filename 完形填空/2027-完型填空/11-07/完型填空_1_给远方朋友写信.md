# 中考英语完形填空：给远方朋友写信

## 完形填空原文

My pen friend Emma lives in Canada. We have known each other ___1___ three years, but we have never met in person. Last week, I decided ___2___ her a long letter instead of sending short messages online.

I bought some nice stationery and sat at my desk ___3___ a quiet Sunday morning. At first, I didn’t know ___4___ to start. Then I remembered our first conversation about favorite books, ___5___ made me smile.

In the letter, I wrote about my new school life and asked her several questions ___6___ her winter hobbies. I also drew a small picture of the maple leaves ___7___ she sent me in a photo last autumn.

When I finished, I put the letter into ___8___ envelope and checked the address twice. My father said letters ___9___ more meaningful than emojis because people spend more time choosing each word.

I hope the letter ___10___ safely next month. Waiting for a reply feels slow, but it also feels special.

## 选项

1. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
2. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. writing &emsp;&emsp; C. to write &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote  
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. which  
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
6. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
9. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. will be &emsp;&emsp; D. have been  
10. A. arrive &emsp;&emsp; B. arrived &emsp;&emsp; C. arrives &emsp;&emsp; D. will arrive  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 for（表示一段时间）  
**【解析】** “认识三年了”，for three years 与现在完成时 have known 连用表持续时长。since 后接时间点；in/at 不用于此结构。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide to do sth. 用不定式 to write。decide 后不接动名词或原形过去式作宾语。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 on（具体某天的上午）  
**【解析】** on a quiet Sunday morning 在具体日期/星期几的上午用 on。in the morning 泛指；at 接时刻；to 表方向，均不对。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句/“疑问词 + 不定式” where to start  
**【解析】** “不知从哪里下笔”，where to start 符合语境。what to start 搭配怪；how to start 也可说，但选项中 where 更贴合“开头落笔”的起点；which to start 缺先行范围。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句 which（指代整个主句）  
**【解析】** 非限制性定语从句，which made me smile 指代“我想起第一次聊天”这件事。what 不引导定语从句；who/whom 指人。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 about（关于）  
**【解析】** questions about her winter hobbies 表示“关于她冬季爱好的问题”。with/for/to 与 questions 搭配不符。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词 that（指物，作宾语）  
**【解析】** 先行词为 maple leaves，从句 she sent me 缺宾语，指物可用 that。what 不引导定语从句；who/whom 指人。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 an（元音音素开头）  
**【解析】** envelope 以元音音素开头，用 an envelope。a 用于辅音音素；the 表特指；零冠词不适用“一封信封”。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般将来时 / 宾语从句时态  
**【解析】** 父亲陈述观点“信将比表情符号更有意义”，用一般将来时 will be 表判断/趋势。单纯过去 were 与主句 said 的“观点”搭配不如 will be 自然；are 与主句过去时略不协调；have been 完成体不贴切。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 一般将来时（hope 后宾从表将来）  
**【解析】** I hope (that) the letter will arrive safely next month，next month 为将来时间，用 will arrive。arrive/arrives 时态不对；arrived 为过去。故选 D。
