# 中考英语完形填空：班级黑板报评比

## 完形填空原文

Our class decided ___1___ part in the school blackboard newspaper competition. The theme was "Green Campus", ___2___ we needed both pictures and short English slogans.

Lisa was good ___3___ drawing, so she sketched trees and a recycling bin. I was responsible ___4___ neat handwriting and spelling checks. We argued a little ___5___ the title font, but we solved the problem by voting.

We stayed after school ___6___ three afternoons. The classroom was quiet ___7___ we focused on details like borders and colors. When our teacher praised our teamwork, we felt proud ___8___ tired.

On the judging day, student judges walked along the corridor ___9___ clipboards. Our hearts raced when they stopped ___10___ front of our class board for a long time.

## 选项

1. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. to take &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. took
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. or
3. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. in
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. from
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. while &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
10. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班决定参加学校黑板报评比。decide to do sth. 决定做某事，用不定式 to take。take 原形；taking 动名词；took 过去式。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：主题是“绿色校园”，所以我们需要图画和简短的英语标语。前后为因果关系，用 so 所以。because 因为；but 但是；or 或者。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：莉萨擅长绘画。be good at 擅长……。be good for 对……有益；be good with 善于应付；be good in 较少与 drawing 搭配。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我负责工整书写和拼写检查。be responsible for 对……负责。to 向；of 的；with 和。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们为标题字体争论了一下。argue about 为……争论。argue with sb. 与某人争吵；to/from 搭配不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们放学后连续三个下午留下来。for three afternoons 表示持续的时间段。in 在……里；on 在具体某天；at 在具体时刻。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们专注于边框和颜色等细节时，教室里很安静。while 当……时，引导时间状语从句，强调两个动作同时发生。until 直到；unless 除非；if 如果。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师表扬我们的团队合作时，我们自豪却也疲惫。proud 与 tired 为转折，用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：评比当天，学生评委拿着写字板沿走廊走过。with clipboards 带着写字板，表伴随。in 在……里；on 在……上；by 通过。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：当他们停在我们班黑板前很久时，我们的心都跳得很快。in front of 在……前面。on/at/to 不与 front of 构成此义。故选 C。
