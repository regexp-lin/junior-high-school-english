# 中考英语完形填空：帮爸爸整理旧照片

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, Dad asked me to help him clean the storage room. While moving boxes, I found an old photo album covered with ___1___. Dad's eyes lit up when he saw it. "I haven't seen this in years!" he said excitedly.

We sat on the floor and opened the album together. The first few pages showed Dad as a little boy. He was wearing a big smile and holding a toy car in his ___2___. I was surprised because he looked so different from now. "Is that really you?" I asked, laughing.

Dad pointed to a photo of a group of children standing in front of a school gate. "These were my best ___3___ in primary school," he said. "We used to play football together every day after class." He looked at the photo for a long time, and I could tell he ___4___ those days very much.

Then we saw a photo of Dad and Mom on their wedding day. Mom was wearing a white dress and looked very ___5___. Dad smiled and said, "That was the happiest day of my life." I had never seen this photo before, and it made me feel warm ___6___.

There were also some photos of me as a baby. In one of them, I was crying with my mouth wide open, and Dad was trying to ___7___ me down. "You cried a lot as a baby," Dad joked. "But you also laughed a lot." We both laughed at the funny ___8___.

We spent the whole afternoon looking through the album. Dad decided to put it in the living room so everyone could ___9___ it. I think old photos are like small time machines. They can take you back to the past and ___10___ you of the people and moments that matter most.

## 选项

1. A. water &emsp;&emsp; B. paint &emsp;&emsp; C. dust &emsp;&emsp; D. snow
2. A. pocket &emsp;&emsp; B. hand &emsp;&emsp; C. bag &emsp;&emsp; D. mouth
3. A. teachers &emsp;&emsp; B. friends &emsp;&emsp; C. brothers &emsp;&emsp; D. neighbors
4. A. feared &emsp;&emsp; B. hated &emsp;&emsp; C. missed &emsp;&emsp; D. doubted
5. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. ordinary
6. A. outside &emsp;&emsp; B. inside &emsp;&emsp; C. above &emsp;&emsp; D. below
7. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. calm &emsp;&emsp; C. write &emsp;&emsp; D. turn
8. A. stories &emsp;&emsp; B. movies &emsp;&emsp; C. photos &emsp;&emsp; D. songs
9. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. throw &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoy
10. A. warn &emsp;&emsp; B. remind &emsp;&emsp; C. accuse &emsp;&emsp; D. inform

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一本布满灰尘的旧相册。A 项意为"水"；B 项意为"油漆"；C 项意为"灰尘"；D 项意为"雪"。储藏室里的旧物品上会有 dust。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手里拿着一辆玩具车。A 项意为"口袋"；B 项意为"手"；C 项意为"包"；D 项意为"嘴"。holding...in his hand 表示手拿着。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些是我小学时最好的朋友。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"朋友"；C 项意为"兄弟"；D 项意为"邻居"。best friends 最合语境。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他看了很久，我能看出他非常想念那些日子。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"讨厌"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"怀疑"。missed those days 表示怀念过去。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈穿着白色礼服，看起来很美。A 项意为"疲惫的"；B 项意为"美丽的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"普通的"。婚纱照中 beautiful 最合适。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这让我心里感到温暖。A 项意为"外面"；B 项意为"里面"；C 项意为"上面"；D 项意为"下面"。warm inside 表示内心温暖。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸试图让我安静下来。A 项 put down 意为"放下"；B 项 calm down 意为"使平静"；C 项 write down 意为"写下"；D 项 turn down 意为"调低"。calm me down 意为"安抚我"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们对着有趣的照片一起笑。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"电影"；C 项意为"照片"；D 项意为"歌"。上文在看照片，故是 photos。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸决定把相册放客厅让大家欣赏。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"扔"；D 项意为"欣赏/享受"。enjoy it 表示欣赏。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：旧照片能带你回到过去，提醒你那些重要的人和时刻。A 项意为"警告"；B 项意为"提醒"；C 项意为"指控"；D 项意为"通知"。remind you of 意为"提醒你想起"。故选 B。
