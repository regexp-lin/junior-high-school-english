# 中考英语完形填空：寒假晨跑计划

## 完形填空原文

When the winter vacation started, I made a plan to go jogging every morning. My father thought it was a great idea and decided to ___1___ me. We agreed to wake up at six thirty and run for thirty minutes around the park near our home.

On the first morning, my alarm clock rang, but I did not want to get out of bed. It was so cold ___2___ that I just wanted to stay under the warm blanket. But Dad knocked on my door and said, "A promise is a promise!" So I forced myself to get ___3___.

The air outside was freezing. I could see my breath turning into white mist. We started jogging slowly, and at first, my legs felt stiff and ___4___. But after a few minutes, my body warmed up and I began to feel much better. The park was peaceful in the early morning. There were only a few old people doing tai chi on the grass.

By the end of the first week, I ___5___ wanted to quit several times. My muscles were sore and I felt tired every afternoon. But Dad encouraged me to keep going. He said, "The hardest part is not the running itself—it's ___6___ the habit."

After two weeks, something amazing happened. I started to actually ___7___ the morning runs. I liked the fresh air and the quiet streets. I also noticed that I slept better at night and felt more ___8___ during the day. My friends said I even looked healthier.

When school started again, I decided to ___9___ jogging on weekends. Dad was very happy about my decision. He said that exercise was not just about making the body strong—it also made the mind ___10___. I am grateful that he pushed me to start this habit. It has truly changed my life.

## 选项

1. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. join &emsp;&emsp; D. watch
2. A. inside &emsp;&emsp; B. outside &emsp;&emsp; C. upstairs &emsp;&emsp; D. downstairs
3. A. dressed &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. hurt &emsp;&emsp; D. paid
4. A. soft &emsp;&emsp; B. heavy &emsp;&emsp; C. light &emsp;&emsp; D. loose
5. A. never &emsp;&emsp; B. seldom &emsp;&emsp; C. almost &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
6. A. breaking &emsp;&emsp; B. building &emsp;&emsp; C. hiding &emsp;&emsp; D. losing
7. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. hate &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
8. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. confused &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. energetic
9. A. give up &emsp;&emsp; B. continue &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. delay
10. A. weaker &emsp;&emsp; B. slower &emsp;&emsp; C. stronger &emsp;&emsp; D. sadder

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸觉得是好主意，决定加入我。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"加入"；D 项意为"观看"。join me 意为"加入我一起"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：外面太冷了，我只想留在温暖的被子里。A 项意为"里面"；B 项意为"外面"；C 项意为"楼上"；D 项意为"楼下"。冬天 outside 很冷。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我强迫自己穿好衣服起来。A 项 get dressed 意为"穿好衣服"；B 项 get lost 意为"迷路"；C 项 get hurt 意为"受伤"；D 项 get paid 意为"得到报酬"。起床穿衣用 get dressed。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始腿感觉僵硬而沉重。A 项意为"软的"；B 项意为"沉重的"；C 项意为"轻的"；D 项意为"松的"。stiff and heavy 形容刚开始跑时腿的感觉。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一周结束时我几乎想放弃好几次。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"很少"；C 项意为"几乎"；D 项意为"几乎不"。almost wanted to quit 表示差点要放弃。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最难的部分不是跑步本身，而是养成习惯。A 项意为"打破"；B 项意为"建立"；C 项意为"隐藏"；D 项意为"失去"。building the habit 意为"养成习惯"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两周后我开始真正享受晨跑。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"讨厌"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"害怕"。enjoy the morning runs 表示享受晨跑。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：白天感觉更有精力。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"困惑的"；C 项意为"孤独的"；D 项意为"精力充沛的"。运动后更 energetic。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：开学后我决定在周末继续跑步。A 项意为"放弃"；B 项意为"继续"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"推迟"。continue jogging 意为"继续慢跑"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：运动不仅让身体强壮，也让头脑更强大。A 项意为"更弱"；B 项意为"更慢"；C 项意为"更强"；D 项意为"更悲伤"。mind stronger 与 body strong 对应。故选 C。
