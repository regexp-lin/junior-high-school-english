# 中考英语完形填空：学搓汤圆

## 完形填空原文

The Lantern Festival was coming, so my grandma invited me ___1___ the kitchen to make sweet rice balls. She said family members should learn the tradition ___2___ it disappears.

She mixed glutinous rice flour ___3___ warm water until the dough felt smooth. I tried ___4___ a small ball, but it cracked at once. Grandma laughed, "Add a little more water and press ___5___."

Little by little, I could roll round balls ___6___ filling inside. We boiled them in a pot and watched them dance ___7___ the water rose.

When I tasted my first one, the black sesame was hot ___8___ sweet. Grandma patted my shoulder and said, "Good job. You are ___9___ patient than last year."

I nodded, thinking that food tastes ___10___ when we make it with love.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made
5. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gently &emsp;&emsp; C. gentler &emsp;&emsp; D. most gently
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. while &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. before &emsp;&emsp; D. after
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶邀请我进厨房做汤圆。invite sb into... 表示“邀请进入……”。invite in 亦可，但选项给 into 更明确“进入厨房”。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说家人要学会这项传统，以免它消失。before it disappears 表示“在它消失之前（要学会）”，符合保护传统的语境。if 如果；until 直到；unless 除非，语义不如 before 贴切。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把糯米粉与温水混合。mix...with... 为固定搭配。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我试着做一个小球。try to do sth 表示“努力尝试去做某事”，符合第一次搓汤圆的语境。try doing 多表示“试着用某种方式做”，此处不如 to make 贴切。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：多加一点水，轻轻按压。press 为动词，需用副词 gently 修饰。gentle 为形容词。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我渐渐能搓出里面有馅的圆球。with filling inside 表示“带有内馅”。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把汤圆下锅煮，看着它们在水开时翻滚。when the water rose 表示“当水位上升/水滚起来时”。while 多接延续性动词；before/after 不符合观看翻滚的时机描述。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：黑芝麻馅又烫又甜。hot 与 sweet 为并列形容词，用 and 连接。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：你比去年更有耐心。patient 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more patient；句中有 than。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得用心做出来的食物味道更好。暗含与不用心做的对比，用比较级 better。tastes 为系动词后接形容词。故选 B。
