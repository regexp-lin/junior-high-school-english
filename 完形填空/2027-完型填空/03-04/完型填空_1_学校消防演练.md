# 中考英语完形填空：学校消防演练

## 完形填空原文

Yesterday morning, our school organized ___1___ fire drill. When the alarm rang, our teacher told us ___2___ calm and line up quickly.

We covered our mouths ___3___ wet towels as we had learned in the safety video. The smoke machine made the corridor look a little scary, ___4___ nobody pushed or ran wildly.

On the playground, the firefighters showed us ___5___ to use a fire extinguisher. They also explained ___6___ we should never take the elevator during a fire.

I raised my hand ___7___ a question about escaping from a tall building. The officer answered patiently and praised us ___8___ listening carefully.

After the drill, I felt ___9___ than before because I knew what to do in an emergency. My friend joked that I walked back to class ___10___ I had just finished a race.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. stay &emsp;&emsp; B. to stay &emsp;&emsp; C. staying &emsp;&emsp; D. stayed
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where
6. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. which
7. A. ask &emsp;&emsp; B. asking &emsp;&emsp; C. to ask &emsp;&emsp; D. asked
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. safe &emsp;&emsp; B. safer &emsp;&emsp; C. safest &emsp;&emsp; D. safely
10. A. as if &emsp;&emsp; B. even if &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校组织了一场消防演练。fire 以辅音音素开头，用 a fire drill。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作宾补）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们保持冷静并快速排队。tell sb to do sth 为固定结构，用 to stay。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们用湿毛巾捂住口鼻。with 表示“用；借助”。in/on/by 搭配不如 with 自然。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：烟雾机让走廊看起来有点吓人，但没有人推挤或乱跑。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：消防员向我们展示如何使用灭火器。how to use 表示方式。what to use 强调事物；when/where 不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们还解释为什么火灾时不能乘电梯。explain 后接宾语从句，说明原因用 why。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我举手想问一个问题。不定式 to ask 表目的。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：警官耐心回答并表扬我们认真听讲。praise sb for (doing) sth 表示“因……表扬某人”。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：演练后我感到比以前更安全。felt 为系动词，后接形容词；有 than，用比较级 safer。safely 为副词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：朋友开玩笑说我走回教室的样子就像刚跑完一场比赛。as if 表示“好像；仿佛”。even if 即使；so that 以便；as long as 只要。故选 A。
