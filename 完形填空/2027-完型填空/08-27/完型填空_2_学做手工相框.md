# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工相框

## 完形填空原文

I wanted to give my best friend a special gift ___1___ her birthday. After thinking for a while, I decided to make a photo frame ___2___ hand.

I bought some colored cardboard, stickers, and ribbons from the stationery shop. My older sister ___3___ me cut the cardboard into the right shape. It was not as ___4___ as I thought because the edges had to be perfectly straight.

Next, I decorated the frame ___5___ small flowers and stars. I also wrote "Best Friends Forever" ___6___ the top. The most difficult part was ___7___ the photo in place without damaging it.

When my friend opened the gift, her eyes became ___8___ with tears of joy. "This is the ___9___ gift I have ever received!" she said. I was so touched that I almost cried ___10___. A handmade gift truly carries more love than anything bought from a store.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. with
3. A. helped &emsp;&emsp; B. let &emsp;&emsp; C. made &emsp;&emsp; D. asked
4. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easily &emsp;&emsp; C. hard &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
5. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. under
7. A. fix &emsp;&emsp; B. fixed &emsp;&emsp; C. fixing &emsp;&emsp; D. to fix
8. A. fill &emsp;&emsp; B. filling &emsp;&emsp; C. filled &emsp;&emsp; D. fills
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. well &emsp;&emsp; D. best
10. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. also &emsp;&emsp; C. either &emsp;&emsp; D. neither

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想给好朋友一份生日特别礼物。for one's birthday 表示"为了某人的生日"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定手工制作一个相框。by hand 为固定搭配，表示"手工地"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与用法
**【解析】** 本句意为：我姐姐帮我把硬纸板剪成合适的形状。help sb. (to) do sth. 表示"帮助某人做某事"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与 as...as 结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：这没有我想的那么容易。as ... as 结构中间用形容词/副词原级；语境表示"不像我想的那么简单"，用 easy。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：接着我用小花和星星装饰了相框。decorate ... with ... 表示"用……装饰"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还在顶部写了"Best Friends Forever"。at the top 表示"在顶部"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：最难的部分是把照片固定好又不损坏它。was 后接动名词 fixing 作表语，描述"是什么"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的眼里充满了喜悦的泪水。become filled with 表示"充满"，filled 为过去分词作表语。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我收到过的最好的礼物！由 I have ever received（现在完成时）提示用最高级，good 的最高级为 best。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感动得差点也哭了。too 用于肯定句末尾，表示"也"。故选 A。
