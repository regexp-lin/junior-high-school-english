# 中考英语完形填空：和同学一起打羽毛球

## 完形填空原文

Every Tuesday after school, Lin Tao and I play badminton ___1___ the gym. Yesterday it rained heavily, ___2___ we still went there on time. The floor was a little wet, ___3___ the coach asked us to warm up carefully.

I used ___4___ miss the shuttlecock easily, but now I can hit it ___5___ than before. Lin Tao taught me ___6___ move my feet quickly and keep my eyes on the bird.

During the match, I jumped ___7___ high and smashed the shuttlecock down. My classmates cheered, ___8___ I lost the next point because I was too excited. “Stay calm,” Lin Tao said ___9___ a low voice.

On the way home, we planned ___10___ practice again next week. Exercise makes our friendship stronger.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. steady &emsp;&emsp; B. steadier &emsp;&emsp; C. steadiest &emsp;&emsp; D. the steadiest
6. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
7. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. quite &emsp;&emsp; D. pretty
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（in the gym）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每周二放学后，林涛和我在体育馆打羽毛球。表示在体育馆内部用 in。on 在……上；at 可表地点但 in the gym 更常见；to 表方向。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（转折）
**【解析】** 本句意为：昨天下大雨，但我们仍然准时到达。转折用 but。and 并列；or 选择；so 结果。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（because 表原因）
**【解析】** 本句意为：地面有点湿，因此教练让我们认真热身。前后为因果关系，用 because。if 条件；although 让步；unless 除非。故选 B。
