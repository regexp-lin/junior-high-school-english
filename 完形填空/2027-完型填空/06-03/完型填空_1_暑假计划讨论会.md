# 中考英语完形填空：暑假计划讨论会

## 完形填空原文

Our class had a discussion ___1___ summer holiday plans yesterday. The teacher asked each student ___2___ share their ideas.

Some classmates planned to travel with their families. Others wanted to stay at home and ___3___ new skills. My friend Lily said she would volunteer ___4___ the community library because she loved reading.

I told everyone that I hoped ___5___ my swimming skills during the holiday. Last summer, I was afraid ___6___ deep water, but this year I want to be ___7___ enough to swim in the sea.

The teacher listened to us ___8___ and then gave some advice. She reminded us ___9___ we should also finish our homework on time. The discussion made everyone ___10___ excited about the coming holiday.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. about &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. / &emsp;&emsp; D. of
3. A. learn &emsp;&emsp; B. learned &emsp;&emsp; C. learning &emsp;&emsp; D. to learn
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. to
5. A. improve &emsp;&emsp; B. to improve &emsp;&emsp; C. improving &emsp;&emsp; D. improved
6. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on
7. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. bravely &emsp;&emsp; C. braver &emsp;&emsp; D. bravest
8. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. careless
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. who
10. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. felt &emsp;&emsp; C. feeling &emsp;&emsp; D. to feel

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（about 关于）
**【解析】** 本句意为：昨天我们班开了一个关于暑假计划的讨论会。"discussion about"表示"关于……的讨论"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让每个同学分享他们的想法。"ask sb. to do sth."为固定结构。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词原形（and 连接并列结构）
**【解析】** 本句意为：其他人想待在家里学习新技能。and 连接 stay 和 learn，两者并列，均用原形。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（at + 地点）
**【解析】** 本句意为：Lily 说她要在社区图书馆做志愿者。at 表示在某个具体地点。in 可以但 at 更强调具体场所。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（hope to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我告诉大家我希望暑假能提高游泳技术。"hope to do sth."表示"希望做某事"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be afraid of）
**【解析】** 本句意为：去年夏天，我怕深水。"be afraid of"表示"害怕……"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词原级（adj. + enough to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：今年我想变得足够勇敢去海里游泳。"be + adj. + enough to do"结构中用形容词原级。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师认真地听了我们的分享。修饰动词 listened 用副词 carefully。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 宾语从句（that 引导）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她提醒我们应该按时完成作业。remind sb. that... 引导宾语从句，that 作引导词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 使役动词（make sb. + adj./do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：讨论让每个人都对即将到来的假期感到兴奋。"make sb. + 动词原形"或"make sb. + adj."，此处 feel excited 中 feel 用原形。故选 A。
