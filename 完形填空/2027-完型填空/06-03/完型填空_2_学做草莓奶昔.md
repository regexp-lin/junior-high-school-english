# 中考英语完形填空：学做草莓奶昔

## 完形填空原文

My cousin visited us last Sunday. She is very good ___1___ making drinks. She offered to teach me how to make ___2___ strawberry milkshake.

We prepared six large strawberries, a cup of milk and ___3___ ice cream. "Wash the strawberries first and cut off the green parts," she said ___4___ me.

After that, I put all the ingredients ___5___ the blender. My cousin pressed the button and the machine started ___6___ loudly. In just thirty ___7___, everything was mixed into a smooth pink drink.

I poured the milkshake into two tall glasses. It looked ___8___ beautiful that I took a photo before drinking. The taste was much ___9___ than the ones sold in shops.

My cousin told me the secret was ___10___ fresh fruits. I decided to try different flavors next time.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. for
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
3. A. a few &emsp;&emsp; B. few &emsp;&emsp; C. some &emsp;&emsp; D. any
4. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
6. A. work &emsp;&emsp; B. works &emsp;&emsp; C. working &emsp;&emsp; D. to work
7. A. seconds &emsp;&emsp; B. minutes &emsp;&emsp; C. hours &emsp;&emsp; D. moment
8. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. such &emsp;&emsp; D. too
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well
10. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. used &emsp;&emsp; C. using &emsp;&emsp; D. to use

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be good at）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她非常擅长做饮品。"be good at"表示"擅长……"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她提出教我做一杯草莓奶昔。strawberry 以辅音开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定代词（some 用于肯定句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们准备了六颗大草莓、一杯牛奶和一些冰淇淋。肯定句中表示"一些"用 some。ice cream 为不可数名词，a few/few 修饰可数名词。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（say to sb.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她对我说。"say to sb."表示"对某人说"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（put...into...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把所有材料放进搅拌机。"put...into..."表示"放进……里面"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（start doing sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐按下按钮，机器开始大声运转。"start doing sth."表示"开始做某事"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（语境判断）
**【解析】** 本句意为：仅仅三十秒，一切都被搅拌成了顺滑的粉色饮品。搅拌机工作很快，thirty seconds 最合理。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定结构（so + adj. + that）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它看起来如此漂亮以至于我先拍了张照再喝。"so + adj. + that"为固定结构。such 后接名词短语。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（much + 比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道比店里卖的好多了。than 提示用比较级，much 修饰比较级 better。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动名词作表语
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐告诉我秘诀是使用新鲜水果。主语 the secret 为事物，表语用动名词 using 表示"做某事"。故选 C。
