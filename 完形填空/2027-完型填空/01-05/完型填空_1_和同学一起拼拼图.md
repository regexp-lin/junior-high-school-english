# 中考英语完形填空：和同学一起拼拼图

## 完形填空原文

Last winter, my classmate Wang Lei brought a huge jigsaw puzzle to school. It had one thousand pieces and showed a picture of a beautiful ___1___. He suggested that we work on it together during the lunch break. I thought it sounded like fun, so I agreed.

At first, we poured all the pieces onto a big table. Looking at the pile, I felt a little ___2___. There were so many tiny pieces that it seemed impossible to put them together. But Wang Lei said, "Don't worry. Let's start with the ___3___ pieces and build the frame first."

We sorted the pieces by color and shape. I was in charge of finding all the blue pieces for the sky, while Wang Lei worked on the ___4___ parts. Some other classmates came over and wanted to help too. Soon, six of us were sitting around the table, each focusing on a different section.

The work was slow but ___5___. Whenever someone found the right piece, everyone would cheer. I noticed that the key to finishing a puzzle was not speed but ___6___. You had to try many pieces before finding the one that fit perfectly.

After three days, we finally ___7___ the puzzle. Everyone gathered around to admire it. The picture showed a castle on a mountain with colorful flowers around it. It was even more beautiful than we had ___8___.

Our teacher saw the finished puzzle and praised our teamwork. She said, "Each piece is small and looks ___9___ on its own, but together they create something wonderful." I think the same is true for people. When we work together, we can ___10___ great things that none of us could do alone.

1. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. scene &emsp;&emsp; C. dream &emsp;&emsp; D. song
2. A. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. hopeless &emsp;&emsp; D. pleased
3. A. middle &emsp;&emsp; B. edge &emsp;&emsp; C. center &emsp;&emsp; D. top
4. A. green &emsp;&emsp; B. white &emsp;&emsp; C. red &emsp;&emsp; D. black
5. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. tiring &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoyable &emsp;&emsp; D. painful
6. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. courage &emsp;&emsp; C. patience &emsp;&emsp; D. speed
7. A. broke &emsp;&emsp; B. completed &emsp;&emsp; C. lost &emsp;&emsp; D. returned
8. A. feared &emsp;&emsp; B. imagined &emsp;&emsp; C. forgotten &emsp;&emsp; D. planned
9. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. perfect &emsp;&emsp; C. expensive &emsp;&emsp; D. heavy
10. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. avoid &emsp;&emsp; C. achieve &emsp;&emsp; D. collect

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：拼图展示了一幅美丽的风景画面。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"场景/风景"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"歌曲"。a beautiful scene 描述拼图画面。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看着那堆碎片我感到有点绝望。A 项意为"放松的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"绝望的"；D 项意为"高兴的"。一千块碎片让人觉得 hopeless。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：先从边缘拼块开始搭框架。A 项意为"中间"；B 项意为"边缘"；C 项意为"中心"；D 项意为"顶部"。拼图策略是先拼 edge pieces 做边框。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（颜色）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我负责蓝色天空，王磊做绿色部分。A 项意为"绿色的"；B 项意为"白色的"；C 项意为"红色的"；D 项意为"黑色的"。后文提到山上有城堡和花，green 对应草地/山。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：工作虽慢但令人愉快。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"累人的"；C 项意为"令人愉快的"；D 项意为"痛苦的"。but 表转折，slow but enjoyable。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：完成拼图的关键不是速度而是耐心。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"耐心"；D 项意为"速度"。need to try many pieces 说明需要 patience。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：三天后我们终于完成了拼图。A 项意为"打破"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"丢失"；D 项意为"归还"。completed the puzzle 意为"拼完拼图"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比我们想象的还要美。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"想象"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"计划"。more beautiful than imagined。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每块单独看起来没用，但合在一起能创造美好的东西。A 项意为"没用的"；B 项意为"完美的"；C 项意为"昂贵的"；D 项意为"重的"。useless on its own 与 but together 形成对比。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们合作时，能实现一个人做不到的伟大事情。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"避免"；C 项意为"实现"；D 项意为"收集"。achieve great things 意为"成就大事"。故选 C。
