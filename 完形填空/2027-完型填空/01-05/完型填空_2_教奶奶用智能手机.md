# 中考英语完形填空：教奶奶用智能手机

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, my grandmother got a new smartphone as a gift from my uncle. She was happy but also ___1___ because she had never used one before. "Can you teach me?" she asked. I said yes without thinking twice.

I started with the ___2___ things—how to turn it on, how to make a phone call, and how to send a text message. Grandma held the phone carefully as if it might break. She pressed the buttons very slowly and kept asking, "Am I doing it ___3___?"

The hardest part was teaching her to use WeChat. She could not ___4___ how to type Chinese characters on the small screen. Her fingers were not as flexible as mine, and she often pressed the wrong letters. But she never got frustrated. She just laughed at her ___5___ and tried again.

After a whole afternoon of practice, Grandma could finally send a voice message. She was so excited that she ___6___ sent one to every family member. My uncle received a message saying, "I learned how to use this thing!" Everyone thought it was very cute.

Over the next few days, I taught Grandma how to take photos and ___7___ them on her WeChat Moments. She took pictures of everything—her garden, her cooking, and even our dog. Her friends from the neighborhood soon started ___8___ her posts.

One evening, Grandma held my hand and said, "Thank you for being so ___9___ with me. I know it was not easy." I told her it was actually fun. Teaching her made me realize that learning new things takes time and effort, no matter how ___10___ you are. What matters most is the willingness to try.

## 选项

1. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. brave &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
2. A. strangest &emsp;&emsp; B. hardest &emsp;&emsp; C. simplest &emsp;&emsp; D. funniest
3. A. right &emsp;&emsp; B. fast &emsp;&emsp; C. wrong &emsp;&emsp; D. late
4. A. stand &emsp;&emsp; B. explain &emsp;&emsp; C. figure out &emsp;&emsp; D. give up
5. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; C. dreams &emsp;&emsp; D. excuses
6. A. secretly &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. sadly &emsp;&emsp; D. immediately
7. A. delete &emsp;&emsp; B. share &emsp;&emsp; C. hide &emsp;&emsp; D. print
8. A. ignoring &emsp;&emsp; B. copying &emsp;&emsp; C. liking &emsp;&emsp; D. reporting
9. A. strict &emsp;&emsp; B. patient &emsp;&emsp; C. honest &emsp;&emsp; D. quiet
10. A. old &emsp;&emsp; B. rich &emsp;&emsp; C. young &emsp;&emsp; D. tall

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶高兴但也担心，因为从没用过智能手机。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"勇敢的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。happy but worried 表达矛盾心情。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从最简单的事情开始教。A 项意为"最奇怪的"；B 项意为"最难的"；C 项意为"最简单的"；D 项意为"最有趣的"。从简单的开始教最合理。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她不断问"我做对了吗？"。A 项意为"对/正确"；B 项意为"快"；C 项意为"错"；D 项意为"迟"。学新东西时确认 doing it right。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她不知道怎么在小屏幕上打中文。A 项意为"忍受"；B 项意为"解释"；C 项 figure out 意为"弄明白"；D 项意为"放弃"。could not figure out 表示弄不明白。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她只是笑着面对自己的错误然后再试。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"借口"。按错字母是 mistakes。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她太兴奋了，立刻给每个家人都发了一条。A 项意为"秘密地"；B 项意为"小心地"；C 项意为"伤心地"；D 项意为"立即"。so excited that...immediately 表示迫不及待。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：教奶奶拍照并分享到朋友圈。A 项意为"删除"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"隐藏"；D 项意为"打印"。share them on WeChat Moments。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：邻居朋友们开始给她的帖子点赞。A 项意为"忽视"；B 项意为"复制"；C 项意为"点赞"；D 项意为"举报"。liking her posts 意为"给帖子点赞"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：谢谢你对我这么有耐心。A 项意为"严格的"；B 项意为"有耐心的"；C 项意为"诚实的"；D 项意为"安静的"。patient with me 表示对我有耐心。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：无论多大年纪，学新东西都需要时间和努力。A 项意为"年老的"；B 项意为"富有的"；C 项意为"年轻的"；D 项意为"高的"。文章讲的是老年人学技术，no matter how old 最合语境。故选 A。
