# 中考英语完形填空：帮社区老人量血压

## 完形填空原文

Our school club organized a health day in the community center. I volunteered ___1___ help measure blood pressure for elderly neighbors.

A nurse trained us first. She showed us how to wrap the cuff around the arm. She asked us ___2___ quietly while the machine worked. She also taught us ___3___ to read the numbers on the screen.

My first "patient" was Grandma Liu. She joked that her arm was ___4___ than last year because she ate too many dumplings during the holiday. Her numbers were normal, ___5___ she looked relieved.

Some seniors spoke dialect, ___6___ I listened carefully and repeated key words in Mandarin. When they thanked me, I felt ___7___ useful.

The activity lasted three hours. My legs ached, ___8___ my heart felt light. I learned that small community services can bring neighbors ___9___ together. I hope to take part ___10___ events like this again.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. sit &emsp;&emsp; B. sitting &emsp;&emsp; C. to sit &emsp;&emsp; D. sat
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
4. A. thick &emsp;&emsp; B. thicker &emsp;&emsp; C. thickest &emsp;&emsp; D. thin
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. although
7. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. really &emsp;&emsp; C. true &emsp;&emsp; D. truly
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. far &emsp;&emsp; B. closer &emsp;&emsp; C. apart &emsp;&emsp; D. away
10. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（volunteer to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我志愿帮助给老年邻居量血压。volunteer to do sth. 志愿做某事。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她让我们在机器工作时安静坐着。ask sb. to do sth. 让某人做某事。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：她还教我们如何读屏幕上的数字。how to read 如何读。what 什么；why 为什么；when 何时。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她开玩笑说手臂比去年更粗了。than 提示比较级 thicker。thick 原级；thickest 最高级；thin 细。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：数值正常，她看起来也放心了。并列顺承，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些老人说方言，所以我认真听并用普通话重复关键词。因果关系，用 so。because 因为；if 如果；although 尽管。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当他们感谢我时，我真的觉得自己有用。really 真地，修饰形容词 useful。real 形容词；true 真实的；truly 同 truly，但 felt really useful 更口语自然。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的腿酸了，但心里很轻松。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我了解到小型社区服务能把邻里拉得更近。bring neighbors closer together 让邻里更亲近。far 远；apart 分开；away 离开。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望再次参加这样的活动。take part in 参加，为固定搭配。故选 C。
