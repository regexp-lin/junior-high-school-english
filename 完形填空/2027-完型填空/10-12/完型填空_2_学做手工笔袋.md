# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工笔袋

## 完形填空原文

Our school's sewing club held a workshop last Thursday. The topic was making a simple pencil case from cloth. I was one of the twenty students ___1___ signed up.

The teacher prepared cloth in different colors, needles, thread, and zippers. She warned us ___2___ careful with the needles because they were very sharp. Then she drew a pattern on the blackboard and explained each step clearly.

I chose a piece of dark blue cloth and ___3___ it in half. The teacher said the first thing to do was to sew the two sides together, ___4___ the top open for the zipper. I threaded the needle and started sewing.

Sewing was much ___5___ than I had thought. My stitches were uneven, and the thread kept getting tangled. I almost wanted to ___6___, but then I looked around and saw that most of my classmates were having the same problem. This made me feel ___7___.

After thirty minutes of patient work, I managed ___8___ the sides neatly. Then the teacher helped me attach the zipper. When I finally held the finished pencil case in my hands, I could hardly believe it was made ___9___ me.

I put my pens and erasers inside and zipped it up. The teacher said proudly, "You have all proved that you can create ___10___ useful with your own hands."

## 选项

1. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
2. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. being &emsp;&emsp; C. to be &emsp;&emsp; D. been  
3. A. fold &emsp;&emsp; B. folds &emsp;&emsp; C. folded &emsp;&emsp; D. folding  
4. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. leaving &emsp;&emsp; C. left &emsp;&emsp; D. to leave  
5. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. harder &emsp;&emsp; D. hardest  
6. A. give in &emsp;&emsp; B. give up &emsp;&emsp; C. give out &emsp;&emsp; D. give off  
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
8. A. sew &emsp;&emsp; B. sewed &emsp;&emsp; C. to sew &emsp;&emsp; D. sewing  
9. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
10. A. something &emsp;&emsp; B. anything &emsp;&emsp; C. nothing &emsp;&emsp; D. everything  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词（指人）  
**【解析】** 先行词 students 为人，在从句中作主语，用 who 引导定语从句。which 指物；what 不引导定语从句；whom 指人但作宾语。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（warn sb. to do）  
**【解析】** warn sb. to be careful 意为"警告某人要小心"，不定式作宾补。be 为原形；being 为动名词；been 为过去分词，均不能用于 warn sb. 后。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（并列谓语）  
**【解析】** and 连接 chose 和 folded，两个并列谓语时态一致用过去式。fold 为原形；folds 为三单现在时；folding 为现在分词。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作伴随状语  
**【解析】** leaving the top open 为现在分词短语作伴随状语，表"同时留出顶部开口"。leave 为原形；left 为过去式/过去分词；to leave 表目的但此处为伴随动作更恰当。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 句中有 than，需用比较级。hard 的比较级为 harder。hard 为原级；hardly 意为"几乎不"，含义完全不同；hardest 为最高级。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析  
**【解析】** give up 意为"放弃"，符合"几乎想放弃"的语境。give in 意为"屈服、让步"；give out 意为"分发、耗尽"；give off 意为"发出（气味、光等）"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（隐含比较）  
**【解析】** 发现别人也有同样的问题后让"我"感觉"更好了"（比之前好），为隐含比较，用比较级 better。good 为原级；well 作形容词表"身体好"；best 为最高级。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（manage to do）  
**【解析】** manage to do sth. 意为"设法做成某事"，后接不定式。sew 为原形；sewed 为过去式；sewing 为动名词，均不用于 manage 后。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 by 表被动施事者  
**【解析】** be made by sb. 意为"被某人制作"，by 表示被动语态的施事者。from 表材料（be made from）；of 表材料（be made of）；with 表工具。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 something  
**【解析】** something useful 意为"有用的东西"，something 用于肯定句中，形容词后置修饰。anything 用于否定句/疑问句；nothing 意为"没有东西"；everything 意为"所有东西"，均不如 something 自然。故选 A。
