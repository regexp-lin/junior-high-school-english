# 中考英语完形填空：帮邻居遛狗

## 完形填空原文

My neighbor Mrs. Chen broke her leg last month, so she couldn't walk her dog by ___1___. When I heard about it, I volunteered to help her every evening after finishing my homework.

Her dog, Lucky, is a golden retriever. The first time I took him out, he was too ___2___ to control. He kept pulling the leash and running in all directions. I was almost dragged ___3___ several times.

Gradually, I learned some tricks. My father told me to hold the leash ___4___ and give clear commands like "Sit" and "Stay." I practiced with Lucky every day, and he became more and more ___5___.

One evening, while we were walking in the park, Lucky suddenly stopped and ___6___ at a bush. A small kitten was hiding inside, looking scared. I gently picked ___7___ the kitten and brought it to the neighborhood committee. They helped find its owner the next day.

Mrs. Chen was very grateful ___8___ my help. She said that Lucky had never been so well-behaved before. I told her that ___9___ care of Lucky also taught me a lot about responsibility.

Now Mrs. Chen has recovered, but she still asks me to join her for walks ___10___. Lucky and I have become the best friends.

## 选项

1. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself  
2. A. energy &emsp;&emsp; B. energetic &emsp;&emsp; C. energetically &emsp;&emsp; D. energies  
3. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. down &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. out  
4. A. tight &emsp;&emsp; B. tightly &emsp;&emsp; C. tighter &emsp;&emsp; D. tightest  
5. A. obey &emsp;&emsp; B. obeyed &emsp;&emsp; C. obedient &emsp;&emsp; D. obediently  
6. A. bark &emsp;&emsp; B. barks &emsp;&emsp; C. barked &emsp;&emsp; D. barking  
7. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. up &emsp;&emsp; C. out &emsp;&emsp; D. off  
8. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about  
9. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. took &emsp;&emsp; C. taken &emsp;&emsp; D. taking  
10. A. sometime &emsp;&emsp; B. sometimes &emsp;&emsp; C. some time &emsp;&emsp; D. some times  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 反身代词 by oneself  
**【解析】** by herself 意为"她自己"，表独自完成动作。she 为主格；her 为宾格或形容词性物主代词；hers 为名词性物主代词，均不与 by 搭配表"独自"。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** too + 形容词 + to do  
**【解析】** too...to... 结构中 too 后接形容词，energetic（精力充沛的）为形容词。energy 为名词；energetically 为副词；energies 为名词复数。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 drag down  
**【解析】** be dragged down 意为"被拖倒"，down 表示向下的动作。drag up/in/out 不表"摔倒"之意。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 hold 需用副词 tightly（紧紧地）。tight 作副词时也可修饰动词（如 hold tight），但 tightly 更正式且常用于书面表达。tighter/tightest 为比较级和最高级，此处无比较。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语（more and more + adj.）  
**【解析】** more and more + 形容词原级构成递进比较，became 为系动词后接形容词 obedient（听话的）。obey 为动词；obeyed 为过去式；obediently 为副词。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（并列谓语）  
**【解析】** and 连接两个并列谓语 stopped 和 barked，时态一致用过去式。bark 为原形；barks 为三单现在时；barking 为现在分词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 pick up  
**【解析】** pick up 意为"捡起、抱起"，符合"轻轻抱起小猫"的语境。pick on 意为"欺负"；pick out 意为"挑选"；pick off 意为"摘掉"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be grateful for  
**【解析】** be grateful for sth. 意为"因某事而感激"。of 不与 grateful 搭配；with 不构成此义；about 不常见于此搭配。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 动名词作主语  
**【解析】** taking care of Lucky 为动名词短语作主语，意为"照顾 Lucky 这件事"。take 为原形不能作主语；took 为过去式不能作主语；taken 为过去分词不能单独作主语。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词辨析  
**【解析】** sometimes 意为"有时"，表频率，符合"有时邀请我一起散步"的语境。sometime 意为"某个时候"（单数时间点）；some time 意为"一段时间"；some times 意为"几次"，均不如 sometimes 通顺。故选 B。
