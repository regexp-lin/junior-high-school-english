# 中考英语完形填空：学折收纳纸盒

## 完形填空原文

Our art teacher asked each group ___1___ a storage box out of old cards. My group chose blue paper ___2___ looked thick enough to hold small tools.

At first, I couldn’t understand ___3___ the lines should be folded. My partner drew a simple map on another card ___4___ me follow the steps more easily.

Halfway through, one corner ___5___ by mistake. We had to start again, ___6___ nobody complained. The second try went ___7___ smoothly than the first.

When we finally finished, our box ___8___ on the teacher’s desk with the others. She smiled and said, “These boxes may be simple, ___9___ they show your creativity.” I realized ___10___ teamwork could change a difficult task into a happy memory.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. where  
4. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
5. A. tears &emsp;&emsp; B. tore &emsp;&emsp; C. was torn &emsp;&emsp; D. has torn  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  
8. A. puts &emsp;&emsp; B. put &emsp;&emsp; C. was put &emsp;&emsp; D. has put  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. how  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do）  
**【解析】** ask each group to make... 为 ask sb. to do sth. 结构。make/making/made 不符合 ask 的搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词（指物）  
**【解析】** 先行词为 blue paper，从句缺主语，指物用 which。who/whom 指人；what 不引导定语从句。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词（方式）  
**【解析】** understand 后为宾语从句，表示“线应该如何折叠”，用 how。what/which/where 与 fold the lines 的方式不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）  
**【解析】** drew a map... to help me follow 表目的，用不定式 to help。help/helping/helped 不能直接表目的。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** corner 与 tear“撕裂”为被动关系，且叙述过去，用 was torn。tears 为名词或三单谓语；tore 为主动；has torn 时态与全文不一致。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 转折连词  
**【解析】** “不得不重来”与“没人抱怨”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不当。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级  
**【解析】** than the first 提示比较级，more smoothly 更顺利。very/too 无比较级；most 为最高级缺范围。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** box 被放在讲台上，用 was put。puts/has put 主动且时态不当；put 过去式主动可表示“我们放”，但主语为 our box，盒子不能自己放，需被动。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折）not... but 对应 / simple... but  
**【解析】** “盒子或许简单”与“它们展现创造力”为转折，用 but。May be simple, but they show... 为常见结构。and/or/so 不符。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 how  
**【解析】** realized 后为宾语从句，表示“团队合作如何能把困难任务变成美好回忆”，用 how 表方式。what 需在从句中作主宾；that 无实义；whether 表是否。故选 D。
