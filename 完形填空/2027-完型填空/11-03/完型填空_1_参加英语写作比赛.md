# 中考英语完形填空：英语写作比赛那天

## 完形填空原文

Two weeks ago, our English teacher encouraged me ___1___ an essay competition in the city library. I was shy at first, ___2___ I knew it was a good chance to improve my writing.

I spent five evenings ___3___ my draft. My teacher read it and suggested ___4___ some connecting words to make the ideas clearer. I followed her advice and felt ___5___ confident than before.

On the competition day, I arrived ten minutes early. While other students ___6___ nervously, I took a deep breath and told ___7___ to stay calm. When the topic was announced, I was glad ___8___ I had practiced similar questions at home.

After the results came out, I found that I ___9___ the second prize. My parents were happy, and they said the experience ___10___ me a useful lesson about patience.

## 选项

1. A. enter &emsp;&emsp; B. entering &emsp;&emsp; C. to enter &emsp;&emsp; D. entered  
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
3. A. polish &emsp;&emsp; B. polishing &emsp;&emsp; C. to polish &emsp;&emsp; D. polished  
4. A. add &emsp;&emsp; B. adding &emsp;&emsp; C. to add &emsp;&emsp; D. added  
5. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. many  
6. A. talk &emsp;&emsp; B. talked &emsp;&emsp; C. were talking &emsp;&emsp; D. have talked  
7. A. me &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. mine &emsp;&emsp; D. myself  
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
9. A. win &emsp;&emsp; B. won &emsp;&emsp; C. have won &emsp;&emsp; D. had won  
10. A. teaches &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. has taught &emsp;&emsp; D. had taught  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（encourage sb. to do）  
**【解析】** encourage sb. to do sth. 为固定结构，用不定式 to enter。enter/entering/entered 不能直接作 encourage 的宾补。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 让步连词 although  
**【解析】** “起初害羞”与“知道这是好机会”为让步关系，用 although“虽然”。because 表原因；unless 表除非；if 表条件。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 spend time (in) doing sth.  
**【解析】** spend five evenings polishing 表示“花费五个晚上润色”，用动名词。polish/to polish/polished 不符合 spend 的常见搭配。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（suggest doing）  
**【解析】** suggest 后接动名词作宾语，suggest adding... 表示建议增加连接词。add/to add/added 不符合 suggest 用法。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** than before 提示用比较级 more confident。much 修饰比较级但不单独构成 confident 的比较级；most/many 不适用。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去进行时  
**【解析】** While 强调背景动作“其他学生正在紧张地交谈”，与主句动作同时进行，用 were talking。talk/talked/have talked 不能突出同时进行的氛围。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 反身代词  
**【解析】** tell oneself to stay calm 表示“告诉自己保持冷静”，主语为 I，用 myself。me/my/mine 无“对自己”之意。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 that（形容词 glad 后）  
**【解析】** be glad that... 后接陈述从句，说明高兴的原因，从句成分完整用 that。what/whether/how 不符合 glad 后宾语从句的常见结构。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** “结果公布后我发现自己已获二等奖”，获奖发生在 found 之前，过去的过去，用过去完成时 had won。win 原形；won 一般过去时不能体现先于 found；have won 现在完成时与时间线不符。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 父母说话的动作发生在过去叙事中，用一般过去时 taught（此处 said 后宾语从句谓语与整体过去一致）。teaches 现在时；has taught/had taught 在此语境不如一般过去简洁自然。故选 B。
