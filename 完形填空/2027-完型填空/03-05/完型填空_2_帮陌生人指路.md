# 中考英语完形填空：帮陌生人指路

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, I waited ___1___ my mom near the subway exit. A tourist walked up ___2___ a map in his hand and asked me ___3___ in English.

His pronunciation was unclear, ___4___ I listened twice and guessed he wanted the history museum. I pointed ___5___ the sign across the street and said, "Go straight and turn left ___6___ the second crossing."

He looked worried, so I walked ___7___ him for two minutes to show the crossing. He bowed and gave me a small badge ___8___ a thank you gift.

When my mom arrived, she praised me ___9___ being polite and patient. I realized helping others does not always take ___10___ time, but it can mean a lot.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. for help &emsp;&emsp; D. to help
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. by
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
10. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在地铁口等妈妈。wait for sb 为固定搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位游客手里拿着地图走过来。with 表示“带着；拿着”。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他用英语向我求助。ask sb for help 为固定短语。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的发音不清楚，但我听了两遍并猜测他想去历史博物馆。转折关系用 but。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我指着街对面的指示牌。point at 表示“指向（具体目标）”。point to 侧重方向；point on 不常用。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：直走，在第二个十字路口左转。at the crossing 为常见表达。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：于是我和他一起走两分钟到路口。walk with sb 表示“与某人一起走”。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他鞠躬并把一枚小徽章作为感谢礼物送给我。as 表示“作为”。like 像；for 为了；by 通过。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈表扬我有礼貌且有耐心。praise sb for (doing) sth 表示“因……表扬某人”。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助别人并不总要花很多时间。time 为不可数名词，用 much 修饰。many 修饰可数名词复数。故选 B。
