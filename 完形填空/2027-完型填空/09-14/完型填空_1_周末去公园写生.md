# 中考英语完形填空：周末去公园写生

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday morning, ___1___ the weather was bright and mild, my friend Lily and I went to the city park to sketch nature. We packed pencils, erasers, ___2___ a small folding chair so that we could sit comfortably by the lake.

When we arrived, the park was ___3___ than we had expected. Many families were flying kites, and soft music ___4___ everywhere among the trees. We looked for a quiet corner ___5___ a willow's branches almost touched the water.

Lily draws better than I do, so she ___6___ me a few simple tips ___7___ I put pencil to paper. "Notice the light first," she said. "The side ___8___ faces the sun should look brighter on your page."

I tried ___9___ follow her advice. An hour later, my picture looked much ___10___. We took photos of our sketches before we left. It was a peaceful and meaningful weekend morning.

## 选项

1. A. although &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. busier &emsp;&emsp; C. busiest &emsp;&emsp; D. busily
4. A. hear &emsp;&emsp; B. heard &emsp;&emsp; C. was heard &emsp;&emsp; D. is hearing
5. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. who
6. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. gives &emsp;&emsp; C. gave &emsp;&emsp; D. giving
7. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. /
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词（原因状语从句）
**【解析】** 句意为：因为天气晴朗温和，我和朋友莉莉去城市公园写生。前后为因果关系，应用 because 引导原因状语从句。although 表让步，unless 表“除非”，until 表“直到”，均不符合语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** pencils, erasers 与 a small folding chair 为并列宾语，表示“和、以及”，用 and 连接。but 表转折，or 表选择，so 表结果。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 句中有 than，需用比较级。busy 的比较级为 busier，表示“公园比我们预想的更热闹”。busily 为副词，busiest 为最高级，均不适用。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 主语 soft music 与动词 hear 之间为被动关系，且叙述过去场景，用一般过去时的被动语态 was heard，表示“轻柔的音乐在树林间被听到/四处可闻”。主动形式 hear、heard 及进行时不符合主谓逻辑。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系副词 where）
**【解析】** 先行词 corner 表示地点，从句 a willow's branches almost touched the water 结构完整，需用关系副词 where 在从句中作地点状语，相当于 in which。which/who 在从句中作主语或宾语，when 表时间，均不合适。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 全文叙述上周末经历，谓语动词用一般过去时 gave，表示“她给了我几条简单的建议”。give、gives 时态不对；giving 为非谓语，不能单独作谓语。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（时间关系）
**【解析】** 句意为：在我落笔之前，她先给了我建议。before 表示“在……之前”，符合“先指导再动笔”的顺序。after 表示之后，while 强调同时进行，since 多表“自从”，此处不如 before 贴切。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系代词 which/that）
**【解析】** 先行词 the side 指物，从句 faces the sun 缺主语，用关系代词 which 或 that。选项中只有 which，故选 C。who 指人，what 不引导此类定语从句。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** try to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示“努力/设法做某事”。try to follow her advice 意为“努力按她的建议去做”。for/of/with 均不能与 try 构成此义搭配。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（much 修饰比较级）
**【解析】** much 常修饰比较级，表示“……得多”。look 为系动词，后接形容词；此处暗含与之前对比，用 better。good、well、best 不能与 much 搭配表达此比较含义。故选 C。
