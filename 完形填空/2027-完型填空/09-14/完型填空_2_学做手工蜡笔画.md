# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工蜡笔画

## 完形填空原文

Our art teacher, Mr. Zhou, decided ___1___ a wax-crayon workshop in class last Friday. He said crayons were cheap and safe, ___2___ they were perfect for beginners like us.

First, he showed us how ___3___ the paper still so the wax would not smudge easily. Then he asked us ___4___ light colors before dark ones. "If you press too hard ___5___ the beginning," he warned, "you will have no room for details later."

I chose a picture of a small house ___6___ stood beside a river. I drew the outline carefully, ___7___ I filled the sky with light blue. When I added the roof, my hand shook and the line went crooked. I felt upset, ___8___ Mr. Zhou smiled and said mistakes could be turned into clouds or birds.

Following his idea, I tried ___9___ the "mistake" into flying birds. To my surprise, the work looked ___10___ lively than my first plan. That lesson taught me creativity sometimes begins with a small accident.

## 选项

1. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. having &emsp;&emsp; C. to have &emsp;&emsp; D. had
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. although
3. A. hold &emsp;&emsp; B. to hold &emsp;&emsp; C. holding &emsp;&emsp; D. held
4. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. using &emsp;&emsp; C. to use &emsp;&emsp; D. used
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
6. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
7. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. because
9. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. changing &emsp;&emsp; C. to change &emsp;&emsp; D. changed
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. much

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）
**【解析】** decide 后接不定式作宾语，decide to do sth. 表示“决定做某事”。decided to have a workshop 意为“决定举办一次蜡笔工作坊”。have/having/had 均不能直接跟在 decide 后作宾语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词（因果关系）
**【解析】** 前句说蜡笔便宜又安全，后句说它们很适合初学者，前后为因果顺承关系，用 so 连接，表示“因此、所以”。but 表转折，or 表选择，although 表让步。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（疑问词 + 不定式）
**【解析】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构作 showed 的宾语，show us how to hold the paper 表示“示范如何把纸拿稳”。how 后应接 to hold。hold/holding/held 形式不对。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do）
**【解析】** ask sb. to do sth. 为固定结构，表示“要求/让某人做某事”。asked us to use light colors 意为“让我们先用浅色”。use/using/used 均不符合 ask 的宾补结构。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词（固定短语）
**【解析】** at the beginning 为固定搭配，表示“在开始时”。in/on/to 与 the beginning 搭配不如 at 常见、贴切。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系代词 which）
**【解析】** 先行词 a small house 指物，从句 stood beside a river 缺主语，用关系代词 which 或 that；选项中只有 which。what 不引导定语从句，who/whom 指人。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** “我先仔细画轮廓”与“再用浅蓝色涂天空”为顺承关系，用 and 连接两个分句。if/unless 表条件，until 表“直到”，均不符合此处并列叙述的步骤关系。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折）
**【解析】** 前句写“我很沮丧”，后句写“周老师却微笑着说……”，语义转折，用 but。and 表并列，so 表结果，because 表原因，均不符合“情绪低落 vs 老师鼓励”的对比。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（try to do / try doing 辨析）
**【解析】** try to do sth. 侧重“努力去做”；try doing 侧重“试着做、尝试某种做法”。此处表示努力把那笔“失误”改成飞鸟，用 try to change 更贴切考试常见搭配与句意。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（more + 多音节形容词）
**【解析】** 句中有 than，且 lively 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more lively，表示“比最初的设想更生动”。very/much 不直接构成比较级，most 为最高级。故选 B。
