# 中考英语完形填空：年夜饭准备

## 完形填空原文

On New Year's Eve morning, our kitchen turned ___1___ a busy little factory. My mother wrote a shopping list while my father sharpened his knife ___2___ cutting fish. I was asked to wash vegetables ___3___ running water and peel potatoes carefully.

The air smelled ___4___ ginger, soy sauce, and steaming soup. My little sister wanted to help, ___5___ she dropped a plate and almost cried. Mother hugged her and said mistakes happen when people care ___6___.

We worked in a clear order: soup first, then cold dishes, and hot dishes ___7___. By noon, dumpling filling waited in a big bowl, and trays of chopped ingredients lined ___8___ like soldiers. Father tasted the broth and added a pinch ___9___ salt.

When relatives arrived in the evening, the table was full ___10___ color and laughter. I understood then that preparing the meal was as warm as eating it together.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. across
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. under &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. against
4. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. from
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. enough &emsp;&emsp; B. too much &emsp;&emsp; C. a lot &emsp;&emsp; D. very much
7. A. at first &emsp;&emsp; B. at last &emsp;&emsp; C. at once &emsp;&emsp; D. at least
8. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. off
9. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. in

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房变成了忙碌的小工厂。turn into 变成。A 项 turn on 打开；C 项 turn over 翻转；D 项 turn across 非固定搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父亲磨刀以便切鱼。for 表目的。A 项 to 虽可表目的，但 sharpen...for doing 结构此处用 for cutting 更自然；B 项 of；D 项 with。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在自来水下冲洗蔬菜。under running water 在水流下。A 项 in；C 项 through；D 项 against。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：空气里弥漫着姜、酱油和炖汤的气味。smell of 有……的气味。A 项 like 口语中也可用，但课标常见搭配为 smell of；C、D 不搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹想帮忙，却把盘子摔了。转折用 but。A 项 and；C 项 or；D 项 so。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词/形容词辨析（固定结构）
**【解析】** 本句意为：出错是因为人们足够上心。care enough 足够在意。B 项 too much 后多接名词；C 项 a lot 常作状语但不如 enough 贴合“足够在乎”；D 项 very much 表程度但结构不如 care enough 自然。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：先做汤，凉菜，热菜最后。at last 最后。A 项 at first 起初；C 项 at once 立刻；D 项 at least 至少。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：切好的配料像士兵一样排开。line up 排队；排列。B 项 line out 不常用；C、D 搭配不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：又加了一撮盐。a pinch of salt 固定搭配。A 项 about；C 项 for；D 项 with。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：桌上满是色彩与欢笑。be full of 充满。A 项 with 常作 fill with；C、D 不搭配 full。故选 B。
