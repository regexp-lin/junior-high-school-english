# 中考英语完形填空：看春晚

## 完形填空原文

After our big dinner, we moved the coffee table aside ___1___ make space for fruit and snacks. The TV show Spring Festival Gala started ___2___ eight o'clock, and the living room filled with relatives chatting softly.

My cousin handed me a mandarin ___3___ asked whether I liked the dance program. I nodded, ___4___ my eyes stayed on a funny short sketch. Laughter rose and fell like waves, ___5___ even Grandpa, who seldom stays up late, kept clapping.

During a song, my mother passed hot tea ___6___ everyone. Outside, distant fireworks popped, ___7___ inside we felt safe and warm. When the clock neared midnight, the hosts counted down together. My heart beat ___8___ excitement.

At the final cheer, we hugged each other and said "Happy New Year." That night I learned ___9___ simple screens can connect a family ___10___ shared joy and hope.

## 选项

1. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. in order to &emsp;&emsp; C. such that &emsp;&emsp; D. as to
2. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if
5. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. around &emsp;&emsp; D. among
7. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
8. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. from
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. why
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词/不定式结构辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把茶几挪开以便腾出地方。in order to + 动词原形表目的。A 项 so that 后接从句；C 项 such that；D 项 as to 较少这样用。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：春晚在八点开始。具体时刻用 at。A 项 on 用于某天；B 项 in 用于月/年等；D 项 by 到……为止。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表哥递给我一个橘子并问我……handed 与 asked 并列。A 项 but；B 项 or；D 项 so。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我点头，尽管眼睛一直盯着小品。though 引导让步。A 项 because；C 项 unless；D 项 if。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：笑声此起彼伏，所以连很少熬夜的爷爷也在鼓掌。表结果用 so。A 项 but；B 项 and 并列但逻辑不如因果；D 项 or。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈把热茶递给在座的每个人。pass around 传递、分发给众人。A 项 pass to 递给某人；B 项 for；D 项 among 不搭配 pass。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：外面鞭炮响，而屋里温暖安全。while 表对比。A 项 if；C 项 until；D 项 unless。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我激动得心怦怦跳。with excitement 带着激动。A 项 beat of 不自然；C、D 不搭配。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了简单的屏幕如何把一家人连在一起。learn how... 明白“如何/多么”。A 项 what；C 项 whether；D 项 why。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：通过共同的喜悦与希望。through 表示“通过；凭借”。A 项 in；B 项 by；D 项 beyond。故选 C。
