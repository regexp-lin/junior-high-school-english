# 中考英语完形填空：英语角里敢开口

## 完形填空原文

Every Friday afternoon, our English corner meets ___1___ the reading room. I used to be shy and ___2___ speak English in public. One day, my friend Anna said, “Why ___3___ you join us this week?”

I hesitated, ___4___ I finally agreed. When I arrived, several students were talking ___5___ a film they had watched. The teacher smiled and asked me ___6___ simple question about my hobby.

At first, my face turned red, ___7___ I answered in short sentences. Everyone listened ___8___ and nodded from time to time. Ten minutes later, I felt much ___9___ relaxed than before. Now I know English Corner is ___10___ useful place to practice speaking.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
2. A. shouldn’t &emsp;&emsp; B. couldn’t &emsp;&emsp; C. mustn’t &emsp;&emsp; D. needn’t  
3. A. do &emsp;&emsp; B. did &emsp;&emsp; C. don’t &emsp;&emsp; D. didn’t  
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
5. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
6. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
8. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. more careful &emsp;&emsp; D. more carefully  
9. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. quite  
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 in 表在较大空间内部  
**【解析】** “在阅读室里”用 in the reading room。at 多指具体点；on/to 不符合。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 情态动词表能力（过去）  
**【解析】** used to be shy 暗示过去“不能/不敢在公共场合说英语”，couldn’t 表过去能力或可能性上的“不能”。shouldn’t/mustn’t/needn’t 不表能力。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 提建议句型 Why don’t you...?  
**【解析】** Why don’t you join us? 为固定提建议结构，用一般现在时 don’t。do/did/didn’t 不构成该句型。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “犹豫”与“最终同意”为转折，用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表结果。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 about 表话题  
**【解析】** talk about a film 表示“谈论一部电影”。talk to/with 侧重对象；talk for 不表话题。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词表泛指  
**【解析】** “一个简单问题”，simple 以辅音音素开头，用 a simple question。an 用于元音音素开头；the 表特指此处不强调。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “脸红了”与“仍用短句回答”为转折，用 but。and/so/because 逻辑不符。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** listen 为动词，需用副词 carefully 修饰。careful 为形容词；比较级在此无比较对象提示。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（much 修饰比较级）  
**【解析】** than before 提示比较级；relaxed 为多音节形容词，用 more relaxed；much 修饰比较级表程度。very/too/quite 修饰原级。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词表泛指  
**【解析】** useful 以辅音音素 /j/ 开头，用 a useful place。an 用于元音音素开头；the 表特指此处为泛指。故选 A。
