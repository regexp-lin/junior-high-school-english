# 中考英语完形填空：学做红枣银耳汤

## 完形填空原文

My grandma often cooks healthy soup for our family. Last Sunday, she decided ___1___ me how to make red date and white fungus soup. “This soup is good ___2___ your skin,” she said.

First, we soaked the dried fungus ___3___ cold water for about two hours ___4___ it became soft. Then Grandma told me ___5___ the hard ends carefully. After that, we put the ingredients into ___6___ pot and added enough water.

Grandma let the soup boil, then turned the heat down and asked me ___7___ it slowly with a spoon. “Keep your eyes ___8___ the pot,” she reminded me. About forty minutes later, the kitchen ___9___ sweet. We added some rock sugar and tasted it together. The soup tasted ___10___ better than I expected.

## 选项

1. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. to teach &emsp;&emsp; C. teaching &emsp;&emsp; D. taught  
2. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
5. A. cut &emsp;&emsp; B. cutting &emsp;&emsp; C. to cut &emsp;&emsp; D. cuts  
6. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
7. A. stir &emsp;&emsp; B. stirring &emsp;&emsp; C. to stir &emsp;&emsp; D. stirred  
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
9. A. smell &emsp;&emsp; B. smells &emsp;&emsp; C. smelled &emsp;&emsp; D. is smelling  
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. even &emsp;&emsp; D. quite  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide to teach 为固定搭配。teach/teaching/taught 不能直接跟在 decide 后。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be good for）  
**【解析】** be good for your skin 表示“对皮肤有益”。be good at/to/with 语义不同。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 in 表浸泡在液体中  
**【解析】** soak...in cold water 表示“在冷水中浸泡”。on/at/by 不搭配 soak 表此义。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 连词 until 表“直到……为止”  
**【解析】** “浸泡约两小时直到变软”，until 引导时间状语从句。if/because/although 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell me to cut 为不定式作宾补。cut/cutting/cuts 不符合 tell 的结构。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词表泛指  
**【解析】** “一口锅”为泛指，pot 以辅音音素开头，用 a pot。an/the/零冠词不符合此处泛指可数名词单数。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do）  
**【解析】** ask me to stir 用不定式作宾补。stir/stirring/stirred 不符合 ask 的搭配。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（keep one’s eyes on）  
**【解析】** keep your eyes on the pot 表示“留意锅里的情况”，介词用 on。in/at/for 不构成此固定搭配。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（叙事中过去状态）  
**【解析】** About forty minutes later 提示叙述过去发生的事，厨房“闻起来很香”用 smelled 作系动词过去式。smell 原形与第三人称单数不符；is smelling 表进行时，不符合此处叙述习惯。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 程度副词修饰比较级  
**【解析】** even better 表示“甚至更好/比预想好得多”，even 可修饰比较级。very/more/quite 一般不与比较级 better 这样搭配（more better 为错误形式）。故选 C。
