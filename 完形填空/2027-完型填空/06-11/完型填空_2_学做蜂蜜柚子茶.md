# 中考英语完形填空：学做蜂蜜柚子茶

## 完形填空原文

My mother has been making honey grapefruit tea ___1___ many years. Last Sunday, she finally agreed to teach me the recipe.

We ___2___ a big grapefruit first. Mother peeled off the skin and asked me to cut the white part away ___3___ it tasted bitter. Then she sliced the yellow peel into thin strips.

The peel needed to be soaked in salt water ___4___ about thirty minutes. "This step helps to remove the bitterness," Mother explained ___5___. While waiting, I separated the grapefruit flesh into small pieces.

Next, we put the peel, flesh and some rock sugar into a pot. We cooked them ___6___ low heat, stirring from time ___7___ time. After an hour, the mixture became thick.

When it cooled down, we added three big spoons of honey and ___8___ everything well. I put the tea into a glass jar and stored ___9___ in the fridge.

Two days later, the honey grapefruit tea was ready. Mixed with warm water, it tasted ___10___ than any drink from the shop.

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. since
2. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. buys &emsp;&emsp; C. bought &emsp;&emsp; D. buying
3. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. on
5. A. patient &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. patiently &emsp;&emsp; D. impatient
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
8. A. mix &emsp;&emsp; B. mixes &emsp;&emsp; C. mixed &emsp;&emsp; D. mixing
9. A. them &emsp;&emsp; B. it &emsp;&emsp; C. one &emsp;&emsp; D. this
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（for + 时间段 + 现在完成时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈做蜂蜜柚子茶已经很多年了。for 后接时间段，搭配现在完成时。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们先买了一个大柚子。Last Sunday 提示用过去时 bought。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（because 表原因）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈让我把白色部分切掉因为它尝起来是苦的。because 表原因。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（for + 时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：柚子皮需要在盐水中浸泡大约三十分钟。for 后接时间段。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈耐心地解释道。修饰动词 explained 用副词 patiently。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（on low heat 小火）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们用小火煮它们。"on low heat"为固定搭配。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（from time to time 不时地）
**【解析】** 本句意为：不时地搅拌。"from time to time"表示"不时、偶尔"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时（and 连接并列谓语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们加了三大勺蜂蜜并把所有东西搅拌均匀。and 连接 added 和 mixed，时态一致用过去时。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析（it 指代不可数名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把茶放入玻璃罐并存放在冰箱里。it 指代 the tea（不可数名词）。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than 提示）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它比店里买的任何饮品都好喝。than 提示用比较级 better。故选 C。
