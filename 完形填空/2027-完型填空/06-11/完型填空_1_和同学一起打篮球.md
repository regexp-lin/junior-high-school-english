# 中考英语完形填空：和同学一起打篮球

## 完形填空原文

After school yesterday, my classmates and I went to the playground ___1___ basketball. The weather was warm and perfect for outdoor sports.

We divided ___2___ into two teams of five. I was the captain of one team. Before the game started, I told my teammates to pass the ball ___3___ and work as a team.

The game was very ___4___. The other team scored first, but we didn't give up. I passed the ball to Wang Lei, ___5___ was the tallest in our team. He jumped high and scored a beautiful goal.

In the last minute, the score was 18 ___6___ 18. I got the ball and ran ___7___ I could toward the basket. I threw the ball and it went in! Everyone on my team ___8___ with joy.

After the game, we shook ___9___ with the other team. Although they lost, they played really well. Playing basketball ___10___ only keeps us healthy but also teaches us teamwork.

## 选项

1. A. play &emsp;&emsp; B. played &emsp;&emsp; C. playing &emsp;&emsp; D. to play
2. A. us &emsp;&emsp; B. we &emsp;&emsp; C. ourselves &emsp;&emsp; D. our
3. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. quicker &emsp;&emsp; D. quickest
4. A. excite &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. exciting &emsp;&emsp; D. excitement
5. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what
6. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. as fast as &emsp;&emsp; B. so fast as &emsp;&emsp; C. faster as &emsp;&emsp; D. fastest as
8. A. cheer &emsp;&emsp; B. cheers &emsp;&emsp; C. cheered &emsp;&emsp; D. cheering
9. A. hand &emsp;&emsp; B. hands &emsp;&emsp; C. arms &emsp;&emsp; D. head
10. A. either &emsp;&emsp; B. neither &emsp;&emsp; C. both &emsp;&emsp; D. not

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和同学去操场打篮球。to play 表示目的"去做某事"。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 反身代词（divide ourselves 自己分组）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把自己分成两队，每队五人。"divide ourselves"表示"把我们自己分成"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我告诉队友们快速传球并作为团队合作。修饰动词 pass 用副词 quickly。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词（-ing 形容词修饰事物）
**【解析】** 本句意为：比赛非常激动人心。exciting 修饰事物（game）。excited 修饰人。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句（who 指代人）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把球传给了王磊，他是我们队最高的。先行词 Wang Lei 指人，在从句中作主语，用 who。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（比分用 to）
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后一分钟，比分是 18 比 18。体育比分中用"to"连接双方得分。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定结构（as + adv.原级 + as one can）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我拿到球尽可能快地向篮筐跑去。"as fast as I could"表示"尽可能快地"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们队所有人都欢呼起来。上下文为过去时态，用 cheered。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（shake hands 握手）
**【解析】** 本句意为：赛后我们和对方握手。"shake hands"为固定搭配，hands 用复数。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（not only...but also...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：打篮球不仅让我们保持健康还教会我们团队合作。"not only...but also..."表示"不仅……而且……"。故选 D。
