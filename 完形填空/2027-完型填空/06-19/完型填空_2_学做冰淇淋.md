# 中考英语完形填空：学做冰淇淋

## 完形填空原文

Have you ever tried ___1___ ice cream at home? Last Sunday, I found a simple recipe that only needed three ingredients: heavy cream, condensed milk and vanilla.

First, I poured the heavy cream into a big bowl. Using an electric mixer, I beat ___2___ until it became thick and fluffy. This took about five ___3___, and my arm got quite tired.

Then I added the condensed milk and a few drops of vanilla. I mixed them together ___4___. My mother suggested adding some crushed cookies ___5___ extra flavor.

I poured the mixture into a container and covered it ___6___ plastic wrap. It needed to stay in the freezer for at ___7___ six hours. The hardest part was waiting — I kept opening the freezer to check.

The next morning, the ice cream was ___8___ set. I scooped some into a bowl and took a bite. It was even ___9___ than I expected — creamy, smooth and sweet.

My father asked me ___10___ I could make chocolate flavor next time. I said yes with a big smile.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made
2. A. them &emsp;&emsp; B. it &emsp;&emsp; C. they &emsp;&emsp; D. its
3. A. seconds &emsp;&emsp; B. minutes &emsp;&emsp; C. hours &emsp;&emsp; D. days
4. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gently &emsp;&emsp; C. more gentle &emsp;&emsp; D. most gentle
5. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. to
6. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
7. A. last &emsp;&emsp; B. least &emsp;&emsp; C. less &emsp;&emsp; D. most
8. A. final &emsp;&emsp; B. finally &emsp;&emsp; C. near &emsp;&emsp; D. nearly
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
10. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. which

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（try doing sth. 尝试做某事）
**【解析】** 本句意为：你试过在家做冰淇淋吗？"try doing sth."表示"尝试做某事"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词（it 指代不可数名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用电动搅拌器把它打到浓稠蓬松。it 指代前文的 heavy cream（不可数名词）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（常识判断）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这花了大约五分钟。用搅拌器打奶油约需五分钟，five minutes 最合理。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我轻柔地把它们混合在一起。修饰动词 mixed 用副词 gently。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（for 表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈建议加一些碎饼干来增加风味。"for extra flavor"表示"为了额外的风味"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（cover...with... 用……覆盖）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把混合物倒入容器并用保鲜膜盖好。"cover...with..."表示"用……覆盖"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（at least 至少）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它需要在冰箱里待至少六个小时。"at least"表示"至少"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（finally 终于）
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二天早上，冰淇淋终于凝固了。finally 为副词表示"终于"。final 为形容词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（even + 比较级 + than）
**【解析】** 本句意为：比我预想的还要好。even 修饰比较级 better，than 提示比较关系。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句（if 是否）
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸问我下次能不能做巧克力味的。if 引导宾语从句表示"是否"。故选 B。
