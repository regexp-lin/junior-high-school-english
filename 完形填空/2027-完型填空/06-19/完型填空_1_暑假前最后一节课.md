# 中考英语完形填空：暑假前最后一节课

## 完形填空原文

Today was the last day of school before summer vacation. The atmosphere in the classroom was ___1___ from usual — everyone was both happy and a little sad.

Our head teacher, Mr. Zhang, didn't teach any new lessons. ___2___, he talked about our growth over the past year. He showed us some photos ___3___ at the school sports day and science fair.

"I'm proud of each one of you," he said. "You have all become ___4___ and more responsible." Some girls started crying ___5___ they heard these words.

Then Mr. Zhang gave us some advice for the summer. He told us ___6___ waste too much time on phones. He hoped we would read at least three books and do some exercise ___7___ day.

At the end of the class, he wrote a sentence on the blackboard: "The ___8___ is yours." We all stood up and bowed to him deeply. He had been ___9___ than just a teacher — he was like a friend.

As I walked out of the school gate, I looked back ___10___ the building. I knew I would miss this place during the long summer break.

## 选项

1. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. similar &emsp;&emsp; C. different &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
2. A. Instead &emsp;&emsp; B. However &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Besides
3. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. took &emsp;&emsp; C. taken &emsp;&emsp; D. taking
4. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. braver &emsp;&emsp; C. bravest &emsp;&emsp; D. bravely
5. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
6. A. don't &emsp;&emsp; B. not &emsp;&emsp; C. not to &emsp;&emsp; D. to not
7. A. every &emsp;&emsp; B. each &emsp;&emsp; C. all &emsp;&emsp; D. both
8. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. present &emsp;&emsp; C. future &emsp;&emsp; D. history
9. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. for

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be different from 与……不同）
**【解析】** 本句意为：教室里的气氛和平时不一样。"be different from"表示"与……不同"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（Instead 相反/取而代之）
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任没有教新课，取而代之的是他谈了我们过去一年的成长。Instead 表示"取而代之"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 过去分词作后置定语
**【解析】** 本句意为：他给我们看了一些运动会和科学展上拍的照片。taken 为过去分词作后置定语修饰 photos，表被动。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（比较级 + and + 比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们都变得更勇敢、更有责任感了。"braver and more responsible"为并列比较级结构。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（when 当……时候）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些女生听到这些话就开始哭了。when 表示"当……时候"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. not to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他告诉我们不要在手机上浪费太多时间。"tell sb. not to do sth."为否定不定式结构。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 不定代词（every day 每天）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他希望我们每天做一些运动。"every day"表示"每天"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（future 未来）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他在黑板上写了一句话："未来属于你们。"根据语境，future 最合适。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（more than 不仅仅）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他不仅仅是一位老师——他更像一个朋友。"more than"表示"不仅仅是"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语（look back at 回望）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我回头看了看那栋建筑。"look back at"表示"回头看"。故选 B。
