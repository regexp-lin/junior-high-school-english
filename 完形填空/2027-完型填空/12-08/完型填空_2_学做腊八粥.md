# 中考英语完形填空：学做腊八粥

## 完形填空原文

The Laba Festival is coming, so Grandma decided ___1___ teach me to cook Laba porridge. Early in the morning, we soaked rice, beans, and nuts ___2___ warm water for two hours. Grandma explained ___3___ each ingredient had a special meaning in Chinese culture. When everything became soft, we put them ___4___ a large pot and added enough water. “Stir it slowly, ___5___ it may stick to the bottom,” she reminded me. The kitchen ___6___ filled with a sweet smell while the porridge was boiling. I tasted a spoonful and found it ___7___ sweeter than the porridge at school. Grandma laughed and said love was the secret ingredient. I promised ___8___ I would cook it for my parents next year. Cooking together made me feel ___9___ to my family roots. I also learned ___10___ patience is as important as good recipes.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
4. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
6. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be  
7. A. little &emsp;&emsp; B. a little &emsp;&emsp; C. few &emsp;&emsp; D. a few  
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
9. A. close &emsp;&emsp; B. closer &emsp;&emsp; C. closest &emsp;&emsp; D. closely  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. /  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** decide to do sth.  
**【解析】** decide 后接动词不定式作宾语，即 decided to teach。for/of/with 不构成 decide 的常见宾语结构。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 in warm water  
**【解析】** “在温水里浸泡”用 in warm water。on/at/by 与 soak 搭配不自然。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 that（陈述说明）  
**【解析】** explain 后为宾语从句，从句结构完整，陈述事实，用 that 引导（口语中可省略，但试题常考查 that）。what 需在从句中充当成分；whether 表是否；how 表方式，此处是解释“每种食材有文化含义”这一事实。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** put...into... 固定搭配  
**【解析】** 表示“放进大锅里”用 put them into a large pot。put on 穿上/上演；put at 不常用；put to 表“使遭受”等，不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 or 表“否则”  
**【解析】** 祈使句 + or + 陈述句 表“否则就会……”，即“慢慢搅拌，否则会粘锅底”。and 顺承；but 转折；so 结果，均不如 or 符合提醒语气。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时被动语态  
**【解析】** 叙述过去早晨的经历，厨房“充满了香味”，用一般过去时 was filled with。is 现在时；has been 现在完成；will be 将来，均与叙事时态不一致。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** a little 修饰比较级 sweeter  
**【解析】** sweeter 为比较级，可用 a little 修饰程度，表示“稍微更甜一点”。little 表否定“几乎不”；few/a few 修饰可数名词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** promise 后接 that 引导的宾语从句  
**【解析】** promise 后接宾语从句陈述承诺内容，用 that 引导。what 缺成分；if 表是否，承诺句通常不用 if；how 表方式。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** feel closer to（比较级）  
**【解析】** feel close to 表示“与……更亲近”，此处暗含与以前相比，用比较级 closer。close 原级可通，但语境强调“更贴近家族根脉”，比较级更佳；closest 缺范围；closely 多表“紧密地/仔细地”，与 to my family roots 搭配不如 closer 自然。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** learn 后接 that 引导宾语从句  
**【解析】** learned 后为完整陈述句作宾语从句，用 that 引导。what/which 需在从句中充当成分；零冠词不能连接从句。故选 C。
