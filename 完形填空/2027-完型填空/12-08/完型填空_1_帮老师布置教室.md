# 中考英语完形填空：帮老师布置教室

## 完形填空原文

Our class was chosen ___1___ host a grade-wide reading corner next Monday. On Friday afternoon, Ms. Lin ___2___ several volunteers to stay after school. I raised my hand immediately ___3___ I love decorating spaces. We moved the desks aside ___4___ make room for bookshelves. Tom climbed ___5___ ladder to hang a string of paper cranes, while I arranged posters on the wall. ___6___ we worked for two hours, nobody complained about being tired. Ms. Lin brought hot tea and said our teamwork was ___7___ she had hoped for. Looking at the tidy corner, I understood ___8___ preparation could turn a common classroom ___9___ a warm reading home. I told myself I ___10___ keep volunteering whenever help was needed.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. asks &emsp;&emsp; B. asked &emsp;&emsp; C. is asking &emsp;&emsp; D. will ask  
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
4. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; D. even though  
5. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
6. A. Before &emsp;&emsp; B. Until &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Because  
7. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. who  
8. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. careless &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
10. A. should &emsp;&emsp; B. must &emsp;&emsp; C. would &emsp;&emsp; D. might  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be chosen to do sth.  
**【解析】** “被选去做某事”常用 be chosen to host...。for/of/with 不与 be chosen 构成此固定结构。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 叙述上周五下午发生的事，谓语用一般过去时 asked。asks 现在时；is asking 进行时；will ask 将来时，均与时间背景不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 从属连词 because 表原因  
**【解析】** “我立刻举手”是因为“我喜欢布置空间”，表原因用 because。if 条件；unless 除非；although 让步，均不符合。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** in order to 表目的  
**【解析】** “把桌子挪开”的目的是“给书架腾地方”，用 in order to make room。so that 后接从句；as soon as 时间；even though 让步。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 a 与 ladder  
**【解析】** ladder 以辅音音素开头，泛指“一把梯子”用 a ladder。an 用于元音音素开头；the 特指不足；零冠词不合适。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** although 引导让步状语从句  
**【解析】** “尽管我们忙了两小时，却没人抱怨累”，表让步用 Although。Before 在……前；Until 直到；Because 原因，与后句逻辑不符。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 表语从句连接代词 what（= the thing that）  
**【解析】** was 后为表语从句，从句中 hoped for 缺宾语，表示“她所希望的那种样子”，用 what。that 在从句不充当成分但此处缺宾语；which 需有选择范围；who 指人。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词作定语修饰名词 preparation  
**【解析】** 空后为名词 preparation，需用形容词 careful 表示“充分的准备”。carefully/carelessly 为副词，不能作定语修饰名词；careless 表示“粗心的”，与语境“把普通教室变成温暖阅读角”的积极含义相反。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 turn...into...  
**【解析】** turn a common classroom into a warm reading home 表示“把普通教室变成温暖的阅读之家”。turn...in/on/at 不构成此义。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 情态动词 would 表意愿与决心（叙述中的自我承诺）  
**【解析】** I told myself I would keep volunteering 表示“我告诉自己我会继续志愿帮忙”，would 表从过去视角看将来的意愿或决心。should 偏义务；must 过强；might 语气过弱。故选 C。
