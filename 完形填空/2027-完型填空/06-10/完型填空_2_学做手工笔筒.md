# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工笔筒

## 完形填空原文

In today's art class, we learned to make a pen holder ___1___ an empty can and some colorful paper. The teacher said recycling old things was a great way to protect ___2___ environment.

First, I washed the can and made sure there were no sharp ___3___ on it. Then I measured a piece of paper that was big enough ___4___ wrap around the can.

I spread glue ___5___ on the back of the paper and carefully stuck it onto the can. This was the ___6___ difficult part because the paper kept sliding down.

After the glue dried, I ___7___ some star stickers on the outside. My pen holder looked much ___8___ than before. I also tied a ribbon around it to make it ___9___ special.

The teacher asked us to bring our works home and use ___10___. I put it on my desk right away. Now all my pens and pencils have a beautiful new home.

## 选项

1. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. by
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
3. A. edge &emsp;&emsp; B. edges &emsp;&emsp; C. side &emsp;&emsp; D. sides
4. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. even &emsp;&emsp; B. evenly &emsp;&emsp; C. ever &emsp;&emsp; D. every
6. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
7. A. add &emsp;&emsp; B. adds &emsp;&emsp; C. added &emsp;&emsp; D. adding
8. A. nice &emsp;&emsp; B. nicer &emsp;&emsp; C. nicest &emsp;&emsp; D. the nicest
9. A. look &emsp;&emsp; B. to look &emsp;&emsp; C. looking &emsp;&emsp; D. looked
10. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. they &emsp;&emsp; D. its

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（from 表来源/材料）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学习用一个空罐子和彩纸做笔筒。"make...from..."表示"用……制成（看不出原材料）"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定冠词（the environment 特指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：回收旧物是保护环境的好方法。the environment 为特指"环境"，前需加 the。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词复数
**【解析】** 本句意为：确保罐子上没有锋利的边缘。"no + 名词复数"表示"没有……"，edges 为复数。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构（adj. + enough + to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我量了一张足够大的纸来包裹罐子。"big enough to"为"adj. + enough + to do"结构。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把胶水均匀地涂在纸的背面。修饰动词 spread 用副词 evenly。even 为形容词/副词"甚至"；ever 为"曾经"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（the most + 多音节形容词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是最难的部分。"the most difficult"为多音节形容词最高级。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：胶水干了之后，我在外面贴了一些星星贴纸。上下文为过去时态，用 added。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（much + 比较级 + than）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的笔筒比之前好看多了。than 提示用比较级，much 修饰 nicer。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 使役动词（make + 宾语 + 动词原形）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还在上面绑了一条丝带让它看起来更特别。"make + 宾语 + 动词原形"结构。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词（them 指代复数名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们把作品带回家并使用它们。them 指代前文的 works（复数）。故选 B。
