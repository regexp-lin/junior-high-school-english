# 中考英语完形填空：期末考试复习周

## 完形填空原文

The final exams are coming next week. Our teacher gave us ___1___ review plan to help us prepare. She said reviewing was just as ___2___ as learning new things.

Every evening after dinner, I spend at least two hours ___3___ my notes. I usually start with the subjects that I am ___4___ good at. My weakest subject is math, so I practice extra problems every night.

My friend Li Ming and I sometimes study ___5___. He is better at science ___6___ me, so he explains the difficult parts. In return, I help him ___7___ English grammar, which is my strong point.

Last night, I was so tired that I ___8___ asleep while reading. My mother woke me up and made me a glass of warm milk. She told me not ___9___ too hard and to get enough sleep.

I hope all the effort will ___10___ off. I want to get good grades and make my parents proud.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. important &emsp;&emsp; B. more important &emsp;&emsp; C. most important &emsp;&emsp; D. importantly
3. A. review &emsp;&emsp; B. to review &emsp;&emsp; C. reviewing &emsp;&emsp; D. reviewed
4. A. least &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. little &emsp;&emsp; D. few
5. A. alone &emsp;&emsp; B. lonely &emsp;&emsp; C. together &emsp;&emsp; D. apart
6. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. from
7. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
8. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. felt &emsp;&emsp; C. fall &emsp;&emsp; D. fell
9. A. study &emsp;&emsp; B. studying &emsp;&emsp; C. to study &emsp;&emsp; D. studied
10. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. put &emsp;&emsp; C. pay &emsp;&emsp; D. turn

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师给我们一份复习计划来帮我们备考。review 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定结构（as + adj.原级 + as）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说复习和学新知识一样重要。"as + adj.原级 + as"表示"和……一样……"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（spend time doing sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天晚饭后我花至少两小时复习笔记。"spend time doing sth."为固定结构。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词（least 最不）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我通常先从我最不擅长的科目开始。"least"表示"最少/最不"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（together 一起）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和朋友李明有时一起学习。together 表示"一起"。alone 独自；lonely 孤独的。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（better...than）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他科学比我好。better 后接 than 表示比较。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（help sb. with sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：作为回报，我帮他学英语语法。"help sb. with sth."表示"帮某人做某事"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析/过去时（fell asleep 入睡）
**【解析】** 本句意为：昨晚我太累了以至于看书时睡着了。"fall asleep"表示"入睡"，过去式为 fell。felt 是 feel 的过去式。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. not to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她让我不要太用功并要保证充足睡眠。"tell sb. not to do sth."为固定结构。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语（pay off 取得成效）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望所有的努力会有回报。"pay off"表示"取得成效、有回报"。故选 C。
