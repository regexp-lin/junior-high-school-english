# 中考英语完形填空：学做蛋炒饭

## 完形填空原文

On Sunday evening, Dad stood ___1___ me in the kitchen and taught me how to cook egg fried rice. He said simple food could still taste great ___2___ we paid attention to small details.

First, we cut green onions ___3___ thin pieces and beat two eggs in a bowl. Dad told me ___4___ stir the eggs gently until they looked smooth.

Then we heated the pan and poured in a little oil. When the oil was hot, I poured the eggs ___5___ and watched them turn golden. My heart beat fast ___6___ I was afraid of hot oil splashes.

After that, we added cold rice and kept turning it ___7___ a spatula. Dad reminded me ___8___ press the rice lightly so it wouldn't stick together in big lumps.

Finally, we mixed in the green onions and a pinch of salt. The kitchen smelled warm and familiar. I tasted a spoonful ___9___ smiled because it was better than I had imagined.

Dad patted my shoulder and said, "Cooking is practice." I promised ___10___ try again next weekend.

## 选项

1. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. below &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. between
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. slow &emsp;&emsp; C. slower &emsp;&emsp; D. slowest
6. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. or
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
8. A. not &emsp;&emsp; B. don't &emsp;&emsp; C. not to &emsp;&emsp; D. to not
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. I will &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：周日晚上，爸爸站在我旁边教我炒饭。beside 在……旁边。behind 在……后面；below 在……下方；between 在两者之间。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说只要我们留意小细节，简单的食物也会很好吃。if 如果，引导条件状语从句。unless 除非；although 尽管；until 直到。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们先把葱切成细丝。cut...into thin pieces 把……切成……。in 在……里；on 在……上；at 在。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸让我轻轻搅拌鸡蛋直到看起来顺滑。tell sb. to do sth. 告诉某人做某事，用不定式 to stir。for 为了；of 的；with 和。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：油热后，我慢慢倒入鸡蛋。修饰动词 poured 用副词 slowly。slow 为形容词；slower 比较级；slowest 最高级，此处无比较。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我心跳很快，因为我害怕热油溅出来。because 因为，引导原因状语从句。so 所以；but 但是；or 或者。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们加入冷饭，用铲子不断翻炒。with a spatula 用铲子。in 在……里；by 通过；on 在……上。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸提醒我不要用力按压米饭。remind sb. not to do sth. 提醒某人不要做某事。not 单独不能接动词原形；don't 不能用于 remind 后这种结构；to not 语序不符合英语习惯。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尝了一勺，然后笑了。tasted 与 smiled 为顺承并列，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我答应下周末再试一次。promise to do sth. 答应做某事，用不定式 to try。I will 不能直接接在 promise 后作宾语；that 引导从句需完整从句。故选 B。
