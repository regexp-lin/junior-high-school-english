# 中考英语完形填空：学做糖葫芦

## 完形填空原文

Candied haws, also known as tanghulu, is a ___1___ Chinese snack. During the autumn festival, my uncle decided to teach me how to make them at home.

First, we went to the market and bought fresh hawthorn berries. My uncle picked ___2___ carefully, choosing only the biggest and reddest ones. He said good ingredients are the key ___3___ making delicious food.

Back home, we washed the berries and removed the seeds. Then my uncle heated sugar and water in a pot ___4___ the mixture became thick and golden. "This is the trickiest part," he said. "If the sugar is ___5___ hot, it will turn brown and taste bitter."

I watched him dip each berry into the sugar syrup quickly and place ___6___ on a greased plate. The sugar hardened almost immediately. It looked much ___7___ than I had expected.

When it was my turn to try, my hands were a bit shaky. The first stick wasn't very ___8___ because the coating was uneven. But my uncle told me not ___9___ disappointed. "You already did better than I did on my first try," he said with a warm smile.

By the end of the afternoon, we had made two dozen sticks of tanghulu. I shared ___10___ with my neighbors, and they all said the tanghulu was as tasty as the ones sold on the street.

## 选项

1. A. tradition &emsp;&emsp; B. traditional &emsp;&emsp; C. traditionally &emsp;&emsp; D. traditions  
2. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. they &emsp;&emsp; C. them &emsp;&emsp; D. their  
3. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
4. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. enough &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. too &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
6. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. they  
7. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. easily &emsp;&emsp; D. easiest  
8. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. succeed &emsp;&emsp; C. successful &emsp;&emsp; D. successfully  
9. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. to be &emsp;&emsp; C. being &emsp;&emsp; D. been  
10. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. they &emsp;&emsp; C. them &emsp;&emsp; D. their  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词  
**【解析】** 修饰名词 snack 需用形容词 traditional（传统的）。tradition 为名词；traditionally 为副词；traditions 为名词复数，均不能修饰名词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** picked 为及物动词，后接宾语。代指前文 hawthorn berries（复数），用宾格 them。it 为单数；they 为主格；their 为形容词性物主代词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 the key to  
**【解析】** the key to (doing) sth. 意为"做某事的关键"，to 为介词。of 表所属；for 表目的；at 不构成此搭配。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词 until  
**【解析】** "加热糖和水直到混合物变得浓稠金黄"，until 意为"直到……为止"。after 表"在……之后"；before 表"在……之前"；unless 表"除非"，均不符合。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** too...to... 结构  
**【解析】** 语境为"如果糖太热，就会变棕色变苦"，too hot 意为"太热了"，含消极含义。enough 放形容词后；very 表程度但不含"过度"之义；so 常与 that 搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（复数）  
**【解析】** place 为及物动词，宾语指代前文的 each berry，但联系上下文 dip each berry 为逐个蘸糖浆后放到盘子上的动作，此处 them 指代多个裹好糖浆的山楂。them 为复数宾格。it 为单数；its 为物主代词；they 为主格。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 句中有 than，需用比较级。easy 的比较级为 easier。easy 为原级；easily 为副词；easiest 为最高级，均不与 than 搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** wasn't very 后接形容词作表语。successful（成功的）为形容词。success 为名词；succeed 为动词；successfully 为副词，均不能作系动词 be 的表语。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. not to do）  
**【解析】** tell sb. not to do sth. 意为"告诉某人不要做某事"，否定不定式 not to be。be 缺 to；being 为动名词；been 为过去分词，均不用于 tell sb. not 后。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** shared 为及物动词，后接宾语指代前文的 tanghulu（sticks），用复数宾格 them。it 为单数；they 为主格；their 为形容词性物主代词。故选 C。
