# 中考英语完形填空：参观科技馆

## 完形填空原文

Our school organized a trip to the Science and Technology Museum last Friday. All the students in Grade Nine were excited ___1___ this news.

When we arrived, a guide was already waiting for us at the entrance. She led us ___2___ the first exhibition hall, which was about space exploration. I saw models of rockets and satellites. A short film showed how astronauts live and work ___3___ space. It was really amazing.

In the second hall, there were many interactive machines. We could press buttons and watch ___4___ happen on the screens. My friend Li Hua tried a virtual reality headset and pretended ___5___ walking on the moon. He looked so funny that all of us burst into ___6___.

The most popular area was the robot show. A small robot could dance, answer questions, and even play chess! When I asked ___7___ it could speak English, it replied, "Of course! Nice to meet you!" The whole crowd ___8___ loudly.

Before we left, the guide reminded us that science and technology have changed our daily life ___9___. She hoped that some of us ___10___ become scientists in the future. Her words planted a seed of dream in my heart.

## 选项

1. A. hear &emsp;&emsp; B. heard &emsp;&emsp; C. to hear &emsp;&emsp; D. hearing  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. out &emsp;&emsp; D. from  
3. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. who  
5. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. been &emsp;&emsp; C. being &emsp;&emsp; D. to be  
6. A. laugh &emsp;&emsp; B. laughs &emsp;&emsp; C. laughter &emsp;&emsp; D. laughing  
7. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
8. A. cheer &emsp;&emsp; B. cheered &emsp;&emsp; C. cheering &emsp;&emsp; D. cheers  
9. A. great &emsp;&emsp; B. greatly &emsp;&emsp; C. greater &emsp;&emsp; D. greatest  
10. A. will &emsp;&emsp; B. would &emsp;&emsp; C. can &emsp;&emsp; D. must  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（be excited to do）  
**【解析】** be excited to do sth. 意为"因做某事而兴奋"，不定式表原因。hear 为原形；heard 为过去式；hearing 为现在分词，均不能直接跟在 excited 后表原因。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 into 表进入  
**【解析】** led us into 意为"带领我们进入"，into 表示从外到内的动态方向。in 表示静态位置；out 表出去；from 表来源，均不符合"带领进入展厅"的语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词搭配 in space  
**【解析】** in space 为固定搭配，意为"在太空中"。on/at/by 均不与 space 构成此搭配。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词  
**【解析】** watch what happen 意为"看什么事情发生"，what 引导宾语从句并在从句中作主语。which 需有选择范围；that 在宾语从句中不作成分但此处从句缺主语；who 指人。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（pretend to do）  
**【解析】** pretend to do sth. 意为"假装做某事"，pretend to be walking 表"假装在月球上行走"。be/been/being 均不能直接跟在 pretend 后。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 burst into laughter  
**【解析】** burst into laughter 意为"突然大笑"，laughter 为不可数名词。burst into + 名词为固定结构。laugh 为动词（burst out laughing 也可但需 out + doing）；laughs/laughing 不与 burst into 搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连词（是否）  
**【解析】** "我问它是否能说英语"，表示"是否"用 if 或 whether 引导宾语从句。that 引导陈述句；what/how 为疑问词，不表"是否"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，谓语动词应用过去式 cheered。cheer 为原形；cheering 为现在分词；cheers 为三单现在时，均不匹配时态。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 changed 需用副词 greatly（极大地）。great 为形容词；greater 为比较级形容词；greatest 为最高级形容词，均不能修饰动词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句时态（过去将来时）  
**【解析】** 主句 hoped 为过去时，宾语从句中表将来应用过去将来时 would。will 用于一般将来时；can 表能力；must 表必须，均不符合主句过去时的时态呼应。故选 B。
