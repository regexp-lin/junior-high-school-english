# 中考英语完形填空：参加校运动会跳远

## 完形填空原文

Our school sports meeting was held last week. I signed up for the long jump because I used to be good at ___1___ in PE class.

However, training was harder than I expected. My coach told me to work on my run-up speed and ___2___ at the take-off board. I often stepped on the line, ___3___ my jumps didn't count.

One afternoon, I felt pain in my ankle. The school nurse checked it and asked me ___4___ rest for two days. I worried that I would fall ___5___.

During those two days, I watched videos of athletes and wrote down tips. I also did light exercises ___6___ my legs stayed strong. My teammates sent me messages to cheer me ___7___.

On the competition day, the sky was clear. When my name was called, I took a deep breath, ran fast, and jumped. The sand flew up, and the judge raised a white flag. My result was not the best in the grade, ___8___ it was a new personal record.

Standing on the field, I understood that sports are not only about medals ___9___ about improving yourself. Next year, I will train ___10___ and try again.

## 选项

1. A. jumping &emsp;&emsp; B. swimming &emsp;&emsp; C. singing &emsp;&emsp; D. drawing
2. A. accuracy &emsp;&emsp; B. silence &emsp;&emsp; C. kindness &emsp;&emsp; D. humor
3. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. although
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. down &emsp;&emsp; B. behind &emsp;&emsp; C. out &emsp;&emsp; D. up
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. although
7. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. up &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. out
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. harder &emsp;&emsp; B. slower &emsp;&emsp; C. later &emsp;&emsp; D. less

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词-ing 辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我报名跳远，因为我以前在体育课上擅长跳跃。jumping 跳跃。swimming 游泳；singing 唱歌；drawing 画画。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：教练让我练习助跑速度以及在起跳板上的准确性。accuracy 准确性。silence 安静；kindness 善良；humor 幽默。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我经常踩线，所以成绩不算。因果关系，用 so。because 因为（表原因放前句）；if 如果；although 尽管。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：校医检查后要休息两天。ask sb. to do sth. 要求/让某人做某事。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词短语（fall behind）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我担心自己会落后。fall behind 落后。fall down 摔倒；fall out 脱落/争吵；fall up 非标准搭配。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也做轻度练习，以便腿部保持有力。so 以便、所以，表结果/目的。if 如果；unless 除非；although 尽管。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语（cheer up）
**【解析】** 本句意为：队友发信息让我振作。cheer sb. up 使某人振作。cheer on 为……加油（也可，但 cheer up 更贴合情绪低落）。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：成绩不是年级最好，但刷新了个人纪录。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列结构（not only... but...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：运动不只是奖牌，更是自我提升。not only... but (also)... 不仅……而且……。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：明年我会更刻苦训练再试一次。train harder 更努力训练。slower 更慢；later 更晚；less 更少。故选 A。
