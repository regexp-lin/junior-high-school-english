# 中考英语完形填空：在阳台种绿萝

## 完形填空原文

My mother loves green plants, but our apartment is small. ___1___ spring came, we decided to grow pothos on the balcony.

We bought two pots, soil, and young plants from a flower market. Mom taught me ___2___ holes at the bottom for water to flow out. Then we placed the pots near the railing ___3___ the plants could get enough light.

At first, I watered them every day. A week later, the leaves turned yellow. Mom said I had watered ___4___ much. Plants also need air around their roots.

I changed my habit and only watered the plants ___5___ the soil felt dry. Slowly, new leaves appeared. I used a soft cloth ___6___ dust off the leaves every weekend.

Watching the vines grow longer felt like receiving small gifts ___7___ nature. When I did homework, I liked glancing at the green corner. It made me ___8___ relaxed.

Now I know that caring for plants is like caring for friendship—you can't rush it. You need ___9___ and attention. This summer, I plan to ___10___ a cutting to my best friend so she can start her own green corner.

## 选项

1. A. If &emsp;&emsp; B. When &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. Although
2. A. dig &emsp;&emsp; B. to dig &emsp;&emsp; C. digging &emsp;&emsp; D. dug
3. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. although
4. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. quite &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although
6. A. wipe &emsp;&emsp; B. to wipe &emsp;&emsp; C. wiping &emsp;&emsp; D. wiped
7. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. feeling &emsp;&emsp; C. felt &emsp;&emsp; D. to feel
9. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. times &emsp;&emsp; C. timing &emsp;&emsp; D. timely
10. A. lend &emsp;&emsp; B. send &emsp;&emsp; C. throw &emsp;&emsp; D. hide

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：春天来临时，我们决定在阳台种绿萝。When 当……时。If 如果；Unless 除非；Although 尽管。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（teach sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈教我在盆底挖洞让水流出去。teach sb. to do sth. 教某人做某事。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把花盆放在栏杆附近，以便植物获得充足光照。so 以便、所以。because 因为；if 如果；although 尽管。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词搭配（too much）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说我浇水太多。too much 太多。very 很；quite 相当；so 如此（需搭配 that）。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我改变习惯，只在土壤摸起来干的时候才浇水。if 如果、当……时（此处表条件）。until 直到；unless 除非；although 尽管。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（use sth. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每周末我用软布擦去叶子上的灰尘。use sth. to do sth. 用某物做某事。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看着藤蔓变长就像收到来自大自然的小礼物。from 来自。by 通过；for 为了；with 和。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 使役结构（make sb. do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它让我感到放松。make sb. do sth. 使某人做某事，用动词原形 feel。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你需要时间与关注。time（不可数）时间。times 次数；timing 时机；timely 为形容词。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：今年夏天我打算剪一段枝条送给最好的朋友。send 送、寄。lend 借出；throw 扔；hide 藏。故选 B。
