# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工钥匙扣

## 完形填空原文

Our craft teacher brought some beads, strings and small metal rings to class. She was going to teach us ___1___ make key chains as Father's Day gifts.

I chose blue and white beads ___2___ those are my father's favorite colors. The teacher showed us the steps one ___3___ one. First, we threaded the beads onto a thin string.

It was harder ___4___ I thought. The beads kept falling off because the holes were very ___5___. I had to try several times before I succeeded.

Next, we tied a knot at each end ___6___ the string and attached it to a metal ring. Some students added small letters to ___7___ key chains. I put the letter "D" on mine, which stood ___8___ "Dad."

When I ___9___ the key chain to my father that evening, he was deeply moved. He said it was the ___10___ gift he had ever received. That made me really happy.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. where
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on
4. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. from
5. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. small &emsp;&emsp; C. deep &emsp;&emsp; D. wide
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. on
7. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. gives &emsp;&emsp; C. gave &emsp;&emsp; D. giving
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式（how to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她要教我们如何做钥匙扣。"teach sb. how to do sth."为固定结构。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（because 表原因）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了蓝色和白色的珠子因为那是爸爸最喜欢的颜色。because 表原因。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（one by one 一个接一个）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师一步一步地给我们演示。"one by one"表示"逐个地"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than 提示）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这比我想的要难。harder 为比较级，后接 than。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（语境判断）
**【解析】** 本句意为：珠子一直掉落因为孔很小。孔小导致穿线困难、珠子容易滑落，small 符合语境。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（of 表所属）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在绳子两端各打一个结。"each end of the string"表示"绳子的每一端"。of 表所属。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些同学在他们的钥匙扣上加了小字母。修饰名词 key chains 用形容词性物主代词 their。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（stand for 代表）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我放了一个字母"D"，代表"爸爸"。"stand for"表示"代表"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天晚上我把钥匙扣给爸爸时。"that evening"提示用一般过去时 gave。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（the + 最高级 + ever）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说这是他收到过的最好的礼物。"the + 最高级"配合 ever，用 best。故选 C。
