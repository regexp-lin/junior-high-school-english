# 中考英语完形填空：和爸爸一起去钓鱼

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my father took me to a lake ___1___ fishing. It was my first time to go fishing, and I was very ___2___ about it.

We arrived at the lake early ___3___ the morning. Father taught me how to put the bait on the hook. He told me to throw the line into the water and wait ___4___.

I sat by the lake for almost an hour ___5___ nothing happened. I began to feel bored and wanted to give ___6___. Father said, "Fishing teaches you patience. Good things come to those ___7___ wait."

Just then, I felt something ___8___ my line. "Pull it up slowly!" Father shouted. I did as he said, and a silver fish appeared. It was not very ___9___, but I was so excited.

Father smiled and said this was just the ___10___. We ended up catching five fish that day. It was the best Saturday I ever had.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
2. A. excite &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. exciting &emsp;&emsp; D. excitement
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
4. A. quiet &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. quite &emsp;&emsp; D. quick
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. out
7. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
8. A. pull &emsp;&emsp; B. pulls &emsp;&emsp; C. to pull &emsp;&emsp; D. pulling
9. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. bigger &emsp;&emsp; C. biggest &emsp;&emsp; D. the biggest
10. A. begin &emsp;&emsp; B. begins &emsp;&emsp; C. beginning &emsp;&emsp; D. began

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（for 表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周六爸爸带我去湖边钓鱼。for fishing 表示"为了钓鱼"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常兴奋。excited 表示人的感受；exciting 修饰事物。主语为 I，用 excited。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（in the morning）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一大早就到了湖边。"in the morning"为固定搭配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他让我把鱼线扔进水里然后安静地等待。修饰动词 wait 用副词 quietly。quiet 为形容词。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（but 转折）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在湖边坐了将近一小时但什么也没发生。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语（give up 放弃）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始觉得无聊想放弃。"give up"表示"放弃"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句（who 指代人）
**【解析】** 本句意为：好事属于那些等待的人。先行词 those 指人，在从句中作主语，用 who。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 感官动词 + 宾语 + doing（表正在进行）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感觉有什么东西在拉我的鱼线。"feel sth. doing"表示"感觉某物正在做某事"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词原级
**【解析】** 本句意为：它不是很大，但我非常兴奋。"not very + adj."用形容词原级。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词（beginning 开始）
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸微笑着说这只是个开始。the beginning 表示"开始"，作表语用名词。故选 C。
