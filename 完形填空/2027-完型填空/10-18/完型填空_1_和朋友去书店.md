# 中考英语完形填空：和朋友去书店

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my friend Li Na and I went to the new bookstore downtown. It ___1___ just two weeks ago and had already become popular among students.

The bookstore was beautifully decorated. There were wooden shelves ___2___ the walls, and soft armchairs were placed in every corner. A pleasant smell of coffee filled the air because there was a small café ___3___ the bookstore.

Li Na went straight to the science fiction section. She has been interested in space stories ___4___ she was ten years old. I preferred literature, so I walked to a different area.

I picked up a book of short stories by a young Chinese writer. After reading the first few pages, I found it hard ___5___ the book down. The language was simple but powerful. Each story was about ordinary people ___6___ did extraordinary things.

Two hours passed quickly. Li Na came over with three books in her arms. "I can't decide ___7___ one to buy," she said with a helpless smile. I suggested that she ___8___ them all since the bookstore was having a sale.

We paid for our books and sat in the café for a while. Li Na said she wished there ___9___ more bookstores like this in our city. I agreed. A good bookstore is not just a shop — it is a place ___10___ people can find peace and inspiration.

## 选项

1. A. open &emsp;&emsp; B. opens &emsp;&emsp; C. opened &emsp;&emsp; D. was opened  
2. A. along &emsp;&emsp; B. between &emsp;&emsp; C. above &emsp;&emsp; D. under  
3. A. outside &emsp;&emsp; B. beside &emsp;&emsp; C. inside &emsp;&emsp; D. behind  
4. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. since &emsp;&emsp; D. before  
5. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. to put &emsp;&emsp; C. putting &emsp;&emsp; D. puts  
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
7. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. which  
8. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. bought &emsp;&emsp; C. buying &emsp;&emsp; D. to buy  
9. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. are &emsp;&emsp; C. was &emsp;&emsp; D. were  
10. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. when  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 被动语态（一般过去时）  
**【解析】** 书店"被开业"，it 与 open 为被动关系，且时间为 two weeks ago，用一般过去时被动语态 was opened。open/opens 为主动；opened 为主动过去式。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（方位）  
**【解析】** along the walls 意为"沿着墙壁"，书架沿墙排列。between 意为"在……之间"需两个对象；above 意为"在上方"；under 意为"在下方"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（方位）  
**【解析】** inside the bookstore 意为"在书店里面"，咖啡馆在书店内部。outside 意为"在外面"与"空气中弥漫咖啡香"矛盾；beside 意为"旁边"；behind 意为"后面"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** since 与现在完成时  
**【解析】** has been interested in...since she was ten 构成"现在完成时 + since + 过去时间点"的固定搭配。when 表时间不与完成时搭配；after 表"在……之后"；before 表"在……之前"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** find it + adj. + to do  
**【解析】** find it hard to put the book down 为固定句型，it 为形式宾语，to put 为真正宾语。put 缺 to；putting 为动名词；puts 为三单。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词（指人）  
**【解析】** 先行词 ordinary people 为人，在从句中作主语，用 who。which 指物；whom 作宾语；what 不引导定语从句。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 疑问代词 which  
**【解析】** which one to buy 意为"买哪一本"，which 表在有限范围内选择。that 不引导此类宾语从句；what 表"什么"无选择范围限制；how 意为"怎样"。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 虚拟语气（suggest + 动词原形）  
**【解析】** suggest that sb. (should) do sth. 中 should 可省略，谓语用动词原形 buy。bought 为过去式；buying 为动名词；to buy 为不定式，均不用于此结构。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 虚拟语气（wish + 过去式）  
**【解析】** wish 后接虚拟语气，表与现在事实相反用一般过去时。bookstores 为复数，be 动词用 were。is/are 为一般现在时；was 为单数过去时。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系副词 where  
**【解析】** 先行词 a place 为地点名词，在从句中作地点状语，用 where 引导定语从句。which/that 在从句中作主语或宾语；when 引导时间。故选 B。
