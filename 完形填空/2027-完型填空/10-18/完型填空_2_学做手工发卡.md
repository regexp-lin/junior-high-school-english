# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工发卡

## 完形填空原文

My elder sister, Mei Mei, loves making accessories. Last weekend, she offered ___1___ me how to make hair clips at home. I happily agreed.

She took out a box full of materials: metal clip bases, fabric flowers, small pearls, and a hot glue gun. I was worried about ___2___ the glue gun because it looked dangerous. Mei Mei smiled and said, "As long as you are careful, you ___3___ be fine."

She showed me the process. First, she chose a piece of pink fabric and cut it ___4___ the shape of a rose. Then she folded the petals one by one and fixed them with glue. The whole process took only five minutes, but the result was ___5___ amazing.

It was my turn next. I chose blue fabric because blue is my ___6___ color. Cutting the petals was easy, but folding them neatly was another story. My rose looked more like a cabbage, ___7___ made my sister laugh out loud.

"Don't give up," she said. "Nobody gets it right the first time." She held my hands and guided me ___8___ each step. After three attempts, I finally made a rose that I was satisfied ___9___.

I added two small pearls to the center and glued the flower onto the clip base. When I put it in my hair and looked in the mirror, I couldn't help ___10___. It was simple but beautiful. Mei Mei said she would teach me to make earrings next time.

## 选项

1. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. teaching &emsp;&emsp; D. to teach  
2. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. used &emsp;&emsp; C. using &emsp;&emsp; D. to use  
3. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. may &emsp;&emsp; C. will &emsp;&emsp; D. must  
4. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
5. A. true &emsp;&emsp; B. truly &emsp;&emsp; C. truth &emsp;&emsp; D. truer  
6. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. favorite &emsp;&emsp; C. love &emsp;&emsp; D. prefer  
7. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
8. A. through &emsp;&emsp; B. across &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. along  
9. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
10. A. smile &emsp;&emsp; B. smiled &emsp;&emsp; C. to smile &emsp;&emsp; D. smiling  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（offer to do）  
**【解析】** offer to do sth. 意为"主动提出做某事"，后接不定式。teach 为原形；taught 为过去式；teaching 为现在分词，均不用于 offer 后。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 about + 动名词  
**【解析】** be worried about doing sth. 意为"担心做某事"，about 为介词后接动名词 using。use 为原形；used 为过去式；to use 为不定式，均不能跟在介词后。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 条件从句中的将来时  
**【解析】** "只要你小心，你就会没事"，主句用一般将来时 will 表示对未来的预测。can 表能力；may 表可能性（不确定）；must 表必须，均不如 will 自然。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 cut...into  
**【解析】** cut it into the shape of 意为"把它剪成……的形状"，into 表变化。from 表来源；with 表工具；by 表方式。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰形容词  
**【解析】** truly amazing 意为"真正地令人惊叹"，truly 为副词修饰形容词 amazing。true 为形容词；truth 为名词；truer 为比较级形容词。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词 favorite  
**【解析】** my favorite color 意为"我最喜欢的颜色"，favorite 为形容词。like/love/prefer 为动词，不能直接修饰名词 color。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句  
**【解析】** 先行词为前面整个句子，从句缺主语，用 which 引导非限制性定语从句。that 不引导非限制性定语从句；who 指人；what 不引导定语从句。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 through 表过程  
**【解析】** guide me through each step 意为"引导我完成每一步"，through 表"经历、通过"。across 表"穿过"（平面）；over 表"越过"；along 表"沿着"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be satisfied with  
**【解析】** be satisfied with 意为"对……感到满意"，with 为固定介词。about/for/of 不与 satisfied 构成此搭配。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 can't help doing  
**【解析】** can't help doing sth. 意为"忍不住做某事"，后接动名词。smile 为原形；smiled 为过去式；to smile 为不定式（can't help to do 意为"不能帮忙做"，含义不同）。故选 D。
