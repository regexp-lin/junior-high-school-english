# 中考英语完形填空：参加校园美食节

## 完形填空原文

Our school's food festival ___1___ held on the playground last Friday. Each class set up ___2___ stall and sold homemade dishes to teachers and parents. Colorful banners waved above the crowd, and laughter mixed with the smell of hot oil.

Our group chose dumplings ___3___ they are easy to share. Early in the morning, we chopped vegetables while the meat ___4___ in the fridge. We washed our hands carefully and wore clean aprons because hygiene matters in a school event.

By noon, a long line formed ___5___ our table. I was so busy ___6___ I forgot to drink water. A parent smiled and said our dumplings tasted ___7___ of all the snacks she tried. She even asked for our simple recipe.

We didn't win first prize, ___8___ we earned something better: teamwork. I already look forward to ___9___ festival next year. I hope more students ___10___ join us and taste different cultures through food. Next time, we might try a spicy sauce and write the ingredients on a neat card.

## 选项

1. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be  
2. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. its &emsp;&emsp; C. it's &emsp;&emsp; D. itself  
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. though  
4. A. stays &emsp;&emsp; B. stayed &emsp;&emsp; C. is staying &emsp;&emsp; D. has stayed  
5. A. in front of &emsp;&emsp; B. behind &emsp;&emsp; C. above &emsp;&emsp; D. below  
6. A. enough &emsp;&emsp; B. such &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. too  
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. the best  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
10. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. should &emsp;&emsp; C. must &emsp;&emsp; D. need  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** festival 与 hold 为被动关系，last Friday 为过去时间，用 was held。is/has been/will be 时态不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** stall 为名词，前用 its stall 表示“每个班级各自的摊位”。it 为人称代词主格/宾格；it's 为 it is 缩写；itself 为反身代词。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句连词 because  
**【解析】** “选择饺子”是因为“容易分享”，表原因用 because。if/unless 条件；though 让步。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** while 引导时间状语从句，叙述过去早晨的准备：我们切菜的同时，肉“一直放在冰箱里”，用 stayed 表示过去的状态，与 chopped 时态一致。stays/is staying 为现在时态，与全文过去叙述不一致；has stayed 强调持续到现在，语境为当天早晨的准备，不宜用现在完成。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词短语 in front of  
**【解析】** 排队应在桌子前方，in front of our table 合理。behind/above/below 与排队常识不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 结果状语从句 so... that...  
**【解析】** so busy that I forgot... 为固定结构。such 后接名词短语；enough/too 结构不同。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** of all the snacks she tried 表范围，用 the best。tasted good 无比较；better 缺 than；best 缺 the。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “没拿一等奖”与“得到更珍贵的东西”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定冠词表特指下一次活动  
**【解析】** the festival next year 特指“明年的（这场）美食节”。a/an 泛指不符；零冠词不适合此特指。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词 can 表“能够/可以”  
**【解析】** hope more students can join us 表示希望更多同学“可以”参加。should 表应该；must 表必须；need 表需要，语气过重或不如 can 自然。故选 A。
