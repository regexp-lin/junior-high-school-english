# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工肥皂

## 完形填空原文

In science club, we learned ___1___ soap could be made safely at school. The teacher prepared gloves and goggles ___2___ we started the experiment. She repeated the safety rules twice because hot liquid can burn skin easily.

We melted a soap base in a water bath and stirred ___3___ slowly with a wooden stick. Then we added a few drops of lavender oil ___4___ color and smell. The classroom filled with a clean floral scent, and everyone leaned closer to watch the swirl.

The mixture had to cool down before it ___5___ poured into molds. Waiting was boring, ___6___ I drew shapes on paper to design the labels. My cousin's name looked messy, but I liked the little heart beside it.

The next day, each bar looked smooth and smelled fresh. I gave one bar ___7___ my cousin as a birthday gift. She said it was ___8___ useful thing she had received from me.

This project showed me science is not only about facts, ___9___ about caring for people around ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
2. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. though  
3. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. its &emsp;&emsp; C. they &emsp;&emsp; D. them  
4. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
5. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. will be &emsp;&emsp; D. has been  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. also  
10. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. us  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 how  
**【解析】** learned how soap could be made 表示学习“肥皂是如何被制作的”，how 表方式。what 需在从句中作成分；why 表原因；when 表时间，此处强调制作方法。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词 before 表“在……之前”  
**【解析】** 实验开始前准备好护具，用 before we started。after 相反；if/though 语义不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 人称代词 it 指代不可数/单数名词  
**【解析】** 指代不可数的 mixture 或 melted soap base，用 it。its 为物主代词；they/them 复数不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 for 表目的  
**【解析】** for color and smell 表示“为了色泽和气味”。of/with/by 表目的不如 for 自然。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** before 引导时间状语从句，it 指 mixture，与 pour 为被动关系，叙述过去用 was poured。is/will be/has been 时态与主句 had to 不一致。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 结果连词 so  
**【解析】** “等待很无聊”与“我在纸上画图形设计标签”为因果关系：因为无聊，所以找事做，用 so。but 表转折，语气不如因果贴切；and 仅并列；or 表选择。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 give sth. to sb.  
**【解析】** give one bar to my cousin 为 give sth. to sb.。for/of/with 不构成 give 的标准双宾结构。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 a  
**【解析】** useful 发音以 /j/ 开头，属辅音音素，用 a useful thing；后面 she had received from me 为定语从句修饰 thing。an 用于元音音素开头的单词前；the 表特指时通常需明确唯一或上下文特指，此处泛指“一件实用的东西”更自然；零冠词不能用于可数名词单数泛指。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 not only... but (also)...  
**【解析】** not only... but (also)... 为固定并列结构，表示“不仅……而且……”，第二空用 but。and/or 不能搭配 not only；also 不能单独连接该结构（常为 but also）。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格与介词 around  
**【解析】** around 为介词，后接宾格 us，表示“我们身边的人”。I/me/my 与“我们”群体语境不符。故选 D。
