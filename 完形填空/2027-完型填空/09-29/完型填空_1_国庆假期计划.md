# 中考英语完形填空：国庆假期计划

## 完形填空原文

The National Day holiday is coming soon. My family and I have been talking about ___1___ we should do during the week off. My father wants ___2___ a short trip to the mountains, while my mother prefers staying in the city ___3___ it is less crowded at home.

I have a different idea. I hope ___4___ some time volunteering at the community center because I enjoy helping others and organizing small games for kids. My little brother, ___5___ is only in Grade 3, simply wishes to visit the science museum twice.

After a long discussion, we finally reached ___6___. We will spend the first three days traveling nearby, and then I can join the volunteer team ___7___ the rest of the holiday. We even wrote the timetable on the fridge so that nobody would forget it. “A good plan makes everyone feel ___8___,” my father said with a smile.

I have already started ___9___ my backpack and checking the weather forecast. I believe this holiday will be ___10___ meaningful than staying in bed all day.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where
2. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. taking &emsp;&emsp; C. to take &emsp;&emsp; D. took
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. spend &emsp;&emsp; B. spending &emsp;&emsp; C. to spend &emsp;&emsp; D. spent
5. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
6. A. an agreement &emsp;&emsp; B. a disagreement &emsp;&emsp; C. a problem &emsp;&emsp; D. a mistake
7. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. in
8. A. relax &emsp;&emsp; B. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxing &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxation
9. A. pack &emsp;&emsp; B. packing &emsp;&emsp; C. packed &emsp;&emsp; D. to pack
10. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词  
**【解析】** “talking about ___ we should do” 中，从句缺宾语，表示“该做什么”，应用 what。how 表方式、when 表时间、where 表地点，均与 do 的搭配及全家讨论“活动内容”的语境不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（want to do）  
**【解析】** want 后接不定式作宾语，即 want to do sth.，表示“想要做某事”。take 为原形；taking 常用于 enjoy、keep 等动词后；took 为过去式，不能直接跟在 want 后。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词辨析（原因状语从句）  
**【解析】** 妈妈更愿意待在家里，后面解释原因：家里不那么拥挤。because 引导原因状语从句。if 表条件，until 表时间，unless 表“除非”，均不符合前后逻辑。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** hope 的用法（hope to do）  
**【解析】** hope 后常接不定式 hope to do，表示“希望去做某事”；hope 也可接 that 从句，但一般不直接接动名词作宾语。spend some time volunteering 表示“花些时间做志愿”，用 to spend 正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句关系词  
**【解析】** 先行词为 my little brother（人），从句缺主语，非限制性定语从句用 who 引导。which 指物；whom 在从句中作宾语；whose 表所属，此处不适用。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 名词短语与语境理解  
**【解析】** 下文说大家决定前几天旅行、假期其余时间参加志愿活动等，说明讨论后“达成一致”。reach an agreement 为固定搭配，意为“达成协议、取得一致”。disagreement（分歧）、problem（问题）、mistake（错误）与积极结果矛盾。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词与时间段的搭配  
**【解析】** “for the rest of the holiday” 表示“在假期剩余的时间里”，for + 时间段表持续。on 多用于具体某天；at 表时刻；in 虽可表“在……期间”，但与 the rest of 搭配时，for 更常见、更自然。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（-ed / -ing）  
**【解析】** feel 为系动词，后接形容词说明人的感受。主语是 everyone（人），表示“感到放松的”用 relaxed。relaxing 多修饰事物（令人放松的）；relax 为动词；relaxation 为名词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** start doing / start to do  
**【解析】** start 后可接动名词或不定式，表示“开始做某事”。start packing 与后面 checking 并列，结构平行，均为 start 后的宾语形式，用 packing 顺畅。若用 to pack 亦可，但选项中 packing 与 “already started” 强调动作进行的过程更贴合。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 句中有 than，提示比较级，meaningful 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more meaningful。many/much 不直接修饰 meaningful；most 为最高级，需有范围或 the。故选 C。
