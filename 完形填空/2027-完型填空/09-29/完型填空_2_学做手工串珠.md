# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工串珠

## 完形填空原文

Yesterday afternoon, I went to a DIY workshop ___1___ my cousin to learn how to make a beaded bracelet. The teacher handed us elastic string, colorful beads, and ___2___ small scissors.

“Thread the needle ___3___,” she reminded us. At first, my beads kept sliding off ___4___ I tied a strong knot at the end. My cousin laughed ___5___ but helped me patiently.

We followed the pattern on the paper step ___6___ step. The colors on the table looked like tiny candies, and the whole room smelled sweet because of the fruit tea at the welcome corner. When the bracelet finally fit my wrist, it looked even ___7___ than I had imagined. The teacher praised us ___8___ working carefully and not giving up halfway.

On the way home, I decided ___9___ a matching necklace for my mom next weekend. Making things by hand is not only fun ___10___ also a good way to improve focus.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. but
2. A. a pair of &emsp;&emsp; B. a piece of &emsp;&emsp; C. a bottle of &emsp;&emsp; D. a cup of
3. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. more careful
4. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. before &emsp;&emsp; D. while
5. A. soft &emsp;&emsp; B. softer &emsp;&emsp; C. softly &emsp;&emsp; D. softest
6. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. to
7. A. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; B. more beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. most beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. beautifully
8. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. for
9. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析  
**【解析】** “和表姐一起去手工坊”表示伴随，用 with。and 连接并列成分时需前后结构对称，此处 went... with my cousin 为标准表达；or 表选择；but 表转折。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 量词短语辨析  
**【解析】** scissors（剪刀）通常与 a pair of 搭配，表示“一把剪刀”。a piece of 多修饰纸、面包等；a bottle of、a cup of 与剪刀无关。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** “Thread the needle ___” 中，修饰动词 thread（穿线）要用副词。carefully（仔细地）符合老师提醒的语境。care 为名词；careful 为形容词；more careful 为形容词比较级，不能直接修饰动词。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 连词辨析（时间关系）  
**【解析】** 珠子一直滑掉，应发生在“在末端打好牢固的结之前”。before 表示“在……之前”，符合逻辑：打好结之后才不会滑。when/after/while 均不能准确表达“先滑掉、后打结才解决”的顺序。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** laughed 是动词，需用副词修饰。softly（轻声地）表示表姐善意地轻笑。soft/softer/softest 为形容词形式，不能修饰动词。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 step by step  
**【解析】** step by step 为固定短语，意为“一步一步地”。with/for/to 均不能构成此固定搭配。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 句中有 than，需用比较级。beautiful 为多音节形容词，比较级为 more beautiful。原级与最高级、副词 beautifully 均不符合 than 的结构要求。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 praise sb. for (doing) sth.  
**【解析】** praise sb. for... 表示“因……而表扬某人”，后接原因。praise... of/about/with 均非标准搭配。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** decide to do sth.  
**【解析】** decide 后接不定式作宾语，即 decide to do sth.，表示“决定做某事”。make/making/made 不能直接作 decide 的宾语。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 not only... but also...  
**【解析】** 前半句有 not only，后半句需用 but also 构成固定并列结构，表示“不但……而且……”，空白处填 but。and、or、so 均不能与 not only 构成该固定搭配。故选 C。
