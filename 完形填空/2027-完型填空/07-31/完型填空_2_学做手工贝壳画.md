# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工贝壳画

## 完形填空原文

At the seaside workshop, Clara learned ___1___ make a shell painting. She chose shells ___2___ different sizes and cleaned them ___3___.

She arranged the shells ___4___ a wooden board ___5___ they looked like a small sailboat. The teacher helped her fix them ___6___ strong glue.

When the glue dried, Clara painted the background ___7___ light blue waves. The finished work looked ___8___ lively than a flat poster.

She decided ___9___ give it to her father ___10___ birthday was in August. Handmade gifts always carry warm feelings.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. care &emsp;&emsp; D. caring
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. the most
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. which

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词+不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：克拉拉学习如何做贝壳画。learn how to do sth. 学习如何做某事。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她挑选了不同大小的贝壳。shells of different sizes 不同尺寸的贝壳。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：并把它们仔细清洗干净。修饰动词 cleaned，用副词 carefully。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把贝壳摆放在木板上。on a wooden board 在木板上。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词 / 目的状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：以便它们看起来像一艘小帆船。so that 表目的。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师帮她用强力胶固定。with strong glue 用强力胶。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：克拉拉用浅蓝色波浪绘制背景。paint…with… 用……来画/涂。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：成品比平面海报更有生气。than 提示用 more lively。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她决定把它送给父亲。decide to do sth. 决定做某事。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：父亲的生日在八月。先行词 father 与 birthday 为所属关系，用 whose。故选 C。
