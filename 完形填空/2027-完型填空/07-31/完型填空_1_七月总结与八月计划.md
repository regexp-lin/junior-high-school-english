# 中考英语完形填空：七月总结与八月计划

## 完形填空原文

July has ended, ___1___ Grace wrote a short summary in her diary. She listed the skills ___2___ learned, such as swimming and cooking simple meals.

She also noted two habits ___3___ wanted to improve in August: reading for 30 minutes daily ___4___ going to bed earlier. Her mother suggested ___5___ share the plan with her study group.

Grace believes ___6___ small steps will lead to big changes. She hopes August will be ___7___ productive than July ___8___ she can enter the new term confidently.

She pinned the diary page ___9___ the wall ___10___ she could see it every morning.

## 选项

1. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. although
2. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself
3. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself
4. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. but
5. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself
6. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. how
7. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. the most
8. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
10. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：七月结束了，所以格蕾丝在日记里写了简短总结。因果关系用 so。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词 / 定语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：她列出自己学到的技能。省略关系代词 that 的定语从句中缺主语，用 she，即 the skills (that) she learned。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词 / 定语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：她还记下她想在八月改进的两个习惯。定语从句修饰 habits，从句缺主语，用 she。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天阅读三十分钟并且更早睡觉。并列两项计划，用 and。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 宾语从句 / 虚拟语气（初中常作固定结构识记）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈建议她与学习小组分享计划。suggest (that) sb. (should) do sth. 中 that 可省略，从句主语用人称代词主格 she。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：格蕾丝相信小步会带来大变化。believes that… 陈述性宾语从句用 that。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她希望八月比七月更高效。than 提示用 more productive。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词 / 目的状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：以便她能自信地进入新学期。so that 表目的。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把日记那一页钉在墙上。on the wall 在墙上。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词 / 目的状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：以便她每天早上都能看到。so that 表目的。故选 C。
