# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工布袋

## 完形填空原文

As part of our school's "Green Earth" project, we were asked to make reusable bags to ___1___ plastic ones. Our home economics teacher guided us through the process.

Each student received a piece of cotton cloth. The teacher said cotton bags are better for the ___2___ because they can be used many times and won't pollute the soil.

She taught us the basic steps: measure, cut, fold, and sew. The measuring part was easy, but cutting the cloth in a ___3___ line was challenging. I used a ruler to help and managed to cut it quite well.

Sewing was the part I enjoyed ___4___. The needle went up and down through the cloth like a tiny dancer. My stitches weren't very even, but they were strong ___5___ to hold the bag together.

When the bag was almost done, I added a special touch. I used fabric paint ___6___ a tree on the front. The tree had green leaves and a brown trunk. Underneath, I wrote "Love Our Planet" in English.

My teacher was ___7___ with my design. She said it was not only practical but also ___8___. She encouraged ___9___ to keep thinking about ways to protect the environment.

I ___10___ this bag to the supermarket the following weekend. The cashier complimented it and said she wished more people would use handmade bags instead of plastic ones.

## 选项

1. A. replace &emsp;&emsp; B. repair &emsp;&emsp; C. recycle &emsp;&emsp; D. return  
2. A. environment &emsp;&emsp; B. environmental &emsp;&emsp; C. environmentally &emsp;&emsp; D. environments  
3. A. curve &emsp;&emsp; B. straight &emsp;&emsp; C. round &emsp;&emsp; D. thick  
4. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. the most  
5. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. quite &emsp;&emsp; D. enough  
6. A. draw &emsp;&emsp; B. drawing &emsp;&emsp; C. drew &emsp;&emsp; D. to draw  
7. A. impress &emsp;&emsp; B. impressed &emsp;&emsp; C. impressing &emsp;&emsp; D. impression  
8. A. beauty &emsp;&emsp; B. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. beautifully &emsp;&emsp; D. more beautiful  
9. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. our &emsp;&emsp; C. us &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
10. A. bring &emsp;&emsp; B. brings &emsp;&emsp; C. brought &emsp;&emsp; D. bringing  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 动词辨析  
**【解析】** replace 意为"替代"，制作环保袋来替代塑料袋。repair 意为"修理"；recycle 意为"回收"；return 意为"归还"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 名词作介词宾语  
**【解析】** for the environment 意为"对环境（有利）"，the 后接名词 environment。environmental 为形容词；environmentally 为副词；environments 为复数，此处用单数泛指。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词辨析  
**【解析】** a straight line 意为"一条直线"，剪布时沿直线剪。curve 意为"曲线"（名词）；round 意为"圆形的"；thick 意为"厚的"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** enjoyed the most 意为"最享受的"，the most 为最高级修饰动词 enjoyed。much 为原级；more 为比较级；most 缺冠词 the。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** enough 的位置  
**【解析】** strong enough to hold 意为"足够牢固以承受"，enough 修饰形容词后置。too 含消极义；very/quite 表程度但不表"足够"。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）  
**【解析】** used fabric paint to draw 意为"用布料颜料来画"，to draw 表目的。draw 为原形缺 to；drawing 为动名词；drew 为过去式。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be impressed with  
**【解析】** was impressed with 意为"对……印象深刻"，impressed 作形容词。impress 为动词；impressing 为现在分词；impression 为名词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语（not only...but also...）  
**【解析】** not only practical but also beautiful，beautiful 与 practical 并列作表语。beauty 为名词；beautifully 为副词；more beautiful 为比较级，此处无比较。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** encourage 为及物动词，后接宾格 us。we 为主格；our 为物主代词；ours 为名词性物主代词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** the following weekend 为过去时间标志，动词用过去式 brought。bring 为原形；brings 为三单现在时；bringing 为现在分词。故选 C。
