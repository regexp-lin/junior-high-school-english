# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校广播站

## 完形填空原文

I have always dreamed of ___1___ a radio host. When our school radio station started recruiting new members, I signed up without any hesitation.

The interview took place in a small room next to the teacher's office. Three senior students sat behind a desk and asked me several ___2___. I was nervous at first, but I tried to answer each one as ___3___ as possible.

The last question was the hardest: "Please read this passage aloud." My hands were shaking ___4___ I picked up the paper. But once I started reading, I became calm. The words flowed naturally, and I even added some emotion to my voice.

Two days later, the results ___5___ on the school notice board. I was ___6___ to find my name on the list! I wanted to shout with joy, but I held back because I was in the hallway.

My first broadcast was on the ___7___ Monday. I introduced a piece of English music and shared its background story. Although I made a few small ___8___, most listeners said they enjoyed it.

Now I do a broadcast every Wednesday. It has helped me become more ___9___. My English teacher said my pronunciation has improved a lot since I joined the station. I'm grateful ___10___ this opportunity changed my life.

## 选项

1. A. become &emsp;&emsp; B. becoming &emsp;&emsp; C. became &emsp;&emsp; D. becomes  
2. A. question &emsp;&emsp; B. questions &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. problems  
3. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. clearly &emsp;&emsp; C. clearer &emsp;&emsp; D. clearest  
4. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. post &emsp;&emsp; B. posted &emsp;&emsp; C. were posted &emsp;&emsp; D. are posted  
6. A. thrill &emsp;&emsp; B. thrills &emsp;&emsp; C. thrilling &emsp;&emsp; D. thrilled  
7. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. follows &emsp;&emsp; C. followed &emsp;&emsp; D. following  
8. A. mistake &emsp;&emsp; B. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; C. mistaken &emsp;&emsp; D. mistakenly  
9. A. confidence &emsp;&emsp; B. confident &emsp;&emsp; C. confidently &emsp;&emsp; D. confidences  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 of + 动名词  
**【解析】** dream of doing sth. 意为"梦想做某事"，of 为介词后接动名词 becoming。become 为原形；became 为过去式；becomes 为三单，均不能跟在介词后。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词单复数  
**【解析】** several questions 意为"几个问题"，several 后接可数名词复数。question 为单数；problem 多指"难题/困难"而非"面试问题"；problems 同理。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** as + 副词原级 + as possible  
**【解析】** as clearly as possible 意为"尽可能清楚地"，修饰动词 answer 用副词。clear 为形容词；clearer/clearest 为比较级/最高级，不用于 as...as 结构。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "当我拿起纸时手在发抖"，when 表"当……时候"。before 表"在……之前"；until 表"直到"；unless 表"除非"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 被动语态（一般过去时）  
**【解析】** results 与 post 为被动关系（结果被张贴），且为过去事件，用 were posted。post 为主动原形；posted 为主动过去式；are posted 为现在时。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）  
**【解析】** was thrilled 意为"非常激动"，thrilled 修饰人的感受。thrill 为动词/名词；thrills 为三单/名词复数；thrilling 意为"令人激动的"修饰事物。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词 following 表"接下来的"  
**【解析】** the following Monday 意为"接下来的那个星期一"，following 作形容词。follow 为动词；follows 为三单；followed 为过去式/过去分词。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词单复数（a few + 复数名词）  
**【解析】** a few small mistakes 意为"一些小失误"，a few 后接可数名词复数 mistakes。mistake 为单数；mistaken 为形容词"错误的"；mistakenly 为副词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** become more confident 意为"变得更自信"，become 为系动词后接形容词。confidence 为名词；confidently 为副词；confidences 为名词复数。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连词 that  
**【解析】** be grateful that... 意为"很感激……"，that 引导宾语从句（表内容）。what/which 有疑问含义；how 意为"怎样"，均不符合"感激某件事"的语义。故选 C。
