# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校读书分享会

## 完形填空原文

Our school library held a reading sharing meeting last Wednesday. Students ___1___ favorite genre was science fiction were invited to talk about one book they had read recently.

I chose a novel ___2___ main character travels through time. I practiced my speech at home ___3___ I wouldn’t forget the key ideas.

When it was my turn, my heart beat fast. ___4___, I took a deep breath and began. I explained ___5___ the story changed my view of friendship.

The audience listened quietly and nodded from time to time. After the meeting, a girl ___6___ I didn’t know came up and asked ___7___ she could borrow my book.

I lent it to her happily. On the way home, I thought reading becomes ___8___ meaningful when we share ___9___ thoughts with others.

I have already decided ___10___ another book for the next sharing session.

## 选项

1. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
2. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
3. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
4. A. However &emsp;&emsp; B. Therefore &emsp;&emsp; C. Besides &emsp;&emsp; D. Instead  
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
7. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
8. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  
9. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
10. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. preparing &emsp;&emsp; C. to prepare &emsp;&emsp; D. prepared  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句 whose（表所属）  
**【解析】** Students whose favorite genre was science fiction，favorite genre 与 Students 为所属关系，用 whose。which/who/whom 不能表达“学生的最爱类型”。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句 whose（whose main character）  
**【解析】** a novel whose main character travels... 小说与主角为所属关系。which/who/whom 不表所属。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词 so that（以便）  
**【解析】** practiced... so that I wouldn’t forget... 表目的。although 尽管；unless 除非；if 如果，逻辑不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连接副词 However（然而）  
**【解析】** “心跳很快”与“深呼吸开始”转折，However 放句首。Therefore 因此；Besides 此外；Instead 相反，语境不如 However。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 how  
**【解析】** explained how the story changed my view... 表示“解释故事如何改变我的看法”。what 缺宾语语义；which/when 不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句 who（指人，作主语）  
**【解析】** a girl who I didn’t know，先行词为人，从句缺主语，用 who。which 指物；whom 作宾语；whose 表所属。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 if（是否）  
**【解析】** asked if she could borrow... 表示“问是否能借”。that 无“是否”义；what/how 与 borrow my book 搭配不符。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 more meaningful  
**【解析】** becomes more meaningful when... 隐含与“不分享时”比较，用 more。meaningful 多音节；very/too 无比较；most 缺范围。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词 our  
**【解析】** share our thoughts，名词 thoughts 前用 our。we/us 不能作定语；ours 后不接名词。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decided to prepare another book，decide 后接不定式。prepare/preparing/prepared 不符合搭配。故选 C。
