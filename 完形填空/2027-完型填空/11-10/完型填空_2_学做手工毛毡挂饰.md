# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工毛毡挂饰

## 完形填空原文

During the art workshop, we learned ___1___ cut felt into cute shapes. The teacher warned us ___2___ the scissors because they were sharp.

I drew a little star on blue felt and cut it out slowly. Then I used a needle ___3___ thread to sew a loop at the top. My fingers were sore, ___4___ I didn’t give up.

When I hung the ornament near the window, the winter sunlight made the felt look ___5___ soft cloud. My friend Jake made ___6___ owl, and we compared ___7___ designs happily.

The teacher said our work would ___8___ in the hallway next Monday. That news made everyone cheer ___9___ than before.

On the bus home, I kept the ornament in my bag ___10___ it wouldn’t be crushed.

## 选项

1. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to  
2. A. be careful with &emsp;&emsp; B. to be careful with &emsp;&emsp; C. being careful with &emsp;&emsp; D. careful with  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
5. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. likes &emsp;&emsp; C. likely &emsp;&emsp; D. liking  
6. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
7. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
8. A. show &emsp;&emsp; B. showed &emsp;&emsp; C. be shown &emsp;&emsp; D. be showing  
9. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. louder &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. more loud  
10. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式” how to cut  
**【解析】** learned how to cut felt... 表示“学习如何裁剪”。what to cut 语义可通但此处强调方法；when/where 与 cut into shapes 不如 how 贴切。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（warn sb. to do）  
**【解析】** warned us to be careful with the scissors，warn sb. to do sth. 用不定式。be careful with 缺 to；being careful with 动名词不符合 warn 常见结构；careful with 非完整不定式。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and（needle and thread）  
**【解析】** a needle and thread 为固定搭配“针线”。or/but/so 连接 needle 与 thread 不合理。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “手指疼”与“没放弃”转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 like（像……）  
**【解析】** look like a soft cloud，like 为介词。likes/likely/liking 均错误。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 an（owl 元音音素）  
**【解析】** an owl，owl 以元音音素开头。a/the/零冠词不符。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词 our  
**【解析】** compared our designs，名词 designs 前用 our。we/us 不能作定语；ours 后不接名词。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 含有情态动词的被动语态 would be shown  
**【解析】** work would be shown in the hallway，作品被展示，用被动 be shown。show/showed 为主动；be showing 进行体不贴切。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级（louder）  
**【解析】** than 提示比较级；cheer louder 表示“欢呼得更响亮”，louder 为 loud 的比较级，可作副词用。loud 为原级；loudly 的比较级通常为 more loudly，但选项 D more loud 为错误形式；cheer loud 不如比较结构贴切。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词 so that（以便）  
**【解析】** kept... in my bag so that it wouldn’t be crushed 表目的。although/unless/if 逻辑不符。故选 A。
