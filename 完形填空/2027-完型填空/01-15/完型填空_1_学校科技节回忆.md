# 中考英语完形填空：学校科技节回忆

## 完形填空原文

Last term, our school held a science festival in the gym. Our group decided to build a small bridge ___1___ paper rolls and glue, testing how much weight it could carry.

At first, our design was too ___2___ and broke quickly. We argued a little, ___3___ then we listened to each other's ideas calmly. We added more support on both sides ___4___ the structure became stronger.

On the show day, judges walked ___5___ with clipboards, asking clear questions. When they placed books on our bridge, I held my breath. The bridge stayed ___6___ until the fifth book, and the crowd clapped loudly.

We did not win first prize, ___7___ we received a certificate for teamwork. That experience taught me that failure at the start does not ___8___ the end of a project if people keep trying.

Now, when I face difficult homework, I remember how we ___9___ problems step by step. Science is not only about facts, ___10___ about curiosity and cooperation.

## 选项

1. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. like
2. A. weak &emsp;&emsp; B. heavy &emsp;&emsp; C. wide &emsp;&emsp; D. fresh
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although
5. A. around &emsp;&emsp; B. above &emsp;&emsp; C. below &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
6. A. silent &emsp;&emsp; B. steady &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. empty
7. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
8. A. mean &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. show &emsp;&emsp; D. save
9. A. avoided &emsp;&emsp; B. solved &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. copied
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们小组决定用纸卷和胶水搭建一座小桥，测试它能承受多大重量。with 表示"用（材料/工具）"；from 来自；about 关于；like 像。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我们的设计太脆弱，很快就断了。A 项 weak 脆弱的；B 项 heavy 重的；C 项 wide 宽的；D 项 fresh 新鲜的。与 broke quickly 呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们争了几句，但随后我们冷静地倾听彼此的想法。前后转折：争执 vs 冷静沟通。but 表转折；and 并列；or 选择；so 结果。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在两侧增加支撑，直到结构变得更牢固。until 直到……为止；if 如果；unless 除非；although 尽管。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：展示日那天，评委们拿着写字板四处走动。walk around 四处走动；walk above/below/beyond 不符合语境。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：桥一直稳稳支撑到第五本书放下，人群大声鼓掌。A 项 silent 安静；B 项 steady 稳固的；C 项 lonely 孤独；D 项 empty 空。桥承重应 steady。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们没有获得一等奖，但得到了团队合作证书。前后转折：未得最高奖 vs 仍有收获。because 原因；though 尽管（位置多不同）；but 但是；unless 除非。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那次经历让我明白：开始的失败并不意味着项目的结束，只要大家继续坚持。mean 表示"意味着"；keep 保持；show 展示；save 拯救。does not mean the end 不意味着结束。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在当我遇到难题作业时，会想起我们如何一步步解决问题。solve problems 解决问题；avoid 避免；forget 忘记；copy 抄袭。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：科学不仅关于事实，也关于好奇与合作。not only ... but (also) ... 为固定结构。故选 C。
