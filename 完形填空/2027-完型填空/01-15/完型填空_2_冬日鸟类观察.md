# 中考英语完形填空：冬日鸟类观察

## 完形填空原文

On a cold Saturday, our biology teacher led us to the riverside park for a bird-watching activity. She told us to stay ___1___ and use binoculars instead of running after birds.

At first, I only saw branches and gray sky. I felt ___2___ and thought the trip was a waste of time. Then a small bird landed ___3___ a fence and tilted its head, as if it were studying us ___4___.

I slowly raised my notebook and drew a simple ___5___ of its round body and short tail. The teacher whispered that many birds ___6___ to warmer places in winter, but some stay and look for seeds.

When the sun came out, more birds appeared. Their calls sounded ___7___ music across the quiet field. I realized patience is a kind of ___8___ when you observe nature.

Back in class, I shared my sketches ___9___ my group. We listed the birds we saw and wrote down one new English word for each. That day, winter felt less ___10___ because life was still moving in the air.

## 选项

1. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
3. A. under &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. against
4. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. also &emsp;&emsp; C. either &emsp;&emsp; D. instead
5. A. song &emsp;&emsp; B. sketch &emsp;&emsp; C. joke &emsp;&emsp; D. lie
6. A. fly &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. return &emsp;&emsp; D. agree
7. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. prize &emsp;&emsp; B. skill &emsp;&emsp; C. trouble &emsp;&emsp; D. excuse
9. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. about &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. from
10. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. empty &emsp;&emsp; C. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; D. silent

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们保持安静，用望远镜观察而不是追着鸟跑。观鸟需要安静，quiet 符合语境。loud 吵闹；busy 忙碌；angry 生气。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我只看到树枝和灰蒙蒙的天空，感到无聊，觉得这次出行浪费时间。bored 无聊；proud 自豪；relaxed 放松；surprised 惊讶。与 waste of time 呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后一只小鸟落在栅栏上。on a fence 在栅栏上；under 在下面；through 穿过；against 靠着。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它歪着头，好像也在打量我们。as if it were studying us too 表示"也、同样"；also 一般用于句中不置句末；either 用于否定；instead 相反。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我慢慢举起笔记本，画了它圆身体和短尾巴的简单素描。sketch 素描、速写；song 歌曲；joke 玩笑；lie 谎言。与 drew 搭配。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师轻声说许多鸟冬天会飞往更温暖的地方，但也有一些留下来寻找种子。fly to warmer places 飞往更暖处；forget 忘记；return 返回；agree 同意。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们的叫声在安静的田野上听起来像音乐。sound like 听起来像；sound for/at/with 不表达比喻。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到观察自然时，耐心也是一种能力。skill 技能；prize 奖品；trouble 麻烦；excuse 借口。patience is a skill 合理。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回到教室后，我与小组同学分享了我的速写。share sth. with sb. 与某人分享某物。of/about/from 不用于此结构。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那一天，冬天似乎不再那么空洞，因为空中仍有生命在跃动。empty 可形容冬日单调空洞；long 长；dangerous 危险；silent 安静。与 life was still moving 对比。故选 B。
