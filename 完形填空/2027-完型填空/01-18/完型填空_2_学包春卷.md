# 中考英语完形填空：学包春卷

## 完形填空原文

The day before Spring Festival, Grandma invited me to the kitchen. She placed a bowl of fresh vegetables and thin wrappers on the table and said, "Today you will learn a family skill ___1___." I washed my hands ___2___ sat down beside her. The kitchen lights looked warm, and I could hear fireworks popping far away on the street.

Grandma showed me how to put the filling in the center and fold the sides ___3___. She repeated each movement twice so that I could follow her rhythm without rushing. My first spring roll looked ___4___ a small pillow, not a neat stick. I laughed at myself, ___5___ Grandma encouraged me to try again.

After several tries, my rolls became ___6___ and less likely to break in the pan. Dad came in and smelled the oil heating. He promised ___7___ wait patiently for the first plate. When the spring rolls turned golden, we shared them ___8___ some sweet and sour sauce.

That evening, I felt the holiday was not only about food, ___9___ about love passed down ___10___ generation to generation.

## 选项

1. A. quietly &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. politely &emsp;&emsp; D. happily
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. loudly &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; D. nervously
4. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
6. A. uglier &emsp;&emsp; B. tighter &emsp;&emsp; C. emptier &emsp;&emsp; D. colder
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：今天你要“认真地”学一项家里的手艺。A 项 quietly 安静地；B 项 carefully 认真地；C 项 politely 礼貌地；D 项 happily 开心地。教包春卷强调细心。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我洗了手，然后坐在她旁边。两个顺承动作用 and 连接。A 项 but 转折；B 项 or 选择；D 项 so 表结果，此处非因果。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶示范如何把馅放中间并“慢慢地”折两边。A 项 loudly 大声；B 项 slowly 慢慢地；C 项 carelessly 粗心；D 项 nervously 紧张。教学示范用 slowly。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的第一个春卷看起来“像”小枕头。look like 表示“看起来像”。A 项 after 之后；C 项 for 为了；D 项 with 和。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我自嘲地笑了，“奶奶却/并且”鼓励我再试。此处表顺承连接两个分句：笑了之后奶奶鼓励，用 and。若用 though 需放句首或“laugh, though...”略有别扭；用 and 最顺。A 项 because 因果不当；D 项 unless 除非。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：几次之后，我的春卷包得更“紧”，下锅更不容易散。A 项 uglier 更丑；B 项 tighter 更紧；C 项 emptier 更空；D 项 colder 更冷。与 less likely to break 呼应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词非谓语/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他答应耐心等第一盘。promise to do sth. 为固定搭配。B、C、D 项不能直接接 wait。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们蘸着甜酸酱分享春卷。share...with... 或 eat...with sauce 表“配着/蘸着”。with some sauce 表示搭配酱料。A 项 for 为了；B 项 to 向；D 项 about 关于。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（固定结构）
**【解析】** 本句意为：节日不仅关于食物，“而且”关于代代相传的爱。not only...but (also)... 固定搭配。A、B、D 项不能搭配。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：从一代传到下一代。from...to... 为固定搭配。B 项 by 通过；C 项 on 在；D 项 at 在。故选 A。
