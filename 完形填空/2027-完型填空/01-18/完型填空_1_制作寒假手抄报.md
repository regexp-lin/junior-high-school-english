# 中考英语完形填空：制作寒假手抄报

## 完形填空原文

Our English teacher asked us to make a poster ___1___ our winter vacation. The topic was "Healthy Habits", ___2___ I decided to draw pictures of sports, reading, and enough sleep. At first, I worried ___3___ my handwriting was not neat enough. I stared at the blank paper for a long time, unsure how to start, and almost wanted to give up on the first day.

I collected colorful markers and cut some photos ___4___ old magazines. I also searched online for simple icons of bicycles and books so that my poster would look lively and clear from a distance. My cousin came to visit and offered ___5___ help. She wrote the titles in clear letters ___6___ I painted the borders blue and yellow. Working together made the job ___7___ and more enjoyable.

On Monday, I carried the poster to school ___8___ a big folder. Many classmates praised the design, ___9___ the teacher said it would be displayed on the classroom wall. That moment, I understood teamwork can turn a difficult task ___10___ something beautiful.

## 选项

1. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. against &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where
4. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. my &emsp;&emsp; B. his &emsp;&emsp; C. her &emsp;&emsp; D. our
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
7. A. harder &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. stranger &emsp;&emsp; D. heavier
8. A. under &emsp;&emsp; B. inside &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. against
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. onto &emsp;&emsp; D. over

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们做一张“关于”寒假的海报。A 项 about 关于；B 项 against 反对；C 项 without 没有；D 项 beyond 超出。a poster about... 为常见搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：主题是“健康习惯”，“所以”我决定画运动、阅读和充足睡眠。前因后果用 so。A 项 because 因为（因果颠倒）；C 项 although 尽管；D 项 unless 除非。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（宾语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我担心我的书写不够工整。worry that... 后接宾语从句陈述担心内容。B 项 whether 是否；C 项 when 何时；D 项 where 何地。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从旧杂志上剪下一些照片。cut...from... 表示从……剪下。A 项 on 在……上；C 项 by 通过；D 项 at 在。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐来访，主动提出帮忙。cousin 女性用 her help；若性别不明可用 their，但选项无，语境表姐为女性。A 项 my；B 项 his；D 项 our 与主语 she 不符。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她写标题，“同时/而”我画蓝黄边框。while 表两个动作同时进行。A 项 if 如果；B 项 until 直到；D 项 unless 除非。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：合作让这件事更轻松、更有趣。A 项 harder 更难；B 项 easier 更容易；C 项 stranger 更奇怪；D 项 heavier 更重。与 enjoyable 并列的积极词为 easier。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把海报放进大文件夹带到学校。inside a folder 表示在文件夹里。A 项 under 在下面；C 项 beside 在旁边；D 项 against 靠着。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多同学称赞设计，“而且”老师说会贴在教室墙上。并列补充信息用 and。B 项 but 转折；C 项 or 选择；D 项 so 结果。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：团队合作能把困难任务“变成”美好的东西。turn...into... 为固定搭配。A 项 in；C 项 onto；D 项 over 均不与 turn 构成此义。故选 B。
