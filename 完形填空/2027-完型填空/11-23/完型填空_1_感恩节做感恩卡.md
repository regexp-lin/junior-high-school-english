# 中考英语完形填空：感恩节做感恩卡

## 完形填空原文

Thanksgiving was coming, and our English teacher asked us ___1___ make thank-you cards for someone important. I chose my grandma ___2___ she always cooks for me and tells me interesting stories. I bought thick paper, colored pens, and some small stickers ___3___ a shop near my school. On the way home, I imagined how surprised she would be when she read my message in both English and Chinese. At home, I wrote, "Thank you ___4___ being patient with me." I wanted the words ___5___ look warm, so I drew golden leaves around them. ___6___ I was writing, my cat jumped onto the table and almost knocked over the ink. ___7___, nothing was ruined. On Thanksgiving Day, I gave the card ___8___ my grandma with a hug. Her eyes became wet, ___9___ she smiled and said it was the best gift. That moment, I understood ___10___ a few honest words could mean so much.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
3. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. in  
4. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of  
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. not &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
6. A. When &emsp;&emsp; B. Before &emsp;&emsp; C. After &emsp;&emsp; D. Until  
7. A. Lucky &emsp;&emsp; B. Luck &emsp;&emsp; C. Luckily &emsp;&emsp; D. Unlucky  
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do sth.）  
**【解析】** ask us to make 是固定结构，不定式作宾补；for/of/with 不构成 ask 的标准搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词（原因状语从句）  
**【解析】** 选择奶奶的原因是“她总是给我做饭并讲故事”，表原因用 because；but/so/or 不符合因果逻辑。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词（from a shop 从商店买来）  
**【解析】** bought...from a shop 表示购买来源；on/at/in 与 buy from 的习惯搭配不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（thank you for doing sth.）  
**【解析】** thank you for being patient 是固定介词搭配；to/with/of 均不构成该句型。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（want sth. to do）  
**【解析】** want the words to look warm 为 want + 宾语 + to do；want 后不接动词原形直接作补语；not 会造成语义不通；of/for 不能构成 want sth. to do 结构。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词（时间状语从句：当……正在……）  
**【解析】** When I was writing 表示“当我正在写的时候”，符合猫跳上桌的情节；Before/After/Until 与“正在写”同时进行的情境不符。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词（修饰整个句子，表“幸运的是”）  
**【解析】** Luckily 为评注性副词，放句首表示“幸好”；Lucky 为形容词，Luck 为名词，Unlucky 语义相反。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词（give sth. to sb.）  
**【解析】** give the card to my grandma 是双宾语结构常用介词搭配；give sth. for sb. 不自然；with/at 不符合 give 与间接宾语搭配。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 连词（转折：眼含泪水但微笑）  
**【解析】** “眼睛湿润”与“她微笑并说……”存在轻微转折，用 but；and 纯并列，or 选择，so 结果，均不如 but 细腻。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词（how 表程度：多么/如何）  
**【解析】** understand how a few honest words could mean so much 表示明白“几句真诚的话如何能意义重大”；what 需带名词性成分，why/when 与“意义重大”的程度表达不符。故选 B。
