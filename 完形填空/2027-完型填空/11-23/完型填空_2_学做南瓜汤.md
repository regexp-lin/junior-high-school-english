# 中考英语完形填空：学做南瓜汤

## 完形填空原文

On a cold evening, Dad rolled up his sleeves and said he would teach me ___1___ cook pumpkin soup. Outside, the wind blew against the windows, and the kitchen lights looked especially warm. We cut the pumpkin into pieces ___2___ put them into a big pot with water. Dad told me ___3___ I should keep the heat low after the water boiled. "If the fire is ___4___ high, the soup may taste bitter," he explained. I stirred the pot ___5___ a wooden spoon and watched the steam rise. ___6___ about forty minutes, the pumpkin became soft enough to break easily. Dad let me use a blender ___7___ the soup looked smooth. Finally, we added a little salt and black pepper ___8___ taste. When I took the first spoonful, the warmth spread through my body. The soup was ___9___ than any dish I had tried in restaurants. I told Dad ___10___ proud I felt to learn a family recipe from him.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
4. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. much  
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
6. A. In &emsp;&emsp; B. On &emsp;&emsp; C. At &emsp;&emsp; D. For  
7. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
8. A. improving &emsp;&emsp; B. improved &emsp;&emsp; C. to improve &emsp;&emsp; D. improves  
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. such &emsp;&emsp; D. very  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”（how to cook）  
**【解析】** teach me how to cook 表示教我做菜的方法；what to cook 偏重“做什么菜”，此处已有 pumpkin soup；why/when 不符合“教做菜”的核心信息。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（动作顺承）  
**【解析】** cut...and put... 为连续动作，用 and 连接；but/or/so 不符合顺承关系。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词（that 引导陈述句内容）  
**【解析】** tell me that I should... 中 that 引导陈述内容，可省略但在考题中常出现；if 表“是否”，how 表方式与从句内容重复，what 不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词（too...表“过于……”）  
**【解析】** too high 表示“火太大”，符合“汤可能发苦”的警告；very/so/much 与 high 搭配不如 too 表达“过度”准确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词（with 表示“用……工具”）  
**【解析】** stir...with a wooden spoon 表示用木勺搅拌；in/by/on 不符合工具搭配习惯。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词（for + 时间段）  
**【解析】** For about forty minutes 表示持续约四十分钟；in forty minutes 多表“四十分钟后”，on/at 不与时间段如此搭配。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词（until 引导时间状语从句：直到……为止）  
**【解析】** use a blender until the soup looked smooth 表示一直搅拌/打到顺滑为止；if/although/unless 不符合“过程持续到某结果出现”的逻辑。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）  
**【解析】** add salt and pepper to improve taste 表示“为了提味而加”；improving 表伴随，improved 为过去分词，improves 是谓语形式，均不适合此处目的状语。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than 提示比较）  
**【解析】** than any dish 提示用比较级 better；good/well 为原级，best 为最高级且结构不匹配。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 感叹结构在宾语从句中的连接词（how + 形容词）  
**【解析】** told Dad how proud I felt... 表示告诉他我“多么自豪”；what proud 结构错误；such 后接名词；very 不能引导从句。故选 B。
