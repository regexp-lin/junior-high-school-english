# 中考英语完形填空：和好朋友拍毕业照

## 完形填空原文

Today we took graduation photos at school. Everyone wore the school uniform and looked very ___1___. The photographer asked us to stand in rows on the playground.

My best friend Mei and I made sure to stand ___2___ each other. We ___3___ friends since the first day of middle school. Three years passed so quickly that I could ___4___ believe it.

The photographer told us ___5___ and say "cheese." Some boys made funny faces, ___6___ made everyone laugh. The teacher pretended to be angry but she was actually smiling ___7___.

After the group photo, Mei and I took some pictures just of ___8___. We posed under the big tree where we used to read ___9___. She held my hand and said, "No matter where we go, we will always be best friends."

I felt both happy ___10___ sad at that moment. Happy for the memories, and sad because we might go to different high schools.

## 选项

1. A. smart &emsp;&emsp; B. smartly &emsp;&emsp; C. smarter &emsp;&emsp; D. smartest
2. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. next to &emsp;&emsp; C. far from &emsp;&emsp; D. in front of
3. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. have been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be
4. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. harder &emsp;&emsp; D. hardest
5. A. smile &emsp;&emsp; B. to smile &emsp;&emsp; C. smiled &emsp;&emsp; D. smiling
6. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. what
7. A. her &emsp;&emsp; B. hers &emsp;&emsp; C. herself &emsp;&emsp; D. she
8. A. us &emsp;&emsp; B. we &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ourselves
9. A. together &emsp;&emsp; B. along &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. alone
10. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 系动词 + 形容词
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人穿着校服看起来很精神。looked 为系动词，后接形容词 smart。smartly 为副词。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词短语辨析（next to 旁边）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和好朋友 Mei 确保站在彼此旁边。next to 表示"在……旁边"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 现在完成时（since 提示）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们从初中第一天起就一直是朋友。since 提示用现在完成时 have been。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（hardly 几乎不）
**【解析】** 本句意为：三年过得太快了以至于我几乎不敢相信。hardly 表示"几乎不"。hard 表示"努力地"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：摄影师让我们微笑并说"茄子"。"tell sb. to do sth."为固定结构。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句（which 指代前面整件事）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些男生做鬼脸，这让所有人都笑了。which 引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个事情。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 反身代词（herself）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师假装生气但她自己其实在笑。herself 为反身代词，加强语气。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 反身代词（ourselves）
**【解析】** 本句意为：集体照之后，Mei 和我只拍了我们俩的合照。"of ourselves"表示"我们自己的"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（together 一起）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在常一起读书的大树下摆姿势。together 表示"一起"。alone 表示"独自"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（both...and... 既……又……）
**【解析】** 本句意为：那一刻我既开心又难过。"both...and..."表示"既……又……"。故选 C。
