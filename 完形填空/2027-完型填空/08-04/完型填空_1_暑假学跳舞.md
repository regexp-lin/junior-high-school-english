# 中考英语完形填空：暑假学跳舞

## 完形填空原文

This summer, I joined a dance studio ___1___ my best friend. The coach, ___2___ has won several prizes, asked us to warm up carefully every day. At first, my legs ached ___3___ I practiced basic steps for only ten minutes.

Two weeks later, I could follow the music ___4___ than before. "Don't compare yourself ___5___ others too much," the coach smiled. "Progress matters ___6___ ."

Before the small show at the end of July, we were told ___7___ our costumes early. When the lights came on, I felt nervous, ___8___ the audience clapped warmly. That moment taught me ___9___ courage feels like. Dancing is ___10___ hobby I want to keep.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. for
2. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. easily &emsp;&emsp; B. more easily &emsp;&emsp; C. most easily &emsp;&emsp; D. easier
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. about
6. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. many
7. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. preparing &emsp;&emsp; C. to prepare &emsp;&emsp; D. prepared
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. where
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：今年夏天我和最好的朋友一起加入了舞蹈工作室。with 表示“和……一起”。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：教练曾获得多项奖项，她要求我们每天认真热身。先行词 The coach 指人，从句缺主语，用 who。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我的腿很酸，因为我只练了十分钟的基本步。表原因用 because。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：两周后，我能比以前更容易地跟上音乐。修饰动词 follow，用副词比较级 more easily。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配 compare...with...
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要把自己和别人比较得太多。compare...with... 把……与……比较。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级 / 语境判断
**【解析】** 本句意为：进步最重要。matters most 表示“最重要”，用最高级 most。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：七月底小型演出前，我们被要求提前准备服装。be told to do sth. 被告知做某事。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：灯光亮起时我很紧张，但观众热烈鼓掌。表转折用 but。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：那一刻让我明白了勇气是什么样的。taught me what courage feels like，what 作 like 的宾语。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：跳舞是我想要坚持的一项爱好。泛指一项爱好，hobby 以辅音音素开头，用 a。故选 A。
