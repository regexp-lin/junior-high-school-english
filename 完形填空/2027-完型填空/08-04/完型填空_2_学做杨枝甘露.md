# 中考英语完形填空：学做杨枝甘露

## 完形填空原文

Mango pomelo sago is a popular dessert ___1___ many teenagers love. Yesterday, I followed an online video ___2___ learn it at home. The ingredients ___3___ prepared ahead of time: ripe mango, pomelo, sago, and milk.

While I ___4___ the mango, my dad helped squeeze fresh orange juice. "If the milk ___5___ too hot, the taste will change," he reminded me. I poured everything into a glass jar ___6___ shook it gently.

The final drink looked bright ___7___ smelled sweet. My sister said it was ___8___ delicious than the cup she bought last week. I wrote down the steps ___9___ I would not forget them. Cooking makes summer vacation ___10___ colorful.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
2. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. have been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be
4. A. cut &emsp;&emsp; B. was cutting &emsp;&emsp; C. cuts &emsp;&emsp; D. has cut
5. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. will be &emsp;&emsp; D. has been
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. though
8. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. very
9. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. though
10. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. feels &emsp;&emsp; C. felt &emsp;&emsp; D. feeling

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：杨枝甘露是许多青少年喜欢的甜品。先行词 dessert 指物，从句缺宾语，用 which/that，选项中为 which。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词 / 非谓语表目的
**【解析】** 本句意为：昨天我跟着网络视频在家学习制作。in order to 为了，表目的。so that 后接从句。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：食材提前被准备好……。ingredients 与 prepare 为被动，叙述昨天，用 were prepared。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 过去进行时
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我正在切芒果时，爸爸帮忙榨新鲜橙汁。while 引导的从句常用进行时，过去背景用 was cutting。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 时态（主将从现）
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果牛奶太热，味道就会变。if 引导的条件状语从句，主句 will change，从句用一般现在时 is。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把所有东西倒进玻璃罐并轻轻摇晃。poured 与 shook 并列，用 and。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：成品看起来明亮闻起来香甜。looked bright 与 smelled sweet 并列，用 and。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我妹妹说这比她上周买的那杯更美味。than 提示用 more delicious。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我写下步骤以免忘记。so that 以便/以免（此处取“以便不忘记”，也可用 in order that；选项 so that 表目的最贴切）。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 使役动词 make 的用法
**【解析】** 本句意为：烹饪让我的暑假感觉更有色彩。make + 宾语 + 动词原形作宾补，即 make summer vacation feel colorful。故选 A。
