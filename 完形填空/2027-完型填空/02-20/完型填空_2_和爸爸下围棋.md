# 中考英语完形填空：和爸爸下围棋

## 完形填空原文

My dad enjoys playing Go on weekends. I used to think the game was too slow, ___1___ one rainy afternoon he invited me to try.

He explained the basic rules with simple language. Black moves first, and players take turns ___2___ stones on the board. The goal is to control more ___3___ than the other side.

At first, I placed stones ___4___ thinking. Dad captured several of my groups easily. I felt embarrassed ___5___ wanted to stop.

"Go teaches patience," Dad said. "Look at the whole board, not only one corner." I took his advice and started ___6___ each move for a few seconds longer.

Little by little, I saw connections between stones. I even won one small corner in the second game. Dad praised me ___7___ learning quickly.

Now we play twice a month. The games are still quiet, ___8___ I enjoy the calm thinking time. I have learned that losing is not ___9___ if you understand why you lost.

Dad says Go is like life: you need balance, courage, ___10___ respect for your partner.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. putting &emsp;&emsp; C. to put &emsp;&emsp; D. puts
3. A. space &emsp;&emsp; B. spaces &emsp;&emsp; C. area &emsp;&emsp; D. areas
4. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. without &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. for
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. think &emsp;&emsp; B. thinking &emsp;&emsp; C. to think &emsp;&emsp; D. thought
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. something &emsp;&emsp; B. anything &emsp;&emsp; C. everything &emsp;&emsp; D. nothing
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前觉得这游戏太慢，但一个下雨的下午他邀请我试试。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（take turns doing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：黑棋先下，双方轮流在棋盘上落子。take turns doing sth. 轮流做某事。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：目标是比对方控制更多区域。area 区域、地盘，此处为不可数或抽象概念用 area 更自然；spaces 多指空隙。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我落子不经思考。without thinking 没有思考。with 带有；by 通过；for 为了。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很尴尬并且想停下。并列顺承，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（start doing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我听从建议，开始每一步多想几秒。start doing sth. 开始做某事。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸表扬我学得快。praise sb. for (doing) sth. 因……表扬某人。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：棋局仍然安静，但我享受这种冷静思考的时间。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你明白自己为什么输，失败并不是一切。Losing isn't everything 表示“输并不代表全部/并非世界末日”。something 某事；anything 任何事；nothing 什么也没有。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说围棋像人生：你需要平衡、勇气以及对对手的尊重。balance、courage 与 respect 并列，用 and 连接。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。
