# 中考英语完形填空：学校图书漂流活动

## 完形填空原文

Our school library started a book drift activity last month. Students could bring a book they had finished and ___1___ it on a special shelf. Then they could take another book ___2___.

I brought a science storybook I loved. On the cover, I wrote a short note ___3___ why I liked it. Two days later, the book was gone, ___4___ a mystery novel appeared in its place.

I read the new book during lunch breaks. The plot was exciting, ___5___ the words were not too hard. On the last page, I found a reader's drawing of a lighthouse. It felt like a secret conversation ___6___ strangers.

The librarian said the activity helped save money and reduce waste. More importantly, it built a reading community ___7___ different grades could share ideas.

Now I check the shelf every Wednesday. I hope my old book is traveling ___8___ classroom to classroom. Books are quiet friends, ___9___ they connect people in a gentle way.

I have decided to donate three more books ___10___ the winter holiday ends.

## 选项

1. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. leaving &emsp;&emsp; C. left &emsp;&emsp; D. to leave
2. A. in return &emsp;&emsp; B. in fact &emsp;&emsp; C. in trouble &emsp;&emsp; D. in person
3. A. explain &emsp;&emsp; B. explaining &emsp;&emsp; C. explained &emsp;&emsp; D. to explain
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. among &emsp;&emsp; B. between &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
7. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
8. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
9. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
10. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词形式（could + 动词原形）
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生可以带来已读完的书并把它放在专门的书架上。情态动词 could 后接动词原形 leave。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后他们可以再拿一本书作为交换。in return 作为回报、交换。in fact 事实上；in trouble 处于麻烦中；in person 亲自。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在封面上写了一张短便条，说明我为什么喜欢它。note 与 explain 为主动关系，用现在分词 explaining 作伴随/定语修饰 note。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两天后书不见了，但一本推理小说出现在它的位置。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：情节很刺激，但词汇不太难。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这像陌生人之间的一场秘密对话。between strangers 在陌生人之间。among 用于三者及以上；against 反对；beyond 超出。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：更重要的是，它建立了一个阅读社群，不同年级的学生可以在其中分享想法。先行词 community 表地点/抽象场所，从句缺状语，用 where。which 指物作主宾；who 指人；whose 表所属。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词短语（from... to...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望我的旧书正从一个教室漂到另一个教室。from... to... 从……到……。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：书是安静的朋友，但它们以温和的方式连接人们。转折用 but。because 因为；if 如果；unless 除非。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定在寒假结束前再捐三本书。before 在……之前。after 在……之后；while 当……时；since 自从。故选 B。
