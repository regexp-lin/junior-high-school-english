# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工蜡染布

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school club invited an artist ___1___ teach us traditional batik on cloth. The art room smelled faintly of wax and dye, which made the lesson feel like a real studio. I had never tried it before, ___2___ I felt a little nervous at first. The artist told us ___3___ hot wax could protect some parts of the cloth from the dye, so white patterns would stay. We had to wear gloves ___4___ we wouldn't burn our fingers. I drew a simple flower, ___5___ I poured wax along the lines very slowly. ___6___ mistake I made was that I moved too fast, and the wax cooled too soon. With the teacher's help, I learned ___7___ patiently and wait for the right moment. When the cloth was dipped into the blue dye, it looked dark, ___8___ after we washed away extra wax, the flower appeared clearly. I couldn't believe ___9___ beautiful the work was! I took it home ___10___ show my parents my first batik piece.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
6. A. A &emsp;&emsp; B. An &emsp;&emsp; C. The &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
7. A. work &emsp;&emsp; B. working &emsp;&emsp; C. to work &emsp;&emsp; D. worked  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. such &emsp;&emsp; D. very  
10. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（invite sb. to do sth.）  
**【解析】** invite 后接不定式作宾补，结构为 invite an artist to teach；for/of/with 均不构成 invite 的标准宾补搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（因果关系）  
**【解析】** 前句“我以前从没做过”与后句“一开始有点紧张”是因果：所以紧张；but 表转折，and 表并列递进，or 表选择，均不如 so 贴切。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词（方式/程度）  
**【解析】** 句意为“艺术家告诉我们蜡如何把布的一部分保护起来免受染料渗透”，强调方式/原理，用 how；what 需充当从句成分且语义不符；why 偏重原因，when 偏重时间。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 连词（目的状语从句：so that）  
**【解析】** “戴手套是为了不烫伤手指”，表目的用 so that；if 表条件，although 表让步，unless 表“除非”，均不符合目的关系。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（顺承关系）  
**【解析】** “我先画了一朵简单的花，然后沿着线条慢慢倒蜡”，动作顺承用 and；or/but/so 不符合并列动作逻辑。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 冠词（特指“我犯的那个错误”）  
**【解析】** 后文有定语从句 I made 修饰 mistake，表示特指，用 The mistake；a/an 表泛指“一个错误”，但此处明显在总结特定那一次问题。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（learn to do sth.）  
**【解析】** learn 后常接不定式 to do，表示“学会去做某事”，且与后文 wait 由 and 连接并列（to work... and (to) wait）；learn doing 不如 learn to do 常见；work/worked 不能直接与 learn 构成标准搭配。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折关系）  
**【解析】** “看起来很暗”与“洗掉多余蜡后花纹很清楚”形成转折，用 but；and 表递进，or 表选择，so 表结果，均不如转折准确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 感叹句引导词（how + 形容词）  
**【解析】** 结构为 how beautiful the work was；what 后常接名词短语；such 后接名词；very 不能引导从句。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的表达（in order to + 动词原形）  
**【解析】** 句意为“我把它带回家是为了给父母看我的第一件蜡染作品”，后接动词原形 show，用 in order to；so that 后接从句，because/unless 表原因/条件，不符合目的。故选 A。
