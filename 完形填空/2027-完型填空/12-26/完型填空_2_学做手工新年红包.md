# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工新年红包

## 完形填空原文

In our craft lesson, we learned ___1___ fold red paper into lucky envelopes. Ms. Zhou brought golden stickers ___2___ looked like small fish and taught us where to place them.

I measured the paper twice ___3___ I cut it, because mistakes would waste the nice material. The first envelope I made was a little crooked, ___4___ Ms. Zhou said practice would help. She also showed us ___5___ write the character for “luck” with a brush.

When the bell rang, we put our envelopes ___6___ a basket for a class display. I planned ___7___ one to my grandparents on New Year’s Eve. My friend Kate gave me ___8___ idea: add a short English blessing inside.

I thanked her ___9___ a smile and promised ___10___ more envelopes at home during the holiday.

## 选项

1. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. why to  
2. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. before &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
5. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to  
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
7. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. giving &emsp;&emsp; C. to give &emsp;&emsp; D. gave  
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
10. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构  
**【解析】** *learned how to fold red paper* “学习如何把红纸折成红包”。*how to do* 表方式。*what to* 需缺宾语；*when/why to* 不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词（指物）  
**【解析】** *golden stickers which looked like small fish* 先行词 *stickers* 为物，从句缺主语用 *which*。*who* 指人；*what* 不引导定语从句；*whose* 表所属。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*before*）  
**【解析】** *measured the paper twice before I cut it* “剪裁之前量了两次”。*if/because/unless* 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “第一个红包有点歪”与“老师说练习会有帮助”为转折，用 *but*。*and/so/or* 不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** *showed us how to write the character* “示范如何写‘福/运’字”。*how to write* 表方式。*what to write* 若选则强调“写什么”，不如 *how* 贴合“用笔写”；*when/where to* 不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *put... into...*  
**【解析】** *put our envelopes into a basket* “把红包放进篮子里”。*into* 表进入内部。*in/on/at* 不如 *into* 与 *put* 搭配自然。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*plan to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *planned to give one to my grandparents* “计划在除夕送给爷爷奶奶”。*plan to do* 固定。*give/giving/gave* 不能直接跟在 *planned* 后。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 *an*  
**【解析】** *an idea* 中 *idea* 以元音音素开头，用 *an*。*a* 用于辅音音素开头；此处非特指。*the* 特指不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *with* 表方式  
**【解析】** *thanked her with a smile* “用微笑感谢她”。*with* 表伴随方式。*in/on/for* 搭配不如 *with* 自然。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*promise to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *promised to make more envelopes* “答应假期在家再做更多红包”。*promise to do* 固定。*make/making/made* 不能直接跟在 *promised* 后。故选 C。
