# 中考英语完形填空：和家人去温泉

## 完形填空原文

During the winter break, my family drove ___1___ a hot spring town two hours away. The road was wet ___2___ it had rained the night before, ___3___ Dad drove slowly and safely.

When we arrived, we changed into swimsuits and walked ___4___ the outdoor pools. Steam rose ___5___ the water, and the air smelled clean. My younger brother was afraid ___6___ the hot pool at first, so Mom held his hand.

We stayed ___7___ about two hours and then enjoyed a simple meal at a small restaurant. Dad said the trip helped him relax ___8___ he had been busy for weeks. On the way back, I fell asleep ___9___ I listened to soft music.

I decided ___10___ a short diary entry about the warm water and the quiet mountains.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. toward &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
5. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
8. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. while  
10. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. writing &emsp;&emsp; C. to write &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 *drive to + 地点*  
**【解析】** *drove to a hot spring town* “开车去温泉小镇”。*drive to* 表去向。*for/of/with* 不表方向到达。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句（*because*）  
**【解析】** 路面湿是因为前夜下过雨，因果关系用 *because*。*but/although/unless* 不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *so* 表结果  
**【解析】** 路湿且前因导致“爸爸开得很慢很稳”，表结果用 *so*。*and* 仅并列；*but* 转折；*or* 选择。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *toward* 表方向  
**【解析】** *walked toward the outdoor pools* “朝室外池走去”。*toward* 表朝向。*in/on/at* 不如 *toward* 准确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 *rise from*  
**【解析】** *Steam rose from the water* “蒸汽从水面升起”。*from* 表来源。*on/in/by* 搭配不当。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *be afraid of*  
**【解析】** *was afraid of the hot pool* “害怕热水池”。*afraid of* 固定。*afraid to* 也可表“不敢去做”，但此处接名词短语 *the hot pool*，用 *of* 更自然（害怕某物）。*for/with* 不搭配。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *for + 时间段*  
**【解析】** *stayed for about two hours* “待了大约两小时”。*for* 引出持续时间。*in/on/at* 不用于此结构。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句（*because*）  
**【解析】** “旅行帮他放松”是因为“几周很忙”，因果关系用 *because*。*if/unless* 条件；*although* 让步。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*while*）  
**【解析】** *fell asleep while I listened to soft music* “听着轻音乐时睡着了”，*while* 接延续性动作。*when* 也可，但 *while* 更强调同时进行；*if/until* 不符。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*decide to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *decided to write a short diary entry* “决定写一篇短日记”。*decide to do* 固定。*write/writing/wrote* 不能直接跟在 *decided* 后。故选 C。
