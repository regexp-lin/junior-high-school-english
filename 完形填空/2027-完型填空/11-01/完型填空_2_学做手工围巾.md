# 中考英语完形填空：学织一条手工围巾

## 完形填空原文

Winter was coming, so I decided ___1___ a warm scarf for my grandma. I had never used knitting needles before, ___2___ my cousin offered to teach me at her home last Sunday.

First, she asked me ___3___ the soft yarn carefully and make a simple knot. It took me about twenty minutes ___4___ the first row right. My fingers were slower than ___5___, but I didn’t give up.

After two hours of practice, I could finish three rows ___6___ mistakes than before. My cousin said, “You are doing ___7___ job of all the beginners I have met!” That praise made me work even ___8___.

Before leaving, I promised ___9___ I would keep practicing every weekend until the scarf ___10___. I hope Grandma will like this gift made with love.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
3. A. hold &emsp;&emsp; B. holding &emsp;&emsp; C. to hold &emsp;&emsp; D. held  
4. A. get &emsp;&emsp; B. getting &emsp;&emsp; C. got &emsp;&emsp; D. to get  
5. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself  
6. A. few &emsp;&emsp; B. fewer &emsp;&emsp; C. little &emsp;&emsp; D. less  
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. the best  
8. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. hardest &emsp;&emsp; D. the hardest  
9. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  
10. A. finishes &emsp;&emsp; B. finished &emsp;&emsp; C. is finished &emsp;&emsp; D. was finished  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide 后接不定式作宾语，decide to do sth. 表示“决定做某事”。make/making/made 均不能直接作 decide 的宾语。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 转折连词  
**【解析】** “我从未用过棒针”与“表姐主动教我”为转折，用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表结果。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do）  
**【解析】** ask sb. to do sth. 为固定结构，意为“要求某人做某事”，用不定式 to hold。hold/holding/held 不符合 ask 的搭配。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 固定句型 It takes sb. some time to do sth.  
**【解析】** 该句型中真正主语为不定式，用 to get the first row right。get/getting/got 不能作该句型中的真正主语。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 名词性物主代词  
**【解析】** than 后比较对象为“她的手指（速度）”，用名词性物主代词 hers，相当于 her fingers。she/her/herself 不能单独表示“她的（手指）”。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级与可数名词复数  
**【解析】** mistakes 为可数名词复数，且有 than，用 fewer mistakes 表示“更少的错误”。few 无比较含义；little/less 修饰不可数名词。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级（含定冠词）  
**【解析】** 句中有 of all the beginners 表范围，且 job 为可数名词单数，用 the best job。good/better 无最高级含义；best 作最高级修饰可数名词单数时常加 the。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级（even 修饰）  
**【解析】** even 常修饰比较级，表示“甚至更……”，work harder 更努力。hard 原级；hardest/the hardest 为最高级，此处无三者以上比较语境。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 that  
**【解析】** promised 后为陈述意义的宾语从句，成分完整，用 that 引导（口语中可省略，选项中需选 that）。what 在从句中须充当成分；if/whether 表是否，与句意不符。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句中的一般现在时表将来 + 被动语态  
**【解析】** until 引导的时间状语从句中，主句为 would keep（过去将来），从句常用一般现在时表将来；围巾“被织完”用被动 is finished。finishes 为主动；finished/was finished 时态与主句不协调。故选 C。
