# 中考英语完形填空：学做奶茶

## 完形填空原文

In our life skills class, Ms. Gao taught us ___1___ brew milk tea safely. She heated water in a pot and explained ___2___ we should never leave the stove alone.

She put black tea leaves into a cloth bag ___3___ the soup would stay clear. After the tea turned dark brown, she poured hot milk ___4___ and stirred slowly. The sweet smell filled the classroom, ___5___ everyone leaned forward to watch.

We tasted a little from paper cups. The drink was ___6___ sweet than store-bought milk tea, but I liked the light taste. Ms. Gao reminded us ___7___ wash our hands before touching food next time.

My partner spilled a few drops, ___8___ he quickly wiped the desk. At the end of the lesson, Ms. Gao said cooking was about more ___9___ following steps — it was ___10___ about staying safe with heat and knives.

## 选项

1. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. why to  
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
3. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. as long as &emsp;&emsp; C. even if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
5. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
6. A. little &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. least &emsp;&emsp; D. fewer  
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
9. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
10. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. also &emsp;&emsp; C. either &emsp;&emsp; D. as well  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** *taught us how to brew milk tea* 表示“教我们如何冲泡奶茶”。*what to brew* 虽语法可行，但后文已点明 *milk tea*，此处强调方法与步骤，用 *how to* 更贴切。*when to/why to* 与语境重点不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句 *that* 引导陈述句  
**【解析】** *explained that we should never leave the stove alone*，陈述安全注意事项，用 *that*。*what* 需在从句中充当成分；*whether* 表是否；*how* 表方式，此处为明确的告诫内容，用 *that* 最自然。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 *so that*  
**【解析】** 用布袋装茶叶是为了“让茶汤清澈”，*so that the soup would stay clear*。*as long as* 只要；*even if* 即使；*unless* 除非。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *pour...into...*  
**【解析】** *poured hot milk into (the pot/the tea)* 表示把热牛奶倒入（容器/茶中）。*in/on/at* 不与 *pour* 构成此固定搭配。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *so* 表结果  
**【解析】** 香味弥漫，所以大家探身观看，因果关系用 *so*。*but* 表转折；*or* 表选择；*for* 表原因时语气与位置不如 *so* 直接。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（*than* 提示）  
**【解析】** *less sweet than store-bought milk tea*，*than* 提示比较级，修饰形容词 *sweet* 用 *less*。*fewer* 修饰可数名词；*little/least* 不与 *than* 搭配。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*remind sb. to do*）  
**【解析】** *reminded us to wash our hands*，不定式作宾补。*for/of/with* 不构成 *remind sb. to do*。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *and* 表顺承  
**【解析】** “洒了几滴”与“马上擦干净桌面”为连续动作，用 *and* 连接并列分句。*but* 表转折，语境无对立；*so* 表结果，并非因果关系；*because* 表原因，逻辑颠倒。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 比较结构 *more than*  
**【解析】** *about more than following steps* 表示“不仅仅是按步骤操作”，*more than* 为固定搭配。*more as/more like/more to* 不构成此义。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词 *also* 表“也、还”  
**【解析】** *it was also about staying safe* 表示“还涉及注意安全”。*too* 多放句末；*either* 用于否定句；*as well* 常放句末，此处破折号后句中位置更适合 *also*。故选 B。
