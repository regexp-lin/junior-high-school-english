# 中考英语完形填空：帮社区打扫积雪

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, our volunteer team arrived ___1___ the neighborhood gate at eight o’clock. Heavy snow had covered the paths, ___2___ walking dangerous for elderly neighbors.

Mr. Zhao, the community leader, divided us ___3___ three groups. Some swept the snow to the sides, ___4___ others spread salt on the icy steps. I worked with my cousin; ___5___ was strong enough to push the big shovel, so we took turns.

A grandma brought hot water ___6___ us to warm our hands. She thanked us again and again, ___7___ made us feel shy but happy. By noon, the main road ___8___ cleared, and people could pass safely.

Tired ___9___ we were, nobody complained. I learned ___10___ small actions could make winter mornings warmer for everyone.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
2. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. to make  
3. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. neither &emsp;&emsp; B. either &emsp;&emsp; C. none &emsp;&emsp; D. both  
6. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. about  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
8. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. had been  
9. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. such  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *at* 表小地点/门口  
**【解析】** *arrive at the neighborhood gate*，门口为小地点，用 *at*。*arrive in* 多用于大地点；*on/to* 不与 *arrive* 搭配此义。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作结果状语  
**【解析】** *Heavy snow had covered the paths, making walking dangerous*，逗号后 *making* 表自然而然的结果。*make/made* 无谓语连接会成 run-on；*to make* 多表目的，不如结果状语贴切。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *divide...into...*  
**【解析】** *divided us into three groups* 表示“把我们分成三组”。*divide...to/for/with* 非标准搭配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *while* 表对比  
**【解析】** “一些人扫雪到路边，而另一些人撒盐”，并列对比用 *while*。*if/until/unless* 逻辑不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 *neither*（两者都不）  
**【解析】** 主语为 *I* 与 *my cousin* 两人，后文 *took turns* 表示轮流用力，说明“两人单独都推不动大铲子”更合理，用 *Neither* 表示“两者都不”。*Either* 表“任一”，与轮流推铲的逻辑不符；*none* 用于三者及以上；*both* 与“需要轮流”矛盾。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *for* 表对象/目的  
**【解析】** *brought hot water for us* 表示“为我们带来热水”。*with/of/about* 不如 *for* 贴切表达“给我们用”。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句 *which*  
**【解析】** 指代前面整件事“她一再感谢”，用 *which made us feel shy*。*what* 不引导非限制性定语从句；*who/whom* 指人，先行内容为事。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时的被动语态  
**【解析】** *By noon, the main road had been cleared*，*by noon* 表截至过去某时已完成，且路被清理用被动，过去完成时 *had been cleared* 恰当。*was cleared* 也可叙述，但 *by* 常与完成时连用更严谨；*is/has been* 时态与全文过去叙事不一致。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 让步倒装 *Tired as we were*  
**【解析】** *Tired as we were* 为倒装让步结构，相当于 *Although we were tired*。*Tired like we were* 不成立；*so/such* 不用于该倒装。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 *how*  
**【解析】** *learned how small actions could make...* 表示“了解到小小的行动如何能……”，*how* 表方式/程度。*what* 需充当成分；*why/when* 与“让冬天早晨更温暖”的方式概括不如 *how*。故选 B。
