# 中考英语完形填空：第一次做课堂笔记展示

## 完形填空原文

In English class, our teacher asked each group ___1___ a two-minute presentation about note-taking skills. My hands turned cold ___2___ I had never spoken in front of the whole class like that.

My partner Anna encouraged me ___3___ a calm voice. We chose color codes ___4___ important grammar rules and common mistakes. We also prepared a small poster ___5___ clear examples.

When our turn came, I held the poster ___6___ Anna explained the first part. My legs shook a little, ___7___ I looked at friendly faces in the audience and took a deep breath.

We finished on time. Classmates clapped politely ___8___ asked a few questions. The teacher said our ideas were practical ___9___ easy to follow.

After class, I wrote in my diary, "Courage grows ___10___ small steps." That sentence became my motto for the new month.

## 选项

1. A. prepare &emsp;&emsp; B. to prepare &emsp;&emsp; C. preparing &emsp;&emsp; D. prepared
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. if
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. mark &emsp;&emsp; B. marking &emsp;&emsp; C. to mark &emsp;&emsp; D. marked
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
6. A. while &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. through

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：英语老师请每个小组准备两分钟关于记笔记技巧的展示。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式 to prepare。prepare 原形；preparing 动名词；prepared 过去式。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手发凉，因为我从未在全班面前那样发言过。because 因为。unless 除非；although 尽管；if 如果。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：搭档安娜用平静的声音鼓励我。with a calm voice 用平静的声音，表方式。in 在……里；on 在……上；at 在。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们选择用颜色标记重要的语法规则和常见错误。此处用不定式表目的 to mark。mark 原形；marking 动名词；marked 过去分词。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们还准备了一张带有清晰例句的小海报。with clear examples 带有清晰例子。in 在……里；on 在……上；by 通过。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮到我们时，我举着海报，同时安娜讲解第一部分。while 当……时，可表两个动作同时进行。until 直到；unless 除非；if 如果。故选 A。

Actually blank 6: "I held the poster ___6___ Anna explained" - **while** Anna explained - I held the poster while Anna explained - good.

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的腿有点发抖，但我看着观众友善的面孔并深呼吸。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们礼貌鼓掌，还问了几个问题。clapped 与 asked 为顺承并列，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说我们的想法很实用且容易跟上。practical 与 easy 为并列褒义，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：勇气在一步步小尝试中增长。through small steps 经由小步骤、贯穿积累的过程。from 从；by 常接方式或动名词；with 和。故选 D。
