# 中考英语完形填空：走亲戚

## 完形填空原文

The second day of the new year, we drove ___1___ the countryside to see my aunt. Narrow roads twisted ___2___ fields covered with thin snow. My cousin ran out ___3___ soon as he heard our car horn.

Inside the warm house, a long table was already ___4___ with homemade dishes. Aunt asked about my grades ___5___ a kind voice, and I answered honestly. She did not compare me ___6___ other children; instead she praised my effort.

After lunch, we walked ___7___ the village and fed chickens near a small river. My cousin taught me ___8___ skip stones on the water. The afternoon passed ___9___ than I expected.

When we said goodbye, Aunt packed eggs and vegetables ___10___ us. I waved until the house disappeared behind the hill, already planning our next visit.

## 选项

1. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. through &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. across
2. A. among &emsp;&emsp; B. between &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. against
3. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too
4. A. filled &emsp;&emsp; B. fill &emsp;&emsp; C. filling &emsp;&emsp; D. fills
5. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. against
7. A. over &emsp;&emsp; B. across &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. along
8. A. how can &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. what to &emsp;&emsp; D. why to
9. A. fast &emsp;&emsp; B. faster &emsp;&emsp; C. fastest &emsp;&emsp; D. the fastest
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们开车进入乡下看望姑妈。drive into 驶入/开往。B 项 through 穿过；C 项 over 越过；D 项 across 横穿（平面）。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：窄路蜿蜒穿过田野。through fields 穿过田野之间。A 项 among 在……中；B 项 between 两者之间；D 项 against 靠着。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一听到车喇叭就跑出来。as soon as 一……就……。B 项 so soon as 非标准；C、D 不搭配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词被动语态辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：长桌已摆满家常菜。be filled with 被装满。B 项原形；C 项现在分词；D 项第三人称单数。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：姑妈用温和的声音问成绩。in a ... voice 用……的声音。A 项 by；C 项 with；D 项 on。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她没有把我与其他孩子比较。compare...with... 把……与……比较。A 项 compare to 多表“比作”；B、D 不搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们沿着村子散步。walk along 沿着……走。A 项 over；B 项 across 横穿；C 项 through 穿过内部。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词+不定式辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表哥教我如何打水漂。teach sb. how to do。A 项 how can 结构错误；C 项 what to skip 不通；D 项 why to。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下午过得比预想更快。than 提示比较级 faster。A 项原级；C、D 最高级。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：姑妈给我们装了鸡蛋和蔬菜。pack sth. for sb. 为某人打包。A 项 to；C 项 with；D 项 of。故选 B。
