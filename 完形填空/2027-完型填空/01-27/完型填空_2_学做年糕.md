# 中考英语完形填空：学做年糕

## 完形填空原文

Grandma believes eating niangao brings a ___1___ year because its name sounds like "higher each year." Yesterday she let me help ___2___ a small batch in our kitchen.

First we steamed glutinous rice ___3___ it became soft enough to pound. The wooden hammer felt heavy, ___4___ Grandma guided my hands so each strike landed in the center. Sweet steam rose, ___5___ the windows turned foggy.

We shaped the warm dough ___6___ long bars and pressed dates on top for decoration. Waiting for it ___7___ cool was the hardest part—I wanted to taste everything at once. Finally Grandma cut a slice ___8___ me and said patience is part of cooking.

The niangao was chewy and mildly sweet. I packed two pieces ___9___ my best friend the next day. She smiled and said homemade food always tastes ___10___ than store snacks.

## 选项

1. A. high &emsp;&emsp; B. higher &emsp;&emsp; C. highly &emsp;&emsp; D. height
2. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made
3. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. although
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
8. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：吃年糕寓意“年年更高”。a higher year 与“更高一年”的祝愿呼应（也可用 good，但选项 higher 与 higher each year 双关更贴切）。A 项 high 原级；C 项 highly 副词；D 项 height 名词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她让我帮忙做一小批。help (to) do / let sb. help to make，选项 to make 作 help 的宾语。A 项 make 需 help make；B 项 making；D 项 made。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：先把糯米蒸到足够软。until 直到……为止。A 项 when；C 项 if；D 项 although。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：木锤很重，但奶奶扶着我的手。转折用 but。A 项 and；C 项 or；D 项 so。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：甜蒸汽升起，窗户也变得模糊。并列顺承用 and。A 项 if；B 项 until；D 项 unless。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把温热的面团做成长条。shape...into... 把……塑成……。A 项 in；B 项 on；D 项 at。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定结构）
**【解析】** 本句意为：等它变凉最难。wait for it to cool，此处选项 to 与 cool 构成不定式（句子为 Waiting for it to cool）。B 项 for；C 项 with；D 项 by。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶切了一片给我。cut sth. for sb. 为某人切。A 项 cut to 不符；C 项 of；D 项 with。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二天给朋友带了两块。pack sth. for sb. 为某人打包带去。A 项 to；C 项 with；D 项 by。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：自家做的总比零食店的好吃。than 提示比较级 better。A 项 good；B 项 well 副词；D 项 best。故选 C。
