# 中考英语完形填空：厨房里的煎蛋课

## 完形填空原文

My uncle is a cook. Last Friday, he taught me ___1___ a perfect fried egg for breakfast.

He heated a non-stick pan and added a little oil. "Wait ___2___ the oil is warm, not smoking," he said. I nodded and watched the small bubbles ___3___.

When he broke the egg, the white spread ___4___ a round circle. He covered the pan for thirty seconds ___5___ the top could cook gently.

My first try broke the yolk. I sighed, ___6___ my uncle laughed and let me try again. The second egg looked ___7___ and tasted fine.

Before eating, I sprinkled a pinch of salt ___8___ it. My uncle reminded me ___9___ use too much salt for health reasons.

I used to think cooking skills were ___10___ to learn. That morning I realized patience matters most.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. made
2. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. appear &emsp;&emsp; B. appears &emsp;&emsp; C. appearing &emsp;&emsp; D. appeared
4. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
5. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
9. A. not to &emsp;&emsp; B. don't &emsp;&emsp; C. not &emsp;&emsp; D. didn't
10. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. easily &emsp;&emsp; D. easiest

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（teach sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周五他教我如何做一份完美的煎蛋早餐。teach sb. to do sth. 用不定式 to make。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（until）
**【解析】** 本句意为：等到油温热，但不要冒烟。wait until 等到……为止。when 当……时也可，但 until 更强调“达到合适状态”。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（watch sb./sth. doing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我点头并看着小气泡出现。watch 后接 doing 表观看过程，bubbles 与 appear 为主动进行，用 appearing。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：蛋清摊开像一个圆。like 像……一样。as 作为；for 为了；of ……的。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（目的状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他盖上锅盖三十秒，以便上面能温和地熟透。so that 以便。although 尽管；unless 除非；until 直到。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我叹气，但叔叔笑着让我再试。转折用 but。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二个蛋看起来更好，味道也不错。与第一次比较，用 better。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：吃之前我在蛋上撒了一点盐。on 表示在表面上。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. not to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：叔叔提醒我不要为了健康放太多盐。remind sb. not to do sth. 用 not to use。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词作表语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我过去以为烹饪技巧很容易学。were 后接形容词作表语，用 easy；easily 为副词；easier/easiest 需比较语境。故选 A。
