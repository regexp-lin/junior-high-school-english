# 中考英语完形填空：第一次在班会演讲

## 完形填空原文

Our class held a short speech activity on honesty. When my name was called, my legs felt ___1___ they were made of wood.

I walked to the podium and took a deep breath. I started ___2___ a simple story about returning a lost wallet. At first, my voice shook, ___3___ the sentences became smoother.

I tried to look at the audience, not only ___4___ floor. A friendly smile from our monitor gave me courage. I finished ___5___ time and bowed.

There was silence for a second, ___6___ then applause filled the room. Our teacher said I spoke clearly, though I could practice eye contact ___7___.

That night, I recorded myself ___8___ English for three minutes. I knew confidence would grow ___9___ practice. Next time, I hoped to feel ___10___ nervous than this time.

## 选项

1. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
5. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most
8. A. read &emsp;&emsp; B. reading &emsp;&emsp; C. to read &emsp;&emsp; D. reads
9. A. through &emsp;&emsp; B. past &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. across
10. A. little &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. least &emsp;&emsp; D. fewer

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词/介词辨析（feel like）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的腿好像木头做的一样。feel like 感觉像……。if 如果；that 引导从句需完整结构；so 因此。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（start with）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从一个关于归还失物的简单故事开始。start with 以……开始。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我的声音发抖，但句子渐渐顺了。转折用 but。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我试着看向观众，而不只是盯着地板。特指教室地面用 the floor。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（on time）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我按时讲完并鞠躬。on time 准时。in time 及时。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（but then）
**【解析】** 本句意为：安静了一秒，但随后掌声充满教室。but then 表转折后的发展。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说我讲得很清楚，不过眼神交流还可以再多练。暗含与现在比较，用 more。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（record sb. doing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：那晚我录了三分钟自己读英语。record oneself doing sth. 录下自己做某事的过程，用 reading。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道自信会通过练习增长。through practice 通过练习。by 虽可表方式，但 grow through 更强调“经由过程”。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次我希望比这次不那么紧张。nervous 的比较级用 less nervous。故选 B。
