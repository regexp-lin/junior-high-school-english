# 中考英语完形填空：学校科技节

## 完形填空原文

Our school held its annual Science and Technology Festival last week. Students from every grade were encouraged ___1___ part in different activities, such as robot competitions, science experiments, and invention displays.

My team decided to build a small solar-powered car. We spent two weeks ___2___ materials and testing designs. The most challenging part was making the solar panel work ___3___ to power the motor.

On the day of the festival, the hallway was filled ___4___ colorful posters and models. I noticed that some projects were much more creative ___5___ ours. A group of eighth graders even made a device that could purify water ___6___ sunlight.

When it was our turn to present, I explained ___7___ our car worked while my teammate demonstrated it. The car moved slowly at first, ___8___ as it caught more sunlight, it sped up. The judges looked impressed and asked us several questions.

In the end, we won ___9___ prize for "Best Teamwork." Although it was not the top award, we were still very proud. The festival taught us that science is not just about books — it's about ___10___ ideas into reality.

## 选项

1. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. to take &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. took
2. A. collect &emsp;&emsp; B. collected &emsp;&emsp; C. to collect &emsp;&emsp; D. collecting
3. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
4. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. to
6. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. used &emsp;&emsp; C. using &emsp;&emsp; D. to use
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. which
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
10. A. turn &emsp;&emsp; B. turned &emsp;&emsp; C. turning &emsp;&emsp; D. turns

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个年级的学生都被鼓励参加不同的活动。be encouraged to do sth. 表示"被鼓励做某事"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们花了两周时间收集材料和测试设计。spend time doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最有挑战性的部分是让太阳能板运转良好以驱动电机。修饰动词 work，需用副词 well。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：走廊里挂满了彩色海报和模型。be filled with 表示"充满"。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我注意到有些项目比我们的有创意得多。more creative 为比较级，后接 than。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（现在分词表方式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一组八年级学生甚至制造了一个用阳光净化水的装置。using 作方式状语，表示"通过使用"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句（连接词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我解释了我们的车是如何运作的。how 引导宾语从句，表示"如何、怎样"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小车一开始移动缓慢，但当它接收到更多阳光时就加速了。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后我们获得了一个"最佳团队合作"奖。a prize 表示"一个奖项"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：科学不仅仅是关于书本——它是关于把想法变成现实。about 为介词，后接动名词 turning。故选 C。
