# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工贺卡

## 完形填空原文

Teacher's Day was on September 10th. The evening before, I sat at my desk and started ___1___ a greeting card for my English teacher, Ms. Li.

I picked a piece of light blue cardboard because blue was her ___2___ color. I folded it in half and began to draw a big sunflower on the front. Sunflowers always face the sun, just ___3___ Ms. Li always faces us with warmth and patience.

Inside the card, I wrote a short message ___4___ my neatest handwriting: "Dear Ms. Li, thank you for teaching us not only English ___5___ also how to be good people." I thought for a moment and added, "You are the best teacher I ___6___ ever had."

To make the card look ___7___, I glued a small bow made of yellow ribbon at the corner. I also sprinkled some glitter on the flower petals. The card took me about forty minutes ___8___, but I thought it was worth the effort.

The next morning, I arrived at school early and placed the card ___9___ Ms. Li's desk. When she found it, she read it quietly and looked up with tears in her eyes. "This means more to me ___10___ you can imagine," she said softly. That moment made all my effort worthwhile.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. to make &emsp;&emsp; D. making
2. A. favor &emsp;&emsp; B. favoring &emsp;&emsp; C. favorite &emsp;&emsp; D. favored
3. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. for
4. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. had &emsp;&emsp; B. have &emsp;&emsp; C. has &emsp;&emsp; D. having
7. A. pretty &emsp;&emsp; B. prettier &emsp;&emsp; C. prettiest &emsp;&emsp; D. prettily
8. A. finish &emsp;&emsp; B. finishing &emsp;&emsp; C. finished &emsp;&emsp; D. to finish
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. under
10. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. then

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在书桌前开始制作一张贺卡。start doing sth. 表示"开始做某事"。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 词性辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为蓝色是她最喜欢的颜色。favorite 为形容词，表示"最喜欢的"，修饰 color。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：向日葵总是面向太阳，正如李老师总是用温暖和耐心面对我们。just as 表示"正如"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用最工整的字迹写了一段简短的话。in one's handwriting 表示"用某人的字迹/笔迹"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：感谢您不仅教我们英语，还教我们如何做好人。not only ... but also ... 表示"不仅……而且……"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态（现在完成时）
**【解析】** 本句意为：您是我拥有过的最好的老师。ever 与现在完成时搭配；主语为 I，用 have。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：为了让贺卡看起来更漂亮。make + 宾语 + adj.；此处暗含"比之前更漂亮"的比较，用比较级 prettier。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 不定式（it takes ... to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这张贺卡花了我大约四十分钟完成。sth. takes sb. + 时间 + to do。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把贺卡放在李老师的桌上。on one's desk 表示"在某人桌上"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 比较结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：这对我的意义比你能想象的更大。more ... than ... 为比较结构。故选 C。
