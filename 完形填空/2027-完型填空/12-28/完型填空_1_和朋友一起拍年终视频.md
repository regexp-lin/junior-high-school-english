# 中考英语完形填空：和朋友一起拍年终视频

## 完形填空原文

On Saturday, my friends and I planned ___1___ a short year-end video to remember 2027. Lily brought ___2___ camera, and Tom wrote a funny script. We met at the school gate at two in the afternoon ___3___ the weather was cold but sunny. While we were filming, a classmate passed by and ___4___ us loudly. We all laughed, and that part was ___5___ kept in the final video. After editing for two hours, we posted it online. To our surprise, many people watched it and left kind comments. One stranger wrote, “You look ___6___ you really enjoy teamwork.” That sentence touched me deeply. I replied ___7___ a simple “Thank you.” Later, we decided ___8___ the video to our teachers as a New Year gift. Our English teacher said she was proud ___9___ we had learned to express ourselves in English, too. Looking back, I feel lucky ___10___ such supportive friends around me.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself  
3. A. although &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
4. A. cheer &emsp;&emsp; B. cheered &emsp;&emsp; C. cheers &emsp;&emsp; D. cheering  
5. A. also &emsp;&emsp; B. either &emsp;&emsp; C. too &emsp;&emsp; D. even  
6. A. as if &emsp;&emsp; B. even if &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as  
7. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
8. A. show &emsp;&emsp; B. showing &emsp;&emsp; C. to show &emsp;&emsp; D. showed  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. whether &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
10. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. had &emsp;&emsp; C. to have &emsp;&emsp; D. having  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** plan to do sth.（计划做某事）  
**【解析】** plan 后接不定式作宾语，即 plan to make。make/making/made 不符合 plan 的搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词修饰名词 camera  
**【解析】** Lily brought her camera 表示“她的相机”。she 为主格，hers 为名词性物主代词，herself 为反身代词，均不直接修饰 camera。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 让步状语从句 although（虽然）  
**【解析】** “虽然天冷但很晴朗”，表让步用 although。because 表原因与句意矛盾；unless/if 表条件，不适用。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时与 passed 并列  
**【解析】** While 从句用过去进行时，主句叙述相继动作用一般过去时 cheered，与 passed 并列。cheer/cheers/cheering 时态或结构不对。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词 also 用于句中（也）  
**【解析】** “那一段也被保留在成片里”，肯定句句中用 also。either 用于否定句末；too 多用于句末；even 表“甚至”，语气不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词 as if（好像）  
**【解析】** look as if you enjoy… 表示“看起来好像你们很享受……”。even if 即使；so that 以便；as long as 只要，均不符合语境。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表伴随 reply with…（用……回复）  
**【解析】** reply with a simple “Thank you” 表示“用一句简单的谢谢回复”。for/in/on 与 reply 搭配不如 with 自然。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** decide to do sth.  
**【解析】** decide 后接不定式 to show。show/showing/showed 不符合 decide 用法。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 that（be proud that…）  
**【解析】** be proud that 后接陈述内容，用 that。what 需在从句中作成分；whether 是否；how 如何，此处只需连接陈述句。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词后接不定式 feel lucky to do  
**【解析】** feel lucky to have… 为“感到幸运拥有/有……”。have/had/having 不能直接跟在 lucky 后作补足语，需用 to have。故选 C。
