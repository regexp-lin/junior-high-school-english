# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工雪花挂件

## 完形填空原文

Yesterday afternoon, I joined a DIY workshop ___1___ learn how to make snowflake pendants out of paper and string. The instructor was patient ___2___ every student, especially beginners like me. She told us ___3___ we should fold the paper evenly first. If we rushed, the lines ___4___ look messy. I tried three times ___5___ I finally got a neat snowflake. Then I tied a silver string on it and hung it near the window. When sunlight came in, the snowflake seemed ___6___ tiny stars were dancing on it. My little cousin asked ___7___ she could take one home, and I happily said yes. Before leaving, the instructor reminded us ___8___ sharp scissors carefully. I thanked her and promised I ___9___ follow the safety rules next time, too. On the bus, I thought about ___10___ relaxing the activity was. I hope I can teach my classmates how to make these pendants during the winter break.

## 选项

1. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. of  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
4. A. will &emsp;&emsp; B. would &emsp;&emsp; C. can &emsp;&emsp; D. should  
5. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. before  
6. A. as if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
7. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  
8. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. using &emsp;&emsp; C. to use &emsp;&emsp; D. used  
9. A. will &emsp;&emsp; B. would &emsp;&emsp; C. can &emsp;&emsp; D. must  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. where  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定式表目的 in order to do  
**【解析】** “参加手工坊是为了学习……”，表目的且后接动词原形，用 in order to learn。so that 后接从句；because/although 表原因/让步，不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be patient with sb.  
**【解析】** 对某人有耐心用 with。be patient to 后多接动词；for/of 不构成此固定搭配。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** tell sb. that 引导宾语从句（陈述句）  
**【解析】** tell us that we should… 陈述建议内容。what 需在从句中作成分；whether 是否；how 如何，此处从句已有 should fold，用 that 即可。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 虚拟语气/过去将来：If 过去式，主句 would + 动词原形  
**【解析】** If we rushed（过去式）表示与现在事实相反的假设，主句常用 would look。will 表真实将来；can/should 语气与假设结构不匹配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 连词 before（在……之前；才）  
**【解析】** “试了三次才得到整齐的雪花”，用 before 表“才”。when/while 表当……时；until 多与 not 连用表“直到才”，此处 tried three times before I finally… 更自然。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词 as if（好像；仿佛）  
**【解析】** seem as if… 表示“看起来仿佛……”，符合“阳光照进来，雪花挂件上好像有小星星在跳舞”的比喻。because/although 引导原因或让步从句，与 seem 搭配不当；when 表时间，不如 as if 贴切。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句 if 表“是否”  
**【解析】** ask if she could… 表示“问她是否可以……”。that 无“是否”义；what/whether 在此语境中不如 if 自然（whether 更正式，if 常用）。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** remind sb. to do sth.  
**【解析】** remind us to use sharp scissors… 提醒我们要小心使用剪刀。不定式作宾补。use/using/used 结构不符合 remind 的常见用法。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去将来时（主句过去时，从句表“将会”）  
**【解析】** promised 为过去时，宾语从句表“将会遵守”用 would follow。will 与主句时态不一致；can/must 语气与“承诺下次”不完全匹配。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句中 how 修饰形容词（表程度）  
**【解析】** think about how relaxing the activity was 中 how 修饰形容词 relaxing，表示“多么令人放松”。what 后接名词短语；which/where 不符。故选 B。
