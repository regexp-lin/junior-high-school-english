# 中考英语完形填空：和同学去游乐场

## 完形填空原文

To celebrate the end of the National Day holiday, my classmates and I planned a trip to the amusement park. There were five of us, and we ___1___ forward to this outing for a whole week.

When we got there, the park was ___2___ with people. Colorful lights and cheerful music were everywhere. We first went to the roller coaster because it was one of the most ___3___ rides in the park.

Standing in the long line, I felt a little nervous. My friend Zhang Wei noticed and said, "Don't worry. I'll sit ___4___ you." His words made me feel safer. When the ride started, I screamed so ___5___ that my voice became hoarse afterward.

After the roller coaster, we tried the bumper cars. Everyone drove ___6___ and bumped into each other on purpose. We laughed so hard that our stomachs hurt. It was the ___7___ fun I had had in months.

At noon, we shared a big pizza together. While ___8___, we talked about our favorite moments. Li Mei said the Ferris wheel was her favorite ___9___ she could see the whole city from the top.

Before we left, we took a group photo at the park entrance. I believe this trip will be one of the happiest memories ___10___ our junior high school days.

## 选项

1. A. look &emsp;&emsp; B. looked &emsp;&emsp; C. had looked &emsp;&emsp; D. have looked  
2. A. crowd &emsp;&emsp; B. crowded &emsp;&emsp; C. crowding &emsp;&emsp; D. crowds  
3. A. popular &emsp;&emsp; B. more popular &emsp;&emsp; C. most popular &emsp;&emsp; D. popularity  
4. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. between &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. below  
5. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. louder &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. loudest  
6. A. crazy &emsp;&emsp; B. crazily &emsp;&emsp; C. crazier &emsp;&emsp; D. craziest  
7. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. most  
8. A. eat &emsp;&emsp; B. ate &emsp;&emsp; C. eating &emsp;&emsp; D. to eat  
9. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
10. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. for  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** "我们已经期待了整整一周"发生在"到达游乐场"之前，为"过去的过去"，用过去完成时 had looked。look 为原形；looked 为一般过去时；have looked 为现在完成时，均不体现时间先后。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be crowded with  
**【解析】** be crowded with 意为"挤满了"，crowded 为形容词。crowd 为名词/动词；crowding 为现在分词；crowds 为名词复数，均不与 was...with 搭配。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 最高级结构 one of the most + adj.  
**【解析】** one of the most popular rides 意为"最受欢迎的游乐设施之一"，前有 most，popular 用原级。more popular 为比较级；most popular 与前面的 most 重复；popularity 为名词。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（方位）  
**【解析】** sit beside you 意为"坐在你旁边"，beside 表"在旁边"。behind 意为"在后面"；between 意为"在两者之间"需接两个对象；below 意为"在下方"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** so + 副词 + that  
**【解析】** so...that... 结构中，修饰动词 screamed 需用副词 loudly。loud 为形容词（但也可用作副词，不过 so + adv. + that 结构中 loudly 更规范）；louder/loudest 为比较级/最高级。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** 修饰动词 drove 需用副词 crazily（疯狂地）。crazy 为形容词；crazier/craziest 为比较级/最高级形容词。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** the most + 不可数名词  
**【解析】** the most fun 意为"最多的乐趣"，most 修饰不可数名词 fun 表最高级。many 修饰可数名词复数；more 为比较级；much 为原级。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 省略主语的时间状语从句（while doing）  
**【解析】** while eating 为省略了主语和 be 动词的时间状语从句（= while we were eating）。eat 为原形；ate 为过去式不能跟 while；to eat 为不定式。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "摩天轮是她最喜欢的，因为从顶上能看到整个城市"，because 引导原因状语从句。so 表结果；but 表转折；although 表让步，均不符合因果关系。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 of 表所属  
**【解析】** memories of our junior high school days 意为"初中时光的记忆"，of 表所属关系。on/at/for 不与 memories 构成此义。故选 C。
