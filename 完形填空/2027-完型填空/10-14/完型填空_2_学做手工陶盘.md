# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工陶盘

## 完形填空原文

Our community center offered a free pottery class during the holiday. My mother encouraged me ___1___ it a try, so we went there together on Saturday morning.

The pottery teacher, a young woman named Ms. Xu, greeted us with a bright smile. She said pottery was one of the ___2___ art forms in human history. I was curious ___3___ how people thousands of years ago made their bowls and plates.

Ms. Xu gave each of us a ball of wet clay. She showed us how ___4___ it flat with our hands and then shape it into a plate. The clay felt cold and soft. ___5___ I pressed too hard, the plate became too thin and broke.

"Take it easy," Ms. Xu said gently. "The clay can feel your mood. If you are calm, ___6___ work will be smoother." I took a deep breath and started again. This time, I pressed the clay more ___7___ and the plate kept its shape.

The most exciting step was decorating. I used a small stick to carve a flower pattern on the edge. It wasn't as ___8___ as the teacher's sample, but I was satisfied.

Ms. Xu told us that the plates needed to be dried and then baked in a kiln. She promised ___9___ them back to us in two weeks. I could ___10___ wait to see my finished plate. This class made me fall in love with pottery.

## 选项

1. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. giving &emsp;&emsp; C. to give &emsp;&emsp; D. gave  
2. A. old &emsp;&emsp; B. older &emsp;&emsp; C. oldest &emsp;&emsp; D. elder  
3. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
4. A. press &emsp;&emsp; B. pressed &emsp;&emsp; C. pressing &emsp;&emsp; D. to press  
5. A. If &emsp;&emsp; B. Unless &emsp;&emsp; C. When &emsp;&emsp; D. Before  
6. A. you &emsp;&emsp; B. your &emsp;&emsp; C. yours &emsp;&emsp; D. yourself  
7. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gently &emsp;&emsp; C. gentler &emsp;&emsp; D. gentlest  
8. A. delicate &emsp;&emsp; B. delicately &emsp;&emsp; C. more delicate &emsp;&emsp; D. most delicate  
9. A. send &emsp;&emsp; B. sent &emsp;&emsp; C. sending &emsp;&emsp; D. to send  
10. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. hardest  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（encourage sb. to do）  
**【解析】** encourage sb. to do sth. 意为"鼓励某人做某事"，不定式作宾补。give 为原形；giving 为现在分词；gave 为过去式，均不能用于 encourage sb. 后。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** one of the oldest art forms 意为"最古老的艺术形式之一"，one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词为固定结构。old 为原级；older 为比较级；elder 多修饰家庭成员（如 elder brother）。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be curious about  
**【解析】** be curious about 意为"对……感到好奇"，about 为固定介词。of/for/with 不与 curious 构成此搭配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** "疑问词 + 不定式"作宾语  
**【解析】** how to press 意为"如何压平"，how to do 作 showed 的直接宾语。press 为原形；pressed 为过去式；pressing 为现在分词，均不与 how 搭配构成此结构。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "当我按得太用力时，盘子变得太薄而破了"，when 表"当……时"。if 表条件（也可用但 when 更强调实际发生的事件）；unless 表"除非"；before 表"在……之前"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** 修饰名词 work 需用形容词性物主代词 your。you 为主格/宾格；yours 为名词性物主代词后不接名词；yourself 为反身代词。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级修饰动词  
**【解析】** more gently 意为"更轻柔地"，修饰动词 pressed。gentle 为形容词；gentler 为形容词比较级，不修饰动词；gentlest 为最高级形容词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** as...as 同级比较  
**【解析】** as + 形容词原级 + as 构成同级比较，delicate（精致的）为原级。delicately 为副词；more delicate 为比较级；most delicate 为最高级，均不用于 as...as 结构。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（promise to do）  
**【解析】** promise to do sth. 意为"承诺做某事"，后接不定式。send 为原形；sent 为过去式；sending 为现在分词，均不用于 promise 后。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词 hardly 表否定  
**【解析】** could hardly wait 意为"几乎等不及了"，hardly 表"几乎不"。hard 意为"努力地/硬的"；harder 为比较级；hardest 为最高级，均不表否定含义。故选 C。
