# 中考英语完形填空：帮同学找回丢失的书包

## 完形填空原文

On Monday morning, my best friend Lily ran up to me ___1___ tears in her eyes. She said she ___2___ her schoolbag on the bus and her student ID was inside. I tried ___3___ calm and told her we could work it out together.

First, we went to the school office ___4___ the teacher on duty might help us call the bus company. While we ___5___ for the reply, I posted a short message on the class group chat ___6___ someone had picked it up by mistake. Twenty minutes later, a senior student called ___7___ he had found a bag under a seat.

We rushed to the meeting place. ___8___ opening the bag, Lily smiled — everything was there. She thanked the boy many times and bought him ___9___ small gift the next day. That experience taught me ___10___ a true friend should do when others are in trouble.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
2. A. leaves &emsp;&emsp; B. left &emsp;&emsp; C. has left &emsp;&emsp; D. had left  
3. A. stay &emsp;&emsp; B. to stay &emsp;&emsp; C. staying &emsp;&emsp; D. stayed  
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
5. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. waited &emsp;&emsp; C. were waiting &emsp;&emsp; D. have waited  
6. A. in case &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; D. even though  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  
8. A. Before &emsp;&emsp; B. After &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. If  
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
10. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** with 复合结构（with + 名词 + 介词短语）  
**【解析】** with tears in her eyes 表示“眼里含着泪”，作伴随状语。in tears 为介词短语作表语时常说 She was in tears，此处需接宾语 her 前的结构，用 with 更自然。on/at 与 tears 搭配不当。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** “落书包”发生在 said 之前，为过去的过去，用 had left。left 仅表过去；现在完成时 has left 强调与现在联系，叙述顺序上不如过去完成清晰。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** try to do sth.  
**【解析】** try to stay calm 表示“尽量保持冷静”。try doing 表“试着做/尝试做”另一含义；try stay 缺少不定式符号。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 状语从句连词（原因）  
**【解析】** 我们去学校办公室是因为值日老师可能帮忙联系公交公司，表原因用 because。if 表条件；until 表直到；although 表让步。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去进行时  
**【解析】** While 强调在一段时间内“正在等待回复”，用 were waiting。一般过去 waited 可接受但不如进行体能体现“等待期间”；现在完成时与 While 搭配不自然。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 短语辨析 in case  
**【解析】** in case someone had picked it up 表示“以防有人误拿”，符合在群里发消息的意图。so that 表目的“以便”，后常接情态动词；as soon as 表一……就；even though 表尽管。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词  
**【解析】** called 后接宾语从句，陈述“他发现了一个包”，用 that。whether 表是否；what/which 在从句中需充当成分。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间介词 before/after + doing  
**【解析】** After opening the bag 表示“打开书包之后”，与 smiled 逻辑顺承。Before 则与微笑原因矛盾；Unless/If 表条件，不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词表泛指  
**【解析】** a small gift 表示“一份小礼物”，泛指且 small 以辅音音素开头，用 a。the 表特指；零冠词不适用于可数单数 gift。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句中连接代词 what  
**【解析】** taught me what a true friend should do 中 what 作 do 的宾语，表示“真正的朋友该做什么”。how/when/where 为连接副词，do 后缺宾语则不通。故选 B。
