# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工剪贴画

## 完形填空原文

In our art class, Miss Green asked us ___1___ a collage about “My Hometown”. I chose old magazines, colored paper, and a pair of scissors ___2___ my tools.

She reminded us ___3___ we should plan the layout before we cut anything. I drew a simple river ___4___ the middle of the cardboard first. ___5___ I added green hills on both sides. The hardest part was ___6___ the tiny windows of the houses neatly.

When I showed my work, Miss Green said it was one of ___7___ creative pieces in our group. I realized that patience matters ___8___ than speed in handicrafts. Now the collage ___9___ on the wall of our classroom, and it makes me feel proud ___10___ time I pass by.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
4. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
5. A. However &emsp;&emsp; B. Therefore &emsp;&emsp; C. Then &emsp;&emsp; D. Although  
6. A. cut &emsp;&emsp; B. cutting &emsp;&emsp; C. to cut &emsp;&emsp; D. cuts  
7. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. the most &emsp;&emsp; D. much  
8. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. much  
9. A. hangs &emsp;&emsp; B. hung &emsp;&emsp; C. is hanging &emsp;&emsp; D. was hung  
10. A. some &emsp;&emsp; B. any &emsp;&emsp; C. each &emsp;&emsp; D. every  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** ask sb. to do sth.  
**【解析】** ask us to make 为固定搭配。ask us make 缺少 to；ask doing 结构错误。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 as 表示“作为”  
**【解析】** choose … as my tools 表示“选……作为我的工具”。like 表“像”；for 表目的；with 表伴随，均不如 as 贴切。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** remind 后的宾语从句  
**【解析】** remind us that we should… 中 that 引导陈述性宾语从句。what 需在从句中作成分；if 表是否；how 表方式，与“应该先规划版面”的提醒内容不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 in the middle of  
**【解析】** in the middle of the cardboard 为固定搭配“在硬纸板中央”。on the middle 不地道；at/to 与 middle 搭配不当。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 连接副词表示顺序  
**【解析】** 先画河再添山，表顺承用 Then。However 表转折；Therefore 表结果；Although 引导从句。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动名词作表语  
**【解析】** The hardest part was cutting… 中表语说明主语的内容，用动名词 cutting。不定式 to cut 也可作表语，但此处强调“剪小窗这件事/这个环节”，动名词更自然；was cut 为被动，主语 part 与 cut 逻辑主语应为人，不选。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** “one of + the + 形容词最高级 + 复数名词”  
**【解析】** one of the most creative pieces 为固定结构。more/most 缺少 the 与范围；much 不能构成最高级短语。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级（than）  
**【解析】** matters more than speed 用 more 与 than 构成比较级。many 修饰可数名词；most 为最高级无范围；much 修饰比较级时常说 much more，但选项无 much more。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 一般现在时（状态）  
**【解析】** 描述现在作品挂在墙上这一常态，用一般现在时 hangs（不及物用法 hang-hung 表悬挂；若为“被挂”也可用 is hung，但 hang 作“悬挂”时常用主动表状态）。was hung 强调过去被挂，与 Now 及全句现在感略弱。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** every time 引导时间状语从句  
**【解析】** every time I pass by 表示“每次经过”。each time 也可，但选项为 each/every，every time 更常用于引导从句的固定语感；some/any time 表某时/任何时候，不符。故选 D。
