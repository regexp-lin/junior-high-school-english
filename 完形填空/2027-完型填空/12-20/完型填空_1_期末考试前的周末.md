# 中考英语完形填空：期末考试前的周末

## 完形填空原文

The weekend before final exams always feels ___1___ than a normal one. On Saturday morning, I made a timetable and put it ___2___ the wall above my desk.

Math was my weakest subject, ___3___ I spent two hours on practice problems first. After lunch, I listened to an English podcast ___4___ I cleaned my room; multitasking helped me stay awake.

___5___ my phone buzzed with a game invitation, I turned on “focus mode” without thinking twice. In the evening, Dad brought fruit ___6___ the kitchen and said short walks could help my brain relax.

On Sunday, I reviewed mistakes ___7___ I had written in my notebook. When I felt nervous, I took deep breaths ___8___ counting to ten. Before bed, I packed my bag ___9___ I would not forget anything on Monday.

Looking at the timetable, I thought hard work was important, ___10___ good sleep mattered just as much.

## 选项

1. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. longer &emsp;&emsp; C. longest &emsp;&emsp; D. more long  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
3. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
4. A. while &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
5. A. When &emsp;&emsp; B. Unless &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Before  
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. as if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（*than* 提示）  
**【解析】** *feels longer than a normal one*，*than* 提示用比较级 *longer*。*more long* 为错误形式；原级/最高级不匹配 *than*。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *on the wall*  
**【解析】** 时间表贴在墙表面，用 *on the wall*。*in* 在墙里；*at/by* 不如 *on* 准确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *so* 表结果  
**【解析】** “数学最弱”与“先花两小时做题”为因果关系，用 *so*。*but* 转折；*or* 选择；*although* 让步。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 *while*（与此同时）  
**【解析】** *listened to a podcast while I cleaned my room* 表示边听边整理。*until/unless/because* 与“同时进行两件事”不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 *when*  
**【解析】** *When my phone buzzed... I turned on focus mode* 表示当手机提示音响起时。*Unless* 除非；*Although* 让步；*Before* 在之前，语义不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *from* 表来源  
**【解析】** *brought fruit from the kitchen* 表示从厨房端来水果。*of/off/by* 不如 *from* 表示来源自然。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词（指物）  
**【解析】** 先行词 *mistakes*，从句 *I had written in my notebook* 缺宾语，用 *which*。*what* 不引导定语从句；*who/whose* 指人。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *and* 连接并列谓语  
**【解析】** *took deep breaths and counted to ten* 为两个并列谓语动词，表示连续放松动作，用 *and*。*but/or/so* 逻辑不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 *so that*  
**【解析】** *packed my bag so that I would not forget anything* 表目的“以免周一忘带东西”。*even though* 让步；*as if* 好像；*unless* 除非。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *but* 表转折  
**【解析】** “努力很重要”与“睡眠同样重要”形成对比补充，用 *but* 引出同等重要的另一面。*and* 仅顺承；*or* 选择；*so* 结果。故选 B。
