# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工蜡烛台

## 完形填空原文

Safety comes first ___1___ we make candle holders in the craft workshop. Ms. Wu asked us ___2___ wear gloves and keep water nearby in case of sparks.

We pressed soft clay around a small glass cup ___3___ the candle would stand straight. I smoothed the edges with a wooden tool ___4___ the surface looked even.

While the clay air-dried, we painted patterns ___5___ water-based paint was safer for beginners. Jack painted stars; ___6___ looked like tiny windows under the winter sun.

After firing in the kiln, the holders became as hard ___7___ stone. Ms. Wu placed LED tea lights inside ___8___ we could test the effect safely.

I carried mine home ___9___ both hands because it felt heavier than I expected. Mom said it was ___10___ useful gift she had received from me this year.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until  
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
6. A. mine &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. me &emsp;&emsp; D. myself  
7. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
8. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even if &emsp;&emsp; C. as if &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 *when*  
**【解析】** *Safety comes first when we make candle holders* 表示“制作烛台时安全第一”。*what/whether/how* 引导名词性从句，此处需要时间状语从句连词。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*ask sb. to do*）  
**【解析】** *asked us to wear gloves* 为“要求某人做某事”，用不定式 *to wear*。*for/of/with* 不构成 *ask sb. to do*。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 *so that*  
**【解析】** 把黏土围在玻璃杯外是为了“让蜡烛立直”，*so that the candle would stand straight*。*unless/although/until* 逻辑不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 *until*  
**【解析】** *smoothed the edges... until the surface looked even* 表示一直抹到表面平整。*if/because/unless* 不符合“持续到某结果出现”的语义。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句 *because*  
**【解析】** *painted patterns because water-based paint was safer* 表示选择水性颜料的原因。*so* 表结果；*if* 表条件；*although* 表让步，语义不符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 名词性物主代词 *mine*  
**【解析】** *Jack painted stars; mine looked like tiny windows* 中 *mine* 相当于 *my holder/pattern*，作主语。*my* 为形容词性物主代词，后需接名词；*me/myself* 不能作主语指代“我的作品”。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 原级比较 *as...as...*  
**【解析】** *as hard as stone* 为固定结构“像石头一样硬”，第二个 *as* 引出比较对象。*like/than/so* 不能构成此标准比较结构。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 *so that*  
**【解析】** *placed LED tea lights inside so that we could test the effect safely* 表目的“以便安全测试效果”。*even if/as if/although* 语义不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *with* 表“用、借助”  
**【解析】** *carried mine home with both hands* 表示用双手托着带回家。*in/on/by* 不如 *with* 表示“使用身体部位”自然。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词与可数名词单数  
**【解析】** *it was a useful gift (that) she had received from me this year* 表示“今年我送她的礼物中一件很实用的”，泛指用 *a*。*useful* 以辅音音素 /j/ 开头，不用 *an*；*the* 需明确特指双方已知的唯一礼物，语境更像评价“这是一件实用的礼物”。故选 A。
