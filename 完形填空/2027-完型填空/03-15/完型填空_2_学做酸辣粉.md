# 中考英语完形填空：学做酸辣粉

## 完形填空原文

My cousin runs a small noodle shop. Yesterday she taught me ___1___ cook hot and sour noodles for my family dinner.

We boiled sweet potato noodles ___2___ they turned soft but still chewy. While waiting, she mixed vinegar, soy sauce, chili oil, and a little sugar ___3___ a bowl.

"The taste should be balanced, ___4___ too salty or too sour," she said. I tasted a drop ___5___ nodded, though my tongue burned a little.

We added peanuts, pickled vegetables, and hot soup. The smell rushed ___6___ my nose, and my stomach growled loudly.

When my dad tried the noodles, he raised his thumb. "It's ___7___ than the shop's?" I asked jokingly. He laughed, "It's ___8___ because you made it."

After dinner, my cousin reminded me ___9___ wash the bowls right away. "Clean habits make cooking ___10___ next time," she added.

## 选项

1. A. what to &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. although
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
4. A. always &emsp;&emsp; B. never &emsp;&emsp; C. sometimes &emsp;&emsp; D. often
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
8. A. special &emsp;&emsp; B. specially &emsp;&emsp; C. more special &emsp;&emsp; D. most specially
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. easiest &emsp;&emsp; D. easily

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我怎么给家人做酸辣粉。teach sb. how to cook... 表示“教某人如何烹饪”。what/when/where 与“做法教学”语境不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把红薯粉煮到变软但仍嚼劲。until 表示“直到……为止”。if/because/although 不符合煮制过程描述。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把醋、酱油、辣椒油和少许糖在碗里混合。mix...in a bowl 表示“在碗里混合”。on/at/to 不如 in 贴切容器内部。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道要平衡，既不要太咸也不要太酸。never too...or too... 结构可理解为“不要太极端”，与 balanced 呼应；此处用 never too salty or too sour 表示“别过咸也别过酸”。always/sometimes/often 与平衡要求矛盾。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尝了一滴并点头。tasted 与 nodded 为顺承并列，用 and。or/but/so 不符合动作连贯。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：香味冲进我的鼻子。rush into 表示“冲进”。from/with/by 不如 into 形象。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：比店里的还好吗？than 提示比较级，用 better。good/well 无比较；best 为最高级缺 the 且与 than 不搭。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它很特别，因为是你做的。系动词 is 后接形容词 special。specially 为副词；more special/most specially 形式不当或词性错误。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐提醒我马上洗碗。remind sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配。for/of/with 不符合 remind 结构。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：干净的习惯让下次做饭更容易。暗含对比，用 easier。easy 原级稍弱；easiest 缺范围；easily 为副词，make 后需宾补形容词。故选 B。
