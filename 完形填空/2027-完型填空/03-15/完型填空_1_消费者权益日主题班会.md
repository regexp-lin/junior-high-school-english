# 中考英语完形填空：消费者权益日主题班会

## 完形填空原文

On March 15th, our class held a meeting about consumer rights. Our teacher started ___1___ a short video about fake products and online traps.

We discussed how ___2___ check food labels and keep receipts. "A receipt is ___3___ small paper, but it is strong evidence if something goes wrong," the teacher explained.

In groups, we role-played a scene ___4___ a student returned a broken charger to a shop. I played the shop assistant ___5___ first, and I felt nervous when my classmates asked sharp questions.

Later, we made posters ___6___ simple tips: compare prices, read reviews, and never share payment codes ___7___ strangers.

At the end, our teacher said, "Being a smart consumer means asking questions ___8___ thinking twice." I wrote that sentence ___9___ my notebook and promised myself ___10___ stay calm while shopping online.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
3. A. just &emsp;&emsp; B. still &emsp;&emsp; C. even &emsp;&emsp; D. ever
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. who
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
8. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师先放了一段关于假货和网络陷阱的短视频。start with... 表示“以……开始”。start for/to/at 不符合常见搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们讨论如何查看食品标签并保留小票。how to check 为“疑问词 + 不定式”结构。how for/of/with check 结构不成立。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小票只是一张小纸，但出了问题时它是强有力的证据。just 表示“仅仅；只是”，语气贴切。still/even/ever 不符合对比强调。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们分组表演了一个场景：一名学生把坏充电器退回商店。先行词 scene 表地点，从句缺状语，用 where。what/which/who 不合适。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一开始扮演店员。at first 为固定短语，意为“起初”。in/on/for first 不正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们制作了带有简单提示的海报。posters with tips 表示“带有提示的海报”。in/on/at 不如 with 表示“附带内容”。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：永远不要把付款码分享给陌生人。share sth. with sb. 为固定搭配。to/for/by 不符合 share 的典型结构。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：做一个聪明的消费者意味着多问问题并三思。asking questions 与 thinking twice 为并列，用 and。or/but/so 不符合并列建议语气。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把那句话写在笔记本里。write...in a notebook 表示“写在笔记本里”。on 多指封面或表面一页；at/to 不自然。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：并承诺自己网购时保持冷静。promise oneself to do sth. 用不定式作宾补。for/of/with 不符合 promise 结构。故选 A。
