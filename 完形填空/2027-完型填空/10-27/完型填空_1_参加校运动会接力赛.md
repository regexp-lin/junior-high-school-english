# 中考英语完形填空：参加校运动会接力赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school sports meeting was held last week. I was chosen to run the last leg of the 4×100 meter relay race. The coach said I was the ___1___ runner in our class, so I had to run the anchor leg.

Before the race, our team practiced every day during lunch break. Passing the baton smoothly was even more important ___2___ running fast. We dropped the baton several times during practice, ___3___ made us all anxious.

On the day of the race, the stadium was full of cheering students. I stood at the final position, ___4___ for my teammate to reach me. My palms were sweating, and my legs felt weak.

The gun went off, and our first runner dashed forward. She ran well and passed the baton ___5___ to the second runner. The second runner was also fast, but he almost tripped and ___6___ some time. By the time the third runner started, we were in third place.

Then the baton came to me. I grabbed it tightly and ran as fast as I ___7___. The crowd was screaming my name. I could hear ___8___ but the sound of my own breathing and the wind in my ears.

I crossed the finish line in second place. ___9___ we didn't win, our whole class was excited. Our teacher said that what mattered was not the result but the effort we ___10___ into it.

## 选项

1. A. fast &emsp;&emsp; B. faster &emsp;&emsp; C. fastest &emsp;&emsp; D. very fast  
2. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. from  
3. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
4. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. waited &emsp;&emsp; C. to wait &emsp;&emsp; D. waiting  
5. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. successful &emsp;&emsp; C. successfully &emsp;&emsp; D. succeed  
6. A. lose &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. losing &emsp;&emsp; D. loses  
7. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. could &emsp;&emsp; C. may &emsp;&emsp; D. must  
8. A. something &emsp;&emsp; B. anything &emsp;&emsp; C. nothing &emsp;&emsp; D. everything  
9. A. Because &emsp;&emsp; B. Although &emsp;&emsp; C. Unless &emsp;&emsp; D. If  
10. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. give &emsp;&emsp; D. make  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** the fastest runner 意为"跑得最快的人"，the + 最高级 + 名词在班级范围内。fast 为原级；faster 为比较级；very fast 不用于定冠词 the 后修饰名词。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 比较级搭配 than  
**【解析】** more important than 意为"比……更重要"，than 引出比较对象。as 用于同级比较；like 意为"像"；from 意为"从"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句  
**【解析】** 先行词为前面整个句子"我们掉了好几次接力棒"，从句缺主语，用 which。that 不引导非限制性定语从句；who 指人；what 不引导定语从句。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作伴随状语  
**【解析】** waiting for my teammate 为现在分词短语作伴随状语，描述"我站在终点位置等待"。wait 为原形；waited 为过去式；to wait 为不定式表目的（此处为伴随更自然）。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** passed the baton successfully 意为"成功地传递了接力棒"，修饰动词 passed 用副词。success 为名词；successful 为形容词；succeed 为动词。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（并列谓语）  
**【解析】** and 连接 tripped 和 lost，时态一致用过去式。lose 为原形；losing 为现在分词；loses 为三单。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** as...as I could  
**【解析】** as fast as I could 意为"尽可能快地"，全文为过去叙述，用 could（can 的过去式）。can 为现在时；may 表可能；must 表必须。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 不定代词 nothing but  
**【解析】** could hear nothing but 意为"什么都听不到，只能听到"，nothing but 等于 only。something 用于肯定句；anything 用于否定/疑问句；everything 意为"所有事"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 让步状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "虽然没赢，但全班都很兴奋"，although 引导让步状语从句。because 表原因；unless 表条件；if 表条件。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 put effort into  
**【解析】** put effort into 意为"投入努力"，put...into 为固定搭配。take/give/make 不与 effort into 构成此搭配。故选 A。
