# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工首饰盒

## 完形填空原文

Mother's birthday was coming, and I wanted to make something special for ___1___. After searching online, I decided to make a handmade jewelry box.

I found a step-by-step tutorial on a craft website. The materials I needed ___2___ simple: a small cardboard box, fabric, cotton padding, glue, and decorative ribbon.

First, I cut the fabric into pieces that were big ___3___ to cover each side of the box. Then I spread a thin layer of glue on the cardboard and pressed the fabric onto it carefully. The cotton padding ___4___ between the cardboard and the fabric to make the box soft to the touch.

The ___5___ part was covering the corners. The fabric kept wrinkling, and I had to redo it three times. I was so frustrated that I almost threw everything away. But then I thought about my mother's ___6___ face and continued.

After two hours, the box was finally done. I lined the inside with pink silk and added a small mirror to the lid. The ribbon went around the edges ___7___ a finishing touch.

On Mother's birthday, I presented the box to her after dinner. She opened it and her eyes grew ___8___. "Did you make this yourself?" she asked. When I nodded, she held me tight and whispered, "This is the ___9___ birthday gift I have ever received."

I was ___10___ happy that I forgot all the difficulties I had faced while making it. Love truly makes everything worthwhile.

## 选项

1. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself  
2. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. were &emsp;&emsp; D. are  
3. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. enough &emsp;&emsp; D. quite  
4. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. was put &emsp;&emsp; C. puts &emsp;&emsp; D. is put  
5. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. hardest &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly  
6. A. smile &emsp;&emsp; B. smiled &emsp;&emsp; C. smiles &emsp;&emsp; D. smiling  
7. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. wide &emsp;&emsp; B. widely &emsp;&emsp; C. wider &emsp;&emsp; D. widest  
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. well &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. such  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** for her 意为"给她"，for 为介词后接宾格 her。she 为主格；hers 为名词性物主代词；herself 为反身代词（此处无需强调"她自己"）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 主谓一致与时态  
**【解析】** The materials 为复数名词，全文为过去叙述，用 were。is 为单数现在时；was 为单数过去时；are 为复数现在时。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** enough 的位置  
**【解析】** big enough to cover 意为"足够大以覆盖"，enough 修饰形容词后置。very/quite 表程度但不表"足够"；too 含消极义。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 被动语态（一般过去时）  
**【解析】** cotton padding was put 意为"棉垫被放置"，padding 与 put 为被动关系。put 为主动；puts 为三单主动；is put 为现在时被动。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** the hardest part 意为"最困难的部分"，前有 the，用最高级。hard 为原级；harder 为比较级；hardly 为副词"几乎不"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 现在分词作定语  
**【解析】** my mother's smiling face 意为"妈妈微笑的脸"，smiling 为现在分词作前置定语修饰 face。smile 为动词/名词；smiled 为过去式；smiles 为三单。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 as 表"作为"  
**【解析】** as a finishing touch 意为"作为最后的点缀"，as 表"作为"。for 表目的；like 意为"像"；with 表伴随。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 系动词 grow + 形容词  
**【解析】** eyes grew wide 意为"眼睛瞪大了"，grow 为系动词后接形容词原级 wide。widely 为副词；wider 为比较级；widest 为最高级。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** the best birthday gift I have ever received 为固定最高级句型。best 为 good 的最高级。good 为原级；better 为比较级；well 作形容词表"身体好"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** so...that... 结果状语从句  
**【解析】** so happy that I forgot 构成结果状语从句。very/quite 不与 that 从句搭配；too 常与 to 搭配；such 修饰名词。故选 C。
