# 中考英语完形填空：舞台上的两分钟

## 完形填空原文

Our school talent show ___1___ held in the hall last Friday. I had practiced playing the guitar ___2___ a month, but I still felt nervous when my name ___3___ called.

While waiting backstage, I took a deep breath and told ___4___, “You can do it.” When the lights went on, I walked onto the stage ___5___ a polite bow. The audience became quiet ___6___.

I played a piece that I liked very much. At the end, there was loud applause. I bowed again and ___7___ the stage happily. My music teacher said I had improved a lot ___8___ last year.

On my way home, I thought about how ___9___ support from teachers and classmates meant to me. That experience taught me that ___10___ brave is not about having no fear, but about moving forward with fear.

## 选项

1. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. will be &emsp;&emsp; D. has been  
2. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
3. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be  
4. A. me &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. mine &emsp;&emsp; D. myself  
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
6. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quicker &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. more quick  
7. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. left &emsp;&emsp; C. leaving &emsp;&emsp; D. leaves  
8. A. comparing &emsp;&emsp; B. compares &emsp;&emsp; C. compared &emsp;&emsp; D. compare  
9. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  
10. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. being &emsp;&emsp; C. been &emsp;&emsp; D. to be  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** last Friday 提示过去时；talent show 与 hold 为被动关系，用 was held。is/will be/has been 时态不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 for 表一段时间  
**【解析】** for a month 表示“持续一个月”。since 后多接时间点；in/at 不用于此处表持续练习时长。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** name was called 表示“名字被叫到”，叙述过去事件用过去被动。is/has been/will be 时态不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 反身代词  
**【解析】** tell myself 表示“告诉自己”，主语与宾语同指用 myself。me/my/mine 不强调“自我鼓励”的反身意义。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表伴随方式  
**【解析】** with a polite bow 表示“伴随着礼貌的鞠躬”。in/on/at 不自然搭配 bow 表方式。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰整个句子/动词 became  
**【解析】** became quiet quickly 表示“很快安静下来”，用副词 quickly。quick 为形容词；比较级无比较对象。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时（并列谓语）  
**【解析】** and 连接 bowed 与 left，时态一致用过去式 left。leave/leaving/leaves 形式不对。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（compared with/to）  
**【解析】** compared with last year 表示“与去年相比”，过去分词短语作状语。comparing/compares/compare 结构不对。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** much 修饰不可数名词 support  
**【解析】** how much support 表示“多少支持”，support 为不可数名词，用 much。many 修饰可数；more/most 与 how 搭配不当。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动名词作主语（宾语从句中）  
**【解析】** that being brave is not... 中，being brave 作宾语从句的主语，应用动名词形式 being。be/been/to be 不能直接与 brave 构成主语短语。故选 B。
