# 中考英语完形填空：做一个木相框

## 完形填空原文

For my parents’ anniversary, I planned ___1___ make a wooden photo frame in the school DIY club. Mr. Lin, our coach, told us ___2___ safety rules before we started.

He helped me measure the wood and draw lines ___3___ a ruler. “Always cut ___4___ from your body,” he warned. I used sandpaper ___5___ the edges smooth. The work took longer ___6___ I thought, ___7___ I enjoyed every minute.

When I put our family photo into the frame, it fit ___8___. I wrapped it ___9___ soft paper and hid it in my schoolbag. At dinner, my parents were surprised ___10___ happy to receive my gift.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. remembering &emsp;&emsp; C. to remember &emsp;&emsp; D. remembered  
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
4. A. away &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. down  
5. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
6. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
8. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. perfectly &emsp;&emsp; C. more perfect &emsp;&emsp; D. most perfect  
9. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（plan to do）  
**【解析】** plan to make 为固定搭配。for/of/with 不构成 plan 后接动词表计划内容。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell us to remember 用不定式作宾补。remember/remembering/remembered 不符合 tell 结构。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表工具  
**【解析】** draw lines with a ruler 表示“用尺子画线”。in/on/by 不如 with 贴切表工具。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词 away 与 cut 搭配表安全方向  
**【解析】** cut away from your body 表示“切割时远离身体”，为安全提示常用表达。cut out/off/down 语义不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（use sth. to do）  
**【解析】** use sandpaper to make the edges smooth 表示“用砂纸把边缘磨光滑”。use...doing/done 结构不成立。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 比较级结构（longer than）  
**【解析】** longer than I thought 表示“比我想的更久”。as/like/so 不与 longer 构成比较。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “耗时更久”与“享受每一分钟”为转折，用 but。and/or/so 逻辑不符。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词 fit  
**【解析】** fit perfectly 表示“刚好合适”，用副词修饰动词 fit。perfect 为形容词；perfect 一般不用比较级 more perfect（不规范）。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（wrap ... in ...）  
**【解析】** wrap it in soft paper 表示“用软纸包好”。wrap...with 也可表材料，但 wrap in 更常见表“包在……里”；by/on 不搭配。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 and 连接并列形容词  
**【解析】** surprised and happy 表示“又惊喜又开心”，并列用 and。but/or/so 不符合情感并列。故选 A。
