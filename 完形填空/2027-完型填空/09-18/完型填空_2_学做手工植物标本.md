# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工植物标本

## 完形填空原文

Our school science club held a hands-on workshop last month, ___1___ senior students could learn how to make plant specimens by pressing leaves and flowers. Our teacher brought scissors, absorbent paper, tweezers, ___2___ small stickers, and then she explained each step slowly.

We first collected leaves ___3___ were thin but not too wet. Next, we placed them carefully ___4___ two pieces of cardboard and sandwiched them inside a heavy dictionary to press out the moisture.

"Don't open the book too soon," she warned, ___5___ we were excited to see whether the colors had faded. ___6___ about three weeks, the samples turned paper-thin and fragile.

The final display looked ___7___ than the wrinkled failures I had made at home. I copied the Latin name ___8___ the label and stored the sheet in a plastic sleeve.

This activity showed me ___9___ observation and patience matter in science. I decided ___10___ join the club's field trip next Saturday.

## 选项

1. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. who
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. and
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
4. A. among &emsp;&emsp; B. between &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. across
5. A. although &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
6. A. Before &emsp;&emsp; B. After &emsp;&emsp; C. During &emsp;&emsp; D. Until
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. how
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系副词 where）
**【解析】** 先行词 workshop 表示活动场合/地点，从句 senior students could learn... 结构完整，需用关系副词 where 作地点状语，相当于 at which。which/who 在从句中作主语或宾语，此处从句不缺主宾；when 表时间，不如 where 贴切。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** scissors、absorbent paper、tweezers 与 small stickers 为并列宾语，用 and 连接。but 表转折，or 表选择，so 表结果。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系代词 which）
**【解析】** 先行词 leaves 指物，从句 were thin but not too wet 缺主语，用 which 或 that，选项中为 which。what 不引导此类定语从句；who 指人；whose 表所属，后需接名词。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词（between 的用法）
**【解析】** 句意为：把叶片小心地放在两块硬纸板之间。between 表示“在两者之间”，与 two pieces of cardboard 搭配。among 用于三者及以上；through 表穿过；across 表横跨。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词（让步状语从句）
**【解析】** 句意为：老师提醒不要过早打开书本，尽管我们很想立刻查看颜色是否褪色。although 引导让步状语从句，表示“虽然……但是……”。because 表原因，if 表条件，unless 表“除非”，均不符合“警告与心情”之间的转折让步关系。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词（时间表达）
**【解析】** 句意为：大约三周之后，标本变得又薄又脆。After about three weeks 表示“在……之后”，符合压制标本需要等待的时间逻辑。Before 表示之前，与结果矛盾；During 后多接时间段名词短语，且与 turned 的结果搭配不如 After 自然；Until 强调“直到……为止”，此处强调“等待结束后的变化”，After 更准确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 句中有 than，且 looked 为系动词，后接形容词比较级 better，表示“最终展示比我在家做的皱巴巴失败品更好”。good 为原级，well 作形容词时多表“身体好”，best 为最高级。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** copy/write... on the label 表示“写在标签表面上”，用 on。in 多表在内部；at/to 与 label 搭配不自然。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 宾语从句（连接词 that）
**【解析】** 句意为：这次活动让我明白了观察与耐心在科学中很重要。show sb that... 后接陈述意义的宾语从句，that 在口语或非正式文体中可省略，但试题中常保留以考查宾语从句引导词。从句 observation and patience matter in science 结构完整、表陈述事实，用 that 即可。what 须在从句中充当成分；whether 表“是否”；how 多表方式或程度，此处不如 that 贴切。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** decide to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示“决定做某事”。decide to join 意为“决定参加”。for/of/with 不能与 decide 构成该固定结构。故选 A。
