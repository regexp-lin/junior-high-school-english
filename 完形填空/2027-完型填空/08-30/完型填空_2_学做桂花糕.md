# 中考英语完形填空：学做桂花糕

## 完形填空原文

Autumn was coming, and the sweet smell of osmanthus flowers filled the neighborhood. My mother suggested ___1___ osmanthus cakes together as a weekend activity.

We went to the market and bought rice flour, sugar, and fresh osmanthus flowers. The flowers were so tiny that we had to pick them ___2___. Mother told me to mix the flour ___3___ the sugar first, then add water little by little.

"Don't add ___4___ much water at once, or the dough will be too wet," she warned. I stirred the mixture ___5___ until it became thick and smooth. Then we pressed it into small molds and sprinkled osmanthus flowers ___6___ the top.

The cakes needed to be steamed ___7___ about twenty minutes. While waiting, I cleaned the kitchen so that it would look ___8___ when Father came home. When the steamer was opened, golden cakes appeared ___9___ our eyes. They looked so beautiful!

We enjoyed the warm cakes with tea. I was happy because I ___10___ learned a traditional recipe that I could share with my friends.

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made
2. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. careless &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
3. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. such
5. A. patient &emsp;&emsp; B. patiently &emsp;&emsp; C. impatient &emsp;&emsp; D. impatiently
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. above
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
8. A. tidy &emsp;&emsp; B. tidily &emsp;&emsp; C. messy &emsp;&emsp; D. messily
9. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. behind
10. A. already &emsp;&emsp; B. yet &emsp;&emsp; C. still &emsp;&emsp; D. ever

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈建议一起做桂花糕作为周末活动。suggest doing sth. 表示"建议做某事"，后接动名词。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：花太小了，我们必须小心地摘。修饰动词 pick，需用副词 carefully。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈让我先把面粉和糖混合。mix A with B 表示"把 A 和 B 混合"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要一次加太多水。too much 修饰不可数名词 water，表示"太多"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我耐心地搅拌混合物。修饰动词 stirred，需用副词 patiently。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后在上面撒上桂花。on the top 表示"在顶部/表面上"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：糕需要蒸大约二十分钟。for + 时间段 表示持续的时长。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我打扫了厨房，这样爸爸回来时它看起来很整洁。look 为系动词，后接形容词作表语；tidy 表示"整洁的"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当蒸笼打开时，金色的糕点出现在我们眼前。before our eyes 表示"在我们眼前"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很高兴，因为我已经学会了一个传统配方。already 用于肯定句，表示"已经"。故选 A。
