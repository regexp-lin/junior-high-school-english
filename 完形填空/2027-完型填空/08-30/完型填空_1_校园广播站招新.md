# 中考英语完形填空：校园广播站招新

## 完形填空原文

A notice on the school board caught my eye: the campus radio station was looking ___1___ new members. I had always dreamed of ___2___ a radio host, so I signed up immediately.

The audition was held ___3___ Friday afternoon. About twenty students came to try out. We each had to read a short passage and answer some questions. I practiced ___4___ at home the night before, so I felt quite confident.

When my name ___5___, I walked to the front and picked up the microphone. My hands were shaking a little, ___6___ my voice stayed steady. After the reading, the teacher asked me ___7___ I wanted to join the station. I said that I hoped to share good stories and music ___8___ my schoolmates.

Two days later, the results ___9___ out. I checked the list and found my name on it! I was so excited that I could ___10___ believe it. This was the beginning of a wonderful experience.

## 选项

1. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. after
2. A. become &emsp;&emsp; B. became &emsp;&emsp; C. becoming &emsp;&emsp; D. to become
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. harder &emsp;&emsp; D. hardest
5. A. called &emsp;&emsp; B. was called &emsp;&emsp; C. is called &emsp;&emsp; D. calls
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. but
7. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
9. A. come &emsp;&emsp; B. came &emsp;&emsp; C. comes &emsp;&emsp; D. coming
10. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. nearly &emsp;&emsp; D. always

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：校园广播站正在招新成员。look for 表示"寻找"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直梦想成为一名广播主持人。dream of doing sth. 表示"梦想做某事"，of 为介词，后接动名词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 时间介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：试镜在周五下午举行。具体到某一天用介词 on。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：前一天晚上我在家努力练习。hard 作副词表示"努力地"，hardly 表示"几乎不"，含义完全不同。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我的名字被叫到时。name 与 call 构成被动关系，叙述过去的事用一般过去时被动 was called。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手有点发抖，但声音很稳。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 宾语从句（连接词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师问我为什么想加入广播站。why 表示"为什么"，引导宾语从句询问原因。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望和同学们分享好故事和音乐。share ... with sb. 表示"与某人分享"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：两天后结果出来了。叙述过去发生的事，用一般过去时 came。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我兴奋得几乎不敢相信。hardly 表示"几乎不"，与 could 搭配表示"几乎不能相信"。故选 B。
