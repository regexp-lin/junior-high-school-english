# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工T恤扎染

## 完形填空原文

During the art class yesterday, we learned ___1___ make tie-dye T-shirts. Our teacher brought some plain white T-shirts, rubber bands, and bottles ___2___ special dye.

First, we needed ___3___ the T-shirt tightly with rubber bands ___4___ different patterns could appear. Then we slowly poured the dye onto the cloth. The colors looked much ___5___ after they mixed on the fabric.

While ___6___ for the dye to dry, we talked about our designs excitedly. Someone suggested ___7___ photos of the works and posting them online. I thought it was ___8___ good idea.

___9___ the class was over, we washed the shirts carefully. When I unfolded mine, I couldn't believe ___10___ beautiful it looked. I couldn't wait to wear it at the weekend party.

## 选项

1. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. tie &emsp;&emsp; B. to tie &emsp;&emsp; C. tying &emsp;&emsp; D. tied
4. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. even though &emsp;&emsp; C. as if &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as
5. A. bright &emsp;&emsp; B. brighter &emsp;&emsp; C. brightest &emsp;&emsp; D. more brighter
6. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. waited &emsp;&emsp; C. waiting &emsp;&emsp; D. to wait
7. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. to take &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. taken
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
9. A. Before &emsp;&emsp; B. After &emsp;&emsp; C. Until &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. that

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学习了如何制作扎染 T 恤。learn how to do sth. 表示"学习如何做某事"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（bottles of…）
**【解析】** 本句意为：以及几瓶特制染料。bottles of 表示"几瓶……"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（need to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先，我们需要用橡皮筋把 T 恤扎紧。need to do sth. 表示"需要做某事"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（so that 表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：以便出现不同的图案。so that 引导目的状语从句。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（much 修饰）
**【解析】** 本句意为：颜色在布料上混合后看起来鲜艳得多。much 修饰比较级 brighter。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（while waiting）
**【解析】** 本句意为：在等待染料晾干时。while 后接现在分词 waiting，相当于 while we were waiting。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（suggest doing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：有人建议给作品拍照并传到网上。suggest 后接动名词作宾语。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我认为这是个好主意。good 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（时间先后）
**【解析】** 本句意为：下课后，我们仔细清洗了 T 恤。根据语境，先下课再清洗，用 After。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 感叹结构 / 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不敢相信它看起来有多漂亮。believe 后接宾语从句，修饰形容词 beautiful 用 how。故选 B。
