# 中考英语完形填空：三月学习总结班会

## 完形填空原文

Today is the last day of March. Our head teacher held a class meeting ___1___ review our progress this month.

She first showed us a chart ___2___ our average test scores. Most subjects had improved, ___3___ our English scores went up the most — by eight points.

Then she asked several students ___4___ share their study tips. Zhang Wei said he read English articles for fifteen minutes every morning. Liu Yan said she kept a vocabulary notebook and reviewed it ___5___ bed every night.

I shared my method ___6___: I practiced listening by watching English cartoons. Some classmates were surprised ___7___ this could also be a way of learning.

The teacher also pointed ___8___ some problems. "Too many of you stay up late playing with your phones," she said seriously. "Sleep is as important ___9___ studying."

At the end, we each wrote down one goal for April on a sticky note and put it on the board. Mine was: "Finish homework ___10___ 9 p.m. every day."

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. on
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. before
6. A. also &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. either &emsp;&emsp; D. neither
7. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how
8. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. out &emsp;&emsp; D. in
9. A. than &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. as &emsp;&emsp; D. with
10. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. since &emsp;&emsp; D. until

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/不定式表目的
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任开了一个班会来回顾本月的进步。to review 表目的。for/of/with 不如 to 贴切表示"为了"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她先给我们展示了一张关于平均成绩的图表。a chart about... 表示"关于……的图表"。in/for/at 不如 about 表示"有关"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：大部分科目有提高，而且英语提高最多。前后为递进补充关系，and 表并列。or 为"或者"；but 为"但是"；so 为"所以"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她请几位同学分享学习技巧。ask sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配。for/of/on 不符合 ask 的结构。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她每晚睡前复习词汇本。before bed 表示"睡前"。in bed 为"在床上"；on/at 不符合"睡前"的搭配。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也分享了我的方法。too 放在句末肯定句中表示"也"。also 放句中；either 用于否定句；neither 为"两者都不"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些同学对此感到惊讶——这也可以是一种学习方式。be surprised that... 中 that 引导宾语从句。when/what/how 不如 that 引导陈述事实。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师也指出了一些问题。point out 为固定短语，表示"指出"。point at 为"指向"；point to 为"指向（远处）"；point in 不常用。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** as...as 结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：睡眠和学习一样重要。as important as 表示"和……一样重要"。than 用于比较级；like/with 不符合 as...as 结构。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天晚上九点之前完成作业。before 9 p.m. 表示"九点之前"。after 为"之后"；since 为"自从"；until 为"直到"。故选 B。
