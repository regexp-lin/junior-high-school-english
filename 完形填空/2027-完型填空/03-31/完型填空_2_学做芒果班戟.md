# 中考英语完形填空：学做芒果班戟

## 完形填空原文

My mom loves mango pancake rolls. For her birthday, I decided ___1___ make some as a surprise.

I watched a cooking video and wrote ___2___ the steps. The ingredients were simple: flour, eggs, milk, sugar and fresh mangoes.

First, I mixed the batter in a bowl ___3___ it became smooth. Then I poured a thin layer into a hot pan. It was important to keep the heat ___4___ or the skin would burn.

After the thin pancake cooled down, I whipped the cream ___5___ a hand mixer. The cream became thick and fluffy in just two minutes. I spread it on the pancake ___6___ placed a piece of mango in the center.

The ___7___ part was folding. I tried to wrap it neatly, but the first one broke. I did not give ___8___ and kept trying. By the fourth one, I could fold it perfectly.

I put the rolls in the fridge ___9___ they could stay fresh. When Mom came home and saw them, she was so ___10___ that tears filled her eyes.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. on
2. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. down &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. out
3. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. when
4. A. high &emsp;&emsp; B. low &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. slow
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. easiest &emsp;&emsp; B. hardest &emsp;&emsp; C. fastest &emsp;&emsp; D. slowest
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. off
9. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
10. A. moved &emsp;&emsp; B. scared &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. angry

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定做一些作为惊喜。decide to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示"决定做某事"。for/of/on 不符合 decide 的搭配。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看了烹饪视频并记下了步骤。write down 表示"写下、记录"。write up 为"详细写出"；write off 为"注销"；write out 为"写出完整版"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在碗里搅拌面糊直到变得顺滑。until 表示"直到……为止"。after/before 不如 until 表达"持续搅到某状态"；when 不强调持续性。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：保持低火很重要，否则皮会烧焦。keep the heat low 表示"保持小火"。high 为"高温"会烧焦；fast/slow 修饰速度而非温度。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用手动搅拌器打发奶油。with a hand mixer 表示"用搅拌器"，with 表工具。in/on/by 不如 with 自然表示使用工具。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把奶油涂在饼皮上并在中间放一块芒果。spread 与 placed 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：最难的部分是折叠。后文说"第一个破了"，说明折叠最难，用 hardest。easiest 为"最容易"；fastest/slowest 为"最快/最慢"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我没有放弃并继续尝试。give up 为固定短语，表示"放弃"。give in 为"屈服"；give out 为"分发"；give off 为"发出"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析/目的状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把卷放进冰箱以便保持新鲜。so that 引导目的状语从句。because 表原因；although 表让步；unless 表条件。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈非常感动，眼眶都湿了。moved 表示"感动的"，符合收到惊喜后的情感反应。scared 为"害怕的"；bored 为"无聊的"；angry 为"生气的"。故选 A。
