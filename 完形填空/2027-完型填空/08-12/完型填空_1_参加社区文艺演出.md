# 中考英语完形填空：参加社区文艺演出

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday evening, our community held ___1___ outdoor show to celebrate the summer. My friends and I had practiced a short English song ___2___ two weeks, so we felt both excited and nervous. When it was our turn, the lights on the stage turned ___3___ and the music started.

While singing, I noticed many neighbors ___4___ were waving their hands in the audience. ___5___ mistake, I almost forgot the next line, ___6___ my friend Lily smiled at me and I remembered it at once. After the performance, an old lady came up to us and said, "You sang ___7___ than many professional groups I saw online."

We were tired ___8___ happy that night. On the way home, I thought community activities could make summer ___9___ colorful and help us build confidence. I have already decided ___10___ join the rehearsal for the Mid-Autumn show next month.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. off &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. down
4. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
5. A. By &emsp;&emsp; B. With &emsp;&emsp; C. In &emsp;&emsp; D. On
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. well &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. good
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们社区举办了一场户外演出。outdoor 以元音音素开头，泛指“一场”用 an。a 用于辅音音素开头；the 表特指；零冠词不适用。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词（时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和朋友们已经练了大约两周的英文短歌。for + 时间段表示动作持续多久。since 后多接时间点；in/at 不符合。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语/介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮到我们时，舞台灯光亮起，音乐响起。turn on 表示“打开（灯、设备等）”。turn off 关掉；turn up/down 调大/小。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看见许多邻居在观众席挥手。先行词 neighbors 指人，从句缺主语，用 who。which 指物；whom 作宾语；what 不引导定语从句。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我差点忘了下一句。by mistake 为固定搭配，表示“错误地、不小心地”。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我的朋友莉莉对我笑了笑，我立刻想起来了。前后转折，用 but。and 并列；or 选择；so 结果。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们唱得比我在网上看到的许多专业团体还好。有 than，用比较级 better。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词（转折）
**【解析】** 本句意为：那晚我们又累又开心。tired but happy 表示“累但快乐”，表转折。and 表并列顺承；or/so 不符合。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：社区活动能让暑假更丰富多彩。暗含“更……”，colorful 为多音节形容词，用 more colorful。most 为最高级；much/very 不直接构成比较级。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我已经决定参加下个月中秋演出的排练。decide to do sth. 为固定搭配。故选 A。
