# 中考英语完形填空：学做龟苓膏

## 完形填空原文

My grandma believes that herbal jelly is good ___1___ clearing heat in summer. Yesterday she taught me ___2___ to cook it at home. First, we mixed the powder ___3___ cold water until there were no lumps. "You must stir it slowly, ___4___ it will stick to the bottom of the pot," she warned.

After the liquid boiled, we turned down the heat and waited ___5___ about five minutes. The kitchen ___6___ by a light herbal smell, and I could hardly wait to taste it. When the jelly was poured into a bowl and left to cool, it looked ___7___ a shiny black mirror.

Grandma told me that healthy snacks should ___8___ made with patience. I nodded and promised ___9___ I would practice again next week. The jelly was a little bitter, ___10___ I still loved it because it carried her care.

## 选项

1. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. about
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. when
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. in
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
6. A. filled &emsp;&emsp; B. was filled &emsp;&emsp; C. fills &emsp;&emsp; D. is filling
7. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
8. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. being &emsp;&emsp; C. been &emsp;&emsp; D. to be
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶认为龟苓膏有助于夏天清热。be good for… 表示“对……有好处”。be good at 擅长；be good with 善于应付。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我在家如何做。teach sb. how to do sth. 教某人如何做。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们先把粉末与冷水混合。mix…with… 为固定搭配。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词（否则）
**【解析】** 本句意为：必须慢慢搅拌，否则会粘锅底。or 在此表“否则”。and 并列；but 转折；so 结果。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词（时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：转小火后再等大约五分钟。wait for + 时间段。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 被动语态与时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房里充满了淡淡的草药香。kitchen 与 fill 为被动关系，且叙述过去，用 was filled。filled 缺助动词；主动语态不符。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它看起来像一面黑亮的镜子。look like 看起来像。look as 需接从句；for/of 不搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 被动语态（情态动词后动词原形）
**【解析】** 本句意为：健康的小吃应该耐心制作。should 后接动词原形，被动为 should be made。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我点头并保证下周会再练习。promise 后接陈述意义的 that 从句（that 可省略但选项给出 that）。what/if/how 不符合句意。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：龟苓膏有点苦，但我仍然喜欢，因为里面有她的关爱。转折关系用 but。故选 B。
