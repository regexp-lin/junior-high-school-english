# 中考英语完形填空：学做紫菜包饭

## 完形填空原文

My Korean neighbor Mrs. Park invited me ___1___ learn kimbap on Saturday afternoon. Her kitchen table looked ___2___ a small studio with bowls of rice, vegetables, and shiny seaweed.

She placed a bamboo mat on the table and said, "Spread the rice ___3___, or it will be too thick." I followed her hands and tried ___4___ my best.

We added strips of egg, carrot, and pickled radish. "Roll gently and squeeze ___5___," she coached. My first roll looked uneven, ___6___ it still held together.

Mrs. Park cut the roll into pieces and arranged them ___7___ a plate. The colors looked bright ___8___ spring on a dish.

We shared tea and talked about school life. I thanked her ___9___ the lesson, and she smiled, "Food is a friendly language ___10___ opens doors."

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
3. A. thinly &emsp;&emsp; B. thin &emsp;&emsp; C. thinner &emsp;&emsp; D. thinnest
4. A. me &emsp;&emsp; B. my &emsp;&emsp; C. mine &emsp;&emsp; D. myself
5. A. light &emsp;&emsp; B. lightly &emsp;&emsp; C. lighter &emsp;&emsp; D. lightest
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
8. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. that

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：邻居邀请我学做紫菜包饭。invite sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配。for/of/with 不符合 invite 结构。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的餐桌像一个小工作室。look like 表示“看起来像”。look as 多接从句；for/of 不搭配 look。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把米饭薄薄地摊开。修饰动词 spread 用副词 thinly。thin 为形容词；thinner/thinnest 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我照她的手法尽力去做。try one's best 为固定搭配，主语为 I，用 my。me/mine/myself 不符合搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轻轻卷并轻轻捏紧。修饰动词 squeeze 用副词 lightly。light 为形容词；lighter/lightest 比较级此处不必要。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一卷看起来不均匀，但仍没散。转折关系用 but。or/and/so 不符合。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把寿司摆在盘子上。on a plate 表示“在盘子上”。in/at/to 不如 on 贴切摆盘。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：颜色像盘子上的春天一样鲜亮。like spring on a dish 用介词 like 表比喻。as 多接从句；for/of 不自然。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感谢她的指导。thank sb. for sth. 为固定搭配。to/with/on 不符合 thank 结构。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：食物是一门能打开门的友好语言。先行词 language 指物，关系词在从句中作主语，用 that/which。选项中 that 正确。what 不引导定语从句；who 指人。故选 D。
