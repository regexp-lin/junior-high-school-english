# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工拓印

## 完形填空原文

Our art teacher introduced a special technique called "leaf printing" in class today. It is a way of making art ___1___ real leaves and paint. I had never heard of it before and felt very curious.

The teacher asked us ___2___ some fresh leaves from the school garden. I picked several leaves of different shapes — round ones, long ones, and even a maple leaf ___3___ looked like a little hand.

Back in the classroom, we spread newspaper on our desks to keep them ___4___. Then the teacher showed us how to brush paint evenly ___5___ one side of a leaf. "Use just enough paint — not too much ___6___ too little," she reminded us.

I placed the painted leaf face ___7___ on a piece of white paper and pressed gently. When I peeled the leaf away, a perfect print with every tiny vein appeared! It was much ___8___ beautiful than I expected.

I made five prints using different colors and arranged them ___9___ a circle. The teacher said my work showed great creativity. She hung it on the wall for everyone ___10___ see. I was so proud!

## 选项

1. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. to use &emsp;&emsp; C. using &emsp;&emsp; D. used
2. A. collect &emsp;&emsp; B. collected &emsp;&emsp; C. collecting &emsp;&emsp; D. to collect
3. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. where
4. A. clean &emsp;&emsp; B. clearly &emsp;&emsp; C. cleaning &emsp;&emsp; D. cleaned
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. by
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. down &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. off
8. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. more
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. of

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（现在分词/动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是一种用真实的叶子和颜料来制作艺术品的方法。using 作方式状语，表示"通过使用"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们从校园花园里采集一些新鲜的叶子。ask sb. to do sth. 表示"要求某人做某事"。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系代词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：甚至还有一片枫叶，看起来像一只小手。先行词为 a maple leaf（物），在从句中作主语，用 which。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词作宾补
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在桌上铺了报纸来保持桌面干净。keep + 宾语 + adj.（补语），clean 表示"干净的"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师教我们如何在叶子的一面均匀地刷上颜料。on one side of 表示"在……的一面上"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要太多也不要太少。not too much or too little，or 用于否定句中连接并列成分，表示"也不"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把涂了颜料的一面朝下放在白纸上。face down 表示"朝下"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词修饰比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：比我期望的还要漂亮得多。much/more 修饰比较级 more beautiful；此处 more 已构成比较级，用 much 加强语气。但选项中为 more beautiful than，故 D 与 beautiful 构成 more beautiful。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把它们排列成一个圆圈。in a circle 表示"成圆形排列"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定式表目的
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把它挂在墙上让大家看。for everyone to see 为"for + 宾语 + to do"结构，表示"为了让某人做某事"。故选 C。
