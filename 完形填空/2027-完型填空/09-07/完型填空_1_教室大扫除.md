# 中考英语完形填空：教室大扫除

## 完形填空原文

Every Friday afternoon, our class has a general cleaning time. Last Friday, the teacher divided us ___1___ four groups, and each group was responsible for a different area.

My group was assigned to clean the windows. They were so ___2___ that we could hardly see through them. I filled a bucket with warm water and added a little soap. We started wiping the glass with wet cloths, and the dirt came ___3___ easily.

Wang Hua, the tallest boy in our group, volunteered to clean the ___4___ windows. He stood on a chair and reached as high ___5___ he could. I held the chair steady for him because I was worried he might ___6___.

After an hour of hard work, the classroom looked completely ___7___. The windows were so clean that sunlight poured in brightly. The floor was swept and mopped until it ___8___.

The teacher walked in, looked around, and gave us a big thumbs-up. "Great teamwork!" she said ___9___. We all felt tired but ___10___. A clean environment really makes studying more comfortable.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with
2. A. dirty &emsp;&emsp; B. clean &emsp;&emsp; C. bright &emsp;&emsp; D. dark
3. A. out &emsp;&emsp; B. off &emsp;&emsp; C. down &emsp;&emsp; D. up
4. A. low &emsp;&emsp; B. lower &emsp;&emsp; C. high &emsp;&emsp; D. higher
5. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. as
6. A. drop &emsp;&emsp; B. fall &emsp;&emsp; C. fly &emsp;&emsp; D. jump
7. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. similar &emsp;&emsp; C. different &emsp;&emsp; D. common
8. A. shine &emsp;&emsp; B. shines &emsp;&emsp; C. shone &emsp;&emsp; D. shining
9. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. prouder &emsp;&emsp; C. proudly &emsp;&emsp; D. pride
10. A. satisfy &emsp;&emsp; B. satisfied &emsp;&emsp; C. satisfying &emsp;&emsp; D. satisfaction

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师把我们分成四组。divide ... into ... 表示"把……分成……"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：窗户太脏了，我们几乎看不透。由后文"擦玻璃""污垢容易脱落"可知窗户很脏。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：污垢很容易就被擦掉了。come off 表示"脱落、掉落"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最高的男生主动去擦高处的窗户。由后文"站在椅子上尽力够高处"可知是高处窗户，用 high。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（as ... as）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他尽可能地够到最高处。as high as he could 表示"尽可能高地"。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我帮他扶稳椅子，因为我担心他会掉下来。fall 表示"摔倒、掉下"，符合语境。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：经过一个小时的努力，教室看起来完全不同了。打扫前后对比，用 different。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：地板被扫了又拖，直到闪闪发光。叙述过去的事，用一般过去时 shone。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她自豪地说。修饰动词 said，需用副词 proudly。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（-ed 与 -ing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都感到累但满足。felt 为系动词，后接形容词；satisfied 表示"（人）感到满意的"。故选 B。
