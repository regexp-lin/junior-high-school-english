# 中考英语完形填空：参加班级篮球赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a basketball competition between classes last week. Our class team had been ___1___ for it every afternoon for two weeks.

On the day of the match, the gym was full of students who came to cheer. We played ___2___ Class Three, one of the strongest teams in our grade. I felt both nervous and excited.

The game started quickly. Class Three scored the first basket, ___3___ made the score 0–2. Our captain, Chen Hao, told us to stay calm and focus ___4___ defense. Slowly, we caught up.

In the second half, I got the ball near the three-point line. Without ___5___, I shot the ball. It went straight into the basket! The crowd went wild. That was my ___6___ three-pointer in an official game.

With only one minute ___7___, the score was tied. Chen Hao drove to the basket and made a beautiful layup. The final score was 38–36. We had won!

Our classmates rushed onto the court and hugged us tightly. The head teacher praised us and said, "Your success comes from teamwork, not just ___8___ talent."

After the game, I was too tired ___9___. But lying in bed that night, I replayed every moment in my mind. I realized that working hard together is more ___10___ than winning itself.

## 选项

1. A. practice &emsp;&emsp; B. practiced &emsp;&emsp; C. practicing &emsp;&emsp; D. practices  
2. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. against  
3. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
5. A. hesitate &emsp;&emsp; B. hesitated &emsp;&emsp; C. hesitating &emsp;&emsp; D. hesitation  
6. A. one &emsp;&emsp; B. first &emsp;&emsp; C. once &emsp;&emsp; D. a  
7. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. leaves &emsp;&emsp; C. leaving &emsp;&emsp; D. left  
8. A. person &emsp;&emsp; B. persons &emsp;&emsp; C. personal &emsp;&emsp; D. individual  
9. A. move &emsp;&emsp; B. moving &emsp;&emsp; C. to move &emsp;&emsp; D. moved  
10. A. meaning &emsp;&emsp; B. meaningful &emsp;&emsp; C. meaningfully &emsp;&emsp; D. meanings  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去完成进行时  
**【解析】** had been practicing 构成过去完成进行时，表示"一直在练习"。practice 为原形；practiced 与 had been 不搭配；practices 为三单。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词 against 表对抗  
**【解析】** play against 意为"与……对抗/比赛"。play with 意为"和……一起玩"；for 表为……效力；to 不与 play 构成比赛义。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非限制性定语从句  
**【解析】** 先行词为前面整个句子"三班先得分"，从句缺主语，用 which 引导非限制性定语从句。that 不引导非限制性定语从句；who 指人；what 不引导定语从句。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 focus on  
**【解析】** focus on 意为"专注于"，on 为固定介词。in/at/to 不与 focus 构成此搭配。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词 without + 名词  
**【解析】** without hesitation 意为"毫不犹豫地"，without 为介词后接名词。hesitate 为动词；hesitated 为过去式；hesitating 虽为动名词可接但 hesitation 为更地道的搭配。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 序数词 first  
**【解析】** my first three-pointer 意为"我的第一个三分球"，first 为序数词。one 为基数词不表"第一"；once 意为"一次/曾经"；a 为不定冠词不表顺序。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** with + 名词 + 过去分词（独立主格）  
**【解析】** with only one minute left 意为"只剩一分钟"，left 为过去分词表被动状态（时间被留下）。leave 为原形；leaves 为三单；leaving 为现在分词（与 minute 不构成主动关系）。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词  
**【解析】** personal talent 意为"个人天赋"，personal 为形容词修饰 talent。person 为名词；persons 为名词复数；individual 虽可作形容词但 personal 更常见于此搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** too...to... 结构  
**【解析】** too tired to move 意为"太累了以至于动不了"，too + adj. + to do 为固定结构。move 缺 to；moving 为现在分词；moved 为过去式。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（more + 多音节形容词）  
**【解析】** more meaningful than 意为"比……更有意义"，meaningful 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more。meaning 为名词；meaningfully 为副词；meanings 为名词复数。故选 B。
