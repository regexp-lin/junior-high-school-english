# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸灯笼

## 完形填空原文

In Chinese culture, lanterns are symbols of ___1___ and good luck. Our Chinese teacher organized a lantern-making activity to help us understand traditional culture better.

She brought red and gold paper, scissors, glue, and thin bamboo strips. Before we started, she ___2___ us a video about how lanterns were made in ancient times. I was amazed at how skilled those craftsmen were.

"Today, we will make a ___3___ version," she said. "But the spirit is the same — every lantern carries good wishes." Her words touched ___4___ deeply.

We followed her instructions step by step. First, we cut the red paper into strips and folded them into a round shape. Then we glued the bamboo strips ___5___ a frame to hold the paper in place.

The ___6___ part was attaching the tassel at the bottom. My tassel kept falling off because the glue wasn't strong ___7___. A classmate suggested using a small stapler instead, and it worked perfectly.

When all the lanterns were done, we hung ___8___ in the classroom. The red lanterns swayed gently in the breeze, and the whole room looked warm and festive. Our teacher took a photo and posted it on the school website.

She said this was not ___9___ a craft activity but also a way to pass down culture. I wrote down her words in my notebook because I thought they were very ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. happier &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. happiness  
2. A. show &emsp;&emsp; B. showed &emsp;&emsp; C. shown &emsp;&emsp; D. showing  
3. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. simply &emsp;&emsp; C. simpler &emsp;&emsp; D. simplest  
4. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  
5. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. into  
6. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. hardest &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly  
7. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. enough &emsp;&emsp; D. quite  
8. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. they  
9. A. only &emsp;&emsp; B. just &emsp;&emsp; C. merely &emsp;&emsp; D. simply  
10. A. mean &emsp;&emsp; B. meaning &emsp;&emsp; C. meaningful &emsp;&emsp; D. meaningfully  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词 of 后接名词  
**【解析】** symbols of happiness 意为"幸福的象征"，of 后接名词 happiness（幸福）。happy 为形容词；happier 为比较级形容词；happily 为副词。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，showed 为 show 的过去式。show 为原形；shown 为过去分词（需与 had/has 搭配）；showing 为现在分词。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** a simpler version 意为"一个更简单的版本"，与古代制作方式相比较，用比较级 simpler。simple 为原级；simply 为副词；simplest 为最高级。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** touched 为及物动词，后接宾格 us 作宾语。we 为主格；our 为形容词性物主代词；ours 为名词性物主代词。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 as 表"作为"  
**【解析】** glue the bamboo strips as a frame 意为"把竹条粘作框架"，as 表"作为"。for 表目的；like 意为"像"；into 表变化。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** the hardest part 意为"最难的部分"，前有定冠词 the，用最高级。hard 为原级；harder 为比较级；hardly 为副词意为"几乎不"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** enough 的位置  
**【解析】** strong enough 意为"足够牢固"，enough 修饰形容词时后置。too 意为"太"含消极义但句中已有否定 wasn't；very 表程度但不表"足够"；quite 意为"相当"不合此义。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格（复数）  
**【解析】** hung 为及物动词，宾语指代前文的 lanterns（复数），用宾格 them。it 为单数；their 为形容词性物主代词；they 为主格。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** not only...but also... 结构  
**【解析】** not only...but also... 意为"不仅……而且……"，only 与后文 but also 呼应。just/merely/simply 虽也有"仅仅"之义，但与 but also 的固定搭配为 not only。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语  
**【解析】** were very meaningful 意为"非常有意义"，meaningful 为形容词作表语。mean 为动词"意味着"；meaning 为名词"意义"；meaningfully 为副词。故选 C。
