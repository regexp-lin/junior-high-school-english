# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工树叶画

## 完形填空原文

After our walk in the park, I collected fallen leaves ___1___ wanted to keep the colors. Grandma taught me ___2___ press them between thick books for two days. Then we glued the leaves onto paper ___3___ looked like a golden hillside.

"You can use a thin brush ___4___ add a few birds," she suggested. I tried my best, ___5___ the birds looked more like clouds. We laughed ___6___ and changed the title to "Autumn Wind".

The artwork ___7___ on the wall near the window, ___8___ sunlight could reach it easily. Neighbors said it was ___9___ creative idea they had seen this month. I learned ___10___ beauty can be saved from ordinary things.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. where
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
4. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
6. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. louder &emsp;&emsp; D. loudest
7. A. hang &emsp;&emsp; B. hung &emsp;&emsp; C. was hung &emsp;&emsp; D. hanged
8. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the most &emsp;&emsp; D. more
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. which

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：散步后我捡了落叶，并想把颜色保存下来。并列顺承用 and。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶教我如何把叶子夹在厚书里压两天。teach sb. how to do sth. 故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们把叶子粘在纸上，看起来像金色的山坡。先行词 paper 指物，从句缺主语，用 that。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语表目的
**【解析】** 本句意为：你可以用细毛笔再添几只鸟。in order to 为了，表目的，后接动词原形。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尽力了，但那些鸟看起来更像云。表转折用 but。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们大声笑起来并把标题改成“秋风”。修饰动词 laughed，用 loudly。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：作品被挂在靠窗的墙上……。artwork 与 hang（悬挂）为被动，过去式 hung，被动用 was hung。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：……在那里阳光很容易照到它。先行词 the wall 表地点，从句结构完整，用 where。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：邻居说这是他们本月见过的最有创意的点子之一。this month 作范围提示，用 the most creative。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了美可以从平凡事物中被保存下来。learned 后接 that 引导宾语从句。故选 B。
