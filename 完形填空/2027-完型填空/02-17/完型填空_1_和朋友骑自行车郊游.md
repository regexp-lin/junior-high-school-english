# 中考英语完形填空：和朋友骑自行车郊游

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, Jack and I planned a bike trip along the river park. The weather report said it would be sunny, ___1___ we packed water, snacks, and a small first-aid kit.

We started early to avoid the crowds. The road was smooth, ___2___ the wind felt fresh on our faces. We stopped twice ___3___ photos of birds and bridges.

At noon, we rested under a tree and shared sandwiches. Jack told funny stories, ___4___ I laughed until my stomach hurt. Then we checked our bikes ___5___ riding again.

On the way back, my chain suddenly came off. I tried to fix it, ___6___ my hands were dirty and oily. Jack stayed calm and helped me. Twenty minutes later, we were ___7___ the road again.

When I got home, I was tired ___8___ happy. That trip taught me that a good friend is someone who stays ___9___ you when small problems appear. I can't wait ___10___ our next ride.

## 选项

1. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. although
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. to take &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. took
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
5. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. since
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. against
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：天气预报说晴天，所以我们带了水、零食和小急救包。因果关系，用 so。or 或者；but 但是；although 尽管。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：路面平坦，微风拂面很清新。并列顺承，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（stop to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们停下来两次去拍鸟和桥的照片。stop to do sth. 停下来去做另一件事。stop doing 停止正在做的事。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：杰克讲有趣的故事，所以我笑到肚子疼。因果关系，用 so。because 因为；if 如果；unless 除非。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们在再次骑行前检查自行车。before 在……之前。after 在……之后；while 当……时；since 自从。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我试着修理，但手又脏又油。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：二十分钟后我们又上路了。on the road 在路上。in 在……里；at 在；by 通过。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回到家我又累又开心。tired but happy 累但开心，转折。and 也可表并列，但此处强调对比，用 but 更贴切。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好朋友是在小问题出现时仍陪伴你的人。stay with sb. 和某人待在一起。for 为了；about 关于；against 反对。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我等不及下一次骑行。can't wait for sth. 迫不及待（接名词）；can't wait to do 接动词。此处 next ride 为名词短语，用 for。故选 B。
