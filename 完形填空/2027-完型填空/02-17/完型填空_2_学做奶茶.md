# 中考英语完形填空：学做奶茶

## 完形填空原文

Bubble tea shops are popular, ___1___ my sister wanted to learn to make milk tea at home. We watched a video and wrote down the steps.

We boiled water and brewed black tea. The kitchen smelled strong, ___2___ my sister opened a window. Then we mixed milk and a little sugar. The video said we should stir ___3___ the sugar disappeared.

The hardest part was cooking pearls. We poured them into boiling water and waited. I left to answer a phone call, ___4___ I forgot the time. When I returned, some pearls stuck ___5___ the bottom of the pot.

We laughed and tried again. The second batch was soft ___6___ chewy. We poured tea into two cups and added ice. The taste was not ___7___ the shop's, but it was fresh and less sweet.

Cleaning the pot took time, ___8___ we didn't mind. Making drinks at home is cheaper and ___9___. Next time, we will try fruit tea ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. although
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. although
5. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. as same as &emsp;&emsp; B. the same as &emsp;&emsp; C. same as &emsp;&emsp; D. same like
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. healthiest &emsp;&emsp; B. healthier &emsp;&emsp; C. healthy &emsp;&emsp; D. health
10. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. either &emsp;&emsp; C. also &emsp;&emsp; D. neither

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶茶店很流行，所以我姐姐想在家学做奶茶。因果关系，用 so。or 或者；but 但是；although 尽管。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房味道很重，所以我姐姐打开窗户。so 所以。and 并列；or 或者；but 但是。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：视频说我们要搅拌直到糖融化。until 直到。if 如果；unless 除非；although 尽管。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我去接电话，所以我忘了时间。因果关系，用 so。because 因为；if 如果；although 尽管。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词短语（stick to）
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些珍珠粘在锅底。stick to the bottom 粘在底部。stick on 也可表粘附，但 stick to 更常见。in 在……里；at 在。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二批珍珠又软又有嚼劲。并列形容词，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道和店里不完全一样。the same as 与……相同，为固定搭配。as same as 语序错误；same as 缺 the；same like 不标准。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：洗锅花时间，但我们不介意。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：在家做饮料更便宜也更健康。and 连接两个比较级 cheaper 与 healthier。healthiest 最高级；healthy 原级；health 名词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次我们也会尝试水果茶。肯定句句末表示“也”用 too；either 用于否定句句末；also 通常用于句中；neither 表示“也不”。故选 A。
