# 中考英语完形填空：除夕大扫除

## 完形填空原文

The day before Chinese New Year, our family began a big cleaning ___1___ dawn. My father pushed the heavy sofa ___2___ the wall so that he could sweep behind it. Dust rose in the weak winter light, and my mother handed me a ___3___ cloth to wipe the windows.

She also asked me ___4___ old magazines and sort the books on the shelf. I felt a little tired, ___5___ I understood the tradition: a clean house is said to sweep away bad luck. I carried warm water ___6___ the kitchen to the living room and started mopping.

While I worked, I ___7___ a forgotten red envelope under the chair. Everyone laughed and called it a lucky surprise. ___8___ dinner time, every room looked bright and smelled fresh. My grandmother smiled and said a tidy home welcomes a happy new year. That evening I went to bed ___9___ than usual, feeling both ___10___ and proud of what we had done together.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
2. A. against &emsp;&emsp; B. among &emsp;&emsp; C. through &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
3. A. dry &emsp;&emsp; B. wet &emsp;&emsp; C. cool &emsp;&emsp; D. soft
4. A. throw away &emsp;&emsp; B. to throw away &emsp;&emsp; C. throwing away &emsp;&emsp; D. threw away
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. because
6. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. into &emsp;&emsp; C. across &emsp;&emsp; D. above
7. A. invented &emsp;&emsp; B. discovered &emsp;&emsp; C. created &emsp;&emsp; D. imagined
8. A. By &emsp;&emsp; B. Since &emsp;&emsp; C. During &emsp;&emsp; D. Until
9. A. early &emsp;&emsp; B. earlier &emsp;&emsp; C. earliest &emsp;&emsp; D. the earliest
10. A. sleep &emsp;&emsp; B. sleepy &emsp;&emsp; C. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; D. slept

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们家在黎明时分开始大扫除。at dawn 为固定搭配，表示“在黎明时”。A 项 on 用于具体某天早晨；C 项 in 多与 the morning 连用；D 项 by 表示“到……为止”。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父亲把沉重的沙发推到墙边。against the wall 表示“靠着墙”。B 项 among 在……之中；C 项 through 穿过；D 项 beyond 超出。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：母亲递给我一块湿抹布擦窗户。擦窗常用湿布。A 项 dry 干的；C 项 cool 凉的；D 项 soft 软的，均不如 wet 贴切。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她还让我扔掉旧杂志并整理书架。ask sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配。A、C、D 均不能直接跟在 asked me 后作宾补。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点累，但我理解这一传统。前后为转折关系，用 but。A 项 or 否则；B 项 so 所以；D 项 because 因为。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从厨房把温水端到客厅。from...to... 表示“从……到……”。B 项 into 进入；C 项 across 穿过；D 项 above 在……上方。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：干活时我发现椅子下面有一个被遗忘的红包。discover 强调“发现”已存在之物。A 项 invent 发明；C 项 create 创造；D 项 imagine 想象。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到晚饭时，每个房间都明亮整洁。by dinner time 表示“到晚饭时为止”。B 项 Since 自从；C 项 During 在……期间，后接名词短语但不如 by 自然表“到那时”；D 项 Until 直到。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天晚上我比平时更早睡觉。than 提示用比较级 earlier。A 项 early 原级；C、D 为最高级。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（词性）
**【解析】** 本句意为：既困倦又为一起完成的事自豪。feel 后接形容词作表语，sleepy 困倦的。A 项 sleep 动词/名词；C 项 sleeping 多作定语或动名词；D 项 slept 为过去式。故选 B。
