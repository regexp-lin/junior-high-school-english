# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工果篮

## 完形填空原文

Our art teacher planned a lesson ___1___ we could use recycled paper. She brought colored strips and showed us ___2___ weave them into a small basket. At first, my fingers were stiff, ___3___ I practiced for twenty minutes.

"Try ___4___ hold the base more tightly," she suggested. The basket I made wasn't ___5___ beautiful as hers, ___6___ I still liked it. When I put apples inside, the basket ___7___ heavier, but it didn't break.

My classmates' works were displayed ___8___ the classroom wall. Visitors said these baskets were ___9___ creative gifts they had ever seen. I realized that with patience, even simple materials ___10___ turned into something special.

## 选项

1. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. when
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. where
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. but
5. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. such &emsp;&emsp; C. as &emsp;&emsp; D. more
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. if
7. A. become &emsp;&emsp; B. became &emsp;&emsp; C. becomes &emsp;&emsp; D. becoming
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the most &emsp;&emsp; D. more
10. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. could &emsp;&emsp; C. must &emsp;&emsp; D. should

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的美术老师安排了一节课，在这节课上我们可以使用再生纸。先行词 a lesson 表抽象地点，从句结构完整，用 where（= in which）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”
**【解析】** 本句意为：她带来彩色纸条并示范如何把它们编成小花篮。show sb. how to do sth. 故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我的手指很僵硬，但我练习了二十分钟。表转折用 but。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配 try to do
**【解析】** 本句意为：尽量把底部握得更紧一点。try to do sth. 努力/尽量做某事。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定结构 as...as...
**【解析】** 本句意为：我做的篮子不如她的那么漂亮……。not as/so + 形容词 + as 不如……。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：……但我仍然喜欢它。前后转折，用 but。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我放进苹果时，篮子变得更重了……。叙述过去，用 became。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们的作品被展示在教室的墙上。on the wall 在墙面上。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：参观者说这些篮子是他们见过的最有创意的礼物之一。they had ever seen 表范围，用 the most creative。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 情态动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到只要有耐心，即便简单的材料也能变成特别的东西。主句 realized 为过去时，从句表“能够”，用 could。故选 B。
