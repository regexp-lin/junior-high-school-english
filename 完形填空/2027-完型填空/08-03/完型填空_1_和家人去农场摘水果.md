# 中考英语完形填空：和家人去农场摘水果

## 完形填空原文

Early one morning, we visited a farm ___1___ the hills. The farmer, ___2___ name was Uncle Zhou, gave us each a basket. He said the peaches ___3___ picked when they felt a little soft.

My brother ran ___4___ than me to the orchard, ___5___ he wanted the biggest ones. I followed slowly because I was busy ___6___ photos of butterflies. Uncle Zhou told us ___7___ climb the trees because it was dangerous.

At noon, we paid ___8___ the fruit by weight. Mom thought the price was ___9___ than in the supermarket, but the taste was fresher. On the way home, Dad promised ___10___ us there again next month.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
2. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. whom &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. which
3. A. should be &emsp;&emsp; B. should &emsp;&emsp; C. are &emsp;&emsp; D. were
4. A. fast &emsp;&emsp; B. faster &emsp;&emsp; C. fastest &emsp;&emsp; D. more fast
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
6. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. to take &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. took
7. A. to not &emsp;&emsp; B. not to &emsp;&emsp; C. don't &emsp;&emsp; D. not
8. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. about
9. A. high &emsp;&emsp; B. higher &emsp;&emsp; C. highest &emsp;&emsp; D. highly
10. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. taking &emsp;&emsp; C. to take &emsp;&emsp; D. took

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一天清晨，我们参观了山里的一个农场。in the hills 在山丘/山区一带，为常见表达。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：那位农民名叫周叔叔……。先行词 The farmer，从句中 name 缺定语，用 whose。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 被动语态 / 情态动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说桃子摸起来略软时就应该被采摘。peaches 与 pick 为被动关系，且表建议，用 should be picked。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我弟弟跑得比我快……。than 提示用比较级 faster。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：……因为他想要最大的那些。表原因用 because。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be busy doing
**【解析】** 本句意为：我慢慢跟着，因为我正忙着给蝴蝶拍照。be busy doing sth. 忙于做某事。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式的否定）
**【解析】** 本句意为：周叔叔告诉我们不要爬树，因为危险。tell sb. not to do sth. 告诉某人不要做某事。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配 pay for
**【解析】** 本句意为：中午我们按重量付钱买水果。pay for 为……付款。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈觉得价格比超市更高……。than 提示用比较级 higher 描述价格。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家路上爸爸答应下个月再带我们去那里。promise to do sth. 答应做某事。故选 C。
