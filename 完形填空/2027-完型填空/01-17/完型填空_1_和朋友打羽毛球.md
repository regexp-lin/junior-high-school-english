# 中考英语完形填空：和朋友打羽毛球

## 完形填空原文

On Saturday morning, Lily called me and asked ___1___ I could go to the sports center with her. I hesitated because I was not good at badminton, ___2___ she said we could just play for fun. I finally agreed and put on my sports shoes.

When we arrived, the hall was already ___3___. We found an empty court and started warming up. At first, I missed the shuttlecock many times and felt a little ___4___. Lily did not laugh at me. Instead, she showed me how to hold the racket ___5___ and move my feet quickly.

After thirty minutes, I could hit the shuttlecock ___6___ than before. We took a short break and drank some water. Though we were tired, we kept ___7___ because the game felt exciting. On the way home, I thanked Lily ___8___ her patience.

That day I realized friendship is not ___9___ winning. It is about encouraging each other. Next weekend, I will ___10___ her again and practice more.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. dark &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
4. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
5. A. loudly &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. politely &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
6. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. more often &emsp;&emsp; D. more slowly
7. A. stopping &emsp;&emsp; B. playing &emsp;&emsp; C. arguing &emsp;&emsp; D. leaving
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
9. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. against &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. beyond
10. A. refuse &emsp;&emsp; B. miss &emsp;&emsp; C. invite &emsp;&emsp; D. follow

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：Lily 打电话问我“是否”能和她去体育中心。A 项 what 什么；B 项 when 何时；C 项 if 是否；D 项 how 如何。asked if I could... 表示请求/询问能否做某事。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不太会打羽毛球，“但”她说我们只是打着玩。前后为转折关系。A 项 and 并列；B 项 but 转折；C 项 or 选择；D 项 so 结果。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们到的时候，球馆已经很“热闹/忙碌”。A 项 dark 暗；B 项 quiet 安静；C 项 busy 繁忙、人多；D 项 cold 冷。与后文找空场地呼应。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我多次打空，感到有点紧张/不安。A 项 proud 自豪；B 项 nervous 紧张；C 项 surprised 惊讶；D 项 bored 无聊。初学者失误多会紧张。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她示范如何“仔细地”握拍。A 项 loudly 大声；B 项 carefully 仔细地；C 项 politely 礼貌地；D 项 slowly 慢慢地。教动作强调认真示范。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词/比较结构辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：半小时后，我比以前“更经常”打到球。A 项 worse 更糟；B 项 less 更少；C 项 more often 更频繁；D 项 more slowly 更慢。进步应体现命中次数增加。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然累，我们仍继续“打”。keep doing sth. 表示继续做。A 项 stopping 停止；B 项 playing 进行比赛/玩耍；C 项 arguing 争吵；D 项 leaving 离开。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我因她的耐心向她道谢。thank sb. for sth. 为固定搭配。B 项 to 常接 thank you to... 结构不同；C、D 项不搭配 thank。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：友谊并不“在于”输赢。be about 表示“关于；在于”。B 项 against 反对；C 项 without 没有；D 项 beyond 超出。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下周末我会再“邀请”她一起练。A 项 refuse 拒绝；B 项 miss 想念/错过；C 项 invite 邀请；D 项 follow 跟随。主动约朋友用 invite 更贴切。故选 C。
