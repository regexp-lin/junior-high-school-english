# 中考英语完形填空：第一次学游泳的回忆

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, my father took me to the swimming pool for the first time. I stood ___1___ the shallow end and stared at the blue water. My legs shook ___2___ because I was afraid of going under.

Dad put a bright float around my arms and said, "Trust me. Take a deep breath ___3___ you try." His voice sounded calm, ___4___ my heart still beat fast. I held the edge of the pool and moved one step forward.

The water felt cool ___5___ gentle on my skin. Little by little, I learned to kick my legs and blow bubbles. ___6___ I fell down once, Dad lifted me up ___7___ and told me mistakes were normal. By the end of the lesson, I could swim a few meters ___8___ the float.

Now when I think about that day, I feel thankful. Courage does not mean you are ___9___ afraid; it means you still move forward ___10___ fear. That lesson helps me in school, too.

## 选项

1. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. in
2. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. heavily &emsp;&emsp; D. clearly
3. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. or
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

7. A. angrily &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. sadly &emsp;&emsp; D. secretly

10. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. beyond &emsp;&emsp; D. except

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我站在浅水区“旁边/一侧”。stand by the shallow end 表示在池边浅水区旁。A 项 at 也可表位置，但 by 更强调“在边缘旁”；B 项 on 在水面上不合理；D 项 in 已入水，与后文 stared 矛盾。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的腿抖得很厉害，因为我害怕沉下去。shake heavily 表示抖得厉害。A 项 slowly 慢慢抖，不符合害怕；B 项 quietly 腿不会安静抖；D 项 clearly 不修饰 shake。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在尝试之前先深呼吸。A 项 after 之后；B 项 before 在……之前；C 项 because 因为；D 项 unless 除非。安全提示应先呼吸再尝试。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的声音很平静，“但”我的心仍跳得很快。转折关系用 but。A 项 so 结果；B 项 and 并列；D 项 or 选择。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：水摸起来凉爽“而且”温柔。cool 与 gentle 为并列感受，用 and 连接。A 项 or 表选择；C 项 but 表转折，此处无转折；D 项 so 表结果。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：“当”我摔倒一次时，爸爸把我扶起来。A 项 If 如果；B 项 Although 尽管；C 项 When 当……时；D 项 Until 直到。叙述一次具体经历用 when。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸“迅速地”把我扶起。A 项 angrily 生气地；B 项 quickly 快速地；C 项 sadly 悲伤地；D 项 secretly 秘密地。救人扶起应迅速。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：课程结束时，我能“不用”浮板游几米。A 项 without 没有；B 项 through 穿过；C 项 above 在上方；D 项 against 对抗。进步体现为脱离浮板。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：勇敢并不意味着你“从不”害怕。A 项 always 总是；B 项 never 从不；C 项 seldom 很少；D 项 hardly 几乎不。not never afraid 双重否定易混，本句为 Courage does not mean you are never afraid 错——等等 I made an error!
