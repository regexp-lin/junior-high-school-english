# 中考英语完形填空：参加社区迎新年联欢会

## 完形填空原文

Last night, our community held a party ___1___ welcome the new year. My neighbors practiced singing and dancing for weeks, ___2___ the show looked amazing. I arrived at the hall with my parents at seven, and many volunteers were busy ___3___ chairs for the elderly. A host welcomed everyone ___4___ a warm speech. Then children performed a short play ___5___ made the audience laugh loudly. During the break, I met an old friend ___6___ I hadn’t seen for months. We talked about school life and took a selfie together. ___7___ the party ended, we joined a countdown and shouted “Happy New Year!” I went home with a gift ___8___ was prepared by the community center. I realized ___9___ important it is to stay connected with neighbors. Next year, I hope ___10___ on stage as a volunteer, too.

## 选项

1. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. so that &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
3. A. move &emsp;&emsp; B. to move &emsp;&emsp; C. moving &emsp;&emsp; D. moved  
4. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. who  
7. A. When &emsp;&emsp; B. While &emsp;&emsp; C. Before &emsp;&emsp; D. Since  
8. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. whose  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too  
10. A. stand &emsp;&emsp; B. standing &emsp;&emsp; C. to stand &emsp;&emsp; D. stood  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定式表目的 in order to do  
**【解析】** “举办聚会是为了迎接新年”，表目的用 in order to welcome。so that 后接从句；because/although 表原因/让步，不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 so 表结果  
**【解析】** “邻居们练习数周，所以演出看起来很棒”，因果关系用 so。but 转折；or 选择；unless 条件，不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** be busy doing sth.  
**【解析】** were busy moving chairs 表示正忙着搬椅子。be busy to do 非标准搭配；move/moved 结构不对。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表伴随（with a warm speech）  
**【解析】** welcome everyone with a speech 表示“用一段热情的致辞欢迎”。for/by/on 与 speech 搭配不如 with 自然。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词 which 指代物（a short play）  
**【解析】** play 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。what 不引导定语从句；who/whom 指人。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词 whom 指人并作宾语（作 see 的宾语）  
**【解析】** an old friend whom I hadn’t seen for months 中，先行词为人，从句中 I hadn’t seen 缺宾语，正式书面语用 whom。who 多作主语；which 指物；what 不引导定语从句。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句 when（当……时）  
**【解析】** “当聚会结束时”，when 引导时间点从句。while 多接延续性动词；before/since 与句意不符。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句 which 指代物（a gift）  
**【解析】** a gift which was prepared… 先行词为物，从句缺主语用 which。who/whom 指人；whose 表所属，不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句中 how 修饰形容词 important  
**【解析】** realized how important it is… 为感叹意义的宾语从句。what 后接名词短语；very/too 不能引导从句。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** hope to do sth.  
**【解析】** hope to stand on stage 不定式作宾语。hope 后不接 hope doing 或接双宾 hope sb. to do（无此结构），不能 hope stand/stood。故选 C。
