# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工红灯笼

## 完形填空原文

Making a red paper lantern is a tradition ___1___ many families enjoy before the Spring Festival. This year, my aunt taught my cousin and me ___2___ make one step by step. First, we cut red paper into strips and glued them ___3___ a round frame. My cousin was faster ___4___ me, but his lantern broke when he pulled too hard. Aunt smiled and said mistakes were part of learning, ___5___ we shouldn’t give up. She helped us fix it with tape, and soon the lantern looked bright ___6___ beautiful. We hung it on the balcony, and the wind made it swing ___7___. Neighbors praised us, and I felt ___8___ we had done something meaningful. Aunt also explained ___9___ red means good luck in Chinese culture. I promised that I ___10___ teach my classmates how to fold a simple lantern at school.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whom  
2. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. what to &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
4. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gentler &emsp;&emsp; C. gently &emsp;&emsp; D. more gentle  
8. A. as if &emsp;&emsp; B. even if &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
9. A. why &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
10. A. will &emsp;&emsp; B. would &emsp;&emsp; C. can &emsp;&emsp; D. must  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词 which 指物并作 enjoy 的宾语  
**【解析】** a tradition which many families enjoy 中，先行词 tradition 为物，which 在从句中作 enjoy 的宾语。what 不引导定语从句；who/whom 指人。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** teach sb. how to do sth.  
**【解析】** taught us how to make… 为“疑问词 + 不定式”结构。how 单独后缺 to；what to make 语义可通但与 step by step 教“方法”相比 how to make 更贴切。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 on（粘在一个圆形框架上）  
**【解析】** glued them on a round frame 表示粘到框架表面。in/at/to 与 glue…frame 搭配不如 on 自然。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 + than（faster than me）  
**【解析】** faster 为比较级，后接 than。as/like/so 不与 faster 构成比较结构。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 so 表结果  
**【解析】** “错误是学习的一部分，所以我们不应该放弃”，表结果用 so。and 单纯并列；but 转折；or 选择，语气不如 so。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列形容词 and 连接 bright and beautiful  
**【解析】** looked bright and beautiful 并列作表语。or 表选择；but 转折；so 表结果，均不合适。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词 swing  
**【解析】** swing gently 用副词 gently 修饰动词。gentle 为形容词；gentler/more gentle 为比较级，此处无比较对象。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** as if 引导表语从句（好像）  
**【解析】** felt as if we had done… 表示“感觉好像我们做了有意义的事”。even if 即使；although 虽然；unless 除非，均不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 why 表原因  
**【解析】** explained why red means good luck 解释“为什么红色代表好运”。when/where/how 与“文化含义的原因”不符。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去将来时（主句 promised 为过去时）  
**【解析】** promised that I would teach… 从句用 would 表从过去看将来。will 时态不一致；can/must 语气不符“承诺将会”。故选 B。
