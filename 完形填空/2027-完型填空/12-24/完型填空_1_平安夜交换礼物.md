# 中考英语完形填空：平安夜交换礼物

## 完形填空原文

On Christmas Eve, my cousins came over ___1___ small gifts in their hands. We placed the presents ___2___ the tree and played a guessing game about the shapes. My mom prepared hot chocolate ___3___ everyone could stay warm.

When the clock struck nine, we picked numbers ___4___ decide the order. I was ___5___ because I drew number one and opened a soft scarf first. Then I gave my cousin Lily a notebook ___6___ I knew she loved writing stories.

We laughed loudly ___7___ someone tried to act surprised. Dad recorded a short video ___8___ we could send it to Grandma who was traveling. Before bedtime, Mom reminded us ___9___ thank-you notes the next morning.

That night I thought gifts were not about price, ___10___ about care and attention.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. under &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
3. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until  
4. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
5. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. lucky &emsp;&emsp; C. luckily &emsp;&emsp; D. luckier  
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
7. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
8. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
9. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. writing &emsp;&emsp; C. to write &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote  
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *with* 表伴随  
**【解析】** *came over with small gifts* “手里拿着小礼物过来”。*with* 表伴随。*and/but/or* 为连词，不能直接连接名词短语作伴随状语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *under the tree*  
**【解析】** 礼物通常放在圣诞树下，*under the tree* 最贴切。*in/on/at* 不如 *under* 符合节日场景。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句（*so that*）  
**【解析】** 准备热巧克力的目的是让大家暖和，*so that everyone could stay warm*。*unless/although/until* 语义不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）  
**【解析】** *picked numbers to help decide the order* 用不定式 *to help* 表目的“为了帮助决定顺序”。*help/helping/helped* 不能直接作目的状语。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词作表语（*be + adj.*）  
**【解析】** *I was lucky because I drew number one* 中 *was* 为系动词，后接形容词 *lucky* 作表语，表示“我很幸运”。*luck* 为名词；*luckily* 为副词，不能直接作 *was* 的表语；*luckier* 是比较级，此处无明确比较对象。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句（*because*）  
**【解析】** 送笔记本是因为知道她爱写故事，因果关系用 *because*。*if/unless* 条件；*although* 让步。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*when*）  
**【解析】** *laughed loudly when someone tried to act surprised* “当有人装惊讶时大家大笑”。*if/unless* 条件；*until* 直到。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句（*so that*）  
**【解析】** 录视频是为了“以便发给旅行的奶奶”，*so that we could send it*。*unless/although/if* 不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*remind sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *reminded us to write thank-you notes* “提醒我们去写感谢卡”。*remind sb. to do* 固定。*write/writing/wrote* 不能直接作宾补。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *not... but...*  
**【解析】** *not about price, but about care* 为“不是……而是……”结构，用 *but*。*and/or/so* 不能构成此对比结构。故选 B。
