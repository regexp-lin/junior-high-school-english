# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工许愿瓶

## 完形填空原文

In our art class we made wish jars ___1___ colorful sand and tiny stars. Ms. Gao told us ___2___ choose calm colors if we wanted a peaceful feeling. I poured blue sand ___3___ the bottom first, and then added silver stars slowly.

The hardest part was writing a wish ___4___ a small paper strip. I practiced my sentence several times ___5___ I rolled the paper and tied it with string. My friend Ben held his jar ___6___ with both hands while walking back to his seat, so the sand did not spill.

When we held the jars up to the light, they looked ___7___ than photos online. Ms. Gao said wishes need patience ___8___ time. She encouraged us ___9___ our jars on the shelf until New Year’s Day.

I smiled ___10___ I imagined opening the jar again in January.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. before &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
6. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. more careful  
7. A. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; B. more beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. most beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. beautifully  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. keeping &emsp;&emsp; C. to keep &emsp;&emsp; D. kept  
10. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *with* 表材料/手段  
**【解析】** *made wish jars with colorful sand* “用彩沙制作许愿瓶”。*with* 表“用……材料”。*in/on/at* 不表材料。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*tell sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *told us to choose calm colors* “告诉我们选择柔和的颜色”。*tell sb. to do* 固定。*for/of/with* 不能作此结构补足语。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 *at the bottom*  
**【解析】** *at the bottom* “在底部”。*in the bottom* 不如 *at* 常见；*on/by* 搭配不当。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 *on a small paper strip*  
**【解析】** 把愿望写在纸条表面，用 *on a small paper strip*。*in* 多指写在纸里/心里；*at/to* 搭配不当。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*before*）  
**【解析】** *practiced... before I rolled the paper* “在卷起纸条之前练习了几遍”。*if/because/unless* 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** *held his jar carefully* 用副词 *carefully* 修饰动词 *held*，表示“双手小心地捧着瓶子”，与后文“沙子没有洒出来”一致。*care/careful* 为名词/形容词，不能直接修饰动词；*more careful* 为形容词比较级，不能修饰动词 *held*。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** 句中有 *than*，提示用比较级 *more beautiful than photos online*。*beautiful* 原级不能接 *than*；*most beautiful* 为最高级需 *the* 与范围；*beautifully* 为副词，不能作 *looked* 的表语。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *and*  
**【解析】** *patience and time* 为并列名词短语，“耐心与时间”。*but/or/so* 语义不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*encourage sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *encouraged us to keep our jars on the shelf* “鼓励我们把瓶子放在架子上”。*encourage sb. to do* 固定。*keep/keeping/kept* 不能直接作宾补。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*when*）  
**【解析】** *smiled when I imagined opening the jar* “当我想象一月再打开瓶子时笑了”。*if* 条件；*until* 直到；*unless* 除非。故选 B。
