# 中考英语完形填空：妇女节为妈妈做早餐

## 完形填空原文

On Women's Day, I got up ___1___ than usual to prepare breakfast for my mom. My dad whispered, "Be quiet. Let her sleep ___2___."

I heated milk, toasted bread, and cut fruit ___3___ a heart shape on the plate. My hands shook a little ___4___ I was afraid of waking her too early.

When Mom walked into the kitchen, she froze ___5___ a second and then hugged me tightly. "This is the ___6___ surprise!" she said with tears in her eyes.

She tasted the sandwich and said it was ___7___ delicious. I knew she was encouraging me, ___8___ her smile made my heart warm.

Later, Dad washed the dishes ___9___ I wrote a card with the words "Thank you for your love every day." Mom put the card on the shelf ___10___ her favorite photo of us.

## 选项

1. A. early &emsp;&emsp; B. earlier &emsp;&emsp; C. earliest &emsp;&emsp; D. more early
2. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. longer &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. though &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. at
6. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well
7. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. really &emsp;&emsp; C. more real &emsp;&emsp; D. most real
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. at

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：妇女节那天我比平时起得更早。than 提示用比较级 earlier。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：让她多睡一会儿。sleep longer 表示“睡更久”。later 多表“更晚”，此处 longer 更贴切“延长睡眠”；quietly/slowly 修饰 sleep 不符合爸爸让别吵醒妈妈的语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把水果切成心形摆在盘子里。cut...into... 表示“切成……形状”。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手有点抖，因为我怕太早吵醒她。because 引导原因状语从句。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈愣了一秒然后紧紧抱住我。for a second 表示“一会儿；一秒左右”。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是最好的惊喜！定冠词 the 后接最高级 best。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰形容词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说三明治真的很好吃。修饰形容词 delicious 用副词 really。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道她在鼓励我，而且她的微笑让我心里暖暖的。前后为并列补充关系，用 and。but 表转折，与语境不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：后来爸爸洗碗，我写贺卡。while 表示两个动作同时进行。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈把卡片放在架子上我们最喜欢的一张照片旁边。beside 表示“在……旁边”。故选 C。
