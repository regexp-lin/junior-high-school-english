# 中考英语完形填空：第一次当班长

## 完形填空原文

This term, I was chosen ___1___ monitor of our class for the first time. On the first morning, I arrived early ___2___ open the windows and write the date on the blackboard. Some classmates came in and greeted me ___3___ a warm smile.

At first, I was afraid ___4___ make mistakes when I spoke in front of the class. My head teacher noticed that and told me, “Confidence comes ___5___ practice.” She encouraged me ___6___ a short speech at the class meeting.

I practiced many times ___7___ home. When I stood on the platform, my voice shook a little, ___8___ I finished my job. After that, my classmates clapped loudly. I realized ___9___ role was not about giving orders, ___10___ about serving others. I will keep trying my best.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. as &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. has &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. having  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
5. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
6. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. giving &emsp;&emsp; C. to give &emsp;&emsp; D. gave  
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 as 表身份  
**【解析】** be chosen as... 表示“被选为……”，as 引出身份（班长）。for/to/with 不用于此固定搭配表“担任”。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 动词不定式表目的  
**【解析】** “早早到校是为了开窗、写日期”，用不定式 to open 表目的。for open 结构错误；of/with 不表目的。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表伴随  
**【解析】** greet sb. with a smile 为常见搭配，意为“微笑着问候某人”。have/has/having 不能直接接在 greeted me 后构成此义。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be afraid to do sth.）  
**【解析】** be afraid to make mistakes 表示“害怕/担心会犯错”，afraid 后接不定式符号 to，再接动词原形 make。be afraid of 后接名词或动名词，若用 of 应为 of making，与题干 make 不衔接；for/with 不构成此搭配。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词固定搭配（come from）  
**【解析】** “自信来自练习”，come from 表示“来源于”。come in/at/on 无此义。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（encourage sb. to do）  
**【解析】** encourage sb. to do sth. 为固定结构，用不定式 to give。give/giving/gave 均不能直接跟在 encourage me 后作宾补。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词短语 at home  
**【解析】** “在家练习”用 at home。in/on/to home 为错误或不地道搭配。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “声音有点抖”与“完成了任务”为转折关系，用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表结果。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定冠词表特指  
**【解析】** 特指“班长这一职责/角色”，用 the role。a/an 表泛指；零冠词不适用于此处可数单数特指。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列结构 not... but...  
**【解析】** not... but... 表示“不是……而是……”，与上文 not about giving orders 呼应。and/or/so 不能构成 not... but... 结构。故选 C。
