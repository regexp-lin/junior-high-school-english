# 中考英语完形填空：学做贺年卡

## 完形填空原文

The New Year was coming, ___1___ I wanted to send handmade cards to my friends. I bought some red paper, glue, and stickers ___2___ a stationery shop near my school.

My cousin Lily is good ___3___ drawing, so she taught me patiently. “Fold the paper ___4___ half first,” she said. I followed her advice, ___5___ the card still looked uneven. Lily laughed and told me ___6___ worry.

She showed me how ___7___ cut a small window on the cover. After I practiced ___8___ times, the lines became straight. I wrote “Happy New Year” ___9___ a gold pen and added a tiny bell. When I gave the card ___10___ my best friend, she hugged me tightly.

## 选项

1. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
2. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
3. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
6. A. to not &emsp;&emsp; B. not to &emsp;&emsp; C. don’t &emsp;&emsp; D. not  
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. few &emsp;&emsp; B. a little &emsp;&emsp; C. a few &emsp;&emsp; D. little  
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. use  
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表结果  
**【解析】** “新年快到了，所以我想给朋友寄手工贺卡”，前因后果用 so。but 表转折；or 表选择；because 表原因（若用 because，逻辑应先说原因）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 from 表来源  
**【解析】** buy sth. from a shop 表示“从商店购买”。in/at/on 与 buy...shop 搭配不如 from 准确表“来源”。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（be good at）  
**【解析】** be good at drawing 表示“擅长画画”。good for/in/with 不表示“擅长”。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定短语（fold ... in half）  
**【解析】** fold the paper in half 为固定表达，意为“把纸对折”。on/to 不搭配；into half 不如 in half 地道自然。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “我照做了”与“贺卡仍不平整”为转折，用 but。and 表顺承；so 表结果；or 表选择。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定式的否定式（tell sb. not to do）  
**【解析】** tell sb. not to worry 否定词 not 置于不定式符号 to 之前。to not 语序不规范；don’t 不能作 told me 的宾补结构；单独 not 不完整。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构  
**【解析】** how to cut 作 showed 的宾语，为“疑问词 + to do”。how for/of/with cut 结构错误。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** few / a few 修饰可数名词  
**【解析】** times 为可数名词复数，“练习了几次”用 a few times 表肯定“一些”。few 表否定“几乎没有”；a little/little 修饰不可数名词。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表工具  
**【解析】** write...with a gold pen 表示“用金笔写”。in/by 在此不如 with 贴切表工具；use 为动词，句中已有谓语 wrote。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配（give sth. to sb.）  
**【解析】** give the card to my best friend 表示“把卡片给好友”。give sth. for/of/with sb. 不符合双宾语/介词搭配习惯。故选 B。
