# 中考英语完形填空：寒假第一天去图书馆

## 完形填空原文

Winter vacation started yesterday, and I was ___1___ excited that I could hardly sleep. On the first morning, I decided ___2___ to the city library because I wanted to borrow some books for the holiday. The library opens at nine, ___3___ I arrived ten minutes early and waited outside. When the doors opened, a friendly librarian welcomed us and said, “Please keep ___4___ voice low while reading.” I walked to the Teen Zone and picked three novels ___5___ I had read the reviews online. Later, I sat by the window and read for an hour. Time passed ___6___ than I expected, and I almost missed lunch. Before leaving, I thanked the librarian ___7___ her help. She smiled and told me that reading ___8___ us smarter if we keep doing it every day. I agreed and promised ___9___ I would come back next week. On my way home, I knew winter vacation ___10___ be boring at all.

## 选项

1. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. such &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. too  
2. A. go &emsp;&emsp; B. to go &emsp;&emsp; C. going &emsp;&emsp; D. went  
3. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
4. A. you &emsp;&emsp; B. your &emsp;&emsp; C. yours &emsp;&emsp; D. yourself  
5. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. until  
6. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quicker &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. more quickly  
7. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
8. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. makes &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. if  
10. A. mustn’t &emsp;&emsp; B. shouldn’t &emsp;&emsp; C. couldn’t &emsp;&emsp; D. wouldn’t  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定结构 so…that…（如此……以至于……）  
**【解析】** 空格后是形容词 excited，需用 so 修饰形容词，构成 so excited that…。such 后接名词短语（such + a/an + adj. + n.），此处不适用。very/too 不能与 that 引导的结果状语从句构成该固定搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 decide to do sth.  
**【解析】** decide 后接动词不定式作宾语，即 decide to go。go/going/went 均不符合 decide 的用法。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表因果关系  
**【解析】** 前句“图书馆九点开门”，后句“我提前十分钟到”，为顺承结果关系，用 so（所以）。but 表转折，or 表选择，because 引导原因从句放句首更自然，此处连接两个并列分句用 so 更合适。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** voice 为名词，前面需用形容词性物主代词 your 修饰 keep your voice low。you 为人称代词主格/宾格，yours 为名词性物主代词，yourself 为反身代词，均不直接修饰 voice。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词 after 引导时间状语从句  
**【解析】** 句意：我在网上读过书评之后才挑了三本小说。after 表示“在……之后”，符合语境。before 与事实相反；while 强调同时进行；until 表示“直到”，均不如 after 贴切。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 副词比较级修饰动词 passed  
**【解析】** than 提示用比较级；修饰动词 passed 应用副词，故用 more quickly。quick 为形容词；quicker 多修饰形容词或表“更快”的比较级形容词；quickly 为原级，不能与 than 搭配。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 thank sb. for sth.  
**【解析】** “因某事感谢某人”用 thank…for…。thank…to… 非固定搭配；of/with 不与 thank 构成此义。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 主谓一致（宾语从句中动词的时态与数）  
**【解析】** 宾语从句主语为 reading（动名词作主语，视为单数），且引述一般事实，谓语用一般现在时 makes。make/making/made 与主语单复数或时态不一致。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 that（陈述事实，无实义）  
**【解析】** promised 后为陈述句内容的宾语从句，用 that 引导（口语中可省略，但选项中应选 that）。what 在从句中需充当成分；which 表“哪一个”；if 表“是否”，与 promised 的确定语气不符。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 情态动词 wouldn’t 表将来推测/判断（不会）  
**【解析】** 句意：回家的路上，我知道寒假一点儿也不会无聊。wouldn’t be boring at all 表示“不会无聊”，与上文阅读体验呼应。mustn’t 表禁止；shouldn’t 表不应该；couldn’t 多表“不可能”，与 knew 搭配时 wouldn’t 更自然。故选 D。
