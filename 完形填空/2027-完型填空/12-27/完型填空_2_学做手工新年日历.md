# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工新年日历

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, my art teacher showed us how ___1___ a simple New Year calendar by hand. I had never tried anything like that ___2___, so I felt a little nervous at first. She gave each of us a piece of thick paper, some colored pens, and a small ruler. “First, draw twelve boxes ___3___ each month,” she said. I followed her steps carefully and ___4___ a mistake on the third line. Luckily, she taught me ___5___ to cover it with a cute sticker. After two hours, my calendar looked much ___6___ than I imagined. My classmates said it was one of ___7___ calendars in our group. I took it home and hung it on the wall ___8___ my parents could see it easily. Mom praised me and said the calendar ___9___ by myself was special. I learned that practice makes perfect, and I am going ___10___ more handmade gifts next month.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  
2. A. ago &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. later &emsp;&emsp; D. after  
3. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about  
4. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. makes &emsp;&emsp; D. making  
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
6. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best  
7. A. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; B. more beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. most beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. the most beautiful  
8. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. such that &emsp;&emsp; C. in order to &emsp;&emsp; D. because  
9. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. made &emsp;&emsp; D. to make  
10. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构中的不定式 show sb. how to do  
**【解析】** how 后接不定式作宾语，即 how to make。how make/making/made 结构错误。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词 before 表示“以前”（与完成时连用）  
**【解析】** never…before 为常见搭配，表示“以前从未”。ago 需与时间段连用且多用于过去时；later/after 表示“之后”，与 never 搭配不当。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 for 表对象/用途（为每个月准备的格子）  
**【解析】** draw boxes for each month 表示“为每个月画格子”。of/with/about 在此表意不清。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时与并列谓语 followed  
**【解析】** and 连接两个并列谓语，followed 为过去式，make a mistake 也应用过去式 made。make/makes/making 时态不一致。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式 teach sb. how to do sth.  
**【解析】** 老师教“如何”用贴纸遮盖错误，用 how。what 需宾语；why/when 与“遮盖方法”不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级 + than  
**【解析】** than 提示比较级；look 为系动词，后接形容词 better。good 为原级；well 作形容词表“身体好”，此处指作品外观；best 为最高级。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** “one of + the + 形容词最高级 + 复数名词”  
**【解析】** one of 后需 the + 最高级 + 复数名词，故为 the most beautiful calendars。beautiful/more beautiful/most beautiful 缺少定冠词 the 或结构不完整。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句 so that（以便）  
**【解析】** “挂在墙上以便父母容易看见”，后接句子，用 so that。such that 多接结果；in order to 后接动词原形，不接句子；because 表原因非目的。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作后置定语（被动含义）  
**【解析】** calendar made by myself 表示“我自己做的日历”，made 表被动完成。make/making/to make 作定语均不如过去分词准确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** be going to 表将来计划  
**【解析】** be going to do 为固定结构，故用 to make。make/making/made 不能直接跟在 going 后。故选 B。
