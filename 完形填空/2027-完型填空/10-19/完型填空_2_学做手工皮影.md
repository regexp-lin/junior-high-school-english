# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工皮影

## 完形填空原文

Shadow puppetry is a traditional Chinese art that has a history of over two ___1___ years. Our school invited a folk artist, Mr. Li, to teach us about it.

Mr. Li first told us the story behind shadow puppets. In ancient times, people ___2___ them to tell stories and entertain others. He brought several old puppets with him. They were made ___3___ animal skin and were beautifully painted.

"Today, we'll make simple puppets from thick paper," he said. He drew some characters on the board and asked us to choose one. I picked a monkey because it looked ___4___ to draw.

We traced the outlines onto thick cardboard and cut them out ___5___. Then we used colored markers to paint the details. Mr. Li told us ___6___ to attach small wooden sticks to the joints so that the puppet could move.

When everything was ready, Mr. Li turned ___7___ the lights and set up a white screen. One by one, we went behind the screen and performed with our puppets. It was ___8___ exciting experience.

My monkey puppet danced and jumped on the screen. The audience laughed and clapped. I had never felt so ___9___ before. Mr. Li said that keeping traditional arts alive depends ___10___ young people like us. His words gave me a strong sense of responsibility.

## 选项

1. A. thousand &emsp;&emsp; B. thousands &emsp;&emsp; C. thousandth &emsp;&emsp; D. thousandths  
2. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. uses &emsp;&emsp; C. used &emsp;&emsp; D. using  
3. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
4. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easily &emsp;&emsp; C. easier &emsp;&emsp; D. easiest  
5. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. careless &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly  
6. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
7. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. off &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. down  
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
9. A. pride &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. proudly &emsp;&emsp; D. prouder  
10. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. for  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 数词 + thousand  
**【解析】** two thousand years 意为"两千年"，具体数字后 thousand 不加 s 也不加 of。thousands 用于 thousands of（数以千计的）；thousandth 为序数词"第一千"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** in ancient times 为过去时间，动词用过去式 used。use 为原形；uses 为三单现在时；using 为现在分词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** be made from 与 be made of  
**【解析】** be made from 意为"由……制成"（看不出原材料），动物皮经加工变为皮影，已看不出原始材料。of 用于看得出原材料；by 表被动施事者；with 表工具。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 系动词 look + 形容词  
**【解析】** looked easy to draw 意为"看起来容易画"，look 为系动词后接形容词。easily 为副词；easier/easiest 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** cut them out carefully 意为"仔细地剪出来"，修饰动词 cut 用副词。careful 为形容词；careless 意为"粗心的"；carelessly 意为"粗心地"，均不符合语境。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** "疑问词 + 不定式"作宾语  
**【解析】** told us how to attach 意为"告诉我们如何安装"，how to do 作宾语。what 侧重"什么"；when 侧重"何时"；where 侧重"何处"。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 turn off  
**【解析】** turn off the lights 意为"关灯"，为皮影表演做准备需要关灯。turn on 意为"打开"；turn up 意为"调大"；turn down 意为"调小/拒绝"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 an  
**【解析】** an exciting experience 中 exciting 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，用 an。a 用于辅音音素前；the 为定冠词表特指；零冠词此处不合适。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 系动词 feel + 形容词  
**【解析】** felt so proud 意为"感到如此自豪"，felt 为系动词后接形容词。pride 为名词；proudly 为副词；prouder 为比较级但此处无比较对象。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 depend on  
**【解析】** depend on 意为"取决于、依赖于"，on 为固定介词。at/in/for 不与 depend 构成此搭配。故选 C。
