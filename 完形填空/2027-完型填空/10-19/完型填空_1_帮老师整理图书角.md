# 中考英语完形填空：帮老师整理图书角

## 完形填空原文

Our classroom has a reading corner with about two hundred books. Last week, our English teacher, Ms. Wang, asked ___1___ volunteers to help organize it.

I raised my hand at once because I love books and enjoy keeping things ___2___. After class, three of us stayed behind. Ms. Wang gave us clear instructions: sort the books by type, remove the damaged ones, and make a ___3___ list.

I was in charge of the fiction section. Many books were out of ___4___, and some had missing pages. I felt sad when I found a copy of "Charlotte's Web" with its cover ___5___ off. It used to be my favorite book in primary school.

While sorting, I discovered several books that I ___6___ never seen before. One of them was a collection of English poems. I opened it and read a few lines. The words were so beautiful ___7___ I decided to borrow it after we finished.

It took us about two hours to complete the task. The reading corner looked much ___8___ than before. Ms. Wang was very satisfied and said, "A tidy reading corner can encourage more students ___9___ books."

She also announced that each student could donate one book to the reading corner. ___10___ this way, our collection would keep growing. I thought it was a wonderful idea.

## 选项

1. A. few &emsp;&emsp; B. a few &emsp;&emsp; C. little &emsp;&emsp; D. a little  
2. A. tidy &emsp;&emsp; B. tidily &emsp;&emsp; C. tidier &emsp;&emsp; D. tidiest  
3. A. new &emsp;&emsp; B. newer &emsp;&emsp; C. newest &emsp;&emsp; D. newly  
4. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. order &emsp;&emsp; C. date &emsp;&emsp; D. control  
5. A. tear &emsp;&emsp; B. tore &emsp;&emsp; C. tearing &emsp;&emsp; D. torn  
6. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. has &emsp;&emsp; C. had &emsp;&emsp; D. having  
7. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. that  
8. A. neat &emsp;&emsp; B. neatly &emsp;&emsp; C. neater &emsp;&emsp; D. neatest  
9. A. read &emsp;&emsp; B. reading &emsp;&emsp; C. to read &emsp;&emsp; D. reads  
10. A. On &emsp;&emsp; B. In &emsp;&emsp; C. By &emsp;&emsp; D. At  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** few 与 a few 辨析  
**【解析】** a few 意为"一些"（肯定含义），修饰可数名词复数 volunteers。few 意为"几乎没有"（否定含义）；little/a little 修饰不可数名词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 动词 keep + 宾语 + 形容词  
**【解析】** keep things tidy 意为"保持东西整洁"，tidy 为形容词作宾补。tidily 为副词；tidier/tidiest 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词  
**【解析】** a new list 意为"一份新的清单"，new 为形容词修饰 list。newer/newest 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较；newly 为副词不直接修饰名词。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 out of order  
**【解析】** out of order 意为"杂乱无序"。out of place 意为"不合适"；out of date 意为"过时"；out of control 意为"失控"，均不符合书籍杂乱的语境。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** with + 名词 + 过去分词  
**【解析】** with its cover torn off 意为"封面被撕掉了"，cover 与 tear 为被动关系，用过去分词 torn。tear 为原形；tore 为过去式；tearing 为现在分词（主动）。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去完成时  
**【解析】** 发现之前"从未见过"，即在"发现"这个过去动作之前从未见过，用过去完成时 had never seen。have/has 为现在完成时；having 为现在分词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** so...that... 结果状语从句  
**【解析】** so beautiful that 构成"如此……以至于"的结果状语从句。as 不与 so 搭配构成此结构；which/when 不引导结果状语从句。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** much neater than before 意为"比以前整洁得多"，有 than 需用比较级。neat 为原级；neatly 为副词；neatest 为最高级。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（encourage sb. to do）  
**【解析】** encourage more students to read 意为"鼓励更多学生阅读"，不定式作宾补。read 为原形；reading 为现在分词；reads 为三单。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 in this way  
**【解析】** in this way 意为"用这种方式"，为固定搭配。on/by/at 不与 this way 构成此搭配（by this way 不正确，应为 in this way）。故选 B。
