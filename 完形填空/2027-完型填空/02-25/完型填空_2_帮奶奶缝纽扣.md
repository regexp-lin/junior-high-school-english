# 中考英语完形填空：帮奶奶缝纽扣

## 完形填空原文

Grandma's favorite coat lost a button ___1___ the pocket. She searched the drawer ___2___ couldn't find a matching one, so she looked a little worried.

I offered ___3___ help and found a blue button that was almost the same size. Grandma threaded a needle ___4___ showed me how to tie a strong knot at the end.

"Push the needle ___5___ the cloth slowly," she said. My first try was crooked, ___6___ she didn't laugh at me. Instead, she guided my fingers ___7___ great patience.

After several minutes, the new button stayed firm. Grandma touched it gently ___8___ said, "You have careful hands."

That afternoon, I learned that love is sometimes quiet work ___9___ a needle and thread. I also realized skills need time, ___10___ practice makes progress.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. near
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. my &emsp;&emsp; B. mine &emsp;&emsp; C. me &emsp;&emsp; D. myself
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. across &emsp;&emsp; B. through &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. along
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
10. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. if

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶最喜欢的外套在口袋附近掉了一颗扣子。near the pocket 在口袋附近，符合“扣子位置”描述。in 在……里；on 在……上；at 在。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她翻抽屉但找不到匹配的扣子。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我主动提出帮忙。offer my help 提出我的帮助，形容词性物主代词 my 修饰 help。mine 为名词性物主代词，后不接名词；me 为人称代词宾格，不作定语；myself 与 help 搭配不当。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶穿针并在末端打牢结。threaded 与 showed 并列，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把针慢慢穿过布料。through the cloth 穿过（内部）。across 横穿表面；over 在……上方；along 沿着。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我第一次缝歪了，但她没有嘲笑我。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：相反，她极富耐心地引导我的手指。with great patience 带着极大的耐心。in 在……里；on 在……上；at 在。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶轻轻摸了摸扣子并说话。touched 与 said 并列，用 and。or 或者；but 但是；so 所以。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天下午我明白，爱有时是用针线完成的安静工作。with a needle and thread 用针线。in 在……里；on 在……上；by 通过。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也意识到技能需要时间，因为练习带来进步。because 因为。unless 除非；although 尽管；if 如果。故选 A。
