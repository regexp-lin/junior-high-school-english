# 中考英语完形填空：参加环保活动捡垃圾

## 完形填空原文

Our city held an environmental protection event last Saturday. Hundreds of volunteers gathered in the central park to pick up litter. My family and I were among ___1___.

We arrived early in the morning. The organizer handed out gloves, trash bags, and special tools for ___2___ up garbage. He divided us into teams and assigned each team a different area.

My team was responsible for the area near the lake. I was ___3___ to see how much trash was on the ground — plastic bottles, food wrappers, and even old shoes. It was hard to believe that people could be so ___4___.

We worked for three hours without stopping. My back ached, and my hands were sore, ___5___ I felt proud. We collected twelve bags of trash in total. My little sister, who is only eight, ___6___ three bags by herself!

After the cleanup, we sorted the trash into different bins: plastic, paper, and other waste. The organizer said that ___7___ the trash was collected, it would be sent to a recycling center.

He also gave a short speech. He told us that if everyone ___8___ a small effort, the environment would become much cleaner. I totally agreed ___9___ him.

On the way home, I noticed that the park looked ___10___ more beautiful than before. I decided to join this kind of event more often. Protecting the earth is everyone's duty.

## 选项

1. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs  
2. A. pick &emsp;&emsp; B. picked &emsp;&emsp; C. picking &emsp;&emsp; D. picks  
3. A. shock &emsp;&emsp; B. shocked &emsp;&emsp; C. shocking &emsp;&emsp; D. shocks  
4. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. careless &emsp;&emsp; D. carefully  
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but  
6. A. fill &emsp;&emsp; B. fills &emsp;&emsp; C. filled &emsp;&emsp; D. filling  
7. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although  
8. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. makes &emsp;&emsp; C. made &emsp;&emsp; D. making  
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. about  
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. quite &emsp;&emsp; D. much  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 介词 among + 宾格  
**【解析】** among them 意为"在他们之中"，among 为介词后接宾格 them。they 为主格；their 为物主代词；theirs 为名词性物主代词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 for + 动名词  
**【解析】** for picking up 意为"用于捡拾"，for 为介词后接动名词 picking。pick 为原形；picked 为过去式；picks 为三单。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去分词作表语（表感受）  
**【解析】** was shocked 意为"感到震惊"，shocked 修饰人的感受。shock 为动词/名词；shocking 意为"令人震惊的"修饰事物；shocks 为名词复数。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词辨析  
**【解析】** 乱扔垃圾说明人们"粗心、不在意"，careless（不小心的、不负责任的）合适。care 为名词/动词；careful 意为"小心的"与语境相反；carefully 为副词。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折）  
**【解析】** "腰疼手痛"与"感到自豪"为转折关系，用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表因果。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，动词用过去式 filled。fill 为原形；fills 为三单现在时；filling 为现在分词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词  
**【解析】** "垃圾收集之后会被送到回收中心"，after 引导时间状语从句。before 表"之前"；unless 表"除非"；although 表"虽然"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 虚拟语气（if + 过去式, would + 动词原形）  
**【解析】** if everyone made a small effort, the environment would become... 为虚拟条件句，表与现在事实相反的假设，if 从句用一般过去式。make 为原形；makes 为三单现在时；making 为现在分词。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 agree with  
**【解析】** agree with sb. 意为"同意某人的看法"，with 为固定介词。agree to 后接计划/提议；agree on 后接双方同意的事项；agree about 不常用。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** much + 比较级  
**【解析】** much more beautiful 意为"漂亮得多"，much 修饰比较级加强程度。very 不修饰比较级；too 意为"太"；quite 修饰原级。故选 D。
