# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工相册

## 完形填空原文

My best friend, Xiao Ling, was going to move to another city next month. I wanted to give her a ___1___ gift, so I decided to make a handmade photo album for her.

I collected photos of us from the past three years. There were photos of us at school, in the park, and at each ___2___ birthday parties. Looking at them brought back so many happy memories.

First, I bought some thick colored paper and folded it in half to make ___3___. Then I arranged the photos in order from our first meeting ___4___ the most recent one. Under each photo, I wrote a short sentence describing the moment.

The most difficult part was decorating the ___5___. I used markers to draw flowers and stars, and I stuck small stickers on the corners. It took me almost a whole afternoon, but I ___6___ mind at all because every minute was filled with love.

On her last day at school, I gave the album to Xiao Ling. She opened it slowly, and tears ___7___ in her eyes. "This is the most thoughtful gift anyone has ever given ___8___," she whispered.

We hugged each other tightly. I told her that ___9___ matter how far apart we were, our friendship would never change. She nodded and promised ___10___ in touch.

That moment, I understood that handmade gifts carry something money can never buy — true love and sincerity.

## 选项

1. A. special &emsp;&emsp; B. specially &emsp;&emsp; C. more special &emsp;&emsp; D. most special  
2. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. another &emsp;&emsp; C. other's &emsp;&emsp; D. others  
3. A. page &emsp;&emsp; B. pages &emsp;&emsp; C. paper &emsp;&emsp; D. papers  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
5. A. cover &emsp;&emsp; B. corner &emsp;&emsp; C. content &emsp;&emsp; D. copy  
6. A. don't &emsp;&emsp; B. didn't &emsp;&emsp; C. doesn't &emsp;&emsp; D. won't  
7. A. appear &emsp;&emsp; B. appeared &emsp;&emsp; C. appearing &emsp;&emsp; D. appears  
8. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
9. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. no &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
10. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. keeping &emsp;&emsp; C. to keep &emsp;&emsp; D. kept  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词  
**【解析】** a special gift 意为"一份特别的礼物"，special 为形容词修饰 gift。specially 为副词；more special/most special 为比较级/最高级，此处无比较。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 名词所有格  
**【解析】** each other's birthday parties 意为"彼此的生日聚会"，other's 为所有格形式。other 缺所有格标志；another 指另一个（单数）；others 为代词。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词复数  
**【解析】** make pages 意为"制作页面"，一本相册有多页，用复数 pages。page 为单数；paper 意为"纸"不表"页面"；papers 意为"试卷/论文"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** from...to... 结构  
**【解析】** from our first meeting to the most recent one 意为"从第一次见面到最近一次"，from...to... 为固定搭配。for/with/by 不与 from 构成此结构。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 名词辨析  
**【解析】** the cover 意为"封面"，装饰相册最显眼的部分是封面。corner 意为"角落"；content 意为"内容"；copy 意为"副本"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时否定  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，"我一点也不介意"用 didn't mind。don't/doesn't 为现在时；won't 为将来时。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时  
**【解析】** 全文为过去叙述，tears appeared 为过去式。appear 为原形；appearing 为现在分词；appears 为三单现在时。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** has given me 意为"给过我"，give 后接间接宾语用宾格 me。I 为主格；my 为物主代词；mine 为名词性物主代词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** no matter how 让步状语从句  
**【解析】** no matter how far apart 意为"无论多远"，no matter how 引导让步状语从句。it matter 不构成此结构；that matter 不正确；what matter 不与 how 搭配。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（promise to do）  
**【解析】** promise to keep in touch 意为"承诺保持联系"，promise 后接不定式。keep 为原形缺 to；keeping 为动名词；kept 为过去式。故选 C。
