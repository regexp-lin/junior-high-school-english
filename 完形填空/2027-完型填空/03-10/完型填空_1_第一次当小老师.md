# 中考英语完形填空：第一次当小老师

## 完形填空原文

Last Tuesday, Ms. Liu asked me to ___1___ a ten minute review before the test. My face turned hot ___2___ I had never taught the class before.

I wrote key points on the blackboard ___3___ neat handwriting. When I turned around, thirty pairs of eyes watched me ___4___.

I started with two easy questions. Hands went up quickly, ___5___ I felt braver. A boy asked a tricky problem, and I explained it twice ___6___ he nodded.

Near the end, my voice shook a little, ___7___ the class still thanked me with applause. Ms. Liu said I spoke ___8___ than last semester.

After class, several students told me my examples were clear. I understood ___9___ teachers work so hard to prepare lessons. That night I practiced speaking ___10___ front of the mirror again, hoping to do better next time.

## 选项

1. A. lead &emsp;&emsp; B. leads &emsp;&emsp; C. leading &emsp;&emsp; D. led
2. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. though &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. quiet &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. quieter &emsp;&emsp; D. quietest
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. though
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. clear &emsp;&emsp; B. more clearly &emsp;&emsp; C. most clearly &emsp;&emsp; D. clearest
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
10. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. to

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与非谓语
**【解析】** 本句意为：刘老师让我带领同学们做考前十分钟复习。ask sb to do sth 中 to 已给出，空白处填动词原形 lead，与 a review 搭配表示“带领复习”。leads 为第三人称单数；leading/led 形式不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的脸发烫，因为我从没给全班讲过课。because 引导原因状语从句。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用工整的板书把要点写在黑板上。with neat handwriting 表示“用……（方式）”。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：三十双眼睛静静地看着我。修饰动词 watched 用副词 quietly。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们很快举手，因此我更勇敢了。因果关系用 so。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我讲了两遍直到他点头。until 表示“直到……为止”。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：快结束时我的声音有点抖，但全班仍鼓掌感谢。转折用 but。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：刘老师说我说得比上学期更清楚。修饰动词 spoke 用副词 clearly；有 than，用 more clearly。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了老师为何如此认真备课。表示原因用 why。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：那晚我又对着镜子练习讲话。in front of the mirror 为固定短语。故选 C。
