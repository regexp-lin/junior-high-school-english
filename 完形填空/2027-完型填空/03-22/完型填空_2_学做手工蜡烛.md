# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工蜡烛

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, my sister and I decided ___1___ make candles at home. We watched a tutorial video online ___2___ prepared the materials: wax, wicks and some color dyes.

First, we melted the wax in a small pot. It took about ten minutes ___3___ the wax turned into clear liquid. My sister added a few drops of red dye, ___4___ the liquid became a beautiful pink.

"Be careful ___5___ the hot wax," Mom reminded us from the kitchen. We poured the liquid into glass cups slowly ___6___ steadily.

Then we put the wicks in the center ___7___ waited for the wax to cool down. I was so excited ___8___ I kept checking every two minutes.

After an hour, the candles were finally ready. They looked ___9___ little pink flowers. My sister lit one and the soft light filled the room.

"This is the ___10___ gift for Mom's birthday!" she whispered. I nodded with a big smile.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. on
2. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. when
4. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
6. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. although
9. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. of
10. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. well

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和姐姐决定在家做蜡烛。decide to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示"决定做某事"。for/of/on 不符合 decide 的搭配。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们看了教程视频并准备了材料。watched 与 prepared 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：蜡融化成透明液体之前花了大约十分钟。It took... before... 表示"过了……才……"。after/until/when 不如 before 准确表达"花了时间才变成"的意思。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：姐姐加了几滴红色染料，液体就变成了漂亮的粉色。两个动作顺承发生，用 and。or/but/so 不如 and 自然。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：小心热蜡。be careful with... 表示"小心对待……"。of/for/at 不如 with 贴切地表达"注意某物"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们慢慢地、稳稳地将液体倒入玻璃杯。slowly 与 steadily 并列修饰动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把灯芯放在中间并等蜡冷却。put 与 waited 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词/so...that 结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太兴奋了以至于每两分钟就去看一次。so...that... 表示"如此……以至于……"。when/because/although 不符合 so 引导的结果状语从句。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们看起来像小粉花。look like... 表示"看起来像"。as 多接从句；for/of 不搭配 look。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是给妈妈最好的生日礼物！定冠词 the 后用最高级 best。good 为原级；better 为比较级；well 为副词。故选 C。
