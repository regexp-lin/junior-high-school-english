# 中考英语完形填空：帮同学补课

## 完形填空原文

My classmate Zhang Wei had been absent from school ___1___ a whole week because of illness. When he came back, he found it hard to ___2___ up with the lessons. The teacher asked me to help him after school.

Every afternoon, we sat in the empty classroom and went ___3___ the notes together. Zhang Wei was a quick learner, ___4___ some topics still confused him. I tried to explain ___5___ in the simplest way possible.

One day, he asked me a question about English grammar that I couldn't answer ___6___. Instead of pretending to know, I told him honestly, "Let me check and get back to you tomorrow." He nodded and said he ___7___ my honesty.

After two weeks of hard work, Zhang Wei ___8___ a great test score. He was so happy that he bought me a bottle of juice. "Without ___9___ help, I couldn't have done it," he said gratefully.

Helping others not only made me feel good but also helped me ___10___ my own knowledge. It was truly a win-win experience.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. catch &emsp;&emsp; C. make &emsp;&emsp; D. pick
3. A. across &emsp;&emsp; B. over &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. after
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. something &emsp;&emsp; B. anything &emsp;&emsp; C. everything &emsp;&emsp; D. nothing
6. A. either &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. also &emsp;&emsp; D. neither
7. A. appreciate &emsp;&emsp; B. appreciated &emsp;&emsp; C. appreciating &emsp;&emsp; D. appreciation
8. A. get &emsp;&emsp; B. gets &emsp;&emsp; C. got &emsp;&emsp; D. getting
9. A. you &emsp;&emsp; B. your &emsp;&emsp; C. yours &emsp;&emsp; D. yourself
10. A. review &emsp;&emsp; B. reviewed &emsp;&emsp; C. reviewing &emsp;&emsp; D. reviews

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：张伟因病缺课整整一周。for + 时间段表示持续的时长。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（动词短语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他发现很难跟上课程进度。catch up with 表示"赶上、跟上"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（动词短语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一起复习笔记。go over 表示"复习、仔细查看"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：张伟学得很快，但有些话题仍然让他困惑。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尽量用最简单的方式解释一切。everything 表示"一切、所有事情"，指所有知识点。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他问了我一个英语语法问题，我也答不上来。either 用于否定句末尾，表示"也（不）"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说他欣赏我的诚实。said 引导的宾语从句中，从句用过去时 appreciated。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：经过两周的努力，张伟取得了很好的考试成绩。叙述过去发生的事，用一般过去时 got。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词（形容词性物主代词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有你的帮助，我做不到。help 为名词，前面需用形容词性物主代词 your。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（省略 to 的不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助别人也帮助我复习了自己的知识。help sb. (to) do sth.，to 可省略，用动词原形 review。故选 A。
