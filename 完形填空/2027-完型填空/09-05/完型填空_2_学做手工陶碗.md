# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工陶碗

## 完形填空原文

Our school organized a pottery class as part of the after-school activities this term. I was one of the first students ___1___ sign up. I had seen pottery making in movies and always thought it looked ___2___.

The teacher prepared clay, a pottery wheel, and some simple tools ___3___ us. She first demonstrated how to center the clay on the wheel. It looked easy, ___4___ when I tried, the clay kept flying off.

"Don't press too hard. Let your hands move ___5___ with the wheel," the teacher advised. After several ___6___, I finally managed to keep the clay in place. Then I slowly shaped it into a small bowl.

The bowl was not ___7___ perfect — one side was a bit higher than the other. But the teacher said that was what made handmade things ___8___. "No two pieces are exactly alike," she smiled.

We left our bowls to dry for a week. ___9___ they were dry enough, the teacher helped us glaze and fire them in a kiln. When I held the finished bowl in my hands, it felt ___10___ heavier than before. I decided to give it to my mother as a present.

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. interest &emsp;&emsp; B. interesting &emsp;&emsp; C. interested &emsp;&emsp; D. interests
3. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but
5. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gently &emsp;&emsp; C. quick &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
6. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. tries &emsp;&emsp; C. tried &emsp;&emsp; D. trying
7. A. complete &emsp;&emsp; B. completely &emsp;&emsp; C. completing &emsp;&emsp; D. completed
8. A. special &emsp;&emsp; B. specially &emsp;&emsp; C. common &emsp;&emsp; D. commonly
9. A. Unless &emsp;&emsp; B. Until &emsp;&emsp; C. After &emsp;&emsp; D. Before
10. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. most

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定式作定语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我是第一批报名的学生之一。当名词前有 the first/last 等序数词修饰时，常用不定式作后置定语。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（-ed 与 -ing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直觉得做陶看起来很有趣。looked 为系动词，后接形容词；interesting 修饰事物，表示"令人感兴趣的"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师为我们准备了粘土、陶轮和一些简单的工具。for 表示"为了……"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看起来很容易，但当我试的时候，粘土一直飞出去。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让你的手随着轮子轻柔地移动。修饰动词 move，需用副词 gently。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词单复数
**【解析】** 本句意为：经过好几次尝试。several 后接可数名词复数 tries。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：碗并不完全完美。修饰形容词 perfect，需用副词 completely。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这正是手工制品特别之处。make sth. + adj.（宾语补足语），special 表示"特别的"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：等它们足够干了之后，老师帮我们上釉并烧制。After 引导时间状语从句，表示"在……之后"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：它感觉比以前重了很多。much 修饰比较级 heavier，表示"……得多"。故选 C。
