# 中考英语完形填空：学做桂花糕

## 完形填空原文

Grandma loves sweet osmanthus flowers, so Mom decided ___1___ teach me to make osmanthus cakes at home. The kitchen table ___2___ with rice flour, sugar, and honey. Warm steam later fogged the window, and the sweet smell drifted into the living room.

First, we mixed the dry ingredients ___3___ a bowl. Grandma reminded me ___4___ add the flowers gently, or the lovely smell ___5___ disappear too soon. She also taught me how to sift the flour so the cakes would feel soft.

While the steamer worked, I watched the clock and felt ___6___ excited than hungry. When the cakes were done, they looked ___7___ small golden moons. Dad took pictures and said they were almost too pretty to eat.

Grandma tasted one and said it was ___8___ sweet snack she had this autumn. I offered ___9___ to our neighbors, too. That day, I learned cooking could ___10___ shared love into a room. Even a small plate can carry a family's warm memories across seasons.

## 选项

1. A. / &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of  
2. A. cover &emsp;&emsp; B. covered &emsp;&emsp; C. was covered &emsp;&emsp; D. was covering  
3. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
5. A. will &emsp;&emsp; B. would &emsp;&emsp; C. can &emsp;&emsp; D. must  
6. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  
7. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. likes &emsp;&emsp; C. liking &emsp;&emsp; D. liked  
8. A. nice &emsp;&emsp; B. nicer &emsp;&emsp; C. nicest &emsp;&emsp; D. the nicest  
9. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs  
10. A. turn &emsp;&emsp; B. turns &emsp;&emsp; C. turning &emsp;&emsp; D. be turned  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide to teach 为 decide to do sth.。decide 后不接 decide / for/of 此类结构表“决定做”。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态  
**【解析】** table 与 cover 为被动关系“桌上摆满了……”，且叙述过去，用 was covered。cover/covered 缺助动词；was covering 为主动进行，语义不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 in 与容器搭配  
**【解析】** mix... in a bowl 表示“在碗里混合”。on/at/by 与 bowl 搭配不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do）  
**【解析】** remind me to add... 为 remind sb. to do sth.。for/of/with 不构成 remind 的正确结构。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 过去将来时 / 情态动词 would 表推测结果  
**【解析】** 主句 reminded 为过去时，从句“香味会太快消失”用 would 表从过去看将来可能发生的结果。will 与过去主句时态不一致；can/must 语气不符。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** than hungry 提示比较级，excited 的比较级用 more excited。many/much 修饰名词；most 为最高级。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 like 表“像……”  
**【解析】** looked like small golden moons 表示“看起来像小月亮”。likes 为动词第三人称单数；liking/liked 不能接名词短语作补语。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** she had this autumn 表范围，用 the nicest sweet snack。nice/nicer 无最高级语境；nicest 缺 the。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** offered 后接宾语，指代 cakes，用人称代词宾格 them。they 为主格；their/theirs 为物主代词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 情态动词后接动词原形  
**【解析】** could 后接动词原形 turn，could turn shared love into... 表示“能把……变成……”。turns/turning 不符合 could 用法；be turned 被动与“主动传递爱”的主动表达不符。故选 A。
