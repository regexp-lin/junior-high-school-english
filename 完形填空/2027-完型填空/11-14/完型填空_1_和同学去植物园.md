# 中考英语完形填空：和同学去植物园

## 完形填空原文

On a sunny Sunday, Lisa and I visited the city botanical garden ___1___ bus. We bought tickets online ___2___ we could skip the long line at the gate. Birds sang in the trees, and a light wind moved the leaves like small green flags.

The air smelled fresher ___3___ in the downtown streets. We took photos beside a pond ___4___ many koi fish swam slowly. A guide told us ___5___ names of several rare plants. She also reminded us not to pick flowers inside the greenhouse.

At noon, we rested on a bench and shared sandwiches ___6___ each other. Lisa said this trip was ___7___ relaxing than staying at home on phones. We drank cold water and planned which path to explore next.

Before leaving, we picked ___8___ fallen leaves for a science project. I hope ___9___ we can come again in spring ___10___ the cherry trees bloom. Lisa said she would bring a sketchbook next time and draw the flowers in pencil lines first.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
2. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until  
3. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. who  
5. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. between  
7. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. much  
8. A. few &emsp;&emsp; B. a few &emsp;&emsp; C. little &emsp;&emsp; D. a little  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
10. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. though  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 by 表示交通方式  
**【解析】** by bus 为固定搭配“乘公交车”。in/on/at 与 bus 搭配不如 by 标准（on the bus 强调在车内也可，但题眼为 by bus）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句连词 so that  
**【解析】** “网上购票”的目的是“可以跳过排队”，表目的用 so that。although 让步；unless 条件；until 直到，语义不符。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 比较级标志 than  
**【解析】** fresher 为比较级，后接 than 引出比较对象。as/like/to 不构成比较结构。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系副词 where  
**【解析】** 先行词 pond 表地点，从句 fish swam 缺地点状语，用 where。what 不引导定语从句；which/who 在从句中作主语/宾语，此处需状语。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定冠词表特指  
**【解析】** names of several rare plants 表特指那些植物的名称，用 the。a/an 表泛指；零冠词不适合此特指语境。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 share sth. with sb.  
**【解析】** share sandwiches with each other 表示“互相分享三明治”。to/for/between 不构成 share 的常见搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（more + 形容词）  
**【解析】** than staying at home 提示比较级，relaxing 为多音节形容词，用 more relaxing。very/much 不接 than；most 为最高级。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** a few 修饰可数名词复数  
**【解析】** leaves 为可数名词复数，肯定含义“捡了一些落叶”，用 a few。few 表否定；little/a little 修饰不可数名词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 that  
**【解析】** hope that we can come... 中 that 引导宾语从句。hope 后不接 hope what；if 虽偶见，但中学阶段更稳妥用 that 表愿望；how 表方式不符。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句连词 when  
**【解析】** “春天再来”与“樱花盛开”为时间关系，用 when。if 条件；because 原因；though 让步。故选 B。
