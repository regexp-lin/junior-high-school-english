# 中考英语完形填空：参加英语角

## 完形填空原文

Our school English Corner meets ___1___ Thursday after lunch in the garden near the library. Last week, the topic was “travel plans,” ___2___ many students shared ideas about summer trips. I felt shy ___3___ I had never spoken English in front of strangers. A senior student smiled ___4___ me and asked simple questions about my hometown. ___5___ I answered slowly, she listened patiently and repeated my sentences more naturally. By the end of the hour, I could describe ___6___ favorite park without looking at my notebook. The teacher reminded us ___7___ polite when we disagreed with others. I went home happier ___8___ when I arrived because I had made two new friends. Now I believe practice is ___9___ useful tool for building confidence. I have decided ___10___ every meeting if my schedule allows.

## 选项

1. A. at &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. by
2. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. If &emsp;&emsp; B. When &emsp;&emsp; C. Although &emsp;&emsp; D. Unless
6. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine
7. A. be &emsp;&emsp; B. being &emsp;&emsp; C. to be &emsp;&emsp; D. been
8. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. when
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
10. A. attend &emsp;&emsp; B. attending &emsp;&emsp; C. to attend &emsp;&emsp; D. attended

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（星期）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学校英语角在每周四午餐后在图书馆旁的小花园举行。在星期几用 on，故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周的话题是“旅行计划”，许多同学分享了关于暑假出行的想法。并列顺承，用 and，故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很害羞，因为我从未在陌生人面前说过英语。说明原因用 because，故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位学姐对我微笑…… smile at sb. 表示“对某人微笑”，故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然我回答得很慢，但她耐心倾听…… 让步关系用 Although，故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：……我能描述我最喜欢的公园…… 修饰 favorite park 用 my，故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师提醒我们在与他人意见不同时要有礼貌。remind sb. to be...，故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 比较级结构（than 引导比较状语从句）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我回家时比刚到那里时更开心，因为我交到了两个新朋友。happier than when I arrived 表示“比……更开心”，than 引出比较对象，故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：……我相信练习是建立自信的一种有用工具。useful 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a，故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果时间允许，我决定参加每一次活动。decide to do sth.，故选 C。
