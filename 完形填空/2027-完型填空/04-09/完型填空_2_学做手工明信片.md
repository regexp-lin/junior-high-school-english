# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工明信片

## 完形填空原文

My cousin returned from studying abroad, ___1___ she brought me beautiful stamps. We decided ___2___ handmade postcards together and mail them to friends. I cut thick paper ___3___ the size of my palm and painted soft clouds on the front. She taught me ___4___ leave enough space for the address. While the paint was drying, we wrote warm messages ___5___ the back in both Chinese and English. I almost forgot ___6___ a stamp in the corner, but she reminded me just ___7___ time. At the post office, the clerk said our cards were ___8___ creative than many printed ones. Walking home, I imagined ___9___ friends would smile when they opened the envelopes. This small project made me think ___10___ words can travel farther when they carry a personal touch.

## 选项

1. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. into
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
6. A. stick &emsp;&emsp; B. sticking &emsp;&emsp; C. to stick &emsp;&emsp; D. stuck
7. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
8. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. whether
10. A. this &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. it &emsp;&emsp; D. one

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐从国外学习回来，还给我带了漂亮的邮票。并列顺承，用 and，故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们决定一起做手工明信片并寄给朋友。decide to do sth.，故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把厚卡纸裁成手掌大小。cut...into... 表示“把……裁成……”，故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（teach sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我要留出足够空间写地址。teach sb. to do sth.，故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：……我们在背面用中英文写下温暖的祝福。on the back 表示“在背面”，故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（forget to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我差点忘记在角落贴邮票。forget to do sth. 表示“忘记去做某事（未做）”，故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语（in time）
**【解析】** 本句意为：……但她在最后一刻提醒了我。in time 表示“及时”，故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：邮局工作人员说我们的卡片比许多印刷品更有创意。than 提示用 more creative，故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家的路上，我想象朋友们打开信封时会怎样微笑。表示方式/程度用 how，故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个小项目让我觉得，当话语带有个人温度时，它们能走得更远。think 后为宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 that 引导，故选 B。
