# 中考英语完形填空：全国助残日主题班会

## 完形填空原文

On China's National Day ___1___ Helping Persons with Disabilities, our class held a special meeting. The monitor played ___2___ short film about barrier-free facilities in public places. Many students took notes ___3___ they watched. After the film, our teacher asked us ___4___ share ideas in groups. I said we should offer seats ___5___ people who need help on buses. Another classmate suggested learning some simple sign language ___6___ communicate better. Our teacher praised us ___7___ thinking about respect instead of pity. At the end of the class meeting, we wrote ___8___ promises on colorful cards and put them on the wall. I decided ___9___ volunteer at least twice this semester. That day made me understand ___10___ small actions can make a big difference.

## 选项

1. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. in
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
3. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. while &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. / &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
6. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. / &emsp;&emsp; C. volunteering &emsp;&emsp; D. volunteered
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. how

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（Day for …）
**【解析】** 本句意为：在全国助残日这一天……。National Day for Helping Persons with Disabilities 为常见表达，用 for 表示“为了/旨在”。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班长播放了一个关于公共场所无障碍设施的短片。short 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（while 表示“当……时”，可接延续性动词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多同学边看边记笔记。while they watched 强调观看过程中同时记笔记。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（ask sb. to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们分组分享想法。ask sb. to do sth. 后接不定式。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（offer sth. for sb. / seats for …）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们应该在公交车上给需要帮助的人让座。offer seats for people 表示“为……让座/提供座位”。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词短语（in order to 表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：另一位同学建议学一些简单的手语，以便更好地交流。表目的用 in order to。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（praise sb. for …）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师表扬我们想到尊重而不是怜悯。praise sb. for doing sth. 为固定搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析（形容词性物主代词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在彩色卡片上写下我们的承诺……。修饰 promises 用 our。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定本学期至少做两次志愿服务。decide to volunteer，不定式作宾语。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天让我明白小小的行动也能带来很大改变。understand 后宾语从句结构完整，用 that。故选 B。
