# 中考英语完形填空：第一次参加辩论赛

## 完形填空原文

Our English teacher started an after-school debate club. The topic was whether students should use phones at school. I joined the team ___1___ supported the "no phones" side.

The first week was difficult. I had to read articles, underline key facts, and learn ___2___ to organize an argument. My teammate Kevin helped me practice opening sentences ___3___ home.

During the real debate, my knees shook. When the judge asked for our first speaker, I stood up ___4___ than I expected. My voice was not loud, ___5___ each sentence was clear.

The other team raised strong points. Instead of shouting, we listened and took notes. In the free debate part, I used one example ___6___ our teacher had mentioned in class. That made our side more convincing.

We didn't win, ___7___ the judge said we showed good manners and logic. After the match, I felt tired ___8___ satisfied. Debating is like sports for the mind—you train, you lose sometimes, ___9___ you still grow.

I have signed up ___10___ next month's topic about reading paper books versus e-books.

## 选项

1. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. after
4. A. fast &emsp;&emsp; B. faster &emsp;&emsp; C. fastest &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我加入了支持“禁用手机”一方的队伍。先行词 team 指物，从句缺主语，用 that/which 均可，本题选项中 that 正确。who 指人；whose 表所属。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：学习如何组织论证。how to organize 如何组织。what 什么；why 为什么；when 何时。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：队友凯文帮我在家练习开场句。at home 在家。in 在……里；on 在……上；after 在……之后。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我站起来比预想的更快。than 提示比较级 faster。fast 原级；fastest 最高级；slowly 慢地。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的声音不大，但每句话都很清楚。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用了一个老师在课上提到的例子。先行词 example 指物，从句缺宾语，可用 that/which；选项有 that，选 that。what 不引导定语从句；who 指人；whose 表所属。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们没有赢，但裁判说我们展现了礼貌与逻辑。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比赛后我又累又满足。tired but satisfied 累但满足。and 也可；此处强调对比用 but。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你会训练，有时会输，但你仍会成长。转折用 but。and 表顺承；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我已报名参加下个月关于纸质书与电子书的辩题。sign up for 报名参加。故选 B。
