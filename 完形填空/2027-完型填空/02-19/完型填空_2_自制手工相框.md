# 中考英语完形填空：自制手工相框

## 完形填空原文

I wanted to send my grandparents a photo of our family trip. ___1___ buying a frame online, I decided to make one with cardboard and string.

I measured the photo and cut a window in the cardboard. The edges were rough, ___2___ I covered them with washi tape. Then I painted the border light blue ___3___ match the sea in the picture.

The hardest part was tying string into a simple pattern. My fingers were clumsy, ___4___ I practiced five times until it looked neat. Finally, I wrote the date on the back ___5___ a marker.

When my grandparents called, they said the frame made the photo feel "closer." Their voices sounded ___6___ on the phone, and I smiled.

This project taught me that handmade gifts take time, ___7___ they carry personal warmth. I have already started ___8___ for my cousin's birthday gift.

Next time, I will try using recycled wood ___9___ it is safer with adult help. Creativity is not about perfect skills ___10___ about willing hearts.

## 选项

1. A. Instead of &emsp;&emsp; B. Because of &emsp;&emsp; C. Thanks to &emsp;&emsp; D. As for
2. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but
3. A. in order to &emsp;&emsp; B. such that &emsp;&emsp; C. as soon as &emsp;&emsp; D. even though
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
6. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. soft &emsp;&emsp; C. loud &emsp;&emsp; D. bright
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. plan &emsp;&emsp; B. planning &emsp;&emsp; C. to plan &emsp;&emsp; D. planned
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until
10. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我没有在网上买相框，而是决定用纸板和绳子做一个。instead of 而不是、代替。because of 因为；thanks to 多亏；as for 至于。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：边缘很粗糙，所以我用纸胶带包住。因果关系，用 so。and 并列；or 或者；but 但是。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把边框涂成浅蓝色，以与照片里的大海相配。in order to 为了，表目的。such that 如此……以致；as soon as 一……就；even though 尽管。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手指很笨，但我练习了五次直到看起来整齐。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后我用马克笔在背面写下日期。with a marker 用马克笔。in 在……里；on 在……上；by 通过。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们在电话里的声音听起来很温柔。soft 温柔的、轻柔的。cold 冷的；loud 大声的；bright 明亮的。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工作品耗时，但它们承载个人温度。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（start doing）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我已经开始为表弟的生日礼物做计划。start doing/to do 开始做，planning 符合语境。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次如果有大人帮忙更安全，我会尝试用回收木料。if 如果。unless 除非；although 尽管；until 直到。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列结构（not... but...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：创造力不在于技巧完美，而在于用心。not... but... 不是……而是……。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。
