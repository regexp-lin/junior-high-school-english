# 中考英语完形填空：春天校园里的樱花

## 完形填空原文

When March wind turns softer, the cherry trees ___1___ our teaching building become pink clouds. Students stop ___2___ photos between classes.

Yesterday, our biology teacher took us ___3___ to observe the flowers. She explained ___4___ petals fall to protect the seeds inside. I wrote the key words ___5___ my notebook carefully.

Some petals landed on my shoulders ___6___ light snow. I picked one up and smelled ___7___, but there was only the scent of spring rain.

After school, my friends and I sat on a bench ___8___ the tree. We talked about future trips and promised ___9___ each other when the flowers bloom again next year.

Walking home, I thought nature was ___10___ generous teacher that never charges a fee.

## 选项

1. A. in the front of &emsp;&emsp; B. in front of &emsp;&emsp; C. behind &emsp;&emsp; D. below
2. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. to take &emsp;&emsp; C. taking &emsp;&emsp; D. took
3. A. out &emsp;&emsp; B. outside &emsp;&emsp; C. inside &emsp;&emsp; D. away
4. A. why &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where
5. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
6. A. as if &emsp;&emsp; B. even if &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. as long as
7. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. its &emsp;&emsp; C. them &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. under &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. to write &emsp;&emsp; C. writing &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：教学楼前的樱花树变成粉色云团。in front of 表示“在……（外部的）前面”。in the front of 指内部前部。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们课间停下来拍照。stop to do sth 表示“停下来去做另一件事”。stop doing 表示停止正在做的事，此处为停下（走路）去拍照，用 to take。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生物老师带我们去户外观察花。take sb outside 表示“带到外面”。take out 常需宾语或搭配不同。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她解释花瓣为何会落下以保护里面的种子。表示原因用 why。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把关键词认真记在笔记本里。in my notebook 表示“在笔记本里”。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些花瓣落在我肩上，就像轻雪。as if 表示“好像”。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我捡起一片闻了闻它。指代单数花瓣 one，用 it。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：放学后我们坐在树下长椅上。under the tree 表示“在树下”。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们约定明年花再开时要互相写信。promise to do sth 为固定搭配。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大自然是一位从不收学费的慷慨老师。generous 以辅音音素开头，用 a。故选 A。
