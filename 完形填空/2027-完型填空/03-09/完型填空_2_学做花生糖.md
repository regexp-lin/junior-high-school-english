# 中考英语完形填空：学做花生糖

## 完形填空原文

My uncle owns a snack workshop. During the spring fair, he taught my sister and me ___1___ peanut candy step by step.

We roasted peanuts ___2___ they turned golden and peeled off the skins. Then we cooked sugar syrup ___3___ a thick pan until it became amber colored.

"You must stir all the time, ___4___ the sugar will burn," he warned. My sister took a turn, ___5___ her arm soon got tired.

When the syrup was ready, we mixed in the nuts quickly and pressed the mixture ___6___ a wooden frame. After it cooled, we cut it ___7___ small squares.

The first piece stuck ___8___ my teeth, but the nutty sweetness made me smile. Uncle said, "Good cooks learn ___9___ their mistakes."

We packed some candy ___10___ neighbors. Their praise felt warmer than the sugar.

## 选项

1. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. to make &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. made
2. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. though
3. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. from &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
10. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他一步步教我和妹妹做花生糖。teach sb to do sth 为固定结构。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把花生烤到金黄。until 表示“直到……为止”。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后在厚底锅里熬糖浆。in a thick pan 表示“在锅里”。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：必须一直搅拌，否则会糊。or 表示“否则”。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹接力搅拌，但她的胳膊很快就酸了。转折关系用 but。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把混合物压进木框里。press...into... 表示“压进……里”。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冷却后切成小块。cut...into... 表示“切成……”。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一块粘在我牙齿上。stick to 表示“粘住”。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好厨师会从错误中学习。learn from mistakes 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们装了一些糖给邻居。for neighbors 表示“给邻居（带去）”。故选 B。
