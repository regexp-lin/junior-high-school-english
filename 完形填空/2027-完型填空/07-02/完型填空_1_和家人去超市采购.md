# 中考英语完形填空：和家人去超市采购

## 完形填空原文

On Saturday morning, my parents and I went ___1___ a big supermarket near our home. Mom had made ___2___ shopping list the night before, so we wouldn't forget anything important.

In the fruit section, Dad picked some apples ___3___ looked fresh and sweet. I helped carry the basket ___4___ it became heavier and heavier. Then we walked ___5___ the dairy area to buy milk and yogurt.

While Mom ___6___ the prices on the shelves, I noticed a sign that said "Buy one, get one free". I pointed it ___7___ her and she smiled, saying we could save a little money.

At the checkout, all our things ___8___ scanned by a young worker quickly. On the way home, Dad drove ___9___ than usual because the ice cream might melt. That trip taught me ___10___ plan shopping more wisely next time.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. in
2. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. although
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at
6. A. compares &emsp;&emsp; B. compared &emsp;&emsp; C. was comparing &emsp;&emsp; D. is comparing
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
8. A. are &emsp;&emsp; B. were &emsp;&emsp; C. have been &emsp;&emsp; D. will be
9. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. more careful &emsp;&emsp; C. most carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. more carefully
10. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（go to + 地点）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和父母去了家附近的一家大型超市。go to a supermarket 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈前一晚列了一张购物清单。shopping 以辅音音素开头，泛指用 a。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句（which 指物）
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸挑了一些看起来新鲜又甜的苹果。先行词 apples 为物，从句缺主语，用 which。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（until 表"直到"）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我帮忙提篮子，直到它越来越重。until 表示动作持续到某时。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（walk to）
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们走向奶制品区。walk to 表示"走向"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 过去进行时
**【解析】** 本句意为：当妈妈正在比较货架上的价格时。While 强调同时进行，叙述过去用过去进行时 was comparing。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（point sth. to sb.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把标牌指给她看。point…to sb. 表示"把……指给某人"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 一般过去时的被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：在收银台，我们所有的东西都被一名年轻工作人员快速扫描。things 与 scan 为被动关系，叙述过去用 were scanned。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词比较级（修饰动词 drove）
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸开车比平时更小心。than 提示比较级；修饰动词 drove 用副词 carefully 的比较级 more carefully。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：那次采购教会我下次如何更明智地规划购物。teach sb. how to do 表示"教某人如何做"。故选 A。
