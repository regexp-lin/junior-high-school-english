# 中考英语完形填空：夏日游泳课

## 完形填空原文

This summer, my parents signed me up ___1___ a swimming class. I was a little nervous because I had never learned to swim ___2___.

On the first day, the coach introduced ___3___ and told us the rules. He said safety was the most important thing in the pool. We must always listen ___4___ his instructions.

The coach started by teaching us how to hold our breath under water. I was scared at first, but he was very ___5___ and guided me step by step. By the end of the first lesson, I could float ___6___ my back.

During the second week, we learned the freestyle stroke. Moving my arms and legs at the same time was not ___7___ easy as I thought. I swallowed some water a few times, ___8___ I didn't give up.

After three weeks of training, I ___9___ swim across the pool without stopping. My coach said I made great progress. Now I really enjoy ___10___ in the cool water on hot summer days.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. ago &emsp;&emsp; D. later
3. A. he &emsp;&emsp; B. him &emsp;&emsp; C. his &emsp;&emsp; D. himself
4. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
5. A. patient &emsp;&emsp; B. patiently &emsp;&emsp; C. patience &emsp;&emsp; D. impatient
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. as
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
9. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. may &emsp;&emsp; C. could &emsp;&emsp; D. must
10. A. swim &emsp;&emsp; B. to swim &emsp;&emsp; C. swimming &emsp;&emsp; D. swam

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（sign up for 报名）
**【解析】** 本句意为：今年夏天，父母给我报了游泳班。"sign up for"表示"报名参加"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（before 之前）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点紧张因为我以前从没学过游泳。before 表示"之前"，常与完成时连用。ago 不用于完成时。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 反身代词（introduced himself 自我介绍）
**【解析】** 本句意为：教练做了自我介绍。"introduce oneself"表示"自我介绍"。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（listen to 听从）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们必须一直听从他的指示。"listen to"为固定搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 系动词 + 形容词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他非常有耐心，一步步指导我。was 为系动词，后接形容词 patient。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（float on one's back 仰浮）
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一节课结束时我能仰浮了。"float on one's back"为固定搭配。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定结构（not as...as 不如……）
**【解析】** 本句意为：同时动手臂和腿不像我想的那么容易。"not as + adj. + as"表示"不如……"。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（but 转折）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我呛了几口水，但没有放弃。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 情态动词（could 表过去的能力）
**【解析】** 本句意为：经过三周的训练，我能不停地游过泳池。could 表示过去获得的能力。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（enjoy doing sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我非常享受在炎热夏天的凉水中游泳。"enjoy doing sth."后接动名词。故选 C。
