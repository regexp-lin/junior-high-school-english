# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工陶艺碗

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my family visited a pottery studio. We signed up for a beginner class ___1___ learn how to make clay bowls.

The teacher gave each of us a ball of soft clay. She showed us how to place it ___2___ the spinning wheel. "Use your hands to shape it gently," she said. "Don't press too ___3___ or the clay will break."

I tried my best to keep my hands ___4___, but the clay kept wobbling. The teacher came over and guided my fingers. ___5___ her help, my bowl slowly took shape.

My mother's bowl looked ___6___ better than mine. She used to take art classes when she was ___7___. My father's bowl was the funniest — it looked more like a hat ___8___ a bowl.

After shaping, we chose colors to paint ___9___ bowls. I picked sky blue because it reminded me of the sea. The teacher told us the bowls needed to be baked in a kiln for two days.

When we ___10___ them up the following week, I was so happy to hold my very first handmade bowl.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. harder &emsp;&emsp; D. hardest
4. A. steady &emsp;&emsp; B. steadily &emsp;&emsp; C. steadier &emsp;&emsp; D. steadiest
5. A. Under &emsp;&emsp; B. With &emsp;&emsp; C. For &emsp;&emsp; D. By
6. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. most
7. A. young &emsp;&emsp; B. younger &emsp;&emsp; C. youngest &emsp;&emsp; D. youth
8. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. than &emsp;&emsp; D. for
9. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours
10. A. pick &emsp;&emsp; B. picks &emsp;&emsp; C. picked &emsp;&emsp; D. picking

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式表目的）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们报名参加了初学者课程来学习如何做陶碗。to learn 表示目的。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（on 在……上面）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我们怎样把泥放在转盘上。on 表示"在……上面"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（hard 用力地）
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要按得太用力否则泥会碎。"too hard"表示"太用力"。hard 作副词意为"用力地"；hardly 意为"几乎不"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词 + 形容词（keep + 宾语 + adj.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尽力保持双手稳定。"keep + 宾语 + adj."结构，steady 为形容词。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（with one's help 在某人帮助下）
**【解析】** 本句意为：在她的帮助下，我的碗慢慢成形了。"With one's help"表示"在某人帮助下"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词修饰比较级（much + 比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈的碗看起来比我的好得多。much 修饰比较级 better。many/more/most 不修饰比较级。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词原级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她年轻时上过美术课。when she was young 为固定表达，用形容词原级。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 比较级（more like...than...）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它看起来更像帽子而不是碗。"more like...than..."表示"与其说像……不如说像……"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们选了颜色来给我们的碗上色。修饰名词 bowls 用形容词性物主代词 our。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 一般过去时
**【解析】** 本句意为：下周我们去取碗时。"the following week"提示过去时间，用 picked。故选 C。
