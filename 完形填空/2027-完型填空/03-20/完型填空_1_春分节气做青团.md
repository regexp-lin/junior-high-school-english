# 中考英语完形填空：春分节气做青团

## 完形填空原文

The Spring Equinox arrived, ___1___ Grandma said it was a good day to make sweet green rice balls. The kitchen smelled ___2___ mugwort and sticky rice flour.

We washed fresh mugwort leaves and boiled them ___3___ they turned soft. Grandma squeezed out the water and mixed the paste ___4___ flour until the dough looked like jade.

She wrapped red bean paste inside each ball. "Press gently, ___5___ the skin will break," she warned. I failed twice, ___6___ the third one looked round and smooth.

We steamed the balls over medium heat. When the lid lifted, white steam rose ___7___ a small cloud.

Grandpa tasted one and nodded, "This is the taste ___8___ spring." I laughed, knowing food can carry seasons ___9___ memories.

Before bedtime, I wrote a short English diary ___10___ the day and used the word "qingtuan" with pride.

## 选项

1. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
2. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. like &emsp;&emsp; D. for
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. although
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. but
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
8. A. about &emsp;&emsp; B. of &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. at
9. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
10. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. on

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：春分到了，奶奶说今天是做青团的好日子。顺承并列用 and。but/or/so 不符合叙事衔接。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房里有艾草和糯米粉的气味。smell of... 表示“有……的气味”。smell like 也可说，但 of 更强调气味来源成分。with/for 不典型。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把新鲜艾草煮到变软。until 表示“直到……为止”。if/because/although 不符合煮制过程。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把草泥和面粉混合。mix...with... 为固定搭配，表示“把……与……混合”。to/for/at 不符合 mix 搭配。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轻轻按压，否则会破皮。or 表示“否则”。and/so/but 不符合提醒语气。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我失败了两次，但第三个看起来又圆又光滑。转折关系用 but。and/or/so 不符合。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：白汽像小云一样升起。like a small cloud 为介词短语表比喻。as 多接从句；for/of 不自然。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这就是春天的味道。the taste of spring 表示“春天的味道”。about/on/at 不如 of 贴切名词修饰。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：食物能承载季节与记忆。seasons 与 memories 并列，用 and。or/but/so 不符合并列关系。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：睡前我写了一篇关于这一天的英语日记。a diary about the day 表示“关于这一天的日记”。of/for/on 不如 about 贴切“内容主题”。故选 C。
