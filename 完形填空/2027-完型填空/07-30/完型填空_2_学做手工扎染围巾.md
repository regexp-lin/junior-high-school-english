# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工扎染围巾

## 完形填空原文

In the studio, Nina learned ___1___ tie-dye a cotton scarf. She folded the cloth ___2___ rubber bands to create patterns.

She dipped it ___3___ dye carefully and waited ___4___ the color looked even. The teacher warned her ___5___ wear gloves so her hands would not get stained.

When the scarf dried, the colors looked ___6___ brighter than Nina expected. She tied it ___7___ her bag as a decoration. Her friends said it was ___8___ more creative gift than any other they had seen.

Nina promised ___9___ try a T-shirt next time. She realized traditional skills could be ___10___ fun than video games sometimes.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. although
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. too &emsp;&emsp; D. so
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at
8. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. the &emsp;&emsp; C. an &emsp;&emsp; D. /
9. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. trying &emsp;&emsp; C. to try &emsp;&emsp; D. tried
10. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. the most

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词+不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：妮娜学习如何扎染一条棉围巾。learn how to do sth. 学习如何做某事。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：她用橡皮筋折叠布料以形成图案。with rubber bands 用橡皮筋。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把围巾小心浸入染料中。dip…into… 把……浸入……。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：一直等到颜色看起来均匀。until 表示“直到……为止”。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师提醒她戴手套。warn sb. to do sth. 警告/提醒某人做某事。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：颜色比妮娜预想的更亮得多。修饰比较级 brighter 用 much。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把围巾系在包上作装饰。tie…to… 把……系到……上。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词 / 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：朋友们说这是比他们见过的任何其他礼物都更有创意的礼物。more creative gift 前用不定冠词 a 表示“一件”。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：妮娜答应下次尝试扎染 T 恤。promise to do sth. 承诺做某事。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她意识到传统技艺有时比电子游戏更有趣。than 提示用 more fun。故选 B。
