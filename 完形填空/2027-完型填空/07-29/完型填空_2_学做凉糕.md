# 中考英语完形填空：学做凉糕

## 完形填空原文

Amy's mother taught her ___1___ cook a cool summer jelly called lianggao. They boiled mung bean starch ___2___ water until it turned thick.

The mixture ___3___ into a flat box and cooled in the fridge. Amy cut it ___4___ cubes and poured brown sugar syrup on top.

The dessert tasted smooth ___5___ refreshing. Amy thought it was ___6___ sweet than ice cream, ___7___ she could eat a little more.

She wanted ___8___ the recipe to her pen pal. She wrote down each step ___9___ she would not forget. Making traditional food made her feel closer ___10___ her family culture.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how to &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. on
3. A. pour &emsp;&emsp; B. poured &emsp;&emsp; C. was poured &emsp;&emsp; D. pours
4. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. into &emsp;&emsp; D. at
5. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. and
6. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. least &emsp;&emsp; D. too
7. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
8. A. send &emsp;&emsp; B. sending &emsp;&emsp; C. to send &emsp;&emsp; D. sent
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. at

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词+不定式
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈教她如何做凉糕。teach sb. how to do sth. 教某人如何做某事。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她们把绿豆淀粉与水煮沸直到变稠。mix/boil…with water 用水熬煮混合。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 被动语态
**【解析】** 本句意为：混合物被倒入平底盒中。mixture 与 pour 为被动关系，叙述过去用 was poured。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：艾米把它切成小块。cut…into cubes 切成方块。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：甜点尝起来顺滑又清爽。并列形容词用 and。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：她觉得比冰淇淋不那么甜。than 提示用 less sweet。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：所以她可以多吃一点。因果关系，用 so。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她想把这个配方寄给笔友。want to do sth. 想要做某事。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词 / 目的状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：她写下每一步以免忘记。so that 表目的（此处含“以便不忘记”）。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词 / 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：让她感觉更贴近家庭文化。feel close to… 与……更亲近。故选 C。
