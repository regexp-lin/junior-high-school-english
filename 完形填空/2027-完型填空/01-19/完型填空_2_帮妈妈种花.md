# 中考英语完形填空：帮妈妈种花

## 完形填空原文

On a sunny afternoon, Mom asked me to help her ___1___ the balcony garden. We carried soil, small pots, and packets of seeds ___2___ the supermarket. I thought the work would finish in ten minutes, ___3___ I was wrong. The balcony looked small, yet preparing the soil and labeling each pot took much longer than I expected.

Mom taught me to dig small holes and drop the seeds ___4___. She explained that different flowers need different amounts of sunlight, so we had to plan where each pot should stand later. "Do not bury them too deep," she reminded me ___5___. My hands were dirty, ___6___ I enjoyed the earthy smell. A neighbor waved ___7___ us from the next balcony and praised Mom for her green plants.

Weeks later, tiny green shoots appeared. I watered them every morning ___8___ checking the weather forecast. Mom smiled and said, "Plants grow slowly, ___9___ they teach us patience." I nodded, realizing care is a kind of love ___10___ words.

## 选项

1. A. paint &emsp;&emsp; B. build &emsp;&emsp; C. clean &emsp;&emsp; D. plant
2. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. by
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. gently &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
5. A. angrily &emsp;&emsp; B. softly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. rudely
6. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
7. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. during
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. until
10. A. above &emsp;&emsp; B. below &emsp;&emsp; C. beyond &emsp;&emsp; D. against

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈让我帮忙“打理/种植”阳台小花园。A 项 paint 粉刷；B 项 build 建造；C 项 clean 打扫；D 项 plant 种植，与后文 seeds、土壤呼应。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们从超市搬回土、小花盆和种子袋。from 表示来源。A 项 on 在……上；B 项 at 在（小地点）；D 项 by 通过。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以为十分钟就能做完，“但”我错了。转折用 but。A 项 and 并列；C 项 or 选择；D 项 so 结果。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把种子“轻轻地”放进小坑。A 项 carelessly 粗心；B 项 quickly 快，未必强调轻柔；C 项 gently 轻柔；D 项 loudly 大声。播种动作常用 gently。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她“轻声地”提醒我别埋太深。A 项 angrily 生气；B 项 softly 温柔地、轻声地；C 项 loudly 大声；D 项 rudely 粗鲁。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手很脏，“但”我喜欢泥土气息。转折用 but。A 项 because 因为；B 项 so 所以；D 项 unless 除非。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：邻居在隔壁阳台向我们挥手。wave to sb. 为固定搭配。B 项 for；C 项 at 虽可见 wave at，但 wave to 更常用于“向某人挥手致意”；D 项 with 不搭配 wave。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我每天早上在查看天气预报“之后”给它们浇水。先看预报再决定是否需要浇水，更符合生活逻辑。A 项 after 在……之后；B 项 before 在……之前，若理解为“先浇水再查预报”，顺序不如 after 自然；C 项 without 不查就浇，不够稳妥；D 项 during 在……期间，搭配生硬。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：植物长得慢，“但”它们教我们耐心。转折用 but。A 项 if；B 项 unless；D 项 until。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关心是一种“超越”语言的爱。beyond words 表示“难以言表；超越言语”。A 项 above 在上方；B 项 below 在下方；D 项 against 反对。故选 C。
