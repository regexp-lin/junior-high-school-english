# 中考英语完形填空：参加社区棋类比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, our community held a chess and Chinese chess competition for teenagers. I signed up ___1___ Chinese chess because I had practiced with my grandfather during the holiday. On the match day, I arrived early and felt ___2___ when I saw so many strong players. Some parents stood quietly behind the players, and the quiet clicking of pieces made the hall feel serious.

My first opponent was a boy ___3___ glasses. He moved his pieces quickly, ___4___ I tried to stay calm and think twice. After a long fight, I won ___5___ a small advantage. The audience clapped politely.

In the final round, I lost ___6___ a more experienced player. I felt disappointed ___7___ first, but the organizer gave each of us a medal and said, "You have already shown great ___8___." My grandfather waited outside the hall and patted my shoulder gently, telling me that every master was once a beginner. Walking home, I knew I would keep ___9___ and come back next year ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. sleepy &emsp;&emsp; D. hungry
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. if
5. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. from
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. about
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
8. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. courage &emsp;&emsp; C. wealth &emsp;&emsp; D. silence
9. A. practicing &emsp;&emsp; B. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; C. complaining &emsp;&emsp; D. escaping
10. A. again &emsp;&emsp; B. never &emsp;&emsp; C. alone &emsp;&emsp; D. late

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我报名参加象棋项目。sign up for 表示“报名参加”。A 项 to 不与 sign up 构成此义；C 项 with 表伴随，不符；D 项 at 在。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到许多高手，我感到紧张。A 项 excited 兴奋；B 项 nervous 紧张；C 项 sleepy 困；D 项 hungry 饿。赛前压力更符合 nervous。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一个对手是戴眼镜的男孩。a boy with glasses 为常见表达。A 项 in；B 项 on；D 项 by 均不用于“戴眼镜”描述。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他落子很快，“但”我努力冷静、三思。表转折用 but。A 项 because 因为；B 项 so 所以；D 项 if 如果。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以微弱优势取胜。win by... 表示以某种优势/分差获胜。B 项 for；C 项 to；D 项 from 均不搭配 win 表方式。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我输给了一位更有经验的选手。lose to sb. 为固定搭配，表示“输给某人”。B 项 for；C 项 against 偶见口语，但规范书面语多用 to；D 项 about 关于。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我很失望。at first 为固定短语，表示“起初”。A 项 in first 不规范；B 项 on first 不成立；D 项 for first 不成立。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们已经展现了很大的勇气。A 项 luck 运气；B 项 courage 勇气；C 项 wealth 财富；D 项 silence 沉默。组织者鼓励用语用 courage 贴切。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会继续练习。keep doing sth. 表示继续做；practicing 符合棋手成长。B 项 sleeping；C 项 complaining；D 项 escaping 均不符。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：明年再来一次。again 表示“再一次”。B 项 never 从不；C 项 alone 独自；D 项 late 晚。故选 A。
