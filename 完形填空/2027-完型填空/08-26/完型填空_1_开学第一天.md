# 中考英语完形填空：开学第一天

## 完形填空原文

The first day of the new term finally came. I woke up much ___1___ than usual and put on my school uniform quickly. After a simple breakfast, I walked ___2___ the school gate with my heavy backpack.

Everything looked ___3___ because the school had been repainted during the summer holiday. I tried to find my new classroom, but I ___4___ my way on the third floor. A kind senior student noticed my confusion and ___5___ me to the right room.

When I entered the classroom, several old friends were already there. They waved at me ___6___ big smiles. Our new head teacher, Ms. Chen, introduced ___7___ and told us about the class rules. She spoke ___8___ a gentle voice, which made everyone feel relaxed.

After school, I told my mother ___9___ the exciting day. She was glad that I enjoyed it. "Every new beginning is ___10___ chance to grow," she said with a warm smile.

## 选项

1. A. early &emsp;&emsp; B. earlier &emsp;&emsp; C. earliest &emsp;&emsp; D. the earliest
2. A. through &emsp;&emsp; B. across &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. past
3. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. familiar
4. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. found &emsp;&emsp; C. knew &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot
5. A. brought &emsp;&emsp; B. led &emsp;&emsp; C. carried &emsp;&emsp; D. pushed
6. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. from
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我比平时早起了很多。than usual 提示用比较级，early 的比较级为 earlier。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我背着沉重的书包穿过校门。through 强调从内部穿过（如门、通道），符合语境。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一切看起来都不同了，因为暑假期间学校重新粉刷过。由后文 repainted 可知外观发生变化，用 different。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我在三楼迷了路。lose one's way 表示"迷路"，此处用过去时 lost。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位友善的高年级同学注意到我的困惑，把我带到了正确的教室。led sb. to 表示"引领某人到"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们带着大大的笑容向我挥手。with 表示伴随状态，"带着……"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 反身代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：新班主任陈老师做了自我介绍。introduce oneself 表示"自我介绍"，主语为 Ms. Chen，用 herself。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她用轻柔的声音讲话。in a ... voice 为固定搭配，表示"以……的声音"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：放学后我向妈妈讲了这令人兴奋的一天。tell sb. about sth. 表示"告诉某人关于某事"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 冠词
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一个新的开始都是一次成长的机会。chance 为可数名词单数，且 chance 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 a。故选 A。
