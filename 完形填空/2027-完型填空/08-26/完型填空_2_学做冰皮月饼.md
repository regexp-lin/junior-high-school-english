# 中考英语完形填空：学做冰皮月饼

## 完形填空原文

Mid-Autumn Festival was coming, so my grandmother decided ___1___ me how to make snow-skin mooncakes. She prepared sticky rice flour, sugar, and different fillings ___2___ the kitchen table.

"First, mix the flour and water ___3___," she said while showing me the correct way. I followed her instructions and kneaded the dough until it became ___4___ enough. Then she taught me to wrap the filling ___5___ the dough carefully.

My first mooncake looked quite ugly ___6___ I didn't give up. After several tries, I ___7___ made a round one. Grandmother smiled and said it was even ___8___ than hers. I knew she was just encouraging me, but I still felt ___9___.

We put the mooncakes in the fridge. That evening, the whole family ___10___ them together under the bright moon. Nothing tasted better than homemade food shared with loved ones.

## 选项

1. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. to teach &emsp;&emsp; D. teaching
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. under
3. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. quick &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
4. A. smooth &emsp;&emsp; B. smoothly &emsp;&emsp; C. rough &emsp;&emsp; D. roughly
5. A. into &emsp;&emsp; B. onto &emsp;&emsp; C. inside &emsp;&emsp; D. outside
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. so &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. but
7. A. finally &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. never &emsp;&emsp; D. seldom
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
9. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. happily &emsp;&emsp; C. sad &emsp;&emsp; D. sadly
10. A. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; B. enjoyed &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoying &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoys

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶决定教我做冰皮月饼。decide to do sth. 表示"决定做某事"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她在厨房桌子上准备了糯米粉、糖和各种馅料。on the table 表示"在桌子上"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：先慢慢地把面粉和水搅拌在一起。修饰动词 mix，需用副词 slowly。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我揉面团直到它变得足够光滑。became 为系动词，后接形容词作表语；smooth 表示"光滑的"，符合语境。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我把馅料小心地包在面团里面。wrap ... inside 表示"包裹在……里面"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的第一个月饼看起来很丑，但我没有放弃。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：经过几次尝试，我终于做出了一个圆的。finally 表示"最终、终于"，符合语境。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶笑着说这个甚至比她做的还好。than 提示用比较级，good 的比较级为 better。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我仍然感到高兴。felt 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天晚上，全家人在明亮的月光下一起享用了月饼。叙述过去发生的事情，用一般过去时 enjoyed。故选 B。
