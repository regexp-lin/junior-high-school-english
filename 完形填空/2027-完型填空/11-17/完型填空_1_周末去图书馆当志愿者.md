# 中考英语完形填空：周末去图书馆当志愿者

## 完形填空原文

This weekend, I volunteered ___1___ the city library for the first time. My job was ___2___ help visitors find books and keep the shelves tidy. At 9 a.m., the librarian gave ___3___ a name tag and a short training. A little boy came up and asked, “Excuse me, ___4___ can I find books about space?” I walked with him ___5___ the science corner and showed him a few choices. He thanked me ___6___ many times before he left. In the afternoon, I helped carry boxes that ___7___ full of new magazines. I felt tired, ___8___ I was happy because I learned ___9___ to communicate politely. The librarian said I could come again ___10___ I had free time.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to  
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
3. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
4. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
6. A. so &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. too &emsp;&emsp; D. quite  
7. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. are &emsp;&emsp; C. was &emsp;&emsp; D. were  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. when  
10. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词辨析（地点）  
**【解析】** volunteer at + 小地点（图书馆、学校等）为常见搭配。volunteer in 也可出现，但 at the library 更自然；on/to 与 library 搭配不当。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（be + to do 表职责）  
**【解析】** My job was to help... 中不定式作表语，说明 “工作内容是什么”。for help 结构错误；of/with 不能这样接动词原形表职责。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** gave 为动词，后接宾语，用人称代词宾格 me。I 为主格；my 为形容词性物主代词；mine 为名词性物主代词，均不能单独作 gave 的宾语。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句 / 特殊疑问词  
**【解析】** 男孩问 “在哪里能找到关于太空的书”，where 表地点。what 问事物；which 问哪一个；when 问时间，均与 find books 的地点无关。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 walk with sb. to sp.  
**【解析】** walk with him to the science corner 表示 “和他一起走到科学类书架区域”。for/with/by 单独使用无法准确表达 “走到某地”。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词与名词搭配（so many）  
**【解析】** “如此多次” 用 so many times，so 修饰 many，为常见搭配。very many 偏书面；too many 含 “过多” 的负面色彩；quite many 在此不如 so many 自然。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 主谓一致及时态  
**【解析】** 定语从句先行词 boxes 为复数，且叙述过去发生的事，谓语用 were full of。is/was 与复数主语不一致；are 为现在时，与全文过去语境不符。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 并列连词（转折）  
**【解析】** “很累” 与 “很开心” 为转折关系，用 but。and 表并列；or 表选择；so 表结果，均不符合。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”  
**【解析】** learn how to communicate 表示 “学会如何礼貌交流”。what to communicate 缺宾语内容；why/when 与 “礼貌交流的方式” 不符。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 连词 if 引导条件状语从句  
**【解析】** “如果有空可以再来”，if 表条件。unless 表 “除非”，语义变为 “除非有空否则别来”，与鼓励再来矛盾；although 表让步；until 表 “直到”，均不合适。故选 A。
