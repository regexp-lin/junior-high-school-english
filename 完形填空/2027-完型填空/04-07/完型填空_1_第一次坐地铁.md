# 中考英语完形填空：第一次坐地铁

## 完形填空原文

Last month, Dad took me downtown ___1___ the subway for the first time. We studied the route map carefully ___2___ we entered the station. I felt a little nervous ___3___ the crowd moved quickly through the gates. Dad taught me ___4___ swipe the card and stand on the right side of the escalator. When the train arrived, a polite voice reminded passengers ___5___ wait in line. I sat ___6___ the window and watched tunnels flash past like dark ribbons. An elderly woman dropped her bag, ___7___ I picked it up for her at once. She smiled and said young people today were ___8___ helpful than she had imagined. After we got off, Dad asked ___9___ I would like to take the subway again. I nodded because the trip was ___10___ exciting than scary.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. while &emsp;&emsp; D. until
3. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
4. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. beside &emsp;&emsp; B. besides &emsp;&emsp; C. below &emsp;&emsp; D. between
7. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or
8. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
9. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. whether &emsp;&emsp; C. which &emsp;&emsp; D. who
10. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. quite

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（交通方式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：上个月，爸爸第一次带我乘地铁去市中心。表示交通方式用 by subway，故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在进站之前仔细研究了路线图。before 表示“在……之前”，故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点紧张，因为人群快速通过闸机。说明原因用 because，故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** “疑问词 + to do”结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸教我如何刷卡…… teach sb. how to do sth.，故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（remind sb. to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：……礼貌的语音提示乘客排队等候。remind sb. to do sth.，故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在窗边，看着隧道像深色带子一样掠过。beside the window 表示“在窗边”，故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位老奶奶的包掉了，所以我立刻帮她捡起来。因果关系用 so，故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：……说现在的年轻人比她想象的更有帮助。than 提示用 more helpful，故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：下车后，爸爸问我是否还想再坐地铁。表示“是否”用 whether/if，选项为 whether，故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我点头，因为这次出行兴奋多于害怕。more...than... 结构，故选 A。
