# 中考英语完形填空：和爸爸修自行车

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday morning, I found that the front tire of my bicycle was flat. I was upset because I ___1___ to ride it to the library that afternoon.

My father saw my long face and said, "Why don't we fix it ___2___?" I was surprised because I had always thought repairing bikes was something only professional workers could do.

He took out a small toolbox from the garage and told me to turn the bike ___3___ down. Then he showed me how to remove the tire step by step. I watched ___4___ and tried to remember every detail.

The problem was a tiny nail stuck in the rubber. Father carefully pulled it out and patched the hole ___5___ a special rubber piece and glue. "This is called a puncture repair," he explained.

After waiting for the glue to dry, we put the tire ___6___ on the wheel. Father pumped it up and checked ___7___ there were any other leaks. Everything looked fine.

The whole repair took ___8___ than thirty minutes. I was amazed at how simple it was. Father said, "Many things in life seem difficult, but once you learn them, they become easy."

I thanked him for teaching me such a ___9___ skill. He smiled and said, "You'll find it even ___10___ useful when you grow up." That day, I learned much more than just fixing a bike.

## 选项

1. A. plan &emsp;&emsp; B. planned &emsp;&emsp; C. was planning &emsp;&emsp; D. have planned  
2. A. together &emsp;&emsp; B. alone &emsp;&emsp; C. already &emsp;&emsp; D. either  
3. A. face &emsp;&emsp; B. upside &emsp;&emsp; C. inside &emsp;&emsp; D. outside  
4. A. close &emsp;&emsp; B. closely &emsp;&emsp; C. closed &emsp;&emsp; D. closer  
5. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
6. A. away &emsp;&emsp; B. back &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. out  
7. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. how  
8. A. little &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. few &emsp;&emsp; D. fewer  
9. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. useful &emsp;&emsp; C. used &emsp;&emsp; D. usefully  
10. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. many &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. most  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 过去进行时表计划  
**【解析】** was planning to 意为"正打算"，过去进行时表示过去正在计划的动作。plan 为原形；planned 不体现"正在计划"；have planned 为现在完成时。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词辨析  
**【解析】** fix it together 意为"一起修理它"，together 表"一起"。alone 意为"独自"；already 意为"已经"；either 用于否定句。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 upside down  
**【解析】** turn the bike upside down 意为"把自行车翻过来倒置"。face down 意为"面朝下"不完全符合；inside/outside 意为"里面/外面"不合语境。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词 closely 与 close 辨析  
**【解析】** watched closely 意为"仔细地观看"，closely 表"仔细地、密切地"（抽象含义）。close 作副词时表"靠近地"（物理距离）；closed 为形容词"关着的"；closer 为比较级。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 介词 with 表工具/材料  
**【解析】** patched the hole with a rubber piece and glue 意为"用橡皮片和胶水补好洞"，with 表使用的工具材料。by 表方式；for 表目的；in 表在……里。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 动词短语 put back  
**【解析】** put the tire back on 意为"把轮胎放回去"，back 表"回到原处"。away 意为"收起/拿走"；off 意为"脱下"；out 意为"拿出"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连词（是否）  
**【解析】** checked if there were any other leaks 意为"检查是否还有其他漏洞"，if 表"是否"。that 引导陈述从句；what 意为"什么"；how 意为"怎样"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** less than + 数量  
**【解析】** less than thirty minutes 意为"不到三十分钟"，less 修饰不可数名词 time（此处省略）。little 为原级；few/fewer 修饰可数名词。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词修饰名词  
**【解析】** a useful skill 意为"一项有用的技能"，useful 为形容词修饰 skill。use 为名词/动词；used 为过去式/形容词"习惯的"；usefully 为副词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** even + 比较级  
**【解析】** even more useful 意为"甚至更有用"，even 修饰比较级。much 修饰比较级但此处 more 已是比较级标志词；many 不修饰形容词；most 为最高级。故选 C。
