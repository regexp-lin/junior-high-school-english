# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工水彩画

## 完形填空原文

Art has always been one of my favorite ___1___. Last month, my mother enrolled me in a weekend watercolor class at the community center.

The teacher, Mr. Gao, is a retired artist ___2___ has been painting for over forty years. On the first day, he didn't ask us to paint anything. Instead, he taught us how to hold the brush ___3___ and how to control the amount of water.

"Watercolor is different ___4___ other types of painting," he said. "You need to let the water do part of the work." This idea was new to me because I was used to ___5___ everything precisely with pencils.

In the second class, we began to paint a simple landscape — a sunset over a lake. Mr. Gao told us ___6___ too many details at first. "Start with big areas of color, and then add details later," he advised.

I dipped my brush in orange paint and made a wide stroke across the paper. The color spread ___7___ on the wet surface, creating a soft and dreamy effect. I was amazed ___8___ how beautiful it looked with so little effort.

By the end of the class, everyone's painting was different. ___9___ two paintings looked exactly the same. Mr. Gao said that was the beauty of watercolor — each painting was one of a kind.

I hung my painting on the wall in my room. Every time I look at it, I ___10___ of that peaceful afternoon. I can't wait for the next class.

## 选项

1. A. subject &emsp;&emsp; B. subjects &emsp;&emsp; C. subjective &emsp;&emsp; D. subjectively  
2. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what  
3. A. proper &emsp;&emsp; B. properly &emsp;&emsp; C. more proper &emsp;&emsp; D. most proper  
4. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. from &emsp;&emsp; D. at  
5. A. control &emsp;&emsp; B. controlled &emsp;&emsp; C. controlling &emsp;&emsp; D. controls  
6. A. not worry about &emsp;&emsp; B. to not worry about &emsp;&emsp; C. not to worry about &emsp;&emsp; D. worrying not about  
7. A. nature &emsp;&emsp; B. natural &emsp;&emsp; C. naturally &emsp;&emsp; D. natures  
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. of  
9. A. Not &emsp;&emsp; B. No &emsp;&emsp; C. Neither &emsp;&emsp; D. None  
10. A. think &emsp;&emsp; B. thought &emsp;&emsp; C. thinking &emsp;&emsp; D. thinks  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 名词复数  
**【解析】** one of my favorite subjects 意为"我最喜欢的科目之一"，one of 后接可数名词复数。subject 为单数；subjective 为形容词"主观的"；subjectively 为副词。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词（指人）  
**【解析】** 先行词 a retired artist 为人，在从句中作主语，用 who。which 指物；whom 作宾语；what 不引导定语从句。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** hold the brush properly 意为"正确地握笔"，修饰动词 hold 用副词。proper 为形容词；more proper/most proper 为比较级/最高级。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be different from  
**【解析】** be different from 意为"与……不同"，from 为固定介词。with/to/at 不与 different 构成此标准搭配。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** be used to + 动名词  
**【解析】** be used to doing sth. 意为"习惯于做某事"，to 为介词后接动名词 controlling。control 为原形；controlled 为过去式；controls 为三单。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. not to do）  
**【解析】** tell sb. not to do sth. 意为"告诉某人不要做某事"，not 放在 to 之前。not worry about 缺 to；to not worry about 语序错误；worrying not about 语序错误。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词  
**【解析】** spread naturally 意为"自然地扩散"，修饰动词 spread 用副词。nature 为名词；natural 为形容词；natures 为名词复数。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 be amazed at  
**【解析】** be amazed at 意为"对……感到惊讶"，at 为固定介词。in/on/of 不与 amazed 构成此搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** no + 名词表否定  
**【解析】** No two paintings looked exactly the same 意为"没有两幅画看起来完全一样"，no 直接修饰名词 two paintings。not 不直接修饰名词；neither 指两者都不；none 指三者以上都不（但后不直接接名词 + 动词）。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 一般现在时表习惯  
**【解析】** Every time I look at it, I think of... 描述经常性的习惯动作，用一般现在时。thought 为过去式；thinking 为现在分词；thinks 为三单（但主语为 I）。故选 A。
