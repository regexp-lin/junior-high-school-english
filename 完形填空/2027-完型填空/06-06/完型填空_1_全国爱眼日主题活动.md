# 中考英语完形填空：全国爱眼日主题活动

## 完形填空原文

June 6th is National Eye Care Day. Our school invited ___1___ eye doctor to give us a talk. He told us that protecting our eyes was very ___2___.

The doctor said many students spent too much time ___3___ at screens. This bad habit would make their eyesight ___4___ and worse. He suggested the "20-20-20" rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away ___5___ 20 seconds.

After the talk, the school nurse checked ___6___ eyes one by one. I was surprised to find that my left eye was not as good ___7___ my right eye. The nurse said I should sit ___8___ from the screen when using the computer.

Our teacher also moved the seats for some students ___9___ couldn't see the blackboard clearly. I promised myself to do eye exercises ___10___ every day from now on.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /
2. A. importance &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. importantly &emsp;&emsp; D. unimportant
3. A. look &emsp;&emsp; B. looked &emsp;&emsp; C. looking &emsp;&emsp; D. to look
4. A. bad &emsp;&emsp; B. badly &emsp;&emsp; C. worse &emsp;&emsp; D. worst
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
6. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours
7. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. than &emsp;&emsp; C. as &emsp;&emsp; D. to
8. A. far &emsp;&emsp; B. farther &emsp;&emsp; C. farthest &emsp;&emsp; D. near
9. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
10. A. serious &emsp;&emsp; B. seriously &emsp;&emsp; C. more serious &emsp;&emsp; D. most seriously

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 冠词（泛指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学校请了一位眼科医生给我们做讲座。eye 以元音音素 /aɪ/ 开头，泛指用 an。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 系动词 + 形容词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他告诉我们保护眼睛非常重要。was 为系动词，后接形容词 important。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（spend time doing sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多学生花太多时间盯着屏幕看。"spend time doing sth."为固定结构。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（比较级 + and + 比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个坏习惯会让视力越来越差。"worse and worse"表示"越来越差"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（for + 时间段）
**【解析】** 本句意为：看 20 英尺外的东西 20 秒。for 后接时间段。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：校医逐个检查了我们的眼睛。修饰名词 eyes 用形容词性物主代词 our。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（not as...as... 不如……）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的左眼不如右眼好。"not as + adj. + as"表示"不如……"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：护士说我用电脑时应该坐得离屏幕更远。此处暗含比较意义，farther 表示"更远"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句（who 指代人）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师也给看不清黑板的同学换了座位。先行词 students 指人，在从句中作主语，用 who。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词修饰动词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我保证从现在起每天认真做眼保健操。修饰动词 do 用副词 seriously。故选 B。
