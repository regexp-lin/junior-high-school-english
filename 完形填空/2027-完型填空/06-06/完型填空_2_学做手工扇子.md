# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工扇子

## 完形填空原文

In our art class, the teacher taught us ___1___ to make a paper fan. She prepared colored paper, glue and wooden sticks ___2___ each student.

First, we folded the paper back ___3___ forth into narrow strips. This step seemed easy, but it required a lot of ___4___. My first try was not neat enough, so I had to start again.

Then we glued the bottom of the folded paper ___5___ two sticks. The teacher walked around and helped those ___6___ needed it. She praised me because my fan looked ___7___ than before.

After that, some students drew flowers ___8___ their fans while others wrote short poems. I chose to paint a small bird ___9___ the fan. When I opened it up, the bird seemed to be flying.

Everyone was proud of ___10___ own fan. We took them home as gifts for our families.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. why
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
4. A. patient &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. patiently &emsp;&emsp; D. impatient
5. A. between &emsp;&emsp; B. among &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. behind
6. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whom &emsp;&emsp; D. what
7. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
9. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. to
10. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 疑问词 + 不定式（how to do sth.）
**【解析】** 本句意为：美术老师教我们如何做纸扇。"teach sb. how to do sth."为固定结构。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（for 表对象）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她为每个学生准备了彩纸、胶水和木棍。for 表示"为……"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（back and forth 来回）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把纸来回折成窄条。"back and forth"表示"来回地"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词（a lot of + 名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这一步看似简单，但需要很多耐心。"a lot of"后接名词 patience。patient 为形容词；patiently 为副词。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（between 在两者之间）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把折好的纸底部粘在两根木棍之间。between 表示"在两者之间"。among 用于三者及以上。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句（who 指代人）
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师走来走去帮助那些需要帮助的同学。先行词 those 指人，在从句中作主语，用 who。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级（than 提示）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她表扬我因为我的扇子看起来比之前好了。than 提示用比较级 better。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（on 在表面上）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些同学在扇子上画花。on 表示"在……表面上"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析（on 在表面上）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选择在扇子上画一只小鸟。on 表示"在……表面上"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都为自己的扇子感到骄傲。修饰名词 fan 用形容词性物主代词 their。故选 C。
