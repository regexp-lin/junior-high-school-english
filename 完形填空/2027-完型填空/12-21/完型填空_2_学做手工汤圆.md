# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工汤圆

## 完形填空原文

In our cooking club, we learned ___1___ make tangyuan by hand last week. The teacher asked us ___2___ our hands first and then showed us how to mix warm water with rice flour. The dough felt soft, ___3___ it was hard to keep a perfect round shape at the beginning.

I practiced again and again ___4___ I could roll small balls steadily. My partner Lily was better ___5___ shaping, so she taught me ___6___ press the dough gently with my fingers. When we put black sesame inside, the teacher reminded us ___7___ close the balls carefully, or the filling would come out.

After cooking, we tasted our work. Mine were not the best, ___8___ I was still proud of them. The teacher said practice makes perfect, ___9___ we should keep trying at home. Before we left, she gave each of us a recipe card ___10___ we could share the steps with our parents.

## 选项

1. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to  
2. A. wash &emsp;&emsp; B. washing &emsp;&emsp; C. to wash &emsp;&emsp; D. washed  
3. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. or &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
4. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. until &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
6. A. how to &emsp;&emsp; B. what to &emsp;&emsp; C. when to &emsp;&emsp; D. why to  
7. A. close &emsp;&emsp; B. closing &emsp;&emsp; C. to close &emsp;&emsp; D. closed  
8. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. or  
9. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
10. A. so that &emsp;&emsp; B. unless &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. until  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”结构  
**【解析】** *learned how to make tangyuan* 表示“学习如何做汤圆”。*how to do* 表方式。*what to* 后需缺宾语的不定式；*when/where to* 表时间/地点，与“制作步骤”不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*ask sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *asked us to wash our hands* 为固定结构“要求某人做某事”。*ask sb. doing* 不是中考常见正确结构；*wash/washed* 不能直接作宾补。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** 面团很软与“一开始很难搓圆”形成转折，用 *but*。*and* 表并列；*or* 表选择；*so* 表结果。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*until*）  
**【解析】** *practiced... until I could roll...* 表示一直练到能稳定搓球。*if/because/unless* 逻辑不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定搭配 *be good at*  
**【解析】** *better at shaping* 是 *good at* 的比较级“更擅长塑形”。*in/on/with* 不与 *good* 构成此搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语  
**【解析】** *taught me how to press* “教我如何轻按”。*teach sb. how to do* 常见。*what/when/why to* 与动作方式不匹配。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*remind sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** *reminded us to close the balls* “提醒我们把口捏紧”。*remind sb. to do* 为固定搭配。*close/closing/closed* 不能直接作补足语。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词表转折  
**【解析】** “不是最好”与“仍然自豪”为转折，用 *but*。*and/so/or* 语义不符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 并列连词 *and* 表顺承  
**【解析】** “熟能生巧”与“应该在家继续尝试”为顺承补充，用 *and* 连接两个分句。*but* 转折；*although* 让步；*unless* 条件。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 目的状语从句（*so that*）  
**【解析】** 给食谱卡片的目的是“以便和父母分享步骤”，*so that we could share*。*unless/although/until* 语义不符。故选 A。
