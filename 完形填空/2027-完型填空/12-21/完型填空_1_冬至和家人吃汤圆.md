# 中考英语完形填空：冬至和家人吃汤圆

## 完形填空原文

Dongzhi, also called the Winter Solstice, is ___1___ important festival in my family. Last Friday, Mom woke me up early ___2___ she wanted us ___3___ prepare sweet rice balls together. The kitchen felt warm ___4___ the stove was on, and the smell of ginger spread through the whole house.

Dad rolled the dough ___5___ it became smooth, and I helped fill some balls with black sesame. My little sister was too short ___6___ reach the table, so Dad carried a chair ___7___ her. While we were talking, Grandma told us funny stories about ___8___ she was young.

When the tangyuan floated in the pot, Mom turned off the heat. We sat around the table and tasted them slowly ___9___ they were very hot. Dad said eating tangyuan means we wish ___10___ a warmer year ahead. That night I felt warm inside and smiled before sleep.

## 选项

1. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
3. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
4. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
5. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. while  
6. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
7. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on  
8. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where  
9. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. because &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless  
10. A. we &emsp;&emsp; B. us &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. ours  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 *a/an* 的用法  
**【解析】** *important* 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，泛指“一个重要的节日”用 *an important festival*。*a* 用于辅音音素开头的词前；此处并非特指某一个冬至。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句（*because*）  
**【解析】** “妈妈很早叫醒我”与“她想让我们一起准备汤圆”是因果关系，用 *because*。*but* 表转折；*although* 表让步；*unless* 表“除非”，语义不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（*want sb. to do sth.*）  
**【解析】** 固定结构 *want us to prepare* “想要我们准备”。*want* 后接宾语补足语用不定式 *to do*，不用 *for/of/with* 这种搭配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 原因状语从句（*because*）  
**【解析】** 厨房暖和是因为炉子开着，表原因用 *because*。*when* 强调“当……时”不如原因贴切；*until*“直到”；*unless*“除非”。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 时间状语从句（*until*）  
**【解析】** *rolled the dough until it became smooth* 表示一直揉到面团变光滑。*if/unless* 表条件；*while* 多接延续性动词，此处强调“直到某个结果”用 *until* 更准确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 固定结构 *too...to...*  
**【解析】** *too short to reach the table* 表示“太矮而够不着桌子”。*too...to...* 后接不定式表结果。*for/of/with* 不能接在该结构中。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 介词 *for* 表对象  
**【解析】** *carried a chair for her* “为她搬来一把椅子”。*for* 引出对象。*to* 表方向时不如 *for* 自然；*at/on* 与 *her* 搭配不当。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词 *when*  
**【解析】** *stories about when she was young* 中 *when* 引导宾语从句，表示“她年轻的时候”。*what* 在从句中需作主语/宾语等成分；*how* 表方式；*where* 表地点，均不符。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 让步状语从句（*although*）  
**【解析】** “慢慢品尝”与“汤圆很烫”为让步关系：虽然很烫，仍然慢慢尝。*although* 表让步。*because* 会误为“因为烫所以慢慢尝”，逻辑不如让步准确；*if/unless* 表条件。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** *wish us a warmer year* 中 *wish* 后接双宾语，人称代词作宾语用宾格 *us*。*we* 为主格；*our/ours* 为物主代词，不能作宾语。故选 B。
