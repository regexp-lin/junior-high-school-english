# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校篮球联赛

## 完形填空原文

Our class entered the school basketball league last week. We had been practicing hard ___1___ two months, so everyone was ready.

In the first game, we played ___2___ Class Five. They were tall and strong, but our teamwork was ___3___ than theirs. I passed the ball to Li Ming, and he scored a three-pointer.

At half-time, the coach told us ___4___ stay focused. "Don't be afraid ___5___ making mistakes," he said. "Just keep running."

In the second half, I fell down while ___6___ for the ball. My knee hurt, but I stood up quickly ___7___ continued playing. The crowd cheered loudly.

With thirty seconds left, the score was tied. Wang Wei got the ball ___8___ threw it toward the basket. It went in! We won ___9___ two points.

After the game, we hugged each other ___10___ jumped up and down. It was the happiest moment of the semester.

## 选项

1. A. since &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. in &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. to &emsp;&emsp; C. against &emsp;&emsp; D. for
3. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. on
5. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with
6. A. fight &emsp;&emsp; B. fought &emsp;&emsp; C. fighting &emsp;&emsp; D. to fight
7. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
8. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. in
10. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/时间介词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们已经刻苦训练了两个月。for + 时间段，表示持续了多长时间，常与完成时连用。since 后接时间点；in/at 不表示持续时间段。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们和五班比赛。play against... 表示"与……对阵比赛"。with 表"和……一起"；to/for 不符合比赛搭配。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的团队合作比他们的更好。than 提示用比较级 better。good 为原级；well 为副词；best 为最高级。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：教练让我们保持专注。tell sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示"告诉某人做某事"。for/of/on 不符合 tell 的结构。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要害怕犯错。be afraid of doing sth. 为固定搭配，表示"害怕做某事"。to/for/with 不符合 afraid 的搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/while + doing
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在争抢球时摔倒了。while 后省略主语和 be 动词，用现在分词 fighting。fight 原形不行；fought 为过去式；to fight 为不定式。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我迅速站起来并继续比赛。stood up 与 continued 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合顺承关系。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：王伟拿到球并将球投向篮筐。got 与 threw 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们以两分的优势获胜。win by... 表示"以……（差距）获胜"。for/with/in 不如 by 贴切表示分差。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们互相拥抱并上下跳跃。hugged 与 jumped 为并列顺承动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。
