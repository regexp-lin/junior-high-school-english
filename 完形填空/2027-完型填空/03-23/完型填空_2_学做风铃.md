# 中考英语完形填空：学做风铃

## 完形填空原文

In art class, the teacher taught us ___1___ make wind chimes with shells and strings. I was very interested ___2___ this craft project.

First, we chose shells of different ___3___: white, pink and light brown. I picked the ones that looked ___4___ small stars.

Then we drilled tiny holes in the shells ___5___ tied strings through them. It was not easy because the shells were ___6___ thin that they could break.

After tying five strings of shells, we hung them ___7___ a piece of driftwood. When the wind blew through the window, the shells touched each other ___8___ made soft sounds.

"It sounds like the sea," my deskmate said. I agreed ___9___ her. The gentle noise reminded me of summer vacation at the beach.

I took my wind chime home and hung it outside my bedroom window. Every morning, it wakes me up ___10___ its sweet music.

## 选项

1. A. what to &emsp;&emsp; B. when to &emsp;&emsp; C. how to &emsp;&emsp; D. where to
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for
3. A. sizes &emsp;&emsp; B. colors &emsp;&emsp; C. shapes &emsp;&emsp; D. prices
4. A. as &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. of
5. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
6. A. very &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. such
7. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
8. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so
9. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. for
10. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. by

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** "疑问词 + 不定式"结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师教我们如何用贝壳和绳子做风铃。teach sb. how to do sth. 表示"教某人怎么做"。what/when/where 不符合"方法"语境。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对这个手工项目很感兴趣。be interested in... 为固定搭配，表示"对……感兴趣"。on/at/for 不符合 interested 的搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们选了不同颜色的贝壳。后文提到"白色、粉色和浅棕色"，这些是颜色描述。sizes 为尺寸；shapes 为形状；prices 为价格。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我挑了看起来像小星星的贝壳。look like... 表示"看起来像"。as 多接从句；for/of 不搭配 look。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在贝壳上钻小孔并穿上绳子。drilled 与 tied 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** so...that 结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：贝壳太薄了以至于可能会碎。so + 形容词 + that... 表示"如此……以至于……"。very 后不接 that 从句；too 后接 to do；such 修饰名词。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把它们挂在一块浮木上。hang...on... 表示"挂在……上面"。in/at/with 不如 on 贴切。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 并列连词
**【解析】** 本句意为：贝壳互相碰触并发出柔和的声音。touched 与 made 为顺承并列动作，用 and。or/but/so 不符合。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析/固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我同意她的说法。agree with sb. 为固定搭配，表示"同意某人"。to/on/for 不符合 agree 后接人的搭配。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天早上它用甜美的音乐叫醒我。with 表示"用/以（方式或工具）"。in/for/by 不如 with 自然地表达"伴随着音乐"。故选 B。
