# 中考英语完形填空：学做豆浆

## 完形填空原文

My grandmother makes fresh soy milk every winter morning. Last week, she finally agreed ___1___ me her recipe.

First, we soaked soybeans overnight. The beans became ___2___ and easier to peel. Grandma said patience at this step would make the drink smoother.

The next morning, we rinsed the beans and put them into a blender ___3___ water. The machine made a loud noise, ___4___ the mixture turned milky white.

We poured the blended liquid into a cloth bag and squeezed it ___5___. The juice dripped into a pot, while the dry pulp stayed inside the bag. Grandma explained that the pulp could be used ___6___ plant food.

Then we boiled the juice slowly and kept stirring ___7___ it boiled. A rich smell filled the kitchen. I set the table and waited ___8___.

The soy milk tasted warm and light, not too sweet. Grandma added a little sugar ___9___ I liked it sweeter. Sitting together, we talked about her childhood when soy milk was a rare treat.

I wrote the steps in my notebook. I hope I can make breakfast ___10___ my family on cold mornings, just like she does.

2. A. soft &emsp;&emsp; B. softer &emsp;&emsp; C. softest &emsp;&emsp; D. softly
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
4. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

7. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. after &emsp;&emsp; D. although
8. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. nervously &emsp;&emsp; C. patient &emsp;&emsp; D. patiently
9. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. although &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
10. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. about

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（agree to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周她终于同意把配方教给我。agree to do sth. 同意做某事。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：豆子变得更软，也更容易去皮。became 后接形容词，与 easier 并列用比较级 softer。soft 原级；softest 最高级；softly 副词。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把豆子放进搅拌机并加水。with water 带着水、加水。in 在……里；on 在……上；at 在。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：机器声音很大，但混合物变成乳白色。转折用 but。and 并列；or 或者；so 所以。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把混合液倒进布袋并轻轻挤压。gently 轻轻地，修饰动词 squeezed。gentle 形容词；slowly 慢慢地；careful 形容词。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：豆渣可以用作植物养料。be used as 被用作。like 像；for 为了；by 通过。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们慢慢加热，并不断搅拌直到煮沸。until 直到。if 如果；after 在……之后；although 尽管。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我摆好桌子并耐心地等待。patiently 耐心地，修饰动词 waited。nervous/nervously 紧张；patient 形容词不能直接修饰 waited。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶加了一点糖，因为我喜欢更甜一点。because 因为，引导原因状语从句。if 如果；although 尽管；unless 除非。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望在寒冷的早晨也能为家人做早餐。make breakfast for my family 为家人做早餐。of 的；with 和；about 关于。故选 C。
