# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工纸花

## 完形填空原文

Our class wanted to decorate the classroom for the new term. The teacher ___1___ us to make paper flowers together. I had never made one before, so I was ___2___ curious and nervous.

We were given colored tissue paper and green wire. The teacher showed us the steps ___3___ the screen. First, we stacked several sheets of paper and folded them like a fan. This was ___4___ step of all.

Next, we tied the wire tightly around the ___5___. Then came the tricky part — pulling each layer of paper apart ___6___. Some students pulled too hard and tore the paper. I took my time and was ___7___ not to damage mine.

After about twenty minutes, a beautiful flower appeared in my ___8___. It looked just like a real carnation! I made three more in pink, yellow, and purple. We put all the flowers into a vase ___9___ stood on the teacher's desk.

When the head teacher walked in, she stopped and smiled. "What a ___10___ classroom!" she said. We all felt proud of our teamwork.

## 选项

1. A. told &emsp;&emsp; B. said &emsp;&emsp; C. spoke &emsp;&emsp; D. talked
2. A. all &emsp;&emsp; B. both &emsp;&emsp; C. neither &emsp;&emsp; D. either
3. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. by
4. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. easiest &emsp;&emsp; D. the easiest
5. A. top &emsp;&emsp; B. bottom &emsp;&emsp; C. middle &emsp;&emsp; D. side
6. A. gentle &emsp;&emsp; B. gently &emsp;&emsp; C. rough &emsp;&emsp; D. roughly
7. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. careless &emsp;&emsp; D. carefully
8. A. hand &emsp;&emsp; B. hands &emsp;&emsp; C. head &emsp;&emsp; D. heads
9. A. who &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. what &emsp;&emsp; D. where
10. A. love &emsp;&emsp; B. lovely &emsp;&emsp; C. loving &emsp;&emsp; D. loved

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们一起做纸花。tell sb. to do sth. 表示"告诉/让某人做某事"。said/spoke/talked 不能直接接 sb. to do。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我既好奇又紧张。both ... and ... 表示"既……又……"，连接两个并列的形容词。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师在屏幕上展示步骤。on the screen 表示"在屏幕上"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是所有步骤中最简单的一步。of all 提示用最高级，easy 的最高级为 the easiest。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们把铁丝紧紧地绑在中间。做纸花时需在折叠纸的中间绑紧，middle 合理。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后是棘手的部分——轻轻地把每层纸拉开。修饰动词 pulling，需用副词 gently。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我慢慢来，小心不弄坏。be careful not to do sth. 表示"小心不要做某事"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词单复数
**【解析】** 本句意为：一朵漂亮的花出现在我手中。in one's hands 表示"在某人手中"，双手持花，用复数 hands。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句（关系代词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把所有花放进一个立在老师桌上的花瓶里。先行词为 vase（物），在从句中作主语，用 which。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 词性转换
**【解析】** 本句意为：多么可爱的教室啊！感叹句 What a + adj. + n.，需用形容词 lovely 修饰 classroom。故选 B。
