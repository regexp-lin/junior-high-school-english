# 中考英语完形填空：图书馆办借书证

## 完形填空原文

Our school library opened a new section this term. To borrow books from it, every student ___1___ to get a library card first. I went to the library during lunch break to apply ___2___ one.

The librarian, an elderly woman with grey hair, greeted me ___3___. She asked me to fill in a form with my name, class, and phone number. "It usually takes two days ___4___ your card ready," she said.

While waiting, I looked around the new section. There were ___5___ books about science, history, and art. One book about space caught my eye, and I couldn't help ___6___ through it. The pictures of planets were so amazing ___7___ I forgot the time.

The librarian came over and said, "You seem to be really ___8___ in this book. Would you like me to reserve it for you?" I nodded ___9___ and thanked her.

Two days later, I got my new card. The first book I borrowed was ___10___ book about space. Reading has opened a brand new world for me.

## 选项

1. A. need &emsp;&emsp; B. needed &emsp;&emsp; C. needing &emsp;&emsp; D. needs
2. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. on
3. A. warm &emsp;&emsp; B. warmer &emsp;&emsp; C. warmly &emsp;&emsp; D. warmest
4. A. get &emsp;&emsp; B. getting &emsp;&emsp; C. got &emsp;&emsp; D. to get
5. A. hundred &emsp;&emsp; B. hundreds &emsp;&emsp; C. hundred of &emsp;&emsp; D. hundreds of
6. A. look &emsp;&emsp; B. looking &emsp;&emsp; C. looked &emsp;&emsp; D. to look
7. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. when
8. A. interest &emsp;&emsp; B. interesting &emsp;&emsp; C. interested &emsp;&emsp; D. interests
9. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. happier &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. happiness
10. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个学生都需要先办借书证。叙述过去的事件（这学期），用一般过去时 needed。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我去图书馆申请一张借书证。apply for 表示"申请"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：图书管理员热情地跟我打了招呼。修饰动词 greeted，需用副词 warmly。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 不定式（it takes ... to do）
**【解析】** 本句意为：准备好你的卡通常需要两天。It takes + 时间 + to do sth. 为固定句型。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 数量表达
**【解析】** 本句意为：有数百本关于科学、历史和艺术的书。hundreds of 表示"数百的"，为固定搭配。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配（动名词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我忍不住翻阅起来。can't help doing sth. 表示"忍不住做某事"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 结果状语从句
**【解析】** 本句意为：行星的图片如此令人惊叹以至于我忘了时间。so ... that ... 引导结果状语从句。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你似乎对这本书真的很感兴趣。be interested in 表示"对……感兴趣"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词与副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我高兴地点了点头。修饰动词 nodded，需用副词 happily。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 冠词（特指）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我借的第一本书就是那本关于太空的书。此处特指前文提到的 that book about space，用定冠词 the。故选 C。
