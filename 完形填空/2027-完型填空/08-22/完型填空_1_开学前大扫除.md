# 中考英语完形填空：开学前大扫除

## 完形填空原文

Two days before school started, my family decided ___1___ a thorough cleaning at home. My mother made a list ___2___ divided the tasks fairly among us. I was responsible ___3___ my desk and bookshelf, ___4___ my brother cleaned the windows.

While I was sorting old papers, I found a notebook ___5___ I had used in Grade 7. Many pages were filled ___6___ colorful stickers and short poems. I realized time had passed ___7___ quickly than I expected. “If we finish ___8___ 4 p.m., we can go out for a walk,” Dad said cheerfully.

By the end of the afternoon, the rooms looked ___9___ than they had in weeks. Tired ___10___ happy, we sat on the sofa and planned what to buy for the new term together.

## 选项

1. A. having &emsp;&emsp; B. have &emsp;&emsp; C. to have &emsp;&emsp; D. had
2. A. but &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. and &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. about
4. A. while &emsp;&emsp; B. if &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
5. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. who &emsp;&emsp; D. whose
6. A. of &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. from
7. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
8. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
9. A. clean &emsp;&emsp; B. cleaner &emsp;&emsp; C. cleanest &emsp;&emsp; D. the cleanest
10. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. and &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（不定式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：开学前两天，我们家决定在家里彻底大扫除。decide to do sth. 表示“决定做某事”。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈列了清单，并把任务公平地分给我们。made a list 与 divided 为顺承并列，用 and。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我负责整理书桌和书架。be responsible for 表示“对……负责、负责做……”。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：……而弟弟擦窗户。while 表示对比，“而、与此同时”。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我找到一本七年级用过的笔记本。先行词 notebook 指物，从句缺宾语，用 which。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多页上贴满了彩色贴纸和短诗。be filled with 表示“装满、充满”。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到时间过得比我想象的更快。than 提示用比较级，quickly 的比较级为 more quickly，此处用 more 与 quickly 搭配（选项为 more）。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果我们下午四点前做完，就可以出去散步。by + 时间点 表示“不迟于、在……之前”。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：到了下午晚些时候，房间比几周以来都更干净。than 提示用比较级 cleaner。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析（形容词短语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：累但开心，我们坐在沙发上一起计划新学期要买什么。tired but happy 表示转折。故选 C。
