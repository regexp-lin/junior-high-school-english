# 中考英语完形填空：第一次坐地铁

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, I took the subway for the first time with my older cousin. We were going to a bookstore downtown. I was very ___1___ because I had only seen subways in movies before.

When we arrived at the station, I was amazed by how big it was. There were escalators going up and down, and ___2___ of people were walking in different directions. My cousin showed me how to buy a ticket from the machine. I put in some coins and pressed the right button. A small card came out, and I felt like I had done something really ___3___.

We walked through the ticket gate and waited on the platform. A few minutes later, the train arrived with a loud ___4___. The doors opened and people rushed out. My cousin told me to wait until everyone got off before we stepped ___5___. Inside, the train was clean and bright. We found two empty seats near the door.

As the train started moving, I looked out of the window. Everything outside was ___6___ because we were underground. But soon the train came above ground, and I could see tall buildings and busy streets ___7___ by quickly. It was like watching a fast-forwarded movie.

After four stops, we arrived at our ___8___. My cousin reminded me to keep the ticket because we needed it to exit. When we walked out of the station, I realized the bookstore was just across the street. Taking the subway had been much ___9___ than taking the bus.

On the way home, I was no longer nervous. I even helped an old lady find the right ___10___ on the subway map. She thanked me and said I was very helpful. That made my day even better.

5. A. off &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. over

7. A. flying &emsp;&emsp; B. falling &emsp;&emsp; C. flashing &emsp;&emsp; D. floating
8. A. school &emsp;&emsp; B. destination &emsp;&emsp; C. home &emsp;&emsp; D. office
9. A. slower &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. worse &emsp;&emsp; D. faster
10. A. line &emsp;&emsp; B. seat &emsp;&emsp; C. door &emsp;&emsp; D. ticket

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常兴奋，因为以前只在电影里见过地铁。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"伤心的"。第一次体验新事物感到 excited。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人群朝不同方向走。A 项意为"人群"；B 项意为"成对"；C 项意为"成排"；D 项意为"小组"。crowds of people 表示大量人群。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得自己做了一件很酷的事。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"危险的"；C 项意为"酷的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。第一次自己买票感觉 cool。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：列车伴着巨大的声响到站了。A 项意为"歌曲"；B 项意为"噪音/声响"；C 项意为"嗓音"；D 项意为"喊叫"。地铁进站的 noise。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：等所有人下车后我们再上车。A 项 stepped off 意为"下车"；B 项 stepped out 意为"走出"；C 项 stepped on 意为"上车"；D 项 stepped over 意为"跨过"。上地铁用 step on。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：外面什么都是黑的，因为我们在地下。A 项意为"黑暗的"；B 项意为"明亮的"；C 项意为"多彩的"；D 项意为"清楚的"。地下隧道窗外是 dark。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：高楼和繁忙街道快速闪过。A 项意为"飞"；B 项意为"掉落"；C 项意为"闪过"；D 项意为"漂浮"。flashing by 表示快速闪过。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：四站后我们到达了目的地。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"目的地"；C 项意为"家"；D 项意为"办公室"。destination 表示目的地。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：坐地铁比坐公交快得多。A 项意为"更慢"；B 项意为"更难"；C 项意为"更差"；D 项意为"更快"。地铁比公交 faster。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我帮一位老太太在地铁线路图上找到正确的线路。A 项意为"线路"；B 项意为"座位"；C 项意为"门"；D 项意为"票"。subway map 上找的是 line（线路）。故选 A。
