# 中考英语完形填空：收养流浪小猫

## 完形填空原文

One rainy evening, I heard a strange sound outside our front door. When I opened it, I found a tiny kitten sitting on the doorstep. It was wet and ___1___, looking up at me with big round eyes. My heart melted immediately.

I brought the kitten inside and wrapped it in a warm towel. Mom came over and said, "It must be a stray. Someone probably ___2___ it." The kitten was so small that it could fit in my two hands. I gave it some warm milk, and it drank it ___3___.

The next morning, I asked my parents if we could keep the kitten. Dad was not sure at first because he thought pets were a big ___4___. But when the kitten rubbed its head against Dad's hand, he could not say no. "Fine," he said with a smile. "But you have to take care of it ___5___."

I named the kitten Mimi and took her to the vet for a checkup. The doctor said she was about two months old and was ___6___ healthy. He gave her a vaccine and told me what kind of food to buy. I listened carefully and took ___7___ in a small notebook.

Over the following weeks, Mimi grew quickly. She loved to chase a small ball around the living room and hide behind the ___8___. Every night, she would curl up beside me and purr softly. The sound always made me feel peaceful and relaxed.

Having a pet taught me a lot about responsibility. I had to ___9___ her regularly, clean her space, and play with her every day. Sometimes it was tiring, but seeing her happy face made everything ___10___. Mimi is not just a pet—she is a member of our family now.

## 选项

1. A. full &emsp;&emsp; B. shaking &emsp;&emsp; C. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; D. laughing
2. A. loved &emsp;&emsp; B. followed &emsp;&emsp; C. abandoned &emsp;&emsp; D. protected
3. A. sadly &emsp;&emsp; B. eagerly &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. angrily
4. A. responsibility &emsp;&emsp; B. surprise &emsp;&emsp; C. chance &emsp;&emsp; D. game
5. A. herself &emsp;&emsp; B. himself &emsp;&emsp; C. yourself &emsp;&emsp; D. myself
6. A. hardly &emsp;&emsp; B. basically &emsp;&emsp; C. seriously &emsp;&emsp; D. badly
7. A. photos &emsp;&emsp; B. notes &emsp;&emsp; C. walks &emsp;&emsp; D. turns
8. A. curtains &emsp;&emsp; B. clouds &emsp;&emsp; C. trees &emsp;&emsp; D. fences
9. A. wash &emsp;&emsp; B. feed &emsp;&emsp; C. call &emsp;&emsp; D. catch
10. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. worthwhile &emsp;&emsp; C. impossible &emsp;&emsp; D. unnecessary

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小猫又湿又发抖。A 项意为"饱的"；B 项意为"发抖的"；C 项意为"睡着的"；D 项意为"大笑的"。雨天被淋湿的小猫会 shaking（发抖）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一定是只流浪猫，可能被人遗弃了。A 项意为"爱"；B 项意为"跟随"；C 项意为"遗弃"；D 项意为"保护"。stray 暗示被 abandoned。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它急切地喝了牛奶。A 项意为"伤心地"；B 项意为"急切地"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"生气地"。饥饿的小猫 eagerly 喝奶。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸觉得养宠物是一项很大的责任。A 项意为"责任"；B 项意为"惊喜"；C 项意为"机会"；D 项意为"游戏"。pets are a big responsibility 最合理。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 反身代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但你必须自己照顾它。A 项意为"她自己"；B 项意为"他自己"；C 项意为"你自己"；D 项意为"我自己"。爸爸对"我"说，用 yourself。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：医生说她大约两个月大，基本上是健康的。A 项意为"几乎不"；B 项意为"基本上"；C 项意为"严重地"；D 项意为"严重地/差"。basically healthy 表示基本健康。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我认真听并在小本子上做了笔记。A 项意为"照片"；B 项意为"笔记"；C 项意为"散步"；D 项意为"轮次"。took notes 意为"做笔记"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她喜欢躲在窗帘后面。A 项意为"窗帘"；B 项意为"云"；C 项意为"树"；D 项意为"栅栏"。客厅里猫喜欢躲在 curtains 后面。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我必须定期喂她。A 项意为"洗"；B 项意为"喂"；C 项意为"叫"；D 项意为"抓"。feed her regularly 意为"定期喂食"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到她快乐的脸庞让一切都值得。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"值得的"；C 项意为"不可能的"；D 项意为"不必要的"。worthwhile 表示值得付出。故选 B。
