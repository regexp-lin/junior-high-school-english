# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工拼布

## 完形填空原文

The community center offered a patchwork workshop ___1___ Saturday afternoon. My aunt signed ___2___ up because she knew I liked sewing. The room smelled of fabric and tea, and colorful sample quilts hung on the walls.

We cut old shirts into squares and pinned ___3___ on a large cloth. The teacher told us ___4___ leave a small space between each piece so the cloth could breathe. We chose soft blues and warm reds to make a simple flower pattern.

Sewing straight lines was ___5___ than it looked. My needle broke once, ___6___ the teacher lent me ___7___ spare needle without blame. She also showed me how to hide knots on the back.

When the small quilt was finished, it felt ___8___ of all my DIY projects. I decided ___9___ it on my bed every winter. This hobby also taught me ___10___ old clothes still have value. Even small scraps can become a patch of sky on cloth if you arrange them with care.

## 选项

1. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. on &emsp;&emsp; D. by  
2. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. us  
3. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. its &emsp;&emsp; D. they  
4. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. of &emsp;&emsp; D. with  
5. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. hardest &emsp;&emsp; D. the hardest  
6. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. or &emsp;&emsp; C. but &emsp;&emsp; D. so  
7. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself  
8. A. warm &emsp;&emsp; B. warmer &emsp;&emsp; C. warmest &emsp;&emsp; D. the warmest  
9. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. using &emsp;&emsp; C. to use &emsp;&emsp; D. used  
10. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. how  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 介词 on 与具体某天下午搭配  
**【解析】** on Saturday afternoon 在具体某天的下午用 on。in/at/by 与时间搭配不符此用法。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格与短语 sign sb. up  
**【解析】** sign sb. up 表示“替某人报名”，动词后用人称代词宾格；全文主语为“我”，姑姑替“我”报名，用 me。I 为主格；my 为物主代词不能单独作宾语；us 表“我们”，文中未提与他人一起报名。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格与数的一致  
**【解析】** 指代复数 squares，作 pinned 的宾语，用 them。it/its 表单数；they 为主格。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（tell sb. to do）  
**【解析】** tell us to leave... 为 tell sb. to do sth.。for/of/with 不能构成 tell 的正确结构。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词比较级  
**【解析】** than it looked 提示比较级，用 harder。hard 原级；hardest/the highest 为最高级，句中无范围比较。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 转折连词 but  
**【解析】** “针断了一次”与“老师借我针”形成转折：出现小状况，但老师仍帮忙，用 but。and 顺承；or 选择；so 结果，均不如 but 贴切。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 形容词性物主代词  
**【解析】** spare needle 为名词短语，前用形容词性物主代词 her 指代 the teacher's。she 为主格；hers 为名词性物主代词，后不接名词；herself 为反身代词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**  
**【考点】** 形容词最高级  
**【解析】** of all my DIY projects 表范围，用 the warmest。warm 原级；warmer 缺 than；warmest 缺定冠词 the。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（decide to do）  
**【解析】** decide to do sth. 用不定式 to use。use/using/used 不符合 decide 的搭配。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 that  
**【解析】** taught me that old clothes still have value 中 that 引导宾语从句陈述事实。what 需在从句中作成分；if 表是否；how 表方式。故选 B。
