# 中考英语完形填空：第一次当小老师

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our class started a “Study Buddy” program, and I volunteered ___1___ help Tom with his math homework. He was shy ___2___ first, so I spoke slowly and wrote examples on paper. Sometimes we sat in the library after school, and the quiet air made it easier to focus.

One evening, he brought a difficult problem ___3___ he could not solve alone. Instead of giving the answer, I asked him ___4___ questions to guide his thinking. ___5___ minutes later, his eyes lit up. He wrote the steps neatly and even checked them twice.

From that day on, Tom began ___6___ questions in class more often. Our math teacher praised ___7___ for being patient. I learned teaching is not ___8___ telling; it is listening and encouraging. When Tom waved at me in the hallway, I felt warm inside.

Now I believe everyone can be ___9___ helpful person if we are willing to share ___10___ we know. Helping others also pushes me to review my own notes more carefully before each meeting.

## 选项

1. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. to help &emsp;&emsp; D. helped  
2. A. in &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. for  
3. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. which &emsp;&emsp; C. that &emsp;&emsp; D. who  
4. A. few &emsp;&emsp; B. a few &emsp;&emsp; C. little &emsp;&emsp; D. a little  
5. A. Ten &emsp;&emsp; B. Tenth &emsp;&emsp; C. Tens &emsp;&emsp; D. The ten  
6. A. ask &emsp;&emsp; B. asking &emsp;&emsp; C. to ask &emsp;&emsp; D. asked  
7. A. I &emsp;&emsp; B. me &emsp;&emsp; C. my &emsp;&emsp; D. mine  
8. A. only &emsp;&emsp; B. lonely &emsp;&emsp; C. alone &emsp;&emsp; D. almost  
9. A. a &emsp;&emsp; B. an &emsp;&emsp; C. the &emsp;&emsp; D. /  
10. A. that &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. whether  

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（volunteer to do）  
**【解析】** volunteer to do sth. 为固定搭配，表示“自愿做某事”。help/helping/helped 不符合 volunteer 的用法。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 固定短语 at first  
**【解析】** at first 表示“起初”。in/on/for first 不构成此义。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 定语从句关系代词 that  
**【解析】** 先行词 problem 为物，从句缺宾语，可用 that/which；选项中 that 正确。what 不引导定语从句；who 指人；which 亦可，但本题选项设置选 that。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** a few / few 修饰可数名词  
**【解析】** questions 为可数名词复数，且句意肯定“问了几个问题”，用 a few。few 表否定；little/a little 修饰不可数名词。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 基数词表示数量  
**【解析】** Ten minutes later 表示“十分钟后”，用基数词 Ten。Tenth 为序数词；Tens/the ten 形式错误。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**  
**【考点】** 非谓语动词（begin to do / begin doing）  
**【解析】** begin to ask 表示“开始更常提问”，begin 后可接不定式，强调动作的开始，常用 to do。begin asking 也可，但选项中 to ask 更符合书面表达习惯。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 人称代词宾格  
**【解析】** praised 后接宾语，用人称代词宾格 me。I 为主格；my/mine 为物主代词。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 副词 only 表“仅仅”  
**【解析】** not only telling 表示“不仅仅是告诉”，only 作副词修饰 telling。lonely 表情感孤独；alone 表独自；almost 表几乎，语义不符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**  
**【考点】** 不定冠词 a 与辅音音素开头单词  
**【解析】** helpful 以辅音音素开头，泛指“一个乐于助人的人”，用 a。an 用于元音音素；the 表特指；零冠词不适用于可数名词单数泛指。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**  
**【考点】** 宾语从句连接词 what  
**【解析】** share what we know 中 what 作 know 的宾语，表示“我们所知道的（东西）”。that 在从句中不充当成分，know 缺宾语；how/whether 语义不符。故选 B。
