# 中考英语完形填空：参加新学期分班

## 完形填空原文

The summer holiday was coming ___1___ an end, and our school held a short meeting for all Grade 8 students. The head teacher explained ___2___ we would be divided into new classes according to our interests and learning plans. Many students felt nervous ___3___ they also hoped to meet new friends.

On the first day of the new term, we lined up ___4___ the playground and waited for the lists. When my name was called, I walked to Class 5, ___5___ windows face the sports field. The classroom ___6___ cleaned by volunteers the day before, so everything looked bright and tidy. Our new homeroom teacher welcomed us warmly and said, “This year, we will work together ___7___ we can make progress step by step.”

Some students wanted ___8___ parents to talk with the teacher after class, ___9___ I decided to adapt to the new environment by myself first. I believe ___10___ hard I try, the more confident I will become in the new class.

## 选项

1. A. to &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. at
2. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. that &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. whether
3. A. or &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. because
4. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. by
5. A. which &emsp;&emsp; B. who &emsp;&emsp; C. whose &emsp;&emsp; D. whom
6. A. is &emsp;&emsp; B. was &emsp;&emsp; C. has been &emsp;&emsp; D. had been
7. A. if &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. so that &emsp;&emsp; D. although
8. A. they &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. their &emsp;&emsp; D. theirs
9. A. and &emsp;&emsp; B. but &emsp;&emsp; C. because &emsp;&emsp; D. unless
10. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. the more &emsp;&emsp; D. the most

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：暑假即将结束。come to an end 为固定搭配，表示“结束、到头”。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 宾语从句引导词
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任解释我们将如何分班…… explained 后接宾语从句，从句结构完整、陈述事实，用 that 引导（口语中可省略，但选项中需选 that）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多同学感到紧张，但他们也希望交到新朋友。前后为转折关系，用 but。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在操场上排队。on the playground 表示“在操场上”，为常见搭配。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 定语从句关系词
**【解析】** 本句意为：我走到五班，（五班的）窗户朝向运动场。先行词 Class 5 与 windows 为所属关系，用 whose 作定语。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 被动语态与时态
**【解析】** 本句意为：教室前一天已被志愿者打扫干净。教室是“被打扫”，用被动；the day before 表示“过去的过去”，主句叙述过去场景，打扫发生在更早，用过去完成时的被动 had been cleaned。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：今年我们将一起努力，以便能一步步取得进步。so that 引导目的状语从句，表示“以便、为了”。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词（形容词性物主代词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些学生想让他们的家长课后与老师沟通。修饰名词 parents，用形容词性物主代词 their。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：……但我决定先自己适应新环境。前后对比转折，用 but。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** “the + 比较级…, the + 比较级…”结构
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信我越努力，在新班级里就会越自信。the more…, the more… 表示“越……越……”。故选 C。
