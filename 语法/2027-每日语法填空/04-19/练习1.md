# 04-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界读书日 (World Book Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

April 23 is World Book Day, (1) ______ readers around the globe celebrate the joy of reading. Our school library held a week-long book fair (2) ______ students could swap used books and recommend titles to one another. I brought a novel I had finished twice (3) ______ picked up a science magazine I had wanted for months.

The librarian gave a short speech (4) ______ (stress) that books open doors to different worlds. She also announced a reading challenge: whoever read the (5) ______ (many) pages would receive a prize. By Friday, over 400 books (6) ______ (borrow) from the shelves, and quiet corners were full of students (7) ______ (lose) in stories.

I joined a discussion group (8) ______ we talked about characters that inspired us. That week, I spent at least an hour reading (9) ______ day before bedtime. Books have become (10) ______ (close) friends than TV shows to me.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。非限制性定语从句修饰 `World Book Day`，从句中缺时间状语，“在这一天全球读者庆祝”，用 `when`。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `book fair`，从句中缺地点状语，“在书展上学生可以换书”，用 `where`。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`brought` 与 `picked up` 为并列谓语，表顺承。

4. **答案：stressing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。伴随状语，图书管理员发表演讲同时“强调……”，主语 `librarian` 与 `stress` 为主动关系，用现在分词。

5. **答案：most**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。定冠词 `the` 后接最高级，`many` → `most`，`the most pages` 最多页数。

6. **答案：were borrowed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。书“被借出”，主语 `books` 复数，叙述过去一周情况，用 `were borrowed`。

7. **答案：lost**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`students lost in stories` 过去分词作后置定语，表“沉浸在故事中的学生”，相当于 `who were lost in`。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `discussion group`，从句中缺地点状语，“在小组里我们讨论……”，用 `where`。

9. **答案：every/each**
   - **考点**：限定词。`every day` 或 `each day` 每天，作时间状语。

10. **答案：closer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`close` → `closer`，`friends` 前用形容词比较级作定语。
