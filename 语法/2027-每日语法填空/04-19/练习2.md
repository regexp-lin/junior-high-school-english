# 04-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：独轮车表演 (Unicycle Performance)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

At the city sports festival, a team of teenagers surprised the crowd (1) ______ a unicycle performance on the square. They rode on one wheel (2) ______ lifted hoops while passing balls to one another. The leader, a fifteen-year-old boy, even jumped (3) ______ a low bar without losing balance.

The coach explained that the riders (4) ______ (train) for two years before they could perform in public. "Riding a unicycle is (5) ______ (difficult) than it looks," he said with a smile. Many parents covered (6) ______ (they) mouths when young players rode close to the edge of the stage.

After the show, several students asked (7) ______ they could join the club. The coach advised them to start with safety gear and short practice sessions. I used to think unicycles were just toys, (8) ______ now I respect the skill behind each movement. Watching the performance made me realize (9) ______ courage and teamwork can turn a hobby into art. I hope I will be brave enough (10) ______ (try) something new next year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`surprise sb. with sth.` 用……使某人惊喜，引出表演内容。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。连接并列谓语 `rode` 与 `lifted`，表示同时进行的两个动作；`while passing balls` 为时间状语从句的省略形式。

3. **答案：over**
   - **考点**：介词。`jump over a bar` 从横杆上方越过。

4. **答案：had trained**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`before they could perform` 表示“在公开表演之前已训练两年”，动作发生在过去的过去，用 `had trained`。

5. **答案：more difficult**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，多音节形容词 `difficult` 的比较级为 `more difficult`。

6. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 `mouths`，`they` → 形容词性物主代词 `their`。

7. **答案：whether/if**
   - **考点**：连接词。`asked` 后接宾语从句，表示“是否能加入俱乐部”，用 `whether` 或 `if`。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾以为只是玩具”与“现在尊重技艺”为转折。

9. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。`realize` 后宾语从句，说明“勇气与团队合作如何能把爱好变成艺术”，表方式用 `how`。

10. **答案：to try**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`adj. + enough + to do` 足够……去做某事，不定式作结果状语。
