# 03-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：课外补习利弊 (Pros and Cons of After-school Tutoring)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

After-school tutoring has become a hot topic among parents and students. Some families believe extra classes can help children get (1) ______ (good) grades, while others worry about stress and high costs.

My cousin Lily attends math and English classes every weekend. She says the teachers explain difficult points (2) ______ (slow) than at school, so she feels more confident. Her parents are happy to see her progress, (3) ______ they spend a large part of their income on the fees.

On the other hand, my neighbor Tom stopped going to tutoring last year. He joined the school football team instead and uses his free time (4) ______ (read) novels. His marks dropped a little at first, (5) ______ he learned to manage time by himself. Now he is doing better than before.

Experts suggest that parents should consider each child's needs. Tutoring may be helpful (6) ______ a student falls behind, but it is not the only path to success. Rest and hobbies are also (7) ______ (importance) for healthy growth.

In my opinion, the key is balance. If tutoring makes a student tired and unhappy, it (8) ______ (not be) worth the money. We should ask ourselves whether we are learning (9) ______ real interest or just following others.

Schools and families need to work together (10) ______ students can enjoy learning without feeling trapped by endless classes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`get better grades` 取得更好的成绩。

2. **答案：more slowly**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than at school` 提示比较级，多音节副词用 `more slowly`。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。看到进步感到高兴，但补习费用占家庭收入的很大比例。

4. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`use time to do sth.` 利用空闲时间做某事。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。成绩起初下滑，但后来学会了自我管理，表转折。

6. **答案：if / when**
   - **考点**：连词。`if/when a student falls behind` 若/当学生落后时，补习可能有帮助。

7. **答案：important**
   - **考点**：词性转换。系动词 `are` 后接形容词，`importance` → `important`。

8. **答案：isn't / is not**
   - **考点**：一般现在时否定。主将从现或陈述观点：若补习让人疲惫又不快乐，就不值得。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`with real interest` 带着真正的兴趣学习。

10. **答案：so that**
    - **考点**：目的状语从句。共同努力以便学生能享受学习、不被无尽补习束缚。
