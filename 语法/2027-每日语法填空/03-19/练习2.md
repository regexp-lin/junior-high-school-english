# 03-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：少年足球 (Youth Football)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Football is called "the world's game" for a good reason. In our city, more and more teenagers are joining youth clubs (1) ______ they can train regularly after school.

I started playing football three years ago. At that time, I was shy and (2) ______ (seldom) spoke in class. My PE teacher encouraged me (3) ______ (try) out for the school team. I was not the fastest runner, (4) ______ I worked hard on basic skills every afternoon.

Last term, our team entered a district competition. In the semi-final, we were two goals behind at half-time. The coach told us not (5) ______ (give) up. We passed the ball (6) ______ (patient) and finally scored three goals in the second half.

That victory was (7) ______ (excited) moment of my school life so far. I learned that teamwork matters more than personal glory. On the field, everyone depends on (8) ______ else to do their part.

Playing football has also taught me how to deal with failure. When we lost an important match, I felt upset, (9) ______ my teammates and I promised to train harder. Nothing valuable comes without effort.

I dream of becoming a PE teacher one day (10) ______ I can help more children discover the joy of sports.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `clubs` 表地点，从句缺状语，用 where。

2. **答案：seldom**
   - **考点**：副词。修饰动词 `spoke`，`seldom` 本身为副词“很少”，无需变形。

3. **答案：to try**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`encourage sb. to do sth.` 鼓励某人做某事。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。不是最快的，但努力练基本功。

5. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`tell sb. not to do sth.` 告诉某人不要做某事。

6. **答案：more patiently**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。与上半场对比，更耐心地传球，多音节用 more patiently。

7. **答案：the most exciting**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`of my school life` 范围提示，`excited` 修饰物用 `exciting`，最高级 `the most exciting`。

8. **答案：everyone / everybody**
   - **考点**：不定代词。`everyone else` 其他每个人，互相依赖。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。输了很难过，但队友们相约更刻苦训练。

10. **答案：because / so that**
    - **考点**：状语从句。`because` 表原因（想当体育老师因为想帮助孩子）；若用 `so that` 表目的亦可，此处 `because` 更自然。
