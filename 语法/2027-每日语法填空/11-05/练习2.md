# 11-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：立冬习俗 (Beginning of Winter Customs)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Lidong, or the Beginning of Winter, is one of the 24 solar terms in the traditional Chinese calendar. It __1__ (usual) falls on November 7th or 8th. On this day, people in northern China have a custom __2__ eating dumplings. My family follows this tradition every year. Last Lidong, my grandmother __3__ (teach) me how to make dumplings from scratch. She prepared the filling __4__ pork, cabbage and spring onions. I helped her mix the dough and roll it __5__ thin, round wrappers. Wrapping dumplings was the __6__ (tricky) part — mine kept opening up during boiling! Grandma laughed and said practice makes __7__ (perfectly). In southern China, people prefer __8__ (eat) mutton and chicken soup to warm their bodies. The idea behind Lidong customs is __9__ people should store energy for the cold months ahead. After enjoying a big plate of dumplings, I felt warm and __10__ (satisfy). These traditions connect us with nature's rhythms.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：usually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 falls，用频率副词 usually。

2. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。a custom of doing sth. 做某事的习俗，用介词 of。

3. **答案：taught**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 last Lidong 可知为一般过去时，teach 的过去式为不规则变化 taught。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with pork, cabbage and spring onions 用猪肉、白菜和葱，with 表示"用"。

5. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。roll... into... 把……擀成……，用介词 into。

6. **答案：trickiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，tricky 的最高级为 trickiest（y→iest）。

7. **答案：perfect**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词→形容词）。Practice makes perfect. 熟能生巧。make 后接形容词作宾补，用 perfect。

8. **答案：to eat / eating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。prefer to do sth. / prefer doing sth. 更喜欢做某事，两者均可。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：表语从句连接词。The idea is that... 想法是……，that 引导表语从句。

10. **答案：satisfied**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。feel satisfied 感到满足，-ed 形式表示人的感受。
