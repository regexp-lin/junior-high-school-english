# 11-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园美食节 (School Food Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school organised a food festival last Friday. Each class was asked __1__ (prepare) dishes from different countries. My class chose to make Japanese sushi. We spent the whole morning __2__ (roll) rice and seaweed together. It was more difficult __3__ we thought, because the rice kept falling apart. __4__ (final), with our teacher's help, we made thirty __5__ (piece) of beautiful sushi. Other classes prepared Italian pizza, Indian curry, Thai salad __6__ Mexican tacos. The playground was turned __7__ a huge outdoor restaurant. The air was filled with __8__ (delight) smells from every corner. Students could taste food from each stall by using special tickets. The __9__ (popular) stall was the French dessert table, where students queued for over twenty minutes! I tried foods I __10__ (never eat) before, and the Mexican tacos became my new favourite. The festival showed us how diverse and wonderful world cuisine can be.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be asked to do sth. 被要求做某事，用不定式 to prepare。

2. **答案：rolling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 花时间做某事，用动名词 rolling。

3. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较级标志词。more difficult than 比……更难，用 than。

4. **答案：Finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。置于句首作时间状语，用副词 Finally，注意首字母大写。

5. **答案：pieces**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。thirty 后接可数名词复数，用 pieces。

6. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。列举多个国家的菜肴，最后一个前用 and 连接。

7. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。be turned into 被变成……，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：delightful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词/动词→形容词）。修饰名词 smells，delight 的形容词形式为 delightful。

9. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，popular 为多音节词，最高级用 most popular。

10. **答案：had never eaten**
    - **考点**：动词时态。tried 为一般过去时，之前从未吃过的食物，用过去完成时 had never eaten。
