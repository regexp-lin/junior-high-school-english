# 11-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：冬季防感冒 (Preventing Colds in Winter)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Winter is the season when colds and flu spread most __1__ (easy). Our school nurse, Miss Zhao, recently gave us some __2__ (practice) advice on staying healthy. First, she told us __3__ (wash) our hands frequently, especially before eating and after using the toilet. Germs can survive __4__ surfaces for hours and enter our bodies when we touch our faces. Second, she recommended __5__ (get) enough sleep — at least eight hours a night for teenagers. A __6__ (tire) body is more likely to catch a cold. Third, she said we should eat more foods rich __7__ vitamin C, such as oranges and peppers. These help strengthen __8__ (we) immune system. She also warned against staying in __9__ (crowd) indoor spaces for too long because viruses spread quickly in poorly ventilated rooms. Finally, she reminded us to wear warm clothes and keep __10__ windows in the classroom open for fresh air. Following these tips can help us stay healthy throughout the winter.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 spread，用副词 easily。

2. **答案：practical**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 advice，practice 的形容词形式为 practical。

3. **答案：to wash**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。tell sb. to do sth. 告诉某人做某事，用不定式 to wash。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on surfaces 在表面上，用 on。

5. **答案：getting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。recommend doing sth. 建议做某事，用动名词 getting。

6. **答案：tired**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。a tired body 疲惫的身体，tire 的形容词形式 tired 修饰名词。

7. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。be rich in 富含……，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 immune system，用 our。

9. **答案：crowded**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 indoor spaces，crowd 的形容词形式为 crowded。

10. **答案：the**
    - **考点**：定冠词。特指教室里的窗户，用 the。
