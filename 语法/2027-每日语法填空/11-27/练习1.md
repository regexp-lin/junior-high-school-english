# 11-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：地铁读书族 (Metro Readers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

If you ride the metro in Chinese cities, you will notice that many passengers read during their journey. Some read __1__ (tradition) paper books while others prefer e-books on their phones. I take the metro to school every day, and reading on the train has become one of my favourite __2__ (habit). The ride takes about thirty __3__ (minute), which is just enough time to finish a chapter. I find that reading helps me relax __4__ a busy day. It also makes the commute feel __5__ (short) than it actually is. Last month, I noticed __6__ old man reading the same book as me — "Robinson Crusoe". We started chatting and __7__ (become) friends. He told me he had been reading on the metro __8__ over twenty years! He reads about one hundred books a year. I was deeply __9__ (impress). Inspired by him, I set a goal __10__ (read) at least fifty books this year. Good books can turn boring commutes into wonderful adventures.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 paper books，tradition 的形容词形式为 traditional。

2. **答案：habits**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。one of my favourite habits 最喜欢的习惯之一，用复数 habits。

3. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。thirty 后接可数名词复数，用 minutes。

4. **答案：after**
   - **考点**：介词。after a busy day 忙碌一天之后，用 after。

5. **答案：shorter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than，short 的比较级为 shorter。

6. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。old 以元音音素 /əʊ/ 开头，泛指"一位老人"，用 an。

7. **答案：became**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与 started 并列，用一般过去时，become 的过去式为 became。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for over twenty years 超过二十年，for + 时间段。

9. **答案：impressed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be deeply impressed 深受感动，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

10. **答案：to read**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。a goal to do sth. 做某事的目标，不定式作后置定语。
