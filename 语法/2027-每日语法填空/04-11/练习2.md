# 04-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：航模比赛 (Model Aircraft Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The city model aircraft competition attracts hundreds of middle school students every year. Teams design small planes (1) ______ (weigh) only a few hundred grams, but the rules are strict.

My brother joined a club six months ago. At first, he broke wings and lost screws almost every week. (2) ______ , he watched online tutorials and asked coaches for advice. His plane crashed many times, (3) ______ each failure taught him something new.

Two days before the contest, the model finally flew in a stable circle (4) ______ the sports field. On the big day, wind blew (5) ______ (strong) than we expected. Some teams gave up, (6) ______ my brother's team stayed calm.

When their number was called, the plane rose smoothly and passed through two hoops. The judges nodded and took notes. In the end, our school won second place, (7) ______ was beyond our dream.

Building model aircraft is not cheap, (8) ______ it trains patience and precision. I am thinking about joining the club next term (9) ______ I want to learn basic engineering ideas.

Science hobbies may look difficult, (10) ______ small steps lead to big skills.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：weighing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`planes weighing only ...` 仅重……的飞机。

2. **答案：Instead**
   - **考点**：连接副词。上文写屡次损坏零件，下文转而采取学习教程等措施，`Instead` 表示“相反／转而”。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。坠毁多次，但每次失败都有收获。

4. **答案：above / over**
   - **考点**：介词。在运动场上空盘旋，`above/over the field`。

5. **答案：more strongly**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，修饰动词 `blew`，`strongly` → `more strongly`。

6. **答案：but / while**
   - **考点**：并列连词。一些队放弃，但我哥哥队保持冷静。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。不便宜，但能培养耐心。

9. **答案：because / since / as**
   - **考点**：从属连词。想学习工程基础知识，表原因。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。看似难，但小步积累成大技能。
