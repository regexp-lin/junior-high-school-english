# 04-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：养花日记 (Gardening Diary)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

On my balcony, I keep a few pots of flowers. Writing a gardening diary helps me notice how plants change from week (1) ______ week.

In March, I planted sunflower seeds in soil that I bought (2) ______ the market. At first, nothing happened, and I almost lost hope. (3) ______ , tiny green shoots appeared after a warm rain, which made me cheer loudly.

I record the height of the stems and the color of the leaves. When the flowers bloom, they turn the balcony into (4) ______ bright corner of our home. My mother says caring for plants has made me (5) ______ (patient) than I used to be.

Sometimes insects attack the leaves. I search online for natural ways (6) ______ (solve) the problem instead of using strong chemicals. This habit also teaches me to respect life, even if it is as small (7) ______ an ant.

Gardening is not always successful. Two of my pots died (8) ______ I watered them too much. Failure is part of learning, (9) ______ I started again with new seeds.

If you want to try, begin with one easy plant and watch it grow. You may discover (10) ______ peaceful hobby can be.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`from week to week` 一周又一周。

2. **答案：from / at / in**
   - **考点**：介词。`bought from the market` 从市场买来；或 `at/in the market`。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。起初没动静，然而雨后小芽出现，转折。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a bright corner` 一个明亮的角落。

5. **答案：more patient**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，`patient` 多音节用 `more patient`。

6. **答案：to solve**
   - **考点**：动词不定式作定语。`ways to solve` 解决问题的方法。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定结构。`as small as` 和……一样小。

8. **答案：because / when**
   - **考点**：从属连词。因为浇太多水而死，表原因用 `because`。

9. **答案：so / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。失败是学习的一部分，所以我重新开始；或 `and`。

10. **答案：how**
    - **考点**：连接副词。`discover how peaceful a hobby can be` 宾语从句，感叹意味“多么平静的爱好”。
