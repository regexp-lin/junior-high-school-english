# 07-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：写日记的好处 (The Benefits of Keeping a Diary)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many students in middle school believe ___1___ keeping a diary is a silly habit ___2___ will not improve their test scores. ___3___ (Actual), educational research paints a rather different picture, and many language teachers agree with the findings. Writing about your ordinary day in a few sentences helps you understand ___4___ (you) more clearly and feel less nervous before important exams, which matters when pressure builds near the end of a term. The habit also creates a safe corner where you can experiment with new expressions without fear of public mistakes. If you ___5___ (practice) turning small real-life moments into simple English several times a week, your active vocabulary ___6___ (grow) in a natural way, and grammar points from class discussions often feel ___7___ (easy) to reuse in brand-new sentences of your own. Besides, a personal diary can work ___8___ a light study planner that records both academic goals and daily moods in one place; ___9___ short warm-up paragraph before bed sometimes works better than stressful late-night cramming when you are tired after a long day at school. For these reasons, many caring teachers encourage young learners ___10___ (try) reflective writing for about ten minutes a night so quiet confidence can grow step by step rather than overnight.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句引导词。`believe` 后接宾语从句，从句“keeping a diary is a silly habit…”意思完整，不缺主语或宾语，用 **that** 引导宾语从句（语法填空中常要求显式填 **that**）。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句。先行词是 **habit**，从句 “___ will not improve their test scores” 缺少主语，指物且在从句中作主语，用关系代词 **which**（不可用 **where/when**）。

3. **答案：Actually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。括号 **Actual** 为形容词；置于句首、后用逗号隔开，作评注性状语，需用副词 **Actually**（事实上），注意首字母大写。

4. **答案：yourself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。`understand` 的宾语与主语 **you** 同指一人，表示“更了解自己”，用 **yourself**（由 **you** 转换而来）。

5. **答案：practice**
   - **考点**：动词时态（主将从现）。**If** 引导的条件状语从句表将来情况时，从句常用一般现在时；主语 **you**，谓语用动词原形 **practice**。

6. **答案：grows**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与一般现在时。主语 **your active vocabulary** 为第三人称单数，陈述常态事实，谓语用 **grows**。

7. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**feel** 为系动词，后接形容词；语境暗含与“不会用、难用”相比“更容易运用”，**easy** 用比较级 **easier**，与 **to reuse** 搭配自然。

8. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **work as** 表示“充当；起……作用”，说明日记可以**作为**一种轻便的学习计划方式。

9. **答案：A**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**paragraph** 为可数名词单数，**short** 以辅音音素开头，用 **A** 表示泛指“一篇短短的练笔”；句首大写。

10. **答案：to try**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定结构 **encourage sb. to do sth.**，宾语补足语用不定式 **to try**。
