# 07-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：社区志愿服务 (Community Volunteer Work)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many teenagers today want to give something useful back to ___1___ community where they grow up, ___2___ they are not sure which activity suits them best. They may worry about being too shy, lacking skills, or having little free time between homework and hobbies. Joining a local volunteer group is ___3___ excellent idea because new members ___4___ (usual) receive clear guidance and short training before tasks begin, so nobody is left confused on the first day. Last month, our class ___5___ (visit) a community center ___6___ (help) organize a small charity sale for neighbors ___7___ (live) nearby. Parents and retired teachers came to buy books and snacks, and their friendly smiles made the hall feel warmer than winter outside. Some of us welcomed guests at the door, while others sorted donated sweaters and folded them neatly into large cartons. We ___8___ (sell) handmade bookmarks and collected warm clothes for families who ___9___ (need) them during the cold season. Later, we shared photos online to thank donors, hoping more people would join the next round of service. The busy afternoon showed us ___10___ cooperation and patience can turn simple work into real support for others, and it also left us with a stronger sense of responsibility toward the place we call home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。**community** 后有定语从句 **where they grow up** 限定，表示特指“他们成长所在的那个社区”，用 **the** 表特指（亦可从搭配 **the community** 角度记忆）。

2. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句“想回馈社区”与后句“不确定哪种活动适合自己”为转折关系，用 **but** 连接两个并列分句。

3. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**idea** 可数单数，**excellent** 以元音音素开头，用 **an** 表示“一个很好的主意”。

4. **答案：usually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **receive**，需用副词；**usual** 的副词形式为 **usually**（通常）。

5. **答案：visited**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 **Last month** 表示过去，谓语用一般过去时 **visited**。

6. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。表示“参观社区中心”的目的——去帮助组织义卖，用不定式 **to help** 作目的状语（亦符合 **visit… to do** 的常见叙事结构）。

7. **答案：living**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**neighbors** 与 **live** 之间为主动关系，“住在附近的邻居”，用现在分词 **living** 作后置定语，相当于定语从句 **who lived/who were living** 的简化。

8. **答案：sold**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与 **Last month** 语境一致，且与 **collected** 并列作谓语，用一般过去时 **sold**。

9. **答案：needed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。定语从句 **who ___ them** 修饰 **families**，叙述过去冬季的情况，**need** 用过去式 **needed**。

10. **答案：how**
    - **考点**：宾语从句引导词。**showed us** 后接宾语从句，从句意为“合作与耐心如何能把简单劳动变成对他人的真实帮助”，表示方式，用 **how** 引导（若用 **that** 则仅陈述事实，不如 **how** 贴合“展示给我们看”的语义）。
