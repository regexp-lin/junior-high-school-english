# 02-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园科技节 (School Science Fair)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school held its annual Science Fair last week, and it was the (1) ______ (exciting) one in years. Over 80 student projects (2) ______ (display) in the gymnasium, covering topics from renewable energy to artificial intelligence. My partner Li Hua and I spent two months (3) ______ (prepare) our project — a small robot that could sort recyclable materials automatically. We used sensors to help the robot identify different types of waste and programmed it (4) ______ (place) each item into the correct bin. On the day of the fair, we were both nervous (5) ______ excited. When the judges came to our table, they asked us many (6) ______ (question) about how the robot worked. Li Hua explained the programming while I demonstrated the sorting process. The judges seemed genuinely (7) ______ (impress) by our work. To our great (8) ______ (surprised), we won second place overall! The first prize went to a team that built a device for (9) ______ (clean) polluted water using solar power. Their project was truly remarkable. The Science Fair taught me that science is not just about textbooks and exams — it is about using (10) ______ (know) to solve real problems in creative ways.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most exciting**
   - **考点**：最高级。多音节形容词用 `most exciting`。

2. **答案：were displayed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。项目被展出。

3. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

4. **答案：to place**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`program sth. to do` 编程使某物做某事。

5. **答案：and / yet**
   - **考点**：连词。`nervous and excited` 既紧张又兴奋；或 `yet` 表转折。

6. **答案：questions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many questions` 许多问题。

7. **答案：impressed**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。`be impressed by` 被打动/印象深刻。

8. **答案：surprise**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`To our great surprise` 令我们非常惊讶的是，名词。

9. **答案：cleaning**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `for` 后用动名词。

10. **答案：knowledge**
    - **考点**：词性转换。动词 `know` 转换为名词 `knowledge` 知识。
