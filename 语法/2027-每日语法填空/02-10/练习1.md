# 02-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：垃圾分类 (Waste Sorting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Waste sorting has become a hot topic in China since Shanghai introduced strict garbage classification rules in 2019. Under these rules, household waste must (1) ______ (divide) into four categories: recyclable waste, hazardous waste, wet waste and dry waste. At first, many residents found it (2) ______ (difficulty) to remember which category each item belonged to. Some people even joked that standing in front of the bins was like taking (3) ______ exam. However, after months of practice, most people (4) ______ (get) used to the system. Our city introduced similar rules last year. My family was one of the first (5) ______ (start) sorting waste at home. We placed four different (6) ______ (colour) bins in our kitchen: green for food waste, blue for recyclables, red for hazardous items and grey for other waste. The (7) ______ (hard) part was dealing with food waste. We had to make sure no plastic bags (8) ______ (mix) in with the food scraps. My younger brother complained about the extra work, but our mother explained (9) ______ proper waste sorting is important for the environment. Now, our neighbourhood recycles over 40% of its total waste, (10) ______ is a significant improvement compared to just 15% two years ago. Small actions can lead to big changes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：be divided**
   - **考点**：含情态动词的被动语态。`must be divided` 必须被分为。

2. **答案：difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`find it difficult to do`，形容词作宾补。

3. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`taking an exam`，`exam` 以元音音素开头。

4. **答案：have got / got**
   - **考点**：现在完成时或一般过去时。`get used to` 习惯于。

5. **答案：to start**
   - **考点**：不定式作定语。序数词 `first` 后用不定式。

6. **答案：coloured**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`differently coloured bins` 不同颜色的桶。

7. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the hardest part` 最困难的部分。

8. **答案：were mixed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。塑料袋被混入，与主句过去时一致。

9. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连词。`explained why...` 解释为什么，引导宾语从句。

10. **答案：which**
    - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事。
