# 09-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：班级合唱比赛 (Class Singing Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, our grade __1__ (hold) a class singing competition in the school hall. Each class had to choose __2__ positive English or Chinese song and practice after school. Our monitor divided the work clearly: some students designed simple movements, __3__ others controlled the sound and lights. I was shy at the beginning, __4__ our music teacher encouraged me __5__ (sing) the opening part. On the big day, we wore matching T-shirts and stepped onto the stage __6__ confidence. When the piano started, everyone __7__ (focus) on the rhythm and smiled at each other. __8__ the song ended, the audience gave us loud applause. Though we didn't win first prize, we learned teamwork is __9__ (important) than any medal. I will never forget __10__ exciting experience.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last month 表过去，hold 的过去式为 held。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a positive ... song 一首积极向上的歌。

3. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词或从属连词。some... while others... 一些……而另一些……；或用 and 连接并列分句。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。害羞与老师鼓励之间为转折。

5. **答案：to sing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。encourage sb. to do sth. 鼓励某人做某事。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with confidence 充满信心地。

7. **答案：focused**
   - **考点**：动词时态。比赛当天叙述，focus 用过去式 focused。

8. **答案：When / After**
   - **考点**：从属连词。歌曲结束当刻或结束之后，观众鼓掌。

9. **答案：more important**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than any medal 提示比较级，多音节形容词 important 前加 more。

10. **答案：the / this / that**
   - **考点**：冠词或指示代词。特指这次激动人心的经历，the/this/that exciting experience。
