# 09-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：地球一小时 (Earth Hour)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Earth Hour is a global activity __1__ calls on people to turn off lights for one hour. It usually __2__ (take) place on the last Saturday of March. Last year, my community __3__ (organize) a candlelit walk in the park, and hundreds of neighbors joined __4__ happily. During that hour, we talked about how __5__ save water and reduce plastic waste in daily life. My father explained __6__ even a short blackout could remind us __7__ (value) electricity. Some shops also turned off unnecessary signs __8__ show support. __9__ the event lasts only sixty minutes, its message lasts much longer. I believe small actions, __10__ (work) together, can make a greener planet.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 activity 指物，从句 calls on 缺主语，用 that 或 which。

2. **答案：takes**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。usually 表习惯，一般现在时；主语 It 为第三人称单数，take place 用 takes place。

3. **答案：organized**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last year 表过去，organize 用过去式 organized。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。join in 表示“参加（活动）”，与后面的 happily 搭配自然；若用 join the walk 则通常直接接宾语，此处填空用 in 构成 join in。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：疑问词 + 不定式。how to save water 作 talked about 的宾语。

6. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句引导词。explained 后为宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 that 引导。

7. **答案：to value**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。remind sb. to do sth. 提醒某人做某事。

8. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。关掉招牌是为了表示支持，用 to show。

9. **答案：Although / Though**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导让步状语从句，“尽管活动只持续一小时，其意义更长久”。

10. **答案：working**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。small actions 与 work 为主动关系，用现在分词 working 作后置定语，相当于 which work together。
