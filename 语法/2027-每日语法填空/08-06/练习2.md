# 08-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：水资源保护 (Water Conservation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Water is precious in our daily lives; ___1___ (however), many people still waste it carelessly without noticing the cost. Many adults are busy and forget to fix dripping faucets for weeks. Scientists explain that only ___2___ small fraction of water on Earth is fresh water ___3___ is safe for drinking. Farmers need irrigation for their fields, and lakes as well as rivers support cities nearby, yet the snacks, drinks, and clothes we enjoy every day ___4___ (actual) require huge amounts of water to produce. In modern cities, clean water ___5___ (send) to tall buildings through long pipelines every day, ___6___ even a dripping tap may waste liters quietly while nobody is watching in the kitchen or bathroom. When students brush their teeth, they should turn the tap off ___7___ they finish rinsing, so water does not keep flowing for nothing valuable. In several cities, workers ___8___ (build) new treatment plants in recent years ___9___ (purify) used water for watering public gardens. Schools also encourage each family ___10___ (check) pipes for small leaks and collect rainwater for plants on balconies when possible. If each of us saves a little every day, our rivers will stay fuller and clearer for the next generation, and wildlife along the banks will benefit, too.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词（连接性状语）。*Water is precious* 与 *many people still waste it* 为转折关系；分号后常用 **However** 引出对比，注意首字母大写，后接逗号。*But* 多连接并列分句，若用分号结构，**However** 更正式、常见于说明文。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。*fraction* 为可数名词单数，此处表泛指“一小部分”，*small* 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 **a**，即 *only a small fraction*。*the* 表特指，此处无特指对象。

3. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（定语从句）。先行词 *fresh water* 指物，从句 *___ is safe for drinking* 缺主语，用 **that** 或 **which** 引导定语从句。*where* 在从句中作状语，不适用。

4. **答案：actually**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 *require* 需用副词；*actual* 的副词形式为 **actually**，表示“事实上、实际上需要大量水”。*actual* 为形容词，不可直接修饰动词。

5. **答案：is sent**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。主语 *clean water* 与 *send* 为被动（水“被输送”），且 *every day* 表示经常性情况，用一般现在时的被动语态 **is sent**（**am/is/are + 过去分词**）。主语为不可数名词，谓语用 **is**。

6. **答案：yet / but**
   - **考点**：连词。前半句讲城市每日供水，后半句讲滴水仍会造成浪费，为转折关系，用 **yet** 或 **but**。*so* 表结果，*and* 表并列，均不符合“尽管有管网，浪费仍可能发生”的逻辑。

7. **答案：until / before**
   - **考点**：连词。表示“在漱完口之前/直到漱完口才关水龙头”，用 **until**（直到……为止）或 **before**（在……之前）。*unless* 表条件“除非”，与“应养成关水习惯”的劝导语气不符。

8. **答案：have built**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。*in recent years* 是现在完成时的典型标志，表示过去发生并与现在有关，结构为 **have/has + 过去分词**。主语 *workers* 为复数，故填 **have built**。

9. **答案：to purify**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动词不定式）。空白处表目的——建造处理厂是为了净化废水用于浇灌，用不定式 **to purify**。*purifying* 表伴随或进行时不突出“目的”，此处不定式更贴切。

10. **答案：to check**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动词不定式）。*encourage* 的常见结构为 **encourage sb. to do sth.**，表示“鼓励某人做某事”，故填 **to check**。*check* 为动词原形，不可直接跟在 *encourage each family* 后。
