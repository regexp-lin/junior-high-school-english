# 08-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园篮球赛 (School Basketball Match)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school held ___1___ annual basketball competition ___2___ the playground on a sunny afternoon. Hundreds of students gathered around early ___3___ (cheer) for their favorite teams, and teachers also came to keep order. Some parents stood at the back and took photos whenever a player made a nice shot. Our class team looked confident because we ___4___ (practice) passing and shooting hard for three weeks, and we believed teamwork could make a difference.

The game started at four o'clock. Energy was high on both sides, and every basket was answered with loud applause. At first, the other team moved ___5___ (quick) across the court and scored several baskets; ___6___ our players didn't lose heart. The captain shouted, "Pass ___7___ ball quickly and watch your teammates!" Little by little, we caught up with careful defense. In the final quarter, Wang Lei jumped ___8___ (high) than anyone else, caught a long pass, and scored an ___9___ (importance) three-pointer that tied the game. When the referee blew the final whistle, we ___10___ (win) by two points and the whole grade cheered loudly for us. After the match, we shook hands with the opposing team and thanked our coach for his guidance.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。*annual basketball competition* 由形容词 *annual* 修饰，此处特指学校每年一度的赛事，用定冠词 **the**，即 *held the annual basketball competition*。*a/an* 表泛指，不符合“本校传统年度活动”的语境。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。表示“在操场上”用 **on the playground**（习惯搭配）；*in the playground* 在部分教材中也可见，但中考更常见 **on**。*at* 多接小地点或具体点，此处选 **on** 最稳妥。

3. **答案：to cheer**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动词不定式）。*gathered around early* 后接目的，用不定式 **to cheer**，表示“早早聚在周围是为了给……加油”。*cheering* 表伴随状态时语义不如不定式表目的清晰。

4. **答案：had practiced / had practised**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。主句 *looked confident* 为过去时，*practice* 发生在“比赛当天之前”的过去，表示“过去的过去”，用过去完成时 **had practiced**（英式 **had practised** 亦可）。

5. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。*moved* 为动词，需用副词修饰，*quick* 的副词形式为 **quickly**，表示“对方球队在场上移动得很快”。

6. **答案：However / But**
   - **考点**：连词。分号前讲对方得分，分号后讲我方没有灰心，为转折关系，用 **However**（常置于句首，后接逗号）或 **But**。*Therefore* 表因果，不符合逻辑。

7. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。*ball* 在语境中指比赛中的那颗球，为特指，用 **the**，即 *Pass the ball*。*a ball* 表任意一球，不符合场上指挥传球的语境。

8. **答案：higher**
   - **考点**：形容词和副词的比较等级。后有 *than anyone else*，须用比较级；*high* 的比较级为 **higher**，表示“跳得比任何人都高”。*highly* 多表抽象意义的“高度地”，不用于跳高语境。

9. **答案：important**
   - **考点**：词性转换。括号内 *importance* 为名词，空白处位于 *an* 之后、名词 *three-pointer* 之前，需用形容词 **important** 作定语，表示“一记重要的三分球”。*important* 以元音音素开头，故前文已用 **an**。

10. **答案：won**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。*When the referee blew the final whistle* 表明动作发生在过去，主语 *we* 与 *win* 为主动关系，用一般过去时 **won**，表示“我们以两分获胜”。注意 *win* 的过去式为不规则变化 *won*，不可写成 *winned*。
