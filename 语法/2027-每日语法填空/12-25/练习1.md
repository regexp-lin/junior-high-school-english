# 12-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：寒假研学旅行 (Winter Study Tour)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Study tours have become a popular way for students __1__ (learn) outside the classroom. This winter, our school is organising a five-day study tour __2__ Xi'an, one of China's most historic cities. We will visit the Terracotta Warriors, the Ancient City Wall and the Shaanxi History Museum. Our teacher said the tour __3__ (design) to help us connect what we learn in textbooks __4__ real life. Before the trip, each student needs to prepare a research topic. I have chosen to study the history of the Tang Dynasty because I find it __5__ (extreme) interesting. During the tour, we will also try traditional Xi'an snacks __6__ as roujiamo and biangbiang noodles. I have never __7__ (be) to Xi'an before, so I am very __8__ (excite) about this trip. My parents were a little worried at first, but our teacher promised that all students __9__ (look) after carefully. I believe this study tour will be a __10__ (value) experience that I will remember for a long time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作定语）。a popular way to learn 一种受欢迎的学习方式，不定式作后置定语修饰 way。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。a study tour to Xi'an 去西安的研学旅行，to 表示方向目的地。

3. **答案：was designed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。the tour 与 design 为被动关系，老师过去说的话，用一般过去时被动 was designed。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。connect... with... 把……与……联系起来，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 interesting，用副词 extremely。

6. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。such as 例如、比如，用于举例。

7. **答案：been**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。have never been to 从未去过，have been to 表示曾经去过某地。

8. **答案：excited**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ed 形容词）。be excited about 对……感到兴奋，修饰人用 -ed 形容词。

9. **答案：would be looked**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（过去将来时被动语态）。promised that students would be looked after 承诺学生们将被照顾好，宾语从句中 students 与 look after 为被动关系。

10. **答案：valuable**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。a valuable experience 一次宝贵的经历，修饰名词用形容词。
