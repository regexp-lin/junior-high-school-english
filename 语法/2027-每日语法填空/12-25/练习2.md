# 12-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：雪中送炭——冬日暖心故事 (A Heartwarming Winter Story)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

It was a freezing December evening. An old man was sitting __1__ (quiet) at a bus stop, looking cold and tired. He was wearing only a thin jacket, __2__ was clearly not enough for the winter weather. A middle school student named Xiao Lin happened to pass by. He noticed that the old man __3__ (shiver) badly. Without __4__ (think) twice, Xiao Lin took off his own warm coat and put it around the old man's __5__ (shoulder). The old man looked up in surprise and said, "Thank you, young man. You are so __6__ (kind)." Xiao Lin smiled and replied, "Please keep it. I live __7__ (close) and can get home quickly." A woman nearby __8__ (record) the whole scene with her phone. She posted the video online, and it was __9__ (watch) by over a million people within two days. Many people left comments __10__ (say) that Xiao Lin's behaviour made them believe in the goodness of human nature. Sometimes the smallest act of kindness can warm an entire city.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 sitting，用副词 quietly。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。which 引导非限制性定语从句，指代 a thin jacket，在从句中作主语。

3. **答案：was shivering**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去进行时）。noticed that... was shivering 注意到……正在发抖，表示当时正在进行的动作。

4. **答案：thinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。without thinking 毫不犹豫地，without 为介词，后接动名词。

5. **答案：shoulders**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。the old man's shoulders 老人的肩膀，人有两个肩膀用复数。

6. **答案：kind**
   - **考点**：形容词作表语。so kind 如此善良，be 后接形容词作表语。

7. **答案：nearby**
   - **考点**：副词。live nearby 住在附近，nearby 作副词表示"在附近"。

8. **答案：recorded**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述过去发生的事用一般过去时 recorded。

9. **答案：watched**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般过去时被动语态）。it 与 watch 为被动关系，描述过去的事用 was watched。

10. **答案：saying**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作伴随状语）。left comments saying 留下评论说，comments 与 say 为主动关系，用现在分词。
