# 06-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园跳蚤市场 (School Flea Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school held a flea market on the playground last Friday. Students were encouraged ___1___ (bring) items they no longer needed and sell them at low ___2___ (price). By nine o'clock, more than fifty stalls ___3___ (set) up. Some students sold second-hand books, ___4___ others offered toys, clothes and even homemade snacks. My friend Lucy and I decided to sell our old comic books. We designed a big colourful poster to attract ___5___ (customer). To our surprise, all of our books ___6___ (sell) out within an hour! The money ___7___ (raise) from the market would be donated to children in poor areas. ___8___ teacher said the event taught students the value of sharing and recycling. I think the flea market was not just fun — it was also ___9___ meaningful way to help others. We all hope the school ___10___ (hold) it again next term.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to bring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be encouraged to do sth. 表示"被鼓励做某事"，encourage sb. to do sth. 的被动形式，填 to bring。

2. **答案：prices**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。at low prices 表示"以低价"，price 为可数名词，此处指各种物品的价格，用复数 prices。

3. **答案：had been set**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。by nine o'clock 表示"到九点之前"，stalls 与 set up 之间为被动关系，用过去完成时的被动语态 had been set。

4. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。some...while others... 表示"一些……而另一些……"，填 while。

5. **答案：customers**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。attract customers 表示"吸引顾客"，指多位顾客，用复数 customers。

6. **答案：were sold**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。books 与 sell 之间为被动关系，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were sold。

7. **答案：raised**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，the money raised from... 表示"从……筹集的钱"，填 raised。

8. **答案：Our/The**
   - **考点**：代词/冠词。修饰名词 teacher，根据语境用 Our（我们的老师）或 The（那位老师），填 Our 或 The。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。way 为可数名词单数，a meaningful way 表示"一种有意义的方式"，用不定冠词 a。

10. **答案：will hold**
    - **考点**：动词时态。hope 后接宾语从句，表示对将来的期望，从句用一般将来时，填 will hold。
