# 06-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：睡眠与健康 (Sleep and Health)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Getting enough sleep is very important for teenagers, ___1___ many students do not take it ___2___ (serious). According to doctors, young people aged 13 to 17 need at least eight hours of sleep every night. ___3___, a survey showed that over sixty percent of middle school students sleep less than seven hours on school nights. The ___4___ (main) reasons include too much homework, phone use before bed, and late-night snacking. When we don't get enough sleep, our ___5___ (brain) cannot work properly. We may find it ___6___ (hard) to concentrate in class and our mood may become ___7___ (bad) than usual. Some students try ___8___ (make) up for lost sleep on weekends, but experts say this doesn't really work. The best way ___9___ (improve) sleep quality is to set a fixed bedtime and put away all electronic ___10___ (device) at least thirty minutes before sleeping.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。"睡眠对青少年很重要"与"很多学生不认真对待"之间为转折关系，用 but。

2. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 take，需用副词形式，serious → seriously。take sth. seriously 表示"认真对待某事"。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词。前后为转折关系，"需要至少八小时睡眠"与"超过六成学生睡不到七小时"，用 However，句首大写后加逗号。

4. **答案：main**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 reasons，main 本身就是形容词，表示"主要的原因"，填 main。

5. **答案：brains**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。our brains 表示"我们的大脑"，brain 为可数名词，此处泛指学生群体，用复数 brains。

6. **答案：hard/harder**
   - **考点**：形容词。find it hard to do sth. 表示"发现做某事很难"，填 hard；若理解为"更难"也可填 harder。

7. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than usual 提示用比较级，bad → worse。

8. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。try to do sth. 表示"尝试做某事"，填 to make。

9. **答案：to improve**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。the best way to do sth. 表示"做某事最好的方式"，不定式作后置定语，填 to improve。

10. **答案：devices**
    - **考点**：名词单复数。all + 复数名词，electronic devices 表示"电子设备"，device → devices。
