# 04-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国陶瓷 (Chinese Ceramics)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese ceramics have been admired around the world for (1) ______ smooth shapes and bright glazes. Porcelain from Jingdezhen, (2) ______ is known as the "Porcelain Capital", traveled along ancient trade routes to Europe.

During the holiday, I visited a pottery museum (3) ______ I watched a craftsman shape clay on a wheel. He explained that the clay (4) ______ (mix) with special minerals before it was fired in a kiln. The process seemed (5) ______ (simple) than I had expected, but each step required years of practice.

Visitors were not allowed (6) ______ (touch) the fragile vases, but we could take photos from a distance. A guide said that Chinese ceramics (7) ______ (influence) global art styles for centuries. I used to think bowls were only tools for daily life, (8) ______ now I see them as works of art.

I bought a small cup (9) ______ (color) blue and white as a gift for my mother. I hope she will feel the warmth of history (10) ______ she holds it in her hands.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。指代 `Chinese ceramics`，修饰 `shapes and glazes`，用形容词性物主代词 `their`。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句修饰 `Jingdezhen`，从句缺主语，指物，用 `which`。

3. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `museum`，从句中缺地点状语，“在博物馆里观看工匠”，用 `where`。

4. **答案：was mixed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。黏土“被混合”，叙述制作过程，用 `was mixed`。

5. **答案：simpler**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`simple` → `simpler`，`seemed` 后接形容词。

6. **答案：to touch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be allowed to do sth.` 被允许做某事。

7. **答案：have influenced**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`for centuries` 表示从过去持续到现在的影响，用 `have influenced`。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾以为只是日常用具”与“现在视为艺术品”为转折。

9. **答案：colored/coloured**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，修饰 `cup`，表“被蓝白着色”，`cup colored blue and white`。

10. **答案：when**
    - **考点**：从属连词。引导时间状语从句，“当她把杯子握在手中时”，用 `when`。
