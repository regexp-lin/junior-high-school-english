# 04-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：课堂互动游戏 (Classroom Interactive Games)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our English teacher often uses short games (1) ______ make grammar lessons more lively. Yesterday she divided the class into four groups and asked us (2) ______ (race) to find grammar mistakes in a short passage.

We were given only three minutes, (3) ______ everyone focused on the task quickly. The winning team (4) ______ (choose) to explain their answers in front of the class. I used to be afraid of speaking in public, (5) ______ this game changed my attitude.

When I stood up to share, my voice shook at first, (6) ______ my classmates nodded with encouragement. The teacher praised us for working together (7) ______ (careful) than before. She said that learning is not only about scores (8) ______ also about confidence.

I believe interactive games can turn a quiet classroom (9) ______ a lively space. I hope more teachers will try (10) ______ (create) such activities in their lessons.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，“用游戏使语法课更生动”。

2. **答案：to race**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`ask sb. to do sth.` 要求某人做某事。

3. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句“只给三分钟”与后句“大家迅速专注”为因果关系，用 `so` 引出结果。

4. **答案：was chosen**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。获胜队“被选中”解释答案，用 `was chosen`。

5. **答案：yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。书面语中表转折，“曾害怕公开讲话，然而游戏改变了态度”，`yet` 与 `but` 近义。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“起初声音发抖”与“同学点头鼓励”为转折。

7. **答案：more carefully**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。句中有 `than`，修饰动词 `working`，`careful` → `carefully` 的比较级 `more carefully`。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but also...` 不仅……而且……。

9. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`turn... into...` 把……变成……。

10. **答案：to create**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`try to do sth.` 努力做某事。
