# 12-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校厨艺比赛 (School Cooking Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school held a cooking competition as part of the Life Skills Week. Students from each class had to prepare __1__ dish using ingredients provided by the school. My team decided __2__ (make) tomato egg noodles, which is a classic Chinese home-style dish. We divided the tasks among __3__ (we). Li Hua was in charge of __4__ (cut) the vegetables while I boiled the noodles. Wang Ming, the best cook in our team, fried the eggs and made the tomato sauce. The __5__ (smell) from our cooking station was so good that it attracted many __6__ (visitor). In total, twelve teams participated __7__ the competition. The judges tasted every dish and scored them on taste, appearance and __8__ (create). Our tomato egg noodles received high praise and we won second place. The team that came first made a __9__ (beauty) decorated fruit salad. This competition taught us that cooking is not just __10__ necessary life skill — it can also be a lot of fun.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。a dish 一道菜，dish 为可数名词单数，首次提及用不定冠词 a。

2. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾语）。decide to make 决定做，decide 后接不定式。

3. **答案：ourselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。among ourselves 在我们之间，主语为 we，用反身代词 ourselves。

4. **答案：cutting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。in charge of cutting 负责切，of 为介词，后接动名词。

5. **答案：smell**
   - **考点**：名词。The smell 气味，the 后接名词。

6. **答案：visitors**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。many visitors 许多参观者，many 后接可数名词复数。

7. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。participate in 参加……，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：creativity**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。taste, appearance and creativity 味道、外观和创意，与前面两个名词并列，用名词形式。

9. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。beautifully decorated 精美装饰的，修饰过去分词 decorated 用副词。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。a necessary life skill 一项必要的生活技能，skill 为可数名词单数。
