# 12-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：平安夜文化交流 (Christmas Eve Cultural Exchange)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Yesterday, our English teacher told us about Christmas traditions in __1__ (difference) countries. In the UK, families usually have a big turkey dinner on Christmas Day. Children hang stockings by the fireplace and hope __2__ Santa Claus will fill them with gifts. In Germany, people enjoy __3__ (bake) special Christmas cookies called Lebkuchen. In Australia, __4__ Christmas falls in summer, many families celebrate with a barbecue on the beach! Our teacher also asked us to compare these customs __5__ Chinese New Year traditions. We found that although the festivals are different, they share something __6__ common — they are all about family, love and __7__ (thank). After the lesson, our class decided to hold a small cultural exchange party. Some students __8__ (bring) Christmas cards they had made by __9__ (they), while others shared stories about how their families celebrate Chinese New Year. It was a wonderful way to learn about other cultures and appreciate __10__ (we) own at the same time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。different countries 不同的国家，修饰名词用形容词。

2. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词（宾语从句）。hope that 希望……，that 引导宾语从句。

3. **答案：baking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。enjoy baking 喜欢烘焙，enjoy 后接动名词。

4. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系副词）。In Australia, where Christmas falls in summer 在澳大利亚，那里的圣诞节在夏天，where 引导非限制性定语从句。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。compare... with 把……与……相比较，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in common 共同点，为固定搭配。

7. **答案：thankfulness/thanks**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。与前面的 family, love 并列，需要名词形式，thank 的名词为 thankfulness 或 thanks。

8. **答案：brought**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述过去聚会时的情况，用一般过去时 brought。

9. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。by themselves 靠他们自己，by oneself 为固定搭配。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。our own 我们自己的，修饰名词用形容词性物主代词。
