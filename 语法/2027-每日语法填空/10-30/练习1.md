# 10-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬日暖阳下的集市 (Winter Sunshine Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every Saturday morning, a farmers' market __1__ (hold) in the park near my home. Last weekend, my parents and I went there __2__ the warm winter sunshine. The market was crowded __3__ people of all ages. Farmers from nearby villages set up stalls __4__ (sell) fresh vegetables, fruits and homemade food. One stall __5__ (catch) my eye — it was selling hand-knitted scarves in beautiful __6__ (colour). I bought a red one for my grandmother as __7__ early birthday gift. My mother picked up some organic sweet __8__ (potato) and a jar of local honey. My father enjoyed __9__ (taste) different kinds of cheese. The market had a warm and friendly atmosphere. An old man played the erhu __10__ a bench while children ran around happily. I love these weekend markets because they bring fresh food and joy to our community.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is held**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 market 与 hold 为被动关系，every Saturday morning 表示经常性动作，用一般现在时被动语态 is held。

2. **答案：in / under**
   - **考点**：介词。in/under the warm winter sunshine 在温暖的冬日阳光下。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be crowded with 挤满了……，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：to sell / selling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语 to sell，或现在分词作伴随状语 selling，均可。

5. **答案：caught**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文为过去时态叙述，catch 的过去式为不规则变化 caught。

6. **答案：colours**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。beautiful colours 美丽的颜色，指多种颜色，用复数 colours。

7. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。early 以元音音素 /ɜː/ 开头，泛指"一份提前的生日礼物"，用 an。

8. **答案：potatoes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。some 后接可数名词复数，potato 变复数加 -es，用 potatoes。

9. **答案：tasting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。enjoy doing sth. 喜欢做某事，用动名词 tasting。

10. **答案：on**
    - **考点**：介词。on a bench 在长凳上，用 on 表示在……之上。
