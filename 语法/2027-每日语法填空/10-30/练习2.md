# 10-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：秋冬养生 (Autumn-Winter Health Care)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As the weather gets __1__ (cold), it is important to take care of our health. In traditional Chinese culture, autumn and winter are seasons for __2__ (nourish) the body. My grandmother always says, "Eat warm food and keep your __3__ (foot) warm." She makes herbal soups __4__ ingredients like red dates, goji berries and ginger. These ingredients are believed __5__ (strengthen) the immune system. Doctors also advise us to drink __6__ (much) water than usual because the dry air can harm our skin and throat. Regular exercise is __7__ (equal) important. Even a short walk after dinner can improve blood circulation. However, many teenagers stay indoors __8__ play on their phones instead of exercising. This is __9__ good habit. We should remember that health is our greatest __10__ (wealthy). A little effort each day can make a big difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：colder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。gets colder 变得更冷，暗含与之前相比，用比较级 colder。

2. **答案：nourishing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。for + 动名词，表示目的"用于滋养身体"，用 nourishing。

3. **答案：feet**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。foot 的复数为不规则变化 feet。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with ingredients 用……食材，with 表示"使用、带有"。

5. **答案：to strengthen**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be believed to do sth. 被认为能做某事，用不定式 to strengthen。

6. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than usual，much 的比较级为 more，drink more water 喝更多水。

7. **答案：equally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 important，用副词 equally。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。stay indoors 与 play on their phones 为并列动作，用 and 连接。

9. **答案：not**
   - **考点**：副词。not a good habit 不是一个好习惯，用 not 构成否定。

10. **答案：wealth**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。greatest 后接名词，wealthy 的名词形式为 wealth。
