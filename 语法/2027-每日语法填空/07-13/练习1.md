# 07-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：在线学习 (Online Learning)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Over the past few years, online learning has become a normal part of school life for many teenagers, including me. My class started using mixed online lessons two years ago. At first I ___1___ (find) it really hard to stay focused in front of a computer screen for hours. Slowly, however, I ___2___ (learn) how to take clear notes while watching videos and how to manage my time. Every Monday our teacher uploads new tasks ___3___ a learning platform, and ___4___ allows us to see deadlines and feedback in one place. If the Wi-Fi at home is weak, I ___5___ (usual) ride my bike to the community library and study there quietly. During live lessons, we are asked to turn on our cameras only when necessary, which helps protect students' privacy. After class, I often review the recordings and underline key sentences in my notebook. Some classmates say online study is ___6___ (easy) than going to a real classroom, but I do not agree with them completely. No matter where we study, we should never ___7___ (ignore) helpful suggestions from parents and teachers. ___8___ the long run, forming good study habits matters ___9___ than buying the newest phone or tablet. With patience, self-discipline and daily practice, I believe anyone can ___10___ (success) in a digital learning environment.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：found**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 at first 与后文 slowly, however 形成对比，叙述“刚开始”那一阶段的感受，用一般过去时；find 的过去式为 found。

2. **答案：learned / learnt**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与 at first 的过去情况相对，slowly 之后表示过去逐渐学会的过程，用一般过去时；learn 的过去式为 learned 或 learnt。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。upload sth. to … 表示“把……上传到……”，为固定搭配；此处指把任务上传到学习平台。

4. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。并列句中后半句缺主语，指代前文单数名词 the learning platform，用人称代词主格 it。

5. **答案：usually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词短语 ride my bike，需用副词作状语；usual → usually（通常）。

6. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中出现比较连词 than，easy 为双音节形容词，比较级为 easier，表示“比去实体教室更容易”。

7. **答案：ignore**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。情态动词 should 后接动词原形，否定式 never 仍修饰动词原形，故填 ignore。

8. **答案：In**
   - **考点**：介词与固定搭配。in the long run 表示“从长远来看”，句首首字母大写，填 In。

9. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。matters 为动词，后接 than 构成比较，用 much 的比较级 more，表示“比买最新设备更重要”。

10. **答案：succeed**
    - **考点**：词性转换。情态动词 can 后接动词原形；success 为名词，此处需用动词 succeed，succeed in … 表示“在……方面取得成功”。
