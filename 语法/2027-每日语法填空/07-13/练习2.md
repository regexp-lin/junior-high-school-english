# 07-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国茶文化 (Chinese Tea Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese tea culture is famous around the world and it still influences daily life today. Tea ___1___ (play) an important role in Chinese society for centuries. Every morning, my grandmother ___2___ (heat) water on the stove and makes a whole pot of jasmine tea for the family. The lovely smell spreads through our flat and wakes everyone up gently. On weekends, she sometimes teaches me how to hold a cup with both hands and how to thank the person who pours the tea for me. She says these small actions carry respect and care that words alone cannot express. When her friends visit, she ___3___ (polite) invites them to sit around the table and taste the tea slowly before they talk about anything else. The final taste depends on ___4___ the tea leaves are picked and how skilfully they ___5___ (process) in the factory. If newcomers use boiling water on delicate green tea, it may turn bitter, so it should ___6___ (not boil) for too long. In recent years, many quiet tea houses ___7___ (open) in our city centre, ___8___ teenagers and artists study, read or simply enjoy the peaceful atmosphere. To appreciate Chinese tea ___9___ (deep), we should learn its long history and show ___10___ respect for the workers who grow and pick the leaves.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has played**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 for centuries 表示从过去持续到现在的影响，主语 tea 为不可数名词，视为第三人称单数，用现在完成时 has played。

2. **答案：heats**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。every morning 表示习惯性、反复发生的动作，用一般现在时；主语 my grandmother 为第三人称单数，heat 加 -s 变为 heats。

3. **答案：politely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 invites，需用副词作状语；polite → politely（礼貌地）。

4. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：连词。depend on 后接宾语从句，与后文 how 引导的从句并列；此处表示“茶叶何时采摘”，用 when 引导从句。

5. **答案：are processed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。they 指代 the tea leaves，与 process（加工）之间为被动关系，且陈述一般情况，用一般现在时的被动语态 are processed。

6. **答案：not be boiled**
   - **考点**：动词语态与情态动词。主语 it 指代 green tea 或冲泡语境下的茶，与 boil 为被动关系（被煮沸/用开水久煮）；情态动词 should 的否定被动结构为 should not be done，故填 not be boiled。

7. **答案：have opened**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in recent years 为现在完成时常见时间状语，many tea houses 为复数主语，表示茶馆已经开张并对现在有影响，用 have opened。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 tea houses，定语从句中不缺主语或宾语，需用关系副词 where 在从句中作地点状语，表示“在这些茶馆里”。

9. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 appreciate，需用副词；deep → deeply（深入地）。注意 deep 作副词多用于具体空间，表抽象程度时常用 deeply。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词。名词 respect 前需限定词，与主语 we 呼应，用形容词性物主代词 our，表示“我们对采茶工人的尊重”。
