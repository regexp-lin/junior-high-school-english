# 12-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国功夫 (Chinese Kung Fu)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese kung fu, also known __1__ martial arts, has a very long history. It includes many different styles, such as Shaolin, Wing Chun and Tai Chi. My cousin, Zhang Wei, has been __2__ (learn) Wing Chun for six years. He practises for at __3__ (little) two hours every day. I once asked him __4__ he chose Wing Chun. He told me that he __5__ (inspire) by a kung fu film when he was eight years old. Since then, he __6__ (devote) himself to this art. Kung fu is not just about __7__ (fight). It also teaches people discipline, respect and self-control. Zhang Wei says that the __8__ (hard) part is not the physical training but controlling your mind. Last month, he won first place in a __9__ (nation) youth martial arts competition. His coach was very proud of him. I think Chinese kung fu is __10__ important part of our culture, and more young people should try to learn it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。be known as 被称为……，as 表示"作为"。

2. **答案：learning**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成进行时）。has been learning 一直在学，for six years 表示从过去持续到现在的动作。

3. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：固定短语。at least 至少，little 的最高级为 least。

4. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：宾语从句（连接副词）。asked him why 问他为什么，why 引导宾语从句。

5. **答案：was inspired**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。he 与 inspire 为被动关系，when he was eight 表示过去，用一般过去时被动 was inspired。

6. **答案：has devoted**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Since then 从那以后，与现在完成时连用，用 has devoted。

7. **答案：fighting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。about fighting 关于战斗，about 为介词，后接动名词。

8. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the hardest part 最难的部分，the 提示用最高级。

9. **答案：national**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。national competition 全国比赛，修饰名词用形容词。

10. **答案：an**
    - **考点**：冠词。an important part 一个重要的部分，important 以元音音素开头，用不定冠词 an。
