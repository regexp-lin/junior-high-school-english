# 12-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：写给未来自己的信 (A Letter to My Future Self)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

At the end of last term, our head teacher asked each of us __1__ (write) a letter to our future selves. She said the letters __2__ (open) three years later when we graduate from high school. I sat at my desk and thought for a long time before I __3__ (begin) writing. In the letter, I wrote about my __4__ (dream) for the future. I told my future self that I wanted to become __5__ engineer and build bridges that connect people. I also wrote about the things I was __6__ (thank) for, including my family and my friends who always stand __7__ my side. At the end of the letter, I reminded __8__ (I) to stay kind and never stop learning. Writing this letter was __9__ unusual experience because I had never really thought about my future so __10__ (serious) before. I wonder what I will think when I read it three years from now.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to write**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾补）。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式。

2. **答案：would be opened**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（过去将来时被动语态）。said 引导宾语从句，letters 与 open 为被动关系，用 would be opened。

3. **答案：began**
   - **考点**：动词时态。before I began 在我开始之前，描述过去的动作用一般过去时 began。

4. **答案：dreams**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。my dreams 我的梦想，dream 为可数名词，此处表示多个梦想用复数。

5. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。an engineer 一名工程师，engineer 以元音音素开头，用不定冠词 an。

6. **答案：thankful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be thankful for 对……感恩，be 后接形容词作表语。

7. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。stand by my side 站在我身边，by 表示"在旁边"。

8. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。reminded myself 提醒自己，主语和宾语指同一人时用反身代词。

9. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。an unusual experience 一次不同寻常的经历，unusual 以元音音素开头，用 an。

10. **答案：seriously**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 thought，用副词 seriously。
