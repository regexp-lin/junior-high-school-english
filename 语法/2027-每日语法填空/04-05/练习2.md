# 04-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园壁报 (School Wall Newspaper)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every month, our school puts up a wall newspaper near the library. It is run by (1) ______ student team, and each issue has a different theme, such as "Green Campus" or "Reading Week".

Last term, I was lucky enough (2) ______ (choose) as one of the editors. On the first day, we argued about the layout for hours. Some members wanted more photos, (3) ______ others preferred longer articles. Finally, we reached a compromise that everyone could accept.

We interviewed teachers, collected student poems, and even drew cartoons (4) ______ ourselves. The work was (5) ______ (tire) than I expected, but I learned how to meet deadlines. When the printed copies were posted, students stood in front of the wall (6) ______ (read) every word.

Our teacher praised us for working together. She said the wall newspaper was (7) ______ (value) because it gave shy students a voice. I realized that behind each page (8) ______ (lie) care and patience.

This experience has made me more responsible. Next month, we plan (9) ______ (add) a QR code so readers can watch short videos.

If you walk past the bulletin board, take (10) ______ minute to enjoy what your classmates have created.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a student team` 一个学生团队，泛指。

2. **答案：to be chosen**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词被动。`be lucky enough to be chosen` 足够幸运被选中，用不定式被动。

3. **答案：while / but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`some ... while others ...` 或 `some ... but others ...` 对比。

4. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by ourselves` 我们自己（亲自）。

5. **答案：more tiring**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，`tiring` 多音节用 `more tiring`。

6. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。站着“读”每一个字，用 `reading`。

7. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was` 后接形容词，`value` → `valuable`。

8. **答案：lay / lies**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与一般过去时/现在时。倒装句 `behind each page lay/lies care`，主语 `care` 不可数，现在时 `lies`；叙述过去可用 `lay`（lie 的过去式）。

9. **答案：to add**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`plan to do sth.` 计划做某事。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`take a minute` 花一分钟。
