# 04-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：古代四大发明 (Four Great Inventions)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ancient China gave the world four great inventions: paper-making, printing, gunpowder, and the compass. Among them, paper and printing (1) ______ (change) education deeply because books became cheaper and easier to copy.

Before paper was widely used, people wrote on bamboo slips, (2) ______ were heavy to carry. A scholar named Cai Lun improved the paper-making process, and his method spread to other countries (3) ______ time. Today we still benefit from this invention every day.

The compass helped sailors find directions at sea. It made long journeys (4) ______ (safe) than traveling only by guessing the stars. Gunpowder was first used in fireworks, but later it influenced warfare in ways that history books describe (5) ______ detail.

These inventions show that creativity can shape human progress. When I read about them in class, I felt proud of my culture (6) ______ realized how much we owe to ancient scientists. Their ideas were not only clever, (7) ______ also practical.

We should remember that knowledge (8) ______ (pass) from generation to generation. If we protect our heritage, more young inventors (9) ______ (encourage) to dream big.

In my opinion, the spirit of exploration is just (10) ______ important as the inventions themselves.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：changed / have changed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。若叙述历史影响用一般过去时 `changed`；若强调对现在的影响用现在完成时 `have changed`，主语为复数。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代 `bamboo slips`，从句缺主语，用 `which`。

3. **答案：over / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`over time` 随着时间推移；或 `with time` 随时间。

4. **答案：safer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，`make journeys safer`。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`in detail` 详细地。

6. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`felt proud` 与 `realized` 并列。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only ... but also ...` 不仅……而且……。

8. **答案：is passed / passed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。知识“被传递”，`knowledge is passed`；若作一般陈述也可用主动 `passes`，此处被动更贴切。

9. **答案：will be encouraged**
   - **考点**：一般将来时被动语态。年轻人“将被鼓励”，`if` 条件句主句中表将来。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：固定结构。`as ... as ...` 和……一样……。
