# 09-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：新学期计划 (New Semester Plan)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A new semester has just begun, and I have made a detailed plan to help ___1___ (I) make the most of it. I know that Grade Nine is the key year that will determine ___2___ I can enter a good high school, so I must work harder than ever before.

First of all, I plan ___3___ (spend) at least two hours every evening reviewing what we have learned in class. My ___4___ (weak) subject is maths, so I will do extra exercises and ask my teacher for help whenever I have ___5___ (difficult) understanding a problem.

Secondly, I want to keep ___6___ (improve) my English. Besides doing homework carefully, I will listen to English podcasts on my way to school. I also aim ___7___ (read) one English book every month to expand my vocabulary.

___8___ (three), I need to take better care of my health. Last semester, I often stayed up late and did not get enough sleep, ___9___ made me feel tired during the day. This time, I promise to go to bed before ten o'clock and exercise for at ___10___ (little) thirty minutes every day.

I believe that with hard work and a good plan, I can achieve my goals.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。主语 `I` 与宾语指同一人，"帮助我自己"需用反身代词 **myself**。

2. **答案：whether / if**
   - **考点**：连词（宾语从句）。`determine` 后接宾语从句，表示"决定我是否能进入好高中"，用 **whether** 或 **if** 引导。

3. **答案：to spend**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**plan to do sth.**（计划做某事），故填 **to spend**。

4. **答案：weakest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`My ___ subject` 暗含与其他科目相比的含义，括号给出 `weak`，用最高级 **weakest** 表示"最薄弱的科目"。

5. **答案：difficulty**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 名词）。**have difficulty (in) doing sth.**（做某事有困难），`difficulty` 为不可数名词。

6. **答案：improving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**keep doing sth.**（持续做某事），故填 **improving**。

7. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**aim to do sth.**（目标是做某事），故填 **to read**。

8. **答案：Thirdly / Third**
   - **考点**：词性转换（数词 → 序数副词）。与前面的 `First of all` 和 `Secondly` 对应，表示"第三"，用 **Thirdly** 或 **Third**。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。前面整句"经常熬夜且睡眠不足"作先行内容，逗号后为非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个情况，用 **which**。

10. **答案：least**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。**at least**（至少）为固定搭配，`little` 的最高级为 **least**。
