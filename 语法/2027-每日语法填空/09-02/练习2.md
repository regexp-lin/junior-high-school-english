# 09-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：雨林保护 (Rainforest Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tropical rainforests cover only about six percent of the earth's surface, ___1___ they are home to more than half of the world's plant and animal ___2___ (species). They also play a key role in regulating the global climate by ___3___ (absorb) carbon dioxide and releasing oxygen.

However, rainforests are ___4___ (disappear) at an alarming rate. Every year, millions of hectares of forest ___5___ (cut) down to make room for farms, cattle ranches and roads. This not only destroys the habitats of countless creatures ___6___ also contributes to climate change.

The Amazon Rainforest, often ___7___ (refer) to as "the lungs of the Earth", is the ___8___ (large) tropical rainforest in the world. It stretches across nine countries in South America. Scientists warn that if deforestation continues at the current speed, up to 40 percent of the Amazon could be lost ___9___ 2050.

What can ordinary people do to help? We can start by being more careful about the products we buy. For example, choosing paper and wood products ___10___ (make) from sustainably managed forests is one way to reduce the demand for illegal logging. Every small action matters when it comes to protecting our planet's green lungs.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前半句说热带雨林只覆盖地球表面约6%，后半句说它们却拥有世界上一半以上的动植物，前后为转折关系，故用 **but**。

2. **答案：species**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。`species` 为单复数同形的名词，此处与 `plant and animal` 搭配指多种物种，形式仍为 **species**。

3. **答案：absorbing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**by doing sth.**（通过做某事），介词 `by` 后接动名词，故填 **absorbing**。

4. **答案：disappearing**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**are disappearing**（正在消失），现在进行时表示当前正在发生的趋势，故填 **disappearing**。

5. **答案：are cut**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。`Every year`（每年）提示一般现在时；主语 `millions of hectares of forest` 与 `cut down` 之间为被动关系，故填 **are cut**（down）。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。**not only... but also...**（不仅……而且……）为固定结构，故填 **but**。

7. **答案：referred**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`The Amazon Rainforest` 与 `refer` 之间为被动关系（被称为），用过去分词 **referred** 作插入语/后置定语，**be referred to as**（被称为）。

8. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**the + 最高级** 表示"最大的"，`large` 的最高级为 **largest**。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。**by 2050**（到2050年之前）表示"在某个时间之前"。

10. **答案：made**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`paper and wood products` 与 `make` 之间为被动关系（产品"被制造"），用过去分词 **made** 作后置定语。
