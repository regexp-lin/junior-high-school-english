# 08-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节能减排 (Energy Saving and Emission Reduction)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Energy saving and emission reduction have become important topics around the world. As the population grows, the demand ___1___ energy is increasing rapidly. If we continue ___2___ (waste) energy at the current rate, natural resources will run out much faster than expected.

There are many simple things that we can do in our daily life ___3___ (save) energy. For example, ___4___ (turn) off the lights when leaving a room is a good habit. We should also try to use less hot water because ___5___ (heat) water requires a large amount of electricity or gas. In addition, taking public transport instead of ___6___ (drive) a private car can greatly reduce carbon emissions.

Some schools have already started "green campus" programmes. Students are taught to recycle paper, plastic bottles ___7___ old textbooks. One school in Beijing even installed solar panels on ___8___ (it) roof, which now provides about 20 percent of the school's electricity. These efforts may seem small, ___9___ they can make a real difference when millions of people join in.

Saving energy is not just the ___10___ (responsible) of governments and companies. Everyone can play a part. Let us start today and make our planet a better place for future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。**the demand for...**（对……的需求）为固定搭配，故填 **for**。

2. **答案：wasting / to waste**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**continue doing sth.** 或 **continue to do sth.** 均可表示"继续做某事"，故填 **wasting** 或 **to waste**。

3. **答案：to save**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式 **to save** 作目的状语，表示"为了节约能源"。

4. **答案：Turning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词短语作句子主语，`turn` 变为 **Turning**，句首首字母大写。

5. **答案：heating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。`because` 引导的原因状语从句中，动名词 **heating** 作从句主语，表示"加热水"这件事。

6. **答案：driving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**instead of doing sth.**（而不是做某事），介词 `of` 后接动名词，故填 **driving**。

7. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`paper, plastic bottles ___ old textbooks` 为三个并列名词，用 **and** 连接最后一项。

8. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词（物主代词）。修饰名词 `roof` 需用形容词性物主代词，`it` 的形容词性物主代词为 **its**（指代学校）。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前半句说"这些努力看起来很小"，后半句说"当百万人加入时能产生真正的改变"，前后为转折关系，故用 **but**。

10. **答案：responsibility**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 名词）。定冠词 `the` 后接名词，**responsible** 的名词形式为 **responsibility**（责任），表示"不仅是政府和企业的责任"。
