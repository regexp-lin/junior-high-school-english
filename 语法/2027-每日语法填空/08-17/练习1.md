# 08-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：英语演讲比赛 (English Speech Contest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school held an English speech contest in the lecture hall. Over fifty students from different ___1___ (class) signed up for the competition. The topic was "The Person Who Has Influenced Me Most", ___2___ attracted a lot of attention.

I was one of the ___3___ (compete). To be honest, I felt quite ___4___ (nerve) when I stood on the stage because there were hundreds of people sitting below. My hands were shaking and my voice was not as steady ___5___ I had hoped. However, after the first few sentences, I began to calm down and spoke more ___6___ (fluent).

In my speech, I talked about my grandmother, who taught ___7___ (she) to read and write at the age of sixty. She believed that it was never too late ___8___ (learn) something new. Her story always inspires me whenever I face difficulties.

At the end of the contest, the judges ___9___ (announce) the results. I did not win the first prize, but I got a second prize, which was already beyond my expectation. More ___10___ (important), I learned that the courage to speak up is more valuable than a perfect performance.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：classes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different`（不同的）修饰可数名词复数，`class` 的复数形式为 **classes**。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词为前面整个话题内容，逗号后为非限制性定语从句，在从句中作主语，故用 **which**。

3. **答案：competitors**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。`one of the + 复数名词`（……之一），**compete** 的名词形式（指人）为 **competitor**，用复数 **competitors**。

4. **答案：nervous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。系动词 `felt` 后接形容词作表语，**nerve** 的形容词形式为 **nervous**（紧张的）。

5. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。**not as/so + adj./adv. + as...**（不如……那样），表示"我的声音不如我预期的那样稳定"，故填 **as**。

6. **答案：fluently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `spoke` 需用副词，**fluent** 的副词形式为 **fluently**；`more fluently` 构成比较级，表示"更加流利地"。

7. **答案：herself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。主语 `who`（指代 `my grandmother`，女性）与宾语指同一人，"自学读写"需用反身代词 **herself**。

8. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**It is never too late to do sth.**（做某事永远不晚）为固定句型，`it` 为形式主语，不定式为真正主语，故填 **to learn**。

9. **答案：announced**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去发生的事情用一般过去时，`announce` 的过去式为 **announced**。

10. **答案：importantly**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。**More importantly**（更重要的是）为插入语，修饰整个句子需用副词形式 **importantly**。
