# 08-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑期社会实践 (Summer Social Practice)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This summer, our school organised a social practice programme for students in Grade Eight. We were divided ___1___ four groups, and each group was given a different task. My group's task was to study the ___2___ (use) of plastic bags in our neighbourhood.

Over the course of one week, we visited supermarkets, vegetable markets and small shops. We counted how many plastic bags ___3___ (give) out each day and interviewed shopkeepers ___4___ customers. The results were ___5___ (shock): an average small shop handed out more than 200 plastic bags per day!

We then wrote a report and ___6___ (present) it to the community office. In the report, we suggested that shops should charge for plastic bags and encourage customers ___7___ (bring) their own reusable bags. The community leader was impressed by our work and promised to discuss our ___8___ (suggest) at the next meeting.

This experience taught me that even young students can make a difference ___9___ they take action. Social practice is not just about ___10___ (complete) a school assignment — it is about caring for the world around us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。**be divided into...**（被分成……）为固定搭配，故填 **into**。

2. **答案：use**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。定冠词 `the` 后接名词，`use` 本身既可作动词也可作名词，此处为名词形式 **use**（使用情况）。

3. **答案：were given**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `how many plastic bags` 与 `give out` 之间为被动关系（袋子"被分发"），叙述过去的调查用一般过去时被动语态，故填 **were given**（out）。

4. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`shopkeepers ___ customers` 为两个并列名词，表示"店主和顾客"，用 **and** 连接。

5. **答案：shocking**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。系动词 `were` 后接形容词作表语，修饰事物/结果用 **-ing** 形式，故填 **shocking**（令人震惊的）。

6. **答案：presented**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与前面的 `wrote` 并列，叙述过去发生的事用一般过去时，`present` 的过去式为 **presented**。

7. **答案：to bring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**encourage sb. to do sth.**（鼓励某人做某事），故填 **to bring**。

8. **答案：suggestions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）+ 名词复数。形容词性物主代词 `our` 后接名词，**suggest** 的名词形式为 **suggestion**；此处指多条建议，用复数 **suggestions**。

9. **答案：if / when**
   - **考点**：连词。"即使是年轻的学生也能有所作为，**如果/当** 他们采取行动的话"，用条件连词 **if** 或时间连词 **when**。

10. **答案：completing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**about doing sth.**（关于做某事），介词 `about` 后接动名词，故填 **completing**。
