# 08-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：京剧艺术 (Peking Opera)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Peking Opera, also known ___1___ Beijing Opera, is one of the most important forms of traditional Chinese performing arts. It ___2___ (develop) during the Qing Dynasty and has a history of over 200 years.

A Peking Opera performance ___3___ (combine) singing, acting, martial arts and acrobatics. The actors wear colourful costumes and paint their ___4___ (face) with different patterns. Each colour has a special ___5___ (mean): for example, red represents loyalty and courage, while white often stands for dishonesty.

___6___ (learn) Peking Opera is extremely hard. Young performers usually begin training at the age of seven or eight. They must practise basic ___7___ (skill) such as singing, speaking, acting and fighting every day. It takes many years of hard work before they can perform ___8___ (confident) on stage.

In recent years, Peking Opera ___9___ (face) the challenge of attracting young audiences. To solve this problem, some modern productions have added new elements like digital stage effects. Schools have also started Peking Opera clubs ___10___ that more students can learn about this cultural treasure.

I believe Peking Opera will continue to shine as long as people work together to keep it alive.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。**be known as...**（被称为……）为固定搭配，表示"京剧又被称为北京歌剧"。注意区分 be known for（以……著名）。

2. **答案：developed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`during the Qing Dynasty`（在清朝时期）提示一般过去时，`develop` 的过去式为 **developed**。

3. **答案：combines**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。陈述客观事实用一般现在时，主语 `A Peking Opera performance` 为第三人称单数，故填 **combines**。

4. **答案：faces**
   - **考点**：名词复数。形容词性物主代词 `their` 后接名词，主语 `The actors` 为复数，每个演员各有一张脸，故用复数 **faces**。

5. **答案：meaning**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。形容词 `special` 修饰名词，**mean** 的名词形式为 **meaning**（含义）。

6. **答案：Learning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词短语作句子主语，`learn` 变为 **Learning**，句首首字母大写。

7. **答案：skills**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`basic`（基本的）修饰名词，后面列举了多项技能，故用复数 **skills**。

8. **答案：confidently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `perform` 需用副词，**confident** 的副词形式为 **confidently**。

9. **答案：has faced / has been facing**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**In recent years**（近年来）提示现在完成时；主语 `Peking Opera` 为第三人称单数，故填 **has faced**（也可用现在完成进行时 **has been facing** 强调挑战仍在持续）。

10. **答案：so**
    - **考点**：连词。**so that...**（以便……）引导目的状语从句，表示"以便更多学生能了解这一文化瑰宝"，故填 **so**。
