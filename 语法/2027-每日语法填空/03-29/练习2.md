# 03-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：收藏爱好 (Collecting as a Hobby)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Collecting is a quiet hobby that can last a lifetime. Some people gather stamps, (1) ______ others prefer coins, tickets, or even candy wrappers from different countries.

My classmate Zhao Lin has collected over three hundred postcards since primary school. Each card shows a landmark or a work of art, and she writes the date of (2) ______ (receive) it on the back. Her bedroom wall looks like a small museum.

Collecting teaches patience and organization. You learn to compare prices, judge quality, and trade duplicates (3) ______ friends. Zhao Lin once spent three months searching online (4) ______ a limited edition card about the Silk Road.

Of course, hobbies should not harm our studies or family budget. Our teacher advises us (5) ______ (spend) wisely and to avoid buying fake goods. "A small but true collection is (6) ______ (value) than a large pile of copies," she said.

Last month, the school held an exhibition of student collections. Visitors walked slowly along the tables, (7) ______ (ask) questions and taking photos. I realized that every collection tells a story about its owner's interests.

When I grow up, I might start collecting old maps. For now, I enjoy listening to Zhao Lin's stories and imagining (8) ______ far her postcards have traveled.

If you want to begin a collection, choose something you truly love. Start small, stay curious, and you will find (9) ______ hobby can bring order and beauty to busy days.

Years later, when you open a dusty box, you may smile at the memories (10) ______ each object carries.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`some ... while others ...` 一些人……另一些人……。

2. **答案：receiving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。介词 `of` 后接动名词，`receive` → `receiving`。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`trade duplicates with friends` 与朋友交换重复品。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`search online for` 上网搜寻。

5. **答案：to spend**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`advise sb. to do sth.` 建议理性消费。

6. **答案：more valuable**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than a large pile` 提示。

7. **答案：asking**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。参观者边走边提问。

8. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句。`imagining how far ...` 想象明信片走了多远。

9. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。特指前面提到的收藏这一爱好。

10. **答案：that / which**
    - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `memories` 指物，从句缺宾语。
