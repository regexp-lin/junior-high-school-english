# 03-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：气象知识 (Weather Knowledge)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Understanding weather is not just for scientists. Farmers, pilots, and ordinary travelers all depend on forecasts (1) ______ (plan) their days.

Water vapor rises from oceans and lakes, forms clouds, and later falls as rain or snow. When warm air meets cold air, storms may develop quickly. Last summer, our camping trip was canceled (2) ______ there was a sudden typhoon warning.

Meteorologists use satellites, radar, and computer models (3) ______ (predict) changes. They measure wind speed, air pressure, and humidity. Still, weather can be (4) ______ (predict) than we wish — a sunny morning might turn into a rainy afternoon.

Climate change has made some patterns harder to read. In northern regions, winters have grown (5) ______ (mild) in recent decades, while heavy rain hits southern cities more often.

In science class, we built simple instruments: a bottle barometer and a wind vane. Our teacher asked us (6) ______ (record) temperature at the same time each day for two weeks. The line graph we drew looked like a heartbeat.

Knowing basic weather signs also helps outdoors. Dark clouds moving fast often mean rain is near; a red sky at sunset may promise fine weather (7) ______ the next morning.

If you plan a hike, check the forecast and carry a light raincoat even (8) ______ the sky looks clear. Safety always comes first.

I used to think weather talk was small talk, (9) ______ now I see it connects us to the whole planet.

The more I learn about clouds and winds, (10) ______ more curious I become about the sky above my head.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to plan**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`depend on forecasts to plan` 不定式表目的；或 `forecasts to plan` 作后置定语。

2. **答案：because**
   - **考点**：原因状语从句。`was canceled because there was ...` 因突发台风预警而取消露营。

3. **答案：to predict**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`use ... to predict` 用卫星和模型预测变化。

4. **答案：less predictable**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than we wish` 提示，天气不如我们期望的那样可预测。

5. **答案：milder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与过去几十年对比，冬季变得更温和，`mild` → `milder`。

6. **答案：to record**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`ask sb. to do sth.` 要求记录气温。

7. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`promise fine weather for the next morning` 预示次日早晨好天气。

8. **答案：if / though**
   - **考点**：连词。`even if` 即使看起来晴朗也要带雨衣；`even though` 尽管晴朗——此处 `even if` 更符合安全提示。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。曾以为天气话题是闲聊，但现在……

10. **答案：the**
    - **考点**：固定句型。`The more ..., the more ...` 越……越……。
