# 02-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统武术 (Traditional Martial Arts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese martial arts, commonly (1) ______ (know) as kung fu, have a history spanning thousands of years. There are hundreds of different styles, each with its own unique techniques and (2) ______ (philosopher). Some styles imitate the movements of animals, such as the Tiger, Crane and Monkey styles. My uncle has practised Wing Chun for over twenty years and is a certified (3) ______ (instruct) at a local martial arts school. When I was ten, he started teaching me the basics. At first, I thought kung fu was all about (4) ______ (fight) and cool moves like in the movies. However, my uncle quickly corrected this (5) ______ (misunderstand). He explained that real martial arts focus more on discipline, respect and self-improvement (6) ______ on combat. The training was much (7) ______ (tough) than I had imagined. We spent weeks just practising stances — standing in one position for several minutes without (8) ______ (move). My uncle said that a strong foundation is (9) ______ (extreme) important in martial arts, just as it is in life. Over the years, kung fu has taught me patience, perseverance and humility. It has also kept me physically fit. I am grateful (10) ______ my uncle introduced me to this wonderful tradition, and I hope to continue practising for the rest of my life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：known**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`commonly known as` 通常被称为。

2. **答案：philosophy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`techniques and philosophy` 技法和哲学，名词。

3. **答案：instructor**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a certified instructor` 认证教练，名词。

4. **答案：fighting**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `about` 后用动名词。

5. **答案：misunderstanding**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`this misunderstanding` 这个误解，名词。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。`more on... than on...` 更注重……而非……。

7. **答案：tougher**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much tougher than` 比……艰苦得多。

8. **答案：moving**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后用动名词。

9. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：副词修饰形容词。`extremely important` 极其重要。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：连词。`be grateful that...` 感激……，引导宾语从句。
