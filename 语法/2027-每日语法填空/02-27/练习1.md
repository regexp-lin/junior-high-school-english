# 02-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校图书馆 (School Library)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school library (1) ______ (renovate) last summer and now looks completely different. The old wooden shelves have been replaced with modern white ones, and (2) ______ (comfort) reading chairs have been added throughout the space. The library now holds over 50,000 books, covering subjects from science and history (3) ______ literature and art. There is also a digital section (4) ______ students can use computers to access online databases and e-books. The librarian, Ms. Zhou, is one of the (5) ______ (kind) people I have ever met. She always helps students find the books they need and often recommends new titles based on our (6) ______ (interested). Last month, she introduced a "Book of the Week" programme, in which a different book is displayed and reviewed every Monday. This has encouraged many students to explore genres they would not (7) ______ (normal) read. I personally discovered my love for detective fiction through one of her (8) ______ (recommend). The library has also become a popular place for group study. There are small meeting rooms that can be booked by students (9) ______ want to discuss projects or prepare for exams together. I spend at least two hours in the library every day after school. It has become my second home — a quiet place where I can focus on my work without (10) ______ (disturb).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was renovated**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。`last summer` 提示过去时，图书馆被翻新。

2. **答案：comfortable**
   - **考点**：形容词修饰名词。`comfortable reading chairs` 舒适的阅读椅。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`from... to...` 从……到……。

4. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，修饰 `section`，表地点。

5. **答案：kindest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the kindest people`。

6. **答案：interests**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`based on our interests` 基于我们的兴趣，名词复数。

7. **答案：normally**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`would not normally read` 通常不会读。

8. **答案：recommendations**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`one of her recommendations` 她推荐的其中一本。

9. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `students`，指人。

10. **答案：being disturbed / disturbance**
    - **考点**：动名词的被动或名词。`without being disturbed` 不被打扰；或 `without disturbance`。
