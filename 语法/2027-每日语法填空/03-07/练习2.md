# 03-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学做中国结 (Learning to Make Chinese Knots)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese knots are a beautiful form of traditional handicraft (1) ______ has a history of thousands of years. They are usually made of red cord and are often used as decorations during festivals, (2) ______ (especial) the Spring Festival.

Last Saturday, our school invited a local craftsman (3) ______ (teach) us how to make Chinese knots. When he showed us his finished works, we were all amazed at how (4) ______ (skill) he was. Some of his knots were shaped like butterflies, while (5) ______ looked like fish — a symbol of good fortune in Chinese culture.

The craftsman first taught us the (6) ______ (base) knot, which is called the "flat knot". It seemed simple, but it required great patience. I tried several (7) ______ (time) before I got it right. Some of my classmates found it even (8) ______ (difficult) and asked for extra help.

After an hour of practice, most of us (9) ______ (successful) completed a small Chinese knot. Although our works were far from perfect, we felt proud of (10) ______ (our). The experience gave us a deeper understanding of the beauty behind traditional Chinese art.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰 `handicraft`，在从句中作主语。

2. **答案：especially**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`especially` 副词，表示"尤其是"。

3. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：不定式。`invite sb. to do sth.` 邀请某人做某事。

4. **答案：skilful / skillful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`how skilful he was` 感叹句，`skill` → `skilful` 形容词。

5. **答案：others**
   - **考点**：代词。`Some ... while others ...` 一些……而另一些……。

6. **答案：basic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `knot`，`base` → `basic` 形容词。

7. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several times` 几次，可数名词复数。

8. **答案：more difficult**
   - **考点**：比较级。`even more difficult` 甚至更困难，`even` 修饰比较级。

9. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `completed`，需用副词 `successfully`。

10. **答案：ourselves**
    - **考点**：反身代词。`felt proud of ourselves` 为自己感到骄傲，主语 `we` 对应 `ourselves`。
