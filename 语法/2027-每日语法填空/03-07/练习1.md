# 03-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：共享单车 (Shared Bicycles)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Shared bicycles have become a common sight in Chinese cities. They provide a cheap and (1) ______ (environment) friendly way for people to travel short distances. Users simply scan a QR code on (2) ______ (they) phones to unlock a bike and start riding.

The idea of bike-sharing (3) ______ (introduce) to China around 2016. Within just two years, millions of colourful bikes appeared on the streets of major cities like Beijing, Shanghai and Guangzhou. Many people found (4) ______ very convenient to pick up a bike near a bus station or subway exit and ride it to their workplace.

However, problems also came along. Some users parked the bikes (5) ______ (proper), blocking pavements and causing trouble for pedestrians. Others damaged the bikes (6) ______ purpose. These bad behaviours made city management more difficult.

To solve these problems, the government (7) ______ (work) with bike-sharing companies to set up designated parking areas. Meanwhile, a credit system has been created to encourage users to follow the (8) ______ (rule). If someone parks a bike incorrectly, their credit score (9) ______ (go) down.

I think shared bicycles are a great invention. As long as everyone uses them (10) ______ (responsible), they will continue to make our lives easier and our cities greener.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：environmentally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `friendly`，需用副词形式 `environmentally`。

2. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 `phones`。

3. **答案：was introduced**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。`around 2016` 过去时间，idea 是"被引入的"。

4. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：形式宾语。`find it convenient to do sth.`，`it` 作形式宾语。

5. **答案：improperly**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 前缀。修饰动词 `parked`，且语境为"不当地"，`improper` → `improperly`。

6. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`on purpose` 故意地。

7. **答案：has worked / has been working**
   - **考点**：现在完成时 / 现在完成进行时。表示从过去到现在一直在合作。

8. **答案：rules**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`follow the rules` 遵守规则，用复数。

9. **答案：will go**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`If` 条件状语从句，主句用将来时。

10. **答案：responsibly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `uses`，需用副词 `responsibly`。
