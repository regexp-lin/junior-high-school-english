# 03-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：制作短视频 (Making Short Videos)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Short videos have changed the way teenagers spend their free time. With a phone and a simple app, almost anyone can become a director in minutes.

My sister started a channel about DIY crafts last year. At first, only a few friends watched her clips, (1) ______ her number of followers grew quickly after she posted a video on making paper flowers. Now she spends two hours every weekend (2) ______ (plan) new content.

Making a good video is harder than it looks. You need a clear topic, good lighting, and smooth editing. My sister often films the same scene several times (3) ______ she is satisfied with the result. She also adds soft music (4) ______ (make) the viewing experience more pleasant.

Some teachers worry that short videos may distract students from homework. (5) ______, when used wisely, they can also teach cooking, science, and languages in a lively way.

Last month, our class was asked (6) ______ (create) a two-minute video about traditional festivals. We divided the work: some wrote the script, (7) ______ others operated the camera. The final product was shown at the school art festival, and the audience gave us warm applause.

I have learned that creativity matters more than expensive equipment. If you have a story (8) ______ share, pick up your phone and try. You might be surprised (9) ______ how much you can express in sixty seconds.

Of course, we should protect our privacy and avoid spending too much time online. Balance is the key (10) ______ enjoying short videos without letting them control our lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。起初观看者少，但粉丝增长很快。

2. **答案：planning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`spend time (in) doing sth.` 花费时间做某事。

3. **答案：until / before**
   - **考点**：连词。`until she is satisfied` 直到满意为止；或 `before` 在满意之前多次拍摄。

4. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的，添加音乐以使体验更愉快。

5. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：转折副词。老师担忧，然而合理使用也有益处，句首大写。

6. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be asked to do sth.` 被要求做某事。

7. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`some ... while others ...` 一些人……另一些人……。

8. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：不定式。`have a story to share` 有故事要分享，不定式作后置定语。

9. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be surprised at` 对……感到惊讶。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：介词。`the key to doing sth.` ……的关键，to 为介词，后接动名词。
