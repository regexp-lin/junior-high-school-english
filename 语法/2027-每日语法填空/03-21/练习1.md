# 03-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：森林徒步 (Forest Hiking)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our outdoor club went hiking in Pine Forest Park. The weather was cool and sunny — (1) ______ perfect day for a long walk.

We met at the school gate at seven in the morning and took a bus (2) ______ the foot of the mountain. The guide reminded us (3) ______ (wear) hats and carry enough water. He also warned us not to leave the marked paths because some areas (4) ______ (be) dangerous after heavy rain.

The trail wound through tall trees. Birds sang above our heads, and we could smell pine needles (5) ______ the fresh air. Halfway up, we rested by a clear stream and shared sandwiches. I felt a little tired, (6) ______ the beautiful scenery gave me new energy.

At noon, we reached a wooden platform with a wide view of the valley. Everyone took photos and shouted with joy. Our teacher said hiking is not only exercise (7) ______ also a lesson in patience and teamwork.

On the way down, we picked up litter left by other visitors. "We should leave the forest (8) ______ (clean) than we found it," the guide said. I couldn't agree more.

By four o'clock, we were back on the bus, dirty but happy. That evening, I fell asleep (9) ______ soon as my head touched the pillow.

This trip made me love nature (10) ______ (deep) than before. I am already planning to join the next hike in May.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a perfect day` 泛指美好的一天。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`take a bus to ...` 乘车前往……。

3. **答案：to wear**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`remind sb. to do sth.` 提醒某人做某事。

4. **答案：were**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`some areas` 复数，叙述当时状况用 were。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in the fresh air` 在新鲜空气中。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。有点累，但风景给了新能量。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only ... but also ...` 不仅……而且……。

8. **答案：cleaner**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than we found it` 提示比较级，比发现时更干净。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`as soon as` 一……就……。

10. **答案：more deeply**
    - **考点**：副词比较级。`love ... more deeply than before` 更深地热爱，`than` 提示比较级。
