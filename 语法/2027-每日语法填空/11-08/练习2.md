# 11-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：理性消费 (Rational Spending)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Online shopping has made it very easy __1__ (buy) things. With just a few clicks, products arrive __2__ your door within days. However, many teenagers spend too __3__ money on unnecessary items. My friend Xiao Ming once bought five pairs of trainers __4__ he only needed one. He later admitted that he __5__ (influence) by online advertisements. Our class teacher often reminds us __6__ (be) rational consumers. She says we should always ask __7__ (we) three questions before buying: "Do I really need it?", "Can I afford it?" and "Is there a __8__ (cheap) alternative?" Following these simple rules can help us avoid __9__ (waste) money. I have started keeping a spending diary. Every time I buy something, I write down the price and the reason. This habit has helped me save a lot of money __10__ the past few months. Learning to spend wisely is an important life skill.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to buy**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It + be + 形容词 + to do sth.，it 为形式主语，用 to buy。

2. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。at your door 到你家门口，用 at 表示在某处。

3. **答案：much**
   - **考点**：形容词。too much + 不可数名词，money 为不可数名词，用 much。

4. **答案：although / though**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导让步状语从句，"虽然只需要一双"，用 although 或 though。

5. **答案：was influenced**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 he 与 influence 为被动关系，过去时态，用 was influenced。

6. **答案：to be**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。remind sb. to do sth. 提醒某人做某事，用不定式 to be。

7. **答案：ourselves**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。ask ourselves 问我们自己，we 的反身代词为 ourselves。

8. **答案：cheaper**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。暗含与目标商品相比更便宜的选择，用比较级 cheaper。

9. **答案：wasting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。avoid doing sth. 避免做某事，用动名词 wasting。

10. **答案：over / in**
    - **考点**：介词。over/in the past few months 在过去几个月里，与现在完成时连用。
