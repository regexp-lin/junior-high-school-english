# 11-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：科学纪录片 (Science Documentaries)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I have always enjoyed __1__ (watch) science documentaries. They teach me things __2__ I cannot learn from textbooks alone. Last month, I watched a series called "Planet Earth". The __3__ (photograph) was breathtaking — every frame looked like a painting. One episode showed how polar bears struggle __4__ (survive) as ice melts. Another episode introduced deep-sea creatures that live in complete __5__ (dark). Some of them can produce __6__ (they) own light! The narrator explained complex ideas in __7__ easy-to-understand way. After watching the series, I became much more __8__ (interest) in biology. I started reading science magazines and even signed up __9__ a nature observation club. My father says documentaries are the best way __10__ (explore) the world without leaving home. I agree. A good documentary can spark curiosity and change the way you see the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。enjoy doing sth. 喜欢做某事，用动名词 watching。

2. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 things 指物，从句缺宾语，用 that 或 which。

3. **答案：photography**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the photography was breathtaking 摄影令人叹为观止，photograph 的名词形式（指摄影术）为 photography。

4. **答案：to survive**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。struggle to do sth. 努力做某事，用不定式 to survive。

5. **答案：darkness**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。in complete darkness 在完全的黑暗中，dark 的名词形式为 darkness。

6. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 light，用形容词性物主代词 their。

7. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。easy 以元音音素 /iː/ 开头，泛指"一种容易理解的方式"，用 an。

8. **答案：interested**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。become interested in 对……感兴趣，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。sign up for 报名参加，为固定搭配。

10. **答案：to explore**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。the best way to do sth. 做某事的最好方式，不定式作后置定语。
