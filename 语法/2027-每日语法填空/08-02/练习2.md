# 08-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护濒危动物 (Protecting Endangered Animals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many people do not realize ___1___ serious the problem of endangered animals has become until they see shocking photos on the news. According to a recent report, the number of certain wild species ___2___ (fall) sharply over the past decade, and some populations may disappear forever if nothing changes. Their natural habitats ___3___ (destroy) when people cut down forests ___4___ build new roads without careful planning, leaving many creatures with nowhere safe to find food, raise their young, or escape from sudden storms.

Governments and scientists are working hard ___5___ (protect) these creatures, ___6___ local volunteers are also trying to spread awareness in schools and community centers. Last Friday, a guest speaker visited our class and ___7___ (share) moving stories about rescuing injured birds; she explained how plastic waste and noisy tourists can harm quiet wetlands. She asked us ___8___ (not pick) flowers in protected woods and to keep our voices down when we walk on nature trails. We should refuse ___9___ (buy) products made from rare animals and tell our friends why such trade is cruel and illegal.

If teenagers act responsibly, I am sure our small efforts today ___10___ (lead) to a safer world for wildlife tomorrow, because every choice we make sends a message about what we truly value.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句 / 感叹结构中的连接词。realize 后为宾语从句，从句为 “___ serious the problem … has become”，属于 “how + 形容词 + 主语 + 谓语” 结构，表示“多么严重”，用 how。

2. **答案：has fallen**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。时间状语 over the past decade 表示从过去持续到现在的时段，常与现在完成时连用；主语 the number of … 视为单数，谓语用 has fallen。

3. **答案：are destroyed / have been destroyed**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。habitats 与 destroy 为被动关系；说明客观现状用一般现在时被动 are destroyed；若强调对现在的影响，用现在完成时被动 have been destroyed 也可。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词 / 非谓语中的不定式符号。cut down forests to build … 中不定式表目的，意为“砍林是为了修路”，填 to。

5. **答案：to protect**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。work hard to do sth. 表示“努力去做某事”，用不定式作目的状语，故为 to protect。

6. **答案：and / while**
   - **考点**：连词。前半句讲政府与科学家的努力，后半句讲当地志愿者的工作，为并列或同时进行的补充，用 and 表并列，或用 while 表“与此同时”均可。

7. **答案：shared**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。时间状语 Last Friday 表明动作发生在过去，且与 visited 并列，用一般过去时 shared。

8. **答案：not to pick**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. not to do sth. 为固定结构，意为“要求某人不要做某事”，故用 not to pick。

9. **答案：to buy**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。refuse to do sth. 为固定搭配，意为“拒绝做某事”，故用 to buy。

10. **答案：will lead / can lead**
    - **考点**：动词的时态。主句 I am sure 后为宾语从句，从句中 tomorrow 提示将来结果，用一般将来时 will lead；若表示“能够带来”，用 can lead 也符合语境。
