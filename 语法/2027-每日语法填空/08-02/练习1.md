# 08-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：图书馆的一天 (A Day at the Library)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, I spent the whole morning at ___1___ city library that ___2___ (locate) only a short walk from my school. It opens at eight, and I like its tall shelves, the faint smell of paper, and the polite smiles at the front desk. I went there quite early ___3___ (prepare) for a group history project about ancient trade routes along the Silk Road, ___4___ our teacher had encouraged us ___5___ (use) printed books and academic articles instead of unreliable information from random websites on the Internet, which can waste a lot of time and even lead to mistakes in our report.

When I stepped into the quiet reading room on the second floor, I noticed that almost every seat ___6___ (take) already. ___7___ I still spotted a small wooden table near the window where the morning light fell softly on the desk. I settled down, unpacked my pencil case, and began ___8___ (list) useful dates and names while the librarian walked past ___9___ (noise) with a heavy cart full of returned books. In the corner, several senior students were reviewing for an upcoming exam, whispering questions to each other and marking pages with thin sticky notes. Around noon, my friend Lily arrived with a simple lunch, and we planned ___10___ (continue) our research together after we ate, hoping to finish the outline before the library closed at five.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。city library 在定语从句 “that … from my school” 的限定下，指双方语境中明确的那座市图书馆，表特指，故用定冠词 the。

2. **答案：was located**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。library 与 locate 之间为被动关系（图书馆“坐落于”某地）；叙述过去周六的经历，用一般过去时的被动语态 was located。

3. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。此处说明去图书馆的目的，用动词不定式作目的状语，即 went there to prepare for…。

4. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：连词。后文“老师鼓励我们用纸质书……”是前文“早早去图书馆”的原因，表原因状语从句，用 because、as 或 since 均可。

5. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。encourage sb. to do sth. 为固定结构，意为“鼓励某人做某事”，故用不定式 to use。

6. **答案：had been taken / was taken**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。seat 与 take 之间为被动关系（座位被占）；若强调“在我到之前已被占”，用过去完成时被动 had been taken 更贴切；用一般过去时被动 was taken 表示当时状态，中考中通常也可接受。

7. **答案：But / Yet**
   - **考点**：连词。上文“几乎没空位”与下文“仍找到一张小桌”为转折关系，用 But 或 Yet（书面语）连接。

8. **答案：to list / listing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。begin to do sth. 与 begin doing sth. 均可表示“开始做某事”，故 to list 或 listing 皆可。

9. **答案：noisily**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 walked，需用副词；noise 的副词形式为 noisily。

10. **答案：to continue**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。plan to do sth. 为固定搭配，意为“计划做某事”，用不定式 to continue。
