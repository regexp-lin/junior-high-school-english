# 05-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：街头表演 (Street Performance)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

On weekend evenings, a small square near my home becomes lively when street performers arrive 1. ______ their guitars and drums. Last Saturday, a singer stood 2. ______ the fountain and sang folk songs while people sat on benches. A girl danced 3. ______ (graceful) beside him, and the crowd clapped in rhythm. Someone placed coins in 4. ______ open guitar case as a thank-you. I talked with a young man 5. ______ painted faces for children. He said he did not care about money much; he just enjoyed 6. ______ (see) strangers smile. "The street is 7. ______ stage for everyone," he added. His words reminded me 8. ______ art can be close to daily life. Before we left, my little brother bought 9. ______ balloon animal and waved 10. ______ (happy) at the performers.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**with their guitars and drums** 表示“带着吉他鼓”，**with** 表伴随，故填 **with**。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。**by the fountain** 表示“在喷泉旁边”，故填 **by**。

3. **答案：gracefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **danced**，用副词 **gracefully**。

4. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**open** 以元音音素开头，**an open guitar case**，故填 **an**。

5. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词 **a young man**（人），定语从句中作主语，故填 **who** 或 **that**。

6. **答案：seeing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**enjoy doing sth.**，故填 **seeing**。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **stage** 单数，表泛指“一个舞台”，故填 **a**。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**remind me** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a balloon animal**，故填 **a**。

10. **答案：happily**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **waved**，用副词 **happily**。
