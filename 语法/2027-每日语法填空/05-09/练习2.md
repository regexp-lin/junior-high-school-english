# 05-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：创意写作 (Creative Writing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Creative writing is not only about grammar; it is about imagination. In our writing club, the teacher gives us 1. ______ unusual picture each week and asks us 2. ______ (build) a story around it. At first, I worried that my ideas were not good 3. ______ . Later I learned 4. ______ first draft can be messy. We read 5. ______ (we) work aloud, then give suggestions 6. ______ each other. Last month, I wrote about 7. ______ robot that learns to feel lonely. My classmates said the ending was 8. ______ (surprise) and touching. The teacher encouraged me 9. ______ (send) the story to a youth magazine. I am still waiting for 10. ______ reply, but I already feel proud of finishing it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **picture** 单数，**unusual** 以元音音素开头，故填 **an**。

2. **答案：to build**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**ask sb. to do sth.**，故填 **to build**。

3. **答案：enough**
   - **考点**：副词。**good enough** 表示“足够好”，**enough** 修饰形容词后置，故填 **enough**。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a first draft** 表示“初稿”，故填 **a**。

5. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 **work**，用形容词性物主代词 **our**。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**give suggestions to each other**，故填 **to**。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a robot**，故填 **a**。

8. **答案：surprising**
   - **考点**：词性转换。描述事物特征“令人惊讶的”，用形容词 **surprising**。

9. **答案：to send**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**encourage sb. to do sth.**，故填 **to send**。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。**wait for a reply**，故填 **a**。
