# 07-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：太空探索 (Space Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, space exploration ___1___ (become) more important than ever before. Many countries are ___2___ (work) together to send astronauts to the Moon again. Scientists believe ___3___ we will be able to build a research station that ___4___ (operate) on the lunar surface for many years. Engineers are testing new rockets that are also ___5___ (design) to carry more people and heavy equipment into orbit safely.

The journey to space is never easy. Astronauts must train for years before they ___6___ (allow) to fly into the unknown. During their training, they learn how ___7___ (use) special tools in the laboratory and how to stay calm when gravity ___8___ (be) almost zero in a simulator. ___9___ astronaut who leaves Earth needs great courage, patience, and a strong sense of duty.

Some people wonder ___10___ the cost is so high. In fact, space science helps us solve problems on Earth and understand nature more deeply than before. International cooperation has already brought valuable data about climate change and better communication technology for everyday life. Many schools now organize lively science clubs and invite engineers to talk about their real work. Young students often say that they hope to join space teams someday and make discoveries that change our world for the better.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。时间状语 *in recent years*（近年来）表示从过去持续到现在的变化，主语 *space exploration* 为第三人称单数，故用 **has become**，表示“已经变得”。

2. **答案：working**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在进行时）。句中已有 be 动词 *are*，与 *many countries* 构成“许多国家正在……”，需用现在分词 **working**，表示现阶段正在进行的合作。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词（宾语从句引导词）。*believe* 后接宾语从句，从句 *we will be able to build...* 结构完整、陈述事实，用 **that** 引导；口语中 *that* 有时可省略，但语法填空通常要求填 **that**。

4. **答案：will operate**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般将来时）。定语从句修饰 *a research station*，与主句中 *will be able to build* 的将来计划一致，表示“将在月球表面运行多年”，故用 **will operate**。若试题强调客观描述“站点的功能”，少数语境下也可用 **operates**，但本篇以将来规划为主，**will operate** 最佳。

5. **答案：designed**
   - **考点**：动词语态（一般现在时的被动语态）。*rockets* 与 *design* 之间是被动关系（火箭被设计），结构为 *are + 过去分词*，故填 **designed**。

6. **答案：are allowed**
   - **考点**：动词语态（一般现在时的被动语态）。*they*（指宇航员）与 *allow* 是“被允许”，且描述常规规定，用一般现在时被动 **are allowed**；*before they are allowed to fly* 表示“在获准飞行之前”。

7. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。*how to do sth.* 为固定结构，意为“如何做某事”，故在 *how* 后填 **to use**，与后文 *how to stay calm* 并列。

8. **答案：is**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。*gravity* 为不可数名词，作主语时谓语用单数；此处描述在模拟器中的常态情况，用一般现在时 **is**。

9. **答案：Every / Each**
   - **考点**：限定词/代词。*astronaut* 为可数名词单数，句意为“每一位/每一位离开地球的宇航员都需要……”，填 **Every** 或 **Each** 均可表示整体中的个体（*Every* 更常见于初中教材）。

10. **答案：why**
    - **考点**：连接副词（宾语从句）。*wonder* 后接疑问含义的从句，“想知道为什么花费这么高”，用 **why** 引导宾语从句 *why the cost is so high*。
