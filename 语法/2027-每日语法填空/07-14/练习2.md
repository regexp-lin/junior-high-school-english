# 07-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学做饭 (Learning to Cook)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, my cousin Lily decided ___1___ (learn) to cook because she will live alone at university soon. At first, she ___2___ (use) a knife carelessly and cut ___3___ finger while she ___4___ (prepare) vegetables for the first time. She felt embarrassed, but she did not want to give up.

Her mother showed ___5___ how to work safely in the kitchen. She asked Lily to read each instruction ___6___ (careful) before turning on the stove or touching hot pans. After a few weeks of practice, Lily could cook simple dishes such as eggs and noodles ___7___ (good) than before, though she still needed help with difficult recipes.

One Sunday, she wanted ___8___ (make) a whole meal by herself to surprise her parents. She followed the recipe step by step, but she nearly ___9___ (burn) the rice because she was busy chatting on her phone in another room. The smell in the kitchen reminded her that cooking needs attention as well as skill.

That small accident taught ___10___ an important lesson: when you cook, you should stay focused and patient. Now Lily often practices on weekends and enjoys sharing tasty food with her family. She has even started keeping a small notebook of useful tips and simple recipes that she can repeat without help. She says learning to cook makes her feel readier for independent life away from home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。*decide to do sth.* 为固定搭配，意为“决定做某事”，故在 *decided* 后填 **to learn**。

2. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。全文叙述 *last month* 开始的一段经历，此处描述过去发生的具体行为“她当时用刀很粗心”，用 **use** 的过去式 **used**。

3. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。*finger* 为名词，前面需用物主代词表示“她的手指”，主语为 *she*，故填 **her**。

4. **答案：was preparing**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去进行时）。*while* 引导时间状语从句，强调“切到手指”与“正在备菜”同时发生，主语 *she* 为第三人称单数，故用 **was preparing**。

5. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。*showed* 为及物动词，后接间接宾语，指“妈妈向她展示”，故填宾格 **her**。

6. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词变副词）。修饰动词 *read* 要用副词，括号内 *careful* 的副词形式为 **carefully**，表示“仔细地阅读每一步说明”。

7. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有比较连词 *than*，且 *good* 的比较级为不规则变化 **better**，表示“比以前做得更好”。

8. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。*want to do sth.* 为固定搭配，意为“想要做某事”，故填 **to make**。

9. **答案：burned / burnt**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。*nearly* 后接谓语动词，叙述过去某个周日发生的事，*burn* 的过去式为 **burned** 或 **burnt**（英式常用 *burnt*，美式常用 *burned*，中考一般两种均可）。

10. **答案：her**
    - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。*taught* 后接双宾语结构 *teach sb. sth.*，此处指“这件事教会了她一个道理”，间接宾语用宾格 **her**，指 Lily。
