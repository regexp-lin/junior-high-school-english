# 08-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：制作风筝 (Making Kites)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Kite-making is a traditional Chinese craft that ___1___ (date) back over two thousand years. It is said that the first kite ___2___ (make) of wood by a Chinese philosopher named Mozi. Later, people began to use bamboo and paper, which were much ___3___ (light) and easier to fly.

Last summer, I joined a kite-making workshop in our community centre. The ___4___ (instruct) was an old craftsman named Uncle Wang, who had been making kites ___5___ he was a teenager. He showed us how to build a simple diamond-shaped kite step ___6___ step.

First, we tied two bamboo sticks together to form a cross. Then we stretched a piece of coloured paper over the frame and glued it ___7___ (careful). The most creative part was ___8___ (paint) the kite. I drew a big red fish on mine because fish are symbols of good luck in Chinese culture.

When we finished, Uncle Wang took us to a nearby field ___9___ (test) our kites. The wind was just right, and my fish kite flew ___10___ (high) than most of the others. I felt so proud of what I had made with my own hands.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：dates**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。**date back**（追溯到）描述客观事实用一般现在时；定语从句中的主语 `that` 指代 `craft`（单数），故谓语用第三人称单数 **dates**。

2. **答案：was made**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `the first kite` 与 `make` 之间为被动关系（风筝"被制成"），叙述历史传说用一般过去时被动语态，故填 **was made**。

3. **答案：lighter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**much + 比较级** 表示"……得多"，`light` 的比较级为 **lighter**。

4. **答案：instructor**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。定冠词 `The` 后接名词，**instruct** 的名词形式（指人）为 **instructor**（教导者/老师）。

5. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。**had been making...** 为过去完成进行时，表示从过去某时一直持续到另一过去时间点的动作，`since` 引导时间状语从句表示"自从"。

6. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。**step by step**（一步一步地）为固定搭配，故填 **by**。

7. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `glued` 需用副词，**careful** 的副词形式为 **carefully**。

8. **答案：painting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作表语）。系动词 `was` 后接动名词作表语，说明主语 `The most creative part` 的具体内容，故填 **painting**。

9. **答案：to test**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式 **to test** 作目的状语，表示"去测试我们的风筝"。

10. **答案：higher**
    - **考点**：副词比较级。**than** 提示比较关系，修饰动词 `flew` 需用副词比较级，`high` 的比较级为 **higher**。
