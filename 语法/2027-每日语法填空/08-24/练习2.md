# 08-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：噪音污染 (Noise Pollution)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When we talk about pollution, most people think of dirty air or water. However, noise pollution is also a serious problem that ___1___ (affect) millions of people's daily lives. It comes from many sources, ___2___ (include) traffic, construction sites, factories and even loud music.

Studies have shown that long-term exposure to loud noise can cause ___3___ (vary) health problems. It may lead to hearing loss, high blood pressure and difficulty ___4___ (sleep). Children who live in noisy areas tend to perform ___5___ (poor) in school than those in quiet neighbourhoods, because noise makes it hard for them ___6___ (concentrate) on their studies.

In our city, the government has taken several ___7___ (step) to reduce noise pollution. For example, heavy trucks are not allowed ___8___ (enter) the city centre during the daytime. Construction work must stop ___9___ ten o'clock at night. Green belts with thick trees have also been planted along major roads to absorb sound.

As individuals, we can also protect ___10___ (we) from noise pollution. Wearing earplugs in noisy environments, keeping the volume low when listening to music, and choosing quieter places to study are all good practices.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：affects**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。陈述客观事实用一般现在时；定语从句中 `that` 指代 `a serious problem`（单数），故谓语用第三人称单数 **affects**。

2. **答案：including**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词/介词用法。**including**（包括）在此作介词，后接具体的噪音来源。句中已有谓语 `comes`，故用 **including** 补充说明。

3. **答案：various**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。修饰名词 `health problems` 需用形容词，**vary** 的形容词形式为 **various**（各种各样的）。

4. **答案：sleeping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**have difficulty (in) doing sth.**（做某事有困难），故填 **sleeping**。

5. **答案：more poorly**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。**than** 提示比较关系，修饰动词 `perform` 需用副词比较级，`poorly` 的比较级为 **more poorly**。

6. **答案：to concentrate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**it is hard for sb. to do sth.**（做某事对某人来说很难），`it` 为形式主语，不定式为真正主语，故填 **to concentrate**。

7. **答案：steps**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several`（几个）后接可数名词复数，`step` 的复数形式为 **steps**。**take steps to do sth.**（采取措施做某事）。

8. **答案：to enter**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be (not) allowed to do sth.**（被允许/不被允许做某事），故填 **to enter**。

9. **答案：after / at**
   - **考点**：介词。**stop after ten o'clock**（十点之后停止）表示必须在晚上十点后停工，故填 **after**；也可填 **at** 表示"在十点钟停止"。

10. **答案：ourselves**
    - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。主语 `we` 与宾语指同一群人，"保护我们自己"需用反身代词 **ourselves**。
