# 08-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：海洋探索 (Ocean Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ocean covers more than 70 percent of ___1___ earth's surface, yet we know very little about what lies beneath the waves. Scientists say that we ___2___ (explore) less than five percent of the ocean floor so far.

In recent years, deep-sea technology ___3___ (develop) rapidly. Researchers now use underwater robots ___4___ (collect) data from the deepest parts of the ocean. These machines can reach places ___5___ are too dangerous for humans to go. Last year, a team of scientists discovered several new ___6___ (species) of fish near an underwater volcano. The fish had unusual shapes and colours, which ___7___ (complete) surprised the research team.

However, ocean exploration is not just about finding new creatures. It also helps us understand climate change ___8___ (well) than before. The ocean absorbs a large amount of heat and carbon dioxide from the atmosphere, so ___9___ (study) the ocean is important for predicting future weather patterns.

I hope that one day I can become ___10___ ocean scientist and explore the mysteries hidden in the deep blue sea.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。**the earth** 为专有名词，表示"地球"时通常加定冠词 **the**；此处 `the earth's surface` 指"地球的表面"。

2. **答案：have explored**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间标志词 **so far**（到目前为止）提示使用现在完成时，主语 `we` 为复数，故填 **have explored**。

3. **答案：has developed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 **In recent years**（近年来）提示使用现在完成时，主语 `deep-sea technology` 为第三人称单数，故填 **has developed**。

4. **答案：to collect**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**use sth. to do sth.**（使用某物来做某事），不定式 **to collect** 表示目的。

5. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `places` 指地点但在从句中作主语（不是作地点状语），故用关系代词 **that** 或 **which**（不用 where）。

6. **答案：species**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。**species** 为单复数同形的名词，前面有 `several new` 修饰，表示复数含义，形式仍为 **species**。

7. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 **surprised** 需用副词，**complete** 的副词形式为 **completely**，表示"完全地"。

8. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。**than before** 明确提示比较关系，修饰动词 **understand** 需用副词比较级，**well** 的比较级为 **better**。

9. **答案：studying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。空格处位于句首作主语，动词 `study` 需变为动名词 **studying**，构成"研究海洋是……"的主语。

10. **答案：an**
    - **考点**：冠词。`ocean scientist` 中 `ocean` 以元音音素开头，表示"一名海洋科学家"需用不定冠词 **an**。
