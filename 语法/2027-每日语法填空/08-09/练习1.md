# 08-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑假读书计划 (Summer Reading Plan)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This summer holiday, my teacher asked each of us ___1___ (make) a reading plan. She said that reading could open a door ___2___ a wider world. I chose three books from the school library, and one of ___3___ (they) was a novel about adventure.

At first, I found it ___4___ (difficulty) to finish 30 pages a day because some words were new to me. However, after looking them up in the dictionary, I ___5___ (gradual) understood the story better. The main character in the novel was a young boy ___6___ dreamed of sailing across the ocean. He faced many challenges, but he never gave up. His courage touched me ___7___ (deep).

By the end of July, I had already finished ___8___ (read) all three books. I wrote a short review for each book and shared it with my classmates online. Many of them said my reviews were ___9___ (help) and encouraged them to read more.

Now I truly believe that books are our best ___10___ (friend). They teach us things that we cannot learn in the classroom alone.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定结构 **ask sb. to do sth.**（请求某人做某事），故填不定式 **to make**。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**a door to...**（通往……的门）为固定搭配，表示"通向更广阔世界的一扇门"，故填 **to**。

3. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词。**one of + 复数名词/宾格代词**（……之一），`they` 在介词 `of` 后应用宾格形式 **them**。

4. **答案：difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。句型 **find it + adj. + to do sth.**（发现做某事……），`it` 为形式宾语，真正宾语是后面的不定式，空格处需填形容词 **difficult** 作宾补。

5. **答案：gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 **understood** 需用副词，**gradual** 的副词形式为 **gradually**，表示"逐渐地"。

6. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `a young boy` 指人，在从句中作主语，故用关系代词 **who** 或 **that**。

7. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 **touched** 需用副词，**deep** 的副词形式 **deeply** 表示"深深地（在情感层面）"。注意 deep 也可作副词但多指物理深度。

8. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定搭配 **finish doing sth.**（完成做某事），`finish` 后接动名词，故填 **reading**。

9. **答案：helpful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。系动词 `were` 后接形容词作表语，**help** 的形容词形式为 **helpful**，表示"有帮助的"。

10. **答案：friends**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`books` 是复数概念，与之对应的 `friend` 也应用复数形式 **friends**，表示"书是我们最好的朋友们"。
