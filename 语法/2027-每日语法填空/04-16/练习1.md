# 04-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：戏曲进校园 (Opera in Schools)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, our school invited a well-known Beijing opera troupe (1) ______ (perform) in the school hall. It was (2) ______ first time that many of us had watched traditional opera live on stage. The actors wore colorful masks (3) ______ moved gracefully to the ancient music.

The show was organized (4) ______ part of a project (5) ______ (aim) at helping teenagers understand local culture. "Chinese opera can be much (6) ______ (interest) than many people expect," the headmaster said. While the artists were performing classic scenes, students in the front rows (7) ______ (invite) to try simple hand movements.

Afterwards, a leading actor shared stories about his training and (8) ______ (patient) answered every question. Our music teacher has promised (9) ______ (set) up a small opera club next term. I used to find opera difficult to understand, (10) ______ this lively experience opened my eyes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to perform**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`invite sb. to do sth.` 邀请某人做某事，此处为不定式作宾语补足语，表示剧团“来校演出”。

2. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。`It was the first time that...` 为固定句型，序数词 `first` 前用定冠词 `the`。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`wore colorful masks` 与 `moved gracefully` 为并列谓语，表顺承，用 `and` 连接。

4. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`as part of...` 意为“作为……的一部分”，说明演出在学校项目中的定位。

5. **答案：aimed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`a project aimed at helping...` 为过去分词短语作后置定语，表“旨在……的项目”，`be aimed at` 的省略形式。

6. **答案：more interesting**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`much` 修饰比较级；`interest` → `interesting`，多音节形容词比较级前加 `more`。

7. **答案：were invited**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。主语 `students` 与 `invite` 为被动关系，且叙述过去活动，用 `were invited`。

8. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `answered`，`patient` → `patiently` 副词。

9. **答案：to set**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`promise to do sth.` 承诺做某事，不定式作宾语。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。前句“曾觉得难懂”与后句“这次经历让我改观”为转折关系，用 `but`。
