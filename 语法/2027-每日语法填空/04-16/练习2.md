# 04-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：海外研学 (Overseas Study Tour)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This summer, thirty students from our grade will go on (1) ______ overseas study tour to Britain. The two-week program (2) ______ (design) for those who want to improve their English and learn about local history. We will stay with host families (3) ______ attend classes at a partner school every morning.

Before we leave, we (4) ______ (ask) to read several books about British culture. Our teachers believe that reading widely will make the trip (5) ______ (meaning). During the tour, each student (6) ______ (keep) a diary in English and share photos with classmates online.

"I have never traveled abroad by (7) ______ before," said Li Ming. "I am a little nervous, (8) ______ I am also excited." Students are required (9) ______ (follow) safety rules during the whole journey. We hope this experience (10) ______ (help) us become more independent and confident.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`overseas` 以元音音素开头，`go on an overseas study tour` 一次海外研学，用不定冠词 `an`。

2. **答案：is designed**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。主语 `The program` 与 `design` 为被动关系，陈述客观安排，用 `is designed`。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`stay with host families` 与 `attend classes` 为并列动作，用 `and` 连接。

4. **答案：have been asked**
   - **考点**：现在完成时被动语态。强调“出发前已被要求读完”，动作发生在过去但对现在有影响，主语 `we` 与 `ask` 被动，用 `have been asked`。

5. **答案：meaningful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`make + 宾语 + 形容词` 使……如何；`meaning` → `meaningful` 有意义的。

6. **答案：will keep**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。叙述研学期间将要做的事情，与上文 `During the tour` 呼应，用 `will keep`。

7. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词。`by oneself` 独自；引语主语为 `I`，用反身代词 `myself`。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“有点紧张”与“也很兴奋”为转折。

9. **答案：to follow**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be required to do sth.` 被要求做某事，不定式作主语补足语。

10. **答案：will help**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。`hope` 后宾语从句叙述将来结果，“希望这次经历将会帮助我们”，用 `will help`。
