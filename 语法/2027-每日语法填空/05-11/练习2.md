# 05-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：公共图书馆 (Public Libraries)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A public library is 1. ______ quiet place where anyone can read for free. In our city, the new library opens 2. ______ 9 a.m. to 8 p.m. on weekdays. On the ground floor, there is 3. ______ café where people can drink tea while reading magazines. Upstairs, students borrow textbooks 4. ______ prepare for exams. Last month, I joined 5. ______ reading club and discussed a science book with strangers 6. ______ became friends later. A librarian showed me 7. ______ to search e-books on the computer. She said libraries are changing, 8. ______ their purpose stays the same: to open minds. If you return books 9. ______ time, you will not pay fines. I believe 10. ______ a library card is a key to many worlds.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **place** 单数，表泛指，故填 **a**。

2. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。**from...to...** 表示“从……到……”，故填 **from**。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a café**，故填 **a**。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的“为了备考”，故填 **to**。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a reading club**，故填 **a**。

6. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词 **strangers**（人），定语从句中作主语，故填 **who** 或 **that**。

7. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。**how to search** 表示“如何检索”，故填 **how**。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“图书馆在变”与“宗旨不变”为转折关系，故填 **but**。

9. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。**on time** 表示“按时”，故填 **on**。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：连接词。**believe** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。
