# 05-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：汉服文化 (Hanfu Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Hanfu, traditional Chinese clothing, has 1. ______ (become) popular among teenagers in recent years. On Culture Day, our school encouraged students 2. ______ (wear) hanfu to class. I chose 3. ______ light blue robe with wide sleeves and tied my hair with 4. ______ ribbon. At first, I felt shy walking in the hall, 5. ______ my friends smiled and gave me thumbs-up. During the history lesson, the teacher explained 6. ______ different designs showed social roles in ancient times. We also learned 7. ______ to fold the cloth carefully after taking photos. After school, a club member showed us 8. ______ old painting of ladies in hanfu. I realized hanfu is not only clothing; it is 9. ______ bridge between the past and the present. Young people are proud 10. ______ (share) this culture with the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**has become** 为现在完成时，**become** 的过去分词仍为 **become**。

2. **答案：to wear**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**encourage sb. to do sth.**，故填 **to wear**。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **robe** 单数，表泛指，故填 **a**。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a ribbon**，故填 **a**。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前后为转折关系，故填 **but**。

6. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**explained** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

7. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。**how to fold** 表示“如何折叠”，故填 **how**。

8. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**old** 以元音音素开头，**an old painting**，故填 **an**。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a bridge**，故填 **a**。

10. **答案：to share**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be proud to do sth.**，故填 **to share**。
