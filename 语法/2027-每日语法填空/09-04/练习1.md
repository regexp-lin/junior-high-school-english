# 09-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园消防演练 (School Fire Drill)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Tuesday, our school held a fire drill to make sure all students and teachers knew what to do in case of ___1___ emergency. The drill was organised by the school safety office ___2___ helped by the local fire department.

At ten o'clock, the fire alarm suddenly went ___3___. Some students were frightened at first, but the teachers quickly told everyone ___4___ (stay) calm. We were reminded to cover our noses and mouths with wet cloths and to walk in a line towards the ___5___ (near) exit. Running was strictly ___6___ (forbid) because it could cause panic and accidents.

Within five minutes, all 2,000 students had gathered ___7___ (safe) on the playground. The headmaster praised our ___8___ (perform) and said it was much faster than the drill we did last year. He also pointed out that a few classes had not followed the correct route, ___9___ could be dangerous in a real fire.

After the drill, two firefighters gave us a talk about fire ___10___ (prevent). They showed us how to use a fire extinguisher and reminded us never to play with matches or leave electrical devices on overnight. I learned a lot from this experience.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。**in case of an emergency**（万一发生紧急情况），`emergency` 以元音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **an**。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`organised by... ___ helped by...` 为两个并列的被动结构，用 **and** 连接。

3. **答案：off**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。**go off**（响起，发出声响）为固定搭配，指警报器响起，故填 **off**。

4. **答案：to stay**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**tell sb. to do sth.**（告诉某人做某事），故填 **to stay**。

5. **答案：nearest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**the + 最高级** 表示"最近的出口"，`near` 的最高级为 **nearest**。

6. **答案：forbidden**
   - **考点**：动词语态。`was` 后接过去分词构成被动语态；**forbid** 的过去分词为不规则变化 **forbidden**，表示"跑步是严格禁止的"。

7. **答案：safely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `had gathered` 需用副词，**safe** 的副词形式为 **safely**。

8. **答案：performance**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。形容词性物主代词 `our` 后接名词，**perform** 的名词形式为 **performance**（表现）。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。前面整句"几个班没有按照正确路线"作先行内容，逗号后为非限制性定语从句，用 **which** 指代前面的整个情况。

10. **答案：prevention**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。`fire prevention`（防火），名词 `fire` 修饰另一个名词，**prevent** 的名词形式为 **prevention**。
