# 09-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国陶瓷 (Chinese Ceramics)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has a long and proud tradition of making ceramics. In fact, the English word "china" ___1___ (it) is another name for porcelain, which shows just how closely Chinese culture is linked ___2___ this ancient craft.

The earliest Chinese pottery dates back to around 20,000 years ago. However, it was during the Tang and Song ___3___ (dynasty) that Chinese ceramics truly reached their peak. Potters in places like Jingdezhen, Jiangxi Province, developed techniques for creating fine porcelain that was both beautiful ___4___ durable. Their works ___5___ (export) to countries all over the world through the Silk Road and maritime trade routes.

One of the most ___6___ (value) types of Chinese ceramics is blue-and-white porcelain. These pieces feature delicate blue ___7___ (paint) on a white background. The patterns often include flowers, dragons and landscapes. Today, antique blue-and-white porcelain can sell for ___8___ (million) of dollars at international auctions.

Making traditional ceramics requires great patience and skill. First, the clay must ___9___ (shape) on a potter's wheel. Then it is dried, painted and fired in a kiln at very high temperatures. The whole process can take several weeks ___10___ (complete).

Despite the rise of modern manufacturing, traditional Chinese ceramics remain popular worldwide as works of art.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。`the English word "china" itself`（"china"这个英文单词本身），反身代词 **itself** 用于强调。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**be linked to...**（与……联系在一起）为固定搭配，故填 **to**。

3. **答案：dynasties**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the Tang and Song`（唐朝和宋朝）提到两个朝代，`dynasty` 的复数形式为 **dynasties**。

4. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。**both... and...**（既……又……）为固定结构，表示"既美观又耐用"。

5. **答案：were exported**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `Their works` 与 `export` 之间为被动关系（作品"被出口"），叙述历史事实用一般过去时被动语态，故填 **were exported**。

6. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。**the most + 多音节形容词** 构成最高级，**value** 的形容词形式为 **valuable**（有价值的）。

7. **答案：paintings**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）+ 名词复数。形容词 `delicate blue` 修饰名词，**paint** 的名词形式为 **painting**（绘画/图案），此处指多处图案，用复数 **paintings**。

8. **答案：millions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**millions of...**（数百万的……）为固定搭配，表示大量金额，当 million 与 of 搭配时需加 **s**。

9. **答案：be shaped**
   - **考点**：动词语态。情态动词 `must` 后接动词原形；主语 `the clay` 与 `shape` 之间为被动关系（陶土"被塑形"），故填 **be shaped**。

10. **答案：to complete**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**take + 时间 + to do sth.**（花……时间做某事），不定式 **to complete** 表示"完成"整个过程。
