# 06-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：3D 打印技术 (3D Printing Technology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

3D printing is one of the most ___1___ (excite) technologies of our time. Unlike traditional manufacturing, which often ___2___ (require) expensive moulds, 3D printers can create objects layer ___3___ layer using plastic, metal or even food materials. This technology has been ___4___ (wide) used in many fields. In medicine, doctors have ___5___ (successful) printed artificial bones to help patients recover from injuries. Architects use 3D models ___6___ (show) clients what a building will look like before it ___7___ (build). Some companies have even started printing houses, ___8___ can be completed in just a few days. At our school's science fair, my team printed a small robot hand that could open and close its ___9___ (finger). It took us two weeks to design and only four hours to print. My teacher said 3D printing would change the way we make things ___10___ the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：exciting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the most + 形容词构成最高级，修饰 technologies（物），用 exciting。

2. **答案：requires**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。which 指代 traditional manufacturing（单数），陈述一般事实用一般现在时，require → requires。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。layer by layer 表示"一层一层地"，固定搭配，填 by。

4. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 used，需用副词形式，wide → widely。

5. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 printed，需用副词形式，successful → successfully。

6. **答案：to show**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"用3D模型来展示"，填 to show。

7. **答案：is built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。it 与 build 之间为被动关系，before 引导的时间状语从句用一般现在时表将来，填 is built。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，先行词为 houses，用 which 引导。

9. **答案：fingers**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。its fingers 表示"它的手指"（机器人手有多个手指），finger → fingers。

10. **答案：in**
    - **考点**：介词。in the future 表示"在未来"，固定搭配，填 in。
