# 06-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：动物园之行 (A Visit to the Zoo)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our family went to the Guangzhou Zoo. It was a sunny day, perfect ___1___ an outing. We arrived early, but the zoo ___2___ (be) already full of visitors. The first animals we saw were the elephants. ___3___ (they) huge ears flapped slowly in the warm breeze. My little sister was especially attracted ___4___ the panda house. She pressed her face against the glass, ___5___ (watch) two baby pandas rolling around playfully. We also visited the bird park, ___6___ hundreds of colourful birds flew freely above our heads. A tour guide explained that many of the animals ___7___ (rescue) from the wild and could not survive on their own. The zoo provides them ___8___ a safe home and proper medical care. I used to think zoos only existed for ___9___ (entertain), but now I understand they play an important role in animal ___10___ (protect).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。perfect for 表示"适合"，填 for。

2. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。the zoo 为单数主语，叙述过去事件用一般过去时，be → was。

3. **答案：Their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 huge ears，用形容词性物主代词，they → Their，句首大写。

4. **答案：to/by**
   - **考点**：介词。be attracted to/by 表示"被……吸引"，填 to 或 by。

5. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，pressed her face..., watching...，填 watching。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 the bird park（地点），在从句中作地点状语，用 where。

7. **答案：had been rescued**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。animals 与 rescue 之间为被动关系，explained 为过去时，rescue 发生在 explain 之前，用过去完成时的被动语态 had been rescued。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。provide sb./sth. with 表示"为……提供"，固定搭配，填 with。

9. **答案：entertainment**
   - **考点**：词性转换。for 后接名词，entertain → entertainment。

10. **答案：protection**
    - **考点**：词性转换。animal protection 为名词短语，表示"动物保护"，protect → protection。
