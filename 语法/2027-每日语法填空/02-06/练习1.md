# 02-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园音乐节 (School Music Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every December, our school (1) ______ (organize) a music festival to celebrate the end of the semester. Students from all grades are encouraged to participate, whether they are (2) ______ (experience) musicians or complete beginners. This year's festival was the (3) ______ (big) one we have ever had, with over fifty performances across two stages. My best friend Wang Lei and I formed a band last spring. We practiced (4) ______ (play) guitar every day after school for six months to prepare for the festival. When our turn came, we performed (5) ______ original song that Wang Lei had written about friendship. To our surprise, the audience started singing along (6) ______ the second chorus. It was one of the (7) ______ (proud) moments of my life. Not all performances were about popular music. Some students played classical Chinese instruments such as the erhu and the guzheng, (8) ______ others performed traditional folk songs. There was even a group that combined hip-hop dancing with Beijing Opera singing, creating something (9) ______ (complete) new and unexpected. The festival reminds us that music has the power (10) ______ (bring) people together regardless of their backgrounds and interests.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：organizes**
   - **考点**：一般现在时三单。`Every December` 表示习惯性动作，主语 `school` 为三单。

2. **答案：experienced**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`experienced musicians` 有经验的音乐家。

3. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the biggest one we have ever had`。

4. **答案：playing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`practice doing sth.` 练习做某事。

5. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`an original song`，`original` 以元音音素开头。

6. **答案：during / at / by**
   - **考点**：介词。`during the second chorus` 在第二段副歌时。

7. **答案：proudest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the proudest moments`。

8. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。表示对比，"而/然而"，引导并列对比。

9. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：副词修饰形容词。`completely new`，修饰 `new`。

10. **答案：to bring**
    - **考点**：不定式作定语。`the power to bring` 把人们聚在一起的力量。
