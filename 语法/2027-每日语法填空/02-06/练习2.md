# 02-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家庭菜园 (Home Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Growing your own vegetables at home has become (1) ______ (increase) popular in recent years. My mother decided to start a small garden on our balcony after (2) ______ (watch) a TV program about urban farming. She bought some pots, soil and seeds, and soon our balcony (3) ______ (fill) with tomato plants, peppers and herbs. I was skeptical at first — I did not think anything could grow well in such a small (4) ______ (spacious). However, my mother proved me wrong. Within two months, the tomato plants (5) ______ (grow) taller than me. The first time we picked fresh tomatoes from our own garden, the whole family was (6) ______ (excite). They tasted much (7) ______ (sweet) than the ones from the supermarket. Taking care of the garden has taught me several important lessons. First, plants need (8) ______ (water) regularly, but not too much, or their roots will rot. Second, different plants have different (9) ______ (need) — some prefer sunlight while others grow better in the shade. Most importantly, I have learned that patience is key. You cannot rush nature; you can only provide the right conditions (10) ______ wait for the results.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰形容词。`increasingly popular` 越来越受欢迎。

2. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `after` 后用动名词形式。

3. **答案：was filled**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。阳台被填满了植物。

4. **答案：space**
   - **考点**：词性转换。形容词 `spacious`（宽敞的）转换为名词 `space`（空间）。

5. **答案：had grown**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。在过去某个时间（摘番茄）之前已经长高了。

6. **答案：excited**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。表示人感到兴奋的状态。

7. **答案：sweeter**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much sweeter than`，`much` 修饰比较级。

8. **答案：to be watered / watering**
   - **考点**：`need to be done` 或 `need doing`，两种形式都表示被动含义。

9. **答案：needs**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different needs` 不同的需求。

10. **答案：and**
    - **考点**：连词。`provide... and wait...` 两个并列的动词。
