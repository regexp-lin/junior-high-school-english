# 06-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：丝绸之路 (The Silk Road)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Silk Road was an ancient network of trade routes ___1___ connected China with the West. It ___2___ (name) after silk, one of the most valuable goods traded along the route. Over two thousand years ago, Chinese ___3___ (merchant) carried silk, tea and porcelain westward, while Western traders brought glass, gold and spices ___4___ China. The journey was ___5___ (extreme) long and dangerous. Travellers had to cross vast deserts and high mountains, facing sandstorms and ___6___ (freeze) temperatures. ___7___, the Silk Road was not just about trade. It also served ___8___ a bridge for cultural exchange. Buddhism, for example, spread to China along this route. Today, the Belt and Road Initiative aims ___9___ (rebuild) these ancient connections through modern infrastructure. Many countries ___10___ (join) the project so far, hoping to strengthen economic cooperation across continents.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 trade routes（物），在从句中作主语，用 that 或 which 引导定语从句。

2. **答案：was named**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。it 与 name 之间为被动关系，叙述历史事实用一般过去时的被动语态，填 was named。

3. **答案：merchants**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。Chinese merchants 表示"中国商人们"，merchant → merchants。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。brought...to China 表示"带到中国"，填 to。

5. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 long and dangerous，需用副词形式，extreme → extremely。

6. **答案：freezing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。freezing temperatures 表示"冰冻的温度"，freeze → freezing，现在分词作定语。

7. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词。前后为转折关系，"丝路不仅仅关乎贸易"，用 However。

8. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。serve as 表示"充当、作为"，固定搭配，填 as。

9. **答案：to rebuild**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。aim to do sth. 表示"旨在做某事"，填 to rebuild。

10. **答案：have joined**
    - **考点**：动词时态。so far 提示用现在完成时，many countries 为复数主语，填 have joined。
