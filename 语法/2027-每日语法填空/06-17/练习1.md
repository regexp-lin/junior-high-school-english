# 06-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校辩论赛 (School Debate)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school held an English debate competition last Thursday. The topic was "Should students ___1___ (allow) to use mobile phones in school?" Eight teams from different grades took part ___2___ the event. My team argued that phones should be banned during school hours. We spent a whole week ___3___ (collect) facts and examples to support our view. Standing on stage, I felt my heart beating ___4___ (fast) than ever before. The opposing team made some strong points — they said phones could be used as ___5___ (learn) tools and that banning them completely was ___6___ (fair). I had to think quickly and find ___7___ (weakness) in their arguments. In the end, our team won because we presented ___8___ (we) ideas more clearly and used stronger evidence. The judges praised ___9___ teams for their excellent performance. This competition taught me ___10___ important it is to think critically and express opinions logically.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：be allowed**
   - **考点**：动词语态。students 与 allow 之间为被动关系，should 后接动词原形的被动形式，填 be allowed。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。take part in 表示"参加"，固定搭配，填 in。

3. **答案：collecting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"，collect → collecting。

4. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。than ever before 提示用比较级，fast → faster。

5. **答案：learning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。learning tools 表示"学习工具"，现在分词作定语，learn → learning。

6. **答案：unfair**
   - **考点**：词性转换。根据语境，对方认为"完全禁止是不公平的"，fair → unfair，加否定前缀。

7. **答案：weaknesses**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。find weaknesses 表示"找出弱点"，weakness 为可数名词，此处指多个弱点，用复数 weaknesses。

8. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 ideas，用形容词性物主代词，we → our。

9. **答案：both**
   - **考点**：代词。both teams 表示"双方队伍"，评委表扬了两支队伍，填 both。

10. **答案：how**
    - **考点**：连词。how important it is to... 为感叹句式作宾语从句，表示"批判性思维有多重要"，填 how。
