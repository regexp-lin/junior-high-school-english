# 03-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：春游计划 (Spring Outing Plan)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Spring has arrived, and our class is busy planning a one-day outing. The destination (1) ______ (choose) last Friday after a heated discussion in class.

Some students wanted to visit a theme park, (2) ______ others preferred a nature park. Finally, we voted for Green Lake Park because it is not only beautiful but also free. The monitor (3) ______ (list) the activities we could do: boating, picnicking, and flying kites.

We divided the work into groups. Group One will contact the bus company to compare prices. Group Two (4) ______ (responsible) for food and drinks. I joined Group Three, which will prepare games and first-aid supplies.

Our teacher reminded us (5) ______ (check) the weather forecast before we leave. If it rains, we may need to change the date. She also told us (6) ______ bring light jackets and comfortable shoes.

The total cost (7) ______ (expect) to be around 80 yuan per person, which is less than most of us (8) ______ (think). Parents who want to join can pay an extra fee.

I am looking forward to the trip with my classmates. Spring outings are a tradition in many Chinese schools, and they help us get closer to nature (9) ______ each other.

I hope this plan (10) ______ (carry) out smoothly and that everyone will have a happy and safe day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was chosen**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。目的地“被选定”，上周五发生的事。

2. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`some ... while others ...` 表示对比。

3. **答案：listed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。班长 listed 列出活动，与 voted 时态一致。

4. **答案：is responsible**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be responsible for` 对……负责，陈述计划用一般现在时。

5. **答案：to check**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`remind sb. to do sth.` 提醒某人做某事。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`tell us to bring` 告诉我们要带……。

7. **答案：is expected**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。费用“预计为”，`be expected to`。

8. **答案：thought**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`than most of us thought` 比我们原先想的（更便宜），think → thought。

9. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`nature and each other` 亲近自然与彼此，与 `get closer to` 搭配。

10. **答案：will be carried**
    - **考点**：一般将来时被动语态。计划“被实施”，`carry out` 用被动。
