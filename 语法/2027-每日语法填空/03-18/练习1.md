# 03-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：古典音乐 (Classical Music)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When people talk about classical music, they often think of long concerts in grand halls. In fact, this kind of music has a much (1) ______ (wide) influence than many teenagers imagine.

Last month, our school invited a young violinist to give a talk. She told us how she practiced (2) ______ (hour) every day when she was our age. "Classical pieces are like mountains," she said. "You don't climb to the top in one day."

After the talk, she played a short piece by Mozart. The room fell silent, and I closed my eyes (3) ______ I could feel the notes flowing like a gentle stream. I realized I had never listened so (4) ______ (close) before.

My deskmate, who used to listen only to pop songs, said he wanted to search for more classical works online. Our music teacher was pleased and lent (5) ______ several CDs from the school library.

Classical music is not only for professionals. It can help us relax after a busy week and improve our concentration (6) ______ we study. Some scientists have found that certain pieces may even boost memory.

Of course, understanding the history behind the music takes time. (7) ______, if we stay curious, we will discover a rich world full of beauty.

This weekend, I plan to visit a small concert with my parents. I hope it will be (8) ______ enjoyable start to my journey into classical music.

Whether you play an instrument or not, give classical music a chance. You might find (9) ______ becomes a lifelong friend that stays with you (10) ______ thick and thin.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：wider**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，`wide` → `wider`。

2. **答案：hours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`practice hours` 练习数小时，可数名词复数。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。闭上眼睛并且感受音符，顺承关系。

4. **答案：closely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `listened`，`close` → `closely`。

5. **答案：him**
   - **考点**：人称代词宾格。`lend sb. sth.` 借给某人某物，指代 deskmate 男性用 him。

6. **答案：when / while**
   - **考点**：时间状语从句。学习时提高注意力，when 或 while 均可。

7. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：转折副词。理解历史需要时间，然而若保持好奇……，句首大写。

8. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`enjoyable` 以元音音素开头，用 an。

9. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。指代 `classical music`，`find it becomes ...`。

10. **答案：through**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`through thick and thin` 共患难、始终不渝。
