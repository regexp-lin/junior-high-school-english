# 05-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：生物多样性 (Biodiversity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Biodiversity means there are many kinds of living things in ___1___ same area, from tiny insects to tall trees and hidden plants under the soil. It keeps nature balanced because different species depend on ___2___ other in food chains and ecosystems. Sadly, human activities have destroyed some habitats, and several animals have become ___3___ (endanger) in the past few decades. Scientists say we should protect wetlands and forests ___4___ it is too late to repair the damage. In our science club, students ___5___ (record) bird sounds and plant names every month to learn how local life changes with seasons. ___6___ project helps us realize that even a small park can hold surprising life. If we reduce litter and save water at home, we ___7___ (help) biodiversity in quiet but meaningful ways. Everyone can be ___8___ part of the solution, not ___9___ problem. Protecting life around us is ___10___ duty we owe to the next generation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。固定搭配 the same，表示“同一区域”，用定冠词 the。

2. **答案：each**
   - **考点**：代词。固定短语 each other，表示“彼此、互相依赖”，用 each。

3. **答案：endangered**
   - **考点**：词性转换。系动词 become 后接形容词作表语，表示“濒危的”，用 endangered。

4. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：连词。引导时间状语从句，表示“在为时过晚、来不及修复破坏之前”，我们应保护湿地与森林，用 before。

5. **答案：record**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。every month 表示经常性动作，用一般现在时；主语 students 为复数，谓语动词用原形 record。

6. **答案：The**
   - **考点**：冠词。特指上文提到的科学社团项目，用定冠词 The，句首大写。

7. **答案：will help**
   - **考点**：动词时态。If 引导条件状语从句，遵循“主将从现”或主句表将来结果时常用 will + 动词原形，表示“将会帮助生物多样性”。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。固定搭配 a part of，表示“……的一部分”，用不定冠词 a。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。problem 可数，表示“一个问题”，用不定冠词 a。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。duty 可数，表示“一项责任”，duty 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 a。
