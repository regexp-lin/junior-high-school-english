# 10-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园厨艺大赛 (School Cooking Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school held its first cooking competition last Thursday. Each class had to send two students __1__ (represent) them. My partner and I decided __2__ (make) tomato egg noodles, a dish my mother often cooks. We brought all the __3__ (ingredient) from home, including fresh tomatoes, eggs and handmade noodles. When the contest began, the kitchen was filled __4__ the sound of chopping and frying. I was in charge __5__ cutting vegetables while my partner boiled the noodles. At first, I cut the tomatoes too __6__ (thick), so I had to redo them. Time flew by __7__ (quick) and we barely finished before the bell rang. The judges tasted every dish and gave __8__ (they) scores. To our delight, we won __9__ (two) place! The head judge said our noodles tasted just __10__ home-cooked food. It was the best compliment we could receive.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to represent**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"派两名学生去代表"，用 to represent。

2. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。decide to do sth. 决定做某事，用不定式 to make。

3. **答案：ingredients**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。all the 后接可数名词复数，表示"所有食材"，用 ingredients。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be filled with 充满……，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。be in charge of 负责……，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：thickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 cut，用副词 thickly，表示"切得太厚"。

7. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 flew by，用副词 quickly。

8. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 scores，用形容词性物主代词 their。

9. **答案：second**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词→序数词）。second place 第二名，two 的序数词为 second。

10. **答案：like**
    - **考点**：介词。taste like 尝起来像……，like 作介词表示"像"。
