# 10-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：重阳节 (Double Ninth Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Double Ninth Festival falls __1__ the ninth day of the ninth lunar month. It is a traditional Chinese festival __2__ (know) for respecting the elderly. On this day, people climb mountains, drink chrysanthemum tea and wear dogwood __3__ (plant). In ancient times, people believed these activities could drive __4__ bad luck. Today, the festival __5__ (regard) as a day to show love and care for old people. Last year, our class visited a nursing home on this festival. We __6__ (prepare) fruit and handmade cards for the elderly. Some students performed songs __7__ dances, which made the old people very __8__ (happiness). One grandpa told us stories about his __9__ (young). He said he felt less lonely because of our visit. The experience taught me that a little __10__ (kind) can go a long way.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on + 具体日期，on the ninth day 在第九天。

2. **答案：known**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。be known for 以……闻名，此处用过去分词 known 作后置定语修饰 festival。

3. **答案：plants**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。dogwood plants 茱萸植物，此处为复数概念，用 plants。

4. **答案：away**
   - **考点**：副词。drive away 驱走、赶走，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：is regarded**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 the festival 与 regard 为被动关系，描述现在的情况，用一般现在时被动语态 is regarded。

6. **答案：prepared**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 last year 可知为一般过去时，用 prepared。

7. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。songs 与 dances 并列，用 and 连接。

8. **答案：happy**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。make sb. + 形容词（宾语补足语），用 happy。

9. **答案：youth**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。his youth 他的年轻时代，young 的名词形式为 youth。

10. **答案：kindness**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。a little 后接不可数名词，kind 的名词形式为 kindness。
