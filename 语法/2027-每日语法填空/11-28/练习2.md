# 11-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：宠物领养 (Pet Adoption)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last spring, my family adopted a cat from the local animal shelter. We named __1__ (she) Doudou. When we first brought her home, she was very __2__ (fright) and hid under the sofa for two days. My mother put food and water near __3__ hiding spot and spoke to her __4__ (gentle). Slowly, Doudou began to trust us. Now she follows me __5__ (every) I go in the house. Taking care of a pet is a big __6__ (responsible). I feed her twice a day and clean her litter box __7__ (regular). We also take her to the vet for check-ups. Our vet told us that thousands of cats and dogs __8__ (abandon) in China every year. Many of them end up in shelters __9__ on the streets. He encouraged people to adopt pets instead of __10__ (buy) them from shops. I agree — every animal deserves a loving home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。name 后接宾语，指代母猫，用宾格 her。

2. **答案：frightened**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。was very frightened 非常害怕，-ed 形式表示人/动物的感受。

3. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 hiding spot，用 her。

4. **答案：gently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 spoke，用副词 gently。

5. **答案：everywhere**
   - **考点**：副词。everywhere I go 我去的每一个地方，every 的相关形式 everywhere。

6. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。a big responsibility 一份重大的责任，responsible 的名词形式为 responsibility。

7. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 clean，用副词 regularly。

8. **答案：are abandoned**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。cats and dogs 与 abandon 为被动关系，every year 表示经常性事实，用一般现在时被动 are abandoned。

9. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：并列连词。in shelters or on the streets 在收容所或街头，or 表示"或者"。

10. **答案：buying**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。instead of + 动名词，of 为介词，后接 buying。
