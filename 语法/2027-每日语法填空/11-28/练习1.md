# 11-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国桥梁工程 (Chinese Bridge Engineering)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China is known __1__ a leader in bridge engineering. Over the past few decades, Chinese engineers __2__ (build) some of the world's longest and tallest bridges. The Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge, __3__ opened in 2018, is the __4__ (long) sea-crossing bridge in the world. It stretches about 55 kilometres and __5__ (connect) three important cities. Building such a massive structure was extremely __6__ (challenge). Engineers had to deal __7__ strong ocean currents, typhoons and complex seabed conditions. The project took over eight years __8__ (complete). Another remarkable bridge is the Duge Beipanjiang Bridge in Guizhou, which stands at a __9__ (high) of 565 metres above the river below. When I saw pictures of it, I could __10__ (hard) believe that humans built such a thing. These bridges not only make travel faster but also show the amazing ability of Chinese engineers.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。be known as 被称为……，为固定搭配。

2. **答案：have built**
   - **考点**：动词时态。over the past few decades 与现在完成时连用，用 have built。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词为桥梁名称，非限制性定语从句用 which。

4. **答案：longest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the longest 最长的，用 longest。

5. **答案：connects**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。主语 It 为第三人称单数，描述事实用一般现在时 connects。

6. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。was extremely challenging 极具挑战性，challenge 的形容词形式为 challenging。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。deal with 应对、处理，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：to complete**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It took + 时间 + to do sth. 花费多长时间做某事，用不定式 to complete。

9. **答案：height**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。at a height of 在……的高度，high 的名词形式为 height。

10. **答案：hardly**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。could hardly believe 几乎无法相信，hardly 表示"几乎不"。
