# 12-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冰雪运动进校园 (Ice and Snow Sports at School)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

With the __1__ (success) hosting of the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics, ice and snow sports have become more and more popular in China. Our school __2__ (introduce) ice skating lessons this year to encourage students to try winter sports. Every Friday afternoon, we go to a nearby ice rink __3__ our PE teacher. At first, I was afraid of __4__ (fall) on the ice. I held the railing tightly and moved very __5__ (slow). My teacher told me that the most important thing was __6__ (keep) my balance. After several weeks of practice, I __7__ finally skate without any help. Now I am one of the __8__ (fast) skaters in my class. Ice skating has not only made me __9__ (strong) but also taught me that we should never give up when __10__ (face) difficulties. I hope more schools will offer winter sports courses in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：successful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 hosting，需要形容词 successful。

2. **答案：has introduced**
   - **考点**：动词时态。this year 今年，强调对现在产生的影响，用现在完成时 has introduced。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with our PE teacher 和体育老师一起，with 表示"和……一起"。

4. **答案：falling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。be afraid of falling 害怕摔倒，of 为介词，后接动名词。

5. **答案：slowly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 moved，用副词 slowly。

6. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作表语）。the most important thing was to keep 最重要的事情是保持……，不定式作表语。

7. **答案：could/can**
   - **考点**：情态动词。could finally skate 终于能滑了，表示经过练习获得的能力，用 could（过去）或 can（叙述转为现在）。

8. **答案：fastest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the fastest 最快的……之一，one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词。

9. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。made me stronger 使我更强壮，"make + 宾语 + 比较级"结构，与后文 but also 暗含对比。

10. **答案：facing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。when facing difficulties 面对困难时，when 引导时间状语从句的省略形式，主语与 face 为主动关系，用现在分词。
