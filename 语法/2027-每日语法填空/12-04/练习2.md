# 12-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：二手书交换活动 (Second-hand Book Exchange)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our school organised a second-hand book exchange activity. Each student was asked __1__ (bring) at least two books that they had already finished __2__ (read). The event took place in the school hall. When I arrived, there __3__ (be) already hundreds of books on the tables. I brought a science fiction novel __4__ a collection of short stories. A girl from Class 5 was very __5__ (interest) in my novel, so she exchanged it for a book about animals. I was quite happy because I love animals. __6__ the activity, I got three new books without __7__ (spend) any money. Our head teacher said this activity was __8__ (meaning) than simply throwing old books away. It helped us learn to share __9__ reduce waste at the same time. Many students hope that the school __10__ (hold) this kind of activity more often in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to bring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾补）。be asked to do sth. 被要求做某事，用不定式 to bring。

2. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。finish reading 读完，finish 后接动名词。

3. **答案：were**
   - **考点**：there be 句型与主谓一致。there were hundreds of books 有数百本书，复数名词用 were。

4. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。a science fiction novel and a collection 一本科幻小说和一本短篇集，and 连接两个并列名词。

5. **答案：interested**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ed 形容词）。be interested in 对……感兴趣，修饰人用 interested。

6. **答案：Through/During**
   - **考点**：介词。through/during the activity 通过/在活动期间，表示时间或方式。

7. **答案：spending**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。without spending 不花费，without 为介词，后接动名词。

8. **答案：more meaningful**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。more meaningful than 比……更有意义，多音节形容词用 more + 原级构成比较级。

9. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。share and reduce 分享并减少，and 连接两个并列动词。

10. **答案：will hold**
    - **考点**：动词时态。in the future 在将来，hope that 后接一般将来时 will hold。
