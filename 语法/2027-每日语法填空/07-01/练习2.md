# 07-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：红色旅游 (Red Tourism)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Red tourism refers to visiting places ___1___ (connect) with China's revolutionary history. It has become very popular ___2___ young people in recent years. During the summer holiday, my class visited Jinggangshan in Jiangxi Province. The tour guide told us that this was ___3___ the first revolutionary base was established in 1927. We walked along mountain paths that revolutionary soldiers ___4___ (walk) almost a hundred years ago. At the memorial hall, we saw old photographs and personal ___5___ (belong) of the heroes. Their stories were so ___6___ (move) that several students had tears in their eyes. We also watched a short play ___7___ (perform) by local actors, which brought history to life. In the evening, we sat together and shared ___8___ (we) thoughts about the trip. Everyone agreed that ___9___ (understand) history is important for building a better future. This trip made me ___10___ (deep) appreciate the peaceful life we enjoy today.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：connected**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，places connected with 表示"与……相关的地方"，connect → connected。

2. **答案：among**
   - **考点**：介词。popular among young people 表示"在年轻人中流行"，填 among。

3. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：连词。where 引导表语从句，表示"第一个革命根据地建立的地方"，填 where。

4. **答案：had walked**
   - **考点**：动词时态。almost a hundred years ago 表示更早的过去，主句叙述过去事件，用过去完成时 had walked。

5. **答案：belongings**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。personal belongings 表示"个人物品"，belong → belonging → belongings。

6. **答案：moving**
   - **考点**：词性转换。so + 形容词 + that...，stories（物）用 moving（令人感动的）。

7. **答案：performed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，a short play performed by... 表示"由……表演的短剧"，perform → performed。

8. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 thoughts，用形容词性物主代词，we → our。

9. **答案：understanding**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。动名词作主语，表示"理解历史这件事"，understand → understanding。

10. **答案：deeply**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 appreciate，需用副词形式，deep → deeply。
