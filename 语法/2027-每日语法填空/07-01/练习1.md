# 07-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：无人机技术 (Drone Technology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Drones were once seen as expensive toys, but they have become ___1___ (true) useful tools in many fields. Farmers use drones ___2___ (spray) crops with water and fertiliser, saving both time and labour. In disaster areas, drones help rescue teams find ___3___ (survive) who are trapped in hard-to-reach places. The film industry also ___4___ (rely) heavily on drones for aerial photography. Last month, our school ___5___ (hold) a drone-flying competition. Students had to guide their drones through an obstacle course ___6___ crashing. My team spent weeks ___7___ (practise) and adjusting our drone's controls. On the day of the competition, we were very nervous because the wind was ___8___ (strong) than expected. Luckily, our drone performed well and we finished in second place. The experience taught me that drone technology requires ___9___ only programming skills but also quick thinking. I believe drones will play an even ___10___ (big) role in our lives in the years to come.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 useful，需用副词形式，true → truly。

2. **答案：to spray**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。use sth. to do sth. 表示"用某物做某事"，填 to spray。

3. **答案：survivors**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。find survivors 表示"找到幸存者"，survive → survivor → survivors。

4. **答案：relies**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。the film industry 为单数主语，陈述一般事实用一般现在时，rely → relies。

5. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：动词时态。last month 提示用一般过去时，hold → held。

6. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。without crashing 表示"不撞到障碍物"，填 without。

7. **答案：practising**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"，practise → practising。

8. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than expected 提示用比较级，strong → stronger。

9. **答案：not**
   - **考点**：连词。not only...but also... 表示"不仅……而且……"，填 not。

10. **答案：bigger**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。even + 比较级表示"甚至更大的"，big → bigger。
