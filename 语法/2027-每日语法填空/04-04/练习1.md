# 04-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：3D打印技术 (3D Printing Technology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

3D printing sounds like science fiction, but it is already used in schools, hospitals, and factories. A 3D printer builds objects layer (1) ______ layer, following a digital design. This technology can create models (2) ______ are hard to make by hand.

In our technology class, we were asked to design a small bridge. First, we drew the plan on a computer. Then the machine (3) ______ (print) it out in plastic within two hours. Our teacher said this method saves material (4) ______ it only uses what is truly needed.

Some doctors now print simple tools for operations. Engineers test car parts (5) ______ (make) by 3D printers before producing them in metal. Of course, the machines are still expensive, and users must learn (6) ______ (operate) them safely.

I believe 3D printing will become (7) ______ (common) in daily life than it is today. Young people should keep learning about new tools, (8) ______ can help them solve problems creatively. With patience, even a beginner can turn (9) ______ idea into something you can hold in your hand.

In the future, more inventions (10) ______ (create) with the help of this amazing technology.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`layer by layer` 一层一层地。

2. **答案：which / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `models`，从句缺主语。

3. **答案：printed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述课堂上的经历，与 `were asked` 时态一致，`print` → `printed`。

4. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。后句解释节省材料的原因。

5. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`parts made by 3D printers` 被打印出的零件，或 `that were made`。

6. **答案：to operate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`learn to do sth.` 学习做某事，用 `to operate`。

7. **答案：more common**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，`common` 双音节常用 `more common`。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。逗号后非限制性定语从句，指代 `tools`，从句缺主语，用 `which`。
