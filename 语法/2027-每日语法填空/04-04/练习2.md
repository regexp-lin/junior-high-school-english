# 04-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：公益跑步 (Charity Run)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Sunday, our city held a charity run to raise money for children in poor areas. More than three thousand people took part in it, (1) ______ attracted attention from several local newspapers. Each runner paid a small fee, and all the money (2) ______ (send) to a local charity.

The event started at seven in the morning. Before the race, we warmed up together (3) ______ a coach led us through simple exercises. I was a little nervous (4) ______ I had never run five kilometers in one go.

During the run, volunteers stood along the road (5) ______ (offer) water and encouragement. Strangers cheered for one another, (6) ______ made the hard journey feel warmer. When I finally crossed the finish line, I was breathing heavily, (7) ______ I felt proud of myself.

This experience taught me that exercise is not only about speed. It is also about caring for others. Next year, I will train (8) ______ (hard) and invite my cousin to join me. I hope the charity run (9) ______ (hold) every spring so that more people can take action.

Even a small step forward can mean (10) ______ lot to someone in need.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事“三千多人参赛”，在从句中作主语，用 `which`。

2. **答案：was sent / would be sent**
   - **考点**：被动语态。钱“被送往”慈善机构；叙述过去事件用一般过去时被动 `was sent`；若强调过去将来的安排也可用 `would be sent`，此处以 `was sent` 为宜。

3. **答案：while / when / as**
   - **考点**：从属连词。在我们热身时教练带着做动作，表时间用 `while`/`when`/`as`。

4. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。紧张是因为从未跑过五公里，表原因。

5. **答案：offering**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。志愿者站在路边并“提供”水，主动关系用 `offering`。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面“陌生人互相加油”，用 `which`。

7. **答案：but / yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。喘得厉害但很自豪，转折用 `but` 或 `yet`。

8. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。与今年相比明年“更努力”训练，隐含比较用 `harder`。

9. **答案：will be held / is held**
   - **考点**：被动语态。活动“被举办”，表将来用 `will be held`；表规律也可用 `is held`。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`mean a lot` 意义重大。
