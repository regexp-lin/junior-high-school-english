# 06-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国高铁 (China's High-Speed Railway)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's high-speed railway, also known ___1___ the bullet train, has changed the way people travel. With a top speed of 350 kilometres per hour, it is one of the ___2___ (fast) train systems in the world. The network now covers more than 40,000 kilometres, ___3___ (connect) major cities across the country. A trip from Guangzhou to Beijing, which used to take over twenty hours by regular train, can now ___4___ (complete) in just eight hours. My family often takes the high-speed train when we visit ___5___ (we) relatives in Wuhan. The ___6___ (comfortable) seats and smooth ride make the journey very ___7___ (enjoy). Passengers can use free WiFi, charge their devices, and even order meals online during the trip. The ___8___ (develop) of high-speed rail has also boosted the economy of many smaller cities ___9___ giving them better access to major markets. China's high-speed railway is truly something ___10___ the whole nation can be proud of.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。be known as 表示"被称为"，固定搭配，填 as。

2. **答案：fastest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，fast → fastest。

3. **答案：connecting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语/结果状语，covers more than 40,000 km, connecting major cities...，填 connecting。

4. **答案：be completed**
   - **考点**：动词语态。a trip 与 complete 之间为被动关系，can 后接动词原形的被动形式，填 be completed。

5. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 relatives，用形容词性物主代词，we → our。

6. **答案：comfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 seats，需用形容词形式，comfort → comfortable。

7. **答案：enjoyable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。make the journey enjoyable 表示"使旅程令人愉快"，enjoy → enjoyable。

8. **答案：development**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the + 名词 + of，develop → development。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。by giving them 表示"通过给予它们"，by doing sth. 表方式，填 by。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 something（不定代词），只能用 that 引导定语从句，填 that。
