# 06-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：社交媒体的影响 (The Impact of Social Media)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Social media has become a major part of teenagers' daily lives. A recent study showed that the average middle school student spends about two hours a day ___1___ (scroll) through social media apps. On the one hand, these platforms allow us ___2___ (stay) connected with friends and learn about the world. On ___3___ other hand, spending too much time on social media can be ___4___ (harm). Many students compare ___5___ (they) with others online and feel unhappy about their own lives. Some even lose sleep because they can't stop ___6___ (check) their phones. Experts suggest ___7___ teenagers should limit screen time to no more than one hour per day. My school recently introduced a "No Phone Day" every Wednesday. ___8___ first, many students complained, but now most of us enjoy the ___9___ (free) of not being tied to our devices. Learning to use social media ___10___ (wise) is an important skill for our generation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：scrolling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"，scroll → scrolling。

2. **答案：to stay**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。allow sb. to do sth. 表示"允许某人做某事"，填 to stay。

3. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。on the other hand 表示"另一方面"，固定搭配，填 the。

4. **答案：harmful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。be harmful 表示"有害的"，harm → harmful。

5. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：代词。compare themselves with others 表示"把自己和他人比较"，主语 many students 为复数，用反身代词 themselves。

6. **答案：checking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。can't stop doing sth. 表示"忍不住做某事"，stop 后接动名词，填 checking。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。suggest that... 引导宾语从句，填 that。

8. **答案：At**
   - **考点**：介词。at first 表示"起初"，句首大写，填 At。

9. **答案：freedom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the + 名词，enjoy the freedom 表示"享受自由"，free → freedom。

10. **答案：wisely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 use，需用副词形式，wise → wisely。
