# 01-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：健康饮食 (Healthy Eating)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A balanced diet is essential for maintaining good health, especially for teenagers who are still (1) ______ (grow). Doctors recommend that students should eat plenty of fruits, vegetables and whole grains every day. These foods are rich (2) ______ vitamins and minerals that help our bodies function properly. However, many young people prefer (3) ______ (eat) fast food such as burgers, fried chicken and chips. While these foods taste (4) ______ (deliciously), they contain too much fat, sugar and salt, which can lead to serious health problems. A recent study (5) ______ (show) that nearly 30% of teenagers in China are overweight. This is largely because of unhealthy eating habits and a lack of exercise. My mother is a nutritionist, and she has always encouraged our family to eat (6) ______ (health). She prepares meals using fresh ingredients and avoids (7) ______ (use) too much oil or seasoning. At first, I did not enjoy the food she cooked because it tasted quite plain. But gradually, I got used to it and even started to appreciate the natural (8) ______ (flavour) of different foods. Now I feel much (9) ______ (energetic) than before, and I rarely get sick. I think it is important for every student (10) ______ (develop) good eating habits early in life, as they will benefit us for years to come.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：growing**
   - **考点**：现在进行时。`are still growing` 表示仍在成长。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be rich in` 为固定搭配，表示"富含"。

3. **答案：to eat**
   - **考点**：不定式。`prefer to do sth` 表示"更喜欢做某事"。

4. **答案：delicious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`taste` 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。

5. **答案：showed / has shown**
   - **考点**：时态。`A recent study showed` 用一般过去时，或 `has shown` 用现在完成时均可。

6. **答案：healthily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `eat` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：using**
   - **考点**：动名词。`avoid doing sth` 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：flavours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the natural flavours of different foods`，不同食物有多种风味。

9. **答案：more energetic**
   - **考点**：比较级。多音节形容词的比较级用 `more + adj`，`than` 为标志。

10. **答案：to develop**
    - **考点**：不定式。`It is important for sb to do sth` 为固定句型。
