# 01-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：音乐的力量 (The Power of Music)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Music is a universal language that connects people across cultures and (1) ______ (nation). Whether you are listening to a pop song, a classical symphony or a traditional folk tune, music has the power to change your mood and touch your heart. Research has found that listening to music can reduce stress and improve (2) ______ (concentrate). That is why many students like to listen to soft music while (3) ______ (study). My friend Zhang Wei is one of the (4) ______ (talented) musicians in our school. He started (5) ______ (learn) the piano at the age of five, and now he can play over a hundred pieces from memory. Last year, he (6) ______ (invite) to perform at the city's New Year concert. I went to watch him, and I was deeply (7) ______ (move) by his performance. The music he played was so beautiful (8) ______ some people in the audience were brought to tears. After the concert, I told Zhang Wei that I wished I (9) ______ (can) play an instrument as well as he did. He smiled and said, "It is never too late to start. The most important thing is to enjoy (10) ______ process." His words encouraged me to sign up for guitar lessons the following week.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：nations**
   - **考点**：名词复数。与 `cultures` 并列，用复数形式。

2. **答案：concentration**
   - **考点**：词性转换。动词 `improve` 后接名词作宾语。

3. **答案：studying**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`while studying` 省略了主语和助动词，表伴随。

4. **答案：most talented**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the most talented musicians`，多音节形容词最高级。

5. **答案：learning / to learn**
   - **考点**：动名词/不定式。`start doing / to do sth`，两种形式均可。

6. **答案：was invited**
   - **考点**：被动语态。他"被邀请"演出，`last year` 表过去时。

7. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰人用 `-ed` 形式，`deeply moved` 表示"深受感动的"。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so beautiful that...` 结构。

9. **答案：could**
   - **考点**：虚拟语气。`I wished I could play`，表示与现在事实相反的愿望，`wish` 后用过去时。

10. **答案：the**
    - **考点**：冠词。`enjoy the process`，特指学习音乐的过程。
