# 04-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：无现金支付 (Cashless Payment)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Cashless payment has changed the way people shop. With a phone or a card, customers can pay (1) ______ carrying paper money. Many young people cannot remember (2) ______ last time they used coins in a supermarket.

QR codes are common in cafés and bookstores. You scan the code, enter the amount, and the money (3) ______ (transfer) in seconds. Small businesses like this method (4) ______ it reduces the risk of receiving fake notes.

However, cashless life is not perfect. Elderly people who do not own smartphones may feel (5) ______ (leave) behind. Some rural areas still have weak network signals, (6) ______ makes mobile payment difficult.

Last week, my grandmother asked me (7) ______ (help) her link a bank card to a payment app. It took us an hour, (8) ______ she smiled when she paid for fruit by herself.

Technology should serve everyone. I hope designers will keep working on simpler interfaces (9) ______ are easy for seniors to use.

Cash may not disappear soon, (10) ______ digital wallets will surely play a bigger role in our future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。不用带纸币即可支付。

2. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。`the last time` 上一次，特指。

3. **答案：is transferred**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。钱“被转账”，描述常态。

4. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。后句解释商家喜欢的原因。

5. **答案：left**
   - **考点**：过去分词作补语。`feel left behind` 感到被落下，`leave` → `left`。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句。

7. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`ask sb. to do sth.` 请某人做某事。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。花了一小时，但她笑了，转折。

9. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `interfaces`，从句缺主语。

10. **答案：but / yet**
    - **考点**：并列连词。现金未必很快消失，但电子钱包作用更大，转折。
