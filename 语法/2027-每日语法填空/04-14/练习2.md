# 04-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：二十四节气 (24 Solar Terms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The twenty-four solar terms divide the Chinese lunar year into seasons and farming periods. Farmers in ancient times watched the sun, wind, and rain (1) ______ decide when to plant or harvest.

Qingming and Grain Rain are familiar to many students. During these periods, the weather changes (2) ______ (quick) than in winter. My grandfather still uses an old calendar (3) ______ lists every term in traditional characters.

In class, we learned that each term has a poem or a proverb. For example, people say you should eat eggs (4) ______ the Beginning of Summer arrives. These customs sound simple, (5) ______ they carry wisdom passed down for centuries.

Modern technology predicts weather more exactly, (6) ______ the solar terms remain useful for understanding nature. Artists paint them on posters, and chefs design seasonal menus.

I think young people should not treat the terms (7) ______ boring facts in textbooks. If we connect them with travel or cooking, they can become (8) ______ (live) lessons about Chinese culture.

Last week, I marked the next solar term on my phone and planned a family hike. Small rituals make me feel closer (9) ______ the rhythm of the year.

I hope this knowledge (10) ______ (share) with friends around the world through short videos.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：动词不定式作目的状语。观察天气“以便”决定何时耕种。

2. **答案：more quickly**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，修饰动词 `changes`，`quickly` → `more quickly`。

3. **答案：which / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `calendar`，从句缺主语。

4. **答案：when / before / after**
   - **考点**：从属连词。立夏到来时/前后吃蛋，依习俗选用 `when` 等。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。习俗看似简单，但承载智慧。

6. **答案：but / yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。科技更精确，但节气仍有价值。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`treat ... as ...` 把……当作……。

8. **答案：lively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `lessons`，`live` → `lively` 生动的。

9. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`feel closer to` 更贴近……。

10. **答案：will be shared / can be shared**
    - **考点**：被动语态。知识“被分享”，表将来或可能性。
