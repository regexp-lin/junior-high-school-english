# 11-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：老城区改造 (Old Town Renovation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The old town near my grandparents' home __1__ (go) through a big renovation over the past two years. The narrow lanes used to be dirty and crowded, with broken __2__ (wall) and leaking roofs everywhere. Now, the buildings __3__ (repair) and painted in traditional colours. New street lights __4__ (install) to make the area safer at night. The most exciting change is that the old market square __5__ (turn) into a cultural plaza. Local artists display their __6__ (work) there, and musicians perform on weekends. My grandmother is very happy __7__ the changes. She says the neighbourhood is much __8__ (clean) and more comfortable than before. However, some people worry that renovation may destroy the __9__ (origin) character of the old town. They think modern shops and cafés do not belong there. I believe the key is to find __10__ balance between development and preservation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has gone**
   - **考点**：动词时态。over the past two years 与现在完成时连用，主语 the old town 为第三人称单数，用 has gone。

2. **答案：walls**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。broken walls 破旧的墙壁，用复数 walls。

3. **答案：have been repaired**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。buildings 与 repair 为被动关系，结合语境用现在完成时被动语态 have been repaired。

4. **答案：have been installed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。lights 与 install 为被动关系，用现在完成时被动语态 have been installed。

5. **答案：has been turned**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。the old market square 与 turn into 为被动关系，用现在完成时被动语态 has been turned。

6. **答案：works**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。works 作品（复数），display their works 展示他们的作品。

7. **答案：about / with**
   - **考点**：介词。be happy about/with 对……感到高兴，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：cleaner**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，后有 than before，clean 的比较级为 cleaner。

9. **答案：original**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 character，origin 的形容词形式为 original。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。find a balance 找到一种平衡，balance 此处为可数名词，用 a。
