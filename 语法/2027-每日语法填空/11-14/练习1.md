# 11-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界儿童日 (World Children's Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

November 20th is World Children's Day. It was created by the United Nations __1__ (promote) the welfare of children worldwide. Sadly, not all children have the chance __2__ (go) to school or live in safe conditions. According to UNICEF, over 250 million children around the world are out __3__ school. Many of them have to work long hours __4__ (support) their families. In some countries, girls are not allowed __5__ (receive) education at all. These __6__ (fair) situations need our attention. Our school held a charity event on this day to raise money __7__ children in need. Students sold handmade crafts and donated the __8__ (earn). We raised over 5,000 yuan in total. A teacher who __9__ (work) in a rural school as a volunteer shared her experience with us. She said the children there were eager __10__ (learn) but lacked basic supplies like books and pencils. Her talk inspired many of us to continue helping.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to promote**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了促进"，用 to promote。

2. **答案：to go**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。the chance to do sth. 做某事的机会，不定式作后置定语。

3. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。be out of school 失学，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：to support**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了养家"，用 to support。

5. **答案：to receive**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be allowed to do sth. 被允许做某事，用不定式 to receive。

6. **答案：unfair**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→反义形容词）。由语境可知这些是不公平的状况，fair 加前缀 un- 构成 unfair。

7. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。raise money for 为……筹款，用 for。

8. **答案：earnings**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。donated the earnings 捐赠了收入，earn 的名词形式为 earnings（通常用复数）。

9. **答案：had worked / worked**
   - **考点**：动词时态。一位曾在农村学校做过志愿者的老师，用过去完成时 had worked 或一般过去时 worked。

10. **答案：to learn**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。be eager to do sth. 渴望做某事，用不定式 to learn。
