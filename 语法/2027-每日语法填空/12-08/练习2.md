# 12-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家乡的变化 (Changes in My Hometown)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My hometown is a small town in Hunan Province. Over the past ten years, it __1__ (change) a lot. When I was young, the roads were narrow and __2__ (dust). There was only one small shop __3__ the whole town. People had to travel to the city to buy most things. Now, wide roads __4__ (build) and there are several supermarkets nearby. A new hospital was also opened last year, __5__ makes it much easier for people to see a doctor. My grandfather often tells me __6__ different the town looks compared to the past. He remembers __7__ (walk) for two hours to reach the nearest school when he was a boy. Today, there is a modern school just ten __8__ (minute) walk from our house. Although the town has become more __9__ (convenience), some old traditions remain unchanged. Neighbours still visit each other during festivals and share home-cooked food. I love my hometown because it is a place __10__ both the old and the new come together perfectly.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has changed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Over the past ten years 在过去十年间，与现在完成时连用，用 has changed。

2. **答案：dusty**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。roads were dusty 路面尘土飞扬，与 narrow 并列作表语，用形容词 dusty。

3. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in the whole town 在整个镇上，in 表示"在……里"。

4. **答案：have been built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（现在完成时被动语态）。roads 与 build 为被动关系，Now 暗示对现在的影响，用现在完成时被动 have been built。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。which 引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事。

6. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句（连接副词）。how different 多么不同，how 引导宾语从句，修饰形容词 different。

7. **答案：walking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。remember walking 记得曾经走路，remember doing 表示记得做过的事。

8. **答案：minutes'**
   - **考点**：名词所有格。ten minutes' walk 十分钟的步行路程，表示时间的名词所有格。

9. **答案：convenient**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。become more convenient 变得更方便，become 为系动词，后接形容词。

10. **答案：where**
    - **考点**：定语从句（关系副词）。a place where 一个……的地方，where 引导定语从句修饰 place，在从句中作地点状语。
