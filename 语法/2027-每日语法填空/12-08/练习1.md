# 12-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：盲人图书馆志愿者 (Volunteering at a Library for the Blind)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every Saturday morning, I volunteer at a special library __1__ serves blind and visually impaired people. My job is __2__ (read) books aloud and record them as audiobooks. Before I started, I did not know how __3__ (difficulty) it was to read clearly for a long time. I had to learn to control my speed and pronounce every word __4__ (correct). The library __5__ (set) up five years ago by a kind-hearted teacher named Mr. Chen. He believed that blind people __6__ (deserve) the same chance to enjoy books as everyone else. So far, the library __7__ (produce) over 3,000 audiobooks. Many blind readers have written letters to thank __8__ volunteers. One letter said, "Your voices are like __9__ (window) to the world for us." Reading those letters always makes me feel proud. I think __10__ (help) others is one of the most meaningful things we can do.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。that/which 引导定语从句修饰 library，在从句中作主语。

2. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作表语）。My job is to read 我的工作是朗读，不定式作表语说明主语的具体内容。

3. **答案：difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。how difficult it was 它有多难，how + 形容词 + 主语 + 谓语，感叹句/宾语从句结构。

4. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 pronounce，用副词 correctly。

5. **答案：was set**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。five years ago 五年前，library 与 set up 为被动关系，用一般过去时被动 was set (up)。

6. **答案：deserved**
   - **考点**：动词时态。主句 believed 为过去时，宾语从句需时态一致，用一般过去时 deserved。

7. **答案：has produced**
   - **考点**：动词时态。So far 到目前为止，与现在完成时连用，用 has produced。

8. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。the volunteers 特指图书馆的志愿者们，用定冠词 the。

9. **答案：windows**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。windows to the world 通向世界的窗户，like 后用复数表示泛指。

10. **答案：helping**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。helping others 帮助他人，动名词短语作主语。
