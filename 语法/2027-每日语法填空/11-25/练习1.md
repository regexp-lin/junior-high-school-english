# 11-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：小小志愿翻译官 (Young Volunteer Translator)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last autumn, our city hosted __1__ international marathon. Thousands of runners from around the world came __2__ (participate). The organising committee needed volunteer translators, so I signed up __3__ (excited). After a short training session, I was assigned __4__ a help desk near the starting line. My job was __5__ (help) foreign runners with directions, registration and other questions. On race day, a tall man from Kenya approached me. He spoke English __6__ (fluent), but he could not read the Chinese signs. I helped him find the changing room and the water station. He thanked me __7__ a warm smile and said, "You speak English very __8__ (good)!" I also assisted a Japanese family __9__ had got lost on the way to the viewing area. Using simple English and hand gestures, I guided them __10__ (success) to their seats. The experience not only improved my English but also gave me a sense of pride.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。international 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，泛指"一场国际马拉松"，用 an。

2. **答案：to participate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。came to participate 来参加，不定式作目的状语。

3. **答案：excitedly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 signed up，用副词 excitedly。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。be assigned to 被分配到……，用 to。

5. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。My job was to do sth. 我的工作是做某事，用不定式 to help 作表语。

6. **答案：fluently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 spoke，用副词 fluently。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。thanked me with a warm smile 带着温暖的微笑感谢我，with 表示"带着"。

8. **答案：well**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 speak，用副词 well（good 的副词形式）。

9. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 a Japanese family 指人，从句缺主语，用 who 或 that。

10. **答案：successfully**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 guided，用副词 successfully。
