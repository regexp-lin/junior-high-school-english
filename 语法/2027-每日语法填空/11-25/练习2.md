# 11-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：夜市文化 (Night Market Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Night markets are an important part of Chinese food culture. Last Friday, my family visited a famous night market in our city. The street was __1__ (crowd) with people and lined with dozens of food stalls. The air was filled __2__ mouth-watering smells. My father bought grilled lamb skewers, __3__ are his all-time favourite. My mother tried a bowl of spicy __4__ (noodle) from a Sichuan stall. I chose stinky tofu because I was curious about __5__ it tasted. To my surprise, it was __6__ (actual) quite delicious! Besides food, the market also had stalls __7__ (sell) handmade jewellery, toys and local snacks. A street musician was playing the guitar near the entrance, __8__ (attract) a small crowd. The atmosphere was __9__ (live) and full of energy. My parents said night markets had been part of Chinese life __10__ the Tang Dynasty. I think they are a wonderful way to experience local culture and bring people together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：crowded**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be crowded with 挤满了，crowd 的形容词形式为 crowded。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be filled with 充满……，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 grilled lamb skewers 指物，非限制性定语从句用 which。

4. **答案：noodles**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。a bowl of noodles 一碗面，noodles 通常用复数。

5. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。curious about 后接宾语从句，表示"它尝起来怎么样"，用 how。

6. **答案：actually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰整个句子表示"实际上"，用副词 actually。

7. **答案：selling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作后置定语）。stalls 与 sell 为主动关系，用现在分词 selling。

8. **答案：attracting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作结果状语）。表示自然结果"吸引了一小群人"，用 attracting。

9. **答案：lively**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。atmosphere was lively 气氛活跃，live 的形容词形式为 lively。

10. **答案：since**
    - **考点**：介词。since the Tang Dynasty 自唐朝以来，与现在完成时连用。
