# 11-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：科技博物馆 (Science and Technology Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, our class visited the Science and Technology Museum in the city centre. The museum has four __1__ (floor), each with a different theme. On the first floor, we saw models of ancient Chinese __2__ (invent) like the compass, printing and gunpowder. The second floor __3__ (focus) on modern technology, including robots and artificial intelligence. I was most impressed __4__ a robot that could play the piano. It performed a piece by Chopin __5__ (beautiful). On the third floor, there was __6__ interactive space area where visitors could experience __7__ it feels like to walk on the moon. I put on a special suit and "walked" in low gravity — it was __8__ (amazing) fun! The top floor had a 4D cinema __9__ showed a film about ocean exploration. The seats moved with the waves, and we even felt water spray on our faces. The visit lasted about three hours, __10__ nobody wanted to leave. I decided that I would come back again soon.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：floors**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。four 后接可数名词复数，用 floors。

2. **答案：inventions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。ancient Chinese inventions 中国古代发明，invent 的名词形式为 invention，用复数 inventions。

3. **答案：focused / focussed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文为过去时态叙述，用一般过去时 focused。

4. **答案：by / with**
   - **考点**：介词。be impressed by/with 被……打动，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 performed，用副词 beautifully。

6. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。interactive 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，泛指"一个互动太空区域"，用 an。

7. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。experience 后接宾语从句，表示"感受在月球上行走是什么样的"，用 what。

8. **答案：amazingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 fun，用副词 amazingly 表示"令人惊讶地"。

9. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 a 4D cinema 指物，从句缺主语，用 that 或 which。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。前句说参观持续三小时，后句说没人想离开，语义转折，用 but。
