# 11-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园定向越野 (School Orienteering)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our PE teacher introduced orienteering to us this month. It is __1__ outdoor sport that combines running with map reading. Last Thursday, we had our first orienteering race __2__ the nearby park. Each team received a map __3__ (mark) with several checkpoints. We had to find all the checkpoints in the __4__ (short) time possible. My team had four __5__ (member). I was in charge of reading the map while others ran ahead. At first, we took a wrong turn and __6__ (waste) five minutes. I felt __7__ (terrible) sorry, but my teammates encouraged me to keep __8__ (try). We quickly found the right path and reached the next checkpoint. The race was exciting because we had to make quick __9__ (decide) at every crossing. In the end, our team finished __10__ (two). Although we did not win, we learned a lot about teamwork and navigation. I cannot wait for the next race!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。outdoor 以元音音素 /aʊ/ 开头，泛指"一项户外运动"，用 an。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in the nearby park 在附近的公园里，用 in 表示在某地。

3. **答案：marked**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。map 与 mark 为被动关系，用过去分词 marked 表示"标记了检查点的"。

4. **答案：shortest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，in the shortest time 在最短时间内，用 shortest。

5. **答案：members**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。four 后接可数名词复数，用 members。

6. **答案：wasted**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与 took 并列，用一般过去时 wasted。

7. **答案：terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 sorry，用副词 terribly 表示程度"非常"。

8. **答案：trying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。keep doing sth. 继续做某事，用动名词 trying。

9. **答案：decisions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。make decisions 做决定，decide 的名词形式为 decision，此处用复数 decisions。

10. **答案：second**
    - **考点**：词性转换（基数词→序数词）。finish second 获得第二名，two 的序数词为 second。
