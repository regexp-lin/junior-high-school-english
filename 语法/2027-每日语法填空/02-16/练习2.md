# 02-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节水意识 (Water Conservation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Water is essential for all living things, yet many people do not realize how (1) ______ (value) it is. Although the Earth is covered mostly by water, only about 3% of it is fresh water, and much of that is locked in ice caps. This means the amount of water (2) ______ (avail) for human use is actually very limited. In China, water shortage is a serious problem, (3) ______ (particular) in northern regions. Some cities have less water per person than countries in the Middle East. To address this issue, the government (4) ______ (take) many measures in recent years, including building water transfer projects and promoting water-saving technologies. However, government action alone is not enough — everyone needs to do (5) ______ (they) part. There are many simple ways to save water in daily life. For example, turning (6) ______ the tap while brushing your teeth can save up to 8 litres of water per minute. Taking shorter showers instead of baths (7) ______ (save) even more. Collecting rainwater for (8) ______ (water) plants is another easy method. At our school, we started a "Water Warriors" campaign last month. Students are encouraged to report any leaking taps (9) ______ they find. So far, the campaign has helped fix over twenty leaks, (10) ______ (save) thousands of litres of water. Every drop counts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`how valuable it is` 感叹句/宾语从句中用形容词。

2. **答案：available**
   - **考点**：形容词作定语。`the amount of water available` 可用的水量，后置定语。

3. **答案：particularly**
   - **考点**：副词。`particularly in northern regions` 尤其在北方地区。

4. **答案：has taken**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`in recent years` 近年来。

5. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：物主代词。`do their part` 尽自己的一份力。

6. **答案：off**
   - **考点**：副词/短语动词。`turn off the tap` 关掉水龙头。

7. **答案：saves**
   - **考点**：一般现在时三单。动名词短语 `Taking shorter showers` 作主语，谓语用三单。

8. **答案：watering**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `for` 后用动名词。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `taps`。

10. **答案：saving**
    - **考点**：现在分词作结果状语。自然产生的结果。
