# 02-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：博物馆参观 (Museum Visit)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, our history teacher took us on a field trip to the Guangdong Provincial Museum. It was my first visit, and I was truly (1) ______ (impress) by the size and beauty of the building. The museum has four floors, each (2) ______ (devote) to a different theme. We started on the ground floor, which displayed ancient pottery and bronze (3) ______ (object) dating back thousands of years. Our teacher explained that these items (4) ______ (dig) up from archaeological sites across Guangdong Province. On the second floor, we explored an exhibition about the Maritime Silk Road. I learned that Guangzhou (5) ______ (be) one of the most important trading ports in the world for centuries. The exhibition included models of ancient ships and maps (6) ______ showed the trading routes between China and other countries. The third floor was my favourite because it featured an interactive exhibit (7) ______ visitors could try on virtual traditional costumes using augmented reality technology. My classmate Liu Wei took a photo of (8) ______ (he) dressed as a Tang Dynasty official, and we all laughed. By the time we finished (9) ______ (tour) the entire museum, three hours had passed. The visit made history feel much more real and (10) ______ (alive) than reading about it in textbooks.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：impressed**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。`was impressed by` 被……打动。

2. **答案：devoted**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`each devoted to` 每层致力于。

3. **答案：objects**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`bronze objects` 青铜器物。

4. **答案：had been dug**
   - **考点**：过去完成时的被动语态。在展出之前已经被挖掘出来。

5. **答案：has been**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`for centuries` 几个世纪以来，强调持续。

6. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `maps`。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，修饰 `exhibit`，表地点。

8. **答案：himself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`took a photo of himself` 拍了他自己的照片。

9. **答案：touring**
   - **考点**：动名词。`finish doing sth.` 完成做某事。

10. **答案：alive**
    - **考点**：形容词。`feel alive`，`alive` 只作表语的形容词，此处已给出原型。
