# 01-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：动画电影 (Animated Films)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Animated films have been entertaining audiences of all ages for decades. From the classic Disney princess stories to modern computer-generated masterpieces, animation has come a long way. One of the most (1) ______ (influence) animated films in recent years is Ne Zha, a Chinese film (2) ______ (release) in 2019. The film tells the story of a boy who refuses (3) ______ (accept) his fate and fights against prejudice. Ne Zha was a massive hit at the box office, (4) ______ (earn) over five billion yuan and becoming the highest-grossing animated film in Chinese history at that time. What makes a great animated film? First, it needs a (5) ______ (power) story that audiences can relate to. The best animated films deal with universal themes such as love, courage and the struggle (6) ______ good and evil. Second, the characters must be well-developed and memorable. Think of Monkey King, Elsa or Totoro — these characters have become cultural (7) ______ (icon) recognised around the world. Third, the animation quality (8) ______ (it) matters greatly. Thanks to advances in technology, today's animated films look more (9) ______ (real) and stunning than ever before. I am proud to see that Chinese animation (10) ______ (improve) rapidly in recent years. I believe that one day, Chinese animated films will be just as popular as Hollywood ones on the global stage.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：influential**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `films` 需要形容词形式，`influence` → `influential`。

2. **答案：released**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a film released in 2019`，被动关系。

3. **答案：to accept**
   - **考点**：不定式。`refuse to do sth` 为固定搭配。

4. **答案：earning**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随结果，"从而赚了"。

5. **答案：powerful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `story` 需要形容词形式。

6. **答案：between**
   - **考点**：介词。`the struggle between good and evil`，"善与恶之间的斗争"。

7. **答案：icons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`cultural icons`，多个文化偶像。

8. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`the animation quality itself`，强调动画质量"本身"。

9. **答案：realistic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`look more realistic`，系动词后接形容词，`real` → `realistic`。

10. **答案：has improved / has been improving**
    - **考点**：现在完成时。`in recent years` 为现在完成时标志。
