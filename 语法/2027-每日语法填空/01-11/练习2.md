# 01-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统手工艺 (Traditional Handicrafts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has a rich tradition of handicrafts that (1) ______ (pass) down from generation to generation. Paper cutting, embroidery and pottery are just a few examples of the many art forms that Chinese craftspeople have perfected over (2) ______ (century). My grandmother is skilled (3) ______ making paper cuttings. She can create incredibly detailed designs using nothing more than a pair of scissors and a sheet of red paper. When I was younger, I used to sit beside her and watch in (4) ______ (amaze) as her hands moved quickly and precisely. She once told me that she (5) ______ (learn) the art from her own grandmother when she was just six years old. Sadly, many traditional handicrafts are in danger of (6) ______ (disappear). Young people today are more interested (7) ______ modern technology than in learning ancient skills. To address this problem, some schools have started offering classes in traditional arts and crafts. Our school, for example, invites local craftspeople to teach students how (8) ______ (make) pottery and calligraphy brushes. These classes are very popular and have helped many students develop a deeper (9) ______ (appreciate) for Chinese culture. I think it is our (10) ______ (responsible) to protect and preserve these precious cultural treasures for future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have been passed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时被动。手工艺"被传承"至今，用现在完成时被动。

2. **答案：centuries**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`over centuries` 表示"经过数个世纪"。

3. **答案：at / in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be skilled at/in` 为固定搭配，表示"擅长"。

4. **答案：amazement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`in amazement` 为固定搭配，介词后接名词。

5. **答案：had learned / had learnt**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。她告诉我之前已经学会，"过去的过去"用过去完成时。

6. **答案：disappearing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`in danger of doing sth`，介词 `of` 后接动名词。

7. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be interested in` 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：不定式。`teach sb how to do sth`，疑问词 + 不定式结构。

9. **答案：appreciation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a deeper appreciation`，形容词后接名词。

10. **答案：responsibility**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`our responsibility`，物主代词后接名词。
