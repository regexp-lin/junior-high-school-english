# 06-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园广播站 (School Radio Station)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has a radio station ___1___ broadcasts during lunch break every day. This semester, I joined the radio team ___2___ a host. My job is to read the news, share interesting stories and play music for the ___3___ (student). Before each show, I have to spend at least an hour ___4___ (choose) topics and writing scripts. The ___5___ (hard) part is speaking clearly and naturally into the microphone. When I first started, my voice sounded stiff ___6___ I was too nervous. My partner, Li Hua, is much more ___7___ (experience) than me. She taught me to imagine that I ___8___ (talk) to a close friend rather than a large audience. Gradually, I became ___9___ (confident) and even started to enjoy it. Now many classmates tell me they look forward ___10___ our show every day. That really makes all the effort worthwhile.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a radio station（物），在从句中作主语，用 that 或 which 引导定语从句。

2. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。as a host 表示"作为主持人"，填 as。

3. **答案：students**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。the students 表示"全体学生"，student → students。

4. **答案：choosing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"，choose → choosing。

5. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"最难的部分"，hard → hardest。

6. **答案：because**
   - **考点**：连词。解释声音僵硬的原因——太紧张了，用 because 引导原因状语从句。

7. **答案：experienced**
   - **考点**：词性转换。much more + 形容词构成比较级，experience → experienced（有经验的）。

8. **答案：was talking**
   - **考点**：动词时态。imagine that... 后用虚拟或描述想象中的情境，此处指"想象自己正在和好朋友说话"，用过去进行时 was talking。

9. **答案：more confident**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。became 为系动词，表示"变得更自信"，confident 为多音节词，用 more confident。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：介词。look forward to 表示"期待"，固定搭配，填 to。
