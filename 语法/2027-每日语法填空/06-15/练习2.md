# 06-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：航天员的生活 (Life of Astronauts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Living in space is very different ___1___ living on Earth. Astronauts on the Chinese space station have to follow a strict ___2___ (day) schedule. They wake up at six in the morning and begin their work after a simple breakfast. Since there is no gravity, even ___3___ (eat) becomes a challenge. Food must be ___4___ (special) prepared so it does not float away. Astronauts drink water from sealed bags ___5___ of glasses. ___6___ (exercise) is also very important. Without it, their bones and muscles would become ___7___ (weak) over time. Every day, they spend about two hours ___8___ (work) out on special machines. At night, they sleep in sleeping bags attached ___9___ the wall. Despite the difficulties, most astronauts say that ___10___ (see) Earth from space is the most amazing experience of their lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。be different from 表示"与……不同"，固定搭配，填 from。

2. **答案：daily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 schedule，需用形容词形式，day → daily。

3. **答案：eating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。动名词作主语，表示"吃东西这件事"也变成了挑战，eat → eating。

4. **答案：specially**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 prepared，需用副词形式，special → specially。

5. **答案：instead**
   - **考点**：副词。instead of 表示"而不是"，用密封袋而非玻璃杯喝水，填 instead。

6. **答案：Exercise/Exercising**
   - **考点**：名词/非谓语动词。作主语，可用名词 Exercise 或动名词 Exercising，句首大写。

7. **答案：weaker**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。become weaker 表示"变得更弱"，weak → weaker。

8. **答案：working**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"，work → working。

9. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。be attached to 表示"被固定在……上"，固定搭配，填 to。

10. **答案：seeing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。动名词作主语，表示"从太空看地球这件事"，see → seeing。
