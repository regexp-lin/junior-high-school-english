# 01-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：体育精神 (Sportsmanship)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Sportsmanship means playing fair, respecting your opponents and accepting the results of a competition with grace. It is a quality that is valued not only in sports (1) ______ also in everyday life. I learned an important lesson about sportsmanship during a basketball game last year. Our team (2) ______ (play) against a much stronger team from another school. By half-time, we were already losing by twenty points, and some of my teammates wanted to give up. However, our coach encouraged us (3) ______ (keep) fighting until the very end. He reminded us that the purpose of the game was not just about (4) ______ (win), but about improving ourselves and showing respect for the sport. In the second half, we played with renewed energy and (5) ______ (determine). Although we still lost the game, we managed to narrow the gap (6) ______ (significant). After the final whistle, something unexpected happened. The captain of the opposing team came over and shook hands with each of us. He told us that we were the (7) ______ (tough) opponents they had faced all season and that he admired our fighting spirit. His words meant more to me than any trophy. That day, I realised that true (8) ______ (great) in sport is not measured by how many games you win, but by how you behave on (9) ______ off the field. A good sportsman treats every opponent with respect, whether they are (10) ______ (strong) or weaker.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 结构。

2. **答案：was playing**
   - **考点**：过去进行时。描述过去某一特定时间正在进行的比赛。

3. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb to do sth` 为固定搭配。

4. **答案：winning**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `about` 后接动名词形式。

5. **答案：determination**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `energy` 并列，需要名词形式。

6. **答案：significantly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `narrow` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：toughest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the toughest opponents they had faced all season`。

8. **答案：greatness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`true greatness`，形容词 `true` 后接名词。

9. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`on and off the field`，"场内和场外"。

10. **答案：stronger**
    - **考点**：比较级。`whether they are stronger or weaker`，与 `weaker` 对应。
