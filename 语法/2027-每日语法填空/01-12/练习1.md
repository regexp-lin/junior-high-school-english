# 01-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：海洋生物 (Marine Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ocean covers more than 70% of the Earth's surface and is home to an incredible variety of life forms. Scientists estimate that there are over 230,000 known marine species, and many more remain (1) ______ (discover). Coral reefs, often called the "rainforests of the sea," are among the most (2) ______ (biology) diverse ecosystems on our planet. They provide food and shelter (3) ______ thousands of species of fish, sea turtles and other creatures. Unfortunately, coral reefs around the world are under serious threat. Rising ocean (4) ______ (temperature) caused by climate change are leading to coral bleaching, a process in which corals lose their colour and eventually die. Pollution is another major problem. Every year, approximately eight million tonnes of plastic waste (5) ______ (end) up in the ocean, harming marine animals that mistake it (6) ______ food. I once watched a documentary about a sea turtle (7) ______ had a plastic straw stuck in its nose. The image was so (8) ______ (heart) that I decided to stop using single-use plastics from that day on. Protecting marine life requires the efforts of everyone. Governments should pass (9) ______ (strict) laws against ocean pollution, and ordinary people can help by (10) ______ (reduce) their plastic consumption and supporting ocean conservation projects.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：undiscovered**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`remain undiscovered`，系动词后接形容词，加否定前缀 `un-`。

2. **答案：biologically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `diverse` 需要副词形式。

3. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`provide sth for sb` 表示"为……提供"。

4. **答案：temperatures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`rising ocean temperatures`，指上升的海洋温度。

5. **答案：ends**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。`Every year` 表示经常性动作，主语 `waste` 为不可数名词，谓语用单数。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`mistake A for B` 为固定搭配，表示"把A误认为B"。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰 `a sea turtle`，在从句中作主语。

8. **答案：heartbreaking**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so heartbreaking that...`，修饰事物用复合形容词。

9. **答案：stricter**
   - **考点**：比较级。暗含与现有法律相比，需要"更严格的"法律。

10. **答案：reducing**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词形式。
