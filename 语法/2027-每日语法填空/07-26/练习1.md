# 07-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：端午节 (Dragon Boat Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Dragon Boat Festival is one of ___1___ most important traditional festivals in China. It usually falls ___2___ the fifth day of the fifth lunar month, when the weather is warm and plants grow fast. People remember Qu Yuan, ___3___ was a great poet and loved his country deeply. His story has been passed down for centuries, and it still touches people’s hearts today. Some people also hang wormwood on their doors because they believe it can keep the family healthy. On this special day, many families enjoy ___4___ (make) zongzi together at home. Grandparents often teach children how to fold the leaves carefully. These sticky rice dumplings ___5___ (wrap) in bamboo leaves and then ___6___ (boil) for several hours until they smell wonderful. In some riverside towns, exciting dragon boat races ___7___ (hold) every year. Teams train hard for weeks because they want to win honor for their villages. The long, narrow boats look like ___8___ (dragon) flying over the water, and the sound of drums fills the air. ___9___ the races start, crowds along the banks cheer loudly and wave colorful flags. In recent years, more teenagers ___10___ (learn) about the festival through school activities and short videos online, so the old traditions stay alive in a modern way.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。在“one of + the + 形容词最高级 + 可数名词复数”结构中，最高级前必须用定冠词 **the**，表示“最……之一”。此处为 *one of the most important traditional festivals*。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。表示在具体某一天（含序数、节日日期）用 **on**；*in* 多用于月份、年份、季节，*at* 多用于具体时刻。此处为“农历五月初五这一天”，故用 **on**。

3. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词（定语从句）。先行词 *Qu Yuan* 指人，从句 *___ was a great poet* 缺主语，用 **who** 引导非限制性定语从句，在从句中作主语。*which* 指物，*whom* 作宾语，均不适用。

4. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。*enjoy* 后接动词时须用动名词作宾语，即 **enjoy doing sth.**，表示“喜欢做某事”。故填 **making**。

5. **答案：are wrapped**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。主语 *These sticky rice dumplings* 与 *wrap* 之间是被动关系（粽子“被包裹”），且描述一般事实，用一般现在时的被动语态：**am/is/are + 过去分词**。主语为复数，故填 **are wrapped**。

6. **答案：are boiled**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。主语仍为 *These sticky rice dumplings*（与第五空同一主语，由 *and then* 连接并列谓语），粽子“被煮”，须用一般现在时被动语态，与 *are wrapped* 结构对称，故填 **are boiled**。

7. **答案：are held**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。主语 *dragon boat races* 与 *hold* 为被动（比赛“被举办”），*every year* 表示习惯性、经常性，用一般现在时被动：**are held**。

8. **答案：dragons**
   - **考点**：名词的数。*dragon* 为可数名词，前有 *like* 表示类比，且“多条龙舟”为复数概念，须用复数 **dragons**。

9. **答案：When / As**
   - **考点**：连词。引导时间状语从句，表示“当比赛开始时”，用 **When** 或 **As**（当……时）。*While* 后多接延续性动词，*start* 为短暂动词，**When/As** 更自然。

10. **答案：have learned / have learnt**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。*In recent years*（近年来）是现在完成时的典型时间标志，表示从过去持续到现在的影响，结构为 **have/has + 过去分词**。主语 *teenagers* 为复数，故填 **have learned**（英式 **have learnt** 亦可）。
