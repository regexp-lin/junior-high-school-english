# 07-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：阅读的力量 (The Power of Reading)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many people say that reading opens ___1___ door to a wider world. When you read ___2___ good book, you can travel through time and space without leaving your chair. A quiet corner and a few pages may become your favorite place to rest after a busy day. Reading helps us ___3___ (understand) different cultures and ideas that we cannot see in our small town. It also makes our minds ___4___ (strong) than before if we keep the habit for years. Some students think reading is boring, ___5___ they seldom pick up a storybook after class. They would rather watch short videos on their phones. ___6___ fact, even ten minutes of reading every day can bring big changes to your vocabulary and thinking. Good readers often write ___7___ (clear) because they have stored more words and sentence patterns in ___8___ (they) minds. They know how to express feelings and arguments in a simple but powerful way. Last month, our school library ___9___ (organize) a “Reading Week”, and hundreds of students took part in it. There were book talks, reading corners, and small prizes for active readers. Since then, more of ___10___ (they) have fallen in love with reading during breaks, and the library has become noisier—in a happy way.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。固定表达 **open the door to...** 表示“打开通往……的大门”，此处 *door* 为特指（通往更广阔世界的那扇门），用定冠词 **the**。不宜用 *a*，因习惯上与比喻义中的“那扇门”搭配用 **the**。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。*book* 为可数名词单数，此处表泛指“一本好书”，*good* 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 **a**。

3. **答案：understand / to understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。*help sb. (to) do sth.* 为固定结构，表示“帮助某人做某事”，不定式符号 *to* 可省略。故填 **understand** 或 **to understand** 均可（中考两种形式常都接受，填 **understand** 更常见）。

4. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有比较连词 **than**，且 *make + 宾语 + 形容词* 作宾补，须用形容词 **strong** 的比较级 **stronger**，表示“比以前更强健/更强大（指思维方面）”。

5. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。前句“一些学生认为阅读很无聊”与后句“他们课后很少拿起故事书”为因果关系，用 **so** 连接，表示“因此、所以”。*but* 表转折，*and* 表并列，均不符合逻辑。

6. **答案：In**
   - **考点**：介词。固定短语 **in fact** 表示“事实上”，句首首字母大写，填 **In**。

7. **答案：more clearly**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。修饰动词 *write* 须用副词 *clearly*；句中隐含与以前或与不常阅读的人相比，“写得更清楚”，用副词比较级 **more clearly**（*clearly* 为多音节副词，比较级加 **more**）。

8. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：词性转换（人称代词 → 物主代词）。提示词 *they* 在名词 *minds* 前须变为形容词性物主代词 **their**，表示“他们的头脑/思维”。*they* / *them* 不能直接修饰名词。

9. **答案：organized / organised**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。*Last month* 为一般过去时时间状语，谓语用过去式。**organize** 的过去式为 **organized**（英式拼写 **organised** 亦可）。

10. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：词性转换（人称代词主格 → 宾格）。提示词 *they* 位于介词 *of* 之后，须用宾格 **them**（*more of them* 表示“他们中的更多人”）。主格 *they* 不可作介词宾语。
