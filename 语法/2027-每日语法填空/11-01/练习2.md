# 11-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校田径队 (School Track Team)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I joined the school track team at the beginning of this term. Our coach, Mr. Lin, is very strict __1__ us. He makes us run five kilometres every morning __2__ breakfast. At first, I could __3__ (hard) finish the whole distance. My legs felt like jelly after each training session. However, after weeks of practice, I have become much __4__ (strong) and faster. Mr. Lin often says, "Pain is __5__ (temporary) but glory lasts forever." He also teaches us the __6__ (correct) way to warm up before running to avoid __7__ (injury). Last week, I took part in the district __8__ (athlete) competition. I was nervous __9__ the race started, but once I began running, I forgot all my fears. I finished __10__ (three) in the 400-metre race! Although I did not win a gold medal, Mr. Lin said he was proud of my progress.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be strict with sb. 对某人严格，为固定搭配。

2. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：介词。before breakfast 在早餐前，用 before。

3. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。could hardly finish 几乎不能完成，hard 的副词形式 hardly 表示"几乎不"。

4. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，与 faster 并列，strong 的比较级为 stronger。

5. **答案：temporary**
   - **考点**：形容词用法。is 后接形容词作表语，temporary 表示"暂时的"。若给提示词需确认原形即可。

6. **答案：correct**
   - **考点**：形容词用法。修饰名词 way，用形容词 correct 表示"正确的"。

7. **答案：injuries**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。avoid injuries 避免受伤，injure 的名词形式为 injury，此处用复数 injuries。

8. **答案：athletics**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词/名词作定语）。athletics competition 田径比赛，athlete 的相关形式 athletics 作定语。

9. **答案：before / when**
   - **考点**：从属连词。before/when the race started 在比赛开始前/当比赛开始时，引导时间状语从句。

10. **答案：third**
    - **考点**：词性转换（基数词→序数词）。finish third 获得第三名，three 的序数词为 third。
