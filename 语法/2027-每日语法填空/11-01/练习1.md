# 11-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：消防安全日 (Fire Safety Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

November 9th is National Fire Safety Day in China. To prepare for it, our school held a fire drill last Monday. At 10 a.m., the alarm __1__ (ring) loudly across the campus. Every student was required __2__ (leave) the classroom immediately. Our teacher reminded us __3__ (not push) each other and to stay calm. We covered our mouths with wet cloths and walked __4__ (quick) to the playground. It took less __5__ three minutes for all 2,000 students to gather at the safe area. After the drill, a firefighter gave __6__ (we) a talk about fire __7__ (safe). He showed us how to use a fire extinguisher and explained the __8__ (important) of checking smoke alarms at home. He warned that many fires __9__ (cause) by careless behaviour, such as leaving candles burning or overloading electrical sockets. I learned that staying calm and knowing the escape routes are the __10__ (key) to surviving a fire.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rang**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 last Monday 和 at 10 a.m. 可知为一般过去时，ring 的过去式为不规则变化 rang。

2. **答案：to leave**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be required to do sth. 被要求做某事，用不定式 to leave。

3. **答案：not to push**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。remind sb. not to do sth. 提醒某人不要做某事，否定形式为 not to push。

4. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 walked，用副词 quickly。

5. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较级标志词。less than 少于、不到，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。give 后接间接宾语，用宾格 us。

7. **答案：safety**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。fire safety 消防安全，safe 的名词形式为 safety。

8. **答案：importance**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。the importance of 对……的重要性，important 的名词形式为 importance。

9. **答案：are caused**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 fires 与 cause 为被动关系，描述一般事实，用一般现在时被动语态 are caused。

10. **答案：keys**
    - **考点**：名词单复数。the keys to 通往……的关键（staying calm 和 knowing the escape routes 两个主语），用复数 keys。
