# 09-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：运动改变生活 (Sports Change Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Two years ago, I was often sleepy in class and lacked confidence. __1__ my doctor advised me to exercise regularly, I started jogging for twenty minutes every morning. At the beginning, my legs ached, __2__ I told myself not to give up. Little __3__ little, I could run longer routes and breathe __4__ (easy). Sports also helped me join a small running club __5__ I met partners who encouraged me before exams. Now I sleep __6__ (good) at night and feel more energetic during lessons. My parents say I smile __7__ (much) than before. I understand sports do not only build __8__ strong body __9__ also shape a positive mind. I will keep this habit __10__ it becomes part of my lifestyle.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：When / After**
   - **考点**：从属连词。当医生建议之后开始锻炼，When 或 After 引导时间状语从句。

2. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。腿疼与自我鼓励不放弃为转折。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定短语。little by little 逐渐地。

4. **答案：more easily**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。与锻炼前对比，呼吸更顺畅，修饰动词 breathe 用副词 easily 的比较级 more easily。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 club 表地点，从句 I met partners 结构完整，用 where 在从句中作地点状语，相当于 in which。

6. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级与固定搭配。sleep well 睡得好，与过去对比用 well 的比较级 better。

7. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。than before 提示比较级，much 修饰动词 smile 时比较级为 more。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。build a strong body 强健体魄。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定结构。not only ... but also ... 不但……而且……。

10. **答案：until / till**
   - **考点**：从属连词。keep this habit until it becomes part of my lifestyle 坚持直到成为生活方式的一部分。
