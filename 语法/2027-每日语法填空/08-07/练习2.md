# 08-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统节日美食 (Traditional Festival Food)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the Spring Festival, my grandmother always prepares ___1___ (tradition) dishes to show her love for the family. The dining table is crowded with fish, dumplings, and other treats that carry good wishes for the new year. This year she taught me ___2___ (make) sweet rice balls on the Lantern Festival evening. The kitchen smelled wonderful, and I felt ___3___ (real) proud when my first ball floated in the hot water ___4___ any trouble.

On the Mid-Autumn Festival, shops sell many kinds of mooncakes, and the round moon above the city seems to invite every family outdoors. Some of them ___5___ (fill) with nuts and seeds, while others taste much ___6___ (sweet) because of extra sugar. My parents tell us not to eat too many, ___7___ they agree that shared joy matters more than food itself when we sit together under the bright moon.

The Dragon Boat Festival reminds people ___8___ Qu Yuan, a poet deeply respected in Chinese history. Families make zongzi together and enjoy exciting boat races on the river while drums beat loudly along the banks. Last year my uncle's team rowed ___9___ (fast) than any other group in our town, and we cheered ___10___ (happy) for him at the finish line. Those warm memories help me understand why festival food is never only about taste.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 dishes，需用形容词；tradition 的形容词形式为 traditional，表示“传统的”。

2. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。teach sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配，意为“教某人做某事”，故用动词不定式 to make。

3. **答案：really**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰形容词 proud，需用副词；real 的副词形式为 really。

4. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。根据语境，汤圆顺利浮起、没有出问题，表示“没有、无”，用 without；without any trouble 意为“毫无困难地”。

5. **答案：are filled**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。主语 Some of them（指 mooncakes）与 fill 之间为被动关系（月饼被填入馅料）；陈述一般情况，用一般现在时的被动语态 are filled。be filled with 为固定搭配，意为“装满……”。

6. **答案：sweeter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 常用来修饰比较级，表示“……得多”；此处将不同月饼的甜度对比，故用 sweet 的比较级 sweeter。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前句说不要吃太多，后句说分享的快乐更重要，语义上为转折关系，用 but。

8. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。remind sb. of sth./sb. 为固定搭配，意为“使某人想起……”，故用 of。

9. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。句中出现 than，表示两者比较；修饰动词 rowed，需用副词 fast 的比较级 faster。

10. **答案：happily**
    - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 cheered，需用副词；happy 的副词形式为 happily。
