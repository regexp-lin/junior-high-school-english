# 08-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：参观动物园 (Visiting the Zoo)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Yesterday my cousins and I visited the zoo ___1___ has just opened near our neighborhood. We had read many posts about it online, so everyone felt excited long before we reached the gate. We arrived ___2___ 9:30 a.m. ___3___ we could listen to the caretakers' talk about animal care. The zoo was much bigger than it looked on the website, and we ___4___ (decide) to rent a small cart so that we could save some energy for the long walk ahead of us.

Our first stop was the Panda House, where cool air mixed with the smell of fresh bamboo. Visitors stood quietly while two giant pandas ate bamboo ___5___ (slow). A friendly guide told us that wild pandas spend about twelve hours a day ___6___ (eat) leaves because this food is not very rich in energy. My little cousin listened ___7___ (careful) and wrote down several interesting facts in her small notebook.

On our way to the Bird World, we met a group of pupils who argued ___8___ (noise) about which path to take. Colorful parrots near the gate repeated simple greetings, which made the little children laugh again and again. In the afternoon we watched ___9___ elephant show in an open theatre beside a quiet lake. The animals were clever enough ___10___ (paint) simple pictures before a large crowd, and many visitors raised their phones to record the lovely moment. When the sun went down, we left with tired legs and happy hearts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 the zoo 指物，从句 "___ has just opened near our neighborhood" 缺少主语，需用指物的关系代词 that 或 which 引导定语从句。

2. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。表示在具体时刻前用 at，如 at 9:30 a.m.，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。前后为因果关系：早点到达，以便能听饲养员讲解。so 引导结果状语从句，相当于“因此、所以”，符合语境逻辑。

4. **答案：decided**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文叙述昨天经历，以一般过去时为主；and 连接并列谓语，与 was 并列，故用 decide 的过去式 decided。

5. **答案：slowly**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 ate，需用副词；slow 的副词形式为 slowly。

6. **答案：eating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 为固定结构，意为“花费时间做某事”，故用 eat 的 -ing 形式 eating。

7. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 listened，需用副词；careful 的副词形式为 carefully。

8. **答案：noisily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 argued，需用副词；noise 的副词形式为 noisily，表示“大声地、吵闹地”。

9. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。show 为可数名词单数，此处泛指“一场大象表演”；exciting 以元音音素开头，故用不定冠词 an。

10. **答案：to paint**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。形容词 + enough + to do sth. 为固定结构，表示“足够……去做某事”，故用动词不定式 to paint。
