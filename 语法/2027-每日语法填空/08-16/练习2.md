# 08-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：社区花园 (Community Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In our neighbourhood, there is a small community garden ___1___ was built three years ago. Before that, the land was just an empty space covered ___2___ weeds and rubbish. A group of retired ___3___ (teacher) suggested turning it into a garden, and the local government agreed to support the project.

Now the garden ___4___ (look) completely different. There are rows of vegetables, colourful flowers, and even a few fruit trees. Every morning, you can see elderly people ___5___ (water) the plants and chatting happily with each other. Children also love ___6___ (play) there on weekends. The garden has become the ___7___ (live) place in the whole community.

My grandmother is one of the most active ___8___ (volunteer) in the garden. She goes there almost every day and takes great pride in the tomatoes she grows. She often gives fresh vegetables to the ___9___ (neighbour) who live nearby. "Sharing makes everything taste ___10___ (good)," she always says with a warm smile.

The community garden has not only made our neighbourhood more beautiful, but also brought people closer together. I hope more communities will build gardens like ours in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `a small community garden` 指物，在从句中作主语，故用关系代词 **that** 或 **which**。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**be covered with...**（被……覆盖）为固定搭配，故填 **with**。

3. **答案：teachers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`A group of`（一群）后接可数名词复数，`teacher` 的复数形式为 **teachers**。

4. **答案：looks**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。`Now`（现在）提示一般现在时，主语 `the garden` 为第三人称单数，`look` 在此为系动词，故填 **looks**。

5. **答案：watering**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**see sb. doing sth.**（看见某人正在做某事），强调正在进行的动作，故填 **watering**。

6. **答案：playing / to play**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**love doing sth.** 或 **love to do sth.** 均可（喜欢做某事），故填 **playing** 或 **to play** 均可。

7. **答案：liveliest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**the + 最高级** 表示"最……的"，`lively`（生动的、热闹的）的最高级为 **liveliest**（以辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i 加 -est）。注意：括号给出 `live`，其形容词形式 `lively` 的最高级为 **liveliest**。

8. **答案：volunteers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**one of the most active + 复数名词**（最活跃的……之一），`volunteer` 的复数为 **volunteers**。

9. **答案：neighbours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。定冠词 `the` 后接名词，由后面定语从句 `who live nearby` 可知是多个邻居，故用复数 **neighbours**。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。系动词 `taste` 后接形容词；语境暗含比较含义（分享让一切尝起来更好），`good` 的比较级为 **better**。
