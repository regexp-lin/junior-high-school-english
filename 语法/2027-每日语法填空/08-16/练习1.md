# 08-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：爬山经历 (Mountain Climbing Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, my father and I climbed Mount Baiyun, one of the ___1___ (high) mountains near our city. We got up early and arrived at the foot of the mountain ___2___ seven o'clock in the morning.

The trail was steep and narrow. At first, I walked very fast and felt ___3___ (energy). But after climbing for about an hour, my legs became heavier and heavier. I wanted ___4___ (give) up and go back. My father noticed my tiredness and said, "Don't look at the top. Just focus ___5___ the next step." His words encouraged me ___6___ (great).

We rested under a big tree and drank some water. An old man ___7___ was climbing alone passed by us and said with a smile, "Young man, the view at the top is worth all the effort!" I felt ___8___ (shame) of myself for wanting to quit so easily.

After another hour of climbing, we ___9___ (final) reached the top. The view was breathtaking — I could see the entire city below, and the clouds were floating just above ___10___ (we) heads. At that moment, I understood that the best things in life often come after the hardest struggles.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：highest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词**（最……的之一），`high` 的最高级为 **highest**。

2. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。表示"在几点钟"用介词 **at**，`at seven o'clock` 表示"在七点"。

3. **答案：energetic**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。系动词 `felt` 后接形容词作表语，**energy** 的形容词形式为 **energetic**（精力充沛的）。

4. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**want to do sth.**（想要做某事），故填 **to give**。

5. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。**focus on...**（专注于……）为固定搭配，故填 **on**。

6. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `encouraged` 需用副词，**great** 的副词形式为 **greatly**，表示"极大地鼓励了我"。

7. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `An old man` 指人，在从句中作主语，故用关系代词 **who** 或 **that**。

8. **答案：ashamed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。**feel ashamed of...**（对……感到羞愧），**shame** 的形容词形式为 **ashamed**，表示人的内心感受。

9. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `reached` 需用副词，**final** 的副词形式为 **finally**，表示"终于到达"。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词（物主代词）。修饰名词 `heads` 需用形容词性物主代词，`we` 的形容词性物主代词为 **our**。
