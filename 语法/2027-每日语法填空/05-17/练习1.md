# 05-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：文化多样性 (Cultural Diversity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Living in ___1___ city with people from different backgrounds helps us see the world in a wider way and learn to appreciate small differences. In our school, students celebrate various festivals, ___2___ they can taste food, enjoy music, and learn simple phrases in another language. ___3___ teacher once said that respect is the key to understanding, and patience is just as important as knowledge. When we listen to ___4___ (difference) opinions calmly, we discover new ideas instead of ___5___ (argue). Last term, our class ___6___ (hold) a culture fair where each group introduced ___7___ tradition from another country and shared photos and stories. The event ___8___ (prove) that curiosity can bring students closer together. Cultural diversity is not ___9___ problem to solve; it is ___10___ gift that makes our community richer and more colorful if we keep an open mind.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。泛指“一座有多种背景人群的城市”，city 可数，用不定冠词 a。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句修饰先行词 festivals，在从句中作地点状语，表示“在这些节日里”，用 where。

3. **答案：A**
   - **考点**：冠词。泛指“一位老师”，teacher 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 A，句首大写。

4. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 opinions，用形容词 different，表示“不同的观点”。

5. **答案：arguing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。介词 instead of 后接动名词，表示“而不是争论”，故用 arguing。

6. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 Last term 表示过去，谓语动词用一般过去时 held。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。表示“一种来自另一国家的传统”，tradition 可数，用不定冠词 a。

8. **答案：proved**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去文化周的结果，用一般过去时 proved。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。problem 可数，表示“一个问题”，用不定冠词 a。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。gift 可数，表示“一份礼物”，用不定冠词 a。
