# 05-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：智能手表 (Smartwatches)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Smartwatches have become popular among teenagers because they are more than ___1___ tool for telling time. Many models can track steps, measure heart rate, and even remind users ___2___ drink water during busy study days when they forget to rest. ___3___ some people worry that students may depend on screens too much, others believe smart features help build ___4___ (health) habits if they are used with self-control. My cousin bought ___5___ smartwatch last year, and he says it encourages him ___6___ (go) cycling after dinner. However, we should remember ___7___ (not play) with notifications in class, ___8___ it is impolite and distracting for classmates and teachers. Schools often ask students ___9___ (turn) off alarms during exams. If used wisely, technology can support our goals ___10___ causing serious problems in our daily life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。tool 可数，表示“不仅仅是一种看时间的工具”，用不定冠词 a。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。固定结构 remind sb. to do sth.，表示“提醒某人做某事”，用不定式符号 to。

3. **答案：Although**
   - **考点**：连词。引导让步状语从句，表示“尽管有些人担心学生过度依赖屏幕”，主句用 others believe 表转折对比，Although 最常用。

4. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 habits，用形容词 healthy，表示“健康的习惯”。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。泛指“一块智能手表”，smartwatch 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 a。

6. **答案：to go**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定结构 encourage sb. to do sth.，表示“鼓励某人做某事”，用不定式 to go。

7. **答案：not to play**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。remember not to do sth. 表示“记得不要做某事”，否定词 not 置于不定式前。

8. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：连词。后文解释课堂上玩通知不礼貌的原因，引导原因状语从句，用 because/as/since。

9. **答案：to turn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定结构 ask sb. to do sth.，表示“要求某人做某事”，用不定式 to turn。

10. **答案：without**
    - **考点**：介词。表示“在不给……造成严重问题的前提下支持目标”，用 without，后接动名词 causing。
