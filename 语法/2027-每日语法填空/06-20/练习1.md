# 06-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：父亲节 (Father's Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Father's Day falls ___1___ the third Sunday of June every year. My father is a quiet man who ___2___ (rare) shows his emotions. He works as an engineer and often comes home late, looking ___3___ (tire). When I was young, I didn't understand why he was always so busy. But now I know he has been working hard ___4___ (provide) a better life for our family. Last Father's Day, I wanted to do something special ___5___ him. I woke up early and made breakfast by ___6___ (I). The eggs were a little burnt and the toast was too ___7___ (salt), but my father ate everything with a big smile. After breakfast, I gave him a hand-written letter ___8___ (express) my gratitude. As he read it, I noticed his eyes ___9___ (fill) with tears. He hugged me tightly and said, "This is the ___10___ (good) gift I have ever received." That moment meant the world to me.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。在具体某一天用介词 on，on the third Sunday of June。

2. **答案：rarely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 shows，需用副词形式，rare → rarely。

3. **答案：tired**
   - **考点**：词性转换。looking 为系动词，后接形容词作表语，描述人的状态用 tired（感到疲倦的）。

4. **答案：to provide**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了给家人提供更好的生活"，填 to provide。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。do something special for him 表示"为他做一些特别的事"，填 for。

6. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词。by myself 表示"独自"，主语为 I，对应反身代词 myself。

7. **答案：salty**
   - **考点**：词性转换。too + 形容词，表示"太咸了"，salt → salty。

8. **答案：expressing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作定语/状语，a hand-written letter expressing my gratitude 表示"表达感恩的手写信"，填 expressing。

9. **答案：were filled**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。eyes 与 fill 之间为被动关系，叙述过去时刻用一般过去时的被动语态，be filled with tears 表示"充满泪水"。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级 + 名词 + I have ever received，good → best。
