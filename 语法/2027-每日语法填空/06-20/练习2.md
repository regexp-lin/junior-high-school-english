# 06-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：沙漠中的绿洲 (An Oasis in the Desert)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's Kubuqi Desert was once one of the ___1___ (dry) and most lifeless places in the country. Strong sandstorms often swept across the land, ___2___ (destroy) crops and homes in nearby villages. For decades, local people struggled ___3___ (survive) in such harsh conditions. Then, in the 1980s, a group of ___4___ (determine) workers began planting trees along the desert's edge. The work was ___5___ (incredible) tough — many seedlings died in the first year. But they didn't give ___6___. Year after year, they kept planting. Today, over one ___7___ (three) of the desert has been turned into green land. This ___8___ (remark) achievement has attracted attention from around the world. The United Nations praised it ___9___ a model for fighting desertification. The story of Kubuqi teaches us ___10___ with enough determination, even the impossible can become possible.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：driest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词，与 most lifeless 并列，dry → driest。

2. **答案：destroying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作结果状语，sandstorms swept..., destroying crops and homes...，填 destroying。

3. **答案：to survive**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。struggle to do sth. 表示"努力做某事"，填 to survive。

4. **答案：determined**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 workers，determined 为形容词，表示"坚定的"，determine → determined。

5. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 tough，需用副词形式，incredible → incredibly。

6. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。give up 表示"放弃"，固定搭配，填 up。

7. **答案：third**
   - **考点**：分数表达。one third 表示"三分之一"，three → third。

8. **答案：remarkable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 achievement，需用形容词形式，remark → remarkable。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。praise sth. as 表示"称赞某事为"，填 as。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：连词。teaches us that... 引导宾语从句，表示"教会了我们……"，填 that。
