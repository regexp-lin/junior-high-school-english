# 12-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：无人超市体验 (Experiencing an Unmanned Supermarket)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, I visited an unmanned supermarket near my home. It was __1__ (complete) different from traditional shops. There were no cashiers or shop assistants. __2__ (enter) the store, I had to scan a QR code on my phone. Inside, cameras and sensors tracked __3__ items I picked up. The shelves were well organised and the products were __4__ (clear) labelled with prices. I chose some snacks and a bottle of juice. When I walked __5__ the exit gate, the payment was __6__ (automatic) deducted from my account. The whole __7__ (shop) experience took less than five minutes! My father told me that this kind of store __8__ (use) artificial intelligence and big data to operate. He thinks unmanned supermarkets will become __9__ (common) in the future as technology continues to develop. Although it was convenient, I have to say I missed the friendly "hello" from a real shop assistant. Technology makes life __10__ (easy), but human connection is still important.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 different，用副词 completely。

2. **答案：To enter**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。To enter the store 为了进入商店，不定式作目的状语。

3. **答案：which/what**
   - **考点**：宾语从句/定语从句。tracked which/what items I picked up 追踪我拿了哪些商品，which/what 引导宾语从句。

4. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 labelled，用副词 clearly。

5. **答案：through**
   - **考点**：介词。walked through the exit gate 走过出口闸门，through 表示"穿过"。

6. **答案：automatically**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 deducted，用副词 automatically。

7. **答案：shopping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作定语）。shopping experience 购物体验，动名词修饰名词表示用途。

8. **答案：uses**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。this kind of store uses 这种商店使用，描述客观事实用一般现在时，主语为第三人称单数。

9. **答案：more common**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。become more common 变得更普遍，多音节形容词用 more + 原级构成比较级。

10. **答案：easier**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。makes life easier 使生活更容易，"make + 宾语 + 比较级"结构。
