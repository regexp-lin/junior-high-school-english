# 12-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家庭大扫除迎新年 (Family Cleaning for the New Year)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In China, it is a tradition to do a thorough house cleaning before the New Year. People believe that __1__ (clean) the house can sweep away bad luck and make room __2__ good fortune in the coming year. Yesterday, my whole family __3__ (take) part in the big clean-up. My father washed all the windows until they were __4__ (spot) clean. My mother tidied up the kitchen and threw away things we no longer __5__ (need). I was responsible __6__ organising my bookshelf and cleaning my bedroom. My little sister helped by __7__ (dust) the furniture, although she made more mess than she cleaned! After working __8__ (busy) for the whole afternoon, our home looked brand new. We also put up red couplets on the front door and hung __9__ (colour) lanterns in the living room. Standing in the middle of our fresh, bright home, I felt a sense of __10__ (satisfy). It is amazing how a clean home can lift everyone's spirits and fill the house with hope for the new year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：cleaning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。cleaning the house 打扫房子，动名词短语作主语。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。make room for 为……腾出空间，for 表示"为了"。

3. **答案：took**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Yesterday 昨天，描述过去发生的事用一般过去时 took。

4. **答案：spotlessly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→副词）。spotlessly clean 一尘不染，修饰形容词 clean 用副词 spotlessly。

5. **答案：needed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。we no longer needed 我们不再需要的，主句为过去时，从句也用过去时。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。be responsible for 负责……，为固定搭配。

7. **答案：dusting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。by dusting 通过掸灰尘，by 为介词，后接动名词。

8. **答案：busily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 working，用副词 busily。

9. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。colourful lanterns 五彩的灯笼，修饰名词用形容词。

10. **答案：satisfaction**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a sense of satisfaction 一种满足感，of 后接名词。
