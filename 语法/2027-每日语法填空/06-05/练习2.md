# 06-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：网络安全 (Internet Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Internet has brought us great convenience, ___1___ it also comes with risks. Every year, thousands of teenagers fall victim ___2___ online scams. Some websites look professional but are actually ___3___ (design) to steal personal information. Experts advise us never ___4___ (share) passwords or home addresses with strangers online. If someone you don't know sends you a ___5___ (usual) message, the safest thing to do is ignore it. My school ___6___ (recent) held a talk on Internet safety. The speaker told us a true story about a student ___7___ lost a lot of money after clicking on a fake link. It was a ___8___ (power) reminder that we should always think twice before ___9___ (act) online. Learning to protect ___10___ (we) in the digital world is just as important as staying safe in the real one.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。"带来便利"与"也有风险"之间为转折关系，用 but。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。fall victim to 表示"成为……的受害者"，固定搭配，填 to。

3. **答案：designed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be designed to do sth. 表示"被设计来做某事"，are 后接过去分词构成被动语态，填 designed。

4. **答案：to share**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。advise sb. (never) to do sth. 表示"建议某人（绝不）做某事"，填 to share。

5. **答案：unusual**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 message，根据上下文语境（陌生人发来的消息应为"不寻常的"），usual → unusual，加否定前缀。

6. **答案：recently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 held，需用副词形式，recent → recently。

7. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a student（人），在从句中作主语，用 who 引导定语从句。

8. **答案：powerful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 reminder，需用形容词形式，power → powerful。

9. **答案：acting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。before 为介词，后接动名词，act → acting。

10. **答案：ourselves**
    - **考点**：代词。protect oneself 表示"保护自己"，主语为 we，对应反身代词 ourselves。
