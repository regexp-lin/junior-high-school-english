# 06-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：博物馆参观 (Visiting a Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Museums are wonderful places ___1___ we can learn about history, art and science. Last month, our class took a trip to the Guangdong Museum. A guide ___2___ (lead) us through several exhibition halls, each with a different theme. The ancient pottery section was ___3___ (particular) impressive. Some pieces were over two thousand ___4___ (year) old, yet they still looked ___5___ (colour) and elegant. We also watched a short film about how silk ___6___ (produce) in ancient China. It was ___7___ (hard) than I expected to understand all the steps involved. ___8___ caught my attention most was a bronze sword from the Warring States period. The guide said it ___9___ (discover) by a farmer while digging in his field. I took many photos and decided to read more about Chinese history ___10___ I got home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 places（地点），在从句中作地点状语，用 where 引导定语从句。

2. **答案：led**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文叙述过去事件（last month），lead → led。

3. **答案：particularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 impressive，需用副词形式，particular → particularly。

4. **答案：years**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。two thousand 后接可数名词复数，year → years。

5. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。looked 为系动词，后接形容词作表语，colour → colourful。

6. **答案：was produced**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。silk 与 produce 之间为被动关系，描述古代的事用一般过去时的被动语态，填 was produced。

7. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，hard → harder。

8. **答案：What**
   - **考点**：连词。What 引导主语从句，表示"最吸引我注意力的东西"，what = the thing that，句首大写，填 What。

9. **答案：had been discovered**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。it 与 discover 之间为被动关系，said 为过去时，发现发生在"说"之前，用过去完成时的被动语态 had been discovered。

10. **答案：after/when**
    - **考点**：连词。表示"回到家后"，引导时间状语从句，填 after 或 when。
