# 03-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：国际妇女节 (International Women's Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

March 8th is International Women's Day, a day to celebrate the (1) ______ (achieve) of women around the world. On this special day, many schools and companies hold activities (2) ______ (show) respect for women.

This year, our school organized a speech competition with the topic "The Woman I Admire Most". I chose to talk about my grandmother. She grew up in a small village where very (3) ______ girls had the chance to go to school. Despite the difficulties, she taught (4) ______ (she) to read and write. Later, she became a village doctor and spent her whole life (5) ______ (help) others.

My classmate Li Hua talked about Tu Youyou, the Chinese scientist (6) ______ won the Nobel Prize in 2015. He explained how she (7) ______ (discover) a treatment for malaria after years of research. Her story inspired all of us to work hard and never give (8) ______.

After the competition, our teacher reminded us that Women's Day is not just about giving (9) ______ (flower) or gifts. It is about recognising the important role women play in society and (10) ______ (treat) them with equal respect every day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：achievements**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`the achievements of women` 女性的成就，`achieve` → `achievement`，复数。

2. **答案：to show**
   - **考点**：不定式作目的状语。举办活动的目的是表达尊重。

3. **答案：few**
   - **考点**：限定词。`very few girls` 极少数女孩，`few` 修饰可数名词复数，表否定。

4. **答案：herself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`taught herself` 自学，主语 `she` 对应 `herself`。

5. **答案：helping**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time (in) doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

6. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰 `the Chinese scientist`，在从句中作主语。

7. **答案：had discovered / discovered**
   - **考点**：过去完成时 / 一般过去时。在获奖之前已经发现了治疗方法。

8. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`give up` 放弃。

9. **答案：flowers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`giving flowers` 送花，用复数。

10. **答案：treating**
    - **考点**：动名词。与 `recognising` 并列，作 `about` 的宾语。
