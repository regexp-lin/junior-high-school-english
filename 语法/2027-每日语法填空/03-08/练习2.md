# 03-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园农场 (School Farm)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has a small farm behind the teaching building. It (1) ______ (set) up three years ago as part of a project to help students learn about agriculture. Each class is given a small piece of land to grow (2) ______ (vegetable).

Every Tuesday afternoon, we go to the farm to take care of our plants. At the beginning, none of us knew how (3) ______ (plant) seeds properly. We either put too many seeds in one hole (4) ______ forgot to water them regularly. Our science teacher, Ms. Chen, patiently showed us the correct way to do everything.

After weeks of hard work, we were (5) ______ (delight) to see tiny green shoots coming out of the soil. It felt like watching our own children (6) ______ (grow) up. By the end of the semester, we had harvested tomatoes, cucumbers and some of the (7) ______ (big) carrots I had ever seen.

The school farm has taught me several important (8) ______ (lesson). First, I learned that growing food requires patience and effort. Second, I developed a deeper (9) ______ (appreciate) for farmers and the food on our table. Most (10) ______ (important), I realized that nature can bring us great joy if we treat it with care.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was set**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。`three years ago` 过去时间，农场是"被建立的"。

2. **答案：vegetables**
   - **考点**：名词复数。种植各种蔬菜，用复数。

3. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：不定式。`know how to do sth.` 知道如何做某事。

4. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`either ... or ...` 要么……要么……。

5. **答案：delighted**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。`be delighted to do sth.` 高兴地做某事，修饰人的感受。

6. **答案：grow**
   - **考点**：省略 to 的不定式作宾补。`watch sb./sth. do sth.` 看着……做某事。

7. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`some of the biggest carrots I had ever seen` 我见过的最大的胡萝卜。

8. **答案：lessons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several important lessons` 几个重要的教训/经验。

9. **答案：appreciation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a deeper appreciation` 更深的感激/欣赏，名词形式。

10. **答案：importantly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`Most importantly` 最重要的是，副词修饰整个句子。
