# 11-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：古建筑修复 (Restoring Ancient Buildings)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ancient buildings are important parts of our __1__ (culture) heritage. They tell stories about history and the __2__ (wise) of our ancestors. However, many old buildings in China are in poor condition and need __3__ (repair). Last summer, I visited a 300-year-old temple that __4__ (restore) by a team of skilled craftsmen. The project __5__ (take) three years to complete. The head craftsman explained that they used traditional __6__ (method) and materials, such as lime mortar and wooden joints. No nails __7__ (use) in the original structure, which showed amazing engineering skills. He told me that the __8__ (big) challenge was finding young people willing to learn these ancient techniques. Most young craftsmen today prefer working __9__ modern buildings because the pay is higher. Without new trainees, these traditional skills may be __10__ (lose) forever. I think schools should teach students about the importance of preserving cultural heritage.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：cultural**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 heritage，culture 的形容词形式为 cultural。

2. **答案：wisdom**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。the wisdom of our ancestors 祖先的智慧，wise 的名词形式为 wisdom。

3. **答案：repairing / to be repaired**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。need doing = need to be done 需要被修缮，用 repairing 或 to be repaired。

4. **答案：was being restored / had been restored**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。temple 与 restore 为被动关系，表示参观时正在修复或已被修复，用 was being restored 或 had been restored。

5. **答案：took**
   - **考点**：动词时态。It took + 时间 + to do sth. 花了多长时间做某事，过去时态用 took。

6. **答案：methods**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。traditional methods 传统方法，此处指多种方法，用复数 methods。

7. **答案：were used**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 no nails 与 use 为被动关系，过去时态用 were used。

8. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，big 的最高级为 biggest（双写 g 加 -est）。

9. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。work on modern buildings 从事现代建筑的工作，on 表示"从事"。

10. **答案：lost**
    - **考点**：动词语态。may be lost 可能会失传，be + 过去分词构成被动语态，lose 的过去分词为 lost。
