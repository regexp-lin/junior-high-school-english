# 11-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界善意日 (World Kindness Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

World Kindness Day is celebrated __1__ November 13th every year. It reminds us that small acts of kindness can make a big __2__ (differ) in people's lives. Last year, our school launched __3__ "Kindness Week" campaign. Students were encouraged __4__ (do) at least one kind thing each day. Some helped __5__ (young) students carry heavy schoolbags. Others wrote thank-you notes to teachers and cleaning staff. I decided to bake cookies and give __6__ (they) to the security guards who stand at the gate every morning. When I handed over the box, the guard's eyes __7__ (light) up with surprise and joy. He said nobody had ever done that __8__ him before. My friend Zhang Lei started a "compliment wall" __9__ students could post nice messages about their classmates. Within three days, the wall was covered __10__ (complete) with colourful notes. The week showed us that kindness is contagious — one good deed often inspires another.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on + 具体日期，on November 13th 在11月13日。

2. **答案：difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。make a big difference 产生重大影响，differ 的名词形式为 difference。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一个善良周活动"，用 a。

4. **答案：to do**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be encouraged to do sth. 被鼓励做某事，用不定式 to do。

5. **答案：younger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。younger students 更年幼的学生，暗含与自己相比更小。

6. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。give 后接宾语，指代 cookies，用 them。

7. **答案：lit / lighted**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文为过去时态，light up 的过去式为 lit 或 lighted。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。done that for him 为他做过那样的事，for 表示"为"。

9. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 wall 为地点名词，从句不缺主宾语，用 where。

10. **答案：completely**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰过去分词 covered，用副词 completely。
