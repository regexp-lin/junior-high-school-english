# 07-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：英语角活动 (English Corner Activities)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school runs a lively English Corner ___1___ the reading room every week. Teachers prepare posters, soft music, and simple snacks so that the space feels warm and welcoming. This meaningful activity ___2___ (hold) on Wednesday afternoons, and about forty students from different grades usually ___3___ (take) part in the discussions, role-play games, and quick debates. Sometimes we talk about films, school trips, or interesting news from English websites. At first, some newcomers were too shy to speak ___4___ they worried that others might laugh at their pronunciation or grammar errors.

Ms. Chen, ___5___ experienced English teacher, told us that making mistakes was a natural part of learning. She also reminded us that listening carefully was as important as speaking. She encouraged everyone ___6___ (practice) short dialogues in pairs before joining larger groups. Gradually, we found that communicating in English was not as ___7___ (difficulty) as we had imagined, and every small success brought smiles. Many students even became ___8___ (confidence) enough to perform short skits in front of their classmates at the end of each month. Their clear voices and creative lines often drew warm applause from the audience.

Last term, our whole class ___9___ (invite) to share useful learning tips with Grade 7 students. We introduced simple habits such as reading aloud for ten minutes a day and keeping a small notebook for new phrases. We felt truly proud ___10___ we could help them take the first brave step into spoken English and see their eyes light up with interest.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。表示活动发生的地点，“在阅览室里”用介词 *in*，与 *the reading room* 构成地点状语。易混点：*at* 多指较小或较具体的点，*in* 强调在某一空间内部。

2. **答案：is held**
   - **考点**：一般现在时的被动语态。主语 *This meaningful activity* 与动词 *hold* 之间是被动关系（活动被举办），且句中有时间状语 *every week / on Wednesday afternoons*，表示经常性安排，故用一般现在时被动语态 *is held*。

3. **答案：take**
   - **考点**：一般现在时与主谓一致。主语 *about forty students* 为复数，且描述常态，谓语用动词原形 *take*；固定搭配 *take part in* 表示“参加”。

4. **答案：because**
   - **考点**：连词。前句说新生太害羞不敢开口，后句解释原因（担心被嘲笑），前后为因果关系，用从属连词 *because* 引导原因状语从句。

5. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。*experienced* 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，表示“一位有经验的英语老师”需用 *an*，而非 *a*。

6. **答案：to practice**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动词不定式）。固定结构 *encourage sb. to do sth.*（鼓励某人做某事），此处用不定式 *to practice* 作宾语补足语。

7. **答案：difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换与形容词原级。结构 *not as … as* 中间用形容词或副词原级；*was* 为系动词，后接形容词作表语，故将名词 *difficulty* 转为形容词 *difficult*。

8. **答案：confident**
   - **考点**：词性转换。系动词 *became* 后需形容词作表语，*confidence* 的形容词形式为 *confident*；*confident enough to do sth.* 表示“足够自信去做某事”。

9. **答案：was invited**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。时间状语 *Last term* 表明过去；主语 *our whole class* 与 *invite* 为被动关系（班级被邀请），且 *class* 此处强调整体，谓语常用单数，故用 *was invited*。

10. **答案：when**
    - **考点**：连词。*felt proud* 后接时间状语从句，说明“在能够帮助他们的那一刻感到自豪”，用 *when* 引导时间状语从句；若理解为原因也可用 *because*，但此处强调“在……时候”更贴合语境与中考常见考法。
