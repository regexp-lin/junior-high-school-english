# 07-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国武术 (Chinese Martial Arts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese martial arts, ___1___ are often called kung fu in English, have impressed people around the world with their beauty and spirit. International audiences are often surprised by the balance between speed and control that performers show on stage or in training halls. The earliest reliable records ___2___ (date) back more than a thousand years, and these arts ___3___ (develop) slowly into hundreds of styles over time. Each style has its own footwork, hand forms, and training ideas that pass carefully from teachers to students. ___4___ someone practices slowly in a quiet park at sunrise, another person may train for hours in a noisy professional school in the afternoon. Young learners may begin with basic stances, while older masters still repeat simple moves to refine their skills.

In modern life, martial arts are still seen ___5___ a meaningful way to stay ___6___ (health) and build both physical power and inner ___7___ (strong). Community centers and school clubs now offer short courses for people who sit for long hours at work or study. The ___8___ (popular) of Chinese kung fu has risen sharply abroad since classic action films spread ___9___ (wide) across many countries in the 1970s and 1980s. Today thousands of foreign learners fly to China every year ___10___ (learn) traditional skills from local masters. They also want to understand ancient culture and take part in friendly competitions that welcome beginners as well as experts. For many of them, the journey is not only about fighting moves but also about patience and respect.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。逗号后为非限制性定语从句，先行词为 *Chinese martial arts*（物），从句缺主语，故用 *which* 引导；*that* 不能用于非限制性定语从句。

2. **答案：date**
   - **考点**：一般现在时与固定搭配。主语 *The earliest reliable records* 为复数，叙述客观事实用一般现在时；固定短语 *date back (to …)* 表示“追溯到……”，此处用动词原形 *date*。

3. **答案：have developed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。时间状语 *over time* 强调从过去持续到现在的变化过程，主语 *these arts* 为复数，故用现在完成时 *have developed*，表示武术已经发展成多种流派。

4. **答案：While**
   - **考点**：连词。前后两句对比两种不同场景（一人在公园慢练、另一人在武校长时间训练），用 *While* 引导让步或对比状语从句，意为“而；当……时；尽管”，句首首字母大写。

5. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 *be seen as* 表示“被视为；被看作”，说明武术在当代生活中被看作一种有意义的锻炼方式。

6. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。系动词 *stay* 在此表示“保持（某种状态）”，后接形容词；*health* 的形容词形式为 *healthy*，*stay healthy* 意为“保持健康”。

7. **答案：strength**
   - **考点**：词性转换。形容词 *inner* 修饰名词，与 *physical power* 并列；*strong* 的名词形式为 *strength*（不可数），表示“内在力量、意志力”。

8. **答案：popularity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。定冠词 *The* 后需名词作主语；*popular* 的名词形式为 *popularity*，*the popularity of …* 表示“……的流行程度”。

9. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 *spread* 需用副词；*wide* 的副词形式为 *widely*，*spread widely* 表示“广泛传播”。

10. **答案：to learn**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（动词不定式）。*fly to China* 后常用不定式表目的，说明“来中国是为了学习传统技艺”，故填 *to learn*；相当于 *in order to learn* 的省略形式，为中考高频结构。
