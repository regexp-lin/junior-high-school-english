# 04-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：科技馆参观 (Science Museum Visit)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Yesterday our grade visited the science museum, (1) ______ we explored interactive exhibits about space, robots, and clean energy. The guide led us into a dark room (2) ______ a huge model of the Earth hung from the ceiling.

We tried simple experiments (3) ______ (understand) how electricity travels through wires. My favorite part was the VR area, (4) ______ we could "walk" on the surface of Mars. When I took off the headset, I felt a little dizzy, (5) ______ I still wanted to try again.

The museum staff explained that the exhibits (6) ______ (update) every month so that visitors would always see something new. Before we left, we attended a short lecture (7) ______ a scientist shared tips on studying physics. I took many photos (8) ______ I could show my parents at dinner.

This trip made science feel (9) ______ (close) to daily life than before. I hope our school will organize (10) ______ visit next term.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。非限制性定语从句修饰 `museum`，从句中缺地点状语，“在馆内我们探索……”，用 `where`。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `room`，从句中缺地点状语，“房间里悬挂着地球模型”，用 `where`。

3. **答案：to understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，“做实验以理解”。

4. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。非限制性定语从句修饰 `area`，从句中缺地点状语，“在 VR 区可以体验”，用 `where`。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“有点晕”与“仍想再试”为转折。

6. **答案：are updated**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。展品“每月被更新”，陈述客观安排，用 `are updated`。

7. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `lecture`，从句中缺时间状语，“在讲座期间科学家分享”，用 `when`。

8. **答案：so that/in order that**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导目的状语从句，“拍照以便晚餐时给父母看”，用 `so that`。

9. **答案：closer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`feel` 后接形容词比较级，`close` → `closer`。

10. **答案：another**
    - **考点**：限定词。`organize another visit` 再组织一次参观，表泛指又一次。
