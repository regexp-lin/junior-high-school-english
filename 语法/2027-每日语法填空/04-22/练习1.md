# 04-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：非遗传承 (Intangible Cultural Heritage)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Intangible cultural heritage includes traditional music, crafts, and festivals that communities pass down from generation (1) ______ generation. In our city, several young artists have decided (2) ______ (learn) old skills from elderly masters because they worry that some traditions might disappear.

Last weekend, I watched a puppet show (3) ______ a young performer told stories of heroes with colorful wooden figures. The strings moved so smoothly that the audience (4) ______ (forget) they were watching puppets, not real actors. After the show, the artist explained how the puppets (5) ______ (make) and painted by hand.

She told us that every performance (6) ______ (practice) at least a hundred times before it could tour schools. "Heritage is alive only (7) ______ people keep practicing it," she said. I felt deeply touched (8) ______ her words.

I used to think heritage was something distant in museums, (9) ______ now I see it can live in classrooms and streets. I hope I will be brave enough (10) ______ (spread) what I love about our culture to others.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`from generation to generation` 一代又一代。

2. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`decide to do sth.` 决定做某事。

3. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `puppet show`，从句中缺地点状语，“在演出中年轻人讲述故事”，用 `where`。

4. **答案：forgot**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去周末演出，`so... that` 结果状语从句中谓语用过去式 `forgot`。

5. **答案：were made**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。木偶“被制作并手工上色”，主语 `puppets` 复数，用 `were made`。

6. **答案：had been practiced**
   - **考点**：过去完成时被动语态。`before it could tour` 表示在巡演前已练习，过去的过去且被动，用 `had been practiced`；也可用 `was practiced` 若强调单数。

7. **答案：as long as/if**
   - **考点**：从属连词。`only if` 或 `as long as` 表示“只要人们坚持练习”，表条件。

8. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`be touched by...` 被……打动。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾以为遥远”与“现在看到可以走进课堂与街道”为转折。

10. **答案：to spread**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`adj. + enough + to do` 足够……去做某事。
