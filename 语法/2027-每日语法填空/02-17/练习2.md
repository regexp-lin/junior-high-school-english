# 02-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园篮球赛 (School Basketball Tournament)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Basketball is the most popular sport at our school, and every spring we hold a class tournament that lasts for two (1) ______ (week). This year, our class team made it all the way to the final, (2) ______ was an incredible achievement considering we had lost in the first round last year. Our team captain, Zhang Hao, is the (3) ______ (tall) player in our grade and an excellent leader. He encouraged everyone to practise hard, and we trained every afternoon for a month before the tournament (4) ______ (begin). In the final match, we played against Class 5, who (5) ______ (win) the championship for the past three years. The game was extremely intense. By half-time, we were losing by eight points, and some of my teammates looked (6) ______ (disappoint). During the break, Zhang Hao gave us a short but powerful speech. He reminded us that we had worked too hard (7) ______ (give) up now. His words lifted our spirits. In the second half, we played with renewed energy and (8) ______ (gradual) closed the gap. With only ten seconds left, our point guard Li Chen made an (9) ______ (believe) three-point shot to win the game. The entire class rushed onto the court to celebrate. That day, I truly understood (10) ______ teamwork and determination mean.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：weeks**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`two weeks` 两周。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面进入决赛这件事。

3. **答案：tallest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the tallest player in our grade`。

4. **答案：began**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`before the tournament began` 在锦标赛开始前。

5. **答案：had won**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`for the past three years` 在此之前连续赢了三年。

6. **答案：disappointed**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。`looked disappointed` 看起来很失望，描述人。

7. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`too... to do` 太……而不能。

8. **答案：gradually**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`gradually closed the gap` 逐渐缩小差距。

9. **答案：unbelievable**
   - **考点**：词性转换/前缀。`an unbelievable shot` 难以置信的投篮。

10. **答案：what**
    - **考点**：连接代词。`understood what teamwork and determination mean`，引导宾语从句。
