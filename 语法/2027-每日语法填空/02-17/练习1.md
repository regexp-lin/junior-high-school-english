# 02-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：智能家居 (Smart Home)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Smart home technology has developed rapidly in recent years, making our daily lives more (1) ______ (convenience). With just a voice command or a tap on your smartphone, you can control almost everything in your house — from lights and air conditioners (2) ______ door locks and security cameras. My family moved into a new apartment last year, and my father decided to turn it (3) ______ a smart home. He installed smart speakers in every room, which allow us (4) ______ (play) music, check the weather and set alarms simply by speaking. The smart lighting system is my favourite feature. It can (5) ______ (programme) to change colours based on the time of day — warm yellow in the evening and bright white in the morning. My mother was a bit worried about privacy (6) ______ first. She did not feel comfortable having devices that were always (7) ______ (listen). However, after learning how to adjust the privacy settings, she became much more (8) ______ (relax). The technology is not perfect, though. Sometimes the voice assistant (9) ______ (misunderstand) our commands and does something completely unexpected. Last week, when I asked it to turn off the bedroom light, it started playing loud music at midnight instead! Despite these occasional (10) ______ (problem), I believe smart home technology will continue to improve and become a standard part of modern living.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：convenient**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more convenient`，系动词后用形容词。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`from... to...` 从……到……。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`turn sth. into` 把某物变成。

4. **答案：to play**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`allow sb. to do sth.` 允许某人做某事。

5. **答案：be programmed**
   - **考点**：含情态动词的被动语态。`can be programmed` 能被编程。

6. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`at first` 起初。

7. **答案：listening**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`were always listening`，过去进行时表示总是在听。

8. **答案：relaxed**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。`became more relaxed`，描述人的状态。

9. **答案：misunderstands**
   - **考点**：一般现在时三单。`Sometimes` 表示有时会发生的事。

10. **答案：problems**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`these occasional problems` 这些偶尔的问题。
