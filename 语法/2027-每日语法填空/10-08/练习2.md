# 10-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：大气保护 (Atmosphere Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The atmosphere protects Earth __1__ harmful rays from space. __2__ ozone layer is like a thin shield high above us. If CFCs were still widely used, the hole over Antarctica __3__ (grow) even larger. Luckily, many countries __4__ (ban) dangerous chemicals since the 1980s. Teenagers can help __5__ choosing buses over private cars when possible. Our science teacher told us __6__ trees absorb CO₂ while they make food in sunlight. Students also painted posters __7__ (call) for cleaner air last term. Clean air is not __8__ gift from nature alone; it requires daily care. __9__ everyone tries a little harder, blue skies will return more often. I wrote __10__ my notebook that protecting the atmosphere starts with small choices.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。protect... from... 保护……免受……的伤害。

2. **答案：The**
   - **考点**：定冠词。the ozone layer 臭氧层，为独一无二事物，用定冠词 the，句首大写。

3. **答案：would grow**
   - **考点**：动词时态。If 引导与现在事实相反的虚拟条件（were still used），主句用 would + 动词原形，表示推测结果 would grow。

4. **答案：have banned**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since the 1980s 为现在完成时标志，主语 countries 为复数，用 have banned。

5. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。by doing sth. 通过做某事，表示方式。

6. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。told us 后接宾语从句，陈述科学事实，用 that 引导。

7. **答案：calling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。posters 与 call for 为主动关系，用现在分词 calling 作后置定语，表示“呼吁更洁净空气的海报”。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。gift 可数名词单数，泛指“一份礼物”，用 a。

9. **答案：If**
   - **考点**：连词。引导条件状语从句，“如果每个人多努力一点，蓝天就会更常见”。

10. **答案：in**
    - **考点**：介词。write in one’s notebook 在笔记本里写下，为固定搭配。
