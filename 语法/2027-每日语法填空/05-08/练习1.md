# 05-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：母亲节 (Mother's Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mother's Day falls 1. ______ the second Sunday of May in many countries. This year, I wanted to do something special because my mom 2. ______ (work) extra hours in March. On Saturday evening, I cleaned the kitchen while she 3. ______ (read) a book in the living room. Then I baked 4. ______ simple cake and wrote "Thank you" on 5. ______ with cream. When she saw the surprise, her eyes turned wet, 6. ______ she smiled and hugged me tightly. "This is 7. ______ (sweet) gift today," she said. We sat together and talked about my childhood photos. I realized 8. ______ love does not always need expensive presents; time and care matter 9. ______ (much). Before going to bed, I promised 10. ______ (help) more with housework from now on.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。具体某一天或“五月的第二个星期日”前用 **on**，故填 **on**。

2. **答案：worked**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去三月发生的事，用一般过去时 **worked**。

3. **答案：was reading**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**while** 引导的时间状语从句表示过去某时刻正在进行的动作，用过去进行时 **was reading**。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **cake** 单数，表泛指，故填 **a**。

5. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。指代 **a simple cake**，作介词 **on** 的宾语，故填 **it**。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前后为转折关系，故填 **but**。

7. **答案：the sweetest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。由范围 **today** 可知用 **sweet** 的最高级 **the sweetest**。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**realized** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

9. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。与昂贵礼物作隐性对比，“时间和关心更重要”，用 **much** 的比较级 **more**。

10. **答案：to help**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**promise to do sth.**，故填 **to help**。
