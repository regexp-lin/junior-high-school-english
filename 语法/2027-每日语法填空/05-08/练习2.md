# 05-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：防灾减灾 (Disaster Prevention)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school takes disaster drills seriously because knowing what 1. ______ do can save lives. Every term, we practice 2. ______ (leave) the building in order when the alarm rings. Teachers remind us 3. ______ cover our heads with bags 4. ______ we stay under desks during an earthquake. Last year, a firefighter visited 5. ______ gave a talk on how 6. ______ (use) a fire extinguisher. He showed us a video about a family 7. ______ escaped safely because they had a plan. After the lesson, we checked the exits in our classroom and made sure nothing 8. ______ (block) the doors. Parents were also invited 9. ______ (attend) a meeting on emergency kits at home. Disasters may not happen often, 10. ______ preparation is always necessary.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：“疑问词 + 不定式”。**what to do** 作 **knowing** 的宾语，故填 **to**。

2. **答案：leaving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**practice doing sth.**，故填 **leaving**。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**remind sb. to do sth.**，故填 **to**。

4. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：连词。**when** 引导时间状语从句，表示“当我们躲在课桌下时”，故填 **when**。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。**visited** 与 **gave** 并列，故填 **and**。

6. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：“疑问词 + 不定式”。**how to use** 作介词 **on** 的宾语，故填 **to use**。

7. **答案：that / who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 **a family**，定语从句中作主语，指人可用 **who**，指整体也可用 **that**，故填 **that** 或 **who**。

8. **答案：blocked**
   - **考点**：动词时态。宾语从句叙述过去检查的结果，**nothing blocked the doors** 表示“没有东西挡住门”，用一般过去时 **blocked**。

9. **答案：to attend**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be invited to do sth.**，故填 **to attend**。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。前后为转折关系，故填 **but**。
