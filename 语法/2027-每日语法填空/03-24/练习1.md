# 03-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国桥梁 (Chinese Bridges)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China is home to some of the longest and (1) ______ (impress) bridges in the world. They cross deep valleys, wide rivers, and busy cities, connecting people and goods.

The Hong Kong–Zhuhai–Macau Bridge, opened in 2018, is one of the (2) ______ (great) sea crossings ever built. Engineers had to solve problems of strong winds and busy shipping lanes. The project took nearly nine years (3) ______ (complete).

In the mountains of Guizhou, high bridges seem to float above the clouds. Workers built them piece by piece in conditions (4) ______ many would find frightening. These bridges have shortened travel time (5) ______ hours to minutes for local villagers.

Ancient stone bridges also tell stories of Chinese skill. The Zhaozhou Bridge, built over 1,400 years ago, is still (6) ______ use today. Its curved design has inspired modern architects around the world.

Bridge building is not only about steel and concrete. It shows how human beings overcome natural barriers (7) ______ (work) together. When I watch documentaries about these projects, I feel proud of my country.

Of course, building bridges costs a lot of money and energy. Planners must think about the environment (8) ______ they start construction. If a new road harms wildlife, better solutions should (9) ______ (find).

I dream of becoming an engineer (10) ______ day and designing bridges that are both safe and beautiful.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most impressive**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。与 `longest` 并列，`one of the + 最高级`，多音节用 most impressive。

2. **答案：greatest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the greatest sea crossings`。

3. **答案：to complete**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`It takes time to complete` 花费时间完成，不定式作主语真正主语。

4. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `conditions`，从句 `many would find frightening` 缺宾语。

5. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`from hours to minutes` 从数小时缩短到数分钟。

6. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`in use` 仍在使用中。

7. **答案：to work**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的：人类克服自然障碍是为了携手合作，`to work together` 作目的状语。

8. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：连词。动工前先考虑环境。

9. **答案：be found**
   - **考点**：含情态动词的被动语态。更好的解决方案应被找到，`should be found`。

10. **答案：one / some**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`one day` 将来某一天；`some day` 亦可。
