# 03-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：模拟联合国 (Model United Nations)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Model United Nations (MUN) is a popular activity in many middle schools. Students play the roles of diplomats (1) ______ discuss global issues such as climate change and education.

Last month, I took part in a MUN conference in our city. Each school represented a different country. I was assigned to speak for Kenya, a country (2) ______ I knew little about before I prepared for the meeting.

The first day was challenging. We had to read long background guides and use formal English (3) ______ (express) our points. I practiced my opening speech again and again (4) ______ I could pronounce every word clearly.

During the debate, students from other teams raised questions about water resources. I listened carefully and took notes (5) ______ (quick). When it was my turn, I suggested building more wells in rural areas and shared data I had found online.

Our teacher said MUN is not about winning arguments. It is about learning to respect others and find common ground. By the end of the third day, we had written a draft resolution (6) ______ (include) ideas from several countries.

The experience made me more interested in international news. I also realized (7) ______ English could be a powerful tool for communication.

If I have another chance, I will choose (8) ______ (difficult) topic than last time so that I can push myself further.

I hope more schools will organize MUN activities (9) ______ students can broaden their horizons without leaving the classroom.

To anyone who feels nervous about public speaking, I would say: take a deep breath and try. You might discover (10) ______ braver side of yourself.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `diplomats` 指人，从句缺主语。

2. **答案：which / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。`a country which/that I knew little about` 先行词指物，关系代词作介词 `about` 的宾语。

3. **答案：to express**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`use English to express` 用正式英语表达观点。

4. **答案：until / so that**
   - **考点**：连词。`until I could pronounce` 直到能读清；或 `so that` 以便。

5. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `took`，`quick` → `quickly`。

6. **答案：including**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作后置定语，`a draft resolution including ideas`。

7. **答案：that / how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句。`realized that English could be ...` 或 `how English could be`。

8. **答案：a more difficult**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than last time` 提示，`a more difficult topic`。

9. **答案：so that**
   - **考点**：目的状语从句。组织活动以便学生开阔眼界。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。`a braver side` 更勇敢的一面。
