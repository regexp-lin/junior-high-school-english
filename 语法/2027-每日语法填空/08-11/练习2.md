# 08-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统中医药 (Traditional Chinese Medicine)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traditional Chinese Medicine, or TCM, is one of the ___1___ (old) medical systems in the world. It has a history of more ___2___ two thousand years. Even today, millions of people in China and other countries still use it to treat various ___3___ (illness).

One of the most important ideas behind TCM is balance. Chinese doctors believe that the human body needs to keep a balance ___4___ yin and yang. When this balance ___5___ (break), people may feel sick. TCM doctors use different methods to help patients get well, ___6___ (include) herbal medicine, acupuncture and massage.

Herbal medicine is probably the most ___7___ (wide) used method. Doctors mix several kinds of herbs together and ask patients ___8___ (drink) them as tea. Each herb has its own special function. Acupuncture, ___9___ uses thin needles to stimulate certain points on the body, is also very popular around the world now.

In recent years, TCM ___10___ (get) more and more attention from international scientists. Many of them are doing research to find out how traditional herbs work. I believe TCM will continue to play an important role in keeping people healthy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词**（最……的之一），`old` 的最高级为 **oldest**。

2. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词/介词。**more than**（超过，多于）为固定搭配，表示"两千多年"的历史，故填 **than**。

3. **答案：illnesses**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`various`（各种各样的）修饰可数名词复数，`illness` 的复数形式为 **illnesses**。

4. **答案：between**
   - **考点**：介词。**a balance between A and B**（A与B之间的平衡），`between... and...` 表示两者之间，故填 **between**。

5. **答案：is broken**
   - **考点**：动词语态。主语 `this balance` 与 `break` 之间为被动关系（平衡"被打破"），在条件状语从句中用一般现在时表将来，故填 **is broken**。

6. **答案：including**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词/介词用法。**including** 在此作介词，意为"包括"，后接具体的方法名称。句中已有谓语 `use`，故逗号后用 **including** 补充说明。

7. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰过去分词 `used` 需用副词，`wide` 的副词形式 **widely** 表示"广泛地"。此处 `the most widely used` 为最高级结构。

8. **答案：to drink**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**ask sb. to do sth.**（要求某人做某事），故填 **to drink**。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `Acupuncture` 指物，逗号后为非限制性定语从句，在从句中作主语，故用 **which**（非限制性定语从句不能用 that）。

10. **答案：has got / has gotten**
    - **考点**：动词时态。**In recent years**（近年来）提示使用现在完成时，主语 `TCM` 为第三人称单数，故填 **has got** 或 **has gotten**。
