# 08-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：周末义卖活动 (Weekend Charity Sale)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our school held a charity sale on the playground. The purpose of the event was ___1___ (raise) money for children in poor mountain areas. Students from every class were encouraged to take part ___2___ it.

My classmates and I spent the whole week ___3___ (prepare) for the sale. Some of us made handmade bookmarks and greeting cards, while ___4___ brought old books and toys that were still in good condition. We set up a colourful booth and decorated it ___5___ balloons and ribbons.

The sale began at nine o'clock in the morning. To our ___6___ (surprising), many parents and teachers also came to support us. A little girl was very interested in a toy bear and asked her mother ___7___ (buy) it for her. By noon, almost everything on our table ___8___ (sell) out. We were the ___9___ (three) class to sell out all the goods, which made us very happy.

In total, our school raised over 20,000 yuan that day. The money will ___10___ (use) to buy school supplies for the children in need. I felt proud to be part of such a meaningful event.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to raise**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**The purpose was to do sth.**（目的是做某事），不定式作表语说明目的，故填 **to raise**。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **take part in...**（参加……），故填 **in**。

3. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定结构 **spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth.**（花时间做某事），故填 **preparing**。

4. **答案：others**
   - **考点**：代词。**some... others...**（一些人……另一些人……）为固定搭配，表示不同的人做不同的事，故填 **others**。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**decorate... with...**（用……装饰……）为固定搭配，故填 **with**。

6. **答案：surprise**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 名词）。固定搭配 **to one's surprise**（令某人惊讶的是），介词 `to` 后需接名词，故将 `surprising` 转换为名词 **surprise**。

7. **答案：to buy**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**ask sb. to do sth.**（请求某人做某事），故填 **to buy**。

8. **答案：had been sold**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。**By noon**（到中午时）表示"过去某时间之前"的动作已完成，用过去完成时；主语 `everything` 与 `sell` 为被动关系，故填 **had been sold**。

9. **答案：third**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词 → 序数词）。**the + 序数词 + 名词 + to do sth.** 表示"第几个做某事的"，`three` 的序数词形式为 **third**。

10. **答案：be used**
    - **考点**：动词语态。情态动词 `will` 后接动词原形；主语 `The money` 与 `use` 之间为被动关系（钱"被用来"购买），故填 **be used**。
