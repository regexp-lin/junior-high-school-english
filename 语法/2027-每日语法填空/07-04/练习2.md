# 07-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：DIY手工制作 (DIY Crafts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

DIY, which stands ___1___ "Do It Yourself", has become a popular hobby among teenagers. Instead of buying expensive gifts, many young people prefer ___2___ (make) things with their own hands. Last week, my art teacher showed us how to turn old T-shirts ___3___ tote bags. We didn't need any ___4___ (specially) tools — just scissors and a marker. The steps were ___5___ (surprise) simple. First, we cut off the sleeves and the neckline. Then we drew lines at the bottom and cut strips along them. Finally, we tied the strips ___6___ (tight) in pairs to close the bottom. Within thirty minutes, each of us had a ___7___ (colour) handmade bag. My classmate Liu Yan added flowers and butterflies to ___8___ (she), making it look like a piece of art. DIY projects not only save money ___9___ also encourage creativity. I have since started watching DIY videos online and have already made several ___10___ (gift) for my friends. There is something special about receiving a handmade present.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。stand for 表示"代表"，固定搭配，填 for。

2. **答案：making/to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。prefer doing/to do sth. 表示"更喜欢做某事"，填 making 或 to make。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。turn A into B 表示"把 A 变成 B"，固定搭配，填 into。

4. **答案：special**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 tools，需用形容词形式，specially → special。

5. **答案：surprisingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 simple，需用副词形式，surprise → surprisingly。

6. **答案：tightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 tied，需用副词形式，tight → tightly。

7. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 handmade bag，需用形容词形式，colour → colourful。

8. **答案：hers**
   - **考点**：代词。added flowers to hers，hers 为名词性物主代词，指代 her bag，she → hers。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。not only...but also... 表示"不仅……而且……"，固定搭配，填 but。

10. **答案：gifts**
    - **考点**：名词单复数。several 后接可数名词复数，gift → gifts。
