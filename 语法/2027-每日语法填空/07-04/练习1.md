# 07-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：游泳安全 (Swimming Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Summer is the best season for swimming, but it is also the time ___1___ drowning accidents happen most often. Every year, hundreds of children lose ___2___ (they) lives in rivers, lakes and pools. Most of these ___3___ (tragedy) could have been avoided if proper safety rules had been followed. First, never swim alone — always go with an adult or ___4___ experienced swimmer. Second, avoid swimming in rivers or lakes ___5___ you are unfamiliar with, as hidden currents can be extremely ___6___ (danger). Third, even if you are a good swimmer, don't overestimate your ___7___ (able). If you feel tired, get out of the water ___8___ (immediate). Our school invited a lifeguard last week ___9___ (teach) us basic water rescue techniques. He showed us how to throw a rope to someone in trouble and how to call ___10___ help properly. Learning these skills could make the difference between life and death.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 the time（时间），在从句中作时间状语，用 when。

2. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 lives，用形容词性物主代词，they → their。

3. **答案：tragedies**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。these 后接可数名词复数，tragedy → tragedies。

4. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。experienced 以元音音素开头，表示"一个有经验的游泳者"，用 an。

5. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 rivers or lakes（物），在从句中作 with 的宾语，用 that 或 which。

6. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。be extremely dangerous 表示"极其危险的"，danger → dangerous。

7. **答案：ability**
   - **考点**：词性转换。your + 名词，表示"你的能力"，able → ability。

8. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 get out，需用副词形式，immediate → immediately。

9. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。invite sb. to do sth. 表示"邀请某人做某事"，填 to teach。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。call for help 表示"呼救"，固定搭配，填 for。
