# 08-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：海洋污染 (Ocean Pollution)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ocean is home to millions of ___1___ (live) things, from tiny plankton to huge whales. ___2___, human activities are causing serious damage to this important ecosystem. Ocean pollution has become one of the greatest ___3___ (threat) to marine life.

Every year, about eight million tons of plastic waste ___4___ (dump) into the ocean. Plastic bags, bottles and packaging materials float on the water surface or sink to the bottom. Many sea animals mistake these items ___5___ food and eat them. A recent report showed that over 100,000 marine animals ___6___ (kill) by plastic pollution each year.

Oil spills are another major source of ocean pollution. When oil ___7___ (leak) from ships or underwater pipelines, it spreads ___8___ (rapid) across the water. The thick layer of oil prevents sunlight from reaching underwater plants and makes it ___9___ (possible) for fish to breathe normally.

What can we do to help? First, we should reduce our use of single-use plastics. Second, we should support ___10___ (organise) that work to clean up the ocean. Every effort counts. If we all work together, we can still save our ocean before it is too late.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：living**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。修饰名词 `things` 需用形容词，**live** 的形容词形式 **living** 表示"活的、有生命的"。

2. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词（转折连接词）。前一句说海洋是生物的家园，下一句说人类活动造成损害，两句之间为转折关系。句首独立使用，后接逗号，故填 **However**。

3. **答案：threats**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**one of the greatest + 复数名词**（最大的……之一），`threat` 的复数形式为 **threats**。

4. **答案：is dumped**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。`Every year`（每年）提示一般现在时；主语 `eight million tons of plastic waste` 为不可数概念（整体看作单数），与 `dump` 之间为被动关系（废物"被倾倒"），故填 **is dumped**。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。**mistake A for B**（把 A 误认为 B）为固定搭配，故填 **for**。

6. **答案：are killed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。`each year`（每年）提示一般现在时，主语 `over 100,000 marine animals` 为复数，与 `kill` 之间为被动关系，故填 **are killed**。

7. **答案：leaks**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。When 引导的条件/时间状语从句中用一般现在时；主语 `oil` 为不可数名词（第三人称单数），故填 **leaks**。

8. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `spreads` 需用副词，**rapid** 的副词形式为 **rapidly**，表示"迅速地蔓延"。

9. **答案：impossible**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加前缀）。**make it + adj. + for sb. to do sth.**（使得某人做某事变得……），根据语境"油层使鱼无法正常呼吸"，需在 `possible` 前加否定前缀，故填 **impossible**。

10. **答案：organisations**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）+ 名词复数。动词 `support` 后接名词作宾语，**organise** 的名词形式为 **organisation**；此处指多个组织，用复数 **organisations**。
