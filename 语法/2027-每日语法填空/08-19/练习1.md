# 08-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：雨天的故事 (A Rainy Day Story)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

It was raining heavily last Thursday when I left school. I did not have ___1___ umbrella with me because the weather forecast had said it would be sunny all day. I stood at the school gate, ___2___ (hope) the rain would stop soon.

After waiting for about fifteen minutes, the rain showed no sign of ___3___ (stop). I was getting more and more anxious because I had a piano lesson at four o'clock. Just then, a girl from the class next to ___4___ (I) walked up to me. "Would you like to share my umbrella?" she asked with a friendly smile. I ___5___ (thank) her and we walked together towards the bus stop.

On the way, she told me that her name was Lin Mei and she ___6___ (move) to our school only a month ago. We talked about our favourite ___7___ (subject) and hobbies, and I found that we had a lot ___8___ common. By the time we reached the bus stop, we had already become good friends.

The rain finally stopped when my bus came. I waved goodbye to Lin Mei and felt ___9___ (true) grateful for her kindness. Sometimes a small act of ___10___ (kind) from a stranger can brighten your whole day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`umbrella` 为可数名词单数，以元音音素开头，泛指"一把雨伞"需用不定冠词 **an**。

2. **答案：hoping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。句中已有谓语 `stood`，逗号后为非谓语结构。主语 `I` 与 `hope` 之间为主动关系，故用现在分词 **hoping** 作伴随状语。

3. **答案：stopping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**no sign of doing sth.**（没有……的迹象），介词 `of` 后接动名词，`stop` 双写末尾辅音字母加 -ing，故填 **stopping**。

4. **答案：mine**
   - **考点**：代词（名词性物主代词）。`the class next to mine` 中 `mine` = `my class`，介词 `to` 后用名词性物主代词 **mine** 替代 `my class`。

5. **答案：thanked**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去发生的事情用一般过去时，`thank` 的过去式为 **thanked**。

6. **答案：had moved**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`told`（过去时）引导的宾语从句中，"搬到我们学校"发生在"告诉"之前，即过去的过去，需用过去完成时 **had moved**。

7. **答案：subjects**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`favourite`（最喜欢的）修饰名词，此处指多个科目，故用复数 **subjects**。

8. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。**have a lot in common**（有很多共同点）为固定搭配，故填 **in**。

9. **答案：truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰形容词 `grateful` 需用副词，**true** 的副词形式为 **truly**（去 e 加 -ly），表示"真正地感激"。

10. **答案：kindness**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 名词）。介词 `of` 后接名词，**kind** 的名词形式为 **kindness**，表示"善意之举"。
