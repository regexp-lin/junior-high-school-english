# 02-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：传统手工艺 (Traditional Handicrafts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has a long history of traditional handicrafts that (1) ______ (pass) down from generation to generation. Paper cutting, for example, is one of the (2) ______ (old) folk arts in Chinese culture. It dates back to the 6th century and (3) ______ (remain) popular today. During festivals, red paper cuttings are placed on windows and doors to bring good luck. Another famous craft is kite making, (4) ______ originated in Weifang, Shandong Province. Every spring, a grand kite festival (5) ______ (hold) there, attracting visitors from around the world. My uncle is a skilled kite maker. He taught (6) ______ (I) how to build a simple butterfly kite last summer. I was amazed by the amount of patience it required — each piece of bamboo had to be bent (7) ______ (careful) into the right shape. Traditional crafts face a serious problem: fewer young people are willing (8) ______ (learn) them. Many young people think these skills are old-fashioned and prefer (9) ______ (spend) their time on technology instead. To solve this issue, some schools have introduced handicraft courses to help students appreciate these (10) ______ (culture) treasures. By learning traditional crafts, young people can connect with their heritage while developing creativity and patience.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have been passed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。手工艺被代代传承，强调延续至今。

2. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the oldest folk arts`。

3. **答案：remains**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。陈述现在的事实，主语 `It` 用三单。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，修饰 `kite making`。

5. **答案：is held**
   - **考点**：一般现在时的被动语态。`Every spring` 表示每年定期举行。

6. **答案：me**
   - **考点**：人称代词宾格。`taught me`，作宾语。

7. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。修饰 `bent`。

8. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be willing to do sth.` 愿意做某事。

9. **答案：spending**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`prefer doing sth.` 更喜欢做某事。

10. **答案：cultural**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `treasures`，用形容词形式。
