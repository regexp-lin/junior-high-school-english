# 02-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园戏剧社 (School Drama Club)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last September, our school started a drama club, and I was one of the first students (1) ______ (join). At the beginning, I was too shy to speak loudly on stage, let alone act in front of (2) ______ audience. Our drama teacher, Ms. Chen, encouraged us by saying that everyone (3) ______ (have) the potential to be a great performer. She gave us various exercises to build (4) ______ (we) confidence, such as tongue twisters and improvisation games. After three months of (5) ______ (train), we put on our first play — a modern adaptation of "Journey to the West." I played the role of the Monkey King, (6) ______ was both exciting and terrifying. On the night of the performance, the auditorium was packed (7) ______ students and parents. When I first stepped onto the stage, my hands were shaking. But as soon as I delivered my first line, something magical (8) ______ (happen) — the nervousness disappeared and I completely became my character. The audience laughed and clapped at all the right moments. After the show, many people told me that my performance was the (9) ______ (good) part of the entire play. That experience taught me a valuable lesson: you will never know what you are capable of (10) ______ you try.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to join**
   - **考点**：不定式作定语。序数词 `first` 后用不定式修饰名词。

2. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`in front of an audience`，`audience` 以元音音素开头。

3. **答案：had / has**
   - **考点**：时态。宾语从句中陈述普遍道理可用一般现在时 `has`；也可跟主句过去时一致用 `had`。

4. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：物主代词。修饰名词 `confidence`，用形容词性物主代词。

5. **答案：training**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后用动名词形式。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个事情。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be packed with` 挤满了。

8. **答案：happened**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去发生的动作。

9. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the best part`，在整个演出中最好的部分。

10. **答案：until**
    - **考点**：连词。`not... until...` 直到……才……。
