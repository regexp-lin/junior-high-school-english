# 06-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：图书馆的一天 (A Day at the Library)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The city library is one of my favourite ___1___ (place) to spend weekends. Last Sunday, I went there early and found a seat ___2___ the window. The library was quiet, with only the soft sound of pages ___3___ (turn). I picked up a novel about a young girl's journey across Africa. The story was so ___4___ (interest) that I forgot about lunch. A librarian noticed me and ___5___ (kind) brought me a glass of water. She told me that the library ___6___ (have) over 200,000 books and that new ones ___7___ (add) every month. I was surprised ___8___ (learn) that the oldest book in the collection was more than three hundred years old. Before I left, I borrowed two books and promised ___9___ (return) them on time. Reading opens a door ___10___ a wider world, and the library is the best place to start that journey.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：places**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。one of + 复数名词，表示"我最喜欢的地方之一"，place → places。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。by the window 表示"在窗户旁边"，填 by。

3. **答案：being turned**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。pages 与 turn 之间为被动关系，of 后接动名词的被动形式，填 being turned。

4. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。so + 形容词 + that...，story（物）用 interesting（令人感兴趣的），填 interesting。

5. **答案：kindly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 brought，需用副词形式，kind → kindly。

6. **答案：had**
   - **考点**：动词时态。宾语从句中，主句 told 为过去时，从句也用过去时态，have → had。

7. **答案：were added**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。new ones 与 add 之间为被动关系，与主句时态一致用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were added。

8. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be surprised to do sth. 表示"惊讶地发现……"，填 to learn。

9. **答案：to return**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。promise to do sth. 表示"承诺做某事"，填 to return。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：介词。a door to... 表示"通往……的大门"，填 to。
