# 06-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节约用水 (Saving Water)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Water is one of the most ___1___ (value) resources on Earth, yet many people waste it every day. Do you know that only about one percent of the world's water is fresh enough ___2___ people to drink? In some African countries, children have to walk for hours just ___3___ (get) clean water. Meanwhile, in big cities, people often leave the tap ___4___ (run) while brushing their teeth. A small drip from a broken tap can waste up ___5___ 100 litres of water a day. Our school recently started a campaign ___6___ (call) "Every Drop Counts". Students ___7___ (encourage) to take shorter showers and reuse water for watering plants. Last week, our class collected rainwater ___8___ used it to clean the classroom floor. These small actions may seem unimportant, ___9___ together they can make a huge difference. It is everyone's duty to protect ___10___ (we) water resources.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the most + 形容词构成最高级，修饰名词 resources，value → valuable。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。enough for sb. to do sth. 表示"足够……去做某事"，填 for。

3. **答案：to get**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"只是为了获取干净的水"，填 to get。

4. **答案：running**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。leave sth. doing 表示"让某物持续处于某种状态"，leave the tap running 表示"让水龙头一直开着"，填 running。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。up to 表示"多达"，固定搭配，填 to。

6. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，a campaign called... 表示"一个叫做……的活动"，填 called。

7. **答案：are encouraged**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。students 与 encourage 之间为被动关系，recently started 的活动目前仍在进行，用一般现在时的被动语态 are encouraged。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。collected rainwater 与 used it 为两个并列动作，用 and 连接。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。"这些小行动看似不重要"与"它们加起来能产生巨大影响"之间为转折关系，用 but。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 water resources，用形容词性物主代词，we → our。
