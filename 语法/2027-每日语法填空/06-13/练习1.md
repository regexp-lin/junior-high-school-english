# 06-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：野生动物保护 (Wildlife Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Giant pandas are one of the most ___1___ (love) animals in the world. They live mainly in the bamboo forests of Sichuan, China. ___2___, their population dropped sharply in the twentieth century because of habitat ___3___ (lose) and illegal hunting. To save these gentle creatures, the Chinese government ___4___ (build) over sixty nature reserves since the 1960s. Thanks ___5___ these efforts, the number of wild pandas has risen to about 1,800. Scientists are also working ___6___ (hard) than ever to study panda behaviour and help them reproduce in captivity. A panda named Hua Hua recently became an Internet star, ___7___ (attract) millions of fans online. Many people travel long ___8___ (distance) just to see her at the Chengdu Research Base. Protecting wildlife is not only about saving one species — it is about keeping the whole ecosystem ___9___ (balance). Everyone can help ___10___ simply refusing to buy products made from wild animals.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：loved**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the most loved 为最高级形式，loved 为过去分词作形容词，表示"最受喜爱的"，love → loved。

2. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词。前后为转折关系，"大熊猫可爱"与"数量急剧下降"，用 However。

3. **答案：loss**
   - **考点**：词性转换。habitat loss 为名词短语，表示"栖息地丧失"，lose → loss。

4. **答案：has built**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since the 1960s 提示用现在完成时，the Chinese government 为单数主语，填 has built。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。thanks to 表示"多亏了"，固定搭配，填 to。

6. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。than ever 提示用比较级，hard → harder。

7. **答案：attracting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作结果状语，became an Internet star, attracting millions of fans...，填 attracting。

8. **答案：distances**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。long distances 表示"很远的距离"，distance 为可数名词，此处用复数，填 distances。

9. **答案：balanced**
   - **考点**：词性转换。keep sth. + adj. 表示"使某物保持……状态"，balance → balanced。

10. **答案：by**
    - **考点**：介词。by doing sth. 表示"通过做某事"，填 by。
