# 06-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：书信往来 (Pen Pals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Two years ago, our English teacher asked each of us to find a pen pal from another country. I was lucky ___1___ (get) a pen pal named Emma from Australia. At first, we ___2___ (write) letters to each other by post. It usually took about ten days for a letter ___3___ (arrive). Waiting for her reply was both exciting and ___4___ (anxiety). In her letters, Emma described life in Sydney — the beautiful beaches, the ___5___ (friend) people and the unique wildlife. She was especially proud ___6___ the Opera House, which she called the most beautiful building in ___7___ world. I told her about Chinese festivals and sent her some traditional bookmarks as ___8___ (gift). Now we communicate through email, ___9___ is much faster and more convenient. ___10___ (write) to Emma has not only improved my English but also given me a window into a different culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to get**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be lucky to do sth. 表示"幸运地做了某事"，填 to get。

2. **答案：wrote**
   - **考点**：动词时态。at first 与 two years ago 搭配，叙述过去的事件，write → wrote。

3. **答案：to arrive**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It takes + 时间 + for sth. to do sth. 句型，填 to arrive。

4. **答案：anxious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。and 连接两个并列形容词作表语，与 exciting 并列，anxiety → anxious。

5. **答案：friendly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 people，需用形容词形式，friend → friendly。

6. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。be proud of 表示"为……感到自豪"，固定搭配，填 of。

7. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。in the world 表示"在世界上"，world 为独一无二事物，用定冠词 the。

8. **答案：gifts**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。some 后接可数名词复数，gift → gifts。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代通过 email 沟通这件事，用 which 引导。

10. **答案：Writing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。动名词作主语，表示"给 Emma 写信这件事"，write → Writing，句首大写。
