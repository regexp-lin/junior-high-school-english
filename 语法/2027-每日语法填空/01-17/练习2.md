# 01-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：登山冒险 (Mountain Climbing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mountain climbing is a sport that tests both your physical strength and mental (1) ______ (tough). Last autumn, my father and I decided to climb Mount Huangshan, one of the most (2) ______ (fame) mountains in China. We set out early in the morning, when the air was fresh and cool. The first few kilometres of the trail were relatively easy, with wide stone steps (3) ______ (lead) gently upward through a forest of pine trees. However, as we climbed (4) ______ (high), the path became steeper and narrower. At some points, we had to use iron chains (5) ______ (attach) to the rock face to pull ourselves up. My legs ached, and I wanted to stop several (6) ______ (time), but my father kept encouraging me to push forward. He said, "The view from the top is always worth the effort." After about five hours of climbing, we (7) ______ (final) reached the summit. The view that greeted us was beyond anything I had imagined. Seas of white clouds stretched out below us, with mountain peaks (8) ______ (poke) through like islands in a vast ocean. At that moment, I understood (9) ______ my father meant. All the tiredness and pain disappeared, replaced by a deep sense of achievement. Standing on top of that mountain, I felt as (10) ______ I could do anything in the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：toughness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `physical strength` 并列，需要名词形式。

2. **答案：famous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most famous mountains`，修饰名词需要形容词。

3. **答案：leading**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`stone steps leading upward`，主动关系。

4. **答案：higher**
   - **考点**：比较级。`as we climbed higher`，越爬越高。

5. **答案：attached**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`iron chains attached to the rock face`，被动关系。

6. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several times`，"好几次"。

7. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `reached` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：poking**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`with mountain peaks poking through`，`with` + 宾语 + 现在分词，表伴随。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`understood what my father meant`，`what` 引导宾语从句。

10. **答案：if / though**
    - **考点**：连词。`felt as if I could do anything`，`as if` 表示"好像"。
