# 01-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：科学实验 (Science Experiments)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Science experiments are an exciting part of learning that allows students to explore how the world works through hands-on activities. Our science teacher, Mr Chen, is known (1) ______ making his classes both fun and educational. Last week, he guided us through a fascinating experiment (2) ______ (design) to show how plants absorb water. We placed white carnations in glasses of water that (3) ______ (dye) different colours — red, blue and green. Mr Chen told us to observe the flowers (4) ______ (careful) over the next few days. At first, nothing seemed (5) ______ (happen). But by the second day, we noticed that the petals of the carnations had started to change colour! The white flowers gradually took on the colour of the water they were sitting in. Mr Chen explained that this happened because plants draw water up through their stems using tiny tubes called xylem. As the coloured water (6) ______ (travel) up the stem, it reached the petals and changed (7) ______ (they) colour. This experiment helped us understand the concept of capillary action in a very (8) ______ (vision) way. Many students in our class said it was the most (9) ______ (enjoy) science lesson they had ever had. I think experiments like this are important (10) ______ they make abstract scientific ideas concrete and easy to understand.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be known for` 表示"因……而出名"。

2. **答案：designed**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`an experiment designed to show`，被动关系。

3. **答案：had been dyed**
   - **考点**：过去完成时被动。水在实验前已经"被染"了颜色。

4. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `observe` 需要副词形式。

5. **答案：to happen**
   - **考点**：不定式。`nothing seemed to happen`，`seem to do sth`。

6. **答案：travelled**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。描述实验中发生的过程。

7. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：物主代词。`changed their colour`，指代 `the petals`。

8. **答案：visual**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `way` 需要形容词形式。

9. **答案：enjoyable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most enjoyable science lesson`，修饰事物。

10. **答案：because**
    - **考点**：连词。引导原因状语从句，解释实验重要的原因。
