# 05-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国园林 (Chinese Gardens)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese gardens are famous for rocks, water, bridges, and plants that ___1___ (create) harmony between human life and nature. ___2___ visiting Suzhou last spring, I learned why designers leave empty space ___3___ purpose and how light changes the mood of a garden. A small window can frame ___4___ scene like a living painting, and a winding path invites visitors ___5___ (walk) slowly. These gardens are not only ___6___ (beauty) but also full of cultural meanings. Poets once wrote ___7___ the moon reflected in the pond, and those lines are still read ___8___ today. To protect the old trees, workers must ___9___ (repair) broken tiles carefully. ___10___ you stand quietly by the lotus pond, you may feel time slow down.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：create**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。陈述一般事实，用一般现在时；主语 rocks, water... 为复数概念，谓语动词用原形 create。

2. **答案：While**
   - **考点**：连词。While visiting... 为 While I was visiting... 的省略结构，表示“在游览苏州时”，与主句 learned 搭配自然。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。固定短语 on purpose，表示“故意地、有意地”，用 on。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。scene 可数，表示“一幅如画的场景”，用不定冠词 a。

5. **答案：to walk**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。invite sb. to do sth. 表示“邀请某人做某事”，用不定式 to walk。

6. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。not only... but also 连接并列成分，与 full of 对应，用形容词 beautiful 作表语。

7. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。write about... 表示“写关于……的诗”，用 about。

8. **答案：aloud**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 read，表示“大声朗读”，用副词 aloud（与 loudly 近义，read aloud 为常见搭配）。

9. **答案：repair**
   - **考点**：谓语动词。情态动词 must 后接动词原形，故用 repair。

10. **答案：When**
    - **考点**：连词。引导时间状语从句，表示“当你静静地站在荷花池边时”，主句表感受，用 When。
