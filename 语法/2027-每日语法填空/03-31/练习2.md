# 03-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：水果采摘 (Fruit Picking)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When spring turns into early summer, farms on the outskirts of our city open their gates to visitors. Families drive out to pick strawberries, cherries, and peaches straight (1) ______ the trees.

Last Saturday, my parents and I joined a picking tour organized (2) ______ a local travel agency. We arrived at eight o'clock, (3) ______ the air still smelled of dew and grass.

The farmer handed us baskets and explained how (4) ______ (tell) ripe fruit from unripe ones. Ripe strawberries are bright red and come off the stem (5) ______ (easy); green ones stay firm and sour.

My little brother tried to fill his basket in five minutes, (6) ______ many berries at the bottom were crushed. The farmer laughed and taught him to place fruit gently, layer (7) ______ layer.

At noon, we paid for what we had picked and enjoyed a simple lunch under a tent. Homemade jam and yogurt (8) ______ (serve) with fresh bread — the sweetest meal I had tasted in weeks.

Fruit picking is more than a shopping trip. Children learn where food comes from, and adults relax away from screens. Some farms even offer classes on composting (9) ______ visitors can turn peels into soil.

If you go next month, remember (10) ______ (bring) a hat and sunscreen. The fields are sunny, and time passes quickly when you are searching for the reddest berry in a green sea of leaves.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from / off**
   - **考点**：介词。`straight from the trees` 从树上直接采摘；`off` 亦可。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`organized by` 由旅行社组织。

3. **答案：when / while**
   - **考点**：连词。`arrived at eight, when the air still smelled` 到达时空气仍带着露水。

4. **答案：to tell**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`how to tell` 疑问词 + 不定式。

5. **答案：easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `come off`，`easy` → `easily`。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。想快速装满，但底层草莓被压坏。

7. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`layer by layer` 一层一层地。

8. **答案：were served**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。果酱与酸奶被端上。

9. **答案：so that**
   - **考点**：目的状语从句。开设堆肥课以便把果皮变成土壤。

10. **答案：to bring**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`remember to bring` 记得带（将要做的事）。
