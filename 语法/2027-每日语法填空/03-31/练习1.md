# 03-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：愚人节趣事 (April Fools' Day Fun)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

April 1 is known in many countries as a day for harmless jokes. People try to trick friends with funny stories, (1) ______ good humor should always come before hurt feelings.

Last year, my cousin posted online that his family (2) ______ (move) to another city. Relatives called in panic before he wrote "April Fools!" under the photo. His mother was not amused and made him apologize (3) ______ (public).

In our school, students are not allowed to play jokes that disturb classes or cause fear. The student union suggested safer ideas, such (4) ______ changing classroom clocks by five minutes or writing silly headlines on the blackboard.

My friend Li Na hid a rubber spider in a classmate's pencil case. When the girl opened it, she screamed, (5) ______ everyone laughed, including herself. The spider looked surprisingly real because Li Na had painted it (6) ______ (care).

Teachers sometimes join the fun. Our English teacher once announced a surprise test, waited until we took out our pens, and then smiled, "Just kidding." We sighed (7) ______ relief and promised not to trust her announcements too quickly on April 1.

Jokes like these can brighten a busy term, (8) ______ we should remember that trust is precious. A joke that crosses the line may leave scars (9) ______ (long) than laughter.

If you plan to trick someone today, ask yourself whether both sides will smile (10) ______ the truth appears.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。开玩笑逗乐，但善意应优先于伤人。

2. **答案：would move / was going to move**
   - **考点**：过去将来时。谎称“要搬家”，从过去视角看将来，用 would move；或 `was going to move`。

3. **答案：publicly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `apologize`，`public` → `publicly`。

4. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`such as` 例如。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。她尖叫，但大家都笑了。

6. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `painted`，`care` → `carefully`。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`sigh with relief` 松了一口气。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。玩笑能点亮学期，但信任珍贵。

9. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than laughter` 提示，伤痕比笑声更持久。

10. **答案：when / after**
    - **考点**：连词。真相出现时双方是否仍会微笑。
