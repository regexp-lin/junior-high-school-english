# 09-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：周末郊游 (Weekend Countryside Trip)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, my family drove to __1__ small village two hours away from the city. The air there smelled fresher, and we could hear birds singing __2__ the trees. My little cousin __3__ (pick) wild flowers along the path, __4__ my uncle warned her not to touch unknown plants. At noon, we __5__ (enjoy) a simple lunch with fresh vegetables from local farmers. After a short rest, we walked to __6__ hill and __7__ (take) many photos on __8__ top. Dad said living close __9__ nature __10__ (help) people relax. On the way home, I felt tired, but my heart was full of joy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a small village 表泛指“一个小村庄”。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。birds singing in the trees 表示鸟在树林中鸣叫。

3. **答案：picked**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last Saturday 表过去，pick 的过去式为 picked。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。摘花与叔叔警告之间为转折关系。

5. **答案：enjoyed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去午餐活动，enjoy 用过去式 enjoyed。

6. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。walked to a hill 爬一座小山。

7. **答案：took**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与 walked 并列，take photos 的过去式为 took。

8. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。on the top 特指该小山的山顶。

9. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。close to nature 亲近自然。

10. **答案：helps**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。宾语从句中陈述一般道理，动名词短语 living close to nature 作主语，谓语用第三人称单数 helps。
