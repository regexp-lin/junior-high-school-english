# 09-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：3D打印技术 (3D Printing Technology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

3D printing technology __1__ (change) many industries in recent years. A printer builds objects layer __2__ layer according to digital models. Engineers __3__ (use) it to make spare parts quickly, __4__ doctors have printed models of bones to plan difficult operations. Some schools even bought 3D printers __5__ students could turn creative ideas into real models in science clubs. __6__ the technology is helpful, it also raises questions about waste and safety. We should learn __7__ (use) it wisely instead of printing everything we like. I believe 3D printing __8__ (become) cheaper and greener in the future __9__ more young inventors join the field. Maybe one day we __10__ (see) printed houses in our neighborhood.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has changed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in recent years 为现在完成时常见标志，主语 technology 为单数，用 has changed。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定短语。layer by layer 一层一层地。

3. **答案：have used / use**
   - **考点**：动词时态。若强调已经用于多个案例，用现在完成时 have used；陈述一般事实也可用一般现在时 use（主语 Engineers 复数）。

4. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。工程师用途与医生用途并列顺承，用 and。

5. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：从属连词。学校购买打印机是为了让学生能把创意变成实物，so 引导目的或结果状语从句（so ... could ...）。

6. **答案：Although / Though**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导让步状语从句，“尽管技术有用，但也带来问题”，句首大写。

7. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。learn to do sth. 学习做某事，用不定式 to use。

8. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in the future 表将来，用一般将来时 will become。

9. **答案：when / if**
   - **考点**：从属连词。when 表“当更多年轻发明家加入时”；if 表“如果”，均通顺。

10. **答案：will see / may see**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Maybe one day 表将来推测，用 will see 或 may see。
