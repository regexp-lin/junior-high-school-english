# 01-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：保持乐观 (Staying Optimistic)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Life is full of ups and downs, and how we respond to challenges often determines our (1) ______ (happy). Optimistic people tend to see difficulties as temporary setbacks rather than permanent (2) ______ (fail). They believe that things will get better and that they have the power to make positive changes. Research has shown that optimistic people are (3) ______ (healthy) and live longer than those who focus on the negative side of things. My grandmother is one of the most optimistic people I (4) ______ (ever meet). Despite facing many hardships in her life, she always finds something (5) ______ (smile) about. When I complained about failing a maths test last month, she told me a story about her own childhood. She grew up in a poor village (6) ______ there was no school nearby. She had to walk three hours each way just to attend class. Many of her friends dropped (7) ______ of school because the journey was too long, but my grandmother refused to give up. She said, "Every problem has a solution if you look hard enough." Her story put my own problems (8) ______ perspective. A bad grade on one test was nothing compared to (9) ______ she had gone through. Since that conversation, I have tried to adopt a more positive attitude towards life. When something goes wrong, instead of complaining, I ask (10) ______ (I), "What can I learn from this?" This simple change in thinking has made a huge difference in my daily life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：happiness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`determines our happiness`，动词后接名词。

2. **答案：failures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`permanent failures`，多次失败。

3. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`healthier...than those who...`，`than` 为标志。

4. **答案：have ever met**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`I have ever met`，表示"我曾见过的"。

5. **答案：to smile**
   - **考点**：不定式。`finds something to smile about`，不定式作后置定语。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a poor village`，在从句中作地点状语。

7. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词。`drop out of school` 为固定短语，表示"辍学"。

8. **答案：into / in**
   - **考点**：介词。`put...into/in perspective` 表示"正确看待"。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`compared to what she had gone through`，`what` 引导宾语从句。

10. **答案：myself**
    - **考点**：反身代词。`ask myself`，主语为 `I`。
