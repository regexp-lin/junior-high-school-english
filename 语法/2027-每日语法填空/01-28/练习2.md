# 01-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：滑雪体验 (Skiing Adventure)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Skiing has become an (1) ______ (increase) popular winter sport in China, especially since the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics. Last January, my family went to a ski resort in Hebei Province for a three-day holiday. It was my first time (2) ______ (ski), and I was both nervous and excited. After renting our equipment, we took a lesson from a professional instructor. He taught us the basic techniques, including how to stop, turn and control our speed. The most important rule, he said, was to keep your knees (3) ______ (bend) at all times. My younger brother picked up the skills much (4) ______ (fast) than I did. Within an hour, he was already gliding confidently down the beginner slopes. I, on the other hand, spent most of the morning (5) ______ (fall) over. Every time I tried to turn, my skis would cross and I would end up face-down in the snow. It was embarrassing, (6) ______ I refused to give up. By the afternoon, I had finally learned (7) ______ (make) basic turns without falling. The feeling of (8) ______ (slide) smoothly down a snowy slope with the cold wind on my face was absolutely thrilling. On the last day, my father took me to a slightly (9) ______ (steep) slope. My heart was pounding as I looked down from the top, but I took a deep breath and pushed off. The run lasted only about two minutes, but it felt like the most (10) ______ (excite) two minutes of my life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `popular` 需要副词形式。

2. **答案：skiing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`my first time skiing`，固定用法。

3. **答案：bent**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`keep your knees bent`，`keep + 宾语 + 过去分词` 表状态。

4. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much faster than I did`，`than` 为标志。

5. **答案：falling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 为固定搭配。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前后转折，"尴尬但不放弃"。

7. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：不定式。`learned to make`，`learn to do sth`。

8. **答案：sliding**
   - **考点**：动名词。`The feeling of sliding`，介词 `of` 后接动名词。

9. **答案：steeper**
   - **考点**：比较级。`a slightly steeper slope`，比初学者坡道更陡。

10. **答案：exciting**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物/时间用 `-ing` 形式。
