# 10-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家乡特产 (Hometown Specialties)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every region in China has its own special __1__ (product). My hometown, a small city in Guangdong, is famous __2__ its lychees. These sweet fruits have been grown there for __3__ (hundred) of years. Each summer, the trees are covered __4__ clusters of bright red lychees. My grandpa owns a small lychee garden. He takes great pride __5__ looking after the trees. Last July, I helped him __6__ (pick) lychees during the harvest season. We got up early and worked __7__ (careful) to avoid damaging the branches. Grandpa said the __8__ (fresh) lychees are the ones eaten straight from the tree. He was right — they tasted __9__ (wonder)! Nowadays, thanks to fast delivery services, people across China can enjoy fresh lychees __10__ a day or two. My grandpa is happy that more people get to taste the fruit he has spent his whole life growing.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：products**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。special products 特产，此处指各种特产，用复数 products。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。be famous for 因……而闻名，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。hundreds of 数百个，为固定搭配，hundred 加 s。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be covered with 被……覆盖，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。take pride in 对……感到自豪，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：pick / to pick**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help sb. (to) do sth. 帮助某人做某事，to 可省略，填 pick 或 to pick 均可。

7. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 worked，用副词 carefully。

8. **答案：freshest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"最新鲜的荔枝"，用 freshest。

9. **答案：wonderful / wonderfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。taste 作感官系动词后接形容词 wonderful 作表语；若理解为"品尝起来非常棒"也可用副词 wonderfully。

10. **答案：within / in**
    - **考点**：介词。within/in a day or two 在一两天内，表示时间范围。
