# 10-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：秋季收获 (Autumn Harvest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Autumn is the season of harvest. Last October, our class took a trip __1__ a farm in the suburbs. When we arrived, the fields were golden with ripe rice. The farmer, Uncle Zhang, explained __2__ the harvesting process worked. First, a large machine cut the rice stalks. Then the grains were __3__ (separate) from the stalks and dried in the sun. We were allowed __4__ (try) cutting rice by hand. It was much __5__ (tire) than I imagined! After working for just twenty minutes, my arms felt sore. Uncle Zhang told us that before machines were __6__ (invent), farmers had to do everything by hand. They worked from sunrise __7__ sunset every day during harvest season. I felt __8__ (grate) for the food on my table. In the afternoon, we picked __9__ (apple) in the orchard and tasted freshly made apple juice. It was the __10__ (sweet) juice I had ever had. The trip gave us a deeper understanding of farming life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。take a trip to 去……旅行，用介词 to 表示目的地。

2. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。explained 后接宾语从句，表示"过程如何运作"，用 how。

3. **答案：separated**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 grains 与 separate 为被动关系，过去时态，用 were separated。

4. **答案：to try**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be allowed to do sth. 被允许做某事，用不定式 to try。

5. **答案：more tiring**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than，tiring 为多音节词（-ing 形容词），比较级用 more tiring。

6. **答案：invented**
   - **考点**：动词语态。machines 与 invent 为被动关系，were invented 一般过去时被动语态。

7. **答案：to / till / until**
   - **考点**：介词。from sunrise to/till/until sunset 从日出到日落，表示时间范围。

8. **答案：grateful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。feel grateful for 对……感到感恩，用形容词 grateful。

9. **答案：apples**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。pick apples 摘苹果，用复数 apples。

10. **答案：sweetest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级 + ... I had ever had，用 sweetest。
