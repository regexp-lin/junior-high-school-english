# 06-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：儿童节回忆 (Children's Day Memories)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

June 1st is International Children's Day, and it always brings back sweet ___1___ (memory) of my childhood. When I was young, my parents would take me to the park ___2___ this special day. There were colourful balloons everywhere, and I was always attracted ___3___ the candy stalls. One year, my father ___4___ (dress) up as a clown to surprise me. He looked so funny ___5___ I couldn't stop ___6___ (laugh). My mother prepared a big chocolate cake, which was the ___7___ (delicious) thing I had ever tasted. Now I am a teenager, and Children's Day ___8___ (mean) something different to me. It reminds me ___9___ the importance of keeping a childlike heart. No matter how old we grow, we should never forget ___10___ (enjoy) the simple pleasures of life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：memories**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。sweet 修饰名词，memory 为可数名词，此处指多段回忆，用复数 memories。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。在具体某一天用介词 on，on this special day。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。be attracted by 表示"被……吸引"，填 by。

4. **答案：dressed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。one year 指过去某一年，用一般过去时，dress → dressed。

5. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。so...that... 引导结果状语从句，表示"如此滑稽以至于我停不下来"，填 that。

6. **答案：laughing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。stop doing sth. 表示"停止做某事"，couldn't stop laughing 表示"忍不住大笑"，填 laughing。

7. **答案：most delicious**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，且后有 ever tasted 提示最高级，delicious 为多音节词，用 most delicious。

8. **答案：means**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。now 提示一般现在时，Children's Day 为单数主语，mean → means。

9. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。remind sb. of sth. 表示"提醒某人想起某事"，固定搭配，填 of。

10. **答案：to enjoy**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。forget to do sth. 表示"忘记去做某事（未做）"，语义为"不要忘记去享受"，填 to enjoy。
