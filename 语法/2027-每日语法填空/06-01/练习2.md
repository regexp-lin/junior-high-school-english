# 06-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：机器人技术 (Robotics)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Robots are playing an increasingly important role ___1___ our daily lives. In factories, robots can work twenty-four hours a day without ___2___ (get) tired. Some hospitals have started using robots to deliver medicine to ___3___ (patient). Last year, our school ___4___ (organize) a robotics competition, and students were asked ___5___ (design) a robot that could complete simple tasks. My team spent three weeks ___6___ (build) a small robot arm. ___7___ was the most challenging project I had ever worked on. Although our robot didn't win first prize, the judges praised it ___8___ its creative design. Scientists believe that robots will become even ___9___ (smart) in the future. However, they also warn that robots should be used as tools to help ___10___ (we), not to replace us completely.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。play a role in 表示"在……中发挥作用"，固定搭配，填 in。

2. **答案：getting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。without 为介词，后接动名词，get → getting。

3. **答案：patients**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。deliver medicine to patients，指多位病人，用复数 patients。

4. **答案：organized**
   - **考点**：动词时态。last year 提示用一般过去时，organize → organized。

5. **答案：to design**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be asked to do sth. 表示"被要求做某事"，ask sb. to do sth. 的被动形式，填 to design。

6. **答案：building**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"，build → building。

7. **答案：It**
   - **考点**：代词。It 指代前文提到的项目（building a small robot arm），作主语，句首大写，填 It。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。praise sb. for sth. 表示"因为某事表扬某人"，填 for。

9. **答案：smarter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。even + 比较级表示"甚至更加"，smart → smarter。

10. **答案：us**
    - **考点**：代词。help 后接宾格代词作宾语，we → us。
