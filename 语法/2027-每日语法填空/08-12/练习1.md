# 08-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：社区志愿服务 (Community Volunteer Service)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Volunteering is becoming ___1___ (popular) among young people in China than ever before. Last weekend, I joined a community volunteer programme ___2___ the first time, and it turned out to be a wonderful experience.

Our task was to help clean up the park near our neighbourhood. When we arrived there at eight in the morning, we ___3___ (shock) to see rubbish everywhere — plastic bags, drink bottles and food wrappers were lying on the ground. Our team leader divided us ___4___ several groups. Each group was responsible for ___5___ (clean) a different area of the park.

I worked with two other students. We picked up litter carefully and put it into large bags. An old man sitting on a bench watched us for a while and then came over to help. He told us that he ___6___ (live) near the park for over thirty years and it ___7___ (use) to be much cleaner. His words made us work even ___8___ (hard).

After three hours of hard work, the park looked completely ___9___ (difference). The paths were clean, the flowers could be seen clearly, and the whole place smelled fresh. I felt very happy because I knew that our small ___10___ (act) could make a big difference to the community.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：more popular**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**than** 提示需要比较级，`popular` 为多音节形容词，比较级为 **more popular**。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **for the first time**（第一次），故填 **for**。

3. **答案：were shocked**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `we` 与 `shock` 之间为被动关系（我们"被震惊"），叙述过去发生的事用一般过去时被动语态，故填 **were shocked**。

4. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **divide... into...**（把……分成……），故填 **into**。

5. **答案：cleaning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be responsible for doing sth.**（负责做某事），介词 `for` 后接动名词，故填 **cleaning**。

6. **答案：had lived**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`told` 为过去时，老人说他在公园附近住了三十多年（**for over thirty years**），动作从更早的过去延续到过去那个时间点，需用过去完成时 **had lived**。

7. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：固定结构。**used to do sth.**（过去常常做某事 / 过去曾经是……），表示过去的状态或习惯，现在已不再如此，故填 **used**。

8. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。**even + 比较级** 表示"更加……"，`hard` 的比较级为 **harder**，修饰动词 `work`。

9. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。系动词 `looked` 后接形容词作表语，**difference** 的形容词形式为 **different**，表示"看起来完全不同了"。

10. **答案：actions**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）+ 名词复数。形容词 `small` 修饰名词，**act** 的名词形式为 **action**；根据语境"我们的小小行动"可指多个行动，用复数 **actions**。
