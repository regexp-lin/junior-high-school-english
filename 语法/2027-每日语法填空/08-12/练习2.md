# 08-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：风力发电 (Wind Power)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever seen those tall white towers with huge spinning blades standing in open fields? They are wind turbines, and they are used ___1___ (produce) electricity from the power of wind. Wind power is one of the ___2___ (clean) sources of energy available to us today.

Unlike coal or oil, wind does not run out and it does not cause ___3___ (pollute). That is ___4___ more and more countries are choosing to build wind farms. A single large wind turbine can produce enough electricity ___5___ (power) hundreds of homes. In China, wind farms can ___6___ (find) in many provinces, especially in the north and northwest ___7___ the wind blows strongly throughout the year.

However, wind power also has some ___8___ (disadvantage). For example, wind does not blow at the same speed all the time, so the amount of electricity produced is not always stable. Some people also complain ___9___ the noise made by the spinning blades, saying it disturbs their daily life.

Despite these problems, scientists are working ___10___ (active) to improve wind energy technology. I believe that in the future, wind power will become an even more important part of our energy system.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to produce**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be used to do sth.**（被用来做某事），不定式表示目的，故填 **to produce**。注意区分 be used to doing sth.（习惯于做某事）。

2. **答案：cleanest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词**（最……的之一），`clean` 的最高级为 **cleanest**。

3. **答案：pollution**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。动词 `cause` 后接名词作宾语，**pollute** 的名词形式为 **pollution**（不可数名词），表示"造成污染"。

4. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：表语从句连接词。**That is why...**（那就是为什么……），`why` 引导表语从句，表示原因与结果之间的关系。

5. **答案：to power**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**enough + 名词 + to do sth.**（足够的……来做某事），不定式 **to power** 表示结果/目的。

6. **答案：be found**
   - **考点**：动词语态。情态动词 `can` 后接动词原形；主语 `wind farms` 与 `find` 为被动关系（风电场"被发现/被看到"），故填 **be found**。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词为 `the north and northwest`（表示地点），从句 `the wind blows strongly` 中不缺主语或宾语，缺少地点状语，故用关系副词 **where**。

8. **答案：disadvantages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`some`（一些）后接可数名词复数，`disadvantage` 的复数形式为 **disadvantages**。

9. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **complain about...**（抱怨……），故填 **about**。

10. **答案：actively**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `are working` 需用副词，**active** 的副词形式为 **actively**，表示"积极地"。
