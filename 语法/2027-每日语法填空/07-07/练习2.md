# 07-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：可再生能源 (Renewable Energy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As fossil fuels become ___1___ (scarce), the world is turning to renewable energy sources such as solar, wind and hydropower. China has invested ___2___ (heavy) in renewable energy over the past decade. It is now the world's ___3___ (large) producer of solar panels. In many rural areas, solar panels ___4___ (install) on rooftops to provide electricity for families. Wind farms can be seen across northern China, with giant turbines ___5___ (spin) day and night. The Three Gorges Dam, the biggest hydropower station in the world, ___6___ (supply) clean energy to millions of homes since it began operating. These energy sources have one thing ___7___ common — they do not produce harmful emissions. However, there are still challenges. Solar panels only generate electricity when the sun ___8___ (shine), and wind power depends ___9___ weather conditions. Scientists are working on better energy storage technology ___10___ (solve) these problems. If we continue to develop renewable energy, a cleaner and greener future is within reach.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：scarcer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。become scarcer 表示"变得更稀缺"，scarce → scarcer。

2. **答案：heavily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 invested，需用副词形式，heavy → heavily。

3. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the world's + 最高级，large → largest。

4. **答案：have been installed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。panels 与 install 之间为被动关系，描述已完成的动作对现在有影响，用现在完成时的被动语态，填 have been installed。

5. **答案：spinning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。with + 名词 + 现在分词，构成 with 复合结构，turbines 主动旋转，填 spinning。

6. **答案：has supplied**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since it began operating 提示用现在完成时，the Three Gorges Dam 为单数主语，填 has supplied。

7. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。have sth. in common 表示"有共同点"，固定搭配，填 in。

8. **答案：is shining/shines**
   - **考点**：动词时态。when 引导时间状语从句，表示"太阳照射时"，用一般现在时 shines 或现在进行时 is shining。

9. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。depend on 表示"取决于"，固定搭配，填 on。

10. **答案：to solve**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了解决这些问题"，填 to solve。
