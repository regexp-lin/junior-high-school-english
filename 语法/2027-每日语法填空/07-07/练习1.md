# 07-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：古镇游览 (Visiting an Ancient Town)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the National Day holiday, my family visited Zhouzhuang, one of the ___1___ (famous) water towns in China. The town is over 900 years old and ___2___ (surround) by canals and bridges. As we walked along the narrow stone streets, we could see old houses ___3___ (stand) on both sides of the water. Local residents still live in some of these ___4___ (history) buildings. We took a small wooden boat and floated ___5___ the main canal. The boatman sang a folk song in the local dialect, ___6___ added a special charm to the ride. We stopped at a tea house ___7___ we enjoyed traditional green tea and rice cakes. My favourite spot was the Zhang House, a well-preserved mansion ___8___ (build) during the Ming Dynasty. The guide explained that the house ___9___ (design) with a clever water drainage system hundreds of years ago. I took many photos to share ___10___ my classmates. Ancient towns like Zhouzhuang are living museums that connect us with our past.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most famous**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，famous 为多音节词，用 most famous。

2. **答案：is surrounded**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。the town 与 surround 之间为被动关系，描述现状用一般现在时的被动语态，填 is surrounded。

3. **答案：standing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作后置定语/补语，houses standing on both sides 表示"矗立在两侧的房屋"，填 standing。

4. **答案：historic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 buildings，history → historic（有历史意义的）。

5. **答案：along**
   - **考点**：介词。floated along the canal 表示"沿着运河漂浮"，填 along。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面唱歌这件事，用 which。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 a tea house（地点），在从句中作地点状语，用 where。

8. **答案：built**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，a mansion built during... 表示"建于……的宅邸"，build → built。

9. **答案：was designed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。the house 与 design 之间为被动关系，hundreds of years ago 提示一般过去时的被动语态，填 was designed。

10. **答案：with**
    - **考点**：介词。share sth. with sb. 表示"和某人分享某物"，填 with。
