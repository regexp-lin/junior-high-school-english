# 08-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校合唱团 (School Choir)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school choir has been one of the most popular clubs ___1___ our campus for more than five years, attracting students from different grades who share a love for music. Last spring, Ms. Liu, our music teacher, ___2___ (decide) to prepare us for the city competition that would take place in July. At first only twenty students signed up, ___3___ more classmates joined when they heard we would sing a classical piece ___4___ (write) by a well-known composer from Europe. Every Wednesday afternoon we practiced ___5___ (careful) in the music room for two hours so that our voices could blend softly and clearly during both verses and the chorus. The schedule was quite full, yet the singers remained ___6___ (patience) because they loved performing in front of an audience and receiving warm applause. Ms. Liu always explains ___7___ teamwork comes before personal glory in a choir, and she often praises students who listen to each other during rehearsal instead of showing off alone. Before the final show, we were told ___8___ (not arrive) late because the judges would come at least thirty minutes earlier than the audience. On the stage we wore blue uniforms ___9___ made us look tidy under the bright lights, and our families waved excitedly from the seats near the front row. When our names were read out as second-place winners, we knew hard work ___10___ (final) paid off and the months of practice had been worthwhile.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on campus 为固定搭配，表示“在校园里”；此处说明合唱团是校园上受欢迎的社团之一。

2. **答案：decided**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述去年春天已发生的动作，用一般过去时；decide 的过去式为 decided。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前半句“起初只有二十人报名”与后半句“更多人加入”为转折关系，用 but 连接。

4. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。a piece 与 write 之间为被动关系，“由……创作的曲子”，用过去分词 written 作后置定语。

5. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 practiced，需用副词作状语；careful → carefully（认真地）。

6. **答案：patient**
   - **考点**：词性转换。系动词 remained 后接形容词作表语，表示“保持耐心”；patience（名词）→ patient（形容词）。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。explains 后接宾语从句，从句结构完整（teamwork comes before…），用 that 引导；口语中 that 有时可省略，但语法填空通常填 that。

8. **答案：not to arrive**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式的否定）。主动结构为 tell sb. not to do sth.；此处为被动 we were told not to arrive，仍用 not to do 形式。

9. **答案：which / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词为 uniforms，指物，从句缺主语，用 which 或 that 引导限制性定语从句。

10. **答案：finally**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词短语 paid off，需用副词；final → finally（最终；终于）。
