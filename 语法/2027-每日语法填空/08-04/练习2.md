# 08-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：垃圾分类 (Waste Sorting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our community launched a new waste sorting program ___1___ March this year to encourage greener living habits among local families and reduce unnecessary landfill waste. Many volunteers, including my cousin Tom, ___2___ (work) near the recycling bins in our neighborhood every weekend since then, patiently answering questions from elderly residents who were nervous about making mistakes. ___3___ people found the colorful labels confusing at the beginning, they slowly understood ___4___ plastic bottles and clean paper should be placed into the blue bins according to the official guide on the wall. Food waste needs ___5___ (collect) separately on certain days so that it can later be turned into useful fertilizer for neighborhood gardens and flower beds along the paths. The environmental committee also printed large posters ___6___ (explain) why batteries and broken lights must never be thrown into the same bag as ordinary household rubbish, since improper mixing may cause pollution and safety risks in processing centers. If everyone follows the simple rules, our streets and small parks ___7___ (become) much cleaner in the near future. My family has started ___8___ (use) three different bags in the kitchen, ___9___ has reduced small arguments about which colored lid to open on rushed weekday mornings when everyone is in a hurry. Tom often says that such small daily choices matter ___10___ (much) than simply waiting for the government or large companies to solve every environmental problem alone, and he invites classmates to help during the holidays.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。月份前用介词 in，in March 表示“在三月”；此处说明项目启动的时间。

2. **答案：have worked**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。时间状语 since then（指代项目启动以来）表示从过去持续到现在的动作，且与 every weekend 呼应，主语 volunteers 为复数，用 have worked。

3. **答案：Although / Though**
   - **考点**：让步状语从句连词。句意为“尽管起初人们觉得标签难懂，他们后来还是逐渐明白了……”，主从句为让步关系；句首首字母大写。

4. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。understood 后为宾语从句，陈述“塑料瓶和干净纸张应放进蓝色桶”这一事实，从句结构完整，用 that 引导。

5. **答案：collecting / to be collected**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。sth. needs doing 与 sth. needs to be done 同义，表示“某事需要被……”；故填 collecting 或 to be collected。

6. **答案：explaining**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。posters 与 explain 为主动关系，“海报解释了为什么……”，用现在分词 explaining 作后置定语，相当于 posters which explain…。

7. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：动词时态（主将从现）。if 引导的条件状语从句用一般现在时 follows，主句表示将来结果，用一般将来时 will become。

8. **答案：using / to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。start doing sth. 与 start to do sth. 在此语境均可表示“开始做某事”，中考中 using 与 to use 常视为均可接受。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。逗号后为非限制性定语从句，which 指代前面整句话“我家已开始使用三种不同垃圾袋”这一情况，在从句中作主语。

10. **答案：more**
    - **考点**：副词比较级。后出现 than，表示“比仅仅等待……更重要”；much 修饰动词 matter 时，比较级用 more，matter more than…。
