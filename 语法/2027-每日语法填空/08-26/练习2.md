# 08-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：自然灾害防护 (Natural Disaster Prevention)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Natural disasters such as earthquakes, floods and typhoons can happen at any time. They are powerful enough ___1___ (destroy) buildings, roads and even entire cities within hours. Therefore, it is ___2___ (extreme) important for everyone to learn how to protect themselves.

Earthquakes are among the most ___3___ (frighten) natural disasters. When an earthquake ___4___ (strike), the first thing you should do is to drop, take cover and hold on. If you are indoors, hide under a strong table or desk and stay away ___5___ windows and heavy objects that might fall on you. Do not try to run outside ___6___ the shaking stops.

Floods are another common disaster in southern China. During the rainy season, rivers may overflow and ___7___ (cause) serious damage to farmland and homes. To prepare for floods, families should store enough food, clean water and medicine. It is also wise to keep ___8___ (importance) documents in waterproof bags.

Our school holds disaster drills ___9___ (two) a year so that students know what to do in an emergency. Last month's earthquake drill went very ___10___ (smooth) — all 1,200 students evacuated the building in less than three minutes. Being well-prepared can save lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to destroy**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**enough + to do sth.**（足够……以至于能做某事），不定式表示结果，故填 **to destroy**。

2. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰形容词 `important` 需用副词，**extreme** 的副词形式为 **extremely**。

3. **答案：frightening**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。修饰事物 `natural disasters` 用 **-ing** 形式的形容词，故填 **frightening**（令人恐惧的）。

4. **答案：strikes**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。When 引导的时间状语从句中用一般现在时表将来；主语 `an earthquake` 为第三人称单数，故填 **strikes**。

5. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。**stay away from...**（远离……）为固定搭配，故填 **from**。

6. **答案：until**
   - **考点**：连词。**not... until...**（直到……才……），表示"不要试图跑到外面，直到震动停止"，故填 **until**。

7. **答案：cause**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`may overflow and ___` 中 `and` 连接两个并列动词，均由情态动词 `may` 支配，故填动词原形 **cause**。

8. **答案：important**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。修饰名词 `documents` 需用形容词，**importance** 的形容词形式为 **important**。

9. **答案：twice**
   - **考点**：词性转换（数词 → 副词）。**twice a year**（一年两次），**two** 的频率副词形式为 **twice**。

10. **答案：smoothly**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `went` 需用副词，**smooth** 的副词形式为 **smoothly**，`go smoothly` 意为"进展顺利"。
