# 08-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中秋节 (Mid-Autumn Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Mid-Autumn Festival, ___1___ falls on the 15th day of the eighth lunar month, is one of the most important traditional festivals in China. It is a time for family ___2___ (gather) and giving thanks for the harvest.

The festival has a long history. It is said that ancient emperors ___3___ (use) to worship the moon in autumn to celebrate a good harvest. Over time, the tradition ___4___ (spread) to ordinary people. Today, families across China get together on this special night to enjoy mooncakes, drink tea and admire the full moon.

Mooncakes are the most popular food ___5___ (associate) with the festival. They come in many different ___6___ (flavour), from traditional lotus seed paste to modern ice cream fillings. Sharing mooncakes with family and friends is a way of ___7___ (express) love and good wishes.

The story of Chang'e is probably the most ___8___ (fame) legend connected to the Mid-Autumn Festival. According to the story, Chang'e drank a magic potion and flew to the moon, ___9___ she has lived ever since. On the night of the festival, people look up at the bright moon and think ___10___ their loved ones who are far away.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `The Mid-Autumn Festival` 指物/节日，逗号后为非限制性定语从句，在从句中作主语，故用 **which**。

2. **答案：gathering / gatherings**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。`family gathering`（家庭团聚）中 **gathering** 作名词，也可用复数 **gatherings**。

3. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：固定结构。**used to do sth.**（过去常常做某事），表示过去的习惯或状态，故填 **used**。

4. **答案：spread**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述历史演变用一般过去时；`spread` 为不规则动词，过去式与原形同形，仍为 **spread**。

5. **答案：associated**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`food` 与 `associate` 之间为被动关系（食物"被联系到"），用过去分词 **associated** 作后置定语，**be associated with**（与……有关）。

6. **答案：flavours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many different`（许多不同的）修饰可数名词复数，`flavour` 的复数形式为 **flavours**。

7. **答案：expressing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**a way of doing sth.**（做某事的方式），介词 `of` 后接动名词，故填 **expressing**。

8. **答案：famous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。**the most + 多音节形容词** 构成最高级，**fame** 的形容词形式为 **famous**。

9. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `the moon` 为地点名词，从句 `she has lived ever since` 中不缺主语或宾语，缺少地点状语，故用 **where**。

10. **答案：of / about**
    - **考点**：介词。**think of / think about...**（想念/想到……）为固定搭配，故填 **of** 或 **about**。
