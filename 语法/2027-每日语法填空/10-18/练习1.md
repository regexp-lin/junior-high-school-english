# 10-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园摄影比赛 (School Photography Contest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school organised a photography contest last month. The theme was "Beauty Around __1__ (we)". Students were encouraged __2__ (take) photos of anything beautiful in daily life. I decided to photograph the sunrise from our school rooftop. I got up __3__ five o'clock and rushed to school. The sky changed from dark blue __4__ orange and then to gold. It was the __5__ (amazing) scene I had ever seen. I pressed the shutter button at exactly the right moment. Over three hundred photos __6__ (submit) by students from all grades. A team of art __7__ (teacher) judged the entries. To my surprise, my sunrise photo won __8__ (one) prize! The headmaster said the contest helped students pay more __9__ (attend) to the beauty of nature. I am now thinking about __10__ to photograph next. Perhaps I will try night scenes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：Us**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。介词 around 后接宾格形式，作为标题首字母大写，用 Us。

2. **答案：to take**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be encouraged to do sth. 被鼓励做某事，用不定式 to take。

3. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。at + 具体时间点，at five o'clock 在五点钟。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。from... to... 从……到……，表示颜色渐变过程。

5. **答案：most amazing**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，amazing 为多音节词，最高级用 most amazing。

6. **答案：were submitted**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 photos 与 submit 为被动关系，过去时态，复数主语用 were submitted。

7. **答案：teachers**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。a team of 后接可数名词复数，表示"一组教师"，用 teachers。

8. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词→序数词）。first prize 一等奖，one 的序数词为 first。

9. **答案：attention**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。pay attention to 注意……，为固定搭配，用名词 attention。

10. **答案：what**
    - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。think about 后接宾语从句，表示"拍什么"，从句缺宾语，用 what。
