# 10-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护蜜蜂 (Protecting Bees)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Bees are small creatures, __1__ they play a huge role in our ecosystem. They help pollinate about one __2__ (three) of the food we eat, including apples, strawberries and almonds. __3__ bees, many crops would fail and food prices would rise sharply. Sadly, the number of bees __4__ (drop) rapidly in recent years. Scientists believe this is __5__ (main) caused by pesticides, habitat loss and climate change. Some farmers have already noticed __6__ (few) bees visiting their fields than before. To protect bees, we can do several things. First, avoid __7__ (use) harmful chemicals in gardens. Second, plant flowers __8__ provide nectar all year round. Schools can also help __9__ setting up small bee gardens on campus. Every effort counts, no matter how small it __10__ (be). Let's work together to save these important pollinators!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说蜜蜂小，后句说作用大，语义转折，用 but。

2. **答案：third**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词→序数词）。one third 三分之一，为分数表达，three 的序数词为 third。

3. **答案：Without**
   - **考点**：介词。Without bees 没有蜜蜂的话，表示虚拟含义，注意首字母大写。

4. **答案：has dropped**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 in recent years 可知用现在完成时，the number of 作主语视为单数，用 has dropped。

5. **答案：mainly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰过去分词 caused，用副词 mainly。

6. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than before，bees 为可数名词，用 fewer（few 的比较级）。

7. **答案：using**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。avoid doing sth. 避免做某事，用动名词 using。

8. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 flowers 指物，从句缺主语，用 that 或 which。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。help by doing sth. 通过做某事来帮助，by 表示方式。

10. **答案：is**
    - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。no matter how 引导让步状语从句，主语 it 为单数，陈述一般事实用一般现在时 is。
