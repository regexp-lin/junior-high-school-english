# 11-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：全国交通安全日 (National Traffic Safety Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

December 2nd is China's National Traffic Safety Day. It was created __1__ (raise) public awareness about road safety. Every year, thousands of people are killed or __2__ (injure) in traffic accidents. Many of these accidents __3__ (cause) by careless driving, such as using phones while driving or running red lights. Teenagers are also at risk. Some students ride bicycles __4__ wearing helmets or cross roads without looking both ways. Last week, a police officer visited our school __5__ gave a talk about traffic safety. He showed us __6__ (shock) videos of real accidents. The images were hard __7__ (watch), but they reminded us how __8__ (danger) the roads can be. He taught us several rules: always wear a seatbelt, never jaywalk, and make eye __9__ (contact) with drivers before crossing. He also said that wearing bright clothes at night makes pedestrians more __10__ (visible) to drivers. I promised myself to follow these rules and share them with my family.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to raise**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了提高"，用 to raise。

2. **答案：injured**
   - **考点**：动词语态。are killed or injured 被杀害或受伤，injure 的过去分词为 injured。

3. **答案：are caused**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。accidents 与 cause 为被动关系，描述一般事实，用 are caused。

4. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。without wearing helmets 不戴头盔，without + 动名词表示否定伴随。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。visited our school 与 gave a talk 为并列动作，用 and 连接。

6. **答案：shocking**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 videos，-ing 形式描述事物的特征，用 shocking。

7. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be hard to do sth. 难以做某事，用不定式 to watch。

8. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。how dangerous 多么危险，danger 的形容词形式为 dangerous。

9. **答案：contact**
   - **考点**：名词用法。make eye contact with 与……进行目光接触，为固定搭配。

10. **答案：visible**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。more visible 更容易被看到，visible 表示"可见的"。
