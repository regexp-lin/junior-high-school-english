# 11-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬季骑行 (Winter Cycling)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many people stop cycling when winter arrives, __1__ my father rides his bike to work every day, even in the __2__ (cold) weather. He says winter cycling is not as terrible __3__ people think. The key is to dress __4__ (proper). He wears layers of clothing, gloves and a warm hat under his helmet. He also puts bright __5__ (light) on his bike because winter mornings are dark. Last Saturday, he invited me __6__ (join) him for a ride along the river. I was __7__ (worry) about the cold, but once we started riding, I warmed up quickly. The riverside path was quiet and __8__ (peace). We could see frost sparkling on the grass beside us. Dad pointed __9__ a group of ducks swimming in the cold water and said, "If they can handle winter, so can we!" The ride lasted about an hour. When we got home, Mum had made hot chocolate __10__ us. It was the perfect end to a winter morning ride.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说很多人冬天不骑车，后句说父亲仍骑车，语义转折，用 but。

2. **答案：coldest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the coldest weather 最冷的天气，用最高级 coldest。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：副词/连词。not as... as... 不如……，同级比较的否定形式。

4. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 dress，用副词 properly。

5. **答案：lights**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。bright lights 明亮的灯，用复数 lights。

6. **答案：to join**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。invite sb. to do sth. 邀请某人做某事，用不定式 to join。

7. **答案：worried**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be worried about 对……担心，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

8. **答案：peaceful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。与 quiet 并列作表语，peace 的形容词形式为 peaceful。

9. **答案：at / out**
   - **考点**：副词/介词。point at 指向 / point out 指出，两者均可。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。made hot chocolate for us 为我们做了热巧克力，for 表示"为"。
