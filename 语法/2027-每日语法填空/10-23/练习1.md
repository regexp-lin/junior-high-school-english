# 10-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：手语学习 (Learning Sign Language)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever tried __1__ (communicate) without speaking? Last term, our school invited a sign language teacher to give us a special lesson. She taught us basic hand signs for greetings __2__ common words. At first, it seemed difficult __3__ (remember) all the movements. However, after practising for a while, I found it quite __4__ (interest). The teacher explained that sign language is the main way of __5__ (communicate) for deaf people. In China, there __6__ (be) millions of hearing-impaired people who depend on it every day. She showed us a video about a deaf girl __7__ won a dancing competition. The girl could not hear the music, __8__ she felt the rhythm through vibrations in the floor. Her story was truly __9__ (inspire). Since that lesson, some of my classmates and I have joined a sign language club. We hope to use __10__ we have learned to help deaf people in our community.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：communicating / to communicate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。try doing sth. 尝试做某事 / try to do sth. 努力做某事，两者均可。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。greetings 与 common words 并列，用 and 连接。

3. **答案：to remember**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It + be + 形容词 + to do sth. 为固定句型，it 为形式主语，用 to remember。

4. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。find it + 形容词（宾语补足语），-ing 形式修饰事物特征，用 interesting。

5. **答案：communication**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。way of + 名词，用名词 communication。

6. **答案：are**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。there be 句型，主语 millions of hearing-impaired people 为复数，一般现在时用 are。

7. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 girl 指人，从句缺主语，用 who 或 that。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说听不到音乐，后句说通过振动感受节奏，语义转折，用 but。

9. **答案：inspiring**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。truly inspiring 真正鼓舞人心的，-ing 形式修饰事物/故事的特征。

10. **答案：what**
    - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。use 后接宾语从句，表示"我们所学到的"，从句缺宾语，用 what。
