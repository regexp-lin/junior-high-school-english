# 10-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：太阳能发电 (Solar Power)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Solar power is becoming one of the most important __1__ (source) of clean energy. Unlike coal or oil, sunlight is free and will never run __2__. Solar panels work by __3__ (turn) sunlight into electricity. In China, huge solar farms have been __4__ (build) in desert areas where there is plenty of sunshine. The Tengger Desert Solar Park is __5__ (large) than any other solar farm in the world. It covers __6__ area of about 1,200 square kilometres. Many families have also installed solar panels __7__ their rooftops. My uncle put them on his house two years __8__ and has saved a lot of money on electricity bills since then. __9__ (science) are still working to make solar panels cheaper and more efficient. If solar energy __10__ (use) more widely, we can greatly reduce air pollution and slow down climate change.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：sources**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。one of the + 名词复数，用 sources。

2. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词。run out 用完、耗尽，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：turning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。by + 动名词，表示方式"通过……"，用 turning。

4. **答案：built**
   - **考点**：动词语态。have been built 现在完成时被动语态，build 的过去分词为 built。

5. **答案：larger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than any other，用比较级 larger。

6. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。an area of 面积为……，area 以元音音素 /eə/ 开头，用 an。

7. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on their rooftops 在屋顶上，用 on 表示在……之上。

8. **答案：ago**
   - **考点**：副词。two years ago 两年前，ago 与一般过去时连用。

9. **答案：Scientists**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→名词复数）。作主语，表示"科学家们"，用复数 Scientists，注意首字母大写。

10. **答案：is used**
    - **考点**：动词时态与语态。if 引导的条件状语从句，主语 solar energy 与 use 为被动关系，从句用一般现在时被动 is used。
