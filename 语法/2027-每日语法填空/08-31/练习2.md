# 08-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：可回收材料 (Recyclable Materials)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every day, people produce a huge amount of waste. Much of it ends up in landfills, ___1___ it can take hundreds of years to break down. However, a large part of our daily waste is actually recyclable. If ___2___ (handle) properly, these materials can be turned into new products.

Paper is one of the ___3___ (easy) materials to recycle. Old newspapers, magazines and cardboard boxes can all be collected and ___4___ (send) to recycling factories. There, they are broken down into pulp and made ___5___ new paper. Recycling one ton of paper can save about 17 trees, which is ___6___ (true) remarkable.

Plastic is another common recyclable material, ___7___ not all types of plastic can be recycled. Bottles and containers ___8___ (mark) with a recycling symbol are usually accepted. Glass and metal are also highly recyclable. In fact, aluminium cans can be recycled an ___9___ (limit) number of times without losing quality.

Our school has placed recycling bins in every classroom and corridor. Students ___10___ (encourage) to sort their waste into different categories: paper, plastic, glass and other rubbish. By developing this habit from a young age, we can all contribute to a cleaner planet.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `landfills`（垃圾填埋场）为地点名词，从句中不缺主语或宾语，缺少地点状语，故用 **where**。

2. **答案：handled**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。条件状语从句的省略形式，完整为 `If (they are) handled properly`，主语与主句一致时省略主语和 be 动词，保留过去分词 **handled**。

3. **答案：easiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词**（最……的之一），`easy` 的最高级为 **easiest**。

4. **答案：sent**
   - **考点**：动词语态。与前面的 `collected` 并列，均由 `can be` 支配，构成被动语态，`send` 的过去分词为 **sent**。

5. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。**be made into...**（被制成……）表示将原材料加工为成品，故填 **into**。注意区分 be made of/from（由……制成）。

6. **答案：truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰形容词 `remarkable` 需用副词，**true** 的副词形式为 **truly**。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前半句说"塑料是常见的可回收材料"，后半句说"不是所有类型的塑料都能回收"，两者为转折关系，故用 **but**。

8. **答案：marked**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`Bottles and containers` 与 `mark` 之间为被动关系（瓶子和容器"被标记"），用过去分词 **marked** 作后置定语。

9. **答案：unlimited**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加前缀）。根据语境"铝罐可以被无限次回收"，在 `limited` 前加否定前缀 `un-`，故填 **unlimited**。

10. **答案：are encouraged**
    - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `Students` 与 `encourage` 之间为被动关系（学生"被鼓励"），描述现在的情况用一般现在时被动语态，故填 **are encouraged**。
