# 08-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑假总结 (Summer Holiday Summary)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The summer holiday is coming to ___1___ end. Looking back on the past two months, I feel that this has been one of the most ___2___ (meaning) summers of my life.

At the beginning of the holiday, I made a plan ___3___ (list) all the things I wanted to achieve. First, I wanted to improve my English, so I read two English novels and watched several English films ___4___ subtitles. My vocabulary has grown a lot, and I feel much ___5___ (confident) when speaking English now.

Second, I learned ___6___ (swim) at the local pool. My coach was very patient and taught me the basic strokes step by step. After four weeks of practice, I was finally able to swim 200 metres ___7___ stopping. I never thought I could do it!

Third, I spent quality time with my family. We ___8___ (take) a trip to Guilin, where we enjoyed the beautiful scenery along the Li River. The mountains and water there were so amazing ___9___ I took over 300 photos. My father said it was the best family trip we ___10___ (have) in years.

Now I am ready to start the new school year with more energy and confidence.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。**come to an end**（结束）为固定搭配，`end` 以元音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **an**。

2. **答案：meaningful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。**the most + 多音节形容词** 构成最高级，**meaning** 的形容词形式为 **meaningful**（有意义的）。

3. **答案：listing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词 **listing** 作后置定语修饰 `a plan`，表示"列出所有事项的计划"。也可理解为 `to list`（不定式作目的状语），两者均可接受。

4. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。**without subtitles**（无字幕地）表示为了提高英语能力而不看字幕观影，故填 **without**。

5. **答案：more confident**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**much + 比较级** 表示"……得多"，`confident` 为多音节形容词，比较级为 **more confident**。

6. **答案：to swim / swimming**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**learn to do sth.** 或 **learn doing sth.** 均可表示"学会做某事"，但 **learn to swim**（学游泳）更为常见。

7. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。**without stopping**（不停歇地），表示"不停下来地游了200米"，故填 **without**。

8. **答案：took**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述暑假期间发生的过去事件用一般过去时，**take a trip**（去旅行）中 `take` 的过去式为 **took**。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。**so + adj. + that...**（如此……以至于……），`amazing` 为形容词，故填 **that** 引导结果状语从句。

10. **答案：had had**
    - **考点**：动词时态。`said`（过去时）引导的宾语从句中，`in years` 表示"多年来"，且相对于 `said` 那个过去时间点之前的经历，用过去完成时 **had had**。
