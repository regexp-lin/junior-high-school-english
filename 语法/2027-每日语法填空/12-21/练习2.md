# 12-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园无手机日 (School Phone-free Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school recently introduced a "Phone-free Day" every Wednesday. On this day, students are not allowed __1__ (use) their mobile phones from morning to evening. At first, many students felt __2__ (comfort) without their phones. Some even said they did not know __3__ to do during breaks. However, after a few weeks, something __4__ (surprise) happened. Students began __5__ (talk) to each other more. The playground became __6__ (live) than before because more students went outside to play games instead of __7__ (stare) at screens. The school library also saw __8__ increase in the number of visitors. I noticed that I could concentrate much __9__ (well) in class on Wednesdays. Our head teacher said the purpose of this activity was not to take phones away from us forever, __10__ to remind us that there is a wonderful world beyond our screens. I think it is a great idea.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。be allowed to do sth. 被允许做某事，用不定式。

2. **答案：uncomfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加前缀构成反义词）。felt uncomfortable 感到不舒服，根据语境"没有手机"应为否定含义，加前缀 un-。

3. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：宾语从句（连接代词）。what to do 做什么，"疑问词 + to do"结构。

4. **答案：surprising**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ing 形容词）。something surprising 令人惊喜的事情，修饰 something 的形容词后置。

5. **答案：talking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。began talking 开始交谈，begin 后接动名词。

6. **答案：livelier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。livelier than 比……更热闹，than 提示用比较级，lively 的比较级为 livelier。

7. **答案：staring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。instead of staring 而不是盯着，instead of 后接动名词。

8. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。an increase 一个增长，increase 以元音音素开头，用不定冠词 an。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。concentrate much better 集中注意力好得多，well 的比较级为 better。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。not... but... 不是……而是……，为固定搭配。
