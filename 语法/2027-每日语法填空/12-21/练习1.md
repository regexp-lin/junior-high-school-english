# 12-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：养蚕记 (Raising Silkworms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This spring, our science teacher gave each student a box of silkworm eggs as part of __1__ biology project. We were asked to raise them and record their growth. When the tiny worms __2__ (hatch) from the eggs, they were only about three millimetres long. I fed them fresh mulberry __3__ (leaf) every day. Watching them eat was quite __4__ (fun). They munched on the leaves so fast that I could __5__ (actual) hear the crunching sound! After about four weeks, the silkworms began __6__ (spin) cocoons. Each worm wrapped __7__ (it) in a thin silk thread. The cocoons were white, golden or even light pink. Our teacher explained that a __8__ (sing) cocoon could produce up to 900 metres of silk thread. I was amazed __9__ this tiny creature could create something so incredible. In ancient China, silk __10__ (trade) along the famous Silk Road and became one of the country's most valuable exports.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。a biology project 一个生物项目，project 为可数名词，首次提及用不定冠词 a。

2. **答案：hatched**
   - **考点**：动词时态。When the worms hatched 当蚕孵化时，描述过去发生的事用一般过去时。

3. **答案：leaves**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。mulberry leaves 桑叶，leaf 的复数为 leaves。

4. **答案：funny/fun**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。quite funny/fun 很有趣，be 后接形容词作表语。

5. **答案：actually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 hear，用副词 actually 表示"确实"。

6. **答案：to spin/spinning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。begin to spin/spinning 开始吐丝，begin 后可接不定式或动名词。

7. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。wrapped itself 把自己包裹起来，主语 each worm 与宾语指同一物，用反身代词。

8. **答案：single**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。a single cocoon 一个茧，single 作形容词表示"单个的"。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词（宾语从句）。amazed that... 对……感到惊讶，that 引导宾语从句。

10. **答案：was traded**
    - **考点**：动词时态与语态。In ancient China 在古代中国，silk 与 trade 为被动关系，用一般过去时被动 was traded。
