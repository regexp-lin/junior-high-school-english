# 01-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：诗歌与文学 (Poetry and Literature)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Reading literature is one of the (1) ______ (good) ways to broaden your mind and deepen your understanding of the human experience. Among all literary forms, poetry holds a special place in Chinese culture. From the Tang Dynasty poems of Li Bai and Du Fu (2) ______ the Song Dynasty ci of Su Shi, Chinese poetry has a history spanning thousands of years. These ancient poets used simple yet powerful language (3) ______ (express) their emotions and observations about life. Even today, their works continue to touch the hearts of (4) ______ (read) around the world. In our Chinese class, we are required (5) ______ (memorise) several classical poems each month. At first, I found this task quite (6) ______ (challenge), because the language of ancient poetry is very different from modern Chinese. However, as I learned more about the (7) ______ (mean) behind the words, I began to appreciate the beauty and wisdom (8) ______ (contain) in these poems. My favourite poem is "Quiet Night Thought" by Li Bai. Whenever I read it, I can picture the poet (9) ______ (stand) alone under the moonlight, thinking about his faraway home. The poem perfectly captures the feeling of homesickness, (10) ______ is something that people of every generation can relate to.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the best ways`，"最好的方式之一"。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from...to...` 结构，表示"从……到……"。

3. **答案：to express**
   - **考点**：不定式。`used language to express`，不定式表目的。

4. **答案：readers**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`the hearts of readers`，`read` → `reader` → `readers`。

5. **答案：to memorise**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be required to do sth`，被动语态中的不定式。

6. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found this task quite challenging`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

7. **答案：meaning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the meaning behind the words`，冠词后接名词。

8. **答案：contained**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the beauty and wisdom contained in these poems`，被动关系。

9. **答案：standing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`picture the poet standing`，感知动词后接现在分词表正在进行。

10. **答案：which**
    - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个内容。
