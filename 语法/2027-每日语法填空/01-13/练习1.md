# 01-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园友谊 (School Friendship)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Friendships formed during school years often last a lifetime. My best friend, Zhao Yue, and I have known each other (1) ______ we were in primary school. We met on the very first day of Year One, when she noticed that I was sitting alone and came over to share her crayons (2) ______ me. From that moment, we became inseparable. Over the years, we have shared countless happy memories together. We have gone to the same schools, joined the same clubs and supported each other through difficult times. Last year, when I was struggling with maths, Zhao Yue (3) ______ (spend) hours after school helping me understand the problems. She was so patient that she never got annoyed, even when I asked the same question (4) ______ (repeat). Thanks to her help, my maths grade (5) ______ (improve) significantly by the end of the term. Of course, no friendship is perfect. Zhao Yue and I have had (6) ______ (disagree) from time to time. Once, we had a big argument about something so silly that neither of us can even remember what it was about now. We did not speak to each other for a whole week. (7) ______ (eventual), I realised how much I missed her and wrote her a letter (8) ______ (apologise) for my behaviour. She forgave me immediately, and our friendship became even (9) ______ (strong) after that experience. I am truly grateful to have such a wonderful friend, and I hope we will remain close no matter (10) ______ life takes us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since we were in primary school`，`since` 引导时间状语从句，与现在完成时搭配。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`share sth with sb` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：spent**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last year` 为过去时标志。

4. **答案：repeatedly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `asked` 需要副词形式。

5. **答案：had improved / improved**
   - **考点**：时态。`by the end of the term` 到学期末（过去的时间点），用过去完成时或一般过去时。

6. **答案：disagreements**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`have had disagreements`，多次分歧。

7. **答案：Eventually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。句首修饰整个句子，需要副词形式。

8. **答案：apologising / to apologise**
   - **考点**：现在分词/不定式。`wrote her a letter apologising for`（现在分词作伴随状语）或 `to apologise for`（不定式表目的）均可。

9. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`even stronger`，`even` 修饰比较级，`after that` 暗含比较。

10. **答案：where**
    - **考点**：连接副词。`no matter where life takes us`，表示"无论生活将我们带到哪里"。
