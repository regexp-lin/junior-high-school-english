# 10-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统中医 (Traditional Chinese Medicine)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traditional Chinese Medicine, or TCM, has a history of __1__ (thousand) of years. It is quite different __2__ Western medicine in many ways. TCM doctors believe that the human body is __3__ whole system, and every part is connected. They use methods like acupuncture and herbal medicine __4__ (treat) patients. My grandmother often makes herbal soup for our family. She says it helps __5__ (build) a strong body. Last month, I had a bad cold and didn't get __6__ (good) after taking Western medicine. My mother took me to __7__ TCM doctor. He checked my pulse carefully and gave me some herbs. After __8__ (drink) the herbal tea for three days, I recovered completely. __9__ the taste was bitter, I had to admit it worked well. Today, more and more people around the world are becoming __10__ (interest) in TCM.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：thousands**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。thousands of 数以千计的，为固定搭配，thousand 加 s。

2. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。be different from 与……不同，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。whole system 为可数名词短语，泛指"一个完整的系统"，用 a。

4. **答案：to treat**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了治疗病人"，用 to treat。

5. **答案：build / to build**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help (to) do sth. 帮助做某事，to 可省略，填 build 或 to build 均可。

6. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。get better 变好/好转，good 的比较级为 better。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一位中医大夫"，用不定冠词 a。

8. **答案：drinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。after 为介词，后接动名词 drinking。

9. **答案：Although / Though**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导让步状语从句，"虽然味道苦，但不得不承认有效"，用 Although 或 Though。

10. **答案：interested**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。become interested in 对……感兴趣，-ed 形式修饰人的感受。
