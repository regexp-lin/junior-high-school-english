# 10-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园足球赛 (School Football Match)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Wednesday, our class played __1__ exciting football match against Class Five. Both teams had practised hard __2__ the match. When the whistle blew, everyone rushed onto the field. Our striker, Zhang Wei, ran __3__ (fast) than anyone else and scored the first goal in just ten minutes. The crowd cheered __4__ (loud). However, Class Five soon equalised with a brilliant header. By halftime, the score __5__ (be) 1-1. During the break, our coach told us __6__ (stay) calm and work as a team. In the second half, I passed the ball __7__ Zhang Wei, and he shot it straight into the net. We were so excited __8__ we hugged each other on the field. The match ended 2-1. It was one of the __9__ (proud) moments of my school life. Playing football has taught __10__ (we) the value of teamwork.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。exciting 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，泛指"一场精彩的比赛"，用 an。

2. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：介词/连词。表示在比赛之前练习，用 before。

3. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。后有 than anyone else，用比较级 faster。

4. **答案：loudly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 cheered，用副词 loudly。

5. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。主语 the score 为单数，全文为过去时态，用 was。

6. **答案：to stay**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。tell sb. to do sth. 告诉某人做某事，用不定式 to stay。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。pass sth. to sb. 把……传给某人，用介词 to。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：结果状语从句。so + 形容词 + that... 如此……以至于……，为固定句型。

9. **答案：proudest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，proud 的最高级为 proudest。

10. **答案：us**
    - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。teach 后接宾语，用宾格 us。
