# 11-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我的家务日 (My Chore Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday was my "chore day". My parents were both busy __1__ work, so I decided to take care of the housework by __2__ (I). First, I washed all the dirty __3__ (dish) in the sink. Then I swept the floor and mopped it until it was __4__ (shine). The most difficult task was doing the laundry. I had to separate white clothes __5__ coloured ones, which I had never done before. __6__ (lucky), I remembered my mother's instructions. After hanging the clothes outside __7__ (dry), I started cooking lunch. I made fried rice __8__ eggs and vegetables — my father's favourite dish. When my parents came home, they were pleasantly __9__ (surprise) to see a clean house and a warm meal on the table. My mother gave me a big hug and said she was proud __10__ me. That day, I truly understood how hard my parents work every day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with / at**
   - **考点**：介词。be busy with sth. / be busy at work 忙于工作，用 with 或 at。

2. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。by myself 我自己，独自，I 的反身代词为 myself。

3. **答案：dishes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。all the 后接可数名词复数，dish 的复数为 dishes。

4. **答案：shining / shiny**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。was 后接形容词作表语，shine 的形容词形式为 shining 或 shiny。

5. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。separate... from... 把……与……分开，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：Luckily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。置于句首作评注性状语，用副词 Luckily，注意首字母大写。

7. **答案：to dry**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了晾干"，用 to dry。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。fried rice with eggs and vegetables 加鸡蛋和蔬菜的炒饭，with 表示"加上"。

9. **答案：surprised**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be surprised to see 惊喜地看到，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

10. **答案：of**
    - **考点**：介词。be proud of 为……感到自豪，为固定搭配。
