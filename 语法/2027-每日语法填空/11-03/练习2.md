# 11-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：绿色出行 (Green Travel)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

With the increase of private cars, traffic jams and air pollution have become __1__ (bad) in many cities. To solve these problems, the government encourages people __2__ (choose) green ways of travelling. Riding a bicycle is one of the __3__ (healthy) and most eco-friendly options. Shared bikes, __4__ can be found on almost every street corner, make cycling even more convenient. Taking the underground is another good choice __5__ it is fast and produces less pollution __6__ driving. Some people also choose __7__ (walk) short distances instead of driving. My family has been practising green travel __8__ last year. My father sold his car and started taking the bus to work. My mother rides __9__ electric bike to the office. I walk to school every day. We have not only saved money __10__ also reduced our carbon footprint. I believe that if everyone makes small changes, our cities will become cleaner and greener.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。bad 的比较级为不规则变化 worse，暗含与之前相比更严重。

2. **答案：to choose**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。encourage sb. to do sth. 鼓励某人做某事，用不定式 to choose。

3. **答案：healthiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，healthy 的最高级为 healthiest（y→iest）。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 shared bikes 指物，非限制性定语从句用 which。

5. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导原因状语从句，表示"因为它快且污染少"，用 because/as/since。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较级标志词。less pollution than driving 比开车产生更少的污染，用 than。

7. **答案：to walk**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。choose to do sth. 选择做某事，用不定式 to walk。

8. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。since last year 自去年以来，与现在完成进行时连用。

9. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。electric 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，泛指"一辆电动自行车"，用 an。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。not only... but also... 不仅……而且……，为固定搭配。
