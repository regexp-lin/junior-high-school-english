# 11-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：皮影戏 (Shadow Puppetry)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Shadow puppetry is one of China's oldest __1__ (form) of storytelling. It dates back over 2,000 years __2__ the Han Dynasty. Performers use flat puppets made of animal leather to cast shadows on a white screen. __3__ moving the puppets behind the screen, they bring characters to life. The puppets are __4__ (beautiful) painted with bright colours and fine details. Last month, a shadow puppet master visited our school. He performed a story from "Journey __5__ the West". The characters Sun Wukong and Zhu Bajie looked __6__ (incredible) realistic on the screen. We were amazed __7__ the master controlled several puppets at the same time while also providing __8__ (differ) voices for each character. After the show, he let some students try __9__ (move) the puppets. It was much harder than it looked! He told us that sadly, __10__ (few) young people today are willing to learn this art. I think we should do more to protect and promote our traditional performing arts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：forms**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。one of the + 名词复数，用 forms。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。date back to 追溯到……，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：By**
   - **考点**：介词。by moving 通过移动，by + 动名词表示方式，注意首字母大写。

4. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰过去分词 painted，用副词 beautifully。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。"Journey to the West"《西游记》，专有名词中的介词 to。

6. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 realistic，用副词 incredibly。

7. **答案：that / because**
   - **考点**：连词。be amazed that 惊讶于……，that 引导原因/宾语从句。

8. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 voices，differ 的形容词形式为 different。

9. **答案：moving / to move**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。try doing sth. 尝试做某事 / try to do sth. 努力做某事，两者均可。

10. **答案：fewer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。fewer young people 更少的年轻人，few 的比较级为 fewer，修饰可数名词复数。
