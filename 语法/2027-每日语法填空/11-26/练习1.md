# 11-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园跳绳比赛 (School Rope Skipping Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Rope skipping is a simple __1__ effective exercise. Our school holds a rope skipping competition every November. This year, I represented my class in the __2__ (person) speed event. The rule was simple: skip as many __3__ (time) as possible in one minute. I had been practising every day __4__ two months. My best record was 180 skips per minute. On the day of the competition, the sports field was full of __5__ (cheer) students. When the whistle blew, I started skipping __6__ (rapid). Everything went well until I tripped at around 40 seconds. My heart sank, __7__ I quickly recovered and continued. When time was up, my total was 165 skips. I felt __8__ (disappoint) at first because I knew I could do better. However, my score was still enough __9__ (earn) third place. The winner, a girl from Class Three, achieved an amazing 210 skips! She __10__ (obvious) trained very hard. The competition taught me that setbacks are just part of the journey.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but / yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。simple but/yet effective 简单但有效，语义转折。

2. **答案：personal**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 speed event，person 的形容词形式为 personal。

3. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。as many times as possible 尽可能多次，time 表示"次数"为可数名词，用 times。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for two months 持续两个月，for + 时间段。

5. **答案：cheering**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作定语）。cheering students 正在欢呼的学生，主动关系用现在分词。

6. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 skipping，用副词 rapidly。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说心沉了，后句说迅速恢复，语义转折，用 but。

8. **答案：disappointed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。feel disappointed 感到失望，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

9. **答案：to earn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。enough to do sth. 足以做某事，用不定式 to earn。

10. **答案：obviously**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 trained，用副词 obviously。
