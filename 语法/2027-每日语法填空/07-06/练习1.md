# 07-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：天文观测 (Stargazing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When I was ten, my father bought me a small telescope as ___1___ birthday gift. That gift opened a door to the universe for me. Every clear night, I set up the telescope on our balcony and spend hours ___2___ (look) at the stars. The first time I saw Saturn's rings ___3___ my own eyes, I was so excited ___4___ I couldn't sleep that night. Since then, I ___5___ (learn) to identify many constellations, such as Orion, the Big Dipper and Cassiopeia. Last August, there was a meteor shower ___6___ was visible across most of China. My family drove to a mountain area ___7___ (avoid) the city lights. We lay on blankets, watching shooting stars flash across the dark sky. My mother counted over twenty meteors ___8___ just one hour. It was one of the most ___9___ (beauty) scenes I have ever witnessed. Studying astronomy has taught me how tiny we are compared ___10___ the vast universe, and that makes me treasure every moment on Earth.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。birthday gift 为可数名词单数，表示"一份生日礼物"，用不定冠词 a。

2. **答案：looking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"，look → looking。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with my own eyes 表示"用我自己的眼睛/亲眼"，填 with。

4. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。so...that... 引导结果状语从句，填 that。

5. **答案：have learned/have learnt**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since then 提示用现在完成时，I 为主语，填 have learned/learnt。

6. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a meteor shower（物），在从句中作主语，用 that 或 which。

7. **答案：to avoid**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了避开城市灯光"，填 to avoid。

8. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in just one hour 表示"在仅仅一小时内"，填 in。

9. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the most + 形容词构成最高级，beauty → beautiful。

10. **答案：to/with**
    - **考点**：介词。compared to/with 表示"与……相比"，固定搭配，填 to 或 with。
