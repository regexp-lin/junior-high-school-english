# 07-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：旧物改造 (Upcycling)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Upcycling is the process of turning old or ___1___ (use) items into something new and useful. Unlike recycling, which breaks materials down, upcycling gives them a ___2___ (two) life in a creative way. At our school's Green Week event, students ___3___ (ask) to bring something old from home and transform it. Li Mei turned an old ladder ___4___ a bookshelf by painting it white and adding wooden boards. Zhang Wei used empty glass bottles to make ___5___ (colour) flower vases. I collected worn-out jeans from my family and sewed them into a patchwork cushion cover. It was ___6___ (hard) than I expected because the denim was very thick. The teacher said my work showed great ___7___ (create). The event attracted over two hundred students, and the best works ___8___ (display) in the school hall for a whole week. Many students were surprised to see ___9___ beautiful everyday rubbish could become. Upcycling is a fun and practical way ___10___ (reduce) waste and protect our planet.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：useless**
   - **考点**：词性转换。old or useless items 表示"旧的或没用的物品"，use → useless（无用的）。

2. **答案：second**
   - **考点**：序数词。a second life 表示"第二次生命"，two → second。

3. **答案：were asked**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。students 与 ask 之间为被动关系，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were asked。

4. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。turn A into B 表示"把 A 变成 B"，固定搭配，填 into。

5. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 flower vases，需用形容词形式，colour → colourful。

6. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than expected 提示用比较级，hard → harder。

7. **答案：creativity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。great + 名词，表示"极大的创造力"，create → creativity。

8. **答案：were displayed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。works 与 display 之间为被动关系，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were displayed。

9. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连词。how beautiful... could become 为感叹句式作宾语从句，表示"日常垃圾能变得多美"，填 how。

10. **答案：to reduce**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。a way to do sth. 表示"做某事的方式"，不定式作后置定语，填 to reduce。
