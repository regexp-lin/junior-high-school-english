# 02-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：急救知识 (First Aid Knowledge)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Knowing basic first aid can save (1) ______ (life) in emergency situations. Last month, our school invited a doctor from the local hospital (2) ______ (teach) us essential first aid skills. The training session lasted two hours and covered topics such as treating burns, stopping bleeding and performing CPR. The doctor explained that when someone (3) ______ (injure), the first thing to do is to stay calm and call for help. Panicking only makes the situation (4) ______ (bad). For minor burns, he advised us to hold the affected area under cool running water for at (5) ______ ten minutes. This helps reduce pain and prevents further damage (6) ______ the skin. We also practised CPR on training dummies. The doctor showed us how to place our hands (7) ______ (correct) on the chest and how to push at the right speed. He said that even if you are not a (8) ______ (medicine) professional, performing CPR can keep blood flowing to the brain until an ambulance (9) ______ (arrive). I found the training extremely useful and believe that first aid should be a required course in every school. After all, accidents can happen anywhere and at any time, (10) ______ being prepared can make the difference between life and death.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：lives**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`save lives` 拯救生命，复数表泛指。

2. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`invite sb. to do sth.` 邀请某人做某事。

3. **答案：is injured**
   - **考点**：一般现在时的被动语态。当某人受伤时，描述一般情况。

4. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：比较级。`makes the situation worse` 使情况更糟。

5. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`at least` 至少。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`damage to` 对……的损害。

7. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`place hands correctly` 正确地放置。

8. **答案：medical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`medical professional` 医疗专业人士，形容词修饰名词。

9. **答案：arrives**
   - **考点**：时间状语从句。`until an ambulance arrives`，从句用一般现在时表将来。

10. **答案：and**
    - **考点**：连词。并列关系，事故可能发生且做好准备很重要。
