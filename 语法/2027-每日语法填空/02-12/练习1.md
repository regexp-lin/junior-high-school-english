# 02-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：书信交友 (Pen Pals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Two years ago, my English teacher introduced a pen pal programme that connected students in our school (1) ______ teenagers in other countries. I was paired with a girl named Emma from Australia. At first, I was worried that my English was not good (2) ______ (much) to write letters. However, my teacher encouraged me to try, saying that practice was the best way (3) ______ (improve). My first letter to Emma was short and full of grammar (4) ______ (mistake), but she replied with a warm and friendly message that made me feel much (5) ______ (relaxed). Over the past two years, Emma and I (6) ______ (exchange) more than forty letters. Through our correspondence, I have learned a lot about Australian culture, including their love for barbecues and outdoor (7) ______ (activity). Emma has also become very interested (8) ______ Chinese festivals and food. She says her favourite Chinese dish is kung pao chicken, (9) ______ she learned to cook from a recipe I sent her. Writing to Emma has not only improved my English (10) ______ also given me a true friend on the other side of the world. We hope to meet each other in person someday.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`connect A with B` 把A和B联系起来。

2. **答案：enough**
   - **考点**：副词。`good enough to do`，`enough` 放在形容词后面。

3. **答案：to improve**
   - **考点**：不定式作定语。`the best way to improve` 提高的最好方式。

4. **答案：mistakes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`full of mistakes` 充满错误。

5. **答案：more relaxed**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much more relaxed`，`much` 修饰比较级。

6. **答案：have exchanged**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Over the past two years` 提示从过去到现在。

7. **答案：activities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`outdoor activities` 户外活动。

8. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be interested in` 对……感兴趣。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `kung pao chicken`。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but also...` 不但……而且……。
