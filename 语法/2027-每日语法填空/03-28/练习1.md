# 03-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园足球联赛 (School Football League)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The school football league is the (1) ______ (exciting) event of the spring term. Twelve classes compete in a round-robin format, and only the top four teams move on to the knockout stage.

Our class team has trained every Tuesday and Thursday since March. The captain, Wang Lei, is a fast striker (2) ______ has scored eight goals so far. He encourages everyone to pass the ball (3) ______ (frequent) instead of trying solo runs.

In last year's final, we lost to Class 5 in the last minute. This memory pushes us (4) ______ (train) harder. Our PE teacher says teamwork is even (5) ______ (important) than individual skill when the match reaches its closing moments.

Yesterday's game against Class 8 ended in a 2–2 draw. The crowd cheered loudly (6) ______ our goalkeeper blocked a dangerous shot. After the whistle, players from both sides shook hands — a scene (7) ______ showed true sportsmanship.

The league is not only about trophies. It teaches students how to handle pressure and respect opponents. Even students who do not play come to cheer, (8) ______ creates a strong sense of belonging.

If we reach the final again, I believe we (9) ______ (give) our best performance. Win or lose, the friendships built on the field will last.

Next week, we will face Class 3 on the new artificial turf. Rain or shine, the match (10) ______ (go) on as planned.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most exciting**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the most exciting event`，多音节用 most。

2. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `striker` 指人，从句缺主语。

3. **答案：more frequently**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。与“单干”对比，更频繁传球，多音节用 more frequently。

4. **答案：to train**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`push sb. to train harder` 促使某人更刻苦训练。

5. **答案：more important**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than individual skill` 提示，`more important`。

6. **答案：when / as**
   - **考点**：连词。`cheered when/as our goalkeeper blocked` 当守门员扑出射门时欢呼。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `scene` 指物，从句缺主语。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句关系代词。指代前面整句，营造归属感。

9. **答案：will give**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`If we reach` 主将从现，主句用 will give。

10. **答案：will go**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。`Rain or shine` 无论晴雨，比赛将按计划进行。
