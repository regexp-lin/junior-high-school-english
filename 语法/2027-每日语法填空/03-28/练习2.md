# 03-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：制作陶器 (Pottery Making)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Pottery making is one of the oldest crafts in human history. In China, delicate bowls and vases from ancient kilns still surprise visitors in museums around the world.

Last Sunday, I joined a pottery workshop in the cultural center. The teacher first showed us how (1) ______ (prepare) the clay by removing air bubbles. Then she placed a lump of wet clay on a spinning wheel and used her hands (2) ______ (shape) a simple cup.

When my turn came, the wheel turned faster (3) ______ I expected, and my cup collapsed at once. Everyone laughed kindly, and the teacher told me (4) ______ (not worry). "Clay teaches patience," she said.

On my third try, I managed to make a small bowl. It looked uneven, (5) ______ I smoothed the surface with a wooden tool. After that, we left our works in the studio (6) ______ they could dry slowly.

Two weeks later, I returned to paint patterns on my bowl — blue waves and a tiny fish. The final step was glazing and firing in a kiln. When I held the finished product, it felt (7) ______ (heavy) than I had imagined, as if it carried the weight of time.

Pottery connects us with the earth. Working with clay calms the mind and trains our hands. Many art schools now offer short courses (8) ______ teenagers can experience traditional skills.

I don't plan to become a professional potter, (9) ______ I will surely visit the studio again. Creating something useful with my own hands brought me quiet joy.

If you are looking for a hobby (10) ______ combines art and science, pottery might be a good choice.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`how to prepare` 疑问词 + 不定式。

2. **答案：to shape**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`use hands to shape` 用手塑形，不定式表目的。

3. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。`faster than I expected` 比预想的更快。

4. **答案：not to worry**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`tell me not to worry` 告诉某人不要担心。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。碗不平整，但用工具抹平表面。

6. **答案：so that / in order that**
   - **考点**：目的状语从句。留在工作室以便慢慢晾干。

7. **答案：heavier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than I had imagined` 提示，`heavy` → `heavier`。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `courses` 作抽象地点。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。不打算当职业陶艺家，但会再去工作室。

10. **答案：that / which**
    - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `hobby` 指物，从句缺主语。
