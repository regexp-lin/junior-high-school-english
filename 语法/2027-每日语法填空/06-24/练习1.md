# 06-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园美术展 (School Art Exhibition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last week, our school organized an art exhibition in the main hall. Students from all grades ___1___ (invite) to display their artworks. The exhibition included paintings, ___2___ (draw), paper cuts and clay sculptures. I submitted an oil painting of the Pearl River at sunset. It took me two weeks ___3___ (complete) it because I wanted every detail to be perfect. When I walked into the hall on the opening day, I was amazed ___4___ the variety of talent in our school. A girl from Grade 7 had created a ___5___ (wonder) portrait using only coloured pencils. Her lines were so fine that the face looked almost ___6___ (reality). The art teacher, Mr. Liu, told us ___7___ art is not about being perfect — it is about expressing ___8___ (you). The exhibition lasted for five days, and hundreds of ___9___ (visit) came to see it. I didn't win any prize, ___10___ the experience encouraged me to keep painting.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：were invited**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。students 与 invite 之间为被动关系，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were invited。

2. **答案：drawings**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。与 paintings、paper cuts、clay sculptures 并列，用复数 drawings。

3. **答案：to complete**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It takes sb. + time + to do sth. 句型，填 to complete。

4. **答案：by/at**
   - **考点**：介词。be amazed by/at 表示"对……感到惊叹"，填 by 或 at。

5. **答案：wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 portrait，需用形容词形式，wonder → wonderful。

6. **答案：real**
   - **考点**：词性转换。looked 为系动词，后接形容词作表语，表示"看起来几乎是真实的"，reality → real。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。told us that... 引导宾语从句，填 that。

8. **答案：yourself**
   - **考点**：代词。express yourself 表示"表达你自己"，主语隐含为 you，用反身代词 yourself。

9. **答案：visitors**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。hundreds of visitors 表示"数百位参观者"，visit → visitor → visitors。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：连词。"没有获奖"与"这段经历鼓励了我"之间为转折关系，用 but。
