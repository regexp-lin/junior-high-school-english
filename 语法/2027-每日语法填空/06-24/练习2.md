# 06-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：地震安全 (Earthquake Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Earthquakes can happen at any time and in almost any place. Knowing how to protect ___1___ (you) during an earthquake could save your life. Our school holds earthquake drills twice ___2___ year so that students know what to do in an emergency. When the alarm rings, we must ___3___ (immediate) get under our desks and cover our heads with our hands. It is important not ___4___ (run) towards the doors or windows because ___5___ (fall) objects may cause serious injuries. If you are outdoors, move to an open area away ___6___ buildings and trees. After the shaking stops, check ___7___ (you) and the people around you for injuries. Don't use ___8___ (elevator) — take the stairs instead. Last month, a ___9___ (power) earthquake hit a region in western China. Thanks to early warning systems and good preparation, many lives ___10___ (save). Being prepared is the best way to stay safe.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：yourself**
   - **考点**：代词。protect yourself 表示"保护你自己"，主语隐含为 you，用反身代词 yourself。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。twice a year 表示"一年两次"，固定搭配，填 a。

3. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 get，需用副词形式，immediate → immediately。

4. **答案：to run**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It is important not to do sth. 句型，not 放在 to 前面，填 to run。

5. **答案：falling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作定语，falling objects 表示"正在掉落的物体"，fall → falling。

6. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。away from 表示"远离"，固定搭配，填 from。

7. **答案：yourself**
   - **考点**：代词。check yourself 表示"检查你自己"，填 yourself。

8. **答案：elevators**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。don't use elevators 表示"不要使用电梯"，elevator 为可数名词，此处泛指，用复数。

9. **答案：powerful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 earthquake，需用形容词形式，power → powerful。

10. **答案：were saved**
    - **考点**：动词时态与语态。lives 与 save 之间为被动关系，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were saved。
