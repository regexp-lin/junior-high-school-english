# 09-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：开学第一天 (First Day of School)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Today was the first day of the new school year, and I felt both excited ___1___ a little nervous. I got up earlier than ___2___ (usually) and put on my new school uniform. After a quick breakfast, I said goodbye to my parents and rode my bicycle to school.

When I arrived, the campus ___3___ (decorate) with colourful banners and balloons for the opening ceremony. Hundreds of students were gathering on the playground, ___4___ (chat) happily with their friends about what they had done during the summer holiday.

Our headmaster gave ___5___ encouraging speech. He told us that Grade Nine would be the most ___6___ (challenge) year of our middle school life, but he believed that every student had the ability ___7___ (succeed). His words filled me with determination.

After the ceremony, I walked into my new classroom and found that some of my ___8___ (classmate) had changed a lot over the summer. Li Ming had grown much ___9___ (tall) and Wang Fang had cut her long hair short. Our new head teacher, Ms. Chen, introduced ___10___ (she) and shared her expectations for the coming year.

I can feel that this is going to be a year full of hard work and growth.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。**both... and...**（既……又……）为固定结构，表示"既兴奋又有点紧张"。

2. **答案：usual**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词 → 形容词）。**earlier than usual**（比平常更早）为固定搭配，`than` 后接形容词 **usual**（通常的）。

3. **答案：was decorated / had been decorated**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `the campus` 与 `decorate` 之间为被动关系（校园"被装饰"），叙述过去看到的场景用一般过去时被动 **was decorated**（或用过去完成时被动 **had been decorated** 强调装饰在到达前已完成）。

4. **答案：chatting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。句中已有谓语 `were gathering`，逗号后为非谓语结构。主语 `students` 与 `chat` 为主动关系，用现在分词 **chatting** 作伴随状语。

5. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`encouraging` 以元音音素开头，`speech` 为可数名词单数，表示"一场鼓舞人心的演讲"，故用不定冠词 **an**。

6. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。**the most + 多音节形容词** 构成最高级，**challenge** 的形容词形式为 **challenging**（具有挑战性的），修饰事物用 -ing 形式。

7. **答案：to succeed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**the ability to do sth.**（做某事的能力），不定式作后置定语修饰 `ability`，故填 **to succeed**。

8. **答案：classmates**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`some of my`（我的一些）后接可数名词复数，`classmate` 的复数形式为 **classmates**。

9. **答案：taller**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**much + 比较级** 表示"……得多"，`tall` 的比较级为 **taller**。

10. **答案：herself**
    - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。主语 `Ms. Chen` 为女性，"介绍自己"需用反身代词 **herself**。**introduce oneself**（自我介绍）。
