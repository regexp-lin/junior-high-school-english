# 09-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：航天科技 (Space Technology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's space technology has made ___1___ (remark) progress over the past two decades. From sending the first Chinese astronaut into space to building a permanent space station, each achievement has filled the nation ___2___ pride.

In 2003, Yang Liwei became the first Chinese astronaut ___3___ (travel) into space aboard the Shenzhou 5 spacecraft. The mission lasted about 21 hours, during ___4___ Yang orbited the Earth 14 times. His ___5___ (succeed) journey made China the third country in the world to send a human into space independently.

Since then, China ___6___ (launch) a series of Shenzhou missions. Astronauts have carried out spacewalks, conducted scientific experiments and even ___7___ (grow) plants in zero gravity. The completion of the Tiangong space station in 2022 was another major milestone. The station ___8___ (allow) Chinese scientists to carry out long-term research in space.

Looking ahead, China plans to send astronauts to the moon ___9___ the end of this decade. Scientists are also working on ___10___ (develop) new types of rockets that will be more powerful and environmentally friendly. The journey to the stars has only just begun.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：remarkable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。修饰名词 `progress` 需用形容词，**remark** 的形容词形式为 **remarkable**（显著的、了不起的）。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**fill... with...**（使……充满……）为固定搭配，`filled the nation with pride` 意为"让全国充满自豪"。

3. **答案：to travel**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**the first + 名词 + to do sth.**（第一个做某事的人），序数词后用不定式作后置定语，故填 **to travel**。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。`during which`（在此期间），**which** 指代前面的整个事件或时间段，介词 `during` 后接关系代词 **which**（不可用 that）。

5. **答案：successful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。修饰名词 `journey` 需用形容词，**succeed** 的形容词形式为 **successful**。

6. **答案：has launched**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**Since then**（从那以后）提示现在完成时；主语 `China` 为第三人称单数，故填 **has launched**。

7. **答案：grown**
   - **考点**：动词形式。与前面的 `carried out` 和 `conducted` 并列，均由助动词 `have` 支配构成现在完成时，`grow` 的过去分词为 **grown**。

8. **答案：allows**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。陈述当前事实用一般现在时，主语 `The station` 为第三人称单数，故填 **allows**。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。**by the end of...**（到……末）为固定搭配，表示"到本十年末"。

10. **答案：developing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**work on doing sth.**（致力于做某事），介词 `on` 后接动名词，故填 **developing**。
