# 06-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：民间故事 (Folk Tales)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Folk tales have been passed down from generation ___1___ generation for thousands of years. They teach important ___2___ (lesson) about kindness, honesty and wisdom. One of the most popular Chinese folk tales is "The Cowherd and the Weaver Girl". It tells the story of a poor young man ___3___ falls in love with a fairy from heaven. They get married and live ___4___ (happy) together until the Heavenly Queen separates them. Once a year, on the ___5___ (seven) day of the seventh lunar month, thousands of magpies form a bridge across the Milky Way so the couple can be ___6___ (unite). This romantic story has ___7___ (inspire) countless poems, songs and even festivals. In many parts of China, the day ___8___ (know) as Chinese Valentine's Day. My grandmother used to tell me this story when I was little. She always said that folk tales are not just old stories — they carry the ___9___ (wise) of our ancestors. I think it is our duty ___10___ (keep) them alive for future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。from generation to generation 表示"一代一代地"，固定搭配，填 to。

2. **答案：lessons**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。important lessons 表示"重要的教训/课程"，lesson → lessons。

3. **答案：who/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a poor young man（人），在从句中作主语，用 who 或 that。

4. **答案：happily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 live，需用副词形式，happy → happily。

5. **答案：seventh**
   - **考点**：序数词。the + 序数词 + day，表示"第七天"，seven → seventh。

6. **答案：united**
   - **考点**：词性转换。be united 表示"团聚"，过去分词作表语，unite → united。

7. **答案：inspired**
   - **考点**：动词时态。has + 过去分词构成现在完成时，inspire → inspired。

8. **答案：is known**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。the day 与 know 之间为被动关系，陈述一般事实用一般现在时的被动语态，填 is known。

9. **答案：wisdom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the + 名词 + of，carry the wisdom 表示"承载着智慧"，wise → wisdom。

10. **答案：to keep**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。it is our duty to do sth. 句型，it 为形式主语，真正主语为不定式，填 to keep。
