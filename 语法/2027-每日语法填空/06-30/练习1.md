# 06-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：时间管理 (Time Management)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Time management is a skill ___1___ every student needs to master. Many teenagers complain that they don't have enough time, ___2___ the real problem is often poor planning. A good way to start is ___3___ (make) a daily to-do list. Write down all the tasks you need to finish, and then put them in order of ___4___ (important). It is best ___5___ (deal) with the most difficult tasks first, when your energy level is the ___6___ (high). Avoid distractions such as checking your phone every few minutes. Studies show that it takes about fifteen minutes to regain full ___7___ (concentrate) after being interrupted. Another useful strategy is ___8___ (break) large tasks into smaller steps. Instead of telling yourself "I need to finish this project," try saying "I will work on it for thirty minutes." You ___9___ (be) surprised at how much you can achieve. Remember, time is fair to everyone — it gives each of us exactly twenty-four hours a day. The difference lies ___10___ how we choose to use it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a skill，在从句中作 master 的宾语，用 that 引导定语从句。

2. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。"抱怨时间不够"与"真正的问题是计划不好"之间为转折关系，用 but。

3. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。a good way to start is to do sth. 表示"一个好的开始方式是做某事"，填 to make。

4. **答案：importance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。of + 名词，in order of importance 表示"按重要性排序"，important → importance。

5. **答案：to deal**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It is best to do sth. 句型，it 为形式主语，真正主语为不定式，填 to deal。

6. **答案：highest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"精力最充沛的时候"，high → highest。

7. **答案：concentration**
   - **考点**：词性转换。full + 名词，表示"充分的注意力"，concentrate → concentration。

8. **答案：to break**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。Another useful strategy is to do sth.，不定式作表语，填 to break。

9. **答案：will be**
   - **考点**：动词时态。表示将来会发生的事，用一般将来时 will be。

10. **答案：in**
    - **考点**：介词。lie in 表示"在于"，固定搭配，填 in。
