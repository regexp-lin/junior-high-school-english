# 04-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：地震防灾 (Earthquake Preparedness)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Earthquakes cannot be predicted exactly, (1) ______ schools can teach students how to stay safe. Last week, our city held a drill, and everyone took it seriously.

When the alarm rang, we hid under desks (2) ______ covered our heads with schoolbags. The teacher shouted, "Wait (3) ______ the shaking stops!" After a minute, we lined up and walked downstairs in order.

In the playground, the headmaster explained (4) ______ we should not use elevators during an earthquake. He also showed us an emergency bag (5) ______ (fill) with water, food, and a flashlight.

At home, my family has made a simple plan. We decided on a meeting place near the community gate (6) ______ phones fail. My father checked the heavy shelves (7) ______ (tie) to the wall so they will not fall easily.

These steps may seem small, (8) ______ they can save lives. I hope every student remembers the rules and shares them (9) ______ younger brothers or sisters.

Being prepared does not mean living in fear. It means we respect nature and protect (10) ______ we love.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。无法准确预测，但学校能教如何避险。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。躲桌下并护头，并列用 `and`。

3. **答案：until / till**
   - **考点**：从属连词。等到摇晃停止，`wait until`。

4. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`explained why ...` 解释为什么不应使用电梯。

5. **答案：filled**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a bag filled with` 装满……的袋子。

6. **答案：if / when**
   - **考点**：从属连词。如果电话失灵/当电话失灵时，用 `if` 或 `when`。

7. **答案：tied**
   - **考点**：过去分词作宾补。`shelves tied to the wall` 被绑在墙上的架子，或 `had the shelves tied`。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。步骤看似小，但能救命。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`share sth. with sb.` 与……分享。

10. **答案：what**
    - **考点**：连接代词。`protect what we love` 保护我们所爱的（事物），宾语从句缺宾语。
