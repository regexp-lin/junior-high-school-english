# 04-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园诗歌朗诵 (School Poetry Recitation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school poetry recitation contest takes place every April. Students can choose classic poems (1) ______ write their own short pieces about spring or friendship.

This year, I spent two weeks preparing a poem (2) ______ (call) "The Road Not Taken". At first, I read it aloud in my bedroom, but my voice sounded flat. My Chinese teacher suggested that I pay attention (3) ______ rhythm and pauses.

On the day of the contest, the hall was decorated with paper flowers. When my name (4) ______ (call), I walked to the microphone with shaking hands. The lights felt hot, (5) ______ I took a deep breath and began.

The audience listened quietly (6) ______ I reached the last line. Then applause broke out, louder (7) ______ I had imagined. I did not win first prize, (8) ______ I received encouragement from teachers and classmates.

Poetry is not only about beautiful words; it is about honest feelings. I have learned to express myself (9) ______ (clear) than before. Next year, I will try (10) ______ (write) an original poem in English.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：并列连词。选择经典诗“或者”自己写，用 `or`。

2. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a poem called ...` 名为……的诗。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`pay attention to` 注意。

4. **答案：was called**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。名字“被叫到”，用 `was called`。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。灯光很热，但我深呼吸开始，转折。

6. **答案：until / till**
   - **考点**：从属连词。观众静静听到最后一行，`until`。

7. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。比较级 `louder than`。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。没得一等奖，但收到鼓励。

9. **答案：more clearly**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。修饰动词 `express`，`than` 提示比较级。

10. **答案：to write / writing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`try to do` 努力做；`try doing` 尝试做。此处“尝试写一首原创诗”可用 `to write` 或 `writing`。
