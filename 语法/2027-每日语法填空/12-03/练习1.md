# 12-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬至习俗 (Winter Solstice Customs)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Winter Solstice, also known __1__ Dongzhi in Chinese, is one of the most important __2__ (tradition) festivals in China. It usually falls __3__ December 21st or 22nd every year. On this day, people in northern China enjoy __4__ (eat) dumplings, while those in the south prefer tangyuan, a kind of sweet rice ball. My grandmother __5__ (make) dumplings for our family every Winter Solstice since I was a little child. She always says that dumplings can keep us __6__ (warm) during the cold winter. Last year, I __7__ (learn) how to make dumplings from her for the first time. Although my dumplings did not look as __8__ (good) as hers, they tasted wonderful. The Winter Solstice is not just about food. It is also a time __9__ family members get together and share their love. I think this is __10__ makes traditional festivals so special.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。be known as 被称为……，as 表示"作为"。

2. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 festivals，需要形容词 traditional。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。在具体某一天前用介词 on。

4. **答案：eating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。enjoy eating 喜欢吃……，enjoy 后接动名词。

5. **答案：has made**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since I was a little child 自我小时候以来，与现在完成时连用，用 has made。

6. **答案：warm**
   - **考点**：形容词作宾补。keep us warm 使我们保持温暖，keep + 宾语 + 形容词。

7. **答案：learned/learnt**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last year 去年，表示过去发生的事，用一般过去时 learned 或 learnt。

8. **答案：good**
   - **考点**：形容词原级（as...as 结构）。as good as 和……一样好，as...as 之间用原级。

9. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系副词）。a time when 一个……的时候，when 引导定语从句修饰 time。

10. **答案：what**
    - **考点**：主语从句（连接代词）。what makes... 是……的东西，what 引导主语从句，在从句中作主语。
