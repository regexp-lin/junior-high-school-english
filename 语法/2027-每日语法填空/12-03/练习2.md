# 12-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：少年航模比赛 (Youth Model Aircraft Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, our school __1__ (hold) a model aircraft competition. Students from different __2__ (class) were invited to take part in it. My partner Wang Lei and I spent two weeks __3__ (prepare) for the competition. We watched many videos online and __4__ (careful) studied how to build a strong model plane. On the day of the competition, more than fifty teams came to the playground. When it was our turn, I felt a little __5__ (nerve). However, Wang Lei encouraged me __6__ saying, "Just believe in ourselves!" We launched our plane into the sky. It flew __7__ (high) than most of the other planes and stayed in the air for nearly two minutes. __8__ the end, we won second prize. It was one of the __9__ (happy) moments in my school life. This experience taught me that teamwork and hard work can lead __10__ great results.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last month 上个月，描述过去发生的事，用一般过去时 held。

2. **答案：classes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。different classes 不同的班级，different 后接复数名词。

3. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 花时间做某事，用动名词 preparing。

4. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 studied，用副词 carefully。

5. **答案：nervous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。feel nervous 感到紧张，feel 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。

6. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。by saying 通过说……，by 表示方式方法。

7. **答案：higher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。higher than 比……更高，than 提示用比较级。

8. **答案：In**
   - **考点**：介词。in the end 最终、最后，为固定短语。

9. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the happiest 最快乐的……之一，one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：介词。lead to 导致、通向……，to 为介词。
