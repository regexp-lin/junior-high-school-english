# 04-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中医药文化 (Traditional Chinese Medicine)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, a museum of traditional Chinese medicine opened downtown, and our biology teacher organized a visit (1) ______ us. Inside the hall, we saw old books, bronze needles, and jars (2) ______ (fill) with dried herbs. A guide explained (3) ______ ancient doctors used plants to treat fevers and pain.

We were shown how (4) ______ (prepare) simple herbal tea that may help people sleep better. "TCM pays attention to the balance (5) ______ the body and nature," she said. Some students took notes carefully (6) ______ they could share the knowledge with grandparents at home.

At the end of the tour, we watched a short film (7) ______ introduced famous physicians in history. I used to think TCM was mysterious, (8) ______ the visit helped me understand its scientific side. I plan (9) ______ (read) more about herbs and healthy habits. I believe young people should be proud of this part of (10) ______ (we) culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`organize a visit for us` 为我们组织参观，`for` 表对象。

2. **答案：filled**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`jars filled with dried herbs` 过去分词作后置定语，表“装满干草药的罐子”，相当于 `which were filled with`。

3. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。`explained` 后宾语从句，说明古代医生“如何”用药草治病，表方式。

4. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`show sb. how to do` “疑问词 + 不定式”作直接宾语。

5. **答案：between**
   - **考点**：介词。`between... and...` 在……与……之间，指身体与自然的平衡。

6. **答案：so that/in order that**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导目的状语从句，“认真记笔记以便回家与长辈分享”，用 `so that`。

7. **答案：which/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `film`，从句缺主语，用 `which` 或 `that`。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾以为神秘”与“参观帮助理解科学一面”为转折。

9. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`plan to do sth.` 计划做某事。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 `culture`，`we` → 形容词性物主代词 `our`。
