# 04-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：地球日环保行动 (Earth Day Environmental Action)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

On Earth Day, our class volunteered to clean the riverbank near our school. We arrived early (1) ______ gloves and rubbish bags, ready to pick up plastic bottles and food packages. The local officer (2) ______ (brief) us on safety rules before we spread out along the path.

Some parents joined the activity (3) ______ they wanted to set a good example for their children. While we were working, a reporter interviewed students (4) ______ (ask) how they felt about protecting nature. "Every small action counts," said Lin Tao, (5) ______ voice sounded confident on camera.

By noon, we (6) ______ (collect) five large bags of waste, and the grass looked much (7) ______ (green) than in the morning. The officer thanked us and hoped similar events (8) ______ (hold) every term. I went home tired (9) ______ proud, knowing that I had done something useful for the planet.

Earth belongs to all of us, (10) ______ we should treat it with respect every day, not only on Earth Day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`arrive with gloves and bags` 带着手套和垃圾袋到达，表伴随。

2. **答案：briefed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`brief sb. on sth.` 向某人简要说明，叙述过去活动，用过去式 `briefed`。

3. **答案：because/as/since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导原因状语从句，说明家长参加的原因。

4. **答案：asking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，记者边采访边提问，`reporter` 与 `ask` 为主动关系。

5. **答案：whose**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句修饰 `Lin Tao`，在从句中作定语修饰 `voice`，用 `whose`。

6. **答案：had collected**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`By noon` 到中午为止已收集完，动作在过去某一时间点前完成，用 `had collected`。

7. **答案：greener**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，草地比早晨更绿，`green` → `greener`。

8. **答案：would be held**
   - **考点**：过去将来时被动语态。`hoped` 后宾语从句中，“希望类似活动每学期被举办”，从过去看将来且为被动，用 `would be held`。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`tired but proud` 累却自豪，表转折。

10. **答案：so**
    - **考点**：并列连词。前句“地球属于我们大家”与后句“应每天尊重它”为因果关系，用 `so` 引出结果。
