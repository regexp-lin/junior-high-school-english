# 02-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：户外探险 (Outdoor Adventure)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, my father took me on a three-day hiking trip in the mountains of northern Guangdong. It was the first time I (1) ______ (camp) outdoors, and I was both excited and a little scared. We packed our backpacks with food, water, a tent and sleeping bags. The trail was (2) ______ (steep) than I had expected, and after just two hours of walking, my legs were already aching. My father, who is an (3) ______ (experience) hiker, told me to take small steps and breathe (4) ______ (steady). Following his advice made a huge difference. By late afternoon, we reached a clearing near a stream and set (5) ______ our tent. As darkness fell, we built a small campfire and cooked a simple dinner. Sitting under the stars with nothing (6) ______ the sound of crickets around us was an incredible feeling. I had never seen so (7) ______ stars in my life — the sky looked like a painting. The next morning, we woke up early and continued our hike to the summit. The view from the top was absolutely (8) ______ (breathe). We could see rolling green mountains stretching all the way to the horizon. At that moment, I (9) ______ (understand) why my father loves hiking so much. The trip taught me that the best (10) ______ (reward) in life often come after the hardest efforts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：had camped**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`It was the first time + 过去完成时` 固定句型。

2. **答案：steeper**
   - **考点**：比较级。`steeper than I had expected` 比预期的更陡。

3. **答案：experienced**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`an experienced hiker` 有经验的徒步者。

4. **答案：steadily**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`breathe steadily` 平稳地呼吸。

5. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：短语动词。`set up the tent` 搭建帐篷。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：介词。`nothing but` 除了……什么都没有。

7. **答案：many**
   - **考点**：`so many + 可数名词复数`。`so many stars` 如此多的星星。

8. **答案：breathtaking**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`absolutely breathtaking` 令人叹为观止的。

9. **答案：understood**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`At that moment` 在那一刻。

10. **答案：rewards**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`the best rewards` 最好的回报。
