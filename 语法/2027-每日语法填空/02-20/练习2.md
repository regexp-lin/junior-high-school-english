# 02-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园环保行动 (School Environmental Action)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school launched a "Green Campus" initiative last September to reduce waste and promote (1) ______ (environment) awareness among students. The programme includes several practical measures. First, all classrooms (2) ______ (equip) with recycling bins for paper, plastic and glass. Students are taught how to sort their waste (3) ______ (proper). Second, the school canteen stopped (4) ______ (provide) disposable chopsticks and plastic cups. Instead, students are encouraged to bring their own reusable containers. At first, some students complained about the (5) ______ (convenient), but they gradually realized (6) ______ important it was for the environment. Third, a student-led "Green Team" was formed to carry (7) ______ regular inspections and organize environmental activities. As a member of the Green Team, I help check whether each class has sorted its waste correctly. We also organized a tree-planting event last March, during which over 200 trees (8) ______ (plant) on the school grounds. The results of the initiative have been (9) ______ (remark). Our school now produces 40% less waste than it did a year ago. The headmaster has praised the programme and said it sets a good example (10) ______ other schools in the district. I am proud to be part of this positive change.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：environmental**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`environmental awareness` 环保意识，形容词修饰名词。

2. **答案：have been equipped / were equipped**
   - **考点**：被动语态。教室被配备了回收箱。

3. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`sort waste properly` 正确地分类垃圾。

4. **答案：providing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`stop doing sth.` 停止做某事。

5. **答案：inconvenience**
   - **考点**：词性转换/前缀。`the inconvenience` 不方便，名词。

6. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：感叹句/宾语从句。`how important it was` 多么重要。

7. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：短语动词。`carry out inspections` 开展检查。

8. **答案：were planted**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。`last March` 提示过去时。

9. **答案：remarkable**
   - **考点**：形容词。`have been remarkable` 一直很显著，系动词后用形容词。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`set an example for` 为……树立榜样。
