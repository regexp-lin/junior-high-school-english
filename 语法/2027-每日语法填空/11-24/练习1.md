# 11-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：小型水力发电 (Small Hydropower)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Water has been used as a source of energy __1__ ancient times. In mountain villages, small streams and rivers can generate __2__ (electric) through small hydropower stations. Unlike huge dams, these stations have little impact __3__ the environment. My uncle lives in a village in Guizhou Province. A small hydropower station __4__ (build) there ten years ago. Before that, the village had no reliable power supply and people __5__ (depend) on candles and oil lamps at night. Now, the station provides clean energy for the __6__ (entire) village. The water flows from a hill and turns turbines that produce electricity. My uncle told me the station __7__ (run) by local villagers who were trained by engineers. It costs very __8__ (little) to maintain compared to solar or wind power. The government is encouraging more mountain communities __9__ (set) up similar stations. This approach not only provides energy __10__ also creates jobs for local people. Small hydropower is a great example of using nature wisely.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。since ancient times 自古以来，与现在完成时连用。

2. **答案：electricity**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。generate electricity 发电，electric 的名词形式为 electricity。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。have impact on 对……有影响，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：was built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。station 与 build 为被动关系，由 ten years ago 可知为一般过去时被动语态 was built。

5. **答案：depended**
   - **考点**：动词时态。before that 表示过去，描述之前的状况用一般过去时 depended。

6. **答案：entire**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词→形容词）。修饰名词 village，entirely 的形容词形式为 entire。

7. **答案：is run**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。station 与 run 为被动关系，描述现在的情况，用一般现在时被动语态 is run。

8. **答案：little**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词。costs very little 花费很少，little 用作副词。

9. **答案：to set**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。encourage sb. to do sth. 鼓励某人做某事，用不定式 to set。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。not only... but also... 不仅……而且……，为固定搭配。
