# 11-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学编织 (Learning to Knit)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, my grandmother taught me __1__ (knit). She has been knitting scarves, sweaters and hats __2__ she was a young girl. She says it is a skill that every generation should pass __3__. First, she showed me how to hold the __4__ (needle) and cast on the yarn. It looked simple when she did it, but my __5__ (finger) kept getting tangled. After many __6__ (fail) attempts, I finally managed to knit my first row. It was far __7__ perfect — some stitches were too tight __8__ others were too loose. Grandmother laughed gently and said, "Nobody gets it right the first time." She sat __9__ (patient) beside me for two hours, guiding my hands. By the end of the afternoon, I had knitted a small square. It was uneven and had several holes, __10__ I was still proud of it. Grandmother said she would help me finish a complete scarf by Christmas. I am looking forward to giving handmade gifts to my friends this winter.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to knit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。teach sb. to do sth. 教某人做某事，用不定式 to knit。

2. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。since she was a young girl 自从她还是个年轻女孩时起，与现在完成进行时连用。

3. **答案：down / on**
   - **考点**：副词。pass down 传承 / pass on 传递，两者均可。

4. **答案：needles**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。编织需要两根针，用复数 needles。

5. **答案：fingers**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。my fingers 我的手指（多根），用复数 fingers。

6. **答案：failed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。failed attempts 失败的尝试，fail 的过去分词 failed 作前置定语。

7. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。far from perfect 远非完美，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。some... others... 结构，用 while 表示对比或 and 表示并列。

9. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 sat，用副词 patiently。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。前句说不均匀有洞，后句说仍然自豪，语义转折，用 but。
