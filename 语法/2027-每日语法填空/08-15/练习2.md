# 08-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电子阅读与纸质阅读 (E-reading vs Paper Reading)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

With the development of technology, more and more people choose to read on electronic ___1___ (device) such as smartphones and tablets. Some people believe that e-reading is much ___2___ (convenient) than reading paper books because you can carry thousands of books in one small device.

However, many students and teachers still prefer ___3___ (read) paper books. A recent study showed that people who read on paper could remember information ___4___ (well) than those who read on screens. One possible reason is that turning pages ___5___ (help) the brain build a clearer picture of the text's structure. Another reason is that the bright light from screens may make our eyes ___6___ (tire) more quickly.

My English teacher, Mr. Wang, often tells us ___7___ we should spend at least 30 minutes a day reading paper books. He says that the feeling of holding a real book in your hands is something ___8___ an e-reader cannot provide. I agree with ___9___ (he) to some extent. There is indeed something special about the smell of a new book and the sound of turning pages.

Of course, both ways of reading have their own ___10___ (advantage). The most important thing is not what we read on, but that we keep reading every day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：devices**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`electronic`（电子的）修饰名词，后面列举了 `smartphones and tablets`，说明是多种设备，故用复数 **devices**。

2. **答案：more convenient**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**much + 比较级** 表示"……得多"，且后有 `than`；`convenient` 为多音节形容词，比较级为 **more convenient**。

3. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**prefer doing sth.**（更喜欢做某事），也可用 **prefer to do sth.**，但此处与前文 `choose to read` 形成对比，用 **reading** 更自然。

4. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。**than** 提示比较关系，修饰动词 `remember` 需用副词比较级，`well` 的比较级为 **better**。

5. **答案：helps**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。陈述客观事实用一般现在时；动名词短语 `turning pages` 作主语，谓语动词用第三人称单数形式 **helps**。

6. **答案：tired**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。**make + 宾语 + 宾补**，宾语 `our eyes` 指物/身体部位，但此处描述眼睛的状态"感到疲劳"，用 **tired** 作宾补。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词（宾语从句）。**tells us that...**（告诉我们……），`that` 引导宾语从句，说明告诉的内容。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `something` 为不定代词，其后的定语从句只能用 **that** 引导（不可用 which）。

9. **答案：him**
   - **考点**：代词。介词 `with` 后接宾格代词，`he` 的宾格形式为 **him**。

10. **答案：advantages**
    - **考点**：名词复数。**their own**（他们各自的）修饰名词，此处指两种阅读方式各有多个优点，故用复数 **advantages**。
