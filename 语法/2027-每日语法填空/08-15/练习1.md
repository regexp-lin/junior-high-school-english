# 08-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学做饭 (Learning to Cook)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the summer holiday, I decided to learn how ___1___ (cook) because I wanted to prepare a surprise dinner for my parents. I found a cooking video online and chose to make kung pao chicken, ___2___ is my father's favourite dish.

I went to the supermarket and bought all the ___3___ (ingredient) I needed: chicken, peanuts, dried chilli peppers and some vegetables. Back home, I washed the chicken ___4___ (careful) and cut it into small pieces. Then I followed the steps in the video one ___5___ one.

The ___6___ (difficult) part was controlling the heat. At first, the oil was too hot and it splashed everywhere. I nearly burned ___7___ (I)! After turning down the fire, I added the chicken pieces and stir-fried them until they turned golden brown. Next, I put in the sauce and peanuts. The kitchen was soon filled ___8___ a wonderful smell.

When my parents came home, they ___9___ (surprise) to see a full dish on the table. My mother said the chicken tasted even ___10___ (good) than the one from the restaurant. I was so proud of myself. From that day on, I have been cooking dinner for my family every weekend.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to cook**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**how to do sth.**（如何做某事），疑问词 + 不定式结构作宾语，故填 **to cook**。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `kung pao chicken` 指物，逗号后为非限制性定语从句，在从句中作主语，故用 **which**。

3. **答案：ingredients**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`all the`（所有的）后接可数名词复数，`ingredient` 的复数形式为 **ingredients**。

4. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `washed` 需用副词，`careful` 的副词形式为 **carefully**，表示"仔细地清洗"。

5. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。**one by one**（一个一个地，逐步地）为固定搭配，表示按步骤操作。

6. **答案：difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。修饰名词 `part` 需用形容词，**difficulty** 的形容词形式为 **difficult**。此处 `The difficult part` 意为"困难的部分"。

7. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。主语 `I` 与宾语指同一人，"差点烧到我自己"需用反身代词 **myself**。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**be filled with...**（充满……）为固定搭配，故填 **with**。

9. **答案：were surprised**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态/词性。主语 `they` 与 `surprise` 之间为被动关系（他们"被惊到"），用一般过去时被动语态 **were surprised**；也可视为系表结构 `were surprised`（感到惊讶）。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。**even + 比较级** 表示"更加……"，且后有 `than`，`good` 的比较级为不规则变化 **better**。
