# 10-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：雨林保护 (Rainforest Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tropical rainforests are often called the "lungs of the Earth" __1__ they produce a large amount of oxygen. They are home __2__ over half of the world's plant and animal species. However, rainforests are __3__ (disappear) at a shocking rate. Every minute, an area the size of a football field __4__ (destroy). The main reasons include illegal logging, farming and mining. When trees __5__ (cut) down, animals lose their habitats and many species face __6__ (extinct). The __7__ (destroy) of rainforests also speeds up global warming because trees absorb carbon dioxide. Fortunately, many organisations are working hard __8__ (save) these precious ecosystems. They encourage local people to find __9__ (sustain) ways of making a living. Some countries have created protected areas __10__ no logging is allowed. If we all take action now, there is still hope for the world's rainforests.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：because**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导原因状语从句，表示"因为它们产生大量氧气"，用 because。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。be home to 是……的栖息地/家园，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：disappearing**
   - **考点**：动词时态。be + doing 构成现在进行时，表示"正在消失"，用 disappearing。

4. **答案：is destroyed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 an area 与 destroy 为被动关系，every minute 提示一般现在时，用 is destroyed。

5. **答案：are cut**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。when 引导的时间状语从句，主语 trees 与 cut down 为被动关系，一般现在时用 are cut。

6. **答案：extinction**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词/动词→名词）。face extinction 面临灭绝，extinct 的名词形式为 extinction。

7. **答案：destruction**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the destruction of 对……的破坏，destroy 的名词形式为 destruction。

8. **答案：to save**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。work hard to do sth. 努力做某事，用不定式 to save。

9. **答案：sustainable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 ways，sustain 的形容词形式为 sustainable（可持续的）。

10. **答案：where**
    - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 protected areas 为地点名词，从句不缺主宾语，用 where 引导。
