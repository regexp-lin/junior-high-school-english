# 10-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：万圣节文化 (Halloween Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Halloween is a Western festival __1__ (celebrate) on October 31st every year. It has __2__ long history dating back to ancient Celtic traditions. People believed that on this night, ghosts would return to the world of the __3__ (live). To scare them away, people wore scary costumes and lit bonfires. Today, Halloween is mostly about fun. Children dress __4__ in costumes and go from door __5__ door, saying "Trick or treat!" to collect sweets. __6__ (carve) pumpkins into jack-o'-lanterns is another popular activity. Last year, our English teacher organised a Halloween party __7__ the whole class. We decorated the classroom with spider webs and __8__ (colour) lights. I dressed up as a vampire, and my friend wore a witch's hat. The __9__ (funny) costume was Tom's — he came as a giant banana! We played games, told ghost stories and had __10__ wonderful time. It was a great way to learn about Western culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：celebrated**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。festival 与 celebrate 为被动关系，用过去分词 celebrated。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a long history 悠久的历史，history 此处为可数名词，用 a。

3. **答案：living**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词/形容词）。the living 活人，live 的现在分词形式 living 用作名词。

4. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词。dress up 装扮、打扮，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。from door to door 挨家挨户，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：Carving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词短语作主语，Carving pumpkins 雕刻南瓜，注意首字母大写。

7. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。organised a party for 为……组织派对，for 表示"为了"。

8. **答案：colourful / colored**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 lights，colour 的形容词形式为 colourful（五彩的）或 colored（有颜色的）。

9. **答案：funniest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，funny 的最高级为 funniest（y→iest）。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。have a wonderful time 度过美好时光，为固定搭配。
