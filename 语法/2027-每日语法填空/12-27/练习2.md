# 12-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国民间年画 (Chinese New Year Paintings)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese New Year paintings, or nianhua, are a __1__ (colour) form of folk art with a long history. They __2__ (tradition) feature images of door gods, lucky fish, fat babies and beautiful flowers. People put them on walls and doors during the Spring Festival to express their __3__ (wish) for good luck and happiness. The art of nianhua __4__ (pass) down from generation to generation for hundreds of years. Different regions have their own __5__ (style). For example, Tianjin Yangliuqing nianhua are famous __6__ their bright colours and fine details. Last winter, I visited a nianhua workshop in Weifang, Shandong Province. An old artist showed me how he carved patterns __7__ a wooden block and then printed them onto paper. Each step required great __8__ (patient) and skill. He told me that __9__ (few) young people are willing to learn this craft nowadays. He hopes more people will pay attention to __10__ (protect) traditional folk arts before they disappear.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。a colourful form 一种色彩丰富的形式，修饰名词用形容词。

2. **答案：traditionally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 feature，用副词 traditionally。

3. **答案：wishes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。their wishes 他们的愿望，wish 为可数名词，此处表示多种祝愿用复数。

4. **答案：has been passed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（现在完成时被动语态）。for hundreds of years 数百年来，art 与 pass down 为被动关系，用 has been passed。

5. **答案：styles**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。their own styles 各自的风格，own 后接名词，不同地区有不同风格用复数。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。be famous for 以……闻名，for 表示原因。

7. **答案：on/into**
   - **考点**：介词。carved patterns on/into a wooden block 在木板上雕刻图案，on/into 表示位置或方向。

8. **答案：patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。great patience 极大的耐心，great 修饰名词，patient 的名词形式为 patience。

9. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。fewer young people 更少的年轻人，young people 为可数名词复数，用 fewer 修饰。

10. **答案：protecting**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。pay attention to protecting 注意保护，to 为介词，后接动名词。
