# 12-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：年终总结与新年目标 (Year-end Review and New Year Goals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As the year comes to __1__ end, our head teacher asked each of us to write a year-end review and set goals __2__ the coming year. Looking back, I realised that I __3__ (grow) a lot this year. In January, I was too shy __4__ (speak) in front of the class. Now I can give presentations __5__ (confident). My English grades have also improved __6__ (great) thanks to daily reading practice. Of course, there were some __7__ (disappoint) moments too. I failed my maths mid-term exam, which made me __8__ (feel) upset for days. But my teacher helped me find my weak points and I worked hard to catch up. For the new year, I have set three goals. First, I want to read at __9__ (little) twenty books. Second, I plan to learn basic guitar. Third, I hope to become a __10__ (good) listener so that I can support my friends when they need me. I believe that with effort, all these goals are achievable.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。come to an end 结束，为固定搭配，end 以元音音素开头用 an。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。goals for the coming year 来年的目标，for 表示"为了、对于"。

3. **答案：had grown/have grown**
   - **考点**：动词时态。realised that I had grown 意识到我已经成长了很多，过去完成时表示在意识到之前已发生的成长；也可用 have grown 表示到说话时的总结。

4. **答案：to speak**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。too shy to speak 太害羞而不敢说，"too + 形容词 + to do"结构。

5. **答案：confidently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 give，用副词 confidently。

6. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 improved，用副词 greatly。

7. **答案：disappointing**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ing 形容词）。disappointing moments 令人失望的时刻，修饰事物用 -ing 形容词。

8. **答案：feel**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式省略 to）。made me feel 使我感到，"make + 宾语 + 动词原形"结构。

9. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：固定短语。at least 至少，little 的最高级为 least。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。a better listener 一个更好的倾听者，暗含与现在的对比，用比较级 better。
