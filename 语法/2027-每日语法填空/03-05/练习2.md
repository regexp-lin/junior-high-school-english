# 03-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国高铁 (China's High-Speed Railway)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's high-speed railway, also known (1) ______ "gaotie", has become a symbol of modern China. With a total length of over 42,000 kilometres, it is the (2) ______ (long) high-speed rail network in the world.

The first high-speed railway line in China (3) ______ (open) in 2008, connecting Beijing and Tianjin. Since then, the network (4) ______ (expand) rapidly across the country. Today, passengers can travel from Beijing to Shanghai, a distance of about 1,300 kilometres, in just four and a half (5) ______ (hour).

High-speed trains are popular among Chinese people for several (6) ______ (reason). First, they are much (7) ______ (fast) than regular trains. Second, the tickets are not as expensive as plane tickets, (8) ______ makes them a good choice for most travellers. Third, the trains run very frequently, so passengers do not need to wait long.

I took the high-speed train for the first time last summer when my family (9) ______ (travel) to Xi'an. I was amazed by how smooth and quiet the ride was. Looking out of the window, I could see beautiful countryside (10) ______ (pass) by quickly. It was truly an enjoyable experience.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be known as` 被称为。

2. **答案：longest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the longest ... in the world` 世界上最长的。

3. **答案：was opened / opened**
   - **考点**：一般过去时（被动）。`in 2008` 表过去时间。铁路"被开通"用被动或主动均可。

4. **答案：has expanded**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 从那时起到现在，用现在完成时。

5. **答案：hours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`four and a half hours` 四个半小时，复数形式。

6. **答案：reasons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several reasons` 几个原因，`several` 后接可数名词复数。

7. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much faster than` 比……快得多。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。`which` 指代前面整个句子的内容。

9. **答案：travelled**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`last summer` 过去时间标志词。

10. **答案：passing**
    - **考点**：现在分词作宾补。`see sth. doing` 看到某物正在……，强调进行。
