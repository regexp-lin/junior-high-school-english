# 03-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园跳蚤市场 (School Flea Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every semester, our school organizes a flea market on the playground. Students can sell things they no longer need, such as old books, toys and (1) ______ (use) clothes. The money they earn is usually donated (2) ______ charity.

Last week, I decided to sell some of my old storybooks. I spent the whole evening (3) ______ (prepare) for the event. I cleaned each book carefully and put price tags on (4) ______ (they). The next morning, I arrived at school early and set up my stall near the front gate.

To my (5) ______ (surprised), many students came to my stall. A girl named Xiao Mei bought three books at once. She said she (6) ______ (look) for these books for a long time. Another boy asked me (7) ______ I could give him a discount, and I happily agreed.

By the end of the day, I (8) ______ (sell) almost all of my books and earned 85 yuan. Together with my classmates, we raised over 2,000 yuan for a children's hospital. It was one of the most (9) ______ (meaning) experiences I have ever had. I believe (10) ______ small act of kindness can make a big difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`used clothes` 旧衣服，已使用过的衣服。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`donate ... to ...` 把……捐给……。

3. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time (in) doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

4. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。介词 `on` 后用宾格代词 `them`。

5. **答案：surprise**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`to one's surprise` 令某人惊讶的是，名词形式。

6. **答案：had been looking**
   - **考点**：过去完成进行时。表示在买书之前一直在寻找，强调持续性。也可接受 `had looked`。

7. **答案：if / whether**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。一般疑问句变为宾语从句用 `if/whether`。

8. **答案：had sold**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`By the end of the day` 到那天结束时，截止过去某时间，用过去完成时。

9. **答案：meaningful**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most meaningful` 最有意义的，`meaning` → `meaningful`。

10. **答案：every / each**
    - **考点**：限定词。`every/each small act` 每一个小小的善举。
