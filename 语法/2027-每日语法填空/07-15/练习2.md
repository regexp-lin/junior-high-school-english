# 07-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：暑假旅行计划 (Summer Vacation Trip)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This June, my family and I are planning ___1___ exciting two-week summer vacation on China’s southern coast. We have bought round-trip plane tickets ___2___ a seaside city ___3___ welcomes tourists with summer concerts, bright night markets, and quiet bays where fishing boats return at dawn with silver nets. The hotel we reserved ___4___ (locate) on a side street near the beach, and workers pass out cold drinks ___5___ guests on hot afternoons. Dad saved our boarding passes on his phone and showed us how to read gate updates in case the schedule shifts at the last minute.

I ___6___ (pack) my clothes since last Monday because I worry about ___7___ (forget) my charger in the drawer at home, and my sister promises to check every socket with me before we lock the door. My brother wants ___8___ (sign) up for beginner surfing classes, but Mom insists he ___9___ (must) watch two safety videos before going into deep water. Grandma already asked for lighthouse photos and a bag of dried mango from the night market. We also circled a small hill behind the port on our paper map because Mom says the sunrise looks wider from there. Blog posts explain that coastal showers ___10___ (usual) come without much warning in July, so we always put folding umbrellas in our day packs and keep an extra towel in Dad’s backpack for sudden rain.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。可数名词单数 “vacation” 表泛指，前面形容词 **exciting** 以元音音素开头，用不定冠词 **an**（a 用于辅音音素开头）。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。“去某地的机票”常用 **tickets to + 地点**，与 tickets for 相比，to 更侧重目的地方向，中考阅读与语法填空中很常见。

3. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词为 “a seaside city”，从句 “___ welcomes tourists...” 缺主语且指物，用 **that** 或 **which** 均可（限制性定语从句）。

4. **答案：is located**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 “The hotel” 与 locate 为被动关系（旅馆“被坐落/位于”），用被动结构 **be located**；陈述现状用一般现在时 **is located**。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。pass out sth. **to** sb. 表示“把某物分发给某人”，**to** 引出间接宾语（guests）。

6. **答案：have packed / have been packing**
   - **考点**：动词时态。“since last Monday” 为现在完成时标志，主语为 I，用 **have packed**；若强调反复收拾、动作延续，**have been packing** 也合理，中考优先 **have packed**。

7. **答案：forgetting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。介词 **about** 后须接动名词作宾语，动词 forget 变为 **forgetting**（worry about doing sth.）。

8. **答案：to sign**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。want 后接不定式作宾语，即 **want to do sth.**，故填 **to sign**；sign up for 为固定搭配“报名参加”。

9. **答案：must**
   - **考点**：情态动词。insist 后的宾语从句中，妈妈坚持他“必须”先看安全视频，**must** 表示义务与要求；所给词为 must 时形式不变。

10. **答案：usually**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **come** 需用副词，形容词 usual 变为 **usually**（通常），说明七月份阵雨“常常”来得突然。
