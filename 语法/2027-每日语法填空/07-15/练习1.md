# 07-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：保护古建筑 (Protecting Ancient Buildings)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many cities across China take great pride in ___1___ ancient buildings that have stood quietly for hundreds of years. These remarkable structures, ___2___ were carefully built with wood, brick, and stone, have become symbols of local culture and attract visitors from both home and abroad. In narrow old lanes, tour guides often describe emperors, artists, and daily life from centuries past so that young listeners understand why the walls deserve respect. Unfortunately, air pollution, acid rain, and extreme weather ___3___ (make) the materials weaker year by year. Some careless tourists also ___4___ (write) their names or draw pictures on the walls, which seriously damages the surface that experts are trying to preserve.

The local government has passed strict laws ___5___ (protect) these valuable heritage sites. Since last year, a professional conservation team ___6___ (check) each historic compound every month and sends detailed reports if new cracks appear. Skilled workers must be ___7___ (patience) because traditional repairs take several months and require rare crafts passed down through families. More and more middle school students have joined volunteer clubs ___8___ (learn) how to measure old beams and take clear photos for digital archives that scientists can study. ___9___ every citizen follows the visiting rules and refuses to touch the carvings, these national treasures will survive for our grandchildren. Remember, every old doorway and painted beam ___10___ (matter) to the story of who we are.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。主语为 “Many cities”，空格后为名词短语 “ancient buildings”，需用形容词性物主代词 **their** 表示“这些城市的古建筑”，不能用定冠词 the（此处强调归属关系）。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。逗号后为非限制性定语从句，先行词为 “These remarkable structures”，指物且在从句中作主语，用 **which**；that 一般不用于非限制性定语从句。

3. **答案：have made**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 “year by year” 与 “weaker over time” 表示从过去持续到现在的影响，主语为复数 “pollution, acid rain, and extreme weather”（视为复数概念），用现在完成时 **have made**。

4. **答案：have written**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与上一句并列，同样表示已经造成后果的动作，主语 “Some careless tourists” 为复数，用现在完成时 **have written**，与 “draw” 并列说明破坏行为。

5. **答案：to protect**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。“通过法律”的目的是“保护遗址”，用 **to protect** 表目的；pass laws to do sth. 为常见结构。

6. **答案：has checked / has been checking**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间标志 “Since last year” 要求用现在完成时；主语 “a professional conservation team” 为单数，谓语用 **has checked**。若强调“反复检查、一直进行”，**has been checking** 也可接受，中考中完成时为主流答案。

7. **答案：patient**
   - **考点**：词性转换。系动词 **be** 后需形容词作表语，名词 patience 变为形容词 **patient**（耐心的）。

8. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。join clubs 的目的是学习，用不定式 **to learn** 作目的状语，与练习 1 中 “clubs to do” 类结构一致。

9. **答案：If**
   - **考点**：连词。主句为 “these national treasures will survive”，从句为条件 “every citizen follows...”，引导条件状语从句用 **If**；注意句首大写。

10. **答案：matters**
    - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。主语为 “every old doorway and painted beam”，由 **every** 修饰的并列主语通常视为单数概念，谓语用第三人称单数；陈述事实用一般现在时 **matters**。
