# 04-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：农村电商 (Rural E-commerce)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, e-commerce has helped farmers sell fruit and vegetables directly (1) ______ customers in cities. My uncle, (2) ______ lives in a mountain village, now runs an online shop on his phone.

Last summer, I visited his home and watched him pack oranges (3) ______ (send) to buyers thousands of miles away. He explained that good photos and honest descriptions (4) ______ (matter) more than fancy words. "If the fruit is sweet, customers will come back," he said.

Young villagers have also learned (5) ______ (use) short videos to show how tea leaves (6) ______ (pick) and dried. Thanks to their effort, the village's income (7) ______ (rise) steadily since 2022.

I used to think the countryside was far behind the city, (8) ______ technology has shortened the distance. I believe rural e-commerce will become even (9) ______ (success) when more roads and networks are built. I hope I can help (10) ______ (promote) local products after I graduate.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`sell sth. to sb.` 把某物卖给某人。

2. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句修饰 `uncle`，从句缺主语，指人，用 `who`。

3. **答案：to send**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，“打包橙子以便寄给买家”。

4. **答案：matter**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。宾语从句陈述客观事实，主语 `photos and descriptions` 复数，谓语用动词原形 `matter`。

5. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`learn to do sth.` 学习做某事。

6. **答案：are picked**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。茶叶“被采摘”，描述生产过程，用 `are picked`。

7. **答案：has risen**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`since 2022` 表示从过去持续到现在的增长，主语 `income` 为单数，用 `has risen`。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾以为农村落后”与“科技缩短距离”为转折。

9. **答案：more successful**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `even` 修饰比较级，表“更成功”，`success` → `successful` → `more successful`。

10. **答案：(to) promote**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`help (to) do sth.` 帮助做某事，不定式作宾语补足语，`to` 可省略。
