# 04-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界舞蹈日 (World Dance Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

April 29 is World Dance Day, (1) ______ dancers in many countries perform in streets, theaters, and schools. Our school held a short show on the playground, (2) ______ students presented folk dances from different provinces.

I practiced with my team for three weeks before we stepped onto the stage. The music started, and our hearts beat (3) ______ (fast) than usual. Though we were nervous, we smiled (4) ______ each other and finished the routine without missing a step.

The audience clapped loudly (5) ______ the music ended. A foreign teacher told us that our movements were even (6) ______ (grace) than she had expected. After the performance, we took photos (7) ______ colorful ribbons still tied around our wrists.

Dancing teaches me that confidence grows (8) ______ practice. I used to hide at the back of the room, (9) ______ now I enjoy standing in the front row. I hope I will keep (10) ______ (train) after World Dance Day is over.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。非限制性定语从句修饰 `World Dance Day`，从句中缺时间状语，“在这一天各国舞者表演”，用 `when`。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。非限制性定语从句修饰 `playground`，从句中缺地点状语，“在操场上学生展示……”，用 `where`。

3. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。句中有 `than`，`fast` → `faster` 修饰动词 `beat`。

4. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`smile at each other` 相视而笑。

5. **答案：when/after/as soon as**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导时间状语从句，“音乐结束时报以掌声”，可用 `when`/`after`/`as soon as`。

6. **答案：more graceful**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`grace` 需先变为形容词 `graceful`，再构成 `more graceful`。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`with + 名词` 表伴随状语，“手腕仍系彩带时拍照”。

8. **答案：through**
   - **考点**：介词。`through practice` 通过练习，表方式。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾躲在后排”与“现在喜欢站前排”为转折。

10. **答案：training**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`keep doing sth.` 持续做某事，动名词作宾语。
