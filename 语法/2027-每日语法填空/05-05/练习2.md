# 05-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：减少食物浪费 (Reducing Food Waste)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every year, large amounts of food 1. ______ (throw) away while many people still go hungry. Our school has started 2. ______ project called "Clean Plate" to change this habit. First, the cooks prepare meals according to 3. ______ number of students who order lunch online. Second, we are encouraged 4. ______ (take) only what we can eat. On the wall of the dining hall, posters remind us 5. ______ food does not grow on shelves; it comes from farmers' hard work. Last month, the waste 6. ______ (weigh) after lunch became 30% less than before. My friend Tim used 7. ______ leave half a bowl of rice, but now he finishes 8. ______ every time. Teachers say saving food is not 9. ______ small thing—it shows respect for life. If each of us pays attention, the world can become 10. ______ (good) little by little.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：are thrown**
   - **考点**：被动语态与主谓一致。**amounts** 作主语时谓语常用复数；食物被扔掉，用一般现在时的被动 **are thrown**。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **project** 单数，表泛指“一个项目”，故填 **a**。

3. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。**the number of...** 表示“……的数量”，故填 **the**。

4. **答案：to take**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be encouraged to do sth.** 表示“被鼓励做某事”，故填 **to take**。

5. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**remind us** 后接宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 **that** 引导。

6. **答案：weighed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**the waste weighed after lunch** 中 **weighed** 为过去分词作后置定语，相当于“午饭后被称过重后的废弃物”，故填 **weighed**。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。**used to do sth.** 表示“过去常常做某事”，故填 **to**。

8. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。指代 **a bowl of rice** 或 **the rice**，用 **it** 作 **finishes** 的宾语。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**not a small thing** 表示“不是一件小事”，故填 **a**。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。**little by little** 暗示与之前比较，用 **good** 的比较级 **better**。
