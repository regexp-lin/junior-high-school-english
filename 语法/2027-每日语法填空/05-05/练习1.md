# 05-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园美术展 (School Art Exhibition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's art exhibition opened 1. ______ the hall last Monday and will last for ten days. Students from every grade 2. ______ (send) in over two hundred works, including paintings, photos, and paper art. My painting shows 3. ______ sunset over the river, and I spent three weekends 4. ______ (finish) the details. When visitors walked in, many of 5. ______ stopped in front of a huge dragon made of recycled bottles. The artist explained 6. ______ she chose those colors to show hope. Our headmaster praised the show and said art helps us see life 7. ______ (different). He also thanked the parents 8. ______ helped carry the heavy frames. On the closing day, there will be 9. ______ award ceremony for the best pieces. I feel lucky 10. ______ (be) part of such a creative team.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。“在大厅里举办”用 **in the hall**，故填 **in**。

2. **答案：sent**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述已发生的事，**send in** 的过去式为 **sent**，与 **works** 搭配表示“提交作品”。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **sunset** 在此表泛指“一幅河上的日落景象”，sunset 以辅音音素开头，故填 **a**。

4. **答案：finishing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**spend time (in) doing sth.** 表示“花费时间做某事”，故填 **finishing**。

5. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词。指代 **visitors**，作介词 **of** 的宾语，用宾格 **them**。

6. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连接词。宾语从句表示“她为何选择那些颜色”，缺原因状语含义，故填 **why**。

7. **答案：differently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **see** 需用副词，形容词 different 的副词形式为 **differently**。

8. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 **parents**（人），定语从句中作主语，故填 **who** 或 **that**。

9. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**award** 以元音音素开头，**an award ceremony** 表示“一场颁奖典礼”，故填 **an**。

10. **答案：to be**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**feel lucky to do sth.** 表示“做某事感到很幸运”，故填 **to be**。
