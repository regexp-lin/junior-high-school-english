# 07-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：移动支付 (Mobile Payment)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China is leading the world in mobile payment technology. In most Chinese cities, people can pay for almost ___1___ (any) with their smartphones — from restaurant meals to taxi rides. Apps like WeChat Pay and Alipay have made ___2___ possible to go out without carrying cash. Even small street vendors now accept mobile ___3___ (pay). According to a recent report, over 90% of urban residents in China use mobile payment ___4___ (regular). This technology is not only convenient ___5___ also very secure. Each transaction ___6___ (protect) by passwords, fingerprints or facial recognition. My grandmother, ___7___ is 75 years old, learned to use mobile payment last year. At first, she was worried about ___8___ (make) mistakes, but now she uses it confidently. She even sends red envelopes to ___9___ (we) during festivals through her phone! While mobile payment brings many benefits, experts remind us ___10___ (keep) our personal information safe and avoid sharing our payment codes with strangers.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：anything**
   - **考点**：代词。pay for anything 表示"支付任何东西"，any → anything。

2. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。make it possible to do sth.，it 为形式宾语，真正宾语为后面的不定式，填 it。

3. **答案：payment/payments**
   - **考点**：词性转换。mobile payment 为名词短语，表示"移动支付"，pay → payment。

4. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 use，需用副词形式，regular → regularly。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。not only...but also... 表示"不仅……而且……"，固定搭配，填 but。

6. **答案：is protected**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。each transaction 与 protect 之间为被动关系，陈述一般事实用一般现在时的被动语态，填 is protected。

7. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 my grandmother（人），非限制性定语从句中作主语，用 who。

8. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。about 为介词，后接动名词，worried about making mistakes 表示"担心犯错"，填 making。

9. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词。sends red envelopes to us 表示"给我们发红包"，介词 to 后接宾格，we → us。

10. **答案：to keep**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。remind sb. to do sth. 表示"提醒某人做某事"，填 to keep。
