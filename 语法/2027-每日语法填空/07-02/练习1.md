# 07-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：森林徒步 (Forest Hiking)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Hiking through a forest is one of the best ways ___1___ (relax) and get close to nature. Last weekend, I went on a forest hike with a group of friends. The trail was about eight kilometres long, ___2___ (wind) through tall pine trees and over small streams. We set off early in the morning while the air was still cool and fresh. Birds ___3___ (sing) happily in the treetops as we walked. Along the way, our guide pointed ___4___ several rare plants and explained how they survived in the wild. The ___5___ (steep) section of the trail took us nearly an hour to climb. By the time we reached the top, everyone was ___6___ (exhaust) but thrilled. The view from the summit ___7___ (be) absolutely stunning — layers of green mountains stretched as far as the eye could see. We took plenty of photos ___8___ sat down for a picnic lunch. I was amazed at ___9___ peaceful the forest was compared to the noisy city. My friend joked that he wished he could live there forever. Hiking has taught me to slow ___10___ and appreciate the beauty around us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to relax**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。the best ways to do sth. 表示"做某事最好的方式"，不定式作后置定语，填 to relax。

2. **答案：winding**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，the trail..., winding through...，填 winding。

3. **答案：were singing**
   - **考点**：动词时态。as we walked 表示"当我们走的时候"鸟一直在唱，用过去进行时 were singing。

4. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。point out 表示"指出"，固定搭配，填 out。

5. **答案：steepest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"最陡的一段"，steep → steepest。

6. **答案：exhausted**
   - **考点**：词性转换。was 后接形容词作表语，描述人的感受用 exhausted（精疲力竭的）。

7. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。the view 为单数主语，叙述过去事件用一般过去时，be → was。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。took photos 与 sat down 为两个并列动作，用 and 连接。

9. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连词。how peaceful the forest was 为感叹句式作宾语从句，表示"森林有多安宁"，填 how。

10. **答案：down**
    - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。slow down 表示"慢下来"，固定搭配，填 down。
