# 08-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园跳蚤市场 (School Flea Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every year before the summer holiday, our school holds a flea market on the playground. Students can sell things they no longer need and buy second-hand items ___1___ low prices. This year's flea market was the ___2___ (big) one we had ever had.

Each class set up a stall decorated with hand-drawn posters. My group sold old books, stationery and some homemade snacks. To attract more ___3___ (customer), we even put up a sign that said "Buy two, get one free!" It ___4___ (work) really well — our stall was always crowded with people.

The most popular item at our stall was a collection of comic books ___5___ (own) by my classmate Liu Yang. He had kept them in perfect condition, so many students were willing ___6___ (pay) a good price for them. Within an hour, all the comics ___7___ (sell) out.

After the flea market, we counted the money we had earned. Our class made a total of 860 yuan, which was more ___8___ any other class. We decided ___9___ (donate) half of the money to a charity and use the rest to buy new books ___10___ our class library.

The flea market taught us the value of recycling and sharing.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。**at low prices**（以低价）为固定搭配，表示价格用介词 **at**。

2. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**the + 最高级** 与 `we had ever had` 搭配，`big` 的最高级为 **biggest**（双写末尾辅音字母加 -est）。

3. **答案：customers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`more`（更多的）修饰可数名词复数，`customer` 的复数形式为 **customers**。

4. **答案：worked**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去发生的事用一般过去时，`work` 的过去式为 **worked**。

5. **答案：owned**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a collection of comic books` 与 `own` 之间为被动关系（漫画书"被拥有/属于"），用过去分词 **owned** 作后置定语。

6. **答案：to pay**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be willing to do sth.**（愿意做某事），故填 **to pay**。

7. **答案：had been sold**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。**Within an hour**（一小时之内）表示在过去某段时间内完成的动作；主语 `all the comics` 与 `sell` 之间为被动关系，用过去完成时被动语态 **had been sold**（out）。

8. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词/介词。**more than...**（比……多），用于比较结构，故填 **than**。

9. **答案：to donate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**decide to do sth.**（决定做某事），故填 **to donate**。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。**buy sth. for...**（为……买某物），表示目的，"为我们班级图书馆买新书"，故填 **for**。
