# 08-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：人工湿地 (Artificial Wetlands)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Wetlands are areas of land ___1___ are covered with shallow water for most of the year. They are sometimes called "the kidneys of the earth" because they can ___2___ (natural) filter and clean dirty water. However, many natural wetlands ___3___ (destroy) over the past century due to farming and urban development.

To solve this problem, scientists have come up ___4___ the idea of building artificial wetlands. These are man-made systems ___5___ (design) to copy the water-cleaning functions of natural wetlands. They use special plants and soil layers to remove ___6___ (harm) substances from wastewater.

One successful example is the Qunli Stormwater Park in Harbin. Before it was built, heavy rain often caused ___7___ (flood) in nearby neighbourhoods. The park's wetland system now ___8___ (collect) rainwater, filters it through layers of gravel and plants, and releases clean water back into the river. The park has also become a popular place for residents ___9___ (relax) and enjoy nature.

Artificial wetlands are much ___10___ (cheap) to build and maintain than traditional water treatment plants. They also provide habitats for birds and other wildlife. As more cities face water pollution challenges, artificial wetlands may become an important solution for the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `areas of land` 指物，在从句中作主语，故用关系代词 **that** 或 **which**。

2. **答案：naturally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `filter and clean` 需用副词，**natural** 的副词形式为 **naturally**，表示"自然地过滤"。

3. **答案：have been destroyed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。**over the past century**（在过去一个世纪中）提示现在完成时；主语 `many natural wetlands` 与 `destroy` 之间为被动关系，故填 **have been destroyed**。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**come up with...**（想出……）为固定搭配，表示"想出了建造人工湿地的主意"。

5. **答案：designed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`man-made systems` 与 `design` 之间为被动关系（系统"被设计"），用过去分词 **designed** 作后置定语。

6. **答案：harmful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词/动词 → 形容词）。修饰名词 `substances` 需用形容词，**harm** 的形容词形式为 **harmful**（有害的）。

7. **答案：floods / flooding**
   - **考点**：名词。动词 `caused` 后接名词作宾语，**flood** 可用复数 **floods**（多次洪水）或不可数形式 **flooding**（洪涝灾害）。

8. **答案：collects**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。`now`（现在）提示一般现在时，描述目前的运作方式；主语 `The park's wetland system` 为第三人称单数，故填 **collects**。

9. **答案：to relax**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**a place for sb. to do sth.**（一个供某人做某事的地方），不定式 **to relax** 作后置定语修饰 `place`。

10. **答案：cheaper**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。**much + 比较级** 表示"……得多"，且后有 `than`，`cheap` 的比较级为 **cheaper**。
