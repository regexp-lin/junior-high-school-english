# 07-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：登山运动 (Mountain Climbing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mountain climbing is a sport that tests both physical ___1___ (strong) and mental willpower. Last October, my father and I climbed Baiyun Mountain in Guangzhou. We chose the steepest route, ___2___ starts from the south gate and goes straight to the top. The first kilometre was easy, but ___3___ we climbed higher, the path became increasingly narrow and rocky. My legs ___4___ (ache) and I wanted to stop several times. My father kept ___5___ (encourage) me, saying, "Don't look down. Just focus on the next step." After two hours of ___6___ (continue) climbing, we finally reached the summit. The view from the top was ___7___ (absolute) breathtaking — we could see the entire city ___8___ (spread) below us like a giant map. Other climbers congratulated us, and we felt a strong sense of ___9___ (accomplish). My father said that climbing a mountain is much like facing challenges in life — the ___10___ (hard) it is, the more rewarding the view at the top.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：strength**
   - **考点**：词性转换。physical strength 为名词短语，表示"体力"，strong → strength。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，先行词为 the steepest route，用 which。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。as we climbed higher 表示"随着我们爬得越高"，as 引导时间/方式状语从句。

4. **答案：were aching/ached**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述当时的身体状态，用过去进行时 were aching（持续疼痛）或一般过去时 ached。

5. **答案：encouraging**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。keep doing sth. 表示"持续做某事"，encourage → encouraging。

6. **答案：continuous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 climbing，需用形容词形式，continue → continuous。

7. **答案：absolutely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 breathtaking，需用副词形式，absolute → absolutely。

8. **答案：spread/spreading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。see sth. spread/spreading below 表示"看到城市展开在下方"，过去分词 spread（结果状态）或现在分词 spreading 均可。

9. **答案：accomplishment/achievement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。a sense of + 名词，表示"一种成就感"，accomplish → accomplishment。

10. **答案：harder**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。the + 比较级..., the + 比较级... 表示"越……越……"，hard → harder。
