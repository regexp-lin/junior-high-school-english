# 07-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家乡的变化 (Changes in My Hometown)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My hometown has changed a lot over the past ten years. When I was a child, there ___1___ (be) only a few small shops along the main street. People had to travel to the city centre for anything ___2___ (special). Now, a large shopping mall ___3___ (build) right in the middle of town. There are also new parks, a modern library and ___4___ sports centre with a swimming pool. The roads have been ___5___ (wide) and new bus lines connect our town to the city. My grandfather often says he can ___6___ (hard) recognise the place where he grew up. What ___7___ (impress) me most is the new riverside walk. It stretches for three kilometres along the river, with trees, benches and ___8___ (colour) flower beds on both sides. In the evening, families go there ___9___ (walk), chat and enjoy the fresh air. Although I am happy about the ___10___ (develop), I sometimes miss the quiet little town of my childhood.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：were**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。there be 句型，最近的名词为 shops（复数），叙述过去情况用 were。

2. **答案：special**
   - **考点**：词性转换。anything special 表示"任何特别的东西"，不定代词后接形容词作后置定语。注意此处 special 不需变形，已是形容词。

3. **答案：has been built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。mall 与 build 之间为被动关系，表示已建成且对现在有影响，用现在完成时的被动语态 has been built。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。a sports centre 表示"一个体育中心"，可数名词单数用不定冠词 a。

5. **答案：widened**
   - **考点**：词性转换/动词时态。have been widened 构成现在完成时的被动语态，表示"道路已被拓宽"，wide → widened。

6. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。can hardly recognise 表示"几乎认不出"，hard → hardly（几乎不），注意语义变化。

7. **答案：impresses**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。what 引导主语从句，视为第三人称单数，陈述现在的感受用一般现在时，impress → impresses。

8. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 flower beds，需用形容词形式，colour → colourful。

9. **答案：to walk**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。go there to walk 表示"去那里散步"，不定式作目的状语，填 to walk。

10. **答案：development**
    - **考点**：词性转换。the + 名词，about the development 表示"关于发展"，develop → development。
