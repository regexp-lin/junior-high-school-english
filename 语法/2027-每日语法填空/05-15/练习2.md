# 05-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：青少年创业 (Teen Entrepreneurship)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Starting ___1___ small business is not easy for teenagers, ___2___ many young people still try to turn their ideas into reality with courage and patience. Last summer, my classmate Lily ___3___ (launch) an online shop ___4___ sells handmade cards with warm messages inside. She spent weeks ___5___ (design) each product and learning how to manage money, answer messages, and pack orders carefully. ___6___ first, only a few friends supported her, but her sales ___7___ (grow) quickly after she posted short videos on social media. Her parents were worried about her grades, ___8___ they agreed that she should balance study and work instead of giving up her dream. Now Lily is much ___9___ (confident) than before because she has learned to solve problems on her own and communicate with customers politely. Teen entrepreneurship teaches us ___10___ (be) creative and responsible when we face challenges step by step.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。business 可数，表示“创办一家小企业”，small 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 a。

2. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前句说对青少年不容易，后句说仍有人尝试，语义转折，用 but。

3. **答案：launched**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 Last summer 表示过去，谓语动词用一般过去时 launched。

4. **答案：which / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰先行词 shop，在从句中作主语，指物，用 which 或 that。

5. **答案：designing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定结构 spend time (in) doing sth.，表示“花费时间做某事”，故用 designing。

6. **答案：At**
   - **考点**：介词。固定短语 at first，表示“起初”，句首首字母大写。

7. **答案：grew**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去发生的变化，after 引导的从句中主句动作发生在过去，用一般过去时 grew。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。父母担心成绩与同意她平衡学业与工作之间为转折关系，用 but。

9. **答案：more confident**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 than before，且 much 修饰比较级，confident 为多音节形容词，比较级为 more confident。

10. **答案：to be**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定结构 teach sb. to do sth.，表示“教某人做某事”，故用不定式 to be。
