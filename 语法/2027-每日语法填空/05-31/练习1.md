# 05-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：运动会 (Sports Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school sports day was held ___1___ the last Thursday of October. Students from all grades gathered on the playground early ___2___ the morning. The 100-metre race was the most ___3___ (excite) event of the day. When the starting gun fired, eight ___4___ (runner) dashed forward like arrows. Li Ming, a boy from our class, ran much ___5___ (fast) than anyone else and crossed the finish line first. We cheered so ___6___ (loud) that our voices went hoarse. In the afternoon, the relay race ___7___ (attract) even more attention. Our team was behind at the beginning, ___8___ we didn't give up. The last runner caught up and overtook the leader just before ___9___ (reach) the finish line. That moment taught ___10___ an important lesson: never stop trying until the very end.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。在具体某一天用介词 on，on the last Thursday of October。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in the morning 表示"在早上"，固定搭配。

3. **答案：exciting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the most + 形容词构成最高级，修饰名词 event（物），用 exciting（令人兴奋的）。

4. **答案：runners**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。eight 后接可数名词复数，runner → runners。

5. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。than 提示用比较级，修饰动词 ran，fast → faster。

6. **答案：loudly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。so...that... 结构中修饰动词 cheered，需用副词形式，loud → loudly。

7. **答案：attracted**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文叙述过去事件，In the afternoon 承接上文，用一般过去时，attract → attracted。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。"我们队一开始落后"与"我们没有放弃"之间为转折关系，用 but。

9. **答案：reaching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。before 为介词，后接动名词形式，reach → reaching。

10. **答案：us**
    - **考点**：代词。teach 后接宾格代词作间接宾语，根据语境指"我们"，填 us。
