# 05-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：健康饮食 (Healthy Eating)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Eating healthy food is one of the ___1___ (easy) ways to keep fit. Doctors suggest that teenagers should have ___2___ balanced diet including vegetables, fruit, meat and grains. ___3___, many students prefer fast food ___4___ home-cooked meals because it tastes better and saves time. A recent survey ___5___ (show) that nearly half of middle school students skip breakfast at ___6___ once a week. This is very ___7___ (harm) to their bodies. Without enough energy in the morning, students may find ___8___ difficult to focus in class. My mother always ___9___ (tell) me, "You are what you eat." I think she is right. If we develop good eating ___10___ (habit) now, we will benefit from them for the rest of our lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：easiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词，表示"最容易的方式之一"，easy → easiest。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。diet 为可数名词单数，a balanced diet 表示"均衡的饮食"，用不定冠词 a。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词。前后为转折关系，"应该均衡饮食"与"许多学生更喜欢快餐"之间用 However，句首大写后加逗号。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。prefer A to B 表示"比起 B 更喜欢 A"，填 to。

5. **答案：showed/shows**
   - **考点**：动词时态。a recent survey 可搭配一般过去时（叙述已完成的调查结果）或一般现在时（引用调查结论），填 showed 或 shows 均可。

6. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：副词。at least 表示"至少"，固定搭配，填 least。

7. **答案：harmful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。is 后接形容词作表语，harm → harmful（有害的）。

8. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。find it difficult to do sth.，it 为形式宾语，真正宾语为后面的不定式，填 it。

9. **答案：tells**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。always 提示一般现在时，主语 my mother 为第三人称单数，tell → tells。

10. **答案：habits**
    - **考点**：名词单复数。eating habits 表示"饮食习惯"，习惯通常为复数概念，habit → habits。
