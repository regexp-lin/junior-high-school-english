# 01-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：建筑之美 (The Beauty of Architecture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Architecture is more than just the construction of buildings — it is a form of art that reflects the culture, history and values of a society. China has a long and rich architectural tradition that (1) ______ (date) back thousands of years. Ancient Chinese buildings, such as temples and palaces, were (2) ______ (typical) made of wood and featured curved roofs, red columns and intricate carvings. The Forbidden City in Beijing, (3) ______ took 14 years to complete, is perhaps the finest example of traditional Chinese architecture. It contains nearly a thousand (4) ______ (build) and covers an area of 720,000 square metres. In contrast to these ancient (5) ______ (structure), modern Chinese architecture is characterised by bold designs and cutting-edge technology. Cities like Shanghai and Shenzhen are home to some of the (6) ______ (tall) and most innovative skyscrapers in the world. The Shanghai Tower, for instance, rises 632 metres into the sky, (7) ______ (make) it the second tallest building on Earth. What I find most (8) ______ (fascinate) about architecture is how it combines function and beauty. A well-designed building does not just provide shelter — it also inspires the people (9) ______ use it. I have always dreamed of becoming an architect. I want to design buildings that are not only beautiful but also environmentally (10) ______ (friend), so that they can stand the test of time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：dates**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。`dates back thousands of years`，描述事实，第三人称单数。

2. **答案：typically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `made` 需要副词形式。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `The Forbidden City`。

4. **答案：buildings**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`nearly a thousand buildings`，数量词后接复数。

5. **答案：structures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`these ancient structures`，指代前文提到的多个建筑。

6. **答案：tallest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`some of the tallest skyscrapers`。

7. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随结果，"从而使它成为"。

8. **答案：fascinating**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

9. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰 `the people`，在从句中作主语。

10. **答案：friendly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`environmentally friendly` 为复合形容词，表示"环保的"。
