# 01-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园义卖会 (School Charity Sale)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school held a charity sale last month to raise money for children in need. The event was a huge success, (1) ______ (attract) hundreds of students, teachers and parents. Each class was given a booth where they could sell handmade items, second-hand books and home-cooked food. My class decided to sell cookies and cupcakes. We spent the entire weekend before the sale (2) ______ (bake) in the school kitchen. It was hard work, but we had a great time working together as a team. On the day of the sale, our booth was one of the (3) ______ (popular) ones. The smell of freshly baked cookies drew people in from across the playground. Within the first hour, we had already sold more (4) ______ half of our products. By the end of the day, we had raised over 2,000 yuan — far (5) ______ (much) than we had expected! The money (6) ______ (collect) from the charity sale was donated to a rural school in Guizhou Province. Our headmaster showed us (7) ______ (photo) of the school, where many students did not have enough textbooks (8) ______ school supplies. Knowing that our efforts would help these children receive a (9) ______ (good) education filled us with a sense of pride and purpose. The charity sale taught me that even small (10) ______ (contribute) can make a meaningful difference in the lives of others.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：attracting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随结果，"从而吸引了"。

2. **答案：baking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the most popular ones`，多音节形容词最高级。

4. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`more than half`，比较级结构。

5. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：比较级。`far more than we had expected`，`far` 修饰比较级。

6. **答案：collected**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`The money collected from`，被动关系。

7. **答案：photos**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`showed us photos`，展示多张照片。

8. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`textbooks or school supplies`，否定句中"和"用 `or`。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`receive a better education`，暗含与现有教育条件的比较。

10. **答案：contributions**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`small contributions`，形容词 `small` 后接名词复数。
