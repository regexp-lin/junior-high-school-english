# 01-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：手机与青少年 (Smartphones and Teenagers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Smartphones have become an essential part of modern life, and most teenagers in China now own one. While smartphones offer many benefits, such as convenient (1) ______ (communicate) and easy access to information, they can also cause problems if (2) ______ (use) improperly. One of the biggest concerns is screen addiction. Many students spend hours every day (3) ______ (watch) short videos, playing games or scrolling through social media. This not only wastes valuable study time (4) ______ also harms their eyesight and sleep quality. Research has shown that the blue light (5) ______ (emit) by phone screens can disrupt our natural sleep patterns, making it (6) ______ (hard) to fall asleep at night. Another problem is cyberbullying. Some teenagers use social media to post hurtful (7) ______ (comment) about their classmates, which can cause serious emotional damage. To deal with these issues, many schools have introduced rules that limit smartphone use during school hours. My school, for example, requires all students (8) ______ (hand) in their phones at the beginning of each day. At first, I found it difficult to be without my phone, (9) ______ I soon realised that I was able to concentrate much better in class. I think the key is to use smartphones (10) ______ (wise) — as a tool for learning and communication, rather than a source of distraction.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：communication**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`convenient communication`，形容词后接名词。

2. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`if used improperly` 省略了 `they are`，被动。

3. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend hours doing sth` 为固定搭配。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 结构。

5. **答案：emitted**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the blue light emitted by phone screens`，被动关系。

6. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`making it harder to fall asleep`，暗含与之前的比较。

7. **答案：comments**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`hurtful comments`，多条评论。

8. **答案：to hand**
   - **考点**：不定式。`require sb to do sth` 为固定搭配。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前后转折，"一开始觉得难……但很快意识到……"。

10. **答案：wisely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `use` 需要副词形式。
