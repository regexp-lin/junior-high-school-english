# 01-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：准备过春节 (Preparing for Spring Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Spring Festival is the most important festival in Chinese culture. As the festival approaches, families across the country begin their (1) ______ (prepare) weeks in advance. The house must be cleaned from top (2) ______ bottom, as this symbolises sweeping away bad luck and welcoming good fortune. Doors and windows are decorated (3) ______ red paper cuttings and couplets bearing wishes for health, happiness and prosperity. Shopping for the festival is always a busy affair. Markets and supermarkets are packed with people (4) ______ (buy) food, new clothes and gifts. My mother usually makes a long shopping list and takes me along (5) ______ (help) carry the bags. The food for the New Year's Eve dinner is (6) ______ (particular) important, as each dish has a special meaning. Fish represents abundance, noodles symbolise long life, and dumplings are shaped like ancient gold coins to bring (7) ______ (wealthy). In our family, making dumplings together on New Year's Eve is a tradition that has lasted for (8) ______ (generation). Everyone gathers around the kitchen table, chatting and laughing while wrapping dumplings with various (9) ______ (fill). My grandmother always hides a coin inside one lucky dumpling. Whoever finds it is said to have good fortune for the whole year. The (10) ______ (excite) of Spring Festival never fades, no matter how old you are.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：preparations**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`begin their preparations`，名词复数表示各种准备工作。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from top to bottom` 为固定短语，表示"从上到下"。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be decorated with` 为固定搭配，表示"用……装饰"。

4. **答案：buying**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`people buying food`，现在分词作后置定语。

5. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：不定式。`takes me along to help`，不定式表目的。

6. **答案：particularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `important` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：wealth**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`bring wealth`，动词后接名词作宾语。

8. **答案：generations**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`for generations` 表示"世世代代"。

9. **答案：fillings**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`various fillings`，各种馅料。

10. **答案：excitement**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`The excitement of Spring Festival`，冠词后接名词。
