# 01-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园安全 (School Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

School safety is something that concerns every student, teacher and parent. Ensuring a safe learning environment is one of the most important (1) ______ (responsible) of any school. Our school takes safety very (2) ______ (serious) and has put in place a number of measures to protect students from potential dangers. Fire drills are conducted once a month so that everyone knows exactly what to do (3) ______ case of an emergency. During a drill, students must leave the building in an orderly manner and gather at the (4) ______ (design) assembly point within three minutes. Last week, our school also invited a police officer to give a talk (5) ______ personal safety. She taught us how to recognise dangerous situations and how (6) ______ (protect) ourselves. One important tip she shared was to always tell a trusted adult if we feel (7) ______ (threat) or uncomfortable. She also reminded us never to share personal information with (8) ______ (strange) online. In addition to physical safety, our school pays attention to students' mental health. A professional counsellor (9) ______ (employ) full-time to help students deal with stress, anxiety and other emotional challenges. Students can visit the counsellor's office at any time during school hours without (10) ______ (make) an appointment. I feel lucky to study in a school that cares so deeply about our well-being.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：responsibilities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most important responsibilities`。

2. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`takes safety seriously`，修饰动词需要副词。

3. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in case of` 为固定搭配，表示"万一，以防"。

4. **答案：designated**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the designated assembly point`，表示"指定的"。

5. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`give a talk on` 表示"就……作演讲"。

6. **答案：to protect**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to protect ourselves`，疑问词 + 不定式。

7. **答案：threatened**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`feel threatened`，系动词后接 `-ed` 形式描述人的感受。

8. **答案：strangers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`share information with strangers`。

9. **答案：is employed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。辅导员"被雇用"，一般现在时被动。

10. **答案：making**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后接动名词形式。
