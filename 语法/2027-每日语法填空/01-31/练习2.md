# 01-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：火车旅行 (Travelling by Train)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's high-speed railway network is one of the (1) ______ (great) engineering achievements of modern times. With a total length of over 42,000 kilometres, it is the longest and (2) ______ (busy) high-speed rail system in the world. Travelling by high-speed train has become the preferred choice for millions of Chinese people, as it is fast, comfortable and (3) ______ (relative) affordable. Last summer, my family took a high-speed train from Guangzhou to Beijing. The journey covered about 2,200 kilometres and took only eight hours, (4) ______ was much faster than driving or taking a regular train. As we sped through the countryside at over 300 kilometres per hour, I pressed my face against the window and watched the landscape change (5) ______ (dramatic). Green rice paddies gave way to golden wheat fields, and flat plains (6) ______ (replace) by rolling hills. One of the things I enjoyed most about the trip was the (7) ______ (free) to walk around. Unlike aeroplanes, trains offer plenty of space to stretch your legs, and there is no need (8) ______ (fasten) your seatbelt. I spent part of the journey reading a novel and part of it chatting with a (9) ______ (friend) elderly couple sitting opposite me. They told me stories about what train travel was like thirty years ago, when journeys (10) ______ (take) two or three times longer and the carriages were often uncomfortably crowded. How things have changed!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：greatest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the greatest achievements`。

2. **答案：busiest**
   - **考点**：最高级。与 `the longest` 并列，用最高级。

3. **答案：relatively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `affordable` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个事实。

5. **答案：dramatically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `change` 需要副词形式。

6. **答案：were replaced**
   - **考点**：被动语态。平原"被"丘陵"取代"，过去时被动。

7. **答案：freedom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the freedom to walk around`，冠词后接名词。

8. **答案：to fasten**
   - **考点**：不定式。`there is no need to do sth` 为固定句型。

9. **答案：friendly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `couple` 需要形容词形式。

10. **答案：took**
    - **考点**：一般过去时。`thirty years ago` 为过去时标志。
