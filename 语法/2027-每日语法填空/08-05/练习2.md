# 08-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国书法 (Chinese Calligraphy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

For thousands of years, Chinese calligraphy ___1___ (be) treasured as an important part of traditional culture. Unlike typing on a keyboard, brush writing asks for patience and steady hands, so each line carries both skill and emotion. In our city library, there is a quiet room ___2___ beginners practice every weekend, and soft music often plays in the background. Long tables are covered with felt pads, brushes, and small dishes filled with dark ink. When I entered for ___3___ first time, the smell of ink seemed fresh and calming. A volunteer teacher showed me ___4___ (proper) how to hold the brush vertically and control the ink. My early strokes were heavy and uneven, ___5___ she still praised my courage and told interesting stories about ancient masters. She asked me ___6___ (copy) simple characters daily and compare my lines with the models in the book. Month ___7___ month, I noticed my writing became smoother, and I began ___8___ (understand) why scholars in ancient times loved writing poems on fans. Last month, our school ___9___ (hold) an art festival, and one of ___10___ (I) was displayed near the school gate. Many classmates stopped to read the neat characters. This quiet hobby also helps me stay calm when exams are near, and I feel proud that the old art still connects young people with our long history.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has been**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。*For thousands of years* 表示从过去延续到现在的一段时间，常与现在完成时连用，强调“一直被珍视”至今；主语 *Chinese calligraphy* 为不可数概念，谓语用 **has been + 过去分词**。*is treasured* 表一般现在亦可接受，但中考阅读里“for + 长时间”更典型配 **现在完成时**。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词（定语从句）。先行词 *a quiet room* 表地点，从句 *beginners practice* 结构完整（主谓齐全），缺地点状语，用 **where**（= in which）引导定语从句。*which* 在从句中须作主语或宾语，此处不适用。

3. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。固定表达 **for the first time** 表示“第一次”，序数含义已隐含在 *first* 中，前面须用定冠词 **the**。

4. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 *showed*（展示、示范的方式）须用副词；*proper* 的副词形式为 **properly**，表示“正确地、规范地”。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说早期笔画“又重又不匀”，后句说老师仍表扬勇气，语义转折，用 **but**。*However* 后常有逗号且多独立成句，此处一空填 **but** 更贴切。

6. **答案：to copy**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。*ask sb. to do sth.* 为固定结构，意为“要求某人做某事”，故填 **to copy**。不可填动名词 *copying*（*ask* 后不接 *doing* 表此类意义）。

7. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。**month by month** 表示“一月又一月地、逐月”，与 **day by day**、**little by little** 同类，介词用 **by**。

8. **答案：to understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。*begin* 后接动词时，常用 **begin to do sth.**（亦可见 *begin doing*，但 *begin to understand* 在表示“逐渐开始明白”时极为常见），故填 **to understand**。

9. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。时间状语 *Last month* 表示过去，主语 *our school* 与 *hold* 为主动关系（学校举办艺术节），谓语用一般过去时 **held**。*hold* 的过去式为不规则变化 **held**。

10. **答案：mine**
   - **考点**：代词。*one of* 后须接名词或代词，表示“……中的一个”。此处指“我的作品中的一幅”，空格后无名词，须用名词性物主代词 **mine**（= my work / my piece），不能用 *me* 或 *my*（*my* 后须接名词）。
