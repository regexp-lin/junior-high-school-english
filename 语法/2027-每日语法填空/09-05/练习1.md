# 09-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中华慈善日 (China Charity Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

September 5th is China Charity Day, a day set aside ___1___ (encourage) people to help those in need. Charity, in its ___2___ (simple) form, means giving time, money or resources to others without expecting anything in return.

In China, the tradition of helping others has a history of thousands of years. Ancient philosophers like Confucius taught that ___3___ (care) for the poor and the sick was the duty of every member of society. Today, this spirit lives on through countless charity ___4___ (organise) and individual volunteers.

Modern technology has made it much ___5___ (easy) to participate in charity work. Through smartphone apps, people can donate money to disaster relief, education projects or medical treatments with just a few ___6___ (click). During the COVID-19 pandemic, millions of Chinese citizens ___7___ (donate) masks, food and money to help those ___8___ were affected.

Our school has also been active in charity. Every year, students collect used ___9___ (cloth) and books to send to children in remote villages. Last year, we raised enough money ___10___ (build) a small library for a rural primary school. Seeing the children's happy faces in the photos they sent us was the best reward.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to encourage**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**set aside to do sth.**（留出来以做某事），不定式 **to encourage** 表示目的。

2. **答案：simplest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**in its simplest form**（以其最简单的形式），`simple` 的最高级为 **simplest**。

3. **答案：caring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。`that` 引导的宾语从句中需要主语，动名词 **caring** 作主语，表示"关心穷人和病人"这件事。

4. **答案：organisations**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）+ 名词复数。`countless`（无数的）修饰可数名词复数，**organise** 的名词形式为 **organisation**，复数为 **organisations**。

5. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**much + 比较级** 表示"……得多"，`easy` 的比较级为 **easier**。

6. **答案：clicks**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few`（几个）后接可数名词复数，`click` 的复数形式为 **clicks**。

7. **答案：donated**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`During the COVID-19 pandemic`（在新冠疫情期间）指过去的时间段，用一般过去时，`donate` 的过去式为 **donated**。

8. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `those`（那些人）指人，在从句中作主语，故用关系代词 **who**。

9. **答案：clothes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`used clothes`（旧衣服），**cloth** 作"衣服"讲时用复数形式 **clothes**。

10. **答案：to build**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**enough + 名词 + to do sth.**（足够的……来做某事），不定式 **to build** 表示结果/目的。
