# 09-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：北极熊的困境 (Polar Bears in Trouble)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The polar bear is one of the most ___1___ (power) predators on Earth. It can weigh up to 700 kilograms and run at speeds of up to 40 kilometres ___2___ hour. However, this magnificent animal is now facing a serious threat — the loss of its icy home.

Polar bears depend ___3___ sea ice to hunt seals, their main source of food. In winter, the bears walk across frozen seas and wait patiently near breathing holes ___4___ seals come up for air. But as global temperatures rise, the Arctic ice ___5___ (melt) earlier each spring and forming later each autumn. This means polar bears have less time ___6___ (hunt) and must go longer periods without food.

Scientists have observed that many polar bears are becoming ___7___ (thin) than they were twenty years ago. Some females do not have enough energy ___8___ (feed) their cubs, leading to lower survival rates among the young. If the ice continues to shrink at the current rate, experts warn that the polar bear population could ___9___ (reduce) by two thirds by the end of this century.

What can we do to help? The most important step is ___10___ (fight) climate change by reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Protecting the polar bear means protecting the entire Arctic ecosystem.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：powerful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。**the most + 多音节形容词** 构成最高级，**power** 的形容词形式为 **powerful**。

2. **答案：per / an**
   - **考点**：介词/冠词。**per hour** 或 **an hour**（每小时）均为固定搭配，表示速度单位。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。**depend on...**（依赖……）为固定搭配，故填 **on**。

4. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `breathing holes`（呼吸孔）表示地点，从句中不缺主语或宾语，缺少地点状语，故用 **where**。

5. **答案：is melting**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述当前正在发生的变化趋势用现在进行时，主语 `the Arctic ice` 为第三人称单数，故填 **is melting**。

6. **答案：to hunt**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**have less time to do sth.**（有更少的时间做某事），不定式 **to hunt** 作后置定语修饰 `time`。

7. **答案：thinner**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**than** 提示比较关系，`thin` 的比较级为 **thinner**（双写末尾辅音字母加 -er）。

8. **答案：to feed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**have enough energy to do sth.**（有足够的精力做某事），不定式 **to feed** 表示目的。

9. **答案：be reduced**
   - **考点**：动词语态。情态动词 `could` 后接动词原形；主语 `the polar bear population` 与 `reduce` 之间为被动关系（种群数量"被减少"），故填 **be reduced**。

10. **答案：to fight**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**The most important step is to do sth.**（最重要的一步是做某事），不定式 **to fight** 作表语说明具体内容。
