# 06-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：图书推荐 (Book Recommendation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Reading is one of the ___1___ (good) habits a student can develop. This semester, our English teacher asked each of us ___2___ (recommend) a book to the class. I chose "Charlotte's Web" because it is a story about friendship and ___3___ (brave). The book tells the story of a pig named Wilbur ___4___ is saved by a clever spider called Charlotte. Although the language is simple, the messages behind it are deep and ___5___ (touch). When I first read it in primary school, I didn't ___6___ (full) understand its meaning. But rereading it now, I find ___7___ much more moving. The ending made me cry because Charlotte ___8___ (die) after helping Wilbur. Her ___9___ (self) love for her friend is something we can all learn from. After my presentation, several classmates told me they wanted to read it ___10___. I was happy that my recommendation inspired others.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，good → best。

2. **答案：to recommend**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 表示"要求某人做某事"，填 to recommend。

3. **答案：bravery**
   - **考点**：词性转换。and 连接两个并列名词，friendship 与 bravery 并列，brave → bravery。

4. **答案：who/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a pig named Wilbur（拟人化的动物），在从句中作主语，用 who 或 that。

5. **答案：touching**
   - **考点**：词性转换。and 连接两个并列形容词作表语，deep and touching，修饰 messages（物），用 touching。

6. **答案：fully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 understand，需用副词形式，full → fully。

7. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。find it much more moving，it 指代前文的书，填 it。

8. **答案：died**
   - **考点**：动词时态。because 引导原因状语从句，叙述故事情节用一般过去时，die → died。

9. **答案：selfless**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 love，表示"无私的爱"，self → selfless。

10. **答案：too**
    - **考点**：副词。too 表示"也"，放在句末，表示"他们也想读"，填 too。
