# 06-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：夜市美食 (Night Market Food)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Night markets are an important part of Chinese food culture. They come alive after dark, ___1___ (fill) the streets with delicious smells and bright lights. Last Friday, my friends and I visited a famous night market in our city. The moment we arrived, we ___2___ (attract) by the amazing variety of snacks. There ___3___ (be) grilled lamb skewers, stinky tofu, bubble tea and much more. Li Ming couldn't resist ___4___ (try) the spicy crayfish, which is one of the ___5___ (popular) dishes at night markets. I chose a bowl of handmade rice noodles topped ___6___ fresh vegetables and a fried egg. The taste was even ___7___ (good) than I expected. We sat ___8___ small plastic stools, chatting and laughing while enjoying our food. The night market gives people a chance ___9___ (relax) after a long day of work. It truly shows ___10___ special Chinese street food culture is.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：filling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，come alive..., filling the streets...，填 filling。

2. **答案：were attracted**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。we 与 attract 之间为被动关系，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were attracted。

3. **答案：were**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。there be 句型中，be 的形式与最近的名词一致，grilled lamb skewers 为复数，叙述过去事件用 were。

4. **答案：trying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。can't resist doing sth. 表示"忍不住做某事"，resist 后接动名词，填 trying。

5. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，popular 为多音节词，用 most popular。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。topped with 表示"上面放着……"，填 with。

7. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。even + 比较级 + than，good → better。

8. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。sit on stools 表示"坐在凳子上"，填 on。

9. **答案：to relax**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。a chance to do sth. 表示"做某事的机会"，不定式作后置定语，填 to relax。

10. **答案：how**
    - **考点**：连词。how special... is 为感叹句式作宾语从句，表示"中国街头美食文化有多特别"，填 how。
