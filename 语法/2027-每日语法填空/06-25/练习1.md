# 06-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学习乐器 (Learning a Musical Instrument)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Playing a musical instrument is one of the most ___1___ (reward) hobbies a teenager can have. I started learning the piano when I was seven years old. My mother took me to a music school ___2___ I had my first lesson with Mrs. Li. She was a strict ___3___ patient teacher. In the beginning, I found it ___4___ (bore) to practise scales over and over again. Sometimes I wanted to give ___5___, but my mother always encouraged me to keep trying. As the years went ___6___, I ___7___ (gradual) fell in love with the piano. Now I can play some of Chopin's pieces, which once seemed ___8___ (possible) for me. Playing the piano has taught me ___9___ discipline and perseverance mean. Last month, I ___10___ (perform) at the school concert for the first time and received warm applause from the audience.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rewarding**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the most + 形容词构成最高级，修饰名词 hobbies，reward → rewarding（有回报的）。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 a music school（地点），在从句中作地点状语，用 where。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。strict 与 patient 之间为转折关系，"严格但有耐心"，用 but。

4. **答案：boring**
   - **考点**：词性转换。find it boring 表示"觉得无聊"，it 指代 to practise scales（事/物），用 boring。

5. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。give up 表示"放弃"，固定搭配，填 up。

6. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：副词。as the years went by 表示"随着岁月流逝"，go by 表示"（时间）流逝"，填 by。

7. **答案：gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 fell in love，需用副词形式，gradual → gradually。

8. **答案：impossible**
   - **考点**：词性转换。seemed impossible 表示"曾经看起来不可能"，possible → impossible，加否定前缀。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连词。what discipline and perseverance mean 为宾语从句，what 在从句中作 mean 的宾语，填 what。

10. **答案：performed**
    - **考点**：动词时态。last month 提示用一般过去时，perform → performed。
