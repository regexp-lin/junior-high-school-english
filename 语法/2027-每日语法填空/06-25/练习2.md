# 06-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：快递行业 (The Express Delivery Industry)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Online shopping has grown rapidly in China, and so has the express delivery industry. Every day, over 300 million parcels ___1___ (deliver) across the country. Behind each delivery, there is a hardworking courier who races against time. My neighbour, Uncle Wang, has worked ___2___ a courier for five years. He gets up at four in the morning and doesn't finish ___3___ late at night. During big shopping festivals like "Double Eleven", the number of packages ___4___ (increase) dramatically, and couriers have to work even ___5___ (hard). Despite the heavy workload, Uncle Wang always greets customers ___6___ a smile. He once told me that the ___7___ (happy) moment of his day is when a customer says "thank you". Some companies are now testing delivery drones and robots to help reduce the ___8___ (press) on couriers. ___9___ these technologies become widely used, it will still take time. We should all be more ___10___ (respect) towards the people who bring packages to our doors.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：are delivered**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。parcels 与 deliver 之间为被动关系，every day 提示一般现在时，填 are delivered。

2. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。work as 表示"担任、作为"，填 as。

3. **答案：until**
   - **考点**：连词。doesn't finish until late at night 表示"直到深夜才结束"，not...until 句型，填 until。

4. **答案：increases**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。the number of 后接复数名词，但谓语与 number 一致用单数，陈述事实用一般现在时，填 increases。

5. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。even + 比较级表示"甚至更加努力"，hard → harder。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with a smile 表示"带着微笑"，填 with。

7. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"最开心的时刻"，happy → happiest。

8. **答案：pressure**
   - **考点**：词性转换。reduce the pressure 为名词短语，表示"减轻压力"，press → pressure。

9. **答案：Before**
   - **考点**：连词。Before these technologies become widely used 表示"在这些技术被广泛使用之前"，引导时间状语从句，填 Before。

10. **答案：respectful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。be more respectful towards 表示"对……更加尊重"，respect → respectful。
