# 07-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：公共交通出行 (Travelling by Public Transport)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Travelling by public transport is ___1___ good choice for many middle school students in big cities. In recent years, it ___2___ (become) ___3___ (popular) than riding in a private family car during rush hour ___4___ roads are crowded with traffic. Buses, subways, and light rail usually run on time, ___5___ commuters often arrive at school or work without serious delay. Online maps and travel apps also help passengers plan routes, compare ticket prices, and avoid getting lost in unfamiliar streets. For students who live far from school, a monthly pass may cut down daily costs and reduce the pressure on parents who drive long distances every morning.

To protect the environment, local governments encourage people ___6___ (choose) green ways ___7___ go to school or the office. New energy buses are now ___8___ (wide) used in downtown areas, and they make ___9___ (little) noise and give off fewer pollutants than older models. Some cities have built bicycle-sharing stations near subway exits so that people can finish the last part of a journey in a flexible way. While ___10___ (wait) at the platform or bus stop, everyone is expected to queue politely, give seats to those in need, and keep the surroundings tidy for the next group of travellers.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。*a good choice* 表示“一种不错的选择”，泛指可数名词单数，*good* 以辅音音素开头，故用 **a**。

2. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。*in recent years* 提示用现在完成时，强调近几年来的变化；主语 *it* 为第三人称单数，故 **has become**。

3. **答案：more popular**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有比较连词 *than*，且 *popular* 为多音节形容词，比较级前加 **more**，故 **more popular**。

4. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：连词。引导时间状语从句，说明“在道路拥堵的时段”，表示“当……时候”，填 **when**。

5. **答案：so / and**
   - **考点**：连词。前句说公交等准点，后句说人们常能准时到达，为因果关系用 **so**；若理解为并列补充，也可用 **and**。中考中 **so** 更贴合“因此、所以”的逻辑。

6. **答案：to choose**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定搭配 *encourage sb. to do sth.*（鼓励某人做某事），动词不定式作宾语补足语，故 **to choose**。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：小品词/不定式符号。*ways to do sth.* 表示“做某事的方式”，后面用不定式 *to go*，故填 **to**。

8. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 *used*（被使用），需用副词；*wide* 的副词形式为 **widely**，表示“广泛地”。

9. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。*noise* 为不可数名词，与后文 *fewer pollutants* 并列比较，表示“更少的噪音”，*little* 的比较级为 **less**。

10. **答案：waiting**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。连词 *while* 引导省略结构，完整形式为 *while they are waiting*；主从句主语一致且含 *be* 动词时，可省略为 *while waiting*，故填 **waiting**。
