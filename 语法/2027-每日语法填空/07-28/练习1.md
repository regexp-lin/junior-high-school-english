# 07-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国剪纸艺术 (Chinese Paper-cutting Art)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese paper-cutting is ___1___ traditional art form that has a very long history in China. As one of the best-known folk arts, it ___2___ (know) around the world ___3___ a symbol of good luck, happiness, and strong family ties. With only a pair of small scissors and several pieces of bright red paper, skilled craftspeople can cut out ___4___ (wonder) patterns of flowers, birds, and lively figures from ancient folk stories. The colour red stands for good wishes, so paper-cuttings are especially popular during important festivals. In the countryside, elders sometimes teach children basic folding skills on winter evenings, passing down stories together with the scissors.

During the Spring Festival, many families paste paper-cuttings on windows and doors ___5___ they hope to welcome the new year in a warm and joyful way. ___6___ recent years, this traditional art ___7___ (attract) more and more teenagers who want to keep the craft alive. They often upload practice videos online and plan ___8___ (share) skills with friends. Local museums and schools also organize short classes so that beginners can take home ___9___ (they) ___10___ (create) and feel proud of Chinese culture. Behind the simple tools lie patience and imagination: a steady hand can turn plain paper into moving stories that speak without a single spoken word.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。此处表示“一种传统艺术形式”，为泛指可数名词单数 *art form*，且 *traditional* 以辅音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **a**。

2. **答案：is known**
   - **考点**：动词的时态与语态。主语 *it* 指代 paper-cutting，与 *know* 之间为被动关系（“被人们所知”）；陈述客观事实用一般现在时，故用一般现在时的被动语态 **is known**。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 *be known as* 表示“作为……而闻名”，后接身份或象征意义，故填 **as**。

4. **答案：wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。空白处修饰名词 *patterns*，需用形容词；括号内 *wonder* 为名词/动词，其形容词形式为 **wonderful**。

5. **答案：when / because / as**
   - **考点**：连词。句意为“许多家庭在门窗上贴剪纸，此时/因为他们希望以温暖欢乐的方式迎接新年”。*when* 引导时间状语从句；若强调原因，也可用 *because* 或 *as*。中考语境下 **when** 最常见。

6. **答案：In**
   - **考点**：介词。固定短语 *in recent years*（近年来），作时间状语，句首首字母大写，故填 **In**。

7. **答案：has attracted**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。*in recent years* 常与现在完成时连用，表示从过去持续到现在的影响；主语 *this traditional art* 为第三人称单数，故 **has attracted**。

8. **答案：to share**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定结构 *plan to do sth.*（计划做某事），动词不定式作宾语，故填 **to share**。

9. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 *creations*，需用形容词性物主代词；主语为 *beginners*，对应 **their**。

10. **答案：creations**
    - **考点**：名词单复数。形容词性物主代词 *their* 后需接名词；括号 *create* 为动词，名词形式为 *creation*（作品），此处泛指多件作品，用复数 **creations**。
