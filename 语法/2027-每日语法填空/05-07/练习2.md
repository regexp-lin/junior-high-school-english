# 05-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：科技改变教育 (Technology Changes Education)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Technology has changed the way we learn. In our classroom, there is 1. ______ smart screen instead of only a blackboard. Teachers can play short videos 2. ______ show how experiments work. If someone is absent, he or she can watch 3. ______ recorded lesson at home. Online platforms also allow us 4. ______ (submit) homework and get feedback faster. However, screens cannot replace everything. My English teacher still asks us 5. ______ (read) paper books because turning pages helps memory. She says we should use digital tools 6. ______ (wise) instead of letting them control our time. Last week, we had 7. ______ debate on whether phones should be allowed in school. I argued 8. ______ they are useful for learning if there are clear rules. In the end, we agreed 9. ______ balance is the key. Education in the future may look different, 10. ______ its goal will stay the same: to help us grow.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **screen** 单数，表泛指“一块智能屏幕”，smart 以辅音音素开头，故填 **a**。

2. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 **short videos**（物），定语从句中作主语，故填 **that** 或 **which**。

3. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。特指“那节被录下来的课”，故填 **the**。

4. **答案：to submit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**allow sb. to do sth.** 表示“允许某人做某事”，故填 **to submit**。

5. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**ask sb. to do sth.** 表示“要求某人做某事”，故填 **to read**。

6. **答案：wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **use** 需用副词，形容词 wise 的副词形式为 **wisely**。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**have a debate** 表示“进行一场辩论”，故填 **a**。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**argued** 后接宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 **that** 引导。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**agreed** 后接宾语从句，说明认同的内容，用 **that** 引导。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。前后句“未来教育可能看起来不同”与“目标仍将相同”为转折关系，故填 **but**。
