# 05-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：小镇旅行 (Small Town Travel)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the holiday, my family drove 1. ______ a small town known for its old bridges and quiet canals. We stayed at 2. ______ bed-and-breakfast run by a friendly couple. On the first morning, we walked along stone streets 3. ______ (line) with small shops. A local guide told us 4. ______ the town was built 5. ______ the Ming Dynasty. At noon, we tried special noodles 6. ______ (make) from rice flour. They tasted 7. ______ (wonder) and cost less than in big cities. In the afternoon, we took 8. ______ boat ride and watched birds near the reeds. My sister took many photos because she did not want 9. ______ (forget) anything. Before we left, we bought 10. ______ handmade bag as a gift for Grandma. Slow travel like this helps us breathe and think.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**drive to + 地点** 表示“开车前往某地”，故填 **to**。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词短语 **bed-and-breakfast** 单数，表泛指“一家民宿”，故填 **a**。

3. **答案：lined**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**streets lined with shops** 中 **lined** 为过去分词作后置定语，表“两旁排列着店铺的街道”，故填 **lined**。

4. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**told us** 后接宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 **that** 引导。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。表示“在某个朝代”用 **in the Ming Dynasty**，朝代前用介词 **in**，故填 **in**。

6. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**noodles made from rice flour** 中 **made** 为过去分词作后置定语，表“由米粉制成的面条”，故填 **made**。

7. **答案：wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。**taste** 为系动词，后接形容词作表语，名词 wonder 的形容词形式为 **wonderful**。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**take a boat ride** 表示“乘船游览”，故填 **a**。

9. **答案：to forget**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**want to do sth.** 表示“想要做某事”，否定为 **did not want to forget**，故填 **to forget**。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **bag** 单数，handmade 以辅音音素开头，故填 **a**。
