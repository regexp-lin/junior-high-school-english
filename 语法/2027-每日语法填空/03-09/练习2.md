# 03-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：英语演讲比赛 (English Speech Contest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, I took part in the school English speech contest. It was the (1) ______ (one) time I had spoken English in front of a large audience, and I was very nervous.

My topic was "The Importance of Learning a Foreign Language". I spent two weeks (2) ______ (write) and practising my speech. Every evening after dinner, I stood in front of the mirror and practised again and again. My mother would sit nearby and listen, offering (3) ______ (help) suggestions from time to time.

On the day of the contest, there were twenty (4) ______ (compete) from different classes. When it was my turn, my hands were shaking and my voice was a little (5) ______ (steady) at first. But as I looked at my teacher's encouraging smile, I (6) ______ (gradual) calmed down and began to speak more naturally.

I talked about (7) ______ learning English had opened up a new world for me. I mentioned that I could now read English novels, watch foreign films (8) ______ subtitles, and even make friends with students from other countries.

To my great joy, I won second prize! The judges said my pronunciation was clear and my speech was well (9) ______ (organize). Although I didn't get the first prize, I was still proud of (10) ______ (I). The experience taught me that preparation and courage are the keys to success.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。`the first time` 第一次。

2. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time (in) doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

3. **答案：helpful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `suggestions`，用形容词 `helpful`。

4. **答案：competitors**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`twenty competitors` 二十名选手，`compete` → `competitor`。

5. **答案：unsteady**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 前缀。由语境知声音不稳定，`steady` → `unsteady`。

6. **答案：gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `calmed down`，用副词 `gradually`。

7. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。`how learning English had opened up ...` 学英语如何打开了……。

8. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`watch foreign films without subtitles` 不需要字幕看外国电影。

9. **答案：organized**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`was well organized` 组织得很好，被动含义。

10. **答案：myself**
    - **考点**：反身代词。`was proud of myself` 为自己感到骄傲，主语 `I` 对应 `myself`。
