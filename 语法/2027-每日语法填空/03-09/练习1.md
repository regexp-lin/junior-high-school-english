# 03-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：熊猫保护 (Panda Conservation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The giant panda is one of the most (1) ______ (love) animals in the world. With their black-and-white fur and round faces, they have captured the hearts of people everywhere. However, pandas were once in great danger of (2) ______ (die) out.

In the 1980s, there (3) ______ (be) only about 1,100 giant pandas left in the wild. The main reasons for their decline were habitat loss and a low (4) ______ (bear) rate. To protect these gentle creatures, the Chinese government set up more than 60 nature reserves (5) ______ pandas could live safely.

Scientists have also made great efforts in panda (6) ______ (breed). The Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding is one of the (7) ______ (famous) centres in the world. Every year, visitors from different countries come here to see baby pandas and learn about conservation.

Thanks to these efforts, the number of wild pandas (8) ______ (rise) to over 1,800. In 2021, China announced that giant pandas were no (9) ______ classified as endangered. This is wonderful news, (10) ______ it does not mean we can stop protecting them. We must continue to take care of these national treasures.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：loved**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the most loved animals` 最受喜爱的动物。

2. **答案：dying**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词，`die out` 灭绝。

3. **答案：were**
   - **考点**：一般过去时 + there be 句型。`In the 1980s` 过去时间，`pandas` 复数用 `were`。

4. **答案：birth**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a low birth rate` 低出生率，`bear` → `birth` 名词。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句修饰 `nature reserves`，表地点。

6. **答案：breeding**
   - **考点**：动名词作定语。`panda breeding` 熊猫繁殖。

7. **答案：most famous**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the most famous centres` 最著名的中心之一。

8. **答案：has risen**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。表示从过去到现在数量已经上升，主语 `the number of` 用单数。

9. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`no longer` 不再。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：转折连词。这是好消息，但不意味着可以停止保护，表转折。
