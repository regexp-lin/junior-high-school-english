# 10-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：感恩教师 (Being Grateful to Teachers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mr. Wang has been my English teacher __1__ two years. He always comes to class with a warm smile __2__ his face. Unlike other teachers who simply follow textbooks, he tries __3__ (make) every lesson fun and interesting. He once dressed up as Shakespeare __4__ (teach) us about famous plays. The whole class couldn't stop __5__ (laugh). When I failed an exam last term, I felt __6__ (terrible) upset. Mr. Wang noticed my sadness and had a long talk __7__ me after school. He said failure was nothing but a stepping stone __8__ success. His words encouraged me __9__ (great). Now I have become one of the __10__ (top) students in English. I will never forget what he has done for me.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for + 时间段，与现在完成时连用，表示"持续了两年"。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。a smile on one's face 脸上带着微笑，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。try to do sth. 努力做某事，用不定式 to make。

4. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了教我们"，用 to teach。

5. **答案：laughing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。couldn't stop doing sth. 忍不住做某事，用动名词 laughing。

6. **答案：terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 upset，用副词 terribly 表示程度"极其"。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。have a talk with sb. 与某人谈话，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。a stepping stone to success 通往成功的垫脚石，用介词 to 表方向。

9. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 encouraged，用副词 greatly。

10. **答案：top**
    - **考点**：形容词用法。one of the top students 最优秀的学生之一，top 本身为形容词，无需变形。
