# 10-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国茶文化 (Chinese Tea Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tea is one of the __1__ (popular) drinks in the world, and China is its birthplace. According to an old legend, Emperor Shen Nong __2__ (discover) tea by accident about 5,000 years ago. Since then, tea __3__ (become) an important part of Chinese daily life. There are many __4__ (kind) of tea, such as green tea, black tea and oolong tea. Each type has __5__ own special taste and colour. In some areas, people enjoy __6__ (drink) tea while chatting with friends. The traditional tea ceremony is __7__ art that requires patience and skill. When you visit a Chinese family, the host will usually offer you a cup __8__ hot tea. It is considered impolite __9__ (refuse) it directly. Tea not only brings people closer together __10__ also benefits our health in many ways.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，popular 为多音节词，最高级用 most popular。

2. **答案：discovered**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 about 5,000 years ago 可知为一般过去时，用 discovered。

3. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 since then 可知用现在完成时，主语 tea 为第三人称单数，用 has become。

4. **答案：kinds**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。many 后接可数名词复数，用 kinds。

5. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 taste，表示"它自己的"，用形容词性物主代词 its。

6. **答案：drinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。enjoy doing sth. 喜欢做某事，用动名词 drinking。

7. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。art 以元音音素 /ɑː/ 开头，泛指"一门艺术"，用 an。

8. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。a cup of tea 一杯茶，为固定搭配。

9. **答案：to refuse**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It is + 形容词 + to do sth. 为固定句型，it 为形式主语，真正主语是 to refuse。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。not only... but also... 不仅……而且……，为固定搭配。
