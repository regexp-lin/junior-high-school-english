# 09-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国结 (Chinese Knots)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Chinese knot is __1__ traditional decoration with a long history. People often tie red ropes __2__ beautiful patterns to express good wishes. In our craft lesson, Ms. Zhang __3__ (show) us how __4__ (make) a simple knot step by step. At first my fingers were clumsy, __5__ I practiced again and again after class. Slowly, the lines became neater, and the knot looked __6__ (love). My grandmother hung it __7__ the door and said it brought warmth __8__ the family. Nowadays, Chinese knots appear __9__ gifts, clothes, and even phone straps. I hope this art __10__ (pass) down to more young hands in creative ways.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a traditional decoration 一种传统装饰。

2. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。tie ropes into patterns 将绳编成图案。

3. **答案：showed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。手工课叙述过去，show 用过去式 showed。

4. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：疑问词 + 不定式。how to make 作 showed 的直接宾语。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。起初笨拙与课后反复练习为转折。

6. **答案：lovely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。look 为系动词，后接形容词，love 的形容词形式为 lovely。

7. **答案：on / above**
   - **考点**：介词。挂在门上 on the door；above 亦可表示门上方。

8. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。bring warmth to the family 给家庭带来温暖。

9. **答案：on / in**
   - **考点**：介词。appear on gifts 出现在礼品上；in gifts 作为礼品的一部分，on 更自然。

10. **答案：will be passed / can be passed**
   - **考点**：被动语态与时态。艺术被传承，用被动；in creative ways 与 hope 连用表将来或可能性，will be passed 或 can be passed。
