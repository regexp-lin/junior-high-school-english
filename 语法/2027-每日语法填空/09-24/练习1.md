# 09-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校环保日 (School Green Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our School Green Day __1__ (fall) on the first Friday of September each year. The student union asked each class __2__ design a poster about saving resources. In the morning assembly, the headmaster said we should treat the earth __3__ our common home. My classmates and I collected used bottles on the playground __4__ put them into recycling bins. We also made signs __5__ remind others not to litter. During the science talk, a guest __6__ (explain) how waste sorting helps factories reuse materials. __7__ lunch, we chose meals with less leftover food. I realized green habits are not __8__ one-day show—they need daily practice. If everyone keeps trying, our campus __9__ (look) cleaner and more beautiful. I am proud __10__ (be) part of this meaningful day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：falls**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。each year 表示规律性安排，用一般现在时；主语 Our School Green Day 为单数，fall 用第三人称单数 falls。fall on 表示“适逢某日”。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事。

3. **答案：as / like**
   - **考点**：介词。treat the earth as our common home 把地球当作共同家园；like 表“像”也可在部分语境使用，as 更贴切。

4. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。collected 与 put 为顺承并列。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。制作标语是为了提醒不乱扔垃圾。

6. **答案：explained**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述当天活动，explain 用过去式 explained。

7. **答案：At / For**
   - **考点**：介词。At lunch 在午餐时；For lunch 就午餐而言，At lunch 更常见。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。not a one-day show 不是一场“一日秀”。

9. **答案：will look**
   - **考点**：动词时态。if 从句一般现在时，主句表将来用 will look。

10. **答案：to be**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be proud to be part of... 为……的一员而自豪。
