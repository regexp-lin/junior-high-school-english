# 10-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家书传情 (Family Letters)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In the age of smartphones, few people write letters any more. However, my grandmother still keeps a box full __1__ old letters from my grandfather. They were written __2__ the 1970s, when Grandpa worked far away from home. Every letter started __3__ "Dear Ling" and ended with "Miss you __4__ (deep)." Grandma said those letters __5__ (mean) everything to her during that hard time. Last Sunday, she showed them to me. The paper had turned yellow, __6__ the words were still clear. I was __7__ (move) by the simple but warm sentences. Grandpa once wrote, "I hope our __8__ (child) will live a better life than we do." Reading these words made me __9__ (think) about how lucky I am. I decided to write __10__ letter to my parents to express my love. Sometimes the old ways are the best.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。full of 充满……，为固定搭配。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in the 1970s 在20世纪70年代，年代前用介词 in。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。start with 以……开始，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 miss，用副词 deeply。

5. **答案：meant**
   - **考点**：动词时态。said 为一般过去时，宾语从句中描述过去的情况，用过去时 meant。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说纸张变黄，后句说字迹仍清晰，语义转折，用 but。

7. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be moved by 被……感动，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

8. **答案：children**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。child 的复数为不规则变化 children。

9. **答案：think**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。make sb. do sth. 使某人做某事，make 后接省略 to 的不定式，用动词原形 think。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一封信"，letter 以辅音音素开头，用 a。
