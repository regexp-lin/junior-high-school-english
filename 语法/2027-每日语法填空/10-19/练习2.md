# 10-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：社区花园 (Community Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A community garden __1__ (open) near our neighbourhood last spring. Families can rent a small plot of land and grow __2__ (they) own vegetables. My parents and I chose a plot beside __3__ old oak tree. We planted tomatoes, cucumbers and peppers. At first, I knew __4__ (nothing / little) about gardening, so my father taught me how to prepare the soil and water the plants __5__ (proper). Every weekend, we spent about two hours __6__ (work) in the garden. It was hard but __7__ (enjoy). The best part was meeting other families. An elderly couple next to __8__ (we) often shared gardening tips with us. Mrs. Li, __9__ is over seventy, grows the most beautiful roses in the whole garden. The community garden has brought neighbours much __10__ (close) together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：opened**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 last spring 可知为一般过去时，用 opened。

2. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 vegetables，用 their。

3. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。old 以元音音素 /əʊ/ 开头，泛指"一棵老橡树"，用 an。

4. **答案：nothing / little**
   - **考点**：不定代词/副词。know nothing about 对……一无所知；或 know little about 对……知之甚少，两者均可。

5. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 water，用副词 properly。

6. **答案：working**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 花时间做某事，用动名词 working。

7. **答案：enjoyable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。与 hard 并列作表语，用形容词 enjoyable 表示"令人愉快的"。

8. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。介词 next to 后接宾格，用 us。

9. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 Mrs. Li 指人，非限制性定语从句缺主语，用 who（不可用 that）。

10. **答案：closer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，close 的比较级为 closer，表示"更加亲近"。
