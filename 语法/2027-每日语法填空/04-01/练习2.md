# 04-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：手账日记 (Bullet Journal)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A bullet journal is (1) ______ simple notebook that helps people plan their lives in a creative way. Instead of buying expensive planners, many teenagers enjoy designing their own pages. They use symbols, short lists, and colorful pens (2) ______ (mark) important dates.

My cousin Lily started her first bullet journal last year. She says it has made her (3) ______ (organized) than before. Every evening, she spends ten minutes (4) ______ (write) down what she did and what she will do tomorrow. This habit helps her worry (5) ______ (little) about forgetting homework.

Some students even draw small cartoons beside their tasks. (6) ______ a task is finished, they fill in a tiny circle next to it. The journal is not only a tool (7) ______ also a record of personal growth. When Lily looks back at old pages, she feels proud of how much she (8) ______ (achieve).

If you want to try bullet journaling, you need (9) ______ patience at the beginning. Start with one page a week, and you will find it (10) ______ (real) useful for managing your time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`simple` 以辅音音素开头，泛指“一本简单的笔记本”，用 `a`。

2. **答案：to mark**
   - **考点**：动词不定式作目的状语。用笔等“为了标注重要日期”，表目的用 `to mark`。

3. **答案：more organized**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than before` 提示比较级，`organized` 为多音节词，用 `more organized`。`make sb. + adj.` 结构。

4. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`spend time (in) doing sth.` 花费时间做某事，用 `writing`。

5. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。与“之前”隐含对比，担心“更少”，`little` 的比较级为 `less`，修饰动词 `worry`。

6. **答案：When / After / If**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导时间或条件从句：“当一项任务完成时/在任务完成之后”，用 `When` 或 `After`；若理解为“如果完成了就填圈”，也可用 `If`。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only ... but also ...` 不仅……而且……。

8. **答案：has achieved**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。回顾过去页面时，强调“已经取得的成就”，主句过去时，从句可用现在完成时表截止现在的结果；或 `had achieved` 表“过去的过去”。中考常见答案为 `has achieved`（与 `how much` 搭配表到现在为止）。

9. **答案：some / a little**
   - **考点**：限定词/代词。`patience` 不可数，表示“需要一点耐心”，用 `some patience` 或 `a little patience`。

10. **答案：really**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `useful`，`real` → `really` 副词。
