# 12-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园跑操 (School Morning Running)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has a tradition of morning running. Every day, all students are required __1__ (run) around the playground for twenty minutes before the first class. At first, many students __2__ (complain) about it because they preferred __3__ (stay) in bed a little longer. I was one of them. However, after a few weeks, I noticed that I felt more __4__ (energy) throughout the day. Our PE teacher explained that morning exercise helps __5__ (we) brain work better, which is good for learning. He also said that students __6__ exercise regularly are less likely to get sick. Now I actually enjoy the morning run. I like __7__ (breath) the fresh air and chatting with my friends while running. Last month, the school held a running competition __8__ classes. Our class came __9__ (one) because everyone tried their best. The experience taught us that sticking to something __10__ seems hard at the beginning can bring unexpected rewards.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to run**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。be required to do sth. 被要求做某事，用不定式。

2. **答案：complained**
   - **考点**：动词时态。At first 起初，描述过去的情况用一般过去时 complained。

3. **答案：to stay**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾语）。prefer to do sth. 更喜欢做某事，用不定式。

4. **答案：energetic**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。feel more energetic 感觉更有精力，feel 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。

5. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。our brain 我们的大脑，修饰名词用形容词性物主代词。

6. **答案：who/that**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。who/that 引导定语从句修饰 students，在从句中作主语。

7. **答案：breathing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。like breathing 喜欢呼吸，like 后接动名词表示习惯性喜好。

8. **答案：between/among**
   - **考点**：介词。between/among classes 班级之间，表示多个班级之间的竞争。

9. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。came first 取得第一名，one 的序数词为 first。

10. **答案：that/which**
    - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。that/which 引导定语从句修饰 something，在从句中作主语。
