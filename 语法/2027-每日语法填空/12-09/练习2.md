# 12-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：老年人学用智能手机 (Elderly People Learning to Use Smartphones)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My grandmother got her first smartphone last month. She was both excited __1__ nervous because she had never used one before. I decided __2__ (teach) her step by step. First, I showed her how to make phone __3__ (call). Then I taught her to send voice messages, which was much __4__ (easy) for her than typing. The __5__ (big) challenge was helping her use WeChat. She kept __6__ (forget) how to open it. I wrote down every step on a piece of paper so that she __7__ follow the instructions by herself. After two weeks of practice, she could __8__ (final) video-call her old friends in other cities. She was so happy that she called me __9__ (immediate) to say thank you. Nowadays, many elderly people face the same problem. I think young people should be more __10__ (patience) and willing to help them keep up with modern technology.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。both... and... 既……又……，为固定搭配。

2. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾语）。decide to do sth. 决定做某事，用不定式。

3. **答案：calls**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。make phone calls 打电话，call 为可数名词，此处泛指多次通话用复数。

4. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。easier than 比……更容易，than 提示用比较级，easy 的比较级为 easier。

5. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the biggest challenge 最大的挑战，the 提示用最高级，big 的最高级为 biggest。

6. **答案：forgetting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。keep forgetting 总是忘记，keep doing sth. 持续做某事。

7. **答案：could**
   - **考点**：情态动词。so that she could 以便她能够，so that 引导目的状语从句，用 could 表示能力。

8. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 video-call，用副词 finally。

9. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 called，用副词 immediately，表示"立即"。

10. **答案：patient**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。be more patient 更有耐心，be 后接形容词作表语。
