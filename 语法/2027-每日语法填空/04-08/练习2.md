# 04-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市农场 (Urban Farming)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Urban farming is becoming popular in crowded cities. On rooftops and balconies, people grow vegetables in boxes (1) ______ (make) from recycled wood. This idea turns empty spaces into small green corners.

In our neighborhood, volunteers built (2) ______ community garden last spring. We divided the land into plots, and each family got (3) ______ area to plant tomatoes or lettuce. A retired engineer taught us how (4) ______ (mix) soil and compost.

At first, I worried that the plants would die because I knew (5) ______ about farming. Luckily, older neighbors checked on us every weekend. They showed me how to water the crops (6) ______ (gentle) and how to remove harmful insects by hand.

Harvest day was the (7) ______ (excite) moment of the season. Children ran between the rows, (8) ______ (carry) baskets of peppers. We shared the food with elderly people (9) ______ lived alone nearby.

Urban farming will not solve all food problems, but it teaches us to value resources. I believe more schools (10) ______ (build) similar projects so students can learn outside the classroom.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`boxes made from recycled wood` 由回收木材制成的箱子。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a community garden` 一个社区菜园。

3. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`area` 以元音音素开头，`an area`。

4. **答案：to mix**
   - **考点**：“疑问词 + 不定式”。`how to mix` 如何混合。

5. **答案：little / nothing**
   - **考点**：代词。`knew little about` 对农业知之甚少；或 `nothing`。

6. **答案：gently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `water`，`gentle` → `gently`。

7. **答案：most exciting**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the ... moment of the season` 表范围，用最高级。

8. **答案：carrying**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。孩子们奔跑并“提着”篮子。

9. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `people`，从句缺主语。

10. **答案：will build / should build**
    - **考点**：动词时态。表将来或建议，更多学校将/应建立类似项目。
