# 04-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：民宿旅行 (Homestay Travel)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the last holiday, my family chose a homestay in a mountain village instead of (1) ______ (stay) in a big hotel. The house belonged to a friendly couple (2) ______ had lived there for twenty years.

On the first evening, the hostess cooked local dishes for us. The flavors were (3) ______ (strong) than those in city restaurants, but they tasted fresh and real. Her husband told stories about the old bridge (4) ______ stood near the river.

In the morning, we walked along narrow paths and watched farmers work in the fields. My little sister picked wild flowers and shouted with joy (5) ______ she saw butterflies. I took photos, hoping (6) ______ (share) the beauty with my classmates later.

Living with strangers for three days sounded difficult at first. However, we learned to respect different habits, (7) ______ helped us become more open-minded. When we left, we promised (8) ______ (visit) again next autumn.

Homestay travel is not always cheaper, (9) ______ it offers memories that money cannot buy. If you plan a trip like this, book a room early (10) ______ the busy season arrives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：staying**
   - **考点**：动名词。`instead of doing` 代替做某事，用 `staying`。

2. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `couple`，从句缺主语。

3. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示比较级。

4. **答案：which / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `bridge`，从句缺主语。

5. **答案：when / as**
   - **考点**：从属连词。当她看到蝴蝶时，用 `when` 或 `as`。

6. **答案：to share**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`hope to do sth.` 希望做某事。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句，用 `which`。

8. **答案：to visit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`promise to do sth.` 承诺做某事。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。不一定更便宜，但提供金钱买不到的回忆。

10. **答案：before**
    - **考点**：从属连词。在旺季到来之前预订，`before` 引导时间状语从句。
