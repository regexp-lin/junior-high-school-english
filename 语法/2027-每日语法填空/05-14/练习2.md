# 05-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园马拉松 (School Mini Marathon)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's mini marathon is only three kilometers, 1. ______ it still challenges beginners. Last Saturday morning, runners gathered 2. ______ the sports field and warmed up together. Teachers marked 3. ______ safe route with flags and placed water stations along the way. When the starting gun sounded, I ran 4. ______ slowly than I expected because I wanted 5. ______ (save) energy for the last hill. Volunteers stood 6. ______ the road and shouted encouragement. Near the finish line, I saw my friend Lily 7. ______ (fall) behind, so I slowed down to run beside her. We crossed the line hand in hand and received 8. ______ medal each. The PE teacher praised us 9. ______ team spirit and reminded everyone 10. ______ (stretch) after the race. Tired but happy, I knew the race was about friendship as much as speed.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“只有三公里”与“仍然挑战初学者”为转折关系，故填 **but**。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。**on the sports field** 表示“在运动场上”，故填 **on**。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a safe route**，故填 **a**。

4. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。由 **than** 可知用 **slowly** 的比较级 **more slowly**。

5. **答案：to save**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**want to do sth.**，故填 **to save**。

6. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。**stand by the road** 表示“站在路边”，故填 **by**。

7. **答案：fall**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**see sb. do sth.** 表示“看见某人做某事（全过程）”，故填 **fall**。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**received a medal each**，故填 **a**。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。**praise sb. for sth.** 表示“因某事表扬某人”，故填 **for**。

10. **答案：to stretch**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**remind sb. to do sth.**，故填 **to stretch**。
