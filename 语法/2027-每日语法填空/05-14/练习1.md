# 05-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：故宫文创 (Palace Museum Creative Products)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Creative products from the Palace Museum turn history into objects we can touch. Last month, I bought 1. ______ notebook with patterns from ancient paintings. The cover feels smooth, 2. ______ the pages are thick enough for ink pens. My cousin collects 3. ______ (color) bookmarks shaped like roof animals. She says each design tells 4. ______ story from the old palace. Online shops also sell mugs, bags, 5. ______ even earphones with traditional patterns. Some artists use digital tools 6. ______ (make) new images, while others copy lines 7. ______ (care) by hand. Young customers like these products because they are 8. ______ (culture) and useful at the same time. A guide told us 9. ______ creative work helps people feel close to heritage. I hope more museums will share 10. ______ ideas in this way.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a notebook**，故填 **a**。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。描述封面与内页，表并列，故填 **and**。

3. **答案：colorful / colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 **bookmarks** 需用形容词，名词 color 的形容词形式为 **colorful**（英式 **colourful** 亦可）。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**tell a story**，故填 **a**。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。列举并列宾语，故填 **and**。

6. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的，故填 **to make**。

7. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **copy**，用副词 **carefully**。

8. **答案：cultural**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 **useful** 并列作表语，用形容词 **cultural**。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**told us** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

10. **答案：their**
    - **考点**：代词。指代 **more museums**，修饰 **ideas**，用形容词性物主代词 **their**。
