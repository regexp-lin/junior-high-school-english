# 01-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：郑和下西洋 (Zheng He's Voyages)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Long before European explorers set sail across the Atlantic Ocean, Chinese navigator Zheng He had already led seven great voyages across the Indian Ocean. Between 1405 and 1433, Zheng He commanded a fleet of over 300 ships and 27,000 crew members — the (1) ______ (large) naval expedition the world had ever seen at that time. His treasure ships were enormous, measuring over 120 metres in (2) ______ (long), which was several times (3) ______ (big) than the ships Columbus would later use. Zheng He's voyages took him to more than 30 countries, (4) ______ (stretch) from Southeast Asia to the east coast of Africa. Unlike many European explorers who sought to conquer new lands, Zheng He's main purpose was to establish friendly (5) ______ (relation) and promote trade between China and other nations. He brought gifts of silk, porcelain and tea (6) ______ the rulers he visited, and in return, he received exotic goods such as spices, gems and rare animals. One of the most (7) ______ (fascinate) stories is that he brought back a giraffe from Africa, (8) ______ was displayed in the imperial court and caused great excitement. Sadly, after Zheng He's death, the Ming government decided (9) ______ (stop) the voyages, and China's great age of exploration came to an end. Nevertheless, Zheng He remains one of the most (10) ______ (admire) figures in Chinese history, a symbol of courage, diplomacy and the spirit of exploration.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the largest naval expedition`，范围限定用最高级。

2. **答案：length**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`in length` 表示"在长度上"，介词后接名词。

3. **答案：bigger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`several times bigger than`，倍数比较。

4. **答案：stretching**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随状语，"延伸到"。

5. **答案：relations / relationships**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`friendly relations`，外交关系。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`brought gifts to the rulers`，表示"带给"。

7. **答案：fascinating**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `a giraffe`。

9. **答案：to stop**
   - **考点**：不定式。`decide to do sth` 为固定搭配。

10. **答案：admired**
    - **考点**：过去分词。`the most admired figures`，表示"最受敬仰的"。
