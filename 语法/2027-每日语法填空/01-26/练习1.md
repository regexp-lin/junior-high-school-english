# 01-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：社区花园 (Community Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A community garden is a shared green space where neighbours come together to grow plants, vegetables and flowers. Our neighbourhood set up a community garden last spring, and it has since become one of the most popular (1) ______ (gather) spots in the area. The garden (2) ______ (locate) on a piece of land that used to be an empty car park. A group of enthusiastic residents, (3) ______ (include) my mother, came up with the idea and worked hard to make it a reality. They cleared the rubbish, brought in fresh soil and built raised garden beds for everyone to use. Each family was given a small plot of land to grow (4) ______ they wanted. My mother and I chose to plant tomatoes and sunflowers. Taking care of the plants taught me a lot about (5) ______ (patient) and responsibility. You cannot rush a plant to grow — you simply have to water it, give it sunlight and wait. Over the following months, I watched in amazement as tiny seeds (6) ______ (transform) into tall, healthy plants bearing bright red tomatoes and golden sunflowers. The community garden has brought many (7) ______ (benefit) to our neighbourhood. It has made the area (8) ______ (green) and more beautiful. More (9) ______ (important), it has helped neighbours who barely knew each other become friends. People now chat and share gardening tips while tending their plots. It is wonderful to see how a simple idea can have such a (10) ______ (power) impact on a community.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：gathering**
   - **考点**：动名词作定语。`gathering spots`，聚集的场所。

2. **答案：is located**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`be located on` 表示"位于"。

3. **答案：including**
   - **考点**：介词。`including my mother`，表示"包括"。

4. **答案：whatever / what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`grow whatever/what they wanted`，引导宾语从句。

5. **答案：patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `about` 后接名词。

6. **答案：transformed / were transformed**
   - **考点**：时态。`transformed into`，描述变化过程。

7. **答案：benefits**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many benefits`，`many` 修饰可数名词复数。

8. **答案：greener**
   - **考点**：比较级。`made the area greener and more beautiful`，与之前相比。

9. **答案：importantly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`More importantly`，副词修饰整个句子。

10. **答案：powerful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `impact` 需要形容词形式。
