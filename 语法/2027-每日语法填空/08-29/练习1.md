# 08-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学编程 (Learning to Code)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Computer programming, or coding, is becoming an ___1___ (increase) important skill in the modern world. Many schools have started offering coding classes to students as young as eight or nine. I first became interested ___2___ coding when I was in Grade Six.

At that time, my cousin showed me a simple game he ___3___ (create) by himself using a programming language called Python. I was amazed that a teenager could build something so cool just by ___4___ (type) lines of code on a computer. I asked him ___5___ (teach) me, and he agreed happily.

Learning to code was not easy at first. I often made mistakes and my programmes ___6___ (not work) as expected. Sometimes I spent hours ___7___ (try) to find a single error. But every time I solved a problem, I felt a great sense of ___8___ (achieve).

Now I can build simple websites and small games. Last month, I even developed an app that helps our class monitor track homework ___9___ (submit). My teacher said it was one of the most ___10___ (create) projects she had ever seen from a student.

I believe that coding will open many doors for me in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 副词）。修饰形容词 `important` 需用副词，**increase** 的副词形式为 **increasingly**（日益增长地）。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。**be / become interested in...**（对……感兴趣）为固定搭配，故填 **in**。

3. **答案：had created**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`showed`（过去时）为时间参照点，表哥创建游戏发生在"展示"之前，即过去的过去，用过去完成时 **had created**。

4. **答案：typing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**by doing sth.**（通过做某事），介词 `by` 后接动名词，故填 **typing**。

5. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**ask sb. to do sth.**（请求某人做某事），故填 **to teach**。

6. **答案：didn't work / did not work**
   - **考点**：动词时态（否定形式）。叙述过去的情况用一般过去时否定式，`work` 的一般过去时否定为 **didn't work**。

7. **答案：trying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth.**（花时间做某事），故填 **trying**。

8. **答案：achievement**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。**a sense of + 名词**（一种……感），**achieve** 的名词形式为 **achievement**（成就感）。

9. **答案：submission / submissions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。动词 `track`（追踪）后接名词作宾语，**submit** 的名词形式为 **submission**（提交），可用单数或复数 **submissions**。

10. **答案：creative**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。**the most + 多音节形容词** 构成最高级，**create** 的形容词形式为 **creative**（有创造力的）。
