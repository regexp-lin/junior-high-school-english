# 08-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统刺绣 (Traditional Embroidery)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese embroidery is one of the oldest and most ___1___ (beauty) handicrafts in the world. With a history of more than 3,000 years, it ___2___ (regard) as a shining treasure of Chinese culture.

There are four major styles of Chinese embroidery: Su embroidery from Suzhou, Xiang embroidery from Hunan, Yue embroidery from Guangdong and Shu embroidery from Sichuan. Each style has ___3___ (it) own unique features. For example, Su embroidery is famous ___4___ its fine and delicate stitching, while Xiang embroidery is known for using bold colours to create lifelike images of animals.

My grandmother is ___5___ (skill) at Yue embroidery. She learned it from her mother when she was just a little girl. I often watch her work — her fingers move so ___6___ (quick) and steadily that I can ___7___ (hard) follow them. She once spent three months ___8___ (complete) a large piece showing a peacock spreading its tail. The colours were so vivid ___9___ the peacock almost seemed to come alive.

Unfortunately, fewer and fewer young people are willing to learn traditional embroidery today. Many worry that this precious art form may gradually ___10___ (appear). I hope more efforts will be made to pass it on to future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。**the most + 多音节形容词** 构成最高级，与 `oldest` 并列，**beauty** 的形容词形式为 **beautiful**。

2. **答案：is regarded**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。陈述客观事实用一般现在时；主语 `it`（指刺绣）与 `regard` 之间为被动关系（刺绣"被视为"），故填 **is regarded**。

3. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词（物主代词）。修饰名词 `own unique features` 需用形容词性物主代词，`it` 的形容词性物主代词为 **its**。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。**be famous for...**（因……而著名）为固定搭配，故填 **for**。

5. **答案：skilful / skilled**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。系动词 `is` 后接形容词作表语，**skill** 的形容词形式为 **skilful**（熟练的）或 **skilled**。**be skilful/skilled at**（擅长……）。

6. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。**so + adv. + that...**（如此……以至于……），修饰动词 `move` 需用副词 **quickly**，与 `steadily` 并列。

7. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。**can hardly do sth.**（几乎不能做某事），**hard** 的副词形式 **hardly** 表示"几乎不"。

8. **答案：completing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth.**（花时间做某事），故填 **completing**。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。**so + adj. + that...**（如此……以至于……），`vivid` 为形容词，故填 **that** 引导结果状语从句。

10. **答案：disappear**
    - **考点**：词性转换（加前缀）。`may` 为情态动词，后接动词原形；根据语境"这种珍贵的艺术形式可能会逐渐消失"，在 `appear` 前加否定前缀 `dis-`，故填 **disappear**。
