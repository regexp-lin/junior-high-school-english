# 07-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：夜市美食 (Night Market Food)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Night markets have become popular meeting places for both locals and tourists in many cities across China. Street food there is cheap, fresh, and full of local flavor. Last Saturday evening, my cousin and I visited ___1___ famous night market in our city because we had heard so much about it online. The weather was pleasant, and strings of warm yellow lights hung above the crowded lanes, making everything look inviting. As soon as we arrived, we were attracted by ___2___ wonderful smell of grilled meat and sweet snacks that filled the cool night air. Stalls lined both sides of the road, and friendly sellers waved to passers-by with smiles. Many people ___3___ (walk) around happily, carrying small bags of food and looking for the next treat to sample. My stomach was already growling, so we decided to try something filling first. We stopped ___4___ a stall that sold handmade noodles, where a long queue showed how popular it was. The cook ___5___ (skill) pulled the dough into long, thin strips in front of us while customers watched ___6___ interest, phones in hand. I ordered a bowl of spicy noodles and found ___7___ (they) even ___8___ (delicious) than I had expected. ___9___ we were eating, a street musician began to play soft music nearby, and several children danced along without shame. Before we left, we also tried some fruit juice ___10___ (make) from fresh oranges to take away for my aunt. It was truly a tasty and lively night we would love to repeat.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。此处表示“一个著名的夜市”，famous 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 a 表示泛指。

2. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。smell 后有 of 短语限定，特指“烤肉和甜点的香味”，用定冠词 the。

3. **答案：were walking**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去某一时刻正在进行的动作，与上下文 arrived、stopped 等过去背景一致，用过去进行时 were walking（主语 people 表复数概念）。

4. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。stop at 表示“在某处停下”，stall 为小地点，用 at。

5. **答案：skillfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 pulled 需用副词，skill 的副词形式为 skillfully（也可拼写 skilfully）。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 with interest 表示“饶有兴趣地”，作方式状语。

7. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词。作动词 found 的宾语，指代前面的 noodles，用人称代词宾格 them。

8. **答案：more delicious**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 than，delicious 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more delicious。

9. **答案：While / When**
   - **考点**：连词。引导时间状语从句，表示“在我们吃东西的时候”，While 与 When 均可接延续性动词 eating（While 更强调同时进行）。

10. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。juice 与 make 为被动关系，“由新鲜橙子制成的果汁”，用过去分词 made 作后置定语。
