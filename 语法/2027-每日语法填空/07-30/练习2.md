# 07-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：森林保护 (Forest Conservation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Forests are often called the “lungs of the Earth” because they take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen. Trees also help keep soil in place when heavy rain falls, and they provide homes for birds, insects, and many other creatures. ___1___, in many areas, large areas of forest ___2___ (cut) down every year for farming or building, which worries environmental groups around the world. This not only harms wildlife ___3___ also influences the climate, since fewer trees means less carbon dioxide can be absorbed naturally. To solve the problem, our government ___4___ (take) several measures in recent years, including stricter laws and public education programs. For example, some nature reserves ___5___ (set) up to protect rare plants and animals from further loss. Many young people volunteer ___6___ (plant) trees on hillsides each spring, wearing gloves and carrying buckets of water under the sun. Scientists say that if we work together and keep these habits, the situation ___7___ (get) better soon. ___8___ important thing is to reduce unnecessary use of paper products in daily life, such as shopping bags and takeaway boxes. Everyone should remember ___9___ forests belong to all of us, not just ___10___ a few companies that profit from wood. Small actions can make a big difference when millions of people share the same goal.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连词。前句讲森林的重要性，本句讲森林遭砍伐，为转折关系；空后有逗号，用 However（注意大写）比 but 更合适。

2. **答案：are cut**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。every year 表示经常性情况，用一般现在时；forests 与 cut down 为被动关系，主语为复数，故用 are cut。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。固定结构 not only ... but also ... 表示“不但……而且……”。

4. **答案：has taken**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 in recent years 常与现在完成时连用，主语 government 此处作整体，谓语用单数，故 has taken。

5. **答案：have been set**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。reserves 与 set up 为被动；强调“已经建立”且与 in recent years 语境一致，用现在完成时的被动语态 have been set。

6. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定搭配 volunteer to do sth. 表示“自愿做某事”，用不定式 to plant。

7. **答案：will get**
   - **考点**：动词时态。在 if 引导的条件状语从句中，主句表将来结果常用一般将来时（从句用一般现在时表将来），故主句用 will get。

8. **答案：An**
   - **考点**：冠词。表示“一件重要的事情”，important 以元音音素开头，用不定冠词 An（句首大写）。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。引导宾语从句，作 remember 的宾语，陈述事实用 that（口语中可省略，但填空题常填 that）。

10. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。belong to 为固定短语，意为“属于”；not just to a few companies 与前面 belong to all of us 结构平行。
