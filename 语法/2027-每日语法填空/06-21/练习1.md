# 06-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑假计划 (Summer Holiday Plans)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Summer holiday is coming, and my classmates are making different plans. Some of them will travel ___1___ their families, while others plan to take extra courses. My friend Wang Lei ___2___ (decide) to join a swimming club because he wants to learn ___3___ to swim properly. Li Mei is going to volunteer at a children's hospital. She thinks it will be a ___4___ (meaning) experience. As for me, I have made a list of ten books that I want ___5___ (finish) during the holiday. I also hope ___6___ (improve) my cooking skills by helping my mother in the kitchen. Of course, we shouldn't spend ___7___ whole summer studying. It's equally important to relax and have fun. My family ___8___ (plan) a trip to Yunnan next month. I'm looking forward to ___9___ (see) the beautiful Erhai Lake. I believe this summer ___10___ (be) both productive and enjoyable.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。travel with their families 表示"和家人一起旅行"，填 with。

2. **答案：has decided**
   - **考点**：动词时态。表示已经做出的决定，对现在有影响，用现在完成时，单数主语填 has decided。

3. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连词。how to swim 表示"如何游泳"，learn how to do sth.，填 how。

4. **答案：meaningful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 experience，需用形容词形式，meaning → meaningful。

5. **答案：to finish**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。want to do sth. 表示"想要做某事"，填 to finish。

6. **答案：to improve**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。hope to do sth. 表示"希望做某事"，填 to improve。

7. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。the whole summer 表示"整个夏天"，whole 前用定冠词 the。

8. **答案：is planning**
   - **考点**：动词时态。next month 提示将来动作，be planning 表示"正在计划"（现在进行时表将来），填 is planning。

9. **答案：seeing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。look forward to doing sth. 表示"期待做某事"，to 为介词，后接动名词，填 seeing。

10. **答案：will be**
    - **考点**：动词时态。I believe 后接宾语从句，表示对将来的期望，用一般将来时 will be。
