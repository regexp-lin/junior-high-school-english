# 06-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：书法艺术 (The Art of Calligraphy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese calligraphy is not just a way of ___1___ (write) — it is considered one of the highest forms of art in China. The tools are simple: a brush, ink, paper and an inkstone, ___2___ (know) together as the "Four Treasures of the Study". Learning calligraphy requires great ___3___ (patient) because each stroke must be done with care. My grandfather has practised calligraphy ___4___ over forty years. Watching him write is like watching a ___5___ (dance) perform on stage — every movement is smooth and graceful. He often tells me that calligraphy can calm the mind and train people ___6___ (concentrate). Last year, he encouraged me to attend a calligraphy class ___7___ was held every Saturday morning. At first, my characters looked ___8___ (terrible) messy. But with regular practice, I ___9___ (gradual) improved. Now I can write some basic characters quite well. I feel proud ___10___ I see my work hanging on the wall at home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。of 为介词，后接动名词，a way of writing 表示"一种书写方式"，填 writing。

2. **答案：known**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作补充说明，known together as 表示"合称为"，填 known。

3. **答案：patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。great 修饰名词，patient → patience。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for over forty years 表示"四十多年来"，与现在完成时搭配，填 for。

5. **答案：dancer**
   - **考点**：词性转换。a dancer 表示"一个舞者"，dance → dancer。

6. **答案：to concentrate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。train sb. to do sth. 表示"训练某人做某事"，填 to concentrate。

7. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a calligraphy class（物），在从句中作主语，用 that 或 which。

8. **答案：terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 messy，需用副词形式，terrible → terribly。

9. **答案：gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 improved，需用副词形式，gradual → gradually。

10. **答案：when**
    - **考点**：连词。when I see... 表示"当我看到……时"，引导时间状语从句，填 when。
