# 01-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：自律的力量 (The Power of Self-discipline)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Self-discipline is the ability to control your own behaviour and make (1) ______ (you) do what you know you should do, even when you do not feel like it. It is one of the most important qualities a student can have. Without self-discipline, it is nearly (2) ______ (possible) to achieve long-term goals, no matter how talented you are. I used to be a very (3) ______ (organise) person. I would leave my homework until the last minute and often forget to bring the right textbooks to school. My grades (4) ______ (suffer) as a result. Then, at the start of Year Eight, my father sat me down and had a serious talk with me. He said, "Talent is nothing (5) ______ discipline. The most successful people in the world are not always the smartest — they are the ones who work the (6) ______ (hard)." His words hit me deeply. From that day on, I decided (7) ______ (change) my habits. I started waking up thirty minutes earlier each morning to review my notes. I created a study timetable and stuck to it strictly. Whenever I felt the urge to check my phone, I would remind (8) ______ (I) of my goals. It was not easy at first, and there were many times (9) ______ I wanted to give up. But gradually, self-discipline became a habit. My grades improved (10) ______ (dramatic), and I felt more confident and in control of my life than ever before.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：yourself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`make yourself do sth`，主语隐含 `you`。

2. **答案：impossible**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`nearly impossible`，加否定前缀 `im-`。

3. **答案：disorganised / unorganised**
   - **考点**：词性转换。根据后文语境（拖延作业、忘带课本），需要加否定前缀，表示"无条理的"。

4. **答案：suffered**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的情况。

5. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`Talent is nothing without discipline`，表示"没有纪律，才华一文不值"。

6. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`work the hardest`，副词最高级。

7. **答案：to change**
   - **考点**：不定式。`decide to do sth` 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`remind myself of my goals`，主语为 `I`。

9. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。`there were many times when I wanted to give up`，`when` 引导定语从句修饰 `times`。

10. **答案：dramatically**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词形式。
