# 01-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：跨文化交流 (Cross-cultural Communication)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In today's globalised world, the ability to communicate across cultures has become (1) ______ (increasing) important. Our school recently launched a pen-pal programme that connects students with peers from other countries. I was paired with a girl named Emily from London, and we have been (2) ______ (write) to each other for six months now. Through our letters, I (3) ______ (learn) so much about British culture — from their afternoon tea tradition to their love of football. Emily told me that British people talk about the weather a lot, (4) ______ surprised me because Chinese people rarely do that. In return, I have shared many aspects of Chinese culture with her. She was (5) ______ (particular) fascinated by the Mid-Autumn Festival and asked me to send her some mooncakes. When she received them, she said they were unlike (6) ______ (something) she had ever tasted before. One of the most valuable (7) ______ (lesson) I have learned from this experience is that cultural (8) ______ (different) should be celebrated rather than feared. What may seem strange in one culture is often perfectly (9) ______ (normally) in another. By understanding and respecting these differences, we can build (10) ______ (strong) connections with people from all over the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `important` 需要副词形式。

2. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：现在完成进行时。`have been writing` 表示从过去持续到现在的动作。

3. **答案：have learned / have learnt**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`for six months` 表示一段时间，强调到目前的结果。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个事实。

5. **答案：particularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `fascinated` 需要副词形式。

6. **答案：anything**
   - **考点**：代词。`unlike anything she had ever tasted`，`anything` 用于比较。

7. **答案：lessons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most valuable lessons`。

8. **答案：differences**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`cultural differences`，多种文化差异。

9. **答案：normal**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`perfectly normal`，副词后接形容词。

10. **答案：stronger**
    - **考点**：比较级。`build stronger connections`，暗含与之前的比较。
