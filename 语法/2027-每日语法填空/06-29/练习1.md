# 06-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校食堂改革 (School Cafeteria Reform)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school cafeteria used to be a place ___1___ students would complain about every day. The food was tasteless, and the ___2___ (choose) were limited. Last September, the school decided to make some big changes. First, they hired a new chef ___3___ had ten years of experience in restaurant cooking. Then, a student committee ___4___ (set) up to collect opinions about the menu. Based on the ___5___ (suggest), the cafeteria now offers a wider variety of dishes, including vegetarian options and local specialities. Fresh fruit is provided ___6___ free after every meal. The dining area has also been ___7___ (complete) redesigned with new tables, better lighting and a self-service drink station. Since the changes ___8___ (make), the number of students eating in the cafeteria has doubled. Even teachers say the food tastes much ___9___ (good) than before. This experience shows that ___10___ (listen) to students' voices can lead to real improvement.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a place，在从句中作 about 的宾语（complain about），用 that 或 which。

2. **答案：choices**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。the choices 表示"可选择的种类"，choose → choice → choices。

3. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a new chef（人），在从句中作主语，用 who。

4. **答案：was set**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。committee 与 set up 之间为被动关系，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 was set。

5. **答案：suggestions**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。the suggestions 表示"建议"，suggest → suggestion → suggestions。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for free 表示"免费地"，固定搭配，填 for。

7. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 redesigned，需用副词形式，complete → completely。

8. **答案：were made**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。changes 与 make 之间为被动关系，since 从句用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were made。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级 + than，good → better。

10. **答案：listening**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。动名词作主语，表示"倾听学生的声音这件事"，listen → listening。
