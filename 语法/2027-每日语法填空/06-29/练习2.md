# 06-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：候鸟迁徙 (Bird Migration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every autumn, millions of birds fly south to escape the cold winter. This ___1___ (nature) phenomenon is called bird migration. Some birds travel ___2___ (thousand) of kilometres, crossing mountains, deserts and even oceans. The Arctic Tern holds the record for the ___3___ (long) migration of any bird — it flies from the Arctic to the Antarctic and back every year. Scientists have been studying migration patterns ___4___ many years. They use tiny tracking devices ___5___ (attach) to birds' legs to monitor their routes. Surprisingly, young birds are able to find their way ___6___ ever having made the journey before. They seem ___7___ (guide) by the Earth's magnetic field and the position of the sun. In China, Poyang Lake in Jiangxi Province is ___8___ important stopover for migratory birds. Every winter, ___9___ (hundred) of thousands of birds gather there, creating a spectacular sight. Protecting these resting sites is essential ___10___ keeping bird populations healthy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：natural**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 phenomenon，需用形容词形式，nature → natural。

2. **答案：thousands**
   - **考点**：名词复数。thousands of 表示"成千上万的"，固定搭配，thousand → thousands。

3. **答案：longest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"最长的迁徙"，long → longest。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for many years 与现在完成进行时搭配，表示"持续多年"，填 for。

5. **答案：attached**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，devices attached to birds' legs 表示"安装在鸟腿上的设备"，attach → attached。

6. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。without ever having made 表示"从未经历过的情况下"，填 without。

7. **答案：to be guided**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。seem to do sth. 表示"似乎"，birds 与 guide 之间为被动关系，填 to be guided。

8. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。important 以元音音素开头，an important stopover 表示"一个重要的中转站"，用 an。

9. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。hundreds of thousands of 表示"数十万的"，hundred → hundreds。

10. **答案：for/to**
    - **考点**：介词。be essential for/to 表示"对……是必不可少的"，填 for 或 to。
