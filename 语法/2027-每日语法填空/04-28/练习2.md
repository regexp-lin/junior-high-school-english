# 04-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市公共交通 (Urban Public Transport)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Urban public transport helps millions of people reach their workplaces and schools on time. In our city, buses and subways run (1) ______ (frequent) during rush hours than at midnight. Many commuters prefer the subway (2) ______ it is faster than driving on crowded roads.

Last month, a new subway line (3) ______ (open) near the airport. Passengers can transfer between lines (4) ______ following clear signs on the platform. The ticket machines accept both cash (5) ______ mobile payment, which makes travel easier for tourists.

I used to ride to school alone by car, (6) ______ now I take the bus with classmates to reduce pollution. On the bus, I read English articles (7) ______ I listen to light music. I believe public transport will become even (8) ______ (popular) when more lines are connected.

Drivers and station workers start their shifts before sunrise, (9) ______ passengers should be polite and follow safety rules. I hope I can help (10) ______ (spread) green travel ideas among my friends.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：more frequently**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。句中有 `than`，修饰动词 `run`，`frequent` → `frequently` 的比较级 `more frequently`。

2. **答案：because/as/since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导原因状语从句，说明偏好地铁的原因。

3. **答案：was opened**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。新线路“被开通”，叙述过去事件，用 `was opened`。

4. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by + V-ing` 表示方式，“通过跟随指示牌换乘”。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`both... and...` 既……又……。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾乘私家车上学”与“现在乘公交”为转折。

7. **答案：while/as/when**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导时间状语从句，“读文章时听音乐”，表同时进行，可用 `while`/`as`/`when`。

8. **答案：more popular**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `even` 修饰比较级，表“更受欢迎”，`popular` → `more popular`。

9. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句“司机与工作人员日出前上班”与后句“乘客应礼貌守规”为因果关系，用 `so` 引出结果。

10. **答案：(to) spread**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`help (to) do sth.` 帮助做某事。
