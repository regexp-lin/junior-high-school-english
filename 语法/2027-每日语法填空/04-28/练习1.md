# 04-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：五一劳动节 (Labour Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Labour Day on May 1 honors workers (1) ______ have contributed to society with their skills and effort. This year, my family did not travel far; instead, we decided (2) ______ (celebrate) at home by cooking together.

My father rose early (3) ______ buy fresh vegetables from the market. My mother cleaned the kitchen while I set the table (4) ______ colorful plates. By noon, the apartment (5) ______ (fill) with the smell of soup and roast chicken.

After lunch, we watched a TV program (6) ______ introduced model workers from different fields. I was impressed (7) ______ a nurse who worked night shifts for twenty years without complaint. I used to think holidays were only for fun, (8) ______ that day helped me understand the meaning of labor.

In the evening, I wrote a short speech for class about (9) ______ I had learned from my parents' daily work. I hope I can become (10) ______ (responsible) than before when I take on small tasks at home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：who/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `workers`，从句缺主语，指人，用 `who` 或 `that`。

2. **答案：to celebrate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`decide to do sth.` 决定做某事。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，“早起去买菜”。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`set the table with colorful plates` 用彩盘摆桌。

5. **答案：was filled**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。公寓“充满香味”，`be filled with`，叙述过去，用 `was filled`。

6. **答案：which/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `program`，从句缺主语，用 `which` 或 `that`。

7. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`be impressed by...` 对……印象深刻。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾以为假期只是玩乐”与“那天帮助理解劳动意义”为转折。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接词。介词 `about` 后接宾语从句，从句缺宾语，“从父母日常工作中学到的东西”，用 `what`。

10. **答案：more responsible**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`responsible` 为多音节形容词，比较级用 `more responsible`。
