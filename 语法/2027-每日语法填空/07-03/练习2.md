# 07-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国传统节气 (Chinese Solar Terms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The 24 Solar Terms is an ancient Chinese calendar system that ___1___ (divide) the year into 24 periods based on the sun's position. It ___2___ (create) by Chinese farmers over 2,000 years ago to guide agricultural activities. Each term has ___3___ (it) own name and special meaning. For example, "Grain Rain" marks the best time for ___4___ (plant) crops, while "Great Cold" warns of the coldest days of winter. In 2016, the system was added ___5___ UNESCO's list of Intangible Cultural Heritage. Even today, many Chinese people follow the solar terms to decide when ___6___ (eat) certain foods or adjust their daily routines. My grandmother always prepares special dishes ___7___ (accord) to the current solar term. On "Start of Autumn", she makes pork stew because she believes it helps the body prepare ___8___ the coming cold season. Scientists have found that these traditional ___9___ (practice) often have a real scientific basis. The 24 Solar Terms show ___10___ closely Chinese culture is connected to nature.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：divides**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。that 引导定语从句，先行词为 system（单数），陈述事实用一般现在时，填 divides。

2. **答案：was created**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。it 与 create 之间为被动关系，over 2,000 years ago 提示一般过去时的被动语态，填 was created。

3. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 own name，用形容词性物主代词 its。

4. **答案：planting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。for 为介词，后接动名词，the best time for planting 表示"种植的最佳时机"，填 planting。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。be added to 表示"被添加到"，固定搭配，填 to。

6. **答案：to eat**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。when to eat 表示"什么时候吃"，疑问词 + 不定式作宾语，填 to eat。

7. **答案：according**
   - **考点**：介词短语。according to 表示"根据"，固定搭配，accord → according。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。prepare for 表示"为……做准备"，固定搭配，填 for。

9. **答案：practices**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。these 后接可数名词复数，practice → practices。

10. **答案：how**
    - **考点**：连词。how closely... is connected to 表示"多么紧密地联系"，how 引导宾语从句，填 how。
