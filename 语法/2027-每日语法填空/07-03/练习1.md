# 07-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑期兼职 (Summer Part-Time Job)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many teenagers look for part-time jobs during summer ___1___ (holiday). Working part-time can teach young people valuable life skills such as ___2___ (responsible), teamwork and time management. Last summer, my older sister got a job at a local bookshop. Her ___3___ (day) tasks included arranging books on shelves, helping customers find what they needed and operating the cash register. She worked four hours a day, five days ___4___ week. The pay was not high, ___5___ she said she learned more there than in any classroom. One of the ___6___ (big) challenges was dealing with difficult customers. Some would complain loudly about the price or return books ___7___ no reason. My sister had to remain polite and find a ___8___ (solve) every time. By the end of the summer, she ___9___ (become) much more confident and mature. She told me that the experience helped her understand how hard ___10___ (we) parents work to support the family.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：holidays**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。summer holidays 表示"暑假"，holiday → holidays。

2. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。such as 后接名词，与 teamwork 和 time management 并列，responsible → responsibility。

3. **答案：daily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 tasks，需用形容词形式，day → daily。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。five days a week 表示"每周五天"，a 表示"每一"，填 a。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。"薪水不高"与"她说学到了很多"之间为转折关系，用 but。

6. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，big → biggest。

7. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for no reason 表示"没有理由地"，固定搭配，填 for。

8. **答案：solution**
   - **考点**：词性转换。a + 名词，find a solution 表示"找到解决办法"，solve → solution。

9. **答案：had become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。by the end of the summer 提示"到夏天结束时"，用过去完成时 had become。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 parents，用形容词性物主代词，we → our。
