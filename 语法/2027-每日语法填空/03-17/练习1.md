# 03-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市涂鸦艺术 (Street Art)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, street art has changed many neighborhoods in our city. What used to be boring gray walls have become colorful paintings that tell local stories.

Last summer, a group of young artists (1) ______ (invite) to paint a long wall near the river. They worked for two weeks, and the final work showed both modern buildings and traditional markets. Passers-by stopped (2) ______ (watch) the artists and took photos every day.

However, not everyone welcomed the project at first. Some shop owners worried that the paintings might look messy. The team leader, a woman (3) ______ (name) Lin Yue, held several meetings to explain their ideas. She promised that all materials (4) ______ (be) safe for the environment.

Gradually, people began to understand the value of public art. The wall became (5) ______ popular tourist spot, and nearby cafés saw more customers. A local newspaper described the project as "a bridge (6) ______ art and daily life".

Street art is still developing in China. Many cities now set aside special areas (7) ______ artists can work freely. Students in our school also started a club to learn basic painting skills.

I believe street art makes cities warmer and (8) ______ (live). When we walk along the river, we feel proud of our community. If more people respect the artists' work, this form of culture (9) ______ (continue) to grow.

Next month, Lin Yue's team will start (10) ______ new project in another district. I can't wait to see what they will create.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：were invited**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。艺术家“被邀请”来作画，主语为复数。

2. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`stop to do sth.` 停下来去做另一件事（观看），与 `stop doing` 区分。

3. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a woman named Lin Yue` 一位名叫林悦的女性。

4. **答案：would be**
   - **考点**：过去将来时。主句 `promised` 为过去时，从句表示“将会安全”，用 would。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a popular tourist spot` 泛指一处热门景点。

6. **答案：between**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`between A and B` 在艺术与日常生活之间。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `areas` 表地点，从句缺状语，用 where。

8. **答案：livelier / more lively**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与 `warmer` 并列，`and` 连接两个比较级，城市更温暖、更有活力。

9. **答案：will continue**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`If` 引导的条件状语从句用一般现在时，主句用将来时（主将从现）。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。`start a new project` 开始一个新项目。
