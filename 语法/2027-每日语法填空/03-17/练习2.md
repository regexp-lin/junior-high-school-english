# 03-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园辩论赛 (School Debate)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Debating is becoming more and more popular in middle schools. It helps students express opinions clearly and listen to others (1) ______ (careful).

Two weeks ago, our grade held a debate on whether students should carry smartphones to school. I was chosen (2) ______ one of the speakers for the supporting side. At first, I felt so nervous (3) ______ my hands shook while holding the card.

Our team spent five afternoons (4) ______ (prepare) arguments and examples. We searched news reports and even interviewed a parent (5) ______ works as a software engineer. She shared how phones can be used for safe communication after school activities.

On the day of the debate, the hall was full of students and teachers. The opposing team argued that phones might cause distraction and unfair comparison. We replied that schools could set clear rules instead of (6) ______ (simple) banning devices.

After three rounds, the judges scored each team on content, language, and teamwork. (7) ______ the end, we lost by a small margin, but I was still proud of our effort.

The experience taught me that winning is not everything. What matters is learning to see an issue (8) ______ different sides. My English teacher said I had made great progress in pronunciation and logic.

Now I understand (9) ______ debate is called "the art of speaking". I hope there will be (10) ______ (many) such activities next term so that more classmates can join in.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `listen`，`careful` → `carefully` 副词。

2. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be chosen as` 被选为……。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：结果状语从句。`so ... that ...` 如此……以至于……。

4. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`spend time (in) doing sth.` 花费时间做某事。

5. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `a parent` 指人，从句缺主语，用 who。

6. **答案：simply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动名词 `banning`，`simple` → `simply` 副词“仅仅”。

7. **答案：In**
   - **考点**：固定短语。`In the end` 最后，句首大写。

8. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`see ... from different sides` 从不同角度看问题。

9. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：宾语从句引导词。`understand why ...` 理解为什么辩论被称为……。

10. **答案：more**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`more such activities` 更多此类活动，`many` → `more`。
