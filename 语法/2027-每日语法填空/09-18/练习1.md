# 09-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园安全教育 (Campus Safety Education)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last week, our school __1__ (hold) a campus safety education week for all grades. Teachers showed us how __2__ stay calm during a fire drill and where the nearest exits were. We also learned __3__ to report strangers who tried to enter the teaching building __4__ permission. The school officer said, "Safety rules are not __5__ boring list—they protect everyone's life." He asked us __6__ (follow) traffic signs on the way home and never run after each other on wet stairs. My class __7__ (discuss) several real cases, and we realized small careless actions could cause big trouble. __8__ the end of the meeting, each student received __9__ simple safety handbook. I believe if we take safety seriously every day, our campus __10__ (become) a much safer place.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last week 表示过去，hold 的过去式为 held，表示学校举办了安全教育周。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：疑问词 + 不定式。how to stay calm 作 showed 的直接宾语，相当于 how we should stay calm，用 to stay。

3. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：疑问词 + 不定式。learn how to report 表示“学习如何报告”，与上文 how to stay 结构并列。

4. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。enter ... without permission 表示“未经许可进入”，符合陌生人擅自入楼的语境。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。boring list 表泛指“一份枯燥的清单”，boring 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

6. **答案：to follow**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 为固定结构，意为“要求某人做某事”。

7. **答案：discussed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述上周活动中的环节，用一般过去时 discussed。

8. **答案：At**
   - **考点**：固定短语。at the end of the meeting 在会议结束时，句首大写。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a simple safety handbook 一本简易安全手册，表泛指。

10. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。if 引导条件状语从句用一般现在时 take，主句表将来结果，用一般将来时 will become（主将从现）。
