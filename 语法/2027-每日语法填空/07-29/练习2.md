# 07-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：网络安全 (Internet Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Nowadays many teenagers spend ___1___ long time online every day, so learning about Internet safety has become necessary for students and parents alike. Last month, our school ___2___ (invite) a police officer ___3___ give us a useful talk in the school hall. He explained ___4___ (clear) that we must not share our personal information with strangers without ___5___ (think) twice, even when the person sounds friendly or offers free gifts in a message.

Some students enjoy ___6___ (post) photos on social media, but those pictures might ___7___ (show) too much about their daily lives, such as their school uniforms or street signs near their homes. The officer asked us ___8___ (turn) off location services on our phones when they are not needed, and he showed several simple steps on the screen. My teacher also warned us against ___9___ (click) unknown links or strange files, because criminals sometimes hide viruses behind attractive pictures or short videos.

If you feel uncomfortable ___10___ someone you meet in a game or a chat room, tell your parents or teachers at once instead of keeping secrets. Remember that staying safe online is a skill, and it grows stronger when you keep practicing good habits every day. Small choices, like using strong passwords and logging out on public computers, can make a big difference in the long run.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。固定搭配 spend a long time (in) doing sth.，表示“花很长时间……”，long 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

2. **答案：invited**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 Last month 表示过去，谓语用一般过去时；invite 的过去式为 invited。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式符号）。invite sb. to do sth. 邀请某人做某事，此处用不定式 to give 作宾语补足语。

4. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：副词。修饰动词 explained，表示“清楚地解释”，用副词 clearly（clear + -ly）。

5. **答案：thinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 without 后接动名词，without thinking twice 意为“不三思、轻率地”。

6. **答案：posting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。enjoy doing sth. 喜欢做某事，固定搭配，故用 posting。

7. **答案：show**
   - **考点**：情态动词后的动词形式。might 为情态动词，后接动词原形，故填 show（might show 表示“可能会显示/暴露”）。

8. **答案：to turn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式作宾语补足语，故填 to turn。

9. **答案：clicking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。warn sb. against doing sth. 警告某人不要做某事，against 为介词，后接动名词 clicking。

10. **答案：with**
    - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 feel uncomfortable with sb.，表示“和某人在一起感到不安/不舒服”，符合网络交友语境。
