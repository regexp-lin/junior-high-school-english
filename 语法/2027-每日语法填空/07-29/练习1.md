# 07-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：种植蔬菜 (Growing Vegetables)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, my family decided to start ___1___ small vegetable garden behind our house. We wanted to eat food ___2___ was both fresh and cheap. First, we spent two weekends clearing the grass and ___3___ (prepare) the soil with some natural compost. My father ___4___ (build) four wooden boxes with recycled wood, and we placed them in the sunniest corner of our balcony where the vegetables could enjoy sunlight for most of the day.

Compared ___5___ buying vegetables from the supermarket every week, home gardening seemed ___6___ (healthy) and more meaningful to us. In early spring, we ___7___ (plant) tomatoes, lettuce, and carrots together as a family activity. At first, nothing seemed ___8___ (happen), ___9___ tiny green shoots appeared when warm days finally arrived. Those little leaves brought smiles to everyone in our home.

We learned to water the plants ___10___ (careful) in the early morning because July suddenly became very hot and dry. On windy days, we covered the young plants with thin nets so that they would not break easily. Sometimes we watched short videos online, and useful tips helped us solve small problems in time. My little sister even drew pictures of our carrots and tomatoes in her notebook. When the first tomatoes turned red, we finally understood why many people say gardening is good for both the body and the mind. The experience also brought us closer as a family, and we are already planning what to grow next year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。此处表示泛指“一个小菜园”，small 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 a。若特指双方已知的那一块地，才用 the。

2. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（定语从句）。先行词是 food，从句 “___ was both fresh and cheap” 缺少主语，指物，用 that 或 which 引导定语从句。

3. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。and 连接与 clearing 并列的成分，作 spend time (in) doing 结构中 in 后宾语，介词后接动名词，故用 preparing。

4. **答案：built**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述去年夏天发生的事，全文主体为一般过去时；build 的过去式为 built。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 compared with / to，表示“与……相比”，此处指与去超市买菜相比。

6. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 than buying...，且与 more meaningful 并列，healthy 用比较级 healthier。

7. **答案：planted**
   - **考点**：动词时态。In early spring 仍属过去叙述，谓语用一般过去时，plant 的过去式为 planted。

8. **答案：to happen**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。固定结构 seem to do sth.，表示“似乎……”，故填 to happen。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说“起初好像什么也没发生”，后句说“小芽出现了”，语义转折，用 but。

10. **答案：carefully**
    - **考点**：副词。修饰动词 water，用副词形式；careful 的副词为 carefully。
