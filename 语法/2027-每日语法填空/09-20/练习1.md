# 09-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我的阅读分享 (My Reading Sharing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This term, our teacher asked each student __1__ (give) a short reading sharing in class. I chose __2__ novel about a brave teenager who solved problems with wisdom. While __3__ (prepare) my speech, I wrote down three questions __4__ my classmates could discuss in groups. On the sharing day, I felt nervous __5__ first, but their warm smiles helped me calm down. After I finished, several friends said the story made them think __6__ (deep) about friendship. Our teacher praised my clear voice and __7__ (suggest) that I borrow more books from the reading corner. Now I spend at least thirty minutes __8__ (read) every night. I believe reading opens __9__ door to a bigger world, __10__ it also improves my writing skills.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式 to give。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a novel about... 一本关于……的小说，表泛指。

3. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。while preparing 为 while I was preparing 的省略，主从句主语一致且含 be 动词时，可省略主语与 was，保留现在分词 preparing。

4. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 questions 指物，从句 my classmates could discuss 中 discuss 缺宾语，用 that 或 which 作宾语（亦可省略，但填空题宜写 that/which）。

5. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：固定短语。at first 起初、一开始。

6. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 think 用副词，deep 的副词形式为 deeply。

7. **答案：suggested**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与 praised 并列，叙述过去课堂情景，用一般过去时 suggested。

8. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 花费时间做某事，用 reading。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。opens a door to... 打开通往……的一扇门。

10. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。阅读打开世界与提高写作并列顺承，用 and。
