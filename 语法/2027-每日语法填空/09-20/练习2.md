# 09-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：动物迁徙 (Animal Migration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Animal migration is one of __1__ most amazing scenes in nature. Every year, many species travel long distances __2__ find food, safer places, or better weather. For example, some birds fly thousands of kilometers __3__ they cannot stay in cold areas in winter. Scientists have discovered that animals use the sun, stars, or even Earth's magnetic field __4__ (guide) themselves. __5__, human activities such as building roads and cutting forests have made journeys __6__ (danger) than before. Protecting wetlands and nature reserves can help migrants rest __7__ feeding along the way. If we reduce light pollution near rivers __8__ lakes, many fish may return to spawn __9__ (success). I think everyone should learn __10__ these journeys and support wildlife protection.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。one of the + 形容词最高级 + 名词复数，most amazing 前用定冠词 the。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。travel ... to find 表示迁徙是为了寻找食物等。

3. **答案：because / when / if**
   - **考点**：从属连词。because 表原因“因为不能待在寒冷地区”；when/if 表时间或条件，根据课堂讲解择一，because 最常见。

4. **答案：to guide**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。use sth. to do sth. 用……去做某事，用不定式 to guide。

5. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。上文讲动物如何导航，下文讲人类活动带来威胁，语义转折。

6. **答案：more dangerous**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than before 提示比较级，danger 的形容词为 dangerous，多音节前加 more。

7. **答案：while / when**
   - **考点**：连词。rest while feeding 表示在沿途进食时休息，while 强调同时进行。

8. **答案：or / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。rivers and lakes 或 rivers or lakes 列举水体，and 表并列，or 表选择，中考常用 and。

9. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 return 用副词，success 的副词为 successfully。

10. **答案：about / from**
   - **考点**：介词。learn about these journeys 了解这些迁徙；learn from 从这些旅程中学习，about 更贴合“了解常识”。
