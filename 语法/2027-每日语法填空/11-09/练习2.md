# 11-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园戏剧社 (School Drama Club)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I joined the school drama club at the beginning of this year. Our director, Miss Wang, is very __1__ (passion) about theatre. She selects a play for us __2__ (perform) each term. This term, we are rehearsing "A Midsummer Night's Dream" __3__ Shakespeare. I was given the role of Puck, a __4__ (mischief) fairy. Learning the lines was not easy because the language is quite different __5__ modern English. I had to practise __6__ (read) them aloud every evening. Miss Wang also taught us __7__ (express) emotions through body language. "Your face and hands must tell the story __8__ clearly as your words," she said. After weeks of rehearsal, we __9__ (final) performed on the school stage last Friday. The audience laughed, clapped and even stood up at the end. It was the __10__ (proud) moment of my school life. Drama has taught me confidence and the power of storytelling.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：passionate**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。be passionate about 对……充满热情，passion 的形容词形式为 passionate。

2. **答案：to perform**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。for us to perform 供我们表演，不定式作目的/后置定语。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。by Shakespeare 莎士比亚所写的，by 表示"由……创作"。

4. **答案：mischievous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 fairy，mischief 的形容词形式为 mischievous（淘气的）。

5. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。be different from 与……不同，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。practise doing sth. 练习做某事，用动名词 reading。

7. **答案：to express**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。teach sb. to do sth. 教某人做某事，用不定式 to express。

8. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：副词/连词。as clearly as 与……一样清楚，as... as... 为同级比较结构。

9. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 performed，用副词 finally。

10. **答案：proudest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，proud 的最高级为 proudest。
