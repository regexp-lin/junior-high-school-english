# 11-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：保护野生鸟类 (Protecting Wild Birds)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China is home __1__ over 1,400 species of wild birds, making it one of the __2__ (rich) countries in bird diversity. However, many species are __3__ danger because of habitat loss and illegal hunting. The Chinese crested ibis was once almost extinct, with only seven birds __4__ (leave) in the wild in 1981. Thanks to decades of conservation efforts, __5__ (it) population has grown to over 7,000. Bird __6__ (watch) has become a popular hobby among nature lovers. Every autumn, birdwatchers gather at lakes and wetlands __7__ (observe) migratory birds. Last November, I joined a local birdwatching group for the first time. An experienced guide taught us how to use binoculars and identify __8__ (differ) species by their songs. We spotted a beautiful kingfisher __9__ (sit) on a branch near the water. The guide whispered, "Stay quiet, __10__ you'll scare it away." That moment was truly magical.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。be home to 是……的栖息地，为固定搭配。

2. **答案：richest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，rich 的最高级为 richest。

3. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。be in danger 处于危险中，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：left**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。birds 与 leave 为被动关系，left 表示"剩余的"。

5. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 population，用形容词性物主代词 its。

6. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词构成复合名词）。Bird watching 观鸟，为复合名词。

7. **答案：to observe**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了观察候鸟"，用 to observe。

8. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 species，differ 的形容词形式为 different。

9. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作后置定语/宾补）。spotted a kingfisher sitting 看到翠鸟正坐在……，主动关系用现在分词 sitting。

10. **答案：or**
    - **考点**：并列连词。祈使句 + or... 否则……，表示"保持安静，否则你会把它吓跑"。
