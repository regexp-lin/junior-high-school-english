# 03-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家乡的变化 (Changes in My Hometown)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Great changes (1) ______ (take) place in my hometown over the past ten years. It used to be a small and quiet village (2) ______ few tall buildings. Most people lived in old houses and worked on farms. The roads were narrow and (3) ______ (dust), and it took a long time to travel to the nearest city.

Now everything is different. Wide roads (4) ______ (build) to connect the village with the city, and many modern apartment buildings have appeared. A large shopping mall (5) ______ (open) last year, which made life much more (6) ______ (convenience) for the local people.

(7) ______ thing that makes me happy is the new park in the centre of the village. It is a beautiful place (8) ______ people can go jogging, play chess or simply enjoy the fresh air. My grandmother often goes there to practise tai chi with her friends every morning.

Of course, some things have not changed. The river that runs (9) ______ the village is still clean and clear. The villagers are still warm and (10) ______ (friend). I love my hometown, both the old and the new.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have taken**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`over the past ten years` 在过去十年里，用现在完成时。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`a village with few tall buildings` 用 `with` 表示"带有、具有"。

3. **答案：dusty**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `narrow` 并列作表语，`dust` → `dusty` 形容词。

4. **答案：have been built**
   - **考点**：现在完成时被动语态。路是"被修建的"，与上文时态呼应。

5. **答案：opened / was opened**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`last year` 表过去时间。`open` 可用主动或被动均可。

6. **答案：convenient**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more convenient` 比较级，`convenience` → `convenient` 形容词。

7. **答案：One / Another**
   - **考点**：代词 / 限定词。`One thing that ...` 有一件事……，引出具体事例。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句修饰 `place`，表示地点。

9. **答案：through**
   - **考点**：介词。`runs through the village` 穿过村庄，河流穿越用 `through`。

10. **答案：friendly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `warm` 并列作表语，`friend` → `friendly` 形容词。
