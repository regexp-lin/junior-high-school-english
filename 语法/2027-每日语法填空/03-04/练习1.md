# 03-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：风筝制作 (Kite Making)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Kite making is a traditional Chinese craft that has been passed down for (1) ______ (thousand) of years. Weifang, a city in Shandong Province, (2) ______ (know) as the "Kite Capital of the World". Every April, people from different countries come here to take part (3) ______ the International Kite Festival.

Last month, our art teacher taught us how (4) ______ (make) a simple kite. First, we prepared bamboo sticks and colourful paper. Then we cut the paper into the shape of a butterfly and (5) ______ (careful) attached it to the bamboo frame. The most challenging part was (6) ______ (tie) the string at the right point so that the kite could fly steadily.

When we took our kites to the playground, I was excited to see mine fly high in the sky. (7) ______, my classmate Li Ming was not so lucky. His kite kept falling down because the tail was too (8) ______ (short) to keep it balanced. With my help, he added a longer tail and his kite (9) ______ (final) flew up.

Making kites taught (10) ______ (we) patience and creativity. It is a wonderful way to experience traditional culture while having fun.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：thousands**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`thousands of` 数以千计的，表示概数时用复数。

2. **答案：is known**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。`be known as` 被称为，陈述客观事实。

3. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`take part in` 参加。

4. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：不定式。`teach sb. how to do sth.` 教某人如何做某事。

5. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `attached`，需用副词 `carefully`。

6. **答案：tying**
   - **考点**：动名词作表语。`The most challenging part was tying ...` 主语是 part，表语用动名词。

7. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：转折连接词。前句说"我"成功了，后句说李明不顺利，逻辑转折。

8. **答案：short**
   - **考点**：`too + 形容词 + to do` 结构。太短了以至于不能保持平衡。

9. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `flew`，需用副词 `finally`。

10. **答案：us**
    - **考点**：代词宾格。`taught` 后接宾语，用宾格 `us`。
