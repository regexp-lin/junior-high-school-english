# 03-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：旧物改造 (Upcycling Old Items)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Upcycling means turning old or unwanted items into something new and (1) ______ (value). Unlike recycling, which breaks materials down, upcycling gives them a second life (2) ______ destroying their original form.

My mother is a big fan of upcycling. She has turned our home into a small gallery of (3) ______ (create) works. Old glass jars have become beautiful candle holders. Worn-out jeans (4) ______ (transform) into fashionable bags. Even broken furniture has been repaired and repainted in bright (5) ______ (colour).

Inspired by my mother, I started my own upcycling project last month. I collected some old T-shirts that no one (6) ______ (wear) any more and decided to make them into cushion covers. My mother taught me how (7) ______ (sew), and I spent every weekend working on the project.

The (8) ______ (difficult) part was cutting the fabric evenly. I had to measure (9) ______ (care) and cut slowly. After three weekends, I finished four cushion covers. They now sit on our sofa and look surprisingly good.

Upcycling is not only good for the environment (10) ______ also a fun way to express creativity. I encourage everyone to look at old items with fresh eyes before throwing them away.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `new` 并列作宾补，`value` → `valuable` 形容词。

2. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`without destroying` 不破坏，表否定条件。

3. **答案：creative**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `works`，`create` → `creative` 形容词。

4. **答案：have been transformed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时被动语态。牛仔裤是"被改造的"，与上下文时态一致。

5. **答案：colours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`bright colours` 鲜艳的颜色，多种颜色用复数。

6. **答案：wore**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。描述过去的状态，没有人再穿的旧 T 恤。

7. **答案：to sew**
   - **考点**：不定式。`teach sb. how to do sth.` 教某人如何做某事。

8. **答案：most difficult / hardest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`The most difficult part` 最困难的部分。

9. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `measure`，`care` → `carefully` 副词。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only ... but also ...` 不仅……而且……。
