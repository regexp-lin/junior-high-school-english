# 03-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园摄影比赛 (School Photography Contest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Photography has become one of the most popular hobbies among teenagers. With smartphones getting (1) ______ (good) and better, almost everyone can take high-quality photos. Our school recently held a photography contest with the theme "Beautiful Moments in Daily Life".

Over 200 students (2) ______ (submit) their works. The photos covered a wide range of topics — from sunrise over the school playground (3) ______ a grandmother's wrinkled but gentle hands. I was deeply (4) ______ (move) by many of the photos.

My friend Chen Jie entered the contest with a photo of an old man (5) ______ (read) a newspaper under a banyan tree. The light and shadows in the photo were (6) ______ (perfect) balanced. She spent three days waiting at the same spot before she (7) ______ (catch) the right moment.

The judges were three (8) ______ (profession) photographers from the city. They said they were impressed by the students' ability to find beauty in ordinary life. The first prize (9) ______ (award) to a photo showing a street cleaner smiling in the early morning mist.

I didn't win any prize this time, (10) ______ the contest inspired me to pay more attention to the beauty around me. Sometimes the most touching scenes are right in front of our eyes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`getting better and better` 越来越好。

2. **答案：submitted**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。描述过去发生的比赛，提交作品。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`from ... to ...` 从……到……。

4. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。`be deeply moved by` 被深深打动，修饰人的感受。

5. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`an old man reading a newspaper` 一位正在读报的老人。

6. **答案：perfectly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `balanced`，`perfect` → `perfectly` 副词。

7. **答案：caught**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`before she caught the right moment` 在她抓拍到合适时机之前，`catch` → `caught`。

8. **答案：professional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `photographers`，`profession` → `professional`。

9. **答案：was awarded**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。奖项是"被授予的"。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：转折连词。没获奖，但比赛激发了灵感，表转折。
