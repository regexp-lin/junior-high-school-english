# 09-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：智能家居 (Smart Home)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Imagine coming home after a long day at school. The door ___1___ (unlock) automatically as it recognises your face. The air conditioner has already been turned on, and the room is at a ___2___ (comfort) temperature. This is not a scene from a science fiction film — it is the reality of a smart home.

A smart home uses technology ___3___ (connect) everyday devices to the Internet. Lights, cameras, door locks and even kitchen appliances can all ___4___ (control) through a smartphone app or by voice commands. For example, you can tell a smart speaker ___5___ (play) your favourite music or ask it about tomorrow's weather.

Smart homes offer many ___6___ (benefit). They can help save energy by ___7___ (automatic) turning off lights and appliances when no one is in the room. They also improve home security ___8___ cameras and sensors can detect unusual activities and send alerts to the homeowner's phone.

However, some people worry about privacy. Since smart devices collect a large amount of personal data, there is a risk that this information could be stolen ___9___ hackers. It is important for companies to develop ___10___ (strong) security systems to protect users' data.

Despite these concerns, the smart home market is growing rapidly. More and more families are choosing to make their homes smarter and more convenient.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：unlocks**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。描述智能家居的日常运作用一般现在时，主语 `The door` 为第三人称单数，故填 **unlocks**。

2. **答案：comfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。修饰名词 `temperature` 需用形容词，**comfort** 的形容词形式为 **comfortable**（舒适的）。

3. **答案：to connect**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**use sth. to do sth.**（使用某物来做某事），不定式 **to connect** 表示目的。

4. **答案：be controlled**
   - **考点**：动词语态。情态动词 `can` 后接动词原形；主语 `Lights, cameras...` 与 `control` 之间为被动关系（设备"被控制"），故填 **be controlled**。

5. **答案：to play**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**tell sb. to do sth.**（告诉/命令某人做某事），故填 **to play**。

6. **答案：benefits**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many`（许多）修饰可数名词复数，`benefit` 的复数形式为 **benefits**。

7. **答案：automatically**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词短语 `turning off` 需用副词，**automatic** 的副词形式为 **automatically**。

8. **答案：because**
   - **考点**：连词。后面 `cameras and sensors can detect...` 是解释智能家居如何提高安全性的原因，用 **because** 引导原因状语从句。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。被动语态中表示动作执行者用 **by**，`stolen by hackers` 表示"被黑客窃取"。

10. **答案：stronger**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。语境暗含"比现有系统更强"的比较含义，`strong` 的比较级为 **stronger**，表示"开发更强大的安全系统"。
