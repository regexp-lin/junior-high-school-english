# 09-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我的新同桌 (My New Deskmate)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

On the second day of the new semester, our head teacher rearranged the ___1___ (seat) in the classroom. I was placed next to a boy ___2___ (call) Zhang Hao, who had just transferred from another school.

At first, I was a little ___3___ (comfortable) because I did not know him at all. He seemed very quiet and kept ___4___ (he) during the first few days. While I chatted with other classmates during breaks, he preferred ___5___ (sit) alone and reading a thick science fiction novel.

One afternoon, I got stuck on a difficult maths problem. I ___6___ (try) several methods but none of them worked. Zhang Hao noticed my frustration and offered to help. To my surprise, he explained the problem so ___7___ (clear) that I understood it within minutes. I thanked him and we started talking about our favourite subjects.

It turned out that Zhang Hao was ___8___ (interest) in science and maths but was not confident about his English. I told him that I could help him practise English if he helped me ___9___ maths. He agreed with a big smile.

Since then, we ___10___ (become) close friends. I have learned that you should never judge a person just by their appearance. Sometimes the quietest people have the most to offer.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：seats**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the seats`（座位），教室里有多个座位，`seat` 的复数形式为 **seats**。

2. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a boy` 与 `call` 之间为被动关系（男孩"被叫做"），用过去分词 **called** 作后置定语。

3. **答案：uncomfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加前缀）。系动词 `was` 后接形容词；根据语境"我有点不自在，因为完全不认识他"，在 `comfortable` 前加否定前缀 `un-`，故填 **uncomfortable**。

4. **答案：to himself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）+ 介词。**keep to oneself**（独处，不与人交往）为固定搭配，主语为 `He`（男性），故填 **to himself**。

5. **答案：sitting / to sit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**prefer doing sth.** 或 **prefer to do sth.**（更喜欢做某事）均可，故填 **sitting** 或 **to sit**。

6. **答案：had tried**
   - **考点**：动词时态。"尝试了几种方法"发生在"张浩注意到我的沮丧"之前，即过去的过去，用过去完成时 **had tried**。

7. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。**so + adv. + that...** 结构中修饰动词 `explained` 需用副词，**clear** 的副词形式为 **clearly**。

8. **答案：interested**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。**be interested in...**（对……感兴趣），修饰人的感受用 **-ed** 形式，故填 **interested**。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**help sb. with sth.**（在某方面帮助某人），故填 **with**。

10. **答案：have become**
    - **考点**：动词时态。**Since then**（从那以后）提示现在完成时；主语 `we` 为复数，故填 **have become**。
