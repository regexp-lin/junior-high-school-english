# 08-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：共享单车 (Shared Bicycles)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Shared bicycles first appeared in Chinese cities around 2016, and they quickly became a popular way of ___1___ (get) around. People could unlock a bike by scanning a QR code on their phones, ride it to ___2___ (they) destination and then leave it at any convenient spot.

The idea behind shared bikes was simple ___3___ brilliant: provide a cheap and green form of transport that could solve the "last kilometre" problem. Many people used them for short trips between bus stops, subway stations and their homes ___4___ offices.

However, shared bicycles also brought some ___5___ (expect) problems. In some cities, too many bikes ___6___ (place) on pavements, blocking the way for pedestrians. Some users parked the bikes ___7___ (careless), and others even damaged them on purpose. These problems made city governments think ___8___ (serious) about how to manage the shared bike industry.

Over the past few years, new rules ___9___ (introduce) to improve the situation. Companies must now limit the number of bikes and set up proper parking areas. Users who break the rules may receive ___10___ lower credit score, which discourages bad behaviour.

Today, shared bicycles continue to be an important part of urban transport in China. With better management, they can truly make our cities cleaner and more convenient.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：getting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**a way of doing sth.**（做某事的方式），介词 `of` 后接动名词，故填 **getting**。

2. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（物主代词）。修饰名词 `destination` 需用形容词性物主代词，`they` 的形容词性物主代词为 **their**。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。**simple but brilliant**（简单却精妙），`but` 连接两个形容词表示转折/对比关系。

4. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`their homes ___ offices` 表示"他们的家或办公室"，两者为选择关系，故用 **or**。

5. **答案：unexpected**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加前缀）。修饰名词 `problems` 需用形容词；根据语境"共享单车也带来了一些意想不到的问题"，`expect` 变为形容词 `expected`，再加否定前缀 `un-`，故填 **unexpected**。

6. **答案：were placed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `too many bikes` 与 `place` 之间为被动关系（单车"被放置"），叙述过去的情况用一般过去时被动语态，故填 **were placed**。

7. **答案：carelessly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `parked` 需用副词，**careless** 的副词形式为 **carelessly**，表示"随意地停放"。

8. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `think` 需用副词，**serious** 的副词形式为 **seriously**，表示"认真思考"。

9. **答案：have been introduced**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。**Over the past few years**（在过去几年中）提示现在完成时；主语 `new rules` 与 `introduce` 之间为被动关系，故填 **have been introduced**。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。`credit score`（信用分）为可数名词单数，`lower` 以辅音音素开头，此处泛指"一个更低的信用分"，故用不定冠词 **a**。
