# 08-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：参观科学馆 (Visiting a Science Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our class visited the Guangdong Science Centre, one of the ___1___ (large) science museums in South China. We ___2___ (tell) to gather at the school gate at eight o'clock, and a coach bus took us there within an hour.

The museum had many ___3___ (amaze) exhibitions. The first hall we entered was about the solar system. There was a huge model of the sun hanging from the ceiling, ___4___ (surround) by eight planets. A guide explained how the planets move around the sun and ___5___ it takes the Earth one year to complete a full orbit.

My favourite part was the robotics exhibition. I saw a robot ___6___ could play chess and even have simple conversations with visitors. When I asked it a question in English, it answered me ___7___ (correct) and made everyone laugh. My classmate Zhang Wei was so interested ___8___ he stayed there for an extra twenty minutes after the rest of us had moved on.

Before ___9___ (leave), we watched a 4D film about deep-sea creatures. The special effects made us feel as if we ___10___ (be) really swimming in the ocean. It was truly an unforgettable trip that made science come alive for all of us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词**（最……的之一），`large` 的最高级为 **largest**。

2. **答案：were told**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `We` 与 `tell` 之间为被动关系（我们"被告知"），叙述过去的事用一般过去时被动语态，故填 **were told**。

3. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。修饰名词 `exhibitions` 用形容词，**amaze** 的形容词形式 **amazing** 表示"令人惊叹的"（修饰事物用 -ing 形式）。

4. **答案：surrounded**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a huge model of the sun` 与 `surround` 之间为被动关系（模型"被行星环绕"），用过去分词 **surrounded** 作后置定语/状语。

5. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连词（宾语从句）。`explained` 后接宾语从句，此处解释的是"为什么地球需要一年时间来完成一个完整的轨道运行"，故用 **why**。也可理解为 `that`，但从语义"解释原因"的角度 **why** 更合适。

6. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `a robot` 指物，在从句中作主语，故用关系代词 **that** 或 **which**。

7. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `answered` 需用副词，**correct** 的副词形式为 **correctly**，表示"正确地回答"。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。**so + adj. + that...**（如此……以至于……），`interested` 为形容词，故填 **that** 引导结果状语从句。

9. **答案：leaving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。介词 `Before` 后接动名词，故填 **leaving**，表示"在离开之前"。

10. **答案：were**
    - **考点**：虚拟语气。**as if + 从句** 表示与事实相反的假设，"好像我们真的在海里游泳"实际上并非如此，从句中用过去时（虚拟语气），主语 `we` 对应 **were**。
