# 03-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园植物角 (School Plant Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

At the end of the corridor on the third floor, a sunny corner has turned into a small garden. Pots of mint, tomatoes, and sunflowers line the windowsill — a project (1) ______ our biology group started last term.

Every Monday, two students water the plants and write down their growth in a shared notebook. We measure height in centimeters and note the color of new leaves. These records will become part of our research report (2) ______ the school science fair.

The project was not always smooth. In February, several seedlings died (3) ______ we forgot to cover them on a cold night. We felt guilty, (4) ______ the teacher said failure is also data. We planted new seeds and built a simple plastic cover.

Now the tomato plants have grown taller (5) ______ we expected. Tiny yellow flowers have appeared, and we hope fruit (6) ______ (form) before summer vacation.

This corner has changed more than the air quality. Classmates stop by (7) ______ (chat) during breaks, and even teachers borrow mint leaves for tea. The plants seem (8) ______ (create) a warmer atmosphere in a busy building.

Looking after living things teaches responsibility. If every class cares for a corner of the school, our environment (9) ______ (become) greener step by step.

Next month, we plan to add a rain barrel (10) ______ we can collect water for the plants on dry days.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `project` 指物，从句缺宾语。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`report for the science fair` 为科技节准备的报告。

3. **答案：because / when / after**
   - **考点**：连词。`because we forgot` 因为忘记覆盖而冻死；或 `when` 表时间。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。我们内疚，但老师说失败也是数据。

5. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。`taller than we expected` 比预期更高。

6. **答案：will form**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`hope (that) fruit will form` 希望暑假前结果。

7. **答案：to chat**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`stop by to chat` 顺便过来聊天。

8. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`seem to do sth.` 似乎营造了更温暖的氛围。

9. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`If every class cares` 主将从现。

10. **答案：so that**
    - **考点**：目的状语从句。添加雨水桶以便收集雨水。
