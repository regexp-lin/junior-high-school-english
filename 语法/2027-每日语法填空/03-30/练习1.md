# 03-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：长城 (The Great Wall)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Great Wall winds across northern China like a stone dragon. It was built and rebuilt over many dynasties (1) ______ (protect) borders and connect military stations.

Today, some sections are crowded with tourists, (2) ______ quieter parts offer peaceful walks among old trees. Badaling and Mutianyu are famous for their wide steps, while Jiankou attracts hikers who enjoy a challenge.

I visited the wall with my parents last October. We arrived early (3) ______ (avoid) the midday crowds and climbed until our legs ached. Standing on a high tower, I saw mountains roll into the distance like waves (4) ______ (freeze) in green and gray.

A guide told us stories about signal fires and soldiers (5) ______ (guard) the passes centuries ago. He also reminded us not to carve names on the bricks, (6) ______ such behavior damages cultural heritage.

UNESCO lists the Great Wall as a World Heritage Site. Protecting it requires constant repair work. Workers use traditional methods whenever possible (7) ______ the ancient appearance can be kept.

Many foreigners learn about China through pictures of the wall. It has become (8) ______ symbol of both history and national spirit. Poets compare it to a strong will that refuses (9) ______ (break).

If you ever walk along its stones, listen to the wind. You may feel (10) ______ you are standing between the past and the present.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to protect**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的：修建长城是为了保卫边防。

2. **答案：while / whereas**
   - **考点**：并列连词。一些地段拥挤，而安静地段则适合漫步，表对比。

3. **答案：to avoid**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。早到以避开中午人流。

4. **答案：frozen**
   - **考点**：过去分词作宾补或定语。`like waves frozen in green` 像凝固的波浪。

5. **答案：guarding**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`soldiers guarding the passes` 守卫关口的士兵。

6. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：原因状语从句。不要在砖上刻字，因为会破坏遗产。

7. **答案：so that**
   - **考点**：目的状语从句。使用传统方法以便保持原貌。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a symbol of` 一种象征。

9. **答案：to be broken**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`refuse to be broken` 拒绝被击垮，含被动意义。

10. **答案：as if / though**
    - **考点**：表语从句。`feel as if you are standing` 仿佛站在过去与现在之间。
