# 10-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护森林 (Forest Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Forests cover about thirty percent of the earth's land. They provide homes __1__ millions of animals and produce the oxygen we breathe. __2__, human activities are destroying forests at an alarming speed. Every year, large areas of trees __3__ (cut) down for wood and farmland. This causes serious problems such __4__ soil erosion and climate change. Scientists warn that if we __5__ (not protect) forests now, many species will disappear. Some countries have already taken action. For example, China __6__ (plant) billions of trees over the past few decades. Young volunteers are also __7__ (active) involved in tree-planting campaigns. Last spring, our class planted fifty __8__ (tree) along the riverbank. It was hard work, __9__ everyone felt proud. Protecting forests is not just the government's job — it is __10__ (we) responsibility too.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。provide sth. for sb. 为……提供……，用介词 for。

2. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词（逻辑衔接）。前句说森林的好处，后句说人类活动破坏森林，语义转折，用 However，注意首字母大写和逗号。

3. **答案：are cut**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 large areas of trees 与 cut down 为被动关系，由 every year 可知为一般现在时的被动语态 are cut。

4. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。such as 例如，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：don't protect**
   - **考点**：动词时态。if 引导的条件状语从句，主句用一般将来时，从句用一般现在时，否定形式为 don't protect。

6. **答案：has planted**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 over the past few decades 可知用现在完成时，主语 China 为第三人称单数，用 has planted。

7. **答案：actively**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 involved，用副词 actively。

8. **答案：trees**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。fifty 后接可数名词复数，用 trees。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说辛苦，后句说自豪，语义转折，用 but。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 responsibility，用形容词性物主代词 our。
