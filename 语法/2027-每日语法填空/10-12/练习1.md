# 10-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园读书节 (School Reading Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school held a reading festival last week. It __1__ (last) for three days and attracted hundreds of students. On the first day, each class set up __2__ booth to display their favourite books. I was surprised __3__ (see) so many different types of books, from science fiction __4__ history. My friend Li Hua recommended a novel about a young explorer. It was one of the __5__ (good) books I had ever read. The author described the journey so __6__ (vivid) that I could picture every scene. On the second day, a famous writer __7__ was invited to our school gave a wonderful speech. She told us __8__ reading could open doors to new worlds. Many students asked her questions, and she answered them __9__ (patient). The festival ended with a book exchange. I traded two old books __10__ three new ones. What a meaningful event!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：lasted**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 last week 可知为一般过去时，last 的过去式为 lasted。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。booth 为可数名词单数，泛指"一个展位"，用不定冠词 a。

3. **答案：to see**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be surprised to do sth. 惊讶地做某事，用不定式 to see。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。from... to... 从……到……，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，表示"最……之一"，用 best。

6. **答案：vividly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 described，用副词 vividly。

7. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 writer 指人，从句缺主语，用 who 或 that。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。told us 后接宾语从句，从句意义完整不缺成分，用 that 引导。

9. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 answered，用副词 patiently。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。trade... for... 用……交换……，为固定搭配。
