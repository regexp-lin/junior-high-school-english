# 07-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：参观博物馆 (Visiting a Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, my classmates and I visited ___1___ science museum in our city. We had looked forward to this school trip for weeks, ___2___ everyone arrived at the gate earlier than usual. It was ___3___ first time for many of us to see so many wonderful exhibits together. As soon as we entered, a guide welcomed us warmly and ___4___ (lead) us to a ___5___ (science) exhibition hall on the second floor, where several new inventions ___6___ (put) on show last month. We listened to every word of the introduction and took notes quietly. The hall was full of visitors of different ages, but people still waited in line in an orderly way.

The museum ___7___ (build) only two years ago, but it has already become very popular ___8___ teenagers from ___9___ (difference) schools. Volunteers walked around and answered questions patiently, which made the tour more enjoyable. In one corner, we watched a short film that showed how robots help doctors in hospitals. I was surprised ___10___ (find) that some machines could even read feelings through people’s voices and expressions. Before we left, our teacher asked us to write down what we had learned. On the way home, I kept thinking about the visit and decided to read more about modern technology in my spare time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。此处泛指“一座科学博物馆”，*science* 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 **a**。

2. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。前因后果：“期待已久，所以大家到得早”，用表示结果的并列连词 **so**。

3. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词与固定结构。*It was the first time for sb. to do sth.* 中序数词 *first* 前用定冠词 **the**。

4. **答案：led**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述上周六活动，与 *welcomed* 并列，谓语用一般过去时；*lead* 的过去式为 **led**。

5. **答案：scientific**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 *exhibition hall*，需用形容词；*science* 的形容词形式为 **scientific**（科学方面的展厅）。

6. **答案：were put**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。*inventions* 与 *put* 为被动（被展出），且时间状语 *last month* 为过去，主语为复数，用一般过去时被动语态 **were put**。*put on show* 意为“展出”。

7. **答案：was built**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。*The museum* 与 *build* 为被动关系，“两年前”为过去时间，用 **was built**。

8. **答案：among / with**
   - **考点**：介词。*popular among teenagers* 表示在青少年群体中受欢迎；*popular with teenagers* 亦正确，中考常考 **among** 或 **with**。

9. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 *schools*，需用形容词；*difference* 的形容词形式为 **different**。

10. **答案：to find**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。*be surprised to do sth.* 为固定搭配，表示“惊讶地发现……”，用不定式 **to find**。
