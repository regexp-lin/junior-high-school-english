# 07-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：塑料污染 (Plastic Pollution)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Plastic bags and bottles are cheap and convenient, ___1___ they are causing serious problems for the environment. Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste ___2___ (throw) into rivers and oceans, and the water near some cities has turned ___3___ (ugly) than before. Many sea animals mistake plastic for food and ___4___ (sad) lose their lives, which makes environmental workers feel helpless. On beaches, volunteers often spend whole weekends picking up bottles and bags left by careless visitors.

Scientists say that plastic ___5___ (take) hundreds of years to break down completely. ___6___ we keep using plastic without thinking, the pollution will become ___7___ (bad) and worse. Some governments are trying to pass stricter laws, and several cities have already stopped ___8___ (give) out free plastic bags in supermarkets. Volunteers also organize activities ___9___ teach people how to sort waste properly. Even small shops are encouraged to charge a little money for a plastic bag so that customers will remember to bring their own.

As students, we should try our best to reduce plastic use in ___10___ daily life. We can also share what we learn with family members and friends. If each of us refuses unnecessary straws and carries a reusable cloth bag when shopping, our planet will become much cleaner in the future, little by little.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前句讲塑料“便宜方便”，后句讲“造成严重环境问题”，语义转折，用 **but**。

2. **答案：are thrown**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。*Every year* 表经常性情况，用一般现在时；垃圾被扔进河湖，主语 *millions of tons of plastic waste* 作整体描述时常用复数概念的被动 **are thrown**。

3. **答案：uglier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 *than before*，用比较级；*ugly* 以“辅音字母 + y”结尾，变比较级为 **uglier**。

4. **答案：sadly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 *lose*，用副词；*sad* 的副词形式为 **sadly**。

5. **答案：takes**
   - **考点**：动词时态和主谓一致。宾语从句陈述客观事实，用一般现在时；主语 *plastic* 不可数，谓语用第三人称单数 **takes**，表示“降解要花费很长时间”。

6. **答案：If**
   - **考点**：连词。引导条件状语从句：“如果我们继续不假思索地使用塑料……”，句首首字母大写，填 **If**。

7. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。*worse and worse* 为固定搭配“越来越糟”，*bad* 的比较级为 **worse**。

8. **答案：giving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。*stop doing sth.* 表示停止正在进行的动作（停止发放免费塑料袋）；*stop to do* 表示停下去做另一件事，此处语境不符，故用 **giving**。

9. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。*activities to teach people...* 中不定式 **to** 表示“用来教人们……的活动”，表目的。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词。修饰 *daily life*，与主语 *we* 一致，用形容词性物主代词 **our**。
