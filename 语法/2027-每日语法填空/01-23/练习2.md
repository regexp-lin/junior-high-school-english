# 01-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国武术 (Chinese Martial Arts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese martial arts, commonly known as kung fu, have a history of several thousand years. They are not only a form of (1) ______ (physics) exercise but also an important part of Chinese cultural heritage. There are hundreds of different styles of kung fu, each with (2) ______ own unique characteristics. Tai chi, for example, is known for its slow, flowing movements, while Wing Chun focuses (3) ______ fast punches and close-range combat. My uncle is a martial arts instructor who (4) ______ (practise) kung fu since he was eight years old. He runs a small training school near our home, where he teaches both children and adults. I started learning from him two years ago, and it has been one of the most (5) ______ (challenge) yet rewarding experiences of my life. Training sessions usually begin with warm-up exercises (6) ______ (follow) by basic stance practice. Standing in a horse stance for ten minutes may sound easy, but it is (7) ______ (actual) exhausting. My legs would tremble, and I often wanted to quit. My uncle always said, "Kung fu is not about (8) ______ (fight). It is about discipline, respect and self-improvement." These words helped me understand that martial arts training goes far (9) ______ physical skills — it shapes your character and teaches you to face challenges with courage. Today, Chinese martial arts (10) ______ (enjoy) by millions of people worldwide, serving as a bridge between Chinese culture and the rest of the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：physical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `exercise` 需要形容词形式。

2. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：物主代词。`its own unique characteristics`，指代 `each` (style)。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`focus on` 为固定搭配。

4. **答案：has practised / has been practising**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`since he was eight years old` 为标志。

5. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物/体验用 `-ing` 形式。

6. **答案：followed**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`warm-up exercises followed by`，被动关系。

7. **答案：actually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `exhausting` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：fighting**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `about` 后接动名词。

9. **答案：beyond**
   - **考点**：介词。`goes far beyond physical skills`，表示"远远超出"。

10. **答案：are enjoyed**
    - **考点**：被动语态。武术"被"全世界数百万人"喜爱"，一般现在时被动。
