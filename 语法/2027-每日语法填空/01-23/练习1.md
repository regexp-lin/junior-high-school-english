# 01-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校食堂改革 (School Canteen Reform)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The school canteen is a place (1) ______ students eat lunch every day, so it plays an important role in their health and well-being. (2) ______ (fortunate), many school canteens in the past offered meals that were high in oil and salt but low in nutrition. Students often complained that the food was tasteless and (3) ______ (attract). Our school decided to change this by launching a canteen reform programme last September. First, the school hired a professional nutritionist to design (4) ______ (health) menus. The new meals include more vegetables, fruits and lean meat, with (5) ______ (little) fried food than before. Second, the canteen was redecorated to create a more pleasant dining environment. Bright colours were painted on the walls, and fresh flowers (6) ______ (place) on each table. The changes made a huge difference. Students began to look forward (7) ______ lunchtime instead of dreading it. The most surprising result was that food waste (8) ______ (decrease) by nearly 40% within the first three months. When students enjoy their meals, they are less likely (9) ______ (throw) food away. Our headmaster said that the reform had been (10) ______ (success) beyond expectations and encouraged other schools to follow our example. I think every student deserves to eat nutritious and delicious food at school.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a place`，在定语从句中作地点状语。

2. **答案：Unfortunately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。句首副词修饰整个句子，表示"不幸的是"。

3. **答案：unattractive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。加否定前缀 `un-`，与 `tasteless` 并列。

4. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：比较级。暗含与以前的菜单相比，设计"更健康的"菜单。

5. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：比较级。`less fried food than before`，`food` 为不可数名词，用 `less`。

6. **答案：were placed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。鲜花"被放置"在桌上，过去时被动。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`look forward to` 为固定搭配，表示"期待"。

8. **答案：decreased / had decreased**
   - **考点**：时态。描述改革后的结果，用一般过去时或过去完成时。

9. **答案：to throw**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be likely to do sth` 为固定搭配。

10. **答案：successful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`had been successful`，系动词后接形容词。
