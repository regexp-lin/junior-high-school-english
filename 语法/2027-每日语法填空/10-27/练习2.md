# 10-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园志愿者服务站 (School Volunteer Service Station)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school set up a volunteer service station at the school gate this September. It __1__ (run) by students from different classes. Every day, two volunteers stand at the station __2__ (help) visitors and new students find their way around. They also collect __3__ (lose) items and return them to their owners. I signed up as __4__ volunteer last month. On my first day, a nervous __5__ (one)-grader came to me and asked __6__ the headmaster's office was. I walked with her and showed her the way. She thanked me with a big smile, __7__ made my day. The station has become very __8__ (use) to everyone. Besides giving directions, volunteers also hand __9__ leaflets about school events. Our head teacher praised us, saying that a school is not just a place for studying but also __10__ place for learning to serve others.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is run**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 It（service station）与 run 为被动关系，描述现在的情况，用一般现在时被动语态 is run。

2. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了帮助"，用 to help。

3. **答案：lost**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作定语）。lost items 丢失的物品，lose 的过去分词 lost 作前置定语。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一名志愿者"，volunteer 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

5. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词→序数词）。first-grader 一年级学生，one 的序数词为 first。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。asked 后接宾语从句，表示"校长办公室在哪里"，从句缺地点状语，用 where。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词为前面整个事件，非限制性定语从句用 which 指代前述内容。

8. **答案：useful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。become useful 变得有用，use 的形容词形式为 useful。

9. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词。hand out 分发，为固定搭配。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一个学习服务他人的地方"，place 以辅音音素开头，用 a。
