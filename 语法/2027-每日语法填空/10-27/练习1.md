# 10-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：养蚕记 (Raising Silkworms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When I was in primary school, our science teacher gave each student several silkworm eggs. I was very __1__ (excite) and put them in a small box at home. After a few days, tiny black worms __2__ (appear) from the eggs. I fed them fresh mulberry __3__ (leaf) every day. Watching them eat was fascinating — they munched so __4__ (noise) that I could hear the sound from across the room! The silkworms grew __5__ (big) and bigger each day. About a month later, they stopped __6__ (eat) and began spinning silk threads. Each worm wrapped __7__ (it) in a white cocoon. My mother told me that silk, one of China's greatest __8__ (invent), comes from these tiny creatures. I was amazed __9__ such small animals could produce something so beautiful. Some of my cocoons later turned __10__ moths, which laid new eggs. The whole cycle was a wonderful lesson about nature.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：excited**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be excited 感到兴奋，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

2. **答案：appeared**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 after a few days 和全文过去时态可知，用一般过去时 appeared。

3. **答案：leaves**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。leaf 的复数为不规则变化 leaves（f→ves）。

4. **答案：noisily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 munched，用副词 noisily。

5. **答案：bigger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。bigger and bigger 越来越大，比较级 + and + 比较级表示"越来越……"。

6. **答案：eating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。stop doing sth. 停止做某事，蚕停止进食，用动名词 eating。

7. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。each worm 为单数第三人称，它把自己包裹起来，用反身代词 itself。

8. **答案：inventions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。one of + 名词复数，invent 的名词形式为 invention，用复数 inventions。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。be amazed that... 惊讶于……，that 引导宾语从句。

10. **答案：into**
    - **考点**：介词。turn into 变成……，为固定搭配。
