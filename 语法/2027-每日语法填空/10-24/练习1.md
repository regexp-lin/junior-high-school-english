# 10-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：联合国日 (United Nations Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

October 24th is United Nations Day. The UN __1__ (set) up in 1945, after the end of World War II. Its main purpose is __2__ (keep) world peace and help countries work together. Today, the UN has 193 member __3__ (country). It works on many global issues, __4__ as poverty, climate change and education. One of its most famous programmes is UNICEF, __5__ helps children around the world get food, medicine and schooling. Last week, our teacher organised a discussion __6__ the role of the UN. We learned that China is one of the five permanent __7__ (member) of the Security Council. My classmate Wang Lei said he wanted to become __8__ diplomat and work at the UN when he grows up. I think it is important __9__ young people to understand international affairs. After all, we live in __10__ (increase) connected world where every country depends on one another.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was set**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 The UN 与 set up 为被动关系，由 in 1945 可知为一般过去时被动语态 was set (up)。

2. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。purpose is to do sth. 目的是做某事，用不定式 to keep 作表语。

3. **答案：countries**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。193 后接可数名词复数，country 变复数为 countries。

4. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。such as 例如，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 UNICEF 指组织（物），非限制性定语从句用 which。

6. **答案：about / on**
   - **考点**：介词。a discussion about/on 关于……的讨论，用 about 或 on。

7. **答案：members**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。five 后接可数名词复数，用 members。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一名外交官"，diplomat 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。It is important for sb. to do sth. 对某人来说做某事很重要，for 引出逻辑主语。

10. **答案：increasingly**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→副词）。修饰形容词 connected，用副词 increasingly（日益地）。
