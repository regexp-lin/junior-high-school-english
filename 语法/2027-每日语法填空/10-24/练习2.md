# 10-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园自然观察 (School Nature Observation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our biology teacher, Miss Chen, started a nature observation project this term. Every Tuesday afternoon, she takes us to the school garden __1__ (observe) plants and insects. Last week, we studied the life cycle of butterflies. Miss Chen gave each group __2__ magnifying glass so we could look at tiny eggs on the leaves. I was amazed __3__ how small and delicate they were. She asked us to draw __4__ we saw in our observation notebooks. My drawing of a caterpillar was not very __5__ (real), but Miss Chen praised my effort. She told us that __6__ (careful) observation is the foundation of science. Some students even __7__ (discover) a rare species of beetle near the pond. Miss Chen was __8__ (extreme) excited and took photos of it. Our __9__ (find) were later shared with the whole school at the science exhibition. I have become much more __10__ (aware) of the natural world around me thanks to this project.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to observe**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了观察"，用 to observe。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。magnifying glass 为可数名词单数，泛指"一个放大镜"，用 a。

3. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。be amazed at 对……感到惊讶，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。draw 后接宾语从句，表示"我们看到的东西"，从句缺宾语，用 what。

5. **答案：realistic**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→形容词）。real 的形容词延伸形式 realistic 表示"逼真的、写实的"，更符合语境。

6. **答案：careful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词→形容词）。修饰名词 observation，用形容词 careful。

7. **答案：discovered**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文为过去时态叙述，用一般过去时 discovered。

8. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 excited，用副词 extremely。

9. **答案：findings**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。find 的名词形式为 finding，此处指多项发现，用复数 findings。

10. **答案：aware**
    - **考点**：形容词用法。become more aware of 变得更加意识到，aware 为形容词，much more 修饰比较级。
