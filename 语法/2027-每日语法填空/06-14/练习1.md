# 06-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市绿化 (Urban Greening)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Green spaces are essential for the health of both people and cities. Trees and parks help clean the air by ___1___ (absorb) harmful gases. They also provide shade on hot summer days, ___2___ (make) outdoor activities more enjoyable. In recent years, many cities in China ___3___ (plant) millions of trees along roads and in public areas. Guangzhou, ___4___ is known as the "Flower City", has been a great example. Walking through Yuexiu Park, you can see tall banyan trees ___5___ have stood there for over a hundred years. Studies show that people who live near green spaces tend to feel ___6___ (happy) and less stressed. Some schools have even started rooftop gardens ___7___ students can grow flowers and vegetables. The government plans ___8___ (create) more pocket parks in crowded neighbourhoods so that everyone can enjoy nature without ___9___ (travel) far. A city with more green is not just ___10___ (beauty) — it is also healthier for all its residents.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：absorbing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。by 为介词，后接动名词，表示"通过吸收"，absorb → absorbing。

2. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作结果状语，provide shade..., making outdoor activities more enjoyable，填 making。

3. **答案：have planted**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in recent years 提示用现在完成时，many cities 为复数主语，填 have planted。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，先行词为 Guangzhou（专有名词），用 which 引导。

5. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 tall banyan trees（物），在从句中作主语，用 that 或 which。

6. **答案：happier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与 less stressed 并列，表示"更快乐且压力更小"，happy → happier。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 rooftop gardens（地点），在从句中作地点状语，用 where。

8. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。plan to do sth. 表示"计划做某事"，填 to create。

9. **答案：travelling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。without 为介词，后接动名词，travel → travelling。

10. **答案：beautiful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。is 后接形容词作表语，beauty → beautiful。
