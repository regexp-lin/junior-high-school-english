# 06-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：独自旅行的成长 (Growing Up Through Solo Travel)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, my parents allowed me ___1___ (take) a train to visit my cousin in Changsha by myself. It was the first time I ___2___ (travel) alone, and I felt both nervous and excited. At the station, I had to figure out which platform to go to ___3___ any help. A kind lady noticed my ___4___ (confuse) expression and showed me the way. On the train, I sat next ___5___ a university student who shared interesting stories about his travels. The five-hour journey passed ___6___ (surprising) quickly. When I ___7___ (final) arrived in Changsha, my cousin was already waiting at the exit. During the week, we visited Orange Island, ___8___ is a famous landmark along the Xiang River. We also tried the local rice noodles, which were much ___9___ (spicy) than what I was used to. This trip taught me that ___10___ (step) out of your comfort zone is the best way to grow.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to take**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。allow sb. to do sth. 表示"允许某人做某事"，填 to take。

2. **答案：had travelled**
   - **考点**：动词时态。It was the first time + 过去完成时，表示"那是我第一次独自旅行"，填 had travelled。

3. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。without any help 表示"没有任何帮助"，填 without。

4. **答案：confused**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 expression（描述人的表情状态），用 confused（感到困惑的），confuse → confused。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。next to 表示"在……旁边"，固定搭配，填 to。

6. **答案：surprisingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰副词 quickly，需用副词形式，surprising → surprisingly。

7. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 arrived，需用副词形式，final → finally。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，先行词为 Orange Island（物/专有名词），用 which 引导。

9. **答案：spicier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级 + than，spicy → spicier。

10. **答案：stepping**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。动名词作主语，表示"走出舒适区这件事"，step → stepping。
