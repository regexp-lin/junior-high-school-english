# 12-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国传统节气 (Chinese Traditional Solar Terms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The 24 solar terms are a traditional Chinese calendar system __1__ was created thousands of years ago. Ancient farmers used them __2__ (guide) their agricultural work throughout the year. Each solar term __3__ (describe) a change in weather, nature or farming activities. For example, "Rain Water" means the weather becomes __4__ (warm) and rainfall increases. "Grain Rain" is the best time __5__ (plant) crops. In 2016, this system __6__ (add) to UNESCO's list of Intangible Cultural Heritage. Today, many Chinese people __7__ (still follow) the solar terms in their daily lives. My grandfather checks the solar terms before deciding when __8__ (harvest) his vegetables. During "Start of Winter", my family always eats dumplings together. It is __9__ tradition that has been passed down for generations. The solar terms connect us __10__ nature and remind us of the wisdom of our ancestors.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 a traditional Chinese calendar system 指物，在从句中作主语，用 that 或 which。

2. **答案：to guide**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。use sth. to do sth. 用……来做……，用不定式 to guide。

3. **答案：describes**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。each solar term 为第三人称单数，描述客观事实用一般现在时 describes。

4. **答案：warmer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。becomes warmer 变得更暖和，暗含与之前相比，用比较级 warmer。

5. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。the best time to do sth. 做某事的最好时机，用不定式 to plant 作后置定语。

6. **答案：was added**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。this system 与 add 为被动关系，in 2016 表过去时间，用一般过去时被动 was added。

7. **答案：still follow**
   - **考点**：动词时态。today 表示现在，many people 为复数主语，用一般现在时 still follow。

8. **答案：to harvest**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。when to harvest 何时收获，"疑问词 + to do"结构作宾语。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。a tradition 一个传统，泛指一种传统习惯，用不定冠词 a。

10. **答案：with / to**
    - **考点**：介词。connect... with / to 将……与……联系起来，with 或 to 均可。
