# 12-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园义卖活动 (School Charity Sale)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school organised a charity sale __1__ (raise) money for children in rural areas. Every class set up a stall in the playground and sold different __2__ (item). Some students brought handmade crafts, __3__ others offered second-hand books and toys. My class sold cookies that we __4__ (bake) the night before. We decorated our stall __5__ colourful balloons and posters. The sale began at 2 p.m. and __6__ (last) for three hours. Many teachers and parents came to support us. Our cookies were so __7__ (taste) that they sold out within the first hour. By the end of the day, the whole school __8__ (collect) over 15,000 yuan. The money __9__ (send) to Project Hope to help build a new library in a village school. I felt __10__ (real) happy that we could make a difference through such a simple activity.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to raise**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。to raise money 为了筹集资金，用不定式表目的。

2. **答案：items**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。different items 不同的物品，different 后接可数名词复数。

3. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。Some... while others... 一些人……而另一些人……，while 表对比。

4. **答案：had baked**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。the night before 表示"在卖之前的那个晚上"，发生在过去的过去，用 had baked。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。decorated... with 用……装饰，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：lasted**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与 began 并列，描述过去事件，用一般过去时 lasted。

7. **答案：tasty**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词/动词→形容词）。so tasty 如此美味，taste 的形容词形式为 tasty。

8. **答案：had collected**
   - **考点**：动词时态。by the end of the day 到那天结束时，表示截至过去某一时刻的结果，用过去完成时 had collected。

9. **答案：was sent / will be sent**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。money 与 send 为被动关系，描述款项被寄出，用 was sent（已寄出）或 will be sent（将被寄出）均可。

10. **答案：really**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 happy，用副词 really。
