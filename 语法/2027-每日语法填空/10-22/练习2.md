# 10-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：河流保护 (River Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Rivers are the __1__ (life) blood of a city. They provide water for drinking, farming and __2__ (industry) use. However, many rivers in China were __3__ (serious) polluted in the past because factories dumped waste water directly __4__ them. Dead fish and rubbish could __5__ (see) floating on the surface. In recent years, the government has taken strong action __6__ (clean) up polluted rivers. Factories are no longer allowed __7__ (pour) untreated water into rivers. Along the banks, trees and flowers __8__ (plant) to prevent soil erosion. Local volunteers also help __9__ picking up litter every weekend. Thanks to these efforts, many rivers are becoming cleaner. Some cities have even built beautiful parks __10__ rivers, where families enjoy walking and cycling. Protecting rivers is protecting our future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：life**
   - **考点**：名词作定语。lifeblood 生命线/命脉，life 作定语修饰 blood，也可写作一个词 lifeblood。

2. **答案：industrial**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 use，用形容词 industrial（工业的）。

3. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰过去分词 polluted，用副词 seriously。

4. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。dump... into... 把……倾倒进……，用介词 into 表示方向。

5. **答案：be seen**
   - **考点**：动词语态。could 后接动词原形，主语 fish and rubbish 与 see 为被动关系，用 be seen。

6. **答案：to clean**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。take action to do sth. 采取行动做某事，用不定式 to clean。

7. **答案：to pour**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be allowed to do sth. 被允许做某事，用不定式 to pour。

8. **答案：have been planted**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 trees and flowers 与 plant 为被动关系，结合语境为现在完成时被动语态 have been planted。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。help by doing sth. 通过做某事来帮助，by 表示方式。

10. **答案：along / beside**
    - **考点**：介词。along/beside rivers 沿着河流/在河边，表示地理位置。
