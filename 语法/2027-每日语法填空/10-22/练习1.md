# 10-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学做陶艺 (Learning Pottery)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, I joined a pottery class at the cultural centre. The teacher, Mr. Liu, has been teaching pottery __1__ over twenty years. He showed us how to shape wet clay __2__ a spinning wheel. It looked easy when he did it, __3__ I soon found it was much __4__ (hard) than I expected. My first bowl turned out __5__ (complete) lopsided. Mr. Liu smiled and said, "Don't worry. Every __6__ (begin) makes mistakes." He guided my hands gently and taught me __7__ (control) the speed of the wheel. On my __8__ (three) try, I finally made a small bowl that looked decent. After shaping, we put the bowls in a special oven __9__ (call) a kiln to harden them. Mr. Liu said we could paint them next week. I can't wait __10__ (see) the finished product. Pottery is truly a beautiful art.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for + 时间段，与现在完成时连用，表示"超过二十年"。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on a spinning wheel 在旋转的转盘上，用 on 表示在……上面。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说看起来简单，后句说发现很难，语义转折，用 but。

4. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，后有 than，hard 的比较级为 harder。

5. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 lopsided，用副词 completely。

6. **答案：beginner**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。every 后接可数名词单数，begin 的名词形式为 beginner（初学者）。

7. **答案：to control**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。teach sb. to do sth. 教某人做某事，用不定式 to control。

8. **答案：third**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词→序数词）。my third try 我的第三次尝试，three 的序数词为 third。

9. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。oven 与 call 为被动关系，用过去分词 called 表示"被称为"。

10. **答案：to see**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。can't wait to do sth. 迫不及待做某事，用不定式 to see。
