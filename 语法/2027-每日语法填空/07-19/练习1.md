# 07-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：和爷爷奶奶一起生活 (Living with Grandparents)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My parents work in ___1___ city far from our hometown, so I have lived with my grandparents ___2___ I was seven. They are both in their late sixties, but they still look after me with endless patience. At first, I missed my parents a lot and sometimes felt lonely at night. However, my grandparents have ___3___ (create) a warm home for me by telling stories, teaching me to cook simple dishes, and listening carefully whenever I worry about school.

Every morning, Grandpa ___4___ (read) the newspaper in his favourite chair while Grandma prepares breakfast in the kitchen. On weekends, we visit the local market together and buy fresh vegetables and fruit. In the evening, we often take ___5___ walk in the park near our community after dinner, chatting about our day and watching children play.

Last month, Grandma ___6___ (fall) ill suddenly and stayed in bed for several days. I tried ___7___ (help) her with housework, washed clothes, and even cooked rice though it was not perfect. My parents came back when they ___8___ (hear) the news and stayed until Grandma could walk around the house again.

Now Grandma is much ___9___ (good), and we all feel thankful ___10___ each other’s love and care. Living with grandparents has taught me what family really means.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。此处表示“一座离家乡很远的城市”，为泛指，且 *city* 以辅音音素开头，故用不定冠词 *a*。若用 *the* 则特指双方已知的那座城市，文中无此语境。

2. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。主句为现在完成时 *have lived*，从句为一般过去时 *was seven*，表示“从七岁起一直到现在”，用 *since* 引导时间状语从句。*for* 后接时间段，不接从句。

3. **答案：created**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。句中有助动词 *have*，谓语需用过去分词构成现在完成时 *have created*，表示过去动作对现在的影响（温暖的家已经形成并持续存在）。

4. **答案：reads**
   - **考点**：一般现在时与主谓一致。由 *Every morning* 及并列句中 *prepares* 可知为习惯性动作；主语 *Grandpa* 为第三人称单数，动词加 *-s*。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词与固定搭配。*take a walk* 为固定短语，意为“散步”，中间必须用不定冠词 *a*。

6. **答案：fell**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。时间状语 *Last month* 表示过去，动词 *fall* 的过去式为不规则变化 *fell*。

7. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。*try to do sth.* 表示“努力去做某事”，符合语境“我努力帮她做家务”；*try doing* 多表示“试着做（试一试）”，此处侧重尽力帮忙，用 *to help* 更贴切。

8. **答案：heard**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。主句 *came back* 为过去时，从句叙述同一过去时间背景下“听到消息”的动作，用一般过去时；动词 *hear* 的过去式为不规则变化 *heard*。*when* 已在文中给出，考查从句谓语形式。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有程度副词 *much*，修饰比较级；*good* 的比较级为 *better*，表示身体“好多了”，与生病时形成对比。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。*feel thankful for sth.* 为固定搭配，表示“因……而感激”，后接名词短语 *each other’s love and care*。
