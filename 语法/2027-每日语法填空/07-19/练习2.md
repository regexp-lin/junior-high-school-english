# 07-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：极端天气与气候变化 (Extreme Weather and Climate Change)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, many countries ___1___ (experience) more extreme weather than before. Heavy storms, sudden floods, and long heatwaves have appeared in places where such events used to be rare. These disasters have caused serious ___2___ (problem) for farmers, whose crops die, and for cities, where roads are blocked and hospitals become busy. Scientists say that human activities such as burning fossil fuels are one of ___3___ main reasons for these changes, though nature also plays a role.

___4___ we cannot stop all natural disasters, we can still take action to slow down harmful habits and protect the environment around us. For example, using public transport instead of private cars ___5___ (reduce) air pollution clearly if more people choose buses or underground trains for daily trips. Some students have also started ___6___ (plant) trees in their communities every spring so that neighbourhoods stay cooler and cleaner in summer.

Last summer, our school held ___7___ interesting talk about climate change and how teenagers can help. The speaker told us that small habits matter in everyday life, such as saving water while brushing teeth and turning off lights ___8___ they are not needed. I think everyone should try ___9___ (hard) to make the earth a ___10___ (safe) place for future generations, because we only have one shared home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have experienced**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。时间状语 *In recent years*（近年来）表示从过去持续到现在的阶段，常与现在完成时连用；主语 *many countries* 为复数，用 *have experienced*。

2. **答案：problems**
   - **考点**：名词复数。*problem* 为可数名词，前有形容词 *serious* 且无冠词限定单数，且暴风雨、热浪带来多种问题，应用复数 *problems*。

3. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。*one of + the + 形容词最高级 + 可数名词复数* 为固定结构，*main* 虽非典型最高级形式，但此处 *the main reasons* 表示“主要原因”，特指人们所讨论的那些原因，用定冠词 *the*。

4. **答案：Although/Though**
   - **考点**：连词。前后为让步关系：“虽然我们无法阻止所有灾害，我们仍可以采取行动”，句首用 *Although* 或 *Though* 引导让步状语从句；注意句首字母大写。

5. **答案：will reduce / can reduce**
   - **考点**：动词时态。*if* 引导条件状语从句用一般现在时 *choose*，主句表示在该条件下将会产生的结果，可用一般将来时 *will reduce*；或用 *can reduce* 表示“能够减少”，均符合中考常见考法。

6. **答案：planting / to plant**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。*start doing sth.* 与 *start to do sth.* 均可表示“开始做某事”，植树为持续性活动，*planting* 很常见；*to plant* 也可接受。

7. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。*interesting* 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，泛指“一场有趣的讲座”用 *an interesting talk*；*hold a talk* 为常见搭配，但当名词前有以元音音素开头的形容词时，不定冠词用 *an*。

8. **答案：when/if**
   - **考点**：连词。表示“在不需要的时候关灯”或“如果不需要就关灯”，用 *when*（当……时）或 *if*（如果）引导时间/条件状语从句均可。

9. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。*try harder* 表示“更加努力”，暗含与现状或过去对比；*hard* 的比较级为 *harder*（*hardly* 意为“几乎不”，不可混淆）。

10. **答案：safer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。不定冠词 *a* 后需形容词修饰 *place*；语境为“为后代创造一个更安全的地方”，隐含与现在对比，用 *safe* 的比较级 *safer*。
