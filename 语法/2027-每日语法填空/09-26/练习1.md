# 09-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：国庆节准备 (National Day Preparation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

National Day is coming, and our city looks busier __1__ more joyful than usual. Last weekend, volunteers __2__ (hang) red flags along the main streets, __3__ shopkeepers cleaned windows carefully. My class __4__ (plan) a short video about hometown changes, and we practiced lines every afternoon. Mom bought __5__ small national badge for me, saying we should show love __6__ our country in daily actions. Dad checked the train tickets early __7__ we could visit grandparents on the holiday. I also promised __8__ (finish) homework ahead so that the trip would be relaxing. __9__ the grand celebration arrives, I feel proud __10__ (live) in such a peaceful and developing nation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。busier 与 more joyful 并列，用 and 连接。

2. **答案：hung**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last weekend 表过去，hang 表示悬挂时过去式为 hung。

3. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词或从属连词。志愿者挂旗与店主擦窗可表同时进行用 while，或简单并列用 and。

4. **答案：planned / was planning**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去准备过程，用一般过去时 planned；若强调当时持续进行可用 was planning。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a small national badge 一枚小徽章。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。show love for our country 表达对祖国的爱。

7. **答案：so / so that**
   - **考点**：从属连词。提前查票以便假期探望祖父母，so 或 so that 引导目的或结果状语从句。

8. **答案：to finish**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。promise to do sth. 承诺做某事。

9. **答案：As / When**
   - **考点**：从属连词。随着/当盛大庆典临近，As 或 When 引导时间状语从句。

10. **答案：to live**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。feel proud to live in... 为生活在……而自豪。
