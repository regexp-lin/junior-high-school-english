# 09-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护母亲河 (Protecting the Mother River)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The "mother river" is __1__ important symbol for many Chinese cities. It feeds fields, carries boats, and __2__ (give) people drinking water after careful treatment. __3__, factories used to pour waste into it, and rubbish floated on the surface. In recent years, the government __4__ (close) some polluting plants and built new sewage pipes. Citizens are also invited __5__ (join) clean-up walks on weekends. Last spring, our team picked up plastic bags __6__ the bank and recorded water color changes. Scientists say the river is getting __7__ (clear) than ten years ago. If we keep guarding __8__ every day, future children __9__ (see) fish swimming freely again. Protecting the mother river means protecting __10__ own home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。important 以元音音素开头，an important symbol 一个重要象征。

2. **答案：gives**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。与 feeds、carries 并列，陈述客观事实用一般现在时，主语 It 为第三人称单数，give 用 gives。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。上文讲河流益处，下文讲过去污染，语义转折。

4. **答案：has closed / closed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。In recent years 常与现在完成时连用，has closed；若强调过去动作也可用 closed。

5. **答案：to join**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。invite sb. to do sth. 邀请某人做某事。

6. **答案：along / on**
   - **考点**：介词。along the bank 沿着河岸；on the bank 在河岸上。

7. **答案：clearer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than ten years ago 提示比较级，clear 的比较级为 clearer。

8. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。指代 the river，用 it 作 guarding 的宾语。

9. **答案：will see / can see**
   - **考点**：动词时态。if 条件句主将从现，主句用 will see；若表可能性用 can see。

10. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。protecting our own home 保护我们自己的家园，形容词性物主代词 our。
