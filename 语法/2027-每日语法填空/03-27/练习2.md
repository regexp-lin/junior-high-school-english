# 03-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：新能源汽车 (New Energy Vehicles)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Air pollution in big cities has pushed scientists and engineers to look for cleaner ways to travel. New energy vehicles (NEVs), (1) ______ include electric cars and plug-in hybrids, are becoming more common on Chinese roads.

My uncle bought an electric car last year. He says charging at home is cheaper (2) ______ filling the tank at a gas station, though long trips still require careful planning. He uses an app (3) ______ (find) charging stations along the highway.

The government has built thousands of public chargers and offered tax cuts to buyers. Some cities allow NEVs (4) ______ (use) bus lanes during rush hour, which saves time for drivers.

Of course, challenges remain. Batteries need rare metals, and electricity is not always "green" (5) ______ it comes from coal plants. Recycling used batteries is another problem that researchers are working to solve.

Car companies are racing to improve battery life and safety. A manager on TV said that in ten years, the price of electric cars might drop (6) ______ (low) than that of traditional cars.

As a student, I cannot afford a car yet, (7) ______ I support the idea of cleaner transport. I ride a bike to school whenever possible and turn off lights when I leave the classroom.

If more families choose NEVs, the air in our city (8) ______ (become) fresher in the long run. Technology alone is not enough — everyone's daily choices matter.

Walking past a row of charging stations near our school, I imagine (9) ______ quiet future street without noisy engines. That day may come sooner (10) ______ we expect.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句关系代词。先行词 `NEVs` 指物，从句缺主语。

2. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。`cheaper than` 比加油更便宜。

3. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`use an app to find` 用应用查找充电桩。

4. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`allow NEVs to use` 允许新能源车使用公交专用道。

5. **答案：if / when**
   - **考点**：连词。`if/when it comes from coal plants` 若/当电力来自煤电时。

6. **答案：lower**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示，`drop lower`。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。买不起车，但支持清洁能源交通。

8. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`If` 从句一般现在时，主句用 will。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`imagine a quiet future street` 想象一条安静的未来街道。

10. **答案：than**
    - **考点**：比较级。`sooner than we expect` 比预期更快。
