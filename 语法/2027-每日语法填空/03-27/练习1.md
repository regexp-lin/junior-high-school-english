# 03-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：孔子与儒家 (Confucius and Confucianism)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Confucius is one of the (1) ______ (famous) thinkers in Chinese history. Born in the state of Lu over two thousand years ago, he spent much of his life traveling from state to state, hoping rulers (2) ______ (accept) his ideas about kindness and order.

He believed that education should be open to students (3) ______ different family backgrounds. "Learning without thinking is labor lost," he once said — a sentence (4) ______ still appears in textbooks today.

Confucianism stresses respect for parents and teachers, honesty in speech, and care for the community. These values have shaped East Asian cultures for centuries. In modern schools, students still read short passages from *The Analects*, trying (5) ______ (understand) their deeper meanings.

Some people argue that certain old rules are no longer suitable. (6) ______, many educators believe we should keep the spirit of Confucius while updating the details. For example, showing respect does not mean giving up independent thinking.

Last week, our history teacher showed us a documentary about Confucius' tomb in Qufu. Standing in front of ancient cypress trees, I felt (7) ______ I had touched a long chain of memory.

If young people learn about Confucius with an open mind, they may find (8) ______ his wisdom is not only about the past, (9) ______ also about how to live well with others today.

The more I read, the more I respect this quiet teacher. His words are not loud, (10) ______ they continue to guide millions of people.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most famous**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级`，`famous` → `most famous`。

2. **答案：would accept**
   - **考点**：过去将来时。主句 `hoped` 为过去时，从句表示“希望统治者会接受”，用 would。

3. **答案：from / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`from different backgrounds` 来自不同家庭背景，`with` 亦可。

4. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `sentence` 指物，从句缺主语。

5. **答案：to understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`try to do sth.` 努力理解。

6. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：转折副词。有人质疑，然而许多教育者认为……，句首大写。

7. **答案：as if / though**
   - **考点**：表语从句。`felt as if I had touched` 仿佛触摸到记忆之链。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句。`find that his wisdom is ...` 发现他的智慧……。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only ... but also ...` 不仅……而且……。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：转折连词。话不响亮，却继续指引人们。
