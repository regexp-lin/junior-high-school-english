# 11-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园棋艺社 (School Chess Club)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese chess, also known __1__ xiangqi, has been played for thousands of years. Our school started a chess club last month, and I was one of the first __2__ (student) to join. The club meets every Wednesday afternoon in the library. Our coach, Mr. Huang, is a retired chess master __3__ has won many national competitions. He teaches us opening strategies and common __4__ (mistake) to avoid. I used to think chess was boring, __5__ after a few lessons, I changed my mind completely. Chess requires deep __6__ (think) and patience. You have to plan several moves ahead and try __7__ (predict) your opponent's strategy. Last week, I played __8__ match against my best friend Wang Jun. The game lasted nearly an hour. Just when I thought I __9__ (win), he made a surprising move and turned the situation around. I lost, but I learned a valuable lesson: never underestimate your opponent. Chess has made me a more __10__ (care) thinker in my daily studies as well.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。be known as 被称为……，为固定搭配。

2. **答案：students**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。one of the first + 名词复数，用 students。

3. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 chess master 指人，从句缺主语，用 who 或 that。

4. **答案：mistakes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。common mistakes 常见错误，用复数 mistakes。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说以为无聊，后句说改变了想法，语义转折，用 but。

6. **答案：thinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语/名词）。deep thinking 深度思考，think 的动名词/名词形式为 thinking。

7. **答案：to predict**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。try to do sth. 努力做某事，用不定式 to predict。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一场比赛"，match 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

9. **答案：was winning**
   - **考点**：动词时态。I thought I was winning 我以为我快要赢了，用过去进行时表示当时正在进行的状态。

10. **答案：careful**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。a more careful thinker 一个更细心的思考者，care 的形容词形式为 careful。
