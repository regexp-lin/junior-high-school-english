# 11-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界糖尿病日与健康生活 (World Diabetes Day and Healthy Living)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

November 14th is World Diabetes Day. Diabetes is __1__ serious disease that affects how your body uses blood sugar. According to recent data, the number of diabetes patients __2__ China has reached over 100 million. Many people do not even know they have it __3__ it develops slowly. Doctors say an unhealthy diet and lack of exercise are two major __4__ (cause) of the disease. Eating too __5__ (many) sugary foods and drinks increases the risk greatly. Our school nurse gave a talk last week about __6__ (prevent) diabetes. She told us to eat more vegetables and whole grains __7__ (instead) of junk food. She also __8__ (recommend) at least 60 minutes of physical activity every day. My father was diagnosed __9__ diabetes two years ago. Since then, he has changed his diet __10__ (complete) and exercises every morning. His health has improved a lot. I think everyone should take their health seriously, starting from a young age.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。serious 以辅音音素开头，泛指"一种严重的疾病"，用 a。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in China 在中国，用 in 表示在某个国家/地区。

3. **答案：because**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导原因状语从句，表示"因为它发展缓慢"，用 because。

4. **答案：causes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。two major causes 两个主要原因，用复数 causes。

5. **答案：many**
   - **考点**：形容词。too many + 可数名词复数，sugary foods and drinks 为可数名词，用 many。

6. **答案：preventing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。about + 动名词，表示"关于预防糖尿病"，用 preventing。

7. **答案：instead**
   - **考点**：副词。instead of 代替、而不是，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：recommended**
   - **考点**：动词时态。由 last week 可知为一般过去时，用 recommended。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be diagnosed with 被诊断患有……，为固定搭配。

10. **答案：completely**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 changed，用副词 completely。
