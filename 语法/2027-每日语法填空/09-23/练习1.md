# 09-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：秋天的果园 (Autumn Orchard)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In late autumn, Uncle Wang's orchard looks __1__ a golden painting. Apples and pears hang __2__ the branches, and the sweet smell fills the air. Last Sunday, my parents __3__ (take) me there to help __4__ the harvest. We wore gloves, climbed ladders carefully, and put ripe fruit __5__ large baskets. Uncle Wang taught me how __6__ (choose) the ones without bruises. __7__ we were tired, we laughed a lot because the work felt meaningful. He gave us __8__ basket of fruit to take home, saying sharing is the best part of farming. On the way back, I thought autumn is not only __9__ season of harvest, __10__ also a season of warmth among families.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：like**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。look like 看起来像，比喻果园如金色画卷。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。水果挂在树枝上，用 on the branches。

3. **答案：took**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last Sunday 表过去，take sb. to... 用 took。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。help with the harvest 帮忙收获。

5. **答案：into / in**
   - **考点**：介词。put fruit into baskets 放进篮子里；in 也可接受。

6. **答案：to choose**
   - **考点**：疑问词 + 不定式。how to choose 作 taught 的宾语。

7. **答案：Although / Though**
   - **考点**：从属连词。尽管累但很开心，表让步。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a basket of fruit 一篮水果。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a season of harvest 一个收获的季节。

10. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定结构。not only ... but also ... 不但……而且……。
