# 09-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：无人驾驶汽车 (Self-driving Cars)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Self-driving cars are no longer just ideas in science fiction. They use cameras, radar, and smart programs __1__ (help) drivers stay safe on busy roads. Many companies have tested vehicles __2__ can brake or change lanes by themselves. __3__ some people worry about computer mistakes, engineers keep __4__ (improve) the systems every year. In several cities, passengers __5__ (ride) robotaxis on short trips already. Rules are still __6__ (discuss) so that these cars can share streets with bicycles and walkers. I think if the technology becomes __7__ (rely), it may reduce accidents caused __8__ human carelessness. __9__, we must never forget that humans should stay responsible __10__ road safety.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。使用设备与程序是为了帮助驾驶安全。

2. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 vehicles，从句 can brake 缺主语，指物用 that/which。

3. **答案：Although / Though / While**
   - **考点**：从属连词。尽管有人担心，工程师仍在改进，表让步。

4. **答案：improving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。keep doing sth. 持续做某事，用 improving。

5. **答案：have ridden / are riding**
   - **考点**：动词时态。already 常与现在完成时连用，have ridden；若强调现阶段试点也可用 are riding。

6. **答案：being discussed / discussed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。规则仍被讨论，用现在进行时的被动 being discussed；或 are still discussed。

7. **答案：reliable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。become 后接形容词，rely 的形容词为 reliable。

8. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。caused by human carelessness 由人类粗心引起。

9. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。上文讲技术益处，下文强调人的责任，转折。

10. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。be responsible for 对……负责。
