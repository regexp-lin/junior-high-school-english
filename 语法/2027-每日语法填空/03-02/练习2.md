# 03-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：未来职业 (Future Career)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

"What do you want to be when you grow up?" This is a question that almost every child (1) ______ (ask) at some point. When I was young, my answer changed frequently — astronaut, doctor, teacher, chef. Now that I am in Grade 9, the question feels much more (2) ______ (seriously). Our school recently organized a Career Day, during which professionals from various fields came to share their experiences with us. A software engineer talked about how technology (3) ______ (shape) the future of work. She said that many jobs that exist today might not exist in twenty years, and new jobs that we cannot even imagine will be (4) ______ (create). An architect showed us (5) ______ (amaze) designs of eco-friendly buildings and explained the importance of (6) ______ (combine) art with science. A doctor shared stories about saving lives and the great (7) ______ (responsible) that comes with the profession. After listening to all the speakers, I realized that choosing a career is not just about finding a job — it is about discovering what you are truly passionate (8) ______. My father always tells me that the happiest people are those (9) ______ do what they love for a living. I am still exploring my interests, but I know that whatever path I choose, I want to make a positive (10) ______ (differ) in the world. The future is full of possibilities, and I am excited to see where it takes me.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is asked**
   - **考点**：一般现在时的被动语态。每个孩子都会被问到。

2. **答案：serious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feels more serious`，系动词后用形容词。

3. **答案：is shaping / will shape**
   - **考点**：现在进行时/一般将来时。技术正在或将要塑造未来。

4. **答案：created**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`will be created` 将被创造。

5. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`amazing designs` 令人惊叹的设计。

6. **答案：combining**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后用动名词。

7. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the great responsibility` 重大的责任，名词。

8. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be passionate about` 对……充满热情。

9. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `those`，指人。

10. **答案：difference**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`make a positive difference` 产生积极的影响，名词。
