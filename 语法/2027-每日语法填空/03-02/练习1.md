# 03-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：志愿者精神 (Spirit of Volunteering)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Volunteering is a wonderful way to give back to the community and develop valuable life skills. In China, the number of registered (1) ______ (volunteer) has grown to over 200 million, reflecting a strong culture of helping others. I first became a volunteer when I was twelve years old. My mother took me to a community centre (2) ______ we helped organize a food drive for families in need. Although it was a simple task, the (3) ______ (grateful) on people's faces when they received the food packages left a deep impression (4) ______ me. Since then, I (5) ______ (participate) in many different volunteer activities. Last summer, I joined a team that helped clean up beaches along the coast. We worked for six hours under the hot sun, (6) ______ (collect) bags of plastic waste that had washed ashore. It was tiring but incredibly (7) ______ (reward). Through volunteering, I have learned skills that cannot be taught in a (8) ______ (class), such as teamwork, communication and problem-solving. More importantly, volunteering has made me a more empathetic person. I now pay closer attention to the (9) ______ (need) of people around me and try to help whenever I can. I strongly encourage every young person to find a cause they care about and volunteer their time. As Mahatma Gandhi once said, "The best way to find (10) ______ (you) is to lose yourself in the service of others."

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：volunteers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`registered volunteers` 注册志愿者。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，修饰 `community centre`，表地点。

3. **答案：gratitude**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the gratitude on people's faces` 人们脸上的感激，名词。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`leave an impression on sb.` 给某人留下印象。

5. **答案：have participated**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 从那时到现在。

6. **答案：collecting**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。收集塑料垃圾。

7. **答案：rewarding**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`incredibly rewarding` 令人非常有收获感的。

8. **答案：classroom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`in a classroom` 在教室里，名词。

9. **答案：needs**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the needs of people` 人们的需求。

10. **答案：yourself**
    - **考点**：反身代词。`find yourself` 发现你自己。
