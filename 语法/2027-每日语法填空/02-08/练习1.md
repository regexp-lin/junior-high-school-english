# 02-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：网络安全 (Internet Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Internet has made our lives more convenient, but it has also brought new risks, (1) ______ (especial) for young people. Every year, thousands of teenagers fall victim (2) ______ online scams or cyberbullying. One common trick used by scammers is to send fake messages pretending to be from banks or (3) ______ (office) organizations. These messages often ask users to click on a link and enter their (4) ______ (person) information. Once the information is given, it can be used to steal money or identities. Cyberbullying is another serious issue. Some students have reported (5) ______ (receive) threatening or hurtful messages from anonymous accounts. The emotional damage caused by cyberbullying can be just as (6) ______ (harm) as physical violence. To stay safe online, there are several rules that everyone (7) ______ follow. First, never share your password with anyone, not even your (8) ______ (close) friends. Second, think carefully before posting anything on social media — once something is online, it is almost (9) ______ (possible) to remove completely. Third, if you encounter any suspicious activity, tell a trusted adult (10) ______ (immediate). Remember, the Internet is a tool, and like any tool, it should be used wisely.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：especially**
   - **考点**：副词。`especially for young people`，特别是对年轻人。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`fall victim to` 成为……的受害者。

3. **答案：official**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `organizations`，用形容词形式。

4. **答案：personal**
   - **考点**：形容词修饰名词。`personal information` 个人信息。

5. **答案：receiving**
   - **考点**：动名词。`report doing sth.` 报告说做了某事。

6. **答案：harmful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`as harmful as`，同级比较中用形容词。

7. **答案：should**
   - **考点**：情态动词。表示建议"应该遵守"。

8. **答案：closest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`your closest friends` 你最亲密的朋友。

9. **答案：impossible**
   - **考点**：词性转换 / 前缀。`almost impossible to remove`，不可能的。

10. **答案：immediately**
    - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`tell someone immediately` 立即告诉。
