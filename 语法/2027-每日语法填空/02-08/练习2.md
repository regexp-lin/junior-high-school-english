# 02-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校运动会 (School Sports Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's annual sports day (1) ______ (hold) last Friday on the track and field ground. The weather was perfect — sunny but not too hot, with a gentle breeze (2) ______ (blow) across the field. Students from all classes gathered early in the morning, wearing their team colours and cheering loudly. I signed up for two events: the 100-metre sprint and the long jump. The sprint was my (3) ______ (strong) event. When the starting gun fired, I ran as (4) ______ (fast) as I could. The crowd was screaming my name, which gave me extra (5) ______ (energetic). I crossed the finish line in first place with a time of 12.3 seconds — a new personal (6) ______ (record)! The long jump, however, did not go so (7) ______ (good). I slipped on the takeoff board and only managed a distance of 3.8 metres. I was disappointed, (8) ______ my coach reminded me that every athlete has bad days. The most memorable moment of the day was the relay race. Our class was in (9) ______ last place until the final runner, Zhang Wei, overtook three competitors in the last 100 metres. The entire class went wild (10) ______ he crossed the finish line first. It was truly an unforgettable day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was held**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。`last Friday` 提示过去时，运动会被举办。

2. **答案：blowing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。`with a breeze blowing` 独立主格结构。

3. **答案：strongest**
   - **考点**：最高级。暗含在所有项目中最强的，用最高级。

4. **答案：fast**
   - **考点**：同级比较。`as fast as I could`，原级。

5. **答案：energy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`extra energy`，形容词后接名词。

6. **答案：record**
   - **考点**：名词。`a new personal record` 新的个人纪录，原型即可。

7. **答案：well**
   - **考点**：副词。`go well` 进展顺利，系动词 `go` 后用副词。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。失望但教练提醒我，表转折。

9. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。`in the last place` 在最后一名，序数词/最高级前用 `the`。

10. **答案：when / as**
    - **考点**：时间连词。当他冲过终点线时。
