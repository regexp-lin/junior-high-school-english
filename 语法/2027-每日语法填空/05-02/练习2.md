# 05-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：国际友谊 (International Friendship)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, ten students from Canada visited our school, and we tried 1. ______ best to make them feel at home. On the first morning, we gave 2. ______ a tour of the library and the lab. Although we spoke different languages, we found 3. ______ easy to communicate with smiles and gestures. My partner Anna was interested 4. ______ Chinese paper cutting, so I taught her 5. ______ (make) a simple window flower. She was surprised that the paper could be cut 6. ______ (beautiful). In the afternoon, we played basketball on the playground. Everyone cheered loudly when Anna scored 7. ______ goal. Before they left, we exchanged small gifts and promised 8. ______ (keep) in touch online. This experience showed me 9. ______ friendship has no borders, and kindness is 10. ______ language that everyone understands.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。**try one's best** 与主语 **we** 一致，用形容词性物主代词 **our**。

2. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词。指代 **ten students**，作 **gave** 的宾语，用宾格 **them**。

3. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。**find it + adj. + to do sth.** 中 **it** 为形式宾语，故填 **it**。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。**be interested in...**，故填 **in**。

5. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**teach sb. to do sth.**，故填 **to make**。

6. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **cut**，用副词 **beautifully**。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**score a goal**，故填 **a**。

8. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**promise to do sth.**，故填 **to keep**。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**showed me** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **language** 单数，表泛指，故填 **a**。
