# 06-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市公共交通 (Public Transport)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Public transport plays a key role in ___1___ (reduce) traffic problems in big cities. Every day, millions of people take buses, subways and trams to get ___2___ work. Compared with driving private cars, public transport is much ___3___ (cheap) and produces less pollution. In Guangzhou, the metro system ___4___ (grow) rapidly over the past decade. New lines ___5___ (build) to connect more areas of the city right now. My family used to drive everywhere, ___6___ now we prefer taking the subway. It is not only faster ___7___ also more environmentally friendly. However, during rush hours, trains can be extremely ___8___ (crowd). The government is working hard to solve this problem ___9___ adding more trains and extending operating hours. I believe that ___10___ more people choose public transport, our city will become a better place to live.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：reducing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。in 为介词，后接动名词，reduce → reducing。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。get to work 表示"去上班"，固定搭配，填 to。

3. **答案：cheaper**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级表示"便宜得多"，cheap → cheaper。

4. **答案：has grown**
   - **考点**：动词时态。over the past decade 提示用现在完成时，the metro system 为单数主语，填 has grown。

5. **答案：are being built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。right now 提示正在进行，lines 与 build 之间为被动关系，用现在进行时的被动语态 are being built。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。"过去开车"与"现在更喜欢坐地铁"之间为转折关系，用 but。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。not only...but also... 表示"不仅……而且……"，固定搭配，填 but。

8. **答案：crowded**
   - **考点**：词性转换。be extremely crowded 表示"极其拥挤的"，crowd → crowded，过去分词作表语。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。by doing sth. 表示"通过做某事"，填 by。

10. **答案：if**
    - **考点**：连词。引导条件状语从句，表示"如果更多人选择公共交通"，填 if。
