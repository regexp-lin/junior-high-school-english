# 06-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：社区志愿服务 (Community Volunteer Work)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our class went to a nursing home to do volunteer work. It was the ___1___ (one) time that many of us had visited such a place. When we arrived, some elderly people ___2___ (sit) in the garden, enjoying the warm sunshine. We were divided ___3___ small groups and given different tasks. My group helped clean the rooms ___4___ another group prepared fruit for the elderly. I talked with an 85-year-old grandmother ___5___ told me interesting stories about her youth. She spoke slowly but very ___6___ (clear). Her eyes lit up when she ___7___ (describe) her first trip to Beijing. I was deeply ___8___ (move) by her words. Before leaving, we promised ___9___ (visit) them again next month. Volunteering is not just about ___10___ (help) others — it also makes us better people.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。It was the first time that... 表示"这是第一次……"，one → first。

2. **答案：were sitting**
   - **考点**：动词时态。when we arrived 表示到达时，老人们正坐在花园里，用过去进行时 were sitting。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。be divided into 表示"被分成"，固定搭配，填 into。

4. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。表示"我的小组打扫房间的同时另一组准备水果"，两个动作同时进行，用 while。

5. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 an 85-year-old grandmother（人），在从句中作主语，用 who 引导定语从句。

6. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 spoke，需用副词形式，clear → clearly。

7. **答案：described**
   - **考点**：动词时态。when 引导的时间状语从句，全文为过去时态，describe → described。

8. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：词性转换。be deeply moved 表示"被深深打动"，move 的过去分词作表语，表示人的感受，填 moved。

9. **答案：to visit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。promise to do sth. 表示"承诺做某事"，填 to visit。

10. **答案：helping**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。about 为介词，后接动名词，help → helping。
