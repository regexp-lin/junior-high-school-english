# 04-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：世界健康日 (World Health Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

World Health Day falls on April 7th each year. It reminds us (1) ______ pay attention to both physical and mental health. Many schools organize special lectures and sports games (2) ______ this day.

In our class meeting, the monitor shared some simple habits that matter. For example, drinking enough water is (3) ______ (easy) said than done when we are busy with homework. We should also go to bed early instead of (4) ______ (stay) up late playing games.

The school doctor explained (5) ______ teenagers need at least eight hours of sleep. She warned that long screen time may lead to headaches (6) ______ poor eyesight. Some students took notes seriously, (7) ______ others promised to change their routines.

After the talk, I decided to ride my bike to school twice a week. Exercise helps me feel (8) ______ (energy) in class. I know good health is not something we can buy; it is built little (9) ______ little through daily choices.

I hope everyone can treat World Health Day (10) ______ a starting point, not just a date on the calendar.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`remind sb. to do sth.` 提醒某人做某事，用 `to`。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。具体某一天前用 `on`。

3. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，`easier said than done` 说起来容易做起来难。

4. **答案：staying**
   - **考点**：动名词。`instead of doing` 代替做某事，用 `staying`。

5. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`explained why ...` 解释为什么青少年需要至少八小时睡眠。

6. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。头痛与视力下降并列。

7. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。一些学生记笔记，而另一些承诺改变，用 `while` 表对比；或 `and` 表并列。

8. **答案：energetic / more energetic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel` 后接形容词，`energy` → `energetic`；或与过去比较用 `more energetic`。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`little by little` 逐渐地。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：介词。`treat ... as ...` 把……当作……。
