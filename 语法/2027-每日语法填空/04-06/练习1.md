# 04-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：观鸟活动 (Birdwatching)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Birdwatching is a quiet hobby that connects people with nature. Instead of staying indoors, you can go to parks or wetlands (1) ______ (look) for different species. All you need is a pair of binoculars, a notebook, and (2) ______ lot of patience.

Last weekend, our biology club organized a birdwatching trip. We arrived at the lake (3) ______ dawn, before the city noise woke up. Our teacher told us (4) ______ (not make) loud sounds, or the birds would fly away.

We stood in the grass and counted (5) ______ many birds we could see. A boy in our group spotted a kingfisher, (6) ______ feathers shone blue in the morning light. Everyone held their breath (7) ______ it dived into the water.

This activity taught me to observe the world (8) ______ (careful). Birds are sensitive to pollution, so protecting their homes means protecting our environment (9) ______ .

I have kept a list of the birds I saw, and next month I hope to see even (10) ______ (many) species in the mountains.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to look**
   - **考点**：动词不定式作目的状语。去公园“为了寻找”不同种类，用 `to look`。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`a lot of` 许多。

3. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。`at dawn` 在黎明时。

4. **答案：not to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词否定。`tell sb. not to do sth.` 告诉某人不要做某事。

5. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。`how many` 多少，引导宾语从句。

6. **答案：whose**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `kingfisher`，`feathers` 与鸟为所属关系，用 `whose`。

7. **答案：when / as**
   - **考点**：从属连词。当它扎入水中时，大家屏住呼吸。

8. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `observe`，`careful` → `carefully`。

9. **答案：too / as well**
   - **考点**：副词。`our environment too` 也保护我们的环境；或 `as well`。

10. **答案：more**
    - **考点**：比较级。`even` 修饰比较级，看到“更多”种类，`many` → `more`。
