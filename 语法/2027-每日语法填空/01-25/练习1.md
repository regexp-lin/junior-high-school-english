# 01-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：体育偶像 (Sports Heroes)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Sports heroes inspire millions of people around the world with their talent, hard work and determination. One of my greatest heroes is Su Bingtian, the Chinese sprinter who made history by becoming the first Asian man (1) ______ (run) the 100 metres in under 9.83 seconds at the Tokyo Olympics. What makes his achievement even more (2) ______ (remark) is that he accomplished it at the age of 31, an age when most sprinters have already retired. Su Bingtian's success did not come (3) ______ (easy). He faced numerous injuries and setbacks throughout his career. At one point, many people suggested that he should give (4) ______ running because of his age. But Su refused to listen to the doubters. Instead, he worked (5) ______ (hard) than ever, adjusting his technique and training methods. His coach once described him (6) ______ the most disciplined athlete he had ever worked with. What I admire most about Su Bingtian is not just his (7) ______ (athlete) ability but his mental strength. He has shown that with enough perseverance, it is possible (8) ______ (break) through barriers that others consider impossible. His story has motivated countless young (9) ______ (China) to pursue their dreams in sport. As he once said in an interview, "Age is just a number. What matters is (10) ______ much effort you are willing to put in."

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to run**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the first...to do sth`，序数词后用不定式作定语。

2. **答案：remarkable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`even more remarkable`，系动词后接形容词。

3. **答案：easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `come` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词。`give up` 为固定短语，表示"放弃"。

5. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`harder than ever`，`than` 为标志。

6. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`describe sb as` 表示"把某人描述为"。

7. **答案：athletic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `ability` 需要形容词形式。

8. **答案：to break**
   - **考点**：不定式。`it is possible to do sth`，形式主语结构。

9. **答案：Chinese**
   - **考点**：名词。`young Chinese` 指"年轻的中国人"，`Chinese` 单复数同形。

10. **答案：how**
    - **考点**：连接副词。`how much effort`，引导表语从句。
