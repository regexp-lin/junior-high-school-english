# 01-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：古镇旅游 (Visiting an Ancient Town)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China is home to many ancient towns that have preserved their traditional charm for centuries. During the National Day holiday, my family visited Zhouzhuang, a water town in Jiangsu Province that (1) ______ (know) as the "Venice of the East." The moment we stepped into the town, it felt as if we (2) ______ (travel) back in time. Narrow stone streets wound between whitewashed houses with dark tiled roofs. Small wooden bridges arched gracefully (3) ______ quiet canals where elderly residents washed vegetables and fishermen cast their nets. We took a boat ride through the town, and our boatman sang traditional folk songs in a deep, (4) ______ (melody) voice. He pointed out several historic (5) ______ (residence) that had been home to wealthy merchants during the Ming and Qing dynasties. One of them had been (6) ______ (care) restored and was now open to visitors. Inside, we admired beautiful wooden furniture, silk embroidery and antique porcelain (7) ______ (display) in glass cases. The local food was another highlight of our trip. We tried soy-braised pork knuckle, a famous Zhouzhuang dish that (8) ______ (prepare) according to a recipe passed down for over 400 years. It was incredibly tender and (9) ______ (flavour). As I walked through the ancient streets that evening, watching lanterns reflect in the still water below, I felt a deep connection (10) ______ the past. Places like Zhouzhuang remind us of the beauty and wisdom of traditional Chinese life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is known**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`is known as`，被称为"东方的威尼斯"。

2. **答案：had travelled**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`as if we had travelled back`，虚拟语气表与过去事实相反。

3. **答案：over**
   - **考点**：介词。`arched over quiet canals`，桥"横跨"运河。

4. **答案：melodious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `voice` 需要形容词形式。

5. **答案：residences**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several historic residences`，多座宅邸。

6. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `restored` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：displayed**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`porcelain displayed in glass cases`，被动关系。

8. **答案：is prepared / was prepared**
   - **考点**：被动语态。菜肴"被制作"，按照传统配方。

9. **答案：flavourful / flavoursome**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `tender` 并列，系动词后接形容词。

10. **答案：to / with**
    - **考点**：介词。`a connection to/with the past`，表示"与过去的联系"。
