# 12-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：陶瓷艺术体验 (Ceramic Art Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Sunday, our art teacher took us to a ceramics studio __1__ (experience) the art of pottery. The studio was located in a quiet street not far __2__ our school. A master potter greeted us warmly and showed us the __3__ (base) techniques of making pottery. He sat at the potter's wheel and __4__ (skill) shaped a lump of clay into a beautiful vase in just a few minutes. We were all amazed __5__ his speed and precision. Then it was our turn to try. I put a piece of clay on the wheel and tried to shape it, __6__ the clay kept falling apart. The potter came over and __7__ (patient) guided my hands. With his help, I __8__ (manage) to make a small bowl. It was not perfect, but I felt very proud of __9__ (I). The potter told us that ceramics is one of China's greatest __10__ (contribute) to world culture. After this experience, I have a much deeper appreciation for this ancient art form.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to experience**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。took us to... to experience 带我们去体验，不定式作目的状语。

2. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。not far from 离……不远，far from 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：basic**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。basic techniques 基础技巧，修饰名词用形容词。

4. **答案：skilfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 shaped，用副词 skilfully。

5. **答案：at/by**
   - **考点**：介词。amazed at/by 对……感到惊讶，at/by 表示原因。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。tried to shape 想塑形，but the clay kept falling apart 但粘土不断散开，语义转折用 but。

7. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 guided，用副词 patiently。

8. **答案：managed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述过去发生的事，用一般过去时 managed。

9. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。proud of myself 为自己感到骄傲，主语和介词宾语指同一人用反身代词。

10. **答案：contributions**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。greatest contributions 最伟大的贡献，contribute 的名词形式为 contribution，此处用复数。
