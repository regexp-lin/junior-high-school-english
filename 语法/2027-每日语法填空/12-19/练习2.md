# 12-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：留守儿童关爱 (Caring for Left-behind Children)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In many rural areas of China, some children grow up without their parents because their parents have gone to big __1__ (city) to work. These children __2__ (call) "left-behind children". They usually live with their grandparents __3__ other relatives. Last summer, our school organised a volunteer trip to a village in Guizhou Province. We spent a week there, __4__ (teach) the children English, art and music. A ten-year-old girl named Xiao Mei told me that she __5__ (not see) her parents for over a year. She missed them very much, __6__ she never complained. Instead, she studied __7__ (hard) than anyone in her class because she wanted to make her parents proud. Her story touched me __8__ (deep). Before we left, we gave the children books, stationery and warm __9__ (cloth) for the winter. Although we could only stay for a short time, I hope our visit brought some __10__ (happy) to their lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：cities**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。big cities 大城市，city 的复数为 cities。

2. **答案：are called**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般现在时被动语态）。children 与 call 为被动关系，描述客观事实用一般现在时被动 are called。

3. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：并列连词。grandparents or other relatives 祖父母或其他亲戚，or 表示"或者"。

4. **答案：teaching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作伴随状语）。teaching the children 教孩子们，与主句主语 we 为主动关系，用现在分词。

5. **答案：had not seen/hadn't seen**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。told me 为过去时，for over a year 表示在那之前持续的状态，用过去完成时 had not seen。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。missed them very much 很想念，but she never complained 但她从不抱怨，语义转折用 but。

7. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。harder than 比……更努力，than 提示用比较级。

8. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 touched，用副词 deeply。

9. **答案：clothes**
   - **考点**：名词。warm clothes 暖和的衣服，cloth 的"衣服"义用复数形式 clothes。

10. **答案：happiness**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。some happiness 一些幸福，some 后接名词，happy 的名词形式为 happiness。
