# 11-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园羽毛球赛 (School Badminton Match)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Badminton is one of the most popular sports __1__ Chinese students. Our school holds a badminton tournament every November. This year, over sixty __2__ (play) signed up for the competition. I partnered __3__ my classmate Liu Yang for the doubles event. We had been __4__ (practise) together after school for three weeks. On the day of the match, we felt both nervous __5__ excited. Our first opponents were strong, but we managed __6__ (win) 21-18 in a close game. The semifinal was even __7__ (tough). We were behind by five points at one stage. Liu Yang kept encouraging me, saying "Don't give __8__!" With great effort, we caught up and __9__ (final) won by just two points. Unfortunately, we lost the final to the top-seeded pair. They were simply __10__ (good) than us. Still, winning the silver medal was a great achievement. I have learned that success comes from both practice and a never-give-up spirit.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：among**
   - **考点**：介词。popular among 在……中受欢迎，among 表示"在……当中"。

2. **答案：players**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。sixty players 六十名选手，play 的名词形式为 player，用复数 players。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。partner with sb. 与某人搭档，用 with。

4. **答案：practising**
   - **考点**：动词时态。had been practising 过去完成进行时，表示比赛前一直在练习。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。both... and... 既……又……，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：to win**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。manage to do sth. 设法做成某事，用不定式 to win。

7. **答案：tougher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。even 修饰比较级，tough 的比较级为 tougher。

8. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词。give up 放弃，为固定搭配。

9. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 won，用副词 finally。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than us，good 的比较级为不规则变化 better。
