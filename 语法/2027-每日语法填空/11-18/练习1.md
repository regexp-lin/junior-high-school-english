# 11-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：海绵城市 (Sponge City)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever wondered why some cities flood __1__ (easy) after heavy rain? The answer lies in too much concrete and too __2__ (little) green space. To solve this problem, China launched the "Sponge City" programme in 2015. The idea is __3__ (design) cities that can absorb rainwater like a sponge. __4__ (tradition) drainage systems simply push water away, but sponge cities store and reuse it. Key features include permeable pavements, rain gardens and rooftop vegetation. These measures allow rainwater __5__ (sink) into the ground naturally. Several cities, __6__ as Wuhan and Shenzhen, have already adopted this approach. In Wuhan, new parks __7__ (build) with special soil that absorbs water quickly. The results have been __8__ (impress) — flooding has decreased by 30 percent in pilot areas. Experts believe that sponge cities are not __9__ good for flood control but also helpful in improving water quality. As urbanisation continues, the sponge city concept will become even __10__ (important) in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 flood，用副词 easily。

2. **答案：little**
   - **考点**：形容词。too little + 不可数名词，green space 为不可数名词，用 little。

3. **答案：to design**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。The idea is to do sth. 想法是做某事，用不定式 to design 作表语。

4. **答案：Traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 drainage systems，tradition 的形容词形式为 traditional，注意首字母大写。

5. **答案：to sink**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。allow sth. to do sth. 允许某物做某事，用不定式 to sink。

6. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。such as 例如，为固定搭配。

7. **答案：have been built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。parks 与 build 为被动关系，already 提示现在完成时，用 have been built。

8. **答案：impressive**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。have been impressive 令人印象深刻，impress 的形容词形式为 impressive。

9. **答案：only**
   - **考点**：副词。not only... but also... 不仅……而且……，not 后接 only。

10. **答案：more important**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。even 修饰比较级，important 为多音节词，比较级用 more important。
