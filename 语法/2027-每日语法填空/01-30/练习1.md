# 01-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：感恩父母 (Being Grateful to Parents)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Parents are the people who love us the most in the world, yet we often take their love for (1) ______ (grant). They work hard every day to provide us with food, shelter and education, but how often do we stop (2) ______ (say) "thank you"? I must admit that I used to be quite ungrateful. I would complain when my mother asked me to do chores, and I rarely (3) ______ (appreciate) the sacrifices my father made to support our family. Everything changed one evening when I accidentally overheard my parents (4) ______ (talk) in the kitchen. My mother was worried about the rising cost of my school fees, and my father said he would take on extra work to make sure I had everything I (5) ______ (need). Hearing their conversation, I felt a wave of (6) ______ (guilty) wash over me. I realised how selfish I had been. From that day on, I decided to show my (7) ______ (grateful) through actions, not just words. I started helping with household chores without being asked. I made an effort to study (8) ______ (hard) so that my parents' investment in my education would not be wasted. On Mother's Day, instead of simply buying a gift, I wrote my mother a long letter expressing all the things I was thankful (9) ______. She cried when she read it and said it was the (10) ______ (good) gift she had ever received. That moment taught me that showing love does not require money — it just requires sincerity.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：granted**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`take...for granted` 表示"认为……理所当然"。

2. **答案：to say**
   - **考点**：不定式。`stop to say` 表示"停下来去说"（表目的）。

3. **答案：appreciated**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的习惯，与 `would complain` 并列。

4. **答案：talking**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`overheard my parents talking`，感知动词后接现在分词。

5. **答案：needed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。间接引语中的时态后移。

6. **答案：guilt**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a wave of guilt`，介词后接名词。

7. **答案：gratitude**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`show my gratitude`，动词后接名词。

8. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`study harder`，暗含与以前相比更努力。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be thankful for` 表示"因……而感激"。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：最高级。`the best gift she had ever received`。
