# 01-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护野生动物 (Protecting Wildlife)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Wildlife conservation is one of the most urgent challenges facing our planet. According to the World Wildlife Fund, more than 10,000 species are currently at risk of (1) ______ (extinct). Habitat destruction, illegal hunting and climate change are the main threats (2) ______ (drive) many animals towards extinction. The giant panda, once one of the most (3) ______ (danger) species in the world, is a success story that gives us hope. Thanks to decades of conservation efforts by the Chinese government and (4) ______ (it) partners, the panda population has grown from about 1,100 in the 1980s to over 1,800 today. Nature reserves (5) ______ (establish) across southwestern China to protect the pandas' bamboo forest habitat. Breeding programmes have also played a key role in (6) ______ (increase) their numbers. However, many other species are not so lucky. The Yangtze River dolphin, also known (7) ______ the baiji, was declared functionally extinct in 2006. Despite years of effort, scientists (8) ______ (fail) to find a single living specimen. The loss of the baiji serves (9) ______ a painful reminder of what happens when we do not act quickly enough. I believe that every individual has a part (10) ______ (play) in wildlife conservation. We can start by learning about endangered species, supporting conservation organisations and making sustainable choices in our daily lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：extinction**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`at risk of extinction`，介词后接名词。

2. **答案：driving**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`the main threats driving many animals`，主动关系。

3. **答案：endangered**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most endangered species`，表示"最濒危的"。

4. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：物主代词。`its partners`，指代 `the Chinese government`。

5. **答案：have been established**
   - **考点**：现在完成时被动。保护区"已经被建立"，强调到目前为止的结果。

6. **答案：increasing**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `in` 后接动名词形式。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`also known as` 表示"也被称为"。

8. **答案：failed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的事实。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`serves as a reminder` 表示"充当提醒"。

10. **答案：to play**
    - **考点**：不定式。`has a part to play`，不定式作后置定语。
