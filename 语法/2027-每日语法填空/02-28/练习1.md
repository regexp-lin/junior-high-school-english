# 02-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：夜市文化 (Night Market Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Night markets are an important part of Chinese food culture, and they have been gaining (1) ______ (popular) in recent years thanks to social media. These lively markets usually open in the evening and offer a wide variety of street food, snacks and drinks at (2) ______ (afford) prices. Last Friday, my friends and I visited a famous night market in the old town area. The market stretched along a narrow street for nearly one kilometre, with (3) ______ (hundred) of stalls on both sides. The air was filled with the (4) ______ (delight) smell of grilled meat, spicy noodles and fresh fruit juices. We tried at least ten different snacks, including lamb skewers, stinky tofu and bubble tea. My favourite was a pancake filled with egg and spring onions that (5) ______ (make) right in front of us. Watching the vendor flip the pancake (6) ______ (skill) was like watching a performance. Besides food, the night market also had stalls (7) ______ (sell) handmade jewellery, phone accessories and toys. I bought a handmade bracelet for my sister, (8) ______ loved it. Some people worry that night markets are too noisy and create litter problems. While these concerns are (9) ______ (understand), I believe the benefits outweigh the drawbacks. Night markets bring communities together and provide small business (10) ______ (own) with valuable income. They are a tradition worth preserving.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：popularity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`gaining popularity` 获得人气，动词后用名词。

2. **答案：affordable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`at affordable prices` 以实惠的价格，形容词修饰名词。

3. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`hundreds of` 数以百计的。

4. **答案：delightful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the delightful smell` 令人愉悦的气味，形容词修饰名词。

5. **答案：was made**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。煎饼被制作。

6. **答案：skilfully**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`flip the pancake skilfully` 熟练地翻转煎饼。

7. **答案：selling**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`stalls selling jewellery` 卖珠宝的摊位。

8. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，修饰 `sister`，指人。

9. **答案：understandable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`these concerns are understandable` 可以理解的。

10. **答案：owners**
    - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`business owners` 商家/业主。
