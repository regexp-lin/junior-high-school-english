# 02-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：毕业感想 (Graduation Reflections)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As our final semester begins, the reality that we are about (1) ______ (leave) middle school is starting to sink in. Three years ago, I walked through the school gates as a shy and uncertain twelve-year-old. Now, I am a much more (2) ______ (confidence) person, thanks to the experiences and friendships I have gained here. Looking back, some of my (3) ______ (fond) memories involve simple moments — laughing with friends during lunch, (4) ______ (struggle) through difficult maths problems together, and cheering for our class at sports day. Of course, there were also challenging times. I remember feeling (5) ______ (complete) overwhelmed during my first set of final exams. I thought I would never survive the pressure. But with the support of my teachers and classmates, I not only survived (6) ______ thrived. Our class teacher, Mr. Liu, has had the (7) ______ (great) influence on me. He always believed in us, even when we did not believe in (8) ______ (we). He taught us that failure is not the opposite of success — it is part of success. As we prepare for the zhongkao and move on to high school, I know (9) ______ the lessons I have learned here will stay with me forever. To my classmates: no matter where life takes us, let us never forget the bond we share. And to our teachers: thank you for (10) ______ (shape) us into the people we are today.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to leave**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be about to do sth.` 即将做某事。

2. **答案：confident**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a more confident person`，形容词修饰名词。

3. **答案：fondest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`my fondest memories` 我最美好的回忆。

4. **答案：struggling**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `laughing` 和 `cheering` 并列。

5. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：副词修饰形容词。`completely overwhelmed` 完全被压垮。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but (also)...`。

7. **答案：greatest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the greatest influence` 最大的影响。

8. **答案：ourselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`we did not believe in ourselves` 我们不相信自己。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。引导宾语从句。`I know that the lessons...`。

10. **答案：shaping**
    - **考点**：动名词。`thank you for doing sth.`，介词后用动名词。
