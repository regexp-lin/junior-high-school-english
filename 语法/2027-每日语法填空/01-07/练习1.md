# 01-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：环保从我做起 (Protecting the Environment)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Environmental protection is one of the most pressing issues of our time. Every year, millions of tonnes of plastic waste (1) ______ (dump) into the oceans, causing serious harm to marine life. Forests are being cut down at an (2) ______ (alarm) rate, and air pollution continues to threaten the health of people in many cities. As students, we may feel that there is little we can do about these huge problems. (3) ______, small actions can make a big difference when everyone does their part. For instance, we can start (4) ______ reducing our use of single-use plastics. Instead of buying bottled water, we can carry (5) ______ (reuse) water bottles. We can also save energy by (6) ______ (turn) off lights and electronic devices when they are not in use. Another simple but (7) ______ (effect) way to help the environment is to plant trees. Last spring, our class (8) ______ (participate) in a tree-planting activity organised by the local government. We planted over two hundred trees in a park near our school. Our teacher told us that a single tree can absorb about 22 kilograms of carbon dioxide per year, (9) ______ means that our small effort would have a lasting impact. I believe that if everyone takes responsibility (10) ______ protecting our planet, we can create a cleaner and greener world for future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is dumped / are dumped**
   - **考点**：被动语态。废物"被倾倒"，`Every year` 表示经常性动作，用一般现在时被动。

2. **答案：alarming**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `rate`，用 `-ing` 形式的形容词，表示"令人担忧的"。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。前后语义转折，表示"然而"。

4. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`start by doing sth`，表示"从做某事开始"。

5. **答案：reusable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `water bottles` 需要形容词形式。

6. **答案：turning**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词形式。

7. **答案：effective**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `way` 需要形容词形式。

8. **答案：participated**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last spring` 为过去时标志。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，`which` 指代前面整个句子。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。`take responsibility for` 为固定搭配，表示"对……承担责任"。
