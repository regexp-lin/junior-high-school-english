# 01-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：一次难忘的旅行 (An Unforgettable Trip)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last October, my family went on a trip to Guilin, a city in southern China that is famous (1) ______ its stunning natural scenery. We took the high-speed train from Guangzhou, and the journey (2) ______ (last) about three hours. As soon as we arrived, we were amazed by the breathtaking landscape — tall limestone mountains (3) ______ (rise) from the flat ground like pillars reaching towards the sky. On our first day, we took a boat ride along the Li River. The water was so clear (4) ______ we could see fish (5) ______ (swim) beneath the surface. Along the way, we passed small villages where farmers were working in their rice fields. Our guide explained that many of the rock (6) ______ (formation) along the river had been given poetic names by ancient Chinese scholars. The next day, we visited the famous Reed Flute Cave, (7) ______ is known for its amazing stalactites and stalagmites. Colourful lights had been (8) ______ (install) inside the cave to highlight the natural beauty of the rock formations. The whole experience felt like walking through a fairy-tale world. The trip to Guilin was one of the most (9) ______ (memory) experiences of my life. It reminded me how lucky we are to live in a country with such (10) ______ (diversity) and beautiful landscapes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be famous for` 为固定搭配，表示"因……而著名"。

2. **答案：lasted**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的旅程，`lasted` 表示"持续了"。

3. **答案：rising**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`mountains rising from the flat ground`，主动关系。

4. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so clear that...` 结构。

5. **答案：swimming**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`see fish swimming`，表示看到鱼正在游。

6. **答案：formations**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`rock formations`，指多个岩石构造。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `Reed Flute Cave`。

8. **答案：installed**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`had been installed`，过去完成时被动，灯已被安装。

9. **答案：memorable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most memorable experiences`，`memory` → `memorable`。

10. **答案：diverse**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `landscapes` 需要形容词形式，`diversity` → `diverse`。
