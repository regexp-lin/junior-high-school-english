# 03-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：露营初体验 (First Camping Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I will never forget my first camping trip. It happened last October when my father suggested that we (1) ______ (go) camping in the mountains. I was so excited that I could (2) ______ (hard) sleep the night before.

We drove for two hours to reach the campsite. After (3) ______ (arrive), we started to set up the tent. It was more difficult (4) ______ I had expected, but with Dad's help, we managed to finish it in half an hour. Then we collected some dry wood and made a campfire. Sitting around the fire, we cooked (5) ______ (we) dinner — simple but delicious noodles.

As night fell, the sky became (6) ______ (extreme) clear. I looked up and saw thousands of stars shining above us. It was the (7) ______ (beautiful) night sky I had ever seen. Dad pointed out several constellations and told me stories about (8) ______ (they).

In the middle of the night, I (9) ______ (wake) up by a strange sound outside the tent. I was a little scared, but Dad told me it was just the wind blowing through the trees. I felt safe and quickly went back to sleep.

The next morning, we took a walk (10) ______ the lake before packing up. That camping trip taught me to appreciate nature and spend quality time with my family.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：go / should go**
   - **考点**：虚拟语气 / 宾语从句。`suggest that sb. (should) do sth.` 建议某人做某事。

2. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`could hardly sleep` 几乎睡不着，`hardly` 表否定。

3. **答案：arriving**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `After` 后接动名词。

4. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较级连接词。`more difficult than` 比……更困难。

5. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 `dinner`，用 `our`。

6. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `clear`，用副词 `extremely`。

7. **答案：most beautiful**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the most beautiful ... I had ever seen` 我见过的最美的。

8. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。介词 `about` 后用宾格 `them`。

9. **答案：was woken**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。"我"是被声音吵醒的，被动。

10. **答案：by / around / beside**
    - **考点**：介词。`took a walk by/around/beside the lake` 在湖边散步。
