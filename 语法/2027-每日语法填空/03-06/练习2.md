# 03-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校合唱团 (School Choir)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Joining the school choir was one of the best (1) ______ (decide) I have ever made. When I was in Grade 7, my music teacher noticed that I had a good voice and encouraged me (2) ______ (join) the choir.

At first, I was nervous because I had never sung in front of so many people before. The choir had over forty (3) ______ (member), and we practised together every Wednesday afternoon. Our conductor, Mr. Wang, was very strict (4) ______ us. He always said, "Singing is not just about your voice; it is about teamwork and (5) ______ (express) emotions together."

After three months of hard practice, we (6) ______ (give) the chance to perform at the city-level singing competition. I still remember how (7) ______ (nerve) I felt when I stood on the big stage for the first time. But as soon as the music started, all my fear (8) ______ (appear). We sang with all our hearts, and the audience gave us warm applause.

We didn't win the first prize, (9) ______ we came in second place. More importantly, I learned that working together as a team can create something truly wonderful. Now I practise singing every day, and I hope (10) ______ (become) a professional singer in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：decisions**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`one of the best decisions` 最好的决定之一，`decide` → `decision`，复数。

2. **答案：to join**
   - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb. to do sth.` 鼓励某人做某事。

3. **答案：members**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`over forty members` 超过四十名成员。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be strict with sb.` 对某人严格。

5. **答案：expressing**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `teamwork` 并列，作 `about` 的宾语。

6. **答案：were given**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。"我们"是"被给予机会的"。

7. **答案：nervous**
   - **考点**：形容词。`how nervous I felt` 感叹句结构，`felt` 后接形容词。

8. **答案：disappeared**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`all my fear disappeared` 恐惧消失了，注意词义和时态。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。没赢第一名，但获得了第二名，表转折。

10. **答案：to become**
    - **考点**：不定式。`hope to do sth.` 希望做某事。
