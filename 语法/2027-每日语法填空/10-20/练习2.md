# 10-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学骑自行车 (Learning to Ride a Bicycle)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I still remember the day when I learned __1__ (ride) a bicycle. I was seven years old and my father took me to the park __2__ a Sunday morning. He held the back of the seat __3__ I pedalled forward slowly. "Don't look down. Keep your eyes on the road __4__ (ahead)!" he shouted. Several times I lost __5__ (I) balance and fell off. My knees were scratched and __6__ (pain), but I refused to give up. After about an hour of practice, my father __7__ (quiet) let go of the seat without telling me. I rode forward for nearly fifty __8__ (metre) before I realised I was riding alone! I was __9__ surprised that I almost forgot to stop. That day I learned an important __10__ (life) lesson: if you keep trying, you will succeed.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to ride**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。learn to do sth. 学习做某事，用不定式 to ride。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on a Sunday morning 在某个星期日的早晨，具体某天的早/午/晚用 on。

3. **答案：while / as**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导时间状语从句，表示"当我往前蹬时"，用 while 或 as。

4. **答案：ahead**
   - **考点**：副词。ahead 作后置定语修饰 road，the road ahead 前方的路。若给提示词则无需变形。

5. **答案：my**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 balance，用形容词性物主代词 my。

6. **答案：painful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。与 scratched 并列作表语，用形容词 painful 表示"疼痛的"。

7. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 let go，用副词 quietly。

8. **答案：metres**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。fifty 后接可数名词复数，用 metres。

9. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：副词（结果状语从句）。so + 形容词 + that... 如此……以至于……，用 so。

10. **答案：life**
    - **考点**：名词作定语。life lesson 人生课程，名词 life 修饰 lesson。
