# 10-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：航天科技 (Space Technology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has made great __1__ (achieve) in space technology over the past decades. In 2003, Yang Liwei became the first Chinese astronaut __2__ (travel) into space. Since then, China __3__ (send) many astronauts to space stations. The Chang'e programme, __4__ is named after a moon goddess, has successfully landed spacecraft on the moon. Scientists have even collected __5__ (sample) from the lunar surface and brought them back to Earth. These achievements would not have been possible __6__ the hard work of thousands of engineers. My dream is __7__ (become) an aerospace engineer one day. Last month, I watched a documentary __8__ the Shenzhou missions. It was __9__ (true) inspiring to see how the astronauts trained. Space exploration not only advances science __10__ also encourages young people to dream big.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：achievements**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。great achievements 伟大的成就，achieve 的名词形式为 achievement，此处用复数 achievements。

2. **答案：to travel**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。the first + 名词 + to do sth.，序数词修饰的名词后用不定式作后置定语，用 to travel。

3. **答案：has sent**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since then 与现在完成时连用，主语 China 为第三人称单数，用 has sent。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 The Chang'e programme 指物，非限制性定语从句中用 which（不可用 that）。

5. **答案：samples**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。collect 后接可数名词，此处指多个样本，用复数 samples。

6. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。would not have been possible without 没有……就不可能，表示虚拟含义。

7. **答案：to become**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。My dream is to do sth. 我的梦想是做某事，用不定式 to become 作表语。

8. **答案：about / on**
   - **考点**：介词。a documentary about/on 关于……的纪录片，用 about 或 on。

9. **答案：truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 inspiring，用副词 truly。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。not only... but also... 不仅……而且……，为固定搭配。
