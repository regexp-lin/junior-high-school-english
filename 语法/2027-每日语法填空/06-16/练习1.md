# 06-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：二手书交换 (Second-Hand Book Exchange)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever thought about what happens to the books you no ___1___ read? Instead of throwing them away, why not exchange them with others? Our community library ___2___ (start) a book exchange programme last March. People can bring old books and take home new ones for free. The rules are simple: the books must be ___3___ good condition, and each person can exchange up ___4___ five books at a time. Since the programme began, more than three thousand books ___5___ (exchange). My favourite find was a science fiction novel ___6___ (write) by a Chinese author. I ___7___ (hear) of him before, but the story turned ___8___ to be fantastic. The programme ___9___ (real) encourages people to read more while reducing waste. I think every neighbourhood should have ___10___ (it) own book exchange corner.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：副词。no longer 表示"不再"，固定搭配，填 longer。

2. **答案：started**
   - **考点**：动词时态。last March 提示用一般过去时，start → started。

3. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in good condition 表示"状况良好"，固定搭配，填 in。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。up to 表示"多达"，固定搭配，填 to。

5. **答案：have been exchanged**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。since the programme began 提示用现在完成时，books 与 exchange 之间为被动关系，填 have been exchanged。

6. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，a novel written by... 表示"由……写的小说"，write → written。

7. **答案：hadn't heard**
   - **考点**：动词时态。主句 turned out 为过去时，"之前没听说过他"发生在更早之前，用过去完成时的否定形式 hadn't heard。

8. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。turn out to be 表示"结果证明是"，固定搭配，填 out。

9. **答案：really**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 encourages，需用副词形式，real → really。

10. **答案：its**
    - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 own book exchange corner，用形容词性物主代词 its（注意不是 it's）。
