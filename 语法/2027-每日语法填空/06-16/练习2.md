# 06-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：过敏与自我保护 (Allergies and Self-Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Allergies are much more common ___1___ most people think. An allergy happens when the body's immune system reacts to something that is ___2___ (usual) harmless, such as pollen, dust or certain foods. In China, the number of people ___3___ (suffer) from allergies has been rising steadily. Common symptoms include sneezing, itchy eyes and skin ___4___ (rash). In serious cases, allergies can even be life-___5___ (threaten). My classmate Xiao Ming is allergic ___6___ peanuts. He has to check food labels ___7___ (careful) before eating anything. His parents taught him ___8___ to do if he accidentally eats something containing peanuts. He always carries medicine with ___9___ just in case. Doctors suggest that people with allergies should visit a specialist regularly ___10___ (make) sure their condition is under control.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。more common than 构成比较级结构，表示"比大多数人认为的更常见"，填 than。

2. **答案：usually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 harmless，需用副词形式，usual → usually。

3. **答案：suffering**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作后置定语，people suffering from allergies 表示"患过敏症的人"，填 suffering。

4. **答案：rashes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。skin rashes 表示"皮疹"，与前面 sneezing、itchy eyes 并列，rash → rashes。

5. **答案：threatening**
   - **考点**：词性转换。life-threatening 为复合形容词，表示"危及生命的"，threaten → threatening。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。be allergic to 表示"对……过敏"，固定搭配，填 to。

7. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 check，需用副词形式，careful → carefully。

8. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连词。what to do 表示"该做什么"，teach sb. what to do，what 引导宾语从句，填 what。

9. **答案：him**
   - **考点**：代词。with him 表示"随身携带"，介词后接宾格代词，填 him。

10. **答案：to make**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"以确保病情受控"，填 to make。
