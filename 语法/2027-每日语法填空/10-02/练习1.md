# 10-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：参观故宫 (Visiting the Forbidden City)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our school organized __1__ trip to the Palace Museum, also known as the Forbidden City. __2__ we arrived early, there were already long lines at the entrance. Our guide, __3__ voice was clear and lively, explained how emperors lived and worked hundreds of years ago. I was amazed __4__ the huge golden roofs and red palace walls. While walking __5__ the long corridor, I took photos __6__ (careful) so that I would not disturb other visitors. Some halls are so famous __7__ they appear in many history books. My friend Lily said she __8__ (read) about the museum online before, but seeing it in person felt more real. Before leaving, we bought postcards __9__ (send) to our pen pals abroad. This visit helped me understand __10__ ancient Chinese culture is still alive in modern life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。trip 可数名词单数，泛指“一次旅行”，用 a trip。

2. **答案：Although / Though**
   - **考点**：从属连词。“虽然我们到得早，但门口已排长队”，让步状语从句用 Although 或 Though。

3. **答案：whose**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 guide，从句 voice 与 guide 为所属关系，“导游的声音”，用 whose。

4. **答案：at / by**
   - **考点**：介词。be amazed at/by... 对……感到惊叹，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：through / along**
   - **考点**：介词。沿着长廊走，用 along the corridor；从空间穿过也可用 through。

6. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 took，用副词 carefully。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：固定句型。so... that... 如此……以至于……，引导结果状语从句。

8. **答案：had read**
   - **考点**：动词时态。主句 said 为过去时，“在那之前已经读过”，过去的过去，用过去完成时 had read。

9. **答案：to send**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。买明信片目的是寄给笔友，用不定式作目的状语 to send。

10. **答案：why / how**
    - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。understand 后接从句，表示“为何/如何古代文化仍在现代生活中延续”，用 why 或 how。
