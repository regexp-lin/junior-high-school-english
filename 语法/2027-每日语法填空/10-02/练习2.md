# 10-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：数字阅读时代 (Digital Reading Era)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Today, more teenagers read on phones and e-readers __1__ on paper books. Digital libraries are open 24 hours a day, __2__ students can borrow e-books without leaving home. My brother enjoys listening to audiobooks __3__ he is doing chores. __4__, some teachers worry that screen time may hurt our eyes if we are not careful. Last month, our class __5__ (hold) a debate on whether paper reading is better than digital reading. I argued that both forms are useful __6__ we choose wisely. For example, I still print important articles __7__ (read) them with a pen in hand. Apps can also record new words __8__ (automatic), which saves time. In the end, we agreed __9__ (develop) a healthy reading habit matters more than the tool itself. I believe reading, __10__ it happens online or offline, will always open new worlds for us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：并列比较结构。more... than... 比……更多，与前面 more teenagers read on phones 构成比较。

2. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前后为两个分句，“数字图书馆全天开放，因此学生可在家借电子书”，表结果用 so。

3. **答案：while / when / as**
   - **考点**：从属连词。表示“在做家务时听有声书”，时间状语从句用 while/when/as。

4. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。上文讲便利，本句讲担忧，表转折，However 后常用逗号。

5. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last month 表示过去，谓语用一般过去时 held。

6. **答案：if / when**
   - **考点**：从属连词。“只要我们明智选择，两种形式都有用”，条件用 if；若理解为“当我们明智选择时”也可用 when。

7. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。print articles to read... 不定式表目的。

8. **答案：automatically**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 record，用副词 automatically。

9. **答案：developing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。agreed that 后接宾语从句，从句主语表示“养成健康阅读习惯”这件事，用动名词 developing 作主语。
