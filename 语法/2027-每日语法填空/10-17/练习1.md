# 10-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：社区图书馆改造 (Community Library Renovation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The old community library near my home __1__ (rebuild) last summer. Before the renovation, it was small and dark, with only a few hundred books on the __2__ (shelf). Now it has become a bright, modern space __3__ people of all ages love to visit. The new library has three floors. The ground floor is __4__ (design) for children, with colourful walls and soft reading mats. The second floor offers a quiet area __5__ adults can read newspapers and magazines. The top floor has a computer room __6__ several meeting rooms. My favourite part is the rooftop garden __7__ you can read under the open sky. Since the library reopened, the number of visitors __8__ (grow) by 200 percent. I go there __9__ (two) a week to borrow books. It is amazing how a library can bring __10__ whole community together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was rebuilt**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 library 与 rebuild 为被动关系，由 last summer 可知为一般过去时被动语态 was rebuilt。

2. **答案：shelves**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。shelf 的复数形式为不规则变化 shelves（f→ves）。

3. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 space 指物，从句缺宾语（love to visit 的宾语），用 that 或 which。

4. **答案：designed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。be designed for 为……而设计，用过去分词 designed。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 area 为地点名词，从句不缺主宾语，用 where 引导。

6. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。a computer room 与 several meeting rooms 并列，用 and 连接。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 rooftop garden 为地点，从句不缺主宾语，用 where。

8. **答案：has grown**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since the library reopened 表示"自从……以来"，主句用现在完成时，主语 the number 为单数，用 has grown。

9. **答案：twice**
   - **考点**：词性转换（数词→频率副词）。twice a week 每周两次，two 的副词形式为 twice。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。a whole community 整个社区，whole 前用不定冠词 a。
