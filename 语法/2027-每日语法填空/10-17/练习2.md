# 10-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学做面包 (Learning to Bake Bread)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, my mother taught me how __1__ (bake) bread at home. First, we mixed flour, sugar, yeast and warm water in __2__ large bowl. She told me __3__ (stir) the mixture slowly until it became smooth dough. Then we covered it with a cloth and waited __4__ it to rise. After about an hour, the dough grew to __5__ (two) its original size. I was very surprised! Next, we shaped the dough __6__ small round balls and placed them on a baking tray. My mother put them in the oven, __7__ had been heated to 180 degrees. The kitchen was soon filled __8__ a wonderful smell. Twenty minutes __9__ (late), the bread was ready. It tasted even __10__ (good) than the bread from the bakery. I felt so proud of myself!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to bake**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。how to do sth. 如何做某事，用不定式 to bake。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。bowl 为可数名词单数，泛指"一个大碗"，用 a。

3. **答案：to stir**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。tell sb. to do sth. 告诉某人做某事，用不定式 to stir。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。wait for sb./sth. to do 等待……做某事，用介词 for。

5. **答案：twice**
   - **考点**：词性转换（数词→倍数副词）。twice its original size 原来大小的两倍，two 的倍数表达为 twice。

6. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。shape... into... 把……塑造成……，用介词 into。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 the oven 指物，非限制性定语从句中用 which（不可用 that）。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be filled with 充满……，为固定搭配。

9. **答案：later**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→比较级/副词）。时间 + later 表示"……之后"，用 later。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than，good 的比较级为不规则变化 better。
