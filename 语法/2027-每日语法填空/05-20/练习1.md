# 05-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：图书漂流 (Book Drifting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Book drifting is ___1___ activity that allows readers to share books freely instead of keeping them on ___2___ shelf forever and forgetting the joy of stories. In our reading corner, each book carries ___3___ note with the last reader’s thoughts and the date, so the next owner feels connected to strangers. When I picked up ___4___ novel about space travel, I was surprised ___5___ (find) encouraging words from a stranger. Students are asked ___6___ (write) short reviews and pass the books to someone else within two weeks. This practice not only saves money ___7___ also builds trust among classmates. Since the program started, more than three hundred books ___8___ (travel) from hand to hand. I believe book drifting makes reading ___9___ (social) and turns the library into ___10___ warmer community space.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。activity 可数，且以元音音素开头，表示“一项活动”，用不定冠词 an。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。泛指“一个书架上”，shelf 可数，用不定冠词 a。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。note 可数，表示“一张便条”，用不定冠词 a。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。novel 可数，表示“一本关于太空旅行的小说”，用不定冠词 a。

5. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。形容词 surprised 后接不定式作原因或结果状语，be surprised to do sth.，用 to find。

6. **答案：to write**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be asked to do sth. 表示“被要求做某事”，用不定式 to write。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。固定结构 not only... but also...，表示“不仅……而且……”，用 but。

8. **答案：have traveled / have travelled**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Since the program started 为现在完成时标志，主语 books 为复数，用 have traveled（英式 travelled 亦可）。

9. **答案：social**
   - **考点**：词性转换。make sth. + 形容词作宾语补足语，表示“使阅读具有社交性”，用形容词 social。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。space 可数，表示“一个更温暖的社区空间”，用不定冠词 a。
