# 05-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：海洋垃圾清理 (Ocean Cleanup)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Plastic waste floating in the ocean harms fish, birds, and even humans ___1___ eat seafood from polluted areas. ___2___ spring, volunteers in our city join beach cleanups ___3___ collect bottles, bags, and broken toys. Last Saturday, my team ___4___ (fill) ten large bags in two hours, and we were tired ___5___ proud. A guide explained ___6___ tiny pieces are the hardest to remove because they look like food. To solve the problem, we should reduce plastic use ___7___ home and support recycling programs. Scientists are also testing new nets ___8___ (design) to catch waste without hurting animals. If governments, schools, and families work together, the ocean ___9___ (become) cleaner step by step. Everyone agrees ___10___ clean water is a treasure we must protect.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰先行词 humans，在从句中作主语，指人，用 who 或 that。

2. **答案：In**
   - **考点**：介词。表示“在春天”，用 In spring，句首大写 In。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。不定式作目的状语，join cleanups to collect... 表示“参加清理以收集……”，用 to。

4. **答案：filled**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 Last Saturday 表示过去，谓语动词用一般过去时 filled。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。tired 与 proud 之间为转折关系，用 but。

6. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。引导宾语从句，作 explained 的宾语，从句结构完整，用 that。

7. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。固定短语 at home，表示“在家中”，用 at。

8. **答案：designed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。nets 与 design 之间为被动关系，用过去分词 designed 作后置定语，表示“被设计用来……的网”。

9. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。If 引导条件状语从句，主句用一般将来时 will become。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：连词。引导宾语从句，作 agrees 的宾语，从句结构完整，用 that。
