# 12-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校心理健康讲座 (School Mental Health Lecture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Tuesday, a psychologist __1__ (invite) to our school to give a talk about mental health. She told us that it is completely __2__ (normally) to feel stressed or anxious sometimes, especially before exams. __3__, if the bad feelings last too long, we should ask for help. She shared several __4__ (way) to deal with stress. First, we can talk to someone we trust, __5__ as a parent, a teacher or a close friend. Second, doing exercise __6__ (be) a good way to relax because it helps our body produce happy chemicals. Third, we should make sure we get __7__ (enough) sleep every night. The talk lasted for about an hour, and all the students listened __8__ (careful). After the talk, many of my classmates said they felt much __9__ (good) about handling pressure. I also learned that taking care of __10__ (we) mental health is just as important as keeping our body healthy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was invited**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般过去时被动语态）。Last Tuesday 上周二，a psychologist 与 invite 之间为被动关系，用 was invited。

2. **答案：normal**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词→形容词）。it is normal to feel 感到……是正常的，is 后接形容词作表语，去掉 -ly。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：转折副词。前面说"有时感到有压力是正常的"，后面说"如果持续太久应该求助"，语义转折用 However。

4. **答案：ways**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。several ways 几种方法，several 后接复数名词。

5. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。such as 例如、比如，用于举例。

6. **答案：is**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。doing exercise 做运动，动名词短语作主语，谓语用第三人称单数 is。

7. **答案：enough**
   - **考点**：形容词。enough sleep 足够的睡眠，enough 修饰名词时放在名词前。

8. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 listened，用副词 carefully。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。felt much better 感觉好多了，good 的比较级为 better，much 修饰比较级。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。our mental health 我们的心理健康，修饰名词用形容词性物主代词 our。
