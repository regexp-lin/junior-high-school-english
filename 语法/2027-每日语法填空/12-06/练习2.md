# 12-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国剪纸艺术 (Chinese Paper-cutting Art)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese paper-cutting is one of the __1__ (old) folk arts in China. It has a history of over 1,500 years. People usually use red paper __2__ (create) beautiful designs such as flowers, animals and Chinese characters. During the Spring Festival, paper-cuts __3__ (put) on windows and doors to bring good luck. My grandmother is very skilled __4__ paper-cutting. She can turn a piece of paper __5__ a lively butterfly in just a few minutes. Last summer, she taught __6__ (I) how to make simple paper-cuts. I found it much __7__ (difficult) than I had expected. My hands were not steady enough, and I __8__ (accident) cut the paper in the wrong places several times. But my grandmother was very __9__ (patience) with me. She said that practice makes perfect. Now I can make some basic designs by __10__ (I). I am proud that this beautiful art form is part of our culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the oldest 最古老的……之一，one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词。

2. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。use red paper to create 用红纸来创作，to create 作目的状语。

3. **答案：are put**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般现在时被动语态）。paper-cuts 与 put 之间为被动关系，描述习惯性动作用一般现在时被动 are put。

4. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。be skilled at 擅长……，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。turn... into 把……变成……，into 表示转变。

6. **答案：me**
   - **考点**：代词（宾格）。taught me 教我，动词后接人称代词宾格。

7. **答案：more difficult**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。more difficult than 比……更难，多音节形容词用 more + 原级构成比较级。

8. **答案：accidentally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 cut，用副词 accidentally。

9. **答案：patient**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。be patient with 对……有耐心，be 后接形容词作表语。

10. **答案：myself**
    - **考点**：反身代词。by myself 独自地、靠自己，by oneself 为固定搭配。
