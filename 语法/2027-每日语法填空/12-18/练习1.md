# 12-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国载人航天 (China's Manned Space Programme)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's manned space programme has made remarkable __1__ (achieve) over the past two decades. In 2003, Yang Liwei became the first Chinese astronaut __2__ (travel) into space. Since then, China __3__ (send) many more astronauts into orbit. The Tiangong space station, __4__ was completed in 2022, is now home to Chinese astronauts who conduct scientific experiments in space. Living in space is very different __5__ living on Earth. Astronauts have to get used to __6__ (float) in zero gravity. Even simple tasks like eating and sleeping become __7__ (challenge). They must also exercise for at least two hours every day to keep their __8__ (body) healthy. Despite the difficulties, Chinese astronauts work __9__ (hard) to carry out their missions. Their courage and dedication have inspired millions of young people, __10__ (include) me, to dream of exploring the universe one day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：achievements**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。remarkable achievements 卓越的成就，achieve 的名词形式为 achievement，此处用复数表示多项成就。

2. **答案：to travel**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作定语）。the first Chinese astronaut to travel 第一个进入太空的中国宇航员，序数词修饰名词后用不定式作定语。

3. **答案：has sent**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Since then 从那以后，与现在完成时连用，用 has sent。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。which 引导非限制性定语从句，指代 The Tiangong space station，在从句中作主语。

5. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。be different from 与……不同，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：floating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。get used to floating 习惯漂浮，to 为介词，后接动名词。

7. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。become challenging 变得具有挑战性，become 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。

8. **答案：bodies**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。their bodies 他们的身体，body 的复数为 bodies。

9. **答案：hard**
   - **考点**：副词。work hard 努力工作，hard 本身可作副词，表示"努力地"。

10. **答案：including**
    - **考点**：介词。including me 包括我在内，including 用作介词，表示"包括"。
