# 12-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园手账文化 (School Bullet Journal Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Bullet journaling has become a popular hobby __1__ middle school students in China. A bullet journal is a notebook __2__ you write down your plans, goals, thoughts and daily experiences. What makes it special is that you can decorate each page __3__ (creative) with drawings, stickers and colourful pens. My friend Li Mei started __4__ (keep) a bullet journal at the beginning of this school year. She uses it to plan her study schedule and track her __5__ (day) habits. She has also created special pages for her __6__ (read) list and travel wishes. I was so impressed __7__ her beautiful journal that I decided to start my own. At first, I found it difficult to make my pages look as nice __8__ hers. But she told me that the journal does not need to be perfect. The most important thing is __9__ (write) down your thoughts honestly. Now I write in my journal every night before bed. It __10__ (help) me reflect on my day and sleep better.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：among**
   - **考点**：介词。among middle school students 在中学生中间，among 表示"在……之中"。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系副词）。a notebook where 一个在其中……的笔记本，where 引导定语从句修饰 notebook，在从句中作地点状语。

3. **答案：creatively**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 decorate，用副词 creatively。

4. **答案：keeping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。started keeping 开始记录，start 后接动名词表示"开始做某事"。

5. **答案：daily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。daily habits 日常习惯，修饰名词用形容词 daily。

6. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作定语）。reading list 阅读清单，动名词修饰名词表示用途。

7. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。be impressed by 被……打动，by 表示原因。

8. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：比较结构。as nice as 和……一样好看，as...as 表示"和……一样"。

9. **答案：to write**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作表语）。The most important thing is to write 最重要的事情是写下，不定式作表语说明主语的内容。

10. **答案：helps**
    - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。It helps 它帮助，描述现在的习惯用一般现在时，it 为第三人称单数。
