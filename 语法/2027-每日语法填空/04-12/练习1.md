# 04-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：快递驿站 (Express Delivery Station)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Near our neighborhood gate, a small express delivery station opened last year. Residents pick up parcels there (1) ______ of waiting for couriers at home all day.

When I arrived yesterday afternoon, several people (2) ______ (stand) in line. The worker scanned codes quickly and handed out packages (3) ______ a smile. If a box is too heavy, customers can borrow (4) ______ simple cart for free.

My mother often shops online, so our family receives three (5) ______ four packages a week. Sometimes I help her carry boxes upstairs. Once, we received a wrong item, and the return process took longer (6) ______ we expected.

The station also collects used boxes for recycling. I think this idea is (7) ______ (environment) friendly because it reduces waste. Many young volunteers help sort materials on weekends.

Express services have changed daily life, (8) ______ they also remind us to protect personal information on labels. Before throwing a box away, I always tear off my name and phone number.

I hope more communities (9) ______ (build) similar stations so that life becomes easier for elderly people who live alone.

In a busy city, a tiny room near the gate can mean (10) ______ lot to busy families.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：instead**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`instead of` 代替/而不是，后文应有 `of`——但一空一词。看原句：`pick up parcels there (1) ______ of waiting` — 应为 **instead** of，`instead of` 为固定短语，空处填 **instead**。

2. **答案：were standing**
   - **考点**：过去进行时。当我到达时，几个人正排队。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`with a smile` 带着微笑。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`borrow a simple cart` 借一辆简易手推车。

5. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`three or four` 三四个。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`longer than` 比……更长。

7. **答案：environmentally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `friendly`，`environment` → `environmentally`，`environmentally friendly` 环保的。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。快递改变了生活，但也提醒保护个人信息。

9. **答案：will build / can build**
   - **考点**：动词时态。希望更多社区将/能够建立类似驿站。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`mean a lot` 意义重大。
