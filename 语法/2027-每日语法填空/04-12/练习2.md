# 04-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：广场舞 (Square Dancing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every evening, lively music fills the small square in front of our community center. Groups of neighbors gather there (1) ______ (dance) together, and their steps follow the same rhythm.

My grandmother is one of the regular dancers. She says square dancing keeps her (2) ______ (health) than sitting at home watching TV. At first, she felt shy, (3) ______ now she leads the front row when a new song starts.

Not everyone enjoys the noise. Some young parents have complained that the speakers are (4) ______ (loud) than necessary. After several meetings, dancers agreed to lower the volume (5) ______ nine o'clock at night.

I think communication solved the problem (6) ______ than anger. When both sides listen, a community becomes (7) ______ (harmony). Last week, I even joined them for ten minutes and laughed a lot.

Square dancing is more (8) ______ a hobby; it is a way for older people to make friends. If you pass by, remember (9) ______ (give) them a friendly smile instead of judging them.

Music and movement can connect generations (10) ______ we are willing to understand each other.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to dance**
   - **考点**：动词不定式作目的状语。聚集“为了”跳舞。

2. **答案：healthier / more healthy**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，`keep sb. healthy` 的比较级 `healthier`。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。起初害羞，但现在领舞，转折。

4. **答案：louder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than necessary` 提示比较级。

5. **答案：after / before**
   - **考点**：介词。`after nine o'clock` 九点后调低音量；或 `before` 九点前。语境为降噪协议，常用 **after** nine 降低音量。

6. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than anger` 提示比较级，`better than`。

7. **答案：harmonious / more harmonious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`becomes` 后接形容词，`harmony` → `harmonious`。

8. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`more than` 不仅仅。

9. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`remember to do` 记得去做。

10. **答案：if / when**
    - **考点**：从属连词。如果我们愿意互相理解，音乐与运动能连接代际。
