# 03-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：游泳课 (Swimming Lessons)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Swimming is an important life skill that every child should learn. (1) ______ (sad), many students in China do not know how to swim. To change this, our school decided to add swimming to the PE curriculum this year.

The swimming pool is located behind the gym. It was (2) ______ (new) built and has six lanes. Our PE teacher, Coach Li, is a former professional (3) ______ (swim) who once competed at the national level. He is strict but very patient with beginners.

On the first day of class, most of us were afraid of (4) ______ (get) into the deep water. Coach Li told us not (5) ______ (worry) and started by teaching us how to float. He held each student's back and slowly let go until we could float (6) ______ our own.

After four weeks of training, I (7) ______ (be) able to swim across the pool without stopping. The feeling of gliding through the water is (8) ______ (true) amazing. My classmate Xiao Hong, who used to be scared of water, can now swim even (9) ______ (fast) than me!

Swimming has not only improved my physical (10) ______ (fit) but also boosted my confidence. I look forward to every swimming class now.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：Sadly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`Sadly` 副词修饰整个句子，表"令人遗憾的是"。

2. **答案：newly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `built`，`new` → `newly` 副词。

3. **答案：swimmer**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a professional swimmer` 职业游泳运动员，`swim` → `swimmer`。

4. **答案：getting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`be afraid of doing sth.` 害怕做某事。

5. **答案：to worry**
   - **考点**：不定式。`tell sb. not to do sth.` 告诉某人不要做某事。

6. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`on our own` 独自地，靠自己。

7. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`After four weeks of training` 过去时间背景，`be able to` 能够。

8. **答案：truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `amazing`，`true` → `truly` 副词。

9. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：比较级。`even faster than me` 甚至比我更快，`even` 修饰比较级。

10. **答案：fitness**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`physical fitness` 身体健康，`fit` → `fitness` 名词。
