# 03-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：二手书交换 (Second-hand Book Exchange)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Reading is one of my favourite hobbies. Over the years, I (1) ______ (collect) hundreds of books. However, my bookshelf was getting too full, and I didn't have room for any more. That's when I heard about the second-hand book exchange programme in our neighbourhood.

The programme is simple: you bring books you have already read and exchange them (2) ______ books brought by others. There is no need to pay anything — it is completely free. The exchange takes place at the community centre (3) ______ the first Saturday of every month.

Last weekend, I brought fifteen books and went to the exchange event. I was (4) ______ (surprise) to see so many people there. There were at least two hundred books (5) ______ (spread) out on long tables. I spent about an hour (6) ______ (look) through them and finally picked five that interested me the most.

One of the books I found was a novel by Mo Yan. I had been wanting to read this book for a long time but could never find it in the (7) ______ (near) bookshop. I was so happy that I almost jumped up!

The book exchange is a (8) ______ (wonder) way to give old books a new life. It not only saves money (9) ______ also reduces waste. I will (10) ______ (definite) go again next month.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have collected**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Over the years` 表示从过去到现在这些年。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`exchange A for B` 用 A 交换 B。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on the first Saturday` 在第一个星期六，具体某天用 `on`。

4. **答案：surprised**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。`be surprised to do sth.` 惊讶地……，修饰人的感受。

5. **答案：spread**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`books spread out on long tables` 铺展在长桌上的书，`spread` 过去分词与原形同形。

6. **答案：looking**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time (in) doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

7. **答案：nearest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the nearest bookshop` 最近的书店。

8. **答案：wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `way`，`wonder` → `wonderful` 形容词。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only ... but also ...` 不仅……而且……。

10. **答案：definitely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `go`，`definite` → `definitely` 副词。
