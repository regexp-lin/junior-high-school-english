# 12-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统染布技艺 (Traditional Tie-dye Craft)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tie-dye is a __1__ (tradition) craft that has been practised in China for over a thousand years. It is especially famous in Dali, Yunnan Province. During our school trip last October, we visited a small workshop __2__ local artisans demonstrated the tie-dye process. First, they folded white cloth and tied it __3__ (tight) with string. Then they dipped the cloth __4__ natural plant dye. After __5__ (wait) for several hours, they untied the string and the cloth showed __6__ (beauty) blue and white patterns. Each piece was unique because no two people tie the cloth in exactly the same way. I tried __7__ (make) my own tie-dye handkerchief. The artisan helped me choose the right pattern and guided me through every step. When I __8__ (see) the final result, I could not believe my eyes — it was __9__ (much) more amazing than I had imagined. This experience gave me a deeper __10__ (understand) of traditional Chinese crafts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。traditional craft 传统工艺，修饰名词用形容词。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系副词）。a workshop where 一个……的工坊，where 引导定语从句修饰 workshop，在从句中作地点状语。

3. **答案：tightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 tied，用副词 tightly。

4. **答案：into/in**
   - **考点**：介词。dip... into/in 把……浸入……，表示将布放入染料中。

5. **答案：waiting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。after waiting 等待之后，after 为介词，后接动名词。

6. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。beautiful patterns 美丽的图案，修饰名词用形容词。

7. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾语）。try to make 尝试制作，try to do sth. 努力去做某事。

8. **答案：saw**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述过去的体验，用一般过去时 saw。

9. **答案：much**
   - **考点**：副词修饰比较级。much more amazing 精彩得多，much 修饰比较级加强程度。

10. **答案：understanding**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a deeper understanding 更深入的理解，a 和 deeper 提示需要名词形式。
