# 12-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园天文观测 (School Astronomy Observation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday evening, our school's science club organised __1__ astronomy observation activity on the rooftop of the teaching building. About forty students gathered there __2__ (watch) the stars. Our science teacher, Mr. Wang, brought two __3__ (profession) telescopes for us to use. He pointed out several bright stars and __4__ (patient) explained how to find different constellations. I was amazed to see Saturn's rings __5__ my own eyes for the first time. It looked much __6__ (beautiful) than in any textbook picture. Wang Lei, who __7__ (be) interested in astronomy since he was five, could name almost every star in the sky. He told us that the light from some stars __8__ (take) thousands of years to reach the Earth. That fact made me feel how small we are __9__ the universe. The activity lasted until ten o'clock, and none of us wanted __10__ (leave). I hope our school will hold more events like this in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。an astronomy observation activity 一次天文观测活动，astronomy 以元音音素开头，用不定冠词 an。

2. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。gathered there to watch 聚集在那里观看，不定式作目的状语。

3. **答案：professional**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。professional telescopes 专业望远镜，修饰名词用形容词。

4. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 explained，用副词 patiently。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with my own eyes 用我自己的眼睛，with 表示"用"。

6. **答案：more beautiful**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。more beautiful than 比……更美丽，多音节形容词用 more + 原级构成比较级。

7. **答案：has been**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since he was five 自他五岁起，与现在完成时连用，用 has been。

8. **答案：takes**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述客观事实用一般现在时，the light 为第三人称单数，用 takes。

9. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in the universe 在宇宙中，in 表示"在……里面"。

10. **答案：to leave**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾语）。want to leave 想要离开，want 后接不定式。
