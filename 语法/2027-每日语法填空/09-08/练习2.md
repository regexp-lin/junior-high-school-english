# 09-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国园林 (Chinese Gardens)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Sunday, I visited ___1___ famous Chinese garden in Suzhou with my parents. As soon as we entered, we were attracted ___2___ the peaceful sound of water running over stones.

The guide told us that these gardens ___3___ (build) hundreds of years ago. Designers tried ___4___ (create) a small world inside the walls ___5___ visitors could enjoy nature without leaving the city.

We walked along winding paths and saw bridges, pavilions, ___6___ fish swimming in clear ponds. Every corner looked ___7___ (difference) from the one before, ___8___ made us stop to take pictures.

My father, ___9___ is interested in history, listened carefully to stories about ancient poets. I think Chinese gardens teach people ___10___ (slow) down and find balance in a busy life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。**garden** 为可数名词单数，泛指“一座著名的中国园林”，**famous** 以辅音音素开头，故填 **a**。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。**be attracted by...**（被……吸引）为被动结构中的动作发出者标记，故填 **by**。

3. **答案：were built**
   - **考点**：动词时态和语态。主语 **gardens** 与 **build** 为被动关系；时间状语 **hundreds of years ago** 表示过去，用一般过去时的被动语态 **were built**。

4. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**try to do sth.**（努力/试图做某事），故填 **to create**。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 **walls** 所围成的空间/园林环境，定语从句中缺地点状语，故用 **where**（= in which）。

6. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。**bridges, pavilions** 与 **fish swimming...** 为并列宾语，列举所见景物，故填 **and**。

7. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。**look** 为系动词，后接形容词；**difference** 的形容词形式为 **different**，**look different from...**（看起来与……不同）。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句话“每个角落都不同”，在从句中作主语，故填 **which**。

9. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 **My father**，指人，非限制性定语从句中作主语，故填 **who**。

10. **答案：to slow**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**teach sb. to do sth.**（教某人做某事），故填 **to slow**，**slow down** 为“放慢节奏”。
