# 08-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：摄影日记 (Photo Diary)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Photography has been my hobby ___1___ I was ten years old. My father gave me a small digital camera as a birthday present, and I have been taking photos ever since. Now I keep ___2___ photo diary, recording the interesting moments in my daily life.

Every morning on my way to school, I look for things that others might not notice: a dewdrop ___3___ (hang) on a leaf, a cat sleeping on a wall, or the way sunlight falls ___4___ the old brick buildings. I believe there is beauty everywhere if you look ___5___ (close) enough.

Last month, I entered a photography contest ___6___ (organise) by the city's Youth Centre. I submitted a photo of an old couple ___7___ (walk) hand in hand through a park covered in autumn leaves. The judges said the photo ___8___ (successful) captured a feeling of warmth and love. I was very excited when I heard that I won ___9___ (two) prize.

My dream is ___10___ (become) a professional photographer one day. I want to travel around the world and share the stories behind my pictures with people everywhere.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。**has been my hobby since...**（自从……以来一直是我的爱好），`since` 引导时间状语从句，与现在完成时搭配。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`photo diary`（摄影日记）为可数名词单数，此处泛指"一本摄影日记"，`photo` 以辅音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **a**。

3. **答案：hanging**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`a dewdrop` 与 `hang` 之间为主动关系（露珠"悬挂着"），用现在分词 **hanging** 作后置定语。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。**fall on...**（照射在……上面），表示阳光落在建筑物上，故填 **on**。

5. **答案：closely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `look` 需用副词，**close** 的副词形式 **closely** 表示"仔细地"。注意 close 也可作副词（表物理距离近），但此处指"仔细观察"，用 **closely**。

6. **答案：organised**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a photography contest` 与 `organise` 之间为被动关系（比赛"被组织"），用过去分词 **organised** 作后置定语。

7. **答案：walking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`an old couple` 与 `walk` 之间为主动关系（老夫妇"正在走"），用现在分词 **walking** 作后置定语，描述照片中的动态画面。

8. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `captured` 需用副词，**successful** 的副词形式为 **successfully**。

9. **答案：second**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词 → 序数词）。`won ___ prize`（获得……奖），`two` 的序数词形式为 **second**，即"二等奖"。

10. **答案：to become**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**My dream is to do sth.**（我的梦想是做某事），不定式 **to become** 作表语，说明梦想的具体内容。
