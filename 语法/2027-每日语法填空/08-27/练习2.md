# 08-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：低碳生活 (Low-carbon Living)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As global warming becomes ___1___ increasingly serious issue, more people are starting to pay attention to low-carbon living. A low-carbon lifestyle means ___2___ (reduce) the amount of carbon dioxide we produce in our daily life.

There are many practical ways to live a low-carbon life. One of the ___3___ (easy) ways is to save electricity at home. We should turn off lights and electrical appliances when they are not ___4___ use. Another effective method is to choose public transport or ride a bicycle instead of ___5___ (drive) a car. Walking short distances is not only good for the environment ___6___ also beneficial to our health.

At school, our class has started a "Green Week" campaign. We encourage everyone ___7___ (use) both sides of paper and to bring reusable water bottles. Last week alone, we ___8___ (collect) over 50 kilograms of recyclable waste, which was sent to a recycling centre.

Some people think that one person's efforts are too small to make a ___9___ (differ). However, if each of the eight billion people on Earth makes even a tiny change, the result ___10___ (be) amazing. Low-carbon living starts with small steps, and every step counts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`issue` 为可数名词单数，`increasingly` 以元音音素开头，表示"一个日益严重的问题"需用不定冠词 **an**。

2. **答案：reducing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**means doing sth.**（意味着做某事），`mean` 作"意味着"讲时后接动名词，故填 **reducing**。

3. **答案：easiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词**（最……的之一），`easy` 的最高级为 **easiest**。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。**in use**（在使用中）为固定搭配，`not in use` 表示"不在使用中"，故填 **in**。

5. **答案：driving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**instead of doing sth.**（而不是做某事），介词 `of` 后接动名词，故填 **driving**。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。**not only... but also...**（不仅……而且……）为固定结构，故填 **but**。

7. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**encourage sb. to do sth.**（鼓励某人做某事），故填 **to use**。

8. **答案：collected**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`Last week alone`（仅上周）提示一般过去时，`collect` 的过去式为 **collected**。

9. **答案：difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 名词）。**make a difference**（产生影响/有所不同）为固定搭配，`differ` 的名词形式为 **difference**。

10. **答案：will be**
    - **考点**：动词时态。主句为一般将来时，表示"结果将会是惊人的"，主语 `the result` 为第三人称单数，故填 **will be**。条件状语从句 `if... makes...` 中用一般现在时，主句用一般将来时。
