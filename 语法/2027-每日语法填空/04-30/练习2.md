# 04-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：太阳能发电 (Solar Power)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Solar power is playing a bigger role in fighting climate change. Large panels are often placed on factory roofs or in open fields (1) ______ they can receive plenty of sunlight. The energy they produce (2) ______ (convert) into electricity and sent to nearby communities.

Last month, our science class visited a solar farm on a hillside. An engineer explained how the system (3) ______ (work) on cloudy days. "Even when the sky looks gray, the panels still create power, (4) ______ the amount is smaller," he said.

I used to believe renewable energy was too expensive for ordinary families, (5) ______ prices have dropped in recent years. Some schools in our province (6) ______ (install) solar lights in classrooms to save money and protect the environment.

Students discussed what else we could do at home, such (7) ______ turning off unused lights. I hope I can study physics (8) ______ (hard) in order to understand clean energy better. If young people care about the planet, the future (9) ______ (look) brighter than we expect.

Walking back to the bus, I looked at the blue sky and felt proud that technology and nature could work together (10) ______ harmony.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `roofs or... fields`，从句中缺地点状语，“在能接收充足阳光的地方”，用 `where`。

2. **答案：is converted**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。能量“被转化为”电力，陈述客观过程，主语 `The energy` 为单数，用 `is converted`。

3. **答案：works**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。宾语从句说明系统如何在阴天运转，陈述客观情况，主语 `the system` 为单数，用 `works`。

4. **答案：but/yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“仍能发电”与“数量较少”为转折，用 `but` 或 `yet`。

5. **答案：but/yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾以为太贵”与“近年价格下降”为转折。

6. **答案：have installed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`in recent years` 与“已安装”呼应，主语 `schools` 复数，用 `have installed`。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`such as` 例如，引出例子。

8. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`in order to understand better` 暗示更努力，`hard` → `harder`。

9. **答案：will look**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`If` 引导条件状语从句，主句叙述将来结果，“未来看起来会更明亮”，用 `will look`。

10. **答案：in**
    - **考点**：介词。`in harmony` 和谐地，固定搭配。
