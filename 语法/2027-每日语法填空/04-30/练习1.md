# 04-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：美食文化节 (Food Culture Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's food culture festival turned the gym into a lively market last Friday. Each class set up a booth (1) ______ students sold homemade snacks from different regions. The air (2) ______ (mix) with the smell of dumplings, spicy noodles, and sweet rice balls.

I helped prepare a dish (3) ______ my grandmother had taught me. While we were cooking, visitors lined up (4) ______ (patient) even though the wait was long. A judge walked around (5) ______ tasted every dish before choosing the winners.

The headmaster said the festival was more (6) ______ (education) than a normal lesson because it connected culture with daily life. I used to think cooking was only adults' work, (7) ______ now I understand teamwork in the kitchen.

After the event, we shared leftover food (8) ______ cleaners and security guards to express our thanks. I hope similar festivals (9) ______ (hold) every year. This experience made me love my hometown flavors (10) ______ (deep) than before.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `booth`，从句中缺地点状语，“在摊位上学生售卖……”，用 `where`。

2. **答案：was mixed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。空气“与香味混合”，`be mixed with`，叙述过去活动，用 `was mixed`。

3. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `dish`，从句缺宾语，指物，用 `that` 或 `which`。

4. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `lined up`，`patient` → `patiently` 副词。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`walked around` 与 `tasted` 为并列谓语，表顺承。

6. **答案：educational**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more` 后接形容词，`education` → `educational` 有教育意义的。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾以为只是大人的事”与“现在理解厨房里的合作”为转折。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`share sth. with sb.` 与某人分享某物。

9. **答案：will be held**
   - **考点**：一般将来时被动语态。`hope` 后宾语从句，“希望类似节日每年被举办”，用 `will be held`。

10. **答案：more deeply**
    - **考点**：副词比较级。句中有 `than`，修饰动词 `love`，`deep` → `deeply` 的比较级 `more deeply`。
