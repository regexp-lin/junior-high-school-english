# 03-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国象棋 (Chinese Chess)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese chess, or Xiangqi, is one of the most popular board games in China. It (1) ______ (play) by millions of people every day, from parks in big cities to quiet villages.

The game is played on a board (2) ______ nine lines wide and ten lines long. Each player controls sixteen pieces, including generals, soldiers, and cannons. Unlike Western chess, which (3) ______ (develop) in Europe, Xiangqi has its own rules and symbols that show Chinese military ideas from ancient times.

My grandfather taught me how (4) ______ (move) the pieces when I was eight. At first, I lost every game, (5) ______ I never gave up. Slowly, I learned to think several steps ahead and to protect (6) ______ important pieces.

Last month, our school organized a Xiangqi competition. I was nervous (7) ______ I faced stronger players, but my teacher encouraged me to stay calm. To my surprise, I reached the final and won the second prize.

Playing Xiangqi is not only about winning. It trains our memory and patience. Many parents believe (8) ______ the game helps teenagers build logical thinking. Some cities even (9) ______ (hold) classes for beginners during winter holidays.

If you are interested in Chinese culture, why not try this ancient game? You may find (10) ______ both challenging and fun.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is played**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。象棋“被”很多人下，陈述事实用一般现在时。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`a board with ...` 带有……规格的棋盘，说明伴随特征。

3. **答案：was developed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。国际象棋在欧洲“被发展出来”，定语从句中叙述历史事实。

4. **答案：to move**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`how to do sth.` 疑问词 + 不定式作宾语。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。总是输却不放弃，表转折。

6. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。`the important pieces` 特指重要的棋子。

7. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：时间状语从句。`be nervous when ...` 当面对更强对手时感到紧张。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句引导词。`believe that ...` 后接陈述句，that 可保留。

9. **答案：hold**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。`Some cities ... hold classes` 主语复数，陈述经常性安排。

10. **答案：it**
    - **考点**：代词。`find it + adj.` 中 `it` 作形式宾语，指代 `this ancient game`。
