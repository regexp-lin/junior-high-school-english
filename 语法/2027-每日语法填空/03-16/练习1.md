# 03-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：雨后彩虹 (Rainbow After Rain)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, a heavy rainstorm hit our city in the afternoon. At first, the sky turned dark, and strong winds (1) ______ (blow) through the streets. Many people hurried home, (2) ______ I stayed in the classroom to finish my homework.

About an hour later, the rain stopped. When I walked out of the school gate, I noticed (3) ______ unusual — a bright arc of colors appeared in the sky. It was the (4) ______ (clear) rainbow I had ever seen. Several students took out their phones and began (5) ______ (take) photos.

A science teacher happened to pass by. She explained (6) ______ a rainbow forms when sunlight passes through tiny water drops in the air. "Nature always gives us surprises (7) ______ we learn to observe," she said with a smile.

That evening, I searched online for more facts about rainbows. I found (8) ______ interesting that different cultures have different stories about them. Some people see a rainbow (9) ______ a sign of hope after difficulties.

The experience taught me that beauty often hides behind storms. If we stay patient and keep (10) ______ (look) up, we may find something wonderful waiting for us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：blew**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去发生的暴风雨，`blow` 的过去式为 `blew`。

2. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。许多人赶回家，而“我”留在教室，前后为转折关系。

3. **答案：something**
   - **考点**：不定代词。`notice something unusual` 注意到不寻常的事物，形容词后置修饰。

4. **答案：clearest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the ... I had ever seen` 结构提示用最高级，`clear` → `clearest`。

5. **答案：taking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`begin doing sth.` 开始做某事，也可用 `begin to take`，此处用动名词更自然。

6. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句引导词。`explain how ... forms` 解释彩虹如何形成。

7. **答案：if**
   - **考点**：条件状语从句。`if we learn to observe` 如果我们学会观察，自然会有惊喜。

8. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：形式宾语。`find it + adj. + that 从句`，`it` 指代后面的 that 从句。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`see ... as ...` 把……视为……。

10. **答案：looking**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`keep doing sth.` 持续做某事，用动名词。
