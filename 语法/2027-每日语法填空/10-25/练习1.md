# 10-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：传统灯笼 (Traditional Lanterns)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Lanterns have been an important part of Chinese culture __1__ ancient times. They were first used for __2__ (light) in the dark, but gradually became symbols of hope and celebration. During the Lantern Festival, streets are decorated with lanterns of various __3__ (shape) and colours. Some look like animals __4__ others look like flowers. The most traditional type is the red paper lantern, __5__ is often hung at doorways to bring good luck. Last month, I visited a lantern workshop in our town. An old craftsman showed me the __6__ (complete) process of making a lantern by hand. He first built a bamboo frame, then __7__ (cover) it with thin red paper. Painting designs on the paper was the __8__ (difficult) part. He had been making lanterns __9__ he was fifteen. His skill was amazing! I tried to make one __10__ (I), but it did not turn out very well. Still, I enjoyed every moment of the experience.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。since ancient times 自古以来，since 表示"从……起"。

2. **答案：lighting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。for + 动名词，表示用途，用 lighting。

3. **答案：shapes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。various 表示"各种各样的"，后接可数名词复数 shapes。

4. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。some... others... 结构，可用 while 表示对比，也可用 and。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 the red paper lantern 指物，非限制性定语从句用 which。

6. **答案：complete**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 process，用形容词 complete 表示"完整的"。

7. **答案：covered**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与 built 并列，用一般过去时 covered。

8. **答案：most difficult**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，difficult 为多音节词，最高级用 most difficult。

9. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。since he was fifteen 自从他十五岁起，主句为现在完成进行时，用 since 引导时间状语从句。

10. **答案：myself**
    - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。by myself 我自己，I 的反身代词为 myself。
