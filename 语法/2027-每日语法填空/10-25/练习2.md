# 10-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：无人超市 (Unmanned Supermarket)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A new unmanned supermarket opened in our neighbourhood last month. There are no cashiers — customers __1__ (simple) scan a code on their phones to enter. Cameras and sensors track __2__ items you pick up. When you walk out, the total is __3__ (automatic) charged to your account. I visited the store with my mother last Sunday. She was __4__ (amaze) at how convenient it was. We spent only ten __5__ (minute) buying everything we needed. My mother said it was much __6__ (fast) than shopping at a traditional supermarket. However, not everyone likes unmanned stores. My grandpa thinks they are __7__ (comfortable) because he does not know how to use a smartphone. __8__ people are also worried that cameras may collect too __9__ personal information. Despite these concerns, unmanned stores are becoming more popular in big __10__ (city). Technology is changing the way we shop.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：simply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 scan，用副词 simply。

2. **答案：which / what**
   - **考点**：宾语从句/定语从句连接词。track 后接的从句表示"你拿了哪些商品"，用 which 或 what。

3. **答案：automatically**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰过去分词 charged，用副词 automatically。

4. **答案：amazed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be amazed at 对……感到惊讶，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

5. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。ten 后接可数名词复数，用 minutes。

6. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，后有 than，fast 的比较级为 faster。

7. **答案：uncomfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→反义形容词）。由语境可知爷爷觉得不舒服/不方便，comfortable 加前缀 un- 构成反义词 uncomfortable。

8. **答案：Some**
   - **考点**：不定代词。Some people 一些人，置于句首首字母大写。

9. **答案：much**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词。too much + 不可数名词，personal information 为不可数名词，用 much。

10. **答案：cities**
    - **考点**：名词单复数。big cities 大城市，city 变复数为 cities。
