# 01-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：人工智能 (Artificial Intelligence)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is changing the way we live and work. From smart speakers that respond to our voice commands (1) ______ self-driving cars that navigate busy streets, AI technology is everywhere. In recent years, AI (2) ______ (become) an important tool in many fields, including medicine, education and entertainment. For example, AI programmes can now help doctors identify (3) ______ (disease) more quickly and accurately than before. In schools, AI-powered apps are being used (4) ______ (create) personalised learning plans for students. These apps can analyse a student's (5) ______ (weak) and strengths, and then recommend exercises that target specific areas for improvement. However, the rapid development of AI has also raised some (6) ______ (concern). Many people worry that robots and machines will replace human (7) ______ (work) in the future, leading to widespread unemployment. Others fear that AI could be used in (8) ______ (harm) ways if it falls into the wrong hands. Despite these concerns, most experts believe that AI will bring more benefits (9) ______ risks. The key is to develop AI (10) ______ (responsible) and ensure that it serves the interests of all people, not just a few.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from...to...` 结构，表示"从……到……"。

2. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`In recent years` 为现在完成时标志词。

3. **答案：diseases**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`identify diseases`，泛指多种疾病。

4. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be used to do sth` 表示"被用来做某事"。

5. **答案：weaknesses**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。与 `strengths` 并列，`weak` → `weakness` → `weaknesses`。

6. **答案：concerns**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`some concerns`，`some` 修饰可数名词复数。

7. **答案：workers**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`human workers` 指"人类工人"。

8. **答案：harmful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `ways` 需要形容词形式。

9. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`more benefits than risks`，比较级结构。

10. **答案：responsibly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `develop` 需要副词形式。
