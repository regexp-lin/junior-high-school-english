# 01-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园运动会 (School Sports Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday was our school's annual Sports Day, and the whole campus was filled (1) ______ excitement. Students from every class had been preparing for this event for weeks. The morning began with the opening ceremony, during (2) ______ the school band played an energetic march. Then the competitions started. The first event was the 100-metre sprint. My classmate, Liu Yang, ran so (3) ______ (fast) that nobody else could keep up with him. He crossed the finish line in just 12.3 seconds, (4) ______ (set) a new school record. In the afternoon, I took part in the relay race with three of my (5) ______ (close) friends. We had practised the baton handover many times to make sure it went (6) ______ (smooth). When it was my turn to run, I could hear my classmates (7) ______ (cheer) loudly from the stands. Their support gave me extra energy, and I managed (8) ______ (overtake) two runners before passing the baton to the next person. In the end, our team finished in second place. Although we did not win the gold medal, we were still proud of (9) ______ (we). Sports Day reminded me that the true value of competition lies not in (10) ______ (win), but in pushing yourself to do your best and enjoying the journey.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be filled with` 为固定搭配，表示"充满"。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。`during which` 引导非限制性定语从句，`which` 指代开幕式。

3. **答案：fast**
   - **考点**：副词。`so fast that...`，`fast` 本身既是形容词也是副词，无需变形。

4. **答案：setting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随结果，"从而创下了新纪录"。

5. **答案：closest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`three of my closest friends`，最高级作前置定语。

6. **答案：smoothly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `went` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：cheering**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`hear sb doing sth`，表示听到某人正在做某事。

8. **答案：to overtake**
   - **考点**：不定式。`manage to do sth`，表示"设法做到"。

9. **答案：ourselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`were proud of ourselves`，主语为 `we`。

10. **答案：winning**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `in` 后接动名词形式。
