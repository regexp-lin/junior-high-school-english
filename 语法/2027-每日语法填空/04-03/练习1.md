# 04-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：夏令营 (Summer Camp)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every July, thousands of teenagers attend summer camps across the country. These camps offer (1) ______ chance to learn new skills, make friends, and stay away from screens for a while. Last year, I joined (2) ______ outdoor camp near a lake, and it turned out to be the (3) ______ (good) vacation I have ever had.

On the first day, we were divided into small teams. Our coach asked us (4) ______ (build) a simple shelter with ropes and branches. At first, I thought the task was (5) ______ (possible), but teamwork made everything easier. By sunset, we were tired (6) ______ happy.

During the week, we also went kayaking and learned (7) ______ (safe) rules near the water. One night, we sat around the fire and shared stories. I realized that I had become (8) ______ (confidence) than before.

When the camp ended, many of us exchanged phone numbers. I still keep the photos (9) ______ were taken on the last day. This summer, I hope (10) ______ (invite) to join a science camp in the mountains.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`chance` 可数单数，泛指“一次机会”，`a chance to do sth.`。

2. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`outdoor` 以元音音素开头，用 `an outdoor camp`。

3. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the ... I have ever had` 表示范围，用 `good` 的最高级 `best`。

4. **答案：to build**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`ask sb. to do sth.` 要求某人做某事，用不定式 `to build`。

5. **答案：impossible**
   - **考点**：词性转换与语境。起初认为任务“不可能”，`possible` 加否定前缀 `im-` 得 `impossible`。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。累但开心，转折关系用 `but`。

7. **答案：safety**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `rules`，用名词作定语，`safe` → `safety`，`safety rules` 安全守则。

8. **答案：more confident**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than before` 提示比较级，`confidence` → 形容词 `confident`，多音节用 `more confident`。

9. **答案：which / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `photos`，从句缺主语，用 `which` 或 `that`。

10. **答案：to be invited**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词被动。`hope to be done` 希望被邀请，用不定式的被动式 `to be invited`。
