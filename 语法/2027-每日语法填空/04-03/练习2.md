# 04-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：清明节 (Qingming Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Qingming Festival is (1) ______ important Chinese festival for remembering family members (2) ______ have passed away. It usually falls in early April, when spring returns and trees turn green.

On this day, many families visit tombs (3) ______ (clean) the stones and place fresh flowers. Some people also bring food that their loved ones enjoyed. It is a quiet time (4) ______ reflection, not for loud celebrations.

In recent years, more cities have encouraged "green" Qingming activities. Instead of burning paper money, citizens can choose (5) ______ (plant) trees or release flowers into rivers. These changes help protect the air (6) ______ keep old traditions meaningful.

Schools often organize talks so that students can understand (7) ______ the festival matters. Last term, our teacher showed us poems (8) ______ (write) by ancient writers about missing home.

Personally, I think Qingming teaches us to value life (9) ______ honor our roots. Even if we are busy, we should spare (10) ______ moment to think about where we come from.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`important` 以元音音素开头，泛指“一个重要的节日”，用 `an`。

2. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰人 `family members`，从句缺主语，用 `who` 或 `that`。

3. **答案：to clean**
   - **考点**：动词不定式作目的状语。扫墓“为了”清洁墓碑，用 `to clean`。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`a time for reflection` 反思的时刻，`for` 表用途。

5. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`choose to do sth.` 选择做某事，用 `to plant`。

6. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。保护空气与保持传统意义并列，用 `and`。

7. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`understand why ...` 理解为什么节日重要，引导宾语从句。

8. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`poems written by ...` 被古人所写的诗，`write` → `written`。

9. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`value life` 与 `honor our roots` 并列，用 `and`。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。`spare a moment` 抽出片刻，固定搭配。
