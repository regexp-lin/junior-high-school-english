# 04-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园安全教育 (Campus Safety Education)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every term, our school holds a safety week (1) ______ students learn how to deal with fires, earthquakes, and online risks. On Monday morning, firefighters came (2) ______ trucks and demonstrated how to use extinguishers correctly.

We practiced covering our mouths (3) ______ wet towels and moving to open areas in an orderly way. The teacher stressed that staying calm is even (4) ______ (important) than running fast. During the drill, everyone (5) ______ (follow) the instructions without pushing or shouting.

In the afternoon, a police officer gave a talk about (6) ______ (avoid) strangers who ask for personal information online. She warned us never (7) ______ (share) passwords with classmates, even if they claim to help. "Safety rules protect not only you (8) ______ also your family," she said.

After the activities, I realized that accidents can happen (9) ______ we are not careful enough. I wrote down emergency numbers in my notebook (10) ______ I could find them quickly if needed.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `safety week`，从句中缺时间状语，“在安全教育周里学生学习……”，用 `when`。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`come in trucks` 乘卡车/消防车来到，表示乘坐方式。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`cover... with wet towels` 用湿毛巾捂住口鼻。

4. **答案：more important**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`even` 修饰比较级，用 `more important`。

5. **答案：followed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述演练中发生的事，用过去式 `followed`。

6. **答案：avoiding**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。介词 `about` 后接动名词，`avoid` → `avoiding`。

7. **答案：to share**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`warn sb. never to do sth.` 警告某人绝不要做某事。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but also...` 不仅……而且……。

9. **答案：if/when**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导条件或时间状语从句，“若我们不够小心/当我们不够小心时，事故可能发生”，可用 `if` 或 `when`。

10. **答案：so that/in order that**
    - **考点**：从属连词。引导目的状语从句，“记下号码以便需要时快速找到”，用 `so that`。
