# 04-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：竹编工艺 (Bamboo Weaving)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Bamboo weaving is a traditional craft (1) ______ has been practiced in southern China for hundreds of years. Skilled workers split bamboo into thin strips and then weave them into baskets, chairs, (2) ______ even small animals.

Last month, an elderly craftsman visited our school and showed us (3) ______ to make a simple pen holder. His fingers moved so quickly (4) ______ we could hardly follow his steps. "Patience is (5) ______ important than speed," he smiled.

The bamboo he brought (6) ______ (dry) in the sun for weeks, so it would not break easily. While we were practicing, he corrected our mistakes (7) ______ (gentle) and encouraged us to try again. By the end of the lesson, I (8) ______ (finish) a rough but lovely container.

I used to think handmade work was out of date, (9) ______ now I respect the wisdom behind each pattern. I hope this craft (10) ______ (pass) on to more young people in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `craft`，从句缺主语，用 `which` 或 `that`。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。列举 `baskets, chairs` 与 `even small animals`，最后一项前用 `and` 连接并列宾语。

3. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。`showed us how to make` “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语，表方式。

4. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：固定句型。`so... that...` 如此……以至于……，`that` 引导结果状语从句。

5. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`important` 为多音节形容词，比较级用 `more important`。

6. **答案：had been dried**
   - **考点**：过去完成时被动语态。竹子“在带来之前已被晾晒”，动作发生在 `brought` 所表示的情境之前，且为被动，用 `had been dried`。

7. **答案：gently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `corrected`，`gentle` → `gently` 副词。

8. **答案：had finished**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`By the end of the lesson` 到课程结束时已完成，动作在过去时间点前完成，用 `had finished`。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾以为过时”与“现在尊重智慧”为转折。

10. **答案：will be passed**
    - **考点**：一般将来时被动语态。工艺“将被传承”，主语 `craft` 与 `pass on` 为被动关系，表将来，用 `will be passed`。
