# 09-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：沙漠治理 (Desert Management)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Deserts are not always lifeless places. __1__ China, workers and volunteers have spent years planting trees and grass __2__ (fix) moving sand. They dig channels __3__ rainwater can sink in and feed roots slowly. Some areas __4__ (turn) green again, and wild animals have returned. Scientists also study __5__ to choose plant types that need little water. __6__, strong winds and droughts still make the work hard. Local farmers now grow grapes __7__ sell dried fruit online to increase income. I watched a documentary and was moved __8__ their patience. If more young people learn about desert control, they __9__ (bring) fresh ideas to the field. A greener edge of the desert __10__ (mean) a safer environment for cities nearby.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：In**
   - **考点**：介词。表示在中国境内，In China，句首大写。

2. **答案：to fix**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。种树种草是为了固定流沙。

3. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 channels 表地点，从句 rainwater can sink in 结构完整，用 where 在从句中作地点状语，表示“让雨水渗入的渠道”。

4. **答案：have turned**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。强调过去动作对现在的影响“已再度变绿”，用 have turned；主语 areas 复数。

5. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：疑问词 + 不定式。study how to choose 研究如何选择。

6. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。尽管有成果，风沙与干旱仍使工作艰难。

7. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。grow grapes 与 sell dried fruit 并列。

8. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。be moved by... 被……感动。

9. **答案：will bring / may bring**
   - **考点**：动词时态。if 从句一般现在时，主句表将来 will bring；may bring 表可能性。

10. **答案：means**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。陈述一般道理，主语 A greener edge 为单数，一般现在时用 means。
