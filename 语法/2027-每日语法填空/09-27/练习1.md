# 09-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园科技创新赛 (School Innovation Contest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school innovation contest __1__ (aim) to encourage students to solve real-life problems with creative ideas. This year, over forty teams entered the competition, __2__ topics ranged from smart trash bins to simple medical helpers. My group designed __3__ small device that reminds elderly people __4__ (take) medicine on time. We tested it again and again __5__ the teachers said the model worked steadily. On the final stage, each team had three minutes __6__ explain their projects clearly. __7__ the judges asked difficult questions, we answered __8__ (polite) and showed our data charts. __9__ we only won third prize, we learned how to cooperate and accept failure calmly. Next year, I want __10__ (try) a greener invention about saving water.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：aims / aimed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。若介绍赛事宗旨为常态，用一般现在时 aims；若叙述本届比赛目的用 aimed。以 aims 为常规答案。

2. **答案：whose**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 teams，从句 topics ranged... 中 topics 与 teams 为所属关系，用 whose topics。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a small device 一个小装置。

4. **答案：to take**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。remind sb. to do sth. 提醒某人做某事。

5. **答案：until / before**
   - **考点**：从属连词。反复测试直到老师说稳定；或在老师认可之前不断测试，until 更常见。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作定语）。three minutes to explain 用来解释的三分钟。

7. **答案：When / While / After**
   - **考点**：从属连词。当评委提问时/之后，我们礼貌回答。

8. **答案：politely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 answered 用副词，polite 的副词为 politely。

9. **答案：Although / Though**
   - **考点**：从属连词。尽管只得三等奖，仍学到合作与面对失败。

10. **答案：to try**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。want to do sth. 想要做某事。
