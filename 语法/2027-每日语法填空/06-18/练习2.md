# 06-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家庭搬迁 (Moving to a New Home)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, my family moved to a new flat on the other side of the city. I was sad about ___1___ (leave) my old neighbourhood because I had lived there ___2___ I was born. On the last day, I walked through every room, ___3___ (remember) the happy moments I had spent there. My best friend Chen Wei came to say goodbye. He gave me a photo album ___4___ (fill) with pictures of us together. I could hardly hold back my ___5___ (tear). The new flat is much ___6___ (big) and has a nice balcony where I can read books. The neighbours seem ___7___ (friend) — an old lady next door even brought us a plate of homemade cookies. My father says every change brings new ___8___ (opportunity). Although I still miss my old home, I am slowly ___9___ (get) used to the new place. I believe I ___10___ (make) new friends here soon.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：leaving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。about 为介词，后接动名词，be sad about leaving 表示"因离开而难过"，填 leaving。

2. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。since I was born 表示"自从我出生以来"，引导时间状语从句，与前面的过去完成时搭配，填 since。

3. **答案：remembering**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，walked through every room, remembering...，填 remembering。

4. **答案：filled**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，a photo album filled with... 表示"装满了……的相册"，fill → filled。

5. **答案：tears**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。hold back tears 表示"忍住眼泪"，tear 为可数名词，用复数 tears。

6. **答案：bigger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级，表示"大得多"，big → bigger。

7. **答案：friendly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。seem 为系动词，后接形容词作表语，friend → friendly。

8. **答案：opportunities**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。new opportunities 表示"新的机遇"，opportunity 为可数名词，用复数。

9. **答案：getting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词/时态。be getting used to 表示"正在逐渐习惯"，get → getting，构成现在进行时。

10. **答案：will make**
    - **考点**：动词时态。I believe 后接宾语从句，表示对将来的期望，soon 提示一般将来时，填 will make。
