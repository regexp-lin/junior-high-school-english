# 06-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：急救知识 (First Aid Knowledge)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Knowing basic first aid can save ___1___ (life) in emergencies. Last week, our school invited a doctor ___2___ (give) us a talk on first aid. She began by asking how many of us knew what to do if someone suddenly ___3___ (stop) breathing. Only a few hands went up. The doctor then ___4___ (patient) demonstrated CPR on a practice dummy. She explained that the first three ___5___ (minute) after a person's heart stops are the most critical. ___6___ someone acts quickly, the patient's chance of survival drops sharply. We also learned how to treat common injuries such ___7___ burns, cuts and nosebleeds. For a burn, you should hold the ___8___ (affect) area under cool running water for at least ten minutes. ___9___ important point is to stay calm and call 120 ___10___ (immediate). The talk made me realise that first aid is a skill everyone should master.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：lives**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。save lives 表示"挽救生命"，life 的复数形式为 lives。

2. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。invite sb. to do sth. 表示"邀请某人做某事"，填 to give。

3. **答案：stopped**
   - **考点**：动词时态。主句 asked 为过去时，if 引导的宾语从句用一般过去时，stop → stopped。

4. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 demonstrated，需用副词形式，patient → patiently。

5. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。three 后接可数名词复数，minute → minutes。

6. **答案：Unless**
   - **考点**：连词。Unless someone acts quickly 表示"除非有人迅速行动（否则存活率急剧下降）"，引导条件状语从句，填 Unless。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。such as 表示"例如"，固定搭配，填 as。

8. **答案：affected**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作定语，the affected area 表示"受影响的部位"，affect → affected。

9. **答案：Another**
   - **考点**：代词。Another important point 表示"另一个要点"，句首大写，填 Another。

10. **答案：immediately**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 call，需用副词形式，immediate → immediately。
