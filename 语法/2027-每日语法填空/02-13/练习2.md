# 02-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园义卖 (School Charity Sale)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school organized a charity sale to raise money for children in rural areas. Each class was responsible (1) ______ setting up a stall and selling donated items. My class decided to sell homemade cookies, bookmarks and small handicrafts. We spent the entire week before the event (2) ______ (get) everything ready. Some students baked cookies at home, while (3) ______ designed colourful bookmarks using recycled paper. On the day of the sale, the school playground (4) ______ (turn) into a lively market. Students and teachers walked from stall to stall, looking for interesting items to buy. Our cookies were the first to (5) ______ (sell) out — they disappeared within thirty minutes! By the end of the day, our class (6) ______ (raise) over 2,000 yuan, which was the (7) ______ (high) amount among all classes. The total money collected by the whole school reached 28,000 yuan. Our headmaster announced that the money (8) ______ (use) to buy books and school supplies for a primary school in Guizhou Province. Participating in the charity sale made me realize how (9) ______ (power) collective action can be. When everyone contributes a little, the result can be (10) ______ (true) extraordinary.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be responsible for` 负责。

2. **答案：getting**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

3. **答案：others**
   - **考点**：代词。`some... while others...` 一些人……而另一些人……。

4. **答案：was turned**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。操场被变成了市场。

5. **答案：sell**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the first to sell out` 第一个卖完的。

6. **答案：had raised**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`By the end of the day` 到那天结束时。

7. **答案：highest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the highest amount` 最高金额。

8. **答案：would be used**
   - **考点**：过去将来时的被动语态。宣布这笔钱将被用于……。

9. **答案：powerful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`how powerful` 多么强大，感叹句中用形容词。

10. **答案：truly**
    - **考点**：副词修饰形容词。`truly extraordinary` 真正非凡的。
