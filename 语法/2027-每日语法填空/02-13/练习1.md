# 02-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国茶文化 (Chinese Tea Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tea is deeply rooted in Chinese culture and has a history of over 4,000 years. Legend has it that tea (1) ______ (discover) by Emperor Shen Nong when a few tea leaves accidentally fell into his pot of (2) ______ (boil) water. Since then, tea has played an important role in Chinese daily life and social customs. There are many different (3) ______ (type) of Chinese tea, including green tea, black tea, oolong tea and white tea. Each type has its own unique flavour and health (4) ______ (benefit). Green tea, for example, is rich in antioxidants and is believed to help people stay (5) ______ (health). The Chinese tea ceremony is a traditional art form that requires great skill and patience. The person (6) ______ prepares the tea must pay close attention to every detail, from the temperature of the water (7) ______ the time the leaves are steeped. My grandmother is an expert at (8) ______ (make) tea. She always says that a good cup of tea can calm the mind and bring people (9) ______ (close) together. Nowadays, tea houses can be found in almost every city in China, (10) ______ (offer) a peaceful place for people to relax and enjoy conversation over a cup of tea.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was discovered**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。茶被发现，描述过去的事件。

2. **答案：boiling**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`boiling water` 沸腾的水。

3. **答案：types**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many different types` 许多不同种类。

4. **答案：benefits**
   - **考点**：名词复数。多种健康益处，用复数。

5. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：形容词。`stay healthy` 保持健康，系动词后用形容词。

6. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `the person`，指人。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`from... to...` 从……到……。

8. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：动名词。`be an expert at doing sth.` 擅长做某事。

9. **答案：closer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`bring people closer together` 使人们更亲近。

10. **答案：offering**
    - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。提供一个安静的地方。
