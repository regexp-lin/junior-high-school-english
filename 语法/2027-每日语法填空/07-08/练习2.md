# 07-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：光盘行动 (Clean Plate Campaign)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Food waste is a serious problem in China. According to a report, Chinese restaurants throw away enough food to feed 200 million people every year. To fight this problem, the government launched the "Clean Plate Campaign", ___1___ (encourage) people to finish all the food on their plates. The campaign has been ___2___ (wide) promoted through social media, posters and school programmes. In our school cafeteria, signs ___3___ (place) on every table to remind students not to waste food. A special team of student volunteers checks the bins after lunch ___4___ (see) how much food is left. If a class wastes too much, they will receive a ___5___ (warn). Since the campaign started, food waste in our school ___6___ (reduce) by over fifty percent. Many students now order only ___7___ they can eat, instead of piling their plates high. Some restaurants have also introduced smaller ___8___ (portion) and offer discounts to customers ___9___ finish their meals. Saving food is not just about money — it shows respect ___10___ the farmers who work hard to grow it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：encouraging**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语/结果状语，launched the campaign, encouraging people...，填 encouraging。

2. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 promoted，需用副词形式，wide → widely。

3. **答案：are placed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。signs 与 place 之间为被动关系，描述现在的状态用一般现在时的被动语态，填 are placed。

4. **答案：to see**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了看看剩了多少食物"，填 to see。

5. **答案：warning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。a + 名词，receive a warning 表示"收到一个警告"，warn → warning。

6. **答案：has been reduced**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。since the campaign started 提示用现在完成时，food waste 与 reduce 之间为被动关系，填 has been reduced。

7. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连词。what they can eat 表示"他们能吃的量"，what 引导宾语从句并在从句中作 eat 的宾语，填 what。

8. **答案：portions**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。smaller portions 表示"更小的份量"，portion → portions。

9. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 customers（人），在从句中作主语，用 who。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。show respect for 表示"对……表示尊重"，固定搭配，填 for。
