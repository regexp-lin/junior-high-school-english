# 07-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：班级团建活动 (Class Team-Building Activity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our class went to a park for a team-building day. The purpose was ___1___ (strengthen) friendship and improve cooperation among classmates. We were divided ___2___ four teams, each with a creative team name and flag. The first activity was a treasure hunt. Each team received a map with clues ___3___ (lead) to hidden items around the park. My team worked together ___4___ (efficient) and found all ten items in just forty minutes. The second activity was more ___5___ (challenge) — a relay race that included running, jumping and carrying water balloons. Our team was in the lead until Zhang Wei ___6___ (accident) dropped the last balloon. We all laughed instead of blaming him. At the end of the day, the teacher announced the ___7___ (win). Although my team came third, we had the ___8___ (much) fun. The day taught us that winning is not the only thing ___9___ matters. ___10___ is the teamwork and laughter that truly count.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to strengthen**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。the purpose was to do sth. 表示"目的是做某事"，不定式作表语，填 to strengthen。

2. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。be divided into 表示"被分成"，固定搭配，填 into。

3. **答案：leading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作后置定语，clues leading to... 表示"指向……的线索"，填 leading。

4. **答案：efficiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 worked，需用副词形式，efficient → efficiently。

5. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。more + 形容词构成比较级，描述活动（物），用 challenging。

6. **答案：accidentally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 dropped，需用副词形式，accident → accidentally。

7. **答案：winners**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。announce the winners 表示"宣布获胜者"，win → winner → winners。

8. **答案：most**
   - **考点**：副词最高级。had the most fun 表示"玩得最开心"，much → most。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 the only thing（被 only 修饰），只能用 that 引导定语从句。

10. **答案：It**
    - **考点**：代词。It is...that... 为强调句型，强调"团队合作和欢笑才是真正重要的"，填 It。
