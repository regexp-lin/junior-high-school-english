# 01-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：绘画艺术 (The Art of Painting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Painting is one of the oldest forms of human (1) ______ (express). From the cave paintings of prehistoric times to the digital art of today, people have always used images to record their experiences and emotions. In China, traditional ink-wash painting, known (2) ______ guohua, has a history of over 2,000 years. Unlike Western oil painting, which emphasises realistic detail, Chinese painting focuses (3) ______ capturing the spirit and mood of the subject. My art teacher, Ms Zhou, is an accomplished ink-wash painter (4) ______ works have been exhibited in galleries across the country. She has been teaching me to paint for the past two years, and I have made great (5) ______ (improve) under her guidance. One of the (6) ______ (difficult) things about ink-wash painting is controlling the brush. The artist must hold the brush (7) ______ (vertical) and use different amounts of pressure to create lines of varying thickness. Too much water makes the ink spread (8) ______ (control), while too little water makes the strokes look dry and lifeless. It takes years of practice to master this delicate balance. Last month, I completed a painting of bamboo that I was (9) ______ (real) proud of. Ms Zhou displayed it on the classroom wall, and several students came up to tell me how much they liked it. Her (10) ______ (encourage) has given me the confidence to keep pursuing my passion for art.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：expression**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`human expression`，形容词后接名词。

2. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`known as` 表示"被称为"。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`focus on` 为固定搭配，表示"专注于"。

4. **答案：whose**
   - **考点**：关系代词。`whose works` 引导定语从句，表所属关系。

5. **答案：improvement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`great improvement`，形容词后接名词。

6. **答案：most difficult**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the most difficult things`，多音节形容词最高级。

7. **答案：vertically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `hold` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：uncontrollably**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `spread` 需要副词，加否定前缀。

9. **答案：really**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `proud` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：encouragement**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`Her encouragement`，物主代词后接名词。
