# 01-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节约用水 (Saving Water)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Water is essential for all living things, yet many people take it (1) ______ granted. Although about 70% of the Earth's surface is covered by water, only a tiny fraction of it is fresh water that is (2) ______ (suit) for drinking. In many parts of the world, people still do not have access (3) ______ clean water. China is one of the countries facing serious water (4) ______ (short). With a population of over 1.4 billion, the demand for water continues to grow, while pollution and climate change are reducing the (5) ______ (available) of clean water sources. What can we do to help? First, we should be more (6) ______ (care) about how much water we use at home. Simple actions like turning off the tap while (7) ______ (brush) your teeth or taking shorter showers can save a surprising amount of water. Second, we should avoid (8) ______ (pollute) rivers and lakes by not throwing rubbish into them. Third, we can support water conservation projects in our communities. Last month, our class (9) ______ (visit) a water treatment plant, where we learned how dirty water is cleaned and recycled. The experience opened our eyes to the complex process (10) ______ (involve) in providing clean water to millions of people. It made us realise how precious water truly is and why we must protect it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`take sth for granted` 为固定搭配，表示"认为理所当然"。

2. **答案：suitable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is suitable for`，系动词后接形容词。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`have access to` 为固定搭配，表示"有机会获得"。

4. **答案：shortages / shortage**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`water shortage`，名词形式。

5. **答案：availability**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the availability of`，冠词后接名词。

6. **答案：careful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be more careful`，系动词后接形容词。

7. **答案：brushing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`while brushing your teeth`，省略了主语和助动词。

8. **答案：polluting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`avoid doing sth` 为固定搭配。

9. **答案：visited**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last month` 为过去时标志。

10. **答案：involved**
    - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the complex process involved in`，被动关系。
