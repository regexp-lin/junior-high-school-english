# 11-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：古诗词之美 (The Beauty of Ancient Chinese Poetry)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ancient Chinese poetry is like __1__ treasure that has been passed down for thousands of years. Even today, many people enjoy __2__ (recite) poems by Li Bai, Du Fu and other great poets. These poems express deep __3__ (feel) about nature, friendship and life in just a few short lines. Our Chinese teacher, Mrs. Zhao, is very passionate __4__ teaching poetry. She says the best way to appreciate a poem is __5__ (read) it aloud and feel its rhythm. Last week, she asked us to choose our favourite poem and explain __6__ we liked it. I chose "Quiet Night Thoughts" by Li Bai because it perfectly __7__ (describe) the feeling of missing home. Many classmates chose poems about the beauty of nature. Mrs. Zhao was pleased that we showed such __8__ (interested) in classical literature. She told us that learning ancient poetry helps us __9__ (understand) Chinese culture and improves our writing skills. I agree — the more poems I read, the __10__ (rich) my vocabulary becomes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一份宝藏"，treasure 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

2. **答案：reciting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。enjoy doing sth. 喜欢做某事，用动名词 reciting。

3. **答案：feelings**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。deep feelings 深厚的情感，feel 的名词形式为 feeling，此处用复数 feelings。

4. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。be passionate about 对……充满热情，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。the best way is to do sth. 最好的方式是做某事，用不定式 to read 作表语。

6. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。explain 后接宾语从句，表示"为什么喜欢它"，用 why。

7. **答案：describes**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。it 为第三人称单数，描述诗歌的特征为一般现在时，用 describes。

8. **答案：interest**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。such interest in 对……的兴趣，interested 的名词形式为 interest。

9. **答案：understand / to understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help sb. (to) do sth. 帮助某人做某事，to 可省略，填 understand 或 to understand。

10. **答案：richer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。the more... the richer... 越多……就越丰富……，为"the + 比较级, the + 比较级"结构。
