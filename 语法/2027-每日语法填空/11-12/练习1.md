# 11-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界学生日 (World Students' Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

November 17th is World Students' Day. It was established __1__ (honour) APJ Abdul Kalam, the former president of India and a great scientist. Kalam believed that education was the most __2__ (power) tool to change the world. He often said, "Dream is not __3__ you see in sleep; dream is something which does not let you __4__ (sleep)." Our school celebrated this day __5__ holding a special assembly. Students from different grades shared __6__ (they) dreams on stage. A ninth-grader named Li Mei told us she wanted to become __7__ doctor and work in rural areas where medical resources are limited. Her speech __8__ (touch) everyone's heart. Another student talked about his dream of __9__ (design) sustainable cities. The headmaster encouraged us __10__ (set) goals and work towards them every day. He reminded us that today's students are tomorrow's leaders. Hearing these inspiring stories made me think seriously about my own future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to honour**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了纪念"，用 to honour。

2. **答案：powerful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。the most powerful tool 最有力的工具，power 的形容词形式为 powerful。

3. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：表语从句连接词。Dream is not what you see 梦想不是你看到的，what 引导表语从句，从句缺宾语。

4. **答案：sleep**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。let sb. do sth. 让某人做某事，let 后接省略 to 的不定式，用动词原形 sleep。

5. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。by holding 通过举办，by + 动名词表示方式。

6. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 dreams，用 their。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一名医生"，doctor 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

8. **答案：touched**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文为过去时态叙述，用一般过去时 touched。

9. **答案：designing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。dream of doing sth. 梦想做某事，of 为介词，后接动名词 designing。

10. **答案：to set**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。encourage sb. to do sth. 鼓励某人做某事，用不定式 to set。
