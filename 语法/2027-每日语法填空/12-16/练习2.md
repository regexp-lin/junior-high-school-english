# 12-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：冬日温泉之旅 (A Winter Hot Spring Trip)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last winter holiday, my family went to a hot spring resort in Guangdong. It was my __1__ (one) time to visit a hot spring. When we arrived, the resort was __2__ (surround) by green mountains and bamboo forests. The air was fresh and clean. There were different pools with water at different __3__ (temperature). Some pools had flower petals floating __4__ the surface. My mother chose a pool with rose petals because she said it was good __5__ the skin. I preferred the cooler pools where I could swim around __6__ (free). My little brother was afraid of the hot water at first, __7__ he soon got used to it. We spent the whole afternoon __8__ (enjoy) the warm water and the beautiful scenery. In the evening, we had a __9__ (tradition) Cantonese dinner at the resort restaurant. The food was __10__ (absolute) delicious. It was one of the most relaxing holidays we have ever had.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。my first time 我第一次，one 的序数词为 first。

2. **答案：surrounded**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作表语）。was surrounded by 被……环绕，resort 与 surround 为被动关系。

3. **答案：temperatures**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。different temperatures 不同的温度，different 后接复数名词。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on the surface 在表面上，on 表示接触面。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。be good for 对……有好处，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：freely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 swim，用副词 freely。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。前面说"害怕热水"，后面说"很快习惯了"，语义转折用 but。

8. **答案：enjoying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 花时间做某事，用动名词 enjoying。

9. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。traditional dinner 传统的晚餐，修饰名词用形容词。

10. **答案：absolutely**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 delicious，用副词 absolutely 表示程度。
