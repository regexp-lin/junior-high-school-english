# 12-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：图书漂流活动 (Book Drifting Activity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever heard of "book drifting"? It is an activity __1__ people leave books in public places for strangers to pick up and read. Our school started a book drifting project last month. Students were encouraged to donate books that they __2__ (finish) reading. We put these books on special shelves in the school garden, the canteen and the library corridor. Anyone can take a book __3__ (free) and return it after reading. So far, over 500 books __4__ (collect) and shared. I donated two of my __5__ (favour) novels. A few days later, I found a note __6__ (stick) inside one of the drifting books. It said, "Thank you for sharing this wonderful story! It made my day __7__ (bright)." I was __8__ (deep) touched by the message. The project has helped create a __9__ (love) reading culture in our school. Our head teacher plans __10__ (expand) the project to nearby communities next term.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系副词）。an activity where 一个……的活动，where 引导定语从句修饰 activity，在从句中作地点状语（在这个活动中）。

2. **答案：had finished**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。they had finished 他们已经读完的，主句为过去时 were encouraged，从句描述更早完成的动作用过去完成时。

3. **答案：freely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 take，用副词 freely 表示"自由地"。

4. **答案：have been collected**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（现在完成时被动语态）。So far 到目前为止，books 与 collect 为被动关系，用 have been collected。

5. **答案：favourite**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。my favourite novels 我最喜爱的小说，修饰名词用形容词。

6. **答案：stuck**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作定语）。a note stuck inside 贴在里面的便条，note 与 stick 为被动关系，用过去分词作后置定语。

7. **答案：brighter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。made my day brighter 让我的一天更美好，"make + 宾语 + 比较级"结构。

8. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 touched，用副词 deeply。

9. **答案：lovely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。a lovely reading culture 美好的阅读文化，修饰名词用形容词 lovely。

10. **答案：to expand**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾语）。plan to expand 计划扩展，plan 后接不定式。
