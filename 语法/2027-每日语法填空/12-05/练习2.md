# 12-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园编程课 (School Programming Class)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This term, our school has started a new course — programming. Every Wednesday, we learn __1__ (use) a simple programming language called Python. Our teacher, Mr. Zhang, makes the lessons very __2__ (interest) by letting us create small games. In the __3__ (one) class, he taught us how to make a calculator. Although it was simple, we were all __4__ (excite) to see our own programme working. I think programming is __5__ (use) than many other skills because it helps us solve problems __6__ a logical way. My classmate Li Ming is especially good __7__ it. He has already built a weather app __8__ can show the temperature and weather conditions of any city. The rest of us are still learning the basics, __9__ we are making progress every week. Mr. Zhang often tells us that making __10__ (mistake) is a natural part of learning to programme. I hope I can create my own app one day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾语）。learn to use 学习使用，learn 后接不定式表示"学会做某事"。

2. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ing 形容词作宾补）。make the lessons interesting 使课堂有趣，make + 宾语 + 形容词。

3. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。the first class 第一节课，one 的序数词为 first。

4. **答案：excited**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ed 形容词）。be excited to see 兴奋地看到，修饰人的情感用 -ed 形容词。

5. **答案：more useful**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。more useful than 比……更有用，多音节形容词用 more + 原级构成比较级。

6. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in a logical way 以逻辑的方式，in a ... way 为固定搭配。

7. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。be good at 擅长……，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。that/which 引导定语从句修饰 app，在从句中作主语。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。前句说"仍在学基础"，后句说"每周都在进步"，语义转折用 but。

10. **答案：mistakes**
    - **考点**：名词单复数。making mistakes 犯错误，mistake 为可数名词，此处泛指多种错误用复数。
