# 12-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：红色旅游 (Red Tourism)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the National Day holiday, my family went on a "red tourism" trip to Yan'an, __1__ is known as the revolutionary capital of China. We visited several historic __2__ (site) there, including the Yan'an Revolution Memorial Hall. A guide told us many __3__ (move) stories about the Red Army. I was deeply impressed __4__ the spirit of those young soldiers who were willing to give up everything for the country. After __5__ (visit) the memorial hall, we climbed Baota Mountain. The view from the top was __6__ (amaze). My father took lots of photos and said he __7__ (come) here with his parents thirty years ago. The trip helped me __8__ (understand) the history of our country much better. I think every student __9__ visit these places at least once. Red tourism is a great way to learn about the past __10__ remember the heroes who fought for our peaceful life today.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。which 引导非限制性定语从句，指代 Yan'an，在从句中作主语。

2. **答案：sites**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。several historic sites 几个历史遗址，several 后接复数名词。

3. **答案：moving**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ing 形容词）。moving stories 感人的故事，修饰物用 -ing 形容词。

4. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。be impressed by 被……打动，by 表示"被"。

5. **答案：visiting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。after visiting 参观之后，after 为介词，后接动名词。

6. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ing 形容词作表语）。The view was amazing 景色令人惊叹，修饰事物用 -ing 形容词。

7. **答案：had come**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。said he had come 说他曾来过，主句为过去时，从句描述更早的动作用过去完成时。

8. **答案：understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式省略 to）。help sb. (to) do sth. 帮助某人做某事，help 后接不定式，to 可省略。

9. **答案：should**
   - **考点**：情态动词。should visit 应该参观，表示建议。

10. **答案：and**
    - **考点**：并列连词。learn about the past and remember the heroes 了解过去并铭记英雄，and 连接两个并列动词。
