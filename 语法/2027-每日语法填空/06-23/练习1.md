# 06-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：跑步与自律 (Running and Self-Discipline)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I started running every morning six months ago, and it has ___1___ (complete) changed my life. At first, I could only run for about five minutes before ___2___ (feel) out of breath. But I told ___3___ (I) to keep going, even when it was hard. Slowly, my body became ___4___ (strong) and I could run farther. Now I can easily finish a three-kilometre run ___5___ stopping. The key to my progress is ___6___ (regular) — I run at the same time every day, rain ___7___ shine. Running also helps me study better. After a morning run, my mind feels much ___8___ (clear) and I can concentrate more easily ___9___ class. My parents are proud of the change in me. Last month, I even took part in a city marathon ___10___ finished the five-kilometre race. Running has taught me that small daily efforts can lead to big results.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 changed，需用副词形式，complete → completely。

2. **答案：feeling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。before 为介词，后接动名词，feel → feeling。

3. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词。told myself 表示"告诉自己"，主语与宾语为同一人，用反身代词 myself。

4. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。became 为系动词，表示"变得更强壮"，strong → stronger。

5. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。without stopping 表示"不停下来地"，填 without。

6. **答案：regularity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。is 后接名词作表语，the key to my progress is regularity，regular → regularity。

7. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。rain or shine 表示"风雨无阻"，固定搭配，填 or。

8. **答案：clearer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级，表示"清晰得多"，clear → clearer。

9. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in class 表示"在课堂上"，填 in。

10. **答案：and**
    - **考点**：连词。took part in a marathon 与 finished the race 为两个并列动作，用 and 连接。
