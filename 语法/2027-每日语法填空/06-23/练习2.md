# 06-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：智能家居 (Smart Home)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Smart home technology is ___1___ (change) the way we live. With just a few taps on a smartphone, people can control lights, air conditioners and even door locks from ___2___ (any). In my uncle's house, everything ___3___ (connect) to a central system. When he says "Good morning" to the voice assistant, the curtains open ___4___ (automatic) and soft music starts playing. The fridge can tell him when food is about ___5___ (run) out and suggest a shopping list. At night, smart sensors detect ___6___ someone is in the room and turn off the lights if it is empty. These features make daily life much ___7___ (easy) and help save energy at the same time. Of course, some people worry about privacy ___8___ all their personal data is stored online. Experts advise ___9___ (set) strong passwords and updating the system ___10___ (regular) to stay safe. Despite these concerns, smart homes are likely to become the standard in the near future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：changing**
   - **考点**：动词时态。is changing 构成现在进行时，表示"正在改变"，change → changing。

2. **答案：anywhere**
   - **考点**：代词。from anywhere 表示"从任何地方"，any → anywhere。

3. **答案：is connected**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。everything 与 connect 之间为被动关系，用一般现在时的被动语态，填 is connected。

4. **答案：automatically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 open，需用副词形式，automatic → automatically。

5. **答案：to run**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be about to do sth. 表示"即将做某事"，填 to run。

6. **答案：whether**
   - **考点**：连词。detect whether someone is in the room 表示"检测是否有人在房间里"，引导宾语从句，填 whether。

7. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级，表示"容易得多"，easy → easier。

8. **答案：because**
   - **考点**：连词。解释人们担心隐私的原因——所有个人数据都存储在网上，用 because。

9. **答案：setting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。advise doing sth. 表示"建议做某事"，set → setting。

10. **答案：regularly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 updating，需用副词形式，regular → regularly。
