# 08-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校科技节 (School Science Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This year our school ___1___ (hold) its annual Science Festival in the gym on a bright Friday morning. ___2___ main goal of the event was to show that science is not only something we read about in books ___3___ something we can touch, test, and share. Two weeks earlier, our headteacher told every class to prepare ___4___ hands-on display that parents could look at, try, and discuss with student guides. Posters lined the walls, and the air smelled faintly of glue, lemon juice, and fresh paper.

On the opening day, parents arrived with cameras, and younger brothers and sisters rushed from table to table like explorers. My teammates and I ___5___ (work) hard on a small windmill connected to ___6___ LED light. When the fan blew across the blades, the tiny bulb started to shine, and the audience clapped ___7___ (excite). Between performances, we answered questions ___8___ (patient) and ___9___ (write) down every useful idea in a thick notebook so we would not forget what we had learned from others.

In the final hour, judges walked around with score sheets. When they stopped at our booth, my hands were shaking, ___10___ (luck), their encouraging smiles helped me speak slowly and clearly about circuits and teamwork. I left the gym tired but proud, already dreaming of a bolder invention for next year's festival.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。全文叙述已发生的校园活动，主语 our school 与 hold 为主动关系（学校举办活动），应用一般过去时；hold 的过去式为 held。

2. **答案：The**
   - **考点**：冠词。特指“这次科技节的主要目标”，用定冠词 the；句首首字母大写为 The。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。not only... but (also)... 为固定搭配，表示“不仅……而且……”，also 可省略，故填 but。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。hands-on display 为可数名词单数，此处泛指“一个可动手操作的展示项目”，hands 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 a。

5. **答案：worked**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。叙述科技节准备阶段已发生的动作，与全文过去时态一致，work 的过去式为 worked。

6. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。LED 读音以元音音素 /e/ 开头，表示“一盏 LED 灯”，用不定冠词 an。

7. **答案：excitedly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词/名词 → 副词）。修饰动词 clapped，需用副词；形容人因兴奋而鼓掌，用 excitedly（兴奋地）。

8. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 answered，需用副词；patient 的副词形式为 patiently（耐心地）。

9. **答案：wrote**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。与 answered 并列，同处过去语境中，谓语动词用一般过去时；write 的过去式为 wrote。

10. **答案：luckily**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 副词）。逗号后分句表转折补充，需用副词作评注性状语，意为“幸运的是”；luck 的副词形式为 luckily，句首小写（在逗号后）。
