# 08-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节约粮食 (Saving Food)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school ___1___ (launch) a "Clean Plate" project last month ___2___ order to cut down on waste in the dining hall. Before lunch, monitors put up posters ___3___ clearly explain how many kilos of leftovers our school produces each week and why that number should make us think twice. Many students did not take the message ___4___ (serious) at first, and some still piled their trays high just out of habit. ___5___ (luck), short talks during morning meetings, together with photos of empty landfills, helped everyone see the problem from a calmer and kinder point of view.

The cooks tried ___6___ (hard) to make dishes healthier and less oily, and they agreed to give smaller first servings ___7___ students who were still hungry could return to the window for more without feeling embarrassed. During the first week, I often left rice on ___8___ tray because I spent too much time chatting with friends. ___9___ (gradual), I learned to eat slowly, take only what I could finish, and give quiet thanks before each meal. Our class even began ___10___ (share) oranges and apples after P.E. classes so that fresh fruit would not hide in schoolbags until it turned soft.

Last Friday, the headmaster praised our effort and said saving food is a simple way to respect farmers' labor and care for the environment. I finally understood why my grandmother always asked me to finish every grain in my bowl.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：launched**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。时间状语 last month 表明动作发生在过去，谓语用一般过去时；launch 的过去式为 launched。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in order to 为固定短语，意为“为了……”，表目的，故填 in。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。posters 为先行词，其后为定语从句，从句中缺少主语，指物，用关系代词 that（which 亦可，中考中 that 很常见）。

4. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词短语 take the message，需用副词；serious 的副词形式为 seriously；take...seriously 意为“认真对待……”。

5. **答案：Luckily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 副词）。位于句首，修饰整个句子，作评注性状语，表示“幸运的是”；luck 的副词形式为 luckily，句首首字母大写。

6. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：形容词或副词的比较级。语境暗含与之前对比，“更努力地”改进菜品，try harder 为常见搭配，故用 hard 的比较级 harder。

7. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。前后为因果关系：先少打一点，这样仍饿的同学可以再取餐。so 引导结果分句，相当于 so that 引导目的/结果（此处填一词用 so 即可）。

8. **答案：my**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 tray，表示“我的餐盘”，用形容词性物主代词 my。

9. **答案：Gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰整个后续分句，表示“逐渐地”，用副词；gradual 的副词形式为 gradually，句首首字母大写。

10. **答案：to share**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。begin to do sth. 表示“开始做某事”，此处指班级开始“分享水果”这一具体行为，用不定式 to share；begin doing sth.（sharing）在部分试卷中也可接受，但 to share 更为常见。
