# 07-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园安全 (Campus Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the first week of this term, our school ___1___ (organize) a one-week program about campus safety for students from Grade Seven to Grade Nine. The main aim was to show us ___2___ following simple rules every day could keep us away from many unnecessary accidents inside and outside the school. In the opening speech, our head teacher clearly explained ___3___ we should never run wildly in the hallways, push others on the crowded stairs, or block emergency doors with bags and sports equipment. She also reminded us that knowing where the nearest exits are can save precious time when danger comes suddenly.

On Tuesday afternoon, a young police officer ___4___ (give) us an interesting talk about staying safe on the Internet. He used real examples and told us never ___5___ share our passwords, home addresses, or parents' phone numbers with strangers we meet online through games or chat groups. The very next morning, we practiced ___6___ (leave) the teaching building in less than two minutes when the fire alarm sounded. Our class teacher said we should always ___7___ (use) the stairs instead of the lift during an emergency, because lifts may stop between floors.

Everyone ___8___ (follow) the instructions seriously, and nobody played jokes during the drill. At the closing ceremony, the principal thanked us warmly and promised ___9___ (hold) similar activities every term in the future. We believe ___10___ kind of training helps us develop better habits and truly protects young lives on campus.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：organized**
   - **考点**：动词时态。短文叙述已发生的“本学期第一周”活动，主语 our school 为第三人称单数，但此处 organize 作谓语，一般过去时说明过去组织的活动，用 organized（美式亦可用 organised，中考常见 organized）。

2. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词（宾语从句引导词）。show us 后接宾语从句，说明“遵守简单规则**如何**能让我们远离事故”，表示方式用 how，而非 that（that 仅陈述事实，此处强调方式、途径）。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词（宾语从句引导词）。explained 后接多个由 and 连接的宾语从句时，第一个从句常用 that 引导（口语中可省略，但填空题需补全），that 在从句中不充当成分，仅起连接作用。

4. **答案：gave**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与全文叙述过去一周活动一致，give sb a talk 表示“做讲座、作报告”，give 的过去式为 gave。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词中的不定式符号。tell us never to do sth. 为固定结构，否定词 never 置于 to 之前，即 never to share，表示“告诉我们绝不要分享……”。

6. **答案：leaving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。practice 作动词时，后接动名词作宾语，即 practice doing sth.（练习做某事），故填 leaving。

7. **答案：use**
   - **考点**：动词形式。情态动词 should 后接动词原形，故填 use，不用 uses 或 used。

8. **答案：followed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述演练当天大家的行为，与后半句 played 并列，均为一般过去时，follow 的过去式为 followed。

9. **答案：to hold**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。promise 后接不定式作宾语，即 promise to do sth.（承诺做某事），故填 to hold。

10. **答案：this**
    - **考点**：代词。this kind of training 表示“这种培训”，特指上文提到的安全演练与教育活动；也可用 that，但 this 更常指“刚提到的、近指”的内容，此处 this 更自然。
