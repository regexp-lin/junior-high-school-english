# 07-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：听音乐的好处 (Benefits of Listening to Music)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many teenagers enjoy ___1___ (listen) to music whenever they have spare time, ___2___ only a small number of them understand why it is so popular around the world. Recent studies show that slow rhythms can help the brain ___3___ (relax) after students sit for hours ___4___ (do) difficult homework and revision. On nights before big exams, quiet music may even help you fall asleep ___5___ (easy) and wake up with a clearer mind the next morning.

Some teachers have noticed that a short break with favorite songs makes classes feel less boring and helps students return to lessons with fresh energy. Music also changes ___6___ teenagers plan their study time and deal with pressure from exams and competitions. Some learners report that light classical sounds help them ___7___ (think) more clearly about hard questions, while fast pop songs cheer ___8___ (they) up between tiring tasks. ___9___ addition, practicing an instrument trains patience and discipline, ___10___ are qualities that benefit many school subjects from math to art. Researchers say careful listening may support memory over the long term if teenagers still concentrate on their work instead of getting lost in loud lyrics during study time. Doctors still remind young people not to wear earphones too long or play music at a harmful volume, because good listening habits matter as much as the songs themselves.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：listening**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。enjoy 后接动名词作宾语，即 enjoy doing sth.（喜欢做某事），listen 以重读闭音节结尾，变动名词时双写 n 再加 -ing，故为 listening。

2. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说“很多人喜欢听音乐”，后句说“只有少数人明白为何如此流行”，语义转折，用 but 连接两个分句。

3. **答案：relax**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help 的常见结构为 help sb. (to) do sth.，不定式符号 to 常可省略，填空题中填动词原形 relax 即可，表示“帮助大脑放松”。

4. **答案：doing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。after students sit for hours 后若接“做作业”这一持续行为，常用介词 + doing 或此处用现在分词短语作伴随/时间补充：sit for hours doing homework 表示“坐着好几个小时（一直）做作业”，doing 与 students 构成逻辑上的主谓关系，故填 doing。

5. **答案：easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词变副词）。修饰动词短语 fall asleep 需用副词，easy 的副词形式为 easily，表示“更容易入睡”。

6. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词（宾语从句引导词）。动词 changes 后接宾语从句，说明音乐改变的是青少年“**如何**规划学习时间、应对压力”，从句中缺方式状语含义，用 how 引导，语法规范，避免 the way how 类重复结构。

7. **答案：think**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help sb. (to) do sth. 结构中，填空题通常填动词原形 think，表示“帮助他们更清晰地思考难题”。

8. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。cheer up 为“动词 + 副词”短语，宾语为人称代词时须用宾格且放在 cheer 与 up 之间，即 cheer them up，they 的宾格为 them。

9. **答案：In**
   - **考点**：固定搭配中的介词。In addition 为固定短语，意为“此外、而且”，句首首字母大写，故填 In。

10. **答案：which**
    - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句修饰前面整个主句或先行词 patience and discipline（两者合译为品质），从句缺主语且指物/抽象概念，用 which 引导，which are qualities 表示“而这些品质有利于……”。
