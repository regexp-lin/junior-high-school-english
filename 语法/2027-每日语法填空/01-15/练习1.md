# 01-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：极地探险 (Polar Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The polar regions of the Earth are among the most extreme environments known to humankind. The Arctic in the north and the Antarctic in the south are both covered in vast sheets of ice, with temperatures that can drop below minus 50 degrees Celsius. Despite these harsh conditions, many brave explorers have ventured into these frozen lands throughout history. One of the most famous polar (1) ______ (explore) was Sir Ernest Shackleton. In 1914, he led an expedition to cross Antarctica on foot. Unfortunately, his ship, the Endurance, became (2) ______ (trap) in pack ice and was eventually crushed. Shackleton and his crew (3) ______ (force) to abandon the ship and camp on the floating ice for months. The situation seemed (4) ______ (hope), but Shackleton refused to give up. He made a daring journey in a small lifeboat across 1,300 kilometres of the roughest ocean on Earth (5) ______ (seek) help. (6) ______ (remarkable), all 28 members of his expedition survived. Today, scientists continue to explore the polar regions, (7) ______ not for adventure but for research. They study the ice cores (8) ______ (learn) about past climates and monitor changes in the polar ice caps to understand the effects (9) ______ global warming. The polar regions may seem far away from our daily lives, but what happens there affects the whole planet. It is (10) ______ (extreme) important that we protect these fragile ecosystems for future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：explorers**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`polar explorers`，指多位探险家。

2. **答案：trapped**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`became trapped`，系动词后接过去分词表状态。

3. **答案：were forced**
   - **考点**：被动语态。他们"被迫"弃船，过去时被动。

4. **答案：hopeless**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`seemed hopeless`，加后缀 `-less` 表示"无望的"。

5. **答案：to seek**
   - **考点**：不定式。`made a journey...to seek help`，不定式表目的。

6. **答案：Remarkably**
   - **考点**：词性转换。句首修饰整个句子，用副词形式。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not for adventure but for research`，`not...but...` 结构。

8. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：不定式。`study the ice cores to learn about`，不定式表目的。

9. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`the effects of global warming`，表示"全球变暖的影响"。

10. **答案：extremely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `important` 需要副词形式。
