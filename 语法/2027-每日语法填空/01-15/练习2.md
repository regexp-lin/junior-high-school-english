# 01-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学做饭 (Learning to Cook)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Learning to cook is a valuable life skill that every young person should develop. Last winter holiday, I decided to learn how (1) ______ (cook) some simple dishes so that I could help my parents in the kitchen. My mother agreed (2) ______ (teach) me, and we started with something basic — tomato and egg stir-fry. She showed me how to crack eggs into a bowl and beat them until they were smooth. Then she taught me how to cut tomatoes into small (3) ______ (piece) without hurting (4) ______ (I). The most challenging part was controlling the heat. I turned the flame up too high at first, and the eggs almost (5) ______ (burn). My mother laughed and told me that cooking is all about practice and patience. Over the following weeks, I learned to make several (6) ______ (dish), including fried rice, noodle soup and steamed fish. Each dish required different (7) ______ (skill) and techniques, which made the learning process very (8) ______ (interest). The proudest moment came when I cooked dinner for my whole family (9) ______ my own. My father said it was the (10) ______ (delicious) meal he had ever had. I know he was exaggerating, but his words filled me with joy and motivated me to keep improving.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to cook**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to do sth`，疑问词 + 不定式结构。

2. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：不定式。`agree to do sth` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：pieces**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`small pieces`，切成小块。

4. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 `I`，对应 `myself`。

5. **答案：burned / burnt / got burned**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。描述过去的动作，鸡蛋差点"烧焦"。

6. **答案：dishes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several dishes`，`several` 修饰可数名词复数。

7. **答案：skills**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different skills`，不同的技能。

8. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物/过程用 `-ing` 形式。

9. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on my own` 为固定短语，表示"独自地"。

10. **答案：most delicious**
    - **考点**：最高级。`the most delicious meal he had ever had`，多音节形容词最高级。
