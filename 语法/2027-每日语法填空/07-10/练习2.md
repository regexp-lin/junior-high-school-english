# 07-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市记忆与老建筑 (City Memories and Old Buildings)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Old buildings tell the stories of a city's past. In Guangzhou, you can still find many ___1___ (tradition) buildings from the early twentieth century, especially in the Shangxiajiu area. These buildings, with ___2___ (they) unique mix of Chinese and Western styles, are called qilou. They ___3___ (build) by overseas Chinese who returned home with new ideas. A qilou typically has covered walkways on the ground floor, ___4___ (provide) shade for shoppers and protection from rain. In recent years, the government ___5___ (spend) a lot of money on restoring these old buildings. Some have been turned ___6___ museums, cafes and art galleries. Walking through these streets feels like ___7___ (travel) back in time. My grandfather grew up in one of these buildings. He often tells me stories about the ___8___ (neighbour) where everyone knew each other by name. He worries that as the city grows ___9___ (rapid), young people will forget the history behind these walls. I think it is important to preserve old buildings because they give a city ___10___ (it) character and soul.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 buildings，需用形容词形式，tradition → traditional。

2. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 unique mix，用形容词性物主代词，they → their。

3. **答案：were built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。they 与 build 之间为被动关系，叙述历史事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were built。

4. **答案：providing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，has covered walkways, providing shade...，填 providing。

5. **答案：has spent**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in recent years 提示用现在完成时，the government 为单数主语，填 has spent。

6. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。be turned into 表示"被变成"，固定搭配，填 into。

7. **答案：travelling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。like 为介词，后接动名词，feels like travelling 表示"感觉像在旅行"，填 travelling。

8. **答案：neighbourhood**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the neighbourhood 表示"那个街区"，neighbour → neighbourhood。

9. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 grows，需用副词形式，rapid → rapidly。

10. **答案：its**
    - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 character and soul，用形容词性物主代词 its。
