# 07-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：夏日防暑 (Beating the Summer Heat)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Summers in Guangzhou can be extremely hot, with temperatures often ___1___ (rise) above 35°C. Heatstroke is a common problem that affects many people, ___2___ (especial) the elderly and young children. To stay safe in hot weather, doctors recommend ___3___ (drink) plenty of water throughout the day, even if you don't feel thirsty. It is also important ___4___ (avoid) going outdoors during the hottest hours, usually between noon and three o'clock. If you ___5___ go outside, wear a hat and apply sunscreen to protect your ___6___ (skin). Light-coloured clothing is ___7___ (good) than dark clothes because it reflects more sunlight. Our school has ___8___ (short) outdoor PE classes during the hottest months. Students now exercise in an air-conditioned indoor gym ___9___ of on the playground. My mother always prepares cold mung bean soup ___10___ us in summer. She says it is a traditional way to cool down and stay healthy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rising**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。with + 名词 + 现在分词，构成 with 复合结构，temperatures 主动上升，填 rising。

2. **答案：especially**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰后面的名词短语，起强调作用，especial → especially。

3. **答案：drinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。recommend doing sth. 表示"建议做某事"，drink → drinking。

4. **答案：to avoid**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It is important to do sth. 句型，it 为形式主语，填 to avoid。

5. **答案：must/do**
   - **考点**：助动词/情态动词。If you must/do go outside 表示"如果你必须/确实要外出"，表强调，填 must 或 do。

6. **答案：skin**
   - **考点**：名词。protect your skin 表示"保护你的皮肤"，skin 为不可数名词，保持原形。

7. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，good → better。

8. **答案：shortened**
   - **考点**：词性转换。has shortened 构成现在完成时，表示"已经缩短了"，short → shortened。

9. **答案：instead**
   - **考点**：副词。instead of 表示"而不是"，在室内而非操场上运动，填 instead。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。prepare sth. for sb. 表示"为某人准备某物"，填 for。
