# 07-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：深海探索 (Deep Sea Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The deep ocean remains one of the least explored places ___1___ Earth. More people have been to space than to the deepest part of the sea. In 2020, Chinese scientists used a submersible called Fendouzhe ___2___ (reach) a depth of 10,909 metres in the Mariana Trench. This was a ___3___ (history) achievement that made headlines worldwide. The pressure at such depths is over 1,000 times ___4___ (great) than on the surface. Without ___5___ (advance) technology, it would be impossible for humans to survive there. During the dive, scientists ___6___ (discover) several new species of fish and shrimp that had never ___7___ (see) before. These creatures have adapted to live in complete ___8___ (dark) and extreme cold. The expedition also collected rock samples from the ocean floor, ___9___ will help researchers understand the Earth's geological history. Exploring the deep sea is just as important ___10___ exploring space, because what lies beneath the waves could hold the key to many scientific mysteries.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on Earth 表示"在地球上"，填 on。

2. **答案：to reach**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。use sth. to do sth. 表示"用某物做某事"，填 to reach。

3. **答案：historic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 achievement，history → historic（具有历史意义的）。

4. **答案：greater**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。times + 比较级 + than，表示"比表面大1000倍以上"，great → greater。

5. **答案：advanced**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 technology，advance → advanced（先进的），过去分词作定语。

6. **答案：discovered**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去的下潜事件，用一般过去时，discover → discovered。

7. **答案：been seen**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。that 引导定语从句，species 与 see 之间为被动关系，had never been seen 构成过去完成时的被动语态。

8. **答案：darkness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。complete + 名词，in complete darkness 表示"在完全的黑暗中"，dark → darkness。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，先行词为 rock samples，用 which。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：连词。as important as 表示"和……一样重要"，just as...as 结构，填 as。
