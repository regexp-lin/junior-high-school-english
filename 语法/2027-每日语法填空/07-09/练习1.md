# 07-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑假读书计划 (Summer Reading Plan)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

At the beginning of summer holiday, my teacher gave each of us a reading list with fifteen books on ___1___. She said reading was the best way to improve ___2___ (we) English during the long break. I set a goal for ___3___ (I): finish at least eight books before school starts. To stay on track, I created a reading schedule. Every morning, I read for one hour after breakfast, and before bed, I read for ___4___ (add) thirty minutes. The first book I chose was "Wonder" by R.J. Palacio. It is about a boy ___5___ facial difference makes him look different from other children. The story ___6___ (teach) me that true beauty comes from the inside. I was so deeply ___7___ (touch) that I finished it in just three days. After reading each book, I write a short review in my notebook. My father reads them and sometimes ___8___ (share) his own thoughts with me. So far, I ___9___ (complete) six books and I am confident that I will reach my goal. Reading ___10___ (wide) my view of the world and makes me a more thoughtful person.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。on it 指代 the reading list，"上面有十五本书"，填 it。

2. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 English，用形容词性物主代词，we → our。

3. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词。set a goal for myself 表示"给自己设定目标"，主语为 I，用反身代词 myself。

4. **答案：additional/an additional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词短语 thirty minutes，需用形容词形式，add → additional。

5. **答案：whose**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a boy（人），在从句中修饰名词 facial difference，表所属关系，用 whose。

6. **答案：taught**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述读完后的感受，用一般过去时，teach → taught。

7. **答案：touched**
   - **考点**：词性转换。was deeply touched 表示"被深深感动"，描述人的感受用过去分词 touched。

8. **答案：shares**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。my father 为第三人称单数，描述习惯性动作用一般现在时，share → shares。

9. **答案：have completed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。so far 提示用现在完成时，I 为主语，填 have completed。

10. **答案：widens**
    - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。Reading（动名词）作主语视为第三人称单数，描述一般事实用一般现在时，widen → widens。
