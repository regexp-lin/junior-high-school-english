# 12-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：海洋垃圾清理 (Ocean Rubbish Clean-up)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ocean pollution has become one of the __1__ (serious) environmental problems in the world. Every year, millions of tonnes of rubbish __2__ (throw) into the sea. Most of it is plastic, __3__ takes hundreds of years to break down. Sea animals often mistake plastic bags __4__ food and eat them. This can make them __5__ (terrible) ill or even cause death. Last summer, I took part in a beach clean-up activity __6__ (organise) by a local environmental group. About one hundred volunteers __7__ (come) to the beach that morning. We worked together for three hours and __8__ (collect) over 200 bags of rubbish. I was shocked to see so __9__ waste on the beach. The experience made me realise that each of us has a __10__ (responsible) to protect the ocean. Now I always bring my own bags when shopping and try to use less plastic in daily life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most serious**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the most serious 最严重的……之一，多音节形容词用 most + 原级构成最高级。

2. **答案：is thrown**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般现在时被动语态）。rubbish 与 throw 为被动关系，Every year 表示经常性动作，用一般现在时被动 is thrown。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。which 引导非限制性定语从句，指代 plastic，在从句中作主语。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。mistake... for... 把……误认为……，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 ill，用副词 terribly，表示程度。

6. **答案：organised**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作定语）。activity organised by 由……组织的活动，activity 与 organise 为被动关系，用过去分词。

7. **答案：came**
   - **考点**：动词时态。that morning 那天早上，描述过去发生的事，用一般过去时 came。

8. **答案：collected**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与前面的 worked 并列，描述过去的动作，用一般过去时 collected。

9. **答案：much**
   - **考点**：形容词（修饰不可数名词）。so much waste 如此多的垃圾，waste 为不可数名词，用 much 修饰。

10. **答案：responsibility**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。have a responsibility 有一份责任，a 提示需要名词，responsible 的名词形式为 responsibility。
