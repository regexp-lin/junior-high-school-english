# 12-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园合唱团 (School Choir)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I joined the school choir at the __1__ (begin) of this term. At first, I was not confident about my __2__ (sing) voice. Our music teacher, Ms. Liu, told me that everyone can learn to sing __3__ (beautiful) with enough practice. She asked us __4__ (practise) for one hour every afternoon after school. During rehearsals, we learned how to breathe __5__ (proper) and how to match our voices with others. It was not easy __6__ all. Sometimes I felt tired and wanted to give up. __7__ Ms. Liu always encouraged us by saying, "You are getting better every day." Last week, we __8__ (give) our first performance at the school art festival. When we stood on the stage and sang together, I felt __9__ strong sense of belonging. The audience clapped loudly and some teachers even had tears in __10__ (they) eyes. That moment made all the hard work worth it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：beginning**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。at the beginning of 在……开始时，begin 的名词形式为 beginning。

2. **答案：singing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作定语）。singing voice 歌唱嗓音，动名词修饰名词表示用途。

3. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 sing，用副词 beautifully。

4. **答案：to practise**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾补）。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式。

5. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 breathe，用副词 properly。

6. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：固定短语。not... at all 一点也不……，为固定搭配。

7. **答案：But**
   - **考点**：转折连词。前面说"想放弃"，后面说"老师鼓励我们"，语义转折用 But。

8. **答案：gave**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last week 上周，描述过去的事用一般过去时 gave。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。a strong sense of 一种强烈的……感，sense 为可数名词，前有形容词修饰，用不定冠词 a。

10. **答案：their**
    - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。their eyes 他们的眼睛，修饰名词用形容词性物主代词 their。
