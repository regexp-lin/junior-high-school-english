# 03-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园心理健康 (Mental Health at School)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mental health is just as important as physical health, (1) ______ it often receives less attention. Many teenagers face stress from schoolwork, exams and social relationships, but they don't always know how (2) ______ (deal) with it.

Our school recently set up a counselling room (3) ______ students can talk about their problems in a safe and private environment. Two (4) ______ (profession) counsellors are available every day during lunch break and after school.

Last week, our school also held a Mental Health Week. Several (5) ______ (activity) were organized, including a lecture on managing stress, a group discussion about friendship, and a relaxation workshop. I attended the workshop and learned some (6) ______ (use) breathing techniques to calm myself down when I feel anxious.

One of the (7) ______ (speaker) was a doctor from a local hospital. She told us that it is completely (8) ______ (normally) to feel sad or worried sometimes. What matters is that we should not keep our feelings (9) ______ ourselves. Talking to someone we trust — a parent, a teacher or a friend — can make a real difference.

I think our school (10) ______ (do) a great job in caring about students' mental health. I hope more schools will follow this example.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。心理健康同样重要，但往往受到较少关注，表转折。

2. **答案：to deal**
   - **考点**：不定式。`know how to deal with sth.` 知道如何应对某事。

3. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句修饰 `counselling room`，表地点。

4. **答案：professional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `counsellors`，`profession` → `professional` 形容词。

5. **答案：activities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`Several activities` 几项活动。

6. **答案：useful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `breathing techniques`，`use` → `useful` 形容词。

7. **答案：speakers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`One of the speakers` 其中一位演讲者，`one of + 复数`。

8. **答案：normal**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is completely normal` 是完全正常的，形容词作表语（注意 normally 是副词，此处需形容词）。

9. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`keep sth. to ourselves` 把事情藏在心里。

10. **答案：is doing / has done**
    - **考点**：现在进行时 / 现在完成时。表示学校目前正在做 / 已经做了很好的工作。
