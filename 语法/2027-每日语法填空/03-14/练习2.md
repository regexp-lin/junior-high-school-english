# 03-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国饺子 (Chinese Dumplings)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Dumplings, or "jiaozi" in Chinese, are one of the most (1) ______ (tradition) foods in China. They have been enjoyed by Chinese people for over 1,800 years. Making and eating dumplings is (2) ______ important part of Chinese festivals, especially the Spring Festival and the Winter Solstice.

Making dumplings is a family activity (3) ______ brings everyone together. First, the dough is prepared by mixing flour and water. Then it is rolled into thin, round (4) ______ (wrap). The filling usually (5) ______ (consist) of minced meat, vegetables and various seasonings.

I learned to make dumplings from my grandmother when I was six. She was the best dumpling (6) ______ (make) in our family. I can still remember how (7) ______ (patient) she taught me to fold the edges properly. At first, my dumplings looked terrible — some were too big (8) ______ others fell apart during cooking. But after years of practice, I can now make dumplings almost as (9) ______ (good) as hers.

Every Chinese New Year, our whole family gathers around the table to make dumplings together. We chat, laugh and sometimes hide a coin inside one dumpling. Whoever (10) ______ (find) the coin is said to have good luck for the whole year. It is these warm moments that make dumplings so special to me.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `foods`，`tradition` → `traditional` 形容词。

2. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`an important part` 重要的一部分，`important` 元音开头。

3. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰 `activity`，在从句中作主语。

4. **答案：wrappers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`thin, round wrappers` 薄而圆的饺子皮，`wrap` → `wrapper`。

5. **答案：consists**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。描述通常情况，主语 `The filling` 第三人称单数。

6. **答案：maker**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the best dumpling maker` 最好的包饺子的人，`make` → `maker`。

7. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `taught`，`patient` → `patiently` 副词。

8. **答案：and / while**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`some were too big and/while others fell apart` 一些太大，另一些散开了。

9. **答案：well**
   - **考点**：原级比较。`as well as` 和……一样好，`good` 对应副词 `well`（修饰动词 `make`）。

10. **答案：finds**
    - **考点**：一般现在时。`Whoever finds ...` 表示普遍情况/习惯，用一般现在时。
