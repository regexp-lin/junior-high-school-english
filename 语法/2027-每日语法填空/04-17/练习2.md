# 04-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：编程入门 (Introduction to Programming)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has opened an after-school course (1) ______ introduction to programming for beginners. In the first lesson, the teacher showed us how a few lines of code could make a cartoon cat move (2) ______ the screen. Everyone stared at the monitor (3) ______ (wide)-eyed and curious.

We were told that logical thinking matters (4) ______ (much) than memorizing long texts. Working in pairs, we tried (5) ______ (write) a simple program that printed "Hello" on the display. (6) ______ we made several mistakes, the teacher encouraged us to keep testing our ideas.

By the end of the term, I realized programming was not as hard (7) ______ I had imagined. My partner and I even designed a mini game (8) ______ our classmates could play during breaks. The experience taught me that patience and creativity are just (9) ______ important as talent. I am considering (10) ______ (take) a deeper course next year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`a course on...` 关于……的课程，引出主题。

2. **答案：across**
   - **考点**：介词。`move across the screen` 在屏幕上移动，表从一侧到另一侧。

3. **答案：wide**
   - **考点**：合成形容词中的形容词形式。`wide-eyed` 睁大眼睛的，作伴随状语，用形容词而非副词。

4. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。句中有 `than`，`matters more than` 比……更重要，`much` 的比较级为 `more`。

5. **答案：to write**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`try to do sth.` 努力做某事（强调尽力尝试），此处表示尝试编写程序。

6. **答案：Although/Though**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导让步状语从句，“尽管我们犯了不少错误”，与主句“老师仍鼓励我们”形成让步关系。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定结构。`not as/so... as...` 不如……那样，中间用形容词原级 `hard`。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `a mini game`，从句缺地点状语，“同学们在课间可以玩的游戏”，用 `where`。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定结构。`as... as...` 和……一样，`just as important as talent` 与天赋同等重要。

10. **答案：taking**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`consider doing sth.` 考虑做某事，动名词作宾语。
