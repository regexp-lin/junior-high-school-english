# 04-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：小区垃圾分类 (Community Waste Sorting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last spring, our neighborhood started a waste-sorting project (1) ______ (call) "Green Home". Volunteers put four colored bins at the gate, and posters were put up (2) ______ (explain) what should go into each bin. At first, some residents felt the rules were too strict, (3) ______ they soon got used to the new habit.

Every weekend, student helpers (4) ______ (stand) beside the bins to guide elderly neighbors. "We have learned how (5) ______ (separate) kitchen waste from other rubbish," said Grandma Wang with a smile. The community office also organized small talks (6) ______ parents could ask questions freely.

Thanks to everyone's effort, the amount of mixed waste (7) ______ (drop) by 30 percent so far. The manager said they were planning (8) ______ (build) a compost corner for fallen leaves next month. I believe such programs make our city (9) ______ (clean) than it used to be. (10) ______ everyone plays a part, a greener future is possible.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`a project called "Green Home"` 过去分词作后置定语，表“被称为……的项目”，相当于定语从句 `which was called`。

2. **答案：to explain**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。张贴海报的目的是解释规则，不定式作目的状语。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句“起初觉得规则太严”与后句“很快习惯”为转折关系。

4. **答案：stood**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去周末反复发生的志愿活动，用过去式 `stood`。

5. **答案：to separate**
   - **考点**：“疑问词 + 不定式”结构。`learn how to separate...` 学会如何分离，作 `learn` 的宾语。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `talks`，在从句中作地点状语，“在讲座上家长们可以提问”，用 `where`。

7. **答案：has dropped**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。时间状语 `so far` 到目前为止，主语 `the amount` 为单数，用 `has dropped`。

8. **答案：to build**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`plan to do sth.` 计划做某事。

9. **答案：cleaner**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`make + 宾语 + 形容词比较级` 使城市比以前更干净。

10. **答案：If**
    - **考点**：从属连词。引导条件状语从句，“若每个人都出一份力”，句首首字母大写。
