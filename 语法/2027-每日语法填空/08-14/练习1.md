# 08-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑期旅行日记 (Summer Travel Diary)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, my family went on a trip to Yunnan Province. It was the ___1___ (long) journey I had ever taken — we spent two days on the train before we finally arrived ___2___ Kunming.

The weather in Kunming was lovely. It was neither too hot ___3___ too cold, which was perfect for sightseeing. On the first day, we visited the Stone Forest, a famous scenic spot ___4___ (know) for its amazing rock formations. The rocks looked like animals, trees and even people. I could not help ___5___ (take) hundreds of photos there.

On the second day, we drove to Dali. The old town was full ___6___ traditional buildings and small shops. My mother bought ___7___ beautiful silver bracelet from a local craftsman. In the evening, we sat by Erhai Lake and watched the sunset. The sky ___8___ (turn) red and gold, and the lake reflected the colours like a mirror. It was one of the most ___9___ (beauty) scenes I had ever seen.

Before leaving Yunnan, I wrote in my diary: "Travelling teaches us things that textbooks ___10___ (can) not. I hope I can visit more places in the future."

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：longest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**the + 最高级** 与后面的 `I had ever taken` 搭配，表示"我曾经历过的最长的旅程"，`long` 的最高级为 **longest**。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。**arrive in + 大地点**（到达某城市/国家），Kunming 为城市名，故填 **in**。

3. **答案：nor**
   - **考点**：连词。**neither... nor...**（既不……也不……），前面有 `neither`，对应填 **nor**。

4. **答案：known**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a famous scenic spot` 与 `know` 之间为被动关系（景点"被知道/以……闻名"），用过去分词 **known** 作后置定语，**be known for**（以……著名）。

5. **答案：taking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**can't help doing sth.**（忍不住做某事），`help` 后接动名词，故填 **taking**。

6. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。**be full of...**（充满……）为固定搭配，故填 **of**。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`bracelet`（手镯）为可数名词单数，此处泛指"一个漂亮的银手镯"，`beautiful` 以辅音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **a**。

8. **答案：turned**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去发生的事情用一般过去时，`turn` 在此为系动词，表示"变成"，故填 **turned**。

9. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。**the most + 多音节形容词** 构成最高级，`beauty` 的形容词形式为 **beautiful**，故 `the most beautiful scenes`。

10. **答案：cannot / can**
    - **考点**：情态动词。括号给出 `can`，原文为 `things that textbooks ___ not`，表示"课本不能教给我们的东西"，故填 **cannot**（或 **can** 配合后面的 `not` 一起表否定）。
