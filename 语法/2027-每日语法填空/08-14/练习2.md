# 08-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：野生动物保护区 (Wildlife Reserve)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, our geography teacher took us to visit a wildlife reserve in Sichuan Province. The reserve ___1___ (set) up in 2005 to protect giant pandas and other rare animals. It covers ___2___ area of over 200 square kilometres.

When we entered the reserve, a guide led us along a forest path. She told us ___3___ there were about 80 giant pandas living in this area. We walked ___4___ (quiet) so that we would not disturb them. After about thirty ___5___ (minute) of walking, we were lucky enough to spot a mother panda and her baby sitting under a tall bamboo tree. The baby panda was playing with a branch while ___6___ (it) mother was eating bamboo leaves nearby. The scene was so lovely ___7___ everyone took out their cameras at once.

The guide also explained that giant pandas were once in great danger of ___8___ (die) out. Thanks to years of protection efforts, their population ___9___ (increase) steadily. However, she warned that we should never stop protecting them because their natural habitat is still ___10___ (threat) by human activities such as farming and road building.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was set**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `The reserve` 与 `set up`（建立）之间为被动关系（保护区"被建立"），时间状语 `in 2005` 提示一般过去时，故填 **was set**（up）。

2. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。**cover an area of...**（覆盖……面积）为固定搭配，`area` 以元音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **an**。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词（宾语从句）。`told us` 后接宾语从句，从句 `there were about 80 giant pandas...` 结构完整，用 **that** 引导（可省略，但此处填空需要填写）。

4. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `walked` 需用副词，`quiet` 的副词形式为 **quietly**，表示"安静地走"。

5. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`thirty`（三十）后接可数名词复数，`minute` 的复数形式为 **minutes**。

6. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词（物主代词）。修饰名词 `mother` 需用形容词性物主代词，`it` 的形容词性物主代词为 **its**（注意不要与 it's 混淆）。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。**so + adj./adv. + that...**（如此……以至于……）为固定结构，引导结果状语从句，故填 **that**。

8. **答案：dying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**in danger of doing sth.**（处于做某事的危险中），介词 `of` 后接动名词，**die** 变动名词时去 e 加 -ing，故填 **dying**。

9. **答案：has increased**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`steadily`（稳步地）和语境表示从过去到现在持续增长的结果，用现在完成时；主语 `their population` 为第三人称单数，故填 **has increased**。

10. **答案：threatened**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 过去分词/形容词）。**be threatened by...**（被……威胁），`threat` 的动词形式为 `threaten`，此处用过去分词 **threatened** 与 `is` 构成被动语态。
