# 10-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：乡村振兴 (Rural Revitalization)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, many villages in China __1__ (change) a lot thanks to the rural revitalization plan. New roads __2__ (build) so that farmers can send fresh fruit to cities more quickly. Young people __3__ used to leave for big cities are now returning to start small businesses. My uncle runs a homestay __4__ tourists can experience farm life and taste local food. He always tells us __5__ honesty and hard work bring long-term success. Last summer, I helped him __6__ (post) photos of the golden rice fields online, and bookings doubled in a week. The village library, __7__ was once empty, is now full of children reading storybooks. Volunteers also teach seniors __8__ (use) smartphones safely. Everyone agrees that a stronger countryside means __9__ (good) lives for more people. I hope __10__ I can study agriculture science in the future and give back to my hometown.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have changed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。In recent years 常与现在完成时连用，强调“已经发生变化”，主语 villages 为复数，用 have changed。

2. **答案：have been built / were built**
   - **考点**：动词语态与时态。道路“被修建”，用被动语态；若强调结果与现在的关联用现在完成时 have been built；若仅叙述过去动作用 were built。

3. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：定语从句。先行词 people，从句缺主语（young people who used to leave），用关系代词 who。

4. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 homestay 表地点，从句 tourists can experience... 结构完整，用 where 作地点状语。

5. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。tells us 后接宾语从句，从句 honesty and hard work bring... 成分完整，用 that 引导（that 可省略，但填空题常填 that）。

6. **答案：(to) post**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help sb. (to) do sth. 帮助某人做某事，to 可省略，填 post 或 to post 均可。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句。先行词 library，非限制性定语从句缺主语，指物用 which。

8. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。teach sb. to do sth. 教某人做某事，用不定式 to use。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与 stronger 并列，且含“比以往更好”之意，用 good 的比较级 better。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：宾语从句。I hope 后接 that 引导的宾语从句（that 可省略，填空常填 that），表示“希望自己将来能……”。
