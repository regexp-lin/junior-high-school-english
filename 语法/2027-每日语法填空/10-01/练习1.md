# 10-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：国庆节 (National Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

October 1st is __1__ important day for every Chinese. On this day, people celebrate __2__ (we) country’s birthday with flags, songs, and family gatherings. Last year, I __3__ (watch) the flag-raising ceremony on TV with my grandparents. The soldiers marched so __4__ (care) that everyone felt proud. My cousin, __5__ studies history, told us stories about the founding of New China. We also planned __6__ (visit) Tian’anmen Square during the holiday, but the rain made us stay at home. __7__, we cooked a big meal together and talked about our dreams. Mom said that peace __8__ (not come) easily, so we should study hard and love our homeland. I promised __9__ (try) my best to be helpful at home and at school. When the evening fireworks lit up the sky, I whispered to myself, “Happy birthday, __10__ dear motherland!”

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`important` 以元音音素开头，表示“一个重要日子”用 an important day。

2. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 country，需用 our 表示“我们的国家”，不能用主格 we。

3. **答案：watched**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 Last year 表示过去，谓语用一般过去时 watched。

4. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 marched，用副词 carefully。

5. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 cousin 指人，从句缺主语，非限制性定语从句用 who。

6. **答案：to visit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。plan to do sth. 计划做某事，用不定式 to visit。

7. **答案：Instead / However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。上文说计划去广场，下文因雨在家，表转折或“相反”，用 Instead（相反）或 However（然而）。

8. **答案：didn’t come / does not come**
   - **考点**：动词时态与否定。若作客观真理“和平来之不易”，可用一般现在时 doesn’t come；若叙述妈妈当时说的话，可用过去时 didn’t come。中考常接受一般现在时表普遍道理。

9. **答案：to try**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。promise to do sth. 承诺做某事。

10. **答案：my**
    - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。与主语 I 呼应，对祖国说“我亲爱的祖国”，用 my dear motherland。
