# 07-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：一次特别的生日 (A Special Birthday)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday was my fourteenth birthday, but I thought nobody ___1___ (remember) it at all. My parents behaved as if the day were only ___2___ ordinary weekend: there was no birthday song at breakfast, no carefully wrapped box on my desk, just a long list of chores in the kitchen. ___3___ I had finished wiping the table, Mom looked at her watch and said we should drive to Grandma's house immediately. I nodded silently, guessing that nobody ___4___ (care) about how I felt inside.

The journey through the rainy fields felt endless. When we reached the old brick house, Grandma opened the heavy door ___5___ (slow), wearing a serious face. She took my hand and whispered that I should go straight into the dark sitting room. ___6___ I crossed the doorway, bright fairy lights flashed on and more than twenty classmates cheered, "Happy birthday!" Every wall ___7___ (cover) with handmade stars, and on a low table a chocolate cake ___8___ (stand), with small candles already burning.

Lily pressed a glittering card ___9___ (write) by the whole group into my arms. "Your mom and dad ___10___ (help) us keep the secret for days," she explained. I laughed and cried at the same time. That moment taught me that real love sometimes hides behind quiet routines.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：remembered**
   - **考点**：动词时态。主句为过去时 *thought*，宾语从句叙述“当时我认为过去已发生的事”，用一般过去时；*nobody* 作主语，谓语用过去式 *remembered*。

2. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。*ordinary* 以元音音素开头，且 *weekend* 为可数名词单数、表泛指，用不定冠词 *an*。

3. **答案：After**
   - **考点**：连词。表示“在擦完桌子之后”，引导时间状语从句，用 *After*（句首大写）。*When* 若表示“当……时”也可，但此处强调先后顺序 *After* 更贴切。

4. **答案：cared**
   - **考点**：动词时态。*guessing* 后为宾语从句，叙述过去的心理活动，用一般过去时 *cared*；*care about* 为固定搭配，表示“在乎”。

5. **答案：slowly**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 *opened*，用副词 *slowly*。

6. **答案：When / As**
   - **考点**：连词。引导时间状语从句，表示“当我跨过门槛时”，用 *When* 或 *As* 均可。

7. **答案：were covered**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。*walls* 与 *cover* 为被动关系，且全文为过去背景，用一般过去时的被动语态 *were covered*。

8. **答案：stood**
   - **考点**：动词时态。*stand* 表示“矗立、摆放”，此处叙述过去场景，用一般过去时 *stood*（与前面 *flashed*、*were covered* 一致）。

9. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。*card* 与 *write* 为被动关系，用过去分词 *written* 作后置定语，相当于 *a card which was written by...*。

10. **答案：helped**
    - **考点**：动词时态。直接引语中叙述过去已发生的动作，用一般过去时 *helped*；*help sb. (to) do sth.* 为固定结构。
