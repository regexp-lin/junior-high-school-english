# 07-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：绿色能源 (Green Energy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The world's rapidly growing need for electricity ___1___ (lead) scientists and engineers to hunt for cleaner solutions that keep skies blue above both crowded cities and quiet villages. Burning huge amounts of coal ___2___ (pollute) the sky with smog and harms people's health in many neighborhoods, ___3___ governments around the globe are racing to expand solar fields and wind corridors along coasts, deserts, and hills. These renewable tools are described as green energy because they usually cause ___4___ (little) harm to the soil and the ocean than traditional plants ___5___ operators follow safety rules and upgrade old machines on time.

Off the coast of my province, a modern wind farm ___6___ (complete) late last year after long talks with local fishers about sea routes; its tall white turbines ___7___ (catch) sea breezes day and night while computers track wind speed in the control room. Engineers explain that one rotation can ___8___ (generate) enough power for a family for several hours when the weather stays steady. Nearby schools organise visits ___9___ students can watch real-time data on a large screen, compare the figures with their textbooks, and ask questions freely about future jobs in clean technology. Of course, storing extra electricity in huge batteries remains difficult for many towns; ___10___ researchers keep testing new materials in the lab, hoping sunny and windy days will never be wasted again.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has led**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。表示从过去持续到现在的影响或结果，“不断增长的需求已经促使……”，主语 *need* 为单数，用 *has led*；*lead sb. to do sth.* 表示“促使某人做某事”。

2. **答案：pollutes**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。陈述客观事实，用一般现在时；动名词短语 *Burning huge amounts of coal* 作主语，视为单数，谓语用 *pollutes*。

3. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前后为因果关系：燃煤污染严重，因此各国政府争相发展太阳能与风能，用 *so* 连接两个分句。

4. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 *than traditional plants*，*little* 的比较级为 *less*，表示“更少的危害”。

5. **答案：when / if**
   - **考点**：连词。引导状语从句：在运营商遵守安全规则的前提下/当运营商遵守安全规则时，危害更小；*when* 表时间，*if* 表条件，中考中两者在此语境均可接受，更常见为 *when*。

6. **答案：was completed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。*wind farm* 与 *complete* 为被动关系，且时间状语 *late last year* 为过去，用一般过去时的被动语态 *was completed*。

7. **答案：catch**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。说明风机常态性“捕捉海风”，用一般现在时；主语 *turbines* 为复数，谓语用动词原形 *catch*。

8. **答案：generate**
   - **考点**：情态动词后动词形式。*can* 后接动词原形，故填 *generate*。

9. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句修饰 *visits*，从句中缺地点状语（在这些参观活动中学生可以……），用 *where*，相当于 *on which*。

10. **答案：yet / but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。前句说储存电力仍困难，后句说研究人员仍在实验室测试新电池，语义为转折，用 *yet*（常用于书面语转折）或 *but* 均可；分号后若用 *however* 通常需加逗号，故此处一词填空优先 *yet* 或 *but*。
