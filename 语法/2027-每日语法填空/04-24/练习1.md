# 04-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：纪录片之夜 (Documentary Night)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our English club hosts a documentary night (1) ______ a month in the multimedia room. Last Friday, we watched a film about ocean life (2) ______ was filmed by young divers. The pictures were so clear (3) ______ we felt as if we were swimming beside whales.

Before the screening, the host briefly introduced the director and explained (4) ______ the team had protected sea turtles during shooting. Many students took notes because they planned (5) ______ (write) short reviews for the school blog.

During the discussion, someone asked (6) ______ the crew had faced any danger. The teacher encouraged us to express opinions politely, even (7) ______ we disagreed with one another. I used to fall asleep during long videos, (8) ______ this film kept my eyes wide open from start to finish.

Documentaries help me see the world (9) ______ (wide) than textbooks alone. I am looking forward to (10) ______ (watch) the next episode with my friends.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：once**
   - **考点**：副词。`once a month` 每月一次，表频率。

2. **答案：which/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `film`，从句缺主语，用 `which` 或 `that`。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：固定句型。`so... that...` 如此……以至于……。

4. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。`explained` 后宾语从句，说明团队“如何”保护海龟，表方式。

5. **答案：to write**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`plan to do sth.` 计划做某事。

6. **答案：whether/if**
   - **考点**：连接词。`asked` 后宾语从句，“摄制组是否面临危险”，用 `whether` 或 `if`。

7. **答案：if/when**
   - **考点**：从属连词。`even if` 即使（意见不一致也要礼貌），或 `even when` 即使在……情况下；此处常用 `even if`。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“过去常睡着”与“这部片让我全程专注”为转折。

9. **答案：more widely**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`see` 为动词，用副词修饰；句中有 `than`，`wide` → `widely` 的比较级 `more widely`，表示“更广泛地”。

10. **答案：watching**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`look forward to doing sth.` 中 `to` 为介词，后接动名词。
