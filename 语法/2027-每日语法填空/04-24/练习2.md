# 04-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：手语学习 (Learning Sign Language)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last term, our class invited a teacher from the deaf community (1) ______ give us lessons in sign language. At first, moving our fingers (2) ______ (feel) awkward, but we practiced the alphabet every morning in front of mirrors.

The teacher told us that facial expressions matter (3) ______ (much) than many beginners realize. When she told a funny story without speaking, we laughed (4) ______ loudly as if we had heard every word. I was moved (5) ______ her patience and humor.

We also learned simple sentences (6) ______ "Thank you" and "How are you?" so that we could greet hearing-impaired neighbors in our building. On weekends, some volunteers (7) ______ (visit) a local cafe where staff use signs to take orders.

I used to feel shy about (8) ______ (make) mistakes, but my classmates always cheered me on. Now I understand (9) ______ communication does not depend only on sound. I hope more schools will offer courses (10) ______ this kind in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`invite sb. to do sth.` 邀请某人做某事，不定式作宾语补足语。

2. **答案：felt**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述上学期初学时的感受，用过去式 `felt`。

3. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。句中有 `than`，`matter more than` 比……更重要，`much` 的比较级为 `more`。

4. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定结构。`as... as if...` 此处为 `laughed as loudly as if`，`as` 与副词搭配引导方式；亦可理解为 `as loudly as` 的省略扩展，核心为与 `as if` 引导的从句呼应。

5. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`be moved by...` 被……感动。

6. **答案：like**
   - **考点**：介词。`sentences like...` 像……这样的句子，表举例。

7. **答案：visit**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。描述习惯性周末志愿活动，主语 `volunteers` 复数，用动词原形 `visit`。

8. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。介词 `about` 后接动名词。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。`understand` 后宾语从句，说明理解的内容，从句结构完整，用 `that` 引导。

10. **答案：of**
    - **考点**：介词。`courses of this kind` 这种课程，与 `this kind` 搭配用 `of`。
