# 12-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园广播站 (School Broadcasting Station)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I have been a member of the school broadcasting station __1__ the beginning of Grade 8. Every morning, my partner and I arrive at school fifteen minutes __2__ (early) than other students to prepare the programme. Our daily broadcast includes school news, weather reports and sometimes interesting __3__ (story) about science or history. __4__ (prepare) the content is not easy. We spend a lot of time __5__ (choose) topics that students will find useful or entertaining. I still remember my first broadcast __6__ (clear). My voice was shaking and I __7__ (make) several mistakes. But our adviser, Ms. Li, told me not __8__ (worry) because everyone feels nervous at the beginning. Over time, I have become much more confident. Many students tell me that they enjoy __9__ (listen) to our programme. It gives me a great sense of achievement. I believe this experience will be __10__ (value) for my future career.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词/连词。since the beginning of 自……以来，与现在完成时连用。

2. **答案：earlier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。earlier than 比……更早，than 提示用比较级。

3. **答案：stories**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。interesting stories 有趣的故事，story 的复数为 stories。

4. **答案：Preparing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。Preparing the content 准备内容，动名词短语作主语。

5. **答案：choosing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 花时间做某事，用动名词。

6. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 remember，用副词 clearly。

7. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述过去第一次广播时的情况，用一般过去时 made。

8. **答案：to worry**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。tell sb. not to do sth. 告诉某人不要做某事，否定不定式 not to worry。

9. **答案：listening**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。enjoy listening 喜欢听，enjoy 后接动名词。

10. **答案：valuable**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。be valuable 有价值的，be 后接形容词作表语。
