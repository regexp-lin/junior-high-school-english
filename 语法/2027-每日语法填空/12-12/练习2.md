# 12-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：候鸟迁徙 (Bird Migration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every autumn, millions of birds fly from northern regions to __1__ (warm) places in the south. This amazing journey __2__ (call) migration. Some birds travel thousands of kilometres __3__ (reach) their winter homes. Scientists have found that birds use the sun, stars and even the Earth's magnetic field to find __4__ (they) way. The bar-tailed godwit holds the record __5__ the longest non-stop flight — over 12,000 kilometres from Alaska to New Zealand! It is hard to imagine how __6__ a small bird can fly such a long distance __7__ stopping. Last year, I went to Poyang Lake in Jiangxi Province with my family __8__ (see) the migrating birds. Thousands of white cranes __9__ (gather) there at that time. They looked so graceful. My father told me that Poyang Lake is one of the most important __10__ (place) for migrating birds in the world. We should do our best to protect these wetlands.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：warmer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。warmer places 更温暖的地方，与北方的寒冷地区形成对比，暗含比较。

2. **答案：is called**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。journey 与 call 为被动关系，描述客观事实用一般现在时被动 is called。

3. **答案：to reach**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。travel... to reach 迁徙以到达，不定式作目的状语。

4. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。their way 它们的路，修饰名词用形容词性物主代词。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。the record for 关于……的纪录，for 表示"对于、就……而言"。

6. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：副词/形容词。such a small bird 如此小的一只鸟，such + a/an + 形容词 + 名词。

7. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。without stopping 不停歇地，without 表示"不、没有"。

8. **答案：to see**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。went to... to see 去……看，不定式作目的状语。

9. **答案：were gathering/had gathered**
   - **考点**：动词时态。at that time 在那时，描述当时正在进行的状态用过去进行时 were gathering 或已完成的聚集用过去完成时 had gathered。

10. **答案：places**
    - **考点**：名词单复数。one of the most important places 最重要的……之一，one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词。
