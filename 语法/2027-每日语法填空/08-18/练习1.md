# 08-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我的宠物 (My Pet)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Two years ago, my parents gave me a golden retriever puppy as a birthday present. I ___1___ (name) him Lucky because I felt I was the ___2___ (lucky) girl in the world to have such a lovely friend.

When Lucky first came to our home, he was very small and could ___3___ (hard) walk steadily. I had to feed him four ___4___ (time) a day and take him outside regularly. My mother was a little worried ___5___ having a dog in the house, but Lucky quickly won her heart with his big brown eyes and wagging tail.

Now Lucky has grown ___6___ a strong and energetic dog. Every morning, he wakes me up ___7___ licking my face. After school, he always waits for me at the door, ___8___ (wag) his tail excitedly. On weekends, I take him to the park ___9___ he can run freely and play with other dogs.

Taking care of a pet is not always easy. I have to clean up after him and make sure he ___10___ (stay) healthy. But the love and joy he brings to our family make everything worthwhile.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`Two years ago` 提示一般过去时，`name` 的过去式为 **named**。

2. **答案：luckiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**the + 最高级** 表示"最……的"，`lucky` 的最高级为 **luckiest**（以辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i 加 -est）。

3. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。情态动词 `could` 后接副词修饰动词 `walk`，**hard** 的副词形式 **hardly** 意为"几乎不"，表示小狗"几乎不能稳当地走路"。注意 hardly 与 hard（努力地）含义完全不同。

4. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`four`（四）后接可数名词复数，`time` 作"次数"讲时为可数名词，复数为 **times**。

5. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。**be worried about...**（对……感到担忧）为固定搭配，故填 **about**。

6. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。**grow into...**（成长为……）为固定搭配，表示"长成了一只强壮而精力旺盛的狗"。

7. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。**by doing sth.**（通过做某事），表示方式，"通过舔我的脸来叫醒我"，故填 **by**。

8. **答案：wagging**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。句中已有谓语 `waits`，逗号后为非谓语结构。主语 `he`（Lucky）与 `wag` 之间为主动关系，故用现在分词 **wagging** 作伴随状语。

9. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `the park` 为地点名词，从句 `he can run freely and play with other dogs` 中不缺主语或宾语，缺少地点状语，故用 **where**。

10. **答案：stays**
    - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。**make sure (that)...** 引导宾语从句，陈述经常性行为用一般现在时，主语 `he` 为第三人称单数，故填 **stays**。
