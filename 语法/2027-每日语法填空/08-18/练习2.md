# 08-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：丝绸之路 (The Silk Road)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Silk Road was an ancient network of trade routes ___1___ connected China with the West. It ___2___ (name) after silk, one of the most valuable goods ___3___ (trade) along the road. The Silk Road played an important role in the ___4___ (develop) of world civilisation.

The route stretched for thousands of kilometres, from Chang'an (today's Xi'an) all the way ___5___ the Mediterranean Sea. Merchants travelled along it ___6___ (carry) not only silk, but also spices, tea, porcelain and precious stones. The journey was long and ___7___ (danger), as travellers had to cross vast deserts and high mountains.

However, the Silk Road was much more than just a trade route. It also served as a bridge for cultural exchange. Through it, different ___8___ (country) shared their knowledge of science, art, religion and medicine. For example, papermaking technology ___9___ (spread) from China to the Arab world and then to Europe along this route.

Today, China has proposed the Belt and Road Initiative, ___10___ aims to build a modern version of the Silk Road. It is helping to strengthen trade and friendship between countries once again.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `trade routes` 指物，在从句中作主语，故用关系代词 **that** 或 **which**。

2. **答案：was named**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `It`（指 The Silk Road）与 `name` 之间为被动关系（"被命名为"），叙述历史事实用一般过去时被动语态，故填 **was named**。

3. **答案：traded**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`goods` 与 `trade` 之间为被动关系（货物"被交易"），用过去分词 **traded** 作后置定语，修饰 `goods`。

4. **答案：development**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。定冠词 `the` 后接名词，**develop** 的名词形式为 **development**，表示"世界文明的发展"。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**from... to...**（从……到……）表示路线的起点和终点，`all the way to` 意为"一直到"。

6. **答案：carrying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。句中已有谓语 `travelled`，非谓语结构表伴随。主语 `Merchants` 与 `carry` 之间为主动关系，故用现在分词 **carrying** 作伴随状语。

7. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。系动词 `was` 后接形容词作表语，与 `long` 并列，**danger** 的形容词形式为 **dangerous**。

8. **答案：countries**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different`（不同的）修饰可数名词复数，`country` 的复数形式为 **countries**（以辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i 加 -es）。

9. **答案：spread**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述历史事件用一般过去时；`spread` 为不规则动词，过去式与原形相同，仍为 **spread**。

10. **答案：which**
    - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `the Belt and Road Initiative`（"一带一路"倡议）指物，逗号后为非限制性定语从句，在从句中作主语，故用 **which**。
