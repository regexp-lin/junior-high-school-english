# 01-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：图书馆的魅力 (The Magic of Libraries)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Libraries have always been special places (1) ______ people can explore new ideas and discover different worlds through books. Our city library, which (2) ______ (build) over fifty years ago, is still one of the most popular places in the neighbourhood. Every Saturday morning, you can see children and adults (3) ______ (sit) quietly in the reading room, completely absorbed in their books. Last month, the library introduced a new programme (4) ______ (call) "Reading Buddies." It pairs older students with (5) ______ (young) ones to help them improve their reading skills. My sister joined the programme as a volunteer, and she has been helping a seven-year-old boy named Xiao Jun ever since. At first, Xiao Jun was too shy (6) ______ (read) aloud. He would whisper the words so (7) ______ (soft) that my sister could hardly hear him. But after several weeks of practice, he became much more (8) ______ (confidence). Now he even reads stories to his classmates during break time. The library also hosts various events, including book fairs, author talks and writing (9) ______ (compete). These activities encourage people of all (10) ______ (age) to develop a love for reading. I believe that libraries will continue to play an important role in our lives for many years to come.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `places`，在定语从句中作地点状语，用 `where`。

2. **答案：was built**
   - **考点**：被动语态。图书馆"被建造"，`over fifty years ago` 表过去时。

3. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`see sb doing sth` 表示看到某人正在做某事。

4. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a programme called "Reading Buddies"`，被动关系。

5. **答案：younger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`older students` 与 `younger ones` 形成对比。

6. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：不定式。`too shy to read aloud`，`too...to...` 结构。

7. **答案：softly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `whisper` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：confident**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`became much more confident`，系动词 `became` 后接形容词。

9. **答案：competitions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。与 `book fairs` 和 `author talks` 并列，用复数形式。

10. **答案：ages**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`people of all ages` 为固定搭配，表示"所有年龄段的人"。
