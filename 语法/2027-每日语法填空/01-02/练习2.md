# 01-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：滑冰初体验 (My First Ice Skating Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I will never forget my first ice skating experience. It happened during the winter holiday when I (1) ______ (be) twelve years old. My cousin invited me to go skating at an outdoor rink near her home. I had never tried ice skating before, so I felt both excited and (2) ______ (nerve). When we arrived at the rink, I was amazed by the (3) ______ (beauty) scene — hundreds of tiny lights hung above the ice, making everything look magical. My cousin helped me put (4) ______ my skates and led me onto the ice. The moment I stepped onto the rink, my legs started shaking. I held onto the side rail (5) ______ (tight) and moved forward very slowly. Meanwhile, my cousin skated around me (6) ______ (effort), doing spins and jumps as if she (7) ______ (do) it all her life. After about thirty minutes of practice, I finally managed (8) ______ (skate) a short distance without falling. It was one of the (9) ______ (proud) moments of my life! By the end of the afternoon, I could skate across the rink without (10) ______ (hold) onto anything. That experience taught me that trying new things may be scary at first, but the reward is always worth it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的经历，主语 `I` 对应 `was`。

2. **答案：nervous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `excited` 并列，系动词 `felt` 后接形容词 `nervous`。

3. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `scene` 需要形容词形式。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`put on` 表示"穿上"，为固定短语。

5. **答案：tightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `held` 需要副词形式。

6. **答案：effortlessly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `skated` 需要副词，`effort` → `effortlessly`（毫不费力地）。

7. **答案：had been doing**
   - **考点**：过去完成进行时。`as if she had been doing it all her life`，表示"好像她一辈子都在做"，虚拟语气中用过去完成进行时。

8. **答案：to skate**
   - **考点**：不定式。`manage to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"设法做到"。

9. **答案：proudest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the proudest moments`，"最……之一"用最高级。

10. **答案：holding**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后接动名词形式。
