# 11-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国陶瓷 (Chinese Ceramics)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China is known __1__ the birthplace of ceramics. In fact, the English word "china" itself __2__ (mean) porcelain. The art of making ceramics dates back about 10,000 years. Jingdezhen, a city in Jiangxi Province, has been called the "Porcelain Capital" __3__ the Song Dynasty. The blue-and-white porcelain produced there is famous __4__ (world). Last summer, I visited Jingdezhen with my parents. We watched craftsmen __5__ (shape) clay on spinning wheels. Their __6__ (move) were quick and precise. One craftsman told us it takes at least five years __7__ (master) the basic techniques. We also visited a museum __8__ displayed ancient ceramic pieces from different dynasties. The colours and patterns on the porcelain were __9__ (extreme) beautiful. I bought a small blue-and-white teacup __10__ a souvenir. Every time I drink tea from it, I think of the amazing craftsmen in Jingdezhen.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。be known as 被称为……，为固定搭配。

2. **答案：means**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。主语 the English word "china" 为第三人称单数，描述事实用一般现在时，用 means。

3. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。since the Song Dynasty 自宋朝以来，与现在完成时连用。

4. **答案：worldwide**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→副词/形容词）。is famous worldwide 在世界范围内闻名，world 的副词/形容词形式为 worldwide。

5. **答案：shape / shaping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。watch sb. do/doing sth. 看某人做/正在做某事，shape 或 shaping 均可。

6. **答案：movements**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。their movements 他们的动作，move 的名词形式为 movement，复数为 movements。

7. **答案：to master**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It takes + 时间 + to do sth. 做某事花费多长时间，用不定式 to master。

8. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 a museum 指物，从句缺主语，用 that 或 which。

9. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 beautiful，用副词 extremely。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：介词。as a souvenir 作为纪念品，as 表示"作为"。
