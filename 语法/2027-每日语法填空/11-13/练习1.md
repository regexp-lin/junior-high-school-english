# 11-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬季运动 (Winter Sports)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Winter sports have become __1__ (increase) popular in China since the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics. Skiing and ice skating are now enjoyed __2__ millions of people. Last December, my family went to a ski resort in Hebei Province. It was my first time __3__ (ski), and I was both excited and nervous. A coach taught me the basic __4__ (skill): how to balance on the skis, how to stop and how to turn. At first, I fell down so __5__ (frequent) that I lost count. My younger brother, __6__ had learned skiing before, moved down the slope __7__ (smooth) like a professional. I felt a little embarrassed, __8__ I did not give up. By the end of the day, I could ski down a beginner's slope __9__ falling. My father said he was proud of my __10__ (determine). I plan to practise more this winter and try intermediate slopes next time!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→副词）。修饰形容词 popular，用副词 increasingly。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。enjoyed by millions of people 被数百万人喜爱，by 表示被动的施动者。

3. **答案：to ski / skiing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It was one's first time to do sth. / doing sth. 第一次做某事，两者均可。

4. **答案：skills**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。basic skills 基本技巧，此处指多项技能，用复数 skills。

5. **答案：frequently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。so frequently that 如此频繁以至于，修饰动词 fell down。

6. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 brother 指人，非限制性定语从句缺主语，用 who。

7. **答案：smoothly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 moved down，用副词 smoothly。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说有点尴尬，后句说没有放弃，语义转折，用 but。

9. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。without falling 没有摔倒，用 without + 动名词表示否定伴随。

10. **答案：determination**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。my determination 我的决心，determine 的名词形式为 determination。
