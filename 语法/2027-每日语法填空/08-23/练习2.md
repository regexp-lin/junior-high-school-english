# 08-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：茶文化 (Tea Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tea is the most ___1___ (wide) consumed drink in the world after water. China, ___2___ is regarded as the birthplace of tea, has a tea-drinking history of over four thousand years.

According to legend, tea ___3___ (discover) by Emperor Shennong around 2737 BC. One day, while he was boiling water under a tree, some leaves fell into the pot. The water turned into a pleasant drink, and the emperor ___4___ (enjoy) it so much that he asked people to learn more about the plant.

There are many different ___5___ (type) of Chinese tea, including green tea, black tea, oolong tea and white tea. Each type has its own special flavour and health ___6___ (benefit). For example, green tea is believed ___7___ (be) good for the heart, while oolong tea may help with weight control.

Tea is not just a drink in China — it is also ___8___ important part of social life. When guests come to visit, offering a cup of tea is a way of ___9___ (show) respect and friendliness. The Chinese tea ceremony, which involves carefully preparing and serving tea, is considered one of the finest ___10___ (express) of Chinese culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰过去分词 `consumed` 需用副词，**wide** 的副词形式 **widely** 表示"广泛地"。`the most widely consumed` 为最高级结构。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `China` 为专有名词，逗号后为非限制性定语从句，在从句中作主语，故用 **which**。

3. **答案：was discovered**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `tea` 与 `discover` 之间为被动关系（茶"被发现"），时间 `around 2737 BC` 提示一般过去时被动语态，故填 **was discovered**。

4. **答案：enjoyed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去的传说故事用一般过去时，`enjoy` 的过去式为 **enjoyed**。

5. **答案：types**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many different`（许多不同的）修饰可数名词复数，`type` 的复数形式为 **types**。

6. **答案：benefits**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`health benefits`（健康益处），`benefit` 作可数名词，此处与 `flavour` 并列且指多种功效，用复数 **benefits**。

7. **答案：to be**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be believed to do sth.**（被认为……），故填 **to be**。

8. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`important` 以元音音素开头，`part` 为可数名词单数，表示"一个重要组成部分"需用不定冠词 **an**。

9. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**a way of doing sth.**（做某事的方式），介词 `of` 后接动名词，故填 **showing**。

10. **答案：expressions**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）+ 名词复数。**one of the finest + 复数名词**（最精美的……之一），**express** 的名词形式为 **expression**，用复数 **expressions**。
