# 08-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园义工日 (School Volunteer Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has a tradition of holding a Volunteer Day every October. On that day, all students go out of the campus and do something ___1___ (mean) for the community. This year, our class was assigned to visit a care home for the elderly.

We arrived at the care home at nine o'clock, each of us ___2___ (carry) a small gift. The old people there welcomed us ___3___ (warm) and seemed very happy to see young faces. Some of my classmates helped clean the rooms ___4___ others sat down and chatted with the elderly.

I spent most of my time with an 85-year-old man ___5___ (name) Mr. Chen. He told me stories about his life when he was young. He said that he ___6___ (work) as a teacher for over forty years before he retired. I was deeply ___7___ (move) by his dedication to education.

Before we left, we performed a few songs and a short play for the old people. The ___8___ (perform) made them laugh and some even had tears in ___9___ (they) eyes. Mr. Chen held my hand and said, "Thank you for coming. Your visit is the best medicine for ___10___ (lonely)."

That day taught me that giving is always more rewarding than receiving.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：meaningful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。不定代词 `something` 后接形容词作后置定语，**mean** 的形容词形式 **meaningful**（有意义的）。

2. **答案：carrying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（独立主格结构）。`each of us` 与主句主语不同，构成独立主格；`us` 与 `carry` 为主动关系，故用现在分词 **carrying**。

3. **答案：warmly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `welcomed` 需用副词，**warm** 的副词形式为 **warmly**。

4. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。`Some... ___ others...`（一些人……另一些人……）表示两组人同时做不同的事，用 **while**（然而/与此同时）连接两个并列分句。

5. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`an 85-year-old man` 与 `name` 之间为被动关系（老人"被叫做"），用过去分词 **named** 作后置定语。

6. **答案：had worked**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`said`（过去时）引导的宾语从句中，"当了四十多年老师"发生在"说"之前，即过去的过去；`for over forty years` 表示持续性动作，用过去完成时 **had worked**。

7. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。**be deeply moved by...**（被……深深感动），修饰人的感受用 **-ed** 形式，故填 **moved**。

8. **答案：performance**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。定冠词 `The` 后接名词，**perform** 的名词形式为 **performance**，作句子主语。

9. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（物主代词）。修饰名词 `eyes` 需用形容词性物主代词，指代 `the old people`（复数），故填 **their**。

10. **答案：loneliness**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 名词）。介词 `for` 后接名词，**lonely** 的名词形式为 **loneliness**（孤独），表示"治愈孤独的最好良药"。
