# 03-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：植树节 (Tree Planting Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

March 12th is China's Tree Planting Day, a day when millions of people across the country plant trees to help protect the environment. The tradition (1) ______ (begin) in 1979 when the Chinese government decided to designate a special day for tree planting. Since then, China has planted (2) ______ (billion) of trees, making it one of the (3) ______ (green) countries in the world in terms of reforestation efforts. Last year, our school organized a tree-planting activity in a park near the river. We were divided (4) ______ groups of four, and each group was responsible for planting three trees. Our teacher showed us how to dig the hole deep enough (5) ______ the roots could spread properly. It was harder (6) ______ I expected — the soil was dry and full of rocks. My arms ached after digging for just ten minutes. But when I finally placed the young tree in the ground and covered its roots with soil, I felt a great sense of (7) ______ (satisfy). Trees are essential for life on Earth. They absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen, helping to fight climate (8) ______ (changeable). They also prevent soil erosion and provide habitats for countless species of (9) ______ (wild). Scientists estimate that a single tree can absorb about 22 kilograms of carbon dioxide per year. By (10) ______ (plant) more trees, we are investing in a greener and healthier future for the next generation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：began**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`in 1979` 表示过去的时间。

2. **答案：billions**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`billions of` 数十亿的。

3. **答案：greenest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the greenest countries`。

4. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be divided into` 被分成。

5. **答案：so that**
   - **考点**：连词。`deep enough so that the roots could spread`，引导目的状语从句。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较级。`harder than I expected` 比我预期的更难。

7. **答案：satisfaction**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a sense of satisfaction` 满足感，名词。

8. **答案：change**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`climate change` 气候变化，名词。

9. **答案：wildlife**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`species of wildlife` 野生动物物种。

10. **答案：planting**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `By` 后用动名词。
