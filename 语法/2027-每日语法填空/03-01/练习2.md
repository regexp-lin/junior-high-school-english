# 03-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：小小发明家 (Young Inventor)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Thirteen-year-old Chen Xiao has always been fascinated (1) ______ how things work. When he was just eight, he took apart his mother's alarm clock to see what was inside — and (2) ______ (success) put it back together. Since then, he (3) ______ (create) over twenty small inventions, ranging from a solar-powered phone charger to an automatic plant-watering system. His most recent invention is a smart schoolbag that can weigh (4) ______ (it) and warn students when it is too heavy. The idea came to him after he noticed that many of his classmates complained about back pain caused (5) ______ carrying heavy bags. Chen Xiao spent three months (6) ______ (design) and building the prototype. He used a small pressure sensor connected to a tiny screen on the strap. When the weight exceeds 5 kilograms, the screen displays a warning message. The invention won first prize at the city-level Youth (7) ______ (Innovate) Competition last November. Several companies have expressed interest in (8) ______ (produce) the smart schoolbag commercially. Chen Xiao's story is an inspiration to young people everywhere. It shows that age is not a barrier (9) ______ creativity and that even the (10) ______ (simple) observations about daily life can lead to meaningful inventions.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be fascinated by` 被……迷住。

2. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`successfully put it back together` 成功地重新组装。

3. **答案：has created**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 从那以后到现在。

4. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`weigh itself` 自动称重。

5. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`caused by` 由……引起的。

6. **答案：designing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

7. **答案：Innovation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`Youth Innovation Competition` 青年创新大赛，名词。

8. **答案：producing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`interest in doing sth.` 对做某事感兴趣。

9. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`a barrier to` 对……的障碍。

10. **答案：simplest**
    - **考点**：最高级。`even the simplest observations` 即使最简单的观察。
