# 07-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电子竞技 (E-Sports)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

E-sports, or electronic sports, ___1___ (become) a billion-dollar industry in recent years. Professional gamers compete in tournaments watched ___2___ millions of fans online and in stadiums. In 2022, e-sports was included as a medal event at the Asian Games, ___3___ gave it official recognition as a competitive sport. Some people think e-sports is just ___4___ (play) video games, but professional gamers train as ___5___ (hard) as traditional athletes. They practise for over ten hours a day and follow strict diets to keep their minds ___6___ (sharply). Top players need excellent reaction speed, teamwork and strategic ___7___ (think). However, parents and teachers often worry that teenagers may spend too much time ___8___ (game) and neglect their studies. My school has a clear rule: gaming is only allowed after homework ___9___ (finish). I enjoy watching e-sports matches, but I know the difference ___10___ entertainment and professional competition. Balance is the key.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in recent years 提示用现在完成时，e-sports 视为单数概念，填 has become。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。watched by millions 表示"被数百万人观看"，填 by。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事（被列入亚运会），用 which。

4. **答案：playing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。is just playing 表示"只是在玩"，也可理解为动名词作表语，填 playing。

5. **答案：hard**
   - **考点**：副词原级。as...as 结构中用原级，train as hard as 表示"和……一样刻苦训练"，填 hard。

6. **答案：sharp**
   - **考点**：词性转换。keep their minds sharp 表示"保持头脑敏锐"，keep + 宾语 + 形容词，sharply → sharp。

7. **答案：thinking**
   - **考点**：词性转换。strategic thinking 为名词短语，表示"策略性思维"，think → thinking。

8. **答案：gaming**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 或 spend time gaming，game → gaming。

9. **答案：is finished**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。homework 与 finish 之间为被动关系，after 引导时间状语从句，用一般现在时的被动语态表条件，填 is finished。

10. **答案：between**
    - **考点**：介词。the difference between A and B 表示"A 与 B 之间的区别"，填 between。
