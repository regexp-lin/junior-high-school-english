# 07-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园心理健康 (Mental Health at School)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mental health is just as important ___1___ physical health, especially for teenagers. However, many students feel embarrassed about ___2___ (talk) about their emotions. A survey conducted by our school showed that nearly forty percent of students ___3___ (experience) stress or anxiety at some point during the semester. The ___4___ (main) causes include exam pressure, conflicts with friends and family problems. To help students cope, the school has set up a counselling room ___5___ students can talk to trained teachers in private. Every Wednesday, a ___6___ (profession) psychologist visits the school and offers free sessions. My friend Li Na used to feel very anxious before exams. After ___7___ (attend) several counselling sessions, she learned some useful relaxation ___8___ (technique), such as deep breathing and positive self-talk. Now she handles exam stress much ___9___ (well) than before. Experts remind us that asking for help is not a sign of weakness — it is a sign of ___10___ (strong).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。as important as 表示"和……一样重要"，just as...as 结构，填 as。

2. **答案：talking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。about 为介词，后接动名词，feel embarrassed about talking 表示"对谈论感到尴尬"，填 talking。

3. **答案：had experienced**
   - **考点**：动词时态。at some point during the semester 表示在调查之前某段时间经历的，调查本身是过去的事，用过去完成时 had experienced。

4. **答案：main**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 causes，main 本身就是形容词，表示"主要的原因"，填 main。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 a counselling room（地点），在从句中作地点状语，用 where。

6. **答案：professional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 psychologist，需用形容词形式，profession → professional。

7. **答案：attending**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。after 为介词，后接动名词，after attending 表示"参加之后"，填 attending。

8. **答案：techniques**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。some 后接可数名词复数，technique → techniques。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。much + 比较级 + than，well → better。

10. **答案：strength**
    - **考点**：词性转换。a sign of + 名词，strong → strength。
