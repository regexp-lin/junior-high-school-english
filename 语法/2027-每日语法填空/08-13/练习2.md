# 08-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市交通问题 (Urban Traffic Problems)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traffic jams have become one of the biggest ___1___ (problem) in many large cities. Every morning and evening, thousands of cars, buses and trucks fill the streets, ___2___ (cause) long delays for people who are trying to get to work or school.

There are several reasons ___3___ traffic is getting worse. First, the number of private cars ___4___ (grow) rapidly over the past ten years. Many families now own at least one car. Second, some roads were built many years ago and they are too ___5___ (narrow) to handle so much traffic. Third, some drivers do not follow traffic rules, which makes the situation even ___6___ (bad).

To solve these problems, city governments have taken different measures. ___7___ example, some cities have built more subway lines so that people can travel ___8___ (quick) underground instead of driving. Others have set up special lanes for buses to encourage people ___9___ (use) public transport.

As students, we can also do our part. Walking or riding a bicycle to school is not ___10___ good for the environment but also good for our health. If everyone makes a small change, our cities will become less crowded in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：problems**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词**（最……的……之一），`problem` 需用复数形式 **problems**。

2. **答案：causing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。句中已有谓语 `fill`，逗号后无并列连词，需用非谓语。主语 `cars, buses and trucks` 与 `cause` 之间为主动关系，故用现在分词 **causing** 作结果状语。

3. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `reasons`（原因）后接定语从句，从句 `traffic is getting worse` 中不缺主语和宾语，表示原因用 **why**。

4. **答案：has grown / has been growing**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**over the past ten years**（在过去十年里）提示使用现在完成时；主语 `the number of private cars` 看作整体，为第三人称单数，故填 **has grown**（也可用现在完成进行时 **has been growing** 强调持续增长）。

5. **答案：narrow**
   - **考点**：词性辨析。**too + 形容词/副词 + to do sth.**（太……以至于不能……），此处 `narrow` 本身已是形容词，括号中给出的即为正确形式 **narrow**，无需变形。

6. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**even + 比较级** 表示"更加……"，`bad` 的比较级为不规则变化 **worse**。

7. **答案：For**
   - **考点**：介词。**For example**（例如）为固定搭配，句首首字母大写，故填 **For**。

8. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `travel` 需用副词，`quick` 的副词形式为 **quickly**，表示"快速地出行"。

9. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**encourage sb. to do sth.**（鼓励某人做某事），故填 **to use**。

10. **答案：only**
    - **考点**：副词。固定结构 **not only... but also...**（不仅……而且……），此处 `not` 已给出，空格处需填 **only** 与后面的 `but also` 构成完整结构。
