# 08-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家庭聚餐 (Family Dinner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every Sunday evening, my whole family gets together for a big dinner. It is ___1___ tradition that has lasted for as long as I can remember. No matter how busy we are during the week, we always make time ___2___ this special meal.

Usually, my grandmother is the one ___3___ cooks for us. She is good at ___4___ (make) traditional dishes, such as braised pork and steamed fish. The food she makes always ___5___ (taste) amazing. My mother often helps her in the kitchen, while my father sets the table and pours drinks for everyone.

Last Sunday, I decided ___6___ (cook) a dish by myself for the first time. I chose tomato and egg stir-fry because it seemed ___7___ (easy) than other dishes. Although the tomatoes were cut into pieces of different ___8___ (size), the final result was not bad at all. My grandparents were ___9___ (please) and said it was the most delicious tomato and egg dish they had ever tasted. I knew they were just being kind, ___10___ their words still made me very happy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`tradition` 为可数名词单数，此处泛指"一个传统"，`tradition` 以辅音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **a**。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。**make time for...**（为……腾出时间）为固定搭配，故填 **for**。

3. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `the one` 指人，在从句中作主语，故用关系代词 **who** 或 **that**。

4. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be good at doing sth.**（擅长做某事），介词 `at` 后接动名词，故填 **making**。

5. **答案：tastes**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。叙述习惯性动作用一般现在时；主语 `The food` 为第三人称单数，故填 **tastes**。`taste` 在此为系动词，后接形容词 `amazing`。

6. **答案：to cook**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**decide to do sth.**（决定做某事），故填 **to cook**。

7. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**than** 提示比较级，`easy` 的比较级为 **easier**（以辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i 加 -er）。

8. **答案：sizes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**different**（不同的）修饰可数名词复数，`size` 的复数形式为 **sizes**，表示"不同大小"。

9. **答案：pleased**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。系动词 `were` 后接形容词作表语，修饰人的感受用 **-ed** 形式，故填 **pleased**（感到高兴的）。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：连词。前半句说"我知道他们只是客气"，后半句说"他们的话仍然让我很开心"，前后为转折关系，故用 **but**。
