# 02-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：敬老院探访 (Visiting a Nursing Home)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Sunday, our class visited a nursing home as part of a community service project. When we arrived, about thirty elderly residents (1) ______ (sit) in the garden, enjoying the sunshine. Some of them looked lonely because they rarely received (2) ______ (visit) from family members. We had prepared several activities to brighten their day. First, a group of students performed traditional songs and dances, (3) ______ brought big smiles to the elderly people's faces. Then, some of us sat down with the residents to chat and listen to their stories. I talked with an 85-year-old woman (4) ______ (name) Grandma Chen. She told me about her life as a teacher for over forty years. Her stories were so (5) ______ (fascinate) that I completely lost track of time. Another group of students helped the (6) ______ (nurse) with daily tasks, such as organizing the library and cleaning the common areas. Before leaving, we gave each resident a handmade card with a warm message (7) ______ (write) inside. Many of them were moved to tears. On the bus ride home, I reflected on the experience. It made me realize that elderly people do not need expensive gifts — they simply need someone (8) ______ (spend) time with them and show them that they are not (9) ______ (forget). I have decided to visit the nursing home at (10) ______ once a month from now on.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：were sitting**
   - **考点**：过去进行时。到达时老人们正坐在花园里。

2. **答案：visitors / visits**
   - **考点**：名词。`received visitors/visits` 收到来访者/探望。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面的表演。

4. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a woman named Grandma Chen` 一位叫陈奶奶的女士。

5. **答案：fascinating**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`so fascinating` 如此迷人的，修饰故事。

6. **答案：nurses**
   - **考点**：名词复数。帮助护士们做日常工作。

7. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a message written inside` 写在里面的信息。

8. **答案：to spend**
   - **考点**：不定式作定语。`someone to spend time with them`。

9. **答案：forgotten**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`are not forgotten` 没有被遗忘，被动语态。

10. **答案：least**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`at least` 至少。
