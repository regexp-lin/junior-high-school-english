# 02-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：烹饪课 (Cooking Class)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This semester, our school introduced a new elective course — cooking. It quickly became one of the (1) ______ (popular) courses among students. The class is taught by a professional chef (2) ______ used to work at a five-star hotel. Each week, we learn to prepare a different dish. So far, we (3) ______ (learn) how to make fried rice, tomato and egg soup, and even pizza. The teacher always begins by explaining the importance of food (4) ______ (safe) and hygiene. We must wash our hands thoroughly and wear aprons before (5) ______ (touch) any ingredients. Last week, we learned to make sweet and sour pork. The teacher demonstrated each step (6) ______ (patient) while we watched carefully. Then it was our turn to try. I was nervous about cutting the meat because I had never used (7) ______ kitchen knife before. Fortunately, the teacher showed me the correct technique, and I managed to do it without (8) ______ (hurt) myself. The cooking process took about forty minutes. When I finally tasted my own dish, I was surprised at how (9) ______ (good) it was. My classmates and I agreed that learning to cook is an essential life skill (10) ______ everyone should have. I now help my mother in the kitchen every weekend, and she is very pleased with my progress.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the most popular courses`，多音节形容词。

2. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `chef`，指人。

3. **答案：have learned / have learnt**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`So far` 到目前为止。

4. **答案：safety**
   - **考点**：名词作定语。`food safety` 食品安全。

5. **答案：touching**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `before` 后用动名词。

6. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`demonstrated patiently` 耐心地演示。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a kitchen knife` 一把菜刀，泛指。

8. **答案：hurting**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后用动名词。

9. **答案：good**
   - **考点**：形容词。`how good it was` 感叹句/宾语从句中用形容词原级。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `skill`，先行词被 `essential` 修饰时常用 `that`。
