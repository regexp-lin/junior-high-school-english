# 02-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：寒假旅行 (Winter Holiday Trip)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last winter holiday, my family took a trip to Harbin, the capital city of Heilongjiang Province. We had never (1) ______ (be) to northeastern China before, so everything was new and exciting for us. The temperature dropped to minus 25 degrees on the day we arrived, which was much (2) ______ (cold) than anything I had ever experienced. My mother wrapped (3) ______ (she) in three layers of clothing, but she still felt freezing. The highlight of our trip was (4) ______ (visit) the famous Ice and Snow World. Hundreds of huge ice (5) ______ (sculpture) were displayed across the park, each one (6) ______ (light) up with colourful lights. I was particularly impressed by a life-sized ice castle that took over 200 workers nearly a month (7) ______ (build). We also tried some local food, including (8) ______ traditional Harbin sausage that my father could not stop eating. On our last day, we went skiing at a nearby resort. I fell down many times, (9) ______ I still had a wonderful time. The trip taught me that stepping out of your comfort zone and (10) ______ (try) new things is always worth it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：been**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`had never been to` 从未去过。

2. **答案：colder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much colder than`，`much` 修饰比较级。

3. **答案：herself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语和宾语一致时用反身代词。

4. **答案：visiting**
   - **考点**：动名词作表语。`The highlight was visiting...`。

5. **答案：sculptures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`Hundreds of` 后接可数名词复数。

6. **答案：lit / lighted**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`each one lit up`，独立主格结构，被点亮。

7. **答案：to build**
   - **考点**：固定句型。`It takes sb. time to do sth.`，此处变形用法。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指一种传统哈尔滨香肠。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。摔倒多次但仍然很开心，表转折。

10. **答案：trying**
    - **考点**：动名词。与 `stepping` 并列，`stepping... and trying...` 作主语。
