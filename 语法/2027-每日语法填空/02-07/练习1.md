# 02-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：无人机技术 (Drone Technology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Drones, also known as unmanned aerial vehicles, have changed the way we live and work. Originally (1) ______ (develop) for military purposes, drones are now widely used in agriculture, photography, delivery and even entertainment. In agriculture, farmers use drones to monitor their crops from the sky. The drones can quickly identify areas (2) ______ plants are unhealthy and need attention. This saves farmers a great deal of time compared (3) ______ walking through large fields on foot. Delivery drones are another exciting (4) ______ (develop). Several companies (5) ______ (test) drone delivery services in recent years, allowing customers to receive packages within minutes of (6) ______ (place) an order. However, there are still some challenges that need to (7) ______ (solve) before drone delivery becomes common. Safety is the biggest concern — no one wants a heavy package (8) ______ (fall) from the sky onto their head. In China, drone light shows have become a popular replacement (9) ______ traditional fireworks at major events. Thousands of drones fly in formation, creating (10) ______ (amaze) patterns and images in the night sky without producing any smoke or noise pollution.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：developed**
   - **考点**：过去分词作状语。`Originally developed for military purposes`，被开发。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，修饰 `areas`，表示地点。

3. **答案：to / with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`compared to / with` 与……相比。

4. **答案：development**
   - **考点**：词性转换。前有形容词 `exciting`，需用名词形式。

5. **答案：have been testing / have tested**
   - **考点**：现在完成进行时或现在完成时。`in recent years` 提示。

6. **答案：placing**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后用动名词。

7. **答案：be solved**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`need to be solved` 需要被解决。

8. **答案：falling / to fall**
   - **考点**：`want sth. doing / to do`。`want a package falling/to fall`。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`a replacement for` 对……的替代。

10. **答案：amazing**
    - **考点**：-ing 形容词。修饰物，`amazing patterns` 令人惊叹的图案。
