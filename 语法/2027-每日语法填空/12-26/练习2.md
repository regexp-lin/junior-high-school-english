# 12-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：制作新年贺卡 (Making New Year Cards)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As the New Year approaches, our art teacher asked us __1__ (design) handmade greeting cards for our family and friends. She showed us several __2__ (example) and gave us coloured paper, glitter and markers. I decided to make a card for my grandmother, who lives in __3__ (distance) city. I drew a big red lantern on the front cover because lanterns __4__ (symbol) good luck in Chinese culture. Inside the card, I wrote a heartfelt message __5__ (thank) her for always caring about me. I also attached a small photo of our family __6__ was taken during last year's Spring Festival. My classmate Zhang Yu made an __7__ (amaze) pop-up card with a 3D flower that opens __8__ you unfold the card. Everyone was impressed by his __9__ (create) design. At the end of the class, we put all our cards on display. It was wonderful to see so __10__ (many) beautiful cards full of love and good wishes. I can't wait to send mine to my grandmother.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to design**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾补）。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式。

2. **答案：examples**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。several examples 几个示例，several 后接复数名词。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。a distant city 一座遥远的城市，city 为可数名词单数，首次提及用 a。

4. **答案：symbolise**
   - **考点**：动词时态。lanterns symbolise 灯笼象征，描述客观事实用一般现在时，lanterns 为复数，动词用原形。

5. **答案：thanking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作定语/状语）。a message thanking her 一条感谢她的信息，message 与 thank 为主动关系，用现在分词。

6. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。that/which 引导定语从句修饰 photo，在从句中作主语。

7. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ing 形容词）。an amazing pop-up card 一张令人惊叹的弹出式贺卡，修饰事物用 -ing 形容词。

8. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：时间连词。when you unfold 当你打开的时候，when 引导时间状语从句。

9. **答案：creative**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。creative design 有创意的设计，修饰名词用形容词。

10. **答案：many**
    - **考点**：形容词。so many cards 如此多的贺卡，so many + 可数名词复数。
