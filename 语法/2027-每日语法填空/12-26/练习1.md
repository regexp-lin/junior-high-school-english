# 12-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校篮球联赛 (School Basketball League)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's basketball league has been running __1__ three weeks now. Every class has a team, and the games take place every Tuesday and Thursday afternoon. I play __2__ a guard on our class team. Our coach, Mr. Liu, is very strict but also very __3__ (support). He makes us practise dribbling and shooting for an hour __4__ each game. In yesterday's match, we played __5__ Class 7, the strongest team in our grade. At half-time, we were losing by ten points. Everyone felt __6__ (disappoint), but Mr. Liu told us to stay calm and focus on __7__ (defend). In the second half, we played much __8__ (hard) and slowly closed the gap. With only thirty __9__ (second) left, I scored a three-point shot that tied the game! The crowd went wild. We eventually won in overtime. It was the most __10__ (thrill) game I have ever played. I learned that never giving up can create miracles.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for three weeks 持续三周，for 表示时间段。

2. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。play as a guard 担任后卫，as 表示"作为"。

3. **答案：supportive**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。very supportive 非常支持人的，be 后接形容词作表语。

4. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：介词。before each game 每场比赛之前，before 表示时间先后。

5. **答案：against**
   - **考点**：介词。play against 与……对阵，against 表示"对抗"。

6. **答案：disappointed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ed 形容词）。felt disappointed 感到沮丧，修饰人的感受用 -ed 形容词。

7. **答案：defence/defending**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词/名词。focus on defence/defending 专注于防守，on 为介词，后接名词或动名词。

8. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。played much harder 打得更努力，much 修饰比较级。

9. **答案：seconds**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。thirty seconds 三十秒，基数词后接复数名词。

10. **答案：thrilling**
    - **考点**：词性转换（-ing 形容词）。the most thrilling game 最激动人心的比赛，修饰事物用 -ing 形容词。
