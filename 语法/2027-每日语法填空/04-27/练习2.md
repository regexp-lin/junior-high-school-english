# 04-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：研学旅行 (Educational Travel)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Educational travel combines learning with real-world experience. Last month, our school arranged a three-day trip (1) ______ a famous university and two museums. The bus left at dawn (2) ______ we could avoid heavy traffic.

On the first day, a professor gave a lecture (3) ______ teenagers could ask questions about future careers. I took notes (4) ______ (care) because I hoped the information would help me choose subjects wisely.

On the second day, we visited a history museum (5) ______ ancient tools were displayed in glass cases. The guide explained how people in the past (6) ______ (solve) problems without modern machines. I was surprised (7) ______ creative they were.

During the trip, we were asked (8) ______ (work) in groups and finish a short report. My teammates and I argued a little at first, (9) ______ we learned to listen to each other. I used to dislike homework during holidays, (10) ______ this journey felt different because it connected books with life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`a trip to...` 去……的旅行。

2. **答案：so that/in order that**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导目的状语从句，“黎明出发以便避开拥堵”，用 `so that`。

3. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `lecture`，从句中缺时间状语，“在讲座期间青少年可提问”，用 `when`。

4. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `took`，`care` → `carefully` 副词。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `museum`，从句中缺地点状语，“在博物馆里展出”，用 `where`。

6. **答案：solved**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`in the past` 叙述过去人们如何解决问题，用过去式 `solved`。

7. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。`be surprised how...` 惊讶于他们“多么”有创造力，引导宾语从句。

8. **答案：to work**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be asked to do sth.` 被要求做某事。

9. **答案：yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。书面语中表转折，“起初有些争执，然而学会倾听”，`yet` 与 `but` 近义。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。“曾不喜欢假期作业”与“这次旅行不同”为转折。
