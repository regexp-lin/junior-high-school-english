# 04-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：劳动节前夕 (Before Labour Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The week before Labour Day is always busy in our neighborhood. Shopkeepers clean windows, gardeners trim trees, (1) ______ volunteers paint old benches in the park. My parents and I joined a community team (2) ______ organized a charity sale on Sunday morning.

We arrived at six o'clock (3) ______ set up tables near the gate. Many neighbors brought clothes, books, and toys (4) ______ they no longer needed. A boy about my age carried two heavy boxes (5) ______ (help) his grandmother.

The money we raised (6) ______ (send) to a local children's home after the holiday. I felt tired after standing for hours, (7) ______ I was proud of what we had done. "Labour is not only about strength (8) ______ also about love," my mother said.

That evening, fireworks lit up the sky, (9) ______ everyone stopped to watch. I realized that holidays become (10) ______ (warm) when people work together for others.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。三个并列分句列举节前忙碌场景，最后一项前用 `and` 连接。

2. **答案：which/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `team`，从句缺主语，用 `which` 或 `that`。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`arrived` 与 `set up` 为并列谓语，表顺承。

4. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `clothes, books, and toys`，从句缺宾语，指物，可用 `that` 或 `which`。

5. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，“搬重箱以帮助祖母”。

6. **答案：would be sent/was sent**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。善款“在假期后被送往”，叙述过去计划或安排，可用 `would be sent`（过去将来时被动）或 `was sent`（一般过去时被动）。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“站了数小时很累”与“为所做自豪”为转折。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but also...` 不仅……而且……。

9. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。连接前后两个分句，“烟花点亮夜空，大家驻足观看”，表顺承。

10. **答案：warmer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`become` 后接形容词比较级，暗含与独自过节对比，“更温暖”，`warm` → `warmer`。
