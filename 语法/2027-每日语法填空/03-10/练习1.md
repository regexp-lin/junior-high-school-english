# 03-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：地球一小时 (Earth Hour)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Earth Hour is a worldwide event that takes place on the last Saturday of March every year. During this event, people are encouraged to turn (1) ______ their lights for one hour from 8:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m. The goal is to raise (2) ______ (aware) about climate change and the need to protect our planet.

The first Earth Hour (3) ______ (hold) in Sydney, Australia in 2007. Since then, it (4) ______ (grow) into one of the largest environmental events in the world. Every year, millions of people in over 190 countries take part in this activity.

Last Saturday, our family (5) ______ (join) Earth Hour for the first time. We turned off all the lights and electronic devices in the house. At first, it felt strange (6) ______ (sit) in the dark. But soon we began to enjoy the (7) ______ (peace) atmosphere. We sat by the window and looked at the stars, something we (8) ______ (rare) do in our busy daily lives.

My little sister asked me (9) ______ turning off lights for just one hour could really help the Earth. I told her that Earth Hour is not only about saving energy for sixty (10) ______ (minute). It is about reminding people that every small action counts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：off**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`turn off` 关闭（灯、电器等）。

2. **答案：awareness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`raise awareness` 提高意识，名词形式。

3. **答案：was held**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。`in 2007` 过去时间，活动是"被举办的"。

4. **答案：has grown**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 从那时到现在。

5. **答案：joined**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last Saturday` 过去时间标志词。

6. **答案：to sit / sitting**
   - **考点**：不定式 / 动名词作主语。`it felt strange to sit / sitting in the dark`，`it` 作形式主语。

7. **答案：peaceful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `atmosphere`，`peace` → `peaceful` 形容词。

8. **答案：rarely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `do`，`rare` → `rarely` 副词，表"很少"。

9. **答案：if / whether**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。一般疑问句变宾语从句用 `if/whether`。

10. **答案：minutes**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`sixty minutes` 六十分钟。
