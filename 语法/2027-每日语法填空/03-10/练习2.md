# 03-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：邻里关系 (Neighbourhood Relationships)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In today's fast-paced world, many people hardly know their neighbours. They come home, close the door and live in (1) ______ (they) own little world. However, good neighbourhood relationships can make our lives much (2) ______ (happy).

I am lucky to live in a community (3) ______ neighbours care about each other. Mrs. Zhang, who lives next door, often brings us fresh dumplings on weekends. Mr. Li across the street (4) ______ (voluntary) helps elderly people carry heavy bags when they come back from the market.

Last winter, something happened that made me realize how important good neighbours are. One evening, my parents were both at work and I was home alone when the water pipe suddenly (5) ______ (burst). Water was everywhere and I didn't know what (6) ______ (do). I ran to Mrs. Zhang's door and knocked (7) ______ (anxious). She immediately came over with her husband and they quickly fixed the pipe.

Without their help, the (8) ______ (situate) would have been much worse. After that experience, I began to make more effort to be a good neighbour (9) ______. I help carry packages, water plants and always greet people with a (10) ______ (warm) smile. Building good relationships with our neighbours is truly worth the effort.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。`their own little world` 他们自己的小世界。

2. **答案：happier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much happier` 更快乐，`much` 修饰比较级。

3. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句修饰 `community`，表地点。

4. **答案：voluntarily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `helps`，`voluntary` → `voluntarily` 副词。

5. **答案：burst**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`burst` 的过去式与原形相同，爆裂。

6. **答案：to do**
   - **考点**：不定式。`know what to do` 知道该做什么，疑问词 + 不定式。

7. **答案：anxiously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `knocked`，`anxious` → `anxiously` 副词。

8. **答案：situation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the situation` 情况，名词形式。

9. **答案：myself / too / as well**
   - **考点**：反身代词 / 副词。`a good neighbour myself` 我自己也做一个好邻居。

10. **答案：warm**
    - **考点**：形容词。修饰名词 `smile`，用形容词 `warm`（注意不是词性转换，`warmly` 不修饰名词）。
