# 01-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：地铁出行 (Travelling by Metro)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The metro has become one of the most popular forms of public transport in many Chinese cities. Guangzhou opened (1) ______ (it) first metro line in 1997, and since then, the network (2) ______ (expand) to cover almost every corner of the city. Today, millions of passengers rely on the metro (3) ______ (get) to work, school and other destinations every day. One of the biggest (4) ______ (advantage) of the metro is its speed. Because metro trains run underground, they are not affected (5) ______ traffic jams on the surface. A journey that might take an hour by bus can often be completed in just twenty minutes by metro. The metro is also much (6) ______ (environment) friendly than private cars, as it produces far (7) ______ (few) carbon emissions per passenger. However, riding the metro during rush hour can be quite an experience. The carriages are often so (8) ______ (crowd) that there is barely enough room to stand. People squeeze in at every stop, and getting off at your station requires careful planning and quick (9) ______ (move). Despite the discomfort of peak-hour travel, I still prefer the metro to other forms of transport. It is cheap, reliable and allows me to read or listen to music during my journey. I hope that more cities in China will continue (10) ______ (build) metro systems to make public transport more accessible for everyone.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：物主代词。`its first metro line`，表示广州的。

2. **答案：has expanded**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`since then` 为现在完成时标志。

3. **答案：to get**
   - **考点**：不定式。`rely on the metro to get to`，不定式表目的。

4. **答案：advantages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the biggest advantages`，"最大的优势之一"。

5. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`be affected by` 为固定搭配，表示"受到……影响"。

6. **答案：environmentally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `friendly` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`far fewer carbon emissions`，`emissions` 为可数名词，用 `fewer`。

8. **答案：crowded**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so crowded that...`，系动词后接形容词。

9. **答案：movement / movements**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`quick movement`，形容词后接名词。

10. **答案：to build**
    - **考点**：不定式。`continue to do sth` 表示"继续做某事"。
