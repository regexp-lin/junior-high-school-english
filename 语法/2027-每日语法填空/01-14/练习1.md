# 01-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：时间管理 (Time Management)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Time is one of our most valuable resources, yet many students struggle with managing it (1) ______ (effective). How often do you find yourself rushing to finish homework at the last minute or staying up late because you spent too much time (2) ______ (scroll) through your phone? Poor time management not only affects your academic (3) ______ (perform) but also increases stress and reduces the quality of your sleep. The good news is that time management is a skill that can (4) ______ (learn) and improved with practice. Here are some tips that have helped me become more (5) ______ (product). First, make a to-do list every evening for the next day. Writing down your tasks helps you see (6) ______ needs to be done and allows you to prioritise. Second, break large tasks (7) ______ smaller, more manageable pieces. Instead of trying to write an entire essay in one sitting, set a goal of writing just one paragraph at a time. Third, avoid multitasking. Studies have shown that people who try to do several things at once are actually (8) ______ (little) efficient than those who focus on one task at a time. Finally, remember to schedule regular breaks. Your brain works (9) ______ (good) when it has time to rest and recharge. By following these simple strategies, you will find that there are enough hours in the day to complete your work and still have time for the activities you enjoy (10) ______ (much).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：effectively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `managing` 需要副词形式。

2. **答案：scrolling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：performance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`academic performance`，形容词后接名词。

4. **答案：be learned / be learnt**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`can be learned`，情态动词后接动词原形被动。

5. **答案：productive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`become more productive`，系动词后接形容词。

6. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`see what needs to be done`，`what` 引导宾语从句。

7. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`break...into...` 表示"把……分成……"。

8. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：比较级。`less efficient than`，`than` 为标志。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`works better when it has time to rest`，暗含与不休息时的比较。

10. **答案：most**
    - **考点**：最高级。`the activities you enjoy most`，副词最高级不加 `the`。
