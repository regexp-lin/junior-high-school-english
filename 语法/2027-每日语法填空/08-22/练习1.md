# 08-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：爷爷的菜园 (Grandpa's Vegetable Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My grandpa has a small vegetable garden behind his house. He has been ___1___ (work) in it every day since he retired five years ago. To him, the garden is not just a place to grow food — it is a source of joy and ___2___ (happy).

Every spring, Grandpa plans carefully what ___3___ (plant). He usually grows tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers and several kinds of leafy greens. He never uses chemical fertilisers ___4___ pesticides. Instead, he makes his own compost from kitchen waste, which he says is much ___5___ (safe) for the soil.

I often visit him on weekends and help with the garden work. He has taught me how ___6___ (water) the plants properly — not too much and not too little. He also taught me to pull out weeds by ___7___ (they) roots so that they do not grow back. Although the work can be tiring, I always feel ___8___ (relax) after spending time in the garden.

The ___9___ (good) part of having a vegetable garden is eating what you grow. There is nothing ___10___ (fresh) than a tomato picked straight from the vine. Grandpa always says, "If you treat the earth well, the earth will treat you well in return."

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：working**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**has been + doing**（现在完成进行时），表示从过去开始一直持续到现在并可能继续的动作，`since he retired five years ago` 为时间标志，故填 **working**。

2. **答案：happiness**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 名词）。`a source of joy and ___` 中 `and` 连接两个并列名词，**happy** 的名词形式为 **happiness**。

3. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**what to do**（做什么），疑问词 + 不定式结构作宾语，故填 **to plant**。

4. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。否定句中，`fertilisers ___ pesticides`（化肥还是农药）用 **or** 连接并列成分，表示"既不用化肥也不用农药"。

5. **答案：safer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**much + 比较级** 表示"……得多"，`safe` 的比较级为 **safer**。

6. **答案：to water**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**how to do sth.**（如何做某事），疑问词 + 不定式结构作宾语，故填 **to water**。

7. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（物主代词）。修饰名词 `roots` 需用形容词性物主代词，指代 `weeds`（复数），故填 **their**。

8. **答案：relaxed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。系动词 `feel` 后接形容词作表语，修饰人的感受用 **-ed** 形式，故填 **relaxed**（感到放松的）。

9. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**The + 最高级 + part**（最……的部分），`good` 的最高级为不规则变化 **best**。

10. **答案：fresher**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。**nothing + 比较级 + than...**（没有什么比……更……），`fresh` 的比较级为 **fresher**。此句式等同于最高级含义。
