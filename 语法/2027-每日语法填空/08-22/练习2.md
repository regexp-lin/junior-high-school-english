# 08-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：极地探险 (Polar Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Polar exploration is one of the most challenging ___1___ (adventure) in human history. The Arctic and Antarctic regions are extremely cold, with temperatures sometimes dropping below ___2___ (ninety) degrees Celsius. Yet brave explorers have risked their ___3___ (life) to discover the secrets of these frozen lands.

In 1911, Norwegian explorer Roald Amundsen became the first person ___4___ (reach) the South Pole. He and his team spent months ___5___ (travel) across the ice sheet, facing fierce winds and terrible snowstorms. Their success was largely due ___6___ careful planning and the use of sled dogs.

Today, polar exploration ___7___ (carry) out mainly by scientists rather than adventurers. They build research stations in both the Arctic and Antarctic ___8___ they can study climate change, wildlife and the ice itself. China has ___9___ (successful) set up several research stations in Antarctica, including the Zhongshan Station and the Great Wall Station.

The polar regions are often ___10___ (call) "the last wilderness on Earth". They hold important clues about our planet's past and future. Protecting these areas from pollution and overexploitation is a responsibility that belongs to all of us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：adventures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**one of the most + 形容词最高级 + 复数名词**（最……的之一），`adventure` 的复数形式为 **adventures**。

2. **答案：ninety**
   - **考点**：数词。此处 `ninety` 在括号中给出，表示"零下九十度"，数词作定语修饰 `degrees`，保持原形 **ninety**。

3. **答案：lives**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**risk one's life**（冒生命危险），主语为复数 `explorers`，对应 `life` 的复数形式 **lives**（以 -fe 结尾，变 f 为 v 加 -es）。

4. **答案：to reach**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**the first person to do sth.**（第一个做某事的人），序数词后用不定式作后置定语，故填 **to reach**。

5. **答案：travelling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth.**（花时间做某事），故填 **travelling**。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**be due to...**（由于……）为固定搭配，表示原因，故填 **to**。

7. **答案：is carried**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。`Today`（如今）提示一般现在时；主语 `polar exploration` 为第三人称单数，与 `carry out` 之间为被动关系（探险"被进行"），故填 **is carried**（out）。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `the Arctic and Antarctic` 为地点，从句中不缺主语或宾语，缺少地点状语，故用 **where**。

9. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `set up` 需用副词，**successful** 的副词形式为 **successfully**。

10. **答案：called**
    - **考点**：动词语态。主语 `The polar regions` 与 `call` 之间为被动关系（极地"被称为"），与 `are` 构成一般现在时被动语态，故填 **called**。
