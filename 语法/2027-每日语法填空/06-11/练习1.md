# 06-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家庭烹饪 (Cooking at Home)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Cooking is a useful life skill that every teenager should learn. Last Sunday, I decided ___1___ (cook) dinner for my family for the first time. I chose to make tomato and egg noodles because it seemed like the ___2___ (simple) dish. First, I washed the tomatoes and cut ___3___ into small pieces. Then I cracked two eggs into a bowl and beat them ___4___ (quick). When the oil in the pan was hot enough, I poured ___5___ eggs in. Everything went well ___6___ I tried to stir-fry the tomatoes — they splashed everywhere! My mother stood beside me, ___7___ (give) me advice patiently. After about twenty minutes, the noodles ___8___ (be) finally ready. They didn't look as pretty ___9___ my mother's, but my family said they were delicious. That experience taught me that cooking requires both skill and ___10___ (patient).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to cook**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。decide to do sth. 表示"决定做某事"，填 to cook。

2. **答案：simplest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"最简单的菜"，simple → simplest。

3. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词。cut 后接宾格代词作宾语，指代前文的 tomatoes（复数），填 them。

4. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 beat，需用副词形式，quick → quickly。

5. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。特指前面提到的打好的鸡蛋，用定冠词 the。

6. **答案：until**
   - **考点**：连词。Everything went well until... 表示"一切顺利直到……"，引导时间状语从句，填 until。

7. **答案：giving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，stood beside me, giving me advice...，填 giving。

8. **答案：were**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。the noodles 为复数主语，叙述过去事件用一般过去时，be → were。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。as...as... 表示"和……一样"，not as pretty as 表示"不如……好看"，填 as。

10. **答案：patience**
    - **考点**：词性转换。and 连接两个并列名词，skill 与 patience 并列，patient → patience。
