# 06-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：人工智能 (Artificial Intelligence)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is ___1___ (rapid) changing our world. From voice assistants on our phones ___2___ self-driving cars on the road, AI technology is everywhere. Many people are excited about ___3___ AI can do. For example, AI programmes can now write essays, translate languages and even compose music. In hospitals, AI helps doctors make more ___4___ (accuracy) diagnoses by ___5___ (analyse) large amounts of medical data. However, not everyone is happy about these ___6___ (develop). Some workers worry that AI will take ___7___ (they) jobs in the future. Teachers are also concerned ___8___ students might use AI to cheat on homework. The truth is, AI is just a tool — it depends ___9___ how we use it. If we learn to work with AI ___10___ of fearing it, we can achieve things that were impossible before.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 changing，需用副词形式，rapid → rapidly。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。from A to B 表示"从 A 到 B"，填 to。

3. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连词。what 引导宾语从句，作 do 的宾语，表示"AI 能做什么"，填 what。

4. **答案：accurate**
   - **考点**：词性转换。more + 形容词构成比较级，修饰名词 diagnoses，accuracy → accurate。

5. **答案：analysing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。by 为介词，后接动名词，表示"通过分析"，analyse → analysing。

6. **答案：developments**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。these 后接可数名词复数，develop → development → developments。

7. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 jobs，用形容词性物主代词，they → their。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。be concerned that... 表示"担心……"，that 引导宾语从句，填 that。

9. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。depend on 表示"取决于"，固定搭配，填 on。

10. **答案：instead**
    - **考点**：副词。instead of 表示"而不是"，与 fearing it 搭配，填 instead。
