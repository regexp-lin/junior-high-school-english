# 04-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园话剧表演 (School Play Performance)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Putting on a school play takes courage and teamwork. This spring, our grade chose a short comedy about a robot (1) ______ learns to understand human feelings. The story sounded simple, but turning it into a live show was (2) ______ real challenge.

Auditions were held in March. Dozens of students tried out for the leading roles, and only eight (3) ______ (choose) in the end. I was surprised (4) ______ my best friend got the part of the robot because she used to be shy.

For six weeks, we rehearsed after school. The director, (5) ______ was our English teacher, asked us to memorize every line. Sometimes we argued about costumes, (6) ______ we never gave up on one another.

On the night of the performance, the hall was full. When the curtain rose, my heart beat so fast (7) ______ I forgot my nervousness. The audience laughed and clapped at the right moments.

After the show, parents said it was the (8) ______ (funny) play they had seen this year. I learned that stage fright is natural, (9) ______ practice can turn fear into confidence.

Next year, I want to work behind the scenes (10) ______ (design) the lights. Even small tasks can make a big difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `robot`，从句缺主语。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a real challenge` 一个真正的挑战。

3. **答案：were chosen**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。八人“被选中”，`eight` 指人，复数，用 `were chosen`。

4. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：连接词。`be surprised when ...` 当……时感到惊讶。

5. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句修饰人 `director`，用 `who`。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。有时争论但从不放弃，转折用 `but`。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：固定句型。`so ... that ...` 如此……以至于……。

8. **答案：funniest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the ... they had seen this year` 表范围，用最高级 `funniest`。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。怯场很自然，但练习能把恐惧变自信。

10. **答案：to design**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`want to do` 想要做；`work ... to design` 表目的用 `to design`。
