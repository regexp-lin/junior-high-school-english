# 04-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：太极拳 (Tai Chi)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tai chi is a traditional Chinese martial art that looks slow but requires great balance. Many elderly people practice it in parks every morning, (1) ______ they believe it helps them stay flexible and calm.

Last month, our community center offered free lessons for teenagers. At first, I thought the movements were too easy (2) ______ be useful. However, after ten minutes, my legs began (3) ______ (shake) and I understood why it is called "moving meditation".

The coach told us to breathe deeply and imagine that we (4) ______ (push) an invisible ball. Every gesture has a name (5) ______ a story behind it. Although I could not remember all the steps, I enjoyed the peaceful music.

Scientists say tai chi may improve sleep and reduce stress. Compared with running, it puts (6) ______ (little) pressure on the knees, (7) ______ makes it suitable for people of different ages.

Now I practice twice a week with my grandfather. He moves more smoothly than I do, (8) ______ he has learned for thirty years. I hope this art (9) ______ (pass) on to more young people in the future.

If you want to try it, find (10) ______ experienced teacher and start with the basics.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。后句解释老人晨练的原因。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定结构。`too ... to ...` 太……而不能……，此处“太简单而……”表误以为太简单以至于没用。

3. **答案：to shake**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`begin to do sth.` 开始做某事，腿开始发抖，用 `to shake`。

4. **答案：were pushing**
   - **考点**：过去进行时。想象当时正在推一个无形的球，与 `told` 呼应，用 `were pushing`。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。每个动作有名字和故事，并列用 `and`。

6. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与跑步相比对膝盖压力“更小”，`little` → `less`。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句，用 `which`。

8. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。动作更流畅是因为学了三十年。

9. **答案：will be passed / can be passed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。艺术“被传承”，表将来用 `will be passed`。

10. **答案：an**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。`experienced` 以元音音素开头，用 `an`。
