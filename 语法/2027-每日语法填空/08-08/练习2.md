# 08-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：减少空气污染 (Reducing Air Pollution)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Air pollution has become ___1___ serious problem in many cities around the world. Thick smog often covers the sky, ___2___ makes it hard for people to breathe freely. Experts warn that if we do nothing to deal with it, the situation will get ___3___ (bad) in the coming years.

___4___ (fortunate), the good news is that governments and citizens are beginning to work together. More buses and subways are provided so that people can choose public transport instead ___5___ driving private cars every day. In some areas, factories ___6___ (ask) to reduce waste gas, and new rules have been introduced to control the use of coal. Some cities have also planted thousands of trees along busy roads to help absorb dust and noise.

As students, we can ___7___ (contribution) to cleaner air in our daily life. For example, we should save electricity at home because power plants may produce more ___8___ (pollute) when they work. Walking or cycling to school is another simple way when the distance is not too far. Besides, planting more trees in our neighborhood helps take in harmful gases and makes the environment ___9___ (clean). Even small actions will add up if everyone takes them seriously.

In my opinion, clean air is something we must fight for together. We should change our old habits ___10___ (complete) and never lose hope. Only in this way can we hope to see more blue skies in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`problem` 为可数名词单数，此处泛指“一个严重的环境问题”，`serious` 以辅音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **a**。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。前面整句“浓雾常常笼罩天空”作先行内容，后面从句缺主语且前有逗号，为非限制性定语从句指代整个主句，用 **which**（不可用 that 引导此类非限制性定语从句指代前文整句）。

3. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`get` 在此为系动词，后接形容词；语境表示“若不治理，情况会变得更糟”，含与现在比较的意味，故用 **bad** 的比较级 **worse**。

4. **答案：Fortunately**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰整个句子“好消息是……”，作评注性状语，需用副词 **Fortunately**（“幸运的是”）；句首首字母大写。

5. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **instead of** 表示“而不是”，后接动名词 `driving`，故填 **of**。

6. **答案：are asked / have been asked**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。主语 `factories` 与动词 ask 之间为被动关系（工厂“被要求”减排）；叙述现状可用一般现在时被动 **are asked**；强调已经出台的规定也可用现在完成时被动 **have been asked**。中考中 **are asked** 更常见。

7. **答案：contribute**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 动词）。情态动词 **can** 后接动词原形；**contribution** 的动词形式为 **contribute**，与 **to cleaner air** 构成 **contribute to...**（为……做贡献）。

8. **答案：pollution**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。`produce` 后需接名词作宾语；**pollute** 的名词形式为 **pollution**（不可数），表示“产生更多污染”。

9. **答案：cleaner**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。结构 **make + 宾语 + 宾补**，语境暗含“种树使环境比以前更干净”，故用 **clean** 的比较级 **cleaner**。

10. **答案：completely**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 **change** 需用副词；**complete** 的副词形式为 **completely**，表示“彻底地改变旧习惯”。
