# 08-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园艺术节 (School Art Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, our school held ___1___ wonderful art festival in the school hall. It lasted for two whole days, and hundreds of students took part ___2___ it with great interest. The theme of this year's festival was "Youth and Dreams", ___3___ (mean) that we should be brave enough to turn our ideas into real actions.

On the first morning, the headmaster gave ___4___ opening speech. He said that art could help us express our feelings and understand each other ___5___ (deep). After that, different clubs set up their booths along the corridor. My best friend Tom, ___6___ (take) photos of our school almost every week, showed a series of works about campus life. Many visitors stopped ___7___ (watch) his exhibition and asked him questions about how he took those pictures.

In the afternoon, there was a talent show on the stage. Some students played ___8___ violin, while others performed modern dance. What impressed me most was a short play ___9___ (write) by students in Grade Nine; it told a story about friendship. The actors spoke clearly and the whole audience laughed ___10___ (happy) from time to time.

When the festival ended, I felt a little tired, but I was sure that such activities make our school life more colorful and meaningful.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`festival` 为可数名词单数，此处表示泛指“一届精彩的校园艺术节”，`wonderful` 以辅音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **a**（不用 an）。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **take part in** 表示“参加（活动）”，`it` 指代前面的 `art festival`，因此填 **in**。

3. **答案：meaning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。句中已有谓语 `was`，且无并列连词，逗号后需用非谓语。主语 `The theme` 与动词 mean 之间为主动关系（主题“意味着……”），故用现在分词 **meaning** 作伴随/补充说明，相当于 which means。

4. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`speech` 可数单数，`opening` 以元音音素开头，表示“一场开幕致辞”需用不定冠词 **an**。

5. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 **understand** 需用副词；**deep** 的副词形式为 **deeply**，表示“更深入地（相互理解）”。

6. **答案：taking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。主句已有谓语 `showed`，`Tom` 与 `take` 之间为主动关系（Tom 主动拍照），故用现在分词 **taking** 构成非限制性定语，相当于非限制性定语从句 who takes... 的省略形式。

7. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定结构 **stop to do sth.** 表示“停下来（手头的事）去做另一件事”。此处指游客停下脚步去看展览，故用 **to watch**；区别于 stop doing（停止正在做的事）。

8. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。演奏西洋乐器前通常加定冠词 **the**，如 **play the violin / the piano**，为固定用法。

9. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a short play` 与 `write` 之间为被动关系（剧本“被撰写”），故用过去分词 **written** 作后置定语，相当于定语从句 which was written... 的省略形式；分号后另起一句说明内容，避免与 written 结构冲突。

10. **答案：happily**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 **laughed** 需用副词，**happy** 的副词形式为 **happily**。
