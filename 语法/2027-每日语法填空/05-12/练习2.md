# 05-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：露天电影 (Outdoor Movie Night)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When summer evenings turn cool, our community sets up 1. ______ outdoor movie night in the square. Volunteers carry chairs, test the projector, 2. ______ hang a white screen between two trees. Last Friday, families arrived early 3. ______ (save) good seats. Children ran around until the host asked them 4. ______ (sit) down quietly. The film was about 5. ______ brave dog that helped a lost boy find home. People laughed 6. ______ (warm) at the funny scenes and shared popcorn. When the wind blew stronger, someone brought 7. ______ extra blankets for the elderly. I did not expect 8. ______ night under the stars to feel 9. ______ (magic). After the movie ended, neighbors thanked each other 10. ______ small favors. Moments like these make a city feel smaller and kinder.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**outdoor** 以元音音素开头，**an outdoor movie night**，故填 **an**。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。三个并列谓语，最后一项用 **and** 连接，故填 **and**。

3. **答案：to save**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的“为了占到好座位”，故填 **to save**。

4. **答案：to sit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**ask sb. to do sth.**，故填 **to sit**。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a brave dog**，故填 **a**。

6. **答案：warmly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **laughed**，用副词 **warmly**。

7. **答案：some**
   - **考点**：代词/限定词。**some extra blankets** 表示“一些额外的毯子”，故填 **some**。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**expect a night**，故填 **a**。

9. **答案：magical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。**feel** 为系动词，后接形容词，名词 magic 的形容词形式为 **magical**。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。**thank sb. for sth.** 表示“因某事感谢某人”，故填 **for**。
