# 05-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：国际家庭日 (International Day of Families)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The International Day of Families reminds us 1. ______ love at home shapes who we become. In my family, we are busy, 2. ______ we still eat dinner together on Sundays. My father tells jokes, my mother 3. ______ (serve) soup, and my sister shares news from school. Last year, when I failed 4. ______ math exam, they did not shout at me. Instead, they sat beside me and helped me find 5. ______ better way to study. Grandpa often says family is 6. ______ team where everyone plays 7. ______ important part. On May 15th, our community held 8. ______ picnic and invited neighbors to bring homemade food. I realized 9. ______ small traditions build trust. I wrote 10. ______ thank-you note to my parents that night.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**reminds us** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

2. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前后为转折关系，故填 **but**。

3. **答案：serves**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。并列谓语描述习惯性动作，用一般现在时；主语 **my mother** 为第三人称单数，故填 **serves**。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**fail a math exam** 表示“一次数学考试不及格”，故填 **a**。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a better way**，故填 **a**。

6. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a team**，故填 **a**。

7. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**important** 以元音音素开头，**play an important part**，故填 **an**。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**held a picnic**，故填 **a**。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**realized** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。**a thank-you note**，故填 **a**。
