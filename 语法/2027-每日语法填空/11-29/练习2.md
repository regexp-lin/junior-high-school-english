# 11-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国民间剪纸艺术 (Chinese Paper-Cutting Art)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese paper-cutting is one of the __1__ (old) folk arts in China. It has a history of over 1,500 years. People use scissors or knives __2__ (cut) paper into beautiful patterns. Common designs include flowers, animals __3__ Chinese characters that represent good luck. During the Spring Festival, red paper-cuts __4__ (put) on windows and doors to bring happiness. My grandmother is very good __5__ this art. She learned it from her mother when she was just a little __6__ (girl). She can create a lifelike butterfly in only a few __7__ (minute). Last year, she __8__ (teach) me the basics. At first, I found it quite difficult __9__ I kept making mistakes. But she encouraged me to be patient, and gradually I __10__ (improve). Now I can make simple designs by myself. Paper-cutting is not just a craft — it carries the wisdom and love of generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the oldest 最古老的……之一，old 的最高级为 oldest。

2. **答案：to cut**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。use sth. to do sth. 用……来做……，用不定式 to cut 表目的。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。flowers, animals and Chinese characters 并列三个名词短语，用 and。

4. **答案：are put**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。paper-cuts 与 put 为被动关系，描述习惯性动作，用一般现在时被动 are put。

5. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。be good at 擅长……，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：girl**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。a little girl 一个小女孩，a 后接单数名词 girl。

7. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。a few minutes 几分钟，a few 后接可数名词复数 minutes。

8. **答案：taught**
   - **考点**：动词时态。last year 表示过去时间，teach 的过去式为 taught。

9. **答案：because / as**
   - **考点**：连词。表示原因"因为我总是犯错"，用 because 或 as。

10. **答案：improved**
    - **考点**：动词时态。与上文 encouraged 一致，描述过去事件，用一般过去时 improved。
