# 11-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园戏剧表演 (School Drama Performance)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, our school held its annual drama festival. Each class was required __1__ (prepare) a short play. My class decided to perform a story about a young inventor __2__ changed the world with a simple idea. I was chosen __3__ the lead actor, which made me both excited and nervous. We practised every afternoon __4__ three weeks. Our director, Li Mei, was very strict. She asked us __5__ (repeat) the same scene again and again until it was perfect. On the night of the __6__ (perform), the hall was full of students and parents. When the curtain went up, my hands were __7__ (shake). But as soon as I began speaking, all my fear __8__ (appear). I became the character completely. The audience laughed __9__ the funny parts and clapped loudly at the end. Our class won second prize, and it was one of the __10__ (proud) moments of my school life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be required to do sth. 被要求做某事，用不定式 to prepare。

2. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 a young inventor 指人，在从句中作主语，用 who 或 that。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。be chosen as 被选为……，as 表示"作为"。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for three weeks 持续三周，for 接一段时间。

5. **答案：to repeat**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式 to repeat。

6. **答案：performance**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the night of the performance 演出当晚，perform 的名词形式为 performance。

7. **答案：shaking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作表语）。my hands were shaking 我的手在发抖，表示正在进行的状态。

8. **答案：disappeared**
   - **考点**：动词时态。all my fear disappeared 恐惧消失了，描述过去发生的事，用一般过去时。注意 appear 变为 disappeared（消失），根据语境需加前缀 dis-。

9. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。laugh at 对……笑，为固定搭配。

10. **答案：proudest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the proudest moments 最骄傲的时刻之一，proud 的最高级为 proudest。
