# 04-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：社区志愿服务 (Community Volunteer Service)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Community volunteer service teaches teenagers to care for neighbors and the environment. On weekends, students clean parks, visit elderly people, or help organize charity sales.

Last Saturday, I joined a team (1) ______ (work) at a food bank near my home. We sorted cans, rice, and oil into boxes, (2) ______ were sent to families in need. The room was cold, (3) ______ nobody complained.

An organizer explained that volunteers must arrive on time and wear masks (4) ______ safety. She also reminded us (5) ______ (respect) each visitor's privacy when handing out bags.

At noon, a boy thanked us in a low voice. His words made me feel warmer (6) ______ the winter wind outside. I realized that volunteering is not about showing off; it is about sharing what we have.

Compared with playing games at home, this work was (7) ______ (tire), but it gave me a sense of purpose. My parents said they were proud (8) ______ my choice.

If more young people take part in service activities, life in our city will become (9) ______ (kind).

I have signed up for next month's river cleanup, and I hope my experience (10) ______ (encourage) others to join as well.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：working**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`a team working at ...` 在……工作的团队。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代 `boxes`。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。房间冷，但无人抱怨。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for safety` 为了安全。

5. **答案：to respect**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`remind sb. to do sth.` 提醒某人做某事。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`warmer than` 比……更温暖。

7. **答案：more tiring**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与在家玩游戏相比更累，`tiring` 用 `more tiring`。

8. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be proud of` 为……骄傲。

9. **答案：kinder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与参与志愿服务前相比，城市生活“更友善”，`become` 后接形容词比较级 `kinder`。

10. **答案：will encourage / can encourage**
    - **考点**：动词时态。希望经历将能鼓励他人，`will encourage`。
