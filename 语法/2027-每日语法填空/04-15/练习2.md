# 04-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电竞与青少年 (E-sports and Teenagers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

E-sports has grown into a serious industry with professional teams and large stadium events. Many teenagers dream of becoming players, (1) ______ they also face worries from parents and teachers.

Some adults believe that games are (2) ______ (harm) to eyesight and school grades. They are not completely wrong; sitting for hours without breaks can cause neck pain (3) ______ poor sleep.

However, e-sports also trains quick thinking and teamwork. A friend of mine joined a school club, (4) ______ he learned to plan strategies and communicate clearly. His coach told him that discipline matters (5) ______ than talent alone.

Schools are beginning to offer guidance instead of simple bans. Students are asked (6) ______ (balance) study and hobbies and to stop playing (7) ______ exams are near.

I enjoy watching matches on weekends, (8) ______ I never stay up too late. I know that a healthy routine is the foundation (9) ______ everything else.

If the industry keeps developing rules to protect young players, e-sports may win (10) ______ (wide) support from families in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but / yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。许多青少年想成为选手，但也面临担忧。

2. **答案：harmful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be harmful to` 对……有害，`harm` → `harmful`。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。颈部疼痛与睡眠不足并列。

4. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `club`，在俱乐部里学到……，用 `where`。

5. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：比较级标志词。`than talent` 提示比较级，`matters more than`。

6. **答案：to balance**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be asked to do sth.` 被要求做某事。

7. **答案：when / if**
   - **考点**：从属连词。考试临近时停止玩，用 `when`；或 `if` 如果考试临近。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。喜欢看比赛，但不熬夜太晚。

9. **答案：for / of**
   - **考点**：介词。`the foundation for everything else` 其他一切的基础。

10. **答案：wider / more wide**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。与现在相比“更广泛”的支持，`wide` → `wider`（或 `more wide` 较少用）。
