# 05-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：太空探索 (Space Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever looked up at the night sky and wondered what ___1___ (lie) beyond the stars? Space exploration has always been one of the ___2___ (great) dreams of humankind. In 1969, Neil Armstrong became the first person ___3___ (walk) on the Moon. Since then, scientists ___4___ (send) many spacecraft to explore distant planets. China's space programme has made ___5___ (amaze) progress in recent years. The Tianwen-1 mission ___6___ (successful) landed on Mars in 2021, ___7___ made the whole nation proud. Astronauts living on the space station need to exercise for ___8___ least two hours every day ___9___ their muscles can stay strong. Though space travel is still dangerous, thousands ___10___ young people dream of becoming astronauts one day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：lies**
   - **考点**：动词时态。what 引导宾语从句，描述客观事实用一般现在时，what 作主语为第三人称单数，填 lies。

2. **答案：greatest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词，表示"最伟大的梦想之一"，填 greatest。

3. **答案：to walk**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。the first person to do sth.，序数词修饰名词后用不定式作后置定语，填 to walk。

4. **答案：have sent**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since then 提示用现在完成时，主语 scientists 为复数，填 have sent。

5. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 progress，需用形容词形式，amaze → amazing（令人惊叹的），填 amazing。

6. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 landed，需用副词形式，successful → successfully，填 successfully。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事（着陆火星），用 which 引导。

8. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。固定短语 at least，表示"至少"，填 at。

9. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。so (that) 引导目的状语从句，表示"以便他们的肌肉保持强壮"，填 so。

10. **答案：of**
    - **考点**：介词。thousands of 表示"成千上万的"，固定搭配，填 of。
