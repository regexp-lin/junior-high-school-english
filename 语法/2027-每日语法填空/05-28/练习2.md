# 05-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统手工艺 (Traditional Crafts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, I visited a small village ___1___ my grandmother grew up. An old lady there taught me how ___2___ (make) paper-cutting, a traditional Chinese art form. She ___3___ (practise) the craft for over fifty years and her ___4___ (work) looked like beautiful paintings. The tools were simple: just a pair of ___5___ (scissor) and red paper. ___6___ first, my cuts were rough and uneven. The lady smiled ___7___ (patient) and told me not to rush. She said the most important thing ___8___ (be) to enjoy the process, not just the result. After three hours of practice, I ___9___ (final) finished my first piece — a little fish, which is ___10___ symbol of good luck in Chinese culture. I felt so proud of myself!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 village（地点），在从句中作地点状语，用 where 引导定语从句。

2. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。how to do sth. 表示"如何做某事"，teach sb. how to do sth.，填 to make。

3. **答案：had practised**
   - **考点**：动词时态。for over fifty years 与过去时间点结合，表示"到那时已经练习了50多年"，用过去完成时 had practised。

4. **答案：works**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。work 作"作品"义时为可数名词，her works 表示"她的多件作品"，后面 looked 也表明复数，填 works。

5. **答案：scissors**
   - **考点**：名词复数。a pair of scissors，剪刀恒为复数形式，填 scissors。

6. **答案：At**
   - **考点**：介词。固定短语 at first，表示"起初"，位于句首需大写，填 At。

7. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 smiled，需用副词形式，patient → patiently。

8. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。the most important thing 为单数主语，全文为过去时态，填 was。

9. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 finished，需用副词形式，final → finally。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。symbol 为可数名词单数，表示"一个象征"，用不定冠词 a。
