# 09-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：登长城 (Climbing the Great Wall)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Great Wall winds __1__ mountains like a huge stone dragon. Millions of tourists visit it every year __2__ feel its history and strength. Last autumn, I went there __3__ my cousins on a cool morning. Some steps were steep, __4__ we held the handrails and rested on the towers. A friendly guide __5__ (explain) how soldiers guarded the border centuries ago. Standing on the wall, I could see __6__ far the land stretched under the blue sky. I took photos __7__ (send) to my grandparents later that day. This trip made me understand __8__ our ancestors worked together __9__ (protect) the homeland. Climbing the Great Wall was tiring, __10__ it filled my heart with pride.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：through / along**
   - **考点**：介词。蜿蜒穿过群山 through mountains；along 表示沿着山脊亦可。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。visit ... to feel 为了感受历史与力量。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with my cousins 与表兄妹一同前往。

4. **答案：but / so**
   - **考点**：并列连词。台阶陡峭与抓扶手休息可为转折 but；或因果关系 so 表因此小心。

5. **答案：explained**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last autumn 叙述过去，explain 用过去式 explained。

6. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句引导词。see how far the land stretched 看到土地延伸多远。

7. **答案：to send**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。拍照是为了之后发给祖父母。

8. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句引导词。understand how our ancestors worked 理解祖先如何协作。

9. **答案：to protect**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。work together to protect 一起保卫家园。

10. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。疲惫与自豪为转折。
