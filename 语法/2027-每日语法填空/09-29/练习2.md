# 09-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：湿地保护 (Wetland Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Wetlands are often called the "kidneys of the earth" __1__ they clean water and store floods. Birds rest there while __2__ (migrate), and many fish lay eggs in shallow grass. __3__, cities and farms have taken away large areas of wetlands. Some species have lost safe homes, __4__ the food chain was influenced. Volunteers now plant reeds and remove rubbish __5__ weekends. Scientists also set cameras __6__ (watch) rare animals without disturbing them. Our school raised money __7__ build a small information board beside __8__ local lake. I believe if laws are carried out __9__ (strict), wetlands can recover step __10__ step.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导原因状语从句，说明为何被称为地球之肾。

2. **答案：migrating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。while migrating 为 while they are migrating 的省略，主从主语一致且含 be 动词时省略。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。上文讲湿地功能，下文讲被占用，转折。

4. **答案：and / so**
   - **考点**：并列连词。物种失去家园与食物链受影响为并列或因果顺承。

5. **答案：on / at**
   - **考点**：介词。on weekends 在周末；at weekends 英式亦可。

6. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。set cameras to watch 安装相机以观察。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。raised money to build 筹款以修建。

8. **答案：a / the**
   - **考点**：冠词。a local lake 一处当地湖泊；若特指已知湖泊用 the。

9. **答案：strictly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词短语 carried out 用副词，strict 的副词为 strictly。

10. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定短语。step by step 一步一步地。
