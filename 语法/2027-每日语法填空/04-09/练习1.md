# 04-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学弹吉他 (Learning Guitar)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Learning to play the guitar was my dream since I watched a street musician in summer. Last year, my parents bought me (1) ______ second-hand guitar and signed me up for weekend lessons.

At the beginning, my fingers hurt (2) ______ I pressed the strings. The teacher told me that pain was normal, (3) ______ I should practice only twenty minutes a day. Slowly, the tips of my fingers grew (4) ______ (thick), and the chords sounded clearer.

Now I can play simple songs for my family after dinner. Music brings us closer together, (5) ______ makes the effort worthwhile. Compared with piano, the guitar is easier (6) ______ (carry) to picnics and parties.

Sometimes I record myself and listen for mistakes. I know I still have (7) ______ long way to go before I can perform on stage. (8) ______ , I enjoy every small progress.

My teacher says talent matters, (9) ______ practice matters more. If you want to start, find a comfortable instrument and keep (10) ______ (try). The melody will reward you sooner or later.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a second-hand guitar` 一把二手吉他。

2. **答案：when / as / while**
   - **考点**：从属连词。当按弦时手指疼，表时间。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。疼痛正常，但每天只应练习二十分钟，转折。

4. **答案：thicker**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与初学时比较，指尖变“更厚”，隐含比较用 `thicker`。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句。

6. **答案：to carry**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be easy to do` 易于携带，用主动形式表被动意义。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`a long way to go` 还有很长的路要走。

8. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。尽管如此，我享受每一点进步。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。天赋重要，但练习更重要。

10. **答案：trying**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`keep doing sth.` 坚持做某事。
