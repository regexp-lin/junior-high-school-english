# 04-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：无人便利店 (Unmanned Convenience Store)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Near our school gate, a small unmanned convenience store opened last month. Customers scan a QR code (1) ______ the door, pick up snacks, and pay with their phones. There is no cashier (2) ______ the system checks everything with cameras and sensors.

At first, many students were curious (3) ______ this new shopping experience. Some worried that items might cost (4) ______ (much) than in ordinary shops, but the prices are almost the same. Others wondered (5) ______ teenagers could enter without parents.

The owner explained that the store closes (6) ______ midnight for safety reasons. If someone forgets (7) ______ (pay), the app will remind him or her later. So far, few people (8) ______ (catch) stealing on camera.

I think unmanned stores save time for busy workers. However, they may reduce job chances for people (9) ______ used to work as shop assistants. Technology always brings both benefits and challenges.

In the future, such stores (10) ______ (become) more common if laws and rules keep improving.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on / at**
   - **考点**：介词。`scan a QR code on the door` 门上的二维码；或 `at the door`。

2. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。没有收银员，因为系统用摄像头检查，表原因。

3. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be curious about` 对……好奇。

4. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，`more than`。

5. **答案：whether / if**
   - **考点**：连接词。`wondered whether/if` 想知道是否……

6. **答案：after / before**
   - **考点**：介词。`after midnight` 午夜后关门；或 `before midnight` 午夜前关门。语境“为安全原因”常指深夜关门，用 `after midnight` 表示午夜后关闭；若指午夜前关门用 `before`。此处取 **before** 午夜关门更安全——即 **before** midnight the store closes = 在午夜前关门。或 **at** midnight。采用：**before** midnight 在午夜之前关门（夜间不营业）。

7. **答案：to pay**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`forget to do` 忘记去做（未做），用 `to pay`。

8. **答案：have been caught**
   - **考点**：现在完成时被动语态。`so far` 与现在完成时连用，很少人“被抓到”偷窃。

9. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `people`，`people who used to work`。

10. **答案：will become**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。`in the future` 表将来。
