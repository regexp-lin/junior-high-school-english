# 01-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校社团 (School Clubs)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

School clubs play an important role in the (1) ______ (develop) of students' interests and abilities. Our school offers over twenty different clubs, ranging from sports and music (2) ______ science and debate. At the beginning of each school year, there is a club fair (3) ______ students can learn about each club and sign up for the ones that interest them. I joined the photography club last September, and it has been one of the best (4) ______ (decide) I have ever made. Before joining the club, I knew very little about (5) ______ (take) good photos. I would simply point my phone at something and press the button. But our club advisor, Ms Li, taught us that photography is much more than that. She showed us how to use light, colour and composition (6) ______ (tell) a story through images. Every weekend, our club goes on photo walks around the city. We explore different (7) ______ (neighbourhood), looking for interesting scenes to capture. Last month, my photo of an elderly man (8) ______ (play) chess in the park won first prize in a citywide photography contest. I felt (9) ______ (extreme) proud when I saw my picture displayed in the exhibition hall. Being part of the photography club has not only improved my (10) ______ (technique) skills but also taught me to see beauty in everyday life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：development**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`in the development of`，介词后接名词形式。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`ranging from...to...` 表示"范围从……到……"。

3. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a club fair`，在定语从句中作地点状语。

4. **答案：decisions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the best decisions`，"最好的决定之一"。

5. **答案：taking**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `about` 后接动名词形式。

6. **答案：to tell**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to use...to tell a story`，不定式表目的。

7. **答案：neighbourhoods**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different neighbourhoods`，`different` 修饰可数名词复数。

8. **答案：playing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`an elderly man playing chess`，主动关系。

9. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `proud` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：technical**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `skills` 需要形容词形式。
