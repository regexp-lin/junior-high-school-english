# 01-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：发明与创造 (Inventions and Creativity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Throughout history, great inventions have changed the way people live. The compass, gunpowder, papermaking and printing are known (1) ______ the Four Great Inventions of ancient China. These inventions had a huge impact (2) ______ the development of human civilisation. Today, new inventions continue to appear at an amazing speed. The smartphone, (3) ______ was first introduced in 2007, has completely transformed how we communicate, learn and entertain (4) ______ (we). But where do great inventions come from? Many people think that inventors are born geniuses, (5) ______ this is not always the case. Most inventions are the result of years of hard work and countless (6) ______ (fail). Thomas Edison, the inventor of the light bulb, famously said, "I have not failed. I've just found 10,000 ways that won't work." His words remind us that (7) ______ (patient) and perseverance are essential qualities for anyone who wants to create something new. In our school, students are encouraged (8) ______ (think) creatively and come up with their own ideas. Last term, our science teacher asked us to design something that could solve a problem in our daily lives. My partner and I (9) ______ (build) a small device that waters plants (10) ______ (automatic) when the soil becomes dry. Although it was a simple invention, we were very proud of what we had achieved.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be known as` 表示"被称为"。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`have an impact on` 为固定搭配，表示"对……有影响"。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `The smartphone`。

4. **答案：ourselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`entertain ourselves`，主语为 `we`。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前后语义转折，"许多人认为……但这并非总是如此"。

6. **答案：failures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`countless failures`，`countless` 修饰可数名词复数。

7. **答案：patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `perseverance` 并列作主语，需要名词形式。

8. **答案：to think**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be encouraged to do sth`，被动语态中的不定式。

9. **答案：built**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last term` 为过去时标志，`build` 的过去式为 `built`。

10. **答案：automatically**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `waters` 需要副词形式。
