# 07-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我最好的朋友 (My Best Friend)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tom and I have been classmates ___(1)___ Grade Seven, and he is ___(2)___ boy I trust most in our school. Even during the busiest weeks before exams, he still finds time to ask classmates how they feel and share simple study plans. What I admire about him is that he quietly notices who needs help and never asks for attention in return. He is not only good at science ___(3)___ also patient when explaining difficult problems to anyone who feels nervous before a test. Last term, he ___(4)___ (choose) as the class monitor because everyone admired his sense of duty and his honest way of solving disagreements. At the parents’ meeting, many parents were ___(5)___ (surprise) to hear how politely he spoke about teamwork and how much he valued every classmate’s idea. English used to be ___(6)___ (weak) part of my studies, but Tom encouraged me every day and reminded me that mistakes are part of learning. With his help, I have ___(7)___ (make) noticeable progress this year and feel much ___(8)___ (confidence) when reading aloud in front of the whole class. He often says that a real friend should keep ___(9)___ (learn) from others and never look down on anyone. I am sure our friendship will last ___(10)___ we face new challenges in senior high, because true friends grow stronger together when life changes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。表示“从七年级以来一直同班”，与现在完成时 have been 搭配，用 since 引出起点；for 后接时间段（如 for three years），此处 Grade Seven 为时间点，故用 since。

2. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。最高级结构 “the boy I trust most” 中，形容词最高级 most 前必须用定冠词 the。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。固定搭配 not only … but also …，表示“不但……而且……”，句中已有 also，空处填 but。

4. **答案：was chosen**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 he 与 choose 为被动关系（被选为班长），时间状语 Last term 表过去，故用一般过去时的被动语态 was chosen。

5. **答案：surprised**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词）。主语为 many parents（人），表“感到惊讶”用 surprised；surprising 多修饰事物，意为“令人惊讶的”。

6. **答案：the weakest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。语境为“英语曾是我学习中最薄弱的一环”，在诸多科目中比较，用 weak 的最高级 the weakest。

7. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与现在完成时 have 构成 have made progress，make progress 为固定搭配，过去分词为 made。

8. **答案：more confident**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级；括号内为名词 confidence，需转为形容词 confident，表示与过去相比“更自信”，填 more confident。

9. **答案：learning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。keep 后接动名词作宾语，keep doing sth. 表示“持续做某事”，故填 learning。

10. **答案：when / as**
    - **考点**：从属连词。引导时间状语从句，“当我们面临高中的新挑战时，友谊仍会持续”，when 或 as（当……时）均可；若强调让步也可用 though，但此处时间关系更自然，推荐 **when**。
