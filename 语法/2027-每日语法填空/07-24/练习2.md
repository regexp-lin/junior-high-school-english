# 07-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：骑行的乐趣 (The Joy of Cycling)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Cycling has become ___(1)___ popular way for teenagers to stay active after school. In our city, more bike lanes have appeared near parks and schools, and the soft sound of wheels on the path makes my short journey feel like a little holiday. ___(2)___ I ride along the river near my home, I can forget the pressure of homework for a while and simply enjoy the wind on my face. My father bought me a new bike ___(3)___ my fourteenth birthday, and we have gone on family trips ___(4)___ (regular) ever since. At first, I was afraid of ___(5)___ (fall) on wet roads, but practice made me ___(6)___ (steady) on the seat than before. Last weekend, our club ___(7)___ (organize) a twenty-kilometer ride to the countryside. We packed water and snacks, greeted workers in the gardens, listened to birds singing in the trees, and stopped to take photos when the hills turned gold in the afternoon sun. The air there smelled ___(8)___ (fresh) than in the city, and ___(9)___ (cheer) farmers waved to us from the fields. Cycling does not pollute the environment, and it costs ___(10)___ than driving a car. That is why I call it a simple but meaningful hobby that keeps both my body and my mind healthy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。way 为可数名词单数，表示“一种受欢迎的方式”，popular 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

2. **答案：When / While**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导时间状语从句，“当我沿河骑行时”，主从句描述同时发生的背景，用 When 或 While 均可。

3. **答案：on / for**
   - **考点**：介词。on one’s birthday 为固定搭配“在某人生日那天”；也可用 for 表示“为十四岁生日（而买）”，中考更常见 **on**。

4. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词）。修饰动词短语 have gone on family trips，需用副词 regularly，表示“定期地、经常地”。

5. **答案：falling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。介词 of 后接动名词，be afraid of doing sth.，故填 falling。

6. **答案：steadier / more steady**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than before，括号内 steady 为形容词，用比较级 steadier（辅音 + y 结尾改 y 为 i 加 er）；也可用 more steady，中考常给 **steadier**。

7. **答案：organized / organised**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 Last weekend 表过去，主语 our club 与 organize 为主动关系，用一般过去时 organized（英式拼写 organised 亦可）。

8. **答案：fresher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than in the city，fresh 的比较级为 fresher，表示乡村空气比城里更清新。

9. **答案：cheerful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词）。修饰名词 farmers，需用形容词 cheerful，表示“开朗热情的农民”，由 cheer 派生为 cheerful。

10. **答案：less**
    - **考点**：副词比较级。cost 在此为动词，意为“花费（金钱）”；与开车相比骑行更省钱，用 little 的比较级 less 与 than 搭配，构成 costs less than …。
