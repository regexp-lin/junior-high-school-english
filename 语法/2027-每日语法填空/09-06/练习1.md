# 09-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校辩论赛 (School Debate Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last week, our school held an English debate competition on the topic "Should students be allowed ___1___ (use) smartphones at school?" Teams from six classes took part, and our class was lucky enough ___2___ (enter) the final round.

I was one of the three debaters on our team. We argued that smartphones should ___3___ (ban) during school hours because they distract students from ___4___ (study). We prepared for the debate by ___5___ (collect) data and examples to support our points.

The opposing team, ___6___, made some strong arguments too. They said that smartphones could be useful learning ___7___ (tool) if used properly. For example, students could use educational apps to review lessons or search ___8___ information online.

The debate lasted for about an hour. Both teams spoke ___9___ (passion) and gave convincing arguments. In the end, the judges announced that our team won by a very narrow margin. The head judge praised both sides and said it was the ___10___ (good) debate she had seen in the school's history.

Through this experience, I learned that seeing things from different perspectives is important.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be allowed to do sth.**（被允许做某事），故填 **to use**。

2. **答案：to enter**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**be + adj. + enough + to do sth.**（足够……以至于能做某事），故填 **to enter**。

3. **答案：be banned**
   - **考点**：动词语态。情态动词 `should` 后接动词原形；主语 `smartphones` 与 `ban` 之间为被动关系（手机"被禁止"），故填 **be banned**。

4. **答案：studying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**distract sb. from doing sth.**（使某人从做某事上分心），介词 `from` 后接动名词，故填 **studying**。

5. **答案：collecting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**by doing sth.**（通过做某事），介词 `by` 后接动名词，故填 **collecting**。

6. **答案：however**
   - **考点**：副词（转折连接词）。前面说我方的论点，此处转折引出对方的强论点，用 **however**（然而），两端有逗号分隔。

7. **答案：tools**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`useful learning tools`（有用的学习工具），`tool` 的复数形式为 **tools**。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。**search for...**（搜索……）为固定搭配，故填 **for**。

9. **答案：passionately**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `spoke` 需用副词，**passion** 的副词形式为 **passionately**（充满激情地）。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。**the + 最高级** 与 `in the school's history` 搭配，`good` 的最高级为不规则变化 **best**。
