# 09-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市噪音治理 (Urban Noise Control)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Living in a big city can be exciting, but it also means dealing ___1___ constant noise. Cars honking, construction machines ___2___ (roar), and loudspeakers blaring — these sounds follow city residents throughout the day.

Noise pollution is not just annoying; it is a real health problem. Research has shown that people who ___3___ (expose) to high levels of noise over long periods may develop hearing problems, sleep disorders and even heart disease. Children ___4___ (live) near busy roads are also more likely to have trouble ___5___ (focus) at school.

To tackle this problem, many cities ___6___ (take) action in recent years. Some have built sound barriers along highways to block traffic noise from reaching nearby ___7___ (community). Others have introduced strict rules about when and where construction work can ___8___ (carry) out. In some residential areas, car horns are completely banned during the night.

Technology is also playing ___9___ important role. New building materials with better soundproofing properties are being developed. Some companies have even created "quiet zones" inside buildings, where special walls and windows keep ___10___ (most) of the outside noise out.

A quieter city is a healthier city. It is everyone's responsibility to help reduce unnecessary noise.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**deal with...**（应对……）为固定搭配，故填 **with**。

2. **答案：roaring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`construction machines roaring`（建筑机器轰鸣），现在分词 **roaring** 构成独立主格结构，表示正在发出声音的状态。

3. **答案：are exposed**
   - **考点**：动词语态。**be exposed to...**（暴露于……）为固定搭配；定语从句中陈述一般性事实用一般现在时，主语 `people` 为复数，故填 **are exposed**。

4. **答案：living**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`Children` 与 `live` 之间为主动关系（孩子们"住在"忙碌的路边），用现在分词 **living** 作后置定语。

5. **答案：focusing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**have trouble (in) doing sth.**（做某事有困难），故填 **focusing**。

6. **答案：have taken**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**in recent years**（近年来）提示现在完成时，主语 `many cities` 为复数，故填 **have taken**。

7. **答案：communities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`nearby`（附近的）修饰可数名词复数，`community` 的复数形式为 **communities**。

8. **答案：be carried**
   - **考点**：动词语态。情态动词 `can` 后接动词原形；主语 `construction work` 与 `carry out` 之间为被动关系，故填 **be carried**（out）。

9. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。**play an important role**（发挥重要作用）为固定搭配，`important` 以元音音素开头，故用不定冠词 **an**。

10. **答案：most**
    - **考点**：代词/形容词。**most of...**（大部分的……），`most` 在此作代词，表示"大部分外界噪音"。注意括号给出 `most`，此处无需变形。
