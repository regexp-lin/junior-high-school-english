# 07-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园运动会 (School Sports Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school held ___1___ annual sports day on ___2___ large sports field behind the teaching building. Bright flags waved in the wind while students marched in with their class banners, shouting energetic slogans. The head teacher gave a short speech about fair play, and the crowd clapped until the music started again. It was ___3___ (we) first chance ___4___ (take) part in team competitions after ___5___ long winter break, so everyone wanted ___6___ (do) their best when the starter's gun finally rang out across the field.

During the morning, athletes tried the long jump, high jump, and shot put, while volunteers handed out water and helped injured students rest in the shade. Classmates who were not competing still stayed nearby, taking photos and keeping score on clipboards. The PE teachers ___7___ (work) with the student union for weeks to prepare the tracks, timing equipment, and medals. Parents sat on the stands, cheering loudly as they ___8___ (watch) each exciting race. The relay was ___9___ (clear) the most popular event, and Tom, ___10___ had hurt his knee earlier, still decided to run the final leg for Class 3. His classmates hugged him at the finish line, smiling though they missed first place. That sunny afternoon, we all learned that teamwork and courage could mean more than a gold medal, and we promised to train harder before next year's games.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词。空格处需用形容词性物主代词或名词所有格类指代，与主语 *our school* 呼应；*school* 为单数事物，用 **its** 表示“学校的”，构成 *hold its annual sports day*（举办其一年一度的运动会）。不可用 *it's*（= it is）。

2. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。特指教学楼后面的那块运动场，为双方共知的具体地点，用定冠词 **the**。

3. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：词性转换（人称代词 → 形容词性物主代词）。括号 *we* 为人称代词主格，空后为名词短语 *first chance*，需用形容词性物主代词 **our** 表示“我们的第一次机会”。

4. **答案：to take**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。*chance* 后常接不定式作定语，表示“做某事的机会”，即 *chance to take part in...*；*take part in* 为固定搭配，意为“参加”。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。*winter break* 可数化泛指“一段漫长的寒假”，*long* 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 **a**。

6. **答案：to do**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。*want* 后接宾语再接不定式作宾补：*want everyone to do their best*，故填 **to do**。

7. **答案：had worked**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。运动会已于上周五举行，而“与学生会一起筹备”发生在运动会之前，表示“过去的过去”，用 **had worked**。

8. **答案：watched**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文叙述过去事件，*as* 引导时间状语从句，主句为过去进行时 *were cheering*，从句常用一般过去时描述同时发生的动作，故用 **watched**。

9. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。括号 *clear* 为形容词，此处修饰形容词 *popular*，需用副词 **clearly**，表示“显然是最受欢迎的项目”。

10. **答案：who**
    - **考点**：关系代词。逗号后为非限制性定语从句，先行词为 *Tom*（指人），从句缺主语，用 **who** 引导。*that* 一般不用于非限制性定语从句。
