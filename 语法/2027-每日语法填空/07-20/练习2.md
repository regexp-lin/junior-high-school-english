# 07-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：人工智能的未来 (The Future of Artificial Intelligence)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many ___1___ (science) experts believe ___2___ artificial intelligence will reshape ___3___ way people work and learn in the coming decades. Modern programs can ___4___ (analyze) huge amounts of data in seconds, and their suggestions often ___5___ (seem) surprisingly human ___6___ first glance when we read them on a phone screen. Engineers keep improving these tools, yet ordinary users still find it difficult to judge how reliable each answer really is.

On ___7___ surface, it is hard ___8___ (tell) whether a short essay ___9___ (write) by a student or by software, so honest communication about tool use matters in every subject. Some teachers worry that chatbots may stop teenagers ___10___ (think) deeply when they finish homework alone at night, while parents hope schools will teach clear limits. District offices are discussing updated honor codes, and students are learning to describe when and how they used an assistant. In a brighter scenario, AI tutors might explain difficult readings step by step and give shy learners extra chances to practice speaking without fear of mistakes. If campuses set fair rules and design debates that cannot be solved by copy-and-paste answers, technology may stay a partner that saves time but still leaves room for teamwork, creativity, and careful judgment in real classrooms around the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：scientific**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。括号 *science* 为名词，此处修饰名词 *experts*，需用形容词 **scientific**，表示“科学领域的专家”。

2. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。*believe* 后接宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 **that** 引导（口语中可省略，但语法填空通常要求填出 *that*）。

3. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。*way* 后有定语从句 *people work and learn* 限定，表示特指“人们工作与学习的方式”，用定冠词 **the**。

4. **答案：analyze**
   - **考点**：动词形式。情态动词 *can* 后接动词原形，故填 **analyze**（英式拼写 *analyse* 亦正确，全文美式统一用 *analyze*）。

5. **答案：seem**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。主语 *suggestions* 为复数，全文为一般现在时陈述观点，谓语用动词原形 **seem**。

6. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **at first glance**，意为“乍一看”。

7. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。固定搭配 **on the surface**，意为“表面上”。

8. **答案：to tell**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。句型 *It is + adj. + to do sth.*，*it* 为形式主语，真正主语为不定式短语，故填 **to tell**。

9. **答案：was written**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般过去时的被动语态）。*essay* 与 *write* 为被动关系，且描述“写好的短文”这一已完成状态，语境为过去背景下的判断，用 **was written**。*whether... or...* 连接并列结构，与 *by software* 对称。

10. **答案：thinking**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。*stop sb. (from) doing sth.* 表示“阻止某人做某事”，此处用动名词 **thinking** 作宾语，说明担心学生不再深入思考。
