# 07-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：手机的利与弊 (Pros and Cons of Mobile Phones)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Nowadays, it seems ___1___ almost every teenager carries a mobile phone. Over the past few years, phones ___2___ (change) the way we work, study, and spend our free time. With several taps on the screen, we can read news, listen to songs, ___3___ (use) learning apps, and relax comfortably at home. In addition, we can map out routes, pay bills, and join online groups to share hobbies with people from different cities. Many schools now discuss whether students should be allowed to bring phones to campus, because the topic touches both safety and learning efficiency.

___4___, mobile phones are not perfect tools. Some students are ___5___ (addict) to online games, and they often stay up very late ___6___ they lose themselves in exciting but meaningless levels. Staring at bright screens for hours is ___7___ (harm) to our eyes and may cause headaches. A few young people have grown ___8___ (depend) on their phones and feel nervous when there is no Wi-Fi around. Even during family meals, some teenagers keep staring at small screens instead of talking with their parents, which worries many adults who hope for warmer communication at home.

In my opinion, we teenagers should learn to use technology ___9___ (wise). ___10___ we plan our time carefully and treat phones as helpers, they will bring us more advantages than problems.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。*It seems that...* 为固定句型，*it* 为形式主语，*that* 引导主语从句（说明“似乎……”的内容），*that* 在从句中不充当成分，无词义。

2. **答案：have changed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 *Over the past few years*（过去几年至今）是现在完成时的典型标志，主语 *phones* 为复数，故用 *have changed*，表示变化从过去持续到现在并对现在有影响。

3. **答案：use**
   - **考点**：动词形式并列。*can* 后接并列的多个动词原形：*read*, *listen*, *use*, *relax*，结构对称，均省略重复的 *can*，故填动词原形 *use*。

4. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：逻辑衔接词。上文讲手机的便利，本段转而讲缺点，为转折关系；空后有逗号，句首用 *However*（然而），注意首字母大写。

5. **答案：addicted**
   - **考点**：词性转换与固定搭配。*be addicted to...* 表示“对……上瘾”，需用形容词 *addicted*；*addict* 为动词/名词，此处必须用过去分词形式的形容词。

6. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：从属连词。句意为“他们常常熬夜到很晚，这时/当他们沉浸在游戏里时”；*when* 引导时间状语从句，说明 *stay up late* 与 *lose themselves* 同时发生，也可用 *because* 表因果，但 *when* 更侧重“一……就……/在……时候”，与中考常见考点一致。

7. **答案：harmful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。*be harmful to...* 为固定搭配，“对……有害”；*harm* 为名词/动词，此处需形容词 *harmful*。

8. **答案：dependent**
   - **考点**：词性转换。*grow* 在此作系动词，后接形容词，表示“变得……”；*depend* 的形容词形式为 *dependent*，*grow dependent on* 意为“变得依赖……”。

9. **答案：wisely**
   - **考点**：副词。修饰动词 *use*，需用副词；*wise* 的副词形式为 *wisely*，表示“明智地使用科技”。

10. **答案：If**
    - **考点**：条件状语从句。句意为“如果我们认真规划时间并把手机当作帮手，它就会利大于弊”；主句为一般将来时含义（*will bring*），从句用一般现在时表将来，主从复合句由 *If* 引导条件状语从句，句首大写。
