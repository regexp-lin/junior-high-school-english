# 07-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：乡村生活体验 (Experiencing Country Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last July, I ___1___ (spend) two weeks with my grandparents in a small village far from the noise of the city. It was ___2___ first time that I had lived in the countryside for such a long period, ___3___ I felt a little nervous when the journey began.

However, as soon as I arrived, I knew I would enjoy myself. The air there was much ___4___ (fresh) than in our town, and every morning I could hear birds ___5___ (sing) happily in the trees behind the house. My grandfather, ___6___ has lived in the village for more than sixty years, patiently taught me how ___7___ (plant) potatoes and tomatoes. At first the physical work seemed quite ___8___ (possible) for a city boy like me, but with his help I gradually got used to it. During those days, I also walked along narrow paths between green fields and waved to friendly neighbours who always greeted us with warm smiles.

In the evening, we often sat in the yard and watched the stars. The sky looked clearer and wider than ___9___ I had imagined. One night, Grandma told me an old story about her childhood and said the stone well near the kitchen ___10___ (use) every day to draw water for the family. That simple country life opened my eyes and taught me to value what we have.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：spent**
   - **考点**：动词时态。句首时间状语 *Last July* 表明动作发生在过去，全文叙述去年暑假经历，谓语动词用一般过去时；*spend* 的过去式为不规则变化 *spent*。

2. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。*It was the first time that...* 为固定结构，序数词 *first* 前须用定冠词 *the*，表示“第一次……”。

3. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前后两分句为因果关系：因为是第一次去乡下住那么久，所以一开始有点紧张；用 *so* 连接结果，比 *and* 更贴切逻辑。

4. **答案：fresher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有比较连词 *than*，且 *much* 可修饰比较级，故将 *fresh* 变为比较级 *fresher*，表示乡村空气比城里“新鲜得多”。

5. **答案：singing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。*hear sb. doing sth.* 表示“听到某人正在做某事”，强调早晨持续听到的情景；若用 *sing* 则偏重于听到全过程，此处语境更适合 *singing*。

6. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句修饰先行词 *My grandfather*，从句缺主语且指人，故用 *who*（不可用 *that* 引导此类非限制性定语从句）。

7. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。*teach sb. how to do sth.* 为固定结构，“疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语，故填 *to plant*。

8. **答案：impossible**
   - **考点**：词性转换与否定前缀。上文说城里孩子一开始干农活，语境应是“似乎不可能”；*possible* 加否定前缀 *im-* 得 *impossible*，与 *but I never gave up* 形成转折。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。*than* 后接宾语从句，从句中 *imagined* 缺宾语，表示“我所想象的样子/程度”，用 *what* 引导（= *the thing that*）。

10. **答案：was used**
    - **考点**：动词时态与语态。*well* 与 *use* 之间为被动关系（井被用来打水）；叙述奶奶童年往事，用一般过去时的被动语态 *was used*；*be used to do sth.* 表示“被用来做某事”，注意与 *used to do*（过去常常）区分。
