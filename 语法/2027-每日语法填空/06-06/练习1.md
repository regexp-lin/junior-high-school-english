# 06-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：端午节 (Dragon Boat Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Dragon Boat Festival is one of the ___1___ (old) traditional festivals in China. It ___2___ (fall) on the fifth day of the fifth month in the Chinese lunar calendar. People celebrate this day ___3___ eating zongzi and watching dragon boat races. The festival is closely connected ___4___ the story of Qu Yuan, a great poet who lived more than two thousand years ago. ___5___ (he) poems are still widely read today. According to legend, when Qu Yuan threw ___6___ (he) into the Miluo River, local people raced their boats to save him. They also dropped rice ___7___ the river to keep the fish from ___8___ (eat) his body. Today, making zongzi has become a family activity that brings ___9___ (generation) together. My grandmother always makes the best zongzi, ___10___ I have tried many times to copy her recipe without success!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词，表示"最古老的传统节日之一"，old → oldest。

2. **答案：falls**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。描述每年固定发生的事实，用一般现在时，主语 it 为单数，fall → falls。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。by doing sth. 表示"通过做某事"来庆祝，填 by。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be connected with 表示"与……有联系"，固定搭配，填 with。

5. **答案：His**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 poems，用形容词性物主代词，he → His，句首大写。

6. **答案：himself**
   - **考点**：代词。threw himself into 表示"投身于"，主语与宾语为同一人，用反身代词 himself。

7. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。drop sth. into 表示"把东西投入"，强调从外到内的动作，填 into。

8. **答案：eating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。keep...from doing sth. 表示"阻止……做某事"，from 为介词，后接动名词，填 eating。

9. **答案：generations**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。brings generations together 表示"将几代人聚在一起"，generation → generations。

10. **答案：though/although**
    - **考点**：连词。"奶奶做的最好"与"我尝试多次都没成功"之间为让步关系，用 though 或 although。
