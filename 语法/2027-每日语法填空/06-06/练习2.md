# 06-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电动汽车 (Electric Vehicles)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Electric vehicles, also ___1___ (know) as EVs, are becoming more and more popular around the world. Unlike traditional cars, EVs do not produce harmful gases, ___2___ makes them much ___3___ (friend) to the environment. China has become the ___4___ (large) market for electric vehicles. In many cities, you can see charging stations ___5___ parking lots and shopping centres. My uncle bought ___6___ electric car last year. He says it costs much ___7___ (little) to charge than to fill a tank with petrol. However, some people are still worried ___8___ the limited driving range. What if the battery runs out in the middle of ___9___ journey? Engineers are working hard to develop batteries that can last longer and charge ___10___ (fast). With better technology, EVs will surely replace most petrol cars in the near future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：known**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。also known as 为插入语，过去分词短语作状语，表示"也被称为"，填 known。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个事实（不产生有害气体），用 which 引导。

3. **答案：friendlier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级，表示"对环境友好得多"，friendly → friendlier。

4. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"最大的市场"，large → largest。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in parking lots and shopping centres 表示"在停车场和购物中心里"，填 in。

6. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。electric 以元音音素开头，表示"一辆电动汽车"，用不定冠词 an。

7. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。than 提示用比较级，cost less 表示"花费更少"，little → less。

8. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。be worried about 表示"担心"，固定搭配，填 about。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。in the middle of a journey 表示"在旅途中间"，journey 为可数名词单数，用不定冠词 a。

10. **答案：faster**
    - **考点**：副词比较级。charge faster 表示"充电更快"，与 last longer 并列，fast → faster。
