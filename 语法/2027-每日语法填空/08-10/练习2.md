# 08-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：太空探索 (Space Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Space has always been a place full ___1___ mystery and wonder. For thousands of years, people have looked up at the night sky and asked ___2___ (they) what lies beyond the stars.

In 1969, the American astronaut Neil Armstrong became the ___3___ (one) person to walk on the moon. His famous words — "That's one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind" — ___4___ (remember) by people all over the world even today. Since then, many countries ___5___ (send) spacecraft into space to learn more about the universe.

China's space programme has also made great ___6___ (achieve) in recent years. The Chang'e missions successfully landed on the moon, and the Tianhe space station ___7___ (build) step by step. Chinese astronauts have carried out several spacewalks, ___8___ (show) the world China's growing strength in space technology.

However, exploring space is not easy ___9___ cheap. It requires years of hard work and billions of dollars. Some people wonder whether the money should be spent on solving problems here on Earth instead. In my opinion, space exploration is still worth ___10___ (do) because it helps us understand our planet better and may even save humanity in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **be full of...**（充满……），此处 `a place full of mystery` 意为"一个充满神秘的地方"。

2. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。主语 `people` 与宾语指同一群人，"人们问自己"需用反身代词 **themselves**。

3. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词 → 序数词）。**the + 序数词 + 名词 + to do sth.**（第一个做某事的人），`one` 的序数词形式为 **first**。

4. **答案：are remembered**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `His famous words` 与 `remember` 之间为被动关系（话语"被记住"），时间标志 `even today` 提示一般现在时，主语为复数，故填 **are remembered**。

5. **答案：have sent**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**Since then**（从那以后）提示使用现在完成时，主语 `many countries` 为复数，故填 **have sent**。

6. **答案：achievements**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 名词）。`great` 修饰名词，**achieve** 的名词形式为 **achievement**；前面有 `great` 且表示多项成就，需用复数 **achievements**。

7. **答案：has been built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `the Tianhe space station` 与 `build` 之间为被动关系（空间站"被建造"），`step by step` 暗示从过去持续到现在的过程，用现在完成时被动语态 **has been built**。

8. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。句中已有谓语 `have carried out`，逗号后为非谓语结构，主语 `Chinese astronauts` 与 `show` 为主动关系，故用现在分词 **showing** 作伴随状语。

9. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。**not easy or cheap** 表示"既不容易也不便宜"，否定句中用 **or** 连接两个并列形容词（相当于 neither... nor...）。

10. **答案：doing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定搭配 **be worth doing sth.**（值得做某事），`worth` 后接动名词主动形式表示被动含义，故填 **doing**。
