# 08-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校图书角 (School Reading Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last semester, our class set up a reading corner at the back of the classroom. It was one of the ___1___ (good) ideas we had ever had. Each student was asked ___2___ (bring) at least two books from home and put them on the shelf for everyone to share.

At first, only a few students showed interest ___3___ the reading corner. Most of us preferred playing games on our phones during break time. However, things began to change when our monitor, Li Hua, started ___4___ (recommend) a different book each week. She would write a short introduction and stick it on the wall next to the shelf.

Slowly, more and more students ___5___ (attract) by those interesting introductions. Some of us even formed a small book club. We would meet every Friday afternoon ___6___ (discuss) the books we had read that week. I still remember how ___7___ (excite) I felt when I shared my thoughts about a detective story for the first time.

By the end of the semester, nearly every student in our class ___8___ (read) at least five books. Our head teacher was very ___9___ (pride) of us. She said that the reading corner had not only improved our reading skills ___10___ also helped us learn to share and communicate with each other.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。**one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词**（最……的之一），`good` 的最高级为 **best**。

2. **答案：to bring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**ask sb. to do sth.**（要求某人做某事），被动形式 `was asked to do sth.`，故填 **to bring**。

3. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。固定搭配 **show interest in...**（对……表现出兴趣），故填 **in**。

4. **答案：recommending**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**start doing sth.** 或 **start to do sth.** 均可表示"开始做某事"；此处 **recommending** 强调持续性的推荐行为，更符合语境。

5. **答案：were attracted**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `more and more students` 是复数，与 `attract` 之间为被动关系（学生"被吸引"），时态为一般过去时，故填 **were attracted**。

6. **答案：to discuss**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式 **to discuss** 在此作目的状语，表示"为了讨论"每周读过的书。

7. **答案：excited**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。修饰人的感受用 **-ed** 形式的形容词，`excite` → **excited**，表示"我感到多么兴奋"。

8. **答案：had read**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**By the end of the semester**（到学期末）表示过去某个时间点之前已完成的动作，需用过去完成时 **had read**。

9. **答案：proud**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词 → 形容词）。系动词 `was` 后接形容词作表语，**pride** 的形容词形式为 **proud**；固定搭配 **be proud of**（对……感到自豪）。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：连词。固定结构 **not only... but also...**（不仅……而且……），故填 **but**。
