# 06-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：乡村旅行 (A Trip to the Countryside)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, my family drove to a small village two hours away ___1___ the city. The air there was much ___2___ (fresh) than in Guangzhou, and the sky was so blue that it looked like a huge painting. We stayed at a farmhouse ___3___ (own) by a friendly old couple. In the morning, I helped them feed the ___4___ (chicken) and collect eggs. It was my first time ___5___ (touch) a real chicken, and I was a bit scared at first. After breakfast, we walked along a narrow path ___6___ led to a beautiful lake. My father taught me how to fish, ___7___ I didn't catch anything all morning. My mother sat under a tree, ___8___ (read) her favourite novel quietly. In the evening, the old couple cooked ___9___ (we) a wonderful dinner with vegetables from their own garden. The trip made me ___10___ (real) appreciate the peace and beauty of country life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。away from 表示"远离"，two hours away from the city 意为"距城市两小时路程"，填 from。

2. **答案：fresher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级 + than，表示"新鲜得多"，fresh → fresher。

3. **答案：owned**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，a farmhouse owned by... 表示"由……拥有的农舍"，填 owned。

4. **答案：chickens**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。feed the chickens 表示"喂鸡"，指多只鸡，用复数 chickens。

5. **答案：to touch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It was one's first time to do sth. 表示"某人第一次做某事"，填 to touch。

6. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a narrow path（物），在从句中作主语，用 that 或 which 引导定语从句。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。"爸爸教我钓鱼"与"我整个上午什么都没钓到"之间为转折关系，用 but。

8. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，sat under a tree, reading...，表示"坐在树下读书"，填 reading。

9. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词。cook sb. sth. 表示"给某人做饭"，sb. 用宾格形式，we → us。

10. **答案：really**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 appreciate，需用副词形式，real → really。
