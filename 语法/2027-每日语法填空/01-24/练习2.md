# 01-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：探访爷爷奶奶 (Visiting Grandparents)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every winter holiday, my family travels to my grandparents' home in a small town in Sichuan Province. The journey takes about six hours by train, but I always look forward to it because visiting my grandparents is one of the (1) ______ (happy) times of the year. My grandparents live in a traditional courtyard house (2) ______ (surround) by bamboo groves and fruit trees. Life there is very different from the busy city life I am used to. There are no tall buildings (3) ______ noisy traffic — just open fields, fresh air and the sound of birds singing. My grandfather is a retired teacher who spends most of his time (4) ______ (tend) his vegetable garden. He grows tomatoes, peppers and cabbage, and he is always proud (5) ______ (show) me how much his plants have grown since my last visit. My grandmother is the best cook I know. She can turn the (6) ______ (simple) ingredients into the most amazing meals. Her specialty is mapo tofu, which she makes (7) ______ (use) fresh Sichuan peppercorns from her own garden. The dish is so spicy that it (8) ______ (make) my eyes water, but I cannot stop eating it because it tastes so good. During my visits, my grandfather often tells me stories about (9) ______ life was like when he was young. These stories help me understand and appreciate the (10) ______ (sacrifice) my grandparents made to give our family a better life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the happiest times`。

2. **答案：surrounded**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a house surrounded by`，被动关系。

3. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。否定句中"和"用 `or`，`no tall buildings or noisy traffic`。

4. **答案：tending**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 为固定搭配。

5. **答案：to show**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be proud to do sth` 表示"自豪地做某事"。

6. **答案：simplest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the simplest ingredients`，与后面 `the most amazing meals` 形成对比。

7. **答案：using**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随方式，"用……来制作"。

8. **答案：makes**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。描述菜肴的特点，第三人称单数。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`what life was like`，`what` 引导宾语从句。

10. **答案：sacrifices**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`the sacrifices my grandparents made`，多次牺牲。
