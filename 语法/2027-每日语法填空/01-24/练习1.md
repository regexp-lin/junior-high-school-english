# 01-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：急救知识 (First Aid Knowledge)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Knowing first aid can save lives. In an emergency, the actions taken in the first few minutes are often the most (1) ______ (critically) ones. That is why our school recently organised a first aid training course for all Year Nine students. The course (2) ______ (teach) by a professional nurse from the local hospital. She began by explaining the basic principles of first aid. The most important rule, she said, is to stay calm. Panicking in an emergency only makes things (3) ______ (bad). She then demonstrated several essential (4) ______ (skill), including how to perform CPR, how to stop bleeding and how to treat burns. We were given dummies to practise CPR on, and it was much (5) ______ (hard) than it looked. You have to press down firmly on the chest at a steady rhythm, which requires both strength and focus. The nurse also taught us (6) ______ to do if someone is choking. She showed us the Heimlich manoeuvre, a technique (7) ______ (use) to clear a blocked airway. Each student had to practise the manoeuvre on a partner until they could do it (8) ______ (correct). By the end of the course, I felt much more (9) ______ (confidence) about handling emergencies. I hope I will never need to use these skills, but it is (10) ______ (comfort) to know that I am prepared if something does happen.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：critical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰代词 `ones` 需要形容词形式。

2. **答案：was taught**
   - **考点**：被动语态。课程"被讲授"，过去时被动。

3. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：比较级。`makes things worse`，`bad` 的比较级为 `worse`。

4. **答案：skills**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several essential skills`，`several` 修饰复数。

5. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much harder than it looked`，`than` 为标志。

6. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`what to do`，疑问词 + 不定式结构。

7. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a technique used to clear`，被动关系。

8. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `do` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：confident**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt more confident`，系动词后接形容词。

10. **答案：comforting**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`it is comforting to know`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。
