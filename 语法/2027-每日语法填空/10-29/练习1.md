# 10-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园急救培训 (School First Aid Training)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Monday, a doctor from the Red Cross came to our school __1__ (give) a first aid training session. She told us that knowing basic first aid __2__ (skill) can save lives in an emergency. First, she taught us how __3__ (deal) with cuts and burns. We learned to clean a wound with water and cover __4__ with a clean bandage. Then she showed us __5__ (perform) CPR on a practice dummy. It was harder __6__ it looked! You have to press the chest firmly and keep __7__ steady rhythm. She said that __8__ (give) CPR within the first four minutes is crucial for the patient's survival. We also learned the Heimlich manoeuvre, which is used when someone __9__ (choke) on food. By the end of the session, every student felt more __10__ (confidence) about handling emergencies. I hope I will never need to use these skills, but I am glad I know them.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了进行急救培训"，用 to give。

2. **答案：skills**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。first aid skills 急救技能，此处指多种技能，用复数 skills。

3. **答案：to deal**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。how to do sth. 如何做某事，用不定式 to deal。

4. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。指代前文的 a wound，用 it。

5. **答案：to perform**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。show sb. how to do sth. 向某人展示如何做某事（省略 how），或直接 showed us to perform，用不定式 to perform。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较级标志词。harder 为比较级，后接 than 引出比较对象。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a steady rhythm 一个稳定的节奏，rhythm 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

8. **答案：giving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。Giving CPR within the first four minutes 在前四分钟内进行心肺复苏，动名词短语作主语。

9. **答案：is choking**
   - **考点**：动词时态。when 引导的时间状语从句，表示"某人正在被食物噎住时"，用现在进行时 is choking。

10. **答案：confident**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。feel confident about 对……感到自信，confidence 的形容词形式为 confident。
