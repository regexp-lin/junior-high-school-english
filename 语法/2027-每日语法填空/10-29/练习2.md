# 10-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：科学家的故事 (A Scientist's Story)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tu Youyou is a Chinese scientist __1__ discovered artemisinin, a medicine that has saved millions of __2__ (life). She was born in 1930 and studied __3__ (medicine) plants at university. In the 1960s, malaria was killing many people, __4__ (especial) in tropical countries. The Chinese government asked scientists __5__ (find) a cure. Tu Youyou led a research team and studied hundreds of ancient Chinese medicine books. After years of hard work, she __6__ (final) found that a plant called sweet wormwood could fight malaria. The team tested the medicine __7__ themselves first to make sure it was safe. In 2015, Tu Youyou __8__ (award) the Nobel Prize in Medicine. She was the __9__ (one) Chinese woman to receive this honour. Her story shows us __10__ determination and traditional knowledge can change the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 scientist 指人，从句缺主语，用 who 或 that。

2. **答案：lives**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。millions of 后接可数名词复数，life 的复数为 lives。

3. **答案：medical**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 plants，medicine 的形容词形式为 medical。

4. **答案：especially**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰整个介词短语 in tropical countries，用副词 especially。

5. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式 to find。

6. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 found，用副词 finally。

7. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。test... on... 在……身上测试，用介词 on。

8. **答案：was awarded**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 Tu Youyou 与 award 为被动关系，由 in 2015 可知为一般过去时被动语态 was awarded。

9. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词→序数词）。the first Chinese woman 第一位中国女性，one 的序数词为 first。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。shows us 后接宾语从句，从句意义完整不缺成分，用 that 引导。
