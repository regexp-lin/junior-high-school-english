# 04-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统染布 (Traditional Tie-dyeing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the cultural week, our art teacher introduced us to traditional tie-dyeing, (1) ______ ancient skill from southwest China. We folded white cloth into shapes and (2) ______ (tie) it tightly with strings before putting it into natural dyes. The patterns looked like clouds or flowers, and each piece was (3) ______ (complete) different from the others.

The teacher explained that tie-dyeing (4) ______ (use) plant colors for centuries. "Every family used to have (5) ______ (they) own special recipe," she said. While the cloth was drying in the yard, we watched the colors become (6) ______ (deep) in the afternoon sun.

I was surprised (7) ______ simple tools could create such rich designs. My classmates and I took photos and posted them online, (8) ______ many netizens praised our work. This hands-on lesson helped me understand (9) ______ our ancestors turned daily life into art. I hope more young people will be interested (10) ______ learning old crafts like this.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`ancient` 以元音音素开头，`an ancient skill` 泛指一项古老技艺。

2. **答案：tied**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与前面 `folded` 并列作谓语，`and` 已给出，此处填 `tie` 的过去式 `tied`，表示“绑紧”。

3. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `different`，`complete` → `completely` 副词。

4. **答案：has been used**
   - **考点**：现在完成时被动语态。染缬“被使用”植物染料，时间状语 `for centuries` 表示从过去持续到现在的动作，主语 `tie-dyeing` 为单数，用 `has been used`。

5. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰 `own special recipe`，`they` → 形容词性物主代词 `their`，指代 `every family`。

6. **答案：deeper**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。在阳光下颜色“变得更深”，暗含与之前对比，`deep` → `deeper`；`become` 后可接形容词比较级。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`be surprised that...` 引导宾语从句，说明惊讶的内容。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。连接前后两个分句：“我们把照片发到网上”与“许多网友称赞我们的作品”为顺承并列，用 `and`。

9. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。`understand` 后宾语从句，“祖先如何把日常生活变成艺术”，用 `how` 表方式。

10. **答案：in**
    - **考点**：介词。`be interested in doing sth.` 对做某事感兴趣。
