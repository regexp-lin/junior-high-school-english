# 04-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：亲子运动会 (Parent-Child Sports Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our school held a parent-child sports day on the playground. The event (1) ______ (begin) at eight o'clock, and hundreds of families arrived early (2) ______ colorful team shirts. The opening show included a short dance (3) ______ students and their parents moved together to lively music.

One of the funniest games was the three-legged race. My father and I tied our legs (4) ______ a soft band and practiced counting "one, two" before we ran. We didn't win first prize, (5) ______ we laughed louder than anyone else. Another popular activity was the tug-of-war, (6) ______ teams pulled the rope with all their strength.

Teachers recorded every exciting moment (7) ______ cameras and later shared the photos on the school website. At the closing ceremony, the headmaster thanked all the parents (8) ______ (help) make the day unforgettable. Tired (9) ______ happy, we returned home with medals, snacks, and warm memories. Such activities strengthen the bond (10) ______ schools and families.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：began**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述上周六已发生的事，`Last Saturday` 与具体时刻，谓语用过去式 `began`。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in colorful team shirts` 穿着彩色队服，表穿着状态。

3. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `dance`，从句中缺地点状语，“在舞蹈中大家齐舞”，用 `where`。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`tie... with...` 用……把……绑在一起。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“没拿第一”与“笑得最大声”为转折。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `tug-of-war`（或活动场景），从句描述在该活动中发生的事，用 `where` 作状语。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`record... with cameras` 用相机记录。

8. **答案：for helping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`thank sb. for doing sth.` 因做某事感谢某人，介词 `for` 后接动名词。

9. **答案：yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`tired yet happy` 疲惫却快乐，`yet` 作并列连词，与 `but` 近义，常见于书面语。

10. **答案：between**
    - **考点**：介词。`between... and...` 在……与……之间，指学校与家庭之间的联系。
