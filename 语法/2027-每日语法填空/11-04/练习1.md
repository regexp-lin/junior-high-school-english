# 11-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：古典音乐欣赏 (Classical Music Appreciation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Sunday, my parents took me to __1__ concert at the Grand Theatre. It was my first time __2__ (listen) to a live classical music performance. The orchestra played pieces __3__ Beethoven and Mozart. When the music started, I was __4__ (immediate) drawn in by the powerful sound. The violins sang sweetly __5__ the drums boomed like thunder. I noticed that the conductor moved his hands so __6__ (grace) that he looked like a dancer. The audience sat in complete __7__ (silent), enjoying every note. During the intermission, my father explained that Beethoven wrote some of his __8__ (great) works after becoming deaf. I could __9__ (hard) believe it! His story showed me that true passion can overcome any difficulty. The concert lasted for two hours, __10__ it did not feel long at all. I hope to learn to play the violin one day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。泛指"一场音乐会"，concert 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

2. **答案：to listen**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It was one's first time to do sth. 某人第一次做某事，用不定式 to listen。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。pieces by Beethoven 贝多芬的作品，by 表示"由……创作"。

4. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰过去分词 drawn in，用副词 immediately。

5. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。violins 与 drums 的声音对比，用 while 表示对比或 and 表示并列。

6. **答案：gracefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。so + 副词 + that... 如此……以至于，修饰动词 moved，用 gracefully。

7. **答案：silence**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。in complete silence 在完全的安静中，silent 的名词形式为 silence。

8. **答案：greatest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。some of his greatest works 他最伟大的一些作品，用 greatest。

9. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。could hardly believe 几乎无法相信，hard 变为 hardly 表示"几乎不"。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。前句说持续两小时，后句说感觉不长，语义转折，用 but。
