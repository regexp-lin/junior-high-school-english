# 11-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护土壤 (Soil Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Soil is one of the most __1__ (value) natural resources on Earth. Without healthy soil, plants cannot grow and food production will be __2__ (serious) affected. However, soil pollution has become __3__ growing problem in many parts of the world. Factories dump chemical waste __4__ the ground, and farmers sometimes use too __5__ (many) pesticides. These harmful substances enter the soil and can remain there __6__ years. They not only damage crops __7__ also affect the health of people who eat the food. In recent years, the Chinese government __8__ (take) several steps to protect soil. New laws have been passed __9__ (prevent) illegal dumping. Scientists are also developing __10__ (nature) methods to clean polluted soil, such as using special plants that can absorb harmful chemicals. Protecting soil is protecting our food and our future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。one of the most + 形容词最高级，value 的形容词形式为 valuable。

2. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰过去分词 affected，用副词 seriously。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a growing problem 一个日益严重的问题，problem 为可数名词单数，用 a。

4. **答案：into / onto**
   - **考点**：介词。dump... into/onto the ground 把……倾倒到地面上/地下，用 into 或 onto。

5. **答案：many**
   - **考点**：形容词。too many + 可数名词复数，pesticides 为可数名词，用 many。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for years 持续数年，for + 时间段。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。not only... but also... 不仅……而且……，为固定搭配。

8. **答案：has taken**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in recent years 与现在完成时连用，主语 the Chinese government 为第三人称单数，用 has taken。

9. **答案：to prevent**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了防止"，用 to prevent。

10. **答案：natural**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 methods，nature 的形容词形式为 natural。
