# 03-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：春天的花 (Spring Flowers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When winter loosens its grip, the first flowers push through the soil as if they cannot wait (1) ______ (see) the sun. In our school garden, cherry trees turn pink, and daffodils line the paths.

Last Friday, our biology teacher led us outside (2) ______ a science lesson. She asked us to observe how petals are shaped differently (3) ______ (attract) bees and butterflies. Some flowers smell sweet; others have almost no scent at all.

I knelt beside a bed of tulips and sketched their colors in my notebook. A classmate wondered (4) ______ these plants knew when to bloom. The teacher explained that temperature and daylight send signals to their roots.

Farmers in the countryside are even busier in spring. They sow seeds and hope for enough rain. A warm spell (5) ______ (follow) by cold days may harm young buds, so people watch the weather forecast closely.

City parks also prepare for flower festivals. Workers hang signs (6) ______ visitors not to step on the grass. Photographers arrive early (7) ______ (catch) the soft morning light.

Spring flowers do not last long, (8) ______ is exactly why we treasure them. Their short beauty reminds us to notice small joys in daily life.

After class, I bought a small pot of violets (9) ______ my grandmother. She placed it near the window and said spring had come into her room (10) ______ last.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to see**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`cannot wait to do sth.` 迫不及待做某事。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`lead us outside for a lesson` 为了上课带到户外。

3. **答案：to attract**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的：花瓣形状不同是为了吸引昆虫。

4. **答案：how / whether**
   - **考点**：宾语从句。`wondered how plants knew` 想知道植物如何知道；或 whether。

5. **答案：followed**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a warm spell followed by cold days` 暖期之后紧接寒潮，相当于定语从句 `which is followed by` 的省略形式。

6. **答案：asking / to ask**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`hang signs asking visitors not to` 悬挂告示要求游客不要……；或 `signs to ask`。

7. **答案：to catch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`arrive early to catch` 早到以捕捉晨光。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句，`which is exactly why`。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`buy sth. for sb.` 为祖母买花。

10. **答案：at**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`at last` 终于。
