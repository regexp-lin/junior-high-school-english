# 03-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电子阅读 (E-reading)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

E-reading has changed the way millions of people enjoy books. With a light tablet or phone, a whole library can fit into a bag — something (1) ______ would have sounded like science fiction twenty years ago.

I started using an e-reader last year. At first, I missed the smell of paper and the sound of turning pages. Gradually, I discovered advantages I had not expected. I can adjust the font size when my eyes feel (2) ______ (tire), and the built-in dictionary helps me look up new words instantly.

Borrowing e-books from the public library is also convenient. I no longer worry about late fees (3) ______ the books return themselves on the due date. Many classics are even free (4) ______ (download).

Of course, e-reading has weaknesses. Staring at a bright screen for hours may cause headaches. Some students use reading apps (5) ______ (play) games during class, which worries teachers.

Experts suggest taking breaks every thirty minutes and reading paper books before bedtime (6) ______ sleep comes more easily.

Publishers are now experimenting with interactive stories (7) ______ readers choose different endings. Whether such works will replace traditional novels remains (8) ______ (see).

In my opinion, paper and digital books can coexist. What matters is not the format (9) ______ the habit of reading itself.

If you have never tried e-reading, borrow a device and explore. You might find (10) ______ opens a new door to knowledge.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `something` 不定代词，关系代词用 that。

2. **答案：tired**
   - **考点**：形容词。`feel tired` 感到疲劳，`tire` → `tired` 修饰人。

3. **答案：because / since**
   - **考点**：连词。电子书到期自动归还，所以不必担心滞纳金，表原因。

4. **答案：to download**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be free to download` 可免费下载。

5. **答案：to play**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`use apps to play games` 用阅读类应用玩游戏，不定式表目的。

6. **答案：so that**
   - **考点**：目的状语从句。睡前读纸质书，以便更容易入睡。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `stories` 作抽象地点，读者在其中选择不同结局。

8. **答案：to be seen**
   - **考点**：固定结构。`remain to be seen` 仍有待观察。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not ... but ...` 重要的不是版式，而是阅读习惯。

10. **答案：it**
    - **考点**：代词。宾语从句（可省略 that）的主语：`find (that) it opens a new door`，`it` 指代电子阅读或这种尝试。
