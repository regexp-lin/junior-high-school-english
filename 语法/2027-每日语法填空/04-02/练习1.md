# 04-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国功夫电影 (Chinese Kung Fu Movies)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese kung fu movies have won the hearts of audiences around the world. Many famous actors, such as Bruce Lee and Jet Li, (1) ______ (know) for their amazing skills on screen. These films are not just about fighting; they often tell stories about honor, friendship, and hard work.

Last month, our class watched a classic kung fu movie (2) ______ was made in the 1980s. The action scenes looked so real (3) ______ we could feel the power of every punch. The director (4) ______ (spend) months training the actors before shooting began.

Young people today still enjoy this kind of cinema (5) ______ it connects them with Chinese culture. Some students even join martial arts clubs (6) ______ (learn) basic moves after watching the films. They say that practicing kung fu makes them (7) ______ (strong) both physically and mentally.

In my opinion, a good kung fu movie should show respect (8) ______ traditional values. It should also inspire viewers to face difficulties (9) ______ (brave). I hope more Chinese kung fu stories (10) ______ (bring) to the big screen in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：are known**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。`be known for` 因……而闻名，主语 `actors` 复数，用 `are known`。

2. **答案：which / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `movie`，从句缺主语，用 `which` 或 `that`。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：固定句型。`so ... that ...` 如此……以至于……，`that` 引导结果状语从句。

4. **答案：spent**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。描述过去拍电影前的事，与 `began` 时态一致，`spend` → `spent`。

5. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：从属连词。后句解释年轻人仍喜欢此类电影的原因，表原因。

6. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：动词不定式作目的状语。加入武术俱乐部“为了学习”基本动作。

7. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`make sb. + adj.` 语境中隐含“与过去/不练时相比更强壮”，用 `stronger`；若强调“强壮”也可用 `strong`，但 `both physically and mentally` 常搭配 `stronger`。

8. **答案：for / to**
   - **考点**：介词。`show respect for` 对……表示尊重；或 `respect to` 在部分搭配中可见，中考常用 `for`。

9. **答案：bravely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `face`，`brave` → `bravely` 副词。

10. **答案：will be brought / are brought**
    - **考点**：被动语态与时态。故事“被搬上银幕”，`in the future` 可用一般将来时被动 `will be brought`；若表一般情况也可用 `are brought`。
