# 04-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统乐器 (Traditional Musical Instruments)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traditional musical instruments play (1) ______ important role in Chinese culture. The guzheng, pipa, and erhu are loved by people of all ages. Their sounds can be soft (2) ______ powerful, like a conversation between the heart and the strings.

In our school music festival, a group of students (3) ______ (give) a performance on the guzheng last Friday. The audience sat quietly (4) ______ the music started. Everyone was amazed by how fast the players' fingers (5) ______ (move) across the strings.

Learning a traditional instrument takes time and patience. My friend Mei has practiced the pipa for three years, and she can play (6) ______ (beautiful) than when she first began. She says that each practice (7) ______ (feel) like a short journey into history.

These instruments are more than tools for making music; they carry stories from the past. We should try (8) ______ (understand) the culture behind the notes. If young people lose interest in them, part of our heritage may (9) ______ (disappear) slowly.

I believe that with more school programs, traditional instruments (10) ______ (continue) to be part of our future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`play an important role in` 在……中扮演重要角色，`important` 元音前用 `an`。

2. **答案：but / yet**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`soft` 与 `powerful` 语义转折，“既温柔又有力”，用 `but`（或 `yet`）。

3. **答案：gave**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`last Friday` 明确过去时间，`give` → `gave`。

4. **答案：until / before / when**
   - **考点**：从属连词。`sat quietly until the music started` 直到音乐开始；或 `when` 当音乐开始时。

5. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。宾语从句中描述过去表演时手指的移动，用 `moved`。

6. **答案：more beautifully**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。修饰动词 `play`，`than` 提示比较级，`beautifully` → `more beautifully`。

7. **答案：feels**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与一般现在时。宾语从句主语 `each practice` 为单数，陈述常态用 `feels`。

8. **答案：to understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`try to do sth.` 努力做某事，用 `to understand`。

9. **答案：disappear**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`may` 后接动词原形；语境为若年轻人失去兴趣，文化遗产可能“慢慢消失”，用 `disappear`。

10. **答案：will continue**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。`I believe` 后宾语从句表对未来的期望，用 `will continue`。
