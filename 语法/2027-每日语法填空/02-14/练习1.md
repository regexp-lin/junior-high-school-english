# 02-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：元宵节 (Lantern Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Lantern Festival, which falls (1) ______ the fifteenth day of the first lunar month, marks the end of the Spring Festival celebrations. It is one of the most (2) ______ (colour) and joyful festivals in Chinese culture. On this night, streets and parks are decorated with thousands of lanterns in various shapes and sizes, (3) ______ (create) a magical atmosphere. One of the most popular traditions is guessing lantern riddles. Riddles (4) ______ (write) on pieces of paper and attached to lanterns. People walk around, trying to solve as many riddles as they can. Those who answer (5) ______ (correct) often receive small prizes. Another beloved tradition is eating tangyuan, sweet rice balls filled with sesame, peanut (6) ______ red bean paste. The round shape of tangyuan symbolizes family (7) ______ (unite) and togetherness. In some regions, dragon and lion dances (8) ______ (perform) in the streets, accompanied by the loud beating of drums. Last year, my family went to a lantern festival in the local park. My little sister was so (9) ______ (fascinate) by a huge rabbit-shaped lantern that she refused (10) ______ (leave) until the park closed. The Lantern Festival truly captures the warmth and joy of Chinese tradition.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on the fifteenth day` 在某一天用 `on`。

2. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most colourful festivals`，形容词修饰名词。

3. **答案：creating**
   - **考点**：现在分词作结果状语。自然而然的结果。

4. **答案：are written**
   - **考点**：一般现在时的被动语态。谜语被写在纸上，描述习俗。

5. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`answer correctly` 正确地回答。

6. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。列举馅料选项，芝麻、花生或红豆沙。

7. **答案：unity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`family unity` 家庭团结，名词。

8. **答案：are performed**
   - **考点**：一般现在时的被动语态。舞龙舞狮被表演，描述习俗。

9. **答案：fascinated**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。`so fascinated by` 被……深深吸引。

10. **答案：to leave**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`refuse to do sth.` 拒绝做某事。
