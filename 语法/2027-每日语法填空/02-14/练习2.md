# 02-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：自行车骑行 (Cycling)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Cycling has become one of the most popular forms of exercise and transportation in Chinese cities. Shared bicycles, (1) ______ can be found on almost every street corner, have made cycling more convenient than ever. My father is an enthusiastic cyclist who rides his bike to work every day regardless (2) ______ the weather. He says cycling is not only good for his health (3) ______ also helps him avoid the stress of being stuck in traffic. Last October, our family participated (4) ______ a 50-kilometre charity cycling event along the Pearl River. It was the (5) ______ (far) I had ever ridden. The first 30 kilometres were enjoyable — we rode past beautiful parks and riverside scenery. However, the last 20 kilometres were extremely (6) ______ (tire). My legs felt like they were made of stone, and I seriously considered (7) ______ (give) up. But my father kept encouraging me, saying, "You are (8) ______ (strong) than you think!" His words gave me the motivation to keep going. When I finally crossed the finish line, I felt a sense of (9) ______ (achieve) that I had never experienced before. That ride taught me that our bodies are capable of far more than our minds (10) ______ (believe).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `shared bicycles`。

2. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`regardless of` 不管/不论。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but also...` 不仅……而且……。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`participate in` 参加。

5. **答案：farthest / furthest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the farthest I had ever ridden` 我骑过的最远距离。

6. **答案：tiring**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。修饰物/事情，`the last 20 km were tiring`。

7. **答案：giving**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`consider doing sth.` 考虑做某事。

8. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`stronger than you think` 比你想的更强。

9. **答案：achievement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a sense of achievement` 成就感，介词后用名词。

10. **答案：believe**
    - **考点**：一般现在时。`than our minds believe` 比我们头脑所认为的，陈述事实。
