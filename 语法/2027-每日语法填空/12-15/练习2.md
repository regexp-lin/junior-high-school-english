# 12-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校跳绳大赛 (School Rope Skipping Contest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Rope skipping is a simple __1__ effective form of exercise. Last Thursday, our school held its annual rope skipping contest. Students from all __2__ (grade) took part in it. There were three events: individual speed skipping, double-under and group skipping. I signed up __3__ the individual speed event. To prepare for the contest, I had been __4__ (practise) every day for two weeks. My goal was __5__ (skip) more than 180 times in one minute. When the whistle blew, I jumped as __6__ (quick) as I could. My heart was beating fast and my legs felt __7__ (tire), but I did not stop. In the end, I __8__ (manage) to skip 192 times! I was __9__ (extreme) proud of myself. The most exciting event was group skipping, where ten students had to jump together in perfect timing. Watching them was truly __10__ (impress). Our PE teacher said that rope skipping is good for building both strength and coordination.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折/并列连词。simple but effective 简单但有效，but 连接两个形成对比的形容词。

2. **答案：grades**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。all grades 所有年级，all 后接复数名词。

3. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。sign up for 报名参加……，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：practising**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成进行时）。had been practising 一直在练习，表示过去某一时间之前持续进行的动作。

5. **答案：to skip**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作表语）。My goal was to skip 我的目标是跳……，不定式作表语说明主语的内容。

6. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。as quickly as I could 尽可能快地，as...as 之间用副词修饰动词 jumped。

7. **答案：tired**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ed 形容词作表语）。felt tired 感到疲劳，feel 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。

8. **答案：managed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。In the end 最后，描述过去的结果用一般过去时 managed。

9. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 proud，用副词 extremely 表示程度。

10. **答案：impressive**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。was truly impressive 真的令人印象深刻，be 后接形容词作表语。
