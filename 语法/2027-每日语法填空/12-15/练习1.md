# 12-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市夜跑活动 (City Night Running)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Night running has become __1__ (increase) popular among young people in Chinese cities. My father started night running two years ago. He runs along the river near our home for about five __2__ (kilometre) every evening. He says it is a great way __3__ (relax) after a busy day at work. Last month, he invited me to join him. I was a little worried __4__ I was not sure whether I could keep up with him. On our first run together, he ran much __5__ (fast) than me. I was out of breath after just one kilometre. He slowed __6__ and encouraged me to go at my own pace. __7__ (gradual), I got used to the rhythm. Now I can run three kilometres __8__ stopping. The best part about night running is the peaceful __9__ (surround). The city lights reflect on the river, and the cool breeze feels wonderful __10__ our faces. Running together has also given my father and me more time to talk and share our thoughts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 popular，用副词 increasingly 表示"越来越"。

2. **答案：kilometres**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。five kilometres 五公里，基数词 five 后接复数名词。

3. **答案：to relax**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作定语）。a great way to relax 一个放松的好方法，不定式作后置定语修饰 way。

4. **答案：because**
   - **考点**：原因连词。because I was not sure 因为我不确定，because 引导原因状语从句。

5. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。faster than 比……更快，than 提示用比较级。

6. **答案：down**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。slow down 慢下来、减速，为固定短语。

7. **答案：Gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰整个句子，用副词 Gradually 表示"逐渐地"。

8. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。without stopping 不停歇地，without 表示"没有"。

9. **答案：surroundings**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the peaceful surroundings 宁静的环境，surround 的名词形式为 surroundings（常用复数）。

10. **答案：on**
    - **考点**：介词。on our faces 在我们脸上，on 表示接触表面。
