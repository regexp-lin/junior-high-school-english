# 02-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电影院体验 (Cinema Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Going to the cinema is one of my favourite weekend (1) ______ (activity). Although we can easily watch movies at home on our phones or computers, I still prefer the cinema experience. There is something magical about (2) ______ (sit) in a dark room with a huge screen and powerful sound system. Last Saturday, my friends and I went to see a new science fiction film. We arrived at the cinema thirty minutes early (3) ______ that we could get good seats. The film told the story of a group of astronauts (4) ______ were sent on a mission to find a new home for humanity. The special effects were so (5) ______ (real) that I felt as if I (6) ______ (be) actually in space. The plot was full of unexpected twists, and the ending was the most (7) ______ (surprise) part of all. After the film, we went to a nearby café to discuss (8) ______ we had just watched. My friend Chen Wei thought the film was too long, but I (9) ______ (agree) — I thought every scene was necessary for telling the story. One thing we all agreed on was that the lead actor gave an (10) ______ (outstand) performance. It was definitely one of the best films we had seen all year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：activities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of my favourite activities` 最喜欢的活动之一。

2. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `about` 后用动名词。

3. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。`so that` 以便，引导目的状语从句。

4. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `astronauts`，指人。

5. **答案：realistic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so realistic`，`real` 转换为 `realistic`（逼真的）。

6. **答案：were**
   - **考点**：虚拟语气。`as if I were in space`，与事实相反的假设。

7. **答案：surprising**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`the most surprising part`，修饰物。

8. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`discuss what we had watched`，引导宾语从句并作宾语。

9. **答案：disagreed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`but` 表转折，叙述过去的观点，disagree 不同意。

10. **答案：outstanding**
    - **考点**：形容词。`an outstanding performance` 出色的表演。
