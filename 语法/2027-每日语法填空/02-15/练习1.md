# 02-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：乡村支教 (Rural Teaching Volunteer)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My elder sister, who is a university student, volunteered to teach at a primary school in a remote mountain village last summer. The school had only three (1) ______ (teacher) and about sixty students. The classrooms were old and poorly equipped — there were no computers, and some desks were (2) ______ (break). Despite these difficult conditions, the children were eager (3) ______ (learn) and showed great enthusiasm in every lesson. My sister taught them English and art. She said the (4) ______ (hard) part was communicating with the younger children, (5) ______ could not understand Mandarin very well and mainly spoke their local dialect. To overcome this challenge, she used pictures, songs and games to make her lessons (6) ______ (interest) and easy to follow. After two months, many of the students could introduce (7) ______ (they) in simple English. The experience changed my sister (8) ______ (deep). She told me that before going to the village, she had taken many things for granted — good schools, plenty of books and reliable Internet. Now she (9) ______ (understand) how fortunate she is and wants to do more to help children in need. She is planning to return to the same village this summer, and this time I have decided to go (10) ______ her.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：teachers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`three teachers` 三位老师。

2. **答案：broken**
   - **考点**：过去分词作表语。`desks were broken` 桌子是坏的。

3. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be eager to do sth.` 渴望做某事。

4. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the hardest part` 最难的部分。

5. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，修饰 `the younger children`。

6. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`make lessons interesting`，修饰物用 -ing。

7. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`introduce themselves` 介绍他们自己。

8. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`changed her deeply` 深深地改变了她。

9. **答案：understands**
   - **考点**：一般现在时三单。`Now` 表示现在的状态。

10. **答案：with**
    - **考点**：介词。`go with her` 和她一起去。
