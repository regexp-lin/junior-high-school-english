# 02-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：拜年习俗 (New Year Greetings)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Paying New Year visits, or "bainian," is one of the most cherished traditions during the Spring Festival period. Starting from the first day of the new lunar year, people visit friends and (1) ______ (relative) to exchange greetings and good wishes. When visiting, it is customary to bring gifts such as fruit, tea or sweets. The choice of gift is important — for example, you should never give a clock, (2) ______ the Chinese word for "giving a clock" sounds similar to a phrase (3) ______ (mean) "attending a funeral." During these visits, children are the (4) ______ (lucky) ones, as they receive red envelopes from their elders. A typical greeting between adults is "Gong Xi Fa Cai," (5) ______ means "Wishing you wealth and prosperity." In the past, people would visit each household in person. However, as life has become (6) ______ (busy), many people now send their greetings (7) ______ text messages or video calls. My grandmother thinks this is a shame. She believes that face-to-face visits are much more (8) ______ (meaning) than digital messages. She has a point — there is something special about sitting in someone's living room, drinking tea and (9) ______ (chat) about the past year that cannot be replicated on a screen. This year, I made a conscious effort (10) ______ (visit) all my elderly relatives in person. The joy on their faces when they saw me made it all worthwhile.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：relatives**
   - **考点**：名词复数。与 `friends` 并列，用复数形式。

2. **答案：as / because**
   - **考点**：连词。引导原因状语从句，解释为什么不能送钟。

3. **答案：meaning**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`a phrase meaning`，主动关系。

4. **答案：luckiest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the luckiest ones`。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面的短语。

6. **答案：busier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`as life has become busier`，暗含与过去的比较。

7. **答案：through / via**
   - **考点**：介词。`send greetings through text messages`，表示通过某种方式。

8. **答案：meaningful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`much more meaningful`，系动词后接形容词。

9. **答案：chatting**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `sitting` 和 `drinking` 并列。

10. **答案：to visit**
    - **考点**：不定式。`made an effort to visit`，不定式作后置定语。
