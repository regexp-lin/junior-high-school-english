# 02-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：冰雪运动 (Ice and Snow Sports)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Winter sports have been growing (1) ______ (rapid) in popularity across China, especially since the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics. Before the Olympics, very few people in southern China (2) ______ (try) skiing or ice skating. Now, indoor ski resorts and ice rinks can (3) ______ (find) in many major cities. My cousin, who lives in Guangzhou, went skiing for the first time last February. At first, she was afraid (4) ______ falling, but after a few lessons, she was able to ski down a beginner slope (5) ______ (confident). She enjoyed the experience so much that she (6) ______ (plan) to visit a real mountain resort next winter. The government has set a goal of getting 300 million people (7) ______ (involve) in ice and snow sports. This goal has already been met — in fact, the number of participants has grown far (8) ______ (large) than expected. Ice hockey and figure skating (9) ______ especially popular among young people. Schools across the country have started offering winter sports courses as part of their PE programs. The hope is (10) ______ China will continue to develop strong athletes who can compete at the highest level in future Winter Olympics.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`have been growing rapidly`，修饰现在完成进行时。

2. **答案：had tried**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`Before the Olympics` 表示在过去某个时间之前的动作。

3. **答案：be found**
   - **考点**：含情态动词的被动语态。`can be found`，被发现。

4. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be afraid of doing sth.` 害怕做某事。

5. **答案：confidently**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。修饰 `ski down`。

6. **答案：is planning / plans**
   - **考点**：时态。根据 `next winter` 可知用现在进行时表将来或一般现在时。

7. **答案：involved**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`get sb. involved in`，使某人参与。

8. **答案：larger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`far larger than expected`，`far` 修饰比较级。

9. **答案：are**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`Ice hockey and figure skating` 为复数主语，用 `are`。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：连词。`The hope is that...`，引导表语从句。
