# 12-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：共享单车的利与弊 (Pros and Cons of Bike-sharing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Bike-sharing has changed the way people travel in Chinese __1__ (city). With just a smartphone, you can unlock a shared bicycle and ride it to your __2__ (destine). It is cheap, convenient and good for the __3__ (environ). However, bike-sharing also brings some problems. Many bikes __4__ (park) in the wrong places, blocking pavements and causing trouble for pedestrians. Some people even damage the bikes __5__ purpose. Last month, our school held a debate about whether bike-sharing does more good __6__ harm. Those who supported it argued that it __7__ (great) reduces air pollution and traffic jams. Those who were against it pointed __8__ that the messy parking situation needs to be solved first. After the debate, most students agreed __9__ bike-sharing is a good idea but that users need to follow the rules. I think the key is for everyone to be more __10__ (response). If we all park bikes properly and take care of them, bike-sharing can truly make our cities better.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：cities**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。Chinese cities 中国城市，city 的复数为 cities。

2. **答案：destination**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。your destination 你的目的地，your 后接名词。

3. **答案：environment**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。the environment 环境，the 后接名词。

4. **答案：are parked**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般现在时被动语态）。bikes 与 park 为被动关系，描述普遍现象用一般现在时被动 are parked。

5. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on purpose 故意地，为固定搭配。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。more good than harm 利大于弊，more... than... 表示"比……更多"。

7. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 reduces，用副词 greatly。

8. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。point out 指出，为固定短语。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词（宾语从句）。agreed that 同意……，that 引导宾语从句。

10. **答案：responsible**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。be more responsible 更有责任感，be 后接形容词作表语。
