# 12-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校期末表彰大会 (School End-of-term Awards Ceremony)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school held its end-of-term awards ceremony in the auditorium. The __1__ (excite) in the air was obvious as students gathered to celebrate the __2__ (achieve) of the past term. The ceremony began with a speech __3__ our headmaster. He praised all students for their hard work and reminded us that every small effort __4__ (count). Several awards were given out, including "Outstanding Student", "Most Improved" and "Best Team Player". My classmate Wang Fang won the "Most Improved" award. She __5__ (work) incredibly hard this term and her grades went from the bottom to the top ten of our class. When she walked onto the stage, the whole audience __6__ (clap) for her. She looked both surprised __7__ proud. In her short speech, she __8__ (simple) said, "Never compare yourself __9__ others. Just try to be better than you were yesterday." Her words touched everyone in the hall. The ceremony ended with a wonderful choir __10__ (perform). It was a perfect way to wrap up the term.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：excitement**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。The excitement 兴奋感，the 后接名词，excite 的名词形式为 excitement。

2. **答案：achievements**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。celebrate the achievements 庆祝成就，achieve 的名词形式为 achievement，此处用复数。

3. **答案：by/from**
   - **考点**：介词。a speech by/from our headmaster 校长的演讲，by/from 表示"来自"。

4. **答案：counts**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。every small effort counts 每一份小小的努力都重要，every + 单数名词作主语，谓语用第三人称单数。

5. **答案：had worked**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。had worked this term 这学期一直在努力，在获奖（过去）之前已付出的努力，用过去完成时。

6. **答案：clapped**
   - **考点**：动词时态。When she walked 当她走上台时，描述过去的动作用一般过去时 clapped。

7. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。both surprised and proud 既惊讶又骄傲，both... and... 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：simply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 said，用副词 simply 表示"简单地"。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。compare yourself with 把自己和……比较，为固定搭配。

10. **答案：performance**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a choir performance 合唱表演，choir 修饰名词，perform 的名词形式为 performance。
