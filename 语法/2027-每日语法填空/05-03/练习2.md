# 05-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园微电影 (School Micro Film)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's film club decided 1. ______ (shoot) a micro film about friendship before the term ended. The story was simple: two classmates solve 2. ______ problem together after 3. ______ misunderstanding. We borrowed 4. ______ camera from the art teacher and wrote the script on the weekend. During filming, the actors had to repeat one scene many times 5. ______ it started to rain. "Don't worry," the director said. "We can make the rain look 6. ______ (interest) on screen." After two weeks of editing, the film was 7. ______ (show) on Youth Day. When the lights went down and the music began, everyone watched 8. ______ (we) work quietly. At the last scene, the whole hall cheered. I realized 9. ______ a short video could carry so much warmth. Making this film was 10. ______ unforgettable experience for all of us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to shoot**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**decide to do sth.** 表示“决定做某事”，故填 **to shoot**。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **problem** 单数，表泛指“一个问题”，故填 **a**。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**misunderstanding** 在此表泛指“一次误会”，且以辅音音素开头，故填 **a**。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **camera** 单数，表“一台相机”，故填 **a**。

5. **答案：until**
   - **考点**：连词。**until** 引导时间状语从句，表示“一直重复某一镜头，直到开始下雨为止”，故填 **until**。

6. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。**look** 为系动词，后接形容词；修饰物（the rain）用 **interesting**（令人感兴趣的）。

7. **答案：shown**
   - **考点**：被动语态。主语 **the film** 与 **show** 为被动关系，**was + 过去分词**，show 的过去分词为 **shown**。

8. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 **work** 需用形容词性物主代词，故填 **our**。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**realized** 后接宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 **that** 引导。

10. **答案：an**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **experience** 表泛指“一次难忘的经历”，unforgettable 以元音音素开头，故填 **an**。
