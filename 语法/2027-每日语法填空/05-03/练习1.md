# 05-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：立夏习俗 (Beginning of Summer Customs)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Beginning of Summer is one 1. ______ the twenty-four solar terms in China. It usually falls 2. ______ early May, when the weather turns warmer and plants grow 3. ______ (quick). In my hometown, families like to eat eggs on this day because people believe eggs bring good health 4. ______ the hot season. Grandmother also cooks special rice 5. ______ fresh peas. "This meal connects us 6. ______ the land," she always says. Last year, our class visited a farm 7. ______ (learn) how farmers decide the best time to plant seeds. The guide told us that traditions are not just old habits; they teach us 8. ______ (respect) nature. If we keep these customs alive, younger children will understand 9. ______ their roots are and why seasons matter. After the trip, I wrote 10. ______ short poem about summer in my diary.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。**one of + 复数名词** 表示“……之一”，故填 **of**。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。月份前用介词 **in**，**in early May** 表示“在五月初”，故填 **in**。

3. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **grow** 需用副词，形容词 quick 的副词形式为 **quickly**。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。表示“在炎热的季节里”用 **in the hot season**，季节前常用介词 **in**，故填 **in**。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**cook rice with fresh peas** 表示“用新鲜豌豆煮饭”，**with** 表“用；带有”，故填 **with**。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**connect...to...** 表示“把……与……联系起来”，故填 **to**。

7. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。此处表目的“为了学习……”，用不定式 **to learn** 作目的状语。

8. **答案：to respect**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**teach sb. to do sth.** 表示“教某人做某事”，故填 **to respect**。

9. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：连接词。宾语从句中缺少地点含义，“他们的根在哪里”，故填 **where**。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **poem** 单数，表“一首短诗”，short 以辅音音素开头，故填 **a**。
