# 05-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园乒乓球赛 (School Table Tennis Tournament)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last week, our school held 1. ______ table tennis tournament in the gym. Twenty-four players from eight classes took part, and matches were played 2. ______ groups first. I was nervous before my first game because my opponent was 3. ______ (tall) boy in our grade. He served fast, 4. ______ I moved my feet quickly and returned the ball. The score stayed close 5. ______ the last point. When I finally won, my classmates cheered 6. ______ (loud). In the semifinal, I lost to a girl 7. ______ had practiced for years. She thanked me 8. ______ a firm handshake. Although I did not get 9. ______ gold medal, I learned 10. ______ staying calm matters as much as skill.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **tournament** 单数，表泛指，故填 **a**。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。**in groups** 表示“分组进行”，故填 **in**。

3. **答案：the tallest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。由范围 **in our grade** 可知用 **tall** 的最高级 **the tallest**。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前后为转折关系，故填 **but**。

5. **答案：until**
   - **考点**：连词。**until the last point** 表示“直到最后一分”，故填 **until**。

6. **答案：loudly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **cheered**，用副词 **loudly**。

7. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词 **a girl**（人），定语从句中作主语，故填 **who** 或 **that**。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**thank sb. with a handshake** 表示“以握手致谢”，故填 **with**。

9. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。**the gold medal** 特指金牌，故填 **the**。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：连接词。**learned** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。
