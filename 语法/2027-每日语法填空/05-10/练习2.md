# 05-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：古村落保护 (Protecting Ancient Villages)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ancient villages are living museums, 1. ______ they need careful protection. In one mountain village, old houses 2. ______ (build) of wood and stone have stood for centuries. Last year, volunteers came 3. ______ (repair) broken roofs and clean the stone paths. They also talked with local people about 4. ______ tourists should behave politely. A guide explained 5. ______ some traditions, such as hanging red paper, carry special meanings. Visitors are asked not 6. ______ (touch) the carvings on the doors. If we respect the past, these places will remain 7. ______ (live) for future generations. My uncle, 8. ______ is a designer, helps choose colors that match the old style. He believes small changes can make 9. ______ big difference. I hope more students will join programs 10. ______ this and learn history with their hands.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“古村落是活博物馆”与“需要细心保护”为转折关系，故填 **but**。

2. **答案：built**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**houses built of...** 中 **built** 为过去分词作后置定语，表“由……建造的房子”，故填 **built**。

3. **答案：to repair**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的“来修补……”，故填 **to repair**。

4. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。介词 **about** 后接宾语从句，表示“游客应如何文明举止”，故填 **how**。

5. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**explained** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

6. **答案：to touch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**ask sb. not to do sth.**，故填 **to touch**。

7. **答案：alive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。**remain alive** 表示“继续存在、保持活力”，用形容词 **alive**（**live** 作形容词多表“现场的”，此处用 **alive** 更贴切）。

8. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词 **My uncle**（人），非限制性定语从句中作主语，故填 **who**。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**make a big difference** 为固定搭配，故填 **a**。

10. **答案：like**
    - **考点**：介词。**programs like this** 表示“像这样的项目”，故填 **like**。
