# 07-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：一节难忘的课 (An Unforgettable Lesson)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our science teacher, Mr. Chen, gave us ___1___ lesson that I will never forget. Instead of reading from the textbook, he brought ___2___ small plant and a glass tank filled with water into the classroom. "Today we will see ___3___ plants really 'drink' water," he said with ___4___ (excite) smile. The whole room fell silent, and we all leaned a little closer to the desk.

He asked Tom ___5___ (tie) a piece of string gently around the stem and then put the plant in the tank. For the next twenty minutes, we watched ___6___ (patient) as tiny bubbles appeared on the leaves. Mr. Chen explained that the plant was taking in carbon dioxide and that the process ___7___ (call) photosynthesis. Even students who usually found science boring began to take notes. I had never thought science could be so clear and lively before that moment.

At the end of the class, he encouraged us ___8___ (ask) questions. I raised my hand and asked ___9___ we could try the same experiment at home. He said yes and reminded us ___10___ (work) with an adult. Some classmates promised to share photos of their own mini experiments in our group chat. That lesson taught me that learning is not only about words on a page—it is about seeing and understanding the world around us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。lesson 为可数名词单数，此处表示“一节（难忘的）课”，表泛指，且 lesson 以辅音音素开头，故用不定冠词 a。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。small plant 首次提到，表泛指“一株小植物”，small 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

3. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。see 后为宾语从句，从句意为“植物究竟如何‘喝水’”，表示方式，用 how。

4. **答案：an excited**
   - **考点**：词性转换与冠词。修饰名词 smile，需用形容词；形容人的神情用 excited（感到兴奋的）；excite 以元音音素开头，前面用 an。

5. **答案：to tie**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 为固定结构，意为“要求某人做某事”，故用动词不定式 to tie。

6. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 watched，需用副词；patient 的副词形式为 patiently。

7. **答案：was called**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。主语 the process 与 call 之间为被动关系（过程被称为……）；叙述过去课堂上的事，用一般过去时的被动语态 was called。

8. **答案：to ask**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。encourage sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配，意为“鼓励某人做某事”，用不定式 to ask。

9. **答案：if / whether**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。asked 后为宾语从句，意为“我们是否可以在家做同样的实验”，表示“是否”，用 if 或 whether。

10. **答案：to work**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。remind sb. to do sth. 为固定搭配，意为“提醒某人做某事”，用不定式 to work。
