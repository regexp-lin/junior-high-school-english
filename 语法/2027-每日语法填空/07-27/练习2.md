# 07-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市绿化 (Urban Greening)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many cities around the world are trying to become greener places for people ___1___ live in. Trees and parks do not just make streets ___2___ (beauty); they also help clean the air and reduce noise from traffic. Birds and insects return when green areas grow, which makes neighborhoods feel alive again. In some crowded areas, new vertical gardens ___3___ (build) on the walls of tall buildings so that residents can enjoy a touch of nature without ___4___ (go) far from home.

Experts say that urban greening is one of ___5___ (good) ways to fight air pollution and high temperatures in summer. ___6___ more trees are planted along roads, the city becomes cooler and more pleasant. Local governments have spent millions of dollars on this project, and the results ___7___ (be) encouraging so far. Citizens are also invited to join tree-planting events on weekends. These activities help young people learn why every single tree matters in a busy city.

However, caring for these green spaces needs long-term effort. Volunteers often help water young plants and pick up litter in the parks. Small actions like these keep public gardens tidy and welcoming. If everyone plays ___8___ part, our cities will be ___9___ (healthy) and more comfortable in the future. I believe that a greener city is not only ___10___ dream—it is a goal we can reach together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：不定式作后置定语。places for people to live in 表示“供人们居住的地方”，for sb. to do sth. 为常见结构，live 与 places 逻辑上搭配，用 to live。

2. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换与宾语补足语。make + 宾语 + 形容词，表示“使……变得……”；beauty 的形容词形式为 beautiful。

3. **答案：are built**
   - **考点**：动词的时态和语态。主语 vertical gardens 与动词 build 之间为被动关系（花园被建在墙上）；陈述当前普遍情况，用一般现在时的被动语态 are built。

4. **答案：going**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。without 为介词，后接动词时需用动名词形式 going。

5. **答案：the best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。“one of + the + 形容词最高级 + 可数名词复数”表示“最……的……之一”，good 的最高级为 best，前加 the。

6. **答案：As / When**
   - **考点**：连词。句意为“当沿路种下更多树时，城市变得更凉爽、更宜人”，表时间或伴随，可用 As（随着/当……时）或 When（当……时）。

7. **答案：have been**
   - **考点**：动词的时态。时间状语 so far（到目前为止）常与现在完成时连用；主语 the results 为复数，故用 have been。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词与固定搭配。play a part 为固定短语，意为“发挥作用；尽一份力”，part 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

9. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。and 连接并列结构，后文为 more comfortable，前文也应用比较级与“未来更……”呼应；healthy 的比较级为 healthier。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。dream 可数，此处表示“不仅仅是一个梦想”，表泛指，dream 以辅音音素开头，用 a。
