# 07-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：网上购物 (Online Shopping)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Nowadays, online shopping is growing ___1___ a fast speed among middle school students across the country. When they need new schoolbags or sports shoes, many teenagers prefer ___2___ (order) those items from trusted websites rather than spend a whole afternoon in crowded malls. ___3___ a few simple steps on a phone, they can compare colours and sizes, read comments from other buyers, and finish payment, then wait for the courier ___4___ (call) when the package is near their home. During holidays, delivery services are often busier, ___5___ most orders still arrive within a few days if the address is written clearly. Group deals among classmates have also become popular, because sharing delivery fees makes small orders more affordable.

For most families, price is still highly ___6___ (importance) when they choose products online. Wise buyers often visit several shops to get ___7___ (good) value for money before they press "confirm". Yet the lowest price does not always mean ___8___ (good) choice; some clothes look nice in photos but ___9___ (feel) uncomfortable in real life. If you ignore the size chart, you may feel quite ___10___ (disappoint) when you open the box. In addition, many platforms allow returns within a week, so comparing products carefully can help you avoid wasting pocket money.

Safety matters online, too. Never follow strange links or tell strangers your verification codes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。`at a fast speed` 为固定搭配，表示“以很快的速度”，说明网上购物在青少年中增长得快。根据空格后的 `a fast speed` 可判断此处填介词 `at`，不能用 `with` 或 `in` 的常见错误搭配。

2. **答案：to order**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`prefer to do sth. rather than do sth.` 为固定结构，表示“宁愿做……而不愿做……”，`rather than` 后多用动词原形，前面 `prefer` 后须用不定式 `to order`。若用 `ordering` 则与 `prefer` 的常见中考结构不符。

3. **答案：With / In**
   - **考点**：介词。`With a few simple steps` 表示“通过几个简单步骤”，强调方式；也可用 `In a few simple steps`（在几个简单步骤之内完成）。两者在语义连贯的前提下均可接受，中考更常见 `With` 表“借助、通过”。

4. **答案：to call**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`wait for sb. to do sth.` 为固定句型，意为“等待某人做某事”，此处指等待快递员打电话，故填不定式 `to call`。不可填 `calling`，因不符合该句型。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前半句说节假日快递更忙，后半句说多数订单仍在几天内送达，语义为转折，故用并列连词 `but`。也可用 `yet`（书面语），但 `but` 最贴近初中教材用法。

6. **答案：important**
   - **考点**：词性转换。括号内为名词 `importance`，句中作表语且前有副词 `highly` 修饰，需用形容词 `important`，构成 `be highly important`，表示“非常重要”。不可填 `importance`，因系动词后此处需要形容词作表语。

7. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。下文隐含在多家网店之间比较，且 `get ... value for money` 表示“物有所值”，暗含“更划算”，故用 `good` 的比较级 `better`。没有 `than` 时，语境仍支持用比较级表示“更优的性价比”。

8. **答案：the best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`mean the best choice` 表示“意味着最佳选择”，与上文“最低价”形成对比：便宜不一定对应“最好”的选择。`good` 的最高级为 `best`，且特指在若干选项中的“最佳”，前面加定冠词 `the`。

9. **答案：feel**
   - **考点**：动词时态与并列谓语。`but` 连接两个并列谓语，前面为 `look`（一般现在时），主语 `some clothes` 为复数，后面动词也用原形 `feel`，与 `look` 并列。不可加 `s`，主语为复数。

10. **答案：disappointed**
    - **考点**：词性转换（-ed 形容词）。主语为 `you`，表示人的感受，用过去分词转化的形容词 `disappointed`（感到失望的）。`disappointing` 用于修饰事物，表示“令人失望的”，此处若填则与主语人称不符。
