# 07-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：海洋动物保护 (Protecting Ocean Animals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ocean covers more than seventy percent of the earth, ___1___ it provides a home for whales, sharks, and countless smaller fish. Coral reefs support colourful species that tourists love to watch. Sadly, human activities ___2___ (become) a serious threat in the past few decades. Oil spills poison water, ___3___ loud noises from ships may confuse dolphins that use sound ___4___ (find) their way and food. City garbage flows down rivers and gathers in patches on the surface, where turtles sometimes mistake plastic bags for jellyfish. Warming seawater and stronger storms are damaging coastal habitats that young fish rely on for shelter.

Many countries have ___5___ (take) action by setting up protected areas where fishing ___6___ (be) strictly controlled. Non-governmental organizations also run campaigns ___7___ (teach) children how plastic may cause ___8___ (die) in sea birds when sharp pieces hurt their bodies. Scientists say we should not buy jewellery made from tortoise shell or coral, ___9___ these habits encourage illegal trade. Volunteers sometimes dive into shallow water to free animals from old ropes and nets. Beach schools invite scientists to explain how simple choices, such as carrying a metal straw, can reduce harm to turtles and seals.

If everyone refuses single-use plastic on picnics and beach trips, the future ___10___ (look) brighter for ocean life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前一分句讲海洋占地球表面比例，后一分句讲海洋为生物提供家园，两句为顺承并列关系，故用 `and`。不可用 `but`（无转折），也不宜用 `so`（此处并非直接因果关系）。

2. **答案：have become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 `in the past few decades`（在过去几十年里）是现在完成时的典型标志，表示从过去持续到现在的影响；主语 `human activities` 为复数，助动词用 `have`，故填 `have become`。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。`Oil spills poison water` 与 `loud noises ... may confuse dolphins` 为两个并列的人类危害，用 `and` 连接。也可用 `while` 表对比，但 `and` 最直接。

4. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。`use sth. to do sth.` 表示“用某物做某事”，此处指海豚用声音来辨别路线和寻找食物，故填 `to find`。这是典型的动词不定式作目的状语。

5. **答案：taken**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。句首已有 `have`，与过去分词构成现在完成时，`take action` 的过去分词为 `taken`，故填 `taken`。注意不规则变化 `take — took — taken`。

6. **答案：is**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。`where` 引导定语从句修饰 `areas`，从句主语为不可数名词 `fishing`（捕鱼活动），谓语用单数；陈述一般规定或现状，用一般现在时 `is`。不可填 `are`。

7. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`run campaigns to do sth.` 中不定式 `to teach` 作目的状语，说明开展宣传活动的目的是教育孩子。也可理解为 `campaigns` 后接不定式表目的，为中考常见结构。

8. **答案：death**
   - **考点**：词性转换。括号内为动词 `die`，空前为动词 `cause`，`cause` 后需接名词作宾语，故填名词 `death`（死亡）。不可填 `dying` 除非语境强调过程，此处“导致死亡”用 `death` 更自然。

9. **答案：because / as / since**
   - **考点**：连词（原因状语从句）。后半句“这些习惯助长非法交易”是前半句“不应购买玳瑁壳、珊瑚制品”的原因，故用 `because`（语气最强）、`as` 或 `since`（书面语中表已知原因）均可。

10. **答案：will look**
    - **考点**：动词时态。`If` 引导条件状语从句，从句用一般现在时 `refuses` 表将来，主句须用一般将来时，故填 `will look`。符合“主将从现”原则。不可填 `looks`，否则与条件句时态搭配不当。
