# 12-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：回顾一年的成长 (Reflecting on a Year of Growth)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As I sit at my desk on the last day of the year, I can't help __1__ (think) about everything that has happened in the past twelve months. This year __2__ (be) full of both challenges and joys. In March, I entered my school's English speech competition and won third prize. It was the first competition I __3__ (ever win). In June, I said goodbye to some of my __4__ (close) friends who moved to other cities. That was one of the __5__ (sad) moments of the year. During the summer holiday, I volunteered __6__ a local nursing home and read stories to the elderly. Their warm smiles taught me the true __7__ (mean) of giving. In September, our school welcomed a new English teacher __8__ came from the UK. His classes were so lively __9__ everyone loved them. Now, as I look forward to the coming year, I feel more confident and __10__ (prepare) than ever. I know there will be new challenges, but I also know I am strong enough to face them.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：thinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。can't help thinking 忍不住想，can't help doing sth. 表示"忍不住做某事"。

2. **答案：has been**
   - **考点**：动词时态。This year 今年，到目前为止的情况，用现在完成时 has been。

3. **答案：had ever won**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。the first competition I had ever won 我曾经赢过的第一个比赛，在过去某个时间点之前的经历用过去完成时。

4. **答案：closest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。my closest friends 我最亲密的朋友，close 的最高级为 closest。

5. **答案：saddest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the saddest moments 最伤心的时刻之一，one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词。

6. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。volunteered at a nursing home 在养老院做志愿者，at 表示"在某处"。

7. **答案：meaning**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the true meaning 真正的含义，the 和 true 后接名词。

8. **答案：who/that**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。who/that 引导定语从句修饰 teacher，在从句中作主语。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：结果状语从句。so lively that 如此生动以至于……，so... that... 为固定句型。

10. **答案：prepared**
    - **考点**：词性转换（过去分词作形容词）。feel prepared 感到准备好了，与前面的 confident 并列作表语。
