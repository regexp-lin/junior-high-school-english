# 12-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：跨年夜倒计时 (New Year's Eve Countdown)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tonight is New Year's Eve, the last night of the year. My family has a tradition of __1__ (stay) up late to welcome the new year together. This evening, my mother prepared a table full of __2__ (delight) food, including dumplings, spring rolls and sweet rice cakes. After dinner, we sat in the living room and watched the New Year's Eve gala __3__ TV. The performances were so __4__ (entertain) that we laughed and clapped throughout the show. At 11:50 p.m., my father suggested that we go to the balcony __5__ (watch) the fireworks. The night sky was cold __6__ clear. As the clock struck midnight, fireworks __7__ (light) up the sky in every direction. People in the neighbourhood cheered and shouted, "Happy New Year!" I made a wish __8__ (quiet) in my heart. I wished for good health for my family and better grades for __9__ (I) in the coming year. Standing there with my family under the __10__ (beauty) fireworks, I felt truly grateful. A new year means a fresh start, and I am ready to embrace it with open arms.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：staying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。a tradition of staying up 熬夜的传统，of 为介词，后接动名词。

2. **答案：delightful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。delightful food 令人愉快的美食，修饰名词用形容词。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on TV 在电视上，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：entertaining**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ing 形容词）。so entertaining 如此有趣，so + 形容词 + that 引导结果状语从句。

5. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。go to the balcony to watch 去阳台看，不定式作目的状语。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。cold but clear 寒冷但晴朗，but 连接两个形成对比的形容词。

7. **答案：lit**
   - **考点**：动词时态。As the clock struck 当钟敲响时，描述过去的动作用一般过去时，light 的过去式为 lit。

8. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 made a wish，用副词 quietly。

9. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。for myself 为我自己，与主语 I 指同一人用反身代词。

10. **答案：beautiful**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。beautiful fireworks 美丽的烟花，修饰名词用形容词。
