# 12-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：地铁文明乘车 (Civilised Behaviour on the Metro)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The metro is one of the __1__ (convenient) ways to travel in big cities. Millions of people ride it every day. __2__, not everyone knows how to behave properly on the metro. Last week, our class had a discussion __3__ civilised metro behaviour. We all agreed that passengers should queue up and let people get __4__ the train before getting on. It is also important to offer your seat to those __5__ need it more, such as elderly people, pregnant women and young __6__ (child). Playing loud music or videos __7__ (be) considered rude because it disturbs other passengers. Our teacher shared a story about a boy who __8__ (quiet) picked up rubbish left by other passengers. His small act of __9__ (kind) was recorded by a security camera and later went viral online. The story reminds us that a little consideration for others can make __10__ big difference. Let's all be more thoughtful when using public transport.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most convenient**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the most convenient 最方便的……之一，多音节形容词用 most + 原级构成最高级。

2. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：转折副词。前面说地铁很便利，后面说不是所有人都知道如何文明乘车，语义转折用 However。

3. **答案：about/on**
   - **考点**：介词。a discussion about/on 关于……的讨论，about/on 表示"关于"。

4. **答案：off**
   - **考点**：介词（短语动词）。get off the train 下车，off 表示"离开"。

5. **答案：who/that**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。who/that 引导定语从句修饰 those，在从句中作主语。

6. **答案：children**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。young children 年幼的孩子，child 的复数为 children。

7. **答案：is**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。Playing loud music or videos 播放大声的音乐或视频，动名词短语作主语，谓语用第三人称单数 is。

8. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 picked up，用副词 quietly。

9. **答案：kindness**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。act of kindness 善举，of 后接名词，kind 的名词形式为 kindness。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。make a big difference 产生很大影响，为固定搭配，用不定冠词 a。
