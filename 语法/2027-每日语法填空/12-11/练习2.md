# 12-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：制作手工肥皂 (Making Handmade Soap)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, my mother and I attended a handmade soap workshop at the community centre. The instructor __1__ (show) us how to make soap using natural materials. We needed olive oil, coconut oil and some dried __2__ (flower). The process was not as simple __3__ I had thought. First, we had to measure every ingredient __4__ (accurate). Then we mixed them together and heated the mixture __5__ (slow) for about thirty minutes. The most __6__ (enjoy) part was adding colours and shapes. I chose to make my soap in the shape of __7__ star. After the soap was __8__ (complete) cooled down, I could see the tiny flowers inside it. It looked like a small work of art. My mother made two pieces — one for __9__ (she) and one as a gift for our neighbour. The instructor told us that handmade soap is better for the skin __10__ it contains no harmful chemicals. I plan to make more soaps and give them to my friends as Christmas presents.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：showed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last weekend 上周末，描述过去发生的事用一般过去时 showed。

2. **答案：flowers**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。dried flowers 干花，some 后接可数名词复数。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词（比较结构）。not as simple as 不像……那么简单，as...as 表示"和……一样"。

4. **答案：accurately**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 measure，用副词 accurately。

5. **答案：slowly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 heated，用副词 slowly。

6. **答案：enjoyable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。the most enjoyable part 最令人愉快的部分，修饰名词 part 用形容词。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。in the shape of a star 呈星形，star 为可数名词，首次提及用不定冠词 a。

8. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 cooled down，用副词 completely 表示程度。

9. **答案：herself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。for herself 给她自己，for oneself 为固定搭配。

10. **答案：because**
    - **考点**：原因连词。because it contains 因为它含有，because 引导原因状语从句。
