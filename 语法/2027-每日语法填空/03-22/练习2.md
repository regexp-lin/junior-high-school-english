# 03-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园科学实验室 (School Science Lab)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's new science lab opened last semester. It has bright lights, modern equipment, and strict safety rules that every student must follow.

Before entering the lab, we put on long coats and safety glasses. The teacher checks (1) ______ we have tied our hair back if it is long. No food or drinks (2) ______ (allow) inside.

In our first experiment, we observed how plants absorb water. We placed white flowers in bottles filled with colored water. Twenty-four hours later, the petals turned light pink and blue. The whole class cheered (3) ______ we saw the result under the microscope.

Another lesson I enjoyed was about acids and bases. We used red cabbage juice (4) ______ (test) unknown liquids. When the juice changed color, we recorded the data in our notebooks (5) ______ (care).

The lab assistant, Mr. Han, always reminds us (6) ______ (wash) hands after touching chemicals. He says science is exciting, (7) ______ safety always comes first.

Thanks to the new lab, more students have joined the science club. We are planning to build a small weather station on the roof next month. If the project succeeds, we (8) ______ (share) our findings with younger classes.

I used to think science was only about memorizing facts. Now I know it is about asking questions and (9) ______ (search) for answers with patience.

I believe every school should give teenagers enough space (10) ______ explore the world around them.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / whether / if**
   - **考点**：宾语从句。`checks that/whether/if we have tied` 检查是否已扎好头发，that 表陈述事实，whether/if 表“是否”。

2. **答案：are allowed**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。食物饮料不被允许带入，rules 陈述规定。

3. **答案：when / as**
   - **考点**：连词。`cheered when we saw` 当看到结果时欢呼。

4. **答案：to test**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`use juice to test` 用菜汁来检测，不定式表目的。

5. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `recorded`，`care` → `carefully`。

6. **答案：to wash**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`remind sb. to do sth.` 提醒某人做某事。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。科学令人兴奋，但安全第一。

8. **答案：will share**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`If the project succeeds` 主将从现。

9. **答案：searching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`about asking ... and searching` 介词后动名词，与 asking 并列。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：不定式。`give sb. space to explore` 给某人探索的空间，不定式作定语。
