# 03-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国剪纸 (Chinese Paper Cutting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese paper cutting is an ancient folk art that turns red paper into delicate pictures. It is often seen at weddings and Spring Festival celebrations (1) ______ people wish for good luck.

Last winter, I visited a small museum in the old town. There I met Grandma Zhou, an artist (2) ______ (age) seventy-five, who has practiced paper cutting for over sixty years. She showed me how to fold the paper and use small scissors (3) ______ (create) patterns of flowers and animals.

At first, my hands were clumsy, and I cut the paper into pieces by mistake. Grandma Zhou laughed kindly and told me not (4) ______ (lose) heart. "Every master was once (5) ______ beginner," she said.

After two hours of practice, I managed to finish a simple design of a fish. It was not as beautiful as Grandma Zhou's works, (6) ______ I took it home and put it on my window. My parents were surprised (7) ______ my progress.

Paper cutting is more than decoration. In northern villages, windows (8) ______ (cover) with red paper cuts to keep away bad luck in winter. Today, young designers are combining traditional skills (9) ______ modern styles to make new products such as bookmarks and lamp covers.

I hope this art will be passed down to future generations. If schools offer more hands-on courses, more students (10) ______ (fall) in love with paper cutting.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `celebrations` 表时间，从句缺状语，用 when。

2. **答案：aged**
   - **考点**：形容词。`an artist aged seventy-five` 七十五岁的艺术家。

3. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`use scissors to create` 用剪刀创作，不定式表目的。

4. **答案：to lose**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`tell sb. not to do sth.` 告诉某人不要做某事。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`once a beginner` 曾经也是初学者。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。不如大师作品美，但我带回家装饰窗户。

7. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be surprised at` 对……感到惊讶。

8. **答案：are covered**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。窗户“被”剪纸覆盖，陈述习俗。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`combine ... with ...` 把传统与现代风格结合。

10. **答案：will fall**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。`If` 从句一般现在时，主句用 will（主将从现）。
