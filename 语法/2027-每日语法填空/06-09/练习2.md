# 06-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：共享单车 (Shared Bicycles)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Shared bicycles have changed the way people travel in Chinese cities. ___1___ just scanning a QR code on your phone, you can unlock a bike and ride it anywhere. This service is especially ___2___ (help) for short trips between the subway station ___3___ your home. According to a report, shared bikes ___4___ (use) over 50 million times a day in China. They are not only convenient ___5___ also good for the environment because they produce zero pollution. ___6___, there are some problems. Some riders park bikes ___7___ (care) on narrow sidewalks, blocking the way for pedestrians. Others even damage the bikes on purpose. To solve these issues, companies have developed better ___8___ (lock) systems and introduced rules ___9___ (punish) bad behaviour. I think shared bicycles are a great ___10___ (invent), but we all need to use them responsibly.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：By**
   - **考点**：介词。by doing sth. 表示"通过做某事"，By scanning 表示"通过扫描"，句首大写，填 By。

2. **答案：helpful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。is 后接形容词作表语，help → helpful。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。between A and B 表示"在 A 和 B 之间"，填 and。

4. **答案：are used**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。bikes 与 use 之间为被动关系，a day 提示一般现在时，复数主语用 are used。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。not only...but also... 表示"不仅……而且……"，固定搭配，填 but。

6. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词。前后为转折关系，"有很多优点"与"也有一些问题"，用 However。

7. **答案：carelessly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 park，需用副词形式，care → carelessly（粗心地）。注意添加后缀 -lessly。

8. **答案：locking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词/词性转换。locking systems 表示"锁定系统"，现在分词作定语，lock → locking。

9. **答案：to punish**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，rules to punish bad behaviour 表示"惩罚不良行为的规则"，填 to punish。

10. **答案：invention**
    - **考点**：词性转换。a great + 名词，表示"一项伟大的发明"，invent → invention。
