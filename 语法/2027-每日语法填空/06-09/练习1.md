# 06-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园话剧表演 (School Drama Performance)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's annual drama competition ___1___ (take) place last Wednesday. This year, six classes presented short plays based ___2___ famous stories. My class chose "The Little Prince" because it carries a deep message about friendship and ___3___ (grow). We spent two weeks ___4___ (prepare) for the show. I played the role of the fox, who teaches the prince that "___5___ is only with the heart that one can see rightly." Remembering all the lines was ___6___ (easy) part for me, but acting with real emotion was much ___7___ (difficult). On the night of the show, the hall was packed ___8___ students and parents. When the curtain rose, my heart beat fast. ___9___, once I stepped onto the stage, everything came ___10___ (nature). Our play won second prize, and we were all proud of what we had achieved together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：took**
   - **考点**：动词时态。last Wednesday 提示用一般过去时，take → took。take place 表示"举行"。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。be based on 表示"基于"，固定搭配，填 on。

3. **答案：growth**
   - **考点**：词性转换。and 连接两个并列名词，friendship 与 growth 并列，grow → growth。

4. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 表示"花时间做某事"，prepare → preparing。

5. **答案：It**
   - **考点**：代词。It is...that... 为强调句型，"只有用心才能看到本质"，填 It，句首大写。

6. **答案：the easiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"最简单的部分"，easy → the easiest。

7. **答案：more difficult**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级，difficult 为多音节词，用 more difficult。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be packed with 表示"挤满了"，固定搭配，填 with。

9. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词。前后为转折关系，"心跳加速"与"一上台一切都自然了"，用 However。

10. **答案：naturally**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 came，需用副词形式，nature → naturally。come naturally 表示"自然而然地到来"。
