# 09-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校文化墙 (School Culture Wall)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has built __1__ new culture wall near the library this term. It shows students' paintings, calligraphy, and short English articles __2__ (write) by different classes. When I __3__ (walk) past it yesterday, I stopped __4__ read a poem about hometown memories. My best friend hung up __5__ photo of our volunteer team, and many teachers __6__ (leave) encouraging comments beside it. The wall makes the corner __7__ (color) and lively, __8__ it also helps newcomers understand our school spirit. I hope more clubs __9__ (share) their ideas on it next month. If everyone takes part, the wall __10__ (tell) richer stories about us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a new culture wall 一面新的文化墙，表泛指。

2. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。articles 与 write 为被动关系，用 written by... 表示“由……撰写”。

3. **答案：walked**
   - **考点**：动词时态。yesterday 表过去，walk 用过去式 walked。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。stop to do sth. 停下来去做另一件事（停下来读诗），区别于 stop doing（停止正在做的事）。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a photo of our volunteer team 一张照片。

6. **答案：left**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述昨天见闻，leave comments 用过去式 left。

7. **答案：colorful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。make + 宾语 + 形容词作宾补，color 的形容词形式为 colorful。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。使角落更生动与帮助新生了解校风为并列关系。

9. **答案：will share / can share**
   - **考点**：动词时态。next month 表将来，用 will share；若表能力许可也可用 can share。

10. **答案：will tell**
   - **考点**：动词时态。if 引导条件状语从句用一般现在时 takes，主句表将来用 will tell（主将从现）。
