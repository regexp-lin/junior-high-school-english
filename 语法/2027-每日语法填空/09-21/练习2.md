# 09-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节约用电 (Saving Electricity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Electricity makes modern life convenient, __1__ wasting it also harms the environment. At home, my family has made __2__ simple rule: turn off lights __3__ we leave a room. We replaced old bulbs with LED ones, __4__ use less power and last longer. My sister used __5__ forget her charger plugged in, but now she unplugs it __6__ she goes to school. On hot days, we set the air conditioner __7__ 26℃ instead of keeping it too low. Schools often tell students __8__ (save) energy by using natural light in the daytime. If each person changes __9__ small habit, the total saving will be huge. Saving electricity is not __10__ difficult task—it is a daily choice we can all make.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。电带来便利与浪费伤害环境为转折关系。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。a simple rule 一条简单的规定。

3. **答案：when / before**
   - **考点**：从属连词。when we leave 离开时；before we leave 离开前，均通顺。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 LED ones，从句 use less power 缺主语，指物用 which。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。used to do sth. 过去常常做某事。

6. **答案：when / before**
   - **考点**：从属连词。上学前拔掉充电器，when 或 before 均可。

7. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。set ... at 26℃ 将空调设定在 26 摄氏度。

8. **答案：to save**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。tell sb. to do sth. 告诉某人做某事。

9. **答案：a / one**
   - **考点**：冠词或代词。changes a small habit / changes one small habit 改变一个小习惯。

10. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。not a difficult task 并非一项困难的任务。
