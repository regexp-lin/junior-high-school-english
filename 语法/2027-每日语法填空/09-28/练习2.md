# 09-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：快时尚的影响 (The Impact of Fast Fashion)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Fast fashion means clothes are produced quickly __1__ sold at low prices. Many teenagers enjoy buying new styles every season, __2__ they may not wear each item many times. __3__, this habit creates huge waste and pressures factories to work long hours. Making one cotton shirt can consume __4__ water than people imagine. Some brands have started __5__ (use) recycled cloth, __6__ more consumers still chase short-lived trends. Our class discussed __7__ to build a "swap day" for second-hand clothes. I decided __8__ (repair) my old jeans instead of throwing them away. If we choose quality __9__ quantity, the planet will breathe __10__ (easy).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。快速生产与低价销售并列。

2. **答案：but / though**
   - **考点**：并列连词。喜欢买新衣与穿得不多为转折，but 或 though（though 作副词放句末更常见，此处用 but）。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。上文讲消费乐趣，下文讲浪费与加班压力，转折。

4. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：比较级结构。more water than 比……更多的水。

5. **答案：to use / using**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。start to do / start doing 均可表示开始做某事。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。品牌采用回收布料与消费者追逐潮流为转折。

7. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：疑问词 + 不定式。discussed how to build 讨论如何建立。

8. **答案：to repair**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。decide to do sth. 决定做某事。

9. **答案：over / rather than**
   - **考点**：介词或短语。choose quality over quantity 重质不重量；rather than quantity 亦可，over 更简洁。

10. **答案：more easily**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。与现状对比，地球会“更容易”喘息，修饰动词 breathe 用 easily 的比较级 more easily。
