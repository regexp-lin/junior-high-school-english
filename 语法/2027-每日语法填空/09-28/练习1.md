# 09-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国象棋 (Chinese Chess)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese chess is __1__ ancient board game enjoyed by millions of people. The pieces move on lines instead of squares, __2__ each side tries to protect its own general. My grandfather __3__ (teach) me the basic rules when I was eight. At first I lost every game, __4__ I enjoyed thinking several steps ahead. He told me calmness matters __5__ than quick moves. After school, we often play __6__ the stone table in the park, and neighbors sometimes stop __7__ (watch) quietly. Playing chess helps me learn __8__ to face pressure and respect opponents. I believe this tradition __9__ (stay) alive because it trains the mind __10__ (wise).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。ancient 以元音音素开头，an ancient board game 一种古老的棋类游戏。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。棋子走线与双方设法护帅为并列关系。

3. **答案：taught**
   - **考点**：动词时态。when I was eight 表过去，teach 的过去式为 taught。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。输棋与享受思考几步之后为转折。

5. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。than quick moves 提示比较级，much 的比较级 more 修饰 matters。

6. **答案：at / by**
   - **考点**：介词。at the stone table 在石桌旁；by 也可表示在旁边。

7. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。stop to do sth. 停下来去做某事（停下来观看）。

8. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：疑问词 + 不定式。learn how to face pressure 学习如何面对压力。

9. **答案：will stay / can stay**
   - **考点**：动词时态。because 引导原因，主句表判断或展望，will stay 或 can stay。

10. **答案：wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 train 用副词；此处 trains the mind wisely；若用形容词结构 train the mind to be wise 则空白不适配，故用 wise 的副词 wisely 表示“明智地训练思维”。
