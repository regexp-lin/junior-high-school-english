# 12-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园英语角 (School English Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has an English Corner every Thursday afternoon. It takes place in the garden behind the library. Students __1__ are interested in practising English gather there and talk about various topics. Last week, the topic was "My Dream Job". I was a little nervous because it was my first time __2__ (speak) in front of so many people. A senior student named Chen Li noticed my __3__ (nerve) and came over to chat with me. She spoke English very __4__ (fluent) and encouraged me to relax and just enjoy the __5__ (converse). Gradually, I became more confident and shared my dream of __6__ (become) a journalist. To my surprise, several students found my ideas __7__ (interest) and asked me questions. The English Corner not only helps us improve __8__ (we) spoken English but also gives us a chance to make new friends. Our English teacher, Mr. Zhao, who started this activity three years __9__, says that the best way to learn a language is __10__ (use) it as much as possible.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：who/that**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。who/that 引导定语从句修饰 Students，在从句中作主语。

2. **答案：to speak/speaking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。it was my first time to speak/speaking 这是我第一次发言，"It is the first time + to do/doing"结构。

3. **答案：nervousness**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。noticed my nervousness 注意到我的紧张，my 后接名词，nerve→nervousness。

4. **答案：fluently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 spoke，用副词 fluently。

5. **答案：conversation**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。enjoy the conversation 享受对话，the 后接名词。

6. **答案：becoming**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。dream of becoming 成为……的梦想，of 为介词，后接动名词。

7. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换（-ing 形容词作宾补）。found my ideas interesting 觉得我的想法有趣，"find + 宾语 + 形容词"结构。

8. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。our spoken English 我们的口语，修饰名词用形容词性物主代词。

9. **答案：ago**
   - **考点**：副词。three years ago 三年前，ago 表示"以前"。

10. **答案：to use**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作表语）。the best way... is to use 最好的方法是使用，不定式作表语说明主语的内容。
