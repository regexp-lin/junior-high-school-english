# 12-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：自然灾害与应急演练 (Natural Disasters and Emergency Drills)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Natural disasters such as earthquakes and floods can happen at any time. __1__ (know) what to do in an emergency is very important. Last Wednesday, our school held __2__ earthquake emergency drill. When the alarm went off, all students quickly got __3__ their desks and covered their heads with their hands. After the shaking stopped, we were told __4__ (leave) the building in an orderly manner. Each class followed a specific route that __5__ (mark) with green arrows on the wall. The whole school __6__ (success) evacuated to the playground within three minutes. Our head teacher praised us __7__ being quick and calm. He then explained that China lies in one of the world's most active earthquake __8__ (zone). Therefore, it is __9__ (necessary) for every student to know basic survival skills. He also showed us how to use a fire extinguisher and give simple first aid. I think schools should hold __10__ (many) drills like this so that students can be better prepared for emergencies.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：Knowing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。Knowing what to do 知道做什么，动名词短语作主语。

2. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。an earthquake emergency drill 一次地震应急演练，earthquake 以元音音素开头，用 an。

3. **答案：under**
   - **考点**：介词。got under their desks 躲到课桌下面，under 表示"在……下方"。

4. **答案：to leave**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。be told to do sth. 被告知做某事，用不定式。

5. **答案：was marked**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。route 与 mark 为被动关系，描述过去演练时的情况用一般过去时被动 was marked。

6. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 evacuated，用副词 successfully。

7. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。praise sb. for 表扬某人……，for 表示原因。

8. **答案：zones**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。earthquake zones 地震带，zone 为可数名词，此处用复数。

9. **答案：necessary**
   - **考点**：词性转换。it is necessary for 对……来说是必要的，it is + 形容词 + for + sb. 结构。

10. **答案：more**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。more drills 更多的演练，many 的比较级为 more。
