# 12-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：邻里互助 (Neighbourhood Mutual Help)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In our neighbourhood, there is a tradition of __1__ (help) each other. Last month, Mrs. Wang, an elderly woman living alone, fell ill and was sent __2__ hospital. Her neighbours quickly organised __3__ (they) to help. Some of them cooked meals and delivered them to her every day. __4__ (other) helped clean her flat and water her plants. My mother visited her three __5__ (time) a week and kept her company. When Mrs. Wang __6__ (return) home two weeks later, she was __7__ (great) moved by everyone's kindness. She said she felt __8__ (luck) to have such caring neighbours. In big cities, people often complain that neighbours don't know __9__ other. But in our community, things are different. We believe that a good neighbour is __10__ (good) than a distant relative. This spirit of mutual help makes our neighbourhood feel like a big family.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：helping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。a tradition of helping 互助的传统，of 为介词，后接动名词。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。be sent to hospital 被送往医院，to 表示方向。

3. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。organised themselves 自发组织起来，主语与宾语指同一群人，用反身代词。

4. **答案：Others**
   - **考点**：代词。Some... Others... 一些人……另一些人……，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。three times a week 每周三次，time 表示"次数"时为可数名词，three 后用复数。

6. **答案：returned**
   - **考点**：动词时态。two weeks later 两周后，描述过去发生的事用一般过去时 returned。

7. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 moved，用副词 greatly。

8. **答案：lucky**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。felt lucky 感到幸运，feel 为系动词，后接形容词作表语。

9. **答案：each**
   - **考点**：代词。each other 彼此、互相，为固定搭配。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。better than 比……更好，good 的比较级为 better。
