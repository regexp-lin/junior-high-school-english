# 12-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校科技节 (School Science and Technology Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's annual Science and Technology Festival was held last week. It was a three-day event __1__ (fill) with exciting activities. On the first day, students displayed their science projects in the hall. My team built a small robot __2__ could follow a line on the ground. We __3__ (work) on it for over a month. Many visitors stopped __4__ (look) at our robot and asked us questions about how it worked. On the second day, there was a rocket-launching competition. Each team had to design __5__ (they) own water rocket and launch it as high as possible. The __6__ (win) team's rocket reached an amazing height of 50 metres! The last day featured a lecture __7__ (give) by a scientist from a local university. She talked about the __8__ (late) developments in artificial intelligence. Her speech was so __9__ (wonder) that everyone listened with great interest. The festival not only expanded our knowledge __10__ also inspired many of us to explore the world of science.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：filled**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作定语）。an event filled with 一个充满……的活动，event 与 fill 为被动关系，用过去分词。

2. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系代词）。that/which 引导定语从句修饰 robot，在从句中作主语。

3. **答案：had worked**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。had worked for over a month 已经做了一个多月，表示在展示（过去）之前已完成的动作。

4. **答案：to look**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。stopped to look 停下来看，stop to do 表示"停下来去做某事"。

5. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。their own 他们自己的，修饰名词用形容词性物主代词。

6. **答案：winning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词/形容词作定语）。the winning team 获胜的队伍，winning 作前置定语。

7. **答案：given**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作定语）。a lecture given by 由……做的讲座，lecture 与 give 为被动关系，用过去分词。

8. **答案：latest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the latest developments 最新的发展，late 的最高级（意为"最新的"）为 latest。

9. **答案：wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。so wonderful 如此精彩，so + 形容词 + that 引导结果状语从句。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：并列连词。not only... but also... 不仅……而且……，为固定搭配。
