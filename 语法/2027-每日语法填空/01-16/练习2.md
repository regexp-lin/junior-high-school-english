# 01-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：端午节 (The Dragon Boat Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Dragon Boat Festival, also known (1) ______ Duanwu Jie, is one of the most important traditional festivals in China. It (2) ______ (celebrate) on the fifth day of the fifth month of the Chinese lunar calendar, usually falling in June. The festival is held in memory of Qu Yuan, a famous poet and statesman who lived during the Warring States period. According to legend, Qu Yuan was so upset about the (3) ______ (corrupt) of the government that he threw (4) ______ (he) into the Miluo River. When the local people heard the news, they raced out in their boats (5) ______ (save) him, but sadly it was too late. To prevent fish from (6) ______ (eat) his body, they threw rice wrapped in bamboo leaves into the water. This is believed to be the origin of zongzi, the sticky rice dumplings that are (7) ______ (traditional) eaten during the festival. Dragon boat racing is another exciting tradition (8) ______ (associate) with the festival. Teams of paddlers work together to drive their boats through the water as (9) ______ (quick) as possible, cheered on by enthusiastic crowds on the riverbanks. In 2009, the Dragon Boat Festival (10) ______ (add) to UNESCO's list of Intangible Cultural Heritage, recognising its importance to world culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`also known as` 表示"也被称为"。

2. **答案：is celebrated**
   - **考点**：被动语态。节日"被庆祝"，一般现在时被动。

3. **答案：corruption**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the corruption of the government`，冠词后接名词。

4. **答案：himself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`threw himself into the river`，主语为 `he`。

5. **答案：to save**
   - **考点**：不定式。`raced out...to save him`，不定式表目的。

6. **答案：eating**
   - **考点**：动名词。`prevent...from doing sth` 为固定搭配。

7. **答案：traditionally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `eaten` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：associated**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`tradition associated with the festival`，被动关系。

9. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：副词。`as quickly as possible`，同级比较用副词。

10. **答案：was added**
    - **考点**：被动语态。节日"被加入"名录，`In 2009` 表过去时。
