# 01-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：梦想职业 (Dream Careers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

"What do you want to be when you grow up?" This is a question that almost every child (1) ______ (ask) at some point. Some dream of becoming doctors, others want to be teachers, and many are attracted to careers in technology. My dream is (2) ______ (become) a marine biologist. I have been fascinated by ocean life (3) ______ I was a little girl, when my father took me to the aquarium for the first time. Watching the dolphins leap out of the water and the jellyfish float (4) ______ (grace) through their tanks sparked a passion that has never faded. To achieve my dream, I know I need to work hard in school, (5) ______ (especial) in science and maths. I also try to read as many books about marine biology (6) ______ I can. Last summer, I attended a week-long marine science camp (7) ______ (organise) by a local university. During the camp, we visited a research station where scientists were studying the (8) ______ (behave) of sea turtles. I was amazed by their dedication and the important work they were doing. The camp confirmed my belief that this was the career I was (9) ______ (mean) to pursue. Of course, the path to becoming a marine biologist is long and challenging. But I believe that with enough (10) ______ (determine) and passion, I will make my dream come true one day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is asked**
   - **考点**：被动语态。孩子"被问到"这个问题，一般现在时被动。

2. **答案：to become**
   - **考点**：不定式。`My dream is to become`，不定式作表语。

3. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since I was a little girl`，与现在完成时搭配。

4. **答案：gracefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `float` 需要副词形式。

5. **答案：especially**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰介词短语 `in science and maths`，用副词表示"尤其"。

6. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`as many books...as I can`，同级比较。

7. **答案：organised**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a camp organised by`，被动关系。

8. **答案：behaviour**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the behaviour of sea turtles`，冠词后接名词。

9. **答案：meant**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`was meant to pursue`，被动语态，表示"注定要"。

10. **答案：determination**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`enough determination`，`enough` 后接名词。
