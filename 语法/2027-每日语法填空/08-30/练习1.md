# 08-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：帮助邻居 (Helping Neighbours)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mrs. Li is an 80-year-old woman who lives alone in the flat next to ___1___ (our). Her children all work in other cities and can only come home during holidays. My mother often tells me that we should help her whenever we can, because being a good neighbour is one of the most basic ___2___ (quality) of a kind person.

Every Saturday morning, I go to Mrs. Li's flat to help her ___3___ the housework. I usually sweep the floor, take out the rubbish and sometimes help her carry heavy bags from the market. She always thanks me ___4___ (warm) and insists on giving me some fruit or homemade biscuits.

Last winter, Mrs. Li ___5___ (catch) a bad cold and had to stay in bed for a whole week. My mother cooked soup and porridge for her every day. I went to the pharmacy ___6___ (buy) medicine and helped her measure her temperature. ___7___ (gradual), she recovered and was able to walk around again.

One day, she told me that our help meant more to her ___8___ we could imagine. She said she sometimes felt very ___9___ (lone), but knowing that kind neighbours lived next door made her feel much ___10___ (safe). Her words touched my heart deeply.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：ours**
   - **考点**：代词（名词性物主代词）。`next to ours` 中 `ours` = `our flat`，介词 `to` 后用名词性物主代词 **ours** 替代 `our flat`。

2. **答案：qualities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**one of the most basic + 复数名词**（最基本的……之一），`quality` 的复数形式为 **qualities**。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**help sb. with sth.**（帮助某人做某事）为固定搭配，故填 **with**。

4. **答案：warmly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `thanks` 需用副词，**warm** 的副词形式为 **warmly**。

5. **答案：caught**
   - **考点**：动词时态。`Last winter` 提示一般过去时；**catch a cold**（感冒）为固定搭配，`catch` 的过去式为不规则变化 **caught**。

6. **答案：to buy**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式 **to buy** 作目的状语，表示"去药房买药"。

7. **答案：Gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰整个句子的副词放在句首，**gradual** 的副词形式为 **Gradually**（逐渐地），句首首字母大写。

8. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词/介词。**more... than...**（比……更多），`meant more to her than` 表示"对她的意义比我们想象的更多"。

9. **答案：lonely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词辨析）。系动词 `felt` 后接形容词作表语，**lone** 的拓展形容词 **lonely** 表示"孤独的"（侧重内心感受），而 `lone` 多作定语表示"独自的"。

10. **答案：safer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。**much + 比较级** 表示"……得多"，`safe` 的比较级为 **safer**。
