# 08-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：土壤保护 (Soil Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Soil is one of the most valuable ___1___ (resource) on our planet. It takes hundreds of years for nature to form just one centimetre of fertile topsoil. Without healthy soil, plants cannot grow, and ___2___ humans nor animals can survive.

___3___, soil around the world is being damaged at an alarming rate. Overuse of chemical fertilisers has made the soil ___4___ (poor) in quality. Deforestation — the cutting down of large areas of forest — has left the ground ___5___ (protect), making it easy for wind and rain to wash the topsoil away. This process, ___6___ (know) as soil erosion, is especially serious in mountainous regions.

What can ___7___ (do) to protect the soil? First, farmers should be encouraged to use organic fertilisers instead of chemical ones. Organic matter helps keep the soil rich ___8___ nutrients. Second, planting trees and grass on hillsides can ___9___ (effective) prevent soil erosion. Third, governments should pass stricter laws to limit activities that damage the land.

As students, we can start by ___10___ (learn) more about soil and its importance. Even small actions, like planting a tree in the schoolyard, can help protect this precious resource for future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：resources**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**one of the most valuable + 复数名词**（最珍贵的……之一），`resource` 的复数形式为 **resources**。

2. **答案：neither**
   - **考点**：连词。**neither... nor...**（既不……也不……），前后对应，`nor` 已出现在后面，故前面填 **neither**。

3. **答案：However / Unfortunately**
   - **考点**：副词（转折/情感连接词）。前文说土壤珍贵，此处转折说土壤正在被破坏，用 **However**（然而）或 **Unfortunately**（不幸的是）。

4. **答案：poorer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**make + 宾语 + 宾补**，此处暗含与原来相比的意味，`poor` 的比较级为 **poorer**，表示"使土壤质量变得更差"。

5. **答案：unprotected**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加前缀）。**leave + 宾语 + 宾补**（使……处于某种状态），根据语境"砍伐森林使地面失去保护"，在 `protected` 前加否定前缀 `un-`，故填 **unprotected**。

6. **答案：known**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`This process` 与 `know` 之间为被动关系（过程"被称为"），用过去分词 **known** 作后置定语/插入语，**be known as**（被称为）。

7. **答案：be done**
   - **考点**：动词语态。`What can be done...?`（能做什么……？），情态动词 `can` 后接被动语态 **be done**，主语 `What` 与 `do` 之间为被动关系。

8. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。**be rich in...**（富含……）为固定搭配，`keep the soil rich in nutrients` 表示"保持土壤富含养分"。

9. **答案：effectively**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `prevent` 需用副词，**effective** 的副词形式为 **effectively**。

10. **答案：learning**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**start by doing sth.**（从做某事开始），介词 `by` 后接动名词，故填 **learning**。
