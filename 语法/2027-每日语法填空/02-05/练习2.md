# 02-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：美食博主 (Food Blogger)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My sister Li Mei has always loved cooking, and two years ago she started (1) ______ food blog to share her recipes with people online. At first, she only had a few (2) ______ (follow), but her creative dishes quickly attracted attention. Her most popular post is a video showing how (3) ______ (make) traditional Cantonese dim sum at home. It (4) ______ (watch) over two million times so far. What makes her blog special is that she focuses on (5) ______ (health) eating. She believes that delicious food does not have to be unhealthy, so she always tries to use fresh (6) ______ (ingredient) and reduce the amount of oil and sugar. One of her latest recipes is a low-sugar mooncake that tastes just as (7) ______ (good) as the traditional version. Last month, a food magazine invited her (8) ______ (write) a monthly column about home cooking. She was thrilled because this was something she had dreamed (9) ______ for years. Li Mei often tells me that the (10) ______ (great) reward of being a food blogger is not fame or money, but the joy of helping others eat better and live healthier.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a food blog` 表示泛指一个博客，首次提到。

2. **答案：followers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few` 后接可数名词复数。

3. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to make`，疑问词 + 不定式。

4. **答案：has been watched**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。`so far` 提示现在完成时，视频被观看。

5. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：形容词修饰名词。`healthy eating` 健康饮食。

6. **答案：ingredients**
   - **考点**：名词复数。食材有多种，用复数。

7. **答案：good**
   - **考点**：同级比较。`just as good as`，`as...as` 中间用原级。

8. **答案：to write**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`invite sb. to do sth.` 邀请某人做某事。

9. **答案：of / about**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`dream of / about` 梦想。

10. **答案：greatest**
    - **考点**：最高级。`the greatest reward`，最大的回报。
