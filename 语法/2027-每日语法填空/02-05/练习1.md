# 02-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：海洋保护 (Ocean Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ocean covers more than 70% of the Earth's surface and is home (1) ______ millions of species. However, human activities (2) ______ (cause) serious damage to marine ecosystems in recent decades. Plastic pollution is one of the biggest (3) ______ (threat). Every year, approximately eight million tons of plastic waste ends up in the ocean, (4) ______ (harm) sea creatures such as turtles, dolphins and seabirds. Many animals mistake plastic bags (5) ______ jellyfish and eat them, which can lead to death. Another major problem is overfishing. When too many fish (6) ______ (catch) from one area, the ecosystem loses its balance. Some species may even disappear completely. Scientists warn that if we (7) ______ (not take) action now, up to half of all marine species could be lost by the end of this century. Fortunately, many countries have started to take (8) ______ (step) to protect the ocean. Marine protected areas have been established where fishing and (9) ______ (pollute) are strictly controlled. Young people can also make a difference by (10) ______ (reduce) their use of single-use plastics and participating in beach clean-up events.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be home to` 是……的家园/栖息地。

2. **答案：have caused**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`in recent decades` 表示从过去到现在的时间段。

3. **答案：threats**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the biggest threats`，`one of + 最高级 + 复数`。

4. **答案：harming**
   - **考点**：现在分词作结果状语。表示自然而然的结果。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`mistake A for B` 把A误认为B。

6. **答案：are caught**
   - **考点**：一般现在时的被动语态。鱼被捕捞，且描述一般情况。

7. **答案：don't take**
   - **考点**：条件状语从句。`if` 引导的条件句中用一般现在时表将来。

8. **答案：steps**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`take steps to do sth.` 采取措施做某事。

9. **答案：pollution**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `fishing` 并列作主语，用名词形式。

10. **答案：reducing**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后用动名词。
