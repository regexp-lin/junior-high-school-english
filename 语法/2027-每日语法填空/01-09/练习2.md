# 01-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市与乡村 (City Life vs Country Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

People often debate whether it is better to live in the city or in the countryside. Each has its own advantages and (1) ______ (disadvantage). Cities offer more job (2) ______ (opportunity) and better access to education, healthcare and entertainment. However, city life can be very (3) ______ (stress). The streets are always crowded, the air is often polluted, and the cost of living is much (4) ______ (high) than in rural areas. On the other hand, the countryside provides a peaceful and relaxing environment. People who live there enjoy fresh air, open spaces and a (5) ______ (close) connection with nature. My grandparents live in a small village in Hunan Province. Every summer, I visit them and spend two weeks (6) ______ (enjoy) the simple pleasures of country life. I love waking up to the sound of birds (7) ______ (sing) outside my window and walking through the rice fields in the early morning. The villagers are all very friendly and always willing (8) ______ (help) each other. Life moves at a much (9) ______ (slow) pace there, which gives people more time to relax and appreciate the little things. Although I enjoy (10) ______ (visit) the countryside, I think I would prefer to live in a city when I grow up, because I want to have more opportunities to pursue my dreams.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：disadvantages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。与 `advantages` 并列，用复数形式。

2. **答案：opportunities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`more job opportunities`，`more` 修饰可数名词复数。

3. **答案：stressful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`can be very stressful`，系动词后接形容词，`stress` → `stressful`。

4. **答案：higher**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much higher than`，`than` 为比较级标志。

5. **答案：closer**
   - **考点**：比较级。暗含与城市生活的比较，更亲近的联系。

6. **答案：enjoying**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`spend time doing sth` 为固定搭配。

7. **答案：singing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`the sound of birds singing`，鸟儿"正在唱歌"的声音。

8. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be willing to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"愿意做某事"。

9. **答案：slower**
   - **考点**：比较级。`at a much slower pace`，与城市节奏相比更慢。

10. **答案：visiting**
    - **考点**：动名词。`enjoy doing sth` 为固定搭配。
