# 01-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：博物馆之旅 (A Visit to the Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Museums are wonderful places that allow us to learn about history, science and art in an interactive way. Last Saturday, our class went on a field trip to the Provincial Museum. It was my (1) ______ (one) time visiting such a large museum, and I was thrilled. The museum (2) ______ (divide) into several sections, each focusing on a different period of Chinese history. We started with the ancient civilisation section, where we saw bronze vessels and jade carvings that (3) ______ (make) thousands of years ago. Our history teacher, Mr Wang, served (4) ______ our guide and explained the stories behind these artefacts. He told us that the Shang Dynasty was one of the (5) ______ (early) dynasties to develop a writing system. The characters (6) ______ (carve) on oracle bones were the ancestors of the Chinese characters we use today. I found this (7) ______ (absolute) fascinating. After lunch, we moved on to the natural history section, which featured life-sized models of dinosaurs. Some of them were so realistic (8) ______ several students screamed in surprise. We also visited the science and technology hall, (9) ______ we could try hands-on experiments. I spent most of my time there building a simple circuit and learning about electricity. The trip not only broadened our knowledge (10) ______ also sparked our curiosity to explore more.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。`my first time`，基数词 `one` 变为序数词 `first`。

2. **答案：was divided**
   - **考点**：被动语态。博物馆"被划分"为几个区域。

3. **答案：were made**
   - **考点**：被动语态。器物"被制作"于几千年前，过去时被动。

4. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`serve as` 表示"充当，担任"。

5. **答案：earliest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the earliest dynasties`，"最早的朝代之一"。

6. **答案：carved**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`characters carved on oracle bones`，被动关系。

7. **答案：absolutely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `fascinating` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so realistic that...` 结构。

9. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the science and technology hall`，在从句中作地点状语。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 结构，表示"不仅……而且……"。
