# 09-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：食品安全 (Food Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Food safety influences everyone's health. Dirty hands, old oil, __1__ unclean water may cause stomach problems. Last month, our school __2__ (invite) a doctor __3__ (give) a talk about reading food labels. She told us __4__ we should check dates and storage conditions before buying snacks. Street food sometimes tastes good, __5__ we must choose stalls with clear licenses. At home, Mom always washes vegetables __6__ running water and stores raw meat __7__ separate containers away from cooked dishes. If food smells strange, we should throw it away __8__ (immediate). The government keeps testing products __9__ the market and punishes illegal sellers. Remember, safe eating habits protect you __10__ unnecessary risks.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and / or**
   - **考点**：并列连词。列举危险因素，用 and 连接最后一项；若在否定或选择语境可用 or，此处 and 更自然。

2. **答案：invited**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last month 表过去，invite 用过去式 invited。

3. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。invite sb. to do sth. 邀请某人做某事。

4. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句引导词。told us 后为宾语从句，从句结构完整，用 that 引导。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。街头美食好吃与要选择有证照摊位为转折。

6. **答案：under / with**
   - **考点**：介词。under running water 在流水下冲洗；with running water 亦可。

7. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。stores raw meat in separate containers 将生肉存放在单独的容器里，与熟食分开存放，符合食品安全操作。

8. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 throw 用副词，immediate 的副词为 immediately。

9. **答案：on / in**
   - **考点**：介词。testing products on the market 检测市场上的产品；in the market 亦可。

10. **答案：from / against**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。protect ... from/against ... 保护……免受……
