# 09-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：国庆假期计划 (National Day Holiday Plan)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The National Day holiday __1__ (last) for seven days, so my family made a careful plan. We will visit my aunt in Hangzhou first __2__ we can enjoy West Lake in early autumn. My sister has booked train tickets online __3__ she will not wait in long lines. Dad wants __4__ (visit) a history museum, __5__ Mom prefers trying local snacks on old streets. I listed three books __6__ I plan to read on rainy afternoons. __7__, we will set aside one day to rest at home. I also promised __8__ (help) with housework before the trip. If everything goes smoothly, this holiday __9__ (become) both fun and relaxing. Good planning helps us avoid rushing __10__ the last minute.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：lasts / will last**
   - **考点**：动词时态。若陈述惯例“国庆长假共七天”，一般现在时 lasts；若针对即将到来的假期也可用 will last。

2. **答案：so / because**
   - **考点**：从属连词。先去杭州以便/因为能游西湖，so that 若两字则拆成 so we can；此处单空用 so 引导结果或目的从句常见。

3. **答案：so / so that**
   - **考点**：从属连词。提前订票以便不必排长队，so 或 so that。

4. **答案：to visit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。want to do sth. 想要做某事。

5. **答案：while / but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。爸爸想去博物馆与妈妈偏好小吃形成对比，while 表对比，but 表转折。

6. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 books，从句 I plan to read 缺宾语，用 that/which。

7. **答案：Besides / Also**
   - **考点**：连接副词。补充说明还会留一天在家休息。

8. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。promise to do sth. 承诺做某事。

9. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。if 条件句主将从现，主句用 will become。

10. **答案：at / until**
   - **考点**：固定短语。at the last minute 在最后一刻；until the last minute 直到最后一刻。
