# 06-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：雨天的故事 (A Rainy Day Story)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

It was raining ___1___ (heavy) when I left school last Friday. I had forgotten to bring my umbrella, so I stood under the school gate, ___2___ (hope) the rain would stop soon. After waiting for fifteen minutes, the rain showed no sign of ___3___ (stop). Just then, an old man walked past me, ___4___ (hold) a large umbrella. He noticed my situation and ___5___ (offer) to share his umbrella with me. We walked together ___6___ the rain to the nearest bus stop. On the way, he told me he was a ___7___ (retire) teacher and that he used to teach at our school thirty years ago. His voice was warm and kind, ___8___ reminded me of my own grandfather. When we reached the bus stop, I thanked him ___9___ (sincere). He smiled and said, "Next time, remember ___10___ (bring) your umbrella!" I waved goodbye as he disappeared into the rain.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：heavily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 raining，需用副词形式，heavy → heavily。

2. **答案：hoping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，stood under the school gate, hoping...，填 hoping。

3. **答案：stopping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。of 为介词，后接动名词，sign of stopping 表示"停止的迹象"，填 stopping。

4. **答案：holding**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，walked past me, holding a large umbrella，填 holding。

5. **答案：offered**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文叙述过去事件，offer → offered。

6. **答案：in/through**
   - **考点**：介词。in the rain 或 through the rain 表示"在雨中/冒雨"，填 in 或 through。

7. **答案：retired**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 teacher，过去分词作定语，retire → retired（退休的）。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面的整个描述（声音温暖善良），用 which。

9. **答案：sincerely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 thanked，需用副词形式，sincere → sincerely。

10. **答案：to bring**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。remember to do sth. 表示"记得去做某事（未做）"，填 to bring。
