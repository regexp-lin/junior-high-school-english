# 06-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园欺凌 (School Bullying)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

School bullying is a serious problem that ___1___ (affect) millions of students worldwide. Bullying can take many ___2___ (form), including physical violence, name-calling and spreading rumours online. Victims often feel helpless and are afraid ___3___ (tell) anyone about their experience. A classmate of ___4___ (I) was once bullied by older students who took his lunch money every day. He kept silent for weeks ___5___ a teacher noticed something was wrong. The school ___6___ (immediate) took action and held a special meeting for all students. The principal reminded us that bullying is never acceptable and that everyone ___7___ (deserve) to be treated with respect. If you see someone ___8___ (bully), don't just stand by — speak up or report it to an adult. It is ___9___ (we) responsibility to make school a safe and ___10___ (friend) place for everyone.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：affects**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。that 引导定语从句，先行词为 a serious problem（单数），陈述一般事实用一般现在时，填 affects。

2. **答案：forms**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。many 后接可数名词复数，form → forms。

3. **答案：to tell**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be afraid to do sth. 表示"害怕做某事（不敢做）"，填 to tell。

4. **答案：mine**
   - **考点**：代词。a classmate of mine 表示"我的一个同学"，of + 名词性物主代词，I → mine。

5. **答案：until**
   - **考点**：连词。kept silent...until... 表示"一直沉默直到……"，填 until。

6. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 took，需用副词形式，immediate → immediately。

7. **答案：deserves**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。everyone 为第三人称单数，用一般现在时，deserve → deserves。

8. **答案：being bullied**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。see sb. being done 表示"看到某人正在被做某事"，someone 与 bully 之间为被动关系，填 being bullied。

9. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 responsibility，用形容词性物主代词，we → our。

10. **答案：friendly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。and 连接两个并列形容词，safe and friendly，friend → friendly。
