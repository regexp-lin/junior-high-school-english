# 10-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：全球变暖 (Global Warming)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Scientists warn __1__ the Earth is getting warmer because of human activities. Many factories still burn coal, __2__ produces large amounts of CO₂. If we continue __3__ (ignore) these signs, polar ice may melt faster. Last year, several countries __4__ (sign) an agreement to cut down greenhouse gases. Teenagers can also play a part __5__ riding bikes instead of asking parents for car rides every day. My school __6__ (start) a “Low Carbon Week”, and students saved electricity by turning off lights. Teachers told us __7__ even small habits matter when millions of people act together. Everyone agrees __8__ (protect) the environment should start at home, such as sorting waste __9__ (proper). Nobody wants __10__ (live) on an overheated planet, so we must change now.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。warn that... 后接宾语从句，说明警告的内容，从句结构完整，用 that 引导（口语中可省略，填空题常填 that）。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。指代前面“工厂仍烧煤”这一情况，在非限制性定语从句中作主语，用 which。

3. **答案：ignoring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。continue doing sth. 表示持续做某事；亦可用 continue to ignore，此处 ignoring 强调“继续忽视”的行为。

4. **答案：signed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 Last year 表示过去，谓语用一般过去时 signed。

5. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。play a part by doing... 通过做某事发挥作用，by 表方式。

6. **答案：started**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述学校已开展的活动，与后文 saved 一致，用一般过去时 started。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。tell sb. that... 后接宾语从句，说明老师告诉我们的内容。

8. **答案：protecting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。agrees 后为宾语从句，从句主语用动名词短语 protecting the environment，表示“保护环境”这件事。

9. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动名词短语 sorting waste，用副词 properly。

10. **答案：to live**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。want to do sth. 想要做某事，用不定式 to live。
