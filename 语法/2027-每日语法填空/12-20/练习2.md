# 12-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：古镇旅行 (A Trip to an Ancient Town)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the winter holiday, my parents took me to Wuzhen, one of the most __1__ (fame) water towns in China. The town is known __2__ its well-preserved ancient buildings and beautiful canals. As we walked along the narrow stone paths, I felt __3__ if I had travelled back in time. Old wooden houses stood on __4__ (both) sides of the canal, and small boats slowly passed under the stone bridges. We visited a traditional dyeing workshop __5__ long pieces of blue cloth were hung out to dry in the sun. The sight was __6__ (true) impressive. In the afternoon, we sat in a teahouse and __7__ (enjoy) a cup of local green tea. The tea tasted __8__ (slight) sweet and very refreshing. My father told me that this town __9__ (have) a history of more than 1,300 years. I took many __10__ (photo) to help me remember this wonderful trip. I hope to visit more ancient towns in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：famous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。the most famous 最著名的，修饰名词 water towns 用形容词的最高级。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。be known for 以……闻名，for 表示原因。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。as if 好像、仿佛，引导方式状语从句。

4. **答案：both**
   - **考点**：限定词。both sides 两边，both 修饰复数名词。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句（关系副词）。a workshop where 一个在那里……的工坊，where 引导定语从句修饰 workshop。

6. **答案：truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 impressive，用副词 truly。

7. **答案：enjoyed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与前面的 sat 并列，描述过去的动作用一般过去时 enjoyed。

8. **答案：slightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 sweet，用副词 slightly 表示"稍微"。

9. **答案：has/had**
   - **考点**：动词时态。has a history 有……的历史，描述客观事实用一般现在时 has；也可在过去转述语境下用 had。

10. **答案：photos**
    - **考点**：名词单复数。many photos 许多照片，many 后接可数名词复数。
