# 12-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬季流感预防 (Preventing Winter Flu)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Winter is the season when flu spreads most __1__ (easy). Our school nurse, Ms. Chen, gave us a talk about how __2__ (prevent) the flu last Monday. She said that __3__ (wash) hands frequently is one of the most effective ways to avoid __4__ (get) sick. We should wash our hands with soap for at __5__ twenty seconds each time. She also reminded us __6__ (wear) masks in crowded places and to keep our __7__ (classroom) well ventilated by opening windows regularly. Getting enough sleep and eating __8__ (health) food are equally important because they help strengthen our immune system. If someone already __9__ (have) flu symptoms such as fever, cough or a sore throat, they should stay at home and see a doctor as soon __10__ possible. Following these simple steps can greatly reduce the risk of catching the flu.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 spreads，用副词 easily。

2. **答案：to prevent**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。how to prevent 如何预防，"疑问词 + to do"结构。

3. **答案：washing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。washing hands 洗手，动名词短语作主语。

4. **答案：getting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作宾语）。avoid getting 避免生病，avoid 后接动名词。

5. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：固定短语。at least 至少，little 的最高级为 least。

6. **答案：to wear**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作宾补）。remind sb. to do sth. 提醒某人做某事，用不定式。

7. **答案：classrooms**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。our classrooms 我们的教室，our 暗示多间教室，用复数。

8. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。healthy food 健康的食物，修饰名词用形容词。

9. **答案：has**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。If someone already has 如果某人已经有了，someone 为第三人称单数，用 has。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：固定短语。as soon as possible 尽快，为固定搭配。
