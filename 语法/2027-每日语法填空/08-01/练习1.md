# 08-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：建军节 (Army Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

August 1st is ___1___ important national festival in China that carries strong emotion for families in every province. On Army Day, ___2___ falls on August 1st every year, millions of citizens walk into sunny squares, quiet parks, and educational museums to thank the People's Liberation Army for guarding the homeland carefully while many other people follow colourful live broadcasts at home. Teenagers feel especially proud ___3___ they watch neat parades, powerful military music, and respectful flag-raising ceremonies on high-definition television screens.

The meaningful holiday offers ___4___ (good) chance for middle schools to discuss why long-lasting peace must be treasured in a busy world. The army ___5___ (establish) in 1927, and it ___6___ (grow) steadily into a strong modern force that owns advanced equipment and strict training plans. In various towns along the coast and in the mountains, community events ___7___ (hold) near national monuments after dawn, ___8___ happy families bring fresh flowers, sing patriotic songs together, and take photos with smiling children beside red flags.

Powerful documentaries ___9___ (show) young soldiers practising in strong wind, heavy rain, or deep snow remind students that courage is built through patient, repeated effort rather than luck alone. During the week, young volunteers sometimes ___10___ (send) warm thank-you cards or short videos to service members online to express sincere support and childlike respect.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`important` 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，表示“一个重要的节日”要用不定冠词 **an**，不能用 a。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（定语从句）。逗号后为非限制性定语从句，先行词是 **Army Day**，从句缺主语，指物用 **which**（此处不可用 that 引导非限制性定语从句）。

3. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：连词。引导时间状语从句，表示“当他们观看……时感到自豪”，与主句动作同时发生，用 **when**。

4. **答案：the best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级（提示词 good）。语境表示“最好的机会之一”，用 **good → better → best**，且最高级前加定冠词 **the**。

5. **答案：was established**
   - **考点**：动词时态和语态（提示词 establish）。军队是“被建立”的，用被动语态；1927 年为过去时间，用一般过去时的被动 **was established**。

6. **答案：has grown**
   - **考点**：动词时态（提示词 grow）。时间状语 **Over the past century / many decades** 表示从过去持续到现在的过程，用现在完成时 **has grown**（主语 it 为第三人称单数）。

7. **答案：are held**
   - **考点**：动词时态和语态（提示词 hold）。活动是“被举办”，且描述常态/每年情况，用一般现在时被动 **are held**（主语 events 为复数）。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。连接前后两个分句（活动被举办；家庭献花、唱歌、拍照），表示顺承并列，用 **and**。

9. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（提示词 show）。名词 **documentaries** 后需用现在分词 **showing** 作后置定语，相当于 *documentaries that show...*，说明纪录片的内容。

10. **答案：send**
    - **考点**：动词时态（提示词 send）。时间状语 **sometimes** 与 **During the week** 表示经常或反复发生的动作，用一般现在时；主语 **volunteers** 为复数，谓语用动词原形 **send**。
