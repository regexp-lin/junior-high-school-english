# 08-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学游泳 (Learning to Swim)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, my cousin Lily made up ___1___ mind to learn swimming after watching ___2___ exciting school relay race on Sports Day. She believed it would keep her ___3___ (health) and teach patience when small mistakes happen. ___4___ first, the blue water in the huge indoor pool looked very scary, ___5___ she stayed on the top step for several nervous minutes while other kids splashed happily nearby.

Her father ___6___ (promise) to remain within arm’s reach every afternoon. ___7___ patient coach showed Lily how ___8___ (float) on her back with ___9___ light kickboard, then asked her ___10___ (not, laugh) at slower classmates who were still practising basic kicks. Step by step, Lily copied the coach’s slow arm movements, kicked beside the wall, and blew tiny bubbles under the surface exactly as she was told. She practised holding her breath, relaxed her shoulders, and learned to trust the water instead of fighting it. Two weeks later, she crossed the shallow lane without touching the bottom and even helped a younger girl find her lost goggles beside the pool ladder. Before the summer course ended, she joined a friendly club session and realised swimming was less about speed and more about calm breathing and steady practice. Looking back, she jokes that the hardest stroke was not freestyle but the courage to take the first step down from the edge.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词。固定搭配 **make up one’s mind**（下定决心），主语是 **Lily**（女性），用形容词性物主代词 **her**。

2. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。**exciting** 以元音音素开头，表示泛指“一场精彩的接力赛”，用 **an**。

3. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换（提示词 health）。**keep + sb. + 形容词** 表示“使某人保持某种状态”，名词 **health** 要变为形容词 **healthy**。

4. **答案：At**
   - **考点**：固定短语。**At first** 意为“起初、一开始”，作时间状语，注意句首大写。

5. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。前句说水看起来很可怕，后句说她一直站在最上一级台阶，存在因果关系（因为害怕所以不敢下去），用 **so** 连接结果。

6. **答案：promised**
   - **考点**：动词时态（提示词 promise）。叙述去年夏天发生的事，主句谓语用一般过去时 **promised**，与全文叙事时态一致。

7. **答案：A**
   - **考点**：冠词。泛指“一位有耐心的教练”，**patient** 以辅音音素 /p/ 开头，用不定冠词 **A**（句首大写）。

8. **答案：to float**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（提示词 float）。**how to do sth.** 为“疑问词 + 不定式”结构，作 showed 的宾语，填 **to float**。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。泛指“一块轻的浮板”，**light** 以辅音音素开头，用 **a**。

10. **答案：not to laugh**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词的否定（提示词 laugh）。**ask sb. not to do sth.** 要求某人不要做某事，否定词 **not** 加在不定式前，填 **not to laugh**。
