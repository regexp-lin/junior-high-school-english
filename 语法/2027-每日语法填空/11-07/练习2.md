# 11-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：植树造林 (Tree Planting and Reforestation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Trees are essential __1__ life on Earth. They clean the air, prevent floods and provide shade. China has been carrying out tree-planting programmes __2__ the 1970s. One of the most famous is the Three-North Shelter Forest Programme, __3__ aims to stop desert expansion in northern China. Over the years, billions of trees __4__ (plant) across dry and sandy areas. As a result, some deserts __5__ (slow) turned into green land. My school also takes part __6__ tree planting every spring. Last March, I planted five young trees along the roadside __7__ my classmates. The work was tiring — we had __8__ (dig) deep holes and carry heavy buckets of water. But seeing the tiny trees standing in a row made us feel __9__ (pride). Our teacher told us that a single tree can absorb about 22 kilograms of carbon dioxide per year. If everyone plants just one tree, the effect will be __10__ (believe). Let's make our planet greener together!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to / for**
   - **考点**：介词。be essential to/for 对……至关重要，为固定搭配。

2. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。since the 1970s 自20世纪70年代以来，与现在完成进行时连用。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词为专有名词（项目名），非限制性定语从句用 which。

4. **答案：have been planted**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。over the years 与现在完成时连用，trees 与 plant 为被动关系，用 have been planted。

5. **答案：slowly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 turned，用副词 slowly。

6. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。take part in 参加，为固定搭配。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with my classmates 和同学们一起，with 表示"与……一起"。

8. **答案：to dig**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。have to do sth. 不得不做某事，用不定式 to dig。

9. **答案：proud**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。feel proud 感到自豪，pride 的形容词形式为 proud。

10. **答案：unbelievable**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。will be + 形容词，believe 的形容词形式为 believable，加前缀 un- 构成 unbelievable（难以置信的）。
