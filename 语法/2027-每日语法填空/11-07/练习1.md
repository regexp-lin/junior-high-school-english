# 11-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：记者节采访体验 (Journalists' Day Interview Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

November 8th is Journalists' Day in China. To celebrate, our school newspaper club organised a special event. Each member was given a task __1__ (interview) someone on campus. I chose to interview our principal, Mr. Wu. Before the interview, I prepared a list of __2__ (question) carefully. I was quite nervous __3__ I knocked on his office door. __4__, Mr. Wu welcomed me warmly and made me feel at ease. I asked him __5__ he decided to become a teacher. He smiled and told me that his own primary school teacher had __6__ (inspire) him deeply. The interview lasted about twenty minutes. Mr. Wu spoke __7__ (clear) and shared many interesting stories. I recorded everything on my phone and later wrote __8__ 800-word article. My editor said it was one of the __9__ (good) pieces in the school newspaper this term. The experience taught me that being a journalist requires both courage __10__ careful preparation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to interview**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。a task to do sth.，不定式作后置定语修饰 task，用 to interview。

2. **答案：questions**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。a list of 后接可数名词复数，用 questions。

3. **答案：when / as**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导时间状语从句，表示"当我敲门时"，用 when 或 as。

4. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词（逻辑衔接）。前句说紧张，后句说校长热情欢迎，语义转折，用 However。

5. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。asked 后接宾语从句，表示"为什么决定当老师"，用 why。

6. **答案：inspired**
   - **考点**：动词时态。had inspired 过去完成时，inspire 的过去分词为 inspired。

7. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 spoke，用副词 clearly。

8. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。800 读作 eight hundred，eight 以元音音素 /eɪ/ 开头，用 an。

9. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，good 的最高级为 best。

10. **答案：and**
    - **考点**：并列连词。both... and... 既……又……，为固定搭配。
