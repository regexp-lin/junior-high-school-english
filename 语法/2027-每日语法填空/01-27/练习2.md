# 01-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电子游戏的利与弊 (Video Games: Pros and Cons)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Video games are one of the most popular forms of entertainment among teenagers today. A recent survey found that nearly 80% of Chinese students play video games (1) ______ (regular). While many parents and teachers view gaming (2) ______ a waste of time, research suggests that video games can actually have some positive effects. Certain types of games, such as puzzle games and strategy games, can help improve problem-solving (3) ______ (skill) and hand-eye coordination. Multiplayer online games can also teach teamwork, as players must cooperate with each other (4) ______ (achieve) common goals. However, the negative effects of excessive gaming cannot (5) ______ (ignore). Many students become so addicted to games (6) ______ they neglect their studies, skip meals and stay up late into the night. This can lead to poor academic (7) ______ (perform), health problems and strained relationships with family members. My classmate Wang Hao is a good example. He used to be an excellent student, but after he became obsessed with a popular online game, his grades dropped (8) ______ (sharp). His parents were very worried and eventually decided to limit his gaming time to one hour per day. At first, Wang Hao was (9) ______ (happy) about the new rule. But over time, he realised that he had more time for other activities, and his grades began to recover. I think the key is moderation — playing games is fine as long as it does not interfere (10) ______ your responsibilities.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `play` 需要副词形式。

2. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`view...as...` 表示"把……视为……"。

3. **答案：skills**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`problem-solving skills`，多种技能。

4. **答案：to achieve**
   - **考点**：不定式。`cooperate to achieve`，不定式表目的。

5. **答案：be ignored**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`cannot be ignored`，情态动词后接被动。

6. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so addicted...that...` 结构。

7. **答案：performance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`academic performance`，形容词后接名词。

8. **答案：sharply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `dropped` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：unhappy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。加否定前缀 `un-`，对新规定"不高兴"。

10. **答案：with**
    - **考点**：介词。`interfere with` 为固定搭配，表示"干扰，妨碍"。
