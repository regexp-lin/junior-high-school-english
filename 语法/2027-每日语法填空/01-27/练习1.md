# 01-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：晨读的好处 (The Benefits of Morning Reading)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Reading in the morning is a habit that many successful people share. Studies have shown that our brains are most active and (1) ______ (focus) in the early hours of the day, making it the perfect time to absorb new information. Many schools in China have a morning reading session, (2) ______ (usual) lasting about twenty minutes, during which students read aloud from their textbooks. At first, I found morning reading quite boring. I could not understand (3) ______ it was necessary to read the same texts over and over again. However, my English teacher explained that reading aloud helps improve (4) ______ (pronounce) and builds confidence in speaking. She was right. After several months of regular morning reading, I noticed that my spoken English had improved (5) ______ (significant). I could read sentences more (6) ______ (fluent) and my vocabulary had expanded considerably. Encouraged by this progress, I decided to go a step further. Every morning before school, I now spend fifteen minutes reading an English storybook (7) ______ my own. I keep a notebook beside me to write down any new words I come (8) ______ (cross). Then, during my lunch break, I review these words and try to use them in sentences. This method has proven to be extremely (9) ______ (effect) for improving my English. I would recommend morning reading to any student who wants to become a (10) ______ (good) reader and speaker.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：focused**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `active` 并列，系动词后接形容词。

2. **答案：usually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰分词 `lasting` 需要副词形式。

3. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`could not understand why it was necessary`，引导宾语从句。

4. **答案：pronunciation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`improve pronunciation`，动词后接名词。

5. **答案：significantly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词形式。

6. **答案：fluently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `read` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on my own` 表示"独自地"。

8. **答案：across**
   - **考点**：副词。`come across` 为固定短语，表示"偶然遇到"。

9. **答案：effective**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be extremely effective`，系动词后接形容词。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：比较级。`a better reader and speaker`，暗含与现状比较。
