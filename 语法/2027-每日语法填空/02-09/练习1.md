# 02-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：社区图书角 (Community Reading Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In our neighbourhood, there is a small reading corner (1) ______ was set up by a group of retired teachers three years ago. It is located in the lobby of our apartment building and contains over 500 books that residents can borrow (2) ______ (free). The idea came from Mr. Wang, a former Chinese teacher who noticed that many children in the building spent too much time (3) ______ (play) video games. He believed that if children had easy (4) ______ (accessible) to books, they would naturally develop a love for reading. At first, only a few families donated books. But as more people learned about the project, donations started pouring in. Now the collection (5) ______ (include) novels, science books, history books and even some English storybooks. Every Saturday morning, Mr. Wang organizes a reading session (6) ______ children aged 6 to 12. During these sessions, he reads stories aloud and encourages the children (7) ______ (discuss) what they have heard. The reading corner has become one of the most popular places in our community. Parents are grateful because their children (8) ______ (become) more interested in reading over the past two years. Mr. Wang says (9) ______ proudest achievement is seeing a child pick up a book voluntarily instead of reaching for a phone. His story shows that one person's effort can truly make (10) ______ difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `reading corner`。

2. **答案：freely**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`borrow freely` 自由借阅。

3. **答案：playing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

4. **答案：access**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`easy access to` 容易获取，形容词后用名词。

5. **答案：includes**
   - **考点**：一般现在时三单。描述目前的状态，主语 `the collection` 为三单。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`a session for children` 为孩子们举办的活动。

7. **答案：to discuss**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`encourage sb. to do sth.` 鼓励某人做某事。

8. **答案：have become**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`over the past two years` 提示从过去持续到现在。

9. **答案：his**
   - **考点**：物主代词。修饰名词 `achievement`，Mr. Wang 用 `his`。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`make a difference` 产生影响。
