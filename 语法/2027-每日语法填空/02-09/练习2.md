# 02-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：太空探索梦想 (Space Exploration Dream)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ever since I was a little boy, I have dreamed of becoming (1) ______ astronaut. My interest in space began when my father took me to a planetarium for the (2) ______ (one) time at the age of five. I was completely amazed (3) ______ the beauty of the stars and planets displayed on the dome ceiling. From that day on, I started reading everything I could find about space (4) ______ (explore). China's space programme has made remarkable (5) ______ (achieve) in recent years. The successful landing of the Chang'e-5 probe on the Moon and the operation of the Tiangong space station have inspired millions of young people like me. I often imagine what it would be like (6) ______ (float) in zero gravity, looking down at the Earth through a window. My science teacher once told me that becoming an astronaut requires not only excellent physical fitness (7) ______ also strong academic performance, especially in maths and physics. This (8) ______ (motivate) me to study harder. Although the journey to space is incredibly (9) ______ (challenge), I believe that with determination and hard work, nothing is truly out of reach. As the famous saying (10) ______ (go), "Shoot for the moon. Even if you miss, you will land among the stars."

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`an astronaut`，`astronaut` 以元音音素开头。

2. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。`for the first time` 第一次。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be amazed by` 被……所震撼。

4. **答案：exploration**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`space exploration` 太空探索，名词作定语。

5. **答案：achievements**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`remarkable achievements` 显著的成就，多个成就用复数。

6. **答案：to float**
   - **考点**：不定式。`what it would be like to do sth.` 做某事会是什么样子。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but also...` 不但……而且……。

8. **答案：has motivated / motivates**
   - **考点**：时态。这件事激励了我更努力学习，现在完成时或一般现在时均可。

9. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`incredibly challenging`，副词修饰形容词。

10. **答案：goes**
    - **考点**：一般现在时三单。`As the saying goes` 常言道，固定表达。
