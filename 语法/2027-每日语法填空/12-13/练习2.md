# 12-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园农场 (School Farm)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has a small farm behind the science building. It __1__ (set) up two years ago as part of a hands-on learning project. Each class is given a small plot of land __2__ (grow) vegetables and herbs. My class chose to plant tomatoes and peppers this term. We take turns __3__ (water) the plants every day. At first, some of the seedlings __4__ (die) because we gave them too __5__ water. Our biology teacher taught us that different plants need different __6__ (amount) of water and sunlight. We also learned how __7__ (use) natural fertiliser instead of chemicals. After three __8__ (month) of careful work, we finally harvested our first batch of tomatoes. They were smaller than those in the supermarket __9__ they tasted much sweeter. The school farm has taught me that growing food is __10__ (real) hard work. I now have much more respect for farmers.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was set**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。two years ago 两年前，it 与 set up 为被动关系，用一般过去时被动 was set (up)。

2. **答案：to grow**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的）。is given... to grow 被给予……来种植，不定式作目的状语。

3. **答案：watering**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。take turns doing sth. 轮流做某事，用动名词。

4. **答案：died**
   - **考点**：动词时态。At first 起初，描述过去发生的事用一般过去时 died。

5. **答案：much**
   - **考点**：形容词（修饰不可数名词）。too much water 太多水，water 为不可数名词，用 much 修饰。

6. **答案：amounts**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。different amounts 不同的量，different 后接复数名词。

7. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。how to use 如何使用，"疑问词 + to do"结构。

8. **答案：months**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。three months 三个月，基数词 three 后接复数名词。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。前面说"比超市的小"，后面说"味道更甜"，语义转折用 but。

10. **答案：really**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词短语 hard work（此处作表语），用副词 really 表示程度。
