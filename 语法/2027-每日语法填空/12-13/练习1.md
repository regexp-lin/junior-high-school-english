# 12-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：南京大屠杀公祭日 (Nanjing Massacre Memorial Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

December 13th is the National Memorial Day for the victims of the Nanjing Massacre. On this day, ceremonies __1__ (hold) across China to remember the people who lost their __2__ (life) during that dark period of history. In 1937, the Japanese army invaded Nanjing and __3__ (kill) about 300,000 innocent civilians within six weeks. It was one of the __4__ (cruel) events in modern history. In 2014, the Chinese government __5__ (official) set December 13th as a national memorial day. Every year, sirens sound throughout the city of Nanjing __6__ one minute. People stop what they are doing and stand in __7__ (silent). Students in our school also take part in memorial __8__ (activity). We watch documentaries, visit the memorial hall online and discuss __9__ we can learn from history. Remembering the past is not about hatred. __10__ is about cherishing peace and making sure such tragedies never happen again.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：are held**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般现在时被动语态）。ceremonies 与 hold 为被动关系，描述每年的惯例用一般现在时被动 are held。

2. **答案：lives**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。lost their lives 失去了生命，life 的复数为 lives。

3. **答案：killed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与前面的 invaded 并列，描述过去的事件，用一般过去时 killed。

4. **答案：cruellest/most cruel**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the cruellest 最残忍的……之一，cruel 的最高级为 cruellest 或 most cruel。

5. **答案：officially**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 set，用副词 officially。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for one minute 持续一分钟，for 表示时间段。

7. **答案：silence**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。stand in silence 默默站立，in 后接名词，silent 的名词形式为 silence。

8. **答案：activities**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。memorial activities 纪念活动，activity 的复数为 activities。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：宾语从句（连接代词）。what we can learn 我们能学到什么，what 引导宾语从句，在从句中作宾语。

10. **答案：It**
    - **考点**：代词。It is about 它是关于……，It 指代前面提到的 remembering the past 这件事。
