# 05-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园电影节 (School Film Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school held ___1___ film festival in the auditorium last week, and the theme was “Stories Around Us.” Students were allowed to submit short films ___2___ they had finished within two months, including documentaries about clubs and comedies about classroom life. ___3___ jury included teachers and two graduates ___4___ worked in the media industry and offered honest advice. The winning team ___5___ (receive) a trophy and a chance ___6___ (show) their work on the city’s education website. Before the awards, the host asked the audience ___7___ (keep) phones silent and respect every creator. ___8___ film may look simple, but behind it there is teamwork, patience, and countless edits. I realized that cinema is not only entertainment ___9___ also a way to express feelings. ___10___ was truly exciting to see classmates on the big screen and hear the crowd cheer.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。festival 可数，表示“一场电影节”，用不定冠词 a。

2. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰先行词 films，在从句中作宾语，指物，用 that 或 which。

3. **答案：The**
   - **考点**：冠词。特指“评审团”，用定冠词 The，句首大写。

4. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰先行词 graduates，在从句中作主语，指人，用 who 或 that。

5. **答案：received**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述上周活动，用一般过去时 received。

6. **答案：to show**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。名词 chance 后接不定式作定语，a chance to do sth. 表示“做某事的机会”，用 to show。

7. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 表示“要求某人做某事”，用不定式 to keep。

8. **答案：A**
   - **考点**：冠词。泛指“一部电影”，film 以辅音音素开头，用不定冠词 A，句首大写。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。固定结构 not only... but also...，表示“不仅……而且……”，用 but。

10. **答案：It**
    - **考点**：代词。固定句型 It is/was + adj. + to do sth.，It 作形式主语，真正的主语为不定式短语 to see...，故填 It。
