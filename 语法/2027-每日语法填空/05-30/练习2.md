# 05-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：海洋保护 (Ocean Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ocean covers more than seventy percent of ___1___ Earth's surface, yet we know very little about it. Every year, about eight million tons of plastic waste ___2___ (throw) into the sea. This has caused serious ___3___ (pollute) and killed countless marine animals. Scientists warn that ___4___ we don't take action now, many species will disappear within the next few ___5___ (decade). Some countries have already started ___6___ (ban) single-use plastics like bags and straws. In our daily life, we can make a ___7___ (differ) by using reusable bottles and ___8___ (refuse) plastic packaging. A beach clean-up event ___9___ (hold) in our city last month, and over five hundred volunteers joined in. Protecting the ocean is not just the government's job — it is a responsibility that belongs ___10___ all of us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。the Earth 为专有名词，表示"地球的表面"，用定冠词 the。

2. **答案：is thrown**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。plastic waste 与 throw 之间为被动关系，every year 提示一般现在时，waste 为不可数名词（单数），填 is thrown。

3. **答案：pollution**
   - **考点**：词性转换。serious 为形容词，修饰名词，pollute（动词）→ pollution（名词），填 pollution。

4. **答案：if**
   - **考点**：连词。引导条件状语从句，表示"如果我们现在不采取行动"，用 if。

5. **答案：decades**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。a few 后接可数名词复数，decade → decades。

6. **答案：banning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。start doing sth. 表示"开始做某事"，start 后接动名词，填 banning。

7. **答案：difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换。make a difference 为固定搭配，表示"产生影响"，differ → difference。

8. **答案：refusing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。by 后接动名词，与前面 using 并列，表示"通过拒绝塑料包装"，refuse → refusing。

9. **答案：was held**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。event 与 hold 之间为被动关系，last month 提示一般过去时，event 为单数，填 was held。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：介词。belong to 表示"属于"，固定搭配，填 to。
