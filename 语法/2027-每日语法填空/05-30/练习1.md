# 05-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园音乐节 (School Music Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every spring, our school holds a music festival ___1___ brings students and teachers together. Last Friday, the festival ___2___ (begin) with a piano performance by a girl from Class 3. She played so ___3___ (beautiful) that the whole audience fell silent. After that, a group of boys ___4___ (sing) an English pop song on stage. ___5___ voices blended perfectly, and everyone clapped along. I was nervous because it was my first time ___6___ (perform) in front of so many people. My hands were shaking when I picked ___7___ the guitar. However, ___8___ soon as I started playing, all my fear disappeared. The festival lasted ___9___ three hours, but nobody wanted it to end. Music has a special power — it ___10___ (connect) hearts without words.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 a music festival（物），在从句中作主语，用 that 或 which 引导定语从句。

2. **答案：began**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last Friday 提示用一般过去时，begin → began。

3. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 played，需用副词形式，beautiful → beautifully。

4. **答案：sang**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文叙述过去发生的事件，after that 承接上文，用一般过去时，sing → sang。

5. **答案：Their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 voices，指代前文 a group of boys，用形容词性物主代词 Their，句首大写。

6. **答案：to perform**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It was one's first time to do sth. 表示"某人第一次做某事"，填 to perform。

7. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。pick up 表示"拿起"，pick up the guitar 意为"拿起吉他"，填 up。

8. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。as soon as 表示"一……就……"，引导时间状语从句，填 as。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。last for + 时间段，表示"持续了三个小时"，填 for。

10. **答案：connects**
    - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。主语 it 指代 music（单数），陈述一般性事实用一般现在时，填 connects。
