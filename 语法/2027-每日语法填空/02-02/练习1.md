# 02-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：年夜饭 (New Year's Eve Dinner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The New Year's Eve dinner, known as "nianyefan" in Chinese, is arguably the most important meal of the entire year. It is a time (1) ______ family members, no matter how far apart they live, come together to share food, laughter and love. In my family, preparations for the dinner begin several days in (2) ______ (advance). My mother visits the market to buy the (3) ______ (fresh) ingredients, while my father cleans the house from top to bottom. On the day itself, the whole family (4) ______ (gather) in the kitchen to help with the cooking. The menu always includes several dishes that carry (5) ______ (symbol) meanings. Fish is a must-have because the Chinese word for fish, "yu," sounds the same as the word for "surplus," (6) ______ (represent) the hope for abundance in the coming year. Dumplings are shaped like ancient gold ingots, symbolising wealth and (7) ______ (prosperous). Noodles are served uncut to represent long life. This year, my grandmother taught me how to make (8) ______ (tradition) steamed rice cakes, which symbolise rising to higher positions. The kitchen was filled with steam and the delicious smell of cooking, and I felt (9) ______ (incredible) happy to be part of the process. When we finally sat down to eat, there were over fifteen dishes on the table. Looking at my family's (10) ______ (smile) faces, I realised that the food was only part of what made this dinner special — it was the togetherness that truly mattered.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。`a time when family members come together`，修饰时间名词。

2. **答案：advance**
   - **考点**：名词。`in advance` 为固定搭配，表示"提前"。

3. **答案：freshest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the freshest ingredients`，买最新鲜的食材。

4. **答案：gathers**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。描述每年发生的惯常动作，`the whole family` 视为整体，用单数。

5. **答案：symbolic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `meanings` 需要形容词形式。

6. **答案：representing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随，解释鱼的象征意义。

7. **答案：prosperity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `wealth` 并列，需要名词形式。

8. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `steamed rice cakes` 需要形容词。

9. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `happy` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：smiling**
    - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`smiling faces`，主动关系。
