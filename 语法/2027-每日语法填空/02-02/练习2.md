# 02-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：舞狮表演 (Lion Dance Performance)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The lion dance is one of the most (1) ______ (spectacle) traditional performances in Chinese culture. (2) ______ (perform) during festivals and special occasions, it is believed to bring good luck and drive away evil spirits. The dance usually involves two performers working together inside a large lion costume — one controls the head, (3) ______ the other forms the body and tail. The movements of the lion are accompanied by the loud beating of drums, cymbals and gongs, (4) ______ (create) an electrifying atmosphere. Last Spring Festival, a lion dance troupe came to perform in our neighbourhood. Hundreds of people gathered in the square (5) ______ (watch) the show. The performance began with the lion (6) ______ (sleep) peacefully. Then, as the music grew louder, the lion slowly woke up, blinking its big eyes and shaking its furry head. It started to dance, leaping and rolling with (7) ______ (amaze) agility. The crowd cheered as the lion climbed onto a tower of tables stacked three (8) ______ (metre) high and performed daring acrobatic moves at the top. The (9) ______ (strong) and coordination of the two dancers inside the costume was truly impressive. After the performance, I spoke to one of the dancers. He told me he had been (10) ______ (train) since the age of ten and that lion dancing was not just exercise — it was an art form that required years of dedication.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：spectacular**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most spectacular performances`，修饰名词。

2. **答案：Performed**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`Performed during festivals`，过去分词作状语，被动。

3. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：连词。一个控制头部，"而"另一个控制身体。

4. **答案：creating**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随结果。

5. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：不定式。`gathered to watch`，不定式表目的。

6. **答案：sleeping**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`with the lion sleeping`，`with` + 宾语 + 现在分词。

7. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `agility` 用形容词。

8. **答案：metres**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`three metres high`，数词后接名词复数。

9. **答案：strength**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `coordination` 并列作主语，需要名词形式。

10. **答案：training**
    - **考点**：现在分词。`had been training`，过去完成进行时。
