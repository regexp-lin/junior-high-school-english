# 02-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：守岁传统 (The Tradition of Staying Up on New Year's Eve)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Staying up on New Year's Eve, known as "shousui" in Chinese, is one of the oldest Spring Festival traditions. It is said that the tradition dates back (1) ______ ancient times, when people believed that a monster called "Nian" would come out on New Year's Eve to harm villagers. To protect (2) ______ (they), the villagers would stay awake all night, making loud noises and (3) ______ (light) firecrackers to scare the monster away. Although most people no longer believe in the legend, the tradition of staying up has (4) ______ (survive) for thousands of years. Today, "shousui" is more about spending quality time with family. On New Year's Eve, after the big dinner, my family usually gathers in the living room to watch the Spring Festival Gala on television. The show (5) ______ (broadcast) live from Beijing and features singing, dancing, comedy sketches and acrobatics. It has been a tradition (6) ______ 1983 and is watched by hundreds of millions of people across the country. While watching the show, we snack on sunflower seeds, peanuts and dried fruits. My mother always prepares a big plate of tangerines, (7) ______ symbolise good luck and wealth. As midnight approaches, the (8) ______ (excite) in our house builds. When the clock strikes twelve, firecrackers (9) ______ (explode) outside, and the sky is lit up with colourful fireworks. We hug each other and exchange New Year greetings. The tradition of "shousui" reminds me that the most (10) ______ (value) thing in life is the time we spend with the people we love.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`dates back to ancient times`，`date back to` 为固定搭配。

2. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`To protect themselves`，主语为 `the villagers`。

3. **答案：lighting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。与 `making` 并列，表伴随。

4. **答案：survived**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`has survived for thousands of years`。

5. **答案：is broadcast / is broadcasted**
   - **考点**：被动语态。节目"被播出"，一般现在时被动。

6. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。`since 1983`，自1983年以来。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `tangerines`。

8. **答案：excitement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the excitement builds`，冠词后接名词。

9. **答案：explode**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。描述每年发生的惯常场景。

10. **答案：valuable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`the most valuable thing`，修饰名词需要形容词。
