# 02-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：春节庆祝 (Celebrating Spring Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Spring Festival, also known as Chinese New Year, is the biggest and most (1) ______ (joy) celebration in China. It marks the beginning of a new year on the lunar calendar, and the (2) ______ (celebrate) usually last for about fifteen days. For most Chinese families, the festival begins with a thorough house cleaning. This tradition (3) ______ (believe) to sweep away bad luck and make room for incoming good fortune. Doors are decorated with red couplets and paper cuttings, and red lanterns (4) ______ (hang) outside houses to create a festive atmosphere. The highlight of Spring Festival is the New Year's Eve dinner, (5) ______ the whole family gathers around the table to enjoy a feast. The dinner table is usually loaded with (6) ______ (dish) that carry special meanings. After dinner, many families stay up all night, a tradition known (7) ______ "shousui," to welcome the New Year. Children are usually the (8) ______ (happy) people during Spring Festival because they receive red envelopes containing lucky money from their elders. I remember (9) ______ (feel) incredibly excited every time I received a red envelope as a child. As I have grown older, I have come to appreciate the deeper meaning of Spring Festival — it is a time for families (10) ______ (reunite) and for people to express their hopes for the year ahead.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：joyful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most joyful celebration`，修饰名词需要形容词。

2. **答案：celebrations**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the celebrations usually last`，各种庆祝活动。

3. **答案：is believed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`This tradition is believed to`，传统"被认为"。

4. **答案：are hung**
   - **考点**：被动语态。灯笼"被挂"在外面。

5. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the New Year's Eve dinner`（暗含时间），在从句中作时间状语。

6. **答案：dishes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`loaded with dishes`，多道菜。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`known as "shousui"`，"被称为"。

8. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the happiest people`。

9. **答案：feeling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`remember feeling`，表示"记得曾感到"（回忆过去的经历）。

10. **答案：to reunite**
    - **考点**：不定式。`a time for families to reunite`，不定式表目的。
