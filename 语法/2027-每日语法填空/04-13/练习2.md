# 04-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：民族服装 (Traditional Ethnic Clothing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traditional ethnic clothing tells stories about history and identity. In southwest China, bright skirts and silver jewelry often appear (1) ______ festivals and weddings.

Last month, our art museum held an exhibition. Mannequins wore embroidered jackets (2) ______ (make) by local artists. A guide explained that each pattern had (3) ______ special meaning related to nature or family.

Visitors were not allowed (4) ______ (touch) the cloth, but they could take photos from a distance. I was attracted to a robe with cloud patterns, (5) ______ looked elegant under the lights.

Some teenagers worry that ethnic styles look old-fashioned. (6) ______ , designers now mix traditional cuts with modern colors. Young models walked on a small stage, (7) ______ (show) that heritage can be fashionable.

My cousin bought a modern jacket inspired by Miao designs. She wears it to school parties, (8) ______ her classmates always ask where she found it.

Clothing is more than fabric; it carries pride. If we learn the stories behind the stitches, we will respect diversity (9) ______ (deep).

I hope such exhibitions (10) ______ (visit) by more students in the coming years.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：at / during**
   - **考点**：介词。`at festivals` 在节日期间；或 `during festivals`。

2. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`jackets made by local artists` 由当地艺术家制作的夹克，`make` → `made`。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`a special meaning` 一种特别的含义。

4. **答案：to touch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be allowed to do sth.` 被允许做某事。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句，指代 `robe`，从句缺主语。

6. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。年轻人担心过时，然而设计师融合传统与现代，转折。

7. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。模特走台并“展示”传统可以很时尚。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。她穿去参加聚会，同学们总问哪里买的，顺承。

9. **答案：more deeply**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。与不了解故事时相比，“更深地”尊重多样性；或 `deeply` 原级。

10. **答案：will be visited / can be visited**
    - **考点**：被动语态。展览“被参观”，表将来用 `will be visited`。
