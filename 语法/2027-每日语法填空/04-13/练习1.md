# 04-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园国际日 (School International Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's International Day is the busiest event of the year. Each class chooses (1) ______ country and prepares games, music, and food from that culture.

Last Friday, my class presented Canada. We painted maple leaves on the walls and taught visitors (2) ______ (say) simple greetings in English and French. A classmate dressed as a hockey player (3) ______ explained the rules of the sport.

In the gym, students walked from booth to booth with passports (4) ______ (print) on paper. Every time they finished an activity, they received (5) ______ stamp. The line for the Japanese tea ceremony was the (6) ______ (long) of all.

International Day is not only fun; it helps us understand (7) ______ the world is connected. I tasted spicy snacks from Thailand and learned a folk dance from Mexico. These experiences were (8) ______ (educate) than reading a textbook alone.

Teachers hope the event will encourage students (9) ______ (study) foreign languages with curiosity. When the closing music played, many of us promised (10) ______ (come) back next year with even better ideas.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`choose a country` 选择一个国家。

2. **答案：to say**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`teach sb. to do sth.` 教某人做某事。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。打扮成球员并解释规则。

4. **答案：printed**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`passports printed on paper` 印在纸上的护照。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`received a stamp` 得到一个印章。

6. **答案：longest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the ... of all` 表范围。

7. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`understand how the world is connected` 世界如何相连。

8. **答案：more educational**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示比较级，`educational` 多音节用 `more educational`。

9. **答案：to study**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`encourage sb. to do sth.` 鼓励某人做某事。

10. **答案：to come**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`promise to do sth.` 承诺做某事。
