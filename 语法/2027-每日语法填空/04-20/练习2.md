# 04-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市慢生活 (Slow City Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, "slow living" has become a popular topic in big cities. Many young people (1) ______ (choose) to walk or cycle instead of rushing everywhere by car. They believe that slowing down helps them notice small details that are (2) ______ (easy) to miss when life moves too fast.

On weekends, a group of friends (3) ______ (gather) in a quiet neighborhood cafe to read books or chat for hours. They turn off phones (4) ______ they can fully enjoy the moment. The owner of the cafe, who used to work in an office, said he felt (5) ______ (health) after switching to this slower life.

City planners are also supporting the idea (6) ______ building more green spaces and bike lanes. "A city should not only be efficient (7) ______ friendly to human feelings," one official said. I believe that balance (8) ______ (find) between speed and peace is important for everyone.

Living slowly does not mean (9) ______ (be) lazy. It means learning to value time and relationships. If we can spare half an hour a day for a walk, we may discover (10) ______ our city looks more beautiful than we thought.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have chosen**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`In recent years` 与“近年来已选择”呼应，强调对现在的影响，用 `have chosen`。

2. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与 `when life moves too fast` 隐含对比，慢下来时更容易注意到细节，“更容易”用 `easier`。

3. **答案：gather**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。描述习惯性周末活动，主语 `friends` 复数，用动词原形 `gather`。

4. **答案：so that/in order that**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导目的状语从句，“关掉手机以便充分享受当下”，可用 `so that`（后接情态动词 `can`）。

5. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`felt` 后接形容词，与过去办公室生活对比，“感觉更健康”，`health` → `healthier`。

6. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by + V-ing` 表示方式，“通过修建更多绿地与自行车道”。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but (also)...` 不仅……而且……。

8. **答案：found**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`balance found between...` 过去分词作后置定语，表“在速度与宁静之间找到的平衡”，相当于 `that is found`。

9. **答案：being**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`mean doing sth.` 意味着做某事，动名词作宾语。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：连接词。`discover` 后宾语从句，说明发现的内容，从句结构完整，用 `that` 引导。
