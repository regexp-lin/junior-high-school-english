# 04-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园天文观测 (School Stargazing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday night, members of our astronomy club carried telescopes (1) ______ the rooftop to watch Jupiter and its moons. The sky was unusually clear, and the city lights (2) ______ (turn) down for Earth Hour, so the stars looked brighter than usual.

Our teacher explained how (3) ______ (use) the lens and focus on distant objects. While we were waiting, she told us that ancient Chinese astronomers (4) ______ (record) the movements of planets without modern tools. I was amazed (5) ______ they could predict eclipses so accurately.

Through the telescope, I saw tiny dots (6) ______ (move) around Jupiter. The club president (7) ______ (take) photos and promised to share them later. Before we packed up, we made a wish (8) ______ we could see a meteor shower next month.

This experience taught me that science is not only about formulas (9) ______ also about observation and patience. I hope more students will join us (10) ______ (explore) the sky above our campus.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`carry... to the rooftop` 把……搬到楼顶，表方向。

2. **答案：were turned**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。`Earth Hour` 期间灯光被调暗，主语 `lights` 与 `turn` 为被动关系，叙述当晚情况，用 `were turned`。

3. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`explain how to use` “疑问词 + 不定式”作宾语，说明如何使用镜头。

4. **答案：had recorded**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。叙述“古代天文学家在我们讨论之前早已记录”，过去的过去，用 `had recorded`。

5. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。`be amazed that...` 引导宾语从句，说明感到惊讶的内容。

6. **答案：moving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`see sb./sth. doing sth.` 看见……正在做某事，现在分词作宾语补足语，表示卫星正在移动。

7. **答案：took**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与 `promised` 并列叙述当晚发生的事，用 `took`。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。`make a wish that...` 同位语从句说明愿望内容。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but also...` 不仅……而且……。

10. **答案：to explore**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`join us to explore` 不定式作目的状语。
