# 03-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：寄宿生活 (Boarding School Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When I first arrived at boarding school, I felt both excited and (1) ______ (nerve). It was the first time I had been away from my family for such a long time. Everything was new and (2) ______ (familiar) to me.

The daily schedule was strict. We had to get up at 6:15 every morning and make our beds (3) ______ (neat) before going to breakfast. Classes started at 7:30 and lasted until 5:00 in the afternoon. After dinner, we had two hours of self-study, (4) ______ was followed by a thirty-minute free time before lights out.

At first, I had difficulty (5) ______ (fall) asleep in the dormitory because my roommates often whispered to each other late at night. I also missed my mother's cooking — the food in the canteen was not (6) ______ good as what I ate at home.

However, things got better as time went (7) ______. I made several close friends and we helped each other (8) ______ our studies. My roommate Zhang Wei taught me maths, and I helped him improve his English (9) ______ (pronounce).

Now I have been at boarding school for two years, and it (10) ______ (feel) like my second home. Living independently has made me a stronger and more responsible person.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：nervous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `excited` 并列作表语，`nerve` → `nervous` 形容词。

2. **答案：unfamiliar**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 前缀。语境表示不熟悉，`familiar` → `unfamiliar`。

3. **答案：neatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `make`，`neat` → `neatly` 副词。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。`which` 指代前面的自习时间。

5. **答案：falling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`have difficulty (in) doing sth.` 做某事有困难。

6. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not as/so good as` 不如……好，原级比较。

7. **答案：by / on**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`as time went by/on` 随着时间的推移。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`help each other with ...` 在……方面互相帮助。

9. **答案：pronunciation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`his English pronunciation` 他的英语发音，名词形式。

10. **答案：feels**
    - **考点**：一般现在时。`Now` 表现在，描述当前感受，主语 `it` 第三人称单数。
