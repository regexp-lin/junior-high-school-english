# 03-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：皮影戏 (Shadow Puppet Show)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Shadow puppet shows are one of the (1) ______ (old) forms of storytelling in China. They date back over 2,000 years and were once one of the most popular forms of (2) ______ (entertain) in Chinese villages.

A shadow puppet is a flat figure (3) ______ (make) of leather or paper. The puppeteer holds the puppet behind a thin white screen and uses a bright light to create shadows. By (4) ______ (move) the puppet's arms and legs, the puppeteer can make the characters walk, dance and even fight.

Last month, an old artist named Mr. Liu (5) ______ (invite) to our school to perform a shadow puppet show. He told us that he (6) ______ (learn) this art from his grandfather when he was only eight years old. He has been performing for over fifty years.

The show he performed was based (7) ______ a famous Chinese legend — "Journey to the West". When the Monkey King appeared on the screen, the audience cheered (8) ______ (loud). Mr. Liu's hands moved so (9) ______ (quick) that we could hardly follow them.

After the show, Mr. Liu expressed his worry that fewer and fewer young people are interested in learning this traditional art. He hoped that more students (10) ______ (will) try to keep this beautiful art form alive.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the oldest forms` 最古老的形式之一。

2. **答案：entertainment**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`forms of entertainment` 娱乐形式，名词。

3. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a figure made of leather` 用皮革制成的形象。

4. **答案：moving**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `By` 后接动名词，表示方式。

5. **答案：was invited**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。`Last month` 过去时间，艺术家是"被邀请的"。

6. **答案：had learned / had learnt**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`told us that he had learned ...` 过去的过去。

7. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be based on` 基于。

8. **答案：loudly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `cheered`，`loud` → `loudly` 副词。

9. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so quickly that ...` 如此快以至于……，副词修饰动词 `moved`。

10. **答案：would**
    - **考点**：过去将来时。`hoped that ... would ...` 希望……会……，主句过去时，从句用 `would`。
