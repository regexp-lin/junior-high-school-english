# 11-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬日阳光 (Winter Sunshine)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

On cold winter days, there is nothing __1__ (good) than sitting in warm sunshine. Last Sunday, I spent the whole afternoon on the balcony with my cat, Mimi. The sky was perfectly __2__ (clearly) and the sun shone gently through the glass. I wrapped __3__ (I) in a thick blanket and started reading a novel. Mimi curled up __4__ my lap, purring softly. The warmth of the sun made me feel __5__ (peace) and relaxed. I looked out at the bare trees __6__ noticed a few sparrows hopping on the branches. Even in winter, nature has __7__ (it) own beauty. As the sun slowly moved __8__ (west), the light turned golden. Shadows grew __9__ (long) and the air became cooler. I did not want __10__ (move), but it was time to go inside and help Mum prepare dinner. That quiet afternoon taught me to appreciate the simple pleasures in life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。nothing better than 没有什么比……更好的，good 的比较级为 better。

2. **答案：clear**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词→形容词）。was 后接形容词作表语，用 clear。

3. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。wrapped myself in 把自己裹在……里，I 的反身代词为 myself。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on my lap 在我的腿上，用 on。

5. **答案：peaceful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。feel peaceful 感到平静，peace 的形容词形式为 peaceful。

6. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。looked out 与 noticed 为并列动作，用 and 连接。

7. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 beauty，用形容词性物主代词 its。

8. **答案：westward / west**
   - **考点**：副词。moved westward/west 向西移动，west 可作副词使用。

9. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。grew longer 变得更长，暗含随时间推移的变化。

10. **答案：to move**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。want to do sth. 想要做某事，用不定式 to move。
