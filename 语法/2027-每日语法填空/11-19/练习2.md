# 11-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：人工智能与学习 (AI and Learning)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Artificial intelligence is changing the way students learn. AI-powered apps can now help us __1__ (solve) maths problems, translate languages and even check __2__ (write) assignments. My English teacher introduced an AI tool __3__ gives us instant feedback on our essays. It points out grammar __4__ (mistake) and suggests better vocabulary. I find it very __5__ (help). However, some people worry that students may rely __6__ AI too much and stop thinking for __7__ (they). Our headmaster held a meeting to discuss this topic. He said AI should be __8__ (use) as a tool, not a replacement for hard work. He also warned that not everything AI produces is __9__ (accuracy). We should always check the information __10__ (careful). I agree that AI can be a powerful learning partner, but it can never replace the effort of studying with our own brains.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：solve / to solve**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help sb. (to) do sth. 帮助某人做某事，to 可省略。

2. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作定语）。written assignments 书面作业，write 的过去分词 written 作前置定语。

3. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 an AI tool 指物，从句缺主语，用 that 或 which。

4. **答案：mistakes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。grammar mistakes 语法错误，用复数 mistakes。

5. **答案：helpful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。find it helpful 觉得它有帮助，help 的形容词形式为 helpful。

6. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。rely on 依赖，为固定搭配。

7. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。stop thinking for themselves 停止独立思考，students 对应复数反身代词 themselves。

8. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：动词语态。should be used 应该被使用，be + 过去分词构成被动语态。

9. **答案：accurate**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。is accurate 是准确的，accuracy 的形容词形式为 accurate。

10. **答案：carefully**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 check，用副词 carefully。
