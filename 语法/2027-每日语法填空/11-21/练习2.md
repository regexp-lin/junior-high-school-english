# 11-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：文化遗产保护 (Cultural Heritage Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has 56 World Heritage Sites, __1__ (make) it one of the countries with the most heritage listings. These sites include the Great Wall, the Forbidden City __2__ the Terracotta Warriors. They are treasures that belong not just __3__ China but to the whole world. However, protecting them is a huge challenge. Millions of __4__ (tourist) visit these sites every year, causing wear and damage. Air pollution also __5__ (threat) ancient stone carvings and paintings. The government has set __6__ strict rules to limit the number of daily visitors. __7__ (addition), advanced technology such as 3D scanning is being used to create digital copies __8__ important artefacts. This means that even if the originals are damaged, __9__ (they) details will be preserved forever. Our history teacher once said, "If we lose our cultural heritage, we lose our __10__ (identify)." These words have stayed with me. I believe that each of us has a responsibility to protect these priceless treasures.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作结果状语）。表示自然结果"使得中国成为……"，用 making。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。列举多个世界遗产，最后一个前用 and 连接。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。belong to 属于，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：tourists**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。millions of 后接可数名词复数，用 tourists。

5. **答案：threatens**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。主语 air pollution 为第三人称单数，描述一般事实用 threatens。

6. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词。set up 建立、制定，为固定搭配。

7. **答案：Additionally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→副词）。置于句首作连接副词，addition 的副词形式为 additionally，注意首字母大写。

8. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。copies of 对……的复制品，用 of。

9. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 details，用 their。

10. **答案：identity**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。lose our identity 失去我们的身份认同，identify 的名词形式为 identity。
