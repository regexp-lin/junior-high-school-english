# 11-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园冬季长跑 (School Winter Long-distance Running)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every winter, our school organises a long-distance running event __1__ (encourage) students to stay active in cold weather. This year, the run was held __2__ a chilly December morning. Over 800 students lined up at the starting point. The course was 3 kilometres long, winding __3__ the school campus and the nearby park. When the whistle blew, I started running at a steady pace. After the first kilometre, my __4__ (lung) burned and my legs felt heavy. I wanted to stop, __5__ I remembered my coach's words: "Your body wants to quit, but your mind is __6__ (strong) than your body." So I kept going. The trees along the path had lost all their __7__ (leaf), and frost covered the ground. Despite the cold, I could feel __8__ (I) warming up from the inside. I finally crossed the finish line in fifteenth place. It was not a top result, __9__ I was proud that I did not give up. Our PE teacher said that winter running __10__ (build) both physical and mental strength. I believe he is right.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to encourage**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了鼓励学生"，用 to encourage。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on a chilly December morning 在一个寒冷的十二月早晨，具体的某天早上用 on。

3. **答案：through / around**
   - **考点**：介词。winding through/around 蜿蜒穿过/绕过，表示路线经过。

4. **答案：lungs**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。lungs 肺（通常用复数），用 lungs。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说想停下，后句说记起教练的话，语义转折，用 but。

6. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than your body，strong 的比较级为 stronger。

7. **答案：leaves**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。leaf 的复数为不规则变化 leaves。

8. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。feel myself warming up 感到自己在暖起来，I 的反身代词为 myself。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说不是顶尖成绩，后句说为没放弃而自豪，语义转折，用 but。

10. **答案：builds**
    - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。winter running 为动名词短语作主语（视为第三人称单数），陈述一般事实用一般现在时 builds。
