# 07-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：科技馆参观 (Visiting a Science Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Guangdong Science Centre is one of the ___1___ (large) science museums in Asia. Our class visited it last Thursday as part of a school trip. The moment we entered, we were attracted by a giant model of the solar system ___2___ (hang) from the ceiling. In the physics hall, we tried hands-on experiments that explained how magnets and electricity ___3___ (work). The most popular section was the VR experience zone, ___4___ visitors could "travel" to outer space or walk on the ocean floor. I ___5___ (put) on a VR headset and found ___6___ (I) standing on the surface of Mars! The experience was so realistic ___7___ I almost forgot I was in a museum. Another interesting area was the robotics hall. A small robot could dance, answer ___8___ (question) and even play chess ___9___ visitors. Our guide told us that the museum ___10___ (update) its exhibitions every year to keep up with the latest scientific discoveries. I left the museum feeling inspired and curious about the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，large → largest。

2. **答案：hanging**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作后置定语，a model hanging from the ceiling 表示"悬挂在天花板上的模型"，model 与 hang 为主动关系，填 hanging。

3. **答案：work**
   - **考点**：动词时态。how 引导的宾语从句，解释科学原理（一般事实），用一般现在时，主语 magnets and electricity 为复数，填 work。

4. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 the VR experience zone（地点），在从句中作地点状语，用 where。

5. **答案：put**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去事件用一般过去时，put 的过去式与原形相同，填 put。

6. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：代词。found myself standing 表示"发现自己站在"，主语为 I，用反身代词 myself。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。so...that... 引导结果状语从句，填 that。

8. **答案：questions**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。answer questions 表示"回答问题"，question → questions。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。play chess with visitors 表示"和参观者下棋"，填 with。

10. **答案：updates**
    - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。every year 提示一般现在时，the museum 为单数主语，update → updates。
