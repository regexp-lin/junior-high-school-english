# 07-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：食品安全 (Food Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Food safety is closely related ___1___ everyone's health. In recent years, several food safety problems ___2___ (report) in the news, making people more careful about what they eat. To protect ___3___ (consume), the government has introduced stricter laws on food production and ___4___ (sell). All food products must now carry clear labels ___5___ (show) ingredients, production dates and expiry dates. When shopping, it is important to check ___6___ the food has passed quality inspections. At home, we should wash vegetables ___7___ (thorough) before cooking and keep raw meat separate from other foods. Eating at street stalls may be ___8___ (taste), but it can also be risky if the food is not prepared ___9___ (proper). My mother always tells me to avoid buying snacks from unlicensed vendors. She believes that ___10___ (choose) safe food is one of the most basic ways to take care of our health.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。be related to 表示"与……相关"，固定搭配，填 to。

2. **答案：have been reported**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。in recent years 提示用现在完成时，problems 与 report 之间为被动关系，填 have been reported。

3. **答案：consumers**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。protect consumers 表示"保护消费者"，consume → consumer → consumers。

4. **答案：sales**
   - **考点**：词性转换。and 连接两个并列名词，production and sales 表示"生产和销售"，sell → sale → sales。

5. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作后置定语，labels showing ingredients 表示"标注了成分的标签"，填 showing。

6. **答案：whether**
   - **考点**：连词。check whether 表示"检查是否"，引导宾语从句，填 whether。

7. **答案：thoroughly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 wash，需用副词形式，thorough → thoroughly。

8. **答案：tasty**
   - **考点**：词性转换。be tasty 表示"美味的"，taste → tasty。

9. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 prepared，需用副词形式，proper → properly。

10. **答案：choosing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。动名词作主语，表示"选择安全食品这件事"，choose → choosing。
