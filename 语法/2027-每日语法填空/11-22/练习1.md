# 11-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园科幻写作比赛 (School Sci-fi Writing Contest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our English department held a science fiction writing contest last month. The theme was "Life in 2100". I spent three weekends __1__ (write) my story about a boy who travels to Mars. In my story, humans have already built cities __2__ the red planet. People live in glass domes and grow food in __3__ (special) designed greenhouses. The boy __4__ (discover) an underground lake and proves that life once existed on Mars. My English teacher helped me __5__ (improve) the plot by adding more details. She said a good story needs to be both __6__ (imagine) and logical. I also asked my father, who is __7__ engineer, to check the scientific facts in my story. Over 200 students __8__ (enter) the contest. I was nervous waiting for the results. When I heard my name __9__ (call) as the second-prize winner, I could __10__ (hard) believe it! The contest has made me want to write more stories in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 花时间做某事，用动名词 writing。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。on the red planet 在这颗红色星球上，用 on 表示在星球表面。

3. **答案：specially**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰过去分词 designed，用副词 specially。

4. **答案：discovers**
   - **考点**：动词时态。讲述故事内容用一般现在时，主语 the boy 为第三人称单数，用 discovers。

5. **答案：improve / to improve**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help sb. (to) do sth.，to 可省略。

6. **答案：imaginative**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be imaginative 有想象力的，imagine 的形容词形式为 imaginative。

7. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。engineer 以元音音素 /e/ 开头，泛指"一名工程师"，用 an。

8. **答案：entered**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文为过去时态叙述，用一般过去时 entered。

9. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作宾补）。heard my name called 听到我的名字被叫到，name 与 call 为被动关系，用过去分词 called。

10. **答案：hardly**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。could hardly believe 几乎无法相信，hardly 表示"几乎不"。
