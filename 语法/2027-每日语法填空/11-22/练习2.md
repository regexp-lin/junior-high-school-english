# 11-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护地下水 (Protecting Groundwater)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Groundwater is water stored beneath the Earth's surface. It provides drinking water __1__ nearly half of the world's population. In China, about 70 percent of rural areas depend __2__ groundwater for daily use. However, groundwater __3__ (pollute) is becoming a serious issue. Chemicals from factories and pesticides from farms can seep into the ground and contaminate the water below. Once groundwater __4__ (become) polluted, it is __5__ (extreme) difficult to clean. The process may take decades __6__ even centuries. To protect groundwater, the government has banned factories from __7__ (dump) waste into the soil. Farmers are also encouraged to use __8__ (few) chemicals and adopt organic farming methods. Scientists have developed new __9__ (technique) to monitor groundwater quality using sensors placed deep underground. Each of us can help too — for example, by __10__ (proper) disposing of batteries and electronics instead of throwing them in the rubbish bin.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。provide sth. for sb. 为……提供，用 for。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。depend on 依赖、依靠，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：pollution**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。groundwater pollution 地下水污染，pollute 的名词形式为 pollution。

4. **答案：becomes**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。once 引导的条件状语从句用一般现在时，主语 groundwater 为第三人称单数，用 becomes。

5. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 difficult，用副词 extremely。

6. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：并列连词。decades or even centuries 几十年甚至几个世纪，or 表示"或者"。

7. **答案：dumping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ban sb. from doing sth. 禁止某人做某事，from 为介词，后接动名词 dumping。

8. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。chemicals 为可数名词，用 fewer（few 的比较级）。

9. **答案：techniques**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。new techniques 新技术，用复数 techniques。

10. **答案：properly**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动名词 disposing，用副词 properly。
