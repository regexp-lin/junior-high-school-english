# 01-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：冬日庆典 (A Winter Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, our school (1) ______ (hold) its annual Winter Festival. It was one of the (2) ______ (big) events of the school year, and every student was excited about it. The festival took place in the school playground, which (3) ______ (decorate) with colourful lights and paper snowflakes. There were many activities for students to enjoy. Some booths sold handmade crafts, (4) ______ others offered traditional Chinese snacks such as tanghulu and dumplings. A group of students from the drama club performed a short play (5) ______ (base) on a famous fairy tale. Their acting was so wonderful (6) ______ the audience clapped and cheered loudly. I volunteered at the charity booth, where we collected donations for children in rural areas. It made me feel proud to know that our (7) ______ (effort) could make a real difference in someone's life. The highlight of the festival was the talent show in the evening. My classmate, Wang Mei, surprised everyone (8) ______ singing a beautiful song that she had written (9) ______ (her). Her voice was clear and powerful, and she received the (10) ______ (loud) applause of the night. The Winter Festival brought the whole school together and created wonderful memories for all of us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last weekend` 表示过去的时间，`hold` 的过去式为 `held`。

2. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the biggest events`，"最……之一"用最高级。

3. **答案：was decorated**
   - **考点**：被动语态。操场"被装饰"，主语 `which` 指代 `playground`，用过去时被动。

4. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。`Some..., while others...` 表示对比，"一些……而另一些……"。

5. **答案：based**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a short play based on a famous fairy tale`，被动关系。

6. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so...that...` 结构，表示"如此……以至于……"。

7. **答案：efforts**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`our efforts` 指"我们的努力"，此处用复数表示多次付出。

8. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`surprised everyone by doing sth`，表示"通过做某事让大家惊讶"。

9. **答案：herself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`she had written herself`，强调她自己亲手写的。

10. **答案：loudest**
    - **考点**：最高级。`the loudest applause of the night`，在整晚中最响亮的掌声。
