# 01-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：新年决心 (New Year Resolutions)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every January, millions of people around the world make New Year's resolutions. A resolution is a promise you make to (1) ______ (you) to become a better person in the coming year. Some people want to eat (2) ______ (healthy) food, while others hope to spend more time with their families. My best friend, Li Ming, is one of the most (3) ______ (determine) people I know. Last year, he made a resolution (4) ______ (read) at least one book every month. At first, it was difficult for him to find time because he was busy (5) ______ (prepare) for exams. However, he managed to read during lunch breaks and before going to bed. By the end of the year, he (6) ______ (read) fifteen books — even more than his original goal! This year, I have decided to follow his example. I want to learn (7) ______ musical instrument. My father bought me a guitar last month, and I have been practising every day since then. Although my fingers hurt at the (8) ______ (begin), I am getting better little by little. Studies show that it takes about 21 days to form (9) ______ new habit. The key to success is not to give up, even when things get tough. I believe that if we set realistic goals and work towards (10) ______ (they) with patience, we can achieve anything.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：yourself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`make a promise to yourself`，主语为 `you`，对应反身代词 `yourself`。

2. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`eat healthier food` 与后句 `while others` 形成对比，暗含比较。

3. **答案：determined**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰人用 `-ed` 形式的形容词，`determined` 表示"有决心的"。

4. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：不定式。`a resolution to do sth`，不定式作后置定语。

5. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`be busy doing sth` 为固定搭配。

6. **答案：had read**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`By the end of the year` 表示到过去某个时间点之前已完成，用过去完成时。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`a musical instrument`，泛指"一种乐器"，`musical` 以辅音音素开头。

8. **答案：beginning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`at the beginning` 为固定搭配，介词后接名词。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`form a new habit`，泛指"一个新习惯"。

10. **答案：them**
    - **考点**：代词。介词 `towards` 后接宾格代词，代指前文的 `goals`。
