# 11-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市公共图书馆 (City Public Libraries)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Public libraries play __1__ important role in our communities. They provide free access __2__ books, magazines and digital resources for everyone. The library near my home __3__ (build) five years ago. It is a modern building with bright reading rooms and a quiet study area. I go there __4__ (two) a week to borrow books. Last Saturday, I attended a lecture on Chinese poetry that __5__ (hold) in the library's meeting hall. The speaker, a university professor, explained the __6__ (beautiful) of Tang Dynasty poems in a way that was easy __7__ (understand). About fifty people came to the event, and all of them enjoyed it __8__ (great). Libraries are not just places for reading. They also offer workshops, art exhibitions and children's storytelling __9__ (activity). My younger sister loves the picture book corner __10__ she can sit on soft cushions and read for hours. I believe libraries will continue to be important, even in the digital age.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。play an important role 发挥重要作用，important 以元音音素开头，用 an。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。access to 对……的使用/获取，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：was built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。library 与 build 为被动关系，five years ago 表过去时间，用一般过去时被动 was built。

4. **答案：twice**
   - **考点**：词性转换（数词→频率副词）。twice a week 每周两次，two 的相应形式为 twice。

5. **答案：was held**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。a lecture 与 hold 为被动关系，描述过去事件，用一般过去时被动 was held。

6. **答案：beauty**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。the beauty of 的美，beautiful 的名词形式为 beauty。

7. **答案：to understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be easy to understand 容易理解，"主语 + be + adj. + to do"结构。

8. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 enjoyed，用副词 greatly。

9. **答案：activities**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。与前面的 workshops, exhibitions 并列，用复数 activities。

10. **答案：where**
    - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 the picture book corner 表示地点，在从句中作地点状语，用 where。
