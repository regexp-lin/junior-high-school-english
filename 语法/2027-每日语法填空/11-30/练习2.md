# 11-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：青少年睡眠健康 (Teen Sleep Health)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Getting enough sleep is very important for teenagers. Scientists suggest that young people __1__ (age) 13 to 17 should sleep eight to ten hours every night. __2__, many students fail to get enough rest. A recent survey showed that over 60% of Chinese middle school students sleep __3__ (little) than eight hours on school nights. There are several __4__ (reason) for this. Heavy homework loads and the use of electronic __5__ (device) before bed are the main ones. Staring at phone screens makes __6__ harder for the brain to relax. My friend Wang Lei used to stay up late __7__ (play) mobile games. He often felt tired in class and his grades began to drop. After his parents talked __8__ him seriously, he decided to change. Now he puts his phone away at 9 p.m. and reads a book __9__ (stead). Within a month, he noticed that he could concentrate much __10__ (well) during lessons. A good night's sleep truly makes a difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：aged**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。aged 13 to 17 年龄在13到17岁的，用过去分词修饰 young people。

2. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词（转折关系）。前句说应该睡8-10小时，后句说很多学生做不到，语义转折用 However。

3. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。less than 少于，little 的比较级为 less。

4. **答案：reasons**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。several reasons 几个原因，several 后接可数名词复数。

5. **答案：devices**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。electronic devices 电子设备，用复数 devices。

6. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词（形式宾语）。makes it harder for the brain to relax，it 作形式宾语，真正的宾语是后面的不定式。

7. **答案：playing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作伴随状语）。stay up late playing 熬夜玩，用现在分词表示伴随。

8. **答案：to / with**
   - **考点**：介词。talk to / talk with sb. 与某人交谈，to 或 with 均可。

9. **答案：instead**
   - **考点**：词性转换。instead 替代地、作为替代，stead 的相关形式 instead，表示"取而代之"。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。much better 好得多，well 的比较级为 better，much 修饰比较级。
