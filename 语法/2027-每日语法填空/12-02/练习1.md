# 12-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：乡村振兴与直播带货 (Rural Revitalisation and Livestream Shopping)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, livestream shopping __1__ (become) a powerful tool for helping farmers sell their products. Many young people have returned __2__ their home villages to start online businesses. Li Hua is one of them. After __3__ (graduate) from university in 2022, she went back to her village in Guizhou and began selling local fruits through live broadcasts. At first, only a few __4__ (person) watched her streams. She did not give up. Instead, she learned how __5__ (attract) more viewers by improving her presentation skills. Gradually, her audience __6__ (grow) from dozens to thousands. Now she __7__ (successful) sells over 500 kilograms of fruit every week. Her business has also created jobs __8__ other villagers. "Livestream shopping is not just about making money," Li Hua says. "It is about __9__ (show) the world the beauty of our countryside." Thanks to people like her, many rural areas in China are becoming __10__ (rich) and more vibrant than ever before.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in recent years 与现在完成时连用，表示从过去到现在的变化，用 has become。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。return to 返回到……，to 表示方向。

3. **答案：graduating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。after graduating 毕业后，after 为介词，后接动名词 graduating。

4. **答案：people**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。a few people 几个人，person 的复数为 people。

5. **答案：to attract**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。how to attract 如何吸引，"疑问词 + to do"结构。

6. **答案：grew**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述过去发生的变化过程，用一般过去时 grew。

7. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 sells，用副词 successfully。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。create jobs for 为……创造工作，for 表示对象。

9. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作表语）。is about showing 关于向世界展示，about 为介词，后接动名词 showing。

10. **答案：richer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。richer and more vibrant than 比以前更富裕更有活力，rich 的比较级为 richer。
