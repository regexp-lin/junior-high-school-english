# 12-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：友谊与互相帮助 (Friendship and Mutual Help)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

True friendship is one of the __1__ (great) treasures in life. My best friend, Chen Wei, and I __2__ (know) each other since primary school. We do almost everything together — studying, playing basketball and __3__ (share) our secrets. Last winter, I broke my leg in a basketball game and had to stay at home for six weeks. I was worried __4__ falling behind in my studies. Chen Wei came to my house every day after school __5__ helped me go through the lessons I had __6__ (miss). He also brought me notes that __7__ (write) carefully by our classmates. __8__ his help, I did not fall behind at all. When the exam results came out, I got even __9__ (high) scores than before. I was deeply moved and wanted to do something for him in return. Friendship is not about how long you have known __10__ other. It is about who stands by your side when you need them most.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：greatest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the greatest 最伟大的……之一，great 的最高级为 greatest。

2. **答案：have known**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since primary school 自小学以来，与现在完成时连用，用 have known。

3. **答案：sharing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词与并列结构）。与前面的 studying, playing 并列，用动名词 sharing。

4. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。be worried about 担心……，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。came to my house and helped me 来我家并帮我，and 连接两个并列动作。

6. **答案：missed**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去分词）。the lessons I had missed 我落下的课程，过去完成时 had missed。

7. **答案：were written**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。notes 与 write 为被动关系，that 引导定语从句，描述过去的事用一般过去时被动 were written。

8. **答案：With**
   - **考点**：介词。with his help 在他的帮助下，with 表示"有、带有"。

9. **答案：higher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。higher... than 比……更高，high 的比较级为 higher。

10. **答案：each**
    - **考点**：代词。each other 彼此、互相，为固定搭配。
