# 06-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园篮球赛 (School Basketball Game)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Basketball is the most popular sport ___1___ our school. Every semester, a basketball tournament ___2___ (organize) for all grades. Last Friday, our class played against Class 5 in the semi-final. The game was ___3___ (extreme) exciting from start to finish. At half-time, we were three points behind, and everyone looked ___4___ (worry). Our coach told us to stay calm and work as ___5___ team. In the second half, Li Wei scored two three-pointers in ___6___ row, which completely changed the situation. The crowd ___7___ (go) wild with excitement. With only ten ___8___ (second) left, the score was tied. I got the ball and made a quick decision — I passed it to Zhang Hao, ___9___ was closer to the basket. He jumped high and scored the ___10___ (win) point just before the buzzer. We won 52 to 50!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in our school 表示"在我们学校里"，填 in。

2. **答案：is organized**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。tournament 与 organize 之间为被动关系，every semester 提示一般现在时，单数主语用 is organized。

3. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 exciting，需用副词形式，extreme → extremely。

4. **答案：worried**
   - **考点**：词性转换。looked 为系动词，后接形容词作表语，描述人的状态用 worried（感到担忧的）。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。work as a team 表示"作为一个团队合作"，team 可数名词单数，用不定冠词 a。

6. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。in a row 表示"连续地"，固定搭配，填 a。

7. **答案：went**
   - **考点**：动词时态。全文叙述过去比赛，go → went。go wild 表示"变得疯狂"。

8. **答案：seconds**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。ten 后接可数名词复数，second → seconds。

9. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 Zhang Hao（人），非限制性定语从句中作主语，用 who。

10. **答案：winning**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 point，现在分词作定语，the winning point 表示"制胜一分"，win → winning。
