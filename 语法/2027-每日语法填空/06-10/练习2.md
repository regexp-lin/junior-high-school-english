# 06-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国茶文化 (Chinese Tea Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tea is deeply rooted ___1___ Chinese culture and has a history of over four thousand years. It ___2___ (believe) that tea was first discovered by Shen Nong, an ancient Chinese emperor. There are many different ___3___ (type) of tea in China, including green tea, black tea and oolong tea. Each type has ___4___ (it) own special taste and health benefits. In southern China, drinking tea is not just a habit — it is a way of ___5___ (show) respect to guests. When someone pours tea for you, it is polite ___6___ (tap) your fingers on the table to say "thank you". My grandfather drinks tea every morning. He says tea helps him think more ___7___ (clear) and keeps him ___8___ (health). Chinese tea culture has also spread to other countries. In Japan, the tea ceremony has become ___9___ art form. People around the world are ___10___ (increasing) interested in learning about the story behind each cup of tea.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。be rooted in 表示"根植于"，固定搭配，填 in。

2. **答案：is believed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。It is believed that... 表示"人们相信……"，用一般现在时的被动语态。

3. **答案：types**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。many different + 复数名词，type → types。

4. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 own special taste，用形容词性物主代词 its（注意不是 it's）。

5. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。of 为介词，后接动名词，a way of showing 表示"一种表达……的方式"，填 showing。

6. **答案：to tap**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。it is + adj. + to do sth. 句型，it 为形式主语，真正主语为不定式，填 to tap。

7. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 think，需用副词形式，clear → clearly。

8. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。keep sb. + adj. 表示"使某人保持……状态"，health → healthy。

9. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。art 以元音音素开头，an art form 表示"一种艺术形式"，用不定冠词 an。

10. **答案：increasingly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 interested，需用副词形式，increasing → increasingly。
