# 05-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界红十字日 (World Red Cross Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

May 8th is World Red Cross Day, 1. ______ people honor volunteers who help others in trouble. The Red Cross was set up 2. ______ serve people in wars and disasters. In our city, teenagers can join training courses 3. ______ (learn) first aid. Last year, when a heavy storm hit the area, many workers brought food and blankets 4. ______ those who had lost their homes. My cousin took part in the work and said he felt tired 5. ______ happy because every small gift mattered. Schools also organize talks 6. ______ help students understand how 7. ______ (stay) calm in an emergency. A nurse showed us 8. ______ to tie a bandage correctly. She reminded us 9. ______ a simple skill may save a life. Helping others is not about being perfect; it is about being 10. ______ (prepare) to act when someone needs you.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。非限制性定语从句修饰 **May 8th**（表时间），从句中缺时间状语，故填 **when**。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的“为了服务……”，故填 **to**。

3. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的“为了学习急救”，故填 **to learn**。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**bring sth. to sb.**，故填 **to**。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。**tired** 与 **happy** 为转折关系，故填 **but**。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的“为了帮助学生们理解……”，故填 **to**。

7. **答案：to stay**
   - **考点**：“疑问词 + 不定式”。**how to stay calm** 作 **understand** 的宾语，故填 **to stay**。

8. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。**how to tie a bandage** 表示“如何包扎绷带”，故填 **how**。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**remind us** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

10. **答案：prepared**
    - **考点**：词性转换。**be prepared to do sth.**，用形容词 **prepared**。
