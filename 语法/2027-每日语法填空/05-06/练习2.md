# 05-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：自然教育 (Nature Education)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

More schools are taking students outdoors because nature is 1. ______ best classroom without walls. Last spring, our biology teacher led us 2. ______ a forest park to study plants. We carried notebooks, magnifying glasses, 3. ______ a simple map. Along the path, we observed how trees 4. ______ (grow) taller year by year. A guide warned us not 5. ______ (step) on young grass or pick wild flowers. "If you listen carefully, you can hear birds singing 6. ______ (happy) above the trees," she said. Some classmates recorded the sounds 7. ______ their phones. Back at school, we wrote reports and realized 8. ______ humans depend on clean air and water. Protecting nature is not someone else's job—it starts 9. ______ each of us. Even picking up one piece of litter on 10. ______ hike makes a difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：定冠词。形容词最高级 **best** 前用 **the**。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**lead sb. to + 地点**，故填 **to**。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。列举并列宾语，故填 **and**。

4. **答案：grow**
   - **考点**：动词时态。宾语从句描述客观规律，用一般现在时；主语 **trees** 为复数，故填 **grow**。

5. **答案：to step**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**warn sb. not to do sth.**，故填 **to step**。

6. **答案：happily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **singing**，用副词 **happily**。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**with their phones** 表示“用手机”，故填 **with**。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**realized** 后接宾语从句，用 **that** 引导。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**start with each of us** 表示“从我们每一个人开始”，故填 **with**。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。**on a hike**，故填 **a**。
