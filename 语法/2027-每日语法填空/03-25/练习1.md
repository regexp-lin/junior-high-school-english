# 03-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市雕塑 (City Sculptures)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Walk through any modern Chinese city, and you will notice metal and stone figures standing in squares, parks, and near museums. City sculptures have become important symbols of local culture.

In my hometown, a tall stainless-steel work (1) ______ (rise) beside the river. It looks like a wave frozen in time. Every evening, lights shine on its surface, (2) ______ (make) it visible from far away.

The artist said he wanted to show the energy of our port city. Not everyone liked the design at first — some citizens thought it was too abstract. (3) ______, after reading the explanation on the sign, more people began to appreciate its meaning.

Public art can also honor history. Near our school stands a bronze statue of a writer (4) ______ lived in the 1930s. Students often leave flowers at its feet on important anniversaries.

Of course, placing a sculpture is not an easy decision. Experts must consider wind, safety, and (5) ______ the work matches the surroundings. A piece that looks perfect in one place may seem out of place in another.

Last year, our art class visited a foundry (6) ______ workers turn sketches into large models. We watched molten metal (7) ______ (pour) into molds — a process both dangerous and fascinating.

I believe good sculptures make cities warmer and (8) ______ (interest). They invite people to stop, think, and talk. If more young artists are given chances to show their talent, our streets (9) ______ (become) even more lively.

Next time you pass a strange statue, don't hurry. Take a minute to guess what story it might (10) ______ (tell).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rises**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。主语 `a tall ... work` 单数，陈述现状用第三人称单数。

2. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：现在分词作结果状语。灯光照射使其从远处可见，表自然而然的结果。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：转折副词。起初并非人人都喜欢，然而了解含义后……，句首大写。

4. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `writer` 指人，从句缺主语。

5. **答案：whether / if**
   - **考点**：宾语从句。`consider whether/if ... matches` 考虑作品是否与周围环境相配。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `foundry` 表地点，从句缺状语。

7. **答案：poured / being poured**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`watch sth. done` 观看金属被倒入；或 `being poured` 强调进行中的被动。

8. **答案：more interesting**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与 `warmer` 并列，城市更温暖、更有趣。

9. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`If` 从句一般现在时，主句用 will。

10. **答案：tell**
    - **考点**：情态动词后接动词原形。`might tell` 可能讲述。
