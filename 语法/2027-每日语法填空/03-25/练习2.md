# 03-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家庭自驾游 (Family Road Trip)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last National Day holiday, my parents decided to take a road trip to the mountains instead of joining crowded tour groups. We packed snacks, maps, and a first-aid kit (1) ______ the trunk of our car.

The journey took six hours. My father drove carefully on narrow roads (2) ______ my mother played soft music. I sat in the back seat, (3) ______ (watch) green hills pass by the window.

We stopped twice at service areas to stretch our legs. At noon, we ate noodles at a small restaurant (4) ______ the owner recommended a hiking trail nearby. The food was simple, (5) ______ it tasted wonderful after a long ride.

In the afternoon, we reached a village known for ancient trees. We walked along a stone path and took photos of a waterfall. My legs ached a little, (6) ______ I felt relaxed and free.

That night, we stayed in a wooden inn. Crickets sang outside the window, and I fell asleep (7) ______ I was listening to my parents' quiet conversation.

Road trips teach families to solve problems together. When our GPS stopped working, we used a paper map (8) ______ (find) the way. The experience brought us closer than any expensive gift.

If we plan another trip next year, I hope we can invite my grandparents (9) ______ (come) along. The car may be crowded, (10) ______ the memories will be sweeter.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in the trunk` 在后备箱里。

2. **答案：while / as**
   - **考点**：连词。父亲开车同时母亲放音乐，表同时进行。

3. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。`I sat ... watching` 坐着看山景。

4. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `restaurant` 表地点，从句缺状语。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。食物简单但长途之后很美味。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。腿酸但感到放松自由。

7. **答案：while / as**
   - **考点**：连词。`fell asleep while listening` 听着父母说话入睡。

8. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`use a map to find` 用地图找路。

9. **答案：to come**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`invite sb. to do sth.` 邀请某人同行。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：转折连词。车可能拥挤，但回忆更甜。
