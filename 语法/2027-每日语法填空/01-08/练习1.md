# 01-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：网上购物 (Online Shopping)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Online shopping has changed the way people buy things. With just a few (1) ______ (click) on a smartphone, you can order almost anything and have it (2) ______ (deliver) to your door within days. According to a recent report, China is now the (3) ______ (large) online shopping market in the world, with hundreds of millions of consumers making purchases on platforms like Taobao and JD every day. There are many advantages (4) ______ shopping online. First, it saves time. You do not need to travel to different shops or wait in long queues. Second, it is often (5) ______ (cheap) to buy things online because internet stores have lower costs than physical shops. (6) ______, online shopping also has some disadvantages. One major problem is that you cannot touch or try on the products before (7) ______ (buy) them. This sometimes leads to disappointment when the item looks (8) ______ (complete) different from the pictures on the website. Another concern is the security of personal (9) ______ (inform). Hackers may steal your bank details if you are not careful. To stay safe, experts advise people (10) ______ (shop) only on trusted websites and to change their passwords regularly.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：clicks**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few clicks`，`a few` 修饰可数名词复数。

2. **答案：delivered**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`have it delivered`，`have sth done` 结构，表示让某事被完成。

3. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the largest online shopping market in the world`，最高级表示范围内最大。

4. **答案：of / to**
   - **考点**：介词。`advantages of/to doing sth`，表示"做某事的优势"。

5. **答案：cheaper**
   - **考点**：比较级。上下文暗含网购与实体店的比较。

6. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。前文讲优点，后文转折讲缺点。

7. **答案：buying**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `before` 后接动名词形式。

8. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `different` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：information**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`personal information`，形容词后接名词，`information` 为不可数名词。

10. **答案：to shop**
    - **考点**：不定式。`advise sb to do sth` 为固定搭配。
