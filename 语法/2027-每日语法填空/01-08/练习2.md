# 01-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家庭聚餐 (Family Dinner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In Chinese culture, sharing a meal together is one of the most important ways for families to stay (1) ______ (connect). Every Sunday evening, my grandparents invite the whole family to their house for dinner. My grandmother is an (2) ______ (excellence) cook who can turn simple ingredients into (3) ______ (amaze) dishes. She usually starts preparing in the morning, and by the time we arrive, the kitchen is already filled (4) ______ wonderful smells. My grandfather's speciality is making dumplings. He learned the skill from (5) ______ (he) mother many years ago, and now he is teaching it to me. Making dumplings together has become a tradition (6) ______ brings our family closer. While we eat, everyone shares stories about (7) ______ happened during the week. My uncle always tells funny jokes that make us laugh until our stomachs hurt. My little cousin, who is only four years old, tries (8) ______ (copy) everything the adults do, which is incredibly (9) ______ (adore). These Sunday dinners mean more to me than just food. They are a reminder that no matter how busy our lives become, it is important to make time (10) ______ the people we love. I hope this tradition will continue for many generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：connected**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`stay connected` 表示"保持联系"，系动词后接形容词化的过去分词。

2. **答案：excellent**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `cook` 需要形容词形式。

3. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式，表示"令人惊叹的"。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be filled with` 为固定搭配，表示"充满"。

5. **答案：his**
   - **考点**：物主代词。`his mother`，与主语 `He` 对应。

6. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句修饰 `tradition`，在从句中作主语。

7. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`what happened during the week`，`what` 引导宾语从句并在从句中作主语。

8. **答案：to copy**
   - **考点**：不定式。`try to do sth` 表示"努力做某事"。

9. **答案：adorable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is incredibly adorable`，系动词后接形容词。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。`make time for sb` 表示"为某人腾出时间"。
