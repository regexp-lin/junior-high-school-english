# 07-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：包饺子 (Making Dumplings)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Sunday morning, my grandmother taught me ___1___ important skill — making dumplings at home. In our family, this tradition often brings three generations together during festivals. The soft dough and the rich filling always remind us of shared stories from past years. First, she asked me ___2___ (help) her prepare the filling: we ___3___ (mix) pork, vegetables and a little salt in a big bowl. She taught me to season it slowly so that the flavor could spread evenly inside the mixture. "Remember to cut the vegetables into ___4___ (small) pieces first," she said with a warm smile. I tried to follow each step ___5___ (careful), though my fingers trembled with excitement. I had watched adults wrap dumplings many times, yet doing it myself felt surprisingly difficult at the beginning.

The hardest part was folding the thin skins without tearing them. My first dumplings looked rather funny, ___6___ Grandma kept encouraging me. She placed her hands over mine and guided the edges until a neat pattern appeared. ___7___ (final), one of them really looked like ___8___ (she). We lined them up neatly on a wooden board and put them into ___9___ pot of boiling water. While we waited, steam rose like thin clouds above the stove. Soon the kitchen was filled with a mouth-watering smell that drew my parents into the room. When I tasted my own work, I believed nothing could ___10___ (taste) better. On that sunny afternoon, I not only learned a practical skill but also understood why food can carry so much love.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`important` 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，表示“一项重要的技能”时要用不定冠词 **an**，不能用 a。

2. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动词不定式）。固定结构 **ask sb. to do sth.**（请/让某人做某事），此处需用不定式 **to help** 作宾语补足语。

3. **答案：mixed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述上周日发生的事，主句与并列谓语均用一般过去时；**mix** 的过去式为 **mixed**，与前面的 **asked** 叙事时态一致。

4. **答案：small**
   - **考点**：形容词。空格在名词 **pieces** 前作定语，需用形容词原级 **small**；**into small pieces** 为常见搭配（切成小块）。

5. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：形容词与副词。修饰动词 **follow** 要用副词；**careful** 的副词形式为 **carefully**。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前句说饺子“看起来好笑”，后句说奶奶“一直鼓励我”，语义转折，用并列连词 **but**。

7. **答案：Finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。在句首作评注性状语，表示“最后；终于”，用副词 **Finally**；注意句首大写。

8. **答案：hers**
   - **考点**：代词。比较结构 **look like** 后需接名词性成分，表示“她的（饺子）”，用名词性物主代词 **hers**，相当于 **her dumplings**。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。**pot** 可数名词单数，此处泛指“一口锅”，**pot** 以辅音音素开头，用 **a**。

10. **答案：taste**
    - **考点**：动词形式。情态动词 **could** 后接动词原形，故用 **taste**（尝起来）。**nothing could taste better** 表示“没有比这更好吃的了”。
