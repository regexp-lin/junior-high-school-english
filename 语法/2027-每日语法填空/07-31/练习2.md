# 07-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：珊瑚礁保护 (Coral Reef Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Coral reefs look like colorful underwater forests, ___1___ they support thousands of marine species. Tiny fish hide among the branches, while larger hunters swim nearby, so the whole system works like a busy neighborhood underwater. However, these precious ecosystems ___2___ (face) serious threats today. Rising sea temperatures ___3___ (cause) many corals to lose their bright colors, a process ___4___ (call) "coral bleaching". Without their partners, the reef community becomes weaker and may even collapse. Local fishing communities then lose income and food sources, and tourists see fewer lively scenes beneath the waves.

In recent decades, many reefs around the world ___5___ (damage) by pollution, coastal development and overfishing. Scientists warn that ___6___ we fail to take action, more underwater gardens may turn into grey, lifeless areas. To fight this crisis, some countries ___7___ (set) up marine protected zones where harmful fishing is banned, and teams of volunteers also help ___8___ (plant) young corals on damaged beds. They work slowly but patiently, hoping each fragile piece will grow stronger in clean water.

Teenagers can contribute, too. For instance, we may choose reef-safe sunscreen ___9___ entering the water, and we should never stand on or break corals ___10___ we snorkel or dive. Saving reefs is not a job for scientists alone — it is a shared duty for everyone who loves the ocean.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。前后两分句为顺承关系：珊瑚礁像海底森林，“并且”它们维系着大量物种。用并列连词 **and** 连接两个完整分句。

2. **答案：are facing**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间状语 **today** 与 **serious threats** 强调当前阶段正在发生的情况，用**现在进行时 are facing** 表示“正面临”；主语 **ecosystems** 为复数，be 动词用 **are**。

3. **答案：cause**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。主语 **Rising sea temperatures** 为复数概念，谓语动词用复数形式 **cause**；此处陈述普遍的科学事实，用**一般现在时**即可，与后文 **becomes** 等现在时态一致。

4. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。**process** 与 **call** 之间为被动关系（过程被称为……），用过去分词 **called** 作后置定语，相当于定语从句 **which is called**。

5. **答案：have been damaged**
   - **考点**：动词时态和语态。时间标志 **In recent decades** 常与**现在完成时**连用；**reefs** 与 **damage** 为被动关系（被破坏），故用**现在完成时的被动语态 have been damaged**。

6. **答案：if**
   - **考点**：连词。引导条件状语从句：**如果我们**不采取行动，更多区域可能……。用 **if** 表示条件。

7. **答案：have set**
   - **考点**：动词时态。**set up** 表示“建立”。强调过去动作对现在的影响（保护区已存在并发挥作用），用**现在完成时 have set**；主语 **countries** 为复数，助动词用 **have**。**set** 的过去分词仍为 **set**。

8. **答案：plant**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。固定搭配 **help (to) do sth.**，**help** 后接动词原形 **plant**；**to** 常可省略，中考填空中以 **plant** 为规范答案。

9. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：介词。表示“在进入水中之前”选择对珊瑚友好的防晒霜，用 **before**（在……之前），后接动名词 **entering**。

10. **答案：when**
    - **考点**：连词。引导时间状语从句：在浮潜或潜水**时**不要踩踏或折断珊瑚。用 **when** 表示“当……时候”，既可接短暂性动词 **dive**，也符合并列 **snorkel or dive** 的用法。
