# 03-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：养蚕记 (Raising Silkworms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last spring, our biology teacher gave each group a small box of silkworm eggs. "You will see how silk (1) ______ (produce) in nature," she said with a smile.

We placed the eggs in a warm corner of the classroom. A week later, tiny black worms appeared and began (2) ______ (eat) mulberry leaves we brought from home. Every morning, someone had to clean the box and add fresh leaves. The job was tiring, (3) ______ nobody complained.

As the silkworms grew, they changed color from black to white. They ate day and night until they became much (4) ______ (fat) than rice grains. Finally, each worm climbed onto a straw frame and spun a soft cocoon around (5) ______.

I will never forget the moment I saw the first cocoon. It looked like a cotton ball, (6) ______ it was actually the silkworm's home for its next stage of life. Inside, the worm would turn into a pupa and later break out (7) ______ a moth.

Our teacher explained that silk farming began in China thousands of years ago. The Silk Road carried beautiful cloth to faraway lands. Today, machines can make silk (8) ______ (quick), but many farmers still raise silkworms by hand.

This project taught me to respect small lives. I also learned (9) ______ teamwork could turn a difficult task into sweet memories.

When summer came, we set the moths free in the school garden. Watching them fly away, I felt (10) ______ I had said goodbye to old friends.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is produced**
   - **考点**：一般现在时被动语态。丝绸在自然界中“被生产”，宾语从句陈述事实。

2. **答案：eating / to eat**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`begin eating/to eat` 开始吃桑叶。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。工作累但无人抱怨。

4. **答案：fatter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than rice grains` 提示，`fat` → `fatter`。

5. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`around itself` 围绕自身结茧。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。看起来像棉球，但实际上是……。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`break out as a moth` 作为蛾破茧而出。

8. **答案：more quickly**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。机器制丝更快，与手工隐含对比，用 more quickly。

9. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句。`learned how teamwork could ...` 学到团队合作如何……。

10. **答案：as if / though**
    - **考点**：表语从句。`felt as if I had said goodbye` 仿佛与老友告别。
