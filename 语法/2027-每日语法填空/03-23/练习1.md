# 03-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：外卖文化 (Food Delivery Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In big cities, tapping a phone screen and waiting for hot meals at the door has become part of daily life. Food delivery apps connect restaurants (1) ______ customers who are too busy to cook.

My uncle runs a small noodle shop. He says online orders have brought him (2) ______ (many) customers than three years ago. However, he also faces new challenges. During rush hours, cooks work under pressure (3) ______ (prepare) dozens of meals at the same time.

Some people worry that young adults are losing basic cooking skills. They order takeout even (4) ______ weekends when they have free time. Nutrition experts remind us that restaurant food may contain more salt and oil (5) ______ home-cooked dishes.

On the positive side, delivery services save time for students and workers. In bad weather, riders still hurry through rain and wind (6) ______ (bring) food on time. Many of them wear helmets and follow traffic rules (7) ______ (careful).

Governments are setting clearer standards for packaging and food safety. A few communities have built shared kitchens (8) ______ residents can learn simple recipes at low cost.

As for me, I enjoy trying different flavors, (9) ______ I try to cook at least twice a week. Balance is important: technology can make life easier, (10) ______ we should not depend on it for everything.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with / to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`connect A with/to B` 把餐厅与顾客连接起来。

2. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than three years ago` 提示比较级，`more customers`。

3. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`under pressure to prepare` 有压力去准备，不定式表方面/目的。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on weekends` 在周末。

5. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。`more ... than ...` 比家常菜含更多油盐。

6. **答案：to bring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`hurry ... to bring` 匆忙送餐，不定式表目的。

7. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `follow`，`careful` → `carefully`。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 `kitchens` 表地点，从句缺状语。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。喜欢尝试不同口味，但尽量每周自己做饭。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：转折连词。科技让生活更轻松，但不应事事依赖。
