# 01-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国民间故事 (Chinese Folk Tales)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese folk tales have been told and retold for thousands of years, passing (1) ______ wisdom and values from one generation to the next. One of the most beloved stories is "The Cowherd and the Weaver Girl," a romantic tale about two lovers who are (2) ______ (separate) by the Milky Way and can only meet once a year, on the seventh day of the seventh lunar month. Another well-known story is "Yu Gong Moves Mountains," which tells of an old man who was (3) ______ (determine) to remove two huge mountains that blocked the path in front of his house. When others laughed at him and told him his task was (4) ______ (possible), Yu Gong replied that even if he could not finish the work, his sons and grandsons would carry it on. His perseverance eventually (5) ______ (impress) the gods, who sent two giants to move the mountains away. This story has become a symbol of (6) ______ (patient) and determination in Chinese culture. Folk tales like these are not just entertaining stories — they carry deep (7) ______ (moral) lessons that are still relevant today. They teach us the importance of kindness, hard work and never giving up in the face of (8) ______ (difficult). In schools, teachers often use folk tales (9) ______ (help) students learn about Chinese culture and develop good character. I think these stories deserve (10) ______ (tell) again and again, so that their wisdom is never forgotten.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：down**
   - **考点**：副词。`passing down` 表示"传承，传递"。

2. **答案：separated**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`are separated by`，被动语态。

3. **答案：determined**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was determined to`，表示"决心做某事"。

4. **答案：impossible**
   - **考点**：词性转换。加否定前缀 `im-`，`impossible` 表示"不可能的"。

5. **答案：impressed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的故事情节。

6. **答案：patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a symbol of patience`，介词后接名词。

7. **答案：moral**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `lessons` 需要形容词形式。

8. **答案：difficulty / difficulties**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`in the face of difficulty`，介词后接名词。

9. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：不定式。`use folk tales to help students`，不定式表目的。

10. **答案：to be told**
    - **考点**：不定式被动。`deserve to be told`，表示"值得被讲述"。
