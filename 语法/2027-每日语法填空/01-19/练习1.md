# 01-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：骑行运动 (Cycling)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Cycling has become (1) ______ (increase) popular among young people in China in recent years. Not only is it a great form of exercise, but it is also an eco-friendly way to travel. Shared bicycles, (2) ______ can be found on almost every street corner, have made cycling more accessible than ever. My older brother is a keen cyclist who rides his bike to work every day regardless (3) ______ the weather. He says that cycling helps him stay fit and allows him to avoid the stress of being stuck (4) ______ traffic. Last summer, he invited me to join him on a weekend cycling trip to a lake about 30 kilometres from our city. I was a bit worried at first because I had never cycled such a long (5) ______ (distant) before. He told me not to worry and promised that we would take plenty of breaks along the way. We set off at six in the morning to avoid the midday heat. The ride through the countryside was (6) ______ (absolute) beautiful. We passed golden wheat fields, crossed small stone bridges and cycled along tree-lined roads (7) ______ sunlight filtered through the leaves above. When we finally arrived at the lake, I felt tired (8) ______ happy. We sat by the water, eating the sandwiches we (9) ______ (bring) with us and enjoying the peaceful scenery. That trip made me realise that cycling is not just about (10) ______ (get) from one place to another — it is about enjoying the journey itself.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `popular` 需要副词形式。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `Shared bicycles`。

3. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`regardless of` 为固定搭配，表示"不管，不顾"。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`being stuck in traffic` 表示"堵在路上"。

5. **答案：distance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`such a long distance`，形容词后接名词。

6. **答案：absolutely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `beautiful` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `tree-lined roads`，在从句中作地点状语。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`tired but happy`，表示转折。

9. **答案：had brought**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。三明治是出发前已经"带好"的，"过去的过去"。

10. **答案：getting**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `about` 后接动名词形式。
