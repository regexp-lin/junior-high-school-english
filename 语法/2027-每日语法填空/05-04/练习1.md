# 05-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：护士的故事 (A Nurse's Story)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Aunt Lin has worked as 1. ______ nurse in our community hospital for fifteen years. She often says her job is busy 2. ______ meaningful. Every morning, she checks patients' temperature and makes sure medicine 3. ______ (give) on time. One winter night, an old man was sent in because he felt sick. His family could not arrive 4. ______ once, so Aunt Lin stayed 5. ______ him and explained every step 6. ______ (clear). "You are not alone," she said softly. The man held her hand and nodded. Later, his son wrote 7. ______ thank-you letter to the hospital. Aunt Lin's story reminds us 8. ______ care is not only about skills but also about patience. Young people in my class now talk about 9. ______ (become) nurses or doctors more seriously. I believe small acts of kindness can change 10. ______ person's whole day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **nurse** 单数，表泛指“一名护士”，故填 **a**。

2. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。**busy** 与 **meaningful** 为转折关系，“忙碌但有意义的”，故填 **but**。

3. **答案：is given**
   - **考点**：被动语态与主谓一致。**medicine** 与 **give** 为被动关系，且描述常规情况用一般现在时，medicine 为不可数名词，谓语用 **is given**。

4. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。**at once** 为固定搭配，表示“立刻”，故填 **at**。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。**stay with sb.** 表示“陪在某人身边”，故填 **with**。

6. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **explained** 需用副词，形容词 clear 的副词形式为 **clearly**。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a thank-you letter** 表示“一封感谢信”，thank 以辅音音素开头，故填 **a**。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。**remind us that...** 中 **that** 引导宾语从句，说明提醒的内容，故填 **that**。

9. **答案：becoming**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。介词 **about** 后接动名词，**talk about doing sth.**，故填 **becoming**。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **person** 单数，表泛指“一个人的一整天”，故填 **a**。
