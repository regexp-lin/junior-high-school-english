# 05-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：棋牌运动 (Board Games)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many people think board games are only for children, but they are 1. ______ (wide) enjoyed by adults as well. In our school club, students meet twice 2. ______ week to play chess and Chinese chess. These games train the mind and teach us 3. ______ (think) before we move a piece. Last term, we held 4. ______ small match in the reading room. I played against 5. ______ boy from Class 8. The game lasted 6. ______ an hour, and neither of us wanted 7. ______ (give) up. In the end, we reached 8. ______ draw and shook hands. Our teacher said that winning is not the only goal; learning 9. ______ (wise) from mistakes matters more. Now I spend less time on phone games 10. ______ more time face to face with friends over a board. That feels healthier and happier.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **enjoyed**，用副词 **widely**。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**twice a week**，故填 **a**。

3. **答案：to think**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**teach sb. to do sth.**，故填 **to think**。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。可数名词 **match** 单数，表泛指，故填 **a**。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a boy from Class 8**，故填 **a**。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。**last for an hour** 表示“持续一小时”，故填 **for**。

7. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**want to do sth.**，故填 **to give**。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**reach a draw** 表示“打成平局”，故填 **a**。

9. **答案：wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动名词 **learning**，用副词 **wisely**。

10. **答案：and**
    - **考点**：并列连词。**less time... and more time...** 并列，故填 **and**。
