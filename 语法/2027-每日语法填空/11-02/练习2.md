# 11-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园图书交换 (School Book Exchange)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school library held a book exchange event last Wednesday. Students could bring books they had already __1__ (read) and trade them for new ones. I brought three novels that I __2__ (finish) reading during the summer holiday. When I arrived at the library, tables were covered __3__ hundreds of books of all __4__ (kind). Some were adventure stories __5__ others were science fiction or history books. I spent nearly an hour __6__ (look) through the piles. Finally, I found a book about deep-sea creatures __7__ caught my attention immediately. The pictures inside were __8__ (absolute) stunning. I also picked up a collection of short stories __9__ (write) by a young author. In return, I left my three books on the table for other students to choose. The event was a great __10__ (succeed) — over 500 books found new owners that day!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：read**
   - **考点**：动词时态。had already read 过去完成时，read 的过去分词仍为 read（发音 /red/）。

2. **答案：had finished**
   - **考点**：动词时态。带到图书馆之前就已经读完，动作发生在过去的过去，用过去完成时 had finished。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。be covered with 被……覆盖，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：kinds**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。all kinds 各种各样的，kind 用复数 kinds。

5. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。some... others... 结构，可用 while 表示对比，也可用 and。

6. **答案：looking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。spend time (in) doing sth. 花时间做某事，用动名词 looking。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 a book 指物，从句缺主语，用 that 或 which。

8. **答案：absolutely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 stunning，用副词 absolutely。

9. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。stories 与 write 为被动关系，用过去分词 written。

10. **答案：success**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a great success 巨大的成功，succeed 的名词形式为 success。
