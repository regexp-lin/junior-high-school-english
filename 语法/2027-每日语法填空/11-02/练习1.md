# 11-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：天文观星 (Stargazing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday night, our science club organised a stargazing event on the school rooftop. The sky was clear and the stars shone __1__ (bright). Our teacher brought two __2__ (telescope) so we could observe the night sky more closely. First, he pointed __3__ the Big Dipper and explained how ancient travellers used it __4__ (navigate). Then we looked at the moon __5__ the telescope. I was amazed to see the craters on __6__ (it) surface so clearly. The __7__ (beautiful) sight of the evening was Jupiter, __8__ appeared as a bright dot with four tiny moons around it. Our teacher told us that light from some stars takes __9__ (million) of years to reach the Earth. That means we are actually looking __10__ the past! The experience made me realise how vast and mysterious the universe is.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：brightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 shone，用副词 brightly。

2. **答案：telescopes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。two 后接可数名词复数，用 telescopes。

3. **答案：out / at**
   - **考点**：介词/副词。point out 指出 / point at 指向，两者均可。

4. **答案：to navigate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。use sth. to do sth. 用某物做某事，用不定式 to navigate。

5. **答案：through**
   - **考点**：介词。through the telescope 通过望远镜，用 through 表示"透过"。

6. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 surface，用形容词性物主代词 its。

7. **答案：most beautiful**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，beautiful 为多音节词，最高级用 most beautiful。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 Jupiter 指物，非限制性定语从句用 which。

9. **答案：millions**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。millions of 数百万个，为固定搭配。

10. **答案：into**
    - **考点**：介词。look into the past 回望过去，into 表示"进入"。
