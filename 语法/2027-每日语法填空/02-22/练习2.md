# 02-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：时间管理 (Time Management)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As a Grade 9 student, I often feel that there are not enough hours in the day. Between schoolwork, extracurricular (1) ______ (activity) and family responsibilities, managing my time (2) ______ (effective) has become a real challenge. Last month, my class teacher gave us a lecture on time management that completely changed my approach. She introduced a simple but (3) ______ (power) technique called the "four-quadrant method." According to this method, all tasks can (4) ______ (divide) into four categories based on their urgency and importance. Tasks that are both urgent and important should be done (5) ______ (one). Important but not urgent tasks should be scheduled for later. Urgent but (6) ______ (important) tasks can be delegated to others, and tasks that are neither urgent nor important should be avoided altogether. I started applying this method to my daily study plan. Instead of spending hours (7) ______ social media after school, I now complete my most important homework first. Then I use the remaining time for less urgent tasks (8) ______ as reviewing notes or practising English. The result has been (9) ______ (amaze) — my grades have improved, and I actually have more free time than before. The key lesson I have learned is that being busy does not mean being (10) ______ (product). It is not about how much time you have, but how wisely you use it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：activities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`extracurricular activities` 课外活动。

2. **答案：effectively**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`managing time effectively` 有效地管理时间。

3. **答案：powerful**
   - **考点**：形容词。`a simple but powerful technique`，与 `simple` 并列。

4. **答案：be divided**
   - **考点**：含情态动词的被动语态。`can be divided` 能被划分。

5. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：副词。`should be done first` 应该首先完成。

6. **答案：unimportant**
   - **考点**：前缀。`urgent but unimportant` 紧急但不重要的，加否定前缀。

7. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`spend time on social media` 在社交媒体上花时间。

8. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`such as` 例如。

9. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`has been amazing` 令人惊叹的，修饰结果。

10. **答案：productive**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`being productive` 有效率的，形容词。
