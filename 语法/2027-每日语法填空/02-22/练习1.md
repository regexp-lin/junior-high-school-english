# 02-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：动物园研学 (Zoo Study Trip)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our biology class took a study trip to the Guangzhou Zoo last Thursday. The purpose of the trip was to learn about animal (1) ______ (behave) and conservation efforts. The zoo is home (2) ______ over 400 species of animals from around the world. Our first stop was the panda enclosure. Two giant pandas (3) ______ (name) Mei Mei and Da Zhuang were munching on bamboo lazily. Our teacher told us that giant pandas eat up to 38 kilograms of bamboo a day, (4) ______ takes them about 16 hours. I was shocked to learn how much time they spend just (5) ______ (eat)! Next, we visited the African animals section. The giraffes were the (6) ______ (impressive) creatures I had ever seen in person. Their necks were much (7) ______ (long) than I had imagined. A zookeeper gave us a talk about the challenges of protecting endangered species. She explained that habitat (8) ______ (lose) is the biggest threat to wildlife worldwide. Many animals are losing their homes because forests (9) ______ (cut) down to make way for farming and development. She emphasized that everyone can help by supporting conservation organizations and making (10) ______ (sustain) choices in daily life. The trip was both educational and enjoyable.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：behaviour**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`animal behaviour` 动物行为，名词形式。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be home to` 是……的栖息地。

3. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`Two pandas named...` 两只名叫……的熊猫。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代吃竹子这件事。

5. **答案：eating**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

6. **答案：most impressive**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the most impressive creatures`，多音节用 `most`。

7. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much longer than` 比想象的长得多。

8. **答案：loss**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`habitat loss` 栖息地丧失，名词。

9. **答案：are being cut**
   - **考点**：现在进行时的被动语态。森林正在被砍伐。

10. **答案：sustainable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`sustainable choices` 可持续的选择，形容词修饰名词。
