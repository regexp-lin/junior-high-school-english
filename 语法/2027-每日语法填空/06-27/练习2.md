# 06-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：露营体验 (Camping Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Camping has become ___1___ (increase) popular among young Chinese families. Last October, my family decided to go camping near a lake in the countryside. We packed a tent, sleeping bags and some food, then drove for about two ___2___ (hour). When we arrived, the view was ___3___ (breath) — golden leaves covered the ground and the lake shone like a mirror. My father taught me how ___4___ (set) up the tent. It was not as easy ___5___ it looked! After several attempts, we ___6___ (final) got it right. In the evening, we made a campfire and roasted marshmallows. Sitting under the stars, I could hear nothing ___7___ the sound of crickets and running water. It was so peaceful ___8___ I wished time would stop. The next morning, I woke up to the songs of birds ___9___ (sing) in the trees. We cooked a simple breakfast and then went for a hike around the lake. This trip helped me understand ___10___ nature is the best place to recharge.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 popular，需用副词形式，increase → increasingly。

2. **答案：hours**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。two 后接可数名词复数，hour → hours。

3. **答案：breathtaking**
   - **考点**：词性转换。was 后接形容词作表语，breath → breathtaking（令人叹为观止的）。

4. **答案：to set**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。how to do sth. 表示"如何做某事"，teach sb. how to do sth.，填 to set。

5. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。not as easy as 表示"没有看起来那么容易"，as...as 结构，填 as。

6. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 got，需用副词形式，final → finally。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。nothing but 表示"除了……什么都没有"，固定搭配，填 but。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。so...that... 引导结果状语从句，表示"如此安宁以至于……"，填 that。

9. **答案：singing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作后置定语/补语，birds singing in the trees 表示"在树上唱歌的鸟"，填 singing。

10. **答案：why**
    - **考点**：连词。understand why... 表示"理解为什么……"，why 引导宾语从句，填 why。
