# 06-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：自然灾害与防范 (Natural Disasters and Prevention)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Natural disasters such as floods, typhoons and earthquakes cause great damage every year. China ___1___ (experience) several serious floods in recent years, especially during the summer rainy season. In July 2021, heavy rainfall hit the city of Zhengzhou, ___2___ (cause) severe flooding in just a few hours. Many streets were turned ___3___ rivers, and thousands of people had to be rescued. Experts say that the ___4___ (frequent) of extreme weather events is increasing because ___5___ climate change. To reduce the damage caused by natural disasters, the government has invested heavily ___6___ early warning systems. These systems can send alerts to people's phones ___7___ (minute) before a disaster strikes. Schools also teach students basic ___8___ (survive) skills, such as how to find safe shelter and ___9___ to do after an earthquake. Being prepared can make the difference ___10___ life and death.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has experienced**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in recent years 提示用现在完成时，China 为单数主语，填 has experienced。

2. **答案：causing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作结果状语，heavy rainfall hit..., causing severe flooding...，填 causing。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。be turned into 表示"被变成"，固定搭配，填 into。

4. **答案：frequency**
   - **考点**：词性转换。the + 名词 + of，修饰名词位置，frequent → frequency。

5. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。because of 表示"因为"，后接名词短语，填 of。

6. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。invest in 表示"投资于"，固定搭配，填 in。

7. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。minutes before 表示"几分钟前"，minute → minutes。

8. **答案：survival**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 skills，survival skills 表示"生存技能"，survive → survival。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连词。what to do 表示"该做什么"，what 引导宾语从句，填 what。

10. **答案：between**
    - **考点**：介词。the difference between A and B 表示"A 与 B 之间的区别"，填 between。
