# 03-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国航天 (China's Space Programme)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's space programme has made (1) ______ (remark) progress in recent decades. From sending the first Chinese astronaut into space to building a space station, each step has filled the nation (2) ______ pride.

In 2003, Yang Liwei became the first Chinese person (3) ______ (travel) into space aboard the Shenzhou V spacecraft. His (4) ______ (succeed) mission marked China as the third country in the world to send a human into space independently.

Since then, China (5) ______ (complete) many important space missions. In 2019, the Chang'e 4 probe made the first-ever landing on the far side of the moon. Scientists (6) ______ (work) on this project for years before it finally succeeded. The Tianwen-1 mission in 2021 also landed a rover on Mars, (7) ______ was a historic achievement.

The Chinese Space Station, known (8) ______ Tiangong, began construction in 2021 and was completed in 2022. Astronauts now live and work inside it, conducting scientific experiments that cannot be done on Earth. Many young people dream of (9) ______ (become) astronauts one day.

I believe China's space programme will continue to reach new (10) ______ (high) in the years to come. The stars are no longer just something we look at — they are places we can actually go.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：remarkable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `progress`，`remark` → `remarkable` 形容词。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`fill ... with ...` 用……充满。

3. **答案：to travel**
   - **考点**：不定式作后置定语。`the first person to do sth.` 第一个做某事的人。

4. **答案：successful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `mission`，`succeed` → `successful` 形容词。

5. **答案：has completed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 从那时到现在。

6. **答案：had worked / had been working**
   - **考点**：过去完成时 / 过去完成进行时。`before it finally succeeded` 在成功之前一直在工作。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。`which` 指代前面整个事件。

8. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be known as` 被称为。

9. **答案：becoming**
   - **考点**：动名词。`dream of doing sth.` 梦想做某事。

10. **答案：heights**
    - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`reach new heights` 达到新高度，`high` → `height`，复数。
