# 03-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：DIY手工课 (DIY Craft Class)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

DIY, which stands (1) ______ "Do It Yourself", has become increasingly popular among young people in China. Many schools now offer DIY craft classes to develop students' (2) ______ (create) and hands-on skills.

Last Thursday, our school held a special DIY class. The theme was "Turning Waste (3) ______ Treasure". Each student was asked to bring some waste materials from home, such as empty bottles, old newspapers and broken toys. The task was to turn these (4) ______ (use) items into something beautiful and practical.

I brought several plastic bottles and decided to make a pencil holder. First, I cut the bottles into different (5) ______ (shape) using scissors. Then I painted them in bright colours and glued them together. It took me about two hours (6) ______ (finish) the work. Although it wasn't perfect, I was quite satisfied (7) ______ the result.

My friend Wang Lei was even more (8) ______ (impress). He made a small robot out of old circuit boards and tin cans. It could actually move its arms when you pressed a button! Everyone (9) ______ (clap) when he showed his work to the class.

Our teacher said that DIY is not just about making things. It teaches us to think (10) ______ (creative) and value the resources around us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`stand for` 代表。

2. **答案：creativity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`develop students' creativity` 培养学生的创造力，名词形式。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`turn ... into ...` 把……变成……。

4. **答案：useless**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 后缀。语境表示无用的物品，`use` → `useless`。

5. **答案：shapes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different shapes` 不同的形状。

6. **答案：to finish**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It takes sb. time to do sth.` 花费某人多少时间做某事。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be satisfied with` 对……满意。

8. **答案：impressive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`even more impressive` 更令人印象深刻的，形容词。

9. **答案：clapped**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去发生的事件，`clap` → `clapped`。

10. **答案：creatively**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `think`，用副词 `creatively`。
