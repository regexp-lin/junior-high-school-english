# 11-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园绿色食堂 (School Green Canteen)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school canteen recently became a "green canteen". The headmaster announced the plan __1__ the school assembly last month. The goal is to reduce food waste and use more __2__ (environment) friendly packaging. First, the canteen stopped __3__ (provide) disposable chopsticks and plastic cups. Instead, students now bring __4__ (they) own reusable containers. Second, the portion sizes __5__ (adjust) so that students take only what they can finish. Signs on every table remind us __6__ (not waste) food. Third, leftover food is collected and sent to a local farm __7__ it is turned into compost. Since the programme started, food waste __8__ (reduce) by 40 percent. Many students support the changes, __9__ some miss the convenience of disposable items. I think a little inconvenience is worth it __10__ the environment's sake. A green canteen is a small step, but it sends a powerful message.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。at the school assembly 在学校集会上，用 at。

2. **答案：environmentally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→副词）。修饰形容词 friendly，用副词 environmentally。

3. **答案：providing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。stop doing sth. 停止做某事，食堂停止提供一次性筷子，用动名词 providing。

4. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 containers，用 their。

5. **答案：have been adjusted**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。portion sizes 与 adjust 为被动关系，描述已完成的变化，用现在完成时被动语态 have been adjusted。

6. **答案：not to waste**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。remind sb. not to do sth. 提醒某人不要做某事，用 not to waste。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系副词。先行词 a local farm 为地点名词，从句不缺主宾语，用 where。

8. **答案：has been reduced**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。since the programme started 与现在完成时连用，food waste 与 reduce 为被动关系，用 has been reduced。

9. **答案：although / though**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导让步状语从句，"虽然有些人怀念一次性物品的便利"，用 although 或 though。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。for the environment's sake 为了环境着想，for... sake 为固定搭配。
