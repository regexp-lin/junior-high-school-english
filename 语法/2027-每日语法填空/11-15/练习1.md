# 11-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：感恩节与感恩 (Thanksgiving and Gratitude)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Thanksgiving is __1__ American holiday celebrated on the fourth Thursday of November. On this day, families gather together to enjoy a big meal and give thanks for __2__ they have. Although Thanksgiving is not a Chinese festival, the spirit of __3__ (grateful) is universal. Last November, our English teacher asked each student __4__ (write) a "Thank You" letter. I wrote mine to my mother. She works long hours as a nurse but still finds time __5__ (cook) for me every evening. I told __6__ (she) how much I appreciated her love and sacrifice. When she read the letter, tears rolled __7__ her cheeks. She hugged me __8__ (tight) and said it was the __9__ (good) gift she had ever received. Several classmates wrote to friends, teachers and even the school cleaners. One student thanked the bus driver __10__ always drove them safely to school. The activity reminded us that expressing gratitude makes both the giver and the receiver feel warm.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。American 以元音音素 /ə/ 开头，泛指"一个美国节日"，用 an。

2. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。for 后接宾语从句，表示"他们所拥有的"，从句缺宾语，用 what。

3. **答案：gratitude**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。the spirit of gratitude 感恩的精神，grateful 的名词形式为 gratitude。

4. **答案：to write**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式 to write。

5. **答案：to cook**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。find time to do sth. 找到时间做某事，用不定式 to cook。

6. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词宾格）。tell 后接宾语，用宾格 her。

7. **答案：down**
   - **考点**：副词。tears rolled down her cheeks 眼泪滚下她的脸颊，down 表示向下。

8. **答案：tightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 hugged，用副词 tightly。

9. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the best gift 最好的礼物，good 的最高级为 best。

10. **答案：who / that**
    - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 the bus driver 指人，从句缺主语，用 who 或 that。
