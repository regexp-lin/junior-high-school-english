# 04-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：劳动最光荣 (The Glory of Labour)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The saying "Labor is the most glorious" reminds us (1) ______ respect every kind of honest work. In our class meeting, students shared stories about parents (2) ______ jobs were far from easy.

Li Hua's father is a delivery man (3) ______ starts his motor early every morning. Wang Fang's mother works in a hospital and often returns home (4) ______ midnight. Listening to them, I realized (5) ______ I had taken my comfortable life for granted.

The teacher asked us (6) ______ (list) three tasks we could do to support our families. I wrote "wash dishes, fold clothes, and water the plants." She praised these ideas and said small duties could make us (7) ______ (independence).

After school, I swept the floor without (8) ______ (ask). When my mother came back, she hugged me and said my effort meant (9) ______ (much) to her than any gift. I hope I can keep (10) ______ (practice) what I have promised.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`remind sb. to do sth.` 提醒某人做某事。

2. **答案：whose**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `parents`，在从句中作定语修饰 `jobs`，表示“父母们的工作”，用 `whose`。

3. **答案：who/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。定语从句修饰 `man`，从句缺主语，指人，用 `who` 或 `that`。

4. **答案：after**
   - **考点**：介词。`after midnight` 午夜之后，表示时间。

5. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连接词。`realized` 后宾语从句，说明意识到的内容，从句结构完整，用 `that` 引导。

6. **答案：to list**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`ask sb. to do sth.` 要求某人做某事。

7. **答案：independent**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`make + 宾语 + 形容词` 使……独立，`independence` → `independent`。

8. **答案：being asked**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。介词 `without` 后接动名词；含被动含义“没有被要求就扫地”，用动名词的被动式 `being asked`。

9. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`mean more to her` 对她来说意味着更多，`much` 的比较级为 `more`。

10. **答案：practicing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`keep doing sth.` 持续做某事。
