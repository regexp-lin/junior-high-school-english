# 04-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：花卉市场 (Flower Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

On Sunday morning, my aunt drove me to a flower market on the edge of the city. Stalls lined both sides of the road, (1) ______ sellers shouted prices in cheerful voices. The air smelled (2) ______ (sweet) than usual because of roses and lilies.

We stopped at a small tent (3) ______ a young couple was packing potted plants into boxes. They explained that each plant (4) ______ (need) different amounts of sunlight and water. I chose a pot of mint (5) ______ I could use its leaves to make tea at home.

While we were paying, a sudden rain began to fall, (6) ______ everyone rushed to pull plastic sheets over the flowers. Ten minutes later, the sun came out again, and drops on the petals shone (7) ______ tiny diamonds.

I used to buy plastic decorations for my desk, (8) ______ living plants make the room feel fresher. I hope I can take care of my mint (9) ______ (patient) and watch it grow. This trip taught me (10) ______ beauty can be simple and close to daily life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。连接两个分句，“摊位沿道路两侧排开，摊主们高声叫卖”，表顺承并列。

2. **答案：sweeter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，`smell` 为系动词，后接形容词比较级，`sweet` → `sweeter`。

3. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。定语从句修饰 `tent`，从句中缺地点状语，“在帐篷里年轻夫妇打包”，用 `where`。

4. **答案：needs**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。宾语从句陈述客观养护常识，主语 `each plant` 表单数概念，谓语用第三人称单数 `needs`。

5. **答案：because/since/as**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导原因状语从句，“选择薄荷因为可在家泡茶”，表原因。

6. **答案：and/so**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句“突然下雨”与后句“大家拉塑料布”为顺承或因果，可用 `and` 或 `so`。

7. **答案：like**
   - **考点**：介词。`shine like tiny diamonds` 像小钻石一样闪耀，表比喻。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。“曾买塑料装饰”与“活植物让房间更清新”为转折。

9. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词短语 `take care of`，`patient` → `patiently` 副词。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：连接词。`taught me` 后宾语从句，说明道理，从句结构完整，用 `that` 引导。
