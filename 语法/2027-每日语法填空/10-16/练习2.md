# 10-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园植物角 (School Plant Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our class recently set up a plant corner by the window. Each student was asked __1__ (bring) at least one potted plant from home. Within a week, the corner was filled with over twenty __2__ (plant) of different shapes and sizes. Some students brought small cacti __3__ others chose flowering plants like roses. I brought a little mint plant __4__ my grandmother gave me. Taking care of the plants has become part of our daily routine. Every morning, two students are __5__ (responsibility) for watering them. We also keep __6__ record of each plant's growth in a notebook. Our science teacher said that plants can improve the air quality __7__ (great). Besides, looking at green plants helps us relax __8__ we feel stressed. The plant corner has made our classroom much __9__ (beautiful) than before. Everyone agrees __10__ it was a wonderful idea.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to bring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be asked to do sth. 被要求做某事（被动语态中的不定式），用 to bring。

2. **答案：plants**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。over twenty 后接可数名词复数，用 plants。

3. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。some... others... 结构，可用 while 表示对比，也可用 and 并列。

4. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 a little mint plant 指物，从句缺宾语，用 that 或 which。

5. **答案：responsible**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。be responsible for 对……负责，用形容词 responsible。

6. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。keep a record of 记录……，为固定搭配。

7. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 improve，用副词 greatly。

8. **答案：when / whenever**
   - **考点**：从属连词。引导时间状语从句，表示"当我们感到压力时"，用 when 或 whenever。

9. **答案：more beautiful**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than before，beautiful 为多音节词，比较级用 more beautiful。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。agree 后接宾语从句，从句意义完整不缺成分，用 that 引导。
