# 10-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我的偶像 (My Role Model)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Everyone has a role model, and mine is my father. He works __1__ a doctor at the city hospital. Every morning, he gets up earlier __2__ the rest of the family and leaves home before sunrise. He often tells me that being a doctor means __3__ (put) patients first. Last year, during a serious flood, he __4__ (send) to the disaster area to help the injured. He stayed there for two __5__ (week) without coming home. My mother and I were __6__ (worry) about him, but we knew he was doing something important. When he __7__ (final) returned, he looked tired but smiled at us. He said the __8__ (happy) thing in the world was seeing patients recover. His words touched my heart __9__ (deep). I hope that one day I can become a person __10__ helps others just like my father.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。work as 担任……职务，为固定搭配。

2. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较级标志词。earlier 为比较级，后接 than 引出比较对象。

3. **答案：putting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。mean doing sth. 意味着做某事，用动名词 putting。

4. **答案：was sent**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 he 与 send 为被动关系，由 last year 可知为一般过去时被动语态 was sent。

5. **答案：weeks**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。two 后接可数名词复数，用 weeks。

6. **答案：worried**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be worried about 对……担心，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

7. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 returned，用副词 finally。

8. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"世界上最开心的事"，用 happiest。

9. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 touched，用副词 deeply。

10. **答案：who / that**
    - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 person 指人，从句缺主语，用 who 或 that。
