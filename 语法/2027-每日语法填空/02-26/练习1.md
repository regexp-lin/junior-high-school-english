# 02-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市公园 (City Parks)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

City parks play a vital role in the lives of urban residents. They provide green spaces (1) ______ people can escape the noise and stress of city life. Guangzhou is fortunate to have many beautiful parks, and the one closest to my home is Yuexiu Park. It covers an area of over 86 hectares, (2) ______ (make) it one of the largest urban parks in China. Every morning, the park is full of people (3) ______ (do) various activities. Some elderly people practise tai chi under the trees, (4) ______ others jog along the paths or dance in groups. On weekends, families bring their children to the playground, and couples walk hand (5) ______ hand around the lake. I often go to Yuexiu Park to study. There is a quiet area near the bamboo garden that is perfect for (6) ______ (read). The fresh air and peaceful surroundings help me concentrate much (7) ______ (well) than staying in my bedroom. The park also has great (8) ______ (history) significance. The Five Rams Statue, (9) ______ (locate) at the top of Yuexiu Hill, is the most famous symbol of Guangzhou. Studies have shown that spending time in green spaces can improve both physical and mental (10) ______ (healthy). I believe every city should invest in creating more parks for its residents.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，修饰 `spaces`，表地点。

2. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：现在分词作结果状语。使它成为最大的城市公园之一。

3. **答案：doing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`people doing various activities` 做各种活动的人。

4. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。表示对比，"而/另一些人"。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`hand in hand` 手牵手。

6. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `for` 后用动名词。

7. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much better than`，`well` 的比较级是 `better`。

8. **答案：historical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`historical significance` 历史意义，形容词修饰名词。

9. **答案：located**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`located at the top` 位于山顶。

10. **答案：health**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`physical and mental health` 身心健康，名词。
