# 02-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：英语角 (English Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every Thursday evening, a group of students from our school gathers in the library for an English Corner session. The sessions (1) ______ (organize) by our English teacher, Ms. Li, who believes that practising speaking is the key (2) ______ mastering a language. Each session has a different topic, such as travel, technology, sports or culture. Students take turns (3) ______ (share) their opinions, and everyone is encouraged to speak only in English. When I first joined the English Corner six months ago, I was too nervous (4) ______ (say) anything. I was afraid that other students would laugh at my (5) ______ (pronounce). But Ms. Li created a supportive atmosphere (6) ______ no one was judged for making mistakes. She always said, "The only real mistake is not trying." Gradually, I became (7) ______ (comfortable) with speaking English. I started by asking simple questions and then moved on to (8) ______ (express) more complex ideas. Now I can hold a five-minute conversation in English without (9) ______ (switch) to Chinese. The English Corner has not only improved my speaking skills but also helped me make new friends. Some of the most (10) ______ (interest) people I know are classmates I met there. I would recommend English Corner to any student who wants to improve their spoken English.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：are organized**
   - **考点**：一般现在时的被动语态。活动被组织，描述常规活动。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`the key to doing sth.` 做某事的关键。

3. **答案：sharing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`take turns doing sth.` 轮流做某事。

4. **答案：to say**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`too nervous to say` 太紧张而说不出来。

5. **答案：pronunciation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`my pronunciation` 我的发音，名词。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，修饰 `atmosphere`，表示抽象地点。

7. **答案：more comfortable**
   - **考点**：比较级。`became more comfortable`，多音节形容词。

8. **答案：expressing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`moved on to expressing`，`to` 是介词。

9. **答案：switching**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后用动名词。

10. **答案：interesting**
    - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`the most interesting people` 最有趣的人。
