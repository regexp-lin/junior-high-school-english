# 02-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：跳蚤市场 (Flea Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school held a flea market last Saturday to encourage recycling and teach students about money management. Each class was given a space in the playground to set (1) ______ a stall. Students could sell items they no longer needed, such as old books, clothes, toys and stationery. My classmates and I decided to sell handmade bookmarks and second-hand novels. We spent two evenings (2) ______ (design) the bookmarks, using dried flowers and colourful paper. On the day of the market, the playground was transformed (3) ______ a busy shopping street. There were more than thirty stalls, and each one (4) ______ (decorate) creatively to attract customers. Some students even made posters and shouted slogans to promote their (5) ______ (product). I was in charge of (6) ______ (sell) the bookmarks. At first, I felt embarrassed about calling out to customers, but after seeing how (7) ______ (success) other stalls were, I gathered my courage and started advertising enthusiastically. Our bookmarks sold out within an hour! By the end of the day, our class had earned 800 yuan, all of (8) ______ was donated to a children's charity. The flea market was not only a fun experience (9) ______ also a meaningful lesson about the value of reusing things instead of throwing them (10) ______. I hope we can hold such events more often.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：短语动词。`set up a stall` 搭建一个摊位。

2. **答案：designing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be transformed into` 被转变成。

4. **答案：was decorated**
   - **考点**：一般过去时的被动语态。每个摊位被创意装饰。

5. **答案：products**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`their products` 他们的产品。

6. **答案：selling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`in charge of doing` 负责做某事。

7. **答案：successful**
   - **考点**：形容词。`how successful other stalls were` 其他摊位多么成功。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。`all of which` 引导非限制性定语从句。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but also...`。

10. **答案：away**
    - **考点**：短语动词。`throw them away` 把它们扔掉。
