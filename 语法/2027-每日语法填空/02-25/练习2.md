# 02-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：雨天回忆 (Rainy Day Memories)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I have always loved rainy days. There is something comforting about the sound of raindrops (1) ______ (fall) on the roof. My fondest rainy day memory takes me back to when I was seven years old. It was a Sunday afternoon, and my grandmother and I were (2) ______ (trap) at home because of a heavy downpour. Instead of feeling bored, my grandmother suggested that we bake cookies together. She opened her old recipe book and chose a recipe (3) ______ she had learned from her own mother. I remember standing on a stool so that I could reach the kitchen counter, carefully (4) ______ (measure) the flour and sugar. My grandmother was very patient with me, even when I spilled milk all (5) ______ the floor. The sweet smell of baking cookies soon filled the (6) ______ (entirely) house. When they were finally ready, we sat by the window, (7) ______ (eat) warm cookies and watching the rain together. My grandmother told me stories about her childhood — how she used to run barefoot in the rain (8) ______ catch frogs in the rice fields. Those were the (9) ______ (simple) yet happiest stories I had ever heard. Now, whenever it rains, I think of that afternoon and the special bond between my grandmother and me. Rainy days are no longer just about bad (10) ______ (weigh) — they are about warm memories and the people we love.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：falling**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`raindrops falling on the roof` 雨滴落在屋顶上。

2. **答案：trapped**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`were trapped at home` 被困在家里。

3. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，修饰 `a recipe`。

4. **答案：measuring**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。仔细地量面粉和糖。

5. **答案：over**
   - **考点**：介词。`all over the floor` 洒满了一地。

6. **答案：entire**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the entire house` 整个房子，形容词修饰名词。

7. **答案：eating**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。`eating cookies and watching the rain`。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`run barefoot and catch frogs` 两个并列动作。

9. **答案：simplest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the simplest yet happiest stories`。

10. **答案：weather**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`bad weather` 坏天气，名词。注意 `weigh`（动词称重）转为 `weather`（天气）是干扰项，此处给词 `weigh` 需转换为相关名词 `weather`。
