# 08-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校音乐会 (School Concert)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Wednesday, our school held its annual music concert in the auditorium. The concert was ___1___ (organise) by the school's art department and it lasted for about two hours. Both students and parents ___2___ (invite) to enjoy the performances.

The concert began with a piano piece ___3___ (play) by a girl from Class Five. Her fingers moved across the keys so ___4___ (smooth) that the whole audience was attracted at once. After that, the school choir sang two songs. Their voices blended together ___5___ (beautiful), filling the hall with warmth and harmony.

The most exciting moment came when a group of boys performed a drum show. They beat the drums with great energy, and the powerful rhythm made the audience clap ___6___ (they) hands along with the beat. It was ___7___ wonderful performance that everyone stood up and cheered loudly.

My best friend Wang Lei played the guitar and sang ___8___ English song. Although he made a few small ___9___ (mistake), his voice was full of emotion. After the concert, many classmates told him that his singing ___10___ (touch) their hearts deeply.

I hope our school will hold more events like this in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：organised**
   - **考点**：动词语态。主语 `The concert` 与 `organise` 之间为被动关系（音乐会"被组织"），与 `was` 构成一般过去时被动语态，故填 **organised**。

2. **答案：were invited**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 `Both students and parents` 为复数，与 `invite` 之间为被动关系（"被邀请"），一般过去时被动语态为 **were invited**。

3. **答案：played**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a piano piece` 与 `play` 之间为被动关系（曲子"被演奏"），用过去分词 **played** 作后置定语。

4. **答案：smoothly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。**so + adv. + that...** 结构中修饰动词 `moved` 需用副词，**smooth** 的副词形式为 **smoothly**。

5. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词 → 副词）。修饰动词 `blended` 需用副词，**beautiful** 的副词形式为 **beautifully**。

6. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（物主代词）。修饰名词 `hands` 需用形容词性物主代词，主语 `the audience`（观众）指代一群人，与前面 `they` 对应，故填 **their**。

7. **答案：such a**
   - **考点**：固定结构。**such a/an + adj. + 名词 + that...**（如此……以至于……），`performance` 为可数名词单数，`wonderful` 以辅音音素开头，故填 **such a**。

8. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`English` 以元音音素开头，泛指"一首英文歌"需用不定冠词 **an**。

9. **答案：mistakes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few`（几个）后接可数名词复数，`mistake` 的复数形式为 **mistakes**。

10. **答案：had touched**
    - **考点**：动词时态。`told`（过去时）之后的宾语从句中，"唱歌打动了他们的心"发生在"告诉"之前，即过去的过去，需用过去完成时 **had touched**。
