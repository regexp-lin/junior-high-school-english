# 08-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：健康饮食 (Healthy Eating)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Nowadays, many teenagers prefer to eat fast food ___1___ as hamburgers, fried chicken and pizza. These foods taste good, but eating too ___2___ of them can be harmful to our health. Doctors say that an unhealthy diet is one of the main ___3___ (reason) for obesity among young people.

A balanced diet should include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains and protein. Experts suggest that we ___4___ (eat) at least five portions of fruits and vegetables every day. These foods are rich ___5___ vitamins and minerals that our bodies need to grow and stay strong.

___6___ (drink) enough water is also very important. Many students forget to drink water during the school day and only drink sweet beverages. However, drinks with too much sugar can lead ___7___ tooth decay and weight gain. It is much ___8___ (health) to drink plain water or unsweetened tea.

My mother always prepares a lunchbox for me with rice, vegetables and a piece of fruit. At first, I thought it was ___9___ (bore) compared with fast food. But after eating homemade meals for a few months, I noticed that I felt more energetic and could concentrate ___10___ (well) in class than before.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：副词/限定词。**such as...**（例如……）用于举例说明，列举具体的快餐食物，故填 **such**。

2. **答案：much**
   - **考点**：代词。**too much**（太多）修饰不可数名词或代替不可数概念。此处 `too much of them` 表示"吃太多这类食物"，故填 **much**。

3. **答案：reasons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。**one of the main + 复数名词**（主要的……之一），`reason` 的复数形式为 **reasons**。

4. **答案：(should) eat**
   - **考点**：虚拟语气/动词原形。**suggest that sb. (should) do sth.**（建议某人做某事），`that` 从句中用 **(should) + 动词原形**，故填 **eat**。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。**be rich in...**（富含……）为固定搭配，故填 **in**。

6. **答案：Drinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词短语作句子主语，`drink` 变为 **Drinking**，句首首字母大写。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。**lead to...**（导致……）为固定搭配，故填 **to**。

8. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。**much + 比较级** 表示"……得多"，`healthy` 的比较级为 **healthier**（以辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i 加 -er）。

9. **答案：boring**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词 → 形容词）。系动词 `was` 后接形容词作表语，修饰事物（lunchbox / it）用 **-ing** 形式，故填 **boring**（令人无聊的）。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：副词比较级。**than before** 提示比较关系，修饰动词 `concentrate` 需用副词比较级，`well` 的比较级为 **better**。
