# 03-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园读书分享会 (School Book Sharing Event)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school held a book sharing event in the hall. Each class was asked to send two students to share their (1) ______ (favour) books with the whole school. I was lucky enough (2) ______ (choose) as one of the speakers from my class.

The book I shared waserta"Mo"Mo"Mo"Robinson Crusoe", which tells the story of a man who survived on (3) ______ island alone for twenty-eight years. I had read this book three times before I (4) ______ (stand) on the stage that day. When I talked about how Robinson never gave up even in the (5) ______ (difficult) situations, many students nodded their heads in agreement.

After my speech, a girl from Class 5 shared a book about Chinese history. She spoke so (6) ______ (confident) that everyone was attracted by her words. She explained (7) ______ learning history helps us understand the present and plan for the future.

The event lasted for two hours, and over ten books (8) ______ (introduce) to us. Our headmaster said this was one of the (9) ______ (good) school activities this year, and he hoped that more students would develop a habit of reading. Since then, the library (10) ______ (become) more popular than ever before.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：favourite**
   - **考点**：词性转换。形容词修饰名词 `books`，`favourite` 本身即为形容词形式。

2. **答案：to be chosen**
   - **考点**：不定式被动。`enough to do` 结构，且"我"是被选出的，需用被动 `to be chosen`。

3. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`island` 以元音音素开头，用 `an` 表泛指。

4. **答案：stood**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`before` 引导的时间状语从句，主句用过去完成时，从句用一般过去时。

5. **答案：most difficult**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the + 最高级`，在最困难的情况下。

6. **答案：confidently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `spoke`，需用副词形式。

7. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。`how` 引导宾语从句，表示"……如何帮助我们"。

8. **答案：were introduced**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。书是"被介绍给我们的"，主语 `books` 为复数。

9. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the best` 最好的……之一。

10. **答案：has become**
    - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 从那以后，表示从过去到现在，用现在完成时。
