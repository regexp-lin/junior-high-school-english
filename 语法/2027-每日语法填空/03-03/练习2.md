# 03-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：丝绸之路 (The Silk Road)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Silk Road is one of the most famous trade routes in human history. It (1) ______ (connect) China with countries in Central Asia, the Middle East and Europe over two thousand years ago. The name "Silk Road" was given by a German (2) ______ (geography) in the 19th century because silk was one of the main goods (3) ______ (carry) along this route.

In ancient times, Chinese merchants travelled (4) ______ camels through vast deserts and over high mountains. The journey was extremely (5) ______ (danger), but the rewards were great. Besides silk, tea and porcelain were also popular products that attracted foreign (6) ______ (buy).

The Silk Road was not just about trade. It served (7) ______ a bridge for cultural exchange between the East and the West. People shared knowledge of science, art and religion along the way. For example, papermaking technology (8) ______ (spread) from China to the Arab world and then to Europe through this route.

Today, China has proposed the "Belt and Road Initiative" to build (9) ______ (strong) connections with countries around the world. This modern version of the Silk Road aims to bring nations (10) ______ (close) together through trade and cooperation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：connected**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`over two thousand years ago` 表示过去的时间，用一般过去时。

2. **答案：geographer**
   - **考点**：词性转换。冠词 `a` 后需要名词，由 `geography` 变为表人名词 `geographer`。

3. **答案：carried**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`goods carried along this route` 沿这条路线运输的货物。

4. **答案：on / by**
   - **考点**：介词。`travel on/by camels` 骑骆驼旅行，介词搭配。

5. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was` 后接形容词作表语，`danger` → `dangerous`。

6. **答案：buyers**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`buy` → `buyer` 表人名词，`foreign buyers` 需用复数。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`serve as` 充当、作为。

8. **答案：spread** (过去式)
   - **考点**：一般过去时。描述历史事件，且 `spread` 的过去式与原形相同。

9. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：比较级。隐含与之前相比更强的联系，用比较级 `stronger`。

10. **答案：closer**
    - **考点**：比较级。`bring nations closer together` 使各国更加紧密，比较级作状语。
