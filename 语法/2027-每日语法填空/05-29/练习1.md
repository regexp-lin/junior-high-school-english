# 05-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：在线学习 (Online Learning)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Online learning has become ___1___ important part of modern education. During the past few years, millions of students ___2___ (get) used to studying at home through the Internet. At first, many parents worried ___3___ their children could not concentrate without a teacher nearby. However, research shows that students who manage their time ___4___ (wise) can learn just as ___5___ (good) online as in a classroom. One big advantage is ___6___ students can watch recorded lessons again if they don't understand something. Of course, there are also ___7___ (challenge). Staring at a screen for too long ___8___ (hurt) your eyes, and it is easy ___9___ (feel) lonely without classmates around. To get the best results, experts suggest ___10___ (take) short breaks every thirty minutes and joining online study groups.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。important 以元音音素开头，表示"一个重要的部分"，用不定冠词 an。

2. **答案：have got**
   - **考点**：动词时态。during the past few years 提示用现在完成时，主语 millions of students 为复数，填 have got。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。worried 后接宾语从句，用 that 引导（也可省略），填 that。

4. **答案：wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 manage，需用副词形式，wise → wisely。

5. **答案：well**
   - **考点**：副词原级。as...as 中间用原级，修饰动词 learn 用副词 well，good → well。

6. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。主语为 One big advantage，is 后接表语从句，用 that 引导。

7. **答案：challenges**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。there are 提示后接复数名词，challenge → challenges。

8. **答案：hurts**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。动名词短语 staring at a screen for too long 作主语，谓语用第三人称单数，陈述客观事实用一般现在时，填 hurts。

9. **答案：to feel**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。it is + adj. + to do sth. 句型，it 为形式主语，真正主语为不定式，填 to feel。

10. **答案：taking**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。suggest doing sth. 表示"建议做某事"，suggest 后接动名词，填 taking。
