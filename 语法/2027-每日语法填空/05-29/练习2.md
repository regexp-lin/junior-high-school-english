# 05-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：收养流浪动物 (Adopting Stray Animals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Two months ago, my family decided ___1___ (adopt) a dog from the local animal shelter. When we arrived, dozens of dogs ___2___ (bark) excitedly behind the fences. A small brown dog caught my eye — it was the ___3___ (quiet) one in the room, sitting alone in a corner. The staff told us that it ___4___ (find) on the street three weeks before and was still very shy. My mother bent down and spoke to it ___5___ (gentle). Slowly, the dog walked towards ___6___ and licked her hand. We knew at once that this was ___7___ right dog for our family. Now, two months ___8___ (late), the dog has become the ___9___ (happy) member of our home. If you want a pet, please consider adopting one ___10___ of buying from a shop. You might save a life!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to adopt**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。decide to do sth. 表示"决定做某事"，填 to adopt。

2. **答案：were barking**
   - **考点**：动词时态。when we arrived 表示到达那一刻，狗正在叫，用过去进行时 were barking。

3. **答案：quietest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"房间里最安静的那只"，quiet → quietest。

4. **答案：had been found**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。it 与 find 之间为被动关系，three weeks before 表示在过去某时间之前，用过去完成时的被动语态 had been found。

5. **答案：gently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 spoke，需用副词形式，gentle → gently。

6. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词。walked towards 后接宾格代词，根据上文 my mother 可知指代"她"，填 her。

7. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。right 表示特指"对的那只狗"，用定冠词 the。

8. **答案：later**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。two months later 表示"两个月后"，late → later。

9. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"家里最快乐的成员"，happy → happiest。

10. **答案：instead**
    - **考点**：副词。instead of 表示"而不是"，与 buying from a shop 搭配，填 instead。
