# 05-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：助残日 (Day for People with Disabilities)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's Day for People with Disabilities calls on everyone 1. ______ (show) respect and offer help. In our school, the student union set up 2. ______ workshop on sign language last month. A teacher from the local deaf community taught us 3. ______ to greet others with hand signs. At first, my fingers felt stiff, 4. ______ I practiced every evening in front of a mirror. On the open day, we guided visitors 5. ______ wheelchairs along the ramps we had checked carefully. A boy named Leo shared 6. ______ story about learning to play basketball with one hand. His courage moved many students, 7. ______ some promised to join the volunteer team. The headmaster said barriers are not always in streets; sometimes they are in 8. ______ minds. If we listen 9. ______ (patient), we will understand needs better. After the event, I started 10. ______ (notice) small things I had ignored before.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to show**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**call on sb. to do sth.** 表示“号召某人做某事”，故填 **to show**。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a workshop**，故填 **a**。

3. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接词。**how to greet** 表示“如何问候”，故填 **how**。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前后为转折关系，故填 **but**。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。**in wheelchairs** 表示“坐轮椅”，故填 **in**。

6. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a story**，故填 **a**。

7. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。连接两个分句，表顺承，故填 **and**。

8. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰 **minds**，用形容词性物主代词 **our**。

9. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **listen**，用副词 **patiently**。

10. **答案：to notice**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。**start to do sth.** 表示“开始做某事”，故填 **to notice**。
