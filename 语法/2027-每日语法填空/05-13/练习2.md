# 05-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：播客节目 (Podcasts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Podcasts have become 1. ______ popular way to learn English on the way to school. My favorite show invites guests 2. ______ talk about science in simple words. Each episode lasts about twenty minutes, 3. ______ is perfect for a short ride. The host speaks clearly 4. ______ repeats key sentences, so I can follow 5. ______ (easy). Sometimes I pause the audio 6. ______ write new phrases in my notebook. Last week, the program introduced 7. ______ inventor who builds cheap tools for farmers. I was so interested 8. ______ I searched for more videos online. My teacher suggested 9. ______ (listen) to the same episode twice. She says repetition makes language skills 10. ______ (strong) step by step.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**a popular way**，故填 **a**。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**invite sb. to do sth.**，故填 **to**。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。非限制性定语从句修饰前文整句，用 **which**。

4. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。**speaks** 与 **repeats** 并列，故填 **and**。

5. **答案：easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 **follow**，用副词 **easily**。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的“为了记下短语”，故填 **to**。

7. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。**inventor** 以元音音素开头，**an inventor**，故填 **an**。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。**so...that...** 引导结果状语从句，故填 **that**。

9. **答案：listening**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。**suggest doing sth.**，故填 **listening**。

10. **答案：stronger**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。**step by step** 暗示逐步更强，用 **strong** 的比较级 **stronger**。
