# 03-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园英语报 (School English Newspaper)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every month, students in our school look forward to a special publication — the English Corner newspaper. It is written, edited, and printed entirely (1) ______ students.

The newspaper was started in 2019 by two Grade Nine students (2) ______ wanted to share learning tips. Over time, it has grown into a colorful four-page magazine (3) ______ covers campus news, book reviews, and jokes.

I joined the team last term as a reporter. My first task was (4) ______ (interview) a foreign teacher about American holidays. I was nervous, (5) ______ I prepared questions in advance and recorded the answers carefully. The article (6) ______ (publish) on the front page, which made me proud.

The editors work hard to check grammar and spelling. They also choose photos that match each story. Sometimes (7) ______ is difficult to decide which article to put on the front page because there are so many good ones.

To encourage more readers, the club recently added English puzzles and a "Letter to the Editor" section. Teachers say reading the paper (8) ______ (help) students improve writing skills in a fun way.

If you have ideas for the next issue, send (9) ______ email to the office on the second floor. Who knows? Your story might (10) ______ (choose) for the headline news next month.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。被动含义“由学生编写、编辑、印刷”，`by students`。

2. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `students` 指人，从句缺主语，用 who。

3. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `magazine` 指物，从句缺主语。

4. **答案：to interview**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`My first task was to do ...` 表语用不定式。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。紧张但提前准备。

6. **答案：was published**
   - **考点**：一般过去时被动语态。文章被刊登在头版。

7. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：形式主语。`It is difficult to decide ...` 真正主语为不定式。

8. **答案：helps**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。动名词短语 `reading the paper` 作主语，谓语用单数。

9. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：不定冠词。`send an email`，email 以元音音素开头。

10. **答案：be chosen**
    - **考点**：含情态动词的被动语态。故事“被选中”，`might be chosen`。
