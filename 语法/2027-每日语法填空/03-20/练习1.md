# 03-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界水日 (World Water Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

March 22 is World Water Day. It reminds people that fresh water is a precious resource (1) ______ should not be wasted.

Although Earth is known as the "blue planet", only about three percent of water is fresh, and much of (2) ______ is locked in ice. In many areas, people still lack safe drinking water. Droughts and pollution have made the problem (3) ______ (bad) in recent years.

Our school organized a week-long campaign last year. Students (4) ______ (design) posters and gave short speeches during morning meetings. We also measured how much water we used (5) ______ brushing teeth and taking showers. The results shocked us — small habits can save liters every day.

Scientists are working on new ways to reuse water in factories and cities. Farmers are learning to use drip irrigation (6) ______ (reduce) waste. Governments are building more pipes to send clean water to villages.

However, technology alone cannot solve everything. Each of us can turn off taps (7) ______ they are not needed and fix leaks at home. Saving water is not just for one day; it is a duty we owe to future generations.

I have promised myself (8) ______ (be) more careful from now on. If everyone takes action, we can make sure that rivers and lakes (9) ______ (remain) healthy for years to come.

Let's remember: every drop counts, and no effort is too small (10) ______ it comes to protecting our water.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 `resource` 指物，从句缺主语，用 that 或 which。

2. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。指代 `fresh water`，`much of it` 其中大部分。

3. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`make the problem worse` 使问题更严重，与隐含的“以前”对比。

4. **答案：designed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与 `gave` 并列，描述去年活动。

5. **答案：while / when**
   - **考点**：连词。刷牙、洗澡时用水量，用 while 或 when 引导时间状语。

6. **答案：to reduce**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式表目的，用滴灌以减少浪费。

7. **答案：when / if**
   - **考点**：连词。`when/if they are not needed` 当不需要时/若不需要时关掉水龙头。

8. **答案：to be**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`promise oneself to do sth.` 向自己承诺做某事。

9. **答案：will remain**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。宾语从句表示“在未来多年里保持”，用 will remain 与 `for years to come` 呼应。

10. **答案：when**
    - **考点**：固定句型。`when it comes to ...` 当谈到……时。
