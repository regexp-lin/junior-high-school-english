# 12-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬天里的流浪猫救助 (Rescuing Stray Cats in Winter)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When winter comes, life becomes very hard __1__ stray cats. They have to find food and shelter in the freezing cold. My classmate Liu Jia is a big animal lover. She has been __2__ (rescue) stray cats in our neighbourhood since last year. So far, she __3__ (save) more than twenty cats. She feeds them every morning before school and has built several small __4__ (shelter) for them using old boxes and blankets. Last month, she found a thin kitten __5__ (shake) in the rain. She brought it home, dried it __6__ (careful) and took it to the vet. The kitten was very weak but slowly recovered __7__ Liu Jia's care. She named it Duoduo. Now Duoduo is one of the __8__ (healthy) cats in her home. Liu Jia's story has inspired many students in our school to care __9__ stray animals. She says, "Every life __10__ (deserve) to be treated with kindness."

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。life becomes hard for 对……来说生活变得困难，for 表示"对于"。

2. **答案：rescuing**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成进行时）。has been rescuing 一直在救助，since last year 表示持续到现在的动作。

3. **答案：has saved**
   - **考点**：动词时态。So far 到目前为止，与现在完成时连用，用 has saved。

4. **答案：shelters**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。several shelters 几个遮蔽所，several 后接复数名词。

5. **答案：shaking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作宾补）。found a kitten shaking 发现一只小猫在发抖，kitten 与 shake 为主动关系，用现在分词。

6. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 dried，用副词 carefully。

7. **答案：under**
   - **考点**：介词。under Liu Jia's care 在刘佳的照顾下，under 表示"在……之下"。

8. **答案：healthiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the healthiest 最健康的……之一，one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词。

9. **答案：about/for**
   - **考点**：介词。care about/for 关心、照顾，为固定搭配。

10. **答案：deserves**
    - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。Every life deserves 每一条生命都值得，every + 单数名词作主语，谓语用第三人称单数。
