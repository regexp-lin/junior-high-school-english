# 12-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国传统节气 (Chinese Traditional Solar Terms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The 24 Solar Terms are an ancient Chinese system __1__ (use) to describe the changes of seasons and weather. They __2__ (create) by Chinese farmers thousands of years ago to guide agricultural activities. In 2016, this system was __3__ (official) added to UNESCO's Intangible Cultural Heritage list. Each solar term has __4__ (it) own name, such as the Beginning of Spring, Grain Rain and Winter Solstice. My grandfather, who is a farmer, still follows the solar terms __5__ (close). He knows exactly when to plant seeds and when __6__ (harvest) crops based on these ancient guidelines. He often says, "The solar terms are the __7__ (wise) gift from our ancestors." In our school, we learned about the science __8__ the solar terms. They are based on the position of the sun and __9__ (actual) reflect real changes in temperature and rainfall. I think it is amazing __10__ a system created so long ago is still useful today.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作定语）。a system used to describe 一个用来描述的系统，system 与 use 为被动关系，用过去分词。

2. **答案：were created**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。thousands of years ago 数千年前，they 与 create 为被动关系，用一般过去时被动 were created。

3. **答案：officially**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 added，用副词 officially。

4. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。its own name 它自己的名字，修饰名词用形容词性物主代词 its。

5. **答案：closely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 follows，用副词 closely 表示"密切地"。

6. **答案：to harvest**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。when to harvest 什么时候收获，"疑问词 + to do"结构。

7. **答案：wisest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the wisest gift 最智慧的礼物，the 提示用最高级。

8. **答案：behind**
   - **考点**：介词。the science behind 背后的科学，behind 表示"在……背后"。

9. **答案：actually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 reflect，用副词 actually 表示"实际上"。

10. **答案：that**
    - **考点**：连词（主语从句）。It is amazing that... ……是令人惊叹的，it 为形式主语，that 引导主语从句。
