# 10-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：秋天的果园 (Autumn Orchard)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every autumn, my grandparents invite us to __1__ (they) orchard in the countryside. The orchard is full __2__ apple trees, pear trees and orange trees. When we arrived last Saturday, the branches were heavy with ripe __3__ (fruit). Grandpa taught me how __4__ (pick) apples without damaging the branches. I __5__ (careful) climbed a short ladder and reached for the biggest apple I could find. Grandma prepared a delicious lunch __6__ fresh vegetables from her garden. After lunch, we sat under a tree and enjoyed the __7__ (peace) afternoon. My little sister ran around __8__ (happy), collecting fallen leaves of different colours. It was much __9__ (cool) in the orchard than in the city. I wish I __10__ live there every day!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 orchard，用形容词性物主代词 their。

2. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。be full of 充满……，为固定搭配。

3. **答案：fruits / fruit**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。fruit 作"水果"统称时不可数，作"各种水果"时可用复数 fruits，此处均可接受。

4. **答案：to pick**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。teach sb. how to do sth. 教某人如何做某事，用不定式 to pick。

5. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 climbed，用副词 carefully。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with 表示"用，带有"，此处指午餐搭配新鲜蔬菜。

7. **答案：peaceful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 afternoon，用形容词 peaceful。

8. **答案：happily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 ran around，用副词 happily。

9. **答案：cooler**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 than in the city，用比较级 cooler。

10. **答案：could**
    - **考点**：虚拟语气。I wish + 过去时态，表示对现在的虚拟愿望，live 前用 could 表示"希望能够"。
