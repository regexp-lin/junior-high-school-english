# 10-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：参观消防站 (Visiting a Fire Station)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our class visited the local fire station. A tall firefighter __1__ (name) Captain Chen welcomed us at the gate. He showed us around and explained how different __2__ (tool) worked. We were amazed __3__ the huge red fire engine. Captain Chen told us that his team __4__ (receive) over 200 emergency calls last year. He said the most important thing was to arrive at the scene as __5__ (quick) as possible. Then he taught us some basic skills __6__ escaping from a fire. For example, we should cover our __7__ (nose) with wet towels and stay low. One student asked __8__ they felt scared during a rescue. Captain Chen smiled and replied, "Of course we do, __9__ saving lives always comes first." Before we left, he reminded us __10__ (check) fire equipment at home regularly.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。firefighter 与 name 为被动关系，用过去分词 named 表示"名叫……的"。

2. **答案：tools**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。different 暗示多种工具，用复数 tools。

3. **答案：at / by**
   - **考点**：介词。be amazed at/by 对……感到惊讶，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：had received**
   - **考点**：动词时态。told 为一般过去时，last year 表示"去年"，在过去时间点的更早之前收到电话，用过去完成时 had received；亦可理解为直接引述过去事实用一般过去时 received，两者均可接受。

5. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。as... as possible 中间填副词修饰动词 arrive，用 quickly。

6. **答案：for / of**
   - **考点**：介词。skills for/of doing sth. 做某事的技巧，用 for 或 of。

7. **答案：noses**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。our 对应复数主语 we，每人一个鼻子，用复数 noses。

8. **答案：if / whether**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。asked 后接一般疑问意义的宾语从句，用 if 或 whether 引导，意为"是否"。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句承认害怕，后句说救人优先，语义转折，用 but。

10. **答案：to check**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。remind sb. to do sth. 提醒某人做某事，用不定式 to check。
