# 10-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市热岛效应 (Urban Heat Island Effect)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

On hot summer afternoons, downtown streets feel even hotter __1__ the suburbs. Scientists call this the urban heat island effect. Tall buildings trap heat, while concrete roads __2__ (absorb) more sunlight than grass. Cars and air conditioners also push temperatures __3__ (high). Last July, our science group __4__ (measure) temperatures in the park and on a busy road. The difference was about four degrees, __5__ surprised many passers-by. To cool cities, planners suggest planting more trees and __6__ (paint) roofs light colors. Some communities __7__ (begin) green roof projects already. If each family saves energy, the city __8__ (become) a little milder. Young citizens should learn __9__ each bus trip helps reduce heat __10__ well as pollution.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：介词。比较级 hotter 后接比较对象，用 than 引出 the suburbs。

2. **答案：absorb**
   - **考点**：动词时态。while 连接并列分句，说明客观情况，用一般现在时；主语 roads 为复数，谓语用动词原形 absorb。

3. **答案：higher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。push temperatures higher 把温度推得更高，隐含与之前对比，用 high 的比较级 higher。

4. **答案：measured**
   - **考点**：动词时态。Last July 表示过去，谓语用一般过去时 measured。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。指代前面“温差约四度”这件事，在非限制性定语从句中作主语，用 which。

6. **答案：painting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。suggest doing sth. 建议做某事；and 连接与 planting 并列的动名词，用 painting。

7. **答案：have begun**
   - **考点**：动词时态。时间标志 already 常与现在完成时连用，表示“已经开始”，用 have begun。

8. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：动词时态。If 引导条件状语从句用一般现在时表将来，主句用一般将来时 will become。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。learn 后接宾语从句，说明学到的内容，从句结构完整，用 that 引导。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。as well as 除……之外还；既……又……，连接 heat 与 pollution。
