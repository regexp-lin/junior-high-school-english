# 11-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：我的读书笔记 (My Reading Notes)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Keeping reading notes has been one of the __1__ (good) habits I have ever developed. My Chinese teacher encouraged us __2__ (start) a reading notebook at the beginning of this year. Every time I finish a book, I write down the title, author and a short __3__ (summarise). I also record my favourite sentences and explain __4__ they moved me. At first, I found it __5__ (bore) to write notes after reading. I just wanted to move on __6__ the next book. But gradually, I realised that taking notes helped me __7__ (understand) books more deeply. Now I have filled three notebooks. Looking back at my __8__ (early) notes, I can see how much my thinking has grown. Last month, our teacher asked us __9__ (share) our reading notes with the class. Many students were impressed __10__ the interesting ideas I had recorded. My teacher said, "A good reader is not just someone who reads a lot, but someone who thinks a lot." I could not agree more.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the best habits 最好的习惯之一，good 的最高级为 best。

2. **答案：to start**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。encourage sb. to do sth. 鼓励某人做某事，用不定式 to start。

3. **答案：summary**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a short summary 简短的摘要，summarise 的名词形式为 summary。

4. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。explain 后接宾语从句，表示"为什么让我感动"，用 why。

5. **答案：boring**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。found it boring 觉得无聊，-ing 形式描述事物的特征。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。move on to 继续到……，to 表示方向。

7. **答案：understand / to understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help sb. (to) do sth. 帮助某人做某事，to 可省略。

8. **答案：earliest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。my earliest notes 我最早的笔记，early 的最高级为 earliest。

9. **答案：to share**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事，用不定式 to share。

10. **答案：by / with**
    - **考点**：介词。be impressed by/with 对……印象深刻，为固定搭配。
