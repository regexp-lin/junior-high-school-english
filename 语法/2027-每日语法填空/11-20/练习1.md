# 11-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬日暖饮 (Winter Warm Drinks)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When winter comes, nothing warms you up quite __1__ a hot drink. In China, people have enjoyed warm beverages __2__ thousands of years. Ginger tea is one of the __3__ (popular) winter drinks. My grandmother makes it __4__ fresh ginger, brown sugar and red dates. She says it helps fight __5__ (cold) and improves blood circulation. Another favourite is hot milk tea. The tradition of __6__ (add) milk to tea came from the grasslands of Inner Mongolia. Nowadays, milk tea shops can __7__ (find) on almost every street corner. My friends and I love trying new __8__ (flavour). Last week, I tried a taro milk tea that tasted __9__ (amazing) good. However, our health teacher reminds us that many milk teas contain too much sugar, __10__ is bad for our teeth and health. She suggests choosing less sugar or making drinks at home. I think a cup of homemade ginger tea is healthier and just as delicious.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：like**
   - **考点**：介词。nothing... quite like... 没有什么能像……一样，like 表示"像"。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。for thousands of years 数千年来，for + 时间段。

3. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，popular 为多音节词，最高级用 most popular。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。makes it with 用……来制作，with 表示材料。

5. **答案：colds**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。fight colds 抗感冒，cold 作"感冒"为可数名词，用复数 colds。

6. **答案：adding**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。the tradition of + 动名词，of 为介词，后接 adding。

7. **答案：be found**
   - **考点**：动词语态。can 后接动词原形，shops 与 find 为被动关系，用 be found。

8. **答案：flavours**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。new flavours 新口味，用复数 flavours。

9. **答案：amazingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 good，用副词 amazingly。

10. **答案：which**
    - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词为前面整个事实（含糖太多），非限制性定语从句用 which。
