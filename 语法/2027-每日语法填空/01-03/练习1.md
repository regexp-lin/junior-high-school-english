# 01-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：太空探索 (Space Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Space exploration has always fascinated human beings. Since the first satellite (1) ______ (send) into orbit in 1957, scientists have made remarkable progress in (2) ______ (understand) the universe. China's space programme has achieved great (3) ______ (succeed) in recent years. In 2003, Yang Liwei became the first Chinese astronaut (4) ______ (travel) into space, making China the third country in the world to send a person into orbit (5) ______ (independent). Since then, China has launched several space stations and even landed a rover on Mars. Many students dream (6) ______ becoming astronauts one day. However, the training required is extremely tough. Astronauts must be in excellent (7) ______ (physically) condition, and they need to study subjects such as physics, engineering and medicine. They also have to learn how to work (8) ______ a team, because cooperation is essential during space missions. Although space travel is still dangerous and (9) ______ (cost), scientists believe that ordinary people will be able to visit space in the future. Some companies are already (10) ______ (develop) spacecraft designed for space tourism. Perhaps one day, a trip to the Moon will be as common as flying to another country!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was sent**
   - **考点**：被动语态。卫星"被发送"到轨道，`since` 引出过去事件，用一般过去时被动。

2. **答案：understanding**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `in` 后接动名词形式。

3. **答案：success**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`great success`，形容词后接名词形式。

4. **答案：to travel**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the first...to do sth`，序数词后用不定式作定语。

5. **答案：independently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `send` 需要副词形式。

6. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`dream of doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"梦想做某事"。

7. **答案：physical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `condition` 需要形容词形式。

8. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`work as a team` 表示"作为一个团队工作"。

9. **答案：costly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `dangerous` 并列，需要形容词形式。

10. **答案：developing**
    - **考点**：现在进行时。`are already developing` 表示正在进行的动作。
