# 01-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国茶文化 (Chinese Tea Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tea is one of the most (1) ______ (wide) consumed beverages in the world, and China is proud to be its birthplace. According to legend, tea was (2) ______ (discover) by Emperor Shen Nong nearly 5,000 years ago when some tea leaves accidentally fell into his pot of boiling water. He found the taste pleasant, and tea (3) ______ (drink) has been a part of Chinese culture ever since. There are many different (4) ______ (type) of Chinese tea, including green tea, black tea, oolong tea and white tea. Each type has (5) ______ own unique flavour and health benefits. Green tea, for example, is rich in antioxidants and is believed to help people stay (6) ______ (health). In China, serving tea to guests is considered a sign (7) ______ respect and hospitality. The traditional Chinese tea ceremony is a beautiful art form that requires patience and skill. During the ceremony, the tea master carefully (8) ______ (select) the finest tea leaves and brews them at just the right temperature. The purpose is not only (9) ______ (enjoy) the taste of the tea, but also to create a peaceful atmosphere where people can relax and connect with one another. Today, tea houses can (10) ______ (find) in almost every city in China, bringing people together over a shared love of this ancient drink.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `consumed` 需要副词形式。

2. **答案：discovered**
   - **考点**：被动语态。茶"被发现"，用一般过去时被动。

3. **答案：drinking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`tea drinking` 作主语，动名词构成复合名词。

4. **答案：types**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many different types`，`many` 修饰可数名词复数。

5. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：物主代词。`its own unique flavour`，`its` 指代 `each type`。

6. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`stay healthy`，系动词 `stay` 后接形容词。

7. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`a sign of respect` 为固定搭配，表示"尊重的标志"。

8. **答案：selects**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。描述茶道中的惯常动作，主语 `the tea master` 为第三人称单数。

9. **答案：to enjoy**
   - **考点**：不定式。`The purpose is not only to enjoy...but also to create...`，不定式作表语。

10. **答案：be found**
    - **考点**：被动语态。`can be found` 表示"能被找到"，情态动词后接动词原形被动。
