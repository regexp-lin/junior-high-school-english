# 02-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：机器人竞赛 (Robot Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, our school's robotics team took part in the Provincial Youth Robot Competition. Our team (1) ______ (consist) of five members, each with a different role: two programmers, two builders and one strategist. We had been preparing for the competition (2) ______ six months. The challenge was to build a robot that could (3) ______ (navigate) through a maze, pick up objects and deliver them to a specific location. Our robot, (4) ______ we named "Lightning," was equipped with sensors and a mechanical arm. On the day of the competition, we were the (5) ______ (young) team among all the participants. Some teams came from high schools and even had professional (6) ______ (guide). Despite being at a disadvantage, we stayed calm and focused. During the first round, Lightning performed (7) ______ (perfect) and completed the task in record time. The crowd cheered loudly, and I could see the judges nodding (8) ______ approval. In the final round, however, one of the sensors malfunctioned, and our robot got stuck (9) ______ a corner. We had only sixty seconds to fix it. Working together under pressure, we managed to restart the sensor and Lightning finished the course just in time. We ended up (10) ______ (win) third place — an amazing result for our first competition.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：consisted**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`consisted of` 由……组成，叙述过去。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for six months` 表示持续的时间段。

3. **答案：navigate**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`could navigate` 情态动词后用原形。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `robot`。

5. **答案：youngest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the youngest team among all`。

6. **答案：guidance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`professional guidance` 专业指导，名词。

7. **答案：perfectly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`performed perfectly` 表现完美。

8. **答案：in / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`nodding in/with approval` 赞许地点头。

9. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`got stuck in a corner` 卡在角落里。

10. **答案：winning**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`end up doing sth.` 最终做了某事。
