# 02-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：阅读的力量 (The Power of Reading)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Reading has always been an important part of my life. My parents started reading bedtime stories to me when I was just two years old, and by the age of five I could read simple picture books (1) ______ my own. Now, as a teenager, I read at (2) ______ (little) two books every month. My favourite genre is science fiction because it takes me to worlds that are completely (3) ______ (differ) from our own. One book that had a particularly deep impact (4) ______ me was "A Brief History of Time" by Stephen Hawking. Although much of the science was beyond my (5) ______ (understand), the way Hawking explained complex ideas in simple language was truly inspiring. After (6) ______ (read) it, I became fascinated with physics and started spending more time on my science homework. Research has shown that people who read regularly tend to have (7) ______ (good) vocabulary, stronger critical thinking skills and greater empathy than those who do not. Reading also (8) ______ (help) reduce stress — studies show that just six minutes of reading can lower stress levels by up to 68%. Unfortunately, many young people today prefer watching short videos (9) ______ reading books. I think this is a loss because videos cannot provide the same depth of thought that books offer. I hope more of my classmates (10) ______ (discover) the joy of reading and make it a lifelong habit.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`on my own` 独自地。

2. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`at least` 至少。

3. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`completely different from` 完全不同于。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`have an impact on` 对……有影响。

5. **答案：understanding**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`beyond my understanding` 超出我的理解范围，名词。

6. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `After` 后用动名词。

7. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`better vocabulary... than those who do not`。

8. **答案：helps**
   - **考点**：一般现在时三单。`Reading also helps` 阅读也有助于。

9. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`prefer doing A to doing B` 或 `prefer A to B`。

10. **答案：will discover**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。`I hope + 从句` 表达将来的愿望。
