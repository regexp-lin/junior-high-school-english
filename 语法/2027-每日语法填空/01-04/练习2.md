# 01-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：我的宠物狗 (My Pet Dog)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Having a pet can bring great (1) ______ (happy) to a family. Two years ago, my parents surprised me with a golden retriever puppy on my birthday. I (2) ______ (name) him Doudou, and he quickly became the most important member of our family. Doudou is an (3) ______ (extreme) friendly dog who loves everyone he meets. Whenever I come home from school, he runs to the door and wags his tail (4) ______ (excited). Taking care of a pet, however, is not as easy (5) ______ it seems. I have to walk Doudou twice a day, no matter how tired I am. I am also responsible (6) ______ feeding him and keeping him clean. At first, I found these tasks rather (7) ______ (tire), but now they have become a natural part of my daily routine. Doudou has taught me many valuable (8) ______ (lesson) about life. He has shown me what unconditional love looks like — he never judges me or gets angry with me, even when I make (9) ______ (mistake). When I feel sad or stressed, he always comes and sits beside me quietly, as if he (10) ______ (understand) exactly how I feel. I cannot imagine my life without him.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：happiness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`great happiness`，形容词 `great` 后接名词形式。

2. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的事件，`Two years ago` 为时间标志。

3. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `friendly` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：excitedly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `wags` 需要副词形式。

5. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`not as easy as it seems`，同级比较的否定形式。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be responsible for` 为固定搭配，表示"对……负责"。

7. **答案：tiring**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式，表示"令人疲倦的"。

8. **答案：lessons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many valuable lessons`，`many` 修饰可数名词复数。

9. **答案：mistakes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`make mistakes` 为固定搭配，表示"犯错误"。

10. **答案：understood**
    - **考点**：虚拟语气。`as if he understood`，与现在事实相反的虚拟语气，用一般过去时。
