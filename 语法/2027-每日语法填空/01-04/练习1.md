# 01-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：志愿者服务 (Volunteer Work)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Volunteering is a wonderful way to give back to the community and make a positive difference in the world. Last summer, I joined a volunteer programme that (1) ______ (organise) by our local youth centre. Our task was to help clean up a river that runs (2) ______ the centre of our town. When we first arrived, we were shocked to see how much rubbish (3) ______ (throw) into the water. Plastic bags, bottles and even old tyres (4) ______ (lie) along the riverbank. We spent three full days (5) ______ (remove) the waste, and by the end, the river looked completely different. One of the most (6) ______ (reward) aspects of volunteering is the chance to meet people from different backgrounds. During the clean-up, I became friends (7) ______ a girl named Chen Lu, who was two years (8) ______ (old) than me. She told me that she had been volunteering since she was ten and that it had taught (9) ______ (she) the importance of caring for others. Her words inspired me deeply. Since that experience, I have continued to volunteer on weekends. Whether it is visiting elderly people in nursing homes (10) ______ teaching young children to read, every act of kindness matters. I truly believe that the more we give, the more we receive in return.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was organised**
   - **考点**：被动语态。项目"被组织"，`last summer` 表过去时。

2. **答案：through**
   - **考点**：介词。`runs through the centre`，河流"穿过"城镇中心。

3. **答案：had been thrown**
   - **考点**：过去完成时被动。到达之前垃圾已经"被扔"进水里，用过去完成时被动。

4. **答案：were lying**
   - **考点**：过去进行时。描述到达时看到的场景，垃圾正"躺"在河岸边。

5. **答案：removing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 为固定搭配。

6. **答案：rewarding**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式的形容词，表示"令人有回报的"。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`became friends with sb` 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：older**
   - **考点**：比较级。`two years older than me`，`than` 表示比较。

9. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词。`had taught her`，动词后接宾格代词。

10. **答案：or**
    - **考点**：连词。`Whether...or...` 结构，表示"无论是……还是……"。
