# 02-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园广播站 (School Radio Station)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school radio station broadcasts during lunch break every day, and it has become an important part of school life. I joined the radio station as a host at the (1) ______ (begin) of this semester. My job is to read the daily news, introduce (2) ______ (interest) stories and play music requests from students. Before each broadcast, I spend about an hour (3) ______ (prepare) the script. I need to make sure that the content is both informative and (4) ______ (entertain). The most challenging part of the job is speaking (5) ______ (clear) and naturally into the microphone. When I first started, I was so nervous that my voice kept (6) ______ (shake). My supervisor, a senior student named Zhou Lin, taught me (7) ______ to control my breathing and pace. After weeks of practice, I became much (8) ______ (confident) and even started adding my own commentary between songs. The response from students has been very (9) ______ (encourage). Many people have told me that they look forward to (10) ______ (listen) to my segment every day. Running the school radio station has improved my public speaking skills and taught me the importance of preparation and practice.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：beginning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`at the beginning of` 在……开始时，名词形式。

2. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`interesting stories` 有趣的故事，修饰物。

3. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`spend time doing sth.` 花时间做某事。

4. **答案：entertaining**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`informative and entertaining` 既有信息量又有趣味性。

5. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`speaking clearly` 清楚地说。

6. **答案：shaking**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`keep doing sth.` 一直做某事。

7. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：疑问副词。`taught me how to control` 教我如何控制。

8. **答案：more confident**
   - **考点**：比较级。`became much more confident`，多音节形容词用 `more`。

9. **答案：encouraging**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`very encouraging` 令人鼓舞的，描述事物。

10. **答案：listening**
    - **考点**：动名词。`look forward to doing sth.` 期待做某事，`to` 是介词。
