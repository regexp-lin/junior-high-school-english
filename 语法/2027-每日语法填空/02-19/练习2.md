# 02-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统节日美食 (Traditional Festival Foods)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Food plays a central role in Chinese festivals, and each festival has its own special dishes that carry deep (1) ______ (symbol) meaning. During the Spring Festival, families gather to make dumplings together. The shape of dumplings (2) ______ (resemble) ancient gold ingots, so they represent wealth and good fortune. Zongzi, sticky rice wrapped in bamboo leaves, is the traditional food (3) ______ (eat) during the Dragon Boat Festival. It is said that people first made zongzi to honour the great poet Qu Yuan, (4) ______ drowned himself in a river over 2,000 years ago. The Mid-Autumn Festival would not be complete (5) ______ mooncakes. These round pastries, often filled with lotus seed paste or egg yolk, symbolize reunion and (6) ______ (complete). My grandmother is famous in our family (7) ______ her homemade tangyuan, which she prepares for every Lantern Festival. She (8) ______ (make) them by hand since she was a young girl, and her recipe has been passed down from her own grandmother. I have tried to learn her technique, but my tangyuan never turn out as (9) ______ (perfectly) as hers. She always laughs and tells me that cooking is like any other skill — it takes years of practice (10) ______ (master).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：symbolic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`symbolic meaning` 象征意义，形容词修饰名词。

2. **答案：resembles**
   - **考点**：一般现在时三单。描述饺子的形状特点，客观事实。

3. **答案：eaten**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the food eaten during` 在……期间吃的食物。

4. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，修饰 `Qu Yuan`，指人。

5. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`would not be complete without` 没有……就不完整。

6. **答案：completeness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `reunion` 并列，用名词形式。

7. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be famous for` 因……而出名。

8. **答案：has been making / has made**
   - **考点**：现在完成进行时或现在完成时。`since she was a young girl` 从小到现在。

9. **答案：perfect**
   - **考点**：同级比较。`as perfect as`，`as... as` 中间用原级形容词。

10. **答案：to master**
    - **考点**：固定句型。`It takes time to do sth.` 做某事需要时间。
