# 06-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：科学实验课 (Science Experiment Class)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Science class is always the most popular lesson ___1___ our school. Last Thursday, Mr. Wang asked us to do an experiment about plants and light. We ___2___ (give) two groups of bean seeds. One group was placed near the window where it could receive plenty ___3___ sunlight, while the other was kept in a dark cupboard. Over the following week, we ___4___ (careful) recorded the changes every day. The seeds near the window grew much ___5___ (tall) and turned green quickly. However, the ___6___ (one) in the cupboard became thin and yellow. Mr. Wang explained that plants need light ___7___ (produce) food through a process ___8___ (call) photosynthesis. This experiment helped us ___9___ (understand) why forests are so important to the Earth. It was one of the ___10___ (good) lessons I have ever had!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in our school 表示"在我们学校里"，填 in。

2. **答案：were given**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。we 与 give 之间为被动关系，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were given。

3. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。plenty of 表示"大量的"，固定搭配，填 of。

4. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 recorded，需用副词形式，careful → carefully。

5. **答案：taller**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much + 比较级表示"高得多"，tall → taller。

6. **答案：ones**
   - **考点**：代词。the ones 指代前文的 seeds，用复数形式替代以避免重复，one → ones。

7. **答案：to produce**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"需要光来产生食物"，填 to produce。

8. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。过去分词作后置定语，a process called... 表示"一个叫做……的过程"，填 called。

9. **答案：understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。help sb. (to) do sth. 表示"帮助某人做某事"，help 后接省略 to 的不定式，填 understand。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词，good → best。
