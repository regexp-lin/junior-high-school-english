# 06-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：夏令营 (Summer Camp)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every summer, thousands of students across China go to different ___1___ (kind) of camps. Last July, I attended a seven-day English summer camp in the mountains. When the bus ___2___ (arrive) at the campsite, I was amazed ___3___ the beautiful scenery. Tall trees stood on ___4___ (they) side of the road, and a clear stream ran through the valley. During the camp, we were not allowed ___5___ (use) mobile phones. At first, I felt uncomfortable ___6___ it, but soon I started to enjoy the change. We played team games, learned to cook outdoors, and even ___7___ (sleep) in tents under the stars. The camp leaders taught us how ___8___ (work) together and trust each other. On the last night, everyone sat around the campfire, ___9___ (share) their favourite moments. That week ___10___ (complete) changed my view of summer holidays.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：kinds**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。different + 复数名词，different kinds of 表示"不同种类的"，kind → kinds。

2. **答案：arrived**
   - **考点**：动词时态。when 引导的时间状语从句，全文叙述过去事件，arrive → arrived。

3. **答案：by/at**
   - **考点**：介词。be amazed by/at 表示"对……感到惊叹"，填 by 或 at 均可。

4. **答案：each/either**
   - **考点**：代词。each/either side of the road 表示"路的两边"，指道路两侧都有树，填 each 或 either。

5. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。be allowed to do sth. 表示"被允许做某事"，allow sb. to do sth. 的被动形式，填 to use。

6. **答案：about/with**
   - **考点**：介词。feel uncomfortable about/with sth. 表示"对某事感到不适"，填 about 或 with。

7. **答案：slept**
   - **考点**：动词时态。与前面 played、learned 并列，用一般过去时，sleep → slept。

8. **答案：to work**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。how to do sth. 表示"如何做某事"，teach sb. how to do sth.，填 to work。

9. **答案：sharing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。现在分词作伴随状语，sat around the campfire, sharing...，填 sharing。

10. **答案：completely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 changed，需用副词形式，complete → completely。
