# 02-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：风筝节 (Kite Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Weifang, a city in Shandong Province, is known (1) ______ the "World Capital of Kites." Every April, the International Kite Festival (2) ______ (hold) there, attracting kite enthusiasts from over 70 countries. The festival (3) ______ (run) for more than forty years since it first started in 1984. Last spring, my grandfather took me to Weifang to experience the festival for (4) ______ (I). The sight of thousands of kites filling the sky was absolutely (5) ______ (stun). There were kites in every shape imaginable — dragons, butterflies, eagles and even a huge kite shaped like a traditional Chinese (6) ______ (paint). The most impressive kite was a centipede that stretched over 100 metres long. It took a team of ten people (7) ______ (launch) it into the air. My grandfather, (8) ______ has been making kites since he was a teenager, brought along a handmade swallow kite. He taught me how to read the wind direction and find the (9) ______ (good) angle for takeoff. After several failed attempts, I finally got the kite flying high above the field. There is something deeply (10) ______ (satisfy) about watching your own kite dance in the sky. I now understand why my grandfather has loved this hobby for so many years.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be known as` 被称为/以……著称。

2. **答案：is held**
   - **考点**：一般现在时的被动语态。每年定期举办。

3. **答案：has run / has been running**
   - **考点**：现在完成时/现在完成进行时。`since 1984` 从1984年至今。

4. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`for myself` 亲自体验。

5. **答案：stunning**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`absolutely stunning` 绝对令人惊叹的。

6. **答案：painting**
   - **考点**：名词。`a traditional Chinese painting` 一幅中国画。

7. **答案：to launch**
   - **考点**：固定句型。`It takes sb. to do sth.` 做某事需要某人。

8. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，修饰 `grandfather`。

9. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the best angle` 最佳角度。

10. **答案：satisfying**
    - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`deeply satisfying` 深深令人满足的，修饰事物。
