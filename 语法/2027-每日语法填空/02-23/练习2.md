# 02-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学习压力 (Study Pressure)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many students in China face enormous pressure as they prepare for the zhongkao. A recent survey showed that over 70% of Grade 9 students feel stressed about their (1) ______ (academy) performance. This pressure comes from multiple sources: parents' expectations, peer competition (2) ______ the fear of not getting into a good high school. While a certain amount of pressure can motivate students to work (3) ______ (hard), too much of it can be harmful. Some students suffer from anxiety, sleep (4) ______ (disturb) and even depression. My friend Wang Ming used to stay up past midnight every night (5) ______ (study), believing that more hours meant better results. However, his grades actually dropped because he was too (6) ______ (exhaust) to concentrate during class. A school counsellor helped him develop a (7) ______ (health) study routine. She advised him to study for 45 minutes and then take a 10-minute break. She also recommended regular exercise as a way to reduce stress. After (8) ______ (follow) her advice for a month, Wang Ming's grades improved (9) ______ (significant), and he felt much happier. His experience taught me that taking care of your mental health is just (10) ______ important as studying hard. Balance is the key to success.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：academic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`academic performance` 学业表现，形容词修饰名词。

2. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。三个并列的压力来源，用 `and` 连接最后一个。

3. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`work harder` 更努力地学习。

4. **答案：disturbance(s)**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`sleep disturbance` 睡眠障碍，名词。

5. **答案：studying**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。边熬夜边学习。

6. **答案：exhausted**
   - **考点**：-ed 形容词。`too exhausted to concentrate` 太疲惫以至于无法集中注意力。

7. **答案：healthier / healthy**
   - **考点**：形容词。`a healthier/healthy study routine` 更健康的学习作息。

8. **答案：following**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `After` 后用动名词。

9. **答案：significantly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`improved significantly` 显著提高。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：同级比较。`just as important as` 和……一样重要。
