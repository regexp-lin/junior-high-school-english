# 02-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：绿色出行 (Green Travel)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As cities grow larger and traffic becomes (1) ______ (heavy), more people are looking for greener ways to travel. Green travel means choosing transportation methods that produce less pollution and use (2) ______ (few) resources. Walking, cycling and taking public transport are all examples of green travel. Electric vehicles have also become an important part of the solution. In China, the number of electric cars on the road (3) ______ (increase) dramatically over the past five years. Many cities have built extensive charging networks to make it easier for people (4) ______ (own) electric vehicles. My neighbour recently bought an electric car and says it costs much (5) ______ (little) to run than a petrol car. However, green travel is not just about (6) ______ (change) the type of vehicle you drive. It is also about changing your habits. For short distances, walking (7) ______ cycling is the best option. Not only does it reduce carbon emissions, (8) ______ it also provides excellent exercise. Some companies now encourage their employees to work from home one or two days a week, (9) ______ reduces the number of commuters on the road. If each person makes small (10) ______ (change) in their daily travel habits, the combined effect can be enormous.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：heavier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`becomes heavier`，暗含与以前相比。

2. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`fewer resources`，`resources` 为可数名词。

3. **答案：has increased**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`over the past five years` 提示从过去到现在。

4. **答案：to own**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`make it easier for sb. to do sth.`。

5. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：比较级。`costs much less`，`little` 的比较级是 `less`。

6. **答案：changing**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `about` 后用动名词。

7. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`walking or cycling`，两种选择之一。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：倒装句。`Not only... but (also)...`，前面倒装后用 `but`。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事。

10. **答案：changes**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`small changes` 小的改变。
