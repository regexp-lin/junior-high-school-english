# 02-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：古诗词之美 (Beauty of Ancient Poetry)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ancient Chinese poetry is considered one of the greatest (1) ______ (treasure) of world literature. Poets such as Li Bai, Du Fu and Wang Wei created works that (2) ______ (read) and admired for over a thousand years. Their poems capture the beauty of nature, the pain of separation and the joy of friendship in language that is both simple (3) ______ deeply moving. My Chinese teacher, Mr. Huang, has a passion for teaching poetry. He often says that reading ancient poems is like (4) ______ (have) a conversation with people who lived centuries ago. Every week, he asks us to memorize and recite one poem. At first, many students found this task (5) ______ (bore) because the classical Chinese language is difficult (6) ______ (understand). But Mr. Huang has a special way of making the poems come alive. He tells us the stories behind each poem and explains the emotions the poet (7) ______ (feel) when writing it. Gradually, I began to appreciate the beauty (8) ______ (hide) in these ancient words. My favourite poem is "Quiet Night Thought" by Li Bai. Whenever I read it, I can feel the (9) ______ (lonely) of being far from home. I think this is (10) ______ ancient poetry remains powerful — it speaks to emotions that are shared by people across all ages and cultures.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：treasures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the greatest treasures`。

2. **答案：have been read**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。`for over a thousand years` 持续到现在。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`both simple and deeply moving`，`both... and...`。

4. **答案：having**
   - **考点**：动名词。`is like having` 就像进行一场对话。

5. **答案：boring**
   - **考点**：-ing 形容词。`found this task boring` 觉得任务无聊，修饰物。

6. **答案：to understand**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be difficult to do` 做某事是困难的。

7. **答案：was feeling / felt**
   - **考点**：过去进行时或一般过去时。诗人写诗时正在感受的情感。

8. **答案：hidden**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the beauty hidden in` 隐藏在……中的美。

9. **答案：loneliness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the loneliness` 孤独感，冠词后用名词。

10. **答案：why**
    - **考点**：表语从句。`This is why...` 这就是为什么……。
