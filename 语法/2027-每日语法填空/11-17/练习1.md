# 11-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：温室效应 (The Greenhouse Effect)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The greenhouse effect is a natural process that keeps the Earth warm enough __1__ (support) life. However, human activities have strengthened this effect, __2__ (lead) to global warming. Burning fossil fuels like coal and oil releases large __3__ (amount) of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere. This extra CO₂ traps more heat, causing temperatures __4__ (rise) worldwide. The __5__ (consequent) are alarming: melting glaciers, rising sea __6__ (level) and more extreme weather events. Scientists warn that if we __7__ (not act) quickly, many coastal cities could be flooded by 2100. Governments around the world __8__ (begin) to take action in recent years. China, for example, has promised to reach carbon neutrality __9__ 2060. As students, we can also contribute __10__ reducing our carbon footprint by saving electricity, using public transport and planting trees.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to support**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。enough + to do sth. 足以做某事，用不定式 to support。

2. **答案：leading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作结果状语）。表示自然结果"导致全球变暖"，用 leading。

3. **答案：amounts**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。large amounts of 大量的，amount 用复数 amounts。

4. **答案：to rise**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。cause sth. to do sth. 导致某物做某事，用不定式 to rise。

5. **答案：consequences**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。consequent 的名词形式为 consequence，此处指多种后果，用复数 consequences。

6. **答案：levels**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。rising sea levels 海平面上升，用复数 levels。

7. **答案：don't act**
   - **考点**：动词时态。if 引导条件状语从句，主句用一般将来时，从句用一般现在时，否定用 don't act。

8. **答案：have begun**
   - **考点**：动词时态。in recent years 与现在完成时连用，复数主语用 have begun。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。by 2060 到2060年为止，by 表示"截止到某时"。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：介词。contribute to doing sth. 有助于做某事，to 为介词，后接动名词。
