# 11-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：感恩父母 (Being Grateful to Parents)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Parents are the __1__ (important) people in our lives, yet we often take their love for __2__ (grant). My mother wakes up at 5:30 every morning __3__ (prepare) breakfast for my brother and me. She never complains __4__ being tired. My father works in a factory and doesn't get home __5__ 8 p.m. Despite his long working hours, he always helps me with my homework __6__ (patient). Last Sunday was my mother's birthday. I wanted to do something __7__ (specially) for her. I got up early and made a simple breakfast — toast with scrambled eggs. It was not perfect, __8__ she seemed very touched. My brother drew a __9__ (colour) card with the words "Best Mum in the World". When Mum saw it, she cried __10__ joy. She said our love was the greatest gift. That day I realised that showing love does not have to cost money — sometimes the simplest gestures mean the most.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most important**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the most important people 最重要的人，important 为多音节词，最高级用 most important。

2. **答案：granted**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。take... for granted 视……为理所当然，grant 用过去分词 granted。

3. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了准备早餐"，用 to prepare。

4. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。complain about 抱怨……，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：until / till**
   - **考点**：介词。doesn't get home until 8 p.m. 直到晚上8点才到家，not... until 直到……才。

6. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 helps，用副词 patiently。

7. **答案：special**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词→形容词）。something special 特别的事，不定代词后接形容词后置定语，用 special。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说不完美，后句说她很感动，语义转折，用 but。

9. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 card，colour 的形容词形式为 colourful。

10. **答案：with / for**
    - **考点**：介词。cry with/for joy 喜极而泣，with/for 表示原因。
