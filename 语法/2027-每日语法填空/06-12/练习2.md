# 06-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：英语演讲比赛 (English Speech Contest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, I took part ___1___ the school English speech contest. The topic was "My Dream", and I had only one week ___2___ (prepare). Every night after finishing homework, I practised my speech in front of ___3___ mirror. My English teacher, Ms. Chen, helped me correct my ___4___ (pronounce) and gave me tips on body language. She told me to make eye contact ___5___ the audience and speak with confidence. On the day of the contest, twelve students ___6___ (choose) from different classes to compete. I was the ___7___ (seven) one to go on stage. My voice trembled at the beginning, ___8___ gradually I became calmer. When I finished, the audience gave me warm applause. Although I ___9___ (not win) the first prize, I still felt proud of ___10___ (I). The experience made me believe that courage is more important than perfection.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。take part in 表示"参加"，固定搭配，填 in。

2. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。one week to prepare 表示"一周的时间来准备"，不定式作后置定语，填 to prepare。

3. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。in front of the mirror 表示"在镜子前"，特指自己家的镜子，用定冠词 the。

4. **答案：pronunciation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。my 后接名词，pronounce → pronunciation。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。make eye contact with 表示"与……进行目光交流"，固定搭配，填 with。

6. **答案：were chosen**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。students 与 choose 之间为被动关系，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的被动语态，填 were chosen。

7. **答案：seventh**
   - **考点**：序数词。the + 序数词，表示"第七个"，seven → seventh。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。"开始时声音颤抖"与"渐渐变得平静"之间为转折关系，用 but。

9. **答案：didn't win**
   - **考点**：动词时态。although 引导让步状语从句，叙述过去事件用一般过去时的否定形式，填 didn't win。

10. **答案：myself**
    - **考点**：代词。feel proud of oneself 表示"为自己感到骄傲"，主语为 I，对应反身代词 myself。
