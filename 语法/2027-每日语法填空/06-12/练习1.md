# 06-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：爷爷的菜园 (Grandpa's Vegetable Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My grandpa has a small vegetable garden behind his house. Every morning, he gets up at five o'clock to take care ___1___ it. The garden is full ___2___ different kinds of vegetables — tomatoes, cucumbers, beans and peppers. Grandpa never uses any chemicals. He believes that ___3___ (nature) growing methods produce the ___4___ (healthy) food. I often visit him during holidays and help him with the ___5___ (garden) work. Last summer, he taught me how ___6___ (plant) seeds properly. I dug small holes, dropped the seeds in, and covered ___7___ with soft soil. Grandpa reminded me ___8___ (water) them every evening. After several weeks, tiny green shoots appeared, and I felt a great sense of ___9___ (achieve). Grandpa always says that growing vegetables is like ___10___ (raise) children — you need patience, love and sunshine.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。take care of 表示"照顾"，固定搭配，填 of。

2. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。be full of 表示"充满"，固定搭配，填 of。

3. **答案：natural**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 growing methods，需用形容词形式，nature → natural。

4. **答案：healthiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，表示"最健康的食物"，healthy → healthiest。

5. **答案：gardening**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 work，garden → gardening，gardening work 表示"园艺劳动"。

6. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。how to do sth. 表示"如何做某事"，teach sb. how to do sth.，填 to plant。

7. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词。cover 后接宾格代词，指代前面的 seeds（复数），填 them。

8. **答案：to water**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。remind sb. to do sth. 表示"提醒某人做某事"，填 to water。

9. **答案：achievement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。a sense of + 名词，表示"一种……感"，achieve → achievement。

10. **答案：raising**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。like 为介词，后接动名词，is like raising children 表示"像养育孩子一样"，填 raising。
