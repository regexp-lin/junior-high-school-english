# 12-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：光盘行动 (Clean Plate Campaign)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Food waste is a serious problem in China. According to a report, Chinese restaurants throw __1__ about 18 million tonnes of food every year. That is enough __2__ (feed) millions of hungry people. To deal with this issue, the "Clean Plate Campaign" __3__ (launch) several years ago. It encourages people to order only __4__ they can finish and to take leftovers home. Our school canteen has also joined the campaign. Signs __5__ (put) up on every table to remind students not to waste food. If a student __6__ (finish) all the food on their plate, they receive a small sticker as __7__ reward. At first, some students thought it was __8__ (necessary) because they believed a little waste did not matter. However, our teacher showed us a video about __9__ (child) in poor areas who did not have enough to eat. The video changed many students' attitudes. Now most of us try our __10__ (good) to clean our plates at every meal.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：away**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。throw away 扔掉、浪费，为固定短语。

2. **答案：to feed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作定语/结果状语）。enough to feed 足以喂养，"enough + to do"结构。

3. **答案：was launched**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。several years ago 几年前，campaign 与 launch 为被动关系，用一般过去时被动 was launched。

4. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：宾语从句（连接代词）。order only what they can finish 只点他们能吃完的，what 引导宾语从句，在从句中作宾语。

5. **答案：are put**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般现在时被动语态）。Signs 与 put up 为被动关系，描述现在的情况用一般现在时被动 are put。

6. **答案：finishes**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致。If a student finishes 如果一个学生吃完，a student 为第三人称单数。

7. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。as a reward 作为奖励，reward 为可数名词单数。

8. **答案：unnecessary**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加前缀构成反义词）。thought it was unnecessary 认为没必要，根据语境加前缀 un- 表示否定。

9. **答案：children**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。children in poor areas 贫困地区的孩子们，child 的复数为 children。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：固定短语。try our best 尽我们最大的努力，good 的最高级为 best。
