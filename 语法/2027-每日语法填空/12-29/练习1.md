# 12-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：写日记的好处 (The Benefits of Keeping a Diary)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Keeping a diary is one of the __1__ (simple) yet most rewarding habits a student can develop. I have been writing a diary __2__ I was ten years old. Every evening, I spend about fifteen minutes __3__ (write) down what happened during the day. Sometimes I record happy moments, and sometimes I express my __4__ (worry) or frustrations. __5__ (look) back at my old diaries helps me see how much I __6__ (change) over the years. My English teacher once told me that keeping a diary in English is an __7__ (effect) way to improve writing skills. So I started writing my diary __8__ English last year. At first, I could only write a few __9__ (sentence). Now I can write a full page without much difficulty. Writing a diary also teaches me to observe life more __10__ (careful). I would encourage every student to give it a try.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：simplest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the simplest 最简单的……之一，one of the + 最高级。

2. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。since I was ten 自从我十岁起，since 引导时间状语从句，与现在完成时连用。

3. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 花时间做某事，用动名词。

4. **答案：worries**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。my worries 我的担忧，my 后接名词，worry 的名词复数为 worries。

5. **答案：Looking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。Looking back 回顾，动名词短语作主语。

6. **答案：have changed**
   - **考点**：动词时态。over the years 这些年来，与现在完成时连用，用 have changed。

7. **答案：effective**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。an effective way 一个有效的方法，修饰名词用形容词。

8. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。in English 用英语，in 表示"用某种语言"。

9. **答案：sentences**
   - **考点**：名词单复数。a few sentences 几个句子，a few 后接可数名词复数。

10. **答案：carefully**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 observe，用副词 carefully。
