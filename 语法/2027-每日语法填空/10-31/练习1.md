# 10-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：课后延时服务 (After-school Service)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Since this term, our school __1__ (offer) after-school services every weekday. Students can stay at school __2__ 5:30 p.m. and join various activities. The programme includes homework tutoring, sports clubs __3__ interest classes. I chose the science experiment class because I have always been curious __4__ how things work. Our teacher, Mr. Zhao, designs fun experiments for us __5__ (do) every week. Last Tuesday, we made a simple motor __6__ (use) batteries and copper wire. It was exciting __7__ (watch) the motor spin for the first time! My friend Li Ming joined the basketball team instead. He practises __8__ (hard) than before and has become a much better __9__ (play). The after-school programme has been popular __10__ both students and parents. It gives us more time to explore our interests while reducing parents' worries about childcare.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has offered**
   - **考点**：动词时态。since this term 与现在完成时连用，主语 school 为第三人称单数，用 has offered。

2. **答案：until / till**
   - **考点**：介词。until/till 5:30 p.m. 直到下午5:30，表示持续到某个时间点。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：并列连词。homework tutoring, sports clubs 和 interest classes 三者并列，最后一个前用 and。

4. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。be curious about 对……感到好奇，为固定搭配。

5. **答案：to do**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。for us to do 供我们做，不定式作后置定语修饰 experiments。

6. **答案：using**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作方式状语）。表示"通过使用电池和铜线"，用 using。

7. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It + be + 形容词 + to do sth.，it 为形式主语，用 to watch。

8. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。后有 than before，hard 的比较级为 harder。

9. **答案：player**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a much better player 一个好得多的球员，play 的名词形式为 player。

10. **答案：with / among**
    - **考点**：介词。be popular with/among 在……中受欢迎，为固定搭配。
