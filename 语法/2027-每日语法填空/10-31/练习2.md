# 10-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：候鸟迁徙 (Bird Migration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every autumn, millions of birds fly south to warmer places. This amazing journey __1__ (know) as migration. Some birds travel __2__ (thousand) of kilometres without stopping. They use the sun, stars and the Earth's magnetic field __3__ (find) their way. Scientists are still __4__ (surprise) by how accurate these natural navigation systems are. One of the most famous migratory birds in China is the red-crowned crane. These __5__ (beauty) birds spend summer in the north and winter in the south. Last November, I visited Poyang Lake __6__ my family. It is the __7__ (large) freshwater lake in China and a major resting point for migratory birds. We saw __8__ (hundred) of white swans floating on the water. A guide told us __9__ the birds faced many dangers along the way, including storms and hunters. He said protecting wetlands was essential __10__ the survival of these birds. The experience made me want to do more for wildlife protection.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is known**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。主语 journey 与 know 为被动关系，描述一般事实，用一般现在时被动语态 is known。

2. **答案：thousands**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。thousands of 数千个，为固定搭配，thousand 加 s。

3. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。不定式作目的状语，表示"为了找到方向"，用 to find。

4. **答案：surprised**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be surprised by 对……感到惊讶，-ed 形式表示人的感受。

5. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 birds，beauty 的形容词形式为 beautiful。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。with my family 和家人一起，with 表示"与……一起"。

7. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，large 的最高级为 largest。

8. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。hundreds of 数百个，为固定搭配。

9. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。told us 后接宾语从句，从句意义完整不缺成分，用 that 引导。

10. **答案：for / to**
    - **考点**：介词。be essential for/to 对……至关重要，为固定搭配。
