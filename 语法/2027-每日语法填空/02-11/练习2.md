# 02-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：地铁出行 (Travelling by Metro)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The metro system in Guangzhou has grown (1) ______ (rapid) over the past twenty years. When the first line opened in 1997, it only had a few stations. Today, the network has expanded to over 600 kilometres, (2) ______ (make) it one of the longest metro systems in the world. Millions of people rely (3) ______ the metro to get to work, school and other places every day. I take the metro to school every morning. The journey from my home to school takes about 30 minutes, (4) ______ is much faster than going by bus. During rush hour, the trains can be extremely (5) ______ (crowd), and sometimes I have to let two or three trains pass before I can squeeze in. Despite this inconvenience, the metro is still the most (6) ______ (efficiency) way to travel across the city. One of the (7) ______ (thing) I like most about the Guangzhou metro is its cleanliness. The stations and trains are always kept spotlessly clean by the hardworking (8) ______ (staff). The metro also has excellent (9) ______ (safe) features, including platform screen doors that prevent accidents. I hope that as the metro system continues (10) ______ (expand), more people will choose public transport over private cars, helping to reduce traffic and pollution.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`has grown rapidly` 快速增长。

2. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：现在分词作结果状语。使它成为最长的地铁系统之一。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`rely on` 依赖。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句话。

5. **答案：crowded**
   - **考点**：形容词。`be extremely crowded` 极其拥挤。

6. **答案：efficient**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most efficient way`，最高级用形容词。

7. **答案：things**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the things` 其中之一。

8. **答案：staff**
   - **考点**：名词。`staff` 为集体名词，本身不加 s。

9. **答案：safety**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `features`，用名词作定语 `safety features`。

10. **答案：to expand**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`continue to do sth.` 继续做某事。
