# 02-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：宠物领养 (Pet Adoption)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last spring, my family decided to adopt a dog from the local animal shelter instead of (1) ______ (buy) one from a pet shop. When we arrived at the shelter, there were dozens of dogs waiting (2) ______ (eager) behind their cages, hoping to find a new home. A small brown dog caught my attention immediately. She was sitting quietly in the corner, looking up at me with her big (3) ______ (sadness) eyes. The shelter worker told us that she (4) ______ (abandon) by her previous owner three months earlier. We decided to take her home and (5) ______ (name) her Lucky. The first few weeks were not easy. Lucky was afraid of loud (6) ______ (noise) and would hide under the bed whenever someone knocked on the door. It took a lot of patience to help her feel (7) ______ (safely) in her new environment. Gradually, she became more confident and started playing (8) ______ us in the garden. Now, eight months later, Lucky is the (9) ______ (happy) dog in the neighbourhood. She greets everyone with a wagging tail and loves going for walks. Adopting Lucky was one of the best (10) ______ (decide) our family has ever made.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：buying**
   - **考点**：动名词。`instead of doing sth.`，介词后用动名词。

2. **答案：eagerly**
   - **考点**：副词修饰动词。`waiting eagerly` 热切地等待。

3. **答案：sad**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `eyes`，用形容词 `sad`。

4. **答案：had been abandoned**
   - **考点**：过去完成时的被动语态。在被告知之前已经被遗弃。

5. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与 `decided to take` 并列的过去时动作。

6. **答案：noises**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`loud noises` 各种响声。

7. **答案：safe**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel safe`，感官动词后用形容词作表语。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`play with sb.` 和某人玩耍。

9. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the happiest dog`。

10. **答案：decisions**
    - **考点**：词性转换 + 复数。`one of the best decisions`，名词复数形式。
