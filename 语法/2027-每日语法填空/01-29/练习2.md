# 01-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：垃圾分类 (Waste Sorting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Waste sorting has become a hot topic in China in recent years. In 2019, Shanghai became the first Chinese city (1) ______ (introduce) mandatory waste sorting regulations. Since then, many other cities (2) ______ (follow) suit. Under the new rules, household waste must be separated into four categories: recyclable waste, kitchen waste, hazardous waste and other waste. Different coloured bins are placed in communities to help residents sort their rubbish (3) ______ (correct). At first, many people found the new system (4) ______ (confuse). They were not sure which bin to put certain items in. My neighbour, Aunt Wang, once accidentally threw a used battery into the kitchen waste bin, (5) ______ caused a small commotion among the community volunteers. To help people learn the rules, our community organised several (6) ______ (education) workshops. Volunteers explained the importance of waste sorting and demonstrated how (7) ______ (identify) different types of waste. They also created a WeChat group where residents could ask questions and share tips. Gradually, waste sorting became (8) ______ natural part of everyone's daily routine. The benefits of waste sorting are (9) ______ (significance). By separating recyclable materials from other waste, we can reduce the amount of rubbish sent to landfills and conserve valuable resources. Kitchen waste can be turned (10) ______ compost for gardens, while hazardous materials can be disposed of safely. I believe that waste sorting is a small step that can lead to big changes for our environment.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to introduce**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the first city to introduce`，序数词后用不定式作定语。

2. **答案：have followed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 为现在完成时标志。

3. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `sort` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：confusing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found the new system confusing`，修饰事物用 `-ing`。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面的事件。

6. **答案：educational**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `workshops` 需要形容词形式。

7. **答案：to identify**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to identify`，疑问词 + 不定式结构。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`became a natural part`，泛指"一个自然的部分"。

9. **答案：significant**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`are significant`，系动词后接形容词。

10. **答案：into**
    - **考点**：介词。`be turned into` 表示"被转化为"。
