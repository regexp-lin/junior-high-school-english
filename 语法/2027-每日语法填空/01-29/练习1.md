# 01-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：书法艺术 (The Art of Calligraphy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese calligraphy is one of the (1) ______ (old) and most respected art forms in the world. It is more than just writing — it is a way of expressing beauty, emotion and character through the strokes of a brush. The four treasures of the study — brush, ink, paper and inkstone — are the essential tools (2) ______ (need) for calligraphy. Each tool plays a specific role in creating the perfect piece of work. Learning calligraphy requires great patience and years of practice. A good calligrapher must master (3) ______ (vary) strokes and techniques before they can develop their own style. My grandfather has been practising calligraphy (4) ______ over fifty years. His handwriting is so beautiful that people often compare it (5) ______ the works of ancient masters. Every morning, he wakes up at six o'clock and spends an hour (6) ______ (practise) in his study. He says that calligraphy helps him stay calm and (7) ______ (focus). Last year, my grandfather started teaching me calligraphy. At first, I found it (8) ______ (frustrate) because my strokes looked clumsy and uneven. But he told me not to rush. "Calligraphy is a journey, not a destination," he said (9) ______ (gentle). Slowly, under his guidance, my writing began to improve. Now I practise for thirty minutes every day after finishing my homework. I have come to appreciate calligraphy not just as an art form, (10) ______ as a form of meditation that brings peace to my busy life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the oldest art forms`。

2. **答案：needed**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the essential tools needed for calligraphy`，被动关系。

3. **答案：various**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `strokes` 需要形容词形式。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for over fifty years`，`for` + 一段时间，与现在完成时搭配。

5. **答案：to / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`compare A to/with B` 表示"把A与B相比较"。

6. **答案：practising**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 为固定搭配。

7. **答案：focused**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`stay calm and focused`，系动词后接形容词。

8. **答案：frustrating**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found it frustrating`，修饰事物/经历用 `-ing` 形式。

9. **答案：gently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `said` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：连词。`not just...but (also)...` 结构。
