# 11-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园寻宝活动 (School Treasure Hunt)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school organised a treasure hunt to celebrate the end of the term. Every team __1__ (give) a map and a list of clues. The clues were __2__ (write) in English, which made the activity both fun and __3__ (education). Our team had five members. We started at the school gate and followed the __4__ (one) clue to the library. There, we found a riddle __5__ (hide) inside a book. It took us five minutes __6__ (solve) it. The answer led us __7__ the science lab, where another clue was waiting. The hunt lasted about an hour. Some clues were so difficult __8__ we had to ask passers-by for hints. The team __9__ found all ten items first would win the grand prize — a set of books. __10__ (lucky), our team came in second place and won small notebooks. Although we did not win the top prize, we had a great time working together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was given**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。every team 与 give 为被动关系，过去时态，用 was given。

2. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。be written in English 用英语写成，write 的过去分词为 written。

3. **答案：educational**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。与 fun 并列作宾补/表语，education 的形容词形式为 educational。

4. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：词性转换（基数词→序数词）。the first clue 第一条线索，one 的序数词为 first。

5. **答案：hidden**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。riddle 与 hide 为被动关系，用过去分词 hidden。

6. **答案：to solve**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。It takes + 时间 + to do sth. 做某事花费多长时间，用不定式 to solve。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。led us to 把我们引向……，lead to 通向。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：结果状语从句。so + 形容词 + that... 如此……以至于……，为固定句型。

9. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：定语从句关系代词。先行词 the team 指物（团队），从句缺主语，用 that 或 which。

10. **答案：Unluckily**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。置于句首作评注性状语，表示"不幸的是"，lucky 加前缀 un- 再变副词为 unluckily，注意首字母大写。
