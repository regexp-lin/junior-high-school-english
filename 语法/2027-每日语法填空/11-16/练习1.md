# 11-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：民间艺术 (Folk Art)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has a rich tradition of folk art. From paper cutting to shadow puppetry, these art forms __1__ (pass) down through generations. My grandmother is __2__ (skill) at paper cutting. She can turn a piece of red paper __3__ a beautiful design in minutes. Her favourite patterns include flowers, birds and the Chinese character for "double happiness". When I was little, I loved __4__ (sit) beside her and watching her hands move quickly. She told me she __5__ (learn) the craft from her own grandmother when she was just five years old. Last month, our school invited a folk artist __6__ (teach) us traditional dough modelling. He shaped coloured dough into __7__ (life) animals and characters from Chinese legends. Each student got to make __8__ (they) own dough figure. Mine was supposed to be a dragon, __9__ it looked more like a worm! The artist laughed kindly and helped me fix it. I think it is important for young people __10__ (keep) these traditional arts alive.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have been passed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。these art forms 与 pass down 为被动关系，through generations 暗示持续到现在，用现在完成时被动语态 have been passed。

2. **答案：skilful / skilled**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。be skilful/skilled at 擅长……，skill 的形容词形式为 skilful 或 skilled。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。turn... into... 把……变成……，为固定搭配。

4. **答案：sitting / to sit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。love doing/to do sth. 喜欢做某事，sitting 或 to sit 均可。

5. **答案：had learned / had learnt**
   - **考点**：动词时态。told 为一般过去时，学习手艺发生在更早之前，用过去完成时 had learned。

6. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。invite sb. to do sth. 邀请某人做某事，用不定式 to teach。

7. **答案：lifelike / lively**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 animals，life 的形容词形式 lifelike 表示"栩栩如生的"。

8. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 dough figure，用 their。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：并列连词。前句说本应是龙，后句说看起来像虫，语义转折，用 but。

10. **答案：to keep**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。It is important for sb. to do sth. 对某人来说做某事很重要，用 to keep。
