# 动词短语——常见可分 / 不可分动词短语（Separable and Inseparable Phrasal Verbs）

> 日期：2027 年 02 月 25 日 | 语法分类：词法 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

**动词短语（phrasal verb）** 由"动词 + 副词/介词"构成，整体表示一个特定含义。根据宾语能否插入动词和小品词之间，分为**可分动词短语（separable）** 和**不可分动词短语（inseparable）**。

---

### 2. 可分动词短语

宾语可以放在动词和副词**之间**或**之后**。但如果宾语是**代词（it / them / me...）**，必须放在**中间**。

#### 2.1 常见可分动词短语

| 短语 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| turn on | 打开 | Turn **on** the TV. / Turn the TV **on**. / Turn **it** on. |
| turn off | 关闭 | Turn **off** the light. / Turn **it** off. |
| pick up | 捡起；接（人） | Pick **up** the book. / Pick **it** up. |
| put on | 穿上 | Put **on** your coat. / Put **it** on. |
| take off | 脱掉；起飞 | Take **off** your shoes. / Take **them** off. |
| put away | 收拾好 | Put **away** your toys. / Put **them** away. |
| give back | 归还 | Give **back** my pen. / Give **it** back. |
| wake up | 叫醒 | Wake **up** your sister. / Wake **her** up. |
| write down | 写下 | Write **down** the answer. / Write **it** down. |
| try on | 试穿 | Try **on** the dress. / Try **it** on. |
| work out | 算出 | Work **out** the problem. / Work **it** out. |
| cut down | 砍倒 | Cut **down** the tree. / Cut **it** down. |
| look up | 查阅 | Look **up** the word. / Look **it** up. |
| think over | 仔细考虑 | Think **over** the plan. / Think **it** over. |
| hand in | 上交 | Hand **in** your homework. / Hand **it** in. |
| clean up | 打扫干净 | Clean **up** the room. / Clean **it** up. |

> **核心规则：代词宾语必须放中间！**
> - ✅ Turn **it** on.
> - ❌ Turn on **it**.

---

### 3. 不可分动词短语

宾语只能放在整个短语**后面**，不能插入中间（即使是代词）。

#### 3.1 动词 + 介词（不可分）

| 短语 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| look at | 看 | Look **at** the picture. / Look **at** it. |
| look for | 寻找 | Look **for** your keys. / Look **for** them. |
| look after | 照顾 | Look **after** your sister. / Look **after** her. |
| listen to | 听 | Listen **to** the music. / Listen **to** it. |
| wait for | 等待 | Wait **for** the bus. / Wait **for** it. |
| think of / about | 想到/考虑 | Think **of** a good idea. / Think **of** one. |
| laugh at | 嘲笑 | Don't laugh **at** him. |
| depend on | 依赖 | It depends **on** the weather. / It depends **on** it. |
| worry about | 担心 | Don't worry **about** the exam. |
| agree with | 同意 | I agree **with** you. |
| arrive at / in | 到达 | We arrived **at** the station. |
| belong to | 属于 | The book belongs **to** me. |
| hear from | 收到来信 | I heard **from** my friend. |

> **核心规则：代词也放在介词后面！**
> - ✅ Look **at** it.
> - ❌ Look **it** at.

---

### 4. 判断可分与不可分的技巧

| 类型 | 特征 | 代词位置 |
|------|------|---------|
| 动词 + 副词（可分） | on, off, up, down, away, out, over, in 等副词 | 代词放**中间** |
| 动词 + 介词（不可分） | at, for, after, to, of, about, with, on 等介词 | 代词放**后面** |

> 同一个词（如 on）有时是副词（可分），有时是介词（不可分），需根据具体短语判断。

---

### 5. 标志词 / 关键信号

| 标志 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 代词 it / them / him / her... | 必须注意放置位置 |
| turn on/off, pick up, put on/away, take off | 典型可分短语 |
| look at/for/after, listen to, wait for | 典型不可分短语 |

---

### 6. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | Please turn on it. | Please turn **it** on. | 可分短语代词放中间 |
| 2 | Look it at. | Look **at** it. | 不可分短语宾语放后面 |
| 3 | He picked up it. | He picked **it** up. | 可分短语代词放中间 |
| 4 | Please look the word up in the dictionary. | 此句正确 | 名词宾语可放中间或后面 |
| 5 | She takes after her mother.（像妈妈）→ She takes her after. | She takes **after** her. | take after 不可分 |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

**说明：第 1～10 题为中等难度；第 11～20 题为拔高题。请选出每题的最佳答案。**

**1.** It's dark. Please turn ______ the light.

A. on　　B. off　　C. up　　D. down

**2.** The music is too loud. Can you turn ______ ?

A. it down　　B. down it　　C. it up　　D. up it

**3.** Don't forget to ______ your homework.

A. hand it in　　B. hand in it　　C. hand in　　D. hand it

**4.** Please ______ your new words in the dictionary.

A. look up　　B. look at　　C. look for　　D. look after

**5.** It's cold outside. ______ your coat, please.

A. Take off　　B. Put on　　C. Pick up　　D. Turn on

**6.** Who will ______ the baby while you're away?

A. look at　　B. look for　　C. look after　　D. look up

**7.** The pen is on the floor. Please pick ______.

A. up it　　B. it up　　C. it on　　D. on it

**8.** She ______ the TV and went to bed.

A. turned on　　B. turned off　　C. turned up　　D. put on

**9.** We must ______ a way to solve this problem.

A. think over　　B. think of　　C. think up it　　D. think it

**10.** Don't ______ others. It's not polite.

A. look at　　B. laugh at　　C. look for　　D. wait for

**11.** The shoes are too small. Please ______.

A. try on them　　B. try them on　　C. try it on　　D. try on it

**12.** I don't know the word. Let me ______ in the dictionary.

A. look up it　　B. look it up　　C. look for it　　D. look at it

**13.** She ______ her mother very much. They look alike.

A. takes off　　B. takes after　　C. takes on　　D. takes up

**14.** He ______ his jacket because it was very hot.

A. put on　　B. put off　　C. took off　　D. took on

**15.** Please write ______ and give ______ to me.

A. it down; it　　B. down it; it　　C. it down; them　　D. down it; them

**16.** The old man is ill. We should ______ him.

A. look for　　B. look at　　C. look up　　D. look after

**17.** Don't ______! Everything will be fine.

A. give up　　B. give it up　　C. give up it　　D. give it

**18.** These books belong ______ the library. Please give ______ back.

A. to; it　　B. to; them　　C. for; them　　D. of; it

**19.** She couldn't work ______ the math problem, so she asked her teacher for help.

A. out　　B. it out　　C. out it　　D. on

**20.** —Can I ______ your dictionary?
—Sure, but you must give ______ back tomorrow.

A. borrow; it　　B. borrow; them　　C. lend; it　　D. lend; them

### 逐题解析

**第 1 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：天黑了要**打开**灯，turn **on** the light。
- **排除法**：B turn off 关闭；C/D 不合语境。
- **考查知识点**：turn on 打开。

**第 2 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：音乐太吵要**调小**声，turn down；代词 it 放中间，turn **it** down。
- **排除法**：B 代词不能放后面；C turn up 调大；D 结构错。
- **考查知识点**：可分短语代词放中间；易错点 1。

**第 3 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：别忘了**交**作业，hand in your homework。homework 是名词不是代词，可放后面。
- **排除法**：A hand it in 没有 it 的指代；B 代词不放后面（如果是 it 的话）；D 不完整。
- **考查知识点**：hand in + 名词宾语。

**第 4 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：在字典里**查**生词，用 **look up**。
- **排除法**：B look at 看；C look for 寻找；D look after 照顾。
- **考查知识点**：look up 查阅。

**第 5 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：外面冷，**穿上**外套，**Put on** your coat。
- **排除法**：A take off 脱掉；C pick up 捡起；D turn on 打开。
- **考查知识点**：put on 穿上。

**第 6 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：**照顾**宝宝，**look after**。
- **排除法**：A look at 看；B look for 找；D look up 查。
- **考查知识点**：look after 照顾（不可分）。

**第 7 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：pick up（捡起），代词 it 放中间：pick **it** up。
- **排除法**：A 代词不放后面；C/D 不是 pick up。
- **考查知识点**：可分短语代词放中间。

**第 8 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：**关掉**电视去睡觉，turned **off** the TV。
- **排除法**：A turned on 打开；C turned up 调大；D put on 穿上。
- **考查知识点**：turn off 关闭。

**第 9 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：**想到**解决办法，think **of** a way。
- **排除法**：A think over 仔细考虑（不接 a way 那样自然）；C/D 结构不对。
- **考查知识点**：think of 想到（不可分）。

**第 10 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：不要**嘲笑**别人，**laugh at** others。
- **排除法**：A look at 看；C look for 找；D wait for 等。
- **考查知识点**：laugh at 嘲笑（不可分）。

**第 11 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：鞋子太小要**试穿**，try on；shoes 复数用代词 them，放中间：try **them** on。
- **排除法**：A 代词不放后面；C/D shoes 是复数用 them。
- **考查知识点**：可分短语代词放中间。

**第 12 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：**查**这个单词，look up；代词 it 放中间：look **it** up。
- **排除法**：A 代词不放后面；C look for 寻找不合；D look at 看不合。
- **考查知识点**：可分短语代词放中间。

**第 13 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：长得像妈妈，**take after**（外貌像，不可分）。
- **排除法**：A takes off 脱掉/起飞；C takes on 承担；D takes up 开始从事。
- **考查知识点**：take after 像（不可分）；易错点 5。

**第 14 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：太热了**脱掉**夹克，**took off** his jacket。
- **排除法**：A put on 穿上（反义）；B put off 推迟；D took on 承担。
- **考查知识点**：take off 脱掉。

**第 15 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：write down（写下）代词 it 放中间：write **it** down；give 后接 it。
- **排除法**：B/D 代词不放后面。
- **考查知识点**：可分短语代词放中间。

**第 16 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：老人生病，应该**照顾**他，**look after**。
- **排除法**：A look for 找；B look at 看；C look up 查。
- **考查知识点**：look after 照顾。

**第 17 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：别**放弃**！give up 后无宾语时不需要代词。
- **排除法**：B 有 it 但语境中无指代对象；C 代词不放后面；D 不完整。
- **考查知识点**：give up 放弃（不接宾语时不可分问题不存在）。

**第 18 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：belong **to**（不可分）+ the library；books 复数代词用 **them**，give them back。
- **排除法**：A it 不对应 books；C/D 介词错。
- **考查知识点**：belong to 不可分 + give back 可分代词放中间。

**第 19 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：work out the math problem（算出数学题），名词宾语放后面用 work **out**。
- **排除法**：B 需名词宾语在后时不必用代词；C 代词不放后面；D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：work out 算出。

**第 20 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：**借**别人的字典用 **borrow**；归还用 give back，代词 it（dictionary 单数）放中间。
- **排除法**：B them 数不对；C/D lend 是借出不合语境。
- **考查知识点**：borrow 借入 + give it back 可分代词放中间。
