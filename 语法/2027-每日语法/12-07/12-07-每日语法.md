# 特殊疑问句——疑问词 who / what / which / how 的综合运用（Special Questions — Question Words: who / what / which / how）

> 日期：2027 年 12 月 7 日 | 语法分类：句法 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

特殊疑问句由**疑问词**引导，用来对句子中某个成分提问，不能用 Yes/No 回答。中考常考的疑问词有 who, what, which, where, when, why, how 及其组合形式（how many, how much, how long, how often, how far, how soon, what time, what color 等）。掌握"根据答句选疑问词"是重要的解题技巧。

### 2. 构成形式

**基本结构**：疑问词 + 助动词/be动词 + 主语 + 动词（原形）+ 其他?

| 疑问词 | 提问对象 | 示例问句 | 示例答句 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| who | 人（主语/宾语） | Who is he? | He is my teacher. |
| what | 事物/职业 | What is she doing? | She is reading. |
| which | 哪一个（选择） | Which pen is yours? | The red one. |
| where | 地点 | Where do you live? | I live in Beijing. |
| when | 时间 | When did you arrive? | Yesterday. |
| why | 原因 | Why are you late? | Because I missed the bus. |
| how | 方式/状态 | How do you go to school? | By bus. |

| 组合疑问词 | 提问对象 | 示例 |
|-----------|---------|------|
| how many + 可数名词复数 | 数量 | How many books do you have? |
| how much + 不可数名词 | 数量/价格 | How much water do you need? / How much is it? |
| how long | 时间长度/物体长度 | How long have you been here? |
| how often | 频率 | How often do you exercise? |
| how far | 距离 | How far is it from here to school? |
| how soon | 多久以后 | How soon will he come back? |
| how old | 年龄 | How old are you? |
| what time | 具体时刻 | What time do you get up? |
| what color | 颜色 | What color is your bag? |

### 3. 用法与语境

- **用法一**：疑问词作主语时语序不变
  - 例：Who broke the window?（谁打破了窗户？）——疑问词 who 就是主语，语序同陈述句。
  - 例：What happened yesterday?（昨天发生了什么？）

- **用法二**：疑问词作非主语成分时用倒装语序
  - 例：What did you buy?（你买了什么？）——what 作宾语，需倒装。
  - 例：Where does he live?（他住在哪里？）——where 作状语，需倒装。

- **用法三**：how 系列疑问词的区别
  - How long 问时间长度：— How long did you wait? — For two hours.
  - How often 问频率：— How often do you go swimming? — Twice a week.
  - How far 问距离：— How far is it? — About 5 kilometers.
  - How soon 问"多久以后"：— How soon will he be back? — In two days.（用 in + 时间段回答）

- **用法四**：what 与 which 的区别
  - what 范围不明确：What sport do you like?（你喜欢什么运动？——范围不限）
  - which 范围明确：Which sport do you like, basketball or football?（你喜欢哪个运动，篮球还是足球？——限定范围）

### 4. 标志词 / 关键信号

| 答句关键词 | 对应疑问词 | 示例 |
|-----------|-----------|------|
| 人名/身份 | who / whose | — Tom did it. → Who did it? |
| 事物/动作 | what | — A book. → What did you buy? |
| 地点 | where | — In the park. → Where are they? |
| 时间 | when / what time | — At 7:00. → What time...? |
| 原因（Because...） | why | — Because I was ill. → Why...? |
| 方式（By bus...） | how | — By bike. → How do you...? |
| 数量（可数） | how many | — Three. → How many...? |
| 数量/价格（不可数） | how much | — 50 yuan. → How much...? |
| 时间长度（For...） | how long | — For 2 hours. → How long...? |
| 频率（Twice a week） | how often | — Once a month. → How often...? |
| 距离 | how far | — 5 km. → How far...? |
| 多久以后（In...） | how soon | — In 3 days. → How soon...? |

### 5. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | Who did break the window? | Who broke the window? | 疑问词作主语时不需要助动词 did |
| 2 | How long will he come back? | How soon will he come back? | "多久以后"用 how soon，how long 问持续时间 |
| 3 | How many water do you need? | How much water do you need? | water 不可数，用 how much |
| 4 | What book do you like, this one or that one? | Which book do you like, this one or that one? | 有限定范围的选择用 which |
| 5 | How often did it take you to finish? | How long did it take you to finish? | 问花了多长时间用 how long |

### 逐题解析

**第 1 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：答句 Miss Li 是人名，问"谁"用 Who。
- **排除法**：A. What 问事物/职业；C. Which 问选择；D. How 问方式。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → 标志词：人名对应 who。

**第 2 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：答句 By bus 表方式，用 How。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不问方式。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → 标志词：方式对应 how。

**第 3 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：students 是可数名词，问数量用 How many。
- **排除法**：A. How much 用于不可数。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → 构成形式：how many + 可数名词复数。

**第 4 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：答句 About 3 kilometers 表距离，用 How far。
