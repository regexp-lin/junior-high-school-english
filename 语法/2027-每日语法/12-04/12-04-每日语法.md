# 现在完成时——for 与 since 的用法区别（Present Perfect — for vs. since）

> 日期：2027 年 12 月 4 日 | 语法分类：时态 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

现在完成时（have/has + 过去分词）常与表示**一段时间**的时间状语连用，其中 **for** 和 **since** 是最高频的两个时间标志词。for 后接**一段时间**，since 后接**过去的时间点或从句**。它们只能用于**延续性动词**的现在完成时，非延续性动词需要转换为延续性表达。

### 2. 构成形式

| 标志词 | 后接内容 | 示例 |
|--------|---------|------|
| for | 一段时间 | for three years / for two hours / for a long time |
| since | 过去的时间点 | since 2020 / since last Monday / since three years ago |
| since | 过去时态的从句 | since he came here / since I was a child |

| 句型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + have/has + 过去分词 + for/since... | He has lived here for five years. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + haven't/hasn't + 过去分词 + for/since... | I haven't seen him since last year. |
| 疑问句 | How long + have/has + 主语 + 过去分词? | How long have you been here? |

### 3. 用法与语境

- **用法一**：for + 一段时间
  - 例：She has studied English for six years.（她学英语六年了。）
  - 例：They have lived in Beijing for ten years.（他们在北京住了十年了。）

- **用法二**：since + 过去的时间点
  - 例：He has worked here since 2018.（他从2018年起就在这里工作。）
  - 例：I have known her since childhood.（我从小就认识她。）

- **用法三**：since + 从句（从句用一般过去时）
  - 例：She has been busy since she came back.（她回来以后就一直很忙。）
  - 例：Great changes have taken place since I left my hometown.（自从我离开家乡以来，发生了巨大的变化。）

- **用法四**：非延续性动词 → 延续性动词转换（核心考点！）
  - buy → have：He bought the bike two years ago. → He has had the bike for two years.
  - borrow → keep：She borrowed the book last week. → She has kept the book since last week.
  - come / go → be：He came here three years ago. → He has been here for three years.
  - die → be dead：His dog died two years ago. → His dog has been dead for two years.
  - join → be in / be a member of：He joined the army in 2019. → He has been in the army since 2019.
  - begin / start → be on：The movie started an hour ago. → The movie has been on for an hour.
  - leave → be away from：She left home two days ago. → She has been away from home for two days.
  - open → be open：The shop opened at 8:00. → The shop has been open since 8:00.
  - close → be closed：The library closed an hour ago. → The library has been closed for an hour.

### 4. 标志词 / 关键信号

| 标志词/短语 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------------|------|------|
| for + 时间段 | 持续了多久 | for two weeks |
| since + 时间点 | 从……以来 | since 2020 |
| since + 过去时从句 | 自从…… | since he left |
| How long...? | 提问持续时间 | How long have you been here? |

### 5. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | He has bought the car for two years. | He has had the car for two years. | buy 是非延续性动词，不能与 for 连用 |
| 2 | She has come here since 2020. | She has been here since 2020. | come 是非延续性动词，转为 be here |
| 3 | I have known him for I was a child. | I have known him since I was a child. | "从……以来"用 since 不用 for |
| 4 | He has died for three years. | He has been dead for three years. | die 是非延续性动词，转为 be dead |
| 5 | She has borrowed the book for a week. | She has kept the book for a week. | borrow 是非延续性动词，转为 keep |
