# 句型——There is no doubt that / It is+adj.+that（Common Sentence Patterns）

> 日期：2027 年 04 月 11 日 | 语法分类：句型 / 名词性从句（初中） | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

**There is no doubt that ...** 表示“毫无疑问……”，**that** 引导同位语从句，说明 **doubt** 的内容（**that** 一般**不省略**）。**It is + 形容词 + that ...** 中 **It** 为**形式主语**，真正的主语是 **that** 从句，表示“做某事是……的”。

### 2. 详解：两种句型

| 句型 | 结构要点 | 例句 |
|------|----------|------|
| **There is no doubt that ...** | **there be + no doubt + that-从句** | *There is no doubt that **he tried his best**.* |
| **It is + adj. + that ...** | **It** 作形式主语，**that** 引导主语从句 | *It is important that **we be on time**.*（初中亦常见 *should be / 动词原形* 等） |

### 3. 对比

| 对比 | **There is no doubt that** | **It is clear / true / important that** |
|------|---------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| **核心名词** | **doubt**（怀疑） | 形容词表评价或态度 |
| **开头词** | **There**（存在句） | **It**（形式主语） |

### 4. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 | 错因分析 |
|------|--------|--------|---------|
| 1 | *There is no doubt whether he will come*（无疑时） | *There is no doubt **that** ...* | 无疑用 **that** |
| 2 | *It is important of us to ...* 与 *that* 从句混用结构 | 按句式选用 **for sb. to do** 或 **that** 从句 | 结构平行 |
| 3 | 在 **no doubt** 后误用 **if** | 用 **that** 引导从句 | 连接词误用 |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

**说明**：第 1–10 题为中等难度，第 11–20 题为拔高难度。

**1.** There is no doubt ______ our classmates did their best in the basketball final.  
A. that　　B. if　　C. whether　　D. because

**2.** ______ is no doubt that reading every day opens a bigger world for us.  
A. This　　B. There　　C. It　　D. That

**3.** It is important ______ we hand in the group report before Friday afternoon.  
A. if　　B. whether　　C. that　　D. what

**4.** It was clear ______ Tina had practiced a lot before the school piano exam.  
A. because　　B. if　　C. whether　　D. that

**5.** There is no doubt ______ helping parents with chores makes us more responsible.  
A. that　　B. if　　C. whether　　D. what

**6.** ______ is important that teenagers get enough sleep during busy weekdays.  
A. This　　B. It　　C. There　　D. That

**7.** There is no doubt ______ the new reading room will stay open longer before exams.  
A. whether　　B. if　　C. that　　D. what

**8.** It is true ______ a balanced breakfast helps us stay focused in the morning classes.  
A. because　　B. if　　C. whether　　D. that

**9.** There is no doubt ______ Andy can catch up in math if he asks questions bravely in class.  
A. that　　B. whether　　C. if　　D. what

**10.** It is surprising ______ so many students signed up for the beach clean-up activity.  
A. because　　B. that　　C. if　　D. whether

**11.** There is little doubt ______ our science project will get a prize if we test it again.  
A. whether　　B. if　　C. that　　D. what

**12.** ______ is no doubt that honest feedback helped us improve the class play.  
A. It　　B. This　　C. That　　D. There

**13.** It is strange ______ Bob forgot the English corner time; he is usually very careful.  
A. that　　B. whether　　C. if　　D. which

**14.** There is no doubt ______ riding bikes to school is a green choice for short distances.  
A. if　　B. that　　C. whether　　D. what

**15.** It is well known ______ Hangzhou is beautiful all year round.  
A. whether　　B. if　　C. that　　D. what

**16.** There is no doubt ______ we made the right decision to start the "21-day reading" plan.  
A. if　　B. whether　　C. because　　D. that

**17.** ______ is clear that checking answers carefully can prevent careless mistakes.  
A. It　　B. There　　C. This　　D. That

**18.** It is possible ______ the school concert will be held outdoors if the weather stays fine.  
A. whether　　B. that　　C. if　　D. because

**19.** There is no doubt ______ listening to short English talks every day trains our ears.  
A. if　　B. whether　　C. that　　D. what

**20.** It is certain ______ our grade will show a short video on Parents' Day next month.  
A. whether　　B. if　　C. because　　D. that

### 逐题解析

**第 1 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：**There is no doubt** 后接 **that** 从句，陈述“没有疑问的内容”。
- **排除法**：B/C 多用于“是否”，与“无疑”矛盾；D 引导原因状语从句，结构不符。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → **There is no doubt that ...**。

**第 2 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：固定句式 **There is no doubt that ...**，用 **There**。
- **排除法**：A/C/D 不能构成该固定存在句结构。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → **There** 引导存在句。

**第 3 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：**It is important that ...** 中 **that** 引导主语从句（**it** 为形式主语）。
- **排除法**：A/B 表示“是否”，不适用；D *what* 在从句中需充当成分，本从句意思完整。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → **It is + adj. + that ...**。

**第 4 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：**It is / was clear that ...** 表“很明显……”，用 **that**。
- **排除法**：A 表原因，但本句型优先用 **that** 引出事实；B/C 表“是否”，不符。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → **It is clear that ...**。

**第 5 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：**There is no doubt that ...**。
- **排除法**：同第 1 题思路。
- **考查知识点**：**There is no doubt that ...**。

**第 6 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：**It is important that ...**，用形式主语 **It**。
- **排除法**：**There is no doubt** 用 *There*；本句形容词为 *important*，典型结构为 **It is important that ...**。
- **考查知识点**：**It is important that ...**。

**第 7 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：**There is no doubt that ...**。
- **排除法**：A/B 表是否；D *what* 不作此句式连接词。
- **考查知识点**：**There is no doubt that ...**。

**第 8 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：**It is true that ...** 表“事实是……”。
- **排除法**：A/B/C 与固定句式不符。
- **考查知识点**：**It is true that ...**。

**第 9 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：**There is no doubt that ...**。
- **排除法**：B/C/D 不符合“无疑”后接陈述的习惯用法。
- **考查知识点**：**There is no doubt that ...**。

**第 10 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：**It is surprising that ...**。
- **排除法**：A 表原因，但与 *It is surprising* 的常见搭配是 **that** 从句；C/D 表是否。
- **考查知识点**：**It is + adj. + that ...**。

**第 11 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：**There is little doubt that ...** 与 **no doubt** 类似，后接 **that**。
- **排除法**：A/B 表“是否”；D *what* 不适用。
- **考查知识点**：**doubt + that** 从句（无疑语境）。

**第 12 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：**There is no doubt that ...**。
- **排除法**：**It is no doubt** 非标准句式；此处缺 **There**。
- **考查知识点**：**There is no doubt** 固定开头。

**第 13 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：**It is strange that ...** 后接 **that** 从句说明“奇怪之处”。
- **排除法**：B/C 表是否；D *which* 需有选择范围。
- **考查知识点**：**It is + adj. + that ...**。

**第 14 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：**There is no doubt that ...**，**that** 在选项 B。
- **排除法**：A/C/D 不能正确引导同位语从句。
- **考查知识点**：**There is no doubt that ...**。

**第 15 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：**It is well known that ...** 表“众所周知……”。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不用于该固定表达。
- **考查知识点**：**It is well known that ...**。

**第 16 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：**There is no doubt that ...**。
- **排除法**：A/B 表是否；C *because* 不用于 **doubt** 后的名词性从句结构。
- **考查知识点**：**There is no doubt that ...**。

**第 17 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：**It is clear that ...**，用 **It** 作形式主语。
- **排除法**：B *There is clear* 不成立；C/D 不用于该句式。
- **考查知识点**：**It is clear that ...**。

**第 18 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：**It is possible that ...** 表“有可能……”。
- **排除法**：A *whether* 可出现在 *possible* 后部分语境，但初中常考 **that**；C *if* 不引导此类主语从句居后结构；D 表原因不当。
- **考查知识点**：**It is possible that ...**。

**第 19 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：**There is no doubt that ...**。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不符合连接“无疑内容”的用法。
- **考查知识点**：**There is no doubt that ...**。

**第 20 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：**It is certain that ...** 表“可以肯定的是……”。
- **排除法**：A/B 表是否；C *because* 不能取代 **that** 引导主语从句。
- **考查知识点**：**It is certain that ...**。
