# 动词——使役动词 make/let/have（Causative Verbs）

> 日期：2027 年 03 月 16 日 | 语法分类：动词 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

**使役动词（causative verb）** 表示"使/让/叫某人做某事"。常见的使役动词有 **make, let, have**。它们的共同特点是后面接**宾语 + 宾语补足语**。

---

### 2. make 的用法

#### 2.1 make + sb. + do sth.（使某人做某事）

> **主动语态：make + 宾语 + 动词原形（省略 to）**

- The teacher **made** us **clean** the classroom.
- My mother **makes** me **get** up early.
- Don't **make** the baby **cry**.

#### 2.2 make + sb./sth. + adj.（使某人/某物处于某种状态）

- The news **made** her **happy**.
- What he said **made** me **angry**.
- We should **make** our city **clean**.

#### 2.3 make 的被动语态

被动语态中，省略的 to 要**还原**：

- 主动：The teacher **made** us **clean** the classroom.
- 被动：We **were made to clean** the classroom (by the teacher).

---

### 3. let 的用法

#### 3.1 let + sb. + do sth.（让某人做某事）

> **let + 宾语 + 动词原形（省略 to）**

- **Let** me **help** you.
- **Let's** **go** out for a walk.
- Don't **let** the dog **run** away.

#### 3.2 let 的否定形式

- **Don't let** him **go** there.
- **Let's not** talk about it. （Let's 的否定：Let's not...）

#### 3.3 let 的被动

let 一般**不用被动语态**，改用 **be allowed to**：

- ❌ He was let go.
- ✅ He **was allowed to** go.

---

### 4. have 的用法

#### 4.1 have + sb. + do sth.（让/叫某人做某事）

> **have + 宾语（人）+ 动词原形**

- I'll **have** him **call** you back.
- She **had** her son **clean** his room.

#### 4.2 have + sth. + done（让某事被做/经历某事）

> **have + 宾语（物）+ 过去分词**

| 用法 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 请人做某事 | 让某事被完成 | I need to **have** my hair **cut**. (让人剪头发) |
| 遭遇不幸 | 某事发生在身上 | He **had** his wallet **stolen**. (钱包被偷了) |

#### 4.3 have + sb./sth. + doing（让某人/某物一直做某事）

- He **had** us **laughing** all evening. （让我们笑了一晚上）
- Don't **have** the water **running** all day. （别让水一直流）

---

### 5. 三者对比

| 使役动词 | 结构 | 语气 | 含义差异 |
|---------|------|------|---------|
| **make** | make + sb. + do | 强制/迫使 | 含有"被迫、不得不"的意味 |
| **let** | let + sb. + do | 允许/放任 | 含有"许可、同意"的意味 |
| **have** | have + sb. + do | 安排/吩咐 | 含有"安排、委托"的意味 |

**对比示例：**
- The boss **made** him work overtime. （老板迫使他加班）
- The boss **let** him leave early. （老板允许他早走）
- The boss **had** his secretary send the email. （老板让秘书发邮件）

---

### 6. 其他相关动词

| 动词 | 结构 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **get** | get + sb. + **to do** | 说服某人做（有 to） | I **got** him **to help** me. |
| **help** | help + sb. + (to) do | 帮助某人做 | She **helped** me **(to) carry** the box. |
| **force** | force + sb. + to do | 强迫某人做 | They **forced** him **to leave**. |
| **allow** | allow + sb. + to do | 允许某人做 | My parents **allow** me **to go**. |

> ⚠️ get 后面要加 **to**，make/let/have 后面**不加 to**。

---

### 7. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | The teacher made us to clean. | The teacher made us **clean**. | make + sb. + do（省略 to） |
| 2 | Let me to help you. | Let me **help** you. | let + sb. + do（省略 to） |
| 3 | He was made clean the room. | He was made **to clean** the room. | 被动语态还原 to |
| 4 | I got him help me. | I got him **to help** me. | get + sb. + to do（有 to） |
| 5 | Let's don't go. | **Let's not** go. | Let's 否定形式 |
