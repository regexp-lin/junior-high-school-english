# 主谓一致——就近原则与就远原则（Subject-Verb Agreement — Proximity & Distance Principles）

> 日期：2027 年 11 月 16 日 | 语法分类：句型 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

主谓一致（Subject-Verb Agreement）是指句子中**谓语动词的单复数形式必须与主语保持一致**。在英语中有三大原则：语法一致、意义一致和**就近原则/就远原则**。就近原则指谓语动词与**离它最近的主语**保持一致；就远原则指谓语动词与**较远的主语（真正主语）**保持一致，忽略中间的修饰成分。

### 2. 构成形式

| 原则 | 适用结构 | 谓语看谁 | 示例 |
|------|---------|---------|------|
| **就近原则** | either...or... | 看 or 后面的名词 | Either you or he **is** right. |
| | neither...nor... | 看 nor 后面的名词 | Neither he nor I **am** a teacher. |
| | not only...but also... | 看 but also 后面的名词 | Not only he but also we **are** students. |
| | there be... | 看 be 后最近的名词 | There **is** a book and two pens on the desk. |
| **就远原则** | ...as well as... | 看 as well as 前面的名词 | He as well as his friends **likes** music. |
| | ...together with... | 看前面的名词 | Tom together with his classmates **is** going to the park. |
| | ...along with... | 看前面的名词 | She along with her parents **goes** to church every Sunday. |
| | ...except/besides... | 看前面的名词 | Everyone except Tom **was** there. |
| | ...with... | 看前面的名词 | The teacher with his students **is** visiting the museum. |

| 句型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | Either A or B + 谓语（看B） | Either she or I am going. |
| 否定句 | Neither A nor B + 谓语（看B） | Neither you nor he is wrong. |
| 疑问句 | — | Is there a pen or two books on the desk? |
| There be | There + be + 最近的名词 + ... | There are two boys and a girl in the room. |

### 3. 用法与语境

- **用法一**：就近原则——either...or.../neither...nor.../not only...but also... 谓语看**后面**的主语
  - 例：Either my father or my brothers **are** coming.（要么我爸要么我兄弟们会来。——看 brothers）
  - 例：Not only the students but also the teacher **was** surprised.（不仅学生而且老师都很惊讶。——看 teacher）

- **用法二**：就近原则——There be 句型谓语看**最近**的名词
  - 例：There **is** a pen and two books on the desk.（桌上有一支笔和两本书。——看 pen）
  - 例：There **are** two books and a pen on the desk.（桌上有两本书和一支笔。——看 books）

- **用法三**：就远原则——as well as/together with/along with/with/except/besides 等连接时，谓语看**前面**的主语
  - 例：The teacher, as well as the students, **is** interested in the movie.（老师和学生们一样，对这部电影感兴趣。——看 teacher）
  - 例：Nobody but the twins **was** late.（除了双胞胎没人迟到。——看 Nobody）

- **用法四**：特殊名词的主谓一致
  - each/every/either/neither + 单数名词 → 谓语用单数
  - 例：Each student **has** a dictionary.（每个学生都有一本字典。）
  - 例：Neither answer **is** correct.（两个答案都不对。）

### 4. 标志词 / 关键信号

| 标志词/短语 | 原则 | 示例 |
|------------|------|------|
| either...or... | 就近 | Either he or you are to blame. |
| neither...nor... | 就近 | Neither she nor they have finished. |
| not only...but also... | 就近 | Not only Tom but also his parents are here. |
| there be | 就近 | There is a cat and two dogs. |
| as well as | 就远 | She as well as I is a student. |
| together with / along with | 就远 | He together with us is going. |
| except / besides | 就远 | All except him are here. |

### 5. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | Either you or he are wrong. | Either you or he is wrong. | 就近原则，谓语看 he（单数），用 is |
| 2 | He as well as his friends like music. | He as well as his friends likes music. | 就远原则，谓语看 He（单数），用 likes |
| 3 | There are a book and two pens on the desk. | There is a book and two pens on the desk. | 就近原则，看 a book（单数），用 is |
| 4 | The teacher with his students are visiting the museum. | The teacher with his students is visiting the museum. | with 遵循就远原则，看 teacher（单数），用 is |
| 5 | Not only he but also I is a student. | Not only he but also I am a student. | 就近原则，看 I，用 am |

### 逐题解析

**第 1 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：either...or... 就近原则，谓语看 she（三单），用 is。
- **排除法**：B. are 对应 you；C. am 对应 I；D. be 不能直接作谓语。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → 用法一：either...or 就近原则。

**第 2 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：There be 就近原则，最近的名词是 a pen（单数），用 is。
- **排除法**：A. are 对应复数；C/D. there be 不用 has/have。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → 用法二：There be 就近原则。

**第 3 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：as well as 就远原则，谓语看 He（三单），用 studies。
- **排除法**：A. study 主语三单需加 s；C/D 时态不对。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → 用法三：as well as 就远原则。

**第 4 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：neither...nor 就近原则，谓语看 his friends（复数），用 are。
- **排除法**：A. is 对应单数；C. was 时态不对；D. am 对应 I。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → 用法一：neither...nor 就近原则。

**第 5 题：答案 C**
