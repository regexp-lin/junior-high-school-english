# 定语从句——关系副词 where / when / why（Attributive Clauses — Relative Adverbs: where / when / why）

> 日期：2027 年 12 月 1 日 | 语法分类：从句 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

定语从句除了可以用关系代词（who, which, that）引导外，还可以用**关系副词**引导。关系副词有三个：**where**（在……地方）、**when**（在……时候）、**why**（……的原因）。关系副词在从句中作**状语**，同时起连接主句和从句的作用。

### 2. 构成形式

| 关系副词 | 先行词类型 | 在从句中的作用 | 示例 |
|----------|-----------|---------------|------|
| where | 地点名词（place, city, school, house...） | 地点状语 | This is the school where I studied. |
| when | 时间名词（day, time, year, moment...） | 时间状语 | I'll never forget the day when we first met. |
| why | reason | 原因状语 | Tell me the reason why you were late. |

| 关系副词 | = 介词 + 关系代词 | 示例 |
|----------|-------------------|------|
| where | = in/at/on + which | the city where (= in which) he lives |
| when | = in/on/at + which | the day when (= on which) he arrived |
| why | = for which | the reason why (= for which) he left |

### 3. 用法与语境

- **用法一**：where 引导定语从句修饰地点名词
  - 例：This is the park where we often play.（这是我们经常玩耍的公园。）
  - 例：I want to visit the city where my father was born.（我想去我父亲出生的城市看看。）

- **用法二**：when 引导定语从句修饰时间名词
  - 例：I still remember the day when I first came to this school.（我仍然记得第一天来到这所学校的日子。）
  - 例：Spring is the season when flowers begin to bloom.（春天是花朵开始绽放的季节。）

- **用法三**：why 引导定语从句修饰 reason
  - 例：Do you know the reason why he was angry?（你知道他生气的原因吗？）
  - 例：That's the reason why I didn't go to the party.（那就是我没去参加聚会的原因。）

- **用法四**：关系副词与关系代词的区别
  - 关系代词在从句中作**主语或宾语**
  - 关系副词在从句中作**状语**
  - 例（对比）：
    - This is the school **that/which** was built last year.（that 作主语，学校被建造）
    - This is the school **where** I studied.（where 作状语，我在学校学习）

### 4. 标志词 / 关键信号

| 标志词/短语 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------------|------|------|
| 先行词为地点 + 从句缺状语 | 用 where | the room where he works |
| 先行词为时间 + 从句缺状语 | 用 when | the year when he was born |
| 先行词为 reason + 从句缺状语 | 用 why | the reason why he left |
| 先行词为地点/时间 + 从句缺主语或宾语 | 用 that/which | the school that was built |

### 5. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | This is the school where was built last year. | This is the school that/which was built last year. | 从句缺主语用 that/which，不用 where |
| 2 | I remember the day where we met. | I remember the day when we met. | 先行词是时间名词用 when |
| 3 | This is the city when he was born. | This is the city where he was born. | 先行词是地点名词用 where |
| 4 | Tell me the reason when you were late. | Tell me the reason why you were late. | 先行词是 reason 用 why |
| 5 | The house where I bought is very old. | The house that/which I bought is very old. | buy 是及物动词缺宾语，用 that/which 不用 where |
