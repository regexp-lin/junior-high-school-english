# 主谓一致——就近原则与就远原则（Subject-Verb Agreement — Proximity Principle & Distance Principle）

> 日期：2027 年 9 月 6 日 | 语法分类：句型 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

**主谓一致（Subject-Verb Agreement）** 是指句子中 **谓语动词** 在人称和数上必须与 **主语** 保持一致。中考高频考点集中在两大原则：**就近原则**（谓语与 **靠近的主语** 一致）和 **就远原则**（谓语与 **距离较远的真正主语** 一致，忽略中间插入的修饰成分）。

---

### 2. 构成形式

**（1）就近原则（Proximity Principle）——谓语与最近的主语一致**

| 结构 | 谓语一致对象 | 示例 |
|------|------------|------|
| **There be** + A + and + B | 靠近 be 的第一个名词 | There **is** a book and two pens. |
| **Either … or …** | 靠近谓语的名词 | Either you or he **is** right. |
| **Neither … nor …** | 靠近谓语的名词 | Neither he nor I **am** wrong. |
| **Not only … but also …** | 靠近谓语的名词 | Not only Tom but also his friends **like** soccer. |
| **Not … but …** | 靠近谓语的名词（but 后面） | Not I but he **is** to blame. |

**（2）就远原则（Distance Principle）——谓语与前面的主语一致，忽略插入语**

| 结构 | 谓语一致对象 | 示例 |
|------|------------|------|
| 主语 + **with** + 名词 | 前面的主语 | Tom **with** his friends **is** playing. |
| 主语 + **together with** + 名词 | 前面的主语 | The teacher **together with** the students **is** in the hall. |
| 主语 + **as well as** + 名词 | 前面的主语 | She **as well as** her sisters **likes** music. |
| 主语 + **besides / except / including** + 名词 | 前面的主语 | Everyone **except** Tom **has** finished. |
| 主语 + **along with** + 名词 | 前面的主语 | My mother **along with** my aunts **is** coming. |

---

### 3. 用法与语境

- **用法一：There be 句型（就近原则）**
  - 例：There **is** a cat and two dogs in the yard. （院子里有一只猫和两条狗。）
  - 例：There **are** two dogs and a cat in the yard. （院子里有两条狗和一只猫。）

- **用法二：either … or … / neither … nor …（就近原则）**
  - 例：Either my sister or my parents **are** going to pick me up. （我姐姐或者我父母会来接我。）
  - 例：Neither the students nor the teacher **knows** the answer. （学生和老师都不知道答案。）

- **用法三：not only … but also …（就近原则）**
  - 例：Not only he but also I **am** interested in science. （不仅他，我也对科学感兴趣。）
  - 例：Not only the girls but also the boy **wants** to join the club. （不仅女孩们，那个男孩也想加入俱乐部。）

- **用法四：with / together with / as well as 等（就远原则）**
  - 例：The teacher **with** two students **is** visiting the museum. （老师和两个学生正在参观博物馆。）
  - 例：He **as well as** his classmates **likes** playing basketball. （他和他的同学们都喜欢打篮球。）

- **用法五：except / besides / including 等（就远原则）**
  - 例：All students **except** Jack **have** passed the exam. （除了杰克，所有学生都通过了考试。）
  - 例：Nobody **besides** her **knows** the truth. （除了她，没人知道真相。）

---

### 4. 标志词 / 关键信号

| 标志词/短语 | 原则 | 示例 |
|------------|------|------|
| there be | 就近 | There **are** some books and a pen on the desk. |
| either … or … | 就近 | Either you or she **has** to go. |
| neither … nor … | 就近 | Neither Tom nor his brothers **were** at home. |
| not only … but also … | 就近 | Not only I but also he **is** a student. |
| with / together with / as well as | 就远 | The boy with his dogs **is** running. |
| besides / except / including | 就远 | Everyone except him **wants** to go. |

---

### 5. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | There **are** a book and two pens on the desk. | There **is** a book and two pens on the desk. | There be 就近原则：a book 是单数，用 **is**。 |
| 2 | Tom with his friends **are** playing. | Tom with his friends **is** playing. | with 是就远原则，主语是 Tom（单数），用 **is**。 |
| 3 | Neither he nor I **is** wrong. | Neither he nor I **am** wrong. | 就近原则，靠近的是 **I**，用 **am**。 |
| 4 | She as well as her sisters **like** music. | She as well as her sisters **likes** music. | as well as 就远原则，主语是 She（三单），用 **likes**。 |
| 5 | Not only Tom but also his friends **likes** soccer. | Not only Tom but also his friends **like** soccer. | 就近原则，靠近的是 his friends（复数），用 **like**。 |

### 逐题解析

**第 1 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：There be 就近原则，靠近的是 **a pen**（单数），用 **is**。
- **排除法**：A **are** 不合就近；C、D 不用于 There be。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → **用法一**（There be 就近原则）；**易错点 1**。

**第 2 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：**Either … or …** 就近原则，靠近的是 **Tom**（三单），用 **has**。
- **排除法**：A **have** 与 Tom 不一致；C、D 形式不对。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → **用法二**（either … or …）。

**第 3 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：**with** 就远原则，真正主语是 **The teacher**（三单），用 **is**。
- **排除法**：B **are** 误随 students；C、D 与主语不匹配。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → **用法四**（with 就远原则）；**易错点 2**。

**第 4 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：**Neither … nor …** 就近原则，靠近的是 **I**，用 **am**。
- **排除法**：A **is** 不合；B **are** 不合 I；D 原形。
- **考查知识点**：知识点精讲 → **用法二**；**易错点 3**。

**第 5 题：答案 B**
