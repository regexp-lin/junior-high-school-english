# 不定式——作目的状语与结果状语（Infinitive — as Adverbial of Purpose & Result: too...to / enough to）

> 日期：2027 年 11 月 26 日 | 语法分类：非谓语 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

不定式（to + 动词原形）除了可以作主语和宾语外，还可以作**目的状语**和**结果状语**。目的状语表示"为了做某事"，结果状语表示动作产生的结果。中考常考的三个固定句型是：**in order to**（为了）、**too...to**（太……以至于不能）、**enough to**（足够……可以）。

### 2. 构成形式

| 用法 | 结构 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 目的状语 | to do / in order to do | 为了做…… | He got up early to catch the bus. |
| 目的状语（否定） | in order not to do | 为了不…… | She left early in order not to be late. |
| 结果状语（否定结果） | too + adj./adv. + to do | 太……以至于不能…… | He is too young to go to school. |
| 结果状语（肯定结果） | adj./adv. + enough + to do | 足够……可以…… | She is old enough to look after herself. |
| 结果状语 | so + adj./adv. + that... | 如此……以至于…… | He ran so fast that I couldn't catch him. |

| 句型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定目的 | 主语 + 谓语 + to do | I came here to see you. |
| 否定目的 | 主语 + 谓语 + in order not to do | He spoke quietly in order not to wake the baby. |
| too...to（否定结果） | 主语 + be/动词 + too + adj./adv. + to do | The box is too heavy to carry. |
| enough to（肯定结果） | 主语 + be/动词 + adj./adv. + enough + to do | He is strong enough to lift the box. |

### 3. 用法与语境

- **用法一**：不定式作目的状语，表"为了"
  - 例：She studied hard to pass the exam.（她努力学习是为了通过考试。）
  - 例：He went to the library to borrow some books.（他去图书馆是为了借书。）

- **用法二**：in order to / in order not to 更正式地表达目的
  - 例：In order to improve my English, I read English novels every day.（为了提高英语，我每天读英文小说。）
  - 例：She turned off her phone in order not to be disturbed.（她关掉手机以免被打扰。）

- **用法三**：too...to 表"太……以至于不能"（含否定意义）
  - 例：The baby is too young to dress himself.（这个婴儿太小了，不能自己穿衣服。）
  - 例：It's too dark to see anything.（太黑了，什么也看不见。）

- **用法四**：enough to 表"足够……可以"（enough 放在形容词/副词后面）
  - 例：He is tall enough to reach the shelf.（他够高，能够到架子。）
  - 例：She spoke loudly enough for everyone to hear.（她说得够大声，大家都能听到。）

### 4. 标志词 / 关键信号

| 标志词/短语 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------------|------|------|
| to do（动词前） | 目的状语 | He ran fast to catch the train. |
| in order to | 为了（正式） | In order to save money, he walks to work. |
| too + adj. + to | 太……不能 | too tired to walk |
| adj. + enough + to | 足够……可以 | good enough to eat |
| so...that / such...that | 如此……以至于 | so heavy that I can't carry it |

### 5. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | He is too young to not go to school. | He is too young to go to school. | too...to 本身含否定意义，不需要再加 not |
| 2 | She is enough old to drive. | She is old enough to drive. | enough 放在形容词/副词后面，不是前面 |
| 3 | He is too tired to can't walk. | He is too tired to walk. | too...to 已含否定，to 后接原形动词 |
| 4 | In order to not be late, she left early. | In order not to be late, she left early. | not 放在 to 前面，不是 in order to 中间 |
| 5 | The problem is enough easy to solve. | The problem is easy enough to solve. | enough 修饰形容词时放在后面 |
