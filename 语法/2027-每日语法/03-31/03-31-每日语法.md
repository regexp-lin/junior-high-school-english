# 从句——同位语从句初探（Appositive Clauses — Introduction）

> 日期：2027 年 03 月 31 日 | 语法分类：从句 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

**同位语从句（appositive clause）** 是对前面的名词（通常是抽象名词）进行**解释说明**的从句，说明该名词的**具体内容**。

- The news **that our team won** made us excited.（我们队赢了这个消息让我们兴奋。）

同位语从句中 that 引导的从句解释了 news 的具体内容——"我们队赢了"。

---

### 2. 基本结构

**抽象名词 + that + 完整句子**

| 成分 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 先行名词 | the news / the fact / the idea |
| 连词 | that（最常见）、whether、how、when、where 等 |
| 从句 | 完整句子（that 不作成分，不可省略） |

---

### 3. 常接同位语从句的名词

| 名词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **news** | 消息 | The news **that he passed the exam** is true. |
| **fact** | 事实 | The fact **that the earth is round** is known to all. |
| **idea** | 想法 | I have no idea **when he will come**. |
| **hope** | 希望 | There is hope **that he will recover**. |
| **belief** | 信念 | The belief **that hard work pays off** is important. |
| **thought** | 想法 | The thought **that she might fail** worried her. |
| **suggestion** | 建议 | His suggestion **that we start early** was accepted. |
| **question** | 问题 | The question **whether he is honest** remains. |
| **doubt** | 怀疑 | There is no doubt **that he is right**. |
| **promise** | 承诺 | He made a promise **that he would help us**. |
| **truth** | 真相 | The truth **that he was lying** shocked everyone. |
| **possibility** | 可能性 | There is a possibility **that it will rain**. |
| **order** | 命令 | The order **that all students should attend** was given. |
| **demand** | 要求 | The demand **that he apologize** was reasonable. |
| **information** | 信息 | The information **that the meeting was canceled** came late. |

---

### 4. 引导词的选择

| 引导词 | 用法 | 示例 |
|--------|------|------|
| **that** | 最常见，从句完整，不作成分 | The fact **that** he is honest is clear. |
| **whether** | 表"是否" | The question **whether** he will come is unclear. |
| **when** | 表"什么时候" | I have no idea **when** he left. |
| **where** | 表"在哪里" | The question **where** we should go is hard. |
| **how** | 表"如何" | The problem **how** to solve it troubles us. |
| **why** | 表"为什么" | The reason **why** he left is unknown. |
| **who** | 表"谁" | The question **who** did it remains unsolved. |
| **what** | 表"什么" | I have no idea **what** he said. |

**注意：** that 引导同位语从句时**不能省略**。

---

### 5. 同位语从句 vs. 定语从句

这是中考/高考重要考点。两者都跟在名词后面，但本质不同：

| 对比项 | 同位语从句 | 定语从句 |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| 功能 | 解释名词的**内容** | 修饰/限定名词 |
| that 的作用 | 仅是连词，**不作成分** | 是关系代词，**作成分**（主语/宾语） |
| that 能否省略 | **不能**省略 | 作宾语时**可以**省略 |
| 等价关系 | 名词 = 从句内容 | 名词 ≠ 从句 |

**对比示例：**

| 类型 | 句子 | 分析 |
|------|------|------|
| 同位语从句 | The news **that** our team won excited us. | that 不作成分；news 的内容 = our team won |
| 定语从句 | The news **that** he told me was exciting. | that 作 told 的宾语；news ≠ he told me |

**判断技巧：** 把 that 从句去掉看名词意义是否完整。同位语从句去掉后名词含义不完整；定语从句去掉后名词含义仍完整。

---

### 6. 易错点总结

| 序号 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 | 错因分析 |
|------|---------|---------|----------|
| 1 | The news he won made us happy. | The news **that** he won made us happy. | 同位语从句 that 不可省 |
| 2 | I have no idea if he will come. | I have no idea **whether** he will come. | idea 后用 whether 不用 if |
| 3 | The fact which he is honest is clear. | The fact **that** he is honest is clear. | 同位语从句用 that 不用 which |
| 4 | There is no doubt whether he is right. | There is no doubt **that** he is right. | no doubt 后用 that |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

**说明：第 1～10 题为中等难度；第 11～20 题为拔高题。请选出每题的最佳答案。**

**1.** The news ______ he passed the exam made us happy.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. who

**2.** There is no doubt ______ he will succeed.

A. whether　　B. if　　C. what　　D. that

**3.** I have no idea ______ he will come or not.

A. that　　B. whether　　C. if　　D. what

**4.** The fact ______ the earth goes around the sun is known to all.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. if

**5.** He made a promise ______ he would help me.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. who

**6.** The question ______ we should go there has not been decided.

A. that　　B. whether　　C. what　　D. which

**7.** I have no idea ______ he lives.

A. that　　B. which　　C. what　　D. where

**8.** The thought ______ he might fail worried him a lot.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. where

**9.** Word came ______ our team had won the championship.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. whether

**10.** There is a possibility ______ it will rain tomorrow.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. whether

**11.** The news ______ he told me yesterday was surprising. (定语从句)

A. what　　B. that　　C. /　　D. Both B and C

**12.** The news ______ our school will have a sports meeting is exciting. (同位语从句)

A. what　　B. which　　C. that　　D. /

**13.** His suggestion ______ we start early was accepted by everyone.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. whether

**14.** We heard the news ______ our teacher would go abroad.

A. which　　B. /　　C. what　　D. that

**15.** The question ______ did it remains a mystery.

A. that　　B. who　　C. which　　D. what

**16.** I'm glad at the news ______ he is coming.

A. what　　B. which　　C. that　　D. /

**17.** The information ______ the meeting has been put off hasn't been confirmed.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. whether

**18.** The doctor gave him the advice ______ he should give up smoking.

A. what　　B. which　　C. that　　D. /

**19.** There is some doubt ______ he will come on time.

A. that　　B. whether　　C. what　　D. which

**20.** The order ______ all students must attend the meeting was announced.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. /

### 逐题解析

**第 1 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：news 后接同位语从句，解释消息的内容，用 **that**。从句 he passed the exam 完整，that 不作成分。
- **排除法**：A which 用于定语从句；B what 不合；D who 不合。
- **考查知识点**：同位语从句用 that。

**第 2 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：There is no doubt **that**...（毫无疑问……）是固定搭配。
- **排除法**：A/B 不合 no doubt 后的搭配；C 不合。
- **考查知识点**：no doubt + that。

**第 3 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：or not 提示用 **whether**——whether he will come or not。
- **排除法**：A that 不合；C if 不可与 or not 直接搭配（if...or not 不常用）；D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：whether...or not。

**第 4 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：fact 后接同位语从句用 **that**——the fact that the earth goes around the sun。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：fact + that 同位语从句。

**第 5 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：promise 后接同位语从句用 **that**——a promise that he would help me。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：promise + that。

**第 6 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：表"是否"用 **whether**——whether we should go there。
- **排除法**：A that 不表是否；C/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：question + whether。

**第 7 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：表"在哪里住"用 **where**——where he lives。
- **排除法**：A/B/C 不合语义。
- **考查知识点**：idea + where。

**第 8 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：thought 后接同位语从句用 **that**——the thought that he might fail。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：thought + that。

**第 9 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：Word came that...（消息传来……）是固定句式，word 后接同位语从句。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：Word came that...（同位语从句后置）。

**第 10 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：possibility 后接同位语从句用 **that**。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：possibility + that。

**第 11 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：此处是**定语从句**——the news that he told me（that 作 told 的宾语，可省略）。that 和省略 that（/）都可以。
- **排除法**：A what 不引导定语从句。
- **考查知识点**：定语从句中 that 作宾语可省略。

**第 12 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：此处是**同位语从句**——the news that our school will have a sports meeting。同位语从句 that 不可省略。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合（特别是 D 不可省略）。
- **考查知识点**：同位语从句 that 不可省略。

**第 13 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：suggestion 后接同位语从句用 **that**。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：suggestion + that。

**第 14 题：答案 D**

- **解析**：news 后接同位语从句用 **that**——the news that our teacher would go abroad。
- **排除法**：A which 用于定语从句；B 同位语从句 that 不可省；C 不合。
- **考查知识点**：news + that（同位语从句）。

**第 15 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：question 的同位语从句中缺主语（谁做的），用 **who**。
- **排除法**：A that 不作成分但此处缺主语；C/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：who 引导同位语从句（作主语）。

**第 16 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：news 后接同位语从句用 **that**——the news that he is coming。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：news + that。

**第 17 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：information 后接同位语从句用 **that**。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：information + that。

**第 18 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：advice 后接同位语从句用 **that**——the advice that he should give up smoking。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：advice + that。

**第 19 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：some doubt 后用 **whether**（有疑虑用 whether，没有疑虑 no doubt 用 that）。
- **排除法**：A that 用于 no doubt；C/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：some doubt + whether vs. no doubt + that。

**第 20 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：order 后接同位语从句用 **that**——the order that all students must attend。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：order + that。
