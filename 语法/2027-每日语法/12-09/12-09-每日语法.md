# 形容词和副词——原级比较 as...as（Adjectives & Adverbs — Positive Degree Comparison: as...as）

> 日期：2027 年 12 月 9 日 | 语法分类：形容词和副词 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

原级比较（Positive Degree Comparison）用 **as + 形容词/副词原级 + as** 表示"和……一样"，用 **not as/so + 形容词/副词原级 + as** 表示"不如……"。这是形容词和副词三种比较等级（原级、比较级、最高级）中最基础的一种，中考常与比较级、最高级的互换表达一起考查。

### 2. 构成形式

| 结构 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| as + adj./adv. + as | 和……一样 | He is as tall as his father. |
| not as/so + adj./adv. + as | 不如…… | She is not as/so tall as her brother. |
| as + adj. + a/an + 名词 + as | 和……一样的 | He is as good a student as Tom. |
| 倍数 + as + adj./adv. + as | ……是……的几倍 | This room is three times as big as that one. |

| 常见搭配 | 含义 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| as...as possible | 尽可能…… | Run as fast as possible. |
| as...as + 主语 + can | 尽可能…… | Run as fast as you can. |
| as soon as | 一……就…… | I'll call you as soon as I arrive. |
| as long as | 只要 | As long as you work hard, you'll succeed. |
| as well as | 和……一样好；也 | She speaks English as well as French. |

### 3. 用法与语境

- **用法一**：肯定句中 as...as 表"和……一样"
  - 例：Tom is as clever as Jack.（汤姆和杰克一样聪明。）
  - 例：She runs as fast as her sister.（她跑得和她姐姐一样快。）

- **用法二**：否定句中 not as/so...as 表"不如……"
  - 例：This book is not as/so interesting as that one.（这本书不如那本有趣。）
  - 例：He doesn't run as fast as Tom.（他跑得不如汤姆快。）

- **用法三**：as...as possible / as...as one can 表"尽可能"
  - 例：Please speak as slowly as possible.（请尽可能慢地说。）
  - 例：He tried to run as fast as he could.（他尽力跑得尽可能快。）

- **用法四**：原级比较与比较级的互换
  - A is not as tall as B. = B is taller than A.（A 不如 B 高。= B 比 A 高。）
  - 例：Tom is not as old as Jack. = Jack is older than Tom.

- **用法五**：倍数表达
  - 例：This bridge is twice as long as that one.（这座桥是那座的两倍长。）
  - 例：He has three times as many books as I do.（他的书是我的三倍多。）

### 4. 标志词 / 关键信号

| 标志词/短语 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------------|------|------|
| as...as | 和……一样 | as big as |
| not as/so...as | 不如…… | not as good as |
| as...as possible | 尽可能…… | as early as possible |
| as...as one can | 尽可能…… | as quickly as I can |
| twice/three times as...as | 倍数 | twice as heavy as |

### 5. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | He is as taller as his father. | He is as tall as his father. | as...as 中间用原级，不用比较级 |
| 2 | She is not as beautiful so Mary. | She is not as/so beautiful as Mary. | 第二个 as 不能换成 so |
| 3 | He runs as fastly as Tom. | He runs as fast as Tom. | fast 的副词形式仍是 fast，不是 fastly |
| 4 | This box is as heavier as that one. | This box is as heavy as that one. | as...as 中间用原级 |
| 5 | He is as a good student as Tom. | He is as good a student as Tom. | as + adj. + a/an + 名词 + as 的语序 |
