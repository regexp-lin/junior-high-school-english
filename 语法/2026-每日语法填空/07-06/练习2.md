# 07-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园节水行动 (Water Conservation at School)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Water is precious, but many of us waste it every day without (1) ______ (think). To raise awareness, our school launched a "Save Water" campaign last March. Posters (2) ______ (put) up around the campus, reminding students to turn off taps after (3) ______ (wash) their hands. The science club also gave a presentation (4) ______ how water is cleaned and delivered to our homes. I was shocked to learn that it takes a lot of energy (5) ______ (produce) clean drinking water. In some dry areas of the world, people have to walk for (6) ______ (hour) just to get a bucket of water. Compared with them, we are much (7) ______ (lucky). Since the campaign started, our school (8) ______ (save) about 20 percent of its water use. Some students have even come up (9) ______ clever ideas, such as collecting rainwater for the school garden. I believe that if everyone does a little, together we can make (10) ______ big difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：thinking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`without` 为介词，后接动名词 `thinking`。

2. **答案：were put**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。海报"被张贴"，`last March` 表示过去，主语 `Posters` 为复数，用 `were put`。

3. **答案：washing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`after` 为介词，后接动名词 `washing`。

4. **答案：on / about**
   - **考点**：介词。`a presentation on/about sth` 表示"关于……的报告"。

5. **答案：to produce**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It takes + 名词 + to do sth` 为固定句型，`it` 为形式主语，不定式为真正主语。

6. **答案：hours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`for hours` 表示"好几个小时"，`hour` 用复数。

7. **答案：luckier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`Compared with them`（与他们相比）暗含比较，用 `lucky` 的比较级 `luckier`。

8. **答案：has saved**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since the campaign started` 提示用现在完成时；`our school` 为第三人称单数，用 `has saved`。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`come up with` 为固定搭配，表示"想出（主意）"。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。`make a big difference` 为固定搭配，表示"产生很大影响"。
