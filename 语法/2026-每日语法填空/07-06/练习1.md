# 07-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：参观博物馆 (Visiting a Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, our history teacher took us to the city museum. The museum, which (1) ______ (open) in 2005, has three floors and more than ten thousand exhibits. On the first floor, we saw ancient pottery and bronze tools that (2) ______ (use) by people thousands of years ago. I was amazed at how (3) ______ (skill) the ancient craftsmen were. The second floor was about modern history. A guide explained the stories behind the old (4) ______ (photograph) on the wall. Some of them were taken during very (5) ______ (difficulty) times, and they reminded us to value the peaceful life we have today. The most popular area was on the third floor, (6) ______ visitors could try on traditional costumes and take pictures. My friend Li Wei dressed up (7) ______ an ancient scholar and looked very funny. We all laughed (8) ______ (loud) at him. After the visit, I wrote a report about (9) ______ I had seen and learned. The trip made me (10) ______ (real) interested in Chinese history.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was opened / opened**
   - **考点**：被动语态/一般过去时。博物馆"于2005年开放"，可用被动 `was opened`，也可将 `open` 视为不及物动词用主动形式 `opened`。

2. **答案：were used**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。工具"被人们使用"，`thousands of years ago` 表示过去，主语 `tools` 为复数，用 `were used`。

3. **答案：skilful / skillful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`how + 形容词 + 主语 + 系动词` 为感叹句结构，`skill` 变为形容词 `skilful`（熟练的）。

4. **答案：photographs**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`old` 修饰可数名词，根据语境为多张照片，用复数 `photographs`。

5. **答案：difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `times` 需要形容词，`difficulty` 变为 `difficult`（困难的）。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the third floor`（地点），在非限制性定语从句中作地点状语，用 `where`。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`dress up as` 表示"装扮成……"。

8. **答案：loudly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `laughed` 需要副词，`loud` 变为 `loudly`（大声地）。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。引导宾语从句，`what` 在从句中作 `seen and learned` 的宾语，表示"所见所学"。

10. **答案：really**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `interested` 需要副词，`real` 变为 `really`（真正地）。
