# 07-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国茶文化 (Chinese Tea Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China is the birthplace of tea, and tea culture has a history of over 4,000 years. According to an old legend, tea (1) ______ (discover) by Emperor Shen Nong when some leaves fell into his boiling water. He found the drink both refreshing and (2) ______ (delight). Since then, drinking tea (3) ______ (become) a daily habit for Chinese people. There are many kinds of tea, (4) ______ (include) green tea, black tea, and oolong tea. Different regions are famous for different (5) ______ (variety). For example, Hangzhou is well known (6) ______ its Longjing tea. Making tea is an art in itself. The water should not be too hot, (7) ______ it will destroy the delicate flavour. In a traditional tea ceremony, the host pours tea for guests as a sign of (8) ______ (polite) and respect. My grandfather is a tea lover. He drinks at (9) ______ three cups of green tea every day. He believes tea helps him think more (10) ______ (clear) and stay calm. Tea is not just a drink — it is a bridge that connects the past and the present.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was discovered**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。茶"被发现"，叙述历史传说，主语 `tea` 为单数。

2. **答案：delightful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `refreshing` 并列作宾语补足语，`delight` 变为形容词 `delightful`（令人愉悦的）。

3. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 提示用现在完成时，表示从过去延续到现在的状态。

4. **答案：including**
   - **考点**：现在分词/介词。`including` 作介词用，表示"包括"，后面列举具体种类。

5. **答案：varieties**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different` 修饰可数名词复数，`variety` 变为 `varieties`。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be well known for` 表示"因……而闻名"。

7. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`or` 表示"否则"，相当于 `otherwise`，表示如果水太热就会破坏风味。

8. **答案：politeness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`of` 后接名词，`polite` 变为 `politeness`（礼貌）。

9. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`at least` 表示"至少"。

10. **答案：clearly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `think` 需要副词，`clear` 变为 `clearly`。
