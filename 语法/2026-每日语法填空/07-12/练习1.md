# 07-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：养宠物的好处 (Benefits of Keeping Pets)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Keeping a pet can bring many benefits to a family. My family adopted a golden retriever (1) ______ (name) Lucky two years ago, and he has since become an important member of our household. Every evening, I take him for a walk, (2) ______ keeps me active and healthy. Doctors say that people (3) ______ own pets tend to have lower stress levels. Playing with Lucky always makes me (4) ______ (forget) my worries after a long day at school. Pets also teach children about (5) ______ (responsible). I have to feed Lucky on time, give him clean water, and take him to the vet (6) ______ (regular). At first, I found it tiring, but now I enjoy (7) ______ (look) after him. The (8) ______ (happy) moment of my day is when Lucky runs to the door to welcome me home. His tail wags so fast that his whole body shakes. Of course, keeping a pet requires patience and effort. You should think (9) ______ (careful) before deciding to get one. But if you are ready, a pet can be the (10) ______ (good) friend you will ever have.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a golden retriever named Lucky` 表示"一只叫 Lucky 的金毛犬"，过去分词表被动。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面的事情（遛狗），在从句中作主语，用 `which`。

3. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `people`（人），在定语从句中作主语，用 `who` 或 `that`。

4. **答案：forget**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`make sb + 动词原形` 为使役动词结构。

5. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`about` 为介词，后接名词；`responsible` 变为 `responsibility`（责任感）。

6. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `take` 需要副词，`regular` 变为 `regularly`（定期地）。

7. **答案：looking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`enjoy doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"喜欢做某事"。

8. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词` 表示"最……的"。

9. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `think` 需要副词，`careful` 变为 `carefully`。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词`，`good` 的最高级为 `best`。
