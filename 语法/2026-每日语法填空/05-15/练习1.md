# 05-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：博物馆之夜 (A Night at the Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, I took part in a special event called "A Night at the Museum". For one night only, the city museum stayed open until midnight, and visitors of 1. ________ ages were welcome.

When I arrived at seven in the evening, a long line had already formed outside the gate. Inside, the halls were lit 2. ________ (soft), and gentle music played in the background. A guide led us through the ancient civilization exhibition and explained how tools 3. ________ (use) thousands of years ago.

The most exciting part was the science zone. We were allowed to try simple experiments 4. ________ (we). I mixed liquids carefully and watched colors change — it felt like magic, 5. ________ it was real science.

At ten o'clock, everyone gathered in the lecture hall. A speaker shared stories about 6. ________ (discover) hidden in old paintings. I took notes quickly because I didn't want to forget 7. ________ interesting ideas.

By the time I left, I was tired 8. ________ satisfied. That night, history and science seemed closer to daily life than ever before. If I 9. ________ (have) another chance, I would certainly go again. Events like this help young people see museums not 10. ________ boring places, but as lively classrooms.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **all**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。of all ages 表示"各个年龄段的"，修饰 visitors。

2. **softly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 lit（此处为被动，厅内被柔和地照亮），用副词 softly。

3. **were used**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般过去时的被动语态）。tools 与 use 为被动关系，thousands of years ago 表过去，用 were used。

4. **ourselves**
   - **考点**：代词（反身代词）。try experiments ourselves 表示"亲自尝试实验"，与主语 We 呼应，用 ourselves。

5. **but**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。it felt like magic 与 it was real science 为转折，用 but。

6. **discoveries**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。介词 about 后接名词，discover → discovery，此处表泛指多个发现，用复数 discoveries。

7. **any**
   - **考点**：限定词（无提示词填空）。在否定意义的 want to forget 之后，用 any 修饰 interesting ideas，表示"任何有意思的观点都不想漏掉"。

8. **but**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。tired but satisfied 累但满足，转折关系。

9. **had**
   - **考点**：虚拟语气（与将来事实相反或与现在愿望）。If I had another chance, I would... 表示与现在事实相反的假设，从句用一般过去时 had。

10. **as**
    - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。see...not as...but as... 把……看作……而不是……，固定结构。
