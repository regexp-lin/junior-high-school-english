# 05-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：养成阅读习惯 (Building a Reading Habit)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Reading opens doors to new worlds, 1. ________ building a reading habit is not always easy. Many students say they are too busy 2. ________ (pick) up a book after school.

Here is 3. ________ advice that worked for me. First, I set aside twenty minutes before bed — no phone, just a story. Second, I chose books that matched my interests instead of 4. ________ (follow) others' lists blindly. Third, I kept a small notebook 5. ________ (write) down new words and beautiful sentences.

In the beginning, I could only read a few pages without feeling sleepy. 6. ________ I stuck to my plan, day after day. Two months later, I finished my first novel and felt a strong sense of achievement. Now reading has become part of my routine, 7. ________ brushing my teeth.

My parents have noticed the change, too. They bought me 8. ________ bookshelf and often discuss books with me at dinner. Teachers say my writing has improved because I have been exposed to 9. ________ (vary) styles of language.

If you want to start, choose 10. ________ easy book today and read just one page. Small steps lead to big habits.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **but**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。Reading opens doors 与 building a habit is not easy 为转折关系，用 but 连接两个分句。

2. **to pick**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。too...to... 太……而不能……，too busy to pick up a book。

3. **some**
   - **考点**：限定词（无提示词填空）。advice 不可数，Here is some advice 表示"一些建议"，用 some。

4. **following**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。instead of 为介词短语，后接动名词 following。

5. **to write**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。kept a notebook to write down... 表示"为了记下……而准备笔记本"，用不定式表目的。

6. **But**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。上文讲困难，本句讲坚持，转折用 But；句首大写。

7. **like**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。like brushing my teeth 表示"就像刷牙一样"，用 like 表类比。

8. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。bookshelf 可数单数，表泛指"一个书架"，用 a。

9. **various**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。vary → various，修饰名词 styles，表示接触多种语言风格。

10. **an**
    - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。easy 以元音音素开头，an easy book。
