# 09-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园安全教育 (Campus Safety Education)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Safety is always the top priority at our school. Last Wednesday, we had a special safety education class (1) ______ (teach) by a local firefighter. He began by showing us a short video about fire (2) ______ (prevent). The video explained how most fires in schools (3) ______ (cause) by careless use of electricity. He then demonstrated the correct way to use a fire extinguisher and taught us the basic steps of (4) ______ (escape) from a burning building. "The most important thing is to stay (5) ______," he said. "Panic makes everything (6) ______ (bad)." After the talk, the whole school took part (7) ______ a fire drill. When the alarm sounded, all students walked quickly and (8) ______ (order) to the playground. We gathered at the designated meeting point within three minutes, which (9) ______ (consider) a good time by the firefighter. He praised us for being well-prepared and reminded us that (10) ______ (practise) regularly is the key to staying safe in an emergency. I learned a lot from this event and now feel more confident about what to do if a fire breaks out.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：taught**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a class taught by a firefighter`，过去分词作后置定语，表被动。

2. **答案：prevention**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`fire prevention`，名词修饰名词，`prevent` 变为 `prevention`。

3. **答案：are caused**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`fires are caused by...`，火灾"被引起"，描述一般事实用一般现在时。

4. **答案：escaping**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词 `escaping`。

5. **答案：calm**
   - **考点**：形容词。`stay calm`，系动词后接形容词，表"保持冷静"。

6. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：比较级。`makes everything worse`，`bad` 的比较级为 `worse`。

7. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`take part in` 为固定搭配，表"参加"。

8. **答案：orderly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `quickly` 并列修饰动词 `walked`，`order` 变为副词/形容词 `orderly`（有序地）。

9. **答案：was considered**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`which was considered a good time`，"被认为是"。

10. **答案：practising**
    - **考点**：动名词。动名词 `practising` 作主语。
