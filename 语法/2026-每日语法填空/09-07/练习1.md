# 09-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：剪纸艺术 (Paper-Cutting Art)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Paper-cutting is a traditional Chinese folk art with a history of more (1) ______ 1,500 years. Using just a pair of scissors and a piece of red paper, skilled artists can create (2) ______ (beauty) patterns of flowers, animals and human figures. In 2009, Chinese paper-cutting (3) ______ (add) to UNESCO's list of Intangible Cultural Heritage. My aunt is a paper-cutting artist who (4) ______ (learn) the craft from her grandmother. She can finish a complex design in just a few (5) ______ (minute). Last Spring Festival, she made paper-cuttings for every household in our neighbourhood. The most popular design was a pair of fish, (6) ______ symbolises abundance and good luck in Chinese culture. She also taught a free workshop at the community centre, (7) ______ (attract) over fifty participants. I attended the workshop and tried (8) ______ (cut) a simple butterfly shape. It was harder than I (9) ______ (think)! My butterfly looked more like a moth, but my aunt encouraged me (10) ______ (keep) practising. She says that anyone can learn paper-cutting with patience and dedication.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。`more than 1,500 years` 表示"超过1500年"。

2. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `patterns` 需要形容词，`beauty` 变为 `beautiful`。

3. **答案：was added**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`paper-cutting was added to...`，被列入名单，用被动语态。

4. **答案：learned / learnt**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去学习的事实，用一般过去时。

5. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few minutes`，`a few` 修饰可数名词复数。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，先行词为 `a pair of fish`。

7. **答案：attracting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`attracting over fifty participants` 作结果状语。

8. **答案：to cut**
   - **考点**：不定式。`try to do sth` 表示"尝试做某事"。

9. **答案：thought / had thought**
   - **考点**：时态。`harder than I thought`，比较从句中用一般过去时或过去完成时。

10. **答案：to keep**
    - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb to do sth` 表示"鼓励某人做某事"。
