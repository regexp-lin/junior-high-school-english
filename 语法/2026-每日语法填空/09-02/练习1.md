# 09-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：网络购物 (Online Shopping)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Online shopping has become a part of everyday life in China. With just a few (1) ______ (click), you can buy almost anything you need without leaving home. My mother is a big fan of online shopping. She (2) ______ (buy) clothes, food and household items online for years. She says it is much (3) ______ (easy) to compare prices on the internet than in physical shops. During last year's "Double Eleven" shopping festival, she stayed up until midnight (4) ______ (wait) for the sales to begin. She managed to get several items (5) ______ half price. However, online shopping also has its problems. Sometimes the products look different (6) ______ what they appear in the photos. My mother once ordered a dress that looked beautiful on the screen, (7) ______ when it arrived, the colour and material were completely different. She had to return it, (8) ______ was both time-consuming and disappointing. To shop (9) ______ (wise) online, we should read customer reviews carefully and choose sellers (10) ______ good ratings. It is also important to protect our personal information when making payments.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：clicks**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few clicks`，`a few` 修饰可数名词复数。

2. **答案：has been buying / has bought**
   - **考点**：时态。`for years` 提示用现在完成时或现在完成进行时。

3. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much easier than`，`much` 修饰比较级。

4. **答案：waiting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`stayed up until midnight waiting...`，现在分词作伴随状语。

5. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。`at half price` 表示"以半价"。

6. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`look different from` 表示"看起来与……不同"。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`looked beautiful...but...the colour was different`，前后转折用 `but`。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面"退货"这件事。

9. **答案：wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `shop` 需要副词，`wise` 变为 `wisely`。

10. **答案：with**
    - **考点**：介词。`sellers with good ratings` 表示"有好评的卖家"，`with` 表示"具有"。
