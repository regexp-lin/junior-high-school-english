# 09-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校合唱团 (School Choir)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I have been a member of our school choir (1) ______ two years. Singing in a choir is very different from singing alone. You have to listen to (2) ______ (other) carefully and match your voice with theirs. Our choir teacher, Ms. Li, is very strict but also very (3) ______ (support). She always pushes us to do our best and (4) ______ (praise) us when we improve. Last term, we entered a city-wide singing competition. We practised every day after school for three (5) ______ (week). The song we chose was a traditional Chinese folk song (6) ______ had been rearranged with modern harmonies. On the day of the competition, I was so nervous that my hands (7) ______ (shake) before we went on stage. But once the music started, all my nervousness disappeared. We sang (8) ______ (beautiful) together, and the audience gave us a standing ovation. In the end, we won second place, (9) ______ was the best result our school had ever achieved. The experience taught me that when people work together with the same goal, they can create something truly (10) ______ (amaze).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for two years` 表示持续时间，与现在完成时搭配。

2. **答案：others**
   - **考点**：代词。`listen to others`，`others` 指"其他人"。

3. **答案：supportive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `strict` 并列作表语，需要形容词，`support` 变为 `supportive`。

4. **答案：praises**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。与 `pushes` 并列，主语为 `She`（第三人称单数），用 `praises`。

5. **答案：weeks**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`three weeks`，数词后接复数名词。

6. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a folk song`（物），引导定语从句用 `that` 或 `which`。

7. **答案：were shaking**
   - **考点**：时态。`my hands were shaking` 用过去进行时，表示上台前手一直在发抖。

8. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `sang` 需要副词，`beautiful` 变为 `beautifully`。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，指代获得第二名这件事。

10. **答案：amazing**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`something truly amazing`，不定代词后接形容词作后置定语，`amaze` 变为 `amazing`。
