# 11-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：骑行文化 (Cycling Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Cycling has become one of the most popular ways to get around in Chinese cities. With the rise of bike-sharing services, it is now (1) ______ (easy) than ever to find a bicycle when you need one. You simply scan a code on your phone, unlock the bike and start (2) ______ (ride). When you arrive at your destination, you can park it at any designated spot. The (3) ______ (convenient) of these services has attracted millions of users. Cycling is not only a practical means of transport (4) ______ also a great form of exercise. Doctors recommend cycling because it (5) ______ (strengthen) the heart, improves blood circulation and helps control weight. Unlike running, cycling puts less pressure on the joints, (6) ______ makes it suitable for people of all ages. Last autumn, my family went on a cycling trip along the countryside roads near our city. We rode for about 30 (7) ______ (kilometre) and stopped at a small village for lunch. The fresh air, the golden rice fields and the peaceful atmosphere made me (8) ______ (feel) completely relaxed. It was one of the (9) ______ (enjoy) experiences I have ever had. Now I cycle to school every day instead of taking the bus. It takes a little (10) ______ (long), but I enjoy the morning exercise and the feeling of freedom.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`easier than ever` 提示比较级。

2. **答案：riding**
   - **考点**：动名词。`start doing sth` 表示"开始做某事"。

3. **答案：convenience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。定冠词 `The` 后接名词，`convenient` 变为 `convenience`。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 为固定搭配，表示"不仅……而且……"。

5. **答案：strengthens**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。主语 `it` 为第三人称单数，一般现在时。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个句子。

7. **答案：kilometres**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`30 kilometres`，数词后接可数名词复数。

8. **答案：feel**
   - **考点**：省略 to 的不定式。`make sb do sth`，使役动词后接原形。

9. **答案：most enjoyable**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the most enjoyable experiences I have ever had`。

10. **答案：longer**
    - **考点**：比较级。`a little longer`，`a little` 修饰比较级，暗含与坐公交的比较。
