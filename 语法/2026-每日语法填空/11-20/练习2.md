# 11-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家庭聚餐传统 (Family Dinner Traditions)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In Chinese culture, family dinners hold special (1) ______ (important). Sitting around a table and sharing a meal together is seen (2) ______ a way of strengthening family bonds. In many families, the dinner table is where members catch up on each other's lives and discuss important matters. My family has a tradition of having a big dinner together every Sunday evening. My grandparents, parents, aunts, uncles and cousins all gather at my grandparents' house. My grandmother always (3) ______ (prepare) a feast of eight or nine dishes, including her famous braised fish and sweet-and-sour pork. She says the number of dishes is important because even numbers are considered (4) ______ (luck) in Chinese culture. Everyone sits around a large round table, which (5) ______ (symbol) unity and togetherness. The oldest family members are always served first as a sign of (6) ______ (respectful). During the meal, my grandfather likes (7) ______ (tell) us stories about his childhood, and we all listen (8) ______ (careful). These stories help us understand our family history and appreciate how much life (9) ______ (change) over the years. I treasure these Sunday dinners because they remind me that no matter how busy life gets, spending quality time with family is one of the (10) ______ (important) things in the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：importance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`hold special importance`，形容词后接名词。

2. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be seen as` 表示"被视为"。

3. **答案：prepares**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。`always` 提示经常性动作，主语为第三人称单数。

4. **答案：lucky**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`are considered lucky`，系动词后接形容词作表语。

5. **答案：symbolises**
   - **考点**：词性转换和主谓一致。`symbol` 变为动词 `symbolise`，主语 `which` 指代单数名词。

6. **答案：respect**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `of` 后接名词，`respectful` 变为 `respect`。

7. **答案：to tell / telling**
   - **考点**：不定式/动名词。`like to do / like doing sth` 均可。

8. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `listen` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：has changed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`over the years` 提示用现在完成时。

10. **答案：most important**
    - **考点**：最高级。`one of the most important things`。
