# 07-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：纪录片的魅力 (The Charm of Documentaries)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

While most teenagers prefer action movies or cartoons, I have become a big fan of documentaries. My interest started two years ago when my science teacher (1) ______ (show) us a documentary about the deep ocean. The images of strange creatures (2) ______ (live) in total darkness amazed me. Since then, I (3) ______ (watch) documentaries on topics ranging from ancient civilisations to modern technology. I find them much more (4) ______ (education) than ordinary TV shows. For example, a documentary about the Amazon rainforest taught me that it produces about 20 percent of the world's oxygen. Another one about space made me (5) ______ (wonder) how small our planet is compared (6) ______ the universe. Documentaries can also change the way we think. After (7) ______ (see) a film about plastic pollution, I decided to use (8) ______ (few) plastic bags in my daily life. My parents now enjoy watching documentaries with me. Every Friday evening, we choose one together. My mother says it is much (9) ______ (good) than wasting time on our phones. I agree — there is always something new (10) ______ (learn) from a great documentary.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：showed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`two years ago` 表示过去，`show` 的过去式为 `showed`。

2. **答案：living**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`creatures living in total darkness` 表示"生活在完全黑暗中的生物"。

3. **答案：have watched**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 提示用现在完成时。

4. **答案：educational**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 比较级。`more educational` 中 `education` 变为形容词 `educational`。

5. **答案：wonder**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`make sb + 动词原形` 为使役动词结构。

6. **答案：with / to**
   - **考点**：介词。`compared with/to` 表示"与……相比"。

7. **答案：seeing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`After` 为介词，后接动名词。

8. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`plastic bags` 为可数名词复数，`few` 的比较级为 `fewer`。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

10. **答案：to learn**
    - **考点**：不定式作后置定语。`something new to learn` 表示"值得学习的新东西"。
