# 07-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：我的书包 (My School Bag)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

If you looked inside my school bag, you would find more than just textbooks. It is (1) ______ a small world that carries my daily life. In the front pocket, I keep a pencil case (2) ______ (fill) with pens, erasers, and a ruler. There is also a small mirror and a comb — things my mother insists I take so that I always look (3) ______ (neat). The main compartment holds six textbooks and three notebooks. They are (4) ______ (heavy) than you might think. In fact, my bag weighs about five kilograms, which sometimes makes my shoulders ache. The side pockets hold a water bottle and (5) ______ umbrella. In the hidden pocket at the bottom, I keep a family photo (6) ______ (take) last Spring Festival. Whenever I feel homesick during boarding school, looking at it makes me feel (7) ______ (good). My bag has been with me (8) ______ I started Grade Seven. The zipper has been repaired twice, and the straps show signs of wear, (9) ______ I do not want to replace it. It has carried my hopes, my worries, and my growth over the past three years. To me, this bag is not just a container for (10) ______ (book) — it is a faithful companion.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：like**
   - **考点**：介词。`like a small world` 表示"像一个小世界"。

2. **答案：filled**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a pencil case filled with` 表示"装满了……的笔袋"，过去分词表被动。

3. **答案：neat**
   - **考点**：形容词。`look` 为系动词，后接形容词作表语；括号内 `neatly` 应变为 `neat`。

4. **答案：heavier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than you might think` 提示用比较级，`heavy` 变为 `heavier`。

5. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`an umbrella` 中 `umbrella` 以元音音素 /ʌ/ 开头，用 `an`。

6. **答案：taken**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a family photo taken last Spring Festival` 表示"去年春节拍的全家福"，过去分词表被动。

7. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`feel better` 表示"感觉好些"，隐含与想家时的对比。

8. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since I started Grade Seven` 与现在完成时搭配。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。"拉链修了两次、背带磨损"与"不想换"为转折关系。

10. **答案：books**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`for books` 表示"装书的"，用复数泛指。
