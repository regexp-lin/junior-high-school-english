# 06-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：夏至与节气 (Summer Solstice)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The summer solstice usually arrives around June 21 in the northern part of the world. On this day, daytime is (1) ______ (long) than on any other day of the year, and many cultures hold special activities to welcome the change of seasons. In China, solar terms are part of traditional knowledge, and the solstice reminds farmers (2) ______ the coming busy season in the fields. My grandfather still reads (3) ______ old calendar on the wall and tells me (4) ______ each term means in simple words. Last year, our school organized a short lecture that (5) ______ (introduce) students to the history of the twenty-four terms. After class, we made hand-made fans and wrote wishes on (6) ______ (colour) cards. I used to think solar terms were only about farming, (7) ______ now I see they also shape poems, food, and family habits. Science explains that the earth travels around the sun, (8) ______ literature adds warmth to cold data. When the afternoon light turns soft, I enjoy walking by the lake and feeling the year quietly (9) ______ (move) forward. I believe understanding nature's rhythm can help us live (10) ______ (peace) with time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：longer**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，且描述白昼长度，用 `long` 的比较级 `longer`。

2. **答案：of**  
   - **考点**：介词。`remind sb. of sth.` 为固定搭配，表示“使某人想起某事/提醒某人某事”。

3. **答案：the**  
   - **考点**：冠词。特指墙上那本旧日历，用定冠词 `the`。

4. **答案：what**  
   - **考点**：名词性从句。`tell me` 后为宾语从句，从句缺宾语（每个节气的含义），用连接代词 `what`。

5. **答案：introduced**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述去年学校活动，用一般过去时；定语从句谓语与先行词 `lecture` 搭配，表示“介绍了……”，`introduce` 的过去式为 `introduced`。

6. **答案：colourful**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `cards`，用形容词；括号内 `colour` 变为 `colourful`（英式拼写；美式 `colorful` 在作文中也可接受，但教材统一时常用 `colourful`）。

7. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。前句“曾以为只与农耕有关”与后句“现在也看到还影响诗歌饮食等”形成转折，用 `but`。

8. **答案：while**  
   - **考点**：连词。`Science explains...` 与 `literature adds...` 形成对比并列，用 `while` 表示“而/与此同时”，突出科学与文学的互补。

9. **答案：moving**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`feel + 宾语 + doing` 表示“感受到……正在……”，此处表示感受到岁月在悄悄向前，用现在分词 `moving`。

10. **答案：peacefully**  
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `live`，用副词；`peace` 先变为形容词 `peaceful`，再变为 `peacefully`。`live peacefully with time` 表示与时间平和相处。
