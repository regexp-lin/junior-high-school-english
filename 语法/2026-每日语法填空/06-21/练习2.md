# 06-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中考加油 (Cheering for the Exam)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tomorrow morning, we will walk into the exam hall together, carrying pencils, rulers, and a little (1) ______ (nerve) excitement. Our homeroom teacher lined up (2) ______ the classroom door and gave everyone (3) ______ high five. "You are not fighting alone," she said. "Think of each paper (4) ______ a chance to show what three years (5) ______ (teach) you." Her voice was calm, and it helped me breathe (6) ______ (deep) than before. My parents did not ask how many points I would get; they only prepared my favourite breakfast and told me (7) ______ trust myself. On the way to school, my friend joked that we should treat the exam like a (8) ______ (compete) sports game—serious, but still a game. I know results matter, yet I also know effort is something that nobody can (9) ______ (easy) take away from you. Whatever happens, I will remember the mornings we read aloud together and the evenings we solved problems until the lights went out. Good luck to us, and may our hard work pay (10) ______ in the end.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：nervous**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `excitement`，用形容词；括号内 `nerve` 变为 `nervous`，表示“紧张的兴奋感”。`a little` 已给出，注意与 `a bit of` 同义替换表达。

2. **答案：by**  
   - **考点**：介词。`line up by the door` 表示“在门边排队”，`by` 表位置“在……旁边”。

3. **答案：a**  
   - **考点**：冠词。`give sb. a high five` 为固定表达，表示“击掌鼓励”。

4. **答案：as**  
   - **考点**：介词。`think of...as...` 表示“把……看作……”，此处指把每份试卷当作展示机会。

5. **答案：have taught**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。`what three years have taught you` 表示“三年时光教会了你什么”，强调对现在的影响，用现在完成时 `have taught`。

6. **答案：more deeply**  
   - **考点**：副词比较级。修饰动词 `breathe`，用副词；句中有 `than`，`deep` 的副词形式为 `deeply`，比较级为 `more deeply`。

7. **答案：to**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`tell sb. to do sth.` 为固定搭配，用不定式符号 `to`。

8. **答案：competitive**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词短语 `sports game`，用形容词；`compete` 变为 `competitive`，表示“竞技性的、有竞争意味的”。前面已有不定冠词 `a`。

9. **答案：easily**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `take`，用副词；`easy` 变为 `easily`。`take away from you` 表示“从你这里夺走”。

10. **答案：off**  
    - **考点**：副词与固定搭配。`pay off` 表示“得到回报、取得成功”，常用于努力终有收获。
