# 12-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国民族乐器 (Traditional Chinese Musical Instruments)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has a rich tradition of music, and traditional Chinese musical instruments play a vital role in preserving this heritage. Some of the most well-known instruments include the guzheng, the erhu, the pipa and the dizi. Each instrument has a unique sound that can express a wide range of (1) ______ (emotion). The guzheng, a 21-string plucked instrument, is often described (2) ______ having the most beautiful sound among all Chinese instruments. The erhu, sometimes called the "Chinese violin," can produce (3) ______ (haunt) melodies that move listeners to tears. My sister has been learning the pipa (4) ______ she was nine years old. The pipa is a four-stringed instrument (5) ______ (shape) like a teardrop. It has a history of over 2,000 years and was originally brought to China along the Silk Road. Playing the pipa requires great (6) ______ (precise) because the player must use all five fingers of the right hand to pluck the strings at different speeds and (7) ______ (strong). My sister practises for at least one hour every day. Her dedication has paid off — she recently won first prize at a national youth music competition. The judges (8) ______ (praise) her performance as "technically brilliant and (9) ______ (emotion) powerful." In recent years, traditional Chinese instruments have gained (10) ______ (popular) among young people, partly due to viral videos on social media showing modern songs played on ancient instruments.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：emotions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a wide range of emotions`。

2. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be described as` 表示"被描述为"。

3. **答案：haunting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `melodies` 用 `-ing` 形式。

4. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`has been learning...since she was nine`。

5. **答案：shaped**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`an instrument shaped like a teardrop`。

6. **答案：precision**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`great precision`，形容词后接名词。

7. **答案：strengths / strength**
   - **考点**：名词。与 `speeds` 并列，需要名词形式。

8. **答案：praised**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述比赛中的事件。

9. **答案：emotionally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `powerful` 需要副词。

10. **答案：popularity**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`gained popularity`，动词后接名词。
