# 12-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：新年愿望 (New Year Resolutions)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As the new year approaches, many people make resolutions — promises to themselves about things they want to change or achieve. Common resolutions include exercising more, eating (1) ______ (healthy), learning a new skill and spending less time on social media. However, studies show that only about 10% of people actually keep their resolutions throughout the year. The main reason for this low (2) ______ (succeed) rate is that people set goals that are too vague or too ambitious. For example, saying "I want to exercise more" is too general. A (3) ______ (good) resolution would be "I will jog for thirty minutes every Monday, Wednesday and Friday." This year, I have decided (4) ______ (make) three specific resolutions. First, I will read at least two books per month. I have already made a list of the books I want to read, so I know exactly (5) ______ to start. Second, I will learn to cook one new dish every week. My mother has agreed (6) ______ (teach) me, and I am excited about becoming more (7) ______ (independence) in the kitchen. Third, I will write in my English journal every day, even if it is just a few (8) ______ (sentence). My teacher says that daily writing is one of the (9) ______ (fast) ways to improve language skills. I have written my resolutions on a poster and stuck it on my bedroom wall so that I can see it every day. I believe that (10) ______ I stay focused and take small steps each day, I will achieve all three goals by the end of next year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：more healthily**
   - **考点**：比较级。`eating more healthily`，副词比较级修饰动词。

2. **答案：success**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`low success rate`，形容词后接名词。

3. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`A better resolution`，暗含与前面的比较。

4. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：不定式。`decide to do sth`。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`know exactly where to start`。

6. **答案：to teach**
   - **考点**：不定式。`agree to do sth`。

7. **答案：independent**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more independent`，系动词后接形容词。

8. **答案：sentences**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few sentences`。

9. **答案：fastest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the fastest ways`。

10. **答案：if**
    - **考点**：连词。`if I stay focused`，引导条件状语从句。
