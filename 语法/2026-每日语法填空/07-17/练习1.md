# 07-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校合唱团 (The School Choir)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Joining the school choir was one of the best (1) ______ (decide) I have ever made. I signed up in September, even though I was not sure (2) ______ I could sing well enough. The music teacher, Mr. Zhao, tested my voice and said I had potential. Practices were held twice a week after school, and each session (3) ______ (last) about ninety minutes. At first, I found it difficult (4) ______ (sing) in harmony with others. My voice always seemed too loud (5) ______ too soft. But Mr. Zhao was very patient. He taught us how to breathe (6) ______ (proper) and control our pitch. Gradually, our voices blended together (7) ______ (beautiful). In December, the choir performed at the city's New Year concert. Standing on stage in front of hundreds of people, I felt both nervous and (8) ______ (excite). When we finished our last song, the audience gave us a standing ovation. I saw tears in Mr. Zhao's eyes. That night, I understood the (9) ______ (mean) of teamwork — when many voices become one, the result is (10) ______ (great) than anything a single person can achieve.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：decisions**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`one of the + 复数名词`，`decide` 变为名词 `decision`，复数为 `decisions`。

2. **答案：if / whether**
   - **考点**：连词。引导宾语从句，表示"是否"，用 `if` 或 `whether`。

3. **答案：lasted**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的练习安排，`last`（持续）的过去式为 `lasted`。

4. **答案：to sing**
   - **考点**：不定式。`find it difficult to do sth` 为固定句型，`it` 为形式宾语。

5. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`too loud or too soft` 表示"要么太大声要么太小声"，用 `or` 连接两种可能。

6. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `breathe` 需要副词，`proper` 变为 `properly`。

7. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `blended` 需要副词，`beautiful` 变为 `beautifully`。

8. **答案：excited**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `nervous` 并列作表语，`excite` 变为 `excited`（感到兴奋的），描述人的感受。

9. **答案：meaning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`mean` 变为 `meaning`（含义）。

10. **答案：greater**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than anything` 提示用比较级，`great` 的比较级为 `greater`。
