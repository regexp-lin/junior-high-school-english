# 07-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：人工智能与生活 (AI in Our Lives)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is no longer just a topic in science fiction movies. It (1) ______ (enter) many areas of our daily lives. Voice assistants like Siri and Alexa can answer questions, play music, and even tell (2) ______ (joke). AI is also used in hospitals to help doctors identify (3) ______ (disease) more quickly and accurately. In education, some apps use AI (4) ______ (create) personalised study plans for students. If a student is weak in grammar, the app will give him or (5) ______ more grammar exercises. Many people worry that AI might take away jobs. It is true that some simple tasks (6) ______ (do) by robots in the future. However, experts believe that AI will also create new types of (7) ______ (employ). The key is to keep learning and develop skills that machines cannot easily copy, (8) ______ as creativity and emotional intelligence. As a student, I think it is important for us (9) ______ (learn) about AI so that we can use it wisely rather than fear it. Technology is neither good (10) ______ bad — what matters is how people choose to use it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has entered**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。AI 已经进入日常生活的多个领域，表示从过去到现在的结果。

2. **答案：jokes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`tell jokes` 表示"讲笑话"，用复数。

3. **答案：diseases**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`identify diseases` 表示"识别疾病"，指多种疾病，用复数。

4. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。`use AI to create` 表示"使用 AI 来创建"。

5. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`him or her` 表示"他或她"，用宾格形式与 `him` 对应。

6. **答案：will be done**
   - **考点**：一般将来时 + 被动语态。`in the future` 表示将来，任务"将被"机器人完成。

7. **答案：employment**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`of` 后接名词，`employ` 变为 `employment`（就业，工作）。

8. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`such as` 表示"例如"。

9. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It is important for sb to do sth` 为固定句型，`it` 为形式主语。

10. **答案：nor**
    - **考点**：连词。`neither...nor...` 为固定搭配，表示"既不……也不……"。
