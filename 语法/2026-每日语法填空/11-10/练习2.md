# 11-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：健康饮食 (Healthy Eating)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Eating healthily is essential for both our body and mind. However, with the (1) ______ (popular) of fast food, many teenagers today have poor eating habits. A recent survey showed that nearly 60% of middle school students skip breakfast at (2) ______ (little) once a week. Doctors say this is (3) ______ (extreme) harmful because breakfast provides the energy we need to start the day. Without it, students find it hard (4) ______ (concentrate) in class. My mother has always been strict about our family's diet. She (5) ______ (cook) fresh meals every day since I was born. She believes that home-cooked food is much (6) ______ (healthy) than restaurant food because she can control the amount of oil, salt and sugar. She also makes sure we eat plenty of vegetables and fruit. Last month, our school invited a (7) ______ (nutrition) to give us a talk about balanced diets. He explained that our meals should include five food groups: grains, vegetables, fruits, protein and dairy. He also warned (8) ______ against drinking too many sugary drinks, (9) ______ can lead to tooth decay and obesity. Since the talk, I (10) ______ (start) paying more attention to what I eat. I have replaced soft drinks with water and begun eating more whole grains.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：popularity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`with the popularity of`，介词 `with` 后接名词，`popular` 变为 `popularity`。

2. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：最高级。`at least` 为固定搭配，表示"至少"。

3. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `harmful` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：to concentrate**
   - **考点**：不定式。`find it hard to do sth` 为固定句型。

5. **答案：has cooked / has been cooking**
   - **考点**：现在完成时/现在完成进行时。`since I was born` 提示从过去持续到现在。

6. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than restaurant food` 提示比较级。

7. **答案：nutritionist**
   - **考点**：词性转换。冠词 `a` 后接名词，指人用 `nutritionist`（营养师）。

8. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词。`warn sb against doing sth` 表示"警告某人不要做某事"，此处需要宾格。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，先行词为 `sugary drinks`，指物用 `which`。

10. **答案：have started**
    - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since the talk` 提示用现在完成时。
