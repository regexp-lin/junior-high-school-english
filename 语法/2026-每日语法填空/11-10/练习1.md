# 11-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：太空探索 (Space Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Space exploration has always been one of humanity's greatest dreams. Since the first satellite (1) ______ (send) into space in 1957, humans have made remarkable progress in understanding the universe. China's space programme has achieved many (2) ______ (impress) milestones in recent years. In 2003, Yang Liwei became the first Chinese astronaut (3) ______ (travel) into space. Since then, China (4) ______ (build) its own space station and sent several missions to the moon. The Chang'e-5 mission (5) ______ (successful) brought back lunar samples in 2020, making China the third country to achieve this. Many young people dream (6) ______ becoming astronauts when they grow up. However, the training required is extremely tough. Astronauts must be in excellent (7) ______ (physics) condition and have strong mental endurance. They also need to learn many skills, from (8) ______ (operate) complex equipment to conducting scientific experiments in zero gravity. My cousin is studying aerospace engineering at university. He hopes to work for the Chinese space programme after (9) ______ (graduate). He often tells me that exploring space is not just about visiting other planets — it is about pushing the (10) ______ (boundary) of human knowledge and inspiring future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was sent**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the first satellite` 与 `send` 之间是被动关系，`in 1957` 提示一般过去时。

2. **答案：impressive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `milestones` 需要形容词，`impress` 变为 `impressive`。

3. **答案：to travel**
   - **考点**：不定式作定语。`the first...to do sth` 为固定用法。

4. **答案：has built**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 提示用现在完成时。

5. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `brought back` 需要副词形式。

6. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`dream of doing sth` 表示"梦想做某事"。

7. **答案：physical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `condition` 需要形容词，`physics` 变为 `physical`。

8. **答案：operating**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `from` 后接动名词形式。

9. **答案：graduation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `after` 后接名词，`graduate` 变为 `graduation`。

10. **答案：boundaries**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`the boundaries of human knowledge`，此处表示多个边界用复数。
