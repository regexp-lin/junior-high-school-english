# 04-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：社交能力 (Social Skills)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Good social skills are important for building strong relationships. People with good social skills find it easier to make friends and get 1. ________ well with others.

One key social skill is 2. ________ (listen). Many people like to talk about themselves, but few are truly good listeners. When someone is speaking to you, show that you care by making eye contact and nodding your head. Avoid 3. ________ (look) at your phone or interrupting them.

Another important skill is knowing how to start a conversation. If you feel nervous about talking 4. ________ new people, try starting with simple topics like the weather or a recent event. Asking questions is a good way 5. ________ (keep) the conversation going. Most people enjoy talking about their hobbies and interests.

Body language also matters. Standing with your arms 6. ________ (cross) may make you look unfriendly. A warm smile and an open posture show 7. ________ you are approachable and willing to communicate.

Being polite is the foundation of all social 8. ________ (interact). Saying "please", "thank you" and "excuse me" are small gestures that make a big difference. People feel more 9. ________ (comfort) around those who treat them with respect.

Social skills can be learned and improved with practice. Don't be afraid to step out of your comfort zone. The more you practice, the 10. ________ (easy) it will become.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **along/on**
   - **考点**：动词短语（无提示词填空）。get along/on well with 意为"与……相处融洽"。

2. **listening**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作表语）。One key social skill is listening，动名词作表语。

3. **looking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。avoid doing sth. 意为"避免做某事"。

4. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。talk to sb. 意为"与某人交谈"。

5. **to keep**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。a good way to do sth. 意为"做某事的好方法"。

6. **crossed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。with your arms crossed 构成 with 复合结构，arms 与 cross 之间是被动关系。

7. **that**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。show that... 意为"表明……"，that 引导宾语从句。

8. **interactions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。all social interactions 意为"所有社交互动"，interact → interaction → interactions。

9. **comfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。feel more comfortable 意为"感到更舒适"，comfort → comfortable。

10. **easier**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。the more...the easier... 句型，easy → easier。
