# 04-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：烹饪的乐趣 (The Fun of Cooking)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Cooking is not just about making food. It is a creative activity that brings people together and fills the house with 1. ________ (delight) smells.

I became interested in cooking when I was twelve. One Sunday morning, my parents were both busy, so I decided 2. ________ (make) breakfast by myself. I tried to cook eggs, but I burned them badly. The kitchen was filled 3. ________ smoke, and I felt embarrassed.

However, my mother didn't get angry. She 4. ________ (patient) showed me how to control the heat and told me the 5. ________ (important) of timing. From that day on, I started learning to cook step by step.

Now, I can make several dishes, 6. ________ (include) fried rice, tomato soup and stir-fried vegetables. My family says my cooking is getting 7. ________ (good) and better. Every time they enjoy the food I make, I feel a great sense of pride.

Cooking also teaches useful life 8. ________ (skill). You need to follow instructions, manage your time and be organized. These are qualities that can help you 9. ________ many areas of life.

I think every teenager should learn to cook at least a few simple dishes. It is a skill 10. ________ you will use for the rest of your life. Plus, homemade food is almost always healthier than fast food.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **delightful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 smells，delight → delightful。

2. **to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。decide to do sth. 意为"决定做某事"。

3. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be filled with 意为"充满了……"。

4. **patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 showed，需用副词形式 patiently。

5. **importance**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。the + 名词 + of，important → importance。

6. **including**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词/介词）。including 相当于介词，意为"包括"。

7. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。better and better 意为"越来越好"，good → better。

8. **skills**
   - **考点**：名词复数。life skills 意为"生活技能"，skill 用复数。

9. **in**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。in many areas of life 意为"在生活的很多方面"。

10. **that/which**
    - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 a skill，在从句中作宾语。
