# 08-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：乒乓球精神 (The Spirit of Table Tennis)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Table tennis is often called China's "national ball." It has brought the country countless gold (1) ______ (medal) in international competitions. But to me, table tennis means much more (2) ______ medals. I started playing at the age of seven, when my grandfather took me to a community sports centre. He (3) ______ (teach) me the basic strokes and footwork. Every evening after dinner, we played together for at least one hour. At first, I could not even hit the ball (4) ______ (proper), and it flew off the table every few seconds. My grandfather told me (5) ______ to be discouraged. "Every champion was once a beginner," he said. With daily practice, my skills (6) ______ (gradual) improved. In Grade Eight, I joined the school table tennis team. The training was much (7) ______ (intense) than I had expected. We practised six days a week, and the coach pushed us to our (8) ______ (limit). During matches, I learned the importance of staying calm under (9) ______ (press). Even when I was losing, I told myself to focus on the next point. Last year, our team won the city championship, and it was the (10) ______ (proud) moment of my sporting life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：medals**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`countless` 修饰可数名词复数，`medal` 变为 `medals`。

2. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较连词。`more than` 表示"不仅仅是"。

3. **答案：taught**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的事情，`teach` 的过去式为 `taught`。

4. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `hit` 需要副词，`proper` 变为 `properly`。

5. **答案：not**
   - **考点**：否定词。`tell sb not to do sth` 表示"告诉某人不要做某事"。

6. **答案：gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词，`gradual` 变为 `gradually`。

7. **答案：more intense**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`intense` 为多音节形容词。

8. **答案：limits**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`push sb to their limits` 为固定搭配，表示"逼到极限"。

9. **答案：pressure**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`under` 后接名词，`press` 变为 `pressure`（压力）。

10. **答案：proudest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词`，`proud` 的最高级为 `proudest`。
