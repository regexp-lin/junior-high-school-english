# 08-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：一棵老树 (The Old Tree)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In the centre of our village, there stands an old banyan tree. Nobody knows exactly how old it is, but the oldest villagers say it (1) ______ (be) there when their grandparents were children. The tree is so big that its branches spread out like a huge green umbrella, (2) ______ (provide) shade for an area of about 200 square metres. In summer, it is the (3) ______ (cool) spot in the village. Old people sit under it, playing chess and (4) ______ (tell) stories. Children chase each other around its thick trunk after school. The tree has witnessed many (5) ______ (change) over the years. It stood firm during typhoons that destroyed (6) ______ (build) nearby. It survived a drought that killed most other trees in the area. My grandmother says the tree has a spirit (7) ______ protects the village. Whether or not that is true, the tree (8) ______ (certain) holds a special place in everyone's heart. Last year, the government listed it (9) ______ a protected ancient tree. A small fence was put around it, and a sign was added (10) ______ (tell) visitors its story. I hope it will stand here for many more generations to come.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述祖辈小时候的情况，用过去时。

2. **答案：providing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作结果状语。枝叶伸展开来"提供"了阴凉，表示自然结果。

3. **答案：coolest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词 + in the village`，`cool` 的最高级为 `coolest`。

4. **答案：telling**
   - **考点**：现在分词。与 `playing chess` 并列，用现在分词形式。

5. **答案：changes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many` 修饰可数名词复数。

6. **答案：buildings**
   - **考点**：名词复数。台风摧毁的是附近的多栋建筑，`build` 变为名词 `building`，用复数。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a spirit`（物），在定语从句中作主语。

8. **答案：certainly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `holds` 需要副词，`certain` 变为 `certainly`。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`list sth as` 表示"把……列为"。

10. **答案：telling**
    - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`a sign telling visitors its story` 中现在分词修饰 `sign`，也可理解为目的状语 `to tell`。两者均可，但作定语更自然。
