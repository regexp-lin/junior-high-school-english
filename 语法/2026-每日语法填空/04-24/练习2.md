# 04-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中外文化交流 (Cultural Exchange)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Cultural exchange between countries helps people understand each other better. It builds bridges of friendship and 1. ________ (promote) world peace.

In recent years, more and more Chinese students have gone abroad to study. They bring Chinese culture to other countries while learning about 2. ________ (foreigner) cultures at the same time. Many of them teach their classmates to write Chinese characters, make dumplings 3. ________ perform Chinese martial arts.

At the same time, many foreign students come to China to study. They are often attracted 4. ________ the rich history and rapid development of China. Some of them learn Chinese 5. ________ (paint) and calligraphy, while others study traditional Chinese medicine. Their experiences in China help 6. ________ (they) gain a deeper understanding of Chinese culture.

Cultural exchange also happens through art and entertainment. Chinese movies, music and literature are becoming more and more popular 7. ________ (over) the world. Similarly, Western art and culture have had a great influence 8. ________ Chinese society. This two-way exchange makes our world richer and more 9. ________ (color).

As a student, you can participate in cultural exchange 10. ________ (simple) by being curious about other cultures. Read books from different countries, try foreign food, and make friends from diverse backgrounds. Every small step brings the world closer together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **promotes**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）+ 主谓一致。and 连接并列谓语，主语 it 为第三人称单数。

2. **foreign**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 cultures，foreigner → foreign。

3. **or/and**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。or/and 连接并列动词短语。

4. **by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be attracted by 意为"被……吸引"。

5. **painting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词/名词）。Chinese painting 意为"中国画"，painting 在此作名词。

6. **them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。help + 宾语，代替 foreign students，用宾格 them。

7. **all over**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。all over the world 意为"全世界"。注意提示词 over 前需加 all。

8. **on**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。have an influence on 意为"对……有影响"。

9. **colorful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。more + 形容词构成比较级，color → colorful。

10. **simply**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰介词短语 by being curious，需用副词形式 simply。
