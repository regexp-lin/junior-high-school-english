# 04-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：笔友交流 (Pen Pals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Having a pen pal is a wonderful way to learn about other countries and cultures. I have been writing to my pen pal, Sarah, 1. ________ two years, and it has been an amazing experience.

Sarah lives in London, England. We first got to know each other 2. ________ a school exchange program. Although we have never met face 3. ________ face, we feel like close friends. We write to each other at least once a month, sharing stories about our 4. ________ (day) lives.

Through Sarah's letters, I have learned a lot about British culture. She told me that British people love 5. ________ (drink) tea, especially in the afternoon. She also described the beautiful countryside and the 6. ________ (history) castles in England. Her descriptions are so vivid that I feel 7. ________ if I have visited those places myself.

In return, I tell Sarah about Chinese traditions and customs. She was very 8. ________ (interest) in the Spring Festival and asked me many questions about how we celebrate it. I even sent her a red paper cutting, and she said it was the most beautiful gift she had ever 9. ________ (receive).

Writing to a pen pal has not only improved my English but also broadened my horizons. I would encourage every student 10. ________ (find) a pen pal and start this wonderful journey of cultural exchange.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。for two years 意为"两年了"，与现在完成进行时连用。

2. **through**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。through a program 意为"通过一个项目"。

3. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。face to face 意为"面对面"，是固定搭配。

4. **daily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 lives，day → daily。

5. **drinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。love doing sth. 意为"喜欢做某事"。

6. **historical**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 castles，history → historical。

7. **as**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。as if 意为"好像"，引导方式状语从句。

8. **interested**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be interested in 意为"对……感兴趣"，主语是人用 -ed 形式。

9. **received**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。had ever received 是过去完成时，receive → received。

10. **to find**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。encourage sb. to do sth. 意为"鼓励某人做某事"。
