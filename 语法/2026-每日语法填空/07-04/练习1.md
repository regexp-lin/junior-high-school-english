# 07-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学做饭 (Learning to Cook)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I used to think cooking was only for adults, but I changed my mind after (1) ______ (watch) a TV show about young chefs. The show proved that age did not matter when it came (2) ______ cooking. Inspired by it, I asked my mother to teach me (3) ______ to make tomato and egg stir-fry, one of the (4) ______ (simple) Chinese dishes. She told me to crack two eggs into a bowl first and beat them (5) ______ (gentle). Then I heated some oil in a pan and poured the eggs in. The eggs turned (6) ______ (gold) in just a few seconds. After that, I added tomato pieces and a little sugar. The (7) ______ (whole) process took only about ten minutes. When I tasted the dish, I could not believe I (8) ______ (cook) it by myself. My father said it was even better (9) ______ the one he ate at the restaurant last week. Of course he was joking, but his words still made me (10) ______ (feel) proud. Now I cook this dish every weekend.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：动名词。`after` 为介词，后接动名词 `watching`。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`when it comes to` 为固定搭配，表示"当涉及到……"，`to` 为介词。

3. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`teach sb how to do sth` 表示"教某人如何做某事"。

4. **答案：simplest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`simple` 的最高级为 `simplest`。

5. **答案：gently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `beat` 需要副词，`gentle` 变为 `gently`。

6. **答案：golden**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`turn` 为系动词，后接形容词，`gold` 变为 `golden`（金黄色的）。

7. **答案：whole**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `process` 需要形容词，`wholly`（副词）的形容词形式为 `whole`（整个的）。注意此处括号内给的是 `whole`，不变形。

8. **答案：had cooked**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。"做饭"发生在"品尝"之前（过去的过去），用 `had cooked`。

9. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较连词。`better than` 为比较级结构，用 `than` 引出比较对象。

10. **答案：feel**
    - **考点**：动词原形。`make sb + 动词原形` 为使役动词结构，省略 `to`。
