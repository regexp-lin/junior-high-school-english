# 07-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：公园晨练 (Morning Exercise in the Park)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every morning before school, my grandfather takes me to the park for exercise. We usually arrive there at six, when the air is still fresh and (1) ______ (quietly). The park is one of the most (2) ______ (beauty) places in our neighbourhood. Old people practise tai chi under the trees, and some women dance together (3) ______ music. My grandfather has been doing tai chi (4) ______ he retired ten years ago. His movements are slow but graceful, and they look much (5) ______ (easy) than they actually are. He once told me that tai chi was not just a form of exercise — it also helped people find inner (6) ______ (peaceful). I prefer jogging around the lake. It takes me about twenty (7) ______ (minute) to finish three laps. After (8) ______ (run), I always feel full of energy for the rest of the day. Sometimes we meet our neighbour Mrs. Li, (9) ______ walks her little dog every morning. She always greets us with a warm smile. I think morning exercise is a wonderful habit. It keeps us (10) ______ (health) and connects us with nature.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：quiet**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is` 为系动词，后接形容词作表语；括号内为 `quietly`（副词），应变为形容词 `quiet`（安静的），与 `fresh` 并列。

2. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most + 多音节形容词` 构成最高级，`beauty` 变为 `beautiful`。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`dance to music` 为固定搭配，表示"随着音乐跳舞"。

4. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since he retired` 引导时间状语从句，与现在完成进行时/现在完成时搭配，表示"自从……以来"。

5. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`easy` 变 y 为 i 加 `-er`，得 `easier`。

6. **答案：peace**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`inner` 为形容词，修饰名词；`peaceful` 变为名词 `peace`（平静）。

7. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`twenty` 修饰可数名词复数，`minute` 变为 `minutes`。

8. **答案：running**
   - **考点**：动名词。`After` 为介词，后接动名词 `running`。

9. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `Mrs. Li`（人），引导非限制性定语从句，在从句中作主语，用 `who`。

10. **答案：healthy**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`keep sb + 形容词` 表示"保持某人……的状态"，`health` 变为 `healthy`。
