# 08-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：海洋保护 (Ocean Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ocean covers more than 70% of the Earth's surface and is home (1) ______ millions of species. However, human activities (2) ______ (cause) serious damage to the ocean in recent decades. Every year, about eight million tonnes of plastic waste (3) ______ (dump) into the sea. This plastic harms sea animals (4) ______ may mistake it for food. Scientists have found plastic pieces inside the (5) ______ (stomach) of sea turtles, whales and many kinds of fish. The situation is becoming (6) ______ (bad) and worse. To protect the ocean, many countries have started to ban single-use plastics (7) ______ as bags and straws. Some volunteers organise beach (8) ______ (clean) events on weekends, collecting rubbish left by visitors. As students, there are things we can do too. We should use (9) ______ (few) plastic products in our daily life and recycle as much as possible. We should also spread awareness by (10) ______ (tell) our friends and family about the importance of keeping the ocean clean. Every small action counts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`be home to` 表示"是……的家园"。

2. **答案：have caused**
   - **考点**：时态。`in recent decades` 提示用现在完成时 `have caused`。

3. **答案：is dumped**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`plastic waste` 是不可数名词（单数），"被倾倒"，用一般现在时被动 `is dumped`。

4. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `sea animals`（指动物可用 `who/that`），引导定语从句。

5. **答案：stomachs**
   - **考点**：名词复数。多个动物的胃，`stomach` 用复数 `stomachs`。

6. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：比较级。`worse and worse` 表示"越来越糟"，`bad` 的比较级为 `worse`。

7. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`such as` 表示"例如"。

8. **答案：cleaning / clean-up**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`beach cleaning events` 或 `beach clean-up events`，`clean` 变为名词/动名词修饰 `events`。

9. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`fewer plastic products`，`plastic products` 为可数名词复数，`few` 的比较级为 `fewer`。

10. **答案：telling**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词 `telling`。
