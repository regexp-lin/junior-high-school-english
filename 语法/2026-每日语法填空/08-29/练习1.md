# 08-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家庭菜园 (Family Vegetable Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My grandparents live in a small town and have a vegetable garden behind their house. Every spring, they plant tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers and many (1) ______ (other) vegetables. My grandfather is the one (2) ______ takes care of the garden every day. He gets up early in the morning to water the (3) ______ (plant) and pull out weeds. He says gardening keeps him (4) ______ (health) and gives him something meaningful to do after retirement. Last summer, I spent my holiday at their home and (5) ______ (help) in the garden. Grandpa taught me how to tell the difference (6) ______ useful plants and weeds. He also showed me the right way of (7) ______ (pick) tomatoes — you should twist them gently rather than pull them hard. I was surprised to learn that growing vegetables requires so much (8) ______ (know). After a week of hard work, we (9) ______ (harvest) a big basket of fresh vegetables. Grandma cooked them for dinner, and they tasted much (10) ______ (good) than anything from the supermarket. I now understand why my grandparents love their garden so much.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：other**
   - **考点**：形容词。`many other vegetables` 表示"许多其他蔬菜"，`other` 本身即为形容词原形。

2. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `the one`（指人），引导定语从句用 `who` 或 `that`。

3. **答案：plants**
   - **考点**：名词复数。花园中有多种植物，用复数 `plants`。

4. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`keep him healthy`，`keep + 宾语 + 形容词`，`health` 变为 `healthy`。

5. **答案：helped**
   - **考点**：时态。与 `spent` 并列，用一般过去时 `helped`。

6. **答案：between**
   - **考点**：介词。`tell the difference between A and B` 是固定搭配，表示"区分 A 和 B"。

7. **答案：picking**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词 `picking`。

8. **答案：knowledge**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so much knowledge`，`much` 修饰不可数名词，`know` 变为名词 `knowledge`。

9. **答案：harvested**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去发生的事，用一般过去时 `harvested`。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：比较级。`much better than`，`good` 的比较级为 `better`，`much` 修饰比较级。
