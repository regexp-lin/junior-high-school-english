# 05-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节约粮食 (Saving Food)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

"Who knows that the food on our plate, every grain 1. ________ (come) from hard work." This famous Chinese poem reminds us of the importance of saving food.

In China, food waste is a serious problem. According to a recent report, about 35 million tons of food 2. ________ (waste) every year. That is enough to feed 3. ________ (million) of people. Much of this waste happens in restaurants, school canteens and households.

Many students don't realize 4. ________ much food they throw away every day. Some take more food than they can eat. Others refuse to eat vegetables and only pick 5. ________ (they) favorite dishes. These habits lead to a huge amount of unnecessary waste.

The Chinese government launched a campaign 6. ________ (call) "Clean Your Plate" to encourage people to finish their food. Many restaurants now offer smaller portions and remind customers not to order too 7. ________. Schools have also started teaching students the value of food and the hard work of 8. ________ (farm).

Saving food is not just about money — it is about 9. ________ (respect) the labor of those who grow and prepare it. Next time you sit down for a meal, think about all the effort that went into making it. Take only what you need, and try your 10. ________ (good) to finish every bite.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **comes**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）+ 主谓一致。every grain 为第三人称单数，描述一般事实。

2. **is wasted**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般现在时）。food 与 waste 之间是被动关系，every year 表示经常性。

3. **millions**
   - **考点**：数词用法。millions of 意为"数百万的"，是固定搭配。

4. **how**
   - **考点**：连接副词（无提示词填空）。how much food 意为"多少食物"，引导宾语从句。

5. **their**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 favorite dishes，they → their。

6. **called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作后置定语）。a campaign called... 意为"一个被称为……的运动"。

7. **much**
   - **考点**：副词（无提示词填空）。order too much 意为"点太多（食物）"，much 代指不可数的食物。

8. **farmers**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→名词）+ 复数。the hard work of farmers 意为"农民的辛苦劳动"，farm → farmer → farmers。

9. **respecting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 about 后接动名词形式。

10. **best**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级（不规则变化）。try your best 意为"尽你最大努力"，good → best。
