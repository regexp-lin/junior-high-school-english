# 05-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：科学实验的乐趣 (Fun of Science Experiments)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Science experiments are one of the most exciting parts of school. They allow students to learn by 1. ________ (do), not just by reading textbooks.

Last week, our chemistry teacher showed us how to make a volcano model. She 2. ________ (mix) baking soda and vinegar together, and suddenly, foam shot up into the air like a real eruption! The whole class was 3. ________ (amaze) and cheered loudly. It was much more 4. ________ (interest) than just listening to a lecture.

Our physics teacher, Mr. Zhang, also makes his classes very 5. ________ (live). He once brought a raw egg and asked us whether we could drop it from the third floor 6. ________ breaking it. We had to design a protective container using only paper and tape. My group spent two hours 7. ________ (build) our design, and we were proud when our egg survived the fall!

Experiments teach us more than scientific knowledge. They help us develop important skills such as critical thinking, teamwork and 8. ________ (create). When an experiment fails, we learn to analyze 9. ________ went wrong and try again. This process is just as valuable as getting the right result.

I believe every school should give students more 10. ________ (opportunity) to do hands-on experiments. After all, the best way to understand science is to experience it yourself.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **doing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 by 后接动名词形式。

2. **mixed**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。last week 提示用一般过去时。

3. **amazed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。was amazed 意为"感到惊叹"，主语 the whole class 指人，用 -ed 形式。

4. **interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。more interesting 构成比较级，修饰事物用 -ing 形式。

5. **lively**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词）。make his classes lively 意为"使课堂生动"，live → lively（活泼的、生动的）。

6. **without**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。without breaking it 意为"不打碎它"。

7. **building**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 意为"花时间做某事"。

8. **creativity**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。and 连接并列名词，create → creativity。

9. **what**
   - **考点**：连接代词（无提示词填空）。analyze what went wrong 意为"分析哪里出了问题"，what 引导宾语从句。

10. **opportunities**
    - **考点**：名词复数。more opportunities 意为"更多机会"，opportunity → opportunities。
