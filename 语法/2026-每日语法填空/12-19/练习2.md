# 12-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：同伴压力 (Peer Pressure)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Peer pressure is the influence that people of the same age group have on each other's behaviour and decisions. It is (1) ______ (particular) strong during the teenage years, when young people are eager to fit in and be accepted by their peers. Peer pressure can be positive or (2) ______ (negatively). Positive peer pressure happens when friends encourage each other to study harder, exercise regularly or participate (3) ______ school activities. Negative peer pressure occurs when teenagers feel forced to do things they are uncomfortable with, such as skipping classes, trying cigarettes or (4) ______ (bully) others. My friend Xiao Chen once experienced serious negative peer pressure. A group of older students tried to pressure him (5) ______ copying their homework in exchange for being part of their group. At first, he was tempted because he wanted to be popular. But after thinking it (6) ______, he refused. It was one of the (7) ______ (brave) decisions he has ever made. The older students stopped talking to him, but his true friends respected him even (8) ______ (much). Our school counsellor, Ms Li, often talks to us about how to handle peer pressure. She suggests three strategies: first, learn to say "no" (9) ______ (firm) but politely; second, surround yourself with friends who share your values; and third, talk to a trusted adult (10) ______ you feel overwhelmed. These strategies have helped many students deal with difficult situations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：particularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `strong` 需要副词。

2. **答案：negative**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `positive` 并列，需要形容词形式。

3. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`participate in` 表示"参加"。

4. **答案：bullying**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `skipping` 和 `trying` 并列。

5. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`pressure sb into doing sth` 表示"迫使某人做某事"。

6. **答案：over**
   - **考点**：副词。`think it over` 表示"仔细考虑"。

7. **答案：bravest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the bravest decisions`。

8. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：比较级。`respected him even more`，`even` 修饰比较级。

9. **答案：firmly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `say` 需要副词。

10. **答案：when / if**
    - **考点**：连词。`when/if you feel overwhelmed`，引导条件/时间状语从句。
