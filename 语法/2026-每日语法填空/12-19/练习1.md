# 12-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：电影欣赏 (Film Appreciation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Films are a powerful form of art that can make us laugh, cry, think and dream. A good film tells a (1) ______ (compel) story, creates memorable characters and leaves a lasting impression (2) ______ its audience. In China, the film industry has grown (3) ______ (rapid) over the past two decades. Chinese box office revenues now rank among the (4) ______ (high) in the world. Our school runs a film appreciation club that meets every other Friday. The club (5) ______ (found) three years ago by our English teacher, Ms Wang, who is a huge film fan. Each session, we watch a carefully (6) ______ (select) film and then discuss its themes, characters and filmmaking techniques. Ms Wang teaches us to look beyond the surface of a story and think about what the director is trying (7) ______ (say). Last month, we watched a documentary about the lives of migrant workers in Chinese cities. It was one of the most (8) ______ (move) films I have ever seen. The director followed three families over five years, (9) ______ (show) their struggles, hopes and sacrifices. After the screening, we had a heated discussion about social inequality and the importance of empathy. I believe that watching thoughtful films is just as (10) ______ (education) as reading a good book. They open our eyes to different perspectives and help us become more understanding human beings.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：compelling**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `story` 需要形容词。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`leave an impression on` 表示"给……留下印象"。

3. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `grown` 需要副词。

4. **答案：highest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`among the highest`。

5. **答案：was founded**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`The club` 与 `found` 之间是被动关系，`three years ago` 提示过去时。

6. **答案：selected**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a carefully selected film`，被动关系。

7. **答案：to say**
   - **考点**：不定式。`try to do sth` 表示"试图做某事"。

8. **答案：moving**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物（电影）用 `-ing` 形式。

9. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随状态。

10. **答案：educational**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`as educational as reading`，`education` 变为形容词。
