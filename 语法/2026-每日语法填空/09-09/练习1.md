# 09-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：地铁出行 (Travelling by Metro)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The metro system in our city has made daily travel much (1) ______ (easy) for millions of people. Before the metro was built, most people relied (2) ______ buses and cars, which often got stuck in traffic. Now, a journey that used to take an hour by bus can (3) ______ (complete) in just twenty minutes by metro. I take the metro to school every day. The stations are always clean, and the trains run (4) ______ (exact) on time. One thing I appreciate most is the (5) ______ (safe) system. There are security checks at every entrance, and staff members are always ready to help (6) ______ (passenger) in need. Last month, the city opened a new metro line (7) ______ connects the airport to the city centre. My uncle, who (8) ______ (travel) frequently for work, said it was the best news he had heard all year. However, the metro can be extremely (9) ______ (crowd) during rush hours. Sometimes I have to let two or three trains pass before I can squeeze in. Despite this, I still think the metro is the most (10) ______ (effect) way to get around in a big city.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much easier`，`much` 修饰比较级。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`rely on` 为固定搭配，表"依赖"。

3. **答案：be completed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`can be completed`，情态动词后接动词原形被动。

4. **答案：exactly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰介词短语 `on time` 需要副词，`exact` 变为 `exactly`。

5. **答案：safety**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `system` 需要名词作定语，`safe` 变为 `safety`。

6. **答案：passengers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`passengers in need` 表"需要帮助的乘客"，泛指多人。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a new metro line`（物），引导定语从句。

8. **答案：travels**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。描述经常性动作用一般现在时，主语 `who`（指 `uncle`）为单数。

9. **答案：crowded**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be crowded`，`crowd` 变为过去分词形容词，表"拥挤的"。

10. **答案：effective**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`the most effective way`，修饰名词需要形容词，`effect` 变为 `effective`。
