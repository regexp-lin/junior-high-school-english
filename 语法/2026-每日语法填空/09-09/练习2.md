# 09-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：感恩老师 (Being Grateful to Teachers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

September 10th is Teachers' Day in China, a day (1) ______ we show our appreciation to those who have taught and guided us. This year, our class decided to do something special for our (2) ______ (favour) teacher, Ms. Chen. She has been (3) ______ (teach) us English since we started junior high school. She is always patient and never gets angry (4) ______ we make mistakes. Instead, she encourages us to learn from our errors and try again. To prepare for Teachers' Day, several students stayed after school (5) ______ (decorate) the classroom. They hung colourful balloons and wrote "Happy Teachers' Day" on the blackboard. I was responsible for making a card that (6) ______ (sign) by every student in the class. When Ms. Chen walked (7) ______ the classroom the next morning, she was completely surprised. Her eyes were filled (8) ______ tears of joy. "This is the (9) ______ (nice) gift I have ever received," she said in a shaky voice. We all clapped and thanked her for everything she had done for (10) ______. It was a morning I will never forget.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a day`（时间），定语从句中缺时间状语，用 `when`。

2. **答案：favourite**
   - **考点**：形容词。`our favourite teacher`，`favour` 变为形容词 `favourite`。

3. **答案：teaching**
   - **考点**：时态。`has been teaching` 为现在完成进行时，表示从过去持续到现在。

4. **答案：when / if**
   - **考点**：连词。`when/if we make mistakes` 表示"当/如果我们犯错时"。

5. **答案：to decorate**
   - **考点**：不定式。`stayed after school to decorate...`，不定式表目的。

6. **答案：was signed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`a card that was signed by every student`，卡片"被签名"。

7. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`walked into the classroom` 表示"走进教室"。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be filled with` 表示"充满"。

9. **答案：nicest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the nicest gift I have ever received`，`nice` 的最高级为 `nicest`。

10. **答案：us**
    - **考点**：代词宾格。`for us`，介词后接宾格。
