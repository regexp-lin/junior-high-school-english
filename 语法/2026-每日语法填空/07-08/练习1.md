# 07-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：乡村生活 (Country Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the summer holiday, I went to stay with my uncle in a small village. Life there was completely different (1) ______ city life. My uncle is a farmer. He gets up at five every morning and works in the fields (2) ______ noon. The work is hard, but he enjoys it because he loves the land. I helped him feed the (3) ______ (chicken) and water the vegetables. At first I was not used to (4) ______ (get) up so early, and my arms felt sore after a day's work. However, I soon discovered the (5) ______ (please) of country life. The air was much (6) ______ (clean) than that in the city, and at night I could see (7) ______ (thousand) of stars shining in the sky. My aunt cooked delicious meals (8) ______ fresh ingredients from the garden. Everything tasted so much (9) ______ (good) than the food in supermarkets. When the holiday ended, I did not want to leave. The experience made me (10) ______ (understand) that a simple life can bring true happiness.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`be different from` 为固定搭配，表示"与……不同"。

2. **答案：until / till**
   - **考点**：介词。`until/till noon` 表示"直到中午"。

3. **答案：chickens**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`feed the chickens` 表示"喂鸡"，农场养多只鸡，用复数。

4. **答案：getting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`be used to doing sth` 表示"习惯做某事"，`to` 为介词。

5. **答案：pleasure(s)**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`please` 变为名词 `pleasure`（乐趣）。

6. **答案：cleaner**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than that in the city` 提示用比较级，`clean` 的比较级为 `cleaner`。

7. **答案：thousands**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`thousands of` 表示"成千上万的"，`thousand` 用复数形式，后接 `of`。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`with fresh ingredients` 表示"用新鲜食材"，`with` 表示使用的工具或材料。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词/形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

10. **答案：understand**
    - **考点**：动词原形。`make sb + 动词原形` 为使役动词结构。
