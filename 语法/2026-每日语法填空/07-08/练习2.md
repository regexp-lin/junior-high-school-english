# 07-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：一场篮球赛 (A Basketball Game)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Basketball is one of my favourite sports, and last week I played in an exciting game between Class One and our class. The match (1) ______ (hold) on Wednesday afternoon. Both teams had been practising hard (2) ______ weeks. When the game started, Class One scored the first basket. We did not lose heart and quickly fought back. By half-time, the score was 18 to 20, with Class One (3) ______ (lead). During the break, our captain told us to focus (4) ______ defence and pass the ball more (5) ______ (quick). His advice worked well. In the second half, I (6) ______ (pass) the ball to Zhang Wei, who scored a three-pointer! The crowd cheered so (7) ______ (loud) that the whole gym shook. With only thirty (8) ______ (second) left, we were still two points behind. Then our captain stole the ball and drove to the basket for a lay-up. The final score was 42 to 42 — a draw! (9) ______ nobody won, both teams were proud of themselves. The game taught me that teamwork is (10) ______ (important) than individual skill.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was held**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。比赛"被举行"，`on Wednesday afternoon` 表示过去，用 `was held`。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for weeks` 表示"好几周"，`for` 接一段时间，与过去完成进行时搭配。

3. **答案：leading**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`with + 名词 + 现在分词` 构成 with 复合结构，表示伴随状态，"一班领先"。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`focus on` 为固定搭配，表示"专注于"。

5. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `pass` 需要副词，`quick` 变为 `quickly`。

6. **答案：passed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述比赛中的动作，用一般过去时，`pass` 的过去式为 `passed`。

7. **答案：loudly**
   - **考点**：副词。`so...that...` 结构中修饰动词 `cheered` 用副词 `loudly`。

8. **答案：seconds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`thirty` 修饰可数名词复数，`second` 变为 `seconds`。

9. **答案：Although / Though**
   - **考点**：让步连词。"虽然没有人赢"与"两队都为自己感到骄傲"为让步关系。

10. **答案：more important**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`important` 为多音节形容词，比较级为 `more important`。
