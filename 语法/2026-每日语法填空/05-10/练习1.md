# 05-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家乡的变化 (Changes in My Hometown)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My hometown has changed a lot over the past ten years. It used to be a small and quiet town, but now it 1. ________ (become) a modern city with tall buildings and wide roads.

Ten years ago, there was only one primary school in town. Children had to walk a long way to get to school. Now, several new schools 2. ________ (build) and every child can go to a school near their home. The quality of 3. ________ (educate) has also improved greatly.

The biggest change is the transportation. In the past, most people traveled 4. ________ bicycle or on foot. There were no buses or trains. Today, a new highway connects our town to the nearby big city, and the journey 5. ________ used to take three hours now takes only one.

My grandmother often tells me stories about the old days. She says that life was much 6. ________ (hard) back then. There was not enough food, and 7. ________ (family) had to grow their own vegetables. Now, there is a big supermarket where people can buy 8. ________ (any) they need.

Of course, development has also brought some problems. The river that runs through town is not as clean 9. ________ it used to be. Some old buildings have been torn down to make way for new ones.

I hope my hometown will continue to develop 10. ________ also keep its natural beauty. After all, progress should not come at the cost of the environment.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **has become**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。now 和 over the past ten years 提示用现在完成时。

2. **have been built**
   - **考点**：被动语态（现在完成时）。schools 与 build 之间是被动关系，表示"已被建成"。

3. **education**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the quality of + 名词，educate → education。

4. **by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。by bicycle 意为"骑自行车"，by 表示交通方式。

5. **that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 the journey，在从句中作主语。

6. **harder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，hard → harder。

7. **families**
   - **考点**：名词复数。此处泛指多个家庭，family → families。

8. **anything/everything**
   - **考点**：代词。buy anything/everything they need 意为"买他们需要的任何/所有东西"。

9. **as**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。not as clean as 意为"不如……那么干净"，as...as 结构。

10. **but**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。continue to develop but also keep... 表转折与并列。
