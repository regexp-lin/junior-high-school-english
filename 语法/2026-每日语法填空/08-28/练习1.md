# 08-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市公共自行车 (City Bike-Sharing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Bike-sharing has changed the way people travel in Chinese cities. A few years ago, colourful shared bicycles (1) ______ (appear) on streets everywhere. Users can unlock a bike by (2) ______ (scan) a QR code with their mobile phones. It is one of the (3) ______ (convenient) ways to travel short distances. My father uses a shared bike every day to ride (4) ______ the subway station to his office, which is about two kilometres away. He says it saves him a lot of time (5) ______ is also good for the environment. (6) ______, the rapid growth of bike-sharing has brought some problems. Many bikes were parked carelessly, (7) ______ (block) pavements and causing trouble for pedestrians. Some people even damaged the bikes (8) ______ purpose. The government has introduced new rules to deal (9) ______ these issues. Now, users must park bikes in designated areas, and those who break the rules will receive (10) ______ (punish). I believe bike-sharing will continue to improve and make our cities greener.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：appeared**
   - **考点**：时态。`a few years ago` 提示用一般过去时。

2. **答案：scanning**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词，表示"通过扫码"。

3. **答案：most convenient**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the most convenient ways`，多音节形容词最高级用 `most`。

4. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`from the subway station to his office` 表示"从地铁站到办公室"。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`saves him time and is also good...`，`and` 连接两个并列的谓语。

6. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词（转折）。前后语意转折，用 `However` 表示"然而"。

7. **答案：blocking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`blocking pavements` 作结果状语，与主句构成因果关系。

8. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on purpose` 为固定搭配，表示"故意地"。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`deal with` 为固定搭配，表示"处理、应对"。

10. **答案：punishment**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`receive` 后接名词，`punish` 变为名词 `punishment`。
