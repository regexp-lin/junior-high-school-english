# 08-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：手工制作课 (Handicraft Class)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This term, our school introduced a new course called "Creative Handicrafts". Every Thursday afternoon, we learn to make things with our (1) ______ (hand). So far, we (2) ______ (make) paper flowers, clay animals and wooden photo frames. Our teacher, Mr. Chen, is very (3) ______ (patience) and always shows us each step clearly. Last week, he taught us how (4) ______ (create) a small lantern using red paper and string. It looked simple, (5) ______ it actually required a lot of skill. I cut the paper too (6) ______ (short), so my lantern turned out to be much (7) ______ (small) than everyone else's. My classmate Xiao Hua, however, made a beautiful one. She is the most (8) ______ (skill) student in our class when it comes to handicrafts. I asked her (9) ______ she could teach me some tricks, and she kindly agreed. She told me that the key to making good handicrafts is to be careful and not to rush. I think this course is very (10) ______ (value) because it teaches us not only practical skills but also the importance of patience.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：hands**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`our hands` 表示"我们的双手"，用复数形式。

2. **答案：have made**
   - **考点**：时态。`so far` 提示用现在完成时 `have made`。

3. **答案：patient**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be patient`，系动词后接形容词作表语，`patience` 变为形容词 `patient`。

4. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：不定式。`teach sb how to do sth` 是固定结构。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`looked simple but actually required...` 表转折关系。

6. **答案：short**
   - **考点**：副词/形容词。`cut the paper too short`，`short` 在此作副词修饰 `cut`，保持原形。

7. **答案：smaller**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much smaller than` 表示"比……小得多"，`much` 修饰比较级。

8. **答案：skilful / skillful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most skilful student`，`skill` 变为形容词 `skilful/skillful`。

9. **答案：if / whether**
   - **考点**：连词。`asked her if/whether she could...` 表示"问她是否能……"。

10. **答案：valuable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`is very valuable`，系动词后接形容词，`value` 变为形容词 `valuable`。
