# 12-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：夜市文化 (Night Markets)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Night markets are a beloved part of Chinese culture. They come alive after dark, offering a vibrant mix of street food, handmade goods and live (1) ______ (entertain). You can find night markets in almost every Chinese city, from bustling Beijing to sunny Sanya. The food at night markets is often (2) ______ (consider) some of the best street food in the world. Stalls sell everything from grilled lamb skewers and stinky tofu to fresh fruit juices and sweet dumplings. The (3) ______ (smell) of cooking food fills the air, making it almost impossible not to stop and buy something. Last summer, I visited the famous Shilin Night Market in Taipei with my family. It was one of the most (4) ______ (excite) experiences of my life. The market was packed (5) ______ locals and tourists alike. We tried at least ten different snacks, (6) ______ (include) oyster omelettes, bubble tea and pineapple cakes. My favourite was the fried chicken, which was (7) ______ (crisp) and more flavourful than any I had tasted before. Besides food, the night market also had game stalls where visitors could win prizes by (8) ______ (throw) darts or catching goldfish. My younger brother spent most of his time there, (9) ______ (try) to win a stuffed toy. Night markets are more than just places to eat and shop. They are social spaces (10) ______ people come together to relax and enjoy life. I hope this wonderful tradition will continue for generations to come.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：entertainment**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`live entertainment`，形容词后接名词。

2. **答案：considered**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`is often considered`，被动关系。

3. **答案：smell**
   - **考点**：名词。`The smell of cooking food`，定冠词后接名词。注意 `smell` 本身可作名词。

4. **答案：exciting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be packed with` 表示"挤满了"。

6. **答案：including**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`including` 作介词用，表示"包括"。

7. **答案：crispier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than any I had tasted` 提示比较级。

8. **答案：throwing**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词。

9. **答案：trying**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随状态。

10. **答案：where**
    - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，先行词 `spaces` 为地点。
