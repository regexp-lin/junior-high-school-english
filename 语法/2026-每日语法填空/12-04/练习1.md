# 12-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：垃圾分类 (Recycling and Waste Sorting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Waste sorting has become an important topic in China in recent years. In 2019, Shanghai became the first city (1) ______ (introduce) mandatory waste sorting rules. Residents are now required to separate their rubbish into four categories: recyclable waste, kitchen waste, hazardous waste and other waste. At first, many people found the rules (2) ______ (confuse) because they were not sure which bin to use for each type of rubbish. Some common items, such as used tissues and leftover bones, were (3) ______ (particular) tricky to classify. To help residents, the local government created a mobile app that allows users to search for any item and find out which category it (4) ______ (belong) to. Our school started a waste sorting programme last semester. Four colour-coded bins (5) ______ (place) in every classroom: blue for recyclables, green for kitchen waste, red for hazardous waste and grey for everything else. At first, many students simply threw their rubbish (6) ______ the nearest bin without thinking. Our class monitor suggested that we take turns being the "Waste Sorting Officer" each week. The officer's job is (7) ______ (check) the bins and help classmates sort their waste (8) ______ (correct). After a few weeks, most students got the hang of it. We have also learned that recycling can save energy and reduce pollution. For example, recycling one aluminium can saves enough energy (9) ______ (power) a TV for three hours. These facts have helped us understand (10) ______ waste sorting matters so much.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to introduce**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the first city to do sth` 为固定用法。

2. **答案：confusing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found the rules confusing`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

3. **答案：particularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `tricky` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：belongs**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`it` 为第三人称单数，一般现在时。

5. **答案：were placed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`bins` 与 `place` 之间是被动关系，`last semester` 提示过去时。

6. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`throw rubbish into the bin` 表示"把垃圾扔进桶里"。

7. **答案：to check**
   - **考点**：不定式。`The officer's job is to check...`，不定式作表语。

8. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `sort` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：to power**
   - **考点**：不定式。`enough energy to power` 表示"足够的能量去驱动"。

10. **答案：why**
    - **考点**：连接副词。`understand why waste sorting matters`，引导宾语从句。
