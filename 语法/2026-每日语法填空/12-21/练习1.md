# 12-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国国画 (Traditional Chinese Painting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traditional Chinese painting, also called "guohua," is one of the oldest (1) ______ (continue) art traditions in the world. Unlike Western painting, which emphasises realistic (2) ______ (represent), Chinese painting focuses on capturing the spirit and mood of the subject. Artists use a brush dipped in ink or watercolours to paint on rice paper or silk. The most popular subjects include landscapes, flowers, birds and bamboo. Each subject carries (3) ______ (symbol) meaning. For example, bamboo represents strength and flexibility, while plum blossoms symbolise (4) ______ (persist) in the face of hardship. My art teacher, Ms Shen, specialises (5) ______ traditional Chinese painting. She has been painting for over twenty years and her works (6) ______ (exhibit) in galleries across the country. She teaches us that in Chinese painting, what you leave unpainted is just as important (7) ______ what you paint. Empty space, known as "liubai," gives the viewer room to imagine and interpret. This concept is very different (8) ______ Western art, where the entire canvas is usually covered with colour. Last semester, Ms Shen taught us to paint bamboo using the "xieyi" technique, which involves bold, expressive brushstrokes. At first, my bamboo looked more like a messy pile of sticks. But after weeks of practice, I (9) ______ (learn) to control the brush and create strokes that were both strong and elegant. I now understand why Chinese painting is considered not just an art form but also a form of (10) ______ (person) cultivation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：continuous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `art traditions` 需要形容词。

2. **答案：representation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`realistic representation`，形容词后接名词。

3. **答案：symbolic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `meaning` 需要形容词。

4. **答案：persistence**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`symbolise persistence`，动词后接名词。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`specialise in` 表示"专攻"。

6. **答案：have been exhibited**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。表示从过去到现在的经历，被动关系。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`as important as` 同级比较。

8. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`different from` 表示"不同于"。

9. **答案：had learned / learned**
   - **考点**：过去完成时/一般过去时。经过几周练习后学会了。

10. **答案：personal**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `cultivation` 需要形容词。
