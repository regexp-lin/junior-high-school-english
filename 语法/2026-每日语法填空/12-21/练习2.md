# 12-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：海洋保护 (Ocean Conservation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ocean covers more than 70% of the Earth's surface and is home to an (1) ______ (estimate) 2.2 million species. It provides us with food, regulates the climate and produces over half of the world's oxygen. Yet, human activities are putting the ocean under severe threat. Overfishing has caused many fish (2) ______ (population) to decline sharply. In some areas, fish stocks have dropped by over 90% compared to what they were fifty years ago. Pollution is another major problem. Every year, millions of tonnes of rubbish, (3) ______ (especial) plastic waste, end up in the ocean. This waste harms marine animals and destroys coral reefs, (4) ______ are among the most biodiverse ecosystems on Earth. Climate change is making things even (5) ______ (bad). Rising ocean temperatures cause coral bleaching, and (6) ______ (acid) waters make it harder for shellfish and corals to build their shells. Our school's environmental club organised a beach cleanup last month. About forty students spent a Saturday morning (7) ______ (pick) up rubbish along a one-kilometre stretch of coastline. We collected over 200 kilograms of waste, mostly plastic bottles and bags. The experience was (8) ______ (shock) but also motivating. Since the cleanup, several students (9) ______ (start) a campaign to reduce single-use plastics at school. I believe that protecting the ocean is not someone else's responsibility — it is (10) ______ (our). Every small action counts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：estimated**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`an estimated 2.2 million species`。

2. **答案：populations**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many fish populations`。

3. **答案：especially**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词短语需要副词。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句。

5. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：比较级。`even worse`，`bad` 的比较级。

6. **答案：acidic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `waters` 需要形容词。

7. **答案：picking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth`。

8. **答案：shocking**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物（经历）用 `-ing`。

9. **答案：have started**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since the cleanup` 提示。

10. **答案：ours**
    - **考点**：代词。`it is ours`，名词性物主代词作表语。
