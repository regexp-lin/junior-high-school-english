# 03-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：健康饮食 (Healthy Eating)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Nowadays, many teenagers love eating fast food. They think burgers and fried chicken taste much 1. ________ (good) than home-cooked meals. But doctors warn that eating too much fast food can lead 2. ________ serious health problems.

A balanced diet is very important for growing teenagers. It means we should eat different kinds of food, 3. ________ (include) rice, vegetables, fruit, meat and milk. Green vegetables and fresh fruit are rich in vitamins, 4. ________ help us stay healthy and fight diseases. Protein from meat and eggs is also 5. ________ (need) for our bodies to grow strong.

Good eating habits are just as important as healthy food. Eating breakfast every day is 6. ________ must. It provides the energy we need for 7. ________ whole morning. Some students skip breakfast because they don't get up early enough. This is a very bad habit that will 8. ________ (certain) affect their study.

Besides, we should drink plenty of water instead 9. ________ sweet drinks. Too much sugar is harmful to our teeth and body.

Remember, you are what you eat. By 10. ________ (choose) our food wisely, we can build a strong body and enjoy a happy life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。than 提示需用比较级，good 的比较级为 better，much 修饰比较级表程度加深。

2. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。lead to 意为"导致"，是固定搭配。

3. **including**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。including 在此相当于介词，意为"包括"，后接并列的名词。

4. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，修饰先行词 vitamins，在从句中作主语。

5. **needed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作表语）。be needed for 意为"对……是被需要的"，也可理解为被动含义。

6. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a must 意为"一件必须做的事"，must 用作名词时是可数名词，此处泛指用不定冠词 a。

7. **the**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。the whole morning 特指"整个上午"，需用定冠词 the。

8. **certainly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 affect 需用副词形式，certain → certainly。

9. **of**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。instead of 意为"而不是"，是固定搭配。

10. **choosing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 by 后接动名词形式，表示"通过……方式"。
