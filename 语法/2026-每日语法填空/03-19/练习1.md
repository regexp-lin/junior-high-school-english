# 03-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：青少年与环境保护 (Environmental Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Protecting the environment is one of the most important tasks for us today. Every year, large amounts of waste 1. ________ (throw) into rivers and oceans. This has caused serious 2. ________ (pollute) to the water that we drink and use every day.

Many countries have taken action to fight 3. ________ this problem. In China, new rules were made to reduce the use of plastic bags. People are encouraged to bring their own bags when going shopping. This small change can really make a big 4. ________ (different).

As middle school students, what can we do? First, we should develop the habit 5. ________ saving water and electricity. Turning off the lights when we leave a room is easy but 6. ________ (help). Second, we can ride bicycles instead of 7. ________ (ask) our parents to drive us to school. It is not only good for the environment, but also good for our health.

Some people think that only 8. ________ (science) can solve environmental problems. However, if everyone does a little, our Earth will become much 9. ________ (clean). Let's start acting now and try 10. ________ (make) our planet a better home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **is thrown**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般现在时）。主语 waste 与 throw 之间是被动关系，waste 为不可数名词，谓语用单数形式 is thrown。

2. **pollution**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。形容词 serious 后需接名词，pollute 的名词形式为 pollution（不可数）。

3. **against**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。fight against 意为"与……作斗争"，是固定搭配。

4. **difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。make a big difference 意为"产生很大的影响"，为固定搭配，需将 different 转为名词 difference。

5. **of**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。the habit of doing sth. 意为"做某事的习惯"。

6. **helpful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。but 连接并列成分，前面 easy 是形容词，后面也需填形容词，help → helpful。

7. **asking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。instead of 是介词短语，后接动名词形式。

8. **scientists**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→名词）+ 复数。此处需要表示"科学家"，science → scientist，且指多位科学家，用复数 scientists。

9. **cleaner**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，clean 的比较级为 cleaner。

10. **to make**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。try to do sth. 意为"尽力做某事"。
