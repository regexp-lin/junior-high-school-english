# 07-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我的好邻居 (My Good Neighbour)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mr. Zhang is our next-door neighbour. He is a man in his (1) ______ (fifty) with grey hair and a kind smile. Although he lives alone, he never seems (2) ______ (feel) lonely. He gets along well with everyone in the building. Last month, a water pipe in our kitchen broke while my parents (3) ______ (work) overtime at the office. I did not know what to do, (4) ______ I knocked on Mr. Zhang's door. He came over at once with his tools and fixed the pipe in less than half (5) ______ hour. When I tried to pay him, he refused and said that helping neighbours was the most (6) ______ (nature) thing in the world. Another time, he found a lost cat and looked (7) ______ it for a whole week until the owner appeared. He often brings home-made biscuits to share (8) ______ the children in our building. My mother says that (9) ______ (have) a good neighbour is better than having a distant relative. I agree. Mr. Zhang has taught me that small acts of (10) ______ (kind) can make a big difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：fifties**
   - **考点**：名词复数表年龄段。`in one's fifties` 表示"在某人五十多岁时"，数词变复数。

2. **答案：to feel**
   - **考点**：不定式。`seem to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"似乎做某事"。

3. **答案：were working**
   - **考点**：过去进行时。`while` 引导时间状语从句，表示水管破裂时父母正在加班，用过去进行时 `were working`。

4. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：因果连词。"不知道怎么办"是原因，"敲邻居的门"是结果，用 `so`。

5. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`half an hour` 为固定搭配，表示"半小时"；`hour` 以元音音素 /aʊ/ 开头，用 `an`。

6. **答案：natural**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most + 形容词 + 名词` 为最高级结构，`nature` 变为形容词 `natural`（自然的）。

7. **答案：after**
   - **考点**：介词（短语动词）。`look after` 为固定搭配，表示"照顾"。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`share sth with sb` 为固定搭配，表示"与某人分享"。

9. **答案：having**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。动名词短语 `having a good neighbour` 在句中作主语。

10. **答案：kindness**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`of` 后接名词，`kind` 变为 `kindness`（善良，善举）。
