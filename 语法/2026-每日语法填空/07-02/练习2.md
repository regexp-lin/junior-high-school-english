# 07-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：雨天的乐趣 (Fun on a Rainy Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many people dislike rainy days, but I think they can be full of fun. Last Sunday, it rained (1) ______ (heavy) all morning. Instead of staying in bed, I decided to do something (2) ______ (create). First, I helped my mother bake (3) ______ chocolate cake. The kitchen was filled with a sweet smell that made the whole house feel warm. After lunch, my little sister and I played board games for two hours. She beat me three times, (4) ______ made her extremely proud. Later, I sat by the window, (5) ______ (listen) to the sound of rain hitting the glass. It was so peaceful (6) ______ I almost fell asleep. In the late afternoon, the rain stopped and a rainbow appeared (7) ______ the sky. My sister and I ran outside (8) ______ (excited) to take photos of it. My father told us that a rainbow (9) ______ (form) when sunlight passes through tiny water drops in the air. That was a new piece of knowledge for me. The rainy day turned out to be one of the (10) ______ (happy) Sundays I can remember.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：heavily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `rained` 需要副词，`heavy` 变为 `heavily`（大量地）。

2. **答案：creative**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`something + 形容词` 为固定结构（不定代词后置形容词），`create` 变为 `creative`（有创意的）。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`a chocolate cake` 表示"一个巧克力蛋糕"，泛指，用不定冠词 `a`。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事（她赢了我三次），在从句中作主语，用 `which`。

5. **答案：listening**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。主语 `I` 与 `listen` 为主动关系，表示"坐在窗边，听着雨声"。

6. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so...that...` 结构表示"如此……以至于……"。

7. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in the sky` 为固定搭配，表示"在天空中"。

8. **答案：excitedly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `ran` 需要副词，`excited` 变为 `excitedly`（兴奋地）。

9. **答案：is formed**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。彩虹"被形成"（自然现象），用一般现在时被动语态；`a rainbow` 为单数，用 `is formed`。

10. **答案：happiest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`happy` 变 y 为 i 加 `-est`，得 `happiest`。
