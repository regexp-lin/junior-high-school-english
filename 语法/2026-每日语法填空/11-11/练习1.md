# 11-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：网购狂欢节 (Online Shopping Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

November 11th, also known as "Double Eleven," has become the (1) ______ (big) online shopping festival in China. Every year, millions of people stay up late on the night of November 10th, (2) ______ (wait) for the sales to begin at midnight. The festival (3) ______ (start) in 2009 as a small promotion by an e-commerce company, and it has grown into a global event since then. Last year, the total sales during the festival (4) ______ (reach) over 500 billion yuan. My mother is a big fan of online shopping. She usually makes a list of things she wants to buy weeks (5) ______ the festival. She compares prices on different platforms and puts items in her shopping cart in advance. On the night of the sale, she clicks the "buy" button as (6) ______ (quick) as possible because popular products often sell (7) ______ within minutes. However, my father thinks that many people buy things they don't really need just because the prices are low. He says it is important to be a (8) ______ (wisdom) consumer and only buy what is (9) ______ (true) necessary. I agree with him. Shopping can be fun, but we should learn to tell the difference (10) ______ "want" and "need."

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the biggest online shopping festival`，定冠词 `the` 提示最高级。

2. **答案：waiting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随状态，用现在分词作状语。

3. **答案：started**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`in 2009` 提示过去具体时间。

4. **答案：reached**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last year` 提示过去时间。

5. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：介词。`weeks before the festival` 表示"节日前几周"。

6. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：副词原级。`as quickly as possible` 表示"尽可能快地"。

7. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词。`sell out` 表示"售罄"。

8. **答案：wise**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `consumer` 需要形容词，`wisdom` 变为 `wise`。

9. **答案：truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `necessary` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：between**
    - **考点**：介词。`the difference between...and...` 表示"……和……之间的区别"。
