# 11-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：友谊 (Friendship)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

True friendship is one of the most (1) ______ (value) things in life. A good friend is someone who stands by you in difficult times and (2) ______ (share) your happiness in good times. I have a best friend called Li Ming. We (3) ______ (know) each other since primary school. We first met on the playground when we were both seven years old. He was sitting alone under a tree, looking (4) ______ (unhappy) at the ground. I walked over and asked him (5) ______ was wrong. He told me that his family had just moved to our city and he did not have any friends. I invited him (6) ______ (play) football with me and my classmates, and we have been close friends ever since. Over the years, we have been (7) ______ each other through many ups and downs. When I failed an important exam last year, Li Ming was the first person to comfort me. He told me that one failure did not mean I was (8) ______ failure, and that I should keep (9) ______ (try). His words gave me the courage to work harder, and I did much (10) ______ (well) in the next exam. I am truly grateful to have such a wonderful friend.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`most valuable`，`value` 变为形容词 `valuable`。

2. **答案：shares**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。主语 `A good friend` 为第三人称单数，与 `stands` 并列。

3. **答案：have known**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`since primary school` 提示从过去持续到现在。

4. **答案：unhappily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `looking at` 需要副词形式。

5. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`asked him what was wrong`，`what` 引导宾语从句并在从句中作主语。

6. **答案：to play**
   - **考点**：不定式。`invite sb to do sth` 表示"邀请某人做某事"。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be with each other` 表示"彼此相伴"。

8. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`a failure` 此处 `failure` 为可数名词，表示"一个失败的人"。

9. **答案：trying**
   - **考点**：动名词。`keep doing sth` 表示"继续做某事"。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：比较级。`much better`，`well` 的比较级为 `better`，`much` 修饰比较级。
