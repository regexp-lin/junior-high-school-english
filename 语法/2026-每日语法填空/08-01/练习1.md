# 08-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：致敬军人 (Respect for Soldiers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

August 1st is Army Day in China, a day (1) ______ we honour the soldiers who protect our country. My uncle is a soldier. He (2) ______ (serve) in the army for twelve years and has been stationed in different parts of the country. Life in the army is not easy. Soldiers wake up before dawn and train for (3) ______ (hour) in all kinds of weather. They must follow strict rules and are often far away (4) ______ their families. My uncle once told me that the (5) ______ (hard) part was missing his daughter's first birthday because he was on duty. However, he said that (6) ______ (protect) the people was his greatest honour. When natural disasters strike, soldiers are always among the first (7) ______ (arrive) at the scene. During the floods last summer, my uncle's unit rescued over three hundred (8) ______ (village). He worked for forty-eight hours without sleep. I am extremely proud (9) ______ him. His dedication has taught me that true courage is not the absence of fear — it is choosing to act bravely (10) ______ others need you most.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a day`（时间），在定语从句中作时间状语，用 `when`。

2. **答案：has served**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`for twelve years` 表示持续到现在，主语 `He` 用 `has served`。

3. **答案：hours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`for hours` 表示"好几个小时"。

4. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`far away from` 为固定搭配，表示"远离"。

5. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + part`，`hard` 的最高级为 `hardest`。

6. **答案：protecting**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。`protecting the people` 在 `that` 从句中作主语。

7. **答案：to arrive**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the first to do sth` 中，序数词修饰的名词后用不定式作定语。

8. **答案：villagers**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`rescued` 后接人，`village` 变为 `villager`（村民），用复数。

9. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`be proud of` 为固定搭配，表示"为……感到骄傲"。

10. **答案：when**
    - **考点**：连词。`when others need you most` 引导时间状语从句，表示"当别人最需要你时"。
