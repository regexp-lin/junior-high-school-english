# 08-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：夜市美食 (Night Market Food)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

One of the things I enjoy most about summer is visiting the night market. There is a famous one near the river in my city (1) ______ opens every evening from six to midnight. The moment you step in, you are greeted by the (2) ______ (smell) of grilled meat, fried noodles, and freshly squeezed juice. The market is always (3) ______ (crowd) with people of all ages. My favourite stall sells stinky tofu. I know it smells (4) ______ (terrible), but the taste is actually amazing. My friend Li Ning prefers the barbecued squid, which is cooked right in front of (5) ______ (he). We usually walk around for an hour, (6) ______ (try) different snacks at each stall. Last time, I discovered a new stall (7) ______ (sell) hand-made ice cream with unusual flavours like lavender and cheese. The ice cream was so good (8) ______ I went back twice. Night markets are more than just places to eat. They reflect the local culture and give small business (9) ______ (own) a chance to earn a living. I think they are one of the most (10) ______ (charm) parts of Chinese city life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a famous one`（物），在定语从句中作主语。

2. **答案：smell(s)**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`smell` 本身可作名词（气味），保持原形或复数均可（多种气味混合）。

3. **答案：crowded**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be crowded with` 为固定搭配，`crowd` 变为 `crowded`。

4. **答案：terrible**
   - **考点**：形容词。`smells` 为系动词，后接形容词，括号内 `terribly` 应变为 `terrible`。

5. **答案：him**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`in front of` 后接宾语，`he` 的宾格为 `him`。

6. **答案：trying**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。走路的同时品尝不同小吃，用现在分词。

7. **答案：selling**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`a new stall selling...` 表示"一个卖……的新摊位"。

8. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so good that...` 表示"好到以至于……"。

9. **答案：owners**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`business owners` 表示"企业主/摊主"，`own` 变为 `owner`，用复数。

10. **答案：charming**
    - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most charming`，`charm` 变为 `charming`（迷人的）。
