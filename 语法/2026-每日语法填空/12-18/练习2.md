# 12-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国风筝 (Chinese Kites)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Kite flying is one of China's oldest folk activities, with a history of over 2,000 years. It is said that the first kite (1) ______ (make) of wood by a Chinese philosopher named Mozi. Later, kites were made from bamboo and silk, making them (2) ______ (light) and easier to fly. The city of Weifang in Shandong Province is known (3) ______ the "Kite Capital of the World." Every April, an international kite festival is held there, (4) ______ (attract) kite enthusiasts from dozens of countries. The festival features kites of all shapes and (5) ______ (size), from simple diamond shapes to elaborate dragons that stretch over 100 metres long. My grandfather is a skilled kite maker. He has been making kites by hand (6) ______ more than forty years. Each kite takes him several days (7) ______ (complete) because he paints every detail himself. His kites are so (8) ______ (beauty) that people often mistake them for works of art. Last spring, he taught me how to make a simple butterfly kite. First, we bent thin bamboo sticks into the shape of wings. Then we covered the frame (9) ______ colourful paper and attached a long tail for balance. When we took it to the park and launched it into the sky, I felt a rush of pride watching something I had made with my own hands (10) ______ (soar) high above the treetops.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was made**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the first kite` 与 `make` 之间是被动关系。

2. **答案：lighter**
   - **考点**：比较级。`lighter and easier`，暗含与木质风筝的比较。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be known as` 表示"被称为"。

4. **答案：attracting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表结果的伴随状语。

5. **答案：sizes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`all shapes and sizes` 为固定搭配。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for more than forty years` 表示持续时间。

7. **答案：to complete**
   - **考点**：不定式。`it takes him several days to complete`。

8. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so beautiful`，系动词后接形容词。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`covered the frame with paper` 表示"用纸覆盖框架"。

10. **答案：soar / soaring**
    - **考点**：感官动词。`watching something soar/soaring`，感官动词后接原形或现在分词。
