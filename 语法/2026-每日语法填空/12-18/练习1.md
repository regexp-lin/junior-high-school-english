# 12-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：交友之道 (Making Friends)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Making friends is an essential part of growing up, but it is not always easy. Some people seem to make friends (1) ______ (effort), while others struggle to connect with their peers. Psychologists say that the key to making friends is (2) ______ (be) genuine and showing interest in other people. If you want others to like you, start by (3) ______ (listen) to them carefully and asking questions about their lives. I moved to a new school at the beginning of Grade 8. I did not know anyone, and I felt very (4) ______ (anxiety) on my first day. During lunch break, I sat alone in the canteen, not knowing (5) ______ to talk to. Then a girl named Zhou Ying sat down next to me and said with a smile, "You look like you could use a friend." That simple sentence changed (6) ______ (every). Zhou Ying introduced me to her friends, and before long, I had a whole group of people to hang out (7) ______. What I learned from this experience is that sometimes all it takes is one (8) ______ (friend) gesture to break the ice. Now, whenever I see a new student looking lost, I make (9) ______ a point to go over and introduce myself. I know from experience how much a simple act of (10) ______ (kind) can mean to someone who feels alone.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：effortlessly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `make friends` 需要副词。

2. **答案：being**
   - **考点**：动名词。`the key to` 中 `to` 为介词，后接动名词。

3. **答案：listening**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词。

4. **答案：anxious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt anxious`，系动词后接形容词。

5. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`not knowing who to talk to`。

6. **答案：everything**
   - **考点**：代词。`changed everything` 表示"改变了一切"。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`hang out with` 表示"和……一起玩"。

8. **答案：friendly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `gesture` 需要形容词。

9. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。`make it a point to` 为固定搭配，表示"特意去"。

10. **答案：kindness**
    - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `of` 后接名词。
