# 03-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：伟大的发明 (Great Inventions)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Throughout history, many great inventions have changed the way we live. Some of them were created hundreds of years ago, 1. ________ they are still important today.

Paper is one of the 2. ________ (great) inventions of ancient China. Before paper was invented, people had to write on bamboo or silk, which was very 3. ________ (convenience). The invention of paper made it much easier to record and spread 4. ________ (know).

The light bulb, invented by Thomas Edison, was another world-changing invention. Before that, people could only use candles 5. ________ oil lamps at night. Edison failed thousands of 6. ________ (time) before he finally succeeded. His story tells us that we should never be afraid of 7. ________ (fail).

In modern times, the smartphone has become one of the most important inventions. It allows us 8. ________ (communicate) with people around the world at any time. We can also use it to take photos, play games and search 9. ________ information. It is hard to imagine life without smartphones.

Every great invention starts with a simple idea. If you have a creative mind and are willing to work hard, maybe you will be the next person to change 10. ________ world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后句构成转折关系："几百年前创造的，但今天仍然重要"。

2. **greatest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，great → greatest。

3. **inconvenient**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）+ 前缀。was 后接形容词作表语，convenience → convenient，但根据语境"写在竹简或丝绸上很不方便"，需加否定前缀 in-，答案为 inconvenient。

4. **knowledge**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。spread 后接名词作宾语，know → knowledge（不可数名词）。

5. **or**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。candles or oil lamps 表示选择关系，"蜡烛或油灯"。

6. **times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。thousands of times 意为"数千次"，time 表示"次数"时为可数名词。

7. **failure**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。介词 of 后接名词，fail → failure。be afraid of failure 意为"害怕失败"。

8. **to communicate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。allow sb. to do sth. 意为"允许某人做某事"。

9. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。search for 意为"搜索"，是固定搭配。

10. **the**
    - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。change the world 意为"改变世界"，the world 特指"这个世界"。
