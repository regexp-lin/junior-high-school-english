# 03-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：善良与助人 (Kindness and Helping Others)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A small act of kindness can make a big difference in someone's day. I experienced this 1. ________ (I) last month.

One rainy afternoon, I was walking home from school 2. ________ an umbrella. The rain was getting heavier and heavier. I was completely wet and feeling 3. ________ (hope). Just then, an old lady came up to me and shared her umbrella with me. She walked me all the way to the bus stop, even though it was not on 4. ________ (she) way home.

I was deeply 5. ________ (move) by her kindness. Before she left, I thanked her and asked for her name. She just smiled and said, "You don't need to know my name. Just remember 6. ________ (help) others when they need it."

Her words stayed in my mind. The following week, I saw a little boy 7. ________ (cry) on the street because he was lost. I took him to the nearest police station and waited until his parents came. The look of 8. ________ (grateful) on his parents' faces made me feel warm inside.

Kindness is like a chain — when someone does something nice for you, you want to pass it 9. ________ to others. It doesn't have to be something big. A warm smile, a helping hand, or even a kind word can brighten someone's day.

Let's make the world a 10. ________ (warm) place by spreading kindness wherever we go.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 I，强调"亲自经历"，用反身代词 myself。

2. **without**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。根据后文"雨越来越大，我全身湿透了"，说明是"没带伞"，用 without。

3. **hopeless**
   - **考点**：词性转换/构词法（名词→形容词）。feel 是系动词，后接形容词作表语。hope → hopeless（绝望的），加后缀 -less 表示否定。

4. **her**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 way，she → her。

5. **moved**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→过去分词/形容词）。be moved by 意为"被……感动"，主语是人，用 -ed 形式。

6. **to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。remember to do sth. 意为"记得去做某事"（指未来的动作）。

7. **crying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。see sb. doing sth. 意为"看见某人正在做某事"，强调动作正在进行。

8. **gratitude**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。介词 of 后接名词，grateful → gratitude（感激之情）。

9. **on**
   - **考点**：副词/介词搭配（无提示词填空）。pass it on 意为"传递下去"，是固定搭配。

10. **warmer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。根据语境"让世界变成一个更温暖的地方"，暗含比较意义，warm → warmer。
