# 12-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国花灯 (Chinese Lanterns)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese lanterns have been a symbol of celebration and good fortune for over 2,000 years. They are traditionally made from bamboo frames covered with silk or paper and lit from inside with candles. Today, lanterns come in all shapes and sizes, from simple round ones to elaborate (1) ______ (create) shaped like dragons, phoenixes and lotus flowers. The most important occasion for lanterns is the Lantern Festival, which falls (2) ______ the fifteenth day of the first lunar month. On this night, people gather in parks and along rivers to admire (3) ______ (thousand) of glowing lanterns. Children carry small lanterns through the streets, and families try to solve riddles (4) ______ (write) on the lanterns — a tradition known as "guessing lantern riddles." The city of Zigong in Sichuan Province is famous (5) ______ its spectacular lantern festivals. Every year, artists create massive lantern displays using millions of LED lights and tons of fabric. The festivals attract (6) ______ (visit) from all over the world. My family went to the Zigong Lantern Festival two years ago. It was the most (7) ______ (visually) stunning event I had ever attended. The lanterns were so large that some of them were (8) ______ (tall) than three-storey buildings. My favourite was a giant dragon lantern that stretched across an entire lake, its scales shimmering with (9) ______ (change) colours. I took dozens of photos, but none of them could truly capture the (10) ______ (magic) atmosphere of that evening.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：creations**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`elaborate creations`，形容词后接名词复数。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。具体某一天用 `on`。

3. **答案：thousands**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`thousands of glowing lanterns`。

4. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`riddles written on the lanterns`，被动关系。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be famous for` 表示"因……而著名"。

6. **答案：visitors**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`attract visitors`，动词后接名词复数。

7. **答案：visually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `stunning` 需要副词。

8. **答案：taller**
   - **考点**：比较级。`taller than three-storey buildings`。

9. **答案：changing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`changing colours` 表示"变换的色彩"。

10. **答案：magical**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `atmosphere` 需要形容词。
