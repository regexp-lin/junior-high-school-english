# 12-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：告别晚会 (Farewell Party)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Farewell parties are special occasions where people come together to say goodbye to someone who is leaving. They are often filled with mixed emotions — happiness for the person's new journey and (1) ______ (sad) about the upcoming separation. Last Friday, our class held a farewell party for our English teacher, Ms Wang, who is (2) ______ (move) to another city. Ms Wang has taught us for three years, and she is the most (3) ______ (influence) teacher I have ever had. She made English lessons fun and (4) ______ (engage) by using games, role-plays and real-life scenarios. She always believed in us, even when we did not believe in (5) ______ (our). We spent a whole week (6) ______ (prepare) for the party. Some students decorated the classroom with balloons and streamers, while others prepared performances. I was part of a group that made a photo slideshow (7) ______ (feature) our best moments with Ms Wang over the past three years. When the slideshow played, many students (8) ______ (not can) hold back their tears. The most touching moment was when our class monitor presented Ms Wang with a handmade book. Inside, every student had written a personal message (9) ______ (thank) her for everything she had done. Ms Wang read some of the messages aloud and cried. She told us that being our teacher had been one of the (10) ______ (great) privileges of her life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：sadness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `happiness` 并列，需要名词。

2. **答案：moving**
   - **考点**：现在进行时。`is moving to another city` 表示即将搬走。

3. **答案：influential**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most influential teacher`，修饰名词需要形容词。

4. **答案：engaging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `fun` 并列，修饰事物用 `-ing`。

5. **答案：ourselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`we did not believe in ourselves`。

6. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth`。

7. **答案：featuring**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`a photo slideshow featuring our best moments`。

8. **答案：could not**
   - **考点**：情态动词。`could not hold back their tears` 表示"忍不住流泪"。

9. **答案：thanking**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表目的/伴随。

10. **答案：greatest**
    - **考点**：最高级。`one of the greatest privileges`。
