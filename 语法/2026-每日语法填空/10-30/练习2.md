# 10-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：少年宫 (Children's Palace)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Children's Palace is a place (1) ______ young people can learn and develop new skills outside of school. Our city's Children's Palace offers dozens of courses, including painting, dance, robotics and foreign (2) ______ (language). I have been attending a calligraphy class there (3) ______ last September. Every Saturday morning, I spend two hours practising with a (4) ______ (profession) calligraphy teacher. The class is (5) ______ (small) than my regular school classes — only twelve students — so the teacher can give each of us more personal attention. What I enjoy most about the Children's Palace is the chance (6) ______ (meet) students from different schools who share the same (7) ______ (interested). My calligraphy classmate, Xiao Yun, goes to a school on the other side of the city. We have become close friends and often practise together on weekdays (8) ______ video call. The Children's Palace also organises exhibitions and (9) ______ (compete) where students can showcase their work. Last month, one of my calligraphy pieces (10) ______ (select) for the annual exhibition. I was thrilled to see my work displayed alongside those of much more experienced students. The Children's Palace has played an important role in my growth.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a place`（地点），定语从句缺地点状语。

2. **答案：languages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`foreign languages`，多种语言。

3. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。`since last September`，与现在完成进行时搭配。

4. **答案：professional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `teacher` 需要形容词，`profession` 变为 `professional`。

5. **答案：smaller**
   - **考点**：比较级。`smaller than my regular classes`。

6. **答案：to meet**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the chance to meet`，不定式作后置定语。

7. **答案：interests**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`the same interests`，`interested` 变为名词 `interest`，复数。

8. **答案：by / via**
   - **考点**：介词。`by/via video call` 表示"通过视频通话"。

9. **答案：competitions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。与 `exhibitions` 并列，`compete` 变为名词 `competition`，复数。

10. **答案：was selected**
    - **考点**：被动语态。`one of my pieces was selected`，"被选中"。
