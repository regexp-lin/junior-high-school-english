# 10-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园音乐会 (School Concert)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Music has always been an important part of school life. Every year, our school holds a concert to showcase students' musical (1) ______ (talented). This year, I was selected to perform a piano piece at the concert. I practised for two months (2) ______ (prepare) for the big day. My piano teacher chose a piece by Beethoven that was both (3) ______ (technique) challenging and emotionally moving. On the evening of the concert, the school hall was filled (4) ______ students, parents and teachers. I peeked out from behind the curtain and felt my stomach tighten with nervousness. "Just enjoy the music," my teacher whispered (5) ______ (soft) to me. When it was my turn, I walked to the piano and sat down. The spotlight was (6) ______ bright that I could barely see the audience. I placed my fingers on the keys, took a deep breath and began to play. The first few notes sounded (7) ______ (shake), but gradually I found my rhythm. By the middle of the piece, I had completely forgotten about the audience and was lost (8) ______ the music. When I played the last note and the hall erupted in applause, I felt an (9) ______ (describe) sense of joy. It was one of the (10) ______ (proud) moments of my life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：talent / talents**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`musical talent(s)`，`talented` 变为名词 `talent`。

2. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：不定式。`practised to prepare`，不定式表目的。

3. **答案：technically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `challenging` 需要副词，`technique` 变为 `technically`。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`was filled with` 表示"充满了"。

5. **答案：softly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `whispered` 需要副词。

6. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。`so bright that...` 表示"如此亮以至于"。

7. **答案：shaky**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`sounded shaky`，系动词后接形容词，`shake` 变为 `shaky`。

8. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be lost in the music` 表示"沉浸在音乐中"。

9. **答案：indescribable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加否定前缀）。`an indescribable sense of joy`，表"难以描述的"。

10. **答案：proudest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the proudest moments`。
