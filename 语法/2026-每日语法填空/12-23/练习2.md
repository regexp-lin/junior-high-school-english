# 12-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园安全 (Campus Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Campus safety is a matter of great importance that affects every student and teacher. Schools have a (1) ______ (responsible) to provide a safe environment where students can learn without worry. Over the past few years, our school (2) ______ (introduce) several measures to improve campus security. First, security cameras have been installed at all (3) ______ (enter) and in corridors. These cameras operate 24 hours a day and help monitor any unusual activities. Second, visitors are now required to register at the front gate and show identification before they are allowed (4) ______ (enter) the campus. This ensures that no (5) ______ (authorise) person can get in. Third, the school has hired additional security guards who patrol the campus throughout the day. Our school also pays close attention (6) ______ students' mental health, which is an often overlooked aspect of campus safety. A team of professional counsellors is available for students who feel (7) ______ (stress), anxious or depressed. They offer both individual sessions and group workshops. Last month, they organised a workshop on (8) ______ (deal) with exam anxiety, which was attended by over 100 students. Many participants said they found the strategies very (9) ______ (help). I believe that a truly safe campus is one where students feel protected not only (10) ______ (physical) but also emotionally.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a responsibility`，冠词后接名词。

2. **答案：has introduced**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Over the past few years` 提示。

3. **答案：entrances**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`all entrances`，`enter` 变为名词复数。

4. **答案：to enter**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be allowed to do sth`。

5. **答案：unauthorised**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`no unauthorised person`，加否定前缀。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`pay attention to` 表示"注意"。

7. **答案：stressed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。描述人的感受用 `-ed`。

8. **答案：dealing**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `on` 后接动名词。

9. **答案：helpful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found the strategies very helpful`。

10. **答案：physically**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰被动含义需要副词，与 `emotionally` 并列。
