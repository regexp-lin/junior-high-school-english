# 12-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：圣诞节传统 (Christmas Traditions)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Christmas is one of the most (1) ______ (wide) celebrated festivals in the Western world. It falls on December 25th every year and is a time for family, generosity and joy. The traditions associated (2) ______ Christmas vary from country to country, but some customs are universal. In most Western households, families set up a Christmas tree and decorate it with lights, ornaments and a star on top. Children hang stockings by the fireplace, hoping that Santa Claus (3) ______ (fill) them with presents overnight. On Christmas morning, they rush downstairs to open the gifts (4) ______ (wrap) in colourful paper under the tree. A traditional Christmas dinner usually includes roast turkey, mashed potatoes, cranberry sauce and pudding. In the UK, people also enjoy (5) ______ (pull) Christmas crackers, which contain small toys, paper crowns and jokes. My English teacher, who comes from Canada, shared her Christmas memories with us last week. She told us that her family always (6) ______ (bake) gingerbread cookies together on Christmas Eve. She described the smell of (7) ______ (fresh) baked cookies filling the house as one of her (8) ______ (fond) childhood memories. Although Christmas is not a traditional Chinese holiday, it has become (9) ______ (increase) popular among young people in China. Many shopping centres put up Christmas decorations, and friends exchange small gifts. I think learning about different festivals helps us (10) ______ (broad) our understanding of the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `celebrated` 需要副词。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`associated with` 表示"与……相关"。

3. **答案：will fill**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。孩子们希望圣诞老人将会装满。

4. **答案：wrapped**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`gifts wrapped in colourful paper`，被动关系。

5. **答案：pulling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`enjoy doing sth`。

6. **答案：baked**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的习惯。

7. **答案：freshly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `baked` 需要副词。

8. **答案：fondest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of her fondest childhood memories`。

9. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `popular` 需要副词。

10. **答案：broaden**
    - **考点**：动词原形。`help sb do sth`，`broad` 变为动词 `broaden`。
