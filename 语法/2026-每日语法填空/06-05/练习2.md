# 06-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：未来的学校 (Schools in the Future)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

What will schools look like thirty years from now? Many people believe that heavy schoolbags (1) ______ (replace) by light tablets, and students will be able (2) ______ study any subject at their own pace. Classrooms may turn into open spaces (3) ______ robots help carry books and clean the floor. Teachers will spend (4) ______ (little) time checking homework because smart systems can (5) ______ (mark) exercises in seconds, (6) ______ they will focus more on guiding creative projects. Science lessons might take place in virtual labs (7) ______ students can explore dangerous places safely through VR. Of course, face-to-face talks will still matter, so schools will become more (8) ______ (flex) than today, but the biggest challenge is (9) ______ (make) sure every child has equal access to technology. I believe future schools will be (10) ______ (friend) places than they are now, and every learner will feel supported there.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：will be replaced**  
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。`thirty years from now` 表将来；书包“被取代”，用一般将来时的被动语态 `will be replaced`。

2. **答案：to**  
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be able to do sth.` 中须填 `to`。

3. **答案：where**  
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `open spaces` 表地点，从句结构完整，用关系副词 `where` 作地点状语。

4. **答案：less**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与“系统秒批作业”形成对比，表示老师花在批改上的时间“更少”；`time` 不可数，用 `little` 的比较级 `less`。

5. **答案：mark**  
   - **考点**：动词形式。情态动词 `can` 后接动词原形，故填 `mark`。

6. **答案：so**  
   - **考点**：连词。前后为因果关系：因为系统自动批改，**所以**老师能把精力放在创意项目上，用 `so` 引导结果。

7. **答案：where**  
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `virtual labs` 表抽象地点，从句说明在实验室里能做什么，用 `where`。

8. **答案：flexible**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。`become more` 后接形容词；括号内 `flex` 为词根，须变为形容词 `flexible`，表示“更灵活”。

9. **答案：to make**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`The challenge is to do...` 表语用不定式，说明挑战的具体内容，填 `to make`。`make sure` 为固定搭配。

10. **答案：friendlier / more friendly**  
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 `than they are now`，须用比较级；`friendly` 的比较级为 `friendlier`，也可用 `more friendly`。括号内为 `friend`，应写出正确的形容词比较级形式 **friendlier**（或 **more friendly**）。
