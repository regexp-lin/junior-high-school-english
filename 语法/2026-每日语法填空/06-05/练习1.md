# 06-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界环境日 (World Environment Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

World Environment Day falls (1) ______ June 5th every year. It reminds us that the earth is (2) ______ only home we have, so protecting nature is everyone's duty. This year's theme encouraged young people (3) ______ (take) small steps, such as refusing plastic bags and riding bikes (4) ______ of driving short distances. Our city held a speech contest last week, and the (5) ______ (win) ideas were shared on the Internet. Many students said they would plant trees on hills that (6) ______ (lose) green cover because of fires years ago. Clean air and fresh water are not endless resources; once they (7) ______ (pollute), it takes a long time for nature (8) ______ (recover). If we work together today, future generations will still be able (9) ______ enjoy blue skies and singing birds. Let World Environment Day be (10) ______ start of daily action, not just a single day's celebration.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**  
   - **考点**：介词。具体日期前用 `on`，`on June 5th`。

2. **答案：the**  
   - **考点**：冠词。`only` 修饰名词时，前面常用定冠词 `the`，`the only home`。

3. **答案：to take**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`encourage sb. to do sth.` 用不定式 `to take`。

4. **答案：instead**  
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`instead of` 表示“而不是”，后接动名词 `driving`。

5. **答案：winning**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the winning ideas` 表示“获奖/胜出的创意”，用现在分词 `winning` 作定语修饰 `ideas`。括号内为动词 `win`，需变为起形容词作用的形式。

6. **答案：lost**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。定语从句说明“多年前因火灾失去植被的山坡”，动作发生在过去，与 `years ago` 呼应，用一般过去时 `lost`。若强调“在过去的过去”，也可用 `had lost`，此处 **lost** 即可。

7. **答案：are polluted**  
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。`they` 指 `air and water`，与 `pollute` 被动；`once` 引导条件从句，用一般现在时表将来可能，**are polluted**。

8. **答案：to recover**  
   - **考点**：固定句型。`It takes + time + for sb./sth. + to do`，用不定式 `to recover`。

9. **答案：to**  
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`be able to do sth.` 填 `to`。

10. **答案：the**  
    - **考点**：冠词。`the start of...` 表示“……的开端”，特指用 `the`。
