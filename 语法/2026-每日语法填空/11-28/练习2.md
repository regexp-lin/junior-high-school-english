# 11-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：消防安全 (Fire Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Fire safety is something that every student should take (1) ______ (serious). According to statistics, thousands of fires occur in schools and residential buildings in China each year, and many of them are caused by (2) ______ (care) behaviour. Common causes include leaving cooking unattended, overloading electrical sockets and (3) ______ (smoke) in non-smoking areas. Our school held a fire safety drill last month. A firefighter came to explain the correct procedures to follow in case (4) ______ a fire. He told us that the first thing to do is to stay calm and not to panic. If the fire is small, we should try to put it out (5) ______ a fire extinguisher. If the fire is too large to control, we must leave the building (6) ______ (immediate) through the nearest exit. The firefighter also taught us several (7) ______ (save) tips. For example, if a room is filled with smoke, we should cover our nose and mouth with a wet cloth and crawl along the floor, because the air near the ground is (8) ______ (clean) than the air above. We should never use the lift during a fire. After the talk, the fire alarm (9) ______ (ring), and all students practised evacuating the building. We managed to get everyone out in under four minutes, which the firefighter said was a very good result. He reminded us that being (10) ______ (prepare) for emergencies could save lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`take seriously` 表示"认真对待"，需要副词形式。

2. **答案：careless**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `behaviour` 需要形容词，根据语境用 `careless`（粗心的）。

3. **答案：smoking**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `leaving` 和 `overloading` 并列。

4. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`in case of` 表示"万一发生"。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`put it out with a fire extinguisher` 表示"用灭火器扑灭"。

6. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `leave` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：life-saving**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`life-saving tips` 表示"救命技巧"，`save` 变为复合形容词。

8. **答案：cleaner**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than the air above` 提示比较级。

9. **答案：rang**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述演习过程，`ring` 的过去式为 `rang`。

10. **答案：prepared**
    - **考点**：过去分词作表语。`being prepared for` 表示"为……做好准备"。
