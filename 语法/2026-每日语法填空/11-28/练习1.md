# 11-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：感恩的意义 (The Meaning of Gratitude)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Gratitude is the quality of being thankful and showing (1) ______ (appreciate) for the good things in life. Psychologists say that people who practise gratitude regularly are generally (2) ______ (happy) and healthier than those who do not. Yet in our busy daily lives, we often forget to be grateful for the things we have. I used to take many things for granted — my parents' hard work, my teachers' patience and my (3) ______ (friend) support. It was not until I went through a difficult experience (4) ______ I truly understood the value of gratitude. Last winter, I fell seriously ill and had to stay in hospital for two weeks. During that time, my mother stayed by my side day and night, (5) ______ (sleep) on a small chair next to my bed. My classmates took turns (6) ______ (bring) me homework and class notes so that I would not fall behind. My best friend even recorded the lessons on her phone for me (7) ______ (watch). When I finally recovered and returned to school, I felt a deep sense of (8) ______ (thankful) that I had never felt before. Since then, I (9) ______ (keep) a gratitude journal. Every night before bed, I write down three things I am grateful for. This simple habit has made me more positive and (10) ______ (content) with my life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：appreciation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`showing appreciation`，动词后接名词作宾语。

2. **答案：happier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than those who do not` 提示比较级。

3. **答案：friends'**
   - **考点**：名词所有格。`friends' support` 表示"朋友们的支持"，复数名词加 `'`。

4. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：强调句型。`It was not until...that...` 为强调句型。

5. **答案：sleeping**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随状态。

6. **答案：bringing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`take turns doing sth` 表示"轮流做某事"。

7. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：不定式。`for me to watch` 表示目的。

8. **答案：thankfulness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `of` 后接名词。

9. **答案：have kept**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 提示用现在完成时。

10. **答案：content**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `positive` 并列，`content` 本身即为形容词，表示"满足的"。
