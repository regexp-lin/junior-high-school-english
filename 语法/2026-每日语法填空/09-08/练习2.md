# 09-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：风筝节 (Kite Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Weifang, a city in Shandong Province, is known (1) ______ the "Kite Capital of the World". Every April, the International Kite Festival (2) ______ (hold) there, attracting visitors from all over the world. Last year, my family travelled to Weifang (3) ______ (experience) the festival for the first time. The sky was filled with kites of all shapes and (4) ______ (size) — dragons, butterflies, fish and even cartoon characters. The (5) ______ (large) kite I saw was over 100 metres long! It took a team of ten people (6) ______ (fly) it. I was amazed at how skilfully the kite (7) ______ (maker) had designed each one. My father bought me a small eagle kite, and I spent the whole afternoon (8) ______ (try) to fly it. At first, it kept crashing to the ground, (9) ______ with some help from a friendly local man, I finally got it to soar high in the sky. It was one of the (10) ______ (happy) moments of my holiday. I hope to visit Weifang again someday and learn more about the art of kite-making.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be known as` 表示"被称为"。

2. **答案：is held**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the festival is held` 表示节日"被举办"，每年惯例用一般现在时。

3. **答案：to experience**
   - **考点**：不定式。`travelled to Weifang to experience...`，不定式表目的。

4. **答案：sizes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`all shapes and sizes`，与 `shapes` 并列用复数。

5. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the largest kite I saw`，用最高级。

6. **答案：to fly**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It took sb (time) to do sth` 是固定句型。

7. **答案：makers**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`kite makers`，`make` 变为表示人的名词 `maker`，此处多位制作者用复数。

8. **答案：trying**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth`，用动名词。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`kept crashing...but...finally got it to soar`，前后转折。

10. **答案：happiest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the happiest moments`，`happy` 的最高级为 `happiest`。
