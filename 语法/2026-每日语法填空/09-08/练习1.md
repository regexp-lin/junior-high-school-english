# 09-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：早餐的重要性 (The Importance of Breakfast)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Breakfast is often called the most important meal of the day, (1) ______ many students skip it because they get up too late. A recent survey at our school showed that nearly 30% of students rarely eat breakfast. This is (2) ______ (worry) because studies have proved that eating a good breakfast can improve memory and (3) ______ (concentrate). Without breakfast, students may feel tired and find it hard (4) ______ (pay) attention in class. My mother always makes sure I have a (5) ______ (health) breakfast before leaving home. She usually prepares eggs, milk and some fruit. She believes that a balanced breakfast gives me the energy I need to study (6) ______ (efficient) throughout the morning. Last week, our school (7) ______ (invite) a nutritionist to give a talk about healthy eating habits. She explained that a good breakfast should include protein, whole grains (8) ______ fresh fruit or vegetables. She also warned us (9) ______ eating too much junk food for breakfast, such as chips or sweets. After the talk, several of my classmates decided to change (10) ______ (they) breakfast habits. I hope they will stick to it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前后语义转折，"早餐很重要但很多学生跳过"。

2. **答案：worrying**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`This is worrying`，`worry` 变为现在分词形容词，表"令人担忧的"。

3. **答案：concentration**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `memory` 并列作宾语，需要名词，`concentrate` 变为 `concentration`。

4. **答案：to pay**
   - **考点**：不定式。`find it hard to do sth`，`it` 为形式宾语，真正宾语是不定式。

5. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `breakfast` 需要形容词，`health` 变为 `healthy`。

6. **答案：efficiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `study` 需要副词，`efficient` 变为 `efficiently`。

7. **答案：invited**
   - **考点**：时态。`last week` 提示用一般过去时。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`protein, whole grains and fresh fruit`，`and` 连接并列成分。

9. **答案：against**
   - **考点**：介词。`warn sb against doing sth` 表示"警告某人不要做某事"。

10. **答案：their**
    - **考点**：代词所有格。`change their breakfast habits`，修饰名词用形容词性物主代词。
