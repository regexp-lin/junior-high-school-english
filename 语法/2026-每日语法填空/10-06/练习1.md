# 10-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校编程课 (School Programming Class)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Programming, or coding, is becoming an (1) ______ (essential) skill in the modern world. This term, our school added a programming class to the curriculum. Every Tuesday, we learn the basics of Python, (2) ______ is one of the most popular programming languages. Our teacher, Mr. Guo, says that learning to code teaches us (3) ______ (think) logically and solve problems step by step. In the first few weeks, we learned simple commands like printing text on the screen. It was easy and fun. But as the lessons (4) ______ (become) more complex, many students started to struggle. I spent hours trying to fix a bug in my programme, only (5) ______ (discover) that I had missed a single comma. Mr. Guo laughed and said, "Welcome (6) ______ the world of coding — where a tiny mistake can stop everything from (7) ______ (work)!" Despite the (8) ______ (challenge), I find programming deeply satisfying. There is nothing quite like the feeling of seeing your code run (9) ______ (success) after hours of effort. I believe that understanding coding will give us an advantage in (10) ______ (what) career we choose in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：essential**
   - **考点**：词性。`an essential skill`，修饰名词需要形容词，此处 `essential` 已是形容词。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

3. **答案：to think**
   - **考点**：不定式。`teach sb to do sth` 表示"教某人做某事"。

4. **答案：became**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去发生的事用一般过去时。

5. **答案：to discover**
   - **考点**：不定式。`only to discover` 表示出乎意料的结果。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`Welcome to` 表示"欢迎来到"。

7. **答案：working**
   - **考点**：动名词。`stop sth from doing`，介词 `from` 后接动名词。

8. **答案：challenges**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`despite the challenges`，`challenge` 用复数。

9. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `run` 需要副词，`success` 变为 `successfully`。

10. **答案：whatever**
    - **考点**：连接代词。`whatever career we choose`，`whatever` 表示"无论什么"。
