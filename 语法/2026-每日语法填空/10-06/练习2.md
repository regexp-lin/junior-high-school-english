# 10-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：陪伴老人 (Keeping the Elderly Company)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many elderly people in China live alone because their children work in (1) ______ (distance) cities. Loneliness is a serious problem that affects their physical and mental (2) ______ (healthy). Our school's volunteer club organises regular visits to a local nursing home to keep the elderly company. Last Saturday, I went there with ten other students. We were divided (3) ______ small groups and assigned to different rooms. I was paired with Mrs. Zhang, an 80-year-old woman (4) ______ had been living in the nursing home for five years. She was very happy to see me and immediately started (5) ______ (show) me old photos of her family. She pointed to a photo of a young woman and said (6) ______ (proud), "That's my daughter. She's a doctor in Shanghai." As she talked, I noticed her eyes (7) ______ (light) up with joy. I stayed with her for two hours, listening to her stories and (8) ______ (help) her fold clothes. When it was time to leave, she held my hand (9) ______ (tight) and said, "Thank you for coming. It means more than you know." Her words touched me (10) ______ (deep). I promised to visit her again soon. Sometimes, the best gift we can give someone is simply our time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：distant**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `cities` 需要形容词，`distance` 变为 `distant`。

2. **答案：health**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`physical and mental health`，与形容词搭配需要名词，`healthy` 变为 `health`。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`be divided into` 表示"被分成"。

4. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `an 80-year-old woman`（指人），引导定语从句。

5. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`start doing sth` 表示"开始做某事"。

6. **答案：proudly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `said` 需要副词，`proud` 变为 `proudly`。

7. **答案：light**
   - **考点**：感官动词。`noticed her eyes light up`，`notice + 宾语 + 动词原形`。

8. **答案：helping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。与 `listening` 并列，用现在分词。

9. **答案：tightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `held` 需要副词，`tight` 变为 `tightly`。

10. **答案：deeply**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `touched` 需要副词，`deep` 变为 `deeply`。
