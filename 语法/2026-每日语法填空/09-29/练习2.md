# 09-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：动物园里的大熊猫 (Giant Pandas at the Zoo)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Giant pandas are one of the world's most beloved animals and a national treasure of China. Last spring, my family visited the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding, (1) ______ is the most famous panda centre in the world. We arrived early in the morning because pandas are most (2) ______ (activity) in the cool morning hours. The first thing we saw was a group of baby pandas (3) ______ (roll) around on the grass. They were so adorable that everyone around me started taking photos (4) ______ (immediate). An adult panda sat nearby, (5) ______ (quiet) munching on bamboo. A guide told us that an adult panda can eat up to 38 kilograms of bamboo (6) ______ day. That's an incredible amount! She also explained the (7) ______ (difficult) of panda conservation. In the wild, pandas face threats from habitat loss and a very low birth rate. Thanks (8) ______ decades of conservation efforts, the panda population has grown from about 1,000 in the 1980s to over 1,800 today. However, they are still classified (9) ______ a vulnerable species. Before we left, I bought a small panda toy as a souvenir. It reminds me that protecting wildlife is everyone's (10) ______ (responsible).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

2. **答案：active**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`most active`，系动词后接形容词，`activity` 变为 `active`。

3. **答案：rolling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`saw baby pandas rolling around`，感官动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词。

4. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词短语 `started taking` 需要副词，`immediate` 变为 `immediately`。

5. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `munching` 需要副词，`quiet` 变为 `quietly`。

6. **答案：a / per**
   - **考点**：冠词/介词。`38 kilograms a/per day` 表示"每天38公斤"。

7. **答案：difficulty / difficulties**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the difficulty of`，`difficult` 变为名词 `difficulty`。

8. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`thanks to` 表示"多亏了"。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be classified as` 表示"被归类为"。

10. **答案：responsibility**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`everyone's responsibility`，所有格后接名词，`responsible` 变为 `responsibility`。
