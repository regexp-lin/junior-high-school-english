# 09-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校篮球联赛 (School Basketball League)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school's annual basketball league started last Monday and will last for three weeks. All nine classes in Grade Nine have formed teams and are competing (1) ______ each other. Our class team has five players, and I am the (2) ______ (short) one, but our coach says height is not everything in basketball. What matters most is teamwork and (3) ______ (determine). Our first game was against Class Two, which had some of the (4) ______ (tall) players in our grade. Before the game, our captain reminded us (5) ______ (focus) on defence and pass the ball quickly. The game was intense from the very beginning. Class Two scored first, (6) ______ we didn't lose hope. In the second quarter, I made a three-point shot that brought our team back into the game. My teammates were so excited that they (7) ______ (lift) me off the ground in celebration. In the end, we won 48–45. It was a (8) ______ (narrow) victory, but it felt amazing. After the game, our PE teacher praised us for our (9) ______ (excellent) performance. He said our teamwork was the (10) ______ (good) he had seen all year. We are now looking forward to the next round.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：against**
   - **考点**：介词。`competing against each other` 表示"互相竞争"。

2. **答案：shortest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the shortest one`。

3. **答案：determination**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `teamwork` 并列，需要名词，`determine` 变为 `determination`。

4. **答案：tallest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`some of the tallest players`。

5. **答案：to focus**
   - **考点**：不定式。`remind sb to do sth` 表示"提醒某人做某事"。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`scored first but we didn't lose hope`，前后转折。

7. **答案：lifted**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

8. **答案：narrow**
   - **考点**：形容词。`a narrow victory` 表示"险胜"。

9. **答案：excellent**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `performance` 需要形容词，`excellence` 变为 `excellent`。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the best he had seen`。
