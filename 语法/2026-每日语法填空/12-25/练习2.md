# 12-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：社区花园 (Community Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Community gardens are shared green spaces where residents come together to grow vegetables, fruits and flowers. They bring neighbours closer and turn empty urban land (1) ______ productive, beautiful gardens. Our neighbourhood started a community garden last spring on a piece of (2) ______ (use) land behind the apartment buildings. A group of retired residents, (3) ______ (lead) by Uncle Zhang, cleared the rubbish, prepared the soil and divided the space into small plots. Each family was given one plot and encouraged (4) ______ (grow) whatever they liked. My family chose to plant tomatoes, cucumbers and spring onions. My father, who had grown up in the countryside, was (5) ______ (delight) to get his hands dirty again. He taught me the basics of planting: how to space the seeds, when to water them and how to deal (6) ______ common pests. The garden has brought many (7) ______ (expect) benefits to our community. Neighbours who previously never spoke to each other now chat regularly while tending their plots. Children learn where their food comes from by (8) ______ (watch) vegetables grow from tiny seeds. Several elderly residents say that gardening has improved their (9) ______ (physically) and mental health. Our community garden is proof that even a small piece of land can make a big difference when people work together. I hope more communities (10) ______ (follow) our example in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`turn...into...` 表示"把……变成……"。

2. **答案：unused**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`unused land`，加否定前缀表示"未使用的"。

3. **答案：led**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`led by Uncle Zhang`，被动关系。

4. **答案：to grow**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be encouraged to do sth`。

5. **答案：delighted**
   - **考点**：词性转换。描述人的感受用 `-ed`。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`deal with` 表示"处理"。

7. **答案：unexpected**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`unexpected benefits`，加否定前缀表示"意想不到的"。

8. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词。

9. **答案：physical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `health` 需要形容词，与 `mental` 并列。

10. **答案：will follow**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。`in the future` 提示将来时。
