# 12-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：假期阅读 (Holiday Reading)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The winter holiday is the perfect time for students to catch up on (1) ______ (they) reading. Without the pressure of daily homework and exams, we can finally pick up books we have been wanting to read (2) ______ a long time. Our school library prepares a special "Holiday Reading List" every year, (3) ______ (recommend) books that are suitable for students of different ages and interests. This year, the list includes classic novels, popular science books, biographies and poetry collections. My English teacher suggested that I read "Charlotte's Web" during the holiday. She said it was one of the most (4) ______ (beautiful) written children's books in the English language. I started reading it on the first day of the holiday and (5) ______ (finish) it in just three days because I simply could not put it down. The story of the friendship between a pig named Wilbur and a spider named Charlotte was both (6) ______ (heart) and thought-provoking. It made me think about the true meaning of friendship and sacrifice. My mother always says that a person who reads (7) ______ (regular) is like a tree with deep roots — strong, wise and full of life. I agree with her. Reading has not only expanded my vocabulary (8) ______ also opened my mind to new ideas and perspectives. This holiday, I plan (9) ______ (read) at least five more books. I believe that every book I read makes me a little (10) ______ (good) version of myself.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 `reading` 需要形容词性物主代词。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for a long time` 表示持续时间。

3. **答案：recommending**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随/补充说明。

4. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `written` 需要副词。

5. **答案：finished**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与 `started` 并列叙述。

6. **答案：heartwarming**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`heart` 变为复合形容词 `heartwarming`（暖心的）。

7. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `reads` 需要副词。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...`。

9. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：不定式。`plan to do sth`。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：比较级。`a little better version`，`a little` 修饰比较级。
