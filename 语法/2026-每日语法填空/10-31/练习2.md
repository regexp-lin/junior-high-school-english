# 10-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：做家务 (Doing Housework)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many Chinese parents do all the housework (1) ______ (they) and don't ask their children to help. They believe children should focus entirely on their studies. However, experts say that doing housework is actually (2) ______ (benefit) for teenagers. It helps them develop life skills, builds (3) ______ (responsible) and teaches them to appreciate the hard work of their parents. In my family, everyone has housework duties. I am in charge (4) ______ washing the dishes after dinner and keeping my room tidy. My older sister does the laundry, and my parents handle the (5) ______ (cook) and grocery shopping. At first, I found washing dishes boring and tried to avoid (6) ______ (do) it as much as possible. But my mother said, "You live in this house too, so you should contribute." Her words made me (7) ______ (realise) that housework is a shared responsibility. Now I finish the dishes (8) ______ (quick) and efficiently. I even enjoy it sometimes — it gives me a break from studying and lets my mind relax. Last month, our class had a discussion about (9) ______ students should do housework. Most students agreed that it was important. One classmate said, "How can we take care of a country if we can't even take care of (10) ______ own room?" Everyone laughed, but it was a good point.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`do all the housework themselves`，主语为 `parents`。

2. **答案：beneficial**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is actually beneficial`，系动词后接形容词。

3. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`builds responsibility`，动词后接名词。

4. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`be in charge of` 表示"负责"。

5. **答案：cooking**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`handle the cooking`，`cook` 变为动名词/名词 `cooking`。

6. **答案：doing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`avoid doing sth`。

7. **答案：realise**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`make sb do sth`，使役动词后接原形。

8. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `finish` 需要副词。

9. **答案：whether**
   - **考点**：连词。`a discussion about whether students should do housework`。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：代词所有格。`our own room`，此处说话者代表"我们"。
