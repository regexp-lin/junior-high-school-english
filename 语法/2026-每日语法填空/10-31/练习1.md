# 10-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校服文化 (School Uniform Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Wearing school uniforms is a common practice in China. Most schools require students (1) ______ (wear) uniforms every day. The typical Chinese school uniform consists of a tracksuit in the school's colours, which is comfortable and practical for daily activities. However, many students have mixed feelings about their uniforms. Some students think uniforms are too plain and wish they could wear something more (2) ______ (fashion). Others appreciate them because uniforms save time and money on choosing (3) ______ to wear each morning. Our school recently conducted a survey (4) ______ uniforms. The results showed that 60% of students were satisfied with the current design, while 40% hoped for (5) ______ (improve). Based on the feedback, the school decided to redesign the uniforms. A group of students (6) ______ (invite) to participate in the design process. They worked with a (7) ______ (profession) designer to create new uniforms that were both smart and comfortable. The new uniforms have a more modern look with (8) ______ (good) quality fabric. When the new designs (9) ______ (reveal) at a school assembly, most students were pleased. Our head teacher said, "A good uniform should make students feel proud of (10) ______ (they) school." I agree — I feel much more confident in our new uniform.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to wear**
   - **考点**：不定式。`require sb to do sth`。

2. **答案：fashionable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`something more fashionable`，`fashion` 变为 `fashionable`。

3. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`choosing what to wear`，`what` + 不定式。

4. **答案：on / about**
   - **考点**：介词。`a survey on/about uniforms` 表示"关于校服的调查"。

5. **答案：improvements**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`hoped for improvements`，`improve` 变为名词，复数。

6. **答案：were invited**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`a group of students were invited`。

7. **答案：professional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `designer` 需要形容词。

8. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`better quality fabric`，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

9. **答案：were revealed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the new designs were revealed`，新设计"被揭晓"。

10. **答案：their**
    - **考点**：代词所有格。`their school`。
