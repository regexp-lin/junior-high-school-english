# 07-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学游泳 (Learning to Swim)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Swimming is an important life skill, but I did not learn it (1) ______ I was twelve. That summer, my parents signed me up for a swimming class at the local pool. The coach, a young woman (2) ______ (name) Li Jing, started by teaching us how to float on our backs. I was afraid of putting my face in the water, so I kept (3) ______ (hold) my breath too tightly. Coach Li told me to relax and trust the water to hold me up. It took three lessons (4) ______ I finally floated without sinking. The next step was learning to kick. Coach Li said our legs should move (5) ______ (smooth) like a fish's tail. After two weeks of practice, I could swim across the shallow end of the pool by (6) ______ (I). I felt (7) ______ (extreme) proud. Then we moved to the deep end, which was much more (8) ______ (frighten). But remembering how I had overcome my earlier fears, I jumped in (9) ______ hesitation. By the end of the course, I could swim 50 metres without stopping. Now, swimming is one of my favourite (10) ______ (activity). It keeps me fit and gives me confidence.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：until**
   - **考点**：连词。`not...until...` 表示"直到……才……"。

2. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a young woman named Li Jing` 表示"一个叫李静的年轻女子"。

3. **答案：holding**
   - **考点**：动名词。`keep doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"一直做某事"。

4. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：连词。`It took three lessons before I...` 表示"花了三节课才……"。

5. **答案：smoothly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `move` 需要副词，`smooth` 变为 `smoothly`。

6. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`by myself` 表示"独自地"。

7. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `proud` 需要副词，`extreme` 变为 `extremely`。

8. **答案：frightening**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰物/情境用 `frightening`（令人害怕的），`frighten` 变为 `frightening`。

9. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`without hesitation` 表示"毫不犹豫地"。

10. **答案：activities**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`one of my favourite + 复数名词`，`activity` 变为 `activities`。
