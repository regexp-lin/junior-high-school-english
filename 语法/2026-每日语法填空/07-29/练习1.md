# 07-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校图书角 (The Class Reading Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This term, our class set up a reading corner at the back of the classroom. The idea (1) ______ (come) from our class monitor, who believed that (2) ______ (have) easy access to books would encourage more students to read. Each student was asked to donate at least one book, and within a week, we (3) ______ (collect) over eighty books. Our teacher helped us build a small bookshelf and set some simple rules: borrow up to two books at a time, return them within two weeks, and take good care (4) ______ every book. The reading corner quickly became the (5) ______ (popular) spot in our classroom. During break time, you can always see several students (6) ______ (sit) quietly and reading. Some students (7) ______ never liked reading before have now become regular visitors. Last month, we held a book-sharing event (8) ______ everyone recommended their favourite book to the class. The (9) ______ (present) were creative and fun. I am glad that such a simple idea has made (10) ______ big difference in our class.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：came**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述这个学期发生的事情，`come` 的过去式为 `came`。

2. **答案：having**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。`having easy access to books` 在 `that` 宾语从句中作主语。

3. **答案：had collected**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`within a week` 暗示在某个过去时间点之前已完成收集。

4. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`take care of` 为固定搭配，表示"照顾，爱护"。

5. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词`，`popular` 为多音节形容词。

6. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：现在分词作宾补。`see sb doing sth` 表示"看见某人正在做某事"。

7. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `Some students`（人），在定语从句中作主语。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a book-sharing event`（活动/场合），在定语从句中作状语。

9. **答案：presentations**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。主语需要名词，`present` 变为 `presentation`（展示），多人展示用复数。

10. **答案：a / such a**
    - **考点**：冠词。`make a big difference` 为固定搭配，或 `such a big difference`；此处填 `a` 即可。
