# 10-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电影配音比赛 (Movie Dubbing Contest)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our English teacher organised a movie dubbing contest last Friday. Each group had to choose a scene from an English film and dub it with (1) ______ (they) own voices. My group chose a famous scene from "The Lion King" (2) ______ young Simba talks to his father Mufasa about the stars. We practised for a whole week, trying to match our voices (3) ______ the characters' emotions. I played the role of Mufasa and had to make my voice sound deep and (4) ______ (wisdom). It was (5) ______ (hard) than I expected because I also had to pay attention to the timing of each line. If I spoke too fast or too slow, it wouldn't match the characters' lip (6) ______ (move) on screen. On the day of the contest, we watched each group perform one (7) ______ one. Some groups chose comedy scenes that made everyone laugh, while others picked (8) ______ (drama) scenes that touched our hearts. When it was our turn, we performed (9) ______ (confident) and received warm applause from the audience. The judges praised our group for our (10) ______ (express) voices and accurate pronunciation. We won second place, and I was very proud of our team.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`their own voices`。

2. **答案：where / in which**
   - **考点**：关系副词/介词 + 关系代词。先行词为 `a scene`，从句中缺地点/场景状语。

3. **答案：with / to**
   - **考点**：介词。`match sth with/to` 表示"使……与……匹配"。

4. **答案：wise**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `deep` 并列作补语/表语，需要形容词，`wisdom` 变为 `wise`。

5. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`harder than I expected`。

6. **答案：movements**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`lip movements`，`move` 变为名词 `movement`，复数。

7. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`one by one` 表示"一个接一个"。

8. **答案：dramatic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `scenes` 需要形容词，`drama` 变为 `dramatic`。

9. **答案：confidently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `performed` 需要副词，`confident` 变为 `confidently`。

10. **答案：expressive**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `voices` 需要形容词，`expression` 变为 `expressive`（富有表现力的）。
