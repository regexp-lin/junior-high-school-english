# 10-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：红色旅游 (Red Tourism)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

"Red tourism" refers to visiting places that are connected (1) ______ important events in China's revolutionary history. Last summer, our school organised a trip to Yan'an, a city in Shaanxi Province that played a key role during the Chinese revolution. We visited the former site of the Communist Party headquarters, (2) ______ Mao Zedong and other leaders once lived and worked. The buildings were simple — small cave dwellings carved into the hillside with very basic (3) ______ (furnish). Our guide told us that during the (4) ______ (difficult) years of the revolution, the leaders worked tirelessly under these tough conditions (5) ______ (build) a new China. I was deeply (6) ______ (move) by their dedication and sacrifice. We also visited a museum that displayed old (7) ______ (photograph), letters and personal belongings of revolutionary heroes. One exhibit showed the handwritten notes of a young soldier who had written to his mother before going into battle. His words were so (8) ______ (touch) that several students started to cry. The trip helped me understand that the peaceful and (9) ______ (prosper) life we enjoy today was built on the hard work and sacrifice of (10) ______ (count) people before us. I feel grateful and determined to do my best for our country.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be connected to/with` 表示"与……有关"。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the former site`（地点），定语从句中缺地点状语。

3. **答案：furniture**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`basic furniture`，`furnish` 变为名词 `furniture`（不可数）。

4. **答案：most difficult**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the most difficult years`。

5. **答案：to build**
   - **考点**：不定式。`worked tirelessly to build...`，不定式表目的。

6. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was deeply moved`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

7. **答案：photographs**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`old photographs`，多张照片。

8. **答案：touching**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so touching that...`，修饰事物（文字）用 `-ing` 形式。

9. **答案：prosperous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `peaceful` 并列修饰 `life`，需要形容词，`prosper` 变为 `prosperous`。

10. **答案：countless**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`countless people`，`count` 加后缀 `-less` 表"无数的"。
