# 10-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：民宿体验 (Staying at a Homestay)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Homestays have become a popular choice for (1) ______ (travel) who want to experience local life. Unlike hotels, homestays allow guests to live with local families, eat homemade food and learn about local customs. Last holiday, my family stayed at a homestay in a small village in Yunnan Province. The house (2) ______ (own) by an elderly couple, Mr. and Mrs. Yang, who had been running it (3) ______ five years. They welcomed us (4) ______ (warm) and showed us to our room. It was simple but clean, with a window that overlooked a beautiful valley. Every morning, Mrs. Yang prepared a (5) ______ (tradition) breakfast of rice noodles and pickled vegetables. The food was so (6) ______ (taste) that I always asked for a second helping. During the day, Mr. Yang took us on walks through the village and (7) ______ (introduce) us to the neighbours. We learned how to make local handicrafts and even helped (8) ______ (harvest) tea leaves on a nearby hillside. The peaceful rhythm of village life was so different from the busy city that I felt completely (9) ______ (relax). On our last evening, the Yangs hosted a small dinner party for us. Mrs. Yang cooked her best dishes, and we sat under the stars, talking and (10) ______ (laugh). It was one of the most memorable trips I have ever had.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：travellers**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`travel` 变为表示人的名词 `traveller`，复数。

2. **答案：was owned**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`The house was owned by...`，"被拥有"。

3. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for five years` 表持续时间。

4. **答案：warmly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `welcomed` 需要副词，`warm` 变为 `warmly`。

5. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `breakfast` 需要形容词，`tradition` 变为 `traditional`。

6. **答案：tasty**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so tasty that...`，`taste` 变为形容词 `tasty`。

7. **答案：introduced**
   - **考点**：时态。与 `took` 并列，用一般过去时。

8. **答案：harvest**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`help (to) do sth`。

9. **答案：relaxed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt completely relaxed`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

10. **答案：laughing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。与 `talking` 并列，用现在分词。
