# 10-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：期中考试备考 (Preparing for Mid-Term Exams)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mid-term exams are approaching, and the atmosphere at school has become more (1) ______ (seriously) than usual. Most students are spending extra time (2) ______ (review) their notes and practising past papers. Our head teacher reminded us that good preparation is the key (3) ______ success. She suggested that we make a study plan and stick to it rather than trying to learn everything at the last minute. I followed her advice and created a (4) ______ (detail) timetable for the two weeks before the exams. I divided each day into blocks and assigned different (5) ______ (subject) to each block. I found that studying in short, focused sessions was much more (6) ______ (effect) than studying for hours without a break. My friend Xiao Ming, however, took a different approach. He stayed up late every night, (7) ______ (try) to memorise as much as possible. He looked tired and stressed, and I was worried (8) ______ him. I advised him to get enough sleep because research shows that a well-rested brain (9) ______ (perform) much better than a tired one. He thanked me and promised to change his habits. I believe that if we prepare (10) ______ (wise) and stay healthy, we will all do well in our exams.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：serious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`has become more serious`，系动词后接形容词，`seriously` 变为 `serious`。

2. **答案：reviewing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth`。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`the key to success` 表示"成功的关键"。

4. **答案：detailed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `timetable` 需要形容词，`detail` 变为 `detailed`。

5. **答案：subjects**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different subjects`，多个科目。

6. **答案：effective**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more effective than`，`effect` 变为 `effective`。

7. **答案：trying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`stayed up late, trying to memorise...`，现在分词作伴随状语。

8. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。`be worried about` 表示"担心"。

9. **答案：performs**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。`a well-rested brain performs...`，描述客观事实用一般现在时，单数主语。

10. **答案：wisely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `prepare` 需要副词，`wise` 变为 `wisely`。
