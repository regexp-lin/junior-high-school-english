# 08-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：旧照片的故事 (Stories Behind Old Photos)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, while cleaning the attic, I found a dusty photo album that (1) ______ (belong) to my grandparents. I sat down on the floor and started looking through it. The first photo showed a young couple standing in front of a small brick house. It took me a moment to realise they were my grandparents. They looked so (2) ______ (difference) from now — young, thin, and full of hope. Behind the house, I could see a field of wheat (3) ______ (stretch) to the horizon. My grandmother told me the house (4) ______ (build) by my grandfather with his own hands. They had (5) ______ (hard) any money at the time, so they used whatever materials they could find. Another photo showed my father as a baby, (6) ______ (hold) by my grandmother. He had the same round face he has today, (7) ______ made me laugh. The most (8) ______ (touch) photo was one of my grandparents at their fiftieth wedding anniversary. They sat side by side, hands (9) ______ (join), looking at each other with gentle smiles. Old photos are like time machines — they carry us (10) ______ to moments that would otherwise be forgotten.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：belonged**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述发现相册时的情况，用过去时。

2. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`looked` 为系动词，后接形容词，`difference` 变为 `different`。

3. **答案：stretching**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`a field of wheat stretching to the horizon` 表示"延伸到地平线的麦田"。

4. **答案：was built**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。房子"被建造"，叙述过去。

5. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`hardly any money` 表示"几乎没有钱"，`hard` 变为 `hardly`（几乎不）。

6. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语/伴随状语。`my father...held by my grandmother` 表示"被奶奶抱着"。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面的事情。

8. **答案：touching**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most touching` 中 `touch` 变为 `touching`（感人的）。

9. **答案：joined**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`hands joined` 为独立主格结构，表示"双手相握"，过去分词表被动。

10. **答案：back**
    - **考点**：副词。`carry us back to` 表示"把我们带回到"。
