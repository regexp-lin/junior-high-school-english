# 08-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界各地的问候方式 (Greetings Around the World)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When you travel to a new country, one of the first things you need to learn is how people greet each other. (1) ______ (differ) cultures have very different customs. In China, people usually shake hands or nod their heads as a way of (2) ______ (say) hello. In Japan, people bow to show respect, and the (3) ______ (deep) the bow, the more respect it shows. In France, close friends greet each other (4) ______ kissing on both cheeks. This might seem strange to someone who (5) ______ (grow) up in Asia, but it is completely normal there. In India, many people put their palms together and say "Namaste." This greeting (6) ______ (use) for centuries and carries a meaning of "I respect the spirit in you." In some African countries, friends may greet each other by (7) ______ (touch) foreheads. Understanding these customs is important because a wrong greeting can make people feel (8) ______ (comfortable). My English teacher once told us a funny story about how she (9) ______ (try) to bow in France and accidentally bumped her head against someone's! Learning about greetings helps us become more (10) ______ (culture) aware and respectful travellers.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：Different**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `cultures` 需要形容词，`differ` 变为 `Different`，句首大写。

2. **答案：saying**
   - **考点**：动名词。`of` 为介词，后接动名词 `saying`。

3. **答案：deeper**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`the + 比较级...the + 比较级` 结构，`deep` 的比较级为 `deeper`。

4. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by kissing` 表示"通过亲吻"，`by + doing` 表示方式。

5. **答案：has grown / grew**
   - **考点**：现在完成时/一般过去时。在亚洲长大的人可能觉得奇怪，`has grown up` 或 `grew up` 均可。

6. **答案：has been used**
   - **考点**：现在完成时 + 被动语态。`for centuries` 表示持续至今，这种问候方式"被使用"了数个世纪。

7. **答案：touching**
   - **考点**：动名词。`by` 为介词，后接动名词。

8. **答案：uncomfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 前缀。`feel` 后接形容词，根据语境"错误的问候让人不舒服"，加 `un-` 前缀，得 `uncomfortable`。

9. **答案：tried**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的趣事。

10. **答案：culturally**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `aware` 需要副词，`culture` 变为 `culturally`。
