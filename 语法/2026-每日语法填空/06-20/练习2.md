# 06-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：坚持的力量 (The Power of Persistence)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When I started learning the violin, my fingers hurt and the notes sounded terrible. Many times I wanted to give (1) ______, but my mother reminded me that skills grow slowly, like seeds under the soil. I agreed to practice for fifteen minutes every day, even (2) ______ I was tired after school. At first, progress was hard to notice, (3) ______ I refused to compare myself with others. Month by month, the music became smoother, and my teacher said I had improved (4) ______ (steady). Last month, I performed in front of parents, and their applause made all the boring repetitions feel (5) ______ (value). Now I believe persistence is not about never feeling discouraged—it is about returning to practice (6) ______ you fail again and again. A river cuts through rock not because of its strength, (7) ______ because it keeps flowing. In the same way, small efforts, if (8) ______ (keep) long enough, can lead to surprising results. I still have a long way to go, (9) ______ I am no longer afraid of difficult pieces. I will treat each mistake (10) ______ a step forward.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：up**  
   - **考点**：副词与固定搭配。`give up` 表示“放弃”，为高频短语动词。

2. **答案：when**  
   - **考点**：连词。`even when` 引导让步状语从句，表示“即使当我放学后很累的时候（也坚持练习）”。与 `even if`（即使/假设）相比，此处强调真实情境下的让步，用 `when` 更自然。

3. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。进步“难以察觉”与“拒绝和别人比较”之间为转折关系，用 `but`。

4. **答案：steadily**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved`，用副词；`steady` 变为 `steadily`。

5. **答案：valuable**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。系动词 `feel` 后接形容词；`value` 变为 `valuable`，表示“有意义的、值得的”。

6. **答案：after**  
   - **考点**：连词。表示“在一次次失败之后仍然回到练习”，用 `after` 引导时间状语从句，强调坚持的动作顺序。

7. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词与固定结构。`not...but...` 表示“不是……而是……”，说明河流穿石在于持续流动而非单纯力量。

8. **答案：kept**  
   - **考点**：省略与被动。完整形式为 `if they are kept long enough`，状语从句主谓与主句主语一致时可省略为 `if kept...`，过去分词表被动。

9. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。前句“仍有很长的路要走”与后句“不再害怕难曲”形成转折，用 `but`。

10. **答案：as**  
    - **考点**：介词。`treat...as...` 表示“把……当作……”。
