# 06-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：感谢信 (A Thank-you Letter)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Dear Ms. Li,

I am writing to express my (1) ______ (sincere) thanks for (2) ______ you have done for our class this term. When I was struggling with spoken English, you encouraged me to speak up in class and never laughed (3) ______ (loud) at my mistakes. I still remember the afternoon (4) ______ you stayed after school to help me practice dialogues. Your patience made me braver, and my pronunciation has become (5) ______ (clear) than before. You also recommended useful apps, (6) ______ helped me review new words on the bus. Now I can join the English corner (7) ______ feeling embarrassed. I know a short letter cannot describe all my gratitude, (8) ______ I hope these words can show how much you mean to me. I will keep working hard and try (9) ______ (make) you proud in the new term. Thank you again for being such a caring teacher. I am looking forward to (10) ______ (see) you soon.

Yours sincerely,  
Tom

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：sincere**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `thanks`，用形容词；括号内 `sincere` 本身为形容词，直接填 `sincere`，表示“诚挚的感谢”。若误用副词 `sincerely`，则搭配不当。

2. **答案：what**  
   - **考点**：名词性从句。介词 `for` 后为宾语从句，从句缺宾语（所做的事），用连接代词 `what`，相当于 `the things that`。

3. **答案：loudly**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `laughed`，用副词；`loud` 变为 `loudly`。注意 `laugh at` 结构保留在文中，`at` 不是本题空格。

4. **答案：when**  
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the afternoon`，定语从句结构完整，缺时间状语，用 `when`。

5. **答案：clearer**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，用 `clear` 的比较级 `clearer`，表示“比以前更清晰”。

6. **答案：which**  
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面“推荐实用软件”这件事，在从句中作主语，用 `which`。

7. **答案：without**  
   - **考点**：介词。表示“不再尴尬地参加英语角”，用 `without feeling embarrassed`，`without` 后接动名词。

8. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。前句说信短不足以表达感激，后句仍希望表达心意，形成转折，用 `but`。

9. **答案：to make**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`try to do sth.` 表示“努力去做某事”，用不定式 `to make`。

10. **答案：seeing**  
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`look forward to` 中的 `to` 为介词，后接动名词，故用 `seeing`。
