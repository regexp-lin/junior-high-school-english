# 04-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：记忆与大脑 (Memory and the Brain)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever wondered how your brain 1. ________ (remember) things? Memory is one of the most amazing abilities of the human brain. It allows us to learn, think and make 2. ________ (decide).

There are two main types of memory: short-term memory and long-term memory. Short-term memory only lasts for a few 3. ________ (second). For example, when someone tells you a phone number, you may forget it within a minute 4. ________ you don't write it down. Long-term memory, on the other hand, can last for years or even a lifetime.

So how can we improve our memory? Scientists have found several 5. ________ (use) methods. First, getting enough sleep is very important. During sleep, our brain organizes the information it has received and moves it 6. ________ short-term memory to long-term memory.

Second, exercise is helpful. Regular physical activity increases blood flow to the brain and keeps it 7. ________ (health). Research shows that students who exercise often have 8. ________ (good) memory than those who don't.

Third, try to connect new information 9. ________ something you already know. For example, if you want to remember the English word "island", you can think of the picture of a small piece of 10. ________ (land) surrounded by water. This method makes it easier to recall information later.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **remembers**
   - **考点**：主谓一致（一般现在时）。主语 your brain 是第三人称单数，描述一般事实，动词加 -s。

2. **decisions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。make decisions 意为"做决定"，decide → decision → decisions。

3. **seconds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。a few 修饰可数名词复数，second → seconds。

4. **if/unless**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。if you don't write it down 意为"如果你不写下来"；或 unless 意为"除非"。

5. **useful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 methods，use → useful。

6. **from**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。from...to... 意为"从……到……"。

7. **healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。keep it + 形容词，作宾语补足语，health → healthy。

8. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。than 提示用比较级，good → better。

9. **with/to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。connect A with/to B 意为"把A与B联系起来"。

10. **land**
    - **考点**：名词（无需变形）。a small piece of land 意为"一小块陆地"，land 为不可数名词。
