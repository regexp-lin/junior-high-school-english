# 04-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护地球 (Protecting Our Earth)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Earth is our only home, but it is facing many serious problems. If we don't take action now, the future of our planet will be 1. ________ danger.

One of the biggest problems is air pollution. Factories and cars release large amounts of harmful 2. ________ (gas) into the atmosphere every day. This not only damages the environment 3. ________ also causes health problems for millions of people. In some cities, the air quality is so poor that people have to wear masks when they go outside.

Deforestation is another serious issue. Forests are often 4. ________ (call) the "lungs of the Earth" because they absorb carbon dioxide and produce oxygen. However, large areas of forests 5. ________ (destroy) every year to make room for farms and buildings. Without trees, many animals lose their homes, and the soil becomes 6. ________ (weak).

Ocean pollution is getting worse too. Millions of tons of plastic waste end up in the ocean, 7. ________ (kill) sea creatures and destroying marine ecosystems. A recent study showed that some fish have even been found with tiny pieces of plastic inside 8. ________ (they) bodies.

The good news is that more and more people are becoming 9. ________ (awareness) of these problems. Governments are passing stricter laws, and ordinary people are changing their daily habits. Every small action counts — from recycling paper to planting 10. ________ tree.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **in**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be in danger 意为"处于危险之中"，是固定搭配。

2. **gases**
   - **考点**：名词复数。large amounts of + 名词，gas → gases（表示多种气体）。

3. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。not only...but also... 意为"不仅……而且……"。

4. **called**
   - **考点**：被动语态（过去分词）。are called 构成一般现在时被动语态，意为"被称为"。

5. **are destroyed / are being destroyed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。forests 与 destroy 之间是被动关系，every year 提示经常性动作，用一般现在时被动或现在进行时被动。

6. **weaker**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。become weaker 意为"变得更脆弱"，暗含与之前对比，weak → weaker。

7. **killing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。现在分词作结果状语，表示自然而然的结果。

8. **their**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 bodies，they → their。

9. **aware**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。become aware of 意为"意识到"，awareness → aware。

10. **a**
    - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。planting a tree 意为"种一棵树"，tree 为可数名词单数。
