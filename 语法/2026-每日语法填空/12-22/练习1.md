# 12-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：手工DIY (DIY Crafts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

DIY, which stands for "Do It Yourself," has become a popular hobby among teenagers in China. Instead of buying ready-made items, many young people enjoy (1) ______ (create) things with their own hands. From handmade jewellery and customised phone cases to knitted scarves and painted mugs, the possibilities are (2) ______ (end). Our school's art room is open every Wednesday afternoon for students (3) ______ are interested in DIY projects. A teacher supervises the room and provides basic materials such as paper, glue, fabric and paint. Students are free (4) ______ (make) whatever they want. Last month, I decided to make a birthday gift for my mother. I chose to create a photo album filled (5) ______ our family's favourite memories. I printed out photos, decorated each page with stickers and handwritten messages, and bound the pages together with a ribbon. It took me three afternoons (6) ______ (complete), but the result was something far more (7) ______ (meaning) than anything I could have bought in a shop. When I gave it to my mother, she was so (8) ______ (touch) that she cried. She said it was the (9) ______ (good) gift she had ever received. DIY crafts are not only fun but also teach us practical skills such as problem-solving and (10) ______ (creative). I encourage everyone to try making something by hand — you might be surprised at what you can achieve.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：creating**
   - **考点**：动名词。`enjoy doing sth`。

2. **答案：endless**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the possibilities are endless`，`end` 变为形容词。

3. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，先行词为 `students`。

4. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be free to do sth`。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`filled with` 表示"充满"。

6. **答案：to complete**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It took me three afternoons to complete`。

7. **答案：meaningful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more meaningful than`，`meaning` 变为形容词。

8. **答案：touched**
   - **考点**：词性转换。描述人的感受用 `-ed`。

9. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the best gift she had ever received`。

10. **答案：creativity**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `problem-solving` 并列，需要名词。
