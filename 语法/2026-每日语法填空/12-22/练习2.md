# 12-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：记忆技巧 (Memory Techniques)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Having a good memory is extremely helpful for students, especially when preparing for exams. But memory is not just something you are born with — it can be (1) ______ (improve) through practice and the right techniques. One of the most (2) ______ (effect) memory techniques is called "spaced repetition." Instead of studying all the material at once, you review it at increasing intervals — after one day, then three days, then a week, and so (3) ______. Research has shown that this method helps information stay in long-term memory much (4) ______ (long) than cramming. Another popular technique is the "memory palace," where you associate pieces of information (5) ______ specific locations in a place you know well, such as your home. When you need to remember the information, you simply "walk" through your mental home and find each item (6) ______ you left it. My English teacher taught us a technique for (7) ______ (remember) new vocabulary. She suggested that we create vivid mental images linking the English word to its meaning. For example, to remember the word "enormous," she asked us to imagine an (8) ______ (extreme) large elephant sitting in our classroom. The (9) ______ (strange) the image, the easier it is to remember. Since I started using these techniques, my exam scores (10) ______ (improve) noticeably. I only wish someone had taught me these methods earlier.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：improved**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`can be improved`，被动关系。

2. **答案：effective**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most effective`，`effect` 变为形容词。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：副词。`and so on` 表示"等等"。

4. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than cramming` 提示比较级。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`associate...with...` 表示"将……与……联系起来"。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。`find each item where you left it`。

7. **答案：remembering**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `for` 后接动名词。

8. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `large` 需要副词。

9. **答案：stranger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`The stranger the image, the easier it is`，"the + 比较级, the + 比较级"结构。

10. **答案：have improved**
    - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since I started` 提示现在完成时。
