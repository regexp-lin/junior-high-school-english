# 08-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：广场舞 (Square Dancing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Square dancing has become one of the most popular forms of exercise among middle-aged and elderly Chinese people. Every evening, in parks and public squares across the country, groups of women — and (1) ______ (increase) men — gather to dance together to lively music. My mother joined a square dancing group three years (2) ______ and has not missed a single session since. She says it is the (3) ______ (good) way to stay fit while having fun. The dances are not difficult (4) ______ (learn). New members can usually follow along (5) ______ a week or two of practice. The music ranges from traditional Chinese songs (6) ______ modern pop hits. Some groups even create their own routines. However, square dancing has also caused controversy. Some residents living near public squares complain (7) ______ the loud music, especially late at night. There have been arguments between dancers and (8) ______ (neighbour) in several cities. To solve this problem, some parks have installed time limits on sound equipment, and dancers are asked (9) ______ (keep) the volume at a reasonable level. I think square dancing is a (10) ______ (wonder) activity that brings people together, but dancers should also respect the peace and quiet of their neighbours.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `men` 需要副词，`increase` 变为 `increasingly`（越来越多地）。

2. **答案：ago**
   - **考点**：副词。`three years ago` 表示"三年前"。

3. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the best way`，`good` 的最高级为 `best`。

4. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be difficult to learn` 为固定结构。

5. **答案：after / within**
   - **考点**：介词。`after/within a week` 表示"经过/在一两周内"。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from...to...` 表示"从……到……"。

7. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。`complain about` 为固定搭配，表示"抱怨"。

8. **答案：neighbours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。与 `dancers` 并列，`neighbour` 用复数。

9. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be asked to do sth` 为被动语态中的固定搭配。

10. **答案：wonderful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `activity` 需要形容词，`wonder` 变为 `wonderful`。
