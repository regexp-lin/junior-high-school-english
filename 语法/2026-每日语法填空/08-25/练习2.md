# 08-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：毕业典礼 (Graduation Ceremony)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school held a graduation ceremony for all the ninth-grade (1) ______ (student). It was a special day that none of us (2) ______ (forget) easily. The ceremony began at nine o'clock in the morning. Our headmaster gave a speech, (3) ______ (tell) us to work hard and never give up on our dreams. Then several students (4) ______ (invite) to share their experiences. Li Ming, who was the top student in our grade, spoke about the (5) ______ (important) of setting goals and making plans. His speech was (6) ______ inspiring that many students were moved to tears. After the speeches, we watched a video that (7) ______ (record) our three years of junior high school life. I saw (8) ______ (I) playing basketball with friends, singing at the school concert, and studying late in the classroom. It reminded me (9) ______ all the happy moments we had shared together. At the end of the ceremony, we threw our hats into the air and cheered (10) ______ (loud). It was truly one of the best days of my life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：students**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`all the ninth-grade students`，`all` 修饰可数名词用复数。

2. **答案：will forget / would forget**
   - **考点**：时态。`none of us will forget easily` 表将来不会忘记；也可用 `would` 表虚拟语气。

3. **答案：telling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。句中已有谓语 `gave`，`tell` 作伴随状语，与主语 `headmaster` 是主动关系，用 `telling`。

4. **答案：were invited**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`students` 是"被邀请"，用一般过去时的被动语态 `were invited`。

5. **答案：importance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the importance of` 为固定结构，`important` 变为名词 `importance`。

6. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。`so...that...` 表示"如此……以至于……"。

7. **答案：recorded**
   - **考点**：时态。`that recorded our three years...` 为定语从句，叙述过去的事情，用一般过去时。

8. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 `I`，宾语也指自己，用 `myself`。

9. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`remind sb of sth` 为固定搭配，表示"使某人想起某事"。

10. **答案：loudly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `cheered` 需要副词，`loud` 变为 `loudly`。
