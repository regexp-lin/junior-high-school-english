# 04-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：规律作息 (A Regular Daily Routine)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Having a regular daily routine is one of the best things you can do for your health and study. A well-planned routine helps you manage your time 1. ________ (efficient) and reduces stress.

A good day starts with a good morning. Experts suggest 2. ________ (get) up at the same time every day, even on weekends. After waking up, do some light exercise, such as stretching or jogging. This wakes up your body and 3. ________ (prepare) your brain for the day ahead.

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day. Many students skip breakfast because they get up too 4. ________. But studies show that students who eat breakfast perform much 5. ________ (well) in class than those who don't. A healthy breakfast should include protein, whole grains and fruit.

During school hours, try to stay focused in class and take good 6. ________ (note). After school, set aside a specific time for homework. Don't wait 7. ________ the last minute — finish your homework early so that you have time to relax.

In the evening, limit your screen time. Looking at bright screens before bed makes 8. ________ harder to fall asleep. Instead, try reading a book 9. ________ taking a warm bath.

Finally, go to bed before 10:00 PM. A regular sleep schedule is 10. ________ (extreme) important for growing teenagers. Your body and brain need enough rest to work at their best.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **efficiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 manage，需用副词形式 efficiently。

2. **getting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。suggest doing sth. 意为"建议做某事"。

3. **prepares**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）+ 主谓一致。and 连接并列谓语，与 wakes up 并列，主语 this 为单数。

4. **late**
   - **考点**：副词（无提示词填空）。too late 意为"太晚"，根据语境，学生因为起得太晚而不吃早餐。

5. **better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（不规则变化）。than 提示用比较级，well → better。

6. **notes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。take notes 意为"做笔记"，note 用复数。

7. **until/till**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。until/till the last minute 意为"到最后一刻"。

8. **it**
   - **考点**：形式宾语（无提示词填空）。makes it harder to fall asleep，it 为形式宾语。

9. **or**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。reading a book or taking a warm bath，or 表示选择关系。

10. **extremely**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 important，需用副词形式 extremely。
