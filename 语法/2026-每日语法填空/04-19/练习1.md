# 04-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：历史文化遗产 (Historical Heritage)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has a long history of over 5,000 years. It is home to many world-famous historical sites that attract millions of 1. ________ (visitor) every year.

The Great Wall is probably the most well-known landmark in China. It 2. ________ (build) over 2,000 years ago to protect the country from enemies. Stretching for thousands of kilometers, it is one of the 3. ________ (great) engineering achievements in human history. Climbing the Great Wall is hard work, 4. ________ the view from the top is absolutely breathtaking.

The Forbidden City in Beijing is another must-see attraction. It served 5. ________ the home of Chinese emperors for nearly 500 years. Inside, you can see beautiful palaces, gardens and 6. ________ (count) works of art. Walking through its ancient halls, you can almost feel the weight of history.

The Terracotta Warriors in Xi'an are equally 7. ________ (impress). Thousands of life-sized clay soldiers were discovered underground in 1974. Each warrior has a 8. ________ (differ) face, which shows the incredible skill of ancient Chinese craftsmen.

Protecting these historical sites is very important. Some of them have been 9. ________ (damage) by pollution and too many tourists. We must find ways to preserve them 10. ________ that future generations can also enjoy and learn from these treasures.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **visitors**
   - **考点**：名词复数。millions of 后接可数名词复数，visitor → visitors。

2. **was built**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般过去时）。The Great Wall 与 build 之间是被动关系，over 2,000 years ago 提示过去时。

3. **greatest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，great → greatest。

4. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后句是转折关系："爬长城很辛苦，但风景令人叹为观止"。

5. **as**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。serve as 意为"充当、作为"。

6. **countless**
   - **考点**：词性转换/构词法（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 works of art，count → countless（无数的）。

7. **impressive**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。equally + 形容词，impress → impressive。

8. **different**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词/动词→形容词）。a different face 意为"不同的面孔"，differ → different。

9. **damaged**
   - **考点**：被动语态（现在完成时）。have been damaged 意为"已经被损坏"。

10. **so**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。so that 意为"以便"，引导目的状语从句。
