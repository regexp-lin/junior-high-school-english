# 10-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：班级值日 (Class Duty)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In Chinese schools, students take turns doing class duty, which means (1) ______ (clean) the classroom, wiping the blackboard and arranging the desks. Every day, a different group of students is responsible (2) ______ keeping the classroom tidy. At first, I didn't enjoy doing class duty. I thought it was boring and a waste of time. But our head teacher, Mr. Wei, told us (3) ______ class duty was more than just cleaning. "It teaches you (4) ______ (responsible) and respect for shared spaces," he said. His words changed my attitude. Now I take class duty (5) ______ (serious) and try to do my best every time. Last Friday, it was my group's turn. We swept the floor, (6) ______ (empty) the rubbish bins and watered the plants on the windowsill. My classmate Chen Xin, who is the (7) ______ (tidy) person in our class, also polished the teacher's desk until it shone. When Mr. Wei came in on Monday morning, he was very (8) ______ (please) and praised our group in front of the whole class. He said our classroom was the (9) ______ (clean) it had been all term. We all felt proud. I've come to realise that doing class duty is actually a good way to build a sense of community and (10) ______ (belong) within the class.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：cleaning**
   - **考点**：动名词。`means cleaning`，`mean` 表"意味着"时后接动名词。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be responsible for` 表示"负责"。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`told us that class duty was...`，`that` 引导宾语从句。

4. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`teaches you responsibility`，动词后接名词作宾语，`responsible` 变为 `responsibility`。

5. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`take sth seriously` 表示"认真对待"。

6. **答案：emptied**
   - **考点**：时态。与 `swept` 并列，用一般过去时，`empty` 变为 `emptied`。

7. **答案：tidiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the tidiest person`。

8. **答案：pleased**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was very pleased`，表示人的感受。

9. **答案：cleanest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the cleanest it had been`。

10. **答案：belonging**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `community` 并列，`a sense of belonging` 表示"归属感"，`belong` 变为 `belonging`。
