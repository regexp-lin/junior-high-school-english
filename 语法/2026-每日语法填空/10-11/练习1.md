# 10-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：二十四节气 (The 24 Solar Terms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The 24 Solar Terms is a system (1) ______ (create) by ancient Chinese farmers to guide agricultural activities. It divides the year into 24 periods based on the sun's position in the sky. Each term (2) ______ (reflect) changes in weather, temperature or natural phenomena. For example, "Rain Water" marks the time when temperatures begin to rise and rainfall (3) ______ (increase), while "Frost's Descent" signals the arrival of cold weather. In 2016, the 24 Solar Terms (4) ______ (add) to UNESCO's list of Intangible Cultural Heritage. This was a proud moment for Chinese people because it showed the world the (5) ______ (wise) of ancient Chinese civilisation. In our school, we celebrate the solar terms (6) ______ special activities. During "Start of Spring", students plant seeds in the school garden. On "Winter Solstice", the whole school makes dumplings together, (7) ______ is a fun tradition that everyone looks forward to. I find it fascinating that a system (8) ______ (develop) thousands of years ago is still relevant today. The 24 Solar Terms remind us to live in harmony (9) ______ nature and to respect the rhythm of the seasons. They are a (10) ______ (value) treasure of Chinese culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：created**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a system created by ancient Chinese farmers`，过去分词作后置定语。

2. **答案：reflects**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。`each term reflects...`，描述客观事实用一般现在时，`each` 为单数。

3. **答案：increases**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。`rainfall increases`，单数主语。

4. **答案：were added / was added**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the 24 Solar Terms were/was added to...`，被列入。

5. **答案：wisdom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the wisdom of`，冠词后接名词，`wise` 变为 `wisdom`。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`celebrate with special activities` 表示"用特别的活动来庆祝"。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

8. **答案：developed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a system developed thousands of years ago`，过去分词作后置定语。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`in harmony with` 表示"与……和谐相处"。

10. **答案：valuable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `treasure` 需要形容词，`value` 变为 `valuable`。
