# 非谓语动词——综合辨析（Non-finite Verbs — Comprehensive Practice）

> 日期：2027 年 03 月 18 日 | 语法分类：动词 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

**非谓语动词（non-finite verb）** 是不能独立充当句子谓语的动词形式，包括三种：

| 形式 | 名称 | 结构 |
|------|------|------|
| **to do** | 不定式（infinitive） | to + 动词原形 |
| **doing** | 动名词（gerund）/ 现在分词 | 动词-ing |
| **done** | 过去分词（past participle） | 动词-ed / 不规则变化 |

---

### 2. 动词不定式（to do）

#### 2.1 作主语

- **To learn** English is important. = **It** is important **to learn** English.

#### 2.2 作宾语

常见后接 to do 的动词：

| 动词 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| want, hope, wish, decide, plan, choose, agree, refuse, learn, manage, afford, fail, promise, pretend, offer, expect | I **want to go** home. |

#### 2.3 作宾语补足语

- The teacher told us **to be** quiet.
- I want you **to help** me.

#### 2.4 作目的状语

- He got up early **(in order) to catch** the first bus.

#### 2.5 作定语

- I have something **to tell** you.
- Give me a pen **to write with**.

---

### 3. 动名词（doing）

#### 3.1 作主语

- **Swimming** is good exercise.

#### 3.2 作宾语

常见后接 doing 的动词/短语：

| 动词/短语 | 示例 |
|-----------|------|
| enjoy, finish, mind, practice, avoid, keep, suggest, consider, imagine, miss, give up, feel like, be worth, can't help, be used to, look forward to | I **enjoy reading** books. |

**口诀：** "考虑建议盼原谅（consider, suggest, look forward to, excuse），承认推迟没得商（admit, delay/put off），避免错过继续练（avoid, miss, keep, practice），喜欢想象嫌冒险（enjoy, imagine, mind, risk），完成放弃很值当（finish, give up, be worth）。"

---

### 4. 既接 to do 又接 doing 的动词

| 动词 | + to do | + doing |
|------|---------|---------|
| **remember** | 记得要做（未做） | 记得做过（已做） |
| **forget** | 忘记要做（未做） | 忘记做过（已做） |
| **stop** | 停下来去做（另一件事） | 停止做（正在做的事） |
| **try** | 尽力做 | 尝试做 |
| **go on** | 接着做另一件事 | 继续做同一件事 |
| **mean** | 打算做 | 意味着 |

**经典对比：**
- I **remember to lock** the door. （我记得要去锁门——还没锁）
- I **remember locking** the door. （我记得锁过门了——已锁）
- He **stopped to smoke**. （他停下来去抽烟——去做另一件事）
- He **stopped smoking**. （他戒烟了——停止做的事）

---

### 5. 特殊结构

| 结构 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **too...to** | 太……以至于不能 | He is too young **to** go to school. |
| **enough to** | 足够……可以 | She is old enough **to** go to school. |
| **It takes sb. + 时间 + to do** | 做某事花某人多少时间 | It takes me 30 minutes **to** get there. |
| **It's + adj. + (of/for sb.) + to do** | 做某事是……的 | It's kind **of** you **to** help me. |
| **had better (not) do** | 最好（不）做 | You'd better **not go** out. |
| **would like to do** | 想要做 | I'd like **to have** a cup of tea. |
| **would rather do...than do** | 宁愿做……而不做 | I'd rather **walk** than **take** a bus. |

---

### 6. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | I enjoy to read books. | I enjoy **reading** books. | enjoy 后接 doing |
| 2 | He decided going there. | He decided **to go** there. | decide 后接 to do |
| 3 | I look forward to see you. | I look forward to **seeing** you. | to 是介词，后接 doing |
| 4 | She is used to get up early. | She is used to **getting** up early. | be used to + doing |
| 5 | You'd better to go home. | You'd better **go** home. | had better + do（原形） |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

**说明：第 1～10 题为中等难度；第 11～20 题为拔高题。请选出每题的最佳答案。**

**1.** I enjoy ______ music in my free time.

A. listen to　　B. listening to　　C. to listen to　　D. listened to

**2.** She decided ______ abroad next year.

A. go　　B. going　　C. to go　　D. goes

**3.** It's important for us ______ English well.

A. learn　　B. learning　　C. to learn　　D. learned

**4.** He is too tired ______ any more.

A. to walk　　B. walking　　C. walk　　D. walked

**5.** Would you mind ______ the window?

A. open　　B. to open　　C. opening　　D. opened

**6.** I have something important ______ you.

A. tell　　B. to tell　　C. telling　　D. told

**7.** The teacher asked us ______ quiet in class.

A. be　　B. to be　　C. being　　D. been

**8.** He finished ______ his homework before dinner.

A. do　　B. to do　　C. doing　　D. did

**9.** I remember ______ the door before I left.

A. to lock　　B. locking　　C. lock　　D. locked

**10.** You'd better ______ late for school.

A. not to be　　B. not be　　C. don't be　　D. not being

**11.** She stopped ______ and looked up at the sky.

A. to read　　B. reading　　C. read　　D. reads

**12.** I look forward to ______ from you soon.

A. hear　　B. hearing　　C. heard　　D. to hear

**13.** The boy is old enough ______ to school by himself.

A. go　　B. going　　C. to go　　D. gone

**14.** Don't forget ______ the light when you leave.

A. turn off　　B. to turn off　　C. turning off　　D. turned off

**15.** He ______ to work hard to pass the exam.

A. tried　　B. tries　　C. trying　　D. try

**16.** She is used to ______ up early.

A. get　　B. getting　　C. got　　D. gets

**17.** I'd rather ______ at home than ______ out today.

A. stay; go　　B. to stay; to go　　C. staying; going　　D. stay; to go

**18.** It took me two hours ______ my homework.

A. finish　　B. finishing　　C. to finish　　D. finished

**19.** He pretended ______ asleep when his mother came in.

A. be　　B. being　　C. to be　　D. been

**20.** The question is too difficult for me ______.

A. answer　　B. to answer　　C. answering　　D. answered

### 逐题解析

**第 1 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：enjoy 后接 doing——enjoy **listening to** music。
- **排除法**：A/C/D 形式不合。
- **考查知识点**：enjoy + doing。

**第 2 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：decide 后接 to do——decided **to go**。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：decide + to do。

**第 3 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：It's + adj. + for sb. + **to do**。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：It's + adj. + to do。

**第 4 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：too + adj. + **to do** 表"太……以至于不能"。
- **排除法**：B/C/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：too...to...

**第 5 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：mind 后接 doing——mind **opening** the window。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：mind + doing。

**第 6 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：不定式作后置定语——something important **to tell** you。
- **排除法**：A/C/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：to do 作定语。

**第 7 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：ask sb. **to do** sth.——asked us **to be** quiet。
- **排除法**：A/C/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：ask + sb. + to do。

**第 8 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：finish 后接 doing——finished **doing** homework。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：finish + doing。

**第 9 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：remember doing 表"记得做过"（before I left 说明已做过），用 **locking**。
- **排除法**：A to lock 表"记得要去锁"（未锁）不合语境；C/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：remember doing（已做过）vs. remember to do（未做）。

**第 10 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：had better + do（原形），否定 had better **not** do——**not be** late。
- **排除法**：A 多 to；C 不合；D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：had better (not) do。

**第 11 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：stop doing 表"停止正在做的事"——stopped **reading**（停止阅读，然后抬头看天）。
- **排除法**：A stop to read 表"停下来去阅读"不合语境；C/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：stop doing vs. stop to do。

**第 12 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：look forward to 中 to 是介词，后接 doing——**hearing**。
- **排除法**：A/C/D 不合（易错点 3）。
- **考查知识点**：look forward to + doing。

**第 13 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：adj. + enough + **to do**——old enough **to go**。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：enough to do。

**第 14 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：forget to do 表"忘记要做"（提醒别忘记——还没做），用 **to turn off**。
- **排除法**：A 缺 to；C turning off 表"忘记做过"不合语境；D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：forget to do（未做）vs. forget doing（已做）。

**第 15 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：try to do 表"尽力做"——He **tried** to work hard。根据句意和时态选 A。
- **排除法**：B/C/D 形式不合语境。
- **考查知识点**：try to do 尽力做。

**第 16 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：be used to + doing 表"习惯于做"——is used to **getting** up early。
- **排除法**：A/C/D 不合（易错点 4）。
- **考查知识点**：be used to + doing。

**第 17 题：答案 A**

- **解析**：would rather do...than do——rather **stay** than **go**（原形）。
- **排除法**：B/C/D 形式不对。
- **考查知识点**：would rather do...than do。

**第 18 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：It takes sb. + 时间 + **to do**——took me two hours **to finish**。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：It takes...to do。

**第 19 题：答案 C**

- **解析**：pretend 后接 to do——pretended **to be** asleep。
- **排除法**：A/B/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：pretend + to do。

**第 20 题：答案 B**

- **解析**：too + adj. + for sb. + **to do**——too difficult for me **to answer**。
- **排除法**：A/C/D 不合。
- **考查知识点**：too...for sb. to do。
