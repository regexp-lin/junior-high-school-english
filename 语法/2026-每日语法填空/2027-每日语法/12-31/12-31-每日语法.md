# there be 句型——各时态中的变化（There Be — Forms in Different Tenses）

> 日期：2027 年 12 月 31 日 | 语法分类：句型 | 难度：中等 + 拔高

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 基本概念

there be 句型表示"某处存在某物/某人"，是英语中最常用的句型之一。there 是引导词，真正的主语是 be 动词后面的名词。there be 句型可以与各种时态结合使用，中考中常考其在一般现在时、一般过去时、一般将来时和现在完成时中的形式。

### 2. 构成形式

| 时态 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般现在时 | There is/are + 名词 + 地点 | There is a book on the desk. |
| 一般过去时 | There was/were + 名词 + 地点 | There were many people in the park. |
| 一般将来时 | There will be / There is/are going to be + 名词 | There will be a meeting tomorrow. |
| 现在完成时 | There has/have been + 名词 | There have been many changes here. |

| 句型 | 肯定句 | 否定句 | 疑问句 |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| 一般现在时 | There is a cat. | There isn't a cat. | Is there a cat? |
| 一般过去时 | There were trees. | There weren't trees. | Were there trees? |
| 一般将来时 | There will be rain. | There won't be rain. | Will there be rain? |

### 3. 用法与语境

- **用法一**：there be 表"某处有某物"——表示存在
  - 例：**There is** a hospital near our school.（我们学校附近有一家医院。）
  - 例：**There are** 50 students in our class.（我们班有50个学生。）

- **用法二**：there be 的就近原则——be 动词与最近的名词保持一致
  - 例：There **is** a pen and two books on the desk.（pen 最近，用 is）
  - 例：There **are** two books and a pen on the desk.（two books 最近，用 are）

- **用法三**：there be 的一般过去时——表示过去某处有某物
  - 例：There **was** a big tree in front of my house.（以前我家门前有一棵大树。）
  - 例：There **were** many flowers in the garden last spring.（去年春天花园里有很多花。）

- **用法四**：there be 的一般将来时——表示将来某处会有某物
  - 例：There **will be** a football match this afternoon.（今天下午有一场足球赛。）
  - 例：There **is going to be** a party tonight.（今晚将有一场聚会。——be going to be）
  - 注意：不说 ~~There will have~~ 或 ~~There is going to have~~

- **用法五**：there be 与 have/has 的区别
  - there be：表示"某处存在某物"（强调客观存在）
  - have/has：表示"某人拥有某物"（强调拥有关系）
  - 例：**There is** a computer in the room.（房间里有一台电脑。——存在）
  - 例：I **have** a computer.（我有一台电脑。——拥有）
  - 两者不能混用：❌ There has a computer in the room.

### 4. 标志词 / 关键信号

| 标志词/短语 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------------|------|------|
| on the desk / in the room / near... | 地点状语（there be 常接地点） | There is a cat on the sofa. |
| last year / yesterday | 过去时间 → was/were | There was a park here before. |
| tomorrow / next week | 将来时间 → will be / is going to be | There will be a test next Monday. |
| How many...are there? | 问"有多少"的固定句型 | How many people are there? |

### 5. 易错点与辨析

| 序号 | ❌ 错误写法 | ✅ 正确写法 | 错因分析 |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | There will have a meeting. | There will be a meeting. | there be 将来时用 will be，不用 will have |
| 2 | There has a book on the desk. | There is a book on the desk. | there be 不能用 have/has 代替 |
| 3 | There are a pen and two books. | There is a pen and two books. | 就近原则，a pen 单数用 is |
| 4 | There is going to have rain. | There is going to be rain. | be going to 后用 be，不用 have |
| 5 | Have there many trees here? | Are there many trees here? | there be 的疑问句用 be 提前 |
