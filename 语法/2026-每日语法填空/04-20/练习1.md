# 04-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：公共演讲 (Public Speaking)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Public speaking is a skill that most people find 1. ________ (frighten). In fact, surveys show that many people fear speaking in public even more than they fear death! But public speaking is a skill that can 2. ________ (learn) and improved with practice.

I used to be terrified of standing in front of a crowd. My face would turn red and my mind would go 3. ________. But my English teacher signed me up for a speech competition without 4. ________ (tell) me first. I was angry at him at the time, but now I am grateful.

To prepare, I chose a topic that I was 5. ________ (passion) about — the future of artificial intelligence. I wrote the speech, practiced it in front of a mirror and asked my family 6. ________ (be) my audience. My younger sister kept 7. ________ (make) funny faces, which actually helped me learn to stay focused.

On the day of the competition, I was 8. ________ (terrible) nervous. But once I began speaking, I found my rhythm. I made eye contact with the audience and used hand gestures to emphasize my points. To my 9. ________ (surprising), the audience applauded loudly when I finished.

I didn't win first place, but I learned something far more 10. ________ (value): the courage to stand up and share my ideas with the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **frightening**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。find + 宾语 + 形容词（宾补），修饰事物用 -ing 形式。

2. **be learned/be learnt**
   - **考点**：被动语态。can be + 过去分词，skill 与 learn 之间是被动关系。

3. **blank**
   - **考点**：形容词（无提示词填空）。go blank 意为"脑子一片空白"，是固定搭配。

4. **telling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。without + 动名词。

5. **passionate**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。was passionate about 意为"对……充满热情"，passion → passionate。

6. **to be**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。ask sb. to do sth. 意为"请某人做某事"。

7. **making**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。keep doing sth. 意为"持续做某事"。

8. **terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 nervous，需用副词形式 terribly。

9. **surprise**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。to my surprise 意为"令我惊讶的是"，是固定搭配，surprising → surprise。

10. **valuable**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词/动词→形容词）。more + 形容词构成比较级，value → valuable。
