# 04-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：好书推荐 (Book Recommendations)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Books are like windows to different worlds. A good book can take you on 1. ________ adventure without leaving your room. Today, I'd like to share some of my favorite books.

The first book is "Charlotte's Web" by E.B. White. It tells the story of a spider named Charlotte and 2. ________ (she) friendship with a pig called Wilbur. This book taught me the true meaning of friendship and 3. ________ (sacrifice). It is one of the most 4. ________ (touch) stories I have ever read.

Another book I love is "Robinson Crusoe" by Daniel Defoe. It is about a man who gets 5. ________ (trap) on a deserted island and has to survive on his own. Reading this book made me realize how important it is to stay brave and never give 6. ________, even in the most difficult situations.

For those who like science fiction, "The Three-Body Problem" by Liu Cixin is a 7. ________ (wonder) choice. It takes readers on a journey through space and time. The 8. ________ (imagine) in this book is truly out of this world.

Reading is not just about finishing a book from cover to cover. It is about 9. ________ (think) deeply about what you read and connecting it to your own life. So pick up a book today — you never know 10. ________ adventures await you inside.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **an**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。adventure 以元音音素开头，用不定冠词 an。

2. **her**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 friendship，she → her。

3. **sacrifice**
   - **考点**：名词（无需变形）。and 连接并列名词，sacrifice 意为"牺牲"。注意此处根据语境若提示词为 sacrifice，则保持原形。

4. **touching**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。the most + 形容词最高级，修饰事物用 -ing 形式，touching 意为"感人的"。

5. **trapped**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→过去分词）。get trapped 意为"被困住"，trap → trapped。

6. **up**
   - **考点**：动词短语（无提示词填空）。give up 意为"放弃"。

7. **wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 choice，wonder → wonderful。

8. **imagination**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the + 名词，imagine → imagination。

9. **thinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 about 后接动名词形式。

10. **what**
    - **考点**：连接代词（无提示词填空）。what adventures 意为"什么样的冒险"，what 引导宾语从句，修饰名词 adventures。
