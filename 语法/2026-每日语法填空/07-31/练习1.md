# 07-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园美术展 (The School Art Exhibition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last week, our school held (1) ______ art exhibition in the main hall. Students from all grades were invited to display their works. The exhibition (2) ______ (include) paintings, drawings, sculptures, and photographs. I submitted a watercolour painting of a lotus pond, (3) ______ I had spent two weekends completing. Walking through the hall, I was surprised by the (4) ______ (various) of styles. Some works were realistic, while (5) ______ were abstract. A girl from Grade Nine created a sculpture out of recycled materials that looked both (6) ______ (modern) and thought-provoking. The judges said her work was among the (7) ______ (creative) pieces they had ever seen. My painting did not win a prize, but several teachers praised me (8) ______ my use of colour. One teacher said I had a good eye for detail and should keep (9) ______ (practise). The exhibition was open to parents and neighbours for three days. Many visitors said they were impressed by how (10) ______ (talent) the students were. I felt proud to be part of such a wonderful event.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`an art exhibition` 中 `art` 以元音音素 /ɑː/ 开头，用 `an`。

2. **答案：included**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述上周的展览，用过去时。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `a watercolour painting`，在从句中作宾语。

4. **答案：variety**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`various` 变为 `variety`（多样性）。

5. **答案：others**
   - **考点**：代词。`some...others...` 为固定搭配，表示"一些……另一些……"。

6. **答案：modern**
   - **考点**：形容词。`both modern and thought-provoking` 中 `modern` 保持原形即可。

7. **答案：most creative**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`among the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`creative` 为多音节形容词。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`praise sb for sth` 为固定搭配，表示"因……表扬某人"。

9. **答案：practising**
   - **考点**：动名词。`keep doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"坚持做某事"。

10. **答案：talented**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`how + 形容词 + 主语 + be` 为感叹句/从句结构，`talent` 变为 `talented`（有才华的）。
