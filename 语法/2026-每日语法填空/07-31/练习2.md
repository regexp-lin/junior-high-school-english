# 07-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：暑假打工体验 (A Summer Part-Time Job)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This summer, I worked as a helper at my aunt's bookshop for two weeks. It was my (1) ______ (one) real job, and I learned a lot from the experience. Every morning, I arrived at eight and started by (2) ______ (arrange) books on the shelves. It seemed simple, but sorting hundreds of books (3) ______ category and author actually required great attention. I also helped customers find the books they (4) ______ (look) for. At first, I was too shy to approach (5) ______ (strange), but my aunt encouraged me to greet everyone with a smile. I soon discovered that most customers were friendly and (6) ______ (thank) for the help. The (7) ______ (busy) time was always Saturday afternoon, when the shop was packed with families. I had to work faster and (8) ______ (efficient) during those hours. By the end of the two weeks, I was tired (9) ______ satisfied. My aunt paid me a small salary, and I used it to buy gifts for my parents. More importantly, the experience taught me the value (10) ______ hard work and gave me a taste of what adult life is like.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。`my first real job` 表示"我第一份真正的工作"。

2. **答案：arranging**
   - **考点**：动名词。`by` 为介词，后接动名词，表示方式。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by category and author` 表示"按类别和作者"。

4. **答案：were looking**
   - **考点**：过去进行时。`the books they were looking for` 表示顾客"正在寻找"的书。

5. **答案：strangers**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`approach strangers` 中 `strange` 变为名词 `stranger`（陌生人），复数形式。

6. **答案：thankful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `friendly` 并列作表语，`thank` 变为 `thankful`（感激的）。

7. **答案：busiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + time`，`busy` 变为 `busiest`。

8. **答案：more efficiently**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。与 `faster` 并列，`efficient` 变为副词 `efficiently`，再加 `more` 构成比较级。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。`tired but satisfied` 表示"疲惫但满足"。

10. **答案：of**
    - **考点**：介词。`the value of hard work` 表示"努力工作的价值"。
