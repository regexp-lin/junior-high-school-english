# 09-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：快递小哥 (Delivery Riders)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Delivery riders have become some of the (1) ______ (busy) people in modern China. Rain or shine, they rush through the streets to bring food and packages to (2) ______ (customer) as quickly as possible. Most of them work long hours, sometimes more than twelve hours (3) ______ day. My uncle is a food delivery rider. He (4) ______ (do) this job for three years now. Every morning, he puts on his yellow uniform, checks his electric bike and heads out. He receives orders through (5) ______ app on his phone and must deliver each one within 30 minutes. He told me that the (6) ______ (hard) part of his job is delivering food during heavy rain, when the roads become slippery and (7) ______ (danger). Last winter, he slipped on an icy road and hurt his knee, but he continued (8) ______ (work) because he didn't want to lose income. His story made me (9) ______ (real) appreciate the hard work of delivery riders. Next time you receive a package or a meal, remember to say "thank you" to the person (10) ______ brought it to your door.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：busiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`some of the busiest people`。

2. **答案：customers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`bring...to customers`，顾客不止一个。

3. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`twelve hours a day` 表示"每天十二小时"。

4. **答案：has been doing / has done**
   - **考点**：时态。`for three years now` 提示用现在完成时或现在完成进行时。

5. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`an app`，`app` 以元音音素开头，用 `an`。

6. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the hardest part`。

7. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `slippery` 并列作表语，需要形容词，`danger` 变为 `dangerous`。

8. **答案：working / to work**
   - **考点**：动名词或不定式。`continue doing/to do sth` 均可，表示"继续做某事"。

9. **答案：really**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `appreciate` 需要副词，`real` 变为 `really`。

10. **答案：who / that**
    - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `the person`（指人），引导定语从句。
