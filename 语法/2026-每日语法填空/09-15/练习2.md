# 09-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：参观科技馆 (Visiting a Science Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Sunday, I visited the city science museum with my classmates. It was the (1) ______ (large) science museum I had ever been to. The museum had four floors, each (2) ______ (focus) on a different area of science. On the first floor, we explored the world of physics through hands-on experiments. I was (3) ______ (particular) fascinated by a device that showed how electricity (4) ______ (generate). On the second floor, there was a life-sized model of a human body. We could see how different organs work together (5) ______ (keep) us alive. The third floor was about space exploration, (6) ______ was my favourite part. I sat inside a simulated spaceship and experienced what it feels like (7) ______ (float) in zero gravity. My friend Xiao Wei was so excited that he (8) ______ (want) to stay there all day. The top floor had a 4D cinema (9) ______ we watched a film about deep-sea creatures. The special effects were so realistic that some students screamed in (10) ______ (frighten). The visit was both educational and entertaining. I learned more in one day than I would in a month of reading textbooks.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the largest...I had ever been to`。

2. **答案：focusing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`each focusing on...`，独立主格结构中用现在分词。

3. **答案：particularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `fascinated` 需要副词，`particular` 变为 `particularly`。

4. **答案：is generated / was generated**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`electricity is/was generated`，电力"被产生"。

5. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：不定式。`work together to keep us alive`，不定式表目的。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

7. **答案：to float**
   - **考点**：不定式。`what it feels like to float`，`it` 为形式主语，不定式为真正主语。

8. **答案：wanted**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

9. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a 4D cinema`（地点），定语从句中缺地点状语。

10. **答案：fright**
    - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `in` 后接名词，`frighten` 变为名词 `fright`。
