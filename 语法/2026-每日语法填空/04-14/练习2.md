# 04-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：手工礼物的心意 (Handmade Gifts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever received a handmade gift? Unlike store-bought presents, handmade gifts are special because someone has put their time, effort and love 1. ________ making them.

Last year, I wanted to give my mother something special for Mother's Day. Instead of 2. ________ (go) to a shop, I decided to make a photo album by myself. I collected family photos from the past ten years and 3. ________ (careful) arranged them in a beautiful notebook. Under each photo, I wrote a short message about the memory.

It took me three 4. ________ (day) to finish the album. Some of my friends thought I was 5. ________ (waste) my time. They said it would be much easier 6. ________ (buy) a gift online. But I knew that my mother would like something made with love more than something bought from a store.

When I gave the album to my mother on Mother's Day, she opened it and started 7. ________ (read) the messages. Tears filled her eyes, and she said it was the 8. ________ (good) gift she had ever received. That moment made all my hard work worthwhile.

Handmade gifts don't have to be expensive 9. ________ perfect. What makes them valuable is the thought behind them. A hand-knitted scarf, a painted card or even a jar of homemade cookies — these simple gifts carry 10. ________ (deep) meaning than any expensive present.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **into**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。put...into 意为"把……投入到……中"。

2. **going**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。instead of 后接动名词形式。

3. **carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 arranged，需用副词形式 carefully。

4. **days**
   - **考点**：名词复数。three 修饰可数名词复数，day → days。

5. **wasting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词/现在进行时）。was wasting 构成过去进行时，意为"在浪费"。

6. **to buy**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。it would be much easier to buy，it 为形式主语。

7. **reading**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。start doing sth. 意为"开始做某事"。

8. **best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级（不规则变化）。the + 最高级 + she had ever received，good → best。

9. **or**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。don't have to be expensive or perfect 意为"不必昂贵或完美"，否定句中用 or。

10. **deeper**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，deep → deeper。
