# 04-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：情绪管理 (Managing Emotions)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Emotions are a natural part of life. We all feel happy, sad, angry or worried at times. But learning how 1. ________ (manage) our emotions is an important skill that can improve our relationships and overall well-being.

When something makes you angry, the worst thing to do is to act 2. ________ (immediate). Saying hurtful words or doing something reckless in anger often leads to regret. Instead, try taking a few deep 3. ________ (breath) and counting to ten. This gives your brain time 4. ________ (calm) down and think clearly.

Talking to someone you trust is also 5. ________ great way to deal with negative emotions. When I feel upset, I usually talk to my best friend or write in 6. ________ (I) journal. Just putting my feelings into words makes me feel much 7. ________ (light).

It is also important to understand 8. ________ other people feel. This is called empathy. If a friend seems sad, don't ignore them. A simple question like "Are you OK?" can mean a lot. Being a good 9. ________ (listen) is sometimes more helpful than giving advice.

Remember, it is perfectly normal to have negative emotions. What matters is how we handle them. People who manage their emotions 10. ________ (good) usually have healthier relationships and happier lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **to manage**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。how to do sth. 意为"如何做某事"。

2. **immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 act，需用副词形式 immediately。

3. **breaths**
   - **考点**：名词复数。a few 修饰可数名词复数，breath → breaths。

4. **to calm**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。give sb. time to do sth. 意为"给某人时间做某事"。

5. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a great way 意为"一个很好的方法"，way 为可数名词单数。

6. **my**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 journal，I → my。

7. **lighter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，light → lighter，意为"轻松得多"。

8. **how**
   - **考点**：连接副词（无提示词填空）。understand how other people feel 意为"理解他人的感受"。

9. **listener**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a good listener 意为"一个好的倾听者"，listen → listener。

10. **well**
    - **考点**：副词。修饰动词 manage，good 的副词形式为 well。
