# 06-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：我最喜欢的音乐 (My Favourite Music)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Music plays an important role in my daily life. I usually listen to it while (1) ______ (do) homework or riding the bus. Among all types of music, I like pop songs (2) ______ (well). They have simple words and beautiful melodies that are easy (3) ______ (remember). My favourite singer is Jay Chou, who (4) ______ (create) many wonderful songs since the early 2000s. His music mixes Chinese traditional instruments (5) ______ modern beats, which makes it special. Last year, I started learning to play the guitar (6) ______ myself. At first, my fingers hurt (7) ______ (bad), and I almost gave up. But my mother encouraged me (8) ______ (keep) going. After months of practice, I can now play several songs. Playing music helps me relax when I feel (9) ______ (stress). I believe music is (10) ______ universal language — it connects people across the world without a single word.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：doing**
   - **考点**：现在分词/省略结构。`while doing homework` 是 `while I am doing homework` 的省略形式，主语一致时可省略主语和 be 动词。

2. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：副词最高级。在所有类型中"最喜欢"，表示三者以上的比较，`well` 的最高级为 `best`。

3. **答案：to remember**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be easy to do` 为固定结构，不定式作状语补充说明 `easy`。

4. **答案：has created**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`since the early 2000s` 表示从过去持续到现在，用现在完成时；主语 `who` 指代 `Jay Chou`（第三人称单数），用 `has created`。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`mix A with B` 为固定搭配，表示"把 A 和 B 混合"。

6. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by myself` 表示"独自地，靠自己"，为固定搭配。

7. **答案：badly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `hurt` 需要副词，`bad` 变为 `badly`，表示"严重地"。

8. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"鼓励某人做某事"。

9. **答案：stressed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel` 为系动词，后接形容词；`stress` 变为 `stressed`，表示人"感到有压力的"。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。`a universal language` 表示"一种通用语言"，泛指；注意 `universal` 以辅音音素 /juː/ 开头，用 `a` 而非 `an`。
