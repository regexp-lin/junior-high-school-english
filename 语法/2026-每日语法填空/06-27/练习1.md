# 06-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：一次志愿者活动 (A Volunteer Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our class went to a nursing home to do volunteer work. We arrived at eight in the morning and (1) ______ (divide) into three groups. The first group helped clean the rooms, the second prepared fruit plates, and my group was in charge of (2) ______ (chat) with the elderly. I sat beside an old man (3) ______ name was Mr. Chen. He told me many interesting stories about his (4) ______ (young). He used to be a teacher and had taught maths (5) ______ thirty years before he retired. Listening to him made me realise how (6) ______ (value) experience is. Before we left, we sang several songs together, and the old people clapped their hands (7) ______ (happy). On the bus home, our teacher asked us (8) ______ we had learned from the visit. Many students said they understood the (9) ______ (important) of caring for the elderly. I agreed. It was (10) ______ meaningful day that I will never forget.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：were divided**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。主语 `we` 是"被分成"小组的，且为叙述过去事件，用 `were divided`。

2. **答案：chatting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`in charge of` 中 `of` 为介词，后接动名词 `chatting`。

3. **答案：whose**
   - **考点**：关系代词（所有格）。先行词为 `an old man`，定语从句中 `name` 需要所有格修饰，用 `whose`。

4. **答案：youth**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`his` 后接名词，`young`（形容词）变为名词 `youth`（青年时代）。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for thirty years` 表示"长达三十年"，`for` 接一段时间，与过去完成时搭配。

6. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`how + 形容词 + 名词 + be` 为感叹句结构；`value` 变为形容词 `valuable`（有价值的）。

7. **答案：happily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `clapped`，需要副词；`happy` 变为 `happily`。

8. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。引导宾语从句，从句中 `learned` 缺宾语，表示"学到了什么"，用 `what`。

9. **答案：importance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`important` 变为 `importance`（重要性）。

10. **答案：such a**
    - **考点**：固定结构。`such a + 形容词 + 单数名词 + that...` 表示"如此……以至于……"。注意与 `so...that` 的区别：`so` 后直接接形容词/副词，`such` 后接名词短语。
