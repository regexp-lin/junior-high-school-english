# 08-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：骑行旅行 (A Cycling Trip)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, my father and I went on a three-day cycling trip along the coast. It was the (1) ______ (long) ride I had ever attempted. We set off early on a Saturday morning, each carrying a backpack (2) ______ (fill) with clothes, snacks, and a water bottle. The first day was the easiest — we rode about 40 kilometres on flat roads (3) ______ the sea breeze in our faces. Along the way, we passed fishing villages (4) ______ colourful boats rested on the shore. I took many photos because the scenery was (5) ______ (extreme) beautiful. The second day was much (6) ______ (tough) because we had to climb a steep hill. My legs ached so badly that I wanted (7) ______ (quit). But my father kept encouraging me, saying, "The view from the top will be (8) ______ (good) reward." He was right. When we finally reached the peak, the ocean below looked like a sheet of blue glass. That night, we camped on the beach and (9) ______ (fall) asleep listening to the waves. The trip taught me that the best (10) ______ (memory) are made when we push ourselves beyond our comfort zone.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：longest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词 + I had ever attempted`，`long` 的最高级为 `longest`。

2. **答案：filled**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a backpack filled with` 表示"装满了……的背包"。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`with the sea breeze in our faces` 为 with 复合结构，表伴随。

4. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `fishing villages`（地点），定语从句中缺状语。

5. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `beautiful` 需要副词，`extreme` 变为 `extremely`。

6. **答案：tougher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 隐含（比第一天），用 `tougher`。

7. **答案：to quit**
   - **考点**：不定式。`want to do sth` 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：the best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the best reward` 表示"最好的回报"，`good` 的最高级为 `best`。

9. **答案：fell**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与 `camped` 并列，`fall` 的过去式为 `fell`。

10. **答案：memories**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`the best memories` 表示"最好的回忆"，用复数。
