# 08-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：垃圾食品与健康饮食 (Junk Food vs. Healthy Eating)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Junk food is everywhere — in supermarkets, near schools, and on our phone screens through (1) ______ (advertise). Burgers, chips, and sugary drinks taste great, but they contain too much fat, salt, and sugar. Eating them (2) ______ (regular) can lead to serious health problems such as obesity and heart disease. A study published last year showed that Chinese teenagers today eat three times (3) ______ much junk food as their parents did at the same age. The result is that childhood obesity (4) ______ (become) a growing problem in recent years. So how can we eat more (5) ______ (health)? First, replace soft drinks (6) ______ water or fresh juice. Second, choose fruits and nuts as snacks instead (7) ______ biscuits and sweets. Third, try to cook at home more often, because home-made meals are usually (8) ______ (nutritious) than fast food. My family started meal planning last month. We decide what (9) ______ (eat) for the whole week every Sunday. It saves time and helps us avoid (10) ______ (buy) unnecessary food. Making small changes in diet can have a big effect on our health.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：advertisements / ads**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`through` 后接名词，`advertise` 变为 `advertisement`（广告），用复数。

2. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动名词短语 `eating them` 需要副词，`regular` 变为 `regularly`。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`three times as much...as...` 为倍数表达，表示"是……的三倍"。

4. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`in recent years` 提示用现在完成时，`childhood obesity` 为单数。

5. **答案：healthily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `eat` 需要副词，`health` 变为 `healthily`。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`replace A with B` 为固定搭配，表示"用 B 代替 A"。

7. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`instead of` 为固定搭配，表示"代替，而不是"。

8. **答案：more nutritious**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than fast food` 提示用比较级，`nutritious` 为多音节形容词。

9. **答案：to eat**
   - **考点**：不定式。`what to eat` 为"疑问词 + 不定式"结构，作 `decide` 的宾语。

10. **答案：buying**
    - **考点**：动名词。`avoid doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"避免做某事"。
