# 08-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：古诗词之美 (The Beauty of Ancient Chinese Poetry)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ancient Chinese poetry is a treasure that (1) ______ (pass) down for thousands of years. Poems by Li Bai, Du Fu, and Wang Wei are still (2) ______ (wide) read today. What makes these poems so special? First, they use very few words to express deep (3) ______ (feel). For example, Li Bai's "Quiet Night Thoughts" has only twenty characters, (4) ______ it paints a vivid picture of homesickness that touches millions of readers. Second, ancient poems often describe nature in a way that connects it (5) ______ human emotions. When Du Fu writes about autumn rain, he is really writing about sadness and (6) ______ (lose). My Chinese teacher says that (7) ______ (memorise) poems is not enough — we must also understand the stories behind them. Last month, she asked us (8) ______ (choose) our favourite poem and explain why it moved us. I picked Wang Wei's "Farewell" because its gentle tone reminded me (9) ______ saying goodbye to my best friend who moved to another city. Reading ancient poetry has helped me express my own feelings more (10) ______ (clear). It is a bridge between the past and the present.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has been passed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时 + 被动语态。`for thousands of years` 提示用现在完成时，诗歌"被传承下来"。

2. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `read` 需要副词，`wide` 变为 `widely`（广泛地）。

3. **答案：feelings**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`deep` 修饰名词，`feel` 变为 `feeling`（感情），用复数表示多种情感。

4. **答案：but / yet**
   - **考点**：转折连词。"只有二十个字"与"描绘出生动画面"为转折关系。

5. **答案：with / to**
   - **考点**：介词。`connect A with/to B` 表示"把 A 与 B 联系起来"。

6. **答案：loss**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `sadness` 并列，需要名词，`lose` 变为 `loss`（失去，损失）。

7. **答案：memorising**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。`memorising poems` 在 `that` 从句中作主语。

8. **答案：to choose**
   - **考点**：不定式。`ask sb to do sth` 为固定搭配。

9. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`remind sb of sth` 为固定搭配，表示"使某人想起某事"。

10. **答案：clearly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `express` 需要副词，`clear` 变为 `clearly`。
