# 08-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：雷雨天气 (A Thunderstorm)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Yesterday afternoon, a sudden thunderstorm hit our city. The sky, which had been clear just an hour (1) ______ (early), turned dark grey in minutes. The wind blew so (2) ______ (strong) that several umbrellas on the street were turned inside out. Then, large drops of rain began (3) ______ (fall) from the sky. Within seconds, the streets were (4) ______ (complete) flooded. I was on my way home from school when the storm started. I ran into a shop (5) ______ took shelter there. Through the glass door, I watched lightning flash across the sky, (6) ______ (follow) by loud claps of thunder. The shop owner, a kind old lady, offered me a cup of hot tea. She told me that when she was young, thunderstorms were much more (7) ______ (common) in summer than they are now. After about thirty (8) ______ (minute), the rain began to ease. I thanked the lady and stepped outside. The air smelled fresh and clean, as if the city (9) ______ (wash). Puddles on the road reflected the evening light like tiny (10) ______ (mirror). Sometimes, a storm can make the world look brand new.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：earlier**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`an hour earlier` 表示"一小时前"，`early` 的比较级为 `earlier`。

2. **答案：strongly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so...that...` 结构中修饰动词 `blew` 需要副词，`strong` 变为 `strongly`。

3. **答案：to fall / falling**
   - **考点**：不定式/动名词。`begin to do / begin doing` 均可，表示"开始做某事"。

4. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词/过去分词 `flooded` 需要副词，`complete` 变为 `completely`。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。"跑进商店"和"在那里避雨"为顺承关系，用 `and`。

6. **答案：followed**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`followed by` 表示"伴随着，紧随其后"，过去分词构成独立主格结构。

7. **答案：more common**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`common` 为多音节形容词。

8. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`thirty` 修饰可数名词复数。

9. **答案：had been washed**
   - **考点**：过去完成时 + 被动语态。`as if` 引导虚拟语气，城市"仿佛被洗过了"。

10. **答案：mirrors**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`tiny mirrors` 表示"小镜子"，多个水坑如同多面镜子。
