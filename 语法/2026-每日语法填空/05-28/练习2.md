# 05-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：名人故事——海伦·凯勒 (The Story of Helen Keller)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Helen Keller lost her sight and hearing as a young child, 1. ________ her world seemed silent and dark. With the help of Anne Sullivan, she learned to spell words on her hand and finally understood 2. ________ language could open her mind.

Helen studied 3. ________ (hard) than many people around her. She entered college and later wrote books 4. ________ (tell) her story. Her courage moved readers across the world and showed 5. ________ disabilities do not define a person's value.

In class, we read a passage about her life and discussed 6. ________ patience changes lives. Our teacher said Helen's example teaches us to be grateful 7. ________ healthy senses and to keep trying when tasks feel impossible.

I used to complain about long English texts, 8. ________ Helen's journey made me feel ashamed of my laziness. Now I try to face each lesson 9. ________ (brave).

Her story reminds us that love and education can turn struggles 10. ________ strength.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **so**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。失去视听与“世界仿佛寂静黑暗”为因果关系，用 so。

2. **that / how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词（无提示词填空）。understood 后接从句，说明语言能够打开心智，用 that；若强调“如何打开”，也可用 how。

3. **harder**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。than 提示比较级，hard 的比较级为 harder。

4. **graduated**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时，有提示词）。叙述海伦的生平，大学毕业为过去事件，用 graduated。

5. **to tell**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（有提示词）。写书的目的是讲述经历，用不定式 to tell 作目的状语。

6. **that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词（无提示词填空）。showed 后接从句，从句结构完整，用 that。

7. **how**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词（无提示词填空）。discussed 后接从句，讨论“耐心如何改变人生”，用 how。

8. **for**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。be grateful for 为固定搭配。

9. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。过去抱怨与海伦经历带来的反思形成转折。

10. **bravely**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 face，用 bravely。
