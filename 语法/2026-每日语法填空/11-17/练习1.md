# 11-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：人工智能 (Artificial Intelligence)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Artificial intelligence (AI) is changing the world at an (1) ______ (believe) speed. From smart assistants on our phones to self-driving cars, AI technology has already become a part of our daily lives. In education, AI is being used (2) ______ (help) students learn more effectively. Some apps can analyse a student's weak points and create (3) ______ (person) study plans just for them. Last month, our school introduced an AI-powered English (4) ______ (pronounce) tool. Students can read sentences aloud, and the tool gives them instant feedback on their pronunciation. It even tells them which sounds they need (5) ______ (improve). My classmate Chen Wei, (6) ______ used to be shy about speaking English, has made great progress with this tool. However, many people are also worried about the risks of AI. Some fear that robots will replace human (7) ______ (work) in many industries, leading to widespread unemployment. Others are concerned about privacy, because AI systems collect huge (8) ______ (amount) of personal data. My computer science teacher believes that AI is (9) ______ a tool — it is neither good nor bad. What matters is how humans choose to use it. He encourages us to learn about AI so that we can use it (10) ______ (wise) in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：unbelievable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `speed` 需要形容词，`believe` 变为 `unbelievable`（难以置信的）。

2. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be used to do sth` 表示"被用来做某事"（注意区分 `be used to doing`）。

3. **答案：personalised / personal**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `study plans` 需要形容词形式。

4. **答案：pronunciation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `tool` 的复合名词结构，`pronounce` 变为名词 `pronunciation`。

5. **答案：to improve**
   - **考点**：不定式。`need to do sth` 表示"需要做某事"。

6. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，先行词为人，用 `who`。

7. **答案：workers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`human workers` 表示"人类工人"，复数形式。

8. **答案：amounts**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`huge amounts of` 表示"大量的"。

9. **答案：just / only**
   - **考点**：副词。`AI is just/only a tool`，表示"只不过是一个工具"。

10. **答案：wisely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `use` 需要副词形式。
