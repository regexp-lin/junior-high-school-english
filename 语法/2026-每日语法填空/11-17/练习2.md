# 11-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统手工艺 (Traditional Handicrafts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has a rich history of traditional handicrafts, many of (1) ______ have been passed down through generations. These include paper cutting, embroidery, pottery and bamboo weaving. (2) ______ (fortunate), some of these ancient skills are disappearing because fewer young people are willing to learn them. My uncle is a master of bamboo weaving. He has been making bamboo baskets and furniture (3) ______ he was a teenager. Watching him work is truly (4) ______ (fascinate). His hands move quickly and skilfully, turning plain bamboo strips (5) ______ beautiful objects. He once told me that it took him ten years (6) ______ (master) the craft. "There are no shortcuts," he said. "You must practise the same movements thousands of (7) ______ (time) until they become second nature." In recent years, the government (8) ______ (make) great efforts to protect traditional handicrafts. Some craftspeople have been invited to give (9) ______ (demonstrate) at schools and cultural centres. Our school organised a handicraft exhibition last month, where students could try their hands at paper cutting and clay modelling. It was a great way to help young people appreciate the beauty and (10) ______ (valuable) of these time-honoured traditions.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。`many of which` 引导非限制性定语从句，介词后接 `which`。

2. **答案：Unfortunately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。位于句首作状语，修饰整个句子，`fortunate` 变为 `unfortunately`，再加否定前缀。

3. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`has been making...since he was a teenager`，`since` 引导时间状语从句。

4. **答案：fascinating**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is truly fascinating`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

5. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`turn...into...` 表示"把……变成……"。

6. **答案：to master**
   - **考点**：不定式。`it took him ten years to master`，`it takes sb time to do sth`。

7. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`thousands of times` 表示"数千次"。

8. **答案：has made**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`In recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

9. **答案：demonstrations**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`give demonstrations`，动词 `demonstrate` 变为名词复数。

10. **答案：value**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `beauty` 并列，需要名词形式，`valuable` 变为 `value`。
