# 06-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：端午节 (Dragon Boat Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Dragon Boat Festival falls on (1) ______ fifth day of the fifth lunar month. It is a time (2) ______ families get together and remember Qu Yuan, (3) ______ (know) for his patriotism. In my hometown, people wrap zongzi with bamboo leaves and sticky rice, and the kitchen always smells (4) ______ (wonder). Early in the morning, we watch dragon boats race along the river; the drummers beat the drums so (5) ______ (loud) that the whole town seems wide awake. My grandmother says the festival is not only about food, (6) ______ also about courage and teamwork. Last year, I tried rowing (7) ______ a practice boat for the first time, and my arms (8) ______ (ache), but I enjoyed every minute. Now I understand (9) ______ this tradition has lasted for centuries—it connects us with history and with one another. I hope the colourful flags and cheering crowds will continue to make young people feel proud of (10) ______ (they) culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**  
   - **考点**：冠词。序数词 `fifth` 前用定冠词 `the`，表示农历五月初五这一固定日期。

2. **答案：when**  
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a time`，定语从句结构完整，缺时间状语，用 `when` 引导（= during which）。

3. **答案：known**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词/过去分词。`be known for...` 表示“因……而闻名”；此处为过去分词短语作后置定语，修饰 `Qu Yuan`，相当于 `who is known for...`。

4. **答案：wonderful**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。系动词 `smells` 后接形容词作表语；`wonder` 变为 `wonderful`，表示“闻起来很香/美好”。

5. **答案：loudly**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `beat`，用副词；`loud` 变为 `loudly`。

6. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词与固定结构。`not only...but also...` 表示“不仅……而且……”，为并列递进关系。

7. **答案：on**  
   - **考点**：介词。表示“在船上”用 `on a boat`，为地点介词搭配。

8. **答案：ached**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述去年经历，用一般过去时；括号内 `ache` 的过去式为 `ached`。后接 `but` 表转折，符合语境。

9. **答案：why**  
   - **考点**：名词性从句。`understand` 后为宾语从句，从句说明原因“为何延续数百年”，用连接副词 `why`。

10. **答案：their**  
    - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 `culture`，用形容词性物主代词 `their`，表示“他们的文化”。`be proud of` 已完整出现在文中，注意区分 `they/them/their`。
