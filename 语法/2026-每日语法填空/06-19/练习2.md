# 06-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：如何应对考场紧张 (How to Handle Exam Stress)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

It is normal (1) ______ feel a little nervous before an important exam, but too much worry may influence your performance. When I notice my heart beating fast, I stop (2) ______ take three slow breaths. Then I tell myself that I have practiced many times, and those efforts will not disappear overnight. If a difficult question appears, I read it twice (3) ______ (care) instead of guessing right away. My teacher also advises us to prepare pencils and tickets (4) ______ night so that we won't rush in the morning. On the way to school, I sometimes listen to light music, (5) ______ helps me stay calm. During the exam, I never compare my speed with anyone (6) ______; I focus on my own paper. After I hand in the answer sheet, I avoid checking answers online immediately because that habit only increases (7) ______ (anxious). Looking back, I believe confidence comes from preparation, (8) ______ from empty encouragement. If you treat exams (9) ______ a chance to show what you know, nervousness may turn (10) ______ energy that keeps you alert.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`It is + adj. + to do sth.` 中，`it` 为形式主语，真正主语是不定式，故用 `to feel`。

2. **答案：and**  
   - **考点**：连词。`stop` 与 `take three slow breaths` 为顺承的两个动作，用 `and` 连接（先停下来，再深呼吸）。

3. **答案：carefully**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `read`，用副词；`care` 变为 `carefully`（先变形容词 `careful` 再加 `ly`）。

4. **答案：the**  
   - **考点**：冠词。`the night before` 表示“（考试）前一晚”，特指那一晚，用定冠词 `the`。

5. **答案：which**  
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句话“听轻音乐这件事”，在从句中作主语，用 `which`。

6. **答案：else**  
   - **考点**：代词/副词。`anyone else` 表示“其他任何人”，`else` 修饰不定代词后置。

7. **答案：anxiety**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。动词 `increases` 后接名词作宾语；`anxious` 的名词形式为 `anxiety`（焦虑）。

8. **答案：not**  
   - **考点**：副词与并列结构。与前面的 `from preparation` 形成对照：`confidence comes from preparation, not from...`，用 `not` 表示否定来源。

9. **答案：as**  
   - **考点**：介词。`treat...as...` 为固定搭配，表示“把……当作……”。

10. **答案：into**  
    - **考点**：介词与固定搭配。`turn into` 表示“转变为”，说明紧张可能转化为让你保持专注的能量。
