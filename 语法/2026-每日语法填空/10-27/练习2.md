# 10-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：象棋对弈 (Playing Chinese Chess)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese chess, also called Xiangqi, is a (1) ______ (tradition) board game that has been enjoyed for over a thousand years. The game (2) ______ (play) between two players on a board divided by a "river" in the middle. Each player controls 16 pieces, including generals, advisors, elephants, horses, chariots, cannons and soldiers. The goal is to checkmate the opponent's general. My grandfather (3) ______ (teach) me how to play Xiangqi when I was seven. At first, I could only remember the basic rules and often forgot how each piece (4) ______ (move). My grandfather was very patient. He let me take back wrong moves and (5) ______ (explain) different strategies to me. Over time, I became much (6) ______ (good) at the game. Now we play together every weekend, and the matches between us have become increasingly (7) ______ (compete). Last Sunday, I managed (8) ______ (beat) him for the first time in a fair game. He was surprised but also proud of (9) ______. He shook my hand and said, "The student has surpassed the teacher." Playing Xiangqi has taught me the importance of thinking ahead and (10) ______ (make) careful decisions — skills that are useful both on and off the board.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `board game` 需要形容词，`tradition` 变为 `traditional`。

2. **答案：is played**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the game is played between...`，描述游戏规则用一般现在时被动。

3. **答案：taught**
   - **考点**：时态。`when I was seven` 提示过去时，`teach` 的过去式为 `taught`。

4. **答案：moved / moves**
   - **考点**：时态。`forgot how each piece moved/moves`，宾语从句可用过去时或一般现在时。

5. **答案：explained**
   - **考点**：时态。与 `let` 并列，用一般过去时。

6. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much better at the game`，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

7. **答案：competitive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词（暗含）需要形容词，`compete` 变为 `competitive`。

8. **答案：to beat**
   - **考点**：不定式。`manage to do sth` 表示"成功做到"。

9. **答案：me**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`proud of me`，指代说话对象（孙子）。

10. **答案：making**
    - **考点**：动名词。与 `thinking` 并列，介词 `of` 后接动名词。
