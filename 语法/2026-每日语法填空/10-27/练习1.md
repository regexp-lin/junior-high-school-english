# 10-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：研学旅行 (Study Tour)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Study tours, which combine travel with learning, have become an important part of education in China. Last month, our school organised a two-day study tour to a science and technology park in Shenzhen. We were divided into groups, and each group was assigned a different project (1) ______ (complete). My group's project was to learn about solar energy and build a simple solar-powered device. On the first day, engineers at the park gave us a (2) ______ (detail) introduction to how solar panels work. They explained that solar panels convert sunlight (3) ______ electricity using special materials called semiconductors. I found the topic extremely (4) ______ (fascinate). In the afternoon, we started building our device — a small solar-powered fan. It (5) ______ (require) us to connect solar cells, wires and a motor. The work was much (6) ______ (complicated) than we expected. We made several (7) ______ (mistake) and had to start over twice. But with the help of our guide, we (8) ______ (final) got our fan to spin. Everyone cheered when it started working! On the second day, we presented our projects to the other groups. Our teacher said the tour was more (9) ______ (value) than a week of classroom lessons because we learned by (10) ______ (do). I completely agree.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to complete**
   - **考点**：不定式。`a project to complete`，不定式作后置定语。

2. **答案：detailed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `introduction` 需要形容词，`detail` 变为 `detailed`。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`convert sunlight into electricity`。

4. **答案：fascinating**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found the topic fascinating`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

5. **答案：required**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

6. **答案：more complicated**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much more complicated than`。

7. **答案：mistakes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several mistakes`。

8. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `got` 需要副词。

9. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more valuable than`，`value` 变为 `valuable`。

10. **答案：doing**
    - **考点**：动名词。`by doing`，介词 `by` 后接动名词。
