# 04-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：科技改变生活 (Technology Changes Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Technology has changed our lives in ways that people in the past could never have 1. ________ (imagine). From smartphones to artificial intelligence, new technology is everywhere.

One of the most useful 2. ________ (invent) in recent years is mobile payment. In China, people can pay for almost everything with their phones. Whether you are buying food at a market 3. ________ taking a taxi, you just need to scan a code. It is so convenient that many people have stopped 4. ________ (carry) cash.

Smart home technology is also becoming popular. You can control the lights, air conditioner and even the door lock 5. ________ (simple) by using your voice. Some families have robots that help 6. ________ (they) clean the house. Life has become much 7. ________ (comfortable) than before.

However, we should also be aware 8. ________ the problems that technology brings. Spending too much time on electronic devices is harmful to our eyes and health. Some people become so dependent on technology 9. ________ they can hardly do anything without it.

Technology is a double-edged sword. The key is to use it wisely. We should be the 10. ________ (master) of technology, not its slaves.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **imagined**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去分词）。could never have imagined 构成"情态动词 + have + 过去分词"，表示"过去不可能想象到的"。

2. **inventions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，invent → invention → inventions。

3. **or**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。Whether...or... 意为"无论是……还是……"。

4. **carrying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。stop doing sth. 意为"停止做某事"（stop carrying cash 停止携带现金）。

5. **simply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰介词短语 by using your voice，需用副词形式 simply。

6. **them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。help 后接宾语，代替 some families，用宾格 them。

7. **more comfortable**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，comfortable 为多音节形容词，比较级为 more comfortable。

8. **of**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be aware of 意为"意识到"，是固定搭配。

9. **that**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。so...that... 意为"如此……以至于……"，引导结果状语从句。

10. **masters**
    - **考点**：名词复数。主语是 we，表语需用复数形式 masters。
