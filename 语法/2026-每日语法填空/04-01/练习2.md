# 04-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学习方法 (Study Methods)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every student wants to get good grades, but not everyone knows how to study 1. ________ (efficient). Using the right study methods can make a huge difference.

First, plan your study time. Many students wait until the last minute and then try to learn everything at 2. ________. This is called "cramming", and it rarely works. Instead, you should spread your study over several 3. ________ (day). Research shows that studying a little each day is more 4. ________ (effect) than studying a lot in one night.

Second, take notes 5. ________ you study. Writing things down helps your brain remember 6. ________ (they) better. After class, review your notes and add anything you might have missed. This is a simple 7. ________ very useful habit.

Third, don't just read your textbook again and again. Try to explain what you have 8. ________ (learn) to someone else. If you can teach it clearly, it means you truly understand it.

Finally, get enough sleep. Your brain needs rest to process the information it 9. ________ (receive) during the day. Students who sleep well usually perform 10. ________ (good) in exams than those who stay up late.

Remember, smart study is more important than hard study.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **efficiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 study，需用副词形式 efficiently。

2. **once**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。at once 意为"同时；立刻"，此处指"试图一次学完所有东西"。

3. **days**
   - **考点**：名词复数。several 修饰可数名词复数，day → days。

4. **effective**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。more + 形容词构成比较级，effect → effective（有效的）。

5. **while/when**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。while/when you study 意为"在你学习的时候"，引导时间状语从句。

6. **them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。remember 后接宾语，代替前面的 things，用宾格 them。

7. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。simple but very useful 表示转折，"简单但非常有用"。

8. **learned/learnt**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。have + 过去分词，learn → learned 或 learnt。

9. **receives/has received**
   - **考点**：动词时态。描述大脑在白天接收到的信息，可用一般现在时 receives 或现在完成时 has received。

10. **better**
    - **考点**：副词比较级（不规则变化）。than 提示用比较级，well 的比较级为 better，修饰动词 perform。
