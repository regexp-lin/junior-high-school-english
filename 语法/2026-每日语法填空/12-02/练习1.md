# 12-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：食物浪费 (Food Waste)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Food waste is a serious problem both in China and around the world. It is estimated that roughly one-third of all food (1) ______ (produce) globally is wasted each year. This means millions of tonnes of perfectly good food end up in rubbish bins while hundreds of millions of people go (2) ______ (hunger). In China, the government launched the "Clean Plate" campaign to raise (3) ______ (aware) about food waste. Restaurants are encouraged to offer (4) ______ (small) portions and remind customers not to over-order. Many schools have also joined the effort. Our school canteen now displays posters with messages (5) ______ as "Take only what you can eat" and "Every grain of rice matters." Before the campaign, I never thought much about the food I (6) ______ (leave) on my plate. I would often take more than I could eat and throw the rest away. But after watching a documentary about farmers (7) ______ work day and night to grow our food, I felt deeply ashamed of my (8) ______ (waste) habits. Now I make sure to take only (9) ______ I can finish. If I have leftover food, I take it home instead of throwing it away. These small changes may not solve the global food crisis, but they are a step (10) ______ the right direction.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：produced**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`all food produced globally`，被动关系。

2. **答案：hungry**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`go hungry` 为系表结构，表示"挨饿"。

3. **答案：awareness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`raise awareness` 表示"提高意识"，动词后接名词。

4. **答案：smaller**
   - **考点**：比较级。`smaller portions`，暗含与以前相比较小的份量。

5. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`such as` 表示"例如"。

6. **答案：left**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的习惯。

7. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，先行词为 `farmers`（人）。

8. **答案：wasteful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `habits` 需要形容词，`waste` 变为 `wasteful`。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`only what I can finish`，`what` 引导宾语从句并在从句中作宾语。

10. **答案：in**
    - **考点**：介词。`a step in the right direction` 表示"朝正确方向迈出的一步"。
