# 12-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：京剧艺术 (Peking Opera)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Peking Opera is one of the most treasured art forms in China. With a history of over 200 years, it combines singing, dialogue, acrobatics and mime to tell stories from Chinese history and legend. The performers wear (1) ______ (colour) costumes and elaborate face paint, each colour (2) ______ (represent) a different character trait. For example, red stands for loyalty, while white stands (3) ______ treachery. My grandfather is a huge fan of Peking Opera. He has been watching it (4) ______ he was a young man. Every Sunday morning, he turns on the radio and listens to Peking Opera programmes for hours. He can even sing several famous arias (5) ______ (he). He often says that Peking Opera is much (6) ______ (rich) and more complex than modern pop music. Last month, he took me to see a live Peking Opera performance at the local theatre. I was (7) ______ (initial) reluctant because I thought it would be boring. But to my surprise, the performance was absolutely (8) ______ (fascinate). The actors' movements were graceful and powerful, and the story was full of drama and emotion. I was especially impressed by a fight scene in which two warriors performed (9) ______ (amaze) acrobatic jumps and flips on stage. After the show, my grandfather explained the symbolism behind the face paint and movements. I finally understood (10) ______ Peking Opera is considered a national treasure.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `costumes` 需要形容词。

2. **答案：representing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。独立主格结构，`each colour representing...`。

3. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`stand for` 表示"代表"。

4. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`has been watching...since he was a young man`。

5. **答案：himself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`sing...himself` 表示"亲自唱"。

6. **答案：richer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than modern pop music` 提示比较级。

7. **答案：initially**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `reluctant` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：fascinating**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

9. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `acrobatic jumps` 需要形容词。

10. **答案：why**
    - **考点**：连接副词。`understood why Peking Opera is considered...`，引导宾语从句。
