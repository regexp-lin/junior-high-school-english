# 12-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：公共演讲 (Public Speaking)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Public speaking is a skill that many people find (1) ______ (terrify). In fact, surveys show that public speaking is one of the most common (2) ______ (fear) — some people are even more afraid of it than of heights or spiders. I used to be one of those people. Whenever I had to speak in front of the class, my hands would shake, my voice would tremble and my mind would go (3) ______ (complete) blank. Everything changed when I joined the school debating club in Grade 8. Our club advisor, Ms Chen, was an (4) ______ (experience) public speaker who had won many competitions. She taught us several useful techniques for overcoming stage fright. The most helpful one was thorough (5) ______ (prepare) — she said that confidence comes from knowing your material inside (6) ______. She also taught us the importance of body language: making eye contact, standing up straight and using natural hand gestures. I practised (7) ______ (speak) in front of a mirror every day for weeks. Gradually, I became more comfortable. My first real test came when I (8) ______ (choose) to represent our class in a speech contest. I was nervous, but I remembered everything Ms Chen (9) ______ (teach) me. I took a deep breath, looked at the audience and began speaking clearly and confidently. I did not win the contest, but I received many compliments. That experience showed me that public speaking is not a talent you are born (10) ______ — it is a skill you can learn.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：terrifying**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`find it terrifying`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

2. **答案：fears**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most common fears`。

3. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `blank` 需要副词。

4. **答案：experienced**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `public speaker` 需要形容词。

5. **答案：preparation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`thorough preparation`，形容词后接名词。

6. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词。`inside out` 为固定搭配，表示"彻底地"。

7. **答案：speaking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`practise doing sth` 表示"练习做某事"。

8. **答案：was chosen**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`I` 与 `choose` 之间是被动关系。

9. **答案：had taught**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。教授发生在演讲之前，用过去完成时。

10. **答案：with**
    - **考点**：介词。`a talent you are born with` 表示"与生俱来的才能"。
