# 12-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：机器人助手 (Robot Assistants)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Robots are no longer just characters in science fiction movies — they are becoming a real part of our daily lives. In many Chinese restaurants, robot waiters have been introduced to deliver food (1) ______ customers' tables. These robots can carry several plates at once and navigate through crowded dining areas without (2) ______ (bump) into anything. In hospitals, robots are used to deliver medicine and (3) ______ (guide) patients to different departments. Last month, our school held a technology exhibition where several types of robots were displayed. The most popular one was a small educational robot that could answer (4) ______ (question), tell jokes and even dance. Students gathered around it, (5) ______ (ask) it all kinds of funny questions. It answered most of them (6) ______ (correct), though it got confused when someone asked it a trick question. Another impressive robot was a cleaning robot designed (7) ______ large buildings. It could sweep, mop and vacuum floors all by (8) ______ (it), following a pre-programmed route. The exhibition made me think about what the future might look like. Some experts predict that robots will become as common (9) ______ smartphones within the next twenty years. While this sounds exciting, it also raises important questions about whether robots will take away human jobs. I believe that robots should assist humans, not (10) ______ (replacement) them. The ideal future is one where humans and robots work side by side.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`deliver food to customers' tables` 表示"把食物送到顾客桌上"。

2. **答案：bumping**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后接动名词。

3. **答案：guide**
   - **考点**：动词原形。与 `deliver` 并列，`are used to deliver...and guide...`。

4. **答案：questions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`answer questions` 表示"回答问题"。

5. **答案：asking**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随状态。

6. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `answered` 需要副词。

7. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`designed for large buildings` 表示"为大型建筑设计的"。

8. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`all by itself` 表示"完全自动地"。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`as common as smartphones`，同级比较。

10. **答案：replace**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`should assist...not replace`，与 `assist` 并列需要动词原形。
