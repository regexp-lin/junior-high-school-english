# 11-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：摄影爱好 (Photography)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Photography is more than just taking pictures — it is a way of seeing the world through a different lens. My interest in photography began when my father gave me his old camera (1) ______ my twelfth birthday. At first, I just took random photos of (2) ______ (any) I saw: flowers, buildings, food and even my cat sleeping on the sofa. Most of my early photos were blurry and poorly composed, but I didn't care. I was simply enjoying the process of (3) ______ (capture) moments. Gradually, I started learning about photography techniques. I watched online tutorials and read articles about composition, lighting and colour theory. I discovered that a great photo is not just about the subject — it is about how the (4) ______ (photograph) chooses to frame it. The angle, the distance and the timing can transform an ordinary scene (5) ______ something extraordinary. Last spring, I entered my school's photography contest for the first time. The theme was "Beauty in (6) ______ (day) Life." I submitted a photo of an old man (7) ______ (read) a newspaper in a park, with golden sunlight streaming through the trees behind him. To my (8) ______ (amaze), the photo won second prize! The judge said it was one of the most (9) ______ (emotion) moving entries she had seen. This experience gave me the confidence to keep (10) ______ (improve). Now I carry my camera wherever I go, always ready to capture the next special moment.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on my twelfth birthday` 表示在具体某一天用 `on`。

2. **答案：anything**
   - **考点**：代词。`anything I saw` 表示"我看到的任何东西"。

3. **答案：capturing**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词形式。

4. **答案：photographer**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the photographer` 指"摄影师"，`photograph` 变为人物名词。

5. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`transform...into...` 表示"把……变成……"。

6. **答案：Daily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `Life` 需要形容词，`day` 变为 `daily`。

7. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`an old man reading a newspaper`，现在分词作定语表正在进行。

8. **答案：amazement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`To my amazement` 为固定搭配，介词后接名词。

9. **答案：emotionally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `moving` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：improving**
    - **考点**：动名词。`keep doing sth` 表示"继续做某事"。
