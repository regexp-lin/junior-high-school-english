# 11-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：关爱老人 (Caring for the Elderly)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's ageing population has been growing rapidly in recent years. More and more elderly people live alone because their children work in (1) ______ (distance) cities. This situation has raised concerns about the physical and (2) ______ (emotion) well-being of senior citizens. My grandmother lives by (3) ______ (she) in a small town. After my grandfather passed away three years ago, she often felt lonely. My parents tried to persuade her (4) ______ (move) in with us, but she refused because she did not want to be a burden. Instead, she joined a community centre for the elderly, (5) ______ she has made many new friends. They exercise together in the morning, play cards in the afternoon and sometimes go on short trips. She seems much (6) ______ (happy) now than before. Our school has also been doing its part to help the elderly. Every month, a group of volunteers (7) ______ (visit) a local nursing home. We help with cleaning, sing songs for the residents and listen to their stories. Many of them are eager to talk because they seldom have people to communicate (8) ______. One grandpa told me that our visits were the (9) ______ (bright) part of his month. His words touched my heart deeply. I believe that respecting and caring for the elderly is not just a tradition — it is a (10) ______ (reflect) of how civilised a society truly is.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：distant**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `cities` 需要形容词。

2. **答案：emotional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `physical` 并列修饰 `well-being`，需要形容词。

3. **答案：herself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`by herself` 表示"独自一人"。

4. **答案：to move**
   - **考点**：不定式。`persuade sb to do sth` 表示"说服某人做某事"。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导非限制性定语从句，先行词为 `centre`（地点）。

6. **答案：happier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than before` 提示比较级。

7. **答案：visits**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`a group of volunteers` 强调整体时谓语用单数，`Every month` 提示一般现在时。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`have people to communicate with`，`communicate with sb`。

9. **答案：brightest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the brightest part of his month`。

10. **答案：reflection**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`a reflection of` 表示"……的反映"，冠词后接名词。
