# 12-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：登山运动 (Mountain Climbing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mountain climbing is a sport that tests both physical strength and mental (1) ______ (tough). It requires climbers to push their limits, face their fears and never give (2) ______, even when the path gets steep and dangerous. China is blessed with many magnificent mountains, from the towering Himalayas in the west to the scenic Huangshan in the east. Climbing has become (3) ______ (increase) popular among young professionals as a way to escape the stress of city life. My PE teacher, Mr Sun, is an experienced mountain climber who (4) ______ (summit) over twenty peaks across China. He describes climbing as "the most (5) ______ (honest) conversation you can have with yourself." He says that on a mountain, there are no shortcuts — every step (6) ______ (count). Last October, Mr Sun led a group of students on a hike up Mount Wutong, the (7) ______ (tall) peak in Shenzhen. It was my first serious climb, and I was both excited and nervous. The trail was steeper than I (8) ______ (expect), and after two hours my legs felt like they were on fire. I wanted to stop several times, but my classmates kept cheering me (9) ______. When I finally reached the summit, the view was (10) ______ (breath) — the entire city stretched out below us like a miniature model. That moment made every painful step worthwhile.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：toughness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `strength` 并列，需要名词。

2. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词。`give up` 表示"放弃"。

3. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `popular` 需要副词。

4. **答案：has summited**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。表示从过去到现在的经历。

5. **答案：honest**
   - **考点**：形容词。`the most honest conversation`。

6. **答案：counts**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`every step` 为第三人称单数。

7. **答案：tallest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the tallest peak in Shenzhen`。

8. **答案：had expected**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`steeper than I had expected`，期望发生在攀登之前。

9. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：副词。`cheer sb on` 表示"为某人加油"。

10. **答案：breathtaking**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`the view was breathtaking`，系动词后接形容词。
