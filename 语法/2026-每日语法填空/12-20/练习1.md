# 12-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬至 (The Winter Solstice)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Winter Solstice, known as "Dongzhi" in Chinese, is an important traditional festival that (1) ______ (fall) around December 21st or 22nd each year. It marks the (2) ______ (short) day and the longest night of the year. In ancient China, the Winter Solstice was considered even more important (3) ______ the Spring Festival. Emperors held grand ceremonies to pray for a good harvest in the coming year. Today, the Winter Solstice is mainly (4) ______ (celebrate) as a family gathering occasion, with special foods playing a central role. In northern China, families get together (5) ______ (make) and eat dumplings on this day. There is an old saying that eating dumplings on the Winter Solstice will keep your ears from (6) ______ (freeze) in winter. In southern China, people prefer eating tangyuan — sweet glutinous rice balls served in warm soup. The round shape of tangyuan (7) ______ (represent) family reunion and harmony. Last year, our class held a special Winter Solstice celebration. Our teacher divided us (8) ______ groups, and each group made dumplings with different fillings. Some groups were very (9) ______ (create), making dumplings shaped like animals and flowers. The classroom was filled with laughter and the delicious smell of (10) ______ (fresh) cooked dumplings. It was a wonderful way to experience this ancient tradition together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：falls**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。主语 `the Winter Solstice` 为第三人称单数，描述每年的事实。

2. **答案：shortest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the shortest day`。

3. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`more important than` 提示比较级。

4. **答案：celebrated**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`is mainly celebrated`，被动关系。

5. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：不定式。`get together to make` 表示目的。

6. **答案：freezing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`keep...from doing sth` 表示"阻止……做某事"。

7. **答案：represents**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`The round shape` 为第三人称单数。

8. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`divide...into groups` 表示"分成小组"。

9. **答案：creative**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`were very creative`，系动词后接形容词。

10. **答案：freshly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `cooked` 需要副词。
