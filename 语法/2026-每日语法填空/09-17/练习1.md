# 09-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：老照片的故事 (Stories Behind Old Photos)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Sunday, while cleaning the attic, I found a dusty old photo album that (1) ______ (belong) to my grandparents. I sat down on the floor and began looking through it. The (2) ______ (photo) were all in black and white, showing scenes from decades ago. In one picture, my grandmother was standing in front of a small village school, (3) ______ (hold) a textbook in her hands. She looked very young — perhaps only sixteen or seventeen. I showed the photo to her and asked about it. She told me that she (4) ______ (be) a teacher in that village for ten years before moving to the city. "Those were the (5) ______ (happy) years of my life," she said with a warm smile. Another photo showed my grandfather (6) ______ (ride) a bicycle along a country road. He was carrying a bag full (7) ______ tools because he worked as a repairman at that time. I was surprised to learn how different their lives had been (8) ______ mine. They didn't have smartphones or the internet, but they had something (9) ______ (equal) valuable — close relationships with the people around them. Looking at those old photos helped me (10) ______ (understand) my family's history and made me appreciate the life I have today.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：belonged**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

2. **答案：photos**
   - **考点**：名词复数。相册里有很多照片，用复数。

3. **答案：holding**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。现在分词作伴随状语，与主语是主动关系。

4. **答案：had been**
   - **考点**：时态。`had been a teacher for ten years before moving` 表示在搬家之前已经当了十年老师，用过去完成时。

5. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the happiest years`。

6. **答案：riding**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`showed my grandfather riding`，感官/使役类动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词。

7. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`a bag full of tools`，`full of` 表示"装满"。

8. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`different from` 表示"与……不同"。

9. **答案：equally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `valuable` 需要副词，`equal` 变为 `equally`。

10. **答案：understand**
    - **考点**：动词原形。`help sb (to) do sth`。
