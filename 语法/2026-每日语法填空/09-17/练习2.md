# 09-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市绿化 (Urban Greening)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Green spaces are essential for making cities (1) ______ (live) and healthy. In recent years, our city government (2) ______ (invest) heavily in urban greening projects. New parks (3) ______ (build) in every district, and thousands of trees have been planted along major roads. The results are impressive. The air quality has improved (4) ______ (great), and the city looks much more beautiful than before. Near my home, there is a small park that (5) ______ (complete) last year. It has a jogging path, a children's playground and a pond with (6) ______ (gold) fish. My family goes there almost every evening. Mum does tai chi on the grass (7) ______ Dad and I jog around the path. The park has become a meeting place (8) ______ neighbours can chat and relax together. Studies show that people who live near green spaces tend to be (9) ______ (happy) and healthier than those who don't. This is because nature helps reduce stress and encourages outdoor (10) ______ (active). I hope our city will continue to create more green spaces so that everyone can enjoy the benefits of nature.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：liveable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`making cities liveable`，使役动词 + 宾语 + 形容词补语，`live` 变为 `liveable`。

2. **答案：has invested**
   - **考点**：时态。`in recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

3. **答案：have been built**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`new parks have been built`，公园"被建造"，现在完成时被动。

4. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词，`great` 变为 `greatly`。

5. **答案：was completed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`that was completed last year`，公园"被完工"。

6. **答案：gold / golden**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `fish` 需要形容词，`gold` 可直接作形容词或变为 `golden`。

7. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。`Mum does tai chi while Dad and I jog`，`while` 表示"当……的时候"（同时进行）。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a meeting place`（地点），定语从句中缺地点状语。

9. **答案：happier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`happier and healthier than`。

10. **答案：activity / activities**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`outdoor activity/activities`，`active` 变为名词 `activity`。
