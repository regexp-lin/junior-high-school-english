# 07-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护视力 (Protecting Our Eyesight)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

More and more young people in China are becoming short-sighted. A recent study shows that nearly 80 percent of high school students wear (1) ______ (glass). Experts say one major reason is that students spend too much time (2) ______ (look) at screens. Many teenagers use their phones for hours every day, (3) ______ puts great pressure on their eyes. Another reason is the lack of outdoor activity. Research has proved that (4) ______ (spend) time in natural sunlight helps protect our eyesight. Doctors recommend that children should have at (5) ______ two hours of outdoor activity every day. (6) ______ (sad), most students are too busy with homework to go outside. Our school has taken some steps (7) ______ (improve) the situation. We now have eye exercises twice a day, and the classrooms have been equipped (8) ______ better lighting. Teachers also remind us to follow the "20-20-20" rule: every twenty minutes, look at something twenty feet away (9) ______ at least twenty seconds. I have been following this rule for three months, and my eye doctor says my eyesight has not got any (10) ______ (bad).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：glasses**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`glasses` 表示"眼镜"，总是用复数形式。

2. **答案：looking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事（长时间使用手机），在从句中作主语。

4. **答案：spending**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。`spending time in natural sunlight` 在 `that` 从句中作主语。

5. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`at least` 表示"至少"。

6. **答案：Sadly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰整个句子用副词，`sad` 变为 `sadly`（令人遗憾地），句首首字母大写。

7. **答案：to improve**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。学校采取措施的目的是改善情况，用 `to improve`。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be equipped with` 为固定搭配，表示"配备了"。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for at least twenty seconds` 表示"至少二十秒"，`for` 接一段时间。

10. **答案：worse**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`any worse` 表示"更差"，`bad` 的比较级为 `worse`，`any` 用于否定句强调程度。
