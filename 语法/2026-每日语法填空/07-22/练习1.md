# 07-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学下棋 (Learning to Play Chess)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My grandfather taught me to play Chinese chess when I was nine. He was a retired factory worker, but on the chessboard, he was (1) ______ king. He could think five moves ahead and always seemed to know (2) ______ I was going to do next. Every afternoon, we sat at the stone table in the park and played for hours. In the (3) ______ (begin), I lost every single game. I often got frustrated and wanted to quit. But my grandfather told me that losing was the (4) ______ (fast) way to learn. He said, "Pay attention to the moves that beat you, and you will become (5) ______ (strong) each time." I followed his advice and (6) ______ (slow) improved. After about six months, I finally won my first game against him. I was so excited that I jumped up from my chair, (7) ______ (knock) the pieces all over the ground. My grandfather laughed and said I still had a lot (8) ______ (learn). That was three years ago. Now I can beat most of my classmates, (9) ______ I still cannot win against Grandpa. He remains the (10) ______ (tough) opponent I have ever faced.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`a king` 表示"一个国王/高手"，泛指，此处为比喻用法。

2. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。引导宾语从句，`what I was going to do` 表示"我将要做什么"，`what` 作 `do` 的宾语。

3. **答案：beginning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`in the beginning` 为固定搭配，表示"在最初"，`begin` 变为 `beginning`。

4. **答案：fastest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + way`，`fast` 的最高级为 `fastest`。

5. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`become stronger each time` 表示"每次都变得更强"，隐含递进比较。

6. **答案：slowly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词，`slow` 变为 `slowly`。

7. **答案：knocking**
   - **考点**：现在分词作结果状语。表示"跳起来"的意外结果——把棋子撞翻了一地。

8. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：不定式。`a lot to learn` 表示"很多要学的东西"，不定式作后置定语。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。"能赢大部分同学"与"仍然赢不了爷爷"为转折关系。

10. **答案：toughest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词 + I have ever faced`，`tough` 的最高级为 `toughest`。
