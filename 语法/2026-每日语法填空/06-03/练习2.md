# 06-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：智能机器人 (Smart Robots)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In modern factories, robots work 24 hours a day (1) ______ they never feel bored. Engineers are trying to make them even (2) ______ (smart) so that they can deal with unexpected situations. Some robots (3) ______ (design) to help doctors carry tools in hospitals, while others are used in homes (4) ______ (clean) the floor. Although a robot does not have feelings, it can recognize human voices (5) ______ the help of special programs. Many people wonder (6) ______ robots will take away human jobs in the future. Scientists say new jobs will appear at the same time, (7) ______ workers need to keep learning. The (8) ______ (develop) of AI technology has already changed our daily life, and we must use (9) ______ wisely instead of depending on it too much. I believe robots and humans will work side (10) ______ side in the years ahead.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and**  
   - **考点**：连词。“全天运转”与“从不感到无聊”为并列补充，说明机器人的工作特点，用 `and` 连接。

2. **答案：smarter**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`even` 常用来加强比较级，表示“更聪明”，填 `smarter`。

3. **答案：are designed**  
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。陈述客观事实用一般现在时；`robots` 与 `design` 为被动关系，复数主语用 `are designed`。

4. **答案：to clean**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be used to do sth.` 表示“被用来做某事”，不定式表用途，填 `to clean`。勿与 `be used to doing`（习惯于）混淆。

5. **答案：with**  
   - **考点**：介词固定搭配。`with the help of...` 意为“在……的帮助下”。

6. **答案：whether / if**  
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。`wonder` 后接“是否”类从句，用 `whether` 或 `if`。

7. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。“新工作会出现”与“工人仍需不断学习”形成转折，强调挑战仍在，用 `but`。

8. **答案：development**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the...of` 中间需名词，将 `develop` 变为 `development`。

9. **答案：it**  
   - **考点**：代词。指代上文不可数的 `AI technology`，作 `use` 的宾语，用 `it`。

10. **答案：by**  
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`side by side` 表示“并肩地”。
