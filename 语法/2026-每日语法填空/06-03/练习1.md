# 06-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：传统武术 (Traditional Martial Arts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese martial arts have a history of thousands of years and they are still (1) ______ (wide) practiced today. Many young people go to training halls (2) ______ they hope to improve both strength and self-discipline. A skilled coach usually asks beginners (3) ______ (repeat) basic movements hundreds of times (4) ______ they can perform them correctly. Some forms look (5) ______ (dance), but every step is designed for defense or attack. In recent years, several kung fu movies (6) ______ (make) this tradition known around the world. Foreign friends who come to China are often invited (7) ______ (watch) short performances in parks. To protect this treasure, more schools have decided (8) ______ (include) martial arts in their PE lessons. If you keep practicing, you will realize it is not only a sport (9) ______ also part of our culture. Nothing great can (10) ______ (achieve) in a single day, so patience matters.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：widely**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `practiced`，表示“被广泛练习”，用副词 `widely`。括号内 `wide` 需变为副词。

2. **答案：where**  
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `training halls` 表地点，从句缺状语，用关系副词 `where`（= in which）。

3. **答案：to repeat**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`ask sb. to do sth.` 为固定结构，用不定式 `to repeat`。

4. **答案：before / until**  
   - **考点**：连词。表示“在能正确做出动作之前/直到能正确做出”，反复练基础动作，用 `before`（在……之前）或 `until`（直到……才）。**before** 强调先后顺序更自然。

5. **答案：like**  
   - **考点**：介词。`look like` 表示“看起来像”，后接名词 `dance`（此处作名词“舞蹈”）。无括号，填介词 `like`。

6. **答案：have made**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。`In recent years` 为现在完成时标志，主语 `movies` 为复数，用 `have made`。

7. **答案：to watch**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be invited to do sth.` 用不定式 `to watch`。

8. **答案：to include**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`decide to do sth.` 用不定式 `to include`。

9. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but also...` 连接并列成分，填 `but`。

10. **答案：be achieved**  
    - **考点**：动词语态。主语 `Nothing great` 与 `achieve` 为被动（伟大之事被达成），`can` 后用 `be achieved`。
