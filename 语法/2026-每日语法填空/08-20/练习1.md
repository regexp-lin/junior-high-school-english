# 08-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校心理咨询室 (The School Counselling Room)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school recently opened a counselling room (1) ______ students can talk to a professional psychologist about their worries. The room is on the second floor, next (2) ______ the music classroom. It is decorated with warm colours and soft cushions to make visitors feel (3) ______ (relax). The psychologist, Ms. Ye, is a kind woman in her (4) ______ (thirty). She is available every Tuesday and Thursday afternoon. Students can book an appointment or simply walk in. At first, many students were embarrassed about (5) ______ (go) there because they thought only people with serious problems needed counselling. But Ms. Ye explained that everyone faces (6) ______ (emotion) challenges from time to time, and asking for help is a sign of strength, not (7) ______ (weak). Gradually, more students started visiting. My friend Xiao Wen went there when she felt overwhelmed by exam pressure. She said Ms. Ye helped her develop (8) ______ (health) ways to cope with stress. After just three sessions, she felt much (9) ______ (calm) and more focused. I think every school should have a counselling room. Looking after our mental health is just (10) ______ important as looking after our physical health.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a counselling room`（地点），在定语从句中作状语。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`next to` 为固定搭配，表示"旁边"。

3. **答案：relaxed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel relaxed` 中 `relax` 变为 `relaxed`（放松的），描述人的感受。

4. **答案：thirties**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`in her thirties` 表示"在她三十多岁时"。

5. **答案：going**
   - **考点**：动名词。`about` 为介词，后接动名词。

6. **答案：emotional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `challenges` 需要形容词，`emotion` 变为 `emotional`。

7. **答案：weakness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `strength` 对比，`weak` 变为名词 `weakness`。

8. **答案：healthier / healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `ways` 需要形容词，`health` 变为 `healthy`/`healthier`（更健康的），两者均可。

9. **答案：calmer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`much calmer` 表示"平静得多"。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：连词。`just as important as` 为同级比较结构，表示"同样重要"。
