# 08-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：蝴蝶的一生 (The Life of a Butterfly)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Butterflies are some of the most beautiful creatures on Earth, but their journey to (1) ______ (beautiful) is truly remarkable. A butterfly's life begins as a tiny egg, usually (2) ______ (lay) on the underside of a leaf. After a few days, a caterpillar hatches and begins eating (3) ______ (hunger). It eats leaves non-stop and grows (4) ______ (rapid), shedding its skin several times. When the caterpillar is fully grown, it attaches (5) ______ (it) to a branch and forms a chrysalis. Inside this hard shell, one of nature's most amazing (6) ______ (transform) takes place. The caterpillar's body breaks down completely and rebuilds (7) ______ into a butterfly. This process, called metamorphosis, takes about two weeks. When the butterfly finally emerges, its wings are wet and folded. It must wait (8) ______ they dry before it can fly. I once watched this happen in our school science lab. Seeing the butterfly spread its (9) ______ (colour) wings for the first time was one of the most (10) ______ (move) moments I have ever witnessed. The butterfly's story teaches us that great change often requires patience and struggle.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：beauty**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`to` 后接名词（此处表示目标/目的地），`beautiful` 变为 `beauty`。

2. **答案：laid**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`an egg laid on the leaf` 表示"产在叶子上的卵"，`lay` 的过去分词为 `laid`。

3. **答案：hungrily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `eating` 需要副词，`hunger` 变为 `hungrily`。

4. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `grows` 需要副词，`rapid` 变为 `rapidly`。

5. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。毛毛虫把自己附着在树枝上，主语与宾语指同一对象。

6. **答案：transformations**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`one of + 复数名词`，`transform` 变为 `transformation`。

7. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。身体"重建自身"，指代 the caterpillar's body。

8. **答案：until / till**
   - **考点**：连词。`wait until they dry` 表示"等到翅膀干了"。

9. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `wings` 需要形容词，`colour` 变为 `colourful`。

10. **答案：moving**
    - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most moving` 中 `move` 变为 `moving`（感人的）。
