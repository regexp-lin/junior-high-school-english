# 07-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：足球梦 (Football Dreams)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Football has been my passion ever since I watched my first World Cup match (1) ______ TV at the age of six. The speed, the teamwork, and the excitement of the game (2) ______ (attract) me immediately. I joined the school football team in Grade Seven and have practised almost every day since (3) ______. Our coach, Mr. Huang, is strict but fair. He always tells us that talent alone is not enough — we must also train (4) ______ (hard) than everyone else. Last autumn, our team entered the city league for the first time. We were the (5) ______ (young) team in the competition, and nobody expected us to do well. In the first two games, we lost badly. Some teammates wanted to give (6) ______, but our captain reminded us why we started playing. In the third game, we finally won. The feeling of (7) ______ (score) the winning goal was unbelievable. We did not win the league, but we finished in fifth place, which was much (8) ______ (good) than anyone had predicted. The experience taught me that success is not just about (9) ______ (win); it is about never stopping even when things seem (10) ______ (hope). I will keep chasing my football dream.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on TV` 为固定搭配，表示"在电视上"。

2. **答案：attracted**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述六岁时的感受，用过去时。

3. **答案：then**
   - **考点**：副词。`since then` 表示"从那以后"，与现在完成时搭配。

4. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than everyone else` 提示用比较级，`hard` 的比较级为 `harder`。

5. **答案：youngest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词`，`young` 的最高级为 `youngest`。

6. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`give up` 为固定搭配，表示"放弃"。

7. **答案：scoring**
   - **考点**：动名词。`of` 为介词，后接动名词 `scoring`。

8. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than anyone had predicted` 提示用比较级，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

9. **答案：winning**
   - **考点**：动名词。`about` 为介词，后接动名词 `winning`。

10. **答案：hopeless**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`seem` 为系动词后接形容词，`hope` 变为 `hopeless`（无望的），加后缀 `-less` 表否定。
