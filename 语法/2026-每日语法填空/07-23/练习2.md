# 07-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：第一次演讲 (My First Speech)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I will never forget the day I gave my first speech in front of the whole school. It happened in Grade Eight, when our teacher asked me (1) ______ (represent) our class in the English speech contest. I was both honoured and (2) ______ (terrify). For two weeks, I practised my speech every evening. I stood in front of the mirror and spoke to (3) ______ (I) again and again. My mother listened patiently and offered (4) ______ (help) suggestions. On the day of the contest, my heart beat so (5) ______ (fast) that I could feel it in my throat. When I walked onto the stage, I saw hundreds of faces (6) ______ (stare) at me. My mind went blank for a moment. Then I took a deep (7) ______ (breathe) and began to speak. As I got into the flow, my voice became (8) ______ (steady) and my fear gradually disappeared. By the end, I was actually enjoying (9) ______ (I). The audience clapped warmly, and I won second prize. That speech changed me. I used to be someone (10) ______ avoided attention, but now I welcome every chance to speak in public.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to represent**
   - **考点**：不定式。`ask sb to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"请某人做某事"。

2. **答案：terrified**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `honoured` 并列，描述人的感受，`terrify` 变为 `terrified`（感到害怕的）。

3. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 `I`，对着镜子说给自己听，用 `myself`。

4. **答案：helpful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `suggestions` 需要形容词，`help` 变为 `helpful`。

5. **答案：fast**
   - **考点**：副词。`so...that...` 结构中修饰动词 `beat`；`fast` 本身可作副词，无需变形。

6. **答案：staring**
   - **考点**：现在分词作宾补。`see sb doing sth` 表示"看见某人正在做某事"。

7. **答案：breath**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a deep breath` 表示"深呼吸"，`breathe`（动词）变为名词 `breath`。

8. **答案：steadier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`became steadier` 表示"变得更稳定"，暗含与之前紧张时的对比。

9. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`enjoy myself` 表示"享受，玩得开心"。

10. **答案：who / that**
    - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `someone`（人），在定语从句中作主语。
