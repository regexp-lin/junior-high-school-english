# 04-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：科技创新 (Science and Innovation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Science and innovation have always pushed human society forward. Every great invention started with someone 1. ________ (ask) a simple question: "What if?"

In the field of medicine, scientists have developed vaccines that protect people 2. ________ dangerous diseases. The COVID-19 vaccine, for example, 3. ________ (save) millions of lives around the world. Without the hard work of these scientists, many more people would have died.

China has also made remarkable progress in science and technology. The high-speed train network is one of the 4. ________ (large) in the world, making travel between cities much 5. ________ (fast). Chinese scientists have also sent rovers to Mars and built a space station 6. ________ (independent).

Innovation is not just about big discoveries. It can also happen in our daily lives. For instance, a student in Shenzhen 7. ________ (recent) designed a smart trash can that can sort rubbish automatically. Another student invented a device that helps blind people 8. ________ (walk) safely on the street.

Schools play an important role in encouraging innovation. When students are given the freedom 9. ________ (explore) and experiment, amazing things can happen. Every young person has the potential to become an 10. ________ (invent).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **asking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。someone asking 构成 with 的省略结构或现在分词作后置定语，意为"有人提出一个问题"。

2. **from/against**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。protect sb. from/against 意为"保护某人免受……"。

3. **has saved**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。for example 举例说明事实，且影响持续到现在，用现在完成时。

4. **largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，large → largest。

5. **faster**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。much 修饰比较级，fast → faster。

6. **independently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 built，需用副词形式 independently。

7. **recently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 designed，需用副词形式 recently。

8. **walk**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（省略 to 的不定式）。help sb. (to) do sth. 意为"帮助某人做某事"，to 可省略。

9. **to explore**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作后置定语）。the freedom to explore 意为"探索的自由"。

10. **inventor**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。an + 名词，invent → inventor（发明家）。
