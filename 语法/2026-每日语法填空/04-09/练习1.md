# 04-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：自律与自我管理 (Self-discipline)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Self-discipline means controlling your own behavior and making yourself do things that you should do, even when you don't feel like 1. ________ (do) them. It is a quality that can help you succeed in every area of life.

Many successful people have one thing 2. ________ common: strong self-discipline. They set clear goals and work toward them every day. They don't waste time on unimportant things 3. ________ make excuses for being lazy.

As students, self-discipline is 4. ________ (especial) important during exam season. It is tempting to play games or chat with friends online instead of 5. ________ (study). But if you can control yourself and focus on your work, you will be rewarded with better 6. ________ (grade).

Here are some ways to build self-discipline. First, start with small goals. For example, promise yourself to study for one hour 7. ________ checking your phone. Once you achieve this small goal, you will feel more 8. ________ (confidence) to take on bigger challenges.

Second, create a daily schedule and try your best to follow it. 9. ________ (write) down what you need to do helps you stay organized. Third, find a study partner who can encourage you when you feel like giving up.

Building self-discipline takes time, but the effort is 10. ________ (definite) worth it. Start today, and you will thank yourself in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **doing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。feel like doing sth. 意为"想要做某事"，like 为介词，后接动名词。

2. **in**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。have sth. in common 意为"有共同之处"。

3. **or**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。否定句中 or 连接并列成分，"不浪费时间或找借口"。

4. **especially**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 important，需用副词形式 especially。

5. **studying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。instead of 后接动名词形式。

6. **grades**
   - **考点**：名词复数。better grades 意为"更好的成绩"，grade 用复数。

7. **before**
   - **考点**：介词/连词（无提示词填空）。before checking your phone 意为"在查看手机之前"。

8. **confident**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。feel more confident 意为"感到更有信心"，confidence → confident。

9. **Writing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。Writing down... 在句首作主语，注意首字母大写。

10. **definitely**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 worth，需用副词形式 definitely。
