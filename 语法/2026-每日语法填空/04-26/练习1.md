# 04-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：节约能源 (Saving Energy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Energy is something we use every day, but most people don't think about where it comes 1. ________ or how much of it we waste. Saving energy is important for both the environment and our wallets.

At home, there are many simple ways to save energy. One of the 2. ________ (easy) things is to turn off lights when you leave a room. You might be 3. ________ (surprise) to learn that leaving lights on in empty rooms wastes a huge amount of electricity every year. Switching to LED bulbs can also help because they use 4. ________ (little) energy than traditional bulbs.

Heating and cooling account 5. ________ a large part of household energy use. In winter, wearing warm clothes at home instead of turning up the heater is a smart choice. In summer, using a fan is much more energy-efficient than 6. ________ (run) the air conditioner all day.

In our daily lives, we should try to reduce, reuse and recycle. For example, bringing your own shopping bag 7. ________ (save) the energy needed to produce plastic bags. Recycling paper and plastic means that 8. ________ (few) new materials need to be made from scratch.

The government is also 9. ________ (take) steps to promote clean energy. Solar panels and wind turbines are becoming more common across the country.

Saving energy is everyone's 10. ________ (responsible). Let's start with small changes and build a more sustainable future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **from**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。come from 意为"来自"。

2. **easiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词，easy → easiest。

3. **surprised**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be surprised to learn 意为"惊讶地得知"，主语是人用 -ed 形式。

4. **less**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。than 提示用比较级，little → less，修饰不可数名词 energy。

5. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。account for 意为"占（比例）"。

6. **running**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。than 后接动名词，与前面 using 并列比较。

7. **saves**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）+ 主谓一致。动名词短语 bringing your own shopping bag 作主语，视为第三人称单数。

8. **fewer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。few → fewer，修饰可数名词 materials。

9. **taking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词/现在进行时）。is taking 构成现在进行时。

10. **responsibility**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。everyone's + 名词，responsible → responsibility。
