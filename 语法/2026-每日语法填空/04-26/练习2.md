# 04-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：好邻居 (Good Neighbors)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A good neighbor can make your life much 1. ________ (happy). Having friendly neighbors creates a warm and safe community where everyone helps each other.

I am lucky to have wonderful neighbors. The family next door, the Wangs, 2. ________ (move) in five years ago. Mrs. Wang is a retired teacher who often helps me with my homework. She is so patient 3. ________ she never gets tired of explaining things to me. Mr. Wang loves gardening, and he always shares his fresh vegetables 4. ________ us.

Last winter, something happened that made me truly appreciate my neighbors. My parents were both away on business, and I was home alone. One evening, the heating 5. ________ (sudden) stopped working. I didn't know what to do and felt very 6. ________ (worry).

I knocked on the Wangs' door, and they immediately invited me 7. ________ (stay) at their house. Mrs. Wang made me a bowl of hot soup, and Mr. Wang called a repairman for me. They treated me 8. ________ if I were their own child.

In modern cities, many people don't even know their 9. ________ (neighbor) names. This is sad because good neighbors make life richer and safer. A simple greeting, a helping hand or a plate of homemade food can 10. ________ (strength) the bond between neighbors.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **happier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，happy → happier。

2. **moved**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。five years ago 提示用一般过去时。

3. **that**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。so...that... 意为"如此……以至于"。

4. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。share sth. with sb. 意为"与某人分享某物"。

5. **suddenly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 stopped，需用副词形式 suddenly。

6. **worried**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。feel worried 意为"感到担忧"，主语是人用 -ed 形式。

7. **to stay**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。invite sb. to do sth. 意为"邀请某人做某事"。

8. **as**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。as if 意为"好像"，引导方式状语从句。

9. **neighbors'**
   - **考点**：名词所有格 + 复数。their neighbors' names 意为"邻居们的名字"，neighbor → neighbors'。

10. **strengthen**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→动词）。can + 动词原形，strength → strengthen（加强）。
