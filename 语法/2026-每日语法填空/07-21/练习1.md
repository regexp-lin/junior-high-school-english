# 07-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：夏令营 (Summer Camp)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This summer, I attended a seven-day English camp in the mountains. There were sixty students from different (1) ______ (school) in the province. On the first day, we were divided into six teams, and each team (2) ______ (give) a name in English. Our team was called "The Explorers." Every morning, we had English lessons taught by native speakers. The (3) ______ (teach) method was very different from what we were used to in school. Instead of memorising grammar rules, we learned (4) ______ playing games and acting out stories. In the afternoons, we did outdoor activities (5) ______ as hiking, rock climbing, and canoeing. These activities required us (6) ______ (communicate) in English with our teammates. At first, I was too shy (7) ______ (speak) up, but by the third day, I found (8) ______ (I) talking more freely. On the last night, each team performed a short play in English. Ours was about a group of travellers (9) ______ got lost on a magical island. The audience loved it! The camp was one of the most (10) ______ (reward) experiences of my life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：schools**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different` 修饰可数名词复数。

2. **答案：was given**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。每个队"被给予"一个英文名字，`each team` 为单数。

3. **答案：teaching**
   - **考点**：动名词/现在分词作定语。`teaching method` 表示"教学方法"。

4. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`learn by doing` 表示"通过做来学"，`by` 表示方式。

5. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`such as` 表示"例如"。

6. **答案：to communicate**
   - **考点**：不定式。`require sb to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"要求某人做某事"。

7. **答案：to speak**
   - **考点**：不定式。`too...to...` 表示"太……而不能……"。

8. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`found myself talking` 为 `find + 宾语 + 现在分词` 结构，表示"发现自己在做某事"。

9. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `travellers`（人），在定语从句中作主语。

10. **答案：rewarding**
    - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most rewarding`，`reward` 变为 `rewarding`（有回报的，值得的）。
