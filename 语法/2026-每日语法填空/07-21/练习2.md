# 07-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：高铁改变生活 (High-Speed Rail Changes Lives)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's high-speed rail network is the (1) ______ (large) in the world. It connects hundreds of cities and has made (2) ______ (travel) across the country faster and easier than ever before. A journey that used to take ten hours by ordinary train now takes only three hours by high-speed rail. The trains run at speeds of up (3) ______ 350 kilometres per hour, yet they are (4) ______ (surprise) smooth and quiet. Passengers can work on laptops, read, or simply enjoy the scenery (5) ______ (fly) past the window. High-speed rail has also brought great changes (6) ______ small towns along the routes. Before the railway came, some of these towns (7) ______ (know) by very few people. Now, tourists visit them on weekends, which helps the local economy grow. My hometown got its own high-speed station two years ago. Since then, my father (8) ______ (be) able to commute to the city for work, so our family did not need to move. I am proud that China (9) ______ (build) such an amazing transport system, and I look forward to the day (10) ______ high-speed trains connect even the most remote areas.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + in the world`，`large` 的最高级为 `largest`。

2. **答案：travelling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`make travelling faster` 中 `travelling` 作宾语。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`up to` 为固定搭配，表示"高达"。

4. **答案：surprisingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `smooth` 需要副词，`surprise` 变为 `surprisingly`（出人意料地）。

5. **答案：flying**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`the scenery flying past the window` 表示"飞速掠过窗外的风景"，现在分词表主动。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`bring changes to` 表示"给……带来变化"。

7. **答案：were known**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。`Before the railway came` 表示过去，这些小镇"被很少人所知"。

8. **答案：has been**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 提示用现在完成时，`has been able to` 表示"自那以来一直能够"。

9. **答案：has built**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。描述中国已经建成的交通系统，表示到目前为止的成就。

10. **答案：when**
    - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the day`（时间），在定语从句中作时间状语，用 `when`。
