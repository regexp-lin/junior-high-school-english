# 04-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界读书日 (World Book Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

April 23rd is World Book Day, a day dedicated to celebrating the joy of reading. It was established by UNESCO in 1995 and is now 1. ________ (celebrate) in over 100 countries.

Reading has many benefits. First, it improves our vocabulary. The more we read, the more words we learn, and the 2. ________ (good) our writing becomes. Second, reading helps us think more 3. ________ (deep). When we read a novel, we have to analyze characters, understand plots and make 4. ________ (predict) about what will happen next.

Different people enjoy different types of books. Some prefer fiction, 5. ________ others like non-fiction. Personally, I enjoy reading historical novels because they allow me 6. ________ (learn) about history in an interesting way. My friend Li Ming, on the other hand, loves science fiction. He says it fires up his 7. ________ (imagine).

Our school celebrated World Book Day by organizing a book exchange. Every student brought one book from home and exchanged it 8. ________ a classmate's book. I gave away a detective novel and received a book about space 9. ________ (explore). It turned out to be one of the best books I have ever read!

Reading is a lifelong habit. As the saying goes, "A room 10. ________ books is like a body without a soul." So pick up a book today and start your reading journey.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **celebrated**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般现在时）。is celebrated 意为"被庆祝"，World Book Day 与 celebrate 之间是被动关系。

2. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。the more...the better... 句型，good → better。

3. **deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 think，需用副词形式 deeply。

4. **predictions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。make predictions 意为"做预测"，predict → prediction → predictions。

5. **while**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。while 表示对比关系。

6. **to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。allow sb. to do sth. 意为"允许某人做某事"。

7. **imagination**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。his + 名词，imagine → imagination。

8. **with/for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。exchange A with/for B 意为"用A交换B"。

9. **exploration**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。about space exploration 意为"关于太空探索的"，explore → exploration。

10. **without**
    - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。a room without books 意为"没有书的房间"，引用名言。
