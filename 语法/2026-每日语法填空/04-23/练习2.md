# 04-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：骑行文化 (Bicycle Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China was once known 1. ________ the "Kingdom of Bicycles." In the 1980s, bicycles were the main transportation for most Chinese families. Although cars have largely replaced them, bicycles are making a 2. ________ (come) back in recent years.

Shared bicycles appeared on Chinese streets around 2016 and quickly became very 3. ________ (popularity). People can rent a bike by simply scanning a QR code on their phones. It is cheap, convenient and good for the environment. Now you can see colorful shared bikes parked 4. ________ almost every corner of the city.

Cycling has many 5. ________ (advantage). First, it is a great form of exercise. Riding a bicycle for just thirty minutes a day can help you stay fit and 6. ________ (reduce) stress. Second, unlike cars, bicycles don't produce any pollution, so they are much 7. ________ (friendly) to the environment.

Many cities around the world are 8. ________ (build) more bike lanes to encourage people to cycle. In the Netherlands, for example, there are more bicycles 9. ________ people! The country has built a world-class network of bike paths that makes cycling safe and enjoyable.

Whether you use a bicycle to get to school, exercise or just have fun, cycling is one of the 10. ________ (simple) ways to live a healthier and greener life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **as**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be known as 意为"被称为"。

2. **comeback**
   - **考点**：词性转换/构词法（动词→名词）。make a comeback 意为"卷土重来"，come → comeback。

3. **popular**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。became + 形容词作表语，popularity → popular。

4. **at/on**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。at/on every corner 意为"在每个角落"。

5. **advantages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。many + 可数名词复数，advantage → advantages。

6. **reduce**
   - **考点**：动词原形。and 连接并列动词，与 stay 并列，help you stay fit and reduce stress。

7. **friendlier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，friendly → friendlier。

8. **building**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词/现在进行时）。are building 构成现在进行时。

9. **than**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。more...than... 表示比较。

10. **simplest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，simple → simplest。
