# 12-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国古建筑 (Chinese Architecture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese architecture is admired around the world for its beauty, elegance and deep cultural (1) ______ (significant). From the Forbidden City in Beijing to the ancient water towns of Jiangnan, Chinese buildings reflect thousands of years of artistic and engineering (2) ______ (achieve). One of the most striking features of traditional Chinese architecture is the use of curved roofs. These roofs are not just (3) ______ (visual) appealing — they also serve practical purposes. The upward curves help drain rainwater more (4) ______ (effective) and allow more sunlight to enter the building. Another important feature is the use of wood as the main building material. Unlike Western architecture, which relies (5) ______ (heavy) on stone, traditional Chinese buildings are constructed almost entirely from wood. This makes them lighter and more flexible, which is especially important in earthquake-prone areas. Last summer, my family visited the ancient town of Pingyao in Shanxi Province. The town (6) ______ (list) as a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1997. Walking through its narrow streets, I felt as though I (7) ______ (travel) back in time. The courtyard houses, the city walls and the old temples were all (8) ______ (perfect) preserved. Our guide told us that Pingyao was one of the few places in China (9) ______ you could still see what a traditional Chinese city looked like centuries ago. The visit inspired me to learn more about Chinese architectural history. I hope that these (10) ______ (price) heritage sites will be protected for future generations to appreciate.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：significance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。形容词 `cultural` 后接名词。

2. **答案：achievement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`artistic and engineering achievement`，需要名词形式。

3. **答案：visually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `appealing` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：effectively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `drain` 需要副词形式。

5. **答案：heavily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `relies` 需要副词形式。

6. **答案：was listed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`The town` 与 `list` 之间是被动关系，`in 1997` 提示过去时。

7. **答案：had travelled / were travelling**
   - **考点**：虚拟语气/过去进行时。`as though I had travelled back in time`，表示虚拟。

8. **答案：perfectly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `preserved` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，先行词 `places` 为地点。

10. **答案：priceless**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `heritage sites` 需要形容词，`price` 变为 `priceless`（无价的）。
