# 12-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：肢体语言 (Body Language)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Body language is a powerful form of (1) ______ (communicate) that often speaks louder than words. It includes facial expressions, gestures, posture and eye contact. Experts say that over 60% of the messages we send in face-to-face conversations are conveyed through body language rather (2) ______ spoken words. (3) ______ (understand) body language can help us interact with others more effectively. For example, making eye contact shows that you are paying attention and are interested (4) ______ what the other person is saying. On the other hand, crossing your arms may signal that you are (5) ______ (defend) or closed off to new ideas. Body language can vary greatly across different (6) ______ (culture). A gesture that is considered friendly in one country may be (7) ______ (complete) rude in another. In China, nodding your head means "yes," just as it does in most Western countries. However, in some parts of Greece and Bulgaria, nodding actually means "no." Our English teacher once shared an (8) ______ (interest) story about body language. When she first visited the UK, she was confused because British people stood much (9) ______ (far) apart during conversations than Chinese people do. She later learned that this is because people in different cultures have different ideas about personal space. She advised us to be (10) ______ (mind) of cultural differences when communicating with people from other countries.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：communication**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `of` 后接名词。

2. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`rather than` 表示"而不是"。

3. **答案：Understanding**
   - **考点**：动名词。动名词作主语。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be interested in` 表示"对……感兴趣"。

5. **答案：defensive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。系动词 `are` 后接形容词，`defend` 变为 `defensive`。

6. **答案：cultures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different cultures` 表示"不同文化"。

7. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `rude` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `story` 用 `-ing` 形式。

9. **答案：farther / further**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than Chinese people do` 提示比较级，`far` 的比较级。

10. **答案：mindful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`be mindful of` 表示"注意，留心"，`mind` 变为形容词 `mindful`。
