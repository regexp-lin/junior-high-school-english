# 11-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：餐桌礼仪 (Table Manners)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Good table manners are an important part of everyday life. They show respect for the people we eat with and reflect our upbringing. Different countries have different rules about table manners, (1) ______ some basic principles are universal. In China, there are several customs that diners are expected (2) ______ (follow). For example, the eldest person at the table should start eating first. It is considered (3) ______ (polite) to begin before the elders. Also, you should not stick your chopsticks (4) ______ (vertical) into a bowl of rice, because this resembles incense sticks used at funerals. I remember an embarrassing incident that happened when I was ten. My family went to a formal dinner at a (5) ______ (relative) house. I was so hungry that I started eating before everyone else (6) ______ (sit) down. My mother gently pulled my arm and whispered to me that I should wait. I felt my face (7) ______ (turn) red, but I learned an important lesson that day. Western table manners are quite different (8) ______ Chinese ones. In Western countries, people use knives and forks instead of chopsticks. The knife is held in the right hand, and the fork in the left. It is also important not to put your elbows (9) ______ the table while eating. Whether in China or the West, having good table manners makes dining a more (10) ______ (please) experience for everyone.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前后转折，"不同国家有不同规则，但一些基本原则是通用的"。

2. **答案：to follow**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be expected to do sth` 表示"被期望做某事"。

3. **答案：impolite**
   - **考点**：词性转换。根据语境"在长辈之前开始吃是不礼貌的"，需要加否定前缀。

4. **答案：vertically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `stick` 需要副词形式。

5. **答案：relative's**
   - **考点**：名词所有格。`a relative's house` 表示"一个亲戚的家"。

6. **答案：had sat / sat**
   - **考点**：时态。在其他人就座之前我就开始吃了，可用过去完成时或一般过去时。

7. **答案：turn / turning**
   - **考点**：感官动词。`felt my face turn/turning red`，感官动词后接原形或现在分词。

8. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`be different from` 表示"与……不同"。

9. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`put your elbows on the table` 表示"把手肘放在桌上"。

10. **答案：pleasant**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`a more pleasant experience`，修饰名词需要形容词。
