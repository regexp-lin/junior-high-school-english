# 11-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：科学实验 (Science Experiments)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Science experiments are an exciting way to explore the natural world. They help students understand how things work by (1) ______ (let) them observe and test ideas with their own hands. Our science teacher, Mr Liu, always says that "doing is the best way of (2) ______ (learn)." Every Thursday afternoon, we have a double science lesson in the laboratory. Last week, we did an experiment to test which materials are the best (3) ______ (conduct) of electricity. Mr Liu divided us into groups of four, and each group (4) ______ (give) a battery, some wires and various objects to test, including a metal spoon, a rubber band, a wooden stick and a glass marble. We connected each object to the circuit and checked (5) ______ the light bulb would turn on. Not (6) ______ (surprise), the metal spoon was the only object that allowed electricity to pass through. The experiment helped us understand why electrical wires (7) ______ (make) of metal and covered with rubber or plastic. I especially enjoy experiments because they make (8) ______ (theory) knowledge come alive. It is one thing to read about a scientific concept in a textbook, but it is quite (9) ______ to see it happen right in front of you. I hope to become a scientist one day and conduct experiments that can benefit (10) ______ (social).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：letting**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词，表示方式。

2. **答案：learning**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词形式。

3. **答案：conductors**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the best conductors of electricity`，`conduct` 变为名词 `conductor`，复数形式。

4. **答案：was given**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`each group` 与 `give` 之间是被动关系，一般过去时。

5. **答案：whether / if**
   - **考点**：连词。`checked whether/if the light bulb would turn on`，引导宾语从句表示"是否"。

6. **答案：surprisingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`Not surprisingly` 作句首状语，修饰整个句子。

7. **答案：are made**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`wires` 与 `make` 之间是被动关系，`be made of` 表示"由……制成"。

8. **答案：theoretical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `knowledge` 需要形容词，`theory` 变为 `theoretical`。

9. **答案：another**
   - **考点**：代词。`it is one thing...but it is quite another` 为固定句型，表示"……是一回事，……则是另一回事"。

10. **答案：society**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`benefit society`，动词后接名词作宾语。
