# 06-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我的奶奶 (My Grandmother)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My grandmother is a seventy-year-old woman (1) ______ lives in a small village. She never went to school, but she is one of the (2) ______ (wise) people I know. Every summer, I spend two weeks (3) ______ (stay) with her. She gets up before dawn and works in the garden, where she (4) ______ (grow) vegetables for more than forty years. I once asked her why she never felt (5) ______ (tire) of farm work. She smiled and told me (6) ______ the land was like an old friend — it always gave back if you treated it well. In the evenings, she teaches me to make dumplings. Her hands move so (7) ______ (quick) that I can hardly follow. Although I have tried many (8) ______ (time), my dumplings still do not look as good as (9) ______ (she). I love my grandmother deeply. She shows me that happiness does not come from money (10) ______ from a peaceful heart.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `woman`（人），定语从句中缺主语，用 `who` 引导。

2. **答案：wisest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`wise` 的最高级为 `wisest`。

3. **答案：staying**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"花时间做某事"。

4. **答案：has grown**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`for more than forty years` 表示从过去持续到现在的动作，用现在完成时；主语 `she` 为第三人称单数，用 `has grown`。

5. **答案：tired**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel tired of` 表示"对……感到厌倦"，`tired` 为形容词，描述人的感受。

6. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`told me that...` 中 `that` 引导宾语从句，作 `told` 的直接宾语。

7. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so...that...` 结构中修饰动词 `move` 需要副词；`quick` 变为 `quickly`。

8. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many` 修饰可数名词复数，`time` 作"次数"讲时为可数名词，复数为 `times`。

9. **答案：hers**
   - **考点**：名词性物主代词。`as good as hers` 中 `hers` = `her dumplings`，避免重复，用名词性物主代词。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：连词。`not...but...` 表示"不是……而是……"，构成固定搭配。
