# 06-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：网络学习的利与弊 (Online Learning: Pros and Cons)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Online learning has become a common part of student life since 2020. With just a computer and the Internet, students can attend classes without (1) ______ (leave) home. Many schools (2) ______ (develop) their own learning platforms so far, and thousands of free courses are now available online. One clear advantage is that students are able to watch lessons again if they do not understand (3) ______ the first time. It also saves the time (4) ______ is usually spent on travelling to school. However, online learning has its problems (5) ______ well. Some students find it hard (6) ______ (concentrate) when they study alone at home. Without a teacher standing nearby, it is easy for them (7) ______ (get) distracted by games or social media. Besides, looking at a screen for too long may be (8) ______ (harm) to their eyes. In my opinion, the best way is to combine online and offline learning. If we use technology (9) ______ (wise), it can become a (10) ______ (power) tool for education.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：leaving**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后接动名词形式，`leave` 变为 `leaving`。

2. **答案：have developed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`so far`（到目前为止）提示用现在完成时；主语 `schools` 为复数，用 `have developed`。

3. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。`at the first time` 或习惯用法 `the first time` 前常省略 `at`，但此处语境为"第一次听课时不懂"，`at` 与 `the first time` 搭配表示"在第一次时"。

4. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `time`（时间），在定语从句中作主语，可用 `that` 或 `which`。

5. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`as well` 为固定短语，表示"也，同样"。

6. **答案：to concentrate**
   - **考点**：不定式。`find it hard to do sth` 为固定句型，`it` 为形式宾语，`to concentrate` 为真正宾语。

7. **答案：to get**
   - **考点**：不定式。`it is easy for sb to do sth` 为固定句型，`it` 为形式主语，`to get` 为真正主语。

8. **答案：harmful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be harmful to` 表示"对……有害"，`harm` 变为形容词 `harmful`。

9. **答案：wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `use` 需要副词，`wise` 变为 `wisely`。

10. **答案：powerful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `tool` 需要形容词，`power` 变为 `powerful`（强大的）。
