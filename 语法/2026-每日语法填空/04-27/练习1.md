# 04-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：社交媒体 (Social Media)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Social media platforms like WeChat, Douyin and Weibo have changed the way we communicate. They allow us to share our thoughts, photos and videos 1. ________ people all over the world.

For teenagers, social media can be a useful tool. It makes it easy 2. ________ (keep) in touch with friends and learn about current events. Many students also use it to follow educational accounts that share 3. ________ (interest) knowledge and study tips.

However, social media also has a dark side. One serious problem is cyberbullying. Some people hide 4. ________ (they) behind anonymous accounts and post hurtful comments. This can cause great 5. ________ (harm) to the victims' mental health.

Another problem is the pressure to look "perfect" online. Many users only share the 6. ________ (good) parts of their lives, which makes others feel 7. ________ (satisfy) with their own lives. This feeling of comparison can lead to low self-esteem and even depression.

To use social media 8. ________ (health), here are some tips. First, limit your daily screen time. Second, be kind in your comments — remember that there is a real person behind every 9. ________. Third, don't believe everything you see online. Many photos and stories are carefully edited and don't show the full truth.

Social media is a powerful tool, but we must use it 10. ________ (wise). Don't let it control your life — let it serve you instead.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。share...with... 意为"与……分享"。

2. **to keep**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。makes it easy to do sth.，it 为形式宾语。

3. **interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 knowledge，修饰事物用 -ing 形式。

4. **themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语是 some people，hide themselves 意为"隐藏自己"。

5. **harm**
   - **考点**：名词（无需变形）。great harm 意为"巨大的伤害"，harm 为不可数名词。注意提示词为 harm 的形容词形式 harmful 需转回名词。

6. **best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级（不规则变化）。the best parts 意为"最好的部分"，good → best。

7. **unsatisfied/dissatisfied**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）+ 前缀。根据语境"对自己的生活不满意"，satisfy → satisfied → unsatisfied/dissatisfied。

8. **healthily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 use，health → healthy → healthily。

9. **account**
   - **考点**：名词（无提示词填空）。every account 意为"每个账号"，对应前文提到的 accounts。

10. **wisely**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 use，wise → wisely。
