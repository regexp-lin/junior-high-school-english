# 04-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：登山探险 (Mountain Climbing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mountain climbing is a challenging but rewarding outdoor activity. It tests your physical strength, mental toughness and determination. For many people, 1. ________ (reach) the top of a mountain is a life-changing experience.

Last summer, my father took me to climb Mount Huangshan, one of the most 2. ________ (fame) mountains in China. We started early in the morning 3. ________ the sun had not yet risen. The air was fresh and cool, and the trail was surrounded by tall pine trees.

The first two hours were quite easy. We walked at a comfortable pace and enjoyed the 4. ________ (scene) along the way. But as we climbed 5. ________ (high), the path became steeper and narrower. My legs started to ache, and I wanted to stop.

"Don't look up at how far you still have to go," my father said. "Just focus 6. ________ the next step." His words gave me the courage to keep going. Step 7. ________ step, we made our way up the mountain.

When we finally reached the peak, the view was 8. ________ (absolute) breathtaking. We could see clouds floating below us and distant mountains stretching to the horizon. All the tiredness disappeared in 9. ________ instant. I felt proud of 10. ________ (I) for not giving up.

That climb taught me that the greatest rewards in life come from the hardest journeys.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **reaching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。reaching the top 在句中作主语。

2. **famous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。the most famous 构成最高级，fame → famous。

3. **when**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。when the sun had not yet risen 引导时间状语从句。

4. **scenery**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→名词）。the scenery 意为"风景"，scene → scenery（不可数名词，指自然风景）。

5. **higher**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。as we climbed higher 意为"当我们爬得更高时"。

6. **on**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。focus on 意为"专注于"。

7. **by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。step by step 意为"一步一步地"，是固定搭配。

8. **absolutely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 breathtaking，需用副词形式 absolutely。

9. **an**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。in an instant 意为"一瞬间"，instant 以元音音素开头。

10. **myself**
    - **考点**：反身代词。主语是 I，be proud of myself 意为"为自己感到骄傲"。
