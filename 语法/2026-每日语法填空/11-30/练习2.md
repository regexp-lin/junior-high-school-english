# 11-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国古代发明 (Ancient Chinese Inventions)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China is known for its "Four Great Inventions" — papermaking, printing, gunpowder and the compass. These inventions had a profound influence (1) ______ the development of human civilisation. Paper was invented during the Han Dynasty by a man (2) ______ (call) Cai Lun. Before paper was invented, people wrote on bamboo strips and silk, which were either too heavy (3) ______ too expensive. Cai Lun used tree bark, old cloth and fishing nets to create a (4) ______ (light) and cheaper writing material. His invention made it possible (5) ______ (spread) knowledge more widely. Printing was another ground-breaking invention. The world's (6) ______ (early) known printed book, the Diamond Sutra, was produced in China in 868 AD. Later, Bi Sheng invented movable type printing, which was far more (7) ______ (efficiency) than carving entire pages. This technology eventually spread to Europe and (8) ______ (revolution) the way information was shared. Gunpowder was (9) ______ (original) discovered by Chinese alchemists who were trying to find a potion for immortality. Instead, they accidentally created one of the most powerful substances in history. The compass, first used for navigation during the Song Dynasty, allowed sailors (10) ______ (explore) distant oceans with much greater confidence.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`have an influence on` 表示"对……有影响"。

2. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a man called Cai Lun`，被动关系。

3. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`either too heavy or too expensive`，`either...or...` 表示"要么……要么……"。

4. **答案：lighter**
   - **考点**：比较级。与 `cheaper` 并列，表示"更轻更便宜的"。

5. **答案：to spread**
   - **考点**：不定式。`make it possible to do sth`。

6. **答案：earliest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the earliest known printed book`。

7. **答案：efficient**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more efficient than`，`efficiency` 变为形容词 `efficient`。

8. **答案：revolutionised**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与前文 `spread` 并列，叙述历史事件。

9. **答案：originally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `discovered` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：to explore**
    - **考点**：不定式。`allow sb to do sth` 表示"允许某人做某事"。
