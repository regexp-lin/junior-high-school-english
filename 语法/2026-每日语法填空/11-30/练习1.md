# 11-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：马拉松 (Running a Marathon)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Running a marathon is one of the most challenging physical achievements a person can accomplish. A full marathon covers a distance of 42.195 kilometres, which requires months of (1) ______ (train) and enormous determination. My older sister, Li Na, completed her first marathon last spring. She had always been interested (2) ______ running, but the idea of running such a long distance seemed impossible to her at first. She began training six months before the race. Every morning, she woke up at 5:30 and ran for at (3) ______ (little) one hour. As the weeks went by, she (4) ______ (gradual) increased her distance from 5 kilometres to 10, then to 20 and finally to 30. The training was not easy. She often came home with sore muscles and (5) ______ (bleed) blisters on her feet. There were times when she wanted to quit, but our family kept (6) ______ (encourage) her. On the day of the marathon, I went to cheer for her along the route. When I saw her (7) ______ (run) past the 35-kilometre mark, she looked exhausted but determined. The last few kilometres were the (8) ______ (tough) of all. Her legs were shaking, and every step seemed to require all her remaining energy. But she never stopped. When she finally crossed the finish line, tears streamed (9) ______ her face. Her finishing time was 4 hours and 23 minutes — not the fastest, but it was (10) ______ achievement that made our whole family incredibly proud.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：training**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词/名词。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be interested in` 表示"对……感兴趣"。

3. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：最高级。`at least` 表示"至少"。

4. **答案：gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `increased` 需要副词形式。

5. **答案：bleeding**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`bleeding blisters` 表示"流血的水泡"。

6. **答案：encouraging**
   - **考点**：动名词。`keep doing sth` 表示"持续做某事"。

7. **答案：running**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`saw her running`，感官动词后接现在分词表正在进行。

8. **答案：toughest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the toughest of all` 表示"所有中最难的"。

9. **答案：down**
   - **考点**：副词。`tears streamed down her face` 表示"眼泪流下面颊"。

10. **答案：an**
    - **考点**：冠词。`an achievement`，`achievement` 以元音音素开头。
