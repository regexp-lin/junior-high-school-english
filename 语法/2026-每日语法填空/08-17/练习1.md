# 08-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：节日灯笼 (Festival Lanterns)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Lanterns have been part of Chinese culture for over two thousand years. They were (1) ______ (original) used as light sources, but over time they became symbols of celebration and good fortune. The most important occasion for lanterns is the Lantern Festival, (2) ______ falls on the fifteenth day of the first lunar month. On that night, streets and parks are decorated with (3) ______ (colour) lanterns of all shapes and sizes. My hometown holds a grand lantern fair every year. Last year, my family went to see it, and I was blown away by the (4) ______ (beautiful) of the displays. There were dragon lanterns, lotus lanterns, and even a huge lantern shaped (5) ______ a peacock. Children ran around (6) ______ (carry) small rabbit lanterns, and couples walked hand (7) ______ hand, admiring the lights. My favourite was a lantern that (8) ______ (make) entirely of recycled bottles. It glowed in soft blue light and was (9) ______ creative that the artist won first prize. Making traditional lanterns is a craft that requires great skill and patience. My grandfather once showed me how (10) ______ (build) a simple paper lantern. It took us an entire afternoon, but the result was beautiful.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：originally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `used` 需要副词，`original` 变为 `originally`。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `the Lantern Festival`，在从句中作主语。

3. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `lanterns` 需要形容词，`colour` 变为 `colourful`。

4. **答案：beauty**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`beautiful` 变为 `beauty`。

5. **答案：like**
   - **考点**：介词。`shaped like` 表示"形状像……"。

6. **答案：carrying**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。孩子们跑来跑去的同时"提着"小兔子灯。

7. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`hand in hand` 为固定搭配，表示"手牵手"。

8. **答案：was made**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。灯笼"被制作"，叙述过去的展品。

9. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：副词。`so creative that...` 为 `so...that...` 结构。

10. **答案：to build**
    - **考点**：不定式。`how to do sth` 为固定搭配。
