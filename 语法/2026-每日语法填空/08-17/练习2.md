# 08-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：时间管理 (Time Management)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Time management is a skill that every student needs but few actually master. I used to be someone (1) ______ left everything until the last minute. The night before a deadline, I would stay up late, rushing to finish (2) ______ (assign) that should have been done days earlier. As a result, my work was often careless, and I felt (3) ______ (exhaust) the next day. Everything changed when my form teacher introduced us (4) ______ a method called the "Pomodoro Technique." The idea is simple: work for 25 minutes, then take a five-minute break. After four rounds, take a (5) ______ (long) break of fifteen minutes. The technique works because it breaks big tasks (6) ______ small, manageable chunks. I started using it last month and found that I could get much more (7) ______ (do) in less time. Another useful tip is to write a to-do list every morning. Seeing all my tasks written down makes them feel less (8) ______ (overwhelm). I also learned to say no to (9) ______ (necessary) distractions, like checking social media every few minutes. Now, I finish my homework by eight o'clock most evenings, (10) ______ (leave) me plenty of time to read or relax.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `someone`（人），在定语从句中作主语。

2. **答案：assignments**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`finish assignments` 中 `assign` 变为 `assignment`（作业），用复数。

3. **答案：exhausted**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt` 为系动词，后接形容词，`exhaust` 变为 `exhausted`（疲惫的）。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`introduce sb to sth` 为固定搭配，表示"向某人介绍某事"。

5. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。与之前的五分钟休息对比，用 `longer` 表示"更长的"。

6. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`break...into...` 表示"把……分成……"。

7. **答案：done**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`get more done` 表示"完成更多事情"，`get sth done` 结构。

8. **答案：overwhelming**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel less overwhelming` 中 `overwhelm` 变为 `overwhelming`（压倒性的），描述事物给人的感受。

9. **答案：unnecessary**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 前缀。修饰名词 `distractions`，根据语境应加 `un-` 前缀，得 `unnecessary`（不必要的）。

10. **答案：leaving**
    - **考点**：现在分词作结果状语。表示自然结果——完成作业后"剩下"充足时间。
