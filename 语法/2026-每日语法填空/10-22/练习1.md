# 10-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：雨天的乐趣 (Fun on a Rainy Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Most people dislike rainy days, but I have always found them (1) ______ (enjoy). There is something peaceful about the sound of rain falling on the roof. Last Saturday, it rained heavily all day, and I stayed at home with nothing planned. Instead of feeling bored, I decided (2) ______ (make) the most of the day. In the morning, I curled up on the sofa with a cup of hot cocoa and a good book. The rain outside created a (3) ______ (relax) atmosphere that made me want to read for hours. By noon, I (4) ______ (finish) half the book. In the afternoon, I went into the kitchen and tried baking cookies for the (5) ______ (one) time. I followed a recipe online, but I accidentally added too (6) ______ sugar, so the cookies turned out sweeter than they should have been. Still, my family said they were (7) ______ (delight) and ate them all within an hour! After dinner, the rain (8) ______ (become) lighter. I put on my raincoat and went for a short walk. The streets were empty and quiet. The air smelled fresh, and the wet leaves on the trees looked (9) ______ (particular) green. I splashed through puddles like a child, feeling completely free and happy. When I got home, Mum laughed and said I was as wet (10) ______ a drowned rat. But I didn't care — it had been a perfect day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：enjoyable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found them enjoyable`，`find + 宾语 + 形容词`。

2. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：不定式。`decide to do sth`。

3. **答案：relaxing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `atmosphere` 用 `-ing` 形式形容词。

4. **答案：had finished**
   - **考点**：时态。`by noon` 表示到中午为止，用过去完成时。

5. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。`for the first time`，`one` 变为 `first`。

6. **答案：much**
   - **考点**：副词。`too much sugar`，`sugar` 为不可数名词，用 `much`。

7. **答案：delightful / delicious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`were delightful/delicious`，系动词后接形容词。

8. **答案：became**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

9. **答案：particularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `green` 需要副词。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`as wet as a drowned rat` 为习语比喻"湿透了"。
