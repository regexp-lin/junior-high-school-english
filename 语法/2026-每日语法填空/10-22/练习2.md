# 10-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：助人为乐 (Helping Others Brings Happiness)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

There is an old saying that (1) ______ (help) others is the source of happiness. I used to think this was just empty words, but an experience last winter changed my view (2) ______ (complete). One cold morning, I was walking to school when I noticed an old man (3) ______ (sit) on a bench by the road. He looked confused and kept asking passers-by for (4) ______ (direct) to the hospital. Most people were in a hurry and walked past without stopping. I went up to him and asked (5) ______ I could help. He told me he needed to visit his wife in the hospital but couldn't find the way. The hospital was about two kilometres away, and it was too far for him (6) ______ (walk) alone. I called my teacher to explain the situation, and she agreed to let me be late. I then called a taxi and accompanied the old man to the hospital. When we arrived, his wife was (7) ______ (wait) for him anxiously. Seeing her husband safe, she thanked me with tears in her eyes. "You are a good boy," she said, (8) ______ (hold) my hands tightly. Walking back to school, I felt (9) ______ (warm) inside than I had ever felt before. That day, I truly understood what the saying means. Happiness doesn't always come from receiving — sometimes, the greatest happiness comes from giving to (10) ______.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：helping**
   - **考点**：动名词。动名词 `helping` 作主语。

2. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `changed` 需要副词。

3. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`noticed an old man sitting`，感官动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词。

4. **答案：directions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`ask for directions` 表示"问路"。

5. **答案：if / whether**
   - **考点**：连词。`asked if/whether I could help`。

6. **答案：to walk**
   - **考点**：不定式。`too far for him to walk`，`too...for sb to do`。

7. **答案：waiting**
   - **考点**：时态。`was waiting` 用过去进行时，表示"一直在等"。

8. **答案：holding**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`holding my hands tightly` 作伴随状语。

9. **答案：warmer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`warmer inside than I had ever felt`。

10. **答案：others**
    - **考点**：代词。`giving to others` 表示"给予他人"。
