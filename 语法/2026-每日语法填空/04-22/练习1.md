# 04-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界地球日 (Earth Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

April 22nd is Earth Day, a day 1. ________ people around the world come together to protect our planet. Since it was first celebrated in 1970, Earth Day 2. ________ (become) one of the largest environmental events in the world.

Every year on Earth Day, millions of people take part in 3. ________ (activity) to help the environment. Some people plant trees, while others organize beach clean-ups or recycling drives. Schools also hold special events 4. ________ (educate) students about environmental protection.

This year, our school organized an "Earth Day Challenge." Each class was asked to come up with a plan to reduce waste. My class decided to start a "zero-waste lunch" campaign. We encouraged everyone 5. ________ (bring) reusable containers and water bottles instead of 6. ________ (use) disposable ones.

The results were 7. ________ (surprise) good. In just one week, our class reduced lunch waste 8. ________ nearly 60%. Other classes were impressed and started their own campaigns too.

Earth Day reminds us that protecting the environment is not just a one-day event. It is something we should think about every single day. Small actions, when 9. ________ (do) by millions of people, can lead to big 10. ________ (change).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **when**
   - **考点**：关系副词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 a day，在从句中作时间状语。

2. **has become**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。since 1970 提示用现在完成时。

3. **activities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。take part in activities 意为"参加活动"，activity → activities。

4. **to educate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。表示"为了教育学生"。

5. **to bring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。encourage sb. to do sth. 意为"鼓励某人做某事"。

6. **using**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。instead of 后接动名词形式。

7. **surprisingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 good，需用副词形式 surprisingly。

8. **by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。by nearly 60% 意为"减少了近60%"，by 表示变化的幅度。

9. **done**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。when done by = when (they are) done by，过去分词作状语，省略了 they are。

10. **changes**
    - **考点**：名词复数。big changes 意为"巨大的改变"，change 用复数。
