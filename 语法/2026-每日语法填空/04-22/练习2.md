# 04-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：手机使用与青少年 (Mobile Phone Use Among Teenagers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mobile phones have become an important part of our lives. For teenagers, they are not just tools for communication — they are also used for 1. ________ (learn), entertainment and socializing.

However, spending too much time on phones can cause problems. Many students check their phones first thing in the morning and last thing 2. ________ night. They scroll through social media during meals and even 3. ________ (secret) use phones in class. This takes away time that should be spent on studying or sleeping.

Phone addiction is 4. ________ (become) a serious issue among teenagers. Studies show that teenagers who use phones for more than four hours a day are more likely to feel 5. ________ (anxiety) and depressed. The blue light from screens also makes it 6. ________ (hard) to fall asleep at night.

So how can we use our phones more 7. ________ (responsible)? First, set a daily time limit. Many phones now have built-in tools that track your screen time and remind you 8. ________ you've been using it too long. Second, keep your phone out of the bedroom at night. This simple change can 9. ________ (great) improve your sleep quality.

Parents also play an important role. Instead of simply taking 10. ________ their children's phones, they should help them develop healthy phone habits through communication and trust.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **learning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 for 后接动名词形式。

2. **at**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。at night 意为"在晚上"。

3. **secretly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 use，需用副词形式 secretly。

4. **becoming**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词/现在进行时）。is becoming 构成现在进行时，意为"正在变成"。

5. **anxious**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。feel anxious 意为"感到焦虑"，anxiety → anxious。

6. **harder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。makes it harder to... 意为"使得更难……"。

7. **responsibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 use，需用副词形式 responsibly。

8. **when/if**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。remind you when/if 意为"当……时提醒你"。

9. **greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 improve，需用副词形式 greatly。

10. **away**
    - **考点**：副词/动词短语（无提示词填空）。take away 意为"拿走"。
