# 11-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：团队运动 (Team Sports)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Team sports play an important role in a teenager's growth. They teach us not only physical skills but also (1) ______ (value) life lessons such as cooperation, discipline and respect. Our school basketball team has been one of the (2) ______ (strong) in the district for several years. This year, we entered the city-level championship for the first time. The competition was much (3) ______ (tough) than anything we had experienced before. In the semi-final, we played (4) ______ a team that had won the championship three times in a row. They were taller and more experienced than us. During the first half, we fell behind by twelve points, and some of my teammates looked (5) ______ (disappoint). However, our coach told us not to give up. "The game (6) ______ (not finish) yet," he said. "Play together and trust each other." His words (7) ______ (light) a fire in our hearts. In the second half, we changed our strategy and began to play more (8) ______ (aggressive). We narrowed the gap point by point and (9) ______ (final) tied the score with just thirty seconds left. Although we lost in overtime, every player felt proud because we had given our best. This experience taught me that winning is not everything — (10) ______ truly matters is the spirit of never giving up.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `life lessons` 需要形容词。

2. **答案：strongest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the strongest`，"最……之一"用最高级。

3. **答案：tougher**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than anything we had experienced` 提示比较级。

4. **答案：against**
   - **考点**：介词。`play against` 表示"与……对阵"。

5. **答案：disappointed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`looked disappointed`，描述人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

6. **答案：isn't finished / hasn't finished**
   - **考点**：时态/被动语态。教练在比赛进行中说"比赛还没结束"。

7. **答案：lit / lighted**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`light` 的过去式为 `lit` 或 `lighted`。

8. **答案：aggressively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `play` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `tied` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：what**
    - **考点**：连接代词。`what truly matters` 为主语从句，`what` 在从句中作主语。
