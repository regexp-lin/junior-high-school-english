# 11-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节约用水 (Saving Water)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Water is essential for all living things, yet many people take it for (1) ______ (grant). Although about 70% of the Earth's surface is covered (2) ______ water, only a tiny fraction of it is fresh water that can be used by humans. In many parts of the world, people struggle to get clean (3) ______ (drink) water every day. China is also facing a water shortage. The water resources per person in China are only about a quarter of the world average. Some northern cities are among the most water-stressed (4) ______ (region) in the country. Last month, our school invited a water engineer (5) ______ (give) us a lecture on water conservation. She explained how much water (6) ______ (waste) in our daily lives without us even noticing. For example, a dripping tap can waste up to 20 litres of water a day. She encouraged us to develop good habits, such as turning (7) ______ the tap while brushing our teeth and taking shorter showers. After the lecture, our class decided to launch a "Save Every Drop" campaign. We put up posters around the school and (8) ______ (remind) our schoolmates to use water carefully. We also suggested (9) ______ (collect) rainwater for watering the school garden. These small actions may seem unimportant, but if everyone does their part, they will make a (10) ______ (significance) difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：granted**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`take...for granted` 表示"认为……理所当然"。

2. **答案：with / by**
   - **考点**：介词。`be covered with/by water` 表示"被水覆盖"。

3. **答案：drinking**
   - **考点**：动名词/现在分词作定语。`drinking water` 表示"饮用水"。

4. **答案：regions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the most water-stressed regions`，需要复数形式。

5. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：不定式。`invite sb to do sth` 表示"邀请某人做某事"。

6. **答案：is wasted**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`how much water is wasted`，水与浪费之间是被动关系。

7. **答案：off**
   - **考点**：副词。`turn off the tap` 表示"关掉水龙头"。

8. **答案：reminded**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与前文 `put up` 并列，叙述过去的动作。

9. **答案：collecting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`suggest doing sth` 表示"建议做某事"。

10. **答案：significant**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `difference` 需要形容词。
