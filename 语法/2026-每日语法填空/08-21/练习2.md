# 08-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：二十四节气 (The 24 Solar Terms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The 24 Solar Terms are a traditional Chinese calendar system (1) ______ (create) by ancient farmers. They divide the year into 24 periods based on the sun's position in the sky. Each term lasts about fifteen days and reflects changes in (2) ______ (nature), such as temperature, rainfall, and the growth of plants. For example, "Start of Spring" marks the (3) ______ (begin) of the farming season, while "Great Snow" warns of heavy snowfall. This system was so (4) ______ (science) accurate that farmers relied on it for thousands of years to decide when (5) ______ (plant) and harvest crops. In 2016, the 24 Solar Terms (6) ______ (add) to UNESCO's list of Intangible Cultural Heritage, (7) ______ brought worldwide attention to this ancient wisdom. Even today, the solar terms influence Chinese culture in many ways. People eat special foods (8) ______ certain terms — for instance, dumplings during "Start of Winter." My grandmother always checks the solar terms before (9) ______ (decide) what to plant in her garden. She says modern weather forecasts are useful, but the old calendar is still the (10) ______ (reliable) guide for gardening.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：created**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a system created by ancient farmers` 表示"由古代农民创建的体系"。

2. **答案：nature**
   - **考点**：名词。`changes in nature` 表示"自然中的变化"。注意此处 `nature` 本身即为名词，不需转换。

3. **答案：beginning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`begin` 变为 `beginning`。

4. **答案：scientifically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `accurate` 需要副词，`science` 变为 `scientifically`。

5. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：不定式。`when to plant` 为"疑问词 + 不定式"结构。

6. **答案：were added**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。二十四节气"被加入"联合国教科文组织名录，`In 2016` 表示过去。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代整件事。

8. **答案：during / on**
   - **考点**：介词。`during certain terms` 表示"在某些节气期间"。

9. **答案：deciding**
   - **考点**：动名词。`before` 为介词，后接动名词。

10. **答案：most reliable**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the most reliable guide`，`reliable` 为多音节形容词。
