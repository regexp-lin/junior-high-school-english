# 08-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：街头艺人 (Street Performers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

On a recent trip to Chengdu, I came across some amazing street performers near the Jinli Ancient Street. A young man was playing the guitar and (1) ______ (sing) folk songs with a deep, warm voice. A small crowd had gathered around him, (2) ______ (listen) quietly. Some people dropped coins into his open guitar case, while others simply stood and enjoyed the (3) ______ (perform). What impressed me most was a woman (4) ______ painted portraits of passers-by. She could capture a person's likeness in just ten (5) ______ (minute). I asked her to draw my face, and the result was (6) ______ (surprise) accurate. She told me that she had been painting on the street (7) ______ over five years. It was not an easy life, but she loved the (8) ______ (free) of being her own boss. Street performers add colour and energy to a city. They make public spaces feel more (9) ______ (live) and interesting. However, they often do not receive the recognition they deserve. I think cities should provide more (10) ______ (support) for street artists, because they bring joy to people who might otherwise just rush past.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：singing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。与 `playing` 并列，构成过去进行时 `was playing and singing`。

2. **答案：listening**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。人群聚集并"静静地听"。

3. **答案：performance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`perform` 变为 `performance`。

4. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a woman`（人），在定语从句中作主语。

5. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`ten` 修饰可数名词复数。

6. **答案：surprisingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `accurate` 需要副词，`surprise` 变为 `surprisingly`。

7. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for over five years` 表示"超过五年"。

8. **答案：freedom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`free` 变为 `freedom`。

9. **答案：lively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel more lively` 中 `live` 变为 `lively`（有活力的）。

10. **答案：support**
    - **考点**：名词。`more support` 中 `support` 为不可数名词，`supportive` 是形容词，但此处需要名词修饰。
