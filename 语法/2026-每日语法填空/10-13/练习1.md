# 10-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：盆栽植物 (Growing Potted Plants)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Growing potted plants has become a popular hobby among people who live in cities (1) ______ have no gardens. Even a small balcony or windowsill can be turned (2) ______ a tiny green space. My mother started growing potted plants two years ago, and now our balcony is full of (3) ______ (live) greenery. She grows herbs like mint and basil, (4) ______ she uses in her cooking. She also has several flowering plants that bloom at different times of the year, so there is always something (5) ______ (colour) to look at. I became interested in plants after watching Mum take care of (6) ______ (her). She taught me that each plant has different needs — some prefer direct sunlight (7) ______ others grow better in the shade. Watering is also tricky. Too much water can be just as (8) ______ (harm) as too little. Last month, I bought a small cactus for my desk. It is easy (9) ______ (look) after because it only needs water once a week. Studies have shown that having plants indoors can improve air quality and reduce stress. I've noticed that I feel (10) ______ (calm) and more focused when I study near my plants. I would recommend this hobby to anyone.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：and / but**
   - **考点**：连词。`people who live in cities and/but have no gardens`。

2. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`be turned into` 表示"被变成"。

3. **答案：lively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `greenery` 用形容词，`live` 变为 `lively`（充满生机的）。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，指代 `herbs`。

5. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`something colourful`，不定代词后接形容词作后置定语。

6. **答案：hers**
   - **考点**：名词性物主代词。`take care of hers`，`hers` 代替 `her plants`。

7. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。`some prefer sunlight while others grow in shade`，`while` 表示对比。

8. **答案：harmful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be as harmful as`，`harm` 变为形容词 `harmful`。

9. **答案：to look**
   - **考点**：不定式。`easy to look after`，`be + adj + to do` 结构。

10. **答案：calmer**
    - **考点**：比较级。`feel calmer and more focused`。
