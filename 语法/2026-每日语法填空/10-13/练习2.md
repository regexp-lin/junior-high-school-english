# 10-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：数学竞赛 (Maths Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Maths has always been my favourite subject. Last month, our maths teacher selected five students from our class (1) ______ (represent) our school in the city maths competition. I was honoured to be one of (2) ______. To prepare, we studied together every afternoon for three weeks. Our teacher gave us (3) ______ (challenge) problems that were much harder than what we usually did in class. Some problems were so complex that it took us hours (4) ______ (figure) them out. But with each problem we solved, our confidence grew (5) ______ (strong). On the day of the competition, I was (6) ______ (surprise) calm. The test lasted two hours and included 20 questions. The first ten were (7) ______ (relative) easy, but the last five were extremely difficult. I spent nearly 30 minutes on a single problem about probability (8) ______ finally worked out the answer. When the results (9) ______ (announce) a week later, I was thrilled to learn that I had won a silver medal. Our team also ranked third (10) ______ all the schools in the city. The competition showed me that hard work and teamwork can lead to great achievements.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to represent**
   - **考点**：不定式。`selected five students to represent...`，不定式表目的。

2. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`one of them`，介词 `of` 后接宾格。

3. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `problems` 用 `-ing` 形式形容词。

4. **答案：to figure**
   - **考点**：不定式。`it took us hours to figure them out`。

5. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`grew stronger`，系动词后接比较级。

6. **答案：surprisingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `calm` 需要副词。

7. **答案：relatively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `easy` 需要副词，`relative` 变为 `relatively`。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`spent 30 minutes...and finally worked out...`，`and` 连接两个并列动作。

9. **答案：were announced**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`results were announced`，结果"被公布"。

10. **答案：among**
    - **考点**：介词。`ranked third among all the schools`，`among` 表示"在……之中"。
