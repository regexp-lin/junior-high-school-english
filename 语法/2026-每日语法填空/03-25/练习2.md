# 03-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：梦想与目标 (Dreams and Goals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Everyone has a dream. Some people want to become doctors, while 1. ________ want to be artists or engineers. No matter what your dream is, it is important to work hard to make it come true.

My dream is to become a wildlife photographer. I have been interested 2. ________ animals since I was a little child. I love watching documentaries about wildlife and I always feel 3. ________ (amaze) by the beauty of nature.

Last year, my father gave me a camera as a birthday gift. I was so 4. ________ (excite) that I started taking photos of everything around me. I took photos of birds in the park, flowers in the garden and even insects on the leaves. Although most of my early photos were not very good, I kept 5. ________ (practice).

My teacher told me that 6. ________ (reach) a goal requires both passion and patience. She shared a story about a famous photographer 7. ________ spent ten years in Africa just to take the perfect photo of a lion. His 8. ________ (determine) deeply inspired me.

I know the road to my dream won't be easy. There will be many 9. ________ (difficult) along the way. But I believe that as long as I don't give up, I will 10. ________ (eventual) achieve my goal.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **others**
   - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。some...others... 意为"一些人……另一些人……"，是固定搭配。

2. **in**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be interested in 意为"对……感兴趣"，是固定搭配。

3. **amazed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。feel amazed 意为"感到惊叹"，主语是人，用 -ed 形式。

4. **excited**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。so excited 表示"如此兴奋的"，修饰人的情感用 -ed 形式。

5. **practicing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。keep doing sth. 意为"持续做某事"。

6. **reaching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词短语 reaching a goal 在 that 从句中作主语。

7. **who/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 photographer，先行词指人，在从句中作主语。

8. **determination**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。his 后接名词，determine → determination。

9. **difficulties**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）+ 复数。many 后接可数名词复数，difficult → difficulty → difficulties。

10. **eventually**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 achieve，需用副词形式 eventually。
