# 03-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园生活 (School Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Middle school life is busy but colorful. Every day, we have different classes and activities that keep us 1. ________ (energy) and active.

My favorite subject is science. Our science teacher, Mr. Wang, is one of the 2. ________ (kind) teachers in our school. He never just reads from the textbook. Instead, he likes to do experiments with us so that we can understand things 3. ________ (well) than before.

Besides studying, I also enjoy the after-school clubs. I joined the drama club last September and have been a member 4. ________ then. Every Friday afternoon, we practice acting and 5. ________ (prepare) for the school show. It is great fun, and I have made many new 6. ________ (friend) there.

Of course, school life is not always easy. Sometimes we have too much homework 7. ________ (finish) in one evening. Other times, we feel stressed about the coming exams. But our teachers often tell 8. ________ that hard work will pay off in the end.

I still remember my 9. ________ (one) day at this school. I was nervous and excited at the same time. Now, three years 10. ________ (pass) quickly, and I will soon graduate. I will always treasure these wonderful school memories.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **energetic**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。keep us + 形容词，作宾语补足语，energy → energetic。

2. **kindest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，kind 的最高级为 kindest。

3. **better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（不规则变化）。than 提示用比较级，well 的比较级为 better。

4. **since**
   - **考点**：介词/连词（无提示词填空）。since then 意为"从那时起"，与现在完成时连用。

5. **preparing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。and 连接并列成分，与前面的 acting 并列，用动名词形式 preparing。

6. **friends**
   - **考点**：名词复数。many 修饰可数名词复数，friend → friends。

7. **to finish**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作后置定语）。homework to finish 意为"要完成的作业"，不定式作后置定语修饰 homework。

8. **us**
   - **考点**：代词宾格（无提示词填空）。tell 后接宾语，根据上下文指"我们"，用宾格 us。

9. **first**
   - **考点**：序数词。修饰名词 day，one 的序数词形式为 first，"第一天"。

10. **have passed**
    - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。now 提示与现在有关联，三年已经过去了，用现在完成时 have passed。
