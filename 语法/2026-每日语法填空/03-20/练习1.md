# 03-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：友谊的力量 (The Power of Friendship)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last year, I moved to a new school and felt very 1. ________ (lone). I didn't know anyone, and everything seemed strange to me. During the first week, I just sat 2. ________ myself at lunch and spoke to nobody.

One day, a girl named Lily came over and sat next to me. She 3. ________ (introduce) herself with a warm smile and asked me where I was from. That was the 4. ________ (begin) of our friendship.

Lily helped me a lot in the following months. When I had trouble 5. ________ (understand) math problems, she would explain them to me patiently. When I felt sad, she always knew how 6. ________ (cheer) me up. Thanks to her, I 7. ________ (gradual) got used to the new school life.

Now we are best friends. We share our secrets and support each 8. ________. I have learned that a true friend is someone 9. ________ stands by you no matter what happens.

Looking back, I feel really lucky to have met Lily. She has taught me that friendship is one of the most 10. ________ (value) things in life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **lonely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词辨析）。felt 是系动词，后接形容词作表语。lone 意为"孤单的（强调独自一人）"，lonely 意为"孤独的（强调内心感受）"，根据语境应填 lonely。

2. **by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。by myself 意为"独自地"，是固定搭配。

3. **introduced**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。全文用过去时叙述，introduce 需变为过去式 introduced。

4. **beginning**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the beginning of 意为"……的开端"，定冠词 the 后接名词。

5. **understanding**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。have trouble (in) doing sth. 意为"做某事有困难"，是固定搭配。

6. **to cheer**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。how to do sth. 意为"如何做某事"，不定式作宾语补足语。

7. **gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词短语 got used to，需用副词形式。

8. **other**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。each other 意为"彼此、互相"。

9. **who/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 someone，先行词指人，在从句中作主语，用 who 或 that。

10. **valuable**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词/动词→形容词）。most 后接形容词构成最高级，value → valuable，意为"最有价值的"。
