# 03-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：运动与健康 (Sports and Health)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Exercise plays an important role in keeping us healthy. Doctors suggest that teenagers should do at 1. ________ one hour of exercise every day. However, many students spend most of their time 2. ________ (sit) in the classroom and seldom take part in sports activities.

Running is one of the 3. ________ (easy) ways to exercise. You don't need any special equipment — just a pair of comfortable shoes. If you run for thirty 4. ________ (minute) every day, you will notice great changes in your body. You will feel 5. ________ (strong) and sleep better at night.

Swimming is 6. ________ popular sport among young people. It exercises almost every muscle in your body. Many students learn to swim during the summer vacation, and some of 7. ________ (they) even join the school swimming team.

Team sports like basketball and football are also wonderful 8. ________ (choose). Playing these sports helps you learn how to work with others. You can make new friends and have fun 9. ________ the same time.

No matter what sport you choose, the key is to keep doing it. Don't give 10. ________ easily. Once exercise becomes part of your daily life, you will enjoy it more and more.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **least**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。at least 意为"至少"，是常见固定搭配。

2. **sitting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 意为"花时间做某事"，是固定搭配。

3. **easiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，意为"最……的之一"。

4. **minutes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。thirty 提示为复数概念，minute 需变为复数 minutes。

5. **stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。根据语境"你会感觉更强壮"，暗含比较含义（比之前更强壮），且与后面 better 并列。

6. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。sport 为可数名词单数，此处泛指一项运动，用不定冠词 a。

7. **them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。介词 of 后接代词宾格形式，some of them 意为"他们中的一些人"。

8. **choices**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。are 提示主语为复数，choose 的名词形式为 choice，此处用复数 choices。

9. **at**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。at the same time 意为"同时"，是固定搭配。

10. **up**
    - **考点**：动词短语（无提示词填空）。give up 意为"放弃"，是固定动词短语搭配。
