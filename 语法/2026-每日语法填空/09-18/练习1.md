# 09-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：骑行旅行 (Cycling Trip)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, my father and I went on a cycling trip from Chengdu to a small town in the mountains. It was a three-day journey, and we had to ride about 80 kilometres (1) ______ day. On the first day, the road was flat and easy, so we enjoyed (2) ______ (ride) through fields of sunflowers and past quiet villages. We stopped at a farmhouse for lunch and the owner kindly offered (3) ______ (we) some homemade bread and tea. The second day was much (4) ______ (tough) because we had to climb a steep mountain road. My legs ached so badly that I wanted (5) ______ (give) up several times. But my father kept encouraging me. "One more step, and you'll be (6) ______ (close) to the top," he said. When we (7) ______ (final) reached the mountain pass, the view was breathtaking. Green valleys stretched out below us, and snow-capped peaks stood in the (8) ______ (distant). All the tiredness disappeared in that moment. On the third day, we rode downhill (9) ______ the way to the town. The cool mountain breeze felt wonderful (10) ______ our faces. This trip taught me that great rewards often come after great effort.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a / per**
   - **考点**：冠词/介词。`80 kilometres a/per day` 表示"每天80公里"。

2. **答案：riding**
   - **考点**：动名词。`enjoy doing sth`，`enjoy` 后接动名词。

3. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`offered us some bread`，动词后接间接宾语用宾格。

4. **答案：tougher**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much tougher`，`much` 修饰比较级。

5. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：不定式。`want to do sth` 表示"想要做某事"。

6. **答案：closer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`be closer to the top`，`close` 的比较级为 `closer`。

7. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `reached` 需要副词，`final` 变为 `finally`。

8. **答案：distance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`in the distance` 表示"在远处"，`distant` 变为名词 `distance`。

9. **答案：all**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`all the way` 表示"一路上"。

10. **答案：on / against**
    - **考点**：介词。`felt wonderful on/against our faces` 表示"吹在我们脸上感觉很棒"。
