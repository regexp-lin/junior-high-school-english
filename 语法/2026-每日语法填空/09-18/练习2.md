# 09-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电子书 vs 纸质书 (E-books vs Paper Books)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

With the development of technology, e-books have become (1) ______ (increase) popular. You can store thousands of books on a single device, (2) ______ makes reading very convenient. Many students in my class prefer (3) ______ (read) e-books because they can carry all their textbooks on a tablet without a heavy schoolbag. The screens of modern e-readers are designed (4) ______ (protect) your eyes, so reading for long periods is less tiring. However, paper books still have their (5) ______ (advantage). Some readers say that the feeling of holding a real book and turning its (6) ______ (page) cannot be replaced by any electronic device. My English teacher, Ms. Liu, is a strong supporter (7) ______ paper books. She told us that (8) ______ (study) have shown that people remember information (9) ______ (good) when they read it on paper rather than on a screen. I think both formats have their place. When I travel, I bring my e-reader because it is light and easy (10) ______ (carry). But when I study at home, I prefer paper books because I can highlight important sentences and write notes in the margins.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `popular` 需要副词，`increase` 变为 `increasingly`。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，指代前面整件事。

3. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：动名词。`prefer doing sth` 表示"更喜欢做某事"。

4. **答案：to protect**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be designed to do sth` 表示"被设计来做某事"。

5. **答案：advantages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`their advantages`，有多个优势。

6. **答案：pages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`turning its pages`，一本书有多页。

7. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`a supporter of` 表示"……的支持者"。

8. **答案：studies**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`studies have shown` 表示"多项研究表明"。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`remember information better`，`good/well` 的比较级为 `better`。

10. **答案：to carry**
    - **考点**：不定式。`easy to carry`，`be + adj + to do` 结构。
