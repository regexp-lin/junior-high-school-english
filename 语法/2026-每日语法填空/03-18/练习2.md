# 03-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：阅读的意义与乐趣 (The Importance of Reading)

## 一、 题目
阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In the modern world, video games and short videos take up a lot of our free time. However, reading is still one of the 1. ________ (great) ways to learn about the world. 

Reading opens the door to new worlds. When you read a book, you might travel to a place where you have never 2. ________ (be). You can experience different cultures and understand how other 3. ________ (person) live. Through reading, you can also travel back in time to "communicate" with great thinkers from history.

Besides, reading is good 4. ________ your writing skills. If you read a lot, you will naturally learn how to use words properly and how to express your thoughts 5. ________ (clear). A good reader is usually a good writer.

Many students want to read more, but they don't know what to choose. Actually, the most important thing is 6. ________ (pick) up a book that interests you. It can be a science fiction, a history book, 7. ________ even a funny comic book. Let your interests guide you. If you really don't like a book, don't force 8. ________ (you) to finish it. Just put it away and try another one.

Make reading 9. ________ daily habit. As an old saying goes, "Reading makes a full man." Enjoy every word you read, and you will find you are growing into a 10. ________ (wise) person day by day. 

---

## 二、 答案与解析

1. **greatest**
   - **考点**：形容词的最高级。`one of the + 形容词最高级 + 名词复数`，意为“最伟大的方式之一”。
2. **been**
   - **考点**：词的时态与形式（现在完成时）。`have been to/have been` 常配合 `where` 引导的定语从句，表达“去过某地”的经历。
3. **people** （或 *persons*）
   - **考点**：名词的复数。表示其他不同的人如何生活，`person` 的复数形式通常是 `people`。
4. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。`be good for...` 是固定搭配，意为“对……有益处”。此处指阅读对写作技能有益。
5. **clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词变副词）。修饰前面的动词 `express`，需要用形容词 `clear` 的副词形式 `clearly`。
6. **to pick**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动词不定式作表语）。the most important thing is to do sth. 常常用来解释说明一件最重要的事情是什么。
7. **or**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。此处列举了几类书籍供选择，使用 `or` 表示选择关系（或者）。
8. **yourself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。句子的隐含主语是 `you`（因为是祈使句），这里的宾语指代主语自身，意思是“不要强迫你自己”，应填反身代词 `yourself`。
9. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。`habit` （习惯）是可数名词单数，泛指“一个”日常习惯用不定冠词 `a`。
10. **wiser**
   - **考点**：形容词的比较级。根据句意 `day by day`（一天天地），这里包含“比过去变得更聪明”的渐进含义，应用比较级。
