# 03-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：青少年合理的时间管理 (Time Management)

## 一、 题目
阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Everyone knows that time is valuable. But teenagers often feel they never have enough time 1. ________ (do) all the things they want. To make the most of your time, here is some useful 2. ________ (advise).

First, start by making a list. Write down everything you need to do, 3. ________ (special) the important tasks. This will help you see clearly what must be done. 

Second, learn to say "no". Sometimes we agree to do things just because we don't want to make others unhappy. However, if you are too busy, it's 4. ________ (accept) to tell your friends politely that you can't join 5. ________ (they). 

Third, put away your phones while studying. Many students are distracted by their smart phones. If you spend less time on social media, you will finish your homework much 6. ________ (fast) than before. 

Finally, remember to rest. Study is important, 7. ________ your brain needs a break too. Getting enough sleep is a good way to keep yourself energetic. A 8. ________ (health) body condition will certainly help you think more clearly.

In a word, poor time management makes our life stressful. By trying these methods, you can become 9. ________ master of your own time. Always remember that the 10. ________ (early) you start planning, the better your life will be. 

---

## 二、 答案与解析

1. **to do**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。 `have time/chance to do sth.` 为固定搭配，意为“有时间/机会做某事”。
2. **advice**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词变名词）。`some` 后接不可数名词，`advise` 的名词形式是 `advice`。
3. **especially**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词变副词）。此处修饰后面的名词短语或起强调作用，需用副词形式，意为“尤其是”。
4. **acceptable**
   - **考点**：词义与词性转换（动词变形容词）。在 `it is + adj. + to do sth.` 句型中，要求填写形容词作表语。告诉朋友们你不能加入他们，这是“可以接受的”（acceptable）。
5. **them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`join` 是及物动词，其后作宾语需要用代词宾格形式。
6. **faster**
   - **考点**：副词的比较级。根据后面的 `than` 可知这里需要填比较级形式。
7. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后两句“学习重要”与“大脑也需要休息”是逻辑上的转折关系。
8. **healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词变形容词）。这里做定语修饰 `body condition`，需将名词 `health` 转化为形容词 `healthy`。
9. **the** / **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。`become the master of` 成为……的主人，或 `a master of` 成为一个……的主人。建议填 `the` 表示特指。
10. **earlier**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词的比较级。这里属于 “`the + 比较级，the + 比较级`” 固定句型，意为“越早开始计划，你的生活就会越好”。
