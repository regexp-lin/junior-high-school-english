# 06-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：节约纸张保护森林 (Saving Paper to Protect Forests)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Paper is something we use every day, yet few people think about how many trees (1) ______ (cut) down to produce it. If each student uses one less sheet of paper a week, millions of trees can (2) ______ (save) in a year. We should print on both sides of the paper (3) ______ it is really necessary to print at all. Many homework tasks can (4) ______ finished online now, which helps reduce waste (5) ______ a great degree. Last term our class started a project and collected (6) ______ (use) textbooks for younger students. The librarian was pleased because she did not need to order as (7) ______ (many) new books as before. Saving paper is not (8) ______ difficult habit to build—just carry a small notebook (9) ______ you can rewrite drafts before copying them neatly. Remember: protecting forests starts with the choices (10) ______ we make in daily life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：are cut**  
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态。`trees` 与 `cut down` 为被动；陈述普遍事实用一般现在时，复数主语用 `are cut`。

2. **答案：be saved**  
   - **考点**：动词语态。`trees` 与 `save` 为被动，`can` 后用 `be saved`。

3. **答案：when**  
   - **考点**：连词。表示“当确实有必要打印时，应双面使用纸张”，用 `when` 引导时间状语从句。若理解为条件，少数语境可用 `if`，此处 **when** 更自然。

4. **答案：be**  
   - **考点**：被动语态。`tasks can be finished` 为含情态动词的被动语态，`can` 后接 `be + 过去分词`。

5. **答案：to**  
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`to a great degree` 表示“在很大程度上”，用介词 `to`。

6. **答案：used**  
   - **考点**：形容词/非谓语。`used textbooks` 表示“用过的课本”，过去分词作定语，相当于 `textbooks that have been used`。

7. **答案：many**  
   - **考点**：形容词原级。`as... as` 中间用原级；`books` 为可数名词复数，用 `many`。`as many... as before`。

8. **答案：a**  
   - **考点**：冠词。`not a difficult habit` 表示“并非一种难养成的习惯”，可数单数 `habit` 需不定冠词 `a`。

9. **答案：so**  
   - **考点**：连词。`so` 引导结果状语从句，说明带小本子的目的/结果：可以先打草稿再誊清，表示“以便、这样就能”。

10. **答案：that / which**  
    - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `choices` 指物，从句缺宾语，用 `that` 或 `which`。`make choices`。
