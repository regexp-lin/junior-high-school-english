# 06-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：暑期社会实践 (Summer Social Practice)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last July our school organized a social practice activity in a nearby village. On the first morning we helped farmers pick fruit, (1) ______ was heavier work than we had imagined. The sun burned hot, (2) ______ nobody complained because we knew our help mattered. In the afternoon a volunteer teacher showed us (3) ______ to teach simple English songs to local children. Their eyes shone (4) ______ excitement when they could sing a whole song. We also visited several old people who lived alone and brought them some daily things (5) ______ (buy) with our pocket money. One grandma held my hand tightly and said she felt much (6) ______ (lonely) after our visit. Before leaving, we promised (7) ______ (come) again the next summer. This experience taught me that true learning happens not only in classrooms (8) ______ in real life. I am thankful to everyone (9) ______ helped organize the trip, and I have written a report (10) ______ (describe) what we did for our community.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**  
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事（摘水果很累），用关系代词 `which`，前有逗号。

2. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。“太阳很晒”与“没人抱怨”为转折，用 `but`。

3. **答案：how**  
   - **考点**：疑问词 + 不定式。`showed us how to teach` 表示“教我们如何教”，用 `how`。

4. **答案：with**  
   - **考点**：介词。`shine with excitement` 表示“因兴奋而闪耀/发亮”，用 `with` 表原因或伴随。

5. **答案：bought**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`things` 与 `buy` 为被动关系（被买下的东西），用过去分词 `bought` 作后置定语，相当于 `things that were bought`。

6. **答案：less lonely**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`much` 修饰比较级；拜访后“不那么孤独了”，`lonely` 的比较级用 `less lonely`（孤独感减轻）。

7. **答案：to come**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`promise to do sth.` 用不定式 `to come`。

8. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`not only... but (also)...` 中填 `but`，与上文练习区分此处为固定结构 `not only in classrooms but in real life`（`also` 可省略）。

9. **答案：who**  
   - **考点**：定语从句关系词。先行词 `everyone` 指人，从句缺主语，用 `who`。

10. **答案：to describe**  
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`write a report to describe...` 中不定式表目的，用 `to describe`。也可用分词 `describing` 作后置定语，中考更常见目的状语 **to describe**。
