# 10-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家庭聚餐 (Family Reunion Dinner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In China, family reunion (1) ______ (dinner) are an important tradition during festivals and holidays. They bring family members together to share food, laughter and love. Last National Day, all my relatives gathered at my grandparents' house for a big dinner. My grandmother had been (2) ______ (prepare) for the meal since early morning. She cooked over ten (3) ______ (dish), including her famous braised fish and sweet-and-sour pork. The table was (4) ______ (complete) covered with plates and bowls. When everyone sat down, my grandfather raised his glass and said, "Let us be (5) ______ (thank) for being together." We all clinked our glasses and started eating. The food tasted even (6) ______ (good) than usual, perhaps because we were all together. My little cousins ran around the house (7) ______ (happy), chasing each other and laughing. The adults chatted about work, health and (8) ______ the children were doing at school. After dinner, we sat in the living room and watched old family videos (9) ______ my uncle had recorded years ago. Seeing my parents as young people made everyone laugh. It was a warm and (10) ______ (memory) evening. These gatherings remind me that family is the most important thing in life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：dinners**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`family reunion dinners` 泛指多次聚餐。

2. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：时态。`had been preparing`，过去完成进行时，表示从早上一直准备到那时。

3. **答案：dishes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`over ten dishes`，数词后接复数。

4. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `covered` 需要副词，`complete` 变为 `completely`。

5. **答案：thankful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be thankful for`，系动词后接形容词，`thank` 变为 `thankful`。

6. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`tasted even better than usual`，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

7. **答案：happily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `ran` 需要副词，`happy` 变为 `happily`。

8. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`how the children were doing at school`，`how` 引导宾语从句。

9. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `old family videos`（物），引导定语从句。

10. **答案：memorable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `warm` 并列修饰 `evening`，需要形容词，`memory` 变为 `memorable`。
