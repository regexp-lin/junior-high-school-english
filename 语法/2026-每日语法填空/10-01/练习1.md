# 10-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：长城徒步 (Hiking the Great Wall)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Great Wall is one of the most (1) ______ (impress) structures ever built by humans. During the National Day holiday, my family and I decided (2) ______ (hike) a section of the Great Wall near Beijing called Mutianyu. We set off early in the morning, hoping to avoid the (3) ______ (crowd). When we arrived, the wall stretched out before us like a giant stone dragon winding through the mountains. I was amazed at how steep some sections were. My younger brother (4) ______ (struggle) to climb the narrow steps, so I held his hand and helped him along. After about two hours of (5) ______ (walk), we reached one of the highest watchtowers. From there, we could see the wall (6) ______ (disappear) into the misty mountains in both directions. The view was absolutely breathtaking. Dad told us that the wall (7) ______ (build) over many centuries by different dynasties. Millions of workers, including soldiers and (8) ______ (farm), had contributed to its construction. Many of them died during the process. Standing on the ancient stones, I felt a deep connection (9) ______ history. The Great Wall is not just a tourist attraction — it is a symbol of the Chinese people's strength and (10) ______ (wise). I feel truly proud to be Chinese.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：impressive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most impressive structures`，修饰名词用形容词。

2. **答案：to hike**
   - **考点**：不定式。`decide to do sth` 表示"决定做某事"。

3. **答案：crowds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`avoid the crowds` 表示"避开人群"。

4. **答案：struggled**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

5. **答案：walking**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词。

6. **答案：disappearing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`see the wall disappearing`，感官动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词。

7. **答案：was built / had been built**
   - **考点**：被动语态。长城"被建造"，用过去时或过去完成时被动。

8. **答案：farmers**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`farm` 变为表示人的名词 `farmer`，复数。

9. **答案：to / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`a connection to/with history` 表示"与历史的联系"。

10. **答案：wisdom**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `strength` 并列，需要名词，`wise` 变为 `wisdom`。
