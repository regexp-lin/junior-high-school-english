# 07-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：探索洞穴 (Exploring a Cave)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the holiday, my uncle took me to explore a limestone cave in Guilin. It was a place (1) ______ few tourists visited because it was off the main road. We wore helmets with lights and carried ropes (2) ______ (enter) the cave. The entrance was so narrow (3) ______ we had to crawl through on our hands and knees. Once inside, the cave opened up into a huge space. I was amazed by the rock formations (4) ______ (hang) from the ceiling. My uncle said they were called stalactites and it took (5) ______ (they) thousands of years to form. The air inside was cool and damp. Water dripped (6) ______ (steady) from above, creating small pools on the ground. As we went (7) ______ (deep) into the cave, the darkness grew thicker. Without our headlamps, we would not have been able to see (8) ______ (something) at all. My uncle pointed out tiny crystals on the walls that sparkled like stars. It felt as if we (9) ______ (discover) a hidden world. The whole trip lasted about two hours, and it was one of the most (10) ______ (thrill) adventures I have ever had.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a place`（物），在定语从句中作宾语，可用 `that` 或 `which`。

2. **答案：to enter**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。"携带绳索"的目的是"进入洞穴"。

3. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so...that...` 表示"如此……以至于……"。

4. **答案：hanging**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`rock formations hanging from the ceiling` 表示"悬挂在天花板上的岩石形状"，现在分词表主动。

5. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`It took them thousands of years` 中 `them` 指代 stalactites，作宾语。

6. **答案：steadily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `dripped` 需要副词，`steady` 变为 `steadily`。

7. **答案：deeper**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`went deeper into` 表示"走得更深"，用比较级表示越来越深。

8. **答案：anything**
   - **考点**：不定代词。否定句/虚拟语气中用 `anything` 代替 `something`。

9. **答案：had discovered**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`felt as if...` 后接虚拟语气，也可理解为"仿佛已经发现了"一个隐藏世界。

10. **答案：thrilling**
    - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most thrilling`，`thrill` 变为 `thrilling`（令人激动的）。
