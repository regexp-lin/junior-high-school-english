# 07-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：快递员的一天 (A Day of a Delivery Driver)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever wondered what life is like for a delivery driver? My neighbour, Mr. Liu, (1) ______ (work) as a courier for the past five years. He starts his day at six in the morning and usually does not get home (2) ______ nine at night. Every day, he delivers about 200 packages to homes and offices across the city. The job is (3) ______ (physical) demanding — he lifts heavy boxes, climbs stairs, and rides his electric bike in all kinds of weather. In summer, the heat can reach over 40 degrees, (4) ______ in winter, the cold wind bites his hands. Despite the (5) ______ (hard), Mr. Liu always wears a smile. He says the happiest moment is (6) ______ a customer says "thank you" with a genuine smile. During last year's shopping festival, he (7) ______ (deliver) over 500 packages in a single day! He told me that every package (8) ______ (carry) someone's hope — maybe a birthday gift or a book that a student has been waiting for. His words made me (9) ______ (think) differently about the people who bring things to our doors. They deserve much more (10) ______ (respect) than they usually receive.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has been working / has worked**
   - **考点**：现在完成进行时/现在完成时。`for the past five years` 表示从过去到现在的持续，强调一直在做用 `has been working`。

2. **答案：until / till**
   - **考点**：介词。`not...until nine at night` 表示"直到晚上九点才"。

3. **答案：physically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `demanding` 需要副词，`physical` 变为 `physically`。

4. **答案：and / while**
   - **考点**：连词。`in summer...and/while in winter...` 表示夏天和冬天的对比并列。

5. **答案：hardship(s)**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`Despite the` 后接名词，`hard` 变为 `hardship`（艰辛）。

6. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：连词。`when a customer says...` 引导时间状语从句，表示"当顾客说……时"。

7. **答案：delivered**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`last year's shopping festival` 表示过去的具体时间。

8. **答案：carries**
   - **考点**：主谓一致 + 一般现在时。`every package` 为第三人称单数，描述一般性看法。

9. **答案：think**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`make sb + 动词原形` 为使役动词结构。

10. **答案：respect**
    - **考点**：名词。`more respect` 中 `respect` 为不可数名词，`more` 修饰名词表示"更多的尊重"。
