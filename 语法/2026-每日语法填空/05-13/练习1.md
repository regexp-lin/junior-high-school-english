# 05-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：减少塑料污染 (Reducing Plastic Pollution)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Plastic pollution has become one of the 1. ________ (serious) environmental problems in the world. Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste 2. ________ (throw) into rivers and oceans, and it takes hundreds of years for plastic 3. ________ break down completely.

Many people are trying to change this situation. Last month, our school started a project called "Say No to Single-Use Plastics". Students were asked to bring their own water bottles 4. ________ of buying drinks in plastic bottles. Our teacher also showed us how plastic bags harm sea animals 5. ________ they mistake them for food.

I have decided to play my part. At home, I use cloth bags when I go shopping, and I refuse straws unless they are really 6. ________ (necessary). I have also learned to sort waste 7. ________ (careful) so that some plastic can be recycled. My parents were surprised at first, 8. ________ now they support me.

If everyone takes small steps, we can make a big 9. ________ (different) together. Protecting the planet is not someone else's job — it is 10. ________ responsibility shared by all of us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **most serious**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。句中有 "one of the..." 结构，后接形容词最高级 + 复数名词，表示"最……的……之一"；serious 为多音节词，最高级用 most serious。

2. **are thrown**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般现在时的被动语态）。主语 millions of tons of plastic waste 与 throw 是被动关系，且描述常态事实，用一般现在时被动：are thrown。

3. **to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词中的固定结构（无提示词填空）。It takes + 时间 + for sb./sth. + to do sth.，此处为 for plastic to break down，填不定式符号 to。

4. **instead**
   - **考点**：副词/固定搭配（无提示词填空）。instead of 意为"而不是"，与 bring their own water bottles 形成对比，说明替代买瓶装饮料的做法。

5. **because**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。引导原因状语从句，说明塑料袋伤害海洋动物的原因——动物常常把它们误认为食物。

6. **necessary**
   - **考点**：形容词原级。unless they are really necessary 中系动词 are 后接形容词，necessary 本身已是形容词，无需变形。

7. **carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 sort，需用副词 carefully。

8. **but**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。前句 surprised at first 与后句 now they support me 为转折关系，用 but。

9. **difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。make a big difference 为固定搭配，"产生很大影响"，需用名词 difference。

10. **our**
    - **考点**：物主代词（无提示词填空）。与后文 we all share 呼应，表示"我们的责任"，用形容词性物主代词 our。
