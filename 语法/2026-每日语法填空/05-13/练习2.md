# 05-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园运动会 (School Sports Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school sports day was held last Friday. The weather was perfect — sunny, 1. ________ not too hot. Hundreds of students gathered on the playground early in the morning, and everyone looked excited.

The opening ceremony started at eight o'clock. Each class marched past the judges 2. ________ (wear) colorful uniforms and holding small flags. When our class appeared, we shouted our slogan loudly. I felt 3. ________ proud of being part of the team.

I took part in the girls' 800-meter race. Before the race, I was so nervous 4. ________ my legs were shaking. 5. ________ my classmates cheered for me, I calmed down little by little. Though I didn't win first place, I finished the race 6. ________ (success) and broke my personal record.

After the events, the teachers organized fun games for everyone. We laughed 7. ________ (loud) when our PE teacher tried the tug-of-war and fell onto the grass. By the end of the day, we were tired 8. ________ happy.

Sports day is more than competition. It teaches us teamwork, courage, and how to face 9. ________ (challenge) bravely. I am already looking forward to 10. ________ (take) part in it again next year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **but**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。sunny 与 not too hot 为转折并列，用 but 连接，描述"晴朗但不太热"。

2. **wearing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作伴随状语）。Each class 与 wear 为主动关系，用 wearing 与 holding 并列，表示"穿着……举着……"的伴随动作。

3. **very/really**
   - **考点**：副词（无提示词填空）。修饰形容词 proud，加强语气，填 very 或 really 均可，中考常见 very。

4. **that**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。so...that... 结果状语从句，"如此紧张以至于腿在抖"，填 that。

5. **When/As**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。引导时间状语从句，"当同学们为我加油时"，When 或 As 均可。

6. **successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→副词）。修饰动词 finished，用副词 successfully。

7. **loudly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 laughed，用 loudly；loud 也可作副词，但 loudly 更常见于书面强调。

8. **but**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。tired 与 happy 为转折，"累但开心"，用 but。

9. **challenges**
   - **考点**：名词复数。challenge 作"挑战"可数，face challenges 用复数表泛指。

10. **taking**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。look forward to 中的 to 为介词，后接动名词 taking。
