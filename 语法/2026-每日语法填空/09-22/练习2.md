# 09-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：热心肠的出租车司机 (A Kind Taxi Driver)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

One cold winter evening, I took a taxi home after my extra lesson. The driver was a middle-aged man (1) ______ a friendly smile. As we drove through the busy streets, he asked me about my studies and shared some (2) ______ (interest) stories from his life. He told me he (3) ______ (drive) a taxi for over twenty years. "I've met all kinds of people," he said (4) ______ (proud). "Each passenger has a story." When we (5) ______ (arrive) at my home, I realised that I had left my wallet at school. I felt extremely (6) ______ (embarrass) and didn't know what to do. To my surprise, the driver smiled and said, "Don't worry. Just pay me next time." He gave me his phone (7) ______ (numerous) and told me to call him when I was ready. I was deeply touched by his (8) ______ (kind). The next day, my mother helped me call him and we arranged to meet. When I paid the fare, I also gave him a box of tea as a gift (9) ______ (thank) him for his trust. He was very happy and refused to accept it at first, but I insisted. This experience taught me that trust between (10) ______ (strange) can bring warmth to even the coldest winter night.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`a man with a friendly smile`，`with` 表示"带有"。

2. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `stories` 用 `-ing` 形式的形容词。

3. **答案：had been driving / had driven**
   - **考点**：时态。`for over twenty years` 与过去时间搭配，用过去完成时或过去完成进行时。

4. **答案：proudly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `said` 需要副词，`proud` 变为 `proudly`。

5. **答案：arrived**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

6. **答案：embarrassed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt embarrassed`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

7. **答案：number**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`his phone number`，`numerous` 变为名词 `number`（电话号码）。

8. **答案：kindness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`his kindness`，物主代词后接名词，`kind` 变为 `kindness`。

9. **答案：to thank**
   - **考点**：不定式。`as a gift to thank him`，不定式表目的。

10. **答案：strangers**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`between strangers`，介词 `between` 后接复数名词，`strange` 变为 `strangers`。
