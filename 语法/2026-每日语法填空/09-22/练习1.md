# 09-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园午餐 (School Lunch)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

School lunch is an important part of students' daily life. At our school, lunch (1) ______ (serve) in the canteen from 11:30 to 12:30 every day. The canteen provides a variety of dishes, including rice, noodles, vegetables and meat. Each meal (2) ______ (design) by a professional nutritionist to make sure students get enough (3) ______ (nutritious). Recently, our school introduced a new policy that encourages students (4) ______ (reduce) food waste. Posters with messages like "Take only what you can eat" (5) ______ (hang) on the walls of the canteen. A "Clean Plate" campaign was also launched. Students who finish all their food can get a stamp on (6) ______ (they) meal card. Those who collect thirty stamps will receive a small reward. The campaign has been very (7) ______ (success). Before it started, the canteen threw (8) ______ about 50 kilograms of leftover food every day. Now, that number has been reduced (9) ______ more than half. I think this is a great initiative because wasting food is not only bad for the environment but also (10) ______ (respect) to the farmers who grow it. Every grain of rice represents hard work.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is served**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`lunch is served`，午餐"被供应"。

2. **答案：is designed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`Each meal is designed by...`，"被设计"。

3. **答案：nutrition**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`enough nutrition`，`enough` 修饰名词，`nutritious` 变为 `nutrition`。

4. **答案：to reduce**
   - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb to do sth`。

5. **答案：are hung / have been hung**
   - **考点**：被动语态。海报"被悬挂"，用一般现在时或现在完成时被动。

6. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`their meal card`，物主代词修饰名词。

7. **答案：successful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`has been very successful`，系动词后接形容词。

8. **答案：away**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`throw away` 表示"扔掉"。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`reduced by more than half` 表示"减少了一半以上"。

10. **答案：disrespectful**
    - **考点**：词性转换（加否定前缀）。`disrespectful to the farmers`，表"不尊重的"，`respect` 变为 `disrespectful`。
