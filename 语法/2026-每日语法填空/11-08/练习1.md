# 11-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：气候变化 (Climate Change)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Climate change is one of the biggest (1) ______ (challenge) facing our planet today. Scientists have warned that the Earth's average temperature (2) ______ (rise) by about 1.1 degrees Celsius since the late 19th century. This may not sound like much, but even small changes in temperature can have serious effects (3) ______ the environment. Glaciers are melting, sea levels are rising, and extreme weather events are becoming more (4) ______ (frequency). Last summer, our city experienced the (5) ______ (hot) temperatures ever recorded. Many elderly people were sent to hospital because they were not used to (6) ______ (deal) with such extreme heat. Forests in several provinces caught fire, (7) ______ (destroy) thousands of hectares of woodland. What can we do to help? Experts suggest that everyone should try to reduce (8) ______ (they) carbon footprint. Simple actions like taking public transport, saving electricity and planting trees can make (9) ______ difference. Our school has already started a "Green Campus" programme, and we hope more schools (10) ______ (join) us in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：challenges**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the biggest challenges`，`one of + 最高级 + 复数名词`。

2. **答案：has risen**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`since the late 19th century` 提示用现在完成时，`rise` 的过去分词为 `risen`。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`have effects on` 表示"对……有影响"。

4. **答案：frequent**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more frequent`，`frequency`（名词）需转换为形容词 `frequent`。

5. **答案：hottest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the hottest temperatures ever recorded`，`ever` 提示最高级。

6. **答案：dealing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`be used to doing sth` 表示"习惯于做某事"。

7. **答案：destroying**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表结果的伴随状语，用现在分词形式。

8. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 `carbon footprint` 需要形容词性物主代词 `their`。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`make a difference` 为固定搭配，表示"有所不同"。

10. **答案：will join**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。`in the future` 提示将来时态。
