# 11-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园运动会 (School Sports Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school sports day is always the most exciting event of the year. Last Friday, the annual sports day (1) ______ (hold) on the school playground. Students from all grades took part in various competitions, (2) ______ (include) running, long jump, relay races and tug-of-war. I signed up for the 100-metre race because I have been (3) ______ (practise) hard for months. When the starting gun fired, I ran as (4) ______ (fast) as I could. My heart was beating so hard that I thought it would jump out of (5) ______ chest. In the end, I came in second place and received a silver medal. Although I did not win the gold, I was still proud (6) ______ myself. The most memorable moment of the day was the relay race. Our class team was behind at first, but the last runner, Zhang Wei, managed (7) ______ (overtake) two competitors in the final stretch. The crowd (8) ______ (cheer) wildly when he crossed the finish line first. It was a perfect example of teamwork and (9) ______ (determine). After the sports day, our head teacher told us that the purpose of the event was not just about (10) ______ (win), but about learning to challenge ourselves and support each other.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was held**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the annual sports day` 与 `hold` 之间是被动关系，`Last Friday` 提示一般过去时。

2. **答案：including**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`including` 作介词，表示"包括"。

3. **答案：practising**
   - **考点**：现在完成进行时。`have been practising` 表示一直在练习。

4. **答案：fast**
   - **考点**：原级比较。`as fast as I could`，`as...as` 中间用原级。

5. **答案：my**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 `chest` 需要形容词性物主代词 `my`。

6. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`be proud of` 表示"为……感到骄傲"。

7. **答案：to overtake**
   - **考点**：不定式。`manage to do sth` 表示"成功做到某事"。

8. **答案：cheered**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述运动会当天发生的事情，用一般过去时。

9. **答案：determination**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `teamwork` 并列，需要名词形式，`determine` 变为 `determination`。

10. **答案：winning**
    - **考点**：动名词。`about` 为介词，后接动名词形式。
