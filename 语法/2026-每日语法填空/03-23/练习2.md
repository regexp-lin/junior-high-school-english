# 03-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：旅行的意义 (The Meaning of Travel)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traveling is a wonderful way to learn about the world. Last winter holiday, my family took a trip 1. ________ Yunnan, a beautiful province in southwestern China.

We 2. ________ (arrive) in Kunming on the first day. The weather there was warm and pleasant, which was quite different 3. ________ the cold winter in our hometown. We visited the Stone Forest, where we saw rocks in many amazing 4. ________ (shape). It was hard to believe that nature could create such wonderful works of art.

The next stop was Dali. Walking along the old streets, we enjoyed 5. ________ (taste) local food and buying souvenirs. The local people were very 6. ________ (friend) and always ready to help visitors. I was deeply 7. ________ (impress) by the beautiful Erhai Lake. The water was so clear that we could see the bottom.

On the last day, we went to Lijiang. The old town of Lijiang is one of the most 8. ________ (beauty) places I have ever 9. ________ (see). Walking through the narrow streets at night, I felt as if I had traveled back in time.

This trip taught me that there is so 10. ________ to explore in our country. I hope to visit more places in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。a trip to + 地点，意为"去某地的旅行"。

2. **arrived**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。根据 last winter holiday 和 on the first day，用一般过去时。

3. **from**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be different from 意为"与……不同"，是固定搭配。

4. **shapes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。many 修饰可数名词复数，shape → shapes。

5. **tasting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。enjoy doing sth. 意为"享受做某事"。

6. **friendly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。were 后接形容词作表语，friend → friendly。

7. **impressed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词作表语）。be impressed by 意为"被……所打动/留下深刻印象"，主语是人，用 -ed 形式。

8. **beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。one of the most + 形容词最高级 + 名词复数，beauty → beautiful。

9. **seen**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。have ever seen 意为"曾经见过的"，用现在完成时，see 的过去分词为 seen。

10. **much**
    - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。so much to explore 意为"这么多值得探索的"，much 代指不可数的内容。
