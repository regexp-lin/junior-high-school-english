# 03-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家庭关系 (Family Relationships)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Family is the most important thing in our lives. Our parents work hard every day to give us a 1. ________ (comfort) life. However, as teenagers, we sometimes fail 2. ________ (understand) them.

When I was in Grade 7, I often argued with my mother about small things. She always told me to study harder, 3. ________ I thought she was too strict. One evening, I shouted at her and ran back to my room 4. ________ (angry).

Later that night, I heard a soft knock on my door. My mother came in with a glass of warm milk. She sat beside me and said 5. ________ (soft), "I know you're growing up and you want more freedom. But everything I do is 6. ________ I love you." Her words made me feel so ashamed of 7. ________ (I).

From that day on, I tried to communicate with my parents more 8. ________ (patient). I began to understand that their rules were meant to protect me. Now, I help with housework on weekends and share my school life 9. ________ them at dinner.

A happy family 10. ________ (need) love, respect and understanding from every member. Let's treasure our time with our family.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **comfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 life，需用形容词形式 comfortable。

2. **to understand**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。fail to do sth. 意为"未能做某事"。

3. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后句是转折关系：妈妈叫我努力学习，"但是"我觉得她太严格。

4. **angrily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 ran，需用副词形式 angrily。

5. **softly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 said，需用副词形式 softly。

6. **because**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。表原因，"因为我爱你"，用 because 引导原因状语从句。

7. **myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。feel ashamed of oneself 意为"为自己感到羞愧"，主语是 I，对应反身代词 myself。

8. **patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 communicate，需用副词形式 patiently。

9. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。share sth. with sb. 意为"与某人分享某事"。

10. **needs**
    - **考点**：主谓一致（一般现在时）。主语 a happy family 是第三人称单数，描述一般性事实用一般现在时，动词加 -s。
