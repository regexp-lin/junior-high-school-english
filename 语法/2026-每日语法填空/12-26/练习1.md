# 12-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：年终回顾 (Year-End Reflection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As the year draws to a close, it is a good time to look back and reflect (1) ______ what we have accomplished and what we can improve. I have made it a habit to write a year-end reflection every December. This practice helps me appreciate my (2) ______ (achieve) and set goals for the new year. Looking back at this year, I feel both proud and grateful. (3) ______ (academic), I have improved a lot. My English grades went from average to among the top five in my class. This would not have been possible (4) ______ the guidance of my teacher, Ms Wang, and the support of my study group. I also learned to play the ukulele, which has brought me great (5) ______ (joyful). Of course, not everything went perfectly. I failed to keep my promise of exercising every day. I started the year with great (6) ______ (enthusiastic), going jogging every morning. But as the schoolwork got (7) ______ (heavy), I gradually stopped. By October, I had completely given (8) ______ the routine. This experience taught me that (9) ______ (maintain) a habit requires more than just initial motivation — it requires a plan and the discipline to stick with it even when life gets busy. As I plan for the year ahead, I want to set (10) ______ (realistic) goals and create concrete strategies for achieving them. I believe that each year is a chance to become a better version of ourselves.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`reflect on` 表示"反思"。

2. **答案：achievements**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`appreciate my achievements`，代词后接名词复数。

3. **答案：Academically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。句首作状语，修饰整个句子。

4. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`would not have been possible without`，虚拟语气暗含"没有……就不可能"。

5. **答案：joy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`great joy`，形容词后接名词。

6. **答案：enthusiasm**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`great enthusiasm`，形容词后接名词。

7. **答案：heavier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`got heavier`，暗含比之前更重。

8. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词。`give up` 表示"放弃"。

9. **答案：maintaining**
   - **考点**：动名词。动名词作主语。

10. **答案：realistic**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `goals` 需要形容词。
