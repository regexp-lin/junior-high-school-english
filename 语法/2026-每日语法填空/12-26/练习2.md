# 12-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国皮影戏 (Chinese Shadow Puppetry)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese shadow puppetry is a traditional folk art that dates back over 2,000 years. It is one of the (1) ______ (early) forms of storytelling theatre in the world. In a shadow puppet show, flat puppets made from leather or paper are held behind a translucent screen. A light source behind the puppets creates (2) ______ (colour) shadows that move and dance as the puppeteer manipulates them with thin sticks. The stories often come from Chinese history, legends and folk tales. Each character has a (3) ______ (distinct) designed puppet — heroes are carved with handsome features, while villains have exaggerated, (4) ______ (frighten) expressions. My granduncle is one of the few remaining shadow puppeteers in our county. He learned the art from his father, who had learned it from (5) ______ father before him. He can operate up to five puppets at the same time while (6) ______ (sing) different voices for each character. Watching him perform is like watching a one-man theatre company. Sadly, shadow puppetry is in danger of (7) ______ (disappear). Fewer young people are willing to spend years (8) ______ (learn) this difficult craft. In 2011, Chinese shadow puppetry was recognised by UNESCO as an Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity. Since then, efforts (9) ______ (make) to preserve and promote this art form. Several schools, including ours, have invited shadow puppet artists to perform and (10) ______ (teach) students about this ancient tradition.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：earliest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the earliest forms`。

2. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `shadows` 需要形容词。

3. **答案：distinctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `designed` 需要副词。

4. **答案：frightening**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物（表情）用 `-ing`。

5. **答案：his**
   - **考点**：代词。`his father`，根据上文指代男性。

6. **答案：singing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`while singing` 为时间状语从句的省略。

7. **答案：disappearing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`in danger of doing sth` 表示"有做某事的危险"。

8. **答案：learning**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth`。

9. **答案：have been made**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。`Since then` 提示现在完成时。

10. **答案：teach**
    - **考点**：动词原形。与 `perform` 并列，`have invited...to perform and teach`。
