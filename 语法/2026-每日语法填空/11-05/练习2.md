# 11-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：火锅文化 (Hot Pot Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Hot pot is one of the most popular ways of dining in China. It (1) ______ (enjoy) by people across the country, especially during the cold winter months. The concept is simple: a pot of boiling broth is placed at the centre of the table, and diners cook (2) ______ (vary) ingredients in it, such as thinly sliced meat, vegetables, tofu and noodles. Sichuan-style hot pot is the (3) ______ (spicy) type, using a red chilli and Sichuan pepper broth that makes your lips go numb. My family loves eating hot pot together because it is (4) ______ a meal and a social activity. Everyone sits around the table, (5) ______ (chat) and cooking at the same time. Last weekend, we went to a hot pot restaurant (6) ______ (celebrate) my sister's birthday. The restaurant was so popular that we had to wait for 40 minutes (7) ______ we could get a table. Once seated, we ordered a split pot — one side spicy and the other side mild — so that everyone could enjoy (8) ______ (they) preferred flavour. The meat was (9) ______ (incredible) fresh, and the dipping sauces were customisable. By the end of the meal, we were all full and happy. I think hot pot is popular not just because of the food, but because of the warmth and togetherness it (10) ______ (bring) to the table.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is enjoyed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`It is enjoyed by people`，"被喜爱"。

2. **答案：various**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `ingredients` 需要形容词，`vary` 变为 `various`。

3. **答案：spiciest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the spiciest type`。

4. **答案：both**
   - **考点**：连词。`is both a meal and a social activity`，`both...and...`。

5. **答案：chatting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。现在分词作伴随状语。

6. **答案：to celebrate**
   - **考点**：不定式。不定式表目的。

7. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：连词。`wait for 40 minutes before we could get a table`。

8. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`their preferred flavour`。

9. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `fresh` 需要副词。

10. **答案：brings**
    - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。`the warmth and togetherness it brings`，描述一般事实用一般现在时。
