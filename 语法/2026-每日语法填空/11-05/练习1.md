# 11-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：面对失败 (Facing Failure)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Everyone experiences failure at some point in life, but how we deal with it makes all the (1) ______ (different). Last term, I failed my English speaking test. I had prepared carefully and was confident (2) ______ I would do well. But when I stood in front of the examiners, my mind went blank and I could (3) ______ (hard) say a word. I felt (4) ______ (terrible) embarrassed and disappointed in myself. For the next few days, I didn't want to talk (5) ______ anyone about it. My best friend noticed something was wrong and asked me what had happened. When I told her, she didn't laugh (6) ______ judge me. Instead, she said, "One failure doesn't define (7) ______ (you). What matters is what you do next." Her words gave me the courage to try again. I signed up for the re-test and started practising every day. This time, I also (8) ______ (join) a speaking club to build my confidence. When the re-test came, I was nervous but much better (9) ______ (prepare) than before. I passed with a good score. Looking back, I'm (10) ______ (actual) grateful for that failure because it taught me resilience and the importance of never giving up.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`makes all the difference`，`different` 变为名词 `difference`。

2. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`was confident that I would do well`，`that` 引导宾语从句。

3. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：副词。`could hardly say a word`，`hardly` 表示"几乎不"。

4. **答案：terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `embarrassed` 需要副词。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`talk to anyone` 表示"和任何人说话"。

6. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`didn't laugh or judge me`，否定句中并列用 `or`。

7. **答案：you**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`doesn't define you`，动词后接宾格。

8. **答案：joined**
   - **考点**：时态。与 `started` 并列，用一般过去时。

9. **答案：prepared**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`much better prepared than before`，过去分词作表语。

10. **答案：actually**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `grateful` 需要副词。
