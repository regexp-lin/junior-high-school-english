# 06-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我的中学三年 (My Three Years in Middle School)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When I entered this school three years ago, everything felt strange, and I worried about (1) ______ (make) friends in a new place. Luckily, my deskmate smiled at me on the first day, and we soon found we both loved science fiction. Time runs faster than expected. I have joined the volunteer club, learned to play basketball, and read dozens of books in the library. There were hard moments, too—I once failed a physics test and thought I was the (2) ______ (lucky) person in the world. My teacher told me mistakes were messages, not judgments, and her words helped me stand up again. Now the classroom is quieter in the evenings because graduates are reviewing, (3) ______ I still hear laughter in the hallways. I will miss the red track, the noisy canteen, and the friends (4) ______ have grown up with me. Middle school is not perfect, (5) ______ it has given me courage and curiosity. As I close this chapter, I want to say thank you to everyone (6) ______ offered a hand along the way. I will carry these memories (7) ______ me and walk toward the next stage with hope. No matter where I go, I will remember what my teachers (8) ______ (repeat): learning never ends. These three years (9) ______ (shape) who I am today, and I am proud of how far I have come. The goodbye is near, (10) ______ a new hello is waiting ahead.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：making**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`worry about doing sth.` 为固定搭配，`about` 为介词，后接动名词，故用 `making`。注意与 `worry + that 从句` 的区别。

2. **答案：unluckiest**  
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。语境为考试失利后的消极情绪，表示“世界上最不幸的人”，用 `lucky` 的反义最高级 `unluckiest`；句中有定冠词 `the` 与范围提示 `in the world`。

3. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。前句写夜晚教室更安静，后句写走廊仍有笑声，形成转折对比，用 `but`。

4. **答案：who / that**  
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `friends`，定语从句缺主语，指人，用 `who` 或 `that`。

5. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。`not perfect` 与 `has given me courage` 为转折关系，用 `but`。

6. **答案：who / that**  
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `everyone`，定语从句缺主语，指人，用 `who` 或 `that`（在正式文体中 `who` 更常见）。

7. **答案：with**  
   - **考点**：介词。`carry...with me` 表示“把……带在身边”，用介词 `with`。

8. **答案：repeated**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。`what my teachers repeated` 为宾语从句，从句谓语叙述过去老师常说的话，用一般过去时 `repeated`；括号内 `repeat` 变为过去式。

9. **答案：have shaped**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。`these three years` 强调对现在的影响“塑造了今天的我”，用现在完成时 `have shaped`。

10. **答案：but**  
    - **考点**：连词。告别临近与新的开始在即形成转折，用 `but` 连接两个并列分句。
