# 06-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：地球一小时 (Earth Hour)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Earth Hour is a global event that asks people to turn off non-essential lights for one hour. It is not about sitting in complete darkness, (1) ______ about noticing how much electricity we use every day. Last year, my family joined the activity at home. We put candles on the table, told stories, and listened to the soft sound of rain (2) ______ the window. My little brother was worried at first, but later he said the evening felt (3) ______ (calm) than usual. The next morning, we read an article and learned that many cities had saved a (4) ______ (surprise) amount of energy in sixty minutes. The event also reminds us that protecting the planet is (5) ______ (true) everyone's duty, not only the government's job. Small choices, such as taking shorter showers or riding a bike on sunny days, can add up to (6) ______ big difference. If each person changes a little, the world may become (7) ______ (clean) than we imagine. Some people think one hour is too short to matter, (8) ______ I believe symbols can start habits. Our teacher encouraged us to write posters, and my sentence was simple: "Darkness for an hour, (9) ______ light for years." When the lights came back, I looked at my room and realized I did not need every lamp (10) ______ (stay) on at the same time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词与固定结构。`not...but...` 表示“不是……而是……”，说明活动重在反思日常用电，而不是单纯体验黑暗。

2. **答案：against**  
   - **考点**：介词。`rain against the window` 表示“雨点敲打在窗户上”，`against` 表“碰、抵、映衬”。

3. **答案：calmer**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，用 `calm` 的比较级 `calmer`，表示“比往常更平静”。

4. **答案：surprising**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `amount`，用形容词；`surprise` 变为 `surprising`，表示“令人惊讶的数量”。前面已有不定冠词 `a`。

5. **答案：truly**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰整个判断“是每个人的责任”，用副词；`true` 变为 `truly`，表示“确实、真正地”。

6. **答案：a**  
   - **考点**：冠词。`add up to a big difference` 表示“累积成很大影响”，用不定冠词 `a`。

7. **答案：cleaner**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，用 `clean` 的比较级 `cleaner`。

8. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。前句写“有人认为一小时太短”，后句写“我相信象征能养成习惯”，转折用 `but`。

9. **答案：and**  
   - **考点**：连词。标语前后为并列对仗结构，`Darkness for an hour` 与 `light for years` 用 `and` 连接，表示递进与希望。

10. **答案：to stay**  
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`need sth. to do` 结构表示“需要某物做某事”，此处指不必让每盏灯同时亮着，用不定式 `to stay`。
