# 12-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：塑料污染 (Plastic Pollution)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Plastic pollution has become one of the most pressing environmental (1) ______ (issue) of our time. Every year, approximately eight million tonnes of plastic waste enters the world's oceans, (2) ______ (threaten) marine life and ecosystems. Sea turtles, whales and seabirds often mistake plastic bags and bottles (3) ______ food, which can lead to injury or death. Microplastics — tiny plastic particles (4) ______ (small) than five millimetres — have even been found in drinking water and seafood. This means that plastic pollution is not only an environmental problem but also a (5) ______ (seriously) threat to human health. In China, the government has taken several steps to reduce plastic use. Since January 2021, single-use plastic bags (6) ______ (ban) in major supermarkets and shopping centres. Restaurants are no longer allowed (7) ______ (provide) free plastic straws. Many people now bring their own reusable bags when they go shopping. Our school has also joined the fight against plastic. We replaced all plastic water cups in the canteen (8) ______ paper or bamboo ones. The science club organised an experiment to show students how long different materials take to decompose. We learned that a plastic bottle can take up to 450 years to break (9) ______ in the environment. This shocking fact made many of us (10) ______ (determine) to use less plastic in our daily lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：issues**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most pressing issues`。

2. **答案：threatening**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表结果的伴随状语。

3. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`mistake...for...` 表示"把……误认为……"。

4. **答案：smaller**
   - **考点**：比较级。`smaller than five millimetres`。

5. **答案：serious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `threat` 需要形容词。

6. **答案：have been banned**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。`Since January 2021` 提示现在完成时，`bags` 与 `ban` 是被动关系。

7. **答案：to provide**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be allowed to do sth` 表示"被允许做某事"。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`replace...with...` 表示"用……替换……"。

9. **答案：down**
   - **考点**：副词。`break down` 表示"分解"。

10. **答案：determined**
    - **考点**：过去分词。`made many of us determined`，`make + 宾语 + 形容词`。
