# 12-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：桌游 (Board Games)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In an age of smartphones and video games, board games may seem old-fashioned to some people. However, they are making a strong comeback, especially (1) ______ young people. Board game cafés have been popping up in cities across China, offering a place (2) ______ friends can gather, play games and enjoy each other's company. Board games come in many varieties. Some are strategy games that require careful (3) ______ (plan) and logical thinking. Others are party games (4) ______ (design) for large groups, full of laughter and friendly competition. Chinese chess and Go are among the (5) ______ (old) board games in the world, with histories stretching back thousands of years. My family has a tradition of playing board games together on rainy weekends. My favourite game is a detective game where players must work together to solve a mystery. Each player receives different clues, and we have to share information and think (6) ______ (critic) to find the answer. It is much more fun than playing alone on a computer because it involves real (7) ______ (interact) with people sitting right next to you. Last month, our class organised a board game tournament. Over thirty students (8) ______ (participate). The atmosphere was lively, with students cheering and debating strategies. Even students who (9) ______ (usual) prefer video games admitted that they had a great time. I believe that board games will never truly go out of fashion because the joy of (10) ______ (sit) face to face and playing together cannot be replaced by technology.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：among**
   - **考点**：介词。`especially among young people` 表示"尤其在年轻人中"。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，先行词 `place` 为地点。

3. **答案：planning**
   - **考点**：动名词。`careful planning`，形容词修饰名词/动名词。

4. **答案：designed**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`games designed for large groups`，被动关系。

5. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`among the oldest board games`。

6. **答案：critically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `think` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：interaction**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`real interaction`，形容词后接名词。

8. **答案：participated**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last month` 提示过去时间。

9. **答案：usually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `prefer` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：sitting**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词形式。
