# 05-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：诚实守信 (Honesty and Trust)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Honesty is one of the most important qualities a person can have. It is the foundation of trust, and without trust, no relationship can last 1. ________ (long).

When I was ten years old, I broke my mother's favorite vase while playing football inside the house. I was so 2. ________ (scare) that I hid the broken pieces under the sofa and pretended nothing had happened. But I felt 3. ________ (terrible) guilty all day.

That evening, my mother found the broken vase. She didn't shout 4. ________ me. Instead, she sat beside me and asked gently, "Did you break it?" I looked down and finally told her the 5. ________ (true). To my surprise, she smiled and said, "I'm glad you were honest. The vase can be replaced, but honesty cannot."

Her words taught me 6. ________ valuable lesson. From that day on, I have always tried to be honest, even when it is difficult. Honesty doesn't mean you will never make 7. ________ (mistake). It means you have the courage to admit them.

In our daily lives, dishonesty may seem like an easy way 8. ________ (avoid) trouble. But lies always lead to bigger problems in the end. People who are known 9. ________ being honest are respected and trusted by everyone around them.

As an old Chinese saying goes, "A person without honesty is like a tree without 10. ________ (root)." Let's always choose to be truthful.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **long**
   - **考点**：副词（无需变形）。last long 意为"持续很久"，long 作副词修饰动词 last。

2. **scared**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。so scared 意为"如此害怕"，主语是人用 -ed 形式。

3. **terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 guilty，需用副词形式 terribly。

4. **at**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。shout at sb. 意为"对某人大喊"。

5. **truth**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。the truth 意为"事实"，true → truth。

6. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a valuable lesson 意为"一个宝贵的教训"，lesson 为可数名词单数。

7. **mistakes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。make mistakes 意为"犯错误"，用复数。

8. **to avoid**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作后置定语）。a way to do sth. 意为"做某事的方法"。

9. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be known for 意为"因……而闻名"。

10. **roots**
    - **考点**：名词复数。without roots 意为"没有根"，root 用复数。
