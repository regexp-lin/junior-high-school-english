# 08-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：微笑的力量 (The Power of a Smile)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A smile is the simplest gift anyone can give, yet it can make a world of (1) ______ (different). I discovered this on a Monday morning last month. I was feeling down because I (2) ______ (fail) a maths test the Friday before. As I walked through the school gate with my head low, the security guard greeted me with a bright smile and said, "Good morning! Have a great day!" His (3) ______ (warm) surprised me and lifted my spirits a little. In class, I decided to pass on the kindness. I smiled at my deskmate, who had been looking (4) ______ (worry) about something. She smiled back and whispered, "Thanks, I needed that." By lunchtime, I noticed that smiling made me feel (5) ______ (good) than staying gloomy. Scientists say that when we smile, our brain (6) ______ (release) chemicals that reduce stress and improve mood. Even a fake smile can trick the brain (7) ______ feeling happier. I have since made it a habit to smile at people every day, (8) ______ I know them or not. Of course, a smile cannot solve all problems, but it creates an atmosphere of (9) ______ (friend) and trust. It costs (10) ______ (nothing), yet it is worth everything.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a world of difference` 为固定搭配，表示"天壤之别"，`different` 变为 `difference`。

2. **答案：had failed**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。"考砸"发生在"感到沮丧"之前（过去的过去），用 `had failed`。

3. **答案：warmth**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`His` 后接名词，`warm` 变为 `warmth`（温暖）。

4. **答案：worried**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`look worried` 中 `look` 为系动词，后接形容词，`worry` 变为 `worried`（担心的）。

5. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than staying gloomy` 提示用比较级，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

6. **答案：releases**
   - **考点**：主谓一致 + 一般现在时。描述科学事实，`our brain` 为第三人称单数。

7. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`trick sb into doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"诱使某人做某事"。

8. **答案：whether**
   - **考点**：连词。`whether...or not` 表示"不论是否"。

9. **答案：friendliness / friendship**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`of` 后接名词，`friend` 变为 `friendliness`（友好）或 `friendship`（友谊）。

10. **答案：nothing**
    - **考点**：代词。`It costs nothing` 表示"它不花一分钱"，将 `nothing` 作为宾语。
