# 08-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑假读书计划 (Summer Reading Plan)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

At the start of this summer holiday, I made a reading plan for (1) ______ (I). I chose ten books from different categories: three novels, two science books, two history books, and three collections of essays. My English teacher had always told us that reading (2) ______ (wide) was the key to becoming a good writer. She said the more we read, the (3) ______ (rich) our vocabulary would be. I started with a novel by Mo Yan, (4) ______ won the Nobel Prize for Literature. The story was set in a small village and described the (5) ______ (live) of ordinary farmers. Some parts were difficult (6) ______ (understand), so I had to read them twice. But the effort was worthwhile because the book gave me a completely new view of rural China. Next, I moved on to a science book about the human brain. I was fascinated to learn that our brains continue (7) ______ (develop) until we are about 25 years old. So far, I (8) ______ (finish) six books and am halfway through the seventh. I write a short review after completing each one. My mother says I have become much (9) ______ (quiet) than usual this summer — she finds me in my room (10) ______ (read) almost every afternoon.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语 `I` 与宾语指同一人，用 `myself`。

2. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动名词 `reading` 需要副词，`wide` 变为 `widely`（广泛地）。

3. **答案：richer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`the more...the + 比较级` 为固定结构，`rich` 的比较级为 `richer`。

4. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `Mo Yan`（人），引导非限制性定语从句，作主语。

5. **答案：lives**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`the` 后接名词，`live` 变为名词 `life`（生活），指多人的生活用复数 `lives`。

6. **答案：to understand**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be difficult to do` 为固定结构。

7. **答案：to develop / developing**
   - **考点**：不定式/动名词。`continue to do / continue doing` 均可，表示"继续发展"。

8. **答案：have finished**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`So far` 提示用现在完成时。

9. **答案：quieter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than usual` 提示用比较级，`quiet` 的比较级为 `quieter`。

10. **答案：reading**
    - **考点**：现在分词作宾补。`find sb doing sth` 表示"发现某人正在做某事"。
