# 11-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：园艺 (Gardening)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Gardening may seem like an activity for older people, but more and more young people are discovering the joy of growing (1) ______ (they) own plants. Our school has a small garden behind the science building where students can learn about plant growth first-hand. I joined the gardening club at the beginning of this term. Our teacher, Ms Lin, assigned each member a small plot of land and asked us (2) ______ (decide) what to plant. I chose to grow tomatoes because they are one of the (3) ______ (easy) vegetables for beginners. Ms Lin taught us the basics of gardening: how to prepare the soil, how deep (4) ______ (plant) the seeds and how often to water them. She stressed that plants need the right amount of sunlight, water and (5) ______ (nutritious) to grow well. I watered my tomato plants every morning before class. At first, nothing seemed to happen, and I began to wonder (6) ______ I was doing something wrong. But after about two weeks, tiny green shoots appeared above the soil. I was so excited that I (7) ______ (take) a photo and sent it to my parents immediately. Watching the plants grow taller day (8) ______ day gave me a wonderful sense of achievement. Last week, I (9) ______ (final) harvested my first ripe tomato. It was small and not perfectly shaped, but it tasted better than any tomato I (10) ______ (ever buy) from a shop.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 `plants` 需要形容词性物主代词。

2. **答案：to decide**
   - **考点**：不定式。`ask sb to do sth` 表示"要求某人做某事"。

3. **答案：easiest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the easiest vegetables`。

4. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how deep to plant the seeds`，"疑问词 + to do"结构。

5. **答案：nutrition / nutrients**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `sunlight` 和 `water` 并列，需要名词形式。

6. **答案：if / whether**
   - **考点**：连词。`wonder if/whether` 表示"想知道是否"。

7. **答案：took**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的事件。

8. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`day by day` 表示"日复一日"。

9. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `harvested` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：had ever bought**
    - **考点**：过去完成时。与过去的动作 `tasted` 比较，表示"曾经买过的"用过去完成时。
