# 11-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国武术 (Chinese Martial Arts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese martial arts, commonly known (1) ______ kung fu, have a history of several thousand years. They originated as methods of self-defence and military training, but over time they (2) ______ (develop) into a comprehensive system that combines physical exercise with philosophy and art. There are hundreds of different styles of kung fu, (3) ______ can be broadly divided into two categories: external styles that emphasise speed and power, and internal styles that focus on breathing and mental (4) ______ (strong). My PE teacher, Mr Wang, is a martial arts master. He has been practising Shaolin kung fu (5) ______ over thirty years. Every Monday and Thursday, he teaches us basic kung fu movements during PE class. The first thing he taught us was the horse stance — standing with your feet wide apart and bending your knees as if (6) ______ (sit) on an invisible chair. It looks simple, but holding the position for even one minute is (7) ______ (extreme) tiring. Mr Wang says that the horse stance is the foundation of all kung fu. Without it, (8) ______ advanced technique would be built on shaky ground. Besides physical training, Mr Wang also teaches us the martial arts code of conduct. He tells us that a true martial artist should be humble, (9) ______ (discipline) and respectful. Kung fu is not about fighting — it is about building a (10) ______ (strong) body and a stronger mind.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`known as` 表示"被称为"。

2. **答案：have developed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。表示从过去发展到现在的结果。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `styles of kung fu`。

4. **答案：strength**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`mental strength` 为名词短语，`strong` 变为 `strength`。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for over thirty years` 表示持续时间。

6. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`as if sitting` 为省略形式（省略了 `you were`）。

7. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `tiring` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：any / every**
   - **考点**：限定词。`any/every advanced technique` 表示"任何/每一个高级技巧"。

9. **答案：disciplined**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `humble` 和 `respectful` 并列，需要形容词，`discipline` 变为 `disciplined`。

10. **答案：strong**
    - **考点**：形容词原级。`a strong body and a stronger mind`，前者用原级，后者用比较级形成递进。
