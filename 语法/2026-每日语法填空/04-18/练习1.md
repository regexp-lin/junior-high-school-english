# 04-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：急救知识 (First Aid Knowledge)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Accidents can happen at any time and in any place. Knowing basic first aid skills can save 1. ________ (life) and prevent injuries from getting worse.

One of the most common 2. ________ (injure) is a cut or a wound. If someone gets a cut, the first thing to do is to wash the wound 3. ________ clean water. Then, press a clean cloth against it to stop the bleeding. After that, cover it with a bandage. If the cut is deep, the person should be taken to the hospital 4. ________ (immediate).

Burns are another common type of injury. If someone 5. ________ (burn) by hot water, cool the burned area under running water for at least ten minutes. Do not put ice 6. ________ (direct) on the burn, as this can cause more damage to the skin.

Knowing how to perform CPR is also very important. CPR, 7. ________ stands for Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation, is used when someone's heart stops beating. It 8. ________ (teach) in many schools now because it can keep a person alive until doctors arrive.

Our school organized a first aid training session last month. A doctor from the local hospital came and showed us the 9. ________ (correct) way to handle different emergencies. The training was very 10. ________ (practice), and I believe everyone should learn these skills.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **lives**
   - **考点**：名词复数。save lives 意为"拯救生命"，life → lives（不规则变化）。

2. **injuries**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。one of the most common + 名词复数，injure → injury → injuries。

3. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。wash...with 意为"用……清洗"。

4. **immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 should be taken，需用副词形式 immediately。

5. **is burned/is burnt**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般现在时）。someone 与 burn 之间是被动关系，用一般现在时被动语态。

6. **directly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 put，需用副词形式 directly。

7. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，which 指代 CPR。

8. **is taught**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般现在时）。CPR 与 teach 之间是被动关系，now 提示用一般现在时。

9. **correct**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词→形容词）。修饰名词 way，correctly → correct。注意提示词可能是 correct 的某种变形，此处需要形容词原形。

10. **practical**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词/名词→形容词）。very + 形容词，practice → practical。
