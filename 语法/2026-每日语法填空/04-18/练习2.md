# 04-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：才艺展示 (Talent Show)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every year, our school holds a talent show. It is one of the events that students look forward to 1. ________ (much).

This year's talent show 2. ________ (hold) last Friday in the school hall. Over twenty groups signed up, and the performances were 3. ________ (amaze). There were singers, dancers, comedians and even a magic show.

The most impressive performance was by a group of girls from Class 3. They performed a traditional Chinese dance 4. ________ wore beautiful costumes. Their 5. ________ (move) were so graceful that the whole audience was attracted. When they finished, everyone stood up and 6. ________ (clap) for nearly a minute.

My friend Tom and I also took part. We played a guitar duet — a song 7. ________ we had practiced for three weeks. I was a little nervous at first, but once the music started, I forgot about the audience and just enjoyed 8. ________ (play). The audience gave us a warm welcome, and we felt very happy.

The 9. ________ (judge) had a hard time choosing the winners because all the performances were excellent. In the end, the dance group won first prize, and Tom and I got third place.

The talent show is not just about 10. ________ (win). It gives every student a chance to shine and show their unique talents. I can't wait for next year's show!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **most**
   - **考点**：副词最高级。look forward to most 意为"最期待的"，much → most。

2. **was held**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般过去时）。talent show 与 hold 之间是被动关系，last Friday 提示过去时。

3. **amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。were 后接形容词作表语，修饰事物用 -ing 形式。

4. **and**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。performed a dance and wore costumes，and 连接并列谓语。

5. **movements**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。their + 名词，move → movement → movements。

6. **clapped**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。and 连接并列谓语，与 stood 并列，clap → clapped（双写辅音+ed）。

7. **that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 a song，在从句中作 practiced 的宾语。

8. **playing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。enjoy doing sth. 意为"享受做某事"。

9. **judges**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。the judges 意为"评委们"，judge 用复数。

10. **winning**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 about 后接动名词形式。
