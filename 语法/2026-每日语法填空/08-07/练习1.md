# 08-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园戏剧社 (The School Drama Club)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I joined the school drama club at the beginning of this school year, and it has become one of the most (1) ______ (enjoy) parts of my week. Every Thursday afternoon, we meet in the school hall to rehearse plays and practise acting. Our director, Ms. Qian, is a former actress (2) ______ once performed on the national stage. She teaches us how to use our voices, facial (3) ______ (express), and body language to bring characters to life. Last term, we performed a short play (4) ______ (base) on a folk tale. I was given the role of a wise old man. At first, I found it hard to act like someone much (5) ______ (old) than me. Ms. Qian suggested that I observe my grandfather's (6) ______ (move) and way of speaking. I spent a weekend (7) ______ (watch) him carefully and took notes. When I finally stepped on stage, I (8) ______ (surprise) everyone — including myself — with a convincing performance. The audience clapped (9) ______ (enthusiasm). Being in the drama club has taught me that acting is not just about pretending; it is about (10) ______ (understand) other people's feelings and experiences.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：enjoyable**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most enjoyable`，`enjoy` 变为 `enjoyable`。

2. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a former actress`（人），在定语从句中作主语。

3. **答案：expressions**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`facial expressions` 表示"面部表情"，`express` 变为 `expression`，用复数。

4. **答案：based**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a short play based on a folk tale` 表示"基于民间故事的短剧"。

5. **答案：older**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than me` 提示用比较级，`old` 的比较级为 `older`。

6. **答案：movements**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。观察爷爷的"动作"，`move` 变为 `movement`，用复数。

7. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：surprised**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述演出时的情况，`surprise` 的过去式为 `surprised`。

9. **答案：enthusiastically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `clapped` 需要副词，`enthusiasm` 变为 `enthusiastically`。

10. **答案：understanding**
    - **考点**：动名词。`about` 为介词，后接动名词。
