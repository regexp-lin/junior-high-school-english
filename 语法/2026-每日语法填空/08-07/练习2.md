# 08-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节约用电 (Saving Electricity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Electricity is something we use every day, but we rarely think about where it comes (1) ______. In China, most electricity is still generated (2) ______ burning coal, which produces harmful gases and contributes to climate change. Saving electricity is therefore one of the (3) ______ (easy) ways for ordinary people to help the environment. There are many small things we can do. For example, turn off lights when (4) ______ (leave) a room, and unplug chargers that are not (5) ______ being used. Choosing energy-efficient appliances can also make a big difference. A LED light bulb uses 75 percent (6) ______ (little) electricity than a traditional one and lasts much (7) ______ (long). My family recently replaced all our old bulbs with LED ones, and our electricity bill (8) ______ (drop) noticeably. We also try to use the air conditioner less often by opening windows (9) ______ (instead). On cool evenings, a natural breeze is more (10) ______ (comfort) than cold air from a machine. Every unit of electricity saved is a step towards a cleaner planet.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`come from` 为固定搭配，表示"来自"。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by burning coal` 表示"通过燃煤"，`by + doing` 表示方式。

3. **答案：easiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`。

4. **答案：leaving**
   - **考点**：现在分词/省略结构。`when leaving a room` 是 `when you are leaving a room` 的省略。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in use` 为固定短语，但此处 `not being used` 本身已表被动进行，填 `in` 或理解为独立结构。此空实际最佳填法：将 `not being used` 看完整，空格处不填（但题目要求每空必填），因此 `in` 表示"正在使用中"的补充表达。

6. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`electricity` 为不可数名词，用 `less`。

7. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`long` 的比较级为 `longer`。

8. **答案：has dropped**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`recently` 提示用现在完成时，表示近期发生的变化。

9. **答案：instead**
   - **考点**：副词。`instead` 表示"作为替代"，此处括号内给出 `instead` 无需变形。

10. **答案：comfortable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`more comfortable than` 构成比较级，`comfort` 变为 `comfortable`。
