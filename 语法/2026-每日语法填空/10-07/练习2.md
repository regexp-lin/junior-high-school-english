# 10-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：果园采摘 (Fruit Picking at an Orchard)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Fruit picking has become a popular weekend activity for families in China. Last Sunday, my family drove to an orchard in the suburbs (1) ______ (pick) strawberries. The orchard was much (2) ______ (big) than I had imagined, with rows and rows of strawberry plants stretching across the fields. A friendly worker at the entrance gave each of us a basket and (3) ______ (explain) the rules. She told us to only pick the ripe, red (4) ______ (one) and to be careful not to step on the plants. I bent down and started picking. The strawberries smelled (5) ______ (wonder), and I couldn't resist eating a few right away. They were much (6) ______ (sweet) than the ones sold in supermarkets. My little sister enjoyed (7) ______ (she) so much that she didn't want to leave. She ran between the rows, (8) ______ (laugh) and filling her basket with the biggest strawberries she could find. By noon, we had collected three full baskets. We paid (9) ______ the strawberries by weight and also bought some fresh orange juice. On the drive home, everyone agreed that picking fruit (10) ______ (be) much more fun than simply buying it from a shop.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to pick**
   - **考点**：不定式。`drove to an orchard to pick strawberries`，不定式表目的。

2. **答案：bigger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much bigger than`。

3. **答案：explained**
   - **考点**：时态。与 `gave` 并列，用一般过去时。

4. **答案：ones**
   - **考点**：代词。`the ripe, red ones` 指代 `strawberries`，用 `ones` 代替复数名词。

5. **答案：wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`smelled wonderful`，系动词后接形容词，`wonder` 变为 `wonderful`。

6. **答案：sweeter**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much sweeter than`。

7. **答案：herself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`enjoyed herself`，主语为 `My little sister`。

8. **答案：laughing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`ran between the rows, laughing and filling...`，现在分词作伴随状语。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`pay for` 表示"付钱买"。

10. **答案：was**
    - **考点**：时态。`agreed that picking fruit was...`，宾语从句时态与主句一致，用过去时。
