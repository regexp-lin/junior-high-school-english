# 10-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：交换生经历 (Exchange Student Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last year, our school welcomed an exchange student (1) ______ (name) David from the United Kingdom. He spent three months studying with us and staying with a host family. At first, David found it (2) ______ (challenge) to adjust to life in China. The food, the language and even the way people greeted each other were all different (3) ______ what he was used to. But he was very open-minded and eager (4) ______ (learn). He started taking Chinese lessons and could introduce (5) ______ (he) in simple Chinese within two weeks. In class, David was always active and asked many (6) ______ (question). He was especially interested in Chinese history and often had long discussions (7) ______ our history teacher after class. He also joined the school football team, where he quickly made friends with his (8) ______ (teammate). During the Spring Festival, his host family invited him to make dumplings together. He said it was one of the (9) ______ (happy) experiences of his life. When it was time for David to leave, many students were sad. He promised (10) ______ (come) back one day and said that China had become his second home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a student named David`，过去分词作后置定语。

2. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found it challenging`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

3. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`different from` 表示"与……不同"。

4. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：不定式。`eager to learn` 表示"渴望学习"。

5. **答案：himself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`introduce himself`，主语为 `He`。

6. **答案：questions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many questions`。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`have discussions with sb` 表示"与某人讨论"。

8. **答案：teammates**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`his teammates`，队友不止一个。

9. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the happiest experiences`。

10. **答案：to come**
    - **考点**：不定式。`promise to do sth` 表示"承诺做某事"。
