# 11-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：音乐教育 (Music Education)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Music plays an important role in education. Research has shown that students who learn to play a musical instrument tend to perform (1) ______ (well) in other subjects, especially maths and languages. Music (2) ______ (train) helps develop concentration, memory and creativity, which are all essential skills for learning. In our school, every student is required (3) ______ (choose) at least one musical activity. Some students join the choir, while (4) ______ prefer learning instruments such as the piano, violin or flute. I chose to learn the guitar two years ago. At first, I found it very painful because pressing the strings made my (5) ______ (finger) sore. I almost gave up after the first month. However, my music teacher, Mr Chen, (6) ______ (patient) encouraged me to keep going. He told me that every (7) ______ (success) musician had gone through the same struggle. Gradually, I became more skilled, and now I can play several songs quite (8) ______ (fluent). Last month, I performed at the school concert for the first time. I was nervous at first, but once I started (9) ______ (play), I forgot about the audience and just enjoyed the music. The experience taught me that anything is possible (10) ______ you are willing to put in the effort.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`perform better in other subjects`，暗含与不学音乐的学生比较，`well` 的比较级为 `better`。

2. **答案：training**
   - **考点**：动名词作定语。`Music training` 表示"音乐训练"，此处 `train` 变为名词/动名词。

3. **答案：to choose**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be required to do sth` 表示"被要求做某事"。

4. **答案：others**
   - **考点**：代词。`some...while others...` 为固定搭配，表示"一些……另一些……"。

5. **答案：fingers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。手指有多个，需要用复数形式。

6. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `encouraged` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：successful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `musician` 需要形容词。

8. **答案：fluently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `play` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：playing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`start doing sth` 表示"开始做某事"。

10. **答案：if**
    - **考点**：连词。`if you are willing to...` 引导条件状语从句，表示"如果"。
