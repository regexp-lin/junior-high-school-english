# 11-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：环境保护 (Environmental Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Protecting the environment is the duty of every citizen. With the rapid development of industry, pollution (1) ______ (become) a serious problem in many parts of the world. Rivers are polluted by factory waste, and the air in big cities is often filled (2) ______ harmful gases. These problems not only damage nature but also threaten human (3) ______ (healthy). Our school has been actively promoting environmental (4) ______ (aware) among students. Last semester, we launched a campaign (5) ______ (call) "Go Green Together." Students were encouraged to bring (6) ______ (they) own water bottles instead of buying plastic ones, and to use both sides of paper when writing. We also organised a tree-planting activity in a nearby park, (7) ______ we planted over 200 saplings in just one afternoon. The most creative part of the campaign was a contest where students turned old materials (8) ______ useful items. One group made a beautiful lamp from empty bottles, and another group created a bookshelf from cardboard boxes. These projects showed that many things we throw away can (9) ______ (actual) be reused. I believe that if everyone makes small changes in their daily life, we can make a big difference (10) ______ the environment.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。表示过去发生并对现在有影响的动作。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be filled with` 表示"充满"。

3. **答案：health**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`human health`，修饰语为 `human`，需要名词 `health`。

4. **答案：awareness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`environmental awareness`，形容词后接名词，`aware` 变为 `awareness`。

5. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a campaign called "Go Green Together"`，被动关系。

6. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 `water bottles` 需要形容词性物主代词。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导非限制性定语从句，先行词 `park` 为地点，用 `where`。

8. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`turn...into...` 表示"把……变成……"。

9. **答案：actually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `be reused` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：介词。`make a difference to` 表示"对……产生影响"。
