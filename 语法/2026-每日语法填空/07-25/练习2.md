# 07-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：照顾生病的妈妈 (Taking Care of My Sick Mum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Wednesday, my mother caught a bad cold and had to stay in bed. My father was away on a business trip, so it was up to me to take care of (1) ______ (she). When I woke up in the morning, I found her forehead was burning hot. I (2) ______ (immediate) gave her some medicine and a glass of warm water. Then I went to the kitchen and tried (3) ______ (cook) porridge for her. It was the (4) ______ (one) time I had cooked by myself, and I was quite nervous. I put too much water in at first, so the porridge turned out very thin. But my mother still ate it (5) ______ said it was delicious, which made me feel (6) ______ (warm) inside. After breakfast, I washed the dishes and cleaned the house. In the afternoon, I went to the pharmacy (7) ______ (buy) more medicine. The pharmacist asked about my mother's symptoms and recommended the right medicine. By evening, her fever had gone down and she looked much (8) ______ (good). She hugged me and said, "When did you grow up so (9) ______?" I smiled and said, "I just want to take care of you (10) ______ the way you always take care of me."

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`take care of` 后接宾语，`she` 的宾格为 `her`。

2. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `gave` 需要副词，`immediate` 变为 `immediately`。

3. **答案：to cook**
   - **考点**：不定式。`try to do sth` 表示"努力/尝试去做某事"。

4. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。`the first time` 表示"第一次"，`one` 的序数词为 `first`。

5. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`ate it and said` 两个动作是顺承关系，用 `and` 连接。

6. **答案：warm**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel warm` 中 `feel` 为系动词，后接形容词 `warm`，注意括号内给的词做比较级变形不合语境，此处就用原级。

7. **答案：to buy**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。去药房的目的是买药，用 `to buy`。

8. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`much better` 表示"好多了"，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

9. **答案：fast / quickly**
   - **考点**：副词。`grow up so fast/quickly` 表示"长大得这么快"，此处为无提示词填空。

10. **答案：in**
    - **考点**：介词。`in the way` 表示"以……的方式"。
