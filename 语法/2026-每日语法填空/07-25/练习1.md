# 07-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：二手书店 (The Second-Hand Bookshop)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

There is a small second-hand bookshop on a quiet street near my school. It (1) ______ (run) by an old man called Uncle Chen. Unlike modern bookstores, his shop is not big or fancy, but it is full (2) ______ treasures. The shelves are packed with (3) ______ (thousand) of old books, from classic novels to science magazines. Every book has its own story — some have notes (4) ______ (write) in the margins, and others have faded covers that show years of use. I love (5) ______ (visit) Uncle Chen's shop on Saturday afternoons. He always recommends books to me based on (6) ______ he thinks I will enjoy. Last week, he handed me a copy of "The Old Man and the Sea" and said it would teach me about (7) ______ (brave) and never giving up. I bought it for only five yuan, (8) ______ was much cheaper than a new copy. Some people say printed books will (9) ______ (appear) in the digital age, but I disagree. Holding a real book and turning its pages is a (10) ______ (please) that a screen can never replace.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is run**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。书店"被经营"，描述现在的状态。

2. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`be full of` 为固定搭配，表示"充满"。

3. **答案：thousands**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`thousands of` 表示"数以千计的"，`thousand` 用复数。

4. **答案：written**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`notes written in the margins` 表示"写在页边的笔记"，过去分词表被动。

5. **答案：visiting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`love doing sth` 表示"喜欢做某事"。

6. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。引导宾语从句，`what he thinks I will enjoy` 表示"他认为我会喜欢的书"，`what` 作 `enjoy` 的宾语。

7. **答案：bravery**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`about` 后接名词，`brave` 变为 `bravery`（勇气）。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面的价格（五元），在从句中作主语。

9. **答案：disappear**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`will` 后接动词原形，`appear` 加前缀 `dis-` 变为 `disappear`（消失）。

10. **答案：pleasure**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`a` 后接名词，`please` 变为 `pleasure`（乐趣）。
