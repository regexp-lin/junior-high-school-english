# 08-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校运动会 (School Sports Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school sports day is one of the events that students look forward to (1) ______ (much). It takes place in November every year and (2) ______ (last) for two days. This year, I signed up for the 800-metre race and the long jump. I had been (3) ______ (train) hard for weeks before the competition. On the day of the race, the weather was perfect — sunny (4) ______ not too hot. When the starting gun fired, I ran as (5) ______ (fast) as I could. There were twelve (6) ______ (runner) in total, and I managed to finish in third place. I was very proud of (7) ______ (I), even though I didn't win first place. My classmates cheered for me enthusiastically, (8) ______ made me feel warm and happy. In the afternoon, I watched my friend Wang Lei compete in the high jump. He jumped 1.65 metres, (9) ______ (set) a new school record. The sports day reminded us that taking part is more important than (10) ______ (win). It was a day full of excitement and team spirit.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most**
   - **考点**：副词最高级。`look forward to most` 表示"最期待"，`much` 的最高级为 `most`。

2. **答案：lasts**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。主语为 `It`（单数），描述每年惯例用一般现在时，`last` 加 `-s`。

3. **答案：training**
   - **考点**：时态。`had been training` 为过去完成进行时，表示比赛前一直在训练。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`sunny but not too hot` 表示"晴朗但不太热"，`but` 表转折。

5. **答案：fast**
   - **考点**：原级比较。`as fast as I could` 为同级比较结构，用原形。

6. **答案：runners**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`twelve runners`，数词后接可数名词复数。

7. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`be proud of myself`，主语为 `I`，用反身代词。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事。

9. **答案：setting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`setting a new school record` 作结果状语，表自然而然的结果。

10. **答案：winning**
    - **考点**：动名词。`than` 是介词（此处），后接动名词 `winning`；或理解为 `than winning` 与 `taking part` 并列。
