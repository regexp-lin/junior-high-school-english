# 08-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑期支教 (Summer Volunteer Teaching)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, I joined a volunteer programme and spent two weeks (1) ______ (teach) English in a small village in Guizhou Province. The school there was old but clean, and the children were eager (2) ______ (learn). Most of them had never met a person from a big city before, so they asked me all (3) ______ (kind) of questions about my life. I prepared my lessons (4) ______ (careful) every evening because I wanted to make each class interesting and useful. One of the (5) ______ (big) challenges was the language barrier. Some younger students could only speak their local dialect and found (6) ______ difficult to understand standard Mandarin. I used pictures, songs and simple games to help (7) ______ (they) learn basic English words. By the end of the two weeks, the children could introduce (8) ______ (them) in English and sing several English songs. On the last day, they gave me handmade cards (9) ______ said "Thank you, Teacher!" The experience made me (10) ______ (realise) how lucky I am to have good educational resources, and it also inspired me to help more people in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：teaching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`spend time (in) doing sth` 是固定搭配，用动名词 `teaching`。

2. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be eager to do sth` 表示"渴望做某事"。

3. **答案：kinds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`all kinds of` 表示"各种各样的"，`kind` 用复数。

4. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `prepared` 需要副词，`careful` 变为 `carefully`。

5. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the biggest challenges` 表示"最大的挑战之一"。

6. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：形式宾语。`found it difficult to understand...`，`it` 作形式宾语，真正的宾语是后面的不定式。

7. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`help them learn`，动词 `help` 后接宾格 `them`。

8. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`introduce themselves` 表示"自我介绍"，主语为 `the children`。

9. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `cards`（物），引导定语从句用 `that` 或 `which`。

10. **答案：realise**
    - **考点**：使役动词。`make sb do sth` 中 `do` 用原形，`realise` 保持原形。
