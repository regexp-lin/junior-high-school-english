# 10-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：运动与健康 (Exercise and Health)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Regular exercise is one of the best things we can do for our (1) ______ (healthy). Doctors recommend that teenagers get at least 60 minutes of physical activity every day. However, a recent study found that many Chinese students are not (2) ______ (activity) enough because they spend too much time studying and sitting at desks. Last year, I noticed that I was gaining weight and feeling tired all the time. My parents were worried about (3) ______ and suggested I start exercising. I began by jogging for 20 minutes every morning before school. At first, it was hard to get up early, (4) ______ after a few weeks, it became a habit. I also joined the school swimming team, which practises three times (5) ______ week. Swimming is a great exercise because it works every muscle in the body (6) ______ putting too much stress on the joints. My coach, Mr. Li, has been teaching us different (7) ______ (stroke) and breathing techniques. Since I started exercising (8) ______ (regular), my health has improved greatly. I have more energy, sleep (9) ______ (good) and can concentrate better in class. I no longer need my afternoon nap to get through the day. I strongly recommend that every student (10) ______ (find) a sport they enjoy and make exercise a part of their daily routine.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：health**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`our health`，物主代词后接名词，`healthy` 变为 `health`。

2. **答案：active**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`are not active enough`，系动词后接形容词。

3. **答案：me**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`worried about me`，介词后接宾格。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`was hard at first but became a habit`，前后转折。

5. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`three times a week` 表示"每周三次"。

6. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`without putting too much stress`，表示"不会造成太大压力"。

7. **答案：strokes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different strokes` 表示"不同的泳姿"。

8. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `exercising` 需要副词。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`sleep better`，`well` 的比较级为 `better`。

10. **答案：find**
    - **考点**：虚拟语气。`recommend that sb (should) do sth`，从句用动词原形。
