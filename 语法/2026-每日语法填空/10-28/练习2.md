# 10-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：微信群 (WeChat Groups)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

WeChat groups have become an essential part of communication in China. Nearly everyone belongs (1) ______ at least one group — family groups, class groups, work groups and hobby groups. Our class has a WeChat group (2) ______ our teacher posts homework and important notices. It is very convenient because we can check the information at any time. My parents also have a family group (3) ______ (call) "Happy Family" where relatives share photos, news and holiday (4) ______ (greet). Every morning, my grandmother sends a "Good morning" message with a flower picture. Some relatives think it's a bit old-fashioned, (5) ______ nobody has the heart to tell her. However, WeChat groups can also cause problems. Some people send too (6) ______ (many) messages, making it hard to find important information. In our class group, students sometimes chat about things (7) ______ are not related to school, which annoys our teacher. Last week, she set new rules for the group: only school-related messages are allowed, and students must be (8) ______ (respect) when communicating. I think these rules are (9) ______ (reason). WeChat groups are useful tools, but like all tools, they need to be used (10) ______ (proper) to be effective.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`belong to` 为固定搭配。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a WeChat group`，从句中缺地点/场所状语。

3. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a group called "Happy Family"`，过去分词作后置定语。

4. **答案：greetings**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`holiday greetings` 表示"节日祝福"。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前后转折。

6. **答案：many**
   - **考点**：形容词。`too many messages`，`messages` 为可数名词复数，用 `many`。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `things`（物），引导定语从句。

8. **答案：respectful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be respectful`，系动词后接形容词，`respect` 变为 `respectful`。

9. **答案：reasonable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`are reasonable`，`reason` 变为 `reasonable`。

10. **答案：properly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `used` 需要副词，`proper` 变为 `properly`。
