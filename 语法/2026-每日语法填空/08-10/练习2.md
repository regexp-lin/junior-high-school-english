# 08-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：跳蚤市场 (The Flea Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our school held a flea market in the playground. Students could sell things they no (1) ______ needed, such as old clothes, toys, and books. Each class was given a table, and everyone helped to set up their stall. My class decided (2) ______ (sell) second-hand books and hand-made bookmarks. I was in charge of (3) ______ (price) the items. We set most books at three to five yuan, which was much (4) ______ (cheap) than their original price. The market opened at two in the afternoon, and (5) ______ the first hour, we had already sold half of our books. A girl from another class bought five novels and said she was (6) ______ (delight) to find so many good reads at such low prices. My classmate Li Hua attracted many (7) ______ (customer) with his loud and funny announcements. He was (8) ______ a good salesman that I thought he should consider a career in marketing! By four o'clock, almost everything was sold. The money (9) ______ (raise) was donated to a local charity. The flea market taught us an important lesson: one person's unwanted item can become another person's (10) ______ (value) treasure.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`no longer` 表示"不再"。

2. **答案：to sell**
   - **考点**：不定式。`decide to do sth` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：pricing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`in charge of` 中 `of` 为介词，后接动名词。

4. **答案：cheaper**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级。

5. **答案：within / in**
   - **考点**：介词。`within/in the first hour` 表示"在第一个小时内"。

6. **答案：delighted**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was delighted to` 表示"很高兴地"，`delight` 变为 `delighted`（感到高兴的）。

7. **答案：customers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many` 修饰可数名词复数。

8. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定结构。`such a good salesman that...` 为 `such...that...` 结构。

9. **答案：raised**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`the money raised` 表示"筹集到的钱"。

10. **答案：valuable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `treasure` 需要形容词，`value` 变为 `valuable`（有价值的）。
