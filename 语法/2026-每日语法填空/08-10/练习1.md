# 08-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：传统手工艺 (Traditional Handicrafts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has a rich tradition of handicrafts, from paper cutting to porcelain making. These arts (1) ______ (develop) over centuries and reflect the wisdom of Chinese people. One of the most well-known handicrafts is paper cutting. In the past, almost every family would decorate their windows with red paper cuts during Spring Festival. The (2) ______ (design) were usually animals, flowers, or characters that symbolised good luck. My grandmother is (3) ______ (skill) at paper cutting. With just a pair of scissors and a piece of red paper, she can create (4) ______ (amaze) works of art in minutes. She has been doing this since she (5) ______ (be) a young girl. Last year, she taught me (6) ______ to make a simple butterfly pattern. It looked easy when she did it, but my version was far (7) ______ (bad) than hers. I cut too deeply and the paper fell apart. She laughed and told me that practice was the only secret (8) ______ mastering any craft. Today, fewer young people are interested in (9) ______ (tradition) handicrafts, which worries many cultural experts. I think schools should offer classes on these arts (10) ______ that they are not lost to future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have been developed / have developed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时（被动/主动）。`over centuries` 表示从过去到现在的持续过程。

2. **答案：designs**
   - **考点**：名词复数。主语需要名词，`design` 本身可作名词，用复数表示多种图案。

3. **答案：skilful / skillful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be skilful at` 表示"擅长"，`skill` 变为 `skilful`。

4. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `works` 需要形容词，`amaze` 变为 `amazing`（令人惊叹的）。

5. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`since she was a young girl` 表示过去的时间点。

6. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`teach sb how to do sth` 表示"教某人如何做某事"。

7. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than hers` 提示用比较级，`bad` 的比较级为 `worse`。

8. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`the secret to doing sth` 表示"做某事的秘诀"，`to` 为介词。

9. **答案：traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `handicrafts` 需要形容词，`tradition` 变为 `traditional`。

10. **答案：so**
    - **考点**：连词。`so that` 引导目的状语从句，表示"以便"。
