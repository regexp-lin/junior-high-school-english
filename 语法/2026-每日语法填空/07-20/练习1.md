# 07-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国功夫 (Chinese Kung Fu)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese kung fu, also known (1) ______ martial arts, has a history of several thousand years. It includes many different styles, such as tai chi, wing chun, and Shaolin boxing. In the past, people learned kung fu (2) ______ (main) for self-defence. Today, it is practised both as a sport and as a way to keep (3) ______ (fitly). Millions of people around the world (4) ______ (take) up kung fu in recent years, thanks to popular movies by Bruce Lee and Jackie Chan. These films made Chinese kung fu (5) ______ (know) to people in every corner of the globe. My cousin has been learning Shaolin boxing (6) ______ he was eight. He trains four times a week at a local martial arts school. The training is very tough — he has to get up at five and practise basic moves (7) ______ (repeat) for hours. But he never complains. He says kung fu teaches him not only physical (8) ______ (strong) but also discipline and respect. Last year, he won first prize in a provincial competition, (9) ______ made the whole family proud. I admire his dedication and hope to start learning kung fu (10) ______ (me).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be known as` 表示"被称为"，也可说 `also known as`（也被称为）。

2. **答案：mainly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词短语 `learned kung fu` 需要副词，`main` 变为 `mainly`。

3. **答案：fit**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`keep fit` 表示"保持健康"，`fit` 为形容词；括号内 `fitly` 应变为 `fit`。

4. **答案：have taken**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`in recent years` 提示用现在完成时，主语 `Millions of people` 为复数。

5. **答案：known**
   - **考点**：过去分词作宾补。`make sth known to sb` 表示"使某事为某人所知"。

6. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since he was eight` 与现在完成进行时搭配，表示"自从……以来"。

7. **答案：repeatedly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `practise` 需要副词，`repeat` 变为 `repeatedly`（反复地）。

8. **答案：strength**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`physical` 修饰名词，`strong` 变为名词 `strength`（力量）。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代他获得第一名这件事，在从句中作主语。

10. **答案：myself**
    - **考点**：反身代词。此处 `myself` 为 `by myself` 的简略表达或强调"我自己也"，也可理解为强调代词。
