# 07-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：城市绿化 (Urban Greening)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As cities grow larger, green spaces become more and more important. Trees and parks provide clean air, reduce noise, and give people a place (1) ______ (relax). However, in many cities, buildings have replaced parks, and concrete has taken (2) ______ place of grass. This has led to problems such as the urban heat island effect, (3) ______ means city centres are several degrees hotter than the surrounding countryside. To deal with this, many cities (4) ______ (begin) large-scale greening projects. Rooftop gardens are being built on top of office buildings, and vertical forests — tall buildings (5) ______ (cover) with trees — are appearing in some cities. In my neighbourhood, the government recently turned an old car park (6) ______ a small public garden. Now, elderly people go there every morning to do exercise, and children play on the grass after school. The garden has also become home (7) ______ several kinds of birds. My mother says it is the (8) ______ (good) change our neighbourhood has seen in years. I think every city should have more green spaces. They make the air (9) ______ (fresh) and people's lives (10) ______ (happy).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to relax**
   - **考点**：不定式作后置定语。`a place to relax` 表示"一个放松的地方"。

2. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。`take the place of` 为固定搭配，表示"取代"。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `the urban heat island effect`，在从句中作主语。

4. **答案：have begun**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。描述城市已经开始的绿化工程，表示从过去到现在的行动。

5. **答案：covered**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`buildings covered with trees` 表示"被树木覆盖的建筑"，过去分词表被动。

6. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`turn...into...` 为固定搭配，表示"把……变成……"。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`become home to` 表示"成为……的家园"。

8. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词`，`good` 的最高级为 `best`。

9. **答案：fresher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`make the air fresher` 表示"使空气更清新"，隐含与之前的对比。

10. **答案：happier**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。与 `fresher` 并列，表示"使人们的生活更幸福"。
