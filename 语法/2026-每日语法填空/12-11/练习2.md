# 12-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：古代丝绸之路 (The Ancient Silk Road)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Silk Road was an ancient network of trade routes that connected China (1) ______ the Mediterranean Sea. It got its name from the silk that Chinese (2) ______ (merchant) sold to traders from Central Asia, the Middle East and Europe. However, silk was just one of many goods (3) ______ (trade) along this route. Spices, tea, porcelain and precious metals also flowed back and forth between East and West. The Silk Road was not just about trade — it was also a channel for cultural exchange. Through the Silk Road, Buddhism (4) ______ (spread) from India to China, and Chinese inventions like paper and gunpowder reached Europe. Different peoples shared ideas about art, science and (5) ______ (medicine) knowledge. The journey along the Silk Road was long and (6) ______ (danger). Traders had to cross vast deserts, climb high mountain passes and face the risk of bandits. The trip from Xi'an to Rome could take several years. Despite these (7) ______ (difficult), the Silk Road remained active for over 1,500 years. In recent years, China has proposed the "Belt and Road Initiative," which aims (8) ______ (revive) the spirit of the ancient Silk Road by building modern trade and infrastructure links between countries. This initiative (9) ______ (already connect) over 140 nations. I find it (10) ______ (fascinate) that an idea from thousands of years ago still inspires cooperation between countries today.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`connected China to/with the Mediterranean Sea`。

2. **答案：merchants**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`Chinese merchants`。

3. **答案：traded**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`goods traded along this route`，被动关系。

4. **答案：spread**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述历史事件，`spread` 的过去式仍为 `spread`。

5. **答案：medical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `knowledge` 需要形容词。

6. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `long` 并列，需要形容词。

7. **答案：difficulties**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`these difficulties`，指多种困难。

8. **答案：to revive**
   - **考点**：不定式。`aim to do sth` 表示"旨在做某事"。

9. **答案：has already connected**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`already` 提示用现在完成时。

10. **答案：fascinating**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`find it fascinating`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。
