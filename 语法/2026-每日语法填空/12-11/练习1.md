# 12-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：手写与打字 (Handwriting vs Typing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

With the widespread use of computers and smartphones, many people now type more than they write by hand. Some educators worry that handwriting is becoming a (1) ______ (lose) art. A recent survey found that 65% of teenagers could not write (2) ______ (neat) because they rarely practised handwriting outside of school. However, research suggests that handwriting has several advantages (3) ______ typing. Studies show that students who take notes by hand remember the information (4) ______ (well) than those who type their notes. This is because writing by hand (5) ______ (require) more mental effort, which helps the brain process and store information more (6) ______ (effective). Handwriting is especially important in China because Chinese characters are complex and require precise strokes. My Chinese teacher often says that handwriting is a window (7) ______ a person's character. She requires us to practise calligraphy for fifteen minutes every day. At first, many students (8) ______ (complain) that it was a waste of time. But over the months, our handwriting has improved (9) ______ (significant), and some students have even developed a genuine interest in calligraphy. Of course, typing is also an essential skill in today's digital world. The key is to find a balance (10) ______ the two. We should embrace technology without abandoning the art of handwriting.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：lost**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a lost art` 表示"一门失落的艺术"。

2. **答案：neatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `write` 需要副词。

3. **答案：over**
   - **考点**：介词。`advantages over typing` 表示"优于打字的优势"。

4. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than those who type` 提示比较级，`well` 的比较级。

5. **答案：requires**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`writing by hand` 为动名词短语作主语，视为第三人称单数。

6. **答案：effectively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `process and store` 需要副词。

7. **答案：into / to**
   - **考点**：介词。`a window into/to a person's character` 表示"了解一个人性格的窗口"。

8. **答案：complained**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`At first` 提示最初阶段的过去事件。

9. **答案：significantly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词。

10. **答案：between**
    - **考点**：介词。`a balance between the two` 表示"两者之间的平衡"。
