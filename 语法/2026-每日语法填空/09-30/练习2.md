# 09-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：自动售货机 (Vending Machines)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Vending machines have become a common sight in Chinese cities. You can find them in subway stations, shopping malls, hospitals and even (1) ______ schools. They sell everything from drinks and snacks (2) ______ fresh fruit and hot meals. Our school recently installed two vending machines near the playground. Students can buy water and healthy snacks between (3) ______ (class). The machines are easy (4) ______ (use) — you just scan a QR code with your phone and select what you want. My friend Li Wei thinks they are very (5) ______ (convenience) because he doesn't have to go all the way to the school shop during the short break. However, not everyone is happy about the machines. Some parents worry that students will spend too much money (6) ______ unnecessary snacks. Our head teacher (7) ______ (address) this concern by making sure the machines only stock healthy options — no sweets, no fizzy drinks and no junk food. She also set a daily spending limit (8) ______ each student's account. I think this is a (9) ______ (reason) approach. Vending machines can be helpful (10) ______ long as they are managed properly.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in schools` 表示"在学校里"。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from drinks and snacks to fresh fruit` 表示"从……到……"。

3. **答案：classes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`between classes` 表示"课间"。

4. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be easy to use`，`be + adj + to do` 结构。

5. **答案：convenient**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`are very convenient`，系动词后接形容词，`convenience` 变为 `convenient`。

6. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`spend money on sth` 表示"花钱在某物上"。

7. **答案：addressed / has addressed**
   - **考点**：时态。用一般过去时或现在完成时均可。

8. **答案：for / on**
   - **考点**：介词。`a spending limit for/on each student's account` 表示"对每个学生账户的消费限额"。

9. **答案：reasonable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `approach` 需要形容词，`reason` 变为 `reasonable`。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`as long as` 表示"只要"。
