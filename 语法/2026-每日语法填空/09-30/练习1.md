# 09-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：戏曲进校园 (Traditional Opera in Schools)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

To help young people learn about traditional Chinese culture, many schools have started "Opera into Campus" programmes. Last term, a Peking Opera troupe (1) ______ (invite) to perform at our school. Before the show, most of us knew very (2) ______ (little) about Peking Opera. We thought it was something only old people enjoyed. However, the performance completely changed (3) ______ (we) minds. The actors wore (4) ______ (colour) costumes and elaborate face paint. Their movements were graceful and powerful at the same time. The lead actress played the role of Hua Mulan, the legendary female warrior who disguised (5) ______ (she) as a man to fight in her father's place. Her singing voice was so (6) ______ (impress) that the whole audience was amazed. After the show, the actors held a workshop and taught us some basic (7) ______ (move). We learned how to walk, gesture and even sing a simple melody. I found it much (8) ______ (hard) than it looked! The experience helped me understand (9) ______ Peking Opera has been treasured as a national art form for over 200 years. I now have a much (10) ______ (great) respect for this ancient art.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was invited**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`a troupe was invited to perform`，"被邀请"。

2. **答案：little**
   - **考点**：副词。`knew very little about`，`little` 修饰动词 `knew` 表"几乎不了解"。

3. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`changed our minds`，物主代词修饰名词。

4. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `costumes` 需要形容词，`colour` 变为 `colourful`。

5. **答案：herself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`disguised herself as a man`，主语为 `who`（指 Hua Mulan，女性）。

6. **答案：impressive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so impressive that...`，`impress` 变为形容词 `impressive`。

7. **答案：movements**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`basic movements`，`move` 变为名词 `movement`，复数。

8. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much harder than it looked`。

9. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`understand why Peking Opera has been treasured...`，`why` 引导宾语从句。

10. **答案：greater**
    - **考点**：比较级。`a much greater respect`，`much` 修饰比较级。
