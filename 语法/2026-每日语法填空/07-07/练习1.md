# 07-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冰淇淋的故事 (The Story of Ice Cream)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Almost everyone loves ice cream, (1) ______ few people know its long history. The earliest form of ice cream (2) ______ (make) in China about 2,000 years ago. People mixed snow with fruit juice and honey to create a frozen treat. Later, the idea (3) ______ (bring) to Europe by travellers. In the 17th century, Italian chefs developed recipes that were (4) ______ (close) to the ice cream we eat today. Nowadays, there are hundreds of (5) ______ (flavour) to choose from, such as chocolate, strawberry, and green tea. The (6) ______ (popular) of ice cream continues to grow, especially in summer. However, eating too (7) ______ ice cream is not good for health. It contains a lot of sugar, which can lead to weight gain (8) ______ tooth problems. Doctors advise us (9) ______ (enjoy) it in moderation. My favourite flavour is mango. Every time I eat a scoop on a hot day, I feel as if the whole world becomes (10) ______ (cool).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but / yet**
   - **考点**：转折连词。"几乎人人爱冰淇淋"与"很少人了解其历史"为转折关系。

2. **答案：was made**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。最早的冰淇淋"被制作"于中国，`about 2,000 years ago` 表示过去。

3. **答案：was brought**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。这个想法"被带到"欧洲，主语 `the idea` 为单数。

4. **答案：closer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。隐含与早期冰淇淋的对比，"更接近"现代冰淇淋，用 `closer`。

5. **答案：flavours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`hundreds of` 修饰可数名词复数，`flavour` 变为 `flavours`。

6. **答案：popularity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词作主语，`popular` 变为 `popularity`（受欢迎程度）。

7. **答案：much**
   - **考点**：形容词/限定词。`ice cream` 为不可数名词，用 `much` 修饰，表示"太多"。

8. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`weight gain` 和 `tooth problems` 为并列关系，用 `and` 连接。

9. **答案：to enjoy**
   - **考点**：不定式。`advise sb to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"建议某人做某事"。

10. **答案：cooler**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`becomes cooler` 表示"变得更凉爽"，隐含与吃冰淇淋前的对比。
