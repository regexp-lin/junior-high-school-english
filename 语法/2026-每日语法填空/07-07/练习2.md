# 07-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：我的榜样 (My Role Model)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My role model is my elder sister, Chen Yue. She is a college student (1) ______ studies computer science. What I admire most about her is her (2) ______ (determine) to overcome difficulties. When she was in middle school, she was not good at maths at all. Instead of (3) ______ (give) up, she spent every evening working on extra problems. (4) ______ (slow), her scores improved, and she finally got the (5) ______ (high) mark in the final exam. Her story taught me that talent is less important (6) ______ hard work. Now she is learning to build apps and has already (7) ______ (win) two prizes in programming contests. She often tells me (8) ______ to manage my time, such as making a daily plan and (9) ______ (finish) the hardest tasks first. I want to follow in her footsteps. I believe that if I keep (10) ______ (try), I can achieve my goals too.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a college student`（人），在定语从句中作主语，用 `who` 或 `that`。

2. **答案：determination**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`her` 后接名词，`determine` 变为 `determination`（决心）。

3. **答案：giving**
   - **考点**：动名词。`instead of` 中 `of` 为介词，后接动名词 `giving`。

4. **答案：Slowly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰整个句子需要副词，`slow` 变为 `slowly`（慢慢地），句首首字母大写。

5. **答案：highest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词`，`high` 的最高级为 `highest`。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较连词。`less important than` 构成比较级结构，表示"不如……重要"。

7. **答案：won**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`has already` 提示用现在完成时，`win` 的过去分词为 `won`。

8. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`tell sb how to do sth` 表示"告诉某人如何做某事"。

9. **答案：finishing**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `making a daily plan` 并列，用动名词 `finishing`。

10. **答案：trying**
    - **考点**：动名词。`keep doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"坚持做某事"。
