# 09-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：班级黑板报 (Class Blackboard Newspaper)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In Chinese schools, blackboard newspapers are a common and (1) ______ (colour) tradition. Every month, each class is asked to design a new one on the back blackboard. Our class takes this task very (2) ______ (serious). Last week, it was our turn to create a blackboard newspaper on the theme of "Dreams and Goals". Our monitor, Li Fang, (3) ______ (choose) as the team leader. She divided us into three groups: one for writing articles, one for drawing (4) ______ (illustrate), and one for designing the layout. I was in the writing group. We discussed (5) ______ topics to include and decided on three: "How to set goals", "Famous people and their dreams" and "Our class dreams for the future". I wrote the article about famous people, (6) ______ (use) stories of scientists and athletes as examples. The drawing group created beautiful pictures of stars and rockets to match the theme. It took us two afternoons (7) ______ (finish) everything. When the blackboard newspaper was ready, our head teacher came to check it. She was very (8) ______ (please) with the result and said it was the (9) ______ (good) one she had seen this year. Her praise made all of (10) ______ feel proud. Creating the blackboard newspaper together strengthened our teamwork and class spirit.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `common` 并列修饰 `tradition`，需要形容词，`colour` 变为 `colourful`。

2. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `takes` 需要副词，`serious` 变为 `seriously`。

3. **答案：was chosen**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`Li Fang was chosen as the team leader`，"被选为"。

4. **答案：illustrations**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`drawing illustrations`，`illustrate` 变为名词 `illustration`，复数形式。

5. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`discussed what topics to include`，`what` 修饰 `topics`，引导宾语从句。

6. **答案：using**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`using stories...as examples`，现在分词作伴随状语。

7. **答案：to finish**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It took us two afternoons to finish...`，`It takes sb time to do sth`。

8. **答案：pleased**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was very pleased`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

9. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the best one she had seen`。

10. **答案：us**
    - **考点**：代词宾格。`all of us`，介词后接宾格。
