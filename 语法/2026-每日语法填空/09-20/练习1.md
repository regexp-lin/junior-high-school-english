# 09-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：古镇游览 (Visiting an Ancient Town)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the National Day holiday, my family visited Zhouzhuang, one of the most famous ancient (1) ______ (town) in southern China. The town is over 900 years old and is known (2) ______ its beautiful canals and traditional architecture. As we walked along the narrow stone streets, we could see old houses (3) ______ (build) in the Ming and Qing dynasties. Wooden boats moved slowly through the canals, (4) ______ (carry) tourists who wanted to enjoy the scenery from the water. We hired a boat and spent an hour floating through the town. The boatman sang a local folk song (5) ______ a soft voice, which added a special charm to the experience. For lunch, we tried the town's famous wansan pork, a dish that (6) ______ (cook) for over eight hours until it becomes extremely (7) ______ (tenderness). It was the most (8) ______ (deliciously) pork I had ever tasted. After lunch, we visited a small museum that displayed traditional farming tools and household items. I was interested (9) ______ how people lived centuries ago without modern technology. The trip gave me a (10) ______ (deep) appreciation for China's rich cultural heritage.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：towns**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most famous ancient towns`。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be known for` 表示"因……而闻名"。

3. **答案：built**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`houses built in the Ming and Qing dynasties`，过去分词作后置定语，表被动。

4. **答案：carrying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`carrying tourists`，现在分词作伴随状语。

5. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in a soft voice` 表示"用柔和的声音"。

6. **答案：is cooked**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`a dish that is cooked for over eight hours`，描述烹饪方法用一般现在时被动。

7. **答案：tender**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`becomes extremely tender`，系动词后接形容词，`tenderness` 变为 `tender`。

8. **答案：delicious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most delicious pork`，修饰名词用形容词，`deliciously` 变为 `delicious`。

9. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be interested in` 为固定搭配。

10. **答案：deeper**
    - **考点**：比较级。`a deeper appreciation`，暗含与之前相比更深的理解。
