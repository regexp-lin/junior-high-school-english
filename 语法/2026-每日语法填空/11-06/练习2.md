# 11-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：太极拳 (Tai Chi)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tai Chi is an ancient Chinese martial art that combines slow, graceful movements (1) ______ deep breathing and meditation. Unlike other martial arts that focus on speed and power, Tai Chi emphasises balance, flexibility and inner peace. It is practised by millions of people around the world and is especially popular (2) ______ older adults. My grandmother has been practising Tai Chi (3) ______ over twenty years. Every morning at six o'clock, she goes to the park near our home, where a group of people gather to do Tai Chi together. They move slowly and (4) ______ (grace) in unison, like a gentle dance. My grandmother says that Tai Chi has (5) ______ (great) improved her health. Before she started, she often suffered from back pain and poor sleep. Now, at the age of seventy, she is (6) ______ (health) and more energetic than many people half her age. Last summer, she encouraged me (7) ______ (join) her. I was reluctant at first because I thought Tai Chi was too slow and boring. But after just a few sessions, I realised how (8) ______ (challenge) it actually is. Holding certain poses for a long time requires a lot of strength and (9) ______ (concentrate). I also noticed that I felt (10) ______ (calm) and more focused after each practice. Now I understand why my grandmother loves it so much.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`combines...with...` 表示"把……与……结合"。

2. **答案：among**
   - **考点**：介词。`popular among older adults` 表示"在老年人中流行"。

3. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for over twenty years` 表持续时间。

4. **答案：gracefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `slowly` 并列修饰动词 `move`，`grace` 变为 `gracefully`。

5. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词。

6. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`healthier and more energetic than...`。

7. **答案：to join**
   - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb to do sth`。

8. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`how challenging it actually is`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

9. **答案：concentration**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `strength` 并列，需要名词，`concentrate` 变为 `concentration`。

10. **答案：calmer**
    - **考点**：比较级。`felt calmer and more focused`。
