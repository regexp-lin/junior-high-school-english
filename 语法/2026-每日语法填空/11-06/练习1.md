# 11-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：公共交通礼仪 (Public Transport Etiquette)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Good manners on public transport make the journey (1) ______ (pleasant) for everyone. However, some people seem to forget basic etiquette once they step onto a bus or train. Common problems include playing music (2) ______ (loud) without headphones, eating strong-smelling food and taking up extra seats with bags. Last Tuesday, something happened on my school bus that made me think about this issue. A boy from another class was watching videos on his phone at full volume. The noise (3) ______ (disturb) everyone around him, but nobody said anything. Finally, our bus monitor, Li Hua, politely asked him (4) ______ (turn) down the volume or use headphones. The boy looked (5) ______ (embarrass) and immediately put on his earphones. It was a small incident, but it showed the importance of (6) ______ (speak) up when someone's behaviour affects others. Our school has a list of "Bus Rules" that all students are expected (7) ______ (follow). These include giving up your seat to elderly people, keeping the bus clean and (8) ______ (not talk) loudly during the journey. Most students obey these rules, (9) ______ there are always a few who need reminding. I believe good public transport manners are a reflection (10) ______ a person's character. How we behave in public spaces says a lot about who we are.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：more pleasant**
   - **考点**：比较级。`make the journey more pleasant`，暗含比较。

2. **答案：loudly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `playing` 需要副词，`loud` 变为 `loudly`。

3. **答案：disturbed**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

4. **答案：to turn**
   - **考点**：不定式。`ask sb to do sth`。

5. **答案：embarrassed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`looked embarrassed`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

6. **答案：speaking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`the importance of speaking up`，介词 `of` 后接动名词。

7. **答案：to follow**
   - **考点**：不定式。`are expected to follow`。

8. **答案：not talking**
   - **考点**：动名词（否定形式）。与 `giving up` 和 `keeping` 并列，`not talking` 为动名词否定形式。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`most students obey...but there are always a few...`，前后转折。

10. **答案：of**
    - **考点**：介词。`a reflection of` 表示"……的反映"。
