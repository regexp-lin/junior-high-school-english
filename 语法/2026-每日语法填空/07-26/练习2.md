# 07-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：睡眠与健康 (Sleep and Health)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Getting enough sleep is essential for teenagers, yet many students do not take it (1) ______ (serious). A survey of middle school students found that over half of them sleep (2) ______ (little) than eight hours per night. The main reasons include heavy homework, late-night phone use, and after-school tutoring classes. Doctors say that teenagers need eight to ten hours of sleep each night. Without enough rest, the brain cannot work (3) ______ (proper), and students may find it difficult to concentrate (4) ______ class. Poor sleep can also weaken the immune system, making us more likely (5) ______ (catch) colds and other illnesses. There are several ways to improve sleep quality. First, try to go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even (6) ______ weekends. This helps your body build a regular rhythm. Second, avoid (7) ______ (use) electronic devices for at least thirty minutes before bedtime, because the blue light from screens keeps the brain (8) ______ (activity). Third, make your bedroom dark and quiet — a comfortable environment is one of the (9) ______ (key) to sleeping well. Since I started following these tips, I (10) ______ (notice) a big improvement in both my sleep and my school performance.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`take sth seriously` 为固定搭配，表示"认真对待某事"，`serious` 变为 `seriously`。

2. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`little` 的比较级为 `less`，修饰不可数概念"时间"。

3. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `work` 需要副词，`proper` 变为 `properly`。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`concentrate in class` 表示"在课堂上集中注意力"。

5. **答案：to catch**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be likely to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"可能做某事"。

6. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on weekends` 表示"在周末"。

7. **答案：using**
   - **考点**：动名词。`avoid doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"避免做某事"。

8. **答案：active**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`keep + 宾语 + 形容词` 表示"保持……的状态"，`activity` 变为 `active`。

9. **答案：keys**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the + 复数名词`，`key` 变为 `keys`。

10. **答案：have noticed**
    - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since I started` 提示用现在完成时，主语 `I` 用 `have noticed`。
