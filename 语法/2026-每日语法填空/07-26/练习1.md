# 07-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：风筝节 (The Kite Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last spring, my family went to Weifang, a city in Shandong Province that is famous (1) ______ its kite festival. The festival (2) ______ (hold) every April and attracts visitors from all over the world. When we arrived at the main field, I was amazed to see thousands of kites (3) ______ (fly) high in the sky. They came in all shapes and sizes — dragons, butterflies, eagles, and even cartoon (4) ______ (character). The largest kite was over one hundred metres long! My father bought a simple kite for me and taught me how (5) ______ (control) it. It was harder than it looked. The wind kept changing (6) ______ (direct), and my kite crashed to the ground several times. But with practice, I finally got it into the air. (7) ______ (watch) it dance against the blue sky gave me a wonderful feeling of freedom. In the evening, there was a special show (8) ______ kites with LED lights lit up the night sky. It was one of the most (9) ______ (magic) scenes I had ever seen. The festival helped me understand (10) ______ Chinese kite-making is considered an important cultural heritage.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be famous for` 表示"因……而闻名"。

2. **答案：is held**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。节日"每年被举办"，描述惯例性活动。

3. **答案：flying**
   - **考点**：现在分词作宾补。`see sth doing` 表示"看见某物正在做"。

4. **答案：characters**
   - **考点**：名词复数。与前面的 dragons、butterflies 等并列，用复数。

5. **答案：to control**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to do sth` 为固定搭配。

6. **答案：direction**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`changing direction` 中需要名词，`direct` 变为 `direction`（方向）。

7. **答案：Watching**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。`Watching it dance` 在句中作主语，句首大写。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a special show`（场景/活动），在定语从句中作地点/场景状语，用 `where`。

9. **答案：magical**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most magical`，`magic` 变为形容词 `magical`。

10. **答案：why**
    - **考点**：连接副词。引导宾语从句，`why Chinese kite-making is considered...` 表示"为什么中国风筝制作被视为……"。
