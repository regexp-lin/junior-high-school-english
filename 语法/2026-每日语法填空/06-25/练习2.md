# 06-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：保护海洋 (Protecting the Ocean)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ocean covers more than seventy percent of the Earth's surface and is home (1) ______ millions of species. However, human activities (2) ______ (cause) serious damage to it in recent decades. Plastic waste is one of the (3) ______ (big) problems. Every year, about eight million tonnes of plastic (4) ______ (throw) into the sea, and much of it is eaten by fish and sea birds. Scientists warn that (5) ______ we do not act now, many ocean creatures will disappear forever. Some countries have already started to take action. For example, several coastal cities have banned single-use plastic bags, (6) ______ has helped reduce pollution. Students can also make a (7) ______ (differ). When you visit the beach, remember (8) ______ (pick) up any rubbish you see. At home, try to use (9) ______ (few) plastic products than before. Protecting the ocean is not just the government's job — it is the (10) ______ (responsible) of every person on Earth.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`be home to` 为固定搭配，表示"是……的家园/栖息地"。

2. **答案：have caused**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。时间状语 `in recent decades`（近几十年来）提示用现在完成时，表示过去到现在的持续影响；主语 `activities` 为复数，用 `have caused`。

3. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`big` 的最高级为 `biggest`（双写辅音字母加 `-est`）。

4. **答案：is thrown**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。塑料"被扔进"大海，`Every year` 表示经常性行为，用一般现在时被动语态；`plastic` 为不可数名词，用 `is thrown`。

5. **答案：if**
   - **考点**：条件连词。`if we do not act now` 引导条件状语从句，表示"如果我们不行动"。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个事件（禁止使用一次性塑料袋），在从句中作主语，用 `which`。

7. **答案：difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`make a difference` 为固定搭配，表示"产生影响/起作用"；`differ` 变为名词 `difference`。

8. **答案：to pick**
   - **考点**：不定式。`remember to do sth` 表示"记得要做某事"（未做之事），用不定式。

9. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than before` 提示用比较级；`plastic products` 为可数名词复数，`few` 的比较级为 `fewer`。

10. **答案：responsibility**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`the` + 名词结构，`responsible`（形容词）变为名词 `responsibility`（责任）。
