# 06-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园运动会 (School Sports Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday was our school sports day, and it was one of the (1) ______ (exciting) days of the year. The playground (2) ______ (decorate) with colourful flags the night before. When the morning came, every class marched onto the field (3) ______ their own team uniform. I took part in the 100-metre race (4) ______ the long jump. At the starting line, my heart was beating (5) ______ (fast) than ever. The moment the whistle blew, I ran with all my strength. I did not win first place, (6) ______ I was happy because I beat my personal record. My classmate Li Hua surprised everyone (7) ______ winning the 800-metre race — nobody expected him (8) ______ (finish) first. After the events, we sat together, (9) ______ (share) water and snacks. That afternoon taught us that sports are not just about medals; they are about (10) ______ (team) spirit and friendship.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most exciting**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`exciting` 为多音节形容词，最高级为 `most exciting`。

2. **答案：was decorated**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。操场"被装饰"，主语 `playground` 为单数，且时间为过去（`the night before`），用 `was decorated`。

3. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in their own team uniform` 表示"穿着各自的队服"，`in` 表示"穿着"。

4. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`the 100-metre race` 与 `the long jump` 为并列关系，用 `and` 连接。

5. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than ever` 提示用比较级；`fast` 的比较级为 `faster`，修饰动词 `beating`。

6. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。"没有得第一"与"很开心"之间是转折关系，用 `but`。

7. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by + doing` 表示"通过……方式"，此处表示"通过赢得比赛让大家惊讶"。

8. **答案：to finish**
   - **考点**：不定式。`expect sb to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"期望某人做某事"。

9. **答案：sharing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表示伴随状语，坐在一起的同时分享水和零食，用现在分词 `sharing`。

10. **答案：team**
    - **考点**：词性转换/名词作定语。`team spirit` 为固定搭配，表示"团队精神"，名词 `team` 作定语修饰 `spirit`。
