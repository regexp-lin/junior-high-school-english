# 12-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：共享经济 (Sharing Economy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The sharing economy is a new business model that has grown (1) ______ (rapid) in China over the past decade. It allows people to share resources such as cars, bicycles, power banks and even office spaces, rather than owning everything (2) ______ (they). Bike-sharing was one of the first sharing services (3) ______ (appear) in Chinese cities. Users can unlock a shared bicycle by scanning a QR code and return it at any (4) ______ (design) parking spot. This service has solved the "last kilometre" problem for millions of commuters. Power bank sharing is another popular service. Have you ever been in a situation (5) ______ your phone was about to die and you had no charger? With power bank sharing, you can rent a portable charger from a machine at any shopping mall or restaurant. The service usually (6) ______ (cost) only a few yuan per hour. (7) ______, the sharing economy also has its problems. Some users do not treat shared items (8) ______ (care), and damaged or abandoned bikes have created a mess in some cities. Companies have had to spend large (9) ______ (sum) of money on repairs and management. Experts say that the success of the sharing economy depends not only on technology but also on the public's sense of (10) ______ (responsible). If everyone uses shared resources with respect, the system can benefit us all.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `grown` 需要副词形式。

2. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`owning everything themselves` 表示"自己拥有一切"。

3. **答案：to appear**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the first...to do sth` 为固定用法。

4. **答案：designated**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`designated parking spot` 表示"指定停车点"，`design` 变为 `designated`。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，先行词 `situation` 为抽象地点。

6. **答案：costs**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`The service` 为第三人称单数，`usually` 提示一般现在时。

7. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。前后转折，"然而，共享经济也有问题"。

8. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `treat` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：sums**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`large sums of money` 表示"大笔资金"。

10. **答案：responsibility**
    - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `of` 后接名词。
