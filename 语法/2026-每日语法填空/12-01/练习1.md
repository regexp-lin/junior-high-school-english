# 12-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬季活动 (Winter Activities)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Winter is a season that some people love and others dread. For those who enjoy cold weather, it offers a variety of exciting (1) ______ (activity) that are impossible during other seasons. Ice skating, skiing and snowboarding are among the most popular winter sports. In northern China, where temperatures often drop (2) ______ minus 20 degrees Celsius, winter sports have a long tradition. The Harbin Ice and Snow Festival, (3) ______ (hold) every January, attracts millions of visitors from around the world. The festival features enormous ice sculptures (4) ______ (light) up with colourful LED lights, creating a magical winter wonderland. Even in southern China, where snow is rare, people find ways to enjoy the season. My family lives in Guangzhou, where it almost never (5) ______ (snow). Last December, my parents took me to an indoor ski resort for the first time. I was so excited that I could (6) ______ (hard) sleep the night before. When I stepped onto the artificial snow, I immediately slipped and fell. Learning to ski was much more (7) ______ (challenge) than I had imagined. I fell down countless times, but each time I got up and tried again. By the end of the day, I was able to slide down a gentle slope (8) ______ (fall). The instructor told me that I was a fast (9) ______ (learn). Now I am looking forward to my next visit. I have even started saving money (10) ______ (buy) my own skiing equipment.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：activities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a variety of exciting activities`。

2. **答案：to / below**
   - **考点**：介词。`drop to/below minus 20 degrees` 表示"降到零下20度"。

3. **答案：held**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the festival held every January`，被动关系。

4. **答案：lit**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`sculptures lit up with lights`，被动关系。

5. **答案：snows**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。主语 `it` 为第三人称单数，描述一般事实用一般现在时。

6. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`could hardly sleep`，`hardly` 表示"几乎不"。

7. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more challenging than`，`challenge` 变为形容词。

8. **答案：without falling**
   - **考点**：介词 + 动名词。`without falling` 表示"没有摔倒"。

9. **答案：learner**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a fast learner` 表示"学得快的人"。

10. **答案：to buy**
    - **考点**：不定式。`save money to buy` 表示"攒钱去买"，不定式表目的。
