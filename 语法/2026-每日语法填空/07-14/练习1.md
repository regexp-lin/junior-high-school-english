# 07-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：跑步的乐趣 (The Joy of Running)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Running is one of the (1) ______ (easy) sports to start because it requires no special equipment. I began running two years ago when my doctor told me I needed more (2) ______ (physically) activity. At first, I could only run for five minutes (3) ______ stopping. My legs felt heavy and my breathing was difficult. But I did not give up. I made a plan and increased the (4) ______ (distant) a little every week. After three months, I was able to run five kilometres (5) ______ a time. The feeling of crossing the finish line was (6) ______ (true) wonderful. Now, running has become a habit that I cannot imagine (7) ______ (live) without. I usually run along the river path near my home. The scenery changes (8) ______ the seasons — cherry blossoms in spring, green leaves in summer, golden fields in autumn, and bare branches in winter. Running not only keeps me fit (9) ______ also clears my mind. Whenever I feel upset, a thirty-minute run makes everything seem (10) ______ (good) than before. I plan to run a half-marathon next year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：easiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`easy` 变 y 为 i 加 `-est`。

2. **答案：physical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `activity` 需要形容词，括号内 `physically` 变为 `physical`。

3. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`without stopping` 表示"不停下来"。

4. **答案：distance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`distant` 变为 `distance`（距离）。

5. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。`at a time` 为固定搭配，表示"每次，一次"。

6. **答案：truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `wonderful` 需要副词，`true` 变为 `truly`。

7. **答案：living**
   - **考点**：动名词。`imagine doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"想象做某事"。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`change with the seasons` 表示"随季节变化"。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 为固定搭配。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than before` 提示用比较级，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。
