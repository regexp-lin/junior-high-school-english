# 07-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：网络安全 (Internet Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Internet has made our lives more convenient, but it also brings risks that we must be aware (1) ______. Every year, thousands of teenagers become victims of online fraud. Some people pretend to be friends and trick young users (2) ______ sharing personal information. Once your name, address, or phone number (3) ______ (fall) into the wrong hands, it can be used for harmful purposes. To stay safe, there are several things you should do. First, never give your password to (4) ______ (someone) you do not fully trust. Second, be careful when you receive (5) ______ (message) from strangers, especially those that offer free gifts or prizes. Third, keep your software (6) ______ (update) to prevent viruses. If something online makes you feel (7) ______ (comfort), tell a parent or teacher right away. Our school invited a police officer last month (8) ______ (give) a talk on Internet safety. He said that thinking before (9) ______ (click) was the simplest but most (10) ______ (effect) way to protect yourself. I think his advice is worth remembering.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`be aware of` 为固定搭配，表示"意识到，知道"。

2. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`trick sb into doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"诱骗某人做某事"。

3. **答案：falls**
   - **考点**：主谓一致 + 一般现在时。`once` 引导条件状语从句，用一般现在时；主语由 `or` 连接，就近原则取 `phone number`（单数），用 `falls`。

4. **答案：anyone**
   - **考点**：词性转换/代词。`someone` 在否定句中变为 `anyone`，表示"任何人"。

5. **答案：messages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。收到多条消息，`message` 用复数 `messages`。

6. **答案：updated**
   - **考点**：过去分词作宾补。`keep + 宾语 + 过去分词` 表示"保持……被……的状态"，软件"被更新"。

7. **答案：uncomfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 反义词。`feel` 为系动词后接形容词；根据语境应加前缀 `un-`，表示"不舒服的，不安的"。

8. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。学校邀请警官的目的是做讲座，用 `to give`。

9. **答案：clicking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`before` 为介词，后接动名词 `clicking`。

10. **答案：effective**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `simplest` 并列作定语修饰 `way`，`effect` 变为形容词 `effective`（有效的）。
