# 03-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：音乐的魅力 (The Magic of Music)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Music is a universal language that can be 1. ________ (understand) by people all over the world. No matter where you come from, a beautiful song can touch your heart.

I started learning the piano when I was six years old. At first, I found it very difficult and wanted to give it 2. ________. Practicing the same piece again and again was really 3. ________ (bore). But my mother always encouraged me 4. ________ (keep) going. She said that nothing worth having comes easy.

After years of hard work, I 5. ________ (final) played my first song perfectly at a school concert. The audience clapped their 6. ________ (hand) warmly. That moment was one of the 7. ________ (proud) moments in my life. I was so glad that I didn't give up.

Music has brought many good things 8. ________ my life. When I feel stressed about exams, playing the piano helps me relax. When I feel lonely, music is like a friend who 9. ________ (stay) by my side.

I believe that everyone should try to learn a musical instrument. It doesn't matter 10. ________ you are talented or not — what matters is that you enjoy it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **understood**
   - **考点**：被动语态。can be + 过去分词，music 与 understand 之间是被动关系，understand 的过去分词为 understood。

2. **up**
   - **考点**：动词短语（无提示词填空）。give up 意为"放弃"，结合语境"想要放弃"。

3. **boring**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。bore 变为形容词，修饰事物用 boring（令人无聊的），修饰人用 bored。

4. **to keep**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。encourage sb. to do sth. 意为"鼓励某人做某事"。

5. **finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 played，需用副词形式 finally。

6. **hands**
   - **考点**：名词复数。clap their hands 意为"鼓掌"，hand 需用复数形式。

7. **proudest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数。proud 的最高级为 proudest。

8. **into/to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。bring sth. into/to one's life 意为"把某物带入某人的生活"。

9. **stays**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）+ 主谓一致。who 引导定语从句，先行词 a friend 是第三人称单数，描述客观状态用一般现在时，动词加 -s。

10. **whether**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。whether...or not 意为"是否……"，引导主语从句。
