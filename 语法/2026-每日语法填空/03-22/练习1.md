# 03-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：志愿者服务 (Volunteering)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Volunteering is a great way to help others and improve yourself at the same time. Last summer, I joined a volunteer program and had one of the most meaningful 1. ________ (experience) in my life.

Our team went to a small village to teach children there. The village was far from the city, and many families were too poor 2. ________ (send) their children to good schools. We taught them English, math and art for two weeks. Seeing the 3. ________ (happy) on their faces made us feel proud.

During those days, I learned many things that I 4. ________ (not learn) in my own school. I learned to cook simple meals and wash clothes by 5. ________. More importantly, I realized how lucky I was to have a comfortable life.

One little girl told me that she wanted to be 6. ________ teacher when she grew up. Her dream 7. ________ (touch) my heart deeply. I promised her that I would come back the next year.

Volunteering is not just about 8. ________ (give) — you also receive a lot. It teaches you to care about others and helps you become a 9. ________ (good) person. If everyone is willing to lend a hand, the world will be 10. ________ (full) of love and warmth.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **experiences**
   - **考点**：名词复数。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，experience 表示"经历"时为可数名词。

2. **to send**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。too + adj. + to do sth. 意为"太……而不能做某事"。

3. **happiness**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。定冠词 the 后接名词，happy 的名词形式为 happiness。

4. **couldn't/can't learn**
   - **考点**：动词时态与否定。根据语境"在自己学校学不到的东西"，定语从句中用一般过去时否定形式 couldn't learn（也可接受 can't learn）。

5. **myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词（无提示词填空）。by myself 意为"独自地"，是固定搭配，与前文主语 I 对应。

6. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。表示职业用 be a/an + 职业名词，此处泛指"一名教师"。

7. **touched**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。叙述过去发生的事情，touch 变为过去式 touched。

8. **giving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 about 后接动名词形式。

9. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。根据语境"帮助你成为一个更好的人"，good 的比较级为 better。

10. **full**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词）。be full of 意为"充满……"，是固定搭配。注意给出的提示词 full 本身就是所需形式，考查学生对 be full of 搭配的掌握。
