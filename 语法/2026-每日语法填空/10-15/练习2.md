# 10-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园环保行动 (Campus Environmental Action)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Environmental protection starts with small actions, and our school has been (1) ______ (active) promoting green habits among students. This year, we launched a campus-wide campaign (2) ______ (call) "Green School, Green Future". The campaign includes several initiatives. First, every classroom has been equipped with recycling bins so that students can sort their waste (3) ______ (proper). Second, the school shop has stopped (4) ______ (provide) plastic bags and now uses paper bags instead. Third, (5) ______ class has adopted a small area of the campus to keep clean and green. Our class is responsible for the garden near the teaching building. We water the plants, pick (6) ______ litter and plant new flowers every season. The campaign has also changed students' daily habits. Many students now bring their (7) ______ (own) water bottles instead of buying plastic ones. Some even ride bicycles or walk to school to reduce carbon (8) ______ (emit). Our head teacher is very pleased with the progress. She says our school (9) ______ (use) 30% less electricity compared to last year, thanks to students remembering to turn (10) ______ lights and fans when they leave the classroom. Small changes do make a big difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：actively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `promoting` 需要副词，`active` 变为 `actively`。

2. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a campaign called "Green School, Green Future"`，过去分词作后置定语。

3. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `sort` 需要副词，`proper` 变为 `properly`。

4. **答案：providing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`stop doing sth` 表示"停止做某事"。

5. **答案：each / every**
   - **考点**：限定词。`each/every class` 表示"每个班级"。

6. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`pick up` 表示"捡起"。

7. **答案：own**
   - **考点**：形容词。`bring their own water bottles`，`own` 强调"自己的"。

8. **答案：emissions**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`carbon emissions`，`emit` 变为名词 `emission`，复数。

9. **答案：has used**
   - **考点**：时态。`compared to last year` 暗含从去年到现在的对比，用现在完成时。

10. **答案：off**
    - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`turn off` 表示"关闭"。
