# 10-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：丝绸之路 (The Silk Road)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Silk Road was an ancient network of trade routes that connected China (1) ______ the Mediterranean Sea. It got its name from the silk trade, which was one of the most (2) ______ (profit) businesses along the route. (3) ______, the Silk Road was not just about silk. Merchants also traded spices, gold, jewels and many other (4) ______ (good). More importantly, the Silk Road served as a bridge for cultural exchange. Through it, Chinese inventions like paper and gunpowder (5) ______ (spread) to the West, while Western ideas in astronomy and medicine reached China. The journey along the Silk Road was long and (6) ______ (danger). Travellers had to cross vast deserts, climb high mountains and face the risk of being (7) ______ (attack) by bandits. Despite these hardships, thousands of merchants and explorers made the journey, driven by the desire (8) ______ (discover) new lands and opportunities. Today, China has launched the "Belt and Road Initiative", a modern version of the Silk Road that aims (9) ______ (strengthen) trade and cooperation between countries. It has already brought (10) ______ (economy) benefits to many nations. The spirit of the Silk Road — openness, exchange and mutual benefit — continues to inspire the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`connected China to/with the Mediterranean Sea`。

2. **答案：profitable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most profitable businesses`，`profit` 变为 `profitable`。

3. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词（转折）。前后语意转折。

4. **答案：goods**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many other goods` 表示"许多其他商品"。

5. **答案：spread**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述历史事件用一般过去时，`spread` 的过去式仍为 `spread`。

6. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `long` 并列作表语，需要形容词，`danger` 变为 `dangerous`。

7. **答案：attacked**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`being attacked by bandits`，`attack` 的过去分词。

8. **答案：to discover**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the desire to discover`，不定式作后置定语。

9. **答案：to strengthen**
   - **考点**：不定式。`aims to do sth` 表示"旨在做某事"。

10. **答案：economic**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `benefits` 需要形容词，`economy` 变为 `economic`。
