# 08-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：拼图的启示 (Lessons from a Jigsaw Puzzle)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, my family worked together on a 1,000-piece jigsaw puzzle. It was a picture of a beautiful European village. The moment we opened the box, I was overwhelmed by the number of tiny (1) ______ (piece). They all looked the same to me. My mother suggested starting with the edge pieces, (2) ______ were easier to identify. So we sorted all the pieces by colour and shape first. This step took almost an hour, but it made the (3) ______ (remain) work much smoother. My father was responsible (4) ______ the sky area, which was the (5) ______ (hard) part because all the pieces were blue. He tried again and again without (6) ______ (lose) patience. My younger sister worked on the colourful houses, and I focused (7) ______ the trees and garden. Piece by piece, the picture began to take shape. When we finally placed the last piece at midnight, we cheered with (8) ______ (excite). The puzzle taught me several life lessons. First, big tasks become (9) ______ (manage) when you break them into small steps. Second, teamwork makes difficult things possible. And third, patience is always (10) ______ (reward) in the end.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：pieces**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the number of` 后接可数名词复数。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `the edge pieces`，在从句中作主语。

3. **答案：remaining**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the remaining work` 表示"剩余的工作"，`remain` 变为 `remaining`（剩余的）。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be responsible for` 为固定搭配，表示"负责"。

5. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + part`。

6. **答案：losing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`without` 为介词，后接动名词。

7. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`focus on` 为固定搭配，表示"专注于"。

8. **答案：excitement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`with` 后接名词，`excite` 变为 `excitement`（兴奋）。

9. **答案：manageable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`become` 为系动词，后接形容词，`manage` 变为 `manageable`（可管理的）。

10. **答案：rewarded**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`is rewarded` 表示"是有回报的"，或理解为 `reward` 变为 `rewarded`（被回报的）。也可填 `rewarding`（值得的）。
