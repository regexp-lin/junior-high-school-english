# 08-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：写日记的好处 (Benefits of Keeping a Diary)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I have been keeping a diary (1) ______ Grade Seven. Every night before bed, I spend about fifteen minutes (2) ______ (write) down what happened during the day and how I felt about it. At first, I wrote only a few sentences, but now my entries are much (3) ______ (long) and more detailed. Keeping a diary has brought me many benefits. First, it has improved my (4) ______ (write) skills. Because I write every day, I find it (5) ______ (easy) to express my thoughts in both Chinese and English. Second, a diary helps me deal (6) ______ negative emotions. When I feel angry or sad, writing about it helps me calm (7) ______ and see things more clearly. Third, reading old entries is a wonderful way (8) ______ (remember) the past. Last week, I looked back at what I (9) ______ (write) two years ago and smiled at the silly worries I used to have. Some people prefer typing on their phones, but I still use a paper notebook. There is something special about the feeling of a pen (10) ______ (move) across the page.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。`since Grade Seven` 与现在完成进行时搭配，表示"自从七年级以来"。

2. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`much longer` 表示"长得多"，与之前对比。

4. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：动名词作定语。`writing skills` 表示"写作技能"。

5. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`find it easier to...` 表示"发现更容易做……"，隐含与之前的对比。

6. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`deal with` 为固定搭配，表示"处理，应对"。

7. **答案：down**
   - **考点**：副词。`calm down` 为固定搭配，表示"平静下来"。

8. **答案：to remember**
   - **考点**：不定式。`a wonderful way to do sth` 中不定式作后置定语。

9. **答案：had written**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。"写日记"发生在"回顾"之前（过去的过去），用 `had written`。

10. **答案：moving**
    - **考点**：现在分词。`the feeling of a pen moving` 中 `of` 后接名词短语，`pen moving` 为动名词复合结构，表示"笔在页面上移动的感觉"。
