# 04-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：DIY 手工制作 (DIY Projects)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

DIY stands for "Do It Yourself." It means making or fixing things on your own instead of 1. ________ (buy) them or paying someone else to do it. DIY projects are not only fun but also a great way to develop 2. ________ (practice) skills.

Last weekend, I decided to make a bookshelf for my room. I found a tutorial video online and followed the 3. ________ (step) carefully. First, I measured the space where the bookshelf would go. Then I went to a hardware store with my father to buy wood and 4. ________ (nail).

Building the bookshelf was 5. ________ (hard) than I expected. I accidentally cut a piece of wood too short, so I had to start that part again. My father helped me hold the pieces together while I used a hammer to put in the nails. It 6. ________ (take) us the whole afternoon to finish.

When the bookshelf was finally done, I painted it blue — my favorite color. I then put all my books on 7. ________ neatly. Looking at the finished product, I felt 8. ________ (proud) than ever before. I had built something useful with my own 9. ________ (hand)!

DIY teaches us important lessons. It shows us that we can solve problems 10. ________ (creative) and that making mistakes is just part of the learning process. Why not try a DIY project this weekend?

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **buying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。instead of 后接动名词形式。

2. **practical**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词/名词→形容词）。修饰名词 skills，practice → practical。

3. **steps**
   - **考点**：名词复数。follow the steps 意为"按照步骤"，step 用复数。

4. **nails**
   - **考点**：名词复数。and 连接并列名词，nail → nails。

5. **harder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，hard → harder。

6. **took**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。It took sb. time to do sth. 意为"花了某人多少时间做某事"，take → took。

7. **it**
   - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。it 代替前面的 the bookshelf。

8. **prouder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，proud → prouder。

9. **hands**
   - **考点**：名词复数。with my own hands 意为"用我自己的双手"，hand 用复数。

10. **creatively**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 solve，需用副词形式 creatively。
