# 04-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：榜样的力量 (Role Models)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Everyone needs a role model — someone who inspires you to become a 1. ________ (good) person. A role model can be a family member, a teacher, a famous person, or even a friend.

My role model is my grandfather. He grew up in a poor village and had very 2. ________ (limit) chances to go to school. But he never stopped 3. ________ (learn). He taught himself to read by borrowing books from neighbors. Later, he became a respected doctor in our town and spent his whole life 4. ________ (help) others.

What I admire most about my grandfather is his kindness. He never turned 5. ________ anyone who came to him for help. Even after he retired, he continued to offer free medical advice to villagers. His motto was, "Being kind costs 6. ________ (something), but it means everything."

My grandfather also taught me the value of hard work. "There are no shortcuts in life," he often said. "If you want to 7. ________ (success), you must be willing to work harder than everyone else." These words have stayed 8. ________ me and guided me through many difficult times.

A good role model doesn't have to be 9. ________ (fame). They just need to have qualities that inspire you to be your best self. Think about who your role model is and what you can learn 10. ________ them.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。a better person 意为"一个更好的人"，good → better。

2. **limited**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。very limited chances 意为"非常有限的机会"，limit → limited。

3. **learning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。never stopped learning 意为"从未停止学习"，stop doing sth. 表示"停止做某事"。

4. **helping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 意为"花时间做某事"。

5. **away**
   - **考点**：副词/动词短语（无提示词填空）。turn away 意为"拒绝、赶走"。

6. **nothing**
   - **考点**：代词。costs nothing 意为"不花任何代价"。注意提示词为 something，根据语境"善良不花什么，但意味着一切"，需要否定形式 nothing。

7. **succeed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→动词）。want to + 动词原形，success → succeed。

8. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。stay with me 意为"陪伴着我"。

9. **famous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。be famous 意为"出名的"，fame → famous。

10. **from**
    - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。learn from 意为"向……学习"。
