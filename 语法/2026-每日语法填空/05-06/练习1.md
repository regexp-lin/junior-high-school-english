# 05-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：地震防灾 (Earthquake Preparedness)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Earthquakes strike without much warning, 1. ________ knowing what to do can save lives. Last Friday, our school held a 2. ________ (safe) drill on the playground, and every class practised the “drop, cover, and hold on” steps.

When the alarm sounded, we hid 3. ________ our desks and protected our heads with schoolbags. Teachers counted students 4. ________ (quick) and guided us to the open field in order. The whole building 5. ________ (empty) in about four minutes.

After the drill, a firefighter gave 6. ________ short talk. He reminded us to prepare 7. ________ emergency bag with water, a flashlight, and 8. ________ copy of important phone numbers. He also warned us not to use 9. ________ (elevator) during strong shaking.

My family has now fixed heavy shelves to the wall and agreed on a 10. ________ (meet) place outside our building. Being ready does not stop earthquakes, but it helps us stay calmer when the ground moves.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **so**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后为因果关系：地震几乎无法预警，因此懂得应对能救命。

2. **safety**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词，有提示词）。a safety drill 为固定表达“安全演练”，safe → safety。

3. **under**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。躲在课桌下用 under our desks。

4. **quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 counted，用副词 quickly。

5. **was emptied**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般过去时的被动语态，有提示词）。building 与 empty 为被动关系，叙述上周五演练，用 was emptied，表示“楼内人员被疏散、楼被清空”。

6. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。give a short talk 表示“做简短讲话”。

7. **an**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。emergency 以元音音素开头，用 an emergency bag。

8. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a copy of... 表示“一份……的复印件/清单”。

9. **elevators**
   - **考点**：名词复数（有提示词）。泛指勿使用电梯，常用复数 elevators；若强调“电梯这一类设施”用复数更符合语境。

10. **meeting**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词，有提示词）。a meeting place 表示“集合地点”，meet → meeting。
