# 06-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：一本好书的力量 (The Power of a Good Book)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Books have been my best friends since I was a child. My mother often read bedtime stories to me, and that is how my love for (1) ______ (read) began. When I was ten, I received a novel (2) ______ (call) "Charlotte's Web" as a birthday gift. It was about the friendship between a pig and a spider. I was deeply (3) ______ (move) by the story and finished it in just two days. After that, I started going to the library every weekend. The librarian (4) ______ (recommend) different books to me each time, from science fiction (5) ______ history. Reading has taught me a lot. It broadens my mind and helps me understand people (6) ______ are different from me. Whenever I feel sad (7) ______ lonely, I open a book and lose (8) ______ (I) in another world. Some people say that reading is a dying habit in the age of short videos, (9) ______ I disagree. A good book can change the way you think, and its influence may last a (10) ______ (life).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：动名词。`love for` 中 `for` 为介词，后接动名词 `reading`，表示"对阅读的热爱"。

2. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`a novel called "Charlotte's Web"` 表示"一本叫做《夏洛特的网》的小说"，`called` 为过去分词修饰 `novel`。

3. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be moved by` 表示"被……感动"，`moved` 为过去分词作形容词，描述人的感受。

4. **答案：recommended**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的经历，用一般过去时；`recommend` 的过去式为 `recommended`。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from...to...` 表示"从……到……"，表示书籍种类范围。

6. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `people`（人），在定语从句中作主语，用 `who` 或 `that`。

7. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`sad or lonely` 表示"悲伤或孤独"，`or` 连接两个并列的形容词。

8. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`lose myself in` 表示"沉浸在……中"，主语为 `I`，反身代词用 `myself`。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。前面说"有人认为阅读是正在消亡的习惯"，后面说"我不同意"，为转折关系，用 `but`。

10. **答案：lifetime**
    - **考点**：词性转换/合成词。`a lifetime` 表示"一生"，`life` 与 `time` 合成为 `lifetime`，作名词。
