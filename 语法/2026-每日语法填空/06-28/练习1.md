# 06-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市交通 (City Traffic)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traffic has become a big problem in many large (1) ______ (city) in China. Every morning, thousands of cars fill the roads, and it sometimes takes over an hour to drive just a few (2) ______ (kilometre). To solve this problem, the government (3) ______ (build) new subway lines in recent years. The subway is not only faster (4) ______ also cheaper than driving. My father used to drive to work, but now he prefers (5) ______ (take) the subway because it saves both time and money. Another useful solution is shared bikes. People can ride them (6) ______ the subway station to their office, which is good for the environment. However, some riders park the bikes (7) ______ (care), blocking pavements and causing trouble for pedestrians. I think everyone should follow the rules (8) ______ using shared bikes. If we all work together, our city (9) ______ (become) a better place to live in. Good traffic planning is one of the (10) ______ (key) to building a modern city.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：cities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many` 修饰可数名词复数，`city` 以辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i 加 `-es`，得 `cities`。

2. **答案：kilometres**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few` 修饰可数名词复数，`kilometre` 变为 `kilometres`。

3. **答案：has built**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`in recent years`（近年来）提示用现在完成时；`the government` 为第三人称单数，用 `has built`。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 为固定搭配，表示"不仅……而且……"。

5. **答案：taking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`prefer doing sth` 表示"更喜欢做某事"（也可用 `prefer to do`，但 `prefer doing` 更常见于一般性偏好）。

6. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`from...to...` 表示"从……到……"，描述骑车的起点和终点。

7. **答案：carelessly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `park` 需要副词；`care` 变为 `carelessly`（粗心地），注意 `careless` + `-ly`。

8. **答案：when / while**
   - **考点**：连词。`when/while using` 引导时间状语从句（省略形式），表示"在使用共享单车时"。

9. **答案：will become**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。`if` 引导的条件状语从句用一般现在时，主句用一般将来时，即"主将从现"。

10. **答案：keys**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the + 复数名词` 为固定结构，表示"……之一"，`key` 变为 `keys`。
