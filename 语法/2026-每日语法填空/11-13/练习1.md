# 11-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：养宠物 (Keeping Pets)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Keeping a pet can bring a lot of joy and (1) ______ (happy) to a family. Dogs and cats are the most common pets in China, but some people also keep birds, fish or even rabbits. Our family has a golden retriever (2) ______ (name) Doudou. We adopted him from an animal shelter three years ago. At that time, he was very thin and (3) ______ (fright) of people. It took us several weeks (4) ______ (gain) his trust. Now he is the (5) ______ (friend) dog in our neighbourhood. Every morning, my father takes Doudou for a walk in the park. Doudou enjoys (6) ______ (run) around and playing with other dogs. He also likes fetching sticks, and he never gets tired (7) ______ this game. Taking care of a pet is a big (8) ______ (responsible). We have to feed him on time, take him to the vet regularly and keep him clean. Sometimes it feels like a lot of work, (9) ______ the love Doudou gives us in return makes it all worthwhile. Studies have shown that having a pet can reduce stress and make people (10) ______ (happy). I cannot imagine our family life without Doudou.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：happiness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `joy` 并列，需要名词形式，`happy` 变为 `happiness`。

2. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a golden retriever named Doudou`，被动含义。

3. **答案：frightened**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`frightened of people`，描述人或动物的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

4. **答案：to gain**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It took us several weeks to gain his trust`，`it takes sb time to do sth`。

5. **答案：friendliest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the friendliest dog in our neighbourhood`，范围限定用最高级。

6. **答案：running**
   - **考点**：动名词。`enjoy doing sth` 表示"喜欢做某事"。

7. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`get tired of` 表示"厌倦"。

8. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a big responsibility`，冠词后接名词。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前后语意转折，"有时觉得工作量大，但……"。

10. **答案：happier**
    - **考点**：比较级。`make people happier`，暗含比较"比之前更快乐"。
