# 11-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：交通安全 (Road Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Road safety is a topic that concerns everyone, especially students who walk or cycle to school every day. In China, traffic accidents remain one of the (1) ______ (lead) causes of injury among young people. Many of these accidents could be (2) ______ (prevent) if people followed basic traffic rules. Last Wednesday, our school held a road safety workshop. A police officer came and gave us a (3) ______ (detail) presentation about common traffic dangers. He showed us several videos of accidents (4) ______ (cause) by careless behaviour, such as crossing the road while looking at a phone or running a red light. The videos were so (5) ______ (shock) that some students could not watch. The officer reminded us (6) ______ always using zebra crossings and waiting for the green light before crossing. He also stressed the importance of wearing helmets when (7) ______ (ride) bicycles or electric scooters. According to him, wearing a helmet can reduce the risk of head injury (8) ______ up to 70%. After the workshop, our school (9) ______ (set) up a "Safety Monitor" team. Every morning, several students stand at the school gate to help younger children cross the road (10) ______ (safe). I volunteered to join this team because I believe that road safety is everyone's responsibility.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：leading**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`leading causes` 表示"主要原因"。

2. **答案：prevented**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`could be prevented`，情态动词后接 `be + 过去分词`。

3. **答案：detailed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `presentation` 需要形容词，`detail` 变为 `detailed`。

4. **答案：caused**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`accidents caused by careless behaviour`，被动关系。

5. **答案：shocking**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式，`the videos were so shocking`。

6. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`remind sb of doing sth` 表示"提醒某人做某事"。

7. **答案：riding**
   - **考点**：动名词。`when riding` 为时间状语从句的省略形式（省略主语和 be 动词）。

8. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by up to 70%` 表示"最多降低 70%"。

9. **答案：set**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`After the workshop` 提示过去时间，`set` 的过去式仍为 `set`。

10. **答案：safely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `cross` 需要副词形式。
