# 10-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：竹子的妙用 (The Wonders of Bamboo)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Bamboo is one of the most (1) ______ (use) plants in the world. It grows mainly in Asia and can reach (2) ______ (it) full height in just a few months. Some species grow as fast as 90 centimetres (3) ______ day! In China, bamboo has been used for thousands of years to make a wide variety of products. Houses, bridges, baskets, chopsticks and even paper can all (4) ______ (make) from bamboo. The plant is also an important food source — bamboo shoots are a (5) ______ (delight) ingredient in many Chinese dishes. My uncle runs a small workshop in the countryside (6) ______ he makes bamboo furniture by hand. I visited his workshop last summer and was amazed at his (7) ______ (skill). He could transform a simple bamboo pole into a beautiful chair within a few hours. He told me that bamboo is not only strong and flexible (8) ______ also environmentally friendly. Unlike trees, which take decades (9) ______ (grow), bamboo can be harvested every few years without (10) ______ (harm) the plant. This makes it one of the most sustainable materials on Earth. I believe bamboo will play an even bigger role in our future as we look for greener alternatives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：useful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most useful plants`，`use` 变为 `useful`。

2. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`its full height`。

3. **答案：a / per**
   - **考点**：冠词/介词。`90 centimetres a/per day`。

4. **答案：be made**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`can all be made from bamboo`。

5. **答案：delightful / delicious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `ingredient` 需要形容词，`delight` 变为 `delightful/delicious`。

6. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a small workshop`（地点），定语从句缺地点状语。

7. **答案：skill / skills**
   - **考点**：名词。`amazed at his skill(s)`，`skill` 用作名词。

8. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...`。

9. **答案：to grow**
   - **考点**：不定式。`take decades to grow`，`It takes time to do sth`。

10. **答案：harming**
    - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后接动名词。
