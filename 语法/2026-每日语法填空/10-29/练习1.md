# 10-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校艺术节 (School Art Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school holds an art festival every November, and it is one of the events that students look forward to (1) ______ (much). This year's theme was "Colours of Youth". Every class prepared a performance — some chose singing, others chose dancing or drama. Our class decided to put on a short play (2) ______ (base) on a famous Chinese fairy tale. We spent three weeks (3) ______ (rehearse). I was given the role of the narrator, (4) ______ meant I had to memorise a lot of lines. Our drama teacher, Ms. Xu, helped us with our stage presence and (5) ______ (express). She taught us to project our voices so that even the audience in the back rows could hear us (6) ______ (clear). On the night of the festival, the school hall was packed. When the curtain opened, I was so nervous that I almost forgot my opening line. But once I started (7) ______ (speak), the nervousness vanished. The audience laughed at the funny parts and fell silent during the (8) ______ (emotion) scenes. At the end, we received a standing ovation, (9) ______ was the best reward we could have asked for. Our play won second prize overall. The experience taught me that art has the power to bring people (10) ______ (close) and create shared moments of joy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most**
   - **考点**：副词最高级。`look forward to most`，`much` 的最高级为 `most`。

2. **答案：based**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a short play based on...`，过去分词作后置定语。

3. **答案：rehearsing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth`。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

5. **答案：expressions / expression**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`stage presence and expression(s)`，`express` 变为名词。

6. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `hear` 需要副词。

7. **答案：speaking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`once I started speaking`，`start doing sth`。

8. **答案：emotional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `scenes` 需要形容词，`emotion` 变为 `emotional`。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

10. **答案：closer**
    - **考点**：比较级。`bring people closer` 表示"拉近人们的距离"。
