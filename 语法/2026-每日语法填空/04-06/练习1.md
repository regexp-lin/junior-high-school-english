# 04-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园安全教育 (Campus Safety Education)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Safety is always the first thing we should think about. Every year, thousands of students get hurt in school accidents that could have been 1. ________ (avoid).

Fire safety is one of the most important topics. Our school holds fire drills twice a year so that students know what 2. ________ (do) in an emergency. During a drill, we practice walking to the playground quickly 3. ________ calmly. Running or pushing may cause even more danger.

Traffic safety is also a big concern. Many students ride bicycles or walk to school. They should always follow traffic 4. ________ (rule) and look carefully before 5. ________ (cross) the road. Wearing a helmet when riding a bike can protect your head from serious 6. ________ (injure).

In the classroom, there are also safety rules to follow. Students should not run in the hallways 7. ________ play with sharp objects like scissors. When doing science experiments, 8. ________ is necessary to wear safety glasses and follow the teacher's instructions.

Our school has set up a safety education program. Every month, a 9. ________ (profession) trainer comes to teach us first aid skills. So far, we 10. ________ (learn) how to deal with burns, cuts and other common injuries.

Remember: safety comes first. Being careful today can prevent accidents tomorrow.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **avoided**
   - **考点**：被动语态（过去分词）。could have been avoided 意为"本可以避免的"，情态动词 + have been + 过去分词。

2. **to do**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。what to do 意为"做什么"，疑问词 + 不定式结构作宾语。

3. **and**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。quickly and calmly 表示并列关系，"快速而冷静地"。

4. **rules**
   - **考点**：名词复数。traffic rules 意为"交通规则"，用复数。

5. **crossing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 before 后接动名词形式。

6. **injuries**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。serious 修饰名词，injure → injury，用复数 injuries。

7. **or**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。否定句中用 or 连接并列成分，"不应该在走廊跑步或玩尖锐物品"。

8. **it**
   - **考点**：形式主语（无提示词填空）。it is necessary to do sth.，it 为形式主语，真正主语是后面的不定式。

9. **professional**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 trainer，profession → professional。

10. **have learned/have learnt**
    - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。so far 提示用现在完成时。
