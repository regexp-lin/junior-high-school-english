# 04-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：艺术与创造力 (Art and Creativity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Art is all around us. From the paintings on museum walls to the designs on our clothes, art makes our world more 1. ________ (color) and interesting.

Many people think that art is only for 2. ________ (talent) people. But actually, everyone has the ability to create. Creativity is like a muscle — the more you use it, the 3. ________ (strong) it becomes. You don't need to be a professional artist to enjoy drawing, painting or making crafts.

In our school, the art class is one of the most 4. ________ (enjoy) classes. Last week, our art teacher asked us to create a poster about protecting the ocean. Each group came up 5. ________ different ideas. Some students drew beautiful sea creatures, while others used recycled materials to make 3D models. The 6. ________ (final) results were amazing.

Art is also a great way to express 7. ________ (you). When you feel happy, sad or angry, you can put your emotions into a painting or a song. Many famous artists created their 8. ________ (great) works during difficult times in their lives.

9. ________ (study) art can also help you in other subjects. Research shows that students who take art classes usually think more 10. ________ (creative) and solve problems better. So pick up a paintbrush and start creating!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **colorful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。more + 形容词构成比较级，color → colorful。

2. **talented**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 people，talent → talented（有天赋的）。

3. **stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。the more...the stronger... 意为"越……就越强"。

4. **enjoyable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。most + 形容词构成最高级，enjoy → enjoyable（令人愉快的）。

5. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。come up with 意为"想出（主意）"，是固定搭配。

6. **final**
   - **考点**：词性转换（副词→形容词）。修饰名词 results，finally → final。注意此处提示词为 final 的副词形式 finally，需转回形容词。

7. **yourself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。express yourself 意为"表达你自己"，主语为 you，宾语用反身代词。

8. **greatest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。their + 最高级，great → greatest。

9. **Studying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词 Studying 在句首作主语，注意首字母大写。

10. **creatively**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 think，需用副词形式 creatively。
