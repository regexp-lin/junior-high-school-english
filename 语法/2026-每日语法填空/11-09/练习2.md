# 11-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国书法 (Chinese Calligraphy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese calligraphy is considered one of the (1) ______ (fine) art forms in Chinese culture. It has a history of more than 3,000 years and (2) ______ (remain) popular to this day. Unlike Western writing, Chinese calligraphy requires a special brush, ink, paper and inkstone, which are known (3) ______ the "Four Treasures of the Study." My father is a calligraphy enthusiast. He has been practising calligraphy (4) ______ he was ten years old. Every evening after dinner, he spends at least one hour (5) ______ (write) characters at his desk. He once told me that calligraphy is not just about making characters look (6) ______ (beauty); it is also a way of training one's patience and concentration. Last year, he encouraged me to take up this hobby. I found it much (7) ______ (difficult) than I had expected. Holding the brush correctly and controlling the thickness of each stroke requires years of practice. My first few attempts looked (8) ______ (terrible), but my father told me not (9) ______ (give) up. After months of daily practice, I can now write some basic characters quite (10) ______ (neat). I have come to understand why so many people around the world are fascinated by this ancient art.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：finest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the finest art forms`，"最……之一"用最高级。

2. **答案：remains**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。描述现在仍然流行的事实，主语 `It` 为第三人称单数。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be known as` 表示"被称为"。

4. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`has been practising...since he was ten years old`，`since` 引导时间状语从句。

5. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 表示"花时间做某事"。

6. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`make characters look beautiful`，`look` 为系动词后接形容词。

7. **答案：more difficult**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than I had expected` 提示比较级，多音节形容词用 `more difficult`。

8. **答案：terrible**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`looked terrible`，系动词 `look` 后接形容词原级（此处无比较含义）。

9. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：不定式。`tell sb not to do sth` 表示"告诉某人不要做某事"。

10. **答案：neatly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `write` 需要副词，`neat` 变为 `neatly`。
