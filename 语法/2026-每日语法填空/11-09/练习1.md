# 11-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：志愿服务 (Volunteering)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Volunteering is a wonderful way to help others and develop new skills at the same time. Last month, our school (1) ______ (organise) a volunteering programme at a local nursing home. About thirty students signed up, and I was one of (2) ______ (they). We visited the nursing home every Saturday afternoon for four weeks. The elderly residents there were very happy (3) ______ (see) us. Some of them rarely had visitors, so our company meant a lot to them. During each visit, we helped with different tasks. Some students read newspapers (4) ______ the old people, while others helped clean their rooms or played chess with them. I spent most of my time (5) ______ (teach) a 78-year-old grandmother how to use a smartphone. She wanted to learn how to make video (6) ______ (call) to her grandchildren who live in another city. At first, she found it very (7) ______ (confuse) because there were too many buttons on the screen. But after several lessons, she (8) ______ (successful) made her first video call. The smile on her face was the (9) ______ (great) reward I have ever received. This experience taught me that giving is more (10) ______ (enjoy) than receiving.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：organised**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last month` 提示过去时间。

2. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词。介词 `of` 后接宾格代词 `them`。

3. **答案：to see**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be happy to do sth` 表示"很高兴做某事"。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`read newspapers to sb` 表示"给某人读报纸"。

5. **答案：teaching**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 表示"花时间做某事"。

6. **答案：calls**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`video calls` 为可数名词复数，意为"视频通话"。

7. **答案：confusing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式，`she found it very confusing`。

8. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `made` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：greatest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the greatest reward I have ever received`，`ever` 提示最高级。

10. **答案：enjoyable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`more enjoyable than`，需要形容词形式，`enjoy` 变为 `enjoyable`。
