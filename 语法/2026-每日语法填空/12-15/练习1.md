# 12-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：火车旅行 (Travel by Train)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's high-speed railway network is the (1) ______ (large) in the world, stretching over 40,000 kilometres. It connects major cities across the country, making travel faster and more convenient (2) ______ ever before. Taking a high-speed train from Beijing to Shanghai, a distance of about 1,300 kilometres, takes only four and a half hours. Before the high-speed railway (3) ______ (build), the same journey took over ten hours by regular train. Last winter holiday, my family travelled to Xi'an by high-speed train. It was my first time (4) ______ (ride) one, and I was amazed at how (5) ______ (smooth) and quietly it moved. The train reached speeds of up to 350 kilometres per hour, but inside the carriage, you could (6) ______ (hard) feel any shaking. The seats were comfortable, and there was plenty of space for luggage. I spent most of the journey looking out of the window. The scenery (7) ______ (change) dramatically as we moved from north to south — from flat plains covered in snow to green hills and terraced fields. My mother used the onboard Wi-Fi (8) ______ (catch) up on her work emails, while my father took a nap. We arrived in Xi'an feeling fresh and (9) ______ (relax), ready to explore the ancient capital. I think high-speed trains are one of the (10) ______ (great) achievements of modern China.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the largest in the world`。

2. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`more convenient than ever before` 提示比较级。

3. **答案：was built**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the high-speed railway` 与 `build` 之间是被动关系。

4. **答案：riding / to ride**
   - **考点**：动名词/不定式。`It was my first time riding/to ride one`。

5. **答案：smoothly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `moved` 需要副词，与 `quietly` 并列。

6. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：副词。`could hardly feel` 表示"几乎感觉不到"。

7. **答案：changed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述旅途中的情景。

8. **答案：to catch**
   - **考点**：不定式。`used the Wi-Fi to catch up`，表示目的。

9. **答案：relaxed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feeling fresh and relaxed`，描述人的感受用 `-ed`。

10. **答案：greatest**
    - **考点**：最高级。`one of the greatest achievements`。
