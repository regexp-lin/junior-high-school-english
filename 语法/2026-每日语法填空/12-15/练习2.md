# 12-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：街头艺术 (Street Art)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Street art has evolved from simple graffiti on walls to a (1) ______ (recognise) form of artistic expression. In cities around the world, you can find (2) ______ (colour) murals, creative sculptures and imaginative installations in public spaces. These artworks often carry powerful messages about social issues, environmental (3) ______ (protect) or cultural identity. Some people argue that street art makes cities more (4) ______ (live) and interesting. Others believe that it is nothing more (5) ______ vandalism that damages public property. However, attitudes towards street art (6) ______ (change) significantly in recent years. Many cities now have designated areas where artists are free (7) ______ (create) their work legally. In Shenzhen, there is a famous art village where (8) ______ (build) are covered from top to bottom with colourful paintings. Artists from all over the country come there to display their talent. Last autumn, our art class visited the village on a field trip. We were deeply (9) ______ (impress) by the creativity and skill on display. Our art teacher encouraged us to try creating our own street art on large sheets of paper. It was much (10) ______ (hard) than I expected because working on a large scale requires different techniques from those used on small canvases.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：recognised**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a recognised form` 表示"一种被认可的形式"。

2. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `murals` 需要形容词。

3. **答案：protection**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`environmental protection`，形容词后接名词。

4. **答案：lively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more lively and interesting`，`live` 变为 `lively`。

5. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`nothing more than` 表示"不过是"。

6. **答案：have changed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`in recent years` 提示。

7. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be free to do sth` 表示"可以自由做某事"。

8. **答案：buildings**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`buildings are covered` 表示多栋建筑。

9. **答案：impressed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`were deeply impressed`，描述人的感受用 `-ed`。

10. **答案：harder**
    - **考点**：比较级。`than I expected` 提示比较级。
