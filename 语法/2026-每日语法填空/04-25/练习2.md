# 04-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：职业规划 (Career Planning)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As middle school students, it may seem too early to think about our future careers. But 1. ________ (actual), career planning should start early. Knowing what you want to be helps you set goals and stay 2. ________ (motivation).

There are many factors to consider when choosing a career. First, think about your interests and 3. ________ (strong). If you love animals, maybe a career as a vet is right for you. If you are good at math, you might enjoy 4. ________ (work) as an engineer or a data scientist.

Second, learn about different 5. ________ (profession). Talk to adults about their jobs — ask them what they do every day, what they enjoy, and what challenges they face. You can also search 6. ________ information online or read books about different careers.

Third, don't be afraid 7. ________ (change) your mind. Many successful people changed their career paths several times before finding the right one. What matters is that you keep exploring and learning.

Our school organized a "Career Day" last month. Several 8. ________ (profession) from different fields came to share their experiences. A doctor told us about the 9. ________ (satisfy) of helping patients recover. A software engineer showed us how exciting it is to create new technology. Their stories inspired many of us to think more 10. ________ (serious) about our futures.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **actually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰整个句子，actual → actually。

2. **motivated**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。stay motivated 意为"保持积极性"，主语是人用 -ed 形式。

3. **strengths**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）+ 复数。and 连接并列名词，strong → strength → strengths。

4. **working**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。enjoy doing sth. 意为"喜欢做某事"。

5. **professions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）+ 复数。different + 名词复数，professional → profession → professions。

6. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。search for 意为"搜索"。

7. **to change**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。be afraid to do sth. 意为"害怕做某事"。

8. **professionals**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）+ 复数。several + 名词复数，profession → professional → professionals。

9. **satisfaction**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the + 名词 + of，satisfy → satisfaction。

10. **seriously**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 think，需用副词形式 seriously。
