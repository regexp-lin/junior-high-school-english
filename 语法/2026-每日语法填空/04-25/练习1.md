# 04-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：时光胶囊 (Time Capsule)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A time capsule is a container filled with objects and messages from the present. People bury it underground, and it is opened years later 1. ________ a way to communicate with the future.

Last month, our class decided to create a time capsule. The teacher asked each student 2. ________ (choose) one item and write a letter to their future self. We would bury the capsule in the school garden and open it ten years 3. ________ (late).

Choosing what to put in was not easy. Some students put in photos of 4. ________ (they) and their friends. Others chose meaningful objects, such as a favorite book, a concert ticket or a small toy. I decided to put in my diary, 5. ________ recorded my thoughts and dreams during middle school.

Writing the letter was even 6. ________ (hard). What do you say to yourself ten years from now? I wrote about my current life, my hobbies and what I hoped 7. ________ (achieve) by then. I also wrote about my family and friends, wondering whether we would still be as close as we are now.

On the day we buried the capsule, everyone 8. ________ (feel) excited. We took photos and made a promise to come back together in 2037. The 9. ________ (think) of opening the capsule in the future fills me with 10. ________ (expect) and joy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **as**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。as a way to 意为"作为一种方式"。

2. **to choose**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。ask sb. to do sth. 意为"要求某人做某事"。

3. **later**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。ten years later 意为"十年后"，late → later。

4. **themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。photos of themselves 意为"他们自己的照片"。

5. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，which 指代 my diary。

6. **harder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。even 修饰比较级，hard → harder。

7. **to achieve**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。hope to do sth. 意为"希望做某事"。

8. **felt**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。叙述过去的事情，feel → felt。

9. **thought**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the thought of 意为"想到……"，think → thought。

10. **expectation**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。with + 名词，expect → expectation（期待）。
