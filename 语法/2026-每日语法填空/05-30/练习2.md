# 05-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：非物质文化遗产 (Intangible Cultural Heritage)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Intangible cultural heritage includes music, crafts, festivals, and stories passed down by living people. It may not sit in a glass case, 1. ________ it shapes how communities remember their roots.

Last month, I watched a paper-cutting master at work. Her scissors moved 2. ________ (steady), and a red rabbit appeared in minutes. She explained that each pattern 3. ________ (carry) wishes for health and luck.

Young learners are now recording old songs on phones and posting videos online. Some people worry technology will replace tradition, 4. ________ I think sharing can attract more fans if we respect the original meaning.

Our school started a club where students practice shadow puppet shows. Lines 5. ________ (translate) into simple English so foreign guests can follow the story. The first performance felt clumsy, 6. ________ the audience still clapped with encouragement.

Protecting heritage is not only 7. ________ job for museums; it is also a duty for ordinary families. My grandmother taught me 8. ________ (make) dumplings, and that lesson was as valuable as any textbook.

When heritage stays alive in daily life, culture remains warm 9. ________ touchable.

I hope more teenagers will join this quiet revolution and pass skills 10. ________ the next generation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“未必摆在玻璃柜里”与“却塑造社群如何铭记根源”形成转折。

2. **steadily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 moved，用 steadily。

3. **carries**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致（一般现在时，有提示词）。解释一般规律，each pattern 作主语，谓语用第三人称单数 carries。

4. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“有人担忧技术取代传统”与“我认为分享若能尊重原意可吸引爱好者”形成转折。

5. **were translated**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般过去时的被动语态，有提示词）。lines 与 translate 为被动关系，叙述首次演出前准备，用 were translated。

6. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。表演笨拙与观众仍鼓掌形成转折。

7. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。not only a job for... 表示“不只是博物馆的工作”。

8. **to make**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（有提示词）。teach sb. to do sth.，用不定式 to make。

9. **and**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。warm 与 touchable 并列作表语，用 and。

10. **to**
    - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。pass skills to the next generation 表示“把技艺传给下一代”；也可用 pass sth. on to。
