# 05-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中考冲刺 (Final Sprint for the High School Entrance Exam)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The final sprint before the high school entrance exam is tiring, 1. ________ it can also be meaningful. Our class agreed to arrive ten minutes early each day to read key notes together. The morning air in the classroom felt serious 2. ________ hopeful at the same time.

Math used to be my weakest subject, 3. ________ I asked the teacher for a weekly plan. She suggested I solve five problems every evening and mark 4. ________ I got stuck. Slowly, my speed improved.

We stopped some hobbies for a while, 5. ________ we still played basketball on Friday afternoons to relax. Exercise helped us sleep 6. ________ (good) and return to books with clearer minds.

Parents cooked simple meals and tried not to ask too many 7. ________ (question). Their quiet support meant a lot.

When I entered the exam hall, I told myself that three years of effort would not disappear in 8. ________ single day. I wrote 9. ________ (careful) and checked my answers before handing in the paper.

The sprint ended, 10. ________ the discipline I built stayed with me.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“疲惫”与“也有意义”形成转折。

2. **and / yet**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。serious 与 hopeful 同时描述教室里的氛围，at the same time 提示两者并存，用 and 表并列最自然；用 yet 表“严肃却又充满希望”亦可。

3. **so**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。数学薄弱与向老师求助为因果关系，用 so。

4. **where**
   - **考点**：连接副词（无提示词填空）。mark where I got stuck 表示“标出卡壳的地方”，where 引导宾语从句并在从句中作地点状语。

5. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。暂停爱好与周五仍打球放松形成转折。

6. **better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。与运动前对比，睡得更好，用 well 的比较级 better。

7. **questions**
   - **考点**：名词复数（有提示词）。many 后接可数名词复数 questions。

8. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。in a single day 表示“在一天之内”。

9. **carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 wrote，用 carefully。

10. **but**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“冲刺结束”与“养成的自律仍在”形成转折。
