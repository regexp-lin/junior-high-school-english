# 09-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：课间操 (Morning Exercises)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Morning exercises are a daily routine at almost every school in China. At our school, all students gather on the playground at 9:40 a.m. for a 20-minute exercise session. The exercises include stretching, jumping and a set of movements (1) ______ (perform) to music. Our PE teacher, Mr. Zhao, stands at the front and leads us through each routine. He is very (2) ______ (energy) and always has a big smile on his face. Most students enjoy the morning exercises because they help us wake up and stay (3) ______ (focus) for the rest of the morning. (4) ______, some students think the exercises are boring and would prefer to use the time for studying. I used to be one of (5) ______ (they). I thought doing the same exercises every day was a waste of time. But last year, I (6) ______ (notice) that I often felt sleepy during the first class in the morning. After I started taking the morning exercises more (7) ______ (serious), I found that I had more energy and could concentrate (8) ______ (good) in class. Research has shown that even short periods of physical activity can improve brain function and (9) ______ (reduce) stress. So next time you feel lazy about morning exercises, remember that they are (10) ______ (design) to help you, both physically and mentally.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：performed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`movements performed to music`，过去分词作后置定语，表被动。

2. **答案：energetic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is very energetic`，系动词后接形容词，`energy` 变为 `energetic`。

3. **答案：focused**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`stay focused`，系动词后接形容词/过去分词，表"保持专注"。

4. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：副词（转折）。前后语意转折，用 `However`。

5. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`one of them`，介词 `of` 后接宾格。

6. **答案：noticed**
   - **考点**：时态。`last year` 提示用一般过去时。

7. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `taking` 需要副词，`serious` 变为 `seriously`。

8. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`concentrate better in class`，`well/good` 的比较级为 `better`。

9. **答案：reduce**
   - **考点**：动词原形。与 `improve` 并列，`can improve...and reduce...`。

10. **答案：designed**
    - **考点**：被动语态。`are designed to help you`，"被设计来帮助你"。
