# 09-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：民间故事 (A Folk Tale)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Long ago, there was a poor farmer (1) ______ lived at the foot of a mountain. Every day, he worked hard in his field from sunrise to sunset. One day, while he (2) ______ (dig) in the soil, he found a golden goose. He was so surprised that he could (3) ______ (hard) believe his eyes. He took the goose home and discovered that it laid a golden egg every morning. The farmer and his wife became (4) ______ (rich) day by day. However, (5) ______ (greed) soon took over. The farmer's wife said, "Why don't we cut open the goose and get all the golden eggs at once?" The farmer thought this was a (6) ______ (wonder) idea. But when he cut the goose open, there (7) ______ (be) no golden eggs inside at all. The goose was dead, and the farmer (8) ______ (lose) his source of wealth forever. He sat down and cried (9) ______ (bitter), but it was too late. This story teaches us an important lesson: being too greedy can cause us (10) ______ (lose) what we already have. Patience and contentment are the keys to a happy life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a farmer`（指人），引导定语从句。

2. **答案：was digging**
   - **考点**：时态。`while he was digging` 用过去进行时，表示"当他正在挖地时"。

3. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：副词。`could hardly believe`，`hardly` 表示"几乎不"。

4. **答案：richer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`became richer day by day` 表示"日益富裕"。

5. **答案：greed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。主语位置需要名词，`greedy` 变为 `greed`（贪婪）。

6. **答案：wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `idea` 需要形容词，`wonder` 变为 `wonderful`。

7. **答案：were**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。`there were no golden eggs`，`eggs` 为复数，用 `were`。

8. **答案：lost**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时，`lose` 的过去式为 `lost`。

9. **答案：bitterly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `cried` 需要副词，`bitter` 变为 `bitterly`。

10. **答案：to lose**
    - **考点**：不定式。`cause sb to do sth` 表示"导致某人做某事"。
