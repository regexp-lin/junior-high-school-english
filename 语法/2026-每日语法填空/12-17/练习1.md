# 12-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冰雪运动 (Ice and Snow Sports)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics brought ice and snow sports into the spotlight in China. Since then, (1) ______ growing number of young people have taken up winter sports. The government's goal of engaging 300 million people in winter sports has already been (2) ______ (achieve). Ice skating and skiing are the two most popular winter sports among Chinese teenagers. Many cities have built indoor ice rinks and ski (3) ______ (resort), making these sports accessible even in southern regions where natural snow is rare. My cousin lives in Harbin, (4) ______ is often called China's "Ice City." She has been figure skating since she was five and has won several provincial (5) ______ (compete). Watching her perform is truly magical — she glides across the ice so (6) ______ (grace) that it looks effortless. She told me that it took years of intense training (7) ______ (reach) her current level. She practises for four hours every day, often waking up at 4 am. Despite the tough schedule, she never complains because skating is her (8) ______ (great) passion. I tried ice skating for the first time last winter. I held onto the railing tightly, afraid of (9) ______ (fall). But with my cousin's help, I slowly gained confidence and managed to skate across the rink without support. It was a thrilling experience that made me understand (10) ______ people love winter sports so much.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`a growing number of` 为固定搭配，表示"越来越多的"。

2. **答案：achieved**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`has been achieved`，被动关系。

3. **答案：resorts**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`ski resorts` 表示"滑雪场"。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `Harbin`。

5. **答案：competitions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several competitions`。

6. **答案：gracefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `glides` 需要副词。

7. **答案：to reach**
   - **考点**：不定式。`it took years to reach`，`it takes time to do sth`。

8. **答案：greatest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`her greatest passion`。

9. **答案：falling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`afraid of falling`，介词后接动名词。

10. **答案：why**
    - **考点**：连接副词。`understand why people love...`，引导宾语从句。
