# 12-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：观鸟 (Bird Watching)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Bird watching, also known as birding, is a hobby enjoyed by millions of people worldwide. It involves observing birds in their natural habitats and (1) ______ (identify) different species by their appearance, songs and behaviour. Unlike many other hobbies, birding requires very little equipment — just a pair of binoculars and a good field guide are enough to get (2) ______ (start). China is home (3) ______ over 1,400 bird species, making it one of the most (4) ______ (diversity) countries for birding in the world. From the cranes of northeastern wetlands to the parrots of tropical Yunnan, there is always something new to discover. My biology teacher, Mr Zhao, is an enthusiastic birder. He (5) ______ (take) students on birding trips to the local wetland park every spring and autumn. During these trips, he teaches us how to identify birds by their calls and (6) ______ (fly) patterns. He also explains the important role that birds play in the ecosystem, such as controlling insect (7) ______ (populate) and spreading plant seeds. Last October, we were lucky enough to spot a rare black-faced spoonbill during one of our trips. Mr Zhao was so (8) ______ (excite) that he almost dropped his binoculars. He told us that there were (9) ______ (few) than 6,000 of these birds left in the world. Seeing such a rare creature in the wild made me appreciate how important it is to protect (10) ______ (nature) habitats.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：identifying**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `observing` 并列。

2. **答案：started**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`get started` 表示"开始"。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`be home to` 表示"是……的栖息地"。

4. **答案：diverse**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most diverse countries`，`diversity` 变为形容词。

5. **答案：takes**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。`every spring and autumn` 提示经常性动作。

6. **答案：flying**
   - **考点**：动名词/现在分词作定语。`flying patterns` 表示"飞行模式"。

7. **答案：populations**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`insect populations`。

8. **答案：excited**
   - **考点**：词性转换。描述人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

9. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`fewer than 6,000`，修饰可数名词用 `fewer`。

10. **答案：natural**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `habitats` 需要形容词。
