# 12-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：作业争论 (The Homework Debate)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The question of whether students should have homework has been debated for years. Some educators believe that homework (1) ______ (help) students review what they have learned and develop self-discipline. Others argue that too much homework puts unnecessary pressure (2) ______ students and robs them of their free time. In China, many middle school students spend three to four hours on homework every evening. A survey conducted last year found that 80% of students wished they had (3) ______ (little) homework so they could spend more time on hobbies and rest. In response, the government introduced the "Double Reduction" policy to lighten students' workload. Schools are now required (4) ______ (limit) the amount of homework (5) ______ (assign) to students each day. My school has made several changes since the policy was introduced. Homework (6) ______ (reduce) by about 30%, and teachers are encouraged to design more creative and meaningful tasks instead of repetitive exercises. For example, our English teacher now asks us to watch a short video and discuss it in class, rather (7) ______ copying vocabulary lists ten times. I think this approach is much more (8) ______ (effect) because it makes learning fun. However, not everyone agrees with the policy. Some parents worry that (9) ______ (reduce) homework will cause their children to fall behind. They believe that hard work is the key to success. I think the best solution is to find a balance — homework should be (10) ______ (meaning) and manageable, not overwhelming.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：helps**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`homework` 为不可数名词，第三人称单数。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`put pressure on sb` 表示"给某人施加压力"。

3. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：比较级。`less homework`，`homework` 不可数，用 `less`。

4. **答案：to limit**
   - **考点**：不定式。`are required to do sth`。

5. **答案：assigned**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`homework assigned to students`，被动关系。

6. **答案：has been reduced**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。`since the policy was introduced` 提示现在完成时。

7. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`rather than` 表示"而不是"。

8. **答案：effective**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more effective`，`effect` 变为形容词。

9. **答案：reducing**
   - **考点**：动名词。动名词作主语。

10. **答案：meaningful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `manageable` 并列，需要形容词。
