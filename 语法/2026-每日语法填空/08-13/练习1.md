# 08-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：水墨画 (Chinese Ink Painting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese ink painting is an art form that has been practised (1) ______ thousands of years. Unlike Western oil painting, which uses (2) ______ (colour) paints to fill every corner of the canvas, ink painting often leaves large areas of blank space. This blank space is not empty — it invites the viewer (3) ______ (imagine) what might be there. The main tools are simple: a brush, ink, water, and rice paper. But creating a good painting requires years of practice. My art teacher, Mr. Sun, is a master of ink painting. He can paint a vivid bird with just three or four (4) ______ (stroke) of his brush. He says the key is to capture the spirit of the subject rather than copy every (5) ______ (detail). Last month, he asked us to paint bamboo. Bamboo is one of the most (6) ______ (common) painted subjects in Chinese art because it represents strength and (7) ______ (honest). I practised the basic strokes dozens of (8) ______ (time) before I was satisfied with my work. When I showed the painting to my grandmother, she said it reminded (9) ______ of the ones she saw in her childhood. I was so happy that I framed it and hung it (10) ______ my bedroom wall.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for thousands of years` 表示"数千年来"。

2. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `paints` 需要形容词，`colour` 变为 `colourful`。

3. **答案：to imagine**
   - **考点**：不定式。`invite sb to do sth` 为固定搭配。

4. **答案：strokes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`three or four` 修饰可数名词复数。

5. **答案：detail**
   - **考点**：名词。`every detail` 表示"每个细节"，`every` 后接单数名词。

6. **答案：commonly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `painted` 需要副词，`common` 变为 `commonly`；`the most commonly painted` 为最高级形式。

7. **答案：honesty**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `strength` 并列，需要名词，`honest` 变为 `honesty`。

8. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`dozens of times` 表示"几十次"。

9. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`remind sb of sth` 中 `sb` 用宾格；指代奶奶，用 `her`。

10. **答案：on**
    - **考点**：介词。`on the wall` 表示"在墙上"。
