# 08-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：考试焦虑 (Dealing with Exam Anxiety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Almost every student has experienced exam anxiety at some point. A certain amount of nervousness is (1) ______ (nature) and can even help us perform better. But when anxiety becomes too (2) ______ (strength), it can affect our health and results. Before big exams, some students cannot sleep well, lose their appetite, or feel (3) ______ (sick) than usual. My friend Xiao Ming used to be so anxious that his hands (4) ______ (shake) during tests. Last year, our school psychologist taught us several ways (5) ______ (manage) exam stress. The first method is deep breathing. When you feel nervous, take five slow breaths, (6) ______ (focus) on the air going in and out. The second is positive self-talk. Instead of saying "I'm going to fail," tell (7) ______ (you), "I have prepared well and I will do my best." The third is proper preparation. (8) ______ (study) a little each day is much more effective than cramming the night before. Since learning these techniques, Xiao Ming (9) ______ (become) much calmer during tests. He even got his best score last month! Exam anxiety is normal, but it does not have to control (10) ______. With the right tools, we can turn our nervousness into positive energy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：natural**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is` 后接形容词作表语，`nature` 变为 `natural`。

2. **答案：strong**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`too + 形容词` 结构，`strength` 变为 `strong`。

3. **答案：sicker**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than usual` 提示用比较级，`sick` 的比较级为 `sicker`。

4. **答案：shook**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`used to be` 引导过去习惯，`shake` 的过去式为 `shook`。

5. **答案：to manage**
   - **考点**：不定式。`ways to do sth` 中不定式作后置定语。

6. **答案：focusing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。呼吸的同时"集中注意力"在空气的进出上。

7. **答案：yourself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语隐含 `you`，宾语指向同一人，用 `yourself`。

8. **答案：Studying**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。`Studying a little each day` 在句中作主语，句首大写。

9. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since learning these techniques` 提示用现在完成时。

10. **答案：us**
    - **考点**：代词宾格。`control us` 中 `us` 作宾语，与全文中 `we/our` 一致。
