# 05-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：考前复习策略 (Exam Review Strategies)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Exam season can feel stressful, 1. ________ a clear review plan makes a difference. First, list the topics you find 2. ________ (difficulty) and give them extra time. Short study blocks work 3. ________ (good) than one long night without sleep.

Second, turn mistakes into lessons. When I missed a grammar point, I copied the correct sentence 4. ________ read it aloud the next morning. My notebook became 5. ________ useful tool instead of a pile of fear.

Third, ask teachers questions bravely. They have seen many exams and can explain problems 6. ________ (quick). I also review with classmates because explaining a topic to others helps me understand it 7. ________ (deep).

Remember to sleep well and eat regularly. A tired brain forgets things 8. ________ (easy) than a rested one. On the exam day, take a deep breath and trust the effort you 9. ________ (put) in.

Pressure is normal, 10. ________ you are not alone in facing it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **but / yet**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“考试季有压力”与“清晰复习计划能改变局面”形成转折。

2. **difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词，有提示词）。find sth. + 形容词，difficulty → difficult。

3. **better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。than 提示比较级，well 的比较级为 better，修饰动词 work。

4. **and**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。copied 与 read 为顺承并列，用 and。

5. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a useful tool，useful 以辅音音素 /j/ 开头，用 a。

6. **quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 explain，用 quickly。

7. **more deeply / deeper**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。与之前理解程度对比，用 more deeply；deep 作副词时比较级也可用 deeper。

8. **more easily**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。than 提示比较级，easy 的副词为 easily，多音节比较级用 more easily。

9. **have put**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时，有提示词）。trust the effort you have put in 表示“相信你已付出的努力”，强调对现在的影响，用 have put。

10. **but**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“有压力很正常”与“你并非独自面对”形成转折或安慰性对比，用 but。
