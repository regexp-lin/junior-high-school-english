# 05-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：乡村振兴 (Rural Revitalization)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Rural revitalization brings new roads, better clinics, and lively markets back to villages. Many young workers have returned home 1. ________ start small businesses because they see hope in their hometowns.

During the winter holiday, I visited my uncle's fruit farm. Online sales 2. ________ (help) his family earn more money, and tourists came to pick strawberries on sunny weekends. Uncle said the village used to be quiet, 3. ________ now children's laughter fills the square again.

Local schools 4. ________ (receive) new computers last year, and volunteers teach art after class. Farmers are also learning 5. ________ (use) apps to check weather and prices.

The government plans to support green farming so that rivers can stay clean 6. ________ years to come. I took photos of the clean stream and posted them 7. ________ my blog.

I used to think cities were the only exciting places, 8. ________ my trip changed that idea. Country life today mixes tradition 9. ________ modern ideas.

If we respect the land and work together, rural areas will grow 10. ________ (strong) than before.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的，无提示词）。return home to start... 表示“回家是为了创业”，用 to。

2. **have helped**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时，有提示词）。强调网络销售对收入已产生持续影响，用 have helped；主语 sales 为复数。

3. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“过去安静”与“如今广场充满笑声”形成转折。

4. **received**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时，有提示词）。last year 为明确过去时间，用 received。

5. **to use**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（有提示词）。learn to do sth.，用不定式 to use。

6. **for / in**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。for years to come / in years to come 表示“在未来的岁月里”。

7. **on**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。post sth. on one's blog 表示“发布在博客上”。

8. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“曾以为只有城市精彩”与“旅行改变想法”形成转折。

9. **with**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。mix A with B 表示“将传统与现代理念结合”。

10. **stronger**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级（有提示词）。than before 提示比较级，strong → stronger。
