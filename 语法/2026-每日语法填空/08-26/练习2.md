# 08-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园广播站 (School Radio Station)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has a radio station that broadcasts during lunchtime every day. I (1) ______ (be) a member of the radio team since last September. My job is to choose music and read news to the (2) ______ (whole) school. At first, I was very nervous about (3) ______ (speak) into the microphone because I was afraid of (4) ______ (make) mistakes. My teacher encouraged me and said, "Don't worry. Everyone makes mistakes (5) ______ they are learning." Her words gave me confidence. Now I feel much (6) ______ (comfortable) reading aloud in front of the microphone. Every week, we receive letters (7) ______ students who want to hear their favourite songs. Sometimes they also share (8) ______ (story) about their school life. Last month, a boy wrote to us about how he (9) ______ (overcome) his fear of public speaking, which touched many listeners. Running the radio station has taught me that even a (10) ______ (quietly) voice can be heard when you have something meaningful to say.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have been**
   - **考点**：时态。`since last September` 提示用现在完成时，`I have been a member`。

2. **答案：whole**
   - **考点**：形容词。`the whole school` 表示"全校"，`whole` 已给出提示词，直接用原形作定语。

3. **答案：speaking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`about` 是介词，后面接动名词形式 `speaking`。

4. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：动名词。`be afraid of doing sth` 是固定搭配，介词 `of` 后接动名词。

5. **答案：when / while**
   - **考点**：连词。`when/while they are learning` 表示"当他们在学习的时候"。

6. **答案：more comfortable**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much more comfortable` 表示"舒服得多"，`much` 修饰比较级。

7. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`receive letters from sb` 表示"收到某人的来信"。

8. **答案：stories**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`share stories` 表示分享多个故事，`story` 变为复数 `stories`。

9. **答案：overcame / had overcome**
   - **考点**：时态。描述过去发生的事用一般过去时 `overcame`，或用过去完成时 `had overcome` 表示"克服"发生在"写信"之前。

10. **答案：quiet**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `voice` 需要形容词，`quietly` 变为形容词 `quiet`。
