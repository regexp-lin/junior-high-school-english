# 08-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：夜市美食 (Night Market Food)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Night markets are an important part of Chinese food culture. Every summer evening, the streets near my home (1) ______ (fill) with the smell of delicious food. There are dozens of stalls (2) ______ (sell) everything from barbecued meat to sweet desserts. My favourite stall is run (3) ______ an old couple who make the best fried noodles in our city. They have been cooking at this market (4) ______ over twenty years. The noodles taste so good (5) ______ people are willing to wait in a long line for them. Last weekend, I took my foreign friend Tom to the night market. He was amazed at the (6) ______ (various) of food available. He tried stinky tofu for the (7) ______ (one) time and found it surprisingly (8) ______ (taste). "I never expected street food in China to be this good!" he said (9) ______ (excited). We also had some fresh fruit juice to cool (10) ______ (we) down on the hot summer night. Tom said he wanted to come back every evening.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：are filled**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the streets` 是"被充满"，且 `every summer evening` 表经常性动作，用一般现在时被动语态 `are filled`。

2. **答案：selling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`stalls selling everything` 现在分词作后置定语，修饰 `stalls`。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`be run by sb` 表示"由某人经营"。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for over twenty years` 表示持续了二十多年，与现在完成进行时搭配。

5. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so good that...` 表示"如此好以至于……"。

6. **答案：variety**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the variety of` 表示"……的多样性"，`various` 变为名词 `variety`。

7. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。`for the first time` 表示"第一次"，`one` 变为序数词 `first`。

8. **答案：tasty**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found it tasty` 中 `tasty` 作宾语补足语，`taste` 变为形容词 `tasty`。

9. **答案：excitedly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `said` 需要副词，`excited` 变为 `excitedly`。

10. **答案：ourselves**
    - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 `we`，用反身代词 `ourselves`，`cool ourselves down` 表示"给自己降温"。
