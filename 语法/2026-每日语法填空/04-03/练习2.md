# 04-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：电影与文化 (Movies and Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Movies are one of the most popular forms of entertainment in the world. They tell stories, express 1. ________ (emotion), and help people understand different cultures.

Chinese movies have gained more and more 2. ________ (popular) around the world in recent years. Films like "Ne Zha" and "The Wandering Earth" have been 3. ________ (huge) successful. They show the world the creativity and talent of Chinese 4. ________ (film) makers.

Watching movies in English is a great way to improve your language skills. You can learn new words and expressions 5. ________ listening to real conversations. It also helps you understand 6. ________ native speakers actually talk in daily life. At first, you might need subtitles to follow the story, but 7. ________ you watch more, your listening ability will improve.

Of course, not all movies are suitable 8. ________ students. Some contain violence or other unhealthy content. It is important for teenagers to choose movies 9. ________ (wise) and learn to tell the difference between good and bad influences.

A good movie can open your eyes to a 10. ________ (wide) world. It can make you laugh, cry, think and dream. So next time you watch a movie, pay attention not just to the story, but also to the culture behind it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **emotions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。express 后接名词作宾语，emotion 用复数表示"各种情感"。

2. **popularity**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。more and more 修饰名词，popular → popularity。

3. **hugely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 successful，需用副词形式 hugely。

4. **film**
   - **考点**：名词作定语（无需变形）。film makers 意为"电影制作人"，film 作定语修饰 makers，不需变形。

5. **by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。by listening to 意为"通过听……"，by 表示方式。

6. **how**
   - **考点**：连接副词（无提示词填空）。understand how 意为"了解……如何"，how 引导宾语从句。

7. **as/when**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。as/when you watch more 意为"当你看得更多的时候"，引导时间状语从句。

8. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be suitable for 意为"适合……"，是固定搭配。

9. **wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 choose，需用副词形式 wisely。

10. **wider**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。根据语境"打开你的眼界看到一个更广阔的世界"，暗含比较，wide → wider。
