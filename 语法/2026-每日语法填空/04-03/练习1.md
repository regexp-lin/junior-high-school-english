# 04-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：垃圾分类 (Waste Sorting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Waste sorting has become a hot topic in China. Many cities have introduced new rules that require people 1. ________ (separate) their rubbish into different categories.

In most cities, waste is divided into four types: recyclable waste, kitchen waste, harmful waste and other waste. Each type has its own colored bin. For example, blue bins are used 2. ________ recyclable items like paper and plastic bottles, while green bins are for kitchen waste. The purpose of waste sorting is 3. ________ (protect) the environment and save resources.

At first, many people found it 4. ________ (confuse) because they didn't know which bin to use. Some people just threw everything into 5. ________ same bin as before. However, as time went by, more and more people 6. ________ (begin) to follow the new rules.

Our school has also taken action. We have different bins in every 7. ________ (class), and students take turns to be the "waste sorting monitor." The monitors help check 8. ________ the rubbish has been sorted correctly.

Waste sorting may seem like a small thing, but it can make a big 9. ________ (different) to our planet. If everyone does 10. ________ (they) part, we can build a cleaner and greener world together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **to separate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。require sb. to do sth. 意为"要求某人做某事"。

2. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be used for 意为"被用于……"，后接名词或动名词。

3. **to protect**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作表语）。the purpose is to do sth. 意为"目的是做某事"。

4. **confusing**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。find it + 形容词，修饰事物用 -ing 形式，confusing 意为"令人困惑的"。

5. **the**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。the same 意为"同一个"，same 前必须加定冠词 the。

6. **began**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。as time went by 提示用过去时，begin → began。

7. **classroom**
   - **考点**：词性转换/构词法（名词合成）。every 后接单数名词，class → classroom（教室）。

8. **whether/if**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。check whether/if 意为"检查是否……"，引导宾语从句。

9. **difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。make a big difference 意为"产生很大影响"，different → difference。

10. **their**
    - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 part，they → their，do their part 意为"尽自己的一份力"。
