# 04-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：感恩父母 (Being Grateful to Parents)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our parents give us love and care from the day we are born. But how often do we stop and think about everything they have done 1. ________ us?

My mother is a nurse. She works long hours at the hospital and often comes home 2. ________ (tire). But no matter how late it is, she always checks my homework and prepares 3. ________ (I) lunch box for the next day. I used to take all of this for granted until one thing changed my mind.

Last month, my mother got sick and had to stay in bed for a week. Suddenly, nobody woke me up in the morning. Nobody reminded me 4. ________ (bring) my textbooks. The house felt empty and 5. ________ (quiet) than usual. I realized how much I depended on her.

During that week, I tried my best to take care of her. I cooked simple meals, 6. ________ they didn't taste very good. I also cleaned the house, 7. ________ was harder than I expected. But seeing my mother smile made all the hard work 8. ________ (worth).

Now I understand that love is not just about 9. ________ (receive). We should also learn to give back. Even small things, like saying "thank you" or giving them a hug, can make our parents feel 10. ________ (happiness).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。done for us 意为"为我们所做的"，for 表示"为了"。

2. **tired**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。come home tired 意为"疲惫地回家"，tired 作伴随状态的补语，修饰人用 -ed 形式。

3. **my**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 lunch box，I → my。

4. **to bring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。remind sb. to do sth. 意为"提醒某人做某事"。

5. **quieter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，quiet → quieter。

6. **but/though**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后句构成转折关系："我做了简单的饭菜，但味道不太好"。

7. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，which 指代前面"打扫房子"这件事。

8. **worthwhile/worth it**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。make + 宾语 + 形容词，worth → worthwhile（值得的）。也可理解为 worth it。

9. **receiving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 about 后接动名词形式。

10. **happy**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。make sb. feel + 形容词，happiness → happy。
