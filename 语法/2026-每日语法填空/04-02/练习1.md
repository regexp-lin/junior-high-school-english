# 04-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：博物馆参观 (Visiting a Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our class went to the National Science Museum. It was the first time many of us 1. ________ (visit) such a large museum.

The museum had five floors, and each floor showed different topics. On the first floor, there were models of dinosaurs. Some of them were so huge 2. ________ we had to look up to see their heads. A guide told us that dinosaurs 3. ________ (disappear) about 65 million years ago, and scientists are still trying to find out the exact reason.

The 4. ________ (two) floor was about space. We saw a model of the solar system and learned interesting 5. ________ (fact) about each planet. I was surprised to learn that a day on Venus is 6. ________ (long) than a year on Venus! Our teacher explained that this is because Venus rotates very 7. ________ (slow).

My favorite part was the 8. ________ (interact) area on the fourth floor. We could do science experiments 9. ________ ourselves. I tried an experiment about electricity and successfully lit up a small light bulb. It made me feel like a real scientist.

We spent the whole day at the museum, but we still didn't have enough time to see everything. I told my parents that I wanted to go back again. 10. ________ (visit) museums is a great way to learn outside the classroom.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **had visited**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时）。"It was the first time + (that) + 主语 + had done" 是固定句型，意为"这是某人第一次做某事"。

2. **that**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。so...that... 意为"如此……以至于……"，引导结果状语从句。

3. **disappeared**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。"about 65 million years ago" 提示用一般过去时。

4. **second**
   - **考点**：序数词。the + 序数词，two → second。

5. **facts**
   - **考点**：名词复数。interesting facts 意为"有趣的事实"，fact 为可数名词，此处为复数。

6. **longer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，long → longer。

7. **slowly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 rotates，需用副词形式 slowly。

8. **interactive**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 area，interact → interactive（互动的）。

9. **by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。by ourselves 意为"独自地、靠我们自己"。

10. **Visiting**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词 Visiting 在句首作主语，注意首字母大写。
