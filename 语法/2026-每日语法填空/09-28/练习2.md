# 09-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：错题本 (Mistake Notebook)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

One of the most useful study tools I have ever discovered is the mistake notebook. My maths teacher first introduced it to our class at the (1) ______ (begin) of this school year. She asked each student (2) ______ (prepare) a special notebook for recording wrong answers from tests and homework. The idea is simple: whenever you make a mistake, you copy the question into your notebook, write down the correct answer and explain (3) ______ you got it wrong. I was sceptical at first and thought it was (4) ______ waste of time. But after using it for two months, I realised its value. By reviewing my mistake notebook (5) ______ (regular), I could see patterns in my errors. I discovered that I often made mistakes with fraction (6) ______ (calculate) and geometry proofs. Once I identified these weak areas, I could focus my study time more (7) ______ (effective). My maths scores improved (8) ______ (steady) from 75 to 92 in just one term. Now I keep mistake (9) ______ (notebook) for all my major subjects. My classmate Zhang Wei, who started his mistake notebook the same time as me, has also seen great (10) ______ (improve). We both agree that the key is not just writing down mistakes but actually learning from them.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：beginning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`at the beginning of`，`begin` 变为名词 `beginning`。

2. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：不定式。`ask sb to do sth` 表示"要求某人做某事"。

3. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`explain why you got it wrong`，`why` 引导宾语从句，表"为什么"。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`a waste of time` 为固定搭配。

5. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `reviewing` 需要副词，`regular` 变为 `regularly`。

6. **答案：calculations**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`fraction calculations`，`calculate` 变为名词 `calculation`，复数。

7. **答案：effectively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `focus` 需要副词，`effective` 变为 `effectively`。

8. **答案：steadily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词，`steady` 变为 `steadily`。

9. **答案：notebooks**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`mistake notebooks for all my major subjects`，多个科目用复数。

10. **答案：improvement**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`great improvement`，形容词修饰名词，`improve` 变为 `improvement`。
