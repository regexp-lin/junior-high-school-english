# 09-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：街头艺人 (Street Performers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Street performers add colour and life to our cities. On a warm Saturday afternoon, you might see (1) ______ (music) playing guitars, dancers performing on sidewalks, or painters creating art right before your eyes. Last month, while walking through a busy pedestrian street with my friends, we came (2) ______ a man playing the violin. He stood at a corner, his eyes (3) ______ (close), completely lost in the music. The melody was so (4) ______ (beauty) that passers-by stopped to listen. A small crowd gathered around (5) ______. Some people dropped coins into the open violin case at (6) ______ (he) feet. After he finished the piece, everyone clapped (7) ______ (enthusiastic). My friend told me that the man used to be a professional musician (8) ______ had performed in concert halls. He chose to play on the street because he wanted to bring music (9) ______ ordinary people. I found his story very inspiring. Not everyone performs for fame or money — some people perform simply because they love what they do and want (10) ______ (share) that love with the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：musicians**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`music` 变为表示人的名词 `musician`，复数。

2. **答案：across**
   - **考点**：介词（短语动词）。`come across` 表示"偶然遇到"。

3. **答案：closed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。独立主格结构 `his eyes closed`，表"闭着"。

4. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so beautiful that...`，`beauty` 变为形容词 `beautiful`。

5. **答案：him**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`gathered around him`，介词后接宾格。

6. **答案：his**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`at his feet`，物主代词修饰名词。

7. **答案：enthusiastically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `clapped` 需要副词，`enthusiastic` 变为 `enthusiastically`。

8. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a professional musician`（指人），引导定语从句。

9. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`bring music to ordinary people` 表示"把音乐带给普通人"。

10. **答案：to share**
    - **考点**：不定式。`want to do sth` 表示"想要做某事"。
