# 11-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：传统手工月饼 (Traditional Handmade Mooncakes)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mooncakes are an essential part of the Mid-Autumn Festival, but in recent years, factory-produced mooncakes have largely replaced the handmade ones. My grandmother still makes mooncakes (1) ______ hand every year, using a recipe that has been in our family (2) ______ generations. Last September, she invited me to help her. We started by preparing the filling — a (3) ______ (mix) of lotus seed paste and salted egg yolks. She showed me how to wrap the filling in dough and press each mooncake into a wooden mould (4) ______ (careful). The mould had beautiful patterns of flowers and Chinese characters. Getting the shape right was (5) ______ (hard) than I expected. My first few mooncakes looked quite (6) ______ (mess), but Grandma laughed and said they had "character". After (7) ______ (shape) all the mooncakes, we brushed them with egg wash and baked them in the oven for about 25 minutes. The kitchen was soon filled (8) ______ a warm, sweet aroma. When the mooncakes came out, they were golden brown and looked (9) ______ (absolute) perfect. We packed some into gift boxes and gave them to our relatives and neighbours. Everyone said they tasted much (10) ______ (good) than the ones from shops. I felt proud to help keep our family tradition alive.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`makes mooncakes by hand` 表示"手工制作月饼"。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for generations` 表示"世世代代"。

3. **答案：mixture**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a mixture of`，冠词后接名词，`mix` 变为 `mixture`。

4. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `press` 需要副词。

5. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`harder than I expected`。

6. **答案：messy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`looked quite messy`，系动词后接形容词，`mess` 变为 `messy`。

7. **答案：shaping**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `after` 后接动名词。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`was filled with` 表示"充满了"。

9. **答案：absolutely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `perfect` 需要副词。

10. **答案：better**
    - **考点**：比较级。`much better than`，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。
