# 11-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：时间管理 (Time Management)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Time management is a skill that every student needs (1) ______ (learn). With homework, extra lessons, hobbies and social activities, it can be hard to find enough time for everything. Last year, I often felt overwhelmed because I didn't know how (2) ______ (organise) my time properly. I would waste hours on my phone and then rush (3) ______ (finish) my homework late at night. This made me tired the next day, and my grades started to drop. My father noticed the problem and suggested I try using a (4) ______ (day) planner. I was sceptical at first, (5) ______ I decided to give it a try. Every evening, I now write down the tasks I need to complete the next day and estimate how long each one will take. I also set (6) ______ (priority) — important and urgent tasks come first, while less important ones can wait. One of the biggest (7) ______ (change) I made was limiting my screen time to 30 minutes a day. This alone freed up nearly two hours (8) ______ studying and exercise. Within a month, my grades improved and I felt much (9) ______ (little) stressed. I realised that time management is not about doing (10) ______ (much) things — it's about doing the right things at the right time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：不定式。`need to learn`。

2. **答案：to organise**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to organise`，疑问词 + 不定式。

3. **答案：to finish**
   - **考点**：不定式。`rush to finish` 表示"赶着完成"。

4. **答案：daily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `planner` 需要形容词，`day` 变为 `daily`。

5. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`was sceptical but decided to try`，前后转折。

6. **答案：priorities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`set priorities` 表示"设定优先级"。

7. **答案：changes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the biggest changes`。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`freed up hours for studying` 表示"腾出时间用于学习"。

9. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much less stressed`，`little` 的比较级为 `less`。

10. **答案：more**
    - **考点**：比较级。`doing more things`，暗含比较。
