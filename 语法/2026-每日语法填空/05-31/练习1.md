# 05-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：世界无烟日 (World No Tobacco Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

World No Tobacco Day falls on May 31st each year. It reminds the public 1. ________ smoking harms the lungs and heart. Many countries hold talks and posters 2. ________ (encourage) teenagers to stay away from cigarettes.

My cousin once thought vaping looked cool, 3. ________ a doctor showed him photos of damaged tissue. He felt scared and decided to quit 4. ________ (immediate). Now he runs in the park and says fresh air tastes 5. ________ (sweet) than smoke.

Schools teach students to refuse offers politely and to choose healthy hobbies instead. Second-hand smoke is unfair 6. ________ people nearby, so laws ban smoking in buses and restaurants.

We should support family members 7. ________ want to stop smoking and cheer every small progress 8. ________ make.

Protecting health is not a one-day event; it needs daily choices. If you have never lit a cigarette, keep 9. ________ wise decision. If you are trying to quit, remember that help is always 10. ________ (close) than you think.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **that**
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词（无提示词填空）。reminds the public that... 后接从句说明提醒内容，从句结构完整，用 that。

2. **to encourage**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（有提示词）。hold talks... to encourage... 不定式表目的，用 to encourage。

3. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“曾以为电子烟很酷”与“医生展示受损组织照片”形成转折。

4. **immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 quit，用 immediately。

5. **sweeter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（有提示词）。than smoke 提示比较级，sweet → sweeter；taste 为系动词，后接形容词。

6. **to**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。be unfair to sb. 表示“对某人不公平”。

7. **who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。定语从句修饰 family members，从句缺主语，指人，用 who 或 that。

8. **they**
   - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。cheer every small progress they make，they 指代 family members，与定语从句常见结构 progress (that) they make 一致。

9. **that**
   - **考点**：指示代词/限定词（无提示词填空）。keep that wise decision 表示“坚持那个明智决定”，指从未吸烟的选择。

10. **closer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级（有提示词）。than you think 提示比较级，close → closer；help is closer 表示“帮助比你想象的更近”。
