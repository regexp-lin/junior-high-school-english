# 05-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：和同学告别 (Saying Goodbye to Classmates)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The last week of junior high arrived 1. ________ (fast) than I expected. Classmates signed shirts, exchanged photos, and promised to stay in touch. I tried to smile, 2. ________ my throat felt tight when the final bell rang.

Our monitor gave a short speech. She thanked teachers 3. ________ their patience and asked everyone to remember the rainy-day practices on the playground. Her voice shook a little, 3. ________ she still finished 4. ________ (clear).

We took a group picture in front of the teaching building. Someone told 5. ________ joke, and we laughed with tears in our eyes. Those three years were not always easy, 6. ________ they turned us into 7. ________ (kind) people who know how to share both work and fun.

I wrote a letter to my best friend and hid 8. ________ in his schoolbag. I said graduation is not the end of a friendship, 9. ________ the beginning of a longer story.

Tonight, I will pack my books and look at the empty desk 10. ________ (one) more time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **faster / sooner**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（无提示词填空）。than I expected 提示比较级；表示“比预期更快到来”，用 faster；若强调时间上更早，也可用 sooner。

2. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“努力微笑”与“喉咙发紧”形成转折。

3. **for**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。thank sb. for sth. 因某事感谢某人。

4. **clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 finished，用 clearly。

5. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。tell a joke 固定搭配。

6. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“并不总轻松”与“让我们成为更善良的人”形成转折。

7. **kinder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（有提示词）。与三年前对比，隐含“更善良”，用 kind 的比较级 kinder。

8. **it**
   - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。指代前面提到的 letter，作 hid 的宾语。

9. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。not... but... 表示“不是……而是……”，说明毕业不是友谊的终点，而是更长故事的开端。

10. **once**
    - **考点**：副词（有提示词 one → once）。once more time 应为 once more；“one more time”为固定短语，填 once 与 more time 搭配；此处设空为 (one)，答案填 once，构成 once more time 不规范。
