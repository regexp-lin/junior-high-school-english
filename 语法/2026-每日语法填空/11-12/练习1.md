# 11-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：社交媒体 (Social Media)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Social media has changed the way people communicate with (1) ______ other. Platforms like WeChat and Douyin allow users (2) ______ (share) photos, videos and thoughts instantly. According to a recent report, about 73% of Chinese teenagers use social media (3) ______ (regular). While social media offers many benefits, it also brings some problems. One major concern is that teenagers spend too much time (4) ______ (scroll) through their phones instead of studying or exercising. A study found that students who use social media for more than three hours a day perform (5) ______ (bad) in school than those who limit their screen time. Another problem is cyberbullying. Some people post (6) ______ (hurt) comments online, which can cause serious emotional damage to young people. My friend Xiao Hong once received several mean messages from (7) ______ anonymous account. She felt so upset that she (8) ______ (not want) to go to school for a whole week. Fortunately, her parents and teachers helped her get through it. They reported the account to the platform, and it (9) ______ (block) within 24 hours. This experience taught us that we should always be kind online and report any bad behaviour (10) ______ we see it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：each**
   - **考点**：代词。`each other` 为固定搭配，表示"彼此"。

2. **答案：to share**
   - **考点**：不定式。`allow sb to do sth` 表示"允许某人做某事"。

3. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `use` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：scrolling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 表示"花时间做某事"。

5. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than those who...` 提示比较级，`badly` 的比较级为 `worse`。

6. **答案：hurtful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `comments` 需要形容词，`hurt` 变为 `hurtful`。

7. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`an anonymous account`，`anonymous` 以元音音素开头，用 `an`。

8. **答案：didn't want**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的事件，否定形式用 `didn't want`。

9. **答案：was blocked**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`it`（the account）与 `block` 之间是被动关系，过去时。

10. **答案：when / if**
    - **考点**：连词。`when/if we see it` 表示"当/如果我们看到时"，引导时间/条件状语从句。
