# 11-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中医文化 (Traditional Chinese Medicine)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) is a medical system that has been used in China (1) ______ thousands of years. Unlike Western medicine, which focuses on treating specific diseases, TCM takes a holistic approach — it treats the whole person rather (2) ______ just the symptoms. The basic idea behind TCM is that the body must maintain a balance of "yin" and "yang" to stay (3) ______ (health). When this balance is disrupted, people get sick. TCM includes many different treatments, such as herbal medicine, acupuncture and massage. Acupuncture, the practice of (4) ______ (insert) thin needles into specific points on the body, is perhaps the most (5) ______ (wide) known TCM treatment outside China. My grandfather is a retired TCM doctor. He spent his entire career (6) ______ (help) patients with traditional methods. Even after retirement, neighbours still come to him for advice when they feel unwell. He often (7) ______ (prepare) herbal teas for our family, especially during winter. He says that certain herbs can strengthen the immune system and prevent (8) ______ (we) from catching colds. In recent years, TCM (9) ______ (attract) growing attention around the world. Many countries have begun to include TCM in their healthcare systems. This makes my grandfather very proud, (10) ______ he believes that TCM is a treasure that belongs to all of humanity.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for thousands of years` 表示持续时间。

2. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`rather than` 表示"而不是"。

3. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`stay healthy`，系动词 `stay` 后接形容词作表语。

4. **答案：inserting**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词形式。

5. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `known` 需要副词形式。

6. **答案：helping**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 表示"花时间做某事"。

7. **答案：prepares**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。描述经常性动作，`often` 提示一般现在时，主语为第三人称单数。

8. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词。`prevent sb from doing sth`，需要宾格代词 `us`。

9. **答案：has attracted**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`In recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

10. **答案：because / as**
    - **考点**：连词。引导原因状语从句，表示"因为"。
