# 07-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：暑假计划 (Summer Holiday Plans)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Summer holiday is coming, and I have already made a plan for (1) ______ (I). First, I will spend two weeks (2) ______ (learn) to swim at a sports centre near my home. Swimming is not only fun (3) ______ also a useful life skill. Then my family will travel to Yunnan, a province (4) ______ is known for its beautiful scenery and diverse cultures. I have been looking forward to (5) ______ (see) the ancient town of Lijiang. After we come back, I plan to read at least five (6) ______ (book) during the rest of the holiday. My English teacher (7) ______ (suggest) several novels to us before the term ended. She said reading in English was one of the (8) ______ (effective) ways to improve our vocabulary. Besides study and travel, I will help my parents with housework. I think I should learn to take care of (9) ______ (me) instead of depending on others. I hope this summer will be both relaxing and (10) ______ (meaning).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 `I`，宾语指向同一人，用反身代词 `myself`。`make a plan for myself` 表示"为自己制定计划"。

2. **答案：learning**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 为固定搭配，表示"不仅……而且……"。

4. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a province`（物），在定语从句中作主语，用 `that` 或 `which`。

5. **答案：seeing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`look forward to` 中 `to` 为介词，后接动名词 `seeing`。

6. **答案：books**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`five` 修饰可数名词复数，`book` 变为 `books`。

7. **答案：had suggested / suggested**
   - **考点**：过去完成时/一般过去时。`before the term ended` 表示"在学期结束之前"，建议发生在学期结束之前（过去的过去），用 `had suggested`；用一般过去时 `suggested` 也可接受。

8. **答案：most effective**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`effective` 为多音节形容词，最高级为 `most effective`。

9. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`take care of myself` 表示"照顾自己"，主语为 `I`，用 `myself`。

10. **答案：meaningful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`both...and...` 连接两个并列形容词，`relaxing` 与 `meaningful` 并列，`meaning` 变为形容词 `meaningful`（有意义的）。
