# 07-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园垃圾分类 (Waste Sorting at School)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school started a waste sorting programme last September. Four coloured bins (1) ______ (place) in every classroom: blue for recyclable items, green for kitchen waste, red for harmful things, and grey for other rubbish. At (2) ______ beginning, many students felt confused and threw waste into the wrong bins. To solve this problem, the student council made posters (3) ______ (show) what belonged to each category. They also invited an expert to give a talk (4) ______ the importance of waste sorting. Gradually, we got used to (5) ______ (separate) our rubbish. Now, our school produces much (6) ______ (little) unsorted waste than before. The canteen even turns food waste (7) ______ fertiliser for the school garden. I am proud of the progress we (8) ______ (make) so far. Waste sorting may seem like a small thing, but it has a big effect (9) ______ the environment. If every school in the country (10) ______ (do) the same, our planet will be much cleaner.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：were placed**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。垃圾桶"被放置"在教室里，叙述过去的事件，主语 `bins` 为复数，用 `were placed`。

2. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。`at the beginning` 为固定搭配，表示"在开始时"，用定冠词 `the`。

3. **答案：showing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`posters showing what...` 表示"展示……的海报"，现在分词修饰 `posters`。

4. **答案：on / about**
   - **考点**：介词。`a talk on/about sth` 表示"关于某事的讲座"。

5. **答案：separating**
   - **考点**：动名词。`get used to doing sth` 表示"习惯做某事"，`to` 为介词，后接动名词。

6. **答案：less**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than before` 提示用比较级；`waste` 为不可数名词，用 `little` 的比较级 `less`。

7. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`turn...into...` 为固定搭配，表示"把……变成……"。

8. **答案：have made**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`so far`（到目前为止）提示用现在完成时；主语 `we` 用 `have made`。

9. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`have an effect on` 为固定搭配，表示"对……产生影响"。

10. **答案：does**
    - **考点**：主谓一致 + "主将从现"。`if` 引导条件状语从句用一般现在时；`every school` 为第三人称单数，用 `does`。
