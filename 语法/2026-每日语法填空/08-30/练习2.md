# 08-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：社区志愿服务 (Community Volunteer Service)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Volunteering is a great way for teenagers to give back to their communities. Our school (1) ______ (organise) a community service day last month, and over 200 students took part in it. We (2) ______ (divide) into several groups, each with a different task. My group was responsible (3) ______ visiting a local nursing home. When we arrived, the elderly people were very happy (4) ______ (see) us. We helped them clean their rooms, chatted with them and even performed some short (5) ______ (programme) for them. An 85-year-old grandmother held my hand and told me that our visit was the (6) ______ (happy) thing that had happened to her in months. Her words touched me (7) ______ (deep). Another group of students went to clean up a nearby park. They collected rubbish, painted benches and planted (8) ______ (flower) along the paths. By the end of the day, the park looked much (9) ______ (clean) and more beautiful. I think volunteering not only benefits (10) ______ community but also helps us grow as individuals. I hope more students will join us next time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：organised**
   - **考点**：时态。`last month` 提示用一般过去时。

2. **答案：were divided**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`We were divided into...`，主语 `We` 是"被分成"，用被动。

3. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be responsible for` 表示"负责……"。

4. **答案：to see**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be happy to do sth` 表示"高兴做某事"。

5. **答案：programmes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`some short programmes`，`some` 修饰可数名词用复数。

6. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the happiest thing`，`happy` 的最高级为 `happiest`。

7. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `touched` 用副词，`deep` 变为 `deeply`。

8. **答案：flowers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`planted flowers`，种了多种花，用复数。

9. **答案：cleaner**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much cleaner`，`much` 修饰比较级 `cleaner`。

10. **答案：the**
    - **考点**：冠词。`the community` 表特指这个社区。
