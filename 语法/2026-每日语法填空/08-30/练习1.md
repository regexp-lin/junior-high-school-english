# 08-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：露营体验 (Camping Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, my family went camping near a lake in the countryside. It was the first time that I (1) ______ (sleep) in a tent. We arrived at the campsite in the afternoon and spent about an hour (2) ______ (set) up our tent. Dad made a campfire while Mum prepared food (3) ______ dinner. As night fell, the sky became incredibly clear. I had never seen (4) ______ many stars before. My little sister was so excited that she kept (5) ______ (point) at different star patterns. Dad told us stories about the constellations, which made the evening even (6) ______ (enjoyable). However, sleeping outdoors was not as (7) ______ (comfort) as I had expected. The ground was hard, and strange (8) ______ (sound) from the forest kept waking me up. I heard owls, insects and even what I thought was a fox. Despite these small (9) ______ (difficulty), the camping trip was a wonderful experience. It taught me to appreciate nature and to enjoy the (10) ______ (simple) things in life. I can't wait to go again next summer.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：had slept**
   - **考点**：时态。`It was the first time that sb had done sth` 是固定句型，从句用过去完成时。

2. **答案：setting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`spend time (in) doing sth`，用动名词 `setting`。

3. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`prepared food for dinner` 表示"为晚餐准备食物"。

4. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：副词。`so many stars`，`so` 修饰 `many`，表示"这么多"。

5. **答案：pointing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`keep doing sth` 表示"持续做某事"。

6. **答案：more enjoyable**
   - **考点**：比较级。`even more enjoyable`，`even` 修饰比较级，多音节形容词用 `more`。

7. **答案：comfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`not as comfortable as`，同级比较结构中用形容词原级，`comfort` 变为 `comfortable`。

8. **答案：sounds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`strange sounds` 表示"奇怪的声音"，此处指多种声音。

9. **答案：difficulties**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`these small difficulties`，`these` 提示用复数，`difficulty` 变为 `difficulties`。

10. **答案：simple**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`the simple things`，修饰名词 `things` 用形容词，`simple` 已经是形容词（或 `simplicity` 变回 `simple`，此处保持原形即可）。
