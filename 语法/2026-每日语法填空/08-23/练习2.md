# 08-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：日落的美丽 (The Beauty of Sunsets)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

There is something about sunsets that never fails to move me. No matter how many times I see one, each (1) ______ (feel) like the first. Last weekend, I climbed to the top of a small hill near my home to watch the sunset. The sky slowly changed colour — from bright blue (2) ______ soft orange, then to deep red and purple. The clouds looked (3) ______ if they were on fire. As the sun (4) ______ (sink) below the horizon, everything was bathed in a warm golden light. I sat there (5) ______ (quiet), with no phone and no music, just watching the show that nature (6) ______ (put) on for free every evening. A gentle wind blew through my hair, and I felt all my stress (7) ______ (disappear). For those few minutes, the world seemed perfectly at peace. I took a few photos, but they could not capture the true (8) ______ (beautiful) of what I saw. Some things are (9) ______ (good) experienced in person than through a screen. My grandmother once told me, "If you ever feel lost, go watch a sunset. It will remind you that there is always a new beginning (10) ______ (wait) for you." I think she was right.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：feels**
   - **考点**：主谓一致 + 一般现在时。`each` 为第三人称单数，描述一般性感受。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from...to...` 表示"从……到……"。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`as if` 表示"好像"。

4. **答案：sank**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`sink` 的过去式为 `sank`，叙述当时的场景。

5. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `sat` 需要副词，`quiet` 变为 `quietly`。

6. **答案：puts**
   - **考点**：一般现在时。`put on a show` 为固定搭配，表示"上演"，描述大自然每天的惯例，主语 `nature` 为第三人称单数。

7. **答案：disappear**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`felt all my stress disappear` 为感官动词 `feel` + 宾语 + 动词原形结构。

8. **答案：beauty**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the true` 后接名词，`beautiful` 变为 `beauty`。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

10. **答案：waiting**
    - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`a new beginning waiting for you` 表示"一个等待着你的新开始"。
