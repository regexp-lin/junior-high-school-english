# 08-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家庭菜谱传承 (Family Recipes)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every family has its own special recipes, and (1) ______ (we) is no exception. My grandmother's braised pork belly is a dish that everyone in our family (2) ______ (love) deeply. She first learned to make it from her mother more than fifty years (3) ______. The recipe has been passed down through three (4) ______ (generation). What makes her version special is the secret mix of spices she uses. She never measures anything — she simply adds a pinch of this and a dash of that, guided (5) ______ years of experience. I have watched her cook it many times and tried to copy her method, but my version never tastes quite as (6) ______ (good) as hers. She laughs and says, "The secret ingredient is love." Last Spring Festival, she finally agreed (7) ______ (write) down the recipe for me. We spent a whole afternoon in the kitchen together. She told me stories about each spice — where to buy the (8) ______ (fresh) ones and how to store them (9) ______ (proper). I wrote everything in a small notebook. Someday, I will teach my own children to make this dish, (10) ______ the tradition continues. Food is not just about taste — it is about memory and family bonds.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：ours**
   - **考点**：名词性物主代词。`ours` = `our family`，避免重复。

2. **答案：loves**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`everyone` 为第三人称单数，用 `loves`。

3. **答案：ago**
   - **考点**：副词。`more than fifty years ago` 表示"五十多年前"。

4. **答案：generations**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`three` 修饰可数名词复数。

5. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`guided by years of experience` 表示"凭借多年经验"。

6. **答案：good**
   - **考点**：形容词原级。`as good as` 为同级比较结构，用原级。

7. **答案：to write**
   - **考点**：不定式。`agree to do sth` 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：freshest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the freshest ones` 表示"最新鲜的"。

9. **答案：properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `store` 需要副词。

10. **答案：so**
    - **考点**：连词。`so the tradition continues` 表示"这样传统就延续下去了"，`so` 表示结果/目的。
