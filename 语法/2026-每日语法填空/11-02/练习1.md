# 11-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园欺凌 (School Bullying)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

School bullying is a serious problem that affects (1) ______ (thousand) of students across the world. Bullying can take many forms — physical violence, verbal abuse and even cyberbullying through social media. Our school takes this issue very (2) ______ (serious) and has a zero-tolerance policy. Last week, our head teacher gave a speech about bullying at the morning assembly. She said that bullying is never (3) ______ (accept), no matter what form it takes. She encouraged students who are being bullied (4) ______ (speak) up and ask for help. "Silence only makes the problem (5) ______ (bad)," she warned. She also told bystanders that they have a responsibility to stand up (6) ______ victims instead of looking away. After the speech, each class held a discussion about how to prevent bullying. In our class, we came up with several (7) ______ (suggest). First, we proposed creating a "buddy system" so that no student has to be alone during breaks. Second, we suggested (8) ______ (set) up an anonymous reporting box where students can report incidents without (9) ______ (fear). Our teacher praised us for our thoughtful ideas. She said that building a kind and (10) ______ (respect) school environment is everyone's responsibility. I hope that one day, no student will have to suffer from bullying.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：thousands**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`thousands of` 表示"数千"。

2. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`takes this issue seriously`。

3. **答案：acceptable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is never acceptable`，系动词后接形容词，`accept` 变为 `acceptable`。

4. **答案：to speak**
   - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb to do sth`。

5. **答案：worse**
   - **考点**：比较级。`makes the problem worse`，`bad` 的比较级为 `worse`。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`stand up for victims` 表示"为受害者挺身而出"。

7. **答案：suggestions**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`several suggestions`，`suggest` 变为名词 `suggestion`，复数。

8. **答案：setting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`suggest doing sth`。

9. **答案：fear**
   - **考点**：名词。`without fear`，介词后接名词。

10. **答案：respectful**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `kind` 并列修饰 `environment`，需要形容词，`respect` 变为 `respectful`。
