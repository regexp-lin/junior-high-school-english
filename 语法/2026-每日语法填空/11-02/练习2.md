# 11-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：DIY礼物 (DIY Gifts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

With my mother's birthday (1) ______ (approach), I wanted to give her something special. Instead of buying a gift from a shop, I decided to make one (2) ______. I watched several tutorial videos online and chose to make a handmade photo album. First, I collected our favourite family photos from the past few years and printed them (3) ______ at a nearby shop. Then I bought some coloured paper, stickers and ribbons. I spent three evenings (4) ______ (cut) and arranging the photos, writing (5) ______ (caption) under each one and decorating the pages. For the cover, I drew a picture of our family and wrote "To the (6) ______ (good) Mum in the World" in gold letters. The album (7) ______ (contain) thirty photos, each with a short message about why that moment was special to me. On her birthday, I gave her the album after dinner. She opened it slowly, and as she turned each page, her eyes filled (8) ______ tears. "This is the most (9) ______ (meaning) gift I have ever received," she said, hugging me tightly. My father was also (10) ______ (touch) and said he wished he had thought of such a wonderful idea. I learned that the best gifts are not always the most expensive ones — they are the ones made with love.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：approaching**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`with my mother's birthday approaching`，独立主格结构。

2. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`make one myself`，强调"亲自做"。

3. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`printed them out` 表示"打印出来"。

4. **答案：cutting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth`。

5. **答案：captions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`writing captions under each one`，多张照片配多个说明。

6. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the best Mum`。

7. **答案：contained**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

8. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`her eyes filled with tears`。

9. **答案：meaningful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most meaningful gift`，`meaning` 变为 `meaningful`。

10. **答案：touched**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`was also touched`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。
