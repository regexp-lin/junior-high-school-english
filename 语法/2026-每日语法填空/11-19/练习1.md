# 11-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校图书馆 (School Library)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A good school library is like a treasure chest full of knowledge. Our school library was (1) ______ (complete) renovated during the summer holiday. The old, cramped reading room has been transformed (2) ______ a bright, spacious learning centre with comfortable sofas and modern study desks. The number of books has also increased (3) ______ (dramatic). We now have over 30,000 books, covering subjects from science fiction to Chinese history. The library (4) ______ (equip) with an electronic search system, which makes it much easier for students (5) ______ (find) the books they need. Our librarian, Ms Zhou, is one of the (6) ______ (kind) people I have ever met. She always greets students with a warm smile and helps them choose suitable books. She believes that reading is the (7) ______ (good) way to broaden one's horizons. Every month, she organises a book club meeting (8) ______ students can share their thoughts on the books they have read. I joined the club at the beginning of this term, and it has been a wonderful experience. Last week, we (9) ______ (discuss) a novel about a boy's adventure in the Amazon rainforest. Everyone had different opinions, and the conversation was very (10) ______ (live). I look forward to every meeting because it helps me see stories from different perspectives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `renovated` 需要副词形式。

2. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`be transformed into` 表示"被转变为"。

3. **答案：dramatically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `increased` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：is equipped**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the library` 与 `equip` 之间是被动关系，`be equipped with` 表示"配备有"。

5. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：不定式。`it is easier for students to find`，不定式作真正主语。

6. **答案：kindest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the kindest people I have ever met`，`ever` 提示最高级。

7. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the best way`，`good` 的最高级为 `best`。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，先行词 `meeting` 为抽象地点，用 `where`。

9. **答案：discussed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last week` 提示过去时间。

10. **答案：lively**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`the conversation was very lively`，系动词后接形容词作表语，`live` 变为 `lively`。
