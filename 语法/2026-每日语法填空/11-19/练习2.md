# 11-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国剪纸 (Chinese Paper Cutting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese paper cutting, also called "jianzhi," is a folk art with a history of over 1,500 years. It involves (1) ______ (cut) patterns out of red paper using scissors or a knife. The designs often feature animals, flowers, Chinese characters and scenes from daily life. Paper cuttings are (2) ______ (common) used as decorations during festivals, especially the Spring Festival. People stick them on windows and doors to bring good luck and (3) ______ (happy). My aunt is a (4) ______ (skill) paper-cutting artist. She learned this art from (5) ______ grandmother when she was just six years old. She can create amazingly detailed designs without drawing any guidelines first. I once watched her make a paper cutting of a dragon. It took her only twenty minutes to finish, (6) ______ the result was breathtaking — every scale and whisker was perfectly shaped. Last spring, she (7) ______ (invite) to teach paper cutting at our school's art festival. She brought her own tools and patiently showed us the basic (8) ______ (technique). Most students were surprised at how difficult it was. Even the (9) ______ (simple) design required steady hands and great patience. In 2009, Chinese paper cutting was added (10) ______ UNESCO's list of Intangible Cultural Heritage. This recognition has helped raise awareness of this beautiful art form around the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：cutting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`involves doing sth` 表示"涉及做某事"。

2. **答案：commonly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `used` 需要副词形式。

3. **答案：happiness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `good luck` 并列，需要名词形式。

4. **答案：skilled / skilful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `artist` 需要形容词形式。

5. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词。修饰名词 `grandmother` 需要形容词性物主代词，根据上文主语为 `She`。

6. **答案：and / but**
   - **考点**：连词。`It took her only twenty minutes...and/but the result was breathtaking`，可表并列或轻微转折。

7. **答案：was invited**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`she` 与 `invite` 之间是被动关系，`Last spring` 提示过去时。

8. **答案：techniques**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`basic techniques`，多种技巧用复数。

9. **答案：simplest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`Even the simplest design`，`the` 提示最高级。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：介词。`be added to` 表示"被加入到"。
