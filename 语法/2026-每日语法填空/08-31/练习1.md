# 08-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：机器人技术 (Robotics)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Robots are playing an (1) ______ (increase) important role in our daily lives. In factories, robots can work faster and more (2) ______ (accurate) than humans. In hospitals, robotic arms are used (3) ______ (perform) delicate surgeries. At home, robot vacuum cleaners help keep our floors (4) ______. Last year, our school started a robotics club, and I was one of the first students (5) ______ (join). We learned how to build simple robots using basic materials. Our teacher, Mr. Liu, is an (6) ______ (engine) who has been working in the field of artificial intelligence for over ten years. He taught (7) ______ (we) that building a robot requires knowledge of maths, science and programming. In April, our team entered a city-level robotics (8) ______ (compete). We designed a robot that could sort rubbish into different bins. Although we (9) ______ (not win) the first prize, we received a special award for creativity. The experience showed me that the future of technology depends (10) ______ the young people who dare to innovate and experiment.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `important` 需要副词，`increase` 变为 `increasingly`。

2. **答案：accurately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `work`（与 `faster` 并列）需要副词，`accurate` 变为 `accurately`。

3. **答案：to perform**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be used to do sth` 表示"被用来做某事"。

4. **答案：clean**
   - **考点**：形容词。`keep our floors clean`，`keep + 宾语 + 形容词补语`。

5. **答案：to join**
   - **考点**：不定式。先行词被序数词 `the first` 修饰时，后接不定式作定语。

6. **答案：engineer**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`an engineer`，冠词 `an` 后接名词，`engine` 变为 `engineer`（工程师）。

7. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`taught us`，动词后接宾格。

8. **答案：competition**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`robotics competition`，名词修饰名词，`compete` 变为名词 `competition`。

9. **答案：didn't win**
   - **考点**：时态。`Although we didn't win...`，叙述过去的事用一般过去时否定形式。

10. **答案：on**
    - **考点**：介词。`depend on` 为固定搭配，表示"取决于、依赖"。
