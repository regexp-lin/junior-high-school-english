# 08-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国书法 (Chinese Calligraphy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese calligraphy is one of the oldest art forms in the world. It (1) ______ (practise) for thousands of years and is still loved by people of all ages today. My grandmother is a calligraphy lover. She has been writing calligraphy (2) ______ she retired fifteen years ago. Every morning, she spreads rice paper on her desk and picks (3) ______ her brush. She says the act of writing calms her mind and helps her (4) ______ (stay) focused. I started learning calligraphy from her when I was eight years old. At first, I found it really hard (5) ______ (control) the brush. My strokes were either too thick (6) ______ too thin. But my grandmother never lost her temper. She (7) ______ (patient) guided my hand and told me that practice makes perfect. After years of effort, my calligraphy has improved (8) ______ (great). Last year, one of my works (9) ______ (choose) for a school exhibition, and many teachers praised it. Calligraphy has taught me that beautiful things often come from the (10) ______ (simple) tools — just a brush, some ink and a piece of paper.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has been practised**
   - **考点**：时态与被动。`for thousands of years` 提示用现在完成时，且 `calligraphy` 是"被练习"，用被动语态。

2. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since she retired` 表示"自从她退休以来"，与现在完成进行时连用。

3. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`pick up` 表示"拿起"。

4. **答案：stay**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`help sb (to) do sth`，`help` 后可接省略 `to` 的不定式。

5. **答案：to control**
   - **考点**：不定式。`find it hard to do sth`，`it` 为形式宾语，真正宾语为不定式。

6. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`either...or...` 表示"要么……要么……"。

7. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `guided` 需要副词，`patient` 变为 `patiently`。

8. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词，`great` 变为 `greatly`。

9. **答案：was chosen**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`one of my works` 是"被选中"，用一般过去时被动 `was chosen`。

10. **答案：simplest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the simplest tools`，`simple` 的最高级为 `simplest`。
