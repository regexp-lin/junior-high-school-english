# 11-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国传统节日——中秋节 (The Mid-Autumn Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Mid-Autumn Festival is one of the (1) ______ (important) traditional festivals in China. It falls (2) ______ the fifteenth day of the eighth month in the Chinese lunar calendar. On this special day, family members gather together to enjoy mooncakes and admire the full moon. The festival (3) ______ (celebrate) for thousands of years, and it carries deep meaning about family reunion and togetherness. There is a famous legend (4) ______ (connect) to this festival — the story of Chang'e flying to the moon. According to the story, Chang'e drank a magic potion and flew to the moon, where she has lived ever since. People look up at the moon and think of her (5) ______ (sad). In modern times, the ways of (6) ______ (celebrate) the festival have changed a lot. When my grandparents were young, they (7) ______ (make) mooncakes by hand at home. Now, most people prefer buying them from shops. Mooncakes come in many (8) ______ (variety) today, including ice cream and chocolate flavours. Though the customs have evolved, the spirit of the festival remains the same. No matter (9) ______ busy people are, they try their best to return home and spend this night (10) ______ their loved ones.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：most important**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the most important` 为"最……之一"的固定结构。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。表示在具体某一天用 `on`。

3. **答案：has been celebrated**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。`for thousands of years` 提示现在完成时，`the festival` 与 `celebrate` 之间是被动关系。

4. **答案：connected**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`a famous legend connected to this festival`，`connected to` 意为"与……相关的"。

5. **答案：sadly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `think of` 需要副词形式。

6. **答案：celebrating**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词形式。

7. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`When my grandparents were young` 提示过去的时间。

8. **答案：varieties**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many` 后接可数名词复数形式。

9. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`No matter how busy people are` 为让步状语从句，表示"无论多么忙"。

10. **答案：with**
    - **考点**：介词。`spend time with sb` 表示"与某人共度时光"。
