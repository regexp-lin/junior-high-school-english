# 11-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：在线学习 (Online Learning)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Online learning has become an important part of modern education. During the past few years, millions of students (1) ______ (experience) studying at home through the Internet. At first, many students found it difficult to stay (2) ______ (focus) without a teacher standing in front of them. My classmate Wang Lei told me that he (3) ______ (usual) fell asleep during online classes because nobody was watching him. However, online learning also has its advantages. Students can watch recorded lessons as many (4) ______ (time) as they want, which helps them understand difficult concepts (5) ______ (well) than before. Some subjects, such as maths and science, are (6) ______ (easy) to learn through videos because students can pause and replay the explanations. Of course, not every student has equal (7) ______ (accessible) to technology. In some rural areas, families cannot afford computers or stable Internet connections. The government (8) ______ (take) steps to solve this problem since 2020. New computer labs have been built in countryside schools, and free tablets (9) ______ (give) to students from poor families last year. I believe that online learning will continue to grow, but it should never completely replace face-to-face teaching. The best approach is (10) ______ combination of both.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have experienced**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`During the past few years` 提示用现在完成时，主语 `millions of students` 为复数。

2. **答案：focused**
   - **考点**：过去分词作表语。`stay focused` 表示"保持专注"，`focused` 描述人的状态。

3. **答案：usually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `fell asleep` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`as many times as` 表示"多次"，`time` 作"次数"讲时为可数名词。

5. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than before` 提示需要比较级，`well` 的比较级为 `better`。

6. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：比较级。此处暗含与其他学习方式的比较，`easy` 变为 `easier`。

7. **答案：access**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`equal` 为形容词修饰名词，`accessible`（形容词）需转换为名词 `access`。

8. **答案：has taken**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`since 2020` 提示用现在完成时，主语 `The government` 为第三人称单数。

9. **答案：were given**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`tablets` 与 `give` 之间是被动关系，`last year` 提示用一般过去时。

10. **答案：a**
    - **考点**：冠词。`a combination of both` 为固定搭配，表示"两者的结合"。
