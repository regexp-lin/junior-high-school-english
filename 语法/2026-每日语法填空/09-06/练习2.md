# 09-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：自律的重要性 (The Importance of Self-Discipline)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Self-discipline is one of the most important (1) ______ (quality) a student can have. It means being able to control yourself and focus on what needs (2) ______ (do), even when you don't feel like it. My older brother, Li Jun, is a great example of a self-disciplined person. He gets up at six every morning (3) ______ (go) jogging, no matter how cold it is outside. After breakfast, he (4) ______ (study) for two hours before going to school. He never wastes time (5) ______ social media or video games during study hours. When I asked him (6) ______ he managed to be so disciplined, he smiled and said, "It wasn't easy at the (7) ______. I had to force myself at first, but over time it became a habit." He also told me that setting small, (8) ______ (achieve) goals every day helped him stay on track. Thanks to his self-discipline, Li Jun ranked first in his class last year and (9) ______ (admit) to one of the best high schools in our city. His story has taught me that talent alone is not enough — success belongs to those (10) ______ are willing to work hard every single day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：qualities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most important qualities`，`one of` 后接复数名词。

2. **答案：to be done**
   - **考点**：不定式被动。`what needs to be done`，`need to be done` 表示"需要被做"。

3. **答案：to go**
   - **考点**：不定式。`gets up early to go jogging`，不定式表目的。

4. **答案：studies**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。描述日常习惯用一般现在时，主语 `He` 为第三人称单数。

5. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`waste time on` 表示"把时间浪费在……上"。

6. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`asked him how he managed...`，`how` 引导宾语从句，表"如何"。

7. **答案：beginning / start**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`at the beginning/start` 表示"起初"。

8. **答案：achievable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `goals` 需要形容词，`achieve` 变为 `achievable`（可实现的）。

9. **答案：was admitted**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`was admitted to` 表示"被录取"。

10. **答案：who**
    - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `those`（指人），引导定语从句用 `who`。
