# 09-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家乡的桥 (The Bridge in My Hometown)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

There is an old stone bridge in my hometown that has stood for over 300 years. It (1) ______ (build) during the Qing Dynasty and is still used by local people today. The bridge stretches across a wide river and (2) ______ (connect) two villages. My grandfather often takes me to the bridge on summer evenings. We sit on the stone railing and watch the sunset (3) ______ (paint) the sky in shades of orange and pink. He tells me stories about the bridge — how it survived floods and wars, and how the (4) ______ (village) worked together to repair it after each disaster. The bridge is more than just a piece of architecture; it is a symbol (5) ______ the community's strength and unity. In recent years, the local government (6) ______ (decide) to protect the bridge as a historical site. A new modern bridge was built nearby (7) ______ that heavy vehicles no longer need to cross the old one. (8) ______ (tour) from other cities often come to take photos of the ancient bridge. I feel very (9) ______ (pride) of this bridge and the history it (10) ______ (represent). I hope it will stand for another 300 years.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was built**
   - **考点**：被动语态。桥"被建造"，`during the Qing Dynasty` 提示过去时。

2. **答案：connects**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。主语为 `The bridge`（单数），描述客观事实用一般现在时。

3. **答案：painting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`watch the sunset painting the sky`，感官动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词，表正在进行。

4. **答案：villagers**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the villagers`，`village` 变为表示人的名词 `villagers`。

5. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`a symbol of` 表示"……的象征"。

6. **答案：has decided**
   - **考点**：时态。`in recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

7. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。`so that` 引导目的状语从句，表"以便"。

8. **答案：Tourists**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`tour` 变为表示人的名词复数 `tourists`（游客们）。

9. **答案：proud**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel proud of`，系动词后接形容词，`pride` 变为 `proud`。

10. **答案：represents**
    - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。主语为 `it`（单数），描述客观现状用一般现在时。
