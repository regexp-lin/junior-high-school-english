# 09-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：乡村教师 (A Village Teacher)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mr. Zhang has been a teacher in a remote mountain village (1) ______ over thirty years. After graduating from college, he could have found a well-paid job in the city, (2) ______ he chose to return to his hometown and teach the children there. When he first arrived, the school had only two (3) ______ (classroom) and very few teaching materials. He spent his own money (4) ______ (buy) books and supplies for his students. Over the years, he (5) ______ (teach) more than a thousand students, many of whom have gone on to attend university. One of his former students, now a (6) ______ (success) doctor in Beijing, said, "Without Mr. Zhang, I would never have had the chance (7) ______ (receive) a good education." The local government has recognised Mr. Zhang's contributions and awarded him the title of "Outstanding Teacher". (8) ______ he has received many offers to work in bigger cities, he has always refused. "These children need me," he says (9) ______ (simple). "This is (10) ______ I belong." His dedication has inspired many young people to consider teaching in rural areas.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for over thirty years` 表持续时间，与现在完成时搭配。

2. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`could have found...but chose to...`，前后转折。

3. **答案：classrooms**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`two classrooms`，数词后接复数名词。

4. **答案：buying**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend money (in) doing sth` 是固定搭配。

5. **答案：has taught**
   - **考点**：时态。`over the years` 提示用现在完成时。

6. **答案：successful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `doctor` 需要形容词，`success` 变为 `successful`。

7. **答案：to receive**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the chance to do sth` 表示"做某事的机会"。

8. **答案：Although / Though**
   - **考点**：连词。`Although he has received...he has always refused`，表让步。

9. **答案：simply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `says` 需要副词，`simple` 变为 `simply`。

10. **答案：where**
    - **考点**：表语从句。`This is where I belong` 表示"这是我所属的地方"，`where` 引导表语从句。
