# 09-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：二手物品交换市场 (Second-Hand Exchange Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our school held a second-hand exchange market on the playground. Students were encouraged to bring items they no (1) ______ needed and trade them with others. I brought some old books and a pair of (2) ______ (use) headphones. Before the market opened, I set up my stall and arranged everything (3) ______ (neat). When the market started, it was (4) ______ (busy) than I had expected. Students crowded around the stalls, looking for bargains. A girl (5) ______ (name) Xiao Fang was interested in my headphones and offered to exchange them for a board game. I (6) ______ (happy) agreed because I had been wanting a new game to play. By the end of the day, most students had (7) ______ (success) exchanged their items. The market raised awareness (8) ______ recycling and reducing waste. Our teacher told us that this kind of event was not only fun (9) ______ also meaningful. She hoped we would learn to value things and avoid (10) ______ (throw) away items that are still useful. I think more schools should organise events like this.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`no longer` 表示"不再"。

2. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a pair of used headphones`，过去分词作形容词修饰名词，表"用过的"。

3. **答案：neatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `arranged` 需要副词，`neat` 变为 `neatly`。

4. **答案：busier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`busier than I had expected`，`busy` 的比较级为 `busier`。

5. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a girl named Xiao Fang`，过去分词作后置定语。

6. **答案：happily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `agreed` 需要副词，`happy` 变为 `happily`。

7. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `exchanged` 需要副词，`success` 变为 `successfully`。

8. **答案：of / about**
   - **考点**：介词。`raise awareness of/about` 表示"提高对……的意识"。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 表示"不仅……而且……"。

10. **答案：throwing**
    - **考点**：动名词。`avoid doing sth` 是固定搭配，`avoid` 后接动名词。
