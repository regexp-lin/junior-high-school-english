# 03-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：有效沟通 (Effective Communication)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Communication is a skill that we use every day. Good communication helps us build strong 1. ________ (relation) with the people around us.

However, many teenagers find it 2. ________ (difficulty) to express their feelings. Some students are too shy to speak up in class, 3. ________ others may say things without thinking and hurt their friends' feelings. Both situations show that we need to learn how to communicate 4. ________ (proper).

Listening is just 5. ________ important as speaking. When someone is talking to you, give them your full attention. Don't look at your phone 6. ________ interrupt them. By listening carefully, you show respect and 7. ________ (you) friends will feel valued.

Body language also plays a big role in communication. A warm smile, eye contact and a friendly nod can make others feel 8. ________ (comfort). On the other hand, crossing your arms or looking away may send a negative message, even if you don't mean it.

When you have a disagreement with someone, try to stay calm. 9. ________ (shout) or blaming others will only make things worse. Instead, explain your point of view politely and try to find a solution that works for 10. ________ (everyone).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **relationships**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。strong 修饰名词，relate → relation → relationship，build strong relationships 意为"建立牢固的关系"，用复数。

2. **difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。find it + 形容词 + to do sth.，it 为形式宾语，difficulty → difficult。

3. **while**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。while 表示对比关系："一些学生太害羞不敢发言，而另一些……"。

4. **properly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 communicate，需用副词形式 properly。

5. **as**
   - **考点**：固定句式（无提示词填空）。as...as 意为"和……一样"，just as important as 意为"和……同样重要"。

6. **or**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。否定句中用 or 连接并列成分，"不要看手机或打断他们"。

7. **your**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 friends，you → your。

8. **comfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。make sb. feel + 形容词，comfort → comfortable。

9. **Shouting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词 shouting 在句首作主语，注意首字母大写。

10. **both/everyone**
    - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。根据语境"找到对双方都适用的解决方案"，可填 both（双方）。若提示词为 everyone，则保持原形即可（everyone 本身已是正确形式）。
