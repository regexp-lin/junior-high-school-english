# 03-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：春天的户外活动 (Spring Outdoor Activities)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Spring is the best season for outdoor activities. After a long, cold winter, everything comes back to life. The trees turn green, and flowers start 1. ________ (bloom) everywhere.

Last weekend, my classmates and I went on a spring outing to a park near our school. The weather was 2. ________ (wonder) — warm sunshine and a gentle breeze. We rode our bicycles 3. ________ (get) there, and the whole trip took about twenty minutes.

When we arrived, we were amazed 4. ________ the beautiful scenery. The cherry blossoms were in full bloom, and many people were taking photos under the 5. ________ (tree). We found a quiet spot by the lake and sat down on the grass.

Some of us flew kites while others played badminton. I brought my guitar and played some songs for my friends. Everyone 6. ________ (sing) along happily. It was one of the 7. ________ (relaxing) afternoons I had ever had.

At noon, we shared the food that we had prepared 8. ________ home. There were sandwiches, fruit and homemade cookies. The food tasted even 9. ________ (delicious) when we ate it outdoors.

Before we left, we made sure to pick up all our rubbish. Keeping the park clean is something 10. ________ (that) everyone should do. What a wonderful day it was!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **blooming / to bloom**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。start doing sth. 或 start to do sth. 均可，意为"开始绽放"。

2. **wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。was 后接形容词作表语，wonder → wonderful。

3. **to get**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。表示"为了到达那里"，用不定式表目的。

4. **by/at**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be amazed by/at 意为"对……感到惊叹"。

5. **trees**
   - **考点**：名词复数。定冠词 the 后接名词，根据语境是多棵树，tree → trees。

6. **sang**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。叙述过去的活动，sing → sang（不规则变化）。

7. **most relaxing**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，relaxing 的最高级为 most relaxing。

8. **at**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。at home 意为"在家里"，表示食物是在家准备好的。

9. **more delicious**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。even 修饰比较级，delicious 为多音节形容词，比较级为 more delicious。

10. **that**
    - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 something，当先行词为不定代词时，关系代词只能用 that。
