# 10-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：同学之间的互助 (Helping Each Other Among Classmates)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In our class, we have a "Study Buddy" system (1) ______ students are paired up to help each other with their studies. I am paired with Zhang Hao, who is excellent at maths but finds English quite (2) ______ (difficulty). On the other hand, English is my strongest subject, (3) ______ I struggle with maths. We meet twice a week after school to study together. During our sessions, I help him with English grammar and (4) ______ (pronounce), and he explains maths problems to me in a way that is easy (5) ______ (understand). His patience has helped me improve my maths score (6) ______ 15 points since last month. Last week, he taught me a clever method for (7) ______ (solve) geometry problems. He drew diagrams and broke down each step so (8) ______ (clear) that I finally understood a concept I had been confused about for weeks. I was so grateful that I bought him his favourite snack as a thank-you gift. Our teacher says the Study Buddy system has been very (9) ______ (benefit) for the whole class. Students who once felt too shy to ask questions in class now have someone they can turn to for help. I believe that (10) ______ (learn) together is not only more effective but also more enjoyable than studying alone.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `system`（抽象地点），定语从句中缺地点/方式状语，用 `where`。

2. **答案：difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`finds English quite difficult`，`find + 宾语 + 形容词`。

3. **答案：but / while**
   - **考点**：连词。`English is my strongest subject but/while I struggle with maths`，前后转折/对比。

4. **答案：pronunciation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `grammar` 并列作宾语，需要名词，`pronounce` 变为 `pronunciation`。

5. **答案：to understand**
   - **考点**：不定式。`easy to understand`，`be + adj + to do` 结构。

6. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`improve by 15 points` 表示"提高了15分"。

7. **答案：solving**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `for` 后接动名词。

8. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `broke down` 需要副词，`clear` 变为 `clearly`。

9. **答案：beneficial**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`has been very beneficial`，系动词后接形容词，`benefit` 变为 `beneficial`。

10. **答案：learning**
    - **考点**：动名词。动名词 `learning` 作主语。
