# 10-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：盲人导盲犬 (Guide Dogs for the Blind)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Guide dogs play a vital role in helping blind people live (1) ______ (independence). These specially trained dogs can lead their owners safely through busy streets, avoid (2) ______ (obstacle) and even stop at traffic lights. Last month, a blind woman and her guide dog visited our school to give a talk. Her name was Ms. Lin, and her dog, a golden retriever (3) ______ (call) Sunny, sat quietly beside her throughout the presentation. Ms. Lin told us that she (4) ______ (lose) her sight in an accident ten years ago. At first, life was extremely difficult. She couldn't go anywhere (5) ______ help. Then she applied for a guide dog and waited for two years. When Sunny (6) ______ (final) arrived, everything changed. "Sunny gave me the (7) ______ (free) to go out alone again," she said. She explained that training a guide dog takes about 18 months and costs a lot of money. In China, there (8) ______ (be) still very few guide dogs compared to the number of blind people who need them. Ms. Lin hoped that more people would support guide dog programmes. She also asked us to remember one important rule: never touch (9) ______ distract a guide dog while it is working. After the talk, many students were deeply (10) ______ (move) and wanted to learn more about helping the visually impaired.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：independently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `live` 需要副词，`independence` 变为 `independently`。

2. **答案：obstacles**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`avoid obstacles`，障碍物不止一个。

3. **答案：called**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a golden retriever called Sunny`，过去分词作后置定语。

4. **答案：had lost**
   - **考点**：时态。`told us that she had lost her sight ten years ago`，过去完成时表示在"告诉"之前已发生。

5. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`couldn't go anywhere without help` 表示"没有帮助就哪也去不了"。

6. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `arrived` 需要副词，`final` 变为 `finally`。

7. **答案：freedom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the freedom to go out`，冠词后接名词，`free` 变为 `freedom`。

8. **答案：are**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`there are still very few guide dogs`，`dogs` 为复数。

9. **答案：or**
   - **考点**：连词。`never touch or distract`，`or` 连接两个并列动词。

10. **答案：moved**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`were deeply moved`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。
