# 03-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市与乡村生活 (City Life vs. Country Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

People often talk about whether city life or country life is 1. ________ (good). In fact, both have their own advantages and disadvantages.

Living in a city is very convenient. There are big shopping malls, hospitals and schools everywhere. Public transportation makes it easy for people 2. ________ (get) around. Young people especially enjoy city life 3. ________ there are more job opportunities and entertainment choices.

However, city life also has problems. The air is often 4. ________ (pollute) because of heavy traffic and factories. The streets are always 5. ________ (crowd) with people, and the noise can be really annoying. Besides, the cost of living in a big city is much 6. ________ (high) than in the countryside.

Country life, on the other hand, is quiet and peaceful. People there can breathe fresh air and enjoy the beauty of 7. ________. The pace of life is slower, and neighbors know each other well. My grandparents live in a small village, and every time I visit 8. ________ (they), I feel completely relaxed.

But the countryside also has its problems. It is not easy to find good 9. ________ (doctor) or schools nearby. Some young people choose to leave their villages and move to cities for a better future.

In my opinion, there is no perfect place to live. The most important thing is to find a place 10. ________ makes you happy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。两者比较（city life 和 country life），good 的比较级为 better。

2. **to get**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。make it easy for sb. to do sth. 意为"使某人容易做某事"，it 为形式宾语。

3. **because**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。后面解释原因"因为有更多的工作机会"，用 because 引导原因状语从句。

4. **polluted**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→过去分词/形容词）。be polluted 意为"被污染的"，polluted 作表语。

5. **crowded**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→过去分词/形容词）。be crowded with 意为"挤满了……"，是固定搭配。

6. **higher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，high → higher。

7. **nature**
   - **考点**：名词（无提示词填空）。the beauty of nature 意为"大自然的美"。

8. **them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。visit 后接宾语，代替前面的 grandparents，用宾格 them。

9. **doctors**
   - **考点**：名词复数。good doctors 意为"好医生"，此处泛指多位医生，用复数。

10. **that/which**
    - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 a place，在从句中作主语。
