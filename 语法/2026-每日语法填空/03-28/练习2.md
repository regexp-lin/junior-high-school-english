# 03-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：理性消费 (Smart Shopping)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Shopping is something we all do. But do you know how to shop 1. ________ (wise)? Being a smart shopper means spending your money carefully and getting the best value.

Many teenagers love following the latest fashion trends. They want to buy the same clothes 2. ________ their favorite stars wear. However, fashion changes 3. ________ (quick), and what is popular today may become outdated tomorrow. It is not wise 4. ________ (spend) all your pocket money on things you don't really need.

Online shopping has made buying things much 5. ________ (simple). With just a few clicks, you can order anything from anywhere. But be careful — some products look great in 6. ________ (photo) but turn out to be disappointing when they arrive. Always read other 7. ________ (customer) reviews before making a decision.

Here are some tips for smart shopping. First, make a list before you go shopping. This helps you avoid 8. ________ (buy) unnecessary things. Second, compare prices at different stores 9. ________ you can find the best deal. Third, learn to tell the difference between what you want and what you truly need.

Money is hard to earn. If we learn to manage 10. ________ (we) money well from a young age, it will benefit us for the rest of our lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 shop，需用副词形式 wisely。

2. **that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 clothes，在从句中作 wear 的宾语。

3. **quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 changes，需用副词形式 quickly。

4. **to spend**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。It is not wise to do sth.，it 为形式主语，真正的主语是不定式短语。

5. **simpler**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，simple → simpler。

6. **photos**
   - **考点**：名词复数。in photos 意为"在照片中"，指多张照片，用复数。

7. **customers'**
   - **考点**：名词所有格 + 复数。other customers' reviews 意为"其他顾客的评论"，customer → customers（复数）+ 所有格 's（复数名词以 s 结尾直接加 '）。

8. **buying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。avoid doing sth. 意为"避免做某事"。

9. **so**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。so (that) 引导目的状语从句，意为"以便"。

10. **our**
    - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 money，we → our。
