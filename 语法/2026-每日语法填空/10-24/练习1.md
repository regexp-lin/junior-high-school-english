# 10-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家乡的变化 (Changes in My Hometown)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My hometown has changed a lot in the past ten years. When I was little, it was a quiet town with only a few shops and no high-rise buildings. The roads were narrow, and there (1) ______ (be) no subway or modern transport. People mostly got around by bicycle or on foot. Now, my hometown has been transformed (2) ______ a modern city. A new subway line (3) ______ (open) last year, connecting the town centre to the neighbouring city. Shopping malls, parks and (4) ______ (entertain) centres have been built everywhere. The old market (5) ______ my grandmother used to buy vegetables has been replaced by a huge supermarket. My parents are happy about most of these (6) ______ (change), but they sometimes miss the (7) ______ (simple) of the old days. "Life was slower but (8) ______ (warm) back then," my mother often says. She is right — the sense of community was much (9) ______ (strong) when everyone knew their neighbours. Now, many people live in tall apartments and (10) ______ (rare) talk to the people next door. I hope that as my hometown continues to grow, it will find a way to keep the warmth of the past while embracing the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：时态与主谓一致。`there was no subway`，`no subway` 为单数概念。

2. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`be transformed into` 表示"被转变为"。

3. **答案：was opened / opened**
   - **考点**：时态。`last year` 提示过去时。

4. **答案：entertainment**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`entertainment centres`，名词作定语，`entertain` 变为 `entertainment`。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the old market`（地点），定语从句缺地点状语。

6. **答案：changes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`most of these changes`。

7. **答案：simplicity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`miss the simplicity`，冠词后接名词，`simple` 变为 `simplicity`。

8. **答案：warmer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`slower but warmer`，暗含与现在的比较。

9. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much stronger`。

10. **答案：rarely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `talk` 需要副词，`rare` 变为 `rarely`。
