# 10-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：制作手抄报 (Making a Hand-Written Newspaper)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Hand-written newspapers are a unique tradition in Chinese schools. Students create them by writing articles and drawing (1) ______ (picture) on large sheets of paper. Last week, our English teacher asked us to make a hand-written newspaper on the topic of "My Dream". Each student had to create (2) ______ (they) own page and include at least one article and one illustration. I decided to write about my dream of becoming (3) ______ environmental scientist. I spent the whole weekend working on my page. First, I designed a (4) ______ (colour) border using markers and coloured pencils. Then I wrote my article (5) ______ (careful) in my neatest handwriting. I also drew a picture of the Earth surrounded (6) ______ green trees and clean oceans. When I finished, I showed it to my mother. She said it looked very (7) ______ (profession) and suggested I add a few inspiring quotes. I found a quote by Albert Einstein: "Imagination is more important (8) ______ knowledge." On Monday, all the pages (9) ______ (collect) and put together into a class newspaper. Our teacher displayed it on the classroom wall. She said my page was one of the (10) ______ (creative) ones in the class. Making the newspaper was hard work, but seeing the final result made it all worthwhile.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：pictures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`drawing pictures`，画多幅画。

2. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`their own page`。

3. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`an environmental scientist`，`environmental` 以元音音素开头。

4. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `border` 需要形容词，`colour` 变为 `colourful`。

5. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `wrote` 需要副词。

6. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`surrounded by green trees`，`be surrounded by` 表"被……环绕"。

7. **答案：professional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`looked very professional`，系动词后接形容词，`profession` 变为 `professional`。

8. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。`more important than knowledge`。

9. **答案：were collected**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`all the pages were collected`，"被收集"。

10. **答案：most creative**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the most creative ones`。
