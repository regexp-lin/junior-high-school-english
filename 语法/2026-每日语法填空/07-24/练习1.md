# 07-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家乡的变化 (Changes in My Hometown)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My hometown is a small city in Guangdong. Over the past ten years, it (1) ______ (change) so much that I can hardly recognise it. When I was little, there were only a few narrow roads and most families got around (2) ______ bicycle. Now, wide highways connect our city to the provincial capital, and a modern bus system (3) ______ (build) last year. The old market where my grandmother used to buy vegetables (4) ______ (replace) by a large supermarket. New apartment buildings have risen everywhere, making the skyline look completely (5) ______ (difference). The biggest change is the new central park. It covers an area of fifty hectares and (6) ______ (include) a lake, walking paths, and a children's playground. On weekends, it is always crowded (7) ______ families enjoying themselves. My father says the park is much (8) ______ (beautiful) than anything he imagined when he was young. Although I am happy about these improvements, I sometimes miss the old town. The small lanes, the street vendors, and the sound of neighbours (9) ______ (chat) in the evening — these things are difficult (10) ______ (forget).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has changed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Over the past ten years` 提示用现在完成时，主语 `it` 为第三人称单数。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by bicycle` 表示"骑自行车"，`by + 交通工具` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：was built**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。公交系统"被建造"，`last year` 表示过去。

4. **答案：has been replaced**
   - **考点**：现在完成时 + 被动语态。老市场"已被取代"，表示过去到现在的变化。

5. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`look` 为系动词，后接形容词，`difference` 变为 `different`。

6. **答案：includes**
   - **考点**：主谓一致 + 一般现在时。主语 `It` 为第三人称单数，描述公园的现状。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be crowded with` 为固定搭配，表示"挤满了"。

8. **答案：more beautiful**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`beautiful` 为多音节形容词。

9. **答案：chatting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`the sound of neighbours chatting` 表示"邻居闲聊的声音"，动名词/现在分词作 `of` 的宾语。

10. **答案：to forget**
    - **考点**：不定式。`be difficult to do` 为固定结构，不定式作状语补充说明 `difficult`。
