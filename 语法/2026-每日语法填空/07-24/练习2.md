# 07-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：阅读与思考 (Reading and Thinking)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A wise person once said, "Reading without thinking is like eating without (1) ______ (digest)." I did not understand this until I started keeping a reading journal. Before that, I used to rush through books without (2) ______ (stop) to reflect. I could finish a novel in two days, (3) ______ a week later, I could barely remember what it was about. My Chinese teacher noticed this problem and suggested that I write a short response (4) ______ each chapter. At first, it felt like extra homework. But gradually, I found that writing (5) ______ (force) me to think more (6) ______ (deep) about the characters, the plot, and the themes. For example, while reading "Journey to the West," I asked (7) ______ (I) why the Monkey King kept getting into trouble. Was it just his wild nature, or was the author trying to say something about (8) ______ (free) and rules? These questions made the story far more (9) ______ (interest) than before. Now I read more (10) ______ (slow) but understand much more. Quality matters more than quantity when it comes to reading.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：digesting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`without` 为介词，后接动名词 `digesting`。

2. **答案：stopping**
   - **考点**：动名词。`without` 为介词，后接动名词 `stopping`。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。"两天看完一本书"与"一周后几乎记不住"为转折关系。

4. **答案：after / to**
   - **考点**：介词。`after each chapter` 表示"在每章之后"；`to each chapter` 表示"针对每章"，两者均可。

5. **答案：forced**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`found that writing forced me...` 叙述过去的发现，用过去时。

6. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `think` 需要副词，`deep` 变为 `deeply`（深入地）。

7. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`ask myself` 表示"问自己"，主语为 `I`。

8. **答案：freedom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`about` 后接名词，`free` 变为 `freedom`（自由）。

9. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 比较级。`far more interesting than` 中 `far` 强化比较级，`interest` 变为 `interesting`。

10. **答案：slowly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `read` 需要副词，`slow` 变为 `slowly`。
