# 10-20 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：地震逃生知识 (Earthquake Safety Knowledge)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Earthquakes are natural disasters that can happen without (1) ______ (warn). Knowing what to do during an earthquake can save your life. Last week, our school invited a disaster prevention expert (2) ______ (give) a talk on earthquake safety. He began by explaining why earthquakes occur. The Earth's surface is made (3) ______ of large plates that are constantly moving. When these plates push against each other, the ground shakes. The expert then taught us the basic rules of earthquake survival. The most important rule is "Drop, Cover and Hold On". If you are indoors, you should drop to the ground, take cover under a (4) ______ (strength) piece of furniture like a desk, and hold on until the shaking (5) ______ (stop). You should stay away (6) ______ windows and heavy objects that might fall. If you are outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings and power lines. The expert also warned us (7) ______ (not run) during an earthquake because falling objects are extremely (8) ______ (danger). After the talk, our school held an earthquake drill. All students were able to respond (9) ______ (quick) and correctly. The expert praised us and said, "Being (10) ______ (prepare) is the best protection." I felt much safer knowing what to do.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：warning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`without warning`，介词后接名词，`warn` 变为 `warning`。

2. **答案：to give**
   - **考点**：不定式。`invited sb to do sth` 表示"邀请某人做某事"。

3. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`be made up of` 表示"由……组成"。

4. **答案：strong**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `piece of furniture` 需要形容词，`strength` 变为 `strong`。

5. **答案：stops**
   - **考点**：时态与主谓一致。`until the shaking stops`，一般现在时表示将来的条件。

6. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`stay away from` 表示"远离"。

7. **答案：not to run**
   - **考点**：不定式否定形式。`warn sb not to do sth` 表示"警告某人不要做某事"。

8. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`extremely dangerous`，`danger` 变为 `dangerous`。

9. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `respond` 需要副词。

10. **答案：prepared**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`being prepared`，系动词后接过去分词形容词，表"有准备的"。
