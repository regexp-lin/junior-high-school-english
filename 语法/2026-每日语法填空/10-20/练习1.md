# 10-20 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：乒乓球 (Table Tennis)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Table tennis, often called "the national sport" of China, has been (1) ______ (wide) popular for decades. Chinese players have dominated international competitions, (2) ______ (win) countless gold medals at the Olympic Games. The sport is loved because it is easy to learn, requires little space and can be played (3) ______ people of all ages. There are table tennis tables in parks, community centres and almost every school in China. I started playing table tennis when I was eight. My father, who was a good (4) ______ (play) in his youth, taught me the basic skills. He showed me how to hold the bat (5) ______ (correct) and how to serve with spin. At first, I lost every match against him. But he told me not to be (6) ______ (discourage). "Every champion started as a beginner," he said. Over the years, I have improved (7) ______ (great) and can now beat most of my classmates. Last month, I entered the school table tennis tournament and reached the semi-finals. Although I didn't win, (8) ______ experience was valuable. I learned that staying calm under pressure is just (9) ______ important as having good skills. I plan to keep practising and hope to make it to the finals next year. Table tennis has given me not only joy but also the (10) ______ (determine) to keep improving.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `popular` 需要副词，`wide` 变为 `widely`。

2. **答案：winning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`winning countless gold medals` 作伴随/结果状语。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`be played by people of all ages` 表示"被各年龄段的人玩"。

4. **答案：player**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a good player`，`play` 变为表示人的名词 `player`。

5. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `hold` 需要副词。

6. **答案：discouraged**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`not to be discouraged`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

7. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词。

8. **答案：the**
   - **考点**：冠词。`the experience` 特指这次比赛的经历。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`just as important as`，同级比较。

10. **答案：determination**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `joy` 并列，需要名词，`determine` 变为 `determination`。
