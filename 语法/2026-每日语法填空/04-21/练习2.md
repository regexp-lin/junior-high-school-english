# 04-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：零花钱管理 (Managing Pocket Money)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Most teenagers receive pocket money from their parents. But do you know how to manage your money 1. ________ (wise)? Learning about money management at a young age can benefit you for the rest of your life.

The first step is to keep a record of how you spend your money. Write down every purchase you make, no matter how 2. ________ (small) it is. At the end of each week, look at the list and ask yourself: "Was this 3. ________ (real) necessary?" You may be surprised to find how much money you waste on things you don't actually need.

4. ________ (save) money is also very important. Try to put aside at least 20% of your pocket money every month. You can open a savings account at a bank, 5. ________ is a safe place to keep your money. Over time, your savings 6. ________ (grow), and you can use them for something meaningful.

It is normal to want to buy things for 7. ________ (you), but learn to tell the difference between needs and wants. A new pair of shoes when your old ones are broken is a need. 8. ________ the latest limited-edition sneakers are usually just a want.

Some students have started small businesses to earn extra money, such as 9. ________ (sell) handmade crafts or tutoring younger students. This teaches them valuable skills like responsibility and hard work.

Money is a tool, not a goal. If we learn to use it 10. ________ (proper), it can help us live a better life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 manage，需用副词形式 wisely。

2. **small**
   - **考点**：形容词（无需变形）。no matter how small 意为"无论多小"，how + 形容词引导让步状语从句。

3. **really**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 necessary，需用副词形式 really。

4. **Saving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。Saving money 在句首作主语。

5. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，which 指代 a savings account / a bank。

6. **will grow**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般将来时）。over time 暗示未来发展趋势。

7. **yourself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。for yourself 意为"给你自己"，主语隐含为 you。

8. **But**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。but 表转折，"需要"与"想要"形成对比。

9. **selling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。such as 后接动名词形式。

10. **properly**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 use，需用副词形式 properly。
