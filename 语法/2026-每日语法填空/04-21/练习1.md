# 04-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：创造性思维 (Creative Thinking)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Creative thinking is the ability to look at things in new and different ways. It is not something only artists or 1. ________ (music) have — everyone can develop it.

One way to become more creative is to ask "why" and "what if" more often. For example, when Newton saw an apple 2. ________ (fall) from a tree, he didn't just ignore it. Instead, he asked 3. ________ (he), "Why does the apple fall down instead of up?" This simple question led to one of the most important 4. ________ (discover) in science.

Another way is to try new things. If you always do the same things in the same way, your brain gets used to the pattern and stops thinking 5. ________ (creative). But when you step out of your comfort zone — trying a new hobby, reading a different type of book, 6. ________ talking to new people — your brain is forced to think in new ways.

Making 7. ________ (mistake) is also part of the creative process. Many great inventions were the result of accidents. The microwave oven, for example, was invented 8. ________ a scientist noticed that the chocolate bar in his pocket melted near a radar machine.

Schools should encourage creative thinking by giving students more freedom 9. ________ (explore) ideas. Tests that only measure memorization do not develop creativity. Instead, projects, discussions and hands-on activities are much 10. ________ (good) for building creative minds.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **musicians**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→名词）+ 复数。or 连接并列名词，music → musician → musicians。

2. **falling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。see sb./sth. doing sth. 意为"看见某物正在做某事"。

3. **himself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语是 he（Newton），ask himself 意为"问自己"。

4. **discoveries**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。one of the most important + 名词复数，discover → discovery → discoveries。

5. **creatively**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 thinking，需用副词形式 creatively。

6. **or**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。or 连接并列的三个动名词短语，表示列举。

7. **mistakes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。make mistakes 意为"犯错误"，mistake 用复数。

8. **when/after**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。when/after a scientist noticed... 引导时间状语从句。

9. **to explore**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。freedom to explore 意为"探索的自由"。

10. **better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。much 修饰比较级，good → better。
