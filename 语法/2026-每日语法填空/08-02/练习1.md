# 08-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：急救知识 (First Aid Knowledge)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Knowing first aid can save lives, especially in (1) ______ (expect) situations. Last term, our school organised a first aid training course. A nurse from the city hospital came to teach us basic (2) ______ (skill). She showed us how to stop bleeding, treat burns, and perform CPR. We practised (3) ______ plastic models until we could do it (4) ______ (correct). The nurse stressed that the first few minutes after an accident (5) ______ (be) crucial. If a person stops (6) ______ (breathe), brain damage can begin within four minutes. Therefore, calling an ambulance and giving first aid at once is extremely (7) ______ (importance). She also warned us (8) ______ some common mistakes. For example, you should never move someone with a possible spine injury, and you should not put toothpaste on a burn. I was surprised to learn how many things I (9) ______ (do) wrong before. After the course, I felt more confident about handling emergencies. I think first aid should (10) ______ (teach) in every school, because anyone could face a dangerous situation at any time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：unexpected**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 前缀。修饰名词 `situations` 需要形容词，`expect` 变为 `unexpected`（意外的），加前缀 `un-`。

2. **答案：skills**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`basic skills` 表示"基本技能"，用复数。

3. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`practise on models` 表示"在模型上练习"。

4. **答案：correctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `do` 需要副词，`correct` 变为 `correctly`。

5. **答案：are**
   - **考点**：主谓一致 + 一般现在时。`the first few minutes` 为复数主语，描述一般性事实。

6. **答案：breathing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`stop breathing` 表示"停止呼吸"（`stop doing` 表示停止正在做的事）。

7. **答案：important**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is` 后接形容词作表语，`importance` 变为 `important`。

8. **答案：about / against**
   - **考点**：介词。`warn sb about/against sth` 为固定搭配，表示"警告某人注意/防范……"。

9. **答案：had been doing / had done**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。学习之前一直做错的事情，过去的过去，用 `had been doing` 或 `had done`。

10. **答案：be taught**
    - **考点**：被动语态。`should` 后接动词原形，急救知识"应该被教授"，用 `be taught`。
