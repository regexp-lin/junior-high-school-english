# 08-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园花园 (The School Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Behind our school building, there is a small garden that (1) ______ (manage) by the biology club. It was just a patch of bare land two years ago, but now it has been transformed (2) ______ a green oasis. The garden has four raised beds, each growing different (3) ______ (plant): tomatoes, peppers, lettuce, and sunflowers. Every Wednesday afternoon, club members water the plants, pull out weeds, and check (4) ______ insects. Our biology teacher, Mr. Fang, uses the garden as an outdoor classroom. He often takes us there to observe plant growth and teach us about (5) ______ (nature) cycles. Watching a seed grow into a plant is much more (6) ______ (excite) than reading about it in a textbook. Last month, our tomatoes were ready to harvest. We picked over thirty (7) ______ (kilo) of ripe, red tomatoes! Some (8) ______ (send) to the school canteen, and the rest were shared among club members. I took some home, and my mother made a (9) ______ (delight) tomato soup with them. The school garden has taught me that taking care of (10) ______ (live) things requires patience, but the rewards are always worth the wait.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：is managed**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。花园"被管理"，描述当前的情况。

2. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`be transformed into` 表示"被转变为"。

3. **答案：plants**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different` 修饰可数名词复数，后面列举了多种植物。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`check for` 表示"检查是否有"。

5. **答案：natural**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `cycles` 需要形容词，`nature` 变为 `natural`。

6. **答案：exciting**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 比较级。`more exciting than` 中 `excite` 变为 `exciting`（令人兴奋的），修饰事物。

7. **答案：kilos**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`thirty` 修饰可数名词复数，`kilo` 变为 `kilos`。

8. **答案：were sent**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。西红柿"被送到"食堂，主语 `Some` 为复数。

9. **答案：delightful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `soup` 需要形容词，`delight` 变为 `delightful`（美味的，令人愉悦的）。

10. **答案：living**
    - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`living things` 表示"有生命的东西"，现在分词修饰名词。
