# 08-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：陪伴老人 (Spending Time with the Elderly)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My grandfather turned eighty last month. As he has grown (1) ______ (old), he spends most of his time at home alone because my grandmother passed away three years (2) ______. During the holiday, I made a decision (3) ______ (visit) him every weekend. At first, I was not sure what we would talk about. But once we started chatting, the (4) ______ (converse) flowed naturally. He told me stories about his childhood that I (5) ______ (never hear) before — how he walked five kilometres to school barefoot and how he saw the first television in the village. His eyes lit up (6) ______ he spoke about the past. I also taught him how to use a smartphone so that he could video-call his old friends. He was a (7) ______ (quickly) learner than I expected. Within a week, he could send voice messages and take selfies! Spending time with him has made me (8) ______ (real) that elderly people have so much wisdom to share. They just need someone (9) ______ (listen). Now, my grandfather says Sunday is the day he looks forward to the (10) ______ (much). That makes me happy too.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：older**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`as he has grown older` 表示"随着他变老"。

2. **答案：ago**
   - **考点**：副词。`three years ago` 表示"三年前"。

3. **答案：to visit**
   - **考点**：不定式。`make a decision to do sth` 为固定搭配。

4. **答案：conversation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`converse` 变为 `conversation`。

5. **答案：had never heard**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。在他讲述之前我"从未听过"这些故事（过去的过去）。

6. **答案：when / as**
   - **考点**：连词。`when/as he spoke` 表示"当他说起……时"。

7. **答案：quicker**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than I expected` 提示用比较级，括号内 `quickly` 变为形容词 `quick` 的比较级 `quicker`。

8. **答案：realise**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`make sb + 动词原形` 为使役动词结构；括号内 `real` 变为动词 `realise`。

9. **答案：to listen**
   - **考点**：不定式。`someone to listen` 中不定式作后置定语，修饰不定代词。

10. **答案：most**
    - **考点**：副词最高级。`the most` 为 `much` 的最高级，表示"最"。
