# 07-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：家庭电影之夜 (Family Movie Night)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every Friday evening, my family has a tradition (1) ______ we all look forward to — movie night. After dinner, we make popcorn and choose a film (2) ______ (watch) together. My father usually picks action movies, while my mother prefers (3) ______ (romance) ones. My little brother always votes for cartoons. To be fair, we take (4) ______ (turn) choosing each week. Last Friday, it was my turn. I chose a film about a teenager who travelled across the country (5) ______ (find) his lost dog. The story was both funny (6) ______ touching. My mother cried during the ending scene, and my brother laughed at her, which made the whole family burst (7) ______ laughter. We did not go to bed (8) ______ almost midnight. Movie night is special not because of the films (9) ______ (they), but because it gives our family time to stay close. In today's busy world, (10) ______ (spend) quality time with family is more important than anything else.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a tradition`，后面有 `we all look forward to`，`that` 在定语从句中作 `to` 的宾语。

2. **答案：to watch**
   - **考点**：不定式作后置定语。`a film to watch` 表示"一部要看的电影"。

3. **答案：romantic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `ones`（代指 movies）需要形容词，`romance` 变为 `romantic`。

4. **答案：turns**
   - **考点**：名词复数/固定搭配。`take turns doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"轮流做某事"，`turn` 用复数。

5. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。少年穿越国家的目的是找回丢失的狗，用 `to find`。

6. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`both...and...` 为固定搭配，表示"既……又……"。

7. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`burst into laughter` 为固定搭配，表示"突然大笑"。

8. **答案：until**
   - **考点**：介词/连词。`not...until...` 表示"直到……才……"。

9. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`the films themselves` 表示"电影本身"，反身代词起强调作用。

10. **答案：spending**
    - **考点**：动名词作主语。动名词短语 `spending quality time with family` 在句中作主语。
