# 07-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：植树活动 (Tree Planting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

On March 12th, our school organised a tree planting activity on a bare hill outside the city. About two hundred students took part (1) ______ it. We set out early in the morning, each carrying a shovel and a bottle of water. When we reached the hill, our teacher divided us (2) ______ groups of four. My group was given six young trees (3) ______ (plant). We dug holes, placed the trees carefully, and then filled the holes (4) ______ soil. The work was harder than I (5) ______ (expect). After planting just three trees, my back ached and my shirt was wet with sweat. But looking at the (6) ______ (stand) trees, I felt a sense of achievement. By noon, all the teams (7) ______ (finish) their tasks. More than three hundred trees were planted that day. Our teacher told us that (8) ______ (plant) trees helps reduce air pollution and prevents soil from (9) ______ (wash) away by rain. I hope to come back next year to see how tall our trees (10) ______ (grow).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`take part in` 为固定搭配，表示"参加"。

2. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`divide...into...` 为固定搭配，表示"把……分成……"。

3. **答案：to plant**
   - **考点**：不定式作后置定语。`six young trees to plant` 表示"六棵要种的树"，不定式修饰名词。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`fill...with...` 为固定搭配，表示"用……填满"。

5. **答案：had expected**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。"预期"发生在"感到辛苦"之前（过去的过去），用 `had expected`。

6. **答案：standing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作前置定语。`standing trees` 表示"站立着的树"，现在分词表主动和进行。

7. **答案：had finished**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`By noon`（到中午时）表示过去某一时间点之前已完成的动作，用过去完成时。

8. **答案：planting**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。动名词短语 `planting trees` 在 that 从句中作主语。

9. **答案：being washed**
   - **考点**：动名词的被动形式。`prevent...from being done` 表示"防止……被……"，土壤"被冲走"用被动。

10. **答案：have grown**
    - **考点**：现在完成时。`how tall our trees have grown` 表示到将来回来时树已经长了多高，但此处在宾语从句中，也可理解为 `will have grown`（将来完成时）。中考层面 `have grown` 即可接受。
