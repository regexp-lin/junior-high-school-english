# 09-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：围棋 (The Game of Go)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Go, known as "weiqi" in Chinese, is one of the (1) ______ (old) board games in the world. It was invented in China more than 4,000 years ago and has been played by people of all ages ever (2) ______. The rules of Go are simple — two players take turns (3) ______ (place) black and white stones on a board — but the strategies are extremely complex. My grandfather taught me how to play Go when I was seven. He said it was a game that could train my mind and teach me (4) ______ (patient). At first, I found it difficult because there were so many (5) ______ (possibility) to consider with each move. I lost to my grandfather hundreds of (6) ______ (time) before I won my first game. That victory, although small, gave me great (7) ______ (confident). I continued practising and joined the school Go club in Grade Seven. Our club meets every Tuesday after school, and we play matches (8) ______ each other. Last year, I represented our school in a city-wide Go competition. I was nervous but managed (9) ______ (stay) calm during the matches. In the end, I finished in fifth place, (10) ______ was a result I was proud of.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the oldest board games`。

2. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：副词。`ever since` 表示"从那时起"。

3. **答案：placing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`take turns (in) doing sth` 表示"轮流做某事"。

4. **答案：patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`teach me patience`，动词后接名词作宾语，`patient` 变为 `patience`。

5. **答案：possibilities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`so many possibilities`，`many` 修饰可数名词复数。

6. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`hundreds of times` 表示"数百次"。

7. **答案：confidence**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`great confidence`，形容词修饰名词，`confident` 变为 `confidence`。

8. **答案：against**
   - **考点**：介词。`play matches against each other` 表示"互相比赛"。

9. **答案：to stay**
   - **考点**：不定式。`manage to do sth` 表示"设法做成某事"。

10. **答案：which**
    - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。
