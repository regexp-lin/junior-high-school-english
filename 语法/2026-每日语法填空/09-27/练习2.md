# 09-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：国庆节 (National Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

October 1st is China's National Day, (1) ______ marks the founding of the People's Republic of China in 1949. It is a day of great celebration across the country. The week-long holiday, often called "Golden Week", gives families a chance (2) ______ (travel) and spend time together. In our city, the streets are (3) ______ (beautiful) decorated with red lanterns and national flags. Last National Day, my family visited Beijing for the first time. We arrived (4) ______ the evening of September 30th and could already feel the festive atmosphere. Early the next morning, we went to Tian'anmen Square to watch the flag-raising ceremony. Thousands of people had gathered there before (5) ______ (we). When the national flag (6) ______ (rise) slowly against the dawn sky, everyone stood in silence. I felt extremely proud to be Chinese. After the ceremony, we visited the Palace Museum, (7) ______ was even more magnificent than I had imagined. The ancient buildings, with (8) ______ (they) red walls and golden roofs, told stories of a glorious past. We spent the rest of the holiday (9) ______ (explore) other famous sites, including the Great Wall and the Summer Palace. National Day always reminds me of how far our country (10) ______ (come) and how much there is to be grateful for.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，先行词为 `October 1st`。

2. **答案：to travel**
   - **考点**：不定式。`a chance to travel`，不定式作后置定语。

3. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `decorated` 需要副词，`beautiful` 变为 `beautifully`。

4. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on the evening of September 30th`，具体某天的晚上用 `on`。

5. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。介词 `before` 后接宾格。

6. **答案：rose**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时，`rise` 的过去式为 `rose`。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，先行词为 `the Palace Museum`。

8. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`their red walls`，指代 `buildings`。

9. **答案：exploring**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth`。

10. **答案：has come**
    - **考点**：时态。`how far our country has come` 表示"我们的国家走了多远"，现在完成时。
