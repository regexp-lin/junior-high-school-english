# 06-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：一次难忘的经历 (An Unforgettable Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last winter, our class went on a study trip to a science museum. It was (1) ______ first time I had worked on a group project with students from other schools, so I felt nervous (2) ______ excited at the same time. In the robotics hall, a guide explained how machines could be programmed (3) ______ (perform) simple tasks. While we were discussing our ideas, I dropped my notebook, and a stranger picked it up and (4) ______ (hand) it to me politely. That small kindness made me less shy, and soon I was able to share my opinions (5) ______ (clear). At the end of the day, our team won praise (6) ______ the teachers because we solved a puzzle together. On the bus back, I thought about what I had (7) ______ (learn): teamwork matters more than grades alone. I still keep the ticket stub in my drawer, and whenever I see it, the memory seems as vivid (8) ______ it happened yesterday. That trip was an (9) ______ (forget) experience in my middle school life, and it also taught me (10) ______ be grateful to strangers.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：the**  
   - **考点**：冠词。`the first time` 为固定表达，序数词 `first` 前用定冠词 `the`。

2. **答案：and**  
   - **考点**：连词。`nervous` 与 `excited` 为并列的两种情绪，且与 `at the same time` 搭配，用 `and` 表示“既紧张又兴奋”。

3. **答案：to perform**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`be programmed to do sth.` 表示“被设定/编程去做某事”，用不定式作补足语，填 `to perform`。

4. **答案：handed**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述过去经历，与 `picked` 并列，用一般过去时；括号内 `hand` 的过去式为 `handed`。注意 `hand sth. to sb.` 的搭配。

5. **答案：clearly**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `share`，用副词；`clear` 变为 `clearly`。

6. **答案：from**  
   - **考点**：介词。`win praise from sb.` 表示“赢得某人的表扬”，用介词 `from`。

7. **答案：learned**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。`had learned` 为过去完成时，表示“在回程路上回想在此之前已经学到的内容”；括号内 `learn` 的过去分词为 `learned`（英式亦可用 `learnt`）。

8. **答案：as**  
   - **考点**：连词与固定结构。`as vivid as` 为同级比较，表示“和……一样清晰生动”，第二个 `as` 引导比较状语从句。

9. **答案：unforgettable**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。括号内 `forget` 需变为形容词 `unforgettable`；句中已给出不定冠词 `an`，因 `unforgettable` 以元音音素开头，搭配正确。

10. **答案：to**  
    - **考点**：非谓语动词。`teach sb. to do sth.` 为固定搭配，用不定式符号 `to`。
