# 10-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：社区图书角 (Community Book Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Reading can open up new worlds, but not everyone has easy access (1) ______ books. To solve this problem, a group of volunteers in our community set up a free book corner in the neighbourhood park last year. They collected (2) ______ (hundred) of second-hand books from residents and arranged them neatly on shelves under a wooden pavilion. The book corner operates on the honour system — anyone can borrow a book without (3) ______ (register), and they are trusted to return it when they finish. At first, some people doubted (4) ______ the system would work. They worried that books would be stolen or (5) ______ (damage). However, the results have been (6) ______ (surprise) positive. Not only have most books been returned, but many people have also donated new ones. The collection has grown from 300 books to over 800. Every weekend, you can see children and elderly people (7) ______ (sit) on the benches, (8) ______ (quiet) reading. My neighbour, Mrs. Chen, who is 72 years old, goes there almost every day. She says the book corner has made her retirement much (9) ______ (enjoy). The success of this project proves that when a community works together, even a small idea can make a big (10) ______ (different).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`have access to` 为固定搭配，表示"有机会接触/获取"。

2. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`hundreds of` 表示"数百个"。

3. **答案：registering**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后接动名词。

4. **答案：whether / if**
   - **考点**：连词。`doubted whether/if the system would work`，`whether/if` 引导宾语从句。

5. **答案：damaged**
   - **考点**：被动语态。与 `stolen` 并列，`damage` 用过去分词。

6. **答案：surprisingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `positive` 需要副词，`surprise` 变为 `surprisingly`。

7. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`see people sitting`，感官动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词。

8. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `reading` 需要副词，`quiet` 变为 `quietly`。

9. **答案：more enjoyable**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much more enjoyable`，多音节形容词比较级用 `more`。

10. **答案：difference**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`make a big difference`，`different` 变为名词 `difference`。
