# 10-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：沙漠之旅 (A Trip to the Desert)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last autumn, my family took a trip to the Mingsha Sand Dunes in Dunhuang. It was my first time (1) ______ (visit) a real desert, and the experience was unforgettable. As we approached the dunes, I was struck by the (2) ______ (vast) of the golden sand stretching endlessly under the blue sky. We hired a camel and rode along the edge of the desert. The camel moved slowly and (3) ______ (steady), and I felt like an ancient traveller on the Silk Road. When we reached the top of a tall dune, the guide told us (4) ______ (slide) down on a wooden board. I was scared at first, but the (5) ______ (excite) was too great to resist. As I slid down, the sand produced a strange humming sound, (6) ______ is why the dunes are called "Singing Sand Mountains". In the evening, we visited the nearby Crescent Moon Spring, a small oasis (7) ______ has existed in the desert for over 2,000 years. The moon-shaped pool (8) ______ (surround) by sand dunes looked like something from a fairy tale. I took many photos to remember this (9) ______ (magic) place. The desert taught me that beauty can be found even in the (10) ______ (harsh) environments.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：visiting / to visit**
   - **考点**：动名词/不定式。`It was my first time visiting/to visit`，两种形式均可。

2. **答案：vastness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the vastness of the golden sand`，冠词后接名词，`vast` 变为 `vastness`。

3. **答案：steadily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `slowly` 并列修饰动词 `moved`，`steady` 变为 `steadily`。

4. **答案：to slide**
   - **考点**：不定式。`tell sb to do sth` 表示"告诉某人做某事"。

5. **答案：excitement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the excitement was too great`，冠词后接名词，`excite` 变为 `excitement`。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，指代沙子发出的嗡嗡声这件事。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a small oasis`（物），引导定语从句。

8. **答案：surrounded**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`The pool surrounded by sand dunes`，过去分词作后置定语，表被动。

9. **答案：magical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `place` 需要形容词，`magic` 变为 `magical`。

10. **答案：harshest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the harshest environments`。
