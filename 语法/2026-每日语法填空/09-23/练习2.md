# 09-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：高铁出行 (Travelling by High-Speed Train)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's high-speed railway network is one of the (1) ______ (long) in the world, stretching over 40,000 kilometres. High-speed trains, also called "bullet trains", can travel at speeds of up (2) ______ 350 kilometres per hour. They have made long-distance travel much faster and more (3) ______ (comfort) than before. Last month, my family took a high-speed train from Guangzhou to Wuhan. The journey, (4) ______ used to take over ten hours by regular train, was completed in just four hours. I was amazed at how (5) ______ (smooth) the train ran — I could (6) ______ (hard) feel any shaking. The seats were spacious, and there was free Wi-Fi on board. My father said he (7) ______ (remember) the days when people had to stand on crowded trains for hours. "The development of high-speed rail has truly changed Chinese people's (8) ______ (live)," he said. However, tickets for bullet trains are more (9) ______ (expense) than regular trains, which means not everyone can afford them. The government is working on (10) ______ (make) high-speed travel more accessible to ordinary people. I believe that in the future, bullet trains will connect every corner of China.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：longest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the longest`。

2. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`up to 350 km/h` 表示"高达"。

3. **答案：comfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `faster` 并列作比较级，`comfort` 变为 `comfortable`（`more comfortable`）。

4. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，先行词为 `the journey`。

5. **答案：smoothly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `ran` 需要副词，`smooth` 变为 `smoothly`。

6. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`could hardly feel`，`hardly` 表示"几乎不"。

7. **答案：remembered**
   - **考点**：时态。`said he remembered...`，宾语从句中用一般过去时。

8. **答案：lives**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`people's lives`，`live` 变为名词 `life`，复数为 `lives`。

9. **答案：expensive**
   - **考点**：比较级。`more expensive than`，多音节形容词比较级用 `more`。

10. **答案：making**
    - **考点**：动名词。`work on doing sth`，介词 `on` 后接动名词。
