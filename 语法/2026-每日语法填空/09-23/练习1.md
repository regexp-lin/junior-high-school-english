# 09-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：日记写作 (Diary Writing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I have been keeping a diary (1) ______ I was ten years old. Every night before bed, I sit at my desk and write about the events of the day. Sometimes I describe interesting things that (2) ______ (happen); other times I write about my feelings and (3) ______ (think). My diary is like a best friend to (4) ______ I can tell everything. Looking back at my old diaries is always (5) ______ (fascinate). It is amazing to see how much I (6) ______ (change) over the years. When I was ten, I wrote about toys and cartoons. Now, I write about my dreams, (7) ______ (friendship) and the challenges of growing up. My Chinese teacher says that keeping a diary is one of the (8) ______ (good) ways to improve writing skills. She encourages all her students to try it. She says the key is to write (9) ______ (honest) and not to worry about making mistakes. "A diary is for yourself," she reminds us. "There is no right (10) ______ wrong way to write one." I agree with her and plan to keep this habit for the rest of my life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since I was ten years old`，与现在完成进行时搭配。

2. **答案：happened / have happened**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述一般过去或到现在已经发生的事。

3. **答案：thoughts**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`my feelings and thoughts`，`think` 变为名词 `thought`，复数。

4. **答案：whom / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。`a best friend to whom I can tell everything`，介词 `to` 后接 `whom`；也可理解为 `which`。

5. **答案：fascinating**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is always fascinating`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

6. **答案：have changed**
   - **考点**：时态。`over the years` 提示用现在完成时。

7. **答案：friendships**
   - **考点**：名词复数。与 `dreams` 和 `challenges` 并列，用复数。

8. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the best ways`。

9. **答案：honestly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `write` 需要副词，`honest` 变为 `honestly`。

10. **答案：or**
    - **考点**：连词。`no right or wrong way` 表示"没有对或错的方式"。
