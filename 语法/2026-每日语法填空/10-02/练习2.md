# 10-02 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：爷爷的菜谱 (Grandpa's Recipe)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My grandfather is the best cook in our family. He learned to cook (1) ______ his own grandmother when he was just a boy. Over the years, he has created many (2) ______ (origin) recipes that no restaurant can match. His most famous dish is braised pork belly, which takes nearly three hours (3) ______ (prepare). He says the secret is (4) ______ (patient) — you must cook it on a very low heat and never rush. Last weekend, I asked him to teach me (5) ______ to make his braised pork. He was delighted and said, "It's time someone in this family (6) ______ (learn) my recipes!" We went to the market together and carefully selected the (7) ______ (fresh) ingredients. Back in the kitchen, he showed me every step. First, we blanched the pork to remove (8) ______ (it) smell. Then we added soy sauce, sugar, ginger and star anise. As the dish simmered on the stove, the most (9) ______ (amaze) aroma filled the entire house. When it was finally ready, I tasted it and closed my eyes in happiness. It was (10) ______ (absolute) perfect. Grandpa smiled and said, "Now this recipe lives on through you."

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`learn from sb` 表示"向某人学习"。

2. **答案：original**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `recipes` 需要形容词，`origin` 变为 `original`。

3. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：不定式。`takes three hours to prepare`，`It takes time to do sth`。

4. **答案：patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the secret is patience`，系动词后接名词作表语，`patient` 变为 `patience`。

5. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`teach me how to make`，疑问词 + 不定式。

6. **答案：learned / learnt**
   - **考点**：虚拟语气/时态。`It's time (that) someone learned...`，`It's time + 从句` 用一般过去时表虚拟。

7. **答案：freshest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the freshest ingredients`。

8. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`remove its smell`，`it` 的所有格为 `its`。

9. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most amazing aroma`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

10. **答案：absolutely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `perfect` 需要副词，`absolute` 变为 `absolutely`。
