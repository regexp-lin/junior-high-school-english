# 10-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：跳绳运动 (Skipping Rope)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Skipping rope is a simple but (1) ______ (effect) form of exercise that has been popular in China for centuries. It requires very little equipment — just a rope and a flat surface. At our school, skipping rope is part of the PE curriculum, and every student (2) ______ (expect) to pass a skipping test at the end of each term. Last year, I could only skip about 80 times in one minute, which was below the passing standard (3) ______ 120. I felt embarrassed and decided to practise harder. Every morning before school, I spent fifteen minutes (4) ______ (skip) in the courtyard. My mother sometimes joined me, and we made it (5) ______ a fun family activity. Gradually, my speed and stamina improved. I also watched online videos to learn different (6) ______ (technique), such as double-unders and criss-crosses. These tricks were much (7) ______ (difficult) than basic skipping, but mastering them gave me great (8) ______ (satisfy). By the end of the term, I could skip 165 times per minute, (9) ______ was well above the standard. My PE teacher praised my (10) ______ (improve) and used me as an example to motivate other students. This experience taught me that consistent effort always pays off.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：effective**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `simple` 并列修饰 `form`，需要形容词，`effect` 变为 `effective`。

2. **答案：is expected**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`every student is expected to pass`，"被期望"。

3. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`the passing standard of 120` 表示"120的及格标准"。

4. **答案：skipping**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth`。

5. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`made it into a fun activity` 表示"把它变成一项有趣的活动"。

6. **答案：techniques**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different techniques`，多种技巧。

7. **答案：more difficult**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much more difficult than`，多音节形容词比较级用 `more`。

8. **答案：satisfaction**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`great satisfaction`，形容词修饰名词，`satisfy` 变为 `satisfaction`。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

10. **答案：improvement**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`my improvement`，物主代词后接名词，`improve` 变为 `improvement`。
