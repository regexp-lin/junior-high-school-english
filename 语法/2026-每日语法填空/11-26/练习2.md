# 11-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：空气污染 (Air Pollution)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Air pollution is a growing problem that affects people's health and quality of life. In many large cities, the air quality index (AQI) often reaches (1) ______ (healthy) levels, especially during winter. The main sources of air pollution include vehicle emissions, factory smoke and the (2) ______ (burn) of fossil fuels for heating. Breathing polluted air can lead to serious (3) ______ (ill) such as asthma, lung disease and heart problems. Children and the elderly are particularly (4) ______ (vulnerability) to these health risks. In recent years, the Chinese government (5) ______ (introduce) a series of measures to fight air pollution. Stricter emission standards have been set for factories and vehicles. Cities are also encouraging people to use public transport (6) ______ of driving private cars. Green spaces and urban forests are being planted to help filter the air. Thanks to these efforts, the air quality in many cities has improved (7) ______ (notice). Beijing, for example, used to be known (8) ______ its heavy smog. But in the past few years, the number of blue-sky days has increased significantly. However, there is still a long way (9) ______ (go). Everyone can contribute by choosing cleaner ways to travel, saving energy at home and supporting (10) ______ (environment) friendly policies.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：unhealthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。根据语境"达到不健康的水平"，需加否定前缀 `un-`。

2. **答案：burning**
   - **考点**：动名词。`the burning of fossil fuels`，动名词作名词。

3. **答案：illnesses**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`serious illnesses such as...`，列举多种疾病。

4. **答案：vulnerable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`are particularly vulnerable to`，系动词后接形容词。

5. **答案：has introduced**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`In recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

6. **答案：instead**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`instead of` 表示"代替，而不是"。

7. **答案：noticeably**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be known for` 表示"因……而闻名"。

9. **答案：to go**
   - **考点**：不定式。`a long way to go` 表示"还有很长的路要走"。

10. **答案：environmentally**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `friendly` 需要副词形式，`environment` 变为 `environmentally`。
