# 11-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学习乐器 (Learning a Musical Instrument)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Learning a musical instrument is a rewarding journey that requires patience and (1) ______ (dedicate). I began learning the piano when I was seven years old. My parents believed that music education was just as important (2) ______ academic studies. My first piano teacher, Mrs Zhao, was a strict (3) ______ caring woman. She insisted that I practise scales for at least thirty minutes every day before playing any songs. I found it extremely (4) ______ (bore) at the time and often complained to my mother about it. However, as I grew (5) ______ (old), I began to appreciate why those basic exercises were so important. They helped me develop strong finger control and a good sense of rhythm. By the age of twelve, I (6) ______ (learn) to play several classical pieces by Chopin and Mozart. The feeling of (7) ______ (play) a difficult piece from start to finish without any mistakes was incredibly (8) ______ (satisfy). Last year, I passed the Grade 8 piano examination, (9) ______ is the highest level for amateur musicians. My parents were so proud that they bought me a brand-new digital piano. Looking back, I am grateful that my parents encouraged me (10) ______ (stick) with the piano even when I wanted to quit. Music has enriched my life in ways I never imagined.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：dedication**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `patience` 并列，需要名词形式。

2. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`as important as` 为同级比较结构。

3. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`strict but caring`，表示"严格但有爱心"。

4. **答案：boring**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found it boring`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

5. **答案：older**
   - **考点**：比较级。`as I grew older` 表示"随着我长大"。

6. **答案：had learned / had learnt**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`By the age of twelve` 表示"到十二岁时"，用过去完成时。

7. **答案：playing**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词形式。

8. **答案：satisfying**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰 `feeling`（事物）用 `-ing` 形式。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代 `Grade 8 piano examination`。

10. **答案：to stick**
    - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb to do sth` 表示"鼓励某人做某事"。
