# 07-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：写给老师的一封信 (A Letter to My Teacher)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Dear Mr. Wang,

I am writing to thank you for everything you (1) ______ (do) for us over the past three years. When I first entered this school, I was a shy girl (2) ______ was afraid of speaking in class. You noticed this and encouraged me to answer questions, even when my answers (3) ______ (be) not perfect. You told me that making (4) ______ (mistake) was part of learning. Gradually, I became more (5) ______ (confidence) and started to enjoy English lessons. I still remember the day (6) ______ you organised a speech contest. I was so nervous that my (7) ______ (hand) were shaking. But when I finished, you gave me a big smile and said, "Well done!" That moment meant more to me (8) ______ any prize. You have not only taught me English (9) ______ also shown me how to believe in myself. As I move on to high school, I will carry your lessons (10) ______ (deep) in my heart.

Your student,
Li Hua

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have done**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`over the past three years` 表示从过去到现在，用现在完成时 `have done`。

2. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a shy girl`（人），在定语从句中作主语，用 `who` 或 `that`。

3. **答案：were**
   - **考点**：主谓一致 + 一般过去时。叙述过去的情况，`answers` 为复数，用 `were`。

4. **答案：mistakes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`make mistakes` 表示"犯错"，为固定搭配，用复数。

5. **答案：confident**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`became more + 形容词` 构成比较级结构，`confidence` 变为 `confident`。

6. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the day`（时间），在定语从句中作时间状语，用 `when`。

7. **答案：hands**
   - **考点**：名词复数。人有两只手，`hand` 用复数 `hands`。

8. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较连词。`more...than...` 构成比较级结构。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 为固定搭配，表示"不仅……而且……"。

10. **答案：deeply**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `carry` 需要副词，`deep` 变为 `deeply`（深深地）。
