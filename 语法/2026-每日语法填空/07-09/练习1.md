# 07-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：地铁出行 (Travelling by Subway)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The subway has become an important part of city life in China. It (1) ______ (carry) millions of passengers every day. Compared with driving, travelling by subway is much (2) ______ (fast) during rush hours because it does not get stuck in traffic. A ride that takes an hour by car may only need twenty minutes (3) ______ subway. The stations are kept very clean, and clear signs are put up (4) ______ (help) passengers find their way. Most subway systems now have smart card machines at the entrance, (5) ______ makes it convenient to buy tickets. My city opened its first subway line five years ago, and three more lines (6) ______ (add) since then. However, the subway can be extremely (7) ______ (crowd) during morning and evening rush hours. Sometimes I have to wait (8) ______ two or three trains pass before I can get on. Experts suggest (9) ______ (travel) at off-peak times if possible. Overall, the subway is one of the (10) ______ (green) ways to travel in a big city.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：carries**
   - **考点**：主谓一致 + 一般现在时。`every day` 表示一般性事实，主语 `It` 为第三人称单数，动词加 `-es`。

2. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。`Compared with driving` 暗含比较，`fast` 的比较级为 `faster`。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by subway` 表示"乘地铁"，`by + 交通工具` 为固定搭配。

4. **答案：to help**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。标识被设置的目的是"帮助乘客找到路"，用 `to help`。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事（有智能卡机器），在从句中作主语，用 `which`。

6. **答案：have been added**
   - **考点**：现在完成时 + 被动语态。`since then` 提示用现在完成时，地铁线"被增加"，主语 `lines` 为复数，用 `have been added`。

7. **答案：crowded**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be crowded` 表示"拥挤的"，`crowd` 变为过去分词/形容词 `crowded`。

8. **答案：until / till**
   - **考点**：连词。`wait until/till` 表示"等到……才"，引导时间状语从句。

9. **答案：travelling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`suggest doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"建议做某事"。

10. **答案：greenest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`green` 的最高级为 `greenest`。
