# 09-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：传统武术 (Traditional Martial Arts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese martial arts, also known (1) ______ kung fu, have a long and rich history. They include many different styles, such as Tai Chi, Wing Chun and Shaolin kung fu. My grandfather has been practising Tai Chi (2) ______ he was a young man. Every morning, he goes to the park and performs his routines gracefully. He moves so (3) ______ (slow) and smoothly that he looks like he is dancing. He once told me that Tai Chi is not just about (4) ______ (fight). "It is a way to keep your body and mind in (5) ______ (harmonious)," he said. Inspired by my grandfather, I joined the school's martial arts club last year. Our coach, Mr. Sun, is a former national champion who (6) ______ (win) many competitions in his youth. He teaches us Changquan, a style that involves powerful kicks and fast (7) ______ (move). Training is very tough — we practise for two hours every afternoon. At first, my muscles were so sore that I could (8) ______ (hard) walk the next day. But as the weeks passed, I became (9) ______ (strong) and more flexible. Last month, I participated in my first martial arts competition and won a bronze medal. It was a small achievement, but it (10) ______ (mean) a lot to me.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be known as` 表示"被称为"。

2. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since he was a young man`，与现在完成进行时搭配。

3. **答案：slowly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `moves` 需要副词，`slow` 变为 `slowly`。

4. **答案：fighting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`about fighting`，介词 `about` 后接动名词。

5. **答案：harmony**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`in harmony`，介词后接名词，`harmonious` 变为 `harmony`。

6. **答案：won**
   - **考点**：时态。`in his youth` 提示过去时，`win` 的过去式为 `won`。

7. **答案：movements**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`fast movements`，`move` 变为名词 `movement`，复数。

8. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`could hardly walk`，`hardly` 表示"几乎不"。

9. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`became stronger and more flexible`。

10. **答案：meant**
    - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时，`mean` 的过去式为 `meant`。
