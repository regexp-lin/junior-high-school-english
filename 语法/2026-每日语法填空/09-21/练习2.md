# 09-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：第一次坐飞机 (First Time on a Plane)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last July, I flew to Beijing for the first time. I had never been (1) ______ a plane before, so I was both excited and nervous. My mother drove me to the airport two hours before the (2) ______ (fly). After checking in, we went through security and waited at the boarding gate. The airport was huge — there were (3) ______ (hundred) of passengers walking in every direction. When it was time to board, I walked down the long corridor and found my seat by the window. As the plane took (4) ______, I gripped the armrest (5) ______ (tight) and closed my eyes. The engines roared loudly, and I could feel the plane (6) ______ (shake). After a few minutes, the shaking stopped and the plane flew (7) ______ (steady) above the clouds. I looked out of the window and was amazed at the view. The cities below looked like tiny models, and the clouds looked close enough (8) ______ (touch). A flight attendant came by and offered me juice and snacks. She was very friendly and made me feel (9) ______ ease. The flight lasted about two and a half hours. When we landed in Beijing, I felt proud of (10) ______ (I) for overcoming my fear. It turned out that flying was not scary at all!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`been on a plane`，乘坐飞机用 `on`。

2. **答案：flight**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`before the flight`，冠词后接名词，`fly` 变为 `flight`。

3. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`hundreds of` 表示"数百个"。

4. **答案：off**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`take off` 表示"起飞"。

5. **答案：tightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `gripped` 需要副词，`tight` 变为 `tightly`。

6. **答案：shaking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`feel the plane shaking`，感官动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词。

7. **答案：steadily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `flew` 需要副词，`steady` 变为 `steadily`。

8. **答案：to touch**
   - **考点**：不定式。`close enough to touch`，`enough + to do` 结构。

9. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。`feel at ease` 表示"感到轻松自在"。

10. **答案：myself**
    - **考点**：反身代词。`proud of myself`，主语为 `I`，用反身代词。
