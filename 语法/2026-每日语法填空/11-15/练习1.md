# 11-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：阅读习惯 (Reading Habits)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Reading is often described as a window to the world. Through books, we can travel to distant lands, meet (1) ______ (interest) characters and learn about different cultures without leaving our homes. However, a recent survey showed that the average reading time among Chinese teenagers (2) ______ (drop) significantly over the past decade. Many students prefer watching short videos (3) ______ reading books, because videos require less effort and provide instant entertainment. Our Chinese teacher, Ms Wang, has been trying hard to change this trend. She (4) ______ (create) a "Reading Corner" in our classroom last year, filled with novels, magazines and poetry collections. Every Friday, the last class period (5) ______ (devote) to free reading. Students can choose any book they like and read (6) ______ (quiet) for forty minutes. At first, some students found it boring and wanted to use their phones instead. But after a few weeks, most of them started to enjoy it. One of my classmates, who had never finished a whole book before, (7) ______ (read) five novels so far this year. Ms Wang also asks us to write a short book review after (8) ______ (finish) each book. She says that writing about what we read helps us think more (9) ______ (deep) about the content. I have discovered that reading is not only educational but also one of the (10) ______ (relax) activities in my daily life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `characters` 用 `-ing` 形式形容词。

2. **答案：has dropped**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`over the past decade` 提示用现在完成时。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`prefer A to B` 表示"比起 B 更喜欢 A"。

4. **答案：created**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`last year` 提示过去时间。

5. **答案：is devoted**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the last class period` 与 `devote` 之间是被动关系，`be devoted to` 表示"被用于"。

6. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `read` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：has read**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`so far this year` 提示用现在完成时。

8. **答案：finishing**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `after` 后接动名词形式。

9. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `think` 需要副词形式。

10. **答案：most relaxing**
    - **考点**：最高级。`one of the most relaxing activities`，"最……之一"用最高级。
