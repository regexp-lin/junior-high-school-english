# 11-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国茶文化 (Chinese Tea Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tea is not just a drink in China — it is a way of life. China is widely regarded (1) ______ the birthplace of tea, and the tradition of drinking tea dates back about 5,000 years. There are many different (2) ______ (type) of Chinese tea, including green tea, black tea, oolong tea and white tea. Each type has its own unique flavour and health benefits. My grandmother is very (3) ______ (knowledge) about tea. She can tell the quality of tea just by (4) ______ (smell) it. She taught me that the best green tea (5) ______ (pick) in early spring, when the leaves are young and tender. She also showed me the proper way of (6) ______ (make) tea: first warm the teapot, then add the right amount of tea leaves, and finally pour hot water at the correct (7) ______ (temperate). "Making tea is like making friends," she often says. "It requires patience and care." In recent years, tea culture has become (8) ______ (increase) popular among young people. Many modern tea shops have opened in cities, (9) ______ (offer) creative tea drinks mixed with fruit and milk. While these new drinks are tasty, my grandmother believes that nothing can compare (10) ______ a cup of pure, traditionally brewed tea.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`be regarded as` 表示"被认为是"。

2. **答案：types**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many different types`，`many` 后接可数名词复数。

3. **答案：knowledgeable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be very knowledgeable about`，系动词后接形容词，`knowledge` 变为 `knowledgeable`。

4. **答案：smelling**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词，表示"通过……"。

5. **答案：is picked**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the best green tea` 与 `pick` 之间是被动关系，描述一般事实用一般现在时。

6. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词形式。

7. **答案：temperature**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the correct temperature`，形容词修饰名词。

8. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `popular` 需要副词形式。

9. **答案：offering**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随状语，用现在分词形式。

10. **答案：with**
    - **考点**：介词。`compare with` 表示"与……相比"。
