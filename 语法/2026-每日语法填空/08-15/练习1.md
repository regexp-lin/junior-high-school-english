# 08-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：公共图书馆 (The Public Library)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The new city library opened last year, and it has quickly become my favourite place (1) ______ (spend) weekends. The building itself is a work of art — (2) ______ (design) to look like an open book from the outside. Inside, there are five floors of books, a children's reading area, and a quiet study hall. Anyone can get a library card for free (3) ______ showing their ID. Since it opened, more than 100,000 people (4) ______ (register) as members. I usually go there on Saturday mornings. The reading hall on the third floor is my favourite spot because it has large (5) ______ (window) that let in plenty of natural light. It is (6) ______ (quiet) place I know for studying. The library also holds free events, (7) ______ as book talks, film screenings, and art exhibitions. Last month, a famous writer came to give a talk about her latest novel. The hall was so (8) ______ (pack) that many people had to stand. She spoke about the importance of (9) ______ (read) in the digital age and encouraged young people to develop a love for books. I left the library that day feeling (10) ______ (inspire). A good library is not just a building full of books — it is a gateway to knowledge and imagination.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to spend**
   - **考点**：不定式。`my favourite place to spend weekends` 中不定式作后置定语。

2. **答案：designed**
   - **考点**：过去分词。`designed to look like...` 为过去分词短语作补充说明，表示"被设计成……的样子"。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by showing their ID` 表示"通过出示身份证"。

4. **答案：have registered**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since it opened` 提示用现在完成时。

5. **答案：windows**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`large windows` 表示"大窗户"，用复数。

6. **答案：the quietest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词 + I know`，`quiet` 的最高级为 `quietest`。

7. **答案：such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`such as` 表示"例如"。

8. **答案：packed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so packed that...` 中 `packed` 为形容词，表示"挤满了的"。

9. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：动名词。`of` 为介词，后接动名词。

10. **答案：inspired**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`feeling inspired` 表示"感到受鼓舞的"，过去分词描述人的感受。
