# 08-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：养蚕的经历 (Raising Silkworms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

This spring, my science teacher gave each student a box of silkworm eggs as a class project. We were asked to raise the silkworms and record their (1) ______ (grow) process. I had never taken care of insects before, so I was both curious and a little nervous. The eggs were tiny, no (2) ______ (big) than a grain of rice. After about a week, they hatched into small black worms. I fed them fresh mulberry leaves every day. The worms ate (3) ______ (hungry) — I could actually hear them chewing! As they grew (4) ______ (large), they changed colour from black to white. My mother was not happy about (5) ______ (have) worms in the house, but she helped me find mulberry trees in the neighbourhood. After about a month, the silkworms began to spin (6) ______ (cocoon). Each worm wrapped (7) ______ (it) in a shiny white thread. The science teacher explained that this thread (8) ______ (use) for thousands of years to make silk — one of China's greatest (9) ______ (invent). Two weeks later, beautiful white moths emerged from the cocoons. Watching the complete life cycle of a silkworm was one of the most (10) ______ (education) experiences I have ever had.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：growth**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `process` 需要名词/形容词，`grow` 变为 `growth`（生长），名词作定语。

2. **答案：bigger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`no bigger than` 表示"不比……大"，用比较级。

3. **答案：hungrily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `ate` 需要副词，`hungry` 变为 `hungrily`。

4. **答案：larger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`as they grew larger` 表示"随着它们长大"。

5. **答案：having**
   - **考点**：动名词。`about` 为介词，后接动名词。

6. **答案：cocoons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。每条蚕都结茧，多条蚕结多个茧，用复数。

7. **答案：itself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`each worm wrapped itself` 中主语与宾语指同一对象，用 `itself`。

8. **答案：had been used**
   - **考点**：过去完成时 + 被动语态。`for thousands of years` 表示持续性，丝线"被使用"，在 `explained` 之前的状态。

9. **答案：inventions**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`greatest` 后接名词，`invent` 变为 `invention`，用复数。

10. **答案：educational**
    - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most educational`，`education` 变为 `educational`。
