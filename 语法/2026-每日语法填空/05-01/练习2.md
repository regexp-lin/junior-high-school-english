# 05-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：尊重劳动者 (Respecting All Workers)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every day, we benefit from the hard work of people we may never notice. The cleaners who sweep the streets, the farmers who grow our food, the delivery workers who bring packages to our doors — they all deserve our 1. ________ (respect) and gratitude.

Li Hua is a school cleaner. She arrives at school at 5:30 every morning, long before any student 2. ________ (wake) up. She sweeps the playground, cleans the restrooms and makes sure everything is tidy 3. ________ students arrive. Her work is hard but she never complains.

"I feel happy when I see the students walk into a clean school," she said with a smile. "It makes all the early 4. ________ (morning) worth it." 5. ________ (sad), not everyone treats cleaners with respect. Some students throw rubbish on the ground without thinking about who has to pick it 6. ________.

In our society, there is no job that is more important or 7. ________ (little) important than another. A doctor saves lives, but without farmers, there would be no food 8. ________ (eat). A teacher educates students, but without construction workers, there would be no 9. ________ (school) to study in.

On this Labor Day, let's take a moment to thank the people who work hard to make our lives 10. ________ (good). A simple "thank you" can brighten their day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **respect**
   - **考点**：名词（无需变形）。our respect and gratitude 意为"我们的尊敬和感激"，respect 在此为名词。

2. **wakes**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）+ 主谓一致。before 引导时间状语从句，描述经常性动作，any student 为单数。

3. **before**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。before students arrive 意为"在学生到达之前"。

4. **mornings**
   - **考点**：名词复数。all the early mornings 意为"所有清晨"，morning 用复数。

5. **Sadly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰整个句子，sad → sadly，句首大写。

6. **up**
   - **考点**：副词/动词短语（无提示词填空）。pick it up 意为"捡起它"。

7. **less**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。more...or less... 意为"更重要或更不重要"，little → less。

8. **to eat**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作后置定语）。no food to eat 意为"没有食物可吃"。

9. **schools**
   - **考点**：名词复数。no schools 意为"没有学校"，school 用复数。

10. **better**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。make our lives better 意为"使我们的生活更好"，good → better。
