# 05-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：劳动节假期 (Labor Day Holiday)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The May Day holiday is one of the most popular holidays in China. Many people take this chance to travel, spend time with family or simply relax after months of hard work.

This year, my family planned a three-day trip to Hangzhou. We set off early in the morning 1. ________ avoid the heavy traffic. The drive took about four hours, but none of us felt bored because we played word games and 2. ________ (listen) to music along the way.

When we arrived, the first place we visited was the West Lake. It was even more 3. ________ (beauty) than I had imagined. The water was so clear that we could see fish 4. ________ (swim) below the surface. We rented a boat and enjoyed the peaceful 5. ________ (surround) for about an hour.

On the second day, we explored Lingyin Temple, one of the 6. ________ (old) temples in China. The temple was very crowded 7. ________ visitors, but the atmosphere was still calm and spiritual. I was deeply impressed 8. ________ the ancient architecture and the huge Buddha statue inside.

During the holiday, I also tried some 9. ________ (fame) Hangzhou dishes, including West Lake Fish and Longjing Shrimp. The food was 10. ________ (absolute) delicious! I will definitely visit Hangzhou again in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **to**
   - **考点**：不定式作目的状语（无提示词填空）。to avoid 意为"为了避免"。

2. **listened**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。and 连接并列谓语，与 played 并列。

3. **beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。more beautiful 构成比较级，beauty → beautiful。

4. **swimming**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。see sth. doing 意为"看见某物正在做某事"。

5. **surroundings**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the peaceful surroundings 意为"宁静的环境"，surround → surroundings（常用复数）。

6. **oldest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，old → oldest。

7. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be crowded with 意为"挤满了……"。

8. **by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be impressed by 意为"对……印象深刻"。

9. **famous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 dishes，fame → famous。

10. **absolutely**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 delicious，需用副词形式 absolutely。
