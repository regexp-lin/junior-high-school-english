# 05-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：制作短视频 (Making Short Videos)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Short videos are everywhere on phones today. Many teenagers enjoy recording daily life, sharing hobbies, or 1. ________ (tell) jokes in less than a minute. Some even dream of becoming professional creators 2. ________ the future.

Last month, I tried to make my first video about cooking a simple dish. I wrote a short script, set up my phone on a tripod, and turned on the kitchen light 3. ________ (bright). The first few takes were terrible — I forgot my lines 4. ________ the oil made loud noises.

5. ________ I didn't give up. I watched free lessons online and learned how to cut clips and add subtitles. After a week of practice, I posted the video on a youth platform. To my surprise, it received hundreds of likes, and several strangers left 6. ________ (encourage) comments.

Making videos is not only fun 7. ________ also a way to practice expression and patience. Of course, we must protect our privacy and avoid copying others' work without 8. ________ (permit).

My teacher reminded us that content should spread positive energy. If a video might hurt someone's feelings, it is 9. ________ (polite) not to upload it.

I am still a beginner, 10. ________ I am excited to keep learning. Perhaps one day I will film a story about my hometown and show its beauty to the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **telling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。or 连接与 recording、sharing 并列，用 telling。

2. **in**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。in the future 将来，固定搭配。

3. **brightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 turned on（开灯），用副词 brightly 表示"把灯开得亮一些"。

4. **and**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。forgot my lines 与 the oil made loud noises 为两个并列的糟糕情况，用 and 连接。

5. **But**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。上文讲失败，本句讲坚持，转折用 But，句首大写。

6. **encouraging**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 comments，用形容词 encouraging 表示"令人鼓舞的评论"。

7. **but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。not only...but also... 不但……而且……。

8. **permission**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。without 为介词，后接名词 permission；permit 作名词表"许可证"时语境不同，此处用 permission 表"许可"。

9. **impolite**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词否定前缀）。根据句意"若会伤人感情，上传是不礼貌的"，在 polite 前加 im- 构成 impolite。

10. **but**
    - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。still a beginner 与 excited to keep learning 为转折，用 but。
