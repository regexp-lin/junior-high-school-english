# 05-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：网络谣言与信息辨别 (Internet Rumors and Information Literacy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

A funny photo or shocking headline can spread online in minutes. Not all shared messages are true — some are rumors 1. ________ (create) to attract clicks or cause panic. Learning to tell fact 2. ________ fiction has become an important skill for teenagers.

My school library held a workshop last term. The speaker taught us to check the source before we believe a story. If the website is unknown 3. ________ the writer hides their name, we should stay doubtful. We were also told to compare information from 4. ________ (difference) websites and look for official notices from governments or schools.

Once, I almost forwarded a message about "free gifts" 5. ________ a famous company. Luckily, my brother searched online and found it was fake. I felt embarrassed, 6. ________ I learned a lesson: sharing without thinking may hurt friends and family.

Teachers say critical thinking is 7. ________ (safe) than trusting everything on a screen. When in doubt, it is better to wait and ask someone we trust.

In my class chat group, we made a rule — no forwarding unconfirmed news. I believe that with care and patience, we can use the Internet 8. ________ (wise) and build a cleaner online environment together.

The fight against rumors will continue, 9. ________ each of us can start with small actions. Remember: being responsible online is not 10. ________ option, but a duty.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **created**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。rumors created to attract... 过去分词短语作后置定语，表被动"被制造出来的谣言"。

2. **from**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。tell fact from fiction 辨别真伪，区分事实与虚构。

3. **or**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。unknown 与 the writer hides 为并列的两种可疑情况，在否定/条件语境中用 or 连接。

4. **different**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 websites，用形容词 different。

5. **from**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。a message about "free gifts" from a famous company 表示"来自某著名公司的信息"，用 from 表来源。

6. **but**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。embarrassed 与 learned a lesson 为转折，用 but。

7. **safer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than trusting everything 提示比较级，safe → safer。

8. **wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 use，用副词 wisely。

9. **but**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。The fight will continue 与 each of us can start 为转折，表示"斗争仍会继续，但每个人都可以从小事做起"。

10. **an**
    - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。option 以元音音素开头，not an option 表示"不是可选项"，用 an。
