# 07-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：DIY手工制作 (DIY Crafts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Making things by hand has become a popular hobby among young people. Unlike buying ready-made products, DIY crafts allow us (1) ______ (create) something unique and personal. My interest in DIY started when my aunt gave me a book about paper folding. Following the (2) ______ (instruct) in the book, I learned to make animals, flowers, and even small boxes out of coloured paper. The process required both patience (3) ______ attention to detail. One small mistake could ruin the whole piece. At first, most of my works looked quite (4) ______ (ugly) compared with the pictures in the book. But I did not give up, and my skills gradually (5) ______ (improve). Last Mother's Day, I made a paper bouquet for my mum. It took me three evenings (6) ______ (complete), but the look on her face when she received it was (7) ______ (total) worth the effort. She said it was the (8) ______ (nice) gift she had ever received. Now I also make greeting cards and bookmarks for friends. They are always (9) ______ (please) to receive hand-made gifts because they know real thought and love have been put (10) ______ them.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to create**
   - **考点**：不定式。`allow sb to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"允许某人做某事"。

2. **答案：instructions**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`the` 后接名词，`instruct` 变为 `instruction`（说明），常用复数 `instructions`。

3. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`both...and...` 为固定搭配，表示"既……又……"。

4. **答案：ugly**
   - **考点**：形容词。`looked` 为系动词，后接形容词；括号内 `ugly` 本身即为形容词，无需变形。

5. **答案：improved**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去技能逐步提升的过程，用一般过去时。

6. **答案：to complete**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It takes sb + 时间 + to do sth` 为固定句型。

7. **答案：totally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词短语 `worth the effort` 需要副词，`total` 变为 `totally`。

8. **答案：nicest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词 + she had ever received`，`nice` 的最高级为 `nicest`。

9. **答案：pleased**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be pleased to do sth` 表示"高兴做某事"，`please` 变为 `pleased`（感到高兴的）。

10. **答案：into**
    - **考点**：介词。`put...into...` 表示"把……投入到……中"，此处表示投入心思和爱意。
