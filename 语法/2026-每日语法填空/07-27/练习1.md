# 07-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园广播站 (The School Radio Station)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has a student-run radio station that broadcasts during lunchtime. I have been a host there (1) ______ last September. Every Monday and Thursday, I play music, read news, and share interesting stories (2) ______ my schoolmates. Before each show, I spend about an hour (3) ______ (choose) songs and preparing my script. The (4) ______ (hard) part is making sure my voice sounds natural and (5) ______ (friend) through the speakers. At first, I was so nervous that I often read too fast and (6) ______ (make) mistakes. My advisor, Ms. Chen, told me to take a deep breath and imagine I was just chatting with a friend. Her advice worked (7) ______ (wonder). Now I feel quite comfortable behind the microphone. The radio station has taught me valuable (8) ______ (skill) such as public speaking, time management, and teamwork. It has also made me more (9) ______ (confidence) in expressing my ideas. Last month, we received a letter from a student (10) ______ said our programme helped her get through a difficult time. That letter meant the world to me.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。`since last September` 与现在完成进行时搭配，表示"自从去年九月以来"。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`share sth with sb` 为固定搭配，表示"与某人分享"。

3. **答案：choosing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

4. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + part`，`hard` 的最高级为 `hardest`。

5. **答案：friendly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `natural` 并列作补语，`friend` 变为 `friendly`（友好的）。

6. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与 `read` 并列，叙述过去的情况，`make` 的过去式为 `made`。

7. **答案：wonderfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `worked` 需要副词，`wonder` 变为 `wonderfully`（极好地）。

8. **答案：skills**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`such as` 后列举了多项技能，用复数 `skills`。

9. **答案：confident**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more confident` 构成比较级，`confidence` 变为形容词 `confident`。

10. **答案：who / that**
    - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a student`（人），在定语从句中作主语。
