# 05-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：生物多样性日 (Biodiversity Day)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

May 22 is celebrated as the International Day for Biological Diversity, 1. ________ reminds us that every species matters. It helps people understand 2. ________ important it is to keep balanced ecosystems.

Forests and wetlands provide shelter 3. ________ countless plants and animals. However, habitats 4. ________ (destroy) when rivers are polluted or trees are cut down too quickly. Scientists have called on communities 5. ________ (act) together and set up protected areas.

Our club watched a documentary last week and designed posters 6. ________ (show) local birds. We also learned simple habits, 7. ________ (include) carrying reusable bottles instead of buying drinks packed 8. ________ plastic. If teenagers spread green ideas, biodiversity may stay 9. ________ (safe) in the long run.

The teacher said the task was not just homework—it was a promise 10. ________ future generations.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句或 International Day...，在从句中作主语，用 which。

2. **how**
   - **考点**：连接副词（无提示词填空）。感叹/程度结构 how important it is... 作 understand 的宾语从句，表示“它有多么重要”。

3. **for**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。provide shelter for... 表示“为……提供庇护所”，for 引出对象。

4. **are destroyed**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般现在时的被动语态，有提示词）。habitats 与 destroy 为被动关系，陈述普遍现象，用 are destroyed。

5. **to act**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式，有提示词）。call on sb. to do sth. 表示“号召某人做某事”，故填 to act。

6. **to show**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式表目的，有提示词）。设计海报是为了展示当地鸟类，用不定式表目的：posters to show...。

7. **including**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词/介词用法（有提示词）。including carrying... 表示“包括随身携带……在内”，说明习惯的具体例子。

8. **in**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。packed in plastic 表示“用塑料包装”，in 表材料或方式。

9. **safer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（有提示词）。in the long run 暗示与现状对比，“更安全”，用 safe 的比较级 safer。

10. **to**
    - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。a promise to future generations 表示“对后代的一份承诺”，to 表对象（亦可视作固定搭配记忆）。
