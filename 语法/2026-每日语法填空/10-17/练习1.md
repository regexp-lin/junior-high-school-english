# 10-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国茶文化 (Chinese Tea Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tea is deeply rooted in Chinese culture and has been enjoyed (1) ______ thousands of years. China is the birthplace of tea, and today it remains one of the world's (2) ______ (large) tea producers. There are many different (3) ______ (type) of Chinese tea, including green tea, black tea, oolong tea and pu'er tea. Each type has its own unique flavour and health (4) ______ (beneficial). My grandmother is a tea lover who takes the art of tea-making very (5) ______ (serious). She always uses a traditional clay teapot and heats the water to exactly the right (6) ______ (hot). She says the way you make tea is just as important (7) ______ the tea itself. Last year, she took me to a tea plantation in Hangzhou, where we watched workers (8) ______ (pick) the tender tea leaves by hand. The guide explained that the best tea leaves (9) ______ (harvest) in early spring when they are young and fresh. We also attended a tea ceremony, during which we learned the proper way of serving and (10) ______ (drink) tea. The experience gave me a much deeper understanding of Chinese tea culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for thousands of years` 表持续时间。

2. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the world's largest producers`。

3. **答案：types**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`many different types`。

4. **答案：benefits**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`health benefits`，`beneficial` 变为名词 `benefit`，复数。

5. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`takes sth seriously`。

6. **答案：temperature / heat**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the right temperature/heat`，`hot` 变为名词。

7. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`just as important as`，同级比较。

8. **答案：picking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`watched workers picking`，感官动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词。

9. **答案：are harvested**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the best tea leaves are harvested`，茶叶"被采摘"。

10. **答案：drinking**
    - **考点**：动名词。与 `serving` 并列，介词 `of` 后接动名词。
