# 10-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：课后服务 (After-School Services)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, many schools in China (1) ______ (introduce) after-school service programmes to help working parents. At our school, the programme runs from 4:00 to 5:30 p.m. on weekdays. During this time, students can choose to do homework, join interest (2) ______ (club) or receive extra tutoring. The programme is (3) ______ (complete) free for all students. I usually stay for the homework session. Having a quiet place (4) ______ (study) with teachers available to answer questions is very helpful. By the time I get home, I have already finished most of my homework, (5) ______ gives me more free time in the evening. My classmate Xiao Ling uses the time (6) ______ (different). She has joined the painting club and spends the afternoon (7) ______ (create) beautiful artworks. She says the club has helped her develop her (8) ______ (artist) skills and discover a new passion. Some parents were worried that the programme would put too much pressure on students, (9) ______ most students actually enjoy the extra time at school. A recent survey showed that over 80% of students found the after-school services (10) ______ (benefit). I think these programmes are a great way to make the most of the school day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have introduced**
   - **考点**：时态。`in recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

2. **答案：clubs**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`interest clubs`，多个社团。

3. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `free` 需要副词，`complete` 变为 `completely`。

4. **答案：to study**
   - **考点**：不定式。`a quiet place to study`，不定式作后置定语。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，指代前面整件事。

6. **答案：differently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `uses` 需要副词，`different` 变为 `differently`。

7. **答案：creating**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth`。

8. **答案：artistic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `skills` 需要形容词，`artist` 变为 `artistic`。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`were worried...but most students enjoy...`，前后转折。

10. **答案：beneficial**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`found the services beneficial`，`find + 宾语 + 形容词`。
