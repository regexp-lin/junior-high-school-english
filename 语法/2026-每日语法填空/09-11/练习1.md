# 09-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学做饭 (Learning to Cook)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, I decided to learn how to cook because my parents were both busy with work. I found a simple recipe online for tomato and egg stir-fry, one of the (1) ______ (easy) Chinese dishes to make. First, I went to the market and bought some fresh (2) ______ (tomato) and eggs. Back home, I washed the tomatoes and cut them into small (3) ______ (piece). Then I cracked two eggs into a bowl and beat them (4) ______ a pair of chopsticks. When the oil in the pan was hot enough, I poured the eggs in. They cooked much (5) ______ (fast) than I had expected, and I almost burned them! After (6) ______ (take) the eggs out, I added the tomatoes with some salt and sugar. The kitchen was soon filled (7) ______ a wonderful smell. Finally, I put the eggs back and mixed everything together. When my parents came home, they were surprised (8) ______ (find) dinner already on the table. "It tastes (9) ______ (well) than the restaurant!" Dad said with a big smile. Mum hugged me and said she was very proud of (10) ______. That evening, I felt a great sense of achievement.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：easiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the easiest dishes`。

2. **答案：tomatoes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。以 `-o` 结尾的名词 `tomato` 复数加 `-es`。

3. **答案：pieces**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`small pieces` 表示"小块"。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`beat them with chopsticks`，`with` 表示"用（工具）"。

5. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much faster than`，`much` 修饰比较级。

6. **答案：taking**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `after` 后接动名词 `taking`。

7. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be filled with` 表示"充满"。

8. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be surprised to find` 表示"惊讶地发现"。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`tastes better than`，`well/good` 的比较级为 `better`。

10. **答案：me**
    - **考点**：代词宾格。`proud of me`，介词后接宾格。
