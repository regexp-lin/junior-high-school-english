# 09-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：汉服文化 (Hanfu Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, Hanfu — traditional Chinese clothing — (1) ______ (become) increasingly popular among young people. On streets, in parks and at cultural events, you can often see people (2) ______ (dress) in beautiful Hanfu. The designs vary from dynasty to dynasty, but they all share elegant lines and rich (3) ______ (colour). My classmate Li Mei is a Hanfu enthusiast who (4) ______ (own) more than twenty sets. She wears Hanfu to school every Friday, which is our school's "Traditional Culture Day". She says wearing Hanfu makes her feel (5) ______ (connect) to Chinese history and proud of her cultural roots. Last month, our school organised a Hanfu fashion show, and Li Mei was invited to be one of the (6) ______ (model). She wore a Tang Dynasty-style outfit (7) ______ bright red and gold. As she walked across the stage, the audience burst (8) ______ applause. The show helped students understand that Hanfu is not just about fashion; it is a way of (9) ______ (keep) traditional culture alive. Some people worry that the Hanfu trend is just a passing fashion, (10) ______ I believe it represents a deeper respect for our heritage that will last for a long time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：时态。`in recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

2. **答案：dressed**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`see people dressed in Hanfu`，`be dressed in` 表示"穿着"。

3. **答案：colours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`rich colours` 表示"丰富的色彩"。

4. **答案：owns**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。`who` 指代 `Li Mei`（单数），描述现状用一般现在时。

5. **答案：connected**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel connected to`，`feel` 后接过去分词作表语，表"感到与……有联系"。

6. **答案：models**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the models`，`one of` 后接复数。

7. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`an outfit in bright red and gold`，`in` 表示穿着某种颜色/风格。

8. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`burst into applause` 表示"突然鼓掌"。

9. **答案：keeping**
   - **考点**：动名词。`a way of doing sth`，介词 `of` 后接动名词。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：连词。`some people worry...but I believe...`，前后转折。
