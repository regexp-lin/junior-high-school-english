# 06-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：展望高中生活 (Looking Forward to High School)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

High school sounds like a bigger stage with new subjects, longer reading lists, and more freedom to choose clubs. I am curious about chemistry experiments, and I also hope to join the debate team (1) ______ I can learn to express ideas clearly. Of course, I know the lessons will be harder, so I plan to manage my time more (2) ______ (wise) than I do now. My cousin warned me not to leave homework (3) ______ the last minute, and I wrote his advice in my notebook as (4) ______ reminder. Another thing that excites me is meeting classmates from different junior schools; I believe fresh friendships will make the campus feel (5) ______ (warm). I have already borrowed two novels from the library to improve my reading speed, (6) ______ my Chinese teacher says will matter in every subject. On weekends, I want to keep running because a strong body supports a clear mind. I am a little afraid of getting lost in (7) ______ new building, but maps on the phone can help. Most importantly, I will respect teachers, ask questions bravely, and treat exams (8) ______ a way to check progress instead of a battlefield. High school may be challenging, (9) ______ I am ready to grow. When September comes, I will walk through the gate with a smile and tell myself that the best preparation for tomorrow is doing my best (10) ______ (today).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**  
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the debate team`（抽象场合/团队活动），定语从句结构完整，缺状语，用 `where` 引导，相当于 `on which team`。若强调目的，部分语境也可用 `so that`，但本题从定语从句角度最稳妥的填法是 `where`。

2. **答案：wisely**  
   - **考点**：词性转换与副词比较级。句中已有 `more...than` 构成比较级框架，空格只需填副词原级；`wise` 变为 `wisely`，修饰动词 `manage`。

3. **答案：until**  
   - **考点**：介词。`until the last minute` 表示“直到最后一分钟”，说明不要拖延作业。`till` 与 `until` 同义，书面语常用 `until`。

4. **答案：a**  
   - **考点**：冠词。`as a reminder` 表示“作为提醒”，用不定冠词 `a`。

5. **答案：warmer**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。语境暗含与现在对比，校园因新友谊而“更温暖”，用 `warm` 的比较级 `warmer`；`feel` 为系动词，后接形容词。

6. **答案：which**  
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面“借小说提高阅读速度”这件事，在从句中作 `says` 的宾语，用 `which`。

7. **答案：a**  
   - **考点**：冠词。`a new building` 表示“一栋新楼”，表泛指，用 `a`。

8. **答案：as**  
   - **考点**：介词。`treat...as...` 表示“把……当作……”。

9. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。`challenging` 与 `ready to grow` 为转折关系，用 `but`。

10. **答案：today**  
    - **考点**：副词（时间状语）。括号内 `today` 形式不变，强调“把今天的事做到最好”，在句中作时间状语。
