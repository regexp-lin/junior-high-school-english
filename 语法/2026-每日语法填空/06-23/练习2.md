# 06-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中考后的暑假 (Summer After the Exam)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When the final bell rings, summer begins like a long breath after running. At first, I slept until noon and watched movies without looking at the clock, enjoying the freedom I had dreamed (1) ______ for months. A week later, boredom knocked on the door, so I (2) ______ (make) a list of small goals: reading two novels, learning to cook two dishes, and volunteering at the community centre every Tuesday. My mother laughed and said a good holiday should feel (3) ______ (relax), not empty. I followed her advice and started jogging in the park (4) ______ sunrise; the cool air made my mind clear. I also visited my grandparents in the countryside, helped Grandpa water vegetables, and listened to stories about his youth. Those afternoons passed slowly, (5) ______ they left warm pictures in my memory. Before August ends, I want to preview a few high school topics so that I will feel (6) ______ (little) stressed in September. I know I still need time to play, (7) ______ I also want to enter the new term with confidence. Summer after the exam is a (8) ______ (price) gift—it thanks us for the effort we gave, and it asks us to rest, explore, and get ready. I am learning to balance fun and growth, and this lesson may be (9) ______ (good) than any single test score. No matter how hot the days are, I tell myself to stay patient, because every season brings its own homework for the heart. When I look back, I think I will remember not only the score on paper, (10) ______ the sunlight on the road home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：of**  
   - **考点**：介词。`dream of` 为固定搭配；定语从句修饰 `freedom`，完整结构为 `freedom (that/which) I had dreamed of`。

2. **答案：made**  
   - **考点**：动词时态。叙述考试后的暑假安排，用一般过去时；括号内 `make` 的过去式为 `made`。`make a list` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：relaxing**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel` 为系动词，后接形容词；描述假期“令人放松”，用 `relaxing`（`relaxed` 多描述人）。

4. **答案：before**  
   - **考点**：介词。`before sunrise` 表示“在日出之前”，说明晨跑时间。

5. **答案：but**  
   - **考点**：连词。下午“过得很慢”与“留下温暖画面”之间为转折关系，用 `but`。

6. **答案：less**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。表示“压力更小”，`little` 的比较级为 `less`，修饰形容词 `stressed`。

7. **答案：yet**  
   - **考点**：连词。表示“虽然还需要玩耍时间，然而也想自信迎接新学期”，用 `yet` 表转折，语气与 `but` 接近但更书面，也避免与后文 `not only...but...` 的 `but` 完全重复。

8. **答案：precious**  
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `gift`，用形容词；括号内 `price` 变为 `precious`，表示“珍贵的”。前面已有不定冠词 `a`。

9. **答案：better**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 `than`，用 `good` 的比较级 `better`，表示“比任何一次分数更重要/更有意义”。

10. **答案：but**  
    - **考点**：连词与固定结构。`not only...but (also)...` 表示“不仅……而且……”，与 `not only` 呼应用 `but`（`also` 可省略）。
