# 09-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：邻居之间的友情 (Friendship Between Neighbours)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mr. Wang and Mr. Li have been neighbours for over fifteen years. They live next door to each (1) ______ in a quiet street. Despite their different (2) ______ (hobby), they have become the best of friends. Mr. Wang enjoys gardening, while Mr. Li prefers (3) ______ (play) chess. Every Saturday morning, they meet at Mr. Wang's garden to chat over a cup of tea. Mr. Wang often shares the (4) ______ (vegetable) he grows with Mr. Li's family, and in return, Mr. Li teaches Mr. Wang's grandson how to play chess. Their friendship was tested last winter when a heavy snowstorm (5) ______ (hit) the area. The roads were (6) ______ (complete) blocked, and many elderly people had difficulty getting food and medicine. Without (7) ______ (hesitate), Mr. Wang and Mr. Li went door to door, checking on their elderly neighbours and (8) ______ (bring) them supplies. One elderly woman was so grateful that she cried and said, "You are the (9) ______ (kind) people I know." The community praised them (10) ______ their selfless actions. Their story shows that good neighbours can make life much better.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：other**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`each other` 表示"彼此"。

2. **答案：hobbies**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different hobbies`，`hobby` 变为复数 `hobbies`。

3. **答案：playing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`prefer doing sth` 表示"更喜欢做某事"。

4. **答案：vegetables**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the vegetables he grows`，蔬菜有多种，用复数。

5. **答案：hit**
   - **考点**：时态。`last winter` 提示过去时，`hit` 的过去式仍为 `hit`。

6. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `blocked` 需要副词，`complete` 变为 `completely`。

7. **答案：hesitation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `without` 后接名词，`hesitate` 变为 `hesitation`。

8. **答案：bringing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。与 `checking` 并列，用现在分词。

9. **答案：kindest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the kindest people I know`。

10. **答案：for**
    - **考点**：介词。`praise sb for sth` 表示"因为某事表扬某人"。
