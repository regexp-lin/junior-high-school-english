# 09-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：课外阅读计划 (After-School Reading Plan)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school has introduced an after-school reading plan to encourage students to read more widely. Under this plan, each student is expected (1) ______ (read) at least two books per month outside of textbooks. The school library has prepared a recommended (2) ______ (read) list that covers fiction, non-fiction, science and history. To make the plan more (3) ______ (interest), our English teacher created a "Reading Passport". Every time we finish a book, we write a short review and get a stamp in our passport. The student who (4) ______ (collect) the most stamps by the end of the year will receive a special prize. So far, I (5) ______ (read) six books since the plan started in September. My favourite one is a novel about a boy (6) ______ travels around the world in 80 days. The story is so (7) ______ (excite) that I finished it in just three days. My friend Xiao Hong prefers non-fiction. She recently finished a book about the history of (8) ______ (invent), which she found very informative. (9) ______ of us agree that reading has broadened our horizons and improved our writing skills. I would recommend this plan to any school that wants to develop students' love (10) ______ learning.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to read**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be expected to do sth` 表示"被期望做某事"。

2. **答案：reading**
   - **考点**：动名词/现在分词。`a recommended reading list`，`reading` 作定语修饰 `list`。

3. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`make the plan more interesting`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式的形容词。

4. **答案：collects**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。`the student who collects...`，描述将来情况的条件/定语从句用一般现在时。

5. **答案：have read**
   - **考点**：时态。`so far` 与 `since September` 提示用现在完成时。

6. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a boy`（指人），引导定语从句用 `who` 或 `that`。

7. **答案：exciting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so exciting that...`，修饰事物（故事）用 `-ing` 形式。

8. **答案：inventions**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`the history of inventions`，`invent` 变为名词 `invention`，指多项发明用复数。

9. **答案：Both**
   - **考点**：代词。`Both of us agree`，两人都同意，用 `both`。

10. **答案：of / for**
    - **考点**：介词。`love of/for learning` 表示"对学习的热爱"。
