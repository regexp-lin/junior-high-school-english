# 04-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：花园里的乐趣 (The Fun of Gardening)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Gardening is a relaxing and educational activity. It teaches us about nature and gives us a sense of 1. ________ (achieve) when we see our plants grow.

Last spring, my family decided 2. ________ (start) a small vegetable garden in our backyard. We didn't have much experience, so we read books and watched videos to learn 3. ________ basics. Then we bought some seeds and tools from a local shop.

My father helped me dig the soil while my mother 4. ________ (plant) the seeds. I was in charge of watering the plants every morning and evening. It was 5. ________ (surprise) how quickly the tiny seeds began to sprout. Within two weeks, green shoots appeared above the soil.

Taking care of a garden requires patience. Sometimes the weather was too hot and the 6. ________ (leaf) turned yellow. Other times, insects came and ate our vegetables. We had to find 7. ________ (nature) ways to keep them away without using chemicals.

After three months, we 8. ________ (final) harvested our first batch of tomatoes, cucumbers and peppers. The taste was much 9. ________ (fresh) than the ones from the supermarket. We were all proud of what we had grown 10. ________ (us).

Gardening has brought our family closer together and taught me that good things take time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **achievement**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a sense of + 名词，achieve → achievement。

2. **to start**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。decide to do sth. 意为"决定做某事"。

3. **the**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。learn the basics 意为"学习基础知识"，特指园艺方面的基础知识。

4. **planted**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。while 引导时间状语从句，与 helped 并列叙述过去动作。

5. **surprising**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。it was surprising how... 意为"令人惊讶的是……"，修饰事物用 -ing 形式。

6. **leaves**
   - **考点**：名词复数（不规则变化）。the leaves 意为"叶子"，leaf → leaves。

7. **natural**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 ways，nature → natural。

8. **finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 harvested，需用副词形式 finally。

9. **fresher**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，fresh → fresher。

10. **ourselves**
    - **考点**：反身代词。主语是 we，强调"我们自己种的"，us → ourselves。
