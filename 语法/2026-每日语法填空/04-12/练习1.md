# 04-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：外语学习 (Language Learning)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Learning a foreign language is one of the most 1. ________ (reward) things you can do. It opens up new opportunities and helps you communicate with people from all over the world.

English is the most 2. ________ (wide) spoken language in the world. In China, students begin to learn English at a young age. However, many of them still find it difficult to speak English 3. ________ (fluent) after years of study. Why is that?

One reason is that students spend too much time 4. ________ (memorize) grammar rules and vocabulary lists, but they don't practice speaking enough. Language is meant to be used, 5. ________ just studied in a textbook.

Here are some 6. ________ (effect) ways to improve your English. First, try to use English in your daily life. You can label objects in your room 7. ________ English or keep a diary in English. Second, watch English movies without subtitles. This might be difficult at first, 8. ________ it will greatly improve your listening skills.

Third, don't be afraid of 9. ________ (make) mistakes. Every mistake is a chance to learn. Even native speakers make errors sometimes.

The key to language learning is practice. The more you use it, the 10. ________ (natural) it will feel. So start speaking English today!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **rewarding**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。most + 形容词最高级，reward → rewarding（值得的、有回报的）。

2. **widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰过去分词 spoken，需用副词形式 widely。

3. **fluently**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 speak，需用副词形式 fluently。

4. **memorizing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 意为"花时间做某事"。

5. **not**
   - **考点**：副词（无提示词填空）。not just 意为"不仅仅"，与前面 meant to be used 构成对比。

6. **effective**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 ways，effect → effective。

7. **in**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。in English 意为"用英语"。

8. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后句是转折关系："开始很难，但会大大提高听力"。

9. **making**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。be afraid of doing sth. 意为"害怕做某事"。

10. **more natural/more naturally**
    - **考点**：形容词/副词比较级。the more...the more natural... 句型，natural 的比较级为 more natural。
