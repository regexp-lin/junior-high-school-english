# 08-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：盲盒文化 (Blind Box Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Blind boxes have become hugely popular among young Chinese people in recent years. A blind box is a small package (1) ______ (contain) a toy figure, but you do not know which one you will get until you open it. The fun lies (2) ______ the surprise. Some people buy them just for enjoyment, while others treat them as (3) ______ (collect) items. A single box usually costs between 50 and 100 yuan, which seems (4) ______ (reason). However, because each series has a rare "hidden" figure, some buyers spend (5) ______ (hundred) of yuan trying to find it. This has raised concerns among parents and (6) ______ (educate). They worry that the excitement of opening blind boxes is similar to gambling and may lead young people to spend money (7) ______ (wise). Several cities have already introduced rules to protect minors from (8) ______ (buy) too many blind boxes. On the other hand, supporters argue that collecting blind box figures is no (9) ______ (difference) from collecting stamps or cards. I think the key is to set a budget and stick to it. As long as we control (10) ______ (we) spending, blind boxes can be a fun and harmless hobby.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：containing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`a small package containing a toy figure` 表示"包含一个玩具公仔的小包裹"。

2. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`lie in` 表示"在于"。

3. **答案：collectible**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `items` 需要形容词，`collect` 变为 `collectible`（可收藏的）。

4. **答案：reasonable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`seems` 为系动词，后接形容词，`reason` 变为 `reasonable`（合理的）。

5. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`hundreds of yuan` 表示"数百元"。

6. **答案：educators**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。与 `parents` 并列，`educate` 变为 `educator`（教育者），用复数。

7. **答案：unwisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 前缀。修饰动词 `spend` 需要副词，`wise` 变为 `unwisely`（不明智地），根据语境应加 `un-` 前缀。

8. **答案：buying**
   - **考点**：动名词。`from` 为介词，后接动名词。

9. **答案：different**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`no different from` 表示"与……没有不同"，`difference` 变为 `different`。

10. **答案：our**
    - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 `spending`，`we` 变为 `our`。
