# 08-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：一次徒步旅行 (A Hiking Adventure)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last October, I joined a school hiking trip to the Baiyun Mountain. It was a cool autumn day, perfect for (1) ______ (walk) outdoors. Our group of thirty students (2) ______ (lead) by two teachers and a professional guide. The trail started gently, but after the first kilometre, it became (3) ______ (steep) and narrower. Large rocks and tree roots covered the path, so we had to watch our (4) ______ (step) carefully. Along the way, the guide pointed out different species of plants and insects. He told us that the mountain (5) ______ (be) home to over 500 kinds of plants. I was surprised (6) ______ (learn) that some of them could only be found in this area. Halfway up, we stopped at a stream to rest. The water was ice-cold but (7) ______ (incredible) refreshing. After three hours of climbing, we finally reached the summit. The view from the top was breathtaking — we could see the (8) ______ (entirely) city spread out below us. Everyone cheered and took photos. On the way down, my legs were shaking with (9) ______ (tire), but my heart was full of satisfaction. The hike reminded me that the (10) ______ (good) things in life are often waiting at the top of a long, difficult climb.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：walking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`for` 为介词，后接动名词。

2. **答案：was led**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。学生小组"被带领"，`group` 为集合名词作单数。

3. **答案：steeper**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`became steeper and narrower` 表示变得更陡更窄，暗含与前段路的对比。

4. **答案：steps**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`watch our steps` 表示"注意脚步"，用复数。

5. **答案：was**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。间接引语中，主句为过去时，从句也用过去时。

6. **答案：to learn**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be surprised to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"惊讶地发现"。

7. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `refreshing` 需要副词，`incredible` 变为 `incredibly`。

8. **答案：entire**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `city` 需要形容词，`entirely` 变为 `entire`（整个的）。

9. **答案：tiredness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`with` 后接名词，`tire` 变为 `tiredness`（疲劳）。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the best things` 表示"最好的事物"。
