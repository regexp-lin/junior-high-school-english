# 09-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：皮影戏 (Shadow Puppetry)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Shadow puppetry is one of the (1) ______ (old) forms of entertainment in China. It uses flat figures made of leather or paper, which (2) ______ (control) by sticks behind a lit screen. The audience watches the (3) ______ (move) shadows tell stories of heroes, legends and everyday life. Last month, a shadow puppetry master (4) ______ (invite) to our school to give a performance. The hall was packed (5) ______ excited students. When the lights went off and the show began, everyone fell silent. The puppets danced and fought on the screen, and the master provided all the voices (6) ______ (he). His skill was truly (7) ______ (amaze). After the show, he explained how the puppets are made. Each puppet takes about a week to carve and paint. He told us that he (8) ______ (practise) this art since he was a child and learned it from his grandfather. Sadly, fewer young people are interested (9) ______ learning shadow puppetry today. "I hope some of you will help keep this beautiful art alive," he said. His words inspired several of my (10) ______ (classmate) to sign up for his weekend workshop.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：oldest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the oldest forms`。

2. **答案：are controlled**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`figures...are controlled by sticks`，人偶"被操控"。

3. **答案：moving**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the moving shadows`，现在分词作形容词，表"移动的"。

4. **答案：was invited**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`a master was invited`，大师"被邀请"，过去时。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be packed with` 表示"挤满了"。

6. **答案：himself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`provided all the voices himself`，主语为 `he`，用反身代词强调"亲自"。

7. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was truly amazing`，修饰物/技能用 `-ing` 形式。

8. **答案：had been practising / had practised**
   - **考点**：时态。`since he was a child` 与过去的 `told us` 搭配，用过去完成时或过去完成进行时。

9. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be interested in` 为固定搭配。

10. **答案：classmates**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`several of my classmates`，`several` 修饰复数。
