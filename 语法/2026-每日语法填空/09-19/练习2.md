# 09-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校心理辅导 (School Counselling)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mental health is just as important as physical health, (1) ______ it is sometimes overlooked among teenagers. Our school has a counselling room (2) ______ students can talk to a professional counsellor about their problems. Ms. Zhao, our school counsellor, is a warm and (3) ______ (understand) person. She has helped many students deal (4) ______ stress, anxiety and relationship issues. Last term, my friend Xiao Lin was under great pressure because of the coming exams. He couldn't sleep well and often felt (5) ______ (hope). I encouraged him to visit Ms. Zhao. At first, he was (6) ______ (reluctance) because he thought asking for help was a sign of weakness. But after just one session, he felt much (7) ______ (good). Ms. Zhao taught him some (8) ______ (practice) techniques for managing stress, such as deep breathing and time management. She also reminded him that it is perfectly (9) ______ (normally) to feel stressed sometimes. Over the following weeks, Xiao Lin's mood improved greatly, and he performed well in his exams. His experience taught (10) ______ (we) that there is no shame in seeking help — in fact, it takes courage and strength.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but / yet**
   - **考点**：连词。`is important but sometimes overlooked`，前后转折。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a counselling room`（地点），定语从句中缺地点状语。

3. **答案：understanding**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`an understanding person`，现在分词作形容词，表"善解人意的"。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`deal with` 表示"处理、应对"。

5. **答案：hopeless**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt hopeless`，系动词后接形容词，`hope` 加后缀 `-less` 表"绝望的"。

6. **答案：reluctant**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was reluctant`，系动词后接形容词，`reluctance` 变为 `reluctant`。

7. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`felt much better`，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

8. **答案：practical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `techniques` 需要形容词，`practice` 变为 `practical`。

9. **答案：normal**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is perfectly normal`，系动词后接形容词，`normally` 变为 `normal`。

10. **答案：us**
    - **考点**：代词宾格。`taught us`，动词后接宾格。
