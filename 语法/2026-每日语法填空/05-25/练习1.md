# 05-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：无现金社会 (Cashless Society)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

More people in China are experiencing a cashless society 1. ________ these years. With a smartphone, you can 2. ________ (easy) pay for meals, rides, and tickets. Many young customers 3. ________ (prefer) mobile payments to paper money because they feel the process is faster and safer.

However, some seniors find the change 4. ________ (challenge). A community volunteer said, "We should teach older neighbors to scan QR codes 5. ________ patience." Local banks have also started to offer 6. ________ (week) classes for beginners.

Payment technology keeps 7. ________ (improve), so cash may appear less often in daily shopping. Still, experts warn everyone to guard 8. ________ personal data and avoid 9. ________ (share) passwords with strangers.

Cashless life is convenient, 10. ________ it asks for careful online habits as well.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **over**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。over these years 表示“这些年来”，说明变化持续至今，常与现在趋势描写搭配；during these years 亦可，但 over 更常见于叙述阶段性社会变迁。

2. **easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 pay，需用副词 easily。

3. **prefer**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时，有提示词）。主语 Many young customers 为复数，陈述普遍现象，用动词原形 prefer；prefer A to B 为固定结构。

4. **challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词/动词→形容词，有提示词）。find + 宾语 + 形容词作宾补，challenge → challenging。

5. **with**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。with patience 表示“耐心地”。

6. **weekly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词，有提示词）。修饰 classes，week → weekly。

7. **improving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（有提示词）。keep doing sth. 为固定搭配，用 improving。

8. **their**
   - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。指代前面的 everyone，作形容词性物主代词修饰 personal data；现代英语中常用 their 作单数泛指的物主代词。

9. **sharing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（有提示词）。avoid 后接动名词，avoid doing sth.，故填 sharing。

10. **but**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后转折：便利与需要谨慎习惯并存。
