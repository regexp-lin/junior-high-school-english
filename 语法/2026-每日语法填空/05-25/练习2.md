# 05-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：宿舍生活 (Dormitory Life)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Living in a school dormitory felt strange 1. ________ first, but I soon got used to it. Every morning, we wake up 2. ________ (early) than the day students because we need extra time to reach the dining hall.

Our dorm leader is strict 3. ________ noise after 10 p.m. She asks us to keep 4. ________ voices down and finish homework 5. ________ (quiet). Once I left the light on by mistake, 6. ________ I said sorry to my roommates the next morning.

I usually sweep the floor 7. ________ my roommate wipes the windows. The tasks sound easy, 8. ________ they help us learn responsibility. During exam season, we studied late and 9. ________ (encourage) one another to stay confident.

Those nights 10. ________ (be) tiring, yet they became warm memories I still treasure.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **at**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。at first 固定短语“起初”。

2. **earlier**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。than 提示比较级；修饰 wake up 用 earlier。

3. **about**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。be strict about sth. 对某事要求严格。

4. **our**
   - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。与 us 呼应，修饰 voices。

5. **quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰 finish，用 quietly。

6. **so**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。表结果：忘关灯，所以次日道歉。

7. **while**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。连接两个同时进行的动作，表对比。

8. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。easy 与 help us learn 之间为转折。

9. **encouraged**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时，有提示词）。与 studied 并列，叙述过去，用 encouraged。

10. **were**
    - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致（有提示词）。Those nights 为复数，过去时用 were。
