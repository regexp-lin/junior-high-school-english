# 09-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节水行动 (Water-Saving Campaign)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Water is one of the most precious (1) ______ (resource) on Earth, yet many people waste it every day without (2) ______ (realise) it. Our school recently launched a water-saving campaign to raise (3) ______ (aware) among students. Posters (4) ______ (put) up around the campus, showing shocking facts about water shortages in different parts of the world. Did you know that about 2 billion people (5) ______ (live) in areas where clean water is hard to get? During the campaign, each class was asked to come (6) ______ with practical ideas for saving water. Our class suggested (7) ______ (install) water-saving taps in the school bathrooms. Another class proposed collecting rainwater (8) ______ (water) the school garden. The best ideas (9) ______ (choose) and put into action by the school. Since the campaign started, our school's water usage has dropped (10) ______ 15%. This shows that small changes in our daily habits can make a big difference.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：resources**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most precious resources`，`one of` 后接复数名词。

2. **答案：realising**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后接动名词。

3. **答案：awareness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`raise awareness`，动词后接名词作宾语，`aware` 变为 `awareness`。

4. **答案：were put**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`Posters were put up`，海报"被张贴"。

5. **答案：are living / live**
   - **考点**：时态。描述当前现状用一般现在时或现在进行时。

6. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`come up with` 表示"想出、提出"。

7. **答案：installing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`suggest doing sth`，`suggest` 后接动名词。

8. **答案：to water**
   - **考点**：不定式。`collecting rainwater to water the garden`，不定式表目的。

9. **答案：were chosen**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`The best ideas were chosen`，想法"被选出"。

10. **答案：by**
    - **考点**：介词。`dropped by 15%` 表示"下降了15%"。
