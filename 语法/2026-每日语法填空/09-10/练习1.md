# 09-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家庭电影夜 (Family Movie Night)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every Friday evening, my family has a tradition that we all look forward (1) ______ — family movie night. We take turns (2) ______ (choose) which film to watch. This week, it was my turn, and I picked a comedy about a group of animals trying to save their forest home. Before the film started, Mum prepared some snacks (3) ______ Dad set up the projector in the living room. My younger brother could (4) ______ (hard) wait — he kept jumping on the sofa with excitement. Once the movie began, the whole family became (5) ______ (quiet). We laughed, gasped and even shed a few (6) ______ (tear) during the emotional scenes. The story was not only (7) ______ (fun) but also meaningful. It taught us about the importance of protecting the environment and working (8) ______ a team. After the film, we sat together and (9) ______ (share) our thoughts. Dad said it reminded him of his childhood in the countryside. Mum thought the message about teamwork was (10) ______ (particular) powerful. These Friday nights bring our family closer together, and I treasure every one of them.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`look forward to` 为固定搭配。

2. **答案：choosing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`take turns (in) doing sth` 表示"轮流做某事"。

3. **答案：while / and**
   - **考点**：连词。`Mum prepared snacks while/and Dad set up the projector`，表示同时进行或并列。

4. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`could hardly wait`，`hardly` 表示"几乎不"，注意与 `hard` 意义不同。

5. **答案：quiet**
   - **考点**：形容词。`became quiet`，`become` 作系动词后接形容词。

6. **答案：tears**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few tears`，`a few` 修饰可数名词复数。

7. **答案：funny**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`not only funny but also meaningful`，`fun` 变为形容词 `funny`。

8. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`work as a team` 表示"作为一个团队合作"。

9. **答案：shared**
   - **考点**：时态。与 `sat` 并列，用一般过去时 `shared`。

10. **答案：particularly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `powerful` 需要副词，`particular` 变为 `particularly`。
