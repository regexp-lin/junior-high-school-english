# 04-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：毕业寄语 (Graduation Wishes)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Three years of middle school life will soon come to 1. ________ end. As graduation approaches, I can't help looking back on the wonderful memories we have shared together.

I still remember the first day I walked 2. ________ the school gate. Everything was new and exciting. Over the past three years, I 3. ________ (grow) not only in height but also in mind. The teachers here have taught me so much more than just textbook knowledge. They have taught me how 4. ________ (think) independently, how to face challenges and how to be a kind person.

My classmates have also been an important part of my journey. We studied together, laughed together and sometimes even 5. ________ (cry) together. The friendship we have built is one of the most 6. ________ (treasure) things in my life. I hope we will stay in touch no matter 7. ________ we go in the future.

Graduation is not an ending — it is a new 8. ________ (begin). As we step into high school, there will be new challenges 9. ________ (wait) for us. But I believe that the lessons we have learned here will prepare us well.

To my 10. ________ (love) teachers and classmates: thank you for making these three years unforgettable. May we all have a bright and beautiful future!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **an**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。come to an end 意为"结束"，end 以元音音素开头。

2. **through**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。walk through the gate 意为"走过大门"。

3. **have grown**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。over the past three years 提示用现在完成时。

4. **to think**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。how to think 意为"如何思考"。

5. **cried**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。and 连接并列谓语，与 studied、laughed 并列，cry → cried。

6. **treasured**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。the most treasured 构成最高级，treasure → treasured（珍贵的）。

7. **where**
   - **考点**：连接副词（无提示词填空）。no matter where 意为"无论去哪里"。

8. **beginning**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a new beginning 意为"一个新的开端"，begin → beginning。

9. **waiting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作后置定语）。challenges waiting for us 意为"等待着我们的挑战"。

10. **beloved**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。my beloved teachers 意为"我敬爱的老师们"，love → beloved。
