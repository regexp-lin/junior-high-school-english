# 04-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：劳动的价值 (The Value of Labor)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

May 1st is International Labor Day, a day to honor 1. ________ (work) around the world. It reminds us that every job matters and that we should respect people who work hard to make our lives better.

In modern society, many teenagers grow up without doing much physical labor. Their parents do most of the housework, and their only "job" is 2. ________ (study). As a result, some students don't know how to cook, wash clothes or even clean their 3. ________ (room).

Our school believes that labor education is just 4. ________ important as academic education. Every Friday afternoon, students are required to participate in various labor activities. Some classes clean the campus, 5. ________ others work in the school garden. Last month, our class was responsible 6. ________ maintaining the school library.

At first, some students 7. ________ (complain) about having to do physical work. But after a few weeks, most of them changed their minds. They found that labor could be enjoyable and 8. ________ (satisfy). There is a special feeling of pride when you see the results of your hard work.

Labor teaches us important 9. ________ (lesson) about responsibility and teamwork. It also helps us understand and appreciate the efforts of those who serve us every day — the cleaners, the cooks, the bus 10. ________ (drive) and so many others.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **workers**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。honor workers 意为"向劳动者致敬"，work → worker → workers。

2. **to study / studying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式/动名词作表语）。their only job is to study / studying。

3. **rooms**
   - **考点**：名词复数。their rooms 意为"他们的房间"，room 用复数。

4. **as**
   - **考点**：固定句式（无提示词填空）。just as important as 意为"和……同样重要"。

5. **while**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。while 表示对比关系。

6. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be responsible for 意为"负责"。

7. **complained**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。at first 叙述过去的情况，complain → complained。

8. **satisfying**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。and 连接并列形容词，修饰事物用 -ing 形式，satisfying 意为"令人满足的"。

9. **lessons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。important lessons 意为"重要的课程/教训"，lesson 用复数。

10. **drivers**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。the bus drivers 意为"公交车司机"，drive → driver → drivers。
