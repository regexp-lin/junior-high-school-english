# 09-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园植树活动 (School Tree-Planting Activity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school organised a tree-planting activity to celebrate National Tree-Planting Day. All the students in Grade Nine were divided (1) ______ groups of five, and each group was given three young trees to plant. Our group chose a spot near the school gate (2) ______ there was plenty of sunshine. First, we dug holes in the ground using shovels. The soil was (3) ______ (hard) than we expected, so it took us quite a long time. Then we placed the trees carefully into the holes and filled them (4) ______ soil. I was in charge of (5) ______ (water) the trees. As I poured water around each tree, I imagined how tall they (6) ______ (grow) in a few years. Our teacher told us that trees are important because they produce oxygen, prevent soil erosion and provide homes (7) ______ birds and insects. She also mentioned that a single tree can absorb about 22 kilograms of carbon dioxide per year, (8) ______ helps fight climate change. By the end of the afternoon, over 100 trees (9) ______ (plant) across the campus. Looking at the new trees standing in neat rows, I felt a sense of (10) ______ (satisfy). I plan to come back often to check on "my" trees.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`be divided into` 表示"被分成"。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a spot`（地点），定语从句中缺地点状语，用 `where`。

3. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：比较级。`harder than we expected`。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`fill them with soil` 表示"用土填满"。

5. **答案：watering**
   - **考点**：动名词。`be in charge of doing sth`，介词 `of` 后接动名词。

6. **答案：would grow**
   - **考点**：时态。`imagined how tall they would grow`，过去将来时。

7. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`provide homes for` 表示"为……提供栖息地"。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，指代前面整件事。

9. **答案：had been planted**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`by the end of the afternoon` 提示用过去完成时被动。

10. **答案：satisfaction**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`a sense of satisfaction`，`of` 后接名词，`satisfy` 变为 `satisfaction`。
