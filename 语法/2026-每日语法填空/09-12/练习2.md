# 09-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：智能手表 (Smart Watches)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Smart watches have become one of the most popular electronic (1) ______ (device) among teenagers. Unlike traditional watches that only tell the time, smart watches can do many things. They can track your (2) ______ (day) steps, monitor your heart rate and even remind you to drink water. My parents bought me a smart watch (3) ______ my birthday last year. At first, I mainly used it (4) ______ (check) messages and answer phone calls. But gradually, I discovered more (5) ______ (use) features. The watch has a built-in alarm that wakes me up gently every morning. It also records how long and how (6) ______ (good) I sleep each night. The data helps me understand my sleep patterns and (7) ______ (make) improvements. However, our school does not allow students (8) ______ (wear) smart watches during class, because they can be distracting. I agree with this rule. When I'm studying, I need to focus (9) ______ my work without any interruptions. Some of my classmates think smart watches are (10) ______ (necessary), but I find mine quite helpful as long as I use it wisely.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：devices**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most popular devices`。

2. **答案：daily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `steps` 需要形容词，`day` 变为 `daily`。

3. **答案：for / on**
   - **考点**：介词。`for/on my birthday` 表示"在我生日时（作为生日礼物）"。

4. **答案：to check**
   - **考点**：不定式。`use it to check`，`use sth to do sth` 表"用某物做某事"。

5. **答案：useful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `features` 需要形容词，`use` 变为 `useful`。

6. **答案：well**
   - **考点**：副词。`how well I sleep`，修饰动词 `sleep` 用副词 `well`。

7. **答案：make**
   - **考点**：动词原形。与 `understand` 并列，`helps me understand...and make...`。

8. **答案：to wear**
   - **考点**：不定式。`allow sb to do sth` 表示"允许某人做某事"。

9. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`focus on` 为固定搭配，表示"集中精力于"。

10. **答案：unnecessary**
    - **考点**：词性转换（加否定前缀）。根据上下文，有些同学认为智能手表"不必要"，`necessary` 加前缀变为 `unnecessary`。
