# 05-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：街头艺术 (Street Art)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Street art used to be seen as messy graffiti, but today many cities welcome bright murals 1. ________ celebrate local history. Last month, our art club joined a 2. ________ (locate) community project to paint a long wall beside the river.

The design was created by a young painter 3. ________ had studied abroad. She asked us to mix soft blues and warm oranges 4. ________ (show) the meeting of water and sunset. While we worked, passers-by stopped 5. ________ (take) photos and left encouraging notes.

Not everyone likes paint on public surfaces. Some worry about damage to 6. ________ (they) property. Our teacher explained 7. ________ we used special outdoor paint and got official permission first. She also said art should respect neighbors and avoid covering important signs.

When the mural was finished, the gray wall became 8. ________ outdoor gallery. I realized street art can be 9. ________ (education) if it is planned with care. It turns ordinary corners 10. ________ small stages for stories everyone can share.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰先行词 murals，在从句中作主语，指物用 that 或 which。

2. **local**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词，有提示词）。a local community project 表示“本地/社区层面的项目”，locate → local。

3. **who/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰先行词 painter，指人在从句中作主语用 who 或 that。

4. **to show**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语，有提示词）。用不定式说明调色目的：为了表现水与晚霞的交融。

5. **to take**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式，有提示词）。stop to do sth. 表示“停下来去做另一件事”，此处指路人停下来拍照。

6. **their**
   - **考点**：代词（人称代词→物主代词，有提示词）。修饰名词 property，they → their，damage to their property 表示“对他们财产的损坏”。

7. **that**
   - **考点**：连接词（无提示词填空）。引导宾语从句，作 explained 的宾语，陈述事实用 that。

8. **an**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。outdoor 以元音音素开头，用 an outdoor gallery。

9. **educational**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词，有提示词）。在系动词 be 后作表语，education → educational。

10. **into**
    - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。turn... into... 为固定搭配，意为“把……变成……”。
