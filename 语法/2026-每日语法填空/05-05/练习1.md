# 05-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：独立生活能力 (Independent Living Skills)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When I entered middle school, my parents decided I should learn to live 1. ________ (much) independently. They taught me how to wash clothes, cook simple meals, and manage 2. ________ (I) time on weekends.

At first, I burnt an egg and forgot 3. ________ turn off the iron. Instead of shouting, my mother wrote 4. ________ short checklist on the fridge. Each task seemed tiny, 5. ________ together they built my confidence.

Now I can plan a week’s worth of snacks, clean my room 6. ________ (tidy), and fix a 7. ________ (loose) button on my shirt. Last month, when both parents were away on business, I looked after myself 8. ________ (good) than before.

Independent living does not mean refusing help. It means knowing 9. ________ to ask for advice and how to learn 10. ________ mistakes. These skills will matter long after we leave home.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **more**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。括号内 much 提示比较含义，与多音节副词 independently 搭配用 more，构成 more independently。

2. **my**
   - **考点**：代词（有提示词）。修饰名词 time，用形容词性物主代词 my。

3. **to**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式，无提示词填空）。forget to do sth. 表示“忘记去做某事（未做）”，与 forget doing 区分。

4. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a short checklist 泛指“一张简短的清单”。

5. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后转折：每项任务看似很小，但合在一起增强了信心。

6. **tidily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 clean，用副词 tidily。

7. **loose**
   - **考点**：形容词（有提示词）。修饰名词 button，表示“松动的”，用形容词原形 loose（勿与动词 lose 混淆）。

8. **better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。后有 than，修饰动词短语 looked after，good → well → better。

9. **when**
   - **考点**：连接副词（无提示词填空）。know when to ask for advice 为“疑问词 + 不定式”结构，表示“知道何时求助”。

10. **from**
    - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。learn from mistakes 为固定搭配，意为“从错误中学习”。
