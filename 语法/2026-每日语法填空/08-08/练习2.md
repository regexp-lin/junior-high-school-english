# 08-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：敬老院探访 (Visiting a Nursing Home)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Sunday, our Youth League organised a visit to a nursing home on the outskirts of the city. Twenty students volunteered (1) ______ (go), and I was one of them. When we arrived, the elderly residents were sitting in the garden, (2) ______ (enjoy) the warm sunshine. Some of them smiled at us, (3) ______ others looked a bit shy. We had prepared a short show, including singing, dancing, and a magic trick. The magic trick made everyone laugh, especially an old lady (4) ______ kept clapping her hands like a little child. After the show, we sat down and chatted with the residents. I spoke with Mr. Zheng, a retired army officer in (5) ______ (he) eighties. He told me about his life in the army and how the country (6) ______ (change) since he was young. His stories were (7) ______ (fascinate), and I could have listened for hours. Before we left, we gave each resident a hand-made card with warm (8) ______ (wish) written inside. Many of them were visibly (9) ______ (move). On the bus home, I thought about how (10) ______ (lone) some elderly people must feel. I decided to visit the nursing home more often in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to go**
   - **考点**：不定式。`volunteer to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"自愿做某事"。

2. **答案：enjoying**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。老人坐在花园里"享受"阳光。

3. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。`some...while others...` 表示"一些人……而另一些人……"。

4. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `an old lady`（人），在定语从句中作主语。

5. **答案：his**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。`in his eighties` 表示"在他八十多岁时"。

6. **答案：had changed**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。描述他年轻时到讲述时之间国家的变化，用过去完成时。

7. **答案：fascinating**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`were` 后接形容词作表语，`fascinate` 变为 `fascinating`（迷人的），描述事物。

8. **答案：wishes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`warm wishes` 表示"温馨祝福"，为固定表达，用复数。

9. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`visibly moved` 表示"明显地被感动了"，过去分词作形容词。

10. **答案：lonely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`how lonely` 中 `how` 后接形容词，`lone` 变为 `lonely`（孤独的）。
