# 08-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：漫画与创意 (Comics and Creativity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I have been drawing comics since I was in primary school. What started as a simple hobby has grown (1) ______ a real passion. In my comics, I create characters that live in a world full of magic and adventure. My main character, Xiaoming, is a boy (2) ______ discovers he can talk to animals. The idea came to me one day while I (3) ______ (walk) my dog in the park. I saw a group of birds on a branch and wondered what they might be (4) ______ (say) to each other. That evening, I drew the first page of my comic. Drawing comics requires more than just art skills. You also need to be a good (5) ______ (story). Each panel must tell part of the story (6) ______ (clear), and the dialogue has to sound natural. I usually spend about three hours (7) ______ (complete) one page. It is hard work, but seeing the (8) ______ (finish) product makes me very proud. Last year, I posted some of my comics online, and they received hundreds of (9) ______ (like). A few readers even asked me when I would publish a full book. I have not made that decision (10) ______, but the dream is definitely there.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`grow into` 表示"发展成为"。

2. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a boy`（人），在定语从句中作主语。

3. **答案：was walking**
   - **考点**：过去进行时。`while` 引导时间状语从句，表示"当……时正在"。

4. **答案：saying**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`might be saying` 为过去将来进行时，表示"可能正在说"。

5. **答案：storyteller**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a good storyteller` 表示"一个好的讲故事的人"，`story` 变为 `storyteller`。

6. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `tell` 需要副词，`clear` 变为 `clearly`。

7. **答案：completing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：finished**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the finished product` 表示"完成的作品"。

9. **答案：likes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`hundreds of likes` 表示"数百个点赞"，`like` 作名词用复数。

10. **答案：yet**
    - **考点**：副词。`have not...yet` 表示"还没有"，用于现在完成时的否定句。
