# 11-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：思乡之情 (Homesickness)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Homesickness is a feeling that many people experience when they are away (1) ______ home for a long time. It is especially common among students who go to boarding school or study in another city. My cousin, Liu Yang, went to a boarding school in Guangzhou last September. It was the (2) ______ (one) time he had lived away from home. During the first few weeks, he felt extremely (3) ______ (home) and called his parents almost every night. He missed his mother's cooking, his own bedroom and even the noisy neighbours (4) ______ he used to complain about. He told me that the (5) ______ (hard) part was eating dinner alone in the school canteen while thinking about his family gathering around the table at home. His classmates noticed that he looked unhappy and tried (6) ______ (cheer) him up. They invited him to play basketball after school and shared snacks from their hometowns with him. (7) ______ (gradual), he began to adjust to his new life. He made close friends and started enjoying his classes. Now, six months (8) ______ (late), he says that the school feels like a second home. He still misses his family, of course, (9) ______ he has learned that homesickness is a natural emotion that gets (10) ______ (easy) with time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`away from home` 表示"远离家"。

2. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。`the first time` 表示"第一次"。

3. **答案：homesick**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt homesick`，系动词后接形容词。

4. **答案：that / who / whom**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，先行词为 `neighbours`（人）。

5. **答案：hardest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the hardest part` 用最高级。

6. **答案：to cheer**
   - **考点**：不定式。`try to do sth` 表示"努力做某事"。

7. **答案：Gradually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。句首作状语，修饰整个句子，需要副词形式。

8. **答案：later**
   - **考点**：比较级。`six months later` 表示"六个月后"。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前后转折，"仍然想家，但已学会……"。

10. **答案：easier**
    - **考点**：比较级。`gets easier with time` 表示"随时间变得容易"。
