# 11-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：智能家居 (Smart Homes)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Smart home technology has made our lives more convenient than (1) ______ before. With just a voice command or a tap on a smartphone, people can control lights, air conditioning, security cameras and even kitchen appliances. These (2) ______ (device) are connected to the Internet and can be managed remotely, (3) ______ means you can turn on the heating before you arrive home on a cold day. My uncle recently renovated his flat and turned it (4) ______ a fully smart home. He installed smart speakers in every room, and they respond to voice commands in both Chinese and English. He can ask the speaker (5) ______ (play) music, set alarms or check the weather forecast. The smart lighting system (6) ______ (automatic) adjusts the brightness based on the time of day, and the smart lock allows family members to enter using facial (7) ______ (recognise). I visited his flat last weekend and was amazed at how (8) ______ (comfort) everything was. The curtains opened by (9) ______ (they) when the sun rose, and the coffee machine started brewing coffee at the exact time he usually wakes up. However, smart homes also raise some concerns. If the Internet connection fails, many devices may stop (10) ______ (work). There are also worries about hackers stealing personal information through connected devices. Despite these issues, it seems clear that smart homes will become increasingly common in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：ever**
   - **考点**：副词。`more convenient than ever` 表示"比以往任何时候都方便"。

2. **答案：devices**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`These devices`，指代多种设备。

3. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个句子。

4. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`turn...into...` 表示"把……变成……"。

5. **答案：to play**
   - **考点**：不定式。`ask sb/sth to do sth` 表示"要求做某事"。

6. **答案：automatically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `adjusts` 需要副词形式。

7. **答案：recognition**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`facial recognition` 表示"面部识别"，形容词后接名词。

8. **答案：comfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`how comfortable everything was`，系动词后接形容词。

9. **答案：themselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`by themselves` 表示"自动地，自行"。

10. **答案：working**
    - **考点**：动名词。`stop doing sth` 表示"停止做某事"（设备停止运作）。
