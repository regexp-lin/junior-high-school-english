# 04-17 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：个人成长 (Personal Growth)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Growing up means more than just getting taller. It means becoming a 1. ________ (good) version of yourself every day. Personal growth is a lifelong journey that requires courage and 2. ________ (determine).

When I was in Grade 7, I was afraid of speaking in public. My voice would shake whenever I stood in front of the class. One day, my teacher asked me 3. ________ (give) a short speech. I was so nervous that I wanted to refuse, 4. ________ something inside me said, "Just try."

I spent the whole evening 5. ________ (prepare) my speech. When I stood on the stage the next day, my heart was beating fast. But as I started speaking, I 6. ________ (notice) that my classmates were listening carefully. Their supportive smiles gave me 7. ________ (confident).

After the speech, my teacher said, "You did a wonderful job!" Those words meant the world 8. ________ me. From that moment on, I decided to face my fears instead of running away from them.

Now, I have become much 9. ________ (brave) than before. I have learned that growth happens when we step outside our comfort zone. Every challenge is an opportunity to discover something new about 10. ________ (we).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。a better version 意为"一个更好的版本"，good → better。

2. **determination**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。and 连接并列名词，determine → determination。

3. **to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。ask sb. to do sth. 意为"请某人做某事"。

4. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后句是转折关系："我想拒绝，但内心有个声音说……"。

5. **preparing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 意为"花时间做某事"。

6. **noticed**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。叙述过去发生的事，notice → noticed。

7. **confidence**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。gave me + 名词，confident → confidence。

8. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。mean the world to sb. 意为"对某人意义重大"。

9. **braver**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，brave → braver。

10. **ourselves**
    - **考点**：反身代词。主语是 we，about 后接反身代词 ourselves。
