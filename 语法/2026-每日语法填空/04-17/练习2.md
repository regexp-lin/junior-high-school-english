# 04-17 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：图书馆的价值 (The Value of Libraries)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Libraries are one of the most important places in a community. They provide free access 1. ________ books, magazines and digital resources for everyone.

Our school library is a place I visit almost every day. It has over 20,000 books, covering topics from science and history 2. ________ art and literature. The 3. ________ (library) there are always friendly and ready to help us find what we need.

One of the things I like most about the library is its quiet 4. ________ (atmospheric). Unlike my noisy home, the library gives me a peaceful space where I can 5. ________ (true) focus on my studies. I often stay there until it 6. ________ (close) in the evening.

Libraries are not just about books anymore. Many modern libraries now offer computers with Internet access, allowing people 7. ________ (search) for information online. Some libraries also hold events such as reading clubs, lectures and workshops. These activities encourage people of all 8. ________ (age) to keep learning.

Sadly, with the rise of smartphones and e-books, 9. ________ (few) young people visit libraries than before. Some people even think that libraries will disappear in the future. But I disagree. A library is more than a building full of books — it is a symbol of 10. ________ (know) and community.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。access to 意为"获取……的途径"。

2. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。from...to... 意为"从……到……"。

3. **librarians**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→名词）+ 复数。library → librarian（图书管理员），用复数 librarians。

4. **atmosphere**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。its + 名词，atmospheric → atmosphere（氛围）。

5. **truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 focus，需用副词形式 truly。

6. **closes**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）+ 主谓一致。until 引导时间状语从句，主从句时态一致；it 为第三人称单数。

7. **to search**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。allow sb. to do sth. 意为"允许某人做某事"。

8. **ages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。of all ages 意为"所有年龄段的"。

9. **fewer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，few → fewer，修饰可数名词 young people。

10. **knowledge**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a symbol of + 名词，know → knowledge。
