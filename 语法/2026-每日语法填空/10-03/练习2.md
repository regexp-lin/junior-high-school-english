# 10-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：养蚕 (Raising Silkworms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When I was in primary school, our science teacher gave each student a box of silkworm eggs (1) ______ a project. I was fascinated by the idea of watching tiny creatures grow and change. After about ten days, the eggs hatched into small black (2) ______ (worm). I fed them fresh mulberry leaves every day and kept their box clean. The silkworms ate (3) ______ (hunger) and grew quickly. Within a few weeks, they had become much (4) ______ (large) — fat, white and about five centimetres long. Then one day, something (5) ______ (amaze) happened. The silkworms began (6) ______ (spin) silk around themselves, forming cocoons. It took each silkworm about three days to finish (7) ______ (it) cocoon. The cocoons were beautiful — some white and some golden. My mother told me that silk (8) ______ (produce) from these cocoons for thousands of years in China. She explained that the Silk Road was named after this precious material, (9) ______ was once as valuable as gold. Eventually, moths emerged from the cocoons, laid eggs and the cycle began again. This experience taught me to appreciate the (10) ______ (wonder) of nature and the patience required to observe them.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`as a project` 表示"作为一个项目"。

2. **答案：worms**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`small black worms`，多条蚕用复数。

3. **答案：hungrily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `ate` 需要副词，`hunger` 变为 `hungrily`。

4. **答案：larger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`had become much larger`，`much` 修饰比较级。

5. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `something` 用 `-ing` 形式形容词（不定代词后置形容词）。

6. **答案：to spin / spinning**
   - **考点**：不定式或动名词。`begin to do/doing sth` 均可。

7. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`finish its cocoon`，`it` 的所有格为 `its`。

8. **答案：has been produced**
   - **考点**：时态与被动。`for thousands of years` 提示用现在完成时，丝绸"被生产"。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，先行词为 `this precious material`。

10. **答案：wonders**
    - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`the wonders of nature`，`wonder` 作名词表"奇迹"，复数。
