# 10-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：3D 打印技术 (3D Printing Technology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

3D printing is one of the most exciting (1) ______ (technology) of our time. It allows people to create three-dimensional objects by printing them layer (2) ______ layer using special materials. This technology (3) ______ (use) in many fields today, including medicine, engineering and education. Our school recently bought a 3D printer for the science lab. Our technology teacher, Ms. Huang, taught us how (4) ______ (design) simple objects on a computer and then print them out. The first thing I printed was a small model of the Eiffel Tower. It took about two hours (5) ______ (complete), and the result was surprisingly (6) ______ (detail). My classmate Chen Wei designed a phone stand, which was not only creative (7) ______ also very practical. Ms. Huang explained that 3D printing is already being used to make artificial (8) ______ (body) parts, such as hands and ears, for patients who need them. Scientists are even (9) ______ (research) how to print human organs in the future. I find it hard to imagine a world (10) ______ broken objects can simply be reprinted instead of thrown away. 3D printing truly has the power to change our lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：technologies**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most exciting technologies`。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`layer by layer` 表示"一层一层地"。

3. **答案：is used**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`this technology is used in many fields`，技术"被使用"。

4. **答案：to design**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to design`，疑问词 + 不定式。

5. **答案：to complete**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It took two hours to complete`。

6. **答案：detailed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was surprisingly detailed`，`detail` 变为形容词 `detailed`。

7. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...`。

8. **答案：body**
   - **考点**：名词作定语。`artificial body parts`，`body` 作定语修饰 `parts`。

9. **答案：researching**
   - **考点**：时态。`are even researching`，现在进行时表示正在进行的研究。

10. **答案：where**
    - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a world`，定语从句中缺地点/抽象场景状语，用 `where`。
