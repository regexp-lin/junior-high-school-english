# 04-08 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：文化多样性 (Cultural Diversity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our world is a beautiful place because of its diversity. People from different countries have different languages, customs and 1. ________ (believe). Learning about other cultures can help us become more open-minded.

In some Asian countries, 2. ________ (take) off your shoes before entering someone's home is a sign of respect. In Western countries, people usually shake 3. ________ (hand) when they meet for the first time. In some African cultures, music and dance are important parts of daily life. Each culture has its own way of 4. ________ (show) feelings and communicating.

Food is another area 5. ________ cultural differences are easy to see. In India, many people are vegetarians because of their religious 6. ________ (believe). In Japan, it is polite to make noise while eating noodles. In France, meals are 7. ________ (see) as a time for family and friends to enjoy together.

With globalization, different cultures are meeting and mixing more than ever before. This can sometimes lead 8. ________ misunderstandings. That is why it is so important for young people to learn about and respect other cultures.

At our school, we hold an International Culture Day every year. Students dress up in 9. ________ (tradition) clothes from different countries and share food and stories. It is always the most 10. ________ (excite) event of the year!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **beliefs**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。and 连接并列名词，believe → belief → beliefs。

2. **Taking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词 Taking 在句首作主语，注意首字母大写。

3. **hands**
   - **考点**：名词复数。shake hands 意为"握手"，hand 用复数。

4. **showing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 of 后接动名词形式。

5. **where**
   - **考点**：关系副词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 area，在从句中作地点状语。

6. **beliefs**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。religious beliefs 意为"宗教信仰"，believe → belief → beliefs。

7. **seen**
   - **考点**：被动语态（过去分词）。be seen as 意为"被视为"，see → seen。

8. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。lead to 意为"导致"。

9. **traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 clothes，tradition → traditional。

10. **exciting**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。the most + 形容词最高级，修饰事物用 -ing 形式，exciting 意为"令人兴奋的"。
