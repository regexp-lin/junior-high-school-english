# 04-08 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：慈善与公益 (Charity and Giving)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Charity means helping others without expecting anything in return. It is a way of spreading love and 1. ________ (kind) to people in need.

There are many ways to do charity work. Some people donate money to organizations that help the poor. Others volunteer their time to 2. ________ (teach) children in rural areas or visit elderly people in nursing homes. Every act of charity, no matter how small, can make someone's life 3. ________ (good).

Our school held a charity sale last month. Students brought old books, toys and clothes from home and 4. ________ (sell) them at low prices. The money we raised 5. ________ (send) to a school in a mountain village to buy new desks and books. We were proud to know that our effort could help other students who were 6. ________ (lucky) than us.

In China, there is a famous saying: "When one person is in trouble, help comes from all 7. ________ (direction)." This spirit of helping each other is deeply rooted 8. ________ Chinese culture. Whenever there is a natural disaster, people across the country quickly donate money and supplies to help 9. ________ (them) affected.

Charity is not just 10. ________ the rich. Anyone can be generous with their time, skills or even a kind word. Let's all try to make a positive difference in the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **kindness**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。and 连接并列名词，与 love 并列，kind → kindness。

2. **teach**
   - **考点**：动词原形。to teach 是不定式，volunteer one's time to do sth. 意为"自愿花时间做某事"。

3. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。make someone's life better 意为"使某人的生活更好"，good → better。

4. **sold**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。and 连接并列谓语，与 brought 并列，sell → sold。

5. **was sent**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般过去时）。money 与 send 之间是被动关系，用一般过去时被动语态。

6. **less lucky / unlucky**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级/前缀。than 提示比较，less lucky 意为"没那么幸运的"；或用 unlucky（不幸的）但需改为比较级形式。建议填 less lucky。

7. **directions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。all directions 意为"四面八方"，direction 用复数。

8. **in**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be rooted in 意为"扎根于"，是固定搭配。

9. **those**
   - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。those affected 意为"那些受影响的人"，those 指代人，后接过去分词短语作后置定语。

10. **for**
    - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。not just for the rich 意为"不仅仅是为了富人"，for 表示"对于"。
