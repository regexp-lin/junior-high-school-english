# 08-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校服争议 (The School Uniform Debate)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Should students wear school uniforms? This is a question that has been debated (1) ______ many years. In China, most schools require students to wear uniforms every day. (2) ______ (support) say uniforms create a sense of unity and prevent students from (3) ______ (compare) each other's clothes. When everyone (4) ______ (dress) the same way, family wealth becomes less visible, and students can focus more on their studies. However, not all students agree. Some argue that uniforms limit their (5) ______ (free) to express themselves. They believe that choosing what to wear is (6) ______ important part of growing up. Others complain that many school uniforms are poorly designed and not (7) ______ (comfort) to wear, especially in summer. Last week, our school held a debate on this topic. The team in favour of uniforms presented (8) ______ (strong) arguments and won. But even the winning team agreed that schools should listen to students' opinions and improve the design of uniforms. I think both sides have valid points. Wearing a uniform can build school pride, (9) ______ students should also be given chances to express their (10) ______ (individual) in other ways.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for many years` 表示"很多年"，与现在完成时搭配。

2. **答案：Supporters**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。主语需要名词，`support` 变为 `supporter`（支持者），用复数，句首大写。

3. **答案：comparing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`prevent sb from doing sth` 为固定搭配，`from` 为介词。

4. **答案：is dressed / dresses**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`everyone` 为第三人称单数，`is dressed` 表示"穿着"（被动），或 `dresses` 表示"穿衣"（主动）。

5. **答案：freedom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`their` 后接名词，`free` 变为 `freedom`。

6. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`an important part` 中 `important` 以元音音素开头，用 `an`。

7. **答案：comfortable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`not comfortable to wear` 中 `comfort` 变为 `comfortable`。

8. **答案：stronger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`stronger arguments` 表示"更有力的论点"，隐含与对方的对比。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。"穿校服可以建立校园自豪感"与"学生也应有机会表达个性"为转折关系。

10. **答案：individuality**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`their` 后接名词，`individual` 变为 `individuality`（个性）。
