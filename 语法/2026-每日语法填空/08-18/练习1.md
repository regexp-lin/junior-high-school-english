# 08-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：钓鱼的乐趣 (The Joy of Fishing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Fishing has been my grandfather's favourite hobby for as long as I can remember. Last summer, he finally took me along on one of his (1) ______ (trip). We drove to a quiet lake about two hours from the city. The water was so clear (2) ______ I could see fish swimming below the surface. My grandfather (3) ______ (patient) taught me how to put bait on the hook and cast the line into the water. Then we sat on the bank and waited. Fishing requires a lot of (4) ______ (patient), which is something I usually lack. After an hour of (5) ______ (sit) still with no result, I was ready to give up. But my grandfather told me that the (6) ______ (real) joy of fishing was not in catching fish — it was in (7) ______ (be) close to nature and slowing down. Just as he finished speaking, I felt a strong pull on my line! I jumped up and (8) ______ (careful) reeled in the fish. It was a beautiful silver carp, much (9) ______ (big) than I expected. My grandfather laughed and said it was beginner's luck. We released the fish back into the lake because he believed in fishing (10) ______ a sport, not for food. That day, I understood why he loved fishing so much.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：trips**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of his trips` 表示"他的一次出行"。

2. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so clear that...` 为 `so...that...` 结构。

3. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `taught` 需要副词，`patient` 变为 `patiently`。

4. **答案：patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a lot of` 后接名词，`patient` 变为 `patience`（耐心）。

5. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`of` 为介词，后接动名词。

6. **答案：real**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `joy` 需要形容词，括号内 `really` 变为 `real`。

7. **答案：being**
   - **考点**：动名词。`in` 为介词，后接动名词 `being`。

8. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `reeled in` 需要副词，`careful` 变为 `carefully`。

9. **答案：bigger**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than I expected` 提示用比较级。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：介词。`fishing as a sport` 表示"把钓鱼当作运动"。
