# 11-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：笔友 (Pen Pals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Having a pen pal from another country is a fantastic way to learn about different cultures and practise a foreign language. I started writing to my pen pal, Emma, three years ago through a school exchange programme. Emma lives in London and is the same age (1) ______ me. At first, we wrote letters by hand and sent them by post. It usually took about two (2) ______ (week) for a letter to arrive. The (3) ______ (excite) of receiving a handwritten letter from the other side of the world was something truly special. In her letters, Emma (4) ______ (describe) her life in England — the food she ate, the places she visited and the festivals she celebrated. I was particularly (5) ______ (surprise) to learn that British people eat fish and chips wrapped in newspaper. In return, I told her about Chinese customs, such as the tradition of (6) ______ (give) red envelopes during the Spring Festival. Now we mostly communicate through email and video calls, which are much (7) ______ (convenient) than traditional mail. Last summer, Emma and her family (8) ______ (come) to China for a holiday, and we finally met in person. I took her to visit the Great Wall and taught her how (9) ______ (use) chopsticks. She said it was the (10) ______ (good) trip she had ever had. We are already planning for me to visit London next year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`the same age as me` 表示"和我同龄"。

2. **答案：weeks**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`two weeks`，数词后接复数名词。

3. **答案：excitement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。定冠词 `The` 后接名词，`excite` 变为 `excitement`。

4. **答案：described**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去通信中的内容。

5. **答案：surprised**
   - **考点**：词性转换。描述人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

6. **答案：giving**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词形式。

7. **答案：more convenient**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than traditional mail` 提示比较级，多音节形容词用 `more`。

8. **答案：came**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`Last summer` 提示过去时间。

9. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：不定式。`teach sb how to do sth` 表示"教某人如何做某事"。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：最高级。`the best trip she had ever had`，`ever` 提示最高级。
