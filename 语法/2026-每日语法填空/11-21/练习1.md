# 11-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：野生动物保护 (Wildlife Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Wildlife protection has become an urgent issue around the world. Many animal species are facing the danger of (1) ______ (extinct) due to habitat loss, pollution and illegal hunting. According to scientists, the rate at which species are disappearing is nearly 1,000 times (2) ______ (fast) than the natural rate. The giant panda is one of China's most iconic (3) ______ (animal). For decades, it was listed as an endangered species. Thanks to the great efforts (4) ______ (make) by the Chinese government and conservation organisations, the panda population has been growing steadily. In 2021, the giant panda was downgraded from "endangered" (5) ______ "vulnerable," which was wonderful news. However, many other animals still need our help. The Yangtze finless porpoise, for example, (6) ______ (number) fewer than 1,100 in the wild. Protecting these animals requires more than just government action — it needs the participation of every citizen. Our school runs a wildlife awareness club that I (7) ______ (join) last year. We organise talks, film screenings and poster campaigns to educate our schoolmates (8) ______ the importance of biodiversity. Last semester, we raised money for a wildlife reserve by (9) ______ (sell) handmade badges. It felt great to know that our small contributions could help protect animals (10) ______ are in danger.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：extinction**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `of` 后接名词，`extinct` 变为 `extinction`。

2. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：比较级。`1,000 times faster than` 表示"快 1000 倍"。

3. **答案：animals**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most iconic animals`。

4. **答案：made**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`the great efforts made by...`，被动关系。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from...to...` 表示"从……到……"。

6. **答案：numbers**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`The Yangtze finless porpoise` 为第三人称单数，`number` 作动词用。

7. **答案：joined**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`last year` 提示过去时间。

8. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。`educate sb about sth` 表示"教育某人关于某事"。

9. **答案：selling**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词，表示方式。

10. **答案：that / which**
    - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，先行词为 `animals`，在从句中作主语。
