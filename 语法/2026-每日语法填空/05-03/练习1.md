# 05-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：传统手工艺 (Traditional Handicrafts)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Traditional handicrafts carry a nation’s memory in wood, clay, and thread. In our town, an old paper-cutting artist 1. ________ (teach) free classes every Saturday, and her works 2. ________ (sell) in the museum shop next door.

I joined the class last winter. At first, my scissors felt heavy and my lines were uneven, 3. ________ the teacher smiled and said practice would help. She showed us how a single piece of red paper could be folded and cut 4. ________ a lively image of a fish or a flower.

“Paper-cutting is 5. ________ (much) than decoration,” she explained. “It tells stories about festivals and family wishes.” While we worked, soft music played in the background, 6. ________ made the room feel peaceful.

By spring, I 7. ________ (finish) three small projects and gave one to my grandmother. She hung it on the window, where the morning light made the patterns shine. That moment, I understood why these skills should be passed down — they connect us 8. ________ the past and warm the present.

Young people are now learning crafts online, 9. ________ short videos make difficult steps easier to follow. I hope more classmates will try something handmade, 10. ________ our culture can stay alive in busy modern life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **teaches**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时，主谓一致，有提示词）。描述经常性动作，主语 an old paper-cutting artist 为第三人称单数，teach → teaches。

2. **are sold**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般现在时的被动语态，有提示词）。works 与 sell 为被动关系，“被售卖”，复数主语用 are sold。

3. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后为转折关系：起初不顺，但老师鼓励。

4. **into**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。be cut into 表示“被剪成……”，与 a lively image 搭配。

5. **more**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（有提示词）。后有 than，much 在此作名词用法“重要得多”需用 more than，构成 more than decoration。

6. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句话，在从句中作主语。

7. **had finished**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时，有提示词）。By spring 表示到春天为止，相对于过去 joined 的时间点之前已完成，用 had finished。

8. **with**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。connect us with the past 为常见搭配，表示“把我们与过去联系起来”。

9. **because**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。引导原因状语从句，说明短视频如何推动年轻人学习手工艺。

10. **so**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。表示结果：希望大家尝试手工，因此传统文化能在忙碌的现代生活中延续。
