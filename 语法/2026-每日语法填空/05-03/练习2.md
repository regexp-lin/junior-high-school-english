# 05-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：早起的好处 (Benefits of Waking Up Early)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

For a long time I stayed up late playing games, and every morning felt like 1. ________ fight against the alarm clock. Last term, my coach advised me to sleep earlier and rise 2. ________ (early) than usual. I doubted it would work, 3. ________ I decided to try for two weeks.

The first mornings were hard, yet I noticed small changes soon. I had time to eat 4. ________ warm breakfast instead of rushing out with 5. ________ empty stomach. On the way to school, the streets were quieter, and I could review English words 6. ________ (calm).

In class I felt 7. ________ (little) sleepy and answered questions 8. ________ (active). Even my afternoon runs became easier because I was not running on nervous energy alone. My parents said I looked 9. ________ (health), and my grades improved a little.

Waking up early is not magic, but it gives you space to plan the day. If you want 10. ________ better habit, start tonight by putting your phone away one hour earlier.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。feel like a fight 表示“像一场战斗”，fight 可数，泛指用 a。

2. **earlier**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。后有 than usual，early 的比较级为 earlier。

3. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后转折：心存怀疑，但仍决定尝试。

4. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a warm breakfast 泛指“一顿热腾腾的早餐”。

5. **an**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。empty 以元音音素开头，用 an empty stomach，表示“空着肚子”。

6. **calmly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 review，用副词 calmly。

7. **less**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（有提示词）。little 的比较级为 less，less sleepy 表示“没那么困”。

8. **actively**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 answered，用副词 actively，与上文 calmer 的状态形成并列改进。

9. **healthier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（有提示词）。look 为系动词，后接形容词；暗含与过去对比，health → healthy → healthier。

10. **a**
    - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a better habit 表示“一个更好的习惯”。
