# 04-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：摄影的乐趣 (The Joy of Photography)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Photography is a wonderful hobby that allows you to capture beautiful moments in life. With a camera in hand, you can see the world from a completely different 1. ________ (angry).

I first became interested in photography two years ago. My uncle, who is a professional 2. ________ (photograph), showed me some of his works. I was amazed by how a single photo could tell 3. ________ powerful story. From that day on, I started taking photos with my phone.

At first, my photos were not very good. The colors looked dull and the composition was 4. ________ (please). But my uncle taught me some basic skills, such as how to use natural light and how to choose the right 5. ________ (angel) for a shot. 6. ________ (slow), my photos began to improve.

Now, I take photos wherever I go. I especially enjoy 7. ________ (photograph) nature — the sunrise, the clouds, flowers after rain. Last month, one of my photos 8. ________ (choose) for the school art exhibition. I felt so happy when I saw it hanging on the wall.

Photography has taught me 9. ________ (notice) the small, beautiful things in life that I used to ignore. It has also made me more patient, because sometimes you have to wait for hours just to get the 10. ________ (perfectly) shot.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **angle**
   - **考点**：词性辨析/拼写。根据语境"从完全不同的角度看世界"，此处需要 angle（角度），注意与 angry 区分，a different angle。

2. **photographer**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→名词）。a professional photographer 意为"一名专业摄影师"，photograph → photographer。

3. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。tell a story 意为"讲述一个故事"，story 为可数名词单数。

4. **unpleasing/displeasing**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）+ 前缀。was 后接形容词作表语，根据语境（照片不好）需加否定前缀，please → pleasing → unpleasing/displeasing（不令人满意的）。

5. **angle**
   - **考点**：名词拼写辨析。choose the right angle 意为"选择正确的角度"。注意提示词 angel（天使）与 angle（角度）的拼写区分。

6. **Slowly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰整个句子，slow → slowly，句首大写。

7. **photographing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。enjoy doing sth. 意为"喜欢做某事"。

8. **was chosen**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般过去时）。one of my photos 与 choose 之间是被动关系，last month 提示用一般过去时。

9. **to notice**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。teach sb. to do sth. 意为"教会某人做某事"。

10. **perfect**
    - **考点**：词性转换（副词→形容词）。修饰名词 shot，需用形容词形式 perfect。
