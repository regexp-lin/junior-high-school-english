# 04-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：尊老敬老 (Respecting the Elderly)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In Chinese culture, respecting the elderly has always been an important tradition. It is believed that old people deserve to be treated with 1. ________ (patient) and kindness because of their experience and wisdom.

My grandmother is eighty-two years old. She 2. ________ (live) through many difficult times, including wars and natural disasters. Although she didn't receive much education, she is one of the 3. ________ (wise) people I know. Her life stories always teach me something new.

Every weekend, I visit my grandparents and help them with housework. I clean the house, wash dishes and sometimes cook dinner 4. ________ them. My grandmother enjoys 5. ________ (tell) me stories about her childhood, and I enjoy listening. These moments are very 6. ________ (meaning) to both of us.

Sadly, not all elderly people are 7. ________ (good) taken care of. Some live alone and feel lonely because their children are too busy 8. ________ (visit) them. This is a problem that society needs 9. ________ (pay) more attention to.

We should remember that everyone will grow old someday. The way we treat 10. ________ elderly today reflects the values of our society. A society that respects its old people is a society that has a bright future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。介词 with 后接名词，patient → patience。

2. **has lived**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。描述从过去到现在的经历，用现在完成时。

3. **wisest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，wise → wisest。

4. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。cook dinner for them 意为"为他们做晚饭"。

5. **telling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。enjoy doing sth. 意为"喜欢做某事"。

6. **meaningful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。very + 形容词，meaning → meaningful。

7. **well**
   - **考点**：词性辨析/副词。修饰过去分词 taken care of，需用副词 well（good 的副词形式）。

8. **to visit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。too busy to do sth. 意为"太忙而无法做某事"。

9. **to pay**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。need to do sth. 意为"需要做某事"。

10. **the**
    - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。the elderly 意为"老年人（群体）"，the + 形容词表示一类人。
