# 12-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：晨练 (Morning Exercise)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Morning exercise is a common habit among Chinese people, especially the elderly. If you visit any park in China early in the morning, you will see groups of people (1) ______ (do) various forms of exercise, from tai chi and fan dancing to jogging and stretching. Many people believe that exercising in the morning is (2) ______ (good) for the body than exercising at other times of the day because the air is (3) ______ (fresh) and the mind is clearest after a night's rest. Our school requires all students to do morning exercises on the playground before classes begin. Every day at 7:20, the entire school gathers together, and we follow along to music for about fifteen minutes. The exercises include stretching, jumping and (4) ______ (simple) aerobic movements. Some students used to complain (5) ______ having to wake up early for morning exercises. My classmate Li Hua once joked that it was the most (6) ______ (pain) part of his day. However, our PE teacher explained that morning exercise helps wake up the body, improve blood (7) ______ (circulate) and boost concentration for the classes ahead. To make morning exercises more (8) ______ (enjoy), our school recently introduced a new routine set to popular music. Students voted on (9) ______ songs to use, and the chosen routine includes moves from hip-hop and street dance. Since the change, participation has increased (10) ______ (great), and even Li Hua admits that he looks forward to morning exercises now.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：doing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`see people doing sth`，感官动词后接现在分词。

2. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than exercising at other times` 提示比较级。

3. **答案：freshest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the air is freshest`，暗含一天中最新鲜。

4. **答案：simple**
   - **考点**：形容词。修饰名词 `aerobic movements`。

5. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。`complain about doing sth` 表示"抱怨做某事"。

6. **答案：painful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most painful part`，`pain` 变为形容词。

7. **答案：circulation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`blood circulation` 表示"血液循环"，名词。

8. **答案：enjoyable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more enjoyable`，`enjoy` 变为形容词。

9. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`voted on which songs to use`，`which` 引导宾语从句。

10. **答案：greatly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `increased` 需要副词。
