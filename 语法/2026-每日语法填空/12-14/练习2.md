# 12-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国象棋 (Chinese Chess)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese chess, known as "xiangqi," is one of the most popular board games in China. It has been played (1) ______ more than a thousand years. The game is played on a board with intersecting lines, and each player controls sixteen pieces (2) ______ (include) a general, advisors, elephants, horses, chariots, cannons and soldiers. The objective is to capture the (3) ______ (oppose) player's general. My grandfather taught me how to play Chinese chess when I was eight years old. He is an excellent player who has won several (4) ______ (compete) at the community level. He always says that chess is not just a game — it is a training ground for the mind. Playing chess requires (5) ______ (patient), strategic thinking and the ability (6) ______ (predict) your opponent's moves. Every Sunday afternoon, my grandfather and I sit in the living room and play a few rounds. He usually wins, but I have been getting (7) ______ (close) to beating him recently. Last month, I almost won a game. I had captured most of his pieces and was about to checkmate his general. But he made a (8) ______ (brilliance) move that I had not anticipated, and he turned the entire game around. I was frustrated at first, (9) ______ he reminded me that learning from defeat is more important than winning. He told me that the best chess players are those (10) ______ never stop analysing their mistakes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for more than a thousand years` 表示持续时间。

2. **答案：including**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`including` 作介词用，表示"包括"。

3. **答案：opposing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`the opposing player's general` 表示"对方的将帅"。

4. **答案：competitions**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several competitions`。

5. **答案：patience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`requires patience`，动词后接名词。

6. **答案：to predict**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the ability to do sth` 表示"做某事的能力"。

7. **答案：closer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`getting closer to beating him`。

8. **答案：brilliant**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `move` 需要形容词。

9. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。前后转折。

10. **答案：who / that**
    - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，先行词为 `those`（人）。
