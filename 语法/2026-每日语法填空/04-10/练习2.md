# 04-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：未来的生活 (Life in the Future)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

What will life be like in the future? Many people have tried 1. ________ (answer) this question. While nobody knows for sure, scientists believe that technology will make our lives very 2. ________ (differ) from today.

In the future, self-driving cars may fill the streets. People won't need 3. ________ (drive) anymore — they can just sit back and enjoy the ride. Traffic accidents, which are 4. ________ (most) caused by human error, will be greatly reduced.

Our homes will also change. Smart houses will be able to adjust the temperature, lighting and even music according 5. ________ our mood. Robots will do most of the housework, such as cooking and 6. ________ (clean). People will have more free time to spend 7. ________ their families and hobbies.

Medicine will make great progress too. Scientists are 8. ________ (work) on new treatments that could cure diseases which are incurable today. People may live much 9. ________ (long) than they do now.

Of course, the future also brings challenges. We will need to find ways to feed a 10. ________ (grow) population and deal with environmental problems. But if we use technology wisely, the future can be bright and full of hope.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **to answer**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。try to do sth. 意为"尝试做某事"。

2. **different**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词/名词→形容词）。very + 形容词，differ → different。

3. **to drive**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。need to do sth. 意为"需要做某事"。

4. **mostly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰 caused by（过去分词短语），需用副词形式 mostly。

5. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。according to 意为"根据"，是固定搭配。

6. **cleaning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。such as 后接动名词，与 cooking 并列。

7. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。spend time with sb. 意为"与某人共度时光"。

8. **working**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词/现在进行时）。are working 构成现在进行时，表示"正在研究"。

9. **longer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，long → longer。

10. **growing**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词作定语）。growing population 意为"不断增长的人口"，growing 作前置定语。
