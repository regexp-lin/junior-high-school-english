# 04-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：兴趣爱好 (Hobbies)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Having a hobby is important for both children and adults. A hobby gives you something enjoyable to do in your free time and helps you relax after a 1. ________ (tire) day.

There are all kinds of hobbies. Some people enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, fishing or 2. ________ (camp). Others prefer quieter hobbies such as reading, drawing or collecting stamps. There is no right or wrong hobby — the best hobby is the one 3. ________ makes you happy.

My hobby is building model planes. I started this hobby when I was ten years old. My grandfather gave me my first model kit 4. ________ a birthday gift. At first, I couldn't even understand the 5. ________ (instruct), and it took me a whole week to finish the first plane. But I never felt bored because I 6. ________ (true) enjoyed the process.

Now I have 7. ________ (build) more than thirty model planes. They are displayed on the shelves in my room. Some of my friends think it is a waste 8. ________ time, but I disagree. Building models has taught me to be patient and careful. It has also improved my ability to solve 9. ________ (problem) because I often need to figure out how the parts fit together.

I believe that everyone 10. ________ (need) a hobby. It brings joy to our lives and helps us discover new sides of ourselves.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **tiring**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。修饰名词 day（事物），用 -ing 形式，tiring 意为"令人疲惫的"。

2. **camping**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。or 连接并列成分，与 hiking、fishing 并列，用动名词形式。

3. **that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 the one，在从句中作主语。

4. **as**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。as a birthday gift 意为"作为生日礼物"。

5. **instructions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。the 后接名词，instruct → instruction → instructions（说明书通常用复数）。

6. **truly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 enjoyed，需用副词形式 truly。

7. **built**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。have + 过去分词，build → built。

8. **of**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。a waste of time 意为"浪费时间"。

9. **problems**
   - **考点**：名词复数。solve problems 意为"解决问题"，problem 用复数。

10. **needs**
    - **考点**：主谓一致（一般现在时）。everyone 为第三人称单数，动词加 -s。
