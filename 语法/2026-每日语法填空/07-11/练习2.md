# 07-11 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：海边旅行 (A Trip to the Beach)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last July, my family drove to Sanya for a five-day holiday. It was the first time I (1) ______ (see) the sea in real life. When I stood on the beach and looked at the endless blue water, I could hardly believe my (2) ______ (eye). The sand was soft and warm (3) ______ my feet. My sister and I ran into the waves, (4) ______ (scream) with joy. We spent the whole morning (5) ______ (build) a sandcastle that was almost as tall as my brother. In the afternoon, my father took us snorkelling. Under the water, I saw colourful fish (6) ______ (swim) among the coral. It was one of the most (7) ______ (amaze) things I had ever experienced. That evening, we sat on the beach and watched the sunset. The sky turned orange and pink, and a gentle breeze blew (8) ______ the ocean. My mother said it was the (9) ______ (peaceful) moment she had had in months. I took many photos during the trip, (10) ______ my favourite one is still the picture of our family smiling under the coconut trees.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：had seen**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`It was the first time + 主语 + had done` 为固定句型，表示"那是某人第一次做某事"。

2. **答案：eyes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。人有两只眼睛，`eye` 用复数 `eyes`。

3. **答案：under**
   - **考点**：介词。`under my feet` 表示"在我的脚下"。

4. **答案：screaming**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。`screaming with joy` 表示跑进海浪时的伴随动作——尖叫。

5. **答案：building**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

6. **答案：swimming**
   - **考点**：现在分词作宾语补足语。`see sb/sth doing` 表示"看见……正在做"，强调看到正在进行的动作。

7. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 最高级。`the most amazing` 为最高级形式，`amaze` 变为 `amazing`（令人惊叹的），修饰 `things`（物）。

8. **答案：from / across**
   - **考点**：介词。`blow from/across the ocean` 表示"从海洋吹来"或"越过海洋吹来"。

9. **答案：most peaceful**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词`，`peaceful` 为多音节形容词，最高级为 `most peaceful`。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：转折连词。"拍了很多照片"与"最喜欢的还是那张全家福"之间为转折关系。
