# 07-11 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园科技节 (School Science Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our school held its annual science festival last week. Students from every grade (1) ______ (invite) to show their science projects. My partner and I built a small robot that could follow a line on the floor. We spent over a month (2) ______ (design) and testing it. On the day of the festival, the hall was filled (3) ______ amazing inventions. One team created a device (4) ______ turned food waste into fertiliser. Another group made a model bridge strong enough (5) ______ (hold) fifty kilograms. The judges, (6) ______ were science teachers from other schools, examined each project (7) ______ (careful). When they announced the results, our robot won second prize! We were (8) ______ (thrill) because we had worked so hard. The (9) ______ (win) team built a solar-powered car — it was truly impressive. Our head teacher said the festival helped students develop (10) ______ (create) thinking and problem-solving skills. I can't wait for next year's festival.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：were invited**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。学生"被邀请"展示项目，`last week` 表示过去，主语 `Students` 为复数。

2. **答案：designing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be filled with` 为固定搭配，表示"充满"。

4. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a device`（物），在定语从句中作主语，用 `that` 或 `which`。

5. **答案：to hold**
   - **考点**：不定式。`enough + to do sth` 表示"足够……可以做某事"，不定式作结果状语。

6. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `The judges`（人），引导非限制性定语从句，作主语，用 `who`。

7. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `examined` 需要副词，`careful` 变为 `carefully`。

8. **答案：thrilled**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be thrilled` 表示"感到兴奋的"，过去分词作形容词描述人的感受。

9. **答案：winning**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`the winning team` 表示"获胜的队伍"，现在分词修饰名词。

10. **答案：creative**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `thinking` 需要形容词，`create` 变为 `creative`（创造性的）。
