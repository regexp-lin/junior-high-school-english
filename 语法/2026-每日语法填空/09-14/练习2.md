# 09-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校辩论赛 (School Debate Competition)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, our school held its annual debate competition. The topic was "Should students be allowed (1) ______ (use) mobile phones at school?" I was chosen to be on the team that argued against it. To prepare, our team spent two weeks (2) ______ (research) and practising. We read articles, collected data and discussed our arguments (3) ______ each other. On the day of the debate, I was the (4) ______ (three) speaker on our team. When it was my turn, my heart was beating so (5) ______ (quick) that I thought everyone could hear it. But once I started speaking, my nervousness faded away. I presented our argument (6) ______ (clear) and supported it with strong evidence. The opposing team was also very well-prepared. Their first speaker made some (7) ______ (power) points about how phones can be useful learning tools. It was hard to argue (8) ______ that. In the end, the judges gave us a (9) ______ (narrow) victory. They said both teams performed (10) ______ (excellence), but our evidence was slightly stronger. The debate taught me the importance of critical thinking and the ability to express ideas under pressure.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be allowed to do sth` 表示"被允许做某事"。

2. **答案：researching**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth`。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`discuss...with each other` 表示"互相讨论"。

4. **答案：third**
   - **考点**：序数词。`the third speaker`，`three` 变为序数词 `third`。

5. **答案：quickly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `beating` 需要副词，`quick` 变为 `quickly`。

6. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `presented` 需要副词，`clear` 变为 `clearly`。

7. **答案：powerful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `points` 需要形容词，`power` 变为 `powerful`。

8. **答案：against**
   - **考点**：介词。`argue against` 表示"反驳、争辩反对"。

9. **答案：narrow**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a narrow victory`，修饰名词用形容词，`narrow` 本身即为形容词。

10. **答案：excellently**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `performed` 需要副词，`excellence` 变为 `excellently`。
