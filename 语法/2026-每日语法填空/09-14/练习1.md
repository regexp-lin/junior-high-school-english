# 09-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中秋节 (Mid-Autumn Festival)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Mid-Autumn Festival, which (1) ______ (fall) on the 15th day of the eighth lunar month, is one of the most important traditional festivals in China. On this day, family members gather together to enjoy mooncakes and admire the full moon. The festival (2) ______ (celebrate) for over a thousand years. According to legend, a beautiful woman (3) ______ (name) Chang'e flew to the moon after drinking a magic potion. Her husband, Hou Yi, missed her so much (4) ______ he made mooncakes and offered them to the moon every year, hoping she could see his love. This is said to be the origin of (5) ______ (eat) mooncakes. In my family, we always have a big dinner on Mid-Autumn night. After dinner, we sit in the courtyard, (6) ______ (enjoy) mooncakes and tea under the moonlight. My grandmother tells us stories about Chang'e and the Jade Rabbit while my (7) ______ (young) cousins play with lanterns. Last year, it rained on Mid-Autumn night, so we couldn't see the moon. We were a little (8) ______ (disappoint), but we still had a wonderful time together. The festival reminds (9) ______ (we) that no matter how busy life gets, spending time with family is the thing that (10) ______ (matter) most.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：falls**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。描述每年固定发生的事用一般现在时，`which` 指代 `The Mid-Autumn Festival`（单数）。

2. **答案：has been celebrated**
   - **考点**：时态与被动。`for over a thousand years` 提示用现在完成时，节日"被庆祝"用被动。

3. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a woman named Chang'e`，过去分词作后置定语。

4. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so much that...` 表示"如此……以至于"。

5. **答案：eating**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词 `eating`。

6. **答案：enjoying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`sit in the courtyard, enjoying...`，现在分词作伴随状语。

7. **答案：younger**
   - **考点**：比较级。`my younger cousins` 表示"我的表弟表妹们"（较年幼的）。

8. **答案：disappointed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`were disappointed`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

9. **答案：us**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`reminds us`，动词后接宾格。

10. **答案：matters**
    - **考点**：主谓一致。`the thing that matters most`，先行词 `the thing` 为单数，从句谓语用 `matters`。
