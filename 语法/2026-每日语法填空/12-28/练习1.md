# 12-28 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：冬令营 (Winter Camp)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Winter camps offer students a chance to learn new things and make friends during the holiday. Last winter, I attended a five-day English camp organised by a language school. Students from different cities (1) ______ (gather) at a mountain resort in Zhejiang Province. I was excited but also a little nervous because I did not know anyone (2) ______. On the first day, we were divided into teams and given team names. My team was called "The Snow Leopards." Our team leader was a university student named David, (3) ______ came from the United States. He spoke excellent Chinese but insisted that we communicate only (4) ______ English during camp activities. Each day was packed with activities. In the morning, we had English classes that focused on (5) ______ (speak) and listening skills. The classes were very different from those at school — instead of (6) ______ (sit) at desks and doing exercises, we played games, acted out scenes and had debates. In the afternoon, we did outdoor activities such as hiking, orienteering and team-building challenges. My favourite activity was the campfire talent show on the (7) ______ (four) night. Each team had to perform a short play in English. Our team performed a comedy about a group of explorers (8) ______ (lose) in a forest, and we won the "Funniest Performance" award. By the end of the camp, I had made several good friends and felt much more (9) ______ (confidence) speaking English. The experience taught me that the best way to learn a language is to use it in real (10) ______ (situate).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：gathered**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的事件。

2. **答案：there**
   - **考点**：副词。`did not know anyone there` 表示"不认识那里的任何人"。

3. **答案：who**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，先行词为人。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`communicate in English` 表示"用英语交流"。

5. **答案：speaking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`focused on speaking`，介词后接动名词。

6. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`instead of sitting`，介词后接动名词。

7. **答案：fourth**
   - **考点**：序数词。`the fourth night`。

8. **答案：lost**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`explorers lost in a forest`。

9. **答案：confident**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt more confident`，系动词后接形容词。

10. **答案：situations**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`real situations`，形容词后接名词复数。
