# 12-28 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：智慧农业 (Smart Farming)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Smart farming, also known as precision agriculture, uses modern technology to make farming more efficient and (1) ______ (sustain). Traditional farming relies heavily on manual labour and experience, but smart farming uses sensors, drones and artificial intelligence to monitor crops and manage resources more (2) ______ (accurate). For example, soil sensors can measure moisture levels and tell farmers exactly when and how much (3) ______ (water) their crops. Drones equipped (4) ______ cameras can fly over large fields and identify areas where plants are unhealthy. This allows farmers to take action before problems become serious. In China, smart farming (5) ______ (adopt) rapidly in recent years. The government has encouraged farmers to use technology by providing training and financial support. In some areas, fully automated greenhouses can grow vegetables (6) ______ any human intervention. Temperature, humidity and lighting are all controlled by computers, (7) ______ (result) in higher yields and less waste. My uncle is a farmer in Shandong Province. Two years ago, he installed a smart irrigation system in his apple orchard. The system uses weather data and soil information (8) ______ (determine) the exact amount of water each tree needs. Since (9) ______ (install) the system, his water usage has decreased by 40%, while his apple production has increased by 20%. He says that smart farming has made his life much (10) ______ (easy) and his farm more profitable.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：sustainable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `efficient` 并列，需要形容词。

2. **答案：accurately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `manage` 需要副词。

3. **答案：to water**
   - **考点**：不定式。`tell farmers when and how much to water`。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`equipped with cameras` 表示"配备了摄像头"。

5. **答案：has been adopted**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。`in recent years` 提示，被动关系。

6. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`without any human intervention` 表示"无需人工干预"。

7. **答案：resulting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表结果的伴随状语。

8. **答案：to determine**
   - **考点**：不定式。表目的。

9. **答案：installing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`Since installing` = `Since he installed`。

10. **答案：easier**
    - **考点**：比较级。`much easier`，`much` 修饰比较级。
