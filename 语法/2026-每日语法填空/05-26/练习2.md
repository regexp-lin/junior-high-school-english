# 05-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：课间十分钟 (The Ten-Minute Break)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The ten-minute break between classes sounds short, 1. ________ it means a lot to students. We stretch our legs, drink water, and chat 2. ________ friends about funny moments in class.

Our head teacher encourages us to look out of the window and rest 3. ________ (we) eyes. She says a relaxed mind works 4. ________ (well) in the next lesson. Some classmates play badminton outside, 5. ________ others finish homework quickly in the classroom.

Yesterday, the bell rang 6. ________ (sudden), and everyone rushed to the playground. I tripped over a bag 7. ________ my deskmate caught my arm in time. I thanked him and realized 8. ________ small act of care can warm a busy day.

When the music for the next class started, we walked back quietly. Those ten minutes 9. ________ (be) not wasted; they helped us return to study with fresh energy.

I believe schools should protect break time 10. ________ it truly supports healthy learning.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“听起来很短”与“对学生意义重大”为转折。

2. **with**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。chat with friends 与朋友聊天。

3. **our**
   - **考点**：代词（有提示词 we → 形容词性物主代词）。修饰 eyes，用 our。

4. **better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级（有提示词）。与放松前暗含对比，“下一节课更有效率”，用 well 的比较级 better。

5. **while**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。some... while others... 表对比，“一些……而另一些……”。

6. **suddenly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 rang，用 suddenly。

7. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。绊倒与同桌及时拉住形成转折。

8. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a small act of care 表示“一个小小的关心举动”，可数名词单数需不定冠词。

9. **were**
   - **考点**：动词时态与主谓一致（有提示词）。主语 Those ten minutes 为复数，叙述昨天，用 were。

10. **because / as / since**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。保护课间时间的原因是它确实有助于健康学习，表原因用 because（或 as/since）。
