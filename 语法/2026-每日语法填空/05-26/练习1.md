# 05-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：航天精神 (The Spirit of Space Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China's space achievements have inspired millions of teenagers. The Long March rockets look powerful, 1. ________ they also carry the dreams of teamwork and courage. Many students say they want to become scientists 2. ________ can serve the country in the future.

Last month, our school invited an engineer 3. ________ (give) a talk about satellite design. He explained that failure is not the end if people keep learning from mistakes. His team 4. ________ (work) on the same program for years before the successful launch. His words made us think 5. ________ (deep) about perseverance.

Space exploration is expensive and difficult, 6. ________ it pushes human knowledge far forward. We should respect every worker behind the launch pad and take pride 7. ________ China's steady progress.

As young learners, we may not enter space soon, 8. ________ we can study harder every day. That is 9. ________ (we) own way to honor the spirit of exploration.

The journey to the stars begins 10. ________ small steps in the classroom.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **and**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。承接“火箭看起来很强大”，并补充“也承载着团队与勇气的梦想”，表并列或递进，用 and。

2. **who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。定语从句修饰 scientists，从句缺少主语且指人，用 who 或 that。

3. **to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（有提示词）。invite sb. to do sth.，用不定式 to give。

4. **had worked**
   - **考点**：动词时态（过去完成时，有提示词）。for years before the successful launch 表示“在成功发射之前已持续多年”，强调“过去的过去”，用 had worked。

5. **deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 think，用 deeply。

6. **but / yet**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。“耗资且艰难”与“推动人类认知向前”形成转折，用 but 或 yet。

7. **in**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。take pride in 固定搭配。

8. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。短期内无法进入太空与“仍可通过努力学习致敬航天精神”形成转折。

9. **our**
   - **考点**：代词（有提示词 we → 形容词性物主代词）。one's own way 结构，填 our。

10. **with**
    - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。begin with 表示“以……开始”。
