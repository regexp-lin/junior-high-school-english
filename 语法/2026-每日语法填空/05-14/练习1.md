# 05-14 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：人工智能 (Artificial Intelligence)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is changing the way we live and work. Many people believe that AI will become even 1. ________ (important) in the next few decades than it is today.

AI tools can already translate languages, recognize faces, and answer questions in seconds. Some hospitals use AI 2. ________ (help) doctors find diseases earlier. However, there are also worries. If machines can do many jobs 3. ________ (fast) than humans, what will happen to workers?

Experts say we should not fear progress 4. ________ we learn to use AI wisely. Schools are now teaching students how to search for information and think critically, 5. ________ they will not depend on AI for everything. In my opinion, AI is like a powerful tool — it can be dangerous 6. ________ it is used without care.

Last week, our science teacher asked us 7. ________ (write) a short report on AI in daily life. I interviewed my uncle, 8. ________ works as a programmer. He told me that good programs are always written by creative people, no matter how smart the computer seems.

The future of AI depends on human choices. As long as we put safety and kindness first, technology may bring us 9. ________ (many) opportunities than problems. I hope that I 10. ________ (study) AI more deeply when I go to university.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **more important**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。句中有 than，且 even 修饰比较级，important 为多音节形容词，比较级用 more important。

2. **to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。use sth. to do sth.，表示"用 AI 来帮助医生"，填 to help。

3. **faster**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。than humans 提示比较级，修饰动词 do，用副词 fast 的比较级 faster。

4. **if**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。引导条件状语从句，表示"如果我们学会明智地使用 AI，就不必害怕技术进步"。

5. **so**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后为因果关系：学校教学生检索与批判性思维，以便他们不会事事依赖 AI，用 so 引导结果。

6. **if**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。引导条件状语从句，表示"如果使用时不谨慎，就可能带来危险"。

7. **to write**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。ask sb. to do sth. 固定结构，填 to write。

8. **who**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句修饰 my uncle，指人在从句中作主语，用 who。

9. **more**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than problems 提示比较级，many 的比较级为 more，more opportunities。

10. **will study**
    - **考点**：动词时态（一般将来时）。hope 后的宾语从句中，when I go to university 指将来，用 will study 表示将来会深入学习 AI。
