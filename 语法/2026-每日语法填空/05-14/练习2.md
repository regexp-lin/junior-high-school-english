# 05-14 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：给老师的一封信 (A Letter to My Teacher)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Dear Ms. Wang,

I am writing to thank you for what you 1. ________ (do) for me this term.

When I first entered your class, I was shy and afraid 2. ________ (speak) English in public. My pronunciation was poor, and I often stayed silent 3. ________ group discussions. You noticed my problem and never pushed me too hard. Instead, you encouraged me to read short passages aloud after class and praised me 4. ________ (warm) whenever I made progress.

Gradually, I became 5. ________ (confident) than before. Last month, I even volunteered 6. ________ (give) a short speech at the English corner. Though my voice shook at the beginning, I completed it — 7. ________ was a big step for me.

You have taught me not only language skills 8. ________ also how to face difficulties with a positive attitude. I will always remember your words: "Mistakes are proof 9. ________ you are trying."

Thank you again for your patience and care. I wish you good health and 10. ________ (happy) every day!

Yours sincerely,  
Li Hua

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **have done**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。thank you for what you have done 强调"本学期以来已经为我做的事"，对现在有影响，用现在完成时。

2. **to speak**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。be afraid to do sth. 害怕做某事，填 to speak。

3. **during**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。during group discussions 表示"在小组讨论期间"，说明保持沉默的时间背景。

4. **warmly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 praised，用副词 warmly。

5. **more confident**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than before 提示比较级，confident 为多音节词，用 more confident。

6. **to give**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。volunteer to do sth. 自愿做某事，填 to give。

7. **It**
   - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。指代上文"我完成了演讲"这件事，用 It 作主语，It was a big step for me 表示"这对我来说是很大一步"。

8. **but**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。not only...but also... 不但……而且……。

9. **that**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。同位语从句，proof that you are trying，that 引导从句说明 proof 的内容。

10. **happiness**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。and 连接与 good health 并列的名词，wish you happiness，用名词 happiness。
