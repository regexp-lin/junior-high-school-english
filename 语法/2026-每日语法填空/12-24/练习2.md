# 12-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：平安夜 (Christmas Eve)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Christmas Eve, the night before Christmas Day, is a time of warmth, anticipation and togetherness in many cultures. In Western countries, families often spend the evening (1) ______ (prepare) for the next day's celebrations. Children leave out milk and cookies for Santa Claus, and some families attend a midnight church service. My pen pal, Sarah, lives in London and always tells me about her family's Christmas Eve traditions. Her family gathers in the living room, drinks hot chocolate and watches classic Christmas films together. She says the atmosphere is so (2) ______ (peace) that she wishes the night could last forever. In China, Christmas Eve has developed its own unique traditions, (3) ______ (particular) among young people. One popular custom is giving friends "ping'an guo" — apples wrapped in (4) ______ (colour) paper. This tradition comes from the fact that the Chinese word for Christmas Eve, "ping'an ye," contains the word "ping'an," meaning "peace and safety," which sounds similar (5) ______ the word for "apple." Last Christmas Eve, my classmates and I exchanged apples in class. Our teacher allowed us (6) ______ (have) a small party during the last period. We decorated the classroom with paper snowflakes and played games. One student (7) ______ (dress) up as Santa Claus and handed out small gifts that we had prepared for each other. It was one of the (8) ______ (happy) evenings I have ever spent at school. Some people argue that Chinese students should not celebrate Western holidays. (9) ______, I think there is nothing wrong with enjoying a festival that brings people closer together, as long as we also value and preserve our own cultural (10) ______ (tradition).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth`。

2. **答案：peaceful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`so peaceful`，系动词后接形容词。

3. **答案：particularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰介词短语需要副词。

4. **答案：colourful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `paper` 需要形容词。

5. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`similar to` 表示"与……相似"。

6. **答案：to have**
   - **考点**：不定式。`allow sb to do sth`。

7. **答案：dressed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`dressed up as` 表示"装扮成"。

8. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the happiest evenings`。

9. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。前后转折。

10. **答案：traditions**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`cultural traditions`。
