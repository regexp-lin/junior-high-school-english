# 12-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：跳蚤市场 (Flea Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Flea markets are places where people sell second-hand goods at low prices. They are popular in many countries and have recently become trendy in Chinese cities as well. Our school holds a flea market at the end of each semester, (1) ______ students can sell items they no longer need — old textbooks, clothes, toys and sports equipment. The money raised is donated to charity. I (2) ______ (take) part in the school flea market three times so far. Each time, it has been a fun and (3) ______ (reward) experience. Last semester, I sold some story books that I had (4) ______ (already finish) reading. I set up a small table in the playground and priced each book between two and five yuan. To my surprise, all my books (5) ______ (sell) out within an hour. The most popular one was a detective novel that three students wanted to buy at the same time! I also enjoy browsing other students' stalls. You never know what hidden treasures you might find. Last time, I discovered a barely used English dictionary (6) ______ only three yuan. It was such a good deal that I bought it without (7) ______ (hesitate). Flea markets teach students the value of recycling and (8) ______ (share). Instead of throwing things away, we give them a second life. I think this is a much (9) ______ (smart) way to deal with items we no longer use. The best part is knowing that the money goes to help people (10) ______ need it most.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，先行词为 `flea market`（地点）。

2. **答案：have taken**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`so far` 提示。

3. **答案：rewarding**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `fun` 并列，修饰事物用 `-ing`。

4. **答案：already finished**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`had already finished reading`。

5. **答案：were sold**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`all my books` 与 `sell` 之间是被动关系。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for only three yuan` 表示价格。

7. **答案：hesitation / hesitating**
   - **考点**：词性转换/动名词。介词 `without` 后接名词或动名词。

8. **答案：sharing**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `recycling` 并列。

9. **答案：smarter**
   - **考点**：比较级。`a much smarter way`，`much` 修饰比较级。

10. **答案：who / that**
    - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，先行词为 `people`。
