# 07-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：一次露营经历 (A Camping Experience)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, I went camping with my classmates in a mountain area. It was the (1) ______ (one) time for most of us to sleep in tents. After a three-hour bus ride, we arrived at a flat field (2) ______ a river. Our teacher showed us how (3) ______ (put) up tents, and we worked in pairs. My partner and I spent almost an hour (4) ______ (struggle) with the poles before our tent finally stood up. In the evening, we gathered around a campfire and (5) ______ (sing) songs. The stars above us looked much (6) ______ (bright) than those we see in the city. Someone told a ghost story, and my friend Wang Lei was so scared (7) ______ he refused to go to the toilet alone. At night, the temperature dropped (8) ______ (sharp), and I wished I had brought a thicker sleeping bag. Despite the cold, I slept (9) ______ (surprising) well because I was too tired. The next morning, we were woken up by bird songs. It was (10) ______ unforgettable experience that brought us closer together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：first**
   - **考点**：序数词。`the first time` 表示"第一次"，`one` 的序数词形式为 `first`。

2. **答案：beside / near / by**
   - **考点**：介词。`beside/near/by a river` 表示"在河边"，介词表示位置关系。

3. **答案：to put**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"如何做某事"。

4. **答案：struggling**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

5. **答案：sang**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与 `gathered` 并列，叙述过去的动作，`sing` 的过去式为 `sang`。

6. **答案：brighter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`bright` 的比较级为 `brighter`。

7. **答案：that**
   - **考点**：连词。`so...that...` 表示"如此……以至于……"。

8. **答案：sharply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `dropped` 需要副词，`sharp` 变为 `sharply`（急剧地）。

9. **答案：surprisingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰副词 `well` 需要副词，`surprising` 变为 `surprisingly`（出人意料地）。

10. **答案：an**
    - **考点**：冠词。`an unforgettable experience` 中 `unforgettable` 以元音音素 /ʌ/ 开头，用 `an`。
