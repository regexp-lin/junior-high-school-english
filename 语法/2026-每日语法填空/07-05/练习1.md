# 07-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：手机的利与弊 (Pros and Cons of Mobile Phones)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Mobile phones have changed our lives in many ways. Today, nearly every family (1) ______ (own) at least one smartphone. We use them to search for (2) ______ (inform), take photos, and keep in touch with friends. Some educational apps make it (3) ______ (easy) for students to review lessons at home. However, spending too much time on phones can cause problems. Many teenagers are so (4) ______ (addict) to short videos that they forget to do their homework. Doctors say that (5) ______ (stare) at a small screen for hours is bad for our eyes. Some students even use their phones (6) ______ class, which seriously affects their studies. Last term, my school introduced a rule: all phones must (7) ______ (hand) in to teachers before the first lesson. At first, some students complained (8) ______ the rule, but soon they found they could concentrate much (9) ______ (well) in class. I think the key is to control (10) ______ (we) and use phones wisely rather than let phones control us.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：owns**
   - **考点**：主谓一致 + 一般现在时。`every family` 为第三人称单数，描述一般性事实，动词加 `-s`。

2. **答案：information**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`search for` 后接名词，`inform` 变为 `information`（信息），不可数名词。

3. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`make it easier for sb to do sth` 表示"使某人做某事更容易"，隐含与"不用 app"的对比。

4. **答案：addicted**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be/become addicted to` 为固定搭配，表示"对……上瘾"；`so addicted...that...` 构成结果状语从句。

5. **答案：staring**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。动名词短语 `staring at a small screen for hours` 在句中作主语。

6. **答案：in / during**
   - **考点**：介词。`in class` 或 `during class` 表示"在课堂上"。

7. **答案：be handed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`must` 为情态动词，后接动词原形；手机"被上交"，用被动形式 `be handed`。

8. **答案：about**
   - **考点**：介词。`complain about` 为固定搭配，表示"抱怨……"。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。修饰动词 `concentrate`，`well` 的比较级为 `better`。

10. **答案：ourselves**
    - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 `we`，宾语指向同一对象，用 `ourselves`。
