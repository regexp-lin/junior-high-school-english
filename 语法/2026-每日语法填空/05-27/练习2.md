# 05-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：英语戏剧表演 (English Drama Performance)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our grade held an English drama performance last Friday. The hall was full, 1. ________ the lights went down and the curtain rose. I played a doctor in a story about kindness, 2. ________ made me both nervous and excited.

We practised 3. ________ three weeks before the show. Our English teacher helped us pronounce difficult lines 4. ________ (correct) and taught us how to use gestures on stage. She said acting is not only about memory, 5. ________ also about feeling.

During the performance, I forgot one sentence for a second. 6. ________ (fortunate), my partner spoke a little 7. ________ (slow), which gave me time to recover. The audience clapped warmly when the play ended.

After the show, parents told us they were proud 8. ________ our teamwork. This experience improved 9. ________ (I) confidence in speaking English.

I hope there will be 10. ________ chance next year to try a comedy.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **and**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。大厅坐满、灯光变暗、幕布升起为顺承并列，用 and。

2. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整件事，用 which。

3. **for**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。for three weeks 表示动作持续的时间段。

4. **correctly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 pronounce，用 correctly。

5. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。not only... but also... 固定结构。

6. **Fortunately**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰整个句子，用副词 Fortunately，句首大写。

7. **more slowly / slower**
   - **考点**：副词比较级或口语用法（有提示词）。与平时相比“说得稍慢一点”，slowly 的比较级为 more slowly；口语中也可用 slower 修饰动词 spoke。

8. **of**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。be proud of 固定搭配。

9. **my**
   - **考点**：代词（有提示词 I → 形容词性物主代词）。修饰 confidence，用 my。

10. **another / a**
    - **考点**：限定词（无提示词填空）。another chance 表示“再一次机会”；a chance 亦可，another 更贴合“明年再来一次”。
