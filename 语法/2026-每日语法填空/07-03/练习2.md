# 07-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：动物园之旅 (A Trip to the Zoo)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, my family went to the city zoo. It was a sunny day, perfect for (1) ______ (walk) outdoors. We arrived early in the morning and the zoo was already full (2) ______ visitors. The first animals we saw were the giant pandas. Two of (3) ______ (they) were sitting lazily, eating bamboo. They looked so cute that everyone stopped to take (4) ______ (photo). Next, we visited the monkey area. The monkeys were the (5) ______ (active) animals in the zoo — they jumped from tree to tree and played with each other (6) ______ (happy). My little brother was frightened by a loud roar from the lion house nearby. I told him not (7) ______ (worry) because the animals were behind strong fences. In the afternoon, we watched a dolphin show. The dolphins could jump very (8) ______ (height) out of the water and even "shake hands" with the trainers. Before leaving, we stopped at the gift shop, (9) ______ my mother bought a panda toy for my brother. On the way home, I thought about how important it is to protect wild animals (10) ______ that future generations can also enjoy seeing them.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：walking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`for` 为介词，后接动名词 `walking`。

2. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`be full of` 为固定搭配，表示"充满"。

3. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`of` 为介词，后接代词宾格；`they` 的宾格为 `them`。

4. **答案：photos**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`take photos` 为固定搭配，表示"拍照"，用复数形式。

5. **答案：most active**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词`，`active` 为多音节形容词，最高级为 `most active`。

6. **答案：happily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `played` 需要副词，`happy` 变为 `happily`。

7. **答案：to worry**
   - **考点**：不定式。`tell sb not to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"告诉某人不要做某事"，否定形式在不定式前加 `not`。

8. **答案：high**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `jump` 需要副词，`height`（名词）变为副词 `high`。注意 `highly` 多表示"高度地（抽象意义）"，此处表示物理高度用 `high`。

9. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `the gift shop`（地点），在非限制性定语从句中作地点状语，用 `where`。

10. **答案：so**
    - **考点**：连词。`so that` 引导目的状语从句，表示"以便……"。
