# 07-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园义卖会 (The School Charity Sale)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, our school held a charity sale on the playground. Every class set up a stall and sold things they no (1) ______ needed, such as old books, toys, and handmade crafts. The money (2) ______ (collect) would be donated to children in poor mountain areas. My class decided (3) ______ (sell) home-made cookies and lemonade. We spent the whole Thursday evening (4) ______ (prepare) everything. On the day of the sale, our stall was the (5) ______ (busy) one because the cookies smelled wonderful. A boy from Class Three came to our stall and bought ten cookies (6) ______ once. He said they were even (7) ______ (delicious) than those from the bakery! By the end of the afternoon, we (8) ______ (raise) over 800 yuan. Our head teacher told us that the money would be used to buy school supplies (9) ______ the children who needed them. I felt very proud. The charity sale taught me that giving to others can bring (10) ______ (great) happiness than receiving.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：longer**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`no longer` 表示"不再"，为固定副词短语。

2. **答案：collected**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`the money collected` 表示"被收集的钱"，过去分词表被动。

3. **答案：to sell**
   - **考点**：不定式。`decide to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"决定做某事"。

4. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

5. **答案：busiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + one` 表示"最……的那个"，`busy` 变 y 为 i 加 `-est`。

6. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。`at once` 为固定搭配，表示"一下子，立即"。

7. **答案：more delicious**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`delicious` 为多音节形容词，比较级为 `more delicious`。

8. **答案：had raised**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`by the end of the afternoon` 表示"到下午结束时为止"，`by + 过去时间` 常与过去完成时搭配。

9. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`buy sth for sb` 表示"为某人买某物"。

10. **答案：greater**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than receiving` 提示用比较级，`great` 的比较级为 `greater`。
