# 10-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：外卖文化 (Takeaway Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ordering takeaway food has become an everyday habit for many Chinese people. With food delivery apps, you can choose from (1) ______ (hundred) of restaurants and have a meal delivered to your door within 30 minutes. It is especially convenient (2) ______ people who work long hours and don't have time (3) ______ (cook). However, the rapid growth of the takeaway industry (4) ______ (bring) some environmental problems. Most takeaway meals come in plastic containers, and millions of (5) ______ (use) chopsticks are thrown away every day. A recent report showed that China's food delivery industry produces over 1.6 million tonnes of plastic waste (6) ______ (annual). To reduce this waste, some cities have (7) ______ (begin) encouraging restaurants to use biodegradable packaging instead of plastic. Several delivery apps now offer customers the option of not receiving disposable cutlery with (8) ______ (they) orders. My family has started keeping a set of reusable chopsticks at the office, so we no longer need the ones that come with our meals. Small changes like this may seem (9) ______ (significant), but when millions of people do the same, the impact can be huge. I believe we can enjoy the (10) ______ (convenient) of takeaway food while still protecting our environment.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`hundreds of` 表示"数百个"。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`convenient for people who...` 表示"对……来说方便"。

3. **答案：to cook**
   - **考点**：不定式。`don't have time to cook`，不定式作后置定语。

4. **答案：has brought**
   - **考点**：时态。到目前为止带来的问题，用现在完成时。

5. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`used chopsticks`，过去分词作形容词，表"用过的"。

6. **答案：annually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰整个句子/动词需要副词，`annual` 变为 `annually`。

7. **答案：begun**
   - **考点**：时态。`have begun`，现在完成时，`begin` 的过去分词为 `begun`。

8. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`their orders`。

9. **答案：insignificant**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加否定前缀）。根据语境 `may seem insignificant, but...`，表"看似不重要"。

10. **答案：convenience**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`the convenience of`，冠词后接名词，`convenient` 变为 `convenience`。
