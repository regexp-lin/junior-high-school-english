# 10-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市夜景 (City Night View)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, my family went to Shanghai and spent an evening (1) ______ (admire) the city's spectacular night view from the Bund. As darkness fell, the skyscrapers across the river lit up one (2) ______ one, like giant lanterns against the night sky. The Oriental Pearl Tower, with its two huge (3) ______ (sphere), glowed in changing colours of pink, purple and blue. The reflection of the lights in the Huangpu River created a dazzling scene that left us (4) ______ (speech). My father set up his camera on a tripod and took some long-exposure photos. The results were (5) ______ (stun) — the lights created beautiful trails on the water. "This is one of the (6) ______ (fine) night views in the world," he said proudly. We walked along the Bund, (7) ______ (enjoy) the cool breeze and the sounds of the city. Street musicians played jazz, and couples strolled hand (8) ______ hand. I noticed that the old European-style buildings on our side of the river contrasted (9) ______ (beautiful) with the modern skyscrapers on the other side. It was like looking at two different eras at the same time. The night view reminded me that Shanghai is a city (10) ______ the past and future exist together in harmony.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：admiring**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spent an evening (in) doing sth`。

2. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`one by one` 表示"一个接一个"。

3. **答案：spheres**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`two huge spheres`，数词后接复数。

4. **答案：speechless**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`left us speechless`，`leave + 宾语 + 形容词`。

5. **答案：stunning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`were stunning`，修饰结果/事物用 `-ing` 形式。

6. **答案：finest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the finest night views`。

7. **答案：enjoying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。现在分词作伴随状语。

8. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`hand in hand` 表示"手牵手"。

9. **答案：beautifully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `contrasted` 需要副词。

10. **答案：where**
    - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a city`，定语从句中缺地点状语。
