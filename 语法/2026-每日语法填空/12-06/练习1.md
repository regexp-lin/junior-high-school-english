# 12-06 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校服争议 (School Uniforms)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The topic of school uniforms has been debated for years. Some people believe that uniforms are (1) ______ (benefit) because they create a sense of belonging and reduce peer pressure related to clothing. Others argue that uniforms limit students' freedom of (2) ______ (express) and do not allow them to show their (3) ______ (individual). In China, most schools require students to wear uniforms. The typical Chinese school uniform is a tracksuit in blue, red or green. While it is comfortable and (4) ______ (practice) for daily activities, many students complain that it is not fashionable enough. My classmate, Wang Mei, once said that wearing the same clothes every day made her feel (5) ______ she had no personality. However, our head teacher disagrees. She believes that uniforms help students focus on their studies rather (6) ______ on their appearance. She also points out that uniforms prevent students from (7) ______ (compare) each other's clothes, which can be especially (8) ______ (help) for families that cannot afford expensive brands. Recently, some schools in China (9) ______ (begin) to redesign their uniforms. They are working with fashion designers to create styles that are both smart and comfortable. Our school surveyed students' opinions last month, and most students said they would be happy to wear uniforms (10) ______ the design was more modern and attractive.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：beneficial**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`are beneficial`，系动词后接形容词。

2. **答案：expression**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `of` 后接名词。

3. **答案：individuality**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`show their individuality`，代词后接名词。

4. **答案：practical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `comfortable` 并列，需要形容词。

5. **答案：as if / as though**
   - **考点**：连词。`made her feel as if she had no personality`，引导方式状语从句。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`rather than` 表示"而不是"。

7. **答案：comparing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`prevent sb from doing sth`。

8. **答案：helpful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`especially helpful`，副词修饰形容词。

9. **答案：have begun**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Recently` 提示用现在完成时。

10. **答案：if**
    - **考点**：连词。`if the design was more modern`，引导条件状语从句。
