# 12-06 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：地震知识 (Earthquakes)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Earthquakes are one of nature's most (1) ______ (destroy) forces. They occur when large sections of the Earth's crust suddenly move, releasing enormous amounts of energy. China is located in one of the world's most (2) ______ (active) seismic zones, and earthquakes have struck various parts of the country throughout history. The 2008 Wenchuan earthquake was one of the (3) ______ (deadly) natural disasters in modern Chinese history. It measured 8.0 on the Richter scale and caused widespread destruction. Tens of (4) ______ (thousand) of people lost their lives, and millions (5) ______ (leave) homeless. The disaster taught the nation the importance of earthquake preparedness. Since then, the government (6) ______ (invest) heavily in earthquake-resistant building technology. New schools and hospitals are now built to much (7) ______ (high) safety standards than before. Our school conducts earthquake drills twice a year. During a drill, we practise the "drop, cover and hold on" technique — we drop to the ground, take cover under (8) ______ desk and hold on until the shaking stops. Our teacher also reminded us that if we are outdoors during (9) ______ earthquake, we should move away from buildings and power lines. These drills may seem repetitive, but they could save our (10) ______ (life) in a real emergency.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：destructive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`most destructive forces`，修饰名词需要形容词。

2. **答案：active**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`most active seismic zones`，已给出 `active`，此处保持原级配合 `most`。

3. **答案：deadliest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the deadliest natural disasters`。

4. **答案：thousands**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`Tens of thousands of` 表示"数以万计的"。

5. **答案：were left**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`millions were left homeless`，被动关系。

6. **答案：has invested**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 提示用现在完成时。

7. **答案：higher**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than before` 提示比较级。

8. **答案：a / our**
   - **考点**：冠词/代词。`under a desk` 或 `under our desk`。

9. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`during an earthquake`，`earthquake` 以元音音素开头。

10. **答案：lives**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`save our lives`，`life` 的复数为 `lives`。
