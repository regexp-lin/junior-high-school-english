# 05-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：青年节与青春 (Youth Day and Youth)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Youth Day on May 4th reminds us 1. ________ young people have always played an important role in society. This year, our school organized a 2. ________ (speak) contest titled “Youth in Action”, and I signed up 3. ________ hesitation.

Preparing the speech took two weeks. I read stories about students who 4. ________ (volunteer) during holidays and invented small tools 5. ________ (help) the elderly at home. Their courage made me think about 6. ________ I could contribute to my community.

On the contest day, I was nervous 7. ________ I stepped onto the stage. However, when I saw my classmates cheering, my voice became 8. ________ (steady). I said youth is not about age 9. ________ about responsibility: choosing to study hard, to respect others, and to care for the environment.

After the event, several teachers praised my message. I felt 10. ________ (pride) of every minute I had practised. Youth Day is not just about giving a speech — it is about turning ideas into daily action.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **that**
   - **考点**：连接词（无提示词填空）。reminds us that... 引导宾语从句，说明“提醒我们”的具体内容。

2. **speech**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词，有提示词）。speech contest 为复合名词“演讲比赛”，speak → speech。

3. **without**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。without hesitation 为固定搭配，意为“毫不犹豫地”。

4. **volunteered**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时，有提示词）。叙述过去读到的故事，定语从句中谓语与全文叙事一致，用 volunteered。

5. **to help**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语，有提示词）。发明小工具是为了帮助老人，用 to help。

6. **what**
   - **考点**：连接代词（无提示词填空）。宾语从句作 think about 的宾语，表示“我能贡献什么”，用 what。

7. **when**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。引导时间状语从句：当走上舞台时很紧张。

8. **steadier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（有提示词）。与之前的紧张状态对比，声音变得更稳，steady → steadier。

9. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。not... but... 结构，表示“不是……而是……”。

10. **proud**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词，有提示词）。feel proud of 为固定搭配，pride → proud。
