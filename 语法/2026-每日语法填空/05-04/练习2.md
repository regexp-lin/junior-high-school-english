# 05-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：动物园与野生动物保护 (Zoos and Wildlife Protection)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Modern zoos are changing. They are no longer just places 1. ________ animals are kept for entertainment; many of them now work 2. ________ (close) with scientists to protect rare species.

Last summer I visited 3. ________ wildlife park where injured birds 4. ________ (treat) before being returned to nature. A guide explained 5. ________ each animal had a personal feeding plan to keep it healthy. Visitors were asked not to tap on the glass or use 6. ________ (flash) lights on their phones.

Some people argue that wild animals should live only in forests or oceans. Others believe well-run zoos offer education: children see a panda 7. ________ (eat) bamboo and learn why forests must be saved. Both sides agree 8. ________ poaching and habitat loss are serious threats.

Personally, I support zoos that put conservation first. If we teach the next generation to respect life, 9. ________ world may become 10. ________ (safe) for humans and animals alike.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **where**
   - **考点**：关系副词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 places，在从句中作地点状语。

2. **closely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词，有提示词）。修饰动词 work，用副词 closely，work closely with 表示“与……密切合作”。

3. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。a wildlife park 泛指“一座野生动物园”。

4. **were treated**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（一般过去时的被动语态，有提示词）。birds 与 treat 为被动关系，叙述去年夏天的事，用 were treated。

5. **that**
   - **考点**：连接词（无提示词填空）。引导宾语从句，作 explained 的宾语，陈述事实用 that。

6. **flashing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词/现在分词（有提示词）。flashing lights 表示“闪光灯”，现在分词作定语修饰 lights。

7. **eating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词，有提示词）。see sb./sth. doing sth. 表示“看见……正在做某事”，panda 与 eat 为主动进行关系，用 eating。
