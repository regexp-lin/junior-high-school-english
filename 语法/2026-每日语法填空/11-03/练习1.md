# 11-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：图书馆志愿者 (Library Volunteer)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last summer, I volunteered at the city library for a month. It was an experience that (1) ______ (teach) me a lot about serving the community. My main job was to help (2) ______ (organise) books on the shelves and assist visitors in finding what they needed. The library had over 200,000 books, so learning the classification system was the first thing I had to do. It was more (3) ______ (complicate) than I expected, but the head librarian, Mrs. Wang, was very patient (4) ______ me. She explained that every book has a unique code based on its subject and author. After a week, I could find any book within (5) ______ (minute). One of the most (6) ______ (reward) parts of the job was helping children in the reading room. Every Saturday morning, I read stories (7) ______ a group of children aged four to six. Their eyes would light (8) ______ with wonder, and they would ask me all kinds of funny questions. A little girl once asked me (9) ______ books could talk when nobody was around. I laughed and said, "They talk to us through the words inside." By the end of the month, I had made many friends and developed a (10) ______ (deep) love for reading. I plan to volunteer at the library again next year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：taught**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的经历用一般过去时。

2. **答案：organise**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`help (to) do sth`。

3. **答案：complicated**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more complicated than`，`complicate` 变为 `complicated`。

4. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be patient with sb` 表示"对某人有耐心"。

5. **答案：minutes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`within minutes` 表示"几分钟之内"。

6. **答案：rewarding**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most rewarding parts`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`read stories to a group of children` 表示"给一群孩子读故事"。

8. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`light up` 表示"亮起来"。

9. **答案：if / whether**
   - **考点**：连词。`asked me if/whether books could talk`。

10. **答案：deeper**
    - **考点**：比较级。`a deeper love for reading`。
