# 11-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：冬天来了 (Winter Is Coming)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As November arrives, the temperatures begin to drop (1) ______ (sharp), and winter is just around the corner. In northern China, winter can be extremely cold, with temperatures falling below minus 20 degrees Celsius. However, many people still find ways (2) ______ (enjoy) the season. My family comes from Harbin, one of the (3) ______ (cold) cities in China. Every winter, the city hosts the famous Ice and Snow Festival, which (4) ______ (attract) millions of visitors from around the world. Enormous ice sculptures, some as tall (5) ______ ten-storey buildings, are created by skilled artists. At night, the sculptures are lit up with colourful lights, (6) ______ (create) a magical wonderland. I remember visiting the festival with my parents when I was eight. I was amazed at how (7) ______ (beauty) the ice castles and snow statues were. We also went skiing and ice skating, (8) ______ was my favourite activity. I fell down many times, but each time I got up and tried again. In southern China, winters are milder but often damp and chilly. Many (9) ______ (south) people say they actually feel colder than northerners because most southern homes don't have central heating. (10) ______ (regard) of where you live, winter is a season that brings its own unique charm and challenges.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：sharply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `drop` 需要副词，`sharp` 变为 `sharply`。

2. **答案：to enjoy**
   - **考点**：不定式。`find ways to enjoy`，不定式作后置定语。

3. **答案：coldest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the coldest cities`。

4. **答案：attracts**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。`which` 指代 `the festival`（单数），描述每年发生的事用一般现在时。

5. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`as tall as ten-storey buildings`，同级比较。

6. **答案：creating**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。现在分词作结果状语。

7. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`how beautiful the ice castles were`，`beauty` 变为 `beautiful`。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

9. **答案：southern**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `people` 需要形容词，`south` 变为 `southern`。

10. **答案：Regardless**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`regardless of` 表示"不管"，`regard` 变为 `regardless`。
