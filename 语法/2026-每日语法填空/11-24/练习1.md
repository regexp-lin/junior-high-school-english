# 11-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校社团 (School Clubs)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

School clubs offer students the opportunity to explore their interests and develop skills beyond the regular curriculum. Our school has over twenty clubs, (1) ______ (range) from sports and arts to science and technology. At the beginning of each school year, a club fair is held in the playground, (2) ______ students can visit different booths and sign up for the clubs they are interested in. I am a member of the robotics club, which I consider to be the (3) ______ (cool) club in our school. We meet every Wednesday afternoon to design and build small robots. Our club advisor, Mr Huang, is an engineer (4) ______ volunteers his time to teach us. Under his (5) ______ (guide), we have learned how to programme basic commands and assemble mechanical parts. Last month, our team entered a regional robotics competition. We spent weeks (6) ______ (prepare) for it, often staying at school until late in the evening. The competition was (7) ______ (fierce) than we had expected, with teams from over thirty schools. Our robot had to complete several tasks, including picking up objects and (8) ______ (navigate) through a maze. Although we did not win first place, we were awarded the "Best Design" prize, which made us very (9) ______ (pride). Being in the club has taught me the importance of teamwork and creative (10) ______ (think).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：ranging**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`ranging from...to...` 作定语修饰 `clubs`。

2. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，先行词 `playground` 为地点。

3. **答案：coolest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the coolest club in our school`。

4. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，先行词为人。

5. **答案：guidance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`Under his guidance`，形容词性物主代词后接名词。

6. **答案：preparing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth`。

7. **答案：fiercer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than we had expected` 提示比较级。

8. **答案：navigating**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `picking up` 并列。

9. **答案：proud**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`made us very proud`，`make + 宾语 + 形容词`。

10. **答案：thinking**
    - **考点**：动名词。`creative thinking` 表示"创造性思维"，名词用法。
