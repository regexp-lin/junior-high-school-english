# 11-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：可再生能源 (Renewable Energy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As the world's population grows, the demand for energy continues (1) ______ (increase). For many years, humans have relied heavily on fossil fuels such as coal, oil and natural gas. However, burning these fuels releases harmful gases into the atmosphere, (2) ______ contributes to global warming. This is why many countries are now turning to renewable energy sources. Renewable energy comes from natural sources that can be (3) ______ (constant) replenished, such as sunlight, wind and water. Solar power is one of the (4) ______ (fast) growing forms of renewable energy. In China, huge solar farms (5) ______ (build) in desert areas over the past decade. These farms produce clean electricity without creating any pollution. Wind power is another important renewable source. You may have seen the giant white turbines (6) ______ (stand) in open fields or along coastlines. Each turbine can generate enough electricity to power (7) ______ (hundred) of homes. My uncle works at a wind farm in Inner Mongolia. He told me that the farm (8) ______ (provide) electricity to over 50,000 households since it was completed in 2019. Experts believe that renewable energy will eventually replace fossil fuels (9) ______ our main energy source. To achieve this goal, more investment and (10) ______ (innovate) are needed.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to increase**
   - **考点**：不定式。`continue to do sth` 表示"继续做某事"。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整个句子。

3. **答案：constantly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `replenished` 需要副词形式。

4. **答案：fastest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the fastest growing forms`。

5. **答案：have been built**
   - **考点**：现在完成时的被动语态。`over the past decade` 提示现在完成时，`farms` 与 `build` 是被动关系。

6. **答案：standing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`see sb/sth doing sth`，感官动词后接现在分词表示正在进行。

7. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`hundreds of homes` 表示"数百个家庭"。

8. **答案：has provided**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`since it was completed in 2019` 提示现在完成时。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`replace...as...` 表示"取代……成为……"。

10. **答案：innovation**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `investment` 并列，需要名词形式。
