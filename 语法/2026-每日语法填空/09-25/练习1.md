# 09-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：老年人学用智能手机 (Elderly People Learning to Use Smartphones)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In today's digital world, many elderly people find it challenging (1) ______ (keep) up with new technology. My grandmother got her first smartphone last year, but she had no idea how (2) ______ (use) it. Simple tasks like making a video call or sending a photo seemed (3) ______ (possible) to her. So I decided to teach her step (4) ______ step. Every weekend, I would sit with her and show her one new feature. First, I taught her how to make phone calls and send text (5) ______ (message). Then we moved on to WeChat, which is one of the most (6) ______ (wide) used apps in China. At first, she kept (7) ______ (press) the wrong buttons and getting frustrated. But she never gave up. After a month of practice, she could (8) ______ (final) video-call her old friends in other cities. The first time she saw her best friend's face on the screen, she was so moved that tears rolled (9) ______ her cheeks. "Technology is wonderful," she said. Now, she even shares photos and short (10) ______ (video) in her friends' group chat every day. I am very proud of her.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：不定式。`find it challenging to do sth`，`it` 为形式宾语。

2. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to use it`，疑问词 + 不定式。

3. **答案：impossible**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加否定前缀）。根据语境，对老人来说"似乎不可能"，`possible` 加前缀变为 `impossible`。

4. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`step by step` 表示"一步一步地"。

5. **答案：messages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`text messages` 表示"短信"，复数。

6. **答案：widely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `used` 需要副词，`wide` 变为 `widely`。

7. **答案：pressing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`keep doing sth` 表示"持续做某事"。

8. **答案：finally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词短语 `video-call` 需要副词，`final` 变为 `finally`。

9. **答案：down**
   - **考点**：副词。`tears rolled down her cheeks` 表示"眼泪顺着脸颊流下"。

10. **答案：videos**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`photos and short videos`，与 `photos` 并列用复数。
