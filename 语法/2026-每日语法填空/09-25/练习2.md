# 09-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：秋天的校园 (The Campus in Autumn)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Autumn is my favourite season, and the campus becomes (1) ______ (especial) beautiful during this time. The tall ginkgo trees along the main road turn golden, and their (2) ______ (leaf) fall gently to the ground, creating a soft yellow carpet. Every year when autumn (3) ______ (come), students rush to take photos under the ginkgo trees. It has become one of the (4) ______ (popular) traditions at our school. Last week, our art teacher took us outside to paint the autumn scenery. We sat on the grass with our sketchbooks and tried (5) ______ (capture) the colours of the season. I chose to paint the school pond, (6) ______ was surrounded by red maple trees. Their reflection in the clear water made a (7) ______ (stun) picture. While I was painting, a cool breeze blew some leaves (8) ______ my paper. I decided to glue them onto my painting as decoration. My teacher thought it was a very (9) ______ (create) idea and showed my work to the whole class. The autumn campus reminds us that change can be beautiful. Just (10) ______ the trees let go of their old leaves to prepare for new growth, we should also embrace change in our lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：especially**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `beautiful` 需要副词，`especial` 变为 `especially`。

2. **答案：leaves**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`their leaves`，`leaf` 的复数为 `leaves`。

3. **答案：comes**
   - **考点**：主谓一致与时态。`when autumn comes`，描述每年惯例用一般现在时。

4. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the most popular traditions`。

5. **答案：to capture**
   - **考点**：不定式。`try to do sth` 表示"尝试做某事"。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，先行词为 `the school pond`。

7. **答案：stunning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `picture` 用形容词，`stun` 变为 `stunning`（令人惊叹的）。

8. **答案：onto**
   - **考点**：介词。`blew some leaves onto my paper` 表示"把树叶吹到了我的纸上"。

9. **答案：creative**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `idea` 需要形容词，`create` 变为 `creative`。

10. **答案：as**
    - **考点**：连词。`just as the trees let go...we should also...`，`just as` 表示"正如"。
