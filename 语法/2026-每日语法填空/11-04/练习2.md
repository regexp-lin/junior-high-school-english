# 11-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：鸟类观察 (Birdwatching)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Birdwatching is a hobby that connects people with nature. My biology teacher, Mr. Jiang, introduced our class to birdwatching last autumn. He took us to a wetland park (1) ______ is home to over 100 species of birds. Before the trip, he taught us how to use binoculars and gave each of us a bird identification guide. When we arrived at the park, we had to walk very (2) ______ (quiet) so as not to scare the birds away. The first bird I spotted was a beautiful kingfisher (3) ______ (sit) on a branch near the water. Its feathers were the most (4) ______ (bright) shade of blue I had ever seen. Mr. Jiang whispered that kingfishers are excellent (5) ______ (fisher) — they can dive into water at incredible speed to catch fish. Over the next two hours, our class identified twenty-three different species. I was surprised to learn that so many different birds (6) ______ (live) in our city. Some were (7) ______ (permanent) residents, while others were migratory birds just passing (8) ______. Mr. Jiang explained that wetlands are essential for bird populations because they provide food and (9) ______ (shelter). If wetlands are destroyed, many bird species will lose their homes. The trip inspired me to pay more attention (10) ______ the natural world around me.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a wetland park`（物），引导定语从句。

2. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `walk` 需要副词。

3. **答案：sitting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`a kingfisher sitting on a branch`，现在分词作后置定语。

4. **答案：brightest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the most brightest` 不正确，单音节词用 `brightest`。`the brightest shade`。

5. **答案：fishers**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`excellent fishers`，`fish` 变为 `fisher`（捕鱼者），复数。

6. **答案：lived**
   - **考点**：时态。`so many birds lived in our city`，叙述发现的事实用过去时。

7. **答案：permanent**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `residents` 需要形容词。

8. **答案：through**
   - **考点**：副词。`passing through` 表示"途经、路过"。

9. **答案：shelter**
   - **考点**：名词。`provide food and shelter`，与 `food` 并列。

10. **答案：to**
    - **考点**：介词。`pay attention to` 为固定搭配。
