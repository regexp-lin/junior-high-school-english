# 11-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：网络安全 (Internet Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The internet has made our lives more convenient, but it has also brought new (1) ______ (danger). Cybercrime, online scams and privacy leaks are becoming increasingly common. As teenagers who spend a lot of time online, we need to learn how (2) ______ (protect) ourselves. Last week, a police officer visited our school and gave a talk (3) ______ internet safety. He warned us about several common tricks used by online (4) ______ (criminal). For example, some people send fake messages (5) ______ (pretend) to be from banks or delivery companies. They try to trick you into clicking on links that steal your personal (6) ______ (inform). The officer advised us never to share passwords, home addresses or ID numbers with strangers online. He also reminded us (7) ______ (be) careful about what we post on social media. Photos and messages can (8) ______ (see) by anyone, and once something is posted, it is almost impossible to delete (9) ______ completely. The talk made me much more (10) ______ (awareness) of the risks of being online. I now check every link before clicking and use strong passwords for all my accounts.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：dangers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`new dangers`，多种危险。

2. **答案：to protect**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to protect ourselves`，疑问词 + 不定式。

3. **答案：on / about**
   - **考点**：介词。`a talk on/about internet safety`。

4. **答案：criminals**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`online criminals`，`criminal` 复数。

5. **答案：pretending**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`send messages pretending to be...`，现在分词作伴随状语。

6. **答案：information**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`personal information`，`inform` 变为名词 `information`。

7. **答案：to be**
   - **考点**：不定式。`remind sb to do sth`。

8. **答案：be seen**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`can be seen by anyone`。

9. **答案：it**
   - **考点**：代词。`delete it completely`，`it` 指代前面发布的内容。

10. **答案：aware**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`more aware of`，`awareness` 变为形容词 `aware`。
