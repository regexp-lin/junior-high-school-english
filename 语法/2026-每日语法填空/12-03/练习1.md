# 12-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：留学生活 (Studying Abroad)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Studying abroad is a dream for many Chinese students. It offers the chance to experience a different culture, improve language skills and gain a broader (1) ______ (understand) of the world. My cousin, Zhang Ting, went to Australia for high school two years ago. She told me that the (2) ______ (big) challenge at first was the language barrier. Although she had studied English for years in China, she found it difficult (3) ______ (keep) up with native speakers who talked very fast. She often felt frustrated because she could not express (4) ______ (she) clearly in class discussions. However, she refused to give up. She joined a study group with local students and practised speaking English every day. She also watched Australian TV shows to get used to the local accent. After about six (5) ______ (month), her English improved dramatically. Apart from language, she also had to adapt (6) ______ a completely different education system. In Australia, students are encouraged to think (7) ______ (independent) and express their own opinions. Teachers rarely give standard answers — instead, they ask students to find answers (8) ______ themselves through research and discussion. Zhang Ting found this approach very (9) ______ (refresh) compared to the exam-focused system in China. She says that studying abroad has made her more confident and open-minded. She is now considering (10) ______ (apply) to an Australian university after graduation.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：understanding**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a broader understanding`，冠词后接名词。

2. **答案：biggest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the biggest challenge` 用最高级。

3. **答案：to keep**
   - **考点**：不定式。`find it difficult to do sth` 为固定句型。

4. **答案：herself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`express herself` 表示"表达自己"。

5. **答案：months**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`six months`，数词后接复数。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`adapt to` 表示"适应"。

7. **答案：independently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `think` 需要副词形式。

8. **答案：for / by**
   - **考点**：介词。`find answers for/by themselves` 表示"自己找到答案"。

9. **答案：refreshing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found this approach refreshing`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

10. **答案：applying**
    - **考点**：动名词。`consider doing sth` 表示"考虑做某事"。
