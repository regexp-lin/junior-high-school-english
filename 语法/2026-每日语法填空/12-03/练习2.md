# 12-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：移动支付 (Digital Payments)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has become a global leader in digital payments. In most Chinese cities, you can pay for almost (1) ______ with your smartphone — from a taxi ride to a bowl of noodles at a street stall. The two most popular payment platforms are WeChat Pay and Alipay, which together serve over a billion (2) ______ (use). Using digital payments is incredibly simple. You just open the app, scan a QR code and the money (3) ______ (transfer) instantly. There is no need to carry cash or wait for change. This (4) ______ (convenient) has made digital payments the preferred method for people of all ages. My grandmother, who is 72, learned how to use WeChat Pay last year. She was (5) ______ (nervous) about it at first because she was afraid of making mistakes and (6) ______ (lose) money. My mother spent a whole afternoon (7) ______ (teach) her step by step. Now my grandmother uses it confidently every day — she even sends red envelopes to her grandchildren (8) ______ the Spring Festival through the app. However, some people worry about the risks of a cashless society. If your phone battery (9) ______ (die) or the network goes down, you cannot make any payments. There are also concerns about privacy, as payment platforms collect a large amount of personal data. Despite these issues, it seems (10) ______ (like) that digital payments will continue to grow in popularity.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：anything / everything**
   - **考点**：代词。`pay for almost anything/everything` 表示"几乎为任何事物付款"。

2. **答案：users**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a billion users`。

3. **答案：is transferred**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the money` 与 `transfer` 之间是被动关系。

4. **答案：convenience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`This convenience`，代词后接名词作主语。

5. **答案：nervous**
   - **考点**：形容词。`was nervous about`，系动词后接形容词作表语。

6. **答案：losing**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `making mistakes` 并列，介词 `of` 后接动名词。

7. **答案：teaching**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth`。

8. **答案：during**
   - **考点**：介词。`during the Spring Festival` 表示"在春节期间"。

9. **答案：dies**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。条件状语从句中，主语 `your phone battery` 为第三人称单数，一般现在时表将来。

10. **答案：likely**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`it seems likely that...`，`like` 变为形容词 `likely`。
