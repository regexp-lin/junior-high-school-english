# 03-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：在线学习 (Online Learning)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Online learning has become a common part of students' lives. With the help of the Internet, students can attend classes without 1. ________ (leave) their homes.

There are many advantages of online learning. First, it saves time. Students don't need to spend time 2. ________ (travel) to school. They can start learning as soon as they turn on their computers. Second, they can watch the videos again if they don't understand something 3. ________ (complete). This is much 4. ________ (convenient) than traditional classes.

However, online learning also has some disadvantages. One of the 5. ________ (big) problems is that it is hard for students to stay focused. At home, there are too many things that can distract them, 6. ________ as TV, games and snacks. Without a teacher standing in front of them, some students may feel 7. ________ (sleep) or start doing other things.

Another problem is the lack of communication. In a real classroom, students can discuss 8. ________ their classmates and ask the teacher questions face to face. But in online classes, this kind of interaction 9. ________ (be) often limited.

In my opinion, the best way is to combine online learning 10. ________ traditional learning. We should take advantage of both to improve our study.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **leaving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 without 后接动名词形式。

2. **traveling**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 意为"花时间做某事"。

3. **completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 understand，需用副词形式 completely。

4. **more convenient**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，convenient 为多音节形容词，比较级为 more convenient。

5. **biggest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，big → biggest（双写辅音字母加 -est）。

6. **such**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。such as 意为"例如"，后接举例说明。

7. **sleepy**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。feel 是系动词，后接形容词作表语，sleep → sleepy（困倦的）。

8. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。discuss with sb. 意为"与某人讨论"。

9. **is**
   - **考点**：主谓一致（一般现在时）。主语 this kind of interaction 是第三人称单数，用 is。

10. **with**
    - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。combine A with B 意为"把A与B结合起来"，是固定搭配。
