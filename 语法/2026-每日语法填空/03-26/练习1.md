# 03-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国传统节日 (Chinese Traditional Festivals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has many traditional festivals throughout the year. Each festival has its own special customs and 1. ________ (mean).

The Spring Festival is the 2. ________ (important) festival in China. It usually falls in January or February. Before the festival, people clean their houses 3. ________ (thorough) to sweep away bad luck. On New Year's Eve, families get together to enjoy a big dinner. Children are often given red envelopes 4. ________ contain lucky money.

The Dragon Boat Festival is 5. ________ festival I like most. It is celebrated on the fifth day of the fifth lunar month. People eat zongzi and watch dragon boat 6. ________ (race). This festival is in memory 7. ________ Qu Yuan, a great poet in ancient China.

The Mid-Autumn Festival is a time for family reunions. On that night, the moon is at 8. ________ (it) brightest and fullest. Family members sit together, eating mooncakes and 9. ________ (enjoy) the beautiful moonlight. It is a warm and peaceful moment.

These traditional festivals 10. ________ (connect) Chinese people with their history and culture. They remind us where we come from and what we should treasure.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **meanings**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。and 连接并列名词，mean → meaning，用复数 meanings 表示"多种含义"。

2. **most important**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，important 为多音节形容词，最高级为 most important。

3. **thoroughly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 clean，需用副词形式 thoroughly。

4. **that/which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 red envelopes，先行词指物，在从句中作主语。

5. **the**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。后文有 I like most 作定语修饰 festival，为特指，用定冠词 the。

6. **races**
   - **考点**：名词复数。dragon boat races 意为"龙舟赛"，比赛通常有多场，用复数。

7. **of**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。in memory of 意为"纪念……"，是固定搭配。

8. **its**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 brightest（brightest 为形容词作名词省略），it → its，指月亮的（最亮最圆时）。

9. **enjoying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。and 连接并列成分，与前面的 eating 并列，用现在分词形式。

10. **connect**
    - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）。描述客观事实，主语 festivals 为复数，动词用原形 connect。
