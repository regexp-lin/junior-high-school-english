# 06-30 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：社区花园 (The Community Garden)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

There is a small garden in our community (1) ______ neighbours grow flowers and vegetables together. It (2) ______ (set) up by a retired teacher called Mr. Liu two years ago. At first, only a few families were interested in it. But now, more than thirty families (3) ______ (join) the project. Every Saturday morning, people come to the garden with their tools. Some dig the soil, some water the plants, (4) ______ others pick ripe tomatoes and beans. Working in the garden is a good way of (5) ______ (exercise). It also gives people, especially the elderly, a chance (6) ______ (make) new friends. My mother loves (7) ______ (plant) sunflowers. She says watching them grow taller makes (8) ______ (she) happy. Last autumn, our garden held a small harvest festival. Everyone brought the food they had grown and shared it (9) ______ each other. It was the (10) ______ (warm) neighbourhood event I have ever attended.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a small garden`（地点），定语从句结构完整但缺地点状语，用 `where`。

2. **答案：was set**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。花园"被建立"，`two years ago` 表示过去，主语 `It` 为单数，用 `was set`。`set up` 为固定短语。

3. **答案：have joined**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`now` 暗示从过去到现在的结果——已经有三十多家加入了，用现在完成时 `have joined`。

4. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。`Some...some...and others...` 为并列结构，最后一个并列项前用 `and`。

5. **答案：exercising**
   - **考点**：动名词。`of` 为介词，后接动名词；`exercise` 变为 `exercising`。

6. **答案：to make**
   - **考点**：不定式。`a chance to do sth` 表示"做某事的机会"，不定式作后置定语。

7. **答案：planting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`love doing sth` 表示"喜欢做某事"（表示一般性的爱好），`plant` 变为 `planting`。

8. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`make` 后接宾语，`she` 的宾格为 `her`。此处 `make her happy` 为"使她开心"。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`share sth with sb` 为固定搭配，表示"与某人分享某物"。

10. **答案：warmest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词 + I have ever attended` 为现在完成时配合最高级的典型结构，`warm` 的最高级为 `warmest`。
