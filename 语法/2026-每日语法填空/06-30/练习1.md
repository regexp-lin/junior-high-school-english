# 06-30 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：太空探索 (Space Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Space has always been a place full of mysteries. Since the 1960s, humans (1) ______ (try) to explore the universe. In 1969, Neil Armstrong became the first person (2) ______ (walk) on the moon. His famous words, "That's one small step for a man, one giant leap for mankind," (3) ______ (remember) by people all over the world even today. China has also made great (4) ______ (achieve) in space. The Shenzhou spacecraft has (5) ______ (successful) sent many astronauts into space. In 2021, Chinese astronauts lived and worked on the space station (6) ______ about three months. They did science experiments, took photographs of the Earth, and even gave a live class (7) ______ millions of students watched. Space exploration is expensive and sometimes (8) ______ (danger), but it helps us understand our planet (9) ______ (good) and develop new technology. I dream of becoming an astronaut one day. (10) ______ it takes years of hard training, I am willing to try.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：have been trying / have tried**
   - **考点**：现在完成进行时/现在完成时。`Since the 1960s` 表示从过去持续到现在，强调持续性用 `have been trying`，强调结果用 `have tried`，两者均可。

2. **答案：to walk**
   - **考点**：不定式作后置定语。当被修饰词前有序数词（`the first`）时，用不定式作定语。

3. **答案：are remembered**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。他的话"被人们铭记"（`even today` 提示现在仍是如此），主语 `words` 为复数，用 `are remembered`。

4. **答案：achievements**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`great` 修饰名词，`achieve` 变为 `achievement`；`great achievements` 用复数表示多项成就。

5. **答案：successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `sent` 需要副词，`successful` 变为 `successfully`。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for about three months` 表示"大约三个月"，`for` 接一段时间。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a live class`，在定语从句中作宾语，用 `that` 或 `which`。

8. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is` 后接表语形容词，`danger` 变为 `dangerous`（危险的）。

9. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。隐含与"不探索太空"的对比，`well` 的比较级为 `better`，修饰动词 `understand`。

10. **答案：Even though / Although**
    - **考点**：让步连词。"需要多年艰苦训练"与"愿意尝试"为让步关系，用 `Even though` 或 `Although`。
