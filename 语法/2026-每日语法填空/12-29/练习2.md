# 12-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：博物馆之旅 (Museum Visit)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Museums are windows into the past that allow us to learn about history, art and science in an engaging way. Last weekend, our class visited the Provincial Museum as part of (1) ______ history project. The museum houses over 100,000 artefacts, many of (2) ______ date back thousands of years. The exhibit that impressed me the most was the ancient bronze collection. The intricate designs on these vessels were (3) ______ (incredible) detailed, considering they were made over 3,000 years ago. It is hard to imagine how craftsmen without modern tools could create such (4) ______ (master). Our guide explained that bronze-making was one of the most (5) ______ (advance) technologies of its time. She also told us about the discovery of the artefacts — many (6) ______ (find) by farmers who were digging in their fields. One farmer accidentally unearthed a bronze jar while (7) ______ (plant) rice, not knowing that it was worth a fortune. The museum also had an interactive section where visitors could try (8) ______ (make) simple pottery and calligraphy. I tried my hand at the pottery wheel and made a small bowl. It looked rather (9) ______ (regular), but the experience gave me a deeper appreciation for the skill of ancient potters. Our teacher asked us to write a report about what we had learned. She said that visiting museums is just as (10) ______ (value) as studying from textbooks because it brings history to life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：a / our**
   - **考点**：冠词/代词。`part of a/our history project`。

2. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。`many of which`，介词后接 `which`。

3. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `detailed` 需要副词。

4. **答案：masterpieces**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`such masterpieces`，`master` 变为 `masterpiece`，复数形式。

5. **答案：advanced**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most advanced technologies`。

6. **答案：were found**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`many were found by farmers`，被动关系。

7. **答案：planting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`while planting rice`，时间状语从句的省略。

8. **答案：making**
   - **考点**：动名词。`try doing sth` 表示"尝试做某事"。

9. **答案：irregular**
   - **考点**：词性转换。根据语境"看起来不太规则"，加否定前缀。

10. **答案：valuable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`as valuable as`，`value` 变为形容词。
