# 12-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：目标设定 (Goal Setting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Setting clear goals is one of the most (1) ______ (power) things you can do to improve your life. Goals give us direction, motivation and a sense of purpose. Without goals, it is easy to drift through life without (2) ______ (achieve) anything meaningful. Experts say that effective goals should be SMART — Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant and Time-bound. A SMART goal is much more (3) ______ (like) to be achieved than a vague wish. For example, instead of saying "I want to get better grades," a SMART goal would be "I will improve my maths score by 10 points (4) ______ the end of next semester by doing thirty minutes of maths practice every day." At the beginning of this school year, our head teacher asked each student (5) ______ (set) three personal goals. I wrote mine on a card and kept it in my pencil case where I could see it every day. My first goal was (6) ______ (pass) the English speech contest at our school. To achieve this, I practised speaking in front of a mirror for twenty minutes daily. My second goal was to improve my (7) ______ (fit) by running 3 kilometres three times a week. My third goal was to read one English novel per month. So far, I (8) ______ (achieve) two out of three goals. I won second prize in the speech contest and have been running (9) ______ (regular). I am still working on the reading goal, (10) ______ I am confident that I will complete it by the end of the year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：powerful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most powerful things`，修饰名词需要形容词。

2. **答案：achieving**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `without` 后接动名词。

3. **答案：likely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`more likely to be achieved`，`like` 变为形容词 `likely`。

4. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`by the end of next semester` 表示"到下学期末"。

5. **答案：to set**
   - **考点**：不定式。`ask sb to do sth`。

6. **答案：to pass**
   - **考点**：不定式。`My first goal was to pass`，不定式作表语。

7. **答案：fitness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`improve my fitness`，代词后接名词。

8. **答案：have achieved**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`So far` 提示。

9. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `running` 需要副词。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：连词。前后转折。
