# 12-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：理想职业 (Dream Jobs)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Everyone has a dream job — a career they hope to pursue when they grow up. When I was younger, I wanted to be a firefighter because I thought it was the (1) ______ (brave) job in the world. As I grew older, my interests changed. Now I dream of becoming a marine (2) ______ (biology) because I am fascinated by the ocean and its creatures. Last year, our school held a "Career Day" event. Professionals from different fields (3) ______ (invite) to share their experiences with us. A doctor talked about the (4) ______ (challenge) of saving lives, a software engineer explained how apps are (5) ______ (create), and a wildlife photographer showed us stunning images she had taken (6) ______ Africa. Each speaker's story was inspiring in its own way. What struck me most was something the photographer said: "Choose a job that you love, (7) ______ you will never have to work a day in your life." After the event, our teacher asked us (8) ______ (write) an essay about our dream jobs. I wrote about my wish to study marine life and protect ocean ecosystems. I know that achieving this dream (9) ______ (require) years of hard study, especially in science subjects. But I am willing to put in the effort because I believe that doing (10) ______ you love is the key to a happy and meaningful life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：bravest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the bravest job` 用最高级。

2. **答案：biologist**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a marine biologist` 表示"海洋生物学家"。

3. **答案：were invited**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`Professionals` 与 `invite` 之间是被动关系，过去时。

4. **答案：challenges**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the challenges of saving lives`。

5. **答案：created**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`how apps are created`，被动关系。

6. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in Africa` 表示"在非洲"。

7. **答案：and**
   - **考点**：连词。祈使句 + `and` + 陈述句，表示"如果……就……"。

8. **答案：to write**
   - **考点**：不定式。`ask sb to do sth`。

9. **答案：will require**
   - **考点**：一般将来时。表示将来的事实。

10. **答案：what**
    - **考点**：连接代词。`doing what you love`，`what` 引导宾语从句。
