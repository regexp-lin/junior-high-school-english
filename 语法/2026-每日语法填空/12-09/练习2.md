# 12-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中西方节日对比 (Comparing Chinese and Western Festivals)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China and Western countries have many wonderful festivals, each with its own unique traditions and (1) ______ (celebrate). While some festivals share similar themes, the ways people celebrate them can be quite different. For example, both the Spring Festival and Christmas are family-centred holidays. During the Spring Festival, Chinese families gather for a reunion dinner and exchange red envelopes (2) ______ (contain) money. In Western countries, families come together on Christmas Day to share a special meal and open presents under the Christmas tree. Another interesting (3) ______ (compare) is between the Qingming Festival and Halloween. Qingming is a time for Chinese people to honour their ancestors by visiting graves and (4) ______ (offer) food and flowers. Halloween, (5) ______ has its roots in ancient Celtic traditions, is a time for costumes, trick-or-treating and spooky decorations. Despite their different atmospheres, both festivals involve (6) ______ (remember) the dead. In recent years, Western festivals (7) ______ (become) increasingly popular in China, especially among young people. Many teenagers celebrate Valentine's Day and Christmas, though they often do so in their own (8) ______ (create) ways. My parents say there is nothing wrong (9) ______ learning about other cultures' festivals, as long as we do not forget our own traditions. I agree. I think experiencing both Chinese and Western festivals makes our lives (10) ______ (rich) and more colourful.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：celebrations**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `traditions` 并列，需要名词复数。

2. **答案：containing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`red envelopes containing money`，主动关系。

3. **答案：comparison**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`Another interesting comparison`，形容词后接名词。

4. **答案：offering**
   - **考点**：动名词。与 `visiting` 并列。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句。

6. **答案：remembering**
   - **考点**：动名词。`involve doing sth` 表示"涉及做某事"。

7. **答案：have become**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`In recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

8. **答案：creative**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `ways` 需要形容词。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`nothing wrong with` 表示"……没有什么不好的"。

10. **答案：richer**
    - **考点**：比较级。与 `more colourful` 并列，比较级结构。
