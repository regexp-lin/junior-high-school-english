# 10-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：班级图书角 (Class Reading Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

At the beginning of this term, our head teacher suggested (1) ______ (create) a class reading corner. She asked each student to donate at least two books from home. Within a week, we had collected over 80 books of various (2) ______ (kind) — novels, science books, history books and comic books. A small bookshelf (3) ______ (place) at the back of the classroom, and two students were chosen to serve as "class librarians". Their job is to keep the books organised and make sure they (4) ______ (return) on time. The reading corner quickly became the most popular spot in our classroom. During break time, students rush to the corner to pick (5) ______ a book. My favourite book from the corner is a novel about a boy (6) ______ travels through time. It is so interesting that I can (7) ______ (hard) put it down. Our class librarian, Liu Wei, has created a recommendation board (8) ______ students can write short reviews of the books they have read. This helps others decide (9) ______ to read next. Our head teacher says the reading corner has made our class much (10) ______ (quiet) during break time and has improved our reading habits. She plans to encourage other classes to set up their own reading corners.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：creating**
   - **考点**：动名词。`suggest doing sth` 表示"建议做某事"。

2. **答案：kinds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`various kinds` 表示"各种类型"。

3. **答案：was placed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`a bookshelf was placed at...`，书架"被放置"。

4. **答案：are returned**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`make sure they are returned on time`，书"被归还"。

5. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`pick up a book` 表示"拿起一本书"。

6. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a boy`（指人），引导定语从句。

7. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：副词。`can hardly put it down`，`hardly` 表示"几乎不"。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a recommendation board`，从句中缺地点状语。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`decide what to read next`，`what` + 不定式。

10. **答案：quieter**
    - **考点**：比较级。`much quieter`，`much` 修饰比较级。
