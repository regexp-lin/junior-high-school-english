# 10-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：风力发电 (Wind Power)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As the world looks for cleaner sources of energy, wind power (1) ______ (become) increasingly important. Wind turbines convert the energy of moving air (2) ______ electricity. China is now the world's (3) ______ (large) producer of wind energy, with thousands of wind farms spread across the country. Last summer, I visited a wind farm in Inner Mongolia with my science class. The turbines were (4) ______ (incredible) tall — each one stood over 100 metres high. As we got (5) ______ (close), we could hear the giant blades spinning in the wind. A local engineer explained how the turbines work. When the wind blows, it turns the blades, which are connected (6) ______ a generator inside the turbine. The generator then produces electricity that (7) ______ (send) to homes and factories through power lines. He told us that one turbine can produce enough electricity (8) ______ (power) about 1,500 homes. However, wind power also has its (9) ______ (limit). Turbines only work when there is enough wind, and they can be harmful to birds. Scientists are working on new designs (10) ______ (solve) these problems. Despite the challenges, I believe wind power will play a key role in our clean energy future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has become**
   - **考点**：时态。`as the world looks` 暗示到目前为止，用现在完成时。

2. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`convert...into...` 表示"把……转化为……"。

3. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the world's largest producer`。

4. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `tall` 需要副词。

5. **答案：closer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`got closer` 表示"靠得更近"。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`be connected to a generator` 表示"与……相连"。

7. **答案：is sent**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`electricity that is sent to...`，电力"被输送"。

8. **答案：to power**
   - **考点**：不定式。`enough electricity to power`，`enough + 名词 + to do`。

9. **答案：limitations**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`its limitations`，`limit` 变为名词 `limitation`，复数。

10. **答案：to solve**
    - **考点**：不定式。`new designs to solve these problems`，不定式作后置定语/目的。
