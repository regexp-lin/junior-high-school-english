# 10-25 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：爱护眼睛 (Protecting Our Eyes)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Eye health has become a growing concern among Chinese teenagers. A recent survey showed that over 70% of junior high school students are short-sighted. The main reasons include spending too much time (1) ______ screens and not getting enough outdoor exercise. In our class alone, 35 out of 45 students wear (2) ______ (glass). Our school takes eye health very (3) ______ (serious). Every day at 10 a.m. and 3 p.m., we do a set of eye exercises that (4) ______ (design) to relax the muscles around our eyes. The exercises involve pressing certain points around the eyes (5) ______ (gentle) with our fingers. Many students don't enjoy doing them, but doctors say they really help. Our science teacher also gave us some tips for protecting our eyes. She said we should follow the "20-20-20" rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away (6) ______ at least 20 seconds. She also recommended (7) ______ (spend) at least two hours outdoors every day, as natural sunlight is (8) ______ (benefit) for eye development. Since I started following these tips, my eyesight has stopped (9) ______ (get) worse. I wish I (10) ______ (know) about these tips earlier. Taking care of our eyes is something we should all pay attention to.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`spending time on screens` 表示"花时间在屏幕上"。

2. **答案：glasses**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`wear glasses` 表示"戴眼镜"，`glasses` 为复数名词。

3. **答案：seriously**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`takes sth seriously`。

4. **答案：are designed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`exercises that are designed to relax...`，"被设计来"。

5. **答案：gently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `pressing` 需要副词，`gentle` 变为 `gently`。

6. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for at least 20 seconds` 表示"至少20秒"。

7. **答案：spending**
   - **考点**：动名词。`recommend doing sth` 表示"建议做某事"。

8. **答案：beneficial**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is beneficial for`，系动词后接形容词。

9. **答案：getting**
   - **考点**：动名词。`stop doing sth` 表示"停止做某事"（视力停止变差）。

10. **答案：had known**
    - **考点**：虚拟语气。`I wish I had known...earlier`，对过去的虚拟，用过去完成时。
