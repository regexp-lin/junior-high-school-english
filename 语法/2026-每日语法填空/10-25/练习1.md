# 10-25 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：万圣节活动 (Halloween Activity)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Although Halloween is not a traditional Chinese festival, it (1) ______ (gain) popularity among young people in recent years, especially as a fun cultural activity at schools. Last October, our English teacher organised a Halloween party (2) ______ our class. She asked us to learn about the history and customs of Halloween beforehand. We discovered that it originated in ancient Celtic festivals and (3) ______ (late) became associated with costumes, pumpkins and trick-or-treating. For the party, each student was encouraged (4) ______ (wear) a costume. Some students dressed up as ghosts and witches, while others chose (5) ______ (fun) costumes like animals or cartoon characters. I made a simple robot costume (6) ______ of cardboard boxes. It wasn't the most (7) ______ (beauty) costume, but it made everyone laugh. The classroom (8) ______ (decorate) with orange and black streamers, paper bats and a big pumpkin lantern that Li Ming had carved (9) ______ (he). We played games like "bobbing for apples" and told scary stories with the lights turned off. The party was a great way (10) ______ (learn) about Western culture while having fun. Our teacher said that understanding different cultures helps us become more open-minded.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has gained**
   - **考点**：时态。`in recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`organised a party for our class` 表示"为我们班举办了一个派对"。

3. **答案：later**
   - **考点**：比较级/副词。`later became associated with`，`late` 变为 `later` 表"后来"。

4. **答案：to wear**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be encouraged to do sth`。

5. **答案：funny**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `costumes` 需要形容词，`fun` 变为 `funny`。

6. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词。`made out of cardboard boxes` 表示"用纸箱做成的"。

7. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most beautiful costume`，`beauty` 变为 `beautiful`。

8. **答案：was decorated**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the classroom was decorated with...`。

9. **答案：himself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 `Li Ming`，用 `himself` 强调"亲自"。

10. **答案：to learn**
    - **考点**：不定式。`a great way to learn` 不定式作后置定语。
