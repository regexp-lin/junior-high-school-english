# 07-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：早餐的重要性 (The Importance of Breakfast)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Breakfast is often called the most important meal of the day, (1) ______ many students skip it to save time. A recent survey shows that about 30 percent of middle school students go to school (2) ______ eating breakfast. Doctors say this is very bad for (3) ______ (they) health. After a night's sleep, our body needs energy to start a new day. Without breakfast, students may feel (4) ______ (tire) and find it hard to pay attention in class. Studies have proved that students who eat breakfast (5) ______ (regular) perform much (6) ______ (well) in exams than those who do not. A healthy breakfast does not need to be fancy. A glass of milk, an egg, and some bread are (7) ______ (enough). My mother always wakes up early (8) ______ (prepare) breakfast for the family. She makes sure we eat something (9) ______ (nutrition) before leaving home. I used to skip breakfast, but after (10) ______ (learn) about its benefits, I have never missed it again.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but / yet**
   - **考点**：转折连词。"早餐被认为很重要"与"很多学生跳过它"为转折关系。

2. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`without eating breakfast` 表示"没有吃早餐"。

3. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 `health`，`they` 变为 `their`。

4. **答案：tired**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel` 为系动词，后接形容词，`tire` 变为 `tired`（感到疲倦的）。

5. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `eat` 需要副词，`regular` 变为 `regularly`。

6. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`well` 的比较级为 `better`。

7. **答案：enough**
   - **考点**：形容词/代词。`are enough` 表示"就足够了"，`enough` 用作表语形容词。注意此处括号内给的是 `enough`，无需变形。

8. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。妈妈早起的目的是准备早餐，用 `to prepare`。

9. **答案：nutritious**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`something + 形容词`（不定代词后置形容词），`nutrition` 变为 `nutritious`（有营养的）。

10. **答案：learning**
    - **考点**：动名词。`after` 为介词，后接动名词 `learning`。
