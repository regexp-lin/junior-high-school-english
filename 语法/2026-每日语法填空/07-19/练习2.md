# 07-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：写博客 (Writing a Blog)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Two years ago, my English teacher suggested that I (1) ______ (start) a blog to practise writing. At first, I did not think anyone would read it. But I decided to give it (2) ______ try. My first post was about my daily routine, and to my surprise, several classmates left (3) ______ (comment) saying they enjoyed it. That gave me confidence to keep (4) ______ (write). Since then, I (5) ______ (publish) over a hundred posts on topics ranging from school life to book reviews. Writing a blog has helped me in many ways. It has (6) ______ (great) improved my English vocabulary and grammar. More (7) ______ (important), it has taught me to organise my thoughts clearly. Every time I sit down to write, I ask (8) ______ (I): What is the main idea? Who is my reader? How can I make this (9) ______ (interest)? These questions help me produce better content. Some of my friends have also started their own blogs after seeing (10) ______ (my). I believe that writing is a skill everyone should develop, and a blog is a great place to begin.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：start**
   - **考点**：虚拟语气。`suggest that sb (should) do sth` 为固定句型，`that` 从句中用动词原形（省略 `should`）。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`give it a try` 为固定搭配，表示"试一试"。

3. **答案：comments**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several` 修饰可数名词复数，`comment` 变为 `comments`。

4. **答案：writing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`keep doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"坚持做某事"。

5. **答案：have published**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`Since then` 提示用现在完成时，主语 `I` 用 `have published`。

6. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `improved` 需要副词，`great` 变为 `greatly`。

7. **答案：importantly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`More importantly` 为独立副词短语，修饰整个句子，表示"更重要的是"。

8. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 `I`，宾语指向同一人，用 `myself`。

9. **答案：interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`make this interesting` 中 `interesting` 作宾语补足语，`interest` 变为 `interesting`（有趣的）。

10. **答案：mine**
    - **考点**：名词性物主代词。`mine` = `my blog`，避免重复，用名词性物主代词。
