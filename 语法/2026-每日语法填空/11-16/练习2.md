# 11-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：社区服务 (Community Service)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Community service is a meaningful way for young people to give back to society. Our school encourages every student (1) ______ (take) part in at least one community service project each year. Last weekend, our class went to a nearby community centre to help organise a charity book fair. We collected over 500 (2) ______ (use) books from students and teachers, sorted them by category and set up display tables. The fair was open to the public, and all the money (3) ______ (raise) would go to a local children's hospital. I was responsible (4) ______ the picture book section. Many parents came with their young children, and it was heartwarming to see the (5) ______ (child) eyes light up when they found a book they liked. By the end of the day, we (6) ______ (sell) most of the books and raised over 3,000 yuan. The (7) ______ (satisfy) I felt was beyond words. Community service not only helps (8) ______ (other), but also teaches us important life skills. Through this experience, I learned how to communicate with strangers, work as part of (9) ______ team and solve problems on the spot. I look forward to (10) ______ (participate) in more community activities in the future.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to take**
   - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb to do sth` 表示"鼓励某人做某事"。

2. **答案：used**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`used books` 表示"二手书"。

3. **答案：raised**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`all the money raised` 表示"所筹集的钱"。

4. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be responsible for` 表示"负责"。

5. **答案：children's**
   - **考点**：名词所有格。`the children's eyes`，不规则复数名词所有格加 `'s`。

6. **答案：had sold**
   - **考点**：过去完成时。`By the end of the day` 提示"到某个过去时间点为止"用过去完成时。

7. **答案：satisfaction**
   - **考点**：词性转换。定冠词 `The` 后接名词，`satisfy` 变为 `satisfaction`。

8. **答案：others**
   - **考点**：代词。`helps others` 表示"帮助他人"，此处作宾语。

9. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`part of a team` 表示"团队的一份子"。

10. **答案：participating**
    - **考点**：动名词。`look forward to doing sth`，`to` 为介词，后接动名词。
