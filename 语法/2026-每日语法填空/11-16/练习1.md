# 11-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：时间管理 (Time Management)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Time management is a skill that every student should learn. Many teenagers complain that they do not have enough time to finish homework, practise hobbies and get enough sleep. (1) ______, the real problem is often not the lack of time but the poor use of it. I used to be terrible at managing my time. After school, I would spend hours (2) ______ (watch) videos or chatting with friends online. By the time I started my homework, it was already nine o'clock. I often stayed up until midnight and felt (3) ______ (exhaust) the next morning. My grades began to drop, and my parents became very (4) ______ (worry). One day, my father sat down with me and helped me create a daily schedule. He suggested that I (5) ______ (divide) my after-school time into blocks: one hour for homework, thirty minutes for exercise and thirty minutes for (6) ______ (person) interests. He also taught me the "two-minute rule" — if a task takes less (7) ______ two minutes, do it right away instead of putting it off. I have been following this schedule (8) ______ three months now. The results have been (9) ______ (amaze). I finish my homework earlier, sleep better and even have more free time than before. I have realised that good time management does not mean doing more — it means doing things more (10) ______ (efficient).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：However**
   - **考点**：连接副词。前后句意转折，"很多人抱怨时间不够，然而真正的问题是……"。

2. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth` 表示"花时间做某事"。

3. **答案：exhausted**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt exhausted`，描述人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

4. **答案：worried**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`became worried`，描述人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

5. **答案：(should) divide**
   - **考点**：虚拟语气。`suggest that sb (should) do sth`，`should` 可省略。

6. **答案：personal**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `interests` 需要形容词，`person` 变为 `personal`。

7. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：连词。`less than two minutes` 表示"少于两分钟"。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for three months` 表示持续时间。

9. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the results have been amazing`，修饰事物（结果）用 `-ing` 形式。

10. **答案：efficiently**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `doing` 需要副词形式。
