# 03-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：宠物与责任 (Pets and Responsibility)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many families keep pets at home. Dogs, cats, fish and birds are among the 1. ________ (popular) choices. Having a pet can bring a lot of joy, but it also comes 2. ________ great responsibility.

My family got a golden retriever named Lucky two years 3. ________. At first, I was so excited that I played with him all day long. But soon I realized that 4. ________ (take) care of a dog was not as easy as I thought.

Every morning, I have to get up early to walk Lucky. He needs fresh water and 5. ________ (he) food bowl must be cleaned regularly. When he gets sick, we take him to the vet, which sometimes 6. ________ (cost) a lot of money. Last winter, Lucky caught a cold and we were all very 7. ________ (worry) about him.

Despite all the hard work, Lucky has become an important member of our family. He greets us 8. ________ (happy) when we come home and always makes us laugh with his funny behavior. Studies show that having a pet can reduce stress and help people feel 9. ________ (little) lonely.

If you want to get a pet, think 10. ________ (care) before making the decision. A pet is not a toy — it is a life that depends on you.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **most popular**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。among the + 最高级，popular 为多音节形容词，最高级为 most popular。

2. **with**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。come with 意为"伴随着"，此处指"养宠物伴随着巨大的责任"。

3. **ago**
   - **考点**：时间副词（无提示词填空）。two years ago 意为"两年前"，ago 与一般过去时连用。

4. **taking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词短语 taking care of a dog 在句中作主语。

5. **his**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 food bowl，he → his。

6. **costs**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）+ 主谓一致。which 引导非限制性定语从句，代替前面整个事件，视为单数；描述经常性情况用一般现在时。

7. **worried**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be worried about 意为"担心……"，worried 作表语修饰人的感受。

8. **happily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 greets，需用副词形式 happily。

9. **less**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。little 的比较级为 less，less lonely 意为"不那么孤独"。

10. **carefully**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 think，需用副词形式 carefully。
