# 03-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：太空探索 (Space Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever dreamed of traveling to space? For centuries, humans have looked up at the sky and wondered 1. ________ is out there. Today, space exploration has become one of the most exciting fields in science.

China has made great 2. ________ (achieve) in space technology. In 2003, Yang Liwei 3. ________ (successful) flew into space, becoming the first Chinese astronaut. Since then, China 4. ________ (send) many astronauts into space and even built its own space station.

Living in space is very different from living on Earth. Astronauts have to learn 5. ________ (eat) and sleep in zero gravity. Everything floats in the air, 6. ________ even simple tasks become quite difficult. They also need to do exercise every day to keep their 7. ________ (body) healthy.

Space exploration is not only about 8. ________ (discover) new planets. The technology developed for space travel has also improved our daily lives. For example, the water purification system used by astronauts is now helping people on Earth get 9. ________ (clean) drinking water.

Many young people dream of becoming astronauts. Although the journey to space is full 10. ________ challenges, the spirit of exploration will continue to push humans forward.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **what**
   - **考点**：连接代词（无提示词填空）。wondered 后接宾语从句，what 在从句中作主语，意为"什么东西在外面"。

2. **achievements**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。great 修饰名词，achieve → achievement，且 achievements 用复数表示"多项成就"。

3. **successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 flew，需用副词形式 successfully。

4. **has sent**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。since then 提示用现在完成时，主语 China 是第三人称单数。

5. **to eat**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。learn to do sth. 意为"学习做某事"。

6. **so**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前因后果关系：一切都漂浮在空中，"所以"简单的任务也变得困难。用 so 表示因果。

7. **bodies**
   - **考点**：名词复数。their 提示为复数含义（多位宇航员），body → bodies。

8. **discovering**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 about 后接动名词形式。

9. **cleaner**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。根据语境"获得更干净的饮用水"（比以前更干净），用比较级 cleaner。

10. **of**
    - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be full of 意为"充满……"，是固定搭配。
