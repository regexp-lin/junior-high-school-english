# 12-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校食堂 (School Canteen)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The school canteen is where most students eat lunch every day, so the quality of its food matters a great deal. Our school canteen (1) ______ (undergo) a major upgrade last summer. The old building was torn down and replaced with a modern dining hall that can seat over 800 students at (2) ______ time. The menu has also been completely redesigned. Instead of offering the same few dishes every week, the canteen now provides a (3) ______ (rotate) menu with different options each day. Students can choose from Chinese stir-fries, noodles, dumplings and even some Western-style meals like pasta and salads. The most (4) ______ (welcome) change is the addition of a fresh fruit station, (5) ______ students can pick up free seasonal fruit after their meal. Our head teacher said that the school wanted to make sure students received (6) ______ (balance) nutrition to support their studies. A nutritionist was hired to plan the weekly menus, (7) ______ (ensure) that every meal includes the right mix of protein, vegetables and carbohydrates. Not everyone was happy at first. Some students complained that their favourite junk food (8) ______ (remove) from the menu. My friend Xiao Ming missed the fried chicken so much that he almost cried. However, after a few weeks, most students (9) ______ (get) used to the new menu. Many even admitted that they felt more (10) ______ (energy) during afternoon classes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：underwent**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`last summer` 提示过去时间，`undergo` 的过去式为 `underwent`。

2. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`at a time` 为固定搭配，表示"一次"。

3. **答案：rotating**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`a rotating menu` 表示"轮换菜单"。

4. **答案：welcome**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`The most welcome change`，`welcome` 本身可作形容词。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。引导定语从句，先行词 `station` 为地点。

6. **答案：balanced**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`balanced nutrition` 表示"均衡营养"。

7. **答案：ensuring**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表目的或伴随。

8. **答案：had been removed**
   - **考点**：过去完成时的被动语态。抱怨时食物已被移除，用过去完成时被动语态。

9. **答案：got**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。`after a few weeks` 后叙述结果。

10. **答案：energetic**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`felt more energetic`，系动词后接形容词。
