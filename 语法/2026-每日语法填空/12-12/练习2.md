# 12-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：森林保护 (Forest Conservation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Forests are often called the "lungs of the Earth" because they produce the oxygen we breathe and absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. They are also home (1) ______ millions of plant and animal species. However, deforestation — the large-scale (2) ______ (remove) of trees — is destroying forests at an alarming rate. Every year, an area of forest roughly the size of Portugal (3) ______ (disappear). The main causes of deforestation include farming, logging and urban expansion. When forests are cut down, the animals that live there lose their homes. Many species have already been pushed to the edge of (4) ______ (extinct). Deforestation also worsens climate change because trees store carbon. When they are burned or cut down, the stored carbon (5) ______ (release) into the atmosphere. China has taken significant action to address this problem. The country has launched the world's (6) ______ (large) tree-planting programme, (7) ______ (aim) to increase forest coverage from 22% to 26% by 2035. Millions of volunteers participate in tree-planting events every spring. Our school has been involved in this effort (8) ______ 2018. Each year, our class plants about fifty trees in a nearby mountain area. Watching the saplings grow taller year after year is (9) ______ (incredible) rewarding. I believe that protecting our forests is one of the most urgent tasks (10) ______ (face) humanity today.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`be home to` 表示"是……的家园"。

2. **答案：removal**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the large-scale removal of trees`，定冠词后接名词。

3. **答案：disappears**
   - **考点**：主谓一致。`an area of forest` 为第三人称单数，`Every year` 提示一般现在时。

4. **答案：extinction**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `of` 后接名词。

5. **答案：is released**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the stored carbon` 与 `release` 之间是被动关系。

6. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the world's largest tree-planting programme`。

7. **答案：aiming**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表目的或伴随状态。

8. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词。`since 2018` 表示"自 2018 年以来"。

9. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `rewarding` 需要副词。

10. **答案：facing**
    - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`the most urgent tasks facing humanity`，主动关系。
