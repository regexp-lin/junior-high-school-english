# 07-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：一个特别的生日 (A Special Birthday)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My fourteenth birthday was the most special one I have ever had. That morning, I woke up and found a card on my desk. It (1) ______ (write) by my father, who usually does not express his feelings (2) ______ (direct). The card said he was proud of the person I was becoming. I was so (3) ______ (touch) that tears almost came to my eyes. At school, my best friends (4) ______ (secret) decorated my desk with balloons and small gifts. I could not believe they had done all this (5) ______ me. During lunch, the whole class sang "Happy Birthday," and our teacher brought (6) ______ big chocolate cake. After school, my family took me to an escape room, (7) ______ we had to solve puzzles together to get out. It was much (8) ______ (hard) than we expected, but we managed to escape with only two minutes left! At dinner, my mother cooked all of my favourite (9) ______ (dish). I felt lucky to (10) ______ (surround) by so many people who loved me. That birthday taught me that the best gifts are not things — they are moments shared with the people we care about.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was written**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。卡片"被写"，叙述过去事件，主语 `It` 为单数。

2. **答案：directly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `express` 需要副词，`direct` 变为 `directly`。

3. **答案：touched**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be touched` 表示"被感动"，过去分词作形容词描述人的感受。

4. **答案：secretly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `decorated` 需要副词，`secret` 变为 `secretly`（秘密地）。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`do sth for sb` 表示"为某人做某事"。

6. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`a big chocolate cake` 表示"一个大巧克力蛋糕"，泛指。

7. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `an escape room`（地点），在非限制性定语从句中作地点状语。

8. **答案：harder**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than we expected` 提示用比较级，`hard` 的比较级为 `harder`。

9. **答案：dishes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`all of my favourite dishes` 表示"我所有喜欢的菜"，用复数。

10. **答案：be surrounded**
    - **考点**：被动语态。`be surrounded by` 表示"被……包围"，`to` 后接动词原形的被动形式 `be surrounded`。
