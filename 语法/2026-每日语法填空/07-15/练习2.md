# 07-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：共享经济 (The Sharing Economy)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In recent years, the sharing economy (1) ______ (change) the way people live. Shared bikes, shared cars, and even shared power banks can (2) ______ (find) in most Chinese cities. The idea is simple: instead of buying something you only use (3) ______ (occasion), you can rent it at a low cost. This not only saves money (4) ______ also reduces waste. Take shared bikes (5) ______ an example. Before they appeared, many people drove short distances, (6) ______ added to traffic jams and air pollution. Now, people can pick up a bike with their phone and ride it to the nearest subway station. The (7) ______ (convenient) is amazing. However, the sharing economy also faces challenges. Some users do not take good care of shared items. Broken bikes (8) ______ (lie) on sidewalks have become a common sight in some areas. Experts say that both companies and users should take (9) ______ (responsible) for keeping shared resources in good condition. I believe the sharing economy will become even (10) ______ (popular) in the future if these problems are solved.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：has changed**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`In recent years` 表示从过去到现在的时间段，用现在完成时；`the sharing economy` 为第三人称单数。

2. **答案：be found**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`can` 后接动词原形，共享物品"被发现/找到"，用 `be found`。

3. **答案：occasionally**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `use` 需要副词，`occasion` 变为 `occasionally`（偶尔）。

4. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：连词。`not only...but also...` 为固定搭配。

5. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：介词。`take...as an example` 为固定搭配，表示"以……为例"。

6. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面的事情（开车走短途），在从句中作主语。

7. **答案：convenience**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`The` 后接名词作主语，`convenient` 变为 `convenience`（便利性）。

8. **答案：lying**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`broken bikes lying on sidewalks` 表示"躺在人行道上的坏自行车"，`lie` 的现在分词为 `lying`。

9. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`take responsibility for` 为固定搭配，表示"为……承担责任"。

10. **答案：more popular**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`even + 比较级` 表示"甚至更……"，`popular` 为多音节形容词，比较级为 `more popular`。
