# 11-23 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国神话故事 (Chinese Mythology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Chinese mythology is full of fascinating stories that have been told (1) ______ thousands of years. These tales reflect the wisdom, values and imagination of ancient Chinese people. One of the (2) ______ (popular) myths is the story of Hou Yi shooting down the suns. According to the legend, there were once ten suns in the sky, and the extreme heat (3) ______ (cause) terrible droughts and suffering. A brave archer named Hou Yi climbed to the top of a mountain and shot down nine of the suns, (4) ______ (save) the earth and its people. Another well-known myth is the story of the Monkey King, Sun Wukong. He is a magical monkey (5) ______ was born from a stone. He possesses incredible strength and can transform (6) ______ (he) into 72 different forms. His adventures are recorded in the classic novel "Journey to the West," which (7) ______ (write) by Wu Cheng'en in the 16th century. These mythological stories are not just entertaining — they also teach important (8) ______ (lesson) about courage, loyalty and justice. Many Chinese festivals and customs have their (9) ______ (root) in mythology. For example, the Dragon Boat Festival is linked to the story of Qu Yuan, a patriotic poet who drowned himself in a river. I think it is important for young people (10) ______ (know) these stories because they are a vital part of our cultural heritage.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for thousands of years` 表示持续时间。

2. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：最高级。`One of the most popular myths`。

3. **答案：caused**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述神话中的过去事件。

4. **答案：saving**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表示结果的伴随状语。

5. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导定语从句，先行词为 `monkey`（拟人化），在从句中作主语。

6. **答案：himself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`transform himself into` 表示"把自己变成"。

7. **答案：was written**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`which` 指代小说，与 `write` 之间是被动关系，`in the 16th century` 提示过去时。

8. **答案：lessons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`important lessons`，`teach` 后接复数名词。

9. **答案：roots**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`have their roots in` 表示"根源于"。

10. **答案：to know**
    - **考点**：不定式。`It is important for sb to do sth`，不定式作真正主语。
