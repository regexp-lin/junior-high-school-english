# 11-23 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：露营 (Camping)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Camping is a wonderful way to escape the noise of the city and get closer to nature. Last weekend, my family went camping at a lake about two hours' drive (1) ______ our home. We arrived in the afternoon and set up our tent beside the lake. The scenery was (2) ______ (absolute) breathtaking — the water was crystal clear, and the surrounding mountains (3) ______ (cover) with colourful autumn leaves. After setting up the tent, my father taught me how to build a campfire. He showed me the correct way of (4) ______ (arrange) the wood so that the fire could burn steadily. We grilled sausages and corn over the fire for dinner. Everything tasted much (5) ______ (good) than it would at home, probably because of the fresh air and the beautiful surroundings. As night fell, we sat around the campfire and looked up at the sky. Without the city lights, the stars were (6) ______ (clear) visible than I had ever seen them. My mother pointed out several constellations, (7) ______ (include) the Big Dipper and Orion. I was amazed at (8) ______ many stars there were. In the morning, I was woken up (9) ______ the sound of birds singing. I unzipped the tent and stepped outside into the cool, fresh air. It was one of the most peaceful (10) ______ (morning) of my life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`two hours' drive from our home` 表示"距离我们家两小时车程"。

2. **答案：absolutely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `breathtaking` 需要副词形式。

3. **答案：were covered**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the mountains` 与 `cover` 之间是被动关系，`be covered with`。

4. **答案：arranging**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词形式。

5. **答案：better**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than it would at home` 提示比较级，`good` 的比较级为 `better`。

6. **答案：more clearly**
   - **考点**：比较级。`than I had ever seen them` 提示比较级，副词 `clearly` 的比较级。

7. **答案：including**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`including` 作介词，表示"包括"。

8. **答案：how**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`how many stars there were`，`how` 修饰 `many`。

9. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`be woken up by` 表示"被……吵醒"。

10. **答案：mornings**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`one of the most peaceful mornings`，"最……之一"后接复数。
