# 04-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：良好习惯的养成 (Developing Good Habits)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Good habits are important for everyone. They can help us live a 1. ________ (health) and happier life. But developing good habits is not always easy — it takes time and effort.

One of the most important habits is getting up early. People 2. ________ get up early usually have more time to prepare for the day. They can have a good breakfast, review their lessons and arrive at school 3. ________ time.

Another good habit is reading before bed. Instead of 4. ________ (play) with your phone, try picking up a book. Reading helps your brain relax and 5. ________ (actual) improves the quality of your sleep. Even fifteen minutes of reading can make a difference.

Exercising 6. ________ (regular) is also very important. You don't have to go to the gym — even a short walk after dinner counts. The key is to make it 7. ________ part of your daily routine.

On the other hand, bad habits like staying up late, eating too much junk food and 8. ________ (spend) hours on social media can be harmful. These habits are easy 9. ________ (form) but hard to break.

Scientists say it takes about 21 days to build a new habit. So if you want to change, start with one small habit and stick to it. You will be 10. ________ (surprise) at the results.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **healthier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。and 连接并列比较级（healthier and happier），暗含比以前更健康。

2. **who/that**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 people，在从句中作主语。

3. **on**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。on time 意为"准时"，是固定搭配。

4. **playing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。instead of 后接动名词形式。

5. **actually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 improves，需用副词形式 actually。

6. **regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动名词 exercising 或整个句子，需用副词形式 regularly。

7. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。part 为可数名词，a part of 意为"……的一部分"。

8. **spending**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。and 连接并列成分，与前面的 staying、eating 并列。

9. **to form**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。be easy to do 意为"容易做某事"，不定式作原因状语。

10. **surprised**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be surprised at 意为"对……感到惊讶"，主语是人用 -ed 形式。
