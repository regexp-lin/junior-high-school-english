# 04-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：节约用水 (Saving Water)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Water is one of the most 1. ________ (value) resources on Earth. Although about 70% of the Earth's surface is covered 2. ________ water, only a very small part of it is fresh water that we can use.

In many parts of the world, people are suffering from a serious shortage of water. Some families have to walk for 3. ________ (hour) just to get a bucket of clean water. Without enough water, crops cannot grow, and people may get sick from 4. ________ (drink) dirty water.

China is also facing water problems. The population is large, but the amount of fresh water per person is much 5. ________ (low) than the world average. In some northern cities, people 6. ________ (experience) water shortages for many years.

So what can we do to save water? First, turn 7. ________ the tap when you brush your teeth or wash your hands. A running tap can waste several liters of water per minute. Second, take shorter 8. ________ (shower) instead of baths. Third, collect rainwater and use it 9. ________ (water) plants.

Every drop of water counts. Saving water is not just the government's job — it is the duty of each 10. ________ every one of us. Let's act now before it is too late.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词/动词→形容词）。most + 形容词构成最高级，value → valuable。

2. **with/by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be covered with/by 意为"被……覆盖"。

3. **hours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。for hours 意为"好几个小时"，hour 用复数。

4. **drinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 from 后接动名词形式。

5. **lower**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，low → lower。

6. **have been experiencing / have experienced**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成进行时/现在完成时）。for many years 提示动作持续到现在，用现在完成进行时或现在完成时。

7. **off**
   - **考点**：副词/动词短语（无提示词填空）。turn off 意为"关掉"，是固定搭配。

8. **showers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。shorter showers 意为"更短的淋浴"，shower 用复数。

9. **to water**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。use it to water plants 意为"用它来浇植物"，不定式表目的。

10. **and**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。each and every one of us 意为"我们每一个人"，each and every 是强调搭配。
