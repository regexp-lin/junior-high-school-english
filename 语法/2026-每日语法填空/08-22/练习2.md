# 08-22 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：萤火虫之夜 (A Night of Fireflies)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During a visit to my grandparents' village last summer, I experienced something (1) ______ (magic). After sunset, my cousin and I walked to the edge of the rice fields. The air was warm and still, and the sky was filled (2) ______ stars. Suddenly, tiny lights began to flicker in the darkness. Fireflies! There were (3) ______ (hundred) of them, dancing above the water like moving stars. I had only seen fireflies in (4) ______ (picture) before, so I was completely amazed. My cousin caught one gently in his hands and showed me the soft green glow (5) ______ (come) from its body. He explained that fireflies produce light through a chemical reaction, not heat. That is (6) ______ their light feels cold to the touch. We sat on the bank for nearly an hour, (7) ______ (watch) the fireflies move in patterns. My grandmother told us that fireflies used to be much more (8) ______ (common) when she was young. Pollution and the use of pesticides have (9) ______ (great) reduced their numbers. She said we were lucky to still see so many. That night, as I lay in bed, I thought about how important it is (10) ______ (protect) nature so that future generations can enjoy the same beauty.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：magical**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`something + 形容词`（不定代词后置形容词），`magic` 变为 `magical`。

2. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be filled with` 为固定搭配。

3. **答案：hundreds**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`hundreds of` 表示"数以百计的"。

4. **答案：pictures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`in pictures` 表示"在图片中"，用复数。

5. **答案：coming**
   - **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。`the glow coming from its body` 表示"从它身体发出的光"。

6. **答案：why**
   - **考点**：连接副词。`That is why...` 为固定句型，表示"那就是……的原因"。

7. **答案：watching**
   - **考点**：现在分词作伴随状语。坐在河岸上"观看"萤火虫。

8. **答案：common**
   - **考点**：形容词。`more common` 构成比较级，原形即可。

9. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `reduced` 需要副词，`great` 变为 `greatly`。

10. **答案：to protect**
    - **考点**：不定式。`It is important to do sth` 为固定句型，`it` 为形式主语。
