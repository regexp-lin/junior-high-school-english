# 08-22 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校午餐改革 (School Lunch Reform)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

For years, students in our school complained about the canteen food. The dishes were often too (1) ______ (oil), the vegetables were overcooked, and the choices were limited. Last September, the school decided to make some changes. A new chef (2) ______ (hire) to design a healthier and more balanced menu. Now, every meal includes at least three kinds of vegetables, one protein dish, and fresh fruit. The food is cooked (3) ______ less oil and salt, which makes it much (4) ______ (healthy) than before. At first, some students missed the old fried dishes and felt the new food was (5) ______ (taste). But after a few weeks, most of them got used to the lighter flavours. A survey conducted last month showed that 85 percent of students were (6) ______ (satisfy) with the new menu. Several students even said they had lost weight and felt more (7) ______ (energy). The canteen has also set up a suggestion box (8) ______ students can recommend new dishes. Our class suggested adding hand-made dumplings, and the chef agreed (9) ______ (try) it next week. I think the lunch reform is one of the (10) ______ (good) decisions our school has made this year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：oily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`too + 形容词`，`oil` 变为 `oily`（油腻的）。

2. **答案：was hired**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。新厨师"被聘请"。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`cooked with less oil` 表示"用更少的油烹饪"。

4. **答案：healthier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than before` 提示用比较级。

5. **答案：tasteless**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 后缀。`taste` 加 `-less` 变为 `tasteless`（无味的）。

6. **答案：satisfied**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be satisfied with` 表示"对……满意"。

7. **答案：energetic**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`felt more energetic` 中 `energy` 变为 `energetic`（精力充沛的）。

8. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a suggestion box`，在定语从句中作状语（在建议箱中）。

9. **答案：to try**
   - **考点**：不定式。`agree to do sth` 为固定搭配。

10. **答案：best**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`good` 的最高级为 `best`。
