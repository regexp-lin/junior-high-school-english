# 09-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：英语角 (English Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Every Wednesday afternoon, our school holds an English Corner in the garden. Students from different (1) ______ (grade) come together to practise their spoken English. The English Corner (2) ______ (set) up by our English teacher, Mr. Zhang, two years ago. He believed that students needed more (3) ______ (opportunity) to speak English outside the classroom. At first, only a few students joined. Many were too shy to speak English in front of (4) ______. But Mr. Zhang made the atmosphere relaxing and fun by (5) ______ (organise) games, discussions and role plays. Slowly, more students became (6) ______ (confidence) and the group grew larger. Now, about forty students attend every week. Last Wednesday, we discussed the topic "What do you want to be (7) ______ you grow up?" I said I wanted to become a translator because I love languages and enjoy (8) ______ (help) people communicate. My classmate Li Hua wants to be a pilot so that she can travel around the (9) ______ (worldly). The discussion was lively and everyone had a chance to express (10) ______ (they). I think the English Corner has greatly improved my speaking skills and boosted my confidence.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：grades**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`different grades` 表示"不同年级"。

2. **答案：was set**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`was set up by Mr. Zhang`，"被创建"。

3. **答案：opportunities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`more opportunities`，`more` 修饰可数名词用复数。

4. **答案：others**
   - **考点**：代词。`in front of others` 表示"在他人面前"。

5. **答案：organising**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词。

6. **答案：confident**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`became confident`，系动词后接形容词，`confidence` 变为 `confident`。

7. **答案：when**
   - **考点**：连词。`when you grow up` 表示"当你长大时"。

8. **答案：helping**
   - **考点**：动名词。`enjoy doing sth`，`enjoy` 后接动名词。

9. **答案：world**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`around the world` 为固定搭配，`worldly` 变回名词 `world`。

10. **答案：themselves**
    - **考点**：反身代词。主语为 `everyone`（可用 `themselves` 回指），`express themselves` 表示"表达自己"。
