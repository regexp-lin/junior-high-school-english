# 09-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：垃圾分类 (Rubbish Sorting)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Rubbish sorting has become a hot topic in China. In 2019, Shanghai became the first city (1) ______ (introduce) strict rubbish sorting rules. Since then, more and more cities (2) ______ (follow) its example. Under the new rules, household waste must be separated (3) ______ four categories: recyclable waste, kitchen waste, hazardous waste and other waste. At first, many people found it (4) ______ (confuse) and didn't know which bin to use. Our community set up volunteers at rubbish stations to help residents sort (5) ______ (they) waste correctly. My mother was one of the volunteers. She spent two hours every evening (6) ______ (stand) beside the bins and guiding people. Gradually, most residents got used (7) ______ the new system. The results have been (8) ______ (remark). Our community now recycles three times (9) ______ much waste as before, and the amount of waste sent to landfills has decreased (10) ______ (significant). I believe that rubbish sorting is not just a rule — it is a responsibility that every citizen should take seriously.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to introduce**
   - **考点**：不定式。先行词被序数词 `the first` 修饰，后接不定式作定语。

2. **答案：have followed**
   - **考点**：时态。`since then` 提示用现在完成时。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`be separated into` 表示"被分成"。

4. **答案：confusing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`found it confusing`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式，`it` 为形式宾语。

5. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`sort their waste`，修饰名词用物主代词。

6. **答案：standing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth`。

7. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`get used to` 表示"习惯于"，`to` 为介词。

8. **答案：remarkable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`have been remarkable`，系动词后接形容词，`remark` 变为 `remarkable`。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：倍数表达。`three times as much as before`，倍数 + `as...as` 结构。

10. **答案：significantly**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `decreased` 需要副词，`significant` 变为 `significantly`。
