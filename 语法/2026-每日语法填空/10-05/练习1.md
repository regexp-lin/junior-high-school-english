# 10-05 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：探险公园 (Adventure Park)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, our class went to an adventure park as part of our team-building activity. The park was located in a forest about 30 kilometres (1) ______ the city. When we arrived, a professional trainer gave us safety instructions and helped us put (2) ______ helmets and harnesses. The first challenge was a rope bridge high above the ground. Looking down from the platform, I felt my legs (3) ______ (shake). "Don't look down — just look ahead," the trainer advised. I took a deep breath and stepped onto the bridge. It swung (4) ______ (wild) at first, but I soon found my balance. (5) ______ I reached the other side, my classmates cheered loudly for me. The most (6) ______ (thrill) activity was the zip line. We had to slide along a cable from one treetop to (7) ______. As I flew through the air, I felt a rush of (8) ______ (free) that I had never experienced before. Some activities required teamwork. In one challenge, we had to work together (9) ______ (climb) a 10-metre wall using only ropes and each other's support. No one could do it alone, so we learned to trust and help one (10) ______. The trip was both exciting and meaningful.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`30 kilometres from the city` 表示"距离城市30公里"。

2. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`put on` 表示"穿上"。

3. **答案：shaking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`felt my legs shaking`，感官动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词。

4. **答案：wildly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `swung` 需要副词，`wild` 变为 `wildly`。

5. **答案：When / After**
   - **考点**：连词。`When/After I reached the other side`，表时间。

6. **答案：thrilling**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most thrilling activity`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

7. **答案：another**
   - **考点**：代词。`from one treetop to another` 表示"从一个树顶到另一个"。

8. **答案：freedom**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a rush of freedom`，介词 `of` 后接名词，`free` 变为 `freedom`。

9. **答案：to climb**
   - **考点**：不定式。`work together to climb`，不定式表目的。

10. **答案：another**
    - **考点**：固定搭配。`one another` 表示"彼此"。
