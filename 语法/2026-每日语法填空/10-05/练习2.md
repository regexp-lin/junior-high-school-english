# 10-05 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：独自在家 (Home Alone)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Friday, my parents had to travel to another city for work, so I stayed at home alone for the first time. Before (1) ______ (leave), Mum left me a list of things to do and enough food in the fridge. She also reminded me (2) ______ (lock) all the doors and windows at night. At first, I felt excited about having the whole house to (3) ______ (I). I could watch TV, eat snacks and stay up as late as I wanted! But when evening came and the house grew (4) ______ (quietly), I started to feel a little scared. Every small sound made my heart beat (5) ______ (fast). I heard the wind blowing outside and the old pipes making strange (6) ______ (noise). To calm myself down, I called my best friend on the phone and we talked for nearly an hour. She told me funny stories (7) ______ made me laugh and forget my fear. Before going to bed, I checked all the locks twice and left a light (8) ______ in the hallway. The next morning, I woke up feeling (9) ______ (pride) of myself for getting through the night. When my parents came home, I told them about my experience. Dad smiled and said, "You're (10) ______ (grow) up." It was a small adventure, but it helped me become more independent.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：leaving**
   - **考点**：动名词。`before leaving`，介词 `before` 后接动名词。

2. **答案：to lock**
   - **考点**：不定式。`remind sb to do sth` 表示"提醒某人做某事"。

3. **答案：myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`having the house to myself` 表示"独享房子"，主语为 `I`。

4. **答案：quiet**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the house grew quiet`，`grow` 作系动词后接形容词，`quietly` 变为 `quiet`。

5. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：比较级。`made my heart beat faster`，暗含比平时快。

6. **答案：noises**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`strange noises`，多种声音。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `funny stories`（物），引导定语从句。

8. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：副词。`left a light on` 表示"让灯开着"。

9. **答案：proud**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feeling proud of myself`，`pride` 变为形容词 `proud`。

10. **答案：growing**
    - **考点**：时态。`You're growing up` 用现在进行时，表示"你在长大"。
