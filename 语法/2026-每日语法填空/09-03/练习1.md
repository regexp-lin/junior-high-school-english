# 09-03 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：公交车上的温暖 (Warmth on a Bus)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

One rainy evening last week, I took the bus home after school. The bus was (1) ______ (crowd) and there were no empty seats. I stood near the door, holding the handrail (2) ______ (tight). At the next stop, an elderly woman carrying a heavy bag got on. She looked very tired and had difficulty (3) ______ (stand) steadily. I noticed that nobody offered her a seat. Just as I was about (4) ______ (ask) someone to move, a young man in the back stood up and said, "Please sit here, Grandma." The old woman smiled and thanked (5) ______. What happened next surprised me even (6) ______ (much). A little girl, who was no older than six, took out a tissue from her pocket and (7) ______ (hand) it to the old woman to dry her wet hair. The woman was deeply (8) ______ (touch) and said, "What a kind girl you are!" The whole bus fell (9) ______ (silence) for a moment, and then several passengers smiled at each other. It was a small incident, (10) ______ it filled the bus with warmth on that cold, rainy night.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：crowded**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`The bus was crowded`，系动词后接形容词，`crowd` 变为过去分词形容词 `crowded`。

2. **答案：tightly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `holding` 需要副词，`tight` 变为 `tightly`。

3. **答案：standing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`have difficulty (in) doing sth` 是固定搭配。

4. **答案：to ask**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be about to do sth` 表示"即将做某事"。

5. **答案：him**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`thanked him`，指代前文的 `a young man`，用宾格。

6. **答案：more**
   - **考点**：比较级。`surprised me even more`，`even` 修饰比较级 `more`。

7. **答案：handed**
   - **考点**：时态。与 `took out` 并列，用一般过去时 `handed`。

8. **答案：touched**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was deeply touched`，过去分词表被动或表示人的感受。

9. **答案：silent**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`fell silent`，`fall` 作系动词，后接形容词，`silence` 变为 `silent`。

10. **答案：but**
    - **考点**：连词。`a small incident but it filled...`，前后形成转折。
