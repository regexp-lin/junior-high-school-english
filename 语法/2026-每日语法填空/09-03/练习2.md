# 09-03 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：太空探索 (Space Exploration)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Humans have always been curious about what lies beyond the Earth. Since the (1) ______ (begin) of the Space Age in the 1950s, scientists have made remarkable progress in space exploration. China's space programme (2) ______ (grow) rapidly in recent years. In 2003, Yang Liwei became the first Chinese astronaut (3) ______ (travel) into space. Since then, China (4) ______ (send) many more astronauts to space and has built its own space station. The Mars rover Zhurong, (5) ______ landed on Mars in 2021, was another major achievement. These (6) ______ (success) have made Chinese people very proud. However, space exploration is not easy. It requires huge amounts of money, advanced (7) ______ (technological) and years of preparation. Astronauts must train (8) ______ (extreme) hard to prepare for the challenges of living in space, such as zero gravity and (9) ______ (limit) living space. Despite the difficulties, many young people dream of becoming astronauts. I am one of (10) ______. I believe that with hard work and determination, anything is possible.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：beginning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the beginning of`，`begin` 变为名词 `beginning`。

2. **答案：has grown**
   - **考点**：时态。`in recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

3. **答案：to travel**
   - **考点**：不定式。先行词被序数词 `the first` 修饰时，后接不定式作定语。

4. **答案：has sent**
   - **考点**：时态。`since then` 提示用现在完成时。

5. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，先行词为 `Zhurong`。

6. **答案：successes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`these successes`，`these` 提示用复数，`success` 作可数名词时指"成功的事"。

7. **答案：technology**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`advanced technology`，形容词修饰名词，`technological` 变为名词 `technology`。

8. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰副词 `hard` 需要副词，`extreme` 变为 `extremely`。

9. **答案：limited**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`limited living space`，过去分词作形容词修饰名词，`limit` 变为 `limited`。

10. **答案：them**
    - **考点**：代词宾格。`one of them`，介词 `of` 后接宾格，指代 `many young people`。
