# 08-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：新能源汽车 (New Energy Vehicles)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

New energy vehicles, especially electric cars, are becoming (1) ______ (increase) popular in China. In 2023, China sold more electric cars than any other country in the world. The (2) ______ (govern) has been encouraging people to buy electric cars by offering subsidies and building more charging stations. My uncle bought an electric car last year. He says it is much (3) ______ (cheap) to run than his old petrol car because electricity costs less (4) ______ fuel. The car is also very quiet — you can (5) ______ (hard) hear the engine when it is running. However, electric cars still have some (6) ______ (limit). The biggest concern is the range — most models can only travel 300 to 500 kilometres (7) ______ a single charge. For people who need to drive long (8) ______ (distant), this can be a problem. Finding a charging station in rural areas is also (9) ______ (difficulty) than in cities. Despite these challenges, experts believe that electric cars (10) ______ (replace) most petrol cars within the next twenty years.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `popular` 需要副词，`increase` 变为 `increasingly`（越来越）。

2. **答案：government**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`govern` 变为 `government`。

3. **答案：cheaper**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than his old petrol car` 提示用比较级。

4. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较连词。`costs less than fuel` 构成比较级结构。

5. **答案：hardly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`can hardly hear` 表示"几乎听不到"，`hard` 变为 `hardly`（几乎不）。

6. **答案：limitations**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`some` 修饰可数名词复数，`limit` 变为 `limitation`。

7. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`on a single charge` 表示"一次充电"。

8. **答案：distances**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`long distances` 表示"远距离"，`distant` 变为 `distance`，用复数。

9. **答案：more difficult**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than in cities` 提示用比较级。

10. **答案：will replace**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。`within the next twenty years` 表示将来。
