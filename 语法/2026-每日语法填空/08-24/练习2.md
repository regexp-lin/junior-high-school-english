# 08-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：友谊的考验 (A Test of Friendship)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Zhang Wei and I have been friends (1) ______ we were in kindergarten. We have always done everything together — from homework to sports to playing video games. But last term, our friendship faced its biggest test. It all started when we both (2) ______ (choose) to run for class monitor. At first, we thought it would be fun to compete (3) ______ each other. But as the election got closer, things became tense. Some classmates tried to set us (4) ______ each other by spreading rumours. I heard that Zhang Wei had told others I was not (5) ______ (suit) for the position. I was so angry that I refused (6) ______ (talk) to him for three days. The truth came out during the election debate. Zhang Wei publicly said that I was the most (7) ______ (responsibility) person in our class and that he would support me if I won. I felt (8) ______ (terrible) guilty for doubting him. After the election — which he won — I congratulated him (9) ______ (sincere). He smiled and said, "No election is worth (10) ______ (lose) a friend." We are closer now than ever. The experience taught us both that true friendship can survive any challenge.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since we were in kindergarten` 与现在完成时搭配。

2. **答案：were chosen**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。我们"被选中"参加竞选。

3. **答案：against / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`compete against/with each other` 表示"互相竞争"。

4. **答案：against**
   - **考点**：介词。`set sb against sb` 表示"挑拨离间"。

5. **答案：suitable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`be suitable for` 表示"适合"，`suit` 变为 `suitable`。

6. **答案：to talk**
   - **考点**：不定式。`refuse to do sth` 为固定搭配。

7. **答案：responsible**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most responsible` 构成最高级，`responsibility` 变为 `responsible`。

8. **答案：terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `guilty` 需要副词，`terrible` 变为 `terribly`。

9. **答案：sincerely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `congratulated` 需要副词，`sincere` 变为 `sincerely`。

10. **答案：losing**
    - **考点**：动名词。`be worth doing sth` 为固定搭配，表示"值得做某事"。
