# 10-09 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：旧物改造 (Upcycling Old Items)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Upcycling means turning old or useless items (1) ______ something new and valuable. Unlike recycling, which breaks materials down, upcycling gives them a new purpose (2) ______ (with) destroying them. Our art teacher introduced us to upcycling last term, and it has become one of my favourite (3) ______ (hobby). So far, I (4) ______ (turn) old jeans into a pencil case, made a lamp from glass bottles and created a bookshelf from wooden crates. The most (5) ______ (challenge) project was building a birdhouse out of an old shoebox. I had to measure, cut and glue everything (6) ______ (careful). It took me three afternoons to finish, but the result was (7) ______ (satisfy). I hung the birdhouse in our garden, and within a week, a pair of sparrows moved in! My mother was (8) ______ (impress) and asked me to make one for her friend as well. Our teacher says that upcycling is not only (9) ______ (create) but also good for the environment. By giving new life to old things, we reduce waste and save resources. I encourage everyone (10) ______ (try) upcycling — you might be surprised at what you can create from things you were about to throw away.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`turn sth into sth` 表示"把……变成……"。

2. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`without destroying them` 表示"不破坏它们"。

3. **答案：hobbies**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`one of my favourite hobbies`。

4. **答案：have turned**
   - **考点**：时态。`so far` 提示用现在完成时。

5. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most challenging project`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

6. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `measure, cut and glue` 需要副词。

7. **答案：satisfying**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the result was satisfying`，修饰事物/结果用 `-ing` 形式。

8. **答案：impressed**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was impressed`，表示人的感受用 `-ed` 形式。

9. **答案：creative**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is not only creative but also...`，`create` 变为形容词 `creative`。

10. **答案：to try**
    - **考点**：不定式。`encourage sb to do sth`。
