# 10-09 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：乘船游览 (A Boat Tour)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

During the summer holiday, my family took a boat tour along the Li River in Guilin. The scenery was the most (1) ______ (beauty) I had ever seen. The river wound between tall limestone hills that rose sharply (2) ______ the water. Some of the hills were covered (3) ______ green trees, while others had bare rock faces in interesting shapes. Our guide told us the (4) ______ (name) and legends behind many of the hills. One hill looked like an elephant (5) ______ (drink) water, and another resembled a young woman looking into a mirror. I was amazed at how nature could create such (6) ______ (wonder) shapes. The water was so clear that we could see fish (7) ______ (swim) below the surface. Along the riverbank, we spotted a few fishermen on bamboo rafts. They used trained cormorant birds (8) ______ (catch) fish — a traditional method that has been passed down for (9) ______ (generation). As the boat moved slowly downstream, I felt completely at peace. The gentle breeze, the quiet water and the beautiful mountains made me forget all my worries. When we finally returned to the dock, I wished the journey (10) ______ (be) longer. It was truly a trip I will remember forever.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most beautiful`，`beauty` 变为形容词 `beautiful`。

2. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`rose sharply from the water` 表示"从水面陡然升起"。

3. **答案：with / in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be covered with/in` 表示"被……覆盖"。

4. **答案：names**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`the names and legends`，多个名字。

5. **答案：drinking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`an elephant drinking water`，现在分词作后置定语。

6. **答案：wonderful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `shapes` 需要形容词，`wonder` 变为 `wonderful`。

7. **答案：swimming**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`see fish swimming`，感官动词 + 宾语 + 现在分词。

8. **答案：to catch**
   - **考点**：不定式。`use sth to do sth` 表示"用某物做某事"。

9. **答案：generations**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`for generations` 表示"世世代代"。

10. **答案：were / had been**
    - **考点**：虚拟语气。`wished the journey were/had been longer`，`wish` 后从句用虚拟语气。
