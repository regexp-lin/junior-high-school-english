# 06-02 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：考试焦虑与应对 (Exam Anxiety and Coping)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

It is normal (1) ______ feel a little nervous before an important exam, but some students worry so much (2) ______ they cannot sleep well. Experts suggest taking deep breaths when you feel your heart (3) ______ (beat) fast. You should also make a study plan weeks ahead instead of (4) ______ (stay) up late at the last minute. Talking to someone you trust is helpful, too. Remember that exams only check (5) ______ you have learned, not how smart you are. If you prepare carefully, you will find the questions (6) ______ (easy) than you expected. Never compare yourself with others all the time, (7) ______ it will increase your pressure. After the exam, reward yourself (8) ______ something small and tell yourself that you have done (9) ______ (you) best. With a calm mind and steady effort, exam anxiety can (10) ______ (control) step by step.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**  
   - **考点**：固定句型。`It is + adj. + to do sth.` 中，`it` 为形式主语，真正主语为不定式，故填 `to`，与 `feel` 构成 `to feel`。

2. **答案：that**  
   - **考点**：连词。`so... that...` 引导结果状语从句，意为“如此……以至于……”。看到 `so much` 后接从句，应想到 `that`。

3. **答案：beating**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`feel + 宾语 + doing` 表示“感觉到……正在……”，心脏“正在跳得快”，用现在分词 `beating`。`feel sb./sth. do` 表全过程，此处强调当时状态，用 `beating` 更自然。

4. **答案：staying**  
   - **考点**：非谓语动词。`instead of` 为介词，后接动名词，故用 `staying`。`stay up late` 为固定短语“熬夜”。

5. **答案：what**  
   - **考点**：宾语从句连接词。`check` 后接宾语从句，从句中 `learned` 缺宾语，表示“所学的内容”，用连接代词 `what`。`that` 在从句中不充当成分，此处不适用。

6. **答案：easier**  
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。后有 `than`，用 `easy` 的比较级 `easier`，作宾语补足语（`find + 宾语 + 形容词`）。

7. **答案：because / as / since**  
   - **考点**：连词。后半句说明“不要总和别人比较”的原因，表原因用 `because`（或 `as`/`since`）。

8. **答案：with**  
   - **考点**：介词。`reward sb. with sth.` 表示“用某物奖励某人”，固定搭配。

9. **答案：your**  
   - **考点**：代词。`do one's best` 中 `one's` 需与主语 `you` 一致，用形容词性物主代词 `your`。

10. **答案：be controlled**  
    - **考点**：动词语态。`anxiety` 与 `control` 为被动关系（焦虑被控制），`can` 后接动词原形，故为 `be controlled`。
