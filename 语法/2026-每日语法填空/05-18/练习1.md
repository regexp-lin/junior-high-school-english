# 05-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：无障碍设施 (Accessible Facilities)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

An accessible city cares about everyone, including people 1. ________ wheelchairs, the elderly, and parents with baby strollers. In recent years, more ramps, elevators, and tactile paving 2. ________ (build) in public places such as subway stations, libraries, and parks.

Last Sunday, I went shopping with my aunt, 3. ________ leg was injured in an accident last winter. At first, I worried that the old mall would be hard for her to enter. 4. ________, we found a new slope at the main entrance and wide doors that opened 5. ________ (automatic). Inside, accessible restrooms were clearly marked, and staff members were ready to offer help.

My aunt said she felt 6. ________ (welcome) than before because she could move around without asking strangers for favors all the time. Her words made me realize that friendly design is not 7. ________ extra decoration — it shows respect for every citizen.

Our community will hold a meeting next month to discuss 8. ________ local streets can be improved further. Students are invited to share ideas, and I plan to suggest adding more resting benches and lowering the height of 9. ________ (certain) service windows.

Small changes can make life 10. ________ (easy) for people with different needs. Building an inclusive society starts with details we often overlook.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **in**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。people in wheelchairs 表示"坐轮椅的人们"，为固定搭配。

2. **have been built**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（现在完成时的被动语态）。In recent years 与现在完成时连用，facilities 与 build 为被动，主语为并列复数概念，用 have been built。

3. **whose**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 my aunt，在从句中作定语修饰 leg，用 whose。

4. **However**
   - **考点**：连接副词（无提示词填空）。上文担心，下文发现便利设施，转折用 However，句首大写。

5. **automatically**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 opened，用副词 automatically。

6. **more welcome**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than before 提示比较级；feel welcome 表示"感到受欢迎"，welcome 作形容词时比较级用 more welcome。

7. **an**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。extra 以元音音素开头，not an extra decoration 表示"并非多余的装饰"。

8. **how**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。discuss how local streets can be improved 表示讨论"如何进一步改善"当地街道，how 引导宾语从句。

9. **certain**
   - **考点**：形容词作定语。括号中所给 certain 为形容词，修饰 service windows，表示"某些服务窗口"，此处无需改变词形。

10. **easier**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。make life easier 使生活更容易，easy → easier。
