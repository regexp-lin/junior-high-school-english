# 05-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：校园图书角 (School Reading Corner)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

At the end of the corridor on the second floor, there is a small reading corner 1. ________ (run) by our student union. Shelves are filled with novels, magazines, and popular science books, and anyone may borrow them 2. ________ free during break time.

The corner was set up last term after several classmates raised the idea 3. ________ a class meeting. We collected second-hand books, painted the walls light green, and placed four comfortable chairs near the window. A volunteer on duty checks the notebook 4. ________ students sign their names and return dates.

I usually go there on Wednesday afternoons. Last week, I picked up a book about space exploration and read 5. ________ quietly for half an hour. The quiet atmosphere helps me focus, and I finish homework 6. ________ (fast) than I do at home.

Sometimes, students leave sticky notes inside the books 7. ________ (share) thoughts with the next reader. I wrote one yesterday, saying that the story about teamwork moved me deeply.

Our head teacher praised the project and promised 8. ________ (buy) more new books if we keep the corner tidy. She also reminded us to respect reading rules and keep our voices 9. ________ (low) when others are studying.

I am proud that a simple corner can bring so much warmth to the school. I hope more reading spaces 10. ________ (appear) in other grades soon.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **run**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。a reading corner run by... 过去分词短语作后置定语，表被动"由学生会管理的图书角"。

2. **for**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。for free 免费，固定短语。

3. **at**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。at a class meeting 表示"在班会课上"，说明提出想法的场合。

4. **where**
   - **考点**：关系副词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 the notebook，在从句中作地点状语，学生在本子上签名，用 where。

5. **it**
   - **考点**：代词（无提示词填空）。指代上文 a book about space exploration，read it quietly。

6. **faster**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。than I do at home 提示比较级，修饰动词 finish，用 faster。

7. **to share**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作目的状语）。leave notes... to share thoughts 表示"留下便签以便分享感想"，用不定式表目的。

8. **to buy**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。promise to do sth. 承诺做某事，填 to buy。

9. **low**
   - **考点**：形容词（原级）。keep our voices low 为"宾语 + 形容词"结构，low 作宾语补足语，说明 voices 的状态，括号内 low 保持原形即可。

10. **will appear**
    - **考点**：动词时态（一般将来时）。I hope 后的宾语从句中，soon 表示不久将来，用 will appear 表示"将会出现更多阅读空间"。
