# 07-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我心中的英雄 (My Hero)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

When people talk about heroes, they often think of famous (1) ______ (person). But to me, the greatest hero is my father. He works as a firefighter and has been doing this dangerous job (2) ______ over fifteen years. Every time he receives an emergency call, he rushes to the scene (3) ______ hesitation. I used to worry about him a lot, especially after (4) ______ (hear) news about firefighters who got hurt. One winter night, a big fire broke (5) ______ in a building near our home. My father was one of the first people to arrive. He (6) ______ (carry) two children out of the burning building and helped put out the flames. When he came home the next morning, his face was covered (7) ______ black smoke marks. I gave him a big hug and told him he was the (8) ______ (brave) person in the world. He just smiled and said, "I am only doing (9) ______ (I) job." His words made me (10) ______ (realise) that true heroes do not look for praise — they simply do what is right.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：people**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`famous` 修饰可数名词，`person` 的复数形式为 `people`。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for over fifteen years` 表示"超过十五年"，`for` 接一段时间。

3. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`without hesitation` 表示"毫不犹豫地"。

4. **答案：hearing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`after` 为介词，后接动名词 `hearing`。

5. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词。`break out` 为固定搭配，表示"（火灾、战争等）爆发"。

6. **答案：carried**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的英勇行为，`carry` 变 y 为 i 加 `-ed`。

7. **答案：with / in**
   - **考点**：介词。`be covered with/in` 表示"被……覆盖"。

8. **答案：bravest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the + 最高级 + 名词 + in the world`，`brave` 的最高级为 `bravest`。

9. **答案：my**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 `job`，`I` 的物主代词为 `my`。

10. **答案：realise**
    - **考点**：动词原形。`make sb + 动词原形` 为使役动词结构。
