# 07-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：夏夜乘凉 (Cool Summer Evenings)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Summer evenings in southern China can be very hot, so many families like to sit outside after dinner to enjoy the cool breeze. When I was young, my grandparents had no air conditioner, (1) ______ they used bamboo fans to keep cool. Every evening, neighbours would gather under the old banyan tree in front of (2) ______ (we) house. The grown-ups chatted about daily life (3) ______ the children chased fireflies on the grass. My grandmother often held me on her lap and told me folk tales. One of the stories I remember (4) ______ (clear) is about Chang'e flying to the moon. I always asked her to tell it again and again, and she never got tired (5) ______ repeating it. Those evenings were some of the (6) ______ (happy) moments of my childhood. Today, most families stay inside with air conditioning, and fewer people spend time (7) ______ (talk) to their neighbours. I think something (8) ______ (value) has been lost. Last summer, I tried sitting outside with my family after dinner. We watched the stars and listened to the insects (9) ______ (sing). It was surprisingly (10) ______ (enjoy), and my parents promised to do it more often.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：因果连词。"没有空调"是原因，"用竹扇"是结果，用 `so`。

2. **答案：our**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 `house`，`we` 变为 `our`。

3. **答案：while**
   - **考点**：连词。`while` 表示"与此同时"，大人聊天的同时孩子在追萤火虫。

4. **答案：clearly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `remember` 需要副词，`clear` 变为 `clearly`。

5. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`get tired of` 为固定搭配，表示"对……感到厌烦"。

6. **答案：happiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`some of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`happy` 变为 `happiest`。

7. **答案：talking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time (in) doing sth` 为固定搭配。

8. **答案：valuable**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`something + 形容词`（不定代词后置形容词），`value` 变为 `valuable`。

9. **答案：singing**
   - **考点**：现在分词作宾补。`listen to sb/sth doing` 表示"听到……正在做"，强调进行中的动作。

10. **答案：enjoyable**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`was` 后接形容词作表语，`enjoy` 变为 `enjoyable`（令人愉快的）。
