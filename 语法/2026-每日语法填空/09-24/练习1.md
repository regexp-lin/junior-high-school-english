# 09-24 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：望远镜与星空 (Telescopes and the Night Sky)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Since ancient times, humans have been fascinated (1) ______ the night sky. Early astronomers observed the stars with nothing but their eyes. Everything changed when the telescope (2) ______ (invent) in the early 17th century. With this powerful tool, scientists could see much (3) ______ (far) into space than ever before. For my birthday last year, my parents gave me a small telescope. I was so excited that I set it up on the balcony that very (4) ______ (night). The first thing I saw was the moon. Through the telescope, I could clearly see the craters on (5) ______ (it) surface. It was an (6) ______ (absolute) amazing sight. Over the following weeks, I learned (7) ______ (identify) several planets and constellations. I could spot Jupiter and its (8) ______ (moon) on clear nights, and I once saw Saturn's rings, which left me (9) ______ (speech). My science teacher says that looking at the stars helps us understand how small we are in the universe. I agree. Every time I gaze through my telescope, I feel a deep sense of wonder and (10) ______ (curious) about what lies beyond our planet.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：by / with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be fascinated by/with` 表示"对……着迷"。

2. **答案：was invented**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the telescope was invented`，望远镜"被发明"。

3. **答案：farther / further**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much farther/further into space`，`far` 的比较级。

4. **答案：night**
   - **考点**：名词。`that very night` 表示"就在那天晚上"。

5. **答案：its**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`its surface`，`it` 的形容词性物主代词为 `its`。

6. **答案：absolutely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `amazing` 需要副词，`absolute` 变为 `absolutely`。

7. **答案：to identify**
   - **考点**：不定式。`learn to do sth` 表示"学会做某事"。

8. **答案：moons**
   - **考点**：名词复数。木星有多颗卫星，`moon` 用复数。

9. **答案：speechless**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`left me speechless`，`leave + 宾语 + 形容词`，`speech` 加 `-less` 表"说不出话的"。

10. **答案：curiosity**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `wonder` 并列作宾语，需要名词，`curious` 变为 `curiosity`。
