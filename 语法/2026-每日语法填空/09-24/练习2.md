# 09-24 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校科学实验 (School Science Experiment)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Science experiments are one of the things I enjoy most about school. Last Thursday, our chemistry teacher, Mr. Fang, showed us how to make a simple volcano using baking soda and vinegar. He (1) ______ (begin) by placing a small bottle in the centre of a tray. Then he asked us to build a mountain of clay around the bottle, (2) ______ (leave) the top open. When everything was ready, he poured some baking soda (3) ______ the bottle and added a few drops of red food colouring. "Now watch (4) ______ (careful)," he said, as he slowly poured vinegar into the bottle. Within seconds, red foam started bubbling out of the top, just like a real volcano (5) ______ (erupt)! The whole class cheered with (6) ______ (excite). Mr. Fang then explained the science behind it. He told us that when baking soda (7) ______ (mix) with vinegar, a chemical reaction produces carbon dioxide gas, (8) ______ causes the bubbling effect. He also reminded us that safety is the (9) ______ (important) thing when doing experiments. We should always wear goggles and never taste any chemicals. I think hands-on experiments like this make science much more (10) ______ (interest) than just reading about it in a textbook.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：began**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时，`begin` 的过去式为 `began`。

2. **答案：leaving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`leaving the top open` 作伴随状语。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`poured baking soda into the bottle` 表示"把小苏打倒入瓶中"。

4. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `watch` 需要副词，`careful` 变为 `carefully`。

5. **答案：erupting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`like a volcano erupting`，现在分词表示正在喷发。

6. **答案：excitement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `with` 后接名词，`excite` 变为 `excitement`。

7. **答案：is mixed**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`when baking soda is mixed with vinegar`，小苏打"被混合"。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导，指代化学反应产生的二氧化碳。

9. **答案：most important**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the most important thing`。

10. **答案：interesting**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`much more interesting`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。
