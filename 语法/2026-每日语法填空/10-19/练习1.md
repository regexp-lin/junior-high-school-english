# 10-19 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：光盘行动 (Clean Your Plate Campaign)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Food waste is a serious problem in China. According to statistics, Chinese people waste about 35 million tonnes of food every year, which is enough (1) ______ (feed) 200 million people. To address this issue, the "Clean Your Plate" campaign (2) ______ (launch) a few years ago. The campaign encourages people to order only (3) ______ they can eat and to take leftovers home. Our school has been (4) ______ (active) participating in this campaign. In the canteen, posters remind students (5) ______ (treasure) every grain of rice. A "Clean Plate Monitor" is assigned to each table during lunch to check (6) ______ students finish their food. At first, some students (7) ______ (complain) about the strict rules. They argued that it was their own business how much food they ate. However, our head teacher explained that wasting food is not just a personal matter — it has (8) ______ (society) and environmental consequences. Growing food requires water, land and energy. When food is wasted, all those resources are wasted too. After understanding this, most students changed their (9) ______ (behave). Now, leftovers in our canteen have decreased by over 60%. I am proud to be part of a school that takes food waste (10) ______ (serious).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to feed**
   - **考点**：不定式。`enough to feed`，`enough + to do` 结构。

2. **答案：was launched**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`the campaign was launched`，活动"被发起"。

3. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`order only what they can eat`，`what` 引导宾语从句。

4. **答案：actively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `participating` 需要副词。

5. **答案：to treasure**
   - **考点**：不定式。`remind sb to do sth`。

6. **答案：whether / if**
   - **考点**：连词。`check whether/if students finish their food`。

7. **答案：complained**
   - **考点**：时态。`at first` 暗示过去的情况，用一般过去时。

8. **答案：social**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `consequences` 需要形容词，`society` 变为 `social`。

9. **答案：behaviour**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`changed their behaviour`，`behave` 变为名词 `behaviour`。

10. **答案：seriously**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`takes food waste seriously`。
