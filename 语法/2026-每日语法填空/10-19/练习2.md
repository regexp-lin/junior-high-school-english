# 10-19 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学校消防演练 (School Fire Drill)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Fire drills are held at our school twice a year to make sure students know how to react in case of a fire. Last Tuesday, we had our autumn fire drill. At exactly 10:00 a.m., the fire alarm (1) ______ (go) off. Although we knew it was just a drill, the sudden loud sound still made some students (2) ______ (feel) anxious. Our class teacher told us to stay calm and follow the (3) ______ (evacuate) procedures we had learned. We quickly formed a line and walked (4) ______ (quiet) towards the nearest emergency exit. We were not allowed (5) ______ (use) the lifts — only the stairs. Some students covered their noses and mouths with wet towels, as we had been taught to do in a real fire. We reached the school playground in less (6) ______ three minutes. All 1,200 students and 80 teachers gathered at the designated meeting points. Each class teacher counted (7) ______ (they) students to make sure nobody was left behind. The head teacher praised everyone (8) ______ being orderly and fast. He also reminded us that fire safety is a matter of life and (9) ______ (die) and should never be treated as a joke. Thanks to regular drills, I now feel (10) ______ (confidence) that I would know what to do in a real emergency.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：went**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时，`go off`（响起）的过去式为 `went off`。

2. **答案：feel**
   - **考点**：动词原形。`make sb do sth`，使役动词后接动词原形。

3. **答案：evacuation**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the evacuation procedures`，`evacuate` 变为名词 `evacuation` 作定语。

4. **答案：quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `walked` 需要副词。

5. **答案：to use**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be not allowed to do sth` 表示"不被允许做某事"。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。`in less than three minutes` 表示"不到三分钟"。

7. **答案：their**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`counted their students`。

8. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`praised everyone for being orderly`。

9. **答案：death**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`life and death`，与 `life` 并列，`die` 变为名词 `death`。

10. **答案：confident**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`feel confident`，系动词后接形容词，`confidence` 变为 `confident`。
