# 03-21 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：网络安全 (Internet Safety)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Internet has changed our lives in many ways. It makes it much 1. ________ (easy) for us to get information, communicate with friends and even shop online. However, the Internet can also be 2. ________ (danger) if we don't use it carefully.

One of the biggest problems is personal information safety. Some websites may ask you 3. ________ (provide) your real name, phone number or home address. You should never share this information with 4. ________ (stranger), because it may be used by bad people.

Another problem is Internet addiction. Many teenagers spend hours 5. ________ (play) online games or watching short videos. They find it hard to stop even 6. ________ they know it is bad for their eyes and study. This is not a healthy way to use the Internet.

So how can we stay safe online? First, always use strong passwords and change 7. ________ (they) regularly. Second, don't believe everything you read online. Not all information on the Internet is 8. ________. Some of it may be false or misleading. Third, set a time limit for yourself so that you won't spend too 9. ________ (many) time on your phone.

The Internet is a useful 10. ________ (invent), but we must learn to use it wisely and safely.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **easier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。much 修饰比较级，easy 的比较级为 easier。句型 make it easier for sb. to do sth.。

2. **dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。be 后接形容词作表语，danger 的形容词形式为 dangerous。

3. **to provide**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。ask sb. to do sth. 意为"要求某人做某事"。

4. **strangers**
   - **考点**：名词复数。根据语境此处泛指"陌生人们"，用复数 strangers。

5. **playing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 意为"花时间做某事"。

6. **when/though**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。even when/even though 意为"即使"，引导让步状语从句。

7. **them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。动词 change 后接宾语，代替前面的 passwords，用代词宾格 them。

8. **true**
   - **考点**：形容词（无提示词填空）。is 后接形容词作表语，根据下文"有些可能是虚假或误导的"，此处填 true 意为"真实的"。

9. **much**
   - **考点**：词形辨析。time 为不可数名词，应用 much 修饰，many 修饰可数名词复数，需改为 much。

10. **invention**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。a 后接可数名词单数，invent 的名词形式为 invention。
