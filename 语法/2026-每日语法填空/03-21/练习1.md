# 03-21 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中国传统文化 (Traditional Chinese Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China has a long history and rich culture. Many traditional arts 1. ________ (pass) down from generation to generation for thousands of years.

Paper cutting is one of the oldest folk arts in China. It 2. ________ (usual) uses red paper because red is a symbol of good luck in Chinese culture. During the Spring Festival, people like to put paper cuttings 3. ________ their windows to celebrate the new year.

Chinese calligraphy is another important part of traditional culture. It is not just about 4. ________ (write) characters — it is also considered a form of art. Many students begin to learn calligraphy at 5. ________ early age. Practicing calligraphy can help people become more patient and 6. ________ (peace).

Tea culture also has a special place in Chinese life. China is the 7. ________ (birth) of tea, and drinking tea has been a tradition for over 4,000 years. Different areas produce different 8. ________ (kind) of tea. When guests visit, offering a cup of tea is a way to show 9. ________ (friend) and respect.

Today, more and more young people are becoming interested in traditional culture. They believe it is 10. ________ (they) duty to protect and spread these valuable traditions to the world.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **have been passed**
   - **考点**：被动语态（现在完成时）。arts 与 pass down 之间是被动关系，结合 for thousands of years 表示延续到现在，用现在完成时被动语态。

2. **usually**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 uses，需用副词形式 usually。

3. **on**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。put sth. on the window 意为"把某物贴在窗户上"，用介词 on 表示在表面上。

4. **writing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 about 后接动名词形式。

5. **an**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。at an early age 意为"在很小的年纪"，early 以元音音素开头，用不定冠词 an。

6. **peaceful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。and 连接并列成分，前面 patient 是形容词，后面也需填形容词，peace → peaceful。

7. **birthplace**
   - **考点**：词性转换/构词法（名词合成）。根据句意"中国是茶的发源地"，birth 需加 place 构成 birthplace（发源地）。

8. **kinds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。different 修饰可数名词复数，kind → kinds，different kinds of 意为"不同种类的"。

9. **friendliness**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）。show 后接名词作宾语，friendly 的名词形式为 friendliness，意为"友善"。注意 friend 的形容词形式是 friendly，其名词形式为 friendliness。

10. **their**
    - **考点**：代词（形容词性物主代词）。修饰名词 duty，需用形容词性物主代词 their。
