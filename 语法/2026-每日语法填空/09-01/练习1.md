# 09-01 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：开学第一天 (The First Day of School)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

September 1st is a special day for students across China — it is the first day of the new school year. This morning, I woke up much (1) ______ (early) than usual because I was too excited to sleep. After a quick breakfast, I put on my new school uniform and headed (2) ______ school. The campus looked fresh and clean. New flowers (3) ______ (plant) along the paths, and bright banners hung above the main gate. I walked into my new classroom and found that I (4) ______ (seat) next to a girl I had never met before. Her name was Lin Yue, and she had just (5) ______ (move) to our city from Chengdu. She seemed a little nervous, (6) ______ I introduced myself and offered to show her around. She smiled and thanked me for being so (7) ______ (friend). Our head teacher, Ms. Wang, gave an opening speech, (8) ______ (encourage) us to set clear goals and work hard throughout the year. She also reminded us (9) ______ the importance of teamwork and mutual respect. I left school feeling positive and (10) ______ (energy). I believe this is going to be a great year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：earlier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much earlier than usual`，`much` 修饰比较级。

2. **答案：for / to**
   - **考点**：介词。`headed for/to school` 表示"前往学校"。

3. **答案：had been planted**
   - **考点**：被动语态。花"被种植"，且发生在"我到达"之前，用过去完成时被动。也可接受 `were planted`。

4. **答案：was seated**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`be seated` 表示"被安排坐在"。

5. **答案：moved**
   - **考点**：时态。`had just moved`，过去完成时，表示在"我遇到她"之前已搬来。

6. **答案：so**
   - **考点**：连词。`She seemed nervous, so I introduced myself`，`so` 表因果关系。

7. **答案：friendly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`being so friendly`，`friend` 变为形容词 `friendly`。

8. **答案：encouraging**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`gave a speech, encouraging us...`，现在分词作伴随状语。

9. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`remind sb of sth` 表示"提醒某人某事"。

10. **答案：energetic**
    - **考点**：词性转换。与 `positive` 并列作表语，需要形容词，`energy` 变为 `energetic`。
