# 09-01 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：博物馆之旅 (A Trip to the Museum)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Saturday, our history teacher took us to the city museum. It was a trip that everyone in our class had been looking forward (1) ______. The museum is one of the (2) ______ (large) in our province, with over 50,000 items on display. When we entered the main hall, we were amazed (3) ______ the ancient bronze vessels and jade ornaments. Our guide, a young woman (4) ______ (name) Ms. Zhou, explained the history behind each exhibit in a very (5) ______ (live) way. She told us that some of the items (6) ______ (date) back more than 3,000 years. I was particularly interested in a set of Terracotta (7) ______ (figure) from the Qin Dynasty. They were (8) ______ (incredible) detailed — you could even see the expressions on their faces. My friend Xiao Ming liked the dinosaur fossil section (9) ______ (much) of all. He spent almost an hour there, taking photos and reading every piece of information on the wall. The visit helped us (10) ______ (understand) our country's history much better than any textbook could. I hope we can go again soon.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`look forward to` 是固定搭配，`to` 为介词。

2. **答案：largest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the largest`，用最高级。

3. **答案：by / at**
   - **考点**：介词。`be amazed by/at sth` 表示"对……感到惊讶"。

4. **答案：named**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。`a young woman named Ms. Zhou`，过去分词作后置定语，表"被叫做"。

5. **答案：lively**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`in a lively way` 表示"以生动的方式"，`live` 变为 `lively`（形容词）。

6. **答案：dated**
   - **考点**：时态。`told us that...dated back...`，宾语从句中用一般过去时。

7. **答案：figures**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a set of figures`，一组俑用复数。

8. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `detailed` 需要副词，`incredible` 变为 `incredibly`。

9. **答案：most**
   - **考点**：副词最高级。`most of all` 表示"最……"，`much` 的最高级为 `most`。

10. **答案：understand**
    - **考点**：动词原形。`help sb (to) do sth`，`help` 后接省略 `to` 的不定式。
