# 04-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：自信的力量 (The Power of Confidence)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Confidence is very important for success. 1. ________ (have) confidence means believing in yourself and your abilities. It doesn't mean you are perfect — it means you are brave enough to try.

I used to be a very shy girl. Whenever the teacher asked a question in class, I knew the answer but was afraid 2. ________ raising my hand. I worried that others would laugh at me if I made a 3. ________ (miss).

Everything changed when I joined the speech contest last year. My English teacher encouraged me 4. ________ (sign) up, though I was 5. ________ (extreme) nervous at first. I practiced my speech every day for two weeks. My mother listened to me practice and gave me useful 6. ________ (suggest).

On the day of the contest, my hands were shaking when I stood on the stage. But as soon as I started 7. ________ (speak), I felt a wave of calmness. I looked at the audience and spoke clearly and loudly. To my surprise, I won 8. ________ (three) place!

That experience taught 9. ________ an important lesson: confidence comes from preparation and practice. The more prepared you are, the 10. ________ (confident) you will feel.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **Having**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。动名词 Having 在句首作主语，注意首字母大写。

2. **of**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be afraid of doing sth. 意为"害怕做某事"。

3. **mistake**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。make a mistake 意为"犯错误"，miss → mistake。

4. **to sign**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。encourage sb. to do sth. 意为"鼓励某人做某事"。

5. **extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰形容词 nervous，需用副词形式 extremely。

6. **suggestions**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。useful 修饰名词，suggest → suggestion → suggestions（可数名词复数）。

7. **speaking**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。start doing sth. 意为"开始做某事"。

8. **third**
   - **考点**：序数词。win third place 意为"获得第三名"，three → third。

9. **me**
   - **考点**：代词宾格（无提示词填空）。taught 后接间接宾语，根据上下文填 me。

10. **more confident**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。the more...the more... 句型，confident 为多音节形容词，比较级为 more confident。
