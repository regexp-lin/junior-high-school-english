# 04-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：绿色出行 (Green Transportation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

With the rapid 1. ________ (develop) of cities, traffic problems have become more and more serious. Heavy traffic not only wastes our time 2. ________ also pollutes the air. That is why many cities are now encouraging green transportation.

Riding a bicycle is one of the 3. ________ (green) ways to travel. It produces no pollution at all. In recent years, shared bikes have appeared on the streets of many Chinese 4. ________ (city). People can rent a bike easily by scanning a code on their phones. It is cheap, convenient and good for 5. ________ (we) health.

Public transportation is another good choice. Buses and subways can carry 6. ________ (hundred) of passengers at a time, which greatly reduces the number of cars on the road. If more people choose to take public transportation, the air quality 7. ________ (improve) a lot.

Some people have started using electric cars. These cars are powered 8. ________ electricity instead of gasoline. They are much 9. ________ (quiet) than traditional cars and produce less pollution.

Of course, the best way to reduce traffic is to walk whenever possible. Walking is the 10. ________ (simple) form of exercise, and it doesn't cost anything. Let's all do our part to make our cities cleaner and our lives healthier.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **development**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。the 和 of 之间需填名词，develop → development。

2. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。not only...but also... 意为"不仅……而且……"，是固定关联词组。

3. **greenest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数，green → greenest。

4. **cities**
   - **考点**：名词复数。many 修饰可数名词复数，city → cities。

5. **our**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 health，we → our。

6. **hundreds**
   - **考点**：数词用法。hundreds of 意为"数百的"，是固定搭配，hundred 需加 -s。

7. **will improve**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般将来时）。if 引导的条件状语从句用一般现在时，主句用一般将来时。

8. **by**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be powered by 意为"由……驱动"。

9. **quieter**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，quiet → quieter。

10. **simplest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，simple → simplest。
