# 09-04 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：养宠物的责任 (Responsibility of Keeping Pets)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Many teenagers want to keep pets, but not everyone understands the (1) ______ (responsible) that comes with it. Last year, my parents finally agreed to let me have a dog. I was (2) ______ (thrill) and named him Lucky. At first, everything seemed perfect. Lucky was cute and (3) ______ (play), and I enjoyed spending time with him. However, I soon realised that taking care of (4) ______ dog is a lot of work. I have to walk him twice a day, (5) ______ matter how tired I am or how bad the weather is. I also need to feed him (6) ______ (regular), clean his living area and take him to the vet for check-ups. One day, Lucky got sick and I was extremely (7) ______ (worry). I stayed up all night looking after him until he (8) ______ (get) better. That experience made me understand (9) ______ it truly means to be responsible for another life. Now, I treat Lucky not just as a pet but as a family member. He has taught me patience, love and the importance of keeping (10) ______ (promise). If you want a pet, make sure you are ready for the commitment.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the responsibility`，冠词后接名词，`responsible` 变为 `responsibility`。

2. **答案：thrilled**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was thrilled`，表示人的情感，用 `-ed` 形式。

3. **答案：playful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `cute` 并列作表语，需要形容词，`play` 变为 `playful`。

4. **答案：a**
   - **考点**：冠词。`a dog`，泛指"一只狗"。

5. **答案：no**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`no matter how` 表示"无论多么"。

6. **答案：regularly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `feed` 需要副词，`regular` 变为 `regularly`。

7. **答案：worried**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was extremely worried`，表示人的情感状态，用 `-ed` 形式。

8. **答案：got**
   - **考点**：时态。`until he got better`，叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

9. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。`what it truly means`，`what` 引导宾语从句，在从句中作 `means` 的宾语。

10. **答案：promises**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`keeping promises` 表示"信守承诺"，泛指多个承诺用复数。
