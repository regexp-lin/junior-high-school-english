# 09-04 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学校图书馆改造 (School Library Renovation)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Great news — our school library (1) ______ (renovate) over the summer holiday and reopened last week. The old library was small and dark, with only a few hundred (2) ______ (book) on the shelves. Now, it has been completely transformed (3) ______ a bright, modern learning space. The number of books has increased to over 10,000, covering subjects from science (4) ______ art. There are also comfortable sofas and reading corners (5) ______ students can relax with a good book. One of the most popular additions is the digital reading area. Students can use tablets to access thousands of e-books and online (6) ______ (resource). My classmate Wang Ting was the first person (7) ______ (borrow) a book from the new library. She chose a novel by her favourite (8) ______ (write). "I'm so happy with the new library," she said (9) ______ (cheerful). "It makes me want to read even more." I agree with her completely. A good library can inspire a love of (10) ______ (read) that lasts a lifetime.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：was renovated**
   - **考点**：被动语态。图书馆"被翻新"，`over the summer holiday` 提示过去时，用 `was renovated`。

2. **答案：books**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`a few hundred books`，数量词后接复数。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`be transformed into` 表示"被转变为"。

4. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from science to art` 表示"从科学到艺术"。

5. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `reading corners`（地点），定语从句中缺少地点状语，用 `where`。

6. **答案：resources**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`online resources` 表示"在线资源"，此处泛指多种资源。

7. **答案：to borrow**
   - **考点**：不定式。先行词被序数词 `the first` 修饰，后接不定式作定语。

8. **答案：writer**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`her favourite writer`，`write` 变为表示人的名词 `writer`。

9. **答案：cheerfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `said` 需要副词，`cheerful` 变为 `cheerfully`。

10. **答案：reading**
    - **考点**：动名词。`a love of reading`，介词 `of` 后接动名词。
