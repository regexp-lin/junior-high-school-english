# 04-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：充足睡眠的重要性 (The Importance of Sleep)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Sleep is just as important as eating and exercising. But many teenagers today are not getting enough sleep, and this is 1. ________ (affect) their health and school performance.

Experts say that teenagers need about eight to ten hours of sleep every night. However, a recent survey 2. ________ (show) that more than half of Chinese middle school students sleep less 3. ________ eight hours. The main reasons include heavy homework, playing with phones and staying up late to watch videos.

Lack of sleep can cause many problems. Without enough rest, students may find it hard 4. ________ (concentrate) in class. Their memory becomes 5. ________ (poor), and they are more likely to make mistakes. Some students even fall 6. ________ (sleep) during lessons, which is both embarrassing and harmful to their studies.

Good sleep also affects our emotions. People who don't sleep well often feel 7. ________ (anxiety) or angry. They may get into arguments more 8. ________ (easy) with friends and family members.

Here are some tips for 9. ________ (good) sleep. First, go to bed at the same time every night. Second, avoid looking at screens for at least thirty minutes before bedtime. Third, make your bedroom dark and quiet. Finally, don't drink coffee 10. ________ tea in the evening.

A good night's sleep is the best gift you can give yourself.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **affecting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词/现在进行时）。is affecting 构成现在进行时，表示"正在影响"。

2. **showed/shows**
   - **考点**：动词时态。a recent survey 可用一般过去时 showed（已完成的调查）或一般现在时 shows（陈述调查结果）。

3. **than**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。less than 意为"少于"，是比较结构。

4. **to concentrate**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。find it hard to do sth.，it 为形式宾语。

5. **poorer/worse**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。根据语境"记忆力变差"，poor → poorer 或用 worse。

6. **asleep**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。fall asleep 意为"入睡"，是固定搭配。注意 sleep → asleep。

7. **anxious**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。feel 是系动词，后接形容词作表语，anxiety → anxious。

8. **easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词短语 get into arguments，需用副词形式 easily。

9. **better**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。tips for better sleep 意为"获得更好睡眠的建议"，good → better。

10. **or**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。否定句中用 or 连接并列成分，"不要喝咖啡或茶"。
