# 04-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：逆境与成长 (Adversity and Growth)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Life is not always smooth. Everyone faces difficulties and setbacks at some point. How we deal with these challenges 1. ________ (decide) what kind of person we become.

Thomas Edison is a great example. Before he 2. ________ (success) invented the light bulb, he failed over a thousand times. When someone asked him about his failures, he said, "I have not failed. I have just found 10,000 ways that won't work." His positive attitude toward failure 3. ________ (inspire) millions of people.

As students, we often face difficulties 4. ________ our studies. Some subjects may seem very hard to understand. When we get a low score on an exam, we may feel 5. ________ (disappoint) and want to give up. But remember, every failure is a chance 6. ________ (learn) and improve.

My own experience taught me this lesson. I used to be very weak 7. ________ math. I failed several tests and felt terrible about 8. ________ (I). But instead of giving up, I asked my teacher for help and practiced extra problems every day. After months of hard work, my math grade improved 9. ________ (great).

Difficulties are not our 10. ________ (enemy) — they are our teachers. They make us stronger, wiser and more determined. So next time you face a challenge, smile and say, "I can do this!"

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **decides**
   - **考点**：主谓一致（一般现在时）。主语是 how 引导的主语从句，视为单数，动词加 -s。

2. **successfully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 invented，需用副词形式 successfully。

3. **has inspired**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。Edison 的话从过去到现在一直在激励人们，用现在完成时。

4. **in**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。in our studies 意为"在学习中"。

5. **disappointed**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。feel disappointed 意为"感到失望"，主语是人用 -ed 形式。

6. **to learn**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式作后置定语）。a chance to learn 意为"学习的机会"，不定式作后置定语。

7. **in/at**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be weak in/at 意为"在……方面薄弱"。

8. **myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语是 I，about 后接反身代词 myself，felt terrible about myself 意为"对自己感到很糟糕"。

9. **greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 improved，需用副词形式 greatly。

10. **enemies**
    - **考点**：名词复数。主语 difficulties 是复数，表语也应对应复数，enemy → enemies。
