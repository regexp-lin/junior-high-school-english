# 10-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：无人机应用 (Drone Applications)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Drones, also known as unmanned aerial vehicles, are (1) ______ (rapid) changing many industries. They were originally developed for (2) ______ (military) purposes, but today they are used in agriculture, delivery, photography and even disaster rescue. In farming, drones can spray pesticides over large fields much (3) ______ (fast) than humans. In emergencies, they can deliver medical supplies to areas that are difficult (4) ______ (reach) by road. Last month, our school's technology club bought a small drone and started a drone (5) ______ (fly) course. I was one of the first students (6) ______ (sign) up. Learning to control the drone was trickier (7) ______ I thought. At first, my drone kept crashing into trees and walls. Our instructor, Mr. Jiang, taught us to start with basic movements before attempting (8) ______ (advance) manoeuvres. After two weeks of practice, I could fly the drone (9) ______ (smooth) and even take aerial photos of our campus. The photos looked amazing — the school from above looked completely different from what I was used to (10) ______ (see) every day. I now understand why so many people are excited about drone technology.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rapidly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `changing` 需要副词，`rapid` 变为 `rapidly`。

2. **答案：military**
   - **考点**：形容词。`military purposes`，`military` 本身即为形容词。

3. **答案：faster**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much faster than humans`。

4. **答案：to reach**
   - **考点**：不定式。`difficult to reach`，`be + adj + to do` 结构。

5. **答案：flying**
   - **考点**：动名词/现在分词。`drone flying course`，`fly` 变为 `flying` 作定语。

6. **答案：to sign**
   - **考点**：不定式。先行词被序数词 `the first` 修饰，后接不定式作定语。

7. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较结构。`trickier than I thought`。

8. **答案：advanced**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `manoeuvres` 需要形容词，`advance` 变为 `advanced`。

9. **答案：smoothly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `fly` 需要副词，`smooth` 变为 `smoothly`。

10. **答案：seeing**
    - **考点**：动名词。`be used to doing sth` 表示"习惯于做某事"，`to` 为介词。
