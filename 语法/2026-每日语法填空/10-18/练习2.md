# 10-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：爸爸的故事 (Dad's Story)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

My father doesn't talk much about his childhood, but last Sunday he shared a story that I will never forget. He grew up in a small farming village (1) ______ his family was very poor. As the eldest of four (2) ______ (child), he had to help his parents in the fields before and after school every day. There was no library in the village, and textbooks were the only books he (3) ______ (have) access to. Despite the hard conditions, he was always at the top of his class because he studied (4) ______ (extreme) hard. He told me that he used to read by the light of an oil lamp (5) ______ his family couldn't afford electricity. His biggest dream was (6) ______ (attend) university and change his family's life. When he was eighteen, he (7) ______ (accept) by a university in the city. His parents sold their only cow to pay for his tuition. "That cow paid for my future," he said with tears in (8) ______ (he) eyes. He worked his way through university and eventually became an (9) ______ (engine). Now he makes sure I have everything I need for my education. His story reminds me that I should never take my (10) ______ (opportunity) for granted.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：where**
   - **考点**：关系副词。先行词为 `a small farming village`（地点），定语从句缺地点状语。

2. **答案：children**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`four children`，`child` 的不规则复数。

3. **答案：had**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的情况用一般过去时。

4. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰副词 `hard` 需要副词，`extreme` 变为 `extremely`。

5. **答案：because**
   - **考点**：连词。`read by oil lamp because his family couldn't afford electricity`，因果关系。

6. **答案：to attend**
   - **考点**：不定式。`His dream was to attend university`，不定式作表语。

7. **答案：was accepted**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`was accepted by a university`，"被录取"。

8. **答案：his**
   - **考点**：代词所有格。`tears in his eyes`。

9. **答案：engineer**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`an engineer`，`engine` 变为 `engineer`。

10. **答案：opportunities**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`my opportunities`，多个机会。
