# 10-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：森林防火 (Forest Fire Prevention)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Forest fires are one of the most (1) ______ (destroy) natural disasters. They can spread quickly and cause enormous damage to wildlife, trees and human communities. In China, autumn and spring are the seasons when forest fires are most (2) ______ (like) to occur because the air is dry and the wind is strong. Last October, a forest fire broke (3) ______ in a mountainous area near our city. Firefighters worked day and night for a week (4) ______ (put) it out. The fire (5) ______ (destroy) over 500 hectares of forest and forced hundreds of families to leave their homes. After the fire, our school invited a forest ranger to give a talk about fire (6) ______ (prevent). He told us that most forest fires are caused (7) ______ human carelessness — things like throwing cigarette butts, leaving campfires (8) ______ (unattend) or burning rubbish near the edge of a forest. He said that being careful in nature is everyone's (9) ______ (responsible). He also taught us what to do if we ever find (10) ______ (our) near a forest fire: stay low, cover our nose and mouth, and move against the wind direction to reach safety.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：destructive**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the most destructive disasters`，修饰名词用形容词，`destroy` 变为 `destructive`。

2. **答案：likely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`most likely to occur`，`like` 变为 `likely`。

3. **答案：out**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`break out` 表示"（火灾、战争等）爆发"。

4. **答案：to put**
   - **考点**：不定式。`worked for a week to put it out`，不定式表目的。

5. **答案：destroyed**
   - **考点**：时态。叙述过去的事用一般过去时。

6. **答案：prevention**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`fire prevention`，名词修饰名词，`prevent` 变为 `prevention`。

7. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`are caused by` 表示"由……引起"。

8. **答案：unattended**
   - **考点**：词性转换（加否定前缀）。`leaving campfires unattended`，`attend` 变为过去分词 `unattended`（无人看管的）。

9. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`everyone's responsibility`，所有格后接名词。

10. **答案：ourselves**
    - **考点**：反身代词。`find ourselves near a forest fire`，主语为 `we`。
