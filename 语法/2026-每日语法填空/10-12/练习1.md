# 10-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：学钢琴 (Learning to Play the Piano)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I started learning to play the piano when I was six years old. My mother, who had always dreamed of (1) ______ (be) a pianist, enrolled me in a music school near our home. For the first few years, I practised every day (2) ______ school, even when I was tired. My teacher, Ms. Chen, was strict but fair. She could always tell when I (3) ______ (not practise) enough. "Music requires discipline," she would say. There were times when I wanted to give (4) ______. Practising the same piece over and over again was (5) ______ (bore), and my fingers often ached. But whenever I successfully played a difficult piece, the sense of (6) ______ (achieve) made it all worthwhile. Last year, I performed at the school concert for the first time. I played a piece by Chopin (7) ______ I had practised for three months. Standing on stage under the bright lights, I felt (8) ______ (extreme) nervous. My hands were shaking as I sat down at the piano. But once I started playing, the (9) ______ (nervous) melted away and the music flowed naturally. When I finished, the audience gave me a loud round of applause. That moment was one of the (10) ______ (proud) of my life.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：being**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `of` 后接动名词 `being`。

2. **答案：after**
   - **考点**：介词。`every day after school` 表示"每天放学后"。

3. **答案：hadn't practised**
   - **考点**：时态。`tell when I hadn't practised enough`，过去完成时表在"告诉"之前没有充分练习。

4. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`give up` 表示"放弃"。

5. **答案：boring**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`was boring`，修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

6. **答案：achievement**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the sense of achievement`，介词后接名词，`achieve` 变为 `achievement`。

7. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a piece`（物），引导定语从句。

8. **答案：extremely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `nervous` 需要副词。

9. **答案：nervousness**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the nervousness melted away`，作主语需要名词，`nervous` 变为 `nervousness`。

10. **答案：proudest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the proudest (moments)`，省略了 `moments`。
