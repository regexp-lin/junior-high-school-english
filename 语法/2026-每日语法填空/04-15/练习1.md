# 04-15 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：餐桌礼仪 (Table Manners)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Table manners are an important part of social culture. 1. ________ (know) how to behave properly at the table shows respect for others and makes dining more pleasant for everyone.

In China, there are several table manners that people follow. When you are invited to someone's home for dinner, it is polite to arrive on time. You should wait for the host to tell you where 2. ________ (sit). During the meal, it is considered rude to stick your chopsticks straight up in a bowl of rice, 3. ________ this is associated with funerals.

Western table manners are quite 4. ________ (differ). People use knives and forks instead of chopsticks. The fork is usually held in the left hand and the knife in the 5. ________. It is not polite to put your elbows 6. ________ the table while eating. Also, you should chew your food 7. ________ (quiet) and never talk with your mouth full.

No matter 8. ________ culture you are in, there are some universal rules. Saying "please" and "thank you" is always appreciated. 9. ________ (turn) off your phone during a meal is a sign of respect. And of course, complimenting the cook is a nice thing to do.

Good table manners can leave a 10. ________ (last) impression on others. So next time you sit down for a meal, remember to mind your manners!

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **Knowing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。Knowing how to... 在句首作主语，注意首字母大写。

2. **to sit**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。where to sit 意为"坐在哪里"，疑问词 + 不定式结构。

3. **because/as**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。后面解释原因，用 because 或 as 引导原因状语从句。

4. **different**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词/动词→形容词）。are 后接形容词作表语，differ → different。

5. **right**
   - **考点**：名词（无提示词填空）。与前面 left hand 对应，the right 意为"右手"。

6. **on**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。put your elbows on the table 意为"把手肘放在桌子上"。

7. **quietly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 chew，需用副词形式 quietly。

8. **what/which**
   - **考点**：连接代词（无提示词填空）。no matter what/which culture 意为"无论在哪种文化中"。

9. **Turning**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。Turning off... 在句首作主语，注意首字母大写。

10. **lasting**
    - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。a lasting impression 意为"持久的印象"，last → lasting（作形容词）。
