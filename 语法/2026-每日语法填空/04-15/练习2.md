# 04-15 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：充实的周末 (A Meaningful Weekend)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

How do you usually spend your weekends? Some students stay at home and play video games all day, 1. ________ others choose to do something more meaningful.

Last Saturday, I had one of the 2. ________ (busy) but most enjoyable weekends ever. In the morning, I went to the community center to volunteer. We helped paint the walls of a children's reading room. It was hard work, but 3. ________ (see) the bright colors on the walls made us feel proud.

In the afternoon, I went to the city library with my friend Li Hua. She wanted to borrow some books for a school project about endangered 4. ________ (animal). We spent two hours there, reading and taking notes. The library was so quiet that you could hear a pin 5. ________ (drop).

After the library, we decided to ride our bikes to the park nearby. The weather was perfect — sunny 6. ________ not too hot. We sat under a big tree and talked about our dreams for the future. Li Hua told me that she hoped 7. ________ (become) a marine biologist to protect ocean life. I was deeply 8. ________ (move) by her passion.

In the evening, I helped my mother prepare dinner. We made dumplings together, 9. ________ is a tradition in our family. While eating, I shared my day with my parents. They were happy to hear about my 10. ________ (activity).

A meaningful weekend doesn't need to be expensive or fancy. It just needs to be filled with love, learning and laughter.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **while**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。while 表示对比，"一些学生在家打游戏，而另一些……"。

2. **busiest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。one of the + 最高级，busy → busiest（辅音字母+y 变 i 加 -est）。

3. **seeing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。seeing the bright colors 作主语。

4. **animals**
   - **考点**：名词复数。endangered animals 意为"濒危动物"，用复数。

5. **drop**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（省略 to 的不定式）。hear sb./sth. do sth. 意为"听见某物做某事"，感官动词后接省略 to 的不定式。

6. **but**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。sunny but not too hot 意为"晴朗但不太热"，表转折。

7. **to become**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（不定式）。hope to do sth. 意为"希望做某事"。

8. **moved**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be moved by 意为"被……感动"，主语是人用 -ed 形式。

9. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，which 指代前面整件事"一起包饺子"。

10. **activities**
    - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）+ 复数。hear about my activities 意为"听到我的活动"，active → activity → activities。
