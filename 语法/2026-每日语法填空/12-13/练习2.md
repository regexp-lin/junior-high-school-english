# 12-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：观星 (Stargazing)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Stargazing is one of the oldest human activities. For thousands of years, people have looked up at the night sky with wonder and (1) ______ (curious). Ancient civilisations used the stars to navigate the seas, track the seasons and create calendars. Even today, the beauty of a starry sky can take our breath (2) ______. Unfortunately, light pollution from cities has made it (3) ______ (difficulty) for many people to see the stars clearly. In big cities like Shanghai and Beijing, only the (4) ______ (bright) stars and planets are visible at night. To see the Milky Way, you usually need to travel to a remote area far away (5) ______ city lights. Last summer, my family went on a trip to a dark sky reserve in Qinghai Province. The reserve is one of the (6) ______ (good) places in China for stargazing because there is almost no artificial light. When night fell, I was (7) ______ (complete) amazed by what I saw. Thousands of stars filled the sky, and the Milky Way stretched across the horizon like a glowing river. My father brought a small telescope, and we took turns (8) ______ (look) at Jupiter and its moons. It was incredible to think that the light (9) ______ (reach) our eyes had been travelling through space for years. That night, I lay on the grass and stared at the sky for hours. I felt both incredibly small and deeply (10) ______ (connect) to the universe at the same time.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：curiosity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `wonder` 并列，需要名词。

2. **答案：away**
   - **考点**：副词。`take our breath away` 表示"令人叹为观止"。

3. **答案：difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`made it difficult for people`，`difficulty` 变为形容词。

4. **答案：brightest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`only the brightest stars`。

5. **答案：from**
   - **考点**：介词。`far away from city lights` 表示"远离城市灯光"。

6. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：最高级。`one of the best places`。

7. **答案：completely**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `amazed` 需要副词。

8. **答案：looking**
   - **考点**：动名词。`take turns doing sth` 表示"轮流做某事"。

9. **答案：reaching**
   - **考点**：现在分词作定语。`the light reaching our eyes`，主动关系。

10. **答案：connected**
    - **考点**：过去分词。`deeply connected to the universe`，表示状态。
