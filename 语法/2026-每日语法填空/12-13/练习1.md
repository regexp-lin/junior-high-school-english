# 12-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：送礼文化 (Gift-Giving Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Gift-giving is an important social custom in cultures around the world. In China, giving gifts is a way of showing respect, gratitude and (1) ______ (friend). There are many rules and traditions about gift-giving that Chinese people follow. For example, gifts are usually wrapped in red paper because red (2) ______ (symbol) good luck. It is considered impolite (3) ______ (open) a gift in front of the person who gave it to you. Instead, you should thank the giver and open it (4) ______ (private) later. The amount of money in a red envelope is also important. People avoid giving amounts that include the number four because it sounds like the Chinese word (5) ______ "death." On the other hand, the number eight is considered lucky because it sounds like the word for "wealth." Gift-giving customs differ greatly from country (6) ______ country. In Japan, gifts are always beautifully (7) ______ (wrap), and the wrapping is almost as important as the gift itself. In some Middle Eastern countries, it is (8) ______ (appropriate) to give gifts with the left hand. My English teacher once shared a funny story. When she first came to China, a student gave her an umbrella as a gift. She was pleased until a Chinese friend (9) ______ (tell) her that giving an umbrella can mean "breaking up" because the Chinese word for umbrella sounds like the word for "separation." These cultural (10) ______ (differ) make gift-giving a fascinating topic to study.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：friendship**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `respect` 和 `gratitude` 并列，需要名词。

2. **答案：symbolises**
   - **考点**：词性转换和主谓一致。`red` 作主语为第三人称单数，`symbol` 变为动词。

3. **答案：to open**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It is considered impolite to do sth`。

4. **答案：privately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `open` 需要副词。

5. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`the Chinese word for "death"` 表示"表示'死亡'的中文词"。

6. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`from country to country` 表示"因国而异"。

7. **答案：wrapped**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`are beautifully wrapped`，被动关系。

8. **答案：inappropriate**
   - **考点**：词性转换。根据语境"用左手送礼是不合适的"，加否定前缀。

9. **答案：told**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的事件。

10. **答案：differences**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`These cultural differences`，需要名词复数。
