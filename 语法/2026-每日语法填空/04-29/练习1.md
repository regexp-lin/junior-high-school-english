# 04-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：热带雨林保护 (Protecting Rainforests)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Tropical rainforests are often called the "lungs of the Earth." They cover only about 6% of the Earth's surface, 1. ________ they produce more than 20% of the world's oxygen.

Rainforests are home to an 2. ________ (believe) variety of plants and animals. Scientists estimate that more than half of all living species on Earth can 3. ________ (find) in rainforests. Many of these species have not even been discovered yet. Some plants in rainforests are used to make 4. ________ (medicine) that treat serious diseases.

Sadly, rainforests are 5. ________ (disappear) at an alarming rate. Every year, large areas are cut down for farming, logging and building. This 6. ________ (destroy) not only kills countless plants and animals but also contributes to climate change.

When trees 7. ________ (remove), they can no longer absorb carbon dioxide from the air. This makes global warming 8. ________ (bad) than it already is. The loss of rainforests also affects local communities who depend on them for food, water and shelter.

What can we do to help? First, support 9. ________ (organize) that work to protect rainforests. Second, reduce your use of products that contribute to deforestation, such as palm oil. Third, spread awareness by sharing what you've learned with 10. ________ (other).

Saving the rainforests is saving ourselves.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **but/yet**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后句构成转折："只覆盖6%的面积，但产生超过20%的氧气"。

2. **unbelievable**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。an + 形容词 + 名词，believe → unbelievable（难以置信的），注意 an 提示以元音音素开头。

3. **be found**
   - **考点**：被动语态。can be found 意为"能被发现"，species 与 find 之间是被动关系。

4. **medicines**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→名词）+ 复数。make medicines 意为"制造药物"，medicine → medicines（表示多种药物时可数）。

5. **disappearing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词/现在进行时）。are disappearing 构成现在进行时。

6. **destruction**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）。this destruction 意为"这种破坏"，主语位置需用名词，destroy → destruction。

7. **are removed**
   - **考点**：被动语态（一般现在时）。trees 与 remove 之间是被动关系。

8. **worse**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级（不规则变化）。than 提示用比较级，bad → worse。

9. **organizations**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。support organizations 意为"支持组织"，organize → organization → organizations。

10. **others**
    - **考点**：代词。with others 意为"与其他人"，other → others（代词形式，指"其他人"）。
