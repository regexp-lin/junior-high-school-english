# 04-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：感恩日记 (Gratitude Diary)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Have you ever kept a gratitude diary? It is a diary 1. ________ you write down things you are thankful for. Research shows that 2. ________ (write) a gratitude diary regularly can make you happier and healthier.

I started keeping a gratitude diary six months ago. Every night before bed, I write down three things that I am grateful 3. ________. They don't have to be big things. Sometimes it is a sunny day, a 4. ________ (delight) meal, or a kind word from a friend.

At first, I found it 5. ________ (difficulty) to think of things to be grateful for. I used to focus on problems and complaints — bad grades, strict teachers, too much homework. But as I kept writing, I began to notice the good things in my life that I had been 6. ________ (ignore).

After a few weeks, I noticed a change in 7. ________ (I). I became more positive and patient. When something bad happened, instead of 8. ________ (complain), I tried to find the silver lining. My friends even commented that I seemed 9. ________ (happy) than before.

Keeping a gratitude diary is simple but 10. ________ (power). It trains your brain to focus on the positive side of life. If you haven't tried it yet, I highly recommend starting tonight.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **where**
   - **考点**：关系副词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 a diary，where = in which，在从句中作状语。

2. **writing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词作主语）。writing a gratitude diary 在 that 从句中作主语。

3. **for**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be grateful for 意为"对……感恩"。

4. **delightful**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 meal，delight → delightful。

5. **difficult**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。find it difficult to... 意为"发现做……很困难"，difficulty → difficult。

6. **ignoring**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词/现在分词）。had been ignoring 构成过去完成进行时，意为"一直在忽视"。

7. **myself**
   - **考点**：反身代词。主语是 I，in myself 意为"在我自己身上"。

8. **complaining**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。instead of 后接动名词形式。

9. **happier**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，happy → happier。

10. **powerful**
    - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。but 连接并列成分，与 simple 并列，power → powerful。
