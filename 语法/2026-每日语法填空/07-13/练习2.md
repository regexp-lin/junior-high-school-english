# 07-13 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：学画画 (Learning to Paint)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

I have been interested in painting (1) ______ I was seven years old. That year, my parents sent me to an art class. The teacher, Ms. Lin, was a very (2) ______ (patience) woman. She taught us how to hold a brush and mix (3) ______ (colour). In the beginning, my paintings looked nothing like the real objects. I once drew a cat that everyone (4) ______ (think) was a rabbit! But Ms. Lin never laughed at me. She said that art was not about copying reality — it was about (5) ______ (express) feelings. Her words changed the way I looked (6) ______ painting. I began to paint what I felt instead of what I saw. As I grew (7) ______ (old), I entered several art competitions and won some awards. My (8) ______ (proud) moment was when one of my paintings (9) ______ (choose) to be displayed in the city gallery. Painting has become (10) ______ important part of my life. It helps me relax and gives me a way to share my inner world with others.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：连词。`since I was seven years old` 与现在完成进行时 `have been interested` 搭配，表示"自从……以来"。

2. **答案：patient**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `woman` 需要形容词，`patience` 变为 `patient`（有耐心的）。

3. **答案：colours**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`mix colours` 表示"调色"，颜色有多种，用复数。

4. **答案：thought**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去发生的事情，`think` 的过去式为 `thought`。

5. **答案：expressing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`about` 为介词，后接动名词 `expressing`。

6. **答案：at**
   - **考点**：介词。`look at` 表示"看待"，`the way I looked at painting` 表示"我看待绘画的方式"。

7. **答案：older**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`as I grew older` 表示"随着我长大"，用比较级。

8. **答案：proudest**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`my + 最高级 + 名词` 表示"我最……的"，`proud` 的最高级为 `proudest`。

9. **答案：was chosen**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。画作"被选中"展出，主语为单数，叙述过去事件。

10. **答案：an**
    - **考点**：冠词。`an important part` 中 `important` 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，用 `an`。
