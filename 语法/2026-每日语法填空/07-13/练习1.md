# 07-13 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：减少食物浪费 (Reducing Food Waste)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Food waste is a serious problem around the world. Every year, about one third of all the food (1) ______ (produce) globally is thrown away. In China, the amount of food (2) ______ (waste) in restaurants alone could feed 200 million people. The situation has drawn the (3) ______ (attend) of both the government and the public. Many schools have started "Clean Your Plate" campaigns. In our canteen, students are encouraged (4) ______ (take) only as much food as they can eat. Those (5) ______ finish all their food receive a small sticker as a reward. At home, my family also tries to reduce waste. My mother plans meals (6) ______ (careful) and makes a shopping list before going to the supermarket. She only buys (7) ______ we really need. If there are leftovers, we store (8) ______ (they) in the fridge and eat them the next day. Reducing food waste is not (9) ______ difficult as many people think. It just requires a little planning and a change in our eating (10) ______ (habit).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：produced**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`all the food produced globally` 表示"全球生产的所有食物"，过去分词表被动。

2. **答案：wasted**
   - **考点**：过去分词作后置定语。`the food wasted in restaurants` 表示"在餐厅浪费的食物"。

3. **答案：attention**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`draw the attention of` 表示"引起……的关注"，`attend` 变为 `attention`。

4. **答案：to take**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be encouraged to do sth` 为被动语态中的固定搭配，表示"被鼓励做某事"。

5. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `Those`（指人），在定语从句中作主语，用 `who` 或 `that`。

6. **答案：carefully**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `plans` 需要副词，`careful` 变为 `carefully`。

7. **答案：what**
   - **考点**：连接代词。引导宾语从句，`what we really need` 表示"我们真正需要的东西"，`what` 在从句中作宾语。

8. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`store` 后接宾语，指代 `leftovers`（复数），用 `them`。

9. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`not as...as...` 表示"不如……那样……"，构成同级比较的否定形式。

10. **答案：habits**
    - **考点**：名词复数。`eating habits` 表示"饮食习惯"，习惯通常用复数形式。
