# 10-10 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：跳蚤市场义卖 (Charity Flea Market)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last month, our school organised a charity flea market to raise money for children in poor areas. Every class was asked to set (1) ______ a stall and sell items donated by students. Our class collected books, toys, clothes and handmade crafts. I brought some old storybooks that I (2) ______ (read) many times and no longer needed. On the day of the market, the playground was transformed (3) ______ a lively bazaar. Colourful banners hung everywhere, and cheerful music played (4) ______ the background. My classmate Li Fang was our stall's (5) ______ (good) salesperson. She greeted every customer with a big smile and described each item (6) ______ (enthusiastic). She even created "buy two, get one free" deals that attracted many (7) ______ (buy). By noon, we had sold almost everything. In total, our class raised over 500 yuan, (8) ______ was the highest amount among all the classes. The money will (9) ______ (use) to buy school supplies for children in rural Yunnan. I felt proud knowing that our old items could bring (10) ______ (happy) to someone else. The flea market taught us that giving is just as rewarding as receiving.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：up**
   - **考点**：副词（短语动词）。`set up` 表示"搭建、设立"。

2. **答案：had read**
   - **考点**：时态。`books that I had read many times`，过去完成时表示在"带来"之前已读过。

3. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`be transformed into` 表示"被转变为"。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`in the background` 表示"在背景中"。

5. **答案：best**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`our stall's best salesperson`。

6. **答案：enthusiastically**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `described` 需要副词。

7. **答案：buyers**
   - **考点**：词性转换与复数。`attracted many buyers`，`buy` 变为 `buyer`，复数。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

9. **答案：be used**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`will be used to buy`，钱"将被用来"。

10. **答案：happiness**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`bring happiness`，动词后接名词作宾语，`happy` 变为 `happiness`。
