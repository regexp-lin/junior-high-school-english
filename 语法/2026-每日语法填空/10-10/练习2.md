# 10-10 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：拼图游戏 (Jigsaw Puzzles)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Jigsaw puzzles are a wonderful way to relax and exercise your brain at the same time. My family has been doing puzzles together (1) ______ many years. Every Sunday afternoon, we spread out the pieces on the dining table and work on them as a team. Last month, we started a 1,000-piece puzzle of a famous (2) ______ (paint) — Van Gogh's "Starry Night". It was the (3) ______ (difficult) puzzle we had ever attempted. The sky section was especially hard because most of the pieces were dark blue and looked very (4) ______ (similarity). It took us three weekends just (5) ______ (finish) the sky. My father is the most patient person in our family. He can sit for hours, (6) ______ (try) piece after piece without getting frustrated. My mother prefers to sort the pieces by colour first, which she says makes the process (7) ______ (easy). I usually focus (8) ______ finding edge pieces to build the frame. Working on puzzles together gives us a chance to chat and enjoy each (9) ______ company. After we finished "Starry Night", we glued it and hung it on the wall. Every time I look at it, I (10) ______ (remind) of the happy hours we spent putting it together.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for many years` 表持续时间，与现在完成进行时搭配。

2. **答案：painting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a famous painting`，`paint` 变为名词 `painting`。

3. **答案：most difficult**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the most difficult puzzle`。

4. **答案：similar**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`looked very similar`，系动词后接形容词，`similarity` 变为 `similar`。

5. **答案：to finish**
   - **考点**：不定式。`It took us three weekends to finish`。

6. **答案：trying**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。`sit for hours, trying piece after piece`，现在分词作伴随状语。

7. **答案：easier**
   - **考点**：比较级。`makes the process easier`，暗含比较。

8. **答案：on**
   - **考点**：介词。`focus on` 为固定搭配。

9. **答案：other's**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`each other's company` 表示"彼此的陪伴"。

10. **答案：am reminded**
    - **考点**：被动语态。`I am reminded of`，"被提醒"，用一般现在时被动。
