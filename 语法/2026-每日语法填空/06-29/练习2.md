# 06-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：中国传统美食 (Traditional Chinese Food)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

China is famous (1) ______ its rich food culture. Each region has its own special dishes, and the flavours vary (2) ______ (great) from north to south. In the north, people enjoy noodles and dumplings, while southerners prefer rice and dim sum. One of the (3) ______ (popular) dishes across the country is kung pao chicken, which (4) ______ (invent) in Sichuan province long ago. The dish (5) ______ (make) with chicken, peanuts, and dried chillies. Its spicy taste has attracted food lovers both at home and (6) ______ (broad). My grandmother is an excellent cook. She learned to make tofu dishes from (7) ______ (she) mother when she was just eight years old. Every Spring Festival, our whole family gathers together (8) ______ (prepare) a big dinner. The kitchen is always filled (9) ______ wonderful smells. I think food is more than just something to eat — it carries memories, traditions, and the love of the people (10) ______ cook it.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`be famous for` 为固定搭配，表示"因……而闻名"。

2. **答案：greatly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `vary` 需要副词，`great` 变为 `greatly`（非常地）。

3. **答案：most popular**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。`one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词`，`popular` 为多音节形容词，最高级为 `most popular`。

4. **答案：was invented**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。宫保鸡丁"被发明"，`long ago` 表示过去，主语为单数，用 `was invented`。

5. **答案：is made**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。描述菜品的做法（一般性事实），菜"被制作"，用 `is made`。

6. **答案：abroad**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`at home and abroad` 为固定搭配，表示"在国内外"，`broad` 变为 `abroad`（在国外）。

7. **答案：her**
   - **考点**：形容词性物主代词。修饰名词 `mother`，`she` 的物主代词形式为 `her`。

8. **答案：to prepare**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。家人聚在一起的目的是准备年夜饭，用 `to prepare` 作目的状语。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be filled with` 为固定搭配，表示"充满……"。

10. **答案：who / that**
    - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `people`（人），在定语从句中作主语，用 `who` 或 `that`。
