# 06-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：我的梦想职业 (My Dream Job)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Everyone has a dream about the future. When I was young, I wanted to be an astronaut, (1) ______ now I hope to become a doctor. The change happened after my grandfather (2) ______ (send) to hospital last winter. I saw the doctors and nurses working day and night to save (3) ______ (patient) lives. Their kindness and (4) ______ (profession) spirit touched me deeply. From that moment, I made up my mind (5) ______ (study) medicine in university. I know it will not be easy. Medical students need to learn for at (6) ______ five years, and the courses are much (7) ______ (difficult) than those in middle school. However, I am willing to work hard because helping sick people is the most meaningful thing I can think (8) ______. My parents support my decision. My mother always tells me that a person (9) ______ follows his dream will never feel lost. I believe that with enough effort, my dream (10) ______ (come) true one day.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：but**
   - **考点**：转折连词。"小时候想当宇航员"与"现在想当医生"之间为转折关系，用 `but`。

2. **答案：was sent**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般过去时。爷爷"被送往"医院，主语为第三人称单数且叙述过去事件，用 `was sent`。

3. **答案：patients'**
   - **考点**：名词所有格。`patients' lives` 表示"病人们的生命"，复数名词以 -s 结尾，所有格只加 `'`。

4. **答案：professional**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `spirit` 需要形容词，`profession` 变为 `professional`（专业的）。

5. **答案：to study**
   - **考点**：不定式。`make up one's mind to do sth` 为固定搭配，表示"下定决心做某事"。

6. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：固定搭配。`at least` 表示"至少"，`least` 为 `little` 的最高级。

7. **答案：more difficult**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级；`difficult` 为多音节形容词，比较级为 `more difficult`。

8. **答案：of**
   - **考点**：介词。`think of` 为固定搭配，表示"想到"；此句为定语从句，`of` 被移到句末。

9. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a person`（人），在定语从句中作主语，用 `who` 或 `that`。

10. **答案：will come**
    - **考点**：一般将来时。表示对未来的信念，`one day` 表示将来某天，用一般将来时 `will come`。
