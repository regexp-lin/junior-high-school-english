# 07-18 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：一次误会 (A Misunderstanding)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Li Wei and I have been best friends (1) ______ primary school. We do almost everything together. But last month, something happened that nearly (2) ______ (destroy) our friendship. It started when I saw Li Wei (3) ______ (whisper) to another classmate and laughing. I thought they were talking about me behind my back. I felt (4) ______ (anger) and refused to speak to him for a whole week. Li Wei tried several (5) ______ (time) to explain, but I would not listen. One evening, he left a letter under my door. In the letter, he wrote that he and the other classmate (6) ______ (plan) a surprise birthday party for me. They were whispering because they did not want me (7) ______ (find) out. After reading the letter, I felt (8) ______ (terrible) ashamed of myself. I called him at once and said sorry. He forgave me (9) ______ any hesitation and said, "That's (10) ______ friends are for." I learned an important lesson that day: never judge a situation before you know the full truth.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：since**
   - **考点**：介词/连词。`since primary school` 表示"自从小学以来"，与现在完成时搭配。

2. **答案：destroyed**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述上个月发生的事情，用过去时。

3. **答案：whispering**
   - **考点**：现在分词作宾补。`see sb doing sth` 表示"看见某人正在做某事"。

4. **答案：angry**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`feel` 为系动词，后接形容词，`anger` 变为 `angry`。

5. **答案：times**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`several` 修饰可数名词复数，`time` 作"次数"讲为可数名词。

6. **答案：had been planning / were planning**
   - **考点**：过去完成进行时/过去进行时。在写信之前他们"一直在策划"生日派对，用过去完成进行时更佳，过去进行时也可接受。

7. **答案：to find**
   - **考点**：不定式。`want sb to do sth` 为固定搭配，否定形式 `did not want me to find out`。

8. **答案：terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `ashamed` 需要副词，`terrible` 变为 `terribly`（非常地）。

9. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`without any hesitation` 表示"毫不犹豫地"。

10. **答案：what**
    - **考点**：连接代词。`That's what friends are for.` 为固定表达，表示"这就是朋友的意义"，`what` 引导表语从句。
