# 07-18 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：旧物改造 (Upcycling Old Things)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last weekend, our art teacher asked us to bring old items from home and turn (1) ______ (they) into something new. This activity (2) ______ (call) "upcycling." I brought an old pair of jeans that I had outgrown. With scissors, glue, and a little (3) ______ (imagine), I made a pencil case out of the jean material. My classmate Zhang Ying turned old newspapers (4) ______ a beautiful basket, and Wang Tao made a lamp shade from used plastic bottles. The teacher was pleased (5) ______ our work and said we were more creative (6) ______ she had expected. She explained that upcycling was one of the best ways (7) ______ (reduce) waste. Every year, millions of tonnes of old clothing and household items (8) ______ (send) to landfills. If we learn to reuse them, we can save resources and protect the environment. After the class, many students said they would try (9) ______ (upcycle) things at home. I plan to make a bookshelf from old wooden boxes. It may not look (10) ______ (perfection), but it will be unique and meaningful.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：them**
   - **考点**：代词宾格。`turn them into` 中 `them` 代指 `old items`，作 `turn` 的宾语。

2. **答案：is called**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。这个活动"被叫做"升级改造，描述一般性事实。

3. **答案：imagination**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a little` 修饰名词，`imagine` 变为 `imagination`（想象力），不可数名词。

4. **答案：into**
   - **考点**：介词。`turn...into...` 为固定搭配，表示"把……变成……"。

5. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be pleased with` 为固定搭配，表示"对……满意"。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较连词。`more creative than` 构成比较级结构。

7. **答案：to reduce**
   - **考点**：不定式。`the best ways to do sth` 中不定式作后置定语，修饰 `ways`。

8. **答案：are sent**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。`Every year` 表示一般性事实，物品"被送往"垃圾填埋场，主语为复数。

9. **答案：to upcycle / upcycling**
   - **考点**：不定式/动名词。`try to do sth` 表示"努力去做"，`try doing sth` 表示"尝试做"，两者语境均可。

10. **答案：perfect**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`look` 为系动词，后接形容词，`perfection` 变为 `perfect`。
