# 05-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：暑假计划 (Summer Holiday Plans)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The summer holiday is coming, and my friends are making different plans. Some will travel with their parents, 1. ________ others plan to stay at home and learn new skills online.

As for me, I have made 2. ________ clearer plan than last year. First, I will spend two weeks 3. ________ (visit) my grandparents in the countryside. They are getting older, and I want to help them with housework and listen to their stories. Second, I have signed up for a swimming course because I am still not able to swim 4. ________ (good). Third, I hope to read at least five books, including one 5. ________ (write) in English.

My cousin invited me to join a volunteer team that cleans up the beach. I hesitated at first, 6. ________ I finally agreed because protecting the environment matters to me, too. We will meet every Saturday morning in July.

Of course, I will not forget my homework. Every day, I will review what I 7. ________ (learn) during the term for an hour. If I manage my time 8. ________ (wise), I believe this summer will be both relaxing and meaningful.

When September arrives, I am sure I 9. ________ (feel) proud of what I have done. A good plan is 10. ________ the first step toward a wonderful holiday.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **while**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。Some...while others... 表示两类人的对比，while 连接并列分句。

2. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。plan 可数单数，a clearer plan 表"一个更清晰的计划"。

3. **visiting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。spend time (in) doing sth. 花费时间做某事，用 visiting。

4. **well**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 swim，用副词 well；good 为形容词，不能修饰动词。

5. **written**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（过去分词）。one (book) written in English 过去分词短语作后置定语，表被动"用英语写的"。

6. **but**
   - **考点**：并列连词（无提示词填空）。hesitated 与 agreed 为转折，用 but。

7. **have learned**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。review what I have learned during the term 复习本学期已学内容，强调对现在的影响，用现在完成时（learnt 为英式拼写，亦正确）。

8. **wisely**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 manage，用副词 wisely。

9. **will feel**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般将来时）。When September arrives 表将来时间背景，主句用 will feel。

10. **only**
    - **考点**：副词（无提示词填空）。only the first step 表示"只是第一步"，说明计划只是开端，还需坚持执行。
