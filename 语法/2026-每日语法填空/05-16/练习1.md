# 05-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：城市交通拥堵 (Urban Traffic Congestion)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Living in a big city has many advantages, but traffic jams have become 1. ________ serious problem for millions of commuters. During rush hour, cars move 2. ________ (slow) than walking speed on some roads, and people waste a lot of time 3. ________ (sit) in queues.

To solve the problem, the government has built new subway lines and widened several main streets. More buses 4. ________ (put) into use in the past five years, and bike-sharing programs have encouraged citizens to choose greener travel. Some companies even allow employees to work 5. ________ home twice a week to reduce pressure on the roads.

Experts remind us that technology alone is not enough. 6. ________ every driver follows traffic rules and plans trips wisely, the situation will not improve 7. ________ (full). As students, we can set 8. ________ example by taking public transport or riding bicycles to school when possible.

Last month, our class held a debate on 9. ________ traffic congestion could be controlled better. We learned that small changes in daily habits may bring about 10. ________ (big) results than we expect.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **a**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。serious problem 可数单数表泛指，serious 以辅音音素开头，用 a。

2. **more slowly**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。than walking speed 提示比较级，slowly 为多音节副词，比较级用 more slowly。

3. **sitting**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。waste time (in) doing sth. 浪费时间做某事，用 sitting。

4. **have been put**
   - **考点**：动词时态与语态（现在完成时的被动语态）。in the past five years 与现在完成时连用，buses 与 put 为被动，用 have been put。

5. **from**
   - **考点**：介词（无提示词填空）。work from home 为固定搭配，表示"居家办公"，减轻道路压力。

6. **Unless**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。Unless every driver follows... 除非每位司机都遵守……，引导条件状语从句，相当于 if...not；句首大写。

7. **fully**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 improve，用副词 fully。

8. **an**
   - **考点**：冠词（无提示词填空）。set an example 固定搭配，example 以元音音素开头，用 an。

9. **how**
   - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。debate on how traffic congestion could be controlled better 表示"就如何更好地控制交通拥堵"展开辩论，how 引导宾语从句。

10. **bigger**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。than we expect 提示比较级，big → bigger。
