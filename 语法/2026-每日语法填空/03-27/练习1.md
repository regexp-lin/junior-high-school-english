# 03-27 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：中外饮食文化 (Food Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Food is not just something we eat to stay alive. It is also an important part of culture. Different countries have different food traditions, and learning about them can be very 1. ________ (interest).

In China, rice and noodles are the main foods. Chinese people believe that sharing food at a round table 2. ________ (bring) the family closer. Chopsticks are used instead 3. ________ knives and forks. For Chinese people, food is a way to show love and care. When a guest 4. ________ (come) to your home, preparing a big meal is a sign of welcome.

Western food culture is quite different. In many Western 5. ________ (country), people usually have their own plate of food. Bread, steak and salad are common 6. ________ (dish). Table manners are also important — for example, you should not talk with your mouth 7. ________ (fill) of food.

In Japan, sushi is one of the most famous 8. ________ (tradition) foods. Japanese people pay great attention 9. ________ the appearance of food. They believe that food should look as beautiful as it tastes.

No matter where you are, food always plays a special role in people's lives. The next time you try a dish from another country, remember that you are also 10. ________ (taste) a piece of their culture.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **interesting**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→形容词）。be + 形容词，修饰事物用 -ing 形式，interesting 意为"有趣的"。

2. **brings**
   - **考点**：主谓一致（一般现在时）。that 从句中主语是 sharing food（动名词短语），视为第三人称单数。

3. **of**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。instead of 意为"而不是"，是固定搭配。

4. **comes**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般现在时）+ 主谓一致。when 引导时间状语从句，描述一般情况，主语 a guest 为单数，动词加 -s。

5. **countries**
   - **考点**：名词复数。many 修饰可数名词复数，country → countries（辅音字母+y 变 i 加 -es）。

6. **dishes**
   - **考点**：名词复数。common 后面列举了多种食物，dish → dishes。

7. **full**
   - **考点**：形容词（无需变形）。with your mouth full of food 意为"嘴里塞满食物"，full 作宾语补足语。此处考查 with 复合结构。

8. **traditional**
   - **考点**：词性转换（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 foods，需用形容词形式 traditional。

9. **to**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。pay attention to 意为"注意……"，是固定搭配。

10. **tasting**
    - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词/现在分词）。be + doing 构成现在进行时，表示"你也正在品味他们文化的一部分"。
