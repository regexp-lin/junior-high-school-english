# 03-27 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：大自然与野生动物 (Nature and Wildlife)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Our planet is home to millions of 1. ________ (kind) of animals. From the smallest insects to the largest whales, each species plays an important role in keeping the balance of nature.

However, many wild animals are 2. ________ (face) serious dangers today. Their natural habitats are being destroyed because of human activities. Forests 3. ________ (cut) down to build roads and houses, leaving animals with no place to live. Some animals, such as pandas and tigers, have become endangered.

Luckily, people around the world have started to take action. In China, nature reserves 4. ________ (set) up to protect wild animals. Thanks to years of effort, the number of giant pandas 5. ________ (grow) steadily. This is good news for 6. ________ animal lovers.

What can we do as students? First, we should learn more about wildlife protection. The 7. ________ (much) we know, the more we can do to help. Second, we should refuse to buy products made 8. ________ animal skins or bones. Third, we can volunteer at local animal shelters and help take care of 9. ________ (home) animals.

Every living thing on Earth deserves to be treated with kindness. Let's work together to build a world 10. ________ humans and animals can live in harmony.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **kinds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。millions of 后接可数名词复数，kind → kinds，millions of kinds of 意为"数百万种"。

2. **facing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词/现在进行时）。be + doing 构成现在进行时，表示"正在面临"。

3. **are cut/are being cut**
   - **考点**：被动语态。forests 与 cut down 之间是被动关系，可用一般现在时被动（are cut）或现在进行时被动（are being cut）。

4. **have been set**
   - **考点**：被动语态（现在完成时）。nature reserves 与 set up 之间是被动关系，表示"已经被建立起来"，用现在完成时被动。

5. **has grown**
   - **考点**：动词时态（现在完成时）。the number of + 名词复数作主语，谓语用单数；结合语境表示"已经持续增长"，用现在完成时。

6. **all**
   - **考点**：限定词（无提示词填空）。all animal lovers 意为"所有动物爱好者"，for all... 表泛指。

7. **more**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。the more...the more... 意为"越……越……"，much 的比较级为 more。

8. **from**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be made from 意为"由……制成（看不出原材料）"。

9. **homeless**
   - **考点**：词性转换/构词法（名词→形容词）。修饰名词 animals，home → homeless（无家可归的），加后缀 -less 表否定。

10. **where**
    - **考点**：关系副词（无提示词填空）。引导定语从句修饰 a world，在从句中作地点状语，用 where。
