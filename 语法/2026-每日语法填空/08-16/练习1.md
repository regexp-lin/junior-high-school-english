# 08-16 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：无人机技术 (Drone Technology)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Drones were once toys for hobbyists, but now they (1) ______ (use) in many important fields. Farmers fly drones over their fields to spray pesticides and check the health of crops. This is much more (2) ______ (efficient) than doing it by hand. Delivery companies are testing drones that can carry packages (3) ______ people's homes in minutes. In disaster areas, drones help rescue workers find (4) ______ (survivor) trapped under rubble. They can reach places that are too (5) ______ (danger) for humans to enter. Even film-makers use drones to shoot (6) ______ (amaze) aerial scenes that were impossible before. My cousin is a drone pilot (7) ______ works for an agriculture company. He told me that (8) ______ (operate) a drone is not as easy as it looks. Pilots need to pass (9) ______ exam and get a licence before they can fly one professionally. I went to a drone show last month and was fascinated by the light show — hundreds of drones formed patterns in the night sky. It was the most spectacular display I (10) ______ (ever see).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：are used**
   - **考点**：被动语态 + 一般现在时。无人机"被使用"在多个领域，描述当前事实。

2. **答案：efficient**
   - **考点**：形容词。`more efficient than` 构成比较级，括号内已给出 `efficient`，需保持原形（前有 more）。

3. **答案：to**
   - **考点**：介词。`carry packages to people's homes` 表示"把包裹送到人们家中"。

4. **答案：survivors**
   - **考点**：词性转换 + 名词复数。`find survivors` 中 `survive` 变为 `survivor`（幸存者），用复数。

5. **答案：dangerous**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`too + 形容词 + for sb to do sth`，`danger` 变为 `dangerous`。

6. **答案：amazing**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `scenes` 需要形容词，`amaze` 变为 `amazing`。

7. **答案：who / that**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `a drone pilot`（人），在定语从句中作主语。

8. **答案：operating**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。`operating a drone` 在 `that` 从句中作主语。

9. **答案：an**
   - **考点**：冠词。`an exam` 中 `exam` 以元音音素 /ɪ/ 开头，用 `an`。

10. **答案：had ever seen**
    - **考点**：过去完成时。`the most spectacular display I had ever seen` 为过去完成时配合最高级的典型结构。
