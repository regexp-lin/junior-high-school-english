# 08-16 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：搬家的烦恼与收获 (Moving to a New Home)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last August, my family moved from our old apartment to a new house on the other side of the city. At first, I was very upset because I did not want to leave the neighbourhood (1) ______ I had grown up in. All my friends lived there, and I was afraid of (2) ______ (lose) touch with them. The first few weeks in the new house were (3) ______ (terrible) lonely. I missed walking to school with my best friend Li Wei and playing in the park after dinner. My mother noticed my sadness and (4) ______ (encourage) me to join after-school clubs to meet new people. I took her advice and signed up for the badminton club. The other members were very (5) ______ (welcome) and helped me settle in. One boy, Zhang Hao, became my close friend after we won a doubles match together. Meanwhile, Li Wei and I kept in touch (6) ______ video calls every weekend. He visited my new house once and said it was much (7) ______ (spacious) than the old apartment. Now, six months (8) ______ (late), I feel completely at home. I have realised that moving does not mean (9) ______ (lose) old friends — it means gaining new ones. Change can be scary, but it often brings (10) ______ (expect) joys.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：that / which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `the neighbourhood`（地点，但此处有介词 `in` 后置），在定语从句中作 `in` 的宾语。

2. **答案：losing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`afraid of` 中 `of` 为介词，后接动名词。

3. **答案：terribly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `lonely` 需要副词，`terrible` 变为 `terribly`（非常地）。

4. **答案：encouraged**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。与 `noticed` 并列，叙述过去的事情。

5. **答案：welcoming**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`were` 后接形容词，`welcome` 变为 `welcoming`（热情的，友好的）。

6. **答案：through / by**
   - **考点**：介词。`through/by video calls` 表示"通过视频通话"。

7. **答案：more spacious**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than` 提示用比较级，`spacious` 为多音节形容词。

8. **答案：later**
   - **考点**：副词比较级。`six months later` 表示"六个月后"。

9. **答案：losing**
   - **考点**：动名词。`mean doing sth` 表示"意味着做某事"。

10. **答案：unexpected**
    - **考点**：词性转换 + 前缀。修饰名词 `joys` 需要形容词，`expect` 变为 `unexpected`（意想不到的），加前缀 `un-`。
