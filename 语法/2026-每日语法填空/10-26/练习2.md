# 10-26 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：新能源汽车 (New Energy Vehicles)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

New energy vehicles, especially electric cars, are becoming (1) ______ (increase) common on Chinese roads. The government has been (2) ______ (encourage) people to buy electric cars by offering subsidies and building more charging stations. My father bought an electric car last year, and he is very satisfied (3) ______ it. He says it is much (4) ______ (quiet) than his old petrol car and costs far less to run. Charging the car at home overnight is enough for his daily commute. However, electric cars still have some (5) ______ (disadvantage). The most common complaint is "range anxiety" — the worry that the battery will run out before you (6) ______ (reach) your destination. On long trips, finding charging stations can be (7) ______ (challenge), and charging takes much longer than filling up a petrol tank. My father once had to wait over an hour at a charging station during a holiday trip because all the chargers were (8) ______ (occupy). Despite these issues, experts believe that electric cars will (9) ______ (eventual) replace petrol cars as battery technology improves. I think switching to electric cars is an important step towards (10) ______ (reduce) air pollution and fighting climate change.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：increasingly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `common` 需要副词。

2. **答案：encouraging**
   - **考点**：时态。`has been encouraging`，现在完成进行时。

3. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`be satisfied with` 表示"对……满意"。

4. **答案：quieter**
   - **考点**：比较级。`much quieter than`。

5. **答案：disadvantages**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`some disadvantages`。

6. **答案：reach**
   - **考点**：时态。`before you reach`，时间状语从句中用一般现在时表将来。

7. **答案：challenging**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`can be challenging`，`challenge` 变为形容词 `challenging`。

8. **答案：occupied**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`were occupied`，`occupy` 变为过去分词，表"被占用的"。

9. **答案：eventually**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `replace` 需要副词。

10. **答案：reducing**
    - **考点**：动名词。`towards reducing`，介词 `towards` 后接动名词。
