# 10-26 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：家书 (A Letter from Home)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

In the age of smartphones and social media, few people write letters anymore. But for my grandmother, letters hold a special place in her heart. She has kept every letter she ever (1) ______ (receive) — a thick bundle tied with red string, stored in an old wooden box. Last week, she showed me a letter that my grandfather had written to her fifty years (2) ______. He was working in a factory in another city at the time, and they could only communicate (3) ______ letter. In the letter, he described his daily life — the food he ate, the friends he made and how much he (4) ______ (miss) her. His handwriting was neat and (5) ______ (beauty). Even though the paper had turned yellow with age, the words still felt warm and alive. I was (6) ______ (deep) touched by the love expressed in those simple sentences. My grandmother told me that she read each letter dozens of times, waiting (7) ______ (patient) for the next one to arrive. "Letters are different from messages on a phone," she said. "Each one takes time and effort to write, (8) ______ makes it more precious." Her words made me think about how (9) ______ (easy) we can communicate today, yet how much we have lost. I decided to write a handwritten letter to my grandmother. When she received it, she was the (10) ______ (happy) I had ever seen her.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：received**
   - **考点**：时态。`every letter she ever received`，叙述过去用过去时。

2. **答案：ago**
   - **考点**：副词。`fifty years ago` 表示"五十年前"。

3. **答案：by**
   - **考点**：介词。`communicate by letter` 表示"通过书信交流"。

4. **答案：missed**
   - **考点**：时态。宾语从句中叙述过去的感受，用一般过去时。

5. **答案：beautiful**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `neat` 并列作表语，需要形容词。

6. **答案：deeply**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰过去分词 `touched` 需要副词。

7. **答案：patiently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `waiting` 需要副词。

8. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：非限制性定语从句。逗号后用 `which` 引导。

9. **答案：easily**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `communicate` 需要副词。

10. **答案：happiest**
    - **考点**：形容词最高级。`the happiest I had ever seen her`。
