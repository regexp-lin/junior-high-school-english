# 12-07 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：春节准备 (Chinese New Year Preparations)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

The Spring Festival, also known as Chinese New Year, is the most important festival in China. Preparations usually begin weeks (1) ______ the festival. Families clean their houses thoroughly to sweep away bad luck and make room (2) ______ good fortune. This tradition is called "dust sweeping" and has been practised (3) ______ centuries. After cleaning, families decorate their homes with red lanterns, paper cuttings and Spring Festival couplets. Red is the dominant colour because it is believed (4) ______ (bring) good luck and drive away evil spirits. My family follows these traditions every year. My mother is always the (5) ______ (busy) person in the house during this time. She spends days (6) ______ (shop) for ingredients and preparing special dishes for the New Year's Eve dinner. This dinner, known as the "reunion dinner," is the highlight of the festival. It (7) ______ (consider) the most important meal of the entire year. Every dish has a symbolic (8) ______ (mean). For example, fish represents abundance because the Chinese word for fish sounds like the word for "surplus." Dumplings, shaped like ancient gold coins, symbolise wealth and (9) ______ (prosper). On New Year's Eve, our whole extended family gathers at my grandparents' house. We eat together, watch the Spring Festival Gala on TV and stay up until midnight (10) ______ (welcome) the new year. It is a time of joy, togetherness and hope for the year ahead.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：before**
   - **考点**：介词。`weeks before the festival` 表示"节日前几周"。

2. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`make room for` 表示"为……腾出空间"。

3. **答案：for**
   - **考点**：介词。`for centuries` 表示持续时间。

4. **答案：to bring**
   - **考点**：不定式。`be believed to do sth` 表示"被认为会做某事"。

5. **答案：busiest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`the busiest person` 用最高级。

6. **答案：shopping**
   - **考点**：动名词。`spend time doing sth`。

7. **答案：is considered**
   - **考点**：被动语态。`It is considered` 表示"被认为是"。

8. **答案：meaning**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a symbolic meaning`，形容词后接名词。

9. **答案：prosperity**
   - **考点**：词性转换。与 `wealth` 并列，需要名词形式。

10. **答案：to welcome**
    - **考点**：不定式。表目的，"为了迎接新年"。
