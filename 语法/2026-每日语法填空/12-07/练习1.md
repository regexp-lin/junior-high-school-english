# 12-07 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：睡眠习惯 (Sleep Habits)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Getting enough sleep is crucial for teenagers' physical and mental health. Doctors recommend that teenagers get at (1) ______ (little) eight hours of sleep every night. However, a recent study found that over 70% of Chinese middle school students sleep (2) ______ (few) than eight hours on school nights. The main reasons include heavy homework loads, excessive screen time and early school start times. Lack of sleep can have serious (3) ______ (consequence). Students who do not sleep enough tend to have poorer (4) ______ (concentrate) in class, weaker memory and a higher risk of falling ill. They may also experience mood swings and find it harder (5) ______ (control) their emotions. I used to stay up late almost every night, (6) ______ (play) games on my phone or chatting with friends online. I rarely went to bed before midnight, and I often felt dizzy and exhausted during morning classes. My performance in school started to decline, (7) ______ worried my parents greatly. They decided to set a strict rule: all electronic devices must be turned (8) ______ by 9:30 pm. At first, I was unhappy about this rule. But after following it for a month, I noticed a big (9) ______ (different). I felt more energetic, my grades improved, and I was in a much better mood. Now I understand that a good night's sleep is (10) ______ (probable) the simplest and most effective way to improve our lives.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：least**
   - **考点**：最高级。`at least` 表示"至少"。

2. **答案：fewer**
   - **考点**：比较级。`fewer than eight hours`，`few` 修饰可数名词 `hours`。

3. **答案：consequences**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`serious consequences`，多种后果。

4. **答案：concentration**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`poorer concentration`，形容词修饰名词。

5. **答案：to control**
   - **考点**：不定式。`find it harder to control`。

6. **答案：playing**
   - **考点**：现在分词。表伴随状态。

7. **答案：which**
   - **考点**：关系代词。引导非限制性定语从句，指代前面整句话。

8. **答案：off**
   - **考点**：副词。`be turned off` 表示"被关掉"。

9. **答案：difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`a big difference`，冠词后接名词。

10. **答案：probably**
    - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词短语/判断需要副词。
