# 12-31 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：跨年夜 (New Year's Eve)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

New Year's Eve is one of the most (1) ______ (excite) nights of the year. Around the world, people celebrate the end of one year and the beginning of another with parties, fireworks and special traditions. In many Western countries, the highlight of the evening is the countdown to midnight. People gather in city squares, watching giant screens and (2) ______ (count) down from ten to one together. When the clock strikes twelve, fireworks light up the sky, and everyone shouts "Happy New Year!" In China, the Western New Year is not as significant (3) ______ the Spring Festival, but it is still widely celebrated, especially by young people. Many teenagers attend New Year's Eve parties (4) ______ (organise) by their schools or communities. Some families watch special TV programmes together while (5) ______ (wait) for midnight. Last year, my friends and I decided to celebrate New Year's Eve by (6) ______ (have) a party at my house. We prepared snacks, played board games and shared our (7) ______ (fond) memories of the past year. Each person also wrote a letter to their future self, describing their hopes and dreams for the coming year. We sealed the letters in envelopes and promised (8) ______ (open) them on the following New Year's Eve. At midnight, we went out to the balcony and watched the fireworks in the distance. Standing there with my closest friends, I felt (9) ______ (incredible) grateful for everything I had. It was a perfect way (10) ______ (end) the year.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：exciting**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰事物用 `-ing` 形式。

2. **答案：counting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。与 `watching` 并列，表伴随。

3. **答案：as**
   - **考点**：连词。`not as significant as` 同级比较的否定形式。

4. **答案：organised**
   - **考点**：过去分词作定语。`parties organised by their schools`，被动关系。

5. **答案：waiting**
   - **考点**：现在分词。`while waiting for midnight`，省略了主语。

6. **答案：having**
   - **考点**：动名词。介词 `by` 后接动名词。

7. **答案：fondest**
   - **考点**：最高级。`our fondest memories`。

8. **答案：to open**
   - **考点**：不定式。`promise to do sth`。

9. **答案：incredibly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰形容词 `grateful` 需要副词。

10. **答案：to end**
    - **考点**：不定式。`a perfect way to end the year`，不定式作定语。
