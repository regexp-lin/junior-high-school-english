# 12-31 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：展望未来 (Looking Forward)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

As we stand at the threshold of a new year, it is natural to think about what the future holds. The world is changing at an (1) ______ (precedent) speed, and the opportunities available to young people today are greater than ever before. Technology will continue (2) ______ (shape) our lives in ways we can barely imagine. Self-driving cars, virtual reality classrooms and AI-powered personal assistants may all become part of our daily routine within the next decade. However, with these (3) ______ (advance) come new challenges. Climate change, population growth and resource shortages are problems that our generation will need to solve. I often wonder what kind of world my children will grow up (4) ______. Will the air be clean? Will there be enough food for everyone? Will people live in peace? These questions may seem overwhelming, but I believe in the power of young people to make a (5) ______ (differ). History has shown that great changes often start with small actions taken by (6) ______ (determine) individuals. As students, the most important thing we can do right now is to prepare (7) ______ (our) for the future by studying hard, developing critical thinking skills and cultivating a sense of (8) ______ (responsible) towards our planet and fellow human beings. My teacher once said something that has stayed (9) ______ me: "The future does not just happen — it is built by the choices we make today." As I look forward to the new year, I am filled with hope. I may not know exactly what lies ahead, but I am (10) ______ (confidence) that with hard work, kindness and courage, we can create a brighter tomorrow.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：unprecedented**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`at an unprecedented speed`，`precedent` 加否定前缀变为形容词。

2. **答案：to shape**
   - **考点**：不定式。`continue to do sth`。

3. **答案：advances**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`with these advances`，`advance` 用作可数名词。

4. **答案：in**
   - **考点**：介词。`grow up in` 表示"在……中成长"。

5. **答案：difference**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`make a difference` 为固定搭配。

6. **答案：determined**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `individuals` 需要形容词。

7. **答案：ourselves**
   - **考点**：反身代词。`prepare ourselves for the future`。

8. **答案：responsibility**
   - **考点**：词性转换。介词 `of` 后接名词。

9. **答案：with**
   - **考点**：介词。`has stayed with me` 表示"一直伴随着我"。

10. **答案：confident**
    - **考点**：词性转换。`I am confident that...`，系动词后接形容词。
