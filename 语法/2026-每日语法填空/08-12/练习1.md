# 08-12 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：校园安全演练 (School Safety Drill)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Last Thursday, our school held an earthquake safety drill. At ten o'clock, a loud alarm (1) ______ (ring) through the building. All students were told to get under their desks (2) ______ (immediate) and cover their heads with their hands. After about thirty seconds, the teacher led us outside in an (3) ______ (order) line. We walked quickly (4) ______ running, because running might cause panic and injuries. Within three minutes, the whole school — over 2,000 students and teachers — had gathered safely on the playground. Our principal (5) ______ (praise) everyone for following the rules so well. He reminded us that China is in an active earthquake zone, and knowing how (6) ______ (protect) ourselves is a matter of life and death. Later, a firefighter gave us a talk (7) ______ fire safety. He showed us the correct way to use a fire extinguisher and explained the (8) ______ (important) of checking smoke alarms at home. We also learned that (9) ______ (stay) low to the ground helps you breathe in a smoke-filled room. Although we all hope disasters never happen, being prepared makes us much (10) ______ (safe).

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：rang**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述过去的事件，`ring` 的过去式为 `rang`。

2. **答案：immediately**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词短语 `get under` 需要副词，`immediate` 变为 `immediately`。

3. **答案：orderly**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰名词 `line` 需要形容词，`order` 变为 `orderly`（有秩序的）。

4. **答案：without**
   - **考点**：介词。`walked quickly without running` 表示"快步走但不跑"。

5. **答案：praised**
   - **考点**：一般过去时。叙述校长的表扬，用过去时。

6. **答案：to protect**
   - **考点**：不定式。`how to do sth` 为固定搭配。

7. **答案：on / about**
   - **考点**：介词。`a talk on/about sth` 表示"关于……的演讲"。

8. **答案：importance**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`the` 后接名词，`important` 变为 `importance`。

9. **答案：staying**
   - **考点**：动名词作主语。`staying low` 在 `that` 从句中作主语。

10. **答案：safer**
    - **考点**：形容词比较级。`much safer` 表示"安全得多"，隐含与"不做准备"的对比。
