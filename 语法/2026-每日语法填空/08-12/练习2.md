# 08-12 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：外卖文化 (Takeaway Culture)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Ordering food online has become part of everyday life in China. With just a few clicks on a phone, people can have meals (1) ______ (deliver) to their doorstep within thirty minutes. The takeaway industry (2) ______ (grow) rapidly in recent years. According to a report, over 500 million Chinese people use food delivery apps. This is (3) ______ (convenient) than cooking at home for many busy workers and students. However, the takeaway culture also brings problems. First, the packaging creates a huge amount of plastic (4) ______ (waste). Most takeaway boxes and bags are used only once and then thrown away. Second, eating takeaway food too often is not (5) ______ (health) because restaurant dishes usually contain more oil and salt (6) ______ home-cooked meals. Third, delivery drivers face great risks riding electric bikes at high (7) ______ (speed) through busy traffic. Some of them work over twelve hours a day just (8) ______ (earn) a basic income. I think we should order takeaway less (9) ______ (frequent) and cook at home whenever possible. When we do order, we can choose restaurants (10) ______ use eco-friendly packaging.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **答案：delivered**
   - **考点**：过去分词作宾补。`have meals delivered` 为 `have sth done` 结构，表示"使某物被……"。

2. **答案：has grown**
   - **考点**：现在完成时。`in recent years` 提示用现在完成时。

3. **答案：more convenient**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。`than cooking at home` 提示用比较级，`convenient` 为多音节形容词。

4. **答案：waste**
   - **考点**：名词。`plastic waste` 表示"塑料垃圾"，`waste` 在此为不可数名词。

5. **答案：healthy**
   - **考点**：词性转换。`is not healthy` 中 `is` 后接形容词，`health` 变为 `healthy`。

6. **答案：than**
   - **考点**：比较连词。`more oil and salt than` 构成比较级结构。

7. **答案：speeds**
   - **考点**：名词复数。`at high speeds` 表示"以高速"，用复数形式。

8. **答案：to earn**
   - **考点**：不定式表目的。工作超过十二小时的目的是赚取基本收入。

9. **答案：frequently**
   - **考点**：词性转换。修饰动词 `order` 需要副词，`frequent` 变为 `frequently`；`less frequently` 表示"更少地"。

10. **答案：that / which**
    - **考点**：关系代词。先行词为 `restaurants`（物），在定语从句中作主语。
