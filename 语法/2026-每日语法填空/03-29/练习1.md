# 03-29 中考英语语法填空 练习一

主题：天气与气候变化 (Weather and Climate Change)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Climate change is one of the greatest challenges that the world 1. ________ (face) today. Over the past century, the Earth's temperature has risen 2. ________ (significant), and this trend is continuing.

One of the main causes of climate change is the burning of fossil fuels, such 3. ________ coal and oil. When these fuels are burned, they release harmful gases into the air. These gases trap heat and make the Earth 4. ________ (warm) than it should be. This is known as the greenhouse effect.

The 5. ________ (result) of climate change are serious. Ice in the Arctic is melting, 6. ________ causes the sea level to rise. Many coastal cities may be flooded in the future. Extreme weather events like heavy storms and 7. ________ (drought) are also becoming more frequent.

What can ordinary people do about it? We can start by 8. ________ (save) energy in our daily lives. Turning off lights, using public transportation and planting more 9. ________ (tree) are all helpful. We should also support the development of clean energy sources like solar and wind power.

It is everyone's responsibility to protect our planet. If we don't act now, future 10. ________ (generation) will pay the price for our mistakes.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **is facing / faces**
   - **考点**：动词时态。today 提示当前状态，可用现在进行时 is facing（正在面对）或一般现在时 faces。

2. **significantly**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 has risen，需用副词形式 significantly。

3. **as**
   - **考点**：固定搭配（无提示词填空）。such as 意为"例如"，后接举例。

4. **warmer**
   - **考点**：形容词比较级。than 提示用比较级，warm → warmer。

5. **results**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。the 后接名词，result 作名词用复数 results 表示"多种结果"。

6. **which**
   - **考点**：关系代词（无提示词填空）。引导非限制性定语从句，which 指代前面整个事件"冰在融化"。

7. **droughts**
   - **考点**：名词复数。and 连接并列名词，与前面的 storms 并列，drought → droughts。

8. **saving**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。介词 by 后接动名词形式。

9. **trees**
   - **考点**：名词复数。more 修饰可数名词复数，tree → trees。

10. **generations**
    - **考点**：名词复数。future generations 意为"后代"，generation → generations。
