# 03-29 中考英语语法填空 练习二

主题：团队合作精神 (Teamwork)

## 一、题目

阅读下面短文，在空白处填入一个适当的单词，或填入括号中所给单词的正确形式。

Teamwork is an important skill that everyone needs to learn. Whether at school or at work, being able to work 1. ________ (effective) with others is the key to success.

Last month, our class took part in a science competition. We were divided 2. ________ groups of five. Each group had to design and build a small robot. At first, everyone in my group wanted to be the leader, and we couldn't agree 3. ________ anything. We wasted a whole day just arguing with each other.

Our teacher noticed the problem and gave us some advice. She said, "A good team doesn't need five 4. ________ (lead). It needs five people who respect each other and work together." Her words made us 5. ________ (realize) our mistake.

After that, we sat down and had a calm discussion. We 6. ________ (decide) to give each person a specific task based on their strengths. Tom was good at 7. ________ (design), so he drew the plan. Lisa was the 8. ________ (care) one, so she checked the details. I was responsible for putting the parts together.

With everyone 9. ________ (do) their best, our robot worked perfectly in the competition. We won second place! This experience taught me that no one can do everything alone, 10. ________ together we can achieve great things.

---

## 二、答案与解析

1. **effectively**
   - **考点**：词性转换（形容词→副词）。修饰动词 work，需用副词形式 effectively。

2. **into**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。be divided into 意为"被分成……"，是固定搭配。

3. **on**
   - **考点**：介词搭配（无提示词填空）。agree on 意为"就……达成一致"，是固定搭配。

4. **leaders**
   - **考点**：词性转换（动词→名词）+ 复数。five 提示复数，lead → leader → leaders。

5. **realize**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（省略 to 的不定式）。make sb. do sth. 意为"使某人做某事"，使役动词 make 后接省略 to 的不定式。

6. **decided**
   - **考点**：动词时态（一般过去时）。叙述过去发生的事情，decide → decided。

7. **designing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（动名词）。be good at doing sth. 意为"擅长做某事"，介词 at 后接动名词。

8. **most careful**
   - **考点**：形容词最高级。the + 最高级，careful 为多音节形容词，最高级为 most careful。

9. **doing**
   - **考点**：非谓语动词（现在分词）。with + 名词 + doing 构成 with 复合结构，表示伴随状态。

10. **but**
    - **考点**：连词（无提示词填空）。前后句构成转折关系："没有人能独自完成所有事，但一起我们可以成就伟大的事"。
