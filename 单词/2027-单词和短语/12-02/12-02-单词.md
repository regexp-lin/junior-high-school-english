## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **predicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使基于；断言；以…为依据
- **英文释义**：to state, affirm, or assert; to found or base something on
- **例句**：
  - The success of the plan is predicated on securing additional funding.
  - 该计划的成功是以获得额外资金为基础的。
  - The theory predicates that economic growth leads to social improvement.
  - 该理论断言经济增长会导致社会进步。
- **助记**：pre（预先）+ dic（说）+ ate → 预先说好的条件 → 以…为基础

### 2. **enigmatic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：神秘的；令人费解的；难以捉摸的
- **英文释义**：difficult to interpret or understand; mysterious
- **例句**：
  - The Mona Lisa's enigmatic smile has fascinated art lovers for centuries.
  - 蒙娜丽莎神秘的微笑几个世纪以来一直迷住艺术爱好者。
  - The enigmatic stranger left without revealing his name or purpose.
  - 那位神秘的陌生人没有透露姓名或来意就离开了。
- **助记**：enigma（谜）+ tic → 像谜一样 → 神秘的

### 3. **exasperate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激怒；使恼怒；惹恼
- **英文释义**：to irritate intensely; to infuriate
- **例句**：
  - His constant lateness exasperated his colleagues beyond measure.
  - 他经常迟到使同事们无比恼怒。
  - The slow internet connection exasperated the students trying to submit their work online.
  - 缓慢的网络连接让试图在线提交作业的学生们感到恼怒。
- **助记**：ex（加强）+ asper（粗糙）+ ate → 使变得非常粗糙 → 激怒

### 4. **malleable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可塑的；易变形的；易受影响的
- **英文释义**：easily influenced, shaped, or bent
- **例句**：
  - Gold is one of the most malleable metals, easily shaped into jewelry.
  - 黄金是最具可塑性的金属之一，容易被塑造成珠宝。
  - Young minds are highly malleable and can be shaped by their environment.
  - 年轻人的思想高度可塑，可以被环境所塑造。
- **助记**：malle（锤打）+ able → 能被锤打成形的 → 可塑的

### 5. **sanctimonious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：假装虔诚的；伪善的；道貌岸然的
- **英文释义**：making a show of being morally superior to other people
- **例句**：
  - His sanctimonious lectures about healthy eating annoyed everyone, especially since he ate junk food daily.
  - 他关于健康饮食的伪善说教让每个人都很烦，尤其是他自己每天都吃垃圾食品。
  - The sanctimonious politician was later exposed for the very corruption he had condemned.
  - 这位道貌岸然的政治家后来被揭露犯了他自己谴责的腐败行为。
- **助记**：sancti（圣洁）+ monious → 假装圣洁 → 伪善的

### 6. **conjure** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：变魔术；使想起；召唤
- **英文释义**：to make something appear unexpectedly as if by magic; to call upon a spirit
- **例句**：
  - The chef can conjure a delicious meal from the simplest ingredients.
  - 厨师能用最简单的食材变出一顿美味的饭菜。
  - The old photographs conjured up memories of happier times.
  - 旧照片唤起了对更快乐时光的回忆。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ jure（发誓）→ 一起发誓召唤 → 召唤/变魔术

### 7. **laudable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：值得赞扬的；可钦佩的
- **英文释义**：deserving praise and commendation
- **例句**：
  - Her laudable efforts to protect endangered species earned her international recognition.
  - 她保护濒危物种的值得赞扬的努力为她赢得了国际认可。
  - While his intentions are laudable, the execution of the plan needs improvement.
  - 虽然他的意图值得赞扬，但计划的执行需要改进。
- **助记**：laud（赞美）+ able → 值得赞美的 → 值得赞扬的

### 8. **obviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：消除；使不必要；避免
- **英文释义**：to remove a need or difficulty; to prevent
- **例句**：
  - Regular maintenance can obviate the need for costly repairs later.
  - 定期维护可以消除日后进行昂贵修理的需要。
  - The new technology obviates many of the problems that plagued the previous system.
  - 新技术消除了困扰旧系统的许多问题。
- **助记**：ob（在前面）+ vi（路）+ ate → 提前把路上的障碍清除 → 消除

### 9. **antipathy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：反感；厌恶；敌意
- **英文释义**：a deep-seated feeling of dislike; aversion
- **例句**：
  - She felt a strong antipathy toward dishonest people.
  - 她对不诚实的人有强烈的反感。
  - The antipathy between the two rival teams was evident during the match.
  - 两支对手队伍之间的敌意在比赛中显而易见。
- **助记**：anti（反对）+ pathy（感情）→ 反感情 → 反感

### 10. **unwieldy** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：笨重的；难以操控的；不灵活的
- **英文释义**：difficult to carry or move because of its size, shape, or weight; hard to manage
- **例句**：
  - The unwieldy bureaucratic process delayed the project by several months.
  - 笨重的官僚程序使项目延误了好几个月。
  - She struggled to carry the unwieldy box through the narrow doorway.
  - 她费力地把笨重的箱子搬过狭窄的门口。
- **助记**：un（不）+ wield（挥舞/操控）+ y → 不好操控的 → 笨重的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **perspicacity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：洞察力；敏锐；明辨力
- **英文释义**：the quality of having a ready insight into things; shrewdness
- **例句**：
  - Her perspicacity in business dealings earned her a reputation as a shrewd negotiator.
  - 她在商业交易中的洞察力为她赢得了精明谈判者的声誉。
  - The detective's perspicacity led him to notice the detail that solved the case.
  - 侦探的敏锐使他注意到了破案的细节。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ spic（看）+ acity → 看穿一切的能力 → 洞察力

### 12. **amaranthine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：永不褪色的；不朽的；紫红色的
- **英文释义**：immortal; undying; of a deep reddish-purple color
- **例句**：
  - Their amaranthine love story became the subject of countless poems and songs.
  - 他们永恒的爱情故事成为无数诗歌和歌曲的题材。
  - The garden was filled with amaranthine blooms that seemed to last forever.
  - 花园里满是永不褪色的花朵，似乎永远盛开。
- **助记**：amaranth（不凋花）+ ine → 像不凋花一样 → 永不褪色的

### 13. **pellucid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：清澈透明的；明了的；易理解的
- **英文释义**：translucently clear; easily understood
- **例句**：
  - The pellucid waters of the lake revealed every pebble on the bottom.
  - 湖水清澈透明，露出了湖底的每一块卵石。
  - Her pellucid writing style makes complex scientific concepts accessible to everyone.
  - 她清晰明了的写作风格使复杂的科学概念人人都能理解。
- **助记**：pel（穿过）+ luc（光）+ id → 光线穿过 → 清澈透明的

### 14. **Sisyphean** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：西西弗斯式的；永无止境的徒劳的
- **英文释义**：denoting a task that can never be completed; endlessly laborious
- **例句**：
  - Cleaning the house with three young children felt like a Sisyphean task.
  - 有三个小孩还要打扫房子感觉像是永无止境的徒劳。
  - The Sisyphean nature of the bureaucratic process frustrated everyone involved.
  - 官僚程序西西弗斯式的永无止境性使所有参与者都感到沮丧。
- **助记**：来自希腊神话中的 Sisyphus（西西弗斯），被罚永远推石上山 → 永无止境的徒劳

### 15. **phosphorescence** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：磷光；荧光；冷光
- **英文释义**：light emitted by a substance without combustion or perceptible heat
- **例句**：
  - The phosphorescence of the ocean waves at night created a magical spectacle.
  - 夜晚海浪的磷光创造了一幅神奇的景象。
  - Certain deep-sea creatures use phosphorescence to attract prey in the darkness.
  - 某些深海生物利用磷光在黑暗中吸引猎物。
- **助记**：phosphor（磷/光）+ escence（变成…的状态）→ 发出磷光 → 磷光
