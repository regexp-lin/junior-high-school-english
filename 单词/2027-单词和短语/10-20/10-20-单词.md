## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **allocate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：分配；拨给；划拨
- **英文释义**：to distribute resources or duties for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - The school allocated extra funding for science lab equipment.
  - 学校拨出额外资金购买科学实验室设备。
  - Each department must allocate its budget wisely.
  - 每个部门必须合理分配其预算。
- **助记**：al（向）+ loc（地方）+ ate → 向特定地方分配 → 分配

### 2. **buoyant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有浮力的；乐观的；繁荣的
- **英文释义**：able to float; cheerful and optimistic; (of economy) thriving
- **例句**：
  - Cork is buoyant enough to float on water for hours.
  - 软木的浮力足以在水上漂浮数小时。
  - Consumer spending remained buoyant despite rising prices.
  - 尽管物价上涨，消费支出仍然旺盛。
- **助记**：buoy（浮标）+ ant → 像浮标一样漂浮 → 有浮力的

### 3. **chronic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：慢性的；长期的；积习难改的
- **英文释义**：persisting for a long time; constantly recurring
- **例句**：
  - Chronic stress can lead to heart disease and depression.
  - 长期压力会导致心脏病和抑郁。
  - The city suffers from chronic traffic congestion during rush hours.
  - 这座城市在高峰时段遭受长期的交通拥堵。
- **助记**：chron（时间）+ ic → 时间长的 → 慢性的

### 4. **deviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：偏离；背离；脱离
- **英文释义**：to depart from an established course or accepted standard
- **例句**：
  - The pilot had to deviate from the planned route due to bad weather.
  - 飞行员因恶劣天气不得不偏离计划路线。
  - Students who deviate from the dress code will receive a warning.
  - 违反着装规定的学生将收到警告。
- **助记**：de（偏离）+ vi（路）+ ate → 偏离道路 → 偏离

### 5. **encompass** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：包含；包围；涵盖
- **英文释义**：to surround and have within; to include comprehensively
- **例句**：
  - The national park encompasses mountains, lakes, and forests.
  - 这个国家公园涵盖山脉、湖泊和森林。
  - Her research encompasses both history and sociology.
  - 她的研究涵盖了历史和社会学两个领域。
- **助记**：en（使）+ compass（包围/罗盘）→ 把所有方向都包住 → 包含

### 6. **fluctuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：波动；涨落；变化不定
- **英文释义**：to rise and fall irregularly in number or amount
- **例句**：
  - Oil prices fluctuate depending on global supply and demand.
  - 油价根据全球供需关系波动。
  - Her mood fluctuates between excitement and anxiety before exams.
  - 考试前她的情绪在兴奋和焦虑之间波动。
- **助记**：fluctu（流动/波浪）+ ate → 像波浪般起伏 → 波动

### 7. **gaudy** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：华而不实的；花哨的；俗艳的
- **英文释义**：showy and tasteless; extravagantly bright or colorful
- **例句**：
  - The gaudy decorations clashed with the elegant furniture.
  - 花哨的装饰与优雅的家具格格不入。
  - She wore a gaudy necklace that distracted from her simple outfit.
  - 她戴了一条俗艳的项链，与她简洁的着装不搭。
- **助记**：gaudy 联想 gauze（薄纱）→ 过多薄纱装饰 → 花哨

### 8. **herald** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：预示；宣告；先驱
- **英文释义**：to be a sign that something is about to happen; a messenger or harbinger
- **例句**：
  - The first robin of spring heralds the end of winter.
  - 春天的第一只知更鸟预示着冬天的结束。
  - Newspapers heralded the discovery as a breakthrough in medicine.
  - 报纸宣告这一发现为医学突破。
- **助记**：herald → 使者/传令官 → 预报/宣告

### 9. **intact** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：完好无损的；完整的；未受损的
- **英文释义**：not damaged or impaired in any way; complete
- **例句**：
  - Remarkably, the ancient vase was found intact after centuries underground.
  - 令人惊奇的是，这个古花瓶在地下数百年后被发现完好无损。
  - Despite the earthquake, the bridge remained intact.
  - 尽管发生了地震，这座桥仍然完好。
- **助记**：in（不）+ tact（触碰）→ 未被触碰 → 完好无损

### 10. **mitigate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和；缓解
- **英文释义**：to make less severe, serious, or painful
- **例句**：
  - Wearing sunscreen helps mitigate the risk of skin cancer.
  - 涂防晒霜有助于减轻患皮肤癌的风险。
  - The company took steps to mitigate the environmental damage.
  - 公司采取措施减轻环境损害。
- **助记**：mit（柔软/温和）+ igate → 使温和 → 减轻

### 高一词汇

### 11. **lucrative** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有利可图的；赚钱的；报酬丰厚的
- **英文释义**：producing a great deal of profit
- **例句**：
  - Real estate can be a lucrative investment in growing cities.
  - 在发展中的城市，房地产可以是一项有利可图的投资。
  - She left teaching for a more lucrative career in finance.
  - 她离开教师岗位转向收入更高的金融业。
- **助记**：lucr（利润）+ ative → 有利润的 → 赚钱的

### 12. **nominal** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：名义上的；象征性的；微不足道的
- **英文释义**：existing in name only; very small in comparison to the real value
- **例句**：
  - The president holds nominal power while the prime minister governs.
  - 总统握有名义上的权力而总理实际执政。
  - They charged a nominal fee of one dollar for the community event.
  - 他们为社区活动象征性地收取了一美元费用。
- **助记**：nomin（名字）+ al → 只有名字的 → 名义上的

### 13. **prolific** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：多产的；丰富的；作品众多的
- **英文释义**：producing much fruit, foliage, or many offspring; highly productive
- **例句**：
  - Shakespeare was one of the most prolific playwrights in history.
  - 莎士比亚是历史上最多产的剧作家之一。
  - The prolific author published three novels in a single year.
  - 这位多产的作家一年内出版了三本小说。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lif（生长）+ ic → 不断生长 → 多产的

### 14. **rebuke** `高一` v./n.

- **中文释义**：斥责；谴责；指责
- **英文释义**：to express sharp disapproval or criticism of someone
- **例句**：
  - The judge rebuked the lawyer for disrespectful behavior in court.
  - 法官斥责律师在法庭上不尊重的行为。
  - Her public rebuke of the decision surprised many observers.
  - 她对该决定的公开谴责令许多观察者意外。
- **助记**：re（回）+ buke（打/砍）→ 打回去 → 斥责

### 15. **solicit** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：征求；请求；招揽
- **英文释义**：to ask for or try to obtain something from someone
- **例句**：
  - The charity solicited donations from local businesses.
  - 慈善机构向当地企业征求捐款。
  - Candidates solicited votes by going door to door.
  - 候选人挨家挨户拉票。
- **助记**：sol（整个）+ icit（激发）→ 全力激发 → 征求
