## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **embrace** /ɪmˈbreɪs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：拥抱；欣然接受
- **英文释义**：to hold someone close in your arms; to accept something willingly
- **例句**：
  - She embraced her daughter warmly at the airport.
  - 她在机场热情地拥抱了女儿。
  - We should embrace new ideas and technologies.
  - 我们应该欣然接受新想法和新技术。
- **助记**：em-（在内）+ brace（手臂）→ 用手臂环抱 → 拥抱

### 2. **emerge** /ɪˈmɜːdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：出现；浮现
- **英文释义**：to come out of something or come into view; to become known
- **例句**：
  - The sun emerged from behind the clouds.
  - 太阳从云层后面露出来了。
  - New evidence has emerged in the case.
  - 案件中出现了新证据。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ merge（沉浸）→ 从水中浮出 → 出现

### 3. **emission** /ɪˈmɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：排放；排放物
- **英文释义**：the production and sending out of gas, light, heat, etc.
- **例句**：
  - The government aims to reduce carbon emissions.
  - 政府旨在减少碳排放。
  - Vehicle emissions are a major cause of air pollution.
  - 汽车排放是空气污染的主要原因。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ miss（送）+ -ion → 向外送出 → 排放

### 4. **emphasis** /ˈemfəsɪs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：强调；重点
- **英文释义**：special importance given to something
- **例句**：
  - The school puts great emphasis on reading.
  - 学校非常重视阅读。
  - He spoke with emphasis on the need for teamwork.
  - 他着重强调了团队合作的必要性。
- **助记**：em-（在内）+ phas（展示）+ -is → 在内部展示出来 → 强调

### 5. **encounter** /ɪnˈkaʊntə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：遇到；遭遇
- **英文释义**：to meet someone or something unexpectedly; to face a difficulty
- **例句**：
  - I encountered an old friend on the street yesterday.
  - 我昨天在街上遇到了一个老朋友。
  - We encountered many difficulties during the trip.
  - 旅途中我们遇到了很多困难。
- **助记**：en-（进入）+ counter（对面）→ 进入对面 → 遇到

### 6. **enforce** /ɪnˈfɔːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：执行；强制实施
- **英文释义**：to make sure that people obey a law or rule
- **例句**：
  - The police enforce traffic laws strictly.
  - 警察严格执行交通法规。
  - The new regulation will be enforced from next month.
  - 新规定将从下个月开始实施。
- **助记**：en-（使）+ force（力量）→ 用力量使其执行 → 强制实施

### 7. **engage** /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：参与；从事
- **英文释义**：to take part in an activity; to attract someone's attention
- **例句**：
  - Students should engage in more extracurricular activities.
  - 学生应该参与更多课外活动。
  - The speaker engaged the audience with his humour.
  - 演讲者以幽默吸引了观众。
- **助记**：en-（进入）+ gage（承诺/抵押）→ 投入承诺 → 参与

### 8. **enormous** /ɪˈnɔːməs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：巨大的；庞大的
- **英文释义**：extremely large in size, quantity, or extent
- **例句**：
  - The company made an enormous profit this year.
  - 公司今年获得了巨额利润。
  - An enormous whale swam past our boat.
  - 一头巨大的鲸鱼从我们的船旁游过。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ norm（标准）+ -ous → 超出标准的 → 巨大的

### 9. **ensure** /ɪnˈʃʊə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：确保；保证
- **英文释义**：to make certain that something happens or is the case
- **例句**：
  - Please ensure that all windows are closed before leaving.
  - 离开前请确保所有窗户都关好了。
  - We must ensure the safety of every student.
  - 我们必须确保每个学生的安全。
- **助记**：en-（使）+ sure（确定）→ 使确定 → 确保

### 10. **entertainment** /ˌentəˈteɪnmənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：娱乐；消遣
- **英文释义**：activities designed to give people pleasure or enjoyment
- **例句**：
  - The city offers a wide range of entertainment options.
  - 这座城市提供了丰富的娱乐选择。
  - Movies are a popular form of entertainment.
  - 电影是一种流行的娱乐形式。
- **助记**：entertain（娱乐）+ -ment → 娱乐活动 → 娱乐

### 高一词汇

### 11. **obscure** /əbˈskjʊə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj./v.
- **中文释义**：模糊的；使晦涩
- **英文释义**：not clearly expressed or easily understood; to make something difficult to see
- **例句**：
  - The meaning of the poem is quite obscure.
  - 这首诗的意思相当晦涩。
  - Clouds obscured the view of the mountain.
  - 云层遮挡了山的视线。
- **助记**：ob-（覆盖）+ scure（遮蔽）→ 被遮蔽的 → 模糊的

### 12. **opulent** /ˈɒpjələnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：富丽堂皇的；奢华的
- **英文释义**：ostentatiously rich and luxurious
- **例句**：
  - The hotel had opulent furnishings and gold decorations.
  - 酒店有富丽堂皇的陈设和金色装饰。
  - They lived an opulent lifestyle in their mansion.
  - 他们在豪宅里过着奢华的生活。
- **助记**：opul-（财富）+ -ent → 充满财富的 → 富丽堂皇的

### 13. **paradox** /ˈpærədɒks/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：悖论；矛盾
- **英文释义**：a statement that seems contradictory but may be true
- **例句**：
  - It's a paradox that the busiest people often find the most time.
  - 最忙的人往往最有时间——这是一个悖论。
  - The paradox of choice means more options can lead to less happiness.
  - 选择的悖论意味着更多选项可能导致更少的幸福感。
- **助记**：para-（旁边/反）+ dox（观点）→ 相反的观点 → 悖论

### 14. **pervasive** /pəˈveɪsɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：弥漫的；无处不在的
- **英文释义**：spreading widely throughout an area or group of people
- **例句**：
  - The influence of social media is pervasive in modern society.
  - 社交媒体的影响在现代社会无处不在。
  - A pervasive smell of smoke filled the room.
  - 一股弥漫的烟味充满了房间。
- **助记**：per-（贯穿）+ vas（走）+ -ive → 走遍各处的 → 无处不在的

### 15. **precarious** /prɪˈkeəriəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不稳定的；危险的
- **英文释义**：not securely held or in position; likely to fall or fail
- **例句**：
  - The vase was balanced in a precarious position on the edge of the table.
  - 花瓶岌岌可危地放在桌子边缘。
  - Many workers face a precarious financial situation.
  - 许多工人面临不稳定的财务状况。
- **助记**：prec-（祈祷）+ -arious → 需要祈祷才能安全的 → 不稳定的
