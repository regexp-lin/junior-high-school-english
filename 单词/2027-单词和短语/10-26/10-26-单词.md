## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **arduous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：艰难的；费力的；艰巨的
- **英文释义**：involving or requiring strenuous effort; difficult and tiring
- **例句**：
  - The hikers faced an arduous climb up the steep mountain path.
  - 徒步者面临沿陡峭山路攀登的艰难旅程。
  - Writing a thesis is an arduous task that requires months of dedication.
  - 写论文是一项需要数月投入的艰巨任务。
- **助记**：ard（烧/热）+ uous → 像烈火般考验 → 艰难的

### 2. **brash** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：鲁莽的；自以为是的；性急的
- **英文释义**：self-assertive in a rude, noisy, or overbearing way
- **例句**：
  - The brash young salesman pushed too hard and lost the client.
  - 鲁莽的年轻销售员逼得太紧而失去了客户。
  - His brash comments during the debate offended several panelists.
  - 他在辩论中鲁莽的评论冒犯了几位嘉宾。
- **助记**：brash 联想 crash（碰撞）→ 横冲直撞 → 鲁莽

### 3. **captivate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：迷住；吸引；使着迷
- **英文释义**：to attract and hold the attention or interest of; to charm
- **例句**：
  - The magician's performance captivated the audience for over an hour.
  - 魔术师的表演吸引了观众一个多小时。
  - Her storytelling ability captivates listeners of all ages.
  - 她讲故事的能力迷住了各个年龄段的听众。
- **助记**：captiv（俘获）+ ate → 俘获心灵 → 迷住

### 4. **dispel** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：驱散；消除；打消
- **英文释义**：to make a doubt, feeling, or belief disappear
- **例句**：
  - The manager's explanation dispelled any doubts about the company's future.
  - 经理的解释消除了对公司未来的任何疑虑。
  - Sunrise quickly dispelled the morning fog.
  - 日出很快驱散了晨雾。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ pel（推）→ 推散 → 驱散

### 5. **embark** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：着手；开始从事；上船
- **英文释义**：to begin a course of action; to go on board a ship or aircraft
- **例句**：
  - She embarked on a new career in software engineering after years of teaching.
  - 教了多年书后她着手开始了软件工程的新事业。
  - Passengers embarked on the cruise ship at the harbor.
  - 乘客在港口登上了游轮。
- **助记**：em（进入）+ bark（船）→ 进入船 → 着手/上船

### 6. **forsake** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：抛弃；放弃；离开
- **英文释义**：to abandon or give up something valued
- **例句**：
  - He forsook his comfortable city life to work as a rural doctor.
  - 他放弃了舒适的城市生活去做一名乡村医生。
  - A true friend will never forsake you in times of trouble.
  - 真正的朋友在困难时不会抛弃你。
- **助记**：for（远离）+ sake（缘故）→ 因某种缘故远离 → 抛弃

### 7. **gloat** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：幸灾乐祸；沾沾自喜；得意洋洋
- **英文释义**：to contemplate one's own success or another's misfortune with smugness
- **例句**：
  - It is rude to gloat when your rival fails.
  - 当对手失败时幸灾乐祸是不礼貌的。
  - The winning team tried not to gloat in front of the losers.
  - 获胜队伍尽量不在输家面前得意洋洋。
- **助记**：gloat 联想 glow（发光）→ 脸上发光般得意 → 幸灾乐祸

### 8. **hone** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：磨练；提升；使更锋利
- **英文释义**：to sharpen a blade; to refine or perfect over a period of time
- **例句**：
  - Athletes hone their skills through years of rigorous training.
  - 运动员通过多年严格训练磨练技能。
  - She honed her public speaking ability by practicing in front of a mirror.
  - 她通过在镜子前练习来提升公共演讲能力。
- **助记**：hone → home（回家）→ 把刀带回家磨 → 磨练

### 9. **interim** `初三` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：过渡期；临时的；暂时的
- **英文释义**：the intervening time; provisional; temporary
- **例句**：
  - An interim manager was appointed while the company searched for a new CEO.
  - 公司在寻找新CEO期间任命了一位临时经理。
  - In the interim, please continue working on the current project.
  - 在此期间，请继续处理当前项目。
- **助记**：inter（之间）+ im → 在两者之间的 → 过渡/临时

### 10. **nurture** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：养育；培养；滋养
- **英文释义**：to care for and encourage the growth or development of
- **例句**：
  - Teachers nurture students' curiosity and love for learning.
  - 老师培养学生的好奇心和对学习的热爱。
  - Both nature and nurture play a role in shaping personality.
  - 天性和后天培养都在塑造性格中发挥作用。
- **助记**：nurture 联想 nurse（护士）→ 像护士般照顾 → 养育

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acrimonious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：尖刻的；激烈的；充满火药味的
- **英文释义**：angry and bitter in tone or manner
- **例句**：
  - The acrimonious divorce battle lasted for over two years.
  - 充满火药味的离婚大战持续了两年多。
  - An acrimonious debate over the budget divided the committee.
  - 关于预算的激烈辩论使委员会产生分裂。
- **助记**：acri（尖/辣）+ monious → 语言尖酸 → 尖刻的

### 12. **pandemonium** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：大混乱；喧嚣；乌烟瘴气
- **英文释义**：wild and noisy disorder or confusion; uproar
- **例句**：
  - Pandemonium broke out in the stadium when the home team scored.
  - 主队进球时体育场一片大混乱。
  - The announcement of the cancelled flight caused pandemonium at the gate.
  - 航班取消的通知在登机口引发了一片混乱。
- **助记**：pan（全部）+ demon（恶魔）+ ium → 所有恶魔出动 → 大混乱

### 13. **recapitulate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：概括；重述要点；总结
- **英文释义**：to summarize and state again the main points of
- **例句**：
  - Let me recapitulate the three main points we discussed today.
  - 让我总结一下我们今天讨论的三个主要观点。
  - The teacher recapitulated the lesson at the end of each class.
  - 老师在每节课结束时会重述要点。
- **助记**：re（再）+ capit（头/要点）+ ulate → 再提要点 → 总结

### 14. **surreptitious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：秘密的；偷偷摸摸的；鬼鬼祟祟的
- **英文释义**：kept secret, especially because it would not be approved of
- **例句**：
  - She took a surreptitious glance at her phone during the meeting.
  - 她在会议期间偷偷看了一眼手机。
  - The surreptitious recording of private conversations is illegal.
  - 秘密录制私人对话是违法的。
- **助记**：surrept（偷）+ itious → 偷偷的 → 秘密的

### 15. **wistful** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：渴望的；惆怅的；带有淡淡忧伤的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a feeling of vague or regretful longing
- **例句**：
  - She gazed out the window with a wistful expression, thinking of her homeland.
  - 她带着惆怅的表情凝视窗外，思念着家乡。
  - His wistful tone when speaking of college days revealed deep nostalgia.
  - 他谈到大学时光时惆怅的语气流露出深深的怀旧。
- **助记**：wist（知道/渴望）+ ful → 充满渴望 → 惆怅的
