## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **circumvent** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：规避；绕过；设法回避
- **英文释义**：to find a way around an obstacle or restriction
- **例句**：
  - The students found a clever way to circumvent the school's internet restrictions.
  - 学生们找到了一个巧妙的方法来规避学校的网络限制。
  - Companies sometimes try to circumvent regulations to reduce costs.
  - 公司有时会试图绕过法规以降低成本。
- **助记**：circum（周围）+ vent（走）→ 绕着走 → 规避

### 2. **lucid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：清晰的；明白易懂的；头脑清楚的
- **英文释义**：expressed clearly; easy to understand; mentally clear
- **例句**：
  - The professor gave a lucid explanation that even beginners could understand.
  - 教授给出了一个清晰的解释，即使是初学者也能理解。
  - Despite his illness, the elderly man remained lucid until his final days.
  - 尽管生病，这位老人直到最后几天头脑仍然清醒。
- **助记**：luc（光）+ id → 像光一样明亮 → 清晰的

### 3. **extricate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使摆脱；解救；使脱离困境
- **英文释义**：to free someone or something from a constraint or difficulty
- **例句**：
  - The firefighters managed to extricate the trapped passengers from the wreckage.
  - 消防员设法从残骸中解救出被困的乘客。
  - She struggled to extricate herself from the awkward conversation at the party.
  - 她努力使自己从派对上尴尬的对话中脱身。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ tric（纠缠）+ ate → 从纠缠中出来 → 解救

### 4. **benevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；慈善的；善意的
- **英文释义**：well-meaning and kindly; charitable
- **例句**：
  - The benevolent donor gave millions to fund educational programs for underprivileged children.
  - 这位仁慈的捐赠者捐了数百万资金资助贫困儿童的教育项目。
  - Her benevolent nature made her beloved by everyone in the community.
  - 她仁慈的天性使她深受社区每个人的喜爱。
- **助记**：bene（好）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 好的意愿 → 仁慈的

### 5. **inundate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：淹没；使应接不暇；使不胜负荷
- **英文释义**：to overwhelm with things or people; to flood
- **例句**：
  - After the announcement, the company was inundated with job applications.
  - 公告发布后，公司被求职申请淹没了。
  - Heavy rains inundated the coastal villages, forcing residents to evacuate.
  - 暴雨淹没了沿海村庄，迫使居民撤离。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ und（波浪）+ ate → 波浪涌入 → 淹没

### 6. **pertinent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：相关的；切题的；中肯的
- **英文释义**：relevant or applicable to a particular matter
- **例句**：
  - Please keep your comments pertinent to the topic being discussed.
  - 请让你的评论与正在讨论的话题相关。
  - The lawyer raised several pertinent questions that changed the course of the trial.
  - 律师提出了几个切题的问题，改变了审判的进程。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ tin（持有）+ ent → 贯穿主题 → 相关的

### 7. **amalgamate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；混合；融合
- **英文释义**：to combine or unite to form one organization or structure
- **例句**：
  - The two schools amalgamated to form one larger institution.
  - 两所学校合并组成了一所更大的学校。
  - The chef amalgamated different cultural flavors to create a unique dish.
  - 厨师融合了不同文化的风味创造出一道独特的菜肴。
- **助记**：a + malgam（混合物）+ ate → 使成为混合物 → 合并

### 8. **contentious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有争议的；好争论的
- **英文释义**：causing or likely to cause an argument; controversial
- **例句**：
  - The contentious issue of immigration reform dominated the political debate.
  - 有争议的移民改革问题主导了政治辩论。
  - The meeting became contentious when members disagreed on the budget allocation.
  - 当成员们对预算分配产生分歧时，会议变得充满争议。
- **助记**：content（争论）+ ious → 引起争论的 → 有争议的

### 9. **galvanize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激励；鼓舞；使振奋
- **英文释义**：to shock or excite someone into taking action
- **例句**：
  - The coach's passionate speech galvanized the team to victory.
  - 教练充满激情的演讲鼓舞了球队走向胜利。
  - The environmental disaster galvanized public support for stricter pollution laws.
  - 环境灾难激发了公众对更严格污染法律的支持。
- **助记**：galvan（电流）+ ize → 像电流一样刺激 → 激励

### 10. **nonchalant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：漫不经心的；冷淡的；若无其事的
- **英文释义**：feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety
- **例句**：
  - He tried to appear nonchalant, but his trembling hands betrayed his nervousness.
  - 他试图表现得若无其事，但颤抖的双手暴露了他的紧张。
  - She gave a nonchalant shrug when asked about her exam results.
  - 当被问及考试成绩时，她若无其事地耸了耸肩。
- **助记**：non（不）+ chalant（关心）→ 不关心的 → 漫不经心的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **concatenation** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：一系列；连锁；串联
- **英文释义**：a series of interconnected things or events
- **例句**：
  - A concatenation of unfortunate events led to the company's downfall.
  - 一连串不幸事件导致了公司的倒闭。
  - The theory describes the concatenation of chemical reactions that sustain life.
  - 该理论描述了维持生命的一系列化学反应。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ caten（链条）+ ation → 像链条一样连在一起 → 连锁

### 12. **perspicacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：洞察力强的；敏锐的；有眼力的
- **英文释义**：having a ready insight into and understanding of things
- **例句**：
  - The perspicacious analyst predicted the market crash months before it happened.
  - 那位洞察力强的分析师在市场崩盘前几个月就做出了预测。
  - Her perspicacious observations during the meeting impressed the senior management.
  - 她在会议上敏锐的观察给高层管理人员留下了深刻印象。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ spic（看）+ acious → 看穿一切 → 洞察力强的

### 13. **mellifluous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：甜美的；流畅悦耳的
- **英文释义**：sweet or musical; pleasant to hear
- **例句**：
  - The singer's mellifluous voice enchanted the entire audience.
  - 歌手甜美的声音迷住了全场观众。
  - The poet's mellifluous verses were a joy to read aloud.
  - 诗人流畅悦耳的诗句朗读起来非常愉悦。
- **助记**：melli（蜜）+ flu（流）+ ous → 像蜂蜜一样流淌 → 甜美的

### 14. **perfunctory** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：敷衍的；草率的；应付了事的
- **英文释义**：carried out with a minimum of effort or reflection
- **例句**：
  - He gave a perfunctory nod without even looking up from his phone.
  - 他甚至没有从手机上抬起头，只是敷衍地点了点头。
  - The inspection was perfunctory, lasting only five minutes.
  - 检查很草率，只持续了五分钟。
- **助记**：per（完成）+ funct（功能）+ ory → 只为完成功能 → 敷衍的

### 15. **zeitgeist** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：时代精神；时代潮流
- **英文释义**：the defining spirit or mood of a particular period of history
- **例句**：
  - The novel perfectly captures the zeitgeist of the 1960s counterculture movement.
  - 这部小说完美地捕捉了1960年代反文化运动的时代精神。
  - Social media has become a powerful tool for shaping the zeitgeist of our era.
  - 社交媒体已成为塑造我们时代精神的有力工具。
- **助记**：德语借词，Zeit（时间）+ Geist（精神）→ 时间的精神 → 时代精神
