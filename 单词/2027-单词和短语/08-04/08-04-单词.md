## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abridge** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：删节；缩短；精简
- **英文释义**：to shorten a written work by condensing or cutting parts of it
- **例句**：
  - The publisher decided to abridge the novel to make it suitable for younger readers.
  - 出版商决定删节这部小说，使其适合年轻读者阅读。
  - An abridged version of the dictionary was released for students preparing for exams.
  - 为备考学生发行了一本精简版词典。
- **助记**：a + bridge（桥）→ 用一座桥连接两端，省去中间 → 缩短/删节

### 2. **candid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坦率的；真诚的；直言不讳的
- **英文释义**：truthful and straightforward; frank
- **例句**：
  - I appreciate your candid opinion, even though it was hard to hear.
  - 我感谢你坦率的意见，尽管这很难接受。
  - The politician gave a surprisingly candid interview about his past mistakes.
  - 这位政治家出人意料地坦诚接受了关于他过去错误的采访。
- **助记**：candid 联想 candle（蜡烛）→ 像蜡烛一样光明坦荡 → 坦率的

### 3. **deter** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻止；威慑；使不敢
- **英文释义**：to discourage or prevent someone from doing something by instilling fear or doubt
- **例句**：
  - The high price of the product did not deter customers from buying it.
  - 产品的高价格并没有阻止顾客购买。
  - Heavy penalties are meant to deter people from committing crimes.
  - 严厉的惩罚旨在威慑人们不要犯罪。
- **助记**：de（离开）+ ter（恐惧，如 terror）→ 用恐惧使人离开 → 阻止/威慑

### 4. **erratic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不稳定的；反复无常的；不规律的
- **英文释义**：not even or regular in pattern or movement; unpredictable
- **例句**：
  - His erratic behavior at work worried his colleagues and managers.
  - 他在工作中反复无常的行为让同事和经理们感到担忧。
  - The car moved in an erratic pattern, swerving from lane to lane.
  - 汽车行驶不规律，在车道间来回摇摆。
- **助记**：err（错误/偏离）+ atic → 总是偏离轨道 → 不稳定的

### 5. **futile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：徒劳的；无用的；无效的
- **英文释义**：incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
- **例句**：
  - It was futile to argue with someone who refused to listen to reason.
  - 和一个拒绝听从理性的人争论是徒劳的。
  - After several futile attempts to fix the machine, they called a professional.
  - 在几次徒劳的修理尝试后，他们请来了专业人士。
- **助记**：fut（流/倒）+ ile → 像水一样白白流走 → 徒劳的

### 6. **hamper** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：妨碍；阻碍；束缚
- **英文释义**：to hinder or impede the movement or progress of
- **例句**：
  - Bad weather hampered the rescue efforts in the disaster-hit area.
  - 恶劣天气妨碍了灾区的救援工作。
  - Lack of funding has hampered the development of the new technology.
  - 资金不足阻碍了新技术的发展。
- **助记**：hamper 联想 hammer（锤子）→ 被锤子砸住脚 → 妨碍/阻碍

### 7. **intricate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：错综复杂的；精密的；难理解的
- **英文释义**：very complicated or detailed; having many interrelated parts
- **例句**：
  - The watchmaker carefully assembled the intricate mechanism of the clock.
  - 制表师仔细组装了时钟的精密机械装置。
  - The novel has an intricate plot with many unexpected twists and turns.
  - 这部小说情节错综复杂，有许多出人意料的曲折。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ tric（纠缠）+ ate → 深深纠缠在一起 → 错综复杂的

### 8. **lament** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：哀悼；悲叹；挽歌
- **英文释义**：to express sorrow or regret; a passionate expression of grief
- **例句**：
  - The whole nation lamented the loss of the beloved leader.
  - 全国人民都为敬爱的领袖的逝世而哀悼。
  - She wrote a beautiful lament for her grandmother who passed away last year.
  - 她为去年去世的祖母写了一首优美的挽歌。
- **助记**：la（感叹词"啦"）+ ment → 发出"啦"的悲叹声 → 哀悼

### 9. **nurture** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：培养；养育；滋养
- **英文释义**：to care for and encourage the growth or development of
- **例句**：
  - Parents play a crucial role in nurturing their children's talents and interests.
  - 父母在培养孩子的才能和兴趣方面起着至关重要的作用。
  - The teacher nurtured a love of reading in all her students.
  - 这位老师培养了所有学生对阅读的热爱。
- **助记**：nur（滋养，如 nurse 护士）+ ture → 像护士一样照顾 → 培养/养育

### 10. **replenish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：补充；再装满；重新供应
- **英文释义**：to fill something up again; to restore to a former level or condition
- **例句**：
  - The store needs to replenish its stock before the holiday shopping season.
  - 商店需要在节日购物季之前补充库存。
  - After the long hike, they stopped at a stream to replenish their water supply.
  - 长途徒步后，他们在一条小溪旁停下来补充水源。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ plen（满，如 plenty）+ ish → 再次装满 → 补充

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ameliorate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：改善；改良；缓和
- **英文释义**：to make something bad or unsatisfactory better
- **例句**：
  - The new policies were designed to ameliorate the living conditions of the poor.
  - 新政策旨在改善穷人的生活条件。
  - Planting trees can help ameliorate the effects of air pollution in cities.
  - 种植树木可以帮助缓和城市空气污染的影响。
- **助记**：a + melior（更好的，拉丁语）+ ate → 使变得更好 → 改善

### 12. **clemency** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：仁慈；宽恕；温和
- **英文释义**：mercy; lenience, especially towards someone being punished
- **例句**：
  - The prisoner appealed to the governor for clemency, hoping to have his sentence reduced.
  - 囚犯向州长请求宽恕，希望减轻他的刑期。
  - The judge showed clemency and gave the young offender a second chance.
  - 法官表现出仁慈，给了这位年轻罪犯第二次机会。
- **助记**：clem（温和的）+ ency → 温和的品质 → 仁慈/宽恕

### 13. **ephemeral** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：短暂的；转瞬即逝的
- **英文释义**：lasting for a very short time
- **例句**：
  - The beauty of cherry blossoms is ephemeral — they bloom for only a few days each spring.
  - 樱花的美是短暂的——它们每年春天只绽放几天。
  - Fame can be ephemeral; today's celebrity may be forgotten tomorrow.
  - 名声可以是转瞬即逝的；今天的名人明天可能就被遗忘。
- **助记**：epi（在……上）+ hemer（一天）+ al → 只存在一天的 → 短暂的

### 14. **magnanimous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；慷慨的；高尚的
- **英文释义**：very generous or forgiving, especially towards a rival or someone less powerful
- **例句**：
  - The magnanimous winner congratulated his opponent and praised his effort.
  - 宽宏大量的获胜者向对手表示祝贺并赞扬了他的努力。
  - It was magnanimous of her to forgive those who had wronged her in the past.
  - 她原谅了过去那些伤害过她的人，真是宽宏大量。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 心胸宽大的 → 宽宏大量的

### 15. **sycophant** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：马屁精；谄媚者；阿谀奉承的人
- **英文释义**：a person who acts obsequiously towards someone important to gain advantage
- **例句**：
  - The CEO surrounded himself with sycophants who never challenged his decisions.
  - 这位首席执行官身边围满了从不质疑他决定的马屁精。
  - A true leader values honest advisors over sycophants who only tell them what they want to hear.
  - 真正的领导者重视诚实的顾问，而不是只说他们想听的话的谄媚者。
- **助记**：syco（无花果）+ phant（告发者）→ 古希腊告发偷运无花果的人 → 谄媚者
