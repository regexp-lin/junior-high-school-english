## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **arbitrary** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：任意的；武断的；专横的
- **英文释义**：based on random choice or personal whim rather than any reason or system
- **例句**：
  - The rules seemed arbitrary and unfair to the students.
  - 这些规则在学生们看来既武断又不公平。
- **助记**：arbit（仲裁）+ rary → 一个人说了算的仲裁 → 武断的

### 2. **compass** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：指南针；罗盘；圆规
- **英文释义**：an instrument containing a magnetized pointer that shows the direction of magnetic north; a V-shaped drawing instrument
- **例句**：
  - The hikers used a compass to find their way through the forest.
  - 徒步旅行者用指南针在森林中找路。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ pass（经过）→ 一起经过同一个方向 → 指南针

### 3. **deficit** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：赤字；亏损；不足额
- **英文释义**：the amount by which something is too small; an excess of expenditure over income
- **例句**：
  - The country faces a huge budget deficit this year.
  - 今年该国面临巨额预算赤字。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ fic（做）+ it → 做不够/不足 → 赤字

### 4. **epidemic** `初三` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：n. 流行病；泛滥 adj. 流行性的
- **英文释义**：n. a widespread occurrence of an infectious disease; adj. of the nature of an epidemic
- **例句**：
  - The flu epidemic affected thousands of people.
  - 流感疫情影响了数千人。
- **助记**：epi（在……之上）+ dem（人民）+ ic → 在人民之上蔓延的 → 流行病

### 5. **grieve** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：悲伤；哀悼；使悲痛
- **英文释义**：to feel intense sorrow; to mourn
- **例句**：
  - The whole nation grieved for the victims of the disaster.
  - 全国为灾难中的受害者哀悼。
- **助记**：grieve → grave（坟墓）→ 在坟墓前悲伤 → 哀悼

### 6. **hoist** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 举起；升起 n. 起重装置
- **英文释义**：v. to raise something by means of ropes and pulleys; n. an apparatus for lifting
- **例句**：
  - They hoisted the flag at sunrise.
  - 他们在日出时升起了国旗。
- **助记**：hoist 谐音"嗨嘶特"→ 用力嘶吼着举起来 → 举起

### 7. **landslide** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：山体滑坡；压倒性胜利
- **英文释义**：a collapse of a mass of earth or rock from a mountain; an overwhelming majority of votes
- **例句**：
  - Heavy rains caused a landslide that blocked the road.
  - 大雨引发了山体滑坡，堵塞了道路。
- **助记**：land（土地）+ slide（滑动）→ 土地滑动 → 山体滑坡

### 8. **mortar** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：灰浆；砂浆；迫击炮
- **英文释义**：a mixture of lime with sand and water for bonding bricks; a short cannon
- **例句**：
  - The workers used mortar to hold the bricks together.
  - 工人们用灰浆把砖块粘在一起。
- **助记**：mort（死/混合）+ ar → 把东西混合粘死 → 灰浆

### 9. **premium** `初三` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：n. 保险费；额外费用；奖金 adj. 优质的；高级的
- **英文释义**：n. an amount paid for insurance; adj. of superior quality
- **例句**：
  - Customers are willing to pay a premium for organic food.
  - 顾客愿意为有机食品支付额外费用。
- **助记**：premi（奖励/首要）+ um → 首要的/高级的 → 优质的

### 10. **scrutiny** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：仔细审查；详细检查
- **英文释义**：critical observation or examination
- **例句**：
  - The new policy came under intense scrutiny from the public.
  - 新政策受到公众的严格审查。
- **助记**：scrut（仔细看）+ iny → 仔细看 → 审查

### 高一词汇

### 11. **categorical** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：绝对的；明确的；无条件的
- **英文释义**：unambiguously explicit and direct; unconditional
- **例句**：
  - The manager gave a categorical denial of the accusations.
  - 经理对指控给予了明确的否认。
- **助记**：categor（类别）+ ical → 归入明确类别的 → 明确的

### 12. **disposition** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：性情；倾向；处置
- **英文释义**：a person's inherent qualities of mind and character; the way in which something is arranged
- **例句**：
  - She has a cheerful disposition that makes everyone feel welcome.
  - 她性情开朗，让每个人都感到受欢迎。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ position（位置）→ 把事物放到不同位置 → 安排/性情

### 13. **incessant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不停的；持续不断的
- **英文释义**：continuing without pause or interruption
- **例句**：
  - The incessant rain lasted for three days.
  - 连绵不断的雨持续了三天。
- **助记**：in（不）+ cess（停止）+ ant → 不停止的 → 不断的

### 14. **prerogative** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：特权；专有权利
- **英文释义**：a right or privilege exclusive to a particular individual or class
- **例句**：
  - It is the manager's prerogative to decide who gets promoted.
  - 决定谁升职是经理的特权。
- **助记**：pre（预先）+ rog（要求）+ ative → 预先拥有的要求权 → 特权

### 15. **vicarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：间接体验的；替代的
- **英文释义**：experienced in the imagination through the feelings or actions of another person
- **例句**：
  - She gets vicarious pleasure from reading about other people's travels.
  - 她通过阅读别人的旅行故事获得间接的快乐。
- **助记**：vicar（代理人）+ ious → 通过代理人的 → 间接体验的
