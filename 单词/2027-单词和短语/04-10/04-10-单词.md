## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **allegiance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：忠诚；效忠；忠贞
- **英文释义**：loyalty or commitment to a superior or to a group or cause
- **例句**：
  - The soldiers swore allegiance to their country.
  - 士兵们宣誓效忠他们的国家。
- **助记**：al（加强）+ leg（法律）+ iance → 在法律上加强承诺 → 忠诚

### 2. **cascade** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 瀑布；层叠 v. 倾泻；大量落下
- **英文释义**：n. a small waterfall or a series of waterfalls; v. to fall or hang in a rush
- **例句**：
  - Water cascaded down the mountainside after the rain.
  - 雨后，水顺着山坡倾泻而下。
- **助记**：casc（落下）+ ade → 水不断落下 → 瀑布/倾泻

### 3. **dilemma** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：困境；进退两难；窘境
- **英文释义**：a situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two alternatives
- **例句**：
  - She faced a dilemma: accept the job offer or stay with her family.
  - 她面临一个困境：接受工作邀请还是留在家人身边。
- **助记**：di（两个）+ lemma（命题）→ 两个命题之间难以选择 → 进退两难

### 4. **excavate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：挖掘；发掘；开凿
- **英文释义**：to make a hole or channel by digging; to remove earth carefully to find remains
- **例句**：
  - Archaeologists excavated ancient ruins near the river.
  - 考古学家在河边发掘了古代遗迹。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ cav（洞）+ ate → 从洞里挖出来 → 挖掘

### 5. **feud** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 世仇；长期不和 v. 长期争斗
- **英文释义**：n. a prolonged and bitter quarrel or dispute; v. to carry on a prolonged quarrel
- **例句**：
  - The two families have been feuding for generations.
  - 这两个家族已经争斗了好几代人。
- **助记**：feud 谐音"仇的"→ 有仇的 → 世仇

### 6. **hinder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；阻挡
- **英文释义**：to make it difficult for someone to do something or for something to happen
- **例句**：
  - Bad weather hindered the rescue operation.
  - 恶劣天气阻碍了救援行动。
- **助记**：hind（后面）+ er → 拖到后面 → 阻碍

### 7. **keen** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：敏锐的；热衷的；渴望的
- **英文释义**：having a sharp edge; eager or enthusiastic
- **例句**：
  - She has a keen interest in environmental protection.
  - 她对环境保护有浓厚的兴趣。
- **助记**：keen 谐音"肯"→ 肯干的 → 热衷的

### 8. **novice** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：新手；初学者；生手
- **英文释义**：a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
- **例句**：
  - As a novice driver, he was very cautious on the road.
  - 作为一名新手司机，他在路上非常谨慎。
- **助记**：nov（新）+ ice → 新的人 → 新手

### 9. **plead** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：恳求；辩护；认罪/不认罪
- **英文释义**：to make an emotional appeal; to present a case in a law court
- **例句**：
  - He pleaded with the judge for a lighter sentence.
  - 他恳求法官从轻判决。
- **助记**：plead 谐音"扑里的"→ 扑倒在里面恳求 → 恳求

### 10. **residue** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：残余；剩余物；残渣
- **英文释义**：a small amount of something that remains after the main part has gone
- **例句**：
  - There was a sticky residue left on the table after removing the tape.
  - 撕掉胶带后，桌子上留下了黏黏的残余物。
- **助记**：resid（留下）+ ue → 留下的东西 → 残余

### 高一词汇

### 11. **capitulate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：投降；屈服；让步
- **英文释义**：to cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; to surrender
- **例句**：
  - After weeks of negotiation, the company capitulated to the workers' demands.
  - 经过数周的谈判，公司对工人的要求做出了让步。
- **助记**：capit（头）+ ulate → 低下头 → 投降/屈服

### 12. **exorbitant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：过高的；过分的；离谱的
- **英文释义**：unreasonably high in price or amount
- **例句**：
  - The hotel charges exorbitant prices during the holiday season.
  - 假日期间酒店收费高得离谱。
- **助记**：ex（超出）+ orbit（轨道）+ ant → 超出轨道的 → 过高的/离谱的

### 13. **inexorable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可阻挡的；无情的；不可改变的
- **英文释义**：impossible to stop or prevent; impossible to persuade by request or entreaty
- **例句**：
  - The inexorable march of technology has transformed every aspect of our lives.
  - 技术不可阻挡的进步已经改变了我们生活的方方面面。
- **助记**：in（不）+ exor（请求）+ able → 不可请求改变的 → 不可阻挡的

### 14. **magnanimous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；慷慨的；高尚的
- **英文释义**：generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or less powerful person
- **例句**：
  - The winner was magnanimous in victory, praising his opponent's effort.
  - 胜利者在赢得比赛后表现得很大度，称赞了对手的努力。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（精神）+ ous → 精神上的大 → 宽宏大量的

### 15. **repudiate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：否认；拒绝；否定
- **英文释义**：to refuse to accept or be associated with; to deny the truth or validity of
- **例句**：
  - The government repudiated the allegations of corruption.
  - 政府否认了腐败指控。
- **助记**：re（反）+ pudi（羞耻）+ ate → 反过来感到羞耻而拒绝 → 否认/拒绝
