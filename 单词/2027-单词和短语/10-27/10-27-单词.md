## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **prevail** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：盛行；占上风；获胜
- **英文释义**：to be widespread or predominant; to prove more powerful than opposing forces
- **例句**：
  - Justice will eventually prevail over corruption.
  - 正义终将战胜腐败。
  - A sense of optimism prevailed throughout the community after the good news.
  - 好消息传来后，乐观情绪在整个社区蔓延开来。
- **助记**：pre（前）+ vail（力量）→ 力量在前 → 占上风

### 2. **obscure** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：模糊的；晦涩的；使模糊
- **英文释义**：not clearly expressed or easily understood; to make unclear or difficult to see
- **例句**：
  - The meaning of the poem was obscure to most readers.
  - 这首诗的含义对大多数读者来说晦涩难懂。
  - Thick clouds obscured the mountain peak from view.
  - 厚厚的云层遮住了山峰。
- **助记**：ob（覆盖）+ scure（暗）→ 被遮盖变暗 → 模糊的

### 3. **abundant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：丰富的；充裕的；大量的
- **英文释义**：existing or available in large quantities; plentiful
- **例句**：
  - The region is blessed with abundant natural resources.
  - 该地区拥有丰富的自然资源。
  - There is abundant evidence to support this theory.
  - 有大量证据支持这一理论。
- **助记**：ab（加强）+ und（波浪）+ ant → 像波浪般涌来 → 丰富的

### 4. **moderate** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：适度的；温和的；使缓和
- **英文释义**：average in amount or degree; to make or become less extreme
- **例句**：
  - She exercises at a moderate pace to avoid injury.
  - 她以适度的速度锻炼以避免受伤。
  - The teacher tried to moderate the heated debate between students.
  - 老师试图缓和学生间激烈的辩论。
- **助记**：moder（适度）+ ate → 适度的

### 5. **hazard** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：危险；危害；冒……的风险
- **英文释义**：a danger or risk; to put something at risk
- **例句**：
  - Smoking is a serious health hazard that affects millions of people.
  - 吸烟是影响数百万人的严重健康危害。
  - I would hazard a guess that the project will be delayed by two weeks.
  - 我冒昧猜测这个项目会推迟两周。
- **助记**：hazard 发音似"害怕的" → 危险让人害怕

### 6. **genuine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：真正的；真诚的；名副其实的
- **英文释义**：truly what something is said to be; authentic; sincere
- **例句**：
  - Experts confirmed that the painting was a genuine masterpiece from the Renaissance.
  - 专家证实这幅画是文艺复兴时期的真迹。
  - Her concern for the elderly was genuine and deeply felt.
  - 她对老人的关心是真诚而发自内心的。
- **助记**：genu（出生）+ ine → 天生的 → 真正的

### 7. **flourish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：茂盛生长；繁荣；蓬勃发展
- **英文释义**：to grow or develop in a healthy or vigorous way; to thrive
- **例句**：
  - The garden flourished after weeks of warm rain and sunshine.
  - 经过几周的温暖雨水和阳光，花园生长茂盛。
  - Small businesses flourished in the booming economy.
  - 小型企业在繁荣的经济中蓬勃发展。
- **助记**：flour（花）+ ish → 像花一样绽放 → 繁荣

### 8. **discard** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：丢弃；抛弃；放弃
- **英文释义**：to get rid of something as no longer useful or desirable
- **例句**：
  - Please discard any expired food from the refrigerator.
  - 请把冰箱里过期的食物丢掉。
  - She discarded her old habits and embraced a healthier lifestyle.
  - 她摒弃了旧习惯，拥抱更健康的生活方式。
- **助记**：dis（去除）+ card（卡片）→ 把不要的牌丢掉 → 丢弃

### 9. **comply** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：遵从；服从；依从
- **英文释义**：to act in accordance with a wish, rule, or command
- **例句**：
  - All employees must comply with the company's safety regulations.
  - 所有员工必须遵守公司的安全规定。
  - The factory refused to comply with the new environmental standards.
  - 该工厂拒绝遵从新的环保标准。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ ply（折叠）→ 一起折叠服从 → 遵从

### 10. **trivial** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：琐碎的；不重要的；微不足道的
- **英文释义**：of little value or importance; insignificant
- **例句**：
  - Don't waste your energy on trivial matters that don't affect the outcome.
  - 不要把精力浪费在不影响结果的琐碎小事上。
  - What seemed like a trivial error turned into a major problem.
  - 看似微不足道的错误变成了大问题。
- **助记**：tri（三）+ via（路）→ 三岔路口的闲话 → 琐碎的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **rhetoric** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：修辞学；花言巧语；雄辩术
- **英文释义**：the art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing; language designed to have a persuasive effect
- **例句**：
  - The politician's speech was full of empty rhetoric but lacked substance.
  - 这位政客的演讲充满了空洞的花言巧语，缺乏实质内容。
  - Studying rhetoric helps students become more effective communicators.
  - 学习修辞学有助于学生成为更有效的沟通者。
- **助记**：rhet（说话）+ oric → 说话的艺术 → 修辞学

### 12. **epistemology** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：认识论；知识论
- **英文释义**：the branch of philosophy concerned with the theory of knowledge, especially its nature and limits
- **例句**：
  - Epistemology asks fundamental questions about how we know what we know.
  - 认识论提出关于我们如何知道自己所知的根本问题。
  - The debate between rationalism and empiricism is central to epistemology.
  - 理性主义与经验主义之间的争论是认识论的核心。
- **助记**：episteme（知识）+ ology（学科）→ 研究知识的学问 → 认识论

### 13. **assimilate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：吸收；同化；融入
- **英文释义**：to absorb and integrate information or culture; to cause to become similar
- **例句**：
  - It takes time for immigrants to assimilate into a new culture.
  - 移民融入新文化需要时间。
  - Students need to assimilate a large amount of information before the exam.
  - 学生需要在考试前吸收大量信息。
- **助记**：as（向）+ simil（相似）+ ate → 使变得相似 → 同化

### 14. **jurisprudence** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：法学；法理学
- **英文释义**：the theory or philosophy of law; a legal system
- **例句**：
  - She devoted her career to the study of jurisprudence and legal ethics.
  - 她毕生致力于法理学和法律伦理学的研究。
  - Different countries have different traditions of jurisprudence.
  - 不同国家有不同的法学传统。
- **助记**：juris（法律）+ prudence（谨慎/学问）→ 法律的学问 → 法学

### 15. **paradigm** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：范式；典范；模式
- **英文释义**：a typical example or pattern of something; a model or framework
- **例句**：
  - The invention of the internet created a paradigm shift in how people communicate.
  - 互联网的发明在人们的交流方式上创造了一个范式转变。
  - This experiment serves as a paradigm for future research in the field.
  - 这个实验为该领域的未来研究提供了范式。
- **助记**：para（旁边）+ digm（展示）→ 展示在旁边的样本 → 范式
