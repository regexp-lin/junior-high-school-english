## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **exempt** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：免除的；豁免的；使免除
- **英文释义**：free from an obligation or liability; to free from a duty
- **例句**：
  - Students under sixteen are exempt from paying the entrance fee.
  - 十六岁以下的学生免交入场费。
  - The new policy exempts small businesses from certain taxes.
  - 新政策免除了小企业的某些税款。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ empt（拿）→ 拿出来不用承担 → 免除的

### 2. **ponder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：沉思；仔细考虑
- **英文释义**：to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
- **例句**：
  - She pondered the question for a long time before answering.
  - 她仔细考虑了很久才回答这个问题。
  - The committee is pondering whether to approve the new building plan.
  - 委员会正在考虑是否批准新的建筑计划。
- **助记**：ponder 发音似"胖的" → 脑袋沉甸甸地思考 → 沉思

### 3. **volatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不稳定的；易变的；易挥发的
- **英文释义**：liable to change rapidly and unpredictably; evaporating quickly
- **例句**：
  - The stock market has been extremely volatile this week.
  - 本周股市极其不稳定。
  - His volatile temper makes it difficult to work with him.
  - 他反复无常的脾气让人很难和他共事。
- **助记**：volat（飞）+ ile → 像飞一样变化快 → 不稳定的

### 4. **cultivate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：培养；耕种；栽培
- **英文释义**：to prepare and use land for growing crops; to develop a quality or skill
- **例句**：
  - Farmers cultivate rice in the paddy fields during the spring season.
  - 农民在春季在稻田里种植水稻。
  - Reading widely can help cultivate critical thinking skills.
  - 广泛阅读有助于培养批判性思维能力。
- **助记**：cult（耕作）+ ivate → 耕作 → 培养

### 5. **inhabit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：居住于；栖息于
- **英文释义**：to live in or occupy a place
- **例句**：
  - Polar bears inhabit the Arctic regions of the world.
  - 北极熊栖息于世界的北极地区。
  - The island has been inhabited by indigenous people for thousands of years.
  - 这个岛屿上有原住民居住了数千年。
- **助记**：in（里面）+ habit（居住）→ 住在里面 → 居住于

### 6. **remnant** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：残余物；残留部分；遗迹
- **英文释义**：a small remaining quantity of something; a surviving trace
- **例句**：
  - The ruins are remnants of an ancient civilization.
  - 这些废墟是古代文明的遗迹。
  - Only a few remnants of the original forest still exist.
  - 原始森林仅存少许残余。
- **助记**：re（留下）+ mnant（留）→ 留下来的 → 残余物

### 7. **coerce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：强迫；胁迫；逼迫
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone to do something by using force or threats
- **例句**：
  - No one should be coerced into signing an agreement against their will.
  - 任何人都不应被强迫违背意愿签署协议。
  - The witness claimed he was coerced into giving a false statement.
  - 证人声称自己被胁迫做了虚假陈述。
- **助记**：co（共同）+ erce（力量）→ 用力量控制 → 强迫

### 8. **meticulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；极其仔细的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - The surgeon is meticulous in every procedure she performs.
  - 这位外科医生在她执行的每个手术中都一丝不苟。
  - Meticulous planning is the key to a successful event.
  - 周密的计划是活动成功的关键。
- **助记**：metic（恐惧）+ ulous → 怕出错而仔细 → 一丝不苟的

### 9. **deviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：偏离；偏差；背离
- **英文释义**：to depart from an established course or accepted standard
- **例句**：
  - The bus driver deviated from the usual route to avoid the accident.
  - 公交司机偏离了常规路线以避开事故。
  - He never deviates from his strict daily routine.
  - 他从不偏离严格的日常作息。
- **助记**：de（离开）+ via（路）+ te → 离开道路 → 偏离

### 10. **unanimous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一致同意的；全体一致的
- **英文释义**：fully in agreement; with everyone's support
- **例句**：
  - The decision to raise funds for the charity was unanimous.
  - 为慈善筹款的决定获得了全体一致通过。
  - The jury reached a unanimous verdict of not guilty.
  - 陪审团做出了无罪的一致裁决。
- **助记**：un（一个）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 一条心 → 一致同意的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **surreptitious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：偷偷摸摸的；秘密的；暗中的
- **英文释义**：kept secret, especially because it would not be approved of
- **例句**：
  - She took a surreptitious glance at her phone during the meeting.
  - 她在会议中偷偷瞄了一眼手机。
  - The spy made surreptitious contact with his informant in the park.
  - 间谍在公园里秘密地与线人接触。
- **助记**：sur（下面）+ rept（爬）+ itious → 在下面偷偷爬 → 偷偷摸摸的

### 12. **didactic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：说教的；教诲的；教育性的
- **英文释义**：intended to teach, particularly in having moral instruction as an ulterior motive
- **例句**：
  - Aesop's fables are didactic stories that teach moral lessons through animal characters.
  - 伊索寓言是通过动物角色教导道德课程的说教故事。
  - The professor's didactic approach bored some students but educated many.
  - 教授说教式的教学方法让一些学生感到无聊，但也教育了许多人。
- **助记**：didact（教导）+ ic → 教导性的 → 说教的

### 13. **circumlocution** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：迂回说法；绕弯子；赘述
- **英文释义**：the use of many words where fewer would do, especially in a deliberate attempt to be vague
- **例句**：
  - The politician's answer was full of circumlocution, avoiding the actual question.
  - 政客的回答充满了绕弯子，回避了实际问题。
  - Instead of circumlocution, just say what you mean directly.
  - 别绕弯子了，直接说你想表达什么。
- **助记**：circum（绕圈）+ locution（说话）→ 绕着圈子说话 → 迂回说法

### 14. **inscrutable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可理解的；高深莫测的；难以捉摸的
- **英文释义**：impossible to understand or interpret
- **例句**：
  - The teacher wore an inscrutable expression that left everyone guessing.
  - 老师面带高深莫测的表情，让所有人都在猜测。
  - The meaning behind the ancient symbols remained inscrutable for centuries.
  - 古代符号背后的含义几个世纪以来一直难以理解。
- **助记**：in（不）+ scrut（检查）+ able → 无法检查的 → 不可理解的

### 15. **magniloquent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：夸张的；大言不惭的；高谈阔论的
- **英文释义**：using high-flown or bombastic language
- **例句**：
  - His magniloquent speeches impressed some but annoyed others.
  - 他夸张的演讲让一些人印象深刻，却让另一些人反感。
  - The author's magniloquent style made the book difficult to read.
  - 作者大言不惭的文风使这本书很难读。
- **助记**：magni（大）+ loqu（说话）+ ent → 说大话 → 夸张的
