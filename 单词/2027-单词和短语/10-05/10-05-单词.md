## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **arbitrary** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：任意的；武断的；专横的
- **英文释义**：based on random choice rather than reason; unrestrained
- **例句**：
  - The teacher warned against arbitrary grading without clear criteria.
  - 老师告诫不要没有明确标准就随意打分。
  - He made an arbitrary decision without consulting the team.
  - 他未与团队商量就做了武断决定。
- **助记**：arbitr（裁决）+ ary → 凭个人裁决 → 武断

### 2. **competent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：胜任的；有能力的；称职的
- **英文释义**：having the necessary ability, knowledge, or skill
- **例句**：
  - Only competent drivers should operate heavy machinery.
  - 只有合格驾驶员才能操作重型机械。
  - She proved competent at managing tight deadlines.
  - 她证明自己能胜任紧迫的截止日期。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ pet（追求）+ ent → 能一起完成任务的 → 胜任

### 3. **deduce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：演绎；推断；推论
- **英文释义**：to arrive at a conclusion by reasoning from known facts
- **例句**：
  - From the muddy footprints, the detective deduced someone had entered through the garden.
  - 从泥脚印侦探推断有人从花园进入。
  - We can deduce the author's attitude from word choice in the text.
  - 可从用词推断作者态度。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ duce（引导）→ 向下引导出结论 → 演绎

### 4. **elicit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：引出；探出；套出
- **英文释义**：to draw out a response, answer, or fact from someone
- **例句**：
  - Skillful interviewers can elicit honest answers from shy guests.
  - 熟练的采访者能让害羞的嘉宾说出真心话。
  - The teacher's question elicited lively discussion in class.
  - 老师的问题引发了课堂热烈讨论。
- **助记**：e（出）+ licit（引诱）→ 引出

### 5. **fabricate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：捏造；编造；制造
- **英文释义**：to invent or concoct something false; to construct
- **例句**：
  - He was accused of fabricating evidence in the investigation.
  - 他被指控在调查中捏造证据。
  - The workshop fabricates metal parts for aircraft.
  - 该车间为飞机制造金属零件。
- **助记**：fabric（织物）+ ate → 编织出来 → 编造/制造

### 6. **grievous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严重的；令人悲痛的；惨重的
- **英文释义**：very serious or severe; causing great suffering
- **例句**：
  - The accident caused grievous injuries to several passengers.
  - 事故导致几名乘客重伤。
  - It was a grievous mistake to ignore the safety warnings.
  - 忽视安全警告是严重错误。
- **助记**：grief（悲痛）+ ous → 令人悲痛的

### 7. **hypocritical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚伪的；伪善的
- **英文释义**：behaving in a way that contradicts one's stated beliefs
- **例句**：
  - It is hypocritical to criticize littering while dropping trash yourself.
  - 自己扔垃圾却批评别人乱扔是虚伪的。
  - The politician gave a hypocritical speech about honesty.
  - 那位政治家就诚实发表了虚伪演说。
- **助记**：hypo（在下）+ cris（判断）→ 在下面另有一套判断 → 虚伪

### 8. **illusory** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚幻的；错觉的；迷惑人的
- **英文释义**：based on illusion; not real; deceptive
- **例句**：
  - The sense of security proved illusory when the storm hit.
  - 暴风雨来临时，安全感证明是虚幻的。
  - Mirages in the desert are illusory images of water.
  - 沙漠中的海市蜃楼是水的幻象。
- **助记**：illusion（幻觉）+ ary → 幻觉的

### 9. **jovial** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：快活的；开朗的；友好的
- **英文释义**：cheerful and friendly; full of good humor
- **例句**：
  - The jovial host made every guest feel welcome at the party.
  - 开朗的主人让每位客人在聚会上都感自在。
  - His jovial laugh filled the room with warmth.
  - 他爽朗的笑声让房间充满温暖。
- **助记**：Jov（罗马主神朱庇特，主欢乐）+ ial → 如朱庇特般快乐 → 快活

### 10. **kindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：点燃；激起；引起（兴趣、感情）
- **英文释义**：to set on fire; to arouse or inspire an emotion or interest
- **例句**：
  - Dry leaves kindle easily in a campfire.
  - 干树叶在篝火中容易点燃。
  - The teacher's story kindled a love of history in the students.
  - 老师的故事激发学生对历史的热爱。
- **助记**：kindle 联想 candle（蜡烛）→ 点亮 → 点燃

### 高一词汇

### 11. **laconic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：简洁的；寡言的；精炼的
- **英文释义**：using very few words; terse; concise to the point of bluntness
- **例句**：
  - His laconic reply — "No." — ended the debate abruptly.
  - 他简短的回答“不”让辩论戛然而止。
  - Spartan warriors were famous for laconic wit in ancient Greece.
  - 斯巴达战士在古希腊以言简意赅著称。
- **助记**：来自 Laconia（斯巴达地区）→ 说话像斯巴达人一样简短

### 12. **nihilism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：虚无主义；否定一切
- **英文释义**：the rejection of religious and moral principles; belief that nothing matters
- **例句**：
  - The novel explores nihilism after the loss of traditional values.
  - 这部小说探讨传统价值丧失后的虚无主义。
  - He warned against falling into nihilism when facing failure.
  - 他告诫面对失败时不要陷入虚无主义。
- **助记**：nihil（无）+ ism → 一切皆无的主义

### 13. **obfuscate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使模糊；使混淆；故意使费解
- **英文释义**：to make something unclear or difficult to understand deliberately
- **例句**：
  - The spokesperson obfuscated the facts with vague statistics.
  - 发言人用含糊数据混淆事实。
  - Jargon can obfuscate simple ideas in academic writing.
  - 行话可能让学术写作中的简单想法变得费解。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ scure（暗）→ 弄暗 → 模糊

### 14. **partisan** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：偏袒的；党派性强的；游击队员
- **英文释义**：strongly supporting one side; a strong supporter of a party or cause
- **例句**：
  - Partisan news sites rarely present balanced views.
  - 党派性新闻网站很少呈现平衡观点。
  - During the war, partisans hid in the forest and attacked supply lines.
  - 战争期间游击队员藏在林中袭击补给线。
- **助记**：party（党）+ san → 党派的

### 15. **quandary** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：困境；两难；迷惑
- **英文释义**：a state of perplexity or uncertainty over what to do
- **例句**：
  - She faced a moral quandary about whether to report her friend.
  - 她面临是否举报朋友的道德两难。
  - The budget cuts put the principal in a quandary.
  - 预算削减让校长陷入困境。
- **助记**：谐音“困得里”→ 困在里头 → 困境
