## 📖 短语详解

### 初三短语

### 1. **add fuel to the fire** `初三`

- **中文释义**：火上浇油；使事态更糟
- **用法说明**：指言论或行为使冲突、愤怒加剧。
- **例句**：
  - Insulting comments only added fuel to the fire during the argument.
  - 争吵中侮辱性的话只会火上浇油。
  - Raising prices during a shortage added fuel to the fire of public anger.
  - 短缺时涨价更是火上浇油，激怒公众。
- **近义短语**：make matters worse; exacerbate; inflame tensions
- **易混淆短语**：add fuel to the fire（火上加火）vs play with fire（冒险）—— 不同

### 2. **bite off more than one can chew** `初三`

- **中文释义**：贪多嚼不烂；承担力所不及的事
- **用法说明**：警告不要承诺或承担超出能力的任务。
- **例句**：
  - By signing three contracts at once, he bit off more than he could chew.
  - 他同时签了三份合同，贪多嚼不烂。
  - Don't bite off more than you can chew in your first semester of college.
  - 大学第一学期别选课或任务太多。
- **近义短语**：overstretch; take on too much; eyes bigger than one's stomach
- **易混淆短语**：bite off more than one can chew（贪多）vs bite the bullet（咬紧牙关忍受）—— 不同

### 3. **call it a day** `初三`

- **中文释义**：收工；到此为止；今天就这样
- **用法说明**：表示结束当天工作或讨论。
- **例句**：
  - After ten hours of moving furniture, we called it a day.
  - 搬了十小时家具后，我们收工了。
  - The committee couldn't agree, so the chair called it a day and scheduled another meeting.
  - 委员会无法达成一致，主席宣布休会并另定会议。
- **近义短语**：wrap up; stop for the day; knock off work
- **易混淆短语**：call it a day（收工）vs call it a night（类似，晚上结束）—— 相近

### 4. **get out of hand** `初三`

- **中文释义**：失控；难以控制
- **用法说明**：situation gets out of hand 表示局面恶化到无法驾驭。
- **例句**：
  - The protest got out of hand when a few people started smashing windows.
  - 几人开始砸窗后，抗议失控。
  - If spending gets out of hand, the family budget will collapse.
  - 若开支失控，家庭预算会崩溃。
- **近义短语**：get out of control; escalate uncontrollably; spiral
- **易混淆短语**：get out of hand（失控）vs get the upper hand（占上风）—— 不同

### 高一短语

### 5. **hit the sack** `高一`

- **中文释义**：上床睡觉；就寝
- **用法说明**：非正式口语，同 hit the hay。
- **例句**：
  - We were so exhausted after hiking that we hit the sack immediately after dinner.
  - 徒步后我们累坏了，晚饭后倒头就睡。
  - He usually hits the sack around midnight on weekdays.
  - 工作日他通常午夜左右睡觉。
- **近义短语**：go to bed; turn in; sleep
- **易混淆短语**：hit the sack（睡觉）vs hit the books（用功读书）—— 不同
