## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **harvest** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 收获；收成 v. 收割；收获
- **英文释义**：n. the process or period of gathering crops; v. to gather crops from the fields
- **例句**：
  - The farmers are busy with the harvest in autumn.
  - 农民们秋天忙着收获。
- **助记**：har（谐音"哈"）+ vest（背心）→ 穿着背心哈哈笑地去收获

### 2. **ignorant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无知的；愚昧的
- **英文释义**：lacking knowledge or awareness in general; uneducated
- **例句**：
  - He was ignorant of the rules and broke them accidentally.
  - 他不了解规则，无意中违反了。
- **助记**：ignore（忽视）+ ant → 总是忽视学习的 → 无知的

### 3. **loyal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：忠诚的；忠实的
- **英文释义**：giving or showing firm and constant support or allegiance to a person or institution
- **例句**：
  - A loyal friend will stand by you through thick and thin.
  - 忠诚的朋友会在任何情况下支持你。
- **助记**：loyal 和 royal（皇家的）很像 → 对皇室忠诚的

### 4. **mend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：修理；修补；改善
- **英文释义**：to repair something that is broken or damaged
- **例句**：
  - Can you mend this broken chair for me?
  - 你能帮我修一下这把坏椅子吗？
- **助记**：mend → amend（修改）的简化版 → 修补

### 5. **neutral** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：中立的；中性的
- **英文释义**：not supporting or helping either side in a conflict; impartial
- **例句**：
  - Switzerland remained neutral during both World Wars.
  - 瑞士在两次世界大战中都保持中立。
- **助记**：neu（谐音"牛"）+ tral → 牛一样稳稳地站在中间不偏不倚 → 中立的

### 6. **overnight** `初三` adv./adj.

- **中文释义**：adv. 一夜之间；在晚上 adj. 夜间的；突然的
- **英文释义**：during or for the night; happening suddenly or very quickly
- **例句**：
  - He became famous overnight after the video went viral.
  - 视频走红后他一夜成名。
- **助记**：over（经过）+ night（夜晚）→ 经过一个夜晚 → 一夜之间

### 7. **perfume** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：香水；芳香
- **英文释义**：a fragrant liquid typically made from essential oils, used to give a pleasant smell
- **例句**：
  - She wore a delicate perfume that smelled like roses.
  - 她喷了一种淡雅的香水，闻起来像玫瑰。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ fume（烟/气）→ 完全充满香气 → 香水

### 8. **rigid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：僵硬的；严格的；死板的
- **英文释义**：unable to bend or be forced out of shape; strict and inflexible
- **例句**：
  - The school has rigid rules about uniforms.
  - 学校对校服有严格的规定。
- **助记**：rig（固定装置）+ id → 被固定住的 → 僵硬的

### 9. **skeleton** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：骨架；骨骼；框架
- **英文释义**：the internal framework of bones in a human or animal body
- **例句**：
  - We studied the human skeleton in biology class.
  - 我们在生物课上学习了人体骨骼。
- **助记**：skele（谐音"死壳了"）+ ton → 只剩下壳了 → 骨架

### 10. **tremble** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：颤抖；发抖；哆嗦
- **英文释义**：to shake involuntarily, typically as a result of anxiety, excitement, or frailty
- **例句**：
  - Her hands trembled with fear when she heard the thunder.
  - 听到雷声时，她的手因恐惧而发抖。
- **助记**：t + remble → resemble（相似） → 抖得像筛子一样

### 高一词汇

### 11. **anonymous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：匿名的；无名的
- **英文释义**：not identified by name; of unknown origin
- **例句**：
  - The donation was made by an anonymous donor.
  - 这笔捐款来自一位匿名捐赠者。
- **助记**：an（无）+ onym（名字）+ ous → 无名字的 → 匿名的

### 12. **consolidate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：巩固；合并；加强
- **英文释义**：to make something physically stronger or more solid; to combine into a single unit
- **例句**：
  - The company consolidated its position in the market through mergers.
  - 该公司通过并购巩固了其市场地位。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ solid（坚固的）+ ate → 一起变得坚固 → 巩固

### 13. **disclosure** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：披露；公开；揭露
- **英文释义**：the action of making new or secret information known
- **例句**：
  - The disclosure of the scandal shocked the public.
  - 丑闻的披露震惊了公众。
- **助记**：dis（去掉）+ close（关闭）+ ure → 打开关闭的东西 → 揭露

### 14. **fluctuate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：波动；涨落；变动
- **英文释义**：to rise and fall irregularly in number or amount
- **例句**：
  - Oil prices fluctuate according to supply and demand.
  - 油价根据供需而波动。
- **助记**：fluct（流动）+ uate → 像水一样流动变化 → 波动

### 15. **proximity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：接近；邻近；附近
- **英文释义**：nearness in distance, time, or relationship
- **例句**：
  - The proximity of the school to our home is very convenient.
  - 学校离我们家很近，非常方便。
- **助记**：proxim（接近）+ ity → 接近的状态 → 邻近
