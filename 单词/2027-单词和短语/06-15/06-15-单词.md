## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **amass** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：积累；收集
- **英文释义**：gather together or accumulate a large amount of something
- **例句**：
  - Over the years, she amassed an impressive collection of rare books.
  - 多年来，她积累了令人印象深刻的珍稀书籍收藏。
- **助记**：a + mass（大量）→ 聚集大量 → 积累

### 2. **captivate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：迷住；吸引
- **英文释义**：attract and hold the interest and attention of; charm
- **例句**：
  - The magician captivated the audience with his incredible tricks.
  - 魔术师以他不可思议的技巧迷住了观众。
- **助记**：captiv（抓住）+ ate → 抓住注意力 → 迷住

### 3. **contemplate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：沉思；凝视；考虑
- **英文释义**：look thoughtfully at for a long time; think about something
- **例句**：
  - She sat by the lake, contemplating her future.
  - 她坐在湖边，沉思着她的未来。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ templ（庙宇）+ ate → 在庙宇中一起思考 → 沉思

### 4. **defiant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：反抗的；挑战的
- **英文释义**：showing open resistance or bold disobedience
- **例句**：
  - The defiant student refused to follow the school's dress code.
  - 这个反抗的学生拒绝遵守学校的着装规定。
- **助记**：de（反）+ fi（信任）+ ant → 反对信任 → 反抗的

### 5. **exemplify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：例证；是……的典范
- **英文释义**：be a typical example of; illustrate by giving an example
- **例句**：
  - Her career exemplifies the importance of hard work and dedication.
  - 她的职业生涯例证了努力工作和奉献的重要性。
- **助记**：exempl（例子）+ ify → 用例子展示 → 例证

### 6. **fragile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易碎的；脆弱的
- **英文释义**：easily broken or damaged; delicate
- **例句**：
  - Please handle the glassware carefully — it's very fragile.
  - 请小心处理玻璃器皿——它们很易碎。
- **助记**：frag（碎裂）+ ile → 容易碎裂的 → 易碎的

### 7. **hamper** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：妨碍；阻碍
- **英文释义**：hinder or impede the movement or progress of
- **例句**：
  - Heavy traffic hampered our efforts to arrive on time.
  - 拥堵的交通妨碍了我们准时到达。
- **助记**：hamper 联想 "hammer"（锤子）→ 像锤子一样阻挡前进 → 妨碍

### 8. **instill** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：灌输；逐步培养
- **英文释义**：gradually but firmly establish (an idea or attitude) in a person's mind
- **例句**：
  - The teacher tried to instill a love of reading in her students.
  - 老师试图在学生中培养对阅读的热爱。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ still（滴）→ 一滴一滴地进入 → 灌输

### 9. **meticulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；极其细心的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - The scientist was meticulous in recording every detail of the experiment.
  - 这位科学家在记录实验的每一个细节时一丝不苟。
- **助记**：met（恐惧）+ iculous → 害怕出错 → 一丝不苟的

### 10. **replenish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：补充；重新装满
- **英文释义**：fill something up again; restore to the former level or condition
- **例句**：
  - We need to replenish our food supplies before the storm arrives.
  - 我们需要在暴风雨来临前补充食物供应。
- **助记**：re（重新）+ plen（满）+ ish → 重新装满 → 补充

### 高一词汇

### 11. **circumlocution** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：绕弯子；迂回说法
- **英文释义**：the use of many words where fewer would do, especially to avoid saying something directly
- **例句**：
  - The politician's answer was full of circumlocution and avoided the real question.
  - 这位政客的回答满是迂回说法，回避了真正的问题。
- **助记**：circum（绕圈）+ locut（说话）+ ion → 绕着圈说话 → 绕弯子

### 12. **extemporaneous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：即兴的；无准备的
- **英文释义**：spoken or done without preparation
- **例句**：
  - She gave an extemporaneous speech that impressed the judges.
  - 她发表了一篇即兴演讲，给评委留下了深刻印象。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ tempor（时间）+ aneous → 在时间之外（没准备的时间内）→ 即兴的

### 13. **idiosyncratic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：独特的；特殊癖好的
- **英文释义**：relating to or peculiar to an individual
- **例句**：
  - His idiosyncratic teaching style was loved by some students and puzzled others.
  - 他独特的教学风格受到一些学生的喜爱，也让其他人困惑。
- **助记**：idio（个人的）+ syn（一起）+ cratic → 个人的独特组合 → 独特的

### 14. **pugnacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；爱争吵的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue, quarrel, or fight
- **例句**：
  - The pugnacious boxer never backed down from a challenge.
  - 这位好斗的拳击手从不在挑战面前退缩。
- **助记**：pugn（拳头/打斗）+ acious → 喜欢打斗的 → 好斗的

### 15. **solicitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：关心的；焦虑的；热切的
- **英文释义**：characterized by or showing interest or concern
- **例句**：
  - The nurse was solicitous about the patient's comfort.
  - 护士非常关心病人的舒适度。
- **助记**：solicit（恳求/关心）+ ous → 充满关心的 → 关心的
