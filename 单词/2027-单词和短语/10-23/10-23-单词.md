## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **articulate** `初三` v./adj.

- **中文释义**：清楚表达；善于表达的；发音清晰的
- **英文释义**：to express fluently and coherently; having clear speech
- **例句**：
  - She articulated her ideas so well that everyone understood immediately.
  - 她把想法表达得如此清楚，每个人都立即理解了。
  - An articulate speaker can make complex topics accessible to all.
  - 善于表达的演讲者能让所有人都理解复杂话题。
- **助记**：artic（关节）+ ulate → 像关节般灵活地表达 → 清晰表达

### 2. **bland** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：平淡的；乏味的；温和的
- **英文释义**：lacking strong features or characteristics; not stimulating
- **例句**：
  - The hospital food was bland and tasteless.
  - 医院的食物平淡无味。
  - His bland response to the crisis disappointed many followers.
  - 他对危机的平淡回应令许多追随者失望。
- **助记**：bland 联想 blank（空白）→ 空白无味 → 平淡

### 3. **constrain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：约束；限制；强迫
- **英文释义**：to severely restrict the scope, extent, or activity of; to compel
- **例句**：
  - Budget cuts constrained the school's ability to hire new teachers.
  - 预算削减限制了学校雇用新教师的能力。
  - She felt constrained by the rigid rules of the organization.
  - 她感到被组织严格的规则所束缚。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ strain（拉紧）→ 拉紧在一起 → 约束

### 4. **detract** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减损；降低；转移（注意力）
- **英文释义**：to reduce or take away the worth or value of something
- **例句**：
  - The noisy air conditioner detracted from the enjoyment of the concert.
  - 嘈杂的空调减损了欣赏音乐会的乐趣。
  - A few spelling errors should not detract from the quality of the essay.
  - 几个拼写错误不应降低文章的质量。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ tract（拉）→ 往下拉 → 减损

### 5. **exuberant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情洋溢的；精力充沛的；茂盛的
- **英文释义**：filled with or characterized by a lively energy and excitement
- **例句**：
  - The children were exuberant on the last day of school.
  - 孩子们在学期最后一天热情洋溢。
  - The garden was full of exuberant flowers in every color.
  - 花园里开满了各种颜色的茂盛花朵。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ uber（丰富）+ ant → 丰富溢出 → 热情洋溢

### 6. **fickle** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：善变的；不忠的；反复无常的
- **英文释义**：changing frequently, especially as regards one's loyalties or affections
- **例句**：
  - Fashion is fickle; what is trendy today may be outdated tomorrow.
  - 时尚是善变的；今天流行的明天可能就过时了。
  - Fickle weather forced the organizers to prepare both indoor and outdoor plans.
  - 反复无常的天气迫使组织者准备了室内和室外两套方案。
- **助记**：fickle 联想 pickle（腌菜/困境）→ 让人处于困境的善变

### 7. **grudging** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不情愿的；勉强的；吝啬的
- **英文释义**：given, granted, or allowed only reluctantly or resentfully
- **例句**：
  - He gave a grudging apology after being pressed by his parents.
  - 在父母的逼迫下他勉强道了歉。
  - The rival team earned his grudging respect after the tough match.
  - 经过那场艰苦的比赛，对手赢得了他不情愿的尊重。
- **助记**：grudge（怨恨）+ ing → 带着怨恨的 → 不情愿的

### 8. **hasten** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加速；催促；赶紧
- **英文释义**：to be quick to do something; to cause something to happen sooner
- **例句**：
  - The government hastened to reassure the public after the incident.
  - 事件发生后政府赶紧安抚公众。
  - Smoking hastens the aging process of the skin.
  - 吸烟加速皮肤老化过程。
- **助记**：haste（匆忙）+ n → 使匆忙 → 加速

### 9. **inhibit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：抑制；阻止；妨碍
- **英文释义**：to hinder, restrain, or prevent an action or process
- **例句**：
  - Fear of failure can inhibit creativity and innovation.
  - 对失败的恐惧会抑制创造力和创新。
  - Certain chemicals inhibit the growth of harmful bacteria.
  - 某些化学物质能抑制有害细菌的生长。
- **助记**：in（内）+ hibit（持有/保持）→ 保持在内 → 抑制

### 10. **mosaic** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：马赛克；镶嵌画；拼图
- **英文释义**：a picture or pattern produced by arranging small colored pieces
- **例句**：
  - The ancient Roman mosaic was discovered beneath the modern building.
  - 在现代建筑下方发现了一幅古罗马马赛克镶嵌画。
  - The city is a cultural mosaic of traditions from around the world.
  - 这座城市是来自世界各地传统的文化拼图。
- **助记**：mosaic → 马赛克（音译）→ 镶嵌画

### 高一词汇

### 11. **myriad** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：无数的；大量的；众多
- **英文释义**：a countless or extremely great number
- **例句**：
  - The city offers myriad opportunities for young professionals.
  - 这座城市为年轻专业人士提供了无数机会。
  - Myriad stars filled the sky on that clear winter night.
  - 在那个晴朗的冬夜，无数星星布满天空。
- **助记**：myriad 源自希腊语"一万"→ 引申为"无数"

### 12. **oblivious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不知道的；未察觉的；浑然不觉的
- **英文释义**：not aware of or not concerned about what is happening around one
- **例句**：
  - He was so focused on his phone that he was oblivious to the announcement.
  - 他太专注于手机以至于对广播浑然不觉。
  - The cat was oblivious to the dog approaching from behind.
  - 猫对从后面接近的狗浑然不知。
- **助记**：ob（向）+ livious（遗忘）→ 忘记了 → 浑然不觉

### 13. **propensity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：倾向；习性；癖好
- **英文释义**：an inclination or natural tendency to behave in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - He has a propensity for taking risks that worries his family.
  - 他爱冒险的倾向让家人担忧。
  - The region has a propensity for heavy rainfall during autumn.
  - 该地区在秋季有降大雨的倾向。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ pens（倾斜）+ ity → 向前倾 → 倾向

### 14. **sardonic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：嘲讽的；讥笑的；冷嘲热讽的
- **英文释义**：grimly mocking or cynical
- **例句**：
  - His sardonic smile suggested he didn't believe a word she said.
  - 他嘲讽的微笑暗示他对她说的一个字都不信。
  - The critic wrote a sardonic review of the overhyped movie.
  - 评论家为这部被过度炒作的电影写了一篇冷嘲热讽的评论。
- **助记**：来自 Sardinia（撒丁岛）传说中令人苦笑的植物 → 苦笑/嘲讽

### 15. **ubiquitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；普遍存在的
- **英文释义**：present, appearing, or found everywhere
- **例句**：
  - Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern daily life.
  - 智能手机在现代日常生活中已无处不在。
  - Coffee shops are ubiquitous in this part of the city.
  - 咖啡店在城市的这个区域随处可见。
- **助记**：ubique（到处）+ ous → 到处都有 → 无处不在
