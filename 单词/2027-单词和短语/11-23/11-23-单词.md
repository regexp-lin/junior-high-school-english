## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **rectify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：纠正；改正；矫正
- **英文释义**：to put right; to correct an error or fault
- **例句**：
  - The company quickly rectified the billing error and issued a refund.
  - 公司迅速纠正了账单错误并退了款。
  - We need to rectify the situation before it gets worse.
  - 我们需要在情况恶化之前纠正它。
- **助记**：rect（直）+ ify → 使变直 → 纠正

### 2. **sardonic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：嘲讽的；讥笑的；冷嘲热讽的
- **英文释义**：grimly mocking or cynical
- **例句**：
  - His sardonic smile suggested he didn't believe a word of the explanation.
  - 他嘲讽的微笑暗示他根本不相信那番解释。
  - The critic wrote a sardonic review of the overhyped movie.
  - 评论家对这部过度炒作的电影写了一篇冷嘲热讽的评论。
- **助记**：sardonic 源自希腊一种植物 sardonion，食用后面部抽搐似笑 → 嘲讽的笑

### 3. **bifurcate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：分叉；分成两支
- **英文释义**：to divide into two branches or forks
- **例句**：
  - The river bifurcates at this point, with one branch flowing east and the other west.
  - 河流在这里分叉，一支向东流，另一支向西流。
  - The road bifurcates ahead — take the left fork to reach the village.
  - 前方道路分叉——走左边的岔道到达村庄。
- **助记**：bi（二）+ furc（叉）+ ate → 分成两个叉 → 分叉

### 4. **ominous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不祥的；凶兆的；预示危险的
- **英文释义**：giving the impression that something bad or unpleasant is going to happen
- **例句**：
  - The ominous dark clouds gathering on the horizon warned of an approaching storm.
  - 地平线上聚集的不祥乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。
  - There was an ominous silence after the teacher asked who broke the window.
  - 老师问谁打碎了窗户后出现了一阵不祥的沉默。
- **助记**：omin（预兆）+ ous → 有不好预兆的 → 不祥的

### 5. **confiscate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：没收；充公；查抄
- **英文释义**：to take or seize someone's property with authority
- **例句**：
  - The teacher confiscated the student's phone after catching him texting during class.
  - 老师在发现学生上课发短信后没收了他的手机。
  - Customs officers confiscated the smuggled goods at the border.
  - 海关官员在边境查获了走私货物。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ fisc（国库）+ ate → 归入国库 → 没收

### 6. **judicious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：明智的；审慎的；判断力强的
- **英文释义**：having, showing, or done with good judgment or sense
- **例句**：
  - A judicious use of resources will ensure the project stays within budget.
  - 明智地使用资源将确保项目保持在预算之内。
  - The judicious leader weighed all options carefully before making a decision.
  - 审慎的领导在做决定前仔细权衡了所有选项。
- **助记**：judic（判断）+ ious → 善于判断的 → 明智的

### 7. **denigrate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：诋毁；贬低；抹黑
- **英文释义**：to criticize unfairly; to disparage
- **例句**：
  - She felt hurt when her colleague denigrated her contributions to the project.
  - 当同事贬低她对项目的贡献时，她感到受伤。
  - It is unethical to denigrate competitors to gain a business advantage.
  - 为了获得商业优势而诋毁竞争对手是不道德的。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ nigr（黑）+ ate → 使变黑 → 抹黑

### 8. **eloquent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：雄辩的；有说服力的；传神的
- **英文释义**：fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing
- **例句**：
  - Her eloquent speech moved the entire audience to tears.
  - 她雄辩的演讲感动得全场观众流下了眼泪。
  - The painting was an eloquent expression of the artist's grief.
  - 这幅画是艺术家悲伤的传神表达。
- **助记**：e（出）+ loqu（说话）+ ent → 说出来有力量 → 雄辩的

### 9. **stagnate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：停滞；不发展；变得迟钝
- **英文释义**：to cease developing; to become inactive or dull
- **例句**：
  - The economy has stagnated due to a lack of investment and innovation.
  - 由于缺乏投资和创新，经济已经停滞不前。
  - If you don't challenge yourself, your skills will stagnate.
  - 如果你不挑战自己，你的技能就会停滞不前。
- **助记**：stagn（池塘/停滞）+ ate → 像死水一样 → 停滞

### 10. **pernicious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有害的；致命的；恶性的
- **英文释义**：having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way
- **例句**：
  - The pernicious influence of social media on teenagers' mental health is widely debated.
  - 社交媒体对青少年心理健康的有害影响备受争议。
  - Rumours can have a pernicious effect on a person's reputation.
  - 谣言会对一个人的名誉造成恶性影响。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ nic（杀死）+ ious → 贯穿性地伤害 → 有害的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **petrichor** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：泥土芬芳；雨后泥土的清香
- **英文释义**：a pleasant smell that frequently accompanies the first rain after a long period of warm, dry weather
- **例句**：
  - The petrichor after the summer rain filled the garden with a fresh earthy scent.
  - 夏雨过后的泥土芬芳让花园里充满了清新的泥土香味。
  - She loved the petrichor that came with the first autumn rain.
  - 她喜欢初秋第一场雨带来的泥土清香。
- **助记**：petra（石头）+ ichor（神的血液）→ 石头上神圣的液体 → 雨后泥土清香

### 12. **supercilious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：傲慢的；目中无人的；不屑的
- **英文释义**：behaving or looking as though one thinks one is superior to others
- **例句**：
  - The supercilious waiter looked down on the casually dressed diners.
  - 傲慢的服务员看不起穿着随便的食客。
  - Her supercilious attitude made it difficult for her to make friends.
  - 她目中无人的态度使她很难交到朋友。
- **助记**：super（上面）+ cili（眉毛）+ ous → 扬起眉毛 → 傲慢的

### 13. **surreptitious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：偷偷摸摸的；秘密的；鬼鬼祟祟的
- **英文释义**：kept secret, especially because it would not be approved of
- **例句**：
  - She took a surreptitious glance at the answer sheet during the test.
  - 她在考试中偷偷看了一眼答案纸。
  - The surreptitious meeting between the two spies took place in a dimly lit café.
  - 两名间谍的秘密会面在一家灯光昏暗的咖啡馆里进行。
- **助记**：sur（在下面）+ rept（爬）+ itious → 在下面爬行 → 偷偷摸摸的

### 14. **phantasmagoria** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：光怪陆离的景象；变幻的幻影
- **英文释义**：a sequence of real or imaginary images like those seen in a dream
- **例句**：
  - The carnival was a phantasmagoria of colors, lights, and music.
  - 狂欢节是一幅色彩、灯光和音乐交织的光怪陆离的景象。
  - His fever produced a phantasmagoria of bizarre dreams and hallucinations.
  - 他的高烧产生了一系列奇异梦境和幻觉的光怪陆离景象。
- **助记**：phantasm（幻影）+ agoria（集合）→ 幻影的集合 → 光怪陆离的景象

### 15. **ineffable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：难以言表的；不可名状的
- **英文释义**：too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words
- **例句**：
  - The beauty of the northern lights was simply ineffable.
  - 北极光的美丽简直难以言表。
  - She felt an ineffable sense of peace while meditating by the lake.
  - 在湖边冥想时，她感到一种不可名状的平静。
- **助记**：in（不）+ ef（出）+ fable（说）→ 说不出来的 → 难以言表的
