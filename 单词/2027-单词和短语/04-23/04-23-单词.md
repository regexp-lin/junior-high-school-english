## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **amplify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：放大；增强；详细阐述
- **英文释义**：to increase the volume of sound; to make larger, greater, or stronger
- **例句**：
  - The microphone amplified his voice so the whole hall could hear him.
  - 麦克风放大了他的声音，让整个大厅都能听到。
- **助记**：ampli（大）+ fy（使）→ 使变大 → 放大/增强

### 2. **blunder** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 大错；愚蠢的错误 v. 犯大错；跌跌撞撞
- **英文释义**：n. a stupid or careless mistake; v. to make a stupid mistake; to move clumsily
- **例句**：
  - The goalkeeper made a terrible blunder that cost his team the match.
  - 守门员犯了一个严重的错误，导致球队输掉了比赛。
- **助记**：blunder → blind（盲目）→ 盲目行动导致的错误 → 大错

### 3. **convey** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：传达；表达；运送
- **英文释义**：to communicate a message or feeling; to transport from one place to another
- **例句**：
  - The painting conveys a sense of sadness and loneliness.
  - 这幅画传达了一种悲伤和孤独的感觉。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ vey（路）→ 沿路一起传递 → 传达/运送

### 4. **doctrine** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：教义；学说；主义
- **英文释义**：a belief or set of beliefs held and taught by a Church, political party, or other group
- **例句**：
  - The political party's doctrine emphasizes social equality.
  - 该政党的学说强调社会平等。
- **助记**：doct（教导）+ rine → 教导的内容 → 教义/学说

### 5. **exert** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：施加（力量、影响等）；运用；尽力
- **英文释义**：to apply or bring to bear a force, influence, or quality
- **例句**：
  - Parents exert a strong influence on their children's development.
  - 父母对子女的成长施加着强大的影响。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ert（做）→ 把力量施展出来 → 施加

### 6. **fertile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：肥沃的；富饶的；能生育的
- **英文释义**：producing or capable of producing abundant vegetation or crops; able to conceive
- **例句**：
  - The fertile soil in this valley is perfect for growing vegetables.
  - 这个山谷里肥沃的土壤非常适合种蔬菜。
- **助记**：fert（承载）+ ile → 能承载生命生长的 → 肥沃的

### 7. **grumble** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 抱怨；发牢骚 n. 抱怨声
- **英文释义**：v. to complain about something in a bad-tempered way; n. a complaint
- **例句**：
  - Students grumbled about the amount of homework they had to do.
  - 学生们抱怨作业太多了。
- **助记**：grumble → 联想肚子"咕噜噜"不满地响 → 抱怨

### 8. **inferno** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：大火；火海；地狱
- **英文释义**：a large fire that is dangerously out of control; hell
- **例句**：
  - The warehouse turned into an inferno within minutes.
  - 仓库在几分钟内变成了一片火海。
- **助记**：infern（地狱）+ o → 像地狱之火 → 大火/火海

### 9. **maritime** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：海事的；海运的；沿海的
- **英文释义**：connected with the sea, especially in relation to seafaring commercial or military activity
- **例句**：
  - The country has a long maritime history of exploration and trade.
  - 该国有着悠久的航海探险和贸易历史。
- **助记**：mari（海）+ time → 与海有关的 → 海事的

### 10. **nocturnal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：夜间的；夜行的；夜间活动的
- **英文释义**：done, occurring, or active at night
- **例句**：
  - Owls are nocturnal creatures that hunt at night.
  - 猫头鹰是夜间活动的生物，在夜晚捕猎。
- **助记**：noct（夜晚）+ urnal → 属于夜晚的 → 夜间的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **accentuate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：强调；突出；使更明显
- **英文释义**：to make more noticeable or prominent
- **例句**：
  - The dark background accentuated the bright colours in the painting.
  - 深色背景突出了画中明亮的颜色。
- **助记**：accent（重音）+ uate → 加重音强调 → 强调/突出

### 12. **debacle** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：崩溃；惨败；灾难
- **英文释义**：a sudden and ignominious failure; a fiasco
- **例句**：
  - The product launch was a complete debacle due to technical failures.
  - 由于技术故障，产品发布会彻底失败了。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ bacle（阻挡）→ 阻挡不住地崩溃 → 惨败

### 13. **impetuous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：冲动的；鲁莽的；急躁的
- **英文释义**：acting or done quickly and without thought or care
- **例句**：
  - His impetuous decision to quit school was something he later regretted.
  - 他冲动地决定退学，后来为此感到后悔。
- **助记**：im（向）+ pet（追求）+ uous → 猛烈追求不加思考 → 冲动的

### 14. **parsimonious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：极度节俭的；吝啬的；小气的
- **英文释义**：very unwilling to spend money or use resources
- **例句**：
  - The parsimonious millionaire refused to donate even a small amount to charity.
  - 那位吝啬的百万富翁甚至拒绝向慈善机构捐出一小笔钱。
- **助记**：parsi（节约）+ monious → 过度节约的 → 吝啬的

### 15. **salient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：突出的；显著的；重要的
- **英文释义**：most noticeable or important; prominent
- **例句**：
  - The report highlighted the salient features of the new policy.
  - 报告突出了新政策的显著特点。
- **助记**：sal（跳）+ ient → 跳出来的 → 突出的/显著的
