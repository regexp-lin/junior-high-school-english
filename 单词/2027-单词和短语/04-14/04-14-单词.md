## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **besiege** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：围攻；围困；烦扰
- **英文释义**：to surround a place with armed forces in order to capture it; to overwhelm with requests
- **例句**：
  - The castle was besieged by enemy forces for three months.
  - 城堡被敌军围攻了三个月。
- **助记**：be + siege（围攻）→ 被围攻 → 围困

### 2. **convoy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：护航队；车队；船队
- **英文释义**：a group of ships or vehicles travelling together, often with armed protection
- **例句**：
  - A convoy of trucks delivered supplies to the disaster area.
  - 一支卡车车队向灾区运送了物资。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ voy（道路）→ 一起走在路上 → 车队/护航队

### 3. **discard** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：丢弃；抛弃；放弃
- **英文释义**：to get rid of something as no longer useful or desirable
- **例句**：
  - She discarded the old clothes and donated them to charity.
  - 她扔掉了旧衣服，把它们捐给了慈善机构。
- **助记**：dis（离开）+ card（卡片）→ 把卡片扔掉 → 丢弃

### 4. **enigma** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：谜；神秘的人或事物
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that is mysterious, puzzling, or difficult to understand
- **例句**：
  - The disappearance of the ancient civilization remains an enigma.
  - 这个古代文明的消失至今仍是一个谜。
- **助记**：enigma 来自希腊语，谜语之意 → 谜/神秘

### 5. **fiscal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：财政的；国库的；财务的
- **英文释义**：relating to government revenue, especially taxes; financial
- **例句**：
  - The government announced new fiscal policies to stimulate the economy.
  - 政府宣布了新的财政政策来刺激经济。
- **助记**：fisc（国库）+ al → 与国库相关的 → 财政的

### 6. **gaze** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 凝视；注视 n. 凝视；注视
- **英文释义**：v. to look steadily and intently at something; n. a steady intent look
- **例句**：
  - She gazed out of the window at the falling snow.
  - 她凝视着窗外飘落的雪花。
- **助记**：gaze 谐音"盖子"→ 盯着盖子看 → 凝视

### 7. **immune** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：免疫的；不受影响的；免除的
- **英文释义**：resistant to a particular infection or toxin; not affected by something
- **例句**：
  - After vaccination, the child became immune to the disease.
  - 接种疫苗后，孩子对这种疾病产生了免疫力。
- **助记**：im（不）+ mune（服务/义务）→ 不需要承担义务 → 免除的/免疫的

### 8. **manor** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：庄园；领地；大宅
- **英文释义**：a large country house with lands; the principal house of a landed estate
- **例句**：
  - The old manor has been converted into a luxury hotel.
  - 这座古老的庄园已被改建为一家豪华酒店。
- **助记**：manor 谐音"马诺"→ 马诺家的大庄园 → 庄园

### 9. **opaque** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不透明的；晦涩的；难懂的
- **英文释义**：not able to be seen through; not transparent; hard to understand
- **例句**：
  - The windows were covered with opaque curtains for privacy.
  - 窗户上挂着不透明的窗帘以保护隐私。
- **助记**：o（不）+ paque（透明）→ 不透明的

### 10. **retrieve** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：取回；找回；恢复
- **英文释义**：to get or bring something back; to recover
- **例句**：
  - The dog retrieved the ball from the lake.
  - 狗从湖里把球叼了回来。
- **助记**：re（回）+ trieve（找）→ 找回来 → 取回

### 高一词汇

### 11. **circumscribe** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：限制；约束；在周围画线
- **英文释义**：to restrict within limits; to draw a figure around another
- **例句**：
  - His freedom of speech was circumscribed by strict regulations.
  - 严格的规定限制了他的言论自由。
- **助记**：circum（周围）+ scribe（写/画）→ 在周围画线 → 限制

### 12. **elucidate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：阐明；解释清楚
- **英文释义**：to make something clear; to explain
- **例句**：
  - The professor elucidated the complex theory with simple examples.
  - 教授用简单的例子阐明了这个复杂的理论。
- **助记**：e（出）+ lucid（清楚的）+ ate → 使变得清楚 → 阐明

### 13. **frivolous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：轻浮的；不严肃的；无聊的
- **英文释义**：not having any serious purpose or value; carefree and superficial
- **例句**：
  - The court dismissed the lawsuit as frivolous.
  - 法院认为该诉讼无聊而驳回了它。
- **助记**：friv（轻浮）+ olous → 轻浮的

### 14. **pedantic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：迂腐的；学究气的；过分注重细节的
- **英文释义**：excessively concerned with minor details or rules
- **例句**：
  - His pedantic insistence on grammar rules made the writing class boring.
  - 他对语法规则迂腐的坚持让写作课变得无聊。
- **助记**：pedant（学究）+ ic → 学究气的 → 迂腐的

### 15. **unilateral** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：单方面的；单边的
- **英文释义**：performed by or affecting only one person, group, or country
- **例句**：
  - The country made a unilateral decision to withdraw from the treaty.
  - 该国单方面决定退出该条约。
- **助记**：uni（单一）+ lateral（侧面）→ 单一侧面的 → 单方面的
