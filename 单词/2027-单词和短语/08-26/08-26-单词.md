## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **debilitate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使衰弱；削弱（体力或力量）
- **英文释义**：to make someone or something weak and infirm
- **例句**：
  - The long illness debilitated him so much that he could barely walk.
  - 久病使他极度虚弱，几乎无法行走。
  - Heat waves can debilitate urban power grids.
  - 热浪会削弱城市电网的承受力。
- **助记**：de（去掉）+ bilit（力量）+ ate → 去掉力量 → 使衰弱

### 2. **eclectic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：兼收并蓄的；博采众长的
- **英文释义**：deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad range of sources
- **例句**：
  - Her eclectic wardrobe mixed vintage pieces with modern design.
  - 她的衣橱兼收并蓄，把古着与现代设计混搭。
  - The restaurant offers an eclectic menu drawn from three continents.
  - 这家餐厅的菜单博采三大洲风味。
- **助记**：ec（出）+ lect（选）→ 从各处选出 → 兼收并蓄的

### 3. **fastidious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：挑剔的；一丝不苟的；难讨好的
- **英文释义**：very attentive to accuracy and detail; hard to please
- **例句**：
  - He is fastidious about punctuation in every report.
  - 他对每份报告的标点都一丝不苟。
  - The fastidious cat would only eat one brand of food.
  - 这只挑剔的猫只吃一个牌子的食物。
- **助记**：fastid（厌恶）+ ious → 对不洁厌恶 → 挑剔的

### 4. **gregarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱社交的；群居的
- **英文释义**：fond of company; sociable; living in flocks or herds
- **例句**：
  - She is gregarious and loves hosting weekend parties.
  - 她爱交际，喜欢办周末聚会。
  - Sheep are gregarious animals that stay in flocks.
  - 羊是群居动物，成群生活。
- **助记**：greg（群体，如 aggregate）+ arious → 合群的

### 5. **heretic** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：异教徒；持异见者
- **英文释义**：a person holding an opinion contrary to church doctrine; a dissenter
- **例句**：
  - In history class they studied heretics who challenged medieval authority.
  - 历史课上他们研究了挑战中世纪权威的异见者。
  - He was labeled a heretic for rejecting the party line.
  - 他因拒绝党的路线而被贴上异见者标签。
- **助记**：haire（选择）+ tic → 另选信条 → 异端

### 6. **immutable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可改变的；永恒的
- **英文释义**：unchanging over time; unable to be changed
- **例句**：
  - The laws of physics are considered immutable under normal conditions.
  - 在通常条件下物理定律被认为是不变的。
  - Their friendship proved immutable despite years apart.
  - 多年分离，他们的友谊仍不可改变。
- **助记**：im（不）+ mut（改变）+ able → 不可改变的

### 7. **jocular** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：诙谐的；爱开玩笑的
- **英文释义**：fond of or characterized by joking; humorous and playful
- **例句**：
  - His jocular manner put nervous students at ease.
  - 他诙谐的态度让紧张的学生放松下来。
  - She made a jocular remark about his new haircut.
  - 她拿他的新发型开了句玩笑。
- **助记**：joc（玩笑，如 joke）+ ular → 爱开玩笑的

### 8. **kindred** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：同类的；志趣相投的；亲属（集合）
- **英文释义**：similar in kind; related; one's family and relations
- **例句**：
  - They discovered a kindred spirit in their love of classical music.
  - 他们在对古典乐的热爱中发现了志趣相投的人。
  - All kindred were invited to the family reunion.
  - 所有亲属都被邀请参加家族聚会。
- **助记**：kin（亲属）+ dred → 同宗或同类 → 志趣相投的

### 9. **labyrinth** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：迷宫；错综复杂的事物
- **英文释义**：a complicated irregular network of passages or paths; something intricate
- **例句**：
  - Tourists easily get lost in the labyrinth of narrow streets.
  - 游客很容易在迷宫般的窄巷中迷路。
  - The tax code is a labyrinth few fully understand.
  - 税法如迷宫，很少有人完全理解。
- **助记**：来自希腊神话克里特迷宫 → 错综复杂

### 10. **malice** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：恶意；蓄意伤害
- **英文释义**：the intention or desire to do evil; ill will
- **例句**：
  - The attack was done with malice, not by accident.
  - 这次袭击出于恶意，并非意外。
  - She bore no malice toward her former rival.
  - 她对以前的对手不怀恶意。
- **助记**：mal（坏，如 malice）→ 坏心 → 恶意

### 高一词汇

### 11. **narcissism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：自恋；自我陶醉
- **英文释义**：excessive interest in or admiration of oneself and one's appearance
- **例句**：
  - Constant selfie-taking can reflect unhealthy narcissism.
  - 不停自拍可能反映不健康的自恋。
  - The critic accused the director of narcissism in casting himself as the hero.
  - 评论家指责导演自恋，让自己演主角。
- **助记**：Narcissus（水仙花神自恋）→ 自恋

### 12. **obfuscate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使模糊；使困惑；故意混淆
- **英文释义**：to make something unclear or difficult to understand; to obscure
- **例句**：
  - Politicians sometimes obfuscate the truth with jargon.
  - 政客有时用行话混淆真相。
  - The manual obfuscates rather than clarifies the setup process.
  - 说明书反而把安装过程弄得更糊涂。
- **助记**：ob（加强）+ fus（倒）+ ate → 泼浑水 → 混淆

### 13. **partisan** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：偏袒的；党派性的；游击队员
- **英文释义**：strong supporter of a party, cause, or person; prejudiced in favor
- **例句**：
  - Partisan news channels rarely present both sides fairly.
  - 党派性新闻频道很少公正呈现双方。
  - During the war, partisans attacked supply lines in the forest.
  - 战争期间游击队在森林中袭击补给线。
- **助记**：party（党派）+ san → 党派忠诚的 → 偏袒的

### 14. **quintessential** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：最典型的；精髓的；完美的范例
- **英文释义**：representing the most perfect example of a quality or class
- **例句**：
  - Paris is the quintessential city for art lovers.
  - 巴黎是艺术爱好者心目中最典型的城市。
  - The dish was the quintessential expression of local cuisine.
  - 这道菜是当地美食最典型的代表。
- **助记**：quint（五，第五元素精华）+ essential → 精华中的精华 → 最典型的

### 15. **recant** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：公开撤回（声明、信仰）；宣布放弃
- **英文释义**：to withdraw or disavow a belief or statement formally
- **例句**：
  - Under pressure, the witness recanted his earlier testimony.
  - 在压力下，证人撤回了先前的证词。
  - He recanted his heretical views to avoid punishment.
  - 他为免惩罚而公开放弃异端观点。
- **助记**：re（回）+ cant（唱、说）→ 把说过的话唱回去 → 撤回
