## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **attenuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减弱；使稀薄；使纤细
- **英文释义**：to reduce the force, effect, or value of; to make thin
- **例句**：
  - The thick walls attenuate the noise from outside.
  - 厚厚的墙壁减弱了外面的噪音。
  - The drug's side effects are attenuated when taken with food.
  - 该药物与食物同服时副作用会减轻。
- **助记**：at（加强）+ tenu（薄）+ ate → 使变薄 → 减弱

### 2. **copious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：大量的；丰富的；充裕的
- **英文释义**：abundant in supply or quantity
- **例句**：
  - She took copious notes during the lecture to prepare for the exam.
  - 她在讲座中做了大量笔记来准备考试。
  - The region receives copious rainfall during the monsoon season.
  - 该地区在季风季节降雨量充沛。
- **助记**：copi（丰富）+ ous → 丰富的 → 大量的

### 3. **juxtapose** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：并列；并置；对比
- **英文释义**：to place close together or side by side for comparison
- **例句**：
  - The exhibit juxtaposes modern art with classical paintings.
  - 展览将现代艺术与古典绘画并置对比。
  - The author juxtaposes wealth and poverty to highlight social inequality.
  - 作者将财富与贫穷并列以突出社会不平等。
- **助记**：juxta（旁边）+ pose（放置）→ 放在旁边 → 并置

### 4. **ramification** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：后果；影响；分支
- **英文释义**：a consequence of an action or event, especially when complex or unwelcome
- **例句**：
  - The ramifications of climate change affect every country in the world.
  - 气候变化的影响波及世界上每个国家。
  - You should consider the ramifications before making such a major decision.
  - 在做出如此重大的决定之前，你应该考虑其后果。
- **助记**：ram（分支）+ ification → 分出的支系 → 后果/分支

### 5. **debilitate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使虚弱；使衰弱
- **英文释义**：to make someone weak and infirm
- **例句**：
  - The illness debilitated him for months, making even simple tasks difficult.
  - 疾病使他虚弱了好几个月，连简单的任务都变得困难。
  - The long drought debilitated the agricultural sector of the economy.
  - 长期干旱削弱了经济的农业部门。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ bil（力量）+ itate → 力量下降 → 使虚弱

### 6. **pragmatic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；注重实效的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically
- **例句**：
  - A pragmatic approach to problem-solving focuses on what works in practice.
  - 解决问题的务实方法注重实际中什么行得通。
  - The pragmatic leader prioritized achievable goals over idealistic visions.
  - 务实的领导者将可实现的目标置于理想化愿景之上。
- **助记**：pragm（实践）+ atic → 注重实践的 → 务实的

### 7. **conjecture** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：推测；猜测；推断
- **英文释义**：an opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information
- **例句**：
  - Without concrete evidence, his theory remains nothing more than conjecture.
  - 没有确凿证据，他的理论不过是推测。
  - Scientists conjecture that the ruins date back to the Bronze Age.
  - 科学家推测这些遗迹可以追溯到青铜时代。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ ject（扔）+ ure → 把想法扔在一起 → 推测

### 8. **indolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：懒惰的；懒散的；怠惰的
- **英文释义**：wanting to avoid activity or exertion; lazy
- **例句**：
  - The indolent student rarely completed his homework on time.
  - 这个懒惰的学生很少按时完成作业。
  - An indolent lifestyle can lead to various health problems.
  - 懒散的生活方式可能导致各种健康问题。
- **助记**：in（不）+ dol（痛苦）+ ent → 不愿吃苦 → 懒惰的

### 9. **corroborate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证实；确证；支持
- **英文释义**：to confirm or give support to a statement, theory, or finding
- **例句**：
  - The witness's testimony corroborated the detective's theory about the crime.
  - 证人的证词证实了侦探关于犯罪的理论。
  - Additional research corroborated the initial findings of the study.
  - 额外的研究证实了研究的初步发现。
- **助记**：cor（一起）+ robor（力量）+ ate → 一起加强 → 证实

### 10. **ephemeral** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：短暂的；转瞬即逝的
- **英文释义**：lasting for a very short time
- **例句**：
  - The beauty of cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a few days.
  - 樱花的美丽是短暂的，只持续几天。
  - Social media fame is often ephemeral and quickly forgotten.
  - 社交媒体的名气往往转瞬即逝，很快就被人遗忘。
- **助记**：epi（在上面）+ hemer（日）+ al → 只在一天之上 → 短暂的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **solipsism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：唯我论；自我中心主义
- **英文释义**：the view that the self is all that can be known to exist; extreme self-centeredness
- **例句**：
  - His philosophical position bordered on solipsism, believing only his own mind was certain.
  - 他的哲学立场接近唯我论，认为只有自己的心灵是确定的。
  - The child's solipsism made it hard for him to understand others' feelings.
  - 这个孩子的自我中心主义使他难以理解他人的感受。
- **助记**：sol（独自）+ ips（自己）+ ism → 只有自己 → 唯我论

### 12. **redolent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：芳香的；使人联想到的
- **英文释义**：having a strong smell; strongly reminiscent of something
- **例句**：
  - The kitchen was redolent of freshly baked bread and cinnamon.
  - 厨房里弥漫着刚烤面包和肉桂的芳香。
  - The old house was redolent of a bygone era, with its antique furniture and faded wallpaper.
  - 老房子让人联想到逝去的年代，有着古董家具和褪色的壁纸。
- **助记**：re（回）+ dol（气味）+ ent → 气味回来 → 芳香的

### 13. **catharsis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：宣泄；净化；情感释放
- **英文释义**：the process of releasing and thereby providing relief from strong or repressed emotions
- **例句**：
  - Writing in her diary provided a catharsis for her pent-up emotions.
  - 写日记为她压抑的情绪提供了宣泄。
  - Watching a sad movie can sometimes offer a form of emotional catharsis.
  - 看一部悲伤的电影有时能提供一种情感净化。
- **助记**：cathar（纯净/清洗）+ sis → 清洗情绪 → 宣泄

### 14. **liminal** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：阈限的；过渡的；在边界上的
- **英文释义**：relating to a transitional or initial stage; occupying a position at or on both sides of a boundary
- **例句**：
  - Adolescence is a liminal period between childhood and adulthood.
  - 青春期是童年与成年之间的过渡期。
  - The artist explores liminal spaces — hallways, doorways, and bridges.
  - 这位艺术家探索了过渡空间——走廊、门口和桥梁。
- **助记**：limin（门槛）+ al → 在门槛上 → 阈限的

### 15. **paladin** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：勇士；骑士；英勇的捍卫者
- **英文释义**：a knight renowned for heroism and chivalry; a champion of a cause
- **例句**：
  - She is considered a paladin of human rights, fighting tirelessly for justice.
  - 她被视为人权的捍卫者，为正义不知疲倦地奋斗。
  - The legendary paladins of Charlemagne's court were the greatest knights of their era.
  - 查理曼大帝宫廷中的传奇骑士是他们时代最伟大的骑士。
- **助记**：paladin 源自拉丁语 palatinus（宫廷的）→ 宫廷骑士 → 勇士
