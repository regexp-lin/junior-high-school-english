## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **integrity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：正直；诚实；完整性
- **英文释义**：the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; the state of being whole and undivided
- **例句**：
  - A person of integrity always keeps their promises.
  - 一个正直的人总是信守承诺。
  - The integrity of the building's structure was compromised by the earthquake.
  - 地震破坏了建筑结构的完整性。
- **助记**：integr（完整）+ ity → 人格完整 → 正直

### 2. **reluctant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不情愿的；勉强的；犹豫的
- **英文释义**：unwilling and hesitant; disinclined
- **例句**：
  - He was reluctant to admit that he had made a mistake.
  - 他不情愿承认自己犯了错。
  - The reluctant witness finally agreed to testify in court.
  - 那个犹豫不决的证人最终同意出庭作证。
- **助记**：re（反）+ luct（挣扎）+ ant → 反复挣扎 → 不情愿的

### 3. **deteriorate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：恶化；变坏；退化
- **英文释义**：to become progressively worse
- **例句**：
  - The patient's condition deteriorated rapidly overnight.
  - 患者的病情一夜之间迅速恶化。
  - Air quality in the city has deteriorated due to increased traffic.
  - 由于交通量增加，城市空气质量已经恶化。
- **助记**：deterior（更差）+ ate → 变得更差 → 恶化

### 4. **elaborate** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：精心制作的；详尽的；详细阐述
- **英文释义**：involving many carefully arranged parts; to develop or present in detail
- **例句**：
  - The palace had elaborate decorations with gold and precious stones.
  - 宫殿有用黄金和宝石精心制作的装饰。
  - Could you elaborate on your plan for the science project?
  - 你能详细阐述一下科学项目的计划吗？
- **助记**：e（出）+ labor（劳动）+ ate → 付出劳动制作 → 精心制作的

### 5. **commence** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：开始；着手
- **英文释义**：to begin or start something
- **例句**：
  - The graduation ceremony will commence at ten o'clock sharp.
  - 毕业典礼将在十点整开始。
  - Construction of the new bridge commenced last Monday.
  - 新桥的建设上周一开始了。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ mence（开始）→ 一起开始 → 开始

### 6. **withstand** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：承受；经受住；抵挡
- **英文释义**：to remain undamaged or unaffected by; to resist
- **例句**：
  - The ancient castle was built to withstand attacks from enemy forces.
  - 这座古城堡的建造是为了抵御敌军的攻击。
  - These plants can withstand temperatures as low as minus twenty degrees.
  - 这些植物能承受零下二十度的低温。
- **助记**：with（对抗）+ stand（站立）→ 站着对抗 → 经受住

### 7. **scarce** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：稀缺的；不足的；罕见的
- **英文释义**：insufficient for the demand; not plentiful
- **例句**：
  - Clean drinking water is scarce in many parts of the developing world.
  - 在发展中国家的许多地方，清洁饮用水很稀缺。
  - Jobs were scarce during the economic downturn.
  - 经济衰退期间工作机会很少。
- **助记**：scarce 发音似"死刻死" → 资源紧张到极点 → 稀缺的

### 8. **nurture** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：培养；养育；滋养
- **英文释义**：to care for and encourage the growth or development of; upbringing
- **例句**：
  - Parents should nurture their children's curiosity and creativity.
  - 父母应该培养孩子的好奇心和创造力。
  - The debate between nature and nurture has lasted for centuries.
  - 先天与后天之间的争论已经持续了几个世纪。
- **助记**：nurt（营养）+ ure → 给予营养 → 培养

### 9. **disperse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：分散；驱散；散布
- **英文释义**：to distribute or spread over a wide area; to cause to go in different directions
- **例句**：
  - The police used tear gas to disperse the crowd.
  - 警察使用催泪瓦斯驱散人群。
  - Seeds are dispersed by wind, water, and animals.
  - 种子通过风、水和动物传播。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ perse（散开）→ 分散开来 → 驱散

### 10. **feasible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可行的；行得通的
- **英文释义**：possible and practical to do easily or conveniently
- **例句**：
  - The engineers studied whether it was feasible to build a tunnel under the river.
  - 工程师们研究了在河下建隧道是否可行。
  - With current technology, space tourism is becoming increasingly feasible.
  - 以当前的技术，太空旅游正变得越来越可行。
- **助记**：feas（做）+ ible（能够）→ 能够做到的 → 可行的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **conundrum** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：难题；谜团；困境
- **英文释义**：a confusing and difficult problem or question
- **例句**：
  - How to balance economic growth with environmental protection remains a conundrum.
  - 如何平衡经济增长与环境保护仍是一个难题。
  - The detective faced a conundrum with no clear suspects.
  - 侦探面临一个没有明确嫌疑人的谜团。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ undrum（谜）→ 大家一起困惑的谜 → 难题

### 12. **idiosyncratic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：独特的；特有的；与众不同的
- **英文释义**：relating to the distinctive or peculiar features of an individual
- **例句**：
  - The artist is known for her idiosyncratic painting style that blends realism with fantasy.
  - 这位艺术家以融合现实与幻想的独特画风闻名。
  - Each region has its own idiosyncratic customs and traditions.
  - 每个地区都有自己独特的风俗和传统。
- **助记**：idio（个人）+ syn（一起）+ cratic → 个人特有的混合 → 独特的

### 13. **ubiquitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；普遍存在的
- **英文释义**：present, appearing, or found everywhere
- **例句**：
  - Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
  - 智能手机在现代社会已无处不在。
  - Coffee shops are ubiquitous in the downtown area of the city.
  - 咖啡店在市中心区域随处可见。
- **助记**：ubique（到处）+ ous → 到处都有的 → 无处不在的

### 14. **pontificate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：自以为是地说教；武断地发表意见
- **英文释义**：to express one's opinions in a pompous and dogmatic way
- **例句**：
  - He tends to pontificate about politics at every family gathering.
  - 他总是在家庭聚会上自以为是地大谈政治。
  - Stop pontificating and listen to what others have to say.
  - 别再自作权威了，听听别人怎么说。
- **助记**：pontif（教皇）+ icate → 像教皇一样说话 → 武断说教

### 15. **anachronism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：时代错误；不合时宜的事物
- **英文释义**：a thing belonging to a period other than that in which it exists; something outdated
- **例句**：
  - The movie had an obvious anachronism — a digital watch in a scene set in ancient Rome.
  - 电影有一个明显的时代错误——在古罗马场景中出现了电子手表。
  - Some argue that the monarchy is an anachronism in the modern world.
  - 有人认为君主制在现代世界是不合时宜的。
- **助记**：ana（向后）+ chron（时间）+ ism → 时间倒退 → 时代错误
