## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **vociferous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：大声疾呼的；喧哗的；强烈表达的
- **英文释义**：(especially of a person or speech) vehement or clamorous
- **例句**：
  - The vociferous protesters demanded immediate action on climate change.
  - 大声疾呼的抗议者要求立即对气候变化采取行动。
  - She was a vociferous critic of the government's education policies.
  - 她是政府教育政策的强烈批评者。
- **助记**：voci（声音）+ fer（带来）+ ous → 带来大声音的 → 大声疾呼的

### 2. **indignant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：愤慨的；义愤填膺的
- **英文释义**：feeling or showing anger or annoyance at what is perceived as unfair treatment
- **例句**：
  - The workers were indignant at being asked to work overtime without extra pay.
  - 工人们对被要求加班而没有额外报酬感到愤慨。
  - She wrote an indignant letter to the newspaper about the inaccurate reporting.
  - 她写了一封义愤填膺的信给报纸，抗议不准确的报道。
- **助记**：in（不）+ dign（值得）+ ant → 觉得不值得这样对待 → 愤慨的

### 3. **dilapidated** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：破旧的；破败的；年久失修的
- **英文释义**：(of a building or object) in a state of disrepair or ruin as a result of age or neglect
- **例句**：
  - The dilapidated warehouse was condemned by the city and scheduled for demolition.
  - 破败的仓库被市政府宣布为危房并计划拆除。
  - They bought the dilapidated cottage and spent two years restoring it.
  - 他们买下了这座破旧的小屋，花了两年时间修复它。
- **助记**：di（分离）+ lapid（石头）+ ated → 石头散落 → 破败的

### 4. **acquiesce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：默许；默认；勉强同意
- **英文释义**：to accept something reluctantly but without protest
- **例句**：
  - After hours of debate, she finally acquiesced to the committee's decision.
  - 经过数小时的辩论，她终于默许了委员会的决定。
  - The employees acquiesced to the new policy, though many privately disagreed.
  - 员工们默认了新政策，尽管许多人私下表示不同意。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ quiesce（安静）→ 变安静接受 → 默许

### 5. **ostentatious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：炫耀的；卖弄的；铺张的
- **英文释义**：designed to impress or attract notice; showy
- **例句**：
  - The ostentatious display of wealth at the gala made some guests uncomfortable.
  - 晚会上炫耀的财富展示让一些宾客感到不自在。
  - He drove an ostentatious sports car just to show off to his neighbors.
  - 他开着一辆炫耀的跑车只是为了向邻居们炫耀。
- **助记**：ostent（展示）+ atious → 展示给人看的 → 炫耀的

### 6. **clandestine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：秘密的；暗中进行的；地下的
- **英文释义**：kept secret or done secretively, especially because illicit
- **例句**：
  - The clandestine meeting between the two leaders took place at an undisclosed location.
  - 两位领导人的秘密会晤在一个未公开的地点举行。
  - The resistance fighters carried out clandestine operations behind enemy lines.
  - 抵抗战士在敌后进行了秘密行动。
- **助记**：clandest（隐藏）+ ine → 隐藏的 → 秘密的

### 7. **exacerbate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加剧；使恶化；使更加严重
- **英文释义**：to make a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling worse
- **例句**：
  - The drought exacerbated the food shortage, pushing millions to the brink of starvation.
  - 干旱加剧了食物短缺，将数百万人推向了饥饿的边缘。
  - His careless comments only exacerbated the tension between the two departments.
  - 他不慎的评论只是加剧了两个部门之间的紧张关系。
- **助记**：ex（使）+ acerb（苦/尖锐）+ ate → 使更苦涩 → 使恶化

### 8. **venerable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：令人尊敬的；年高德劭的；古老而庄严的
- **英文释义**：accorded a great deal of respect, especially because of age, wisdom, or character
- **例句**：
  - The venerable professor had been teaching at the university for over forty years.
  - 这位令人尊敬的教授在大学任教已超过四十年。
  - The venerable temple, dating back to the Tang Dynasty, attracts millions of visitors each year.
  - 这座可追溯到唐朝的古老庄严的寺庙每年吸引数百万游客。
- **助记**：vener（崇拜）+ able → 值得崇拜的 → 令人尊敬的

### 9. **proliferate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激增；大量繁殖；迅速扩散
- **英文释义**：to increase rapidly in numbers; to multiply
- **例句**：
  - Social media platforms have proliferated over the past decade.
  - 社交媒体平台在过去十年中激增。
  - Misinformation can proliferate quickly if not fact-checked and corrected.
  - 如果不经事实核查和纠正，虚假信息会迅速扩散。
- **助记**：proli（后代）+ fer（带来）+ ate → 带来大量后代 → 激增

### 10. **arduous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：艰巨的；费力的；艰难的
- **英文释义**：involving or requiring strenuous effort; difficult and tiring
- **例句**：
  - The arduous trek through the mountains took three days to complete.
  - 穿越山脉的艰难跋涉花了三天才完成。
  - Becoming a doctor is an arduous journey that requires years of study and training.
  - 成为医生是一段艰辛的旅程，需要多年的学习和训练。
- **助记**：ardu（高/陡）+ ous → 像攀登陡坡一样 → 艰巨的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **Wanderlust** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：旅行癖；漫游欲；对旅行的强烈渴望
- **英文释义**：a strong desire to travel and explore the world
- **例句**：
  - Her Wanderlust led her to visit over thirty countries before she turned thirty.
  - 她的旅行癖让她在三十岁前访问了三十多个国家。
  - The documentary about remote islands awakened his Wanderlust.
  - 关于偏远岛屿的纪录片唤醒了他的旅行欲望。
- **助记**：Wander（漫游）+ Lust（渴望）→ 漫游的渴望 → 旅行癖（德语借词）

### 12. **gestalt** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：格式塔；整体形态（整体大于部分之和的概念）
- **英文释义**：an organized whole that is perceived as more than the sum of its parts
- **例句**：
  - In gestalt psychology, the whole pattern of an experience is more important than its individual components.
  - 在格式塔心理学中，体验的整体模式比其单个组成部分更重要。
  - The gestalt of the symphony — the interplay of all instruments — created an overwhelming emotional effect.
  - 交响乐的整体形态——所有乐器的互动——创造了压倒性的情感效果。
- **助记**：gestalt 是德语，意为"形态/形状"，强调整体性

### 13. **ikigai** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：生活的意义；值得活下去的理由（日本概念）
- **英文释义**：a Japanese concept meaning a reason for being; the intersection of what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for
- **例句**：
  - The Okinawan concept of ikigai is believed to contribute to the island's remarkably long life expectancy.
  - 冲绳的 ikigai 概念被认为有助于该岛非凡的预期寿命。
  - She found her ikigai at the intersection of teaching, writing, and community service.
  - 她在教学、写作和社区服务的交汇处找到了自己的生活意义。
- **助记**：ikigai（生き甲斐）是日语，生き（活着）+ 甲斐（价值）→ 活着的价值

### 14. **reverie** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：幻想；白日梦；沉思冥想
- **英文释义**：a state of being pleasantly lost in one's thoughts; a daydream
- **例句**：
  - She was lost in reverie, staring out the window at the falling autumn leaves.
  - 她沉浸在幻想中，凝视着窗外飘落的秋叶。
  - The soft piano music induced a pleasant reverie that transported him to another world.
  - 柔和的钢琴音乐引发了一种愉悦的沉思，将他带到了另一个世界。
- **助记**：rêver（法语：做梦）+ ie → 梦的状态 → 幻想/白日梦

### 15. **Weltschmerz** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：世界悲观；对现实与理想差距的痛苦感
- **英文释义**：a feeling of melancholy and world-weariness; sadness over the difference between how the world is and how one thinks it should be
- **例句**：
  - The young idealist experienced profound Weltschmerz upon realizing the gap between his dreams and reality.
  - 年轻的理想主义者在认识到梦想与现实之间的差距后感受到了深深的世界悲观。
  - His poetry is filled with Weltschmerz, lamenting the injustice and suffering in the world.
  - 他的诗歌充满了世界悲观，哀叹着世界上的不公和苦难。
- **助记**：Welt（世界）+ Schmerz（痛苦）→ 因世界而痛苦 → 世界悲观（德语借词）
