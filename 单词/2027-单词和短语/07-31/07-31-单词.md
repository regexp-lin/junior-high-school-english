## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **allude** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：暗指；间接提到
- **英文释义**：to suggest or call attention to indirectly; to hint at
- **例句**：
  - The speaker alluded to the recent scandal without mentioning any names.
  - 演讲者暗指了最近的丑闻，但没有提到任何名字。
  - In her poem, she alluded to Greek mythology to express the theme of loss.
  - 在诗中，她暗引了希腊神话来表达失去的主题。
- **助记**：al（朝向）+ lude（玩弄）→ 拐弯抹角地说 → 暗指

### 2. **bewilder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使困惑；使不知所措
- **英文释义**：to cause someone to become confused or puzzled
- **例句**：
  - The complex instructions bewildered most of the new employees.
  - 复杂的说明让大多数新员工感到困惑。
  - Tourists are often bewildered by the city's winding streets and narrow alleys.
  - 游客们经常被城市的蜿蜒街道和狭窄巷子弄得不知所措。
- **助记**：be（使）+ wild（荒野）+ er → 使人迷失在荒野中 → 使困惑

### 3. **culminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：达到顶点；以……告终
- **英文释义**：to reach a climax or point of highest development
- **例句**：
  - Years of research culminated in a groundbreaking scientific discovery.
  - 多年的研究最终以一项开创性的科学发现达到高潮。
  - The festival culminated in a spectacular fireworks display over the harbor.
  - 节日以海港上空壮观的烟花表演达到高潮。
- **助记**：culmin（顶点）+ ate → 到达顶点

### 4. **disperse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：分散；散布；驱散
- **英文释义**：to distribute or spread over a wide area; to cause to scatter
- **例句**：
  - Police used water cannons to disperse the crowd of protesters.
  - 警察用水炮驱散了抗议人群。
  - Seeds are dispersed by the wind and carried to distant locations.
  - 种子被风散播到遥远的地方。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ perse（散播）→ 四散分开 → 分散

### 5. **fluctuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：波动；起伏不定
- **英文释义**：to rise and fall irregularly in number or amount
- **例句**：
  - Oil prices have fluctuated dramatically over the past year.
  - 过去一年中，油价大幅波动。
  - Her mood fluctuates between optimism and despair depending on the situation.
  - 她的情绪随情况在乐观和绝望之间波动。
- **助记**：fluct（流动）+ uate → 像水波一样起伏 → 波动

### 6. **invoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：援引；祈求；调用
- **英文释义**：to cite or refer to something as authority; to call upon for help
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer invoked the Fifth Amendment to protect his client's rights.
  - 律师援引第五修正案来保护委托人的权利。
  - Ancient priests invoked the gods before going into battle.
  - 古代祭司在出征前祈求神灵保佑。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ voke（叫喊）→ 大声呼唤 → 祈求、援引

### 7. **lament** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：悲叹；哀悼；挽歌
- **英文释义**：to express passionate grief or sorrow; a song or poem of mourning
- **例句**：
  - The whole village lamented the sudden death of the beloved teacher.
  - 全村人哀悼这位深受爱戴的老师的突然离世。
  - She wrote a heartfelt lament for her childhood home that had been demolished.
  - 她为被拆除的童年故居写了一首真挚的挽歌。
- **助记**：la（哀叹声）+ ment → 发出哀叹 → 悲叹

### 8. **nurture** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：培养；养育；培育
- **英文释义**：to care for and encourage the growth or development of someone or something
- **例句**：
  - Good parents nurture their children's talents and support their dreams.
  - 好的父母培养孩子的才能并支持他们的梦想。
  - The school provides a supportive environment that nurtures creativity and innovation.
  - 学校提供了一个培育创造力和创新的支持性环境。
- **助记**：nurt（营养，如 nutrition）+ ure → 给予营养 → 培养

### 9. **rift** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：裂缝；裂痕；分歧
- **英文释义**：a crack, split, or break; a serious disagreement between people
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake caused a deep rift in the surface of the road.
  - 地震在路面上造成了一道深深的裂缝。
  - A growing rift between the two political parties threatened national unity.
  - 两个政党之间日益加深的分歧威胁着国家统一。
- **助记**：rift 发音像"裂开"——裂缝

### 10. **slander** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：诽谤；中伤；诋毁
- **英文释义**：the action of making a false spoken statement damaging to a person's reputation
- **例句**：
  - He sued the newspaper for slander after they published false accusations.
  - 报纸发表虚假指控后，他以诽谤罪起诉了该报。
  - Spreading rumors and slandering your classmates is both cruel and unacceptable.
  - 散布谣言和诋毁同学既残忍又不可接受。
- **助记**：s + land（土地）+ er → 在别人的地盘上说坏话 → 诽谤

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ascertain** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：查明；确定；弄清
- **英文释义**：to find something out for certain; to make sure of
- **例句**：
  - Investigators worked around the clock to ascertain the cause of the plane crash.
  - 调查人员昼夜不停地工作以查明飞机坠毁的原因。
  - We need to ascertain whether the information is reliable before making a decision.
  - 我们需要在做决定之前弄清信息是否可靠。
- **助记**：as（加强）+ certain（确定的）→ 使完全确定 → 查明

### 12. **ephemeral** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：短暂的；转瞬即逝的
- **英文释义**：lasting for a very short time
- **例句**：
  - Fame on social media is often ephemeral — today's viral sensation is tomorrow's forgotten post.
  - 社交媒体上的名气往往是短暂的——今天的病毒式轰动是明天被遗忘的帖子。
  - Cherry blossoms are beloved precisely because their beauty is ephemeral.
  - 樱花之所以被喜爱，正是因为它们的美丽转瞬即逝。
- **助记**：epi（在……上）+ hemer（日）+ al → 只存在一天的 → 短暂的

### 13. **juxtapose** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：并列；并置；对比
- **英文释义**：to place two things close together for comparison or contrast
- **例句**：
  - The exhibition juxtaposed ancient artifacts with contemporary artwork to show cultural evolution.
  - 展览将古代文物与当代艺术品并列展示，以呈现文化演变。
  - The documentary juxtaposes scenes of wealth and poverty to highlight social inequality.
  - 这部纪录片将富裕和贫困的场景并置，以突出社会不平等。
- **助记**：juxta（紧邻）+ pose（放置）→ 放在一起 → 并列

### 14. **paradox** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：悖论；矛盾；自相矛盾的事
- **英文释义**：a statement or situation that seems contradictory but may be true
- **例句**：
  - It's a paradox that the richest country in the world has so many people living in poverty.
  - 世界上最富有的国家却有这么多人生活在贫困中，这是一个悖论。
  - The paradox of choice suggests that having too many options can lead to less satisfaction.
  - 选择悖论表明，拥有太多选项反而会导致满意度降低。
- **助记**：para（旁边/反）+ dox（观点）→ 与常理相悖的观点 → 悖论

### 15. **ubiquitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；普遍存在的
- **英文释义**：present, appearing, or found everywhere
- **例句**：
  - Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern life — almost everyone owns one.
  - 智能手机在现代生活中已经无处不在——几乎每个人都有一部。
  - Coffee shops are ubiquitous in big cities, appearing on nearly every corner.
  - 咖啡店在大城市无处不在，几乎每个街角都有。
- **助记**：ubique（拉丁语：到处）+ ous → 到处都有的 → 无处不在的
