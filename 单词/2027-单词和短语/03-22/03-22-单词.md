## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **admiral** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：海军上将；舰队司令
- **英文释义**：a commander of a fleet or navy; the highest-ranking naval officer
- **例句**：
  - The admiral commanded the entire naval fleet.
  - 海军上将指挥了整个舰队。
- **助记**：admir（钦佩）+ al → 令人钦佩的海军将领 → 海军上将

### 2. **brew** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 酿造；泡（茶/咖啡）；酝酿 n. 酿造的饮料
- **英文释义**：v. to make beer or tea by soaking, boiling, and fermenting; n. a drink made by brewing
- **例句**：
  - She brewed a pot of fresh coffee every morning.
  - 她每天早上泡一壶新鲜咖啡。
- **助记**：brew 谐音"不如"→ 不如来一杯自己酿的 → 酿造

### 3. **cluster** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 簇；群；串 v. 聚集；丛生
- **英文释义**：n. a group of similar things positioned closely together; v. to come together in a cluster
- **例句**：
  - A cluster of stars shone brightly in the night sky.
  - 一簇星星在夜空中闪耀。
- **助记**：clust（靠拢）+ er → 靠拢在一起 → 簇/聚集

### 4. **draft** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 草稿；草案；汇票 v. 起草；征募
- **英文释义**：n. a preliminary version of a piece of writing; v. to prepare a preliminary version
- **例句**：
  - Please review the first draft of my essay.
  - 请审阅我论文的初稿。
- **助记**：draft → draw（画）的变体 → 先画个草图 → 草稿

### 5. **farewell** `初三` n./int.

- **中文释义**：n. 告别；欢送会 int. 再见
- **英文释义**：n. an act of parting or of marking someone's departure; int. goodbye
- **例句**：
  - The students held a farewell party for their teacher.
  - 学生们为老师举行了一场告别派对。
- **助记**：fare（过得）+ well（好）→ 希望你过得好 → 告别/再见

### 6. **grim** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严峻的；冷酷的；令人沮丧的
- **英文释义**：very serious or gloomy; depressing or worrying to consider
- **例句**：
  - The economic outlook remains grim for the coming year.
  - 来年的经济前景依然严峻。
- **助记**：grim → Grimm（格林兄弟）→ 格林童话里的故事常常很残酷 → 严峻的

### 7. **knight** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：骑士；爵士
- **英文释义**：a man awarded a nonhereditary title by a sovereign; a medieval mounted warrior
- **例句**：
  - The knight rode bravely into battle.
  - 骑士勇敢地骑马上战场。
- **助记**：k 不发音，night（夜晚）→ 夜晚守护的人 → 骑士

### 8. **noticeable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：显著的；引人注目的
- **英文释义**：easily seen or noticed; clear or apparent
- **例句**：
  - There has been a noticeable improvement in her grades.
  - 她的成绩有了显著的进步。
- **助记**：notice（注意到）+ able（能够）→ 能够被注意到的 → 显著的

### 9. **pledge** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 誓言；保证 v. 发誓；承诺
- **英文释义**：n. a solemn promise or undertaking; v. to commit by a solemn promise
- **例句**：
  - He made a pledge to protect the environment.
  - 他做出了保护环境的承诺。
- **助记**：pled（恳求）+ ge → 恳求般郑重地发誓 → 承诺

### 10. **stagger** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：蹒跚；踉跄；使震惊
- **英文释义**：to walk or move unsteadily; to astonish greatly
- **例句**：
  - He staggered home after the long journey.
  - 长途跋涉后他踉踉跄跄地走回家。
- **助记**：stag（鹿）+ ger → 像受伤的鹿一样摇摇晃晃 → 蹒跚

### 高一词汇

### 11. **bureaucracy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：官僚制度；官僚主义
- **英文释义**：a system of government in which most decisions are made by officials rather than by elected representatives
- **例句**：
  - Too much bureaucracy can slow down progress.
  - 过多的官僚主义会拖慢进展。
- **助记**：bureau（局/办公室）+ cracy（统治）→ 办公室统治 → 官僚制度

### 12. **derivative** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 派生的；衍生的 n. 衍生物；派生词
- **英文释义**：adj. originating from another source; n. something derived from another source
- **例句**：
  - Many English words are derivatives of Latin roots.
  - 许多英语单词是拉丁词根的派生词。
- **助记**：derive（源自）+ ative → 从某处衍生出来的 → 派生的

### 13. **expenditure** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：支出；开支；经费
- **英文释义**：the action of spending funds; an amount of money spent
- **例句**：
  - The government needs to reduce its expenditure on defense.
  - 政府需要减少国防开支。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ pend（花费）+ iture → 花出去的钱 → 支出

### 14. **jurisdiction** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：管辖权；司法权；管辖范围
- **英文释义**：the official power to make legal decisions and judgments
- **例句**：
  - This case falls under the jurisdiction of the local court.
  - 此案属于地方法院的管辖范围。
- **助记**：juris（法律）+ dict（说）+ ion → 有权说法律的 → 管辖权

### 15. **procurement** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：采购；获取
- **英文释义**：the action of obtaining or procuring something, especially for business purposes
- **例句**：
  - The procurement of raw materials is essential for manufacturing.
  - 原材料的采购对制造业至关重要。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ cure（获取）+ ment → 积极去获取 → 采购
