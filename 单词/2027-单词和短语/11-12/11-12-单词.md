## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **aggregate** `初三` adj./v./n.

- **中文释义**：总计的；聚集；总数
- **英文释义**：formed by the combination of many separate units; to combine into a whole
- **例句**：
  - The aggregate score of all the tests determines your final grade.
  - 所有考试的总分决定你的最终成绩。
  - We need to aggregate the data from different sources for analysis.
  - 我们需要汇总来自不同来源的数据进行分析。
- **助记**：ag（向）+ greg（群体）+ ate → 聚成群体 → 聚集

### 2. **coalesce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；联合；融合
- **英文释义**：to come together and form one mass or whole
- **例句**：
  - Several small streams coalesce to form a larger river downstream.
  - 几条小溪在下游汇合形成一条更大的河流。
  - The different ideas coalesced into a unified plan of action.
  - 不同的想法融合成一个统一的行动计划。
- **助记**：co（一起）+ alesce（生长）→ 一起生长 → 合并

### 3. **desolate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：荒凉的；凄凉的；孤独的
- **英文释义**：deserted of people and in a state of bleak and dismal emptiness
- **例句**：
  - The town became desolate after the factory closed down.
  - 工厂倒闭后，小镇变得荒凉了。
  - She felt utterly desolate after her closest friend moved away.
  - 最好的朋友搬走后，她感到无比凄凉。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ sol（孤独）+ ate → 完全孤独 → 荒凉的

### 4. **reprimand** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：训斥；申斥；谴责
- **英文释义**：a formal expression of disapproval; to rebuke formally
- **例句**：
  - The officer received a reprimand for his unprofessional conduct.
  - 该军官因不专业的行为受到了训斥。
  - She was reprimanded by the principal for violating school rules.
  - 她因违反校规被校长训斥。
- **助记**：re（再）+ prim（按压）+ and → 再次按压训诫 → 训斥

### 5. **expedite** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加速；促进；加快处理
- **英文释义**：to make an action or process happen sooner or more quickly
- **例句**：
  - The new software will expedite the processing of customer orders.
  - 新软件将加快客户订单的处理速度。
  - We need to expedite the delivery to meet the deadline.
  - 我们需要加快交付以赶上截止日期。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ped（脚）+ ite → 把脚迈出去 → 加速

### 6. **pervasive** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：普遍的；弥漫的；无处不在的
- **英文释义**：spreading widely throughout an area or group of people
- **例句**：
  - The influence of technology is pervasive in modern education.
  - 技术的影响在现代教育中无处不在。
  - A pervasive sense of fear gripped the community after the earthquake.
  - 地震后一种弥漫的恐惧感笼罩着社区。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ vas（走）+ ive → 走到每一处 → 弥漫的

### 7. **dwindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减少；缩小；衰落
- **英文释义**：to diminish gradually in size, amount, or strength
- **例句**：
  - The number of wild tigers has dwindled to fewer than 4,000 worldwide.
  - 全球野生老虎的数量已减少到不足4000只。
  - His savings dwindled after months of unemployment.
  - 失业几个月后，他的积蓄逐渐减少。
- **助记**：dwindle 发音似"滴温度" → 温度逐渐下降 → 减少

### 8. **immerse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：沉浸；浸入；使专心
- **英文释义**：to dip or submerge in a liquid; to involve oneself deeply in an activity
- **例句**：
  - She immersed herself in the study of ancient history.
  - 她沉浸在古代历史的研究中。
  - The best way to learn a language is to immerse yourself in the culture.
  - 学习语言的最好方式是沉浸在那种文化中。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ merse（淹没）→ 进入并淹没 → 沉浸

### 9. **mandate** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：授权；命令；委托
- **英文释义**：an official order or commission; to authorize or require
- **例句**：
  - The government mandated wearing masks in all public transportation.
  - 政府强制要求在所有公共交通上佩戴口罩。
  - The election gave the new leader a clear mandate to reform the economy.
  - 选举给了新领导人改革经济的明确授权。
- **助记**：man（手）+ date（给）→ 交到手上 → 授权

### 10. **constrain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：限制；约束；强迫
- **英文释义**：to compel or force towards a particular course of action; to restrict
- **例句**：
  - Budget cuts constrained the school's ability to offer extracurricular activities.
  - 预算削减限制了学校提供课外活动的能力。
  - She felt constrained by the strict rules of the organization.
  - 她感到被组织严格的规定所束缚。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ strain（拉紧）→ 拉紧在一起 → 限制

### 高一词汇

### 11. **effulgent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：光辉灿烂的；明亮的
- **英文释义**：shining brightly; radiant
- **例句**：
  - The effulgent sunrise painted the sky in shades of gold and pink.
  - 光辉灿烂的日出将天空染成了金色和粉红色。
  - Her effulgent smile lit up the entire room.
  - 她灿烂的微笑照亮了整个房间。
- **助记**：ef（出）+ fulg（闪耀）+ ent → 向外闪耀 → 光辉灿烂的

### 12. **tendentious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有偏见的；倾向性的
- **英文释义**：expressing or intending to promote a particular cause or point of view
- **例句**：
  - The documentary was criticized for its tendentious portrayal of the conflict.
  - 这部纪录片因对冲突的倾向性描绘而受到批评。
  - His tendentious arguments revealed a clear political bias.
  - 他有偏见的论点暴露了明显的政治倾向。
- **助记**：tend（趋向）+ entious → 有趋向性的 → 有偏见的

### 13. **palimpsest** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：重写本；被覆盖后又显现的痕迹
- **英文释义**：a manuscript on which earlier writing has been erased and overwritten; something bearing visible traces of an earlier form
- **例句**：
  - The city is a palimpsest of different architectural eras layered upon one another.
  - 这座城市是不同建筑时代层层叠加的重写本。
  - Ancient palimpsests have revealed hidden texts from lost civilizations.
  - 古代重写本揭示了失落文明的隐藏文字。
- **助记**：palin（再次）+ psest（磨光）→ 磨光再写 → 重写本

### 14. **chiaroscuro** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：明暗对比法；明暗处理
- **英文释义**：the treatment of light and shade in drawing and painting
- **例句**：
  - Caravaggio was a master of chiaroscuro, using dramatic contrasts of light and dark.
  - 卡拉瓦乔是明暗对比法的大师，使用光明与黑暗的戏剧性对比。
  - The photographer used chiaroscuro to create a moody atmosphere in the portrait.
  - 摄影师用明暗对比法在肖像中营造了一种阴郁的氛围。
- **助记**：意大利语 chiaro（明亮）+ scuro（暗）→ 明暗 → 明暗对比法

### 15. **pathos** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：感伤；悲情；引起同情的特质
- **英文释义**：a quality that evokes pity or sadness
- **例句**：
  - The film's ending was full of pathos, leaving the audience in tears.
  - 电影的结尾充满了悲情，让观众泪流满面。
  - There was a deep pathos in her voice as she spoke about the disaster.
  - 她谈到那场灾难时声音中带着深深的悲伤。
- **助记**：pathos 希腊语"感受/苦难" → 引起同情的感受 → 悲情
