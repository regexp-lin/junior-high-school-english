## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **arduous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：艰难的；费力的
- **英文释义**：involving or requiring strenuous effort; difficult and tiring
- **例句**：
  - The climb to the mountain summit was an arduous journey.
  - 攀登山顶是一段艰难的旅程。
- **助记**：ard（燃烧）+ uous → 像燃烧一样消耗体力 → 艰难的

### 2. **bountiful** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：丰富的；慷慨的
- **英文释义**：large in quantity; abundant; generous in giving
- **例句**：
  - The farmers celebrated a bountiful harvest this autumn.
  - 农民们庆祝今年秋天的丰收。
- **助记**：bounti（慷慨）+ ful → 充满慷慨的 → 丰富的

### 3. **crave** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：渴望；迫切需要
- **英文释义**：feel a powerful desire for something
- **例句**：
  - After living abroad for years, she craved her mother's home cooking.
  - 在国外生活多年后，她渴望母亲的家常菜。
- **助记**：crave 联想 "brave"（勇敢）→ 勇敢地追求渴望的东西

### 4. **denounce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：谴责；公开指责
- **英文释义**：publicly declare to be wrong or evil; condemn
- **例句**：
  - The international community denounced the use of chemical weapons.
  - 国际社会谴责使用化学武器。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ nounce（宣布）→ 向下宣布（负面的事）→ 谴责

### 5. **emulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：效仿；模仿（以期赶上或超过）
- **英文释义**：match or surpass, typically by imitation
- **例句**：
  - Many young athletes emulate their sports heroes.
  - 许多年轻运动员效仿他们的体育偶像。
- **助记**：emul（竞争）+ ate → 通过竞争来追赶 → 效仿

### 6. **flaunt** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：炫耀；夸示
- **英文释义**：display ostentatiously to provoke envy or admiration
- **例句**：
  - He liked to flaunt his wealth by driving expensive cars.
  - 他喜欢开豪车来炫耀他的财富。
- **助记**：flaunt 联想 "flaunt it"（秀出来）→ 炫耀

### 7. **grim** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严酷的；令人沮丧的；冷酷的
- **英文释义**：very serious or gloomy; depressing; forbidding
- **例句**：
  - The economic outlook remains grim for many developing countries.
  - 对许多发展中国家来说，经济前景依然严峻。
- **助记**：grim 联想 "Grim Reaper"（死神）→ 严酷的

### 8. **intrepid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无畏的；勇敢的
- **英文释义**：fearless; adventurous; often used with approval
- **例句**：
  - The intrepid explorers ventured deep into the unknown jungle.
  - 无畏的探险家们深入了未知的丛林。
- **助记**：in（不）+ trepid（恐惧）→ 不恐惧的 → 无畏的

### 9. **novice** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：新手；初学者
- **英文释义**：a person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation
- **例句**：
  - As a novice, she made some mistakes, but she learned quickly.
  - 作为新手，她犯了一些错误，但学得很快。
- **助记**：nov（新的）+ ice → 新的人 → 新手

### 10. **preclude** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：排除；阻止；妨碍
- **英文释义**：prevent from happening; make impossible
- **例句**：
  - His injury precluded him from participating in the competition.
  - 他的伤势使他无法参加比赛。
- **助记**：pre（在前面）+ clude（关闭）→ 提前关闭 → 排除

### 高一词汇

### 11. **commensurate** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：相称的；相当的
- **英文释义**：corresponding in size or degree; in proportion
- **例句**：
  - The salary should be commensurate with the level of experience required.
  - 薪水应该与所要求的经验水平相称。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ mensur（测量）+ ate → 共同测量相等 → 相称的

### 12. **fastidious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；挑剔的
- **英文释义**：very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail
- **例句**：
  - She is fastidious about cleanliness and keeps her house spotless.
  - 她对清洁非常挑剔，把房子保持得一尘不染。
- **助记**：fastid（厌恶）+ ious → 对不完美感到厌恶 → 挑剔的

### 13. **ineffable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：难以形容的；不可言传的
- **英文释义**：too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words
- **例句**：
  - The beauty of the sunset over the ocean was simply ineffable.
  - 海面上日落的美丽简直难以形容。
- **助记**：in（不）+ ef（出）+ fable（说）→ 说不出来的 → 难以形容的

### 14. **penchant** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：嗜好；爱好；倾向
- **英文释义**：a strong or habitual liking for something; a tendency
- **例句**：
  - She has a penchant for collecting vintage watches.
  - 她有收藏古董手表的嗜好。
- **助记**：pench（倾斜）+ ant → 倾向于 → 嗜好

### 15. **vicarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：间接体验的；替代性的
- **英文释义**：experienced in the imagination through the feelings or actions of another person
- **例句**：
  - Parents often get vicarious pleasure from their children's achievements.
  - 父母常常从孩子的成就中获得间接的快乐。
- **助记**：vicar（代理人）+ ious → 通过代理人体验的 → 间接体验的
