## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **pervasive** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；弥漫的；普遍的
- **英文释义**：spreading widely throughout an area or group of people
- **例句**：
  - The smell of baking bread was pervasive in the small café.
  - 小咖啡馆里到处弥漫着烤面包的香味。
  - Social media has a pervasive influence on teenage self-image.
  - 社交媒体对青少年的自我形象影响无处不在。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ vas（走）+ ive → 走遍各处 → 弥漫

### 2. **query** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：疑问；质问；查询
- **英文释义**：a question, especially one addressed to an official body; to ask
- **例句**：
  - The librarian answered every student's query about the database.
  - 馆员回答了每个学生关于数据库的疑问。
  - Reporters queried the mayor about the delayed subway project.
  - 记者就延误的地铁项目质问市长。
- **助记**：quer（问）+ y → 问题

### 3. **relinquish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：放弃；交出；让出
- **英文释义**：to voluntarily cease to keep or claim; surrender
- **例句**：
  - He refused to relinquish his seat to someone who did not need it more.
  - 他拒绝把座位让给并不更需要的人。
  - The aging king decided to relinquish power to his daughter.
  - 年迈国王决定把权力交给女儿。
- **助记**：re（回）+ linqu（离开）+ ish → 放手离开 → 放弃

### 4. **savvy** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：有见识的；懂行的；常识；技巧
- **英文释义**：shrewd and knowledgeable; practical understanding
- **例句**：
  - Savvy shoppers compare prices online before buying electronics.
  - 精明的购物者买电前会在网上比价。
  - He has political savvy despite his young age.
  - 他年纪虽轻却有政治头脑。
- **助记**：来自法语 savoir（知道）→ 懂行

### 5. **truncate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：截短；删去……的末端；缩短
- **英文释义**：to shorten by cutting off a part; to abbreviate
- **例句**：
  - The editor truncated the article to fit the newspaper column.
  - 编辑为适合报纸版面而删短文章。
  - Long filenames may be truncated on older systems.
  - 旧系统上长文件名可能被截断。
- **助记**：trunc（砍）+ ate → 砍短 → 截短

### 6. **unnerve** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使气馁；使失去勇气；使紧张
- **英文释义**：to make someone lose confidence or courage; to unsettle
- **例句**：
  - The loud crash unnerved everyone in the quiet library.
  - 巨响使安静图书馆里的每个人都紧张起来。
  - Constant criticism can unnerve even confident speakers.
  - 持续批评会让自信的演讲者也胆怯。
- **助记**：un（去掉）+ nerve（勇气）→ 使失去勇气

### 7. **vilify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：诽谤；中伤；辱骂
- **英文释义**：to speak or write about in an abusively disparaging manner
- **例句**：
  - Opponents vilified the reform without reading the full text.
  - 反对者未读全文就诽谤这项改革。
  - Public figures should not vilify journalists who report facts.
  - 公众人物不应诽谤报道事实的记者。
- **助记**：vil（卑鄙）+ ify → 使显得卑鄙 → 诽谤

### 8. **waver** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：动摇；犹豫；摇曳
- **英文释义**：to shake with a quivering motion; to be undecided
- **例句**：
  - Candlelight wavered in the draft from the window.
  - 烛光在窗缝进来的风中摇曳。
  - She wavered between two majors before choosing engineering.
  - 她在两个专业间犹豫许久后选了工程。
- **助记**：wave（波动）+ er → 摇摆 → 犹豫

### 9. **yearn** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：渴望；向往；思念
- **英文释义**：to have an intense longing for something; to pine
- **例句**：
  - Many students yearn for a fairer exam system.
  - 许多学生渴望更公平的考试制度。
  - He yearned to see his grandparents during the long winter break.
  - 漫长寒假里他思念祖父母。
- **助记**：yearn 谐音“ year + n ”→ 年复一年盼望 → 渴望

### 10. **yoke** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：轭；束缚；使结合
- **英文释义**：a wooden crosspiece for oxen; to join or link; to oppress
- **例句**：
  - Farmers once used a yoke to harness two oxen together.
  - 农民曾用轭把两头牛套在一起。
  - Heavy debt can yoke families to endless repayment plans.
  - 沉重债务可能使家庭被无尽还款计划束缚。
- **助记**：yoke 联想 yoga（瑜伽）原义“连接”→ 连接/束缚

### 高一词汇

### 11. **zenith** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：天顶；顶峰；全盛时期
- **英文释义**：the highest point reached by a celestial or other object; peak
- **例句**：
  - At its zenith, the empire controlled trade across three continents.
  - 帝国在全盛时期控制三大洲贸易。
  - Her athletic career reached its zenith with an Olympic gold medal.
  - 她以奥运金牌达到运动生涯顶峰。
- **助记**：zenith 来自阿拉伯语“天顶之路”→ 顶点

### 12. **apotheosis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：神化；典范；巅峰
- **英文释义**：the highest point in the development of something; elevation to divine status
- **例句**：
  - The final scene is the apotheosis of the hero's journey.
  - 终场是英雄之旅的神圣顶点。
  - Some fans treat pop stars with an apotheosis usually reserved for legends.
  - 有些粉丝把流行歌星捧到传奇才配的高度。
- **助记**：apo（向）+ theo（神）+ sis → 成神 → 神化/巅峰

### 13. **boorish** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：粗鲁的；笨拙的；缺乏教养的
- **英文释义**：rough and bad-mannered; coarse
- **例句**：
  - Boorish jokes embarrassed everyone at the formal dinner.
  - 粗鲁的笑话让正式晚宴上所有人尴尬。
  - The host apologized for his guest's boorish behavior.
  - 主人为客人的粗鲁举止道歉。
- **助记**：boor（乡巴佬）+ ish → 像粗人一样的

### 14. **cacophony** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：刺耳杂音；不和谐音；嘈杂
- **英文释义**：a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds
- **例句**：
  - The construction site produced a cacophony of drills and horns.
  - 工地传来电钻与喇叭的刺耳杂音。
  - Modernist poems sometimes embrace cacophony for emotional effect.
  - 现代主义诗歌有时故意用不和谐音制造情绪效果。
- **助记**：caco（恶）+ phony（声音）→ 恶声 → 刺耳嘈杂

### 15. **debacle** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：溃败；惨败；崩溃
- **英文释义**：a sudden and ignominious failure; a fiasco
- **例句**：
  - The product launch turned into a debacle when the website crashed.
  - 网站崩溃使产品发布变成一场惨败。
  - Investors lost confidence after the company's financial debacle.
  - 公司财务崩盘后投资者失去信心。
- **助记**：de（除去）+ bacle（阻挡）→ 防线崩溃 → 溃败
