## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **malevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：恶意的；狠毒的；有坏心的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a wish to do evil to others
- **例句**：
  - The villain's malevolent grin revealed his true intentions.
  - 反派的狞笑暴露了他的真实意图。
  - Malevolent rumors spread quickly through the small town.
  - 恶毒谣言在这个小镇上迅速传开。
- **助记**：male（坏）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 坏心意愿 → 恶意的

### 2. **martinet** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：严格执行纪律者；（尤指）严厉教官
- **英文释义**：a strict disciplinarian, especially in the armed forces
- **例句**：
  - The new coach was a martinet who fined players for being one minute late.
  - 新教练是纪律狂人，球员迟到一分钟都要罚款。
  - Students feared the headmaster, known as a martinet with no tolerance for excuses.
  - 学生们害怕那位校长，他以不容借口、纪律严苛闻名。
- **助记**：来自人名 Martinet（以严厉著称的军官）→ 严厉纪律者

### 3. **mendacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚假的；说谎的；捏造的
- **英文释义**：not telling the truth; lying
- **例句**：
  - The article was full of mendacious claims that were later debunked.
  - 文章充斥后来被驳斥的虚假说法。
  - He told a mendacious story to avoid taking responsibility for the accident.
  - 他编造谎言以逃避对事故的责任。
- **助记**：menda（说谎）+ cious → 习惯性说谎 → 虚假的

### 4. **mordant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：（幽默、批评）尖刻的；辛辣的；（化学）媒染的
- **英文释义**：（of humor or criticism）bitingly sarcastic; sharply critical
- **例句**：
  - Her mordant wit made her a feared but respected columnist.
  - 她尖刻的机智使她成为令人敬畏又受尊敬的专栏作家。
  - The review offered mordant commentary on the director's latest film.
  - 评论对导演的最新影片进行了辛辣点评。
- **助记**：mord（咬）+ ant → 咬人的 → 尖刻的

### 5. **multifarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多种多样的；五花八门的
- **英文释义**：many and of various types; diverse
- **例句**：
  - The organization faces multifarious challenges, from funding to public trust.
  - 该组织面临从资金到公众信任等五花八门的挑战。
  - Her multifarious talents include painting, coding, and public speaking.
  - 她的多种才能包括绘画、编程和演讲。
- **助记**：multi（多）+ far（带来）+ ious → 带来许多种类 → 多种多样的

### 6. **nihilism** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：虚无主义；否定一切传统与权威的态度
- **英文释义**：the rejection of all religious and moral principles; the belief that life is meaningless
- **例句**：
  - Some critics interpreted the novel as an expression of political nihilism.
  - 一些评论家把这部小说解读为政治虚无主义的表达。
  - Teenage nihilism often stems from a sense of powerlessness rather than philosophy.
  - 青少年的虚无主义往往源于无力感，而非哲学思辨。
- **助记**：nihil（无）+ ism → 认为一切皆无意义 → 虚无主义

### 7. **obdurate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽固不化的；执拗的；拒不悔改的
- **英文释义**：stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action
- **例句**：
  - The obdurate landlord refused every reasonable offer to repair the heating system.
  - 顽固的房东拒绝一切修理供暖系统的合理提议。
  - Despite overwhelming evidence, he remained obdurate in his denial.
  - 尽管证据确凿，他仍顽固否认。
- **助记**：ob（加强）+ dur（硬）+ ate → 硬到底 → 顽固的

### 8. **officious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱指手画脚的；过分殷勤讨人嫌的
- **英文释义**：assertive of authority in an annoyingly domineering way; meddlesome
- **例句**：
  - The officious clerk insisted on checking every document three times.
  - 那位多管闲事的职员坚持把每份文件检查三遍。
  - His officious offers of help only slowed down the rescue team.
  - 他过分热心的“帮忙”只会拖慢救援队的速度。
- **助记**：of（朝向）+ fic（做）→ 过分爱做事 → 爱指手画脚

### 9. **opprobrium** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：强烈谴责；耻辱；骂名
- **英文释义**：harsh criticism or censure; public disgrace arising from shameful conduct
- **例句**：
  - The company faced international opprobrium for polluting the river.
  - 该公司因污染河流而面临国际舆论的强烈谴责。
  - He resigned to escape the opprobrium attached to the scandal.
  - 他为摆脱丑闻带来的骂名而辞职。
- **助记**：op（对抗）+ prob（测试/正直）→ 被试出不正直 → 耻辱

### 10. **pariah** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：被排斥者；贱民；局外人
- **英文释义**：an outcast; a person who is despised or rejected by society
- **例句**：
  - After leaking secrets to the press, he became a pariah in the intelligence community.
  - 向媒体泄密后，他在情报界成了被排斥的人。
  - The whistleblower was treated as a pariah by former colleagues.
  - 举报人被前同事当作弃儿对待。
- **助记**：来自印度种姓制度中的 Pariah（贱民阶层）→ 被社会排斥者

### 高一词汇

### 11. **paucity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：少量；缺乏；不足
- **英文释义**：the presence of something only in small or insufficient quantities; scarcity
- **例句**：
  - The paucity of evidence made it impossible to convict the suspect.
  - 证据不足使得无法给嫌疑人定罪。
  - There is a paucity of affordable housing in the city center.
  - 市中心经济适用房严重短缺。
- **助记**：pauc（少）+ ity → 稀少

### 12. **peccadillo** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：轻微过失；小毛病；无伤大雅的小错
- **英文释义**：a small, relatively unimportant offense or sin
- **例句**：
  - Being five minutes late was a peccadillo compared to missing the entire meeting.
  - 与完全缺席会议相比，迟到五分钟只是小过失。
  - The tabloids exaggerated every peccadillo in the celebrity's private life.
  - 小报夸大了那位名人私生活中的每一件小事。
- **助记**：pecc（过错）+ adillo（小）→ 小过错

### 13. **perfidy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：背信弃义；不忠；背叛行为
- **英文释义**：deceitfulness; untrustworthiness; betrayal of trust
- **例句**：
  - The treaty was broken in an act of perfidy that shocked neighboring nations.
  - 条约因背信弃义的行径而被撕毁，邻国为之震惊。
  - Historical accounts condemned the general's perfidy toward his allies.
  - 史书谴责那位将军对盟友的背信弃义。
- **助记**：per（彻底）+ fid（信任）→ 彻底辜负信任 → 背信弃义

### 14. **petulant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：易怒的；任性的；闹脾气的
- **英文释义**：（of a person or their manner）childishly sulky or bad-tempered
- **例句**：
  - She gave a petulant sigh when asked to turn off her phone.
  - 当被要求关掉手机时，她孩子气地叹了口气。
  - His petulant response to constructive criticism alienated his teammates.
  - 他对建设性批评的任性反应疏远了队友。
- **助记**：pet（寻求宠爱）+ ulant → 像小孩撒娇发脾气 → 任性的

### 15. **philistine** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：庸俗的人；对文化艺术毫无兴趣者；（贬）市侩的
- **英文释义**：a person who is hostile or indifferent to culture and the arts; smugly conventional
- **例句**：
  - The critic dismissed the audience as philistines who only wanted loud action films.
  - 评论家把观众贬为只爱看喧闹动作片的庸俗之徒。
  - His philistine attitude toward poetry surprised his literature professors.
  - 他对诗歌的庸俗态度令文学教授们惊讶。
- **助记**：来自圣经 Philistine（非利士人）→ 常与“不懂艺术”联想 → 市侩
