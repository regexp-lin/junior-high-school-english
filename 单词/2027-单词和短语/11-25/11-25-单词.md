## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **alleviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓解；减少
- **英文释义**：to make suffering, deficiency, or a problem less severe
- **例句**：
  - The medication helped alleviate the patient's chronic pain.
  - 药物帮助减轻了患者的慢性疼痛。
  - The charity works to alleviate poverty in developing countries.
  - 该慈善机构致力于缓解发展中国家的贫困。
- **助记**：al（向）+ levi（轻）+ ate → 使变轻 → 减轻

### 2. **dogmatic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：教条的；固执己见的；武断的
- **英文释义**：inclined to lay down principles as incontrovertibly true
- **例句**：
  - His dogmatic approach to teaching left no room for students' creativity.
  - 他教条的教学方式不给学生留下任何创造力的空间。
  - A good scientist should avoid being dogmatic and remain open to new evidence.
  - 一位好的科学家应该避免固执己见，对新证据保持开放态度。
- **助记**：dogma（教条）+ tic → 像教条一样 → 教条的

### 3. **deteriorate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：恶化；变坏；退化
- **英文释义**：to become progressively worse
- **例句**：
  - The building's condition has deteriorated significantly over the past decade.
  - 在过去十年中，这栋建筑的状况显著恶化。
  - Relations between the two countries continued to deteriorate after the diplomatic incident.
  - 外交事件后两国关系继续恶化。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ terior（更坏）+ ate → 变得更坏 → 恶化

### 4. **poignant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：令人心酸的；深刻的；感人的
- **英文释义**：evoking a keen sense of sadness or regret
- **例句**：
  - The poignant farewell speech brought tears to everyone's eyes.
  - 令人心酸的告别演讲让每个人都热泪盈眶。
  - The photograph captured a poignant moment between the soldier and his family.
  - 这张照片捕捉了士兵和家人之间令人感动的一刻。
- **助记**：poign（刺）+ ant → 刺入心中 → 令人心酸的

### 5. **invoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：援引；调用；祈求
- **英文释义**：to cite or appeal to as an authority; to call on for assistance
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer invoked the Fifth Amendment to protect his client's rights.
  - 律师援引第五修正案来保护当事人的权利。
  - The ritual was performed to invoke the blessing of the ancient gods.
  - 进行这个仪式是为了祈求古代神灵的庇佑。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ voke（呼唤）→ 呼唤进来 → 祈求/援引

### 6. **belligerent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：好战的；好斗的；敌对的
- **英文释义**：hostile and aggressive; engaged in a war or conflict
- **例句**：
  - The belligerent attitude of the customer made it difficult for the staff to help him.
  - 客户好斗的态度使工作人员很难帮助他。
  - Both belligerent nations refused to come to the negotiating table.
  - 两个交战国都拒绝来到谈判桌前。
- **助记**：bell（战争）+ iger（进行）+ ent → 进行战争的 → 好战的

### 7. **consolidate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：巩固；合并；加强
- **英文释义**：to make something physically stronger or more solid; to combine into a single more effective whole
- **例句**：
  - The company consolidated its position in the market by acquiring two competitors.
  - 公司通过收购两个竞争对手巩固了其市场地位。
  - After the war, the government worked to consolidate peace and rebuild the nation.
  - 战后，政府致力于巩固和平并重建国家。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ solid（坚固）+ ate → 使一起变坚固 → 巩固

### 8. **precarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不稳定的；危险的；不牢靠的
- **英文释义**：not securely held or in position; dependent on chance
- **例句**：
  - The mountain climbers found themselves in a precarious position on the icy cliff.
  - 登山者发现自己在冰冷的悬崖上处于危险的位置。
  - Many freelancers live a precarious financial existence without a steady income.
  - 许多自由职业者没有稳定收入，过着不稳定的经济生活。
- **助记**：precar（请求/祈祷）+ ious → 要靠祈祷才能安全 → 不稳定的

### 9. **expedite** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加快；加速；促进
- **英文释义**：to make an action or process happen sooner or be accomplished more quickly
- **例句**：
  - Paying an extra fee can expedite the processing of your visa application.
  - 额外付费可以加快你签证申请的处理速度。
  - The new software was designed to expedite the workflow and reduce delays.
  - 这款新软件旨在加速工作流程并减少延误。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ped（脚）+ ite → 把脚伸出去加速 → 加快

### 10. **nebulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：模糊的；朦胧的；不清楚的
- **英文释义**：in the form of a cloud or haze; vague or ill-defined
- **例句**：
  - His plans for the future remained nebulous, with no clear direction.
  - 他对未来的计划仍然模糊不清，没有明确方向。
  - The concept was too nebulous for the students to grasp easily.
  - 这个概念太模糊了，学生们不容易理解。
- **助记**：nebul（云/雾）+ ous → 像云雾一样 → 模糊的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **cynosure** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：注意力的中心；引人注目的人或物
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that is the center of attention or admiration
- **例句**：
  - The stunning bride was the cynosure of all eyes at the wedding reception.
  - 美丽的新娘是婚宴上所有人目光的焦点。
  - The Eiffel Tower remains the cynosure of Paris, attracting millions of visitors annually.
  - 埃菲尔铁塔仍然是巴黎的注目焦点，每年吸引数百万游客。
- **助记**：cyno（狗）+ sure（尾巴）→ 小犬座北极星 → 人人注目的中心

### 12. **veridical** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：真实的；如实的；与事实相符的
- **英文释义**：truthful; coinciding with reality
- **例句**：
  - The scientist sought veridical data that accurately reflected the natural phenomenon.
  - 科学家寻找真实可靠的数据，准确反映自然现象。
  - Her veridical account of the events was confirmed by multiple witnesses.
  - 她对事件的如实叙述得到了多名证人的证实。
- **助记**：veri（真实）+ dic（说）+ al → 说出真实的 → 真实的

### 13. **abstemious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：节制的；有节制的；克制的
- **英文释义**：not self-indulgent, especially when eating and drinking
- **例句**：
  - The abstemious monk ate only one simple meal a day.
  - 节制的僧侣每天只吃一顿简餐。
  - Despite being surrounded by luxury, she maintained an abstemious lifestyle.
  - 尽管身处奢华之中，她保持着节制的生活方式。
- **助记**：abs（远离）+ tem（酒）+ ious → 远离美酒 → 节制的

### 14. **promethean** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：大胆创新的；充满创造力的
- **英文释义**：daringly creative or innovative
- **例句**：
  - The architect's promethean vision transformed the city's skyline.
  - 建筑师大胆创新的愿景改变了城市的天际线。
  - His promethean spirit drove him to challenge conventional wisdom at every turn.
  - 他充满创造力的精神驱使他在每一步都挑战传统观念。
- **助记**：来自希腊神话中的 Prometheus（普罗米修斯），盗取天火造福人类 → 大胆创新的

### 15. **imbroglio** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：混乱局面；纠纷；纷争
- **英文释义**：an extremely confused, complicated, or embarrassing situation
- **例句**：
  - The diplomatic imbroglio between the two nations threatened regional stability.
  - 两国之间的外交纷争威胁着地区稳定。
  - What started as a minor disagreement quickly escalated into a full-blown imbroglio.
  - 一开始只是小分歧很快升级为全面的混乱局面。
- **助记**：意大利语借词，im（进入）+ broglio（纠缠）→ 陷入纠缠 → 混乱局面
