## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **conflagration** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：大火；火灾；大规模冲突
- **英文释义**：an extensive fire that destroys a great deal of land or property; a large-scale conflict
- **例句**：
  - The conflagration destroyed an entire city block before firefighters could contain it.
  - 大火在消防员控制住之前烧毁了整个街区。
  - The assassination of the archduke triggered a conflagration that engulfed all of Europe.
  - 大公的遇刺引发了一场席卷整个欧洲的大规模冲突。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ flagr（燃烧）+ ation → 一起燃烧 → 大火

### 2. **perspicacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：洞察力强的；敏锐的；目光犀利的
- **英文释义**：having a ready insight into and understanding of things
- **例句**：
  - The perspicacious analyst predicted the market crash months before it happened.
  - 洞察力强的分析师在市场崩盘数月前就做出了预测。
  - Her perspicacious observations about human behavior made her an excellent psychologist.
  - 她对人类行为敏锐的观察力使她成为一名出色的心理学家。
- **助记**：per（穿透）+ spic（看）+ acious → 穿透事物看本质 → 洞察力强的

### 3. **vituperate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：痛骂；辱骂；严厉谴责
- **英文释义**：to blame or insult someone in strong or violent language
- **例句**：
  - The critic vituperated the director's latest film, calling it an insult to cinema.
  - 评论家痛骂导演的最新电影，称之为对电影艺术的侮辱。
  - It is unprofessional to vituperate colleagues in public, regardless of the disagreement.
  - 无论分歧如何，当众辱骂同事都是不专业的。
- **助记**：vitu（缺陷）+ per（攻击）+ ate → 攻击缺陷 → 痛骂

### 4. **salubrious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有益健康的；宜人的；卫生的
- **英文释义**：health-giving; healthy; pleasant
- **例句**：
  - The coastal town's salubrious climate attracted retirees from across the country.
  - 沿海城镇宜人的气候吸引了全国各地的退休者。
  - Moving to the countryside provided a more salubrious environment for the children.
  - 搬到乡下为孩子们提供了一个更有益健康的环境。
- **助记**：salubr（健康）+ ious → 有益健康的

### 5. **excoriate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：严厉批评；抨击；擦伤皮肤
- **英文释义**：to criticize severely; to damage or remove the surface of the skin
- **例句**：
  - The newspaper excoriated the government's handling of the refugee crisis.
  - 报纸严厉批评了政府对难民危机的处理方式。
  - The activist excoriated corporations for their environmental negligence.
  - 活动人士抨击企业的环境疏忽。
- **助记**：ex（去除）+ cori（皮）+ ate → 去除皮肤 → 擦伤/严厉批评

### 6. **munificent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：慷慨的；大方的；丰厚的
- **英文释义**：(of a gift or sum of money) larger or more generous than is usual or necessary
- **例句**：
  - The munificent donation from the alumnus funded a new library for the university.
  - 校友慷慨的捐赠为大学资助了一座新图书馆。
  - The host was munificent, ensuring every guest left with a generous gift bag.
  - 主人很慷慨，确保每位客人都带着丰厚的礼品袋离开。
- **助记**：muni（礼物）+ fic（做）+ ent → 做出大礼物 → 慷慨的

### 7. **importune** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：纠缠；强求；再三恳求
- **英文释义**：to ask someone persistently and urgently for something
- **例句**：
  - The beggar importuned passersby for money at the busy intersection.
  - 乞丐在繁忙的十字路口纠缠路人要钱。
  - She importuned her parents for weeks before they finally agreed to let her study abroad.
  - 她纠缠了父母好几个星期，他们才终于同意让她出国留学。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ portun（港口/恳求）+ e → 不断进入恳求 → 纠缠

### 8. **effulgent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：光辉灿烂的；光芒四射的
- **英文释义**：shining brightly; radiant
- **例句**：
  - The effulgent sunset painted the sky in shades of gold and crimson.
  - 光辉灿烂的日落把天空染成金色和深红色。
  - Her effulgent smile lit up the entire room the moment she walked in.
  - 她一走进来，光芒四射的笑容照亮了整个房间。
- **助记**：ef（出）+ fulg（闪光）+ ent → 闪光而出 → 光辉灿烂的

### 9. **abnegate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：放弃；拒绝；自我克制
- **英文释义**：to renounce or reject something desired or valuable
- **例句**：
  - The monk abnegated worldly pleasures in pursuit of spiritual enlightenment.
  - 僧人放弃了世俗享乐以追求精神上的觉悟。
  - True leadership sometimes requires abnegating personal ambition for the greater good.
  - 真正的领导力有时需要为更大的利益放弃个人野心。
- **助记**：ab（远离）+ neg（否认）+ ate → 否认远离 → 放弃

### 10. **impervious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不受影响的；不能渗透的；无动于衷的
- **英文释义**：not allowing fluid to pass through; unable to be affected by
- **例句**：
  - The waterproof jacket is impervious to rain, keeping you dry in any weather.
  - 这件防水夹克不透雨，在任何天气中都能保持干爽。
  - She seemed impervious to criticism, maintaining her confidence despite harsh reviews.
  - 她似乎对批评无动于衷，尽管评价严厉仍保持自信。
- **助记**：im（不）+ pervi（穿过）+ ous → 不能穿过 → 不可渗透的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **susurrus** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：低语声；沙沙声；窃窃私语
- **英文释义**：a whispering or rustling sound
- **例句**：
  - The susurrus of wind through the tall grass created a peaceful atmosphere.
  - 风吹过高草的沙沙声营造了一种宁静的氛围。
  - A susurrus of whispered conversations filled the library as students prepared for exams.
  - 图书馆里充满了学生们备考时窃窃私语的低语声。
- **助记**：susurrus 是拉丁语，模拟低语沙沙的声音 → 拟声词 → 低语声

### 12. **chiaroscuro** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：明暗对比法；光影效果
- **英文释义**：the treatment of light and shade in drawing and painting; a contrast of light and dark
- **例句**：
  - Caravaggio was a master of chiaroscuro, using dramatic contrasts of light and shadow in his paintings.
  - 卡拉瓦乔是明暗对比法的大师，在画作中运用了戏剧性的光影对比。
  - The photographer used chiaroscuro techniques to create a moody, atmospheric portrait.
  - 摄影师使用明暗对比技法创作了一幅情调氛围感十足的肖像。
- **助记**：chiaro（明亮）+ scuro（黑暗）→ 明暗 → 明暗对比法

### 13. **petrichor** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：雨后泥土的芳香；初雨时大地的气息
- **英文释义**：the pleasant, earthy smell produced when rain falls on dry soil
- **例句**：
  - After weeks of drought, the first rain brought the delightful scent of petrichor.
  - 干旱数周后，第一场雨带来了令人愉悦的泥土芳香。
  - She opened the window to let the petrichor fill the house after the summer shower.
  - 夏日阵雨过后，她打开窗户让雨后泥土的芳香充满房间。
- **助记**：petr（石头）+ ichor（神的血液）→ 石头流出的神圣液体的气味 → 雨后泥土芳香

### 14. **eigengrau** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：固有灰（人在完全黑暗中眼睛感知到的深灰色）
- **英文释义**：the uniform dark gray color perceived by the eye in conditions of total darkness
- **例句**：
  - When you close your eyes tightly, the color you see is not pure black but eigengrau.
  - 当你紧闭双眼时，看到的颜色不是纯黑色，而是固有灰。
  - Scientists study eigengrau to understand how the visual system processes absence of light.
  - 科学家研究固有灰以了解视觉系统如何处理光线的缺失。
- **助记**：eigen（固有的）+ grau（灰色）→ 固有灰——德语借词

### 15. **verisimilitude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：逼真性；真实感；貌似真实
- **英文释义**：the appearance of being true or real
- **例句**：
  - The novel's verisimilitude was so striking that readers believed it was based on a true story.
  - 这部小说的逼真性如此惊人，以至于读者相信它是基于真实故事改编的。
  - Good historical fiction requires verisimilitude in its depiction of customs and language.
  - 好的历史小说需要在习俗和语言描写上具有真实感。
- **助记**：veri（真实）+ simil（相似）+ itude → 与真实相似 → 逼真性
