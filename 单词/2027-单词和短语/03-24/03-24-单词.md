## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **blaze** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 火焰；烈火 v. 熊熊燃烧；闪耀
- **英文释义**：n. a very large or fiercely burning fire; v. to burn fiercely or brightly
- **例句**：
  - The blaze destroyed several buildings in the neighborhood.
  - 大火烧毁了附近几栋建筑。
- **助记**：blaze → blase（满不在乎）→ 火烧得满不在乎一样猛烈 → 烈火

### 2. **census** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：人口普查；统计
- **英文释义**：an official count or survey of a population
- **例句**：
  - The government conducts a national census every ten years.
  - 政府每十年进行一次全国人口普查。
- **助记**：cens（评估）+ us → 对我们进行评估统计 → 人口普查

### 3. **deceive** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：欺骗；蒙骗
- **英文释义**：to cause someone to believe something that is not true
- **例句**：
  - Don't be deceived by his friendly appearance.
  - 别被他友善的外表所欺骗。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ ceive（拿）→ 把人往下拿/拉 → 欺骗

### 4. **enzyme** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：酶
- **英文释义**：a substance produced by a living organism which acts as a catalyst in biochemical reactions
- **例句**：
  - Enzymes help speed up the digestion of food.
  - 酶帮助加速食物的消化。
- **助记**：en（在里面）+ zyme（发酵）→ 在体内帮助发酵的 → 酶

### 5. **garment** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：衣服；服装
- **英文释义**：an item of clothing
- **例句**：
  - The factory produces garments for export.
  - 这家工厂生产出口服装。
- **助记**：gar（装饰）+ ment → 用来装饰身体的 → 衣服

### 6. **inherit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：继承；遗传
- **英文释义**：to receive money, property, or a title as an heir; to derive a quality from one's parents
- **例句**：
  - She inherited a large fortune from her grandmother.
  - 她从祖母那里继承了一大笔财产。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ herit（继承人）→ 进入继承人的角色 → 继承

### 7. **marble** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：大理石；弹珠
- **英文释义**：a hard type of stone used in building and sculpture; a small ball used in children's games
- **例句**：
  - The floor of the palace was made of white marble.
  - 宫殿的地板是白色大理石做的。
- **助记**：mar（谐音"马"）+ ble → 像马一样光滑坚硬的石头 → 大理石

### 8. **nuisance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：令人讨厌的人或事；麻烦
- **英文释义**：a person, thing, or circumstance causing inconvenience or annoyance
- **例句**：
  - The loud music from next door is a real nuisance.
  - 隔壁传来的吵闹音乐真让人讨厌。
- **助记**：nu（谐音"怒"）+ isance → 让人发怒的东西 → 讨厌的事

### 9. **portrait** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：肖像；画像；描绘
- **英文释义**：a painting, drawing, or photograph of a person, especially of the face
- **例句**：
  - The artist painted a beautiful portrait of the queen.
  - 画家画了一幅美丽的女王肖像。
- **助记**：port（携带）+ rait → 把人的样子携带/记录下来 → 肖像

### 10. **startle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使吃惊；使惊跳
- **英文释义**：to cause someone to feel sudden shock or alarm
- **例句**：
  - The sudden noise startled the sleeping cat.
  - 突然的响声把睡着的猫吓了一跳。
- **助记**：start（开始/惊动）+ le → 受了一"惊" → 使吃惊

### 高一词汇

### 11. **adjunct** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：n. 附属物；助手 adj. 附属的
- **英文释义**：n. a thing added to something else as a supplementary element; adj. connected in a subordinate way
- **例句**：
  - The lab serves as an adjunct to the main research center.
  - 该实验室作为主要研究中心的附属机构。
- **助记**：ad（到）+ junct（连接）→ 连接到主体上的 → 附属物

### 12. **deficiency** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：缺乏；不足；缺陷
- **英文释义**：a lack or shortage; the state of not having enough of something
- **例句**：
  - Vitamin D deficiency can lead to bone problems.
  - 维生素D缺乏会导致骨骼问题。
- **助记**：de（缺少）+ fic（做）+ iency → 做不到/做不够 → 缺乏

### 13. **exquisite** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：精美的；精致的；剧烈的
- **英文释义**：extremely beautiful and delicate; intensely felt
- **例句**：
  - The museum displayed exquisite porcelain from the Ming Dynasty.
  - 博物馆展出了精美的明代瓷器。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ quisite（追求）→ 追求到极致的 → 精美的

### 14. **indigenous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：本土的；土生土长的
- **英文释义**：originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native
- **例句**：
  - The kangaroo is indigenous to Australia.
  - 袋鼠是澳大利亚的本土动物。
- **助记**：indi（里面）+ gen（产生）+ ous → 在里面产生的 → 本土的

### 15. **repertoire** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：全部节目；全部本领；技能范围
- **英文释义**：a stock of plays, dances, or pieces that a performer knows or is prepared to perform
- **例句**：
  - The pianist has an impressive repertoire of classical pieces.
  - 这位钢琴家有丰富的古典曲目库。
- **助记**：re（反复）+ pertoire（表演）→ 反复表演的节目库 → 全部节目
