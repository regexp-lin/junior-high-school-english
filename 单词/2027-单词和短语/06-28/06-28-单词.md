## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **affluent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：富裕的；丰富的；充裕的
- **英文释义**：having a great deal of money; wealthy
- **例句**：
  - The affluent neighborhood is known for its grand houses and manicured gardens.
  - 这个富裕的社区以其宏伟的房屋和修剪整齐的花园而闻名。
- **助记**：af（向）+ flu（流）+ ent → 财富不断流入 → 富裕的

### 2. **brittle** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易碎的；脆弱的；尖刻的
- **英文释义**：hard but liable to break or shatter easily; lacking warmth or generosity of spirit
- **例句**：
  - The old bones were so brittle that they crumbled at the slightest touch.
  - 这些古老的骨头非常脆，轻轻一碰就碎了。
- **助记**：brit（碎）+ tle → 容易碎的 → 易碎的

### 3. **confiscate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：没收；充公；查扣
- **英文释义**：to take or seize someone's property with authority
- **例句**：
  - The teacher confiscated the student's phone for using it during class.
  - 老师没收了学生的手机，因为他在上课时使用手机。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ fisc（国库）+ ate → 收到国库里去 → 没收

### 4. **dwindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减少；缩小；衰落
- **英文释义**：to diminish gradually in size, amount, or strength
- **例句**：
  - The town's population has dwindled from 10,000 to less than 3,000.
  - 这个镇的人口已从一万减少到不到三千。
- **助记**：dwindle → 联想"缩短了"→ 逐渐减少 → 衰落

### 5. **exempt** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：adj. 免除的；豁免的 v. 免除；豁免
- **英文释义**：adj. free from an obligation or liability; v. to free from an obligation
- **例句**：
  - Students under 16 are exempt from paying the entrance fee.
  - 16 岁以下的学生免交入场费。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ empt（拿走）→ 被拿出去（不用承担）→ 免除

### 6. **gregarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱交际的；群居的；合群的
- **英文释义**：fond of company; sociable; living in flocks or communities
- **例句**：
  - She is a gregarious person who loves attending parties and meeting new people.
  - 她是一个爱交际的人，喜欢参加派对和认识新朋友。
- **助记**：greg（群）+ arious → 喜欢在群体中的 → 爱交际的

### 7. **imminent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：即将来临的；迫在眉睫的
- **英文释义**：about to happen; impending
- **例句**：
  - Scientists warned that an earthquake was imminent in the region.
  - 科学家警告该地区即将发生地震。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ min（突出）+ ent → 突出来了要发生 → 迫在眉睫的

### 8. **ludicrous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：荒谬的；可笑的；滑稽的
- **英文释义**：so foolish, unreasonable, or out of place as to be amusing; ridiculous
- **例句**：
  - The idea of building a bridge to the moon sounds absolutely ludicrous.
  - 建一座通往月球的桥的想法听起来绝对荒谬。
- **助记**：lud（玩）+ icrous → 像在开玩笑 → 荒谬的/可笑的

### 9. **orthodox** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：正统的；传统的；普遍认可的
- **英文释义**：following or conforming to the traditional or generally accepted rules or beliefs
- **例句**：
  - His orthodox approach to medicine made him skeptical of alternative treatments.
  - 他对医学的传统态度使他对替代疗法持怀疑态度。
- **助记**：ortho（正确的）+ dox（观点）→ 正确的观点 → 正统的

### 10. **succumb** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：屈服；屈从；死于
- **英文释义**：to fail to resist pressure, temptation, or some other negative force; to die from a disease
- **例句**：
  - After months of resistance, the city finally succumbed to the invaders.
  - 经过数月的抵抗，这座城市最终向入侵者屈服了。
- **助记**：suc（在下面）+ cumb（躺下）→ 躺倒在下面 → 屈服

### 高一词汇

### 11. **amalgamate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；混合；融合
- **英文释义**：to combine or unite to form one organization or structure
- **例句**：
  - The two small companies amalgamated to form a larger, more competitive firm.
  - 两家小公司合并成为一家更大、更有竞争力的公司。
- **助记**：a + malg（混合）+ amate → 混合在一起 → 合并

### 12. **circumspect** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：谨慎的；小心翼翼的；周全的
- **英文释义**：wary and unwilling to take risks
- **例句**：
  - A circumspect investor always researches thoroughly before making decisions.
  - 谨慎的投资者在做决定之前总是会进行充分的调查。
- **助记**：circum（周围）+ spect（看）→ 看看周围再行动 → 谨慎的

### 13. **iconoclast** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：打破传统的人；反对偶像崇拜者
- **英文释义**：a person who attacks cherished beliefs or institutions; a destroyer of images
- **例句**：
  - The young designer was an iconoclast who challenged every convention in the fashion industry.
  - 这位年轻的设计师是一个打破传统的人，挑战了时尚行业的每一个惯例。
- **助记**：icon（偶像）+ o + clast（打碎）→ 打碎偶像的人 → 反传统者

### 14. **prolific** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：多产的；丰富的；大量的
- **英文释义**：producing much fruit or foliage or many offspring; present in large numbers
- **例句**：
  - Shakespeare was one of the most prolific writers in the history of English literature.
  - 莎士比亚是英国文学史上最多产的作家之一。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lif（生命）+ ic → 不断产生生命 → 多产的

### 15. **zenith** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：顶点；鼎盛时期；天顶
- **英文释义**：the time at which something is most powerful or successful; the highest point
- **例句**：
  - The Roman Empire reached its zenith during the reign of Emperor Trajan.
  - 罗马帝国在图拉真皇帝统治时期达到了鼎盛。
- **助记**：zenith → 联想"至巅"→ 到达顶点 → 鼎盛时期
