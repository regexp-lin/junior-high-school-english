## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **adhere** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：遵守；坚持；粘附
- **英文释义**：to stick firmly to a surface or substance; to follow closely
- **例句**：
  - All employees must adhere to the company's code of conduct.
  - 所有员工必须遵守公司的行为准则。
  - The wallpaper didn't adhere properly to the damp wall.
  - 壁纸没有正确粘附在潮湿的墙上。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ here（粘）→ 粘向 → 遵守/粘附

### 2. **futile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：徒劳的；无用的；无效的
- **英文释义**：incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
- **例句**：
  - It's futile to argue with someone who refuses to listen to reason.
  - 与拒绝听道理的人争论是徒劳的。
  - Their futile attempts to stop the flooding only wasted precious time.
  - 他们阻止洪水的徒劳尝试只是浪费了宝贵的时间。
- **助记**：fut（倒/流）+ ile → 像水一样流走 → 徒劳的

### 3. **congregate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：聚集；集合；会合
- **英文释义**：to gather into a crowd or mass
- **例句**：
  - Thousands of fans congregated outside the stadium before the concert.
  - 演唱会前数千名粉丝聚集在体育场外。
  - Birds congregate near the lake during migration season.
  - 候鸟在迁徙季节聚集在湖边。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ greg（群体）+ ate → 成群聚在一起 → 聚集

### 4. **nominal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：名义上的；象征性的；微不足道的
- **英文释义**：existing in name only; very small in comparison to the real value
- **例句**：
  - He holds a nominal position with no real authority.
  - 他担任一个名义上的职位，没有实权。
  - The land was sold for a nominal fee of one dollar.
  - 这块地以一美元的象征性价格出售。
- **助记**：nomin（名字）+ al → 只有名字的 → 名义上的

### 5. **impoverish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使贫穷；使贫瘠；使枯竭
- **英文释义**：to make poor; to exhaust the strength or richness of
- **例句**：
  - Years of conflict have impoverished the nation's economy.
  - 多年的冲突使这个国家的经济陷入贫困。
  - Over-farming can impoverish the soil and reduce crop yields.
  - 过度耕种会使土壤贫瘠并降低作物产量。
- **助记**：im（使）+ pover（贫穷）+ ish → 使贫穷 → 使贫穷

### 6. **candid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坦率的；直言不讳的；真诚的
- **英文释义**：truthful and straightforward; frank
- **例句**：
  - I appreciate your candid feedback — it helps me improve.
  - 我感谢你坦率的反馈——它帮助我进步。
  - The journalist asked the politician a series of candid questions.
  - 记者向政治家提出了一系列直白的问题。
- **助记**：candid（明亮/白色）→ 像白色一样不掩饰 → 坦率的

### 7. **ostracize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：排斥；排挤；放逐
- **英文释义**：to exclude from a society or group
- **例句**：
  - The group ostracized him after he broke their trust.
  - 他破坏了他们的信任后，被集体排斥。
  - Children who are ostracized by their peers may suffer from low self-esteem.
  - 被同龄人排挤的孩子可能会自尊心低落。
- **助记**：ostra（贝壳）+ cize → 古希腊用贝壳投票放逐人 → 排斥

### 8. **precarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不稳定的；危险的；不确定的
- **英文释义**：not securely held; dependent on chance; dangerously uncertain
- **例句**：
  - The climber was in a precarious position on the narrow ledge.
  - 攀登者在狭窄的岩架上处于危险的位置。
  - Many families live in a precarious financial situation.
  - 许多家庭生活在不稳定的经济状况中。
- **助记**：prec（祈祷）+ arious → 只能靠祈祷的 → 不稳定的

### 9. **subsidy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：补贴；津贴；资助
- **英文释义**：a sum of money granted by the government to assist an industry or business
- **例句**：
  - The government provides subsidies to farmers to keep food prices affordable.
  - 政府向农民提供补贴以保持食品价格实惠。
  - Without the subsidy, the public transportation system would collapse.
  - 没有补贴，公共交通系统将会崩溃。
- **助记**：sub（下面）+ sid（坐）+ y → 坐在下面支撑 → 补贴

### 10. **docile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：温顺的；驯服的；易管教的
- **英文释义**：ready to accept control or instruction; submissive
- **例句**：
  - The docile puppy followed its owner everywhere without any fuss.
  - 温顺的小狗毫无闹腾地到处跟着主人。
  - Contrary to popular belief, most snakes are actually quite docile.
  - 与普遍看法相反，大多数蛇实际上相当温顺。
- **助记**：doc（教导）+ ile → 容易被教导的 → 温顺的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **compendium** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：概要；汇编；手册
- **英文释义**：a collection of concise but detailed information about a subject
- **例句**：
  - The book is a compendium of the world's most important scientific discoveries.
  - 这本书是世界上最重要科学发现的汇编。
  - She keeps a compendium of recipes from different countries.
  - 她收藏了一本来自不同国家的食谱汇编。
- **助记**：com（一起）+ pend（称量）+ ium → 一起称量汇总 → 概要

### 12. **truculent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；凶狠的；蛮横的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant
- **例句**：
  - The truculent customer refused to leave the store and demanded a full refund.
  - 那个蛮横的顾客拒绝离开商店，要求全额退款。
  - His truculent attitude during the negotiation made reaching an agreement impossible.
  - 他在谈判中好斗的态度使达成协议变得不可能。
- **助记**：truc（凶猛）+ ulent → 凶猛的 → 好斗的

### 13. **recondite** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：深奥的；难懂的；晦涩的
- **英文释义**：not known by many people; difficult to understand
- **例句**：
  - The professor's lecture on quantum physics was too recondite for most of the students.
  - 教授关于量子物理的讲座对大多数学生来说太深奥了。
  - The book explores recondite aspects of medieval philosophy.
  - 这本书探讨了中世纪哲学的深奥方面。
- **助记**：re（回）+ cond（隐藏）+ ite → 深深隐藏的 → 深奥的

### 14. **malapropism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：词语误用（把一个词错用为发音相似的另一个词）
- **英文释义**：the mistaken use of a word in place of a similar-sounding one, often with humorous effect
- **例句**：
  - Saying "I have zero taller ants" instead of "zero tolerance" is a classic malapropism.
  - 把"零容忍"说成"零蚂蚁"是一个经典的词语误用。
  - The character's constant malapropisms provided comic relief in the play.
  - 角色不断的词语误用为剧中提供了喜剧效果。
- **助记**：源自谢里丹剧中人物 Mrs. Malaprop → 经常用错词的太太 → 词语误用

### 15. **panegyric** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：赞颂；颂词；颂文
- **英文释义**：a public speech or published text praising someone or something highly
- **例句**：
  - The eulogy was a heartfelt panegyric to the late leader's achievements.
  - 悼词是对已故领导人成就的衷心赞颂。
  - The book reads like a panegyric to the beauty of the natural world.
  - 这本书读起来像是对自然世界之美的颂文。
- **助记**：pan（全部）+ egyr（集会）+ ic → 在集会上全面赞颂 → 颂词
