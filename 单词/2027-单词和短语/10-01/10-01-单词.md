## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **obscure** `初三` v./adj.

- **中文释义**：使模糊；遮掩；晦涩难懂的；默默无闻的
- **英文释义**：to keep from being seen; not discovered or known about; difficult to understand
- **例句**：
  - Fog began to obscure the road, forcing drivers to slow down.
  - 大雾开始使道路变得模糊，司机不得不减速。
  - He spent years working in an obscure village, far from the public eye.
  - 他在一个远离公众视线的偏僻村庄工作了多年。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ scure（盖）→ 盖上挡住 → 遮掩/使模糊

### 2. **plausible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：貌似合理的；花言巧语的
- **英文释义**：seeming reasonable or probable; appearing to be true or fair
- **例句**：
  - Her explanation sounded plausible, so the teacher accepted her late homework.
  - 她的解释听起来合理，所以老师接受了迟交的作业。
  - Advertisers often use plausible statistics to sell their products.
  - 广告商常常用看似可信的数据来推销产品。
- **助记**：plaus（鼓掌，如 applaud）+ ible → 值得鼓掌的 → 貌似有理

### 3. **resilient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有韧性的；能迅速恢复的；适应力强的
- **英文释义**：able to recover quickly from difficulties; tough and flexible
- **例句**：
  - Children are often more resilient than adults expect after a setback.
  - 遭遇挫折后，孩子往往比大人想象的更有韧性。
  - Bamboo is a resilient material that bends instead of breaking in strong winds.
  - 竹子是一种有韧性的材料，大风中弯而不折。
- **助记**：re（回）+ sil（跳，如 salient）+ ient → 能弹回来的 → 有韧性

### 4. **scrutinize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：细查；仔细审视；认真检查
- **英文释义**：to examine or inspect closely and thoroughly
- **例句**：
  - The committee will scrutinize every detail of the budget before approval.
  - 委员会在批准前会细查预算的每一个细节。
  - Journalists scrutinize the politician's speeches for inconsistencies.
  - 记者仔细审视这位政治家的演讲，寻找前后矛盾之处。
- **助记**：scrutin（细查）+ ize → 动词化 → 细查

### 5. **tangible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可触摸的；有形的；切实的
- **英文释义**：perceptible by touch; clear and definite; real
- **例句**：
  - After months of effort, they finally saw tangible results in sales.
  - 经过数月努力，他们终于在销售额上看到了切实的成果。
  - The museum displays tangible artifacts from ancient civilizations.
  - 博物馆展出古代文明的可触摸实物。
- **助记**：tang（接触，如 contact）+ ible → 能接触到的 → 有形的

### 6. **undermine** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：暗中破坏；逐渐削弱；侵蚀……的基础
- **英文释义**：to weaken or damage gradually, especially by secret means
- **例句**：
  - Constant criticism can undermine a student's confidence over time.
  - 持续的批评会逐渐削弱学生的自信心。
  - The scandal threatened to undermine public trust in the government.
  - 这起丑闻有可能削弱公众对政府的信任。
- **助记**：under（在下面）+ mine（挖）→ 在下面挖 → 暗中破坏

### 7. **volatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易变的；不稳定的；（化学品）易挥发的
- **英文释义**：likely to change rapidly and unpredictably; easily evaporated
- **例句**：
  - The stock market has been volatile this month, with sharp ups and downs.
  - 本月股市波动剧烈，大起大落。
  - Store volatile chemicals in a cool, well-ventilated place.
  - 将易挥发的化学品存放在阴凉通风处。
- **助记**：volat（飞，挥发）+ ile → 易飞走的 → 易变/挥发

### 8. **alleviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和；缓解
- **英文释义**：to make suffering, deficiency, or a problem less severe
- **例句**：
  - Medicine can alleviate pain, but rest is also important.
  - 药物能减轻疼痛，但休息同样重要。
  - New policies aim to alleviate traffic congestion in the city center.
  - 新政策旨在缓解市中心的交通拥堵。
- **助记**：al（向）+ levi（轻，如 lever）+ ate → 使变轻 → 减轻

### 9. **concise** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：简明的；简练的；言简意赅的
- **英文释义**：giving a lot of information clearly and in few words
- **例句**：
  - Please write a concise summary of the article in under 200 words.
  - 请用两百词以内写一段简明摘要。
  - Her concise speech impressed the judges at the debate competition.
  - 她在辩论赛上的简短发言给评委留下了深刻印象。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ cis（切）→ 切掉多余 → 简练

### 10. **diligent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：勤奋的；勤勉的；认真的
- **英文释义**：showing care and effort in one's work or duties
- **例句**：
  - Diligent students usually perform better in long-term projects.
  - 勤奋的学生在长期项目中通常表现更好。
  - She is diligent about checking her work before handing it in.
  - 她在交作业前会认真检查。
- **助记**：dilig（选择热爱）+ ent → 用心选择去做 → 勤奋

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ubiquitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；普遍存在的
- **英文释义**：present, appearing, or found everywhere
- **例句**：
  - Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern life.
  - 智能手机在现代生活中已无处不在。
  - Plastic waste is a ubiquitous environmental problem along coastlines.
  - 塑料垃圾是海岸线一带普遍存在的环保问题。
- **助记**：ubi（哪里）+ quit（去）+ ous → 无论去哪都有 → 无处不在

### 12. **paradox** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：悖论；似非而是的说法；自相矛盾的事
- **英文释义**：a statement or situation that seems self-contradictory but may be true
- **例句**：
  - It is a paradox that standing still on a crowded bus can be more tiring than walking.
  - 拥挤的公交车上站着不动比走路还累，这似非而是。
  - The novel explores the paradox of freedom and responsibility.
  - 这部小说探讨自由与责任之间的悖论。
- **助记**：para（并列）+ dox（意见）→ 两种意见并列 → 悖论

### 13. **benign** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：良性的；温和的；无害的
- **英文释义**：gentle and kind; not harmful, especially in medicine
- **例句**：
  - The doctor said the tumor was benign and required no surgery.
  - 医生说肿瘤是良性的，无需手术。
  - She spoke in a benign tone that calmed the nervous audience.
  - 她用温和的语气说话，安抚了紧张的听众。
- **助记**：ben（好）+ ign → 性质好的 → 良性/温和

### 14. **exacerbate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使恶化；使加剧；激怒
- **英文释义**：to make a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling worse
- **例句**：
  - Arguing on social media will only exacerbate the misunderstanding.
  - 在社交网络上争论只会加剧误解。
  - Hot weather can exacerbate breathing problems for some patients.
  - 炎热天气可能加重部分病人的呼吸问题。
- **助记**：ex（向外）+ acerb（酸/尖刻）+ ate → 使更尖锐 → 加剧

### 15. **pragmatic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；实用主义的；重实效的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically; practical
- **例句**：
  - We need a pragmatic solution that works within our limited budget.
  - 我们需要在有限预算内可行的务实方案。
  - She took a pragmatic approach and chose the cheaper, reliable option.
  - 她采取务实态度，选择了更便宜且可靠的方案。
- **助记**：pragma（行为/事务，希腊语）+ tic → 重行为的 → 务实
