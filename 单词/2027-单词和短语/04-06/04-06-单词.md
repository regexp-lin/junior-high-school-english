## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **bandage** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 绷带 v. 用绷带包扎
- **英文释义**：n. a strip of material used to bind a wound; v. to bind with a bandage
- **例句**：
  - The nurse bandaged the patient's injured arm.
  - 护士用绷带包扎了病人受伤的手臂。
- **助记**：band（带子）+ age → 用带子包的东西 → 绷带

### 2. **cradle** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 摇篮；发源地 v. 轻轻地抱着
- **英文释义**：n. a baby's bed or cot; the place where something originates; v. to hold gently
- **例句**：
  - Greece is often called the cradle of Western civilization.
  - 希腊常被称为西方文明的摇篮。
- **助记**：cradle → cradle to grave（从摇篮到坟墓）→ 摇篮

### 3. **drought** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：干旱；旱灾
- **英文释义**：a prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall leading to a shortage of water
- **例句**：
  - The severe drought destroyed many crops this year.
  - 严重的干旱今年毁掉了许多庄稼。
- **助记**：dr（dry 干）+ ought → 干得应该 → 干旱

### 4. **furnish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：装备；布置（家具）；提供
- **英文释义**：to provide a room or building with furniture; to supply someone with something
- **例句**：
  - They furnished the new apartment with modern furniture.
  - 他们用现代家具装饰了新公寓。
- **助记**：furn（提供）+ ish → 提供家具 → 布置

### 5. **herald** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 先驱；传令官 v. 预示；宣布
- **英文释义**：n. a person or thing that announces important news; v. to be a sign that something is about to happen
- **例句**：
  - The blooming flowers herald the arrival of spring.
  - 盛开的花朵预示着春天的到来。
- **助记**：her + ald → "她"宣布消息 → 预示/宣布

### 6. **lavish** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：adj. 奢侈的；慷慨的 v. 慷慨给予
- **英文释义**：adj. sumptuously rich or elaborate; v. to bestow something in generous quantities
- **例句**：
  - The hotel held a lavish wedding reception for the couple.
  - 酒店为这对夫妇举办了一场奢华的婚宴。
- **助记**：lav（洗/冲）+ ish → 像水一样冲过来（很多）→ 奢侈的

### 7. **municipal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：市政的；地方政府的
- **英文释义**：of or relating to a city or town or its governing body
- **例句**：
  - The municipal government is responsible for local roads and parks.
  - 市政府负责当地的道路和公园。
- **助记**：munici（市民）+ pal → 与市民有关的 → 市政的

### 8. **outrage** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 愤怒；暴行 v. 使愤怒；使震惊
- **英文释义**：n. an extremely strong reaction of anger or shock; v. to arouse fierce anger
- **例句**：
  - The decision caused outrage among the public.
  - 这个决定引起了公众的愤怒。
- **助记**：out（超出）+ rage（愤怒）→ 超出正常的愤怒 → 极度愤怒

### 9. **rapture** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：狂喜；极度欢喜
- **英文释义**：a feeling of intense pleasure or joy
- **例句**：
  - The audience watched the performance with rapture.
  - 观众满怀狂喜地观看了演出。
- **助记**：rapt（着迷的）+ ure → 着迷的状态 → 狂喜

### 10. **sustain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：维持；支撑；遭受
- **英文释义**：to strengthen or support; to keep something going over time; to suffer
- **例句**：
  - The bridge can sustain heavy traffic.
  - 这座桥能够承受繁忙的交通。
- **助记**：sus（下面）+ tain（拿住）→ 从下面拿住 → 支撑/维持

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acquiesce** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：默许；默认；勉强同意
- **英文释义**：to accept something reluctantly but without protest
- **例句**：
  - She acquiesced to her parents' decision without argument.
  - 她没有争辩，默许了父母的决定。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ quiesce（安静）→ 安静地接受 → 默许

### 12. **delineate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：描绘；勾画；描述
- **英文释义**：to describe or portray something precisely
- **例句**：
  - The report delineates the boundaries of the protected area.
  - 报告描绘了保护区的边界。
- **助记**：de + line（线）+ ate → 用线条画出来 → 描绘

### 13. **gregarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：合群的；爱社交的
- **英文释义**：fond of company; sociable
- **例句**：
  - She's a gregarious person who loves going to parties.
  - 她是个合群的人，喜欢参加聚会。
- **助记**：greg（群）+ arious → 喜欢在群体中的 → 合群的

### 14. **ostensible** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：表面上的；声称的
- **英文释义**：stated or appearing to be true, but not necessarily so
- **例句**：
  - The ostensible reason for his visit was business, but he really came to see his family.
  - 他来访的表面理由是出差，但实际上是来看家人的。
- **助记**：os（朝向）+ tens（伸展）+ ible → 伸展给人看的 → 表面上的

### 15. **quintessence** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：精华；典范；精髓
- **英文释义**：the most perfect or typical example of a quality or class
- **例句**：
  - This novel is the quintessence of romantic literature.
  - 这部小说是浪漫文学的精华。
- **助记**：quint（第五）+ essence（本质）→ 第五种最纯的本质 → 精华
