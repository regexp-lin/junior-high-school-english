## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **enervate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使虚弱；使无力；使精疲力竭
- **英文释义**：to cause someone to feel drained of energy or vitality
- **例句**：
  - The extreme heat enervated the workers, making it impossible to continue outdoors.
  - 酷热使工人们精疲力竭，无法继续在户外工作。
  - Months of illness had enervated her to the point where she could barely walk.
  - 数月的疾病使她虚弱到几乎走不动路。
- **助记**：e（出）+ nerv（神经/力量）+ ate → 力量流出 → 使虚弱

### 2. **parsimonious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：吝啬的；过分节俭的；小气的
- **英文释义**：unwilling to spend money or use resources; stingy
- **例句**：
  - The parsimonious landlord refused to repair the broken heating system despite tenants' complaints.
  - 吝啬的房东不顾租户的投诉拒绝修理坏掉的暖气系统。
  - His parsimonious nature made it difficult for him to enjoy even small luxuries.
  - 他吝啬的性格使他连享受小小的奢侈都很难。
- **助记**：parsimoni（节省）+ ous → 过度节省的 → 吝啬的

### 3. **expostulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：规劝；告诫；抗辩
- **英文释义**：to express strong disapproval or disagreement
- **例句**：
  - His friends expostulated with him about the dangers of his reckless driving.
  - 他的朋友们就他鲁莽驾驶的危险向他规劝。
  - The teacher expostulated gently with the student about the importance of honesty.
  - 老师温和地告诫学生诚实的重要性。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ postul（要求）+ ate → 提出要求/反对 → 规劝

### 4. **redolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：芳香的；使人想起…的；充满…气息的
- **英文释义**：having a strong smell; strongly reminiscent or suggestive of
- **例句**：
  - The old bookshop was redolent of aged paper and leather bindings.
  - 旧书店里充满了陈旧纸张和皮革装帧的气息。
  - The decor was redolent of a bygone era, with vintage furniture and sepia photographs.
  - 装饰让人想起过去的年代，有着古董家具和棕褐色照片。
- **助记**：re（回）+ dol（气味）+ ent → 气味回来 → 芳香的/使人联想的

### 5. **circumscribe** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：限制；约束；划定范围
- **英文释义**：to restrict within limits; to define the limits of
- **例句**：
  - The new regulations circumscribed the powers of local government officials.
  - 新法规限制了地方政府官员的权力。
  - His options were circumscribed by the limited budget available.
  - 他的选择受到有限预算的限制。
- **助记**：circum（围绕）+ scribe（写/画）→ 画一个圈围起来 → 限制

### 6. **meretricious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：华而不实的；俗丽的；虚有其表的
- **英文释义**：apparently attractive but having no real value or integrity
- **例句**：
  - The meretricious advertisement promised miraculous results that the product could never deliver.
  - 华而不实的广告承诺了产品永远无法实现的奇迹效果。
  - Behind the meretricious facade of the hotel lay rooms that were dirty and poorly maintained.
  - 在酒店华丽的外表背后是肮脏且维护不善的房间。
- **助记**：meretric（卖弄）+ ious → 卖弄的/外表诱人的 → 华而不实的

### 7. **inculcate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：反复灌输；谆谆教导；教育
- **英文释义**：to instill an attitude, idea, or habit by persistent instruction
- **例句**：
  - Parents strive to inculcate good values in their children from a young age.
  - 父母努力从小向孩子灌输良好的价值观。
  - The training program aimed to inculcate a culture of safety among all employees.
  - 培训计划旨在向所有员工灌输安全文化。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ culc（踩/压）+ ate → 反复踩入心中 → 灌输

### 8. **stolid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：迟钝的；木讷的；不动感情的
- **英文释义**：(of a person) calm, dependable, and showing little emotion or animation
- **例句**：
  - The stolid guard stood motionless at the gate regardless of the commotion around him.
  - 木讷的警卫不顾周围的骚动一动不动地站在门口。
  - His stolid demeanor hid a deeply emotional and compassionate personality.
  - 他不动声色的外表下隐藏着一个深感情和富有同情心的人格。
- **助记**：stol（固定/站立）+ id → 固定不动的 → 木讷的

### 9. **desiccate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使干燥；使脱水；使干枯
- **英文释义**：to remove the moisture from something; to dry out completely
- **例句**：
  - The desert sun desiccated the landscape, leaving the soil cracked and barren.
  - 沙漠的阳光使景观干枯，留下龟裂贫瘠的土地。
  - Desiccated coconut is a common ingredient in many baking recipes.
  - 脱水椰丝是许多烘焙食谱中的常见配料。
- **助记**：de（去除）+ sicc（干）+ ate → 去除水分 → 使干燥

### 10. **refractory** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：难以驾驭的；固执的；耐火的
- **英文释义**：stubborn or unmanageable; resistant to treatment
- **例句**：
  - The refractory child refused to follow any of the classroom rules.
  - 难以驾驭的孩子拒绝遵守任何课堂规则。
  - The patient's refractory condition did not respond to conventional treatment methods.
  - 病人难治的状况对常规治疗方法没有反应。
- **助记**：re（反对）+ fract（破裂）+ ory → 反对被破裂/改变 → 固执的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **kairos** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：恰当时机；关键时刻；决定性的瞬间
- **英文释义**：the right, critical, or opportune moment for action
- **例句**：
  - The ancient Greeks distinguished between chronos (ordinary time) and kairos (the perfect moment for action).
  - 古希腊人区分了 chronos（普通时间）和 kairos（行动的完美时刻）。
  - The entrepreneur recognized the kairos and launched her product at precisely the right time.
  - 这位企业家抓住了恰当的时机，在恰好的时间推出了产品。
- **助记**：kairos 是古希腊语，与 chronos（线性时间）对应，强调质而非量的时间

### 12. **ineffable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无法用语言表达的；难以名状的；妙不可言的
- **英文释义**：too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words
- **例句**：
  - The ineffable beauty of the aurora borealis left the travelers completely speechless.
  - 北极光难以名状的美丽让旅行者们完全说不出话来。
  - There was an ineffable sadness in her eyes that no words could capture.
  - 她眼中有一种无法用语言表达的悲伤。
- **助记**：in（不）+ ef（出）+ fable（说）→ 说不出来的 → 无法表达的

### 13. **lacuna** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：空白；缺失；空隙
- **英文释义**：an unfilled space or interval; a gap, especially in a manuscript or logical argument
- **例句**：
  - There is a significant lacuna in our understanding of how the ancient monument was constructed.
  - 在我们对这座古代纪念碑如何建造的理解中存在一个重大空白。
  - The researcher identified several lacunae in the existing literature on the topic.
  - 研究者在该主题的现有文献中发现了几处空白。
- **助记**：lacuna 源自拉丁语 lacus（湖/凹陷）→ 知识中的凹陷 → 空白

### 14. **kenopsia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：空寂之感（对一个通常热闹但此刻空无一人的地方的怪异感觉）
- **英文释义**：the eerie, forlorn atmosphere of a place that is usually bustling but is now empty
- **例句**：
  - Walking through the empty school during summer break, she felt an overwhelming sense of kenopsia.
  - 暑假期间走过空荡荡的学校，她感受到一种压倒性的空寂之感。
  - The kenopsia of the deserted shopping mall on a weekday morning was unsettling.
  - 工作日早晨空无一人的购物中心的空寂之感令人不安。
- **助记**：keno（空）+ opsia（看见）→ 看见空无一人 → 空寂之感

### 15. **komorebi** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：树叶间隙中洒落的阳光
- **英文释义**：the interplay of light and leaves when sunlight shines through trees
- **例句**：
  - The Japanese word komorebi perfectly describes the dappled sunlight filtering through the forest canopy.
  - 日语词 komorebi 完美地描述了阳光透过森林树冠洒下的斑驳光影。
  - She loved reading in the garden, bathed in the gentle komorebi of the afternoon sun.
  - 她喜欢在花园里阅读，沐浴在午后阳光透过树叶洒落的柔和光线中。
- **助记**：komorebi 是日语（木漏れ日），木（树）+ 漏れ（漏下）+ 日（阳光）→ 从树中漏下的阳光
