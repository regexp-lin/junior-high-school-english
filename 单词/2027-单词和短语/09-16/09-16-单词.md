## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **negligent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：疏忽的；粗心大意的；未尽职责的
- **英文释义**：failing to take proper care; showing a lack of attention
- **例句**：
  - The landlord was found negligent in maintaining the fire exits.
  - 房东被认定在维护消防通道方面疏忽职守。
  - Negligent driving caused a serious accident on the highway.
  - 粗心驾驶在高速公路上引发了严重事故。
- **助记**：neg（不）+ lig（选择/收集）→ 未用心照管 → 疏忽的

### 2. **obliterate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：消灭；抹去；覆盖
- **英文释义**：to destroy utterly; to blot out all traces
- **例句**：
  - The explosion obliterated the small building within seconds.
  - 爆炸在几秒内将小楼夷为平地。
  - Time had obliterated most of the ancient inscriptions.
  - 岁月磨灭了大部分古代铭文。
- **助记**：ob（对抗）+ liter（字母）→ 把字迹抹掉 → 抹去

### 3. **pacify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：平息；安抚；使恢复和平
- **英文释义**：to calm anger or agitation; to bring peace to a region
- **例句**：
  - Troops were sent to pacify the restless border towns.
  - 部队被派去平定动荡的边境城镇。
  - She tried to pacify the crying child with a soft song.
  - 她轻声唱歌安抚哭泣的孩子。
- **助记**：pac（和平）+ ify → 使和平 → 平息

### 4. **query** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：疑问；质问；查询
- **英文释义**：a question; to ask for information or express doubt
- **例句**：
  - The journalist raised a query about the source of the funding.
  - 记者对资金来源提出疑问。
  - Students may query the database from any campus computer.
  - 学生可在校园任意电脑上查询数据库。
- **助记**：quer（问）→ 询问

### 5. **ravage** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：蹂躏；严重破坏；劫掠
- **英文释义**：to cause severe damage to; to devastate
- **例句**：
  - The hurricane ravaged coastal villages within hours.
  - 飓风在数小时内蹂躏了沿海村庄。
  - Years of war had ravaged the country’s economy.
  - 连年战争严重摧残了该国经济。
- **助记**：rav（抢）+ age → 抢劫破坏 → 蹂躏

### 6. **salvage** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：抢救；打捞；挽回；抢救出的财物
- **英文释义**：to rescue property from danger; items recovered
- **例句**：
  - Divers attempted to salvage cargo from the sunken ship.
  - 潜水员试图从沉船中打捞货物。
  - He managed to salvage his reputation with a sincere apology.
  - 他以真诚道歉挽回了自己的声誉。
- **助记**：salv（救）+ age → 抢救

### 7. **trample** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：践踏；踩坏；无视（权利）
- **英文释义**：to step heavily on; to treat rights or feelings with contempt
- **例句**：
  - Protesters feared the crowd might trample the fallen child.
  - 抗议者担心人群会踩到跌倒的孩子。
  - The regime trampled on basic human rights.
  - 该政权践踏基本人权。
- **助记**：tramp（重步）+ le → 重踩 → 践踏

### 8. **usurp** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：篡夺；夺取（权力、地位）
- **英文释义**：to seize power or position illegally or by force
- **例句**：
  - The general usurped the throne after the king fled abroad.
  - 国王流亡后，将军篡夺了王位。
  - She accused her rival of usurping her role on the committee.
  - 她指责对手篡夺了她在委员会中的职位。
- **助记**：us（使用）+ rap（抢）→ 强夺使用权 → 篡夺

### 9. **vehement** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：激烈的；猛烈的；（情感）强烈的
- **英文释义**：showing strong feeling; forceful and intense
- **例句**：
  - She was a vehement critic of the government’s policy.
  - 她是政府政策的激烈批评者。
  - The debate grew vehement as both sides refused to yield.
  - 双方互不相让，辩论愈发激烈。
- **助记**：veh（携带）→ 情绪强烈外显 → 激烈的

### 10. **wary** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：谨慎的；提防的；小心翼翼的
- **英文释义**：cautious because of possible danger or problems
- **例句**：
  - Tourists grew wary of pickpockets in crowded markets.
  - 游客在拥挤市场对扒手保持警惕。
  - She was wary of signing a contract without a lawyer.
  - 没有律师在场她对签署合同很谨慎。
- **助记**：ware（注意）→ 小心提防 → 谨慎的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **zealous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：热心的；狂热的；充满热情的
- **英文释义**：showing great energy or enthusiasm for a cause
- **例句**：
  - Zealous volunteers cleaned the beach every weekend.
  - 热心的志愿者每个周末都清理海滩。
  - His zealous defense of tradition alienated younger colleagues.
  - 他对传统的狂热维护疏远了年轻同事。
- **助记**：zeal（热情）+ ous → 热情的

### 12. **augur** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：预示；预兆（好坏）
- **英文释义**：to be a sign of what will happen in the future; to foretell
- **例句**：
  - Dark clouds augured a storm before nightfall.
  - 乌云预示黄昏前将有暴风雨。
  - The strong sales augur well for the company’s recovery.
  - 强劲销售预示着公司复苏良好。
- **助记**：古罗马 augur（占卜官）→ 预兆

### 13. **boorish** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：粗野的；粗鲁无礼的
- **英文释义**：rough and bad-mannered; coarse
- **例句**：
  - His boorish jokes offended several guests at the dinner.
  - 他粗俗的玩笑冒犯了几位晚宴客人。
  - The critic dismissed the film as boorish entertainment.
  - 评论家把该片贬为粗俗娱乐。
- **助记**：boor（乡下粗人）+ ish → 粗野的

### 14. **cajole** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：哄劝；用甜言蜜语劝诱
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone by flattery or gentle urging
- **例句**：
  - She cajoled her brother into driving her to the airport.
  - 她软磨硬泡让哥哥开车送她去机场。
  - Advertisers cajole consumers with promises of happiness.
  - 广告商用幸福的承诺哄劝消费者。
- **助记**：法语 cageoler（叽叽喳喳）→ 用好话哄 → 劝诱

### 15. **diatribe** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：长篇抨击；激烈的谴责
- **英文释义**：a forceful verbal attack against someone or something
- **例句**：
  - The editorial was a diatribe against corruption in politics.
  - 这篇社论是对政治腐败的长篇抨击。
  - He launched into a diatribe about unfair grading.
  - 他就评分不公大发一通牢骚。
- **助记**：dia（穿过）+ tribe（摩擦）→ 激烈摩擦言论 → 抨击
