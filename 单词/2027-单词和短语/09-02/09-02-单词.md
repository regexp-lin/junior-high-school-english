## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **obsolete** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过时的；废弃的；已淘汰的
- **英文释义**：no longer produced or used; out of date
- **例句**：
  - Floppy disks have become obsolete in the age of cloud storage.
  - 在云存储时代，软盘已经过时。
  - The factory was closed because its machinery was obsolete and inefficient.
  - 工厂因设备陈旧低效而关闭。
- **助记**：ob（对抗）+ solete（使用）→ 不再被使用 → 过时的

### 2. **paramount** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：至高无上的；首要的；最重要的
- **英文释义**：more important than anything else; supreme
- **例句**：
  - Patient safety is of paramount concern in every hospital.
  - 在每个医院，患者安全都是头等大事。
  - National security was the paramount issue during the election debate.
  - 选举辩论中，国家安全是最重要的问题。
- **助记**：para（旁边/超越）+ mount（山）→ 站在山顶之上 → 首要的

### 3. **plausible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：貌似合理的；可信的；花言巧语的
- **英文释义**：seeming reasonable or probable; believable
- **例句**：
  - The suspect offered a plausible explanation for his presence at the scene.
  - 嫌疑人对自己出现在现场给出了貌似合理的解释。
  - Her theory sounds plausible, but it still lacks experimental proof.
  - 她的理论听起来可信，但仍缺乏实验证据。
- **助记**：plaus（鼓掌）+ ible → 值得鼓掌的 → 貌似有理的

### 4. **prudent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：谨慎的；精明的；深谋远虑的
- **英文释义**：acting with care and thought for the future; sensible
- **例句**：
  - It is prudent to save money for emergencies even when your income is stable.
  - 即使收入稳定，为紧急情况存钱也是明智的。
  - The prudent investor diversified her portfolio across several industries.
  - 这位谨慎的投资者把资产分散投资于多个行业。
- **助记**：prud（预见）+ ent → 能预见到风险 → 谨慎的

### 5. **redundant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多余的；冗余的；被裁员的
- **英文释义**：not or no longer needed or useful; superfluous
- **例句**：
  - The editor removed redundant sentences that repeated the same idea.
  - 编辑删去了重复同一观点的多余句子。
  - Hundreds of workers were made redundant when the plant relocated overseas.
  - 工厂迁往海外后，数百名工人被裁员。
- **助记**：re（再）+ und（波浪/溢出）→ 一再溢出 → 多余的

### 6. **resilient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有韧性的；能迅速恢复的；适应力强的
- **英文释义**：able to recover quickly from difficulties; tough and flexible
- **例句**：
  - Children are often more emotionally resilient than adults expect.
  - 孩子的情绪恢复力往往比成人想象的更强。
  - Bamboo is a resilient material that bends in strong winds without breaking.
  - 竹子是一种有韧性的材料，强风中弯曲而不折断。
- **助记**：re（回）+ sil（跳，如 salient）→ 能弹回原状 → 有韧性的

### 7. **sporadic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：零星的；偶发的；断断续续的
- **英文释义**：occurring at irregular intervals; scattered or isolated
- **例句**：
  - Sporadic gunfire could be heard throughout the night in the border town.
  - 边境小镇整夜都能听到零星枪声。
  - The disease occurs in sporadic cases rather than large outbreaks.
  - 这种疾病以零星病例出现，而非大规模暴发。
- **助记**：spor（种子）+ adic → 像种子散落各处 → 零星的

### 8. **steadfast** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坚定的；不动摇的；忠诚的
- **英文释义**：resolutely unchanging in purpose, loyalty, or faith
- **例句**：
  - She remained steadfast in her commitment to environmental protection.
  - 她始终坚定地致力于环境保护。
  - The soldiers were steadfast despite heavy casualties and harsh conditions.
  - 尽管伤亡惨重、条件恶劣，士兵们仍然坚定不移。
- **助记**：stead（站立）+ fast（牢固）→ 牢牢站定 → 坚定的

### 9. **tangible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有形的；可触摸的；明确的
- **英文释义**：perceptible by touch; clear and definite; real
- **例句**：
  - After months of effort, they finally achieved tangible results in sales.
  - 经过数月努力，他们终于在销售上取得了实实在在的成果。
  - The benefits of the policy were not always tangible to ordinary citizens.
  - 这项政策的好处对普通市民并不总是看得见摸得着。
- **助记**：tang（接触）+ ible → 可接触的 → 有形的

### 10. **versatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多才多艺的；多用途的；易变的
- **英文释义**：able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities
- **例句**：
  - A versatile chef can cook both traditional dishes and modern fusion cuisine.
  - 多才多艺的厨师既能做传统菜也能做现代融合菜。
  - This versatile tool can be used as a screwdriver, pliers, and knife.
  - 这件多用途工具可用作螺丝刀、钳子和小刀。
- **助记**：vers（转）+ atile → 能转向多种用途 → 多才多艺的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **austere** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：简朴的；严厉的；苦行的
- **英文释义**：severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance; without comfort
- **例句**：
  - The monastery's austere lifestyle appealed to those seeking spiritual discipline.
  - 修道院清苦的生活方式吸引了追求灵修纪律的人。
  - Her austere expression revealed little about what she was really thinking.
  - 她冷峻的表情几乎不透露真实想法。
- **助记**：auster（严厉）→ 像严冬般严酷 → 简朴严厉的

### 12. **capricious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：反复无常的；任性的；变幻莫测的
- **英文释义**：given to sudden and unaccountable changes of mood or behavior
- **例句**：
  - The capricious weather in the mountains made climbing schedules unpredictable.
  - 山区变幻莫测的天气使攀登日程难以预料。
  - Critics dismissed the director's capricious decisions as unprofessional.
  - 批评者认为这位导演任性的决定不够专业。
- **助记**：capr（山羊）→ 像山羊乱跳 → 反复无常

### 13. **denigrate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：诋毁；贬低；污蔑
- **英文释义**：to criticize unfairly; to disparage; to blacken someone's reputation
- **例句**：
  - Opponents tried to denigrate her achievements with baseless rumors.
  - 对手试图用毫无根据的谣言诋毁她的成就。
  - It is unfair to denigrate the entire generation based on a few negative examples.
  - 仅凭少数负面例子就贬低整整一代人是不公平的。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ nigr（黑）→ 涂黑 → 诋毁

### 14. **erstwhile** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：以前的；昔日的；往昔的
- **英文释义**：former; previous; belonging to an earlier time
- **例句**：
  - The erstwhile rivals later became close business partners.
  - 昔日的对手后来成了密切的商业伙伴。
  - Visitors can tour the erstwhile palace, now converted into a museum.
  - 游客可以参观这座昔日宫殿，现已改建为博物馆。
- **助记**：erst（以前）+ while → 从前的时光 → 昔日的

### 15. **fastidious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：挑剔的；苛求的；一丝不苟的
- **英文释义**：very attentive to accuracy and detail; hard to please
- **例句**：
  - The fastidious editor corrected every comma and hyphen in the manuscript.
  - 挑剔的编辑改正了手稿中的每一个逗号和连字符。
  - He is fastidious about hygiene and washes his hands dozens of times a day.
  - 他对卫生极为讲究，每天洗手数十次。
- **助记**：fastid（厌恶）+ ious → 对不洁感到厌恶 → 挑剔的
