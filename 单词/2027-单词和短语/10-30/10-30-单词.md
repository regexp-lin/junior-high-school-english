## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **diminish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减少；缩小；降低
- **英文释义**：to make or become less; to reduce in size, importance, or value
- **例句**：
  - The heavy rain did not diminish the crowd's enthusiasm for the concert.
  - 大雨并未减少观众对演唱会的热情。
  - As the years passed, his influence in the company gradually diminished.
  - 随着岁月流逝，他在公司的影响力逐渐减弱。
- **助记**：di（离开）+ min（小）+ ish → 变小离开 → 减少

### 2. **restrain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：抑制；约束；克制
- **英文释义**：to prevent from doing something; to keep under control
- **例句**：
  - She had to restrain herself from laughing during the serious meeting.
  - 她不得不在严肃的会议中克制自己不笑出来。
  - The police restrained the suspect until backup arrived.
  - 警察控制住了嫌疑人，直到增援到来。
- **助记**：re（向后）+ strain（拉紧）→ 向后拉住 → 约束

### 3. **eloquent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：雄辩的；口才好的；有说服力的
- **英文释义**：fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing
- **例句**：
  - Martin Luther King Jr. was known for his eloquent speeches on civil rights.
  - 马丁·路德·金以其关于民权的雄辩演讲而闻名。
  - Her eloquent writing moved readers to tears.
  - 她有说服力的文字令读者感动落泪。
- **助记**：e（出）+ loqu（说话）+ ent → 说话流畅 → 雄辩的

### 4. **suppress** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：镇压；压制；抑制
- **英文释义**：to forcibly put an end to; to prevent from being expressed
- **例句**：
  - The government attempted to suppress the news about the scandal.
  - 政府试图压制关于丑闻的新闻。
  - He could barely suppress a yawn during the boring lecture.
  - 他在无聊的讲座中几乎忍不住打哈欠。
- **助记**：sup（下）+ press（压）→ 往下压 → 压制

### 5. **tentative** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：暂定的；试探性的；犹豫不决的
- **英文释义**：not certain or fixed; done without confidence
- **例句**：
  - We have a tentative plan to visit Japan next summer.
  - 我们有一个暂定的计划，明年夏天去日本。
  - She took a tentative step onto the frozen lake to test its thickness.
  - 她试探性地踏上冰湖来测试冰的厚度。
- **助记**：tent（尝试）+ ative → 尝试性的 → 试探性的

### 6. **consolidate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：巩固；合并；加强
- **英文释义**：to make something stronger or more solid; to combine into a single whole
- **例句**：
  - The company consolidated its position in the market by acquiring smaller firms.
  - 公司通过收购小公司巩固了其市场地位。
  - After the exam, take time to consolidate what you have learned.
  - 考试后花时间巩固所学的内容。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ solid（坚固）+ ate → 使共同坚固 → 巩固

### 7. **juvenile** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：少年的；幼稚的；少年
- **英文释义**：relating to young people; childish; a young person
- **例句**：
  - The court deals specifically with juvenile offenders under the age of eighteen.
  - 该法院专门处理十八岁以下的少年犯。
  - His juvenile behavior at the meeting embarrassed everyone present.
  - 他在会上幼稚的行为让在场的每个人都尴尬。
- **助记**：juven（年轻）+ ile → 年轻的 → 少年的

### 8. **manifest** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：明显的；显然的；表明；显示
- **英文释义**：clear or obvious to the eye or mind; to display or show
- **例句**：
  - The students' manifest enthusiasm for the project impressed the teacher.
  - 学生们对项目表现出的明显热情给老师留下了深刻印象。
  - Stress can manifest itself in various physical symptoms.
  - 压力可以通过各种身体症状表现出来。
- **助记**：mani（手）+ fest（打击）→ 用手展示 → 明显的

### 9. **persistent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坚持不懈的；持续的；顽固的
- **英文释义**：continuing firmly in a course of action despite difficulty; lasting for a long time
- **例句**：
  - Through persistent effort, she finally passed the difficult exam.
  - 通过坚持不懈的努力，她终于通过了那场困难的考试。
  - The persistent rain caused flooding in several areas.
  - 持续的降雨导致多个地区发生洪水。
- **助记**：per（始终）+ sist（站立）+ ent → 始终站着不走 → 坚持不懈的

### 10. **rebuke** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：斥责；指责；训斥
- **英文释义**：to express sharp disapproval or criticism
- **例句**：
  - The manager rebuked the employee for arriving late three days in a row.
  - 经理因员工连续三天迟到而斥责了他。
  - His harsh rebuke left her feeling hurt and embarrassed.
  - 他严厉的斥责让她感到受伤和尴尬。
- **助记**：re（反）+ buke（打击）→ 反复打击 → 斥责

### 高一词汇

### 11. **euphemism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：委婉语；婉辞
- **英文释义**：a mild or indirect word or expression substituted for one considered too harsh or blunt
- **例句**：
  - "Passed away" is a common euphemism for "died."
  - "去世"是"死亡"的常见委婉语。
  - Politicians often use euphemisms to soften the impact of bad news.
  - 政客们常用委婉语来缓和坏消息的冲击。
- **助记**：eu（好）+ phem（说话）+ ism → 好听的说法 → 委婉语

### 12. **recalcitrant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽抗的；不服从的；难以管教的
- **英文释义**：having an obstinately uncooperative attitude towards authority or discipline
- **例句**：
  - The recalcitrant student refused to follow any of the school rules.
  - 那个顽抗的学生拒绝遵守任何校规。
  - Dealing with recalcitrant patients requires patience and understanding.
  - 对待不服从的病人需要耐心和理解。
- **助记**：re（反）+ calc（踢）+ itrant → 反复踢腿反抗 → 顽抗的

### 13. **magnanimous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；慷慨的；高尚的
- **英文释义**：generous or forgiving, especially towards a rival or someone less powerful
- **例句**：
  - The magnanimous winner congratulated his opponent on a well-fought match.
  - 宽宏大量的获胜者祝贺对手打了一场精彩的比赛。
  - It was magnanimous of her to forgive those who had wronged her.
  - 她原谅了那些曾亏待她的人，真是宽宏大量。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 心灵宏大 → 宽宏大量的

### 14. **promulgate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：颁布；公布；传播
- **英文释义**：to make known publicly; to put a law into effect by official proclamation
- **例句**：
  - The government promulgated new regulations to protect the environment.
  - 政府颁布了新的环境保护法规。
  - The organization works to promulgate knowledge about healthy eating habits.
  - 该组织致力于传播健康饮食习惯的知识。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ mulg（民众）+ ate → 向民众公开 → 颁布

### 15. **verisimilitude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：逼真；貌似真实
- **英文释义**：the appearance of being true or real
- **例句**：
  - The novel's verisimilitude made readers believe the story was based on real events.
  - 这部小说的逼真感让读者相信故事是基于真实事件的。
  - Good acting requires a degree of verisimilitude to convince the audience.
  - 好的表演需要一定程度的逼真感来说服观众。
- **助记**：veri（真实）+ simil（相似）+ tude → 和真实相似 → 逼真
