## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **admonish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：告诫；劝告；训诫
- **英文释义**：to warn or reprimand someone firmly; to advise earnestly
- **例句**：
  - The teacher admonished the students for not doing their homework.
  - 老师训诫了没做作业的学生。
  - His mother admonished him to drive carefully in the rain.
  - 妈妈告诫他雨天要小心开车。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ mon（提醒）+ ish → 向人提醒 → 告诫

### 2. **bewilder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使困惑；使迷茫；使不知所措
- **英文释义**：to cause someone to become perplexed and confused
- **例句**：
  - The complex math problem bewildered most of the class.
  - 这道复杂的数学题让班上大多数人感到困惑。
  - Tourists were bewildered by the city's maze-like streets.
  - 游客被城市迷宫般的街道弄得不知所措。
- **助记**：be（使）+ wild（迷路）+ er → 使人迷路 → 使困惑

### 3. **comply** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：遵从；服从；遵守
- **英文释义**：to act in accordance with a wish, command, or rule
- **例句**：
  - All passengers must comply with the airline's safety regulations.
  - 所有乘客必须遵守航空公司的安全规定。
  - The company refused to comply with the court order.
  - 公司拒绝遵从法庭命令。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ ply（满足）→ 完全满足要求 → 遵从

### 4. **discard** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：丢弃；抛弃；放弃
- **英文释义**：to get rid of something as no longer useful or desirable
- **例句**：
  - Please discard any expired food from the refrigerator.
  - 请把冰箱里过期食品扔掉。
  - The scientist discarded the failed hypothesis and started over.
  - 科学家放弃了失败的假设重新开始。
- **助记**：dis（去除）+ card（卡片）→ 把牌扔掉 → 丢弃

### 5. **exasperate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激怒；使恼怒；使烦躁
- **英文释义**：to irritate or frustrate someone intensely
- **例句**：
  - Repeated delays exasperated the waiting passengers.
  - 一再延误激怒了等候的旅客。
  - His refusal to apologize exasperated his parents.
  - 他拒绝道歉让父母恼怒不已。
- **助记**：ex（加强）+ asper（粗糙）+ ate → 使情绪变粗糙 → 激怒

### 6. **feasible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可行的；可能的；办得到的
- **英文释义**：possible and practical to do easily or conveniently
- **例句**：
  - The engineers confirmed that building the bridge is technically feasible.
  - 工程师们确认建这座桥在技术上是可行的。
  - Is it feasible to finish the project by next Friday?
  - 在下周五前完成项目可行吗？
- **助记**：feas（做）+ ible（能）→ 能做的 → 可行的

### 7. **gullible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：容易上当的；轻信的；好骗的
- **英文释义**：easily persuaded to believe something; credulous
- **例句**：
  - Scammers target gullible people who trust strangers too easily.
  - 骗子专挑容易轻信陌生人的人下手。
  - Children are often gullible because they lack life experience.
  - 孩子们常因缺乏生活经验而容易受骗。
- **助记**：gull（海鸥/欺骗）+ ible → 容易被骗的 → 轻信的

### 8. **havoc** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：浩劫；大破坏；混乱
- **英文释义**：widespread destruction; great confusion or disorder
- **例句**：
  - The typhoon wreaked havoc on coastal villages.
  - 台风对沿海村庄造成了巨大破坏。
  - A computer virus can cause havoc in a company's network.
  - 电脑病毒能在公司网络中造成严重混乱。
- **助记**：havoc 谐音"浩劫" → 大破坏

### 9. **intricate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：错综复杂的；精密的；精细的
- **英文释义**：very complicated or detailed; having many complexly arranged elements
- **例句**：
  - The intricate design of the clock took years to complete.
  - 这座钟的精密设计花了数年才完成。
  - Understanding the intricate rules of chess takes patience.
  - 理解国际象棋错综复杂的规则需要耐心。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ tric（纠缠）+ ate → 纠缠进去 → 错综复杂

### 10. **jubilant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：欢欣鼓舞的；喜气洋洋的
- **英文释义**：feeling or expressing great happiness and triumph
- **例句**：
  - The jubilant crowd celebrated the team's championship victory.
  - 欢腾的人群庆祝球队赢得冠军。
  - She was jubilant after receiving the university acceptance letter.
  - 收到大学录取通知书后她欣喜若狂。
- **助记**：jubil（欢呼）+ ant → 欢呼的 → 欢欣鼓舞的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **laconic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：简洁的；言简意赅的；惜字如金的
- **英文释义**：using very few words; concise to the point of seeming rude
- **例句**：
  - His laconic reply of "maybe" left everyone guessing.
  - 他"也许"这个惜字如金的回答让大家猜测不已。
  - The laconic report summarized a year's work in three pages.
  - 这份简洁的报告用三页纸总结了一年的工作。
- **助记**：来自 Laconia（斯巴达地区）→ 斯巴达人以言简闻名 → 简洁

### 12. **meticulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；细致的；严谨的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - The surgeon was meticulous in preparing for the complex operation.
  - 外科医生为复杂手术做了一丝不苟的准备。
  - Meticulous record-keeping is essential in scientific research.
  - 严谨的记录对科学研究至关重要。
- **助记**：metic（恐惧）+ ulous → 怕出错 → 一丝不苟

### 13. **nuance** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：细微差别；微妙之处；精妙
- **英文释义**：a subtle difference in shade of meaning, expression, or sound
- **例句**：
  - The nuances of tone in Chinese make it challenging for foreign learners.
  - 中文声调的微妙差别使外国学习者感到困难。
  - A skilled translator captures every nuance of the original text.
  - 优秀的翻译能捕捉原文的每一个细微之处。
- **助记**：nu（新）+ ance → 新的微小变化 → 细微差别

### 14. **ominous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不祥的；预兆坏事的；令人担忧的
- **英文释义**：giving the impression that something bad or unpleasant is going to happen
- **例句**：
  - Dark, ominous clouds gathered on the horizon before the storm.
  - 暴风雨前，不祥的乌云聚集在地平线上。
  - The sudden silence felt ominous to the soldiers on patrol.
  - 突然的寂静让巡逻的士兵感到不安。
- **助记**：omin（预兆）+ ous → 有预兆的 → 不祥的

### 15. **pragmatic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；注重实效的；实用主义的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically; practical rather than idealistic
- **例句**：
  - A pragmatic approach to problem-solving focuses on what works.
  - 务实的问题解决方式关注什么管用。
  - The manager took a pragmatic view and cut the failing project.
  - 经理务实地砍掉了失败的项目。
- **助记**：pragma（行动/实践）+ tic → 注重行动 → 务实的
