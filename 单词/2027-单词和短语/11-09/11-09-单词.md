## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **vindicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证明……正确；为……辩护；洗雪冤屈
- **英文释义**：to clear of accusation or blame; to show to be right
- **例句**：
  - The new evidence vindicated the suspect and proved his innocence.
  - 新证据为嫌疑人洗清了罪名，证明了他的清白。
  - History has vindicated her decision to invest in renewable energy.
  - 历史证明了她投资可再生能源的决定是正确的。
- **助记**：vin（力量）+ dic（说）+ ate → 有力地说出真相 → 证明正确

### 2. **tenacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坚韧的；顽强的；固执的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent
- **例句**：
  - The tenacious athlete refused to give up despite multiple injuries.
  - 这位坚韧的运动员尽管多次受伤仍不放弃。
  - She has a tenacious grip on her goals and never lets setbacks discourage her.
  - 她对目标有着坚韧的把握，从不让挫折使自己气馁。
- **助记**：ten（持有）+ acious → 紧紧持有的 → 坚韧的

### 3. **circumvent** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：规避；绕过；巧妙避开
- **英文释义**：to find a way around an obstacle or restriction
- **例句**：
  - Some companies try to circumvent tax laws through offshore accounts.
  - 一些公司试图通过离岸账户规避税法。
  - The hiker circumvented the flooded path by taking a different route.
  - 徒步者绕过被淹的小路，走了另一条路线。
- **助记**：circum（绕圈）+ vent（来）→ 绕着走过来 → 规避

### 4. **lucrative** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有利可图的；赚钱的
- **英文释义**：producing a great deal of profit
- **例句**：
  - Real estate can be a very lucrative investment if done wisely.
  - 如果操作得当，房地产是非常有利可图的投资。
  - She left her lucrative career in finance to pursue her passion for teaching.
  - 她离开了收入丰厚的金融职业，去追求她对教学的热情。
- **助记**：lucr（利润）+ ative → 带来利润的 → 有利可图的

### 5. **fabricate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：捏造；编造；制造
- **英文释义**：to invent or concoct something false; to construct or manufacture
- **例句**：
  - The journalist was fired for fabricating stories to increase readership.
  - 记者因捏造故事增加读者量而被解雇。
  - The parts were fabricated using advanced 3D printing technology.
  - 这些零件是用先进的3D打印技术制造的。
- **助记**：fabric（织物/结构）+ ate → 编织/编造 → 捏造

### 6. **inherent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：固有的；内在的；天生的
- **英文释义**：existing in something as a permanent, essential, or characteristic attribute
- **例句**：
  - There are inherent risks in any kind of financial investment.
  - 任何类型的金融投资都有固有风险。
  - Creativity is an inherent quality that every child possesses.
  - 创造力是每个孩子都具备的内在品质。
- **助记**：in（里面）+ her（粘附）+ ent → 粘在里面的 → 固有的

### 7. **transcend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：超越；超出；胜过
- **英文释义**：to go beyond the range or limits of something
- **例句**：
  - Great art has the power to transcend cultural and linguistic barriers.
  - 伟大的艺术有超越文化和语言障碍的力量。
  - Her achievements transcend those of any previous athlete in the sport.
  - 她的成就超越了这项运动中任何之前的运动员。
- **助记**：trans（超过）+ scend（攀登）→ 攀登超过 → 超越

### 8. **negligible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：微不足道的；可忽略的
- **英文释义**：so small or unimportant as to be not worth considering
- **例句**：
  - The difference in price between the two products is negligible.
  - 两种产品之间的价格差异微乎其微。
  - The environmental impact of the project was deemed negligible.
  - 该项目的环境影响被认为可以忽略不计。
- **助记**：neg（忽略）+ lig（选择）+ ible → 可以选择忽略的 → 微不足道的

### 9. **bolster** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：支持；增强；巩固
- **英文释义**：to support or strengthen
- **例句**：
  - The government introduced measures to bolster the struggling economy.
  - 政府推出了措施来增强萎靡的经济。
  - Positive feedback from customers bolstered the team's confidence.
  - 客户的积极反馈增强了团队的信心。
- **助记**：bolster（枕头/支撑物）→ 像枕头一样支撑 → 支持

### 10. **refute** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：反驳；驳斥；证伪
- **英文释义**：to prove a statement or theory to be wrong or false
- **例句**：
  - The scientist refuted the popular myth with solid experimental data.
  - 科学家用确凿的实验数据驳斥了流行的谬论。
  - She refuted all the allegations made against her in the press.
  - 她驳斥了媒体对她提出的所有指控。
- **助记**：re（回）+ fute（打击）→ 反击回去 → 反驳

### 高一词汇

### 11. **schadenfreude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：幸灾乐祸
- **英文释义**：pleasure derived from the misfortune of others
- **例句**：
  - He felt a guilty sense of schadenfreude when his rival's project failed.
  - 当竞争对手的项目失败时，他有一种内疚的幸灾乐祸感。
  - Schadenfreude is a universal human emotion, though few like to admit it.
  - 幸灾乐祸是一种普遍的人类情感，尽管很少有人愿意承认。
- **助记**：德语 Schaden（损害）+ Freude（快乐）→ 他人的不幸带来的快乐 → 幸灾乐祸

### 12. **lachrymose** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱哭的；催人泪下的；悲伤的
- **英文释义**：tearful or given to weeping; inducing tears
- **例句**：
  - The lachrymose ending of the movie had the entire audience in tears.
  - 电影催人泪下的结局让全场观众都哭了。
  - She gave a lachrymose speech about her late grandmother at the memorial.
  - 她在追悼会上做了一场令人伤感的关于已故祖母的演讲。
- **助记**：lachry（眼泪）+ mose → 充满眼泪的 → 爱哭的

### 13. **ineffable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：难以言表的；无法形容的
- **英文释义**：too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words
- **例句**：
  - The beauty of the northern lights was ineffable — no words could capture it.
  - 北极光的美丽难以言表——没有语言能描述它。
  - She felt an ineffable sense of peace while watching the sunrise.
  - 她在看日出时感到一种难以言表的宁静。
- **助记**：in（不）+ ef（出）+ fable（说）→ 说不出来的 → 难以言表的

### 14. **sesquipedalian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：冗长的（词语）；喜用长词的
- **英文释义**：of or characterized by long words; tending to use very long words
- **例句**：
  - The professor's sesquipedalian vocabulary confused many of his students.
  - 教授冗长的词汇让许多学生感到困惑。
  - Avoid sesquipedalian language in your essays — keep it simple and clear.
  - 在作文中避免使用冗长的词汇——保持简洁明了。
- **助记**：sesqui（一个半）+ ped（脚）+ alian → 一个半脚那么长 → 冗长的

### 15. **verisimilar** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：逼真的；似乎真实的
- **英文释义**：appearing to be true or real
- **例句**：
  - The novel's verisimilar description of wartime London transported readers back in time.
  - 小说对战时伦敦的逼真描述将读者带回了过去。
  - The special effects created a verisimilar alien world on screen.
  - 特效在银幕上创造了一个逼真的外星世界。
- **助记**：veri（真实）+ simil（相似）+ ar → 与真实相似的 → 逼真的
