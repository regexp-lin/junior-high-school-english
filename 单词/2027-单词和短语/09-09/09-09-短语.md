## 📖 短语详解

### 初三短语

### 1. **pass the buck** `初三`

- **中文释义**：推卸责任；甩锅
- **用法说明**：把问题推给别人处理。
- **例句**：
  - Managers who pass the buck demoralize their teams.
  - 爱甩锅的经理会打击团队士气。
  - He tried to pass the buck to his assistant when the client complained.
  - 客户投诉时，他试图把责任推给助理。
- **近义短语**：shift blame; pass the parcel; dodge responsibility
- **易混淆短语**：pass the buck（甩锅）vs pass the baton（交接棒、移交职责）—— 不同

### 2. **put one's foot down** `初三`

- **中文释义**：坚决反对；坚决表态；严加管束
- **用法说明**：表示不再妥协，采取坚定立场。
- **例句**：
  - Parents finally put their foot down and limited screen time to one hour.
  - 父母终于坚决限制屏幕时间为一小时。
  - The union put its foot down and refused further pay cuts.
  - 工会坚决拒绝进一步降薪。
- **近义短语**：take a firm stand; lay down the law; assert authority
- **易混淆短语**：put one's foot down（坚决）vs put one's foot in one's mouth（说错话得罪人）—— 不同

### 3. **ring a bell** `初三`

- **中文释义**：听起来耳熟；有点印象
- **用法说明**：否定句 doesn't ring a bell 表示完全没印象。
- **例句**：
  - The name rings a bell, but I can't remember where we met.
  - 这名字耳熟，但我想不起在哪见过。
  - That melody rings a bell — was it our school song?
  - 这旋律耳熟——是不是我们的校歌？
- **近义短语**：sound familiar; jog one's memory; strike a chord
- **易混淆短语**：ring a bell（耳熟）vs ring the bell（按铃/打铃）—— 不同

### 4. **take with a grain of salt** `初三`

- **中文释义**：对某事半信半疑；有所保留地听
- **用法说明**：take sth. with a grain of salt 表示不完全相信。
- **例句**：
  - Take weather forecasts more than a week away with a grain of salt.
  - 对一周以上的天气预报不必全信。
  - Rumors on social media should always be taken with a grain of salt.
  - 社交媒体上的传言都应半信半疑。
- **近义短语**：be skeptical; doubt; not take at face value
- **易混淆短语**：grain of salt（半信半疑）vs worth one's salt（称职）—— 不同

---

### 高一短语

### 5. **burn the midnight oil** `高一`

- **中文释义**：开夜车；熬夜工作
- **用法说明**：形象化说法，指深夜点灯读书或办公。
- **例句**：
  - She burned the midnight oil for weeks before the national exam.
  - 国考前几周她天天开夜车复习。
  - Programmers often burn the midnight oil before product launch deadlines.
  - 程序员在产品发布截止前常常熬夜。
- **近义短语**：work late into the night; pull an all-nighter (stronger); stay up studying
- **易混淆短语**：burn the midnight oil（熬夜工作）vs burn bridges（断后路）—— 无关
