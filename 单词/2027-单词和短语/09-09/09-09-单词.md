## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **hedonism** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：享乐主义；追求快乐
- **英文释义**：the pursuit of pleasure; the ethical theory that pleasure is the highest good
- **例句**：
  - Critics accused the wealthy elite of shallow hedonism.
  - 批评者指责富豪精英沉溺于肤浅的享乐主义。
  - Ancient Greek philosophers debated whether hedonism could guide a moral life.
  - 古希腊哲学家争论享乐主义能否指导道德生活。
- **助记**：hedon（快乐）→ 以快乐为最高目标 → 享乐主义

### 2. **iconoclast** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：反传统者；打破偶像者；标新立异者
- **英文释义**：a person who attacks cherished beliefs or institutions
- **例句**：
  - Steve Jobs was seen as a tech industry iconoclast who defied conventional design.
  - 乔布斯被视为挑战传统设计的科技界反传统人物。
  - Young iconoclasts challenged the university's outdated curriculum.
  - 年轻的叛逆者质疑大学过时的课程设置。
- **助记**：icon（圣像）+ clast（打破）→ 打破偶像 → 反传统者

### 3. **jejune** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：幼稚的；枯燥乏味的；不成熟的
- **英文释义**：naive, simplistic, and superficial; dull and uninteresting
- **例句**：
  - The critic dismissed the novel as jejune and poorly plotted.
  - 评论家认为这部小说幼稚、结构拙劣。
  - His jejune understanding of economics worried the interviewers.
  - 他对经济学的幼稚理解让面试官担忧。
- **助记**：jej（空腹）→ 空洞无物 → 幼稚乏味

### 4. **kindred** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：同类的；志趣相投的；亲属
- **英文释义**：similar in character; one's family and relations
- **例句**：
  - She found kindred spirits among fellow volunteers at the animal shelter.
  - 她在动物收容所的志愿者中找到了志趣相投的人。
  - Linguists grouped kindred languages into the same family tree.
  - 语言学家将同源语言归入同一家族树。
- **助记**：kin（亲属）+ dred → 同宗同类 → 志趣相投

### 5. **labyrinth** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：迷宫；错综复杂的事物
- **英文释义**：a complicated irregular network of passages or paths; something intricate
- **例句**：
  - Tourists got lost in the labyrinth of narrow streets in the old quarter.
  - 游客在老城区迷宫般狭窄街道中迷路。
  - Tax law is a labyrinth only experts can navigate confidently.
  - 税法错综复杂，只有专家才能从容应对。
- **助记**：来自希腊神话克里特迷宫 → 迷宫

### 6. **malfeasance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：渎职；不法行为；恶行
- **英文释义**：wrongdoing or misconduct, especially by a public official
- **例句**：
  - The mayor resigned amid allegations of financial malfeasance.
  - 市长在财务渎职指控中辞职。
  - Auditors uncovered malfeasance in the charity's bookkeeping.
  - 审计员在慈善机构的账目中发现了不法行为。
- **助记**：mal（坏）+ feas（做）→ 做坏事 → 渎职

### 7. **nadir** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：最低点；最倒霉的时刻；天底
- **英文释义**：the lowest point; the opposite of zenith
- **例句**：
  - The team's morale reached its nadir after five consecutive losses.
  - 五连败后，球队士气跌至谷底。
  - The 1930s were the nadir of the global economy during the Great Depression.
  - 二十世纪三十年代是大萧条时期世界经济的谷底。
- **助记**：与 zenith（天顶）相对 → 最低点

### 8. **obsequious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：谄媚的；巴结的；顺从的
- **英文释义**：obedient or attentive to an excessive degree; fawning
- **例句**：
  - The obsequious assistant agreed with every word the boss said.
  - 谄媚的助理对老板说的每句话都附和。
  - Obsequious flattery made the celebrity uncomfortable at the party.
  - 聚会上令人肉麻的奉承让那位名人很不舒服。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ sequ（跟随）→ 亦步亦趋 → 谄媚

### 9. **pragmatic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；实用主义的；重实效的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically; practical
- **例句**：
  - Engineers took a pragmatic approach to fixing the bridge within budget.
  - 工程师以务实态度在预算内修桥。
  - Pragmatic leaders prioritize results over ideological purity.
  - 务实的领导人把结果置于意识形态纯洁之上。
- **助记**：pragma（行为/事务）→ 重实效 → 务实的

### 10. **quarantine** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：隔离；检疫期
- **英文释义**：isolation to prevent disease spread; the period of isolation
- **例句**：
  - Travelers from high-risk areas were placed under quarantine for two weeks.
  - 来自高风险地区的旅客被隔离两周。
  - The farm quarantined sick animals to protect the herd.
  - 农场隔离病畜以保护整群牲畜。
- **助记**：quarant（四十）→ 原指四十天隔离期 → 隔离

### 高一词汇

### 11. **recant** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：公开撤回；宣布放弃（信仰、言论）
- **英文释义**：to withdraw and disavow a former belief or statement publicly
- **例句**：
  - Under pressure, the witness recanted her earlier testimony.
  - 在压力下，证人撤回了先前的证词。
  - The scientist recanted his theory when new data contradicted it.
  - 新数据与理论矛盾时，这位科学家公开放弃了该理论。
- **助记**：re（回）+ cant（唱）→ 唱反调收回 → 撤回

### 12. **subsume** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：纳入；包含；归入更大类别
- **英文释义**：to include or absorb in something larger or more comprehensive
- **例句**：
  - The new category subsumes several older labels under one heading.
  - 新类别把几个旧标签归并到一个名称下。
  - Marxist theory subsumes individual struggles under class conflict.
  - 马克思主义理论把个人斗争归入阶级冲突框架。
- **助记**：sub（在下）+ sume（拿）→ 拿到下面包容 → 纳入

### 13. **truculent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；凶狠的；尖刻的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant
- **例句**：
  - The truculent teenager argued with every teacher who corrected him.
  - 这名好斗的少年与每位纠正他的老师争吵。
  - Truculent rhetoric dominated the talk show segment.
  - 脱口秀片段中充斥着尖刻言辞。
- **助记**：truc（凶猛）→ 好斗凶狠

### 14. **umbrage** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：不快；愤怒；感到受辱
- **英文释义**：offense or annoyance; a feeling of being insulted
- **例句**：
  - She took umbrage at the implication that she was lazy.
  - 暗示她懒惰让她感到不快。
  - The diplomat's careless joke caused umbrage among foreign guests.
  - 外交官粗心的玩笑让外宾感到受辱。
- **助记**：umbra（阴影）→ 心里蒙上阴影 → 不快

### 15. **verisimilitude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：逼真；貌似真实；似真性
- **英文释义**：the appearance of being true or real; authenticity in art or fiction
- **例句**：
  - Historical novels gain verisimilitude from accurate period details.
  - 历史小说因准确的年代细节而显得逼真。
  - The film's verisimilitude made audiences forget they were watching actors.
  - 影片的逼真感让观众忘记是在看电影。
- **助记**：veri（真）+ simil（相似）+ tude → 与真相似 → 逼真
