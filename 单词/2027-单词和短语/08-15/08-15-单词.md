## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **fecund** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多产的；肥沃的；富有创造力的
- **英文释义**：producing or capable of producing many offspring, crops, or new ideas; fertile
- **例句**：
  - The fecund soil along the river supports three harvests a year.
  - 沿河肥沃的土壤一年可支持三季收成。
  - She is a fecund writer who publishes two novels every year.
  - 她是一位多产的作家，每年出版两部小说。
- **助记**：fec（做/生产，如 factory）+ und → 能不断生产 → 多产的

### 2. **germane** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：密切相关的；切题的；恰当的
- **英文释义**：relevant to a subject under consideration; pertinent
- **例句**：
  - The judge asked the lawyer to limit his remarks to issues germane to the case.
  - 法官要求律师的发言仅限于与案件密切相关的问题。
  - Any evidence not germane to the charge will be excluded.
  - 与指控无关的证据将被排除。
- **助记**：与 German（日耳曼）无关；germ（萌芽/核心）+ ane → 与核心议题相连 → 切题的

### 3. **inchoate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：刚开始的；未充分发展的；不成熟的
- **英文释义**：just begun and not fully formed or developed; rudimentary
- **例句**：
  - The plan was still in an inchoate stage and needed more research.
  - 计划仍处于雏形阶段，需要更多调研。
  - Inchoate feelings of resentment eventually grew into open conflict.
  - 起初模糊的不满情绪最终演变成公开冲突。
- **助记**：in（在内）+ choate（开始）→ 刚开始成形 → 未成熟的

### 4. **ingenuous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：天真的；坦率的；不世故的
- **英文释义**：innocent and unsuspecting; naive
- **例句**：
  - Her ingenuous smile convinced everyone that she meant no harm.
  - 她天真的微笑让大家都相信她并无恶意。
  - The ingenuous tourist fell for the street vendor's exaggerated claims.
  - 天真的游客轻信了街头小贩夸张的说法。
- **助记**：in（向内）+ genu（出生/本性）+ ous → 本性外露 → 天真坦率

### 5. **iniquity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：邪恶；不公正；罪恶行径
- **英文释义**：immoral or grossly unfair behavior; wickedness
- **例句**：
  - The novel exposes the social iniquity of child labor in the nineteenth century.
  - 小说揭露了十九世纪童工现象的社会不公。
  - He spoke out against the iniquity of the corrupt regime.
  - 他公开谴责腐败政权的邪恶行径。
- **助记**：in（不）+ iqu（平等）+ ity → 不平等/不义 → 邪恶

### 6. **insouciant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：漫不经心的；无忧无虑的；满不在乎的
- **英文释义**：showing a casual lack of concern; carefree and unconcerned
- **例句**：
  - He gave an insouciant shrug when asked about the looming deadline.
  - 当被问及临近的截止日期时，他漫不经心地耸了耸肩。
  - Her insouciant attitude sometimes offended colleagues who took work very seriously.
  - 她满不在乎的态度有时会让非常认真对待工作的同事不快。
- **助记**：in（不）+ soucier（担心，法语）→ 不担心 → 无忧无虑

### 7. **interlocutor** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：对话者；参加谈话的人
- **英文释义**：a person who takes part in a dialogue or conversation
- **例句**：
  - In the interview, the journalist proved a sharp interlocutor who asked tough follow-up questions.
  - 在采访中，这位记者作为对话者十分敏锐，追问尖锐。
  - Plato's dialogues feature Socrates as the main interlocutor.
  - 柏拉图的对话录中，苏格拉底是主要对话者。
- **助记**：inter（之间）+ locu（说）+ tor → 在彼此之间说话的人

### 8. **inveterate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：根深蒂固的；积习难改的；成癖的
- **英文释义**：having a particular habit, interest, or way of life that is long-established and unlikely to change
- **例句**：
  - He was an inveterate gambler who could not stay away from casinos.
  - 他是个积习难改的赌徒，离不开赌场。
  - Her inveterate pessimism made it hard for the team to stay motivated.
  - 她根深蒂固的悲观情绪让团队难以保持士气。
- **助记**：in（在内）+ veter（老）+ ate → 老毛病在内 → 根深蒂固的

### 9. **irascible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易怒的；暴躁的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a tendency to be easily angered
- **例句**：
  - The irascible professor was known for snapping at students who arrived late.
  - 那位脾气暴躁的教授以对迟到学生发火闻名。
  - Despite his irascible manner, he was deeply loyal to his friends.
  - 尽管性情暴躁，他对朋友却十分忠诚。
- **助记**：iras（愤怒）+ cible → 容易被点燃怒火 → 易怒的

### 10. **jejune** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：（文章等）空洞乏味的；（人）幼稚浅薄的
- **英文释义**：naive, simplistic, and superficial; dull and uninteresting
- **例句**：
  - The critic dismissed the play as jejune and predictable.
  - 评论家认为这部剧幼稚乏味、毫无新意。
  - His jejune arguments failed to persuade the experienced panel.
  - 他浅薄的论点未能说服经验丰富的评审团。
- **助记**：联想 juvenile（少年的）→ 过于幼稚 → 浅薄

### 高一词汇

### 11. **largesse** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：慷慨馈赠；施舍；大量赠予
- **英文释义**：generosity in bestowing money or gifts upon others; money or gifts given generously
- **例句**：
  - The charity dinner raised millions through the largesse of wealthy donors.
  - 慈善晚宴依靠富人的慷慨捐赠筹集了数百万。
  - Medieval kings often secured loyalty through largesse and land grants.
  - 中世纪国王常以赏赐和封地换取忠诚。
- **助记**：large（大）+ esse（存在）→ 大方的给予 → 慷慨馈赠

### 12. **legerdemain** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：手法技巧；（尤指）诡辩；巧妙花招
- **英文释义**：skillful use of one's hands when performing conjuring tricks; deception or trickery
- **例句**：
  - The magician's legerdemain left the audience unable to see how the coin vanished.
  - 魔术师手法娴熟，观众看不出硬币如何消失。
  - Critics accused the company of financial legerdemain to hide its losses.
  - 批评者指责该公司玩弄财务花招以掩盖亏损。
- **助记**：法语“轻手”（light of hand）→ 戏法手法 → 花招

### 13. **licentious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：放荡的；淫乱的；无视道德规范的
- **英文释义**：promiscuous and unprincipled in sexual matters; disregarding accepted rules
- **例句**：
  - The novel portrays the licentious lifestyle of the aristocracy before the revolution.
  - 小说描绘了革命前贵族阶层放荡的生活方式。
  - Licentious behavior at the festival drew criticism from conservative groups.
  - 节庆中的放荡行为遭到保守团体批评。
- **助记**：licent（许可）+ ious → 给自己过度许可 → 放纵

### 14. **maladroit** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：笨拙的；不灵巧的；不得体的
- **英文释义**：ineffective or clumsy in action or execution
- **例句**：
  - His maladroit attempt at humor only embarrassed everyone at the table.
  - 他拙劣的幽默尝试只会让桌上的每个人尴尬。
  - The maladroit handling of the crisis damaged the government's reputation.
  - 对危机的笨拙处理损害了政府声誉。
- **助记**：mal（坏）+ adroit（灵巧）→ 不灵巧 → 笨拙的

### 15. **malediction** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：诅咒；恶语；咒骂
- **英文释义**：a magical word or phrase uttered with the intention of bringing about evil; a curse
- **例句**：
  - In the fairy tale, the witch's malediction could only be broken by true love.
  - 童话里，女巫的诅咒只有真爱能破除。
  - He muttered a malediction under his breath when he stubbed his toe.
  - 他踢到脚趾时低声咒骂了一句。
- **助记**：mal（坏）+ dict（说）+ ion → 说坏话 → 诅咒
