## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **adhere** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：遵守；坚持；粘附
- **英文释义**：to stick firmly to a surface; to follow or observe a rule or belief
- **例句**：
  - All members must adhere to the club's code of conduct.
  - 所有会员必须遵守俱乐部的行为准则。
  - The tape didn't adhere well to the wet surface.
  - 胶带在潮湿表面上粘不牢。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ here（粘）→ 粘上去 → 坚持/粘附

### 2. **besiege** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：围攻；包围；困扰
- **英文释义**：to surround with armed forces; to crowd around oppressively
- **例句**：
  - Fans besieged the stadium entrance hoping to see the star.
  - 粉丝围在体育场入口希望看到明星。
  - The company was besieged by complaints after the product recall.
  - 产品召回后公司被投诉淹没。
- **助记**：be（使）+ siege（围攻）→ 围攻

### 3. **conceal** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：隐藏；隐瞒；遮盖
- **英文释义**：to keep from sight or discovery; to hide
- **例句**：
  - The sunglasses concealed the sadness in her eyes.
  - 太阳镜遮住了她眼中的悲伤。
  - He was accused of concealing evidence from the police.
  - 他被指控向警方隐瞒证据。
- **助记**：con（完全）+ ceal（隐藏）→ 完全隐藏 → 隐瞒

### 4. **defy** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：违抗；藐视；使无法
- **英文释义**：to openly resist or refuse to obey; to challenge
- **例句**：
  - The brave journalist defied the government's censorship orders.
  - 勇敢的记者违抗了政府的审查命令。
  - The magician's tricks seemed to defy the laws of physics.
  - 魔术师的把戏似乎违背了物理定律。
- **助记**：de（反）+ fy（信）→ 不信/反对 → 违抗

### 5. **embargo** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：禁运；封锁；禁止
- **英文释义**：an official ban on trade or other commercial activity; to impose such a ban
- **例句**：
  - The United Nations imposed an arms embargo on the region.
  - 联合国对该地区实施了武器禁运。
  - Several countries embargoed oil exports during the conflict.
  - 冲突期间几个国家实施了石油出口禁运。
- **助记**：em（进入）+ bargo（禁止/船）→ 禁止船只进出 → 禁运

### 6. **frantic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：疯狂的；狂乱的；焦急万分的
- **英文释义**：wild or distraught with fear, anxiety, or excitement
- **例句**：
  - The mother made frantic phone calls when her child went missing.
  - 孩子失踪后母亲焦急万分地打电话。
  - There was a frantic rush to finish the report before the deadline.
  - 截止日期前大家疯狂赶写报告。
- **助记**：fran（疯狂）+ tic → 疯狂的

### 7. **grieve** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：悲伤；哀悼；使伤心
- **英文释义**：to feel intense sorrow; to mourn
- **例句**：
  - The whole nation grieved the loss of the beloved leader.
  - 全国人民为敬爱的领导人的逝世而悲伤。
  - It takes time to grieve after losing a close friend.
  - 失去挚友后需要时间来哀悼。
- **助记**：grieve → grief（悲伤）→ 感到悲伤

### 8. **humiliate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：羞辱；使丢脸；使蒙羞
- **英文释义**：to make someone feel ashamed and foolish by injuring their dignity
- **例句**：
  - No teacher should humiliate a student in front of the class.
  - 老师不应在全班面前羞辱学生。
  - Losing 10-0 humiliated the defending champions.
  - 以0比10惨败让卫冕冠军蒙羞。
- **助记**：humili（谦卑/低下）+ ate → 使人低下 → 羞辱

### 9. **inquisitive** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：好奇的；好问的；爱探究的
- **英文释义**：curious or inquiring; eager to learn
- **例句**：
  - The inquisitive child asked "why" at least twenty times a day.
  - 好奇的孩子每天至少问二十遍"为什么"。
  - An inquisitive mind is the foundation of scientific discovery.
  - 好奇的头脑是科学发现的基础。
- **助记**：in（内）+ quis（问）+ itive → 不断追问 → 好奇的

### 10. **meager** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：微薄的；贫乏的；不足的
- **英文释义**：lacking in quantity or quality; inadequate
- **例句**：
  - The refugees survived on meager rations of rice and water.
  - 难民靠微薄的米饭和水维持生存。
  - His meager salary barely covered the monthly rent.
  - 他微薄的薪水勉强够付月租。
- **助记**：meager 联想 meagre（同义拼写）→ 贫乏的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **malign** `高一` v./adj.

- **中文释义**：诽谤；中伤；有害的
- **英文释义**：to speak about someone in a spitefully critical manner; harmful
- **例句**：
  - Competitors maligned the company to steal its clients.
  - 竞争对手中伤这家公司以窃取客户。
  - The malign influence of corruption has plagued the institution for years.
  - 腐败的有害影响多年来一直困扰着该机构。
- **助记**：mal（坏）+ ign → 坏的 → 诽谤/有害

### 12. **notoriety** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：臭名；恶名；声名狼藉
- **英文释义**：the state of being famous for something bad
- **例句**：
  - The hacker gained notoriety after crashing a major bank's website.
  - 黑客在攻破一家大型银行网站后变得臭名昭著。
  - The prison gained notoriety for its harsh treatment of inmates.
  - 这座监狱因虐待囚犯而声名狼藉。
- **助记**：notorious（臭名昭著的）→ notoriety（恶名）

### 13. **placate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：安抚；平息；抚慰
- **英文释义**：to make someone less angry or hostile; to pacify
- **例句**：
  - The manager offered a refund to placate the angry customer.
  - 经理提供退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。
  - His gentle tone was enough to placate the frightened child.
  - 他温柔的语气足以安抚受惊的孩子。
- **助记**：plac（满意/平静）+ ate → 使平静 → 安抚

### 14. **quixotic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不切实际的；空想的；堂吉诃德式的
- **英文释义**：exceedingly idealistic; unrealistic and impractical
- **例句**：
  - His quixotic plan to end world hunger overnight was doomed to fail.
  - 他一夜之间消除世界饥饿的不切实际计划注定失败。
  - Some call her vision quixotic, but she insists change is possible.
  - 有些人称她的愿景不切实际，但她坚信改变是可能的。
- **助记**：来自 Don Quixote（堂吉诃德）→ 像堂吉诃德般空想

### 15. **sanguine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：乐观的；自信的；红润的
- **英文释义**：optimistic or positive, especially in a difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - Despite the challenges, she remained sanguine about the project's future.
  - 尽管面临挑战，她仍对项目的未来保持乐观。
  - Investors are less sanguine about the economy after the latest data.
  - 最新数据公布后投资者对经济不再那么乐观。
- **助记**：sanguin（血）+ e → 血色红润 → 乐观/红润
