## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **claim** /kleɪm/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：声称；索取
- **英文释义**：to say that something is true even though it has not been proved; to ask for something as a right
- **例句**：
  - He claims to be the best student in class.
  - 他声称自己是班上最好的学生。
  - She made a claim for compensation after the accident.
  - 事故后她提出了赔偿要求。
- **助记**：claim → "喊来"（谐音）→ 大声喊出来声称 → 声称

### 2. **combat** /ˈkɒmbæt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：战斗；对抗
- **英文释义**：to try to stop something bad from happening; fighting between armed forces
- **例句**：
  - The government is taking steps to combat crime.
  - 政府正在采取措施打击犯罪。
  - He was killed in combat during the war.
  - 他在战争中阵亡了。
- **助记**：com-（共同）+ bat（打）→ 一起打 → 战斗

### 3. **command** /kəˈmɑːnd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：命令；指挥
- **英文释义**：to give an order; to be in charge of a group of people
- **例句**：
  - The officer commanded his soldiers to advance.
  - 军官命令士兵前进。
  - She has a good command of three languages.
  - 她精通三种语言。
- **助记**：com-（加强）+ mand（命令）→ 加强命令 → 指挥

### 4. **comment** /ˈkɒment/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：评论；发表意见
- **英文释义**：a spoken or written remark expressing an opinion
- **例句**：
  - Do you have any comments on my essay?
  - 你对我的文章有什么评论吗？
  - She refused to comment on the rumours.
  - 她拒绝对谣言发表评论。
- **助记**：com-（一起）+ ment（思考）→ 一起思考后发言 → 评论

### 5. **commit** /kəˈmɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：犯（罪）；承诺
- **英文释义**：to do something wrong or illegal; to promise to do something
- **例句**：
  - He committed a serious crime and was sent to prison.
  - 他犯了严重的罪行被送进监狱。
  - She committed herself to finishing the project on time.
  - 她承诺按时完成项目。
- **助记**：com-（加强）+ mit（送）→ 把自己送出去 → 承诺；犯罪

### 6. **communicate** /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：交流；传达
- **英文释义**：to share information or ideas with someone
- **例句**：
  - It's important to communicate clearly with your team.
  - 与团队清晰沟通很重要。
  - She communicated her ideas through a presentation.
  - 她通过演示传达了自己的想法。
- **助记**：commun-（共同）+ -icate → 使共同拥有 → 交流

### 7. **companion** /kəmˈpæniən/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：同伴；伙伴
- **英文释义**：a person you spend time with or travel with
- **例句**：
  - The dog has been her loyal companion for ten years.
  - 这只狗陪伴了她十年。
  - He is a good travel companion.
  - 他是一个好的旅行伙伴。
- **助记**：com-（一起）+ pan（面包）+ -ion → 一起分享面包的人 → 同伴

### 8. **competent** /ˈkɒmpɪtənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有能力的；胜任的
- **英文释义**：having the necessary ability or skills to do something well
- **例句**：
  - She is a competent doctor who handles emergencies well.
  - 她是一位能干的医生，处理紧急情况得心应手。
  - You need to be competent in English to apply for this job.
  - 申请这份工作需要英语能力过关。
- **助记**：com-（一起）+ pet（追求）+ -ent → 一起竞争中胜出 → 有能力的

### 9. **complicated** /ˈkɒmplɪkeɪtɪd/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：复杂的；难懂的
- **英文释义**：consisting of many different parts or aspects; difficult to understand
- **例句**：
  - The instructions are too complicated for me to follow.
  - 这些说明太复杂，我看不懂。
  - The situation became more complicated than expected.
  - 情况变得比预想的更复杂。
- **助记**：com-（一起）+ plic（折叠）+ -ated → 折叠在一起的 → 复杂的

### 10. **concept** /ˈkɒnsept/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：概念；观念
- **英文释义**：an abstract idea or general notion
- **例句**：
  - The concept of freedom means different things to different people.
  - 自由的概念对不同的人有不同的含义。
  - This is a difficult concept for young children to understand.
  - 这对幼儿来说是一个很难理解的概念。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ cept（抓住）→ 把想法抓在一起 → 概念

### 高一词汇

### 11. **debilitate** /dɪˈbɪlɪteɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使虚弱；使衰弱
- **英文释义**：to make someone or something physically weak
- **例句**：
  - The long illness debilitated him greatly.
  - 长期的疾病使他非常虚弱。
  - Lack of funding has debilitated the research programme.
  - 资金不足削弱了研究项目。
- **助记**：de-（向下）+ bil（力量）+ -itate → 力量下降 → 使虚弱

### 12. **deference** /ˈdefərəns/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：敬重；顺从
- **英文释义**：polite respect shown to someone because of their position or age
- **例句**：
  - Students show deference to their teachers in many Asian cultures.
  - 在许多亚洲文化中，学生对老师表示敬重。
  - In deference to his wishes, we changed the plan.
  - 出于对他意愿的尊重，我们改变了计划。
- **助记**：defer（顺从）+ -ence → 顺从的态度 → 敬重

### 13. **denounce** /dɪˈnaʊns/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：谴责；公开指责
- **英文释义**：to publicly declare something to be wrong or evil
- **例句**：
  - The president denounced the terrorist attack.
  - 总统谴责了恐怖袭击。
  - She denounced the company's unfair practices.
  - 她公开指责了公司的不公平做法。
- **助记**：de-（向下）+ nounce（宣布）→ 向下宣布批判 → 谴责

### 14. **desolate** /ˈdesələt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：荒凉的；凄惨的
- **英文释义**：empty and without people; feeling very sad and lonely
- **例句**：
  - The abandoned village was desolate and eerie.
  - 被遗弃的村庄荒凉而诡异。
  - She felt desolate after her best friend moved away.
  - 好朋友搬走后她感到非常凄凉。
- **助记**：de-（完全）+ sol（独自）+ -ate → 完全独自一人的 → 荒凉的

### 15. **deter** /dɪˈtɜː(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：阻止；威慑
- **英文释义**：to prevent someone from doing something by making them afraid of the consequences
- **例句**：
  - The high price did not deter customers from buying the product.
  - 高价格没有阻止顾客购买这个产品。
  - Security cameras deter criminals from breaking in.
  - 监控摄像头威慑犯罪分子使其不敢闯入。
- **助记**：de-（离开）+ ter（吓）→ 吓得离开 → 阻止
