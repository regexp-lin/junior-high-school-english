## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **relinquish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：放弃；交出；让出
- **英文释义**：to voluntarily give up or cease to hold
- **例句**：
  - The king was forced to relinquish his throne after the revolution.
  - 国王在革命后被迫放弃了王位。
  - She reluctantly relinquished control of the company to her successor.
  - 她不情愿地将公司的控制权交给了继任者。
- **助记**：re（回）+ linqu（离开）+ ish → 离开并放回去 → 放弃

### 2. **formidable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：令人敬畏的；可怕的；强大的
- **英文释义**：inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, or capable
- **例句**：
  - The team faces a formidable opponent in the championship finals.
  - 团队在锦标赛决赛中面对一个强大的对手。
  - Learning Mandarin can be a formidable challenge for English speakers.
  - 学习普通话对英语使用者来说可能是一项艰巨的挑战。
- **助记**：formid（恐惧）+ able → 令人恐惧的 → 令人敬畏的

### 3. **impede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to delay or prevent by obstructing; to hinder
- **例句**：
  - The heavy traffic impeded our progress, making us late for the meeting.
  - 拥堵的交通阻碍了我们的行程，使我们开会迟到。
  - Lack of funding continues to impede scientific research in many developing countries.
  - 资金不足继续阻碍着许多发展中国家的科学研究。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ pede（脚）→ 脚被绊住 → 阻碍

### 4. **prevalent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：盛行的；普遍的；流行的
- **英文释义**：widespread in a particular area at a particular time
- **例句**：
  - Cyberbullying has become increasingly prevalent among teenagers.
  - 网络欺凌在青少年中变得越来越普遍。
  - The disease is most prevalent in tropical regions with poor sanitation.
  - 这种疾病在卫生条件差的热带地区最为流行。
- **助记**：pre（前面）+ val（强大）+ ent → 力量在前面蔓延 → 盛行的

### 5. **mollify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：安抚；缓和；使平息
- **英文释义**：to appease the anger or anxiety of someone
- **例句**：
  - The company offered a discount to mollify the disgruntled customers.
  - 公司提供折扣以安抚不满的客户。
  - His sincere apology managed to mollify her anger somewhat.
  - 他真诚的道歉在一定程度上平息了她的愤怒。
- **助记**：moll（软）+ ify → 使变软 → 安抚

### 6. **clandestine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：秘密的；暗中的；偷偷摸摸的
- **英文释义**：kept secret or done secretively, especially because illicit
- **例句**：
  - The spies conducted clandestine meetings in abandoned warehouses.
  - 间谍们在废弃的仓库里进行秘密会面。
  - The clandestine operation was carried out under the cover of darkness.
  - 秘密行动在夜幕的掩护下进行。
- **助记**：cland（秘密）+ estine → 秘密的

### 7. **reconcile** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：和解；调和；使一致
- **英文释义**：to restore friendly relations; to make compatible
- **例句**：
  - After years of conflict, the two brothers finally reconciled.
  - 经过多年的冲突，两兄弟终于和解了。
  - It's difficult to reconcile his public statements with his private actions.
  - 很难使他的公开声明与私下行为保持一致。
- **助记**：re（再）+ concile（召集）→ 再次召集在一起 → 和解

### 8. **amenable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：顺从的；愿意接受的；可改变的
- **英文释义**：open and responsive to suggestion; easily persuaded
- **例句**：
  - The manager was amenable to the team's request for flexible working hours.
  - 经理愿意接受团队关于弹性工作时间的请求。
  - Young children are often more amenable to learning new languages.
  - 幼儿通常更容易接受学习新语言。
- **助记**：a + men（引导）+ able → 能被引导的 → 顺从的

### 9. **calibrate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：校准；校正；调整
- **英文释义**：to mark or adjust an instrument or technique for accuracy
- **例句**：
  - The technician calibrated the equipment to ensure accurate measurements.
  - 技术人员校准了设备以确保测量准确。
  - Teachers should calibrate their expectations based on each student's ability.
  - 教师应该根据每个学生的能力调整期望。
- **助记**：calibr（口径/刻度）+ ate → 调整刻度 → 校准

### 10. **indelible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：难以磨灭的；永久的；不可擦除的
- **英文释义**：making marks that cannot be removed; not able to be forgotten
- **例句**：
  - The experience left an indelible impression on her memory.
  - 这次经历在她的记忆中留下了难以磨灭的印象。
  - He signed the contract with indelible ink to prevent any alterations.
  - 他用不可擦除的墨水签署了合同以防止任何篡改。
- **助记**：in（不）+ del（删除）+ ible → 不能删除的 → 难以磨灭的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **sybarite** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：享乐主义者；纵情声色的人
- **英文释义**：a person who is self-indulgent in their fondness for sensuous luxury
- **例句**：
  - The Roman emperor was known as a sybarite who indulged in lavish banquets and entertainment.
  - 这位罗马皇帝以享乐主义者著称，沉溺于奢华的宴会和娱乐中。
  - She accused him of being a sybarite, spending all his money on fine dining and luxury travel.
  - 她指责他是享乐主义者，把所有钱都花在美食和奢华旅行上。
- **助记**：来自古希腊城市 Sybaris，以居民的奢侈生活闻名 → 享乐主义者

### 12. **draconian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：严酷的；苛刻的；严厉的
- **英文释义**：excessively harsh and severe
- **例句**：
  - The draconian laws imposed severe penalties for even minor offenses.
  - 严酷的法律对即便是轻微的违法行为也施以严厉处罚。
  - Critics described the new regulations as draconian and unnecessarily restrictive.
  - 批评者将新法规描述为严苛且不必要地限制性的。
- **助记**：来自古希腊立法者 Draco（德拉古），以严刑峻法著称 → 严酷的

### 13. **limerence** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：痴迷的爱恋；迷恋状态
- **英文释义**：the state of being infatuated with another person, typically experienced involuntarily
- **例句**：
  - The teenager's limerence for her classmate consumed her every thought.
  - 这个少女对同学的痴迷占据了她的所有思绪。
  - Psychologists distinguish limerence from mature love by its obsessive and involuntary nature.
  - 心理学家通过其痴迷和非自愿的本质将迷恋状态与成熟的爱区分开来。
- **助记**：由心理学家 Dorothy Tennov 于1979年创造的新词 → 迷恋状态

### 14. **perspicuous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：表达清晰的；明了的；简明易懂的
- **英文释义**：clearly expressed and easily understood; lucid
- **例句**：
  - The professor's perspicuous writing style made complex theories accessible to all students.
  - 教授清晰明了的写作风格使所有学生都能理解复杂的理论。
  - A good legal document should be perspicuous, leaving no room for misinterpretation.
  - 一份好的法律文件应该简明易懂，不留任何误读的余地。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ spic（看）+ uous → 能看穿的 → 清晰的（注意区分 perspicacious 洞察力强的）

### 15. **eleemosynary** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：慈善的；施舍的；靠救济的
- **英文释义**：of or relating to charity; charitable
- **例句**：
  - The eleemosynary foundation provides scholarships to students from low-income families.
  - 这个慈善基金会为来自低收入家庭的学生提供奖学金。
  - His eleemosynary efforts have helped thousands of people access clean drinking water.
  - 他的慈善努力帮助数千人获得了清洁饮用水。
- **助记**：eleemosyn（怜悯/施舍）+ ary → 与施舍有关的 → 慈善的
