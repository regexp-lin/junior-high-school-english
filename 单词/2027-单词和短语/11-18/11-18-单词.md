## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **admonish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：告诫；劝告；警告
- **英文释义**：to warn or reprimand someone firmly; to advise earnestly
- **例句**：
  - The teacher admonished the students for talking during the exam.
  - 老师警告了在考试期间说话的学生。
  - His mother admonished him to drive carefully in the heavy rain.
  - 他母亲告诫他在大雨中小心驾驶。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ mon（警告）+ ish → 向某人发出警告 → 告诫

### 2. **transient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：短暂的；临时的；过渡的
- **英文释义**：lasting only for a short time; not permanent
- **例句**：
  - The transient nature of youth reminds us to cherish every moment.
  - 青春的短暂本质提醒我们珍惜每一刻。
  - The hotel caters to transient guests who stay for only one or two nights.
  - 这家酒店专门接待只住一两晚的临时客人。
- **助记**：trans（穿过）+ ient → 穿过就走 → 短暂的

### 3. **usurp** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：篡夺；侵占；霸占
- **英文释义**：to take a position of power illegally or by force
- **例句**：
  - The general usurped the throne through a military coup.
  - 将军通过军事政变篡夺了王位。
  - She felt that the new manager was trying to usurp her authority in the department.
  - 她觉得新经理试图侵占她在部门的权力。
- **助记**：u + surp（抓住）→ 非法抓住权力 → 篡夺

### 4. **meticulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；细致入微的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - The surgeon was meticulous in her preparation for the operation.
  - 外科医生在手术准备上一丝不苟。
  - His meticulous record-keeping made it easy to trace every transaction.
  - 他细致入微的记录使追踪每笔交易变得容易。
- **助记**：met（恐惧）+ iculous → 怕出错 → 一丝不苟的

### 5. **exacerbate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加剧；使恶化；使更严重
- **英文释义**：to make a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling worse
- **例句**：
  - The heavy rain exacerbated the flooding in the low-lying areas.
  - 大雨加剧了低洼地区的洪水。
  - His angry response only exacerbated the tension between the two groups.
  - 他愤怒的回应只是加剧了两个团体之间的紧张关系。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ acerb（尖锐/苦）+ ate → 使更加尖锐 → 加剧

### 6. **penchant** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：偏好；嗜好；强烈爱好
- **英文释义**：a strong or habitual liking for something
- **例句**：
  - She has a penchant for collecting vintage books from the 19th century.
  - 她有收藏19世纪古董书的嗜好。
  - His penchant for adventure led him to travel to remote corners of the world.
  - 他对冒险的偏好使他去了世界偏远的角落。
- **助记**：法语借词，pench（倾斜）+ ant → 倾向于 → 偏好

### 7. **acquiesce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：默许；默认；顺从
- **英文释义**：to accept something reluctantly but without protest
- **例句**：
  - The board of directors eventually acquiesced to the shareholders' demands.
  - 董事会最终默许了股东的要求。
  - She acquiesced to the plan, though she had serious reservations about it.
  - 她默许了该计划，尽管她对此有严重保留意见。
- **助记**：ac（向）+ quiesce（安静）→ 安静地接受 → 默许

### 8. **verbose** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：冗长的；啰嗦的；赘述的
- **英文释义**：using or expressed in more words than are needed
- **例句**：
  - The report was so verbose that most readers lost interest halfway through.
  - 报告太冗长了，大多数读者中途就失去了兴趣。
  - A good writer avoids verbose sentences and aims for clarity.
  - 好作家避免冗长的句子，追求简洁明了。
- **助记**：verb（词）+ ose → 词太多 → 冗长的

### 9. **disseminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：传播；散布；普及
- **英文释义**：to spread or disperse something widely, especially information
- **例句**：
  - The organization disseminates health information to communities in rural areas.
  - 该组织向农村社区传播健康信息。
  - Social media platforms have made it easier to disseminate news rapidly.
  - 社交媒体平台使快速传播新闻变得更容易。
- **助记**：dis（分散）+ semin（种子）+ ate → 撒播种子 → 传播

### 10. **ostentatious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：炫耀的；铺张的；招摇的
- **英文释义**：designed to impress or attract notice; showy
- **例句**：
  - The billionaire's ostentatious display of wealth drew criticism from the public.
  - 亿万富翁炫耀财富的行为招致了公众的批评。
  - She preferred a simple lifestyle and disliked ostentatious behavior.
  - 她喜欢简单的生活方式，不喜欢招摇的行为。
- **助记**：ostent（展示）+ atious → 过度展示 → 炫耀的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **sesquipedalian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：（词语）冗长的；好用长词的
- **英文释义**：characterized by long words; fond of using long words
- **例句**：
  - His sesquipedalian writing style made the academic paper difficult to understand.
  - 他好用长词的写作风格使这篇学术论文难以理解。
  - The professor's sesquipedalian vocabulary impressed some students but confused others.
  - 教授喜用冗长词汇的习惯让一些学生印象深刻，却让另一些人困惑。
- **助记**：sesqui（一个半）+ ped（脚）+ alian → 一个半脚长的 → 冗长的

### 12. **internecine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：内部互相残杀的；两败俱伤的
- **英文释义**：destructive to both sides in a conflict; relating to conflict within a group
- **例句**：
  - The internecine rivalry between the two factions weakened the entire organization.
  - 两派之间的内部互斗削弱了整个组织。
  - The civil war was an internecine conflict that devastated the country for decades.
  - 内战是一场两败俱伤的冲突，使国家遭受了数十年的破坏。
- **助记**：inter（相互）+ nec（杀死）+ ine → 互相残杀的

### 13. **numinous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：超自然的；神圣的；令人敬畏的
- **英文释义**：having a strong religious or spiritual quality; suggesting the presence of a divinity
- **例句**：
  - Standing in the ancient cathedral, she felt a numinous presence surrounding her.
  - 站在古老的大教堂里，她感到一种神圣的存在围绕着她。
  - The numinous beauty of the mountain peak at sunrise left the hikers speechless.
  - 日出时山顶那令人敬畏的美景让徒步旅行者们哑口无言。
- **助记**：numin（神灵）+ ous → 有神灵的 → 神圣的

### 14. **legerdemain** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：戏法；手法；骗术
- **英文释义**：skillful use of one's hands when performing conjuring tricks; trickery or deception
- **例句**：
  - The magician's legerdemain amazed the audience as he made cards disappear.
  - 魔术师的戏法让观众惊叹不已，他让纸牌消失了。
  - The financial legerdemain of the corporation was eventually exposed by auditors.
  - 公司的财务骗术最终被审计人员揭露。
- **助记**：法语借词，léger（轻）+ de（的）+ main（手）→ 手法轻巧 → 戏法

### 15. **apotheosis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：典范；巅峰；神化
- **英文释义**：the highest point in the development of something; elevation to divine status
- **例句**：
  - Many consider this symphony to be the apotheosis of classical music.
  - 许多人认为这部交响曲是古典音乐的巅峰。
  - The Roman practice of apotheosis turned deceased emperors into gods.
  - 罗马的神化习俗将已故皇帝变为神灵。
- **助记**：apo（成为）+ theo（神）+ sis → 成为神 → 神化/巅峰
