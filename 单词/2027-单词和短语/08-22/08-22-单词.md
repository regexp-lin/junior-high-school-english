## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **prudent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：谨慎的；慎重的；精明的
- **英文释义**：acting with or showing care and thought for the future; sensible and careful
- **例句**：
  - It is prudent to save some money each month for unexpected expenses.
  - 每月存些钱以备不时之需是明智的。
  - The company made a prudent decision to delay the launch until the product was fully tested.
  - 公司做出了审慎的决定，在产品充分测试后再推出。
- **助记**：prud（谨慎，如 prudence）+ ent → 具有谨慎品质的 → 谨慎的

### 2. **volatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易变的；不稳定的；（液体）易挥发的
- **英文释义**：liable to change rapidly and unpredictably, especially for the worse; easily evaporated
- **例句**：
  - The stock market has been volatile this week, with prices swinging wildly.
  - 本周股市波动剧烈，价格大起大落。
  - Store volatile chemicals in a cool, well-ventilated place.
  - 将易挥发的化学品存放在阴凉、通风良好的地方。
- **助记**：volat（飞，如 volitation）+ ile → 容易“飞走”挥发或情绪飞变 → 易变的/挥发的

### 3. **tenacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽强的；固执的；附着力强的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent and determined
- **例句**：
  - She is tenacious in pursuing her goals despite repeated setbacks.
  - 尽管屡遭挫折，她仍顽强地追求自己的目标。
  - The ivy has a tenacious grip on the old stone wall.
  - 常春藤牢牢地攀附在古老的石墙上。
- **助记**：ten（握住）+ acious → 紧紧握住不放 → 顽强的

### 4. **benign** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：良性的；温和的；无害的
- **英文释义**：gentle and kind; not harmful or malignant
- **例句**：
  - The doctor said the tumor was benign and could be removed safely.
  - 医生说肿瘤是良性的，可以安全切除。
  - She spoke in a benign tone that put everyone at ease.
  - 她用温和的语气说话，让大家都放松下来。
- **助记**：ben（好，如 benefit）+ ign → 性质好的 → 良性的/温和的

### 5. **austere** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：简朴的；严厉的；苦行的
- **英文释义**：severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance; plain and without decoration
- **例句**：
  - The monks lived an austere life with few possessions.
  - 僧侣们过着简朴的生活，几乎没有财物。
  - His austere expression made it clear he would not tolerate excuses.
  - 他严厉的表情表明他不会容忍任何借口。
- **助记**：联想 austere 与 stern（严厉的）→ 生活或态度严峻简朴 → 简朴的/严厉的

### 6. **obsolete** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过时的；废弃的；已淘汰的
- **英文释义**：no longer produced or used; out of date
- **例句**：
  - Floppy disks are practically obsolete in the age of cloud storage.
  - 在云存储时代，软盘几乎已经淘汰。
  - The factory still uses obsolete equipment that breaks down frequently.
  - 这家工厂仍在使用经常出故障的过时设备。
- **助记**：ob（对抗）+ solete（使用）→ 不再被使用 → 过时的

### 7. **latent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：潜在的；潜伏的；隐藏的
- **英文释义**：existing but not yet developed, visible, or active; hidden
- **例句**：
  - The disease can remain latent for years before symptoms appear.
  - 这种疾病可能在症状出现前潜伏多年。
  - He has a latent talent for music that only recently emerged.
  - 他有潜在的音乐天赋，直到最近才显现出来。
- **助记**：lat（躺、藏）+ ent → 还躺着没冒出来 → 潜在的

### 8. **tacit** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：心照不宣的；默示的；未明说的
- **英文释义**：understood or implied without being stated openly
- **例句**：
  - They reached a tacit agreement not to discuss the matter in public.
  - 他们达成了心照不宣的协议，不在公开场合讨论此事。
  - His silence was taken as tacit approval of the plan.
  - 他的沉默被视为对计划的默许。
- **助记**：tac（安静，如 taciturn）+ it → 安静不说但彼此明白 → 默示的

### 9. **sparse** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：稀疏的；稀少的；简陋的
- **英文释义**：thinly dispersed or scattered; not dense or crowded
- **例句**：
  - Vegetation is sparse in the desert region.
  - 沙漠地区植被稀疏。
  - The audience was sparse on a rainy Tuesday evening.
  - 一个下雨的周二晚上，观众寥寥无几。
- **助记**：sparse 联想 sparse hair（稀疏的头发）→ 稀少的

### 10. **ardent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：热烈的；热情的；充满激情的
- **英文释义**：enthusiastic or passionate, especially in support of something
- **例句**：
  - She is an ardent supporter of environmental protection.
  - 她是环境保护的热心支持者。
  - The team received ardent cheers from the home crowd.
  - 主队观众向球队发出了热烈的欢呼。
- **助记**：ard（燃烧，如 ardor）+ ent → 内心燃烧的 → 热情的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **absolve** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：免除（责任）；赦免；宣告无罪
- **英文释义**：to declare someone free from guilt, obligation, or punishment
- **例句**：
  - The jury absolved the defendant of all charges.
  - 陪审团宣告被告所有指控均不成立。
  - Nothing could absolve him of his moral responsibility for the accident.
  - 什么也无法免除他对这起事故的道义责任。
- **助记**：ab（离开）+ solve（松开）→ 从责任中松开 → 免除

### 12. **reproach** `高一` n./v.

- **中文释义**：责备；指责；羞耻
- **英文释义**：to express disapproval or criticism of someone; blame
- **例句**：
  - She reproached him for forgetting their anniversary.
  - 她责备他忘记了他们的纪念日。
  - His conduct is beyond reproach — everyone trusts him completely.
  - 他的品行无可指摘——大家都完全信任他。
- **助记**：re（回）+ proach（接近）→ 带着不满再次接近对方 → 责备

### 13. **succinct** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：简洁的；简练的；言简意赅的
- **英文释义**：expressed clearly and precisely in few words; brief and clear
- **例句**：
  - Please give a succinct summary of the report in one paragraph.
  - 请用一段话对报告作简明扼要的总结。
  - Her email was succinct but covered all the key points.
  - 她的邮件很简短，但涵盖了所有要点。
- **助记**：suc（下面）+ cinct（捆）→ 捆紧在下面，不松散 → 简练的

### 14. **tangible** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有形的；可触摸的；切实的
- **英文释义**：perceptible by touch; clear and definite; real
- **例句**：
  - After months of work, they finally had tangible results to show.
  - 经过数月努力，他们终于有了可展示的切实成果。
  - The benefits of the new policy are not always tangible at first.
  - 新政策的好处起初并不总是显而易见的。
- **助记**：tang（接触，如 tangent）+ ible → 能接触到的 → 有形的

### 15. **mitigate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和；使缓和
- **英文释义**：to make something less severe, serious, or painful
- **例句**：
  - Trees can help mitigate the effects of urban heat islands.
  - 树木有助于减轻城市热岛效应。
  - The lawyer argued that his client's cooperation should mitigate the sentence.
  - 律师辩称当事人的配合应能减轻刑罚。
- **助记**：miti（温和，如 mild）+ gate → 使变得温和 → 缓和
