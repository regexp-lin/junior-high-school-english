## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **appease** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：安抚；平息；姑息
- **英文释义**：to pacify or placate someone by acceding to their demands
- **例句**：
  - The government tried to appease angry farmers with new subsidies.
  - 政府试图用新补贴来安抚愤怒的农民。
  - Giving in to bullies only appeases them temporarily.
  - 向霸凌者屈服只是暂时安抚了他们。
- **助记**：ap（向）+ pease（和平）→ 使和平 → 安抚

### 2. **blunder** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：大错；愚蠢的错误；犯大错
- **英文释义**：a stupid or careless mistake; to make such a mistake
- **例句**：
  - Sending the email to the wrong person was a costly blunder.
  - 把邮件发错人是一个代价高昂的大错。
  - The goalkeeper blundered and let an easy shot through.
  - 守门员犯了低级失误，让一个简单射门进了球。
- **助记**：blunder 联想 blind（盲目）→ 盲目行事 → 犯大错

### 3. **coerce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：强迫；胁迫；威逼
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone to do something by using force or threats
- **例句**：
  - No one should be coerced into signing a contract they haven't read.
  - 不应强迫任何人签署未阅读的合同。
  - The witness was coerced into changing her testimony.
  - 证人被胁迫更改了证词。
- **助记**：co（一起）+ erce（围/关）→ 关起来逼迫 → 强迫

### 4. **diminish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减少；缩小；贬低
- **英文释义**：to make or become less; to reduce in size, importance, or value
- **例句**：
  - Nothing can diminish his contribution to the science of physics.
  - 没有什么能贬低他对物理学的贡献。
  - The sound of thunder diminished as the storm moved away.
  - 雷声随着暴风雨远去而减弱。
- **助记**：di（分开）+ min（小）+ ish → 变小 → 减少

### 5. **envision** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：设想；展望；想象
- **英文释义**：to imagine as a future possibility; to visualize
- **例句**：
  - She envisioned a world where every child could go to school.
  - 她设想了一个每个孩子都能上学的世界。
  - Engineers envision flying taxis becoming common by 2040.
  - 工程师们设想到2040年飞行出租车将变得普遍。
- **助记**：en（使）+ vision（视野）→ 使看到 → 设想

### 6. **futile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：徒劳的；无用的；无意义的
- **英文释义**：incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
- **例句**：
  - It is futile to argue with someone who refuses to listen.
  - 跟拒绝倾听的人争论是徒劳的。
  - Rescue efforts proved futile after the building collapsed.
  - 建筑倒塌后救援行动证明是无效的。
- **助记**：fut（倒）+ ile → 倒掉的 → 徒劳的

### 7. **grudge** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：怨恨；积怨；不情愿给予
- **英文释义**：a persistent feeling of resentment; to be reluctant to give
- **例句**：
  - He still holds a grudge against the classmate who betrayed him.
  - 他仍对背叛他的同学怀恨在心。
  - She doesn't grudge spending money on her children's education.
  - 她不吝于花钱在子女教育上。
- **助记**：grudge 联想 grumble（抱怨）→ 心中抱怨 → 怨恨

### 8. **hamper** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：妨碍；阻碍；束缚
- **英文释义**：to hinder or impede the movement or progress of
- **例句**：
  - Lack of funding hampered the research project for months.
  - 资金不足使研究项目停滞了数月。
  - Heavy rain hampered rescue operations in the mountain area.
  - 大雨妨碍了山区的救援行动。
- **助记**：hamper 联想 ham（火腿）→ 拖着大火腿走 → 阻碍

### 9. **infringe** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：侵犯；违反；侵害
- **英文释义**：to actively break the terms of a law or agreement; to encroach on rights
- **例句**：
  - Copying software without permission infringes copyright law.
  - 未经许可复制软件侵犯了版权法。
  - The new regulation may infringe on citizens' freedom of speech.
  - 新法规可能侵犯公民的言论自由。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ fringe（边界）→ 闯入边界 → 侵犯

### 10. **kindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：点燃；激发；激起
- **英文释义**：to light a fire; to arouse or inspire an emotion or feeling
- **例句**：
  - A single spark can kindle a great fire.
  - 星星之火可以燎原。
  - The teacher's words kindled a passion for science in her students.
  - 老师的话在学生心中激发了对科学的热情。
- **助记**：kindle → 点燃（Kindle 电子书：点亮知识之光）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **lethargic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：昏睡的；没精打采的；无活力的
- **英文释义**：affected by lethargy; sluggish and apathetic
- **例句**：
  - Students often feel lethargic after a heavy lunch.
  - 学生们在吃过丰盛午餐后常感到昏昏欲睡。
  - The lethargic economy showed few signs of recovery.
  - 萎靡不振的经济几乎没有复苏迹象。
- **助记**：lethargy（昏睡）+ ic → 昏睡的

### 12. **mercurial** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：善变的；反复无常的；活泼的
- **英文释义**：subject to sudden or unpredictable changes of mood or mind
- **例句**：
  - The mercurial weather made it hard to plan outdoor activities.
  - 反复无常的天气让户外活动很难安排。
  - Artists are sometimes known for their mercurial temperament.
  - 艺术家有时以善变的性情闻名。
- **助记**：Mercury（水星/墨丘利）→ 像水银般变化不定 → 善变

### 13. **nonchalant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：漫不经心的；冷淡的；不在乎的
- **英文释义**：feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety
- **例句**：
  - He gave a nonchalant shrug when asked about the exam results.
  - 被问到考试成绩时他漫不经心地耸了耸肩。
  - Her nonchalant attitude hid the worry she truly felt.
  - 她漫不经心的态度掩藏了内心真正的担忧。
- **助记**：non（不）+ chalant（关心）→ 不关心 → 漫不经心

### 14. **opulent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：富裕的；奢华的；丰富的
- **英文释义**：ostentatiously rich and luxurious; lavish
- **例句**：
  - The opulent ballroom was decorated with crystal chandeliers and gold trim.
  - 奢华的舞厅装饰着水晶吊灯和金色装饰。
  - The region's opulent natural resources attracted many settlers.
  - 该地区丰富的自然资源吸引了许多定居者。
- **助记**：opul（财富）+ ent → 有财富的 → 富裕/奢华

### 15. **perfunctory** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：敷衍的；马虎的；应付了事的
- **英文释义**：carried out with a minimum of effort or reflection; cursory
- **例句**：
  - The inspector gave only a perfunctory glance at the safety equipment.
  - 检查员只对安全设备敷衍地扫了一眼。
  - A perfunctory apology is worse than no apology at all.
  - 敷衍的道歉还不如不道歉。
- **助记**：per（彻底）+ funct（完成）+ ory → 只求完成 → 敷衍
