## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **resemble** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：像；类似于
- **英文释义**：to look like or be similar to someone or something
- **例句**：
  - The daughter closely resembles her mother in both appearance and personality.
  - 女儿在外貌和性格上都很像她母亲。
  - This new building resembles an ancient Greek temple.
  - 这座新建筑看起来像一座古希腊神庙。
- **助记**：re（再）+ semble（相似）→ 再次相似 → 像

### 2. **notorious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：臭名昭著的；声名狼藉的
- **英文释义**：famous or well known, typically for some bad quality or deed
- **例句**：
  - The city is notorious for its heavy traffic congestion.
  - 这座城市因交通严重拥堵而臭名昭著。
  - He became notorious after the scandal was exposed in the media.
  - 丑闻被媒体曝光后，他变得声名狼藉。
- **助记**：not（知道）+ orious → 因坏事被人知道 → 臭名昭著的

### 3. **accumulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：积累；积聚；堆积
- **英文释义**：to gather together or acquire an increasing amount of
- **例句**：
  - Over the years, she accumulated a vast collection of rare books.
  - 多年来，她积累了大量珍贵书籍。
  - Dust had accumulated on the shelves during the long vacation.
  - 长假期间架子上积了灰尘。
- **助记**：ac（加强）+ cumul（堆）+ ate → 不断堆积 → 积累

### 4. **vulnerable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：脆弱的；易受伤害的；易受攻击的
- **英文释义**：susceptible to physical or emotional attack or harm
- **例句**：
  - Young children are especially vulnerable to infections during the winter months.
  - 幼儿在冬季特别容易受感染。
  - The coastal towns are vulnerable to flooding during typhoon season.
  - 沿海城镇在台风季节容易遭受洪水侵袭。
- **助记**：vulner（伤）+ able → 容易受伤的 → 脆弱的

### 5. **interfere** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：干涉；妨碍；干扰
- **英文释义**：to prevent from continuing or being carried out; to meddle
- **例句**：
  - Parents should avoid interfering too much in their children's personal lives.
  - 父母应避免过多干涉孩子的个人生活。
  - The bad weather interfered with the satellite signal.
  - 恶劣天气干扰了卫星信号。
- **助记**：inter（之间）+ fere（打击）→ 在中间打击 → 干涉

### 6. **advocate** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：提倡；主张；倡导者
- **英文释义**：to publicly recommend or support; a person who supports a cause
- **例句**：
  - Many doctors advocate regular exercise as a way to prevent heart disease.
  - 许多医生提倡定期锻炼来预防心脏病。
  - She is a strong advocate for gender equality in the workplace.
  - 她是职场性别平等的坚定倡导者。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ voc（声音）+ ate → 向别人发声 → 倡导

### 7. **speculate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：推测；猜测；投机
- **英文释义**：to form a theory or conjecture without firm evidence; to invest in risky ventures
- **例句**：
  - Experts speculate that the economy will recover by the end of the year.
  - 专家推测经济将在年底前复苏。
  - He lost a fortune speculating on the stock market.
  - 他在股市投机中损失了一大笔钱。
- **助记**：spec（看）+ ulate → 看着猜测 → 推测

### 8. **premature** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过早的；不成熟的；早产的
- **英文释义**：occurring or done before the proper time; too early
- **例句**：
  - It would be premature to draw conclusions before all the data is collected.
  - 在收集所有数据之前下结论为时过早。
  - The baby was born premature and needed special medical care.
  - 婴儿早产，需要特殊的医疗照护。
- **助记**：pre（提前）+ mature（成熟）→ 提前成熟 → 过早的

### 9. **illuminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：照亮；阐明；启发
- **英文释义**：to light up; to make something clearer or easier to understand
- **例句**：
  - Colorful lanterns illuminated the streets during the festival.
  - 节日期间彩色灯笼照亮了街道。
  - The professor's explanation illuminated a difficult concept for the students.
  - 教授的解释为学生们阐明了一个难懂的概念。
- **助记**：il（进入）+ lumin（光）+ ate → 使光进入 → 照亮

### 10. **benevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；善良的；慈善的
- **英文释义**：well-meaning and kindly; generous in giving to others
- **例句**：
  - The benevolent old woman donated all her savings to charity.
  - 那位仁慈的老妇人把全部积蓄捐给了慈善机构。
  - A benevolent leader puts the welfare of the people first.
  - 一位仁慈的领导者以人民的福祉为先。
- **助记**：bene（好）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 好意的 → 仁慈的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **cacophony** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：刺耳的声音；不和谐的声音；杂音
- **英文释义**：a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds
- **例句**：
  - The cacophony of car horns and construction noise made it impossible to concentrate.
  - 汽车喇叭和施工噪音的刺耳杂音使人无法集中注意力。
  - The orchestra's warm-up created a brief cacophony before the concert began.
  - 管弦乐队的热身在音乐会开始前制造了短暂的不和谐声音。
- **助记**：caco（坏）+ phony（声音）→ 坏的声音 → 刺耳的声音

### 12. **pragmatic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；实用主义的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically rather than following theories
- **例句**：
  - We need a pragmatic approach to solving this problem rather than idealistic theories.
  - 我们需要用务实的方法来解决这个问题，而不是理想化的理论。
  - She is known as a pragmatic leader who focuses on achievable goals.
  - 她以务实的领导者形象闻名，专注于可实现的目标。
- **助记**：pragm（行动/实践）+ atic → 注重行动实践的 → 务实的

### 13. **capitulate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：投降；屈服；认输
- **英文释义**：to cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; to surrender
- **例句**：
  - After a long siege, the defenders finally capitulated to the enemy forces.
  - 经过长时间围攻，守军最终向敌军投降。
  - The company capitulated to public pressure and changed its controversial policy.
  - 公司在公众压力下屈服，改变了其有争议的政策。
- **助记**：capit（头）+ ulate → 低头 → 投降

### 14. **ostentatious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：炫耀的；卖弄的；铺张的
- **英文释义**：designed to impress or attract notice; showy
- **例句**：
  - He drove an ostentatious sports car to show off his wealth.
  - 他开着一辆炫耀的跑车来展示自己的财富。
  - The wedding was an ostentatious affair with hundreds of guests and lavish decorations.
  - 婚礼场面铺张，有数百位宾客和奢华的装饰。
- **助记**：ostent（展示）+ atious → 过度展示 → 炫耀的

### 15. **acumen** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：敏锐；洞察力；精明
- **英文释义**：the ability to make good judgments and quick decisions, typically in a particular domain
- **例句**：
  - Her business acumen helped her build a successful company from nothing.
  - 她的商业敏锐帮助她从零开始建立了一家成功的公司。
  - Political acumen is essential for any leader navigating complex international relations.
  - 对于任何应对复杂国际关系的领导者来说，政治敏锐至关重要。
- **助记**：acu（尖锐）+ men → 头脑尖锐 → 敏锐
