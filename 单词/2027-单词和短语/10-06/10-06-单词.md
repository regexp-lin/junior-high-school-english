## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **merciful** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；宽大的；幸运的
- **英文释义**：showing mercy; lenient; giving relief from suffering
- **例句**：
  - The judge was merciful and reduced the sentence for the first offender.
  - 法官宽大处理，减轻了初犯的刑期。
  - It was merciful that the storm ended before the concert began.
  - 暴风雨在音乐会开始前停了，真是幸运。
- **助记**：mercy（仁慈）+ ful → 充满仁慈

### 2. **nomadic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：游牧的；流浪的；居无定所的
- **英文释义**：living by moving from place to place; wandering
- **例句**：
  - Nomadic tribes follow seasonal grass for their herds.
  - 游牧部族随季节草场迁徙。
  - Digital nomads lead a nomadic lifestyle with laptops and Wi-Fi.
  - 数字游民带着笔记本和 Wi-Fi 过着流动生活。
- **助记**：noma（游牧）+ dic → 游牧的

### 3. **oppress** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：压迫；使烦恼；使压抑
- **英文释义**：to keep in hardship; to weigh heavily on the mind
- **例句**：
  - Tyrants oppress anyone who speaks against them.
  - 暴君压迫一切反对者。
  - Guilt began to oppress him after he lied to his parents.
  - 对父母撒谎后，内疚开始折磨他。
- **助记**：op（对着）+ press（压）→ 压在头上 → 压迫

### 4. **poignant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：痛切的；心酸的；强烈的
- **英文释义**：evoking a keen sense of sadness or regret; deeply moving
- **例句**：
  - The film ends with a poignant scene of reunion after many years.
  - 影片以多年后重逢的心酸场面结尾。
  - She shared a poignant memory of her grandmother's kitchen.
  - 她讲述了关于祖母厨房的难忘回忆。
- **助记**：poign（刺）+ ant → 刺心的 → 痛切

### 5. **quell** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：镇压；平息；消除（恐惧）
- **英文释义**：to put an end to, especially by force; to calm
- **例句**：
  - Police moved in to quell the riot in the city center.
  - 警方出动平息市中心的骚乱。
  - She took deep breaths to quell her anxiety before the speech.
  - 演讲前她深呼吸以平复焦虑。
- **助记**：quell 联想 kill（消除）→ 平息

### 6. **rebuke** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：指责；训斥；谴责
- **英文释义**：to express sharp disapproval or criticism of someone
- **例句**：
  - The coach rebuked the player for showing disrespect to the referee.
  - 教练斥责球员对裁判不敬。
  - His careless remark earned a stern rebuke from the principal.
  - 他轻率言论遭到校长严厉训斥。
- **助记**：re（回）+ buke（打）→ 打回去 → 斥责

### 7. **sparse** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：稀疏的；稀少的；简陋的
- **英文释义**：thinly dispersed or scattered; meager
- **例句**：
  - Vegetation is sparse in the desert region.
  - 沙漠地区植被稀疏。
  - The apartment was sparse, with only a bed and a desk.
  - 公寓很简陋，只有一张床和一张桌子。
- **助记**：sparse 联想 sparse hair → 稀疏

### 8. **tenacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽强的；固执的；附着力强的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold; persistent; not readily giving up
- **例句**：
  - Tenacious climbers reached the summit despite the storm.
  - 顽强的登山者不顾风暴登顶。
  - She has a tenacious memory for historical dates.
  - 她对历史日期记得特别牢。
- **助记**：ten（握住）+ acious → 紧握不放 → 顽强

### 9. **usurp** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：篡夺；夺取；侵占
- **英文释义**：to seize power or position illegally or by force
- **例句**：
  - The general tried to usurp the throne from the young king.
  - 将军企图从幼王手中篡夺王位。
  - Do not usurp your colleague's responsibilities without permission.
  - 未经允许不要越俎代庖承担同事职责。
- **助记**：us（用）+urp（拿）→ 拿走 → 篡夺

### 10. **vehement** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：激烈的；猛烈的；（情感）强烈的
- **英文释义**：showing strong feeling; forceful, passionate, or intense
- **例句**：
  - She gave a vehement defense of free speech in the debate.
  - 她在辩论中激烈捍卫言论自由。
  - There was vehement opposition to the new tax proposal.
  - 新征税方案遭到强烈反对。
- **助记**：veh（携带）+ ement → 带着强烈情绪 → 激烈

### 高一词汇

### 11. **vacillate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：犹豫；摇摆不定；踌躇
- **英文释义**：to waver between different opinions or actions; be indecisive
- **例句**：
  - Leaders who vacillate in a crisis may lose public trust.
  - 危机中摇摆不定的领导者可能失去公众信任。
  - He vacillated between two universities before choosing one.
  - 他在两所大学间犹豫许久才选定。
- **助记**：vacill（摇摆）+ ate → 摇摆

### 12. **whimsical** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：异想天开的；反复无常的；古怪有趣的
- **英文释义**：playfully quaint or fanciful; unpredictably odd
- **例句**：
  - The garden was full of whimsical statues of animals wearing hats.
  - 花园里满是戴着帽子的动物奇趣雕像。
  - Her whimsical ideas sometimes confused practical teammates.
  - 她异想天开的点子有时让务实的队友困惑。
- **助记**：whim（突发奇想）+ sical → 随奇想而动的

### 13. **xenophobia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：仇外；对外国人的恐惧与排斥
- **英文释义**：dislike of or prejudice against people from other countries
- **例句**：
  - Education can help reduce xenophobia among young people.
  - 教育有助于减少年轻人的仇外心理。
  - The politician was accused of stirring xenophobia for votes.
  - 那位政客被指控为拉票煽动仇外情绪。
- **助记**：xeno（外）+ phobia（恐惧）→ 惧外

### 14. **zealot** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：狂热者；极端分子
- **英文释义**：a person who is fanatical and uncompromising in pursuit of beliefs
- **例句**：
  - Religious zealots refused to tolerate different viewpoints.
  - 宗教狂热分子不容忍不同观点。
  - He was a zealot for environmental protection, protesting every weekend.
  - 他是环保狂热者，每周末都去抗议。
- **助记**：zeal（热情）+ ot → 过度热情的人 → 狂热者

### 15. **amalgamate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；混合；联合
- **英文释义**：to combine or unite to form one structure or organization
- **例句**：
  - The two firms amalgamated to compete with larger rivals.
  - 两家公司合并以与更大对手竞争。
  - Different musical styles amalgamate in her compositions.
  - 她的作品融合了多种音乐风格。
- **助记**：a + malgam（汞合金）+ ate → 像汞合金般融合 → 合并
