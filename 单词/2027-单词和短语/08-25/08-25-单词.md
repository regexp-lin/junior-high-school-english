## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **mercurial** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：善变的；活泼难测的；水银般的
- **英文释义**：subject to sudden changes of mood or mind; lively and quick-witted
- **例句**：
  - The mercurial coach switched tactics several times during the game.
  - 这位性情多变的教练在比赛中数次改变战术。
  - Investors dislike mercurial policies that change without warning.
  - 投资者不喜欢毫无征兆就改变的善变政策。
- **助记**：Mercury（水星/墨丘利）→ 如水银流动般难以捉摸 → 善变的

### 2. **nadir** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：最低点；最糟时刻；（天）天底
- **英文释义**：the lowest point; the time of greatest depression or adversity
- **例句**：
  - Losing the final match was the nadir of his athletic career.
  - 输掉决赛是他运动生涯的最低点。
  - Morale reached its nadir after three months without pay.
  - 三个月未发工资后，士气跌至谷底。
- **助记**：与 zenith（天顶、顶点）相对 → 天底即最低点

### 3. **obliterate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：消灭；抹去；覆盖使消失
- **英文释义**：to destroy utterly; to wipe out all traces of
- **例句**：
  - The bombing nearly obliterated the small village.
  - 轰炸几乎摧毁了整个小村庄。
  - Time had obliterated the inscription on the ancient stone.
  - 岁月抹去了古石头上的铭文。
- **助记**：ob（对抗）+ liter（字母、文字）+ ate → 把文字抹掉 → 消灭

### 4. **palliate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻（痛苦）；掩饰（错误）；辩解
- **英文释义**：to make a disease or its symptoms less severe; to excuse or justify
- **例句**：
  - The medicine can only palliate the pain, not cure the illness.
  - 这种药只能减轻疼痛，不能治病。
  - He tried to palliate his rude remarks with a weak apology.
  - 他用无力的道歉来掩饰粗鲁的言辞。
- **助记**：palli（斗篷，如 pall）→ 像斗篷遮住 → 减轻表面症状

### 5. **quandary** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：困境；左右为难；难题
- **英文释义**：a state of perplexity or uncertainty over what to do in a difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - She was in a quandary about whether to accept the overseas offer.
  - 她对是否接受海外聘用左右为难。
  - The ethical quandary divided the committee for weeks.
  - 这一伦理难题让委员会分歧数周。
- **助记**：发音联想“困得里”→ 陷入困境 → 左右为难

### 6. **rancid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：腐臭的；哈喇的；令人反感的
- **英文释义**：smelling or tasting unpleasant as a result of being old and stale
- **例句**：
  - The butter had turned rancid in the heat.
  - 黄油在高温下变哈喇了。
  - He detected a rancid odor coming from the kitchen drain.
  - 他闻到厨房下水道传来的腐臭味。
- **助记**：ranc（腐烂）+ id → 油脂变质的 → 腐臭的

### 7. **serene** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：宁静的；安详的；晴朗的
- **英文释义**：calm, peaceful, and untroubled; clear and bright (sky)
- **例句**：
  - The lake looked serene under the full moon.
  - 满月下湖面一片宁静。
  - She maintained a serene smile despite the chaos around her.
  - 尽管周围一片混乱，她仍保持着安详的微笑。
- **助记**：seren（晴朗、清澈）→ 心境如晴空 → 宁静的

### 8. **tepid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：温热的；不热情的；平淡的
- **英文释义**：only slightly warm; showing little enthusiasm
- **例句**：
  - The soup was tepid by the time it reached the table.
  - 汤端到桌上时已经温吞了。
  - The audience gave only tepid applause after the speech.
  - 演讲结束后观众只报以稀稀拉拉的掌声。
- **助记**：tep（温）+ id → 微温 → 不冷不热、不热情

### 9. **urbane** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：彬彬有礼的；都市化的；儒雅的
- **英文释义**：courteous and refined in manner; sophisticated and polished
- **例句**：
  - The host was urbane and made every guest feel welcome.
  - 主人彬彬有礼，让每位客人都感到受欢迎。
  - His urbane wit charmed the dinner guests.
  - 他儒雅的机智逗乐了晚宴宾客。
- **助记**：urban（都市）+ e → 都市绅士风度 → 彬彬有礼的

### 10. **vehement** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：激烈的；猛烈的；情绪强烈的
- **英文释义**：showing strong feeling; forceful, passionate, or intense
- **例句**：
  - She expressed vehement opposition to the proposed cuts.
  - 她对拟议的削减表示强烈反对。
  - A vehement storm struck the coast overnight.
  - 一夜之间猛烈的风暴袭击了海岸。
- **助记**：veh（携带）+ ement → 带着强烈情绪冲出来 → 激烈的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **wry** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：扭曲的苦笑；讽刺幽默的；事与愿违的
- **英文释义**：using or expressing dry, especially mocking, humor; twisted into an expression of disgust or disappointment
- **例句**：
  - He gave a wry smile when asked if he enjoyed the long commute.
  - 当被问及是否喜欢长途通勤时，他苦笑了一下。
  - Her wry comments kept the meeting from becoming too tense.
  - 她带讽刺的幽默使会议不至于太紧张。
- **助记**：wry 联想歪嘴笑 → 扭曲的苦笑 → 揶揄的

### 12. **abstruse** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：深奥的；难懂的
- **英文释义**：difficult to understand; obscure and recondite
- **例句**：
  - The philosopher's abstruse theories confused many beginners.
  - 这位哲学家的深奥理论让许多初学者困惑。
  - Legal jargon can be abstruse without a glossary.
  - 没有术语表，法律行话可能十分晦涩。
- **助记**：abs（离开）+ trus（推、挤）→ 推到远处难触及 → 深奥的

### 13. **apocryphal** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：真伪可疑的；杜撰的；（宗教文献）旁经的
- **英文释义**：of doubtful authenticity; probably not true though widely circulated
- **例句**：
  - The story about the president is likely apocryphal.
  - 关于总统的那则传闻很可能是杜撰的。
  - Many apocryphal tales grew around the famous battle.
  - 围绕那场著名战役产生了许多真伪难辨的故事。
- **助记**：apo（离开）+ cryph（隐藏）→ 来源不明藏起来的 → 真伪可疑的

### 14. **berate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：严厉训斥；痛骂
- **英文释义**：to scold or criticize someone angrily and at length
- **例句**：
  - The coach berated the team for their lack of discipline.
  - 教练因球队缺乏纪律而严厉训斥他们。
  - She was berated in public for a minor mistake.
  - 她因小错在当众受到痛骂。
- **助记**：be（加强）+ rate（责骂）→ 狠狠责骂 → 训斥

### 15. **chicanery** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：诡计；狡辩；欺骗手段
- **英文释义**：the use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose
- **例句**：
  - The contract was full of legal chicanery designed to trap consumers.
  - 合同里满是旨在坑害消费者的法律花招。
  - Voters were tired of political chicanery and wanted honesty.
  - 选民厌倦了政治诡计，渴望诚实。
- **助记**：来自法语 chicaner（刁钻争辩）→ 狡辩与诡计
