## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abstain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：戒除；弃权；节制
- **英文释义**：restrain oneself from doing or enjoying something; formally decline to vote
- **例句**：
  - She decided to abstain from eating junk food for a month.
  - 她决定一个月不吃垃圾食品。
- **助记**：abs（远离）+ tain（持有）→ 远离持有 → 戒除

### 2. **cynical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：愤世嫉俗的；多疑的
- **英文释义**：believing that people are motivated purely by self-interest; distrustful of human sincerity
- **例句**：
  - Years of broken promises made her cynical about politics.
  - 多年的食言使她对政治持愤世嫉俗的态度。
- **助记**：cynic（犬儒主义者）+ al → 犬儒的 → 愤世嫉俗的

### 3. **depict** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：描绘；描述
- **英文释义**：show or represent by a drawing, painting, or other art form; describe in words
- **例句**：
  - The mural depicts scenes from the country's history.
  - 这幅壁画描绘了这个国家历史中的场景。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ pict（画）→ 画下来 → 描绘

### 4. **exonerate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：免除罪责；证明无罪
- **英文释义**：absolve someone from blame for a fault or wrongdoing
- **例句**：
  - New evidence exonerated the man who had been wrongly convicted.
  - 新证据证明了这个被错误定罪的人的清白。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ oner（负担）+ ate → 去除负担 → 免除罪责

### 5. **falter** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：犹豫；蹒跚；衰退
- **英文释义**：start to lose strength or momentum; speak hesitantly
- **例句**：
  - Her confidence faltered when she saw the large audience.
  - 看到众多观众时，她的信心动摇了。
- **助记**：fault（过失）+ er → 犯了过失而犹豫 → 犹豫

### 6. **gratify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使满意；使高兴；满足
- **英文释义**：give pleasure or satisfaction to; indulge or satisfy
- **例句**：
  - It gratified her to know that her hard work was appreciated.
  - 知道自己的辛勤工作被认可，她感到很满意。
- **助记**：grat（感恩/愉悦）+ ify → 使愉悦 → 使满意

### 7. **impoverish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使贫穷；使贫乏
- **英文释义**：make (a person or area) poor; deplete the natural fertility of soil
- **例句**：
  - Years of war had impoverished the entire nation.
  - 多年的战争使整个国家变得贫穷。
- **助记**：im（使）+ pover（穷）+ ish → 使变穷 → 使贫穷

### 8. **laudable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：值得称赞的；可嘉的
- **英文释义**：(of an action, idea, or goal) deserving praise and commendation
- **例句**：
  - Her efforts to help the homeless are truly laudable.
  - 她帮助无家可归者的努力确实值得称赞。
- **助记**：laud（赞美）+ able → 值得赞美的

### 9. **ominous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不祥的；预兆的
- **英文释义**：giving the impression that something bad or unpleasant is going to happen
- **例句**：
  - The dark clouds were an ominous sign of the approaching storm.
  - 乌云是暴风雨即将来临的不祥之兆。
- **助记**：omin（预兆）+ ous → 有预兆的 → 不祥的

### 10. **reconcile** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：调和；和解；使一致
- **英文释义**：restore friendly relations between; make compatible
- **例句**：
  - After years of conflict, the two brothers finally reconciled.
  - 经过多年的冲突，两兄弟终于和解了。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ concile（友好）→ 再次友好 → 和解

### 高一词汇

### 11. **calamitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：灾难性的；悲惨的
- **英文释义**：involving calamity; catastrophic or disastrous
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake had calamitous effects on the region's infrastructure.
  - 地震对该地区的基础设施造成了灾难性的影响。
- **助记**：calam（灾难）+ itous → 充满灾难的

### 12. **effulgent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：光辉灿烂的；辉煌的
- **英文释义**：shining brightly; radiant
- **例句**：
  - The effulgent sunset painted the sky in shades of gold and crimson.
  - 光辉灿烂的日落将天空染成金色和深红色。
- **助记**：ef（出）+ fulg（闪光）+ ent → 发出闪光的 → 光辉灿烂的

### 13. **intransigent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不妥协的；固执的
- **英文释义**：unwilling or refusing to change one's views or to agree about something
- **例句**：
  - The intransigent negotiator refused to make any concessions.
  - 这位固执的谈判者拒绝做出任何让步。
- **助记**：in（不）+ transig（妥协）+ ent → 不妥协的

### 14. **supercilious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：目中无人的；傲慢的
- **英文释义**：behaving or looking as though one thinks one is superior to others
- **例句**：
  - His supercilious attitude made him unpopular among his colleagues.
  - 他傲慢的态度使他在同事中不受欢迎。
- **助记**：super（在上面）+ cili（眉毛）+ ous → 扬起眉毛看人 → 傲慢的

### 15. **verisimilitude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：逼真；真实感
- **英文释义**：the appearance of being true or real
- **例句**：
  - The novel's verisimilitude made it feel like a true story.
  - 这部小说的逼真感使它读起来像一个真实的故事。
- **助记**：veri（真的）+ simil（相似）+ itude → 与真实相似 → 逼真
