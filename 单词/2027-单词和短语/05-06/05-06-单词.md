## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **asylum** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：庇护；避难所；精神病院
- **英文释义**：the protection granted by a nation to someone who has left their home country as a political refugee; a place of shelter
- **例句**：
  - Thousands of refugees sought asylum in neighbouring countries.
  - 成千上万的难民在邻国寻求庇护。
- **助记**：a + sylum（安全所）→ 安全的地方 → 庇护所

### 2. **bygone** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 过去的；以前的 n. 往事
- **英文释义**：adj. belonging to an earlier time; n. a thing dating from an earlier time
- **例句**：
  - The museum displays artefacts from a bygone era.
  - 博物馆展示了一个过去时代的文物。
- **助记**：by（经过）+ gone（走了）→ 已经走过的 → 过去的

### 3. **condone** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：容忍；纵容；宽恕
- **英文释义**：to accept or allow behaviour that is considered wrong
- **例句**：
  - The school does not condone any form of bullying.
  - 学校不容忍任何形式的欺凌行为。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ done（做）→ 和他们一起做（不反对）→ 容忍

### 4. **desolate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：荒凉的；凄惨的；孤独的
- **英文释义**：deserted and giving an impression of bleak emptiness; feeling wretchedly unhappy
- **例句**：
  - The abandoned village was a desolate sight with crumbling buildings.
  - 那个被遗弃的村庄一片荒凉，建筑物摇摇欲坠。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ sol（孤独）+ ate → 完全孤独的 → 荒凉的

### 5. **entail** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：需要；牵涉；使成为必要
- **英文释义**：to involve something as a necessary part or consequence
- **例句**：
  - The project entails a significant amount of research and planning.
  - 这个项目需要大量的研究和规划。
- **助记**：en（使）+ tail（尾巴）→ 使尾巴跟随 → 牵涉/必然涉及

### 6. **glimmer** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 微光；一丝（希望等） v. 发出微光
- **英文释义**：n. a faint or wavering light; a faint sign; v. to shine faintly
- **例句**：
  - A glimmer of hope appeared when rescue workers found survivors.
  - 当救援人员找到幸存者时，一丝希望出现了。
- **助记**：glimmer → glitter（闪光）的弱版 → 微光

### 7. **inundate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：淹没；使应接不暇；充满
- **英文释义**：to overwhelm with things to be dealt with; to flood
- **例句**：
  - The customer service department was inundated with complaints after the product recall.
  - 产品召回后，客服部门被投诉淹没了。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ und（波浪）+ ate → 波浪涌入 → 淹没

### 8. **lofty** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：崇高的；高大的；傲慢的
- **英文释义**：of imposing height; of a noble nature; haughty
- **例句**：
  - He set lofty goals for himself and worked tirelessly to achieve them.
  - 他为自己设定了崇高的目标，并不懈努力去实现。
- **助记**：loft（阁楼/高处）+ y → 在高处的 → 崇高的/高大的

### 9. **notch** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 档次；等级；刻痕 v. 赢得；刻出
- **英文释义**：n. an indentation or incision on a surface; a level; v. to score or achieve
- **例句**：
  - The team notched up another victory with a score of 3-1.
  - 球队以3比1的比分又赢得了一场胜利。
- **助记**：notch → 在柱子上刻痕记录成绩 → 等级/赢得

### 10. **pragmatic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；实际的；注重实效的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically
- **例句**：
  - The manager took a pragmatic approach to solving the budget crisis.
  - 经理采取了务实的方法来解决预算危机。
- **助记**：pragma（实践）+ tic → 注重实践的 → 务实的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **accolade** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：荣誉；赞扬；嘉奖
- **英文释义**：an award or privilege granted as a special honour or acknowledgement of merit
- **例句**：
  - The film received the highest accolade at the Cannes Film Festival.
  - 这部电影在戛纳电影节获得了最高荣誉。
- **助记**：ac（向）+ col（脖子）+ ade → 拥抱脖子表示嘉奖 → 荣誉

### 12. **dilettante** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：业余爱好者；半吊子；浅尝辄止的人
- **英文释义**：a person who cultivates an area of interest without real commitment or knowledge
- **例句**：
  - He's a dilettante in art — he knows a little about everything but masters nothing.
  - 他在艺术方面只是个半吊子——什么都知道一点但都不精通。
- **助记**：dilettante → delight（快乐）→ 只是为了开心而做，不深入 → 业余爱好者

### 13. **imperious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：专横的；傲慢的；盛气凌人的
- **英文释义**：assuming power or authority without justification; domineering
- **例句**：
  - Her imperious manner made it difficult for her colleagues to work with her.
  - 她专横的态度使同事们很难与她合作。
- **助记**：imper（命令）+ ious → 总是下命令的 → 专横的

### 14. **nefarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：邪恶的；极坏的；不道德的
- **英文释义**：wicked or criminal
- **例句**：
  - The villain's nefarious plan was foiled by the detective at the last moment.
  - 恶棍邪恶的计划在最后一刻被侦探挫败了。
- **助记**：ne（不）+ far（法律/正义）+ ious → 不正义的 → 邪恶的

### 15. **sanguine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：乐观的；自信的；血红色的
- **英文释义**：optimistic or positive, especially in difficult situations
- **例句**：
  - Despite the setbacks, she remained sanguine about the project's success.
  - 尽管遇到了挫折，她对项目的成功仍然保持乐观。
- **助记**：sanguin（血）+ e → 有血色的、精力充沛的 → 乐观的
