## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **redolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：芳香的；使人联想起……的；充满……气息的
- **英文释义**：strongly reminiscent or suggestive of; fragrant or smelling of
- **例句**：
  - The kitchen was redolent of garlic and fresh herbs.
  - 厨房里弥漫着大蒜和新鲜香草的香味。
  - The old photo was redolent of childhood summers.
  - 这张老照片让人想起童年的夏天。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ ol（闻，如 olfactory）+ ent → 再次闻到 → 芳香的/联想的

### 2. **saccharine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：（态度）甜得发腻的；含糖的；过分感伤的
- **英文释义**：excessively sweet or sentimental; of or like sugar
- **例句**：
  - The movie's saccharine ending disappointed critics who wanted realism.
  - 影片甜腻的结局让希望写实主义的评论家失望。
  - She spoke in a saccharine voice that sounded insincere.
  - 她用甜得发腻的声音说话，听起来不真诚。
- **助记**：sacchar（糖）+ ine → 像糖一样腻 → 过分甜腻的

### 3. **tenuous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：脆弱的；纤细的；依据不足的
- **英文释义**：very weak or slight; insubstantial; thin in consistency
- **例句**：
  - The peace held on a tenuous ceasefire agreement.
  - 和平建立在脆弱的停火协议上。
  - There is only tenuous evidence linking him to the crime.
  - 只有薄弱的证据将他与犯罪联系起来。
- **助记**：tenu（薄，如 thin）+ ous → 薄弱的

### 4. **untenable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：（立场）站不住脚的；难以防守的；难以维持的
- **英文释义**：not able to be maintained or defended against criticism or attack
- **例句**：
  - After the new data, his theory became untenable.
  - 新数据出现后，他的理论站不住脚了。
  - The army's position on the hill was untenable under heavy fire.
  - 在猛烈炮火下，军队在山上的阵地难以守住。
- **助记**：un（不）+ tenable（可维持的）→ 无法维持的

### 5. **venerate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：崇敬；敬重
- **英文释义**：to regard with great respect; to revere
- **例句**：
  - Many cultures venerate ancestors with annual ceremonies.
  - 许多文化以年度仪式崇敬祖先。
  - Students venerate the professor for her lifelong dedication to teaching.
  - 学生们敬重这位教授毕生奉献教育的精神。
- **助记**：vener（尊敬）+ ate → 崇敬

### 6. **waver** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：动摇；犹豫不决；（光、声音）闪烁颤抖
- **英文释义**：to hesitate between choices; to shake with a quivering motion
- **例句**：
  - She wavered between two universities before choosing one.
  - 她在两所大学之间犹豫不决才选定。
  - Candlelight wavered on the walls during the storm.
  - 暴风雨中烛光在墙上摇曳。
- **助记**：wave（波动）→ 摇摆不定 → 动摇

### 7. **xenial** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：好客的；款待异邦人的
- **英文释义**：relating to hospitality between host and guest, especially strangers
- **例句**：
  - The xenial traditions of the island welcome every visitor warmly.
  - 这座岛屿好客的传统热情欢迎每位访客。
  - Ancient texts describe xenial duties toward travelers.
  - 古代文献记载了对旅行者的好客义务。
- **助记**：xeno（外乡人）+ ial → 对外乡人友好的 → 好客的

### 8. **yearn** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：渴望；思念；向往
- **英文释义**：to have an intense longing for something
- **例句**：
  - He yearned to return to the mountains where he grew up.
  - 他渴望回到长大的山区。
  - The refugees yearn for peace and a stable home.
  - 难民渴望和平与稳定的家。
- **助记**：yearn 联想 earnestly yearn → 热切渴望

### 9. **zealot** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：狂热者；（宗教或政治）极端分子
- **英文释义**：a person who is fanatical and uncompromising in pursuit of a cause
- **例句**：
  - Zealots on both sides refused any compromise.
  - 双方的狂热分子都拒绝任何妥协。
  - He was a fitness zealot who exercised twice a day.
  - 他是健身狂人，每天锻炼两次。
- **助记**：zeal（热情）+ ot → 过度热情的人 → 狂热者

### 10. **buoyant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有浮力的；轻快自信的；繁荣的
- **英文释义**：able to float; cheerful and optimistic; keeping the economy up
- **例句**：
  - Cork is naturally buoyant and used in life jackets.
  - 软木天然有浮力，用于救生衣。
  - She remained buoyant despite the bad news.
  - 尽管坏消息传来，她仍保持乐观。
- **助记**：buoy（浮标）+ ant → 能浮起的 → 有浮力的/乐观的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **catharsis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：宣泄；净化；（亚里士多德）通过艺术净化情感
- **英文释义**：the process of releasing strong emotions; emotional purification through art
- **例句**：
  - Writing in a journal provided catharsis after the breakup.
  - 分手后写日记成了一种情感宣泄。
  - Greek tragedy aimed at catharsis through pity and fear.
  - 希腊悲剧旨在通过怜悯与恐惧达到净化。
- **助记**：cathar（净化）→ 情感宣泄净化

### 12. **euphemism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：委婉语；婉辞
- **英文释义**：a mild or indirect word substituted for one considered harsh or blunt
- **例句**：
  - "Passed away" is a euphemism for "died."
  - “过世”是“死”的委婉说法。
  - Office euphemisms often hide unpleasant truths about layoffs.
  - 办公室的委婉语常常掩盖裁员等不快真相。
- **助记**：eu（好）+ phem（说）→ 说好听的话 → 委婉语

### 13. **fallacy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：谬误；错误推理；常见误解
- **英文释义**：a mistaken belief, especially one based on unsound reasoning
- **例句**：
  - It is a fallacy that more hours always mean better grades.
  - 学习时间越长成绩越好是一种谬误。
  - The article exposed several logical fallacies in the argument.
  - 文章揭露了论证中的几处逻辑谬误。
- **助记**：fall（欺骗）+ acy → 骗人的想法 → 谬误

### 14. **galvanize** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：激励；刺激；电镀（镀锌）
- **英文释义**：to shock or excite someone into action; to coat with zinc
- **例句**：
  - The speech galvanized young voters to register in record numbers.
  - 演讲激励年轻选民创纪录地登记投票。
  - Fear of failure galvanized the team to train harder.
  - 对失败的恐惧促使球队更刻苦训练。
- **助记**：来自 Galvani（电流实验）→ 像电击一样刺激 → 激励

### 15. **hegemony** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：霸权；支配权；领导地位
- **英文释义**：leadership or dominance, especially by one state or social group over others
- **例句**：
  - Cultural hegemony can shape what people consider "normal."
  - 文化霸权能塑造人们对“正常”的认知。
  - The empire maintained hegemony over trade routes for centuries.
  - 该帝国数个世纪维持对贸易路线的霸权。
- **助记**：heg（领导）+ emony → 领导支配 → 霸权
