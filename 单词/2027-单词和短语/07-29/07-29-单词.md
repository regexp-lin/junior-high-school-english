## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abduct** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：绑架；劫持；诱拐
- **英文释义**：to take someone away illegally by force or deception
- **例句**：
  - The child was abducted from the playground and found safe three days later.
  - 孩子从游乐场被绑架，三天后被安全找到。
- **助记**：ab（离开）+ duct（引导）→ 引导离开 → 绑架

### 2. **conjecture** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 推测；猜测 v. 推测；猜想
- **英文释义**：n. an opinion formed without proof; v. to form an opinion based on incomplete information
- **例句**：
  - Without solid evidence, any explanation at this point is merely conjecture.
  - 没有确凿的证据，目前的任何解释都只是猜测。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ ject（投掷）+ ure → 一起投出想法 → 推测

### 3. **delegate** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 代表；委员 v. 委派；授权
- **英文释义**：n. a person sent to represent others; v. to entrust a task or responsibility to another person
- **例句**：
  - A good leader knows how to delegate tasks effectively to team members.
  - 好的领导者知道如何有效地将任务委派给团队成员。
- **助记**：de（下）+ leg（派遣）+ ate → 向下派遣 → 委派/代表

### 4. **expedient** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 权宜的；方便的 n. 权宜之计；应急手段
- **英文释义**：adj. convenient and practical although possibly improper; n. a means to an end
- **例句**：
  - The government took the expedient approach of raising taxes to quickly reduce the deficit.
  - 政府采取了增税这一权宜之计来快速减少赤字。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ped（脚）+ ient → 迅速抽脚走出困境 → 权宜的

### 5. **germinate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：发芽；萌生；开始发展
- **英文释义**：to begin to grow or develop; to cause a seed to sprout
- **例句**：
  - The idea for the novel germinated during a long train journey across Europe.
  - 这部小说的想法是在一次穿越欧洲的长途火车旅行中萌生的。
- **助记**：germ（种子/胚芽）+ inate → 种子长出来 → 发芽/萌生

### 6. **innate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：天生的；固有的；与生俱来的
- **英文释义**：inborn; natural; existing from birth
- **例句**：
  - She has an innate talent for music that was obvious even as a young child.
  - 她有与生俱来的音乐天赋，甚至在幼年时就很明显。
- **助记**：in（内部）+ nate（出生）→ 出生时就在内部的 → 天生的

### 7. **negotiate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：谈判；协商；成功通过
- **英文释义**：to try to reach an agreement by discussion; to find a way over or through
- **例句**：
  - The two countries negotiated a trade agreement that benefited both economies.
  - 两国协商了一份对双方经济都有利的贸易协定。
- **助记**：neg（否定）+ oti（闲暇）+ ate → 没有闲暇（忙于谈判）→ 谈判

### 8. **placid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：平静的；温和的；安详的
- **英文释义**：not easily upset or excited; calm and peaceful
- **例句**：
  - The placid lake reflected the surrounding mountains like a giant mirror.
  - 平静的湖面像一面巨大的镜子映照着周围的山脉。
- **助记**：plac（平静/愉快）+ id → 平静的

### 9. **remorse** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：懊悔；自责；内疚
- **英文释义**：deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
- **例句**：
  - He was filled with remorse after realizing how his harsh words had hurt his friend.
  - 当他意识到自己严厉的话语伤害了朋友时，他充满了懊悔。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ morse（咬）→ 再次咬自己 → 自责/懊悔

### 10. **tarnish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使失去光泽；玷污；损害
- **英文释义**：to make or become less valuable or respected; to lose or cause to lose luster
- **例句**：
  - The corruption scandal tarnished the politician's reputation beyond repair.
  - 腐败丑闻永久地损害了这位政客的声誉。
- **助记**：tarnish → 联想"暗（tan）+rish"→ 变暗失去光泽 → 玷污

### 高一词汇

### 11. **circumspect** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：谨慎的；小心翼翼的；考虑周密的
- **英文释义**：wary and unwilling to take risks; prudent
- **例句**：
  - The circumspect investor carefully researched every company before committing any funds.
  - 这位谨慎的投资者在投入任何资金前都仔细研究了每家公司。
- **助记**：circum（周围）+ spect（看）→ 四处张望 → 谨慎的

### 12. **edify** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：启迪；教化；陶冶
- **英文释义**：to instruct or benefit, especially morally or spiritually
- **例句**：
  - The documentary was designed to edify viewers about the importance of environmental conservation.
  - 这部纪录片旨在启迪观众认识环境保护的重要性。
- **助记**：edi（建造）+ fy → 建造精神世界 → 启迪/教化

### 13. **indefatigable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不知疲倦的；不屈不挠的
- **英文释义**：persisting tirelessly
- **例句**：
  - The indefatigable volunteer worked around the clock to help the disaster victims.
  - 这位不知疲倦的志愿者夜以继日地工作帮助灾民。
- **助记**：in（不）+ de（完全）+ fatig（疲劳）+ able → 不会完全疲劳的 → 不知疲倦的

### 14. **quandary** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：困境；进退两难；窘境
- **英文释义**：a state of perplexity or uncertainty over what to do in a difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - She found herself in a quandary, torn between accepting the promotion and staying close to family.
  - 她陷入了困境，在接受晋升和留在家人身边之间左右为难。
- **助记**：quandary → 联想"quan（圈）+ d + ary"→ 被围在圈里出不去 → 困境

### 15. **vestige** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：遗迹；残余；痕迹
- **英文释义**：a trace of something that is disappearing or no longer exists
- **例句**：
  - The ruins are the last vestige of the ancient civilization that once thrived here.
  - 这些废墟是曾在此繁荣的古代文明的最后遗迹。
- **助记**：vestig（脚印/痕迹）+ e → 留下的脚印 → 遗迹/痕迹
