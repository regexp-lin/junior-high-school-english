## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **acrimonious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：尖酸刻薄的；充满怨恨的
- **英文释义**：angry and bitter, typically of speech or debate
- **例句**：
  - The acrimonious divorce proceedings lasted for over two years.
  - 充满怨恨的离婚诉讼持续了两年多。
  - The debate became acrimonious when personal insults were exchanged.
  - 当双方互相进行人身攻击时，辩论变得尖酸刻薄。
- **助记**：acri（尖锐/苦）+ monious → 言辞尖锐的 → 尖酸刻薄的

### 2. **supplement** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：补充；增补；追加
- **英文释义**：to add an extra element or amount to something
- **例句**：
  - She supplements her income by teaching private lessons on weekends.
  - 她通过周末上私人课来补充收入。
  - The textbook is supplemented with online resources and practice exercises.
  - 教科书附有在线资源和练习题作为补充。
- **助记**：sup（在下面）+ ple（填充）+ ment → 在下面填充 → 补充

### 3. **cumbersome** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：笨重的；累赘的；复杂难操作的
- **英文释义**：large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry or use; unwieldy
- **例句**：
  - The cumbersome equipment made it difficult for the soldiers to move quickly.
  - 笨重的装备使士兵们难以快速移动。
  - The bureaucratic process was so cumbersome that it took months to get approval.
  - 官僚程序如此繁琐，获得批准要花好几个月。
- **助记**：cumber（负担）+ some → 有负担的 → 笨重的

### 4. **delineate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：描绘；勾勒；阐明
- **英文释义**：to describe or portray something precisely; to indicate the exact position of a border
- **例句**：
  - The report delineates the key differences between the two proposals.
  - 报告阐明了两个提案之间的主要区别。
  - The map clearly delineates the boundaries between the territories.
  - 地图清楚地勾勒出了各领土之间的边界。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ line（线）+ ate → 完全画出线条 → 描绘

### 5. **intransigent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不妥协的；固执的；不让步的
- **英文释义**：unwilling or refusing to change one's views or to agree about something
- **例句**：
  - The intransigent negotiator refused to make any concessions during the talks.
  - 不妥协的谈判者在会谈中拒绝做出任何让步。
  - Both sides remained intransigent, making a peaceful resolution impossible.
  - 双方都保持不让步的态度，使和平解决成为不可能。
- **助记**：in（不）+ transig（让步）+ ent → 不让步的 → 不妥协的

### 6. **exemplify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：例证；是…的典范；举例说明
- **英文释义**：to be a typical example of; to illustrate by giving an example
- **例句**：
  - Her dedication to charity exemplifies the values of our organization.
  - 她对慈善事业的奉献是我们组织价值观的典范。
  - The teacher used real-world scenarios to exemplify the mathematical concepts.
  - 老师用现实生活中的场景来举例说明数学概念。
- **助记**：exempli（榜样）+ fy → 做榜样 → 例证

### 7. **perfidious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：背信弃义的；不忠的；奸诈的
- **英文释义**：deceitful and untrustworthy
- **例句**：
  - The perfidious ally betrayed their trust at the most critical moment.
  - 背信弃义的盟友在最关键的时刻背叛了他们的信任。
  - History is full of tales of perfidious leaders who turned against their own people.
  - 历史上充满了背信弃义的领导者与自己的人民反目的故事。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ fid（信任）+ ious → 贯穿信任（打破信任）→ 背信弃义的

### 8. **culminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：达到顶点；以…告终；达到高潮
- **英文释义**：to reach a climax or point of highest development
- **例句**：
  - Years of hard work culminated in her winning the national championship.
  - 多年的努力最终以她赢得全国冠军而达到高潮。
  - The festival culminated in a spectacular fireworks display.
  - 节日以壮观的烟花表演告终。
- **助记**：culmin（顶点）+ ate → 到达顶点 → 达到高潮

### 9. **stringent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严格的；严厉的；紧缩的
- **英文释义**：strict, precise, and exacting
- **例句**：
  - The factory must comply with stringent safety regulations.
  - 工厂必须遵守严格的安全法规。
  - Stringent quality control measures ensured that every product met the highest standards.
  - 严格的质量控制措施确保每件产品都符合最高标准。
- **助记**：string（绷紧）+ ent → 绷得很紧 → 严格的

### 10. **proclivity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：倾向；癖性；偏好
- **英文释义**：a tendency to choose or do something regularly; an inclination
- **例句**：
  - His proclivity for procrastination often led to missed deadlines.
  - 他拖延的癖性经常导致错过截止日期。
  - The child showed an early proclivity for music, picking up melodies effortlessly.
  - 这个孩子很早就表现出对音乐的偏好，轻松地学会旋律。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ cliv（倾斜）+ ity → 向前倾斜 → 倾向

### 高一词汇

### 11. **sesquicentennial** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：150周年的；150周年纪念
- **英文释义**：relating to or marking a 150th anniversary
- **例句**：
  - The city held grand celebrations for its sesquicentennial anniversary.
  - 该城市为其150周年纪念举行了盛大庆祝活动。
  - The sesquicentennial edition of the book includes rare photographs and new commentary.
  - 该书的150周年纪念版包括珍贵照片和新评论。
- **助记**：sesqui（一个半）+ centennial（百年纪念）→ 一百五十年纪念

### 12. **chrysalis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：蛹；过渡阶段；蜕变期
- **英文释义**：the pupa of a butterfly enclosed in a cocoon; a preparatory or transitional state
- **例句**：
  - The caterpillar formed a chrysalis before emerging as a beautiful butterfly.
  - 毛毛虫结成蛹，然后蜕变为美丽的蝴蝶。
  - The company is in a chrysalis stage, transforming from a startup into a major corporation.
  - 公司正处于蜕变阶段，从一家初创企业转型为大型企业。
- **助记**：chrys（金色）+ alis → 金色的壳 → 蛹

### 13. **resplendent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：光辉灿烂的；辉煌的；华丽的
- **英文释义**：attractive and impressive through being richly colorful or sumptuous
- **例句**：
  - The queen looked resplendent in her jewel-encrusted gown at the state banquet.
  - 女王在国宴上穿着镶满珠宝的礼服显得光彩夺目。
  - The autumn foliage was resplendent with shades of red, orange, and gold.
  - 秋叶呈现出红、橙、金的色调，辉煌灿烂。
- **助记**：re（加强）+ splend（闪耀）+ ent → 不断闪耀 → 光辉灿烂的

### 14. **fugacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：短暂易逝的；稍纵即逝的
- **英文释义**：tending to disappear; fleeting
- **例句**：
  - The fugacious beauty of the cherry blossoms draws visitors from around the world.
  - 樱花短暂易逝的美丽吸引了来自世界各地的游客。
  - Youth is fugacious — we should make the most of every moment.
  - 青春转瞬即逝——我们应该珍惜每一刻。
- **助记**：fug（逃跑）+ acious → 像在逃跑一样 → 短暂易逝的

### 15. **empyrean** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：天堂；最高天；天上的
- **英文释义**：the highest part of heaven; celestial
- **例句**：
  - Dante described the empyrean as the dwelling place of God in his Divine Comedy.
  - 但丁在《神曲》中将最高天描述为上帝的居所。
  - The empyrean beauty of the starlit sky filled the observers with wonder.
  - 星空的天堂般的美丽让观测者充满了惊叹。
- **助记**：em（在…中）+ pyr（火）+ ean → 在火焰之中（古人认为天空最高处有纯净的火）→ 最高天
