## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **amass** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：积累；聚集；积聚
- **英文释义**：to gather together or accumulate a large amount or quantity of
- **例句**：
  - Over decades, the collector amassed a remarkable collection of rare paintings.
  - 几十年来，这位收藏家积累了一批非凡的珍稀画作收藏。
- **助记**：a（向）+ mass（大量）→ 聚集大量 → 积累

### 2. **comply** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：遵守；服从；依从
- **英文释义**：to act in accordance with a wish, command, or rule
- **例句**：
  - All passengers must comply with the airline's safety regulations during the flight.
  - 所有乘客在飞行期间必须遵守航空公司的安全规定。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ ply（折叠/弯曲）→ 完全弯下身来 → 服从/遵守

### 3. **dubious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可疑的；不确定的；靠不住的
- **英文释义**：hesitating or doubting; not to be relied upon
- **例句**：
  - The detective was dubious about the suspect's alibi for the night of the crime.
  - 侦探对嫌疑人案发当晚的不在场证明持怀疑态度。
- **助记**：dub（二/双重）+ ious → 两面的/不确定的 → 可疑的

### 4. **extract** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 提取；摘录；拔出 n. 提取物；摘录
- **英文释义**：v. to remove or take out; n. a preparation containing the active ingredient of a substance
- **例句**：
  - Scientists extracted DNA samples from the fossils for analysis.
  - 科学家从化石中提取了DNA样本进行分析。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ tract（拉/拖）→ 拉出来 → 提取

### 5. **gregarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱交际的；合群的；群居的
- **英文释义**：fond of company; sociable; living in flocks or loosely organized communities
- **例句**：
  - Her gregarious personality made her popular at every social event she attended.
  - 她爱交际的性格让她在参加的每个社交活动中都很受欢迎。
- **助记**：greg（群）+ arious → 喜欢群体的 → 爱交际的/群居的

### 6. **infringe** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：侵犯；违反；侵害
- **英文释义**：to actively break the terms of a law or agreement; to encroach on someone's rights
- **例句**：
  - The company was sued for infringing on the competitor's patent rights.
  - 该公司因侵犯竞争对手的专利权而被起诉。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ fringe（边界）→ 侵入边界 → 侵犯

### 7. **obliterate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：消灭；抹去；彻底摧毁
- **英文释义**：to destroy utterly; to wipe out
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake obliterated the small town, leaving nothing but rubble.
  - 地震将小镇彻底摧毁，只留下一片废墟。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ liter（字母/痕迹）+ ate → 对着痕迹抹去 → 消灭

### 8. **perpetuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使持续；使永存；使长期存在
- **英文释义**：to make something continue indefinitely
- **例句**：
  - The media can sometimes perpetuate harmful stereotypes about certain groups.
  - 媒体有时会使关于某些群体的有害刻板印象长期存在。
- **助记**：perpetu（永久的）+ ate → 使永久 → 使持续

### 9. **replenish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：补充；重新装满；再供应
- **英文释义**：to fill something up again; to restore to the former level or condition
- **例句**：
  - The store needs to replenish its stock before the holiday shopping season begins.
  - 商店需要在假日购物季开始前补充库存。
- **助记**：re（重新）+ plen（满的）+ ish → 重新变满 → 补充

### 10. **zenith** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：顶点；鼎盛；天顶
- **英文释义**：the time at which something is most powerful or successful; the highest point
- **例句**：
  - The empire reached its zenith during the 18th century before slowly declining.
  - 这个帝国在18世纪达到鼎盛，之后慢慢衰落。
- **助记**：zenith → 天顶（太阳在正头顶的位置）→ 顶点/鼎盛

### 高一词汇

### 11. **antithesis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：对立面；对比；对照
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that is the direct opposite of someone or something else
- **例句**：
  - His calm demeanor was the antithesis of the panic shown by everyone else in the room.
  - 他冷静的举止与房间里其他人表现出的恐慌形成了鲜明对比。
- **助记**：anti（反对）+ thesis（论点）→ 相反的论点 → 对立面

### 12. **erudite** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：博学的；有学问的；学识渊博的
- **英文释义**：having or showing great knowledge or learning
- **例句**：
  - The erudite professor could speak seven languages and had expertise in multiple fields.
  - 这位博学的教授能说七种语言，在多个领域有专业知识。
- **助记**：e（出）+ rud（粗糙/原始）+ ite → 脱离原始无知 → 博学的

### 13. **impunity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：不受惩罚；免罚；豁免
- **英文释义**：exemption from punishment or freedom from the injurious consequences of an action
- **例句**：
  - Corrupt officials acted with impunity, knowing they would never face prosecution.
  - 腐败官员有恃无恐地行事，知道自己永远不会被起诉。
- **助记**：im（不）+ pun（惩罚）+ ity → 不受惩罚 → 免罚

### 14. **plethora** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：过多；大量；过剩
- **英文释义**：a large or excessive amount of something
- **例句**：
  - The internet offers a plethora of information on virtually any topic imaginable.
  - 互联网提供了几乎任何你能想到的话题的大量信息。
- **助记**：pleth（充满）+ ora → 充满了 → 过多/大量

### 15. **sanguine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：乐观的；充满希望的；自信的
- **英文释义**：optimistic or positive, especially in an apparently bad situation
- **例句**：
  - Despite the economic difficulties, the CEO remained sanguine about the company's future.
  - 尽管经济困难，CEO对公司的未来仍然保持乐观。
- **助记**：sangu（血）+ ine → 血气旺盛的 → 乐观的/充满希望的
