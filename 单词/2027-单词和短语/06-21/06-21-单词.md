## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减少；减弱；减轻
- **英文释义**：to become less intense or widespread; to reduce in amount, degree, or intensity
- **例句**：
  - The storm finally abated after three hours of heavy rain.
  - 暴风雨在三小时的大雨后终于减弱了。
- **助记**：a（加强）+ bate（打）→ 把势头打下去 → 减弱

### 2. **candid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坦率的；直言不讳的；坦诚的
- **英文释义**：truthful and straightforward; frank
- **例句**：
  - She gave a candid interview about her struggles with anxiety.
  - 她坦率地接受了关于她焦虑问题的采访。
- **助记**：cand（白/发光）+ id → 内心光明坦荡 → 坦率的

### 3. **deficit** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：赤字；缺口；不足额
- **英文释义**：the amount by which something is too small; an excess of expenditure over revenue
- **例句**：
  - The government is trying to reduce the budget deficit.
  - 政府正在努力减少预算赤字。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ fic（做）+ it → 做出来的不够 → 赤字/缺口

### 4. **enigma** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：谜；难以理解的人或事物
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that is mysterious, puzzling, or difficult to understand
- **例句**：
  - The disappearance of the plane remains an enigma to investigators.
  - 飞机的失踪对调查人员来说仍然是一个谜。
- **助记**：enigma → 联想"疑难码"→ 难以破解的谜

### 5. **formidable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：强大的；令人敬畏的；难以对付的
- **英文释义**：inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, or capable
- **例句**：
  - The team faces a formidable opponent in the championship final.
  - 该队在冠军决赛中面临一个强大的对手。
- **助记**：formid（恐惧）+ able → 令人恐惧的 → 令人敬畏的

### 6. **grueling** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：累人的；艰苦的；折磨人的
- **英文释义**：extremely tiring and demanding
- **例句**：
  - The marathon was a grueling test of endurance for all the runners.
  - 马拉松对所有跑者来说是一场艰苦的耐力考验。
- **助记**：gruel（稀粥）+ ing → 累到只能喝稀粥 → 累人的

### 7. **indifferent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：冷漠的；漠不关心的；无所谓的
- **英文释义**：having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned
- **例句**：
  - He seemed indifferent to the suffering of others around him.
  - 他似乎对周围人的苦难漠不关心。
- **助记**：in（不）+ different（不同的）→ 觉得没什么不同 → 无所谓的

### 8. **lucid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：清晰的；明白易懂的；头脑清醒的
- **英文释义**：expressed clearly; easy to understand; showing ability to think clearly
- **例句**：
  - The professor gave a lucid explanation of the complex theory.
  - 教授对这个复杂的理论给出了清晰的解释。
- **助记**：luc（光）+ id → 像光一样明亮 → 清晰的

### 9. **obscure** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：adj. 模糊的；晦涩的 v. 使模糊；遮掩
- **英文释义**：adj. not discovered or known about; uncertain; v. to keep from being seen; to conceal
- **例句**：
  - The meaning of the ancient text remains obscure to most scholars.
  - 这篇古文的含义对大多数学者来说仍然晦涩难懂。
- **助记**：ob（覆盖）+ scure（遮蔽）→ 被遮蔽的 → 模糊的

### 10. **replenish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：补充；重新装满；再填满
- **英文释义**：to fill something up again; to restore to its former level or condition
- **例句**：
  - We need to replenish our water supplies before continuing the hike.
  - 我们需要在继续徒步之前补充水供给。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ plen（满）+ ish → 再次装满 → 补充

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acquiesce** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：默许；勉强同意；顺从
- **英文释义**：to accept something reluctantly but without protest
- **例句**：
  - After much debate, the board acquiesced to the new policy changes.
  - 经过大量辩论，董事会默许了新的政策变更。
- **助记**：ac（向）+ qui（安静）+ esce → 安静地接受 → 默许

### 12. **dichotomy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：二分法；对立；两极分化
- **英文释义**：a division or contrast between two things that are represented as being opposed or entirely different
- **例句**：
  - There is a growing dichotomy between the rich and the poor in modern society.
  - 现代社会中贫富之间的两极分化日益加剧。
- **助记**：dicho（二）+ tomy（切）→ 切成两半 → 二分法

### 13. **indefatigable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不知疲倦的；不屈不挠的
- **英文释义**：persisting tirelessly; never showing signs of fatigue
- **例句**：
  - The indefatigable researcher spent decades searching for a cure.
  - 这位不知疲倦的研究者花了数十年寻找治疗方法。
- **助记**：in（不）+ de（向下）+ fatig（疲劳）+ able → 不会疲劳的 → 不知疲倦的

### 14. **obsequious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：谄媚的；奉承的；卑躬屈膝的
- **英文释义**：obedient or attentive to an excessive or servile degree
- **例句**：
  - The obsequious waiter hovered around the wealthy customer all evening.
  - 那个谄媚的服务员整晚都围着那位富有的顾客转。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ sequ（跟随）+ ious → 紧紧跟随巴结 → 谄媚的

### 15. **surreptitious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：偷偷摸摸的；秘密的；暗中进行的
- **英文释义**：kept secret, especially because it would not be approved of
- **例句**：
  - She took a surreptitious glance at her phone during the meeting.
  - 她在会议期间偷偷瞥了一眼手机。
- **助记**：sur（在下面）+ rept（爬）+ itious → 在下面偷偷爬行 → 偷偷摸摸的
