## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **wary** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：谨慎的；警惕的；提防的
- **英文释义**：feeling or showing caution about possible dangers or problems
- **例句**：
  - Tourists should be wary of pickpockets in crowded markets.
  - 游客在拥挤市场里应提防扒手。
  - After being cheated once, she grew wary of online deals that seemed too good.
  - 被骗一次后，她对看似好得离谱的网上交易变得谨慎。
- **助记**：war（注视）+ y → 警惕注视 → 谨慎的

### 2. **whimsical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：异想天开的；反复无常的；古怪有趣的
- **英文释义**：playfully quaint or fanciful, especially in an appealing way
- **例句**：
  - The garden was filled with whimsical statues of animals wearing hats.
  - 花园里满是戴着帽子的动物造型异想天开的雕像。
  - Her whimsical sense of humor made dull meetings more bearable.
  - 她古怪有趣的幽默感让沉闷的会议好熬一些。
- **助记**：whim（突发奇想）+ sical → 随兴所至的 → 异想天开的

### 3. **yield** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：产生；屈服；让路；产量
- **英文释义**：to produce or provide; to give way; an amount produced
- **例句**：
  - This experimental plot yields more wheat per hectare than traditional varieties.
  - 这块试验田每公顷产量高于传统品种。
  - The driver failed to yield at the intersection and caused a collision.
  - 司机在路口未让行，导致碰撞。
- **助记**：yield 原义“付出”→ 产出/让步

### 4. **zeal** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：热情；热忱；狂热
- **英文释义**：great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause or objective
- **例句**：
  - Young volunteers worked with zeal to clean up the polluted riverbank.
  - 年轻志愿者满怀热情地清理受污染的河岸。
  - His religious zeal sometimes made calm discussion difficult.
  - 他的宗教热忱有时使平静讨论变得困难。
- **助记**：zeal 与热情、热心相关 → 热忱

### 5. **aberration** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：反常；偏差；失常行为
- **英文释义**：a departure from what is normal, usual, or expected
- **例句**：
  - The warm winter was an aberration caused by a strong El Niño event.
  - 暖冬是强厄尔尼诺现象造成的反常天气。
  - One rude comment was an aberration in an otherwise polite debate.
  - 除了一句粗鲁话，整场辩论 otherwise 都很有礼貌。
- **助记**：ab（离开）+ err（漫游）→ 偏离正轨 → 反常

### 6. **boisterous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：喧闹的；精力充沛的；狂风大作的
- **英文释义**：noisy, energetic, and cheerful; rough and stormy
- **例句**：
  - Boisterous children ran through the playground shouting and laughing.
  - 喧闹的孩子在操场上又喊又笑地奔跑。
  - The boisterous waves made sailing dangerous that afternoon.
  - 那天下午波涛汹涌，航行很危险。
- **助记**：boist（猛推）+ erous → 推搡喧哗 → 喧闹的

### 7. **clemency** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：仁慈；宽大；减刑
- **英文释义**：mercy; lenience, especially toward an offender
- **例句**：
  - The governor granted clemency to several prisoners serving life sentences.
  - 州长对几名无期徒刑犯人予以减刑宽大。
  - The judge showed clemency because the defendant was a first-time offender.
  - 法官因被告系初犯而予以宽大。
- **助记**：clem（温和）→ 温和处理 → 仁慈宽大

### 8. **effervescent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：冒泡的；活泼开朗的；兴奋的
- **英文释义**：giving off bubbles; vivacious and enthusiastic
- **例句**：
  - Sparkling water is effervescent due to dissolved carbon dioxide.
  - 气泡水因溶有二氧化碳而冒泡。
  - Her effervescent personality lit up every classroom she entered.
  - 她活泼开朗的性格让她走进的每间教室都亮了起来。
- **助记**：ef（出）+ ferv（沸）→ 沸腾冒出 → 冒泡/活泼

### 9. **fortitude** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：坚韧；刚毅；不屈不挠
- **英文释义**：courage in pain or adversity; strength of mind
- **例句**：
  - She faced chemotherapy with remarkable fortitude and never complained.
  - 她以非凡的坚韧面对化疗，从不抱怨。
  - The explorers needed fortitude to survive the polar winter.
  - 探险者需要刚毅才能在极地严冬中生存。
- **助记**：fort（强）+ itude → 坚强状态 → 坚韧

### 10. **grapple** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：扭打；努力解决；抓牢
- **英文释义**：to engage in a close fight; to struggle to deal with something difficult
- **例句**：
  - The wrestlers grappled on the mat until one was pinned.
  - 摔跤手在垫子上扭打直到一方被压制。
  - Policymakers must grapple with rising housing costs in major cities.
  - 政策制定者必须努力解决大城市房价飙升问题。
- **助记**：grapple 与 grab（抓）相关 → 扭打/抓住问题

### 高一词汇

### 11. **gregarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：群居的；爱交际的；合群的
- **英文释义**：fond of company; sociable; living in flocks or herds
- **例句**：
  - Elephants are gregarious animals that travel in large family groups.
  - 大象是群居动物，以大族群迁徙。
  - Gregarious people often recharge by spending time with friends rather than alone.
  - 爱交际的人常通过与朋友相处而非独处来恢复精力。
- **助记**：greg（群体）+ arious → 爱扎堆的 → 合群的

### 12. **hegemony** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：霸权；领导权；支配地位
- **英文释义**：leadership or dominance, especially by one state or social group over others
- **例句**：
  - Cultural critics debated American hegemony in global cinema.
  - 文化评论家争论美国在全球电影中的霸权地位。
  - The empire maintained its hegemony through military and economic pressure.
  - 帝国通过军事与经济压力维持霸权。
- **助记**：heg（引导）+ emony → 领导支配 → 霸权

### 13. **immutable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可改变的；永恒的
- **英文释义**：unchanging over time or unable to be changed
- **例句**：
  - Mathematical truths are often regarded as immutable.
  - 数学真理常被视为永恒不变。
  - The constitution establishes certain immutable rights for citizens.
  - 宪法确立了公民的某些不可剥夺权利。
- **助记**：im（不）+ mut（变化）+ able → 不可变

### 14. **juxtaposition** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：并置；并列；对照
- **英文释义**：the fact of two things being seen or placed close together with contrasting effect
- **例句**：
  - The juxtaposition of poverty and luxury in the city shocked foreign visitors.
  - 城市中贫穷与奢华的并置让外国游客震惊。
  - The artist's fame rests on the bold juxtaposition of colors and textures.
  - 这位艺术家的名声建立在色彩与质感的大胆并置上。
- **助记**：juxtapose 的名词形式 → 并置

### 15. **kinetic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：运动的；动力学的；活跃的
- **英文释义**：relating to or resulting from motion
- **例句**：
  - The sculpture included kinetic parts that moved in the wind.
  - 雕塑包含随风运动的动态部件。
  - Physics students calculated the kinetic energy of the rolling ball.
  - 物理系学生计算滚动小球的动能。
- **助记**：kinet（运动）+ ic → 运动的
