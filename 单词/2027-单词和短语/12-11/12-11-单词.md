## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **vilify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：诋毁；丑化；恶意中伤
- **英文释义**：to speak or write about in an abusively disparaging manner
- **例句**：
  - The media vilified the politician after the corruption scandal was exposed.
  - 腐败丑闻曝光后，媒体对这位政治家进行了诋毁。
  - It is wrong to vilify an entire group of people based on the actions of a few.
  - 因为少数人的行为而诋毁整个群体是不对的。
- **助记**：vil（卑鄙）+ ify → 使变得卑鄙 → 诋毁

### 2. **fastidious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；挑剔的；难以取悦的
- **英文释义**：very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail
- **例句**：
  - The fastidious editor caught every typo and grammatical error in the manuscript.
  - 一丝不苟的编辑捕捉到了手稿中的每一个错字和语法错误。
  - She was fastidious about cleanliness, wiping down every surface twice a day.
  - 她对清洁非常挑剔，每天擦拭所有表面两次。
- **助记**：fastidi（嫌恶）+ ous → 对不完美感到嫌恶 → 挑剔的

### 3. **palliate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：缓和；减轻（痛苦）；掩饰
- **英文释义**：to make a disease or its symptoms less severe without removing the cause; to disguise the seriousness of
- **例句**：
  - The medication was designed to palliate the patient's chronic pain, not to cure the underlying condition.
  - 这种药物旨在缓和患者的慢性疼痛，而非治愈根本病症。
  - His apology did little to palliate the damage caused by his thoughtless remarks.
  - 他的道歉几乎没有减轻他轻率言论造成的伤害。
- **助记**：palli（斗篷/覆盖）+ ate → 用斗篷覆盖 → 缓和/掩饰

### 4. **sardonic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：嘲讽的；讥笑的；冷嘲热讽的
- **英文释义**：grimly mocking or cynical
- **例句**：
  - His sardonic smile suggested he knew the plan would fail long before anyone else.
  - 他嘲讽的微笑暗示他早在其他人之前就知道计划会失败。
  - The critic's sardonic review tore apart every aspect of the poorly made film.
  - 评论家冷嘲热讽的评论撕碎了这部烂片的每一个方面。
- **助记**：sardon（一种据说吃了会导致苦笑的植物）+ ic → 苦笑的 → 嘲讽的

### 5. **inundate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：淹没；泛滥；使应接不暇
- **英文释义**：to overwhelm with things or people to be dealt with; to flood
- **例句**：
  - After the celebrity endorsed the product, the company was inundated with orders.
  - 名人代言产品后，公司被订单淹没了。
  - Heavy rains inundated the low-lying areas of the city, causing widespread flooding.
  - 暴雨淹没了城市的低洼地区，造成大面积洪水。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ und（波浪）+ ate → 波浪涌入 → 淹没

### 6. **pugnacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；好争论的；爱挑衅的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue, quarrel, or fight
- **例句**：
  - The pugnacious boxer was known for provoking his opponents before matches.
  - 好斗的拳击手以在比赛前挑衅对手而闻名。
  - Her pugnacious attitude in debates made her a feared opponent.
  - 她在辩论中好斗的态度使她成为令人畏惧的对手。
- **助记**：pugn（战斗）+ acious → 爱战斗的 → 好斗的

### 7. **rescind** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：废除；取消；撤回
- **英文释义**：to revoke, cancel, or repeal a law, order, or agreement
- **例句**：
  - The university rescinded the student's scholarship after discovering the application contained false information.
  - 大学在发现申请包含虚假信息后撤回了学生的奖学金。
  - The contract was rescinded by mutual agreement of both parties.
  - 合同经双方共同协议被取消。
- **助记**：re（回）+ scind（切）→ 切回去 → 取消/废除

### 8. **ebullient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的；充满活力的
- **英文释义**：cheerful and full of energy
- **例句**：
  - The ebullient crowd cheered wildly as the team scored the winning goal.
  - 球队打入致胜球时，热情洋溢的人群疯狂欢呼。
  - Her ebullient personality made her the life of every party.
  - 她热情洋溢的个性使她成为每个派对的灵魂。
- **助记**：e（出）+ bull（沸腾）+ ient → 沸腾涌出 → 热情洋溢的

### 9. **confiscate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：没收；充公；查抄
- **英文释义**：to take or seize someone's property with authority
- **例句**：
  - The teacher confiscated the student's phone after catching him texting during class.
  - 老师在抓到学生上课发短信后没收了他的手机。
  - Customs officers confiscated the illegal goods at the border checkpoint.
  - 海关官员在边境检查站查抄了违禁物品。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ fisc（国库）+ ate → 收入国库 → 没收

### 10. **austere** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严肃的；朴素的；苦行的
- **英文释义**：severe or strict in manner or appearance; having no comforts or luxuries
- **例句**：
  - The austere monk lived in a tiny cell with nothing but a bed and a Bible.
  - 苦行的僧人住在一间只有一张床和一本圣经的小屋里。
  - The office had an austere atmosphere, with bare walls and minimal furniture.
  - 办公室有一种朴素的氛围，光秃秃的墙壁和极少的家具。
- **助记**：auster（干燥/严格）+ e → 干燥严格的 → 严肃朴素的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **chrysalism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：在风雨中待在室内的宁静安适感
- **英文释义**：the amniotic tranquility of being indoors during a thunderstorm
- **例句**：
  - There's a deep sense of chrysalism in curling up with a book while rain hammers against the window.
  - 当雨点敲打窗户时蜷缩着看书，有一种深深的安适感。
  - She cherished the chrysalism of stormy evenings, feeling safe and warm inside her home.
  - 她珍惜暴风雨之夜的宁静安适感，在家中感到安全温暖。
- **助记**：chrysalis（蛹/茧）+ m → 像在茧中一样的安全宁静感

### 12. **fernweh** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：远方之渴；对未曾去过之地的思念
- **英文释义**：an ache for distant places; a craving for travel; the opposite of homesickness
- **例句**：
  - The German word fernweh describes the longing she felt for places she had never visited.
  - 德语词 fernweh 描述了她对从未去过的地方的渴望。
  - Scrolling through travel photos on social media intensified his fernweh.
  - 在社交媒体上翻看旅行照片加剧了他对远方的渴望。
- **助记**：fern（远方）+ weh（痛苦）→ 因远方而痛苦 → 对远方的渴望（与 Heimweh 乡愁相反）

### 13. **meraki** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：全身心投入；将灵魂注入所做之事
- **英文释义**：the soul, creativity, or love put into something; doing something with passion and devotion
- **例句**：
  - The Greek word meraki describes the passion with which the chef prepared every dish.
  - 希腊语词 meraki 描述了厨师准备每道菜时的热忱。
  - You can taste the meraki in her homemade bread — every loaf is made with love and care.
  - 你能从她自制的面包中品尝到全身心的投入——每一条面包都是用爱和心意制作的。
- **助记**：meraki 是希腊语，表达一种将自我完全融入工作中的状态

### 14. **ubuntu** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：人性之美；因他人而存在（非洲哲学概念）
- **英文释义**：a quality that includes the essential human virtues of compassion and humanity; "I am because we are"
- **例句**：
  - The African philosophy of ubuntu emphasizes that our humanity is bound up with others.
  - 非洲的 ubuntu 哲学强调我们的人性与他人息息相关。
  - Ubuntu teaches that a person is a person through other people — we exist in relationship.
  - Ubuntu 教导我们，人因他人而成为人——我们在关系中存在。
- **助记**：ubuntu 是祖鲁语/科萨语，核心理念是"我的存在因为我们的存在"

### 15. **wabi-sabi** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：侘寂（欣赏不完美、无常和不完整之美的日本美学）
- **英文释义**：a Japanese aesthetic centered on the acceptance of transience and imperfection; finding beauty in imperfection
- **例句**：
  - The cracked pottery, repaired with gold in the kintsugi tradition, embodies the spirit of wabi-sabi.
  - 用金缮工艺修复的裂缝陶器体现了侘寂的精神。
  - Wabi-sabi teaches us to find beauty in the weathered, the aged, and the imperfect.
  - 侘寂教导我们在风化的、陈旧的和不完美的事物中发现美。
- **助记**：wabi（简朴之美）+ sabi（岁月之美）→ 在简朴和时间流逝中找到美 → 侘寂
