## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **alleviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓解；缓和
- **英文释义**：to make suffering, a problem, or a bad situation less severe
- **例句**：
  - The new medication helped alleviate the patient's chronic pain.
  - 新药物帮助减轻了患者的慢性疼痛。
  - Planting more trees can alleviate the effects of urban heat islands.
  - 种植更多的树木可以缓解城市热岛效应。
- **助记**：al（加强）+ levi（轻）+ ate → 使变轻 → 减轻/缓解

### 2. **concede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：承认；让步；认输
- **英文释义**：to admit that something is true after first resisting; to surrender or yield
- **例句**：
  - After hours of debate, he finally conceded that his opponent had a valid point.
  - 经过数小时的辩论，他终于承认对手的观点是有道理的。
  - The candidate conceded the election after the final votes were counted.
  - 在最后的选票统计完毕后，候选人承认选举失败。
- **助记**：con（完全）+ cede（让步/走）→ 完全让步 → 承认/认输

### 3. **detrimental** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有害的；不利的
- **英文释义**：tending to cause harm; damaging
- **例句**：
  - Excessive screen time can be detrimental to children's eyesight and sleep quality.
  - 过多的屏幕使用时间可能对儿童的视力和睡眠质量有害。
  - Pollution has had a detrimental effect on the local ecosystem.
  - 污染对当地生态系统产生了不利影响。
- **助记**：detri（磨损）+ mental → 造成磨损的 → 有害的

### 4. **eradicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：根除；消灭；铲除
- **英文释义**：to destroy completely; to put an end to
- **例句**：
  - The World Health Organization aims to eradicate malaria in developing countries.
  - 世界卫生组织旨在消灭发展中国家的疟疾。
  - New farming techniques have helped eradicate many common crop diseases.
  - 新的耕作技术帮助根除了许多常见的农作物病害。
- **助记**：e（出）+ radic（根）+ ate → 连根拔出 → 根除

### 5. **fluctuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：波动；起伏；变动
- **英文释义**：to rise and fall irregularly in number or amount
- **例句**：
  - Oil prices fluctuate depending on global supply and demand.
  - 石油价格根据全球供需情况波动。
  - Her mood tends to fluctuate throughout the day, especially when she is under stress.
  - 她的情绪往往一天之中起伏不定，尤其是在有压力的时候。
- **助记**：fluct（流动）+ uate → 像水流一样上下流动 → 波动

### 6. **hypocrisy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：虚伪；伪善；伪装
- **英文释义**：the practice of claiming to have moral standards to which one's behavior does not conform
- **例句**：
  - It is hypocrisy to preach about environmental protection while driving a gas-guzzling car.
  - 一边宣扬环保一边开着油耗巨大的汽车，这是虚伪的。
  - The politician was accused of hypocrisy for supporting tax cuts while criticizing wealth inequality.
  - 这位政治家因支持减税同时批评贫富不均而被指控伪善。
- **助记**：hypo（在……之下）+ crisy（判断/演戏）→ 在面具之下演戏 → 虚伪

### 7. **lethal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：致命的；致死的；极其有害的
- **英文释义**：sufficient to cause death; extremely dangerous
- **例句**：
  - The bite of this snake is lethal if not treated within two hours.
  - 如果不在两小时内治疗，这种蛇的咬伤是致命的。
  - A lethal combination of speed and icy roads caused the accident.
  - 高速和结冰路面的致命组合导致了这起事故。
- **助记**：leth（死亡，如 Lethe 忘河）+ al → 与死亡相关的 → 致命的

### 8. **obscure** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：模糊的；晦涩的；遮蔽；使模糊
- **英文释义**：not discovered or known about; to keep from being seen or to make unclear
- **例句**：
  - The meaning of the ancient text remains obscure to most scholars.
  - 这篇古代文本的含义对大多数学者来说仍然晦涩难懂。
  - Thick clouds obscured the view of the mountain peak.
  - 浓密的云层遮蔽了山峰的景色。
- **助记**：ob（覆盖）+ scure（暗）→ 被覆盖变暗 → 模糊的/遮蔽

### 9. **prevalent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：流行的；普遍的；盛行的
- **英文释义**：widespread in a particular area or at a particular time
- **例句**：
  - Allergies are particularly prevalent during the spring season.
  - 过敏在春季尤为普遍。
  - The use of smartphones has become increasingly prevalent among young people.
  - 智能手机的使用在年轻人中越来越普遍。
- **助记**：pre（前）+ val（强大）+ ent → 力量在前面占据的 → 流行的/普遍的

### 10. **scrutinize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：仔细检查；审视；细察
- **英文释义**：to examine or inspect closely and thoroughly
- **例句**：
  - The accountant scrutinized every line of the financial report for errors.
  - 会计仔细检查了财务报告的每一行以查找错误。
  - Voters have the right to scrutinize the policies of political candidates.
  - 选民有权审视政治候选人的政策。
- **助记**：scrut（检查）+ inize → 反复检查 → 仔细审视

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acrimonious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：尖刻的；激烈的；充满敌意的
- **英文释义**：angry and bitter, especially in speech or debate
- **例句**：
  - The divorce proceedings became increasingly acrimonious as both sides fought over custody.
  - 随着双方争夺抚养权，离婚程序变得越来越充满敌意。
  - An acrimonious debate erupted in parliament over the proposed budget cuts.
  - 议会中因预算削减提案爆发了激烈的辩论。
- **助记**：acri（尖锐/苦）+ monious → 言辞尖锐的 → 尖刻的

### 12. **convoluted** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：复杂的；费解的；迂回的
- **英文释义**：extremely complex and difficult to follow; intricately folded or twisted
- **例句**：
  - The plot of the movie was so convoluted that most viewers couldn't follow it.
  - 电影的情节如此复杂，大多数观众都看不懂。
  - The company's convoluted organizational structure made decision-making slow and inefficient.
  - 公司复杂的组织架构使得决策缓慢且低效。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ volut（卷/转）+ ed → 卷在一起的 → 复杂的/费解的

### 13. **futile** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：徒劳的；无用的；无效的
- **英文释义**：incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
- **例句**：
  - The rescue team made a futile attempt to reach the survivors before nightfall.
  - 救援队在天黑前徒劳地试图到达幸存者身边。
  - It would be futile to try to change his mind once he has made a decision.
  - 一旦他做了决定，试图改变他的想法是徒劳的。
- **助记**：fut（流/倒）+ ile → 像水一样白白流走 → 徒劳的

### 14. **insidious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：暗中为害的；潜伏的；阴险的
- **英文释义**：proceeding in a gradual, subtle way, but with harmful effects
- **例句**：
  - The insidious effects of air pollution on public health are often underestimated.
  - 空气污染对公共健康的潜在危害经常被低估。
  - Propaganda can be insidious, slowly shaping people's beliefs without them realizing it.
  - 宣传可以是阴险的，在人们不知不觉中慢慢塑造他们的信念。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ sid（坐/埋伏）+ ious → 潜入内部埋伏 → 暗中为害的

### 15. **paradox** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：悖论；矛盾的事物；自相矛盾
- **英文释义**：a statement or situation that seems contradictory but may be true
- **例句**：
  - It is a paradox that the richest country in the world also has some of the poorest communities.
  - 世界上最富有的国家同时也有一些最贫穷的社区，这是一个悖论。
  - The paradox of choice suggests that having too many options can actually make people less happy.
  - 选择悖论表明，拥有太多选项实际上会让人们更不快乐。
- **助记**：para（旁边/超越）+ dox（观点）→ 超越常规观点 → 悖论
