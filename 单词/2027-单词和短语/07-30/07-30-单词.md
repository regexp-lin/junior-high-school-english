## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **acclaim** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：称赞；赞扬；高度评价
- **英文释义**：to praise enthusiastically and publicly; enthusiastic approval or praise
- **例句**：
  - The film was widely acclaimed by critics for its stunning visual effects.
  - 这部电影因其惊人的视觉效果受到影评人的广泛赞誉。
  - Her latest novel has won international acclaim and several literary awards.
  - 她的最新小说赢得了国际赞誉和多个文学奖项。
- **助记**：ac（加强）+ claim（呼喊）→ 大声叫好 → 称赞

### 2. **baffle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使困惑；使迷惑；难住
- **英文释义**：to confuse or perplex someone completely
- **例句**：
  - The detective was baffled by the lack of evidence at the crime scene.
  - 侦探对犯罪现场缺乏证据感到困惑。
  - The sudden disappearance of the ship baffled scientists for decades.
  - 这艘船的突然消失让科学家们困惑了数十年。
- **助记**：联想 "battle（战斗）"——面对难题如同打仗般令人困惑

### 3. **conspire** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：密谋；阴谋策划；共同导致
- **英文释义**：to secretly plan with others to do something harmful or illegal
- **例句**：
  - The rebels conspired to overthrow the government in a midnight meeting.
  - 叛军在午夜会议上密谋推翻政府。
  - Several factors conspired to make the project a complete failure.
  - 几个因素共同导致了该项目的彻底失败。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ spire（呼吸）→ 凑在一起悄悄说话 → 密谋

### 4. **deplore** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：谴责；痛惜；对……深感遗憾
- **英文释义**：to feel or express strong disapproval of something
- **例句**：
  - The international community deplored the use of chemical weapons against civilians.
  - 国际社会谴责对平民使用化学武器。
  - She deplored the decline of traditional craftsmanship in modern society.
  - 她对现代社会传统工艺的衰落深感惋惜。
- **助记**：de（加强）+ plore（哭泣，如 implore）→ 痛哭 → 痛惜、谴责

### 5. **erratic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不稳定的；不规律的；反复无常的
- **英文释义**：not even or regular in pattern or movement; unpredictable
- **例句**：
  - His erratic behavior worried his friends and family.
  - 他反复无常的行为让朋友和家人感到担忧。
  - The stock market has been erratic due to political uncertainty.
  - 由于政治不确定性，股市一直不稳定。
- **助记**：err（错误/偏离）+ atic → 偏离正轨的 → 不稳定的

### 6. **hamper** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：妨碍；阻碍；束缚
- **英文释义**：to prevent someone from easily doing or achieving something
- **例句**：
  - Heavy rain hampered rescue efforts after the earthquake.
  - 大雨阻碍了地震后的救援工作。
  - Lack of funding has hampered the development of renewable energy projects.
  - 资金不足阻碍了可再生能源项目的发展。
- **助记**：hamper 本意是"大篮子"——被大篮子绊住 → 妨碍

### 7. **kindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：点燃；激起；唤起
- **英文释义**：to light a fire; to arouse or inspire an emotion or feeling
- **例句**：
  - The campfire was kindled with dry twigs and leaves.
  - 营火是用干树枝和树叶点燃的。
  - The teacher's inspiring words kindled a passion for science in the students.
  - 老师鼓舞人心的话语激起了学生们对科学的热情。
- **助记**：联想 Kindle 电子书阅读器——点燃阅读热情

### 8. **meander** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：蜿蜒；漫步；漫无目的地走
- **英文释义**：to follow a winding course; to wander aimlessly
- **例句**：
  - The river meanders through the lush green valley.
  - 河流蜿蜒穿过郁郁葱葱的绿色山谷。
  - We meandered through the old town, stopping at little shops along the way.
  - 我们在老城区漫步，沿途在小店驻足。
- **助记**：源自希腊河流 Meander（曲折河）→ 蜿蜒流动

### 9. **pristine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：原始的；崭新的；未受污染的
- **英文释义**：in its original condition; unspoilt; extremely clean
- **例句**：
  - The national park preserved the pristine beauty of the ancient forest.
  - 国家公园保存了古老森林的原始之美。
  - She kept her car in pristine condition, washing it every weekend.
  - 她把车保持得崭新如初，每个周末都洗车。
- **助记**：prist（原始的，如 primitive）+ ine → 原始状态的

### 10. **saturate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使饱和；使湿透；充斥
- **英文释义**：to soak thoroughly; to fill something completely so no more can be absorbed
- **例句**：
  - The heavy downpour saturated the ground within minutes.
  - 暴雨在几分钟内就使地面湿透了。
  - The market is saturated with cheap imitation products.
  - 市场上充斥着廉价的仿制品。
- **助记**：satur（满的，如 satisfy）+ ate → 使充满 → 使饱和

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acquiesce** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：默许；勉强同意；默认
- **英文释义**：to accept something reluctantly but without protest
- **例句**：
  - After hours of negotiation, the board finally acquiesced to the workers' demands.
  - 经过数小时的谈判，董事会终于默许了工人们的要求。
  - She acquiesced in her parents' decision, though she disagreed with it.
  - 她虽然不同意，但还是默认了父母的决定。
- **助记**：ac（加强）+ quiesce（安静）→ 安静地接受 → 默许

### 12. **ebullient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的
- **英文释义**：cheerful and full of energy; enthusiastic
- **例句**：
  - The ebullient crowd cheered wildly as their team scored the winning goal.
  - 当球队打进制胜球时，热情洋溢的观众疯狂欢呼。
  - His ebullient personality made him the life of every party.
  - 他热情洋溢的性格使他成为每场聚会的灵魂人物。
- **助记**：e（出）+ bull（沸腾）+ ient → 沸腾而出 → 热情洋溢

### 13. **magnanimous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；慷慨的
- **英文释义**：very generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or enemy
- **例句**：
  - The magnanimous winner congratulated his opponent and praised his effort.
  - 宽宏大量的获胜者恭喜了对手并赞扬了他的努力。
  - It was magnanimous of her to forgive someone who had wronged her so deeply.
  - 她原谅了一个如此深深伤害她的人，真是宽宏大量。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 心胸宽大的 → 宽宏大量的

### 14. **propensity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：倾向；癖好；习性
- **英文释义**：an inclination or natural tendency to behave in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - He has a propensity for taking risks that sometimes leads to trouble.
  - 他有冒险的倾向，这有时会惹来麻烦。
  - Studies show that children have a natural propensity to learn languages quickly.
  - 研究表明，孩子们有快速学习语言的天生倾向。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ pens（悬挂/倾斜）+ ity → 向前倾斜 → 倾向

### 15. **verbose** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：冗长的；啰嗦的
- **英文释义**：using or expressed in more words than are needed
- **例句**：
  - The professor's verbose lectures often put students to sleep.
  - 教授冗长的讲座经常让学生们昏昏欲睡。
  - Try to make your essay concise rather than verbose — quality matters more than quantity.
  - 尽量让你的文章简洁而不啰嗦——质量比数量更重要。
- **助记**：verb（词语）+ ose（多的）→ 用词过多的 → 冗长的
