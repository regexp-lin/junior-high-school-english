## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **amend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：修正；修改；改进
- **英文释义**：to make minor changes to a text or piece of legislation to improve it
- **例句**：
  - The committee voted to amend the proposal before submitting it to the board.
  - 委员会投票决定在提交给董事会之前修改该提案。
  - The constitution has been amended several times since it was first written.
  - 宪法自首次起草以来已经修改了好几次。
- **助记**：a + mend（修补）→ 去修补 → 修正/修改

### 2. **cumbersome** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：笨重的；累赘的；复杂的
- **英文释义**：large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry or use; unwieldy
- **例句**：
  - The old computer system was cumbersome and slow compared to the new one.
  - 与新系统相比，旧的计算机系统笨重而缓慢。
  - Wearing a space suit is cumbersome, but necessary for survival in outer space.
  - 穿太空服很笨重，但在太空中生存是必要的。
- **助记**：cumber（妨碍/负担）+ some → 带来负担的 → 笨重的/累赘的

### 3. **deteriorate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：恶化；变坏；退化
- **英文释义**：to become progressively worse in quality or condition
- **例句**：
  - The patient's condition deteriorated rapidly after the surgery.
  - 手术后病人的状况迅速恶化。
  - Relations between the two countries have continued to deteriorate over the past year.
  - 两国关系在过去一年中持续恶化。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ terior（更差的）+ ate → 变得更差 → 恶化

### 4. **exemplify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：例证；是……的典范；举例说明
- **英文释义**：to serve as a typical example of; to illustrate by giving an example
- **例句**：
  - Her dedication and hard work exemplify the spirit of the company.
  - 她的奉献精神和努力工作体现了公司的精神。
  - This case exemplifies the challenges faced by many developing nations.
  - 这个案例说明了许多发展中国家面临的挑战。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ empl（范例）+ ify → 拿出来做范例 → 例证/举例说明

### 5. **frantic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：疯狂的；狂乱的；急迫的
- **英文释义**：wild or distraught with fear, anxiety, or other emotion
- **例句**：
  - The mother made frantic efforts to find her missing child in the crowded mall.
  - 这位母亲在拥挤的商场里疯狂地寻找她走失的孩子。
  - There was a frantic rush to leave the building when the fire alarm went off.
  - 火灾警报响起时，人们疯狂地冲出大楼。
- **助记**：frantic 联想 frenzied（疯狂的）→ 极度焦虑不安的 → 疯狂的

### 6. **hasten** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加速；催促；赶紧
- **英文释义**：to be quick to do something; to cause something to happen sooner
- **例句**：
  - The warm weather hastened the blooming of the cherry trees this year.
  - 温暖的天气加速了今年樱花的开放。
  - She hastened to add that her comments were not meant to be critical.
  - 她赶紧补充说她的评论并非出于批评。
- **助记**：haste（匆忙）+ n → 使匆忙 → 加速/催促

### 7. **jeopardize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：危及；损害；使处于危险之中
- **英文释义**：to put something or someone into a situation in which there is danger of loss or harm
- **例句**：
  - Revealing confidential information could jeopardize the entire investigation.
  - 泄露机密信息可能危及整个调查。
  - His reckless behavior jeopardized not only his own safety but that of others.
  - 他鲁莽的行为不仅危及了自己的安全，还危及了他人。
- **助记**：jeopard（危险）+ ize → 使处于危险中 → 危及

### 8. **meander** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：蜿蜒；漫步；闲逛
- **英文释义**：to follow a winding course; to wander aimlessly
- **例句**：
  - The river meanders through the lush green valley before reaching the sea.
  - 河流蜿蜒流过茂密的绿色山谷，最后汇入大海。
  - We spent the afternoon meandering through the old town, admiring the architecture.
  - 我们花了一个下午在老城区漫步，欣赏建筑。
- **助记**：Meander 源自土耳其的蜿蜒曲折的 Menderes 河 → 蜿蜒/漫步

### 9. **pacify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：安抚；平息；使平静
- **英文释义**：to bring to a state of peace or quiet; to calm down
- **例句**：
  - The mother sang a lullaby to pacify her crying baby.
  - 母亲唱了一首摇篮曲来安抚哭泣的婴儿。
  - The government attempted to pacify the angry protesters by promising reforms.
  - 政府试图通过承诺改革来平息愤怒的抗议者。
- **助记**：paci（和平，如 peace）+ fy → 使和平 → 安抚/平息

### 10. **substantiate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证实；证明；使具体化
- **英文释义**：to provide evidence to support or prove the truth of something
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer asked the witness to substantiate her claims with concrete evidence.
  - 律师要求证人用具体证据证实她的说法。
  - The research findings substantiated the theory that exercise improves mental health.
  - 研究结果证实了运动改善心理健康的理论。
- **助记**：sub（在下面）+ stant（站立）+ iate → 在下面站稳提供支撑 → 证实

### 高一词汇

### 11. **capitulate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：投降；屈服；让步
- **英文释义**：to cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; to surrender
- **例句**：
  - After months of siege, the city finally capitulated to the enemy forces.
  - 经过数月的围困，这座城市最终向敌军投降。
  - The company capitulated to public pressure and withdrew the controversial product.
  - 公司屈服于公众压力，撤回了有争议的产品。
- **助记**：capit（头）+ ulate → 低下头 → 投降/屈服

### 12. **dilapidated** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：破旧的；年久失修的；摇摇欲坠的
- **英文释义**：in a state of disrepair or ruin as a result of age or neglect
- **例句**：
  - The dilapidated old house on the hill has been abandoned for over thirty years.
  - 山上那座破旧的老房子已经被废弃了三十多年。
  - The city plans to demolish several dilapidated buildings in the downtown area.
  - 该市计划拆除市中心几座年久失修的建筑。
- **助记**：di（分散）+ lapid（石头）+ ated → 石头散落 → 破旧的/摇摇欲坠的

### 13. **impeccable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无可挑剔的；完美的；无瑕的
- **英文释义**：in accordance with the highest standards of propriety; faultless
- **例句**：
  - Her impeccable taste in fashion made her the envy of all her friends.
  - 她无可挑剔的时尚品味让所有朋友都很羡慕。
  - The hotel is known for its impeccable service and attention to detail.
  - 这家酒店以其无可挑剔的服务和对细节的关注而闻名。
- **助记**：im（不）+ pecc（罪/过错）+ able → 没有过错的 → 无可挑剔的

### 14. **recalcitrant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：桀骜不驯的；难以管教的；顽抗的
- **英文释义**：having an obstinately uncooperative attitude towards authority
- **例句**：
  - The recalcitrant student refused to follow the school's rules despite repeated warnings.
  - 这个桀骜不驯的学生尽管多次被警告，仍拒绝遵守学校的规则。
  - Dealing with recalcitrant employees requires both firmness and patience.
  - 管理桀骜不驯的员工需要坚定和耐心。
- **助记**：re（反）+ calcitrant（踢）→ 回踢抵抗 → 桀骜不驯的

### 15. **whimsical** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：异想天开的；古怪的；变幻莫测的
- **英文释义**：playfully quaint or fanciful; acting or behaving in a capricious manner
- **例句**：
  - The artist is known for her whimsical paintings of fantastical creatures.
  - 这位艺术家以她描绘奇幻生物的异想天开的画作而闻名。
  - He made a whimsical decision to quit his job and travel around the world.
  - 他做了一个异想天开的决定，辞去工作环游世界。
- **助记**：whim（突发奇想）+ sical → 充满奇想的 → 异想天开的
