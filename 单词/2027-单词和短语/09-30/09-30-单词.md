## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **obsolete** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过时的；废弃的；已淘汰的
- **英文释义**：no longer produced or used; out of date
- **例句**：
  - Floppy disks are now obsolete in most offices.
  - 软盘在大多数办公室已遭淘汰。
  - The law became obsolete and was finally repealed.
  - 该法律已过时，最终被废除。
- **助记**：ob（对立）+ sol（习惯）→ 与习惯相悖 → 过时

### 2. **pervasive** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；弥漫的；渗透的
- **英文释义**：spreading widely throughout an area or a group of people
- **例句**：
  - The pervasive smell of smoke lingered for days.
  - 烟味弥漫数日不散。
  - Social media’s influence is pervasive among teenagers.
  - 社交媒体对青少年的影响无处不在。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ vas（走）→ 穿遍各处 → 弥漫

### 3. **rancor** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：深仇；积怨；怨恨
- **英文释义**：bitterness or resentfulness, especially when long-standing
- **例句**：
  - Years of unfair treatment filled him with rancor.
  - 多年不公待遇使他充满怨恨。
  - The debate ended without rancor, surprisingly.
  - 辩论出人意料地没有充满敌意地结束。
- **助记**：ranc（臭、坏）→ 心里发臭 → 积怨

### 4. **sporadic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：零星的；偶发的；断断续续的
- **英文释义**：occurring at irregular intervals or only in a few places; scattered
- **例句**：
  - Sporadic rain fell throughout the afternoon.
  - 下午断断续续地下雨。
  - The disease remained sporadic and did not spread widely.
  - 该病仍呈零星发生，未广泛蔓延。
- **助记**：spor（种子）→ 散落各处 → 零星

### 5. **tenacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽强的；紧握不放的；黏着力强的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent
- **例句**：
  - Tenacious climbers reached the summit despite storms.
  - 顽强的登山者在暴风雨中仍登顶。
  - She has a tenacious memory for names and faces.
  - 她对姓名和长相记得很牢。
- **助记**：ten（握住）+ acious → 紧握不放 → 顽强

### 6. **ubiquitous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；普遍存在的
- **英文释义**：present, appearing, or found everywhere
- **例句**：
  - Smartphones are ubiquitous in modern cities.
  - 智能手机在现代城市无处不在。
  - The ubiquitous logo appeared on every product.
  - 这个随处可见的标志出现在每件产品上。
- **助记**：ubi（哪里）+ quit（去）→ 无论去哪都有 → 无处不在

### 7. **volatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易变的；易挥发的；动荡的
- **英文释义**：liable to change rapidly and unpredictably; easily evaporated
- **例句**：
  - The stock market was volatile after the announcement.
  - 消息公布后股市剧烈波动。
  - Volatile chemicals must be stored carefully.
  - 挥发性化学品须小心存放。
- **助记**：volat（飞）→ 易飞散 → 易变、挥发

### 8. **zealot** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：狂热者；偏激拥护者
- **英文释义**：a person who is fanatical and uncompromising in pursuit of their ideals
- **例句**：
  - Political zealots refused to listen to opposing views.
  - 政治狂热者拒绝听取反对意见。
  - He was a fitness zealot who exercised every day without fail.
  - 他是健身狂，每天必练。
- **助记**：zeal（热情）+ ot → 过度热情的人 → 狂热者

### 9. **penchant** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：嗜好；强烈喜好；倾向
- **英文释义**：a strong habitual liking for something
- **例句**：
  - She has a penchant for collecting vintage stamps.
  - 她有收集老邮票的嗜好。
  - His penchant for exaggeration made his stories entertaining.
  - 他爱夸张，使故事很有趣。
- **助记**：法语 pendre 悬挂 → 心挂在某物上 → 嗜好

### 10. **quiescent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：静止的；沉寂的；休眠的
- **英文释义**：in a state or period of inactivity or dormancy
- **例句**：
  - The volcano has been quiescent for decades.
  - 这座火山已沉寂数十年。
  - During winter the garden was quiescent under snow.
  - 冬日花园在雪下沉寂。
- **助记**：qui（安静）+ escent → 安静不动的 → 静止

### 高一词汇

### 11. **plethora** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：过多；过剩；大量
- **英文释义**：an excess; an amount that is more than sufficient
- **例句**：
  - The report cited a plethora of reasons for the delay.
  - 报告列举了过多拖延理由。
  - A plethora of apps made choosing one difficult.
  - 应用太多，难以选择。
- **助记**：pleth（满）→ 过满 → 过多

### 12. **quandary** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：困惑；进退两难；窘境
- **英文释义**：a state of perplexity or uncertainty over what to do in a difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - She was in a quandary about which university to choose.
  - 她在选哪所大学上左右为难。
  - The ethical quandary divided the committee.
  - 伦理困境使委员会产生分歧。
- **助记**：音近“困在里”→ 困境

### 13. **relegate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：降级；把……贬到；把……归类到
- **英文释义**：to assign an inferior rank or position to; to banish to a lower role
- **例句**：
  - He was relegated to a minor role after the scandal.
  - 丑闻后他被贬到次要岗位。
  - The issue was relegated to a footnote in the report.
  - 该问题在报告中仅被放在脚注里。
- **助记**：re（回）+ leg（送）→ 送回去 → 降级

### 14. **supplant** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：取代；排挤；篡位
- **英文释义**：to take the place of and supersede another person or thing
- **例句**：
  - Email supplanted fax in most offices.
  - 电子邮件在大多数办公室取代了传真。
  - The rival supplanted the captain as team leader.
  - 对手取代队长成为领队。
- **助记**：sub（下）+ plant（脚）→ 踩在别人脚下 → 取代

### 15. **tenuous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：脆弱的；薄弱的；纤细的
- **英文释义**：very weak or slight; insubstantial; thin
- **例句**：
  - The connection between the two events was tenuous at best.
  - 两件事之间的联系最多也只是很弱。
  - They maintained a tenuous peace along the border.
  - 他们在边境维持着脆弱的和平。
- **助记**：tenu（细）→ 细如丝 → 脆弱
