## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **aggravate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加剧；恶化；激怒
- **英文释义**：to make a problem or bad situation worse; to annoy or exasperate
- **例句**：
  - Scratching the wound will only aggravate the infection.
  - 抓挠伤口只会加剧感染。
  - His constant interruptions really aggravated the speaker during the presentation.
  - 他不断的打断真的激怒了演讲者。
- **助记**：ag（加强）+ grav（重）+ ate → 使更加沉重 → 加剧/恶化

### 2. **captivate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：迷住；使着迷；吸引
- **英文释义**：to attract and hold the interest and attention of; to charm
- **例句**：
  - The magician captivated the audience with his incredible illusions.
  - 魔术师用不可思议的幻术迷住了观众。
  - She was captivated by the beauty of the sunset over the ocean.
  - 她被海上日落的美丽所吸引。
- **助记**：capt（抓住）+ ivate → 抓住人心 → 迷住/吸引

### 3. **diligent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：勤奋的；勤勉的；用功的
- **英文释义**：having or showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties
- **例句**：
  - Diligent students who study consistently tend to achieve better results.
  - 坚持学习的勤奋学生往往能取得更好的成绩。
  - The detective conducted a diligent investigation that led to the arrest of the criminal.
  - 侦探进行了一次勤勉的调查，最终逮捕了罪犯。
- **助记**：dili（勤劳）+ gent → 勤劳的 → 勤奋的

### 4. **entail** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：需要；牵涉；使必要
- **英文释义**：to involve something as a necessary or inevitable part or consequence
- **例句**：
  - The job entails working long hours and traveling frequently.
  - 这份工作需要长时间工作和频繁出差。
  - Building a house from scratch entails a great deal of planning and investment.
  - 从零开始建造一座房子需要大量的规划和投资。
- **助记**：en（使）+ tail（尾巴/尾随）→ 后面跟着的必需之物 → 需要/牵涉

### 5. **flawed** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有缺陷的；有瑕疵的；有错误的
- **英文释义**：having or containing a fault or weakness; imperfect
- **例句**：
  - The experiment was flawed because the researchers did not control all variables.
  - 这个实验有缺陷，因为研究人员没有控制所有变量。
  - Even the most beautiful diamond can be flawed when examined under a microscope.
  - 即使是最美丽的钻石在显微镜下也可能有瑕疵。
- **助记**：flaw（缺陷）+ ed → 有缺陷的

### 6. **hostile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：敌意的；不友好的；恶劣的
- **英文释义**：unfriendly; antagonistic; opposed
- **例句**：
  - The audience was hostile to the politician's proposal and booed loudly.
  - 观众对这位政治家的提案持敌意态度，大声嘘声。
  - The desert is a hostile environment where few plants and animals can survive.
  - 沙漠是一个恶劣的环境，很少有动植物能够生存。
- **助记**：host（主人的反面/敌人）+ ile → 像敌人一样的 → 敌意的

### 7. **legitimate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：合法的；正当的；合理的
- **英文释义**：conforming to the law or to rules; justifiable
- **例句**：
  - The company must ensure that all its business activities are legitimate.
  - 公司必须确保其所有商业活动都是合法的。
  - She had a legitimate reason for being absent from work — she was in the hospital.
  - 她缺勤有正当理由——她住院了。
- **助记**：legit（合法）+ imate → 合法的/正当的

### 8. **obstruct** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻塞；妨碍；阻挡
- **英文释义**：to block or fill a passage with an obstacle; to prevent or hinder
- **例句**：
  - A fallen tree obstructed the road, causing a long traffic jam.
  - 一棵倒下的树阻塞了道路，造成了长时间的交通堵塞。
  - The suspect was charged with obstructing justice by lying to the police.
  - 嫌疑人因向警方撒谎而被指控妨碍司法公正。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ struct（建造/堆积）→ 对着堆积障碍 → 阻塞/妨碍

### 9. **profound** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：深刻的；深远的；极度的
- **英文释义**：very great or intense; having great depth of knowledge or thought
- **例句**：
  - The loss of her mother had a profound effect on her emotional well-being.
  - 母亲的去世对她的情感健康产生了深远的影响。
  - The philosopher's profound insights into human nature continue to influence modern thought.
  - 这位哲学家对人性的深刻洞察继续影响着现代思想。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ found（底部）→ 深入到底部 → 深刻的/深远的

### 10. **versatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多才多艺的；多功能的；多用途的
- **英文释义**：able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities
- **例句**：
  - She is a versatile musician who can play the piano, guitar, and violin.
  - 她是一位多才多艺的音乐家，会弹钢琴、吉他和小提琴。
  - This versatile kitchen gadget can chop, blend, and puree food.
  - 这个多功能厨房小工具可以切碎、搅拌和制成泥。
- **助记**：vers（转）+ atile → 能够灵活转换的 → 多才多艺的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acquiesce** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：默许；勉强同意；默认
- **英文释义**：to accept something reluctantly but without protest
- **例句**：
  - She acquiesced to her parents' wishes and chose to study medicine.
  - 她默许了父母的意愿，选择学医。
  - The board acquiesced to the CEO's plan despite having serious reservations.
  - 尽管有严重的保留意见，董事会还是默许了CEO的计划。
- **助记**：ac（加强）+ quiesce（安静）→ 保持安静接受 → 默许

### 12. **disparate** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：迥然不同的；完全不同的；无法比较的
- **英文释义**：essentially different in kind; not allowing comparison
- **例句**：
  - The research team brought together disparate fields such as biology, chemistry, and computer science.
  - 研究团队汇集了生物学、化学和计算机科学等迥然不同的领域。
  - Despite their disparate backgrounds, the two artists found common ground in their love of nature.
  - 尽管背景完全不同，两位艺术家在对自然的热爱中找到了共同点。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ par（平等）+ ate → 不平等/不同的 → 迥然不同的

### 13. **garrulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：话多的；唠叨的；饶舌的
- **英文释义**：excessively talkative, especially on trivial matters
- **例句**：
  - The garrulous old man could talk for hours about his childhood memories.
  - 这位话多的老人可以对他的童年回忆滔滔不绝地讲上几个小时。
  - Her garrulous nature sometimes makes it difficult for others to get a word in.
  - 她唠叨的天性有时让别人很难插嘴。
- **助记**：garr（闲聊）+ ulous → 不停闲聊的 → 话多的/唠叨的

### 14. **obsequious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：奉承的；谄媚的；卑躬屈膝的
- **英文释义**：obedient or attentive to an excessive degree; servile
- **例句**：
  - The obsequious waiter hovered around the wealthy customer's table all evening.
  - 那个谄媚的服务员整个晚上都围着富有客户的桌子转。
  - His obsequious behavior towards the boss made his colleagues uncomfortable.
  - 他对老板卑躬屈膝的行为让同事们感到不舒服。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ sequ（跟随）+ ious → 紧紧跟在后面 → 谄媚的

### 15. **truculent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；蛮横的；粗暴的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant
- **例句**：
  - The truculent customer refused to leave the store and threatened the staff.
  - 蛮横的顾客拒绝离开商店并威胁了员工。
  - His truculent attitude made it impossible to have a productive conversation.
  - 他好斗的态度使得进行有成效的对话变得不可能。
- **助记**：truc（凶猛）+ ulent → 凶猛粗暴的 → 好斗的/蛮横的
