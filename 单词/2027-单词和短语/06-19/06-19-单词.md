## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **austere** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严肃的；简朴的；苦行的
- **英文释义**：severe or strict in manner; having no comforts or luxuries
- **例句**：
  - The monastery offered an austere living environment with no modern comforts.
  - 修道院提供了一个没有现代舒适设施的简朴生活环境。
- **助记**：aus（干燥）+ tere → 干燥无味的生活 → 简朴的

### 2. **cumbersome** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：笨重的；繁琐的
- **英文释义**：large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry or use; slow and complicated
- **例句**：
  - The old regulations were cumbersome and needed to be simplified.
  - 旧规定太繁琐了，需要简化。
- **助记**：cumber（阻碍）+ some → 造成阻碍的 → 笨重的

### 3. **daunting** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：令人畏惧的；使人气馁的
- **英文释义**：seeming difficult to deal with in anticipation; intimidating
- **例句**：
  - Learning a new language can be a daunting task for many adults.
  - 对许多成年人来说，学习一门新语言可能是一项令人畏惧的任务。
- **助记**：daunt（使畏惧）+ ing → 令人畏惧的

### 4. **elicit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：引出；引起；套出
- **英文释义**：evoke or draw out (a response, answer, or fact) from someone
- **例句**：
  - The teacher's question elicited a lively discussion among the students.
  - 老师的问题引发了学生之间的热烈讨论。
- **助记**：e（出）+ licit（引诱）→ 引诱出来 → 引出

### 5. **fabricate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：捏造；编造；制造
- **英文释义**：invent or concoct (something), typically with deceitful intent; construct or manufacture
- **例句**：
  - He fabricated an excuse for being late to the meeting.
  - 他为开会迟到编造了一个借口。
- **助记**：fabric（织物）+ ate → 像织布一样编造 → 捏造

### 6. **galvanize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激励；使振奋；激起
- **英文释义**：shock or excite someone into taking action
- **例句**：
  - The crisis galvanized the community into action.
  - 这场危机激励了社区采取行动。
- **助记**：galvan（电）+ ize → 像电击一样激发 → 激励

### 7. **impasse** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：僵局；死路
- **英文释义**：a situation in which no progress is possible, especially because of disagreement
- **例句**：
  - The negotiations reached an impasse and both sides refused to compromise.
  - 谈判陷入了僵局，双方都拒绝妥协。
- **助记**：im（不）+ passe（通过）→ 无法通过 → 僵局

### 8. **juxtapose** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：并列；并置
- **英文释义**：place close together for contrasting effect
- **例句**：
  - The artist juxtaposed dark and light colors to create a dramatic effect.
  - 艺术家将深色和浅色并列以创造戏剧性效果。
- **助记**：juxta（旁边）+ pose（放置）→ 放在旁边 → 并列

### 9. **nimble** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：敏捷的；灵活的
- **英文释义**：quick and light in movement or action; agile
- **例句**：
  - The nimble dancer moved gracefully across the stage.
  - 敏捷的舞者优雅地穿越舞台。
- **助记**：nimble 联想 "nimbus"（光环）→ 像光一样快 → 敏捷的

### 10. **proliferate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激增；迅速繁殖；扩散
- **英文释义**：increase rapidly in numbers; multiply
- **例句**：
  - Social media platforms have proliferated in the last decade.
  - 社交媒体平台在过去十年中激增。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lifer（生命）+ ate → 生命不断向前增长 → 激增

### 高一词汇

### 11. **anomalous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：异常的；不规则的
- **英文释义**：deviating from what is standard, normal, or expected
- **例句**：
  - The anomalous weather patterns this year have concerned many scientists.
  - 今年的异常天气模式让许多科学家感到担忧。
- **助记**：a（不）+ nomal（正常）+ ous → 不正常的 → 异常的

### 12. **extrapolate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：推断；外推
- **英文释义**：extend the application of a method or conclusion to an unknown situation by assuming existing trends will continue
- **例句**：
  - We can extrapolate from the current data to predict future trends.
  - 我们可以从当前数据推断出未来的趋势。
- **助记**：extra（超出）+ polate（推断）→ 推断到超出的范围 → 推断

### 13. **irrevocable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可撤销的；不可挽回的
- **英文释义**：not able to be changed, reversed, or recovered
- **例句**：
  - Once signed, the contract is irrevocable and cannot be amended.
  - 一旦签署，合同不可撤销且不能修改。
- **助记**：ir（不）+ revoc（撤回）+ able → 不可撤回的

### 14. **philanthropic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：慈善的；乐善好施的
- **英文释义**：(of a person or organization) seeking to promote the welfare of others; generous
- **例句**：
  - The billionaire is known for his philanthropic efforts to fund education.
  - 这位亿万富翁以其资助教育的慈善努力而闻名。
- **助记**：phil（爱）+ anthrop（人类）+ ic → 热爱人类的 → 慈善的

### 15. **serendipity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：意外发现；机缘巧合
- **英文释义**：the occurrence of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way
- **例句**：
  - It was pure serendipity that they met each other at the airport.
  - 他们在机场偶遇纯属机缘巧合。
- **助记**：来自波斯故事中的 Serendip（锡兰）→ 意外的美好发现
