## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **apprehend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：逮捕；理解；忧虑
- **英文释义**：to arrest someone for a crime; to understand or perceive; to anticipate with anxiety
- **例句**：
  - The police apprehended the suspect near the border.
  - 警察在边境附近逮捕了嫌疑人。
- **助记**：ap（朝向）+ prehend（抓住）→ 抓住 → 逮捕/理解

### 2. **buoyant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有浮力的；乐观的；繁荣的
- **英文释义**：able to stay afloat; cheerful and optimistic; (of economy) involving good activity
- **例句**：
  - Despite the setbacks, she remained buoyant and hopeful about the future.
  - 尽管遭遇了挫折，她仍然保持乐观，对未来充满希望。
- **助记**：buoy（浮标）+ ant → 像浮标一样浮起来的 → 有浮力的/乐观的

### 3. **circumvent** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：规避；绕过；避开
- **英文释义**：to find a way around an obstacle; to avoid or get around a rule
- **例句**：
  - The company found a way to circumvent the new tax regulations.
  - 公司找到了规避新税收法规的方法。
- **助记**：circum（环绕）+ vent（走）→ 绕着走 → 规避

### 4. **discard** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：丢弃；抛弃；放弃
- **英文释义**：to get rid of something as no longer useful or desirable
- **例句**：
  - She discarded her old notes and started studying from scratch.
  - 她丢弃了旧笔记，从头开始学习。
- **助记**：dis（去除）+ card（纸牌）→ 把不要的牌扔掉 → 丢弃

### 5. **entail** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：需要；涉及；牵涉
- **英文释义**：to involve something as a necessary or inevitable part
- **例句**：
  - The project will entail significant investment in new technology.
  - 这个项目将需要在新技术方面进行大量投资。
- **助记**：en（使）+ tail（尾巴）→ 拖着尾巴/牵涉 → 需要/涉及

### 6. **flaunt** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：炫耀；夸示；招摇
- **英文释义**：to display ostentatiously in order to provoke admiration or envy
- **例句**：
  - He flaunted his new sports car to impress his neighbours.
  - 他炫耀他的新跑车以给邻居留下深刻印象。
- **助记**：flaunt → 联想"飞浪特"→ 飞扬跋扈地炫耀 → 炫耀

### 7. **hostage** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：人质；抵押品
- **英文释义**：a person seized or held as security for the fulfilment of a condition
- **例句**：
  - The kidnappers demanded a ransom in exchange for the hostages.
  - 绑匪要求赎金以交换人质。
- **助记**：host（主人）+ age → 被当作"主人"扣押的人 → 人质

### 8. **laden** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：装满的；负载的；充满……的
- **英文释义**：heavily loaded or weighed down
- **例句**：
  - The branches were laden with ripe apples ready for picking.
  - 树枝上挂满了等待采摘的成熟苹果。
- **助记**：laden → load（装载）→ 装满了的

### 9. **nurture** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 养育；培养；扶持 n. 养育；培养
- **英文释义**：v. to care for and encourage the growth of; n. the process of caring for and encouraging
- **例句**：
  - Parents should nurture their children's curiosity and creativity.
  - 父母应该培养孩子的好奇心和创造力。
- **助记**：nurture → nurse（护士/养育）→ 像护士一样照顾 → 养育/培养

### 10. **plight** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：困境；苦境；窘况
- **英文释义**：a dangerous, difficult, or unfortunate situation
- **例句**：
  - The documentary highlighted the plight of refugees fleeing from war.
  - 这部纪录片突出了逃离战争的难民的困境。
- **助记**：plight → 联想"扑来的"→ 困难扑面而来 → 困境

### 高一词汇

### 11. **clandestine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：秘密的；暗中的；偷偷摸摸的
- **英文释义**：kept secret or done secretively, especially because illicit
- **例句**：
  - The agents conducted clandestine operations behind enemy lines.
  - 特工在敌后进行秘密行动。
- **助记**：clan（氏族）+ destin（隐蔽）+ e → 氏族中隐蔽的 → 秘密的

### 12. **felicitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：恰当的；巧妙的；幸福的
- **英文释义**：well chosen or suited to the circumstances; pleasing and fortunate
- **例句**：
  - The poet's felicitous choice of words created a vivid and moving image.
  - 诗人恰当的用词营造了一个生动感人的画面。
- **助记**：felic（幸福）+ itous → 令人幸福的 → 恰当的/幸福的

### 13. **magniloquent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：夸夸其谈的；浮夸的；言辞华丽的
- **英文释义**：using high-flown or bombastic language
- **例句**：
  - His magniloquent speeches impressed some but annoyed many who preferred substance over style.
  - 他夸夸其谈的演讲给一些人留下了深刻印象，但也令很多喜欢实质胜于形式的人感到厌烦。
- **助记**：magni（大）+ loqu（说话）+ ent → 说大话的 → 夸夸其谈的

### 14. **querulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱抱怨的；哀叹的；牢骚满腹的
- **英文释义**：complaining in a petulant or whining manner
- **例句**：
  - The querulous patient complained about everything from the food to the temperature of the room.
  - 那个爱抱怨的病人对从食物到房间温度的一切都抱怨不停。
- **助记**：quer（抱怨）+ ulous → 总是抱怨的 → 爱抱怨的

### 15. **veracious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：诚实的；真实的；准确的
- **英文释义**：speaking or representing the truth
- **例句**：
  - The journalist is known for her veracious reporting of complex events.
  - 这位记者以对复杂事件的真实报道而闻名。
- **助记**：ver（真实）+ acious → 真实的 → 诚实的
