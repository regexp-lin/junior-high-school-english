## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **adversary** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：对手；敌手；对立者
- **英文释义**：an opponent in a conflict or contest; an enemy
- **例句**：
  - In the final match, he faced his toughest adversary on the court.
  - 决赛中，他在场上遇到了最强劲的对手。
  - The two nations were adversaries during the cold war period.
  - 冷战时期两国曾是敌对双方。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ vers（转）+ ary → 转向对立面的人 → 对手

### 2. **coherent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：连贯的；一致的；条理清楚的
- **英文释义**：logical and consistent; forming a unified whole
- **例句**：
  - A good essay needs a coherent argument from start to finish.
  - 一篇好作文需要从头到尾论点连贯。
  - She gave a coherent explanation despite feeling nervous.
  - 尽管紧张，她仍作了条理清楚的说明。
- **助记**：co（共同）+ her（粘）+ ent → 粘在一起 → 连贯

### 3. **converge** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：汇聚；集中；（观点）趋同
- **英文释义**：to come together from different directions; to tend toward a point
- **例句**：
  - Several roads converge at the town square.
  - 几条道路在镇广场交汇。
  - Experts from many fields converged on the environmental conference.
  - 各领域专家齐聚这场环保会议。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ verge（倾斜）→ 向一处倾斜 → 汇聚

### 4. **discern** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：看出；识别；觉察
- **英文释义**：to perceive or recognize something with difficulty or effort
- **例句**：
  - In the dark, it was hard to discern shapes more than a few meters away.
  - 黑暗中很难辨认几米外的轮廓。
  - She could discern a note of sadness in his voice.
  - 她能听出他声音里的一丝悲伤。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ cern（分辨）→ 分辨开 → 识别

### 5. **eradicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：根除；消灭；杜绝
- **英文释义**：to destroy completely; to put an end to
- **例句**：
  - The campaign aims to eradicate malaria in the region within ten years.
  - 该运动目标是在十年内在本地区根除疟疾。
  - We must eradicate bullying from school culture.
  - 我们必须从校园文化杜绝欺凌。
- **助记**：e（出）+ radic（根）+ ate → 连根拔出 → 根除

### 6. **frail** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚弱的；脆弱的；易损的
- **英文释义**：physically weak; delicate; easily damaged
- **例句**：
  - The frail old man leaned on a walking stick for support.
  - 虚弱的老人拄着拐杖支撑身体。
  - Human happiness can be frail in the face of sudden loss.
  - 面对突然失去，人的幸福可能很脆弱。
- **助记**：frail 联想 frailty（脆弱）→ 身体单薄 → 虚弱

### 7. **indigenous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：本土的；土生土长的；原住的
- **英文释义**：originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native
- **例句**：
  - The museum displays art by indigenous peoples of the region.
  - 博物馆展出该地区原住民的艺术。
  - Kangaroos are indigenous to Australia.
  - 袋鼠为澳大利亚特有。
- **助记**：indi（在内）+ gen（产生）+ ous → 在当地产生的 → 本土的

### 8. **invoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：援引；祈求；唤起；调用（程序）
- **英文释义**：to call on for support; to cite; to bring into operation
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer invoked a precedent from a similar case.
  - 律师援引了类似案件的判例。
  - The ceremony invoked blessings for the new year.
  - 仪式祈求新年赐福。
- **助记**：in（使）+ voke（喊）→ 喊出来 → 援引/祈求

### 9. **jeopardize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：危及；损害；使陷入危险
- **英文释义**：to put at risk of loss, harm, or failure
- **例句**：
  - One careless mistake could jeopardize the whole experiment.
  - 一次粗心错误可能危及整个实验。
  - Skipping sleep regularly can jeopardize your health.
  - 经常熬夜会损害健康。
- **助记**：jeopard（危险）+ ize → 使处于危险

### 10. **stagnant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：停滞的；不景气的；污浊不流动的
- **英文释义**：showing no activity; dull and sluggish; not flowing
- **例句**：
  - The pond became stagnant after the stream was blocked.
  - 溪流被堵后，池塘水变得不流动而发臭。
  - The economy remained stagnant for two consecutive years.
  - 经济连续两年停滞不前。
- **助记**：stagn（站立）+ ant → 站着不动 → 停滞

---

### 高一词汇

### 11. **abrogate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：废除；取消（法律、协议等）
- **英文释义**：to repeal or abolish formally and officially
- **例句**：
  - The government voted to abrogate the outdated treaty.
  - 政府投票废除过时条约。
  - A court may abrogate a contract if fraud is proven.
  - 若证明存在欺诈，法院可废除合同。
- **助记**：ab（离开）+ rog（询问/法律）+ ate → 使法律离开效力 → 废除

### 12. **belligerent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；交战的；挑衅的
- **英文释义**：hostile and aggressive; engaged in war
- **例句**：
  - His belligerent tone started an argument at the meeting.
  - 他挑衅的语气在会议上引发了争吵。
  - The two belligerent nations finally agreed to a ceasefire.
  - 两个交战国最终同意停火。
- **助记**：belli（战争）+ ger（带来）+ ent → 带来战争的 → 好斗

### 13. **cursory** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：草率的；粗略的；匆匆的
- **英文释义**：hasty and therefore not thorough or detailed
- **例句**：
  - A cursory reading of the contract caused him to miss key clauses.
  - 粗略阅读合同使他漏掉关键条款。
  - The teacher gave only cursory feedback on the rushed homework.
  - 老师对匆忙完成的作业只给了草草反馈。
- **助记**：curs（跑）+ ory → 跑过 → 匆匆

### 14. **disparage** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：贬低；轻视；毁谤
- **英文释义**：to speak of as unimportant or bad; to belittle
- **例句**：
  - It is unfair to disparage her efforts without seeing the full picture.
  - 不了解全貌就贬低她的努力是不公平的。
  - Critics disparaged the film, yet audiences loved it.
  - 影评人贬低这部影片，观众却很喜欢。
- **助记**：dis（否定）+ par（平等）+ age → 使不平等 → 贬低

### 15. **extrapolate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：外推；推断；推知
- **英文释义**：to extend or infer from known data or experience
- **例句**：
  - Scientists extrapolate trends from past climate records.
  - 科学家根据过去气候记录外推趋势。
  - Be careful when you extrapolate small samples to the whole population.
  - 用小样本推断总体时要谨慎。
- **助记**：extra（外）+ polate（推）→ 向外推 → 外推
