## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abdicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：退位；放弃（职责、权力）
- **英文释义**：to renounce one's throne; to fail to fulfill a responsibility or duty
- **例句**：
  - The king abdicated the throne in favor of his eldest son.
  - 国王退位让他的长子继位。
- **助记**：ab（离开）+ dic（说/宣布）+ ate → 宣布离开 → 退位

### 2. **corroborate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证实；确证；支持
- **英文释义**：to confirm or give support to a statement, theory, or finding
- **例句**：
  - The DNA evidence corroborated the witness's testimony.
  - DNA 证据证实了目击者的证词。
- **助记**：cor（一起）+ robor（力量）+ ate → 一起加强 → 证实

### 3. **dissuade** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：劝阻；劝止；打消念头
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone not to take a particular course of action
- **例句**：
  - His parents tried to dissuade him from dropping out of school.
  - 他的父母试图劝阻他辍学。
- **助记**：dis（不/反对）+ suade（劝说）→ 反向劝说 → 劝阻

### 4. **elucidate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阐明；解释；说明
- **英文释义**：to make something clear; to explain
- **例句**：
  - The professor elucidated the complex concept with simple examples.
  - 教授用简单的例子阐明了这个复杂的概念。
- **助记**：e（出）+ lucid（清楚的）+ ate → 使变得清楚 → 阐明

### 5. **gratuitous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无必要的；无理由的；免费的
- **英文释义**：uncalled for; lacking good reason; given free of charge
- **例句**：
  - The film was criticized for its gratuitous violence that served no purpose in the plot.
  - 这部电影因其对剧情毫无意义的无端暴力而受到批评。
- **助记**：gratu（感恩/免费）+ itous → 免费给的/不需要理由的 → 无必要的

### 6. **ignite** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：点燃；引发；激起
- **英文释义**：to catch fire or cause to catch fire; to arouse or inflame
- **例句**：
  - The leader's passionate speech ignited a movement for social reform.
  - 领导人充满激情的演讲引发了一场社会改革运动。
- **助记**：ign（火）+ ite → 使着火 → 点燃/引发

### 7. **myriad** `初三` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：n. 无数；大量 adj. 无数的；种种的
- **英文释义**：n. a countless or extremely great number; adj. countless; innumerable
- **例句**：
  - The city offers a myriad of cultural activities for visitors to enjoy.
  - 这座城市为游客提供了无数的文化活动可以享受。
- **助记**：myriad → 联想"密密的"→ 密密麻麻多到数不清 → 无数

### 8. **petition** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 请愿书；申请 v. 请愿；正式请求
- **英文释义**：n. a formal written request signed by many people; v. to make a formal request
- **例句**：
  - Thousands of residents signed a petition against the proposed highway construction.
  - 数千名居民签署了一份反对拟议公路建设的请愿书。
- **助记**：pet（请求）+ ition → 提出请求 → 请愿

### 9. **revamp** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：翻新；改造；修订
- **英文释义**：to give new and improved form, structure, or appearance to
- **例句**：
  - The restaurant revamped its menu to attract more health-conscious diners.
  - 这家餐厅翻新了菜单以吸引更多注重健康的食客。
- **助记**：re（重新）+ vamp（鞋面/修补）→ 重新修补 → 翻新

### 10. **volatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易变的；不稳定的；易挥发的
- **英文释义**：liable to change rapidly and unpredictably; easily evaporated
- **例句**：
  - The volatile stock market caused many investors to panic and sell.
  - 不稳定的股市导致许多投资者恐慌性抛售。
- **助记**：volat（飞）+ ile → 像飞一样变化快 → 易变的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **bellwether** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：领头者；风向标；先导
- **英文释义**：an indicator or predictor of something; a leader of a trend
- **例句**：
  - Silicon Valley is often seen as the bellwether of technological innovation.
  - 硅谷常被视为科技创新的风向标。
- **助记**：bell（铃）+ wether（阉羊）→ 戴铃的领头羊 → 领头者/风向标

### 12. **consternation** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：惊愕；恐慌；惊慌失措
- **英文释义**：feelings of anxiety or dismay at something unexpected
- **例句**：
  - To our consternation, the bridge had been washed away by the flood.
  - 令我们惊愕的是，桥被洪水冲走了。
- **助记**：con（完全）+ stern（严肃/惊呆）+ ation → 完全惊呆了 → 惊愕

### 13. **fatuous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：愚蠢的；荒唐的；自满的
- **英文释义**：silly and pointless; complacently stupid
- **例句**：
  - His fatuous remarks about the crisis showed his complete lack of understanding.
  - 他关于危机的愚蠢言论表明他完全缺乏理解。
- **助记**：fatu（愚蠢）+ ous → 愚蠢的

### 14. **obfuscate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使困惑；使模糊；混淆
- **英文释义**：to render obscure, unclear, or unintelligible
- **例句**：
  - The politician tried to obfuscate the issue by giving evasive answers.
  - 这位政客试图通过给出回避性的答案来混淆这个问题。
- **助记**：ob（遮挡）+ fusc（暗）+ ate → 使变暗/遮挡 → 使模糊

### 15. **tantamount** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：等同的；相当于的
- **英文释义**：equivalent in seriousness to; virtually the same as
- **例句**：
  - Refusing to act on the evidence is tantamount to allowing the crime to continue.
  - 拒绝根据证据采取行动等同于允许犯罪继续。
- **助记**：tant（相同）+ amount（数量）→ 数量相同的 → 等同的
