## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **adhere** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：坚持；遵守；粘附
- **英文释义**：to stick fast to a surface; to believe in and follow the practices of
- **例句**：
  - All employees must adhere to the company's code of conduct.
  - 所有员工都必须遵守公司的行为准则。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ here（粘）→ 粘住不放 → 坚持/粘附

### 2. **calibrate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：校准；调整；标定
- **英文释义**：to mark an instrument with a standard scale of readings; to adjust precisely
- **例句**：
  - The technician calibrated the laboratory instruments before running the experiment.
  - 技术人员在进行实验之前校准了实验室仪器。
- **助记**：calibr（刻度）+ ate → 标刻度 → 校准/调整

### 3. **derive** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：得到；源自；推导
- **英文释义**：to obtain something from a specified source; to arise from or originate in
- **例句**：
  - Many English words are derived from Latin and Greek roots.
  - 许多英语单词源自拉丁语和希腊语词根。
- **助记**：de（从）+ rive（河流）→ 从河流中引出 → 源自/得到

### 4. **embark** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：着手；开始；上船
- **英文释义**：to begin a course of action; to go on board a ship or aircraft
- **例句**：
  - After graduating, she embarked on a career in international journalism.
  - 毕业后，她开始了国际新闻事业。
- **助记**：em（进入）+ bark（船）→ 上船出发 → 着手/开始

### 5. **fluctuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：波动；变动；起伏不定
- **英文释义**：to rise and fall irregularly in number or amount
- **例句**：
  - Oil prices fluctuate depending on supply and demand in the global market.
  - 油价根据全球市场的供需关系而波动。
- **助记**：fluct（流动）+ uate → 像水流一样上下流动 → 波动

### 6. **integrate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：整合；融入；使成为一体
- **英文释义**：to combine one thing with another so that they become a whole
- **例句**：
  - The school aims to integrate technology into every aspect of the curriculum.
  - 学校旨在将技术融入课程的各个方面。
- **助记**：integr（完整）+ ate → 使完整 → 整合/融入

### 7. **obsolete** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过时的；废弃的；淘汰的
- **英文释义**：no longer produced or used; out of date
- **例句**：
  - Rapid advances in technology have made many older devices obsolete.
  - 技术的快速进步使许多旧设备变得过时。
- **助记**：ob（离开）+ sol（惯用）+ ete → 不再惯用的 → 过时的

### 8. **rapport** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：融洽关系；和谐；亲善
- **英文释义**：a close and harmonious relationship in which the people understand each other's feelings
- **例句**：
  - The new teacher quickly built a good rapport with her students.
  - 新老师很快与学生建立了良好的融洽关系。
- **助记**：rapport → 联想"report（报告）"→ 互相报告/沟通 → 融洽关系

### 9. **succumb** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：屈服；屈从；死于
- **英文释义**：to fail to resist pressure, temptation, or some other negative force
- **例句**：
  - After months of resistance, the garrison finally succumbed to the overwhelming siege.
  - 经过数月的抵抗，驻军最终在压倒性的围攻下屈服了。
- **助记**：suc（下）+ cumb（躺下）→ 躺下屈服 → 屈服

### 10. **zealous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情的；积极的；狂热的
- **英文释义**：having or showing zeal; fervent
- **例句**：
  - The zealous volunteers worked tirelessly to distribute food to the flood victims.
  - 热情的志愿者不知疲倦地为洪灾受害者分发食物。
- **助记**：zeal（热情）+ ous → 充满热情的 → 热情的/狂热的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **conspicuous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：显眼的；引人注目的；明显的
- **英文释义**：standing out so as to be clearly visible; attracting notice or attention
- **例句**：
  - The bright orange safety vest made the construction workers conspicuous from a distance.
  - 鲜橙色的安全背心使建筑工人在远处就很显眼。
- **助记**：con（加强）+ spic（看）+ uous → 非常容易看见的 → 显眼的

### 12. **dogmatic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：教条的；武断的；固执己见的
- **英文释义**：inclined to lay down principles as incontrovertibly true
- **例句**：
  - His dogmatic insistence on following outdated rules frustrated the younger team members.
  - 他教条地坚持遵循过时的规则，让年轻的团队成员感到沮丧。
- **助记**：dogma（教条）+ tic → 教条的/武断的

### 13. **labyrinthine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：迷宫般的；错综复杂的；曲折的
- **英文释义**：like a labyrinth; irregular and twisting; complicated
- **例句**：
  - The labyrinthine bureaucracy made it nearly impossible to get a simple permit approved.
  - 迷宫般的官僚体制使得一个简单的许可证几乎不可能获得批准。
- **助记**：labyrinth（迷宫）+ ine → 像迷宫一样的 → 迷宫般的

### 14. **paradigm** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：范例；典范；范式
- **英文释义**：a typical example or pattern of something; a model
- **例句**：
  - The discovery of the internet created a new paradigm for communication and commerce.
  - 互联网的发现为通信和商业创造了新的范式。
- **助记**：para（在旁边）+ digm（展示）→ 放在旁边展示的模型 → 范例/范式

### 15. **spurious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚假的；伪造的；不合理的
- **英文释义**：not being what it purports to be; false or fake
- **例句**：
  - The detective quickly identified the spurious documents used to forge the identity.
  - 侦探很快识别出了用于伪造身份的虚假文件。
- **助记**：spur（假的/刺）+ ious → 假的 → 虚假的/伪造的
