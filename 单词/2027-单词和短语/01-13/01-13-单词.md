## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **disaster** /dɪˈzɑːstə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：灾难；灾祸
- **英文释义**：a sudden event that causes great damage or suffering
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake was a terrible disaster.
  - 这次地震是一场可怕的灾难。
  - The picnic was a complete disaster — it rained all day.
  - 野餐完全是一场灾难——下了一整天的雨。
- **助记**：dis-（坏的）+ aster（星星）→ 星象不好 → 灾难

### 2. **discipline** /ˈdɪsəplɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：纪律；训练
- **英文释义**：the practice of training people to obey rules; to train someone to behave properly
- **例句**：
  - Good discipline is essential in a classroom.
  - 良好的纪律在课堂上至关重要。
  - Parents need to discipline their children with love.
  - 父母需要用爱来管教孩子。
- **助记**：disciple（门徒）→ 门徒需要训练 → 纪律

### 3. **disguise** /dɪsˈɡaɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：伪装；掩饰
- **英文释义**：to change your appearance so that people cannot recognize you
- **例句**：
  - The spy disguised himself as a waiter.
  - 间谍伪装成了一个服务员。
  - She wore a disguise to avoid being recognized.
  - 她乔装打扮以避免被认出。
- **助记**：dis-（去掉）+ guise（外表）→ 去掉原来的外表 → 伪装

### 4. **dismiss** /dɪsˈmɪs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：解雇；驳回
- **英文释义**：to remove someone from a job; to decide that something is not worth considering
- **例句**：
  - He was dismissed from his job for being late too often.
  - 他因经常迟到而被解雇。
  - The judge dismissed the case due to lack of evidence.
  - 法官因证据不足驳回了案件。
- **助记**：dis-（离开）+ miss（送）→ 把人送走 → 解雇

### 5. **display** /dɪˈspleɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：展示；陈列
- **英文释义**：to put something in a place where people can see it; a show or exhibition
- **例句**：
  - The museum displays artefacts from ancient China.
  - 博物馆陈列着中国古代的文物。
  - There was a beautiful display of fireworks.
  - 有一场美丽的烟花表演。
- **助记**：dis-（展开）+ play（铺展）→ 展开铺展 → 展示

### 6. **dispute** /dɪˈspjuːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：争论；争端
- **英文释义**：a disagreement or argument; to argue about something
- **例句**：
  - The dispute between the two neighbours lasted for years.
  - 两个邻居之间的争端持续了好几年。
  - No one can dispute the fact that the earth is round.
  - 没有人能质疑地球是圆的这个事实。
- **助记**：dis-（分开）+ pute（思考）→ 想法分开 → 争论

### 7. **distinct** /dɪˈstɪŋkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：明显的；不同的
- **英文释义**：clearly different or separate; easily noticed
- **例句**：
  - There is a distinct difference between the two products.
  - 这两种产品之间有明显的区别。
  - The building has a distinct architectural style.
  - 这座建筑有独特的建筑风格。
- **助记**：dis-（分开）+ tinct（刺/标记）→ 分开标记的 → 不同的

### 8. **distribute** /dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：分发；分配
- **英文释义**：to give something to each of a number of people; to spread over an area
- **例句**：
  - The teacher distributed the test papers to the students.
  - 老师把试卷分发给学生。
  - The company distributes its products across the country.
  - 这家公司在全国各地分销产品。
- **助记**：dis-（分开）+ tribute（给予）→ 分开给予 → 分发

### 9. **disturb** /dɪˈstɜːb/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：打扰；使不安
- **英文释义**：to interrupt someone; to make someone worried or upset
- **例句**：
  - Sorry to disturb you, but can I ask a question?
  - 抱歉打扰你，我能问个问题吗？
  - The news disturbed her deeply.
  - 这个消息使她深感不安。
- **助记**：dis-（分离）+ turb（搅动）→ 搅乱了秩序 → 打扰

### 10. **diverse** /daɪˈvɜːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：多样的；不同的
- **英文释义**：showing a great deal of variety; very different
- **例句**：
  - Our school has a diverse student population.
  - 我们学校的学生群体多元化。
  - China has a diverse range of climates.
  - 中国有多种多样的气候。
- **助记**：di-（分开）+ verse（转）→ 向不同方向转 → 多样的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **indifferent** /ɪnˈdɪfrənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：漠不关心的；冷淡的
- **英文释义**：having no interest or concern about something
- **例句**：
  - He seemed indifferent to the suffering of others.
  - 他对别人的苦难似乎漠不关心。
  - She gave an indifferent shrug when asked about the result.
  - 当被问及结果时，她冷淡地耸了耸肩。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ different（不同的）→ 觉得无所谓 → 漠不关心的

### 12. **inherent** /ɪnˈhɪərənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：固有的；内在的
- **英文释义**：existing as a natural or basic part of something
- **例句**：
  - There are inherent risks in every business.
  - 每项生意都有其固有的风险。
  - Creativity is inherent in every child.
  - 创造力是每个孩子内在具有的。
- **助记**：in-（在里面）+ her（粘附）+ -ent → 粘在里面的 → 固有的

### 13. **jurisdiction** /ˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：管辖权；司法权
- **英文释义**：the official power to make legal decisions
- **例句**：
  - This case falls outside the jurisdiction of the local court.
  - 此案不在地方法院的管辖范围内。
  - The police have no jurisdiction over military matters.
  - 警方对军事事务没有管辖权。
- **助记**：juris（法律）+ dict（说）+ -ion → 说法律的权力 → 司法权

### 14. **kindle** /ˈkɪndl/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：点燃；激起
- **英文释义**：to light a fire; to arouse or inspire a feeling
- **例句**：
  - The speech kindled hope in the hearts of the people.
  - 这场演讲点燃了人们心中的希望。
  - She kindled a fire to keep warm.
  - 她生了火来取暖。
- **助记**：kindle → candle（蜡烛）→ 像点蜡烛一样点燃 → 点燃

### 15. **lament** /ləˈment/ `高一`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：哀悼；悲叹
- **英文释义**：to express sadness and regret about something
- **例句**：
  - She lamented the loss of her grandmother.
  - 她为祖母的离世而悲伤。
  - The poem is a lament for lost youth.
  - 这首诗是对逝去青春的悲叹。
- **助记**：lament → "来门特"（谐音）→ 来到门前特别悲伤 → 哀悼
