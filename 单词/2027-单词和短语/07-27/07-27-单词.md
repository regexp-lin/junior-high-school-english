## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **articulate** `初三` v./adj.

- **中文释义**：v. 清晰表达；明确说出 adj. 口齿清晰的；善于表达的
- **英文释义**：v. to express an idea or feeling fluently and coherently; adj. having the ability to speak fluently and clearly
- **例句**：
  - She articulated her concerns about the project in a clear and persuasive manner.
  - 她以清晰有说服力的方式表达了她对项目的担忧。
- **助记**：art（连接/关节）+ iculate → 把想法连接起来表达 → 清晰表达

### 2. **chronicle** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 编年史；年代记 v. 记录；载入编年史
- **英文释义**：n. a factual written account of important events in order of time; v. to record events in order of time
- **例句**：
  - The book chronicles the rise and fall of the Roman Empire over several centuries.
  - 这本书记录了罗马帝国数百年间的兴衰。
- **助记**：chron（时间）+ icle → 按时间记录 → 编年史

### 3. **disseminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：传播；散布；宣传
- **英文释义**：to spread or disperse something, especially information, widely
- **例句**：
  - The organization uses social media to disseminate information about health and safety.
  - 该组织利用社交媒体传播健康和安全信息。
- **助记**：dis（分散）+ semin（种子）+ ate → 播撒种子 → 传播

### 4. **exemplify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：是……的典范；举例说明；体现
- **英文释义**：to be a typical example of; to illustrate or clarify by giving an example
- **例句**：
  - Her dedication to community service exemplifies the values the school hopes to instill.
  - 她对社区服务的奉献体现了学校希望灌输的价值观。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ empl（样本）+ ify → 拿出样本 → 举例说明/是典范

### 5. **hover** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：盘旋；徘徊；犹豫
- **英文释义**：to remain in one place in the air; to linger close at hand in a tentative way
- **例句**：
  - The helicopter hovered above the accident scene while rescue teams worked below.
  - 直升机在事故现场上空盘旋，救援队在下方工作。
- **助记**：hover → 联想"悬浮"→ 停留在空中 → 盘旋/徘徊

### 6. **judicious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：明智的；审慎的；有见识的
- **英文释义**：having, showing, or done with good judgment or sense
- **例句**：
  - A judicious use of resources is essential for the long-term success of any organization.
  - 明智地使用资源对任何组织的长期成功都至关重要。
- **助记**：judic（判断）+ ious → 善于判断的 → 明智的

### 7. **nominal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：名义上的；微不足道的；象征性的
- **英文释义**：existing in name only; very small; far below the real value
- **例句**：
  - The club charges a nominal fee of one dollar per year for membership.
  - 俱乐部每年收取一美元的象征性会费。
- **助记**：nomin（名字）+ al → 只有名字的 → 名义上的

### 8. **pervasive** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：普遍的；遍布的；无处不在的
- **英文释义**：spreading widely throughout an area or group of people
- **例句**：
  - The pervasive smell of smoke lingered in the building for days after the fire.
  - 火灾后，弥漫的烟味在建筑物中持续了好几天。
- **助记**：per（穿透）+ vas（走）+ ive → 到处走/渗透 → 普遍的

### 9. **sanction** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 制裁；批准 v. 批准；认可；制裁
- **英文释义**：n. a threatened penalty for disobeying a rule; official permission; v. to give official permission; to impose a penalty on
- **例句**：
  - The United Nations imposed economic sanctions on the country for violating international law.
  - 联合国因该国违反国际法而对其实施了经济制裁。
- **助记**：sanct（神圣的）+ ion → 神圣的决定 → 批准/制裁

### 10. **traverse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：穿越；横跨；行走
- **英文释义**：to travel across or through
- **例句**：
  - The hikers traversed the mountain range in five grueling days.
  - 徒步旅行者在五天的艰苦行程中穿越了山脉。
- **助记**：tra（穿过）+ verse（转向）→ 穿过转向 → 穿越

### 高一词汇

### 11. **belated** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：迟来的；延误的；姗姗来迟的
- **英文释义**：coming or happening later than should have been the case
- **例句**：
  - She sent a belated birthday card after realizing she had missed the date.
  - 她意识到错过了日期后寄了一张迟来的生日贺卡。
- **助记**：be + lated（晚的）→ 迟到的 → 迟来的

### 12. **diffident** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：缺乏自信的；羞怯的；畏缩的
- **英文释义**：modest or shy because of a lack of self-confidence
- **例句**：
  - The diffident young artist was reluctant to show her work to the public.
  - 这位缺乏自信的年轻艺术家不愿意向公众展示她的作品。
- **助记**：dif（不）+ fid（信任）+ ent → 不信任自己 → 缺乏自信的

### 13. **melancholy** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：n. 忧郁；悲伤 adj. 忧郁的；令人悲伤的
- **英文释义**：n. a deep, pensive sadness; adj. having a feeling of melancholy; sad and depressed
- **例句**：
  - A sense of melancholy filled the room as they looked at old photographs of happier times.
  - 当他们看着过去幸福时光的旧照片时，一种忧郁感弥漫了整个房间。
- **助记**：melan（黑色）+ choly（胆汁）→ 黑胆汁（古人认为忧郁由黑胆汁引起）→ 忧郁

### 14. **phlegmatic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：冷静的；不易激动的；淡漠的
- **英文释义**：having an unemotional and stolidly calm disposition
- **例句**：
  - His phlegmatic response to the crisis surprised those who expected him to panic.
  - 他对危机的冷静反应让那些预期他会恐慌的人感到惊讶。
- **助记**：phlegm（痰/冷淡）+ atic → 像痰一样冷 → 冷静的/不易激动的

### 15. **truculent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；粗暴的；凶猛的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant
- **例句**：
  - The truculent teenager constantly argued with his teachers and parents.
  - 这个好斗的少年不断和老师及父母争吵。
- **助记**：truc（凶猛）+ ulent → 凶猛的 → 好斗的
