## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **anomaly** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：异常；反常现象；异例
- **英文释义**：something that deviates from what is standard, normal, or expected
- **例句**：
  - The warm January weather was an anomaly for this northern region.
  - 一月份的温暖天气对这个北方地区来说是反常现象。
  - Scientists investigated the anomaly in the experimental data.
  - 科学家们调查了实验数据中的异常。
- **助记**：a（不）+ nomal（正常）+ y → 不正常 → 异常

### 2. **begrudge** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：嫉妒；吝惜；不乐意给
- **英文释义**：to envy someone the possession or enjoyment of something; to give reluctantly
- **例句**：
  - No one begrudged her the success she had worked so hard to achieve.
  - 没人嫉妒她辛苦努力换来的成功。
  - He begrudged every cent he had to spend on repairs.
  - 他对花在维修上的每一分钱都心不甘情不愿。
- **助记**：be + grudge（怨恨）→ 怀着怨恨 → 嫉妒/吝惜

### 3. **consolidate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：巩固；合并；统一
- **英文释义**：to make something physically stronger or more solid; to combine into a single unit
- **例句**：
  - The company consolidated its three offices into one large headquarters.
  - 公司将三个办公室合并为一个大总部。
  - After winning the election, the party worked to consolidate power.
  - 赢得选举后该党致力于巩固权力。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ solid（坚固）+ ate → 使坚固在一起 → 巩固

### 4. **dormant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：休眠的；蛰伏的；静止的
- **英文释义**：temporarily inactive; in a state of rest or inactivity
- **例句**：
  - The volcano has been dormant for over a thousand years.
  - 这座火山已休眠了一千多年。
  - Many plants become dormant during the cold winter months.
  - 许多植物在寒冷的冬季月份进入休眠状态。
- **助记**：dorm（睡眠）+ ant → 正在睡的 → 休眠的

### 5. **evade** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：逃避；回避；规避
- **英文释义**：to escape or avoid something, especially by cleverness or trickery
- **例句**：
  - The suspect evaded capture by hiding in the forest for three days.
  - 嫌疑人在森林中躲藏三天以逃避抓捕。
  - He cleverly evaded the interviewer's awkward questions.
  - 他巧妙地回避了采访者尴尬的问题。
- **助记**：e（出）+ vade（走）→ 走出去 → 逃避

### 6. **futility** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：徒劳；无用；无意义
- **英文释义**：lack of effectiveness or success; pointlessness
- **例句**：
  - She finally accepted the futility of arguing with her stubborn brother.
  - 她终于接受了与固执的弟弟争论的徒劳。
  - The futility of the rescue attempt became clear as night fell.
  - 夜幕降临时救援尝试的无望变得明显。
- **助记**：futile（徒劳的）→ futility（名词形式）

### 7. **guise** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：外观；伪装；表面
- **英文释义**：an external form, appearance, or manner of presentation; a pretense
- **例句**：
  - The spy entered the building under the guise of a delivery person.
  - 间谍以快递员的伪装进入大楼。
  - Bullying sometimes comes in the guise of friendly teasing.
  - 欺凌有时以友好玩笑的外表出现。
- **助记**：guise 联想 disguise（伪装）→ 伪装/外观

### 8. **harass** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：骚扰；烦扰；不断打扰
- **英文释义**：to subject to aggressive pressure or intimidation repeatedly
- **例句**：
  - It is illegal to harass someone through persistent unwanted phone calls.
  - 通过持续不断的骚扰电话骚扰他人是违法的。
  - The reporter was harassed online for publishing the controversial article.
  - 记者因发布争议文章在网上受到骚扰。
- **助记**：harass → 哈拉骚（谐音）→ 骚扰

### 9. **inert** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：惰性的；不活泼的；迟钝的
- **英文释义**：lacking the ability or strength to move; chemically inactive
- **例句**：
  - Noble gases like helium and neon are chemically inert.
  - 像氦和氖这样的惰性气体在化学上是不活泼的。
  - The crowd stood inert, too shocked to react to the sudden explosion.
  - 人群呆立不动，太震惊了以至于无法对突然的爆炸作出反应。
- **助记**：in（不）+ ert（活动）→ 不活动 → 惰性的

### 10. **mend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：修补；修理；改善
- **英文释义**：to repair something that is broken or damaged; to improve
- **例句**：
  - She mended the torn jacket with needle and thread.
  - 她用针线修补了撕破的夹克。
  - Time can help mend a broken friendship if both sides are willing.
  - 如果双方都愿意，时间可以帮助修复破裂的友谊。
- **助记**：mend → amend（修改/改善）的简写 → 修补

### 高一词汇

### 11. **maverick** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：特立独行的人；不随大流的；独立思考的
- **英文释义**：an unorthodox or independent-minded person
- **例句**：
  - The maverick scientist challenged the established theory and was proven right.
  - 这位特立独行的科学家挑战了既有理论并被证明是对的。
  - Her maverick approach to design won her international awards.
  - 她独树一帜的设计方法为她赢得了国际奖项。
- **助记**：来自美国牧场主 Samuel Maverick（不给牛打烙印）→ 不随大流

### 12. **orthodox** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：正统的；传统的；普遍接受的
- **英文释义**：conforming to what is generally or traditionally accepted as right
- **例句**：
  - Orthodox medicine relies on scientific evidence and clinical trials.
  - 正统医学依赖科学证据和临床试验。
  - His views were far from orthodox and often sparked debate.
  - 他的观点远非正统，经常引发争论。
- **助记**：ortho（正确）+ dox（观点）→ 正确的观点 → 正统

### 13. **precarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不稳定的；危险的；不确定的
- **英文释义**：not securely held or in position; dependent on chance; uncertain
- **例句**：
  - The mountain climber was in a precarious position on the icy ledge.
  - 登山者在冰冷的岩架上处于危险的位置。
  - Freelancers often face a precarious financial situation.
  - 自由职业者常面临不稳定的财务状况。
- **助记**：prec（祈祷）+ arious → 需要祈祷才安全 → 危险的

### 14. **recluse** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：隐居者；隐士；遁世者
- **英文释义**：a person who lives a solitary life and tends to avoid others
- **例句**：
  - The famous author lived as a recluse in a remote mountain cabin.
  - 这位著名作家在偏远的山间小屋过着隐居生活。
  - Despite his fame, the painter became a recluse in his later years.
  - 尽管名声在外，这位画家晚年成了隐士。
- **助记**：re（回）+ cluse（关）→ 把自己关回去 → 隐居者

### 15. **tenacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：坚韧的；顽强的；执着的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent
- **例句**：
  - The tenacious reporter spent months digging into the corruption story.
  - 执着的记者花了数月挖掘腐败故事。
  - Ivy is a tenacious plant that clings to walls even in harsh weather.
  - 常春藤是一种坚韧的植物，即使在恶劣天气也紧附墙壁。
- **助记**：ten（持有）+ acious → 紧紧持有 → 坚韧/执着
