## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **ameliorate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：改善；改良；使好转
- **英文释义**：to make something bad or unsatisfactory better
- **例句**：
  - The new policies are designed to ameliorate living conditions for low-income families.
  - 新政策旨在改善低收入家庭的生活条件。
  - Medication can ameliorate the symptoms but cannot cure the disease entirely.
  - 药物可以缓解症状但不能完全治愈疾病。
- **助记**：a + melior（更好）+ ate → 使变得更好 → 改善

### 2. **truculent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；好争吵的；粗暴的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant
- **例句**：
  - The truculent customer demanded to speak with the manager immediately.
  - 好斗的客户要求立即与经理说话。
  - His truculent behavior in class led to multiple disciplinary warnings.
  - 他在课堂上好争吵的行为导致了多次纪律警告。
- **助记**：truc（凶猛）+ ulent → 凶猛的 → 好斗的

### 3. **proliferate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激增；迅速繁殖；扩散
- **英文释义**：to increase rapidly in number; to multiply
- **例句**：
  - Online shopping platforms have proliferated in recent years.
  - 近年来在线购物平台激增。
  - If left unchecked, weeds will proliferate and overtake the entire garden.
  - 如果不加控制，杂草会迅速蔓延并占领整个花园。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lifer（后代）+ ate → 向前产生后代 → 激增

### 4. **convoluted** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：复杂的；曲折的；费解的
- **英文释义**：extremely complex and difficult to follow
- **例句**：
  - The plot of the movie was so convoluted that most viewers couldn't follow it.
  - 电影的情节如此复杂，大多数观众都跟不上。
  - The tax code is notoriously convoluted, even for professional accountants.
  - 税法出了名地复杂，即使对专业会计师也是如此。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ volut（卷）+ ed → 卷在一起 → 复杂的

### 5. **beleaguer** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：围困；困扰；使烦恼
- **英文释义**：to cause problems and difficulties for; to besiege
- **例句**：
  - The company has been beleaguered by lawsuits and financial difficulties.
  - 公司一直被诉讼和财务困难所困扰。
  - The beleaguered city held out for three months before surrendering.
  - 被围困的城市坚持了三个月才投降。
- **助记**：be + leaguer（围困）→ 被围困 → 困扰

### 6. **sanguine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：乐观的；自信的；充满希望的
- **英文释义**：optimistic or positive, especially in an apparently bad or difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - Despite the setbacks, she remained sanguine about the project's future.
  - 尽管遭受挫折，她对项目的未来仍然乐观。
  - The investors were sanguine that the economy would recover by next quarter.
  - 投资者对经济将在下个季度复苏持乐观态度。
- **助记**：sanguin（血）+ e → 血色好 → 乐观的

### 7. **supersede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：取代；替代；淘汰
- **英文释义**：to take the place of something previously in use or to make obsolete
- **例句**：
  - Smartphones have largely superseded traditional cameras for everyday photography.
  - 智能手机在日常摄影中已基本取代了传统相机。
  - The new safety guidelines supersede all previous versions.
  - 新的安全指南取代了所有先前的版本。
- **助记**：super（在上面）+ sede（坐）→ 坐在上面 → 取代

### 8. **litigious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：好诉讼的；爱打官司的
- **英文释义**：tending to engage in lawsuits; quarrelsome
- **例句**：
  - The litigious neighbor threatened to sue over every minor dispute.
  - 爱打官司的邻居扬言要对每一个小纠纷提起诉讼。
  - In an increasingly litigious society, companies must be cautious about potential lawsuits.
  - 在一个日益好诉讼的社会中，公司必须对潜在诉讼保持谨慎。
- **助记**：litig（诉讼）+ ious → 爱诉讼的 → 好打官司的

### 9. **encapsulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：概括；浓缩；封装
- **英文释义**：to express the essential features of something succinctly; to enclose in a capsule
- **例句**：
  - This single photograph encapsulates the spirit of the entire movement.
  - 这张照片概括了整个运动的精神。
  - The author encapsulated decades of research into a 300-page book.
  - 作者将数十年的研究浓缩成一本300页的书。
- **助记**：en（使）+ capsul（胶囊）+ ate → 装入胶囊 → 浓缩

### 10. **indignant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：愤愤不平的；义愤填膺的
- **英文释义**：feeling or showing anger or annoyance at what is perceived as unfair treatment
- **例句**：
  - The workers were indignant about the unfair wage cuts.
  - 工人们对不公平的工资削减感到愤愤不平。
  - She wrote an indignant letter to the editor protesting the article's inaccuracies.
  - 她给编辑写了一封义愤填膺的信，抗议文章的不准确之处。
- **助记**：in（不）+ dign（值得）+ ant → 不值得如此对待 → 愤愤不平的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **palimpsest** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：重写本；被覆盖后重新使用的羊皮纸；有多层含义的事物
- **英文释义**：a manuscript or piece of writing material on which the original writing has been effaced to make room for later writing
- **例句**：
  - The ancient palimpsest revealed traces of a much older text beneath the visible writing.
  - 这本古老的重写本在可见文字下面显露出更早文本的痕迹。
  - The city itself is a palimpsest, with layers of history visible in its architecture.
  - 这座城市本身就是一本重写本，在其建筑中可以看到历史的层叠。
- **助记**：palin（再次）+ psest（擦）→ 再次擦掉重写 → 重写本

### 12. **inefficacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无效的；不起作用的
- **英文释义**：not producing the desired effect
- **例句**：
  - The treatment proved inefficacious against the advanced stage of the disease.
  - 该治疗方法对疾病晚期被证明是无效的。
  - Previous attempts at reform were largely inefficacious due to poor implementation.
  - 以前的改革尝试由于执行不力大多无效。
- **助记**：in（不）+ efficacious（有效的）→ 不有效的 → 无效的

### 13. **gossamer** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：蛛丝；薄纱；极其轻薄的
- **英文释义**：a fine, filmy substance of cobwebs; something very light and insubstantial
- **例句**：
  - The morning dew clung to gossamer threads of spider silk in the garden.
  - 晨露挂在花园里蛛丝般纤细的丝线上。
  - She wore a gossamer veil that floated in the gentle breeze.
  - 她戴着一条在微风中飘动的薄如蛛丝的面纱。
- **助记**：gos（鹅）+ samer（夏天）→ 鹅夏天的羽毛般轻盈 → 极其轻薄的

### 14. **machiavellian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：权术的；不择手段的；诡计多端的
- **英文释义**：cunning, scheming, and unscrupulous, especially in politics
- **例句**：
  - The CEO's machiavellian tactics helped him eliminate his rivals within the company.
  - 这位首席执行官不择手段的权术帮助他铲除了公司内部的对手。
  - Critics accused the politician of machiavellian manipulation of the media.
  - 批评者指责该政治家对媒体进行诡计多端的操纵。
- **助记**：来自意大利政治思想家 Machiavelli（马基雅维利），著有《君主论》→ 权术的

### 15. **crepuscular** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：黄昏的；微明的；薄暮的
- **英文释义**：relating to or resembling twilight; active during twilight
- **例句**：
  - The crepuscular light of dusk painted the sky in shades of purple and orange.
  - 黄昏时分微明的光将天空染成了紫色和橙色。
  - Deer are crepuscular animals, most active during dawn and dusk.
  - 鹿是黄昏活动的动物，在黎明和黄昏时最为活跃。
- **助记**：crepusc（黄昏）+ ular → 黄昏的
