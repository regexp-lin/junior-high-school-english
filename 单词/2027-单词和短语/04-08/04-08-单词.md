## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **appliance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：电器；器具；装置
- **英文释义**：a device or piece of equipment designed to perform a specific task, typically a domestic one
- **例句**：
  - The kitchen is equipped with modern appliances.
  - 厨房配备了现代化的电器。
- **助记**：appli（应用）+ ance → 被应用的东西 → 电器/器具

### 2. **ceramic** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 陶瓷的 n. 陶瓷制品
- **英文释义**：adj. made of clay and permanently hardened by heat; n. pots and other articles made from clay
- **例句**：
  - The museum has a fine collection of ceramic vases.
  - 博物馆收藏了许多精美的陶瓷花瓶。
- **助记**：ceram（黏土/陶）+ ic → 用黏土做的 → 陶瓷的

### 3. **destiny** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：命运；天命
- **英文释义**：the events that will necessarily happen to a particular person or thing in the future
- **例句**：
  - She believed it was her destiny to become a doctor.
  - 她相信成为医生是她的命运。
- **助记**：destin（注定）+ y → 注定的事 → 命运

### 4. **fierce** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：凶猛的；猛烈的；激烈的
- **英文释义**：having or displaying an intense or ferocious aggressiveness
- **例句**：
  - The fierce storm destroyed many houses in the village.
  - 猛烈的暴风雨摧毁了村里的许多房屋。
- **助记**：fierce 谐音"飞尔斯"→ 飞来的力量 → 猛烈的

### 5. **gratitude** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：感激；感谢
- **英文释义**：the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation for kindness
- **例句**：
  - She expressed her gratitude to everyone who had helped her.
  - 她向每一个帮助过她的人表达了感激之情。
- **助记**：grat（感激）+ itude → 感激的程度 → 感激

### 6. **juvenile** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 青少年的；幼稚的 n. 青少年
- **英文释义**：adj. of, for, or relating to young people; n. a young person
- **例句**：
  - Juvenile crime rates have decreased in recent years.
  - 近年来青少年犯罪率有所下降。
- **助记**：juven（年轻）+ ile → 年轻的 → 青少年的

### 7. **morality** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：道德；品行；道德观
- **英文释义**：principles concerning the distinction between right and wrong or good and bad behavior
- **例句**：
  - The film raises important questions about morality.
  - 这部电影提出了关于道德的重要问题。
- **助记**：moral（道德的）+ ity → 道德的性质 → 道德

### 8. **perceive** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：感知；察觉；理解
- **英文释义**：to become aware of through the senses; to interpret or regard in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - She perceived a change in his attitude.
  - 她察觉到他态度的变化。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ ceive（抓住）→ 完全抓住信息 → 感知

### 9. **sabotage** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 蓄意破坏 v. 蓄意破坏；暗中破坏
- **英文释义**：n. the deliberate destruction of something; v. to deliberately destroy or damage
- **例句**：
  - The spy was caught trying to sabotage the enemy's communication system.
  - 间谍在试图破坏敌方通信系统时被抓获。
- **助记**：sabotage 谐音"撒泼他就"→ 撒泼搞破坏 → 蓄意破坏

### 10. **voyage** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：航行；航海；旅程
- **英文释义**：a long journey involving travel by sea or in space
- **例句**：
  - Columbus set out on his famous voyage across the Atlantic in 1492.
  - 哥伦布于1492年开始了他著名的横渡大西洋航行。
- **助记**：voy（路）+ age → 长途旅程 → 航行

### 高一词汇

### 11. **articulate** `高一` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：adj. 表达清晰的 v. 清楚地表达；明确说明
- **英文释义**：adj. having or showing the ability to speak fluently and coherently; v. to express clearly
- **例句**：
  - She is an articulate speaker who captivates every audience.
  - 她是一位表达清晰的演讲者，每次都能吸引听众。
- **助记**：art（技巧）+ icul（关节）+ ate → 像关节一样灵活地表达 → 清晰表达

### 12. **dichotomy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：二分法；两极分化；对立
- **英文释义**：a division or contrast between two things that are presented as being entirely different
- **例句**：
  - There is a dichotomy between theory and practice in education.
  - 教育中存在理论与实践的对立。
- **助记**：dicho（二）+ tomy（切）→ 切成两半 → 二分法

### 13. **ephemeral** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：短暂的；转瞬即逝的
- **英文释义**：lasting for a very short time
- **例句**：
  - Fame can be ephemeral — here today, gone tomorrow.
  - 名声可能是短暂的——今天在，明天就没了。
- **助记**：epher（一天）+ al → 只持续一天的 → 短暂的

### 14. **precarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不稳定的；危险的；不确定的
- **英文释义**：not securely held or in position; dangerously likely to fall or collapse
- **例句**：
  - The company is in a precarious financial situation.
  - 公司处于不稳定的财务状况中。
- **助记**：pre（祈祷）+ car（小心）+ ious → 需要祈祷和小心的 → 不稳定的

### 15. **substantiate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：证实；证明……有根据
- **英文释义**：to provide evidence to support or prove the truth of something
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer could not substantiate his client's claims.
  - 律师无法证实其当事人的主张。
- **助记**：sub（下面）+ stant（站立）+ iate → 让论点站得住脚 → 证实
