## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **inevitable** /ɪnˈevɪtəbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不可避免的；必然的
- **英文释义**：certain to happen; unavoidable
- **例句**：
  - Change is inevitable in every society.
  - 变化在每个社会中都是不可避免的。
  - It was inevitable that they would lose the game.
  - 他们输掉比赛是不可避免的。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ evit（避免）+ -able → 不可避免的

### 2. **inferior** /ɪnˈfɪəriə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：较差的；下级的
- **英文释义**：lower in quality or rank
- **例句**：
  - This product is inferior to the one we bought last time.
  - 这个产品比我们上次买的差。
  - Nobody should feel inferior because of their background.
  - 没有人应该因为出身而感到自卑。
- **助记**：infer-（下面）+ -ior → 在下面的 → 较差的

### 3. **infinite** /ˈɪnfɪnət/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：无限的；无穷的
- **英文释义**：limitless or endless in space, extent, or size
- **例句**：
  - The universe is believed to be infinite.
  - 人们认为宇宙是无限的。
  - She has infinite patience with her students.
  - 她对学生有无限的耐心。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ finite（有限的）→ 没有限制的 → 无限的

### 4. **inflation** /ɪnˈfleɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：通货膨胀；膨胀
- **英文释义**：a general increase in prices and fall in the purchasing value of money
- **例句**：
  - Inflation has risen to 5% this year.
  - 今年通货膨胀率上升至5%。
  - High inflation makes life harder for ordinary people.
  - 高通胀让普通人生活更艰难。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ flat（吹）+ -ion → 不断吹大 → 膨胀

### 5. **influence** /ˈɪnfluəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：影响；感化
- **英文释义**：the power to change someone's behaviour or opinion; to affect
- **例句**：
  - Parents have a great influence on their children.
  - 父母对孩子有很大的影响。
  - Music can influence people's emotions.
  - 音乐能影响人的情绪。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ flu（流）+ -ence → 流进某人心中 → 影响

### 6. **initial** /ɪˈnɪʃl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：最初的；首字母
- **英文释义**：existing or occurring at the beginning; the first letter of a name
- **例句**：
  - The initial reaction was one of shock.
  - 最初的反应是震惊。
  - Please write your initials here.
  - 请在这里写上你名字的首字母。
- **助记**：init-（开始）+ -ial → 开始的 → 最初的

### 7. **innocent** /ˈɪnəsnt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：无辜的；天真的
- **英文释义**：not guilty of a crime; having no experience of evil
- **例句**：
  - The man was found innocent of all charges.
  - 这个人被判所有指控无罪。
  - She has an innocent smile that makes everyone like her.
  - 她天真的微笑让所有人都喜欢她。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ noc（伤害）+ -ent → 不伤害人的 → 无辜的

### 8. **innovation** /ˌɪnəˈveɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：创新；革新
- **英文释义**：a new idea, method, or device; the process of creating something new
- **例句**：
  - Technological innovation drives economic growth.
  - 技术创新推动经济增长。
  - The company is known for its innovation in design.
  - 这家公司以设计创新著称。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ nov（新的）+ -ation → 引入新事物 → 创新

### 9. **inquiry** /ɪnˈkwaɪəri/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：询问；调查
- **英文释义**：an act of asking for information; an investigation
- **例句**：
  - I made an inquiry about the course fees.
  - 我咨询了课程费用。
  - The police are conducting an inquiry into the accident.
  - 警方正在对事故进行调查。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ quir（寻求）+ -y → 进入寻求 → 询问

### 10. **insight** /ˈɪnsaɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：洞察力；见解
- **英文释义**：the ability to understand the true nature of something; a deep understanding
- **例句**：
  - The book provides valuable insights into Chinese culture.
  - 这本书提供了对中国文化的宝贵见解。
  - She has great insight into human behaviour.
  - 她对人类行为有深刻的洞察力。
- **助记**：in-（向内）+ sight（看）→ 向内看穿本质 → 洞察力

### 高一词汇

### 11. **brusque** /bruːsk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：粗鲁的；唐突的
- **英文释义**：rudely abrupt or blunt in speech or manner
- **例句**：
  - His brusque manner offended many of his colleagues.
  - 他粗鲁的态度冒犯了很多同事。
  - She gave a brusque reply without looking up.
  - 她头都没抬就粗鲁地回了一句。
- **助记**：brusque → brush（刷）→ 像刷子一样粗糙 → 粗鲁的

### 12. **condescend** /ˌkɒndɪˈsend/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：屈尊；以恩赐态度对待
- **英文释义**：to show that you feel you are superior to others
- **例句**：
  - She condescended to speak to us as if we were children.
  - 她以居高临下的态度跟我们说话，好像我们是小孩。
  - Don't condescend to people just because you have more experience.
  - 不要因为你经验多就以恩赐的态度对人。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ descend（下降）→ 从高处降下来 → 屈尊

### 13. **disparage** /dɪˈspærɪdʒ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：贬低；轻视
- **英文释义**：to regard or represent as being of little worth
- **例句**：
  - He disparaged his rival's achievements in public.
  - 他在公开场合贬低了对手的成就。
  - Don't disparage yourself — you have many talents.
  - 别贬低自己——你有很多才能。
- **助记**：dis-（否定）+ par（相等）+ -age → 说不相等/不配 → 贬低

### 14. **elucidate** /ɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：阐明；解释清楚
- **英文释义**：to make something clear; to explain
- **例句**：
  - The teacher elucidated the complex theory for the students.
  - 老师为学生阐明了复杂的理论。
  - Could you elucidate your point further?
  - 你能进一步解释清楚你的观点吗？
- **助记**：e-（出）+ lucid（清晰的）+ -ate → 使清晰出来 → 阐明

### 15. **frivolous** /ˈfrɪvələs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：轻浮的；不严肃的
- **英文释义**：not having any serious purpose or value
- **例句**：
  - Stop asking frivolous questions and focus on the task.
  - 别问无聊的问题了，专心做事。
  - She was accused of spending money on frivolous things.
  - 她被指责把钱花在无意义的东西上。
- **助记**：frivol-（无价值的）+ -ous → 没有价值的 → 轻浮的
