## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **admonish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：警告；告诫；劝告
- **英文释义**：to warn or reprimand someone firmly; to advise or urge earnestly
- **例句**：
  - The teacher admonished the students for not completing their homework on time.
  - 老师告诫学生们没有按时完成作业。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ mon（警告）+ ish → 向某人发出警告 → 告诫

### 2. **buoyant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：乐观的；有浮力的；上涨的
- **英文释义**：able to stay afloat; cheerful and optimistic; tending to increase
- **例句**：
  - Despite the challenges, she maintained a buoyant attitude throughout the project.
  - 尽管面临挑战，她在整个项目中保持了乐观的态度。
- **助记**：buoy（浮标）+ ant → 像浮标一样浮在水面 → 有浮力的/乐观的

### 3. **clandestine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：秘密的；暗中的；偷偷摸摸的
- **英文释义**：kept secret or done secretively, especially because illicit
- **例句**：
  - The agents held a clandestine meeting to discuss classified information.
  - 特工们举行了一次秘密会议来讨论机密信息。
- **助记**：clan（部族）+ destine（隐藏）→ 在部族中隐藏 → 秘密的

### 4. **disparity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：差距；不等；悬殊
- **英文释义**：a great difference; inequality
- **例句**：
  - The disparity in income between the rich and poor continues to widen.
  - 贫富之间的收入差距继续扩大。
- **助记**：dis（不）+ par（相等）+ ity → 不相等 → 差距

### 5. **evade** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：逃避；回避；规避
- **英文释义**：to escape or avoid, especially by cleverness or trickery
- **例句**：
  - The suspect managed to evade the police for three weeks before being caught.
  - 嫌疑犯设法逃避了警方三周才被抓获。
- **助记**：e（出）+ vade（走）→ 走出去逃掉 → 逃避

### 6. **frivolous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：轻浮的；不严肃的；无价值的
- **英文释义**：not having any serious purpose or value; carefree and not serious
- **例句**：
  - The judge dismissed the frivolous lawsuit as a waste of the court's time.
  - 法官驳回了这项无聊的诉讼，认为它浪费法庭时间。
- **助记**：friv（轻浮）+ olous → 轻浮的 → 不严肃的

### 7. **impeccable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无可挑剔的；完美的；无瑕的
- **英文释义**：in accordance with the highest standards of propriety; faultless
- **例句**：
  - Her impeccable manners and elegant style impressed everyone at the dinner party.
  - 她无可挑剔的礼仪和优雅的风度给晚宴上的每个人留下了深刻印象。
- **助记**：im（不）+ pecc（犯罪/瑕疵）+ able → 不会犯错的 → 无可挑剔的

### 8. **manifest** `初三` v./adj.

- **中文释义**：v. 表明；显示 adj. 明显的；明白的
- **英文释义**：v. to display or show clearly; adj. clear or obvious to the eye or mind
- **例句**：
  - His frustration manifested itself in his increasingly aggressive tone.
  - 他的沮丧通过越来越激进的语气表现了出来。
- **助记**：mani（手）+ fest（打击）→ 用手展示出来 → 表明/明显的

### 9. **plausible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：似乎有理的；貌似可信的；花言巧语的
- **英文释义**：seeming reasonable or probable; appearing worthy of belief
- **例句**：
  - His excuse sounded plausible, but the evidence proved otherwise.
  - 他的借口听起来似乎有理，但证据证明并非如此。
- **助记**：plaus（鼓掌）+ ible → 让人鼓掌叫好的 → 似乎有理的

### 10. **sturdy** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坚固的；结实的；健壮的
- **英文释义**：strongly and solidly built; showing confidence and determination
- **例句**：
  - The hikers wore sturdy boots to protect their feet on the rocky trail.
  - 徒步者穿着结实的靴子以保护双脚在岩石小路上行走。
- **助记**：sturdy → 联想"死缠的"→ 像缠住一样坚固 → 坚固的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **anomaly** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：异常；反常现象；不规则
- **英文释义**：something that deviates from what is standard, normal, or expected
- **例句**：
  - The sudden temperature drop in summer was a weather anomaly that puzzled scientists.
  - 夏季气温的突然下降是一种让科学家困惑的天气异常现象。
- **助记**：a（不）+ nomal（正常）+ y → 不正常的 → 异常

### 12. **esoteric** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：深奥的；只有内行才懂的；秘传的
- **英文释义**：intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with specialized knowledge
- **例句**：
  - The lecture covered such esoteric topics that only a handful of experts could follow.
  - 讲座涵盖了如此深奥的话题，只有少数专家能跟上。
- **助记**：eso（内部）+ teric → 内部人才懂的 → 深奥的

### 13. **malfeasance** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：渎职；不法行为；违法乱纪
- **英文释义**：wrongdoing, especially by a public official
- **例句**：
  - The mayor was removed from office for financial malfeasance.
  - 市长因财务渎职被免职。
- **助记**：mal（坏）+ feas（做）+ ance → 做坏事 → 渎职/不法行为

### 14. **querulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱抱怨的；多牢骚的；爱发牢骚的
- **英文释义**：complaining in a petulant or whining manner
- **例句**：
  - The querulous patient complained about everything from the hospital food to the noise.
  - 这位爱抱怨的病人对医院的伙食和噪音等所有事情都有意见。
- **助记**：quer（抱怨）+ ulous → 不停抱怨的 → 爱抱怨的

### 15. **trenchant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：犀利的；一针见血的；鲜明的
- **英文释义**：vigorous or incisive in expression or style; clear and effective
- **例句**：
  - The journalist's trenchant criticism of the government's policy went viral online.
  - 这位记者对政府政策的犀利批评在网上疯传。
- **助记**：trench（切割/壕沟）+ ant → 像切割一样犀利 → 犀利的
