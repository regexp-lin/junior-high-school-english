## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **emanate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：散发；发出；起源于
- **英文释义**：to issue or spread out from a source
- **例句**：
  - A warm glow emanated from the fireplace, filling the room with light.
  - 壁炉散发出温暖的光芒，房间里充满了光亮。
  - The strange noise seemed to emanate from the basement.
  - 奇怪的声音似乎是从地下室传来的。
- **助记**：e（出）+ man（流）+ ate → 流出来 → 散发

### 2. **intrepid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：勇敢的；无畏的
- **英文释义**：fearless; adventurous
- **例句**：
  - The intrepid journalist traveled to war-torn countries to report the truth.
  - 无畏的记者前往战火纷飞的国家报道真相。
  - Only the most intrepid hikers attempt the treacherous mountain pass in winter.
  - 只有最勇敢的徒步旅行者才会在冬天尝试那条危险的山口。
- **助记**：in（不）+ trepid（恐惧）→ 不恐惧的 → 勇敢的

### 3. **postulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：假定；假设；提出
- **英文释义**：to suggest or assume the existence, fact, or truth of something as a basis for reasoning
- **例句**：
  - The scientist postulated that the universe began with a massive explosion.
  - 科学家假设宇宙始于一次巨大的爆炸。
  - The theory postulates a direct link between diet and longevity.
  - 该理论假定饮食与长寿之间存在直接联系。
- **助记**：post（放置）+ ulate → 放在前面作为前提 → 假定

### 4. **nefarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：邪恶的；罪恶的；不道德的
- **英文释义**：wicked or criminal
- **例句**：
  - The nefarious plot to defraud investors was uncovered by the authorities.
  - 欺骗投资者的邪恶阴谋被当局揭露了。
  - The villain in the story was known for his nefarious deeds.
  - 故事中的反派以其罪恶行径而闻名。
- **助记**：ne（不）+ far（正义/法律）+ ious → 不正义的 → 邪恶的

### 5. **substantiate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证实；证明；使具体化
- **英文释义**：to provide evidence to support or prove the truth of
- **例句**：
  - The prosecution failed to substantiate its claims with sufficient evidence.
  - 控方未能以充分证据证实其主张。
  - Can you substantiate your allegation with concrete examples?
  - 你能用具体例子来证实你的指控吗？
- **助记**：sub（在下面）+ stant（站立）+ iate → 在下面建立支撑 → 证实

### 6. **paradoxical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：矛盾的；自相矛盾的；似非而是的
- **英文释义**：seemingly absurd or self-contradictory but potentially true
- **例句**：
  - It seems paradoxical that exercise, which tires you out, actually gives you more energy.
  - 运动会让你疲惫但实际上给你更多能量，这似乎很矛盾。
  - The paradoxical nature of quantum physics continues to puzzle scientists.
  - 量子物理学的矛盾本质继续困扰着科学家。
- **助记**：para（旁边/异常）+ dox（观点）+ ical → 异常的观点 → 矛盾的

### 7. **extraneous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无关的；外来的；多余的
- **英文释义**：irrelevant or unrelated to the subject being dealt with
- **例句**：
  - The teacher asked students to remove any extraneous information from their essays.
  - 老师要求学生从论文中删除任何无关信息。
  - Extraneous noise from outside disturbed the concentration of the exam candidates.
  - 外面的多余噪音干扰了考生的注意力。
- **助记**：extra（外面）+ neous → 外面来的 → 无关的

### 8. **astute** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：精明的；机敏的；敏锐的
- **英文释义**：having or showing an ability to accurately assess situations and turn this to one's advantage
- **例句**：
  - The astute businesswoman recognized the opportunity and invested early.
  - 精明的女商人识别了机会并早期投资。
  - His astute observation helped the team identify the root cause of the problem.
  - 他敏锐的观察帮助团队找到了问题的根本原因。
- **助记**：astut（狡黠/精明）+ e → 精明的

### 9. **coerce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：强迫；胁迫；强制
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone to do something by using force or threats
- **例句**：
  - The witness was coerced into giving a false testimony.
  - 证人被胁迫作出虚假证词。
  - No one should be coerced into signing an agreement against their will.
  - 任何人都不应被强迫违背意愿签署协议。
- **助记**：co（一起）+ erce（关闭/限制）→ 把人限制住逼迫 → 强迫

### 10. **diligent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：勤勉的；用功的；刻苦的
- **英文释义**：having or showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties
- **例句**：
  - The diligent student spent hours in the library preparing for her final exams.
  - 勤勉的学生在图书馆花了好几个小时准备期末考试。
  - Diligent research is essential for producing a high-quality academic paper.
  - 勤勉的研究对于撰写高质量的学术论文至关重要。
- **助记**：dilig（珍爱/用心）+ ent → 用心对待 → 勤勉的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **tintinnabulation** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：叮当声；铃声；钟声
- **英文释义**：a ringing or tinkling sound
- **例句**：
  - The tintinnabulation of the church bells echoed through the quiet village.
  - 教堂钟声的叮当声回荡在安静的村庄中。
  - Edgar Allan Poe famously used the word tintinnabulation in his poem "The Bells."
  - 爱伦·坡在他的诗作《钟》中著名地使用了 tintinnabulation 这个词。
- **助记**：tintinn（叮当响）+ abul + ation → 叮当作响 → 铃声

### 12. **soporific** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：催眠的；令人昏昏欲睡的
- **英文释义**：tending to induce drowsiness or sleep
- **例句**：
  - The professor's soporific lecture put half the class to sleep.
  - 教授催眠般的讲座让半个班的学生都睡着了。
  - The warm room and soft music had a soporific effect on the audience.
  - 温暖的房间和柔和的音乐对观众有催眠效果。
- **助记**：sopor（睡眠）+ ific → 引起睡眠的 → 催眠的

### 13. **desultory** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：散漫的；无计划的；漫无目的的
- **英文释义**：lacking a plan, purpose, or enthusiasm
- **例句**：
  - They had a desultory conversation about the weather while waiting for the bus.
  - 他们在等公交时漫无目的地聊着天气。
  - His desultory approach to studying resulted in poor exam performance.
  - 他散漫的学习方式导致了糟糕的考试成绩。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ sult（跳）+ ory → 到处乱跳 → 散漫的

### 14. **panegyric** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：颂词；颂辞；赞美的演说
- **英文释义**：a public speech or published text in praise of someone or something
- **例句**：
  - The eulogy at the funeral was a heartfelt panegyric to the beloved teacher.
  - 葬礼上的悼词是对这位受人爱戴的老师的衷心颂辞。
  - The book reads like a panegyric to the golden age of cinema.
  - 这本书读起来像是对电影黄金时代的颂歌。
- **助记**：pan（全部）+ egyr（集会）+ ic → 在集会上全面赞美 → 颂词

### 15. **lissome** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：柔软灵活的；轻盈的；矫健的
- **英文释义**：thin, supple, and graceful
- **例句**：
  - The lissome ballerina moved across the stage with effortless grace.
  - 轻盈的芭蕾舞者毫不费力地优雅地穿越舞台。
  - Her lissome figure glided through the crowd like a willow in the breeze.
  - 她柔软灵活的身影像微风中的柳树一样滑过人群。
- **助记**：lissome = lithe（灵活）+ some → 灵活的 → 柔软灵活的
