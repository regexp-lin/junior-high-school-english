## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **equivocate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：含糊其辞；模棱两可地说；支吾
- **英文释义**：to use ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself
- **例句**：
  - The CEO equivocated when asked about potential layoffs, refusing to give a direct answer.
  - 当被问及可能的裁员时，CEO含糊其辞，拒绝给出直接回答。
  - Stop equivocating and tell us whether you support the proposal or not.
  - 别再模棱两可了，告诉我们你是否支持这个提案。
- **助记**：equi（相等）+ voc（声音）+ ate → 两边都说/声音一样 → 含糊其辞

### 2. **meticulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；极其细心的；严谨的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - The architect's meticulous planning ensured that every detail of the building was perfect.
  - 建筑师一丝不苟的规划确保了建筑的每一个细节都完美。
  - She kept meticulous records of all her experiments and their results.
  - 她对所有实验及其结果保持了严谨的记录。
- **助记**：metus（恐惧）+ ulous → 害怕出错 → 一丝不苟的

### 3. **debacle** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：惨败；彻底失败；大溃败
- **英文释义**：a sudden and ignominious failure; a fiasco
- **例句**：
  - The product launch was a complete debacle, with servers crashing within minutes.
  - 产品发布是一场彻底的惨败，服务器在几分钟内就崩溃了。
  - The military debacle resulted in thousands of casualties and a humiliating retreat.
  - 军事大溃败导致了数千人伤亡和屈辱的撤退。
- **助记**：debacle 源自法语 débâcler（溃决）→ 像堤坝崩溃 → 大溃败

### 4. **surreptitious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：鬼鬼祟祟的；秘密的；偷偷摸摸的
- **英文释义**：kept secret, especially because it would not be approved of
- **例句**：
  - He cast a surreptitious glance at his phone during the meeting.
  - 他在会议期间偷偷看了一眼手机。
  - The spy's surreptitious activities were eventually uncovered by counterintelligence.
  - 间谍秘密的活动最终被反间谍机构发现了。
- **助记**：sur（在下面）+ rept（爬）+ itious → 在下面偷偷爬 → 鬼鬼祟祟的

### 5. **repudiate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：否认；拒绝接受；声明与…无关
- **英文释义**：to refuse to accept or be associated with; to deny the truth or validity of
- **例句**：
  - The politician repudiated the controversial statements attributed to him.
  - 政治家否认了归咎于他的争议性言论。
  - The company repudiated the contract, claiming it had been signed under duress.
  - 公司拒绝接受该合同，声称它是在胁迫下签署的。
- **助记**：re（回）+ pudi（砍/拒绝）+ ate → 砍回去 → 拒绝/否认

### 6. **magniloquent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：夸大的；华而不实的言辞
- **英文释义**：using high-flown or bombastic language
- **例句**：
  - His magniloquent speech was full of grand promises but short on practical plans.
  - 他夸大的演讲充满了宏大的承诺但缺乏实际计划。
  - The magniloquent writing style alienated readers who preferred straightforward prose.
  - 华而不实的写作风格让偏好朴实散文的读者敬而远之。
- **助记**：magni（大）+ loqu（说话）+ ent → 说大话 → 夸大的

### 7. **insinuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：暗示；影射；巧妙地潜入
- **英文释义**：to suggest or hint in an indirect and unpleasant way; to maneuver oneself into a position
- **例句**：
  - Are you insinuating that I had something to do with the missing funds?
  - 你是在暗示我和失踪的资金有关？
  - He managed to insinuate himself into the CEO's inner circle through flattery and charm.
  - 他通过奉承和魅力巧妙地打入了CEO的核心圈子。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ sinu（弯曲）+ ate → 弯弯曲曲地进入 → 暗示/潜入

### 8. **resilient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有弹性的；适应力强的；能迅速恢复的
- **英文释义**：able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions
- **例句**：
  - Children are remarkably resilient and can adapt to new environments faster than adults.
  - 孩子们有着惊人的适应力，能比成年人更快地适应新环境。
  - The resilient economy bounced back quickly from the recession.
  - 有弹性的经济从衰退中快速反弹。
- **助记**：re（回）+ sili（跳）+ ent → 跳回来 → 有弹性的/恢复力强的

### 9. **flagrant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：公然的；明目张胆的；臭名昭著的
- **英文释义**：(of something considered wrong or immoral) conspicuously or obviously offensive
- **例句**：
  - The referee's decision was a flagrant miscarriage of justice that outraged the fans.
  - 裁判的决定是公然的错判，激怒了球迷们。
  - The company's flagrant disregard for environmental regulations led to massive fines.
  - 公司明目张胆地无视环保法规，导致了巨额罚款。
- **助记**：flagr（燃烧）+ ant → 像火一样明显 → 明目张胆的

### 10. **propensity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：倾向；偏好；习性
- **英文释义**：an inclination or natural tendency to behave in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - His propensity for taking risks made him a successful but controversial entrepreneur.
  - 他冒险的倾向使他成为一个成功但有争议的企业家。
  - The cat has a propensity to knock things off tables when seeking attention.
  - 这只猫有在寻求注意力时把东西从桌上推下去的习性。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ pens（悬挂/倾斜）+ ity → 向前倾斜 → 倾向

### 高一词汇

### 11. **bricolage** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：拼凑创作；用手头材料创造性地构建
- **英文释义**：construction or creation from whatever is available; making creative use of whatever materials are at hand
- **例句**：
  - The artist's bricolage combined found objects, scrap metal, and recycled wood into stunning sculptures.
  - 这位艺术家的拼凑创作将拾得物、废金属和回收木材组合成令人惊叹的雕塑。
  - Modern cuisine is a form of bricolage, blending ingredients from diverse culinary traditions.
  - 现代烹饪是一种拼凑创作，融合了来自不同烹饪传统的食材。
- **助记**：bricolage 是法语，bricoler（修修补补）→ 用现有材料创造 → 拼凑创作

### 12. **jouissance** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：极致愉悦；超越快乐的深层享受
- **英文释义**：a French term for a transgressive, excessive kind of pleasure that goes beyond ordinary enjoyment
- **例句**：
  - The philosopher Barthes wrote about the jouissance of reading — a pleasure so intense it borders on ecstasy.
  - 哲学家巴特写道阅读的极致愉悦——一种强烈到接近狂喜的快感。
  - She found jouissance in the act of creation itself, not merely in the finished product.
  - 她在创作行为本身而非成品中找到了极致的愉悦。
- **助记**：jouissance 是法语，jouir（享受）→ 超越普通享受 → 极致愉悦

### 13. **sprezzatura** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：举重若轻；看似毫不费力的优雅
- **英文释义**：studied carelessness; the art of performing a difficult task so gracefully that it looks effortless
- **例句**：
  - The ballet dancer performed with such sprezzatura that the audience forgot how difficult the moves actually were.
  - 芭蕾舞者的表演如此举重若轻，观众忘记了动作实际上有多难。
  - True elegance requires sprezzatura — the ability to make complexity appear simple.
  - 真正的优雅需要举重若轻——让复杂显得简单的能力。
- **助记**：sprezzatura 是意大利语，sprezzare（轻视/不在意）→ 对难度不在意的优雅

### 14. **limerence** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：痴迷之爱；强烈的不由自主的恋爱渴望
- **英文释义**：the state of being infatuated or obsessed with another person, typically involuntary and characterized by a strong desire for reciprocation
- **例句**：
  - Limerence is different from mature love — it involves obsessive thoughts and emotional dependency.
  - 痴迷之爱不同于成熟的爱——它涉及强迫性的思念和情感依赖。
  - The early stage of limerence can feel thrilling but is often unsustainable.
  - 痴迷之爱的早期阶段可能感觉令人兴奋，但往往不可持续。
- **助记**：limerence 由心理学家 Dorothy Tennov 于1979年创造，专门描述这种强烈的不自主恋爱状态

### 15. **hygge** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：惬意（丹麦文化中一种温馨、舒适、满足的感觉）
- **英文释义**：a Danish and Norwegian word for a mood of coziness and comfortable conviviality with feelings of wellness and contentment
- **例句**：
  - The Danish concept of hygge involves candlelight, warm blankets, and good company on a cold evening.
  - 丹麦的 hygge 概念包括在寒冷的夜晚点蜡烛、盖温暖的毯子、有好友相伴。
  - She created a hygge atmosphere at home with soft lighting, hot cocoa, and her favorite music.
  - 她在家里营造了 hygge 的氛围——柔和的灯光、热可可和她最喜欢的音乐。
- **助记**：hygge 是丹麦语/挪威语，表达一种温馨舒适的生活态度和感觉
