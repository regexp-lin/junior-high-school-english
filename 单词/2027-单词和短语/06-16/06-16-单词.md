## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **apprehend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：逮捕；理解；担忧
- **英文释义**：arrest someone for a crime; understand; anticipate with anxiety
- **例句**：
  - The police apprehended the suspect near the train station.
  - 警方在火车站附近逮捕了嫌疑犯。
- **助记**：ap（向）+ prehend（抓住）→ 抓住 → 逮捕/理解

### 2. **candor** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：坦白；直率
- **英文释义**：the quality of being open and honest in expression
- **例句**：
  - I appreciate your candor in telling me the truth about the situation.
  - 我感谢你坦率地告诉我事情的真相。
- **助记**：cand（白色/光亮）+ or → 像光一样透明 → 坦白

### 3. **convene** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：召集；集合；开会
- **英文释义**：come or bring together for a meeting or activity
- **例句**：
  - The committee convened to discuss the new policy proposal.
  - 委员会召开会议讨论新的政策提案。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ vene（来）→ 一起来 → 召集

### 4. **digress** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：离题；偏离主题
- **英文释义**：leave the main subject temporarily in speech or writing
- **例句**：
  - I digress — let me get back to the main point.
  - 我离题了——让我回到主要问题上。
- **助记**：di（离开）+ gress（走）→ 走离主题 → 离题

### 5. **exert** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：施加（压力）；运用；努力
- **英文释义**：apply or bring to bear (a force, influence, or quality)
- **例句**：
  - Parents exert a huge influence on their children's development.
  - 父母对孩子的成长施加巨大的影响。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ert（用力）→ 用力发出 → 施加

### 6. **futile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：徒劳的；无用的
- **英文释义**：incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
- **例句**：
  - It would be futile to argue with someone who refuses to listen.
  - 和一个拒绝倾听的人争论是徒劳的。
- **助记**：fut（流）+ ile → 像水一样流走 → 白费的

### 7. **harbinger** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：先兆；预兆；先驱
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that announces or signals the approach of another
- **例句**：
  - The robin is often considered a harbinger of spring.
  - 知更鸟通常被认为是春天的先兆。
- **助记**：harbor（港口）+ inger → 在港口先到的人 → 先驱

### 8. **insidious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：阴险的；潜伏的；暗中为害的
- **英文释义**：proceeding in a gradual, subtle way, but with harmful effects
- **例句**：
  - The insidious effects of air pollution often go unnoticed until it's too late.
  - 空气污染的隐性危害往往在为时已晚之前不被注意到。
- **助记**：in（在内）+ sid（坐）+ ious → 坐在里面暗中搞破坏 → 阴险的

### 9. **negate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：否定；取消；使无效
- **英文释义**：nullify; make ineffective; deny the existence of
- **例句**：
  - One bad review doesn't negate all the positive feedback we've received.
  - 一条差评并不能否定我们收到的所有正面反馈。
- **助记**：neg（否定）+ ate → 否定

### 10. **preeminent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：卓越的；出类拔萃的
- **英文释义**：surpassing all others; very distinguished in some way
- **例句**：
  - She is the preeminent scholar in the field of artificial intelligence.
  - 她是人工智能领域最卓越的学者。
- **助记**：pre（在前面）+ eminent（杰出的）→ 走在杰出前面 → 出类拔萃的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **amalgamate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；混合
- **英文释义**：combine or unite to form one organization or structure
- **例句**：
  - The two companies decided to amalgamate to become more competitive.
  - 两家公司决定合并以提高竞争力。
- **助记**：a + malgam（合金）+ ate → 像合金一样混合 → 合并

### 12. **elucidate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：阐明；解释清楚
- **英文释义**：make something clear; explain
- **例句**：
  - The professor elucidated the complex theory with simple examples.
  - 教授用简单的例子阐明了复杂的理论。
- **助记**：e（出）+ lucid（清楚的）+ ate → 使变得清楚 → 阐明

### 13. **inexplicable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无法解释的；莫名其妙的
- **英文释义**：unable to be explained or accounted for
- **例句**：
  - His sudden disappearance remains inexplicable to this day.
  - 他的突然失踪至今仍然无法解释。
- **助记**：in（不）+ explicable（可解释的）→ 不可解释的

### 14. **perspicacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：洞察力强的；敏锐的
- **英文释义**：having a ready insight into and understanding of things
- **例句**：
  - The perspicacious detective noticed a clue that everyone else had missed.
  - 这位敏锐的侦探注意到了其他人都忽略的线索。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ spic（看）+ acious → 看穿一切的 → 洞察力强的

### 15. **sanguine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：乐观的；充满希望的
- **英文释义**：optimistic or positive, especially in a difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - Despite the setbacks, she remained sanguine about the project's success.
  - 尽管遇到了挫折，她对项目的成功仍然保持乐观。
- **助记**：sangui（血）+ ne → 血气旺盛的 → 乐观的
