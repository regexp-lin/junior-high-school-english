## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **proclaim** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：宣布；宣告；声明
- **英文释义**：to announce officially or publicly
- **例句**：
  - The president proclaimed a national day of mourning.
  - 总统宣布了全国哀悼日。
  - The results were proclaimed in front of the entire school.
  - 结果在全校师生面前宣布。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ claim（叫喊）→ 向前叫喊 → 宣布

### 2. **adverse** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不利的；有害的；相反的
- **英文释义**：preventing success or development; harmful; unfavorable
- **例句**：
  - Adverse weather conditions forced the cancellation of the outdoor event.
  - 不利的天气状况迫使户外活动取消。
  - The drug may have adverse side effects on some patients.
  - 这种药物可能对某些患者产生不良副作用。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ verse（转）→ 转向不利方向 → 不利的

### 3. **alleviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和；缓解
- **英文释义**：to make suffering, a problem, or a bad situation less severe
- **例句**：
  - Taking a warm bath can help alleviate muscle pain after exercise.
  - 运动后泡个热水澡有助于缓解肌肉疼痛。
  - The government introduced measures to alleviate poverty in rural areas.
  - 政府推出了减轻农村贫困的措施。
- **助记**：al（加强）+ levi（轻）+ ate → 使变轻 → 减轻

### 4. **compulsory** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：强制的；义务的；必修的
- **英文释义**：required by law or a rule; obligatory
- **例句**：
  - Education is compulsory for children between the ages of six and sixteen.
  - 六岁至十六岁的儿童必须接受义务教育。
  - Wearing a seatbelt is compulsory for all passengers.
  - 所有乘客必须系安全带。
- **助记**：compuls（强迫）+ ory → 强迫性的 → 强制的

### 5. **delegate** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：委派；授权；代表
- **英文释义**：to entrust a task or responsibility to another person; a person authorized to represent others
- **例句**：
  - The manager delegated the task to her most experienced team member.
  - 经理把任务委派给了她最有经验的团队成员。
  - Each country sent delegates to the international conference.
  - 每个国家都派代表参加国际会议。
- **助记**：de（下）+ leg（法律/派遣）+ ate → 向下派遣 → 委派

### 6. **erode** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：侵蚀；腐蚀；逐渐减弱
- **英文释义**：to gradually destroy or wear away; to diminish
- **例句**：
  - Waves constantly erode the cliffs along the coastline.
  - 海浪不断侵蚀着海岸线上的悬崖。
  - Public confidence in the system has been eroded by repeated scandals.
  - 反复的丑闻逐渐削弱了公众对该制度的信心。
- **助记**：e（出）+ rode（啃）→ 啃出来 → 侵蚀

### 7. **inception** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：开始；创始；成立
- **英文释义**：the establishment or starting point of an institution or activity
- **例句**：
  - Since its inception in 2005, the company has grown into a global brand.
  - 自2005年成立以来，该公司已发展成为全球品牌。
  - The program has helped thousands of students since its inception.
  - 该项目自创始以来已帮助了数千名学生。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ cept（拿）+ ion → 拿起来开始 → 创始

### 8. **resilient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有弹性的；恢复力强的；能迅速恢复的
- **英文释义**：able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions
- **例句**：
  - Children are often more resilient than adults in coping with change.
  - 儿童在应对变化方面往往比成人更有恢复力。
  - The resilient economy bounced back quickly after the crisis.
  - 有韧性的经济在危机后迅速反弹。
- **助记**：re（回）+ sili（跳）+ ent → 跳回来 → 有弹性的

### 9. **scrutinize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：仔细检查；审视；细察
- **英文释义**：to examine or inspect closely and thoroughly
- **例句**：
  - The auditor scrutinized every financial record for errors.
  - 审计员仔细检查了每一份财务记录是否有错误。
  - She scrutinized the contract before signing it.
  - 她在签字前仔细审视了合同。
- **助记**：scrut（检查）+ inize → 仔细检查 → 审视

### 10. **turbulent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：动荡的；混乱的；汹涌的
- **英文释义**：characterized by conflict, disorder, or confusion; moving violently
- **例句**：
  - The country went through a turbulent period after the revolution.
  - 这个国家在革命后经历了一段动荡时期。
  - The plane encountered turbulent air over the mountains.
  - 飞机在山区上空遇到了气流颠簸。
- **助记**：turb（搅动）+ ulent → 被搅动的 → 动荡的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **extemporaneous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：即兴的；无准备的；临时的
- **英文释义**：spoken or done without preparation
- **例句**：
  - She gave an extemporaneous speech that impressed everyone with its eloquence.
  - 她做了一场即兴演讲，以其雄辩打动了所有人。
  - Extemporaneous debate requires quick thinking and broad knowledge.
  - 即兴辩论需要快速思维和广泛的知识。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ tempor（时间）+ aneous → 在时间之外（没有准备时间）→ 即兴的

### 12. **pernicious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有害的；恶性的；有毒的
- **英文释义**：having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way
- **例句**：
  - The pernicious influence of social media on mental health is well documented.
  - 社交媒体对心理健康的有害影响已有充分记录。
  - Racism is a pernicious force that damages society at every level.
  - 种族主义是一种在各个层面破坏社会的恶性力量。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ nic（杀）+ ious → 完全杀害的 → 有害的

### 13. **vicissitude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：变迁；沧桑；兴衰
- **英文释义**：a change of circumstances or fortune, typically unwelcome
- **例句**：
  - The family endured the vicissitudes of war and eventually rebuilt their home.
  - 这个家庭经受了战争的沧桑，最终重建了家园。
  - Life's vicissitudes teach us to appreciate moments of peace and happiness.
  - 人生的变迁教会我们珍惜平静和幸福的时刻。
- **助记**：vicis（变化）+ situde → 变化的状态 → 变迁

### 14. **equanimity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：镇定；沉着；平静
- **英文释义**：mental calmness, composure, and evenness of temper, especially in difficult situations
- **例句**：
  - She handled the criticism with remarkable equanimity.
  - 她以非凡的镇定应对了批评。
  - Maintaining equanimity during a crisis is a sign of true leadership.
  - 在危机中保持沉着是真正领导力的标志。
- **助记**：equ（平等）+ anim（心灵）+ ity → 心灵平等 → 镇定

### 15. **tautology** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：同义反复；赘述；重言
- **英文释义**：the saying of the same thing twice in different words, generally considered a fault of style
- **例句**：
  - "Free gift" is a tautology because all gifts are inherently free.
  - "免费礼物"是同义反复，因为所有礼物本来就是免费的。
  - Good writing avoids tautology and unnecessary repetition.
  - 好的写作避免同义反复和不必要的重复。
- **助记**：tauto（相同）+ logy（说）→ 说相同的话 → 同义反复
