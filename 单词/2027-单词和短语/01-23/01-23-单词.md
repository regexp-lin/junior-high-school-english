## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **inspire** /ɪnˈspaɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：激励；启发
- **英文释义**：to fill someone with the urge or ability to do something creative or positive
- **例句**：
  - The teacher inspired her students to work harder.
  - 老师激励学生更加努力。
  - The beautiful scenery inspired the artist to paint.
  - 美景激发了艺术家的创作灵感。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ spire（呼吸）→ 把灵感吹进心里 → 激励

### 2. **install** /ɪnˈstɔːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：安装；设置
- **英文释义**：to place equipment in position and make it ready for use
- **例句**：
  - They installed a new air conditioner in the classroom.
  - 他们在教室安装了新空调。
  - Please install the latest version of the software.
  - 请安装最新版本的软件。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ stall（位置）→ 放入位置 → 安装

### 3. **instance** /ˈɪnstəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：例子；实例
- **英文释义**：an example or single occurrence of something
- **例句**：
  - In this instance, I think you are right.
  - 在这个例子中，我认为你是对的。
  - For instance, you could try a different approach.
  - 例如，你可以试试不同的方法。
- **助记**：in-（在内）+ stance（站）→ 站在里面的一个例子 → 实例

### 4. **instinct** /ˈɪnstɪŋkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：本能；直觉
- **英文释义**：an innate pattern of behaviour; a natural tendency
- **例句**：
  - Birds fly south in winter by instinct.
  - 鸟类凭本能在冬天南飞。
  - My instinct told me something was wrong.
  - 我的直觉告诉我有什么不对劲。
- **助记**：in-（内部）+ stinct（刺/推动）→ 内心的推动力 → 本能

### 5. **intellectual** /ˌɪntəˈlektʃuəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：智力的；知识分子
- **英文释义**：relating to the ability to think and understand; a person with great mental ability
- **例句**：
  - Reading is an intellectual activity.
  - 阅读是一种智力活动。
  - She is one of the leading intellectuals in the country.
  - 她是这个国家的杰出知识分子之一。
- **助记**：intellect（智力）+ -ual → 与智力有关的 → 智力的

### 6. **intense** /ɪnˈtens/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：强烈的；紧张的
- **英文释义**：of extreme force, degree, or strength
- **例句**：
  - The heat in the desert was intense.
  - 沙漠里的酷热非常强烈。
  - She felt intense pressure before the exam.
  - 考试前她感到巨大的压力。
- **助记**：in-（加强）+ tense（拉紧）→ 拉得很紧 → 强烈的

### 7. **interfere** /ˌɪntəˈfɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：干涉；妨碍
- **英文释义**：to involve yourself in a situation where you are not wanted; to prevent from working properly
- **例句**：
  - Don't interfere in other people's private affairs.
  - 不要干涉别人的私事。
  - The bad weather interfered with our travel plans.
  - 恶劣的天气妨碍了我们的出行计划。
- **助记**：inter-（之间）+ fere（打击）→ 在中间打击 → 干涉

### 8. **interpret** /ɪnˈtɜːprɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：解释；口译
- **英文释义**：to explain the meaning of something; to translate orally
- **例句**：
  - How do you interpret this poem?
  - 你怎么解读这首诗？
  - She interpreted for the Chinese delegation.
  - 她为中国代表团做口译。
- **助记**：inter-（之间）+ pret（价值/理解）→ 在不同语言之间传达理解 → 口译

### 9. **interval** /ˈɪntəvl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：间隔；幕间休息
- **英文释义**：a period of time between two events; a pause during a performance
- **例句**：
  - There was a short interval between the two classes.
  - 两节课之间有一个短暂的间隔。
  - During the interval, the audience went to get refreshments.
  - 幕间休息时，观众们去买了饮料。
- **助记**：inter-（之间）+ val（墙/空间）→ 两者之间的空间 → 间隔

### 10. **investigate** /ɪnˈvestɪɡeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：调查；研究
- **英文释义**：to carry out a systematic examination or inquiry
- **例句**：
  - The police are investigating the robbery.
  - 警方正在调查抢劫案。
  - Scientists investigated the effects of the new drug.
  - 科学家们研究了这种新药的效果。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ vestig（追踪）+ -ate → 追踪进入真相 → 调查

### 高一词汇

### 11. **gratify** /ˈɡrætɪfaɪ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使满足；使高兴
- **英文释义**：to give pleasure or satisfaction to someone
- **例句**：
  - It gratified her to see her students succeed.
  - 看到学生成功让她感到很欣慰。
  - His curiosity was gratified when he finally found the answer.
  - 当他终于找到答案时，好奇心得到了满足。
- **助记**：grat-（感谢/高兴）+ -ify → 使人高兴 → 使满足

### 12. **heresy** /ˈherəsi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：异端；异教邪说
- **英文释义**：belief or opinion contrary to the accepted doctrine; unorthodox thinking
- **例句**：
  - In the Middle Ages, questioning the Church was considered heresy.
  - 在中世纪，质疑教会被认为是异端。
  - Some people called his ideas economic heresy.
  - 一些人把他的想法称为经济学上的异端。
- **助记**：heres-（选择/异于）+ -y → 选择不同的信仰 → 异端

### 13. **impetuous** /ɪmˈpetʃuəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：冲动的；鲁莽的
- **英文释义**：acting or done quickly without thought or care
- **例句**：
  - His impetuous decision to quit school worried his parents.
  - 他冲动辍学的决定让父母很担心。
  - Don't be so impetuous — think before you act.
  - 不要这么冲动——三思而后行。
- **助记**：im-（进入）+ pet（追求）+ -uous → 不顾一切地追 → 冲动的

### 14. **laconic** /ləˈkɒnɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：简洁的；言简意赅的
- **英文释义**：using very few words; concise to the point of seeming rude
- **例句**：
  - He is known for his laconic style of writing.
  - 他以简洁的写作风格著称。
  - "Fine," was her laconic reply.
  - "好吧"是她简短的回答。
- **助记**：源自 Laconia（斯巴达所在地）→ 斯巴达人以言简意赅著称 → 简洁的

### 15. **maverick** /ˈmævərɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：特立独行的人；不随大流的
- **英文释义**：an independent person who does not follow the usual rules
- **例句**：
  - He's a maverick who always challenges the status quo.
  - 他是一个总是挑战现状的特立独行者。
  - Her maverick approach to teaching made her popular with students.
  - 她不拘一格的教学方法使她很受学生欢迎。
- **助记**：maverick → 源自19世纪一位不给牛打烙印的牧场主 Samuel Maverick → 特立独行
