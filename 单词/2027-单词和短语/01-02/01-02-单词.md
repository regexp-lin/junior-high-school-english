## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **advocate** /ˈædvəkeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：提倡；拥护者
- **英文释义**：to publicly support a particular idea or way of doing things; a person who supports something
- **例句**：
  - She advocates equal rights for all people.
  - 她提倡人人平等。
  - He is a strong advocate of environmental protection.
  - 他是环保的坚定拥护者。
- **助记**：ad-（朝向）+ voc（声音）+ -ate → 朝着某个方向发声 → 提倡

### 2. **affair** /əˈfeə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：事务；事件
- **英文释义**：an event or a series of events; matters of public interest
- **例句**：
  - The government handled the affair very badly.
  - 政府对这件事处理得很糟糕。
  - She doesn't want to get involved in other people's affairs.
  - 她不想参与别人的事务。
- **助记**：af-（朝向）+ fair（做）→ 要去做的事 → 事务

### 3. **afford** /əˈfɔːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：负担得起；提供
- **英文释义**：to have enough money or time to buy or do something
- **例句**：
  - We can't afford a new car right now.
  - 我们现在买不起新车。
  - The window affords a beautiful view of the lake.
  - 这扇窗户提供了湖景美景。
- **助记**：af-（朝向）+ ford（渡口）→ 能到达渡口 → 有能力负担

### 4. **aggressive** /əˈɡresɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：好斗的；有进取心的
- **英文释义**：behaving in an angry and violent way; determined to succeed
- **例句**：
  - The dog becomes aggressive when strangers approach.
  - 当陌生人靠近时，这只狗变得很凶。
  - She has an aggressive approach to business.
  - 她在商业上很有进取心。
- **助记**：ag-（朝向）+ gress（走）+ -ive → 朝别人走去 → 好斗的

### 5. **alert** /əˈlɜːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./v.
- **中文释义**：警觉的；使警觉
- **英文释义**：quick to notice things; to warn someone of a danger
- **例句**：
  - Stay alert when you cross the road.
  - 过马路时要保持警觉。
  - The teacher alerted the students to the fire drill.
  - 老师提醒学生们注意消防演习。
- **助记**：a（一）+ lert（灵活的）→ 保持灵活 → 警觉的

### 6. **alliance** /əˈlaɪəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：联盟；同盟
- **英文释义**：a union or association formed for mutual benefit
- **例句**：
  - The two countries formed a military alliance.
  - 两国结成了军事联盟。
  - The alliance between the companies led to great success.
  - 公司间的联盟带来了巨大成功。
- **助记**：al-（朝向）+ li（绑）+ -ance → 绑在一起 → 联盟

### 7. **allocate** /ˈæləkeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：分配；拨出
- **英文释义**：to give something officially for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - The school allocated more funds for science equipment.
  - 学校拨出更多资金购买科学设备。
  - Each student was allocated a desk and a chair.
  - 每个学生被分配了一张桌子和一把椅子。
- **助记**：al-（朝向）+ loc（地方）+ -ate → 放到各个地方去 → 分配

### 8. **alter** /ˈɔːltə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：改变；修改
- **英文释义**：to make something different; to change in character or composition
- **例句**：
  - She had to alter the dress to make it fit.
  - 她不得不修改裙子以使其合身。
  - Nothing can alter the fact that we are late.
  - 什么也改变不了我们迟到的事实。
- **助记**：alter = other（其他的）→ 变成其他样子 → 改变

### 9. **amateur** /ˈæmətə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：业余爱好者（的）
- **英文释义**：a person who does something as a hobby rather than as a job
- **例句**：
  - He is an amateur photographer.
  - 他是一名业余摄影师。
  - The competition is open to both amateurs and professionals.
  - 比赛对业余选手和专业人士都开放。
- **助记**：amat-（爱）+ -eur → 出于热爱而做 → 业余爱好者

### 10. **ambiguous** /æmˈbɪɡjuəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：模棱两可的；含糊的
- **英文释义**：having more than one possible meaning; not clear
- **例句**：
  - His answer was deliberately ambiguous.
  - 他的回答故意含糊其辞。
  - The law is ambiguous and needs to be clarified.
  - 这条法律含糊不清，需要澄清。
- **助记**：ambi-（两边）+ gu（走）+ -ous → 两边都能走 → 模棱两可的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **admonish** /ədˈmɒnɪʃ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：告诫；训斥
- **英文释义**：to warn someone firmly; to tell someone off for doing something wrong
- **例句**：
  - The teacher admonished the students for being noisy.
  - 老师训斥了吵闹的学生。
  - She admonished him to be more careful in the future.
  - 她告诫他以后要更加小心。
- **助记**：ad-（加强）+ mon（提醒）+ -ish → 强烈提醒 → 告诫

### 12. **adversity** /ədˈvɜːsəti/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：逆境；困境
- **英文释义**：a difficult or unpleasant situation
- **例句**：
  - She showed great courage in the face of adversity.
  - 她在逆境中表现出极大的勇气。
  - Adversity often brings out the best in people.
  - 逆境往往能激发人的潜能。
- **助记**：ad-（朝向）+ vers（转）+ -ity → 转向不好的方向 → 逆境

### 13. **affluent** /ˈæfluənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：富裕的；富足的
- **英文释义**：having a lot of money and a good standard of living
- **例句**：
  - They live in an affluent neighbourhood.
  - 他们住在一个富裕的社区。
  - The city attracts many affluent tourists.
  - 这座城市吸引了许多富裕的游客。
- **助记**：af-（朝向）+ flu（流）+ -ent → 财富流向你 → 富裕的

### 14. **alleviate** /əˈliːvieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：减轻；缓解
- **英文释义**：to make something less severe or serious
- **例句**：
  - The medicine helped to alleviate the pain.
  - 药物帮助减轻了疼痛。
  - The government took measures to alleviate poverty.
  - 政府采取措施缓解贫困。
- **助记**：al-（加强）+ levi（轻）+ -ate → 使变轻 → 减轻

### 15. **amalgamate** /əˈmælɡəmeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：合并；混合
- **英文释义**：to join together to form one large group or organization
- **例句**：
  - The two schools amalgamated into one.
  - 两所学校合并为一所。
  - They decided to amalgamate the two departments.
  - 他们决定合并两个部门。
- **助记**：amalgam（汞合金/混合物）+ -ate → 像合金一样混合在一起 → 合并
