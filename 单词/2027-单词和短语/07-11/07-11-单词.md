## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **allege** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：声称；指控；断言
- **英文释义**：to claim or assert that someone has done something illegal or wrong, typically without proof
- **例句**：
  - The newspaper alleged that the mayor had accepted bribes from local businesses.
  - 报纸声称市长收受了当地企业的贿赂。
- **助记**：al（向）+ lege（法律）→ 向法律方面声称 → 指控/声称

### 2. **brisk** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：轻快的；活跃的；凛冽的
- **英文释义**：active, fast, and energetic; (of weather) cold but pleasantly invigorating
- **例句**：
  - She took a brisk walk around the park every morning before breakfast.
  - 她每天早餐前都会在公园里轻快地散步。
- **助记**：brisk → 联想"布里斯克"→ 风吹得快 → 轻快的/凛冽的

### 3. **contempt** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：蔑视；轻蔑；藐视
- **英文释义**：the feeling that a person or a thing is beneath consideration or worthless; disregard
- **例句**：
  - She looked at the broken promise with utter contempt.
  - 她对这个被打破的承诺充满了轻蔑。
- **助记**：con（加强）+ tempt（轻视）→ 极度轻视 → 蔑视

### 4. **dwindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减少；缩小；衰退
- **英文释义**：to diminish gradually in size, amount, or strength
- **例句**：
  - The village's population has dwindled from over a thousand to just a few hundred.
  - 这个村庄的人口已经从一千多人减少到只有几百人。
- **助记**：dwindle → 联想"d + wind + le"→ 像风一样消散 → 逐渐减少

### 5. **fathom** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 理解；领悟 n. 英寻（水深单位）
- **英文释义**：v. to understand after much thought; n. a unit of length equal to six feet, used in measuring the depth of water
- **例句**：
  - I cannot fathom why she would give up such a promising career.
  - 我无法理解她为什么会放弃如此有前途的事业。
- **助记**：fathom → 原指"伸开双臂的长度"→ 伸手探测水深 → 深入理解

### 6. **hinder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to create difficulties for someone or something, resulting in delay or obstruction
- **例句**：
  - Heavy traffic hindered our progress to the airport and we nearly missed the flight.
  - 严重的交通拥堵阻碍了我们去机场，我们差点错过航班。
- **助记**：hind（后面的）+ er → 拉到后面 → 阻碍

### 7. **lucrative** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有利可图的；赚大钱的；利润丰厚的
- **英文释义**：producing a great deal of profit
- **例句**：
  - The tech industry offers many lucrative career opportunities for skilled engineers.
  - 科技行业为技术熟练的工程师提供了许多利润丰厚的职业机会。
- **助记**：lucr（利润）+ ative → 有利润的 → 有利可图的

### 8. **pertinent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：相关的；恰当的；切题的
- **英文释义**：relevant or applicable to a particular matter; apposite
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer raised several pertinent questions during the cross-examination.
  - 律师在交叉询问中提出了几个切题的问题。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ tin（持有）+ ent → 贯穿始终的 → 相关的/切题的

### 9. **plausible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：似乎合理的；看似可信的；貌似有道理的
- **英文释义**：seeming reasonable or probable; appearing worthy of belief
- **例句**：
  - His excuse sounded plausible, but the teacher did not believe him.
  - 他的借口听起来似乎合理，但老师并不相信他。
- **助记**：plaus（鼓掌/赞同）+ ible → 值得鼓掌的 → 似乎合理的

### 10. **tenacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽强的；坚韧不拔的；执着的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold of something; not readily relinquishing a position or principle
- **例句**：
  - The tenacious young athlete refused to give up despite repeated injuries.
  - 这位坚韧不拔的年轻运动员尽管反复受伤也不愿放弃。
- **助记**：ten（抓住）+ acious → 紧紧抓住的 → 顽强的/执着的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **antipathy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：反感；厌恶；憎恶
- **英文释义**：a deep-seated feeling of dislike; aversion
- **例句**：
  - There was a long-standing antipathy between the two rival families.
  - 两个敌对家族之间存在着长期的反感。
- **助记**：anti（反对）+ pathy（感情）→ 反对的感情 → 反感/厌恶

### 12. **ebullient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的；充满活力的
- **英文释义**：cheerful and full of energy; enthusiastic
- **例句**：
  - The ebullient crowd cheered wildly as the team scored the winning goal.
  - 当球队打入制胜球时，热情洋溢的人群疯狂欢呼。
- **助记**：e（出）+ bull（沸腾）+ ient → 沸腾而出 → 热情洋溢的

### 13. **magnanimous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；慷慨的；高尚的
- **英文释义**：very generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or less powerful person
- **例句**：
  - The magnanimous victor offered aid to his defeated opponents.
  - 这位宽宏大量的胜利者向他的失败对手提供了帮助。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（精神/心灵）+ ous → 心灵宏大的 → 宽宏大量的

### 14. **pernicious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有害的；恶性的；致命的
- **英文释义**：having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way
- **例句**：
  - The pernicious influence of social media on teenagers' mental health is a growing concern.
  - 社交媒体对青少年心理健康的有害影响日益令人担忧。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ nic（杀）+ ious → 完全致死的 → 有害的/致命的

### 15. **surreptitious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：秘密的；暗中进行的；鬼鬼祟祟的
- **英文释义**：kept secret, especially because it would not be approved of
- **例句**：
  - She cast a surreptitious glance at the answer sheet when the teacher was not looking.
  - 她趁老师没看的时候偷偷瞥了一眼答案纸。
- **助记**：sur（下面）+ rept（爬）+ itious → 在下面偷偷爬 → 秘密的/鬼鬼祟祟的
