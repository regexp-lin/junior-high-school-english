## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **compensate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：补偿；赔偿；弥补
- **英文释义**：to give something good in return for harm or loss; to make up for
- **例句**：
  - The company agreed to compensate workers for overtime hours.
  - 公司同意补偿员工的加班时间。
  - Nothing can fully compensate for the loss of a family member.
  - 没有什么能完全弥补失去亲人。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ pens（权衡）+ ate → 重新权衡补偿 → 赔偿

### 2. **deliberate** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：故意的；深思熟虑的；仔细考虑
- **英文释义**：done consciously and intentionally; to think over carefully
- **例句**：
  - The damage was not an accident; it was a deliberate act of vandalism.
  - 损坏并非意外，而是蓄意破坏。
  - The jury deliberated for hours before reaching a verdict.
  - 陪审团商议数小时后才作出裁决。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ liber（权衡）+ ate → 充分权衡 → 蓄意/商议

### 3. **endeavor** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：努力；尽力；尝试
- **英文释义**：to try hard to do something; an attempt to achieve something
- **例句**：
  - We endeavor to finish the project before the deadline.
  - 我们力争在截止日期前完成项目。
  - Learning a musical instrument is a lifelong endeavor.
  - 学习乐器是毕生的努力方向。
- **助记**：en（使）+ deavor（责任）→ 使承担责任 → 努力

### 4. **feasible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可行的；行得通的；合理的
- **英文释义**：possible to do easily or conveniently; likely to succeed
- **例句**：
  - Is it feasible to hold the concert outdoors in winter?
  - 冬天在户外举办音乐可行吗？
  - The engineer proposed a feasible plan to reduce energy use.
  - 工程师提出了可行的节能方案。
- **助记**：feas（做）+ ible → 能做出来的 → 可行

### 5. **inherent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：固有的；内在的；天生的
- **英文释义**：existing in something as a permanent, essential attribute
- **例句**：
  - Risk is inherent in any investment decision.
  - 任何投资决定都固有风险。
  - She has an inherent talent for languages.
  - 她有与生俱来的语言天赋。
- **助记**：in（在内）+ her（粘附）+ ent → 粘在内部的 → 固有

### 6. **mediate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：调解；斡旋；经调解解决
- **英文释义**：to intervene between parties to resolve a dispute
- **例句**：
  - A neutral lawyer was asked to mediate the labor dispute.
  - 一位中立律师被请来调解劳资纠纷。
  - Friends helped mediate between the two arguing classmates.
  - 朋友们帮助调解两名争吵的同学。
- **助记**：medi（中间）+ ate → 站在中间 → 调解

### 7. **notable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：显著的；值得注意的；著名的
- **英文释义**：worthy of attention or notice; remarkable; famous
- **例句**：
  - The city has made notable progress in recycling waste.
  - 该市在废物回收方面取得了显著进展。
  - Several notable scientists attended the conference.
  - 几位著名科学家出席了会议。
- **助记**：not（注意）+ able → 值得注意的

### 8. **obsolete** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过时的；淘汰的；不再使用的
- **英文释义**：no longer produced or used; out of date
- **例句**：
  - Floppy disks are now almost obsolete in personal computing.
  - 软盘在个人电脑领域几乎已淘汰。
  - The old factory equipment became obsolete within five years.
  - 旧工厂设备五年内就过时了。
- **助记**：ob（对抗）+ solete（使用）→ 不再被使用 → 过时

### 9. **proficient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：熟练的；精通的
- **英文释义**：competent or skilled in doing or using something
- **例句**：
  - She is proficient in three programming languages.
  - 她精通三种编程语言。
  - After years of practice, he became proficient at the violin.
  - 多年练习后，他小提琴拉得很熟练。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ fic（做）+ ient → 能向前做下去的 → 熟练

### 10. **supplement** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：补充；增补；补品；增刊
- **英文释义**：to add an extra element; something that completes or enhances
- **例句**：
  - Many athletes supplement their diet with vitamins.
  - 许多运动员用维生素补充饮食。
  - The textbook has an online supplement with extra exercises.
  - 教材配有含额外练习的网络增补材料。
- **助记**：sup（在下）+ ple（填满）+ ment → 在下面再填满 → 补充

### 高一词汇

### 11. **capitulate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：投降；屈服；不再抵抗
- **英文释义**：to cease to resist an opponent or unwelcome demand; surrender
- **例句**：
  - After weeks of siege, the defenders were forced to capitulate.
  - 围困数周后，守军被迫投降。
  - He refused to capitulate to pressure from his peers.
  - 他拒绝向同伴的压力屈服。
- **助记**：capit（头）+ ulate → 低头 → 投降

### 12. **equivocal** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：模棱两可的；含糊其辞的；可疑的
- **英文释义**：open to more than one interpretation; ambiguous; suspicious
- **例句**：
  - The witness gave an equivocal answer that helped neither side.
  - 证人给出了模棱两可的回答，对双方都没帮助。
  - His equivocal attitude made it hard to trust him fully.
  - 他暧昧的态度让人难以完全信任。
- **助记**：equi（相等）+ voc（声音）→ 两种声音相等 → 含糊

### 13. **gregarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱社交的；群居的
- **英文释义**：fond of company; sociable; living in flocks or herds
- **例句**：
  - She is gregarious and makes friends wherever she goes.
  - 她爱交际，走到哪里都能交到朋友。
  - Sheep are gregarious animals that stay together in groups.
  - 羊是群居动物，成群生活。
- **助记**：greg（群）+ arious → 合群的

### 14. **mitigate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和；使不那么严重
- **英文释义**：to make something less severe, serious, or painful
- **例句**：
  - Trees help mitigate the effects of urban heat islands.
  - 树木有助于减轻城市热岛效应。
  - The apology did little to mitigate her anger.
  - 道歉几乎没能缓和她的愤怒。
- **助记**：miti（温和）+ gate → 使温和 → 缓和

### 15. **vernacular** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：本地话；方言；用本国语的
- **英文释义**：the language spoken by ordinary people in a region; native
- **例句**：
  - The guide explained local customs in the vernacular.
  - 导游用方言讲解当地风俗。
  - Vernacular architecture uses materials found nearby.
  - 本土建筑使用当地材料。
- **助记**：vern（本土）+ acular → 本土语言
