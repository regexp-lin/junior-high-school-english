## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **anthology** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：选集；文选；作品集
- **英文释义**：a published collection of poems, stories, or other pieces of writing
- **例句**：
  - The teacher recommended an anthology of short stories for summer reading.
  - 老师推荐了一本短篇小说选集作为暑期阅读。
- **助记**：antho（花）+ logy（采集）→ 采集的花朵 → 精选集

### 2. **blemish** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 瑕疵；缺陷 v. 损害；玷污
- **英文释义**：n. a small mark or flaw that spoils the appearance; v. to spoil or impair
- **例句**：
  - A single scandal can blemish a politician's entire career.
  - 一桩丑闻就能玷污一位政治家的整个职业生涯。
- **助记**：blem（责备）+ ish → 被责备的缺陷 → 瑕疵

### 3. **conspire** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：密谋；共谋；合力促成
- **英文释义**：to make secret plans jointly to commit an unlawful act; to work together
- **例句**：
  - They conspired to overthrow the government.
  - 他们密谋推翻政府。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ spire（呼吸）→ 一起低声商量（同呼吸）→ 密谋

### 4. **disperse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：分散；驱散；散布
- **英文释义**：to distribute or spread over a wide area; to cause to go in different directions
- **例句**：
  - The police used water cannons to disperse the crowd.
  - 警方使用水炮驱散了人群。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ perse（散布）→ 分开散布 → 驱散

### 5. **filament** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：灯丝；细丝；细线
- **英文释义**：a slender threadlike object or fibre; the thin wire in a light bulb
- **例句**：
  - The filament in the old light bulb burned out and needed replacing.
  - 旧灯泡里的灯丝烧断了，需要更换。
- **助记**：fil（线）+ ament → 细细的线 → 灯丝/细丝

### 6. **hamper** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 阻碍；妨碍 n. 大篮子
- **英文释义**：v. to hinder or impede the movement or progress of; n. a basket with a carrying handle
- **例句**：
  - The heavy rain hampered rescue efforts in the disaster zone.
  - 大雨阻碍了灾区的救援工作。
- **助记**：hamper → 搬着大篮子行动不便 → 阻碍

### 7. **kindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：点燃；激发；唤起
- **英文释义**：to light or set on fire; to arouse or inspire an emotion
- **例句**：
  - The teacher's praise kindled the student's passion for learning.
  - 老师的表扬激发了学生的学习热情。
- **助记**：kindle → 点火/点燃 → 激发热情

### 8. **mandate** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 授权；命令；任期 v. 强制执行；授权
- **英文释义**：n. an official order or commission; v. to require or authorize by mandate
- **例句**：
  - The new law mandates that all buildings must have fire exits.
  - 新法律规定所有建筑物必须有消防出口。
- **助记**：man（手）+ date（给予）→ 用手给出命令 → 授权/命令

### 9. **optic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：光学的；眼睛的；视觉的
- **英文释义**：relating to sight or the eye; relating to optics
- **例句**：
  - Optic fibres are used to transmit data at incredible speeds.
  - 光纤被用来以惊人的速度传输数据。
- **助记**：opt（看）+ ic → 与看有关的 → 光学的/视觉的

### 10. **quarrel** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 争吵；口角 v. 争吵；吵架
- **英文释义**：n. an angry argument or disagreement; v. to have an angry argument
- **例句**：
  - The neighbours had a quarrel over the noise from the party.
  - 邻居们因为聚会的噪音发生了争吵。
- **助记**：quarrel → 像切开（quarter）关系一样的争吵 → 争吵

### 高一词汇

### 11. **diatribe** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：长篇抨击；激烈的批评
- **英文释义**：a forceful and bitter verbal attack against someone or something
- **例句**：
  - The editorial was a diatribe against government corruption.
  - 这篇社论是对政府腐败的长篇抨击。
- **助记**：dia（穿过）+ tribe（部落）→ 穿过部落的攻击性言辞 → 长篇抨击

### 12. **insipid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：平淡无味的；乏味的；无趣的
- **英文释义**：lacking flavour; dull and uninteresting
- **例句**：
  - The movie was so insipid that half the audience fell asleep.
  - 这部电影太乏味了，半数观众都睡着了。
- **助记**：in（不）+ sipid（有味的）→ 没有味道的 → 平淡无味的

### 13. **obsequious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：谄媚的；奉承的；恭顺的
- **英文释义**：obedient or attentive to an excessive degree
- **例句**：
  - The obsequious waiter agreed with everything the customer said.
  - 那个谄媚的服务员对顾客说的每句话都表示赞同。
- **助记**：ob（向）+ sequi（跟随）+ ous → 一直跟在后面 → 谄媚的

### 14. **placate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：抚慰；平息；安抚
- **英文释义**：to make someone less angry or hostile
- **例句**：
  - The company offered refunds to placate dissatisfied customers.
  - 公司提供退款来安抚不满意的客户。
- **助记**：plac（平静）+ ate → 使平静 → 抚慰/安抚

### 15. **transient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：短暂的；转瞬即逝的；暂住的
- **英文释义**：lasting only for a short time; impermanent
- **例句**：
  - Fame is transient — what matters is the lasting impact of your work.
  - 名声是短暂的——重要的是你工作的持久影响。
- **助记**：trans（穿越）+ ient → 快速穿越的 → 短暂的
