## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **sullen** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：闷闷不乐的；阴沉的；愠怒的
- **英文释义**：bad-tempered and sulky; gloomy and dull in appearance
- **例句**：
  - He remained sullen throughout the meeting and refused to join the discussion.
  - 整个会议他都闷闷不乐，拒绝参与讨论。
  - The sky turned sullen as the storm approached.
  - 暴风雨将至，天色变得阴沉。
- **助记**：sull（阴沉）联想 sulk（生闷气）→ 闷闷不乐的

### 2. **placid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：平静的；温和的；宁静的
- **英文释义**：not easily upset or excited; calm and peaceful
- **例句**：
  - The lake was placid in the early morning, like a mirror.
  - 清晨湖面平静如镜。
  - Despite the crisis, she kept a placid expression on her face.
  - 尽管危机当头，她仍保持着平静的表情。
- **助记**：plac（平静，如 placate）+ id → 性情平和的 → 平静的

### 3. **innate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：天生的；固有的；内在的
- **英文释义**：inborn; existing from birth as part of one's nature
- **例句**：
  - She has an innate sense of rhythm that makes her a natural dancer.
  - 她有天生的节奏感，是天生的舞者。
  - The ability to learn language is partly innate and partly learned.
  - 学习语言的能力部分天生、部分靠后天。
- **助记**：in（在内）+ nate（出生）→ 与生俱来的 → 天生的

### 4. **docile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：温顺的；易驯服的；听话的
- **英文释义**：ready to accept control or instruction; submissive and easy to manage
- **例句**：
  - The puppy is docile and learns new commands quickly.
  - 这只小狗温顺，很快学会新指令。
  - The teacher wished for a more docile class, but the students were full of questions.
  - 老师希望班级更听话，但学生们问题很多。
- **助记**：doc（教，如 doctor）+ ile → 容易教导的 → 温顺的

### 5. **frugal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：节俭的；朴素的；省钱的
- **英文释义**：sparing or economical with regard to money or food; simple and plain
- **例句**：
  - They lead a frugal life but save enough to travel every year.
  - 他们生活节俭，但每年都能存够钱去旅行。
  - A frugal meal of bread and soup was all they could afford.
  - 他们只负担得起面包和汤的粗茶淡饭。
- **助记**：frug（节俭，拉丁语）→ 节俭的

### 6. **viable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可行的；能独立生存的；有望成功的
- **英文释义**：capable of working successfully; able to survive or develop
- **例句**：
  - The committee concluded that the plan was economically viable.
  - 委员会认为该计划在经济学上可行。
  - Only viable seeds will germinate in the spring soil.
  - 只有有活力的种子才会在春土里发芽。
- **助记**：vi（生命，如 vital）+ able → 能活下去的 → 可行的

### 7. **lucid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：清晰的；易懂的；神志清醒的
- **英文释义**：clearly expressed or easy to understand; rational and clear-headed
- **例句**：
  - She gave a lucid explanation of the complex theory.
  - 她对这一复杂理论作了清晰的解释。
  - In his lucid moments, he could remember details from decades ago.
  - 神志清醒时，他能记起几十年前的细节。
- **助记**：luc（光，如 lucent）+ id → 照亮的、清楚的 → 清晰的

### 8. **somber** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：阴沉的；忧郁的；暗淡的
- **英文释义**：dark or dull in tone or color; solemn and serious
- **例句**：
  - The room was painted in somber shades of gray and brown.
  - 房间漆成灰暗阴沉的色调。
  - A somber mood fell over the crowd when they heard the news.
  - 听到消息时，人群陷入忧郁气氛。
- **助记**：somber 联想 somber sky（阴沉的天空）→ 忧郁的

### 9. **adamant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坚决的；固执的；不容置疑的
- **英文释义**：refusing to be persuaded or to change one's mind; unshakeable
- **例句**：
  - She was adamant that she would not sell the family house.
  - 她坚决不卖祖宅。
  - He remained adamant in his refusal to apologize.
  - 他仍固执地拒绝道歉。
- **助记**：adamant 原义“最坚硬的物质”→ 像金刚石一样不动摇 → 坚决的

### 10. **arduous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：艰巨的；费力的；艰苦的
- **英文释义**：involving or requiring strenuous effort; difficult and tiring
- **例句**：
  - The climbers faced an arduous journey through the snow.
  - 登山者在雪中经历了艰苦行程。
  - Learning a new language as an adult is an arduous task.
  - 成年后学一门新语言是艰巨的任务。
- **助记**：ardu（高、陡）+ ous → 像爬坡一样费力 → 艰巨的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **nebulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：模糊不清的；云状的；朦胧的
- **英文释义**：unclear, vague, or ill-defined; cloudy or hazy
- **例句**：
  - The project's goals remained nebulous until the manager clarified them.
  - 在经理阐明之前，项目目标一直模糊不清。
  - A nebulous shape appeared in the fog ahead of the ship.
  - 船前方的雾中浮现出一个朦胧的影子。
- **助记**：nebula（星云）+ ous → 像星云一样朦胧 → 模糊的

### 12. **ostensible** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：表面上的；貌似真实的；宣称的
- **英文释义**：stated or appearing to be true, but not necessarily so; apparent
- **例句**：
  - The ostensible reason for the visit was business, but he had other motives.
  - 访问的表面理由是公务，但他另有目的。
  - Her ostensible calm hid deep anxiety.
  - 她表面上的平静下藏着深深的焦虑。
- **助记**：ostens（展示）+ ible → 展示给人看的 → 表面的

### 13. **pragmatic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；实用主义的；重实效的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically; practical rather than idealistic
- **例句**：
  - We need a pragmatic solution that works within our budget.
  - 我们需要在预算内可行的务实方案。
  - She has a pragmatic attitude toward career changes.
  - 她对职业变动持务实态度。
- **助记**：pragma（行为、事务）+ tic → 重实际事务的 → 务实的

### 14. **prolific** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：多产的；丰富的；大量结果的
- **英文释义**：producing much fruit, foliage, or offspring; very productive
- **例句**：
  - He was a prolific writer who published over fifty novels.
  - 他是一位多产的作家，出版了五十多部小说。
  - The region is prolific in natural resources.
  - 该地区自然资源丰富。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lific（做）→ 不断做出成果 → 多产的

### 15. **salient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：显著的；突出的；最重要的
- **英文释义**：most noticeable or important; prominent; projecting
- **例句**：
  - The salient points of the report were summarized on the first page.
  - 报告的要点总结在首页。
  - The castle stood on a salient cliff above the river.
  - 城堡矗立在河上方突出的悬崖上。
- **助记**：sali（跳，如 salient 跳出）→ 跳出来的 → 显著的
