## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **probity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：正直；诚实；廉洁
- **英文释义**：the quality of having strong moral principles; honesty and decency
- **例句**：
  - The judge was known for his probity and was trusted by the entire community.
  - 这位法官以正直著称，受到整个社区的信任。
  - Financial probity is essential for anyone handling public funds.
  - 对于任何管理公共资金的人来说，财务廉洁至关重要。
- **助记**：prob（证明/正直）+ ity → 经得起证明的品质 → 正直

### 2. **inveterate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：根深蒂固的；积习难改的；老是如此的
- **英文释义**：having a particular habit, activity, or interest that is long-established and unlikely to change
- **例句**：
  - He was an inveterate liar who couldn't be trusted with even the simplest truth.
  - 他是个积习难改的骗子，连最简单的真话都不能信。
  - The inveterate traveler had visited over sixty countries by the age of forty.
  - 这位老旅行家到四十岁时已经访问了六十多个国家。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ veter（老的）+ ate → 进入老习惯 → 根深蒂固的

### 3. **calumniate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：诽谤；中伤；诬蔑
- **英文释义**：to make false and defamatory statements about someone
- **例句**：
  - The rival politician calumniated her opponent with baseless accusations of corruption.
  - 竞争对手的政治家用毫无根据的腐败指控诽谤她的对手。
  - Those who calumniate others often damage their own reputation in the process.
  - 那些诽谤他人的人往往在过程中也损害了自己的名声。
- **助记**：calumni（诽谤）+ ate → 进行诽谤 → 中伤

### 4. **tenacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坚韧的；顽强的；不屈不挠的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent
- **例句**：
  - Her tenacious spirit helped her overcome every obstacle on the path to success.
  - 她坚韧的精神帮助她克服了通往成功道路上的每一个障碍。
  - The tenacious reporter pursued the story for months until the truth was revealed.
  - 这位顽强的记者追踪这个故事数月，直到真相被揭露。
- **助记**：ten（抓住）+ acious → 牢牢抓住的 → 坚韧的

### 5. **promulgate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：颁布；宣布；传播
- **英文释义**：to make widely known; to put a law into effect by official proclamation
- **例句**：
  - The government promulgated new regulations to protect consumer privacy.
  - 政府颁布了新法规来保护消费者隐私。
  - The organization promulgated its findings through a series of public lectures.
  - 该组织通过一系列公开讲座传播了其研究成果。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ mulg（公众）+ ate → 向公众传达 → 颁布/传播

### 6. **inimical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有害的；敌意的；不利的
- **英文释义**：tending to obstruct or harm; hostile
- **例句**：
  - Prolonged stress is inimical to both physical and mental health.
  - 长期压力对身体和心理健康都有害。
  - The hostile work environment was inimical to creativity and productivity.
  - 充满敌意的工作环境不利于创造力和生产力。
- **助记**：in（不）+ imic（友好）+ al → 不友好的 → 有害的/敌意的

### 7. **abrogate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：废除；取消；撤销
- **英文释义**：to repeal or do away with a law, right, or formal agreement
- **例句**：
  - The new administration abrogated several outdated policies from the previous era.
  - 新政府废除了前一时期的几项过时政策。
  - The treaty was abrogated after both parties failed to meet their obligations.
  - 在双方均未履行义务后，条约被撤销了。
- **助记**：ab（离开）+ rog（要求/法律）+ ate → 使法律离开 → 废除

### 8. **loquacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：话多的；健谈的；喋喋不休的
- **英文释义**：tending to talk a great deal; talkative
- **例句**：
  - The loquacious tour guide entertained visitors with endless stories and anecdotes.
  - 健谈的导游用无穷无尽的故事和轶事招待游客。
  - She became unusually loquacious after a glass of wine at the dinner party.
  - 在晚宴上喝了一杯酒后，她变得异常健谈。
- **助记**：loqu（说话）+ acious → 不断说话的 → 话多的

### 9. **subjugate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：征服；制服；使臣服
- **英文释义**：to bring under domination or control, especially by conquest
- **例句**：
  - The empire sought to subjugate neighboring kingdoms through military conquest.
  - 帝国试图通过军事征服来征服邻近王国。
  - Technology should empower people, not subjugate them to the will of corporations.
  - 技术应该赋予人们力量，而不是让他们臣服于公司的意志。
- **助记**：sub（在下）+ jug（轭）+ ate → 置于轭下 → 征服

### 10. **intrepid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无畏的；勇敢的；刚毅的
- **英文释义**：fearless; adventurous
- **例句**：
  - The intrepid mountaineer scaled the peak despite severe weather warnings.
  - 无畏的登山者不顾严重的天气警告攀登了山峰。
  - Intrepid journalists risked their lives to report from the conflict zone.
  - 无畏的记者冒着生命危险从冲突区域进行报道。
- **助记**：in（不）+ trepid（恐惧）→ 不恐惧的 → 无畏的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **sempiternal** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：永恒的；永久的；无始无终的
- **英文释义**：eternal and unchanging; everlasting
- **例句**：
  - The philosopher spoke of the sempiternal nature of truth — it exists beyond time.
  - 哲学家谈到了真理的永恒本质——它存在于时间之外。
  - The ancient monument stood as a sempiternal reminder of the civilization that built it.
  - 这座古老的纪念碑作为建造它的文明的永恒提醒而矗立。
- **助记**：sempi（永远）+ ternal（永久的）→ 永远永久的 → 永恒的

### 12. **logomachy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：文字之争；咬文嚼字的争辩
- **英文释义**：a dispute about or concerning words; an argument based on semantics
- **例句**：
  - The politicians' debate devolved into mere logomachy, arguing over definitions rather than policy.
  - 政治家们的辩论沦为纯粹的文字之争，争论的是定义而非政策。
  - Philosophical discussions sometimes risk becoming logomachy when terms are not clearly defined.
  - 当术语没有被清晰定义时，哲学讨论有时会沦为咬文嚼字的争辩。
- **助记**：logo（词/言语）+ machy（战斗）→ 言语的战斗 → 文字之争

### 13. **apotropaic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：辟邪的；驱除厄运的
- **英文释义**：supposedly having the power to avert evil influences or bad luck
- **例句**：
  - Many ancient cultures placed apotropaic symbols on doorways to ward off evil spirits.
  - 许多古代文化在门口放置辟邪符号来驱除恶灵。
  - The gargoyles on medieval cathedrals served an apotropaic function, believed to scare away demons.
  - 中世纪大教堂上的石像鬼起着辟邪的作用，被认为能吓走恶魔。
- **助记**：apo（远离）+ trop（转向）+ aic → 使（邪恶）转向远离 → 辟邪的

### 14. **thaumaturge** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：奇迹创造者；魔术师；行奇迹者
- **英文释义**：a performer of miracles; a wonder-worker
- **例句**：
  - In medieval legend, the saint was revered as a thaumaturge who could heal the sick with a touch.
  - 在中世纪传说中，这位圣人被尊为能触手治愈病人的奇迹创造者。
  - The tech CEO was hailed as a modern thaumaturge for transforming the struggling company.
  - 这位科技公司CEO因扭转了这家挣扎的公司而被誉为现代奇迹创造者。
- **助记**：thauma（奇迹）+ turge（工作者）→ 创造奇迹的人 → 奇迹创造者

### 15. **pareidolia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：空想性错视；在随机事物中看到图案的倾向
- **英文释义**：the tendency to perceive a specific, often meaningful image in a random visual pattern
- **例句**：
  - Pareidolia explains why we often see faces in the patterns of clouds or tree bark.
  - 空想性错视解释了为什么我们经常在云的形状或树皮纹理中看到人脸。
  - The famous "Face on Mars" photograph is a well-known example of pareidolia.
  - 著名的"火星上的脸"照片是空想性错视的一个广为人知的例子。
- **助记**：para（旁/偏离）+ eidol（图像）+ ia → 偏离正常的图像感知 → 空想性错视
