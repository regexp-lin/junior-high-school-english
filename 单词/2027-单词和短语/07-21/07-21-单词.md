## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abstain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：戒除；弃权；克制
- **英文释义**：to restrain oneself from doing or enjoying something; to formally decline to vote
- **例句**：
  - The doctor advised him to abstain from alcohol for at least six months.
  - 医生建议他至少戒酒六个月。
- **助记**：abs（离开）+ tain（拿住）→ 拿住自己离开 → 克制/戒除

### 2. **copious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：大量的；丰富的；充裕的
- **英文释义**：abundant in supply or quantity
- **例句**：
  - The student took copious notes during the lecture to prepare for the exam.
  - 学生在讲座中做了大量笔记以准备考试。
- **助记**：copi（丰富）+ ous → 丰富的 → 大量的

### 3. **diligent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：勤勉的；用功的；勤奋的
- **英文释义**：having or showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties
- **例句**：
  - The diligent researcher spent years gathering data for her groundbreaking study.
  - 这位勤勉的研究员花了数年时间为她开创性的研究收集数据。
- **助记**：dilig（珍惜/勤劳）+ ent → 珍惜时间的 → 勤勉的

### 4. **encroach** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：侵占；侵犯；蚕食
- **英文释义**：to intrude on a person's territory or rights; to advance beyond proper limits
- **例句**：
  - Urban development continues to encroach upon natural habitats across the country.
  - 城市开发持续侵占全国各地的自然栖息地。
- **助记**：en（进入）+ croach（钩子）→ 用钩子勾进来 → 侵占

### 5. **foster** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：培养；促进；鼓励；寄养
- **英文释义**：to encourage or promote the development of something; to bring up a child not one's own
- **例句**：
  - The mentoring program fosters a love of reading among disadvantaged children.
  - 这个辅导计划培养了弱势儿童对阅读的热爱。
- **助记**：foster → 联想"food + ster"→ 给予养分 → 培养/促进

### 6. **incline** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 倾向；倾斜 n. 斜坡；倾斜面
- **英文释义**：v. to feel willing or favorably disposed toward; to lean or turn away from a given direction; n. a slope
- **例句**：
  - I'm inclined to agree with your proposal, but I need more time to think it over.
  - 我倾向于同意你的提议，但我需要更多时间考虑。
- **助记**：in（向）+ cline（倾斜）→ 向某方向倾斜 → 倾向/斜坡

### 7. **meager** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：贫乏的；微薄的；不足的
- **英文释义**：lacking in quantity or quality; inadequate
- **例句**：
  - The refugees survived on meager rations of bread and water for weeks.
  - 难民们靠微薄的面包和水配给维持了数周。
- **助记**：meager → 联想"没个（东西）"→ 什么都没有 → 贫乏的

### 8. **propel** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：推进；驱使；促使
- **英文释义**：to drive, push, or cause to move in a particular direction
- **例句**：
  - Her ambition and determination propelled her to the top of her field.
  - 她的雄心和决心推动她达到了所在领域的顶峰。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ pel（推）→ 向前推 → 推进/驱使

### 9. **rhetoric** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：修辞；花言巧语；雄辩术
- **英文释义**：the art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing; language designed to persuade but often lacking substance
- **例句**：
  - The politician's speech was filled with empty rhetoric but offered no concrete solutions.
  - 这位政客的演讲充满了空洞的花言巧语，却没有提供具体的解决方案。
- **助记**：rhet（说话）+ oric → 说话的艺术 → 修辞/雄辩术

### 10. **truncate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：截断；缩短；删节
- **英文释义**：to shorten by cutting off the top or the end
- **例句**：
  - The editor truncated the article from 5000 words to 3000 to fit the magazine.
  - 编辑将文章从5000字截短到3000字以适应杂志版面。
- **助记**：trunc（树干/截断）+ ate → 像砍树干一样截断 → 截断/缩短

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ambivalent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：矛盾的；犹豫不决的；模棱两可的
- **英文释义**：having mixed feelings or contradictory ideas about something
- **例句**：
  - She felt ambivalent about leaving her hometown — excited for new opportunities but sad to leave friends behind.
  - 她对离开家乡感到矛盾——既为新机会感到兴奋又为离开朋友感到难过。
- **助记**：ambi（两个）+ val（力量/价值）+ ent → 两种力量并存 → 矛盾的

### 12. **disparate** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：迥然不同的；不相干的；无法比较的
- **英文释义**：essentially different in kind; not allowing comparison
- **例句**：
  - The committee struggled to reconcile the disparate views of its members.
  - 委员会难以调和其成员之间迥然不同的观点。
- **助记**：dis（不）+ par（相等）+ ate → 不相等的 → 迥然不同的

### 13. **grandiose** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：宏伟的；浮夸的；不切实际的
- **英文释义**：impressive or magnificent in appearance or style; extravagantly or pretentiously imposing
- **例句**：
  - His grandiose plans for the company sounded impressive but lacked practical details.
  - 他对公司的宏伟计划听起来令人印象深刻，但缺乏实际细节。
- **助记**：grand（宏大）+ iose → 过度宏大的 → 宏伟的/浮夸的

### 14. **myopic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：近视的；目光短浅的；缺乏远见的
- **英文释义**：nearsighted; lacking imagination, foresight, or intellectual insight
- **例句**：
  - The company's myopic focus on short-term profits led to its long-term decline.
  - 公司目光短浅地只关注短期利润，导致了长期衰退。
- **助记**：my（闭眼）+ opic（眼/视觉）→ 闭着眼看 → 近视的/目光短浅的

### 15. **pragmatic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；实用主义的；注重实效的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically in a way that is based on practical considerations
- **例句**：
  - The manager took a pragmatic approach to the problem, focusing on what could be done immediately.
  - 经理对这个问题采取了务实的方法，专注于可以立即做的事情。
- **助记**：pragm（行动/事务）+ atic → 注重行动的 → 务实的
