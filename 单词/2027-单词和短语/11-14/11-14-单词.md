## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **incessant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不停的；持续不断的
- **英文释义**：continuing without pause or interruption
- **例句**：
  - The incessant noise from the construction site kept the residents awake.
  - 建筑工地持续不断的噪音让居民无法入睡。
  - She complained about the incessant rain that lasted for an entire week.
  - 她抱怨连续下了整整一周的不停的雨。
- **助记**：in（不）+ cess（停止）+ ant → 不停止的 → 不停的

### 2. **embark** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：开始；着手；上船
- **英文释义**：to begin a course of action; to go on board a ship or aircraft
- **例句**：
  - She decided to embark on a new career in education.
  - 她决定开始教育方面的新事业。
  - The passengers embarked the cruise ship at the port of departure.
  - 乘客们在出发港登上了游轮。
- **助记**：em（进入）+ bark（船）→ 进入船中 → 开始旅程

### 3. **curtail** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：缩减；削减；限制
- **英文释义**：to reduce in extent or quantity; to impose a restriction on
- **例句**：
  - The company was forced to curtail its spending due to declining revenue.
  - 由于收入下降，公司被迫缩减开支。
  - The new curfew curtails the freedom of teenagers to go out at night.
  - 新的宵禁限制了青少年晚上外出的自由。
- **助记**：curt（短）+ ail → 使变短 → 缩减

### 4. **disparity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：差距；不等；不同
- **英文释义**：a great difference; inequality
- **例句**：
  - There is a growing disparity between the rich and the poor in many countries.
  - 许多国家贫富差距在不断扩大。
  - The disparity in education quality between urban and rural areas needs attention.
  - 城乡教育质量的差距需要关注。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ par（平等）+ ity → 不平等 → 差距

### 5. **revoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：撤销；废除；取消
- **英文释义**：to put an end to the validity or operation of a decree, decision, or law
- **例句**：
  - The government revoked his driving license after the serious accident.
  - 严重事故后政府撤销了他的驾照。
  - The university may revoke a degree if academic fraud is discovered.
  - 如果发现学术欺诈，大学可能会撤销学位。
- **助记**：re（回）+ voke（叫）→ 叫回来 → 撤销

### 6. **fervent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情的；热烈的；强烈的
- **英文释义**：having or displaying a passionate intensity
- **例句**：
  - She is a fervent supporter of environmental conservation.
  - 她是环境保护的热情支持者。
  - His fervent wish was to see his hometown one more time.
  - 他强烈的愿望是再看一次家乡。
- **助记**：ferv（热）+ ent → 热的 → 热情的

### 7. **stipulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：规定；约定；明确要求
- **英文释义**：to demand or specify as part of an agreement
- **例句**：
  - The contract stipulates that payment must be made within 30 days.
  - 合同规定必须在30天内付款。
  - The scholarship stipulates a minimum GPA of 3.5.
  - 奖学金规定最低GPA为3.5。
- **助记**：stipul（约定）+ ate → 约定 → 规定

### 8. **nonchalant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：冷淡的；若无其事的；不在乎的
- **英文释义**：feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety or interest
- **例句**：
  - He appeared nonchalant about the exam results, though inside he was nervous.
  - 他对考试成绩表现得若无其事，但内心其实很紧张。
  - Her nonchalant attitude towards the deadline worried her teammates.
  - 她对截止日期不在乎的态度让队友们担忧。
- **助记**：non（不）+ chalant（在意）→ 不在意的 → 若无其事的

### 9. **obsolete** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过时的；淘汰的；废弃的
- **英文释义**：no longer produced or used; out of date
- **例句**：
  - Cassette tapes have become obsolete in the age of digital music.
  - 在数字音乐时代，盒式磁带已经过时了。
  - The rapid pace of technology makes many devices obsolete within a few years.
  - 技术的快速发展使许多设备在几年内就被淘汰。
- **助记**：ob（反对）+ sol（使用）+ ete → 不再使用 → 过时的

### 10. **dismantle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：拆除；拆卸；废除
- **英文释义**：to take apart; to destroy the structure of
- **例句**：
  - Workers began to dismantle the old bridge to make way for a new one.
  - 工人们开始拆除旧桥，为新桥让路。
  - The organization was dismantled after the corruption scandal.
  - 腐败丑闻后该组织被废除了。
- **助记**：dis（去除）+ mantle（外衣/覆盖）→ 去除覆盖物 → 拆除

### 高一词汇

### 11. **penurious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：贫穷的；吝啬的；小气的
- **英文释义**：extremely poor; mean with money
- **例句**：
  - The penurious old man refused to spend money on anything except food.
  - 那个吝啬的老人除了食物外拒绝花钱。
  - Growing up in penurious conditions made her appreciate every opportunity.
  - 在贫穷的环境中长大使她珍惜每一个机会。
- **助记**：penur（贫穷）+ ious → 贫穷的

### 12. **inchoate** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：初始的；未成形的；刚开始的
- **英文释义**：just begun and so not fully formed or developed
- **例句**：
  - The plan was still inchoate and needed much more development.
  - 计划仍处于初始阶段，需要更多的完善。
  - His inchoate ideas gradually took shape as he discussed them with colleagues.
  - 他未成形的想法在与同事讨论后逐渐成型。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ choate（开始）→ 刚进入开始 → 初始的

### 13. **effervescent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：冒泡的；兴高采烈的；活泼的
- **英文释义**：giving off bubbles; vivacious and enthusiastic
- **例句**：
  - Her effervescent personality made her the center of attention at every party.
  - 她活泼的个性使她成为每次聚会的焦点。
  - The effervescent drink tickled her nose as she took a sip.
  - 冒泡的饮料在她喝一口时挠了挠她的鼻子。
- **助记**：ef（出）+ ferv（沸腾）+ escent → 向外沸腾 → 冒泡的/活泼的

### 14. **cognoscenti** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：内行；鉴赏家；行家
- **英文释义**：people who are considered to be especially well informed about a particular subject
- **例句**：
  - The wine was praised by cognoscenti for its complexity and depth.
  - 这款酒因其复杂性和深度而受到行家的赞赏。
  - Only the cognoscenti could appreciate the subtle nuances in his music.
  - 只有内行才能欣赏他音乐中微妙的细节。
- **助记**：意大利语 cognoscere（认知）→ 有认知的人 → 内行

### 15. **aphorism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：格言；警句；箴言
- **英文释义**：a pithy observation that contains a general truth
- **例句**：
  - "Actions speak louder than words" is a well-known aphorism.
  - "行动胜于言语"是一句著名的格言。
  - The philosopher was known for his witty aphorisms about human nature.
  - 这位哲学家以关于人性的机智格言闻名。
- **助记**：aph（从）+ or（界限）+ ism → 从界限中提炼的精华 → 格言
