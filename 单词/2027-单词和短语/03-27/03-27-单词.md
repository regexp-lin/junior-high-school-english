## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **agony** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：极度痛苦；苦恼
- **英文释义**：extreme physical or mental suffering
- **例句**：
  - He was in agony after breaking his leg.
  - 他腿骨折后痛苦万分。
- **助记**：ag（做）+ ony → 做什么都痛 → 极度痛苦

### 2. **capsule** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：胶囊；太空舱；简要总结
- **英文释义**：a small case containing a dose of medicine; a small pressurized cabin for astronauts
- **例句**：
  - The doctor prescribed two capsules a day for the patient.
  - 医生给病人开了每天两粒胶囊。
- **助记**：cap（帽子/盖）+ sule → 带盖的小容器 → 胶囊

### 3. **dazzle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使目眩；使惊叹；眩目
- **英文释义**：to blind temporarily with a sudden bright light; to impress greatly
- **例句**：
  - The fireworks dazzled the spectators.
  - 烟花使观众们眼花缭乱。
- **助记**：dazz（谐音"大炸"）+ le → 大爆炸一样耀眼 → 使目眩

### 4. **erupt** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：喷发；爆发；突然发生
- **英文释义**：to break out or burst forth suddenly and violently
- **例句**：
  - The volcano erupted and sent ash into the sky.
  - 火山爆发，将火山灰喷向天空。
- **助记**：e（出）+ rupt（破裂）→ 破裂涌出 → 喷发

### 5. **friction** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：摩擦；摩擦力；冲突
- **英文释义**：the resistance that one surface encounters when moving over another; conflict or disagreement
- **例句**：
  - Friction between the two teams led to an argument.
  - 两队之间的摩擦导致了争吵。
- **助记**：frict（摩擦）+ ion → 摩擦的状态 → 摩擦

### 6. **haze** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：薄雾；霾；朦胧
- **英文释义**：a slight obscuration of the lower atmosphere, typically caused by fine particles
- **例句**：
  - The mountains were hidden behind a thin haze.
  - 山脉隐藏在一层薄雾之后。
- **助记**：haze 谐音"灰子"→ 灰蒙蒙的空气 → 薄雾/霾

### 7. **literacy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：识字能力；读写能力；素养
- **英文释义**：the ability to read and write; competence in a specified area
- **例句**：
  - Improving literacy rates is a key goal of the education program.
  - 提高识字率是教育项目的关键目标。
- **助记**：liter（文字）+ acy → 与文字相关的能力 → 读写能力

### 8. **oval** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 椭圆形的 n. 椭圆形
- **英文释义**：adj. having a rounded and slightly elongated outline; n. an oval shape
- **例句**：
  - The table in the dining room is oval-shaped.
  - 餐厅里的桌子是椭圆形的。
- **助记**：ov（蛋）+ al → 蛋的形状 → 椭圆形

### 9. **sentinel** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：哨兵；守卫
- **英文释义**：a soldier or guard whose job is to stand and keep watch
- **例句**：
  - A sentinel stood guard at the entrance of the camp.
  - 一名哨兵在营地入口站岗。
- **助记**：senti（感觉）+ nel → 用感觉来守卫的人 → 哨兵

### 10. **turbulent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：动荡的；混乱的；汹涌的
- **英文释义**：characterized by conflict, disorder, or confusion; not stable or calm
- **例句**：
  - The country went through a turbulent period of political change.
  - 国家经历了一段动荡的政治变革时期。
- **助记**：turb（搅动）+ ulent → 被搅动的 → 动荡的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **adversary** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：对手；敌手
- **英文释义**：one's opponent in a contest, conflict, or dispute
- **例句**：
  - The chess player studied his adversary's every move carefully.
  - 棋手仔细研究了对手的每一步棋。
- **助记**：ad（朝向）+ vers（转向）+ ary → 面朝你转过来的人 → 对手

### 12. **coherent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：连贯的；一致的；清楚的
- **英文释义**：logical and consistent; able to speak clearly and make sense
- **例句**：
  - She gave a coherent explanation of the complex theory.
  - 她对这一复杂理论给出了连贯的解释。
- **助记**：co（共同）+ her（粘附）+ ent → 共同粘在一起的 → 连贯的

### 13. **emancipate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：解放；使不受束缚
- **英文释义**：to set free from legal, social, or political restrictions
- **例句**：
  - The law emancipated enslaved people across the country.
  - 这项法律解放了全国被奴役的人们。
- **助记**：e（出）+ man（手）+ cipate（拿）→ 从别人手中拿出来 → 解放

### 14. **paradigm** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：范式；典范；模式
- **英文释义**：a typical example or pattern of something; a model
- **例句**：
  - The discovery caused a paradigm shift in scientific thinking.
  - 这一发现引发了科学思维的范式转变。
- **助记**：para（旁边）+ digm（展示）→ 在旁边展示的模型 → 范式

### 15. **trajectory** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：轨迹；轨道；发展路径
- **英文释义**：the path followed by a projectile or an object; the general direction of development
- **例句**：
  - The trajectory of the rocket was carefully calculated.
  - 火箭的轨迹经过了仔细计算。
- **助记**：tra（穿过）+ ject（投射）+ ory → 投射穿过的路径 → 轨迹
