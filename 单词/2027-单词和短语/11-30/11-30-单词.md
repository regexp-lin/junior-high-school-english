## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **corroborate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证实；确证；支持
- **英文释义**：to confirm or give support to a statement, theory, or finding
- **例句**：
  - Multiple witnesses corroborated the victim's account of the incident.
  - 多名证人证实了受害者对事件的描述。
  - The lab results corroborated the doctor's initial diagnosis.
  - 化验结果证实了医生的初步诊断。
- **助记**：cor（一起）+ robor（力量）+ ate → 一起增加力量 → 证实

### 2. **discerning** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有鉴赏力的；有洞察力的；眼光敏锐的
- **英文释义**：having or showing good judgment
- **例句**：
  - A discerning reader can distinguish between reliable and unreliable sources.
  - 有鉴赏力的读者能区分可靠和不可靠的来源。
  - The restaurant appeals to discerning diners who appreciate fine cuisine.
  - 这家餐厅吸引有鉴赏力的食客，他们懂得欣赏美食。
- **助记**：discern（辨别）+ ing → 善于辨别的 → 有鉴赏力的

### 3. **obliviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使遗忘；抹去记忆
- **英文释义**：to cause to forget; to render oblivious
- **例句**：
  - Time cannot obliviate the memories of those who suffered during the war.
  - 时间无法抹去那些在战争中受苦之人的记忆。
  - He wished he could obliviate the embarrassing moment from everyone's minds.
  - 他希望能从每个人的记忆中抹去那个尴尬的时刻。
- **助记**：oblivi（遗忘）+ ate → 使遗忘

### 4. **surreptitious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：偷偷摸摸的；秘密的；鬼鬼祟祟的
- **英文释义**：kept secret, especially because it would not be approved of
- **例句**：
  - He made a surreptitious phone call to warn his friend about the investigation.
  - 他偷偷打了一个电话警告朋友关于调查的事。
  - The surreptitious transfer of funds raised suspicions among the auditors.
  - 秘密的资金转移引起了审计人员的怀疑。
- **助记**：sur（在下面）+ rept（爬）+ itious → 在下面爬行 → 偷偷摸摸的

### 5. **replenish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：补充；重新装满；补给
- **英文释义**：to fill something up again; to restore to the former level
- **例句**：
  - The store needs to replenish its stock before the weekend sale.
  - 商店需要在周末促销前补充库存。
  - Drinking water throughout the day helps replenish the body's fluids.
  - 全天饮水有助于补充身体的水分。
- **助记**：re（再）+ plen（满）+ ish → 再次充满 → 补充

### 6. **vociferous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：大声疾呼的；喧嚷的；激烈的
- **英文释义**：vehement or clamorous in expressing feelings or opinions
- **例句**：
  - The vociferous protests outside the parliament building lasted for days.
  - 议会大厦外喧嚷的抗议持续了好几天。
  - She was a vociferous advocate for animal rights throughout her career.
  - 她在整个职业生涯中都是动物权利的大声疾呼者。
- **助记**：voci（声音）+ fer（带来）+ ous → 带来大声音的 → 大声疾呼的

### 7. **adjudicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：裁决；判决；评判
- **英文释义**：to make a formal judgment or decision about a problem or disputed matter
- **例句**：
  - An independent panel was appointed to adjudicate the dispute between the two companies.
  - 一个独立小组被任命来裁决两家公司之间的纠纷。
  - The judge adjudicated the case fairly, considering all the evidence presented.
  - 法官公正地判决了此案，考虑了提交的所有证据。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ judic（判断）+ ate → 做出判断 → 裁决

### 8. **capricious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：反复无常的；善变的；任性的
- **英文释义**：given to sudden and unaccountable changes of mood or behavior
- **例句**：
  - The capricious weather in spring makes it difficult to plan outdoor events.
  - 春天反复无常的天气使户外活动难以计划。
  - Her capricious decision-making style frustrated her team members.
  - 她善变的决策风格让团队成员感到沮丧。
- **助记**：capri（山羊）+ cious → 像山羊一样蹦来蹦去 → 反复无常的

### 9. **galvanize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激励；鼓舞；使振奋
- **英文释义**：to shock or excite someone into taking action
- **例句**：
  - The leader's powerful speech galvanized the crowd into action.
  - 领导者有力的演讲激励了群众采取行动。
  - The crisis galvanized the international community to provide immediate aid.
  - 危机激发了国际社会提供紧急援助。
- **助记**：galvan（电流）+ ize → 像电流一样刺激 → 激励

### 10. **indomitable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不屈不挠的；不可征服的
- **英文释义**：impossible to subdue or defeat
- **例句**：
  - Her indomitable spirit helped her overcome a series of devastating setbacks.
  - 她不屈不挠的精神帮助她克服了一系列毁灭性的挫折。
  - The indomitable fortress withstood months of siege without falling.
  - 不可征服的堡垒经受了数月围攻而未陷落。
- **助记**：in（不）+ domit（驯服）+ able → 不可驯服的 → 不屈不挠的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **pareidolia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：空想性错视；在随机图案中看到有意义图像的倾向
- **英文释义**：the tendency to perceive a specific, often meaningful image in a random or ambiguous visual pattern
- **例句**：
  - Pareidolia explains why people often see faces in clouds, rocks, and tree bark.
  - 空想性错视解释了为什么人们经常在云朵、岩石和树皮中看到面孔。
  - The phenomenon of pareidolia is responsible for many supposed sightings of religious figures in everyday objects.
  - 空想性错视现象是许多在日常物品中看到宗教人物的原因。
- **助记**：para（旁边/异常）+ eidol（图像）+ ia → 看到不存在的图像 → 空想性错视

### 12. **ethereal** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：空灵的；飘渺的；超凡脱俗的
- **英文释义**：extremely delicate and light in a way that seems too perfect for this world
- **例句**：
  - The ethereal music of the harp created a dreamlike atmosphere in the concert hall.
  - 竖琴空灵的音乐在音乐厅里营造了如梦如幻的氛围。
  - Her ethereal beauty made her seem like a character from a fairy tale.
  - 她超凡脱俗的美丽使她看起来像童话中的人物。
- **助记**：ether（以太/天空）+ eal → 像天空一样 → 空灵的

### 13. **defenestration** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：将人从窗户扔出的行为；罢免
- **英文释义**：the act of throwing someone out of a window; the act of dismissing someone from a position
- **例句**：
  - The Defenestration of Prague in 1618 is one of history's most unusual political acts.
  - 1618年的布拉格掷出窗外事件是历史上最不寻常的政治行为之一。
  - The sudden defenestration of the CEO shocked the entire business world.
  - 对首席执行官的突然罢免震惊了整个商界。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ fenest（窗户）+ ration → 从窗户扔下去 → 掷出窗外

### 14. **sesquipedalophobia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：长单词恐惧症
- **英文释义**：the fear of long words
- **例句**：
  - Ironically, sesquipedalophobia is itself a very long word, making it terrifying for those who suffer from it.
  - 具有讽刺意味的是，"长单词恐惧症"这个词本身就很长，对患有这种恐惧症的人来说很可怕。
  - Some language learners joke about having sesquipedalophobia when faced with complex vocabulary.
  - 一些语言学习者在面对复杂词汇时开玩笑说自己患有长单词恐惧症。
- **助记**：sesquipedal（冗长的词）+ o + phobia（恐惧症）→ 害怕长词 → 长单词恐惧症

### 15. **eudaimonia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：幸福；人生的圆满；至善之乐
- **英文释义**：a state of happiness and well-being; human flourishing (Aristotle's concept)
- **例句**：
  - Aristotle believed that eudaimonia was the highest human good, achieved through virtuous living.
  - 亚里士多德认为幸福是人类的最高善，通过有德行的生活来实现。
  - True eudaimonia comes not from material wealth but from living a purposeful life.
  - 真正的幸福不是来自物质财富，而是来自过有意义的生活。
- **助记**：eu（好）+ daimon（精灵/命运）+ ia → 好的命运 → 幸福/圆满
