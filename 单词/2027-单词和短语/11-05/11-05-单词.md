## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **perceive** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：感知；察觉；理解
- **英文释义**：to become aware of through the senses; to understand or interpret
- **例句**：
  - Dogs can perceive sounds that are inaudible to humans.
  - 狗能感知到人类听不到的声音。
  - She perceived a change in his attitude after the meeting.
  - 她察觉到会后他态度的变化。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ ceive（抓住）→ 完全抓住信息 → 感知

### 2. **stagnant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：停滞的；不景气的；不流动的
- **英文释义**：showing no activity or growth; not flowing or moving
- **例句**：
  - The economy has been stagnant for the past two years.
  - 过去两年经济一直停滞不前。
  - Mosquitoes breed in stagnant water, so keep drains clear.
  - 蚊子在死水中繁殖，所以要保持排水畅通。
- **助记**：stagn（池塘/停）+ ant → 像池塘一样不流动 → 停滞的

### 3. **amplify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：放大；增强；详述
- **英文释义**：to increase the strength or amount of; to make more detailed
- **例句**：
  - The microphone amplifies the speaker's voice so everyone can hear.
  - 麦克风放大了演讲者的声音，让每个人都能听到。
  - Could you amplify your remarks with some specific examples?
  - 你能用一些具体的例子来详述你的意见吗？
- **助记**：ampli（大）+ fy（使）→ 使变大 → 放大

### 4. **contingent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：取决于……的；偶然的；附带的
- **英文释义**：subject to chance; dependent on something uncertain
- **例句**：
  - Our trip to the beach is contingent on the weather being good.
  - 我们去海边的行程取决于天气好不好。
  - The bonus payment is contingent upon meeting the sales target.
  - 奖金取决于是否达到销售目标。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ ting（触）+ ent → 需要触碰到条件 → 取决于

### 5. **encompass** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：包含；包围；涵盖
- **英文释义**：to surround and have or hold within; to include comprehensively
- **例句**：
  - The curriculum encompasses a wide range of subjects, from science to art.
  - 课程涵盖了从科学到艺术的广泛科目。
  - The national park encompasses over 10,000 acres of wilderness.
  - 国家公园包含超过一万英亩的荒野。
- **助记**：en（使）+ compass（范围）→ 使进入范围 → 包含

### 6. **relegate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：降级；贬低；归入
- **英文释义**：to assign to a lower rank or less important position
- **例句**：
  - The team was relegated to the second division after a poor season.
  - 球队在糟糕的赛季后被降级到第二级联赛。
  - Important issues were relegated to the bottom of the agenda.
  - 重要的议题被降到了议程的最后。
- **助记**：re（回）+ leg（派遣）+ ate → 派回到后面 → 降级

### 7. **stringent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严格的；严厉的；紧缩的
- **英文释义**：strict, precise, and exacting
- **例句**：
  - The factory must comply with stringent safety regulations.
  - 工厂必须遵守严格的安全法规。
  - Stringent budget cuts were necessary to reduce the national debt.
  - 严格的预算削减对于减少国债是必要的。
- **助记**：string（绳子）+ ent → 像绳子一样紧 → 严格的

### 8. **reconcile** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：调和；使和解；使一致
- **英文释义**：to restore friendly relations; to make compatible
- **例句**：
  - The mediator helped reconcile the two feuding families.
  - 调解人帮助两个世仇家庭和解。
  - It's hard to reconcile his words with his actions.
  - 很难使他的言行一致。
- **助记**：re（再）+ concile（协调）→ 再次协调 → 调和

### 9. **trivialize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：轻视；使变得不重要
- **英文释义**：to make something seem less important, significant, or complex than it really is
- **例句**：
  - We should not trivialize the impact of bullying on young people.
  - 我们不应该轻视欺凌对年轻人的影响。
  - The media often trivializes serious issues by oversimplifying them.
  - 媒体常常通过过度简化来轻视严肃的问题。
- **助记**：trivial（琐碎的）+ ize → 使变得琐碎 → 轻视

### 10. **pragmatic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；注重实效的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically
- **例句**：
  - We need a pragmatic solution to this problem, not an idealistic one.
  - 我们需要一个务实的解决方案，而不是理想化的。
  - Her pragmatic approach to management earned her widespread respect.
  - 她务实的管理方式为她赢得了广泛的尊重。
- **助记**：pragm（行动）+ atic → 注重行动的 → 务实的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **hagiography** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：圣徒传记；理想化的传记
- **英文释义**：a biography that treats its subject with undue reverence; the writing of the lives of saints
- **例句**：
  - The book was more hagiography than honest biography, ignoring all his faults.
  - 这本书与其说是诚实的传记，不如说是理想化的圣徒传记，忽略了他所有的缺点。
  - Academic historians criticized the work as hagiography rather than scholarship.
  - 学术历史学家批评这部作品是溢美之词，而非学术研究。
- **助记**：hagio（圣洁的）+ graphy（写作）→ 写圣人的 → 圣徒传记

### 12. **fastidious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：过分挑剔的；苛求的；一丝不苟的
- **英文释义**：very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail
- **例句**：
  - She is fastidious about cleanliness, wiping every surface twice a day.
  - 她对清洁过分挑剔，每天擦拭每个表面两次。
  - The fastidious editor caught errors that everyone else had overlooked.
  - 苛求的编辑发现了其他人都忽略的错误。
- **助记**：fastid（厌恶）+ ious → 对不完美感到厌恶 → 过分挑剔的

### 13. **proselytize** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：劝人改信；传教；宣传
- **英文释义**：to convert or attempt to convert someone from one religion, belief, or opinion to another
- **例句**：
  - He spent years proselytizing about the benefits of a vegetarian diet.
  - 他花了多年时间宣传素食饮食的好处。
  - The organization was accused of proselytizing in schools.
  - 该组织被指控在学校传教。
- **助记**：proselyte（改信者）+ ize → 使人改信 → 传教

### 14. **concatenate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：连接；连结；串联
- **英文释义**：to link things together in a chain or series
- **例句**：
  - In programming, you can concatenate two strings to form a longer one.
  - 在编程中，你可以将两个字符串连接起来形成更长的字符串。
  - The events concatenated to create a perfect storm of consequences.
  - 这些事件接连发生，造成了一场完美风暴般的后果。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ caten（链）+ ate → 用链子连在一起 → 连接

### 15. **synecdoche** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：提喻法；借代
- **英文释义**：a figure of speech in which a part is used for the whole or the whole for a part
- **例句**：
  - Saying "all hands on deck" is a synecdoche, using "hands" to refer to sailors.
  - 说"全体人员各就各位"是提喻法，用"手"来指代水手。
  - "Wheels" referring to a car is a common example of synecdoche.
  - "轮子"指代汽车是提喻法的常见例子。
- **助记**：syn（一起）+ ec（从中）+ doche（接受）→ 从部分接受整体含义 → 提喻法
