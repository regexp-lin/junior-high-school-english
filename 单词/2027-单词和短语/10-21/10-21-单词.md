## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **avid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：热衷的；酷爱的；渴望的
- **英文释义**：having an eager desire for; enthusiastic
- **例句**：
  - She is an avid reader who finishes three books a week.
  - 她是一个热衷的读者，每周读完三本书。
  - Avid fans camped outside the stadium to get the best seats.
  - 狂热的粉丝在体育场外露营以抢到最好的座位。
- **助记**：avid 联想 avoid（避开）的反义 → 不避开 → 渴望/酷爱

### 2. **benign** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：温和的；良性的；无害的
- **英文释义**：gentle and kindly; (of disease) not harmful
- **例句**：
  - The doctor confirmed that the tumor was benign, not cancerous.
  - 医生确认肿瘤是良性的，不是癌症。
  - The benign climate of the island attracts many retirees.
  - 岛上温和的气候吸引了许多退休者。
- **助记**：ben（好）+ ign → 好的 → 温和/良性

### 3. **cogent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：令人信服的；有说服力的；清晰的
- **英文释义**：clear, logical, and convincing
- **例句**：
  - She presented a cogent argument that convinced the entire committee.
  - 她提出了一个令人信服的论点，说服了整个委员会。
  - The report provided cogent evidence for changing the policy.
  - 报告提供了改变政策的有力证据。
- **助记**：cog（思考）+ ent → 经过思考的 → 令人信服

### 4. **disdain** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：蔑视；鄙视；不屑
- **英文释义**：the feeling that someone or something is unworthy; to regard with contempt
- **例句**：
  - He looked at the cheap gift with obvious disdain.
  - 他带着明显的蔑视看着这份廉价的礼物。
  - She disdained the shortcuts others took to succeed.
  - 她不屑于别人为成功所走的捷径。
- **助记**：dis（不）+ dain（值得）→ 不值得看 → 蔑视

### 5. **exert** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：施加；运用；努力
- **英文释义**：to apply or bring to bear a force, influence, or quality
- **例句**：
  - Teachers exert a great influence on their students' character.
  - 老师对学生的性格施加重大影响。
  - She had to exert all her strength to open the stuck window.
  - 她不得不用尽全力才打开卡住的窗户。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ert（力）→ 发出力量 → 施加

### 6. **foresight** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：远见；先见之明；预见
- **英文释义**：the ability to predict or the action of predicting what will happen
- **例句**：
  - Her foresight in investing early proved extremely profitable.
  - 她早期投资的远见证明是极其有利可图的。
  - Good leaders need foresight to prepare for future challenges.
  - 好的领导者需要远见来应对未来挑战。
- **助记**：fore（前面）+ sight（看）→ 提前看到 → 远见

### 7. **garner** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：收集；获得；积累
- **英文释义**：to gather or collect; to acquire by effort
- **例句**：
  - The movie garnered five awards at the international film festival.
  - 这部电影在国际电影节上获得五项大奖。
  - Scientists garnered data from hundreds of experiments.
  - 科学家从数百次实验中收集数据。
- **助记**：garner 联想 garden（花园）→ 在花园里收获 → 收集

### 8. **humdrum** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：单调乏味的；平淡无奇的
- **英文释义**：lacking excitement or variety; dull; monotonous
- **例句**：
  - His humdrum office job made him dream of traveling the world.
  - 他单调乏味的办公室工作让他梦想环游世界。
  - The village's humdrum pace of life appealed to those seeking tranquility.
  - 村子里平淡无奇的生活节奏吸引了寻找宁静的人。
- **助记**：hum（嗡嗡声）+ drum（鼓）→ 嗡嗡嗡单调的鼓声 → 乏味

### 9. **instigate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：发起；唆使；煽动
- **英文释义**：to bring about or initiate an action or event; to provoke
- **例句**：
  - The workers instigated a strike to protest unfair wages.
  - 工人们发起罢工抗议不公平的工资。
  - He was accused of instigating the riot outside the parliament.
  - 他被指控在议会外煽动骚乱。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ stig（刺激）+ ate → 刺激使行动 → 煽动

### 10. **meander** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：蜿蜒；漫步；闲逛
- **英文释义**：to follow a winding course; to wander aimlessly
- **例句**：
  - The river meanders through the valley for miles.
  - 河流蜿蜒穿过山谷数英里。
  - We meandered through the old town, admiring the architecture.
  - 我们在老城区漫步，欣赏着建筑。
- **助记**：来自希腊 Meander 河（蜿蜒曲折的河）→ 蜿蜒

### 高一词汇

### 11. **mollify** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：平息；安慰；缓和
- **英文释义**：to appease the anger or anxiety of someone
- **例句**：
  - The manager tried to mollify the upset client with a full refund.
  - 经理试图用全额退款来安抚不满的客户。
  - A sincere apology can often mollify even the angriest person.
  - 真诚的道歉往往能平息最愤怒的人。
- **助记**：moll（柔软）+ ify → 使变软 → 平息/安慰

### 12. **nonchalance** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：漫不经心；冷淡；不在乎
- **英文释义**：the quality of being casually calm and relaxed
- **例句**：
  - His nonchalance in the face of danger impressed the rescue team.
  - 他面对危险时的镇定自若给救援队留下了深刻印象。
  - She accepted the award with surprising nonchalance.
  - 她以令人意外的淡然接受了奖项。
- **助记**：nonchalant（漫不经心的）→ nonchalance（名词形式）

### 13. **platitude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：陈词滥调；老生常谈；空话
- **英文释义**：a remark or statement that has been overused and is unoriginal
- **例句**：
  - His speech was full of platitudes like "hard work always pays off."
  - 他的演讲充满了"努力总会有回报"之类的陈词滥调。
  - Offering platitudes instead of practical help insults the listener.
  - 提供空话而非实际帮助是对听者的侮辱。
- **助记**：plat（平坦）+ itude → 平淡无奇的话 → 陈词滥调

### 14. **rescind** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：废除；撤销；取消
- **英文释义**：to revoke, cancel, or repeal a law, order, or agreement
- **例句**：
  - The university rescinded his admission after discovering false documents.
  - 大学在发现虚假文件后撤销了他的录取。
  - The government rescinded the controversial regulation following protests.
  - 政府在抗议活动后废除了争议性法规。
- **助记**：re（回）+ scind（切）→ 切回去 → 撤销

### 15. **taciturn** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：沉默寡言的；不爱说话的
- **英文释义**：reserved or uncommunicative in speech; saying little
- **例句**：
  - The taciturn farmer spoke only when absolutely necessary.
  - 沉默寡言的农夫只在绝对必要时才说话。
  - Behind his taciturn exterior, he had a keen sense of humor.
  - 在他沉默的外表背后，他有敏锐的幽默感。
- **助记**：tacit（沉默的）+ urn → 沉默的 → 寡言
