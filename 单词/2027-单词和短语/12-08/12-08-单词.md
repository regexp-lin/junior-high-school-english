## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **expiate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：赎（罪）；补偿；弥补
- **英文释义**：to atone for guilt or wrongdoing
- **例句**：
  - He spent years doing charity work to expiate the guilt he felt for his past actions.
  - 他花了多年做慈善工作来赎他对过去行为的罪恶感。
  - The criminal sought to expiate his crimes through sincere repentance and community service.
  - 罪犯试图通过真诚的忏悔和社区服务来赎罪。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ pi（虔诚）+ ate → 通过虔诚把罪排出 → 赎罪

### 2. **taciturn** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：沉默寡言的；不善言谈的
- **英文释义**：(of a person) reserved or uncommunicative in speech
- **例句**：
  - The taciturn farmer rarely spoke more than a few words at a time.
  - 沉默寡言的农民一次很少说超过几个字。
  - Behind his taciturn exterior lay a deeply thoughtful and sensitive mind.
  - 在他沉默寡言的外表下是一颗深思熟虑且敏感的心灵。
- **助记**：tacit（沉默的）+ urn → 沉默的 → 沉默寡言的

### 3. **exonerate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：免除（责任/罪责）；证明无罪
- **英文释义**：to absolve someone from blame for a fault or wrongdoing
- **例句**：
  - DNA evidence exonerated the man who had been wrongly imprisoned for ten years.
  - DNA证据证明了那个被错误关押十年的人的清白。
  - The investigation exonerated the officer of all charges of misconduct.
  - 调查免除了该官员所有不当行为的指控。
- **助记**：ex（去除）+ oner（负担）+ ate → 去除负担 → 免除罪责

### 4. **egregious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：极其恶劣的；令人震惊的；骇人听闻的
- **英文释义**：outstandingly bad; shocking
- **例句**：
  - The company's egregious violation of safety standards resulted in a massive fine.
  - 公司对安全标准极其恶劣的违反导致了巨额罚款。
  - His egregious spelling errors in the formal report embarrassed the entire department.
  - 他在正式报告中骇人听闻的拼写错误让整个部门尴尬。
- **助记**：e（出）+ greg（群）+ ious → 从群体中突出的（坏的方面）→ 极其恶劣的

### 5. **remonstrate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：抗议；提出异议；规劝
- **英文释义**：to make a forcefully reproachful protest
- **例句**：
  - The citizens remonstrated with the city council over the decision to demolish the historic building.
  - 市民们就拆除历史建筑的决定向市议会提出了抗议。
  - She remonstrated gently with her son about his poor study habits.
  - 她温和地规劝儿子改掉不好的学习习惯。
- **助记**：re（反对）+ monstr（显示）+ ate → 显示反对 → 抗议

### 6. **languid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：慵懒的；无精打采的；缓慢的
- **英文释义**：(of a person, manner, or gesture) lacking energy; slow and relaxed
- **例句**：
  - The languid summer afternoon made everyone feel like taking a nap.
  - 慵懒的夏日午后让每个人都想打个盹。
  - She stretched with languid grace, like a cat waking from a long sleep.
  - 她以慵懒优雅的姿态伸展身体，像一只从长睡中醒来的猫。
- **助记**：langu（疲倦）+ id → 疲倦的 → 慵懒的

### 7. **obfuscate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使模糊；使混乱；使费解
- **英文释义**：to render obscure, unclear, or unintelligible
- **例句**：
  - The technical jargon in the contract was designed to obfuscate the unfair terms.
  - 合同中的技术术语旨在使不公平条款变得模糊费解。
  - Politicians sometimes obfuscate the truth by giving vague and confusing answers.
  - 政客有时通过给出模糊混乱的回答来使真相变得模糊。
- **助记**：ob（遮挡）+ fusc（暗）+ ate → 遮住使变暗 → 使模糊

### 8. **sanguine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：乐观的；充满信心的；面色红润的
- **英文释义**：optimistic or positive, especially in a difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - Despite the economic downturn, she remained sanguine about the company's future.
  - 尽管经济下滑，她对公司的未来仍然保持乐观。
  - His sanguine temperament helped the team stay motivated during the tough project.
  - 他乐观的性情帮助团队在艰难的项目中保持了积极性。
- **助记**：sanguin（血）+ e → 血气充足的 → 乐观的/红润的

### 9. **extirpate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：根除；铲除；消灭
- **英文释义**：to root out and destroy completely
- **例句**：
  - The government launched a campaign to extirpate corruption from public institutions.
  - 政府发起了一场运动来根除公共机构中的腐败。
  - Invasive species can be extremely difficult to extirpate once they become established.
  - 外来入侵物种一旦扎根就极难根除。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ tirp（根）+ ate → 把根拔出 → 根除

### 10. **propitious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：吉利的；有利的；适合的
- **英文释义**：giving or indicating a good chance of success; favorable
- **例句**：
  - The calm weather provided propitious conditions for the satellite launch.
  - 平静的天气为卫星发射提供了有利条件。
  - The new year seemed propitious for starting a business, with the economy on the rise.
  - 随着经济上升，新年似乎是创业的好时机。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ piti（寻求）+ ous → 向前寻求好兆头 → 吉利的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **alexithymia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：述情障碍（无法识别或表达自身情感）
- **英文释义**：the inability to recognize or describe one's own emotions
- **例句**：
  - People with alexithymia often struggle to identify what they are feeling, describing emotions as physical sensations.
  - 有述情障碍的人常常难以识别自己的感受，会将情绪描述为身体感觉。
  - Therapy can help individuals with alexithymia develop greater emotional awareness.
  - 治疗可以帮助有述情障碍的人发展更强的情感意识。
- **助记**：a（无）+ lexi（词）+ thym（情感）+ ia → 没有词来描述情感 → 述情障碍

### 12. **ultracrepidarian** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：越俎代庖的；对自己不懂的领域妄加评论的人
- **英文释义**：noting or pertaining to a person who criticizes or gives opinions beyond their area of expertise
- **例句**：
  - The celebrity's ultracrepidarian comments on vaccine safety drew criticism from medical professionals.
  - 这位名人对疫苗安全性越俎代庖的评论引来了医学专家的批评。
  - Don't be an ultracrepidarian — if you're not an expert in the field, listen more and opine less.
  - 别做妄加评论的人——如果你不是该领域的专家，多听少说。
- **助记**：ultra（超越）+ crepidar（鞋匠）+ ian → 超越鞋匠的范围（源自"鞋匠别评论鞋以外的事"的典故）→ 越俎代庖的

### 13. **sonder** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：恍然意识到每个路人都有同样复杂的人生
- **英文释义**：the realization that each random passerby is living a life as vivid and complex as your own
- **例句**：
  - Standing in a crowded train station, she was struck by sonder — every person around her had their own rich inner world.
  - 站在拥挤的火车站，她突然感受到了 sonder——她周围的每个人都有自己丰富的内心世界。
  - The feeling of sonder can be both humbling and comforting at the same time.
  - Sonder 的感觉可以同时让人感到谦卑和安慰。
- **助记**：sonder 源自"The Dictionary of Obscure Sorrows"，描述一种深刻的人际感悟

### 14. **hiraeth** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：乡愁；对无法回去的故乡的深切思念
- **英文释义**：a deep longing for a home or place that you cannot return to, or that may never have existed
- **例句**：
  - The Welsh concept of hiraeth describes a bittersweet nostalgia for a homeland that feels forever lost.
  - 威尔士语中的 hiraeth 描述了一种对永远失去的故乡的苦甜思念。
  - After decades abroad, he felt an intense hiraeth for the village of his childhood.
  - 在国外生活了几十年后，他对童年的村庄感到强烈的乡愁。
- **助记**：hiraeth 是威尔士语，表达一种无法被简单翻译的深层乡愁

### 15. **toska** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：一种深沉的精神痛苦；莫名的忧愁
- **英文释义**：a Russian word describing a sensation of great spiritual anguish, often without a specific cause
- **例句**：
  - Nabokov described toska as a dull ache of the soul, a longing with nothing to long for.
  - 纳博科夫将 toska 描述为灵魂的钝痛，一种无所渴望的渴望。
  - The bleak winter landscape seemed to embody the very essence of toska.
  - 凄凉的冬日景色似乎体现了 toska 的真正本质。
- **助记**：toska 是俄语，表达一种比简单的悲伤更深沉、更无法名状的精神痛苦
