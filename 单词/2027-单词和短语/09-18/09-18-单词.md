## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **kindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：点燃；激起（兴趣、感情）
- **英文释义**：to light a fire; to arouse an emotion or interest
- **例句**：
  - Dry leaves kindled easily in the summer heat.
  - 干树叶在酷暑中很容易点燃。
  - The teacher’s story kindled a love of history in her students.
  - 老师的故事激发了学生对历史的热爱。
- **助记**：与 candle（蜡烛）同源 → 点火 → 激发

### 2. **loath** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不情愿的；勉强的（常作 loath to do）
- **英文释义**：unwilling or reluctant to do something
- **例句**：
  - She was loath to admit that she had made a mistake.
  - 她不愿承认自己犯了错。
  - He seemed loath to leave the peaceful village.
  - 他似乎舍不得离开这个宁静的村庄。
- **助记**：与 loathe（厌恶）区分：loath 表“不情愿”，loathe 表“厌恶”

### 3. **maraud** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：掠夺；劫掠；四处抢劫
- **英文释义**：to roam in search of things to steal or people to attack
- **例句**：
  - Bandits marauded the countryside during the war.
  - 战争期间匪徒在乡间四处劫掠。
  - Wolves sometimes maraud sheep farms at night.
  - 狼群有时夜间袭击牧羊场。
- **助记**：法语 marauder（游荡作恶）→ 掠夺

### 4. **nominal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：名义上的；象征性的；微不足道的（费用）
- **英文释义**：existing in name only; very small in comparison with expectations
- **例句**：
  - He was the nominal leader while others made real decisions.
  - 他是名义上的领导，实权在别人手中。
  - We paid a nominal fee to join the community library.
  - 我们象征性地交了一点费就加入了社区图书馆。
- **助记**：nomin（名）+ al → 名义上的

### 5. **palpable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可感知的；明显的；摸得着的
- **英文释义**：able to be touched or felt; easily perceived
- **例句**：
  - Tension in the room was almost palpable before the vote.
  - 投票前室内的紧张几乎触手可及。
  - There was a palpable sense of relief when the storm passed.
  - 暴风雨过去后，人们明显松了一口气。
- **助记**：palp（摸）+ able → 可触摸感知的 → 明显的

### 6. **quibble** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：吹毛求疵；琐碎的反对意见
- **英文释义**：to argue about small matters; a petty objection
- **例句**：
  - Let’s not quibble over a few dollars and close the deal.
  - 别为几块钱斤斤计较，把生意敲定吧。
  - His speech was full of quibbles rather than clear proposals.
  - 他的发言满是挑刺而非明确建议。
- **助记**：可能来自拉丁语 quibus（哪些）→ 纠缠细节 → 吹毛求疵

### 7. **rancor** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：深仇；积怨；强烈的怨恨
- **英文释义**：bitterness or resentfulness lasting a long time
- **例句**：
  - Years of unfair treatment left deep rancor among workers.
  - 多年不公待遇在工人心中留下深仇。
  - The debate was polite, but rancor lingered beneath the surface.
  - 辩论表面客气，底下却积怨未消。
- **助记**：ranc（腐臭）→ 心中发臭的恨 → 积怨

### 8. **scoff** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：嘲笑；讥讽；狼吞虎咽（与 at/down 搭配）
- **英文释义**：to speak derisively; to eat greedily (scoff down)
- **例句**：
  - Critics scoffed at the idea that robots could replace teachers.
  - 批评者嘲笑机器人能取代教师的想法。
  - He scoffed at her suggestion without hearing the details.
  - 他没听细节就嘲笑她的建议。
- **助记**：拟声 → 嗤之以鼻的笑声 → 嘲笑

### 9. **tether** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：拴住；约束；（拴动物的）绳
- **英文释义**：to tie with a rope; a rope limiting movement
- **例句**：
  - The dog was tethered to a post outside the shop.
  - 狗被拴在店外的柱子上。
  - Budget cuts tethered the agency’s ability to hire new staff.
  - 预算削减限制了机构招聘新员工的能力。
- **助记**：与 tie（绑）相关 → 拴住

### 10. **thrive** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：繁荣；茁壮成长；蓬勃发展
- **英文释义**：to grow or develop vigorously; to prosper
- **例句**：
  - Small businesses thrive when regulations are clear and fair.
  - 法规清晰公平时小企业容易兴旺。
  - Children thrive in stable, loving environments.
  - 孩子在稳定有爱的环境中茁壮成长。
- **助记**：thrive 联想 strive（努力）→ 努力后兴旺 → 繁荣

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ulterior** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：隐秘的；别有用心的；进一步的（ulterior motive）
- **英文释义**：existing beyond what is obvious; hidden and often dishonest
- **例句**：
  - She suspected he had an ulterior motive for offering free help.
  - 她怀疑他免费帮忙别有用心。
  - The contract contained ulterior clauses that favored the seller.
  - 合同含有有利于卖方的隐秘条款。
- **助记**：ulter（更远）→ 藏在更远处 → 隐秘动机

### 12. **vacillate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：犹豫；摇摆不定；拿不定主意
- **英文释义**：to waver between different opinions or actions
- **例句**：
  - Leaders who vacillate in a crisis lose public confidence.
  - 危机中摇摆不定的领导者会失去公众信任。
  - She vacillated between studying abroad and staying home.
  - 她在出国留学与留在国内之间犹豫不决。
- **助记**：vacill（摇摆）→ 摇摆不定

### 13. **wane** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：减弱；衰落；（月）亏
- **英文释义**：to decrease in strength or intensity; (of the moon) to shrink
- **例句**：
  - Public interest began to wane after the scandal faded from headlines.
  - 丑闻淡出头条后公众兴趣开始减弱。
  - The moon wanes after the full moon phase.
  - 满月之后月亮开始亏缺。
- **助记**：与 wax（盈）相对 → 亏缺/减弱

### 14. **yoke** `高一` n./v.

- **中文释义**：轭；束缚；使结合
- **英文释义**：a wooden crossbar for oxen; to join or oppress
- **例句**：
  - The nation threw off the yoke of colonial rule.
  - 该国摆脱了殖民统治的枷锁。
  - Tradition can yoke young people to expectations they reject.
  - 传统可能把年轻人绑在他们不认同的期望上。
- **助记**：牛轭 → 束缚

### 15. **zenith** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：顶点；鼎盛时期；天顶
- **英文释义**：the highest point; the time of greatest success
- **例句**：
  - The empire reached its zenith in the fifteenth century.
  - 该帝国在十五世纪达到鼎盛。
  - At the zenith of her career, she could choose any role.
  - 事业巅峰时，她可以任选角色。
- **助记**：阿拉伯语 samt arras（天顶路径）→ zenith 天顶/顶峰
