## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **affable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：和蔼可亲的；友善健谈的
- **英文释义**：friendly, good-natured, and easy to talk to
- **例句**：
  - The host was affable and made every guest feel welcome.
  - 主人和蔼可亲，让每位客人都感到受欢迎。
  - An affable smile helped her win trust in the interview.
  - 亲切的微笑帮助她在面试中赢得信任。
- **助记**：af（向）+ fabl（说）→ 好说话的 → 和蔼的

### 2. **brevity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：简洁；短暂
- **英文释义**：shortness of time; concise expression
- **例句**：
  - Brevity is valued in emergency announcements.
  - 紧急广播以简明为要。
  - The brevity of childhood makes parents cherish every moment.
  - 童年短暂，父母更珍惜每一刻。
- **助记**：brev（短）+ ity → 简短

### 3. **cascade** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：小瀑布；大量倾泻；连锁反应
- **英文释义**：a waterfall or series of small falls; to fall in a shower
- **例句**：
  - Water formed a cascade down the rocky cliff.
  - 水流在岩壁上形成小瀑布般倾泻而下。
  - One error cascaded into a system-wide failure.
  - 一个错误引发了连锁的系统故障。
- **助记**：cad（落下）→ 层层落下 → 瀑布/连锁

### 4. **docile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：温顺的；易驯服的；听话的
- **英文释义**：ready to accept control or instruction; submissive
- **例句**：
  - The puppy was docile and easy to train.
  - 小狗温顺，容易训练。
  - A docile workforce may hesitate to demand better pay.
  - 温顺的劳动力可能不敢要求更高工资。
- **助记**：doc（教）→ 易教导的 → 温顺的

### 5. **eclipse** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：日食；月食；使黯然失色
- **英文释义**：blocking of light by a body; to surpass or overshadow
- **例句**：
  - Thousands gathered to watch the solar eclipse.
  - 数千人聚集观看日食。
  - Her performance eclipsed all rivals on stage.
  - 她的表演使台上所有对手都黯然失色。
- **助记**：ec（出）+ lips（离开）→ 光被带走 → 食/遮蔽

### 6. **flout** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：公然藐视；嘲弄（规则）
- **英文释义**：to openly disregard a rule or law; to mock
- **例句**：
  - Drivers who flout speed limits endanger everyone on the road.
  - 藐视限速的司机会危及路上每一个人。
  - He flouted tradition by wearing sneakers to the ceremony.
  - 他穿运动鞋参加仪式，公然藐视传统。
- **助记**：联想 float 漂浮 → 不把规矩“放在眼里” → 藐视

### 7. **grapple** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：扭打；努力解决； grapple with 与……搏斗
- **英文释义**：to wrestle; to struggle to deal with a difficult problem
- **例句**：
  - The nation must grapple with rising inequality.
  - 国家必须努力解决日益严重的不平等。
  - The security guard grappled with the intruder near the gate.
  - 保安在大门附近与闯入者扭打。
- **助记**：grab（抓）→ 扭住不放 → 扭打/努力应对

### 8. **hindrance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：障碍；阻碍；妨碍者
- **英文释义**：a thing that hinders; an obstacle
- **例句**：
  - Lack of funding was a major hindrance to research.
  - 资金不足是研究的主要障碍。
  - Heavy luggage proved a hindrance during the hike.
  - 沉重的行李在徒步中成了累赘。
- **助记**：hind（后）+ rance → 拖在后面 → 阻碍

### 9. **immutable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可改变的；永恒的
- **英文释义**：unchanging over time; unable to be altered
- **例句**：
  - Physical laws appear immutable across the universe.
  - 物理定律在宇宙中似乎不可改变。
  - Some traditions remain immutable despite social change.
  - 尽管社会变迁，有些传统仍不可动摇。
- **助记**：im（不）+ mut（变）+ able → 不可变的

### 10. **jostle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：推挤；争抢；与……竞争
- **英文释义**：to push or bump against; to compete for position
- **例句**：
  - Commuters jostled for space on the crowded train.
  - 通勤者在拥挤的火车上争抢空间。
  - Photographers jostled to get a better angle of the star.
  - 摄影师们推挤着寻找更好的拍摄角度。
- **助记**：just（接近）→ 挤在一起 → 推挤

### 高一词汇

### 11. **knell** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：丧钟；不祥之兆
- **英文释义**：the sound of a bell, especially when rung for a death or funeral
- **例句**：
  - The scandal sounded the knell of his political career.
  - 丑闻敲响了他政治生涯的丧钟。
  - Church bells tolled a solemn knell across the valley.
  - 教堂钟声在山谷中敲响庄重的丧钟。
- **助记**：与 knoll（小丘）谐音联想 → 钟声传过山丘 → 丧钟

### 12. **lexicon** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：词汇；专业词汇表；词典
- **英文释义**：the vocabulary of a language or subject; a dictionary
- **例句**：
  - Medical students must master a vast technical lexicon.
  - 医学生必须掌握大量技术词汇。
  - The book expands the lexicon of modern urban slang.
  - 该书扩展了现代城市俚语的词汇库。
- **助记**：lexis（词）→ 词汇总集

### 13. **maudlin** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：伤感的；多愁善感的；（尤指酒后）易落泪的
- **英文释义**：sentimental in a weak or tearful way, often from self-pity
- **例句**：
  - The film’s maudlin ending disappointed critics who expected subtlety.
  - 影片滥情的结局让期待含蓄的评论家失望。
  - He became maudlin after his third glass of wine.
  - 喝完第三杯酒后他变得多愁善感。
- **助记**：来自 Mary Magdalene 流泪形象 → 伤感落泪

### 14. **nonplussed** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：困惑的；不知所措的
- **英文释义**：so surprised and confused that one does not know how to react
- **例句**：
  - The unexpected question left the candidate completely nonplussed.
  - 这个意外问题让候选人完全不知所措。
  - She stood nonplussed in front of the locked door without her key.
  - 没带钥匙的她站在锁着的门前一脸茫然。
- **助记**：non（不）+ plus（更多办法）→ 再也想不出办法 → 困惑

### 15. **opine** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：发表意见；认为（正式）
- **英文释义**：to express an opinion in a formal or pompous way
- **例句**：
  - Experts opined that the market would stabilize next quarter.
  - 专家认为市场将在下季度企稳。
  - He opined at length during the panel discussion.
  - 他在小组讨论中长篇大论地发表意见。
- **助记**：opin（意见）→ 发表意见
