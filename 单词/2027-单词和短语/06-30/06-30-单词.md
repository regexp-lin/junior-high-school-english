## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **ambiguous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：模糊的；有歧义的；不明确的
- **英文释义**：open to more than one interpretation; having a double meaning
- **例句**：
  - The contract contained several ambiguous clauses that needed clarification.
  - 合同中有几条含糊不清的条款需要澄清。
- **助记**：ambi（两个）+ guous → 有两种理解的 → 模糊的/有歧义的

### 2. **barricade** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 路障；屏障 v. 设路障；阻挡
- **英文释义**：n. an improvised barrier erected across a street; v. to block or defend with a barricade
- **例句**：
  - The protesters built barricades across the main road to block traffic.
  - 抗议者在主干道上设置路障阻断了交通。
- **助记**：barr（障碍）+ icade → 设置障碍物 → 路障

### 3. **cherish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：珍爱；怀有；珍藏
- **英文释义**：to protect and care for lovingly; to hold dear
- **例句**：
  - She cherishes the memories of her childhood in the countryside.
  - 她珍藏着在乡下度过的童年记忆。
- **助记**：cher（亲爱的）+ ish → 把某物当作亲爱的 → 珍爱

### 4. **dormant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：休眠的；潜伏的；静止的
- **英文释义**：temporarily inactive; in a state of rest or inactivity
- **例句**：
  - The volcano has been dormant for over a century but could erupt again.
  - 这座火山已经休眠了一个多世纪，但可能会再次喷发。
- **助记**：dorm（睡觉）+ ant → 在睡觉的 → 休眠的

### 5. **expedite** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加速；促进；加快
- **英文释义**：to make an action or process happen sooner or be accomplished more quickly
- **例句**：
  - We hired extra workers to expedite the construction of the new hospital.
  - 我们雇了额外的工人来加速新医院的建设。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ped（脚）+ ite → 把脚解放出来加快走 → 加速

### 6. **fervent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：热烈的；热情的；强烈的
- **英文释义**：having or displaying a passionate intensity
- **例句**：
  - The fervent supporters cheered loudly throughout the entire match.
  - 热情的支持者在整场比赛中大声欢呼。
- **助记**：ferv（热/沸腾）+ ent → 像沸腾一样热烈 → 热烈的

### 7. **havoc** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：大破坏；浩劫；混乱
- **英文释义**：widespread destruction; great confusion or disorder
- **例句**：
  - The hurricane wreaked havoc on the coastal towns, destroying hundreds of homes.
  - 飓风给沿海城镇造成了巨大破坏，摧毁了数百栋房屋。
- **助记**：havoc → 联想"毁坏客"→ 造成巨大毁坏 → 浩劫

### 8. **jeopardize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：危及；损害；使处于危险中
- **英文释义**：to put something into a situation in which there is a danger of loss, harm, or failure
- **例句**：
  - His reckless behavior could jeopardize the entire team's chances of winning.
  - 他的鲁莽行为可能危及整个团队获胜的机会。
- **助记**：jeopard（危险）+ ize → 使处于危险中 → 危及

### 9. **nonchalant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：若无其事的；漫不经心的；淡定的
- **英文释义**：feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety or interest
- **例句**：
  - He tried to appear nonchalant, but his trembling hands betrayed his nervousness.
  - 他试图表现得若无其事，但颤抖的双手暴露了他的紧张。
- **助记**：non（不）+ chalant（关心）→ 不关心的 → 漫不经心的

### 10. **rebuke** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 斥责；指责 n. 斥责；非难
- **英文释义**：v. to express sharp disapproval or criticism; n. a sharp expression of disapproval
- **例句**：
  - The manager rebuked the employee for repeatedly arriving late to work.
  - 经理因员工多次迟到而斥责了他。
- **助记**：re（回）+ buke（打）→ 打回去 → 斥责

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acrimonious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：尖刻的；辛辣的；充满怨恨的
- **英文释义**：angry and bitter in manner or speech
- **例句**：
  - The acrimonious divorce proceedings dragged on for months.
  - 充满怨恨的离婚诉讼拖了好几个月。
- **助记**：acri（尖锐）+ monious → 尖锐刻薄的 → 尖刻的

### 12. **disparate** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：迥然不同的；完全不同的；不相干的
- **英文释义**：essentially different in kind; not allowing comparison
- **例句**：
  - The committee brought together disparate viewpoints to form a comprehensive plan.
  - 委员会汇集了截然不同的观点来制定一个全面的计划。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ par（相等）+ ate → 不相等的 → 迥然不同的

### 13. **impecunious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：身无分文的；贫穷的
- **英文释义**：having little or no money
- **例句**：
  - The impecunious artist relied on the generosity of patrons to survive.
  - 这位身无分文的艺术家依靠赞助人的慷慨来维持生活。
- **助记**：im（没有）+ pecun（钱）+ ious → 没有钱的 → 身无分文的

### 14. **perspicacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：敏锐的；有洞察力的；目光如炬的
- **英文释义**：having a ready insight into and understanding of things
- **例句**：
  - The perspicacious detective noticed a crucial detail that everyone else had missed.
  - 敏锐的侦探注意到了一个其他人都忽略的关键细节。
- **助记**：per（透过）+ spic（看）+ acious → 能看透的 → 有洞察力的

### 15. **sycophant** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：拍马屁的人；谄媚者；阿谀奉承者
- **英文释义**：a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important in order to gain advantage
- **例句**：
  - The boss was surrounded by sycophants who agreed with everything he said.
  - 老板周围都是对他言听计从的谄媚者。
- **助记**：syco（无花果）+ phant（显示）→ 古希腊指告发无花果走私者以讨好当权者 → 谄媚者
