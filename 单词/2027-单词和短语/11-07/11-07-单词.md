## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **articulate** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：清晰表达的；善于表达的；清楚地说
- **英文释义**：having the ability to speak fluently and coherently; to express clearly
- **例句**：
  - She is an articulate speaker who can explain complex ideas simply.
  - 她是一位善于表达的演讲者，能把复杂的概念解释得很简单。
  - He articulated his concerns about the project during the meeting.
  - 他在会上清楚地表达了对项目的担忧。
- **助记**：artic（关节）+ ulate → 像关节一样灵活表达 → 善于表达的

### 2. **eradicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：根除；消灭；杜绝
- **英文释义**：to destroy completely; to put an end to
- **例句**：
  - Scientists are working to eradicate malaria in tropical regions.
  - 科学家们正在努力在热带地区根除疟疾。
  - The campaign aims to eradicate poverty in the most vulnerable communities.
  - 该活动旨在消除最脆弱社区的贫困。
- **助记**：e（出）+ radic（根）+ ate → 连根拔出 → 根除

### 3. **imminent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：即将发生的；迫近的
- **英文释义**：about to happen; impending
- **例句**：
  - The dark clouds warned of an imminent storm.
  - 乌云预示着一场暴风雨即将来临。
  - The company faced imminent bankruptcy without additional funding.
  - 没有额外资金，公司面临迫在眉睫的破产。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ min（突出）+ ent → 突出到眼前 → 即将发生的

### 4. **prudent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：谨慎的；明智的；精打细算的
- **英文释义**：acting with or showing care and thought for the future
- **例句**：
  - It would be prudent to save some money for unexpected expenses.
  - 为意外开支存些钱是明智的。
  - A prudent investor diversifies their portfolio to reduce risk.
  - 谨慎的投资者会分散投资组合以降低风险。
- **助记**：prud（明智）+ ent → 明智的 → 谨慎的

### 5. **deteriorate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：恶化；变质；退化
- **英文释义**：to become progressively worse
- **例句**：
  - The building's condition has deteriorated significantly over the decades.
  - 这栋建筑的状况几十年来严重恶化。
  - Relations between the two countries deteriorated after the trade dispute.
  - 贸易争端后两国关系恶化了。
- **助记**：deterior（更差）+ ate → 变得更差 → 恶化

### 6. **exemplary** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：模范的；典范的；可作为榜样的
- **英文释义**：serving as a desirable model; very good
- **例句**：
  - Her exemplary behavior earned her the Student of the Year award.
  - 她模范的表现为她赢得了年度学生奖。
  - The teacher praised his exemplary dedication to community service.
  - 老师表扬了他在社区服务方面的模范奉献精神。
- **助记**：exemplar（榜样）+ y → 像榜样一样的 → 模范的

### 7. **substantiate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证实；证明；使有根据
- **英文释义**：to provide evidence to support or prove the truth of
- **例句**：
  - You need to substantiate your claims with reliable evidence.
  - 你需要用可靠的证据来证实你的说法。
  - The scientist was unable to substantiate his theory with experimental data.
  - 科学家无法用实验数据证实他的理论。
- **助记**：sub（下面）+ stanti（站立）+ ate → 使论点站住脚 → 证实

### 8. **whimsical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：古怪的；异想天开的；多变的
- **英文释义**：playfully quaint or fanciful; acting in an unpredictable way
- **例句**：
  - The artist's whimsical illustrations delighted children and adults alike.
  - 这位艺术家异想天开的插画令孩子和大人都喜欢。
  - His whimsical decision to quit his job surprised everyone.
  - 他心血来潮辞职的决定让所有人都感到惊讶。
- **助记**：whim（突发奇想）+ sical → 充满奇想的 → 异想天开的

### 9. **mitigate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和；使缓解
- **英文释义**：to make less severe, serious, or painful
- **例句**：
  - Planting trees can mitigate the effects of climate change.
  - 种树可以减轻气候变化的影响。
  - The company took steps to mitigate the risk of data breaches.
  - 公司采取措施来降低数据泄露的风险。
- **助记**：mit（温和）+ igate → 使变温和 → 减轻

### 10. **sporadic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：零星的；偶发的；不定期的
- **英文释义**：occurring at irregular intervals or only in a few places
- **例句**：
  - There were sporadic outbreaks of violence in the region.
  - 该地区零星发生了暴力事件。
  - Sporadic rainfall provided little relief from the drought.
  - 零星的降雨对缓解旱情几乎没有帮助。
- **助记**：sporad（分散）+ ic → 分散发生的 → 零星的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **antediluvian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：远古的；极其老旧的；过时的
- **英文释义**：of or belonging to the time before the biblical Flood; ridiculously old-fashioned
- **例句**：
  - His antediluvian views on technology make him resistant to using smartphones.
  - 他对技术的老旧观念让他抗拒使用智能手机。
  - The antediluvian computer system desperately needs upgrading.
  - 这套极其老旧的电脑系统迫切需要升级。
- **助记**：ante（之前）+ diluvian（大洪水）→ 大洪水之前的 → 远古的

### 12. **ebullient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的
- **英文释义**：cheerful and full of energy
- **例句**：
  - The ebullient crowd cheered wildly as the team scored the winning goal.
  - 热情洋溢的人群在球队打进制胜球时疯狂欢呼。
  - Her ebullient personality makes her the life of every party.
  - 她热情洋溢的性格使她成为每次聚会的焦点。
- **助记**：e（出）+ bull（沸腾）+ ient → 向外沸腾 → 热情洋溢的

### 13. **perfunctory** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：敷衍的；走过场的；马虎的
- **英文释义**：carried out with a minimum of effort or reflection
- **例句**：
  - He gave a perfunctory nod without really listening to the explanation.
  - 他敷衍地点了点头，并没有真正在听解释。
  - The inspection was perfunctory and failed to identify the safety hazards.
  - 这次检查只是走过场，未能发现安全隐患。
- **助记**：per（通过）+ funct（执行）+ ory → 只是通过执行而已 → 敷衍的

### 14. **plethora** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：过多；大量；过剩
- **英文释义**：a large or excessive amount of something
- **例句**：
  - The internet offers a plethora of information on virtually every topic.
  - 互联网提供了几乎每个话题的大量信息。
  - There is a plethora of restaurants to choose from in this neighborhood.
  - 这个街区有大量餐厅可供选择。
- **助记**：pleth（充满）+ ora → 充满的状态 → 过多

### 15. **zeitgeist** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：时代精神；时代特征
- **英文释义**：the defining spirit or mood of a particular period of history
- **例句**：
  - The film perfectly captures the zeitgeist of the 1960s counterculture movement.
  - 这部电影完美捕捉了1960年代反文化运动的时代精神。
  - Social media has become a reflection of the contemporary zeitgeist.
  - 社交媒体已成为当代时代精神的反映。
- **助记**：德语 Zeit（时间）+ Geist（精神）→ 时间的精神 → 时代精神
