## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **inveigle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：诱骗；哄骗；巧言引诱
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone to do something by means of deception or flattery
- **例句**：
  - The swindler inveigled the elderly couple into investing their life savings.
  - 骗子诱骗这对老夫妇投入了他们的毕生积蓄。
  - She inveigled her way into the exclusive event by pretending to be a journalist.
  - 她假装成记者巧妙地混进了那个高端活动。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ veigle（蒙蔽）→ 蒙蔽着引入 → 诱骗

### 2. **soporific** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：催眠的；令人昏昏欲睡的；安眠药
- **英文释义**：tending to induce drowsiness or sleep; a drug that induces sleep
- **例句**：
  - The professor's soporific lecture sent half the students to sleep.
  - 教授催眠般的讲座让一半学生睡着了。
  - The warm milk acted as a natural soporific, helping her fall asleep quickly.
  - 温牛奶充当了天然的安眠剂，帮助她快速入睡。
- **助记**：sopor（睡眠）+ ific → 引起睡眠的 → 催眠的

### 3. **oscillate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：摆动；振荡；犹豫不决
- **英文释义**：to move or swing back and forth at a regular speed; to waver between opinions
- **例句**：
  - The pendulum oscillated steadily, marking the passage of each second.
  - 钟摆稳定地摆动，标记着每一秒的流逝。
  - She oscillated between accepting the promotion and staying in her current role.
  - 她在接受晋升和留在当前职位之间犹豫不决。
- **助记**：oscill（摆动）+ ate → 摆动 → 振荡/犹豫

### 4. **veracious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：诚实的；真实的；准确的
- **英文释义**：speaking or representing the truth; truthful
- **例句**：
  - The journalist was known for her veracious reporting, never embellishing the facts.
  - 这位记者以真实报道著称，从不修饰事实。
  - A veracious account of the events was essential for the historical record.
  - 对事件的真实叙述对历史记录至关重要。
- **助记**：verac（真实）+ ious → 真实的 → 诚实的

### 5. **obsequious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：谄媚的；奉承的；过分顺从的
- **英文释义**：obedient or attentive to an excessive or servile degree
- **例句**：
  - The obsequious assistant agreed with everything the boss said, no matter how unreasonable.
  - 谄媚的助手对老板说的一切都表示同意，无论多么不合理。
  - His obsequious behavior toward the wealthy donor made his colleagues uncomfortable.
  - 他对富有捐赠者的奉承行为让同事们感到不舒服。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ sequi（跟随）+ ous → 一直跟着 → 谄媚的

### 6. **permeate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：渗透；弥漫；遍及
- **英文释义**：to spread throughout; to pass through the pores of
- **例句**：
  - The smell of freshly brewed coffee permeated the entire house.
  - 新煮咖啡的香味弥漫了整个房子。
  - A sense of optimism permeated the team after their unexpected victory.
  - 出人意料的胜利后，一种乐观情绪弥漫了整个团队。
- **助记**：per（穿过）+ me（走）+ ate → 穿过去 → 渗透

### 7. **recalcitrant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不服从的；顽抗的；桀骜不驯的
- **英文释义**：having an obstinately uncooperative attitude
- **例句**：
  - The recalcitrant student refused to follow the classroom rules despite multiple warnings.
  - 尽管多次警告，这个桀骜不驯的学生仍拒绝遵守课堂规则。
  - Dealing with recalcitrant patients requires patience and firm communication skills.
  - 应对不合作的病人需要耐心和坚定的沟通技巧。
- **助记**：re（反）+ calcitrant（踢）→ 反踢 → 不服从的

### 8. **inscrutable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：高深莫测的；难以理解的；不可捉摸的
- **英文释义**：impossible to understand or interpret
- **例句**：
  - The ancient manuscript contained inscrutable symbols that baffled archaeologists for decades.
  - 古代手稿包含了令考古学家困惑数十年的高深莫测的符号。
  - Her inscrutable expression made it impossible to tell what she was thinking.
  - 她不可捉摸的表情让人无法判断她在想什么。
- **助记**：in（不）+ scrut（审查）+ able → 无法审查 → 高深莫测的

### 9. **fulminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：怒斥；猛烈抨击；爆发
- **英文释义**：to express vehement protest; to explode violently
- **例句**：
  - The senator fulminated against the proposed tax increase during the debate.
  - 参议员在辩论中猛烈抨击了拟议的增税方案。
  - Environmental activists fulminated over the government's inaction on climate change.
  - 环保活动人士对政府在气候变化上的不作为怒斥。
- **助记**：fulmin（雷电）+ ate → 像雷电一样 → 怒斥/爆发

### 10. **magnanimous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；慷慨的；高尚的
- **英文释义**：very generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or someone less powerful
- **例句**：
  - The magnanimous winner congratulated her opponent and praised their performance.
  - 宽宏大量的获胜者恭喜了对手并赞扬了他们的表现。
  - It was magnanimous of him to forgive those who had wronged him.
  - 他原谅了那些伤害过他的人，这很高尚。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 心灵大的 → 宽宏大量的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **apophenia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：幻想性错觉；在无关事物中感知到联系
- **英文释义**：the tendency to perceive meaningful connections between unrelated things
- **例句**：
  - Seeing faces in clouds is a common example of apophenia.
  - 在云中看到人脸是幻想性错觉的常见例子。
  - Conspiracy theorists often exhibit apophenia, linking unrelated events into grand narratives.
  - 阴谋论者经常表现出幻想性错觉，将无关的事件联系成宏大的叙事。
- **助记**：apo（远离）+ phen（显现）+ ia → 显现出远离真相的联系 → 幻想性错觉

### 12. **sesquipedalian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：（词语）冗长的；好用长词的
- **英文释义**：(of a word) polysyllabic; long; characterized by long words
- **例句**：
  - The professor's sesquipedalian vocabulary intimidated first-year students.
  - 教授好用长词的习惯让一年级学生望而生畏。
  - His sesquipedalian writing style, while impressive, made his essays hard to read.
  - 他冗长的写作风格虽然令人印象深刻，却使他的文章难以阅读。
- **助记**：sesqui（一个半）+ ped（脚）+ alian → 一个半脚长 → 冗长的

### 13. **eidetic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：（记忆）过目不忘的；直觉映像的
- **英文释义**：relating to or denoting mental images having unusual vividness and detail, as if actually visible
- **例句**：
  - People with eidetic memory can recall images with extraordinary precision after seeing them only briefly.
  - 拥有过目不忘记忆力的人只需短暂一看就能以非凡的精确度回忆图像。
  - The detective's eidetic recall of faces helped him identify the suspect from a crowd.
  - 侦探对面孔过目不忘的记忆力帮助他从人群中辨认出了嫌疑人。
- **助记**：eidet（看见）+ ic → 像亲眼看见一样 → 过目不忘的

### 14. **numinous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：超自然的；令人敬畏的；有神圣感的
- **英文释义**：having a strong religious or spiritual quality; indicating the presence of a divinity
- **例句**：
  - Standing in the ancient cathedral, she felt a numinous presence that filled her with awe.
  - 站在古老的大教堂里，她感受到一种令人敬畏的神圣存在。
  - The numinous beauty of the starlit desert sky left the travelers speechless.
  - 星光照耀下沙漠天空的神圣之美让旅行者们说不出话来。
- **助记**：numin（神灵）+ ous → 有神灵的 → 超自然的/神圣的

### 15. **syzygy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：朔望；天体排列成一直线
- **英文释义**：a conjunction or opposition, especially of the moon with the sun; an alignment of three celestial bodies
- **例句**：
  - A solar eclipse occurs during syzygy, when the moon passes directly between the Earth and the Sun.
  - 日食发生在朔望时期，即月球直接从地球和太阳之间穿过。
  - The rare syzygy of Jupiter, Saturn, and Earth created a spectacular sight in the night sky.
  - 木星、土星和地球罕见的天体排列在夜空中创造了壮观的景象。
- **助记**：syz（一起）+ ygy（轭）→ 天体被套在一起 → 天体排列成直线
