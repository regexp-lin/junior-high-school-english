## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **arson** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：纵火；放火罪
- **英文释义**：the criminal act of deliberately setting fire to property
- **例句**：
  - The police arrested a man on suspicion of arson.
  - 警方逮捕了一名涉嫌纵火的男子。
- **助记**：ars（燃烧）+ on → 燃烧的行为 → 纵火

### 2. **commute** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 通勤；乘车上下班 n. 上下班路程
- **英文释义**：v. to travel some distance between one's home and place of work regularly; n. the journey
- **例句**：
  - She commutes to work by train every day.
  - 她每天乘火车通勤。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ mute（改变位置）→ 每天来回改变位置 → 通勤

### 3. **dialect** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：方言；土话
- **英文释义**：a particular form of a language which is specific to a region or social group
- **例句**：
  - People in this region speak a unique dialect.
  - 这个地区的人说一种独特的方言。
- **助记**：dia（穿过/之间）+ lect（说话）→ 在某些人之间说的话 → 方言

### 4. **exile** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 流亡；被放逐者 v. 流放；放逐
- **英文释义**：n. the state of being barred from one's native country; v. to expel from a country
- **例句**：
  - The writer spent ten years in exile before returning home.
  - 这位作家流亡了十年后才回国。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ile → 被赶出去的 → 流放

### 5. **grotesque** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：奇形怪状的；荒诞的；丑陋的
- **英文释义**：comically or repulsively ugly or distorted
- **例句**：
  - The movie featured grotesque monsters that scared the audience.
  - 这部电影中奇形怪状的怪物吓到了观众。
- **助记**：grot（洞穴）+ esque → 洞穴里扭曲的壁画 → 奇形怪状的

### 6. **intrude** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：闯入；侵入；打扰
- **英文释义**：to put oneself deliberately into a place or situation where one is unwelcome
- **例句**：
  - I'm sorry to intrude, but I have an urgent message.
  - 抱歉打扰了，但我有一条紧急消息。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ trude（推）→ 硬推着进去 → 闯入

### 7. **merchandise** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：商品；货物
- **英文释义**：goods to be bought and sold
- **例句**：
  - The store displays its merchandise in attractive windows.
  - 这家商店在漂亮的橱窗里展示商品。
- **助记**：merchant（商人）+ ise → 商人卖的东西 → 商品

### 8. **peril** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：危险；险境
- **英文释义**：serious and immediate danger
- **例句**：
  - The climbers were in grave peril during the storm.
  - 暴风雨中登山者处于严重危险之中。
- **助记**：peril 谐音"怕了"→ 让人害怕的 → 危险

### 9. **renounce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：放弃；宣布放弃；声明断绝
- **英文释义**：to formally declare one's abandonment of a claim, right, or possession
- **例句**：
  - He renounced his claim to the throne.
  - 他放弃了对王位的继承权。
- **助记**：re（回）+ nounce（宣布）→ 宣布退回 → 放弃

### 10. **threshold** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：门槛；起点；临界值
- **英文释义**：a strip of wood or stone forming the bottom of a doorway; a level or point at which something begins
- **例句**：
  - The country is on the threshold of a new era.
  - 这个国家正处于新时代的门槛上。
- **助记**：thresh（打谷）+ hold → 门口铺的挡谷子的板子 → 门槛

### 高一词汇

### 11. **arbitrary** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：任意的；武断的；专横的
- **英文释义**：based on random choice or personal whim, rather than reason or system
- **例句**：
  - The decision seemed completely arbitrary and unfair.
  - 这个决定似乎完全是武断和不公平的。
- **助记**：arbit（仲裁）+ rary → 像独裁者一样自行仲裁 → 武断的

### 12. **conspicuous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：显眼的；引人注目的
- **英文释义**：standing out so as to be clearly visible; attracting notice or attention
- **例句**：
  - Her bright red dress was conspicuous in the crowd.
  - 她鲜红色的裙子在人群中非常显眼。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ spic（看）+ uous → 大家都能看到的 → 显眼的

### 13. **dissonance** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：不和谐；不协调；刺耳的声音
- **英文释义**：a lack of harmony; a tension resulting from conflicting elements
- **例句**：
  - There is a dissonance between his words and his actions.
  - 他的言行之间存在不协调。
- **助记**：dis（不）+ son（声音）+ ance → 不和谐的声音 → 不协调

### 14. **proliferate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：激增；大量繁殖；扩散
- **英文释义**：to increase rapidly in numbers; to reproduce or multiply
- **例句**：
  - Social media platforms have proliferated in recent years.
  - 近年来社交媒体平台大量涌现。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lifer（生命）+ ate → 生命向前不断繁衍 → 激增

### 15. **vindicate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：证明……正确；为……平反；辩护
- **英文释义**：to clear someone of blame or suspicion; to show that something is justified
- **例句**：
  - The new evidence vindicated the defendant.
  - 新证据证明被告是无辜的。
- **助记**：vin（赢）+ dic（说）+ ate → 说赢了官司 → 证明正确/平反
