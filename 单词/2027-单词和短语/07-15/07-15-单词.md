## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **apprehend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：逮捕；领会；担忧
- **英文释义**：to arrest someone; to understand or perceive; to anticipate with anxiety
- **例句**：
  - The suspect was apprehended by police just hours after the robbery.
  - 嫌疑人在抢劫发生仅几小时后就被警方逮捕了。
- **助记**：ap（向）+ prehend（抓住）→ 抓住 → 逮捕/领会

### 2. **coalesce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；联合；融合
- **英文释义**：to come together and form one mass or whole
- **例句**：
  - Several small protest groups coalesced into a powerful political movement.
  - 几个小型抗议团体合并成了一个强大的政治运动。
- **助记**：co（一起）+ alesce（生长）→ 一起生长/融合 → 合并

### 3. **discrepancy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：差异；不一致；出入
- **英文释义**：a lack of compatibility or similarity between two or more facts
- **例句**：
  - The auditor found a significant discrepancy between the reported income and actual earnings.
  - 审计员发现报告的收入和实际收入之间存在重大差异。
- **助记**：dis（不）+ crep（声音/一致）+ ancy → 声音不一致 → 差异

### 4. **endorse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：支持；赞同；签署；代言
- **英文释义**：to declare one's public approval or support of; to sign the back of a check
- **例句**：
  - The famous athlete was paid millions to endorse the new sports brand.
  - 这位著名运动员获得数百万报酬为新运动品牌代言。
- **助记**：en（使）+ dorse（背面）→ 在背面签名表示认可 → 支持/签署

### 5. **hamper** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；束缚
- **英文释义**：to hinder or impede the movement or progress of
- **例句**：
  - The lack of funding has seriously hampered the research team's progress.
  - 资金不足严重阻碍了研究团队的进展。
- **助记**：hamper → 联想"绊脚的篮子"→ 绊住脚步 → 阻碍

### 6. **incessant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不断的；不停的；持续的
- **英文释义**：continuing without pause or interruption
- **例句**：
  - The incessant noise from the construction site kept the residents awake all night.
  - 建筑工地持续不断的噪音让居民整夜无法入睡。
- **助记**：in（不）+ cess（停止）+ ant → 不停止的 → 不断的

### 7. **meticulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；细致的；精确的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - The surgeon is meticulous in her preparation for every operation.
  - 这位外科医生对每次手术的准备都一丝不苟。
- **助记**：met（恐惧）+ iculous → 害怕出错 → 一丝不苟的

### 8. **prevalent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：流行的；普遍的；盛行的
- **英文释义**：widespread in a particular area at a particular time
- **例句**：
  - Cyber bullying has become increasingly prevalent among teenagers.
  - 网络欺凌在青少年中越来越普遍。
- **助记**：pre（前面）+ val（强）+ ent → 在前面很强大 → 流行的/普遍的

### 9. **solemn** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：庄严的；严肃的；郑重的
- **英文释义**：formal and dignified; not cheerful or smiling; serious
- **例句**：
  - The soldiers stood in solemn silence at the war memorial ceremony.
  - 士兵们在战争纪念仪式上庄严肃穆地站立着。
- **助记**：solemn → 联想"so + 冷 + mn"→ 如此冷肃 → 庄严的

### 10. **vindicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证明……正确；为……辩护；平反
- **英文释义**：to clear someone of blame or suspicion; to show to be right
- **例句**：
  - New evidence vindicated the man who had been wrongly imprisoned for ten years.
  - 新证据为这位被错误监禁十年的男子平了反。
- **助记**：vind（力量/声称）+ ic + ate → 用力量为自己声称 → 证明正确/辩护

### 高一词汇

### 11. **anachronistic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不合时宜的；时代错误的
- **英文释义**：belonging to a period other than that being portrayed; outdated
- **例句**：
  - The castle's medieval decorations seemed anachronistic in the modern city center.
  - 城堡的中世纪装饰在现代市中心显得不合时宜。
- **助记**：ana（向后）+ chron（时间）+ istic → 时间往后/错位 → 不合时宜的

### 12. **equanimity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：平静；镇定；泰然自若
- **英文释义**：mental calmness and composure, especially in a difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - She faced the devastating news with remarkable equanimity.
  - 她以非凡的镇定面对了这个毁灭性的消息。
- **助记**：equ（平等）+ anim（心灵）+ ity → 心灵平和 → 平静/镇定

### 13. **juxtapose** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：并列；并置；对比放置
- **英文释义**：to place close together for contrasting effect
- **例句**：
  - The artist juxtaposed images of wealth and poverty to highlight social inequality.
  - 艺术家将财富和贫困的图像并置以突出社会不平等。
- **助记**：juxta（紧挨）+ pose（放置）→ 紧挨着放 → 并列/并置

### 14. **recalcitrant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不服从的；桀骜不驯的；顽抗的
- **英文释义**：having an obstinately uncooperative attitude toward authority or discipline
- **例句**：
  - The recalcitrant student refused to follow the school's dress code.
  - 这个桀骜不驯的学生拒绝遵守学校的着装规定。
- **助记**：re（反）+ calc（踢）+ itrant → 反踢/反抗 → 不服从的

### 15. **sycophant** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：谄媚者；马屁精；溜须拍马的人
- **英文释义**：a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important in order to gain advantage
- **例句**：
  - The king was surrounded by sycophants who agreed with everything he said.
  - 国王身边围满了对他的每句话都点头称是的谄媚者。
- **助记**：syco（无花果）+ phant（展示）→ 古希腊告密者展示无花果 → 谄媚者
