## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **accrue** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：积累；自然增加；（利息等）累积
- **英文释义**：to be received by someone in regular or increasing amounts over time
- **例句**：
  - Interest will accrue on the unpaid balance at a rate of five percent per year.
  - 未偿还余额将按每年百分之五的利率累积利息。
  - Benefits accrue to employees who stay with the company for more than five years.
  - 在公司工作满五年以上的员工会累积相应福利。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ crue（增长，同 increase）→ 逐渐增长 → 积累

### 2. **alacrity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：敏捷；乐意；爽快
- **英文释义**：brisk and cheerful readiness to do something
- **例句**：
  - She accepted the challenging assignment with alacrity.
  - 她爽快地接受了这项富有挑战性的任务。
  - The volunteers responded to the appeal for help with surprising alacrity.
  - 志愿者们以出人意料的积极态度响应了求助。
- **助记**：ala（翅膀）联想“轻快”+ crity → 像飞一样快 → 敏捷/乐意

### 3. **berate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：严厉斥责；责骂
- **英文释义**：to scold or criticize someone angrily and at length
- **例句**：
  - The coach berated the team for their lack of discipline during the match.
  - 教练因球队在比赛中的纪律问题而严厉斥责他们。
  - He was berated by his boss in front of the entire office.
  - 他在全办公室同事面前被老板痛骂了一顿。
- **助记**：be（加强）+ rate（责骂/评价）→ 狠狠地骂 → 斥责

### 4. **commodious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽敞的；便利舒适的
- **英文释义**：spacious and comfortable
- **例句**：
  - They rented a commodious apartment near the university campus.
  - 他们在大学附近租了一套宽敞舒适的公寓。
  - The commodious lobby can accommodate over two hundred guests at once.
  - 宽敞的大厅一次可容纳两百多名宾客。
- **助记**：commod（舒适/便利）+ ious → 空间舒适 → 宽敞的

### 5. **construe** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：理解；解释；把……理解为
- **英文释义**：to interpret a word or action in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - The court must construe the contract according to its plain language.
  - 法院必须根据合同字面意思进行解释。
  - His silence could be construed as agreement, though he never said yes.
  - 他的沉默可被理解为同意，尽管他从未说过“是”。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ strue（建造/堆积）→ 把意义“搭”起来 → 解释

### 6. **corrosive** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：腐蚀性的；逐渐起破坏作用的；（喻）刻薄伤人的
- **英文释义**：gradually destructive; having the quality of corroding metal or eating away at something
- **例句**：
  - Salt water has a corrosive effect on untreated metal surfaces.
  - 盐水对未处理的金属表面具有腐蚀作用。
  - Years of resentment had a corrosive impact on their friendship.
  - 多年的怨恨逐渐侵蚀了他们的友谊。
- **助记**：cor（完全）+ ros（咬）+ ive → 不断咬蚀 → 腐蚀性的

### 7. **desiccate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使完全干燥；使脱水；（喻）使失去活力
- **英文释义**：to dry out completely; to remove moisture from
- **例句**：
  - The hot wind desiccated the soil within a few days.
  - 热风几天内就把土壤吹干了。
  - Overwork can desiccate your creativity if you never take a break.
  - 若从不休息，过度工作会使你的创造力枯竭。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ sicc（干）+ ate → 弄干 → 使干燥

### 8. **discordant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不一致的；刺耳的；冲突的
- **英文释义**：disagreeing or incongruous; harsh-sounding
- **例句**：
  - The committee members held discordant views on how to allocate the budget.
  - 委员们在如何分配预算上意见相左。
  - The singer's voice sounded discordant against the soft piano accompaniment.
  - 在柔和的钢琴伴奏下，那名歌手的嗓音显得刺耳。
- **助记**：dis（分离）+ cord（心/和谐）+ ant → 不和谐 → 不一致的

### 9. **disabuse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使醒悟；使打消错误想法
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone that an idea or belief is mistaken
- **例句**：
  - I must disabuse you of the notion that success comes without hard work.
  - 我必须让你打消“成功无需努力”的念头。
  - The documentary disabused viewers of many myths about nutrition.
  - 这部纪录片让观众打消了许多关于营养的错误观念。
- **助记**：dis（去掉）+ abuse（误用/误导）→ 去掉错误认识 → 使醒悟

### 10. **effrontery** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：厚颜无耻；放肆鲁莽
- **英文释义**：insolent or impertinent behavior; shameless boldness
- **例句**：
  - He had the effrontery to ask for a raise after missing every deadline.
  - 他错过了所有截止日期，居然还有脸要求加薪。
  - The journalist criticized the politician's effrontery in denying the obvious facts.
  - 记者批评那位政客公然否认明显事实的厚颜无耻。
- **助记**：ef（向外）+ front（前额/脸面）→ 把脸伸出来不知羞 → 厚颜无耻

### 高一词汇

### 11. **epistolary** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：书信体的；用书信形式写的
- **英文释义**：relating to or denoting literary works in the form of letters
- **例句**：
  - The novel is written in an epistolary format, as a series of letters between friends.
  - 这部小说采用书信体，以朋友间往来书信的形式写成。
  - Epistolary fiction was especially popular in the eighteenth century.
  - 书信体小说在十八世纪尤为流行。
- **助记**：epistle（书信）+ ary → 与书信有关的

### 12. **equivocate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：含糊其辞；支吾；说模棱两可的话
- **英文释义**：to use ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself
- **例句**：
  - When pressed for a clear answer, the witness continued to equivocate.
  - 当被追问要一个明确答复时，证人继续含糊其辞。
  - Politicians are often accused of equivocating on controversial issues.
  - 政客们常被指责在争议性问题上模棱两可。
- **助记**：equi（相等）+ voc（声音）+ ate → 两种说法听起来差不多 → 含糊其辞

### 13. **expunge** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：抹去；删除；使不留痕迹
- **英文释义**：to erase or remove completely; to strike out from a record
- **例句**：
  - The court agreed to expunge his criminal record after he completed rehabilitation.
  - 在他完成康复项目后，法院同意删除他的犯罪记录。
  - She tried to expunge all memories of that painful episode from her mind.
  - 她试图从脑海中抹去那段痛苦经历的所有记忆。
- **助记**：ex（向外）+ pung（刺/点）→ 把墨点刺掉 → 抹去

### 14. **extant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：现存的；尚存的
- **英文释义**：still in existence; surviving
- **例句**：
  - Only three extant manuscripts of the ancient text are known to scholars.
  - 学者们所知该古代文本仅存三部手稿。
  - The museum displays the oldest extant map of the region.
  - 博物馆展出了该地区现存最古老的地图。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ st（站）+ ant → 仍然站出来的 → 尚存的

### 15. **extraneous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无关的；外来的；多余的
- **英文释义**：irrelevant or unrelated to the subject being dealt with
- **例句**：
  - Please omit extraneous details and focus on the main argument.
  - 请省略无关细节，聚焦主要论点。
  - The investigation was complicated by extraneous noise from unreliable witnesses.
  - 不可靠证人的无关干扰使调查变得复杂。
- **助记**：extra（之外）+ neous → 在外面的 → 无关的/外来的
