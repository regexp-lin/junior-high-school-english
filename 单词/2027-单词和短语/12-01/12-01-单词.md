## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **reiterate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：重申；反复说；再次强调
- **英文释义**：to say something again or a number of times, typically for emphasis
- **例句**：
  - The teacher reiterated the importance of submitting homework on time.
  - 老师重申了按时提交作业的重要性。
  - Allow me to reiterate my point: safety must always come first.
  - 请允许我重申我的观点：安全必须始终放在第一位。
- **助记**：re（再）+ iterate（重复）→ 再次重复 → 重申

### 2. **eclectic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：兼收并蓄的；多元的；折衷的
- **英文释义**：deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources
- **例句**：
  - Her eclectic taste in music ranges from classical to hip-hop.
  - 她兼收并蓄的音乐品味从古典到嘻哈无所不包。
  - The restaurant offers an eclectic menu featuring dishes from around the world.
  - 这家餐厅提供来自世界各地菜肴的多元化菜单。
- **助记**：ec（出）+ lect（选择）+ ic → 从各处选择 → 兼收并蓄的

### 3. **bequeath** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：遗赠；传下；留给后代
- **英文释义**：to leave property or a legacy to someone by a will; to pass on
- **例句**：
  - The wealthy philanthropist bequeathed his entire fortune to charity.
  - 这位富有的慈善家将全部财产遗赠给了慈善机构。
  - The ancient civilization bequeathed a rich cultural heritage to future generations.
  - 古代文明给后世留下了丰富的文化遗产。
- **助记**：be + queath（说/给）→ 说好了给 → 遗赠

### 4. **perfunctory** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：敷衍的；草率的；不用心的
- **英文释义**：carried out with a minimum of effort or reflection
- **例句**：
  - His perfunctory apology did nothing to ease her anger.
  - 他敷衍的道歉丝毫没有平息她的愤怒。
  - The security guard gave the bags a perfunctory check before waving people through.
  - 保安人员对包进行了草率的检查，然后就放行了。
- **助记**：per（完成）+ funct（功能）+ ory → 只是为了完成功能 → 敷衍的

### 5. **reprimand** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：训斥；谴责；申斥
- **英文释义**：to express severe disapproval of someone's behavior, especially in a formal statement
- **例句**：
  - The employee was reprimanded for arriving late to work three times in a row.
  - 这名员工因连续三次上班迟到而被训斥。
  - The principal reprimanded the student for cheating on the exam.
  - 校长因学生考试作弊而训斥了他。
- **助记**：re（回）+ primand（按住）→ 把人按住训话 → 训斥

### 6. **autonomous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：自治的；自主的；独立的
- **英文释义**：having self-government; acting independently
- **例句**：
  - The region was granted autonomous status, allowing it to manage its own affairs.
  - 该地区被授予自治地位，允许管理自己的事务。
  - Autonomous vehicles are expected to revolutionize transportation in the near future.
  - 自动驾驶车辆预计将在不久的将来彻底改变交通方式。
- **助记**：auto（自己）+ nom（法则）+ ous → 自己制定法则 → 自治的

### 7. **excoriate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：严厉批评；痛斥；抨击
- **英文释义**：to criticize someone severely
- **例句**：
  - The film was excoriated by critics for its poor script and mediocre acting.
  - 这部电影因剧本差和演技平庸而遭到评论家的严厉批评。
  - The senator excoriated the administration's handling of the crisis.
  - 参议员痛斥了政府对危机的处理方式。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ cori（皮）+ ate → 剥皮 → 严厉批评

### 8. **pervasive** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：普遍的；弥漫的；无处不在的
- **英文释义**：spreading widely throughout an area or a group of people
- **例句**：
  - The pervasive influence of social media has transformed how we communicate.
  - 社交媒体无处不在的影响改变了我们的沟通方式。
  - A pervasive sense of anxiety hung over the community after the disaster.
  - 灾难过后，一种弥漫的焦虑感笼罩着社区。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ vas（走）+ ive → 贯穿到处走 → 弥漫的

### 9. **assimilate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：吸收；同化；融入
- **英文释义**：to take in and fully understand information; to absorb and integrate into a wider society
- **例句**：
  - It took several months for the new immigrants to assimilate into the local culture.
  - 新移民花了好几个月才融入当地文化。
  - Students need time to assimilate the vast amount of information presented in lectures.
  - 学生需要时间来吸收课堂上展示的大量信息。
- **助记**：as（向）+ simil（相似）+ ate → 使变得相似 → 同化

### 10. **idiosyncratic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：独特的；特有的；与众不同的
- **英文释义**：relating to idiosyncrasy; peculiar or individual
- **例句**：
  - The artist's idiosyncratic style set her apart from all other painters.
  - 这位艺术家独特的风格使她与所有其他画家截然不同。
  - He has an idiosyncratic way of organizing his desk that no one else can understand.
  - 他有一种别人无法理解的独特桌面整理方式。
- **助记**：idio（自己的）+ syn（一起）+ cratic → 自己独有的混合体 → 独特的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **synesthesia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：联觉；通感
- **英文释义**：the production of a sense impression relating to one sense by stimulation of another sense
- **例句**：
  - People with synesthesia might "see" music as colors or "taste" words.
  - 有联觉的人可能会把音乐"看成"颜色或"尝到"文字的味道。
  - The poet used synesthesia as a literary device, describing sounds as "warm" and "golden."
  - 诗人运用通感作为修辞手法，将声音描述为"温暖的"和"金色的"。
- **助记**：syn（一起）+ esthe（感觉）+ sia → 感觉融合在一起 → 联觉

### 12. **ineffable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：难以言表的；不可名状的
- **英文释义**：too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words
- **例句**：
  - The ineffable joy of becoming a parent cannot be adequately described.
  - 成为父母的难以言表的喜悦无法用语言充分描述。
  - She experienced an ineffable sense of wonder upon seeing the Grand Canyon for the first time.
  - 她第一次看到大峡谷时体验到一种不可名状的惊叹感。
- **助记**：in（不）+ ef（出）+ fable（说）→ 说不出来的 → 难以言表的

### 13. **Wanderlust** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：旅行癖；漫游欲；对旅行的强烈渴望
- **英文释义**：a strong desire to travel
- **例句**：
  - Her incurable Wanderlust has taken her to over 50 countries across six continents.
  - 她无法治愈的旅行癖带她去了六大洲50多个国家。
  - The beautiful travel photography on social media fuels many people's Wanderlust.
  - 社交媒体上美丽的旅行摄影激发了许多人的漫游欲。
- **助记**：德语借词，Wander（漫游）+ Lust（渴望）→ 对漫游的渴望

### 14. **sonder** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：每个路人都有自己丰富人生的感悟
- **英文释义**：the realization that each random passerby is living a life as vivid and complex as your own
- **例句**：
  - Standing in the busy train station, a feeling of sonder washed over him as he watched the crowds pass by.
  - 站在繁忙的火车站，看着人群走过，一种 sonder 的感觉涌上他心头。
  - The concept of sonder reminds us to be more empathetic toward strangers.
  - Sonder 的概念提醒我们对陌生人更有同理心。
- **助记**：新造词（来自 The Dictionary of Obscure Sorrows），无传统词根

### 15. **querulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱抱怨的；牢骚满腹的；发牢骚的
- **英文释义**：complaining in a petulant or whining manner
- **例句**：
  - The querulous patient complained about everything from the food to the room temperature.
  - 爱抱怨的病人对从食物到室温的一切都不满。
  - His querulous tone made it difficult for anyone to sympathize with his situation.
  - 他牢骚满腹的语气使任何人都难以同情他的处境。
- **助记**：querul（抱怨）+ ous → 爱抱怨的
