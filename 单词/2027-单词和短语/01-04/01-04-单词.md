## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **aspire** /əˈspaɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：渴望；追求
- **英文释义**：to have a strong desire to achieve something great
- **例句**：
  - She aspires to become a famous writer.
  - 她渴望成为一名著名作家。
  - Many young people aspire to make a difference in the world.
  - 许多年轻人渴望改变世界。
- **助记**：a-（朝向）+ spire（呼吸）→ 朝着目标喘息前进 → 渴望

### 2. **assess** /əˈses/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：评估；评价
- **英文释义**：to judge or decide the amount, value, or quality of something
- **例句**：
  - The teacher assessed the students' performance.
  - 老师评估了学生的表现。
  - We need to assess the risks before making a decision.
  - 我们需要在做决定前评估风险。
- **助记**：as-（朝向）+ sess（坐）→ 坐下来仔细看 → 评估

### 3. **assign** /əˈsaɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：分配；指派
- **英文释义**：to give someone a particular job or task
- **例句**：
  - The teacher assigned homework to every student.
  - 老师给每个学生布置了作业。
  - He was assigned to the marketing department.
  - 他被分配到了市场部。
- **助记**：as-（朝向）+ sign（标记）→ 给某人做标记 → 指派

### 4. **assist** /əˈsɪst/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：帮助；协助
- **英文释义**：to help someone do something
- **例句**：
  - The nurse assisted the doctor during the operation.
  - 手术期间护士协助了医生。
  - Can I assist you with anything?
  - 我能帮你什么忙吗？
- **助记**：as-（朝向）+ sist（站）→ 站在旁边帮忙 → 协助

### 5. **associate** /əˈsəʊʃieɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：联想；同事
- **英文释义**：to connect someone or something in your mind; a person you work with
- **例句**：
  - People often associate summer with holidays.
  - 人们经常把夏天和假期联系在一起。
  - She is a business associate of mine.
  - 她是我的商业伙伴。
- **助记**：as-（朝向）+ soci（伙伴）+ -ate → 成为伙伴 → 联想；同事

### 6. **assume** /əˈsjuːm/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：假定；承担
- **英文释义**：to accept something as true without proof; to take on a responsibility
- **例句**：
  - Don't assume that everyone agrees with you.
  - 不要以为所有人都同意你。
  - She assumed the role of team leader.
  - 她承担了队长的角色。
- **助记**：as-（朝向）+ sume（拿取）→ 把观点拿过来当真 → 假定

### 7. **assure** /əˈʃʊə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：保证；使确信
- **英文释义**：to tell someone confidently that something is true or will happen
- **例句**：
  - I assure you that everything will be fine.
  - 我向你保证一切都会好的。
  - She assured her parents that she would study hard.
  - 她向父母保证会好好学习。
- **助记**：as-（加强）+ sure（确定）→ 让人更加确定 → 保证

### 8. **atmosphere** /ˈætməsfɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：大气层；氛围
- **英文释义**：the mixture of gases surrounding the earth; the mood of a place
- **例句**：
  - The earth's atmosphere protects us from harmful rays.
  - 地球大气层保护我们免受有害射线的伤害。
  - The restaurant has a warm and friendly atmosphere.
  - 这家餐厅氛围温馨友好。
- **助记**：atmo-（蒸汽）+ sphere（球体）→ 包围地球的气体层 → 大气层

### 9. **attempt** /əˈtempt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：尝试；企图
- **英文释义**：to try to do something, especially something difficult
- **例句**：
  - He attempted to climb the mountain but failed.
  - 他试图攀登这座山，但失败了。
  - This is her third attempt at passing the driving test.
  - 这是她第三次尝试通过驾照考试。
- **助记**：at-（朝向）+ tempt（诱惑/尝试）→ 尝试去做 → 企图

### 10. **attribute** /əˈtrɪbjuːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：把……归因于；属性
- **英文释义**：to believe something is the result of a particular thing; a quality or feature
- **例句**：
  - She attributes her success to hard work.
  - 她把成功归因于努力工作。
  - Patience is an important attribute of a good teacher.
  - 耐心是好老师的重要品质。
- **助记**：at-（朝向）+ tribute（给予）→ 把原因给予某事 → 归因于

### 高一词汇

### 11. **arduous** /ˈɑːdjuəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：艰难的；费力的
- **英文释义**：involving a lot of effort and energy, especially over a long period
- **例句**：
  - The arduous journey took three months.
  - 这段艰难的旅程花了三个月。
  - Learning a new language is an arduous task.
  - 学一门新语言是一项艰难的任务。
- **助记**：ard-（燃烧）+ -uous → 像燃烧一样耗尽精力 → 艰难的

### 12. **audacious** /ɔːˈdeɪʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：大胆的；鲁莽的
- **英文释义**：showing a willingness to take surprisingly bold risks
- **例句**：
  - His audacious plan surprised everyone.
  - 他大胆的计划让所有人吃惊。
  - It was audacious of her to challenge the professor.
  - 她挑战教授真是胆子不小。
- **助记**：aud-（敢）+ -acious → 敢做的 → 大胆的

### 13. **austere** /ɒˈstɪə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：严肃的；朴素的
- **英文释义**：severe or strict in manner or attitude; having no comforts or luxuries
- **例句**：
  - He lived an austere life in the monastery.
  - 他在修道院过着朴素的生活。
  - The room had an austere, minimalist style.
  - 房间风格朴素而简约。
- **助记**：austere → "奥斯特"（谐音）→ 奥地利的严肃学者 → 严肃的

### 14. **benevolent** /bəˈnevələnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：仁慈的；慈善的
- **英文释义**：well-meaning and kindly; generous in providing aid to others
- **例句**：
  - The benevolent old man donated all his wealth to charity.
  - 这位仁慈的老人把所有财富捐给了慈善机构。
  - She gave a benevolent smile to the children.
  - 她对孩子们露出慈祥的微笑。
- **助记**：bene-（好）+ vol（意愿）+ -ent → 有好意愿的 → 仁慈的

### 15. **brevity** /ˈbrevəti/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：简洁；短暂
- **英文释义**：concise and exact use of words; shortness of time
- **例句**：
  - Brevity is the soul of wit.
  - 简洁是智慧的灵魂。
  - The brevity of summer always makes me sad.
  - 夏天的短暂总让我伤感。
- **助记**：brev-（短）+ -ity → 短 → 简洁；短暂
