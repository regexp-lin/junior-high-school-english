## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **impugn** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：质疑；抨击；指责
- **英文释义**：to dispute the truth, validity, or honesty of
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer impugned the credibility of the witness during cross-examination.
  - 律师在交叉询问中质疑了证人的可信度。
  - He felt his integrity was being impugned by the false accusations.
  - 他觉得自己的人格被虚假的指控所抨击。
- **助记**：im（反对）+ pugn（打击）→ 反对打击 → 质疑

### 2. **cogent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：令人信服的；有说服力的；切中要害的
- **英文释义**：clear, logical, and convincing
- **例句**：
  - She presented a cogent argument that convinced the entire jury.
  - 她提出了一个令人信服的论点，说服了全部陪审团。
  - The report provides a cogent analysis of the economic situation.
  - 报告对经济形势进行了切中要害的分析。
- **助记**：cog（思考）+ ent → 思考充分的 → 令人信服的

### 3. **dissipate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：消散；驱散；挥霍
- **英文释义**：to disappear or cause to disappear; to squander
- **例句**：
  - The morning fog dissipated as the sun rose higher in the sky.
  - 随着太阳升高，晨雾逐渐消散。
  - He dissipated his family's fortune on gambling and extravagant living.
  - 他把家族的财产挥霍在赌博和奢侈生活上。
- **助记**：dis（分散）+ sip（投）+ ate → 分散投掷 → 消散/挥霍

### 4. **inexorable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可阻挡的；无法改变的；不可动摇的
- **英文释义**：impossible to stop or prevent; unyielding
- **例句**：
  - The inexorable march of technology continues to reshape our daily lives.
  - 技术不可阻挡的进步继续重塑着我们的日常生活。
  - Despite their efforts, the inexorable decline of the empire could not be reversed.
  - 尽管他们做出了努力，帝国不可阻挡的衰落仍无法逆转。
- **助记**：in（不）+ exor（祈求）+ able → 无法通过祈求改变 → 不可阻挡的

### 5. **interpolate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：插入；窜改；内插
- **英文释义**：to insert something between other things; to alter a text by inserting new material
- **例句**：
  - The editor interpolated additional notes to clarify the original manuscript.
  - 编辑插入了额外注释以阐明原稿。
  - Scientists interpolated data points to fill gaps in the experimental results.
  - 科学家通过内插数据点来填补实验结果中的空白。
- **助记**：inter（之间）+ pol（修饰）+ ate → 在之间修饰 → 插入

### 6. **pedantic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：学究的；迂腐的；过分注重细节的
- **英文释义**：of or like a pedant; excessively concerned with minor details
- **例句**：
  - His pedantic insistence on correct grammar made casual conversation difficult.
  - 他对正确语法的迂腐坚持使随意交谈变得困难。
  - The reviewer's pedantic criticism focused on trivial errors rather than the book's overall quality.
  - 评论者迂腐的批评聚焦于琐碎的错误而非书的整体质量。
- **助记**：pedant（学究）+ ic → 像学究一样 → 迂腐的

### 7. **prevaricate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：支吾；闪烁其词；搪塞
- **英文释义**：to speak or act in an evasive way
- **例句**：
  - The politician prevaricated when asked directly about the scandal.
  - 当被直接问及丑闻时，政治家支吾其词。
  - Stop prevaricating and give me a straight answer.
  - 别再闪烁其词了，给我一个直接的回答。
- **助记**：pre（偏离）+ varic（走）+ ate → 走偏了路 → 支吾/搪塞

### 8. **sagacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：睿智的；精明的；有远见的
- **英文释义**：having or showing keen mental discernment and good judgment
- **例句**：
  - The sagacious investor foresaw the market downturn and sold his stocks early.
  - 睿智的投资者预见了市场下跌并提前卖出了股票。
  - Her sagacious advice saved the company from making a costly mistake.
  - 她睿智的建议使公司免于犯下代价高昂的错误。
- **助记**：sag（感知）+ acious → 感知力强的 → 睿智的

### 9. **obdurate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽固的；执拗的；冷酷无情的
- **英文释义**：stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action
- **例句**：
  - The obdurate negotiator refused to compromise on any of his demands.
  - 顽固的谈判者拒绝在他的任何要求上妥协。
  - Despite pleas from his family, the obdurate man refused to seek medical treatment.
  - 尽管家人恳求，这个执拗的人拒绝接受治疗。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ dur（硬）+ ate → 对着你硬到底 → 顽固的

### 10. **capitulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：投降；屈服；让步
- **英文释义**：to cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; to surrender
- **例句**：
  - After a prolonged standoff, the rebels finally capitulated to government forces.
  - 经过长时间的对峙，叛军终于向政府军投降。
  - She refused to capitulate to the unreasonable demands of her employer.
  - 她拒绝向雇主不合理的要求屈服。
- **助记**：capit（头）+ ulate → 低头 → 投降

### 高一词汇

### 11. **velleity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：微弱的意愿；空想；未付诸行动的愿望
- **英文释义**：a wish or inclination not strong enough to lead to action
- **例句**：
  - His desire to learn the piano remained a mere velleity — he never actually signed up for lessons.
  - 他学钢琴的愿望只是空想——他从未真正报名上课。
  - Many New Year's resolutions are velleities that fade by February.
  - 许多新年决心都是微弱的意愿，到二月就消退了。
- **助记**：velle（想要）+ ity → 只是想要但不行动 → 空想

### 12. **coruscate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：闪烁；闪耀；才华横溢
- **英文释义**：to flash or sparkle; to exhibit brilliant wit
- **例句**：
  - The diamond coruscated brilliantly under the spotlight.
  - 钻石在聚光灯下闪烁着耀眼的光芒。
  - Her coruscating wit made every conversation with her an absolute delight.
  - 她才华横溢的机智使与她的每次对话都是绝对的愉悦。
- **助记**：corusc（闪光）+ ate → 闪光 → 闪烁

### 13. **anodyne** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：止痛的；温和无害的；镇痛剂
- **英文释义**：not likely to cause offense or disagreement; something that soothes pain
- **例句**：
  - The politician gave an anodyne response that avoided addressing the real issues.
  - 政治家给出了温和无害的回应，回避了真正的问题。
  - Music served as an anodyne for her emotional pain during difficult times.
  - 在困难时期，音乐是她情感痛苦的止痛剂。
- **助记**：an（无）+ odyne（痛苦）→ 无痛苦的 → 止痛的

### 14. **ataraxia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：心灵的宁静；不受干扰的内心平静
- **英文释义**：a state of freedom from emotional disturbance and anxiety; tranquility
- **例句**：
  - The Stoic philosophers believed that ataraxia was the highest state of human well-being.
  - 斯多葛派哲学家认为心灵的宁静是人类幸福的最高境界。
  - Through years of meditation practice, she achieved a state of ataraxia.
  - 通过多年的冥想练习，她达到了心灵宁静的状态。
- **助记**：a（不）+ tarax（扰乱）+ ia → 不被扰乱 → 心灵宁静

### 15. **panacea** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：万灵药；万能解决方案
- **英文释义**：a solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases
- **例句**：
  - There is no panacea for the complex social problems facing the nation.
  - 对于国家面临的复杂社会问题没有万能的解决方案。
  - Technology is often mistakenly regarded as a panacea for all of education's challenges.
  - 技术经常被错误地视为解决所有教育挑战的万灵药。
- **助记**：pan（全部）+ acea（治愈）→ 治愈所有的 → 万灵药
