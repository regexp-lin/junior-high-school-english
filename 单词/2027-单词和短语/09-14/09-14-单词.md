## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **kinship** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：亲属关系；相似；亲切感
- **英文释义**：blood relationship; a feeling of being close or similar
- **例句**：
  - The tribe places great value on kinship and mutual aid.
  - 该部落高度重视亲属关系与互助。
  - There is a kinship between jazz and certain forms of folk music.
  - 爵士乐与某些民间音乐形式之间存在相似之处。
- **助记**：kin（亲属）+ ship（关系）→ 亲属关系

### 2. **lamentable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可悲的；令人惋惜的；质量极差的
- **英文释义**：deplorable; regrettable; disappointing
- **例句**：
  - The team’s performance was lamentable from start to finish.
  - 该队从头到尾的表现都令人失望。
  - It is lamentable that so many children still lack access to education.
  - 如此多的儿童仍无法上学，令人痛心。
- **助记**：lament（悲叹）+ able → 令人悲叹的 → 可悲的

### 3. **meticulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；极为仔细的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - The surgeon is known for her meticulous technique.
  - 这位外科医生以手术精细著称。
  - He kept meticulous records of every expense during the trip.
  - 他旅途中把每笔开支都记录得一丝不苟。
- **助记**：metus（恐惧）→ 害怕出错 → 极其仔细

### 4. **nascent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：初生的；新生的；尚在发展中的
- **英文释义**：just beginning to develop; emerging
- **例句**：
  - The nascent industry needs government support to survive.
  - 新兴产业需要政府扶持才能生存。
  - A nascent friendship grew between the two volunteers.
  - 两位志愿者之间萌发了初生的友谊。
- **助记**：nasci（出生）→ 刚出生的 → 新生的

### 5. **omit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：省略；遗漏；未做
- **英文释义**：to leave out or exclude; to fail to do something
- **例句**：
  - Please do not omit any required fields on the form.
  - 请勿遗漏表格上的任何必填项。
  - He omitted to mention that he had already accepted another offer.
  - 他未提及自己已接受另一份录用。
- **助记**：o（向外）+ mit（送）→ 送走 → 省略/漏掉

### 6. **perennial** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多年生的；长期的；反复出现的
- **英文释义**：lasting for a long time; recurring yearly (plants)
- **例句**：
  - Perennial plants come back every spring without replanting.
  - 多年生植物每年春天都会重新生长，无需再种。
  - Budget deficits became a perennial problem for the city.
  - 预算赤字成了该市长期反复出现的问题。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ enn（年）→ 贯穿多年的 → 长期的

### 7. **quell** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：镇压；平息；消除（恐惧）
- **英文释义**：to put an end to disorder or violence; to suppress
- **例句**：
  - Police were sent to quell the riot in the city center.
  - 警方被派往市中心镇压骚乱。
  - She took deep breaths to quell her anxiety before the interview.
  - 面试前她深呼吸以平复焦虑。
- **助记**：古英语 cwellan（杀死）→ 平息（暴力）

### 8. **relegate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：降级；把……贬到次要地位；移交
- **英文释义**：to assign to a lower position; to banish to an inferior role
- **例句**：
  - The team was relegated to a lower division after poor results.
  - 球队因成绩不佳被降级到低级别联赛。
  - After the scandal, he was relegated to a minor administrative post.
  - 丑闻过后，他被贬到次要行政岗位。
- **助记**：re（回）+ leg（送）→ 送回去 → 降级

### 9. **stipulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：规定；明确要求
- **英文释义**：to demand or specify as part of an agreement
- **例句**：
  - The contract stipulates that payment must be made within thirty days.
  - 合同规定付款须在三十天内完成。
  - University rules stipulate minimum attendance for international students.
  - 校规对留学生出勤有明确要求。
- **助记**：stipul（按条款约定）→ 规定

### 10. **tacit** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：心照不宣的；默示的；不言而喻的
- **英文释义**：understood without being stated openly; implied
- **例句**：
  - There was tacit agreement among members not to discuss salaries.
  - 成员间心照不宣，不谈薪水问题。
  - His silence was taken as tacit approval of the plan.
  - 他的沉默被视为对计划的默许。
- **助记**：tac（安静）→ 不说出口的 → 默示的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **umbrage** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：不快；怨恨；冒犯感
- **英文释义**：offense or annoyance; a feeling of being slighted
- **例句**：
  - She took umbrage at his casual remark about her work.
  - 他随口评论她的工作，令她感到不快。
  - The critic gave umbrage to many fans with a harsh review.
  - 这位评论家的刻薄评论得罪了许多粉丝。
- **助记**：umbra（阴影）→ 心里蒙上阴影 → 不快

### 12. **vicarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：间接体验的；代位的；感同身受的
- **英文释义**：experienced through another person rather than directly
- **例句**：
  - Parents often feel vicarious pride in their children’s achievements.
  - 父母常常因子女的成就而感到与有荣焉。
  - Readers seek vicarious adventure in travel novels.
  - 读者在旅行小说中寻求间接的冒险体验。
- **助记**：vicar（代理人）→ 替代体验 → 间接的

### 13. **waive** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：放弃（权利）；免除；搁置
- **英文释义**：to choose not to demand or enforce a right or rule
- **例句**：
  - The bank agreed to waive the fee for first-time customers.
  - 银行同意免除首次开户客户的手续费。
  - He waived his right to remain silent during questioning.
  - 他放弃了保持沉默的权利。
- **助记**：wave（挥手）→ 挥手放弃 → 放弃权利

### 14. **xenophobia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：仇外；对外国人的恐惧与排斥
- **英文释义**：dislike of or prejudice against people from other countries
- **例句**：
  - The report documents rising xenophobia after the economic crisis.
  - 报告记录了经济危机后仇外情绪的上升。
  - Education can help reduce xenophobia among young people.
  - 教育有助于减少年轻人的仇外心理。
- **助记**：xeno（外）+ phobia（恐惧）→ 仇外

### 15. **zealot** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：狂热者；偏激分子
- **英文释义**：a person who is fanatical and uncompromising in pursuit of a cause
- **例句**：
  - Political zealots refuse to listen to any opposing view.
  - 政治狂热者拒绝听取任何反对意见。
  - He was labeled a zealot for his extreme environmental activism.
  - 他因极端环保行动被称为狂热分子。
- **助记**：zeal（热情）+ ot → 过度热情的人 → 狂热者
