## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **mendacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚假的；说谎的；不诚实的
- **英文释义**：not telling the truth; lying
- **例句**：
  - The mendacious politician was eventually exposed by investigative journalists.
  - 这位说谎的政治家最终被调查记者揭穿了。
  - His mendacious account of the incident contradicted all the eyewitness testimonies.
  - 他对事件虚假的叙述与所有目击者的证词相矛盾。
- **助记**：mend（缺陷）+ acious → 有缺陷的（品格）→ 说谎的

### 2. **inexorable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可阻挡的；无情的；不为所动的
- **英文释义**：impossible to stop or prevent; unrelenting
- **例句**：
  - The inexorable advance of automation is transforming industries worldwide.
  - 自动化不可阻挡的推进正在改变全球各行各业。
  - Time's inexorable passage reminds us to cherish every moment we have.
  - 时间无情的流逝提醒我们珍惜拥有的每一刻。
- **助记**：in（不）+ exor（祈求）+ able → 无法通过祈求改变 → 不可阻挡的

### 3. **castigate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：严厉批评；惩罚；斥责
- **英文释义**：to reprimand someone severely
- **例句**：
  - The coach castigated the team for their lack of effort during the match.
  - 教练严厉批评了球队在比赛中缺乏努力。
  - The editorial castigated the government for its failure to address poverty.
  - 社论严厉批评了政府未能解决贫困问题。
- **助记**：castig（纯净/惩罚）+ ate → 通过惩罚使纯净 → 严厉批评

### 4. **redoubtable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：令人敬畏的；强大的；可怕的
- **英文释义**：(of a person) formidable, especially as an opponent
- **例句**：
  - The redoubtable chess champion had not lost a tournament in over five years.
  - 这位令人敬畏的国际象棋冠军五年多来从未输过一场锦标赛。
  - She was a redoubtable debater whose arguments were nearly impossible to counter.
  - 她是一位令人敬畏的辩论者，她的论点几乎无法反驳。
- **助记**：re（加强）+ doubt（畏惧）+ able → 非常令人畏惧的 → 令人敬畏的

### 5. **mitigate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：缓解；减轻；缓和
- **英文释义**：to make less severe, serious, or painful
- **例句**：
  - Planting trees can help mitigate the effects of climate change.
  - 植树可以帮助缓解气候变化的影响。
  - The company took immediate steps to mitigate the damage caused by the data breach.
  - 公司立即采取措施减轻数据泄露造成的损害。
- **助记**：mit（温和）+ ig + ate → 使变温和 → 缓解

### 6. **pernicious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有害的；恶性的；致命的
- **英文释义**：having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way
- **例句**：
  - The pernicious influence of misinformation on social media undermines public trust.
  - 社交媒体上虚假信息的有害影响破坏了公众信任。
  - Smoking has a pernicious effect on lung health that worsens over time.
  - 吸烟对肺部健康有随时间恶化的有害影响。
- **助记**：per（彻底）+ nic（杀害）+ ious → 彻底杀害 → 有害的

### 7. **extemporize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：即兴发挥；临场发挥；即席演说
- **英文释义**：to compose, perform, or produce something without preparation; to improvise
- **例句**：
  - The speaker's notes were lost, so she had to extemporize her entire presentation.
  - 演讲者的笔记丢了，所以她不得不即兴完成整个演讲。
  - Jazz musicians are skilled at extemporizing, creating beautiful melodies on the spot.
  - 爵士音乐家擅长即兴发挥，当场创造出美妙的旋律。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ tempor（时间）+ ize → 在当时当刻产出 → 即兴发挥

### 8. **mercurial** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：善变的；反复无常的；活泼的
- **英文释义**：(of a person) subject to sudden or unpredictable changes of mood or mind
- **例句**：
  - His mercurial temperament made it difficult for colleagues to know what to expect.
  - 他反复无常的性情让同事们很难知道该期待什么。
  - The mercurial stock market kept investors on edge throughout the week.
  - 善变的股市让投资者整个星期都提心吊胆。
- **助记**：mercur（水银/墨丘利）+ ial → 像水银一样变化不定 → 善变的

### 9. **blandish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：奉承；讨好；哄骗
- **英文释义**：to coax with flattery; to cajole
- **例句**：
  - The salesman blandished the customer with compliments to close the deal.
  - 销售员用恭维话奉承客户以成交。
  - She blandished her way past the security guard with a charming smile and kind words.
  - 她用迷人的微笑和温柔的话语讨好地混过了保安。
- **助记**：bland（温和的）+ ish → 用温和的话语 → 奉承/讨好

### 10. **scrupulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；审慎的；有道德原则的
- **英文释义**：(of a person or process) diligent, thorough, and extremely attentive to details
- **例句**：
  - The accountant was scrupulous in checking every figure before submitting the report.
  - 会计在提交报告前一丝不苟地核对每个数字。
  - A scrupulous journalist always verifies sources before publishing a story.
  - 一个审慎的记者在发表文章前总是核实来源。
- **助记**：scrupul（小石子/顾虑）+ ous → 像被小石子硌脚一样谨慎 → 一丝不苟的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **synesthesia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：联觉（一种感官刺激引发另一种感官体验）
- **英文释义**：a condition in which one sense is simultaneously perceived by one or more additional senses
- **例句**：
  - A person with synesthesia might "see" colors when they hear music.
  - 有联觉的人在听音乐时可能会"看到"颜色。
  - The artist's synesthesia allowed her to perceive sounds as vivid bursts of color on canvas.
  - 这位艺术家的联觉让她将声音感知为画布上鲜明的色彩。
- **助记**：syn（一起）+ esthesia（感觉）→ 感觉一起来 → 联觉

### 12. **crepuscular** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：黄昏的；微光的；（动物）在晨昏活动的
- **英文释义**：of, resembling, or relating to twilight
- **例句**：
  - The crepuscular light cast a golden glow over the landscape.
  - 黄昏的光线为风景披上了一层金色的光辉。
  - Deer are crepuscular animals, most active during dawn and dusk.
  - 鹿是在晨昏活动的动物，在黎明和黄昏时分最活跃。
- **助记**：crepuscul（黄昏）+ ar → 黄昏的

### 13. **thalassophile** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：爱海之人；海洋爱好者
- **英文释义**：a person who loves the sea or ocean
- **例句**：
  - As a lifelong thalassophile, she chose to retire in a cottage overlooking the Pacific.
  - 作为一个终身的海洋爱好者，她选择在一间俯瞰太平洋的小屋退休。
  - The thalassophile spent every vacation at the coast, drawn irresistibly to the sound of waves.
  - 这位爱海之人每次假期都在海边度过，被海浪的声音不可抗拒地吸引。
- **助记**：thalass（海洋）+ phile（爱好者）→ 爱海洋的人 → 海洋爱好者

### 14. **phosphene** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：光幻视（闭眼或揉眼时看到的光点）
- **英文释义**：a ring or spot of light produced by pressure on the eyeball or direct stimulation of the visual system
- **例句**：
  - When you rub your eyes, the colorful patterns you see are phosphenes.
  - 当你揉眼睛时看到的彩色图案就是光幻视。
  - Astronauts have reported seeing phosphenes in space, likely caused by cosmic rays.
  - 宇航员报告在太空中看到了光幻视，可能是由宇宙射线引起的。
- **助记**：phos（光）+ phene（显现）→ 光显现出来 → 光幻视

### 15. **saudade** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：怅惘之情；对失去之物的深沉渴望
- **英文释义**：a deep emotional state of nostalgic or profound melancholic longing for something or someone absent
- **例句**：
  - The Portuguese word saudade captures a longing so deep that it has no direct English equivalent.
  - 葡萄牙语词 saudade 捕捉了一种如此深沉的渴望，以至于英语中没有直接对应的词。
  - Listening to fado music filled her with saudade for the Lisbon of her youth.
  - 听法多音乐让她对年轻时的里斯本充满了怅惘之情。
- **助记**：saudade 是葡萄牙语，表达一种美丽而忧伤的思念与渴望
