## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **advocate** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：提倡；拥护；倡导者
- **英文释义**：to publicly support or recommend; a person who supports a cause
- **例句**：
  - The organization advocates for equal rights and opportunities for all citizens.
  - 该组织倡导所有公民享有平等的权利和机会。
  - She is a passionate advocate for environmental protection and sustainable living.
  - 她是环境保护和可持续生活的热情倡导者。
- **助记**：ad（朝向）+ voc（声音）+ ate → 发出声音支持 → 倡导

### 2. **brisk** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：轻快的；活泼的；凛冽的
- **英文释义**：active, fast, and energetic; (of weather) cold but pleasantly fresh
- **例句**：
  - A brisk morning walk is a great way to start the day with energy.
  - 清晨轻快的散步是精力充沛地开始一天的好方法。
  - Business has been brisk at the new restaurant since it opened last month.
  - 自从上个月开业以来，新餐厅的生意一直很红火。
- **助记**：brisk 联想 breeze（微风）→ 微风般轻快的

### 3. **contemplate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：沉思；凝视；打算
- **英文释义**：to think deeply and at length about something; to consider as a possibility
- **例句**：
  - He sat by the window, contemplating the meaning of life.
  - 他坐在窗边，沉思着人生的意义。
  - The company is contemplating a major restructuring to improve efficiency.
  - 公司正在考虑进行重大重组以提高效率。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ templ（观察，如 temple 庙宇中冥想）+ ate → 静观沉思

### 4. **discrepancy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：差异；不一致；矛盾
- **英文释义**：a lack of compatibility or similarity between two or more things
- **例句**：
  - There is a significant discrepancy between the two reports on the number of casualties.
  - 两份关于伤亡人数的报告之间存在重大差异。
  - The auditor found a discrepancy in the company's financial records.
  - 审计师在公司的财务记录中发现了不一致之处。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ crep（嘎嘎作响）+ ancy → 声音不一致 → 差异

### 5. **encompass** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：包含；包围；涵盖
- **英文释义**：to surround and have or hold within; to include comprehensively
- **例句**：
  - The national park encompasses over 500 square kilometers of wilderness.
  - 国家公园涵盖了超过500平方公里的荒野。
  - Her research encompasses a wide range of topics, from biology to philosophy.
  - 她的研究涵盖了从生物学到哲学的广泛主题。
- **助记**：en（使）+ compass（范围/指南针）→ 使在范围之内 → 包含

### 6. **hoist** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：举起；升起；吊起
- **英文释义**：to raise something by means of ropes, pulleys, or a crane
- **例句**：
  - The sailors hoisted the flag to signal their arrival at the port.
  - 水手们升起旗帜，标志着他们到达了港口。
  - Workers used a crane to hoist the heavy steel beams into place.
  - 工人们用起重机把沉重的钢梁吊装到位。
- **助记**：hoist 发音像"嗨嘶特"——喊着号子用力举起

### 7. **lavish** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：奢侈的；慷慨的；大量给予
- **英文释义**：sumptuously rich or luxurious; to bestow something in generous quantities
- **例句**：
  - The royal family held a lavish banquet to celebrate the prince's wedding.
  - 王室举办了一场奢华的宴会庆祝王子的婚礼。
  - She lavished praise on her students for their outstanding performance.
  - 她对学生们的出色表现大加赞扬。
- **助记**：lav（洗/流淌）+ ish → 东西像水一样流淌 → 奢侈的/慷慨的

### 8. **mingle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：混合；交往；相处
- **英文释义**：to mix or cause to mix together; to move among people at a social event
- **例句**：
  - At the party, guests mingled freely, exchanging stories and laughter.
  - 在聚会上，客人们自由交往，分享故事和欢笑。
  - The smell of fresh bread mingled with the aroma of coffee in the bakery.
  - 面包店里新鲜面包的气味与咖啡的香气混合在一起。
- **助记**：mingle 联想 mix + single → 单个的东西混在一起

### 9. **preclude** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：排除；阻止；使不可能
- **英文释义**：to prevent from happening; to make impossible
- **例句**：
  - His lack of a passport precluded him from traveling abroad.
  - 他没有护照，这使他无法出国旅行。
  - The heavy snowfall precluded any attempt to reach the mountain summit.
  - 大雪阻止了任何到达山顶的尝试。
- **助记**：pre（提前）+ clude（关闭）→ 提前关上门 → 排除/阻止

### 10. **tranquil** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：宁静的；平静的；安详的
- **英文释义**：free from disturbance; calm and peaceful
- **例句**：
  - The tranquil lake reflected the snow-capped mountains like a mirror.
  - 宁静的湖泊像镜子一样映照着雪山。
  - After years of city life, she longed for the tranquil countryside where she grew up.
  - 经过多年的城市生活，她渴望回到她长大的宁静乡村。
- **助记**：tranquil 联想 tranquilizer（镇静剂）→ 使人平静的 → 宁静的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **appease** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：安抚；抚慰；满足
- **英文释义**：to pacify or placate someone by acceding to their demands
- **例句**：
  - The government tried to appease the angry protesters by promising reforms.
  - 政府试图通过承诺改革来安抚愤怒的抗议者。
  - Offering the child ice cream was enough to appease his tantrum.
  - 给孩子冰淇淋就足以安抚他的发脾气。
- **助记**：ap（加强）+ pease（和平，如 peace）→ 使回到和平状态 → 安抚

### 12. **debilitate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使虚弱；使衰弱；削弱
- **英文释义**：to make someone very weak and infirm
- **例句**：
  - The prolonged illness debilitated him to the point where he could barely stand.
  - 长期的疾病使他虚弱到几乎无法站立。
  - Years of economic sanctions have debilitated the country's industrial capacity.
  - 多年的经济制裁削弱了该国的工业能力。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ bil（力量，如 ability）+ itate → 力量向下走 → 使虚弱

### 13. **idiosyncrasy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：特质；怪癖；独特之处
- **英文释义**：a distinctive or peculiar feature or characteristic of a person or thing
- **例句**：
  - One of his many idiosyncrasies is always wearing mismatched socks.
  - 他的众多怪癖之一是总是穿不配对的袜子。
  - Every language has its own idiosyncrasies that make translation challenging.
  - 每种语言都有自己的独特之处，使翻译充满挑战。
- **助记**：idio（个人的）+ syn（一起）+ crasy（混合/气质）→ 个人独特的气质 → 特质

### 14. **misnomer** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：用词不当；误称
- **英文释义**：a wrong or inaccurate name or designation
- **例句**：
  - Calling a koala a "bear" is actually a misnomer — koalas are marsupials, not bears.
  - 把考拉叫做"熊"其实是一个误称——考拉是有袋动物，不是熊。
  - "Rush hour" is a misnomer because traffic barely moves during that time.
  - "高峰时段"是一个误称，因为那段时间交通几乎不动。
- **助记**：mis（错误）+ nomer（名字）→ 错误的名字 → 误称

### 15. **tenacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽强的；坚韧的；固执的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent and determined
- **例句**：
  - The tenacious athlete overcame a serious injury to win the gold medal.
  - 这位顽强的运动员克服了严重的伤病赢得了金牌。
  - Weeds are incredibly tenacious — they keep growing back no matter how often you pull them out.
  - 杂草的生命力极其顽强——无论你拔多少次它们都会再长回来。
- **助记**：ten（抓住，如 tenant 租户）+ acious → 紧紧抓住不放的 → 顽强的
