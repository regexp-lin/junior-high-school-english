## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **placate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：安抚；平息；使息怒
- **英文释义**：to make someone less angry or hostile; to pacify
- **例句**：
  - The manager tried to placate angry customers with refunds.
  - 经理试图用退款安抚愤怒的顾客。
  - Gifts alone cannot placate someone you have deeply hurt.
  - 仅靠礼物无法安抚你深深伤害过的人。
- **助记**：plac（平静）+ ate → 使平静 → 安抚

### 2. **quash** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：撤销；镇压；废止
- **英文释义**：to reject or annul a legal decision; to suppress forcefully
- **例句**：
  - The high court quashed the conviction on procedural grounds.
  - 高等法院以程序理由撤销了判决。
  - The regime quashed protests in the capital within hours.
  - 当局在数小时内镇压了首都的抗议。
- **助记**：与 squash（压扁）联想 → 压下去 → 撤销/镇压

### 3. **redeem** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：赎回；弥补；救赎
- **英文释义**：to recover by payment; to compensate for faults or weaknesses
- **例句**：
  - You can redeem loyalty points for free tickets.
  - 你可以用积分兑换免费票。
  - A single act of courage cannot redeem a lifetime of cowardice.
  - 一次勇敢无法弥补一生的怯懦。
- **助记**：re（回）+ em（买）→ 买回 → 赎回/弥补

### 4. **sparse** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：稀疏的；稀少的；简陋的
- **英文释义**：thinly scattered or distributed; meager
- **例句**：
  - Vegetation is sparse in the desert beyond the oasis.
  - 绿洲之外的沙漠植被稀疏。
  - The apartment was sparse, with only a bed and a desk.
  - 公寓很简陋，只有一张床和一张桌子。
- **助记**：sparse 联想 sparse（稀疏）与 parse（解析）→ 分布稀疏

### 5. **truncate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：截短；删节；截去
- **英文释义**：to shorten by cutting off a part; to cut short
- **例句**：
  - The article was truncated to fit the newspaper column.
  - 文章被截短以适合报纸版面。
  - Budget cuts truncated several planned research projects.
  - 预算削减使若干计划中的研究项目被截断。
- **助记**：trunc（干/茎）+ ate → 砍短 → 截短

### 6. **uphold** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：维护；支持；维持（原判）
- **英文释义**：to confirm or support a principle or decision; to uphold a verdict
- **例句**：
  - Judges must uphold the law without bias.
  - 法官必须无偏见地维护法律。
  - The appeal court upheld the original ruling.
  - 上诉法院维持了原判。
- **助记**：up + hold → 向上托住 → 维护

### 7. **vilify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：诽谤；中伤；污蔑
- **英文释义**：to speak or write about someone in an abusively disparaging way
- **例句**：
  - Opponents vilified the candidate with false rumors.
  - 对手用虚假谣言诽谤候选人。
  - She refused to vilify her ex-partner in interviews.
  - 她拒绝在采访中诋毁前任。
- **助记**：vil（卑鄙）+ ify → 使显得卑鄙 → 诽谤

### 8. **wheedle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：哄骗；用甜言蜜语骗取
- **英文释义**：to use flattery or coaxing to persuade someone
- **例句**：
  - The child wheedled extra pocket money from his grandmother.
  - 孩子从祖母那里哄到了额外的零花钱。
  - He tried to wheedle his way into the VIP section.
  - 他试图用甜言蜜语混进贵宾区。
- **助记**：拟声 → 软语哄骗 → 哄骗

### 9. **yearn** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：渴望；思念；向往
- **英文释义**：to have an intense longing for something
- **例句**：
  - Exiles yearn for the homeland they left behind.
  - 流亡者思念离开的故土。
  - She yearned to study music though her parents preferred medicine.
  - 尽管父母希望她学医，她仍渴望学音乐。
- **助记**：yearn 谐音“焉”→ 心焉向往 → 渴望

### 10. **zeal** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：热情；热忱；狂热
- **英文释义**：great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause
- **例句**：
  - Youth volunteers worked with zeal to clean the riverbanks.
  - 青年志愿者热情高涨地清理河岸。
  - His zeal sometimes blinded him to practical limits.
  - 他的热忱有时使他忽视实际限制。
- **助记**：与 jealous（嫉妒）同源不同义 → zeal 为正面热情

### 高一词汇

### 11. **abstruse** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：深奥的；难懂的
- **英文释义**：difficult to understand; obscure
- **例句**：
  - The philosopher’s lectures were too abstruse for beginners.
  - 这位哲学家的讲座对初学者过于深奥。
  - Legal scholars debated an abstruse clause in the treaty.
  - 法学学者辩论条约中一条深奥的条款。
- **助记**：abs（离开）+ trus（推）→ 推到远处难及 → 深奥

### 12. **burgeon** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：迅速增长；萌芽；蓬勃发展
- **英文释义**：to grow or increase rapidly; to flourish
- **例句**：
  - Tech startups burgeoned in the city after tax incentives.
  - 税收优惠后科技初创企业在该市蓬勃发展。
  - Doubts burgeoned in her mind as evidence appeared.
  - 随着证据出现，她心中的疑虑迅速增多。
- **助记**：bourgeon（法语嫩芽）→ 发芽生长 → 迅速发展

### 13. **chicanery** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：诡辩；欺骗手段；耍花招
- **英文释义**：the use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose
- **例句**：
  - The contract was voided because of financial chicanery.
  - 因财务欺诈合同被宣布无效。
  - Voters were tired of political chicanery during the campaign.
  - 选民厌倦了竞选中的政治花招。
- **助记**：chicane（设障弯道）→ 设局骗人 → 诡诈

### 14. **dichotomy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：二分法；截然对立；巨大分歧
- **英文释义**：a division into two contrasting parts or opinions
- **例句**：
  - The essay explores the dichotomy between tradition and innovation.
  - 文章探讨传统与创新的对立。
  - There is a false dichotomy between work and rest in popular culture.
  - 流行文化中工作与休息存在虚假二元对立。
- **助记**：dicho（一分为二）+ tomy（切）→ 二分

### 15. **ebullient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情洋溢的；奔放的；沸腾的（字面）
- **英文释义**：cheerful and full of energy; bubbling with enthusiasm
- **例句**：
  - The ebullient host kept the party lively until midnight.
  - 热情奔放的主人让派对一直热闹到午夜。
  - Her ebullient personality made her popular among classmates.
  - 她开朗热情的个性使她在同学中很受欢迎。
- **助记**：e（出）+ bull（沸）+ ient → 沸腾外溢 → 热情洋溢
