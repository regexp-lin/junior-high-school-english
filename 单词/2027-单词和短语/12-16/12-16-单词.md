## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **harangue** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：长篇大论地训斥；慷慨激昂的演说
- **英文释义**：to lecture someone at length in an aggressive and critical manner; a lengthy and aggressive speech
- **例句**：
  - The coach harangued the players for their lack of effort during the first half.
  - 教练因球员上半场缺乏努力而长篇训斥他们。
  - The crowd endured a lengthy harangue about moral values before the event began.
  - 活动开始前，人群忍受了一段关于道德价值观的冗长训话。
- **助记**：harangue 源自法语，原指在公众场合的长篇演讲 → 训斥

### 2. **intransigent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不妥协的；固执己见的；不让步的
- **英文释义**：unwilling or refusing to change one's views or to agree about something
- **例句**：
  - The intransigent negotiator refused to make any concessions during the peace talks.
  - 不妥协的谈判者在和平谈判中拒绝做出任何让步。
  - Her intransigent stance on the issue led to a prolonged deadlock.
  - 她在该问题上不让步的立场导致了长期的僵局。
- **助记**：in（不）+ transig（妥协）+ ent → 不妥协的

### 3. **vitiate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：损害；削弱；使无效
- **英文释义**：to spoil or impair the quality or efficiency of; to make legally invalid
- **例句**：
  - The presence of bias in the study vitiated the reliability of the results.
  - 研究中存在的偏见损害了结果的可靠性。
  - A procedural error vitiated the entire contract, rendering it unenforceable.
  - 一个程序错误使整个合同无效，无法执行。
- **助记**：viti（缺陷）+ ate → 使产生缺陷 → 损害

### 4. **perspicuous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：清晰的；明了的；表达清楚的
- **英文释义**：(of an account or representation) clearly expressed and easily understood
- **例句**：
  - The professor was known for her perspicuous explanations of complex theories.
  - 这位教授以对复杂理论清晰的解释而闻名。
  - A perspicuous writing style is essential for effective academic communication.
  - 清晰的写作风格对于有效的学术交流至关重要。
- **助记**：per（穿透）+ spicu（看）+ ous → 能穿透看清的 → 清晰的

### 5. **adulterate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：掺假；掺杂；降低品质
- **英文释义**：to render something poorer in quality by adding another substance
- **例句**：
  - The food manufacturer was fined for adulterating its products with cheaper ingredients.
  - 食品制造商因用便宜原料给产品掺假而被罚款。
  - Adulterated medicines pose a serious risk to public health.
  - 掺假的药品对公众健康构成严重风险。
- **助记**：adulter（污染/掺杂）+ ate → 使掺杂 → 掺假

### 6. **sycophant** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：马屁精；谄媚者；阿谀奉承的人
- **英文释义**：a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important in order to gain advantage
- **例句**：
  - The king was surrounded by sycophants who only told him what he wanted to hear.
  - 国王身边围满了只告诉他想听的话的马屁精。
  - A true leader values honest advisors over sycophants.
  - 真正的领导者重视诚实的顾问而非谄媚者。
- **助记**：syco（无花果）+ phant（显示）→ 显示无花果的人（古希腊典故）→ 告密者/谄媚者

### 7. **attenuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减弱；使稀薄；使变细
- **英文释义**：to reduce the force, effect, or value of
- **例句**：
  - The vaccine uses an attenuated form of the virus to build immunity.
  - 疫苗使用减弱形式的病毒来建立免疫力。
  - Distance attenuates the strength of the radio signal.
  - 距离会减弱无线电信号的强度。
- **助记**：at（加强）+ tenu（薄）+ ate → 使变薄 → 减弱

### 8. **recondite** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：深奥的；晦涩难懂的；冷僻的
- **英文释义**：(of a subject or knowledge) little known; abstruse
- **例句**：
  - The philosopher's recondite theories were understood by only a handful of scholars.
  - 这位哲学家深奥的理论只有少数学者能理解。
  - The book explores recondite aspects of medieval alchemy.
  - 这本书探讨了中世纪炼金术的冷僻方面。
- **助记**：re（回）+ cond（隐藏）+ ite → 隐藏回去的 → 深奥的

### 9. **obviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：消除；避免；使不必要
- **英文释义**：to remove a need or difficulty; to prevent or avoid
- **例句**：
  - Proper planning can obviate the need for last-minute changes.
  - 适当的规划可以消除最后一刻修改的需要。
  - The new software update obviated many of the security concerns.
  - 新的软件更新消除了许多安全顾虑。
- **助记**：ob（对面）+ vi（路）+ ate → 在路对面阻挡 → 消除/避免

### 10. **voracious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：贪婪的；如饥似渴的；食量极大的
- **英文释义**：wanting or devouring great quantities of food or having a very eager approach
- **例句**：
  - She was a voracious reader who finished a book almost every day.
  - 她是一个如饥似渴的读者，几乎每天读完一本书。
  - The voracious appetite of the growing teenager amazed his parents.
  - 正在长身体的青少年惊人的食量让父母惊叹。
- **助记**：vor（吞食）+ acious → 不断吞食的 → 贪婪的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **torschlusspanik** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：关门恐慌（害怕随着年龄增长机会减少的焦虑感）
- **英文释义**：a German word for the fear that time is running out to act; the anxiety of diminishing opportunities as one ages
- **例句**：
  - As she approached forty without having started a family, she experienced a growing sense of torschlusspanik.
  - 随着年近四十仍未成家，她感到日益增长的关门恐慌。
  - Torschlusspanik drives some people to make hasty life decisions they later regret.
  - 关门恐慌驱使一些人匆忙做出后来后悔的人生决定。
- **助记**：Tor（门）+ Schluss（关闭）+ Panik（恐慌）→ 门关上的恐慌（德语借词）

### 12. **sobremesa** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：饭后闲聊时光（饭后围坐在餐桌边聊天的时光）
- **英文释义**：a Spanish term for the time spent lingering at the table after a meal, chatting and enjoying the company
- **例句**：
  - In Spain, the sobremesa after Sunday lunch can last for hours, filled with stories and laughter.
  - 在西班牙，周日午餐后的饭后闲聊可以持续数小时，充满故事和欢笑。
  - The family cherished their sobremesa, considering it the most meaningful part of the meal.
  - 这个家庭珍视饭后闲聊的时光，认为这是用餐中最有意义的部分。
- **助记**：sobre（在…之上）+ mesa（桌子）→ 在桌子上方（饭后还在桌边）→ 饭后闲聊时光

### 13. **flaneur** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：城市漫步者；悠闲的观察者
- **英文释义**：an idle stroller; a person who walks the city in order to experience it
- **例句**：
  - Paris has long been the ideal city for the flaneur, with its wide boulevards and bustling cafes.
  - 巴黎一直是城市漫步者的理想城市，有着宽阔的林荫大道和繁忙的咖啡馆。
  - As a flaneur, he found inspiration in the everyday scenes of urban life.
  - 作为城市漫步者，他在城市生活的日常场景中找到灵感。
- **助记**：flaneur 是法语，flâner（闲逛）→ 在城市中悠闲漫步的人

### 14. **querencia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：安全之所；心灵归属地（感到安全和汲取力量的地方）
- **英文释义**：a Spanish term for a place from which one's strength is drawn; a place where one feels safe and at home
- **例句**：
  - The old family farm was her querencia — a place where she always felt grounded and strong.
  - 老家的农场是她的安全之所——一个让她总是感到踏实和坚强的地方。
  - Everyone needs a querencia, a sanctuary where the soul can rest and recharge.
  - 每个人都需要一个心灵归属地，一个灵魂可以休息和充电的避难所。
- **助记**：querencia 是西班牙语，querer（想要/爱）→ 想要回去的地方 → 安全之所

### 15. **eleutheromania** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：对自由的狂热渴望
- **英文释义**：an intense and irresistible desire for freedom
- **例句**：
  - His eleutheromania led him to quit his corporate job and travel the world as a digital nomad.
  - 他对自由的狂热渴望使他辞去了公司工作，作为数字游民环游世界。
  - The poet's eleutheromania is evident in every verse, celebrating liberation from convention.
  - 诗人对自由的狂热渴望在每一行诗中都显而易见，赞颂着从传统中解放出来。
- **助记**：eleuthero（自由）+ mania（狂热）→ 对自由的狂热 → 对自由的狂热渴望
