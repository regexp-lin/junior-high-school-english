## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **vindicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证明…有理；为…辩护；证实
- **英文释义**：to clear someone of blame or suspicion; to show that something is justified
- **例句**：
  - New evidence vindicated the suspect, proving he was innocent all along.
  - 新证据为嫌疑人洗清了嫌疑，证明他一直是无辜的。
  - The success of the project vindicated her decision to invest early.
  - 项目的成功证明了她早期投资的决定是正确的。
- **助记**：vind（力量/报复）+ icate → 用力量来正名 → 证明有理

### 2. **austere** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：朴素的；严格的；苦行的
- **英文释义**：severe or strict in manner or appearance; having no comforts or luxuries
- **例句**：
  - The monk lived an austere life, with few possessions and simple meals.
  - 这位僧侣过着朴素的生活，几乎没有财产，饮食简单。
  - The austere classroom had only wooden benches and a chalkboard.
  - 朴素的教室里只有木凳和一块黑板。
- **助记**：austere 联想 "奥斯特" → 像苦行僧般严格 → 朴素的

### 3. **capitulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：投降；屈服；让步
- **英文释义**：to cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; to surrender
- **例句**：
  - After months of siege, the fortress finally capitulated to the invading army.
  - 经过数月围攻，堡垒终于向入侵军队投降了。
  - She refused to capitulate to peer pressure and stood by her principles.
  - 她拒绝向同伴压力屈服，坚持自己的原则。
- **助记**：capit（头）+ ulate → 低头 → 投降

### 4. **prolific** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多产的；丰富的；大量的
- **英文释义**：producing much fruit, foliage, or many offspring; producing much creative work
- **例句**：
  - Shakespeare was one of the most prolific writers in English literature.
  - 莎士比亚是英国文学中最多产的作家之一。
  - The prolific apple tree yielded hundreds of fruits this season.
  - 这棵多产的苹果树这个季节结了数百个果实。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lif（生命）+ ic → 不断产生生命 → 多产的

### 5. **rescind** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：撤销；废除；取消
- **英文释义**：to revoke, cancel, or repeal a law, order, or agreement
- **例句**：
  - The company rescinded the job offer after discovering the candidate's false credentials.
  - 公司在发现候选人的虚假资历后撤销了工作邀请。
  - The government rescinded the outdated regulations to encourage innovation.
  - 政府废除了过时的法规以鼓励创新。
- **助记**：re（回）+ scind（切）→ 切回去 → 撤销

### 6. **ambivalent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：矛盾的；犹豫不决的
- **英文释义**：having mixed feelings or contradictory ideas about something
- **例句**：
  - She felt ambivalent about moving abroad — excited yet afraid of leaving home.
  - 她对出国感到矛盾——既兴奋又害怕离开家。
  - Many voters remain ambivalent about which candidate to support.
  - 许多选民对支持哪个候选人仍然犹豫不决。
- **助记**：ambi（两面）+ val（力量）+ ent → 两种力量拉扯 → 矛盾的

### 7. **exonerate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：免除罪责；宣布无罪；开脱
- **英文释义**：to absolve someone from blame or wrongdoing
- **例句**：
  - DNA evidence exonerated the man who had been wrongfully imprisoned for 20 years.
  - DNA 证据为被错误关押了 20 年的男子洗清了罪名。
  - The investigation exonerated the officer of any misconduct charges.
  - 调查免除了该官员的所有不当行为指控。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ oner（负担）+ ate → 移除负担 → 免除罪责

### 8. **gregarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱社交的；群居的；合群的
- **英文释义**：fond of company; sociable; living in flocks or communities
- **例句**：
  - His gregarious nature made him popular at school and in the workplace.
  - 他爱社交的性格使他在学校和职场都很受欢迎。
  - Penguins are gregarious animals that live in large colonies.
  - 企鹅是群居动物，生活在大群落中。
- **助记**：greg（群）+ arious → 喜欢成群 → 爱社交的

### 9. **impervious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不受影响的；不能渗透的
- **英文释义**：not allowing anything to pass through; unable to be affected by
- **例句**：
  - The new material is impervious to water and can withstand extreme temperatures.
  - 这种新材料不透水，能承受极端温度。
  - He seemed impervious to criticism, continuing his work without hesitation.
  - 他似乎不受批评影响，毫不犹豫地继续工作。
- **助记**：im（不）+ pervious（可渗透的）→ 不可渗透的

### 10. **venerate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：崇敬；敬仰；尊崇
- **英文释义**：to regard with great respect and reverence
- **例句**：
  - Many cultures venerate their elders for their wisdom and experience.
  - 许多文化崇敬长辈的智慧和经验。
  - The temple was built to venerate the ancient goddess of harvest.
  - 这座庙宇是为了敬仰古代的丰收女神而建的。
- **助记**：vener（尊敬）+ ate → 尊敬 → 崇敬

### 高一词汇

### 11. **schadenfreude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：幸灾乐祸；对他人不幸的快感
- **英文释义**：pleasure derived from another person's misfortune
- **例句**：
  - There was a hint of schadenfreude in his smile when his rival's project failed.
  - 当他对手的项目失败时，他的微笑中带着一丝幸灾乐祸。
  - Psychologists study schadenfreude as a complex emotion tied to envy and competition.
  - 心理学家将幸灾乐祸作为与嫉妒和竞争相关的复杂情感加以研究。
- **助记**：德语借词，Schaden（伤害）+ Freude（快乐）→ 因他人的伤害而快乐

### 12. **peripatetic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：巡回的；流动的；漫步的
- **英文释义**：traveling from place to place, especially working in various locations
- **例句**：
  - The peripatetic musician performed in a different city every night.
  - 这位巡回演出的音乐家每晚在不同的城市表演。
  - Her peripatetic childhood — moving every two years — made it hard to form lasting friendships.
  - 她漂泊的童年——每两年搬一次家——使她难以建立持久的友谊。
- **助记**：peri（周围）+ patet（走）+ ic → 到处走 → 巡回的

### 13. **insouciant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：漫不经心的；无忧无虑的
- **英文释义**：showing a casual lack of concern; carefree
- **例句**：
  - His insouciant attitude toward deadlines frustrated his team members.
  - 他对截止日期漫不经心的态度让团队成员感到沮丧。
  - She strolled through the park with an insouciant smile, unbothered by the rain.
  - 她带着无忧无虑的微笑漫步在公园里，不受雨水困扰。
- **助记**：in（不）+ souci（担心）+ ant → 不担心的 → 漫不经心的

### 14. **denouement** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：结局；收场；大团圆
- **英文释义**：the final part of a play, movie, or narrative in which matters are explained or resolved
- **例句**：
  - The novel's denouement revealed that the butler was the murderer all along.
  - 小说的结局揭示了管家一直都是凶手。
  - After months of political drama, the election results brought the denouement everyone anticipated.
  - 经过数月的政治闹剧，选举结果带来了大家期待的结局。
- **助记**：法语借词，dé（解开）+ noue（结）+ ment → 解开绳结 → 结局

### 15. **lachrymose** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱哭的；催人泪下的；悲伤的
- **英文释义**：tearful or given to weeping; inducing tears
- **例句**：
  - The lachrymose melody brought the audience to tears during the concert.
  - 催人泪下的旋律在音乐会上让观众流下了眼泪。
  - She became lachrymose whenever she recalled her childhood home.
  - 每当回忆起童年的家，她就变得泪眼婆娑。
- **助记**：lachry（泪）+ mose → 多泪的 → 爱哭的
