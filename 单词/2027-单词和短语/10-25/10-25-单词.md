## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **acclaim** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：赞扬；称赞；欢呼
- **英文释义**：enthusiastic and public praise; to praise enthusiastically
- **例句**：
  - The film received critical acclaim at international festivals.
  - 这部电影在国际电影节上获得了影评界的赞誉。
  - Critics acclaimed her latest novel as a masterpiece.
  - 评论家们称赞她的最新小说为杰作。
- **助记**：ac（向）+ claim（喊/宣称）→ 向某人大声喊好 → 赞扬

### 2. **bygone** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：过去的；以往的；往事
- **英文释义**：belonging to an earlier time; past events
- **例句**：
  - The museum displays tools from a bygone era.
  - 博物馆展示了过去时代的工具。
  - Let bygones be bygones and focus on the future.
  - 让往事过去吧，关注未来。
- **助记**：by（经过）+ gone（过去）→ 已经过去的 → 过去的

### 3. **confront** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：面对；正视；对抗
- **英文释义**：to face up to and deal with a problem or difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - We must confront the reality of climate change head-on.
  - 我们必须正面面对气候变化的现实。
  - The manager confronted the employee about the missing funds.
  - 经理就失踪的资金质问了员工。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ front（前面）→ 面对面 → 面对

### 4. **deplore** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：谴责；强烈反对；对……深感遗憾
- **英文释义**：to feel or express strong disapproval of something
- **例句**：
  - The United Nations deplored the use of violence against civilians.
  - 联合国谴责了对平民使用暴力的行为。
  - Environmentalists deplore the destruction of ancient forests.
  - 环保人士强烈反对毁坏古老森林。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ plore（哭泣）→ 令人痛哭 → 谴责

### 5. **exploit** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：利用；剥削；功绩
- **英文释义**：to use selfishly for profit; a bold or daring feat
- **例句**：
  - Some companies exploit cheap labor in developing countries.
  - 一些公司剥削发展中国家的廉价劳动力。
  - The explorer's exploits in the Arctic were legendary.
  - 探险家在北极的壮举是传奇性的。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ploit（折叠/展开）→ 展开利用 → 利用/功绩

### 6. **fortify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加强；设防；增强
- **英文释义**：to strengthen a place with defensive works; to invigorate
- **例句**：
  - The ancient city was fortified with thick walls and deep moats.
  - 古城用厚墙和深护城河设防。
  - Breakfast cereals are often fortified with vitamins and minerals.
  - 早餐麦片通常被加入维生素和矿物质以增强营养。
- **助记**：fort（强）+ ify → 使强大 → 加强

### 7. **genial** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：和蔼的；亲切的；温和的
- **英文释义**：friendly and cheerful; pleasantly warm
- **例句**：
  - The genial host made every guest feel at home.
  - 和蔼的主人让每位客人都感到宾至如归。
  - His genial personality made him popular among colleagues.
  - 他和蔼可亲的性格使他在同事中很受欢迎。
- **助记**：gen（产生）+ ial → 产生友善 → 和蔼的

### 8. **hefty** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：沉重的；可观的；强壮的
- **英文释义**：large, heavy, and powerful; (of an amount) considerable
- **例句**：
  - The movers struggled with the hefty piano up three flights of stairs.
  - 搬运工吃力地把沉重的钢琴搬上三层楼。
  - The company faced a hefty fine for violating safety regulations.
  - 公司因违反安全规定而面临可观的罚款。
- **助记**：heft（重量）+ y → 有重量的 → 沉重的

### 9. **impoverish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使贫穷；使枯竭；使贫瘠
- **英文释义**：to make someone poor; to exhaust the fertility or resources of
- **例句**：
  - The war impoverished the entire nation for decades.
  - 战争使整个国家贫穷了数十年。
  - Over-farming impoverished the once-fertile soil.
  - 过度耕作使曾经肥沃的土壤变得贫瘠。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ poverish（贫穷）→ 陷入贫穷 → 使贫穷

### 10. **meticulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；细致入微的；严谨的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - The watchmaker's meticulous work produced timepieces of rare beauty.
  - 钟表匠一丝不苟的工作制造出了罕见美丽的钟表。
  - Meticulous planning ensured the event ran smoothly.
  - 细致的规划确保了活动顺利进行。
- **助记**：metic（恐惧）+ ulous → 怕出错 → 一丝不苟

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ephemeral** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：短暂的；转瞬即逝的
- **英文释义**：lasting for a very short time; transient
- **例句**：
  - Fame on social media is often ephemeral, fading within days.
  - 社交媒体上的名气往往转瞬即逝，几天就消退了。
  - Cherry blossoms are beautiful but ephemeral, lasting only about two weeks.
  - 樱花美丽却短暂，只持续约两周。
- **助记**：epi（在……上）+ hemer（天）+ al → 只在一天之上 → 短暂的

### 12. **parochial** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：狭隘的；眼界窄的；地方性的
- **英文释义**：having a limited or narrow outlook; relating to a church parish
- **例句**：
  - His parochial views prevented him from appreciating other cultures.
  - 他狭隘的观点使他无法欣赏其他文化。
  - The company needs to move beyond its parochial focus on local markets.
  - 公司需要超越对本地市场的狭隘关注。
- **助记**：parish（教区）→ parochial → 局限于教区的 → 狭隘的

### 13. **quell** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：镇压；平息；消除
- **英文释义**：to put an end to a rebellion or disorder by force; to suppress
- **例句**：
  - The government sent troops to quell the uprising in the province.
  - 政府派军队镇压了省内的起义。
  - Deep breathing exercises can help quell anxiety before a big test.
  - 深呼吸练习可以帮助在大考前平息焦虑。
- **助记**：quell 联想 quiet（安静）→ 使安静 → 镇压/平息

### 14. **stolid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：迟钝的；麻木的；不动感情的
- **英文释义**：calm, dependable, and showing little emotion or animation
- **例句**：
  - The stolid guard showed no reaction to the prisoner's protests.
  - 麻木的守卫对囚犯的抗议没有任何反应。
  - His stolid expression masked the turmoil he felt inside.
  - 他冷漠的表情掩盖了内心的动荡。
- **助记**：stolid 联想 solid（坚固）→ 像石头般不动 → 迟钝

### 15. **vociferous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：大声疾呼的；喧闹的；激烈表达的
- **英文释义**：vehement or clamorous; expressing feelings loudly and forcefully
- **例句**：
  - Vociferous protests erupted outside the courthouse.
  - 法院外爆发了大声疾呼的抗议。
  - She was a vociferous advocate for animal rights.
  - 她是动物权利的大声疾呼者。
- **助记**：voci（声音）+ fer（带来）+ ous → 带来大声 → 大声疾呼的
