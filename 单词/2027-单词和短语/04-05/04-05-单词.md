## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **affection** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：喜爱；感情；钟爱
- **英文释义**：a gentle feeling of fondness or liking
- **例句**：
  - She showed great affection for her grandmother.
  - 她对祖母表现出了深厚的感情。
- **助记**：af（加强）+ fect（做/影响）+ ion → 对人产生的影响 → 感情

### 2. **brink** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：边缘；边沿
- **英文释义**：the extreme edge of land before a steep drop; a point at which something is about to happen
- **例句**：
  - The country was on the brink of war.
  - 国家处于战争的边缘。
- **助记**：brink → blink（眨眼）→ 眨一下眼就掉下去 → 边缘

### 3. **corridor** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：走廊；过道；通道
- **英文释义**：a long passage in a building from which doors lead into rooms
- **例句**：
  - The office is at the end of the corridor on the left.
  - 办公室在走廊尽头的左边。
- **助记**：corri（跑）+ dor → 在里面跑的通道 → 走廊

### 4. **endure** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：忍受；持久；持续
- **英文释义**：to suffer something painful or difficult patiently; to remain in existence
- **例句**：
  - The explorers had to endure extreme cold during the expedition.
  - 探险者在探险期间不得不忍受极寒。
- **助记**：en（使）+ dure（持续/硬）→ 使自己坚硬持续 → 忍受

### 5. **fragile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易碎的；脆弱的；纤细的
- **英文释义**：easily broken or damaged; delicate
- **例句**：
  - Handle the glass carefully — it's very fragile.
  - 小心拿这个杯子——非常易碎。
- **助记**：frag（碎）+ ile → 容易碎的 → 易碎的

### 6. **hymn** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：赞美诗；圣歌
- **英文释义**：a religious song or poem of praise
- **例句**：
  - The congregation sang hymns during the church service.
  - 会众在教堂礼拜时唱赞美诗。
- **助记**：hymn → him → 为"他"（上帝）唱的歌 → 赞美诗

### 7. **legacy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：遗产；遗留物；留给后人的东西
- **英文释义**：something left or handed down by a predecessor; an amount of money or property left in a will
- **例句**：
  - The artist left a rich legacy of paintings and sculptures.
  - 这位艺术家留下了丰富的绘画和雕塑遗产。
- **助记**：leg（法律）+ acy → 按法律留下来的 → 遗产

### 8. **nominee** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：被提名者；候选人
- **英文释义**：a person who is proposed or formally entered as a candidate for an office or position
- **例句**：
  - The nominee for the award gave a moving speech.
  - 该奖项的被提名者发表了感人的演讲。
- **助记**：nomin（名字）+ ee → 名字被提出来的人 → 被提名者

### 9. **precaution** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：预防措施；防备
- **英文释义**：a measure taken in advance to prevent something dangerous or unpleasant
- **例句**：
  - Take precautions against the cold weather by wearing warm clothes.
  - 穿暖和的衣服来预防寒冷天气。
- **助记**：pre（预先）+ caution（小心）→ 预先小心 → 预防措施

### 10. **siege** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：围攻；包围
- **英文释义**：a military operation in which forces surround a town or building
- **例句**：
  - The city was under siege for several months.
  - 这座城市被围攻了数月。
- **助记**：siege → sit（坐）→ 坐在城外围困 → 围攻

### 高一词汇

### 11. **auspicious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：吉利的；有前途的
- **英文释义**：conducive to success; favorable
- **例句**：
  - The sunny weather was an auspicious start to the new year.
  - 晴朗的天气为新年开了一个好头。
- **助记**：au（鸟）+ spic（看）+ ious → 看鸟的飞行方向占卜 → 吉利的

### 12. **complacent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：自满的；沾沾自喜的
- **英文释义**：showing smug or uncritical satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements
- **例句**：
  - We must not become complacent about our success.
  - 我们不能对自己的成功沾沾自喜。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ plac（满意）+ ent → 完全满意了 → 自满的

### 13. **elaborate** `高一` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：adj. 精致的；复杂的 v. 详细说明；精心制作
- **英文释义**：adj. involving many carefully arranged parts or details; v. to develop in detail
- **例句**：
  - She prepared an elaborate dinner for the guests.
  - 她为客人准备了一顿精心制作的晚餐。
- **助记**：e（出）+ labor（劳动）+ ate → 花了很多劳动做出来的 → 精致的

### 14. **nomenclature** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：命名法；术语；名称体系
- **英文释义**：the devising or choosing of names for things, especially in a science or other discipline
- **例句**：
  - The nomenclature of chemistry can be confusing for beginners.
  - 化学的命名法对初学者来说可能很混乱。
- **助记**：nomen（名字）+ clature（叫）→ 怎么叫名字的规则 → 命名法

### 15. **tangible** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有形的；可触摸的；切实的
- **英文释义**：perceptible by touch; clear and definite; real
- **例句**：
  - We need tangible evidence to support the theory.
  - 我们需要切实的证据来支持这一理论。
- **助记**：tang（触摸）+ ible → 可以触摸到的 → 有形的
