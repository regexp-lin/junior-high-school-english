## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abstain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：戒除；弃权；克制
- **英文释义**：to restrain oneself from doing or enjoying something; to formally decline to vote
- **例句**：
  - The doctor advised him to abstain from alcohol and fatty foods.
  - 医生建议他戒酒并避免油腻食物。
  - Three members abstained from the vote on the controversial proposal.
  - 三名成员在这项有争议的提案投票中弃权。
- **助记**：abs（远离）+ tain（持有）→ 远离不碰 → 戒除/弃权

### 2. **baffle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使困惑；使迷惑；难住
- **英文释义**：to totally bewilder or perplex someone
- **例句**：
  - The sudden disappearance of the airplane baffled investigators for years.
  - 飞机的突然失踪让调查人员困惑了多年。
  - The math problem baffled even the most talented students in the class.
  - 这道数学题连班上最有天赋的学生都被难住了。
- **助记**：baffle 联想 buffalo（水牛）→ 被水牛撞了一下，晕头转向 → 使困惑

### 3. **coherent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：连贯的；条理清楚的；一致的
- **英文释义**：logical and consistent; forming a unified whole
- **例句**：
  - The witness was so nervous that she couldn't give a coherent account of the events.
  - 证人太紧张了，无法对事件作出连贯的叙述。
  - A good essay should present a coherent argument from beginning to end.
  - 一篇好的文章应该从头到尾呈现连贯的论证。
- **助记**：co（共同）+ her（粘附）+ ent → 粘在一起的 → 连贯的/一致的

### 4. **denounce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：谴责；公开指责；揭发
- **英文释义**：to publicly declare to be wrong or evil; to inform against
- **例句**：
  - Human rights organizations denounced the government's treatment of political prisoners.
  - 人权组织谴责了政府对政治犯的待遇。
  - The scientist denounced the study as fraudulent and misleading.
  - 这位科学家谴责该研究是欺诈性的和误导性的。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ nounce（宣布）→ 向下宣布/公开批判 → 谴责

### 5. **elaborate** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：精心制作的；详尽的；详细阐述
- **英文释义**：involving many carefully arranged parts; to develop in detail
- **例句**：
  - The palace was decorated with elaborate carvings and golden ornaments.
  - 宫殿装饰着精心制作的雕刻和金色装饰品。
  - Could you elaborate on your proposal so we can better understand your plan?
  - 你能详细阐述一下你的提案，让我们更好地理解你的计划吗？
- **助记**：e（出）+ labor（劳动）+ ate → 付出大量劳动做出来的 → 精心制作的

### 6. **feeble** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚弱的；无力的；苍白的
- **英文释义**：lacking physical strength; failing to convince or impress
- **例句**：
  - The feeble old man struggled to climb the stairs without assistance.
  - 虚弱的老人在没有帮助的情况下艰难地爬楼梯。
  - His feeble excuse for missing the meeting convinced no one.
  - 他为缺席会议找的苍白借口没有说服任何人。
- **助记**：feeble 联想 fable（寓言）→ 像寓言里弱小的角色 → 虚弱的

### 7. **grim** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严酷的；冷峻的；令人沮丧的
- **英文释义**：very serious or gloomy; depressing or worrying to consider
- **例句**：
  - The grim reality of climate change demands immediate action from all nations.
  - 气候变化的严酷现实要求所有国家立即采取行动。
  - The doctor delivered the grim news with as much compassion as possible.
  - 医生尽可能充满同情心地告知了令人沮丧的消息。
- **助记**：grim 联想 Grimm（格林兄弟）→ 格林童话中有很多严酷的情节 → 严酷的

### 8. **impede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to delay or prevent something by obstructing it; to hinder
- **例句**：
  - Bureaucratic red tape often impedes the progress of important projects.
  - 官僚主义的繁文缛节经常阻碍重要项目的进展。
  - The fallen tree impeded traffic on the main road for several hours.
  - 倒下的树在主干道上阻碍了交通数小时。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ pede（脚）→ 绊住了脚 → 阻碍

### 9. **nominal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：名义上的；象征性的；微不足道的
- **英文释义**：existing in name only; very small; far below the real value
- **例句**：
  - The president is the nominal head of the organization, but the director makes all the decisions.
  - 总裁是组织名义上的领导，但所有决策都由主管做出。
  - They charged only a nominal fee for the service to make it accessible to everyone.
  - 他们只收取象征性的服务费，以使每个人都能负担得起。
- **助记**：nomin（名字）+ al → 只有名字的 → 名义上的

### 10. **resilient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有弹性的；能恢复的；坚韧的
- **英文释义**：able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions
- **例句**：
  - Children are often more resilient than adults give them credit for.
  - 孩子们往往比大人想象的更加坚韧。
  - The resilient economy recovered quickly from the financial crisis.
  - 这个有弹性的经济体从金融危机中迅速恢复。
- **助记**：re（回）+ sili（跳）+ ent → 能弹回来的 → 有弹性的/坚韧的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **antithesis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：对立面；对照；对立
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that is the direct opposite of someone or something else
- **例句**：
  - His quiet, reserved nature was the antithesis of his brother's outgoing personality.
  - 他安静内敛的性格是他兄弟外向性格的对立面。
  - War is the antithesis of everything that the peace movement stands for.
  - 战争是和平运动所代表的一切的对立面。
- **助记**：anti（反对）+ thesis（论点）→ 反论点 → 对立面

### 12. **draconian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：严厉的；严酷的；残暴的
- **英文释义**：excessively harsh or severe, especially of laws or their application
- **例句**：
  - The new traffic laws were criticized as draconian by many drivers.
  - 许多司机批评新交通法规过于严厉。
  - Some countries still enforce draconian punishments for minor offenses.
  - 一些国家仍然对轻微违法行为实施严酷的惩罚。
- **助记**：Draco（德拉古，古希腊严酷的立法者）+ nian → 像德拉古那样严酷的

### 13. **frivolous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：轻浮的；不重要的；无聊的
- **英文释义**：not having any serious purpose or value; carefree and superficial
- **例句**：
  - The judge dismissed the lawsuit as frivolous and a waste of the court's time.
  - 法官以诉讼轻浮且浪费法庭时间为由驳回了案件。
  - She spent her money on frivolous things instead of saving for the future.
  - 她把钱花在了无聊的东西上，而没有为未来存钱。
- **助记**：friv（轻浮）+ ol + ous → 轻浮的/不严肃的

### 14. **ostentatious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：炫耀的；招摇的；虚饰的
- **英文释义**：designed to attract notice or impress others; showy
- **例句**：
  - His ostentatious display of wealth made many people uncomfortable.
  - 他炫耀财富的行为让很多人感到不舒服。
  - The wedding was an ostentatious affair with hundreds of guests and lavish decorations.
  - 婚礼是一场招摇的盛事，有数百位宾客和奢华的装饰。
- **助记**：ostent（展示）+ atious → 大肆展示的 → 炫耀的

### 15. **vindicate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：证明……正确；为……辩护；平反
- **英文释义**：to clear of blame or suspicion; to show to be right or justified
- **例句**：
  - The new evidence vindicated the man who had been wrongly imprisoned for ten years.
  - 新证据为被错误监禁十年的男子平了反。
  - The success of the project vindicated her decision to invest in new technology.
  - 项目的成功证明了她投资新技术的决定是正确的。
- **助记**：vind（力量/惩罚）+ icate → 用力量还原正义 → 证明正确/平反
