## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **evoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：唤起；引起；使人想起
- **英文释义**：to bring or recall to the conscious mind; to elicit a response
- **例句**：
  - The old photograph evoked happy memories of her childhood.
  - 那张老照片唤起了她童年的美好回忆。
  - The musician's performance evoked a strong emotional response from the audience.
  - 音乐家的演出引起了观众强烈的情感共鸣。
- **助记**：e（出）+ voke（叫）→ 叫出来 → 唤起

### 2. **cumbersome** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：笨重的；累赘的；复杂的
- **英文释义**：large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry or use; unwieldy
- **例句**：
  - The old computer system was cumbersome and slow compared to modern software.
  - 旧的电脑系统与现代软件相比既笨重又缓慢。
  - The cumbersome application process discouraged many potential candidates.
  - 繁琐的申请流程让许多潜在候选人望而却步。
- **助记**：cumber（妨碍）+ some → 造成妨碍的 → 笨重的

### 3. **infringe** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：侵犯；违反；侵害
- **英文释义**：to actively break the terms of a law or agreement; to encroach on someone's rights
- **例句**：
  - Downloading pirated software infringes copyright laws.
  - 下载盗版软件侵犯了版权法。
  - The new regulation may infringe on citizens' freedom of speech.
  - 新规定可能侵犯公民的言论自由。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ fringe（边界）→ 进入别人的边界 → 侵犯

### 4. **sovereign** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：主权的；至高无上的；君主
- **英文释义**：possessing supreme or ultimate power; a supreme ruler
- **例句**：
  - Every nation has the right to protect its sovereign territory.
  - 每个国家都有权保护其主权领土。
  - The sovereign addressed the nation in a televised speech.
  - 君主在电视讲话中向全国发表讲话。
- **助记**：sover（在上面）+ eign → 在上面的统治者 → 主权的

### 5. **rectify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：纠正；改正；矫正
- **英文释义**：to put right; to correct something that is wrong
- **例句**：
  - The company promised to rectify the error in the billing statement.
  - 公司承诺纠正账单中的错误。
  - Steps were taken to rectify the situation before it worsened.
  - 在情况恶化之前采取了措施加以纠正。
- **助记**：rect（正确）+ ify → 使正确 → 纠正

### 6. **languish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：衰弱；憔悴；被忽视
- **英文释义**：to lose or lack vitality; to be forced to remain in an unpleasant state
- **例句**：
  - Many talented projects languish due to lack of funding.
  - 许多有才华的项目因缺乏资金而被搁置。
  - The prisoner languished in the dark cell for years.
  - 囚犯在黑暗的牢房中衰弱了多年。
- **助记**：langu（疲倦）+ ish → 感到疲倦 → 衰弱

### 7. **pivotal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：关键的；核心的；至关重要的
- **英文释义**：of crucial importance in relation to the development of something
- **例句**：
  - Her research played a pivotal role in the development of the vaccine.
  - 她的研究在疫苗的开发中起了关键作用。
  - The meeting was a pivotal moment in the company's history.
  - 这次会议是公司历史上的关键时刻。
- **助记**：pivot（枢纽）+ al → 像枢纽一样重要 → 关键的

### 8. **relinquish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：放弃；交出；让出
- **英文释义**：to voluntarily cease to keep or claim; to give up
- **例句**：
  - He was forced to relinquish control of the company to his business partner.
  - 他被迫将公司的控制权交给了商业伙伴。
  - She relinquished her seat on the bus to an elderly woman.
  - 她把公交车上的座位让给了一位老年妇女。
- **助记**：re（回）+ linqu（离开）+ ish → 离开回去 → 放弃

### 9. **succinct** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：简洁的；简明的；言简意赅的
- **英文释义**：briefly and clearly expressed
- **例句**：
  - The manager gave a succinct summary of the quarterly report.
  - 经理对季度报告做了简洁的总结。
  - Keep your answers succinct — the examiner doesn't have time for long essays.
  - 保持答案简明——考官没有时间看长篇大论。
- **助记**：suc（在下面）+ cinct（束紧）→ 紧束在一起 → 简洁的

### 10. **exacerbate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使恶化；加剧；使加重
- **英文释义**：to make a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling worse
- **例句**：
  - The drought was exacerbated by record-high temperatures.
  - 创纪录的高温加剧了旱情。
  - His rude comments only exacerbated the conflict between the two teams.
  - 他粗鲁的评论只会加剧两队之间的冲突。
- **助记**：ex（加强）+ acerb（尖锐/苦）+ ate → 使更尖锐 → 使恶化

### 高一词汇

### 11. **peripatetic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：巡回的；游历的；四处走动的
- **英文释义**：traveling from place to place, especially working or based in various places
- **例句**：
  - The peripatetic musician performed in cities across Europe.
  - 这位巡回音乐家在欧洲各城市演出。
  - She led a peripatetic lifestyle, never staying in one place for long.
  - 她过着四处游历的生活，从不在一个地方久留。
- **助记**：peri（周围）+ patetic（走）→ 四处走的 → 巡回的

### 12. **quixotic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不切实际的；空想的；充满浪漫幻想的
- **英文释义**：exceedingly idealistic; unrealistic and impractical
- **例句**：
  - His quixotic plan to solve world hunger by next year was met with skepticism.
  - 他明年解决世界饥饿问题的不切实际的计划遭到了质疑。
  - Despite being quixotic, her idealism inspired many young activists.
  - 尽管不切实际，她的理想主义激励了许多年轻活动家。
- **助记**：源自唐吉诃德（Don Quixote）→ 像唐吉诃德般的空想 → 不切实际的

### 13. **lugubrious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：悲伤的；愁眉苦脸的；阴郁的
- **英文释义**：looking or sounding sad and dismal
- **例句**：
  - The lugubrious tone of the funeral music added to the somber atmosphere.
  - 葬礼音乐悲伤的曲调更添了一份阴郁的氛围。
  - He wore a lugubrious expression throughout the entire ceremony.
  - 他在整个仪式上都是一副愁眉苦脸的表情。
- **助记**：lugubr（悲伤）+ ious → 悲伤的 → 愁眉苦脸的

### 14. **pellucid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：清澈的；透明的；易懂的
- **英文释义**：translucently clear; easily understood
- **例句**：
  - The pellucid waters of the lake revealed the colorful fish below.
  - 湖水清澈见底，可以看到下面五颜六色的鱼。
  - Her pellucid writing style makes complex scientific concepts accessible to everyone.
  - 她清晰的写作风格使复杂的科学概念人人可以理解。
- **助记**：pel（完全）+ lucid（清晰）→ 完全清晰的 → 清澈的

### 15. **ethos** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：精神气质；社会风气；民族精神
- **英文释义**：the characteristic spirit of a culture, era, or community as manifested in its beliefs
- **例句**：
  - The school's ethos encourages creativity, respect, and independent thinking.
  - 学校的精神气质鼓励创造力、尊重和独立思考。
  - The ethos of the company is centered around innovation and customer service.
  - 公司的精神以创新和客户服务为核心。
- **助记**：ethos 希腊语"品格/精神" → 精神气质
