## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **competent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有能力的；称职的；胜任的
- **英文释义**：having the necessary ability, knowledge, or skill to do something successfully
- **例句**：
  - She is a highly competent doctor who has earned the trust of her patients.
  - 她是一位能力出众的医生，赢得了患者的信任。
  - You need to be competent in at least two languages for this job.
  - 这份工作要求你至少精通两门语言。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ pet（追求）+ ent → 一起追求目标的能力 → 有能力的

### 2. **fluctuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：波动；起伏；变动
- **英文释义**：to rise and fall irregularly in number or amount
- **例句**：
  - Oil prices fluctuate depending on global supply and demand.
  - 石油价格随全球供需关系而波动。
  - Her mood tends to fluctuate dramatically throughout the day.
  - 她的情绪在一天中往往剧烈波动。
- **助记**：fluct（流动）+ uate → 像水一样流动起伏 → 波动

### 3. **subordinate** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：下级的；次要的；下属
- **英文释义**：lower in rank or position; a person under the authority of another
- **例句**：
  - In the army, soldiers must follow orders from their subordinate officers.
  - 在军队里，士兵必须服从上级军官的命令。
  - Personal interests should be subordinate to the goals of the team.
  - 个人利益应服从团队目标。
- **助记**：sub（下面）+ ordin（顺序）+ ate → 在顺序之下 → 下级的

### 4. **provoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激怒；挑衅；引起
- **英文释义**：to stimulate or incite someone to feel or act in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - His rude comments provoked an angry response from the audience.
  - 他粗鲁的评论引起了观众的愤怒回应。
  - The documentary provoked a public debate about climate change.
  - 这部纪录片引发了关于气候变化的公众讨论。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ voke（叫）→ 向前大叫 → 激怒

### 5. **rigorous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严格的；严密的；缜密的
- **英文释义**：extremely thorough, exhaustive, or accurate
- **例句**：
  - The university has a rigorous admission process that selects only the best students.
  - 该大学有严格的录取程序，只选拔最优秀的学生。
  - Scientific experiments require rigorous testing to ensure reliable results.
  - 科学实验需要严密的测试以确保可靠的结果。
- **助记**：rigor（严格）+ ous → 严格的

### 6. **gratitude** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：感激；感谢
- **英文释义**：the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation
- **例句**：
  - She expressed her deep gratitude to the firefighters who saved her home.
  - 她对拯救她家的消防员表达了深深的感激。
  - A simple thank-you note is a wonderful way to show gratitude.
  - 一张简单的感谢卡是表达感激的好方式。
- **助记**：grat（感谢）+ itude → 感谢的状态 → 感激

### 7. **allegiance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：忠诚；效忠；忠心
- **英文释义**：loyalty or commitment to a superior or to a group or cause
- **例句**：
  - Citizens pledged their allegiance to the flag during the ceremony.
  - 公民们在仪式上宣誓效忠国旗。
  - The knight swore allegiance to his king and vowed to protect the kingdom.
  - 骑士向国王宣誓效忠，并发誓保卫王国。
- **助记**：al + legi（法律）+ ance → 遵守法律 → 忠诚

### 8. **impede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to delay or prevent by obstructing; to hinder
- **例句**：
  - Heavy traffic impeded the ambulance's attempt to reach the hospital quickly.
  - 拥堵的交通妨碍了救护车快速到达医院。
  - Lack of proper equipment can impede the progress of scientific research.
  - 缺乏适当的设备会妨碍科研进展。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ ped（脚）→ 卡住脚 → 阻碍

### 9. **tangible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有形的；可触摸的；切实的
- **英文释义**：clear and definite; real; able to be touched or felt
- **例句**：
  - The company needs to show tangible results before investors will commit more funds.
  - 公司需要展示切实的成果，投资者才会投入更多资金。
  - There is no tangible evidence to support the claim.
  - 没有确凿的证据支持这一说法。
- **助记**：tang（触摸）+ ible → 可触摸的 → 有形的

### 10. **replenish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：补充；重新装满
- **英文释义**：to fill something up again; to restore to a former level
- **例句**：
  - We need to replenish our water supplies before continuing the hike.
  - 我们需要在继续徒步前补充水供给。
  - The lake is replenished by rainfall and underground springs.
  - 湖水由降雨和地下泉水补充。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ plen（满）+ ish → 再次装满 → 补充

### 高一词汇

### 11. **soliloquy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：独白；自言自语
- **英文释义**：an act of speaking one's thoughts aloud when alone, especially in a play
- **例句**：
  - Hamlet's "To be or not to be" is one of the most famous soliloquies in literature.
  - 哈姆雷特的"生存还是毁灭"是文学中最著名的独白之一。
  - The actress delivered a moving soliloquy that captivated the entire audience.
  - 女演员表演了一段感人的独白，迷住了全场观众。
- **助记**：soli（独自）+ loqu（说话）+ y → 独自说话 → 独白

### 12. **antithesis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：对立面；对照；对偶
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that is the direct opposite of something else
- **例句**：
  - His calm demeanor was the antithesis of her emotional outbursts.
  - 他冷静的举止与她情绪化的爆发形成对立。
  - The antithesis of love is not hate, but indifference.
  - 爱的对立面不是恨，而是冷漠。
- **助记**：anti（对立）+ thesis（论点）→ 对立的论点 → 对立面

### 13. **obfuscate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使困惑；使模糊；混淆
- **英文释义**：to make obscure, unclear, or unintelligible
- **例句**：
  - The politician tried to obfuscate the issue with complicated jargon.
  - 政客试图用复杂的术语混淆这个问题。
  - Adding too many details can obfuscate the main point of your argument.
  - 添加太多细节会模糊你论点的重点。
- **助记**：ob（覆盖）+ fusc（暗）+ ate → 使变暗 → 使模糊

### 14. **serendipity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：意外发现；机缘巧合
- **英文释义**：the occurrence of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way
- **例句**：
  - The discovery of penicillin was a case of pure serendipity.
  - 青霉素的发现纯粹是机缘巧合。
  - By serendipity, she found the perfect book she had been looking for on a random shelf.
  - 机缘巧合之下，她在一个随意的书架上找到了一直在寻找的完美书籍。
- **助记**：来自波斯童话《Serendip三王子》→ 意外发现美好事物 → 机缘巧合

### 15. **juxtapose** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：并列；并置；对比
- **英文释义**：to place close together or side by side for comparison or contrast
- **例句**：
  - The artist juxtaposed images of war and peace in a powerful exhibition.
  - 艺术家在一个震撼的展览中将战争与和平的图像并置。
  - The documentary juxtaposes the lives of the wealthy and the impoverished.
  - 这部纪录片将富人和穷人的生活进行了对比。
- **助记**：juxta（旁边）+ pose（放置）→ 放在旁边 → 并列
