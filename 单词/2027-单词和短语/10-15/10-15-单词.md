## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **affluent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：富裕的；丰富的；富足的
- **英文释义**：having a great deal of money; wealthy
- **例句**：
  - The affluent neighborhood had large houses with swimming pools.
  - 这个富裕的社区有带游泳池的大房子。
  - An affluent lifestyle does not guarantee happiness.
  - 富足的生活方式不保证幸福。
- **助记**：af（向）+ flu（流）+ ent → 财富流向 → 富裕的

### 2. **baffle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使困惑；难住；使迷惑
- **英文释义**：to totally bewilder or perplex
- **例句**：
  - The disappearance of the plane baffled investigators for years.
  - 飞机的失踪让调查人员困惑了多年。
  - This math puzzle has baffled even the brightest students.
  - 这道数学难题连最聪明的学生都被难住了。
- **助记**：baffle 联想 battle（战斗）→ 在战斗中迷失 → 使困惑

### 3. **contend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：竞争；争辩；声称
- **英文释义**：to struggle to surmount a difficulty; to assert something as a position
- **例句**：
  - Five teams are contending for the championship title this season.
  - 本赛季有五支队伍在争夺冠军。
  - Critics contend that the new policy will increase inequality.
  - 批评者声称新政策会加剧不平等。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ tend（伸展）→ 一起伸展争抢 → 竞争

### 4. **dwindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：缩小；减少；逐渐变弱
- **英文释义**：to diminish gradually in size, amount, or strength
- **例句**：
  - The town's population has dwindled from 5,000 to just 800.
  - 该镇人口已从5000人减少到只有800人。
  - Their savings dwindled quickly after both parents lost their jobs.
  - 父母双双失业后他们的积蓄迅速减少。
- **助记**：dwindle 联想 dwarf（矮小）→ 变矮小 → 缩小

### 5. **elicit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：引出；引起；诱出
- **英文释义**：to evoke or draw out a response, answer, or fact from someone
- **例句**：
  - The comedian's jokes elicited loud laughter from the audience.
  - 喜剧演员的笑话引来观众阵阵大笑。
  - Careful questioning can elicit the truth from a reluctant witness.
  - 仔细的提问可以从不情愿的证人那里诱出真相。
- **助记**：e（出）+ licit（引诱）→ 引出来 → 引出

### 6. **frugal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：节俭的；朴素的；花费少的
- **英文释义**：sparing or economical with regard to money or food
- **例句**：
  - A frugal lifestyle helped them save enough for a house deposit.
  - 节俭的生活方式帮助他们攒够了房屋首付。
  - Despite being wealthy, she remained frugal in her spending habits.
  - 尽管富有，她在消费习惯上依然节俭。
- **助记**：frug（果实）+ al → 只吃果实 → 简朴/节俭

### 7. **gallant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：英勇的；勇敢的；风度翩翩的
- **英文释义**：brave; heroic; chivalrously attentive to women
- **例句**：
  - The gallant firefighter rescued three people from the burning building.
  - 英勇的消防员从着火的大楼中救出三人。
  - He made a gallant effort to finish the race despite his injury.
  - 尽管受伤，他仍英勇地努力完成比赛。
- **助记**：gall（胆）+ ant → 有胆量的 → 英勇的

### 8. **hurdle** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：障碍；栏架；克服困难
- **英文释义**：an obstacle or difficulty; to jump over a barrier while running
- **例句**：
  - Language was the biggest hurdle for the immigrant family.
  - 语言是这个移民家庭面临的最大障碍。
  - She hurdled every challenge and graduated top of her class.
  - 她克服了每一个挑战，以班级第一名的成绩毕业。
- **助记**：hurdle → 跨栏 → 障碍

### 9. **invoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：援引；祈求；调用
- **英文释义**：to cite or appeal to as an authority; to call on for assistance
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer invoked a rarely used clause in the contract.
  - 律师援引了合同中一项很少使用的条款。
  - Ancient civilizations invoked their gods before going to battle.
  - 古代文明在出征前会祈求神灵保佑。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ voke（召唤）→ 召唤进来 → 援引/祈求

### 10. **lament** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：哀悼；悲叹；挽歌
- **英文释义**：to mourn or express sorrow for; a passionate expression of grief
- **例句**：
  - The poet wrote a lament for the soldiers who died in the war.
  - 诗人为战争中牺牲的士兵写了一首挽歌。
  - She lamented the loss of traditional craftsmanship in modern society.
  - 她悲叹现代社会中传统手工艺的消失。
- **助记**：lament 谐音"拉门特"→ 拉开门特别悲伤 → 悲叹

### 高一词汇

### 11. **malevolent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：恶意的；有恶意的；心怀恶意的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a wish to do evil to others
- **例句**：
  - The villain in the film had a malevolent grin that chilled the audience.
  - 电影中反派恶意的笑容让观众不寒而栗。
  - Malevolent gossip can destroy a person's reputation overnight.
  - 恶意的流言可以一夜之间毁掉一个人的名誉。
- **助记**：male（坏）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 坏的意愿 → 恶意的

### 12. **nascent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：新生的；初期的；萌芽的
- **英文释义**：just beginning to develop; not yet fully formed
- **例句**：
  - The nascent democracy faced many challenges in its first year.
  - 新生的民主制度在第一年面临诸多挑战。
  - The nascent tech industry in the region attracted global investors.
  - 该地区萌芽中的科技产业吸引了全球投资者。
- **助记**：nasc（出生）+ ent → 刚出生的 → 新生的

### 13. **obstinate** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：固执的；顽固的；不屈服的
- **英文释义**：stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action
- **例句**：
  - The obstinate negotiator refused every compromise offered.
  - 固执的谈判者拒绝了每一个妥协提议。
  - An obstinate stain on the carpet resisted all cleaning attempts.
  - 地毯上顽固的污渍抵抗了所有清洁尝试。
- **助记**：ob（对面）+ stin（站）+ ate → 站在对面不动 → 固执

### 14. **panacea** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：万灵药；灵丹妙药；万能之策
- **英文释义**：a solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases
- **例句**：
  - Technology is not a panacea for all of education's problems.
  - 技术不是解决所有教育问题的灵丹妙药。
  - Some people view exercise as a panacea for mental health issues.
  - 一些人把运动看作解决心理健康问题的万能之策。
- **助记**：pan（全部）+ acea（治愈）→ 治愈一切 → 万灵药

### 15. **quandary** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：困境；进退两难；窘境
- **英文释义**：a state of perplexity or uncertainty about what to do
- **例句**：
  - She was in a quandary about whether to accept the job offer abroad.
  - 她对是否接受海外工作邀请陷入两难。
  - The ethical quandary forced the doctor to weigh conflicting duties.
  - 伦理困境迫使医生权衡相互冲突的职责。
- **助记**：quandary 联想 quantity（数量）→ 选择太多 → 进退两难
