## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **choleric** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易怒的；暴躁的；（旧医）胆汁质的
- **英文释义**：bad-tempered or irritable; easily angered
- **例句**：
  - The choleric coach yelled at players for minor mistakes.
  - 暴躁的教练因小错就对球员大吼。
  - His choleric temperament made negotiations difficult.
  - 他易怒的性情使谈判困难重重。
- **助记**：choler（胆汁）+ ic → 胆汁过多 → 易怒

### 2. **cozen** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：欺骗；哄骗；诈取
- **英文释义**：to trick or deceive; to cheat
- **例句**：
  - Fraudsters cozened tourists into buying fake antiques.
  - 骗子哄骗游客购买假古董。
  - She felt cozened by the fine print in the contract.
  - 她觉得合同里的细则欺骗了自己。
- **助记**：cousin（堂亲）谐音联想不可靠→ 欺骗（助记仅供参考）

### 3. **cupidity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：贪婪；贪财；强烈贪欲
- **英文释义**：greed for money or possessions; avarice
- **例句**：
  - The scandal exposed the cupidity of officials who took bribes.
  - 丑闻揭露了官员受贿的贪婪。
  - Cupidity drove him to betray his closest friends.
  - 贪欲驱使他背叛最亲密的朋友。
- **助记**：Cupid（爱神）→ 欲望 → 贪婪

### 4. **declivity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：下坡；倾斜面；下倾的斜面
- **英文释义**：a downward slope; a descending surface
- **例句**：
  - The hikers carefully descended the steep declivity toward the valley.
  - 徒步者小心地走下通往山谷的陡坡。
  - A small stream ran along the declivity of the hill.
  - 小溪沿山坡下倾处流淌。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ cliv（倾斜）+ ity → 下坡

### 5. **demur** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：表示异议；反对；犹豫
- **英文释义**：to raise objections or show reluctance; an objection
- **例句**：
  - Several board members demurred at the sudden increase in executive pay.
  - 几名董事对高管薪酬的突然增加表示反对。
  - Without demur, she accepted the extra responsibilities.
  - 她毫无异议地接受了额外职责。
- **助记**：de（偏离）+ mur（延迟）→ 迟疑反对

### 6. **deride** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：嘲笑；嘲弄
- **英文释义**：to express contempt for; ridicule
- **例句**：
  - Critics derided the film as a waste of talent and money.
  - 批评者嘲笑这部电影浪费才华与金钱。
  - He was derided for his outdated ideas about education.
  - 他因过时的教育观念而受到嘲笑。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ ride（笑）→ 向下嘲笑

### 7. **desuetude** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：废弃；不用；过时
- **英文释义**：a state of disuse; the condition of being obsolete
- **例句**：
  - The old law fell into desuetude and was rarely enforced.
  - 旧法已废弃，很少执行。
  - Many customs have passed into desuetude in modern cities.
  - 许多习俗在现代城市中已废弃不用。
- **助记**：de（离开）+ sue（跟随）+ tude → 不再跟随使用 → 废弃

### 8. **dissemble** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：掩饰（真实意图）；伪装
- **英文释义**：to conceal one's true motives or feelings; to feign
- **例句**：
  - The spy dissembled friendship while gathering intelligence.
  - 间谍在收集情报时伪装成朋友。
  - He could not dissemble his disappointment when he heard the news.
  - 听到消息时他无法掩饰自己的失望。
- **助记**：dis（否定）+ semble（相似）→ 不显露真相 → 伪装

### 9. **distrait** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：心不在焉的；注意力不集中的
- **英文释义**：distracted or absent-minded due to worry
- **例句**：
  - She seemed distrait during the meeting, staring at her phone.
  - 开会时她显得心不在焉，盯着手机。
  - His distrait manner worried his parents before the exam results.
  - 成绩公布前他心神不宁的样子让父母担忧。
- **助记**：dis（分离）+ trait（拉）→ 心思被拉走 → 心不在焉

### 10. **dolorous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：悲哀的；忧伤的；令人痛苦的
- **英文释义**：feeling or expressing great sorrow or distress
- **例句**：
  - The choir sang a dolorous hymn at the memorial service.
  - 合唱团在追悼仪式上唱了一首悲伤的赞美诗。
  - A dolorous silence filled the room after the verdict was read.
  - 宣判后，房间里一片悲哀的沉默。
- **助记**：dolor（痛苦）+ ous → 充满痛苦的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **elegiac** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：挽歌的；哀伤怀旧的
- **英文释义**：relating to or characteristic of an elegy; mournful
- **例句**：
  - The novel has an elegiac tone about a lost rural way of life.
  - 小说对逝去的乡村生活方式带着哀伤怀旧的笔调。
  - She wrote an elegiac poem after her grandmother passed away.
  - 祖母去世后她写了一首挽歌风格的诗。
- **助记**：elegy（挽歌）+ ac → 挽歌式的

### 12. **encomium** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：颂词；热烈赞美
- **英文释义**：a speech or piece of writing that praises someone highly
- **例句**：
  - The retiring professor received encomiums from former students worldwide.
  - 退休教授收到了来自世界各地前学生的热烈赞美。
  - The book jacket was covered with encomiums from famous authors.
  - 书封上满是名作家的赞誉之词。
- **助记**：en（进入）+ com（宴会）+ ium → 宴会上致辞赞美 → 颂词

### 13. **ennui** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：厌倦；无聊；倦怠感
- **英文释义**：a feeling of listlessness and dissatisfaction arising from lack of occupation or excitement
- **例句**：
  - Wealthy aristocrats in the novel often suffered from ennui.
  - 小说中的贵族常常感到厌倦无聊。
  - She complained of ennui during the long summer break.
  - 漫长暑假里她抱怨无聊。
- **助记**：法语 ennui → 厌倦

### 14. **ersatz** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：代用的；人造的；劣质的替代品
- **英文释义**：substitute; artificial and inferior in quality
- **例句**：
  - During the war, people baked bread with ersatz flour made from potatoes.
  - 战争期间人们用土豆制成的代用面粉烤面包。
  - The jacket was made of ersatz leather that peeled within a month.
  - 夹克是人造革，一个月内就脱皮了。
- **助记**：德语“替代品”→ 代用品

### 15. **eschew** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：刻意避免；戒绝
- **英文释义**：to deliberately avoid; to abstain from
- **例句**：
  - She eschewed social media to focus on her dissertation.
  - 为专心写论文，她刻意不用社交媒体。
  - Traditional monks eschew worldly pleasures.
  - 传统僧侣戒绝尘世享乐。
- **助记**：es（外）+ chew（咀嚼）→ 不往嘴里嚼 → 回避
