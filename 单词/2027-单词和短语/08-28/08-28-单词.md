## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **improvise** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：即兴创作；临时凑合；即兴表演
- **英文释义**：to create or perform spontaneously without preparation; to make do with available materials
- **例句**：
  - The band had to improvise when the guitarist broke a string.
  - 吉他弦断了，乐队只好即兴发挥。
  - We improvised a shelter from branches and plastic sheets.
  - 我们用树枝和塑料布临时搭了个遮蔽所。
- **助记**：im（不）+ pro（预先）+ vise（看见）→ 不预先看见剧本 → 即兴

### 2. **jargon** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：行话；术语；难懂的话
- **英文释义**：special words or expressions used by a profession or group that are difficult for others to understand
- **例句**：
  - Medical jargon can confuse patients without a glossary.
  - 没有术语表，医学行话会让病人困惑。
  - The manual was full of technical jargon about routers and firewalls.
  - 手册里满是关于路由器与防火墙的技术术语。
- **助记**：jargon 联想 jar（罐子）里装的专业术语 → 行话

### 3. **kinetic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：运动的；动力学的；活跃的
- **英文释义**：relating to or resulting from motion; dynamic
- **例句**：
  - The sculpture included kinetic parts that moved in the wind.
  - 雕塑包含随风而动的运动部件。
  - Kinetic energy increases as the car accelerates downhill.
  - 汽车下坡加速时动能增加。
- **助记**：kine（运动，如 cinema 映画）+ tic → 运动的

### 4. **lampoon** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：讽刺；嘲讽文章；滑稽模仿
- **英文释义**：to publicly criticize or ridicule someone or something by mockery; such a satire
- **例句**：
  - The cartoon lampooned the politician's exaggerated promises.
  - 这幅漫画讽刺了政客夸大的承诺。
  - The magazine was famous for its sharp political lampoons.
  - 该杂志以尖锐的政治讽刺闻名。
- **助记**：lampoon 联想 lamp（灯）照出丑态 → 讽刺

### 5. **mirth** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：欢笑；高兴；欢乐的气氛
- **英文释义**：amusement, especially as expressed in laughter
- **例句**：
  - The comedian's jokes filled the room with mirth.
  - 喜剧演员的笑话让满场欢笑。
  - Holiday gatherings were occasions of food and mirth.
  - 节日聚会是美食与欢笑的时刻。
- **助记**：mirth 联想 merry（欢乐的）→ 欢笑

### 6. **novice** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：新手；初学者；见习修士/修女
- **英文释义**：a person new to and inexperienced in a field or situation
- **例句**：
  - The skiing course is designed for novices who have never worn boots.
  - 滑雪课程是为从未穿雪靴的新手设计的。
  - As a novice driver, she avoided highways at night.
  - 作为新手司机，她夜间不上高速。
- **助记**：nov（新，如 novel）+ ice → 新人 → 新手

### 7. **opaque** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不透明的；难理解的；晦涩的
- **英文释义**：not able to be seen through; not transmitting light; hard to understand
- **例句**：
  - The bathroom window is opaque for privacy.
  - 浴室窗户为隐私做成不透明的。
  - His explanation remained opaque to most listeners.
  - 他的解释对大多数听众仍很晦涩。
- **助记**：op（反）+ aque（水）→ 不像水那样透 → 不透明的

### 8. **plight** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：困境；苦境；艰难处境
- **英文释义**：a dangerous, difficult, or otherwise unfortunate situation
- **例句**：
  - The refugees described the plight of families fleeing the war.
  - 难民描述了逃离战争家庭的困境。
  - Charities work to ease the plight of the homeless in winter.
  - 慈善机构努力缓解流浪者在冬天的苦境。
- **助记**：plight 联想 polite 反义？或 pledge（誓约）→ 陷入困境的誓约 → 困境

### 9. **quibble** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：吹毛求疵；为琐事争辩；诡辩
- **英文释义**：to argue or raise objections about a trivial matter; a petty objection
- **例句**：
  - Let's not quibble over a few dollars on the bill.
  - 我们别为账单上几块钱斤斤计较了。
  - His speech was full of quibbles rather than clear answers.
  - 他的演讲满是诡辩而非明确回答。
- **助记**：quib（谁，拉丁）→ 纠缠细枝末节 → 吹毛求疵

### 10. **rhetoric** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：修辞；雄辩；花言巧语
- **英文释义**：the art of effective speaking or writing; language designed to persuade, sometimes empty
- **例句**：
  - The candidate's rhetoric about change excited young voters.
  - 候选人关于变革的雄辩激发了年轻选民。
  - Critics dismissed the plan as empty rhetoric without action.
  - 批评者认为该计划只是没有行动的空洞辞藻。
- **助记**：rhetor（演说家）+ ic → 修辞学

### 高一词汇

### 11. **synergy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：协同效应；合力大于部分之和
- **英文释义**：the interaction of elements that when combined produce a total effect greater than the sum of individual parts
- **例句**：
  - The merger aimed to create synergy between marketing and research teams.
  - 合并旨在在市场与研发团队之间产生协同效应。
  - Synergy between the two musicians produced a unique sound.
  - 两位乐手之间的配合产生了独特音响。
- **助记**：syn（共同）+ erg（工作）→ 一起工作增效 → 协同

### 12. **turmoil** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：动荡；骚乱；极度混乱
- **英文释义**：a state of great disturbance, confusion, or uncertainty
- **例句**：
  - The country was in political turmoil after the election dispute.
  - 选举争议后该国陷入政治动荡。
  - Emotional turmoil made it hard for her to concentrate.
  - 情绪动荡使她难以集中注意力。
- **助记**：tur（转）+ moil（骚动）→ 翻腾骚动 → 动荡

### 13. **utilitarian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：功利主义的；实用的；追求最大效用的
- **英文释义**：designed to be useful rather than attractive; relating to utilitarianism
- **例句**：
  - The office furniture was utilitarian but comfortable.
  - 办公室家具实用而舒适。
  - A utilitarian approach weighs costs and benefits for the greatest good.
  - 功利主义方法权衡成本与收益以实现最大善。
- **助记**：utility（效用）+ arian → 重效用的 → 功利主义的

### 14. **vicarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：间接体验的；代理的；感同身受的
- **英文释义**：experienced in the imagination through the feelings or actions of another person
- **例句**：
  - Parents feel vicarious pride when their children succeed.
  - 孩子成功时父母会感到替代的骄傲。
  - He lived vicariously through travel blogs he read online.
  - 他通过网上读的旅行博客间接体验世界。
- **助记**：vicar（代理人）+ ious → 替代体验 → 间接的

### 15. **wrought** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：锻造的；精心制作的；（与修饰语连用）处于……状态
- **英文释义**：worked into shape by hammering or effort; made delicately; (in compounds) brought to a state
- **例句**：
  - The gates were made of wrought iron with floral patterns.
  - 大门由带花纹的锻铁制成。
  - She was wrought up before the interview and could not sit still.
  - 面试前她情绪激动，坐立不安。
- **助记**：work 的过去分词 archaic → wrought iron 锻铁
