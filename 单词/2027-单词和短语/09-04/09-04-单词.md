## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **ominous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不祥的；预兆的；威胁的
- **英文释义**：giving the worrying impression that something bad is going to happen
- **例句**：
  - Dark, ominous clouds gathered on the horizon before the storm.
  - 暴风雨前，阴沉不祥的乌云在地平线聚集。
  - His ominous silence made everyone in the room nervous.
  - 他令人不安的沉默让房间里每个人都紧张起来。
- **助记**：omin（预兆）+ ous → 带有坏兆头的 → 不祥的

### 2. **paradox** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：悖论；自相矛盾的说法；似非而是的事
- **英文释义**：a seemingly absurd or self-contradictory statement that when investigated may prove true
- **例句**：
  - The paradox of modern life is that we are more connected yet lonelier than ever.
  - 现代生活的悖论是：我们联系更紧密，却比以往更孤独。
  - Zeno's paradoxes puzzled philosophers for centuries.
  - 芝诺悖论困扰了哲学家数百年。
- **助记**：para（相对）+ dox（意见）→ 相对立的观点 → 悖论

### 3. **quintessential** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：最典型的；精髓的；完美的范例
- **英文释义**：representing the most perfect example of a quality or class
- **例句**：
  - Paris is the quintessential city for lovers of art and architecture.
  - 巴黎是热爱艺术与建筑的人心目中最典型的城市。
  - He was the quintessential English gentleman in manners and dress.
  - 无论举止还是衣着，他都是典型的英国绅士。
- **助记**：quint（五，第五要素）+ essential → 第五元素/精华 → 最典型的

### 4. **refute** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：驳斥；证明……错误
- **英文释义**：to prove a statement or theory to be wrong or false
- **例句**：
  - The scientist refuted the claim with carefully designed experiments.
  - 这位科学家用精心设计的实验驳斥了该说法。
  - His lawyer refuted every accusation with documentary evidence.
  - 他的律师用书面证据驳回了每一项指控。
- **助记**：re（回）+ fut（打）→ 打回去 → 驳斥

### 5. **stagnant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：停滞的；不流动的；萧条的
- **英文释义**：showing no activity; dull and sluggish; not flowing
- **例句**：
  - Stagnant water in the pond became a breeding ground for mosquitoes.
  - 池塘里的死水成了蚊子的孳生地。
  - The economy remained stagnant despite several stimulus packages.
  - 尽管出台了几轮刺激计划，经济仍然停滞不前。
- **助记**：stagn（站立不动）+ ant → 停滞的

### 6. **superfluous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多余的；不必要的；过剩的
- **英文释义**：unnecessary, especially through being more than enough
- **例句**：
  - Remove superfluous words to make your essay more concise.
  - 删去多余词语，使文章更简洁。
  - In a crisis, superfluous luxury goods are the first to be cut from budgets.
  - 危机中，多余的奢侈品最先从预算中砍掉。
- **助记**：super（超过）+ flu（流）→ 流得过多 → 多余的

### 7. **tenacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽强的；固执的；附着力强的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent
- **例句**：
  - Tenacious climbers finally reached the summit after three failed attempts.
  - 顽强的登山者在三次失败后终于登顶。
  - The vine's tenacious roots cracked the concrete sidewalk.
  - 藤蔓坚韧的根须撑裂了水泥人行道。
- **助记**：ten（握住）+ acious → 紧握不放的 → 顽强的

### 8. **unfounded** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无事实根据的；无理由的；站不住脚的
- **英文释义**：having no foundation or basis in fact
- **例句**：
  - The rumors about her resignation were completely unfounded.
  - 关于她辞职的传言完全没有根据。
  - The court dismissed the unfounded allegations against the defendant.
  - 法庭驳回了对被告毫无根据的指控。
- **助记**：un（无）+ founded（有根据的）→ 无根据的

### 9. **vicarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：间接感受到的；代理的；替代的
- **英文释义**：experienced in the imagination through the feelings or actions of another person
- **例句**：
  - Parents often feel vicarious pride when their children win competitions.
  - 子女赢得比赛时，父母常感到与有荣焉。
  - He lived vicariously through adventure novels he read every night.
  - 他每晚读冒险小说，在想象中体验另一种人生。
- **助记**：vicar（代理人）+ ious → 替代体验的 → 间接的

### 10. **warrant** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：使……有必要；保证；授权令
- **英文释义**：to justify or necessitate; a document authorizing police to search or arrest
- **例句**：
  - The seriousness of the threat warrants immediate government action.
  - 威胁的严重性使政府有必要立即采取行动。
  - The officer obtained a warrant before searching the suspect's apartment.
  - 警官搜查嫌疑人公寓前取得了搜查令。
- **助记**：warr（保护）+ ant → 授权保护执法 → 授权令/使正当

### 高一词汇

### 11. **alacrity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：敏捷；乐意；爽快
- **英文释义**：brisk and cheerful readiness; eagerness
- **例句**：
  - The volunteers accepted the difficult task with alacrity.
  - 志愿者们欣然接受了这项艰巨任务。
  - She responded to the invitation with surprising alacrity.
  - 她异常爽快地回应了邀请。
- **助记**：alac（活泼）+ rity → 活泼爽快 → 敏捷乐意

### 12. **besiege** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：围攻；围困；不断打扰
- **英文释义**：to surround with armed forces; to crowd around in an overwhelming way
- **例句**：
  - Medieval armies besieged the castle for months until food ran out.
  - 中世纪军队围困城堡数月，直到粮食耗尽。
  - Reporters besieged the celebrity as soon as she stepped out of the hotel.
  - 名人一出酒店就被记者团团围住。
- **助记**：be（加强）+ siege（包围）→ 围攻

### 13. **conundrum** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：难题；谜语；复杂问题
- **英文释义**：a confusing and difficult problem or question
- **例句**：
  - Climate change presents a moral conundrum for policymakers worldwide.
  - 气候变化给全球决策者出了一道伦理难题。
  - Solving the conundrum required cooperation across several scientific disciplines.
  - 解开这个难题需要多个学科合作。
- **助记**：谐音“难题”→ 难解之谜 → conundrum

### 14. **debilitate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使衰弱；削弱；使无力
- **英文释义**：to make someone or something weak and infirm
- **例句**：
  - The disease debilitated him so much that he could barely walk.
  - 疾病使他极度虚弱，几乎无法行走。
  - Chronic stress can debilitate both mental and physical health.
  - 长期压力会削弱身心健康。
- **助记**：de（去掉）+ bilit（力量）→ 去掉力量 → 使衰弱

### 15. **extrapolate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：外推；推断；推知
- **英文释义**：to extend a method or conclusion to an unknown situation by assuming that existing trends will continue
- **例句**：
  - Economists extrapolate future growth from past five years of data.
  - 经济学家根据过去五年的数据外推未来增长。
  - Be careful when you extrapolate small samples to the whole population.
  - 用小样本外推到总体时要谨慎。
- **助记**：extra（外）+ polate（推）→ 向外推算 → 外推
