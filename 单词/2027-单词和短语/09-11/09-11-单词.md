## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **pragmatic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；实用主义的；重实效的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things in a practical way rather than according to ideals or theories
- **例句**：
  - A pragmatic approach helped the team finish the project on time and under budget.
  - 务实的做法帮助团队按时并在预算内完成了项目。
  - She is pragmatic about career choices and focuses on long-term stability.
  - 她在职业选择上很务实，注重长期稳定。
- **助记**：pragma（希腊语“行为/事务”）+ tic → 注重实际做法的 → 务实的

### 2. **resilient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有韧性的；能迅速恢复的；适应力强的
- **英文释义**：able to recover quickly from difficulties; tough and flexible
- **例句**：
  - Children are often more resilient than adults expect after a setback.
  - 遭遇挫折后，孩子往往比大人想象的更有韧性。
  - The local economy proved resilient despite the global downturn.
  - 尽管全球经济下滑，当地经济仍表现出很强的韧性。
- **助记**：re（再）+ sil（跳，如 salient）→ 跌倒后再跳起来 → 有韧性的

### 3. **obscure** `初三` v./adj.

- **中文释义**：使模糊；遮掩；晦涩难懂的；默默无闻的
- **英文释义**：to keep from being seen; not discovered or known about; unclear
- **例句**：
  - Fog began to obscure the road ahead, forcing drivers to slow down.
  - 雾开始遮住前方的路，迫使司机减速。
  - The meaning of the poem remains obscure without historical context.
  - 没有历史背景，这首诗的含义仍然晦涩难懂。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ scure（盖）→ 盖住 → 使模糊/晦涩

### 4. **obsolete** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过时的；已废弃的；被淘汰的
- **英文释义**：no longer produced or used; out of date
- **例句**：
  - Many skills that were essential decades ago are now obsolete.
  - 许多几十年前必不可少的技能如今已经过时。
  - The factory replaced obsolete machines with more efficient models.
  - 工厂用更高效的机型更换了过时的机器。
- **助记**：ob（对抗）+solete（习惯使用）→ 不再被使用 → 过时的

### 5. **ponder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：深思；仔细考虑；琢磨
- **英文释义**：to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
- **例句**：
  - He paused to ponder whether the risk was worth taking.
  - 他停下来仔细琢磨是否值得冒这个险。
  - Philosophers have pondered the nature of happiness for centuries.
  - 几个世纪以来，哲学家们一直在思考幸福的本质。
- **助记**：pond（重量）→ 心里“称重”衡量 → 深思

### 6. **prudent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：谨慎的；精明的；深谋远虑的
- **英文释义**：acting with care and thought for the future; sensible and careful
- **例句**：
  - It is prudent to save money before making a large purchase.
  - 在进行大额消费之前存钱是谨慎的做法。
  - A prudent investor diversifies rather than putting all eggs in one basket.
  - 精明的投资者会分散投资，而不是孤注一掷。
- **助记**：prud（谨慎，如 prudence）+ ent → 谨慎的

### 7. **scrutinize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：细查；仔细审视；认真检查
- **英文释义**：to examine or look at something carefully and thoroughly
- **例句**：
  - The committee will scrutinize every detail of the proposal.
  - 委员会将仔细审查提案的每一个细节。
  - Journalists scrutinize official statements for inconsistencies.
  - 记者们仔细核对官方声明中的前后矛盾之处。
- **助记**：scrutin（细查）+ ize → 使被细查 → 审视

### 8. **tangible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有形的；可触摸的；切实的；明显的
- **英文释义**：clear and definite; real and able to be touched or experienced
- **例句**：
  - The team wanted tangible results within the first quarter.
  - 团队希望第一季度内看到切实的成果。
  - There is no tangible evidence linking him to the crime.
  - 没有确凿证据将他与这起犯罪联系起来。
- **助记**：tang（接触，如 tangent）+ ible → 可接触的 → 有形的/切实的

### 9. **ubiquitous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；十分普遍的
- **英文释义**：seeming to be everywhere; very common
- **例句**：
  - Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern cities.
  - 智能手机在现代城市中已无处不在。
  - Plastic packaging is so ubiquitous that it harms the environment.
  - 塑料包装如此普遍，以至于危害环境。
- **助记**：ubi（哪里）+ quit（去）+ ous → 无论去哪里都有 → 无处不在的

### 10. **volatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易变的；不稳定的；（化学品）易挥发的
- **英文释义**：likely to change suddenly and unpredictably; easily evaporating
- **例句**：
  - The stock market can be volatile during election seasons.
  - 选举季股市可能波动剧烈。
  - Store volatile liquids in a cool, ventilated place.
  - 将易挥发液体存放在阴凉通风处。
- **助记**：volat（飞，如 volley）+ ile → 易“飞走”的 → 易挥发/易变

### 高一词汇

### 11. **juxtapose** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：并置；把……并列（以对比）
- **英文释义**：to place two things side by side, especially for comparison or contrast
- **例句**：
  - The essay juxtaposes rural poverty with urban wealth.
  - 这篇文章将农村贫困与城市财富并置对照。
  - The artist likes to juxtapose bright colors with dark shadows.
  - 这位艺术家喜欢将明亮色彩与深色阴影并置。
- **助记**：juxta（靠近）+ pose（放置）→ 放在一起 → 并置

### 12. **mitigate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和；使不那么严重
- **英文释义**：to make something less harmful, serious, or painful
- **例句**：
  - Trees can mitigate the urban heat island effect in summer.
  - 树木可以减轻夏季城市热岛效应。
  - Wearing a mask may mitigate the spread of respiratory viruses.
  - 佩戴口罩可能减缓呼吸道病毒的传播。
- **助记**：mitis（温和）+ ate → 使变温和 → 减轻

### 13. **nebulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：模糊不清的；云状的；朦胧的
- **英文释义**：unclear and lacking definite form; vague
- **例句**：
  - The contract’s nebulous wording led to disputes between the parties.
  - 合同中含糊不清的措辞导致双方产生争议。
  - Her plans for the future remained nebulous after graduation.
  - 毕业后她对未来的打算仍然模糊不清。
- **助记**：nebula（星云）+ ous → 像星云一样朦胧 → 模糊不清的

### 14. **ostracize** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：排斥；放逐；使受排挤
- **英文释义**：to exclude someone from a group or society; to shun
- **例句**：
  - He was ostracized by colleagues after reporting the fraud.
  - 举报欺诈行为后，他遭到同事排挤。
  - Societies that ostracize dissenters may silence valuable criticism.
  - 排斥异见者的社会可能会压制有价值的批评。
- **助记**：古希腊 ostrakon（陶片）→ 陶片放逐 → 排斥

### 15. **perfunctory** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：敷衍的；例行公事的；草率的
- **英文释义**：done quickly and without real interest or effort
- **例句**：
  - He gave a perfunctory nod and returned to his phone.
  - 他敷衍地点了点头，又去看手机了。
  - The inspection was so perfunctory that hazards were missed.
  - 检查如此草率，以至于隐患被漏掉了。
- **助记**：per（通过）+ funct（完成）+ ory → 只求做完不求做好 → 敷衍的
