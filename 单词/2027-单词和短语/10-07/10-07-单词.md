## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **benevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；乐善好施的；善意的
- **英文释义**：well meaning and kindly; serving charitable rather than profit-making purposes
- **例句**：
  - A benevolent donor funded the new school library.
  - 一位慈善捐助者出资修建了新校图书馆。
  - She spoke in a benevolent tone to the nervous beginner.
  - 她以和善语气对紧张的新手说话。
- **助记**：bene（好）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 好意的 → 仁慈

### 2. **circumspect** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：谨慎小心的；考虑周全的
- **英文释义**：wary and unwilling to take risks; careful to consider circumstances
- **例句**：
  - Investors became circumspect after the market crash.
  - 股市暴跌后投资者变得谨慎。
  - A circumspect leader listens before making public promises.
  - 谨慎的领导者先倾听再公开承诺。
- **助记**：circum（周围）+ spect（看）→ 环顾四周 → 谨慎

### 3. **deride** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：嘲笑；嘲弄
- **英文释义**：to express contempt for; ridicule openly
- **例句**：
  - Bullies deride classmates who look or speak differently.
  - 欺凌者嘲笑外表或说话不同的同学。
  - Critics derided the plan as unrealistic, but it later succeeded.
  - 批评者嘲笑该计划不切实际，但后来它成功了。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ ride（笑）→ 向下嘲笑 → 嘲弄

### 4. **extol** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：赞美；颂扬；吹捧
- **英文释义**：to praise enthusiastically and often publicly
- **例句**：
  - Poets extol the beauty of nature in spring.
  - 诗人们颂扬春天的自然之美。
  - The coach extolled the team's discipline after the victory.
  - 获胜后教练称赞了球队的纪律。
- **助记**：ex（向外）+ tol（举起）→ 高高举起赞美 → 颂扬

### 5. **fortify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加强；设防于；增强（体质）
- **英文释义**：to strengthen against attack; to add nutrients to food
- **例句**：
  - Soldiers fortified the border town with walls and towers.
  - 士兵用城墙和塔楼加固边镇。
  - Orange juice is fortified with vitamin C.
  - 橙汁中添加了维生素 C。
- **助记**：fort（强）+ ify → 使强 → 加固

### 6. **galvanize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激励；刺激；电镀
- **英文释义**：to shock or excite into action; to coat with zinc
- **例句**：
  - The speech galvanized young people to register to vote.
  - 演讲激励年轻人去登记投票。
  - Fear of failure can galvanize a team to train harder.
  - 对失败的恐惧能激励队伍更刻苦训练。
- **助记**：来自 Galvani（电流实验）→ 像触电般激励 → 激励

### 7. **harbinger** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：先兆；先驱；预告者
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that announces or signals the approach of another
- **例句**：
  - The first robin is often seen as a harbinger of spring.
  - 第一只知更鸟常被视为春天的先兆。
  - Rising prices can be a harbinger of inflation.
  - 物价上涨可能是通货膨胀的前兆。
- **助记**：herb（军队）+ inger → 先行部队 → 先驱

### 8. **impede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to delay or prevent progress by obstructing
- **例句**：
  - Heavy snow impeded traffic on the mountain roads.
  - 大雪阻碍了山区交通。
  - Fear of failure should not impede you from trying new things.
  - 对失败的恐惧不应妨碍你尝试新事物。
- **助记**：im（向内）+ ped（脚）→ 把脚缠住 → 阻碍

### 9. **judicious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：明智的；审慎的；有判断力的
- **英文释义**：having or showing good judgment; sensible and careful
- **例句**：
  - Judicious use of time helps students balance study and rest.
  - 明智地利用时间有助于学生平衡学习与休息。
  - The committee made a judicious choice of neutral experts.
  - 委员会审慎选择了中立专家。
- **助记**：judic（判断）+ ious → 有判断力的

### 10. **linger** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：逗留；徘徊；缠绵不去
- **英文释义**：to stay in a place longer than necessary; to be slow to end
- **例句**：
  - The smell of smoke lingered in the room for hours.
  - 烟味在房间里徘徊了数小时。
  - Do not linger in the hallway during class time.
  - 上课期间不要在走廊逗留。
- **助记**：long（长）+ er → 拖长 → 逗留

### 高一词汇

### 11. **nebulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：模糊不清的；星云状的；朦胧的
- **英文释义**：unclear, vague, or ill-defined; cloudlike
- **例句**：
  - The contract contained several nebulous clauses open to interpretation.
  - 合同中有几处含糊条款可作多种解释。
  - Her artistic goals remained nebulous even after graduation.
  - 毕业后她的艺术目标仍很模糊。
- **助记**：nebula（星云）+ ous → 如云般朦胧

### 12. **obliterate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：抹去；消灭；覆盖
- **英文释义**：to destroy utterly; to blot out or erase completely
- **例句**：
  - The explosion obliterated the small wooden hut.
  - 爆炸把小木屋夷为平地。
  - Time had obliterated most memories of that painful year.
  - 岁月抹去了对那个痛苦年份的大部分记忆。
- **助记**：ob（对抗）+ liter（字母）+ ate → 把字迹涂掉 → 抹去

### 13. **perfidious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：背信弃义的；不忠的；阴险的
- **英文释义**：deceitful and untrustworthy; treacherous
- **例句**：
  - The perfidious ally sold secrets to the enemy.
  - 背信的盟友向敌人出卖机密。
  - History remembers the ruler as a perfidious betrayer of his people.
  - 历史将这位统治者铭记为背弃人民的叛徒。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ fid（信任）+ ious → 贯穿信任而背叛 → 背信

### 14. **quixotic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：堂吉诃德式的；不切实际的；空想的
- **英文释义**：exceedingly idealistic; unrealistic and impractical
- **例句**：
  - His quixotic dream was to end world hunger single-handedly.
  - 他不切实际的梦想是凭一己之力消除世界饥饿。
  - Quixotic projects often run out of funding within months.
  - 空想项目在数月内常会资金耗尽。
- **助记**：来自 Don Quixote（堂吉诃德）→ 不切实际的骑士

### 15. **rancor** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：深怨；积怨；敌意
- **英文释义**：bitterness or resentfulness, especially when long-standing
- **例句**：
  - Years of unfair treatment left rancor in the community.
  - 多年不公在社区中留下了积怨。
  - They debated calmly, without personal rancor.
  - 他们冷静辩论，不带个人恩怨。
- **助记**：ranc（臭/腐）+ or → 腐坏的情绪 → 怨恨
