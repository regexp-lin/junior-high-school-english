## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **ample** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：充足的；丰富的；宽敞的
- **英文释义**：enough or more than enough; plentiful
- **例句**：
  - There is ample space in the parking lot for all the visitors.
  - 停车场有足够的空间容纳所有访客。
- **助记**：ample 和 "ample"（大量）同源 → 充足的

### 2. **complacent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：自满的；得意的
- **英文释义**：showing smug or uncritical satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements
- **例句**：
  - Don't become complacent just because you passed the first round.
  - 不要因为通过了第一轮就自满。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ plac（满意）+ ent → 完全满意的 → 自满的

### 3. **curtail** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：缩减；削减；限制
- **英文释义**：reduce in extent or quantity; impose a restriction on
- **例句**：
  - The government curtailed public spending to reduce the deficit.
  - 政府削减公共开支以减少赤字。
- **助记**：cur（短）+ tail（尾巴）→ 剪短尾巴 → 缩减

### 4. **discrepancy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：差异；不一致
- **英文释义**：a lack of compatibility or similarity between two or more facts
- **例句**：
  - There was a discrepancy between the two witness accounts.
  - 两名证人的证词存在差异。
- **助记**：dis（不）+ crep（响声）+ ancy → 声音不同 → 差异

### 5. **excerpt** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：摘录；节选
- **英文释义**：a short extract from a film, broadcast, or piece of writing
- **例句**：
  - The teacher read an excerpt from the novel to the class.
  - 老师给全班朗读了小说的一段节选。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ cerpt（摘取）→ 摘取出来 → 摘录

### 6. **fickle** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：善变的；不忠实的
- **英文释义**：changing frequently, especially as regards one's loyalties or affections
- **例句**：
  - The weather here is very fickle — it can change from sunny to rainy in minutes.
  - 这里的天气非常善变——可以在几分钟内从晴天变成雨天。
- **助记**：fickle 联想 "flicker"（闪烁）→ 像闪光一样变化无常

### 7. **hostile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：敌意的；不友好的
- **英文释义**：unfriendly; antagonistic; opposed
- **例句**：
  - The crowd was hostile to the visiting team's fans.
  - 人群对客队球迷充满敌意。
- **助记**：host（敌人）+ ile → 像敌人一样的 → 敌意的

### 8. **incessant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不断的；不停的
- **英文释义**：(of something regarded as unpleasant) continuing without pause or interruption
- **例句**：
  - The incessant noise from the construction site kept me awake all night.
  - 建筑工地不间断的噪音让我整夜无法入睡。
- **助记**：in（不）+ cess（停止）+ ant → 不停止的 → 不断的

### 9. **negligent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：疏忽的；粗心的
- **英文释义**：failing to take proper care in doing something
- **例句**：
  - The driver was found to be negligent and caused the accident.
  - 这名司机被认定疏忽驾驶导致了事故。
- **助记**：neg（否定）+ lig（选择）+ ent → 不去选择关注 → 疏忽的

### 10. **profound** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：深刻的；深奥的；极大的
- **英文释义**：(of a state, quality, or emotion) very great or intense; very deep
- **例句**：
  - The book had a profound impact on my way of thinking.
  - 这本书对我的思维方式产生了深刻的影响。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ found（底部）→ 深入到底部 → 深刻的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **beleaguered** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：被围困的；饱受困扰的
- **英文释义**：in a very difficult situation; under siege
- **例句**：
  - The beleaguered mayor faced criticism from all sides.
  - 这位饱受困扰的市长面临来自各方的批评。
- **助记**：be + leaguer（围攻）+ ed → 被围攻的 → 饱受困扰的

### 12. **facetious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：滑稽的；不当地幽默的
- **英文释义**：treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor; flippant
- **例句**：
  - He made a facetious remark during the serious discussion, annoying everyone.
  - 他在严肃的讨论中说了一句不当的玩笑话，惹恼了所有人。
- **助记**：face（脸）+ tious → 脸上总挂着笑 → 滑稽的

### 13. **mercurial** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：善变的；活泼的；水银般的
- **英文释义**：subject to sudden or unpredictable changes of mood or mind
- **例句**：
  - His mercurial temperament made it difficult to predict his reactions.
  - 他善变的性情使人难以预测他的反应。
- **助记**：Mercury（水星/水银）+ ial → 像水银一样变幻不定 → 善变的

### 14. **pertinacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：执着的；固执的
- **英文释义**：holding firmly to an opinion or a course of action
- **例句**：
  - Her pertinacious pursuit of justice finally led to the case being reopened.
  - 她对正义的执着追求最终使案件得以重新审理。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ tin（持有）+ acious → 始终持有 → 执着的

### 15. **sycophant** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：谄媚者；马屁精
- **英文释义**：a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important to gain advantage
- **例句**：
  - The boss was surrounded by sycophants who agreed with everything he said.
  - 老板被一群对他言听计从的马屁精包围着。
- **助记**：syco（无花果）+ phant（展示）→ 古希腊指告密者 → 引申为谄媚者
