## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **basin** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：盆地；水盆；流域
- **英文释义**：a wide round container; a low area of land enclosed by higher land; the area drained by a river
- **例句**：
  - Fill the basin with warm water to wash your hands.
  - 把水盆装满温水来洗手。
- **助记**：basin 谐音"盆是"→ 盆是用来装水的 → 水盆/盆地

### 2. **cunning** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：狡猾的；巧妙的
- **英文释义**：having or showing skill in achieving one's ends by deceit or evasion
- **例句**：
  - The cunning fox escaped from the hunter's trap.
  - 狡猾的狐狸逃出了猎人的陷阱。
- **助记**：cun（谐音"坤"）+ ning → 坤哥宁可用计谋也不硬拼 → 狡猾的

### 3. **dweller** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：居民；居住者
- **英文释义**：a person or animal that lives in a particular place
- **例句**：
  - City dwellers often dream of living in the countryside.
  - 城市居民常常梦想住在乡下。
- **助记**：dwell（居住）+ er（人）→ 居住的人 → 居民

### 4. **famine** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：饥荒；饥馑
- **英文释义**：extreme scarcity of food
- **例句**：
  - The drought caused a terrible famine in the region.
  - 干旱在该地区造成了严重的饥荒。
- **助记**：fam（谐音"饭"）+ ine → 没饭吃了 → 饥荒

### 5. **glory** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：荣誉；光荣；壮丽
- **英文释义**：high renown or honor won by notable achievements; magnificence
- **例句**：
  - The team won glory for the school by winning the championship.
  - 球队赢得冠军为学校争了光。
- **助记**：glor（光辉）+ y → 充满光辉的 → 荣誉

### 6. **insect** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：昆虫
- **英文释义**：a small arthropod animal that has six legs and generally one or two pairs of wings
- **例句**：
  - Butterflies and bees are common insects in the garden.
  - 蝴蝶和蜜蜂是花园里常见的昆虫。
- **助记**：in（里面）+ sect（切）→ 身体被切成几段的小动物 → 昆虫

### 7. **meadow** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：草地；牧场
- **英文释义**：a piece of grassland, especially one used for hay
- **例句**：
  - The children played happily in the green meadow.
  - 孩子们在绿色的草地上快乐地玩耍。
- **助记**：mead（蜜酒）+ ow → 像蜜一样甜美的草地 → 牧场

### 8. **orphan** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：孤儿
- **英文释义**：a child whose parents are dead
- **例句**：
  - The orphan was adopted by a kind family.
  - 这个孤儿被一个善良的家庭收养了。
- **助记**：or（或者）+ phan（谐音"翻"）→ 命运翻转，失去父母 → 孤儿

### 9. **revolt** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 反叛；起义 v. 反抗；使厌恶
- **英文释义**：n. an attempt to end the authority of a person or body by rebelling; v. to rise in rebellion
- **例句**：
  - The people revolted against the cruel ruler.
  - 人民起义反抗残暴的统治者。
- **助记**：re（反）+ volt（转）→ 反过来转向 → 反叛

### 10. **terrify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使恐惧；使害怕
- **英文释义**：to cause someone to feel extreme fear
- **例句**：
  - The horror movie terrified all the children.
  - 那部恐怖电影吓坏了所有的孩子。
- **助记**：terr（恐怖）+ ify（使）→ 使人恐怖 → 使害怕

### 高一词汇

### 11. **apparatus** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：设备；器械；装置
- **英文释义**：the technical equipment or machinery needed for a particular activity or purpose
- **例句**：
  - The laboratory is equipped with modern apparatus.
  - 实验室配备了现代化的设备。
- **助记**：ap + paratus（准备）→ 事先准备好的东西 → 装置/设备

### 12. **contemplate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：沉思；凝视；打算
- **英文释义**：to look at thoughtfully for a long time; to think deeply about
- **例句**：
  - She sat by the window, contemplating the meaning of life.
  - 她坐在窗边，思考人生的意义。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ temple（寺庙）+ ate → 在寺庙中静坐冥想 → 沉思

### 13. **eloquent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：雄辩的；有说服力的；传神的
- **英文释义**：fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer made an eloquent speech in defense of his client.
  - 律师为他的当事人作了一番雄辩的辩护。
- **助记**：e（出）+ loqu（说话）+ ent → 话说得出来且动听 → 雄辩的

### 14. **infringe** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：侵犯；违反
- **英文释义**：to actively break the terms of a law or agreement; to encroach on someone's rights
- **例句**：
  - Downloading pirated software infringes copyright laws.
  - 下载盗版软件侵犯了版权法。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ fringe（边缘）→ 闯入别人的边界 → 侵犯

### 15. **subordinate** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 从属的；次要的 n. 下属；部下
- **英文释义**：adj. lower in rank or position; n. a person under the authority of another
- **例句**：
  - He treated his subordinates with respect and fairness.
  - 他以尊重和公平的态度对待下属。
- **助记**：sub（下面）+ ordin（顺序）+ ate → 排在下面的顺序 → 下属的
