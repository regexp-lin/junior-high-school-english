## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **anecdote** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：轶事；趣闻；小故事
- **英文释义**：a short amusing or interesting story about a real incident or person
- **例句**：
  - The grandfather entertained the children with anecdotes from his youth.
  - 爷爷用他年轻时的趣事逗孩子们开心。
  - The speaker opened her presentation with a humorous anecdote to break the ice.
  - 演讲者用一个幽默的小故事开场来打破僵局。
- **助记**：anec（未发表的）+ dote → 未曾发表的小故事 → 轶事

### 2. **blatant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：明目张胆的；公然的；明显的
- **英文释义**：done openly and unashamedly; very obvious
- **例句**：
  - The company's blatant disregard for safety regulations led to a serious accident.
  - 公司对安全法规的公然漠视导致了一起严重事故。
  - It was a blatant lie, and everyone in the room knew it.
  - 那是一个明目张胆的谎言，房间里每个人都知道。
- **助记**：blat（吹号/大声叫）+ ant → 大声嚷嚷的 → 公然的

### 3. **confiscate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：没收；充公；查抄
- **英文释义**：to officially take away someone's property, especially as a penalty
- **例句**：
  - The teacher confiscated the student's phone because he was using it during class.
  - 老师没收了学生的手机，因为他在上课时使用。
  - Customs officers confiscated the illegal goods at the border.
  - 海关官员在边境没收了违禁物品。
- **助记**：con（共同/完全）+ fisc（国库）+ ate → 收归国库 → 没收

### 4. **diminish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减少；缩小；贬低
- **英文释义**：to make or become less; to reduce in size, extent, or importance
- **例句**：
  - The heavy rain did nothing to diminish the crowd's enthusiasm for the concert.
  - 大雨丝毫没有减少人群对音乐会的热情。
  - His influence in the company has gradually diminished over the past few years.
  - 在过去几年里，他在公司的影响力逐渐减弱。
- **助记**：di（分开）+ min（小）+ ish → 使变小 → 减少

### 5. **exert** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：施加；运用；发挥
- **英文释义**：to apply or bring to bear a force, influence, or quality
- **例句**：
  - Parents exert a powerful influence on their children's values and behavior.
  - 父母对孩子的价值观和行为施加着强大的影响。
  - You need to exert more effort if you want to pass the final exam.
  - 如果你想通过期末考试，你需要付出更多努力。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ert（放出）→ 将力量放出去 → 施加

### 6. **frugal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：节俭的；朴素的；花费少的
- **英文释义**：sparing or economical with regard to money or food
- **例句**：
  - My grandmother lived a frugal life, never wasting a single grain of rice.
  - 我奶奶过着节俭的生活，从不浪费一粒米。
  - Being frugal doesn't mean being cheap — it means spending wisely.
  - 节俭不意味着吝啬——它意味着明智地花钱。
- **助记**：frug（果实）+ al → 只吃果实就够了 → 节俭的

### 7. **immerse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：沉浸；使浸没；使专注于
- **英文释义**：to dip or submerge in a liquid; to involve deeply in an activity
- **例句**：
  - She immersed herself in studying Japanese culture before her trip to Tokyo.
  - 在去东京旅行之前，她沉浸在日本文化的学习中。
  - The chef immersed the vegetables in ice water to keep them crisp.
  - 厨师将蔬菜浸入冰水中以保持其脆度。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ merse（浸入）→ 浸入其中 → 沉浸

### 8. **lurk** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：潜伏；埋伏；暗中存在
- **英文释义**：to remain hidden so as to wait in ambush; to exist unseen
- **例句**：
  - A predator lurked in the shadows, waiting for its prey to approach.
  - 一只掠食者潜伏在阴影中，等待猎物靠近。
  - Unknown dangers lurk beneath the calm surface of the ocean.
  - 未知的危险潜伏在平静的海面之下。
- **助记**：lurk 发音像"落客"——在暗处等待落单的旅客 → 潜伏

### 9. **obstinate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：固执的；顽固的；难以改变的
- **英文释义**：stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or chosen course of action
- **例句**：
  - The obstinate child refused to eat his vegetables no matter what his parents said.
  - 这个固执的孩子无论父母怎么说都不肯吃蔬菜。
  - Despite strong opposition, she remained obstinate in her decision to study abroad.
  - 尽管遭到强烈反对，她仍坚持出国留学的决定。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ stin（站立）+ ate → 对着站不动 → 固执的

### 10. **revere** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：尊敬；崇敬；敬畏
- **英文释义**：to feel deep respect or admiration for something or someone
- **例句**：
  - Confucius is revered as one of the greatest philosophers in Chinese history.
  - 孔子被崇敬为中国历史上最伟大的哲学家之一。
  - The local people revere the ancient temple and visit it regularly to pray.
  - 当地人敬畏这座古庙，定期前往祈祷。
- **助记**：re（反复）+ vere（敬畏）→ 反复表达敬意 → 崇敬

### 高一词汇

### 11. **amalgamate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；混合；联合
- **英文释义**：to combine or unite to form one organization or structure
- **例句**：
  - The two small schools amalgamated to form a larger, more competitive institution.
  - 两所小学校合并成了一个更大、更有竞争力的机构。
  - The artist amalgamated different cultural styles to create a unique painting.
  - 这位艺术家融合了不同的文化风格创作出一幅独特的画作。
- **助记**：a + malgam（混合物）+ ate → 混合在一起 → 合并

### 12. **capricious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：反复无常的；变化莫测的；任性的
- **英文释义**：given to sudden and unaccountable changes of mood or behavior
- **例句**：
  - The capricious weather in spring makes it difficult to plan outdoor events.
  - 春天反复无常的天气使得很难计划户外活动。
  - Working for a capricious boss who changes his mind every day is exhausting.
  - 为一个每天改变主意的任性老板工作令人精疲力竭。
- **助记**：capr（山羊）+ icious → 像山羊一样跳来跳去 → 反复无常的

### 13. **fastidious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：挑剔的；一丝不苟的；难以取悦的
- **英文释义**：very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail
- **例句**：
  - The fastidious chef inspected every dish before it was served to customers.
  - 挑剔的厨师在每道菜端给顾客之前都会仔细检查。
  - She is fastidious about keeping her desk organized and clutter-free.
  - 她对保持桌面整洁有序一丝不苟。
- **助记**：fast（紧的）+ idious → 管得很紧 → 挑剔的

### 14. **luminous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：发光的；明亮的；清晰明了的
- **英文释义**：giving off light; bright or shining; clearly expressed
- **例句**：
  - The luminous stars painted a breathtaking scene across the night sky.
  - 明亮的星星在夜空中绘出了一幅令人叹为观止的画面。
  - Her luminous writing style made complex scientific ideas accessible to ordinary readers.
  - 她清晰明了的写作风格使复杂的科学概念易于普通读者理解。
- **助记**：lumin（光）+ ous → 有光的 → 发光的

### 15. **pragmatic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；实用主义的；注重实际的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically, based on practical considerations
- **例句**：
  - The manager took a pragmatic approach to the problem, focusing on what could be done immediately.
  - 经理对问题采取了务实的态度，关注可以立即做的事情。
  - A pragmatic leader values results over ideology.
  - 一个务实的领导者重视结果胜过意识形态。
- **助记**：pragm（行动/事务）+ atic → 注重行动和事务的 → 务实的
