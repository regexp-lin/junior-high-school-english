## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **amiable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：和蔼的；友善的；亲切的
- **英文释义**：having or displaying a friendly and pleasant manner
- **例句**：
  - Our new neighbour is an amiable woman who always greets us with a smile.
  - 我们的新邻居是一位和蔼的女士，总是微笑着和我们打招呼。
- **助记**：ami（朋友）+ able → 像朋友一样的 → 友善的

### 2. **blight** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 枯萎病；祸害 v. 使枯萎；毁坏
- **英文释义**：n. a plant disease; something that spoils; v. to spoil or damage
- **例句**：
  - Poverty continues to blight the lives of millions of children.
  - 贫困继续毁坏着数百万儿童的生活。
- **助记**：blight → 联想 light（光）被 b 挡住 → 遮光使枯萎 → 枯萎/祸害

### 3. **concise** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：简明的；简洁的；言简意赅的
- **英文释义**：giving a lot of information clearly and in a few words
- **例句**：
  - The teacher asked the students to write concise summaries of the article.
  - 老师让学生们写简洁的文章摘要。
- **助记**：con（全部）+ cise（切）→ 把多余的都切掉 → 简洁的

### 4. **detain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：拘留；扣押；耽搁
- **英文释义**：to keep someone in official custody; to keep someone from proceeding
- **例句**：
  - The police detained several suspects for further questioning.
  - 警方拘留了几名嫌疑人以进一步审问。
- **助记**：de（下来）+ tain（持有）→ 按住不让走 → 拘留

### 5. **engulf** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：吞没；包围；席卷
- **英文释义**：to sweep over something so as to surround or cover it completely
- **例句**：
  - The flames quickly engulfed the entire building.
  - 火焰迅速吞没了整栋建筑。
- **助记**：en（进入）+ gulf（深渊）→ 进入深渊般被吞没 → 吞没

### 6. **gazette** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：公报；官方报刊；报纸
- **英文释义**：an official journal or newspaper, especially one published by a government
- **例句**：
  - The new regulations were published in the government gazette.
  - 新法规刊登在政府公报上。
- **助记**：gazette → 联想"告知他"→ 通过报纸告知他人 → 公报

### 7. **incite** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：煽动；激起；鼓动
- **英文释义**：to encourage or stir up violent or unlawful behaviour
- **例句**：
  - The speech was accused of inciting violence among the protesters.
  - 这篇演讲被指控煽动了抗议者中的暴力行为。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ cite（激起）→ 激起情绪 → 煽动

### 8. **lustre** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：光泽；光彩；荣耀
- **英文释义**：a gentle sheen or soft glow; prestige or glory
- **例句**：
  - The victory added lustre to the school's reputation.
  - 这场胜利为学校的声誉增添了光彩。
- **助记**：lustr（发光）+ e → 发出光芒 → 光泽/荣耀

### 9. **nostalgic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：怀旧的；念旧的
- **英文释义**：feeling, evoking, or characterized by nostalgia
- **例句**：
  - The old photographs made her feel nostalgic about her childhood.
  - 旧照片让她对童年感到怀旧。
- **助记**：nostalg（思乡）+ ic → 思念家乡的 → 怀旧的

### 10. **plight** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：困境；苦境；窘况
- **英文释义**：a dangerous, difficult, or otherwise unfortunate situation
- **例句**：
  - The documentary highlighted the plight of refugees fleeing the war zone.
  - 这部纪录片展示了逃离战区的难民的困境。
- **助记**：plight → flight（飞行）→ 像紧急逃跑一样的困境 → 困境

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acrimonious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：尖刻的；充满火药味的；激烈的
- **英文释义**：angry and bitter, especially in speech or debate
- **例句**：
  - The divorce proceedings became increasingly acrimonious.
  - 离婚程序变得越来越充满火药味。
- **助记**：acri（尖锐）+ monious → 言辞尖锐的 → 尖刻的

### 12. **disparate** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：截然不同的；不相干的
- **英文释义**：essentially different in kind; not allowing comparison
- **例句**：
  - The committee had to reconcile disparate viewpoints to reach a consensus.
  - 委员会不得不调和截然不同的观点以达成共识。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ par（相等）+ ate → 不相等的 → 截然不同的

### 13. **hedonistic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：享乐主义的；追求快乐的
- **英文释义**：devoted to the pursuit of pleasure; sensually self-indulgent
- **例句**：
  - His hedonistic lifestyle eventually caught up with him when his health deteriorated.
  - 他享乐主义的生活方式最终在健康恶化时给了他报应。
- **助记**：hedon（快乐）+ istic → 追求快乐的 → 享乐主义的

### 14. **obsequious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：奉承的；巴结的；卑躬屈膝的
- **英文释义**：obedient or attentive to an excessive or servile degree
- **例句**：
  - The obsequious waiter hovered around the VIP table all evening.
  - 那个巴结的服务员整晚都围着贵宾桌转。
- **助记**：ob（向）+ sequ（跟随）+ ious → 一直跟在后面的 → 卑躬屈膝的

### 15. **tantamount** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：等同于……的；相当于……的
- **英文释义**：equivalent in seriousness to; virtually the same as
- **例句**：
  - Refusing to vote is tantamount to accepting whatever outcome occurs.
  - 拒绝投票等同于接受任何结果。
- **助记**：tant（如此多）+ amount（量）→ 数量如此相当 → 等同于
