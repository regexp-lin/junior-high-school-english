## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **allege** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：声称；断言；指控
- **英文释义**：to claim or assert that someone has done something illegal or wrong, without proof
- **例句**：
  - The newspaper alleged that the mayor had accepted bribes from local businesses.
  - 报纸声称市长收受了当地企业的贿赂。
  - It is alleged that the company dumped toxic waste into the river.
  - 据称该公司向河流中倾倒了有毒废物。
- **助记**：al（加强）+ lege（法律）→ 在法律面前声称 → 指控/断言

### 2. **blunder** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：大错；失误；犯愚蠢的错误
- **英文释义**：a stupid or careless mistake; to make such a mistake
- **例句**：
  - Sending the confidential email to the wrong person was a terrible blunder.
  - 把机密邮件发给了错误的人是一个严重的失误。
  - He blundered into the wrong meeting room and interrupted the presentation.
  - 他误闯进了错误的会议室，打断了演示。
- **助记**：blunder 联想 blind（盲目的）→ 盲目行事 → 犯大错

### 3. **coerce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：强迫；胁迫；迫使
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone to do something by using force or threats
- **例句**：
  - The suspect claimed he was coerced into signing the confession.
  - 嫌疑人声称他是被胁迫签署供词的。
  - No one should be coerced into making decisions against their will.
  - 任何人都不应该被强迫做出违背自己意愿的决定。
- **助记**：co（一起）+ erce（力量）→ 用力量压迫 → 强迫/胁迫

### 4. **deplete** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：耗尽；大量消耗；使枯竭
- **英文释义**：to use up the supply or resources of something
- **例句**：
  - Overfishing has severely depleted the ocean's fish stocks.
  - 过度捕捞已经严重消耗了海洋鱼类资源。
  - The long journey depleted their food and water supplies.
  - 漫长的旅程耗尽了他们的食物和水。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ plete（填满的反义）→ 完全掏空 → 耗尽

### 5. **endorse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：支持；赞同；在背面签名
- **英文释义**：to declare one's public approval or support of; to sign the back of a check
- **例句**：
  - Several famous athletes have endorsed the new sports drink brand.
  - 几位著名运动员为这个新运动饮料品牌代言。
  - The committee endorsed the proposal to build a new community center.
  - 委员会支持建设新社区中心的提案。
- **助记**：en（使）+ dorse（背部）→ 在背面签字表示支持 → 支持/赞同

### 6. **fathom** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：理解；领会；英寻（水深单位）
- **英文释义**：to understand something after much thought; a unit of depth equal to six feet
- **例句**：
  - I cannot fathom why anyone would want to climb that dangerous mountain.
  - 我无法理解为什么有人想要攀登那座危险的山。
  - The wreck lies at a depth of thirty fathoms below the surface.
  - 残骸位于水面下三十英寻的深处。
- **助记**：fathom → 探测水深 → 引申为"深入理解"

### 7. **grudge** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：怨恨；嫉妒；不情愿给予
- **英文释义**：a persistent feeling of ill will or resentment; to be unwilling to give
- **例句**：
  - She still holds a grudge against her former friend for betraying her trust.
  - 她仍然对背叛她信任的前朋友心怀怨恨。
  - I don't grudge him his success — he worked hard for it.
  - 我不嫉妒他的成功——他为此付出了努力。
- **助记**：grudge 发音类似"格鲁吉"→ 心中格格不入的不满 → 怨恨

### 8. **invoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：援引；调用；祈求
- **英文释义**：to cite or appeal to as an authority; to call on for help or support
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer invoked the Fifth Amendment to protect his client's rights.
  - 律师援引第五修正案来保护客户的权利。
  - Ancient civilizations would invoke the gods before going into battle.
  - 古代文明在出征前会祈求神灵保佑。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ voke（声音/呼唤）→ 大声呼唤 → 援引/祈求

### 9. **plausible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：看似合理的；貌似可信的
- **英文释义**：seeming reasonable or probable; appearing worthy of belief
- **例句**：
  - His excuse for being late sounded plausible, but no one really believed him.
  - 他迟到的借口听起来似乎合理，但没有人真正相信他。
  - The detective considered several plausible explanations for the crime.
  - 侦探考虑了几个看似合理的犯罪解释。
- **助记**：plaus（鼓掌）+ ible → 值得鼓掌的 → 看似合理的

### 10. **squander** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：浪费；挥霍；虚度
- **英文释义**：to waste time, money, or opportunities in a reckless or foolish way
- **例句**：
  - He squandered his inheritance on luxury cars and expensive vacations.
  - 他把遗产挥霍在豪华汽车和昂贵的假期上。
  - Don't squander this opportunity — it may never come again.
  - 不要浪费这个机会——它可能再也不会来了。
- **助记**：squander 联想 scatter（散落）→ 把钱像撒花一样散掉 → 挥霍

### 高一词汇

### 11. **belligerent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；挑衅的；交战的
- **英文释义**：hostile and aggressive; engaged in a war or conflict
- **例句**：
  - The belligerent customer demanded to speak with the manager immediately.
  - 这位好斗的顾客要求立刻与经理谈话。
  - The two belligerent nations finally agreed to a ceasefire after months of fighting.
  - 两个交战国在数月的战斗后终于同意停火。
- **助记**：bell（战争）+ iger（发动）+ ent → 发动战争的 → 好斗的

### 12. **delineate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：描绘；描述；勾画
- **英文释义**：to describe or portray something precisely; to outline
- **例句**：
  - The contract clearly delineates the responsibilities of each party.
  - 合同明确勾画了各方的责任。
  - The artist delineated the figure with a few confident strokes of the pencil.
  - 艺术家用几笔自信的铅笔线条勾勒出了人物轮廓。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ line（线）+ ate → 用线条完整勾画 → 描绘

### 13. **gratuitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无端的；无理由的；免费的
- **英文释义**：uncalled for; lacking good reason; given without charge
- **例句**：
  - The movie was criticized for its gratuitous violence that added nothing to the story.
  - 这部电影因其无端的暴力场面而受到批评，这些场面对故事毫无贡献。
  - He made a gratuitous insult that hurt everyone in the room.
  - 他发出了一句无端的侮辱，伤害了房间里的每个人。
- **助记**：gratu（免费的，如 gratuity 小费）+ ous → 白白给的/不必要的 → 无端的

### 14. **nonchalant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：漫不经心的；若无其事的；冷淡的
- **英文释义**：feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety
- **例句**：
  - She tried to appear nonchalant, but her trembling hands gave away her nervousness.
  - 她试图显得若无其事，但颤抖的双手暴露了她的紧张。
  - He gave a nonchalant shrug when asked about the exam results.
  - 当被问及考试成绩时，他漫不经心地耸了耸肩。
- **助记**：non（不）+ chalant（关心/热情）→ 不关心的 → 漫不经心的

### 15. **ubiquitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；普遍存在的
- **英文释义**：present, appearing, or found everywhere
- **例句**：
  - Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society — almost everyone has one.
  - 智能手机在现代社会已经无处不在——几乎人人都有一部。
  - Coffee shops are ubiquitous in this city; you can find one on almost every corner.
  - 咖啡店在这个城市无处不在；几乎每个街角都有一家。
- **助记**：ubique（到处，拉丁语）+ ous → 到处都有的 → 无处不在的
