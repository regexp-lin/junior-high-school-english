## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **emancipate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：解放；使自由；解除束缚
- **英文释义**：to set free from legal, social, or political restrictions
- **例句**：
  - The movement sought to emancipate enslaved people and grant them equal rights.
  - 这场运动旨在解放被奴役的人并赋予他们平等权利。
  - Education can emancipate individuals from the cycle of poverty.
  - 教育能将个人从贫困的循环中解放出来。
- **助记**：e（出）+ man（手）+ cipate（拿）→ 从手中释放 → 解放

### 2. **meander** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：蜿蜒；漫步；闲逛
- **英文释义**：to follow a winding course; to wander aimlessly
- **例句**：
  - The river meanders through the green valley below the mountains.
  - 河流蜿蜒穿过山下的绿色山谷。
  - We meandered through the old streets of the town, enjoying the architecture.
  - 我们在古镇的老街上闲逛，欣赏着建筑。
- **助记**：源自希腊河名 Meander（弯曲的河流）→ 蜿蜒

### 3. **indispensable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可或缺的；必不可少的
- **英文释义**：absolutely necessary; essential
- **例句**：
  - Clean water is indispensable for human survival.
  - 清洁的水对人类生存是不可或缺的。
  - Her experience makes her an indispensable member of the team.
  - 她的经验使她成为团队中不可或缺的成员。
- **助记**：in（不）+ dispensable（可免除的）→ 不可免除的 → 不可或缺的

### 4. **perpetuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使持续；使永久化；使延续
- **英文释义**：to make something continue indefinitely
- **例句**：
  - We must not perpetuate stereotypes through careless language.
  - 我们不能通过不当的语言使刻板印象延续。
  - The scholarship fund was established to perpetuate the donor's legacy.
  - 设立奖学金基金是为了延续捐赠者的遗产。
- **助记**：perpetu（永久）+ ate → 使永久 → 使延续

### 5. **rampant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：猖獗的；肆虐的；蔓延的
- **英文释义**：flourishing or spreading unchecked, especially of something unwelcome
- **例句**：
  - Corruption was rampant in the local government before the reform.
  - 改革前，腐败在地方政府中猖獗。
  - Rampant deforestation has led to the loss of countless species.
  - 猖獗的毁林导致无数物种消失。
- **助记**：ramp（猛冲）+ ant → 像猛冲一样蔓延 → 猖獗的

### 6. **salvage** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：抢救；打捞；挽回
- **英文释义**：to rescue property from a wreck or disaster; the act of saving something
- **例句**：
  - Divers attempted to salvage the cargo from the sunken ship.
  - 潜水员试图从沉船中打捞货物。
  - He tried to salvage his reputation after the embarrassing incident.
  - 他试图在那次尴尬事件后挽回自己的声誉。
- **助记**：salv（拯救）+ age → 拯救 → 抢救

### 7. **clandestine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：秘密的；暗中的；偷偷的
- **英文释义**：kept secret or done secretively, especially because illicit
- **例句**：
  - The agents conducted a clandestine operation to gather intelligence.
  - 特工进行了一次秘密行动来收集情报。
  - They held clandestine meetings to plan the surprise event.
  - 他们举行了秘密会议来策划惊喜活动。
- **助记**：clandestin（隐藏）+ e → 隐藏着做 → 秘密的

### 8. **inaugurate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：就职；开创；揭幕
- **英文释义**：to begin or introduce formally; to admit to office with a ceremony
- **例句**：
  - The new president was inaugurated in a grand ceremony.
  - 新总统在盛大的仪式上就职。
  - The museum inaugurated its latest exhibition with a special gala.
  - 博物馆以一场特别晚会揭幕了最新展览。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ augur（占卜/预兆）+ ate → 以吉兆开始 → 就职

### 9. **truncate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：截短；缩短；删节
- **英文释义**：to shorten by cutting off the top or end
- **例句**：
  - The editor truncated the article to fit the limited space in the newspaper.
  - 编辑将文章截短以适应报纸有限的版面。
  - Long file names are automatically truncated by the system.
  - 长文件名会被系统自动截短。
- **助记**：trunc（截断）+ ate → 截断 → 截短

### 10. **altruistic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：利他的；无私的
- **英文释义**：showing a selfless concern for the well-being of others
- **例句**：
  - Her altruistic decision to donate her savings inspired the entire community.
  - 她无私地捐出积蓄的决定激励了整个社区。
  - Altruistic behavior is often motivated by empathy and compassion.
  - 利他行为往往源于同理心和同情心。
- **助记**：altru（他人）+ istic → 为了他人的 → 利他的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **onomatopoeia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：拟声词；象声词
- **英文释义**：the formation of a word from a sound associated with what is named
- **例句**：
  - Words like "buzz," "hiss," and "splash" are examples of onomatopoeia.
  - "嗡嗡""嘶嘶""哗啦"等词是拟声词的例子。
  - Poets often use onomatopoeia to create vivid sensory experiences.
  - 诗人常用拟声词来创造生动的感官体验。
- **助记**：onoma（名字）+ poeia（创造）→ 用声音创造名字 → 拟声词

### 12. **pusillanimous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：胆小的；怯懦的；缺乏勇气的
- **英文释义**：showing a lack of courage or determination; timid
- **例句**：
  - The pusillanimous leader refused to make any bold decisions.
  - 那位胆小的领导者拒绝做出任何大胆的决定。
  - It would be pusillanimous to back down now after coming so far.
  - 走了这么远现在退缩是怯懦的。
- **助记**：pusill（弱小）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 心灵弱小 → 胆小的

### 13. **denouement** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：结局；收场；大团圆
- **英文释义**：the final outcome of a story or complex sequence of events
- **例句**：
  - The novel's denouement revealed that the detective was the real villain.
  - 小说的结局揭示了侦探才是真正的恶人。
  - The denouement of the political crisis came with the prime minister's resignation.
  - 政治危机的收场是首相的辞职。
- **助记**：法语词，dénouer（解开）→ 解开故事的结 → 结局

### 14. **soporific** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：催眠的；令人昏昏欲睡的
- **英文释义**：tending to induce drowsiness or sleep
- **例句**：
  - The soporific effect of the lecture caused several students to doze off.
  - 讲座的催眠效果导致好几个学生打瞌睡。
  - The warm room and soft music had a soporific effect on the audience.
  - 温暖的房间和柔和的音乐让观众昏昏欲睡。
- **助记**：sopor（深睡）+ ific（使）→ 使人深睡 → 催眠的

### 15. **predilection** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：偏好；偏爱；嗜好
- **英文释义**：a preference or special liking for something
- **例句**：
  - She has a predilection for classical music over modern pop.
  - 比起现代流行乐，她更偏爱古典音乐。
  - His predilection for spicy food is well known among his friends.
  - 他对辣味食物的偏好在朋友中众所周知。
- **助记**：pre（预先）+ dilect（喜爱）+ ion → 预先就喜欢 → 偏好
