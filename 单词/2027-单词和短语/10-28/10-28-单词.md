## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **contemplate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：沉思；凝视；打算
- **英文释义**：to think about something carefully and for a long period; to consider as a possibility
- **例句**：
  - She sat by the window, contemplating the meaning of life.
  - 她坐在窗边，沉思着人生的意义。
  - He is contemplating a career change after ten years in the same job.
  - 在同一岗位工作十年后，他正考虑换个职业。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ temple（寺庙）+ ate → 在寺庙中一起沉思 → 沉思

### 2. **diligent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：勤奋的；勤勉的；用功的
- **英文释义**：having or showing careful and persistent effort
- **例句**：
  - The diligent student always completed her homework on time.
  - 这个勤奋的学生总是按时完成作业。
  - With diligent practice, he mastered the piano piece in just one month.
  - 通过刻苦练习，他仅用一个月就掌握了这首钢琴曲。
- **助记**：dili（勤劳）+ gent（人）→ 勤劳的人 → 勤奋的

### 3. **aspire** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：渴望；有志于；追求
- **英文释义**：to direct one's hopes or ambitions towards achieving something
- **例句**：
  - Many young athletes aspire to compete in the Olympic Games.
  - 许多年轻运动员渴望参加奥运会。
  - She aspires to become a doctor and help people in rural areas.
  - 她立志成为医生，帮助农村地区的人们。
- **助记**：a（向）+ spire（呼吸）→ 朝着目标呼吸 → 渴望

### 4. **fatigue** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：疲劳；疲惫；使疲劳
- **英文释义**：extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion; to cause weariness
- **例句**：
  - After running the marathon, he collapsed from extreme fatigue.
  - 跑完马拉松后，他因极度疲劳而倒下。
  - Long hours of screen time can fatigue your eyes.
  - 长时间看屏幕会使眼睛疲劳。
- **助记**：fatigue 发音似"法提格" → 法国人说"太累了" → 疲劳

### 5. **profound** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：深刻的；深远的；极度的
- **英文释义**：very great or intense; having deep insight or understanding
- **例句**：
  - The book had a profound impact on how I view the world.
  - 这本书对我看待世界的方式产生了深刻的影响。
  - She expressed a profound sense of gratitude to her mentors.
  - 她对导师们表达了深深的感激之情。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ found（底部）→ 深入到底部 → 深刻的

### 6. **conceal** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：隐藏；隐瞒；遮盖
- **英文释义**：to keep from sight or discovery; to hide
- **例句**：
  - He tried to conceal his disappointment behind a forced smile.
  - 他试图用勉强的微笑隐藏自己的失望。
  - The thick bushes concealed the entrance to the secret garden.
  - 茂密的灌木丛遮住了秘密花园的入口。
- **助记**：con（完全）+ ceal（隐藏）→ 完全隐藏 → 隐藏

### 7. **hinder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to create difficulties resulting in delay or obstruction
- **例句**：
  - Bad weather hindered the rescue team's efforts to reach the survivors.
  - 恶劣天气妨碍了救援队抵达幸存者的努力。
  - Lack of funding hinders scientific research in developing countries.
  - 资金不足阻碍了发展中国家的科学研究。
- **助记**：hinder 发音似"后的" → 拖到后面 → 阻碍

### 8. **versatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多才多艺的；多功能的；通用的
- **英文释义**：able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities
- **例句**：
  - She is a versatile actress who can perform comedy, drama, and action roles.
  - 她是一位多才多艺的女演员，能演喜剧、戏剧和动作角色。
  - A Swiss army knife is one of the most versatile tools you can carry.
  - 瑞士军刀是你能携带的最多功能的工具之一。
- **助记**：vers（转）+ atile → 能够转换 → 多才多艺的

### 9. **endorse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：支持；认可；背书
- **英文释义**：to declare one's public approval or support of; to sign the back of a check
- **例句**：
  - Several celebrities have endorsed this new brand of sportswear.
  - 几位名人为这个新运动品牌代言。
  - The committee endorsed the plan to build a new community park.
  - 委员会批准了建造新社区公园的计划。
- **助记**：en（使）+ dorse（背）→ 在背面签名 → 背书/支持

### 10. **plausible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：看似合理的；貌似可信的
- **英文释义**：seeming reasonable or probable; appearing worthy of belief
- **例句**：
  - His excuse sounded plausible, but the teacher didn't believe him.
  - 他的借口听起来似乎合理，但老师不相信他。
  - The detective considered several plausible theories about the crime.
  - 侦探考虑了几个关于这起案件的合理假设。
- **助记**：plaus（鼓掌）+ ible → 值得鼓掌认可的 → 似乎合理的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **dichotomy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：二分法；对立；两分
- **英文释义**：a division or contrast between two things that are opposed or entirely different
- **例句**：
  - There is a clear dichotomy between theory and practice in this field.
  - 在这个领域中，理论和实践之间存在明显的二分。
  - The dichotomy between urban and rural lifestyles continues to widen.
  - 城市与农村生活方式之间的对立持续加大。
- **助记**：dicho（分成两个）+ tomy（切割）→ 切成两半 → 二分法

### 12. **sycophant** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：谄媚者；马屁精；阿谀奉承者
- **英文释义**：a person who acts obsequiously towards someone important in order to gain advantage
- **例句**：
  - The king was surrounded by sycophants who only told him what he wanted to hear.
  - 国王身边围满了只说他爱听话的谄媚者。
  - A good leader can distinguish honest advisors from sycophants.
  - 好的领导者能区分诚实的顾问和阿谀奉承者。
- **助记**：syco（无花果）+ phant（展示）→ 古希腊告发偷运无花果的人 → 谄媚者

### 13. **ameliorate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：改善；改进；使变好
- **英文释义**：to make something bad or unsatisfactory better
- **例句**：
  - The government introduced new policies to ameliorate living conditions for the poor.
  - 政府出台了新政策来改善贫困人口的生活条件。
  - Planting trees can help ameliorate the effects of air pollution.
  - 种树有助于改善空气污染的影响。
- **助记**：a + melior（更好）+ ate → 使变得更好 → 改善

### 14. **quintessential** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：典型的；精髓的；最完美的
- **英文释义**：representing the most perfect or typical example of a quality or class
- **例句**：
  - Paris is the quintessential romantic city with its charming streets and cafes.
  - 巴黎以迷人的街道和咖啡馆成为典型的浪漫城市。
  - She is the quintessential professional — always prepared and confident.
  - 她是典型的专业人士——总是准备充分且充满自信。
- **助记**：quint（第五）+ essential（本质）→ 古代认为的第五元素（精华）→ 典型的

### 15. **nomenclature** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：命名法；术语体系；名称
- **英文释义**：the devising or choosing of names for things, especially in a science or art
- **例句**：
  - The nomenclature used in chemistry follows strict international rules.
  - 化学中使用的命名法遵循严格的国际规则。
  - Learning the nomenclature of biology is essential for medical students.
  - 学习生物学术语对医学生来说至关重要。
- **助记**：nomen（名称）+ clature（叫）→ 叫名字的方式 → 命名法
