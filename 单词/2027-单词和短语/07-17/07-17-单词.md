## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **benevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；慈善的；善意的
- **英文释义**：well-meaning and kindly; charitable
- **例句**：
  - The benevolent old man donated most of his fortune to children's hospitals.
  - 这位仁慈的老人把大部分财产捐给了儿童医院。
- **助记**：bene（好的）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 好意愿的 → 仁慈的/善意的

### 2. **contend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：竞争；主张；声称；应对
- **英文释义**：to struggle in opposition; to assert something as a position in an argument
- **例句**：
  - Several companies are contending for the government contract worth millions.
  - 几家公司正在争夺价值数百万的政府合同。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ tend（伸展/努力）→ 一起努力争夺 → 竞争/主张

### 3. **divert** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：转移；使转向；使消遣
- **英文释义**：to cause to change course or turn from one direction to another; to distract or entertain
- **例句**：
  - Traffic was diverted through side streets while the main road was being repaired.
  - 主干道修缮期间，交通被改道通过小街。
- **助记**：di（离开）+ vert（转）→ 转向离开 → 转移/使转向

### 4. **erode** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：侵蚀；腐蚀；逐渐削弱
- **英文释义**：to gradually wear away by wind, water, or other natural agents; to gradually destroy
- **例句**：
  - Years of heavy rainfall have eroded the hillside, making it prone to landslides.
  - 多年的暴雨侵蚀了山坡，使其容易发生山体滑坡。
- **助记**：e（出）+ rode（啃咬）→ 不断啃咬 → 侵蚀

### 5. **feasible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可行的；可能的；合理的
- **英文释义**：possible to do easily or conveniently
- **例句**：
  - The engineers concluded that building a bridge across the river was technically feasible.
  - 工程师们得出结论，在河上建桥在技术上是可行的。
- **助记**：feas（做）+ ible → 能做的 → 可行的

### 6. **inhibit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：抑制；阻碍；使拘束
- **英文释义**：to hinder, restrain, or prevent an action or process
- **例句**：
  - Shyness can inhibit children from participating in classroom discussions.
  - 害羞会阻碍孩子参与课堂讨论。
- **助记**：in（里面）+ hibit（持有/保持）→ 保持在里面不让出来 → 抑制

### 7. **novice** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：新手；初学者；新入门者
- **英文释义**：a person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation
- **例句**：
  - As a novice cook, she often relied on simple recipes with few ingredients.
  - 作为一个烹饪新手，她经常依赖食材简单的菜谱。
- **助记**：nov（新的）+ ice → 新人 → 新手/初学者

### 8. **quench** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：解渴；扑灭；压制
- **英文释义**：to satisfy one's thirst; to extinguish a fire; to suppress
- **例句**：
  - After the long hike, nothing could quench his thirst better than cold spring water.
  - 经过长途徒步后，没有什么比冰凉的泉水更能解渴了。
- **助记**：quench → 联想"泉池"→ 用泉水浇灭 → 解渴/扑灭

### 9. **resilient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有弹力的；能恢复的；适应力强的
- **英文释义**：able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions
- **例句**：
  - Children are often more resilient than adults when facing life's challenges.
  - 面对生活挑战时，孩子们往往比成年人更有适应力。
- **助记**：re（回）+ sil（弹跳）+ ient → 弹回来 → 有弹力的/能恢复的

### 10. **wary** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：警惕的；小心的；谨慎的
- **英文释义**：feeling or showing caution about possible dangers or problems
- **例句**：
  - Consumers are becoming increasingly wary of sharing personal data online.
  - 消费者越来越警惕在网上分享个人数据。
- **助记**：war（战争）+ y → 像在战争中一样警惕 → 警惕的/小心的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **assiduous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：刻苦的；勤勉的；坚持不懈的
- **英文释义**：showing great care and perseverance
- **例句**：
  - Through assiduous practice, the young pianist mastered the difficult concerto.
  - 通过刻苦练习，这位年轻钢琴家掌握了这首高难度协奏曲。
- **助记**：as（向）+ sid（坐）+ uous → 一直坐着不走 → 勤勉的/刻苦的

### 12. **demagogue** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：煽动者；蛊惑人心的政客
- **英文释义**：a political leader who seeks support by appealing to the desires and prejudices of ordinary people
- **例句**：
  - The demagogue stirred up hatred by blaming immigrants for the country's economic problems.
  - 这个煽动者通过将国家的经济问题归咎于移民来煽动仇恨。
- **助记**：demo（人民）+ gogue（引导）→ 引导/煽动人民的人 → 煽动者

### 13. **languid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：慵懒的；倦怠的；没精打采的
- **英文释义**：displaying or having a disinclination for physical exertion or effort; slow and relaxed
- **例句**：
  - On hot summer afternoons, the cat lay in a languid pose under the shade tree.
  - 在炎热的夏日午后，猫慵懒地躺在树荫下。
- **助记**：langu（虚弱/疲倦）+ id → 疲倦的 → 慵懒的

### 14. **propensity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：倾向；习性；癖好
- **英文释义**：an inclination or natural tendency to behave in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - He has a propensity for taking risks that sometimes leads to spectacular failures.
  - 他有冒险的倾向，这有时会导致惊人的失败。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ pens（悬挂/倾向）+ ity → 向某方向倾斜 → 倾向

### 15. **vindictive** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：报复心强的；怀恨在心的；恶意的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a strong or unreasoning desire for revenge
- **例句**：
  - After being fired, he wrote a vindictive letter attacking his former employer.
  - 被解雇后，他写了一封恶意攻击前雇主的信。
- **助记**：vindict（复仇）+ ive → 有复仇心的 → 报复心强的
