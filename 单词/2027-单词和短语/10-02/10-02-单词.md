## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **ambiguous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：模棱两可的；含糊不清的
- **英文释义**：open to more than one interpretation; unclear or inexact
- **例句**：
  - The contract contained several ambiguous clauses that confused both parties.
  - 合同中有几处含糊条款，令双方困惑。
  - His smile was ambiguous — we could not tell if he agreed or not.
  - 他的微笑令人捉摸不透——我们看不出他是否同意。
- **助记**：ambi（两边）+ guous → 两边都能说 → 模棱两可

### 2. **inevitable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可避免的；必然发生的
- **英文释义**：certain to happen; unable to be avoided or prevented
- **例句**：
  - Conflict was inevitable when neither side would compromise.
  - 双方都不肯让步时，冲突不可避免。
  - Aging is an inevitable part of life that we must learn to accept.
  - 变老是人生中不可避免的一部分，我们必须学会接受。
- **助记**：in（不）+ evit（避免）+ able → 无法避免的

### 3. **prominent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：突出的；显著的；重要的；著名的
- **英文释义**：important; famous; sticking out beyond a surface
- **例句**：
  - She played a prominent role in organizing the charity event.
  - 她在组织慈善活动中发挥了重要作用。
  - The Alps form a prominent mountain range visible from miles away.
  - 阿尔卑斯山构成一道远处可见的雄伟山脉。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ min（突出）+ ent → 向前突出 → 显著

### 4. **reluctant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不情愿的；勉强的
- **英文释义**：unwilling and hesitant; disinclined
- **例句**：
  - He was reluctant to admit that he had made a mistake.
  - 他不愿承认自己犯了错。
  - The cat was reluctant to leave its warm spot by the heater.
  - 猫不情愿离开暖气旁温暖的地方。
- **助记**：re（反）+ luct（挣扎）+ ant → 往回挣扎 → 不情愿

### 5. **explicit** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：明确的；直白的；清晰陈述的
- **英文释义**：stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion
- **例句**：
  - The teacher gave explicit instructions on how to format the essay.
  - 老师对论文格式给出了明确说明。
  - Parents should have explicit talks with teens about online safety.
  - 家长应与青少年坦诚谈论网络安全。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ plic（折）+ it → 向外折开 → 说清楚

### 6. **implicit** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：含蓄的；暗示的；内含的
- **英文释义**：suggested though not directly expressed; implied
- **例句**：
  - There was implicit criticism in her polite refusal.
  - 她礼貌的拒绝中带有含蓄的批评。
  - Trust is implicit in a good friendship.
  - 真挚友谊中信任是不言而喻的。
- **助记**：im（向内）+ plic → 折在里面 → 含蓄

### 7. **paramount** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：至高无上的；首要的；最重要的
- **英文释义**：more important than anything else; supreme
- **例句**：
  - Safety is of paramount importance on a construction site.
  - 在建筑工地上，安全至关重要。
  - The judge stressed that justice was the paramount concern in the trial.
  - 法官强调审判中公正是最重要的考量。
- **助记**：para（超越）+ mount（山）→ 超越群山 → 首要

### 8. **perpetual** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：永恒的；持续的；无休止的
- **英文释义**：never ending or changing; occurring repeatedly
- **例句**：
  - The perpetual noise from the highway disturbed nearby residents.
  - 公路上无休止的噪音打扰了附近居民。
  - They hoped for perpetual peace between the two nations.
  - 他们希望两国之间永享和平。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ pet（追求）+ ual → 一直追求下去 → 持续

### 9. **precede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：先于；在……之前发生或出现
- **英文释义**：to come before something in time, order, or rank
- **例句**：
  - Lunch will be preceded by a short speech from the principal.
  - 午餐前校长将作简短讲话。
  - Thunder often precedes lightning by a few seconds in a storm.
  - 暴风雨中雷声通常比闪电早几秒。
- **助记**：pre（前）+ cede（走）→ 走在前面 → 先于

### 10. **retrieve** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：取回；重新得到；检索（信息）
- **英文释义**：to get back or recover something; to fetch stored data
- **例句**：
  - Divers managed to retrieve the lost keys from the bottom of the lake.
  - 潜水员从湖底找回了丢失的钥匙。
  - You can retrieve your saved documents from the cloud anytime.
  - 你可以随时从云端取回已保存的文件。
- **助记**：re（再）+ trieve（找到）→ 再找回来 → 取回

### 高一词汇

### 11. **dichotomy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：二分法；截然对立；双重性
- **英文释义**：a division or contrast between two things that are opposed
- **例句**：
  - The novel explores the dichotomy between city life and rural traditions.
  - 这部小说探讨城市生活与乡村传统之间的对立。
  - There is a sharp dichotomy in public opinion on the new law.
  - 公众对新法的看法存在尖锐分歧。
- **助记**：dicho（一分为二）+ tomy（切）→ 切成两半 → 二分

### 12. **esoteric** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：深奥的；秘传的；只有内行才懂的
- **英文释义**：intended for or understood by only a small group with special knowledge
- **例句**：
  - The professor's lecture on quantum theory was too esoteric for most freshmen.
  - 教授关于量子理论的讲座对大多数大一新生来说过于深奥。
  - The club discussed esoteric topics in philosophy late into the night.
  - 俱乐部成员深夜讨论哲学中的深奥话题。
- **助记**：eso（向内）+ ter + ic → 藏在内部的 → 秘传/深奥

### 13. **juxtapose** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：并列；并置；把……放在一起对照
- **英文释义**：to place two things side by side for comparison or contrast
- **例句**：
  - The exhibition juxtaposes ancient tools with modern technology.
  - 展览将古代工具与现代技术并置对照。
  - The author juxtaposes humor and tragedy in the same chapter.
  - 作者在同一章中把幽默与悲剧并置。
- **助记**：juxta（靠近）+ pose（放置）→ 并置

### 14. **ostensible** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：表面上的；貌似真实的；公开宣称的
- **英文释义**：stated or appearing to be true, but not necessarily so
- **例句**：
  - His ostensible reason for leaving early was a headache; many doubted it.
  - 他提早离开的表面理由是头痛；许多人表示怀疑。
  - The ostensible goal of the meeting was team building.
  - 会议表面上的目标是团队建设。
- **助记**：ostens（展示）+ ible → 展示出来的 → 表面的

### 15. **proliferate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：激增；扩散；迅速繁殖
- **英文释义**：to increase rapidly in number; to multiply
- **例句**：
  - Fast-food outlets proliferate along the new highway.
  - 快餐店沿着新公路迅速增多。
  - Misinformation can proliferate online within minutes.
  - 错误信息可在几分钟内在网上扩散。
- **助记**：proli（后代）+ fer + ate → 不断产生后代 → 激增
