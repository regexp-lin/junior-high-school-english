## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **alleviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和；缓解
- **英文释义**：to make pain, suffering, or a problem less severe
- **例句**：
  - Medicine can alleviate the symptoms but not cure the disease.
  - 药物能缓解症状但不能治愈疾病。
  - Planting trees helps alleviate air pollution in cities.
  - 种树有助于缓解城市空气污染。
- **助记**：al（加强）+ levi（轻）+ ate → 使变轻 → 减轻

### 2. **benevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；慈善的；善意的
- **英文释义**：well-meaning and kindly; charitable
- **例句**：
  - The benevolent donor funded scholarships for underprivileged students.
  - 慈善捐赠者资助了贫困学生的奖学金。
  - A benevolent smile spread across the teacher's face.
  - 老师脸上露出慈祥的微笑。
- **助记**：bene（好）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 好意的 → 仁慈的

### 3. **candid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坦率的；直言不讳的；真诚的
- **英文释义**：truthful and straightforward; frank
- **例句**：
  - She gave a candid opinion about the project's weaknesses.
  - 她对项目弱点给出了坦率的意见。
  - The interview was surprisingly candid and revealing.
  - 这次采访出人意料地坦诚且富有启发。
- **助记**：cand（白/光）+ id → 像白光般透明 → 坦率

### 4. **deter** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻止；威慑；使打消念头
- **英文释义**：to discourage someone from doing something through fear or doubt
- **例句**：
  - High fines deter drivers from speeding on highways.
  - 高额罚款能阻止司机在高速公路上超速。
  - The cold weather did not deter the hikers from climbing.
  - 寒冷的天气没有阻止徒步者攀登。
- **助记**：de（离开）+ ter（恐吓）→ 吓退 → 阻止

### 5. **eloquent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：雄辩的；有口才的；传神的
- **英文释义**：fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing; vividly expressive
- **例句**：
  - Martin Luther King Jr. was known for his eloquent speeches.
  - 马丁·路德·金以雄辩的演讲闻名。
  - Her silence was more eloquent than any words could be.
  - 她的沉默比任何言语都更传神。
- **助记**：e（出）+ loqu（说）+ ent → 说出来的 → 雄辩的

### 6. **fathom** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：理解；领悟；英寻（水深单位）
- **英文释义**：to understand after much thought; a unit of water depth (6 feet)
- **例句**：
  - Scientists still cannot fathom the full depth of the ocean.
  - 科学家仍无法完全理解海洋的全部深度。
  - I cannot fathom why he would refuse such a generous offer.
  - 我无法理解他为何拒绝如此慷慨的提议。
- **助记**：fathom 原指测水深 → 探索深度 → 理解

### 7. **gratify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使满足；使高兴；满足（欲望）
- **英文释义**：to give pleasure or satisfaction to; to indulge
- **例句**：
  - It gratified her to see students succeed after years of hard work.
  - 看到学生多年努力后成功让她深感欣慰。
  - Fast food gratifies hunger quickly but harms health slowly.
  - 快餐能迅速满足饥饿感但慢慢损害健康。
- **助记**：grat（感激/高兴）+ ify → 使高兴 → 使满足

### 8. **haughty** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：傲慢的；目中无人的；自大的
- **英文释义**：arrogantly superior and disdainful; snobbish
- **例句**：
  - The haughty manager refused to listen to suggestions from juniors.
  - 傲慢的经理拒绝听取下属的建议。
  - Her haughty tone irritated everyone in the meeting room.
  - 她目中无人的语气让会议室里所有人恼火。
- **助记**：haught（高）+ y → 高高在上 → 傲慢的

### 9. **impede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：妨碍；阻碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to delay or prevent by obstructing; to hinder
- **例句**：
  - Bureaucratic red tape can impede the progress of new businesses.
  - 官僚主义繁文缛节会阻碍新企业的发展。
  - Heavy traffic impeded emergency vehicles from reaching the scene.
  - 拥堵的交通阻碍了急救车辆到达现场。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ ped（脚）+ e → 绊住脚 → 阻碍

### 10. **jeopardize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：危及；损害；使处于危险中
- **英文释义**：to put something or someone at risk of loss or harm
- **例句**：
  - Cheating on exams can jeopardize your entire academic career.
  - 考试作弊可能危及你整个学业生涯。
  - Poor diet choices jeopardize long-term health.
  - 不良饮食选择会危害长期健康。
- **助记**：jeopard（危险）+ ize → 使危险 → 危及

### 高一词汇

### 11. **languid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：慵懒的；无精打采的；缓慢的
- **英文释义**：displaying or having a disinclination for physical exertion; slow and relaxed
- **例句**：
  - The cat stretched in a languid pose on the sunny windowsill.
  - 猫在阳光照射的窗台上慵懒地伸展身体。
  - Summer afternoons have a languid quality that invites napping.
  - 夏日午后有一种令人想小睡的慵懒感。
- **助记**：langu（疲倦）+ id → 疲倦的 → 慵懒的

### 12. **magnanimous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；慷慨的；高尚的
- **英文释义**：very generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or less powerful person
- **例句**：
  - The winner was magnanimous in victory, praising the opponent's effort.
  - 获胜者在胜利时很大度，赞扬了对手的努力。
  - A magnanimous leader earns loyalty through kindness, not fear.
  - 宽宏大量的领导者通过善意而非恐惧赢得忠诚。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 大心胸 → 宽宏大量

### 13. **nihilism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：虚无主义；怀疑一切的态度
- **英文释义**：the rejection of all religious and moral principles; a belief that life is meaningless
- **例句**：
  - The novel explores nihilism through a character who questions all values.
  - 小说通过一个质疑一切价值观的角色探讨虚无主义。
  - Critics argue that extreme nihilism leads to apathy and inaction.
  - 批评者认为极端虚无主义会导致冷漠与不作为。
- **助记**：nihil（无/零）+ ism → 什么都没有 → 虚无主义

### 14. **obsequious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：谄媚的；奉承的；卑躬屈膝的
- **英文释义**：obedient or attentive to an excessive degree; servile
- **例句**：
  - The obsequious assistant agreed with every word the boss said.
  - 谄媚的助理对老板说的每句话都表示赞同。
  - Obsequious flattery is easy to detect and hard to respect.
  - 卑躬屈膝的奉承容易被识破且难以令人尊重。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ sequ（跟随）+ ious → 紧紧跟随 → 谄媚

### 15. **parsimonious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：吝啬的；过分节俭的；小气的
- **英文释义**：unwilling to spend money or use resources; stingy
- **例句**：
  - The parsimonious landlord refused to repair the leaking roof.
  - 吝啬的房东拒绝修缮漏水的屋顶。
  - Being parsimonious with praise can damage team morale.
  - 吝惜赞美会损害团队士气。
- **助记**：parsim（节省）+ onious → 过度节省 → 吝啬
