## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **emaciate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使消瘦；使憔悴
- **英文释义**：to make abnormally thin or weak, typically through illness or lack of food
- **例句**：
  - The long famine emaciated the villagers, leaving them barely able to stand.
  - 漫长的饥荒使村民们消瘦不堪，几乎站不住。
  - The stray dog was severely emaciated when the rescue team found it.
  - 流浪狗被救援队发现时已严重消瘦。
- **助记**：e（出）+ maci（瘦）+ ate → 变得瘦出去 → 使消瘦

### 2. **spurious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚假的；伪造的；站不住脚的
- **英文释义**：not being what it purports to be; false or fake
- **例句**：
  - The detective quickly identified the spurious evidence planted at the crime scene.
  - 侦探迅速辨认出了在犯罪现场放置的虚假证据。
  - His spurious claims of expertise were exposed when he couldn't answer basic questions.
  - 当他无法回答基本问题时，他虚假的专业声称被揭穿了。
- **助记**：spur（刺/虚假）+ ious → 带刺的/虚假的 → 伪造的

### 3. **ruminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：深思；反复思考；反刍
- **英文释义**：to think deeply about something; to chew the cud
- **例句**：
  - He spent the weekend ruminating over whether to accept the job offer.
  - 他花了一个周末反复思考是否接受那份工作。
  - It's unhealthy to ruminate endlessly on past mistakes instead of moving forward.
  - 无休止地反复思考过去的错误而不向前看是不健康的。
- **助记**：rumin（反刍）+ ate → 像牛反刍一样反复咀嚼 → 深思

### 4. **truculent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；粗暴的；蛮横的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant
- **例句**：
  - The truculent customer shouted at the cashier over a minor pricing error.
  - 蛮横的顾客因一个小小的价格错误冲收银员大喊大叫。
  - His truculent attitude during negotiations made it impossible to reach a compromise.
  - 他在谈判中好斗的态度使达成妥协变得不可能。
- **助记**：truc（凶猛）+ ulent → 凶猛的 → 好斗的

### 5. **supplant** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：取代；替代；排挤
- **英文释义**：to supersede and replace
- **例句**：
  - Digital streaming has largely supplanted traditional television in many households.
  - 数字流媒体在许多家庭中已基本取代了传统电视。
  - The new manager was accused of trying to supplant the company's founder.
  - 新经理被指控试图排挤公司创始人。
- **助记**：sup（下面）+ plant（种植）→ 从下面种植取而代之 → 取代

### 6. **pellucid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：清澈的；透明的；明晰的
- **英文释义**：translucently clear; easily understood
- **例句**：
  - The pellucid waters of the mountain lake revealed every pebble on the bottom.
  - 山湖清澈的水让湖底的每颗鹅卵石清晰可见。
  - Her pellucid explanation made the complex theory accessible to all students.
  - 她明晰的解释使复杂的理论让所有学生都能理解。
- **助记**：pel（穿过）+ lucid（光/清晰）→ 光能穿过 → 清澈的

### 7. **expurgate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：删除（不当内容）；净化；审查删减
- **英文释义**：to remove matter thought to be objectionable or unsuitable from a text
- **例句**：
  - The publisher expurgated several controversial passages before reprinting the novel.
  - 出版商在重新印刷小说前删除了几段有争议的段落。
  - An expurgated version of the film was released for younger audiences.
  - 一个删减版的电影发行给了年轻观众。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ purg（清洁）+ ate → 清除出去 → 删除不当内容

### 8. **querulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱抱怨的；牢骚满腹的；吹毛求疵的
- **英文释义**：complaining in a petulant or whining manner
- **例句**：
  - The querulous patient constantly complained about the hospital food and service.
  - 爱抱怨的病人不断抱怨医院的食物和服务。
  - Her querulous tone annoyed her coworkers, who wished she would focus on solutions.
  - 她牢骚满腹的语气惹恼了同事们，他们希望她能专注于解决问题。
- **助记**：querul（抱怨）+ ous → 爱抱怨的

### 9. **restitution** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：归还；赔偿；恢复
- **英文释义**：the restoration of something lost or stolen; compensation for injury or loss
- **例句**：
  - The court ordered the company to pay restitution to the victims of the fraud.
  - 法院命令该公司向欺诈受害者支付赔偿。
  - The museum demanded the restitution of artifacts that had been illegally exported.
  - 博物馆要求归还被非法出口的文物。
- **助记**：re（回）+ stitut（建立）+ ion → 重新建立/恢复 → 归还/赔偿

### 10. **amalgamate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；混合；联合
- **英文释义**：to combine or unite to form one organization or structure
- **例句**：
  - The two small schools amalgamated to form a larger, more efficient institution.
  - 两所小学合并成为一个更大、更高效的机构。
  - The chef amalgamated various cuisines to create a unique fusion menu.
  - 厨师融合了各种菜系，创造了独特的融合菜单。
- **助记**：a + malgam（混合物）+ ate → 变成混合物 → 合并

### 高一词汇

### 11. **tmesis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：词语插入法（在复合词中间插入另一个词）
- **英文释义**：the separation of parts of a compound word by an intervening word or words
- **例句**：
  - "Abso-blooming-lutely" is a humorous example of tmesis in colloquial English.
  - "Abso-blooming-lutely"是口语英语中词语插入法的一个幽默例子。
  - The poet used tmesis to add emphasis and rhythm to the verse.
  - 诗人使用词语插入法来增强诗句的强调和韵律。
- **助记**：tmesis 源自希腊语 temnein（切割）→ 把词切开插入 → 词语插入法

### 12. **palimpsest** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：重写本；有覆盖痕迹的事物
- **英文释义**：a manuscript on which earlier writing has been erased and new text written over it; something reused or altered but still bearing traces of its earlier form
- **例句**：
  - The ancient palimpsest revealed traces of a previously unknown Greek text beneath the visible writing.
  - 这份古代重写本在可见文字下面显露出一篇此前未知的希腊文本的痕迹。
  - The city itself is a palimpsest, with layers of history visible in its architecture.
  - 这座城市本身就是一个重写本，建筑中可见层层叠叠的历史。
- **助记**：palin（再次）+ psest（擦）→ 擦去重写 → 重写本

### 13. **shibboleth** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：口令；判断标志；陈规陋习
- **英文释义**：a custom, principle, or belief distinguishing a particular class or group; a word used to identify members of a group
- **例句**：
  - Pronunciation of certain words serves as a shibboleth to identify regional origins.
  - 某些词的发音充当了辨别地区来源的标志。
  - The old management shibboleths about strict hierarchy are being replaced by modern collaborative approaches.
  - 关于严格等级制度的旧管理陈规正被现代协作方式所取代。
- **助记**：shibboleth 源自《圣经》中用发音区分敌友的故事 → 判断标志

### 14. **plangent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：（声音）洪亮而悲伤的；回荡的；哀婉的
- **英文释义**：having a loud, reverberating, and often mournful sound
- **例句**：
  - The plangent notes of the cello filled the concert hall with deep emotion.
  - 大提琴哀婉的音符让整个音乐厅充满了深沉的情感。
  - The plangent cry of seagulls echoed across the empty harbor at dawn.
  - 海鸥哀婉的叫声在黎明时分回荡在空旷的港口上空。
- **助记**：plang（拍打/哀号）+ ent → 拍打发出哀号 → 悲伤洪亮的

### 15. **autochthonous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：土生土长的；原产的；本地的
- **英文释义**：indigenous rather than descended from migrants or colonists
- **例句**：
  - The autochthonous tribes of the region have maintained their traditions for centuries.
  - 该地区土生土长的部落几个世纪以来一直保持着他们的传统。
  - Scientists discovered an autochthonous species of fern that exists nowhere else on Earth.
  - 科学家发现了一种地球上其他地方没有的原产蕨类植物。
- **助记**：auto（自身）+ chthon（土地）+ ous → 自己土地上的 → 土生土长的
