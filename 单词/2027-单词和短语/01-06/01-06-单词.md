## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **campaign** /kæmˈpeɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：运动；战役
- **英文释义**：a series of planned activities to achieve a social or political goal; a military operation
- **例句**：
  - The school launched a campaign against bullying.
  - 学校发起了反对校园欺凌的运动。
  - She campaigned for women's rights for many years.
  - 她为女权运动奔走多年。
- **助记**：camp（营地）+ aign → 在营地中发起行动 → 运动；战役

### 2. **capable** /ˈkeɪpəbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有能力的；能干的
- **英文释义**：having the ability or qualities needed to do something
- **例句**：
  - She is a very capable teacher.
  - 她是一位非常能干的老师。
  - He is capable of solving this problem by himself.
  - 他有能力独自解决这个问题。
- **助记**：cap（抓住）+ -able → 能抓住机会的 → 有能力的

### 3. **capture** /ˈkæptʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：捕获；夺取
- **英文释义**：to catch someone or something; to succeed in recording or describing something
- **例句**：
  - The police captured the escaped prisoner.
  - 警察抓获了逃犯。
  - The photograph captured the beauty of the sunset.
  - 这张照片捕捉到了日落的美。
- **助记**：capt（抓）+ -ure → 抓住 → 捕获

### 4. **casual** /ˈkæʒuəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：随意的；非正式的
- **英文释义**：relaxed and not worried; not formal
- **例句**：
  - He was dressed in casual clothes.
  - 他穿着休闲衣服。
  - It was just a casual remark, don't take it seriously.
  - 那只是随口一说，别当真。
- **助记**：casu-（偶然）+ -al → 偶然的/不经意的 → 随意的

### 5. **category** /ˈkætəɡəri/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：类别；种类
- **英文释义**：a group of things with similar features
- **例句**：
  - Books are divided into different categories in the library.
  - 图书馆的书被分成不同的类别。
  - This product falls into the luxury category.
  - 这个产品属于奢侈品类别。
- **助记**：cate-（完全）+ gory（集合）→ 完全归入一组 → 类别

### 6. **cease** /siːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：停止；终止
- **英文释义**：to stop doing something or stop happening
- **例句**：
  - The factory ceased production last month.
  - 工厂上个月停止了生产。
  - The rain ceased and the sun came out.
  - 雨停了，太阳出来了。
- **助记**：cease → "息丝"（谐音）→ 像丝线一样断了 → 停止

### 7. **certificate** /səˈtɪfɪkət/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：证书；证明
- **英文释义**：an official document proving that something is true
- **例句**：
  - She received a certificate for completing the course.
  - 她获得了课程结业证书。
  - You need a birth certificate to apply for a passport.
  - 你需要出生证明来申请护照。
- **助记**：certif-（确定）+ -icate → 确定某事的文件 → 证书

### 8. **circumstance** /ˈsɜːkəmstəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：情况；环境
- **英文释义**：the conditions and facts that are connected with a situation
- **例句**：
  - Under no circumstances should you open the door to strangers.
  - 在任何情况下都不应该给陌生人开门。
  - We need to consider all the circumstances before deciding.
  - 做决定前我们需要考虑所有情况。
- **助记**：circum-（周围）+ stance（站立）→ 站在周围的条件 → 情况

### 9. **civilization** /ˌsɪvəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：文明；文化
- **英文释义**：a society with its own culture and way of life
- **例句**：
  - Ancient Egyptian civilization lasted for thousands of years.
  - 古埃及文明延续了数千年。
  - Modern civilization depends heavily on technology.
  - 现代文明高度依赖科技。
- **助记**：civil（公民的）+ -ization → 公民社会的形态 → 文明

### 10. **collapse** /kəˈlæps/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：倒塌；崩溃
- **英文释义**：to fall down suddenly; to fail completely
- **例句**：
  - The old building collapsed during the earthquake.
  - 旧楼在地震中倒塌了。
  - She collapsed from exhaustion after the marathon.
  - 她马拉松后因精疲力竭而晕倒了。
- **助记**：col-（一起）+ lapse（滑倒）→ 一起滑倒 → 倒塌

### 高一词汇

### 11. **coerce** /kəʊˈɜːs/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：强迫；胁迫
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone to do something by using force or threats
- **例句**：
  - They tried to coerce him into signing the contract.
  - 他们试图胁迫他签合同。
  - No one should be coerced into giving up their rights.
  - 没有人应该被强迫放弃自己的权利。
- **助记**：co-（一起）+ erce（关闭）→ 把人关起来逼迫 → 强迫

### 12. **complacent** /kəmˈpleɪsnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：自满的；得意的
- **英文释义**：showing smug satisfaction with oneself without being aware of potential dangers
- **例句**：
  - Don't be complacent just because you passed one exam.
  - 不要因为通过了一次考试就自满。
  - The team became complacent after their early victories.
  - 球队在早期的胜利后变得自满了。
- **助记**：com-（完全）+ plac（满意）+ -ent → 完全满意的 → 自满的

### 13. **conundrum** /kəˈnʌndrəm/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：难题；谜
- **英文释义**：a confusing and difficult problem or question
- **例句**：
  - The energy crisis presents a real conundrum for policymakers.
  - 能源危机给决策者带来了一个真正的难题。
  - How to balance work and family life is a common conundrum.
  - 如何平衡工作与家庭是一个常见的难题。
- **助记**：conundrum → "困难drum（鼓）"（谐音）→ 像鼓一样让人头痛的难题

### 14. **cryptic** /ˈkrɪptɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：神秘的；含义隐晦的
- **英文释义**：having a meaning that is mysterious or not easily understood
- **例句**：
  - She left a cryptic message on my desk.
  - 她在我桌上留了一张含义隐晦的便条。
  - His cryptic smile suggested he knew more than he was saying.
  - 他神秘的微笑暗示他知道的比说的多。
- **助记**：crypt（隐藏/地下室）+ -ic → 隐藏的 → 神秘的

### 15. **cynical** /ˈsɪnɪkl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：愤世嫉俗的；怀疑的
- **英文释义**：believing that people are motivated only by self-interest; distrustful
- **例句**：
  - He became cynical about politics after years of broken promises.
  - 多年的食言让他对政治变得愤世嫉俗。
  - Don't be so cynical — not everyone has bad intentions.
  - 别那么愤世嫉俗——不是每个人都心怀不轨。
- **助记**：cynic（犬儒派）+ -al → 犬儒学派认为人都自私 → 愤世嫉俗的
