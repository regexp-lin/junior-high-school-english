## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **accolade** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：嘉奖；荣誉；表扬
- **英文释义**：an award or expression of praise; an acknowledgment of achievement
- **例句**：
  - The film received the highest accolade at the festival.
  - 该片在电影节上获得了最高荣誉。
  - Winning the scholarship was an accolade for her years of hard work.
  - 获得奖学金是对她多年努力的嘉奖。
- **助记**：ac（向）+ col（颈）→ 骑士授勋拍颈 → 荣誉

### 2. **acquiesce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：默许；勉强同意
- **英文释义**：to accept something reluctantly but without protest
- **例句**：
  - She acquiesced to the plan even though she preferred another option.
  - 尽管更倾向另一方案，她仍勉强同意了该计划。
  - Parents sometimes acquiesce to children’s demands for peace at home.
  - 为求家中安宁，父母有时会默许孩子的要求。
- **助记**：ac（向）+ quiesc（安静）→ 安静接受 → 默许

### 3. **belligerent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；挑衅的；交战国（名词用法）
- **英文释义**：hostile and aggressive; engaged in war (noun: belligerent party)
- **例句**：
  - His belligerent tone made the negotiation difficult.
  - 他挑衅的口吻使谈判困难重重。
  - Neutral countries called on both belligerents to cease fire.
  - 中立国呼吁交战双方停火。
- **助记**：belli（战争）+ ger（带来）→ 带来战争的 → 好斗的

### 4. **capitulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：投降；屈服；不再抵抗
- **英文释义**：to surrender; to stop resisting an opponent or demand
- **例句**：
  - The defenders capitulated after supplies ran out.
  - 补给耗尽后守军投降了。
  - She refused to capitulate to pressure from her peers.
  - 她拒绝向同伴的压力屈服。
- **助记**：capit（头）→ 低头投降 → 屈服

### 5. **disparage** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：贬低；轻视；毁谤
- **英文释义**：to speak slightingly of; to belittle the worth of
- **例句**：
  - It is unfair to disparage her efforts without knowing the facts.
  - 在不了解事实的情况下贬低她的努力是不公平的。
  - Critics disparaged the novel, yet it sold millions of copies.
  - 批评者贬低这部小说，但它仍售出数百万册。
- **助记**：dis（否定）+ par（平等）→ 使不平等 → 贬低

### 6. **engender** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：引起；产生；造成
- **英文释义**：to cause or give rise to a feeling or situation
- **例句**：
  - Poverty can engender a sense of hopelessness among youth.
  - 贫困可能在年轻人中引起绝望感。
  - The policy was intended to engender trust between communities.
  - 该政策旨在增进社区间的信任。
- **助记**：en（使）+ gender（产生）→ 使产生 → 引起

### 7. **fortify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加强；设防；补充营养
- **英文释义**：to strengthen physically or mentally; to add nutrients to food
- **例句**：
  - They fortified the walls against possible attack.
  - 他们加固城墙以防袭击。
  - Orange juice is fortified with vitamin D in some countries.
  - 有些国家的橙汁会添加维生素 D。
- **助记**：fort（强）+ ify → 使坚固 → 加强

### 8. **galvanize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：激励；刺激；电镀
- **英文释义**：to shock or excite someone into action; to coat with zinc
- **例句**：
  - The tragedy galvanized the community into supporting new safety laws.
  - 悲剧激励社区支持新的安全法规。
  - Fear of failure can galvanize students to study harder.
  - 对失败的恐惧能促使学生更用功。
- **助记**：来自 Galvani（电流实验）→ 像触电般激励 → 激励

### 9. **heed** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：注意；听从；留心
- **英文释义**：to pay careful attention to advice or warning
- **例句**：
  - Few people heeded the warning until the storm arrived.
  - 暴风雨来临前，很少有人把警告当回事。
  - Take heed of the signs along the hiking trail.
  - 注意徒步路线旁的标识。
- **助记**：与 hark（听）同源 → 倾听 → 注意

### 10. **inundate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：淹没；使应接不暇；大量涌来
- **英文释义**：to overwhelm with a great quantity of something; to flood
- **例句**：
  - Heavy rain inundated the low-lying neighborhoods.
  - 大雨淹没了地势低的街区。
  - The help desk was inundated with calls after the system crashed.
  - 系统崩溃后，服务台电话应接不暇。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ und（波浪）→ 波浪涌入 → 淹没

### 高一词汇

### 11. **iconoclast** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：反传统者；打破偶像者
- **英文释义**：a person who attacks cherished beliefs or institutions
- **例句**：
  - The artist was an iconoclast who rejected academic conventions.
  - 这位艺术家是反对学院派陈规的反传统者。
  - Economic iconoclasts challenged the wisdom of austerity policies.
  - 经济领域的反传统者质疑紧缩政策的明智性。
- **助记**：icon（圣像）+ clast（打破）→ 打破偶像 → 反传统者

### 12. **jettison** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：抛弃；丢弃；（航空）投弃载荷
- **英文释义**：to throw or drop something from an aircraft or ship; to abandon
- **例句**：
  - The crew jettisoned fuel to make an emergency landing.
  - 机组放油以实施紧急着陆。
  - The company jettisoned outdated software to cut costs.
  - 公司为削减成本抛弃了过时软件。
- **助记**：jet（抛）→ 从飞机上抛下 → 抛弃

### 13. **kleptocracy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：盗贼统治；贪官当道的政权
- **英文释义**：a government by corrupt leaders who exploit resources for personal gain
- **例句**：
  - Investigators described the regime as a kleptocracy siphoning oil wealth.
  - 调查人员称该政权为抽走石油财富的盗贼统治。
  - Kleptocracy undermines trust in public institutions.
  - 盗贼统治会削弱公众对机构的信任。
- **助记**：klepto（偷）+ cracy（统治）→ 窃国者治 → 盗贼统治

### 14. **laconic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：（说话）简洁的；寡言的
- **英文释义**：using very few words; concise to the point of rudeness
- **例句**：
  - His laconic reply left everyone guessing his real opinion.
  - 他简短的回答让大家猜不透真实想法。
  - The laconic style of the report suited busy executives.
  - 报告的简洁风格适合忙碌的高管。
- **助记**：来自 Laconia（斯巴达）→ 斯巴达式简短 → 简洁寡言

### 15. **machination** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：阴谋；诡计（常用复数 machinations）
- **英文释义**：a secret and complicated plot, especially for harmful purposes
- **例句**：
  - The novel exposes the political machinations behind the treaty.
  - 小说揭露了条约背后的政治阴谋。
  - She was tired of office machinations and sought a simpler job.
  - 她厌倦了办公室里的勾心斗角，想找一份更简单的工作。
- **助记**：machina（机器/机关）→ 暗中机关 → 阴谋
