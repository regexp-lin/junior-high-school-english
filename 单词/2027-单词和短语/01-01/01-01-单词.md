## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abandon** /əˈbændən/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：放弃；抛弃
- **英文释义**：to give up completely; to leave someone or something permanently
- **例句**：
  - They had to abandon the car in the snow.
  - 他们不得不把车丢在雪地里。
  - Never abandon your dreams no matter how hard life gets.
  - 无论生活多艰难，都不要放弃梦想。
- **助记**：a（一个）+ band（乐队）+ on → 一个乐队解散了 → 放弃；抛弃

### 2. **absorb** /əbˈzɔːb/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：吸收；吸引
- **英文释义**：to take in a liquid, gas, or other substance; to hold someone's attention completely
- **例句**：
  - Plants absorb water through their roots.
  - 植物通过根部吸收水分。
  - She was completely absorbed in her book.
  - 她完全沉浸在书中。
- **助记**：ab-（加强）+ sorb（吸）→ 加强吸收 → 吸收

### 3. **abundant** /əˈbʌndənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：丰富的；充裕的
- **英文释义**：existing in very large quantities; more than enough
- **例句**：
  - The region has abundant natural resources.
  - 该地区拥有丰富的自然资源。
  - There was abundant evidence to prove his innocence.
  - 有充分的证据证明他无罪。
- **助记**：ab-（加强）+ und（波浪）+ -ant → 像波浪一样涌来 → 丰富的

### 4. **accomplish** /əˈkʌmplɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：完成；实现
- **英文释义**：to succeed in doing or completing something
- **例句**：
  - She accomplished all her goals before the age of 30.
  - 她在30岁前实现了所有目标。
  - We accomplished the task ahead of schedule.
  - 我们提前完成了任务。
- **助记**：ac-（朝向）+ complish（填满）→ 把事情做满 → 完成

### 5. **accurate** /ˈækjərət/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：精确的；准确的
- **英文释义**：correct and true in every detail
- **例句**：
  - The report provides an accurate description of the events.
  - 这份报告对事件做了准确的描述。
  - Is this clock accurate?
  - 这个钟准吗？
- **助记**：ac-（朝向）+ cur（关心）+ -ate → 非常用心做 → 精确的

### 6. **accuse** /əˈkjuːz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：指控；控告
- **英文释义**：to say that someone has done something wrong or illegal
- **例句**：
  - He was accused of stealing money from the company.
  - 他被指控从公司偷钱。
  - Don't accuse me without any proof!
  - 没有证据不要指控我！
- **助记**：ac-（朝向）+ cuse（原因）→ 把原因指向某人 → 指控

### 7. **adapt** /əˈdæpt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：适应；改编
- **英文释义**：to change your behaviour to deal with a new situation; to change a book or play for a film
- **例句**：
  - It took him a while to adapt to the new school.
  - 他花了一些时间适应新学校。
  - The novel was adapted into a successful movie.
  - 这部小说被改编成了一部成功的电影。
- **助记**：ad-（朝向）+ apt（适合的）→ 让自己变得适合 → 适应

### 8. **adequate** /ˈædɪkwət/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：足够的；适当的
- **英文释义**：enough in quantity or good enough in quality for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - Make sure you get adequate sleep before the exam.
  - 确保考试前有充足的睡眠。
  - The hotel was adequate but nothing special.
  - 这家酒店还行，但没什么特别的。
- **助记**：ad-（朝向）+ equ（相等）+ -ate → 与需要相等 → 足够的

### 9. **admire** /ədˈmaɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：钦佩；赞赏
- **英文释义**：to respect someone for what they have done; to look at something and think it is attractive
- **例句**：
  - I really admire her courage and determination.
  - 我真的很钦佩她的勇气和决心。
  - We stopped to admire the beautiful sunset.
  - 我们停下来欣赏美丽的日落。
- **助记**：ad-（朝向）+ mire（惊奇）→ 对某人感到惊奇 → 钦佩

### 10. **adolescent** /ˌædəˈlesnt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：青少年（的）
- **英文释义**：a young person who is developing from a child into an adult
- **例句**：
  - Adolescents often face peer pressure at school.
  - 青少年在学校经常面临同伴压力。
  - She is going through a difficult adolescent phase.
  - 她正经历一个困难的青春期阶段。
- **助记**：adol-（成长）+ -escent（开始）→ 开始成长的人 → 青少年

### 高一词汇

### 11. **aberration** /ˌæbəˈreɪʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：偏差；反常行为
- **英文释义**：a fact, action, or way of behaving that is not usual or expected
- **例句**：
  - His rude behaviour was an aberration; he is usually very polite.
  - 他的粗鲁行为只是一时失常；他平时很有礼貌。
  - The warm weather in January was an aberration.
  - 一月份的温暖天气是反常现象。
- **助记**：ab-（偏离）+ err（错误）+ -ation → 偏离正轨 → 偏差；反常

### 12. **abscond** /əbˈskɒnd/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：潜逃；逃跑
- **英文释义**：to escape from a place where you are kept, or to leave secretly
- **例句**：
  - The thief absconded with all the jewels.
  - 小偷带着所有珠宝潜逃了。
  - He absconded from prison last night.
  - 他昨晚从监狱逃跑了。
- **助记**：abs-（离开）+ cond（藏）→ 藏着离开 → 潜逃

### 13. **abstinent** /ˈæbstɪnənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：节制的；禁欲的
- **英文释义**：choosing not to do or have something, especially something enjoyable
- **例句**：
  - He has been abstinent from alcohol for five years.
  - 他已经戒酒五年了。
  - The monks lead an abstinent life.
  - 僧侣们过着节制的生活。
- **助记**：abs-（离开）+ tin（拿住）+ -ent → 把自己从诱惑中拿开 → 节制的

### 14. **acrimonious** /ˌækrɪˈməʊniəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：尖酸的；激烈的
- **英文释义**：angry and full of strong bitter feelings and words
- **例句**：
  - The meeting ended in an acrimonious argument.
  - 会议在一场激烈的争吵中结束。
  - Their acrimonious divorce lasted for two years.
  - 他们激烈的离婚官司持续了两年。
- **助记**：acri-（尖锐）+ -monious → 言辞尖锐的 → 尖酸的

### 15. **adamant** /ˈædəmənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：坚定不移的；固执的
- **英文释义**：impossible to persuade or to change an opinion or decision
- **例句**：
  - She was adamant that she would not change her mind.
  - 她坚定地表示不会改变主意。
  - He remained adamant in his refusal to apologize.
  - 他坚决不道歉。
- **助记**：adam（亚当）+ ant → 像亚当一样固执（不听劝） → 坚定不移的
