## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **assert** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：断言；主张；维护
- **英文释义**：to state a fact or belief confidently and forcefully
- **例句**：
  - She asserted her innocence despite the mounting evidence against her.
  - 尽管对她不利的证据越来越多，她仍断言自己是无辜的。
- **助记**：as（向）+ sert（连接/放置）→ 坚定地放置自己的观点 → 断言

### 2. **contrive** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：设法做到；策划；发明
- **英文释义**：to create or bring about something by deliberate use of skill and artifice
- **例句**：
  - She contrived a clever plan to solve the company's financial crisis.
  - 她策划了一个巧妙的计划来解决公司的财务危机。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ trive（发明）→ 一起想办法发明 → 设法做到

### 3. **deviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：偏离；偏差；背离
- **英文释义**：to depart from an established course or from usual standards
- **例句**：
  - The bus deviated from its regular route due to road construction.
  - 由于道路施工，公交车偏离了常规路线。
- **助记**：de（离开）+ via（路）+ ate → 离开路线 → 偏离

### 4. **encumber** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：妨碍；拖累；使负担
- **英文释义**：to restrict or burden someone or something in a way that makes movement or action difficult
- **例句**：
  - The hikers were encumbered by their heavy backpacks on the steep trail.
  - 徒步者在陡峭的小路上被沉重的背包拖累。
- **助记**：en（使）+ cumber（负担）→ 使有负担 → 妨碍/拖累

### 5. **gallant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：英勇的；殷勤的；堂堂的
- **英文释义**：brave and heroic; chivalrous; attentive to women
- **例句**：
  - The gallant firefighters risked their lives to rescue the trapped family.
  - 英勇的消防员冒着生命危险去营救被困的家庭。
- **助记**：gall（胆量）+ ant → 有胆量的 → 英勇的

### 6. **inconspicuous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不引人注目的；不显眼的
- **英文释义**：not clearly visible or attracting attention; not conspicuous
- **例句**：
  - The spy chose an inconspicuous corner of the café for the secret meeting.
  - 间谍选了咖啡馆一个不起眼的角落进行秘密会面。
- **助记**：in（不）+ conspicuous（显眼的）→ 不显眼的 → 不引人注目的

### 7. **malice** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：恶意；敌意；怨恨
- **英文释义**：the intention or desire to do evil; ill will
- **例句**：
  - The attack was carried out with deliberate malice, not by accident.
  - 这次攻击是蓄意的恶行，并非偶然。
- **助记**：mal（坏）+ ice → 坏的心态 → 恶意

### 8. **placate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：安抚；抚慰；平息
- **英文释义**：to make someone less angry or hostile
- **例句**：
  - The company offered a discount to placate the dissatisfied customers.
  - 公司提供了折扣来安抚不满的顾客。
- **助记**：plac（平静）+ ate → 使平静 → 安抚

### 9. **subsidy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：补贴；补助金；津贴
- **英文释义**：a sum of money granted by the government or a public body to help an industry or business
- **例句**：
  - The government provides subsidies to farmers to keep food prices stable.
  - 政府向农民提供补贴以保持食品价格稳定。
- **助记**：sub（在下面）+ sid（坐）+ y → 在下面支撑 → 补贴

### 10. **vouch** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：担保；保证；证明
- **英文释义**：to assert or confirm as a result of one's own experience
- **例句**：
  - I can vouch for her honesty — she has never lied to me.
  - 我可以为她的诚实作担保——她从未对我撒谎。
- **助记**：vouch → 联想"我确"→ 我确定/我担保 → 担保

### 高一词汇

### 11. **bombastic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：夸大其词的；浮夸的；言过其实的
- **英文释义**：high-sounding but with little meaning; inflated
- **例句**：
  - The politician's bombastic promises failed to convince the skeptical voters.
  - 这位政客夸大其词的承诺未能说服持怀疑态度的选民。
- **助记**：bomb（炸弹）+ astic → 像炸弹一样夸张地爆炸 → 夸大其词的

### 12. **dilettante** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：涉猎者；业余爱好者；半吊子
- **英文释义**：a person who cultivates an area of interest without real commitment or knowledge
- **例句**：
  - He was dismissed as a dilettante who dabbled in art without serious dedication.
  - 他被认为是一个涉猎艺术但没有认真投入的半吊子。
- **助记**：dilettante → delight（快乐）→ 只为快乐而做，不深入 → 业余爱好者

### 13. **insipid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：乏味的；无趣的；淡而无味的
- **英文释义**：lacking flavor; not interesting or exciting; dull
- **例句**：
  - The movie received poor reviews due to its insipid plot and flat characters.
  - 这部电影因其乏味的情节和平淡的角色而收到了差评。
- **助记**：in（不）+ sipid（有味道的）→ 没有味道的 → 乏味的

### 14. **precipitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：险峻的；急剧的；仓促的
- **英文释义**：dangerously high or steep; done suddenly and without careful consideration
- **例句**：
  - The stock market suffered a precipitous decline in a single trading session.
  - 股市在一个交易日内遭受了急剧下跌。
- **助记**：precipit（头朝下落下）+ ous → 像头朝下跌落的 → 险峻的/急剧的

### 15. **sanctimonious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：假装虔诚的；道貌岸然的；伪善的
- **英文释义**：making a show of being morally superior to other people
- **例句**：
  - His sanctimonious lectures about morality annoyed everyone who knew about his own misconduct.
  - 他道貌岸然的道德说教让所有知道他自身不端行为的人都很反感。
- **助记**：sancti（神圣）+ monious → 假装神圣的 → 假装虔诚的
