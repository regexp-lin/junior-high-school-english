## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **adversary** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：对手；敌手；反对者
- **英文释义**：an opponent in a contest, conflict, or dispute
- **例句**：
  - The two athletes treated each other as respected adversaries on the field.
  - 这两位运动员在场上彼此视为值得尊敬的对手。
  - In debate class, you must argue against your assigned adversary’s position.
  - 在辩论课上，你必须反驳指定对手的立场。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ vers（转）+ ary → 转向对立面的人 → 对手

### 2. **benevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；乐善好施的；善意的
- **英文释义**：well-meaning and kindly; charitable
- **例句**：
  - A benevolent donor funded scholarships for rural students.
  - 一位仁慈的捐助者为农村学生提供了奖学金。
  - She spoke in a benevolent tone, offering help without pressure.
  - 她以善意的语气说话，提供帮助却不施加压力。
- **助记**：bene（好）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 好意的 → 仁慈的

### 3. **coerce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：胁迫；强迫；迫使
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone to do something by using force or threats
- **例句**：
  - No one should be coerced into signing a contract they do not understand.
  - 任何人都不应被强迫签署自己不明白的合同。
  - The witness claimed she had been coerced into giving a false statement.
  - 证人声称她曾被迫作虚假陈述。
- **助记**：co（共同）+ erce（驱动）→ 一起推着你做 → 胁迫

### 4. **debilitate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使衰弱；削弱（体力或力量）
- **英文释义**：to make someone or something physically weak
- **例句**：
  - The long illness debilitated him so much that he could barely walk.
  - 久病使他极度虚弱，几乎无法行走。
  - Heat waves can debilitate outdoor workers if breaks are not provided.
  - 若不安排休息，热浪会使户外劳动者体力不支。
- **助记**：de（去掉）+ bilit（力量）+ ate → 去掉力量 → 使衰弱

### 5. **eminent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：杰出的；著名的；显赫的
- **英文释义**：famous and respected within a particular sphere
- **例句**：
  - An eminent scientist was invited to speak at the conference.
  - 一位著名科学家应邀在会议上发言。
  - The city is home to several eminent universities.
  - 这座城市拥有几所杰出的大学。
- **助记**：e（出）+ min（突出）+ ent → 突出于众人 → 杰出的

### 6. **fabricate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：捏造；编造；制造
- **英文释义**：to invent false information; to construct or manufacture
- **例句**：
  - He was accused of fabricating evidence to win the case.
  - 他被指控捏造证据以赢得官司。
  - The workshop fabricates metal parts for aircraft engines.
  - 该车间为飞机发动机制造金属零件。
- **助记**：fabric（织物/结构）+ ate → 编造出结构 → 捏造/制造

### 7. **grievance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：不满；委屈；投诉（尤指对雇主）
- **英文释义**：a real or imagined wrong causing resentment; a formal complaint
- **例句**：
  - Workers filed a grievance over unsafe working conditions.
  - 工人就不安全的工作条件提出了申诉。
  - She aired her grievances calmly during the mediation session.
  - 她在调解过程中冷静地表达了不满。
- **助记**：griev（重，悲伤）+ ance → 心里沉重的事 → 不满

### 8. **hypothetical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：假设的；假定的；推测性的
- **英文释义**：based on a hypothesis; imagined for discussion or argument
- **例句**：
  - Let’s consider a hypothetical situation where fuel prices double.
  - 我们来设想一个油价翻倍的假设情况。
  - The question was purely hypothetical and required no personal answer.
  - 这个问题纯属假设，不需要个人真实回答。
- **助记**：hypo（下）+ thesis（放置）→ 放在下面的前提 → 假设的

### 9. **impede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；延缓
- **英文释义**：to slow or block the progress of something
- **例句**：
  - Heavy snow impeded traffic on the mountain roads.
  - 大雪阻碍了山区公路的交通。
  - Fear of failure should not impede you from trying new things.
  - 对失败的恐惧不应妨碍你尝试新事物。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ ped（脚）→ 把脚缠住 → 阻碍

### 10. **jeopardize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：危及；使陷入危险；损害
- **英文释义**：to put someone or something in a situation where harm or loss is possible
- **例句**：
  - One careless mistake could jeopardize the entire mission.
  - 一次粗心大意可能危及整个任务。
  - Sharing passwords can jeopardize your online security.
  - 共享密码可能危及你的网络安全。
- **助记**：jeopardy（危险）+ ize → 使处于危险 → 危及

### 高一词汇

### 11. **kowtow** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：磕头；卑躬屈膝；唯命是从
- **英文释义**：to act in an excessively subservient way; (literally) to kneel and touch the ground with the forehead
- **例句**：
  - He refused to kowtow to unreasonable demands from the client.
  - 他拒绝对客户的不合理要求唯命是从。
  - The article criticizes leaders who kowtow to foreign pressure.
  - 文章批评那些对外国压力卑躬屈膝的领导人。
- **助记**：来自中文“磕头”音译 → 磕头/屈从

### 12. **labyrinthine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：迷宫般的；错综复杂的；曲折难懂的
- **英文释义**：like a labyrinth; extremely complicated and confusing
- **例句**：
  - The old palace has labyrinthine corridors that confuse tourists.
  - 这座古老宫殿有迷宫般的走廊，让游客迷路。
  - Tax law is labyrinthine and hard for beginners to navigate.
  - 税法错综复杂，初学者难以掌握。
- **助记**：labyrinth（迷宫）+ ine → 像迷宫一样 → 错综复杂的

### 13. **malfeasance** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：渎职；不法行为；（公职人员）不法勾当
- **英文释义**：wrongdoing or illegal conduct, especially by a public official
- **例句**：
  - The mayor was investigated for alleged malfeasance in office.
  - 市长因被指控渎职而受到调查。
  - Auditors uncovered financial malfeasance at the charity.
  - 审计人员在该慈善机构发现了财务不法行为。
- **助记**：mal（坏）+ feas（做）+ ance → 做坏事 → 渎职

### 14. **nihilistic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚无主义的；否定一切价值的
- **英文释义**：rejecting all religious and moral principles; believing life is meaningless
- **例句**：
  - The novel’s protagonist adopts a nihilistic view after repeated failures.
  - 小说主人公在屡次失败后采取了虚无主义态度。
  - Critics warned against nihilistic rhetoric that dismisses all hope.
  - 批评者警告不要采用否定一切希望的虚无主义言论。
- **助记**：nihil（无）+ istic → 认为一切皆无意义 → 虚无主义的

### 15. **obfuscate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使模糊；混淆视听；故意使费解
- **英文释义**：to make something unclear or difficult to understand deliberately
- **例句**：
  - The spokesperson seemed to obfuscate rather than answer directly.
  - 发言人似乎在含糊其辞，而不是直接回答。
  - Jargon can obfuscate simple ideas in policy documents.
  - 行话可能使政策文件中的简单想法变得费解。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ fusc（暗）+ ate → 使变暗 → 使模糊
