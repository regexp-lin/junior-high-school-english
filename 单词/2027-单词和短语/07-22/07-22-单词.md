## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abridge** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：删节；缩短；节略
- **英文释义**：to shorten a book, film, or other work without losing the sense
- **例句**：
  - The publisher released an abridged version of the classic novel for younger readers.
  - 出版社为年轻读者发行了这部经典小说的删节版。
- **助记**：a（向）+ bridge（桥）→ 架桥缩短距离 → 缩短/删节

### 2. **concede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：承认；让步；认输
- **英文释义**：to admit that something is true after first denying it; to surrender or yield
- **例句**：
  - After hours of debate, he finally conceded that the opposing argument had merit.
  - 经过几个小时的辩论，他终于承认对方的论点有道理。
- **助记**：con（完全）+ cede（让出）→ 完全让出 → 承认/让步

### 3. **decipher** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：破译；辨认；解读
- **英文释义**：to succeed in understanding, interpreting, or identifying something
- **例句**：
  - It took experts years to decipher the ancient Egyptian hieroglyphics.
  - 专家们花了数年时间才破译了古埃及象形文字。
- **助记**：de（去除）+ cipher（密码）→ 去除密码 → 破译

### 4. **empathy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：同理心；共情；感同身受
- **英文释义**：the ability to understand and share the feelings of another
- **例句**：
  - A good leader shows empathy toward team members who are going through difficult times.
  - 好的领导者对正在经历困难时期的团队成员表现出同理心。
- **助记**：em（进入）+ pathy（感情）→ 进入他人的感情 → 同理心

### 5. **frantic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：疯狂的；狂乱的；紧张忙乱的
- **英文释义**：wild or distraught with fear, anxiety, or other emotion
- **例句**：
  - The mother made a frantic search for her missing child in the crowded shopping center.
  - 这位母亲在拥挤的购物中心疯狂地寻找她走失的孩子。
- **助记**：frant（疯狂）+ ic → 疯狂的

### 6. **jostle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：推挤；争夺；碰撞
- **英文释义**：to push, elbow, or bump against someone roughly; to compete for something
- **例句**：
  - Fans jostled for position near the stage entrance hoping to see the celebrity.
  - 粉丝们在舞台入口附近互相推挤，希望能看到那位名人。
- **助记**：jostle → 联想"挤(ji)搜(sou)他(t)了(le)"→ 推挤

### 7. **mundane** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：平凡的；世俗的；乏味的
- **英文释义**：lacking interest or excitement; dull; relating to the ordinary world
- **例句**：
  - She longed for adventure to escape the mundane routine of her daily life.
  - 她渴望冒险来逃离日常生活的平凡单调。
- **助记**：mund（世界）+ ane → 世俗世界的 → 平凡的/乏味的

### 8. **procrastinate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：拖延；拖沓；耽搁
- **英文释义**：to delay or postpone action; to put off doing something
- **例句**：
  - Students who procrastinate often end up rushing to finish assignments at the last minute.
  - 拖延的学生往往最终会在最后一刻赶作业。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ crastin（明天）+ ate → 总是推到明天 → 拖延

### 9. **stipulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：规定；约定；明确要求
- **英文释义**：to demand or specify a requirement as part of a bargain or agreement
- **例句**：
  - The contract stipulates that all payments must be made within thirty days.
  - 合同规定所有付款必须在三十天内完成。
- **助记**：stipul（约定/要求）+ ate → 提出要求 → 规定/约定

### 10. **wrath** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：愤怒；盛怒；暴怒
- **英文释义**：extreme anger
- **例句**：
  - The employees feared the wrath of their boss after the project failed miserably.
  - 项目惨败后，员工们害怕老板的盛怒。
- **助记**：wrath → 联想"拉丝（怒气拉丝般蔓延）"→ 愤怒

### 高一词汇

### 11. **clemency** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：仁慈；宽恕；温和
- **英文释义**：mercy; lenience; mildness of weather
- **例句**：
  - The prisoner appealed to the governor for clemency, hoping to have his sentence reduced.
  - 囚犯向州长请求宽恕，希望能减轻刑罚。
- **助记**：clem（温和）+ ency → 温和的态度 → 仁慈/宽恕

### 12. **debilitate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使衰弱；使虚弱；削弱
- **英文释义**：to make someone or something weak and infirm
- **例句**：
  - The prolonged illness debilitated the athlete, forcing her to withdraw from the competition.
  - 长期的疾病使这位运动员身体衰弱，迫使她退出比赛。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ bil（力量）+ itate → 力量向下减弱 → 使衰弱

### 13. **harbinger** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：先兆；先驱；预兆
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that announces or signals the approach of another
- **例句**：
  - The first robin of spring is often seen as a harbinger of warmer weather to come.
  - 春天的第一只知更鸟通常被视为温暖天气即将到来的先兆。
- **助记**：harbor（港口）+ inger → 先到港口的人 → 先驱/先兆

### 14. **obsequious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：谄媚的；奴颜婢膝的；过分恭顺的
- **英文释义**：obedient or attentive to an excessive or servile degree
- **例句**：
  - The obsequious assistant agreed with every word the director said, no matter how unreasonable.
  - 这个谄媚的助理对主管说的每句话都点头称是，不管多不合理。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ sequ（跟随）+ ious → 一直跟着讨好 → 谄媚的

### 15. **recondite** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：深奥的；晦涩的；鲜为人知的
- **英文释义**：little known; abstruse; dealing with very profound or obscure subject matter
- **例句**：
  - The philosopher's recondite theories were accessible only to a small circle of academics.
  - 这位哲学家深奥的理论只有少数学者能理解。
- **助记**：re（回）+ cond（隐藏）+ ite → 隐藏起来的 → 深奥的/晦涩的
