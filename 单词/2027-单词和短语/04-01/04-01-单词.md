## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **aerial** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 空中的；航空的 n. 天线
- **英文释义**：adj. existing, happening, or operating in the air; n. a device for receiving radio or television signals
- **例句**：
  - The aerial view of the city was breathtaking.
  - 城市的鸟瞰景观令人叹为观止。
- **助记**：aer（空气）+ ial → 与空气有关的 → 空中的

### 2. **bribe** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 贿赂 v. 行贿；买通
- **英文释义**：n. a sum of money offered to influence someone's actions; v. to persuade someone with a bribe
- **例句**：
  - The official was arrested for accepting bribes.
  - 这名官员因受贿被逮捕。
- **助记**：bribe 谐音"不来不？"→ 给钱才来 → 贿赂

### 3. **coral** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：珊瑚
- **英文释义**：a hard stony substance formed by the skeletons of marine organisms
- **例句**：
  - The Great Barrier Reef is made up of billions of tiny coral.
  - 大堡礁由数十亿微小的珊瑚组成。
- **助记**：cor（核心）+ al → 海洋的核心/宝石 → 珊瑚

### 4. **dynasty** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：王朝；朝代
- **英文释义**：a line of rulers from the same family
- **例句**：
  - The Tang Dynasty was one of the most prosperous periods in Chinese history.
  - 唐朝是中国历史上最繁荣的时期之一。
- **助记**：dyn（力量）+ asty → 有力量的统治家族 → 王朝

### 5. **flicker** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 闪烁；摇曳 n. 闪烁；闪光
- **英文释义**：v. to shine with an unsteady light; n. an unsteady movement of light
- **例句**：
  - The candle flickered in the wind and then went out.
  - 蜡烛在风中闪烁了一下然后熄灭了。
- **助记**：flick（弹/闪）+ er → 不断闪动 → 闪烁

### 6. **gorgeous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：华丽的；极美的；令人愉快的
- **英文释义**：beautiful; very attractive; very pleasant
- **例句**：
  - The bride looked gorgeous in her white dress.
  - 新娘穿着白色裙子看起来美极了。
- **助记**：gorge（峡谷）+ ous → 像峡谷景色一样壮丽 → 极美的

### 7. **intercept** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：拦截；截住；截取
- **英文释义**：to obstruct or catch something or someone on the way from one place to another
- **例句**：
  - The police intercepted the criminals at the border.
  - 警方在边境拦截了罪犯。
- **助记**：inter（之间）+ cept（抓）→ 在中间抓住 → 拦截

### 8. **monastery** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：修道院；寺院
- **英文释义**：a building or buildings occupied by a community of monks living under religious vows
- **例句**：
  - The ancient monastery sits on top of a mountain.
  - 古老的修道院坐落在山顶上。
- **助记**：mono（单独）+ astery → 独自修行的地方 → 修道院

### 9. **paramount** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：最重要的；至高无上的
- **英文释义**：more important than anything else; supreme
- **例句**：
  - Safety is of paramount importance in this factory.
  - 安全在这家工厂至关重要。
- **助记**：para（旁边/超越）+ mount（山）→ 超越山顶的 → 至高无上的

### 10. **scaffold** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：脚手架；支架；绞刑架
- **英文释义**：a temporary structure on the outside of a building used by workers; a raised platform for executions
- **例句**：
  - Workers erected scaffolds around the building for repairs.
  - 工人们在建筑物周围搭建了脚手架进行维修。
- **助记**：scaf（棚架）+ fold（折叠）→ 可折叠的架子 → 脚手架

### 高一词汇

### 11. **anomaly** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：异常；反常现象
- **英文释义**：something that deviates from what is standard, normal, or expected
- **例句**：
  - The sudden temperature drop was an anomaly for this time of year.
  - 突然的气温下降对于一年中的这个时候来说是异常的。
- **助记**：a（不）+ nom（规律）+ aly → 不符合规律的 → 异常

### 12. **disseminate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：传播；散布
- **英文释义**：to spread or disperse something, especially information, widely
- **例句**：
  - The internet helps disseminate information rapidly.
  - 互联网帮助快速传播信息。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ semin（种子）+ ate → 把种子撒开 → 传播

### 13. **formidable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：强大的；令人敬畏的；难以对付的
- **英文释义**：inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, or capable
- **例句**：
  - The team faces a formidable opponent in the final.
  - 这支队伍在决赛中面临一个强大的对手。
- **助记**：formid（恐惧）+ able → 令人恐惧的 → 令人敬畏的

### 14. **impediment** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：障碍；妨碍；口吃
- **英文释义**：a hindrance or obstruction; a defect in speech
- **例句**：
  - Lack of funding is a major impediment to the project.
  - 缺乏资金是该项目的主要障碍。
- **助记**：im（不）+ ped（脚）+ iment → 脚走不了 → 障碍

### 15. **resilient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有弹性的；适应力强的；能迅速恢复的
- **英文释义**：able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions
- **例句**：
  - Children are remarkably resilient and can adapt quickly to new situations.
  - 孩子们适应力很强，能快速适应新环境。
- **助记**：re（回）+ sili（弹跳）+ ent → 能弹回来的 → 有弹性的/适应力强的
