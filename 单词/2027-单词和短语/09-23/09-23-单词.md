## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **warrant** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：使……有必要；保证；授权；（逮捕）令状
- **英文释义**：to justify or necessitate; a document giving authority
- **例句**：
  - The seriousness of the situation warrants immediate action.
  - 情况的严重性使立即行动成为必要。
  - The police obtained a warrant to search the apartment.
  - 警方获得搜查该公寓的令状。
- **助记**：war（保护）+ rant → 给予正当保护 → 授权/ warrant

### 2. **whimsical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：异想天开的；反复无常的；古怪有趣的
- **英文释义**：playfully quaint or fanciful, especially in an appealing way
- **例句**：
  - The garden was full of whimsical statues and colorful lights.
  - 花园里满是异想天开的雕像和彩灯。
  - His whimsical sense of humor made the class more lively.
  - 他古怪有趣的幽默感让课堂更生动。
- **助记**：whim（突发奇想）+ sical → 充满奇想的 → 异想天开

### 3. **yield** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：产生；屈服；让路；产量
- **英文释义**：to produce or provide; to give way; an amount produced
- **例句**：
  - The experiment yielded surprising results.
  - 实验产生了令人惊讶的结果。
  - Drivers must yield to pedestrians at crosswalks.
  - 司机在人行横道处必须礼让行人。
- **助记**：yield 原义“产出”，引申让步 → 产出让步

### 4. **zealous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：热心的；狂热的；充满热情的
- **英文释义**：having or showing great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause
- **例句**：
  - Zealous volunteers organized the charity event in a week.
  - 热心的志愿者在一周内筹办了慈善活动。
  - He was zealous about protecting the environment.
  - 他对保护环境充满热情。
- **助记**：zeal（热情）+ ous → 充满热情的

### 5. **abrogate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：废除；取消（法律、协议等）
- **英文释义**：to repeal or do away with a law, right, or agreement
- **例句**：
  - The government decided to abrogate the outdated treaty.
  - 政府决定废除过时的条约。
  - Courts cannot abrogate citizens’ fundamental rights without cause.
  - 法院不能无故废除公民的基本权利。
- **助记**：ab（离开）+ rog（询问/规则）→ 使规则离开 → 废除

### 6. **benevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；乐善好施的；善意的
- **英文释义**：well-meaning and kindly; serving charitable purposes
- **例句**：
  - A benevolent donor funded the new school library.
  - 一位仁慈的捐赠者资助了新校图书馆。
  - She spoke in a benevolent tone to calm the child.
  - 她用温和的语气安慰孩子。
- **助记**：bene（好）+ vol（意愿）→ 好意的 → 仁慈的

### 7. **chicanery** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：诡计；狡辩；欺骗手段
- **英文释义**：the use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose
- **例句**：
  - The investigation exposed financial chicanery at the company.
  - 调查揭露了公司内部的财务诡计。
  - Voters were tired of political chicanery during the campaign.
  - 选民厌倦了竞选中的政治花招。
- **助记**：联想 chic（技巧）+ 花招 → 狡诈手段

### 8. **debacle** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：溃败；惨败；大失败
- **英文释义**：a sudden and ignominious failure; a fiasco
- **例句**：
  - The product launch turned into a public debacle.
  - 产品发布成了一场公开的惨败。
  - After the debacle, the coach resigned immediately.
  - 惨败之后，教练立即辞职。
- **助记**：de（否定）+ bac（棒打）→ 被打垮 → 溃败

### 9. **eclectic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：兼收并蓄的；博采众长的；来源多样的
- **英文释义**：deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad range of sources
- **例句**：
  - Her eclectic music playlist included jazz, rock, and classical pieces.
  - 她兼收并蓄的播放列表里有爵士、摇滚和古典乐。
  - The restaurant’s eclectic menu drew customers from many backgrounds.
  - 这家餐厅融合各地风味的菜单吸引了不同背景的顾客。
- **助记**：ec（出）+ lect（选）→ 从各处选出 → 兼收并蓄

### 10. **engender** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：引起；产生；造成（情感、局面）
- **英文释义**：to cause or give rise to a feeling, situation, or condition
- **例句**：
  - Poverty can engender a sense of hopelessness.
  - 贫困可能滋生绝望感。
  - The speech engendered widespread debate online.
  - 这场演讲在网上引发了广泛争论。
- **助记**：en（使）+ gender（产生）→ 使产生 → 引起

### 高一词汇

### 11. **fecund** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：多产的；肥沃的；创造力丰富的
- **英文释义**：producing or capable of producing many offspring or new ideas; fertile
- **例句**：
  - The fecund soil supported three harvests a year.
  - 肥沃的土壤一年可支持三季收成。
  - She is a fecund writer with two novels published each year.
  - 她是一位多产的作家，每年出版两部小说。
- **助记**：fec（做、生产）+ und → 能生产的 → 多产

### 12. **gregarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱社交的；群居的；合群的
- **英文释义**：fond of company; sociable; living in flocks or herds
- **例句**：
  - Gregarious people often enjoy team sports and parties.
  - 爱交际的人通常喜欢团队运动和聚会。
  - Sheep are gregarious animals that stay in flocks.
  - 羊是群居动物，成群生活。
- **助记**：greg（群体）+ arious → 爱群体的 → 合群的

### 13. **hegemony** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：霸权；支配权；主导地位
- **英文释义**：leadership or dominance, especially by one state or social group over others
- **例句**：
  - Scholars studied the cultural hegemony of major media outlets.
  - 学者研究了主流媒体的文化霸权。
  - Economic hegemony allowed the country to set global prices.
  - 经济霸权使该国能够制定全球价格。
- **助记**：heg（领导）+ emony → 领导地位 → 霸权

### 14. **iconoclast** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：反传统者；打破偶像者；攻击传统观念的人
- **英文释义**：a person who attacks cherished beliefs or institutions
- **例句**：
  - The young director was an iconoclast in the conservative film industry.
  - 这位年轻导演在保守的电影业中是个反传统者。
  - Iconoclasts often face criticism from traditionalists.
  - 反传统者常常受到守旧派的批评。
- **助记**：icon（偶像）+ clast（打破）→ 打破偶像 → 反传统者

### 15. **jubilant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：欢欣鼓舞的；喜悦的；欢呼的
- **英文释义**：feeling or expressing great happiness and triumph
- **例句**：
  - The team was jubilant after winning the championship.
  - 夺冠后全队欢欣鼓舞。
  - Jubilant crowds filled the streets on National Day.
  - 国庆日街头挤满了欢呼的人群。
- **助记**：jubil（欢呼）+ ant → 正在欢呼的 → 喜悦的
