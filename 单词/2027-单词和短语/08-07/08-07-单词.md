## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **adhere** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：坚持；遵守；粘附
- **英文释义**：to stick firmly to a surface; to believe in and follow a practice or rule
- **例句**：
  - All employees must adhere to the company's code of conduct.
  - 所有员工必须遵守公司的行为准则。
  - The sticker adhered firmly to the surface and was difficult to remove.
  - 贴纸牢牢粘在表面上，很难撕下来。
- **助记**：ad（加强）+ here（粘附）→ 紧紧粘住 → 坚持/粘附

### 2. **bewilder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使困惑；使茫然；使不知所措
- **英文释义**：to cause someone to become confused and puzzled
- **例句**：
  - The complex instructions bewildered most of the new employees.
  - 复杂的说明使大多数新员工感到困惑。
  - She was bewildered by the sudden change in his attitude towards her.
  - 她对他态度的突然转变感到茫然。
- **助记**：be（使）+ wilder（更荒野的）→ 使人走入荒野般迷路 → 使困惑

### 3. **commence** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：开始；着手；发起
- **英文释义**：to begin; to start something formally
- **例句**：
  - The graduation ceremony will commence at ten o'clock sharp.
  - 毕业典礼将于十点整开始。
  - Construction of the new bridge commenced after years of planning.
  - 经过多年的规划，新桥的建设开始了。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ mence（开始）→ 共同开始 → 开始/发起

### 4. **disperse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：分散；驱散；传播
- **英文释义**：to distribute or spread over a wide area; to cause to go in different directions
- **例句**：
  - The police used water cannons to disperse the crowd of protesters.
  - 警察使用水炮驱散抗议人群。
  - Seeds are dispersed by the wind to grow in new locations.
  - 种子被风传播到新的地方生长。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ perse（撒/散布）→ 向四处撒开 → 分散

### 5. **exempt** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：免除的；豁免的；使免除
- **英文释义**：free from an obligation or liability; to free from a duty
- **例句**：
  - Students under sixteen are exempt from taking the entrance exam.
  - 十六岁以下的学生免于参加入学考试。
  - The new law exempts small businesses from certain tax requirements.
  - 新法律免除了小企业的某些税务要求。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ empt（拿/买）→ 拿出来不用管 → 免除

### 6. **futile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：徒劳的；无用的；无效的
- **英文释义**：incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
- **例句**：
  - All attempts to negotiate a peaceful solution proved futile.
  - 所有通过谈判达成和平解决方案的尝试都被证明是徒劳的。
  - It is futile to resist change — it is better to adapt and move forward.
  - 抵抗变化是徒劳的——最好是适应并向前发展。
- **助记**：fut（流/漏）+ ile → 像从漏洞流走一样白费 → 徒劳的

### 7. **hinder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to create difficulties for, resulting in delay or obstruction
- **例句**：
  - The lack of proper equipment hindered the team's ability to complete the project.
  - 缺乏适当设备阻碍了团队完成项目的能力。
  - Heavy fog hindered visibility and caused several accidents on the road.
  - 大雾妨碍了能见度，并在道路上造成了几起事故。
- **助记**：hinder 联想 behind（在后面）→ 把人拉到后面 → 阻碍

### 8. **jubilant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：欢欣鼓舞的；喜气洋洋的
- **英文释义**：feeling or expressing great happiness and triumph
- **例句**：
  - The jubilant fans celebrated their team's victory with fireworks and music.
  - 欢欣鼓舞的球迷们用烟花和音乐庆祝球队的胜利。
  - She was jubilant when she received the acceptance letter from her dream university.
  - 当她收到梦想大学的录取通知书时，她欢欣鼓舞。
- **助记**：jubil（欢呼）+ ant → 欢呼的 → 欢欣鼓舞的

### 9. **meticulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；细致的；注重细节的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - The scientist was meticulous in recording every detail of the experiment.
  - 这位科学家一丝不苟地记录了实验的每一个细节。
  - Her meticulous planning ensured that the event ran smoothly without any problems.
  - 她细致的规划确保了活动顺利进行，没有任何问题。
- **助记**：meti（恐惧）+ culous → 因害怕出错而小心翼翼 → 一丝不苟的

### 10. **perpetual** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：永久的；持续的；不断的
- **英文释义**：never ending or changing; occurring repeatedly
- **例句**：
  - The perpetual noise from the construction site kept the residents awake all night.
  - 建筑工地持续不断的噪音让居民整夜无法入睡。
  - The city seems to be in a perpetual state of construction and renovation.
  - 这座城市似乎处于永久的建设和翻新状态。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ petu（追求）+ al → 贯穿始终地追求 → 永久的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **complacent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：自满的；沾沾自喜的；不思进取的
- **英文释义**：showing smug or uncritical satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements
- **例句**：
  - The team became complacent after winning three games in a row and lost the final match.
  - 球队在连赢三场后变得自满，最终输掉了决赛。
  - We must not become complacent about safety standards in the workplace.
  - 我们绝不能在工作场所的安全标准上自满。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ plac（满意）+ ent → 完全满意的 → 自满的

### 12. **dichotomy** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：二分法；对立；两分
- **英文释义**：a division or contrast between two things that are opposed or entirely different
- **例句**：
  - There is a clear dichotomy between what politicians say and what they actually do.
  - 政治家所说的和他们实际做的之间存在明显的对立。
  - The dichotomy between rich and poor continues to widen in many countries.
  - 许多国家贫富之间的分化继续加大。
- **助记**：dicho（二/分）+ tomy（切）→ 切成两半 → 二分法/对立

### 13. **formidable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：令人敬畏的；强大的；难以对付的
- **英文释义**：inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, or capable
- **例句**：
  - The defending champion is a formidable opponent that few dare to challenge.
  - 卫冕冠军是一个令人敬畏的对手，很少有人敢挑战。
  - Building the Great Wall of China was a formidable engineering achievement.
  - 建造万里长城是一项令人敬畏的工程成就。
- **助记**：form（形式）+ id + able → 形态让人害怕的 → 令人敬畏的

### 14. **pernicious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有害的；恶性的；致命的
- **英文释义**：having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way
- **例句**：
  - The pernicious effects of smoking on health are well documented by medical research.
  - 吸烟对健康的有害影响已被医学研究充分记录。
  - Gossip can have a pernicious influence on workplace relationships.
  - 八卦可能对职场关系产生有害的影响。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ nic（杀）+ ious → 完全致死的 → 有害的/致命的

### 15. **vacillate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：犹豫不决；摇摆；动摇
- **英文释义**：to alternate or waver between different opinions or actions; to be indecisive
- **例句**：
  - He vacillated between accepting the job offer and staying at his current company.
  - 他在接受工作邀请和留在现在的公司之间犹豫不决。
  - The government has been vacillating on the issue of immigration reform for years.
  - 政府在移民改革问题上多年来一直摇摆不定。
- **助记**：vacill（摇摆）+ ate → 来回摇摆 → 犹豫不决
