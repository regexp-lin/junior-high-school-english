## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **collaborate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：合作；协作；勾结
- **英文释义**：to work jointly on an activity or project
- **例句**：
  - Scientists from different countries collaborated on the research project.
  - 来自不同国家的科学家在这个研究项目上进行了合作。
  - The two companies collaborated to develop a new product line.
  - 两家公司合作开发了新的产品线。
- **助记**：col（共同）+ labor（工作）+ ate → 一起工作 → 合作

### 2. **paradox** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：矛盾；悖论；似非而是的说法
- **英文释义**：a seemingly absurd or contradictory statement that may prove to be true
- **例句**：
  - It's a paradox that the busiest people often have the most free time.
  - 最忙碌的人往往有最多的空闲时间，这是一个悖论。
  - The paradox of choice suggests that too many options can lead to unhappiness.
  - 选择悖论表明，太多的选择可能导致不幸福。
- **助记**：para（旁边）+ dox（观点）→ 两个对立的观点并存 → 悖论

### 3. **disseminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：传播；散布；宣传
- **英文释义**：to spread information, knowledge, or ideas widely
- **例句**：
  - The organization works to disseminate knowledge about health and hygiene.
  - 该组织致力于传播健康和卫生知识。
  - Social media platforms are powerful tools for disseminating news quickly.
  - 社交媒体平台是快速传播新闻的有力工具。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ semin（种子）+ ate → 把种子撒开 → 传播

### 4. **austere** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：朴素的；严肃的；严格的
- **英文释义**：severe or strict in manner or attitude; having no comforts or luxuries
- **例句**：
  - The monks lived an austere life with no modern comforts.
  - 僧侣们过着没有现代舒适条件的朴素生活。
  - The government implemented austere economic policies to reduce spending.
  - 政府实施了严格的经济政策来减少支出。
- **助记**：austere 发音似"奥斯特" → 严格朴素的 → 朴素的

### 5. **implicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：牵连；暗示；使卷入
- **英文释义**：to show someone to be involved in a crime; to convey indirectly
- **例句**：
  - The evidence implicated several high-ranking officials in the corruption scandal.
  - 证据表明几位高级官员与腐败丑闻有牵连。
  - His silence seemed to implicate guilt.
  - 他的沉默似乎暗示了他有罪。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ plic（折叠）+ ate → 折叠进去 → 牵连

### 6. **paramount** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：至上的；最重要的；首要的
- **英文释义**：more important than anything else; supreme
- **例句**：
  - The safety of passengers is of paramount importance to the airline.
  - 乘客安全对航空公司来说至关重要。
  - Quality control is paramount in the pharmaceutical industry.
  - 质量控制在制药行业中是最重要的。
- **助记**：para（旁边）+ mount（山）→ 在山的最高处 → 至上的

### 7. **obliterate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：彻底摧毁；抹去；消灭
- **英文释义**：to destroy utterly; to wipe out all trace of
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake obliterated entire villages in the mountainous region.
  - 地震在山区彻底摧毁了整个村庄。
  - Time had obliterated the inscription on the ancient tombstone.
  - 时间抹去了古墓碑上的铭文。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ liter（字母）+ ate → 对着字母抹去 → 抹去

### 8. **palpable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：明显的；可感知的；可触摸的
- **英文释义**：able to be touched or felt; so intense as to seem almost tangible
- **例句**：
  - The tension in the room was palpable as the results were announced.
  - 宣布结果时，房间里的紧张气氛是显而易见的。
  - There was a palpable sense of relief when the missing child was found.
  - 走失的孩子被找到时，明显可以感受到一种如释重负的感觉。
- **助记**：palp（触摸）+ able → 可触摸到的 → 明显的

### 9. **invoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：援引；唤起；调用
- **英文释义**：to cite or appeal to as an authority; to call on for assistance
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer invoked the Fifth Amendment to protect his client's rights.
  - 律师援引第五修正案来保护客户的权利。
  - The song invoked memories of her childhood summers.
  - 这首歌唤起了她童年夏天的回忆。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ voke（叫）→ 叫进来帮忙 → 援引

### 10. **dormant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：休眠的；潜伏的；蛰伏的
- **英文释义**：temporarily inactive or not growing; in a state of rest
- **例句**：
  - The volcano has been dormant for over a century.
  - 这座火山已经休眠了一个多世纪。
  - The seeds remained dormant through the winter and sprouted in spring.
  - 种子在整个冬天处于休眠状态，春天才发芽。
- **助记**：dorm（睡觉）+ ant → 在睡觉的 → 休眠的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **loquacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：健谈的；话多的
- **英文释义**：tending to talk a great deal; talkative
- **例句**：
  - My loquacious neighbor can talk for hours without stopping.
  - 我那健谈的邻居能连续说上好几个小时。
  - The loquacious guide made the museum tour both informative and entertaining.
  - 健谈的导游使博物馆之旅既有信息量又有趣。
- **助记**：loqu（说话）+ acious → 爱说话的 → 健谈的

### 12. **vituperative** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：辱骂的；责骂的；恶毒的
- **英文释义**：bitter and abusive in language
- **例句**：
  - The debate turned vituperative, with both sides resorting to personal attacks.
  - 辩论变得充满辱骂，双方都诉诸人身攻击。
  - His vituperative comments about his colleague were deemed unprofessional.
  - 他对同事恶毒的评论被认为不专业。
- **助记**：vituper（责骂）+ ative → 责骂性的 → 辱骂的

### 13. **supercilious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：傲慢的；目中无人的；高傲的
- **英文释义**：behaving or looking as though one thinks one is superior to others
- **例句**：
  - His supercilious attitude made it difficult for others to work with him.
  - 他傲慢的态度使别人很难与他共事。
  - She raised a supercilious eyebrow at the suggestion.
  - 她傲慢地扬起眉毛对这个建议表示不屑。
- **助记**：super（在上面）+ cili（眉毛）+ ous → 扬起眉毛看人 → 傲慢的

### 14. **pulchritude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：美貌；美丽
- **英文释义**：beauty; physical attractiveness
- **例句**：
  - The pulchritude of the sunset over the ocean took everyone's breath away.
  - 海上落日的美丽让所有人屏住了呼吸。
  - Ancient Greek poets often celebrated human pulchritude in their works.
  - 古希腊诗人常在作品中赞美人类的美貌。
- **助记**：pulchr（美丽）+ itude → 美丽的状态 → 美貌

### 15. **autodidact** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：自学者；自学成才的人
- **英文释义**：a self-taught person
- **例句**：
  - Leonardo da Vinci is often regarded as one of history's greatest autodidacts.
  - 达芬奇常被视为历史上最伟大的自学者之一。
  - As an autodidact, she taught herself three programming languages.
  - 作为自学者，她自学了三门编程语言。
- **助记**：auto（自己）+ didact（教导）→ 自己教自己 → 自学者
