## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **avert** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：避免；转移（目光）；防止
- **英文释义**：to turn away one's eyes; to prevent something unpleasant
- **例句**：
  - Quick action by the pilot averted a potential disaster.
  - 飞行员的快速反应避免了一场潜在的灾难。
  - She averted her eyes from the disturbing scene.
  - 她把目光从令人不安的场景移开。
- **助记**：a（离开）+ vert（转）→ 转开 → 避免/转移

### 2. **brevity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：简洁；简短；短暂
- **英文释义**：concise and exact use of words; shortness of time
- **例句**：
  - The speaker's brevity was appreciated by the tired audience.
  - 演讲者的简短受到疲惫听众的赞赏。
  - Brevity is the soul of wit, as Shakespeare once wrote.
  - 如莎士比亚所写，简洁是智慧的灵魂。
- **助记**：brev（短）+ ity → 短 → 简洁

### 3. **complacent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：自满的；沾沾自喜的；满足现状的
- **英文释义**：showing smug or uncritical satisfaction with oneself
- **例句**：
  - Don't become complacent after one success; keep improving.
  - 不要因一次成功就自满，要不断进步。
  - A complacent attitude toward safety can lead to accidents.
  - 对安全自满的态度会导致事故发生。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ plac（满意）+ ent → 完全满意 → 自满

### 4. **denounce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：谴责；公开指责；告发
- **英文释义**：to publicly declare to be wrong or evil; to accuse
- **例句**：
  - Human rights groups denounced the government's treatment of prisoners.
  - 人权团体谴责政府对待囚犯的方式。
  - The scientist denounced the research as fraudulent.
  - 这位科学家公开指责该研究具有欺诈性。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ nounce（宣告）→ 往下宣告 → 谴责

### 5. **erode** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：侵蚀；腐蚀；逐渐削弱
- **英文释义**：to gradually destroy or diminish; to wear away
- **例句**：
  - Waves have eroded the coastline over thousands of years.
  - 海浪在数千年间侵蚀了海岸线。
  - Public trust in the media has been eroded by fake news.
  - 公众对媒体的信任被假新闻逐渐侵蚀。
- **助记**：e（出）+ rode（啃）→ 啃出去 → 侵蚀

### 6. **flicker** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：闪烁；忽隐忽现；一闪
- **英文释义**：to shine with an unsteady light; a brief occurrence
- **例句**：
  - The candle flickered in the breeze and then went out.
  - 蜡烛在微风中闪烁了几下然后熄灭了。
  - A flicker of hope appeared when rescuers heard voices underground.
  - 当救援者听到地下的声音时出现了一丝希望。
- **助记**：flick（轻弹）+ er → 像轻弹一样忽明忽暗 → 闪烁

### 7. **grim** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：严酷的；令人沮丧的；阴冷的
- **英文释义**：forbidding or uninviting; very serious or gloomy
- **例句**：
  - The doctor delivered the grim news with a heavy heart.
  - 医生心情沉重地传达了令人沮丧的消息。
  - The factory workers faced grim conditions during the winter.
  - 工人们在冬天面临严酷的工作条件。
- **助记**：grim → Grimm（格林童话）→ 故事里有很多严酷情节

### 8. **hover** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：盘旋；徘徊；悬停
- **英文释义**：to remain in one place in the air; to linger close at hand
- **例句**：
  - A helicopter hovered above the accident site for hours.
  - 一架直升机在事故现场上空盘旋了数小时。
  - Unemployment rates hovered around 8% throughout the year.
  - 失业率全年徘徊在8%左右。
- **助记**：hover → 悬浮（如 hovercraft 气垫船）

### 9. **innate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：先天的；与生俱来的；固有的
- **英文释义**：inborn; natural; existing from birth
- **例句**：
  - Some scientists believe curiosity is an innate human trait.
  - 一些科学家认为好奇心是人类与生俱来的特质。
  - She has an innate talent for playing the piano.
  - 她有弹钢琴的天赋。
- **助记**：in（内）+ nate（出生）→ 出生就有的 → 先天的

### 10. **mingle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：混合；交往；融合
- **英文释义**：to mix or cause to mix together; to move among people socially
- **例句**：
  - The smell of coffee mingled with fresh bread in the bakery.
  - 面包店里咖啡的香气与新鲜面包的味道融合在一起。
  - At the party, guests were encouraged to mingle and make new friends.
  - 在聚会上，客人们被鼓励交流结交新朋友。
- **助记**：mingle 联想 mini（小）+ gle → 小东西混在一起 → 混合

### 高一词汇

### 11. **magnate** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：大亨；巨头；权贵
- **英文释义**：a wealthy or influential person, especially in business or industry
- **例句**：
  - The media magnate owned newspapers, TV stations, and streaming services.
  - 这位媒体大亨拥有报纸、电视台和流媒体服务。
  - Oil magnates of the early 20th century shaped global energy policy.
  - 20世纪初的石油巨头塑造了全球能源政策。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ ate → 大人物 → 大亨

### 12. **novice** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：新手；初学者；生手
- **英文释义**：a person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation
- **例句**：
  - The cooking class is designed for novices who have never used a stove.
  - 这门烹饪课为从未用过炉灶的新手设计。
  - Even a novice can learn to code with the right guidance.
  - 即使是新手在正确的指导下也能学会编程。
- **助记**：nov（新）+ ice → 新人 → 新手

### 13. **paradigm** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：范式；典范；模式
- **英文释义**：a typical example or pattern of something; a model
- **例句**：
  - The invention of the Internet created a new paradigm for communication.
  - 互联网的发明为通信创造了新的范式。
  - Einstein's theory represented a paradigm shift in physics.
  - 爱因斯坦的理论代表了物理学的范式转变。
- **助记**：para（旁边）+ digm（展示）→ 展示在旁边的范本 → 范式

### 14. **quench** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：解渴；扑灭；抑制
- **英文释义**：to satisfy thirst; to extinguish a fire; to suppress
- **例句**：
  - Nothing quenches thirst on a hot day like cold lemonade.
  - 热天没有什么比冰柠檬水更解渴的了。
  - Firefighters worked through the night to quench the forest blaze.
  - 消防员彻夜工作扑灭森林大火。
- **助记**：quench → 浇灭（quench one's thirst 解渴）

### 15. **repudiate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：否认；拒绝接受；驳斥
- **英文释义**：to refuse to accept or be associated with; to deny the truth of
- **例句**：
  - The president repudiated the allegations made by the opposition.
  - 总统驳斥了反对派的指控。
  - She repudiated the old agreement and demanded new terms.
  - 她拒绝接受旧协议并要求新条款。
- **助记**：re（回）+ pud（推）+ iate → 推回去 → 拒绝/否认
