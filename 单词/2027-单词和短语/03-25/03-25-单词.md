## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abrupt** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：突然的；意外的；唐突的
- **英文释义**：sudden and unexpected; brief to the point of rudeness
- **例句**：
  - The meeting came to an abrupt end when the fire alarm went off.
  - 火警响起时，会议突然结束了。
- **助记**：ab（离开）+ rupt（断裂）→ 突然断裂 → 突然的

### 2. **bondage** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：束缚；奴役
- **英文释义**：the state of being a slave or prisoner; a state of being bound
- **例句**：
  - The people finally broke free from bondage.
  - 人民终于摆脱了束缚。
- **助记**：bond（绑定）+ age → 被绑着的状态 → 束缚

### 3. **canyon** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：峡谷
- **英文释义**：a deep gorge, typically one with a river flowing through it
- **例句**：
  - The Grand Canyon is one of the most breathtaking natural wonders.
  - 大峡谷是最令人叹为观止的自然奇观之一。
- **助记**：canyon 谐音"砍用"→ 像是被砍出来的深谷 → 峡谷

### 4. **divert** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：转移；使转向；使分心
- **英文释义**：to cause to change course or turn from one direction to another; to distract
- **例句**：
  - The police diverted traffic away from the accident scene.
  - 警察将交通从事故现场引开。
- **助记**：di（分开）+ vert（转）→ 转向别处 → 转移

### 5. **feeble** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚弱的；无力的；薄弱的
- **英文释义**：lacking physical strength; lacking force or effectiveness
- **例句**：
  - The old man's voice was feeble but determined.
  - 老人的声音微弱但坚定。
- **助记**：feeble 谐音"非薄"→ 非常薄弱的 → 虚弱的

### 6. **gutter** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：排水沟；檐槽；贫民区
- **英文释义**：a shallow trough fixed beneath the edge of a roof; a channel at the side of a street
- **例句**：
  - Rainwater flowed along the gutter into the drain.
  - 雨水顺着排水沟流入下水道。
- **助记**：gut（肠子）+ ter → 像肠子一样的槽 → 排水沟

### 7. **legislation** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：立法；法律；法规
- **英文释义**：laws considered collectively; the process of making or enacting laws
- **例句**：
  - New legislation was introduced to protect the environment.
  - 出台了新法规来保护环境。
- **助记**：legis（法律）+ lation → 制定法律的过程 → 立法

### 8. **nourish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：滋养；养育；培养
- **英文释义**：to provide with food or other substances necessary for growth and health
- **例句**：
  - Good food nourishes both the body and the mind.
  - 好的食物滋养身体和心灵。
- **助记**：nour（营养）+ ish → 给予营养 → 滋养

### 9. **reckless** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：鲁莽的；不顾后果的
- **英文释义**：without thinking or caring about the consequences of an action
- **例句**：
  - Reckless driving is the leading cause of road accidents.
  - 鲁莽驾驶是交通事故的主要原因。
- **助记**：reck（顾虑）+ less（没有）→ 没有顾虑的 → 鲁莽的

### 10. **sermon** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：布道；说教；训诫
- **英文释义**：a talk on a religious or moral subject given during a church service
- **例句**：
  - The priest delivered a powerful sermon on forgiveness.
  - 牧师做了一场关于宽恕的感人布道。
- **助记**：ser（谐音"说"）+ mon → 说教一番 → 布道

### 高一词汇

### 11. **contingency** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：意外事件；应急措施；可能性
- **英文释义**：a future event or circumstance which is possible but cannot be predicted with certainty
- **例句**：
  - We need a contingency plan in case of bad weather.
  - 我们需要一个应对恶劣天气的应急方案。
- **助记**：con + ting（碰）+ ency → 碰巧发生的事 → 意外事件

### 12. **equilibrium** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：平衡；均衡
- **英文释义**：a state in which opposing forces or influences are balanced
- **例句**：
  - The ecosystem maintains a delicate equilibrium.
  - 生态系统维持着微妙的平衡。
- **助记**：equi（相等）+ libr（天平）+ ium → 天平相等的状态 → 平衡

### 13. **hegemony** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：霸权；支配权
- **英文释义**：leadership or dominance, especially by one country or group over others
- **例句**：
  - The country sought to establish its hegemony over the region.
  - 该国试图在该地区建立霸权。
- **助记**：hege（领导）+ mony → 领导一切的权力 → 霸权

### 14. **meticulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；注重细节的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - She is meticulous about keeping her room tidy.
  - 她对保持房间整洁一丝不苟。
- **助记**：metic（小心）+ ulous → 非常小心的 → 一丝不苟的

### 15. **susceptible** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：易受影响的；敏感的；易患……的
- **英文释义**：likely or liable to be influenced or harmed by a particular thing
- **例句**：
  - Children are more susceptible to colds in winter.
  - 孩子们在冬天更容易感冒。
- **助记**：sus（下面）+ cept（接受）+ ible → 容易在下面接受影响的 → 易受影响的
