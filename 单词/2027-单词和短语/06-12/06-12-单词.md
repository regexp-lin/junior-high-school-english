## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **affinity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：亲和力；喜好；密切关系
- **英文释义**：a natural liking for or attraction to a person, thing, or idea
- **例句**：
  - She has a natural affinity for languages and can learn them quickly.
  - 她对语言有天然的亲和力，能够快速学习。
- **助记**：af（向）+ fin（边界）+ ity → 靠近边界 → 亲近 → 亲和力

### 2. **brisk** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：轻快的；活跃的；凉爽的
- **英文释义**：active, fast, and energetic; (of weather) cold but pleasantly invigorating
- **例句**：
  - Business was brisk at the market this morning.
  - 今天早上市场上生意很红火。
- **助记**：brisk 发音像 "快"（kuai）→ 轻快的

### 3. **conjecture** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：推测；猜想
- **英文释义**：an opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information
- **例句**：
  - His theory is based on conjecture rather than solid evidence.
  - 他的理论是基于推测而非确凿证据。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ ject（投/扔）+ ure → 一起抛出想法 → 推测

### 4. **dormant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：休眠的；蛰伏的；暂停活动的
- **英文释义**：(of an animal) in a state of biological rest; temporarily inactive
- **例句**：
  - The volcano has been dormant for over a hundred years.
  - 这座火山已经休眠了一百多年。
- **助记**：dorm（睡觉）+ ant → 睡着的 → 休眠的

### 5. **elusive** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：难以捉摸的；难以找到的
- **英文释义**：difficult to find, catch, or achieve
- **例句**：
  - Success can be elusive, but persistence pays off in the end.
  - 成功可能难以捉摸，但坚持最终会有回报。
- **助记**：e（出）+ lus（玩）+ ive → 玩着躲出去 → 难以捉摸的

### 6. **forlorn** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：孤独的；凄凉的；绝望的
- **英文释义**：pitifully sad and abandoned or lonely
- **例句**：
  - The forlorn puppy sat outside in the rain, waiting for its owner.
  - 那只孤独的小狗在雨中坐着，等待它的主人。
- **助记**：for（完全）+ lorn（失去）→ 完全失去 → 凄凉的

### 7. **grotesque** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：怪诞的；荒唐的；奇丑的
- **英文释义**：comically or repulsively ugly or distorted
- **例句**：
  - The movie featured grotesque monsters that terrified the audience.
  - 这部电影中有吓到观众的怪诞怪物。
- **助记**：grot（洞穴）+ esque → 洞穴里的奇怪东西 → 怪诞的

### 8. **inept** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无能的；笨拙的
- **英文释义**：having or showing no skill; clumsy
- **例句**：
  - His inept handling of the situation made things much worse.
  - 他对局势笨拙的处理使事情变得更糟。
- **助记**：in（不）+ ept（适合）→ 不适合的 → 无能的

### 9. **obscure** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：模糊的；晦涩的；使难以理解
- **英文释义**：(adj.) not clearly expressed or easily understood; (v.) keep from being seen; make unclear
- **例句**：
  - The meaning of the poem remains obscure to many readers.
  - 这首诗的意义对许多读者来说仍然晦涩难懂。
- **助记**：ob（覆盖）+ scure（黑暗）→ 被黑暗覆盖 → 模糊的

### 10. **pivotal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：关键的；核心的
- **英文释义**：of crucial importance in relation to the development or success of something
- **例句**：
  - Her research played a pivotal role in developing the vaccine.
  - 她的研究在开发疫苗方面起到了关键作用。
- **助记**：pivot（枢轴）+ al → 像枢轴一样的 → 关键的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **conflagration** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：大火灾；大冲突
- **英文释义**：an extensive fire that destroys a great deal of land or property
- **例句**：
  - The conflagration destroyed hundreds of homes in the countryside.
  - 大火摧毁了乡村的数百栋房屋。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ flagr（燃烧）+ ation → 一起燃烧 → 大火灾

### 12. **ephemeral** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：短暂的；转瞬即逝的
- **英文释义**：lasting for a very short time
- **例句**：
  - The beauty of cherry blossoms is ephemeral — they bloom for only a week or two.
  - 樱花之美转瞬即逝——它们只开一两周。
- **助记**：epi（在……上）+ hemer（天）+ al → 只在一天之上 → 短暂的

### 13. **iconoclast** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：打破传统者；偶像破坏者
- **英文释义**：a person who attacks cherished beliefs or institutions
- **例句**：
  - The artist was an iconoclast who challenged every convention in the art world.
  - 这位艺术家是一个打破传统者，挑战了艺术界的一切惯例。
- **助记**：icono（图像/偶像）+ clast（打碎）→ 打碎偶像 → 打破传统者

### 14. **parsimonious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：吝啬的；过分节俭的
- **英文释义**：unwilling to spend money or use resources; stingy
- **例句**：
  - The parsimonious billionaire refused to donate even a small sum to charity.
  - 这位吝啬的亿万富翁连一小笔钱都不愿捐给慈善机构。
- **助记**：parsi（节省）+ monious → 过分节省的 → 吝啬的

### 15. **truculent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；粗暴的；凶猛的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant
- **例句**：
  - His truculent attitude made negotiations extremely difficult.
  - 他粗暴的态度使谈判变得极其困难。
- **助记**：truc（凶猛的）+ ulent → 凶猛的 → 好斗的
