## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **laconic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：简洁的；寡言的；精炼的
- **英文释义**：using very few words; terse
- **例句**：
  - His laconic reply — "No." — ended the discussion abruptly.
  - 他简洁的回答——“不。”——让讨论戛然而止。
  - Spartan warriors were famous for their laconic wit.
  - 斯巴达战士以言简意赅的机智著称。
- **助记**：来自 Laconia（斯巴达地区）→ 像斯巴达人一样话少 → 简洁寡言

### 2. **magnanimous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；高尚的；慷慨的
- **英文释义**：generous in forgiving an insult or injury; noble and unselfish
- **例句**：
  - The magnanimous winner praised the opponent's effort despite the defeat.
  - 获胜者宽宏大量，尽管对手落败仍称赞其努力。
  - She was magnanimous enough to lend money to the friend who had betrayed her trust.
  - 她宽宏大量，借钱给曾辜负她信任的朋友。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（心）→ 心胸大 → 宽宏大量

### 3. **nebulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：模糊的；云状的；不清楚的
- **英文释义**：hazy, unclear, or ill-defined
- **例句**：
  - The project's goals remained nebulous even after months of meetings.
  - 开了数月会议，项目目标仍然模糊不清。
  - Nebulous shapes appeared in the fog along the highway.
  - 公路上的雾中浮现出朦胧的影子。
- **助记**：nebul（云）+ ous → 如云般朦胧 → 模糊的

### 4. **opulent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：富丽堂皇的；富裕的；奢华的
- **英文释义**：ostentatiously rich and luxurious
- **例句**：
  - The hotel lobby was decorated in an opulent style with marble and gold.
  - 酒店大堂以大理石与黄金装饰，富丽堂皇。
  - Opulent banquets were held to celebrate the treaty signing.
  - 为庆祝条约签署举办了奢华宴会。
- **助记**：opul（财富）+ ent → 富有的 → 奢华的

### 5. **paucity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：少量；缺乏；不足
- **英文释义**：an insufficient quantity or number; scarcity
- **例句**：
  - The paucity of evidence forced the police to release the suspect.
  - 证据不足迫使警方释放嫌疑人。
  - Rural areas suffer from a paucity of qualified doctors.
  - 农村地区合格医生匮乏。
- **助记**：pauc（少）+ ity → 稀少 → 缺乏

### 6. **querulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱抱怨的；易怒的；挑剔的
- **英文释义**：complaining in a petulant or whining manner
- **例句**：
  - The querulous customer demanded to speak to the manager about minor scratches.
  - 爱抱怨的顾客为轻微划痕坚持要见经理。
  - Jet lag made the children querulous during the long journey.
  - 时差让孩子在长途旅行中爱发牢骚。
- **助记**：quer（抱怨）+ ulous → 爱抱怨的

### 7. **rancor** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：深仇；怨恨；敌意
- **英文释义**：bitterness or resentfulness, especially when long-standing
- **例句**：
  - Decades of border disputes bred deep rancor between the two nations.
  - 数十年的边界争端在两国间埋下了深深的怨恨。
  - She spoke without rancor, hoping to heal the family rift.
  - 她毫无怨恨地讲话，希望弥合家庭裂痕。
- **助记**：ranc（腐臭/苦涩）→ 心中苦涩 → 怨恨

### 8. **sagacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：睿智的；有洞察力的；精明的
- **英文释义**：having or showing keen mental discernment and good judgment
- **例句**：
  - The sagacious investor sold stocks before the market crashed.
  - 睿智的投资者在股市崩盘前抛售股票。
  - Grandparents often offer sagacious advice drawn from long experience.
  - 祖父母常根据漫长人生经验给出睿智建议。
- **助记**：sag（感知）+ acious → 感知敏锐的 → 睿智的

### 9. **taciturn** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：沉默寡言的；不苟言笑的
- **英文释义**：reserved or uncommunicative in speech; saying little
- **例句**：
  - The taciturn coach answered reporters with nods and single words.
  - 沉默寡言的教练对记者只用点头和单词回答。
  - Northern winters seemed to make the villagers even more taciturn.
  - 北方的冬天似乎让村民更加沉默。
- **助记**：tacit（沉默）+ urn → 沉默的 → 寡言的

### 10. **urbane** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：温文尔雅的；有都市气质的；彬彬有礼的
- **英文释义**：courteous and refined in manner, associated with urban life
- **例句**：
  - The diplomat's urbane manners impressed foreign guests at the reception.
  - 外交官温文尔雅的举止给外宾留下深刻印象。
  - He tried to sound urbane, though he had grown up on a remote farm.
  - 他努力显得文雅，尽管他在偏远农场长大。
- **助记**：urban（城市）+ e → 有都市教养的 → 温文尔雅

### 高一词汇

### 11. **vacillate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：犹豫；摇摆不定；踌躇
- **英文释义**：to waver between different opinions or actions; be indecisive
- **例句**：
  - She vacillated between accepting the job offer and staying in graduate school.
  - 她在接受工作邀请与继续读研之间犹豫不决。
  - Leaders who vacillate in a crisis lose public confidence.
  - 危机中摇摆不定的领导人会失去公众信任。
- **助记**：vacill（摇摆）→ 拿不定主意

### 12. **wistful** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：惆怅的；渴望的；带淡淡忧伤的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a feeling of vague or regretful longing
- **例句**：
  - He gave a wistful smile when talking about his childhood village.
  - 谈到童年村庄时，他露出惆怅的微笑。
  - The old photo filled her with wistful memories of summers past.
  - 老照片让她充满对逝去夏日的惆怅回忆。
- **助记**：wish（愿望）+ ful → 带着未竟愿望的 → 惆怅渴望的

### 13. **xenophobia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：仇外；对外国人的恐惧与排斥
- **英文释义**：dislike of or prejudice against people from other countries
- **例句**：
  - History shows that economic crises can fuel xenophobia and discrimination.
  - 历史表明经济危机可能助长仇外与歧视。
  - Education campaigns aim to reduce xenophobia among young voters.
  - 教育宣传旨在减少年轻选民中的仇外心理。
- **助记**：xeno（外族）+ phobia（恐惧）→ 仇外

### 14. **yoke** `高一` n./v.

- **中文释义**：轭；束缚；使结合
- **英文释义**：a wooden crosspiece for harnessing animals; to join or link
- **例句**：
  - The two nations were yoked together by a mutual defense treaty.
  - 两国通过共同防御条约结合在一起。
  - Young workers resented the yoke of outdated company rules.
  - 年轻员工厌恶陈旧公司规章的束缚。
- **助记**：yoke 本义牛轭 → 束缚/结合

### 15. **zeitgeist** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：时代精神；时代思潮
- **英文释义**：the defining spirit or mood of a particular period of history
- **例句**：
  - The film captured the zeitgeist of the 1960s counterculture.
  - 影片捕捉了二十世纪六十年代反文化的时代精神。
  - Tech entrepreneurs often claim to sense the zeitgeist before others do.
  - 科技创业者常声称比别人更早感知时代思潮。
- **助记**：Zeit（时间）+ Geist（精神）→ 时代精神
