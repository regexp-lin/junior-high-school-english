## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **surmount** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：克服；翻越；战胜
- **英文释义**：to overcome a difficulty or obstacle
- **例句**：
  - With determination and hard work, she surmounted every challenge in her path.
  - 凭借决心和努力，她克服了道路上的每一个挑战。
  - The team had to surmount numerous technical obstacles to complete the project.
  - 团队不得不克服众多技术障碍来完成项目。
- **助记**：sur（超过）+ mount（山）→ 翻越高山 → 克服

### 2. **lethargic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：昏昏欲睡的；无精打采的；迟钝的
- **英文释义**：affected by lethargy; sluggish and apathetic
- **例句**：
  - The hot weather made everyone feel lethargic and unmotivated.
  - 炎热的天气让每个人都感到无精打采、毫无动力。
  - After the heavy lunch, the students became lethargic in the afternoon class.
  - 吃完丰盛的午餐后，学生们在下午的课上变得昏昏欲睡。
- **助记**：lethargy（嗜睡）+ ic → 嗜睡的 → 无精打采的

### 3. **promulgate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：颁布；公布；传播
- **英文释义**：to make widely known; to put a law or regulation into effect by official proclamation
- **例句**：
  - The government promulgated new regulations to protect the environment.
  - 政府颁布了保护环境的新法规。
  - The organization works to promulgate awareness of human rights issues.
  - 该组织致力于传播人权问题的意识。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ mulg（公众）+ ate → 向公众宣布 → 颁布

### 4. **spurious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚假的；伪造的；不真实的
- **英文释义**：not being what it purports to be; false or fake
- **例句**：
  - The detective quickly identified the spurious evidence planted at the crime scene.
  - 侦探迅速识别出犯罪现场安放的虚假证据。
  - The company's spurious claims about the product's effectiveness were debunked by independent testers.
  - 该公司关于产品有效性的虚假声明被独立测试人员揭穿了。
- **助记**：spur（假的）+ ious → 假的 → 虚假的

### 5. **circumscribe** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：限制；约束；划定范围
- **英文释义**：to restrict within limits; to define the limits of
- **例句**：
  - The company's growth was circumscribed by strict government regulations.
  - 公司的发展受到严格政府法规的限制。
  - The constitution circumscribes the powers of the executive branch.
  - 宪法限制了行政部门的权力。
- **助记**：circum（周围）+ scribe（写/画）→ 在周围画线 → 限制

### 6. **opulent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：奢华的；富裕的；豪华的
- **英文释义**：ostentatiously rich and luxurious
- **例句**：
  - The opulent ballroom was decorated with crystal chandeliers and gold-trimmed curtains.
  - 奢华的舞厅装饰着水晶吊灯和金边窗帘。
  - The opulent lifestyle of the royal family fascinated visitors from around the world.
  - 王室家族的奢华生活方式吸引了来自世界各地的游客。
- **助记**：opul（财富）+ ent → 充满财富的 → 奢华的

### 7. **insinuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：暗示；含沙射影；巧妙地潜入
- **英文释义**：to suggest or hint at something bad or reprehensible in an indirect way
- **例句**：
  - Are you insinuating that I had something to do with the missing money?
  - 你是在暗示我跟丢失的钱有关吗？
  - He insinuated himself into the group by pretending to share their interests.
  - 他假装和他们有共同兴趣，巧妙地混入了这个群体。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ sinu（弯曲）+ ate → 弯曲着进入 → 暗示/潜入

### 8. **taciturn** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：沉默寡言的；不善言辞的
- **英文释义**：reserved or uncommunicative in speech; saying little
- **例句**：
  - The taciturn farmer answered questions with short, monosyllabic replies.
  - 沉默寡言的农夫用简短的单音节词回答问题。
  - Despite his taciturn nature, he was a keen observer of everything around him.
  - 尽管他沉默寡言，但他是一个敏锐的观察者。
- **助记**：tacit（沉默的）+ urn → 沉默的 → 沉默寡言的

### 9. **extrapolate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：推断；推算；外推
- **英文释义**：to extend the application of a method or conclusion to an unknown situation by assuming that existing trends will continue
- **例句**：
  - Scientists extrapolated from the data to predict the effects of global warming.
  - 科学家从数据推断出全球变暖的影响。
  - It's risky to extrapolate current trends too far into the future.
  - 把当前趋势推断到太遥远的未来是有风险的。
- **助记**：extra（超出）+ pol（推动）+ ate → 向超出范围推动 → 推断

### 10. **sporadic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：零星的；偶发的；不定时的
- **英文释义**：occurring at irregular intervals or only in a few places
- **例句**：
  - Sporadic gunfire could be heard throughout the night in the conflict zone.
  - 在冲突地区，整夜都能听到零星的枪声。
  - She made sporadic visits to the gym, never establishing a regular routine.
  - 她偶尔去健身房，从未建立起规律的锻炼习惯。
- **助记**：sporad（分散）+ ic → 分散发生的 → 零星的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **penumbra** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：半影；边缘地带；模糊区域
- **英文释义**：the partially shaded outer region of a shadow; an area of partial illumination or uncertainty
- **例句**：
  - During the eclipse, the moon's penumbra created a partial shadow over the earth.
  - 在日食期间，月球的半影在地球上形成了部分阴影。
  - The legal concept operates in the penumbra between privacy and public interest.
  - 这个法律概念存在于隐私和公共利益之间的模糊地带。
- **助记**：pen（几乎）+ umbra（影子）→ 几乎有影子 → 半影

### 12. **callipygian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：臀部美丽的
- **英文释义**：having well-shaped buttocks
- **例句**：
  - The ancient Greek statue, Venus Callipyge, celebrates the callipygian ideal of beauty.
  - 古希腊雕像"美臀维纳斯"赞颂了臀部美丽的审美理想。
  - The fashion designer's collection featured outfits that highlighted a callipygian silhouette.
  - 这位时装设计师的系列作品以突出优美臀部轮廓的服装为特色。
- **助记**：calli（美丽）+ pyg（臀部）+ ian → 臀部美丽的

### 13. **plethora** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：过多；过剩；大量
- **英文释义**：a large or excessive amount of something
- **例句**：
  - The internet offers a plethora of information on virtually any topic.
  - 互联网提供了几乎任何主题的大量信息。
  - A plethora of options can sometimes make decision-making more difficult.
  - 过多的选择有时会使决策变得更加困难。
- **助记**：pleth（充满）+ ora → 充满的 → 过多

### 14. **truculent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好斗的；粗暴的；凶猛的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant
- **例句**：
  - The truculent passenger was removed from the flight for threatening the crew.
  - 好斗的乘客因威胁机组人员而被赶下飞机。
  - His truculent manner during the meeting alienated potential allies.
  - 他在会议上粗暴的态度疏远了潜在的盟友。
- **助记**：truc（凶猛）+ ulent → 凶猛的 → 好斗的

### 15. **hiraeth** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：乡愁；对失去之物的深切渴望
- **英文释义**：a deep longing for something, especially one's home or a place or time that no longer exists
- **例句**：
  - Living abroad for years filled her with a profound sense of hiraeth for her homeland.
  - 在国外生活多年使她对故乡充满了深深的乡愁。
  - The old man's hiraeth for the village of his childhood was evident in his wistful stories.
  - 老人对童年村庄的深切怀念在他忧伤的故事中显而易见。
- **助记**：威尔士语借词，无直接词根可分解 → 一种独特的思乡情感
