## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减弱；减轻；废除
- **英文释义**：to become less intense or widespread; to reduce
- **例句**：
  - The storm began to abate after midnight, and the floodwaters slowly receded.
  - 午夜后暴风雨开始减弱，洪水慢慢退去。
  - The city took measures to abate noise pollution near schools.
  - 该市采取措施减轻学校附近的噪音污染。
- **助记**：a（向）+ bate（打）→ 打下去 → 减弱

### 2. **coerce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：胁迫；强迫
- **英文释义**：to persuade someone to do something by using force or threats
- **例句**：
  - The confession was thrown out because police had coerced the suspect.
  - 因警方胁迫嫌疑人，该供述被法庭排除。
  - No one should be coerced into donating organs against their will.
  - 任何人都不应被强迫违背意愿捐献器官。
- **助记**：co（一起）+ erce（驱动）→ 强行驱动 → 胁迫

### 3. **debunk** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：揭穿；驳斥（错误观念）
- **英文释义**：to expose the falseness of a myth, idea, or belief
- **例句**：
  - Scientists debunked the myth that humans use only ten percent of their brains.
  - 科学家揭穿了“人脑只开发10%”的谬论。
  - The article debunks popular diet fads with peer-reviewed studies.
  - 文章用同行评议的研究驳斥流行的饮食风潮。
- **助记**：de（去掉）+ bunk（空话）→ 去掉假话 → 揭穿

### 4. **emulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：仿效；与……竞争；努力赶上
- **英文释义**：to match or surpass, typically by imitation
- **例句**：
  - Young pianists emulate the techniques of their favorite performers.
  - 年轻钢琴家仿效自己喜爱演奏家的技巧。
  - The startup aims to emulate the customer service model of industry leaders.
  - 这家初创公司力求效仿行业龙头的客服模式。
- **助记**：emul（竞争）→ 努力比肩 → 仿效

### 5. **fortify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加强；设防于；强化
- **英文释义**：to strengthen against attack; to strengthen emotionally or physically
- **例句**：
  - Medieval towns fortified their walls with towers and gates.
  - 中世纪城镇用塔楼与城门加固城墙。
  - Orange juice fortified with vitamin D helps prevent bone disease.
  - 添加维生素 D 的橙汁有助于预防骨骼疾病。
- **助记**：fort（强）+ ify → 使坚固 → 加强

### 6. **imbue** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：灌输；使充满；浸透
- **英文释义**：to inspire or permeate with a feeling or quality
- **例句**：
  - The teacher hoped to imbue students with a love of classical literature.
  - 老师希望培养学生对古典文学的热爱。
  - The ceremony was imbued with a sense of solemn dignity.
  - 典礼洋溢着庄严感。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ bue（饮）→ 饮入其中 → 灌输/充满

### 7. **inculcate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：反复灌输；教诲
- **英文释义**：to instill an attitude, idea, or habit by persistent instruction
- **例句**：
  - Parents try to inculcate honesty in children from an early age.
  - 父母自幼向孩子反复灌输诚实。
  - Military training inculcates discipline and teamwork.
  - 军事训练反复灌输纪律与团队精神。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ culc（踩踏）→ 踩进心里 → 灌输

### 8. **jettison** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：抛弃；丢弃；（飞机）投弃
- **英文释义**：to throw or drop something from an aircraft or ship; to abandon
- **例句**：
  - The crew jettisoned fuel to make an emergency landing safer.
  - 机组放油以使紧急着陆更安全。
  - The company jettisoned its unprofitable divisions to survive the recession.
  - 公司为度过衰退抛弃了不盈利的部门。
- **助记**：jet（抛出）→ 从飞机上扔下 → 抛弃

### 9. **kindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：点燃；激起；引起
- **英文释义**：to light or set on fire; to arouse an emotion
- **例句**：
  - Dry leaves kindle easily in the autumn forest — be careful with campfires.
  - 秋叶易燃，使用营火要小心。
  - Her speech kindled hope among the discouraged workers.
  - 她的演讲在沮丧的工人心中燃起希望。
- **助记**：kindle 与 candle（蜡烛）联想 → 点火

### 10. **lament** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：哀悼；痛惜；悲叹
- **英文释义**：to express passionate grief or sorrow; a song or poem mourning a loss
- **例句**：
  - Fans lamented the team's defeat in the final seconds of the game.
  - 球迷为球队在终场前几秒落败而痛惜。
  - The poem is a lament for soldiers who never returned home.
  - 这首诗哀悼未能归乡的士兵。
- **助记**：lament 与 lamentation（哀歌）→ 哀悼

### 高一词汇

### 11. **mendacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚假的；惯于撒谎的
- **英文释义**：not telling the truth; lying
- **例句**：
  - The tabloid published mendacious stories about celebrities to boost sales.
  - 小报刊登关于名人的虚假报道以刺激销量。
  - Mendacious witnesses can face perjury charges in court.
  - 作伪证的证人可能面临伪证罪指控。
- **助记**：mend（缺陷）变体 + 联想“修补谎言”→ 虚假的

### 12. **nihilism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：虚无主义；否定一切
- **英文释义**：the rejection of all religious and moral principles; extreme skepticism
- **例句**：
  - Philosophers debate whether nihilism inevitably leads to despair.
  - 哲学家争论虚无主义是否必然导致绝望。
  - The novel portrays a generation drifting toward political nihilism.
  - 小说描写一代人滑向政治虚无主义。
- **助记**：nihil（无）→ 认为一切皆无意义 → 虚无主义

### 13. **obfuscate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使模糊；使困惑；混淆视听
- **英文释义**：to make obscure, unclear, or unintelligible
- **例句**：
  - The spokesperson obfuscated rather than answering the reporter's direct question.
  - 发言人含糊其词，没有直接回答记者的问题。
  - Technical jargon can obfuscate simple ideas in public documents.
  - 技术术语可能使公共文件中的简单想法变得晦涩。
- **助记**：ob（加强）+ fusc（暗）→ 弄暗 → 使模糊

### 14. **proliferate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：激增；迅速繁殖；扩散
- **英文释义**：to increase rapidly in number; multiply
- **例句**：
  - Misinformation can proliferate on social media within minutes.
  - 错误信息可在几分钟内在社交媒体上大量扩散。
  - Nuclear weapons proliferated during the Cold War era.
  - 冷战时期核武器数量激增。
- **助记**：proli（后代）+ fer + ate → 大量产生后代 → 激增

### 15. **quixotic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：堂吉诃德式的；不切实际的；空想的
- **英文释义**：exceedingly idealistic; unrealistic and impractical
- **例句**：
  - His quixotic quest to save every stray animal strained his finances.
  - 他拯救每只流浪动物的不切实际的追求拖垮了他的经济。
  - Critics dismissed the plan as quixotic but admired its moral ambition.
  - 批评者认为计划不切实际，但钦佩其道德抱负。
- **助记**：来自 Don Quixote（堂吉诃德）→ 空想侠义 → 不切实际的
