## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **anecdote** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：轶事；趣闻；短故事
- **英文释义**：a short amusing or interesting story about a real person or event
- **例句**：
  - The professor began each lecture with a historical anecdote.
  - 教授每堂课都以一则历史轶事开头。
  - Family gatherings are full of anecdotes about grandparents' youth.
  - 家庭聚会上满是关于祖父母年轻时的趣闻。
- **助记**：an（不）+ ec（出）+ dote（给予）→ 未发表的故事 → 轶事

### 2. **bastion** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：堡垒；捍卫者；（喻）某价值观的坚强阵地
- **英文释义**：a projecting part of a fortification; a stronghold defending a principle
- **例句**：
  - The old castle's bastions overlooked the river.
  - 古堡的棱堡俯瞰河流。
  - The university was a bastion of free speech in the region.
  - 这所大学是该地区言论自由的堡垒。
- **助记**：bast（建造）+ ion → 防御建筑 → 堡垒

### 3. **catalyst** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：催化剂；促成变化的人或事
- **英文释义**：a substance that speeds a reaction without being consumed; something that precipitates change
- **例句**：
  - Enzymes act as catalysts in digestion.
  - 酶在消化过程中起催化作用。
  - The scandal was a catalyst for reform in the industry.
  - 丑闻成为行业改革的催化剂。
- **助记**：cata（向下）+ lyst（松散）→ 催化分解 → 催化剂

### 4. **dogma** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：教条；教义；武断信条
- **英文释义**：a principle or set of principles laid down by an authority as incontrovertibly true
- **例句**：
  - Scientific inquiry questions dogma rather than accepting it blindly.
  - 科学探究质疑教条而非盲目接受。
  - Political dogma can blind people to practical solutions.
  - 政治教条会使人看不见务实方案。
- **助记**：dogma 联想 doctrine → 权威信条 → 教条

### 5. **facetious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：乱开玩笑的；滑稽的；（不当场合）轻浮说笑
- **英文释义**：treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor; flippant
- **例句**：
  - A facetious remark at the funeral offended several relatives.
  - 葬礼上不合时宜的玩笑冒犯了几位亲属。
  - He gave a facetious answer to hide his nervousness.
  - 他用玩笑话掩饰紧张。
- **助记**：facet（面）+ ious → 多面玩笑 → 乱开玩笑的

### 6. **guile** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：狡诈；诡计；狡猾
- **英文释义**：sly or cunning intelligence; deceitful cleverness
- **例句**：
  - The villain achieved his goal through guile rather than force.
  - 反派靠狡诈而非武力达到目的。
  - She saw through his guile and refused the offer.
  - 她识破了他的诡计，拒绝了提议。
- **助记**：guile 与 wile（诡计）同源 → 狡诈

### 7. **herald** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：预示；宣布；先驱；传令官
- **英文释义**：to be a sign that something is about to happen; a person or thing announcing something
- **例句**：
  - Warm weather heralded the arrival of spring.
  - 暖和的天气预示着春天来临。
  - The robin is a herald of spring in many cultures.
  - 在许多文化中，知更鸟是春天的使者。
- **助记**：herald 联想 “hero 的先遣” → 预告者

### 8. **impasse** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：僵局；死路；无路可进
- **英文释义**：a situation in which no progress is possible; a deadlock
- **例句**：
  - Negotiations reached an impasse after weeks of talks.
  - 谈判数周后陷入僵局。
  - The couple faced an impasse over whether to have children.
  - 夫妇在是否要孩子的问题上陷入僵局。
- **助记**：im（不）+ passe（通过）→ 过不去 → 僵局

### 9. **juncture** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：关键时刻；接合点；特定时刻
- **英文释义**：a particular point in events or time; a critical occasion
- **例句**：
  - At this juncture we must decide whether to invest or withdraw.
  - 在此关键时刻我们必须决定投资还是撤出。
  - The treaty was signed at a crucial juncture in history.
  - 条约在历史关键时刻签署。
- **助记**：junct（连接）+ ure → 事情接合的关键点 → 关头

### 10. **kinship** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：亲属关系；相似；亲切感
- **英文释义**：blood relationship; a feeling of being related or similar
- **例句**：
  - Kinship ties remain strong in rural communities.
  - 在农村社区亲属纽带仍然很强。
  - She felt a kinship with other immigrants who shared her background.
  - 她与背景相似的移民感到一种亲近感。
- **助记**：kin（亲属）+ ship → 亲属关系

### 高一词汇

### 11. **lethargic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：昏睡的；无精打采的；迟钝的
- **英文释义**：affected by lethargy; sluggish and apathetic
- **例句**：
  - The hot afternoon made everyone lethargic in the classroom.
  - 炎热的下午让教室里每个人都昏昏欲睡。
  - The illness left him lethargic for several weeks.
  - 这场病让他数周都提不起精神。
- **助记**：Lethe（忘川、沉睡）+ argic → 如沉睡般 → 昏睡的

### 12. **monopoly** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：垄断；专卖权；独占
- **英文释义**：the exclusive possession or control of the supply of a commodity or service
- **例句**：
  - The company was fined for abusing its monopoly in the region.
  - 该公司因滥用地区垄断地位被罚款。
  - She has a monopoly on good ideas in our study group — we rely on her.
  - 她在学习小组里独揽好点子——我们都靠她。
- **助记**：mono（单一）+ poly（卖）→ 独家销售 → 垄断

### 13. **nuance** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：细微差别；微妙之处
- **英文释义**：a subtle difference in meaning, expression, or sound
- **例句**：
  - Translators must capture every nuance of the original poem.
  - 译者必须捕捉原诗每一处细微含义。
  - He missed the nuance of her joke and took offense.
  - 他没听出她玩笑的微妙之处而生气了。
- **助记**：nu（云）+ ance → 如云般细微变化 → 细微差别

### 14. **opulent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：富丽堂皇的；奢华的；富裕的
- **英文释义**：ostentatiously rich and luxurious; wealthy
- **例句**：
  - The hotel lobby was decorated in an opulent style with marble columns.
  - 酒店大堂以大理石柱装饰，富丽堂皇。
  - Opulent feasts marked the festival in the ancient capital.
  - 古都的节日以奢华盛宴为标志。
- **助记**：opul（财富）+ ent → 富有的 → 奢华的

### 15. **paucity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：缺乏；稀少；不足
- **英文释义**：smallness of number or quantity; scarcity
- **例句**：
  - There is a paucity of research on this rare disease.
  - 关于这种罕见病的研究很少。
  - The paucity of evidence forced the case to be dismissed.
  - 证据不足导致案件被驳回。
- **助记**：pauc（少）+ ity → 稀少
