## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **latent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：潜在的；潜伏的；隐藏的
- **英文释义**：existing but not yet developed or manifest; hidden
- **例句**：
  - The virus can remain latent in the body for years.
  - 病毒可在体内潜伏多年。
  - He discovered a latent talent for painting in retirement.
  - 退休后他发现了自己潜在的绘画才能。
- **助记**：lat（躺）+ ent → 躺在下面 → 潜在的

### 2. **malevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：恶意的；狠毒的；有坏心的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a wish to do evil to others
- **例句**：
  - The villain shot a malevolent glance at the hero.
  - 反派朝主角投去恶毒的一瞥。
  - Malevolent rumors damaged her reputation unfairly.
  - 恶意谣言不公平地损害了她的名誉。
- **助记**：male（坏）+ vol（意愿）→ 坏心 → 恶意的

### 3. **nascent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：初生的；新生的；刚形成的
- **英文释义**：just coming into existence and beginning to display signs of future potential
- **例句**：
  - The nascent industry needed government support to grow.
  - 新兴产业需要政府扶持才能成长。
  - Their nascent friendship deepened during the trip.
  - 他们初生的友谊在旅途中加深了。
- **助记**：nasc（出生）+ ent → 刚出生的 → 新生的

### 4. **obdurate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽固的；执拗的；不听劝的
- **英文释义**：stubbornly refusing to change one’s opinion or course of action
- **例句**：
  - He remained obdurate despite all evidence against his theory.
  - 尽管所有证据都不利于他的理论，他仍固执己见。
  - The obdurate child refused to apologize.
  - 执拗的孩子不肯道歉。
- **助记**：ob（加强）+ dur（硬）→ 硬到底 → 顽固

### 5. **panacea** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：万灵药；万能之计
- **英文释义**：a solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases
- **例句**：
  - Technology is not a panacea for every educational problem.
  - 技术并非能解决一切教育问题的万灵药。
  - People hoped the new policy would be a panacea for unemployment.
  - 人们希望新政策能成为解决失业的万能药。
- **助记**：pan（全）+ acea（治疗）→ 治百病 → 万灵药

### 6. **quagmire** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：泥潭；困境；难以脱身的局面
- **英文释义**：a soft boggy area of land; an awkward, complex, or hazardous situation
- **例句**：
  - The car was stuck in a quagmire after the heavy rain.
  - 大雨后汽车陷进了泥潭。
  - The country found itself in a diplomatic quagmire.
  - 该国陷入了外交困境。
- **助记**：quag（颤动）+ mire（泥）→ 会颤动的泥 → 泥潭、困境

### 7. **salient** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：显著的；突出的；最重要的
- **英文释义**：most noticeable or important; prominent
- **例句**：
  - The salient points of the report were summarized on the first page.
  - 报告的要点在第一页作了摘要。
  - Her salient feature was her bright smile.
  - 她最突出的特点是灿烂的笑容。
- **助记**：sali（跳）+ ent → 跳出来 → 显著的

### 8. **timorous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：胆小的；畏怯的；胆怯的
- **英文释义**：showing or suffering from nervousness or a lack of confidence
- **例句**：
  - The timorous kitten hid under the sofa when guests arrived.
  - 客人来时，胆怯的小猫躲到沙发下。
  - He gave a timorous reply, barely audible.
  - 他畏怯地回答，几乎听不见。
- **助记**：timor（恐惧）+ ous → 充满恐惧的 → 胆怯的

### 9. **ulterior** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：隐秘的；别有用心的；进一步的
- **英文释义**：existing beyond what is obvious or admitted; hidden
- **例句**：
  - She suspected he had an ulterior motive for offering help.
  - 她怀疑他提供帮助别有用心。
  - The contract had an ulterior clause that favored the company.
  - 合同里有一条有利于公司的隐秘条款。
- **助记**：ulter（那边）→ 藏在另一边 → 隐秘动机

### 10. **venerate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：崇敬；敬重；尊敬
- **英文释义**：to regard with great respect; revere
- **例句**：
  - Many cultures venerate ancestors during festivals.
  - 许多文化在节日里祭祖敬宗。
  - Students venerate the professor for her integrity and wisdom.
  - 学生们因教授的正直与智慧而敬重她。
- **助记**：vener（尊敬）+ ate → 加以尊敬 → 崇敬

### 高一词汇

### 11. **amalgamate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；混合；联合
- **英文释义**：to combine or unite to form one structure or organization
- **例句**：
  - The two banks amalgamated to form a stronger institution.
  - 两家银行合并成更强的机构。
  - Different traditions amalgamated in this multicultural city.
  - 不同传统在这座多元文化城市中融合。
- **助记**：amalgam（汞合金）→ 混在一起 → 合并

### 12. **bilious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：胆汁的；脾气暴躁的；令人作呕的（颜色）
- **英文释义**：affected by or associated with nausea; spiteful; unpleasantly colored
- **例句**：
  - He felt bilious after eating too much greasy food.
  - 吃太多油腻食物后他感到恶心不适。
  - The room was painted a bilious green that hurt the eyes.
  - 房间漆成刺眼的黄绿色。
- **助记**：bile（胆汁）+ ious → 胆汁般的 → 恶心、暴躁

### 13. **commensurate** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：相称的；相当的；成比例的
- **英文释义**：corresponding in size or degree; proportionate
- **例句**：
  - Pay should be commensurate with experience and skills.
  - 薪酬应与经验和技能相称。
  - The punishment was commensurate with the seriousness of the crime.
  - 惩罚与罪行的严重程度相当。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ mensur（测量）→ 同量 → 相称

### 14. **denigrate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：诋毁；贬低；污蔑
- **英文释义**：to criticize unfairly; to disparage; to blacken someone’s reputation
- **例句**：
  - Opponents tried to denigrate her achievements with false claims.
  - 反对者试图用不实之词诋毁她的成就。
  - Do not denigrate others to make yourself look better.
  - 不要通过贬低别人来抬高自己。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ nigr（黑）→ 抹黑 → 诋毁

### 15. **expurgate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：删去（不当内容）；使纯洁
- **英文释义**：to remove objectionable matter from a book, text, etc.
- **例句**：
  - The publisher had to expurgate offensive passages from the memoir.
  - 出版商不得不从回忆录中删去冒犯性段落。
  - An expurgated edition was released for schools.
  - 学校使用的是删节版。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ purg（净化）→ 净化出去 → 删去不当内容
