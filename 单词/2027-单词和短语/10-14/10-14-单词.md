## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **ambiguous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：模棱两可的；含糊不清的；有歧义的
- **英文释义**：open to more than one interpretation; unclear or inexact
- **例句**：
  - The contract's ambiguous wording led to a legal dispute.
  - 合同模棱两可的措辞引发了法律纠纷。
  - His ambiguous answer left us unsure about his real opinion.
  - 他含糊的回答让我们搞不清他的真实看法。
- **助记**：ambi（两边）+ guous → 两边都有道理 → 模棱两可

### 2. **bolster** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：支撑；增强；鼓舞
- **英文释义**：to support or strengthen; to prop up
- **例句**：
  - The new evidence bolstered the lawyer's argument in court.
  - 新证据增强了律师在法庭上的论点。
  - Regular exercise can bolster your immune system.
  - 规律运动能增强你的免疫系统。
- **助记**：bolster（长枕头）→ 靠在枕头上 → 支撑

### 3. **concede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：承认；让步；认输
- **英文释义**：to admit that something is true after first resisting; to surrender
- **例句**：
  - The candidate conceded defeat after the final votes were counted.
  - 最终计票后候选人承认失败。
  - I concede that your plan has some merit.
  - 我承认你的计划有一定道理。
- **助记**：con（完全）+ cede（走/让）→ 完全退让 → 让步

### 4. **disperse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：分散；散开；驱散
- **英文释义**：to spread or distribute over a wide area; to scatter
- **例句**：
  - Police used loudspeakers to disperse the crowd peacefully.
  - 警方用扩音器和平地驱散了人群。
  - Seeds are dispersed by wind, water, and animals.
  - 种子通过风、水和动物传播散开。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ perse（散）→ 散开 → 分散

### 5. **endorse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：认可；支持；代言
- **英文释义**：to declare public approval of; to support officially
- **例句**：
  - Several famous athletes endorsed the new sports drink brand.
  - 几位著名运动员为这个新运动饮料品牌代言。
  - The committee endorsed the proposal unanimously.
  - 委员会一致认可了该提案。
- **助记**：en（使）+ dorse（背）→ 在背面签名 → 认可/背书

### 6. **forge** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：锻造；建立；伪造
- **英文释义**：to form metal by heating and hammering; to create or develop; to counterfeit
- **例句**：
  - The two countries forged a strong alliance during the crisis.
  - 两国在危机中建立了牢固的联盟。
  - He was arrested for forging his boss's signature on company checks.
  - 他因伪造老板在公司支票上的签名而被捕。
- **助记**：forge（铁匠铺）→ 在铁匠铺锤炼 → 锻造/建立

### 7. **grapple** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：搏斗；努力应对；竭力解决
- **英文释义**：to engage in a close fight; to struggle with a difficult problem
- **例句**：
  - The government is grappling with rising unemployment rates.
  - 政府正在努力应对不断攀升的失业率。
  - Wrestlers grappled fiercely on the mat for the gold medal.
  - 摔跤选手在垫子上为金牌激烈搏斗。
- **助记**：grapple 联想 grasp（抓住）→ 抓住对方搏斗 → 搏斗

### 8. **hectic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：忙碌的；紧张的；手忙脚乱的
- **英文释义**：full of incessant activity; very busy and fast-paced
- **例句**：
  - Monday mornings are always hectic at the office.
  - 周一早上在办公室总是忙得不可开交。
  - Despite her hectic schedule, she always finds time to read.
  - 尽管日程繁忙，她总能找到时间阅读。
- **助记**：hectic 联想 hectare（公顷）→ 一大片事要忙 → 忙碌

### 9. **implore** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：恳求；乞求；哀求
- **英文释义**：to beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something
- **例句**：
  - The villagers implored the government to send aid after the flood.
  - 洪水后村民恳求政府派遣援助。
  - She implored her friend to reconsider the risky decision.
  - 她恳求朋友重新考虑那个冒险的决定。
- **助记**：im（加强）+ plore（哭/求）→ 大声哀求 → 恳求

### 10. **knack** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：诀窍；本领；天分
- **英文释义**：an acquired or natural skill at performing a task; a talent
- **例句**：
  - She has a knack for remembering people's names.
  - 她有记住别人名字的天分。
  - Cooking requires a knack that improves with practice.
  - 烹饪需要通过练习提高的诀窍。
- **助记**：knack 联想 knock（敲）→ 敲门的技巧 → 诀窍

### 高一词汇

### 11. **laudable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：值得赞扬的；令人钦佩的
- **英文释义**：deserving praise and commendation
- **例句**：
  - Her laudable effort to clean the river inspired the whole community.
  - 她值得赞扬的清洁河流的努力激励了整个社区。
  - The charity's goals are laudable, even if progress is slow.
  - 慈善机构的目标值得称赞，即使进展缓慢。
- **助记**：laud（赞美）+ able → 值得赞美的

### 12. **mundane** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：平凡的；世俗的；日常的
- **英文释义**：lacking interest or excitement; dull; of this earthly world
- **例句**：
  - She longed for adventure to escape her mundane daily routine.
  - 她渴望冒险来逃离平凡的日常生活。
  - Even mundane tasks like filing can teach you organizational skills.
  - 即使像归档这样的日常琐事也能教你组织能力。
- **助记**：mund（世界）+ ane → 世俗世界的 → 平凡/日常

### 13. **nostalgia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：怀旧；乡愁；念旧之情
- **英文释义**：a sentimental longing for the past; homesickness
- **例句**：
  - Old photographs always fill her with nostalgia for childhood summers.
  - 老照片总让她对童年的夏天充满怀旧之情。
  - The vintage café was designed to evoke nostalgia for the 1980s.
  - 这家复古咖啡馆的设计旨在唤起对上世纪80年代的怀旧感。
- **助记**：nost（回家）+ algia（痛）→ 想家之痛 → 乡愁/怀旧

### 14. **ostentatious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：炫耀的；卖弄的；浮夸的
- **英文释义**：designed to impress or attract notice; showy
- **例句**：
  - His ostentatious display of wealth annoyed his modest neighbors.
  - 他炫耀财富的行为惹恼了朴素的邻居。
  - The ostentatious decorations made the room look gaudy rather than elegant.
  - 浮夸的装饰让房间看起来俗气而非优雅。
- **助记**：ostent（展示）+ atious → 大肆展示 → 炫耀

### 15. **petulant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：易怒的；任性的；暴躁的
- **英文释义**：childishly sulky or bad-tempered; irritable
- **例句**：
  - The petulant child threw a tantrum when denied a second dessert.
  - 任性的孩子被拒绝第二份甜点后大发脾气。
  - A petulant response to criticism shows a lack of maturity.
  - 对批评发脾气的回应显示出缺乏成熟。
- **助记**：pet（追求）+ ulant → 一味追求自己要的 → 任性
