## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **articulate** `初三` adj./v.

- **中文释义**：口齿清晰的；善于表达的；清楚地表达
- **英文释义**：having or showing the ability to speak fluently and coherently; to express clearly
- **例句**：
  - She is an articulate speaker who can explain complex ideas in simple terms.
  - 她是一位口齿清晰的演讲者，能用简单的术语解释复杂的概念。
  - He struggled to articulate his feelings about the sudden loss.
  - 他努力想要清楚地表达他对突然失去亲人的感受。
- **助记**：art（技巧）+ iculate → 说话有技巧的 → 口齿清晰的/善于表达的

### 2. **bolster** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：支持；增强；鼓励
- **英文释义**：to support or strengthen; to prop up
- **例句**：
  - The government announced new measures to bolster the struggling economy.
  - 政府宣布了新措施来增强疲弱的经济。
  - Words of encouragement from her coach bolstered her confidence before the competition.
  - 教练的鼓励话语在比赛前增强了她的信心。
- **助记**：bolster 联想 bolt（螺栓）→ 像螺栓一样起支撑作用 → 支持/增强

### 3. **comply** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：遵从；服从；顺从
- **英文释义**：to act in accordance with a wish, command, or rule
- **例句**：
  - All restaurants must comply with health and safety regulations.
  - 所有餐厅必须遵守健康和安全法规。
  - The company refused to comply with the court order and faced heavy fines.
  - 公司拒绝遵从法院命令，面临巨额罚款。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ ply（满足/折叠）→ 完全满足要求 → 遵从

### 4. **disclose** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：披露；揭露；公开
- **英文释义**：to make known or reveal information previously secret or private
- **例句**：
  - The company was forced to disclose its financial records to the investigators.
  - 公司被迫向调查人员披露其财务记录。
  - She refused to disclose the source of her information to the journalists.
  - 她拒绝向记者透露她的信息来源。
- **助记**：dis（去除）+ close（关闭）→ 去除关闭状态 → 揭露/公开

### 5. **eminent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：杰出的；著名的；卓越的
- **英文释义**：famous and respected within a particular sphere or profession
- **例句**：
  - The university invited an eminent scientist to deliver the keynote speech.
  - 大学邀请了一位杰出的科学家发表主题演讲。
  - She became one of the most eminent lawyers in the country through years of hard work.
  - 经过多年的努力工作，她成为了该国最杰出的律师之一。
- **助记**：e（出）+ min（突出）+ ent → 突出来的 → 杰出的

### 6. **feasible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可行的；可能的；行得通的
- **英文释义**：possible to do easily or conveniently; likely or probable
- **例句**：
  - The engineers determined that building a bridge across the river was technically feasible.
  - 工程师们确定从技术上来说在河上建桥是可行的。
  - Is it feasible to complete the project within the given budget and timeframe?
  - 在给定的预算和时间框架内完成项目是可行的吗？
- **助记**：feas（做）+ ible（能够）→ 能够做到的 → 可行的

### 7. **grievance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：不满；委屈；申诉
- **英文释义**：a real or imagined wrong or cause for complaint
- **例句**：
  - The workers filed a grievance against the company for unfair treatment.
  - 工人们因不公平待遇向公司提出了申诉。
  - She had a legitimate grievance about being passed over for promotion.
  - 她因为被跳过晋升而有合理的不满。
- **助记**：griev（悲伤/沉重）+ ance → 心中沉重的不满 → 委屈/申诉

### 8. **inadvertent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无意的；不经意的；疏忽的
- **英文释义**：not resulting from or achieved through deliberate planning; unintentional
- **例句**：
  - The inadvertent release of confidential data led to a major security breach.
  - 机密数据的无意泄露导致了重大安全漏洞。
  - His inadvertent remark offended several people at the dinner party.
  - 他不经意的言论冒犯了晚宴上的几个人。
- **助记**：in（不）+ ad（朝向）+ vert（转）+ ent → 没有把注意力转过来 → 无意的/疏忽的

### 9. **mundane** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：平凡的；世俗的；日常的
- **英文释义**：lacking interest or excitement; dull; relating to everyday life
- **例句**：
  - After years of exciting travel, she found everyday office work quite mundane.
  - 经过多年令人兴奋的旅行后，她觉得日常的办公室工作相当平凡。
  - Even the most mundane tasks can become enjoyable if you approach them with the right attitude.
  - 即使是最平凡的任务，如果你以正确的态度对待，也会变得愉快。
- **助记**：mund（世界）+ ane → 属于世俗世界的 → 平凡的/日常的

### 10. **redundant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多余的；冗余的；被裁员的
- **英文释义**：not or no longer needed or useful; superfluous
- **例句**：
  - The new software made many of the old processes redundant.
  - 新软件使许多旧流程变得多余。
  - Hundreds of workers were made redundant when the factory closed down.
  - 工厂关闭时，数百名工人被裁员。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ dund（泛滥/溢出）+ ant → 溢出来的多余的 → 冗余的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **anachronism** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：时代错误；过时的事物
- **英文释义**：a thing belonging to a period other than that in which it exists; something outdated
- **例句**：
  - The use of typewriters in a modern office is considered an anachronism.
  - 在现代办公室中使用打字机被认为是过时的。
  - Some critics view the monarchy as an anachronism in a democratic society.
  - 一些批评家认为君主制在民主社会中是一种时代错误。
- **助记**：ana（反/回）+ chron（时间）+ ism → 回到错误的时间 → 时代错误

### 12. **cursory** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：粗略的；草率的；匆忙的
- **英文释义**：hasty and therefore not thorough or detailed
- **例句**：
  - A cursory glance at the report revealed several obvious errors.
  - 粗略地看一下报告就发现了几个明显的错误。
  - The inspector gave the building only a cursory examination before approving it.
  - 检查员只对建筑进行了草率的检查就批准了。
- **助记**：curs（跑）+ ory → 跑着看的 → 粗略的/草率的

### 13. **harbinger** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：先驱；预兆；先兆
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that announces or signals the approach of another
- **例句**：
  - The first robin of spring is often seen as a harbinger of warmer weather.
  - 春天的第一只知更鸟常被视为温暖天气的预兆。
  - The economic downturn was a harbinger of the financial crisis that followed.
  - 经济衰退是随后金融危机的先兆。
- **助记**：harbor（港口）+ inger → 先到港口探路的人 → 先驱/预兆

### 14. **perfunctory** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：敷衍的；例行公事的；不走心的
- **英文释义**：carried out with a minimum of effort or reflection
- **例句**：
  - He gave a perfunctory nod without really paying attention to what was being said.
  - 他敷衍地点了点头，并没有真正注意别人在说什么。
  - The security guard performed only a perfunctory check of the bags before waving people through.
  - 保安只对包进行了例行公事般的检查就挥手让人们通过。
- **助记**：per（完成）+ funct（功能）+ ory → 只是完成功能而已 → 敷衍的

### 15. **supercilious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：目中无人的；傲慢的；高傲的
- **英文释义**：behaving or looking as though one thinks one is superior to others
- **例句**：
  - The supercilious waiter looked down on customers who ordered inexpensive dishes.
  - 那位傲慢的服务员看不起点便宜菜的顾客。
  - Her supercilious attitude made it difficult for her to make friends at the new school.
  - 她目中无人的态度使她在新学校很难交到朋友。
- **助记**：super（上面）+ cili（眉毛）+ ous → 抬高眉毛看人 → 傲慢的
