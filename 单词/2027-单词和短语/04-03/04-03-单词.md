## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **asylum** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：庇护；收容所；精神病院
- **英文释义**：the protection granted by a nation to someone who has left their home country as a political refugee; an institution for the care of the mentally ill
- **例句**：
  - Many refugees sought asylum in neighboring countries.
  - 许多难民在邻国寻求庇护。
- **助记**：a（不）+ sylum（抓）→ 不被抓到的地方 → 庇护所

### 2. **coarse** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：粗糙的；粗俗的；粗劣的
- **英文释义**：rough or loose in texture; rude and offensive
- **例句**：
  - The cloth felt coarse against her skin.
  - 这块布摸起来很粗糙。
- **助记**：coarse → course（课程）→ 这个课程很"粗"糙 → 粗糙的

### 3. **dispatch** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 派遣；发送；迅速处理 n. 急件；快信
- **英文释义**：v. to send off to a destination or for a purpose; n. an official report or message
- **例句**：
  - The company dispatched a team to investigate the problem.
  - 公司派遣了一支队伍去调查问题。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ patch（补丁/片）→ 把东西分发出去 → 派遣

### 4. **feminine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：女性的；有女子气质的
- **英文释义**：having qualities or an appearance traditionally associated with women
- **例句**：
  - She wore a dress with a very feminine design.
  - 她穿了一件非常有女性气质的裙子。
- **助记**：femin（女性）+ ine → 女性的

### 5. **grudge** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 怨恨；积怨 v. 怀恨；不愿给
- **英文释义**：n. a persistent feeling of ill will or resentment; v. to be resentfully unwilling to give
- **例句**：
  - He still holds a grudge against his former friend.
  - 他仍然对他的前朋友怀有怨恨。
- **助记**：grudge 谐音"格如阻"→ 格格不入如阻碍一样 → 怨恨

### 6. **illuminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：照亮；阐明；启发
- **英文释义**：to light up; to help to clarify or explain
- **例句**：
  - The fireworks illuminated the night sky.
  - 烟花照亮了夜空。
- **助记**：il（加强）+ lumin（光）+ ate → 加强光亮 → 照亮

### 7. **morale** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：士气；精神面貌
- **英文释义**：the confidence, enthusiasm, and discipline of a person or group
- **例句**：
  - The victory boosted the team's morale.
  - 这场胜利提振了团队的士气。
- **助记**：moral（道德）+ e → 精神层面的状态 → 士气

### 8. **peach** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：桃子；桃色
- **英文释义**：a round stone fruit that is juicy and has yellow flesh and pink or yellow skin
- **例句**：
  - She bit into the ripe peach and juice ran down her chin.
  - 她咬了一口熟透的桃子，汁水从下巴流下。
- **助记**：peach → beach（海滩）→ 在海滩上吃桃子 → 桃子

### 9. **remnant** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：残余物；遗留物；剩余部分
- **英文释义**：a small remaining quantity of something
- **例句**：
  - The ruins are remnants of an ancient civilization.
  - 这些废墟是古代文明的遗迹。
- **助记**：re（留下）+ mn + ant → 留下来的东西 → 残余物

### 10. **stride** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 大步；进展 v. 大步走
- **英文释义**：n. a long step in walking; progress; v. to walk with long, decisive steps
- **例句**：
  - Science has made great strides in the last century.
  - 科学在过去一个世纪取得了巨大进展。
- **助记**：s + tride（ride 骑）→ 像骑马一样大步走 → 大步/进展

### 高一词汇

### 11. **benevolent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；善意的；慈善的
- **英文释义**：well-meaning and kindly
- **例句**：
  - The benevolent old man donated his savings to charity.
  - 这位仁慈的老人把积蓄捐给了慈善机构。
- **助记**：bene（好的）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 有好的意愿的 → 仁慈的

### 12. **culminate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：达到顶点；以……告终
- **英文释义**：to reach a climax or point of highest development
- **例句**：
  - Years of research culminated in a breakthrough discovery.
  - 多年的研究最终以突破性发现告终。
- **助记**：culmin（顶峰）+ ate → 到达顶峰 → 达到顶点

### 13. **expedite** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：加速；促进
- **英文释义**：to make an action or process happen sooner or be accomplished more quickly
- **例句**：
  - We need to expedite the delivery of these goods.
  - 我们需要加快这些货物的交付。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ped（脚）+ ite → 把脚伸出去快跑 → 加速

### 14. **meander** `高一` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 蜿蜒流动；漫步 n. 曲折的河道
- **英文释义**：v. to follow a winding course; to wander aimlessly; n. a winding curve
- **例句**：
  - The river meanders through the green valley.
  - 河流蜿蜒穿过绿色的山谷。
- **助记**：meander → me + ander（wander 漫游）→ 我在漫游 → 蜿蜒/漫步

### 15. **repercussion** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：反响；后果；影响
- **英文释义**：an unintended consequence occurring some time after an event or action
- **例句**：
  - The decision could have serious repercussions for the economy.
  - 这个决定可能对经济产生严重影响。
- **助记**：re（回）+ percussion（敲击）→ 回击/反弹 → 反响/后果
