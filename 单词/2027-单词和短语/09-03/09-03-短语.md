## 📖 短语详解

### 初三短语

### 1. **at odds with** `初三`

- **中文释义**：与……不一致；与……争执；和……有矛盾
- **用法说明**：be at odds with + n. 表示观点、利益或事实上的冲突。
- **例句**：
  - His casual style is at odds with the formal atmosphere of the law firm.
  - 他随意的风格与律师事务所的正式氛围格格不入。
  - The two witnesses were at odds with each other on key details of the accident.
  - 两名证人在事故关键细节上说法不一。
- **近义短语**：in conflict with; incompatible with; clash with; contradict
- **易混淆短语**：at odds with（不一致）vs by all odds（很可能）—— 含义完全不同

### 2. **boil down to** `初三`

- **中文释义**：归结为；本质是；关键在于
- **用法说明**：sth. boils down to + n./doing 表示把复杂问题简化为核心。
- **例句**：
  - The debate boils down to whether we value freedom more than security.
  - 这场辩论归根结底是我们更重视自由还是安全。
  - Success in this course boils down to consistent practice every day.
  - 这门课能否学好关键在于每天坚持练习。
- **近义短语**：amount to; come down to; reduce to; essentially mean
- **易混淆短语**：boil down to（归结为）vs boil over（沸溢/发怒）—— 后者指情绪爆发

### 3. **get the hang of** `初三`

- **中文释义**：掌握诀窍；熟悉做法；学会操作
- **用法说明**：get the hang of + n./doing 表示经过练习后学会技能。
- **例句**：
  - It took me a week to get the hang of driving a manual car.
  - 我花了一周才掌握开手动挡车的窍门。
  - Don't worry — you'll get the hang of the software after a few tutorials.
  - 别担心——看几个教程后你就会熟悉这款软件。
- **近义短语**：pick up; grasp; master the basics of; get used to
- **易混淆短语**：get the hang of（掌握）vs hang on（坚持/稍等）—— 无词形关联，勿混

### 4. **in the long run** `初三`

- **中文释义**：从长远来看；终究；最终
- **用法说明**：作状语，与 in the short term 相对。
- **例句**：
  - Cutting training budgets may save money now, but it will hurt quality in the long run.
  - 削减培训预算眼下省钱，从长远看会损害质量。
  - In the long run, renewable energy is cheaper than fossil fuels.
  - 从长远看，可再生能源比化石燃料更便宜。
- **近义短语**：ultimately; eventually; over time; in the end
- **易混淆短语**：in the long run（长远）vs in the long term（同义，常可互换）—— 极相近

### 高一短语

### 5. **play devil's advocate** `高一`

- **中文释义**：故意唱反调；假意反驳以检验论点
- **英文释义**：to argue against an idea in order to test its validity or expose weaknesses
- **例句**：
  - The teacher asked a student to play devil's advocate during the ethics discussion.
  - 老师请一名学生在伦理讨论中故意提出反对意见。
  - I don't really disagree — I'm just playing devil's advocate to strengthen our plan.
  - 我并非真反对——只是故意挑刺以便完善我们的计划。
- **近义短语**：argue for the sake of argument; take the opposing view; steel-man the counterargument
- **易混淆短语**：play devil's advocate（检验论点）vs speak of the devil（说曹操曹操到）—— 无关
