## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **affable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：和蔼可亲的；友善健谈的
- **英文释义**：friendly, good-natured, and easy to talk to
- **例句**：
  - The affable host made every guest feel welcome at the dinner party.
  - 和蔼可亲的主人让每位客人在晚宴上都感到宾至如归。
  - Despite his fame, the writer remains affable to readers who approach him.
  - 尽管成名，这位作家对上前搭话的读者仍然和蔼可亲。
- **助记**：af（向）+ fabl（说）→ 善于与人交谈 → 和蔼的

### 2. **benevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：仁慈的；慈善的；乐善好施的
- **英文释义**：well meaning and kindly; serving a charitable rather than profit-making purpose
- **例句**：
  - A benevolent donor funded the construction of the new library.
  - 一位慈善捐助者出资建造了新图书馆。
  - She had a benevolent smile that put nervous students at ease.
  - 她带着仁慈的微笑，让紧张的学生放松下来。
- **助记**：bene（好）+ vol（意愿）→ 好心好意 → 仁慈的

### 3. **cumbersome** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：笨重的；累赘的；难以处理的
- **英文释义**：large, heavy, or difficult to carry or use; unwieldy
- **例句**：
  - The hikers left their cumbersome backpacks at the base camp.
  - 徒步者把笨重的背包留在登山大本营。
  - The approval process is so cumbersome that many small businesses give up.
  - 审批程序过于繁琐，许多小企业只好放弃。
- **助记**：cumber（阻碍）+ some → 带来阻碍的 → 笨重累赘的

### 4. **discrepancy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：差异；不符；不一致
- **英文释义**：a lack of compatibility or similarity between two or more facts
- **例句**：
  - Auditors found a discrepancy between the inventory records and actual stock.
  - 审计员发现库存记录与实际存货不符。
  - There is a growing discrepancy between urban and rural incomes.
  - 城乡收入差距日益扩大。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ crep（裂开）→ 裂开的不一致 → 差异

### 5. **formidable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：强大的；令人敬畏的；难对付的
- **英文释义**：inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, or capable
- **例句**：
  - The team faced a formidable opponent in the championship final.
  - 决赛中队遇到了强大的对手。
  - She has a formidable intellect and rarely loses a debate.
  - 她才智过人，辩论很少落败。
- **助记**：formid（恐惧）+ able → 令人畏惧的 → 强大的

### 6. **gullible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：轻信的；易受骗的
- **英文释义**：easily persuaded to believe something; naive
- **例句**：
  - Gullible consumers often fall for online shopping scams.
  - 轻信的消费者常常落入网购骗局。
  - The con artist targeted elderly people whom he considered more gullible.
  - 骗子专挑他认为更容易上当的老年人下手。
- **助记**：gull（鸥/易骗的人）+ ible → 易上当的

### 7. **hypocritical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚伪的；伪善的
- **英文释义**：behaving in a way that suggests one has higher standards than is really the case
- **例句**：
  - It is hypocritical to lecture others about honesty while lying yourself.
  - 自己撒谎却教训别人要诚实，这是虚伪的。
  - The politician was accused of hypocritical statements about environmental protection.
  - 这位政客关于环境保护的言论被指虚伪。
- **助记**：hypo（在下面）+ crit（判断）→ 在下面另有一套标准 → 虚伪的

### 8. **impetuous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：冲动的；鲁莽的；急躁的
- **英文释义**：acting or done quickly and without thought or care
- **例句**：
  - An impetuous decision to quit school caused him years of regret.
  - 冲动退学让他后悔多年。
  - The impetuous young knight charged into battle without waiting for orders.
  - 急躁的年轻骑士未等命令就冲入战场。
- **助记**：im（向内）+ pet（追求）→ 内心冲动驱使 → 冲动的

### 9. **judicious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：明智的；审慎的；有判断力的
- **英文释义**：having, showing, or done with good judgment or sense
- **例句**：
  - A judicious use of resources helped the charity help more families.
  - 明智地利用资源使慈善机构帮助了更多家庭。
  - The judicious lawyer advised her client to settle out of court.
  - 审慎的律师建议当事人和庭外和解。
- **助记**：judic（判断）+ ious → 善于判断的 → 明智的

### 10. **novice** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：新手；初学者；见习修士/修女
- **英文释义**：a person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation
- **例句**：
  - The skiing instructor patiently taught novices how to brake on gentle slopes.
  - 滑雪教练耐心地教初学者在缓坡上如何刹车。
  - Even a chess novice can learn the basic rules in an afternoon.
  - 即使是国际象棋新手也能在一下午学会基本规则。
- **助记**：nov（新）+ ice → 新人 → 新手

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ignominious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：可耻的；不光彩的；丢脸的
- **英文释义**：deserving or causing public disgrace or shame
- **例句**：
  - The team suffered an ignominious defeat, losing by ten goals.
  - 球队惨败，净失十球，颜面尽失。
  - He left office in ignominious circumstances after the corruption scandal.
  - 腐败丑闻后，他在可耻的情况下离职。
- **助记**：ig（不）+ nomin（名字）+ ious → 名声扫地 → 可耻的

### 12. **juxtapose** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：并置；把……并列对照
- **英文释义**：to place two things close together or side by side for comparison
- **例句**：
  - The exhibition juxtaposes ancient artifacts with modern replicas.
  - 展览将古代文物与现代复制品并置展出。
  - The author juxtaposes rich and poor neighborhoods to highlight inequality.
  - 作者把贫富社区并列描写以突出不平等。
- **助记**：juxta（靠近）+ pose（放置）→ 放在一起 → 并置

### 13. **lethargic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：昏睡的；无精打采的；迟钝的
- **英文释义**：affected by lethargy; sluggish and apathetic
- **例句**：
  - Hot, humid weather made everyone feel lethargic in the afternoon.
  - 闷热潮湿的天气让每个人下午都昏昏欲睡。
  - The patient became lethargic after taking the strong painkillers.
  - 服用强效止痛药后，病人变得无精打采。
- **助记**：leth（遗忘/死亡，Lethe 冥河）+ argic → 像失去活力 → 昏沉的

### 14. **misanthrope** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：厌恶人类者；愤世嫉俗者
- **英文释义**：a person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society
- **例句**：
  - The novel's protagonist is a misanthrope who lives alone in the woods.
  - 小说的主人公是一位独居林中的厌世者。
  - Critics called him a misanthrope, but he simply preferred quiet libraries to parties.
  - 批评者称他愤世嫉俗，但他只是喜欢安静的图书馆胜过聚会。
- **助记**：mis（厌恶）+ anthrop（人）→ 厌恶人 → 厌世者

### 15. **nomenclature** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：命名法；术语体系；名称
- **英文释义**：the devising or choosing of names for things, especially in a science or discipline
- **例句**：
  - Biology students must learn the Latin nomenclature for species classification.
  - 生物专业学生必须学习物种分类的拉丁命名法。
  - The nomenclature of chemical compounds follows strict international rules.
  - 化合物的命名遵循严格的国际规则。
- **助记**：nomen（名字）+ clature → 命名体系 → 术语
