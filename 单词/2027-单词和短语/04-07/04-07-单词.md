## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **ancestor** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：祖先；祖宗
- **英文释义**：a person from whom one is descended, especially one more remote than a grandparent
- **例句**：
  - Our ancestors lived in caves thousands of years ago.
  - 我们的祖先在几千年前住在洞穴里。
- **助记**：anc（古老的）+ est + or → 古老的人 → 祖先

### 2. **captive** `初三` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：n. 俘虏；囚犯 adj. 被俘的；受控制的
- **英文释义**：n. a person who has been taken prisoner; adj. imprisoned or confined
- **例句**：
  - The captives were finally released after months of negotiation.
  - 经过数月谈判，俘虏终于被释放了。
- **助记**：capt（抓住）+ ive → 被抓住的人 → 俘虏

### 3. **diminish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减少；缩小；降低
- **英文释义**：to make or become less
- **例句**：
  - The rain began to diminish by the afternoon.
  - 到下午雨开始减小了。
- **助记**：di + mini（小）+ sh → 变得更小 → 减少

### 4. **epidemic** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：流行病；传染病大爆发
- **英文释义**：a widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in a community
- **例句**：
  - The government acted quickly to contain the epidemic.
  - 政府迅速采取行动控制疫情。
- **助记**：epi（在……之上）+ dem（人民）+ ic → 在人民之上蔓延 → 流行病

### 5. **flock** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 群；一群 v. 聚集；成群结队地去
- **英文释义**：n. a number of birds or animals of one kind together; v. to gather or move in a crowd
- **例句**：
  - Tourists flock to the beach every summer.
  - 每年夏天游客们蜂拥而至到海滩。
- **助记**：flock → block（堵住）→ 一大群堵住了路 → 群/聚集

### 6. **hatch** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 孵化；策划 n. 舱口；孵化
- **英文释义**：v. to emerge from an egg; to devise a plan secretly; n. an opening in a deck or floor
- **例句**：
  - The chicks hatched from their eggs this morning.
  - 小鸡今天早上从蛋里孵出来了。
- **助记**：hatch → catch（抓住）→ 抓住时机破壳而出 → 孵化

### 7. **mingle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：混合；交往；相处
- **英文释义**：to mix or cause to mix together; to move around and talk to people at a social event
- **例句**：
  - Guests mingled freely at the cocktail party.
  - 客人们在鸡尾酒会上自由交流。
- **助记**：mingle → mingle + single → 单身的人混在一起交往 → 混合/交往

### 8. **overwhelm** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：压倒；使不知所措；淹没
- **英文释义**：to overpower or to affect someone very strongly
- **例句**：
  - She was overwhelmed by the kindness of strangers.
  - 陌生人的善意让她不知所措。
- **助记**：over（超过）+ whelm（淹没）→ 被淹没 → 压倒/不知所措

### 9. **rehearsal** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：排练；彩排；预演
- **英文释义**：a practice or trial performance of a play or other work for later public performance
- **例句**：
  - The actors had a final rehearsal before opening night.
  - 演员们在首演之夜前进行了最后一次彩排。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ hearsal（hearing 听）→ 再次听/演 → 排练

### 10. **trophy** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：奖杯；奖品；战利品
- **英文释义**：a cup or other decorative object awarded as a prize for a victory or success
- **例句**：
  - The team proudly held up the championship trophy.
  - 球队自豪地举起了冠军奖杯。
- **助记**：tro（转向）+ phy → 胜利后转向领取的 → 奖杯

### 高一词汇

### 11. **autonomous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：自治的；自主的；独立的
- **英文释义**：having the freedom to govern itself or control its own affairs
- **例句**：
  - The university is an autonomous institution that makes its own decisions.
  - 该大学是一个自主做出决策的独立机构。
- **助记**：auto（自己）+ nom（法则）+ ous → 自己制定法则的 → 自治的

### 12. **conundrum** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：难题；谜语
- **英文释义**：a confusing and difficult problem or question
- **例句**：
  - The detective faced a real conundrum in solving the case.
  - 侦探在破案中面临一个真正的难题。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ undrum → 大家一起都解不开的 → 难题

### 13. **erratic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不稳定的；反复无常的；不规则的
- **英文释义**：not even or regular in pattern or movement; unpredictable
- **例句**：
  - His erratic behavior worried his friends.
  - 他反复无常的行为让朋友们担心。
- **助记**：err（错误/偏离）+ atic → 总是偏离正轨的 → 不稳定的

### 14. **mundane** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：平凡的；世俗的；单调的
- **英文释义**：lacking interest or excitement; dull; of this earthly world rather than spiritual
- **例句**：
  - She was tired of the mundane routine of daily life.
  - 她厌倦了日常生活的单调常规。
- **助记**：mund（世界）+ ane → 世界上普通的事 → 平凡的

### 15. **reiterate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：重申；反复说
- **英文释义**：to say something again or a number of times, typically for emphasis
- **例句**：
  - The teacher reiterated the importance of handing in homework on time.
  - 老师重申了按时交作业的重要性。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ iterate（重复）→ 再次重复 → 重申
