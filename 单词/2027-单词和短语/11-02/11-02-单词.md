## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **depict** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：描绘；描述；刻画
- **英文释义**：to show or represent by a drawing, painting, or in words
- **例句**：
  - The mural depicts scenes of daily life in ancient China.
  - 壁画描绘了中国古代的日常生活场景。
  - The novel depicts the struggles of immigrants in a new country.
  - 这部小说描述了移民在新国家的奋斗历程。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ pict（画）→ 画下来 → 描绘

### 2. **prosperous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：繁荣的；兴旺的；成功的
- **英文释义**：successful in material terms; flourishing financially
- **例句**：
  - The prosperous city attracted businesses and tourists from all over the world.
  - 这座繁荣的城市吸引了来自世界各地的企业和游客。
  - Hard work and innovation led to a prosperous economy.
  - 勤劳和创新带来了繁荣的经济。
- **助记**：prosper（繁荣）+ ous → 繁荣的

### 3. **undermine** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：暗中破坏；逐渐削弱
- **英文释义**：to damage or weaken gradually or insidiously
- **例句**：
  - Constant criticism can undermine a child's confidence.
  - 不断的批评会逐渐削弱孩子的自信心。
  - The scandal undermined public trust in the government.
  - 丑闻破坏了公众对政府的信任。
- **助记**：under（在下面）+ mine（挖矿）→ 在下面挖掘 → 暗中破坏

### 4. **explicit** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：明确的；清楚的；直截了当的
- **英文释义**：stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion
- **例句**：
  - The teacher gave explicit instructions on how to complete the assignment.
  - 老师给出了如何完成作业的明确指示。
  - The contract contains explicit terms about payment deadlines.
  - 合同中包含关于付款期限的明确条款。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ plic（折叠）+ it → 展开说清楚 → 明确的

### 5. **sustain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：维持；支撑；遭受
- **英文释义**：to strengthen or support; to undergo or suffer
- **例句**：
  - The athlete couldn't sustain the intense pace and slowed down in the final lap.
  - 运动员无法维持高强度配速，在最后一圈慢了下来。
  - The building sustained severe damage during the earthquake.
  - 这栋建筑在地震中遭受了严重损坏。
- **助记**：sus（下面）+ tain（持有）→ 在下面支撑 → 维持

### 6. **confine** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：限制；限定；关押
- **英文释义**：to keep or restrict someone or something within certain limits
- **例句**：
  - The doctor confined the patient to bed rest for a week.
  - 医生限制病人卧床休息一周。
  - Please confine your discussion to the topic at hand.
  - 请将讨论限定在当前话题范围内。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ fine（边界）→ 在边界之内 → 限制

### 7. **formidable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：令人敬畏的；强大的；难以对付的
- **英文释义**：inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, or capable
- **例句**：
  - The opposing team is a formidable challenger in this year's tournament.
  - 对手是今年锦标赛中令人敬畏的挑战者。
  - She faces a formidable task in rebuilding the company's reputation.
  - 她面临重建公司声誉这一艰巨任务。
- **助记**：formid（恐惧）+ able → 令人恐惧的 → 令人敬畏的

### 8. **revert** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：恢复原状；回归；重提
- **英文释义**：to return to a previous state, condition, or practice
- **例句**：
  - After the experiment, the system reverted to its original settings.
  - 实验后，系统恢复到了原始设置。
  - Without regular practice, your skills may revert to their earlier level.
  - 没有经常练习，你的技能可能会退回到之前的水平。
- **助记**：re（回）+ vert（转）→ 转回去 → 恢复原状

### 9. **arbitrary** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：任意的；武断的；专横的
- **英文释义**：based on random choice or personal whim rather than reason
- **例句**：
  - The selection process seemed arbitrary, with no clear criteria.
  - 选拔过程似乎很随意，没有明确的标准。
  - Arbitrary rules only serve to frustrate employees.
  - 武断的规定只会让员工感到沮丧。
- **助记**：arbitr（仲裁）+ ary → 由一人仲裁决定 → 武断的

### 10. **exquisite** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：精美的；精致的；极度的
- **英文释义**：extremely beautiful and delicate; intensely felt
- **例句**：
  - The museum displayed exquisite porcelain from the Song Dynasty.
  - 博物馆展出了宋代的精美瓷器。
  - She felt exquisite joy when she held her newborn baby for the first time.
  - 她第一次抱起新生婴儿时感到极度的喜悦。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ quisite（寻求）→ 精心寻找出来的 → 精美的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **draconian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：严酷的；严厉的
- **英文释义**：excessively harsh and severe, especially of laws or punishments
- **例句**：
  - Critics condemned the draconian measures imposed during the state of emergency.
  - 批评者谴责紧急状态期间实施的严酷措施。
  - Some people consider the new traffic penalties to be draconian.
  - 有些人认为新的交通处罚过于严厉。
- **助记**：Draco（古雅典立法者）+ nian → 德拉古的法律极其严厉 → 严酷的

### 12. **perspicacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：洞察力强的；敏锐的；有见地的
- **英文释义**：having a ready insight into and understanding of things; shrewd
- **例句**：
  - A perspicacious investor can spot market trends before they become obvious.
  - 有洞察力的投资者能在市场趋势明朗之前发现它们。
  - Her perspicacious observations about human nature impressed everyone.
  - 她对人性的敏锐观察给每个人留下了深刻印象。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ spic（看）+ acious → 看得透彻 → 洞察力强的

### 13. **extenuate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻（罪行）；使显得不那么严重
- **英文释义**：to make guilt or an offense seem less serious
- **例句**：
  - Nothing can extenuate the severity of this crime.
  - 没有什么能减轻这一罪行的严重性。
  - The lawyer tried to present extenuating circumstances to reduce the sentence.
  - 律师试图提出减轻处罚的情节来减少刑期。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ tenu（薄）+ ate → 使变薄 → 减轻

### 14. **hegemony** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：霸权；领导权；主导地位
- **英文释义**：leadership or dominance, especially by one country or social group over others
- **例句**：
  - The decline of colonial hegemony reshaped the political map of the world.
  - 殖民霸权的衰落重塑了世界政治格局。
  - Several nations competed for hegemony over the trade routes.
  - 几个国家争夺贸易路线的主导权。
- **助记**：hegemon（领导者）+ y → 领导者的权力 → 霸权

### 15. **apotheosis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：神化；典范；顶峰
- **英文释义**：the highest point in the development of something; the elevation of someone to divine status
- **例句**：
  - The final symphony was the apotheosis of the composer's career.
  - 最后一部交响曲是这位作曲家职业生涯的巅峰。
  - Ancient Romans practiced the apotheosis of their emperors after death.
  - 古罗马人在皇帝去世后将其神化。
- **助记**：apo（使成为）+ theosis（神）→ 使成神 → 神化
