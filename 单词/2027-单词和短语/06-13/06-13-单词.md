## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **assimilate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：吸收；融入；消化
- **英文释义**：take in (information, ideas, or culture) and understand fully; integrate into a larger group
- **例句**：
  - It takes time to assimilate all the new information in this course.
  - 消化这门课程中的所有新信息需要时间。
- **助记**：as（向）+ simil（相似）+ ate → 使变得相似 → 融入

### 2. **caliber** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：口径；才能；品质
- **英文释义**：the quality of someone's character or the level of their ability; the diameter of a bullet
- **例句**：
  - We need employees of the highest caliber for this project.
  - 这个项目我们需要最高水平的员工。
- **助记**：caliber 原指枪管口径 → 引申为水准/才能

### 3. **correlate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使相互关联；相关
- **英文释义**：have a mutual relationship or connection, in which one thing affects another
- **例句**：
  - Research shows that exercise correlates with improved mental health.
  - 研究表明运动与改善心理健康有关。
- **助记**：co（共同）+ relate（关联）→ 共同关联 → 相关

### 4. **detrimental** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：有害的；不利的
- **英文释义**：tending to cause harm
- **例句**：
  - Excessive sugar intake is detrimental to your health.
  - 过多的糖分摄入对你的健康有害。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ tri（磨）+ mental → 磨损下去 → 有害的

### 5. **endorse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：认可；支持；背书
- **英文释义**：declare one's public approval or support of; sign the back of a check
- **例句**：
  - Several celebrities have endorsed the new product.
  - 几位名人为新产品代言。
- **助记**：en（使）+ dorse（背）→ 在背面签字 → 背书/认可

### 6. **fortitude** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：坚韧；刚毅；不屈不挠
- **英文释义**：courage in pain or adversity
- **例句**：
  - She showed great fortitude during her long battle with illness.
  - 她在与疾病的长期斗争中表现出了极大的坚韧。
- **助记**：fort（强壮）+ itude → 强壮的品质 → 坚韧

### 7. **holistic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：整体的；全面的
- **英文释义**：characterized by the treatment of the whole person, taking into account mental and social factors
- **例句**：
  - The school takes a holistic approach to education.
  - 这所学校采取全面的教育方式。
- **助记**：hol（整体）+ istic → 整体的

### 8. **inevitable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可避免的；必然的
- **英文释义**：certain to happen; unavoidable
- **例句**：
  - Change is inevitable in a rapidly developing society.
  - 在快速发展的社会中，变化是不可避免的。
- **助记**：in（不）+ evit（避免）+ able → 不能避免的

### 9. **myriad** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：无数的；大量的；无数
- **英文释义**：a countless or extremely great number
- **例句**：
  - There are myriad reasons why students choose to study abroad.
  - 学生选择出国留学的原因有无数个。
- **助记**：myriad 在古希腊代表一万 → 引申为无数

### 10. **precursor** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：先驱；先兆；前身
- **英文释义**：a person or thing that comes before another of the same kind; a forerunner
- **例句**：
  - The invention of the telegraph was a precursor to modern telecommunications.
  - 电报的发明是现代电信的前身。
- **助记**：pre（在前）+ cursor（奔跑者）→ 在前面跑的人 → 先驱

### 高一词汇

### 11. **cacophony** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：刺耳的声音；噪音；不和谐
- **英文释义**：a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds
- **例句**：
  - The cacophony of car horns filled the busy intersection.
  - 刺耳的汽车喇叭声充满了繁忙的十字路口。
- **助记**：caco（坏的）+ phony（声音）→ 坏的声音 → 刺耳的声音

### 12. **equanimity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：镇定；沉着；平静
- **英文释义**：mental calmness, composure, and evenness of temper, especially in a difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - She accepted the bad news with remarkable equanimity.
  - 她以非凡的沉着接受了坏消息。
- **助记**：equ（平等）+ anim（心灵）+ ity → 心灵平等/平衡 → 镇定

### 13. **juxtapose** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：并列；并置
- **英文释义**：place close together for contrasting effect
- **例句**：
  - The exhibition juxtaposes modern art with classical paintings.
  - 展览将现代艺术与古典绘画并列展出。
- **助记**：juxta（旁边）+ pose（放置）→ 放在旁边 → 并列

### 14. **quixotic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不切实际的；空想的
- **英文释义**：exceedingly idealistic; unrealistic and impractical
- **例句**：
  - His quixotic plan to reform the entire system overnight was doomed to fail.
  - 他不切实际地想要一夜之间改革整个系统的计划注定要失败。
- **助记**：来自 Don Quixote（堂吉诃德）→ 像堂吉诃德一样不切实际

### 15. **ubiquitous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无处不在的；普遍存在的
- **英文释义**：present, appearing, or found everywhere
- **例句**：
  - Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern life.
  - 智能手机在现代生活中已无处不在。
- **助记**：ubique（到处）+ ous → 到处都有的 → 无处不在的
