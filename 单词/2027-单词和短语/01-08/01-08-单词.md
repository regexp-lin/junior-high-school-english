## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **compose** /kəmˈpəʊz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：组成；创作
- **英文释义**：to form the parts of something; to write music or poetry
- **例句**：
  - Water is composed of hydrogen and oxygen.
  - 水由氢和氧组成。
  - Mozart composed his first symphony at a very young age.
  - 莫扎特很小就创作了第一部交响曲。
- **助记**：com-（一起）+ pose（放置）→ 放在一起 → 组成；创作

### 2. **compulsory** /kəmˈpʌlsəri/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：强制的；必修的
- **英文释义**：required by law or a rule; obligatory
- **例句**：
  - Education is compulsory for children aged 6 to 15 in China.
  - 在中国，6至15岁的儿童必须接受义务教育。
  - English is a compulsory subject in most schools.
  - 英语在大多数学校是必修科目。
- **助记**：com-（加强）+ puls（推）+ -ory → 强推的 → 强制的

### 3. **concentrate** /ˈkɒnsntreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：集中；专注
- **英文释义**：to give all your attention to something
- **例句**：
  - I can't concentrate with all this noise.
  - 这么吵我无法集中注意力。
  - She concentrated on her studies and passed the exam.
  - 她专注于学习，通过了考试。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ centr（中心）+ -ate → 集中到中心 → 专注

### 4. **conclude** /kənˈkluːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：得出结论；结束
- **英文释义**：to decide that something is true after thinking about it; to bring to an end
- **例句**：
  - The detective concluded that the suspect was guilty.
  - 侦探得出结论认为嫌疑人有罪。
  - The meeting concluded at 5 p.m.
  - 会议在下午5点结束。
- **助记**：con-（完全）+ clude（关闭）→ 完全关上 → 结束；得出结论

### 5. **concrete** /ˈkɒŋkriːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：具体的；混凝土
- **英文释义**：based on facts, not abstract; a building material
- **例句**：
  - Can you give me a concrete example?
  - 你能给我一个具体的例子吗？
  - The bridge is made of steel and concrete.
  - 这座桥由钢铁和混凝土建成。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ crete（生长）→ 凝结在一起 → 混凝土；具体的

### 6. **conduct** /kənˈdʌkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：进行；行为
- **英文释义**：to organize and carry out; the way a person behaves
- **例句**：
  - The scientist conducted an experiment in the lab.
  - 科学家在实验室进行了一项实验。
  - His conduct in school has improved a lot.
  - 他在学校的行为进步了很多。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ duct（引导）→ 引导人们一起做 → 进行

### 7. **confess** /kənˈfes/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：承认；坦白
- **英文释义**：to admit that you have done something wrong
- **例句**：
  - He confessed to cheating on the test.
  - 他承认考试作弊了。
  - She confessed that she had lied about her age.
  - 她坦白说自己谎报了年龄。
- **助记**：con-（完全）+ fess（说）→ 完全说出来 → 坦白

### 8. **conflict** /ˈkɒnflɪkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：冲突；矛盾
- **英文释义**：a serious disagreement; to be in opposition
- **例句**：
  - There is a conflict between the two countries over the border.
  - 两国在边界问题上有冲突。
  - His words conflict with his actions.
  - 他的言行不一。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ flict（打击）→ 互相打击 → 冲突

### 9. **confuse** /kənˈfjuːz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使困惑；混淆
- **英文释义**：to make someone unable to think clearly; to mistake one thing for another
- **例句**：
  - The complicated instructions confused everyone.
  - 复杂的说明让所有人困惑。
  - Don't confuse "affect" with "effect."
  - 不要把"affect"和"effect"搞混了。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ fuse（倒/混合）→ 混在一起 → 混淆

### 10. **consequence** /ˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：后果；结果
- **英文释义**：a result of a particular action or situation
- **例句**：
  - You must face the consequences of your actions.
  - 你必须面对你行为的后果。
  - As a consequence of the heavy rain, the match was cancelled.
  - 由于大雨，比赛被取消了。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ sequ（跟随）+ -ence → 跟随而来的事 → 后果

### 高一词汇

### 11. **diligent** /ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：勤奋的；刻苦的
- **英文释义**：showing careful and persistent work or effort
- **例句**：
  - She is a diligent student who always finishes her homework on time.
  - 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是按时完成作业。
  - Diligent practice is the key to mastering a musical instrument.
  - 勤奋练习是掌握乐器的关键。
- **助记**：di-（分开）+ lig（选择）+ -ent → 选择投入精力 → 勤奋的

### 12. **diminish** /dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：减少；缩小
- **英文释义**：to become or make less, smaller, or less important
- **例句**：
  - The storm's power diminished as it moved inland.
  - 暴风雨深入内陆后力量减弱了。
  - Nothing can diminish her achievements.
  - 什么都无法削弱她的成就。
- **助记**：di-（分离）+ min（小）+ -ish → 变小 → 减少

### 13. **discern** /dɪˈsɜːn/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：辨别；识别
- **英文释义**：to recognize or understand something that is not obvious
- **例句**：
  - It was hard to discern the truth from all the rumours.
  - 从所有谣言中辨别真相很困难。
  - She could discern a faint light in the distance.
  - 她能辨别出远处微弱的光。
- **助记**：dis-（分开）+ cern（筛选）→ 分开筛选 → 辨别

### 14. **dissent** /dɪˈsent/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：异议；不同意
- **英文释义**：disagreement with an official opinion or decision
- **例句**：
  - He expressed his dissent during the meeting.
  - 他在会议上表达了异议。
  - Several members dissented from the committee's decision.
  - 几位成员对委员会的决定持不同意见。
- **助记**：dis-（分离）+ sent（感觉）→ 感觉不同 → 异议

### 15. **dormant** /ˈdɔːmənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：休眠的；蛰伏的
- **英文释义**：not active or growing but having the ability to become active in the future
- **例句**：
  - The volcano has been dormant for over a hundred years.
  - 这座火山已经休眠了一百多年。
  - Many plants remain dormant during winter.
  - 许多植物在冬季处于休眠状态。
- **助记**：dorm（睡觉）+ -ant → 在睡觉的 → 休眠的
