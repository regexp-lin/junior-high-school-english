## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **augment** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：增大；增加；提高
- **英文释义**：to make something greater by adding to it; to increase
- **例句**：
  - She took a part-time job to augment her family's income.
  - 她做了一份兼职来增加家庭收入。
  - The company plans to augment its workforce by hiring fifty new employees.
  - 公司计划通过雇用五十名新员工来扩大其劳动力。
- **助记**：aug（增长，如 August 原意是"尊贵/增大的"）+ ment → 增加

### 2. **benevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：慈善的；仁慈的；善意的
- **英文释义**：well-meaning and kindly; showing goodwill
- **例句**：
  - The benevolent millionaire donated millions to build hospitals in rural areas.
  - 这位慈善的百万富翁捐了数百万来在农村地区建医院。
  - A benevolent smile spread across the old man's face as he greeted the children.
  - 当老人问候孩子们时，慈祥的笑容浮现在他脸上。
- **助记**：bene（好）+ vol（意愿）+ ent → 怀有善意的 → 慈善的

### 3. **curtail** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：缩减；削减；限制
- **英文释义**：to reduce in extent or quantity; to cut short
- **例句**：
  - The government decided to curtail public spending to reduce the national debt.
  - 政府决定缩减公共开支以减少国家债务。
  - Bad weather curtailed our outdoor activities for the weekend.
  - 恶劣天气限制了我们周末的户外活动。
- **助记**：cur（短的，如 curt）+ tail（尾巴）→ 剪短尾巴 → 缩减

### 4. **elicit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：引出；引起；探出
- **英文释义**：to draw out a response, answer, or fact from someone
- **例句**：
  - The comedian's jokes elicited roars of laughter from the audience.
  - 喜剧演员的笑话引来观众阵阵大笑。
  - The detective tried to elicit the truth from the reluctant witness.
  - 侦探试图从不愿开口的证人那里探出真相。
- **助记**：e（出）+ licit（引诱）→ 引出来 → 引出

### 5. **forfeit** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：丧失；被没收；罚金
- **英文释义**：to lose or be deprived of something as a penalty for wrongdoing
- **例句**：
  - If you break the terms of the contract, you will forfeit your deposit.
  - 如果你违反合同条款，你将失去押金。
  - The team had to forfeit the match because they didn't have enough players.
  - 球队因为没有足够的球员不得不放弃比赛。
- **助记**：for（超出）+ feit（做）→ 做过头了 → 被罚没

### 6. **grapple** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：搏斗；努力应对；扭打
- **英文释义**：to struggle or wrestle with something; to deal with a difficult problem
- **例句**：
  - The government is grappling with the challenge of rising unemployment.
  - 政府正在努力应对失业率上升的挑战。
  - The two wrestlers grappled fiercely on the mat for several minutes.
  - 两名摔跤手在垫子上激烈搏斗了好几分钟。
- **助记**：grapple 联想 grab（抓住）+ apple → 抢苹果要搏斗

### 7. **illuminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：照亮；阐明；启发
- **英文释义**：to light up; to help to clarify or explain
- **例句**：
  - Thousands of lanterns illuminated the ancient temple during the festival.
  - 节日期间，数千盏灯笼照亮了古老的寺庙。
  - The professor's lecture illuminated many aspects of the theory that students had found confusing.
  - 教授的讲座阐明了学生们觉得困惑的理论的许多方面。
- **助记**：il（加强）+ lumin（光）+ ate → 发出光 → 照亮

### 8. **mitigate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：缓和；减轻；使缓解
- **英文释义**：to make less severe, serious, or painful
- **例句**：
  - Planting trees along the river helps mitigate the effects of flooding.
  - 在河流沿岸种树有助于减轻洪水的影响。
  - The company took immediate steps to mitigate the damage caused by the data breach.
  - 公司立即采取措施减轻数据泄露造成的损害。
- **助记**：mit（温和的，如 mild）+ ig + ate → 使温和 → 缓和

### 9. **prolific** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多产的；丰富的；大量的
- **英文释义**：producing much fruit or many offspring; producing a large number of works
- **例句**：
  - Shakespeare was one of the most prolific writers in the history of English literature.
  - 莎士比亚是英国文学史上最多产的作家之一。
  - The region is known for its prolific apple orchards that yield tons of fruit each year.
  - 该地区以其丰产的苹果园闻名，每年产出数吨水果。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lif（生命/后代）+ ic → 不断产出后代的 → 多产的

### 10. **volatile** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：易变的；不稳定的；挥发性的
- **英文释义**：liable to change rapidly and unpredictably; easily evaporated
- **例句**：
  - The political situation in the region remains highly volatile.
  - 该地区的政治局势仍然非常不稳定。
  - Gasoline is a highly volatile substance that must be stored carefully.
  - 汽油是一种高度挥发性的物质，必须小心存放。
- **助记**：volat（飞）+ ile → 容易飞走的 → 挥发的/易变的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acquit** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：宣告无罪；无罪释放；表现
- **英文释义**：to free someone from a criminal charge by a verdict of not guilty
- **例句**：
  - The jury acquitted the defendant after finding insufficient evidence.
  - 陪审团因证据不足宣告被告无罪。
  - Despite the pressure, she acquitted herself admirably during the interview.
  - 尽管有压力，她在面试中表现得令人钦佩。
- **助记**：ac（加强）+ quit（释放/离开）→ 完全释放 → 宣告无罪

### 12. **conundrum** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：难题；谜语；令人困惑的问题
- **英文释义**：a confusing and difficult problem or question
- **例句**：
  - Climate change presents a conundrum for policymakers who must balance economic growth with environmental protection.
  - 气候变化给必须平衡经济增长与环境保护的政策制定者带来了难题。
  - The ancient conundrum of whether the chicken or the egg came first still amuses people today.
  - 先有鸡还是先有蛋这个古老的难题至今仍让人们争论不休。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ und（波浪）+ rum → 多重波浪交汇 → 令人困惑的难题

### 13. **exonerate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：免除责任；证明无罪；开脱
- **英文释义**：to absolve someone from blame for a fault or wrongdoing
- **例句**：
  - DNA evidence exonerated the man who had been wrongfully imprisoned for twenty years.
  - DNA证据为被错误关押了二十年的男子证明了无罪。
  - The investigation exonerated the officer of any misconduct during the incident.
  - 调查免除了该警官在事件中任何不当行为的责任。
- **助记**：ex（出去）+ oner（负担）+ ate → 卸下负担 → 免除责任

### 14. **obsequious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：谄媚的；奉承的；卑躬屈膝的
- **英文释义**：obedient or attentive to an excessive degree; servile
- **例句**：
  - The obsequious assistant agreed with everything the boss said, never offering his own opinion.
  - 那个谄媚的助理对老板说的每句话都表示同意，从不提出自己的意见。
  - His obsequious behavior toward wealthy clients made his colleagues uncomfortable.
  - 他对富有客户卑躬屈膝的行为让同事们感到不舒服。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ sequ（跟随）+ ious → 一味跟随别人 → 谄媚的

### 15. **repudiate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：否认；拒绝接受；批驳
- **英文释义**：to refuse to accept or be associated with; to deny the truth of
- **例句**：
  - The politician repudiated the allegations of corruption, calling them completely fabricated.
  - 这位政治家否认了腐败指控，称其完全是捏造的。
  - Several member states repudiated the treaty and refused to sign it.
  - 几个成员国拒绝接受该条约并拒绝签署。
- **助记**：re（反）+ pud（感到羞耻）+ iate → 感到羞耻而拒绝 → 否认
