## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **bolster** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：支持；增强；加固
- **英文释义**：to support or strengthen; to prop up
- **例句**：
  - The government introduced new measures to bolster the struggling economy.
  - 政府推出了新措施来增强疲软的经济。
- **助记**：bolster → 联想"枕垫（bolster 也指长枕头）"→ 用枕垫支撑 → 支持/增强

### 2. **covert** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：秘密的；隐蔽的；暗中的
- **英文释义**：not openly acknowledged or displayed; secret
- **例句**：
  - The intelligence agency conducted a covert operation to gather information on the suspect.
  - 情报机构进行了一项秘密行动以收集嫌疑人的信息。
- **助记**：co + vert（遮盖）→ 完全遮盖的 → 秘密的

### 3. **disdain** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 鄙视；不屑 v. 鄙视；不屑于
- **英文释义**：n. the feeling that someone or something is unworthy; v. to consider beneath oneself
- **例句**：
  - She looked at the cheap imitation with obvious disdain.
  - 她以明显的鄙视看着那个廉价仿制品。
- **助记**：dis（不）+ dain（值得）→ 不值得的 → 鄙视

### 4. **embellish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：装饰；润色；添枝加叶
- **英文释义**：to make something more attractive by the addition of decorative details; to make a story more interesting by adding extra details that are often untrue
- **例句**：
  - He tends to embellish his travel stories with exaggerated details.
  - 他倾向于用夸张的细节来润色他的旅行故事。
- **助记**：em（使）+ bell（美丽）+ ish → 使变美丽 → 装饰/润色

### 5. **gist** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：要点；主旨；大意
- **英文释义**：the substance or essence of a speech or text
- **例句**：
  - I didn't understand every word, but I got the gist of what she was saying.
  - 我不是每个词都懂，但我理解了她说的要点。
- **助记**：gist → 联想"just（只要）"→ 只要知道大意就行 → 要点

### 6. **impetus** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：动力；推动力；促进因素
- **英文释义**：the force or energy with which a body moves; something that makes a process happen more quickly
- **例句**：
  - The successful product launch gave new impetus to the company's expansion plans.
  - 成功的产品发布为公司的扩张计划提供了新的推动力。
- **助记**：im（向）+ pet（追求）+ us → 追求的力量 → 动力/推动力

### 7. **notoriety** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：恶名；声名狼藉；臭名昭著
- **英文释义**：the state of being famous or well known for some bad quality or deed
- **例句**：
  - The scandal brought unwanted notoriety to the once-respected institution.
  - 丑闻给这个曾经受人尊敬的机构带来了不必要的恶名。
- **助记**：notor（众所周知的）+ iety → 众所周知的坏事 → 恶名

### 8. **opulent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：富裕的；奢华的；豪华的
- **英文释义**：ostentatiously rich and luxurious or lavish
- **例句**：
  - The opulent mansion featured marble floors, crystal chandeliers, and gold-plated fixtures.
  - 这座奢华的豪宅拥有大理石地板、水晶吊灯和镀金装置。
- **助记**：opul（财富）+ ent → 充满财富的 → 富裕的/奢华的

### 9. **recede** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：后退；减弱；消退
- **英文释义**：to go or move back or further away from a previous position
- **例句**：
  - As the floodwaters receded, the full extent of the damage became visible.
  - 随着洪水消退，损失的全部程度变得可见。
- **助记**：re（回）+ cede（走）→ 走回去 → 后退/消退

### 10. **stark** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：鲜明的；严酷的；完全的
- **英文释义**：severe or bare in appearance; unpleasantly or sharply clear
- **例句**：
  - There is a stark contrast between the living conditions of the rich and the poor.
  - 富人和穷人的生活条件之间存在鲜明的对比。
- **助记**：stark → 联想"stare（盯着看）"→ 盯着看到的赤裸现实 → 鲜明的/严酷的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **apocryphal** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：杜撰的；不真实的；可疑的
- **英文释义**：of doubtful authenticity; widely believed but probably untrue
- **例句**：
  - The story about Newton discovering gravity when an apple fell on his head may be apocryphal.
  - 牛顿因苹果掉在头上而发现万有引力的故事可能是杜撰的。
- **助记**：apo（远离）+ cryph（隐藏）+ al → 隐藏的/远离真相的 → 杜撰的

### 12. **efficacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有效的；灵验的
- **英文释义**：successful in producing a desired or intended result
- **例句**：
  - Clinical trials proved the new vaccine to be highly efficacious against the virus.
  - 临床试验证明新疫苗对该病毒非常有效。
- **助记**：ef（出）+ fic（做）+ acious → 能做出效果的 → 有效的

### 13. **mercurial** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：反复无常的；善变的；活泼的
- **英文释义**：subject to sudden or unpredictable changes of mood or mind
- **例句**：
  - The director's mercurial temperament made it difficult for the crew to know what to expect each day.
  - 导演反复无常的脾气让剧组很难知道每天该期待什么。
- **助记**：Mercury（水星/水银）+ ial → 像水银一样变化不定 → 反复无常的

### 14. **pontificate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：自以为是地说教；教条地发表意见
- **英文释义**：to express one's opinions in a way considered annoyingly pompous and dogmatic
- **例句**：
  - He loves to pontificate about politics, but rarely listens to anyone else's views.
  - 他喜欢自以为是地谈论政治，但很少听别人的观点。
- **助记**：pontif（教皇）+ icate → 像教皇一样发表神圣言论 → 自以为是地说教

### 15. **sacrosanct** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：神圣不可侵犯的；不可更改的
- **英文释义**：regarded as too important or valuable to be interfered with
- **例句**：
  - Freedom of speech is considered sacrosanct in democratic societies.
  - 言论自由在民主社会中被视为神圣不可侵犯的。
- **助记**：sacro（神圣的）+ sanct（神圣的）→ 双重神圣 → 神圣不可侵犯的
