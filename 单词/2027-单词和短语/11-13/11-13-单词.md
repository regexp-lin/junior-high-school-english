## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **unprecedented** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：史无前例的；空前的
- **英文释义**：never done or known before
- **例句**：
  - The country faced an unprecedented economic crisis during the pandemic.
  - 疫情期间，国家面临了史无前例的经济危机。
  - The team achieved unprecedented success by winning all their matches.
  - 团队赢得了所有比赛，取得了空前的成功。
- **助记**：un（不）+ precedent（先例）+ ed → 没有先例的 → 史无前例的

### 2. **tarnish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：玷污；损害（名誉）；使失去光泽
- **英文释义**：to make less valuable or respected; to lose luster
- **例句**：
  - The scandal tarnished the politician's reputation beyond repair.
  - 丑闻不可挽回地损害了这位政客的声誉。
  - Silver jewelry can tarnish if not stored properly.
  - 银饰如果保存不当会失去光泽。
- **助记**：tarn（暗）+ ish → 变暗 → 失去光泽/玷污

### 3. **propensity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：倾向；嗜好；癖性
- **英文释义**：an inclination or natural tendency to behave in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - He has a propensity for taking risks in his business ventures.
  - 他在商业冒险中有冒风险的倾向。
  - Her propensity to procrastinate often leads to missed deadlines.
  - 她拖延的癖性经常导致错过截止日期。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ pens（悬挂/倾向）+ ity → 向前倾向 → 倾向

### 4. **instigate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：煽动；发起；教唆
- **英文释义**：to bring about or initiate; to urge on
- **例句**：
  - The protest was instigated by a small group of activists.
  - 抗议是由一小群活动分子发起的。
  - He was accused of instigating violence among the crowd.
  - 他被指控在人群中煽动暴力。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ stig（刺）+ ate → 刺入激发 → 煽动

### 5. **exorbitant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过高的；过分的；离谱的
- **英文释义**：unreasonably high in price or amount
- **例句**：
  - The hotel charged exorbitant prices during the holiday season.
  - 假日期间酒店收取了离谱的价格。
  - The exorbitant cost of healthcare is a major concern for many families.
  - 医疗费用过高是许多家庭的主要担忧。
- **助记**：ex（超出）+ orbit（轨道）+ ant → 超出正常轨道 → 过分的

### 6. **antiquated** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：过时的；陈旧的；古老的
- **英文释义**：old-fashioned or outdated
- **例句**：
  - The factory still uses antiquated machinery that should have been replaced years ago.
  - 工厂仍在使用早该淘汰的陈旧机器。
  - His antiquated views on education are no longer relevant in the modern world.
  - 他对教育的过时观点在现代世界已不再适用。
- **助记**：antiqu（古老）+ ated → 古老的 → 过时的

### 7. **encompass** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：包含；涵盖；围绕
- **英文释义**：to surround and have or hold within; to include comprehensively
- **例句**：
  - The training program encompasses both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
  - 培训项目涵盖了理论知识和实践技能。
  - The Great Wall encompasses a vast stretch of mountainous terrain.
  - 长城围绕着大片山区地形。
- **助记**：en（使）+ compass（范围）→ 使在范围内 → 包含

### 8. **delineate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：描绘；勾画；阐明
- **英文释义**：to describe or portray something precisely; to outline
- **例句**：
  - The contract clearly delineates the responsibilities of each party.
  - 合同清楚地阐明了各方的责任。
  - The artist delineated the landscape with fine, detailed strokes.
  - 艺术家用精细的笔触描绘了风景。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ line（线）+ ate → 用线条完全画出 → 描绘

### 9. **placate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：安抚；平息；使满意
- **英文释义**：to make someone less angry or hostile
- **例句**：
  - The manager tried to placate the angry customers with a full refund.
  - 经理试图用全额退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。
  - Offering a public apology did little to placate the protesters.
  - 公开道歉几乎无法平息抗议者的怒火。
- **助记**：plac（平静）+ ate → 使平静 → 安抚

### 10. **orthodox** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：正统的；传统的；保守的
- **英文释义**：conforming to what is generally accepted as right or true
- **例句**：
  - The scientist challenged orthodox theories about the origin of the universe.
  - 科学家挑战了关于宇宙起源的正统理论。
  - She took an orthodox approach to teaching, following the established curriculum.
  - 她采用了传统的教学方法，遵循既定的课程。
- **助记**：ortho（正确）+ dox（观点）→ 正确的观点 → 正统的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **weltanschauung** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：世界观；人生观
- **英文释义**：a particular philosophy or view of life; a worldview
- **例句**：
  - His weltanschauung was shaped by his experiences growing up in a multicultural society.
  - 他的世界观是在多元文化社会中成长的经历塑造的。
  - Understanding a culture's weltanschauung helps in appreciating its art and literature.
  - 理解一个文化的世界观有助于欣赏其艺术和文学。
- **助记**：德语 Welt（世界）+ Anschauung（观点）→ 世界的观点 → 世界观

### 12. **blandishment** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：甜言蜜语；奉承；哄骗
- **英文释义**：a flattering or pleasing statement or action used to persuade someone gently
- **例句**：
  - Despite his blandishments, she refused to change her mind.
  - 尽管他百般奉承，她仍拒绝改变主意。
  - The salesperson used blandishments to convince the customer to buy the expensive product.
  - 销售员用甜言蜜语说服顾客购买昂贵的产品。
- **助记**：bland（温和的）+ ishment → 温和的讨好 → 甜言蜜语

### 13. **imprimatur** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：批准；许可；官方认可
- **英文释义**：an official license or approval, especially to print or publish
- **例句**：
  - The project received the imprimatur of the board of directors.
  - 该项目获得了董事会的批准。
  - Without the government's imprimatur, the book could not be distributed.
  - 没有政府的许可，这本书不能发行。
- **助记**：imprim（印刷）+ atur → 允许印刷 → 批准

### 14. **mellifluous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：悦耳的；甜美流畅的
- **英文释义**：sweet-sounding; pleasant to hear
- **例句**：
  - The singer's mellifluous voice captivated everyone in the concert hall.
  - 歌手悦耳的嗓音迷住了音乐厅里的每一个人。
  - He spoke in a mellifluous tone that put the audience at ease.
  - 他用甜美流畅的语调说话，让听众感到放松。
- **助记**：melli（蜂蜜）+ flu（流动）+ ous → 像蜂蜜般流淌 → 悦耳的

### 15. **hubris** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：傲慢；狂妄自大
- **英文释义**：excessive pride or self-confidence, often leading to downfall
- **例句**：
  - His hubris led him to believe he could never fail, which was his ultimate downfall.
  - 他的狂妄自大使他相信自己永远不会失败，这最终导致了他的覆灭。
  - In Greek tragedies, hubris is often the fatal flaw of the hero.
  - 在希腊悲剧中，傲慢往往是英雄致命的弱点。
- **助记**：hubris 希腊语"过度骄傲" → 傲慢
