## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **amplify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：放大；增强；详述
- **英文释义**：to increase the volume, extent, or importance of something; to make larger or more powerful
- **例句**：
  - The speaker used a microphone to amplify her voice for the large audience.
  - 演讲者使用麦克风为大批观众放大了她的声音。
- **助记**：ampli（大）+ fy（使）→ 使变大 → 放大

### 2. **bleak** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：荒凉的；暗淡的；没有希望的
- **英文释义**：lacking vegetation and exposed to the elements; not hopeful or encouraging
- **例句**：
  - The economic outlook for the country remains bleak.
  - 这个国家的经济前景依然暗淡。
- **助记**：bleak → 联想"不理客"→ 荒凉冷清无人理 → 荒凉的

### 3. **comply** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：遵从；顺从；依从
- **英文释义**：to act in accordance with a wish or command
- **例句**：
  - All restaurants must comply with food safety regulations.
  - 所有餐厅必须遵守食品安全法规。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ ply（满足）→ 完全满足要求 → 遵从

### 4. **deter** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻止；威慑；使打消念头
- **英文释义**：to discourage someone from doing something by instilling doubt or fear
- **例句**：
  - The high price did not deter customers from buying the latest smartphone.
  - 高昂的价格并未阻止顾客购买最新款智能手机。
- **助记**：de（离开）+ ter（恐吓）→ 通过恐吓使离开 → 威慑/阻止

### 5. **endorse** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：支持；赞同；为……代言
- **英文释义**：to declare one's public approval or support of; to recommend a product in an advertisement
- **例句**：
  - Several celebrities endorsed the charity campaign on social media.
  - 几位名人在社交媒体上为这次慈善活动代言。
- **助记**：en（使）+ dorse（背）→ 在背后支持 → 支持/赞同

### 6. **feeble** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚弱的；无力的；薄弱的
- **英文释义**：lacking physical strength; weak; not effective or convincing
- **例句**：
  - The patient's voice was so feeble that the nurse could barely hear her.
  - 病人的声音非常微弱，护士几乎听不到。
- **助记**：feeble → 联想"非饱"→ 吃不饱所以虚弱 → 虚弱的

### 7. **gullible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：轻信的；容易受骗的
- **英文释义**：easily persuaded to believe something; credulous
- **例句**：
  - The gullible tourist was tricked into paying ten times the normal price.
  - 那位轻信的游客被骗支付了十倍于正常价格的钱。
- **助记**：gull（海鸥/欺骗）+ ible → 容易被欺骗的 → 轻信的

### 8. **invoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：援引；调用；祈求
- **英文释义**：to cite or appeal to as an authority; to call on for help; to put into effect
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer invoked the Fifth Amendment to protect his client.
  - 律师援引第五修正案来保护他的客户。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ voke（叫/声音）→ 叫出声来求助 → 援引/祈求

### 9. **nominal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：名义上的；微不足道的；象征性的
- **英文释义**：existing in name only; very small; far below the real value
- **例句**：
  - He was the nominal head of the company, but his wife made all the real decisions.
  - 他是公司名义上的负责人，但他妻子做了所有真正的决定。
- **助记**：nomin（名字）+ al → 只有名字的 → 名义上的

### 10. **relegate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：降级；贬低；归入
- **英文释义**：to consign or dismiss to an inferior rank or position
- **例句**：
  - After a poor season, the football team was relegated to the lower division.
  - 在一个糟糕的赛季后，这支足球队被降级到了低级别联赛。
- **助记**：re（向后）+ leg（派遣）+ ate → 向后派遣 → 降级

### 高一词汇

### 11. **conundrum** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：难题；谜题；困惑
- **英文释义**：a confusing and difficult problem or question
- **例句**：
  - Climate change presents a conundrum for policymakers worldwide.
  - 气候变化给全球政策制定者提出了一个难题。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ undrum → 一起纠缠在一起的 → 难题

### 12. **ebullient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的
- **英文释义**：cheerful and full of energy; enthusiastic
- **例句**：
  - The ebullient crowd cheered wildly as the team scored the winning goal.
  - 当球队打进制胜球时，热情洋溢的人群疯狂欢呼。
- **助记**：e（出）+ bull（沸腾）+ ient → 沸腾溢出 → 热情洋溢的

### 13. **magnanimous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；慷慨的；高尚的
- **英文释义**：very generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or less powerful person
- **例句**：
  - The magnanimous winner offered words of encouragement to his defeated opponent.
  - 宽宏大量的获胜者向他落败的对手送上了鼓励的话语。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 心胸大的 → 宽宏大量的

### 14. **recalcitrant** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：顽抗的；倔强的；不服从的
- **英文释义**：having an obstinately uncooperative attitude toward authority or discipline
- **例句**：
  - The recalcitrant student refused to follow any of the teacher's instructions.
  - 那个倔强的学生拒绝遵从老师的任何指示。
- **助记**：re（回）+ calc（踢）+ itrant → 往回踢 → 顽抗的

### 15. **trepidation** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：恐惧；惊恐；忧虑
- **英文释义**：a feeling of fear or agitation about something that may happen
- **例句**：
  - She approached the job interview with great trepidation.
  - 她怀着极大的忐忑参加了工作面试。
- **助记**：trepid（颤抖）+ ation → 颤抖的状态 → 恐惧/忧虑
