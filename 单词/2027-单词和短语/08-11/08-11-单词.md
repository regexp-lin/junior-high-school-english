## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **apprehend** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：逮捕；理解；忧虑
- **英文释义**：to arrest someone; to understand or perceive; to anticipate with anxiety
- **例句**：
  - The police apprehended the suspect shortly after the robbery took place.
  - 抢劫发生后不久，警方就逮捕了嫌疑人。
  - It took me a while to apprehend the full meaning of his cryptic message.
  - 我花了一些时间才理解他那条神秘信息的全部含义。
- **助记**：ap（朝向）+ prehend（抓住）→ 抓住 → 逮捕/理解

### 2. **cascade** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：瀑布；级联；大量涌出
- **英文释义**：a small waterfall; a large number of things occurring in rapid succession
- **例句**：
  - A cascade of water tumbled down the rocky hillside after the heavy rain.
  - 大雨过后，水流如瀑布般顺着岩石山坡倾泻而下。
  - The announcement triggered a cascade of events that changed the industry forever.
  - 这一公告引发了一连串改变了整个行业的事件。
- **助记**：cascade 联想 case（箱子/情况）→ 像箱子里的东西倾泻而出 → 瀑布/级联

### 3. **defiant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：反抗的；违抗的；挑衅的
- **英文释义**：showing resistance or bold disobedience
- **例句**：
  - The defiant teenager refused to apologize for his rude behavior.
  - 这个反抗的少年拒绝为他的粗鲁行为道歉。
  - With a defiant gesture, she tore up the letter and threw it away.
  - 她以一个反抗的姿态撕碎了那封信并扔掉了。
- **助记**：de（反对）+ fi（信任）+ ant → 不信任权威 → 反抗的

### 4. **eloquent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：雄辩的；口才好的；有说服力的
- **英文释义**：fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing
- **例句**：
  - Martin Luther King Jr. was an eloquent speaker who inspired millions of people.
  - 马丁·路德·金是一位雄辩的演讲者，激励了数百万人。
  - Her eloquent argument convinced the jury to find the defendant not guilty.
  - 她有说服力的论证说服了陪审团判定被告无罪。
- **助记**：e（出）+ loqu（说）+ ent → 善于说出来的 → 雄辩的

### 5. **forbid** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：禁止；不许；妨碍
- **英文释义**：to refuse to allow something; to order someone not to do something
- **例句**：
  - The school strictly forbids the use of mobile phones during classes.
  - 学校严格禁止在上课期间使用手机。
  - His doctor forbade him from eating salty and oily food.
  - 他的医生禁止他吃咸的和油腻的食物。
- **助记**：for（加强）+ bid（命令）→ 强力命令不许 → 禁止

### 6. **hectic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：忙碌的；紧张的；繁忙的
- **英文释义**：full of incessant or frantic activity
- **例句**：
  - The weeks leading up to the wedding were incredibly hectic for the entire family.
  - 婚礼前几周对全家人来说都是非常忙碌的。
  - After a hectic day at work, she just wanted to relax at home.
  - 在工作中忙碌了一天后，她只想在家里放松。
- **助记**：hectic 联想 heck（该死）→ 忙得要命 → 忙碌的

### 7. **kindle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：点燃；激发；激起
- **英文释义**：to light or set on fire; to arouse or inspire
- **例句**：
  - The campfire was difficult to kindle in the damp weather.
  - 在潮湿的天气中很难点燃篝火。
  - The teacher's passion for science kindled a love of learning in her students.
  - 老师对科学的热情激发了学生们对学习的热爱。
- **助记**：kindle 联想 candle（蜡烛）→ 点燃蜡烛 → 点燃/激发

### 8. **malleable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：可塑的；易影响的；可延展的
- **英文释义**：able to be hammered or pressed into shape; easily influenced
- **例句**：
  - Gold is one of the most malleable metals, which makes it ideal for making jewelry.
  - 黄金是最具延展性的金属之一，非常适合制作珠宝。
  - Young children are highly malleable and easily influenced by their surroundings.
  - 幼儿的可塑性很强，很容易受到周围环境的影响。
- **助记**：mall（锤打）+ eable → 能被锤打塑形的 → 可塑的

### 9. **permeate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：渗透；弥漫；遍布
- **英文释义**：to spread throughout; to pass through every part of
- **例句**：
  - The smell of fresh coffee permeated the entire house every morning.
  - 每天早晨新鲜咖啡的香气弥漫整个房子。
  - A sense of optimism permeated the team after their unexpected victory.
  - 意外的胜利后，一种乐观情绪弥漫在整个团队中。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ meate（通过）→ 贯穿通过 → 渗透/弥漫

### 10. **stagnant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：停滞的；不流动的；不景气的
- **英文释义**：having no current or flow; not growing or developing
- **例句**：
  - The pond became stagnant and covered with algae due to lack of fresh water.
  - 由于缺乏新鲜水源，池塘变得停滞不动，长满了水藻。
  - The economy has remained stagnant for several years with little growth.
  - 经济已经停滞了好几年，几乎没有增长。
- **助记**：stagn（池塘/停止）+ ant → 像死水池塘一样 → 停滞的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **circumvent** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：规避；绕过；智胜
- **英文释义**：to find a way around an obstacle or to overcome a problem cleverly
- **例句**：
  - The company found a legal way to circumvent the new regulations.
  - 公司找到了一种合法的方式来规避新法规。
  - Students tried to circumvent the school's internet filter to access blocked websites.
  - 学生们试图绕过学校的网络过滤器来访问被封锁的网站。
- **助记**：circum（周围）+ vent（走）→ 绕着走 → 规避/绕过

### 12. **ebullient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的
- **英文释义**：cheerful and full of energy; enthusiastic
- **例句**：
  - The ebullient crowd cheered wildly as the band took the stage.
  - 当乐队登上舞台时，热情洋溢的人群疯狂欢呼。
  - Her ebullient personality makes her popular at every social gathering.
  - 她热情洋溢的个性使她在每一次社交聚会上都很受欢迎。
- **助记**：e（出）+ bull（沸腾）+ ient → 像沸腾的水一样冒出来 → 热情洋溢的

### 13. **inexorable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可阻挡的；无情的；不可改变的
- **英文释义**：impossible to stop or prevent; relentless
- **例句**：
  - The inexorable march of technology continues to transform every aspect of our lives.
  - 技术不可阻挡的进步继续改变着我们生活的方方面面。
  - Despite their efforts, the inexorable rise in sea levels threatens coastal cities.
  - 尽管他们努力了，海平面无情的上升仍然威胁着沿海城市。
- **助记**：in（不）+ exor（恳求）+ able → 恳求也不会改变的 → 不可阻挡的

### 14. **proclivity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：倾向；癖好；偏好
- **英文释义**：a tendency to choose or do something regularly; an inclination
- **例句**：
  - He has a proclivity for spending money on gadgets he never uses.
  - 他有一种花钱买从不使用的小玩意的癖好。
  - The child showed an early proclivity for music, picking out tunes on the piano at age three.
  - 这个孩子很早就表现出了对音乐的偏好，三岁时就能在钢琴上弹出曲调。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ cliv（倾斜）+ ity → 向某个方向倾斜 → 倾向/癖好

### 15. **trepidation** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：恐惧；惊恐；不安
- **英文释义**：a feeling of fear or anxiety about something that may happen
- **例句**：
  - She approached her first day at the new job with a mixture of excitement and trepidation.
  - 她怀着既兴奋又不安的心情迎接新工作的第一天。
  - The students waited with great trepidation for their exam results to be announced.
  - 学生们怀着极大的不安等待考试成绩公布。
- **助记**：trepid（颤抖/恐惧）+ ation → 颤抖的状态 → 恐惧/不安
