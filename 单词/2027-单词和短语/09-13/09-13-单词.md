## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **alleviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和；缓解
- **英文释义**：to make pain, problems, or suffering less severe
- **例句**：
  - Medicine can alleviate symptoms but may not cure the disease.
  - 药物能缓解症状，但未必能治愈疾病。
  - The government promised measures to alleviate poverty in rural areas.
  - 政府承诺采取措施缓解农村贫困。
- **助记**：al（向）+ levi（轻）+ ate → 使变轻 → 减轻

### 2. **besiege** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：围攻；围困；不断打扰
- **英文释义**：to surround a place to capture it; to crowd around with requests
- **例句**：
  - Reporters besieged the celebrity as soon as she left the building.
  - 这位名人一走出大楼就被记者团团围住。
  - The castle was besieged for months before it finally surrendered.
  - 这座城堡被围困数月后才最终投降。
- **助记**：be + siege（围攻）→ 被围攻 → 围困

### 3. **culminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：（使）达到顶点；（使）告终
- **英文释义**：to reach a climax or final stage; to end in a particular result
- **例句**：
  - Months of tension culminated in a public protest.
  - 数月的紧张局势最终以公开抗议告终。
  - Her career culminated in an award at the national level.
  - 她的职业生涯以获得国家奖项而达到顶峰。
- **助记**：culmin（顶）+ ate → 到达顶点 → 达到高潮

### 4. **delineate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：描绘；勾画；清楚说明
- **英文释义**：to describe or mark the exact outline of; to define precisely
- **例句**：
  - The report delineates the steps needed to reduce carbon emissions.
  - 报告清楚说明了减少碳排放所需的步骤。
  - Artists delineate figures with fine lines before adding color.
  - 画家在上色前用细线勾画人物轮廓。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ line（线）+ ate → 用线画清楚 → 描绘

### 5. **exacerbate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使恶化；使加剧；激怒
- **英文释义**：to make a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling worse
- **例句**：
  - Loud arguments only exacerbated the conflict between neighbors.
  - 大声争吵只会加剧邻里冲突。
  - Cutting water supply during a drought could exacerbate public anger.
  - 干旱期间断水可能激化公众愤怒。
- **助记**：ex（向外）+ acerb（酸/尖刻）→ 使更尖锐 → 加剧

### 6. **flounder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：挣扎；支吾；艰难行事
- **英文释义**：to struggle clumsily; to have difficulty doing something
- **例句**：
  - Without a map, we floundered in the unfamiliar streets.
  - 没有地图，我们在陌生的街道上艰难摸索。
  - He floundered for an answer when the teacher asked a tough question.
  - 老师问难题时，他支吾着答不上来。
- **助记**：联想 flounder（比目鱼）在水中扭动 → 挣扎

### 7. **glean** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：收集（信息）；拾（穗）；逐渐获知
- **英文释义**：to collect information or material bit by bit; to gather leftover grain
- **例句**：
  - Researchers gleaned data from thousands of survey responses.
  - 研究人员从数千份问卷回复中收集数据。
  - From his diary we can glean something of his inner thoughts.
  - 从他的日记中我们可略知他的内心想法。
- **助记**：古义拾麦穗 → 一点点收集 → 搜集

### 8. **harbor** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：怀有（感情）；窝藏；庇护
- **英文释义**：to keep feelings or thoughts in your mind; to give shelter to
- **例句**：
  - She harbored doubts about the plan but said nothing at the meeting.
  - 她对计划心存疑虑，但在会上什么也没说。
  - The village was accused of harboring escaped prisoners.
  - 该村被指控窝藏在逃犯人。
- **助记**：港口 shelter → 引申为庇护、怀有

### 9. **incite** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：煽动；激起；唆使
- **英文释义**：to encourage someone to do something violent or unpleasant
- **例句**：
  - He was arrested for inciting violence on social media.
  - 他因在社交媒体上煽动暴力而被捕。
  - The speech seemed designed to incite the crowd against the police.
  - 这篇演讲似乎意在煽动人群对抗警察。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ cite（召唤）→ 激起行动 → 煽动

### 10. **juncture** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：关键时刻；接合点；关头
- **英文释义**：a particular point in time or stage in a process; a joint
- **例句**：
  - At this critical juncture, we must act together.
  - 在这一关键时刻，我们必须共同行动。
  - The peace talks reached a delicate juncture after the ceasefire broke.
  - 停火破裂后，和谈进入了微妙关头。
- **助记**：junct（连接）+ ure → 连接点 → 关头

### 高一词汇

### 11. **juxtaposition** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：并置；并列对照
- **英文释义**：the fact of placing two things close together for contrast or comparison
- **例句**：
  - The juxtaposition of wealth and poverty in the photo was striking.
  - 照片中贫富并置令人震撼。
  - The essay explores the juxtaposition of tradition and modernity.
  - 文章探讨了传统与现代的并置。
- **助记**：juxtapose 的名词形式 → 并置

### 12. **kindred** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：同类的；志趣相投的；亲属（集合）
- **英文释义**：similar in kind; related; one's family and relations
- **例句**：
  - She found kindred spirits in the reading club.
  - 她在读书俱乐部找到了志趣相投的人。
  - Linguistics and psychology are kindred disciplines in many ways.
  - 语言学心理学在许多方面是相近学科。
- **助记**：kin（亲属）+ red（状态）→ 同类/亲属

### 13. **luminary** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：杰出人物；泰斗；发光体（书面）
- **英文释义**：a famous and respected person in a particular field
- **例句**：
  - The conference featured luminaries from science and the arts.
  - 会议邀请了科学与艺术界的泰斗。
  - She became a luminary in pediatric medicine after decades of research.
  - 经过数十年研究，她成为儿科医学的杰出人物。
- **助记**：lumin（光）+ ary → 发光的人 → 名人

### 14. **myriad** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：无数；大量；无数的
- **英文释义**：a very large number; countless
- **例句**：
  - The internet offers myriad ways to learn a new language.
  - 互联网提供了无数种学习新语言的方式。
  - Myriad stars were visible on the clear mountain night.
  - 晴朗的山夜可见无数星星。
- **助记**：希腊 myrias（一万）→ 极多 → 无数

### 15. **nexus** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：纽带；核心关系；联结网
- **英文释义**：a connection or series of connections linking people or things; a central point
- **例句**：
  - The report examines the nexus between poverty and poor health.
  - 报告考察了贫困与健康不良之间的关联。
  - The city serves as a nexus of trade routes in the region.
  - 这座城市是该地区贸易路线的枢纽。
- **助记**：nex（绑）+ us → 绑在一起 → 纽带
