## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **admonish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：训诫；告诫；警告
- **英文释义**：to warn or reprimand someone firmly; to advise or urge earnestly
- **例句**：
  - The teacher admonished the students for talking during the exam.
  - 老师训诫了考试期间讲话的学生。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ mon（警告）+ ish → 向某人发出警告 → 训诫/告诫

### 2. **consolidate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：巩固；合并；加强
- **英文释义**：to make something physically stronger or more solid; to combine several things into a single more effective whole
- **例句**：
  - The company consolidated its three regional offices into one central headquarters.
  - 公司将三个地区办事处合并为一个中央总部。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ solid（坚固）+ ate → 使一起变坚固 → 巩固/合并

### 3. **deplete** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：耗尽；大量减少；消耗
- **英文释义**：to use up the supply or resources of; to diminish in number or quantity
- **例句**：
  - Overfishing has severely depleted the ocean's fish stocks in many regions.
  - 过度捕捞已严重耗尽了许多地区海洋的鱼类资源。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ plete（充满）→ 完全不充满 → 耗尽

### 4. **esteem** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 尊敬；敬重 v. 尊重；认为
- **英文释义**：n. respect and admiration; v. to respect and admire
- **例句**：
  - The professor is held in high esteem by both students and colleagues.
  - 这位教授深受学生和同事的尊敬。
- **助记**：esteem → 联想"estimate（估价）"→ 给予高评价 → 尊敬

### 5. **flounder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：挣扎；不知所措；进展困难
- **英文释义**：to struggle or stagger helplessly or clumsily; to be in serious difficulty
- **例句**：
  - The new employee floundered during his first presentation, forgetting most of his notes.
  - 新员工在第一次演讲中手足无措，忘记了大部分笔记。
- **助记**：flounder → 联想"鱼在地上扑腾（flounder 也是比目鱼）"→ 挣扎

### 6. **jeopardize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：危及；损害；使处于危险
- **英文释义**：to put someone or something into a situation in which there is a danger of loss, harm, or failure
- **例句**：
  - Skipping classes could jeopardize your chances of passing the final exam.
  - 逃课可能会危及你通过期末考试的机会。
- **助记**：jeopard（危险）+ ize → 使处于危险 → 危及

### 7. **ornate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：华丽的；装饰精美的；过于雕琢的
- **英文释义**：made in an intricate shape or decorated with complex patterns; elaborately adorned
- **例句**：
  - The palace featured ornate ceilings covered with gold leaf and painted murals.
  - 这座宫殿有装饰精美的天花板，覆盖着金箔和彩绘壁画。
- **助记**：orn（装饰）+ ate → 经过装饰的 → 华丽的

### 8. **refurbish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：翻新；整修；修复
- **英文释义**：to renovate and redecorate a building or room
- **例句**：
  - The old library was refurbished and reopened with modern facilities.
  - 旧图书馆经过翻新后重新开放，配备了现代化设施。
- **助记**：re（重新）+ furbish（擦亮/翻新）→ 重新擦亮 → 翻新

### 9. **subside** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：减弱；平息；沉降；消退
- **英文释义**：to become less intense, violent, or severe; to sink to a lower level
- **例句**：
  - The floodwaters began to subside after three days of continuous sunshine.
  - 经过三天持续的阳光后，洪水开始消退。
- **助记**：sub（下）+ side（坐）→ 坐下去 → 减弱/沉降

### 10. **whimsical** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：异想天开的；古怪的；随心所欲的
- **英文释义**：playfully quaint or fanciful, especially in an appealing and amusing way
- **例句**：
  - The children's book was filled with whimsical illustrations of talking animals.
  - 这本儿童书充满了会说话的动物的异想天开的插图。
- **助记**：whim（突发奇想）+ sical → 充满奇想的 → 异想天开的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **bucolic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：田园的；乡村的；牧歌式的
- **英文释义**：relating to the pleasant aspects of the countryside and country life
- **例句**：
  - The artist captured the bucolic charm of the rolling hills and grazing sheep.
  - 画家捕捉了起伏山丘和放牧绵羊的田园魅力。
- **助记**：bu（牛）+ colic → 与牛有关的 → 田园的/乡村的

### 12. **draconian** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：严厉的；残酷的；苛刻的
- **英文释义**：excessively harsh and severe
- **例句**：
  - The draconian measures imposed during the crisis were criticized by human rights groups.
  - 危机期间实施的严厉措施遭到了人权组织的批评。
- **助记**：Draco（德拉古，古希腊严酷的立法者）+ nian → 像德拉古一样严厉 → 严厉的

### 13. **iconoclast** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：偶像破坏者；打破传统的人；反对权威的人
- **英文释义**：a person who attacks cherished beliefs or institutions
- **例句**：
  - The young filmmaker was regarded as an iconoclast who challenged the conventions of Hollywood.
  - 这位年轻导演被视为挑战好莱坞传统的叛逆者。
- **助记**：icono（偶像）+ clast（打碎）→ 打碎偶像的人 → 偶像破坏者

### 14. **querulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱抱怨的；牢骚满腹的；易怒的
- **英文释义**：complaining in a petulant or whining manner
- **例句**：
  - The querulous old man complained about everything from the weather to the food.
  - 这个爱抱怨的老人对天气到食物的一切都有怨言。
- **助记**：quer（抱怨）+ ulous → 不停抱怨的 → 爱抱怨的

### 15. **unscrupulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：不择手段的；无道德的；肆无忌惮的
- **英文释义**：having or showing no moral principles; not honest or fair
- **例句**：
  - The unscrupulous businessman cheated his partners out of millions of dollars.
  - 这个不择手段的商人骗走了合伙人数百万美元。
- **助记**：un（不）+ scrupul（良心/顾虑）+ ous → 没有顾虑的 → 不择手段的
