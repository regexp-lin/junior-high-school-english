## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **astute** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：精明的；敏锐的；机敏的
- **英文释义**：having the ability to accurately assess situations and turn them to one's advantage
- **例句**：
  - An astute investor knows when to buy and when to sell.
  - 精明的投资者知道何时买入何时卖出。
  - The astute detective noticed a detail everyone else had missed.
  - 机敏的侦探注意到了其他人都错过的细节。
- **助记**：astute 联想 acute（敏锐的）→ 敏锐精明

### 2. **blatant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：公然的；明目张胆的；露骨的
- **英文释义**：done openly and unashamedly; very obvious
- **例句**：
  - The politician's blatant lies shocked even his own supporters.
  - 政客公然的谎言甚至令其支持者震惊。
  - Copying someone's homework is a blatant form of cheating.
  - 抄别人作业是一种明目张胆的作弊。
- **助记**：bla（吹/叫）+ tant → 大声叫嚷 → 公然的

### 3. **cumbersome** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：笨重的；累赘的；繁琐的
- **英文释义**：large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry or use; unwieldy
- **例句**：
  - The old desktop computer was cumbersome compared to a modern laptop.
  - 旧台式电脑与现代笔记本相比十分笨重。
  - The cumbersome registration process discouraged many applicants.
  - 繁琐的注册流程让许多申请者望而却步。
- **助记**：cumber（阻碍）+ some → 造成阻碍的 → 笨重/繁琐

### 4. **debilitate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使衰弱；使虚弱；削弱
- **英文释义**：to make someone or something weak or infirm
- **例句**：
  - The long illness debilitated him so much that he could barely walk.
  - 长期疾病使他虚弱到几乎走不了路。
  - Constant stress can debilitate both body and mind.
  - 持续的压力会削弱身心。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ bil（力量）+ itate → 力量下降 → 使衰弱

### 5. **entice** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：诱惑；引诱；吸引
- **英文释义**：to attract or tempt by offering pleasure or advantage
- **例句**：
  - The shop enticed customers with a huge "50% OFF" sign.
  - 商店用巨大的"半价"标牌吸引顾客。
  - Free samples are used to entice people to try new products.
  - 免费样品被用来引诱人们尝试新产品。
- **助记**：en（使）+ tice（引诱）→ 引诱 → 诱惑

### 6. **fervent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：热切的；强烈的；热烈的
- **英文释义**：having or displaying a passionate intensity
- **例句**：
  - The fervent fans cheered wildly as their team scored the winning goal.
  - 热切的球迷在球队进制胜球时疯狂欢呼。
  - She made a fervent plea for peace at the United Nations assembly.
  - 她在联合国大会上发出热切的和平呼吁。
- **助记**：ferv（热/沸腾）+ ent → 沸腾的 → 热切的

### 7. **grimace** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：做鬼脸；痛苦的表情；扭曲面容
- **英文释义**：an ugly, twisted expression on a person's face; to make such an expression
- **例句**：
  - He grimaced when the dentist touched the sore tooth.
  - 牙医碰到痛牙时他龇牙咧嘴。
  - The child made a grimace after tasting the bitter medicine.
  - 孩子尝到苦药后做了一个鬼脸。
- **助记**：grim（严酷）+ ace（脸）→ 严酷的脸 → 痛苦的表情

### 8. **hoard** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：囤积；贮藏；大量储存物
- **英文释义**：to amass and hide or store away; a stock or store of valuables
- **例句**：
  - Squirrels hoard nuts in autumn to prepare for winter.
  - 松鼠在秋天囤积坚果为冬天做准备。
  - During the crisis, people hoarded toilet paper and canned food.
  - 危机期间人们囤积了卫生纸和罐头食品。
- **助记**：hoard 联想 board（板）→ 用板子藏东西 → 囤积

### 9. **incite** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：煽动；激起；鼓动
- **英文释义**：to encourage or stir up violent or unlawful behavior
- **例句**：
  - The speech was accused of inciting hatred among different groups.
  - 这篇演讲被指控煽动不同群体之间的仇恨。
  - Social media posts can incite panic if they spread false information.
  - 如果传播虚假信息，社交媒体帖子会激起恐慌。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ cite（激发）→ 激起 → 煽动

### 10. **lucid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：清晰的；明了的；头脑清醒的
- **英文释义**：expressed clearly; easy to understand; able to think clearly
- **例句**：
  - The professor gave a lucid explanation of quantum physics.
  - 教授对量子物理给出了清晰的解释。
  - Despite his age, the writer remained lucid and sharp-minded.
  - 尽管年纪大了，这位作家仍然头脑清醒、思维敏捷。
- **助记**：luc（光）+ id → 像光一样明亮 → 清晰的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **morose** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：阴郁的；忧郁的；脾气坏的
- **英文释义**：sullen and ill-tempered; gloomy
- **例句**：
  - He became morose after losing the championship match.
  - 输掉冠军赛后他变得阴郁寡言。
  - The morose atmosphere in the office lifted when the good news arrived.
  - 好消息传来时办公室里阴郁的气氛一扫而空。
- **助记**：mor（死/忧）+ ose → 如死般阴沉 → 忧郁的

### 12. **nefarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：邪恶的；极坏的；不法的
- **英文释义**：wicked or criminal; extremely evil
- **例句**：
  - The detective uncovered a nefarious plot to embezzle company funds.
  - 侦探揭露了一个挪用公司资金的邪恶阴谋。
  - History is full of nefarious figures who abused their power.
  - 历史上充满了滥用权力的邪恶人物。
- **助记**：ne（不）+ far（法/正确）+ ious → 不正义的 → 邪恶的

### 13. **palpable** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：可感知的；明显的；可触摸的
- **英文释义**：able to be touched or felt; so intense as to seem almost tangible
- **例句**：
  - The tension in the room was palpable before the results were announced.
  - 结果公布前房间里的紧张气氛几乎触手可及。
  - There was a palpable sense of relief when the crisis ended.
  - 危机结束时有一种明显的如释重负感。
- **助记**：palp（触摸）+ able → 可以触摸的 → 可感知的

### 14. **querulous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：爱抱怨的；牢骚满腹的；吹毛求疵的
- **英文释义**：complaining in a petulant or whining manner
- **例句**：
  - The querulous passenger complained about every detail of the flight.
  - 爱抱怨的乘客对航班的每个细节都有意见。
  - A querulous tone can make others reluctant to help.
  - 牢骚满腹的语气会让别人不愿意帮忙。
- **助记**：quer（抱怨）+ ulous → 爱抱怨的

### 15. **reticent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：沉默寡言的；不愿透露的；含蓄的
- **英文释义**：not revealing one's thoughts or feelings readily; reserved
- **例句**：
  - She was reticent about her personal life during interviews.
  - 她在采访中对个人生活守口如瓶。
  - Reticent witnesses made the investigation more difficult.
  - 沉默寡言的证人使调查更加困难。
- **助记**：re（回）+ tic（沉默）+ ent → 保持沉默 → 寡言的
