## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **beckon** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：招手示意；召唤；吸引
- **英文释义**：to make a gesture encouraging someone to come nearer; to seem appealing
- **例句**：
  - She beckoned to the waiter to bring the menu.
  - 她向服务员招手示意拿菜单。
- **助记**：beck（点头）+ on → 点头让人过来 → 招手示意

### 2. **crumble** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：崩塌；碎裂；瓦解
- **英文释义**：to break or fall apart into small fragments; to gradually disintegrate
- **例句**：
  - The ancient wall crumbled under the weight of centuries.
  - 那面古老的墙在数百年的重压下崩塌了。
- **助记**：crumb（碎屑）+ le → 变成碎屑 → 碎裂/崩塌

### 3. **diagonal** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 对角线的；斜的 n. 对角线
- **英文释义**：adj. joining two non-adjacent corners; n. a straight line joining two opposite corners
- **例句**：
  - She drew a diagonal line across the square.
  - 她在正方形上画了一条对角线。
- **助记**：dia（穿过）+ gon（角）+ al → 穿过角的 → 对角线的

### 4. **endeavor** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 努力；尝试 v. 努力；尽力
- **英文释义**：n. an attempt to achieve a goal; v. to try hard to do or achieve something
- **例句**：
  - He endeavored to finish the project before the deadline.
  - 他努力在截止日期之前完成项目。
- **助记**：en（使）+ deavor（责任）→ 使自己承担责任 → 努力

### 5. **fathom** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 理解；领悟 n. 英寻（水深单位）
- **英文释义**：v. to understand after much thought; n. a unit of length equal to six feet
- **例句**：
  - I can't fathom why she would make such a decision.
  - 我无法理解她为什么会做出这样的决定。
- **助记**：fathom → 探测水深 → 深入理解 → 领悟

### 6. **gloomy** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：阴暗的；忧郁的；悲观的
- **英文释义**：dark or poorly lit; feeling distressed or pessimistic
- **例句**：
  - The gloomy weather made everyone feel depressed.
  - 阴沉的天气让每个人都感到沮丧。
- **助记**：gloom（忧郁）+ y → 充满忧郁的 → 阴暗的/忧郁的

### 7. **infantry** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：步兵；步兵部队
- **英文释义**：soldiers marching or fighting on foot; foot soldiers collectively
- **例句**：
  - The infantry advanced towards the enemy's position at dawn.
  - 步兵在黎明时分向敌方阵地推进。
- **助记**：infant（年轻人）+ ry → 年轻人组成的军队 → 步兵

### 8. **memoir** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：回忆录；自传；传记
- **英文释义**：a historical account written from personal knowledge or experience
- **例句**：
  - The retired general published a memoir about his years in the army.
  - 退休将军出版了一本关于他军旅岁月的回忆录。
- **助记**：mem（记忆）+ oir → 关于记忆的 → 回忆录

### 9. **obstruct** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；阻塞；妨碍
- **英文释义**：to block a road, passage, or view; to prevent the progress of
- **例句**：
  - A fallen tree obstructed the road after the storm.
  - 暴风雨后一棵倒下的树阻塞了道路。
- **助记**：ob（对着）+ struct（建造）→ 对着建造障碍物 → 阻碍

### 10. **prolific** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多产的；丰富的；大量的
- **英文释义**：producing much fruit or foliage or many offspring; producing many works
- **例句**：
  - Shakespeare was one of the most prolific writers in history.
  - 莎士比亚是历史上最多产的作家之一。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lific（产生）→ 不断向前产生 → 多产的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **admonish** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：告诫；警告；训诫
- **英文释义**：to warn or reprimand someone firmly; to advise or urge earnestly
- **例句**：
  - The teacher admonished the students for being disruptive in class.
  - 老师训诫了在课堂上捣乱的学生。
- **助记**：ad（向）+ mon（警告）+ ish → 向某人发出警告 → 告诫

### 12. **lucid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：清晰的；明了的；头脑清楚的
- **英文释义**：expressed clearly; easy to understand; showing ability to think clearly
- **例句**：
  - Despite her illness, she remained lucid and could communicate clearly.
  - 尽管生了病，她仍然头脑清楚，能够清晰地交流。
- **助记**：luc（光）+ id → 像光一样明亮的 → 清晰的

### 13. **pernicious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有害的；恶性的；致命的
- **英文释义**：having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way
- **例句**：
  - The pernicious influence of fake news is undermining public trust.
  - 假新闻的有害影响正在削弱公众信任。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ nic（杀害）+ ious → 完全有杀伤力的 → 有害的

### 14. **sardonic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：嘲讽的；讥笑的；冷嘲热讽的
- **英文释义**：grimly mocking or cynical
- **例句**：
  - He gave a sardonic smile when he heard the excuse.
  - 听到那个借口时，他露出了一个讥讽的微笑。
- **助记**：sardonic 源自 Sardinia 岛上一种令人苦笑的植物 → 嘲讽的

### 15. **trepidation** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：恐惧；惊恐；不安
- **英文释义**：a feeling of fear or agitation about something that may happen
- **例句**：
  - She opened the exam results with great trepidation.
  - 她怀着极大的不安打开了考试成绩。
- **助记**：trepid（颤抖）+ ation → 颤抖的状态 → 恐惧/不安
