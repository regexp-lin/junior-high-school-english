## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **squander** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：挥霍；浪费；糟蹋
- **英文释义**：to waste something, especially money or opportunities, in a reckless way
- **例句**：
  - He squandered his inheritance on luxury cars and expensive vacations.
  - 他把遗产挥霍在豪车和昂贵的假期上。
  - Don't squander this opportunity — it may never come again.
  - 不要浪费这个机会——它可能再也不会来了。
- **助记**：squander 发音像"死光的" → 钱都死光了 → 挥霍

### 2. **tenacious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坚韧的；顽强的；执着的
- **英文释义**：tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent
- **例句**：
  - Her tenacious spirit helped her overcome every obstacle in her career.
  - 她坚韧的精神帮助她克服了职业生涯中的每一个障碍。
  - The tenacious detective refused to give up on the cold case.
  - 那位执着的侦探拒绝放弃这桩悬案。
- **助记**：ten（持有）+ acious → 紧紧持有 → 坚韧的

### 3. **oscillate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：摇摆；振荡；犹豫不决
- **英文释义**：to move or swing back and forth; to waver between different opinions
- **例句**：
  - The pendulum oscillates at a steady rhythm throughout the day.
  - 钟摆整天以稳定的节奏摆动。
  - She oscillated between accepting the job offer and staying at her current company.
  - 她在接受工作邀请和留在现公司之间犹豫不决。
- **助记**：oscill（摇摆）+ ate → 摇来摆去 → 振荡

### 4. **repudiate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：否认；拒绝；驳斥
- **英文释义**：to refuse to accept or be associated with; to deny the truth or validity of
- **例句**：
  - The politician repudiated the allegations made against him during the press conference.
  - 政治家在新闻发布会上否认了对他的指控。
  - The company repudiated the contract, claiming it was signed under duress.
  - 该公司拒绝履行合同，声称合同是在胁迫下签署的。
- **助记**：re（反）+ pud（感到羞耻）+ iate → 感到反感 → 否认/拒绝

### 5. **magnanimous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的；慷慨的
- **英文释义**：very generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or less powerful person
- **例句**：
  - The magnanimous winner congratulated his opponent and praised his efforts.
  - 宽宏大量的获胜者祝贺了对手并赞扬了他的努力。
  - It was magnanimous of her to forgive those who had wronged her.
  - 她原谅了那些曾经伤害她的人，真是宽宏大量。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 心灵宽大 → 宽宏大量的

### 6. **reticent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：沉默寡言的；不愿透露的
- **英文释义**：not revealing one's thoughts or feelings readily
- **例句**：
  - He was reticent about his personal life and rarely shared details with colleagues.
  - 他对个人生活很少透露，很少与同事分享细节。
  - The reticent witness provided very little useful information during the interview.
  - 沉默寡言的证人在采访中提供了很少有用的信息。
- **助记**：ret（网）+ icent → 像被网罩住不说话 → 沉默的

### 7. **coalesce** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：合并；联合；融合
- **英文释义**：to come together and form one mass or whole
- **例句**：
  - Several small streams coalesce to form a large river downstream.
  - 几条小溪在下游汇合形成一条大河。
  - The various protest groups coalesced into a powerful political movement.
  - 各个抗议团体联合成了一个强大的政治运动。
- **助记**：co（一起）+ alesce（生长）→ 一起生长 → 合并

### 8. **disparage** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：贬低；轻视；批评
- **英文释义**：to regard or represent as being of little worth
- **例句**：
  - It is unprofessional to disparage your competitors in front of customers.
  - 在客户面前贬低竞争对手是不专业的。
  - She felt hurt when her classmates disparaged her artwork.
  - 当同学贬低她的画作时，她感到很受伤。
- **助记**：dis（否定）+ par（平等）+ age → 不平等对待 → 贬低

### 9. **immutable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可改变的；永恒不变的
- **英文释义**：unchanging over time or unable to be changed
- **例句**：
  - The laws of physics are considered immutable truths of the universe.
  - 物理定律被认为是宇宙中不可改变的真理。
  - Despite the turmoil around him, his faith remained immutable.
  - 尽管周围一片混乱，他的信念依然不变。
- **助记**：im（不）+ mut（改变）+ able → 不能改变的 → 不可变的

### 10. **superfluous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多余的；过剩的；不必要的
- **英文释义**：unnecessary, especially through being more than enough
- **例句**：
  - The editor removed all superfluous words to make the article more concise.
  - 编辑删除了所有多余的字词，使文章更简洁。
  - With three coats already, buying another one seemed superfluous.
  - 已经有三件大衣了，再买一件似乎多余。
- **助记**：super（超过）+ flu（流）+ ous → 流出来太多 → 多余的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **anathema** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：令人厌恶的事物；诅咒
- **英文释义**：something or someone that is intensely disliked; a formal curse by a religious authority
- **例句**：
  - The idea of raising taxes was anathema to the conservative voters.
  - 增税的想法对保守派选民来说是令人深恶痛绝的。
  - Violence of any kind is anathema to the principles of this organization.
  - 任何形式的暴力都与该组织的原则格格不入。
- **助记**：ana（向上）+ thema（放置）→ 放在祭坛上诅咒 → 诅咒/可憎之物

### 12. **sycophant** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：谄媚者；奉承者；马屁精
- **英文释义**：a person who acts in an excessively flattering way to gain advantage
- **例句**：
  - The king surrounded himself with sycophants who told him only what he wanted to hear.
  - 国王身边围满了只告诉他想听的话的谄媚者。
  - A true friend gives honest advice, unlike a sycophant who only flatters.
  - 真正的朋友会给出诚实的建议，不像马屁精只会奉承。
- **助记**：syco（无花果）+ phant（展示）→ 古希腊告发非法运输无花果的人 → 谄媚者

### 13. **ebullient** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的
- **英文释义**：cheerful and full of energy
- **例句**：
  - The ebullient crowd cheered wildly as the team scored the winning goal.
  - 热情洋溢的人群在球队攻入制胜一球时疯狂欢呼。
  - Her ebullient personality made her the life of every party she attended.
  - 她热情洋溢的性格使她成为每次聚会的灵魂人物。
- **助记**：e（出）+ bull（沸腾）+ ient → 沸腾溢出 → 热情洋溢的

### 14. **lugubrious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：悲哀的；阴郁的；凄惨的
- **英文释义**：looking or sounding sad and dismal
- **例句**：
  - The lugubrious music at the funeral brought everyone to tears.
  - 葬礼上悲哀的音乐让每个人都流下了眼泪。
  - His lugubrious expression suggested that the news was not good.
  - 他阴郁的表情暗示这消息不太好。
- **助记**：lugubr（悲伤）+ ious → 悲伤的 → 阴郁的

### 15. **quixotic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：堂吉诃德式的；不切实际的；异想天开的
- **英文释义**：exceedingly idealistic; unrealistic and impractical
- **例句**：
  - His quixotic dream of reforming the entire education system single-handedly was admirable but impractical.
  - 他堂吉诃德式的单枪匹马改革整个教育体系的梦想令人钦佩但不切实际。
  - The plan to build a utopian city in the desert was dismissed as quixotic.
  - 在沙漠中建造乌托邦城市的计划被认为是异想天开的。
- **助记**：来自文学人物 Don Quixote（堂吉诃德）→ 像堂吉诃德一样不切实际
