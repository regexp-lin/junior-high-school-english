## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **embellish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：装饰；美化；润色
- **英文释义**：to make something more attractive by adding details; to make a story more interesting by exaggerating
- **例句**：
  - The author embellished the story with vivid descriptions of the countryside.
  - 作者用对乡村的生动描写润色了故事。
  - He embellished his resume with exaggerated achievements to impress the interviewer.
  - 他用夸大的成就美化简历以给面试官留下深刻印象。
- **助记**：em（使）+ bell（美丽）+ ish → 使变美丽 → 装饰

### 2. **dauntless** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无畏的；勇敢的；不屈不挠的
- **英文释义**：showing fearlessness and determination
- **例句**：
  - The dauntless explorer ventured into the uncharted jungle despite the dangers.
  - 无畏的探险家不顾危险，冒险进入未知的丛林。
  - Her dauntless spirit inspired the entire team to keep fighting.
  - 她无畏的精神鼓舞了整个团队继续奋斗。
- **助记**：daunt（使害怕）+ less（没有）→ 没有恐惧的 → 无畏的

### 3. **instigate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：煽动；发起；唆使
- **英文释义**：to bring about or initiate an action or event; to urge someone to do something wrong
- **例句**：
  - The rebel leader instigated the uprising against the government.
  - 叛乱领袖煽动了反对政府的起义。
  - The investigation was instigated by complaints from local residents.
  - 调查是由当地居民的投诉发起的。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ stig（刺）+ ate → 用刺激推动 → 煽动

### 4. **plausible** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：似乎合理的；貌似可信的
- **英文释义**：seeming reasonable or probable; appearing worthy of belief
- **例句**：
  - His explanation sounded plausible, but the evidence suggested otherwise.
  - 他的解释听起来似乎合理，但证据表明并非如此。
  - The detective considered several plausible theories about the crime.
  - 侦探考虑了几种关于犯罪的貌似可信的理论。
- **助记**：plaus（鼓掌）+ ible → 值得鼓掌认可的 → 似乎合理的

### 5. **truncate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：截断；缩短；删节
- **英文释义**：to shorten something by cutting off the top or end
- **例句**：
  - The editor truncated the article to fit within the newspaper's word limit.
  - 编辑截短了文章以符合报纸的字数限制。
  - The software truncates file names that exceed 255 characters.
  - 该软件会截断超过255个字符的文件名。
- **助记**：trunc（树干/截）+ ate → 像砍树干一样截断 → 截断

### 6. **frugal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：节俭的；朴素的；省吃俭用的
- **英文释义**：sparing or economical with regard to money or food
- **例句**：
  - Her frugal habits allowed her to save enough money for a down payment on a house.
  - 她节俭的习惯使她攒够了房子的首付款。
  - The family lived a frugal life, never spending more than necessary.
  - 这家人过着节俭的生活，从不多花一分钱。
- **助记**：frug（果实）+ al → 靠果实生活 → 节俭的

### 7. **tantalize** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：逗引；使干着急；吊胃口
- **英文释义**：to torment or tease someone with the sight or promise of something unobtainable
- **例句**：
  - The aroma of freshly baked cookies tantalized the children waiting outside the kitchen.
  - 刚烤好的饼干的香气吊着在厨房外等候的孩子们的胃口。
  - The preview tantalized audiences with glimpses of the upcoming blockbuster.
  - 预告片用即将上映大片的片段吊足了观众的胃口。
- **助记**：来自希腊神话 Tantalus（坦塔洛斯），被罚站在水中却永远喝不到水 → 吊胃口

### 8. **congenial** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：意气相投的；令人愉快的；适宜的
- **英文释义**：pleasant because of a personality or qualities similar to one's own; agreeable
- **例句**：
  - She found her new colleagues congenial and quickly settled into the team.
  - 她觉得新同事们很投缘，很快就融入了团队。
  - The café's congenial atmosphere made it a popular spot for studying.
  - 咖啡馆宜人的氛围使它成为学习的热门场所。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ genial（友好的）→ 友好相处 → 意气相投的

### 9. **supplant** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：取代；替换；排挤
- **英文释义**：to supersede and replace
- **例句**：
  - Digital media has largely supplanted traditional print newspapers.
  - 数字媒体已在很大程度上取代了传统的纸质报纸。
  - The new CEO supplanted the previous leader who had been in charge for over a decade.
  - 新任首席执行官取代了掌权十多年的前任领导。
- **助记**：sup（在下面）+ plant（放置）→ 从下面取而代之 → 取代

### 10. **despondent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：沮丧的；失望的；意志消沉的
- **英文释义**：in low spirits from loss of hope or courage
- **例句**：
  - After failing the exam for the third time, he became deeply despondent.
  - 第三次考试不及格后，他变得非常沮丧。
  - The team grew despondent after losing five games in a row.
  - 连输五场比赛后，团队变得意志消沉。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ spond（承诺/希望）+ ent → 希望向下走 → 沮丧的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **tergiversation** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：变节；出尔反尔；含糊其辞
- **英文释义**：the act of changing one's loyalties or opinions; evasion of straightforward action
- **例句**：
  - The politician's tergiversation on key issues cost him the trust of his supporters.
  - 这位政治家在关键问题上的出尔反尔使他失去了支持者的信任。
  - His constant tergiversation made it impossible to know where he truly stood.
  - 他不断的变节使人无法知道他真正的立场。
- **助记**：tergi（背）+ vers（转）+ ation → 转过背去 → 变节

### 12. **diaphanous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：透明的；轻薄的；精致的
- **英文释义**：light, delicate, and translucent
- **例句**：
  - She wore a diaphanous gown that floated elegantly as she walked.
  - 她穿着一件轻薄的长裙，走路时优雅地飘动。
  - The diaphanous curtains allowed soft sunlight to filter into the room.
  - 透明的窗帘让柔和的阳光透进房间。
- **助记**：dia（穿过）+ phan（显现）+ ous → 光线穿过可以看见 → 透明的

### 13. **effulgent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：灿烂的；光辉四射的
- **英文释义**：shining brightly; radiant
- **例句**：
  - The effulgent sunrise painted the sky in shades of gold and crimson.
  - 灿烂的日出将天空染成了金色和绯红色。
  - Her effulgent smile lit up the entire room at the reception.
  - 她光辉灿烂的微笑照亮了招待会的整个房间。
- **助记**：ef（出）+ fulg（闪耀）+ ent → 向外闪耀 → 灿烂的

### 14. **pusillanimous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：胆怯的；懦弱的；怯懦的
- **英文释义**：showing a lack of courage or determination; timid
- **例句**：
  - The pusillanimous leader avoided making any bold decisions during the crisis.
  - 这位怯懦的领导在危机期间避免做出任何大胆的决定。
  - It would be pusillanimous to abandon the project at this stage.
  - 在这个阶段放弃项目是懦弱的表现。
- **助记**：pusill（非常小）+ anim（精神）+ ous → 精神渺小 → 胆怯的

### 15. **sempiternal** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：永恒的；无始无终的
- **英文释义**：eternal and unchanging; everlasting
- **例句**：
  - The poet wrote about the sempiternal beauty of nature that transcends time.
  - 诗人写到了超越时间的大自然永恒的美。
  - The philosopher argued that truth is sempiternal, unaffected by the passage of ages.
  - 哲学家论证了真理是永恒的，不受时代更替的影响。
- **助记**：sempi（永远）+ ternal（持续）→ 永远持续的 → 永恒的
