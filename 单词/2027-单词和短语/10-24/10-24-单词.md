## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **augment** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：增加；增大；补充
- **英文释义**：to make something greater by adding to it; to increase
- **例句**：
  - He took a part-time job to augment his income.
  - 他做了一份兼职来增加收入。
  - Technology can augment human abilities in many fields.
  - 技术可以在许多领域增强人类的能力。
- **助记**：aug（增加）+ ment → 增加

### 2. **barren** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：贫瘠的；荒芜的；不结果实的
- **英文释义**：too poor to produce much vegetation; not producing results
- **例句**：
  - The barren desert stretched for hundreds of miles without a tree.
  - 贫瘠的沙漠延伸数百英里没有一棵树。
  - Years of barren negotiations produced no agreement.
  - 数年毫无成果的谈判没有达成任何协议。
- **助记**：barren 联想 bare（光秃秃的）→ 光秃荒芜 → 贫瘠

### 3. **compel** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：强迫；迫使；使不得不
- **英文释义**：to force or oblige someone to do something
- **例句**：
  - The evidence compelled the jury to find the defendant guilty.
  - 证据迫使陪审团判定被告有罪。
  - Financial difficulties compelled them to sell the family house.
  - 经济困难迫使他们出售家族房屋。
- **助记**：com（一起）+ pel（推）→ 合力推 → 强迫

### 4. **detain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：拘留；扣押；耽搁
- **英文释义**：to keep someone in official custody; to keep someone from proceeding
- **例句**：
  - The police detained the suspect for questioning overnight.
  - 警方拘留嫌疑人进行通宵审讯。
  - Sorry I'm late; I was detained by an urgent phone call.
  - 抱歉我迟到了，被一个紧急电话耽搁了。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ tain（保持）→ 扣留住 → 拘留

### 5. **expedite** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：加快；促进；迅速办理
- **英文释义**：to make an action or process happen sooner or be accomplished more quickly
- **例句**：
  - The new software will expedite the processing of applications.
  - 新软件将加快申请的处理速度。
  - They hired extra staff to expedite delivery before the holidays.
  - 他们雇了额外的员工以加快假期前的交货速度。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ped（脚）+ ite → 快步走出 → 加快

### 6. **falter** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：犹豫；蹒跚；衰退
- **英文释义**：to start to lose strength or momentum; to speak hesitantly
- **例句**：
  - Her voice faltered when she spoke about her late grandfather.
  - 谈起已故的祖父时她的声音有些颤抖。
  - The economy faltered after the sudden rise in oil prices.
  - 油价骤升后经济出现了衰退。
- **助记**：falter 联想 fault（错误）→ 犯错踉跄 → 犹豫/蹒跚

### 7. **gratitude** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：感激；感恩；感谢之情
- **英文释义**：the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation
- **例句**：
  - She expressed her gratitude to the volunteers who helped rebuild her home.
  - 她向帮助重建家园的志愿者表达了感激之情。
  - A simple "thank you" can show deep gratitude.
  - 一声简单的"谢谢"可以表达深深的感恩。
- **助记**：grat（感激）+ itude → 感激

### 8. **heed** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：注意；留心；听从
- **英文释义**：to pay attention to; to take notice of
- **例句**：
  - You should heed the warning signs before it's too late.
  - 你应该在为时已晚之前留心警告信号。
  - The captain paid no heed to the weather forecast and sailed into the storm.
  - 船长没有理会天气预报，驶入了暴风雨中。
- **助记**：heed → need（需要）→ 需要注意 → 留心

### 9. **irate** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：愤怒的；暴怒的
- **英文释义**：feeling or characterized by great anger; furious
- **例句**：
  - The irate customer demanded to speak with the manager immediately.
  - 愤怒的顾客要求立即与经理通话。
  - Irate residents protested against the noise from the factory.
  - 愤怒的居民抗议工厂的噪音。
- **助记**：ir（怒）+ ate → 愤怒的

### 10. **meddle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：干涉；管闲事；多管闲事
- **英文释义**：to interfere in or busy oneself unduly with something not one's concern
- **例句**：
  - Parents should guide, but not meddle in every aspect of their teenager's life.
  - 父母应该引导而非干涉青少年生活的方方面面。
  - The neighbor was always meddling in other people's business.
  - 邻居总爱管别人的闲事。
- **助记**：meddle 联想 middle（中间）→ 插到中间去 → 干涉

### 高一词汇

### 11. **misanthrope** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：厌世者；愤世嫉俗的人
- **英文释义**：a person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society
- **例句**：
  - The old misanthrope lived alone and refused to speak with his neighbors.
  - 这个老厌世者独居并拒绝与邻居说话。
  - Despite being called a misanthrope, he secretly donated to children's charities.
  - 尽管被称为愤世嫉俗者，他暗中向儿童慈善机构捐款。
- **助记**：mis（恨）+ anthrop（人）+ e → 恨人 → 厌世者

### 12. **penchant** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：嗜好；爱好；强烈倾向
- **英文释义**：a strong or habitual liking for something
- **例句**：
  - The director has a penchant for dramatic plot twists.
  - 这位导演酷爱戏剧性的情节反转。
  - Her penchant for vintage clothing makes her style unique.
  - 她对复古服装的嗜好使她的风格独特。
- **助记**：pench（倾斜/悬挂）+ ant → 心向某处倾斜 → 嗜好

### 13. **quagmire** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：泥沼；困境；进退两难的局面
- **英文释义**：a soft boggy area of land; an awkward or difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - The country found itself in a political quagmire with no easy solution.
  - 这个国家陷入了没有简易解决方案的政治困境。
  - Heavy rain turned the football field into a quagmire.
  - 大雨把足球场变成了一片泥沼。
- **助记**：quag（泥泞）+ mire（沼泽）→ 泥沼 → 困境

### 14. **spurious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：虚假的；伪造的；似是而非的
- **英文释义**：not being what it purports to be; false or fake
- **例句**：
  - The court dismissed the spurious claims made by the plaintiff.
  - 法庭驳回了原告虚假的索赔。
  - Be careful of spurious websites that imitate legitimate brands.
  - 小心模仿正规品牌的虚假网站。
- **助记**：spur（假的）+ ious → 假的 → 虚假的

### 15. **vindicate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：证明……正确；为……辩护；平反
- **英文释义**：to clear someone of blame or suspicion; to show to be right
- **例句**：
  - New DNA evidence vindicated the man who had been wrongly imprisoned.
  - 新的DNA证据为被错误关押的人平了反。
  - Time will vindicate our decision to invest in renewable energy.
  - 时间会证明我们投资可再生能源的决定是正确的。
- **助记**：vindic（报仇/要求公正）+ ate → 要求公正 → 平反
