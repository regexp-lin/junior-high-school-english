## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **hovel** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：简陋小屋；茅舍；肮脏拥挤的住处
- **英文释义**：a small, squalid, or simply constructed dwelling; a miserable hut
- **例句**：
  - The refugees lived in hovels made of cardboard and plastic.
  - 难民住在纸板与塑料搭成的简陋小屋里。
  - Dickens described the hovel where the poor family huddled for warmth.
  - 狄更斯描写了穷人家庭挤在一起取暖的破屋。
- **助记**：hovel 联想 hollow（空空的破屋）→ 陋室

### 2. **impugn** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：质疑；指责；攻击（名誉、论点）
- **英文释义**：to dispute the truth, validity, or honesty of a statement or motive
- **例句**：
  - No one could impugn her integrity after decades of service.
  - 数十年服务后无人质疑她的正直。
  - The lawyer impugned the witness's credibility during cross-examination.
  - 律师在交叉询问中质疑证人的可信度。
- **助记**：im（向内）+ pugn（打）→ 向内攻击论点 → 质疑

### 3. **jettison** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：抛弃；丢弃；（航空）投弃载荷
- **英文释义**：to throw or drop something from an aircraft or ship; to abandon an idea or plan
- **例句**：
  - The plane jettisoned fuel before making an emergency landing.
  - 飞机紧急降落前放油。
  - The company jettisoned the failing division to cut losses.
  - 公司为减损抛弃了亏损部门。
- **助记**：jet（抛出）→ 从船上扔下 → 抛弃

### 4. **knave** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：无赖；骗子；（纸牌）杰克
- **英文释义**：a dishonest or unscrupulous man; (cards) the jack
- **例句**：
  - The story portrayed him as a charming knave who cheated at cards.
  - 故事把他塑造成打牌作弊的无赖。
  - In old English tales, a knave often served a noble with cunning.
  - 在古英语故事中，无赖常狡猾地服侍贵族。
- **助记**：knave 与 knife 古义相关？→ 狡猾之徒 → 无赖

### 5. **lurid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：骇人听闻的；刺眼的；苍白的（面色）
- **英文释义**：presented in vividly shocking or sensational terms; very bright in an unpleasant way
- **例句**：
  - Tabloids published lurid details about the celebrity's divorce.
  - 小报刊登了名人离婚骇人听闻的细节。
  - The lurid neon sign flashed all night outside the motel.
  - 汽车旅馆外刺眼的霓虹灯闪了一夜。
- **助记**：lur（苍白、可怕）→ 令人毛骨悚然的 → 骇人的

### 6. **myopic** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：近视的；目光短浅的
- **英文释义**：nearsighted; lacking imagination, foresight, or intellectual insight
- **例句**：
  - Myopic patients need glasses to read the board.
  - 近视患者需要眼镜才能看清黑板。
  - A myopic focus on quarterly profits hurt long-term research.
  - 只盯着季度利润的目光短浅损害了长期研究。
- **助记**：my（关闭）+ op（眼）→ 眼睛闭合看不清远 → 近视的/短视的

### 7. **nexus** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：联结；核心关系网；枢纽
- **英文释义**：a connection or series of connections linking two or more things; a central focal point
- **例句**：
  - The city is a nexus of trade routes between continents.
  - 这座城市是洲际贸易路线的枢纽。
  - Researchers mapped the nexus between poverty and health outcomes.
  - 研究人员绘制了贫困与健康结果之间的关联网络。
- **助记**：nex（绑）+ us → 绑在一起 → 联结

### 8. **oracle** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：神谕；预言；圣贤；（计算机）甲骨文公司名
- **英文释义**：a priest or priestess through whom a deity speaks; an authoritative or wise utterance
- **例句**：
  - Ancient Greeks traveled to consult the oracle at Delphi.
  - 古希腊人前往德尔斐请示神谕。
  - She was regarded as an oracle on climate policy by journalists.
  - 记者们把她视为气候政策方面的权威。
- **助记**：ora（说）+ cle → 神说的话 → 神谕

### 9. **pique** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：怨恨；愠怒；激起（好奇心、兴趣）
- **英文释义**：a feeling of irritation or resentment; to arouse interest or curiosity
- **例句**：
  - He left in a fit of pique when his idea was rejected.
  - 想法被拒后他愤然离开。
  - The mysterious title piqued readers' curiosity.
  - 神秘的书名激起了读者的好奇心。
- **助记**：pique 法语“刺”→ 心里被刺 → 愠怒；刺一下好奇心 → 激起

### 10. **quail** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：胆怯；畏缩；（鸟）鹌鹑
- **英文释义**：to feel or show fear; to shrink from danger; (n.) a small game bird
- **例句**：
  - Even brave soldiers quail before a firing squad in stories.
  - 故事中即使勇敢士兵在行刑队前也会胆怯。
  - She quailed at the thought of speaking in front of thousands.
  - 想到要在数千人面前演讲她就发怵。
- **助记**：quail（鸟）胆小易惊 → 畏缩

### 高一词汇

### 11. **raillery** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：善意的嘲弄；打趣；揶揄
- **英文释义**：good-humored teasing or banter; playful ridicule
- **例句**：
  - The dinner was full of gentle raillery among old friends.
  - 晚宴上老朋友之间尽是善意的打趣。
  - She accepted the raillery about her new haircut with a smile.
  - 她笑着接受关于新发型的善意取笑。
- **助记**：rail（嘲笑）+ ery → 嘲弄行为 → 打趣

### 12. **saturnine** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：阴沉的；忧郁的；讥讽的（文学色彩）
- **英文释义**：slow and gloomy in manner or appearance; sullen and morose
- **例句**：
  - The saturnine detective rarely smiled during the investigation.
  - 这位阴沉的侦探调查期间很少笑。
  - A saturnine mood hung over the office after layoffs were announced.
  - 宣布裁员后办公室一片阴郁。
- **助记**：Saturn（土星/农神）→ 忧郁气质 → 阴沉的

### 13. **truncate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：截短；删节；切去顶部
- **英文释义**：to shorten something by cutting off the top or end; to abbreviate
- **例句**：
  - The article was truncated to fit the newspaper column.
  - 文章被删节以适合报纸专栏。
  - Heavy snow truncated the branches of the trees along the road.
  - 大雪压断了路边的树枝。
- **助记**：trunc（树干、残干）+ ate → 砍成残桩 → 截短

### 14. **umbrage** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：不快；愤怒；树荫（古语）
- **英文释义**：offense or annoyance; a feeling of being slighted; shade (archaic)
- **例句**：
  - She took umbrage at his casual remark about her work.
  - 她对他随口评价自己工作的话感到不快。
  - He gave no sign of umbrage though the joke was aimed at him.
  - 尽管笑话针对他，他没有表现出恼怒。
- **助记**：umbra（阴影）→ 心里投下阴影 → 不快

### 15. **vicissitude** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：变迁；浮沉；人生的盛衰起伏
- **英文释义**：a change of circumstances over time, especially adverse ones
- **例句**：
  - The novel follows a family through the vicissitudes of war.
  - 小说跟随一个家庭经历战争的盛衰变迁。
  - He remained calm through the vicissitudes of his career.
  - 他在职业生涯的起伏中保持冷静。
- **助记**：vic（变化）+ issitude → 变化无常 → 变迁
