## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **perfunctory** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：敷衍的；马虎的；走过场的
- **英文释义**：(of an action or gesture) carried out with a minimum of effort or reflection
- **例句**：
  - The waiter gave a perfunctory smile before hurrying to the next table.
  - 服务员敷衍地笑了一下就匆匆走向下一桌。
  - His perfunctory apology did nothing to repair the broken trust.
  - 他敷衍的道歉丝毫无助于修复破裂的信任。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ funct（执行）+ ory → 只是完全执行（没有用心）→ 敷衍的

### 2. **malevolent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：恶意的；恶毒的；心怀恶意的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a wish to do evil to others
- **例句**：
  - The malevolent villain plotted to destroy the entire kingdom.
  - 恶毒的反派密谋摧毁整个王国。
  - There was a malevolent gleam in his eyes that made everyone uncomfortable.
  - 他眼中有一种恶意的光芒，让每个人都感到不舒服。
- **助记**：mal（坏）+ evol（意愿）+ ent → 坏的意愿 → 恶意的

### 3. **admonish** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：告诫；警告；温和地责备
- **英文释义**：to warn or reprimand someone firmly but not harshly
- **例句**：
  - The teacher admonished the students for talking during the exam.
  - 老师告诫学生们考试期间不要说话。
  - She admonished her children to be careful when crossing the street.
  - 她告诫孩子们过马路时要小心。
- **助记**：ad（朝向）+ mon（警告）+ ish → 朝着某人警告 → 告诫

### 4. **bellicose** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：好战的；好斗的；挑衅的
- **英文释义**：demonstrating aggression and willingness to fight
- **例句**：
  - The bellicose rhetoric from both nations raised fears of an armed conflict.
  - 两国好战的言论引发了武装冲突的担忧。
  - His bellicose attitude at the negotiating table made compromise impossible.
  - 他在谈判桌上好斗的态度使妥协变得不可能。
- **助记**：belli（战争）+ cose → 倾向战争的 → 好战的

### 5. **perpetuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使永久化；使持续；使不朽
- **英文释义**：to make something continue indefinitely
- **例句**：
  - The documentary aimed to challenge stereotypes rather than perpetuate them.
  - 这部纪录片旨在挑战刻板印象而非使其永久化。
  - Unequal access to education perpetuates the cycle of poverty.
  - 不平等的教育机会使贫困循环延续。
- **助记**：per（始终）+ petu（追求）+ ate → 始终追求延续 → 使永久化

### 6. **nebulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：模糊的；朦胧的；含糊不清的
- **英文释义**：in the form of a cloud or haze; unclear, vague
- **例句**：
  - The company's future plans remained nebulous, with no concrete timeline or goals.
  - 公司的未来计划仍然模糊不清，没有具体的时间表或目标。
  - She had only nebulous memories of her early childhood.
  - 她对早期童年只有模糊的记忆。
- **助记**：nebul（云雾）+ ous → 像云雾一样 → 模糊的

### 7. **circumvent** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：规避；绕过；设法避开
- **英文释义**：to find a way around an obstacle or to evade a rule or restriction
- **例句**：
  - The hackers found a way to circumvent the company's security system.
  - 黑客找到了规避公司安全系统的方法。
  - Some businesses try to circumvent tax regulations through offshore accounts.
  - 一些企业试图通过海外账户规避税收法规。
- **助记**：circum（围绕）+ vent（来）→ 绕着来 → 规避

### 8. **trepidation** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：恐惧；不安；战栗
- **英文释义**：a feeling of fear or agitation about something that may happen
- **例句**：
  - She opened the exam results with great trepidation, fearing the worst.
  - 她怀着极大的不安打开了考试成绩，害怕最坏的结果。
  - The new employees approached their first day with a mixture of excitement and trepidation.
  - 新员工们怀着兴奋与不安交织的心情迎来了第一天。
- **助记**：trepid（恐惧）+ ation → 恐惧的状态 → 恐惧/不安

### 9. **ameliorate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：改善；改良；使好转
- **英文释义**：to make something bad or unsatisfactory better
- **例句**：
  - The new policies were designed to ameliorate living conditions in the poorest neighborhoods.
  - 新政策旨在改善最贫困社区的生活条件。
  - Proper nutrition can ameliorate the symptoms of many chronic diseases.
  - 适当的营养可以改善许多慢性疾病的症状。
- **助记**：a + melior（更好）+ ate → 使更好 → 改善

### 10. **acrimonious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：尖刻的；充满怨恨的；激烈的
- **英文释义**：(typically of speech or a debate) angry and bitter
- **例句**：
  - The acrimonious divorce proceedings lasted over two years.
  - 充满怨恨的离婚诉讼持续了两年多。
  - What began as a polite discussion quickly became acrimonious.
  - 一开始礼貌的讨论很快变得尖刻激烈。
- **助记**：acrimon（辛辣/尖刻）+ ious → 辛辣的 → 尖刻的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **sehnsucht** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：对理想生活的深切渴望；无法满足的向往
- **英文释义**：a German word describing a deep longing for an alternative state of being, often idealized and unattainable
- **例句**：
  - C.S. Lewis described sehnsucht as the inconsolable longing for something we cannot name.
  - C.S.路易斯将 sehnsucht 描述为对我们无法命名之物的无法安慰的渴望。
  - The nostalgic melody evoked a sense of sehnsucht — a yearning for a world more perfect than this one.
  - 怀旧的旋律唤起了一种 sehnsucht 之感——对一个比现实更完美的世界的向往。
- **助记**：sehnsucht 是德语，Sehn（渴望）+ Sucht（沉迷）→ 沉迷于渴望中

### 12. **yugen** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：幽玄（对宇宙之美的深刻感知和神秘的优雅感）
- **英文释义**：a Japanese aesthetic concept describing a profound awareness of the universe that triggers emotional responses too deep for words
- **例句**：
  - Watching the autumn leaves drift slowly down the river, he experienced yugen — a beauty beyond explanation.
  - 看着秋叶缓缓顺河漂下，他体验到了幽玄——一种超越解释的美。
  - The concept of yugen is central to understanding Japanese poetry and Noh theater.
  - 幽玄的概念对理解日本诗歌和能剧至关重要。
- **助记**：yugen（幽玄）是日语，幽（幽深）+ 玄（玄妙）→ 幽深玄妙之美

### 13. **duende** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：灵魂的力量；艺术表演中令人震撼的神秘感染力
- **英文释义**：a Spanish concept of a heightened state of emotion, expression, and authenticity in artistic performance
- **例句**：
  - The flamenco dancer's duende captivated the audience, bringing tears to their eyes.
  - 弗拉门戈舞者的灵魂力量迷住了观众，让他们热泪盈眶。
  - Garcia Lorca wrote that duende is not in the throat but climbs up from the soles of the feet.
  - 加西亚·洛尔卡写道，duende 不在喉咙里，而是从脚底攀升上来。
- **助记**：duende 是西班牙语，原指小精灵/妖精，后引申为艺术中的灵魂力量

### 14. **eudaimonia** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：幸福；至善；人的全面繁荣
- **英文释义**：a Greek concept of happiness or welfare, specifically human flourishing achieved through living virtuously and fulfilling one's potential
- **例句**：
  - Aristotle believed that eudaimonia, not mere pleasure, was the highest human good.
  - 亚里士多德认为 eudaimonia（至善），而非单纯的快乐，是人类最高的善。
  - True eudaimonia comes from living a life of purpose, virtue, and meaningful relationships.
  - 真正的幸福来自过着有目标、有美德和有意义关系的生活。
- **助记**：eu（好）+ daimon（精灵/命运）→ 好的命运/精灵护佑 → 至善/幸福

### 15. **flâneur** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：漫步者；闲逛者（在城市中无目的漫步的观察者）
- **英文释义**：a French word for a person who strolls through a city, observing urban life with detached curiosity
- **例句**：
  - Baudelaire described the flâneur as an artist of modern life, absorbing the beauty of the passing crowd.
  - 波德莱尔将漫步者描述为现代生活的艺术家，吸收着过往人群的美。
  - She spent her weekends as a flâneur, wandering through different neighborhoods and discovering hidden cafes.
  - 她周末当漫步者，穿行于不同的街区，发现隐藏的咖啡馆。
- **助记**：flâneur 是法语，flâner（闲逛）+ eur（人）→ 闲逛的人 → 漫步者
