## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **accommodate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：容纳；适应；提供住宿
- **英文释义**：to provide lodging or room for; to fit in with the wishes or needs of
- **例句**：
  - The hotel can accommodate up to 500 guests at a time.
  - 这家酒店一次最多可容纳500位客人。
- **助记**：ac（向）+ com（一起）+ mod（模式/适应）+ ate → 使一起适应 → 容纳/适应

### 2. **circumvent** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：规避；绕过；避开
- **英文释义**：to find a way around an obstacle; to overcome a problem or difficulty cleverly
- **例句**：
  - The company tried to circumvent the new tax regulations by restructuring its operations.
  - 公司试图通过重组业务来规避新的税收法规。
- **助记**：circum（围绕）+ vent（来）→ 绕着来/走 → 规避/绕过

### 3. **deter** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：威慑；阻止；使不敢
- **英文释义**：to discourage someone from doing something by instilling doubt or fear
- **例句**：
  - The heavy fines failed to deter people from parking illegally.
  - 高额罚款未能阻止人们违法停车。
- **助记**：de（离开）+ ter（恐吓）→ 用恐吓使离开 → 威慑/阻止

### 4. **elicit** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：引出；引起；探出
- **英文释义**：to evoke or draw out a response, answer, or fact from someone
- **例句**：
  - The comedian's jokes elicited roars of laughter from the audience.
  - 喜剧演员的笑话引起了观众的哄堂大笑。
- **助记**：e（出）+ licit（引诱）→ 引诱出来 → 引出/引起

### 5. **grievance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：不满；怨恨；冤屈
- **英文释义**：a real or imagined wrong or other cause for complaint
- **例句**：
  - The workers filed a formal grievance with the management over unsafe working conditions.
  - 工人们就不安全的工作条件向管理层提出了正式申诉。
- **助记**：griev（悲伤/重负）+ ance → 心里的重负 → 不满/怨恨

### 6. **impeccable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无可挑剔的；完美的；毫无瑕疵的
- **英文释义**：in accordance with the highest standards of propriety; faultless
- **例句**：
  - Her impeccable manners and elegant dress impressed everyone at the reception.
  - 她无可挑剔的礼仪和优雅的着装给招待会上的每个人都留下了深刻印象。
- **助记**：im（不）+ pecc（罪/过错）+ able → 没有过错的 → 无可挑剔的

### 7. **liaison** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：联络；联系人；联络员
- **英文释义**：communication or cooperation that facilitates a close working relationship between people or organizations
- **例句**：
  - She served as the liaison between the marketing department and the design team.
  - 她担任市场部和设计团队之间的联络人。
- **助记**：liais（联系/连接）+ on → 联系/联络

### 8. **placate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：安抚；平息；抚慰
- **英文释义**：to make someone less angry or hostile
- **例句**：
  - The manager tried to placate the angry customer by offering a full refund.
  - 经理试图通过全额退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。
- **助记**：plac（平静/愉快）+ ate → 使变平静 → 安抚

### 9. **substantiate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证实；使具体化；证明……有根据
- **英文释义**：to provide evidence to support or prove the truth of
- **例句**：
  - The journalist was unable to substantiate the claims made in the controversial article.
  - 记者无法证实这篇争议文章中的说法。
- **助记**：sub（下面）+ stant（站立）+ iate → 在下面站稳 → 使有根据/证实

### 10. **vex** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使烦恼；使恼怒；使困扰
- **英文释义**：to make someone feel annoyed, frustrated, or worried
- **例句**：
  - The persistent noise from the neighbors vexed him throughout the entire weekend.
  - 邻居持续不断的噪音让他整个周末都很烦恼。
- **助记**：vex → 联想"哇烦（wex）"→ 使烦恼

### 高一词汇

### 11. **chicanery** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：诡辩；欺骗；诡计
- **英文释义**：the use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose
- **例句**：
  - The fraud case revealed years of financial chicanery by the company's executives.
  - 这起欺诈案揭露了公司高管多年的财务欺骗行为。
- **助记**：chican（诡辩）+ ery → 诡辩行为 → 欺骗/诡计

### 12. **emollient** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 使缓和的；舒缓的 n. 润肤剂；缓和剂
- **英文释义**：adj. having the quality of softening or soothing; n. a preparation that softens the skin
- **例句**：
  - The diplomat used emollient language to ease the tensions between the two nations.
  - 外交官用缓和的语言来缓解两国之间的紧张关系。
- **助记**：e（出）+ moll（软）+ ient → 使变软的 → 使缓和的/润肤的

### 13. **litigious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：好诉讼的；爱打官司的；好争论的
- **英文释义**：unreasonably prone to go to law to settle disputes; relating to litigation
- **例句**：
  - The company operates in a highly litigious industry where lawsuits are common.
  - 该公司经营在一个诉讼频繁的行业中。
- **助记**：litig（诉讼）+ ious → 爱诉讼的 → 好打官司的

### 14. **panacea** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：万灵药；灵丹妙药；万能方案
- **英文释义**：a solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases
- **例句**：
  - Technology alone is not a panacea for all the problems facing the education system.
  - 仅靠技术并不是解决教育系统所有问题的万能方案。
- **助记**：pan（全部）+ acea（治疗）→ 治疗全部的药 → 万灵药

### 15. **taciturn** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：沉默寡言的；不爱说话的
- **英文释义**：reserved or uncommunicative in speech; saying little
- **例句**：
  - The taciturn detective rarely spoke, but when he did, everyone listened carefully.
  - 这位沉默寡言的侦探很少说话，但每次他开口时，所有人都仔细聆听。
- **助记**：tacit（沉默的）+ urn → 沉默的 → 沉默寡言的
