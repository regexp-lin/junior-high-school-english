## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **gratuitous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无端的；无理由的；不必要的
- **英文释义**：uncalled for; lacking good reason; unwarranted
- **例句**：
  - The film was criticized for its gratuitous violence that served no narrative purpose.
  - 这部电影因无端的暴力场面而受到批评，这些场面对叙事毫无意义。
  - His gratuitous insult during the meeting was completely unprofessional.
  - 他在会议期间无端的侮辱完全不专业。
- **助记**：gratu（免费/白给的）+ itous → 白给的/没有理由的 → 无端的

### 2. **nefarious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：邪恶的；罪恶的；极坏的
- **英文释义**：(typically of an action or activity) wicked or criminal
- **例句**：
  - The nefarious scheme to defraud investors was uncovered by federal investigators.
  - 联邦调查人员揭露了欺骗投资者的邪恶阴谋。
  - History is filled with examples of nefarious leaders who brought suffering to millions.
  - 历史上充满了给数百万人带来苦难的邪恶领导者的例子。
- **助记**：ne（不）+ far（神圣的法律）+ ious → 不遵守神圣法律的 → 邪恶的

### 3. **extricate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：解救；使脱离；解脱
- **英文释义**：to free someone or something from a constraint or difficulty
- **例句**：
  - Firefighters worked for hours to extricate the driver from the wreckage.
  - 消防员工作了数小时才把司机从残骸中解救出来。
  - She struggled to extricate herself from the toxic relationship.
  - 她努力使自己从有害的关系中解脱出来。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ tric（纠缠）+ ate → 从纠缠中出来 → 解救

### 4. **judicious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：明智的；审慎的；有良好判断力的
- **英文释义**：having, showing, or done with good judgment or sense
- **例句**：
  - A judicious use of resources ensured the project stayed within budget.
  - 审慎地使用资源确保了项目在预算范围内。
  - The judge made a judicious decision that balanced the interests of both parties.
  - 法官做出了平衡双方利益的明智裁决。
- **助记**：judic（判断）+ ious → 善于判断的 → 明智的

### 5. **indomitable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可征服的；不屈不挠的；坚不可摧的
- **英文释义**：impossible to subdue or defeat
- **例句**：
  - Her indomitable spirit helped her overcome cancer and return to competitive sports.
  - 她不屈不挠的精神帮助她战胜了癌症并重返竞技体育。
  - The indomitable resistance of the citizens prevented the city from falling to the invaders.
  - 市民不可征服的抵抗阻止了城市落入侵略者手中。
- **助记**：in（不）+ domit（驯服）+ able → 不可驯服的 → 不可征服的

### 6. **petulant** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：任性的；使小性子的；易怒的
- **英文释义**：(of a person or their manner) childishly sulky or bad-tempered
- **例句**：
  - The petulant child threw a tantrum when his mother refused to buy him candy.
  - 任性的孩子在妈妈拒绝给他买糖时大发脾气。
  - His petulant response to constructive criticism disappointed his mentor.
  - 他对建设性批评任性的回应令导师失望。
- **助记**：petul（攻击/索求）+ ant → 不断索求的 → 任性的

### 7. **vindicate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：证明…正确；为…平反；证实
- **英文释义**：to clear someone of blame or suspicion; to show to be right or justified
- **例句**：
  - The new evidence vindicated the suspect, proving he was innocent all along.
  - 新证据为嫌疑人平反了，证明他一直是无辜的。
  - Time vindicated her controversial decision, which proved to be the right one.
  - 时间证明了她有争议的决定是正确的。
- **助记**：vind（力量/正义）+ ic + ate → 用力量证明正义 → 证明正确

### 8. **lugubrious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：悲伤的；阴郁的；愁容满面的
- **英文释义**：looking or sounding sad and dismal
- **例句**：
  - The lugubrious melody of the violin echoed through the empty church.
  - 小提琴悲伤的旋律在空旷的教堂中回荡。
  - His lugubrious expression at the party dampened everyone's spirits.
  - 他在派对上阴郁的表情让每个人都扫了兴。
- **助记**：lugubr（悲伤/哀悼）+ ious → 悲伤的 → 阴郁的

### 9. **disseminate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：传播；散布；宣传
- **英文释义**：to spread or disperse something, especially information, widely
- **例句**：
  - The organization disseminated educational materials to schools across the country.
  - 该组织向全国各地的学校传播教育材料。
  - Social media makes it easy to disseminate both accurate information and misinformation.
  - 社交媒体使传播准确信息和虚假信息都变得容易。
- **助记**：dis（分散）+ semin（种子）+ ate → 分散播种 → 传播

### 10. **fortuitous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：偶然的；意外的；碰巧的
- **英文释义**：happening by accident or chance rather than design; fortunate
- **例句**：
  - Their fortuitous meeting at the airport led to a lifelong friendship.
  - 他们在机场偶然的相遇导致了终生的友谊。
  - The discovery of penicillin was largely fortuitous, resulting from a contaminated experiment.
  - 青霉素的发现在很大程度上是偶然的，源于一次被污染的实验。
- **助记**：fortuit（偶然）+ ous → 偶然发生的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **tsundoku** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：积读（买书却不读，让书堆积起来）
- **英文释义**：the act of acquiring reading materials but letting them pile up without reading them
- **例句**：
  - His bookshelf was a monument to tsundoku — hundreds of unread books waiting patiently.
  - 他的书架是积读的纪念碑——数百本未读的书在耐心等待。
  - Tsundoku is a habit shared by bibliophiles worldwide who can't resist buying books.
  - 积读是全世界爱书人共有的习惯，他们无法抗拒买书的冲动。
- **助记**：tsundoku（積ん読）是日语，積む（堆积）+ 読く（读）→ 堆积不读

### 12. **mono no aware** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：物哀（对万物无常之美的深切感悟）
- **英文释义**：a Japanese term for the bittersweet awareness of the transience of things; the pathos of things
- **例句**：
  - The Japanese tradition of cherry blossom viewing embodies mono no aware — appreciating beauty precisely because it is fleeting.
  - 日本赏樱的传统体现了物哀——正因为美丽是短暂的才更加珍惜。
  - The film captured a sense of mono no aware, leaving audiences with a beautiful sadness.
  - 这部电影捕捉到了一种物哀之感，给观众留下了一种美丽的忧伤。
- **助记**：mono（物）+ no（的）+ aware（哀）→ 对事物的哀感 → 物哀

### 13. **brio** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：活力；朝气；充沛的精力
- **英文释义**：vigor or vivacity of style or performance
- **例句**：
  - The young conductor led the orchestra with remarkable brio, electrifying the audience.
  - 年轻的指挥家以非凡的活力指挥乐团，让观众感到振奋。
  - She delivered her presentation with such brio that even the most skeptical investors were convinced.
  - 她以充沛的活力进行了演示，甚至最持怀疑态度的投资者都被说服了。
- **助记**：brio 是意大利语，表示活力、热情

### 14. **gemütlichkeit** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：温馨惬意；舒适友好的氛围
- **英文释义**：a German word for a state or feeling of warmth, friendliness, and good cheer
- **例句**：
  - The old pub had an atmosphere of gemütlichkeit, with its crackling fireplace and friendly regulars.
  - 这家老酒吧有一种温馨惬意的氛围，壁炉噼啪作响，常客们友好热情。
  - German Christmas markets are famous for their gemütlichkeit — warm lights, spiced wine, and holiday cheer.
  - 德国圣诞集市以温馨惬意闻名——温暖的灯光、热红酒和节日欢乐。
- **助记**：gemütlich（舒适/惬意）+ keit（性质）→ 舒适惬意的感觉（德语借词）

### 15. **trouvaille** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：意外的发现；偶得之物；意外之喜
- **英文释义**：a lucky find; something of value found by chance
- **例句**：
  - The antique vase was a trouvaille — she discovered it at a flea market for only a few dollars.
  - 这个古董花瓶是意外之喜——她在跳蚤市场上只花了几美元就发现了它。
  - The researcher's trouvaille in the dusty archive changed the entire direction of her study.
  - 研究者在尘封的档案中的意外发现改变了她整个研究的方向。
- **助记**：trouvaille 是法语，trouver（发现）→ 偶然发现的好东西
