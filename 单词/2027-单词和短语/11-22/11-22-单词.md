## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **propensity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：倾向；癖好；天性
- **英文释义**：an inclination or natural tendency to behave in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - He has a propensity for making impulsive decisions without thinking them through.
  - 他有不经深思就冲动决定的倾向。
  - Children have a natural propensity for curiosity and exploration.
  - 儿童天生就有好奇心和探索的倾向。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ pens（倾斜）+ ity → 向前倾斜 → 倾向

### 2. **voracious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：贪婪的；如饥似渴的；狼吞虎咽的
- **英文释义**：wanting or devouring great quantities of food; having a very eager approach
- **例句**：
  - She is a voracious reader, finishing two or three books every week.
  - 她是一个如饥似渴的读者，每周读完两三本书。
  - The voracious appetite of the growing teenager surprised his parents.
  - 这个正在长身体的青少年狼吞虎咽的食欲让他的父母感到惊讶。
- **助记**：vor（吃）+ acious → 拼命吃的 → 贪婪的

### 3. **subjugate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：征服；制伏；使屈从
- **英文释义**：to bring under control or domination, especially by conquest
- **例句**：
  - The empire subjugated numerous smaller nations during its expansion.
  - 帝国在扩张过程中征服了许多小国。
  - She refused to be subjugated by fear and stood up to the bully.
  - 她拒绝被恐惧制伏，勇敢地面对欺凌者。
- **助记**：sub（在下面）+ jug（轭）+ ate → 置于轭下 → 征服

### 4. **innocuous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：无害的；无恶意的；不会冒犯人的
- **英文释义**：not harmful or offensive
- **例句**：
  - What seemed like an innocuous comment actually hurt her feelings deeply.
  - 看似无恶意的评论实际上深深伤害了她的感情。
  - The substance is innocuous and poses no health risks to humans.
  - 这种物质是无害的，不会对人体健康构成风险。
- **助记**：in（不）+ noc（伤害）+ uous → 不伤害的 → 无害的

### 5. **remunerate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：报酬；酬劳；付薪
- **英文释义**：to pay someone for services rendered or work done
- **例句**：
  - The company remunerates its employees generously for overtime work.
  - 公司慷慨地为员工的加班工作支付报酬。
  - Volunteers are not remunerated, but they receive training and experience.
  - 志愿者没有报酬，但他们获得培训和经验。
- **助记**：re（回）+ muner（礼物）+ ate → 回赠礼物 → 报酬

### 6. **steadfast** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坚定不移的；忠诚的；坚毅的
- **英文释义**：resolutely or dutifully firm and unwavering
- **例句**：
  - Her steadfast commitment to the cause inspired others to join.
  - 她对事业的坚定不移激励了其他人加入。
  - A steadfast friend will stand by you through both good times and bad.
  - 一个忠诚的朋友会在你顺境和逆境中都站在你身边。
- **助记**：stead（稳定）+ fast（牢固）→ 稳固牢靠 → 坚定不移的

### 7. **obfuscate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：混淆；使模糊；使困惑
- **英文释义**：to render obscure, unclear, or unintelligible
- **例句**：
  - The politician tried to obfuscate the issue by giving vague and confusing answers.
  - 政治家试图通过给出含糊和令人困惑的回答来混淆问题。
  - Technical jargon can obfuscate the meaning for non-experts.
  - 技术术语可能让非专业人士难以理解。
- **助记**：ob（加强）+ fusc（暗）+ ate → 使变暗 → 使模糊

### 8. **complacent** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：自满的；沾沾自喜的
- **英文释义**：showing smug or uncritical satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements
- **例句**：
  - The team became complacent after winning five games in a row and lost the next one.
  - 球队在连赢五场后变得自满，结果输掉了下一场。
  - We cannot be complacent about the progress we've made — there is still work to do.
  - 我们不能对已取得的进步沾沾自喜——还有工作要做。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ plac（满意）+ ent → 完全满意 → 自满的

### 9. **perpetuate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使永久化；使持续；使不朽
- **英文释义**：to make something continue indefinitely
- **例句**：
  - Stereotypes are perpetuated by media portrayals that lack diversity.
  - 刻板印象因缺乏多样性的媒体表现而被持续传播。
  - The foundation was established to perpetuate the memory of the great scientist.
  - 该基金会的成立是为了使这位伟大科学家的记忆永存。
- **助记**：per（始终）+ petu（追求）+ ate → 始终追求保持 → 使永久化

### 10. **fastidious** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：挑剔的；苛求的；一丝不苟的
- **英文释义**：very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail
- **例句**：
  - The fastidious chef inspected every dish before it left the kitchen.
  - 挑剔的厨师在每道菜端出厨房之前都会检查一番。
  - She was fastidious about her appearance, always perfectly groomed.
  - 她对自己的外表很挑剔，总是打扮得一丝不苟。
- **助记**：fastidi（嫌恶）+ ous → 对不完美感到嫌恶 → 挑剔的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **loquacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：话多的；健谈的；喋喋不休的
- **英文释义**：tending to talk a great deal; talkative
- **例句**：
  - The loquacious guest dominated the dinner conversation, barely letting anyone else speak.
  - 这位健谈的客人主导了晚餐对话，几乎不让别人说话。
  - Her loquacious nature made her an excellent talk show host.
  - 她健谈的性格使她成为一名出色的脱口秀主持人。
- **助记**：loqu（说话）+ acious → 不停说话 → 话多的

### 12. **magniloquent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：夸夸其谈的；言辞浮夸的
- **英文释义**：using high-flown or bombastic language
- **例句**：
  - The politician's magniloquent speeches were full of grand promises but lacked substance.
  - 政治家夸夸其谈的演讲充满了宏大的承诺但缺乏实质。
  - His magniloquent writing style alienated readers who preferred simplicity.
  - 他言辞浮夸的写作风格疏远了偏好简洁的读者。
- **助记**：magni（大）+ loqu（说话）+ ent → 说大话 → 夸夸其谈的

### 13. **defenestrate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：把…从窗户扔出去
- **英文释义**：to throw someone or something out of a window
- **例句**：
  - In the famous Defenestration of Prague, officials were defenestrated from the castle window.
  - 在著名的布拉格掷出窗外事件中，官员们被从城堡窗户扔了出去。
  - He jokingly threatened to defenestrate his old computer out of frustration.
  - 他开玩笑地威胁要把他的旧电脑从窗户扔出去以发泄不满。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ fenest（窗户）+ rate → 从窗户向下扔 → 从窗户扔出

### 14. **serendipity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：意外的幸运发现；机缘巧合
- **英文释义**：the occurrence of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way
- **例句**：
  - The discovery of penicillin was a remarkable case of serendipity.
  - 青霉素的发现是一个非凡的机缘巧合。
  - By sheer serendipity, she met her future business partner at a random café.
  - 纯粹出于机缘巧合，她在一家偶然去的咖啡馆遇到了未来的商业伙伴。
- **助记**：源自波斯童话 The Three Princes of Serendip → 意外发现好事

### 15. **incandescent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：白炽的；光亮夺目的；激情洋溢的
- **英文释义**：emitting light as a result of being heated; full of strong emotion
- **例句**：
  - The incandescent glow of the campfire created a warm atmosphere.
  - 营火白炽的光芒营造了温暖的氛围。
  - She delivered an incandescent performance that brought the audience to their feet.
  - 她呈现了一场激情洋溢的表演，让观众纷纷起立。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ cand（发光/白热）+ escent → 变得白热 → 白炽的
