## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **repine** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：抱怨；不满；苦恼
- **英文释义**：to be discontented or fretful; to complain peevishly
- **例句**：
  - He repined at his misfortune instead of looking for solutions.
  - 他对自己的不幸唉声叹气，而不是寻找解决办法。
  - There is no use repining over decisions that cannot be undone.
  - 为无法挽回的决定抱怨毫无用处。
- **助记**：re（反复）+ pine（渴望/痛苦）→ 反复苦恼 → 抱怨

### 2. **reprobate** `初三` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：堕落者；恶棍；道德败坏的
- **英文释义**：an unprincipled person; depraved or unprincipled
- **例句**：
  - The novel's hero is a charming reprobate with a hidden moral code.
  - 小说的主人公是个有隐藏道德准则的迷人浪子。
  - Reprobate behavior at the fraternity led to its suspension.
  - 兄弟会中的堕落行为导致其被暂停活动。
- **助记**：re（回）+ prob（测试/正直）+ ate → 经检验不正直 → 堕落者

### 3. **rescind** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：废除；撤销；取消（法令、合同）
- **英文释义**：to revoke, cancel, or repeal a law, order, or agreement
- **例句**：
  - The government rescinded the controversial policy after widespread protests.
  - 在广泛抗议后，政府撤销了那项有争议的政策。
  - The university rescinded his degree when plagiarism was proven.
  - 抄袭被证实后，大学撤销了他的学位。
- **助记**：re（回）+ scind（切）→ 切回去 → 撤销

### 4. **restive** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不耐烦的；难驾驭的；躁动不安的
- **英文释义**：impatient or restless, especially under restraint; difficult to control
- **例句**：
  - The restive crowd grew louder as the concert started an hour late.
  - 演唱会迟到一个小时，不耐烦的人群越来越吵。
  - The restive horse refused to jump the fence.
  - 那匹难驾驭的马拒绝跳过栅栏。
- **助记**：rest（休息）+ ive → 想动不想静 → 焦躁（与 restless 相近）

### 5. **ribald** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：粗俗下流的；荤腥玩笑的
- **英文释义**：referring to sexual matters in an amusingly coarse or irreverent way
- **例句**：
  - The comedian's ribald jokes were not suitable for young audiences.
  - 那位喜剧演员的荤段子不适合年轻观众。
  - Medieval fabliaux often featured ribald humor about ordinary life.
  - 中世纪短篇故事诗常包含关于日常生活的粗俗幽默。
- **助记**：联想 rib（肋骨/身体）→ 拿身体开玩笑 → 下流幽默

### 6. **saturnine** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：阴沉忧郁的；性情孤僻的
- **英文释义**：slow and gloomy in temperament; morose
- **例句**：
  - The saturnine detective rarely smiled, even when solving a case.
  - 那位阴沉的侦探即使破案也很少微笑。
  - Rainy weather made the whole city feel saturnine and quiet.
  - 雨天让整座城市显得阴郁而安静。
- **助记**：Saturn（土星/农神）→ 占星术中与忧郁相关 → 阴沉的

### 7. **schadenfreude** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：幸灾乐祸
- **英文释义**：pleasure derived from another person's misfortune
- **例句**：
  - Admitting schadenfreude is embarrassing, but many felt it when the rival team lost.
  - 承认幸灾乐祸令人尴尬，但对手队失利时许多人都有这种感觉。
  - The article explored why social media amplifies schadenfreude toward celebrities.
  - 文章探讨了社交媒体为何放大对名人的幸灾乐祸。
- **助记**：德语 Schaden（损害）+ Freude（快乐）→ 从他人不幸中得乐

### 8. **sedulous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：勤勉的；一丝不苟的；坚持不懈的
- **英文释义**：diligent in application or pursuit; assiduous
- **例句**：
  - Her sedulous attention to detail prevented costly errors in the report.
  - 她对细节一丝不苟，避免了报告中的昂贵错误。
  - The sedulous student reviewed his notes every night without fail.
  - 那位勤奋的学生每晚必复习笔记。
- **助记**：sed（坐）+ ulous → 坐得住冷板凳 → 勤勉

### 9. **sobriquet** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：绰号；诨名
- **英文释义**：a nickname; an affectionate or humorous name given to a person or thing
- **例句**：
  - "The Boss" was Springsteen's sobriquet among his fans.
  - “老大”是斯普林斯汀歌迷给他的绰号。
  - The city earned the sobriquet "Windy City" for reasons beyond weather.
  - 这座城市得了“风城”的绰号，原因不止天气。
- **助记**：法语“在嘴下”→ 挂在嘴边的名字 → 绰号

### 10. **stalwart** `初三` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：坚定的；健壮的；忠实拥护者
- **英文释义**：loyal, reliable, and hardworking; a physically strong supporter
- **例句**：
  - She has been a stalwart defender of free speech for decades.
  - 几十年来她一直是言论自由的坚定捍卫者。
  - The party stalwarts campaigned door to door in the final week.
  - 党的铁杆支持者在最后一周逐户拉票。
- **助记**：stal（= stand 站立）+ wart → 站得稳 → 坚定的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **stolid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：无动于衷的；迟钝冷漠的；表情木然的
- **英文释义**：calm, dependable, and showing little emotion or animation; unexcitable
- **例句**：
  - The accused remained stolid throughout the dramatic testimony.
  - 在戏剧性的证词过程中，被告始终木然。
  - His stolid manner hid a sharp analytical mind.
  - 他冷漠的外表下藏着敏锐的头脑。
- **助记**：联想 solid（实心）→ 情感像实心一样不流动 → 木讷

### 12. **subterfuge** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：诡计；托词；掩饰手段
- **英文释义**：deceit used in order to achieve one's goal; a stratagem
- **例句**：
  - The spy used a series of subterfuges to cross the border undetected.
  - 间谍用一连串诡计在不被发现的情况下越过边境。
  - Honest negotiation is better than legal subterfuge.
  - 坦诚谈判胜过法律上的花招。
- **助记**：subter（私下）+ fuge（逃）→ 私下溜走 → 诡计

### 13. **surfeit** `高一` n./v.

- **中文释义**：过量；腻烦；（使）饮食过度
- **英文释义**：an excessive amount of something; to cause to desire no more of something
- **例句**：
  - A surfeit of cheap credit led to the housing bubble.
  - 廉价信贷过剩导致了房地产泡沫。
  - He surfeited himself on sweets and felt sick afterward.
  - 他甜食吃得过多，之后感到恶心。
- **助记**：sur（超过）+ feit（做）→ 做过头 → 过量

### 14. **tendentious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：有偏见的；宣传性的；倾向明显的
- **英文释义**：expressing or promoting a particular point of view, especially a controversial one
- **例句**：
  - The documentary was criticized as tendentious for omitting key facts.
  - 纪录片因遗漏关键事实而被批评带有偏见。
  - Tendentious headlines can shape public opinion before people read the article.
  - 带有倾向性的标题会在人们读文章之前就影响舆论。
- **助记**：tend（倾向）+ entious → 带倾向的 → 有偏见的

### 15. **timorous** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：胆小的；胆怯的；畏缩的
- **英文释义**：showing or suffering from nervousness, fear, or a lack of confidence
- **例句**：
  - The timorous child hid behind her mother when strangers approached.
  - 陌生人走近时，胆怯的孩子躲在母亲身后。
  - His timorous response suggested he was unprepared for the interview.
  - 他畏缩的回答表明他对面试准备不足。
- **助记**：timor（恐惧）+ ous → 充满恐惧 → 胆怯的
