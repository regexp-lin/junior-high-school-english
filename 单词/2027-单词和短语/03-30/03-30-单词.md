## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **ailment** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：疾病；小病
- **英文释义**：an illness, typically a minor one
- **例句**：
  - Common ailments such as headaches and colds are easy to treat.
  - 像头痛和感冒这样的常见小病很容易治疗。
- **助记**：ail（生病）+ ment → 生病的状态 → 疾病

### 2. **caution** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 小心；谨慎；警告 v. 警告；告诫
- **英文释义**：n. care taken to avoid danger or mistakes; v. to warn someone
- **例句**：
  - The sign cautioned drivers to slow down near the school.
  - 标志警告司机在学校附近减速。
- **助记**：caut（小心）+ ion → 小心的状态 → 谨慎/警告

### 3. **doom** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 厄运；毁灭 v. 注定（失败/毁灭）
- **英文释义**：n. death, destruction, or another terrible fate; v. to condemn to a particular fate
- **例句**：
  - The project was doomed to failure from the start.
  - 这个项目从一开始就注定要失败。
- **助记**：doom 谐音"毒姆"→ 毒辣的命运 → 厄运

### 4. **eternal** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：永恒的；永久的；不朽的
- **英文释义**：lasting or existing forever; without end
- **例句**：
  - The couple promised each other eternal love.
  - 这对夫妇互相承诺了永恒的爱。
- **助记**：etern（永恒）+ al → 永恒的

### 5. **forge** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 锻造；伪造；开辟 n. 锻炉
- **英文释义**：v. to make or shape a metal object by heating; to produce a copy for fraudulent purposes; n. a workshop with a furnace
- **例句**：
  - The blacksmith forged a beautiful iron gate.
  - 铁匠锻造了一扇漂亮的铁门。
- **助记**：forge → force（力量）→ 用力量锻打 → 锻造

### 6. **huddle** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 挤在一起；蜷缩 n. 一群人的密集
- **英文释义**：v. to crowd together; to curl one's body into a small space; n. a close grouping
- **例句**：
  - The children huddled together to keep warm.
  - 孩子们挤在一起取暖。
- **助记**：huddle 谐音"哈斗"→ 冷得哈气抖成一团 → 挤在一起

### 7. **litter** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 垃圾；一窝（幼崽）v. 乱扔；弄乱
- **英文释义**：n. rubbish left lying in a public place; a number of young animals born at one time; v. to make untidy
- **例句**：
  - Please don't litter in the park.
  - 请不要在公园里乱扔垃圾。
- **助记**：litter → little（小的）→ 小碎片散落一地 → 垃圾

### 8. **nurture** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 培养；养育；扶植 n. 养育；培养
- **英文释义**：v. to care for and encourage the growth of; n. the process of caring for and encouraging growth
- **例句**：
  - Parents nurture their children with love and patience.
  - 父母用爱和耐心养育他们的孩子。
- **助记**：nur（营养）+ ture → 给予营养来培养 → 养育

### 9. **sanctuary** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：庇护所；避难所；圣殿
- **英文释义**：a place of refuge or safety; a holy place
- **例句**：
  - The wildlife sanctuary protects endangered animals.
  - 这个野生动物保护区保护濒危动物。
- **助记**：sanct（神圣的）+ uary → 神圣的安全之所 → 庇护所

### 10. **verdict** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：裁决；判决；结论
- **英文释义**：a decision on a disputed issue in a civil or criminal case
- **例句**：
  - The jury reached a verdict of not guilty.
  - 陪审团做出了无罪的裁决。
- **助记**：ver（真实）+ dict（说）→ 说出真相 → 裁决

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ambivalent** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：矛盾的；犹豫不决的
- **英文释义**：having mixed feelings or contradictory ideas about something
- **例句**：
  - She felt ambivalent about accepting the job offer.
  - 她对是否接受这份工作犹豫不决。
- **助记**：ambi（两个）+ valent（价值/力量）→ 两种力量拉扯 → 矛盾的

### 12. **corroborate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：证实；确认
- **英文释义**：to confirm or give support to a statement or theory
- **例句**：
  - The witness's statement corroborated the victim's account.
  - 证人的陈述证实了受害者的说法。
- **助记**：cor（共同）+ robor（力量）+ ate → 共同给力支持 → 证实

### 13. **exonerate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：免除罪责；证明无罪
- **英文释义**：to absolve someone from blame for a fault or wrongdoing
- **例句**：
  - DNA evidence exonerated the wrongfully convicted man.
  - DNA证据证明了被错误定罪之人的清白。
- **助记**：ex（去除）+ oner（负担）+ ate → 去除负担/罪责 → 免除罪责

### 14. **juxtapose** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：并列；并置（以作比较）
- **英文释义**：to place close together or side by side, especially for comparison or contrast
- **例句**：
  - The exhibition juxtaposes ancient and modern art.
  - 这个展览将古代和现代艺术并列展示。
- **助记**：juxta（旁边）+ pose（放置）→ 放在旁边对比 → 并置

### 15. **pragmatic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：务实的；实用主义的
- **英文释义**：dealing with things sensibly and realistically rather than following theories
- **例句**：
  - We need a pragmatic approach to solve this problem.
  - 我们需要一个务实的方法来解决这个问题。
- **助记**：pragma（行动/实践）+ tic → 注重实践的 → 务实的
