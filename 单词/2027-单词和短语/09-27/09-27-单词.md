## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **purport** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：声称；标榜；主旨；意图
- **英文释义**：to claim or appear to be; the meaning or substance of something
- **例句**：
  - The email purports to come from the bank but is a scam.
  - 这封邮件自称来自银行，实为诈骗。
  - The purport of his speech was to call for unity.
  - 他演讲的主旨是呼吁团结。
- **助记**：pur（向前）+ port（携带）→ 带到前面 → 声称、主旨

### 2. **query** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：疑问；质问；查询
- **英文释义**：a question or doubt; to ask formally
- **例句**：
  - She raised a query about the accuracy of the data.
  - 她对数据的准确性提出疑问。
  - Reporters queried the minister on the new policy.
  - 记者就新政策向部长提问。
- **助记**：quer（求）+ y → 寻求答案 → 疑问

### 3. **ramify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：分枝；分岔；使复杂化
- **英文释义**：to branch out; to cause to branch or spread
- **例句**：
  - The river ramifies into smaller streams in the delta.
  - 河流在三角洲分成许多支流。
  - One small decision ramified into many unexpected consequences.
  - 一个小决定衍生出许多意想不到的后果。
- **助记**：rami（枝）+ fy → 使成枝状 → 分枝

### 4. **sanctify** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：使神圣；认可；使正当化
- **英文释义**：to set apart as holy; to make legitimate or binding by religious sanction
- **例句**：
  - Ancient rituals were meant to sanctify the marriage.
  - 古代仪式旨在使婚姻神圣化。
  - Tradition can sanctify practices that seem odd to outsiders.
  - 传统可以使外人看来古怪的做法变得正当。
- **助记**：sanct（神圣）+ ify → 使神圣

### 5. **tout** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：吹捧；兜售；招徕
- **英文释义**：to advertise or praise enthusiastically; to solicit persistently
- **例句**：
  - Street vendors tout souvenirs to tourists.
  - 街头小贩向游客兜售纪念品。
  - Critics accused the company of touting unproven benefits.
  - 批评者指责该公司吹捧未经证实的好处。
- **助记**：大声拉客 → 兜售、吹捧

### 6. **upbraid** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：训斥；责骂；谴责
- **英文释义**：to find fault with someone severely; scold
- **例句**：
  - The coach upbraided the players for lack of discipline.
  - 教练因缺乏纪律而训斥队员。
  - She upbraided herself for forgetting the appointment.
  - 她因忘记约会而自责。
- **助记**：up（向上）+ braid（编）→ 古义拉扯头发 → 责骂

### 7. **vacuous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：空洞的；无思想的；愚蠢的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a lack of thought or intelligence; empty
- **例句**：
  - The article was dismissed as vacuous gossip.
  - 这篇文章被视为空洞的八卦。
  - His vacuous smile hid nothing behind it.
  - 他空洞的微笑背后什么也没有。
- **助记**：vacu（空）+ ous → 空空的 → 空洞

### 8. **wretched** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：悲惨的；极坏的；可怜的
- **英文释义**：in a very unhappy or unfortunate state; of poor quality
- **例句**：
  - They lived in wretched conditions after the flood.
  - 洪水过后他们生活在悲惨的条件下。
  - The hotel room was wretched and overpriced.
  - 旅馆房间又差又贵。
- **助记**：wretch（可怜人）+ ed → 可怜的、糟糕的

### 9. **wily** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：狡猾的；诡计多端的
- **英文释义**：skilled at gaining an advantage, especially deceitfully; cunning
- **例句**：
  - The wily fox escaped the hunters again.
  - 狡猾的狐狸再次逃脱猎人。
  - A wily negotiator secured better terms for his client.
  - 精明的谈判者为委托人争取到更好条件。
- **助记**：wile（诡计）+ y → 多诡计的 → 狡猾

### 10. **yearn** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：渴望；向往；思念
- **英文释义**：to have an intense longing for something
- **例句**：
  - She yearned to return to her hometown after many years abroad.
  - 出国多年后她渴望回到家乡。
  - Readers yearn for stories that touch the heart.
  - 读者渴望触动人心的故事。
- **助记**：yearn 音近“焰”→ 心中如火 → 渴望

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acumen** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：敏锐；精明；洞察力
- **英文释义**：the ability to make good judgments and quick decisions
- **例句**：
  - Her business acumen helped the startup survive its first year.
  - 她的商业敏锐帮助初创公司度过第一年。
  - Political acumen is essential for a diplomat.
  - 政治敏锐对外交官至关重要。
- **助记**：acu（尖锐）+ men → 尖锐的头脑 → 敏锐

### 12. **bowdlerize** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：删改（书籍）以净化内容；删节不当处
- **英文释义**：to remove material considered improper or offensive from a text
- **例句**：
  - The publisher bowdlerized the classic novel for young readers.
  - 出版商删改了这部经典小说以适合年轻读者。
  - Critics opposed bowdlerized editions that lost the author’s voice.
  - 批评者反对删节后失去作者声音的版本。
- **助记**：来自人名 Bowdler → 过度删改 → bowdlerize

### 13. **cupidity** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：贪婪；贪财
- **英文释义**：greed for money or possessions
- **例句**：
  - Cupidity drove him to betray his friends.
  - 贪婪驱使他背叛朋友。
  - The tale warns against the cupidity of rulers.
  - 这则寓言警告统治者勿贪。
- **助记**：Cupid（爱神）→ 误记为贪爱钱财 → 贪婪（实际来自拉丁 cupere 渴望）

### 14. **desiccate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使干燥；使枯竭；失去活力
- **英文释义**：to remove moisture from; to dry thoroughly; to drain of vitality
- **例句**：
  - Hot winds desiccate the soil in summer.
  - 夏季热风使土壤变干。
  - Years of routine work desiccated his creativity.
  - 多年刻板工作使他的创造力枯竭。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ sicc（干）→ 完全弄干

### 15. **enervate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使衰弱；使无力；削弱
- **英文释义**：to weaken or drain of energy or vitality
- **例句**：
  - The illness enervated him for months.
  - 这场病使他虚弱了好几个月。
  - Constant criticism can enervate a child’s confidence.
  - 不断批评会削弱孩子的自信。
- **助记**：e（出）+ nerv（神经）→ 神经抽走 → 无力（勿与 energize 混淆）
