## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **astute** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：精明的；敏锐的
- **英文释义**：having or showing an ability to accurately assess situations and turn them to one's advantage
- **例句**：
  - She made an astute observation about the market trends.
  - 她对市场趋势做出了敏锐的观察。
- **助记**：a + stute（联想 "student"）→ 像好学生一样精明 → 敏锐的

### 2. **candid** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：坦率的；直言不讳的
- **英文释义**：truthful and straightforward; frank
- **例句**：
  - I appreciate your candid feedback on my presentation.
  - 我感谢你对我演示的坦率反馈。
- **助记**：cand（白色/光亮）+ id → 像白色一样透明 → 坦率的

### 3. **conspicuous** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：显眼的；引人注目的
- **英文释义**：standing out so as to be clearly visible; attracting notice
- **例句**：
  - His bright red jacket made him conspicuous in the crowd.
  - 他鲜红色的夹克使他在人群中非常显眼。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ spic（看）+ uous → 大家都能看到的 → 显眼的

### 4. **deviate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：偏离；背离
- **英文释义**：depart from an established course or norm
- **例句**：
  - The pilot had to deviate from the planned route due to bad weather.
  - 由于恶劣天气，飞行员不得不偏离原定航线。
- **助记**：de（偏离）+ vi（路）+ ate → 偏离道路 → 偏离

### 5. **evoke** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：唤起；引起（记忆、情感）
- **英文释义**：bring or recall to the conscious mind; elicit (a response)
- **例句**：
  - The old photograph evoked happy memories of her childhood.
  - 这张老照片唤起了她对童年的美好回忆。
- **助记**：e（出）+ voke（叫）→ 叫出来 → 唤起

### 6. **futility** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：徒劳；无用
- **英文释义**：pointlessness or uselessness
- **例句**：
  - He finally recognized the futility of arguing with her.
  - 他终于认识到和她争论是徒劳的。
- **助记**：fut（倒/流）+ ility → 像水一样流走 → 徒劳

### 7. **hindrance** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：障碍；妨碍
- **英文释义**：a thing that provides resistance or obstruction to something
- **例句**：
  - Lack of sleep can be a serious hindrance to learning.
  - 缺乏睡眠会严重妨碍学习。
- **助记**：hinder（阻碍）+ ance → 阻碍之物 → 障碍

### 8. **integrity** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：正直；诚实；完整性
- **英文释义**：the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; the state of being whole
- **例句**：
  - A leader must demonstrate integrity to earn people's trust.
  - 一个领导者必须展示正直才能赢得人们的信任。
- **助记**：integr（完整的）+ ity → 完整不破碎的品格 → 正直

### 9. **mediocre** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：平庸的；普通的
- **英文释义**：of only moderate quality; not very good
- **例句**：
  - The restaurant received mediocre reviews from food critics.
  - 这家餐厅得到了美食评论家的平庸评价。
- **助记**：medi（中间）+ ocre → 处于中间水平 → 平庸的

### 10. **prolific** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：多产的；丰富的
- **英文释义**：producing much fruit or foliage or many offspring; producing many works
- **例句**：
  - Shakespeare was one of the most prolific writers in English literature.
  - 莎士比亚是英国文学中最多产的作家之一。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lific（制造）→ 不断向前制造 → 多产的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **cumbersome** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：笨重的；累赘的；繁琐的
- **英文释义**：large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry or use; slow and complicated
- **例句**：
  - The old computer system was cumbersome and slow to operate.
  - 旧的电脑系统笨重且操作缓慢。
- **助记**：cumber（阻碍）+ some → 造成阻碍的 → 笨重的

### 12. **ethereal** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：轻飘的；缥缈的；超凡脱俗的
- **英文释义**：extremely delicate and light; heavenly or spiritual
- **例句**：
  - The dancer moved with an ethereal grace that captivated the audience.
  - 舞者以缥缈的优雅舞姿吸引了观众。
- **助记**：ether（以太/天空）+ eal → 天空般的 → 缥缈的

### 13. **loquacious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：话多的；健谈的
- **英文释义**：tending to talk a great deal; talkative
- **例句**：
  - My loquacious neighbor can talk for hours without stopping.
  - 我那健谈的邻居可以不停地说上几个小时。
- **助记**：loqu（说话）+ acious → 不停地说 → 话多的

### 14. **nefarious** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：邪恶的；极坏的
- **英文释义**：wicked or criminal
- **例句**：
  - The villain's nefarious plot was foiled by the detective.
  - 恶棍的邪恶阴谋被侦探挫败了。
- **助记**：ne（不）+ far（正确的/法律）+ ious → 不合法的 → 邪恶的

### 15. **taciturn** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：沉默寡言的；不苟言笑的
- **英文释义**：reserved or uncommunicative in speech; saying little
- **例句**：
  - He was a taciturn man who preferred listening to talking.
  - 他是个沉默寡言的人，更喜欢倾听而不是说话。
- **助记**：tacit（沉默的）+ urn → 沉默的 → 沉默寡言的
