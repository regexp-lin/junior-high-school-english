## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **abstain** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：戒除；弃权；有意避免
- **英文释义**：to restrain oneself from doing something; to refrain from voting
- **例句**：
  - He decided to abstain from sugary drinks for a month.
  - 他决定一个月内不喝含糖饮料。
  - Three committee members abstained in the final vote.
  - 三名委员在最终表决中弃权。
- **助记**：abs（离开）+ tain（保持）→ 保持离开（诱惑）→ 戒除

### 2. **bequeath** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：遗赠；把……传给后代
- **英文释义**：to pass something on or leave it to someone by will
- **例句**：
  - She bequeathed her jewelry collection to her niece.
  - 她把珠宝收藏遗赠给侄女。
  - Older generations bequeath traditions that shape younger ones.
  - 老一辈把塑造后辈的传统传下去。
- **助记**：be（使）+ queath（说）→ 遗嘱里说给谁的 → 遗赠

### 3. **clamor** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：喧嚷；大声要求；嘈杂声
- **英文释义**：a loud and confused noise; to demand loudly
- **例句**：
  - Fans began to clamor for an encore after the concert.
  - 音乐会后歌迷大声要求返场。
  - The clamor of the market made conversation difficult.
  - 市场的嘈杂声让人难以交谈。
- **助记**：clam（叫喊）+ or → 喊叫 → 喧嚷

### 4. **deplete** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：耗尽；使枯竭；大量减少
- **英文释义**：to use up the supply or resources of; to exhaust
- **例句**：
  - Long droughts can deplete water reserves in reservoirs.
  - 长期干旱会耗尽水库蓄水。
  - Overtraining may deplete your energy before the competition.
  - 过度训练可能在赛前耗尽你的体力。
- **助记**：de（去掉）+ ple（满）+ te → 把满的倒空 → 耗尽

### 5. **emulate** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：仿效；与……竞争；努力赶上
- **英文释义**：to match or surpass by imitation; to strive to equal
- **例句**：
  - Young athletes emulate their sports heroes in training habits.
  - 年轻运动员在训练习惯上效仿体育偶像。
  - The program emulates older software on new computers.
  - 该程序在新电脑上模拟旧软件运行。
- **助记**：emul（竞争）+ ate → 努力赶上 → 仿效

### 6. **flounder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：挣扎；支吾；艰难行事
- **英文释义**：to struggle with difficulties; to speak or act clumsily
- **例句**：
  - Without a map, we floundered in the dense forest for hours.
  - 没有地图，我们在密林中挣扎了数小时。
  - He floundered for words when asked about the missing homework.
  - 被问到缺交作业时他支支吾吾。
- **助记**：flounder（比目鱼）→ 鱼在岸上挣扎 → 挣扎

### 7. **glean** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：搜集；拾取（信息、知识）；拾穗
- **英文释义**：to collect information or material bit by bit; to gather leftover grain
- **例句**：
  - Reporters gleaned details from social media posts.
  - 记者从社交媒体帖子中搜集细节。
  - Students glean useful tips from seniors who took the exam before.
  - 学生从往届考生那里收集有用窍门。
- **助记**：glean 联想 clean（收拾干净）→ 把残余拾干净 → 搜集

### 8. **hinder** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；拖后腿
- **英文释义**：to create difficulties for someone or something; impede
- **例句**：
  - Tight shoes hinder running speed on the track.
  - 紧鞋会妨碍跑道上的速度。
  - Fear of failure should not hinder you from entering the contest.
  - 对失败的恐惧不应妨碍你参赛。
- **助记**：hind（后面）+ er → 拖在后面 → 妨碍

### 9. **immutable** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：不可改变的；永恒的
- **英文释义**：unchanging over time; unable to be altered
- **例句**：
  - Mathematical truths seem immutable across cultures and centuries.
  - 数学真理在不同文化与世纪中似乎永恒不变。
  - The constitution establishes immutable rights for citizens.
  - 宪法确立公民不可剥夺的权利。
- **助记**：im（不）+ mut（变）+ able → 不可变

### 10. **jostle** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：推挤；争抢；与……竞争
- **英文释义**：to push, elbow, or bump against someone; to compete roughly
- **例句**：
  - Commuters jostled for space on the crowded train.
  - 通勤者在拥挤列车上争抢空间。
  - Start-ups jostle for attention in a busy app market.
  - 初创公司在拥挤的应用市场中争抢关注。
- **助记**：just（正好）+ le → 挤来挤去找位置 → 推挤

### 高一词汇

### 11. **kinetic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：运动的；动力学的；活跃的
- **英文释义**：relating to or resulting from motion; dynamic
- **例句**：
  - The sculpture included kinetic parts that moved in the wind.
  - 雕塑包含随风运动的活动部件。
  - Kinetic energy increases as the car accelerates downhill.
  - 汽车下坡加速时动能增加。
- **助记**：kine（运动）+ tic → 运动的

### 12. **lurid** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：骇人听闻的；刺眼的；苍白的
- **英文释义**：presented in vividly shocking or sensational terms; glaringly bright
- **例句**：
  - Tabloids publish lurid headlines to attract readers.
  - 小报用耸动标题吸引读者。
  - The lurid sunset painted the sky deep orange and purple.
  - 刺眼的落日把天空染成深橙与紫色。
- **助记**：lur（苍白/鬼怪）+ id → 像鬼火般刺眼骇人 → 骇人

### 13. **myopic** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：近视的；目光短浅的；缺乏远见的
- **英文释义**：nearsighted; lacking imagination, foresight, or intellectual insight
- **例句**：
  - Myopic policies may save money now but harm future growth.
  - 短视政策眼下省钱却损害未来增长。
  - Without glasses, myopic readers hold books very close.
  - 不戴眼镜时，近视读者把书拿得很近。
- **助记**：myo（闭）+ op（眼）+ ic → 眼睛只看见近处 → 近视/短视

### 14. **nemesis** `高一` n.

- **中文释义**：宿敌；难以战胜的对手；报应
- **英文释义**：a long-standing rival or arch-enemy; a downfall caused by retribution
- **例句**：
  - The champion met his nemesis in the young challenger from abroad.
  - 冠军遇到了来自国外的年轻劲敌。
  - Pride proved to be the villain's nemesis in the story.
  - 故事中骄傲成了反派的克星。
- **助记**：来自希腊复仇女神 Nemesis → 克星

### 15. **ostracize** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：排斥；放逐；摈弃
- **英文释义**：to exclude someone from a society or group; to shun
- **例句**：
  - Whistle-blowers were sometimes ostracized by former colleagues.
  - 举报者有时会遭到前同事排斥。
  - The community ostracized anyone who cooperated with the corrupt official.
  - 社区排斥与贪官合作的任何人。
- **助记**：ostrakon（陶片，古希腊投票放逐）→ 放逐/排斥
