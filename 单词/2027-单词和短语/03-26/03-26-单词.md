## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **blanket** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 毯子；覆盖物 v. 覆盖
- **英文释义**：n. a large piece of woolen material used as a covering; v. to cover completely
- **例句**：
  - Snow blanketed the entire village overnight.
  - 雪一夜之间覆盖了整个村庄。
- **助记**：blank（空白）+ et → 在空白的地面上铺上一层 → 毯子/覆盖

### 2. **choir** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：合唱团；唱诗班
- **英文释义**：an organized group of singers, typically one that performs in a church or concert hall
- **例句**：
  - She joined the school choir when she was ten.
  - 她十岁时加入了学校合唱团。
- **助记**：choir 发音近"快儿"→ 快来一起唱的一群人 → 合唱团

### 3. **devote** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：奉献；致力于；专心于
- **英文释义**：to give all or a large part of one's time or resources to a person, activity, or cause
- **例句**：
  - She devoted her life to helping the poor.
  - 她毕生致力于帮助穷人。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ vote（投票/许愿）→ 完全投入 → 奉献

### 4. **esteem** `初三` n./v.

- **中文释义**：n. 尊重；敬重 v. 尊敬；认为
- **英文释义**：n. respect and admiration; v. to respect and admire
- **例句**：
  - The professor is held in high esteem by her students.
  - 这位教授深受学生敬重。
- **助记**：e + steem（steam 蒸汽）→ 热情像蒸汽一样上升 → 尊重

### 5. **gravel** `初三` n.

- **中文释义**：碎石；砂砾
- **英文释义**：a loose mixture of small water-worn or pounded stones
- **例句**：
  - The path was covered with gravel.
  - 小路上铺满了碎石。
- **助记**：grave（坟墓）+ l → 坟墓上铺的石子 → 碎石

### 6. **incline** `初三` v./n.

- **中文释义**：v. 倾向于；倾斜 n. 斜坡
- **英文释义**：v. to lean or turn away from a given direction; to feel willing; n. a slope
- **例句**：
  - I incline to agree with your opinion.
  - 我倾向于同意你的看法。
- **助记**：in（向）+ cline（倾斜）→ 向某个方向倾斜 → 倾向/斜坡

### 7. **mumble** `初三` v.

- **中文释义**：含糊地说；嘟囔
- **英文释义**：to say something indistinctly and quietly
- **例句**：
  - He mumbled an apology and walked away.
  - 他嘟囔了一句道歉就走了。
- **助记**：mum（妈妈/沉默）+ ble → 像闭着嘴说话一样 → 嘟囔

### 8. **overhead** `初三` adv./adj.

- **中文释义**：adv. 在头顶上方 adj. 头顶上的；经费的
- **英文释义**：adv. above the level of the head; adj. situated above; relating to running costs
- **例句**：
  - A plane flew overhead as we were having a picnic.
  - 我们野餐时一架飞机从头顶飞过。
- **助记**：over（上方）+ head（头）→ 在头上方的 → 在头顶上方

### 9. **retail** `初三` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：n. 零售 adj. 零售的
- **英文释义**：n. the sale of goods to the public in relatively small quantities; adj. relating to retail
- **例句**：
  - The retail price of the phone is higher than the wholesale price.
  - 这部手机的零售价比批发价高。
- **助记**：re（再）+ tail（尾巴/小部分）→ 再分成小部分卖 → 零售

### 10. **solemn** `初三` adj.

- **中文释义**：庄严的；严肃的；郑重的
- **英文释义**：formal and dignified; not cheerful or smiling; serious
- **例句**：
  - The soldiers stood in solemn silence during the ceremony.
  - 仪式期间，士兵们庄严肃穆地站着。
- **助记**：sol（太阳）+ emn → 像太阳一样庄严 → 庄严的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **bilateral** `高一` adj.

- **中文释义**：双边的；双方的
- **英文释义**：having or relating to two sides; affecting both sides
- **例句**：
  - The two countries signed a bilateral trade agreement.
  - 两国签署了双边贸易协定。
- **助记**：bi（双）+ lateral（侧面的）→ 两个侧面的 → 双边的

### 12. **demographic** `高一` adj./n.

- **中文释义**：adj. 人口统计的 n. 人口统计数据
- **英文释义**：adj. relating to the structure of populations; n. a section of the population
- **例句**：
  - Demographic changes are affecting the housing market.
  - 人口结构变化正在影响房地产市场。
- **助记**：demo（人民）+ graphic（图表的）→ 关于人民的图表数据 → 人口统计的

### 13. **facilitate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：促进；使容易；使便利
- **英文释义**：to make an action or process easier
- **例句**：
  - Technology can facilitate communication between people.
  - 科技可以促进人们之间的交流。
- **助记**：fac（做）+ il（容易）+ itate → 使做起来容易 → 促进

### 14. **intermediary** `高一` n./adj.

- **中文释义**：n. 中间人；调解人 adj. 中间的
- **英文释义**：n. a person who acts as a link between people to assist negotiations; adj. intermediate
- **例句**：
  - A lawyer served as an intermediary between the two parties.
  - 一名律师充当了双方之间的调解人。
- **助记**：inter（之间）+ medi（中间）+ ary → 在中间的人 → 中间人

### 15. **perpetuate** `高一` v.

- **中文释义**：使永久化；使持续
- **英文释义**：to make something continue indefinitely
- **例句**：
  - We should not perpetuate harmful stereotypes.
  - 我们不应该使有害的刻板印象持续下去。
- **助记**：per（始终）+ petu（追求）+ ate → 始终追求延续 → 使永久化
