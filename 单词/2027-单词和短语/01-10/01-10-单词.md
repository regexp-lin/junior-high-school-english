## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **convince** /kənˈvɪns/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：说服；使确信
- **英文释义**：to make someone believe that something is true; to persuade
- **例句**：
  - She convinced her parents to let her study abroad.
  - 她说服了父母让她出国留学。
  - I'm convinced that he is telling the truth.
  - 我确信他说的是实话。
- **助记**：con-（完全）+ vince（征服）→ 完全征服对方的想法 → 说服

### 2. **cooperate** /kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：合作；配合
- **英文释义**：to work together with someone to achieve a common goal
- **例句**：
  - The two schools cooperated on the science project.
  - 两所学校在科学项目上进行了合作。
  - Please cooperate with the police during the investigation.
  - 调查期间请配合警方。
- **助记**：co-（共同）+ operate（操作）→ 一起操作 → 合作

### 3. **correspond** /ˌkɒrɪˈspɒnd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：通信；符合
- **英文释义**：to communicate by letters or email; to be similar or equivalent
- **例句**：
  - They corresponded with each other for years.
  - 他们通信多年。
  - The results do not correspond with our expectations.
  - 结果与我们的预期不符。
- **助记**：cor-（共同）+ respond（回应）→ 互相回应 → 通信；对应

### 4. **corrupt** /kəˈrʌpt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./v.
- **中文释义**：腐败的；使堕落
- **英文释义**：dishonest and willing to use power for personal gain; to cause moral decline
- **例句**：
  - The corrupt official was finally brought to justice.
  - 腐败的官员终于被绳之以法。
  - Power tends to corrupt, and absolute power corrupts absolutely.
  - 权力容易导致腐败，绝对的权力导致绝对的腐败。
- **助记**：cor-（完全）+ rupt（破裂）→ 完全破裂 → 腐败

### 5. **crucial** /ˈkruːʃl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：关键的；至关重要的
- **英文释义**：of great importance; decisive
- **例句**：
  - This is a crucial moment in the company's history.
  - 这是公司历史上的关键时刻。
  - Good communication is crucial to a successful team.
  - 良好的沟通对成功的团队至关重要。
- **助记**：cruc-（十字架）+ -ial → 站在十字路口的关键抉择 → 关键的

### 6. **cultivate** /ˈkʌltɪveɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：培养；耕种
- **英文释义**：to prepare and use land for growing crops; to develop a skill or quality
- **例句**：
  - Farmers cultivate rice in the southern provinces.
  - 南方省份的农民种植水稻。
  - Reading is a good way to cultivate your imagination.
  - 阅读是培养想象力的好方法。
- **助记**：cult（耕种/文化）+ -ivate → 耕种知识 → 培养

### 7. **currency** /ˈkʌrənsi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：货币；流通
- **英文释义**：the system of money in use in a particular country
- **例句**：
  - The currency of China is the yuan.
  - 中国的货币是人民币。
  - Foreign currency exchange rates change every day.
  - 外汇汇率每天都在变化。
- **助记**：curr（流通）+ -ency → 流通的东西 → 货币

### 8. **curiosity** /ˌkjʊəriˈɒsəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：好奇心；稀奇事物
- **英文释义**：a strong desire to know or learn something
- **例句**：
  - Children have a natural curiosity about the world.
  - 孩子们对世界有天生的好奇心。
  - The old house aroused her curiosity.
  - 那栋老房子引起了她的好奇。
- **助记**：curious（好奇的）+ -ity → 好奇的状态 → 好奇心

### 9. **deadline** /ˈdedlaɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：截止日期；最后期限
- **英文释义**：the latest time or date by which something should be completed
- **例句**：
  - The deadline for the essay is next Friday.
  - 论文的截止日期是下周五。
  - We must meet the deadline no matter what.
  - 无论如何我们必须在截止日期前完成。
- **助记**：dead（死）+ line（线）→ 越过这条线就"死"了 → 最后期限

### 10. **debate** /dɪˈbeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：辩论；讨论
- **英文释义**：a formal discussion on a particular topic; to argue about something
- **例句**：
  - The students held a debate about environmental protection.
  - 学生们就环保问题举行了辩论。
  - Scientists are still debating the cause of the disease.
  - 科学家们仍在讨论这种疾病的成因。
- **助记**：de-（向下）+ bate（打）→ 用言语互相"打击" → 辩论

### 高一词汇

### 11. **exemplary** /ɪɡˈzempləri/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：模范的；典范的
- **英文释义**：serving as a desirable model; representing the best of its kind
- **例句**：
  - Her exemplary behaviour earned her a scholarship.
  - 她模范的表现为她赢得了奖学金。
  - The school set an exemplary standard for other institutions.
  - 这所学校为其他机构树立了典范。
- **助记**：example（榜样）+ -ary → 像榜样一样的 → 模范的

### 12. **fastidious** /fæˈstɪdiəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：挑剔的；一丝不苟的
- **英文释义**：very attentive to accuracy and detail; hard to please
- **例句**：
  - She is fastidious about keeping her room clean.
  - 她对保持房间整洁非常挑剔。
  - The editor was fastidious in checking every word.
  - 编辑对每个字都一丝不苟地检查。
- **助记**：fastidi-（厌恶）+ -ous → 对不完美的事感到厌恶 → 挑剔的

### 13. **fathom** /ˈfæðəm/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：理解；弄清
- **英文释义**：to understand something after much thought
- **例句**：
  - I can't fathom why she made that decision.
  - 我无法理解她为什么做出那个决定。
  - The mystery was too deep to fathom.
  - 这个谜团太深奥，难以理解。
- **助记**：fathom 原义是"英寻"（测水深的单位）→ 测量深度 → 理解深层含义

### 14. **flagrant** /ˈfleɪɡrənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：公然的；明目张胆的
- **英文释义**：conspicuously or obviously offensive
- **例句**：
  - This is a flagrant violation of human rights.
  - 这是对人权的公然侵犯。
  - His flagrant disregard for the rules angered everyone.
  - 他对规则的公然无视激怒了所有人。
- **助记**：flagr-（燃烧）+ -ant → 像火一样明显 → 明目张胆的

### 15. **flourish** /ˈflʌrɪʃ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：繁荣；兴旺
- **英文释义**：to grow or develop in a healthy way; to be very successful
- **例句**：
  - The arts flourished during the Renaissance.
  - 文艺复兴时期艺术繁荣兴盛。
  - Children flourish when they feel safe and loved.
  - 孩子们在感到安全和被爱的环境中茁壮成长。
- **助记**：flour（花）+ -ish → 像花一样盛开 → 繁荣
