# 阅读理解（应用文） **Starry City Planetarium — Visit Guide for School Groups**

> **Starry City Planetarium**  
> **Visit Guide for School Groups 2027**  
> *"Come and catch a galaxy with us."*

---

### 1. Welcome

Welcome, young astronomers! The **Starry City Planetarium** is one of the biggest in eastern China. Every year, we invite more than **60,000 middle school students** to explore stars, planets, and space technology. This short guide will help your class plan a smooth and fun trip.

### 2. Opening Information

- **Open:** Tuesday – Sunday (closed on Mondays)  
- **Hours:** 9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. (last entry 16:00)  
- **Address:** 88 Blue Sky Road, Starry City  
- **Nearest metro:** Line 3, Space Park Station, Exit B

### 3. Ticket Prices (School Groups)

| Type                        | Price     | Notes                                |
|-----------------------------|-----------|--------------------------------------|
| Student                     | 20 yuan   | Must show student card               |
| Teacher (1 per 10 students) | Free      | Free ticket must be booked in advance|
| Extra teacher               | 40 yuan   |                                      |
| Space Dome Show             | 15 yuan   | Optional, about 35 minutes           |
| Guided Tour (English)       | 50 yuan per group | Group = up to 30 students    |

School groups must book **at least 7 days in advance** by phone or e-mail.

### 4. Main Galleries

1. **Earth & Moon Hall** — Interactive models of our planet and its only natural satellite.  
2. **Solar System Hall** — Walk through a 30-metre model of our sun and all planets.  
3. **Starry Sky Dome** — 360° show about seasons of the night sky in China.  
4. **Space Travel Gallery** — Models of rockets, the Chinese space station, and a moon rover.  
5. **Little Scientist Lab** — Hands-on activities for making small paper telescopes.

### 5. Rules Inside

- Please **keep your voices low**; other visitors are also here to enjoy quiet space.  
- **No food or drink** in the galleries. A small café is on Floor 1.  
- **No flash photos** in the Starry Sky Dome.  
- Do not run, climb, or touch the models marked with a red hand sign.  
- Lost items can be found at **Service Point A** on Floor 1.

### 6. Sample Half-Day Visit

| Time        | Activity                       |
|-------------|--------------------------------|
| 9:00–9:20   | Meet at South Gate, check in   |
| 9:20–10:20  | Earth & Moon Hall              |
| 10:20–11:20 | Solar System Hall              |
| 11:20–12:00 | Starry Sky Dome show           |
| 12:00–12:30 | Group photo & short quiz game  |

### 7. How to Book

- **Phone:** 021-6677-8899 (Ms Tang, School Service)  
- **E-mail:** school@starrycity.cn  
- **Online form:** www.starrycity.cn/schools/book  
- Please send the school name, class, number of students, visit date, and whether an English guide is needed.

### 8. A Small Gift

Every student will receive a **free "Space Passport"** with small stickers for each hall they visit. Classes that finish a full stamp collection can also join our **monthly prize draw** (one class gets a free second visit).

**We look forward to seeing you — under the stars.**

**Starry City Planetarium — School Service Team**
