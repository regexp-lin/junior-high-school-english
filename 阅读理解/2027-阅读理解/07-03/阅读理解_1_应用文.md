# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Summer Reading Passport" — City Library Programme**

**CITY PUBLIC LIBRARY · YOUTH SECTION**
*Open the cover. Open the world.*

---

**Event Flyer — July 2027**

The City Public Library is excited to present our annual **"Summer Reading Passport"** programme for middle-school students. Each student receives a small paper passport, with space for ten reading stamps. Every stamp is a door into a different kind of book — and a step toward a free, end-of-summer surprise.

**Who may join?**

- All students from Grade 6 to Grade 9
- Valid library card (free to apply with your school ID if you don't have one)

**When?**

- **Open date:** Wednesday, 7 July 2027
- **Closing date:** Saturday, 28 August 2027 (passports must be returned by 17:00)

**Where?**

- Main building: 2F — *Youth Books Room*
- Branch: 1F — *Quick-Read Corner*, Community Library, Green Road

**How does the passport work?**

1. Pick up a passport at the Youth Section front desk.
2. Choose any book from the "Summer Selection" shelves (about 300 titles, marked with a bright yellow sticker).
3. Read the book.
4. Write 3 – 5 short sentences in your passport about the book.
5. Show the passport to a librarian. After a quick chat, the librarian stamps the page.
6. Collect stamps from different genres — each genre may give only ONE stamp.

**Book genres and their stamps**

| Genre | Example | Stamp colour |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Adventure | "Treasure on Blue Island" | Red |
| Biography | "The Girl Who Counted Stars" | Yellow |
| Science | "How Your Body Wakes Up" | Green |
| History | "Bridges of Old China" | Blue |
| Nature | "A Forest Year" | Brown |
| Poetry | "Short Words, Big Sky" | Pink |
| Drama | "The Play About the Lost Cat" | Purple |
| Mystery | "The Clock That Lied" | Black |
| Sport | "From Bench to Finish Line" | Orange |
| Art & Music | "The Red Violin Dream" | Grey |

**Rewards (according to stamps collected)**

| Stamps | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 3 stamps | A library pen and a paper bookmark |
| 6 stamps | A free cotton bookshelf bag with the library logo |
| 10 stamps | An invitation to the 30 August **Writers' Afternoon** (meet two famous children's authors and enjoy free tea) |

**Reading tips from our librarians**

- Start with one book you already enjoy — build **momentum** before trying new genres.
- Read 20 minutes in the morning and 20 minutes before bed; this feels lighter than one long hour.
- Talk about the book with a friend or family member — speaking helps memory.
- It is OK to stop a book you truly dislike. Pick another one from the same shelf.

**House rules**

1. Each student may borrow two books at a time from the Summer Selection shelves.
2. Do not tear out the yellow sticker; it helps librarians find the next reader for the same book.
3. Please keep your voice low in reading rooms. Use the small study room for group talks.
4. No food or drink in the reading rooms; water in closed bottles is OK.
5. If you lose your passport, you may ask for a new one once. After that, a ¥5 fee is charged.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Open date... Closing date..."  
【解析】7 月 7 日至 8 月 28 日。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Write 3–5 short sentences... After a quick chat..."  
【解析】写短评+与馆员交谈。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"10 stamps — Writers' Afternoon"  
【解析】10 枚章。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"No food or drink in the reading rooms"  
【解析】禁食。A/B/D 均允许。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"After that, a ¥5 fee is charged"  
【解析】第二次补办收 5 元。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。
