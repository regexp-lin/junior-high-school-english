# 阅读理解（应用文） **International Culture Week — School Poster**

① Hello everyone! From **Monday, 19 April** to **Friday, 23 April 2027**, Bright Future Middle School will hold its first **International Culture Week**. Each day, we will "visit" one country through music, food, games, and short lessons — without leaving the school gate. Come and see the world with us!

② **Daily Menu**

| Date | Country | Main Activity | Place | Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 19 April (Mon) | **China** | Opening ceremony + tea art show | School Hall | 9:30 – 10:20 |
| 20 April (Tue) | **Japan** | Origami workshop & short anime clips | Art Room 201 | 14:00 – 15:00 |
| 21 April (Wed) | **France** | French greetings class + mini bakery | Music Room 305 | 14:00 – 15:00 |
| 22 April (Thu) | **Kenya** | African drum & friendship dance | Gym, North Hall | 14:00 – 15:00 |
| 23 April (Fri) | **USA** | Quick English play contest + closing show | School Hall | 14:00 – 16:00 |

③ **"Food Corner" Every Day**

- **Lunch break 12:10 – 12:40** at the school canteen, east side.  
- Three small dishes from each country (10 yuan for a tasting set).  
- Please bring **your own cutlery** to help the earth; a small gift for every student who does.

④ **How to Join**

- No sign-up needed for the short shows at the school hall.  
- Workshops (origami, French greetings, African drum) need sign-up — scan the QR code on the bright blue poster outside Classroom 7-3 before **16 April**.  
- Each workshop takes **20 – 30 students** only. Come early.  
- Bring a small notebook to collect **country stamps** at each station.

⑤ **Special Tips**

1. Please speak **kindly** about every country. We are here to learn, not to laugh at others.  
2. For the Friday play contest, teams of **3 to 5** students perform a 3-minute scene in English. Sign up at the English teachers' office.  
3. Students whose family comes from another country or region are very welcome to share a short story. Tell Mr. Liu before 17 April.  
4. If it rains hard, the outdoor drum activity will move to the Multi-media Hall, Floor 2.  
5. No mobile phones on stage; photos are only allowed from the audience seats.

⑥ **Contact**  
- Ms. Chen, Head of English — cen.teacher@bfms.cn  
- Students' Union, Culture Group — Office Building, Room 108, 12:30 – 13:00

**Five days, five countries, countless friendships — see you next Monday!**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long will the International Culture Week last?**

A. One day.  
B. Three days.  
C. Five days.  
D. One whole month.

**2. What will happen on Wednesday, 21 April?**

A. A tea art show in the school hall.  
B. An origami workshop in Art Room 201.  
C. A French greetings class with a mini bakery.  
D. An English play contest in the school hall.

**3. What does the Food Corner encourage students to bring?**

A. A mobile phone for photos.  
B. Their own cutlery to help the earth.  
C. A reusable umbrella.  
D. A big plastic bag for food.

**4. Which activity needs a sign-up BEFORE 16 April?**

A. Watching the opening ceremony.  
B. Tasting food at the Food Corner.  
C. Joining the African drum workshop.  
D. Passing by the blue poster outside 7-3.

**5. What will happen to the outdoor African drum activity if there is heavy rain?**

A. It will be cancelled for the whole week.  
B. It will be moved to the Multi-media Hall on Floor 2.  
C. It will be changed into a written test.  
D. It will be held on a Saturday instead.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "From Monday, 19 April to Friday, 23 April"  
【解析】从周一到周五共 5 天。A、B、D 不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】表格定位题  
【定位】第②段 21 April (Wed) France 行  
【解析】对应法语问候课 + 迷你面包坊。A、B、D 为其他日期。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "bring your own cutlery to help the earth"  
【解析】自带餐具。A、C、D 无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Workshops... need sign-up... before 16 April"  
【解析】非洲鼓是 workshop，需报名。A 无需报名；B 是用餐；D 非活动。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段第 4 条 "moved to the Multi-media Hall, Floor 2"  
【解析】大雨改至二楼多媒体厅。A、C、D 无依据。故选 B。
