# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Are Mobile Phones Good for Students?**

① Mobile phones are everywhere. Today, even many primary school students have their own phones. Some people think phones are helpful for students, while others believe they do more harm than good. So, are mobile phones good or bad for students?

② Supporters say mobile phones are useful tools. Students can use them to search for information quickly. If they don't understand a word in English, they can look it up in seconds. Phones also help students stay in touch with their parents. After school, a quick call or message can let parents know they are safe. Some educational apps can even make learning more fun and interesting.

③ On the other hand, many teachers and parents worry about the bad effects. The biggest problem is distraction. Students may play games, watch videos, or chat with friends during class. This hurts their grades. Studies show that students who use phones in class score lower on tests. Another problem is health. Staring at a small screen for hours can damage eyesight. Some students stay up late at night playing on their phones, which affects their sleep and energy the next day.

④ There is also the problem of online safety. Young students may not know how to protect their personal information. They might talk to strangers or see harmful content. Cyberbullying is another growing concern. Some students are bullied through social media or messaging apps, and this can cause serious emotional harm.

⑤ Perhaps the answer is not to ban phones completely, but to teach students how to use them wisely. Schools can set clear rules — for example, phones must be turned off during class. Parents can set time limits at home. Most importantly, students should learn self-control. A phone is just a tool. It depends on how you use it.

**注释：** distraction 分心；eyesight 视力；cyberbullying 网络欺凌；self-control 自制力

**Questions:**

1. What is one advantage of mobile phones for students?
   A. They can play games all day.
   B. They can search for information quickly and stay in touch with parents.
   C. They don't need to go to school.
   D. They can avoid talking to teachers.

2. What is the biggest problem of using phones in class according to the passage?
   A. Phones are too expensive.
   B. Distraction — students may play games or chat instead of studying.
   C. Phones break easily.
   D. Teachers don't know how to use phones.

3. How can phone use affect students' health?
   A. It makes them taller.
   B. Staring at screens can damage eyesight, and staying up late affects sleep.
   C. It gives them more energy.
   D. It makes them stronger.

4. What does the writer suggest as the best approach?
   A. Ban phones completely.
   B. Let students use phones freely without rules.
   C. Teach students to use phones wisely, with rules and self-control.
   D. Give phones only to teachers.

5. What does the underlined word "concern" in Paragraph ④ most likely mean?
   A. happiness
   B. joke
   C. worry
   D. game

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Students can use them to search for information quickly." 和 "Phones also help students stay in touch with their parents."

**【解析】** 手机的优点包括快速查找信息和与家长保持联系。A 项整天玩游戏、C 项不用上学、D 项避开老师均非手机优点。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The biggest problem is distraction. Students may play games, watch videos, or chat with friends during class."

**【解析】** 最大问题是分心，学生可能在课堂上玩游戏或聊天。A 项太贵、C 项容易坏、D 项老师不会用均非文中所述最大问题。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Staring at a small screen for hours can damage eyesight. Some students stay up late at night playing on their phones, which affects their sleep."

**【解析】** 长时间盯屏幕损害视力，熬夜玩手机影响睡眠。A 项变高、C 项更有精力、D 项更强壮均与原文相反。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Perhaps the answer is not to ban phones completely, but to teach students how to use them wisely." 和 "students should learn self-control."

**【解析】** 作者建议教学生明智使用手机，并培养自制力，学校和家长可制定规则。A 项完全禁止、B 项无限制使用均过于极端；D 项只给老师不切实际。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "Cyberbullying is another growing concern."

**【解析】** 上文讨论的都是负面问题——网络安全和网络欺凌，因此 "concern" 意为"担忧"。A 项快乐、B 项笑话、D 项游戏均与上下文语境不符。故选 C。
