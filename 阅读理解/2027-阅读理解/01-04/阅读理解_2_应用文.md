# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Greenfield Public Library — January Events**

① Welcome to Greenfield Public Library! Here are our special events for January. All events are free and open to the public.

---

| **Event** | **Date** | **Time** | **Place** | **For** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Story Time for Kids | Every Saturday | 10:00–11:00 | Children's Room | Ages 4–8 |
| Teen Book Club | Jan 10 & Jan 24 | 15:30–17:00 | Meeting Room A | Ages 12–16 |
| Science Talk: Stars and Planets | Jan 15 | 19:00–20:30 | Lecture Hall | All ages |
| Art Workshop: Winter Painting | Jan 18 | 14:00–16:00 | Art Studio | Ages 10–15 |
| Movie Night: "The Little Prince" | Jan 25 | 18:30–20:00 | Lecture Hall | All ages |

---

② **Teen Book Club:** This month we are reading *"Wonder"* by R.J. Palacio. Join us to share your thoughts and meet other young readers. Snacks will be provided!

③ **Science Talk:** Professor Li from City University will give a talk about the solar system. Learn about stars, planets, and the latest space discoveries. You can ask questions at the end.

④ **Art Workshop:** Local artist Ms. Huang will teach you how to paint beautiful winter scenes. All materials are provided. Please sign up at the front desk before January 16. Spaces are limited to 20 students.

⑤ **Contact us:**
- Address: 120 Zhongshan Road
- Phone: 021-3456-7890
- Website: www.greenfieldlibrary.org

---

**Questions:**

1. How often does Story Time for Kids take place in January?
   A. Once a month.
   B. Every Saturday.
   C. Every day.
   D. Twice a month.

2. What book is the Teen Book Club reading this month?
   A. "The Little Prince."
   B. "Harry Potter."
   C. "Wonder" by R.J. Palacio.
   D. "Robinson Crusoe."

3. A 13-year-old student wants to attend both the Teen Book Club and the Art Workshop. When should he go?
   A. Jan 10 and Jan 15.
   B. Jan 10 (or Jan 24) and Jan 18.
   C. Jan 18 and Jan 25.
   D. Every Saturday and Jan 15.

4. What should you do if you want to join the Art Workshop?
   A. Buy a ticket online.
   B. Sign up at the front desk before January 16.
   C. Call Professor Li.
   D. Come directly without signing up.

5. Which event can a family of all ages attend together on a weekend evening?
   A. Teen Book Club.
   B. Art Workshop.
   C. Movie Night: "The Little Prince" on Jan 25.
   D. Story Time for Kids.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格中 Story Time for Kids 的日期为 "Every Saturday"。

**【解析】** 儿童故事时间每个周六都举行。A 项每月一次、C 项每天、D 项每月两次均与"每周六"不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "This month we are reading 'Wonder' by R.J. Palacio."

**【解析】** 本月青少年读书俱乐部读的是 R.J. Palacio 的《Wonder》。A 项是电影之夜的影片；B 项和 D 项均未提及。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 表格中 Teen Book Club 的日期为 "Jan 10 & Jan 24"，Art Workshop 的日期为 "Jan 18"。两者年龄要求均包含 13 岁。

**【解析】** 13 岁学生可参加 1月10日或24日的读书俱乐部和1月18日的艺术工坊。A 项 Jan 15 是科学讲座；C 项缺少读书俱乐部；D 项包含故事时间（4-8岁）不适合。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Please sign up at the front desk before January 16. Spaces are limited to 20 students."

**【解析】** 需要在1月16日之前到前台报名。A 项网上购票、C 项联系李教授、D 项不用报名均不正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 表格中 Movie Night 的日期为 "Jan 25"（周六），时间 "18:30–20:00"（晚上），适用人群 "All ages"。

**【解析】** 1月25日周六晚上的电影之夜对所有年龄开放，适合全家一起参加。A 项读书俱乐部限 12-16 岁；B 项艺术工坊限 10-15 岁且在下午；D 项故事时间限 4-8 岁。故选 C。
