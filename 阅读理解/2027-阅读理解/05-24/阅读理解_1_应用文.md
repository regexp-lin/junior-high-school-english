# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Through My Eyes" — Student Photography Contest 2027**

**"THROUGH MY EYES" · STUDENT PHOTOGRAPHY CONTEST 2027**
*Organized by Sunshine Middle School Art Club & City Museum of Photography*

---

**Theme of the year**
**"A Quiet Corner of My City"**

We invite students to find one small, usually-overlooked place in their city — a tree by the back wall, an old bus seat, the sky above a factory roof, a pair of shoes at the door — and to tell its story in a single photograph.

**Who can enter?**
- All middle school students (Grade 7 – Grade 9) in our city.
- **One** student may send **up to 2** different photos.
- Group photos may be submitted by 2 students **together**, but each student may join only one group.

**Entry deadlines and dates**

| Stage | Date |
| --- | --- |
| Open for entries | **Monday 1 June, 2027** |
| Deadline for entries | **Sunday 28 June, 2027, 23:59** |
| Judges' review | 29 June – 7 July |
| Winners announced | **Friday 9 July, 2027** on the school website |
| Prize ceremony & exhibition | **Saturday 17 July, 14:00**, City Museum of Photography |

**How to send your photo**

1. Use any camera — a phone, a digital camera or a film camera.
2. Save each photo as a **JPEG** file, **no larger than 10 MB**.
3. Name the file like this: *GradeClass_Name_Title.jpg* (for example, "8B_WangHao_QuietWindow.jpg").
4. Send the file(s) to **photo2027@sunshineschool.cn** with a short note (2 – 3 sentences) about why you chose that corner.
5. Photos may be in **colour or black-and-white**. Basic brightness and contrast changes are allowed. Heavy editing or AI-generated images are **not accepted**.

**Prizes**

- 1 × **First Prize**: a new digital camera + a private photography lesson with Mr. Chen Yu.
- 3 × **Second Prize**: a photo book set + printed A3 poster of your photo.
- 10 × **Third Prize**: a one-year free pass to the City Museum of Photography.
- All entrants will get a **digital certificate** and have their photo shown online.

**A few friendly reminders**

- **Safety first**: never climb onto roofs, railway lines or the edge of rivers to take a photo. Ask an adult before going into any quiet place alone.
- **Respect people**: if a person is clearly the subject of your picture, please ask for their permission.
- **Be honest**: the photo must be taken by you between **1 April and 28 June, 2027**.

**Questions?**
Write to **Art Club, Room 203** or email **artclub@sunshineschool.cn**.

*Show us your quiet corner — and teach us how to see our city again.*

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the theme of the photography contest in 2027?**

A. "Stars and the Night Sky."  
B. "A Quiet Corner of My City."

D. "Sports at My School."

**2. How many photos may one student send in?**

A. Only 1 photo.  
B. Up to 2 different photos.  
C. Up to 5 photos.  
D. There is no limit.

**3. When will the winners be announced?**

A. 1 June, 2027.  
B. 28 June, 2027.  
C. 9 July, 2027.  
D. 17 July, 2027.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed?**

A. Using a phone camera.  
B. Making basic brightness and contrast changes.  
C. Sending black-and-white photos.  
D. Sending an AI-generated image.

**5. What does the leaflet ask students to do for safety?**

A. Climb onto roofs to find the best view.  
B. Stand at the edge of a river alone.

D. Take photos inside a running car.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Theme of the year" 部分  
【解析】2027 主题为 "A Quiet Corner of My City"。A/C/D 均不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Who can enter?" 部分 "One student may send up to 2 different photos."  
【解析】每人最多 2 张。A/C/D 均不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Winners announced · Friday 9 July, 2027"  
【解析】A 为开启报名；B 为截止；D 为颁奖礼。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Heavy editing or AI-generated images are not accepted."  
【解析】AI 图片被明确禁止。A/B/C 均允许。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"A few friendly reminders · Safety first"  
【解析】不要上屋顶、铁路线；独自去安静地方须先问大人。A/B/D 与原文相反。故选 C。
