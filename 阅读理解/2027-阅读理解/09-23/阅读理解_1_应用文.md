# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 Autumn Reading Month "One Book a Week"**

---

> **Lihua Middle School — School Library & Chinese Department**
> **Campus Reading Month Notice**
> Issued: **20th September 2027**

**Dear students,**

Cool air and warm tea make October the best month of the year for reading. From **Thursday 1st October** to **Saturday 31st October 2027**, the school library and the Chinese Department will hold our **Autumn Reading Month**, with this year's theme — **"One Book a Week"**. Every Grade-7-to-Grade-9 student is invited to join.

The challenge is simple: **finish four books in October**, leave short reading notes, and take part in at least one public reading event.

---

## Part 1 — Main Activities

| Week | Date | Event | Place |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Week 1 | 1 – 4 Oct | "Meet the Author" talk with local writer **Ms Lin Xue** | Main Hall, Sun 4 Oct, 19:00 |
| Week 2 | 5 – 11 Oct | **English short-story reading corner** (5 authors, 5 evenings) | Library Reading Room, 17:00 – 18:00 |
| Week 3 | 12 – 18 Oct | **Class book-sharing fair** — each class picks one book to share, 10 min per class | Open-air stage, Wed 14 Oct, 15:30 |
| Week 4 | 19 – 25 Oct | **Bilingual (Chinese + English) essay contest** "My Favourite Chapter" | Essays due Fri 23 Oct |
| Week 5 | 26 – 31 Oct | **Final "Night of Reading"** — 200 candles, 50 short readings from students | Main Hall, Fri 30 Oct, 18:30 |

> **Note:** Events marked in bold are part of the "One Book a Week" Challenge; joining **any three** earns you a small **"Autumn Reader" badge**.

---

## Part 2 — How to Join the "One Book a Week" Challenge

1. **Pick your four books** — at least one must be in **English**, and at least one must be a **Chinese classic** from the school's recommended list.
2. **Borrow up to 3 books at a time** from the school library. The library will open **one extra hour**, until **19:00**, every weekday of October.
3. **Write a short reading note** (50 – 150 words in Chinese, or 80 – 120 words in English) for each book; use the **green reading card** handed out by your Chinese teacher.
4. **Hand in your four reading notes** by **Monday 2nd November** to your class reading captain.
5. Students who finish all four books **and** attend at least one public event will receive a **"Year-2027 Autumn Reader"** certificate.

---

## Part 3 — Recommended Reading List

### Chinese Classics (pick at least one)

- *Border Town* (《边城》) — Shen Congwen
- *Ordinary World* (《平凡的世界》, shortened youth edition) — Lu Yao
- *The Scholars* (《儒林外史》, student edition)
- Poems of Li Bai (with notes)

### English Readers (pick at least one)

- *Charlotte's Web* — E. B. White (level A2)
- *The Little Prince* (bilingual) — Saint-Exupéry (level A2)
- *Tuesdays with Morrie* (simplified) — Mitch Albom (level B1)
- *Anne of Green Gables* (adapted) — L. M. Montgomery (level B1)

### Free choice (two more books)

Any book from the library or the school's approved reading list is welcome; **comic magazines only** do not count.

---

## Part 4 — Rules and Reminders

- **Do not bring food or coloured drinks** into the Reading Room. A water bottle with a cap is fine.
- **Mobile phones** must be set to **silent** in the library; taking photos of other readers' faces is forbidden.
- Borrowed books must be returned on time. For each **late day** from November, a reminder will be posted, but no fine will be charged if the book is returned before **Friday 13th November**.
- The **"Night of Reading"** on 30 Oct starts at 18:30. Students wishing to read aloud must sign up by Monday 26 Oct (Room 208) and rehearse briefly with Teacher Song.

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When does the 2027 Autumn Reading Month run, and what is this year's theme?**

A. From 1st to 31st October 2027, with the theme "One Book a Week".  
B. From 1st to 15th October 2027, with the theme "A Book a Day".  
C. Only on the evening of 30th October 2027.  
D. Every weekend in September 2027.

B. Students must pick four books — at least one English book and at least one Chinese classic from the recommended list.  
C. Students can borrow only one book at a time.

**3. A student wants to choose **Charlotte's Web** as his English reader. Will this count?**

A. No — it is not on the recommended list.

A. A water bottle with a cap.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】正文 "from Thursday 1st October to Saturday 31st October 2027 ... 'One Book a Week'"  
【解析】活动时间与主题一致。B、C、D 均错。故选 A。

**2.**
