# 阅读理解（应用文） **Mother's Day Family Workshop at City Library**

> **CITY CENTRAL LIBRARY**  
> **"Warm Words for Mum" — Mother's Day Family Workshop**  
> *Sunday, 10 May 2027 · 09:30–16:00 · Third Floor Activity Hall*

---

Dear students and parents,

To celebrate Mother's Day, the **City Central Library** welcomes families to a **free full-day workshop** on Sunday, 10 May 2027. The day is full of small but meaningful activities, designed to help children say thank-you to the most important women in their lives — mothers, stepmothers, grandmothers and kind aunties. No sign-up fee. All materials are provided.

### Schedule

| Time | Activity | Room |
|------|----------|------|
| 09:30–10:15 | Welcome tea & story circle: *"My Mother, My First Teacher"* | Hall A |
| 10:30–11:30 | **Poem-Writing Workshop**: a famous poet guides you to write a short poem for Mum | Hall A |
| 11:45–12:45 | **Bookmark Making**: paint and decorate a paper bookmark | Hall B |
| 12:45–13:45 | Free lunch break (bring your own, or buy at café, 1F) | — |
| 13:45–14:30 | **"Five Little Thanks" Card Design** | Hall B |
| 14:45–15:30 | Photo corner: free family photos with flowers | Hall C |
| 15:30–16:00 | Closing circle & small gift for every mother | Hall A |

### Sign-Up

- **Online:** www.citylib.cn/mothersday2027  
- **On site:** Info Desk, 1F, City Central Library  
- **Phone:** 020-6677-8899 (during library open hours)  
- **Deadline:** Friday 8 May, 17:00. Seats: **120 families** only.

### What to Bring

- A pen or pencil and a small photo of your mother (for the card).  
- A **flower** (one stem is enough) — we will arrange them together at 14:45.  
- An empty water bottle (free hot water provided).  
- A smile ☺ (all materials such as paper, crayons, paint, ribbons and cards will be supplied).

### Important Notes

1. Workshops are open to students aged **9–16** and their mothers or female guardians.  
2. One parent may attend with up to **two** children.  
3. The library is **quiet area**; please speak in low voices outside the activity halls.  
4. Photographs taken by library staff may be used on the library's official WeChat account. If you prefer not to appear, tell the photographer at the door.  
5. No food in reading areas; please enjoy snacks only in the café or 3F activity hall.

### Small Story Contest

Between 10 May and 31 May, every child who attended the workshop may post a short story (100–300 words in Chinese, or 60–150 words in English) titled *"One Afternoon with Mum"* on the library website. The ten best stories will be printed in the library's May newsletter, and each author will receive a new book.

**Come together. Write a warm word. Take home a shared memory.**

— City Central Library, Youth Services Team

**根据通告内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the workshop MAINLY for?**

A. To celebrate Teachers' Day.  
B. To help children express thanks to mothers and other caring women on Mother's Day.  
C. To sell flowers inside the library.  
D. To collect used books from families.

**2. At what time will families make paper bookmarks?**

A. 09:30–10:15.  
B. 11:45–12:45.  
C. 13:45–14:30.  
D. 15:30–16:00.

**3. Which of the following is TRUE according to the notice?**

A. Families must pay 50 yuan to join.  
B. One parent may attend with up to two children.  
C. Crayons and ribbons must be brought by students themselves.  
D. Photos taken by the library may NEVER be used online.

**4. What should a child do if he or she wants to join the small story contest?**

A. Post a short story titled "One Afternoon with Mum" on the library website.  
B. Send a long article of 2,000 English words to a newspaper.  
C. Record a video and upload it to social media.  
D. Bring an essay to the Hall C photo corner.

**5. Who may join the workshop as a student?**

A. A boy aged 5 with his father only.  
B. A girl aged 14 with her mother.  
C. A university student aged 20.  
D. A working adult without any children.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】开头 "help children say thank-you to the most important women in their lives..."  
【解析】通告目的是让孩子在母亲节向妈妈、外婆、姨妈等表达感谢。A 教师节、C 卖花、D 捐书均与原文不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Schedule 表 11:45–12:45 "Bookmark Making"  
【解析】手工书签活动在 11:45–12:45。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Important Notes 第2条 "One parent may attend with up to two children."  
【解析】每位家长最多可带 2 个孩子。A 活动免费，C 蜡笔等由主办方提供，D 照片可能用于官方微信（家长可选择不出镜）。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Small Story Contest "post a short story... titled 'One Afternoon with Mum' on the library website."  
【解析】需在图书馆官网发表同名短故事。B 字数过多、C 视频、D 交到照相角均不符规则。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Important Notes 第1条 "students aged 9–16 and their mothers or female guardians."  
【解析】学生年龄限制 9–16 岁且需与妈妈/女性监护人同来。A 年龄过低且带的是父亲、C 年龄过大、D 无孩子均不符合。故选 B。
