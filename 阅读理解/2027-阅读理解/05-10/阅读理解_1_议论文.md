# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should Every Classroom Have Living Plants?**

① Walk into almost any school in the world and you will find whiteboards, lockers, posters and desks. Yet one simple thing is still missing from many Chinese middle school classrooms — a few **living plants** in the corner or on the windowsill. Some principals say, "Plants are nice, but the classroom is for learning, not for gardening." I disagree. I believe every classroom should have at least a few living plants, and the benefits go far beyond decoration.

② First, green plants help make the air **fresher**. A classroom of forty students sits together for many hours every day. Students breathe out carbon dioxide, bring in dust from the playground, and sometimes the smell of lunch boxes. Green leaves take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen. Several school experiments have shown that classes with a few small plants also feel slightly cooler and less stuffy on warm afternoons.

③ Second, plants help reduce stress and keep eyes healthy. When students cannot solve a maths problem or worry about a test, looking at something green for a few seconds lowers the heart rate. In fact, doctors recommend that teenagers look at the colour green to **rest their eyes** every 40 minutes of reading. A small pot of green leaves at the back of the room is a cheap, silent eye hospital.

④ Third, taking care of plants is a quiet lesson in **responsibility and patience**. A duty rota can be made in every class: two students each week water, check the soil, and turn the pot towards the sun. Students learn that plants, like friendships, do not grow overnight. Skipping one week can turn a strong green leaf into a sad yellow one. This gentle lesson is hard to teach with a textbook.

⑤ Some teachers worry about **allergies**, spilled water, and plant insects. These concerns are fair, and we should listen to them. The answer is not "no plants at all", but "the right plants". Cactus, small snake plants, spider plants, and pothos grow slowly, need little water, and rarely cause allergies. Heavy flower pots, sharp-leafed plants, or plants that smell too strongly can be avoided on purpose.

⑥ In short, a few small plants will not turn a classroom into a forest, but they can turn a grey corner into a gentle friend. Fresh air, rested eyes, and a daily lesson in care cost very little. I hope our school will soon add two or three small green friends to every classroom — and watch both students and plants grow stronger together.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the writer's main opinion?**

A. Plants belong only in gardens, not in classrooms.  
B. Every classroom should have at least a few living plants.  
C. Classrooms should be covered with flowers from floor to ceiling.  
D. Students should grow vegetables in every classroom.

**2. According to paragraph ②, how do green plants help the classroom air?**

A. They heat up the room.  
B. They take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen.  
C. They bring in more dust from the playground.  
D. They make food smells stronger.

**3. Why do doctors recommend looking at green every 40 minutes?**

A. To make students feel sleepy.  
B. To rest their eyes and lower stress.  
C. To help students run faster.  
D. To remember more phone numbers.

**4. What does the writer suggest as an answer to teachers' worries about allergies and insects?**

A. Banning all plants from school.  
B. Choosing the right kind of plants, such as cactus, snake plants, spider plants and pothos.  
C. Removing all windows.  
D. Spraying very strong chemicals every day.

**5. What is the writer's final hope?**

A. Schools will build a large greenhouse on the roof.  
B. Students will work in flower shops after school.  
C. Schools will soon add two or three small green friends to every classroom.  
D. Plants will replace all classroom books.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段末句 "I believe every classroom should have at least a few living plants..."  
【解析】作者主张每间教室都应有几盆植物。A/C/D 均与原文相反或过度。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段第4-5句 "Green leaves take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen."  
【解析】叶片吸收二氧化碳、释放氧气。A/C/D 均与原文相反。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段末 "doctors recommend that teenagers look at the colour green to rest their eyes..."  
【解析】医生建议用绿色让眼睛休息，也能降低压力。A/C/D 均无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "the answer is not 'no plants at all', but 'the right plants'"  
【解析】作者建议选对的植物。A 全部禁种、C 拆窗、D 使用强化学药都与原文相反。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨归纳题  
【定位】第⑥段倒数第二句 "add two or three small green friends to every classroom"  
【解析】作者希望每间教室添几盆绿植。A/B/D 均超出原意。故选 C。
