# 阅读理解（应用文） **English Drama Club — Open Auditions for *The Little Prince***

---

**Silver Moon Middle School · English Drama Club**  
*Step into the light. Speak your English loud and proud.*

---

**1. The Show**

This year, our English Drama Club will perform **"The Little Prince"** (adapted from the famous book by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry) for the **School Art Festival** in June. The show is in **full English** and will run for about **45 minutes**.

---

**2. Roles Open for Audition**

| Role | Short Description | Number |
|------|------------------|-------|
| The Little Prince | A curious, kind boy traveller — main role | 1 |
| The Pilot | A lonely pilot lost in the desert | 1 |
| The Fox | Teaches the Little Prince about friendship | 1 |
| The Rose | Proud but caring flower | 1 |
| The Businessman / The Lamplighter / The King | Funny grown-up characters | 3 |
| Stars & Desert Chorus | Background singers, no spoken lines | 8–10 |

---

**3. Audition Details**

- **When:** Saturday, 17 April, 2:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m.  
- **Where:** Drama Room, 3F, Art Building  
- **Who can try:** Any student in Grade 7–9. No acting experience is needed.  
- **What to prepare:**  
  1. **A 1-minute English speech** — any topic you love.  
  2. A short **movement piece** (30 seconds) — walk, jump, or wave to show feelings.  
  3. If you want to sing, prepare one English song of your choice.

---

**4. Rehearsals**

- **Every Tuesday & Thursday**, 4:10 p.m. – 5:40 p.m., from 22 April.  
- Two **weekend full-day rehearsals** will take place on **22 May and 5 June**.  
- Students who miss **more than three** rehearsals without a reason may be replaced.

---

**5. Costumes & Fees**

- The club pays for **all costumes and stage props**. Actors do not need to buy clothes.  
- A small fee of **30 yuan** is collected to print nice programmes for the audience.  
- Low-income students may ask Teacher Rose for a waiver.

---

**6. How to Sign Up**

- Step 1: Fill in the online form before **Wednesday, 14 April, 6:00 p.m.**  
- Step 2: Choose up to **two** roles you like from the list.  
- Step 3: Tell us if you can also help with sound, lights, or costume making.  
- Step 4: Come to the Drama Room 10 minutes before your audition time. Please bring your student ID card.

---

> Contact: **drama@smms.edu.cn** · Room 308, Teacher Rose  
> *"It is only with the heart that one can speak rightly." — See you on the stage.*

---

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What play will the Drama Club perform this year?**

A. *Romeo and Juliet*.  
B. *The Little Prince*.  
C. *Alice in Wonderland*.  
D. *Peter Pan*.

**2. Which role is a background role WITHOUT spoken lines?**

A. The Fox.  
B. The Rose.  
C. Stars & Desert Chorus.  
D. The Pilot.

**3. Which of the following is NOT required at the audition?**

A. A 1-minute English speech.  
B. A short movement piece of 30 seconds.  
C. A short original script in Chinese.  
D. An English song (only if you want to sing).

**4. How often will the rehearsals be held during the weekdays?**

A. Only on Mondays.  
B. Every Tuesday and Thursday afternoon.  
C. Every single afternoon.  
D. Only on Friday evenings.

**5. Who can ask Teacher Rose for a waiver of the 30-yuan fee?**

A. Students who come late.  
B. Students who do not speak English well.  
C. Students from low-income families.  
D. Only the main actors.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第1节 "*The Little Prince*"  
【解析】今年剧目为《小王子》。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第2节 "Stars & Desert Chorus... no spoken lines"  
【解析】群众演员无台词。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第3节 "What to prepare"  
【解析】未要求中文原创剧本。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第4节 "Every Tuesday & Thursday"  
【解析】周二、周四排练。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第5节 "Low-income students may ask Teacher Rose for a waiver"  
【解析】低收入家庭可豁免。故选 C。
