# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 Autumn Science & Technology Festival "Young Minds, Big Questions"**

---

> **Lihua Middle School — Student Activities Centre**
> **Poster | Notice | Sign-up Form**
> Released: **8th September, 2027**

**Dear students of Lihua Middle School,**

Autumn is the season of new questions. This year our school will hold the **2027 Autumn Science & Technology Festival — "Young Minds, Big Questions"**, from **Monday 27th September** to **Saturday 2nd October**. Every student from **Grade 7 to Grade 9** is warmly invited to join.

---

## Part 1 — Programme at a Glance

| Day | Date | Main Event | Place | Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mon | 27 Sep | **Opening Ceremony** + Keynote Talk: "Why does science still matter?" | Main Hall | 15:30 – 17:00 |
| Tue | 28 Sep | **Science Show "Wow Lab"** (live experiments on stage) | Main Hall | 15:30 – 16:30 |
| Wed | 29 Sep | **Paper-Airplane Design Competition** | Playground | 15:00 – 17:00 |
| Thu | 30 Sep | **"Little Inventor" Project Fair** (student-made models) | Science Building, 1F lobby | 14:30 – 17:30 |
| Fri | 1 Oct | **Tech-&-Art Movie Night** (film: *Hidden Figures*, subtitled) | Lecture Hall B | 18:30 – 21:00 |
| Sat | 2 Oct | **Closing Ceremony + Awards** | Main Hall | 15:30 – 16:30 |

> **Note:** All activities are held **after regular classes**. Attendance is voluntary but **strongly encouraged**.

---

## Part 2 — Three Contests You Can Join

**1) Paper-Airplane Design Competition**
- **Who:** Individual or team of 2 (must be same grade)
- **Task:** Build a paper airplane using only one A4 sheet. Winner = **longest flight distance**.
- **Prize:** First ¥100 book voucher · Second ¥60 · Third ¥30

**2) "Little Inventor" Project Fair**
- **Who:** Team of **3–5 students**
- **Task:** Show a model that **solves a real daily-life problem** (e.g. a smart dustbin, a water-saving tap).
- **Prize:** Top 3 projects will be displayed in the school library for **one full term**.

**3) 60-Second Science Speech**
- **Who:** Individual, any grade
- **Task:** In **English or Chinese**, explain any scientific idea in **under 60 seconds**.
- **Prize:** Top 5 speakers will join the city-level "Young Voices in Science" contest in December.

---

## Part 3 — How to Sign Up

- Scan the **green QR code** on the poster outside the school library **before 23:59 on Friday 24th September**.
- Or put a paper form into the **blue wooden box** next to Room 207, Science Building.
- Students who need help with the form can visit Teacher Liu in Room 207 **every day from 16:00 to 17:00**.

> **Late sign-ups will not be accepted.** Please check your email on **Monday morning, 27th September** to confirm your time slot.

---

## Part 4 — Friendly Reminders

- Wear **comfortable shoes** on the Paper-Airplane day; the playground will be windy.
- For the Project Fair, **no open flame** and **no sharp tools** are allowed on the display tables.
- Parents are welcome at the **Closing Ceremony** on 2nd October (seats are limited, first come first seated).
- For any question, email **stc@lihuams.edu.cn** or call Teacher Liu at **010-8888-0927** (weekdays only).

---

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When will the 2027 Autumn Science & Technology Festival take place?**

A. From Monday 20th September to Friday 24th September.  
B. From Monday 27th September to Saturday 2nd October.  
C. Only on Saturday 2nd October.  
D. From Monday 27th September to Friday 1st October.

**2. A Grade-8 student wants to work with four classmates to build a model of a smart water-saving tap. Which event should they join?**

A. Paper-Airplane Design Competition.  
B. Science Show "Wow Lab".  
C. "Little Inventor" Project Fair.  
D. 60-Second Science Speech.

**3. Which of the following statements is TRUE according to the notice?**

A. Activities are held during regular class hours.  
B. A student can sign up by putting a paper form into a blue wooden box next to Room 207.  
C. Late sign-ups will still be accepted if you ask nicely.  
D. Parents may enter the Project Fair but not the Closing Ceremony.

**4. What will happen to the top 3 projects at the "Little Inventor" Project Fair?**

A. They will be sold to students at a low price.  
B. They will be shown on national TV.  
C. They will be displayed in the school library for one full term.  
D. They will be sent to the city museum forever.

**5. If a student needs help filling in the sign-up form, what should they do?**

A. Call the headmaster at any time of day.  
B. Go to Teacher Liu in Room 207 every day between 16:00 and 17:00.  
C. Wait until the closing ceremony on 2nd October.  
D. Send a letter to the Student Activities Centre by post.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】通知正文 "from **Monday 27th September** to **Saturday 2nd October**"  
【解析】活动时间为 9 月 27 日至 10 月 2 日。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 2 第 2 项 "'Little Inventor' Project Fair ... Team of 3–5 students ... smart dustbin, a water-saving tap"  
【解析】5 人一组做生活问题模型正对应发明展。其他活动人数或内容不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 3 第 2 项 "put a paper form into the blue wooden box next to Room 207"  
【解析】可放纸质表格到 207 室旁蓝色木箱。A 错：活动均放学后进行；C 错：迟到报名不受理；D 错：家长可参加闭幕式。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 2 第 2 项 "Top 3 projects will be displayed in the school library for one full term"  
【解析】前 3 名将在图书馆展示一整学期。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 3 第 3 条 "visit Teacher Liu in Room 207 every day from 16:00 to 17:00"  
【解析】需帮助者可在下午 4-5 点到 207 室找刘老师。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。
