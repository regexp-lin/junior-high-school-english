# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**A New Year's Promise**

① It was the last day of December. Li Wei sat by the window and watched the snow fall outside. His mother was cooking in the kitchen, and the whole house smelled of dumplings. Li Wei held a small notebook in his hand. On the first page, he had written: "My New Year's Promises."

② Last year, Li Wei had made three promises to himself. He wanted to read twenty books, learn to play the guitar, and help his grandmother more often. But when he looked back, he felt a little sad. He had only read eight books. He had practised the guitar for two weeks and then stopped. He did visit his grandmother, but only during holidays. "I didn't try hard enough," he thought.

③ His father came and sat beside him. "Writing new promises?" he asked. Li Wei nodded but looked unhappy. "I always fail," he said. His father smiled. "You didn't fail. You read eight books — that's still wonderful. And your grandmother was so happy every time you visited. The point is not to be perfect. The point is to keep trying."

④ Li Wei thought about his father's words. Maybe he was right. He picked up his pen and wrote: "Read fifteen books. Practise guitar every weekend. Call Grandma once a week." This time, the goals felt more real. They were not too big, and he believed he could do them.

⑤ At midnight, fireworks lit up the sky. Li Wei stood at the window with his family. "Happy New Year!" they said together. Li Wei held his notebook close. This year, he would not aim for perfect. He would aim for better. And that, he thought, was a very good start.

**注释：** promise 承诺；aim for 以……为目标

---

**Questions:**

1. What was Li Wei doing by the window?
   A. Playing the guitar.
   B. Writing his New Year's promises in a notebook.
   C. Talking to his grandmother on the phone.
   D. Reading a storybook.

2. How many books did Li Wei read last year?
   A. Twenty.
   B. Fifteen.
   C. Eight.
   D. None.

3. Why did Li Wei feel unhappy about last year?
   A. He did not celebrate New Year.
   B. He felt he did not try hard enough to keep his promises.
   C. His father was angry with him.
   D. He lost his notebook.

4. What did Li Wei's father mean by saying "The point is to keep trying"?
   A. Li Wei should give up making promises.
   B. It is more important to keep working toward goals than to be perfect.
   C. Li Wei should only make one promise.
   D. Promises are not important at all.

5. What can we learn from the story?
   A. We should never make New Year's promises.
   B. Setting realistic goals and keeping trying is more important than being perfect.
   C. Li Wei does not love his grandmother.
   D. Li Wei's father was disappointed in him.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Li Wei held a small notebook in his hand. On the first page, he had written: 'My New Year's Promises.'"

**【解析】** 李伟坐在窗边，手里拿着笔记本写新年承诺。A 项弹吉他、C 项给奶奶打电话、D 项看故事书均非原文所述。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "He had only read eight books."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到李伟去年只读了八本书。A 项二十本是目标而非实际完成数；B 项十五本是今年新目标；D 项零本与原文矛盾。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "'I didn't try hard enough,' he thought."

**【解析】** 李伟对自己去年没有努力完成承诺感到不开心。A 项没庆祝新年、C 项父亲生气、D 项丢了笔记本均属无中生有。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "The point is not to be perfect. The point is to keep trying."

**【解析】** 父亲的话意在鼓励李伟：重要的不是做到完美，而是持续努力。A 项放弃承诺与原意相反；C 项只做一个承诺过于片面；D 项承诺不重要也与原意不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "He would not aim for perfect. He would aim for better."

**【解析】** 故事传达的主旨是：设定切实可行的目标并坚持努力比追求完美更重要。A 项不该许承诺、C 项不爱奶奶、D 项父亲失望均与文章主旨和内容不符。故选 B。
