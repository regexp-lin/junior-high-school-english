# 阅读理解（说明文）

**How Do People Celebrate New Year Around the World?**

① New Year is the most widely celebrated holiday in the world. Almost every country has its own way of welcoming the new year. While the date may differ in some cultures, the feeling is the same everywhere: hope, joy, and a fresh start.

② In China, the Spring Festival is the most important celebration. Families get together for a big dinner on New Year's Eve. They eat dumplings in the north and rice cakes in the south. Children receive red envelopes with lucky money. People set off fireworks and put up red decorations. The colour red is believed to bring good luck and drive away bad spirits.

③ In the United States, millions of people watch the famous ball drop in Times Square, New York City. A large, shining ball slowly falls from the top of a building at midnight. People count down together: "Ten, nine, eight..." When the ball reaches the bottom, everyone cheers and hugs. Many Americans also make "New Year's resolutions" — promises to improve themselves in the coming year.

④ In Spain, people have a fun tradition. At midnight, they eat twelve grapes — one for each stroke of the clock. Each grape stands for one month of the year. If the grape is sweet, that month will be good. If it is sour, the month may bring challenges. People try to eat all twelve grapes before the clock stops ringing.

⑤ In Japan, people visit temples and shrines on New Year's Day. They ring a large bell 108 times. Each ring is said to remove one human desire or worry. Families also send New Year's cards to friends and relatives. It is a quiet and peaceful way to begin the year.

**注释：** resolution 决心/承诺；stroke （钟的）敲击声；shrine 神社；desire 欲望

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段 "Almost every country has its own way of welcoming the new year." 以及全文分别介绍了中国、美国、西班牙和日本的庆祝方式。

**【解析】** 文章介绍了世界各地不同的新年庆祝方式，是全文的主旨。A 项只涉及中国，以偏概全；C 项仅涉及美国的决心传统；D 项文章未讨论新年历史。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The colour red is believed to bring good luck and drive away bad spirits."

**【解析】** 原文说红色被认为能带来好运、驱走邪灵。A 项最便宜、C 项唯一喜欢的颜色、D 项政府要求均属无中生有。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "A large, shining ball slowly falls from the top of a building at midnight."

**【解析】** 在纽约时代广场，午夜时分一个大型发光球从建筑物顶部缓缓降落。A 项吃葡萄是西班牙；B 项敲钟是日本；D 项参观寺庙也是日本。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "If it is sour, the month may bring challenges."

**【解析】** 原文说如果葡萄是酸的，那个月可能会有挑战。A 项非常幸运与 sour 对应的含义相反；C 项出国旅行、D 项多吃水果均无依据。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Each ring is said to remove one human desire or worry."

**【解析】** 结合上下文，敲钟的目的是消除人的欲望和烦恼，"remove"意为"去除、带走"。A 项增加与原意相反；C 项保留与上下文矛盾；D 项记住不合语境。故选 B。
