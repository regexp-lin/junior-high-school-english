# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Bus — Student Summer Pass 2027 (Notice)**

**CITY PUBLIC TRANSPORT COMPANY**
*Summer is long. The bus is yours.*

This summer, we are happy to offer a **Student Summer Pass**. With this little blue card in your pocket, you can ride any city bus, any time, from Monday **1 July** to Sunday **31 August, 2027**, for only **50 yuan** in total.

---

**Who can apply?**

- Any student in Grade 1 of primary school up to Grade 12 of senior high, in our city.
- Students **from other cities** who are staying in our city for the summer (with a guardian) are also welcome to apply.
- Every student needs a parent's signature on the paper form.

**Where to apply**

- **City Bus Service Centre**, Room 108, 1F, No. 26 East Wind Road.
- Any **China Post office** in the city.
- Online at *citybus2027.cn/summerpass* (pay by phone and pick up the pass within 3 days).

**When to apply**

- **Paper application**: Monday 16 June – Friday 27 June, 2027, 09:00 – 17:00 (Mon. – Sat.)
- **Online application**: Monday 16 June – Sunday 29 June, 2027, 24 hours a day.
- After 30 June, passes may still be bought, but the price will be **70 yuan**.

**What to bring for a paper application**

1. One 2-inch colour photo (blue background).
2. A copy of your student ID card.
3. A completed paper form, signed by a parent (forms are free at the service centre).
4. 50 yuan in cash, or by mobile payment.

**What the pass can and cannot do**

| Can | Cannot |
| --- | --- |
| Ride any normal city bus. | Ride the airport express shuttle. |
| Ride on weekdays, weekends and holidays. | Be used by another person. |
| Be used for unlimited journeys per day. | Be refunded after 5 July (only lost-pass reissue is possible before 15 July for 20 yuan). |

**Useful route highlights for summer 2027**

- **Bus 52**: City Library → Silver Lake Park (every 10 min., 06:00 – 22:00).
- **Bus 8 (new!)**: Old Town Bus Station → City Museum of History (every 15 min., 07:30 – 21:00). Free Wi-Fi on every bus!
- **Night Bus N2**: Central Square → East Train Station (every 25 min., 22:00 – 00:30). For students who attend summer concerts.

**Important reminders**

1. The pass is **not transferable**. If a conductor sees a student using another student's pass, both passes may be taken away for the rest of the summer.
2. If the pass is lost, please call **12345** within 24 hours, then go to the Service Centre to apply for a re-issue (20 yuan fee).
3. On very hot days, please drink plenty of water on the bus, but avoid hot food; clean bags are welcome, wet bags are not.
4. Students younger than Grade 3 must be with a parent or guardian when riding.

**Questions?**
Hotline **12345** (24 hours). Summer office hours: Mon. – Fri., 08:30 – 17:30.

*Have a safe, cool and happy summer!*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"only 50 yuan in total."  
【解析】早鸟 50 元；30 日后 70 元。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"Where to apply" 三处  
【解析】未提图书馆。A/B/D 均列出。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Cannot · Ride the airport express shuttle."  
【解析】机场快线不在范围。A/B/C 均可乘。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"both passes may be taken away for the rest of the summer."  
【解析】双卡被没收。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Students younger than Grade 3 must be with a parent or guardian..."  
【解析】三年级以下须陪同。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
