# 阅读理解（应用文） **Grade 8 Spring Trip — Lotus Mountain Notice**

① Hello, dear Grade 8 students and parents! Spring has finally arrived, and the whole grade will enjoy a one-day **spring trip** to **Lotus Mountain** on **Friday, April 9, 2027**. This notice tells you everything you need to know before the happy day.

② **Trip Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date | Friday, April 9, 2027 |
| Leave | 7:40 a.m. at the school East Gate |
| Return | About 5:00 p.m. at the same gate |
| Distance | 60 km from the school, about 90 minutes by bus |
| Transport | Four school buses, each with one head teacher |

③ **What We Will Do**

- **Morning: Mountain Walk.** Students walk to the top of Lotus Mountain along the stone path (about 2.5 km, with many rest points). Every student will receive a small flower map.  
- **Lunch: Picnic by the Small Lake.** Please bring your own lunch box. Hot water will be provided by the school.  
- **Afternoon: Nature Photography Activity.** Students will take one photo of a plant, one of a small animal, and one of a teammate's smile. Best three photos from each class will be shown on the school website next week.

④ **Things to Bring**

- A schoolbag with your water bottle (at least 500 ml) and a small pack of tissues.  
- A thin sun hat; the mountain can be windy.  
- Sports shoes (no sandals or slippers).  
- A simple camera or your own phone (for photos only).  
- ¥50 in cash in case of small needs.

⑤ **Please Note**

- Students **must** follow the teacher's flag at all times; do not leave the group.  
- It is **forbidden** to pick flowers, break branches or feed wild animals.  
- No glass bottles, fireworks or sharp tools are allowed.  
- Phones may be used **only** to take photos and to call teachers if needed.  
- If a student feels unwell, please report to the head teacher at once.

⑥ **If It Rains**

If the weather forecast shows heavy rain on Thursday evening, the trip will move to **Friday, April 16**. A short message will be sent to the class group before 9 p.m. on Thursday. Parents, please check the group around that time.

⑦ Please ask a parent to sign the green note at the back of this page, and return it to your head teacher before **Tuesday, April 6**. For more questions, email Ms. Wu at **wu.teacher@no5ms.edu**. Enjoy your spring, and keep your eyes open for the smallest flowers!

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What time will students leave for the trip?**

A. 6:30 a.m.  
B. 7:40 a.m.  
C. 9:00 a.m.  
D. 5:00 p.m.

**2. Which of the following is NOT part of the afternoon activity?**

A. Taking a photo of a plant.  
B. Taking a photo of a small animal.  
C. Picking a beautiful flower.  
D. Taking a photo of a teammate's smile.

**3. Which item is students REQUIRED to bring?**

A. A glass bottle.  
B. A pair of sports shoes.  
C. A small firework.  
D. A sharp knife.

**4. What will happen if heavy rain is forecast on Thursday evening?**

A. The trip will be cancelled forever.  
B. The trip will move to Friday, April 16.  
C. The trip will become an online lesson.  
D. The trip will move to Lotus Park instead.

**5. Before which date must parents sign and return the green note?**

A. Thursday, April 1.  
B. Tuesday, April 6.  
C. Friday, April 9.  
D. Friday, April 16.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Leave 7:40 a.m."  
【解析】7:40 出发。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段拍照活动 & 第⑤段 "forbidden to pick flowers"  
【解析】下午只拍照，采花被禁止。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Sports shoes (no sandals or slippers)"  
【解析】必须穿运动鞋。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段 "move to Friday, April 16"  
【解析】延期至 4 月 16 日。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑦段 "before Tuesday, April 6"  
【解析】4 月 6 日前交回。故选 B。
