# 阅读理解（应用文） **Happy Tails — Animal Shelter Volunteer Notice**

**HAPPY TAILS CITY ANIMAL SHELTER**
*Every little paw matters.*

---

**Call for Student Volunteers — Summer 2027**

Our shelter takes care of more than 300 lost cats and dogs every year. Every summer, we need a small group of student volunteers to help us with simple but very useful tasks. If you love animals and have kind hands, we would love to meet you.

**Who can sign up?**

- Middle school students in Grade 7, 8 or 9.
- You must be **at least 12 years old** on the first day of the volunteer week.
- You must not have serious allergies to cats or dogs; we will ask parents to sign a health form.

**When?**

Four two-day programmes are available this summer. Please pick **one** programme:

| Programme | Date | Group size |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A | 20 – 21 July 2027 | 6 students |
| B | 27 – 28 July 2027 | 6 students |
| C | 10 – 11 August 2027 | 6 students |
| D | 17 – 18 August 2027 | 6 students |

**Where?**

Happy Tails Shelter, No. 8 Pine Lane, West District (bus 115 to Pine Lane stop).

**What will you do?**

- **Morning (09:00 – 11:30):** feed the dogs and cats, clean the food bowls, and change the water.
- **Afternoon (14:00 – 16:30):** gentle walk with quiet dogs in the shelter's small garden; help the staff brush the cats.
- **Evening (16:30 – 17:00):** daily meeting — what went well and what needs attention tomorrow.

**What we provide**

- A simple white T-shirt and cotton gloves on Day 1.
- Free lunch (rice, vegetables, an egg, and fruit) in the staff room.
- A paper certificate at the end of the two days.
- Bus fare to and from the shelter (to a maximum of 20 yuan a day).

**What we do NOT provide**

- Payment. This is a voluntary programme; no money is given.
- Bicycle insurance. Please do not ride to the shelter without your parents' permission.

**Important rules**

1. Never open an animal cage alone. Always wait for a staff member.
2. Never feed an animal any food you bring from home.
3. Put your phone in the locker before entering the animal rooms; loud ring tones can frighten the animals.
4. If you are bitten or scratched, even slightly, tell a staff member immediately. We have a small first-aid room.
5. Please use quiet, slow movements. Animals here have been lost or hurt before, and they need time to trust humans.

**How to sign up**

1. Fill in the form at **www.happytails-vol.cn** before **Sunday 4 July 2027**.
2. Wait for a phone call from Ms. Yu (between 6 – 10 July). She will give you a 10-minute phone chat.
3. If you are chosen, bring the signed parent form on Day 1.

**Contact**
Happy Tails Shelter · 0571-xxxx-9088 · volunteer@happytails.cn
*Kind hands make quiet hearts.*

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How old must a student be to sign up?**

A. At least 10 years old on the first day.  
B. At least 12 years old on the first day.  
C. At least 15 years old on the first day.  
D. No age limit.

**2. How long does each programme last?**

A. 1 day.  
B. 2 days.  
C. 7 days.  
D. 14 days.

**3. Which of the following tasks is NOT listed for volunteers?**

A. Feed the dogs and cats.  
B. Walk gently with quiet dogs in the shelter's garden.  
C. Take the animals home for the night.  
D. Clean food bowls and change water.

**4. What does the shelter NOT provide?**

A. A T-shirt and gloves.  
B. Free lunch.  
C. A paper certificate.  
D. Payment.

**5. What must a volunteer do when entering the animal rooms?**

A. Turn up the phone ring tone.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"at least 12 years old on the first day"  
【解析】首日年满 12 岁。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格"20 – 21 July" 等  
【解析】每期 2 天。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"What will you do?" 列表  
【解析】不允许把动物带回家。A/B/D 均在列表中。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"What we do NOT provide · Payment"  
【解析】不支付酬劳。A/B/C 均提供。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】规则 3  
【解析】进入动物房前须存放手机。A/B/D 均违反规则。故选 C。
