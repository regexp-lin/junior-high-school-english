# 阅读理解（说明文） **AI Around Us: A Quiet Helper in Daily Life**

① When we hear the words "artificial intelligence," or AI, many people think of big robots in science films. In fact, AI is already quietly helping us in many small ways every day. We may use it without even noticing.

② Open your phone, and AI is everywhere. When you type the first two letters of a word, your keyboard quickly guesses the rest. When you take a photo, the camera knows where the face is and makes it clear. Many music apps learn what you like and then play new songs that may fit your taste. Voice helpers, such as simple smart speakers at home, can answer questions, turn on the lamp or play a bedtime story.

③ AI is also useful outside the home. In some big shops, smart cameras count how many people walk in, so that the manager knows the busy hours. In hospitals, AI software helps doctors read pictures of the lungs and find possible problems more quickly. Traffic lights on some city roads can change by themselves based on the number of cars, which makes it easier to drive during rush hours.

④ For students, AI brings both help and new questions. A smart notebook can check the grammar of an English sentence and show the mistake. A math app can explain step by step how to solve a hard problem. However, if a student asks AI to write the whole homework, he or she may miss the chance to really learn. Teachers often remind us: AI is a tool, not a teacher.

⑤ Like any tool, AI works best when we use it in a smart way. We should enjoy its help, but also protect our own thinking. A good question is still our own question; a good idea is still our own idea. AI may be getting smarter every year, but the real key of growing up stays in our hands.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. According to Paragraph ②, how does AI help when you type?**

A. It cleans your phone screen.  
B. It guesses the rest of the word for you.  
C. It sends your letters to friends.  
D. It stops you from typing too fast.

**2. Where is AI NOT mentioned as a helper in the passage?**

A. In hospitals.  
B. On city roads.  
C. In big shops.  
D. In children's birthday parties.

**3. What may happen if a student lets AI do all the homework?**

A. The student will get the highest score in the class.  
B. The student may miss the chance to really learn.  
C. The student will be praised by the teacher.  
D. The student will never use AI again.

**4. What does the phrase "a tool, not a teacher" mean in Paragraph ④?**

A. AI is a kind of pen and paper.  
B. AI cannot be trusted in any subject.  
C. AI can help learning but should not replace real learning.  
D. AI will one day become a school teacher.

**5. What is the writer's main idea?**

A. AI is a dangerous enemy of people.  
B. People should use AI smartly and protect their own thinking.  
C. AI will soon make all jobs disappear.  
D. Only rich families can use AI at home.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "your keyboard quickly guesses the rest."  
【解析】键盘会猜测单词后部。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题（排除型）  
【定位】第③段提到大商场、医院、道路。  
【解析】A、B、C 均被提及，D 生日派对未提。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "if a student asks AI to write the whole homework, he or she may miss the chance to really learn."  
【解析】可能失去学习机会。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】词义/句义猜测题  
【定位】第④段 "AI is a tool, not a teacher."  
【解析】AI 可辅助学习但不能代替学习。A、B、D 均为字面误解。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段 "enjoy its help, but also protect our own thinking."  
【解析】作者强调要聪明使用 AI 并保持独立思考。A、C 过度推断；D 无依据。故选 B。
