# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should Parents Set Strict Time Limits for Video Games?**

① Video games are part of many teenagers' daily life. Some students play for half an hour after homework, while others may play two or three hours on the weekend. Many parents wonder: should we give our children a strict time limit, or should we let them decide by themselves?

② Parents who prefer strict time limits say that video games are simply too attractive. The bright pictures and quick rewards can pull a student's attention for hours. Without a firm rule, a short "ten-minute break" can easily become a whole afternoon. Sitting too long is also bad for eyes and backs. For younger students who do not yet know how to stop, a clear time limit can protect both their health and their study time.

③ On the other side, some parents and students believe that strict rules may not be the best answer. Teenagers are at the age when they start to make their own choices. If every minute of their free time is controlled by parents, they may lose the chance to learn self-control. A few reasonable games can even bring friends together, help with English words, or teach team skills. Strict bans sometimes push students to hide their screens instead of playing less.

④ In my opinion, a family rule is needed, but it should be made together. Parents and children can sit down and agree on how many hours per week are fine, which days are game days, and what the student needs to finish first, such as homework and exercise. If the student keeps the rule well for a month, parents can allow a little more flexible time. In this way, rules are not just orders from parents, but shared promises that help teenagers grow up step by step.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段末句 "should we give our children a strict time limit...?"  
【解析】全文围绕是否严格限制游戏时间。A、C、D 偏离主题。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "The bright pictures and quick rewards can pull a student's attention for hours."  
【解析】鲜艳画面和快速奖励吸引玩家。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "help with English words, or teach team skills."  
【解析】游戏可锻炼团队技能或学习英语。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "a family rule is needed, but it should be made together."  
【解析】建议全家一起制定规则并循序执行。A、B、D 与作者立场不符。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨/写作意图题  
【定位】第④段末句 "Rules are not just orders from parents, but shared promises that help teenagers grow up step by step."  
【解析】作者核心观点：规则应为共同约定。A 为引语；B 为反方论据；D 为反方论据。故选 C。
