# 阅读理解（应用文） **Wang Family's Spring Festival Trip Plan**

① The Wang family is planning a short Spring Festival trip together. Below is the plan drawn up by Mr. Wang, his wife, their son Kevin (14) and their daughter Lily (9). They hope to relax, see friends and enjoy the food of another city.

② **Basic Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Trip | Beijing → Xi'an → Beijing |
| Dates | Lunar Day 2 – Day 5 (4 days, 3 nights) |
| Transport | High-speed train (G-series) |
| Hotel | Green Bamboo Inn, 800 m from Bell Tower |
| Budget | About 8,000 yuan for the whole family |

③ **Day-by-Day Plan**

- **Day 1:** Morning train from Beijing South to Xi'an North. Afternoon: walk on the **Xi'an City Wall**. Evening: small dinner in a local noodle shop near the hotel.  
- **Day 2:** Full day at the **Terracotta Warriors Museum**. An English-Chinese audio guide will be rented for Kevin.  
- **Day 3:** Morning: the **Bell Tower** and the **Drum Tower**. Afternoon: a **calligraphy lesson** for the children in a small art studio. Evening: the famous **Muslim Quarter** for local snacks.  
- **Day 4:** Late morning train back to Beijing. Rest at home and prepare for school.

④ **Tips for Kevin & Lily**

- Bring a warm coat, gloves and a hat. Xi'an can be colder than Beijing.  
- Take a small notebook to write three new things each day in simple English.  
- Keep the hotel card in your pocket in case you cannot find the way.  
- Never leave the parents' sight in crowded places.  
- One hour of games on the train is OK, but the rest of the time is for reading, talking or looking out of the window.

⑤ **Family Promises**

- No one will bring a large camera, only mobile phones.  
- Each family member can pick one "must-see" spot and one "must-try" food.  
- After the trip, the children will make a short English picture book about the four days and show it to grandparents.

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many days will the Wang family stay on this trip?**

A. 2 days.  
B. 3 days.  
C. 4 days.  
D. 5 days.

**2. Where will the family stay in Xi'an?**

A. In a big hotel inside the Terracotta Warriors Museum.  
B. In Green Bamboo Inn, 800 m from the Bell Tower.  
C. In a small tent on the City Wall.  
D. At the home of a local family.

**3. What will Kevin and Lily do on Day 3 afternoon?**

A. Visit the Terracotta Warriors again.  
B. Take a calligraphy lesson in a small art studio.  
C. Fly back to Beijing.  
D. Take a Chinese dance class.

**4. According to the tips, what should the children do in crowded places?**

A. Stay close to their parents and not leave their sight.  
B. Walk 10 metres ahead of their parents.  
C. Keep their eyes closed for safety.  
D. Speak only in English.

**5. What is one of the "family promises"?**

A. Everyone will bring a large camera.  
B. The children will make a short English picture book after the trip.  
C. They will eat at the same restaurant every meal.  
D. They will stay at the hotel all day long.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Lunar Day 2 – Day 5 (4 days, 3 nights)"。  
【解析】共 4 天。A、B、D 均不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Hotel: Green Bamboo Inn, 800 m from Bell Tower."  
【解析】住在距钟楼 800 米的绿竹客栈。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 Day 3 "Afternoon: a calligraphy lesson for the children in a small art studio."  
【解析】下午上书法课。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Never leave the parents' sight in crowded places."  
【解析】拥挤处不能离开父母视线。B、C、D 均不符。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "the children will make a short English picture book about the four days..."  
【解析】孩子们旅行后制作英文图画册。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。
