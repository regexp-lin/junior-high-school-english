# 阅读理解（说明文） **How Do Ants Work Together Without a Boss?**

If you watch ants on a kitchen floor for just a few minutes, you will notice something amazing. Without any sound, without any paper list and without any boss, thousands of ants find food, build long lines and bring every piece back to the nest. How do they do this? Scientists have been asking this question for more than a hundred years.

To begin with, an ant colony is not quite like a human group. There is a queen, but she is **not** a boss. The queen's main job is to lay eggs. She does not give orders about where to search for food or how to build the nest. The small workers decide by themselves, using a simple system of chemicals called **pheromones**.

When a worker ant finds a piece of food, she goes home on a straight path and leaves a thin trail of pheromone on the ground as she walks. The trail has a special smell. When another ant smells it, she follows it back to the food, and she also leaves her own pheromone. Soon, more and more ants go along the same trail, making the smell stronger and stronger. This is why you often see a long, clear line of ants moving between food and the nest.

What happens when the food runs out? Here is the clever part. Pheromone does not last forever — it slowly disappears into the air. If no ant is using the trail, the smell becomes weaker and weaker, and finally it is gone. The whole "road" is deleted. The ants then search for new food in other directions. So the colony never gets stuck on an empty path.

This system also helps ants deal with surprise. In a famous experiment, scientists put a small stone in the middle of an ant trail. At first, the ants bumped into it and walked around it in different ways. But soon the shorter way around got more pheromone, because ants using that way came back faster and left smells more often. After a few hours, almost all the ants were taking the shorter way. They had "found" the best path without any leader.

In recent years, engineers have used this ant idea to plan the routes of delivery trucks and even the movement of robots in a warehouse. They sometimes call it **ant colony optimization**. The next time you see a line of tiny ants in your kitchen, remember: you are looking at one of nature's quiet but most modern computers.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. According to the passage, what is the main job of the queen ant?**

A. To give orders about where to find food.  
B. To lay eggs.  
C. To teach the worker ants to walk in a line.  
D. To build the nest alone.

**2. How do ants "tell" other ants where the food is?**

A. By making small sounds.  
B. By dancing in a circle in the nest.  
C. By leaving a trail of pheromone (a chemical with a smell) on the ground.  
D. By writing special signs with their legs.

**3. Why doesn't the colony stay on an empty path forever?**

A. Because the queen destroys the path.  
B. Because the pheromone slowly disappears into the air if no ant uses it.  
C. Because ants forget the path after one day.  
D. Because humans wipe the path away.

**4. In the stone experiment, why did most ants finally use the shorter way?**

A. Because scientists forced them to do so.  
B. Because the shorter way had less stone dust.  
C. Because ants using the shorter way came back faster and left more pheromone there.  
D. Because the stone was very heavy.

**5. What have engineers learned from the ant system?**

A. How to make honey in a factory.  
B. How to plan the routes of delivery trucks and the movement of robots in a warehouse.  
C. How to grow stronger plants in the desert.  
D. How to teach children to do maths.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 段 "The queen's main job is to lay eggs."  
【解析】蚁后主要产卵。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 段 "leaves a thin trail of pheromone on the ground"  
【解析】信息素留痕。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 段 "Pheromone does not last forever..."  
【解析】信息素会自然消散。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第 5 段  
【解析】更短路径往返更快，气味更浓。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】末段 "engineers have used this ant idea..."  
【解析】用于卡车路线和仓库机器人路径。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
