# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Math Test I Will Never Forget**

The most important math test of last year was on a Tuesday morning in March. Our class had been waiting for it for weeks. Mr. Zhao, our maths teacher, had written "**This test decides who enters the city maths club**" on the blackboard the week before. I wanted to join the club more than anything.

On Monday evening, I studied until 11:30. I checked every wrong answer in my old test papers. I closed my notebook only when my eyes could not stay open. I felt tired, but also ready.

The next morning I walked into the classroom early, sat down, and opened my pencil case. I froze. My scientific calculator was **not** there. My heart started to jump. I remembered: the night before, I had moved it to the kitchen table to find a spare battery, and I had forgotten to put it back. The bus was already on its way back to my neighbourhood; my mother had a meeting; my dad was on a business trip.

I sat with my head down. Around me, other students were taking out their calculators, smiling. I felt my face turn red. The test had still fifteen minutes before it started.

Then Ling Ling, the girl next to me, noticed. Ling Ling was quiet; we had never really talked before. She looked at my empty pencil case and then at her own calculator. Her own calculator was a new one, the same kind I had. She said softly, "I have a spare one in my bag. It's old, but it works. Take it."

I could hardly believe it. "Are you sure? But — this test is so important."

She smiled a small smile. "Mine is the newer one, remember? You'll manage with this. Just be careful; the '+' key sometimes sticks."

When Mr. Zhao put the test on my desk, my hands were still shaking, but I started to breathe again. Ling Ling's old black calculator lay beside my paper like a small quiet friend. The "+" key did stick twice, but the rest worked fine. Ninety minutes later, I finished the test.

Two weeks later, the list of the new maths club members was put on the wall. My name was on it. I looked for Ling Ling's name — and found it too, in the line above mine. I turned to her, and for the first time we laughed together about that morning. From that day on, we walked to maths club together every Thursday.

I got into the club because I had worked hard. But more importantly, on that Tuesday morning, a quiet classmate had **chosen** to help me, and her old calculator had opened a door that could have closed forever.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why was the maths test so important to the narrator?**

A. Because it decided his final school grade.  
B. Because it decided who could enter the city maths club.  
C. Because his father promised him a computer.  
D. Because it was the last test of the year.

**2. What problem did the narrator have that morning?**

A. He was 10 minutes late.  
B. He was feeling very sick.  
C. He had left his calculator at home.  
D. He had lost his pencil case.

**3. Why was Ling Ling able to help him?**

A. Because she had a spare calculator in her bag.  
B. Because she had a new phone and could call his mother.  
C. Because she knew the teacher's answer key.  
D. Because she had finished her own test early.

**4. What small problem did Ling Ling's calculator have?**

A. The screen was broken.  
B. The battery was flat.  
C. The "+" key sometimes stuck.  
D. All keys were too small to use.

**5. What is the main message of the story?**

A. Hard work alone is enough for any success.  
B. Old things are always better than new things.  
C. Kind help from others can change what happens in an important moment.  
D. Students should share all their things every day.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 段 "decides who enters the city maths club"  
【解析】能否进入城市数学俱乐部。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 段 "My scientific calculator was not there"  
【解析】忘带计算器。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 5 段 "I have a spare one in my bag"  
【解析】Ling Ling 有备用计算器。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 5 段末 "the '+' key sometimes sticks"  
【解析】加号键偶尔卡住。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段  
【解析】关键时刻善意相助改变结局。A/B/D 均片面。故选 C。
