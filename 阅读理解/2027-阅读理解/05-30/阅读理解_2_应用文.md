# 阅读理解（应用文） **Yangguang Middle School Canteen — New Summer Menu (June 2027)**

**YANGGUANG MIDDLE SCHOOL CANTEEN**
*"Fresh, light and full of summer energy."*

From **Monday 1 June** to **Sunday 28 August, 2027**, our canteen will use a brand-new summer menu.  
Please read this notice carefully, so that you can enjoy every meal in a safe and healthy way.

---

**Opening hours**

| Meal | Time |
| --- | --- |
| Breakfast | 06:50 – 07:40 |
| Lunch | 11:40 – 12:50 |
| Afternoon light snack | 16:00 – 16:30 |
| Dinner (Grade 9 boarders only) | 17:30 – 18:30 |

Please show your **student meal card** at every meal. Cards cannot be shared with classmates or friends.

**Breakfast highlights**

- Millet porridge with red dates
- Soybean milk (cold or warm — please choose one cup per student)
- Steamed buns with egg and cabbage
- One seasonal fruit: watermelon, peach or plum

**Lunch main dishes (one rice bowl + two dishes + one soup)**

- Cucumber, fried tofu and tomato with rice (Mon. · Wed. · Fri.)
- Mushroom chicken, green beans and pumpkin soup (Tue. · Thu.)
- "Summer Ocean" set: small shrimps, cold cucumber noodles and winter melon soup (**Saturdays only, 30 sets per day**, first come, first served)

**Afternoon light snack**

- A cup of low-sugar soybean milk, **or**
- A small bowl of sweet mung bean soup (very cold, please drink slowly), **or**
- A slice of steamed pumpkin cake.

Only **one** item per student.

**Important new rules for summer**

1. **No running with trays.** The floor near the door is wet-cleaned every hour; please walk calmly.
2. **No mobile phones at the dining table.** Videos at lunch slow down the dining line and often cause spills.
3. **Please take only the amount you can eat.** If you are not sure, ask the staff in a red apron.
4. Leftover rice and clean vegetables may be wrapped and taken to the feeding point for small neighbourhood cats.

**Allergy & health reminders**

- Dishes with peanut, egg or seafood are marked with red stickers on the menu board.
- If you feel sick after any meal (stomach pain, dizziness, rash), go straight to the **School Medical Room, 1F South**. Then please inform the canteen manager, **Mr. Song Yu**.

**Student feedback**

Every Friday, a green "Menu Opinion Box" will be placed near the exit. Please feel free to write your suggestions on a small paper slip. The most loved dish of the week will be kept on the menu the following week.

**Contact**

- Canteen Manager Mr. Song Yu, Office at 1F East.
- Hotline: 020-xxxx-3322 (Mon. – Fri., 08:00 – 17:00).

*Eat well, and study well!*

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long is the lunch time in the new summer menu?**

A. 30 minutes.  
B. 50 minutes.  
C. 70 minutes (11:40 – 12:50).  
D. 2 hours.

**2. What special lunch set is offered only on Saturdays, with limited quantity?**

A. Cucumber, fried tofu and tomato with rice.  
B. Mushroom chicken and pumpkin soup.  
C. "Summer Ocean" set with small shrimps, cold cucumber noodles and winter melon soup.  
D. Millet porridge.

**3. How many items can a student choose from the afternoon light snack?**

A. One item.  
B. Two items.  
C. Three items.  
D. As many as the student wants.

**4. Which action is NOT allowed in the canteen according to the notice?**

A. Showing your meal card at every meal.  
B. Walking calmly with a tray.  
C. Running with a tray because the floor may be wet.  
D. Wrapping clean leftover vegetables to feed neighbourhood cats.

**5. What should students do if they feel sick after a meal?**

A. Wait at home.  
B. Go straight to the School Medical Room on 1F South and then inform Mr. Song Yu.  
C. Ignore it and continue studying.  
D. Eat more to feel better.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Lunch · 11:40 – 12:50"  
【解析】70 分钟。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"'Summer Ocean' set... Saturdays only, 30 sets per day"  
【解析】周六限量。A/B/D 均不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Only one item per student."  
【解析】下午点心每人仅一种。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"No running with trays."  
【解析】禁止跑步端盘。A/B/D 均被允许或鼓励。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"go straight to the School Medical Room, 1F South. Then please inform the canteen manager, Mr. Song Yu."  
【解析】先去医务室再告知。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
