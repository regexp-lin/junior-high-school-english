# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Volunteers Wanted for Our School**

① **Sunshine Middle School Volunteer Club** needs new members this term. We help teachers, support students, and serve our neighborhood. If you are kind, patient, and ready to work in a team, you are welcome to join us. No experience is required—we will train you.

② **Time & Place:** Meetings are on **Fridays** from **4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.** in **Room 302**, Teaching Building A. Please arrive **five minutes early** on your first visit so we can sign you in. If you cannot come because of a school match, tell us one day earlier.

③ **Main Tasks:**  
| Task | What you do | When |
|------|-------------|------|
| Library helper | Put books back on shelves and keep the reading room tidy | Tuesday & Thursday lunch time |
| Sports day helper | Carry small items and guide parents and visitors | Next month (date TBA) |
| Community clean-up | Pick up litter in the park near our school | One Saturday morning each term |

④ **Requirements:** You should be in **Grade 7 or Grade 8**, and you need a signed letter from your parent or guardian. You must wear the school uniform during school activities and follow safety rules outdoors. Please show respect to teachers and neighbors at all times.

⑤ **How to apply:** Fill in the online form on the school website before **April 25**. Add a short note about why you want to volunteer. For questions, email **volunteer@sunshine.edu** or call **010-12345678** on weekdays. Come and make our school a warmer place!

---

**注释：** *litter* 垃圾；*guardian* 监护人

---

## 选择题

**1.** Who is the notice mainly written for?

A. Students who want to join the volunteer club.  
B. Parents who work in the library.  
C. Teachers who need new classrooms.  
D. Visitors from other cities.

**2.** When should volunteers arrive for their first meeting?

A. At 3:55 p.m. on any weekday.  
B. At 4:05 p.m. on Friday.  
C. At 3:55 p.m. on Friday.  
D. At 5:00 p.m. on Tuesday.

**3.** What can we infer about the sports day helper job?

A. It will happen on a Tuesday lunch time.  
B. The exact date is not fixed in this notice.  
C. Helpers must work in the library first.  
D. Only Grade 9 students can do this task.

**4.** According to the table in Paragraph ③, when do library helpers work?

A. On Friday afternoons.  
B. On Monday mornings.  
C. On Tuesday and Thursday at lunch time.  
D. On one Saturday each term.

**5.** What is the writer’s main purpose?

A. To introduce the history of the school.  
B. To complain about litter in the park.  
C. To explain how to borrow books online.  
D. To invite students to apply as volunteers.  

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
**【答案】** A  
**【题型】** 主旨大意  
**【定位】** 第①段 “needs new members… If you are kind… you are welcome to join us.” 及标题 “Volunteers Wanted”。  
**【解析】** 全文为志愿者招募通知，主要读者是希望加入社团的学生，A 正确。B 家长工作图书馆、C 教师要教室、D 外地游客均非通知对象。

---

**2.**  
**【答案】** C  
**【题型】** 细节理解  
**【定位】** 第②段 “Meetings are on **Fridays** from **4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.**” 与 “Please arrive **five minutes early** on your first visit”。  
**【解析】** 首次应在周五会议开始前五分钟到达，即 3:55 p.m.，C 正确。A 任意工作日错误；B 晚到；D 周二五点与会议时间不符。

---

**3.**  
**【答案】** B  
**【题型】** 推理判断  
**【定位】** 第③段表格中 Sports day 一行 “**Next month (date TBA)**”。  
**【解析】** “TBA”（to be announced）表示日期待定，可推断具体日期本通知未确定，B 正确。A 与表格周二周四午休是图书馆工作混淆；C 未要求先做图书馆；D 与④段年级要求 Grade 7 or 8 矛盾。

---

**4.**  
**【答案】** C  
**【题型】** 细节理解  
**【定位】** 第③段表格 Library helper 列 “**Tuesday & Thursday lunch time**”。  
**【解析】** 图书馆助手在周二、周四午休时间工作，C 正确。A 为会议时间；B 未出现；D 为社区清洁时间。

---

**5.**  
**【答案】** D  
**【题型】** 写作意图  
**【定位】** 第①段招募与第⑤段 “Fill in the form… before April 25” 及联系方式。  
**【解析】** 通知目的是招募并指导学生报名志愿者，D 正确。A 校史、B 抱怨垃圾、C 网上借书均未作为写作目的。

---

**参考答案速查：** 1—5 ACBCD
