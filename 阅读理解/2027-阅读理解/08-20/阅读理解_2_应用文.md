# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Public Library — Summer Reading Challenge 2027**

---

## **Read, Grow, Win Together**

Dear young readers,

Every summer, the City Public Library runs the **Summer Reading Challenge** for middle-school students. It is a friendly, open programme that turns the long hot weeks into a beautiful reading season. The 2027 theme is **"Open a Book, Open a Door"**. All Grade 7–9 students with a city library card may join — **free of charge**.

- **Challenge Period:** **Monday July 5 – Sunday August 29, 2027**  
- **Where:** Any city library branch (or read from home)  
- **Goal:** Finish **at least 6 books** from the official list, and record them properly in your Reading Log.

---

## **How the Challenge Works**

1. **Get a Reading Log** — from the info desk of any branch, or download from `citylib.cn/summer2027`.  
2. **Read books from the 2027 list** — three suggested levels (see below).  
3. **Record each book** — title, author, the date you finished it, and a **two-sentence review** in English or Chinese. Parent or teacher's signature is **not** required.  
4. **Return the log** — bring or scan-and-email your finished log to your branch desk by **Sep 5, 23:59**.  
5. **Ceremony & prizes** — **Saturday, Sep 12, 15:00**, Main Library, 3rd-floor hall.

---

## **Book Levels on the 2027 List**

| Level | Example Titles | Suggested Grade | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A. Starter** | *Small Town Stories*, *The Year of the Little Owl* | Grade 7 | Short, daily-life stories |
| **B. Medium** | *The Boy and the Moon River*, *Letters from a Hospital* | Grade 8 | A bit longer, gentle themes |
| **C. Deeper** | *Before the Mountain Woke Up*, *Notes from a Small Kitchen* | Grade 9 | Longer, more feelings & ideas |

> You must read **at least one book** from **each** of the three levels. The other three may be from any level you like. Audio books also count if you can tell the staff the story in one minute.

## **Rewards**

- **Finish 6 books properly:** a **Summer Reader Badge** and a library bookmark.  
- **Top 20 Best Reviews** (judged by librarians): a 100-yuan bookshop voucher.  
- **"Little Book Friend" Award:** for the most helpful reader at Book-Swap Day.  
- **Class Prize:** the class with the most finished logs (school-verified) wins a **mini-library corner** for the classroom — 30 fresh books for the new term.

---

## **House Rules**

- Please keep each book **clean and dry**; no drawing, no folded corners.  
- Food is not allowed inside the reading halls; water bottles only.  
- Late returns cost the usual library fee.  
- If you cannot attend the closing ceremony, your reward will be kept for you for **one month**. Please pick it up at the Main Library info desk.  
- The library is **closed** every **Monday morning** for cleaning.

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many books must a student read to finish the 2027 Summer Reading Challenge properly?**

A. At least 3 books.  
B. At least 6 books, with at least one from each of the three levels.  
C. Exactly 10 books, all from Level C.  
D. All 20 books on the list.

**2. Xiao Meng is going into Grade 8. Which book is most directly suggested for her level?**

A. *Small Town Stories*.  
B. *The Boy and the Moon River*.  
C. *Before the Mountain Woke Up*.  
D. None of the above.

**3. By what date must a student return the finished Reading Log?**

A. July 5, 2027.  
B. August 29, 2027.  
C. September 5, 2027.  
D. September 12, 2027.

**4. Which of the following is **allowed** during the Summer Reading Challenge?**

A. Drawing on the library books.  
B. Folding the corner of each page to remember where you stopped.  
C. Listening to an audio book if you can tell the staff the story in one minute.  
D. Bringing hot noodles into the reading halls to save time.

**5. If a winner cannot come to the closing ceremony on Sep 12, what should she do?**

A. Write to the library and ask for money instead.  
B. Give up the reward completely.  
C. Pick up the reward at the Main Library info desk within one month.  
D. Wait until next year's ceremony.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How the Challenge Works 第 1 点 "at least 6 books" & Book Levels 说明 "at least one book from each of the three levels"  
【解析】规则明确两点：至少 6 本、每层至少 1 本。A、C、D 均与数据不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Book Levels 表 "Level B. Medium — Grade 8 — The Boy and the Moon River"  
【解析】《The Boy and the Moon River》直接对应 B 级、8 年级。A 为 7 年级，C 为 9 年级，D 错误。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How the Challenge Works 第 4 点 "by Sep 5, 23:59"  
【解析】截止日 9 月 5 日。A 是开始日，B 是挑战结束日，D 是颁奖日。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Book Levels 末句 "Audio books also count if you can tell the staff the story in one minute"  
【解析】一分钟复述故事即可算作听书。A、B 违反"keep clean and dry"；D 违反"food not allowed"。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】House Rules 第 4 条 "your reward will be kept for you for one month. Please pick it up at the Main Library info desk"  
【解析】奖品将保留一个月可自取。A、B、D 均与规则不符。故选 C。
