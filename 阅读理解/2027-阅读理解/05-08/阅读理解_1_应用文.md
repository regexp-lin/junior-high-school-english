# 阅读理解（应用文） **Green Earth Club — Weekend Tree-Planting Sign-Up**

> **GREEN EARTH CLUB**  
> **"One Tree, One Future" — Student Planting Weekend**  
> *14–15 May 2027 · Green Ridge Forest Park*

---

Dear middle school students,

The **Green Earth Club** is looking for 60 young volunteers to join our two-day tree-planting weekend in **Green Ridge Forest Park**. Last year, students from twelve schools together planted more than 1,200 small trees. This year, we hope to add 1,500 more, to help the city's new **Forest Belt** plan.

### What You Will Do

| Time | Saturday Programme | Sunday Programme |
|------|---------------------|------------------|
| 08:30–09:00 | Arrival and safety talk | Arrival and warm-up |
| 09:00–11:30 | Digging and planting small pine trees | Watering and fencing young trees |
| 11:30–12:30 | Outdoor picnic lunch (included) | Outdoor picnic lunch (included) |
| 12:30–14:30 | Labelling trees & photo wall | Short forest study walk |
| 14:30–15:00 | Closing circle and certificates | Closing circle and certificates |

### What to Bring

- A **full water bottle** (at least 750 ml).  
- A **sun hat** and long-sleeved sports clothes.  
- Sturdy shoes (no sandals or high heels).  
- A small towel and, if you need, personal medicine.  
- A small notebook for the forest study walk (Sunday only).

*Tools (shovels, gloves, buckets, tree labels) will be provided.*

### Rules and Safety

1. Volunteers must be in **Grades 6–9** (ages 11–15). Grade 10 and above are welcome as student leaders.  
2. Each volunteer must bring a signed **parent permission form**.  
3. Please do not bring drones, expensive cameras, or glass bottles into the forest.  
4. If you have a serious **pollen allergy**, please tell us on the form; we will arrange lighter tasks for you.  
5. All volunteers who finish both days will receive a **Green Ridge Certificate** and **20 community service hours**.

### How to Apply

- **Step 1:** Visit **www.greenearthclub.cn/may2027**  
- **Step 2:** Fill in the online form and upload your parent permission letter.  
- **Step 3:** Wait for the email confirmation within 3 days.  
- **Deadline for applications:** 11 May 2027, 18:00.

### Travel

A free school bus will leave **East Library Square at 07:45** both mornings and return by 16:30. Only one adult guardian per student may ride the bus.

For more information, please email **join@greenearthclub.cn** or call **010-8877-6611** (Mon–Fri, 14:00–17:00).

**Let's make our city a little greener, one tree at a time.**

— Green Earth Club Youth Committee

**根据通告内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the goal of this year's planting weekend?**

A. To plant 1,200 trees.  
B. To plant 1,500 more young trees for the city's Forest Belt plan.  
C. To cut down old trees.  
D. To collect pine cones.

**2. What will volunteers do on Sunday morning?**

A. Dig holes and plant pine trees.  
B. Water and fence the young trees.  
C. Sing songs in the park.  
D. Take a nap under the trees.

**3. Which of the following is NOT needed from the student personally?**

A. A full water bottle.  
B. A sun hat and long-sleeved sports clothes.  
C. A shovel.  
D. Sturdy shoes.

**4. Who can take part as a regular volunteer?**

A. Students in Grades 3–5.  
B. Students in Grades 6–9.  
C. University students only.  
D. Anyone with a pollen allergy, with no limits.

**5. What will a volunteer who finishes both days receive?**

A. Only a free tree.  
B. Only 10 yuan.  
C. A Green Ridge Certificate and 20 community service hours.  
D. A new drone.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开头段 "This year, we hope to add 1,500 more, to help the city's new Forest Belt plan."  
【解析】今年目标是再种 1,500 棵小树，支持城市森林带计划。A 是去年的数字、C 与目标相反、D 无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 Sunday Programme 09:00–11:30 "Watering and fencing young trees"  
【解析】周日上午浇水并给小树围篱。A 是周六、C/D 无依据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】What to Bring 清单及其下方注释 "Tools (shovels, gloves, buckets, tree labels) will be provided."  
【解析】铁锹由主办方提供。A/B/D 都在 "What to Bring" 内，需要学生自带。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules and Safety 第1条 "Grades 6–9 (ages 11–15)"  
【解析】志愿者须为 6–9 年级学生。A 年级过低、C 限定大学生不对、D 过敏说法曲解原意。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules and Safety 第5条 "Green Ridge Certificate and 20 community service hours"  
【解析】完成两天的志愿者获得证书和 20 小时志愿服务。A/B/D 均与原文不符。故选 C。
