# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School Parents' Open Day 2027**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · School Office + Parents' Committee**  
> **Parents' Open Day 2027 — "One Whole School Day, Through Your Child's Eyes"**  
> **Saturday 5 December 2027, 07:40 – 16:30**

Twice a year, Lihua opens its gates to families for a full school day: parents do not just visit, they **become students for one day**. They sit in the classroom from 07:40 to 16:30, eat school lunch, walk to the lab and the library, and watch — gently — how their child spends 27 hours every week between blue walls and warm corridors. The Saturday Open Day in December is the **larger** of the two; this letter is to invite all parents and one chosen family member of every Lihua student.

---

## 📅 The One-Day Timetable

| Time | What Happens | Where |
|---|---|---|
| 07:40 – 07:55 | Morning sign-in, name tags, warm tea | Main Lobby |
| 08:00 – 08:30 | Whole-school flag-raising and short speech by the headmaster | Playground |
| 08:35 – 11:50 | **Four normal lessons** with your child's class | Each child's classroom |
| 11:55 – 12:35 | Lunch with students in the **dining hall** (free) | Dining Hall A or B |
| 12:40 – 13:25 | Short rest + library visit (optional) | Library Floors 2–3 |
| 13:30 – 16:00 | **Three afternoon lessons + one short club activity** | Each child's classroom + club rooms |
| 16:05 – 16:30 | **Parent–Teacher Tea Talk**, three short rounds (15 min each) | Form classroom |

> Please plan to **stay for the whole day** if possible. A whole school day is the only honest way to feel a school's rhythm.

---

## 🎒 What to Bring / How to Dress

- **One photo ID** (for sign-in and the lunch hall).  
- A **small bag** with a notebook and pen — many parents like to take notes during their child's class.  
- A **water bottle** (no disposable cups in the school).  
- Clothes that are **clean, warm, and quiet** — no loud high-heeled shoes in the corridors during lessons.  
- We kindly ask you to **leave your phones on silent** during all lessons, and to step outside the classroom to take any work calls.

---

## 🚫 Three Things We Cannot Do on Open Day

1. **No filming or photographing** any student in the classroom (you may photograph your own child only, in the corridor and during breaks).  
2. **No correcting** other students' answers, even if you are sure of the right one. Let the lesson belong to the teacher.  
3. **No gifts** for any teacher today. The Open Day must remain free of any tradition that might feel uneven.

---

## 🍱 About Lunch

Lunch is **free for all visiting parents** today, paid for by the Parents' Committee. The menu is the same as a normal student day:

- **Dining Hall A**: rice + two warm dishes + one soup + one fruit.  
- **Dining Hall B**: noodles or buns + one warm dish + one soup.

A small **vegetarian table** is set near the south door. If your family has a serious food allergy, please write a short note when you sign in.

---

## 📚 Library and Lab Visits (Optional)

Between 12:40 and 13:25, parents may visit:

- **Library Floor 2** — books your child has borrowed this term (your child's record will be **printed and waiting** with your name tag).  
- **Library Floor 3** — quiet reading and a small "Family Reading Wall" where parents may write a short book recommendation for the school library.  
- **Lab Building, Room 305** — a small live demonstration by Grade 9 students of the new "**rain-water collection**" project, which the city Science Bureau supported in October.

---

## 🗣 The Parent–Teacher Tea Talk (16:05 – 16:30)

Three short rounds, **15 minutes each**, with three of your child's teachers:

- The **form teacher** (15 min in the form classroom).  
- One **subject teacher** chosen by you in advance (10 min in their office).  
- One **club / PE teacher** chosen by your child (5 min in the corridor).

To choose your subject teacher, please reply on the school mini-app → Open Day → "Choose Subject Teacher" by **Wed 26 November, 17:30**.

---

## 🤝 How Children Should Help

We kindly remind every Lihua student to:

- Walk on the **right side** of the corridor between lessons; let parents follow easily.  
- **Greet** any parent you do not know with a soft hello and a small smile.  
- **Look after** any visitor in your row at lunch; pass the soup bowl, point to the napkin shelf.  
- Treat the day as a normal school day. **Do not perform a different child** because your parent is at the back of the room. Be yourself; that is the child your parent has come to meet.

---

## 🙋 Volunteers Wanted (52 students)

- **20 lobby helpers** (07:30–08:30, hand out name tags and tea).  
- **12 corridor guides** during the morning lessons.  
- **8 dining-hall greeters** at lunch.  
- **6 library guides** between 12:40 and 13:25.  
- **6 short-talk timekeepers** for the Tea Talk.

Sign-up form: outside Room 110 or on the school mini-app → Services → Open Day.  
**Deadline: Wed 26 November, 17:30.**

> "Open Day is not a show. It is a quiet day on which a school becomes briefly **transparent**, and a family briefly **bigger**. The classroom is still the classroom; the lunch is still rice and warm soup; the corridor is still long. What is new is only this — your child looks up from her desk, sees you, smiles a small private smile, and goes back to her question. That small private smile, and the trust behind it, is the whole reason we open the gates this Saturday."

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开头段日期与时间表 07:40 – 16:30  
【解析】直引。B、C、D 均错。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题（否定）

**3.**  
【答案】C

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How Children Should Help 段

【答案】B

【定位】结尾 Headmaster's 引言
