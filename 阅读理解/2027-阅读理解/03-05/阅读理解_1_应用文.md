# 阅读理解（应用文） **Youth Cycling Safety Workshop — Invitation**

① Dear Grade 7 & 8 students, do you ride a bicycle to school? Do you enjoy riding to the park at weekends? Cycling is healthy, green and fun, but every year more than **6,000 teenagers** across the country get hurt on the road. The **Sunshine Youth Centre**, together with the **City Traffic Police**, is organising a one-day **Youth Cycling Safety Workshop**. Join us, and learn how to be both **fast** and **safe** on two wheels.

② **Key Information**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Date | **Saturday, 20 March 2027** |
| Time | 8:30 – 16:30 |
| Place | Sunshine Youth Centre (No. 18 Riverside Road) |
| For | Grade 7 – 8 students only |
| Fee | **Free** (lunch & training helmet included) |
| Group size | 60 students (first come, first served) |

③ **Programme of the Day**

- **08:30 – 09:00**　Sign-in & group photo  
- **09:00 – 10:30**　Classroom lesson: 10 common road signs, hand signals & night-time rules  
- **10:45 – 12:00**　Bike check-up: brakes, tyres, chain, bell and lights  
- **12:00 – 13:00**　Free lunch at the centre's canteen  
- **13:00 – 15:00**　Outdoor practice: slow riding, safe turning, stopping in a straight line  
- **15:15 – 16:15**　Small challenge: "safety cycling ring" (no score, just fun!)  
- **16:15 – 16:30**　Small gift & goodbye

④ **What to Bring**

- Your own bicycle (we can also lend you one if you don't have).  
- A **water bottle**, a **towel** and some easy snacks.  
- Sports clothes and **closed-toe shoes**. (Sandals and slippers are not safe.)  
- A signed **parent note** (the form will be sent to your class teacher).

⑤ **What NOT to Bring**

- Earphones or headphones — you must hear the road.  
- Fast scooters or electric bicycles — only normal bicycles are allowed.  
- Pets or bags larger than 30 cm — no room to store them.

⑥ **How to Sign Up**

1. Ask your class teacher for the **orange sign-up form** before **17 March**.  
2. Bring the form with a parent signature to the school office, Room 108.  
3. A confirmation message will be sent to your mum's or dad's phone within two school days.  
4. **Late forms will not be accepted.**

⑦ **Contact**  
- Ms. Zhou (Sunshine Youth Centre) — **bike-safety@syc.org.cn**  
- Phone (14:00 – 17:00 on school days): 027-8866-1234

**Keep your brakes clean, your eyes open, your helmet on — and your smile wide! — Sunshine Youth Centre & City Traffic Police**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Who organized the cycling safety workshop?**

A. A local bike shop and a TV station.  
B. The Sunshine Youth Centre and the City Traffic Police.  
C. Several parents and Grade 9 students.  
D. A sports club outside the city.

**2. Which students CANNOT sign up for this workshop?**

A. Grade 7 students.  
B. Grade 8 students.  
C. Grade 9 students.  
D. Students who have their own bicycles.

**3. What will the students do from 13:00 to 15:00?**

A. Have lunch together at the canteen.  
B. Listen to a classroom lesson on road signs.  
C. Practise slow riding, safe turning and straight-line stopping.  
D. Take a group photo at the school gate.

**4. Which of these is NOT allowed at the workshop?**

A. A water bottle.  
B. Sports clothes.  
C. A normal bicycle.  
D. Earphones.

**5. What should a student do first if he wants to join?**

A. Send an email to Ms. Zhou.  
B. Bring the form with a parent's signature to the school office.  
C. Ask the class teacher for the orange sign-up form.  
D. Wait for a phone call from the youth centre.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段末 2 句 "The Sunshine Youth Centre, together with the City Traffic Police"  
【解析】主办方是青年中心与市交警。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段表格 "For Grade 7 – 8 students only"  
【解析】仅限七、八年级，九年级不可报名。D 项"无自行车"不构成拒绝（可外借）。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "13:00 – 15:00 Outdoor practice: slow riding, safe turning, stopping in a straight line"  
【解析】下午 1–3 点是慢骑、安全转弯、直线刹车的户外训练。A、B、D 属其他时段。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段第 1 条 "Earphones or headphones — you must hear the road."  
【解析】工作坊禁止耳机。A、B、C 属于允许带或使用的。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段第 1 条 "Ask your class teacher for the orange sign-up form"  
【解析】报名第一步是向班主任索取橙色报名表。B 是第二步；D 是第三步的确认；A 不是报名步骤。故选 C。
