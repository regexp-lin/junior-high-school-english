# 阅读理解（应用文） **English Speech Contest — School Poster**

① Sunshine Middle School Students’ Union is glad to announce our yearly English Speech Contest. The topic this year is “Small Steps, Big Dreams.” We hope every student will take part, practice spoken English, and share ideas in a clear and confident way.

② Please read the information below carefully before you sign up.

| Item        | Details                                      |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Date & Time | Friday, May 16, 4:00 p.m. – 6:00 p.m.      |
| Place       | School hall (second floor)                   |
| For whom    | Grade 7 – Grade 9 students                 |
| What to bring | Student ID card & a printed speech (≤ 200 words) |

③ Each speaker will have up to three minutes. Judges will pay attention to pronunciation, body language, and content. The top three winners will receive books and certificates. All participants will get a small notebook as a gift.

④ If you want to join, send your name and class to Mr. Li at speech2027@sunshine.edu before May 9. Late names may not be added to the list. For questions, visit Room 305 in the teaching building on Tuesday or Thursday lunch breaks.

⑤ Come and enjoy an afternoon of English! Let your voice be heard, meet new friends, and show what you can do. We are looking forward to seeing you at the contest.

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main purpose of this poster?**

A. To sell English books to parents.  
B. To invite students to an English speech contest.  
C. To report exam results in the school hall.  
D. To introduce a new English teacher named Mr. Li.

**2. According to the table, which statement is TRUE?**

A. Only Grade 9 students can take part.  
B. The contest will be held in the school library.  
C. Students must bring a computer to the hall.  
D. The speech on paper should be no more than 200 words.

**3. What will all participants receive?**

A. A certificate.  
B. A gold medal.  
C. A small notebook.  
D. A free lunch at school.

**4. It can be inferred that students who sign up on May 10 ______.**

A. will still be on the list if they call Mr. Li  
B. may not be allowed to join because of the deadline  
C. can get a better seat in the school hall  
D. will be asked to give a longer speech

**5. When can students ask Mr. Li questions in person?**

A. Every morning before classes.  
B. On Monday and Wednesday afternoons.  
C. On Tuesday and Thursday at lunch time.  
D. Only on the day of the contest.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第一段 “announce our yearly English Speech Contest” 及全文活动信息。  
【解析】海报目的是通知并邀请学生参加英语演讲比赛。A 售书、C 公布成绩、D 介绍新老师均与内容不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 “a printed speech (≤ 200 words)”。  
【解析】D 与表格中 “≤ 200 words” 一致。A 表格为 Grade 7–9，非仅九年级；B 地点为 school hall，非图书馆；C 需带学生证与打印稿，未要求电脑。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第三段 “All participants will get a small notebook as a gift.”  
【解析】所有参赛者获小笔记本。证书与书籍为前三名（top three winners），非人人有份。故排除 A、B、D，选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第四段 “before May 9. Late names may not be added to the list.”  
【解析】5 月 9 日前报名，逾期可能无法加入名单，故 5 月 10 日报名可能不被接受。A 与 “Late names may not be added” 矛盾；C、D 文中无依据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第四段 “visit Room 305… on Tuesday or Thursday lunch breaks.”  
【解析】当面提问时间为周二、周四午休。A、B、D 与原文不符。故选 C。
