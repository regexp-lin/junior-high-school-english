# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Little Dog in the Rain**

① Last Friday, it rained hard after school. Tom put on his raincoat and walked home slowly along the quiet street. He liked rainy days because the air smelled fresh, but he also watched his step so that he would not slip on the wet ground. His school bag felt a little heavy, yet he whistled a simple song to cheer himself up.

② Near a small park, he heard a weak sound like crying. He looked around and saw a little brown dog under a wooden bench. Its fur was wet and dirty, and it was shaking with cold. Tom’s heart beat faster. He slowly moved closer, spoke in a soft voice, and offered a piece of bread from his bag. The dog ate a little and then looked up at him with sad eyes. Tom waited patiently until the dog seemed to trust him a bit more.

③ Tom knew he could not leave the dog outside in the rain. He carried it carefully to a pet hospital two streets away. The vet checked the dog and said it was hungry but healthy. There was no name tag on its neck, so they could not find the owner at once. Tom paid for some dog food and asked the vet to keep the dog safe for one night. He also wrote down his own phone number just in case someone asked about a missing dog.

④ The next morning, Tom made a simple poster with a photo of the dog and his phone number. He put copies on the notice board at school and near the park. That evening, a woman called. Her voice was excited. She said the dog had run out when she opened her door. She thanked Tom many times and promised to put a small tag on the dog’s collar so that it would be easier to find next time.

⑤ On Monday, Tom saw the woman walk the dog in the park. The dog wagged its tail happily. Tom smiled and felt proud. He learned that caring for a small life is not only kind but also a serious duty. Responsibility means doing the right thing even when it takes extra time and money. He told himself that he would always be ready to help again if a similar thing happened.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What did Tom do right after he found the dog under the bench?**

A. He ran home to get an umbrella.  
B. He offered the dog some bread and spoke softly.  
C. He called the police for help at once.  
D. He took the dog to the pet hospital immediately.

**2. It can be inferred from Paragraph ③ that ______.**

A. the vet refused to help because Tom was a student  
B. the dog was seriously ill and needed an operation  
C. the owner arrived at the hospital that same night  
D. Tom hoped someone might call him if they had news about the dog

**3. What is the best title for the passage?**

A. A Rainy Walk After School  
B. How to Train a Pet at Home  
C. A Kind Boy and a Lost Dog  
D. The Busy Life of a Young Vet

**4. The writer mainly wants to tell readers that ______.**

A. rainy days are perfect for walking in the park  
B. students should always carry bread in their bags  
C. finding a lost dog is more important than homework  
D. helping animals shows kindness and responsibility

**5. When did the owner most likely call Tom?**

A. On Friday night, before Tom went to bed.  
B. On Saturday morning, when Tom was at school.  
C. On Saturday evening, after Tom put up the poster.  
D. On Monday morning, before Tom went to the park.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第二段 “He slowly moved closer, spoke in a soft voice, and offered a piece of bread from his bag.”  
【解析】文中明确写汤姆靠近小狗、轻声说话并拿出面包。A 文中未提跑回家取伞；C 未报警；D “送医”在第三段，是在与狗建立信任之后，并非在板凳下发现后“立刻”发生。故排除 A、C、D，选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第三段 “He also wrote down his own phone number just in case someone asked about a missing dog.”  
【解析】汤姆留下电话号码，可推断他希望有人能提供关于走失狗的消息并联系他。A 与兽医帮忙矛盾；B 兽医说狗饥饿但健康；C 与第四段主人后来才来电不符。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题（标题）  
【定位】全文围绕汤姆救助迷路小狗并找到主人展开。  
【解析】C 概括“善良的孩子与迷路狗”最贴切。A 只强调雨天散步，偏离主线；B 训练宠物，文中无；D 主角不是兽医。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】写作目的题（非单纯细节）  
【定位】末段 “He learned that caring for a small life is not only kind but also a serious duty.”  
【解析】作者通过故事点明：帮助动物体现善良与责任。A、B 为局部或无关细节；C 文中未把找狗与作业作比较。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第四段 “The next morning, Tom made a simple poster… That evening, a woman called.”  
【解析】海报在发现狗的“次日早上”制作，电话在“当天晚上”，即贴出海报的当天晚上（周六晚）。A 为周五；B 早上与 “That evening” 矛盾；D 周一与文中周一“在公园见到”不符。故选 C。
