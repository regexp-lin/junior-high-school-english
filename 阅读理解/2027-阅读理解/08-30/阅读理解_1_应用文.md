# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 English Speech Contest Notice**

> **Lihua Middle School · English Department**
> **"My Voice, My World" — 2027 English Speech Contest**
> **Notice to All Students of Grades 7–9**

---

### 1. What Is It?

An open English speech contest for every student of our school. Each speaker will give a **3-minute prepared speech in English**, followed by **1 minute of questions** from the judges. The topic this year is: **"One small thing that changed the way I see my world."** Funny, serious, personal, or creative answers are all welcome.

### 2. Who Can Join?

| Group | Grades | Max. speakers per class |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Junior Group | Grade 7 | 4 |
| Middle Group | Grade 8 | 4 |
| Senior Group | Grade 9 | 4 |

Every speaker must be a full-time student of Lihua Middle School. Teachers may help with grammar **only**; the ideas, examples and storyline must come from the student.

### 3. Key Dates

- **Wed, 8 Sep** — Online sign-up opens on the school portal.
- **Fri, 17 Sep** — Sign-up closes at 18:00.
- **Mon, 20 Sep** — Class-level rounds held during English lessons.
- **Thu, 30 Sep** — Grade-level semi-finals, 16:00–18:00, School Hall B.
- **Sat, 16 Oct** — Whole-school final, 14:30–17:30, Main Auditorium. **Parents are warmly welcome.**

### 4. How to Prepare

Each speaker should hand in:

1. A **printed draft** of the 3-minute speech (about 350–420 words);
2. A **short note (≤100 words)** explaining why the topic matters to the speaker;
3. **Two small photos** (self photo + a photo of the "small thing" you want to talk about, if possible).

**Please do not use any AI chat tool to write your speech.** Judges will ask follow-up questions to check how well you understand your own words. Speeches mostly written by AI will be disqualified.

### 5. Judging

Five judges (three English teachers + two foreign teachers invited from partner schools) will give marks on four parts:

- **Content & Ideas** 40%
- **Language & Pronunciation** 30%
- **Stage Presence** 20%
- **Answers to Judges' Questions** 10%

### 6. Prizes

- **Gold Award** — 1 per group (book voucher **¥300** + one-year free access to the school English library corner).
- **Silver Award** — 2 per group (book voucher ¥150).
- **Bronze Award** — 3 per group (book voucher ¥80).
- **Best Story Award** (chosen by audience vote) — 1 across the whole school (a small trophy + a public speech training day at the city TV station, on **23 October**).

### 7. Contact

- **Ms Li Wenjing**, English Department office, 3F, Teaching Block A;
- Email: `speech2027@lihuaschool.cn`;
- Office hours: Mon–Fri, 13:00–13:30 and 16:30–17:30.

> **"Your English does not need to be perfect. Your story does need to be yours."**
> — English Department, Lihua Middle School

**注释：** prepared speech 演讲稿；semi-final 半决赛；book voucher 图书代金券；disqualify 取消资格

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the topic of the 2027 English Speech Contest?**

A. "My favourite English song."  
B. "My dream job in 2050."  
C. "One small thing that changed the way I see my world."  
D. "Why I love my school uniform."

**2. How many minutes will each speaker have in total on stage (speech + judges' questions)?**

A. About 2 minutes.  
B. About 3 minutes.  
C. About 4 minutes.  
D. About 10 minutes.

**3. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed according to the notice?**

A. Asking English teachers to help correct grammar.  
B. Bringing a small photo of the "small thing" in your speech.  
C. Using an AI chat tool to write most of your speech.  
D. Joining as a Grade 8 student in the Middle Group.

**4. Where and when will the whole-school final be held?**

A. School Hall B, 30 September.  
B. Main Auditorium, 16 October, in the afternoon.  
C. The school portal, 17 September.  
D. The city TV station, 23 October.

**5. Which line best shows the spirit the organisers want to send to the students?**

A. "Your English does not need to be perfect. Your story does need to be yours."  
B. "Speeches mostly written by AI will be disqualified."  
C. "Gold Award — 1 per group (book voucher ¥300)."  
D. "Office hours: Mon–Fri, 13:00–13:30."

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 条 "The topic this year is: 'One small thing that changed the way I see my world.'"  
【解析】通知明确主题。A、B、D 均未提及。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 条 "3-minute prepared speech ... followed by 1 minute of questions"  
【解析】3 + 1 = 4 分钟。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 条 "Please do not use any AI chat tool ... will be disqualified"  
【解析】禁止 AI 写演讲稿。A 允许老师改语法；B 允许带照片；D 八年级属中级组。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 条 "Sat, 16 Oct — Whole-school final, 14:30–17:30, Main Auditorium"  
【解析】10 月 16 日下午在主礼堂。A 为半决赛；C、D 无对应。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】写作意图题  
【定位】结尾语 "'Your English does not need to be perfect. Your story does need to be yours.'"  
【解析】此句承载活动精神——重视个人故事与真诚表达。B、C、D 为细则或联系方式，无法代表整体精神。故选 A。
