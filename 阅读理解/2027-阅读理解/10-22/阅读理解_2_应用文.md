# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Young Sophora" School Magazine · Call for Submissions (Issue 14)**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Editorial Team of *Young Sophora* 《青槐》**  
> **Winter 2027 Issue (No. 14) — "Small Lights in a Big City"**  
> **Writers from Grade 7, 8 and 9 are warmly welcome.**

Four times a year, our school magazine **《青槐》 *Young Sophora*** is printed in 1,200 copies, sent to every classroom, every teachers' office, every parent who asks for one, and our two partner schools in Gansu. The Winter 2027 issue (No. 14) will be mailed out on **20 December**. Its theme is **"Small Lights in a Big City"** — the tiny moments and people that quietly warm our busy lives. We are now calling for student submissions from across the school.

---

## ✒️ What We Are Looking For

1. **Short prose** (散文/小品文): 400 – 800 Chinese characters, or 250 – 500 English words. A person, a place, a conversation, a smell — anything the theme brings to your mind.  
2. **Poetry** (诗歌): 8 – 24 lines, Chinese or English. Please avoid rhyming just for the sake of rhyming.  
3. **Photo + caption**: one photo taken by you in our city, plus a caption of **20 – 40 Chinese characters** or **15 – 30 English words**. Please do **not** use AI-generated images.  
4. **Illustrated comic page**: one A4 page, no more than 8 panels, on a small true story that made you smile.  
5. **Mini-interview**: a 400-word record of a short talk with a worker you met in the city (noodle-stall owner, postman, school guard, park cleaner, taxi driver, etc.), with their permission.

---

## 📜 Rules for Every Piece

- **Your own work only.** Pieces that are copied from the internet, magazines, or AI will be disqualified, and the writer's name will be quietly shared with all class teachers.  
- **No real last names** of neighbours, classmates or teachers without their permission; you may use a single letter (e.g. "Mr. W") or change the name.  
- Please keep the language **clean and kind**: no mocking of people's looks, jobs, regions, or body shape.  
- One student may submit **at most two pieces** in total, across all types.

---

## 📮 How to Submit

- **Online:** school mini-app → "Magazines" → "Young Sophora" → upload. Save the file as  
  `班级_姓名_体裁_标题.docx` (e.g. `701_LiXing_散文_街角早餐摊.docx`).  
- **Paper:** drop in the green wooden box next to the library desk, marked **"青槐投稿"**.  
- Please include at the top of every piece: your **class**, **name**, **contact phone** (parent's OK), and a **one-sentence description** of why you chose this piece.

---

## ⏳ Important Dates

- **Submission window:** Monday **26 October 2027 → Friday 20 November 2027, 18:00**.  
- **First-round reading by student editors:** 23 – 27 November.  
- **Writer feedback (whether accepted, or asked to revise, or politely returned):** 30 November.  
- **Final issue printed:** 20 December.

---

## 🎁 Small Thanks to Writers

- Every writer whose piece is printed will receive **2 printed copies** of the issue, plus a handwritten card from our teacher-advisor Ms. Xu.  
- **Three "Editor's Pick" pieces per issue** will be read aloud at the **Winter Assembly on 30 December**, and each writer will receive a 150-yuan bookstore voucher.  
- Photos and illustrations chosen for the **cover** will earn an extra one-year free subscription to the city library.
