# 阅读理解（应用文） **Sunshine Community — Volunteer Call for the Elderly Care Week**

**SUNSHINE COMMUNITY CENTRE**
*Small hands, warm hearts — every minute counts.*

---

**Volunteer Recruitment — Summer 2027**

Sunshine Community Centre will hold its yearly **Elderly Care Week** from **Monday 3 August to Saturday 8 August 2027**. We are looking for middle-school volunteers to help make this week a happy one for over 120 senior neighbours.

**Who may apply?**

- Any student in Grade 7, 8 or 9 living in our community or nearby streets.
- Friendly, patient, and willing to work in a team.
- Able to keep to the chosen shift the whole week.
- No special skill needed — all training is given on Sunday 2 August.

**Time and place**

- **Training:** Sunday 2 August, 14:00 – 16:00, Hall B, Sunshine Community Centre
- **Service week:** Monday 3 August – Saturday 8 August, two shifts per day:
  - Morning shift: 08:30 – 11:30
  - Afternoon shift: 15:00 – 17:30
- Place: Elderly Activity Rooms (East Building) and the nearby Sunshine Park.

**Service roles**

| Role | What you do | Number needed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Reading Partner | Read daily news, short stories, or letters to seniors with weak eyes | 10 |
| Tech Helper | Teach seniors how to use video calls, health apps, and simple payments | 8 |
| Walk Buddy | Walk with a senior for 30 minutes in the park, stop for rest every 10 minutes | 12 |
| Craft Assistant | Help seniors make paper flowers, cloth bags, and simple drawings | 10 |
| Tea Table Helper | Prepare light tea and fruit; wash cups; keep tables tidy | 6 |

Students may pick **one** main role and **one** back-up role.

**What we provide**

- A bright green "Young Volunteer" vest (keep as a gift).
- Free fruit, water and a small snack for every shift.
- A certificate at the end of the week with hours of volunteer service recorded.
- A printed "Thank-you letter" signed by the community leader.

**What you should prepare**

- A neat haircut and short, clean nails.
- Comfortable shoes — no high heels, no slippers.
- A small notebook to write names and small tasks.
- A warm smile and a quiet voice — seniors enjoy both.

**Important rules**

1. **Walk slowly** in the East Building; many seniors use walking sticks.
2. **Do not take photos** of any senior without asking them first.
3. **Do not give food or medicine** — only water and the snacks we supply.
4. **Do not lift** a senior who has fallen; call staff Miss Zhao (room 101) at once.
5. **Do not accept money or gifts** from seniors or their families; a "thank you" is the best gift.

**How to sign up**

1. Collect a paper form from your classroom teacher or Room 202 of the Community Centre.
2. Ask a parent to sign the "permission" line at the bottom.
3. Return the form before **Friday 24 July, 17:00**.
4. The list of selected volunteers will be posted on **Tuesday 28 July** at the front door of the centre.

*Questions? Call Miss Zhao at 021-xxxx-7788, or email volunteer@sunshine.cn*

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When does the training take place?**

A. On Friday 24 July.  
B. On Sunday 2 August, 14:00 – 16:00.  
C. On Monday 3 August, early morning.  
D. On Saturday 8 August afternoon.

**2. Which role would most suit a student who loves technology?**

A. Reading Partner.  
B. Walk Buddy.  
C. Tech Helper.  
D. Craft Assistant.

**3. What does the centre provide to volunteers?**

A. A bicycle for each person.  
B. A green "Young Volunteer" vest, free fruit and snack, and a certificate.  
C. A daily payment of 100 yuan.  
D. Free holiday to a theme park.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed?**

A. Taking a senior's photo after asking for their permission.  
B. Giving medicine to a senior.  
C. Reading a short story to a senior with weak eyes.  
D. Calling Miss Zhao when a senior has fallen.

**5. What is the deadline to return the sign-up form?**

A. Friday 24 July, 17:00.  
B. Saturday 25 July.  
C. Tuesday 28 July.  
D. Sunday 2 August.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Training: Sunday 2 August, 14:00–16:00"  
【解析】8 月 2 日下午。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Tech Helper — Teach seniors..."  
【解析】教老人用 App，正适合爱科技的学生。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"What we provide" 列表  
【解析】马甲+水果零食+证书。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】规则 3 "Do not give food or medicine"  
【解析】不许给药。A/C/D 均允许/正确。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Return the form before Friday 24 July, 17:00"  
【解析】24 日 17:00。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。
