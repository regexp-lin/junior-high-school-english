# 阅读理解（应用文） **School Canteen — New Lunch Booking System**

**GREENWOOD MIDDLE SCHOOL · CANTEEN OFFICE**
*Good food, zero waste.*

---

**Notice to Students and Parents — August 2027**

Starting from **Monday, 1 September 2027**, our school canteen will use a new **online lunch booking** system called "**LunchPass**". The aim is to reduce food waste, shorten the queue, and let students pick meals that they really enjoy.

**How it works**

1. Every student gets a personal **LunchPass** QR code, printed on the back of the student ID card.
2. Open the app "**Greenwood Canteen**" on a parent's phone (or on the school tablet at the library) and sign in with the QR code.
3. Each Sunday, from 18:00 to 22:00, pick meals for the whole week.
4. From Monday, show the QR code at the canteen gate; the gate opens only for students who have booked.
5. After the meal, place the tray on the yellow return belt. The system checks if the plate is empty and gives an "eco-point".

**Weekly menu (sample)**

| Day | Option A (meat) | Option B (fish) | Option C (vegetarian) | Rice or noodles |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Monday | Chicken with broccoli | Steamed cod with lemon | Tofu with mushroom | Rice / Noodles |
| Tuesday | Beef stew | Tuna pasta | Corn-and-bean bowl | Rice |
| Wednesday | Pork meatball | Grilled salmon | Eggplant wrap | Noodles |
| Thursday | Chicken curry | Fish soup | Carrot rice balls | Rice |
| Friday | No meat day | No fish day | Vegetable pizza (whole school) | Rice |

All lunches come with: a small bowl of soup + one fresh fruit + one glass of water.

**Prices**

| Lunch | Student price | Guest / parent price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Option A, B or C | ¥15 | ¥22 |
| Extra bowl of rice or noodles | ¥3 | ¥4 |
| Special seasonal lunch (e.g. Mid-Autumn festival) | ¥20 | ¥25 |

Payment is made from a pre-loaded account. Parents can add money through the "Greenwood Canteen" app or at the school office.

**Bonus points**

- Book for all 5 days in a week: +1 point
- Eat all food on the tray (plate nearly empty): +1 point per meal
- Bring a reusable bottle for the whole week: +2 points

**Prize**: every 20 points can be exchanged for a free book from the library shelf "Canteen Reads".

**Rules**

1. Students must book for themselves. **Bookings made by a different student's account** are not allowed; the account will be paused for one week.
2. **No food may be taken out** of the canteen. We want to keep the school clean and prevent attracting insects.
3. Booked lunches cannot be cancelled after **Sunday 22:00**. Please plan carefully.
4. If a student is **ill** and cannot come to school, the parent should email the canteen office before 08:30. In such cases, the lunch cost is not charged.
5. Students with food allergies (nuts, seafood, etc.) must inform the canteen office **in writing** before 25 August.

**Contact**

- Phone: 021-xxxx-4455 (Mr. Wang, 08:30–17:30)
- Office: Building C, Room 108

*A thoughtful lunch keeps a busy mind bright. Thank you for helping us save food and energy.*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Each Sunday, from 18:00 to 22:00"  
【解析】每周日 18:00–22:00 预订。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"a small bowl of soup + one fresh fruit + one glass of water"  
【解析】汤+水果+水。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Bonus points" 列表  
【解析】三种加分方式。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"the account will be paused for one week"  
【解析】账号暂停一周。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"in writing before 25 August"  
【解析】8 月 25 日前书面告知。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
