# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — Autumn Calligraphy & Chinese Culture Week 2027**

> **Lihua Middle School · Student Cultural Committee**
> **Autumn Calligraphy & Chinese Culture Week — 2027**
> **"One Brush, One Breath, One Word" — Open Notice**

---

### 1. Why This Week?

Each autumn, our school invites students to slow down for five days and hold a brush. In a world of fast keyboards and quicker screens, the calligraphy brush asks for something rare — **patience, posture, and a steady breath**. This year, we are also inviting a few foreign pen-pal friends from our partner school in **Melbourne** to visit, watch, and try a few strokes themselves.

### 2. When & Where

- **Main Week**: Monday **11 October** — Friday **15 October 2027**
- **Main Hall**: Teaching Block C, 1F — "Ink Hall" (calligraphy activities)
- **Side Hall**: Teaching Block C, 2F — "Tea & Story Room" (culture talks, quiet reading)
- **Daily Open Hours**:
  - Students: 12:30 – 13:10 (lunch slot) and 16:00 – 17:30 (after class)
  - Guests & Parents: Wednesday 13 October only, 14:30 – 17:00

### 3. Five Daily Themes

| Day | Date | Theme | Activities |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mon | 11 Oct | **Brush & Posture** | Basic strokes; sitting & standing practice |
| Tue | 12 Oct | **Kaishu — Regular Script** | Copy the 8 strokes of 永 (yǒng) |
| Wed | 13 Oct | **Xingshu & Foreign Friends Day** | Open to parents & pen-pal friends |
| Thu | 14 Oct | **Couplets & Autumn Poems** | Write short 5-word poems |
| Fri | 15 Oct | **Exhibition & "Best Breath" Prize** | Student works on open walls |

### 4. Who May Take Part?

- **All students of Grades 7–9**. No earlier experience needed.
- Beginners **join for free**; ink, paper and brushes for practice are supplied by the school.
- Students who want to **keep their own good copy** for the Friday exhibition may buy a higher-quality rice-paper sheet for **¥3** at the check-in desk.

### 5. Rules in the Ink Hall

- Please **remove thick coats** before entering; ink is hard to remove from cloth.
- Phones **stay inside bags**; silent mode is not enough.
- **No food or sugar drinks** inside. A warm water station is provided.
- Help wipe your own table before leaving. **One brush, one breath, one clean desk.**

### 6. Friday Exhibition

- On Friday **15 October**, the Ink Hall becomes an open gallery from **16:00 to 19:00**.
- Up to **three works per student** may be put on the walls, with a small paper label (class + name + date).
- Three audience prizes will be given, chosen by student votes:
  - **Best Breath Prize** — calmest, steadiest writing
  - **Best Growth Prize** — biggest improvement during the week
  - **Best Foreign Friend Prize** — favourite work by a visiting pen-pal friend

### 7. Sign-Up

- **Online**: school portal → "Culture Week 2027"
- **Window**: Monday **20 September** – Sunday **3 October 2027** (before Tuesday 5 October morning assembly)
- After sign-up, you will get:
  - A personal **ink card** with your name and class printed on it;
  - A reminder message **one day before** each day you signed up for.

### 8. A Gentle Reminder

The calligraphy brush is a slow tool. It will forgive your mistakes only if you first **slow yourself down**. Do not worry about beautiful results. Write one honest stroke; then the next. The best students of this week will not always be those with the best works. They will be those who **came every day**, even when their hand was still shaking.

### 9. Contact

- **Ms Zhang Meilin**, Cultural Committee Teacher, Teaching Block C, Room 205.
- School WeChat group: "Lihua Culture Week 2027".
- Emergency: front desk of Teaching Block C.

> **"A good word on paper begins with a quiet breath in the chest."**
> — Lihua Student Cultural Committee

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When is the Calligraphy & Chinese Culture Week mainly held?**

A. 20 September – 3 October 2027.  
B. 11 October – 15 October 2027.  
C. 13 October – 19 October 2027.  
D. 30 October – 3 November 2027.

**2. On which day are parents and the foreign pen-pal friends **especially** invited to visit?**

A. Monday 11 October.  
B. Tuesday 12 October.  
C. Wednesday 13 October.  
D. Friday 15 October.

**3. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed in the Ink Hall?**

A. Practising sitting and standing posture.  
B. Bringing a hot instant-noodle cup for a quick lunch inside.  
C. Using free ink, paper and brushes supplied by the school.  
D. Remove thick coats before entering.

**4. According to the notice, who is most likely to win the "Best Breath Prize" on Friday?**

A. The student who finishes the most works in one hour.  
B. The student whose writing looks most calm and steady.  
C. The student who talks most in the hall.  
D. The student who uses the most ink.

**5. What is the main message of the notice's "gentle reminder" in paragraph 8?**

A. The best result is all that matters.  
B. Every student must write perfectly by Friday.  
C. Patience and daily effort are more important than a beautiful final work.  
D. Only talented students should take part.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 条 "Main Week: Monday 11 October — Friday 15 October 2027"  
【解析】主周 10 月 11 日至 15 日。A 为报名期；C、D 日期不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 条 "Guests & Parents: Wednesday 13 October only"；第 3 条 "Wed 13 Oct Xingshu & Foreign Friends Day"  
【解析】13 日为家长与外国笔友开放日。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节判断题（否定型）  
【定位】第 5 条 "No food or sugar drinks inside"  
【解析】严禁食品进入墨厅，方便面更不被允许。A、C、D 均为被允许/鼓励之事。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 6 条 "Best Breath Prize — calmest, steadiest writing"  
【解析】最平稳、沉着的书写获奖。A、C、D 与评选标准无关。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】写作意图题  
【定位】第 8 条 "The best students of this week ... those who came every day, even when their hand was still shaking"  
【解析】耐心与每日坚持胜于单一成品。A、B、D 均与作者精神相反。故选 C。
