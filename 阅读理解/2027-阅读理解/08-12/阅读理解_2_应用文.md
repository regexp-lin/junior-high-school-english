# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — English Festival 2027**

---

## **Welcome to English Festival 2027!**

From **November 13 to November 19, 2027**, Lihua Middle School will hold its tenth **English Festival** under this year's theme **"English, My Window on the World"**. For one busy week, our whole school will turn into a small English-speaking island — posters, music, drama, and games will fill the corridors. All students (Grade 7–9) and all teachers are warmly invited to join.

Last year, we had over **1,400 students** in 5 events. This year, we have added **two new events** and opened some of them to **our parents**, too.

---

## **Main Events**

| No. | Event | Time | Place | Open to |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Opening Ceremony** | Nov 13, Fri, 14:30 | Main Hall | Whole school |
| 2 | **Speech Contest** (3 min) | Nov 14, Sat, 9:00 | Main Hall | Top 2 from each class |
| 3 | **English Drama Show** (10 min) | Nov 15, Sun, 14:00 | Art Theatre | Class teams |
| 4 | **English Singing Night** | Nov 16, Mon, 18:30 | Big Playground | Student bands & solos |
| 5 | **English Corner with Parents (NEW)** | Nov 17, Tue, 16:30 | Reading Garden | Students + parents |
| 6 | **English Reading Marathon (NEW)** | Nov 18, Wed, 13:00–17:00 | Library | Students (by sign-up) |
| 7 | **Closing Ceremony & Awards** | Nov 19, Thu, 14:30 | Main Hall | Whole school |

---

## **How to Sign Up**

- **Class events (1, 7):** No sign-up. Every student comes with their class.  
- **Speech & Singing (2, 4):** Class teachers choose the top 2 representatives by **Nov 1**.  
- **Drama (3):** Each class forms a team of **4–8 students**, emails its script to `english@lihua-ms.cn` before **Oct 27**.  
- **English Corner with Parents (5):** Students invite **one parent**; please fill in the online form at **lihua.campus.cn/english** by **Nov 10**.  
- **Reading Marathon (6):** Sign up at the Library front desk; **first 80 students** only.

---

## **Rules and Tips**

1. Every speech or drama script must be **in English** (not translated on the spot). You may use simple Chinese stage names or a short Chinese summary in the printed programme.  
2. Please try to use **only English** at the English Corner; volunteer "translators" will help if someone is really stuck.  
3. No videos, no AI voice for the Speech Contest — your voice, your words, your eyes.  
4. **Costumes** for the drama show must be comfortable and safe — no long sharp props.  
5. No food is allowed in the Main Hall or Art Theatre; there is a snack area in the Reading Garden.

---

## **Special Guests**

- **Ms. Emma Carter**, an Australian middle-school teacher and story-teller, will give an opening speech and sit as a judge on the Speech Contest.  
- **Mr. Alex Nkomo**, a science journalist from Kenya, will hold a short talk, "**English and My Village**", on November 15, 16:00, in the Library.  
- **Old alumnae** who now work abroad will join the English Corner with Parents on Nov 17 and share short stories of their lives in new countries.

---

## **Prizes**

- **Best Speech** (1 each in Grades 7–9): a reading lamp and a new English book.  
- **Best Drama** (1 team): 1,200-yuan book-shop voucher + a trophy for the classroom.  
- **Best Singer / Band** (2 in total): a wireless microphone and a framed photo.  
- **Top 10 Readers** (in the marathon): a small badge and free entry to the school reading club for one year.  
- **"Most Friendly Corner"** (voted by parents): a happy-face certificate and a special story-book for the whole class to share.
