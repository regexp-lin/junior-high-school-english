# 阅读理解（记叙文） **A Morning in the School Radio Room**

① When our form teacher Ms. Jiang asked who would like to try a **Monday morning at the school radio station**, I slowly raised my hand. I am Jiayu, Class 8(4), and I have never liked the sound of my own voice in a loud room. But the radio room had always looked — through its small round window — as warm as a tea house. Ms. Jiang smiled. "Monday at seven. Be ten minutes early."

② The radio room sat at the end of the second-floor corridor, behind a green wooden door that said "**Campus Voices**". Inside, the walls were covered with **soft grey foam**. There were two old microphones on a long desk, a glass of water, a stopwatch, and a small clock that was exactly two minutes ahead of the rest of the school — "to keep us a little early, always early," the senior student told me with a tired smile.

③ My partner was Tongyu, a Grade 9 girl I had once seen running in the corridor with a school magazine under her arm. She handed me a thin folder: *Today's Programme*. There were four small parts: **(1) morning greeting, (2) a short poem, (3) a two-minute student news, and (4) one request song from a listener**. I was to read part (2), the poem. My eyes met the title: "*A Slow Start to Monday*." Seven lines. It seemed short. It was not short.

④ At 07:18 the red **"ON AIR"** light above the door turned on. I could hear the playground through the closed window: a thousand feet slowly walking towards the sports ground, a few teachers calling out class numbers. Tongyu took a deep breath, smiled at me, and said softly: "Good morning, dear Lihua. This is Campus Voices — Monday, 1 November 2027." Her voice was low, round, and **clear as a river stone**.

⑤ Then the poem came. I held the page in two hands, because I had read once that both hands make the voice **calmer**. I could feel my own heart in my throat, but I remembered what Ms. Jiang had told us in English class: "**Read to one person, not to the whole school.**" So I closed my eyes for a half-second and decided to read for my younger cousin Chenchen, who often ate breakfast alone. "*The morning begins in the small bird's throat, before the bus...*" My voice, which I had worried about for a week, sounded — in my own ear — **surprised but steady**.

⑥ Part (3) was Tongyu's short news. She told the school that the **library had thirty new picture books** and that the football field had a small repair on Wednesday. Her tone was light, her rhythm was steady, and she never, ever rushed. In the back of the room, the clock ticked softly, two minutes ahead as always.

⑦ The last part was the **request song**. That Monday the song was from a Grade 7 student named Xuemei, for her grandfather, who had begun a long hospital stay. Tongyu said his name, and her voice, for a second, carried something tender. Then she lowered the microphone and pressed play. The song was an old Chinese piece for the **erhu**, played slowly, with long quiet spaces between the notes. For two and a half minutes neither of us spoke. The "ON AIR" light kept burning. I watched the red glow and thought — in some quiet way — of all the classrooms across the school listening to the same small stream of sound.

⑧ At 07:32 we unplugged our microphones. Tongyu turned to me and said: "Thank you, Jiayu. You read the poem **like a person, not a speaker**." I did not know what to say, so I simply nodded and helped her fold the programme sheet. On my way down the stairs, the 'ON AIR' light had already turned off, and the corridor was loud with classmates' voices. But my own steps felt light, as if I had left behind something small and tight in that grey-foam room. **The school radio station, I realised, had not only sent a voice out to the school that morning; it had also quietly returned a new voice to me.** After that Monday, whenever someone asked, I would say yes, without raising my hand to the ceiling — and I always arrived ten minutes early.

**注释：** radio station 广播站；corridor 走廊；foam 泡沫；microphone 麦克风；erhu 二胡

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the writer raise her hand to try a Monday at the radio station?**

A. Because her classmates asked her to.  
B. Because although she had never liked the sound of her own voice in a loud room, the radio room had always looked, through its small round window, as warm as a tea house.  
C. Because the station paid students.  
D. Because she wanted to skip P.E.

**2. What four small parts made up the programme?**

A. A song, a dance, a news, and a joke.  
B. A morning greeting, a short poem, a two-minute student news, and a request song.  
C. Two songs and two news parts.  
D. Only a poem and a song.

**3. Why did the writer hold the poem page with both hands?**

A. Because the page was heavy.  
B. Because she was cold.  
C. Because she had read once that both hands make the voice calmer.  
D. Because she wanted to hide her face.

**4. Why did Tongyu's voice "carry something tender" during part (4)?**

A. Because the song was loud.  
B. Because she was tired.  
C. Because the request song was for a Grade 7 student's grandfather who had just begun a long hospital stay.  
D. Because she did not like the erhu.

**5. What did the writer mean in the last paragraph by "the radio station had also quietly returned a new voice to me"?**

A. The station gave her a free microphone.  
B. Reading on air helped her find a calmer, steadier voice inside herself, so she later said "yes" to new tasks more bravely.  
C. She received a new name.  
D. She could now speak two languages.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "never liked the sound of my own voice ... looked ... as warm as a tea house"  
【解析】双重动机直引。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "(1) morning greeting, (2) a short poem, (3) a two-minute student news, and (4) one request song"  
【解析】节目单直引。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "both hands make the voice calmer"  
【解析】阅读习惯直引。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑦段 "from a Grade 7 student named Xuemei, for her grandfather, who had begun a long hospital stay"  
【解析】背景原因。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑧段 "felt light ... after that Monday ... I would say yes, without raising my hand to the ceiling"  
【解析】外在声音与内在信心的双重成长。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。
