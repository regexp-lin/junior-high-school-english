# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School Winter Uniform-Swap & Donation Week (Nov 2027)**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Student Union + Moral Education Office**  
> **2027 Winter Uniform-Swap & Donation Week**  
> **Your old uniform can still keep someone warm.**

Every year, hundreds of our students grow **two or three centimetres** between September and February. Many autumn and winter uniforms are still almost new — clean, with a zip in good order — but too short at the wrist or too tight at the shoulder. They sit, folded, at the bottom of family cupboards. From **Monday 9 November to Saturday 14 November 2027**, our school will run a simple plan so that these clean uniforms find a second life.

---

## 👕 Two Ways You Can Help

**(1) Swap** — Give a uniform you have outgrown, and take one that fits, **at no cost**.  
**(2) Donate** — Give a uniform or a warm coat that you no longer need, so that it can travel to our partner village schools in Gansu this December.

You may do **one** or **both**.

---

## 📅 Where and When

| Day | Time | Place | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mon 9 Nov | 16:00 – 17:30 | Small hall (east wing) | Dropping off: winter jackets, padded trousers. |
| Tue 10 Nov | 16:00 – 17:30 | Small hall | Dropping off: sports uniforms, fleece. |
| Wed 11 Nov | 16:00 – 17:30 | Small hall | **Swap counter** opens (first half-hour for Grade 7). |
| Thu 12 Nov | 12:30 – 13:30 + 16:00 – 17:30 | Small hall | Swap counter full-open; gentle ironing service. |
| Fri 13 Nov | 16:00 – 17:00 | Small hall | Last swap day; parents welcome to help sort. |
| Sat 14 Nov | 09:00 – 11:00 | School gate | Loading day for Gansu delivery. Volunteers please sign up. |

---

## 📝 Simple Rules for Donated Items

- Clean at home first. Our school washing machines cannot handle a full week's drop-offs.  
- Zips, buttons, knees in good order. No very large holes; a small, well-mended hole is fine.  
- **No underwear, no socks, no shoes**, for hygiene reasons.  
- For donation (not swap), remove name tags if possible — our partners prefer anonymous gifts.

---

## 🏷 How the Swap Counter Works

- Each donated uniform earns you a **small ticket** (one item, one ticket).  
- You may take **up to three uniform pieces** using your tickets, as long as sizes fit.  
- **Tall students and very short students** can ask the Student Union office for help; we keep a small "special sizes" box behind the counter.  
- If a uniform does not suit any of our students in November, it will be quietly moved into the Gansu donation pile in early December.

---

## 🤝 Who Can Volunteer?

- **Folders (10 students a day)** — learn to quickly fold jackets the "airline way".  
- **Checkers (4 students a day)** — check zips, buttons and cleanness with a kind attitude.  
- **Carriers (8 students on Saturday morning)** — move boxes to the delivery van. Need the form-teacher's signed agreement.  
- **Writers (5 students)** — prepare a short Chinese-English label for each Gansu box ("winter jackets, size M, 12 pieces"). Good English writing practice!

Sign up at the Student Union office, Room 218, before **Friday 6 November, 17:00**.

---

## 💡 Small Reminders

- Come **in your own clothes** on drop-off day; you will be moving, folding, and smiling a lot.  
- Bring a small **reusable bag** to carry your swapped items home.  
- All activities are completely free. We do not sell anything and do not accept money in the hall.  
- If you prefer to **donate quietly**, write "ANON" on the label. No one will ask.

---

## 📖 One Story from Last Year

> "Last winter we sent 412 clean uniforms to two partner schools in Gansu. On a Thursday in January, a Grade 8 girl named Xiaoqing wrote to us: '**Thank you for the blue coat. The pocket on the right side still smells a little like oranges. I do not know who this coat belonged to, but she must have been a kind reader.**' The orange smell was from the last owner's schoolbag, during the school fruit week in October. It stayed in the coat all the way to Gansu."

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What are the TWO ways a student can help in this activity?**

A. Buy old uniforms; sell new ones.  
B. Swap a uniform they have outgrown for one that fits, at no cost; and/or donate uniforms or warm coats to partner village schools in Gansu.  
C. Only donate money.  
D. Only lend clothes for a day.

**2. Which of the following items is NOT accepted?**

A. A clean winter jacket.  
B. A well-mended small hole on a sports uniform.  
C. Underwear, socks, or shoes.  
D. Padded trousers with a good zip.

**3. How many uniform pieces can a student take using her tickets?**

A. Only one.  
B. Up to three pieces, as long as the sizes fit.  
C. Unlimited.  
D. Only items from Grade 9.

**4. Who should sign up for the Saturday morning "Carrier" volunteer role?**

A. Anyone who arrives first.  
B. Students with a form-teacher's signed agreement, who will help move boxes to the delivery van.  
C. Only Grade 9 students.  
D. Only parents.

**5. What is the main idea of the story at the end of the page?**

A. Uniforms should smell like oranges.  
B. Students should not bring fruit to school.  
C. Clean, cared-for uniforms can carry real warmth and kindness across long distances, "first to you, then to someone you will never meet".  
D. Gansu schools never say thank you.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Two Ways You Can Help 段  
【解析】双路径直引。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（否定）  
【定位】Simple Rules 段 "No underwear, no socks, no shoes, for hygiene reasons"  
【解析】禁止直引。A、B、D 均合规。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How the Swap Counter Works "up to three uniform pieces using your tickets, as long as sizes fit"  
【解析】上限直引。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Who Can Volunteer "Carriers ... Need the form-teacher's signed agreement"  
【解析】资格直引。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】结尾故事 + "first you, then someone you will never meet"  
【解析】分享温暖的主旨凝练。A、B、D 均偏离。故选 C。
