# 阅读理解（应用文） **Summer English Immersion Camp — "Talk Like a River"**

---

**Talk Like a River** · English Immersion Camp 2027  
*Five days on the Green River, no Chinese, only English.*

---

**1. Programme at a Glance**

A **5-day English-only day camp** by the Green River, designed for middle school students who want to speak English **more** and fear English **less**. Campers will cook, play, explore, and solve problems — all in English, helped by native English tutors and Chinese-English bilingual teachers.

---

**2. Dates & Place**

- **Dates:** Monday, 5 July – Friday, 9 July 2027  
- **Time:** 8:30 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. (Monday to Friday)  
- **Place:** Green River Sports Park (bus pick-up from 4 school gates, see website)

---

**3. Target**

- **Level A** (just-started speakers): Grade 7 students, beginners  
- **Level B** (daily talkers): Grade 7–8 students, around 4 years of English  
- **Level C** (confident talkers): Grade 8–9, ready for speech and debate  
- Campers are **placed in levels** by a short 10-minute English chat on the first day.

---

**4. Main Activities**

| Day | Theme | Sample activity |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | "Hello, New Friend" | Name-games, English songs, team flags |
| 2 | "Outdoor Maths" | Measuring trees, counting steps, market-maths role-play |
| 3 | "Kitchen English" | Making fruit salad + pizza; speaking only English while cooking |
| 4 | "Green Science" | Water sampling; short science talks to classmates |
| 5 | "Stage Day" | 3-minute speeches; a short drama; final awards |

---

**5. Camp Rules**

1. **Chinese-Free Rule:** From the opening bell to the closing bell, speak only English. Need help? Ask a tutor in English.  
2. **Phones** must stay in the locked camp box during activities; returned at home time.  
3. **Closed shoes** required — no sandals or slippers.  
4. **Hats, water bottles, and sunscreen** are strongly recommended.  
5. One **healthy snack box** per day from home is welcome; no sweets before lunch.

---

**6. Fee & Application**

| Item | Cost |
|------|-----|
| Full 5 days | **1,280 yuan** |
| Early-bird fee (before 15 June) | 1,080 yuan |
| Sibling (second child) discount | 200 yuan off |

- **Scholarship:** 10 full-fee waivers for students from low-income families.  
- **How to apply:** Scan the QR code on our poster or visit **www.riverenglish2027.cn**. Deadline: **30 June, 6:00 p.m.**

---

**7. What You Get**

- A **daily diary in English** to track new words, new friends, and new fun  
- A **photo wall memory book** at the end of the camp  
- A **"Talk Like a River" certificate** for your school file  
- For 3 top-scoring campers: a **free online video-call** with a native teacher for 6 months

---

> Questions? Call Teacher Bai at 187-7788-0033 or email **camp@riverenglish.cn**.  
> *"A river speaks to the sea every day. This summer, come and speak with us."*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第1节 "5-day"  
【解析】共五天。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第3节 "short 10-minute English chat"  
【解析】通过10分钟口语对话分级。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第4节 Day 3 "Making fruit salad + pizza"  
【解析】第三天为厨房英语。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第6节 "Early-bird fee (before 15 June) 1,080 yuan"  
【解析】早鸟价1080元。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第7节 "free online video-call... for 6 months"  
【解析】前三名获得6个月外教网课。故选 B。
