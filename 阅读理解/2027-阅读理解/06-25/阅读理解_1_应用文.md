# 阅读理解（应用文） **Community Flea Market — Saturday 17 July 2027**

**SUNNY HARBOUR NEIGHBOURHOOD COMMITTEE**
*Old items, new homes.*

---

**Community Flea Market Notice**

After two years, our small flea market is back! Neighbours of all ages are welcome to come and exchange the things they no longer need. It is a simple way to tidy your home, save money and meet neighbours.

**Basic information**

- **Date:** Saturday 17 July 2027.
- **Time:** 09:30 – 16:30 (rain or shine; in heavy rain, we move to the underground garage).
- **Place:** grassy square in front of Block B, Sunny Harbour Community.
- **Entrance:** free; no ticket needed.

**Who can set up a stall?**

- Any family that lives in our community, one stall per family.
- At least one seller at the stall must be an adult (18+).
- Children and teenagers are very welcome to sit at the stall **with** their parents, and to run the small transactions themselves (this is part of the fun).

**How to sign up for a stall**

1. Ask the security office at Block A for a red form, or download it from the community WeChat group.
2. Fill in your name, flat number, and a short list of the items you plan to sell.
3. Return the form before **Sunday 11 July, 18:00**. Stalls are given first-come, first-served; only 40 stalls in total.
4. On the morning of 17 July, come to the east corner at **08:30** to pick up your stall number and table.

**What can be sold**

- Clean second-hand clothes, shoes, bags, hats.
- Old books, toys, study tools, board games.
- Small electronics (only in working order; we have one volunteer to test before 10:00).
- Home-made non-hot food (biscuits, bread, jam) in sealed packs.

**What CAN NOT be sold**

- Fresh meat, fish or hot cooked dishes (safety reasons).
- Medicine, sharp tools bigger than 15 cm, fireworks, live animals.
- Luxury items or anything valued over **500 yuan**. Our market is for simple daily items only.

**Prices and payment**

- All sellers are asked to put a clear paper price tag on every item.
- Small items should not be priced over 30 yuan.
- Payment can be cash or WeChat/Alipay QR code. Please do NOT take cards.
- Our committee will not handle disputes about price. Sellers and buyers talk politely and decide together.

**Green rules**

- Bring a cloth bag for your purchases. The committee does not provide plastic bags.
- Each stall must take away all unsold items and rubbish by 17:00.
- Sellers who leave rubbish on the grass will not be allowed to set up again next year.

**Special corner**

- The "**Book-for-Book**" corner (near the flower bed): bring one used book, take one. No money involved. Comics are very welcome.

**Contact**

Sunny Harbour Committee · Block A Room 101 · 021-xxxx-2200 · market@sunnyharbour.cn
*See you under the July sun!*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"grassy square in front of Block B..."  
【解析】地点明确。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】可售列表  
【解析】旧玩具与桌游允许。A/C/D 属禁售。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Return the form before Sunday 11 July, 18:00"  
【解析】7 月 11 日前。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"please do NOT take cards"  
【解析】不收银行卡。A/B/C 均可。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Book-for-Book' corner..."  
【解析】一书换一书，不涉及金钱。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
