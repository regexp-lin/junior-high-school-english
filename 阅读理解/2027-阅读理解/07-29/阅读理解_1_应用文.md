# 阅读理解（应用文） **"City Little Hands" — Museum Volunteer Programme for Grade 7–9 Students**

**Central City Museum · Semester 1, 2027**

Hello, young history lovers!

Our **"City Little Hands"** programme is back for its seventh year. Every Saturday of the autumn term, we welcome 36 middle-school students to help visitors, guide younger kids, and learn the quiet, careful skills behind a museum. No experience needed — only curiosity and a kind smile.

---

### Basic Information

- **Dates:** Every Saturday, September 13 – December 13, 2027 (14 sessions)  
- **Time:** 09:30 – 12:00 (lunch box provided after 12:00 for volunteers who stay)  
- **Place:** Central City Museum, 88 Lake Road  
- **Who:** 36 students, Grade 7–9  
- **Cost:** Free. Public bus fare can be reimbursed up to ¥8 per day with ticket receipts.

---

### What Volunteers Do

We have **three teams**. You choose one for the whole term.

| Team | Main Job | Good for students who … |
|------|---------|--------------------------|
| **A. Greeting Team** | Welcome visitors at the gate, answer simple questions, help with maps, guide lost children to their parents. | Are friendly and like talking to strangers. |
| **B. Mini-Guide Team** | Lead groups of 4–6 younger kids (age 7–10) on a short 30-minute "Kids' Tour" of three rooms. Scripts are provided. | Enjoy speaking aloud and working with younger children. |
| **C. Care-and-Clean Team** | Help museum staff clean small display tools, check room temperature, and prepare materials for weekend workshops. | Are patient, careful, and interested in behind-the-scenes work. |

---

### Training Plan

| Week | Content |
|------|---------|
| Week 1 (Sept 13) | Safety & museum rules; team choice |
| Week 2 (Sept 20) | Main exhibits — history of our city |
| Week 3 (Sept 27) | Speaking training (Team B) / Greeting scripts (Team A) / Cleaning tools (Team C) |
| Week 4 (Oct 4) | **Holiday — no session** |
| Weeks 5 – 14 | Regular duty + two short talks by museum curators |

On the last day (Dec 13), each volunteer gives a **2-minute closing speech** in the hall about the most touching moment of the term.

---

### Rules

1. **Arrive 15 minutes early.** Wear the white volunteer T-shirt (given in Week 1) over your own clothes.  
2. **No phones on duty.** Keep your phone in the locker room; use it only on break.  
3. **Be honest.** If you do not know an answer, kindly say "Let me ask my teacher for you." Never make up information.  
4. **Miss no more than 3 sessions** in the whole term, or you cannot receive the final certificate.  
5. **Parents welcome to visit**, but please do not stand next to the student while they are on duty.

### What You Will Get

- A "City Little Hands" certificate, signed by the museum director.  
- **20 community service hours**, officially counted for your school record.  
- A printed album with group photos at the end of the term.  
- One free family pass to any weekend exhibition in 2028.

### How to Apply

1. Fill in the online form at **www.centralcitymuseum.cn/little-hands** by **August 25, 2027, 23:59**.  
2. Upload a **100-word Chinese or English short paragraph**: *Why do I want to join?*  
3. Selected students will receive a phone call by September 3 for a 10-minute friendly interview (at the museum or by video call).  
4. The final list will be published on **www.centralcitymuseum.cn/little-hands** and on our WeChat page on **September 8**.

### Contact

- Central City Museum, Volunteer Office, Room B-12  
- Phone: 0571-8833-0913 (Mon – Fri, 10:00 – 16:00)  
- Email: littlehands@ccm.cn  
- WeChat: search "CCM-LittleHands"

**Come, give a quiet Saturday, and you will take home a whole season of stories.**

*— Central City Museum, July 2027*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Basic Information "14 sessions"  
【解析】共 14 次。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】What Volunteers Do 表 "C. Care-and-Clean Team ... patient, careful..."  
【解析】耐心细致、对幕后感兴趣适合 C 队。A/B 不符；D 与条件矛盾。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules 第 4 条  
【解析】缺席超 3 次即无证书。A/B/D 均与规则相反。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】What You Will Get 全段  
【解析】证书+20 小时+相册+家庭通票。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How to Apply 第 3 条  
【解析】最后一步是 10 分钟友好面试。A/B 为前一步；D 无此项。故选 C。
