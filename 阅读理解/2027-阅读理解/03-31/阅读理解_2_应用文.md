# 阅读理解（应用文） **Green Earth Poster Contest — City Environment Week**

① Hello, young artists! The **City Environment Office** and our school are jointly holding the **2027 Green Earth Poster Contest**. We are looking for bright ideas that help more people protect our **air, water, and forests**. Your pen, your brush, or even your tablet screen can be the next voice of the earth.

② **Contest Basics**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Theme | **"Small Hands, Big Earth"** |
| For | Students of Grade 7, Grade 8, and Grade 9 |
| Entry fee | Free |
| Work size | **A3 paper** (297 × 420 mm) only |
| Style | Hand-drawn OR computer-drawn; mixed style OK |

③ **Poster Topics — Choose ONE**

1. **Save the river** — say no to plastic in our city river.  
2. **Walk, bike, or subway** — smart ways to travel.  
3. **One tree, one year** — students planting one tree every spring.  
4. **The kitchen saves the earth** — reduce food waste at home.  
5. **Small birds, our neighbours** — protect the birds in our parks.

④ **Key Rules**

- Every student can send **at most 2 works**; each work on one topic only.  
- Posters must be your **own idea and drawing**; no copy from the internet.  
- Put these on the back: **name, class, topic number, and a short title**.  
- The contest organisers may translate your title into English and Chinese.  
- Any work with violent, rude, or commercial symbols will not be judged.

⑤ **Important Dates**

- **10 April**　Sign-up opens on the Green Earth website.  
- **28 April**　Hand in the finished poster to the **Art Office, Room 306**, between 12:20 and 13:00 or 16:30 and 17:30.  
- **30 April – 3 May**　Teachers & three guest artists judge.  
- **5 May**　Top 20 posters shown in the school hall.  
- **10 May**　City-level Environment Week opens; best 5 posters printed on **metro station boards** in our city for 15 days.

⑥ **Prizes**

| Level | How many | Prize |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Gold | 1 | A tablet for drawing + a certificate |
| Silver | 3 | A set of quality pens + a certificate |
| Bronze | 6 | A sketch notebook + a certificate |
| Encouragement | 10 | A green planting kit + a certificate |

⑦ **Kind Tips**

1. Think before you draw: which topic touches your heart most?  
2. Plan with a **pencil sketch first**, then add colour.  
3. Use **fewer than 30 Chinese characters** on a poster; let pictures speak.  
4. Keep your poster **clean and flat**; use a big file to carry it.  
5. Photograph your finished work at home, so you will always have a copy.

⑧ **Contact**  
- School Art Office, Ms. He — he.art@bfms.cn  
- Green Earth Contest — info@greenearth.cn / 400-800-2027

**A poster is a small window. Open it, and let more people see a greener tomorrow.**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Who can take part in the Green Earth Poster Contest?**

A. Only Grade 7 students in the city.  
B. Any adult who loves drawing.  
C. Students of Grade 7, 8, and 9.  
D. Children of kindergarten age.

**2. How many works can one student send?**

A. One only.  
B. At most two, each on one topic.  
C. At most five.  
D. As many as the student wants.

**3. When do students hand in the finished poster?**

A. 10 April.  
B. 28 April, during the hand-in time windows.  
C. 30 April to 3 May.  
D. 10 May.

**4. What will happen to the best 5 posters?**

A. They will be thrown away after the contest.  
B. They will be printed on metro station boards for 15 days.  
C. They will be sold at a shop.  
D. They will only be emailed to parents.

**5. Which of the following is NOT a "kind tip" for students?**

A. Plan with a pencil sketch first, then add colour.  
B. Keep the poster clean and flat with a big file.  
C. Use many long sentences and tiny pictures.  
D. Photograph your finished work at home.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Students of Grade 7, Grade 8, and Grade 9"  
【解析】7–9 年级参赛。A、B、D 不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段第 1 条 "at most 2 works; each work on one topic only"  
【解析】最多两份。A、C、D 与原文不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】日期定位题  
【定位】第⑤段 28 April — "Hand in the finished poster"  
【解析】4 月 28 日交稿。A 开放报名；C 评审；D 地铁站展出。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 10 May "best 5 posters printed on metro station boards"  
【解析】前 5 张印于地铁展板。A、C、D 与原文不符。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节否定题  
【定位】第⑦段 "fewer than 30 Chinese characters on a poster; let pictures speak"  
【解析】建议文字少、画面大。A、B、D 都属于建议。故选 C。
