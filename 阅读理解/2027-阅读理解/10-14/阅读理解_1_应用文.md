# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 Autumn "Poems Under the Sky" Recitation Festival**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Chinese & English Departments + School Choir**  
> **2027 Autumn "Poems Under the Sky" Recitation Festival**  
> **An evening of Chinese and English poetry, in our own voices**

The leaves are turning yellow. The wind is cold and clear. On **Friday 30 October 2027**, the school playground will be closed to classes but open to **words** — spoken out loud, by students from Grade 7 to Grade 9. Come and share a poem, or just come to listen.

---

## 🌙 Event at a Glance

- **Date:** Friday 30 October 2027  
- **Time:** 17:30 – 20:00  
- **Place:** School playground (moved to **Lecture Hall** if it rains)  
- **Language:** Chinese or English, up to you  
- **Audience:** students, teachers, parents, alumni — **free admission**, no ticket needed

---

## 🎤 Who Can Perform

- **Solo**: one student on stage, 2 – 4 minutes.  
- **Pair**: two students, 3 – 5 minutes.  
- **Small group**: 3 – 6 students, **maximum 6 minutes**; at least one poem must be **written by a student in the group**.

Every performer must sign up by **Monday 20 October, 17:00**. Forms are at the **Chinese Office (Room 302)** or on the **school mini-app**.

---

## 📜 Rules for Poem Choice

1. **Chinese poems**: any classical poem from Tang or Song dynasties, or a modern Chinese poem published after 1980, is welcome.  
2. **English poems**: choose from the **school reading list** (a green leaflet handed out in English class). Poems outside the list must be approved by your English teacher.  
3. **Student-written poems** are warmly welcomed at the small-group stage, but must be **at least 12 lines** and about a theme you care about: school, family, nature, friendship, the city. Please avoid political slogans and commercial advertising.  
4. **No backing music played from a phone.** If you want music, the School Choir can play **simple live music** (guitar, piano, erhu) if you give us your poem two weeks before the show.

---

## 🎁 Prizes & Awards

- **Best Voice Award** × 3 — 200-yuan bookstore voucher + recording session in the school radio room.  
- **Best Chinese Recitation** × 2, **Best English Recitation** × 2 — 150-yuan voucher each.  
- **Best Original Poem** × 1 — your poem will be printed on the school's 2028 **new-year greeting card** sent to every family.  
- **Warm Audience Award** × 3 (voted by the crowd with paper hearts handed out at the entrance).  
- All performers receive a hand-drawn **Autumn Poem Stamp** by Art Club students.

---

## 📺 Live and Record

- The festival will be **live-streamed** on the school mini-app, so parents working night shifts can still watch.  
- A DVD of the whole evening will be given to each form teacher in mid-November, for future class parties.  
- If any student prefers **not to be filmed**, please tick the "no camera" box when you sign up; we will seat the cameras so you are out of frame.

---

## ☂️ Rain Plan & Safety

- Weather decision posted at **12:00 on 30 Oct** via mini-app.  
- Chairs will be arranged in 12 rows; please keep the centre aisle clear.  
- **No food or drink** on the playground, but hot tea and biscuits are available at the east gate from 17:00.

---
