# 阅读理解（应用文） **Spring Astronomy Night Camp 2027**

---

**Skylight Middle School × Mount White Observatory**  
*One night, one sky, a hundred thousand stars.*

---

**1. A One-Night Trip**

Every year, we run a **one-night astronomy camp** on the quiet, dark side of **Mount White** — a great place to see the stars because of its low light pollution. This year, our theme is "**From the Moon to Mars**". Teachers, students, and astronomy lovers are warmly welcome.

---

**2. Date & Venue**

- **Date:** Saturday, 8 May – Sunday, 9 May 2027  
- **Leaving school:** 2:30 p.m., Saturday (by tourist bus)  
- **Returning to school:** 9:30 a.m., Sunday  
- **Camp site:** Mount White Observatory, altitude 1,200 m

---

**3. Open To**

- Students of **Grade 7–9** in Skylight Middle School (max. **40 students**)  
- Any astronomy-loving teacher or parent (max. **6 adults**)  
- Students must be able to **walk 2 km on mountain paths** without difficulty.

---

**4. Main Activities**

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 4:30 p.m. | Check-in, tent setting-up |
| 5:30 p.m. | Hot dinner at the camp kitchen |
| 6:30 p.m. | "From Moon to Mars" — talk by Dr. Zhang |
| 7:30 p.m. | First star-watching through big telescopes |
| 9:00 p.m. | Group challenge: "Find 5 Constellations" |
| 11:00 p.m. | Sleeping time in tents (4 per tent) |
| 4:45 a.m. | Sunrise from the eastern peak |
| 7:30 a.m. | Breakfast + mini-quiz "Name that Planet" |

---

**5. What to Bring**

| Must bring | Do NOT bring |
|------------|-------------|
| Warm jacket + long trousers | Fireworks of any kind |
| Comfortable shoes | Laser pens / bright torches |
| Water bottle (≥ 500 ml) | Pets |
| Torch with red film (**red light does not hurt night vision**) | Video game consoles |
| Toothbrush, towel, simple personal items | Expensive jewellery |

---

**6. Fee**

- **Student:** 280 yuan (bus + food + tent + insurance)  
- **Adult:** 350 yuan  
- Students from **low-income families** may apply for a **full-fee waiver**. Ask Teacher Lee in Room 205.

---

**7. How to Sign Up**

Step 1: Read this notice carefully with your parents.  
Step 2: Download and print the **"Health + Safety Form"** from the school website.  
Step 3: Ask a parent to **sign** the form.  
Step 4: Hand in the form to Teacher Lee by **Wednesday, 28 April, 5 p.m.**  
Step 5: We will publish the list on Friday, 30 April.
