# 阅读理解（应用文） **Sunrise Summer Camp — Outdoor Programme for Middle-School Students**

**Sunrise Education Centre · August 3 – August 9, 2027**

Hello, explorers of Grade 7–9! Are you tired of long hot afternoons in front of the TV? **Sunrise Summer Camp** is back — seven days of hiking, camping, star-watching, river-study and team games at Green Valley Mountain. Whether you love science, sports, or making new friends, there is something for you.

---

### At a Glance

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Dates** | Monday, Aug 3 – Sunday, Aug 9, 2027 (7 days / 6 nights) |
| **Place** | Green Valley Mountain Base (1.5-hour bus ride from the city) |
| **Who** | Students born between 2012 and 2015 (Grade 7–9 in Sept 2027) |
| **Group size** | 30 students per group; 1 leader to every 6 students |
| **Cost** | ¥1,880 per student (meals, lodging, insurance, guide fee all included) |
| **Sign-up closes** | Friday, July 25, 23:59 |

---

### Daily Schedule (Example)

- **06:30** Morning run by the river  
- **07:30** Breakfast (buffet, student-served)  
- **09:00** Main activity of the day (see table below)  
- **12:00** Lunch and quiet reading hour  
- **14:30** Skill workshop (knot-tying, map-reading, fire-safety, etc.)  
- **17:30** Free time: swimming pool, table tennis, small library  
- **18:30** Dinner around the big wooden table  
- **20:00** Evening talk, songs or star-watching  
- **22:00** Lights out

### Main Activities by Day

| Day | Activity | Notes |
|-----|---------|------|
| Mon | Arrival & campsite games | Leaders teach safety rules |
| Tue | Team hiking (8 km, easy trail) | Bring your own water bottle |
| Wed | River biology: catching and releasing small creatures | Waterproof shoes provided |
| Thu | Star-watching night (telescopes provided) | Bring a warm jacket |
| Fri | "Lost in the Forest" orienteering game | No phones allowed |
| Sat | Cooking day: students prepare lunch in small teams | Parents may join online at 12:30 |
| Sun | Closing ceremony & return bus to city | Arrive city by 18:00 |

---

### What to Bring

- A strong backpack (30 L)  
- Three changes of clothes (including one warm jacket)  
- A pair of comfortable walking shoes  
- Personal items: toothbrush, soap, sunscreen, towel  
- A simple notebook and two pens  
- A small gift from your hometown (for the "share-your-city" night)

### What **NOT** to Bring

- Laptops, game consoles, or expensive jewellery  
- Large amounts of cash (we recommend no more than ¥100 for small shops)  
- Snacks in glass bottles (they break easily on the bus)

---

### How to Sign Up

1. Visit **www.sunrise-camp2027.cn** or scan the QR code on your school poster.  
2. Fill in the online form with your full name, school, grade, and parent's phone number.  
3. Upload a copy of a recent health check (within 6 months).  
4. Pay the fee **online** within 48 hours, or the seat will go to the waiting list.  
5. You will get a "Welcome Letter" and a packing list by email three days before the camp begins.

### Cancellation

- More than 14 days before the camp: **full refund**.  
- 7–14 days before: **80% refund**.  
- Less than 7 days before: **no refund**, but a friend in the same grade may take the seat.

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How much does the summer camp cost, and what is included in the price?**

A. ¥880; meals only.  
B. ¥1,880; meals, lodging, insurance and guide fee are all included.  
C. ¥2,880; only insurance is included.  
D. Free; but students must bring their own food.

**2. What do students do on Wednesday?**

A. Go hiking for 20 km along the highway.  
B. Stay in the room and watch films all day.  
C. Study river creatures — catching and then releasing them.  
D. Practise typing on laptops.

**3. Which of the following items is NOT allowed at the camp?**

A. A simple notebook.  
B. A warm jacket.  
C. A laptop or a game console.  
D. A small gift from the hometown.

**4. Tom signs up for the camp but has to cancel only 5 days before the start date. What is the result, according to the rules?**

A. He gets a full refund and a gift.  
B. He gets 80% of his money back.  
C. He gets no refund, but a friend in the same grade may take his seat.  
D. He is punished and cannot sign up next year.

**5. What is the main purpose of this poster?**

A. To advertise a new restaurant by the river.  
B. To invite middle-school students to join a 7-day outdoor summer camp and explain how to sign up.  
C. To sell camping equipment online.  
D. To ask parents to hire new camp leaders.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】At a Glance 表 "¥1,880... meals, lodging, insurance, guide fee all included."  
【解析】费用 1,880 含全部。A/C/D 错误。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Main Activities by Day 表 "Wed - River biology..."  
【解析】周三研究并释放河中生物。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"What NOT to Bring" "Laptops, game consoles..."  
【解析】笔记本电脑、游戏机禁止。A/B/D 均允许/建议。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】Cancellation "Less than 7 days before: no refund, but a friend... may take the seat."  
【解析】5 天属于 7 天内，不退款但可转让。A/B/D 错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】通篇  
【解析】海报邀请中学生参加七天户外夏令营并说明报名方式。A/C/D 均不符。故选 B。
