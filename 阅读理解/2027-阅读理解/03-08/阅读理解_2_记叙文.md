# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Grandma's Old Songbook**

① Last winter, when we helped Grandma move from her old apartment into a smaller one, I found a thin yellow book under her bed. The cover said **"Mountain Songs of Our Village, 1968"** in old-style Chinese writing. Inside, I found some English words written in pencil beside the Chinese: "birds", "river", "first light". I could not understand why my grandma, who never went to high school, had written English words in a folk songbook from her youth.

② When I asked her about it, she laughed for a long time. "Oh, that old thing," she said. "A young English teacher, Mr. Bennett, lived in our village for two summers when I was sixteen. He wanted to learn Chinese folk songs, so I sang, and he wrote down the tunes. In return, he taught me the English names of small things in the songs." Then Grandma's eyes turned soft. "I only had ten lessons," she said, "but for a country girl in 1968, those were ten bright windows."

③ That evening, I asked Grandma to sing one of the old songs. She chose **"The Moon Over the Stone Bridge"**, about a girl waiting for her father to come home from the river. Her voice was thin and a little shaky, but very clean, like cold water from a mountain spring. My little brother stopped playing on his phone; my mother put down the orange she was peeling; everyone listened.

④ I decided to do something special. I opened my laptop and found a **free music app**. Over the next weekend, I learned how to use its simple tools. I recorded Grandma's singing line by line, added a soft guitar from my cousin Ming, and used the English words from her old book to write a short note about the song in English. I sent the finished song to our family group chat.

⑤ An hour later, my aunt in Canada replied: "I was crying in the coffee shop!" My uncle in Xi'an said, "I haven't heard this song for forty years." Grandma, who was sitting beside me, could not really understand what a "file" was, but she could see her children's words on the screen. She put her hand on mine and said only one sentence: **"The song is not old if you keep singing it."**

⑥ The little yellow book is now on my desk, beside my textbooks. When my English homework feels hard, I open it and see those pencil notes — "birds", "river", "first light". I think of Mr. Bennett and my young grandma sitting by a stone bridge in 1968, two strangers using a tune to build a bridge over their languages. Every time I learn a new English word, I feel I am carrying their small lamp a little farther.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Where did the writer find Grandma's old songbook?**

A. On a shelf in the school library.  
B. In a music shop downtown.  
C. Under Grandma's bed during a move.  
D. In the writer's English teacher's office.

**2. Why are there English words in the Chinese songbook?**

A. Grandma wrote them in high school.  
B. A young English teacher taught Grandma some English words in return for folk songs.  
C. Grandma copied them from the Internet.  
D. The writer's mother wrote them last year.

**3. How did the writer make the song into a modern recording?**

A. He printed the old book in colour.  
B. He asked a music teacher to sing it again.  
C. He used a free music app, added guitar and an English note.  
D. He sent Grandma to a TV show.

**4. What did Grandma say after seeing everyone's messages?**

A. "I do not want to sing any more."  
B. "The song is not old if you keep singing it."  
C. "Please send me back to my old apartment."  
D. "My English is better than yours."

**5. What does the writer feel every time he learns a new English word?**

A. He wants to become an English teacher like Mr. Bennett.  
B. He feels sorry that Grandma did not go to university.  
C. He feels he is carrying a small lamp from Grandma and Mr. Bennett a little farther.  
D. He feels English is harder than Chinese.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "we helped Grandma move... I found a thin yellow book under her bed"  
【解析】书是在奶奶搬家时在床下找到的。A、B、D 均无依据。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "A young English teacher, Mr. Bennett... he taught me the English names"  
【解析】英文单词是 Bennett 老师教奶奶的，用来交换学民歌。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "a free music app... added a soft guitar from my cousin Ming, and used the English words from her old book"  
【解析】他用免费音乐软件录音+表哥伴奏+英文注释。A、B、D 无依据。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段末句 "The song is not old if you keep singing it."  
【解析】奶奶的原话。其他选项均无依据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑥段末句 "I feel I am carrying their small lamp a little farther"  
【解析】作者感到自己在把奶奶和贝纳特老师的小灯继续传下去。A、B、D 属无中生有或偏离原文。故选 C。
