# 阅读理解（应用文） **Blue River Swimming Pool — Summer Student Swim Classes**

> **BLUE RIVER SWIMMING POOL**  
> **SUMMER STUDENT SWIM CLASSES 2027**  
> *Safe water. Kind teachers. Happy summer.*

---

Dear students and parents,

Summer is a wonderful time to learn swimming, but every year our city hospital still reports **many water accidents** involving young people. To help students stay safe and confident in water, **Blue River Swimming Pool** will open four levels of small-group classes from **20 May to 31 August 2027**. All classes are taught by **fully licensed swimming coaches**.

### Class Levels

| Level | Class Name | Age | Content | Group Size |
|-------|------------|-----|---------|------------|
| **L1** | Water Friends (beginners) | 9–12 | Floating, breathing, safe entry, leg kicks | Up to 8 |
| **L2** | Frog Style Start | 11–14 | Breaststroke basics, pool rules, easy turns | Up to 10 |
| **L3** | Freestyle & Back | 13–16 | Front crawl, backstroke, breathing timing | Up to 10 |
| **L4** | Save a Friend | 14–16 | Calling for help, reaching rescue, wearing life vests | Up to 8 |

### Times and Prices

- Each course: **12 lessons × 45 minutes**, twice a week.  
- Morning sessions: Tue/Thu, 08:00–08:45.  
- Evening sessions: Mon/Wed, 18:30–19:15.  
- Weekend sessions: Sat, 09:30–10:15 (L1 and L2 only).  
- **Course fee:** 720 yuan per course. Brothers and sisters from the same family: **10% off** each.

### Safety Rules (read carefully!)

1. Students must bring a **swimming cap** and clean swimwear. Long hair must be tied.  
2. Do not swim **within one hour** after a full meal.  
3. Students with fever, flu, ear infections or open skin wounds should rest at home.  
4. Inside the pool area, **no running** and **no jumping** into shallow water.  
5. One parent is allowed in the waiting room. Parents may not enter the pool deck.

### How to Sign Up

- **Online:** www.brivpool.cn/summer2027 (fill in form and upload a health check copy)  
- **By phone:** 020-6688-3322 (Mon–Fri, 09:00–17:00)  
- **On site:** Reception, Blue River Sports Centre, Gate B  
- **Close date:** 15 May 2027.

A free **trial lesson** (20 minutes, L1 only) is held every Saturday of May at 10:00. Wear swimwear and a cap. No pre-sign-up needed, but seats are limited to the first 20 students.

For any questions, please email **coach.feng@brivpool.cn**. We look forward to seeing you at the pool.

— Blue River Pool Coaching Team

**根据通告内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Which level is MOST suitable for a 10-year-old child who has never swum before?**

A. L1 Water Friends.  
B. L2 Frog Style Start.  
C. L3 Freestyle & Back.  
D. L4 Save a Friend.

**2. How long is each swim lesson?**

A. 20 minutes.  
B. 30 minutes.  
C. 45 minutes.  
D. 60 minutes.

**3. Which of the following is NOT allowed by the safety rules?**

A. Wearing a swimming cap.  
B. Tying long hair.  
C. Jumping into the shallow area of the pool.  
D. Resting at home when having a fever.

**4. When should students sign up at the latest?**

A. 1 May 2027.  
B. 15 May 2027.  
C. 20 May 2027.  
D. 31 August 2027.

**5. Why does Blue River Pool especially open the L4 "Save a Friend" class?**

A. To help students become professional divers.  
B. To teach students basic rescue ideas and reduce water accidents.  
C. To sell more expensive swimwear.  
D. To prepare students for Olympic games only.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Class Levels 表 L1 行 "Age 9–12", "beginners"  
【解析】L1 面向 9–12 岁初学者，10 岁从未游过的孩子最合适。B/C 年龄偏高，D 为高阶救助课。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Times and Prices 第1行 "12 lessons × 45 minutes"  
【解析】每节课 45 分钟。A/B/D 均与原文不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Safety Rules 第4条 "no jumping into shallow water"  
【解析】禁止向浅水区跳入。A/B/D 都是被允许或鼓励的行为。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How to Sign Up "Close date: 15 May 2027"  
【解析】报名截止 5 月 15 日。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】开头 "many water accidents... involving young people" 与 L4 内容 "Calling for help, reaching rescue..."  
【解析】L4 的目的是教授救助常识、减少水上事故。A 职业潜水、C 卖泳装、D 只为奥运均属过度推断或无中生有。故选 B。
