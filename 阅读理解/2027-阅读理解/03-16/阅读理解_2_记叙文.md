# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Growing Tomatoes on Our Balcony**

① Last spring, our school started a "Green Hand" project. Each student had to grow something at home — any plant, small or large — and write a weekly **growth diary** for two months. My parents' flat is on the 15th floor, so there is no garden. There is only a narrow balcony with the afternoon sun. I chose a small packet of **cherry tomato seeds** from the school shop. They were as tiny as sesame, almost nothing in my hand.

② I wanted to do everything on my own. Dad helped me carry two plastic pots from the storeroom, but I chose the soil, watered it and buried the seeds one by one. "Put them half a centimetre deep," the packet said. I used my pinkie finger as a ruler and carefully covered them with soil. That night I dreamt of a jungle of red tomatoes on our small balcony.

③ For the first ten days, nothing happened. I checked the pots before school and after school. Still only brown soil. I began to worry that the seeds were dead. Grandma, who was visiting from the countryside, smiled and said, "Be patient, my city boy. Seeds work in the dark first." On the twelfth morning, I saw two tiny green points pushing up the soil. I was so excited that I shouted at breakfast, and my little sister spilled her milk.

④ As the plants grew taller, problems came too. A week before my maths test, the leaves began to turn yellow. I blamed the weather, the pot, even the school shop. Mum opened her phone and showed me a gardening video: "Maybe too much water. Let the soil dry between watering." I felt silly. I had thought "more water" equals "more love". From then on, I watered only when the top of the soil felt dry.

⑤ One Sunday in late May, a small yellow flower opened. Two weeks later, a round green ball appeared where the flower had been. By June, the ball turned orange, then dark red. I carefully picked it and split it with my sister. The taste was sweet and a little sour — nothing like the tomatoes in the supermarket. My sister said, "It tastes like work." I laughed, but I understood what she meant.

⑥ At the end of the project, I handed my growth diary to Ms. Liu. The last page said, "Tomatoes do not grow faster when you stare at them. They grow when you give them sun, water, patience, and sometimes distance." Ms. Liu put a small red stamp beside my sentence. Every time I see a cherry tomato at the market now, I remember: on our 15th floor balcony, one tiny brown seed once taught me more than a textbook could.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the writer choose to grow cherry tomatoes instead of other plants?**

A. His teacher picked them for him.  
B. His balcony had afternoon sun and cherry tomato seeds were sold at the school shop.  
C. His sister wanted to eat cherry tomatoes every day.  
D. His grandma had already planted some in the countryside.

**2. How deep did the writer plant the tomato seeds?**

A. Two centimetres.  
B. Five centimetres.  
C. Half a centimetre.  
D. Ten centimetres.

**3. What did the writer first think when the seeds did not grow in ten days?**

A. The seeds must be dead.  
B. The sun was not strong enough.  
C. The soil was the wrong colour.  
D. Someone had eaten them.

**4. Why did the tomato leaves turn yellow?**

A. The writer had given them too much water.  
B. The pots were too small.  
C. The weather was too cold.  
D. Insects were eating the leaves.

**5. What does the last sentence of the writer's diary mainly mean?**

A. Plants only grow when people stare at them every minute.  
B. Plants need sunlight, water, patience and some distance to grow well.  
C. Tomatoes from the supermarket are always sweeter.  
D. Only country people can grow plants well.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "narrow balcony with the afternoon sun... a small packet of cherry tomato seeds from the school shop"  
【解析】阳台有阳光且学校出售种子。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "'Put them half a centimetre deep,'"  
【解析】半厘米深。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "began to worry that the seeds were dead"  
【解析】担心种子死了。B、C、D 均无依据。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Maybe too much water. Let the soil dry between watering."  
【解析】浇水过多。B、C、D 均无依据。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑥段 "sun, water, patience, and sometimes distance"  
【解析】作者总结植物成长需要阳光、水、耐心和适度"放手"。A 与原文相反；C、D 偏题。故选 B。
