# 阅读理解（应用文） **Grade 8 English Dubbing Contest — Sign-up Notice**

① Hello, Grade 8 students! Our English Club is holding the **first school-wide English Dubbing Contest** this spring, under the theme **"Voices from Classic Movies"**. Come and lend your voice to a favourite cartoon character, a brave hero, or even a flying dragon! Certificates and a small bookshop gift card are waiting for the top six teams.

② **Basic Information**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Name | Grade 8 English Dubbing Contest |
| Theme | Voices from Classic Movies |
| Date (finals) | Saturday, 17 April 2027, 14:00 – 16:30 |
| Place | Multi-media Hall, Floor 3, Building B |
| Organiser | English Club + Information Technology Club |
| Entry fee | Free |

③ **Who Can Join?**

- Any student of **Grade 8**, class 1 to class 10.  
- **Team size**: 2 – 4 students per team. Each team dubs **ONE** short clip.  
- Clip length: **1 – 2 minutes**, taken from a G-rated English cartoon or family film.  
- A student can join only **one** team.

④ **Important Dates**

- **25 March**　Sign-up opens on the English Club webpage.  
- **10 April**　Sign-up closes at 18:00.  
- **12 April**　Teams receive their practice room times.  
- **15 April**　Technical check: every team sends its film clip (without voice) to the club email **before 17:00**.  
- **17 April**　Finals; the order of performance will be drawn on the spot.

⑤ **How Teams Are Marked**

Each team is given a score out of **100 points**:

- Pronunciation & intonation: **40 points**  
- Emotion & character feeling: **30 points**  
- Team work (turn-taking, timing with the video): **20 points**  
- Creativity (music, costume, sound effect): **10 points**

⑥ **Friendly Rules**

1. Choose a clip that everyone can watch happily — no violence, no rude words.  
2. The English voice must be **live on stage**; recorded voice is not allowed.  
3. Each team has up to **3 minutes** of total stage time, including a 30-second introduction.  
4. Please arrive **30 minutes before** your performance; late teams move to the back of the list.  
5. No adult help on stage; parents and teachers may only watch from the audience seats.

⑦ **Contact**  
- Wang Yue, Head of English Club — yue.wang@bfms.cn (reply within 1 day)  
- Mr. Lin, English teacher — Room 302, Office Building, 12:30 – 13:00  
- Any notice update will be posted on the **English Corner wall**, Floor 1, Building B.

**Bring your voice, bring your courage — the mic is waiting for you!**

**1. Who can join the dubbing contest?**

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Any student of Grade 8... 2 – 4 students per team"  
【解析】参赛者为八年级学生，组队 2~4 人。A、B、D 与原文不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】日期定位题  
【定位】第④段 "10 April — Sign-up closes at 18:00"  
【解析】4 月 10 日是截止日。A 是开放；C 是排练；D 是决赛。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节比较题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Pronunciation & intonation: 40 points"（最高）  
【解析】发音语调占 40 分最高。B、C、D 分值更低。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节否定题  
【定位】第⑥段第 2 条 "recorded voice is not allowed"  
【解析】禁用预录音。A、B、D 均被允许。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Multi-media Hall, Floor 3, Building B"  
【解析】决赛在 B 座 3 楼多媒体厅。A、C、D 为其他用途地点。故选 B。
