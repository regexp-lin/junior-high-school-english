# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should Students Do Housework Every Day?**

① In many Chinese families, housework is usually done by parents or even grandparents. Students are told, "Just study hard; don't worry about the dishes." But recently, a growing number of teachers are suggesting that middle school students should do some housework **every single day**. Is this really a good idea?

② Some parents disagree. They believe that learning is the only job of a student. In their eyes, twenty minutes spent on washing bowls could have been used to do three more math problems. They also worry that their children may break cups, burn their fingers, or simply do the work badly. "We can do it faster and better," one mother said. "Why should a tired child suffer again after a long day of school?"

③ However, these worries may not stand up to a closer look. Doing housework does not really take much time. Making the bed, wiping the table or watering the plants can be finished in ten minutes. Compared with the hours many students spend on short videos, such a small piece of time is far from harmful.

④ More importantly, housework teaches lessons that books alone cannot teach. When a student cooks a bowl of noodles for a tired mother, he begins to understand what "family" means. When she cleans the floor with her father on Saturday morning, she learns that a warm home does not come from the sky, but from the hands of every member. Chinese people have a saying: *"A child who never washes his own bowl will find it hard to build his own life."*

⑤ Science also supports this view. A 2023 study from a university in Beijing followed over 800 students for one year. It found that students who did housework for at least **15 minutes a day** were more confident, slept better, and even got slightly higher marks than those who never helped at home. The simple actions of folding clothes or peeling potatoes seem to train patience and careful attention — the very qualities that every test also needs.

⑥ Of course, housework must not become a new burden. Parents should match the work with the child's age, praise honest effort, and never use housework as a punishment. If both sides treat it as a daily family habit, doing housework will not take learning away; it will **make learning deeper**. A clean bowl, in the end, shapes a careful mind.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What do some parents worry about when their children do housework?**

A. The children may use too much water.  
B. The children may waste time and do the work badly.  
C. The children may become lazy at school.  
D. The children may become too independent.

**2. According to Paragraph 3, what is the writer's reply to such worries?**

A. Housework is more important than homework.  
B. Short videos always make students tired.  
C. Housework takes very little time, much less than videos.  
D. Parents should never touch a broom.

**3. What does the Chinese saying in Paragraph 4 suggest?**

A. Bowls should be bought new every year.  
B. Children who never do chores may find life hard later.  
C. Every bowl in China tells an ancient story.  
D. A good bowl brings good luck to the family.

**4. What did the 2023 study in Beijing find?**

A. Housework leads to worse grades.  
B. Housework makes students less patient.  
C. Students who did housework daily were more confident and slept better.  
D. Students who did housework no longer needed to study.

**5. What is the writer's main opinion on the question?**

A. Students should never do any housework.  
B. Housework is a punishment for lazy students.  
C. Daily housework is good if it suits the child's age.  
D. Only girls should be asked to do housework.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "worry that their children may break cups... simply do the work badly"  
【解析】家长担心浪费时间且做不好。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第③段 "such a small piece of time is far from harmful"  
【解析】作者指出家务耗时极少，远不如刷视频多。A、D 过绝，B 偏题。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第④段俗语 "will find it hard to build his own life"  
【解析】不会洗碗的孩子将来难立业。A、C、D 曲解字面。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "more confident, slept better, and even got slightly higher marks"  
【解析】研究表明做家务的学生更自信、睡得更好。A、B、D 相反或无依据。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】观点态度题  
【定位】第⑥段 "match the work with the child's age... daily family habit"  
【解析】作者赞成"与年龄匹配的每日家务"。A 否定，B、D 偏离。故选 C。

- **peel** [v.] 削皮  
- **confident** [adj.] 自信的
