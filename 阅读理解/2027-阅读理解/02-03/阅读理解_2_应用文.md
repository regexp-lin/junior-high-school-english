# 阅读理解（应用文） **Greenfield Science Museum — Weekend Guide for Students**

① Welcome to **Greenfield Science Museum**! This weekend, the museum is pleased to invite all middle school students and their families to a special two-day event called **"Science in Daily Life."** Come and touch, try, and even play with the wonders of science.

② **Opening Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date | Saturday, May 9 & Sunday, May 10 |
| Opening Hours | 9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. (Last entry 4:00 p.m.) |
| Place | 28 Greenfield Avenue, near Bus Stop *Lily Garden* |
| Tickets | Free for students with a school ID card; ¥20 for adults |

③ **Four Must-See Halls**

- **Hall A — "Light and Colour":** Step into a mirror tunnel and find out why the sky is blue.  
- **Hall B — "Little Farmers":** Plant a bean seed in a glass bottle and take it home. Free for the first 200 students each day.  
- **Hall C — "Robots Among Us":** Watch a robot dog run, and write three lines of simple code to make it turn.  
- **Hall D — "Food and the Body":** Learn why too much sugar is harmful and try a healthy lunch box for only ¥15.

④ **Special Activities**

- **Science Talk** (Saturday 10:30 a.m.): *Why Birds Can Fly but Airplanes Also Need a Plan*, by Professor Zhang from Greenfield University.  
- **Family Workshop** (Sunday 2:00 p.m.): Make a simple water filter with your parents. Please sign up online before Friday night.  
- **Night Sky Show** (Saturday 7:00 – 8:30 p.m.): Look at the real moon through a large telescope on the roof. Limited to 50 families.

⑤ **Kind Reminders**

- Please **do not** bring food or drinks into Halls A and C.  
- Children under 10 must stay with an adult at all times.  
- Photos are welcome, but **please turn off the camera flash** in Hall A.  
- Lost items can be collected at the Service Desk near the main gate before 6:00 p.m.

⑥ For more information, scan the QR code at the school gate, or call **0771-8896-2345** during the day. The museum is looking forward to seeing your curious eyes!

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When will the event "Science in Daily Life" be held?**

A. May 8 (Friday only).  
B. May 9 – May 10 (Saturday and Sunday).  
C. May 10 – May 11 (Sunday and Monday).  
D. Only on Sunday evening.

**2. Which hall offers a free plant for the first 200 students each day?**

A. Hall A.  
B. Hall B.  
C. Hall C.  
D. Hall D.

**3. What will Professor Zhang talk about on Saturday?**

A. How to cook a healthy lunch.  
B. Why birds can fly but planes also need a plan.  
C. How to protect the moon from pollution.  
D. How to build a robot dog at home.

**4. What should visitors NOT do in Hall A?**

A. Walk through the mirror tunnel.  
B. Take a photo without flash.  
C. Use the camera flash.  
D. Talk with their parents quietly.

**5. How can a family join the Family Workshop?**

A. Pay ¥200 at the gate on Sunday.  
B. Sign up online before Friday night.  
C. Call the museum after 6:00 p.m.  
D. Bring their own water filter.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Saturday, May 9 & Sunday, May 10"  
【解析】时间是 5月9日至10日周末两天。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Hall B... Free for the first 200 students each day."  
【解析】B 馆提供每日前 200 名学生免费种豆。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Why Birds Can Fly but Airplanes Also Need a Plan"  
【解析】张教授讲鸟与飞机。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "please turn off the camera flash in Hall A."  
【解析】A 馆须关闪光灯。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Please sign up online before Friday night."  
【解析】家庭工作坊需周五前在线报名。故选 B。
