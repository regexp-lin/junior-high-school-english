# 阅读理解（应用文） **Junior Journalists Wanted — Bright Future School Newspaper**

① Dear students, our school newspaper **"Morning Light"** is entering its 12th year. We are looking for **20 new junior journalists** from Grade 7 and Grade 8 to join us from **May 2027 to January 2028**. If you love asking questions, writing short stories, or taking photos, this is your chance to put your words on the front page.

② **What Will a Junior Journalist Do?**

- Write **one short article** (120 – 300 words) every two weeks. Topics may be: class news, student hobbies, sports events, nearby community, or friendly book and film reviews.  
- Interview teachers, classmates, and school workers; always keep a kind and polite tone.  
- Take simple photos or short 15-second videos as support.  
- Join the **Friday editorial meeting** (4:10 – 5:00 p.m.) in Library Room 205.

③ **Who Can Apply?**

- Students from **Grade 7 or Grade 8** of any class.  
- Basic writing skills in Chinese. English writing is a **bonus**, not a must.  
- Friendly, patient, and willing to share ideas.  
- Able to use a phone or school tablet for text and photos.  
- No prior news experience needed.

④ **How to Apply — Two Simple Steps**

1. Write a **300-character self-introduction** in Chinese, telling us:  
   - Your name, class and one hobby.  
   - Why you want to be a junior journalist.  
   - One topic you would like to cover this term.  
2. Attach **one sample article** (no more than 400 characters) about any event in our school or your neighbourhood.  
3. Send both documents in ONE email to **morning.light@bfms.cn** before **10 April, 22:00**.

⑤ **Selection Plan**

| Date | Step |
| --- | --- |
| 14 April | Email reply: we will invite 40 students to interview. |
| 18 April | Short interview (10 min per student), Library Room 203. |
| 22 April | Final name list posted on the red notice board, Floor 1. |
| 27 April | First meeting: meet the chief editor and receive a small press card. |

⑥ **What You Get**

- An official **"Junior Journalist Press Card"**.  
- Free short training every month on writing, photo, and simple video editing.  
- Priority seats at school events (sports meeting, open day, etc.).  
- Community-service hours signed by the school.  
- A warm team of friends who love stories.

⑦ **A Few Simple Rules**

1. **Truth first.** Always check your facts; never invent a quote.  
2. **Kindness matters.** Protect other students' privacy; ask before taking their photos.  
3. **Deadlines are friendly.** Send your article on time; if you cannot, tell the editor two days before.  
4. **Quiet in meetings.** Listen to others; share in order.  
5. **One warning only.** Three late articles without reason, and the press card must be returned.

⑧ **Contact**  
- Chief editor: Yan Meiyu, Class 8-4 — press.card@bfms.cn  
- Teacher in charge: Mr. Wei, Library Office, 12:20 – 13:00

**The world is full of small, bright stories. Come, and help us bring them to every student's desk.**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many new junior journalists does the school newspaper want to choose?**

A. 10.  
B. 20.  
C. 30.  
D. 40.

**2. How often do the junior journalists need to write an article?**

A. Every day.  
B. Every week.  
C. Every two weeks.  
D. Only once a term.

**3. What should students send in their application email before 10 April?**

A. Only a hand-written letter to the teacher.  
B. A self-introduction and one sample article.  
C. Three photos of their school trip.  
D. A long novel of more than 3,000 characters.

**4. What will happen on 22 April?**

A. Final name list will be posted on the red notice board.  
B. Every student will receive a press card.  
C. All applicants will take a written exam.  
D. The school newspaper will close for the term.

**5. Which of the following is NOT a rule for junior journalists?**

A. Always check facts before writing.  
B. Ask before taking photos of classmates.  
C. Copy interesting lines from other newspapers.  
D. Send articles on time or tell the editor in advance.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "looking for 20 new junior journalists"  
【解析】招募 20 名。A、C、D 不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "one short article every two weeks"  
【解析】每两周一篇。A、B、D 不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "self-introduction" + "one sample article"  
【解析】一份自我介绍加一篇样稿。A、C、D 无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】日期定位题  
【定位】第⑤段 "22 April — Final name list posted"  
【解析】4 月 22 日公布名单。B 为 27 号；C、D 无依据。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节否定题  
【定位】第⑦段五条规则：真实、仁善、守时、安静、警示  
【解析】"抄别报有趣的句子"与"Truth first"相悖。A、B、D 均属规则。故选 C。
