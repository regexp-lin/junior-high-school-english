# 阅读理解（应用文） **Silver Lake Youth Choir — Summer Camp 2027 Registration**

**SILVER LAKE YOUTH CHOIR · SUMMER CAMP 2027**
*"Sing together. Breathe together. Grow together."*

---

**Who are we?**
Silver Lake Youth Choir is a non-profit music group for students aged **10 – 16**. Since 2018, our members have given concerts in hospitals, parks, small towns and even rural schools. Our motto is simple: *music belongs to everyone*.

**Camp overview**

| Item | Information |
| --- | --- |
| Dates | **Sunday 12 July – Saturday 18 July, 2027** |
| Place | Silver Lake Art Centre, 18 Lake Road, Huayang Town |
| Stay | Dormitory (4 beds per room) |
| Meals | 3 meals + 1 afternoon snack, all cooked in the camp kitchen |
| Price | 780 yuan per student (includes stay, meals, music book, T-shirt) |

**Daily schedule (Mon. – Fri.)**

- 07:00 – 08:00 Morning exercise + breakfast
- 09:00 – 11:30 Group singing practice
- 11:30 – 12:30 Lunch
- 12:30 – 14:00 Rest
- 14:00 – 16:30 Small-group work (soprano, alto, tenor, bass)
- 17:00 – 18:30 Dinner + free time
- 19:00 – 20:30 Evening programme (film, games, outdoor concert)
- 21:30 Lights out

On Saturday, a **public concert** will be held in the village's primary school. Parents are welcome!

**Who can join?**

- Any student aged **10 – 16** who **loves singing**.
- No previous choir experience needed, but a short online audition (sing 30 seconds of any song) is required.
- Places are limited: **80 students in total**. First come, first served after passing the audition.

**What to bring**

1. Personal clothes (5 sets), swimming clothes, slippers.
2. A water bottle, a hat and sunscreen.
3. A simple pencil case (we provide note paper and song books).
4. One **home-town snack** (≤ 300 g) for the "Friends' Night" on Wednesday, for sharing.

**What NOT to bring**

- Expensive electronic devices (laptops, gaming consoles).
- More than 100 yuan in cash.
- Any kind of glass bottle.

**How to sign up**

1. Fill in the online form at *silverlakechoir.cn/summer2027* **before Sunday 20 June**.
2. Upload your 30-second audition video in the form (songs in any language are welcome).
3. If you pass, you will get an email before **Wednesday 24 June** with the full packing list and a parent agreement form.

**Important notes**

- Students with a serious breathing problem or very strong heart condition should **not** join, because the camp includes long-time singing and short runs in the morning.
- During the camp, mobile phone time is limited to **20 minutes after dinner**.
- If your family's income is low, please write to us — up to **10 free places** will be given every year.

**Contact**
Camp Director **Mr. Tang Qi** · 021-xxxx-6720 · camp@silverlakechoir.cn

*We cannot wait to hear your voice!*

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Price · 780 yuan per student (includes stay, meals, music book, T-shirt)"  
【解析】含住宿餐食等。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"before Sunday 20 June."  
【解析】20 日前交表。B 为录取通知；C 为开营；D 为闭营。故选 A。

【答案】C

【答案】B

【定位】"On Saturday, a public concert will be held..."
