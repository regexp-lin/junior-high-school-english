# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 National Day "My Motherland in My Eyes" Photo & Story Exhibition**

---

**📣 Notice from the Students' Union & the Art Department**

To celebrate **National Day 2027** and help students see, record and share the great changes in our country, our school is holding a special exhibition:

> **"My Motherland in My Eyes" — Photos and Short Stories by Lihua Students**

All students from Grade 7 to Grade 9 are warmly invited to take part.

---

## 🎯 Theme

Use a **photo** and a **short story** (in English, 120–180 words) to show **one small but real thing** that tells you "my motherland is changing / becoming better". Examples:

- a **new high-speed railway station** in your hometown;
- a **clean river** that used to be dirty;
- **elderly neighbours** using phones to pay, chat with grandchildren abroad, or book a doctor;
- a **"village library"** opened in your grandparents' village;
- a **solar-panel roof** on a farm building;
- a **bus that now reaches** a school that used to be far away;
- **recycling bins** that your community did not have five years ago;
- anything small, ordinary and **true**.

❗ The key word is **small**. Do not take photos of famous buildings or copy from the Internet. We want **your own eyes**.

---

## 📅 Timeline

| Date | What to do |
|------|-----------|
| **Oct 1 – Oct 7** | During the holiday, take **1–3 photos** in your hometown / community. Write a short story for **your favourite one**. |
| **Oct 8 (Wed) – Oct 10 (Fri)** | Hand in your entry **online** through the school WeChat mini-app "Lihua Campus". |
| **Oct 13 (Mon) – Oct 17 (Fri)** | Teacher team reviews entries and chooses **60 works** for the exhibition. |
| **Oct 20 (Mon) – Oct 24 (Fri)** | Exhibition in the **Art Building, 1F Main Hall**, open during lunch break and after school. |
| **Oct 24 (Fri), 15:40** | Prize-giving ceremony, **Lecture Hall**. |

---

## ✅ Rules for Submission

1. **One photo + one story** per student. More than one entry will be cancelled.
2. Photo must be **taken by yourself** during the **National Day holiday (Oct 1 – Oct 7)**. The mini-app will read the **photo date** automatically.
3. Story must be in **English, 120–180 words**, and include: **when**, **where**, **what** you saw, and **what you felt**.
4. Please **ask permission** before taking photos of other people. If a face is clearly seen, we may blur it for privacy.
5. Absolutely **no AI-generated images**. Any AI-generated work will be removed and cannot win any prize.
