# 阅读理解（说明文） **Why Do Bees Dance?**

When we watch a bee flying from one flower to another, it looks as if it is just busy collecting food. But in fact, bees do something far more surprising. After a worker bee finds a good **source** of flowers, she flies back to the hive and performs a small "dance" on the wall of the honeycomb. This dance is actually a message — and it may be one of the most amazing forms of animal "language" on Earth.

Scientists first understood the bees' dance more than seventy years ago. They watched carefully through glass walls and noticed that bees dance in two main ways. The first is called the **round dance**. A bee turns in small circles, first in one direction and then in the other. Other bees gather around, touching her with their **antennae**. The round dance tells them one thing: "Good food is very close to the hive — you only need to search a short distance around."

The second dance is richer. It is called the **waggle dance** — "waggle" because the bee shakes her middle part as she moves. She walks in a short straight line, shakes her body quickly, and then returns to the start in a curved path, making a shape like the number **8**. This simple-looking action carries two pieces of clear information.

The first is **direction**. The straight line in the dance shows the angle between the sun and the flowers. If the line points straight up, it means the flowers are in the same direction as the sun. If the line tilts 30° to the right, the flowers are 30° to the right of the sun. Remarkably, bees can do this calculation even when the sun is behind clouds.

The second is **distance**. The longer a bee shakes, the farther the food is. A quick three-second shake may mean 100 metres; a slow, seven-second shake may mean 900 metres. Because bees share this message with their sisters, one successful bee can turn into one hundred successful bees in only a few minutes. The whole hive works as a team.

Why is this dance so important? Without it, every bee would have to search by herself, waste her energy, and often return with nothing. With the dance, the hive is able to find the best flowers in the shortest time. Scientists have found that hives with "better dancers" usually produce more honey than hives with "poor dancers".

The dance also tells us something bigger: intelligence in nature is not only about brain size. A bee's brain is smaller than a grain of rice, yet it can share position, distance, and direction in a **silent** language of movement. The next time you see a bee flying lazily over a flower, remember: somewhere nearby, she may be about to go home and dance.

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When does a bee perform a dance?**

A. Before she flies out of the hive.  
B. After she finds a good source of flowers.  
C. When she wants to play with other bees.  
D. Only at night.

**2. What does the round dance tell other bees?**

A. The food is very far away.  
B. There is danger inside the hive.  
C. Good food is very close to the hive.  
D. The weather is bad today.

**3. What two pieces of information does the waggle dance share?**

A. The name and colour of the flower.  
B. The price and smell of honey.  
C. The direction and distance of the food.  
D. The age and weight of the bee.

**4. What does a slow, seven-second shake probably mean?**

A. The food is 100 metres away.  
B. The food is about 900 metres away.  
C. The food has been eaten.  
D. The bee is too tired.

**5. What is the main idea of the passage?**

A. Bees are lazy insects that refuse to work alone.  
B. Bees use dances as a kind of language to share where the best flowers are.  
C. Bees live longer than most other insects.  
D. Bees dance only when they feel happy.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"After a worker bee finds a good source of flowers..."  
【解析】找到蜜源后才跳舞。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Good food is very close to the hive..."  
【解析】圆圈舞表近距离蜜源。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"direction..." 与 "distance..."  
【解析】摇摆舞传递方向+距离。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"slow, seven-second shake may mean 900 metres"  
【解析】慢摇 7 秒≈900 m。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】全文  
【解析】蜂舞=传递信息的语言。A/C/D 均偏离。故选 B。
