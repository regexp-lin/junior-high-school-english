# 阅读理解（应用文） **Cloud City Gallery — Student Photography Competition**

**CLOUD CITY GALLERY**
*A quiet place where young eyes speak.*

---

**Student Photography Competition 2027**
This year our gallery holds its first photography competition for middle school students in the city. Do you take pictures on your phone every day? Here is a chance for your pictures to be seen on a real wall.

**Theme**
"**Small Places, Big Stories**".
Take photos in your own neighbourhood — a street corner, a school stair, your kitchen, a small park — and tell us the story behind the photo in 60 – 120 Chinese characters.

**Who can enter**

- All middle school students aged 12 – 16 in our city.
- Students may enter alone, not in groups.
- Each student may send up to **4 photos**.

**Important dates**

| Date | Event |
| --- | --- |
| 1 July – 15 August | Submission open |
| 20 August – 25 August | First review by teachers |
| 28 August | 60 works chosen for the public show |
| 5 September – 5 October | Public show at Cloud City Gallery |
| 6 October, 15:00 | Prize ceremony |

**How to send your photos**

1. Open the gallery's Mini Program "CloudCity-Photo" on your phone.
2. Upload each photo (JPG or PNG, at least 8 MB, no filters that change the face or the colour too much).
3. Fill in: title, date, place, your short story (60 – 120 characters).
4. Click *Submit*. After submitting, you cannot change the photo, so please check carefully.

**Prizes**

- **Gold Prize (1 student)**: a simple camera for beginners + a free private class with photographer **Mei Lin**.
- **Silver Prize (3 students)**: a camera bag + a one-month free ticket for all shows.
- **Bronze Prize (6 students)**: an art book signed by the photographer.
- **Selected Prize (50 students)**: a high-quality paper print of the winning photo.
- All 60 students in the show will receive an official paper certificate.

**Rules**

1. Photos must be taken by **you**. Using another person's photo is strictly banned.
2. AI-made pictures are **not accepted** for this competition.
3. Each photo must include at least one real place or object in our city; please do not send pictures of only the sky or a piece of white paper.
4. Please do not take photos of other people's faces without asking them first.

**Open day for tips**
On **Saturday 3 July, 10:00**, photographer Mei Lin will give a free 90-minute talk at the gallery about "how to tell a story in one photo". Sign-up is free but seats are limited to 80.

**Contact**
Cloud City Gallery · 010-xxxx-4421 · photo@cloudcitygallery.cn
*Look carefully. There is a story at every corner.*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Theme · Small Places, Big Stories"  
【解析】主题"小地方，大故事"。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B

【答案】C

【定位】"Gold Prize (1 student): a simple camera... + a free private class..."

**5.**  
【答案】B
