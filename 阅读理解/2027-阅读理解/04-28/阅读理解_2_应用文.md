# 阅读理解（应用文） **Community Notice — Free Bike Repair Day at Little Sun Park**

> **Little Sun Community Centre**  
> **NOTICE — Spring Free Bike Repair Day**  
> *"A safe bike makes a happy spring."*

---

**DATE:** Saturday, 8 May 2027  
**TIME:** 9:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.  
**PLACE:** East Lawn, Little Sun Park (near West Gate B)  
**FOR:** Residents of Little Sun Community, especially families with children

---

Dear neighbours,

Spring is here and many of us are riding our bikes more often. To help make our rides **safer**, the Little Sun Community Centre, together with the **Green Wheel Cycling Club** and three volunteer student teams from **Little Sun Middle School**, will hold a free bike repair day in the park. Please read the notice carefully.

### 1. What Is Free

- **Checking** the tyres, chain, brakes, and bell  
- **Fixing** small problems (tight brake cable, loose screw, low tyre pressure)  
- **Adjusting** the seat and handlebar height for children  
- **Cleaning and oiling** the chain  
- **Fitting** a free warning bell for bikes without one

### 2. What Costs a Little (at half-price)

| Service                   | Normal Price | On the Day |
|---------------------------|--------------|------------|
| New inner tube            | 30 yuan      | 15 yuan    |
| New brake pad (pair)      | 40 yuan      | 20 yuan    |
| New light (front + rear)  | 60 yuan      | 30 yuan    |

No big services (such as changing a wheel or a full chain set) can be done on the day. Please go to the shop at 12 Pine Street for those.

### 3. How to Join

1. Park your bike **outside the East Lawn**. Please do NOT ride it into the park.  
2. Take a **number card** at the red tent (one bike per number).  
3. Wait for your number on the **green bench area**. Waiting time is usually **20–40 minutes**.  
4. Watch how the volunteer students do the work — you can learn too!  
5. When finished, please take your bike away **within 10 minutes** so others can use the space.

### 4. Important Rules

- Children under 12 **must come with an adult**.  
- Please bring your **community card** or **building pass**.  
- Dogs are welcome but must be on a **short lead**.  
- Running, skating, and selling snacks are **not allowed** on the East Lawn that day.  
- If it rains before 8 a.m., the event moves to the **ground floor** of Building A.

### 5. Volunteer Team

- **Chief mechanic:** Uncle Guo (Green Wheel Cycling Club, 30 years of repair)  
- **Student volunteers:** 15 students from Grade 8 and Grade 9  
- **Safety helpers:** 4 security uncles with yellow vests

### 6. Contact

- **Phone:** 010-8899-3322 (Mr Yuan, community office)  
- **WeChat:** LittleSunCC  
- **Sign-up wall:** notice board next to the grocery store

Please come early; bikes are repaired on a **first-come, first-served** basis. Broken bikes that cannot be fixed on the spot will receive a **discount coupon** for the Pine Street shop.

**Ride safely. Ride kindly. See you on 8 May!**

Little Sun Community Centre

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Who organizes the Free Bike Repair Day?**

A. Only the police station.  
B. Little Sun Community Centre, the Green Wheel Cycling Club, and student volunteers from Little Sun Middle School.

**2. Which of these services is FREE?**

A. Buying a new inner tube.  
B. Buying a new pair of brake pads.

D. Changing the whole wheel.

**3. What should visitors do first when they arrive at the park?**

A. Ride their bikes to the green bench.  
B. Park the bike outside the East Lawn and take a number card at the red tent.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed on the East Lawn that day?**

A. A child who comes with an adult.  
B. A dog on a short lead.  
C. Running, skating, and selling snacks.  
D. Parents with community cards.

**5. What happens if a bike is so broken that it cannot be fixed on the spot?**

A. The owner must throw it away at once.  
B. The owner gets a discount coupon for the Pine Street shop.  
C. The owner must pay 500 yuan.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】通知第2段  
【解析】社区中心+自行车俱乐部+学生志愿者。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Section 1 "What Is Free"  
【解析】检查、安装免费小铃铛等免费。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Section 3 步骤  
【解析】先停车再取号。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Section 4 规则  
【解析】不允许奔跑、滑冰、售卖小吃。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】末段末句  
【解析】得到松街店打折券。故选 B。
