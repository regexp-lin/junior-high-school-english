# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 Autumn Class Hiking Trip "Red Leaves Mountain"**

---

> **Lihua Middle School — Office of Student Affairs**
> **Class Outdoor Activity Notice**
> Issued: **22nd September 2027**

**Dear students and parents,**

Autumn has painted **Red Leaves Mountain** in warm gold and deep red. To help our Grade-7 and Grade-8 students breathe fresh air, walk together and enjoy the colours of the season, our school will hold the **2027 Autumn Class Hiking Trip** on **Saturday 18th October**, from **7:30 to 17:30**.

---

## 1. Trip Facts

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Date | Saturday 18th October 2027 |
| Gathering | 7:30, main gate (east entrance); departure 7:50 sharp |
| Destination | Red Leaves Mountain Forest Park (north of the city) |
| Way of travel | School bus, about 50 min |
| Walking distance | 5.2 km (easy ring trail, gentle slope) |
| Elevation | 420 m at the top; rest stops every 1 km |
| Return | 17:30 back at the main gate |

> **Grades covered:** 7 and 8. Grade 9 students (preparing for Zhongkao) may join only with a parent written note.

---

## 2. What to Bring

**You MUST bring:**

- Student ID card and a **paper permission slip** signed by a parent (green form, handed out by your head teacher).
- Comfortable **sports shoes** (no new shoes — they hurt).
- A **light backpack** (under 3 kg).
- **Two 500 ml water bottles** or one 1-litre bottle.
- A **small lunchbox** (no glass bottles).
- A light jacket for wind; a **small rain-coat** (weather may be cool).
- A **charged mobile phone** only for emergencies (NOT for videos or games during the walk).

**You MUST NOT bring:**

- Alcohol, open flame, lighters, fireworks, sharp knives.
- Expensive electronic devices (tablets, cameras over ¥1 000).
- Pets of any kind.
- Roller-skates or scooters.

---

## 3. Cost

- The trip is **free for all school-bus users**; parents pay **¥45 per student** to cover park entry + insurance.
- Please transfer the ¥45 to the **class accountant** by **Friday 10th October**; late payment means your seat may be given to a student on the waiting list.

---

## 4. Safety Rules

1. Students must **walk in class groups** behind their head teacher. Leaving the main path is forbidden.
2. If you feel unwell, tell the **class teacher immediately**; Nurse Zhao will be on the red mini-bus at the rest point.
3. **No swimming** in the small lake half-way up the mountain — it is colder than it looks.
4. Do **not pick leaves or flowers** from any tree; fallen leaves on the ground may be collected (up to 3 per student).
5. Take all rubbish back in your own bag. **Leave no trace**.

---

## 5. Weather Plan

The trip will go ahead in light rain; in case of **heavy rain, strong wind or thunderstorm**, the trip will be **moved to Saturday 25th October** at the same time and the same meeting point. An SMS will be sent to parents by **Friday 17 Oct, 21:00**.

---

## 6. Photos and Reports

Each class may choose **two student photographers** (with a school-approved camera) to take pictures for the class report. The best group photo and the best 200-word English report will be printed in the November issue of *Morning Breeze*. Please email photos and report drafts to **outdoor@lihuams.edu.cn** before **Wednesday 22 October**.

---

## 7. Contact

- **Bus arrangements:** Mr Chen, ext. 223
- **On-the-day phone:** 010-8888-1810 (only open 7:00 – 18:00 on 18 October)

> **Walk slowly. Look carefully. Breathe deeply. A mountain in autumn is a classroom no textbook can print.**
>
> — **Office of Student Affairs, Lihua Middle School**

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Which grades does the 2027 Autumn Hiking Trip cover?**

A. Only Grade 9.  
B. Grade 7 and Grade 8; Grade 9 students may join only with a parent written note.  
C. Grade 7, Grade 8 and Grade 9, no conditions.  
D. Grade 6 only.

**2. A Grade-8 student wants to bring the following items: a light backpack, a 1-litre water bottle, a small lunchbox, a new pair of shoes he just bought yesterday, and a charged phone. Which item is NOT correct?**

A. Light backpack.  
B. 1-litre water bottle.  
C. New pair of shoes bought yesterday (the notice warns against new shoes).  
D. Small lunchbox.

**3. How much must a parent pay for a student to join the trip, and by when?**

A. ¥20 by 18 October.  
B. ¥45 per student, transferred to the class accountant by Friday 10 October.  
C. ¥100 at the school gate on the day.  
D. Nothing at all — the trip is completely free.

**4. According to the safety rules, which of the following actions is ALLOWED on the trip?**

A. Leaving the main path to explore deeper into the woods.  
B. Swimming in the small lake half-way up the mountain.  
C. Collecting up to 3 fallen leaves from the ground as a souvenir.  
D. Picking flowers and small branches from the trees.

**5. What will happen if the weather on 18 October is a heavy thunderstorm?**

A. The trip will be fully cancelled.  
B. Only Grade-7 students will go; Grade-8 students will stay.  
C. The trip will be moved to Saturday 25 October at the same time and the same meeting point, with an SMS notice by Friday 17 Oct 21:00.  
D. Every student must come to school as usual and take the bus anyway.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 部分 "Grades covered: 7 and 8. Grade 9 ... parent written note"  
【解析】主要面向 7 年级与 8 年级。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节判断题  
【定位】第 2 部分 "Comfortable sports shoes (no new shoes — they hurt)"  
【解析】通知明确不要穿新鞋。A、B、D 均被允许。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 部分 "¥45 per student ... by Friday 10th October"  
【解析】每生 45 元，10 月 10 日前上缴。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节判断题  
【定位】第 4 部分 "fallen leaves on the ground may be collected (up to 3 per student)"  
【解析】可拾取至多 3 片落叶。A、B、D 均违反规则。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 5 部分 "moved to Saturday 25th October ... SMS ... Friday 17 Oct, 21:00"  
【解析】雷雨天改期并短信通知。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。
