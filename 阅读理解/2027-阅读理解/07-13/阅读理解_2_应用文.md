# 阅读理解（应用文） **Harbour Museum — Children & Family Day**

**HARBOUR CITY MUSEUM**
*Walk in. Time travels with you.*

---

**Family Day Leaflet — August 2027**

Harbour City Museum invites all families with middle-school students to our special **Children & Family Day** on **Saturday, 15 August 2027**. This day is made for you — with tours, games, and hands-on rooms where hands are welcome, not forbidden.

**Opening hours on Family Day**

- **Main building:** 09:00 – 18:00 (last entry 17:00)
- **Children & Family Zone (North Wing):** 09:00 – 17:30
- **Garden Cafe (outdoor):** 10:00 – 19:30

**Ticket information**

| Ticket | Price | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Adult (18+) | ¥60 | Half price for local ID holders |
| Child (6 – 17) | ¥20 | With a valid student card |
| Family ticket (2 adults + 2 children) | ¥120 | Must buy together |
| Under 6 | Free | Must be with an adult |
| School group (10+ students) | ¥15 each | Book 3 days in advance |

**Three programmes to choose from**

| Programme | Time | Place | Details |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Young Guide Tour** | 10:00 – 11:00 | Main hall → Gallery 2 → Gallery 5 | A student guide (Grade 9) shares stories of three key items; sign up at the front desk |
| **Map and Compass Workshop** | 13:00 – 14:30 | Children & Family Zone, Room 2 | Use real paper maps and a compass; build a small treasure route around the museum (50 spaces) |
| **Ancient Pottery Hands-on** | 15:00 – 16:30 | Children & Family Zone, Room 4 | Shape a small clay cup; kiln-fired cups can be collected on 29 August (40 spaces) |

**What not to miss in the main building**

- Gallery 2: *Silk and Sea*, items from the sea trade 800 years ago
- Gallery 5: *Old Harbour*, models of our city 100 years ago
- Gallery 7: *New Faces*, paintings of local children, painted by students from our partner schools

**Food and rest**

- **Garden Cafe (outdoor):** sandwiches ¥18, soup ¥12, juice ¥10
- **Water fountains:** near the main hall and on the first floor of the North Wing (free)
- **Picnic area:** behind the North Wing, under the big tree; please take your rubbish with you

**House rules**

1. Photos allowed in most places, **but no flash and no tripods**. In Gallery 2, cameras are not allowed at all — the silk is very sensitive to light.
2. Do not touch items behind glass. In the hands-on rooms, staff will show you what you can touch.
3. **Food and drink** are only allowed in the Garden Cafe and the picnic area. A fine of ¥50 is charged if food is brought into the galleries.
4. Children under 12 must stay with an adult at all times.
5. Running, shouting, and phone calls should be kept outside the galleries. In emergencies, please find the nearest staff in a red vest.

**How to come**

- Metro: Line 2, Harbour Road, Exit A (3 minutes' walk)
- Bus: 88, 120, 186 — Museum Square
- **Free shuttle** between the main gate and the nearest metro from 09:00 to 18:00, every 15 minutes

**Contact**

- Ticket hotline: 021-xxxx-1010 (09:00 – 17:00)
- Email: family@harbourmuseum.cn · Website: www.harbourmuseum.cn/familyday

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. At what time does the Main building close on Family Day?**

A. 16:00.  
B. 17:00.  
C. 18:00.  
D. 19:30.

**2. How much does a family ticket cost (2 adults + 2 children)?**

A. ¥120.  
B. ¥60.  
C. ¥20.  
D. Free.

**3. Which programme may take place in the Children & Family Zone, Room 4?**

A. Young Guide Tour.  
B. Map and Compass Workshop.  
C. Ancient Pottery Hands-on.  
D. School group tour.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed?**

A. Taking photos without a flash in most galleries.  
B. Eating sandwiches in the Garden Cafe.  
C. Bringing a cup of soup into Gallery 5.  
D. Using a compass in the Map and Compass Workshop.

**5. How can a family easily come from the nearest metro station to the museum?**

A. Take a taxi only.  
B. Walk along the main road for 30 minutes.  
C. Take the free shuttle, which runs every 15 minutes.  
D. Rent a bicycle from the museum.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Main building: 09:00 – 18:00"  
【解析】主楼 18 时闭馆。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Family ticket (2 adults + 2 children) — ¥120"  
【解析】家庭票 120 元。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Room 4 — Ancient Pottery Hands-on"  
【解析】陶艺体验在 4 号室。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"Food and drink are only allowed..."  
【解析】展厅内禁食。A/B/D 均允许。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Free shuttle... every 15 minutes"  
【解析】免费穿梭巴士。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。
