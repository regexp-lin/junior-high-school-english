# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Thunder Night and My Little Brother**

① My little brother Tiantian is eight years old, three years younger than me. He is brave in the daytime. He can pick up a caterpillar with his bare hands and race the fastest boys on the playground. But at night, when thunder rolls over our flat, he turns back into a small, frightened child who wants the lamp on.

② Last Friday, a big storm came to our city. At first, there was only a soft wind. Then the sky turned dark green, and fat raindrops began to hit the windows like little stones. Tiantian and I were watching an animal programme in the living room when the lights blinked **once**, **twice**, and went out. The whole flat fell into black. A long, deep roar of thunder shook the walls.

③ Tiantian screamed. I felt his small hand grab my arm so tightly that his nails almost hurt. My first thought was, "I am scared too." But I was the older brother, and I remembered something my mother once told me: "Fear becomes smaller when you give someone a job." So I took a slow breath and said, "Tiantian, I need your help."

④ I took out my phone torch and gave him the **smaller** flashlight from my desk. "Your job," I said, "is to check the windows. Make sure they are all closed tight so the rain does not come in. I will check the candles." His crying stopped at once. He marched around the flat with his little flashlight, saying "Check!" at every window, like a brave soldier. I followed behind and lit two small candles on the kitchen table.

⑤ After ten minutes, we sat under a blanket together on the sofa. The thunder still rolled, but it sounded farther away now. I told Tiantian that thunder is only the **sound** that lightning makes when it heats the air very fast. "It is very loud," I said, "but the sound cannot hurt you. The rain is washing the streets." He nodded slowly, and then asked, "Do clouds also get scared?" I laughed and said, "No, they just cry a little. Then they feel better." He smiled, and for the first time that evening, he looked calm.

⑥ The lights came back at nine o'clock. Mother rushed home from her meeting, worried about both of us. But when she opened the door, she found a quiet living room with two small candles, two empty cups of milk, and two brothers sharing one blanket. Tiantian told her proudly, "I checked every window, Mama!" That night, I fell asleep thinking that sometimes the best way to be brave is simply to stand beside someone smaller, and give fear a useful job.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How old is the writer's little brother?**

A. Six years old.  
B. Seven years old.  
C. Eight years old.  
D. Eleven years old.

**2. What happened when the lights went out?**

A. Tiantian laughed loudly.  
B. A long, deep roar of thunder shook the walls, and Tiantian screamed.  
C. The writer began to cry.  
D. The rain stopped immediately.

**3. What "job" did the writer give his little brother?**

A. To cook dinner.  
B. To run outside and catch the wind.  
C. To check that all the windows were closed tightly.  
D. To go to bed at once.

**4. Why did the writer say, "Fear becomes smaller when you give someone a job"?**

A. Because jobs pay money.  
B. Because having something useful to do helps a person feel calmer and braver.  
C. Because brothers should never talk.  
D. Because children are not afraid of thunder.

**5. What is the main lesson of this story?**

A. Storms are always dangerous.  
B. Younger children should live alone.  
C. Being brave can mean standing beside someone smaller and giving fear a useful job.  
D. Candles are better than lamps.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段第1句 "My little brother Tiantian is eight years old..."  
【解析】小弟弟 8 岁。A/B/D 均不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段末-③段首 "A long, deep roar of thunder shook the walls. Tiantian screamed."  
【解析】停电后紧接一声雷鸣，弟弟大叫。A/C/D 均与原文相反或不实。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段第2句 "Your job is to check the windows. Make sure they are all closed tight..."  
【解析】让弟弟检查窗户是否关严。A/B/D 均为无中生有或相反。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第③段 "Fear becomes smaller when you give someone a job." 结合第④段弟弟停止哭并开始巡查  
【解析】被赋予"任务"后注意力转移、恐惧减轻。A 工作赚钱、C 兄弟沉默、D 孩子不怕均不符。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨归纳题  
【定位】第⑥段末句 "sometimes the best way to be brave is simply to stand beside someone smaller, and give fear a useful job."  
【解析】作者最终领悟：陪伴更小的人、让恐惧有任务，即是勇敢。A/B/D 均与主题不符。故选 C。
