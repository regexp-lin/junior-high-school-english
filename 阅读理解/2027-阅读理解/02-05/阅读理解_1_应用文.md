# 阅读理解（应用文） **Young Volunteers Wanted — Sunshine City Marathon**

① Every April, the **Sunshine City Marathon** welcomes more than 10,000 runners from around the country. This year, the city invites middle school students and their families to work as **young volunteers** on race day. The event is not only a sport, but also a lively lesson in kindness, teamwork and city pride.

② **Basic Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Race Day | Sunday, April 11, 2027 |
| Volunteer Hours | 6:30 a.m. – 1:30 p.m. |
| Meeting Point | North Gate of City Park |
| Free Meal | Breakfast and lunch for every volunteer |
| Souvenir | T-shirt, cap and a printed certificate |

③ **Four Volunteer Groups**

- **Group A – Water Station:** Hand out water and sports drinks along the road. (Ages 13+)  
- **Group B – Cheer Team:** Hold signs, clap and cheer for tired runners. (Ages 12+)  
- **Group C – Green Team:** Keep the road clean and put used cups into special bins. (Ages 12+)  
- **Group D – Finish Line Helpers:** Pass medals, bananas and warm towels to the runners. (Ages 14+, English required)

④ **How to Join**

- Step 1: Visit **www.sunshinemarathon.org/volunteer** before Friday, March 26.  
- Step 2: Fill in your name, class, phone number and the group you want to join. You may choose only **one** group.  
- Step 3: Join the online training meeting on Saturday, April 3, from 7:30 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.  
- Step 4: Wait for an email before April 7. If selected, print the form and ask your parent to sign it.

⑤ **Please Note**

- Volunteers **must** wear their orange marathon T-shirt and closed sports shoes on race day.  
- Please **arrive 30 minutes earlier** than the starting time, or the place in your group may go to another student.  
- Do **not** run on the race road during the race — safety first!  
- Phones should be kept in the small bag. A team teacher will contact your parents if anything happens.

⑥ Every volunteer will also receive **5 hours of community service credit**. If you would like to know more, please email **volunteer@sunshinemarathon.org**. See you in a sunny April morning!

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long will volunteers work on race day?**

A. About 3 hours.  
B. About 5 hours.  
C. About 7 hours.  
D. About 10 hours.

**2. Which group needs volunteers who can speak English?**

A. Group A.  
B. Group B.  
C. Group C.  
D. Group D.

**3. Which of the following is required to join the volunteer team?**

A. Running at least 5 kilometres.  
B. Filling the online form before March 26.  
C. Paying ¥50 on race day.  
D. Winning a medal in school sports.

**4. What should volunteers NOT do on race day?**

A. Wear closed sports shoes.  
B. Arrive 30 minutes earlier.  
C. Run on the race road during the race.  
D. Keep their phone in the small bag.

**5. What will every volunteer get from the event?**

A. A famous runner's autograph.  
B. A free bicycle.  
C. A T-shirt, cap, certificate and 5 hours of community service credit.  
D. A new schoolbag and shoes.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "6:30 a.m. – 1:30 p.m."  
【解析】6:30 至 13:30，共 7 小时。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Group D... Ages 14+, English required"  
【解析】终点组要求英语。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "before Friday, March 26"  
【解析】须在 3 月 26 日前线上报名。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Do not run on the race road during the race"  
【解析】比赛中不得跑上赛道。A、B、D 均要求遵守。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②⑥段 "T-shirt, cap... printed certificate... 5 hours of community service credit"  
【解析】每人获得 T 恤、帽子、证书及 5 小时志愿时长。故选 C。
