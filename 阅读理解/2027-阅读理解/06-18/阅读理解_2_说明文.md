# 阅读理解（说明文） **Why Do We See a Rainbow After the Rain?**

On a summer afternoon, after a heavy rain, the clouds break and the sun comes out again. Then, in the east sky, a wide band of colours appears: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, dark blue and purple. A rainbow. Every child knows it is beautiful, but few children know **how** it is made. The answer is a small story about sunlight, water and angles.

First, we need to remember one fact about sunlight. We call it "white light", but it is really made up of many colours mixed together. When white light passes through a glass prism, we can see the colours come apart. Each colour bends by a slightly different angle. Red bends the least; purple bends the most. This is called **refraction**.

Now we can understand what happens with a rainbow. After a rain, the air is full of tiny **water drops**. Each drop acts like a small, round prism. When a ray of sunlight hits a drop, three things happen: the ray bends as it enters the drop, it reflects off the back of the drop, and it bends again when it comes out. On the way in and on the way out, the drop splits the light into different colours, just like a glass prism.

But why do we see the colours as a big arc in the sky? The answer is angles. Each colour leaves the drop at a slightly different angle (about 40 – 42 degrees from the direction of the sun). When you stand with your back to the sun, all the drops in the sky that are at the right angle — no matter how far or how close — send that colour to your eyes. Together, those drops form a curved line. From your eyes, red comes from a slightly higher arc, purple from a slightly lower one, so we see a rainbow that always has red on the outside and purple on the inside.

Sometimes, you may see **two rainbows** at the same time. The second, fainter rainbow is a bit higher and its colours are in the opposite order. This happens when the sunlight reflects twice inside the water drop before coming out. Because part of the light is lost in the second reflection, the double rainbow is always weaker than the first one.

A rainbow is also a kind of trick of position. Two students standing a few metres apart never see exactly the same rainbow, because each of them sees light from different drops. And if you walk towards a rainbow, you can never touch it; it moves away as you move. In this sense, a rainbow is not a real "thing" in the sky. It is a gift that your eyes draw from sunlight, water and the angle of your own head.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main idea of paragraph 2?**

A. Sunlight is a single, single-colour light.  
B. Sunlight is really made of many colours, and a prism can show them because each colour bends by a different angle.  
C. Rainbows can only appear inside a glass prism.  
D. Red light is the strongest colour.

**2. In a water drop, how many main steps happen to the light?**

A. One.  
B. Two.  
C. Three — the ray bends as it enters, reflects off the back, and bends again as it comes out.  
D. Ten.

**3. Why do we see the colours in the shape of an arc?**

A. Because the sun is shaped like a circle.  
B. Because each colour leaves the drops at a slightly different angle, so all drops at the right angle send that colour to our eyes.  
C. Because clouds always form arcs.  
D. Because wind bends the light into a line.

**4. Why is the second rainbow always weaker than the first?**

A. Because the sun is setting.  
B. Because the sunlight reflects twice inside the drop, and some light is lost in the extra reflection.  
C. Because there is more rain.  
D. Because the sky is dark.

**5. Why can't you ever touch a rainbow?**

A. Because rainbows are afraid of humans.  
B. Because rainbows disappear at noon.  
C. Because a rainbow is not a real object — it depends on your eyes, the drops and the angle, so it moves as you move.  
D. Because the government has banned rainbows.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】段落大意题  
【定位】第 2 段  
【解析】说明白光由多色组成，每色折射角度不同。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 段 "three things happen..."  
【解析】入射折射、背面反射、出射折射。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 段  
【解析】不同颜色以不同角度出射，合适角度的水滴形成弧。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 5 段  
【解析】经过两次反射，能量损失。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】末段  
【解析】彩虹依赖视角，不可触及。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。
