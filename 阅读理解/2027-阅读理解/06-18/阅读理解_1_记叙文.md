# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Night I Was Locked Out**

My parents had gone to a wedding in the next city, and they were coming home very late. My big sister was staying with me at our flat. That Friday evening, Yuan, my best friend, invited me to his house to play a new board game. Before I left, my sister said, "Take the keys. Don't ring the bell too late; I need to sleep."

I laughed and said, "No worries." I took the keys from the small silver hook by the door, or at least I thought I did.

At Yuan's house, we played, we ate noodles, and we watched a short film. At 10:30, I said goodbye. It was raining a little on the way home. When I reached my door at 10:55, I touched my jacket pocket. Empty. I touched my trouser pockets. Empty. Empty, empty, empty. I had no keys.

I rang the bell. Once. Twice. Three times. No answer. My sister was a very deep sleeper, and she must have been dreaming. I called her phone; it kept ringing, then stopped. Obviously, she had put it on silent.

I sat on the cold step outside our door. The rain got a little heavier. I thought of knocking on the neighbours' doors, but most of them were old people who had gone to bed by nine. Then I thought of Yuan. His house was 25 minutes away by bike. But his parents would be worried if I returned so late on my own. So I called my mother's phone instead.

"I'm locked out," I said, trying not to cry. "And Sister is sleeping like a stone."

My mother was silent for a second. Then she said, "Don't worry. Aunt Zhou lives on the fifth floor of your building, right? Knock on her door. She always stays up late reading. Tell her I told you to come. Wait inside her home. We'll be back before midnight."

I had only spoken with Aunt Zhou twice, when we met in the lift. But I climbed the stairs and knocked gently. A light turned on. Aunt Zhou, in a soft old sweater, opened the door. She listened to me, smiled, and said, "Come in. I was just going to drink some hot ginger tea."

In her kitchen, the small yellow lamp made everything warm. She gave me a towel for my hair. While I drank the ginger tea, she read her book and hummed an old song. By 11:45, my parents called my mobile. They were downstairs. I thanked Aunt Zhou and ran home into my mother's arms.

That night, before I slept, I wrote a small note: "**Always take the keys. And always know your neighbours.**" I stuck it on the bedroom mirror. A year later, the note is still there. And my keys always, always go into the inside pocket now.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why were the narrator's parents away that evening?**

A. They were working late at the office.  
B. They had gone to a wedding in the next city.  
C. They had travelled abroad for a business trip.  
D. They were at the cinema with friends.

**2. Why didn't the sister answer the door or phone?**

A. Because she was watching a movie very loudly.  
B. Because she was angry with the narrator.  
C. Because she was a very deep sleeper and had put her phone on silent.  
D. Because she had gone out with friends.

**3. Why did the narrator decide NOT to go back to Yuan's house?**

A. Because the rain had already stopped.  
B. Because Yuan's parents would be worried if he returned so late on his own.  
C. Because Yuan had already gone to bed.  
D. Because the metro had stopped for the night.

**4. What did Aunt Zhou do when the narrator arrived?**

A. She told him to wait outside in the corridor.  
B. She let him in, gave him a towel, and made him hot ginger tea.  
C. She phoned the police.  
D. She sent him home by taxi.

**5. What did the narrator learn from this experience?**

A. Neighbours are always dangerous.  
B. Always take the keys and always know your neighbours.  
C. Never go to a friend's house in the evening.  
D. Rainy nights are always fun.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 段 "a wedding in the next city"  
【解析】父母去邻市参加婚礼。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 段 "deep sleeper... on silent"  
【解析】姐姐睡得深且手机静音。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 5 段 "his parents would be worried..."  
【解析】顾虑同学父母担心。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 7 段  
【解析】阿姨让他进屋、递毛巾、煮姜茶。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段  
【解析】教训：带钥匙+认识邻居。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
