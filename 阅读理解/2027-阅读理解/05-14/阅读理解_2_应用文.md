# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Youth Dance Competition 2027 — Call for Entries**

> **CITY YOUTH CULTURE CENTRE**  
> **"DANCE OF THE WIND" — YOUTH DANCE COMPETITION 2027**  
> *Saturday, 27 June 2027 · Youth Culture Centre, Main Stage*

---

Dear middle school students,

For the tenth year in a row, our **City Youth Culture Centre** invites young dancers to the stage. This summer, the theme of our dance competition is **"Dance of the Wind"** — a free theme that welcomes modern, folk, classical Chinese, hip-hop and creative dance pieces, as long as the idea of "wind" is present somewhere in the choreography or music.

### Who May Join

- Students in **Grades 6–9** (ages 11–15). Students in Grade 10 may join only as team leaders.  
- **Solo, duo, or group** entries (group size up to **10 dancers**).  
- Open to students of every city school, both public and private.

### Categories

| Code | Category | Time Limit | Notes |
|------|----------|------------|-------|
| **S-M** | Modern Dance (solo) | 2 min 30 sec | Any modern style |
| **D-C** | Classical Chinese Dance (duo) | 3 min 00 sec | Traditional music welcome |
| **G-F** | Folk Dance (group) | 3 min 30 sec | Minority or regional dance encouraged |
| **G-H** | Hip-Hop Dance (group) | 3 min 00 sec | Clean lyrics only, PG-rated |
| **G-X** | Creative Dance (group) | 4 min 00 sec | Story-based, any style |

### Dates

- **Entry opens:** 15 May 2027  
- **Entry closes:** 1 June 2027, 20:00  
- **Online video round:** 5–10 June 2027  
- **Live final:** Saturday, 27 June 2027, 14:00–17:30

### Prizes

- Gold Dance Trophy (top in each category): 1,000 yuan dance shop gift card  
- Silver Dance Trophy (second): 500 yuan gift card  
- Bronze Dance Trophy (third): 200 yuan gift card  
- Special **"Wind of Creativity" Award** (chosen by teacher judges): a free summer dance workshop at the Centre.

Every dancer who reaches the live final will receive a **Certificate** and a commemorative fan.

### Rules

1. Each student may join in at most **two** categories.  
2. Music tracks must be pre-recorded (MP3/WAV). Live singing on stage is not allowed.  
3. Costumes must be **suitable for school stage**. Dangerous accessories (such as real fire, very sharp props) are not allowed.  
4. Backstage, dancers must keep quiet and follow the stage manager.  
5. Photos and short clips from the final will be posted on our official account. Students who do not want to appear online must notify us by email at registration.

### How to Enter

- **Step 1:** Visit **www.cityyouthculture.cn/danceofwind2027** and fill in the form.  
- **Step 2:** Upload a **3-minute practice video** (phone video is fine) with your team's name on a paper card in the first five seconds.  
- **Step 3:** Pay the **80-yuan** entry fee per entry. Group entries pay **200 yuan** flat.

For questions, email **dance@cyculture.cn** or call **020-2244-8866** (Tue–Sun, 10:00–17:00).

**May every step carry the wind — light, free and full of life.**

— Youth Dance Committee

**根据通告内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is this year's dance competition theme?**

A. "Stars above the city."  
B. "Dance of the Wind."  
C. "Colours of the sea."  
D. "The power of fire."

**2. Which category has the LONGEST time limit?**

A. S-M Modern Dance (solo).  
B. D-C Classical Chinese Dance (duo).  
C. G-X Creative Dance (group).  
D. G-H Hip-Hop Dance (group).

**3. Which of the following is NOT allowed?**

A. Pre-recorded music tracks.  
B. School-suitable costumes.  
C. Live singing on stage.  
D. Regional folk dances.

**4. How much is the entry fee for a group entry?**

A. 80 yuan per student.  
B. 200 yuan flat.  
C. 500 yuan flat.  
D. Free of charge.

**5. Who will receive the "Wind of Creativity" Award?**

A. The tallest dancer.  
B. The youngest dancer.  
C. A team or dancer chosen by teacher judges for creativity; the award is a free summer dance workshop.  
D. Anyone who pays the highest fee.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开头段 "the theme of our dance competition is 'Dance of the Wind'"  
【解析】主题就是"风之舞"。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Categories 表 G-X "4 min 00 sec"  
【解析】Creative Dance 时长最长 4 分钟。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules 第2条 "Live singing on stage is not allowed."  
【解析】不允许现场唱歌。A/B/D 均符合规则。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How to Enter Step 3 "Group entries pay 200 yuan flat."  
【解析】团体报名统一 200 元。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Prizes 条目 "Special 'Wind of Creativity' Award (chosen by teacher judges): a free summer dance workshop..."  
【解析】由老师评审挑选，奖品为免费暑期舞蹈工作坊。A/B/D 均无据。故选 C。
