# 阅读理解（应用文）

## **School Sports Day Notice**

① **Sunshine Middle School Sports Day 2027** will take place on **Friday, April 24**. All students in Grades 7–9 should arrive at the school playground **before 8:00 a.m.** The opening ceremony starts at 8:30 a.m. Parents are welcome to watch, but they must sit in Area P only.

② **Main events** are listed below. Please read them carefully and choose your role (player or helper) with your class teacher.

| Time | Event | Place |
|------|--------|--------|
| 9:00–11:30 a.m. | Track races (100 m, 400 m, relay) | Playground |
| 2:00–4:30 p.m. | Field events (long jump, shot put) | Sports hall |

③ **Safety rules:** Wear sports shoes. Do not run on wet ground. If you feel sick, tell a teacher at once. Students who break the rules may miss their events.

④ **What to bring:** A water bottle, a sun hat, and your student card. **Do not bring** glass bottles or sharp tools. The school will offer simple snacks, but you may bring your own lunch.

⑤ **Questions?** Call the PE office at **555-0198** or email **pe@sunshine.edu** before April 20. Rain plan: if it rains hard in the morning, we will move the day to **Monday, April 27**, and post news on the school website by 7:00 a.m. on April 24.

**注释：** ceremony 开幕式；relay 接力赛；shot put 铅球

---

### 选择题

**1.** When should students arrive at the playground?

A. Before 7:00 a.m.  
B. Before 8:00 a.m.  
C. At 8:30 a.m.  
D. Before the opening ceremony ends  

**2.** Where will the long jump take place?

A. In Area P.  
B. On the playground.  
C. In the sports hall.  
D. In the classroom building.  

**3.** What can we infer from Paragraph ③?

A. The school takes students’ safety seriously during the sports day.  
B. Students can run on wet ground if they wear sports shoes.  
C. Breaking rules will always cause accidents.  
D. Teachers will not help students who feel sick.  

**4.** What is the main purpose of this notice?

A. To sell sports shoes to parents.  
B. To tell students and parents about the sports day plan and rules.  
C. To invite teachers to a meeting.  
D. To change the school email address.  

**5.** The underlined word **“post”** in Paragraph ⑤ is closest in meaning to ______.

A. pay for  
B. carry away  
C. delete  
D. put information online for others to see  

---

### 答案与解析

**1.**  
**【答案】** B  
**【题型】** 细节理解  
**【定位】** 第①段：“All students in Grades 7–9 should arrive at the school playground **before 8:00 a.m.**”  
**【解析】** 原文明确写学生须在早上8点前到达操场，故选 B。A（7点前）与原文不符；C 是开幕式开始时间，不是到达时间；D 混淆“到达”与“开幕式结束”。

**2.**  
**【答案】** C  
**【题型】** 细节理解  
**【定位】** 第②段表格中 “Field events (long jump, shot put)” 对应 “Sports hall”。  
**【解析】** 跳远属于 field events，地点为 Sports hall，选 C。A 是家长观赛区；B 是径赛场地；D 文中未提。

**3.**  
**【答案】** A  
**【题型】** 推理判断  
**【定位】** 第③段：“**Safety rules:** Wear sports shoes. Do not run on wet ground. If you feel sick, tell a teacher at once.”  
**【解析】** 列出安全规则、禁止湿滑场地奔跑、不适即报告老师，可推断学校非常重视运动会期间的安全，选 A。B 与 “Do not run on wet ground” 矛盾；C 文中只说 “may miss their events”，未说 “always cause accidents”；D 与 “tell a teacher at once” 矛盾。

**4.**  
**【答案】** B  
**【题型】** 写作意图  
**【定位】** 全文：时间地点、项目表格、安全与携带物、联系方式及雨天预案。  
**【解析】** 通知旨在告知师生及家长运动会安排与注意事项，选 B。A、C、D 均非本文目的。

**5.**  
**【答案】** D  
**【题型】** 词义猜测  
**【定位】** 第⑤段：“we will move the day… and **post** news on the school website by 7:00 a.m.”  
**【解析】** 在网站上 post news 意为发布消息，与 “put information online for others to see” 最接近，选 D。pay for（付费）、carry away（带走）、delete（删除）均不合语境。
