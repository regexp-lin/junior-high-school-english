# 阅读理解（记叙文） **From the Bench to the Team**

① When I joined Class 8 last September, the first thing that surprised me was the basketball court behind the teaching building. It was always full of laughter and bouncing balls. I loved watching, but I had never played. My legs felt slow, my body too thin, and a ball seemed almost as big as my head. So for the first two months, I only sat on the wooden bench and watched from a safe corner.

② One Friday afternoon, our PE teacher, Mr. Zhao, came to me as I sat alone. "Li Ming," he said, "you watched three games this week. Maybe it's time you joined one." I shook my head and said, "I'm not good, Mr. Zhao. I'll only make the team lose." He smiled, put a worn orange ball in my hands and said, "Everyone starts on the bench, even Yao Ming. The first step is not winning; it's standing up."

③ That evening, I stayed behind the school and practised shooting. My first twenty shots missed. My wrist hurt, and the ball rolled far each time. A group of Grade 9 boys passed by. One of them, whose name was Kai, turned back and said, "Push with your legs, not only with your arms. And look at the front of the ring, not the back." He showed me three times, then added, "Tomorrow, same place, six o'clock. Come if you're brave."

④ For the next two weeks, I went there every evening. Kai did not always come, but when he did, he was patient. My hands became rough; my school uniform smelled of sweat; but by the end of the second week, I could make five shots out of ten. More importantly, I no longer turned red when a ball came my way.

⑤ Finally, the class match day arrived. Our team needed one more player. Mr. Zhao looked around and called my name. My heart jumped into my throat. For the first five minutes, I only ran up and down the court. Then, with ten seconds left in the first half, Kai, who was on the other team, **accidentally** passed the ball in my direction. I did not think — I just jumped, caught it, and threw it toward the ring. It bounced twice on the rim and finally went in. The whole class shouted. Mr. Zhao clapped so hard that his hat fell off.

⑥ We did not win that match; we lost by six points. But when the game ended, Kai ran across the court, smiled broadly, and gave me a small punch on the shoulder. "Welcome to the team," he said. That night, I wrote only one line in my notebook: *"The longest distance in the world is not from the bench to the court — it is from 'I can't' to 'let me try.'"* From then on, that distance became a little shorter every day.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the writer only watch basketball at first?**

A. He did not like basketball at all.  
B. He felt slow, thin, and thought the ball was too big.  
C. He was afraid of Mr. Zhao.  
D. There was no time after school.

**2. What did Mr. Zhao tell the writer on Friday afternoon?**

A. "You will never play basketball well."  
B. "The first step is not winning; it's standing up."  
C. "You must leave the court at once."  
D. "Study harder before you play."

**3. Who helped the writer practise shooting in the evenings?**

A. Mr. Zhao.  
B. His younger sister.  
C. A Grade 9 boy named Kai.  
D. A student from another school.

**4. What happened on the class match day?**

A. The writer scored the first goal for his team.  
B. The writer refused to play.  
C. The writer's team lost, but the writer scored one basket.  
D. The writer's team won by twenty points.

**5. What is the main idea of the last paragraph?**

A. The writer will change schools next year.  
B. The writer decided never to play again.  
C. The shortest distance is from "I can't" to "let me try."  
D. Winning is the only thing that really matters.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "My legs felt slow, my body too thin"  
【解析】因身体弱、球大而只观看。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "The first step is not winning; it's standing up."  
【解析】赵老师原话。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Kai... showed me three times"  
【解析】九年级的凯教他投篮。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "We did not win... finally went in"  
【解析】队伍输球，作者进了一个。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑥段引语  
【解析】"我不行"到"让我试试"的距离。故选 C。
