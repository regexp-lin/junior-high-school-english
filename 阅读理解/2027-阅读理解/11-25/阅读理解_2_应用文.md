# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School Winter Exam Study-Hall 2027**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Academic Office + Library**  
> **Winter Exam Study-Hall** — *"A quiet room, a warm light, a small plan."*  
> **Mon 7 – Fri 18 December 2027, 17:30 – 20:30**  
> **Venue**: Library Floor 2 (for self-study) + Rooms 204 / 206 (for teacher help desks)

Final exams begin on Monday 21 December. The last two weeks of revision are often the **hardest two weeks of the term**, especially for students whose homes are small, noisy, or far from a quiet desk. Starting this year, our school will open a **Winter Exam Study-Hall** each weekday evening, so that any student who wishes to revise in a calm, warm, well-lit room may stay at school until 20:30.

---

## 🕒 Opening Hours & Sessions

| Session | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **S1** (early) | 17:30 – 18:25 | Silent reading, self-made revision plans, flashcards |
| **Dinner break** | 18:25 – 19:00 | Short dinner in the small dining hall (see below) |
| **S2** (middle) | 19:00 – 19:55 | Silent self-study; one teacher at the help desk |
| **S3** (late) | 20:00 – 20:30 | Optional closing session; reading only, no writing |

- Students may **join only S1, only S2, or all three**. No one is forced to stay the full 3 hours.  
- A soft bell rings at 17:30, 19:00, 20:00 and 20:30.  
- **Silent zone**: absolutely no talking on Library Floor 2; for all questions, walk to the corridor outside or to the help desk in Room 204.

---

## 🧑‍🏫 Teacher Help Desks

From Monday 7 to Thursday 17 December, **two rooms** will have teachers on duty for short, quiet questions:

| Day | Room 204 (Subject) | Room 206 (Subject) |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Maths | English |
| Tue | Physics | Chinese |
| Wed | Maths | Chemistry |
| Thu | English | History / Politics |

- Each question is limited to **5 minutes**; please come with a **specific** question (e.g., "How do I solve Problem 14 on page 62?"), not "Can you help me with my maths?".  
- Please bring your own **book, paper, and pencil**. No printing service in the help rooms.

---

## 🍜 Dinner Arrangement (18:25 – 19:00)

A **small, warm dinner** will be served in the south dining hall for students staying for Study-Hall:

- **Price**: 8 yuan per set; includes one bowl of rice + one hot dish + one soup, or one noodle set. Free for students on the school meal-support list.  
- **Order in advance**: please order on the school mini-app → Dining → Study-Hall Dinner, **before 11:00 of the same day**. Orders after 11:00 cannot be made.  
- **Bring your school meal card** (or the mini-app QR code) and a small reusable water bottle — **no single-use cups**.

---

## 📚 Suggested "One-Page Plan"

A small one-page revision plan, printed in pale blue, will be placed on every Study-Hall table. The idea is simple:

- **17:30 – 18:00**: one hard subject, the one you most want to avoid today.  
- **18:00 – 18:25**: one easier subject, to reward your brain.  
- **19:00 – 19:30**: review today's mistakes quietly (no new topic).  
- **19:30 – 19:55**: one memory task (vocabulary, short formulas, historical dates).  
- **20:00 – 20:30**: light reading — an old lesson, a set of notes, or a small English poem.

Please do **not** copy this plan blindly; write your own on the back of the paper, in pencil, for tomorrow.

---

## 🤫 Silent Rules

Because the space is shared by many, we kindly ask every student to keep the Study-Hall **quiet in five small ways**:

1. **Phones off** (not silent — fully off) during S1, S2 and S3, except for a 5-minute family-message window at 18:55.  
2. **No food or drink** on the library desks; water bottles only, placed on the floor shelf.  
3. **No group study** in the library; pairs/groups may use Room 208 (noisy desks) instead.  
4. **Walk, do not run**, in the corridor. The carpet helps, but we also help.  
5. If you finish early and wish to leave at 19:50, please stand up, push in your chair quietly, bow a small thank-you to the teacher on duty, and walk out slowly.

---

## 🚫 Four Things Not Allowed in the Study-Hall

- **No earphones with long cords**; only small ear-buds (cords get tangled with chairs).  
- **No music with words**; soft instrumental music is fine at low volume.  
- **No boards full of coloured markers**; if you must draw mind-maps, use pencil.  
- **No one younger than Grade 6** in the library; the study-hall is strictly for Grade 7–9 students of our school.

---

## 🙋 Volunteer Roles (30 students)

- **10 silent-zone guides** (help visitors find seats, 17:20–17:40).  
- **6 dining-hall helpers** at 18:25 (wipe tables, guide queue).  
- **6 help-desk runners** in Rooms 204/206 (call the next student quietly).  
- **4 closing-time keepers** at 20:30.  
- **4 quiet-feedback collectors** (hand out pink feedback cards at the end).

Sign-up on the school mini-app → Academic Office → Study-Hall. **Deadline Thu 03 December, 17:30.**
