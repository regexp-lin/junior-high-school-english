# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School International Food-Culture Day 2027**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · English Department + Geography Department + Student Union**  
> **Saturday 14 November 2027 · "Small Plates, Big World"**  
> **A day of cooking, tasting, storytelling — and saying hello in eight languages.**

Our school has students from **13 provinces** and two of our teachers have worked abroad for many years. The world, it seems, is already quietly living inside our classrooms. On **Saturday 14 November 2027**, we will bring it out into the school yard for one big, happy day. Please read this notice carefully. Some parts need you to **act before 5 November**.

---

## 📅 Quick Facts

- **Date:** Saturday 14 November 2027.  
- **Time:** 09:00 – 15:30.  
- **Place:** School playground + canteen + lecture hall.  
- **Who:** all students of Lihua Middle School; parents are warmly welcome from 11:00.  
- **Cost:** **Free** for students; parents please bring their school guest card.

---

## 🌍 The Eight Country Tables

This year, eight student groups (mixed Grade 7, 8, 9) will host a "country table". Each table cooks **one small, safe dish** and tells **one short story** from that country.

| Table | Country | Sample dish | Short story |
|---|---|---|---|
| A | China (Shaanxi) | **Roujiamo** 肉夹馍 | The 2,000-year-old road of wheat |
| B | China (Guangdong) | **Cheung fun** 肠粉 | Morning tea in a busy city |
| C | Japan | **Onigiri** 饭团 | Why is there a little dent on top? |
| D | Italy | **Bruschetta** 番茄面包片 | The garden of Tuscany |
| E | Türkiye | **Simit** 圆环面包 | A bread shaped like a bicycle wheel |
| F | Mexico | **Chicken tortilla wrap** | The corn that built a civilization |
| G | India | **Mild chickpea curry** 鹰嘴豆咖喱 | Spices from sea to kitchen |
| H | Kenya | **Mandazi** 小甜饼 | Songs sung at family breakfast |

All dishes are **egg-, peanut-, and alcohol-free**. Students with food allergies will get a pink wristband at 08:45 with clear labels.

---

## 👩‍🍳 How Students Can Take Part

1. **Be a host at a table.** Sign up in your English teacher's office by **Tuesday 5 November 2027, 17:00**. Only 40 student hosts will be chosen; Grade 9 can join only if they have NOT signed up for another major weekend activity this term.  
2. **Be a small-stage performer.** At 13:00, the lecture hall will host 20-minute "mini-stories from abroad" — Chinese calligraphy, a Kenyan song, an Italian recipe read aloud. Please send a short video or written plan to Ms. Xu (English office) by the same deadline.  
3. **Be a visitor.** If you prefer to enjoy the day quietly, just come with a clean lunch box, a paper tissue, and a small notebook; you may collect a tiny tasting portion from each table.

---

## 🙋 Rules for a Kind Day

- **Eat only what you can finish.** Food left over will be donated to a nearby community centre in the evening; please do not waste.  
- **Listen before you taste.** At every table, the host will tell their 60-second story first. No cutting the line.  
- **Respect beliefs.** Some classmates may choose not to eat certain dishes because of family or religious reasons. Please do not push them.  
- **Languages.** Try to say **"thank you"** in the language of each table (we will print a small card for you). "Xièxie", "Arigatō", "Grazie", "Teşekkürler", "Gracias", "Dhanyavaad", "Asante" — these are the small gifts a day like this leaves in your mouth.

---

## ☔ Bad-Weather Plan

If the forecast for 14 November shows rain or temperatures below 6 °C, the event will move **fully indoors** to the canteen and lecture hall. Decision posted on the school mini-app **before 18:00 on 12 November**. The date itself will **not** change.
