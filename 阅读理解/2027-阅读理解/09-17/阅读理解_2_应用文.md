# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 Autumn Sports Meet "Faster · Stronger · Together"**

---

> **Lihua Middle School — Sports Department**
> **Circular for All Students, Parents and Teachers**
> Issued: **10th September, 2027**

**Dear students,**

Autumn is here, the sky is clear, and the playground is ready. Our school will hold the **2027 Autumn Sports Meet "Faster · Stronger · Together"** on **Thursday 30th September** and **Friday 1st October**, from **8:00 to 16:30** each day on the **main playground**. Every class from Grade 7 to Grade 9 must join, and every student is expected to take part in **at least one event** — as a player, a cheerleader, a reporter, or a volunteer.

---

## 1. Main Events

| Group | Track Events | Field Events | Team Events |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Boys | 100 m / 400 m / 1 500 m / 4×100 m relay | Long jump / Shot put | Tug-of-war · Rope-skipping team |
| Girls | 100 m / 400 m / 800 m / 4×100 m relay | Long jump / High jump | Tug-of-war · Rope-skipping team |
| Mixed | — | — | Grade Relay Race (4 boys + 4 girls per class) |

> **Note:** Each student may sign up for **up to two individual events** plus team events.

---

## 2. Sign-up Rules

1. Fill in the **orange paper form** given out by your PE teacher **before Monday 20th September**.
2. Each class must hand in **one** completed form; individual late forms will **not** be accepted.
3. Students with a **cold, fever, or recent injury** must **not** sign up for track events. A note from the school doctor is required.
4. All signed-up players will receive their lane and time on a posted list outside the PE office on **Wednesday 22nd September**.

---

## 3. Opening & Closing

- **Opening Ceremony** — Thursday 30th Sep, 8:00 at the main playground.
  Each class prepares a **2-minute parade and slogan** in Chinese or English.
- **Closing Ceremony** — Friday 1st Oct, 15:30 at the main playground.
  Top 3 classes in total points will receive the **"Golden Lion Flag"** (kept for one year).

---

## 4. Volunteer Jobs

Students who do not wish to run or jump can still help their class earn points:

- **Sports Reporters** (2 per class) — write a 150-word English report; best report will be printed in *Morning Breeze* student newspaper.
- **Cheer Team** (6–8 per class) — prepare flags and cheers; quietness during other classes' events is required.
- **First-aid helpers** (1 per class) — work with the school nurse; short training on Wednesday 22nd Sep, 15:30, Room 109.

---

## 5. Safety & Equipment

- **Dress code:** sports clothes, **sports shoes only**, school jacket if windy.
- **No glass bottles, no skateboards, no pets** on the playground.
- Drinking water is free at **four water stations** along the playground.
- In case of light rain, events continue; in case of heavy rain or thunder, events move to Saturday 2nd Oct (same time, same place).

> **Emergency contact:** School Nurse Ms Zhao · Room 109 · 010-8888-0139

---

## 6. Parents & Cheerleaders

Parents are warmly welcome on **both days between 9:00 and 12:00**. Please enter through the **east gate** and show your ID card to the guard. **No cars** are allowed inside the school; bike parking is available next to the small south gate.

---

> **Let us run together, cheer together, and grow together. See you on the track!**
>
> — **Sports Department, Lihua Middle School**

---

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When and where will the 2027 Autumn Sports Meet be held?**

A. On Wednesday 22nd September in the gym.  
B. On Thursday 30th September and Friday 1st October, from 8:00 to 16:30, on the main playground.  
C. On Saturday 2nd October only.  
D. Every day in October, inside the school building.

**2. A Grade-7 boy wants to sign up for 100 m, 1 500 m, long jump and the class relay. Is this allowed?**

A. Yes — there is no limit at all.  
B. No — each student may sign up for at most two individual events, plus team events; so four individual events is too many.  
C. Yes — as long as his mother writes a letter.  
D. No — boys are not allowed to do long jump.

**3. A student has caught a cold. What does the circular say he should do?**

A. Still run all events to earn points.  
B. Must NOT sign up for track events; a note from the school doctor is required.  
C. Stay at home for the rest of the term.  
D. Pay a fine of ¥50.

**4. Which of the following is NOT an acceptable way for a non-running student to help the class?**

A. Being a sports reporter who writes a 150-word English report.  
B. Joining the cheer team of 6–8 students.  
C. Serving as a first-aid helper after short training on Wednesday.  
D. Bringing skateboards for classmates to ride during breaks.

**5. What will happen if there is heavy rain or thunder during the Sports Meet?**

A. The Sports Meet will be cancelled completely.  
B. Events will move to Saturday 2nd October at the same time and same place.  
C. All students must stay at home for one week.  
D. Events will continue on the playground as usual.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】通知正文 "Thursday 30th September and Friday 1st October, from 8:00 to 16:30 each day on the main playground"  
【解析】明确给出时间和地点。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 部分 "each student may sign up for up to two individual events plus team events"  
【解析】单项最多 2 项，4 项个人项目超限。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 部分第 3 条 "cold, fever, or recent injury must not sign up for track events. A note from the school doctor is required"  
【解析】感冒禁参径赛且需校医证明。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节判断题  
【定位】第 5 部分 "No glass bottles, no skateboards, no pets on the playground"  
【解析】操场禁止滑板，故带滑板并非被允许的方式。A、B、C 均为通知列出的志愿方式。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 5 部分 "in case of heavy rain or thunder, events move to Saturday 2nd Oct (same time, same place)"  
【解析】大雨/雷雨将改到 10 月 2 日同一时间地点。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。
