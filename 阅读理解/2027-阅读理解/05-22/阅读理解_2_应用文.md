# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Library — Summer Reading Challenge 2027 (Brochure)**

**CITY LIBRARY · YOUNG READERS' CENTRE**  
**Summer Reading Challenge 2027 — "30 Books, 30 Smiles"**

---

**Who can join?**  
All students from **Grade 5 to Grade 9** with a valid City Library card.  
New members may apply for a free library card at the 1F service desk with an ID document and a guardian's signature.

**Activity period**  
**Monday 5 July – Saturday 28 August, 2027** (8 weeks)  
Open every day from **09:00 – 20:00**, except every **first Monday** of the month (staff training day).

**How does it work?**

1. **Sign up online** at www.citylibrary.cn/summer2027 or at the 2F reading desk by **Sunday 4 July**.  
2. Read any book from the library's recommendation list. You may also choose a book of your own — please ask the librarian to confirm it.  
3. After finishing each book, write a **short reading card** (about 100 words in Chinese or 80 words in English) and put it into the **Green Reading Box** near the stairs.  
4. Your progress will be updated on the big wall chart every Friday.

**Book levels and numbers needed for a prize**

| Level | Suitable for | Books to finish |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Bronze | Grade 5–6 | 6 books |
| Silver | Grade 7 | 10 books |
| Gold  | Grade 8–9 | 15 books |

A student who reads **20 or more books** on any level will be named **"Super Reader of the Summer"** and take part in the closing ceremony on **Saturday 3 September**.

**Free weekend events**

- **Every Saturday 10:00 – 11:30** — **Author Talk**: meet a Chinese or foreign writer. First 40 seats only.  
- **Every Sunday 15:00 – 16:00** — **Story Circle**: a librarian reads a chapter aloud; suitable for Grade 5–6 students.  
- **Friday 7 August, 19:00 – 20:30** — **Reading Night Party**: students bring one favourite book and one small snack to share. (Parents welcome.)

**Rules to remember**

- Please keep quiet; talking should be in a low voice.  
- Food and sugary drinks are **not allowed** in the reading rooms, but water is fine.  
- Books may be borrowed for up to **14 days**; one student may borrow **5 books at a time**.  
- Lost or damaged books must be reported within 3 days.

**Need help?**  
Ask any staff member in a **green T-shirt**, or call the Young Readers' Centre at **400-820-7788**, Monday to Friday, 09:00 – 17:00.

*See you in the library this summer!* — Young Readers' Centre, City Library

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Who can join the Summer Reading Challenge 2027?**

A. Only university students.  
B. Students from Grade 5 to Grade 9 with a valid library card.  
C. Only teachers.  
D. Only children under Grade 3.

**2. When is the library closed during the challenge?**

A. Every Sunday.  
B. Every first Monday of the month (staff training day).  
C. Every afternoon.  
D. Every Friday morning.

**3. How many books does a Grade 8 student need to read to win the Gold level?**

A. 6 books.  
B. 10 books.  
C. 15 books.  
D. 30 books.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed in the reading rooms?**

A. Water.  
B. Low-voice talking.  
C. Food and sugary drinks.  
D. Reading quietly.

**5. Who will be called "Super Reader of the Summer"?**

A. A student who borrows five books at a time.  
B. A student who reads 20 or more books during the challenge.  
C. A student who comes to the library every Saturday.  
D. A student who lives nearest to the library.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Who can join?" 部分  
【解析】仅限 5–9 年级且持借书证的学生。A/C/D 均不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Activity period" 部分 "except every first Monday of the month (staff training day)."  
【解析】每月第一个周一为培训日闭馆。A/C/D 均不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 Gold 行 "Grade 8–9 · 15 books"  
【解析】金奖 15 本。A 为铜奖；B 为银奖；D 为超级读者所需。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Rules to remember" 部分 "Food and sugary drinks are not allowed..."  
【解析】食物与含糖饮料禁止进入阅览室。A 水允许；B/D 均可做。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"A student who reads 20 or more books on any level will be named 'Super Reader of the Summer'..."  
【解析】阅读 20 本及以上获称号。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。
