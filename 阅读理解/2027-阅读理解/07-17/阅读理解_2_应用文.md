# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Summer of Skills" — Free Workshops for Grade 7–9 Students**

**City Youth Centre · July 20 – August 10, 2027**

Dear Grade 7–9 students,

The summer holiday is the best time to try something new. This year, **City Youth Centre** and **Greenline Library** are working together to offer **"Summer of Skills"**, a free programme of short workshops for middle-school students. Every workshop lasts **two hours** and is taught by a local expert. Choose any workshop you like — no fee, no test, just come and learn.

---

### Workshop Menu

**1) Kitchen Basics — "Cook One Real Meal"**  
**When:** Every Tuesday, 10:00–12:00  
**Where:** Youth Centre · Room 201 (open kitchen)  
**What:** You will learn to wash rice, use a gas stove safely, cook three simple dishes (tomato-egg, steamed greens, noodle soup), and clean up the kitchen. At the end of the class, you will eat what you cooked — together.  
**Bring:** an apron, a small lunch box (take the leftovers home).  
**Limit:** 14 students per class.

**2) Library Detective — "Find Any Book in 3 Minutes"**  
**When:** Every Wednesday, 14:00–16:00  
**Where:** Greenline Library · 3F Reading Hall  
**What:** Librarians will show you how the book numbers work, how to use the search computers, and how to borrow books from other branches. We will end with a small game: teams race to find ten books as fast as they can.  
**Bring:** your library card (or we will help you make a new one — free).  
**Limit:** 20 students per class.

**3) First Aid for Teens — "Be the Calm One"**  
**When:** Every Thursday, 9:30–11:30  
**Where:** Youth Centre · Room 305  
**What:** A Red Cross trainer will teach you how to stop a nosebleed, what to do if a friend faints, how to make a safe emergency call, and the basic CPR steps on a practice doll. You will get a small certificate at the end.  
**Bring:** a water bottle; wear comfortable clothes.  
**Limit:** 16 students per class.

**4) Phone-Camera Photography — "See Your City Again"**  
**When:** Every Friday, 15:00–17:00  
**Where:** meet outside the Youth Centre gate (we walk to Old Town Street)  
**What:** A local photographer will teach you light, angle, and the "rule of thirds". You will walk, take photos, and come back to choose your best three for the centre's wall.  
**Bring:** a phone (any kind is fine), a cap for the sun.  
**Limit:** 12 students per class.

**5) Weekend Volunteering — "Read to Little Ones"**  
**When:** Every Saturday, 10:00–11:00  
**Where:** Greenline Library · Children's Corner  
**What:** You will read picture books aloud to children aged 4–7. Each volunteer reads one or two books and answers simple questions. It is a chance to practise speaking clearly and to help younger kids love reading. Volunteer hours will be recorded on your school **volunteer card**.  
**Bring:** a quiet voice; we provide the books.  
**Limit:** 8 volunteers per session.

---

### How to Sign Up

- Go to **www.cityyouth2027.cn/summer** (open from July 13).  
- Fill in your name, class, phone number, and tick the workshops you want.  
- You can sign up for **up to 3 different workshops**, but only **one workshop per day**.  
- You will get a confirmation message within 24 hours.

### Rules

- Please arrive **10 minutes early**. Latecomers after 15 minutes cannot join that day.  
- If you cannot come, **cancel online at least 12 hours before** so another student can take your seat.  
- Parents are welcome to pick up and drop off, but they do not need to stay.

### Contact

- Phone: 0571-8866-2027 (10:00–17:00, Monday–Friday)  
- Email: summerskills@cityyouth.cn  
- WeChat: search "CityYouth2027"

**Come, try, fail, laugh, learn.** A skill you learn this summer may help you for many years. See you at City Youth Centre!

*— City Youth Centre & Greenline Library, July 2027*
