# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Junior Volunteer Camp — Summer 2027**

---

## **Hello, Future Volunteer!**

Hot summer days, long free afternoons, and a whole city waiting for your help. **City Junior Volunteer Camp** is back for its **8th year** this summer. It is a short, warm and friendly programme for middle-school students (Grade 7–9) in our city. You will **meet new friends**, **try real community work**, and **earn official volunteer hours** that your school accepts.

- **Dates:** **July 18 – August 15, 2027** (four weeks)  
- **Where:** Different service spots across the city  
- **Open to:** Any middle-school student who is at least **13 years old** on the first day  
- **Fee:** **Free**. The camp even gives you a T-shirt, a lunch box and daily water.

---

## **The Four Service Groups**

| Group | Main Activity | Days / Week | Service Spot | Spots |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Green Hands** | Tree care, rubbish sorting in the park | Mon & Wed, 9:00–11:30 | Central City Park | 40 |
| **Little Teachers** | Simple English games with kids aged 6–9 | Tue & Thu, 15:00–17:00 | East District Library | 30 |
| **Kind Carers** | Chat, read newspapers & play chess with lonely old people | Fri, 9:30–11:30 | Sunshine Care Home | 25 |
| **City Helpers** | Help lost visitors, clean bus stop areas | Sat, 8:30–11:00 | Main Train Station | 20 |

> You may sign up for **only one** group. If more than 115 students apply, the camp office chooses by short written answers.

---

## **How to Sign Up**

1. **Step 1** — Visit `volunteer.city-edu.cn` and fill in the online form. Bring one short answer: "**Why do I want to volunteer?**" (50–80 English words).  
2. **Step 2** — Ask **one teacher** at your school to write a short "okay" note. Upload a photo of the note.  
3. **Step 3** — After you receive an email that says "accepted", come to the **opening meeting** on **July 17, Saturday, 10:00, City Youth Centre** (Room 101) with your school ID card.  
4. **Deadline:** **July 5, 2027, 23:59**. Late forms will not be read.

---

## **Rules for Volunteers**

- Please wear the official **yellow camp T-shirt** on every service day.  
- Arrive **15 minutes early**; if you cannot come, call the group leader **before 8:00** that morning.  
- **No phone games** during service time. You may keep your phone for safety calls only.  
- Always **speak politely** to the people you help. If you feel unsure, ask the group leader.  
- At least **16 attendances** in the four weeks are needed to earn the official certificate.

---

## **What You Get**

- A **City Volunteer Certificate** (signed by the City Youth Office).  
- **30 official volunteer hours** added to your school record.  
- A **group photo album** at the closing ceremony on **August 15, 18:30, City Youth Centre**.  
- **Top 5 Volunteers** (chosen by group leaders and community friends): a small reading lamp and a one-year free pass to the City Museum.

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long does the 2027 City Junior Volunteer Camp last?**

A. Two weeks.  
B. Three weeks.  
C. Four weeks, from July 18 to August 15.  
D. The whole summer holiday, from June to September.

**2. Li Ming, aged 14, would like to practise English with young children. Which group should he choose?**

A. Green Hands.  
B. Little Teachers.  
C. Kind Carers.  
D. City Helpers.

**3. By what date must a student finish the online sign-up form?**

A. July 5, 2027.  
B. July 17, 2027.  
C. July 18, 2027.  
D. August 15, 2027.

**4. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed during service time?**

A. Wearing the official yellow camp T-shirt.  
B. Arriving 15 minutes before the start.  
C. Playing games on a phone while working.  
D. Asking the group leader when you feel unsure.

**5. A student attended only 12 days in four weeks. What is most likely to happen?**

A. The student will get the official certificate as usual.  
B. The student will **not** receive the official certificate, because at least 16 attendances are required.  
C. The student will be chosen as one of the Top 5 Volunteers.  
D. The student will be asked to pay a large fee.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Hello, Future Volunteer 段 "July 18 – August 15, 2027 (four weeks)"  
【解析】原文明确标出起止日期及共四周。A、B、D 均与数据不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】The Four Service Groups 表格第 2 行 "Little Teachers — Simple English games with kids aged 6–9"  
【解析】"Little Teachers" 组的活动是用英语游戏陪 6–9 岁小朋友，与李明的兴趣完全对应。A、C、D 均与活动内容不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How to Sign Up 第 4 点 "Deadline: July 5, 2027, 23:59"  
【解析】原文明确截止日期为 7 月 5 日。B 项为开营会日期，C 项为服务起始日，D 项为结营日，均属张冠李戴。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules for Volunteers 第 3 条 "No phone games during service time"  
【解析】原文明确禁止服务时间玩手机游戏。A、B、D 均为规则允许甚至鼓励的行为。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】Rules for Volunteers 末句 "At least 16 attendances ... are needed to earn the official certificate"  
【解析】由"至少 16 次出勤才能获证"推出 12 次未达线，无法获得证书。A 项与规则矛盾；C 项前提本就不同；D 项无中生有。故选 B。
