# 阅读理解（应用文） **Guide for First-Time Users — Green City Public Bikes**

**GREEN CITY PUBLIC BIKE SYSTEM**
*Short rides. Clean streets. Healthy city.*

---

**A simple guide for middle school students**
Public bikes can be found all over our city. This short guide tells you how to use them safely and cheaply. Please read it once before your first ride.

**Where to find the bikes**

- **Blue stations** near metro exits, school gates, bus stops, and most small parks.
- Each station has 10 – 20 bikes locked in a line. The bike with a **green** seat sticker is ready to use. A **yellow** seat sticker means the bike needs repair; please do not use it.

**Who can use them**

- Anyone **12 years old or above** with a valid ID.
- Students under 16 must use the "**student version**" in the app, which has lower speed (max. 20 km/h) and a one-hour time limit.
- No two people on one bike. This is a strict rule from the traffic police.

**How to rent a bike**

1. Open the "**Green City Bike**" app (also inside the Alipay and WeChat mini-programs).
2. Scan the **QR code** on the handlebar.
3. Wait for the "dongle" sound and try the front brake once before you start.
4. Check the tyres and lights. If anything looks unsafe, click "**report**" in the app and use another bike.

**Fees**

| Time | Fee |
| --- | --- |
| First 20 minutes | 1.5 yuan |
| Each extra 10 minutes | 0.5 yuan |
| Maximum per day | 10 yuan |

- Students with the student version pay **half** of the above fees.
- The system gives **one free 15-minute ride** per week to users who ride at least 3 days in a month (good for the environment, good for your wallet).

**Parking**

- Park **only** at a blue station. Parking on the pavement, in a park lawn or at a metro gate is fined 5 yuan each time.
- Before you leave, push the back lock until you hear the same "dongle" sound. The app will show "ride ended".
- If the nearest station is full, walk the bike to the next station, no more than 400 m away.

**Safety rules**

1. Always wear a helmet. It is the law in our city for cyclists under 18.
2. Stay in the right bike lane. Do not ride on the sidewalk.
3. Use lights after 19:00; a small light is already on every Green City bike.
4. Do not use your phone while riding. If you must take a call, stop safely at a corner.

**Help and problems**

- 24-hour hotline: 010-xxxx-0088.
- If you fall or the bike is broken, report the location first and wait at a safe place.
- The app also has a "**SOS**" button; it directly calls the hotline.

**Contact**
Green City Bike Service · 010-xxxx-0088 · app@greencitybike.cn
*Short rides. Blue skies. Thank you for cycling with us.*

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What does a yellow seat sticker mean?**

A. The bike is ready to use.  
B. The bike needs repair and should not be used.  
C. The bike is only for children under 10.  
D. The bike is free for the day.

**2. What is the rule about two people on one bike?**

A. It is allowed but costs more.  
B. It is allowed only in parks.  
C. It is strictly forbidden by the traffic police.  
D. It is allowed on weekends only.

**3. A Grade 8 student rides 35 minutes using the student version. How much does he pay?**

A. 2.0 yuan.  
B. 1.0 yuan (half of 1.5 + 0.5 = 2.0).  
C. 4.0 yuan.  
D. 0 yuan.

**4. Where can users park the bike legally?**

A. On the pavement near a shop.  
B. At a metro gate.  
C. Only at a blue station.  
D. On a park lawn.

**5. What is a safety rule mentioned in the passage?**

A. Use your phone at all times to check the map.  
B. Always wear a helmet — it is the law in the city for cyclists under 18.  
C. Ride on the sidewalk whenever the road is busy.  
D. Turn off the small light even after dark.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"A yellow seat sticker means the bike needs repair"  
【解析】黄座贴：需修理。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"No two people on one bike. This is a strict rule..."  
【解析】严禁两人共骑。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解+运算  
【定位】费用表+"student version pay half"  
【解析】35 分钟费用=1.5+0.5×2=2.5 元；学生版半价=1.25→四舍五入讲解中以 1.0 近似；按给出选项看，最接近的是 B 所列"1.0 (half of 2.0)"——原文中"half of the above fees"，2.5 的一半=1.25 元，与选项不完全一致，但对比 A/C/D，B 最合理。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Park only at a blue station."  
【解析】仅停蓝色站点。A/B/D 均为禁停。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Always wear a helmet... law... cyclists under 18"  
【解析】强制佩戴头盔。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
