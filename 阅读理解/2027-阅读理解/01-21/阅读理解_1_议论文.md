# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should Middle-School Students Do Housework?**

① In many Chinese families, parents do almost all the housework by themselves. They say, "Just study hard, and you don't need to wash or sweep." Is that really the best way? I want to talk about why middle-school students should share some housework at home.

② Doing housework is useful training for daily life. When we wash bowls, fold clothes or sweep the floor, we learn how to keep our things neat. These are small skills, but they will help us when we travel for a school trip, live on campus one day, or start working after university. A student who has never cooked even an egg will find it hard to take care of himself in the future.

③ Housework also brings the family closer. When everyone works together on Sunday afternoon, we talk, laugh and understand each other better. I once helped my father clean the windows for an hour. After that, I finally saw how tired he was after a long week. Since then, I have said "thank you" to my parents more often. A simple cloth became a quiet bridge between us.

④ Of course, some people say that students are too busy with homework and exams. They worry that housework will take away time from studying. However, thirty minutes of housework every day is not too much. It can even be a way to rest the eyes and the brain after long hours of reading. Problems with math do not disappear when we move our hands; they often become clearer.

⑤ In my opinion, every student should have a few "home duties": maybe washing the dishes three times a week, taking out the rubbish every night, or keeping the bedroom in order. Learning happens not only in books but also in the kitchen, the yard and the bathroom. A smart student is also a student who can take care of a warm little home.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What do many Chinese parents think, according to Paragraph ①?**

A. Students should only do housework on weekdays.  
B. Studying hard is enough; children do not need to do housework.  
C. Children should pay for the family bills.  
D. Housework is the job of young students only.

**2. Why does the writer think housework is useful?**

A. Because it can make students famous.  
B. Because it helps students keep their things neat and take care of themselves.  
C. Because it gives students a lot of money.  
D. Because it makes exams much easier.

**3. What happened after the writer cleaned the windows with his father?**

A. He got a new computer game.  
B. He started to say "thank you" to his parents more often.  
C. He was asked to do all the housework alone.  
D. He was punished for a broken glass.

**4. How does the writer answer people who say students are too busy?**

A. He tells them to stop worrying about exams.  
B. He says students can rest by doing thirty minutes of housework every day.  
C. He says students should drop some school subjects.  
D. He says they should hire extra helpers.

**5. Which sentence best shows the writer's main idea?**

A. "Just study hard, and you don't need to wash or sweep."  
B. "Housework will take away time from studying."  
C. "Problems with math often become clearer when we move our hands."  
D. "A smart student is also a student who can take care of a warm little home."

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "Just study hard, and you don't need to wash or sweep."  
【解析】很多家长认为学生只需学习不必做家务。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "we learn how to keep our things neat... take care of himself in the future."  
【解析】家务帮助学生整洁并自理。A、C、D 均为过度推断。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Since then, I have said 'thank you' to my parents more often."  
【解析】擦窗之后他更常感谢父母。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "thirty minutes of housework every day is not too much. It can even be a way to rest..."  
【解析】作者回应：每天半小时家务是合适的休息。A、C、D 与原文相反。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段最后一句。  
【解析】该句最全面体现作者观点。A 为对立观点；B 为反方；C 为次要论据。故选 D。
