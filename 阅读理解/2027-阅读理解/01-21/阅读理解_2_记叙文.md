# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Blue Wallet in the Snow**

① On my way home from school last Friday, I saw something dark blue lying on the white snow near the bus stop. At first, I thought it was an empty plastic bag. But when I looked closer, I realized it was a small wallet. A few metres away, the bus had just left. Whoever dropped the wallet was gone.

② I picked it up and opened it carefully. Inside, there were a student ID card, a 100-yuan note, a few coins, and a folded photo of an old couple. The ID card had the name "Lin Tao" and the photo of a boy about my age. His school was Number 5 Middle School, across the river. It was getting dark, and my fingers were already cold from standing still.

③ My first thought was to take the wallet home and tell my parents. But then I remembered the worried face of the boy on the card. I imagined him searching the bus stop again and again. I decided to wait for ten more minutes in case he came back. During these ten minutes, three buses stopped, but Lin Tao was not there.

④ So I ran to the small police station near the park. I knew the policeman on duty — he often visited our school to give safety lessons. Officer Wang smiled at me and wrote down every detail. He said he would try to reach Lin Tao's school that same evening. Before I left, he gave me a small thank-you card for "Honest Helpers."

⑤ On Saturday morning, our doorbell rang. A boy of my age stood outside with his mother and a small box of fresh oranges. It was Lin Tao. He had bought this month's English book and his train ticket with the money in that wallet. We became friends on the spot. I learned a simple but important thing: doing the right thing may not need a big plan, only a small, brave decision.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What did the writer first think when he saw the blue thing in the snow?**

A. He thought it was a gift box.  
B. He thought it was an empty plastic bag.  
C. He thought it was a lost umbrella.  
D. He thought it was a dead bird.

**2. What was inside the wallet?**

A. A mobile phone and some keys.  
B. A student ID card, money and a photo.  
C. A letter from Lin Tao's teacher.  
D. A small picture book for children.

**3. Why did the writer decide to wait for ten more minutes?**

A. His parents asked him to do so on the phone.  
B. He hoped that Lin Tao might come back.  
C. He wanted to buy something at the bus stop.  
D. He was too tired to walk home.

**4. What did Officer Wang do after the writer told him everything?**

A. He laughed at the writer for being careful.  
B. He promised to try to reach Lin Tao's school the same evening.  
C. He sent the writer back home alone in the snow.  
D. He said the wallet was too small to be important.

**5. What is the main lesson the writer learned from this story?**

A. It is better not to help strangers.  
B. Small and brave choices can lead to right actions.  
C. Losing things in winter is always dangerous.  
D. Policemen should go to every school every day.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "At first, I thought it was an empty plastic bag."  
【解析】作者起初以为是塑料袋。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "a student ID card, a 100-yuan note, a few coins, and a folded photo..."  
【解析】钱包内有学生证、钱和照片。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "I decided to wait for ten more minutes in case he came back."  
【解析】作者想等 Lin Tao 回来找钱包。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "He said he would try to reach Lin Tao's school that same evening."  
【解析】王警官答应当晚联系学校。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段 "doing the right thing may not need a big plan, only a small, brave decision."  
【解析】作者感悟：做对的事只需一个小小的勇敢决定。A 与整篇相反；C 片面；D 过度推断。故选 B。
