# 阅读理解（应用文） **School Chorus "Sunlight Notes" — Spring Try-outs**

① Hello, singing friends! Our school chorus **"Sunlight Notes"** is welcoming **new members for the 2027 – 2028 school year**. Whether you have sung on a stage before or only in your shower, come and try — music is a language everyone already speaks.

② **Basic Information**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Name | "Sunlight Notes" School Chorus |
| New members | About **25 students** from Grade 7 and Grade 8 |
| Try-out day | **Saturday, 11 April 2027**, 9:00 – 12:00 |
| Place | Music Hall, Floor 2, Building C |
| Teachers | Ms. Deng (conductor) & Mr. Shao (piano) |

③ **Who Can Apply?**

- Any student of **Grade 7** or **Grade 8**.  
- A healthy voice and throat on the try-out day.  
- Able to take part in a **weekly Friday rehearsal** (16:20 – 17:40) during the term.  
- Ability to read simple music notes is a **bonus**; not a must.  
- No age limit on courage; shy students are warmly welcomed.

④ **Try-out Steps**

1. **Short self-introduction** (30 seconds, in Chinese or English).  
2. Sing **one short song of your choice**, about **one minute** long, **without background music**. Any style — folk, pop, classical, school song.  
3. Repeat **three short tones** after Mr. Shao's piano, to check your ear for pitch.  
4. Sing one line of a song you have **never seen** before, shown on paper by Ms. Deng. (Don't worry — they will help you!)

⑤ **Important Dates**

| Date | Step |
| --- | --- |
| 30 March (Mon) | Sign-up starts on the school music club website. |
| 8 April (Thu) | Sign-up closes at 18:00. |
| 9 April (Fri) | Try-out time slot messaged to every applicant. |
| 11 April (Sat) | Try-outs in Music Hall. |
| 14 April (Tue) | Final name list on the **blue notice board** outside Music Hall. |
| 17 April (Fri) | First rehearsal; meet your singing group. |

⑥ **Dress Code for Try-outs**

- School uniform or any **tidy, plain clothes**.  
- Please **avoid** very shiny, very sparkling, or very loose costumes.  
- Comfortable shoes; no very high heels.  
- Long hair should be tied back neatly.

⑦ **Good Things You Will Get**

- A small golden badge with a music note.  
- A share of the chorus T-shirt, free, for every new member.  
- Chance to sing on the stage of the **Spring City Youth Music Night** (12 May 2027).  
- Weekly warm-up time, with good-hearted classmates.  
- A yearly concert in the school hall every December, filmed for your family to watch at home.

⑧ **Simple Rules**

1. Be gentle to your own voice: avoid iced drinks before the try-out.  
2. Try-out time: please arrive **15 minutes early**; late applicants wait to the end of the list.  
3. Parents may wait in the corridor, but should stay **outside** the music hall.  
4. **Phones on silent** during other students' singing — respect every performer.  
5. If you feel unwell, tell the teacher at once; a second chance can be given.

⑨ **Contact**  
- Music Office, Ms. Deng — chorus@bfms.cn  
- Chorus captain, Li Hang, Class 8-3 — hang.li@bfms.cn

**Let's paint a brighter sky with twenty-five new voices — we are waiting for you!**

**1. How many new students does the chorus want to choose?**

B. Comfortable flat shoes.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "About 25 students from Grade 7 and Grade 8"  
【解析】约 25 名。A、C、D 不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】步骤理解题  
【定位】第④段 1 – 2 步  
【解析】自我介绍 + 一分钟无伴奏歌曲。A、C、D 与规则不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】日期定位题  
【定位】第⑤段 8 April — "Sign-up closes at 18:00"  
【解析】8 号为报名截止。A、C、D 非截止日。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节否定题  
【定位】第⑥段 "avoid very shiny, very sparkling, or very loose costumes"  
【解析】避免穿过于浮华服装。A、B、D 合规。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑦段 "Spring City Youth Music Night (12 May 2027)"  
【解析】5 月 12 日舞台演出。A、B、D 无依据。故选 C。
