# 阅读理解（应用文） **Riverside Park — Autumn Leaves Charity Walk "Every Step Counts" 2027**

---

> **Riverside Park + Children's Hope Hospital**  
> **Autumn Leaves Charity Walk 2027 — "Every Step Counts"**  
> **Sunday, 26 October 2027 · 8:30 – 12:00**

Walk a few kilometres through golden autumn leaves, and turn **each step you take** into help for a child at Children's Hope Hospital. Everyone — families, schools, office teams — is warmly invited.

---

## 🍂 Two Routes

| Route | Distance | Best for | Start time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Family Route** | **3 km** (around the lake) | Children aged 5+ and their parents | **9:00** |
| **Classic Route** | **7 km** (lake + maple forest trail) | Students aged 10+ and adults | **9:30** |

Both routes finish at the **Sunrise Plaza** (north gate of the park), where music, tea, hot corn and a small handicraft market are waiting.

---

## 💰 How the Walk Raises Money

1. **Registration fee**: 40 yuan (adult), 20 yuan (student). All fees go to the hospital.  
2. **Each step counts sponsor**: before the walk, you ask friends or family to **sponsor** your walk at **0.05 yuan per step** (or any amount they like).  
3. At the finish line, a small sensor on your shoe number reads the total steps; the hospital will post the grand total on **Monday 27 Oct** on its website.

> **Target for 2027:** **one million yuan** for 80 new sets of child-sized medical beds.

---

## 📝 How to Join

- **Online**: open the WeChat mini-app "Riverside Park", tap "Autumn Walk 2027", fill in name, age, ID number, emergency contact.
- **In person**: at the park's **Visitor Centre**, **Mon – Fri, 10:00 – 17:00**.
- **Closing date**: **Friday 24 October, 17:00**. No on-the-day registration this year.
- **Free shoe-number and T-shirt** given out at registration. Please collect them before the walk day at the Visitor Centre.

---

## 👟 Before the Walk

- Wear **comfortable shoes**, **warm but light clothes**, and bring a water bottle.
- Children under 10 must walk with an adult on the Family Route; parents are responsible for their own children throughout.
- The park will open **free bag storage** at the main gate from 7:30.

---

## 🚑 Safety & Support

- Nine medical teams will be placed along the two routes; each team has a nurse and a doctor from the hospital.
- If you need help during the walk, find the **bright yellow "Help" flags** every 500 metres.
- The walk will continue in light rain. If there is a **storm warning**, the event moves to **Sunday 2 November**, and we will send WeChat messages before 18:00 on 25 October.

---

## 🎁 Thank-You Gifts

All finishers receive:
- A hand-pressed maple-leaf bookmark made by hospital children.
- A digital certificate with your step count and the yuan you raised.
- First 500 finishers: a small paper bag of roasted autumn chestnuts (100 g).

---

*Every step you take matches a small step we hope a sick child will take. See you in the yellow leaves.*

*Riverside Park Office + Children's Hope Hospital Charity Team*  
*Posted 12 October 2027*

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main purpose of the Autumn Charity Walk?**

A. To sell chestnuts to tourists.  
B. To let walkers enjoy the lake and compete for gold medals.  
C. To raise money for Children's Hope Hospital, especially to buy 80 new sets of child-sized medical beds.  
D. To open a new park restaurant.

**2. Which of the following is **NOT** true about the two routes?**

A. The Family Route is 3 km and starts at 9:00.  
B. The Classic Route is 7 km and starts at 9:30.  
C. Both routes finish at Sunrise Plaza.  
D. Children under 5 can join the Classic Route alone.

**3. How should a family register for the walk?**

A. Come to the park on the morning of 26 October and sign up on paper.  
B. Use the WeChat mini-app "Riverside Park" or visit the Visitor Centre on weekdays between 10:00 and 17:00, by Friday 24 October 17:00.  
C. Phone the hospital at midnight.  
D. Write a letter to the park office.

**4. What happens if there is a storm warning on Sunday 26 October?**

A. The walk is cancelled forever.  
B. The walk moves to Sunday 2 November, and WeChat messages are sent before 18:00 on 25 October.  
C. The walk is held indoors.  
D. Only children walk.

**5. What do **all** finishers receive at the end?**

A. A hand-pressed maple-leaf bookmark made by hospital children, and a digital certificate with step count.  
B. 200 yuan in cash.  
C. A big golden trophy.  
D. Free medical care for life.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How the Walk Raises Money + Target "one million yuan for 80 new sets of child-sized medical beds"  
【解析】活动为医院募款买儿童专用病床。A、B、D 均非主要目的。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Before the Walk "Children under 10 must walk with an adult on the Family Route"  
【解析】10 岁以下需成人陪同走家庭路线，不能独自走 Classic。A、B、C 均与原文一致。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How to Join "Online ... WeChat mini-app ... In person ... Visitor Centre, Mon – Fri, 10:00 – 17:00 ... Closing date: Friday 24 October, 17:00. No on-the-day registration."  
【解析】两种方式 + 截止时间明确。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Safety & Support "If there is a storm warning, the event moves to Sunday 2 November, and we will send WeChat messages before 18:00 on 25 October."  
【解析】遇风暴改期 11 月 2 日。A、C、D 均与原文矛盾。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Thank-You Gifts "All finishers receive: A hand-pressed maple-leaf bookmark ... A digital certificate with your step count"  
【解析】全体奖品即两项。B、C、D 均无依据。故选 A。
