# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Wrong Backpack at the Station**

① It was the end of the winter holiday. My mother and I were coming home from my aunt's city on a crowded high-speed train. When we reached **Green River Station** at nine p.m., the platform was full of tired travellers, all pulling suitcases, all in a hurry. I was half-asleep. I picked up my backpack from the rack above my seat and followed Mum to the taxi area outside the station.

② Only at home, when I tried to take out my Chinese homework, I realized something was wrong. The black backpack looked the same as mine, but inside were three thick business books, a pair of grown-up glasses and a small notebook with tiny Chinese notes. My own backpack — with my half-finished homework, my pen case, and my little teddy keychain — was gone.

③ "Someone picked up the wrong one, and so did you," Mum said, looking serious. "We need to find that person." I flipped the notebook and saw a phone number written in pencil on the first page, beside the name **Uncle Zhang**. I held my breath and dialled. A voice on the other side laughed with surprise. "Ha! I have been looking for my books! And I am holding a very small brown teddy bear keychain. Does that belong to you?"

④ Uncle Zhang, it turned out, lived in the same city as us, only ten minutes by bike. We agreed to meet at a small café near the station the next afternoon. I was a little shy to meet a stranger, but Mum said, "Taking care of other people's things is part of being an honest person." So I cleaned the bag carefully, even put Uncle Zhang's glasses case back straight, and carried it to the café.

⑤ Uncle Zhang was a thin man in a grey coat, with kind eyes. He had my bag on the table already. We exchanged bags, laughed about the mix-up, and he bought me a small cup of hot chocolate. When I looked into my own bag, everything was exactly where I had put it — he had not even opened the pen case. "I know how a teenager feels when her homework is missing the night before school," he said, smiling. "I was once like you."

⑥ On the way home, I carried my own backpack again. It felt lighter than before, maybe because my heart was full. I thought about the small dots of kindness in the world: a stranger on a busy platform, a careful hand that did not open my bag, a cup of hot chocolate on a cold afternoon. The same bag, the same shape, the same colour — but one small note with one phone number had turned a mistake into a new friendship.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the writer take the wrong backpack at the station?**

A. He was carrying too many bags.  
B. He was half-asleep and the black backpack looked the same as his.  
C. Uncle Zhang pushed him on purpose.  
D. His mother told him to.

**2. What did the writer find inside the wrong backpack?**

A. A small brown teddy keychain.  
B. His own Chinese homework.  
C. Thick business books, glasses and a notebook.  
D. A new pair of sports shoes.

**3. How did the writer find Uncle Zhang's phone number?**

A. His mother called the train company.  
B. He saw it written in pencil on the first page of the notebook.  
C. Uncle Zhang phoned him first.  
D. He searched Uncle Zhang's name online.

**4. What did the writer and Mum think taking care of other people's things was?**

A. A waste of time.  
B. Only the police's job.  
C. Part of being an honest person.  
D. Something only adults could do.

**5. What is the main feeling the writer wants to share at the end?**

A. Anger at himself for mixing up the bags.  
B. Fear of high-speed trains.  
C. Gratitude for the small dots of kindness around us.  
D. Sadness that Uncle Zhang was too old.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段末 "I was half-asleep... picked up my backpack"  
【解析】作者昏昏欲睡，黑色背包相似而误拿。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "three thick business books, a pair of grown-up glasses and a small notebook"  
【解析】包里有厚书、眼镜、笔记本。A 在自家包中；B、D 无依据。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "I flipped the notebook and saw a phone number written in pencil on the first page"  
【解析】在笔记本首页铅笔写的电话号码。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Taking care of other people's things is part of being an honest person"  
【解析】妈妈把这视为诚实的一部分。A、B、D 不符。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意/情感题  
【定位】第⑥段 "the small dots of kindness in the world"  
【解析】作者最后感念身边的善意。A、B、D 与情感相反。故选 C。
