# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Future Engineers" — Summer Science Camp**

**CITY YOUTH SCIENCE CENTRE**
*Touch. Build. Discover.*

---

**Summer Camp Brochure — July 2027**

The City Youth Science Centre is happy to invite students in Grades 7 and 8 to our ten-day summer camp, **"Future Engineers"**. This camp is not a classroom. It is a hands-on journey where you will build, break, test, and rebuild your ideas.

**Dates, location and fee**

- **Dates:** 12 July – 21 July 2027 (Monday to Thursday, every week)
- **Hours:** 09:00 – 16:00 each day
- **Place:** City Youth Science Centre, No. 88 Park Road (near Metro Line 3, Exit C)
- **Fee:** ¥380 per person for the whole camp (lunch and materials included)
- **Discount:** Students who live with a grandparent aged 70 or over — ¥280.

**Daily schedule (sample)**

| Time | Activity |
| --- | --- |
| 09:00 – 09:30 | Morning meeting, warm-up puzzle |
| 09:30 – 11:30 | Workshop 1: building (robots, bridges, wind-mills, water pumps...) |
| 11:30 – 12:30 | Lunch in the centre's cafeteria |
| 12:30 – 13:00 | Short reading circle (easy science stories) |
| 13:00 – 15:30 | Workshop 2: testing and improving |
| 15:30 – 16:00 | Closing talk, tidy-up, sign-out |

**What you will do (by day)**

- **Day 1–2:** Build a balloon-powered car; measure its distance and improve the design.
- **Day 3–4:** Make a simple water-**filter** from sand, stones and cotton; test it on muddy water.
- **Day 5–6:** Program a small robot to follow a black line on the floor.
- **Day 7–8:** Design a bridge from sticks and rubber bands that can carry 2 kg of books.
- **Day 9:** Team challenge — a surprise task kept secret until the morning.
- **Day 10:** Mini-exhibition — parents visit and students explain their best work.

**What the centre provides**

- All tools and materials: sticks, sand, batteries, small motors, wires, and more.
- Two safety engineers in every workshop.
- Cold water and fruit during breaks.
- A blue "Future Engineers" T-shirt (kept as a gift).

**What you should bring**

- A pen and a plain notebook for design drawings.
- A hat and sunscreen (some testing is done outdoors).
- Comfortable closed shoes — no sandals, no slippers.
- A reusable water bottle.

**Important rules**

1. Students under 13 must be signed in and out by a parent each day.
2. No phones, tablets or smart watches inside the workshops; they can be placed in a locker at the entrance.
3. Do not take motors, batteries, or cutters home; return all tools to the box before leaving.
4. Students who miss more than **three days** without a reason cannot receive the final certificate.
5. No running inside the centre; no food inside the workshops.

**How to sign up**

- Online: visit *www.youthsci.cn/engineers*
- In person: room 204, City Youth Science Centre, Monday to Friday, 14:00 – 17:00
- **Deadline:** Wednesday, 7 July 2027, 17:00
- Only **48** spaces are available — first come, first served!

*Question? Call 021-xxxx-1122 (Ms. Chen) · Email: camp@youthsci.cn*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Students who live with a grandparent aged 70 or over — ¥280"  
【解析】优惠价 280 元。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Day 1–2: Build a balloon-powered car"  
【解析】第 1、2 天做气球动力车。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】规则 2 "No phones, tablets..."  
【解析】中心不提供手机，并且禁入。A/B/C 均提供。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"miss more than three days... cannot receive the final certificate"  
【解析】超过 3 天无故缺席拿不到证书。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Deadline: Wednesday, 7 July 2027, 17:00"  
【解析】7 月 7 日 17:00。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
