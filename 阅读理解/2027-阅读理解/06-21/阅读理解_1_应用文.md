# 阅读理解（应用文） **School Sports Day 2027 — Student Handbook**

**SPRING HILL MIDDLE SCHOOL**
*Sports Day — Saturday 25 September 2027*

---

**To all students,**
Sports Day is one week away. Please read this short handbook carefully so everyone has a safe and happy day. You can also ask your class captain or Mr. Huang from the PE office if anything is unclear.

**When and where**

- **Date:** Saturday 25 September 2027.
- **Time:** 07:30 – 16:30.
- **Place:** school main playground (east gate opens from 07:00; west gate is closed).
- **Bad weather plan:** if it rains heavily all morning, the whole day will be moved to Saturday 2 October.

**Morning schedule**

| Time | Event |
| --- | --- |
| 07:30 – 07:50 | Class lines, morning call |
| 08:00 – 08:25 | Opening ceremony (march, flag, headmaster's short talk) |
| 08:30 – 11:30 | Track events: 100 m, 200 m, 400 m, 800 m, 4 × 100 m relay |
| 11:30 – 12:30 | Lunch in the small hall (class by class) |

**Afternoon schedule**

| Time | Event |
| --- | --- |
| 13:00 – 15:30 | Field events: high jump, long jump, shot put, class tug-of-war |
| 15:30 – 16:10 | Class cheer show (songs, short dances) |
| 16:10 – 16:30 | Closing ceremony (prizes, group photo) |

**Rules for taking part**

1. Every student must sign up by **Friday 12 September, 16:00**.
2. Each student can join at most **two track events and one field event**.
3. Students with a recent injury (in the last 30 days) must show a doctor's note.
4. Class tug-of-war is one team of 12 students per class; first-come, first-chosen.

**Clothes and equipment**

- Wear school sports clothes and clean running shoes. Slippers are not allowed.
- Bring a small hat, a water bottle and one light snack (fruit or a biscuit).
- Do not bring expensive watches or jewellery. The school cannot look after lost items on the field.

**Behaviour rules**

- **Sit with your class.** Do not walk onto the track during races.
- **Cheer warmly, do not tease.** Saying rude things about any student or class is forbidden and may lead to loss of class points.
- Keep your own seating area clean. Put all rubbish in the bins at the east gate corner.
- Finish your lunch in the small hall; do not bring hot soup to the playground.

**First aid and safety**

- A first-aid tent is near the big tree at the south corner. Two school doctors will be there all day.
- If you feel dizzy or very tired, tell your class teacher at once and walk slowly to the tent.
- Students must **not** give any medicine to other students.

**Parents' visit**

- Parents are welcome to watch from the stand on the north side, from 07:30 to 16:30.
- Parents should not enter the track area.
- Photography is allowed, but please do not use flash during the opening ceremony indoor photo.

**Contact**
Mr. Huang, PE office (Room 108) · 021-xxxx-7712 · sports@springhill.cn
*Run, jump, cheer — but most of all, stay safe.*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Bad weather plan..."  
【解析】改至 10 月 2 日。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"at most two track events and one field event"  
【解析】2 项径赛+1 项田赛。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"must show a doctor's note"  
【解析】须医生证明。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】行为规则  
【解析】禁止踏入跑道、禁止说粗话。A/B/D 均允许/要求。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Parents are welcome to watch from the stand on the north side"  
【解析】北侧看台。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
