# 阅读理解（应用文） **Weekend Volunteer Day at Sunshine City Library**

---

**Sunshine City Library — Student Volunteer Call**

Do you love books? Do you want to **help your city and earn service hours**? Our library is looking for **30 middle school student volunteers** for this weekend!

---

**1. Basic Information**

- **Date:** Saturday, 10 April 2027  
- **Time:** 9:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m. (with a 1-hour lunch break)  
- **Place:** Sunshine City Library, 88 Green River Road  
- **Who can join:** Students aged **12–15**, Grade 7 to Grade 9

---

**2. What You Will Do**

| Station | Main Task | Volunteers Needed |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| A. Book Shelving | Put returned books back in the right places | 10 |
| B. Kids' Corner | Read picture stories to young readers (ages 4–7) | 8 |
| C. English Club | Help foreign friends find Chinese learning books | 6 |
| D. Garden Area | Keep the outdoor reading garden **tidy and green** | 6 |

*You may choose your **first** and **second** favourite stations when you sign up.*

---

**3. What You Will Get**

- A **6-hour Volunteer Certificate** (useful for senior high school applications)  
- A free lunch box (vegetarian option available)  
- A small **library badge** and a reading bookmark  
- The right to borrow **10 books** (instead of the usual 5) for the next month

---

**4. How to Sign Up**

1. Scan the QR code on the library's front page.  
2. Fill in the online form before **Thursday, 8 April, 8:00 p.m.**  
3. Wait for an SMS from our team (by Friday noon).  
4. Come to the library by 8:45 a.m. on Saturday with your **student ID card** and a water bottle.

---

**5. Kind Reminders**

- **No bright heels or loose skirts.** Wear comfortable shoes and sports clothes.  
- **Do not bring pets.**  
- If you cannot come, please **tell us 24 hours earlier**, so others can take your place.  
- For any questions, email **volunteer@sunshinelib.org** or call **(028) 6677-5588**.

> *"Reading a book is like making a quiet friend. Sharing one is like making that friend brighter." — Sunshine City Library*

---

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Who can take part in the volunteer day?**

A. All city citizens of any age.  
B. Middle school students aged 12–15.  
C. Only Grade 9 students.  
D. Teachers and parents.

**2. Which station is the BEST choice for a student who enjoys gardening?**

A. Station A.  
B. Station B.  
C. Station C.  
D. Station D.

**3. What special reading benefit can a volunteer get?**

A. Free e-books for life.  
B. Borrowing up to 10 books for the next month.  
C. A prize of 100 yuan.  
D. Free writing lessons.

**4. By when must a student sign up for the event?**

A. Friday at noon.  
B. Saturday at 8:45 a.m.  
C. Thursday at 8:00 p.m.  
D. Sunday at 4:00 p.m.

**5. Which of the following is NOT allowed at the event?**

A. Bringing a water bottle.  
B. Wearing sports shoes.  
C. Bringing a pet dog.  
D. Eating a free lunch box.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第1节 "Students aged 12–15, Grade 7 to Grade 9"  
【解析】年龄12–15岁的初中生可参加。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第2节 "D. Garden Area... Keep the outdoor reading garden tidy and green"  
【解析】花园爱好者应选 D 站。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第3节 "borrow 10 books (instead of the usual 5) for the next month"  
【解析】可多借书至10本。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第4节 "before Thursday, 8 April, 8:00 p.m."  
【解析】报名截止周四晚8点。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第5节 "Do not bring pets."  
【解析】明令禁止带宠物。故选 C。
