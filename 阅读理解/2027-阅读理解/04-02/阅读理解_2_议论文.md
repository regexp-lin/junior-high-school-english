# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should Students Use AI Tools to Help with Their Homework?**

① In recent months, many middle school students have started to use AI chat tools to help them study. With a few typed words, an AI app can explain a maths problem, correct an English sentence, or give ideas for a writing topic. **Should we welcome AI into our homework life, or keep it out of the door?** Different people have very different answers.

② Some students and parents say **yes, use it wisely.** AI can be like a patient tutor that never gets tired. When a student is stuck on a physics question at 10 p.m. and no teacher is online, the AI can show a step-by-step explanation. For students in small towns, where good tutors are hard to find, AI brings fair learning chances. A Grade 8 boy in Yunnan, for example, told reporters that the AI "made geometry suddenly make sense" for him.

③ Others warn that AI can **hurt true learning** if students are careless. If a student simply copies an AI answer into the homework book, his brain does not really work. Over time, his thinking muscles get weaker, like arms that never lift anything heavy. Some teachers also say AI sometimes gives **confident but wrong** answers, especially in history and geography. If students believe everything AI types out, they may learn wrong facts without knowing it.

④ There is also the fairness question. Not every family can pay for high-level AI services. If some classmates use a paid AI to polish every essay, while others work alone with only a dictionary, test results may no longer show the true gap between students' real abilities. Some schools are now thinking about **banning AI tools during homework**, while other schools welcome AI as a new classmate.

⑤ Most teachers believe the answer is **not "yes" or "no", but "how".** AI is a tool, like a calculator; the key is when and how to use it. A good rule might be: first try the question yourself for ten minutes. If you are still stuck, ask AI for **hints**, not for the final answer. After you get the answer, **write out the reasoning in your own words** to make sure you truly understand.

⑥ In the end, homework is not only about writing down the right number; it is about **training a young mind to think**. AI should be the coach on the side of the track, not the runner carrying our legs. If we remember that, we can enjoy the help of AI without losing the strong, careful brain that we are here to build.

【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段 "Should we welcome AI into our homework life, or keep it out of the door?"  
【解析】全文讨论 AI 辅助作业利弊。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "like a patient tutor that never gets tired... small towns"  
【解析】AI 像不知疲倦的家教，特别对小镇学生友好。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "confident but wrong answers"  
【解析】AI 有时答案错误却自信。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "first try... ask AI for hints... write out the reasoning in your own words"  
【解析】先自己做，再用 AI 提示，并亲自复述推理。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段 "coach on the side of the track, not the runner"
