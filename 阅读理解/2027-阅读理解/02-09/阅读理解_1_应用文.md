# 阅读理解（应用文） **Summer English Drama Camp — Invitation**

① Hello, young actors of Riverbank Middle School! Do you enjoy watching English films, reading short plays, or telling stories to your friends? The English Department is happy to invite you to the **5th Summer English Drama Camp**, where students will learn, laugh and shine on the stage.

② **Camp Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Dates | July 12 – July 26, 2027 (two weeks) |
| Daily Hours | 8:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. (Monday – Friday) |
| Place | Room 305 and School Small Hall |
| Fees | ¥500 for materials, costumes and daily lunch |
| Teachers | Ms. Green (UK), Ms. Wang and 6 student leaders |

③ **What We Will Do**

- **Week One – Warm-up & Short Plays:** Simple games, tongue twisters, and two short plays based on *Alice in Wonderland*.  
- **Week Two – One Big Show:** Write and perform a 40-minute play called *A Day in Our Town*. Every student will have at least one line on the stage.  
- **Evening Homework:** No writing homework. Only "listen to one English song before bed and dream of a new line."

④ **Who Can Join**

- Any Grade 7 or Grade 8 student of our school.  
- Students do **not** need to be the best in English. The camp welcomes shy voices too.  
- Each class can send **up to 6 students**. If more students apply, we will hold a friendly draw.

⑤ **How to Sign Up**

- Step 1: Ask your English teacher for a yellow **"Drama Camp Form."**  
- Step 2: Write your name, class, and a short English sentence about yourself. (Example: *"I like cats more than cakes."*)  
- Step 3: Return the form before **Friday, June 12**, to the English Office (Room 102).  
- Step 4: Parents should sign the back of the form. Students under 13 must also bring a simple health note.

⑥ **Final Show & Reminders**

The final show will be held on **Saturday, July 31** in the School Big Hall. Parents and friends are warmly invited; **free tickets** will be given to each family. Please wear comfortable clothes every day, and do **not** bring expensive watches or phones to class. For more help, please email **drama@riverbankms.edu**. See you on the stage, where every shy word becomes a big adventure!

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long will the Summer English Drama Camp last?**

A. One week.  
B. Two weeks.  
C. Three weeks.  
D. One month.

**2. Which of the following is INCLUDED in the ¥500 fee?**

A. A new schoolbag.  
B. A weekend trip to the seaside.  
C. Daily lunch.  
D. A pair of sports shoes.

**3. What will students do in Week Two of the camp?**

A. Play football with foreign coaches.  
B. Perform a 40-minute play called *A Day in Our Town*.  
C. Take two English exams.  
D. Visit a tea factory.

**4. Which of the following students CANNOT join the camp?**

A. A Grade 7 student whose English is just okay.  
B. A Grade 8 student who is quite shy.  
C. A Grade 9 student of our school.  
D. A Grade 7 student whose parents sign the form.

**5. When will the final show be held?**

A. Friday, June 12.  
B. Saturday, July 12.  
C. Saturday, July 31.  
D. Sunday, July 26.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "July 12 – July 26, 2027 (two weeks)"  
【解析】为期两周。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "¥500 for materials, costumes and daily lunch"  
【解析】费用含每日午餐。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Week Two – One Big Show... A Day in Our Town"  
【解析】第二周上演此剧。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Any Grade 7 or Grade 8 student"  
【解析】仅七、八年级可报名，9 年级不行。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段 "Saturday, July 31"  
【解析】汇演定于 7 月 31 日周六。故选 C。
