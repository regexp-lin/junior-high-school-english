> **01-02 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Winter Camp at Sunshine Youth Centre**

① Are you looking for something fun and exciting to do this winter holiday? Come and join our Winter Camp! We have activities for everyone aged 12–15.

---

| **Activity** | **Time** | **Place** | **Teacher** |
|---|---|---|---|
| English Drama | Mon & Wed 9:00–11:00 | Room 201 | Ms. Green |
| Science Experiments | Tue & Thu 9:00–11:00 | Science Lab | Mr. Zhang |
| Basketball Training | Mon to Fri 14:00–16:00 | Sports Hall | Coach Wang |
| Art & Drawing | Wed & Fri 9:00–11:00 | Art Room | Ms. Li |

---

② **Camp dates:** January 13 – January 24 (two weeks)

③ **Fee:** ¥800 for two activities; ¥1,100 for three or more activities.

④ **How to sign up:**
- Visit our website: www.sunshineyouth.com
- Call us at 010-5678-1234 (Mon–Fri, 9:00–17:00)
- Come to our front desk at 56 Heping Road

⑤ **Special offer:** Sign up before January 5 and get a **10% discount**! Groups of three or more friends get an extra free T-shirt each.

⑥ **Note:** Each student must choose at least two activities. Parents need to sign the permission form. Please bring your own lunch.

**注释：** sign up 报名；discount 折扣；permission form 同意书

---

**Questions:**

1. How old should students be to join the Winter Camp?
   A. 10–12.
   B. 12–15.
   C. 15–18.
   D. Any age.

2. If a student chooses English Drama and Art & Drawing, when will the two activities overlap?
   A. Monday.
   B. Tuesday.
   C. Wednesday.
   D. They never overlap.

3. How much will a student pay for three activities if he signs up on January 3?
   A. ¥800.
   B. ¥1,100.
   C. ¥990.
   D. ¥720.

4. Which of the following is TRUE according to the passage?
   A. Basketball Training is only on weekends.
   B. The camp provides lunch for students.
   C. Students must choose at least two activities.
   D. The camp lasts for three weeks.

5. What is the purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell a story about a winter holiday.
   B. To advertise a winter camp and attract students to sign up.
   C. To teach students how to play basketball.
   D. To explain why winter holidays are important.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "We have activities for everyone aged 12–15."

**【解析】** 原文明确说冬令营面向 12–15 岁的学生。A 项 10–12、C 项 15–18、D 项任何年龄均与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 表格中 English Drama 的时间为 "Mon & Wed 9:00–11:00"，Art & Drawing 的时间为 "Wed & Fri 9:00–11:00"。

**【解析】** 两项活动都在周三 9:00–11:00 进行，时间冲突。A 项周一只有 Drama；B 项周二是 Science；D 项说从不重叠不正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "¥1,100 for three or more activities" 和第⑤段 "Sign up before January 5 and get a 10% discount!"

**【解析】** 三项活动原价 ¥1,100，1月5日前报名享 10% 折扣，即 ¥1,100 × 90% = ¥990。A 项 ¥800 是两项的价格；B 项 ¥1,100 未计折扣；D 项 ¥720 计算有误。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Each student must choose at least two activities."

**【解析】** 原文说每个学生必须选择至少两项活动。A 项篮球周一至周五都有，非仅周末；B 项需自带午餐；D 项冬令营为两周而非三周。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文内容为冬令营的活动介绍、时间、费用和报名方式。

**【解析】** 文章目的是宣传冬令营并吸引学生报名参加。A 项讲故事、C 项教打篮球、D 项解释寒假重要性均不是本文目的。故选 B。
