# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Students Have Homework During Winter Holiday?**

① Every winter, the same question comes up: should students have homework during the holiday? Some people say yes. Others say no. Both sides have their reasons, and it is worth thinking about carefully.

② Those who support holiday homework believe it helps students review what they have learned. Without practice, students may forget important knowledge. A maths teacher, Mr. Liu, once said, "If my students don't practise during the holiday, they come back and can't even remember basic formulas." Homework also keeps students in a learning habit. If they do nothing for a whole month, it can be hard to get back into study mode when school starts again.

③ However, many students and parents disagree. They think holidays should be a time for rest and family. After a long and tiring term, students need a break. Too much homework takes away their free time and makes them unhappy. Some students even copy answers from the internet just to finish on time. This kind of homework does not help learning at all. Ms. Chen, a mother of two, said, "My children work so hard during the term. They deserve a real holiday."

④ There may be a middle way. Instead of heavy workbooks, teachers could give creative tasks. For example, students could read a book they like and write a short review. They could keep a diary in English. They could do a small science project at home. These tasks are more interesting and still help students learn. They also give students the freedom to manage their own time.

⑤ In the end, the goal is not to fill the holiday with boring work. It is to help students keep growing while also enjoying their time off. A little homework — the right kind — can do both.

**注释：** formula 公式；deserve 值得/应得；review 评论/回顾

**Questions:**

1. What is the main topic of this passage?
   A. How to do maths homework.
   B. Whether students should have homework during winter holiday.
   C. Why winter holidays are too long.
   D. How teachers prepare for the new term.

2. Why does Mr. Liu support holiday homework?
   A. Because he wants students to be tired.
   B. Because without practice, students may forget what they learned.
   C. Because parents ask him to give homework.
   D. Because he has nothing else to do.

3. What problem does Ms. Chen see with too much homework?
   A. Her children cannot find their textbooks.
   B. Her children work hard during the term and deserve a real holiday.
   C. Her children love doing homework.
   D. Her children's teachers are too strict.

4. What does the writer suggest as a "middle way"?
   A. Give no homework at all.
   B. Give creative and interesting tasks instead of heavy workbooks.
   C. Make the holiday shorter.
   D. Let parents do the homework for children.

5. What is the writer's attitude toward holiday homework?
   A. Completely against it.
   B. Completely in favour of it.
   C. Supportive of a balanced approach — a little homework of the right kind.
   D. The writer has no opinion.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段 "should students have homework during the holiday?"

**【解析】** 全文围绕"学生寒假是否该有作业"展开讨论。A 项如何做数学作业、C 项寒假太长、D 项老师如何准备新学期均非文章主题。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Those who support holiday homework believe it helps students review what they have learned. Without practice, students may forget important knowledge."

**【解析】** 刘老师支持假期作业是因为不练习学生会忘记所学知识。A 项想让学生疲惫、C 项家长要求、D 项无事可做均属无中生有。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "My children work so hard during the term. They deserve a real holiday."

**【解析】** 陈女士认为孩子学期里很努力，应该享有真正的假期。A 项找不到课本、C 项喜欢做作业、D 项老师太严厉均非她的观点。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Instead of heavy workbooks, teachers could give creative tasks."

**【解析】** 作者建议用创意任务代替沉重的练习册，如读书写评论、写英语日记、做科学小项目等。A 项完全不留作业、C 项缩短假期、D 项让家长代写均非作者建议。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "A little homework — the right kind — can do both."

**【解析】** 作者认为适量且合适的作业既能帮助学生成长又能让他们享受假期，态度是支持平衡的方式。A 项完全反对、B 项完全赞成均过于极端；D 项没有观点与全文论述矛盾。故选 C。
