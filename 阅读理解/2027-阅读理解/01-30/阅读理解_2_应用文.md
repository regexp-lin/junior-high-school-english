# 阅读理解（应用文） **Invitation — Monday Evening Reading Club**

*To all Grade 7 students,*

① I am writing to invite you to join our **Monday Evening Reading Club** at the school library. Mr. Zhao, our new English teacher, will lead each meeting. The club is open to all Grade 7 students who enjoy stories in simple English.

② **Meeting Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| When | Every Monday, 6:30 p.m. – 7:30 p.m. |
| Where | Room 203, School Library (West Building) |
| Start date | Monday, March 2 |
| End date | Monday, May 25 (13 meetings) |
| Fee | FREE |

③ **What We Will Do**

- **Read short stories together** from English readers for young learners.  
- **Discuss** the characters and choices in the story in small groups.  
- **Write** a short book report (about 60 words) once a month.  
- **Borrow** one "free pick" book from a special club shelf every week.

④ **Rules to Remember**

- Please **come on time**. If you miss three meetings in a row, you will be asked to leave the club.  
- Please **switch off your phone** during the meeting.  
- **Do not** eat food in the library. Water in a closed bottle is OK.  
- If you borrow a book, **return it in seven days**. Lost books need to be replaced.  
- **Respect** ideas that are different from yours. Calm discussion is welcome.

⑤ **How to Sign Up**

Please come to Room 203 during the **lunch break on Friday, February 13 or 20**. Bring your student card. The club will take at most **30 members** this term, first come, first served. After you sign up, you will receive a small reading passport to write your book titles and notes.

*I look forward to reading with you!*

*— Mr. Zhao*

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Who can join this Reading Club?**

A. Only Grade 9 students.  
B. All Grade 7 students who enjoy English stories.  
C. Only students from the West Building.  
D. Parents of all middle-school students.

**2. How long does one club meeting last?**

A. Twenty minutes.  
B. Half an hour.  
C. One hour.  
D. Two hours.

**3. What is one thing club members will NOT do?**

A. Read short stories from English readers.  
B. Write a short book report once a month.  
C. Eat food during the meeting.  
D. Borrow a book from the club shelf.

**4. What will happen if a student misses three meetings in a row?**

A. He will be fined 100 yuan.  
B. He will be asked to leave the club.  
C. He will be praised for resting.  
D. He will have to write 500 English words.

**5. How can a student sign up for the club?**

A. Send a long letter to the head teacher.  
B. Come to Room 203 during the lunch break on February 13 or 20 with a student card.  
C. Call the library every evening.  
D. Pay 50 yuan at the school gate.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "open to all Grade 7 students who enjoy stories in simple English."  
【解析】面向所有七年级英语爱好者。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "When: 6:30 p.m. – 7:30 p.m."  
【解析】每次 1 小时。A、B、D 均不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（排除型）  
【定位】第④段 "Do not eat food in the library."  
【解析】禁止在图书馆吃东西。A、B、D 均为club 活动。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "If you miss three meetings in a row, you will be asked to leave the club."  
【解析】连续三次缺席将被劝退。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Please come to Room 203 during the lunch break on Friday, February 13 or 20."  
【解析】需在指定时间前往 203 报名，持学生证。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。
