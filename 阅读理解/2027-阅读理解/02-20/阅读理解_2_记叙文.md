# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Grandpa's Old Workshop**

① Behind our family house stood a small, dusty room with a wooden door painted the colour of old tea. Everyone called it "Grandpa's workshop." In my eyes, it was only a dark, messy place — full of tools I did not understand and the strange smell of wood. Grandpa had passed away two years before, when I was eleven, and since then nobody had opened the door.

② Last April, Mom decided that the old room should be cleaned. "Lingling," she said, "you and I will sort it together this Saturday. Grandpa would be happy to see you helping." I agreed, though I was not excited. I imagined dust, spiders, and boring boxes. I had no idea that a small adventure was waiting behind that door.

③ When we pushed the door open, a yellow ray of morning light rushed inside. On the walls, there were neat rows of wooden planes, saws, old chisels and many other tools I could not name. A long bench stood in the middle, and under it I saw a small green box, tied with a faded red ribbon. My heart suddenly felt warm. I untied the ribbon slowly, as if opening a letter from a friend.

④ Inside the box lay a small wooden bird, about the size of my palm. Its wings were carved with hundreds of tiny feathers, each one thinner than a match. On the bottom was a line of neat pencil words: *"For Lingling, when she can read."* My eyes grew hot at once. Grandpa had been working on this bird for me years ago, perhaps during many long evenings I never saw. Mom touched the bird gently and whispered, "He used to say, 'A careful hand keeps a tender heart.'"

⑤ We also found an old notebook. Grandpa had drawn small pictures of each tool with Chinese names written beside them, as if he had been preparing a book to teach me one day. Most surprising of all, we found a tiny unfinished chair, only ten centimetres tall, meant to hold the wooden bird. A small note beside it said: *"When the bird finds its home, she will sing."* I read it twice. Tears that I had saved for two years quietly came down.

⑥ That afternoon, I carried the wooden bird and the small chair to my room. Together, they now sit on my desk, right in front of my English books. When I meet a difficult math problem or a bad mood, I look at the tiny bird and remember my grandpa's slow, patient hands. A careful hand keeps a tender heart — and a grandpa's love, I have learned, does not end when he is gone. It simply waits quietly in an old room, until we are ready to open the door.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How did the writer feel about Grandpa's workshop before she cleaned it?**

A. She thought it was a beautiful place.  
B. She thought it was only a dark, messy place.  
C. She was afraid of the tools inside.  
D. She had never heard of it.

**2. What did the writer and her mother find in the small green box?**

A. An old letter from Grandpa's teacher.  
B. A small wooden bird carved by Grandpa.  
C. A gold ring with a Chinese name.  
D. A few pieces of candy.

**3. What was written on the bottom of the wooden bird?**

A. "Happy Birthday, my dear!"  
B. "For Lingling, when she can read."  
C. "Do not open this box."  
D. "Made in Beijing, 1980."

**4. What did the old notebook show?**

A. Names and drawings of Grandpa's tools.  
B. A list of the family's money.  
C. A diary about Grandpa's illness.  
D. A long story about a stranger.

**5. What does the writer mainly want to tell us?**

A. Grandfathers always leave secrets for their grandchildren.  
B. A grandparent's love can wait quietly until we are ready to open the door.  
C. Old rooms are dangerous and should be closed.  
D. Wooden birds are the best toys for middle school students.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "only a dark, messy place"  
【解析】她原本觉得那只是个昏暗杂乱的房间。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "a small wooden bird"  
【解析】盒子里是小木雕鸟。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段铅笔字 "For Lingling, when she can read."  
【解析】鸟底部的赠言。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "drawn small pictures of each tool with Chinese names"  
【解析】笔记本记录工具与名称。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑥段末句  
【解析】亲人之爱静静等待被发现。故选 B。
