## 📋 How to Pick Up (three simple steps)

1. Come to **Room 102, Main Building** during pick-up hours, with your **student ID**.  
2. Tell the duty student-volunteer the **item number** from this bulletin and one or two **private details** (e.g. for the red diary: what is written on the inside front cover; for the silver ring: the engraving).  
3. Sign your name and date in the small brown pick-up book. No parent approval is needed for non-valuable items. For valuable items (keys, rings, phones, wallets), a short call home is made; the student waits in the corner chair.

**If the item is not yours**, please do not press your case; the volunteer will gently decline.

---

## 🕒 Deadlines (please read carefully)

- **Small items (non-valuable)**: kept **two weeks**. If not claimed by **Friday, 31 December 2027, 17:30**, they will be offered **free** on the "Share-for-Free Shelf" of the school library (Sunlight Corridor, 3F).  
- **Valuable items (keys, rings, wallets, phones, ID cards, bank cards, diaries, bank slips)**: kept **three months**. If not claimed by **Friday, 19 March 2028, 17:30**, they will be handed to the local **Ping'an Road Police Station** (8 minutes' walk east of school).  
- **Diaries and very private writing**: kept **six months**, unopened, and handed to the police station sealed if unclaimed.

**Nothing is ever thrown away. Nothing valuable is sold.**

---

## 🧼 Care While Stored

- All items are placed in **numbered paper boxes** on a shelf; small damp items are first **air-dried** for 24 hours.  
- **Water bottles** are washed with hot water (not soap) before being shelved.  
- **Clothing** is gently brushed; no washing machine is used (too much risk of shrinkage).  
- **Electronics** are not charged or powered on.  
- **Diaries and private letters** are not opened at any time.

---

## 🤝 How to Hand In Found Items (for students and staff)

- **Found on campus** between 07:00 and 17:45: bring directly to Room 102.  
- **Found after hours / before school**: hand to the **Security Gate** duty officer (Mr Guo or Mr Liu); the item reaches Room 102 by 09:30 next school day.  
- **Found off-campus**: please leave it where it is found; only items found **inside** school boundaries are the school's responsibility.  
- **A small paper form** (date, place, finder's name) is filled in at hand-in; the form stays in the Room 102 ledger for six months.

---

## ❗ Quiet Kind Reminders

- **Do NOT open any diary, letter, or envelope** that is not yours, even out of curiosity. This is a soft school rule.  
- **Label your things**: a small name-sticker on the inside of a bottle, on the spine of a notebook, or on the tongue of a shoe, reunites items with their owners **three times faster** (internal data, 2024-2027).  
- **Do NOT offer thanks gifts to finders.** A small "thank you" is enough. Finders are thanked by the school, not by you.  
- **If you lose something you cannot find below**, still come to Room 102 — many items are held discreetly for privacy and not printed in this bulletin (e.g. medical bracelets, family cards).
