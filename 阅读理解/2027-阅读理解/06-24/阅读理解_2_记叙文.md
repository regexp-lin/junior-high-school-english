# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Day I Planted a Tree With My Father**

I was in Grade 6 when my father suddenly took a week of holiday from work and said to me, "On Saturday, we will go to the countryside and plant a tree." I was surprised. My father was always busy. He rarely talked about things like trees or countryside. But his voice was serious, so I said, "OK, Dad."

On Saturday morning at 5:30, we left our flat. My father drove his old blue car to a small village about 90 kilometres away. He did not speak much on the way. The sky was pink, then gold, then soft blue. When we arrived, we walked along a narrow road beside a river, carrying a small shovel, a bucket, and a little green plant — a young cherry tree with just seven leaves.

We stopped at a flat piece of land near a tall rock. My father pointed and said, "Here." He started to dig. I watched for a while, then picked up the other edge of the shovel and dug together with him. His hands were rough and shook a little at each push. I remember being surprised; I had never really looked at my father's hands before.

After the hole was finished, we set the small cherry tree inside. We pushed soil around it. Father went to the river and filled the bucket with water. He poured the water slowly around the tree. Then he sat on the rock and said, "Come. Sit with me for a minute."

I sat next to him. The air smelled of grass and river water. Birds were singing above us.

Father said quietly, "Your grandfather planted a tree in this spot when I was about your age. A plum tree. I was not very good then; I did not listen, and I did not take care of it. By the time I came back here, years later, the tree was gone. So today I want to plant a new tree with my son — and I want you to know this spot."

I did not know what to say. I just looked at the small cherry tree, standing a little crookedly in the wet soil.

"If one day," Father continued, "I am not there to tell you what to do, you can come here. Look at the tree. If you are making the right decision, you'll somehow know."

We did not talk for some time. Later, we ate two hard-boiled eggs and a piece of bread for lunch. Then we drove home.

Four years have passed. My father is fine, thank goodness. But I have been back to that rock three times — once after my maths test, once after I had a big argument with my best friend, and once last December, for no clear reason. The cherry tree is now taller than my shoulder, with more than a hundred small leaves. Each time I sit on the rock, I do not get any magic answer. I only get a quiet feeling, and a picture in my head: my father's rough hands, pushing the shovel with mine.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the narrator's father take a holiday?**

A. Because he was ill.  
B. Because he wanted to plant a tree with his son.  
C. Because he was moving house.  
D. Because he had a school meeting.

**2. What did the father and son take with them?**

A. A big spade, a bucket and an old apple tree.  
B. A small shovel, a bucket, and a young cherry tree with seven leaves.  
C. A ladder, a bucket, and a camera.  
D. A telescope and a basket of food.

**3. Why did the father decide to plant a new tree in this place?**

A. Because the government paid him for it.  
B. Because his grandfather had asked him to.  
C. Because when he was young, his own father planted a plum tree there, and it did not survive — he wanted to plant a new tree with his son.  
D. Because it was a common village tradition.

**4. What did the father say the cherry tree could be for in the future?**

A. A place to buy cherries.  
B. A private playground.  
C. A quiet spot where the narrator could come when he is making a big decision, even if the father is not there to tell him what to do.  
D. A symbol of their success in school.

**5. Which of the following is true?**

A. The narrator has not visited the spot again after that Saturday.  
B. The cherry tree has died in the cold winters.  
C. The narrator has been back three times, and each time he sits on the rock and remembers his father's rough hands.  
D. The father sold the cherry tree to a farmer.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 段 "we will go to the countryside and plant a tree"  
【解析】为种树休假。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 段  
【解析】小铲、桶、七叶樱桃树。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第 6 段  
【解析】继承父辈心愿，与儿子共植。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 8 段  
【解析】遇重大抉择时静坐之处。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】末段  
【解析】已返回三次并想起父亲之手。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。
