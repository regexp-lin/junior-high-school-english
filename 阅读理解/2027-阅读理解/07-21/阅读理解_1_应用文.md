# 阅读理解（应用文） **New School Library — Rules and Guide (September 2027)**

Dear students,

After one year of careful building, our brand-new **Lihua Middle School Library** will finally open on **Monday, September 1, 2027**. To help you enjoy reading and to keep our books in good shape for future students, please read these rules carefully before your first visit.

---

### 1. Opening Hours

| Day | Morning | Afternoon | Evening |
|-----|---------|-----------|---------|
| Mon – Fri | 07:30 – 08:20 | 12:10 – 13:50 | 17:00 – 19:30 |
| Saturday | 09:00 – 12:00 | 14:00 – 17:00 | closed |
| Sunday | closed | 14:00 – 17:00 | closed |

The library is **closed on all public holidays** and on teachers' training days (notices will be put up one week in advance).

---

### 2. How to Borrow and Return

- Every Lihua student receives a **green reading card** for free in the first week of school. Replacement cards cost ¥5.  
- You may borrow **up to 3 books at one time**.  
- Borrowing period: **14 days**. You may extend each book **once** for another 14 days, by scanning the QR code on the inside cover before the due date.  
- All borrowing and returning must be done at the main desk on the first floor. Self-service machines will be added later this autumn.

### 3. Fines and Lost Books

- Overdue: **¥0.20 per book per day**. After 30 days, we will contact the class teacher.  
- Lost or badly damaged book: you must pay **twice** the price printed on the book's price tag, or bring a new copy of the same edition.  
- Small pencil marks can be cleaned by the library staff for free — please do not try to erase them yourself; paper may be torn.

### 4. Rules Inside the Library

- Please keep your voice down. For group discussion, use **Reading Room B** on the second floor.  
- Food is **not allowed** inside. Water bottles are fine if they are closed tightly.  
- Mobile phones must be on silent mode. Short calls may be taken in the corridor.  
- Bags larger than a small backpack should be placed in the free lockers near the entrance.

### 5. Special Corners

- **Ground floor:** newspapers, magazines, and the "New Arrivals" shelf (books for the past 30 days).  
- **Second floor:** novels, short stories, and the English reading corner (over 800 graded readers).  
- **Third floor:** reference books (dictionaries, maps), the study hall, and the quiet exam-preparation area. This floor is only for Grade 9 students before 16:30 on weekdays.

### 6. Reading Events

- **"Read Aloud Friday"** — every Friday, 16:30 – 17:00, students take turns reading a favourite page aloud. All welcome.  
- **Monthly Author Talk** — a local writer visits on the last Thursday of each month, 18:30 – 19:30. Seats are limited; book yours online.  
- **Summer Reading Prize** — read 8 books during the summer, write a short note for each, and you will get a free bookmark and a small gift at the start of the next term.

### 7. Contact

- **Main Desk:** Room 101 (first floor)  
- **Phone:** 0571-8820-3301 (Mon–Fri, during opening hours)  
- **Email:** library@lihua-ms.edu.cn  
- **WeChat group:** ask your class teacher for the QR code on the first day of school.

A quiet library is a gift from everyone to everyone. Take your seat, open a book, and enjoy.

*— Library Team, Lihua Middle School, July 2027*

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many books can a student borrow at one time, and for how long?**

A. 5 books, for 7 days.  
B. 3 books, for 14 days, with one possible 14-day extension.  
C. 10 books, for 30 days, no extension.  
D. 1 book, for one month.

**2. What happens if a student keeps a book for 40 days past the due date?**

A. Nothing happens.  
B. The class teacher will be contacted, and the student pays ¥0.20 per day per book.  
C. The student must leave the school.  
D. The student is given a free new book.

**3. Which rule is correct according to the poster?**

A. Food and drinks of any kind are allowed.  
B. Phone calls can be taken loudly at the reading table.  
C. Bags larger than a small backpack should be placed in the free lockers near the entrance.  
D. Students can mark any book with coloured pens.

**4. On which floor can a Grade 7 student go to borrow a new magazine that came out two weeks ago?**

A. The ground floor, where the "New Arrivals" shelf is.  
B. The third floor reference room.  
C. The roof of the library.  
D. The basement only.

**5. What is the main purpose of this notice?**

A. To sell library cards to parents.  
B. To introduce the opening hours, rules and services of the new school library.  
C. To advertise a new bookstore in the city.  
D. To tell students to stop reading on Fridays.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 条 "up to 3 books... 14 days... extend each book once for another 14 days..."  
【解析】最多 3 本、14 天、可续借一次。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 条 "After 30 days, we will contact the class teacher."  
【解析】超 30 天通知班主任，并按 0.2 元/天/本罚款。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 条 "Bags larger than a small backpack should be placed in the free lockers..."  
【解析】大背包放储物柜。A/B/D 均与规则相反。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第 5 条 "Ground floor: newspapers, magazines, and the 'New Arrivals' shelf (books for the past 30 days)."  
【解析】两周内的新杂志在一楼。B/C/D 均不符。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】通篇  
【解析】公告介绍图书馆开放时间、规则和服务。A/C/D 均非主旨。故选 B。
