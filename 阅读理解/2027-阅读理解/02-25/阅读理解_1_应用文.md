# 阅读理解（应用文） **Spring Book Exchange — Notice**

① Dear fellow readers, the **Student Union of Pinehill Middle School** invites every student to join our yearly event: **"One Book, One New Friend — Spring Book Exchange"**. Take the books you have already finished home with you, and swap them for fresh stories from classmates. No money is needed — only curiosity.

② **Key Information**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Date | **Saturday, 3 April 2027** |
| Time | 9:00 – 11:30 |
| Place | **School Library, Reading Garden (2F)** |
| Who can join | All Grade 7 – 9 students |
| Book limit | Each student may bring **3 books maximum** |

③ **Which books are welcome?**

- Stories, poems, popular science, biographies.  
- Books **in English or Chinese**.  
- Pages must be clean, with no heavy marks or missing pages.  
- Please write your **name, class and a short review** (no more than 30 words) on a sticky note inside the front cover.

④ **Which books are NOT accepted?**

- Textbooks from this school year.  
- Answer books for exams.  
- Magazines older than 10 years.  
- Any book with torn covers.

⑤ **How it works**

1. At 9:00, hand in your books at the **check-in desk** and receive **one ticket** for each accepted book.  
2. From 9:30, walk around the Reading Garden and pick any book on the tables.  
3. Pay one ticket for one book. Tickets cannot be saved for next year.  
4. Leftover books will be **donated** to **Sunshine Village Primary School** in May.

⑥ **Helpful reminders**

- Bring a cloth bag — paper bags break easily.  
- Please keep your voice low; this is still a library.  
- Volunteers wear **yellow caps**. Ask them if you need help.  
- Scan the school WeChat QR code to register **before 31 March**.

**Let us share stories, share smiles, and let good books fly to new hands. — Pinehill Student Union**
