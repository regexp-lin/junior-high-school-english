# 阅读理解（应用文） **Sunrise Community — Monthly Saturday "Book Exchange Corner"**

---

> **Sunrise Community Neighbourhood Centre**  
> **Monthly Saturday — "Book Exchange Corner"**  
> **A free, friendly way to let your books meet new readers**

Do you have books at home that you no longer need, but do not want to throw away? Every month our neighbourhood centre runs a **Book Exchange Corner** in the ground-floor hall. **Bring some. Take some. All free.**

---

## 📅 When & Where

| Date (all Saturdays) | Time | Place |
|---|---|---|
| **11 October 2027** | 9:00 – 12:00 | Ground-floor Hall |
| **8 November 2027** | 9:00 – 12:00 | Ground-floor Hall |
| **13 December 2027** | 9:00 – 12:00 | Ground-floor Hall |

> On **very rainy days** the event moves to the **reading room on the 2nd floor**, and we post a notice on the building's main gate by 7:30 a.m.

---

## 📚 How It Works — Simple Four Steps

1. **Clean** your books at home (no torn pages; no books with personal notes on every page).
2. **Bring** up to **5 books** to the Book Exchange Corner between 9:00 and 11:00.
3. Our helpers will put a small coloured sticker on each of your books. One sticker = one "book coin".
4. Use your "book coins" to take **other people's books** home. One coin for one book.

*Leftover coins are saved in the centre's file and can be used at the next month's event.*

---

## ✅ What We Accept

- Children's picture books, storybooks, young-adult novels.
- School textbooks still in use (Grades 1 – 9).
- English graded readers.
- General-knowledge and science books.
- Cookbooks, hobby books, travel notes.

## ❌ What We Cannot Accept

- Books older than **twenty years**; paper too yellow or brittle to read.
- Books with **torn covers** or many missing pages.
- **Exam papers and private workbooks** filled with a student's own writing.
- **Magazines and newspapers** (please put them in the recycling bins near the main gate).

---

## 🤝 Volunteers Needed

We need **6 student helpers** and **2 adult helpers** each Saturday. Student helpers receive a **certificate of community service (2 hours)**.

- **Sign up:** scan the QR code on the notice board, or e-mail **sunrise.books@city.cn** before **Thursday** of the same week.
- Helpers must come **30 minutes early** to set up tables and sort books.

---

## 📞 Contact & Help

- **Ms. Wang**, Community Library Office, **010-5588-0603**, Mon – Fri, 14:00 – 17:00.
- If you cannot come but would like to donate books, our centre van can pick up **boxes of 10 or more books** within our neighbourhood, with two days' notice.

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What happens at the Book Exchange Corner?**

A. Books are sold for money to the highest bidder.  
B. Residents bring old books, get "book coins", and use them to take home other people's books — all free.  
C. Only adults can take books from the corner.  
D. Books are thrown away in a large recycling machine.

**2. How many books can a resident bring to one event?**

A. As many as they want.  
B. At least ten.  
C. Up to five books.  
D. Only one picture book.

**3. What happens to the event if it is a very rainy Saturday?**

A. It is cancelled with no notice.  
B. It moves to a friend's shop.  
C. It is held in the 2nd-floor reading room, and a notice is put on the main gate by 7:30 a.m.  
D. It is only held online.

**4. Which of the following books is **NOT** accepted?**

A. A clean children's storybook.  
B. A used Grade 8 maths textbook still in use.  
C. An English graded reader.  
D. A workbook completely filled with a student's own writing.

**5. A Grade 9 student wants to help as a volunteer. What must she do?**

A. Arrive at 9:30 on the day and pay 10 yuan.  
B. Scan the QR code on the notice board or e-mail the centre before Thursday of the same week; arrive 30 minutes early to set up.  
C. Phone Ms. Wang at midnight.  
D. Bring 20 books with her.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开篇 "Bring some. Take some. All free." + 四步说明  
【解析】活动模式为以书换书、完全免费。A、C、D 均与原文相反。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How It Works 第 2 步 "Bring up to 5 books"  
【解析】最多 5 本。A 无上限、B "至少 10"、D "只能 1 本"均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】When & Where 备注 "very rainy days ... moves to the reading room on the 2nd floor ... post a notice ... by 7:30 a.m."  
【解析】雨天移至二楼阅览室并提前发告示。A、B、D 均与原文矛盾。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】What We Cannot Accept "Exam papers and private workbooks filled with a student's own writing."  
【解析】填满私人笔迹的练习册不收。A、B、C 均在接受之列。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Volunteers Needed "scan the QR code ... or e-mail ... before Thursday ... come 30 minutes early"  
【解析】报名方式与时间均明示。A、C、D 都违反规则。故选 B。
