# 阅读理解（应用文） **Teen Book Swap Festival — City Library Poster**

> **Green River City Library**  
> **POSTER — Teen Book Swap Festival**  
> **"Trade a book. Meet a reader. Find a new world."**

---

**DATE:** Sunday, 9 May 2027  
**TIME:** 9:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.  
**PLACE:** 2nd Floor Reading Hall, Green River City Library  
**FOR:** Students aged 11 – 16

---

### What is a Book Swap?

Bring **one or two books** that you have read and loved, but no longer need. At our festival, you will put them on the long shared table. Other young readers will do the same. Then you can **walk along, read the notes, and take home new books for free**. Every book you bring gives you **one "book coin"** that you may use to pick up a new one.

### What to Bring

- **1–3 books** in good condition (no torn or dirty pages)  
- A **small sticky note** inside each book, with your name, class, and one sentence like "I loved this book because…"  
- **Your student card** for sign-in  
- Optional: a **bookmark** you have made

### What You Can Do That Day

| Time          | Activity                              | Where              |
|---------------|---------------------------------------|--------------------|
| 9:00–10:30    | Drop off books & get book coins       | Service Counter A  |
| 10:30–12:00   | Open swap (first round)               | Main Swap Table    |
| 12:00–13:30   | "Story Picnic" on the roof garden     | Roof Garden        |
| 13:30–15:00   | Author Talk: *Why I Write for Teens*  | Small Hall 202     |
| 15:00–16:30   | Open swap (second round)              | Main Swap Table    |

The author of the teen novel *The Kite in the Rain*, **Ms Su Yan**, will speak at 13:30 and sign books for free (please line up; no more than two signatures per person).

### Rules to Remember

1. Please **do not swap textbooks**, homework books, or magazines older than three years.  
2. Do not take a book **without a coin**.  
3. Keep your voice **low** in the Reading Hall.  
4. Food and tea are only allowed on the **roof garden**.  
5. Broken or very old books will be given to the library's **paper-recycling box**.

### How to Sign Up

- Scan the QR code on your school bulletin board, **OR**  
- Send "BookSwap + your name + class" to **teenlib@grcl.gov.cn**  
- Deadline: **Thursday, 6 May 2027, 17:00**

Places are limited to **200 students**. First come, first served. We will reply with a ticket number within 24 hours.

**Books travel; new friends come home with you.**  
**See you on 9 May!**

**Green River City Library – Teen Services Team**
