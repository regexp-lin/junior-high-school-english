# 阅读理解（应用文） **Spring Field Trip — Silver Lake Wetland Park**

① Dear Grade 7 students, our annual **Spring Field Trip** is just around the corner! This year we are going to the **Silver Lake Wetland Park**, a large natural park 30 km from the city centre. Bring your eyes, your notebook, and a cheerful heart — the morning dew, the white egrets and a friendly guide are all waiting for us on **Friday, 9 April 2027**.

② **Basic Information**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Date | Friday, 9 April 2027 |
| Gather | **7:15** in front of the school main gate |
| Depart | 7:30 sharp |
| Return | about **16:30** |
| Fee | **60 yuan** (bus + guide + lunch box) |
| Teacher in charge | Mr. Yang (class 7-1), Ms. Zheng (class 7-2) |

③ **Timetable of the Day**

- **07:30 – 08:45**　School bus to Silver Lake Park  
- **09:00 – 10:30**　Guided walk along the Bird Path; look for egrets, wild ducks, and kingfishers  
- **10:45 – 12:00**　Short lecture by park ranger: "How wetlands clean our rivers"  
- **12:00 – 13:00**　Lunch break at the picnic area (lunch box provided)  
- **13:00 – 14:30**　Group task: fill in the **Wetland Worksheet** (3 students per group)  
- **14:30 – 15:30**　Free time at the Children's Garden / lotus pond photo area  
- **15:45 – 16:30**　School bus back to school

④ **What to Bring**

- Your student ID card and a signed parent note.  
- A small **notebook and pen** (the worksheet needs quick writing).  
- A **water bottle**, a **sun hat** and **sunscreen**.  
- A light jacket (mornings near water are cool).  
- **Comfortable walking shoes** — the path is muddy in some places.

⑤ **What NOT to Bring**

- Phones are allowed, but **not game consoles**.  
- No food with strong smell (ducks may come too close).  
- No sharp tools — the park will lend small magnifying glasses.  
- No pets or drones.  
- Please do **not** bring red or yellow balloons; they frighten wild birds.

⑥ **Trip Rules**

1. Stay with your group at all times. If you are lost, walk to the **big blue Info Board** near the lake; a teacher will check there every 30 minutes.  
2. **Do not touch** any wild bird, nest or egg.  
3. Pick up your own rubbish; every group will receive a small green bag at the gate.  
4. In the lecture hall, please keep your voice at a whisper — wetland birds are very easily disturbed.  
5. If it rains lightly, the trip goes on. If there is a thunder storm, the trip will be moved to **16 April**.

⑦ **Contact**  
- Mr. Yang (head teacher of 7-1) — phone 138-9920-0175  
- Ms. Zheng (head teacher of 7-2) — phone 139-7731-8804  
- School trip office — Building 3, Room 102, 12:30 – 13:30 only

**A whole wetland is waiting for us — see you at the main gate! — Bright Future Middle School**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What time should students gather at the school gate?**

A. 7:15.  
B. 7:30.  
C. 9:00.  
D. 16:30.

**2. What will students do from 13:00 to 14:30?**

A. Ride the school bus back to school.  
B. Fill in the Wetland Worksheet in groups of three.  
C. Have a group photo at the lotus pond.  
D. Listen to a second lecture by the park ranger.

**3. Which of the following is NOT allowed on the trip?**

A. A water bottle.  
B. A small notebook.  
C. Red or yellow balloons.  
D. A sun hat.

**4. What should a student do if she gets lost in the park?**

A. Call the school trip office and wait outside the park.  
B. Walk to the big blue Info Board near the lake.  
C. Run back to the school bus immediately.  
D. Find a park ranger and stay at the lecture hall.

**5. What happens if there is a thunder storm on 9 April?**

A. The trip will be cancelled completely.  
B. Students will stay at school and do extra homework.  
C. The trip will be moved to 16 April.  
D. The trip will only visit the Children's Garden.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Gather: 7:15 in front of the school main gate"  
【解析】7:15 集合。B 为出发时间；C、D 为其他时段。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 13:00 – 14:30 "fill in the Wetland Worksheet (3 students per group)"  
【解析】分组完成湿地工作单。其他时段活动不同。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段末 "do not bring red or yellow balloons"  
【解析】红黄气球被禁止。A、B、D 属要带物品。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段第 1 条 "walk to the big blue Info Board near the lake"  
【解析】迷路应到湖边蓝色信息板处。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段第 5 条 "moved to 16 April"  
【解析】遇雷雨改至 4 月 16 日。A、B、D 无依据。故选 C。
