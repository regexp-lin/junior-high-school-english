# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Library — Weekend Workshops for Teens (Spring 2027)**

① Dear young readers, the **Green Hills City Library** is proud to present its new **Saturday Workshop Series** for students aged **11 – 15**. Every Saturday from **March to June 2027**, we open four small, free workshops — one idea, one book, one hour. Join us to read together, make something new, and meet new friends from other schools.

② **Place:** Building B, 2nd floor, reading hall  
**Each workshop:** 60 minutes (45 min activity + 15 min free reading)  
**Fee:** **Free**, but you must sign up online first.  
**Number of seats:** 20 per workshop (first-come, first-served).

③ **Weekly Timetable (Saturdays)**

| Time | Workshop | Main Activity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **09:30 – 10:30** | **Book & Brush** | Read one short chapter, then draw the scene with watercolours |
| **10:45 – 11:45** | **Mini Science Lab** | Try small, safe experiments linked to a science book |
| **14:00 – 15:00** | **English Corner** | Talk in simple English with a volunteer reader (often a university student) |
| **15:15 – 16:15** | **Young Writer's Circle** | Turn a picture into a 100-word short story |

④ **What You Should Bring**

- A small notebook and a pencil.  
- Your **library card**. (Free card-making is offered on Friday afternoons.)  
- Your own water bottle. No drinks in colourful bottles, please.  
- A smile and a question — we love curious readers!

⑤ **What You Should Leave at Home**

- Mobile games and loud earphones.  
- Snacks with strong smell (oranges and bread are fine).  
- Pets and large bags (>35 cm).  
- Any sharp tools — the library will provide safe scissors and brushes.

⑥ **Special Notes**

1. Children under 11 may join the 09:30 **Book & Brush** workshop only, **with a parent**.  
2. If you sign up but cannot come, please **cancel before 18:00 on Friday**. Three missed workshops without cancellation will pause your sign-up right for four weeks.  
3. On rainy days, outdoor breaks will move to the small reading garden on the 3rd floor.  
4. Every student who finishes **ten workshops** before 30 June will receive a **Reading Star Badge** and a small book gift from the mayor's office.

⑦ **How to Sign Up**

- Visit **www.ghcitylib.org/teens** or scan the blue QR code at the library front desk.  
- Select a date and a workshop.  
- A confirmation message will arrive by SMS within 24 hours.  
- Any question? Call **0571-8833-2027** (Tuesday – Sunday, 10:00 – 17:00).

**Open a book, open a door — see you on Saturday! — Green Hills City Library**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Who is the workshop series mainly designed for?**

A. University students.  
B. Students aged 11 – 15.  
C. Teachers from small schools.  
D. Parents with babies.

**2. How long does each workshop last?**

A. 30 minutes.  
B. 45 minutes.  
C. 60 minutes.  
D. 90 minutes.

**3. In which workshop will students turn a picture into a 100-word story?**

A. Book & Brush.  
B. Mini Science Lab.  
C. English Corner.  
D. Young Writer's Circle.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed at the workshops?**

A. A small notebook.  
B. A library card.  
C. Mobile games and loud earphones.  
D. A water bottle.

**5. How can a student get a Reading Star Badge?**

A. By joining any one workshop before 30 June.  
B. By finishing ten workshops before 30 June.  
C. By writing a long book review.  
D. By bringing three new friends to the library.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段第 1 句 "for students aged 11 – 15"  
【解析】受众是 11–15 岁学生。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Each workshop: 60 minutes"  
【解析】每场 60 分钟。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段表格 15:15–16:15 "Young Writer's Circle... Turn a picture into a 100-word short story"  
【解析】把图片变成 100 词小故事属 Young Writer's Circle。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段第 1 条 "Mobile games and loud earphones"  
【解析】手机游戏与大声耳机被明确禁止。A、B、D 属要带物品。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段第 4 条 "finishes ten workshops before 30 June will receive a Reading Star Badge"  
【解析】需在 6 月 30 日前完成 10 场工作坊。其他选项无依据。故选 B。
