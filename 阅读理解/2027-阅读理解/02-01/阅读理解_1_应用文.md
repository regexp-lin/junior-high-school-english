# 阅读理解（应用文） **Spring Art Festival — School Poster**

① Dear students of Riverside Middle School, the long-awaited **Spring Art Festival** is coming again! This year, the theme is **"Colours of Our Lives."** The school welcomes every class, every student, and every parent to enjoy art in many forms during these three exciting days.

② **Festival Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Dates | Thursday, April 16 – Saturday, April 18 |
| Place | School playground & Art Hall |
| Opening Ceremony | April 16, 9:00 a.m. |
| Free entry | All parents and friends with an invitation card |

③ **Main Events**

- **Day 1 (Thu):** Class Painting Wall — each class paints a big cloth together on the theme *My Classroom Today*.  
- **Day 2 (Fri):** Music Night — piano, violin, Chinese gu-zheng and a student choir. 6:30 p.m. at the School Hall.  
- **Day 3 (Sat):** Drama & Dance Show in the morning; Art Market in the afternoon, where students sell their own paintings, bookmarks and small gifts. All money will go to a rural reading program for poor children.

④ **Rules for Participants**

- Every student **in Grade 7 and Grade 8** must sign up for **at least one** activity.  
- Grade 9 students **may** sign up but are not required to.  
- Works must be made by the student himself/herself. **Copied works** will be taken down quietly.  
- Please **do not** run in the Art Hall or touch the paintings with your fingers.  
- The Art Market will use **QR code** payments only. No cash.

⑤ **How to Sign Up**

Fill in the orange **"Art Festival Form"** at the art classroom before **March 25**. Write your name, class, and the event you want to join. You may join up to two events. For more help, visit Ms. Yang during lunch break on Monday and Thursday, or email her at **yang.art@rsms.edu**. Let's make this April full of colours!

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the theme of this year's Spring Art Festival?**

A. "Colours of Our Lives."  
B. "A New Trip Around the World."  
C. "Books and Dreams."  
D. "Old Days, New Hopes."

**2. When will the Music Night be held?**

A. Thursday, April 16, 9:00 a.m.  
B. Friday, April 17, 6:30 p.m.  
C. Saturday, April 18, morning.  
D. Saturday, April 18, afternoon.

**3. Where will the money from the Art Market go?**

A. To the school canteen.  
B. To teachers' book fund.  
C. To a rural reading program for poor children.  
D. To a city football club.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed during the festival?**

A. Touching the paintings with your fingers.  
B. Joining more than one event.  
C. Paying by QR code at the Art Market.  
D. Inviting parents with a card.

**5. Who must sign up for at least one activity?**

A. Only Grade 9 students.  
B. Only parents of Grade 7 students.  
C. All Grade 7 and Grade 8 students.  
D. Only members of the school choir.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "the theme is 'Colours of Our Lives.'"  
【解析】今年主题是"生活的色彩"。其他选项均不符。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Day 2 (Fri): Music Night... 6:30 p.m."  
【解析】音乐之夜周五晚 6:30。A、C、D 均为其他活动。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "All money will go to a rural reading program for poor children."  
【解析】善款用于乡村儿童阅读项目。A、B、D 均无依据。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Please do not run in the Art Hall or touch the paintings with your fingers."  
【解析】禁止触摸画作。B、C、D 均允许。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Every student in Grade 7 and Grade 8 must sign up for at least one activity."  
【解析】七八年级必须参加至少一项。A、B、D 均不符。故选 C。
