# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School Grade 8 — Autumn Outing to Xishan Ancient Town**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Grade 8 Form Teachers' Group**  
> **2027 Autumn Outing Notice**  
> **One day in the old town, one day away from our desks**

Autumn has painted the hills around our city golden and red. After four months of hard study, Grade 8 will spend **Saturday 30 October 2027** at **Xishan Ancient Town**, about 35 km west of our school. Please read this notice carefully with your parents, and keep it until Outing Day.

---

## 📍 Schedule at a Glance

| Time | Activity | Place |
|---|---|---|
| 07:00 | Meet at school south gate | School |
| 07:15 | Buses leave (six coaches, each with a form teacher) | — |
| 08:30 | Arrive; old-street walk; local culture guide | Ancient Town main street |
| 10:30 | "Paper-cutting" workshop (group activity) | Culture Hall |
| 12:00 | Box lunch at Hillside Picnic Ground | Hillside |
| 13:00 | Short mountain hike (3 km, easy slope) | Xishan |
| 15:00 | Tea, photos, group reflection | Tea House |
| 16:00 | Buses leave | — |
| 17:30 | Return to school, parents collect | School |

---

## 🎒 What to Bring

- **School tracksuit** and comfortable sports shoes (no sandals, no new stiff shoes).  
- A light jacket — it can be cool in the hills in the afternoon.  
- A **500 mL water bottle** (refill stations available in the town).  
- Small backpack with: a **student ID card**, a pen, a notebook, one light snack, a paper tissue, and rain gear.  
- **Pocket money: 30 – 50 yuan**, no more. Most meals are already paid for.

---

## 🚫 What Not to Bring

- No large cash, no valuable watches or jewellery.  
- No game consoles, no Bluetooth speakers.  
- No lighters, knives, fireworks, or any spray cans.  
- Mobile phones are **allowed** for emergency contact with parents, but must stay in the bag during the paper-cutting workshop and mountain hike.

---

## 🚌 Safety and Rules

1. **Travel in groups of six.** Each group has one elected group leader (we will choose them in class on Thursday).  
2. Buckle your **seat belt** on the coach. No standing, no eating, no loud games on the road.  
3. During the mountain hike, stay on the marked path. **Do not pick flowers or fruits** from farmers' trees along the way.  
4. If a student feels unwell at any time, **tell your form teacher immediately**. Do not walk alone back to the buses.  
5. The school has arranged **group insurance**; please make sure your parents have signed the yellow consent form.

---

## ✍️ After-Outing Tasks

- Each student writes **a 150-word diary entry** (in Chinese **or** English) about one small moment of the day — a tree, a person, a conversation, a photo. Submit it to your form teacher **by Tuesday 2 November**.  
- Each group of six submits **one paper-cutting work** from the workshop. The Art Office will display the best 10 works in the school library through November.  
- Optional: upload one photo (not a selfie) to the class cloud folder, with a one-sentence caption. Photos showing strangers' faces clearly should first ask permission.

---

## ☔ Bad-Weather Plan

If the weather forecast for 30 October shows **heavy rain or thunder**, the outing will be moved to **Saturday 6 November**. The school will tell every family through the mini-app **before 18:00 on 28 October**.

---

*Let the autumn air clear our heads, and let the old town tell us a few quiet stories.*

*Lihua Middle School, Grade 8 Form Teachers' Group*
