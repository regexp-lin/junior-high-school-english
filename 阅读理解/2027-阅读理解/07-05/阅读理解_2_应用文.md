# 阅读理解（应用文） **Riverside Park — Summer Kite Festival**

**RIVERSIDE PARK · COMMUNITY FESTIVALS OFFICE**
*A string, a wind, a sky — let's meet up there.*

---

**Festival Guide — 30 July 2027**

Riverside Park is proud to host the **Summer Kite Festival**, our biggest event of the year. Families, school clubs, and kite lovers of all ages are welcome. It is free to watch, and kites are on sale at the festival market for those who forget to bring one.

**Date and weather plan**

- **Main day:** Friday, 30 July 2027, 09:00 – 18:00
- **Back-up day:** Saturday, 31 July 2027 (if the main day is stormy or has strong rain)
- A weather notice will be posted on our website at 07:00 on the main day.

**Where to go inside the park**

- **Main Lawn (South Gate)** — kite flying, photo zones
- **Wood Platform (centre)** — handmade kite exhibition
- **Sunset Square (Lake East)** — evening show (17:00 – 18:00)
- **Market Street (West Gate)** — kites, snacks, drinks

**Programme**

| Time | Place | Activity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 09:00 – 10:00 | Main Lawn | Opening, basic rules, free kite string (first 200 visitors) |
| 10:00 – 12:00 | Main Lawn | Free-fly time, young flyers' corner |
| 12:00 – 13:30 | — | Lunch break (you may bring food or buy at Market Street) |
| 13:30 – 15:30 | Wood Platform | Handmade kite workshop (sign up needed, only 40 spaces) |
| 15:30 – 16:30 | Main Lawn | "Long-Tail Challenge" — whose tail touches the ground last? |
| 17:00 – 18:00 | Sunset Square | Evening performance: giant team kites, music, LED tails |

**Tickets and prices**

| Item | Price |
| --- | --- |
| Park entrance (all day) | Free |
| Handmade kite workshop | ¥50 per person (includes kite kit) |
| Kite from the market | from ¥20 to ¥120 (cloth and bamboo models) |
| Snack bag (drink + cake) | ¥25 |
| Guided sunset walk (45 min) | ¥15 |

**Rules for safe flying**

1. **Do not fly kites near the lake.** Falling kites into water are very hard to recover and can damage the ecosystem.
2. **Do not fly above the road** at the South Gate. Strings can tangle with passing cars.
3. **Wear gloves when using long strings.** Thin, fast-moving string can hurt bare hands.
4. **Give every flyer 5 metres of free space.** Strings of nearby kites can easily knot together.
5. No fireworks, no drones, no pets on the main lawn.

**What to bring**

- A hat, sunscreen, a reusable bottle of water.
- Comfortable running shoes (grass can be wet in the morning).
- A small plastic bag for your own rubbish.
- A kite — or ¥20 and a smile if you plan to buy one on the spot.

**Find us**

- **Metro:** Line 5, Riverside Park Station, Exit B
- **Bus:** No. 301, 305, 318, get off at Park Gate
- **Email:** kite@riversidepark.cn · **Phone:** 021-xxxx-2525

See you on the Main Lawn — look up for the tallest blue dragon!

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Back-up day: Saturday, 31 July 2027"  
【解析】主场日暴雨→31 日举办。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Handmade kite workshop (sign up needed, only 40 spaces)"  
【解析】手工风筝坊。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"¥50 per person (includes kite kit)"  
【解析】50 元，含材料。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】规则 1 "Do not fly kites near the lake"  
【解析】禁止湖边放。B/C/D 均允许。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Metro: Line 5, Riverside Park Station, Exit B"  
【解析】5 号线河畔公园站 B 口。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
