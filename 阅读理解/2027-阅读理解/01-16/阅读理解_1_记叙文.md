# 阅读理解（记叙文） **A Little Bird in the Snow**

① It snowed heavily the whole night. When I opened the window on Sunday morning, the world outside was covered by a soft white blanket. I quickly put on my thick coat and ran downstairs to help my father clean the small path in front of our house.

② While we were shovelling the snow, I heard a strange sound from the corner of the yard. It was a weak, short cry. I walked closer and saw a small grey bird sitting on the cold ground. Its wing looked hurt, and its eyes were half closed. The poor little thing was shaking in the wind.

③ I held the bird gently with both hands and took it inside. Mother brought an old shoebox and I put some soft paper in it. The bird stopped shaking after a while. My little sister, Amy, wanted to give it bread, but my father said it was better to offer small pieces of apple and a little warm water first. Amy watched the bird with a worried face and promised not to make any noise.

④ In the afternoon, we took the bird to the pet hospital. The doctor checked its wing and said it was only a soft injury. "Keep it warm for three or four days," he told us, "then let it fly back when the snow melts." My father also showed me how to clean the box and change the water every morning.

⑤ Five days later, the sun came out and the snow on the roof began to melt. I carried the box to the small garden and opened it. The bird looked at us for a moment, shook its wings, and flew up into a tall tree. Amy waved and shouted, "Fly well!" I felt a quiet happiness in my heart. Helping a small life was not a big thing, but it made our cold winter a little warmer.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What was the weather like on Sunday morning?**

A. Rainy and windy.  
B. Snowy and cold.  
C. Sunny and warm.  
D. Foggy and dark.

**2. Where did the writer find the little bird?**

A. On the roof of the house.  
B. Inside the pet hospital.  
C. In the corner of the yard.  
D. Near the tall tree in the park.

**3. What did the father suggest giving the bird first?**

A. Cold milk and biscuits.  
B. Small pieces of apple and warm water.  
C. A large piece of bread.  
D. A bottle of sweet juice.

**4. How long did the family keep the bird at home before letting it go?**

A. One day.  
B. Two days.  
C. Five days.  
D. Two weeks.

**5. What can we learn from this story?**

A. People should keep all wild birds as pets.  
B. Small kind actions can bring quiet happiness.  
C. Winter is the worst season of the whole year.  
D. Children should avoid going out in the snow.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "It snowed heavily the whole night... the world outside was covered by a soft white blanket."  
【解析】大雪后世界覆盖白毯，故为雪天。A 雨、C 晴、D 雾与原文不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "I heard a strange sound from the corner of the yard."  
【解析】在院子角落发现小鸟。A、B、D 均与原文不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "my father said it was better to offer small pieces of apple and a little warm water first."  
【解析】父亲建议先给苹果碎和温水。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Five days later, the sun came out..."  
【解析】五天后放飞，与"三四天保暖"一致。A、B、D 与"five days"不符。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段 "Helping a small life was not a big thing, but it made our cold winter a little warmer."  
【解析】作者感悟是小善举带来温暖与快乐。A 把野鸟当宠物与放飞矛盾；C 片面否定冬天；D 与家人一起铲雪相反。故选 B。
