# 阅读理解（说明文） **Why Does the Moon Change Its Shape?**

① Have you ever noticed that the moon looks different in the sky every night? Sometimes it is a round, bright ball; at other times, it becomes a thin silver line. Some nights we cannot see the moon at all. Many children think the moon "grows" and "gets smaller," but this is not really true.

② The moon itself has a fixed shape. It is a big stone ball going around the earth about once a month. The changes that we see are called the "phases of the moon." They happen because of the positions of the sun, the earth and the moon.

③ The moon does not make its own light. We can see it only because the sun shines on it. Half of the moon is always bright because the sun is shining on that side. The other half is dark. As the moon moves around the earth, we see different parts of the bright half and the dark half. That is why the shape seems to change.

④ When the moon is between the sun and the earth, we see the dark side, and the moon looks invisible. This is called the "new moon." A few days later, a thin curved line appears in the evening sky — this is the "crescent." Then it grows into a "half moon," a "gibbous moon," and finally the big round "full moon." After the full moon, the shape slowly gets thinner again until the new moon returns.

⑤ Learning about the moon is not only fun but also useful. Farmers and fishermen used the phases of the moon to decide the best time for their work long before clocks and calendars were invented. Tonight, when you go outside, look up and try to guess which phase you are seeing. The sky has a quiet, regular story to tell every month.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段问句 "Have you ever noticed that the moon looks different in the sky every night?"  
【解析】全文解释月亮形状变化的原因。A、C、D 均非主题。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "It is a big stone ball going around the earth about once a month."  
【解析】月亮形状固定，绕地球约一月一周。A 与"growth"相反；C 月亮非气态；D 频率错误。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "We can see it only because the sun shines on it."  
【解析】看得到月亮是因为太阳照在上面。A 月亮不发光；B 地球照亮错；D 星星无依据。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "When the moon is between the sun and the earth... This is called the 'new moon.'"  
【解析】此位置对应新月。A、B、C 均对应其他阶段。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "look up and try to guess which phase you are seeing."  
【解析】作者建议今晚抬头看月亮猜相位。A、C、D 与原文不符。故选 B。
