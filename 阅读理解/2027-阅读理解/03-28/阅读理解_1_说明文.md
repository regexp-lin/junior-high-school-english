# 阅读理解（说明文） **How Clouds Are Made**

① Look up on a bright April afternoon. Soft **white islands** move slowly across the blue sky. Some are shaped like sheep, some like giant dragons, some like a mother holding her child. We have all watched clouds since we were small. But have you ever stopped to ask, **what is a cloud really made of, and how does it get up there?**

② The short answer is: a cloud is **a river of water** — but a very strange river. It is not in a long bed on the ground; it floats in the sky. It is not liquid water either; it is made of **millions of tiny drops of water or tiny bits of ice**, so small that we cannot see a single one with the naked eye. When billions of these drops gather together, we see the soft "cotton" above our heads.

③ How does water travel from the ground to the sky? The process has three simple steps, and scientists call it the **water cycle**:

- **Step 1 — Evaporation**. The sun heats the sea, the river, the lake, even wet washing on your balcony. Some water **turns into an invisible gas** called **water vapour** and rises into the warm air.  
- **Step 2 — Cooling**. As the vapour goes up, the air around it gets colder. Cold air cannot hold as much water as warm air. The vapour sticks to **tiny pieces of dust, sea salt, or pollen** — called "condensation nuclei". Around these tiny pieces, water turns back into small drops.  
- **Step 3 — Gathering**. Millions of these drops float together. Our eyes, far below, see this gathering as a **cloud**.

④ Not all clouds look the same. The thin, high clouds that look like horse tails are **cirrus** clouds. The big, flat, low grey clouds that cover the sky before a long rain are **stratus** clouds. The famous "cotton clouds" of summer afternoons are **cumulus** clouds. Very tall, angry-looking towers of dark cloud are **cumulonimbus** — the kind that often bring thunder and heavy rain. **Reading the sky is, in a way, reading a free weather newspaper**: a sky full of cumulus often means a nice day; a sky filling with dark cumulonimbus is telling you to bring your umbrella home from school.

⑤ When the drops in a cloud grow too big, they become too heavy to float. Then they fall as **rain** — or, if the air is cold enough, as **snow** or **hail**. The water returns to the sea, the river, the lake, or the wet washing on your balcony, and the whole journey starts again. In this way, the cloud above you today might, weeks later, be part of a glass of water in a child's hand on the other side of China. Clouds are not just decoration; they are **the earth's great water delivery service**, working quietly for all living things.

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is a cloud mainly made of?**

A. Smoke from factories.  
B. Millions of tiny drops of water or bits of ice.  
C. Pieces of cotton from cotton fields.  
D. Waste gas from cars.

**2. In the water cycle, what does the word "evaporation" mean?**

A. Water turns into invisible gas and rises into the air.  
B. Rain falls down from the sky.  
C. Snow melts on the mountain.  
D. Wind blows dust across the country.

**3. According to the article, tiny pieces of dust or pollen in the air ______.**

A. make the sky always grey and unclean.  
B. help water vapour change back into small drops.  
C. stop clouds from gathering in the sky.  
D. are not related to clouds in any way.

**4. Which kind of cloud is most likely to bring thunder and heavy rain?**

A. Cirrus.  
B. Stratus.  
C. Cumulus.  
D. Cumulonimbus.

**5. What is the writer's main idea in the last paragraph?**

A. Clouds are only beautiful decorations in the sky.  
B. Clouds are the earth's water delivery service for all living things.  
C. Clouds never move to far places.  
D. Umbrellas are only useful in winter.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "millions of tiny drops of water or tiny bits of ice"  
【解析】由极小水滴或冰晶构成。A、C、D 与原文不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】词义理解题  
【定位】第③段 Step 1 "turns into an invisible gas called water vapour"  
【解析】蒸发即水变为水蒸气上升。B、C、D 与蒸发不符。故选 A。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 Step 2 "sticks to tiny pieces of dust... water turns back into small drops"  
【解析】微小尘粒作为凝结核。A、C、D 错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "cumulonimbus — the kind that often bring thunder and heavy rain"  
【解析】积雨云常带雷雨。A、B、C 非此类。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨推断题  
【定位】第⑤段末 "the earth's great water delivery service"  
【解析】云是地球水循环的"快递"。A、C、D 与主旨不符。故选 B。
