# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should Coding Be a Required Subject in Middle School?**

① More and more schools in China are thinking about making coding a required subject in middle school. Some parents are very happy about this idea, while others do not agree. As a middle-school student myself, I would like to share my own opinion.

② Those who support the plan point out that the world is changing fast. Smartphones, AI helpers and even traffic lights are controlled by computer programs. If we only know how to use these products but never understand how they work, we will always be users, not creators. Learning a little coding in middle school can open students' eyes, help them think in a step-by-step way, and prepare them for future studies and jobs. In my class, the students who joined the coding club have become more patient and better at solving math problems.

③ However, people who are against the plan have good reasons too. First, middle-school students already have many subjects and a lot of homework. Adding coding may make their lives even busier. Second, not every student enjoys or needs coding. Some are more interested in art, music or sports, and we should respect these different choices. Third, good coding classes need strong teachers and enough computers. In some country schools, these resources are still hard to find.

④ In my opinion, coding is useful, but it should not be a heavy required subject for every student. A better idea is to offer short coding classes to everyone in Grade 7, so that all students can try it. After that, those who are really interested can continue in clubs or selective courses, while others may choose their own favorite subjects. In this way, we keep the chance to learn without giving up choice and balance.

**1. What is the main question that the writer tries to answer?**

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段末句 "I would like to share my own opinion." 及全文围绕"是否应把编程设为必修"展开。  
【解析】作者明确讨论是否该让中学生必修编程。A、C、D 均偏离主旨。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段第4句 "Learning a little coding in middle school can open students' eyes, help them think in a step-by-step way..."  
【解析】学编程可帮助理解产品原理并培养一步步思考。A 发财、B 停写作业、D 缩短上学日均属无中生有/夸张。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题（排除型）  
【定位】第③段依次列出"作业多""学生兴趣不同""资源不足"。  
【解析】文中提到 A、B、C 三点反对理由，未提及 D "伤害视力"。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第④段 "offer short coding classes to everyone in Grade 7... those who are really interested can continue..."  
【解析】作者建议先在七年级普及短期课程，再让感兴趣的同学继续。A 与"不应成为沉重必修"矛盾；B 并未完全禁止；D 地域限制无依据。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】写作出处/意图题  
【定位】全文第一人称、学生视角、讨论学校课程问题。  
【解析】以中学生身份分析校园话题，最可能发表在学校报纸的议论栏目。A 医学课本、C 菜谱、D 旅行指南均不符。故选 B。
