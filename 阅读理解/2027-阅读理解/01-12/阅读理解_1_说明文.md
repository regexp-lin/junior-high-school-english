# 阅读理解（说明文） **The Beauty of Ice and Snow Sports**

① Every winter, millions of people around the world enjoy ice and snow sports. Some of them are very old, while others appeared only in the last hundred years. Skiing, for example, began thousands of years ago in northern Europe and Asia, when people used wooden boards to walk quickly across deep snow. Today, skiing is both a daily way to get around in some villages and an exciting sport watched by fans all over the world.

② Ice skating has an interesting history too. The first skates were made of animal bones, and people tied them to their shoes with leather strings. Later, metal blades were invented, and skating became smoother and faster. Figure skating, ice hockey and short-track speed skating all grew out of this long tradition. Each of them needs different skills, but all of them ask the players to keep balance on thin, slippery ice.

③ In recent years, snow sports have become more popular in China. New skating rinks and ski resorts have been built in many cities, even in warm southern areas, because of the use of cooling machines. After the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics, more young people started to take lessons in skiing and snowboarding. Schools in some cities have also added short winter sport courses to their physical education classes.

④ Doctors say ice and snow sports are good for health. They improve balance, train the legs and the back, and help young people build courage because falling down is a normal part of learning. However, safety rules must always come first. Players should wear helmets, gloves and proper clothes, warm up carefully, and choose the right level of slope or rink. With good preparation, winter sports can bring fun, friendship and unforgettable memories to people of all ages.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段第2句 "Skiing... began thousands of years ago in northern Europe and Asia."  
【解析】原文明确说滑雪始于数千年前的北欧和亚洲北部。A 地点和时间均错；C 奥运会是现代事件，与起源不符；D 原文未提美国。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段第1句 "The first skates were made of animal bones."  
【解析】首款冰刀由动物骨头制成。A、B、D 均与原文矛盾。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段第2句 "...even in warm southern areas, because of the use of cooling machines."  
【解析】冷却设备使得南方也能建设滑冰场和滑雪场。A 天气变冷无依据；B 停止球赛无中生有；D 北极门票过度推断。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "...falling down is a normal part of learning."  
【解析】摔倒是学习过程中的正常部分。A 贵衣服非文中表达；C 夺金与"学习过程"无关；D 独自夜滑与文意相反。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】全文围绕冰雪运动的历史、发展和好处展开。  
【解析】文章介绍冰雪运动的历史、在中国的普及及益处，属于介绍+说明。A 商业意图与内容不符；B 与"好处"矛盾；D 未做中欧对比。故选 C。
