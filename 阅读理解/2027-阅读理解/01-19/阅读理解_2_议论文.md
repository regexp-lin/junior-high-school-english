# 阅读理解（议论文） **Is It Good for Students to Have a "Star" They Follow?**

① Singers, sports players and movie actors often become the "stars" of young people. Many middle-school students post pictures of their favourite stars on their bedroom walls or their phone screens. Is following a star good or bad for students? Different people have very different answers.

② People who think it is good say that a star can become a kind of model. A hardworking runner who wins a gold medal may push a lazy student to move. A young singer who never gives up after many failures may give lonely students courage. If students pay attention to the efforts behind the success, they can learn lessons about practice, time management and kindness. Some of my classmates started to read more simply because their favourite writer often mentions books in interviews.

③ However, there are real dangers too. Some students spend too much time on their stars. They watch every video, join every online chat group, and even buy expensive products their star advertises, just to feel "close" to him or her. Others only see the bright side and copy the clothes, the hair style or even the bad behaviour. When a star says something wrong, these fans sometimes attack anyone with a different opinion online. Such blind following is far from healthy.

④ I believe students can follow a star, but in a smart way. First, choose a star whose work really shows hard work and kindness. Second, learn from what the star does, not only what the star has. Third, keep your own life busy and colourful: study, read, exercise, spend time with family. A star should be a light at the window, not a sun that blocks our own sky. In this way, following a star becomes a way of growing up, not losing ourselves.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main question in Paragraph ①?**

A. Why some stars live very long lives.  
B. Whether it is good or bad for students to follow a star.  
C. Which singer is the most beautiful.  
D. Why middle schools have so many rules.

**2. Which of the following is mentioned as a good point of following a star?**

A. A star can become a kind of model to push students.  
B. A star always makes students feel angry.  
C. A star can replace parents' love at home.  
D. A star will lend money to poor students.

**3. According to Paragraph ③, what is a sign of blind following?**

A. Taking notes in class carefully.  
B. Buying expensive products just to feel close to the star.  
C. Saying hello to classmates every morning.  
D. Reading a book for fun after dinner.

**4. What does the writer mean by "a light at the window, not a sun that blocks our own sky"?**

A. A star should inspire us without taking over our life.  
B. A star should only appear during the day.  
C. We should close our windows when stars are on TV.  
D. Stars are not as bright as the sun.

**5. What is the writer's final opinion?**

A. Students should not follow any star at all.  
B. Students can follow a star in a smart and balanced way.  
C. Only teachers can choose stars for students.  
D. Stars must stop singing on television.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段末句 "Is following a star good or bad for students?"  
【解析】全文围绕"追星对学生是好是坏"。其他选项偏离主题。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "a star can become a kind of model."  
【解析】明星可成为榜样推动学生。B、C、D 与文意不符。故选 A。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "buy expensive products their star advertises, just to feel 'close' to him or her."  
【解析】购买昂贵产品只为亲近是盲目追星的表现。A、C、D 为正常生活内容。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】词义/句义猜测题  
【定位】第④段 "A star should be a light at the window, not a sun that blocks our own sky."  
【解析】此句比喻：明星应激励我们，但不能挡住自己的天空。A 恰当；B、C、D 为字面误解。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第④段 "students can follow a star, but in a smart way."  
【解析】作者主张在平衡基础上追星。A 全盘否定；C 由教师决定无依据；D 禁止明星唱歌过度推断。故选 B。

**词汇注释（超纲词）**  
- **advertise** [v.] 做广告；代言  
- **blind** [adj.] 盲目的
