# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should PE Be Treated as a Major Subject?**

① In most Chinese middle schools, subjects like Chinese, math and English are called "major subjects." They get the first classes of the morning, the biggest textbooks and the most careful attention from parents. PE — **physical education** — is often placed last, and even more often "borrowed" when a major subject runs out of time. But should PE really stay in a smaller corner? Many people now believe the answer is no.

② Some parents still worry that too much PE may hurt study. In their eyes, every hour on the playground is an hour not spent on math problems. A mother in Guangzhou once told a newspaper, "After forty minutes of running, my son is too tired to open his book." Such worries are honest, but they may come from old habits rather than real facts.

③ Recent research tells a different story. In 2025, a large study in five cities followed over 3,000 middle school students. It found that students who had **at least 45 minutes of hard PE activity** every day actually scored higher on their yearly math and English exams than classmates with fewer PE lessons. Exercise helps the brain get more oxygen, sleep more deeply, and remember new words more quickly. A well-used playground is, in this sense, also a good library.

④ Besides the brain, PE also trains the heart. On the running track, students learn what "patience" really means — one small step after another, even when the legs burn. In a basketball team, they learn to trust their teammates, pass the ball at the right moment, and smile again after a missed shot. Such lessons are hard to find in any paper test, yet they shape the kind of adult a child will become.

⑤ Of course, making PE a major subject does not mean testing every student like an athlete. A fair school should offer many choices — running, swimming, ping-pong, even traditional dance — and allow students with weaker bodies to grow at their own speed. Teachers should focus on **progress**, not on who is the fastest. Students who learn to enjoy any one kind of sport will usually keep a healthy habit for the rest of their lives.

⑥ A strong body, a clear mind and a kind heart — this is what every parent truly wishes for a child. Math and English alone cannot build all three. When PE is treated with the same respect as the "major subjects," school becomes not only a place to pass exams, but also a place to build a life worth living.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What problem about PE is mentioned in Paragraph 1?**

A. Students often miss PE classes because they are too lazy.  
B. PE classes are too dangerous for middle school students.  
C. PE classes are often "borrowed" by major subjects.  
D. There are not enough PE teachers in the school.

**2. Why did the mother in Guangzhou worry about PE?**

A. Her son was always injured on the playground.  
B. She felt PE made her son too tired to study.  
C. She thought PE was only for boys.  
D. She was worried about the cost of sports shoes.

**3. What did the 2025 study find?**

A. Students with more PE lessons scored higher on yearly exams.  
B. Students with less sleep scored higher.  
C. PE had no link to study at all.  
D. Only running could improve the brain.

**4. What does Paragraph 4 mainly talk about?**

A. How to choose the best running shoes.  
B. How PE trains patience and teamwork.  
C. Why basketball is better than other sports.  
D. Why schools should build bigger libraries.

**5. What is the writer's opinion on PE?**

A. PE should be removed from the timetable.  
B. PE should be treated with the same respect as major subjects.  
C. Only strong students should take PE.  
D. PE should be taught only on weekends.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "even more often 'borrowed' when a major subject runs out of time"  
【解析】体育常被主科挪用。A、B、D 无依据。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "too tired to open his book"  
【解析】母亲担心体育让儿子累得无法学习。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "scored higher on their yearly math and English exams"  
【解析】研究显示每天 45 分钟运动者成绩更好。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】段落主旨题  
【定位】第④段 "learn what 'patience' really means... learn to trust their teammates"  
【解析】本段强调体育培养耐心与团队合作。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】观点态度题  
【定位】第⑥段 "treated with the same respect as the 'major subjects'"  
【解析】作者主张体育与主科同等重要。故选 B。
