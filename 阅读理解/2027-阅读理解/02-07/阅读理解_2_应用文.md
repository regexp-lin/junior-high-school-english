# 阅读理解（应用文） **School Book Exchange Market — Notice**

① Do you have old books piled up in the corner of your room? Have you ever wished for a fresh new story to read during the weekend? The student council of **No. 3 Middle School** is happy to announce the **Second School Book Exchange Market**! Bring the books you no longer read, and take home new ones — for free!

② **Event Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date | Saturday, April 24, 2027 |
| Time | 9:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m. |
| Place | Basketball court in front of the Library |
| Open to | All students, teachers and their family members |
| Cost | 100% free — no money involved |

③ **How the Exchange Works**

- **Step 1:** Bring up to **10 used books** in good condition. Textbooks, cartoons with rude words, or books with broken covers will not be accepted.  
- **Step 2:** At the entrance, a volunteer will give you a green "book ticket" for each book you hand in. One book = one ticket.  
- **Step 3:** Walk around the market and choose books you like from the long shelves. Each ticket can be traded for **one** book.  
- **Step 4:** Extra tickets can be saved and used at next year's market.

④ **Special Corners**

- **English Corner:** Novels, picture books and short stories in simple English.  
- **Science Corner:** Easy science books about animals, space and daily inventions.  
- **Chinese Classics Corner:** Poetry, fairy tales and short stories chosen by teachers.  
- **Donation Corner:** Books left here will be sent to a rural school in Guangxi Province. Please add a small note on the first page: *"To a friend I have never met."*

⑤ **Please Note**

- Please **write your name** on a sticky note inside each book before bringing it, so that old friends' names can travel with the book.  
- Do **not** buy or sell books for money anywhere in the market.  
- Eating and drinking are **not** allowed among the shelves.  
- If it rains, the market will move to the school hall. Check the weather notice on Friday evening.

⑥ For more help, please visit the Student Council Room (Room 203) during lunch break on Monday and Wednesday, or send an email to **council@no3ms.edu**. Let's make April a month of sharing — and let every good book find a new reader!

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Saturday, April 24... 9:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m."  
【解析】活动在 4 月 24 日周六上午。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "up to 10 used books in good condition"  
【解析】至多 10 本状况良好的书。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "cartoons with rude words... will not be accepted"  
【解析】含粗话的漫画不被接受。A、C、D 均可接受。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "sent to a rural school in Guangxi Province"  
【解析】捐赠区书籍寄往广西乡村学校。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "If it rains, the market will move to the school hall."  
【解析】若下雨，改至学校礼堂。故选 B。
