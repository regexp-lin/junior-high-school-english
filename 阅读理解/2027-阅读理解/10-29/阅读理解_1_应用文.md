# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School Winter Rope-Skipping Contest 2027**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · P.E. Department + Health Office**  
> **2027 Winter Rope-Skipping Contest**  
> **Warm up the cold, one rope at a time.**

The weather forecast says the first cold wind of winter will reach our city next week. Staying indoors is comfortable, but not healthy. From **Monday 16 November to Saturday 21 November 2027**, the P.E. Department and the Health Office will hold the school's largest ever **Rope-Skipping Contest**. Every student is warmly invited — no matter your speed, no matter your experience.

---

## 🏁 At a Glance

- **When:** 16 – 21 Nov 2027, 16:00 – 17:30 each day (heats); Finals on **Saturday 21 Nov, 09:00 – 11:30**.  
- **Where:** School sports hall (east wing) for Grade 7 and 9; outdoor sports ground for Grade 8.  
- **Contest categories:**
  - **A – 30-second Sprint Skipping** (solo)  
  - **B – 3-minute Endurance Skipping** (solo)  
  - **C – Team Relay** (group of 4, each skipping 45 s)  
  - **D – Double-Dutch Show** (group of 3 or 4, 2 minutes, fancy moves welcome)

---

## 📝 How to Sign Up

1. One student can sign up for **at most two categories**.  
2. Sign up by your **form teacher** or on the **school mini-app → P.E. → Rope-Skipping**.  
3. Deadline: **Monday 9 November, 18:00**. Late entries will not be accepted, except for a team whose member was sick (a short note from parents is enough).  
4. A **medical-check box** must be ticked: ask the school nurse if you are not sure. Students with heart problems, knee problems, or a broken bone in the last three months should not take part in A or B.

---

## 🎽 Rules on Competition Day

- Wear the school tracksuit and **flat, clean sports shoes**. No skate shoes, no heels, no long jewellery.  
- Long hair must be tied back. Girls may wear short hair bands but **no hairpins**.  
- **Each student brings one rope** of the correct length for her height (reach armpit when stood on). Spare ropes are available at the equipment room.  
- **No phones** on the contest floor. Parents and friends may film from the side benches only.

---

## 🧮 How the Score Works

- **Category A:** the greatest number of full skips in 30 seconds.  
- **Category B:** greatest number in 3 minutes; any stop longer than 5 seconds ends the attempt.  
- **Category C:** fastest total time of 4 × 45 s legs; a dropped rope adds 3 extra skips to the count before the next skipper starts.  
- **Category D:** three judges score 1–10 on **difficulty**, **cooperation** and **show** (music allowed).

For categories A, B and C, scores will be measured by a **digital rope counter** lent to us by the city sports office. For category D, judging notes will be handed to each team after the event.

---

## 🥇 Prizes

- **Top 1–3 per category per grade:** medal + 150-yuan bookstore voucher (top 1 only gets the voucher).  
- **Most Improved**: one student per grade whose second-round count rises most from her first round.  
- **Fair-Play Award:** two students per grade who help others during the contest (e.g. lending a rope, comforting a tearful teammate). Voted by teachers.  
- Every student who completes any category gets a **hot-ginger-water voucher** at the canteen that evening.

---

## 🩹 Safety and Kindness

- **A school nurse** will be on site every day, with cold-pack, ice spray and a first-aid kit.  
- If a student twists her ankle or feels sudden chest pain, **stop at once**. Do not be brave about hidden pain.  
- **No booing, no loud laughing** at weaker skippers. A contest is kinder than it looks: the person who drops the rope today may be the person who invites you into a team next winter.

---

## 📣 Last Words from the P.E. Office

> "A good rope is short, light, and honest. It does not lie. It shows you how much winter morning bread you ate, how long you slept, and how hard you practised with patience. We hope the sound of your small, steady rope — *ta, ta, ta* — will be the heartbeat of our school this November."

*See you at the sports hall.*

*P.E. Department + Health Office*  
*Posted 29 October 2027*
