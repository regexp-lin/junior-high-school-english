# 阅读理解（说明文） **The Amazing Octopus**

① In the deep blue ocean, few creatures surprise scientists more than the **octopus**. It does not have a hard shell, sharp teeth, or big muscles, yet it is often called "the most clever animal without a backbone." With eight soft arms and a head full of tiny miracles, the octopus can solve problems, open jars, and even recognise human faces.

② First, the octopus has **three hearts**. Two of these hearts push blood through the gills, where the blood takes in oxygen from the seawater. The third heart sends the fresh blood to the rest of the body. When an octopus is swimming fast, this middle heart actually stops working for a short while. Perhaps this is why the octopus prefers to walk along the seabed rather than swim — walking is simply less tiring.

③ The octopus is also a master of **disguise**. Its skin contains thousands of tiny colour cells, which can quickly change to match the rocks, sand or plants around it. In one second, a bright red octopus can turn into a soft grey shape, almost invisible even to a careful diver. Scientists believe the octopus can sense colour through its skin, even though its eyes see only in black and white.

④ Perhaps most amazing of all is the octopus's brain. A common octopus has about 500 million nerve cells. More than half of them are **not** in the head — they live along the eight arms. In other words, each arm has a little brain of its own. While one arm is opening a jar for food, another may be checking the sea around for danger. This "group of small minds" makes the octopus one of the cleverest invertebrates on Earth.

⑤ Octopuses have also been seen using tools. One kind, the **veined octopus**, carries two halves of a coconut shell on its arms. When danger appears, the octopus jumps inside and closes the shells above its head, like a small person hiding in a tent. Other octopuses have learned to unscrew the lid of a glass jar to reach the crab inside. In a famous aquarium in Germany, one octopus even learned to turn off the lights by shooting water at the bulb!

⑥ These quiet, soft animals do not shine like a dolphin or roar like a whale, yet they remind us that cleverness in nature can take many shapes. The next time you think of a smart creature, do not only think of a dog or a parrot — think also of a gentle octopus, changing colour silently at the bottom of the sea.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "the octopus has three hearts"  
【解析】章鱼三颗心。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "this middle heart actually stops working for a short while"  
【解析】快游时中心心脏暂停，故偏爱走路。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】段落主旨题  
【定位】第③段 "master of disguise... change to match the rocks"  
【解析】本段介绍变色伪装。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "More than half of them... live along the eight arms."  
【解析】大量神经细胞分布于八臂。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "turn off the lights by shooting water at the bulb"  
【解析】该章鱼学会射水关灯。故选 B。
