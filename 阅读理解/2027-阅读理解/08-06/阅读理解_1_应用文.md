# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Green Recycling Challenge 2027 — Less Waste, More School**

---

## **What Is This Challenge?**

The **City Green Recycling Challenge** is a three-month team competition among middle schools (Grade 7–9) in our city. From **October 2027** to **December 2027**, every school that joins will try to **reduce waste**, **collect recyclable items** (paper, plastic bottles, aluminium cans, used batteries), and **share green ideas** with their neighbourhood.

The money made from selling the recyclables will be used to **plant 500 young trees** along the new city river walk and to buy **reading lamps for rural schools**.

> Last year, 42 schools joined. This year, we hope to have **60**.

---

## **Who Can Join?**

- Any middle school (public or private) in the city.  
- Each school forms **one Green Team** of **10–15 students** plus **one teacher adviser**.  
- A school may also invite **Grade 6 helpers** to collect at home, but they do not count in the main team.

---

## **Three Ways to Earn Points**

| # | Task | How Points Are Earned | Max Points |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Weekly collection** | 1 kg of paper = 2 points; 1 kg of plastic = 3 points; 1 kg of cans = 5 points; **batteries are counted by piece** (2 points each, **maximum 200** per school) | 400 |
| 2 | **Creative re-use project** | Turn waste into something useful (lamp, bag, bookshelf, garden pot, toy…). Photos and a 150-word English note must be uploaded. | 300 |
| 3 | **Community lesson** | Your team gives one short lesson (15 min) on recycling to neighbours, a kindergarten, or a primary school; include a short video. | 300 |

*Extra Bonus:* every school that reaches **500 total points** gets **30 bonus points**.

---

## **Collection Rules (Please Read Carefully!)**

1. **Clean first.** Wash plastic bottles and dry paper boxes before handing them in.  
2. **No food waste, no glass.** Both items are not accepted in this challenge for safety reasons.  
3. **Used batteries must be placed in the special red box** provided by the city. They are dangerous if thrown with normal rubbish.  
4. **Weighing day** is every Friday, 15:30–17:00, at the school's main gate. A staff member from the City Clean Company will weigh and record.  
5. **No help from parents' factories or companies.** All recyclables must come from students' homes, classrooms, or street walks. Any school found using "factory help" will lose all points.

## **Prizes**

- **Top 3 schools**: a "Green School" trophy + a **1,500-yuan book budget** for the school library.

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long does the Green Recycling Challenge last?**

A. One week.  
B. Three months, from October to December 2027.  
C. One whole year.  
D. Six months.

**2. How are points for used batteries counted?**

A. 1 kg of batteries = 2 points.  
B. 2 points for each battery, with a maximum of 200 per school.  
C. Each battery gives 10 points, with no maximum.  
D. Batteries are not accepted in the challenge.

**3. Which of the following will **NOT** be accepted in this challenge?**

A. Washed plastic bottles.  
B. Clean, dry paper boxes.  
C. Glass bottles and food waste.  
D. Used batteries placed in the red box.

**4. A school's team is made up of mainly 10–15 students. Grade 6 helpers can:**

A. Be added to the main team to earn more points.  
B. Help collect items at home, but they do not count in the main team.  
C. Take charge of the whole team without a teacher.  
D. Replace the teacher adviser.

**5. If a school uses recyclables brought by a parent's factory, what will happen?**

A. The school will earn extra points.  
B. The school will lose all points in the challenge.  
C. The school will pay a large fine in yuan.  
D. The school will be banned from all city events.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】What Is This Challenge?  
【解析】2027 年 10–12 月，共三个月。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Three Ways to Earn Points 表  
【解析】每节 2 分，每校最多 200 节。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Collection Rules 第2条  
【解析】不接受玻璃与食物垃圾。A/B/D 均可接受。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Who Can Join? 第3条  
【解析】六年级可帮忙收集，但不计入主队。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Collection Rules 第5条  
【解析】工厂助力会失去全部积分（lose all points）。A 反向；C 材料未提"罚款"；D "禁止所有活动"失真。故选 B。
