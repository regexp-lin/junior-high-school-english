# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Stars Above Campus" — School Astronomy Night**

**GREENWOOD MIDDLE SCHOOL · SCIENCE CLUB**
*Look up. Dream big.*

---

**Event Poster — Summer 2027**

The Science Club of Greenwood Middle School is proud to present our yearly **Astronomy Night**: **"Stars Above Campus"**. For one evening, the football field will be turned into a small observatory, and every student is invited to come and enjoy the night sky with us.

**Date & time**

- **Friday, 23 July 2027, 19:30 – 22:00**
- Back-up rainy date: Saturday, 24 July 2027, same time
- If both nights are rainy, the event will be postponed to **August**, and the new date will be posted on the school website.

**Where**

- East Football Field (main stargazing area)
- Science Building, Room 305 (indoor talks and hot drinks)

**Programme**

| Time | Place | Activity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 19:30 – 19:45 | East Football Field | Welcome, safety rules, flashlight check |
| 19:45 – 20:15 | Room 305 | Short talk: *Stars, Planets, and Our Moon* (Mr. Lee) |
| 20:15 – 21:30 | East Football Field | Free stargazing with 3 telescopes, guided by student volunteers |
| 21:30 – 22:00 | Room 305 | Hot chocolate, Q&A, closing words |

**What you will see**

- **Jupiter** and several of its **moons**.
- The belt of **Orion** and the famous **Big Dipper**.
- A possible meteor shower after 21:00 (if the sky is clear).

**What we provide**

- 3 large telescopes.
- 30 pairs of small binoculars (first come, first served).
- Free hot chocolate and cookies in Room 305.
- A printed star map for each participant.

**What to bring**

- A warm jacket or a thin coat (the night is often 10°C colder than the day).
- A small red flashlight (NOT white — white light hurts your "night eyes").
- Your school ID card.
- A notebook and a pencil, if you want to draw what you see.

**Rules for a good night**

1. **Do not run on the field** — telescope legs and cables can be hard to see in the dark.
2. **Do not touch the telescope lens**. Fingerprints are very hard to clean.
3. **Keep your voice low**. Loud noise disturbs both the guides and the animals around the field.
4. **No phone cameras with flash**. The flash ruins everyone's night vision for 10 minutes.
5. Students who want to stay after 22:00 must be picked up by a parent at the main gate.

**How to sign up**

- Scan the green QR code on your classroom door.
- Sign up before **Monday, 19 July, 17:00**.
- Spaces are limited to **180 students**; priority will be given to students who have not joined last year.

**Questions?**
Email: scienceclub@greenwood.edu · Room 305 (Mr. Lee, after 16:00)
*Wear warm, bring friends, and don't forget to make a wish.*

**1. If it rains on Friday 23 July AND Saturday 24 July, what will happen?**

A. The event will be cancelled.  
B. The event will move to August, with a new date posted on the school website.

D. The event will start at noon.

**2. Where will Mr. Lee give his short talk?**

A. On the East Football Field.  
B. At the school gate.  
C. In Room 305 of the Science Building.  
D. In the library.

**3. Which of the following is NOT allowed during the event?**

A. Bringing a red flashlight.  
B. Using a phone camera with flash.  
C. Drawing the stars in a notebook.  
D. Drinking hot chocolate in Room 305.

**4. Why should students bring a warm jacket?**

A. Because the night can be about 10°C colder than the day.  
B. Because the football field has no lights.  
C. Because they will sleep on the field.

**5. Who has priority when signing up?**

A. Teachers of the school.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"If both nights are rainy, postponed to August..."  
【解析】改到 8 月并在官网发布。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】节目表 19:45–20:15 "Room 305"  
【解析】李老师在 305 室讲座。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】规则 4 "No phone cameras with flash"  
【解析】禁止开闪光。A/C/D 均允许。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"often 10°C colder than the day"  
【解析】夜间更冷需保暖。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"priority will be given to students who have not joined last year"  
【解析】优先给未参加过的学生。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。
