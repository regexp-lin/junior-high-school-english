# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 Autumn Photography Contest "Through My Lens"**

> **Lihua Middle School · Art & Media Department**
> **Autumn Photography Contest 2027 — "Through My Lens"**
> **Open Notice to All Students**

---

### 1. About the Contest

"Through My Lens" is a photography contest for every student of Lihua Middle School. The theme this year is: **"One quiet scene of an ordinary day."** We are looking for photos that make viewers slow down for a moment — not cool trips, not filters, not special equipment. A single plate of dumplings on a tired table, morning light on a school wall, or a grandfather feeding sparrows can all be winners.

### 2. When & Where

- **Submission dates**: **Monday 13 October – Friday 31 October 2027**
- **Exhibition dates**: **Monday 17 November – Friday 21 November 2027**
- **Main exhibition**: School Hall A, 1F (open to students 08:00–18:00)
- **Prize ceremony**: **Saturday 22 November, 14:30**, in the Main Auditorium

### 3. Three Categories

| Category | What You May Photograph |
| --- | --- |
| **A — Campus Life** | Everyday moments at Lihua: classrooms, corridors, PE lessons, library |
| **B — Family & Home** | Quiet scenes at home: meals, grandparents, a shared bedroom |
| **C — Our City** | Parks, street food, bus stops, shop windows, neighbourhood lanes |

Each student may send up to **three photos in total**, across any of the three categories.

### 4. Photo Rules

- Photos must be **taken by the student**, in the year **2027 only**.
- Basic adjustment of light and colour is allowed. **Heavy filters, AI generation, adding or removing people and objects — all NOT allowed.**
- **Faces** of classmates and other people should be photographed only with their permission. If in doubt, choose a back view.
- **No photos** taken inside bathrooms, locker rooms, or of medical situations.
- Each photo must come with:
  - A **title** (≤12 words);
  - A short **caption** (≤50 words) telling the story behind the moment;
  - Your **name, class, and contact** (email or school ID).

### 5. How to Submit

- **Online**: school portal → "Art & Media" → "Photo Contest 2027". Please upload JPG files, no larger than **10 MB** each.
- **Paper backup** (optional): one printed version (A4 or smaller), with the title and caption on the back, to Room 203 of Teaching Block B.
- Please submit **before Friday 31 October, 18:00**.

### 6. Judging

A panel of five judges will select the prize-winning photos:

- **Ms Hou Qing**, English and student-newspaper teacher
- **Mr Jiang Xin**, art teacher
- **Mr Chen Lei**, school librarian and weekend photographer
- **Two Grade 9 student judges**, chosen by lottery

They will look at:

1. **Honest feeling** — does the photo show a real moment?
2. **Composition** — is the frame calm and thoughtful?
3. **Technique** — focus, light and clarity.
4. **Short caption** — does it add to the image?

### 7. Prizes

- **Grand Award** — 1 student across all categories: a second-hand but excellent digital camera, donated by a parent; one special feature on the "Morning Breeze" newspaper.
- **Category Gold** — 1 per category: a book voucher of ¥200.
- **Category Silver** — 2 per category: a book voucher of ¥100.
- **Encouragement Award** — 10 photos chosen from first-time entrants: a small printed album.
- **Audience Choice** — 1 photo (voted during the exhibition): a family dinner voucher at a local café.

### 8. Small But Important Reminders

- **Safety first**: never lean off a balcony, walk backwards, or climb a tall structure to "get the shot".
- **Respect first**: if someone asks not to be photographed, say "sorry" and delete the photo immediately.
- **Stories first**: a clear story is worth more than a perfect sunset. One honest plate of noodles can beat ten clever but empty photos.

### 9. Contact

- **Mr Jiang Xin**, Art teacher, Teaching Block B, Room 203
- **Student organiser**: Lin Xinhe, Grade 9 Class 2
- Email: `photo2027@lihuaschool.cn`
- On-site help on submission days: ask any yellow-lanyard volunteer in Hall A.

> **"A photograph is a quiet gift from your eye to another pair of eyes. Give gifts that feel true."**
> — Lihua Art & Media Department

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 条 "The theme this year is: 'One quiet scene of an ordinary day.'"  
【解析】主题明确。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 条 "Each student may send up to three photos in total, across any of the three categories"  
【解析】每人最多三张，可跨组。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节判断题（否定型）  
【定位】第 4 条 "Heavy filters, AI generation, adding or removing people and objects — all NOT allowed"  
【解析】严禁 AI 生成及增删人物。A、B、D 均被允许。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 5 条 "Please submit before Friday 31 October, 18:00"  
【解析】截止时间即 10 月 31 日 18:00。A 为开启日；C 为颁奖；D 为展览末日。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨/写作意图题  
【定位】第 8 条三要点：安全、尊重、故事  
【解析】提醒围绕安全、尊重、真实故事三条。A、C、D 均与原意相悖。故选 B。
