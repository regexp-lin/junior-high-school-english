# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — Mid-Autumn Festival "Moon & Memory" Evening 2027**

> **Lihua Middle School · Student Festival Committee**
> **Mid-Autumn Festival "Moon & Memory" Evening — 2027**
> **A Gentle Invitation to Students, Teachers and Families**

---

### 1. About the Evening

The Mid-Autumn Festival falls this year on **Thursday, 24 September 2027**. Because the next day is still a school day, our school will hold its evening celebration on the **evening before the school holiday**, so that families can later spend the real festival at home. The evening's theme is: **"Moon & Memory — one round moon, one small story from our family."**

### 2. When & Where

- **Date**: Friday, **25 September 2027**
- **Time**: **18:30 – 20:30** (gates open at 18:00)
- **Place**: Lihua Middle School — Central Lawn + Main Auditorium (indoor back-up)
- **End of evening**: School gates close at **21:00 sharp**.

### 3. Three Quiet Corners

The evening is gentle, not loud. Please enjoy it at a soft voice. Three quiet corners are open at the Central Lawn:

| Corner | Name | What You Can Do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| North | **Moon Lantern Corner** | Make a small paper lantern with a short wish inside |
| East | **Family Recipe Corner** | Share one short mooncake story (your grandma's recipe, a relative's old kitchen) |
| West | **"One Round Moon" Poems** | Read or write a four-line poem about the moon on soft card |

There is no contest. Nothing will be marked. The only prize is the memory of the evening itself.

### 4. Performances (Main Auditorium)

- **18:40** — Guzheng: "The Moon at Home" — Lin Xinyu, Grade 9
- **19:00** — Choir: "Shui Diao Ge Tou" (English-Chinese blended version) — School Choir
- **19:20** — Short Story Reading (English): "The Pink Lantern in Grandma's Drawer" — Huang Jianing, Grade 8
- **19:40** — Family Instrumental: **A father-and-daughter duet on flute**, open stage for signed-up families
- **20:00** — Closing: A five-minute silent group moon-watching on the lawn (weather permitting)

### 5. Food & Drinks

- Each student may bring **one small mooncake** from home to share with a neighbour on the lawn. Please label it with **your class + your name**.
- Free **hot pear water** will be offered at the lawn's east side from 18:30 to 20:00.
- **No sweet drinks, no instant noodles, no fireworks** inside the campus.
- If you have food allergies, please add a small sticker (e.g. "nut", "milk") on your mooncake.

### 6. Dress Gently

- School uniform is **not required** this evening.
- We suggest **soft, warm clothes** in quiet colours. A scarf may be useful — late September evenings can be cool.
- Please wear comfortable shoes: the lawn may be slightly wet from the afternoon dew.

### 7. How to Take Part

- **Students**: simply come. No sign-up needed to enjoy the three corners.
- **Families who want to join a performance**: sign up on the school portal → "Mid-Autumn Evening" → "Open Stage", by **Monday 22 September, 18:00**.
- **Mooncake story recordings** (for next year's website): the Festival Committee will quietly record a few short stories on voice; if you would like to contribute, come to the East corner any time between 19:00 and 19:30.

### 8. A Thoughtful Reminder

The Mid-Autumn Festival has always been a festival of **"returning"** — returning to family, returning to memory, returning to a round table under a round moon. This evening at school is not a replacement for that homecoming. It is a small **preparation** — a chance to step out of homework, look up, and notice the shared moon together one evening earlier, before you meet the same moon with your own family the next day.

### 9. Contact

- **Ms Jiang Yue**, Festival Committee Teacher, Room 308, Teaching Block B.
- Email: `midautumn2027@lihuaschool.cn`
- On the night: a blue-vest student help team at Central Lawn Entrance A.

> **"The moon can be seen from many windows, but the same moon is only shared by those who remember."**
> — Lihua Student Festival Committee

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. On which date will the Mid-Autumn "Moon & Memory" Evening be held at the school?**

A. Thursday, 24 September 2027.  
B. Friday, 25 September 2027.  
C. Monday, 22 September 2027.  
D. Saturday, 26 September 2027.

**2. At which corner can students **make a small paper lantern with a short wish inside**?**

A. The Family Recipe Corner in the east.  
B. The Moon Lantern Corner in the north.  
C. The "One Round Moon" Poems Corner in the west.  
D. The Main Auditorium stage.

**3. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed at the evening on campus?**

A. Wearing comfortable shoes.  
B. Bringing one small, labelled mooncake from home.  
C. Bringing instant noodles and setting off fireworks.  
D. Drinking free hot pear water.

**4. When is the deadline for a family to sign up for the Open Stage performance?**

A. 25 September, 18:00.  
B. 22 September, 18:00.  
C. 24 September, 21:00.  
D. 18 September, 12:00.

**5. What does the "thoughtful reminder" (paragraph 8) mainly want to tell students?**

A. That the school evening fully replaces the family reunion at home.  
B. That the school evening is a small preparation — a chance to step out of homework and notice the shared moon together, before meeting it with family the next day.  
C. That students should skip their family reunion to come to school.  
D. That the Mid-Autumn Festival is no longer important.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 条 "Date: Friday, 25 September 2027"  
【解析】活动日即 9 月 25 日星期五。A 为真正的中秋节；C 为报名截止；D 无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 条 "North — Moon Lantern Corner — Make a small paper lantern with a short wish inside"  
【解析】北角做许愿灯笼。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节判断题（否定型）  
【定位】第 5 条 "No sweet drinks, no instant noodles, no fireworks inside the campus"  
【解析】泡面与烟火均被禁。A、B、D 均被允许。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 7 条 "sign up on the school portal ... by Monday 22 September, 18:00"  
【解析】截止时间为 9 月 22 日 18 时。其余均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨/写作意图题  
【定位】第 8 条 "It is a small preparation — a chance to step out of homework, look up, and notice the shared moon together one evening earlier"  
【解析】活动是家宴前的小小预演，而非替代。A、C、D 均与原意相反或不符。故选 B。
