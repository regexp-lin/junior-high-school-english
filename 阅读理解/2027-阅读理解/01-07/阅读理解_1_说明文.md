# 阅读理解（说明文）

**The Importance of Sleep**

① Sleep is not wasted time. It is a natural part of daily life. When you sleep, your body and mind work quietly to prepare you for a new day. Many students believe they can study longer by cutting sleep, but research shows that good sleep improves grades, memory, and mood in clear and lasting ways.

② During sleep, your heart rate slows and your muscles relax. Your body repairs itself: small cuts heal faster, and your **immune** system becomes stronger so you can fight off common illness. Growth hormone is released more at night, which matters especially for teenagers who are still growing taller and need steady energy for school sports.

③ Sleep also helps your brain store what you learned at school. Studies find that people remember new vocabulary and math steps better after enough rest. Without enough deep sleep, attention drops and careless mistakes increase the next morning. In other words, losing sleep can make the same homework take longer and feel harder.

④ Health experts say teenagers usually need about eight to ten hours of sleep each night, yet many get far less because of homework, exams, and screens. Going to bed at a similar time each day, keeping the bedroom dark and quiet, and avoiding heavy meals or exciting games right before bed can help you fall asleep faster and wake up more refreshed.

⑤ If sleep stays poor for weeks, you may feel sleepy in class, get colds more often, or feel worried without a clear reason. Treat sleep as part of your health plan, not something optional you can always trade for extra tasks. A rested mind learns faster and stays calmer than an exhausted one.

---

## 答案与解析

**56.**  
**【答案】** B  
**【题型】** 细节理解  
**【定位】** 第①段第二、三句：“Many students believe they can study longer by cutting sleep, but research shows that good sleep improves grades, memory, and mood in clear and lasting ways.”  
**【解析】** 原文明确说明研究表明充足睡眠有助于成绩、记忆和情绪，B 项与此一致。A 与原文“cutting sleep”的负面含义相反；C 与“mood”矛盾；D 文中未说晚上完全不能学习。

**57.**  
**【答案】** D  
**【题型】** 推理判断  
**【定位】** 第②段：“your immune system becomes stronger… Growth hormone is released more at night, which matters especially for teenagers who are still growing taller…”  
**【解析】** 夜间免疫系统更强、生长激素更多释放，可合理推断夜间休息对青少年成长与健康有益，D 正确。A、B 与文意相反；C 文中未说运动可替代睡眠。

**58.**  
**【答案】** A  
**【题型】** 主旨大意  
**【定位】** 第③段首句：“Sleep also helps your brain store what you learned at school.”及后文关于记忆、注意力与错误的说明。  
**【解析】** 该段中心是睡眠对学习、记忆与第二天注意力的影响，A 概括恰当。B、C 把细节或无关内容当主旨；D 与段中“Without enough deep sleep…”矛盾。

**59.**  
**【答案】** B  
**【题型】** 词义猜测  
**【定位】** 第②段：“your immune system becomes stronger so you can fight off common illness.”  
**【解析】** 由“fight off common illness”可推 immune 与抵御疾病相关，B 正确。A、C、D 与上下文逻辑不符。

**60.**  
**【答案】** C  
**【题型】** 写作意图  
**【定位】** 第⑤段：“If sleep stays poor for weeks, you may feel sleepy… get colds more often… Treat sleep as part of your health plan…”  
**【解析】** 作者说明长期睡眠不足的后果并呼吁把睡眠纳入健康计划，C 准确概括写作目的。A 未提名人；B“always”过于绝对；D 与“feel worried”矛盾。

---

**词汇注释：** **immune** *adj.* 免疫的（与身体抵抗疾病有关）
