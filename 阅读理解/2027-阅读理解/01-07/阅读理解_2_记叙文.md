# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**My First Time Riding a Bike**

① I still remember the summer afternoon when my dad brought home a shiny blue bicycle. It was a little tall for me, but he said I would grow into it soon. I touched the cold metal and imagined riding fast like the older kids in our neighborhood. That picture in my mind made my fingers tighten on the handlebars before I even started.

② Learning began on the quiet path near the river. Dad held the seat while I pedaled. Every few meters I wobbled and stopped, my heart beating wildly. “Look ahead, not at your feet,” he repeated calmly. His voice sounded steadier than my legs, and somehow that helped me try again each time I failed.

③ On the third afternoon, something changed. When I looked at the trees in the distance instead of the ground, the bicycle moved forward in a straighter line. Dad loosened his grip little by little until I realized he was walking beside me, not holding me up. A mix of fear and excitement filled my chest, as if the air itself had become lighter.

④ Then I fell. My knee hurt and my eyes watered, but Dad helped me stand and checked the bike first for scratches—then laughed softly and said, “Falling is part of learning.” I took a deep breath, wiped my face, and climbed back on because I did not want the fear to win.

⑤ That evening I rode two full circles without help. The wind touched my face, and pride rose inside me like warm water. That day I learned that courage is not having no fear at all, but choosing to move forward even when your knees shake and your path is still new.

## 答案与解析

**56.**  
**【答案】** B  
**【题型】** 细节理解  
**【定位】** 第①段：“I touched the cold metal and imagined riding fast like the older kids… That picture in my mind made my fingers tighten on the handlebars…”  
**【解析】** 作者想象像大孩子一样快骑，既期待又紧张，因而握把更紧，B 合理。A 与“a little tall”不符；C、D 文中无依据。

**57.**  
**【答案】** D  
**【题型】** 词义猜测  
**【定位】** 第②段：“Every few meters I wobbled and stopped, my heart beating wildly.”及学骑车的语境。  
**【解析】** 学骑车时频繁停下、心跳很快，说明车身不稳、左右晃动，D 最贴切。A、B、C 与“stopped”及学骑情境不符。

**58.**  
**【答案】** B  
**【题型】** 推理判断  
**【定位】** 第③段：“When I looked at the trees in the distance instead of the ground, the bicycle moved forward in a straighter line.”  
**【解析】** 看远处后车子走得更直，可推断朝远看有助于平衡与方向，B 正确。A 与“not holding me up”矛盾；C、D 无文本支持。

**59.**  
**【答案】** A  
**【题型】** 细节理解  
**【定位】** 第④段：“Dad helped me stand and checked the bike first for scratches—then laughed softly…”  
**【解析】** 父亲先检查自行车是否有擦痕，A 正确。B、C、D 在文中均未出现或与事实不符。

**60.**  
**【答案】** C  
**【题型】** 主旨大意  
**【定位】** 全文从学骑车、摇晃、松手、摔倒到结尾“courage… move forward even when your knees shake”。  
**【解析】** C 概括恐惧、摔倒与勇气，贴合主题。A、B 以偏概全；D 未在文中讨论。
