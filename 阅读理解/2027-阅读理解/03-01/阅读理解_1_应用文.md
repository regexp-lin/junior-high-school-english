# 阅读理解（应用文） **Campus Musical Show — Open Auditions**

① Dear students, the **Blue Sky Middle School Art Centre** is excited to announce the **4th Campus Musical Show**, this year's title: **"The Little Lamp on the Hill"**. It is a warm story about students who run a small reading club for village children. We need singers, dancers, actors and backstage helpers — perhaps you are one of them!

② **Quick Facts**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Show night | **Friday, 14 May 2027**, 19:00 – 20:30 |
| Place | School Grand Theatre (1F) |
| Audition day | **Saturday, 20 March 2027**, 9:00 – 16:00 |
| Audition room | Art Centre, Room 206 |
| Open to | All Grade 7 – 9 students |

③ **Roles we are looking for**

1. **Main actors (8 students)** — Prepare a 2-minute English monologue (you may use the sample on the school website).  
2. **Singers (10 students)** — Sing one verse + chorus of any English song (no more than 2 minutes).  
3. **Dancers (12 students)** — Perform a 90-second dance in any style. Music will be provided by you on a USB.  
4. **Backstage team (15 students)** — Lights, sound, costumes, posters. No performance needed; just a short interview.

④ **How to sign up**

- Scan the **QR code** on the Art Centre notice board.  
- Fill in your name, class, chosen role, and the song or monologue title.  
- Upload a **60-second self-introduction video** in English or Chinese.  
- Deadline: **18 March, 18:00**. Late forms will not be accepted.

⑤ **Audition day — What to bring**

- Student ID card  
- A **water bottle** (no drinks with strong colour)  
- Comfortable clothes and shoes (dancers: bring soft shoes)  
- A USB with your music (if needed)  
- A friendly smile

⑥ **What NOT to bring**

- Strong perfume  
- Mobile phones on loud mode  
- Food with heavy smell

⑦ **Rehearsal schedule (after selection)**

- 10 April – 10 May: every **Tuesday & Thursday**, 16:30 – 18:00  
- Full rehearsal: **12 May**, 14:00 – 17:00  
- Dress rehearsal: **13 May**, 18:30 – 20:30

⑧ **Contact**  
- Ms. Sun (Director) — Art Centre, Room 201  
- Email: bluesky-musical@bs-ms.edu.cn  
- Phone (14:00 – 17:00 on school days): 021-5520-8899

**Lights up, hearts open — come and share your voice on the stage! — Blue Sky Art Centre**
