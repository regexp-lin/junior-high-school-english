# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 Autumn Mental-Health Week**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Psychology Office + Student Union**  
> **2027 Autumn Mental-Health Week**  
> **"Your feelings are not your homework. Put them down for a while."**

The days are getting shorter. Exams are coming. Many of us are secretly carrying a small, heavy bag of **feelings** — worry before tests, fights with a best friend, an empty seat left by a grandparent. From **Monday 26 October to Saturday 31 October 2027**, our school will hold its first **Mental-Health Week**. Every student is welcome. No form, no queue, no mark.

---

## 🗓 Daily Events — One Topic a Day

| Day | Date | Theme | Main Activity | Place |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | 26 Oct | "I Am Tired" | 20-min opening assembly; feeling-card wall | School hall |
| Tue | 27 Oct | "Friends and Small Fights" | Role-play workshop (Grade 7 and 8) | Music Room |
| Wed | 28 Oct | "Before the Exam" | Breathing & study-plan clinic | Library |
| Thu | 29 Oct | "Family" | Open writing: **"A letter I never sent"** | Art Room |
| Fri | 30 Oct | "After a Loss" | Quiet hour + teacher-led sharing | Green Garden |
| Sat | 31 Oct | "Look, I Am Stronger" | Student art show + closing tea | School hall |

Every activity lasts **45 minutes**. You may join **one, several, or all** of them. No attendance is taken.

---

## 🛋 The "Warm-Corner" Room (Open All Week)

- **Where:** Building C, Room 106, next to the teachers' tea room.  
- **When:** 12:10 – 13:30 (noon break) and 16:20 – 17:40 (after class).  
- **Who:** Our school counsellor **Ms. Yang** + two trained senior student volunteers.  
- **What:** a small sofa, a soft blanket, warm water, paper, colour pens. You may talk, or just sit, or draw. Whatever is said there stays there.

If you prefer to **write** rather than **talk**, there is a blue **"Post-Box of Feelings"** on the library wall. Ms. Yang reads it every evening and will reply in writing within three days.

---

## 📘 Small Things You Can Do — For Yourself and Others

1. **For yourself.** Sleep before 23:00 this week. Take a 10-minute walk after dinner. If you feel overwhelmed, tell one trusted adult today, **not tomorrow**.  
2. **For classmates.** Say "Good morning" to someone who usually eats alone. Offer a seat on the bus. Remember: a kind word is a free gift that may change a whole day.  
3. **For family.** Ask a parent or grandparent, "**How was your day?**" and actually listen for 60 seconds before speaking. It is a small question with a long echo.

---

## 🚫 What This Week Is NOT

- This week is **not a mental-health test**; there is no pass or fail.  
- It is **not a list of students' private problems** for anyone to read — everything in the Warm-Corner Room is kept private unless your safety is at risk.  
- It is **not a performance**; you do not need to "look positive" in public. Quiet, tired feelings are also welcome.

---

【题型】细节理解题

**2.**
