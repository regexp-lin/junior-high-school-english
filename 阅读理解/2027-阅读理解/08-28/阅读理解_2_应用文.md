# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — "My Family Recipe" Cooking Contest 2027**

---

## **One Dish, One Family Story**

Dear Grade 7–9 students,

Our school is holding its **first** student cooking contest, **"My Family Recipe" 2027**. We want every student to learn one dish from their own family — grandmother's sweet rice balls, father's tomato noodles, or a quiet Sunday breakfast from a small village. Then bring that dish, and its story, to the Life Kitchen on **Saturday, December 5, 2027**.

- **Theme:** *"A Dish, A Family, A Story."*  
- **Date:** Saturday, December 5, 2027, **9:00–15:00**  
- **Place:** **Life Kitchen** (Room 301, Art Building) + **Main Hall** for tasting  
- **Open to:** All Grade 7–9 students  
- **Fee:** **20 yuan** per student (covers raw materials at school; bring your own special family ingredient if you wish)

---

## **How It Works**

1. **Sign up online** at `cook.lihua-ms.cn` **before Nov 20**. Choose one of **four groups**:
   - **A. Light & Healthy** (soups, salads, steamed dishes)
   - **B. Noodles & Rice** (family noodles, fried rice, dumplings)
   - **C. Sweet Moments** (cakes, puddings, sweet soups)
   - **D. "One Pot" Home Dish** (braised meat, vegetable stews, etc.)

2. **Write a one-page "Family Recipe Card"** (in Chinese or English) before the contest, telling:
   - Who in your family taught you this dish,
   - Why the dish is important to your family,
   - The step-by-step recipe (6–10 steps).  
   Hand in the card at sign-in on Dec 5.

3. **Cook on the day** — you will have **70 minutes** at your cooking station. Only **one dish** per student; no outside help during cooking time.

4. **Share in the Main Hall** — at 13:00, finished dishes are brought to the Main Hall. Teachers, parents and classmates taste a small portion. The contest judge team decides the winners by **14:30**.

5. **Closing & prizes** at 15:00.

---

## **Rules and Safety**

- Students must wear **long trousers**, **closed shoes**, and a **simple apron** (we have extra aprons).  
- Long hair must be tied back; **no loose sleeves** near the gas.  
- **No outside pre-cooked food.** Bringing a chopped onion from home is fine; a full cooked dish is not.  
- Students may use up to **2 pieces** of kitchen equipment from home (e.g. a special bowl or rolling pin). Bring them in a labelled cloth bag.  
- Only **three kinds** of sharp knives are allowed: the school's vegetable knife, the school's small bread knife, and the school's fruit knife. Other blades are not permitted.  
- Every station has a teacher on duty. If fire, spilled oil or a small cut happens, stop and call the teacher right away.

---

## **Judges & Scoring**

| Part | Weight |
|---|---|
| Taste and balance | 40% |
| Cooking skill and safety | 25% |
| Family story (from the Recipe Card) | 20% |
| Presentation on the plate | 15% |

> Three judges will be invited: **Ms. Fu** (our Life Kitchen teacher), **Mr. Hong** (a local chef and father of a Grade 9 student), and **Ms. Li** (a food writer from the city newspaper).

---

## **Prizes**

- **Top 3 in each group:** a small cookbook and a wooden spoon with their name.  
- **Overall "Most Loving Dish" Award** (voted by parents): a family dinner voucher at a local restaurant, and a short interview in the school magazine.  
- **Best Recipe Card Award:** a beautifully bound family cookbook, printed with your card as the first page.  
- **All participants** who finish and share their dish receive **4 service-learning hours** and a "Little Cook" certificate.

---

## **House Notes**

- If you cannot come on Dec 5 because of **illness**, please tell the office before **Dec 3**; your 20-yuan fee will be returned.  
- Photography is welcome, but please **no flash** near the gas stations.  
- After the contest, any leftover food will be packed and given to the **Sunshine Old People's Home** in our district.  
- The school **will not** keep any photos of students on its public page without your family's permission.

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When will the "My Family Recipe" Cooking Contest be held in 2027?**

A. November 20, from 9:00 to 15:00.  
B. December 3, all day.  
C. December 5, from 9:00 to 15:00.  
D. December 15, in the afternoon only.

**2. Xiao Fang plans to cook her grandmother's dumplings. Which group should she choose?**

A. Group A: Light & Healthy.  
B. Group B: Noodles & Rice.  
C. Group C: Sweet Moments.  
D. Group D: "One Pot" Home Dish.

**3. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed during the contest?**

A. Bringing a special rolling pin from home in a labelled cloth bag.  
B. Bringing a chopped onion from home as part of your preparation.  
C. Bringing a fully cooked plate of dumplings from home.  
D. Wearing a simple apron supplied by the school.

**4. Out of the total score, how much is the **Family Story** from the Recipe Card worth?**

A. 40%  
B. 25%  
C. 20%  
D. 15%

**5. What happens to any food that is left after the sharing in the Main Hall?**

A. It is thrown into the rubbish.  
B. It is sold in the school shop the next day.  
C. It is packed and given to the Sunshine Old People's Home.  
D. It is stored in the fridge for the next contest.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开头 "Date: Saturday, December 5, 2027, 9:00–15:00"  
【解析】日期与时段明确。A 为报名截止日，B 为病假通知截止日，D 错误。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How It Works 第 1 点 "B. Noodles & Rice — family noodles, fried rice, dumplings"  
【解析】饺子属于 B 组。A、C、D 主题不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules and Safety 第 3 条 "No outside pre-cooked food. Bringing a chopped onion from home is fine; a full cooked dish is not."  
【解析】切好的洋葱可，整盘做好的饺子不可。A、B、D 均合规。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Judges & Scoring 表 "Family story ... 20%"  
【解析】原文表格数据直接对应。A 为口味，B 为技能，D 为摆盘。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】House Notes "any leftover food will be packed and given to the Sunshine Old People's Home"  
【解析】原文明确去向。A、B、D 均与原文不符。故选 C。
