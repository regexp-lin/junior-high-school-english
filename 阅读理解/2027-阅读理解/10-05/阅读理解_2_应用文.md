# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 Autumn English Speech Contest "My Small City, My Big Story"**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · English Department + Student Union**  
> **Notice: 2027 Autumn English Speech Contest**  
> **Theme — "My Small City, My Big Story"**

We are proud to announce the 14th Lihua English Speech Contest. This year, we invite every student to look at the place you live — your **street, bus stop, park, corner shop or your own flat building** — and turn one small true observation into a **3-minute English speech**.

---

## 🎤 Key Dates

| Stage | Date | Place |
|-------|------|-------|
| **Script deadline** | **Friday 16 Oct**, 17:00 | Hand in to your English teacher |
| **Class round** | **Mon 19 Oct – Fri 23 Oct** | Your own classroom |
| **Grade semi-final** | **Wed 28 Oct**, 15:40 – 17:30 | Lecture Hall (Building B, 2F) |
| **School final** | **Sat 7 Nov**, 14:00 – 17:00 | Main Auditorium |

*Please arrive 15 minutes early at every stage for sound check.*

---

## 📝 Script Rules

1. **English only.** You may write your first draft in Chinese, but the speech itself must be delivered in English.
2. **Length:** between **280 and 340 words** — enough for about 3 minutes of clear speaking.
3. **Theme:** choose **one small, real thing from your daily life** in this city. For example: a kind bus driver, an old snack shop, a new river walk, the quiet morning market, your grandfather's tiny garden on a balcony. **No general topics like "I love my country" — too large, too familiar.**
4. **Sources must be honest.** If you quote a number, a poem line or a news fact, write the source at the end of your paper script.
5. **Absolutely no AI-generated speeches.** Work found to be fully produced by AI will be removed from the contest. Using an AI to check **grammar of your own sentences** is allowed and must be marked with a small "grammar-check" note.

---

- **"Best Story" Award** ×3 — your speech will be recorded and broadcast on the school radio.
- **"Best Pronunciation" Award** ×3 — a one-hour 1-on-1 English club lesson with Ms. Harper (our Australian teacher).

---

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When is the last time to hand in a speech script?**

A. Before the class round on October 19.  
B. Friday, October 16, at 17:00.  
C. Any time before the school final on November 7.  
D. The morning of the semi-final.

**2. Which of the following speech topics would best fit the contest theme?**

A. "I love my great country."  
B. "The future of world peace."  
C. "The old snack shop on my street corner."  
D. "A summary of the history of English."

**3. What is the school's policy on AI in the speech contest?**

A. Students may submit a fully AI-written speech if they say so.  
B. AI tools cannot be used in any way at all, including grammar checking.  
C. Fully AI-generated speeches are removed; using AI to check the grammar of your own sentences is allowed, but must be noted.  
D. Only Grade 9 students may use AI freely.

**4. What does a student receive who wins **First Prize** in the school final?**

A. Only a wooden bookmark.  
B. A one-hour 1-on-1 lesson with Ms. Harper.  
C. Bluetooth headphones.  
D. A 5-book English novel set plus a free one-day trip to the City University English Summer Camp in July 2028.

**5. A Grade 8 student wants her pronunciation checked by Ms. Harper. According to the notice, what is true?**

A. She should go to Room 308 on Tuesday lunchtime.  
B. She should go to Language Lab 2 on Wednesday or Friday afternoons between 16:00 and 17:00, with her draft, first-come-first-served.  
C. She can only see Ms. Harper during the school final.  
D. She must phone Ms. Harper at home.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Key Dates "Script deadline — Friday 16 Oct, 17:00"  
【解析】截稿时间为 10 月 16 日 17:00。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Script Rules 第 3 条 "one small, real thing ... an old snack shop ... No general topics like 'I love my country' — too large"  
【解析】题目要求"身边小事"。A 被原文明确列为反例；B、D 过大过抽象。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Script Rules 第 5 条 "no AI-generated speeches ... Using an AI to check grammar of your own sentences is allowed and must be marked"  
【解析】不允许全 AI 代写，可用 AI 校语法但须注明。A、B、D 均不符。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Prizes "First Prize ×2 — English novel set (5 books) + a free one-day trip to the City University English Summer Camp (July 2028)"  
【解析】原文明列一等奖奖品。A、B、C 均为其他奖项。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Help for You "Ms. Harper, Wed & Fri afternoons (16:00 – 17:00), Language Lab 2. First-come-first-served; bring your draft."  
【解析】Harper 老师的地点、时间、方式均明确。A 把写作辅导与发音混淆、C 过迟、D "打电话"无依据。故选 B。
