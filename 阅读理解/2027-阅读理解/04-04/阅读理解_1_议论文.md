# 阅读理解（议论文） **Is Online Shopping Better Than Shopping in Real Stores?**

① In the last ten years, **online shopping** has grown from a new idea to a daily habit. A Grade 9 student can order a pair of trainers on Friday night and wear them to school on Monday. Her mother can refill the whole kitchen without leaving the sofa. Still, many parents, shopkeepers, and teachers ask an old question in a new way: **is shopping online really better than walking into a real shop?**

② Supporters of online shopping list three strong reasons. First, it is **fast and easy**. With just a few clicks, we can buy anything from a pencil to a TV, and most goods arrive within two or three days. Second, online shops are usually **cheaper**, because they do not pay for big streets, bright lights, or many shop assistants. Third, online shopping gives us **more choices**. A small mountain village can enjoy the same books, clothes, and gifts as a big city, which makes life fairer.

③ However, others are not so happy. They say real shops offer things the internet cannot send. A customer can **touch a fabric**, try on a jacket, or smell a new book. A salesperson can answer questions face to face. Shopping together also becomes a **family event** — mothers and daughters picking shoes on a Saturday afternoon, fathers and sons choosing a new basketball. These small moments do not fit into a mobile phone screen.

④ There are also deeper worries. When more people shop online, many small shops may close, and whole streets can become quieter and emptier. Delivery also brings **plastic bags, foam boxes, and long truck trips**, which hurt the environment. Young shoppers may click "Pay" again and again without really thinking, and one day find that their pocket money is all gone before the end of the month.

⑤ So, which way is the winner? In my view, **there is no need to choose only one**. For daily goods — rice, paper, printer ink — online shopping is faster and often cheaper. But for clothes we care about, for books we want to flip through, and for fresh fruit our grandparents can feel in their own hands, a real shop can still win. The best shoppers mix both, using each for what it does best.

⑥ In the end, shopping is about more than just getting things. It is about saving time, **protecting family life**, supporting neighbours, and keeping the earth healthy. If we remember these goals, then whether we click a button or walk through a shop door, we are making a wise choice.

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main question of the article?**

A. How to open a new online shop.  
B. Whether online shopping is better than shopping in real stores.  
C. Why mobile phones are popular with kids.  
D. How to save money in a supermarket.

**2. Which of the following is NOT given as an advantage of online shopping?**

A. It is fast and easy.  
B. It is usually cheaper.  
C. It gives people more choices.  
D. It lets us touch and smell things before buying.

**3. What concerns do some people have about online shopping?**

A. It makes phones more expensive.  
B. It may close small shops and hurt the environment.  
C. It makes people walk too much.  
D. It only sells toys.

**4. What is the writer's main suggestion?**

A. People should never shop online.  
B. Children should not buy anything.  
C. Mix both online and real-shop shopping, and use each for what it does best.  
D. Only the elderly should shop in real stores.

**5. The phrase "**these goals**" in the last paragraph refers to _______.**

A. saving time, protecting family life, helping neighbours, and keeping the earth healthy  
B. making friends through an app  
C. buying more clothes every month  
D. opening more large shopping malls

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段末问句  
【解析】全文围绕在线购物 vs. 实体购物。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段  
【解析】能摸能闻是实体店优势，非在线优势。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "small shops may close... hurt the environment"  
【解析】小店倒闭与环境问题为主要担忧。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "there is no need to choose only one... mix both"  
【解析】建议两种方式结合。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】词义猜测题  
【定位】第⑥段 "saving time, protecting family life, supporting neighbours, and keeping the earth healthy"  
【解析】these goals 指上述四点。故选 A。
