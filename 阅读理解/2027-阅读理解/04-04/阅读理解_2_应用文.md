# 阅读理解（应用文） **Community Flea Market — Save, Share, Smile**

---

**Green Sunshine Community Flea Market**  
*Because one student's "no longer wanted" is another student's "just perfect".*

---

**1. What is it?**

A one-day street market where students and families **sell, swap, or give away** second-hand books, toys, sports goods, and school items they do not use anymore. All money raised goes to **"Little Lamp"**, a local charity that helps children in mountain schools.

---

**2. Date & Place**

- **Date:** Sunday, 18 April 2027  
- **Time:** 2:00 p.m. – 6:00 p.m.  
- **Place:** Sunshine Community Square (next to Gate 3, Green River Park)

---

**3. Stall (booth) Rules**

- Each stall is **free**, but you must fill in an online form by **Friday, 16 April, 6:00 p.m.**  
- Each family can have **only one** stall. If more than one student in the family wants to join, share a stall.  
- You must bring your own **small table, cloth, and notebook** for sales.  
- All sellers should come before **1:30 p.m.** to set up.  
- Please price each item clearly. **Reasonable bargaining** is welcome; shouting is not.

---

**4. What You May / May Not Sell**

| ✅ Allowed | ❌ Not allowed |
|----------|-------------|
| Used story books, comics | Torn or dirty books |
| Toys, puzzles, board games | Electric goods without cords |
| Small sports items (jump ropes, tennis balls) | Any food with cream or meat |
| Handmade bookmarks, drawings | Live pets or plants |
| Clean clothes in good shape | Sharp knives or lighters |

---

**5. Fun Stations**

- **Book Corner:** Swap a book for a book — no money needed!  
- **Story Tent:** A teacher reads stories for kids, 3:00 – 3:30 p.m.  
- **Kindness Wall:** Write a small message and **give it with a gift** to someone you do not know.  
- **Clean-up Team Sign-up:** The last 10 students who help clean the square will each get a **"Green Star" T-shirt**.

---

**6. Charity Part**

Sellers may choose:

1. **Keep all your earnings** for your next school project.  
2. **Donate 50%** of your earnings to "Little Lamp" and get a **thank-you card** signed by a mountain school child.  
3. **Donate 100%** and your name will be placed on the community **"Sunshine Helpers" wall** for a month.

---

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Where will all the money raised at the flea market go?**

A. To the community office as salary.  
B. To a charity called "Little Lamp" that helps mountain-school children.  
C. To buy new toys for the school library.  
D. To the teacher who plans the event.

**2. Which of the following may students NOT sell at the market?**

A. A clean jump rope.  
B. A second-hand comic book in good shape.  
C. A handmade bookmark.  
D. A lighter from home.

**3. When must a family sign up for a stall?**

A. Before Friday, 16 April, 6:00 p.m.  
B. On Sunday morning at 10:00 a.m.  
C. On Sunday at 1:30 p.m.  
D. No sign-up is needed.

**4. What is special about the "Book Corner"?**

A. You can only read books there, not take them home.  
B. Books can be swapped for books without paying money.  
C. All books there are bought by the teacher first.  
D. Books are sold only to foreign visitors.

**5. How can a seller get a name on the "Sunshine Helpers" wall?**

A. By arriving before 1:30 p.m.  
B. By shouting the loudest at the stall.  
C. By donating 100% of the day's earnings.  
D. By selling food with cream.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第1节 "money raised goes to 'Little Lamp'..."  
【解析】善款流向山区慈善。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第4节 "Not allowed... Sharp knives or lighters"  
【解析】打火机不被允许。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第3节 "by Friday, 16 April, 6:00 p.m."  
【解析】截止周五晚6点。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第5节 "Swap a book for a book — no money needed"  
【解析】书角可以书换书。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第6节 "Donate 100%..."  
【解析】全额捐款名字登墙。故选 C。
