# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — "Every Wall Tells a Story" Mural Project 2027**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Art Department + School Office**  
> **Campus Mural Project: "Every Wall Tells a Story"**  
> **A school-wide call for student painters, writers and photographers**

Some of our school's outdoor walls are **tired, grey, and forgotten**. This autumn, we are going to change six of them. The Art Department is opening a campus-wide project to let students paint, write and photograph **six large murals** that tell the story of Lihua Middle School — from its first day in 1957 to its future.

---

## 🖼️ The Six Walls

| Wall No. | Location | Theme |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Gate of the Main Building | "Our School, 70 Years Old" — key moments 1957 → 2027 |
| **2** | Outside the Canteen | "Hands that Feed Us" — cooks, farmers, delivery uncles |
| **3** | Back of the Library | "Reading Is a Small Journey" — books and travellers |
| **4** | East wall, Playground | "We Run, We Grow" — sport, courage, teamwork |
| **5** | Outside Music Room | "Every Song Is a Friend" — songs from 70 years |
| **6** | Near Bicycle Shed | "The Blue River Behind Our School" — local nature |

---

## 📅 Project Timeline

- **Now – 20 Oct**: Sign-up and team-forming. Each wall needs one small team (2 – 5 students).  
- **21 – 30 Oct**: Teams submit a **paper sketch** (A3 size, colour-pencil ok) **plus** a **120-word English caption**.  
- **1 – 5 Nov**: Teachers + three senior students review and choose **six winning teams** (one for each wall).  
- **8 – 22 Nov** (weekend afternoons, 14:00 – 17:00): painting on the walls, with tools from the Art Room.  
- **Sun, 23 Nov, 15:00**: opening ceremony; parents and alumni are warmly invited.

---

## 🎒 What Every Team Must Include

1. **At least one Grade 7 student.** (So every wall passes its story on to younger members.)  
2. **At least one student who cannot draw well.** The writing and photo parts are just as important.  
3. **One short interview.** Before painting, talk to **one teacher, cook, cleaner, or security guard** who has worked at our school for at least five years. Include one sentence from that interview in your caption.

---

## 🎨 Rules and Materials

- Paint, brushes, cloth, ladders and safety helmets are provided by the school.  
- All paint is **water-based**, so clothes can be washed. Please still wear old clothes.  
- **No pictures of political leaders, no brand logos, no hurtful cartoons of individuals.** Any such design will be removed before painting begins.  
- **AI-generated drafts are welcome at the sketch stage**, but the final paint on the wall must be done by student hands, not by tracing a computer print-out.

---

## ❓ Help and Contact

- **Mr. Deng**, School History Club, after school Wed & Fri, Room 315 — ask for old photos of our school.  
- **"Climbing-up" rule:** teams must have **at least one adult helper present** every time a ladder is used; never climb alone.

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many walls will be painted in this project?**

A. Four  
B. Five  
C. Six  
D. Ten

**2. Which of the following is a rule for **every** team?**

A. The team must have only Grade 9 students.  
B. The team must include at least one Grade 7 student and at least one student who cannot draw well.  
C. The team must have exactly 10 members.  
D. Only girls can join the team.

**3. What should the short interview (in "What Every Team Must Include") focus on?**

A. A famous painter outside the school.  
B. A teacher, cook, cleaner or security guard who has worked at the school for at least five years.  
C. Any student from another school.  
D. A TV star in the city.

**4. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed in the design?**

A. A friendly cartoon of a book travelling in a hot-air balloon.  
B. A big painting of the local river with fish and children.  
C. A drawing that includes a well-known brand logo of a sports company.  
D. A colourful song pattern based on school choir songs.

**5. What is the school's rule about AI?**

A. AI is banned completely from every stage of the project.  
B. The final painting on the wall can be done by simply tracing an AI-printed picture.  
C. AI-generated drafts are welcome at the sketch stage, but the final wall painting must be done by student hands, not by tracing a computer print-out.  
D. Only AI, not students, can design the murals.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格列出 6 面墙 "Wall 1–6"  
【解析】6 面。A、B 少、D 多。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】What Every Team Must Include 第 1、2 条 "At least one Grade 7 student ... at least one student who cannot draw well."  
【解析】两项组队要求。A、C、D 均无依据或与原文相反。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 条 "talk to one teacher, cook, cleaner, or security guard who has worked at our school for at least five years"  
【解析】采访校内老员工。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】Rules and Materials "No pictures of political leaders, no brand logos, no hurtful cartoons ..."  
【解析】品牌标识不被允许。A、B、D 均符合主题、无禁止条款。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules and Materials "AI-generated drafts are welcome at the sketch stage, but the final paint ... must be done by student hands, not by tracing a computer print-out."  
【解析】AI 可用于草图，不可描摹上墙。A、B、D 均不符。故选 C。
