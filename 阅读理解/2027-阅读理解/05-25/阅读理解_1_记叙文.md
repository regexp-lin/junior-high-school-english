# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Day I Lost My Dog in the Park**

① Dou Dou is not a big dog. He has short brown fur, one white paw, and eyes that seem to smile whenever he sees me. Mother says I have cared for him since I was seven. Last Saturday afternoon, the weather was bright and the wind was soft, so I took him to Sunshine Park. I did not know, when I closed the door, that the simplest walk could turn into the longest hour of my life.

② We entered the park from the south gate. As usual, I took off his red leash at the small grass field so that he could run freely. Dou Dou ran three small circles around me, then stopped to look at a yellow butterfly. The next second, he disappeared into the bushes like a dark-brown arrow. "Dou Dou! Come back!" I shouted. The butterfly rose into the sky, alone. My dog was gone.

③ My heart began to beat very fast. I walked into the bushes and called him again and again. Nothing — only the sound of leaves moving. I ran to the lake, to the flower garden, and even to the children's playground. I asked an old man, "Have you seen a small brown dog?" He shook his head kindly. I asked a group of girls; they only looked at each other. Tears began to push against my eyes. I thought of how he slept under my desk every night, and my chest felt tight.

④ I sat down on a bench and tried to think clearly. Where would a curious dog go? I remembered: there was a small food stand behind the library building of the park, where people often drop chicken bones. I stood up and ran. The food stand seller was closing the small window, and next to her foot, eating half a bun that he had found on the ground, was my little brown thief — **Dou Dou**.

⑤ "Is he yours?" the woman asked. I nodded, almost unable to speak. "He came over quietly," she said, "so I gave him some clean water. He looks happy enough." I said "Thank you" three times, each time louder than the last. Dou Dou walked to me, his tail shaking. I did not scold him. I only knelt down, put my cheek against his soft fur, and felt his small heart beating next to mine.

⑥ On the way home, Dou Dou walked close to me, his red leash safely back around his neck. I kept looking at him, afraid he might disappear again. That night, I wrote in my diary: "Today I lost Dou Dou for one hour. In that hour, I learned something. Sometimes we only understand how much we love somebody or some little being when they are **not** by our side. After that, we should be a little more careful, and a little more grateful, every day."

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Where did the writer take Dou Dou last Saturday afternoon?**

A. To a supermarket.  
B. To Sunshine Park.  
C. To the riverside.  
D. To his classroom.

**2. Why did Dou Dou suddenly disappear?**

A. Someone stole him.  
B. He was chasing a yellow butterfly and ran into the bushes.  
C. He was afraid of the wind.  
D. He went home alone.

**3. How did the writer feel while looking for Dou Dou?**

A. Proud and happy.  
B. His heart was beating very fast, and tears began to push against his eyes.  
C. Tired but relaxed.  
D. Bored and sleepy.

**4. Where did the writer finally find Dou Dou?**

A. At the south gate.  
B. Near the playground.  
C. At a food stand behind the library building of the park.  
D. On the bench where the writer sat.

**5. What did the writer learn from this experience?**

A. We should never take a dog to a park.  
B. We only understand how much we love someone when they are not by our side, so we should be more careful and grateful.  
C. Dogs should always eat only chicken bones.  
D. Parks are dangerous places.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "I took him to Sunshine Park."  
【解析】地点为阳光公园。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "stopped to look at a yellow butterfly... disappeared into the bushes..."  
【解析】狗狗被蝴蝶吸引钻进灌木。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题／细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "My heart began to beat very fast... Tears began to push against my eyes."  
【解析】心情焦急难过。A/C/D 均与原文相反。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "there was a small food stand behind the library building of the park..."  
【解析】在图书馆建筑后的小食摊找到。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题／推理判断题  
【定位】第⑥段 "we only understand how much we love somebody or some little being when they are not by our side..."  
【解析】体会珍惜身边所爱。A/C/D 均与原文相反。故选 B。
