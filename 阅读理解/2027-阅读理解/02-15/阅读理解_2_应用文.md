# 阅读理解（应用文） **Green Saturday — Community Clean-up Call**

① Dear students and families of Sunshine Garden Community, our community will hold a half-day **Green Saturday Clean-up** activity this April. We warmly invite middle school students (Grades 6–9) and their parents to come out, lift a trash bag, and make our neighborhood brighter.

② **Activity Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date | Saturday, April 17, 2027 |
| Time | 8:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m. |
| Meeting Point | Community Centre front yard (Gate 2) |
| Target Areas | Sunshine Road, the Small River Park and Lily Street |
| Open to | Students and parents of Sunshine Garden |

③ **Four Groups**

- **Group 1 – Paper Pickers:** Walk along the road and pick up paper, plastic bottles and small litter. Safe for all ages.  
- **Group 2 – River Guards:** Clean the banks of the Small River. (Ages 13+ with a parent)  
- **Group 3 – Little Painters:** Help paint the old grey wall near the gate with bright flowers and birds. Paint will be provided.  
- **Group 4 – Green Reporters:** Take photos, interview volunteers, and write a short English report for the community website. (Students who love English writing)

④ **What to Bring**

- A pair of work gloves (the community will also prepare extra ones).  
- A clean reusable water bottle — no plastic bottles, please.  
- A sun hat and comfortable shoes.  
- If you are in Group 2, an extra pair of shoes and socks.  
- A happy mood — it will make the morning feel shorter!

⑤ **Simple Rules**

- Please **do not** touch sharp metal or broken glass. Ask an adult volunteer to handle it.  
- **Put** recyclable bottles into the green bin, and other litter into the grey bin.  
- **Do not** pick flowers from the park, and **do not** feed the fish with food from home.  
- Lunch will **not** be provided. The activity ends before lunch, and students should go home with their parents.

⑥ **Small Gifts**

Every volunteer will receive a green cotton bag, a handmade bookmark and a "Green Little Hero" certificate. Groups that work carefully will also win extra points for the community's yearly "Family of the Year" prize in December.

⑦ To sign up, please scan the small QR code on the community's notice board before **Thursday, April 15**, or fill in a paper form at the Community Centre during office hours. One parent must sign the form for any student under 14. See you on a bright April Saturday!

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Saturday, April 17... Community Centre front yard"  
【解析】周六上午于社区中心前院。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Group 2 – River Guards... Ages 13+"  
【解析】河流组要求 13 岁以上。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "no plastic bottles, please"  
【解析】不要带塑料瓶。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Put recyclable bottles into the green bin"  
【解析】可回收瓶入绿桶。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段 "green cotton bag, a handmade bookmark and a 'Green Little Hero' certificate"  
【解析】获得三样小礼物。故选 B。
