# 阅读理解（说明文） **Giant Pandas: Why They Still Need Our Help**

① The giant panda is one of the most famous animals in China and in the world. With its black and white fur, round face and slow movements, it always looks gentle and lovely. For millions of years, pandas have lived in the bamboo forests of western China. However, for a long time they were in great danger of dying out.

② Pandas eat almost only bamboo. An adult panda can eat 20 to 40 kilograms of bamboo every day. Because bamboo does not give much energy, pandas need to eat a lot and rest a lot. If the bamboo forest becomes small, pandas have nowhere to find enough food. In the past, people cut down many trees to build roads and farms, so the forests became smaller and pandas were pushed into narrow places.

③ To protect pandas, the Chinese government has worked hard for many years. More than 60 panda nature reserves have been set up. In these reserves, hunting is not allowed, and old bamboo forests are kept safe. Scientists have also built research bases where sick or hurt pandas are treated. Young pandas born in the bases are later trained to live in the wild again.

④ Thanks to these efforts, the number of wild pandas has slowly grown. A few years ago, the giant panda was moved from "endangered" to "vulnerable" on the international list. This is good news, but experts remind us that it does not mean pandas are already safe. They still face problems such as climate change, small living areas and difficulty finding partners in the wild.

⑤ Protecting pandas is not only about one kind of animal. A healthy bamboo forest is also home to many birds, insects and small animals. When we help pandas, we help a whole natural world. Every visitor to a panda park, every donation to a reserve, and every shared picture on the Internet can add a little warmth to this big mission.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did pandas become endangered in the past?**

A. Because they could not have any babies.  
B. Because bamboo forests were cut and became smaller.  
C. Because they were too fat to move around.  
D. Because they only lived at the top of high mountains.

**2. According to Paragraph ②, why must a panda eat so much bamboo every day?**

A. Bamboo is hot and spicy.  
B. Bamboo does not give much energy.  
C. Pandas like to save food for winter.  
D. Pandas eat bamboo only when they are bored.

**3. What have Chinese scientists done in research bases?**

A. Taught pandas to speak English.  
B. Sold pandas to foreign zoos for money.  
C. Treated sick pandas and trained young ones for the wild.  
D. Changed pandas' fur into different colors.

**4. What does the word "vulnerable" in Paragraph ④ probably mean?**

A. Totally safe and growing fast.  
B. Still easily hurt, but a little better than before.  
C. Completely gone from the wild.  
D. Suitable for keeping as a home pet.

**5. What is the writer's main idea in the last paragraph?**

A. Pandas are not important to the forest.  
B. Only the government can protect pandas.  
C. Saving pandas helps protect a whole natural world.  
D. People should stop visiting panda parks.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段末句 "people cut down many trees... the forests became smaller and pandas were pushed into narrow places."  
【解析】森林被砍伐是大熊猫面临危险的主要原因。A 不能生育无依据；C 太胖属无中生有；D 与"竹林"不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Because bamboo does not give much energy, pandas need to eat a lot..."  
【解析】竹子所含能量低，故需大量进食。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "sick or hurt pandas are treated. Young pandas... are later trained to live in the wild again."  
【解析】研究基地负责治疗受伤/生病的熊猫并培训小熊猫回归野外。A、B、D 均无依据。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】词义猜测题  
【定位】第④段 "the giant panda was moved from 'endangered' to 'vulnerable'... experts remind us that it does not mean pandas are already safe."  
【解析】从"濒危"降到该词，表示情况稍好但仍易受伤。A 完全安全与"not already safe"矛盾；C 完全消失与"数量增长"矛盾；D 当宠物与保护语境不符。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段 "When we help pandas, we help a whole natural world."  
【解析】作者强调保护熊猫即保护整片自然。A 与"竹林是多种动物的家"矛盾；B 片面否定其他力量；D 与"每一次参观都是温暖"相反。故选 C。

- **endangered** [adj.] 濒危的  
- **vulnerable** [adj.] 易受伤害的
