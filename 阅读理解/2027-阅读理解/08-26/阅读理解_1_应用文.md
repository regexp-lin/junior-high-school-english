# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — Grade 8 Spring Outdoor Camp 2027**

---

## **Three Days, Three Mountains, One New You**

Dear Grade 8 students and parents,

From **Wednesday, April 21** to **Friday, April 23, 2027**, all Grade 8 students will take part in our school's yearly **Spring Outdoor Camp**. This is not a holiday trip — it is a short, carefully-planned school programme that links geography, team-building and basic outdoor skills.

- **Where:** Mount Qingyun Educational Base (about 90 minutes by bus from school)  
- **Who:** All Grade 8 students + 28 teachers + 2 school nurses  
- **Cost:** 480 yuan per student (includes bus, food, insurance, and two guide books)  
- **Bus departure:** 7:30, School West Gate, Apr 21. Please arrive by 7:10.

---

## **Three-Day Programme**

| Day | Morning | Afternoon | Evening |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 1 (Apr 21, Wed) | Bus to camp + check-in | Walk the 4-km Yellow Path; rock-and-plant notes | Class teacher meeting, early rest |
| Day 2 (Apr 22, Thu) | **Teamwork Station Course** (6 stations) | Mini river study (water test, map-drawing) | **Evening sky watch** (stars and planets) |
| Day 3 (Apr 23, Fri) | Clean-up task + group reflection | Closing ceremony, back to school by 16:30 | (Home evening, no class) |

> The **Teamwork Station Course** includes rope bridge, low wall, memory map, blind walk, bucket transport and trust fall practice. **All stations are safe and insured.**

---

## **What to Pack**

**Must-have:**
- **Sports shoes** (well worn in), **two pairs of socks**.  
- A **small backpack** (not larger than 25 litres).  
- **Warm jacket** — mountain mornings can be cold (8–12 °C).  
- **Rain coat** or folded umbrella.  
- **Water bottle** (at least 750 ml), **sun hat**, **sun cream**.  
- Simple **wash bag**: toothbrush, towel, a small soap.  
- Your **student ID card**.

**Optional:**
- Pen + notebook (extra to the ones given).  
- Camera (no tripod).  
- One light snack per day.

**Please do NOT bring:**
- Laptops or **game consoles**.  
- Any kind of **lighter or matches**.  
- Large amounts of cash (a few small notes are fine).  
- Very bright torches (camp provides team torches).

---

## **Health and Safety**

1. Every student must hand in the **Health Declaration Form** to the class teacher **before April 14**. Students with fever, serious asthma, or a recent bone injury must talk with our school nurse before sign-up.  
2. The camp has a small clinic and a doctor on duty **24 hours**.  
3. **No student may leave the camp area alone**, for any reason, at any time.  
4. Teachers will keep every student's phone during lessons and return them at night for a 30-minute call home.  
5. If there is heavy rain on Day 2 morning, the rope bridge and river study will move to **indoor backup activities** (simple star map drawing + group board games).

---

## **Rules on the Bus and in the Rooms**

- Seat belts on at all times during the bus ride.  
- Every room holds **4 students** — no changing rooms without teacher permission.  
- **Quiet hours** from 22:00 to 6:30. No talking in the corridor after 22:00.  
- Food is not allowed in the rooms; please use the dining hall downstairs.  
- Photos of the camp are fine, but **do not post photos of other students online** unless you have their agreement.

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many days does the Spring Outdoor Camp 2027 last?**

A. 2 days.  
B. 3 days.  
C. 5 days.  
D. 7 days.

**2. Which activity will take place on the **morning** of Day 2?**

A. Bus to the camp and check-in.  
B. Clean-up task and group reflection.  
C. The Teamwork Station Course with 6 stations.  
D. Closing ceremony and return to school.

**3. Which of the following items should students **NOT** bring to the camp?**

A. A sun hat and a water bottle.  
B. Matches or a lighter.  
C. A warm jacket.  
D. A pair of well-worn-in sports shoes.

**4. By what date must the Health Declaration Form be given to the class teacher?**

A. April 10.  
B. April 14.  
C. April 21.  
D. April 23.

**5. What should parents do if they are very worried about something during the three days at camp?**

A. Drive directly to the camp and pick the child up without calling.  
B. Post the worry in the city newspaper.  
C. Call the emergency line **135-8877-0022** during the camp days.  
D. Wait until their child comes home on April 23.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开头日期 "Apr 21 – Apr 23" & 三日表格  
【解析】共三天。A、C、D 均与日程不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Three-Day Programme Day 2 Morning 栏 "Teamwork Station Course (6 stations)"  
【解析】第二天上午即团队课。A、B、D 均属其他时段。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Please do NOT bring "Any kind of lighter or matches"  
【解析】禁止带打火机或火柴。A、C、D 均为推荐携带物。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Health and Safety 第 1 条 "Health Declaration Form ... before April 14"  
【解析】原文明确 4/14 前提交。A 为缴费截止日，C、D 为营程当天。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】Checklist for Parents "An emergency line during the three days: 135-8877-0022"  
【解析】营程期间设有紧急电话。A 违反规则；B、D 均非合理做法。故选 C。
