# 阅读理解（应用文） **Children's Day 2027 — School Drama Club Performance**

**BLUE SKY MIDDLE SCHOOL · DRAMA CLUB**
*"Every Child Deserves a Stage."*

We warmly invite you to our biggest show of the year!

---

**Show title**
"**The Little Tree and the City**"
*A new short play written and performed by Blue Sky Middle School students, in memory of International Children's Day.*

**Date & time**

| Round | Date | Time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Round 1 | Tuesday, **1 June 2027** | 14:00 – 15:30 |
| Round 2 | Tuesday, **1 June 2027** | 19:00 – 20:30 |

**Place**
School Hall (Building A, 2F). Seating begins **20 minutes** before the show.

**Audience**

- All students of Blue Sky Middle School.
- Parents and younger brothers or sisters are welcome, but please note: **children under 4 years old** are not allowed in the hall, because the show uses sudden light and sound.
- Teachers and staff enter free.

**About the play**
"The Little Tree and the City" tells the story of a small tree in a busy shopping street. Workers want to cut it down because it blocks a new billboard. A group of middle school students, walking by, decide to save it. Through songs, simple dances and one quiet letter written by a child to the mayor, the tree, the children and the street learn to live together.

- **Length**: about 90 minutes, including one 10-minute break.
- **Language**: mostly Chinese, with **10 minutes of English dialogue** in the middle — perfect for our English-learning friends!

**Tickets**

- Tickets are **free** and must be collected at the School Life Office, Room 108, **from Monday 24 May to Friday 28 May, 2027**, 12:30 – 13:30 on weekdays.
- Each student may collect **up to 2** tickets (one for himself, one for a family member).
- Please bring your student ID when collecting tickets.
- Any tickets left on 28 May will be given to the school's partner hospital for children under long-term care.

**Important rules in the hall**

1. Please arrive on time. If you come later than 14:15 / 19:15, you will be asked to wait until the break.
2. Turn your phone to silent or **flight mode**. No photos with flash — the strong light hurts the actors' eyes.
3. Food and drinks (except water) are not allowed.
4. Please stay in your seat until the whole show is finished; our youngest audience members may get worried if many people stand up suddenly.

**Extra event — Meet the cast**
After each round, student actors will stand at the school gate for **15 minutes**. You may take photos and write them short messages in the big white "Autograph Book".

**Contact**

- Drama Club Leader, Grade 9 student **Chen Yi**.
- Teacher in charge: **Ms. Zhang Min**, Office 2F East.

*See you at the show — and happy Children's Day!*
