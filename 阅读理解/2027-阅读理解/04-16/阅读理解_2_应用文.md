# 阅读理解（应用文） **Campus Photo Contest — "Beauty in the Small"**

---

**Sunbird Middle School · Student Press Office**  
*A tiny leaf, a classmate's smile, a cat in the back lane — every small thing is big enough for a photo.*

---

**1. Theme**

This year our **annual student photo contest** has one special theme: "**Beauty in the Small**". We want students to look closely at the small, often-missed corners of life — a raindrop on a bike, a window with bright light, a teacher reading a book at noon. No fancy cameras, no far-away scenery is needed.

---

**2. Who Can Join?**

- Any student of **Grade 7 to Grade 9**, no experience needed.  
- Students may enter **as an individual** OR in a small team of **up to 3 people**.  
- Teachers are welcome to advise, but photos must be taken by students themselves.

---

**3. Photo Rules**

1. Each student or team may send up to **three photos**.  
2. Photos taken by **mobile phones** are just as welcome as those from cameras.  
3. Each photo must have a short English title **(no more than 10 words)** and a caption **(30–60 words)** explaining the small story behind it.  
4. Do not **add filters** that change the colour too much. A little sharpening is fine.  
5. No photos of people who have not given permission. Please ask politely before you take a picture.

---

**4. Categories**

| Code | Category | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| SM-01 | Tiny Objects | A pencil tip, a dewdrop, a paper plane |
| SM-02 | Quiet People | A student writing, a worker cleaning |
| SM-03 | Small Corners | Staircases, back lanes, bike racks |
| SM-04 | Free Theme | Anything small that moves you |

---

**5. Key Dates**

- **Opening:** Monday, 19 April 2027  
- **Submission deadline:** Friday, 14 May, 6:00 p.m.  
- **Online public vote:** 18 May – 22 May (school website)  
- **Award ceremony:** Friday, 29 May, 3:30 p.m. (School Hall)

---

**6. Prizes**

- **First Prize (1):** A mid-level camera + a photography book  
- **Second Prize (3):** A quality photo printer  
- **Third Prize (6):** A photo album with school logo  
- **"Golden Eye" Best Caption (1):** Voted by Chinese teachers  
- **"Audience Favourite" (1):** The photo with the most online votes  
- All participants will receive a **"Beauty in the Small" certificate**.

---

**7. How to Send Your Photos**

Upload your photos through the **Press Office mini-website**:  
`https://press.sunbird.edu.cn/photo2027`

Please include:

1. Your name, grade, and class.  
2. Team members' names (if any).  
3. Photo title + caption (English).  
4. A Chinese version of the caption is optional.  

> Any question? Email **press@sunbird.edu.cn** or visit Room 309 at lunch break.
