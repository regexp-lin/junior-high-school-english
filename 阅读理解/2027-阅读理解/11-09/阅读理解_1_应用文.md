# 阅读理解（应用文） **"A Ray of Winter Light" — Lihua Middle School Winter Gala 2027**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Art & Music Department + Student Union**  
> **2027 Winter Campus Gala — "A Ray of Winter Light"**  
> **A warm evening, made by us, for us.**

When the days grow shorter and the city lights come on earlier, a school has two quiet choices: let the quiet cold win, or **gather under one roof and make a little warm noise of our own**. We have chosen the second. On **Friday 18 December 2027, 18:30 – 21:00**, in the school sports hall, Lihua will hold its twelfth Winter Gala, "**A Ray of Winter Light**". Every student is welcome — to perform, to help, or simply to come, laugh, and clap.

---

## 🎭 What Kind of Items We Are Looking For

The Gala will include **18 items** in total, lasting about **2 hours 20 minutes** including short breaks.

**(A) Songs** — 6 items  
- Choir (20+ students) or small group (3–8 students) or solo.  
- One foreign-language item is welcome per evening.

**(B) Dances** — 4 items  
- Class group or small team (5–20 students).  
- Traditional Chinese, modern, street, or folk-from-home-village dances all welcome.

**(C) Instrument Performances** — 3 items  
- Solo or duet.  
- Piano, erhu, guzheng, violin, flute, percussion — new instruments warmly welcomed.

**(D) Short Drama / Cross-talk / Storytelling** — 3 items  
- 4–8 minutes each.  
- Student-written scripts preferred; a short teacher-signed approval is needed for cross-talk.

**(E) "Open Mic" Surprise Item** — 2 items  
- Reserved by the organising team; we will announce the two surprise items only on the evening.

---

## ✍️ How to Apply

1. Fill in the one-page "Gala Application Form" (outside Room 215 or on the school mini-app → Culture → Gala).  
2. Write down:
   - Title and type of your item.  
   - Number of performers and their classes.  
   - How long the item lasts (round to 30 seconds).  
   - Any special stage needs (piano, microphone stands, screen, light colour, etc.).  
3. Submit to the Student Union office, Room 215, by **Sunday 23 November, 18:00**.  
4. Each class may submit **up to 3 items**; solo singers may submit **only 1 solo** each.

---

## 🎟 Audience Tips

- The doors open at **17:45**.  
- **No fresh flower** bouquets inside the hall; handmade paper cards or small handwritten letters are perfect and safer.  
- The evening will be **recorded but not live-streamed**. Each performer can request a free copy of their own item afterwards.  
- A small **quiet corner** with warm chairs will be prepared in the back for students who feel overwhelmed by noise; a teacher will sit there all evening.

---

## 🚫 What We Cannot Accept

- Items that feature only one classmate's appearance, body shape, home town, or family background as a joke.  
- Borrowed online dance videos performed move-for-move without a teacher's prior approval.  
- Items longer than **8 minutes**; we love long work, but this is a Gala, not a concert.  
- Any use of real flame, real water, heavy powder, or sharp objects on stage.

---

## 🙋 Volunteers Needed (60 students)

- **Ushers** (20): welcome parents and guide them to their seats.  
- **Backstage helpers** (15): move chairs, tidy props between items.  
- **Light and sound** (6): trained with Mr. Wang for three afternoons in November.  
- **Photographers / Writers** (4 + 3): take photos and write two short English-Chinese reports for the school paper.  
- **Snack team** (12): prepare warm ginger tea and plain biscuits at the back table between items.

Volunteer sign-up: Room 215, by **Thu 27 November, 17:30**.

---

## 💡 Two Quiet Rules

1. On stage, **we applaud effort, not perfection**. If a classmate's voice shakes, we clap the **loudest**.  
2. After the show, whichever way our night has gone, we help **put the hall back** before we go home.

> "A Gala is not a place where the best students are shown. It is a place where a **cold December evening** is slowly turned, by our clapping and by our kindness, into a **soft, shared ray of light**. Welcome, everyone."  
> — *Student Union, 9 November 2027*
