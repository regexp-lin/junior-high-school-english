# 阅读理解（应用文） **English Short Film Festival — Poster**

① Lights, camera, action! This spring, the English Department of **Moonlight Middle School** is excited to announce the **Third English Short Film Festival**. Students and teachers are warmly invited to make, watch and vote for the best short films of the year. Come and see the stage turn into a small Hollywood!

② **Festival Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Making Period | April 1 – May 10 |
| Festival Day | Saturday, May 15, 2027 |
| Time | 2:30 p.m. – 5:30 p.m. |
| Place | School Small Theatre |
| Audience | All students, teachers and their family members |

③ **Rules for the Short Films**

- Every film must be made **in English** (Chinese subtitles are allowed).  
- Each film must be between **4 and 8 minutes** long.  
- Teams of **2 to 6 students** from Grades 6 – 9 may join.  
- Each team may pick one of three topics:  
  - *A Day I Will Never Forget*  
  - *When Robots Go to School*  
  - *Small Kindness, Big Change*

④ **Four Prizes**

- **Best Film** (1 team): A trophy, ¥500 book coupons per member, and a free English film night at the city cinema.  
- **Best Young Actor / Actress** (1 each): A leather notebook and a small camera.  
- **Best Story** (1 team): An online talk with a young filmmaker from Beijing.  
- **Most Popular by Vote** (1 team): A one-day free film workshop.

⑤ **How to Enter**

- **Step 1:** Write your team name and members on a red form.  
- **Step 2:** Write one short paragraph in English about your story idea.  
- **Step 3:** Hand the form to Mr. Chen (Room 405) before **Tuesday, March 30**.  
- **Step 4:** Send your finished film to **film@moonlightms.edu** before **Monday, May 10**, 9 p.m.

⑥ **Helpful Tips**

- Please **do not** copy scenes from famous movies; original work only.  
- You may borrow two small cameras from the library for one weekend each.  
- Teachers will not score the films — classmates will vote fairly on festival day.  
- If your film shows a classmate's face, please ask that classmate and his/her parent first.

⑦ **Important Notice**

- Phones may be used only to take notes during the festival.  
- Food is **not** allowed inside the theatre; a small snack corner is set up in the hallway.  
- If a student feels unwell, please talk to the volunteer wearing a blue cap.  
- For more help, please email **film@moonlightms.edu** or drop by Room 405 during lunch break.

We look forward to watching your stories on the big screen!

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When is the English Short Film Festival held?**

A. Saturday, March 30, 2027.  
B. Monday, May 10, 2027.  
C. Saturday, May 15, 2027.  
D. Sunday, June 1, 2027.

**2. How long must a short film be?**

A. Less than 2 minutes.  
B. Between 4 and 8 minutes.  
C. Exactly 10 minutes.  
D. More than 15 minutes.

**3. Which of the following is a correct topic to pick?**

A. *A Day I Will Never Forget.*  
B. *The Longest Movie in the World.*  
C. *Why I Hate My Parents.*  
D. *A Secret of My Math Teacher.*

**4. What will the winner of "Most Popular by Vote" receive?**

A. A new mobile phone.  
B. A one-day free film workshop.  
C. A free trip abroad.  
D. A set of expensive cameras.

**5. What is NOT allowed according to the notice?**

A. Copying scenes from famous movies.  
B. Eating food inside the theatre.  
C. Asking a classmate's parent before filming.  
D. Both A and B.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Saturday, May 15, 2027"  
【解析】电影节在 5 月 15 日。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "between 4 and 8 minutes"  
【解析】4 至 8 分钟。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段三个主题之一  
【解析】A 项为允许主题。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Most Popular by Vote... one-day free film workshop"  
【解析】最受欢迎奖含免费电影工作坊。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥⑦段  
【解析】复制名片与影厅内进食都被禁止，两者都符合。故选 D。
