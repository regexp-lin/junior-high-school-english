# 阅读理解（说明文） **Little Bees, Big Job**

① Bees are small insects that many people know well. We often see them flying around flowers on warm afternoons. For some people, a bee is just a small yellow-and-black creature that can sting. In fact, bees do a huge job for our world, and we could not live well without them.

② The most important job of bees is pollination. When a bee flies from one flower to another to drink sweet juice, called nectar, it carries tiny pieces of pollen on its legs. This pollen helps flowers grow into fruits and seeds. Apples, pears, peaches, carrots and many other foods on our table need the help of bees. Scientists say that about one third of the food we eat is connected with pollination.

③ Bees also give us honey. A honey bee visits hundreds of flowers every day and keeps the nectar in its body. Back at the hive, the nectar is changed into honey and stored in wax cells. Honey has been used as food and medicine for thousands of years. Beeswax is also used to make candles, lip balms and many other useful things.

④ However, bees are in trouble. In recent years, the number of bees has dropped in many countries. The reasons are not simple. Pesticides used on farms may hurt bees. Climate change makes flowers bloom earlier or later, so bees cannot find enough food at the right time. Many wild flowers are lost because cities and roads are growing.

⑤ Everyone can help bees in small ways. Farmers can use fewer strong chemicals. Families can plant flowers on their balconies. Students can build simple "insect hotels" with wood and bamboo. When we take care of bees, we also take care of fruits, forests and ourselves. Their small wings carry a very big responsibility.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What do bees carry on their legs when they fly from flower to flower?**

A. Small stones.  
B. Tiny pieces of pollen.  
C. Drops of rain.  
D. Pieces of sugar.

**2. According to Paragraph ②, about how much of our food is connected with pollination?**

A. All of it.  
B. About half.  
C. About one third.  
D. Only a tiny part.

**3. What is NOT mentioned as a use of bees' products?**

A. Making candles.  
B. Making lip balms.  
C. Making medicine.  
D. Making mobile phones.

**4. Which of the following is a reason for the drop in bee numbers?**

A. Bees are afraid of loud music.  
B. Pesticides on farms may hurt bees.  
C. Farmers pay bees too little money.  
D. Bees are tired of visiting old flowers.

**5. What is the writer's main message in the last paragraph?**

A. People should stop eating honey.  
B. Only scientists can save the bees.  
C. Small actions by many people can protect bees and us.  
D. Cities must be built farther away from farms.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "it carries tiny pieces of pollen on its legs."  
【解析】蜜蜂腿上携带花粉。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "about one third of the food we eat is connected with pollination."  
【解析】约三分之一。其他数字不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题（排除型）  
【定位】第③段 "Beeswax is also used to make candles, lip balms..." 及"Honey has been used as food and medicine."  
【解析】蜡烛、润唇膏、药物均被提及，手机未被提及。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Pesticides used on farms may hurt bees."  
【解析】农药伤害蜜蜂。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段 "When we take care of bees, we also take care of fruits, forests and ourselves."  
【解析】作者强调每个人的小小行动共同保护蜜蜂与地球。A 与"蜂蜜用处"矛盾；B 片面；D 过度推断。故选 C。
