# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Youth Winter Games — Ticket & Visitor Information**

① The **City Youth Winter Games** will be held at **Baixue Ice & Snow Park** from **February 6 to February 9**. Teams from 12 middle schools will join five sports. Students, parents and other fans are warmly welcomed to come and cheer.

② **Sports & Venues**

| Date | Sport | Venue |
|------|-------|-------|
| Feb 6 | Opening Ceremony & Short-track Skating | Main Ice Hall |
| Feb 7 | Figure Skating | Main Ice Hall |
| Feb 7 | Ski Cross (beginners) | South Slope |
| Feb 8 | Ice Hockey | Main Ice Hall |
| Feb 9 | Curling & Closing Ceremony | North Hall |

③ **Ticket Information**

- **Student ticket:** 20 yuan per sport (with student card).  
- **Adult ticket:** 50 yuan per sport.  
- **One-day pass** (all games that day): 80 yuan for adults, 40 yuan for students.  
- **Opening & Closing Ceremony** tickets are SEPARATE: 30 yuan each.  
- Children under 1.2 m can enter **for free** but must sit with an adult.

④ **Important Rules**

- Please arrive **at least 30 minutes before** the game.  
- Food and drinks can be bought inside the park. **Outside hot water is allowed**, but glass bottles are **not allowed**.  
- Photos are OK, but **flash lights and loud music** are forbidden during the game.  
- Please **do not step onto the ice** after the game; park staff will guide everyone to the exit safely.

⑤ **How to Get Tickets**

- Buy at the park ticket office from **9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.**  
- Or scan the QR code on the official poster and pay by mobile phone.  
- School group tickets (20+ students) can be booked by teachers at **ticket@baixuepark.com** at least one week before the games.

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many days will the Winter Games last?**

A. Two days.  
B. Three days.  
C. Four days.  
D. Seven days.

**2. On which date is the Ice Hockey game held?**

A. February 6.  
B. February 7.  
C. February 8.  
D. February 9.

**3. How much will a student pay to watch a single sport?**

A. 20 yuan.  
B. 30 yuan.  
C. 50 yuan.  
D. 80 yuan.

**4. Which of the following is allowed in the park?**

A. Glass bottles of cold drinks.  
B. Flash photos during the game.  
C. Warm water brought from home.  
D. Walking onto the ice after the game.

**5. What can a teacher do for a group of 25 students?**

A. Buy only one ticket for the whole group.  
B. Book group tickets by email at least one week in advance.  
C. Enter the park without any ticket at all.  
D. Give free lunch at the Main Ice Hall.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "from February 6 to February 9."  
【解析】共 4 天。其他选项与时间不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Feb 8 | Ice Hockey | Main Ice Hall"。  
【解析】冰球赛在 2 月 8 日。A、B、D 均不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Student ticket: 20 yuan per sport."  
【解析】学生每项 20 元。B、C、D 均为其他票种。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Outside hot water is allowed, but glass bottles are not allowed."  
【解析】自带热水可以。A 玻璃瓶禁止；B 闪光禁止；D 禁止上冰。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑤段 "School group tickets (20+ students) can be booked by teachers at ticket@baixuepark.com at least one week before the games."  
【解析】25 人属于 20 人以上的校团，教师应提前一周发邮件预订。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。
