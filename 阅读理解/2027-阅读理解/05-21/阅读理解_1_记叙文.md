# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Helping Grandma Learn to Use a Smartphone**

① Last Sunday, my grandma came out of her bedroom holding a small black box in her hand. "Lin Lin," she said, "your father bought me this new phone. He says I can make video calls to your mum, but I have pressed every button and nothing happens." Her voice sounded a little tired. I put my homework aside and sat down next to her on the sofa. "Don't worry, Grandma. I will be your teacher today," I smiled.

② First, I showed her how to unlock the screen. She pressed too gently at the beginning, and the phone did not react. Then she pressed too hard, and three apps opened at once. I laughed quietly, but I did not let her see. "Just touch it like a flower petal," I said. She tried again, softly, and the screen lit up. Her eyes opened wide. "Oh!" she whispered, as if she had just seen a bird fly out of a book.

③ Next, we started with video calls, because that was what she wanted most. Mum works in another city and only comes home once a month. I helped Grandma add Mum's number. Then I pressed the green video button. The phone rang twice, and suddenly Mum's face appeared on the screen, smiling. "Mama!" Grandma shouted, so loudly that our cat ran under the table. "I can see you! I can really see you!" Tears shone in her eyes. Mum also wiped her eyes with the back of her hand.

④ After the call, Grandma wanted to learn more. I taught her how to open the camera, how to send a voice message, and how to read the weather every morning. She held a small notebook and wrote everything down in careful Chinese characters: "Green button = answer. Red button = stop. Long press = voice." Her handwriting was slow but very neat, the same handwriting she once used when she was a primary school teacher.

⑤ By the evening, Grandma had already sent Mum four voice messages and two photos of our tomato plants. She looked at her phone the way she looked at her best students many years ago. "Lin Lin," she said, "you are very patient. You did not shout at me even once." I felt my face grow warm. When I was in the first grade, it was Grandma who had sat beside me for a whole afternoon, teaching me how to write the character "好". Today, the roles had quietly changed. I understood, for the first time, that **love travels in two directions**: it comes down from the old to the young, and then it climbs back up, step by step, year by year.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did Grandma come out of her bedroom holding the phone?**

A. She wanted to sell the phone.  
B. She could not make it work and wanted to call Lin Lin's mum.  
C. She wanted to throw it away.  
D. She wanted to give it to the cat.

**2. What happened when Grandma first tried to unlock the screen?**

A. She pressed too gently, then too hard, and three apps opened at once.  
B. She broke the screen.  
C. The phone flew out of her hand.  
D. The phone sang a song.

**3. What made Grandma shout during the video call?**

A. She saw the cat on the screen.  
B. She saw Mum's face and could really see her.  
C. The phone was too hot.  
D. The phone made a loud beep.

**4. How did Grandma remember what she had learned?**

A. She sang a little song.  
B. She asked the cat to remember it.  
C. She wrote everything down in careful Chinese characters in a notebook.  
D. She printed the instructions on the wall.

**5. What does the writer mean by "love travels in two directions"?**

A. Love only goes from parents to children.  
B. Love goes from old to young, and then back from young to old.  
C. Love goes only between friends.  
D. Love only appears during holidays.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "I can make video calls to your mum... I have pressed every button and nothing happens."  
【解析】她想视频通话但不会操作。A/C/D 均与原文相反。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "She pressed too gently... Then she pressed too hard, and three apps opened at once."  
【解析】完全吻合 A。其余均无据。故选 A。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "suddenly Mum's face appeared on the screen, smiling. 'Mama!' Grandma shouted..."  
【解析】因看到妈妈而激动喊叫。A/C/D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "She held a small notebook and wrote everything down in careful Chinese characters..."  
【解析】奶奶用笔记本把每一步记下。A/B/D 均无据。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】词义猜测题／推理判断题  
【定位】第⑤段 "it comes down from the old to the young, and then it climbs back up..."  
【解析】爱是双向的：由长辈到小辈，也由小辈回到长辈。A/C/D 均片面。故选 B。
