# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 October "Books to the Mountain" Donation Campaign**

---

> **Lihua Middle School — Volunteer Student Association**
> **Charity Campaign Notice**
> Issued: **18th September 2027**

**Dear students, teachers and parents,**

This October our school will again hold its annual charity campaign — **"Books to the Mountain" 2027**, in partnership with **Qingshan Middle School**, a small rural school in western Hunan. Last year our school sent **2 348 books** to three village schools and helped build two small reading corners. This year, the target is even bigger: **3 000 suitable books, 120 dictionaries, and a small library fund of ¥8 000**.

---

## Part 1 — What We Accept

We welcome books that are **clean, undamaged, and useful to Grade 5–9 students** in rural areas.

**Accepted:**
- **Textbooks and reference books** for maths, Chinese, English, science, history and geography (2023 edition or newer).
- **English readers** (level A1 – B1, with clear illustrations preferred).
- **Chinese-English dictionaries** (pocket or desk size, printed after 2020).
- **Children's novels, biographies, and science books** in Chinese or simple English.
- **Magazines** such as *Young Readers* or *Bilingual Times*, issues from **2024 onwards**.

**NOT accepted:**
- Books with **broken covers, torn pages or heavy pen-marks**.
- Books older than **10 years** (before 2017), especially textbooks.
- **Comic books** with adult themes.
- Heavy **encyclopedia sets** that cannot fit on small village-school shelves.

---

## Part 2 — How to Donate

### Books

- Drop your books into the **big red wooden box** next to the **school library desk**, between **Monday 5th October** and **Friday 16th October**, from **7:30 to 17:30** daily.
- If you wish to donate **more than 20 books**, please email **books@lihuams.edu.cn** at least one day ahead, and volunteers will help carry them.

### Money

- Money donations from **¥5 to ¥200** are welcome; please use the **blue QR code** posted outside the library.
- All money will be used for **new dictionaries**, **one shared printer**, and the round-trip **shipping cost**. Accounts will be posted on the school website on **30th October**.

### Letters

- With each book, you may slip in a **short hand-written letter** (30–80 words) to a student in Qingshan, telling them who you are and why you are sending this book. Letters must be in **Chinese or simple English**, and must **not** include your home address or phone number.

---

## Part 3 — Volunteer Jobs

If you would also like to **help run** the campaign, apply before **Friday 2nd October**.

- **Book-sorting team** (40 students, Grade 7–9) — sort donated books, 14:00–17:00 on **Saturday 17th October**.
- **Packing team** (30 students) — pack books into boxes, Sunday 18th October, 9:00–12:00.
- **Reporting team** (6 students) — write a report and short video log; best report printed in *Morning Breeze* newspaper.

> Volunteers must wear the school uniform and work shoes; **no open-toe sandals** in the sorting room.

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Which of the following books will NOT be accepted for the "Books to the Mountain" campaign?**

A. A 2025 English reader at A2 level with clear pictures.  
B. A 2023 Chinese-English pocket dictionary in good condition.  
C. A 2014 old history textbook with torn pages and pen-marks.  
D. A 2024 children's science novel about space.

**2. A student has 30 books to donate. What is the correct next step?**

A. Put them into the red wooden box silently, no help needed.  
B. Email **books@lihuams.edu.cn** at least one day ahead so volunteers can help carry them.  
C. Post them directly to Qingshan Middle School.  
D. Hand them to the school headmaster.

**3. What is the money donated in this campaign mainly used for?**

A. For new school uniforms in Lihua Middle School.  
B. For new dictionaries, one shared printer, and the round-trip shipping cost.  
C. For a big end-of-term party.  
D. For online video games.

**4. A Grade-8 student would like to help sort the books. By which date must he apply?**

A. Friday 2nd October.  
B. Monday 5th October.  
C. Saturday 17th October.  
D. Thursday 22nd October.

**5. Which of the following statements is TRUE according to the notice?**

A. Volunteers may wear open-toe sandals in the sorting room.  
B. A donated book may contain a 30–80 word hand-written letter in Chinese or simple English, but must NOT include the donor's home address or phone number.  
C. The school will never tell anyone how the money was spent.  
D. Only teachers, not students, may donate books.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节判断题  
【定位】Part 1 "NOT accepted ... broken covers, torn pages or heavy pen-marks; older than 10 years (before 2017)"  
【解析】2014 年老旧且破损涂画的教材属于不接受之列。A、B、D 均符合接受标准。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 2 Books 第 2 条 "more than 20 books, please email ... at least one day ahead"  
【解析】超过 20 本需提前邮件联系。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 2 Money "new dictionaries, one shared printer, and the round-trip shipping cost"  
【解析】明确列出三大用途。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 3 "apply before Friday 2nd October"  
【解析】志愿者报名截止日为 10 月 2 日周五。B、C、D 均错。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 2 Letters "short hand-written letter (30–80 words) ... Chinese or simple English ... must not include your home address or phone number"  
【解析】可附短信但不得含个人隐私。A 违反 "no open-toe sandals"；C 违反 "Accounts will be posted on school website 30 October"；D 无依据。故选 B。
