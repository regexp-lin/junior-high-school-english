# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Young Handwriting Contest — Notice**

① Dear students, the **16th City Young Handwriting Contest** is back! The City Education Office, together with the Children's Library, warmly invites middle school students to pick up a pen, sit down quietly, and show the beauty of careful handwriting. In the age of the keyboard, the pen still has a voice — come and let yours be heard.

② **Contest Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date | Saturday, May 15, 2027 |
| Time | 2:30 p.m. – 4:00 p.m. (please arrive by 2:10 p.m.) |
| Place | Reading Room 3F, Children's Library |
| Open to | Students of Grades 6 – 9 in the city |
| Entry Fee | Free |

③ **Three Groups**

- **Group A – Chinese Calligraphy (毛笔字):** Students will write a famous Tang poem on a long red paper. Brushes, ink and paper are provided.  
- **Group B – Pen Writing (中英文钢笔):** Students will copy a short article in Chinese or English. They may bring their own pen, but blue or black ink only.  
- **Group C – Creative Handwriting:** Students may design their own Chinese character or English word on a small square of white paper. Creativity and beauty count equally.

④ **Prizes**

- **Gold prize** (1 per group): A set of fine pens, ¥300 book coupons, and a printed certificate.  
- **Silver prize** (3 per group): A high-quality notebook, ¥100 book coupons and a certificate.  
- **Bronze prize** (6 per group): A nice bookmark and a certificate.  
- **Best New Idea** (1 in Group C): A personal handwriting workshop with a famous city artist.

⑤ **How to Sign Up**

- Online: Go to **www.citylib-youth.org/handwriting** and fill in the form before **Sunday, April 25**.  
- At school: Hand a paper form to Ms. Lin, the Chinese teacher, during lunch break.  
- Each student may join **one group only**. You cannot change your group later.

⑥ **Please Note**

- Students must bring their **school ID card** on the day.  
- Phones must be turned off and placed in the teacher's box at the door.  
- Please **do not** talk during the contest. A small red clock will tell you the remaining time.  
- The list of winners will be posted on the library's website on **May 22**. Prizes should be collected before **June 15** at the library.

⑦ For more information, call **0571-8266-4510** during the day, or send an email to **handwriting@citylib.org**. A good handwriting does not only show a letter — it shows a heart at peace. See you on May 15!

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Open to: Students of Grades 6 – 9"  
【解析】仅 6-9 年级可报。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Group A... Brushes, ink and paper are provided."  
【解析】毛笔字组提供工具。故选 A。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "A set of fine pens, ¥300 book coupons, and a printed certificate."  
【解析】金奖内容。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Online... fill in the form before Sunday, April 25. At school: Hand a paper form to Ms. Lin"  
【解析】两种报名方式。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段 "Please do not talk during the contest."  
【解析】比赛中禁止说话。故选 C。
