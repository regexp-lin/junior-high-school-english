> **01-03 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**The Scarf**

① It was a cold January morning. Wang Mei walked to school with her hands in her pockets. She had forgotten her scarf at home again. The wind bit her ears and neck, and she walked as fast as she could.

② At the school gate, she saw an old woman sitting on a bench. The woman was selling small items — hair clips, key chains, and a few colourful scarves. She looked cold too. Her coat was thin and old, and her fingers were red. Wang Mei felt sorry for her but walked past quickly. She had no money with her.

③ During lunchtime, Wang Mei told her friend Liu Jing about the old woman. "She looked so cold," Wang Mei said. "I wish I could help her." Liu Jing thought for a moment. "Why don't we collect some warm clothes? We can ask our classmates." Wang Mei's eyes lit up. "That's a great idea!"

④ That afternoon, they put up a notice in the classroom: "Winter Warmth Drive — Bring old coats, scarves, and gloves for people in need." By the next day, a big box was full. There were seven coats, twelve scarves, five pairs of gloves, and some hats. Even their teacher, Mr. Zhao, brought two thick sweaters.

⑤ After school, Wang Mei and Liu Jing carried the box to the school gate. The old woman was still there. When they gave her a warm coat and a red scarf, her eyes filled with tears. "Thank you, children," she said in a shaky voice. "No one has been so kind to me in a long time." Wang Mei smiled. The cold wind was still blowing, but she felt warm inside.

**注释：** bench 长椅；shaky 颤抖的

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did Wang Mei walk fast to school?
   A. She was late for class.
   B. She forgot her scarf and the wind was cold.
   C. She wanted to buy something at the gate.
   D. She was running in a race.

2. What was the old woman doing at the school gate?
   A. Waiting for her grandchild.
   B. Selling small items like hair clips, key chains, and scarves.
   C. Asking for directions.
   D. Teaching students.

3. What did Liu Jing suggest?
   A. Buy a coat from a shop.
   B. Collect warm clothes from classmates.
   C. Call the police for help.
   D. Ignore the old woman.

4. How did their classmates and teacher respond to the notice?
   A. No one brought anything.
   B. Only two students helped.
   C. Many brought clothes; the box was full the next day with coats, scarves, gloves, and hats.
   D. They complained about it.

5. What can we learn from this story?
   A. Old people should not sit outside.
   B. A small act of kindness can make a big difference and warm people's hearts.
   C. Wang Mei does not like winter.
   D. Liu Jing is a selfish person.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "She had forgotten her scarf at home again. The wind bit her ears and neck, and she walked as fast as she could."

**【解析】** 王梅忘带围巾，风很冷所以走得很快。A 项迟到、C 项想买东西、D 项参加比赛均非原文原因。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The woman was selling small items — hair clips, key chains, and a few colourful scarves."

**【解析】** 老人在校门口卖发卡、钥匙扣和围巾等小物品。A 项等孙子、C 项问路、D 项教学生均无依据。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Why don't we collect some warm clothes? We can ask our classmates."

**【解析】** 刘静建议向同学收集保暖衣物。A 项去商店买、C 项报警、D 项不管老人均非刘静的建议。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "By the next day, a big box was full. There were seven coats, twelve scarves, five pairs of gloves, and some hats. Even their teacher, Mr. Zhao, brought two thick sweaters."

**【解析】** 第二天大箱子就满了，同学和老师都捐了衣物。A 项没人捐、B 项只有两人帮忙、D 项抱怨均与原文不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The cold wind was still blowing, but she felt warm inside."

**【解析】** 故事告诉我们：小小的善举可以带来巨大的温暖和改变。A 项老人不该坐外面、C 项王梅不喜欢冬天、D 项刘静自私均与文章主旨和内容不符。故选 B。
