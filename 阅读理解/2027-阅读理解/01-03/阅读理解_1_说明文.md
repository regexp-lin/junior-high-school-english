# 阅读理解（说明文）

**Why Do We Feel Colder in Winter?**

① Winter is the coldest season of the year. But have you ever wondered why it feels so cold in winter? The answer has to do with the Earth, the sun, and how they work together.

② The Earth moves around the sun in a path called an orbit. At the same time, the Earth is tilted — it leans to one side. This tilt is about 23.5 degrees. Because of this tilt, different parts of the Earth get different amounts of sunlight at different times of the year. In winter, the part of the Earth where you live is tilted away from the sun. The sun's rays hit the ground at a lower angle. This means the sunlight is spread over a larger area, so each spot gets less heat.

③ Another reason is that winter days are shorter. The sun rises later and sets earlier. There are fewer hours of daylight. With less time in the sun, the ground and the air do not warm up as much. At night, the heat escapes quickly into space, making it even colder.

④ Wind also plays a big role. In winter, cold air from the north (in the Northern Hemisphere) moves south. This brings freezing temperatures. When the wind blows on your skin, it takes away your body heat faster. This is called "wind chill." Even if the thermometer says it is minus five degrees, it can feel much colder if the wind is strong.

⑤ So how can we stay warm? Wearing layers of clothing is one of the best ways. The air between the layers works like a blanket and keeps heat close to your body. Drinking warm water, eating hot food, and staying active also help. Understanding why winter is cold can help us prepare for it and enjoy the season!

**注释：** tilt 倾斜；orbit 轨道；angle 角度；thermometer 温度计；layer 层

**Questions:**

1. Why does the Earth have different seasons?
   A. Because the sun changes its size.
   B. Because the Earth is tilted about 23.5 degrees on its axis.
   C. Because people move to different countries.
   D. Because the moon blocks the sun.

2. In winter, what happens to sunlight?
   A. It becomes stronger.
   B. The sun's rays hit at a lower angle and are spread over a larger area.
   C. There is no sunlight at all.
   D. The sun gets closer to the Earth.

3. Why are winter days shorter?
   A. Because people go to bed earlier.
   B. Because the sun rises later and sets earlier.
   C. Because clocks are changed.
   D. Because winter has fewer months.

4. What is "wind chill"?
   A. A type of winter clothing.
   B. A kind of hot drink.
   C. The effect of wind taking away body heat, making it feel colder than the actual temperature.
   D. A weather station.

5. According to the passage, which is a good way to stay warm in winter?
   A. Wearing only one thick coat.
   B. Wearing layers of clothing so air between them keeps heat close to the body.
   C. Drinking cold water.
   D. Sitting still and not moving.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The Earth is tilted — it leans to one side. This tilt is about 23.5 degrees."

**【解析】** 地球倾斜约 23.5 度是产生四季变化的原因。A 项太阳改变大小、C 项人们迁移、D 项月亮遮挡太阳均属无中生有。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The sun's rays hit the ground at a lower angle. This means the sunlight is spread over a larger area."

**【解析】** 冬季阳光以较低角度照射地面，分散到更大面积。A 项变强与原文相反；C 项完全没有阳光过于极端；D 项太阳靠近地球不正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The sun rises later and sets earlier. There are fewer hours of daylight."

**【解析】** 冬天太阳升起晚、落下早，日照时间少。A 项人们早睡、C 项时钟调整、D 项冬季月份少均非正确原因。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "When the wind blows on your skin, it takes away your body heat faster. This is called 'wind chill.'"

**【解析】** 风寒效应是指风吹走体表热量，使体感温度比实际温度更低。A 项冬装、B 项热饮、D 项气象站均与定义不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Wearing layers of clothing is one of the best ways. The air between the layers works like a blanket and keeps heat close to your body."

**【解析】** 穿多层衣服可利用层间空气保暖。A 项只穿一件厚外套非最佳方式；C 项喝冷水与保暖相反；D 项坐着不动与"staying active also helps"矛盾。故选 B。
