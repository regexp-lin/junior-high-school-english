# 阅读理解（应用文） **Reading Month at Riverside Middle School**

① February is our school's **Reading Month**. The library and the Chinese & English teachers have worked together to prepare a rich schedule. We hope every student will find at least one activity that fits his or her interests.

② **Weekly Activities**

| Week | Date | Activity | Place |
|------|------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Feb 2 – Feb 6 | Teacher Book Talk – "Books that Changed Me" | Library (4th floor) |
| 2 | Feb 9 – Feb 13 | Class Reading Circles (student-led) | Classrooms |
| 3 | Feb 16 – Feb 20 | English Poem Writing Workshop | Room 205 |
| 4 | Feb 23 – Feb 27 | Reading Report Show & Best Report Award | School Hall |

③ **Main Rules**

- Each student is asked to read at least **one Chinese book** and **one English book** during the month.  
- The books can be novels, science books, biographies or poem collections, but **comic books and magazines do not count**.  
- Every student should write a simple **reading report** of about **150 words in Chinese** or **80 words in English** at the end of the month.

④ **Prizes**

- **Top 3 Reports**: a gift of 5 new books and a "Little Bookworm" card.  
- **Best 10 Class Reading Circles**: a class name on the library wall.  
- **Lucky Participants**: 20 students who join three or more activities will get a free ticket to the city book fair in March.

⑤ **How to Join**

- Visit the library desk before January 28 to get your **Reading Passport**.  
- Write the titles and dates of the books you read on the passport.  
- Ask a teacher or the librarian to sign it after each activity.  

For more information, please scan the QR code on your class notice board or ask the library teacher, Mr. Wang, during the lunch break on Tuesday and Friday.

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long is the Reading Month at Riverside Middle School?**

A. One week.  
B. Two weeks.  
C. The whole of February.  
D. Only the weekend.

**2. Which activity is held in Room 205?**

A. Teacher Book Talk.  
B. Class Reading Circles.  
C. English Poem Writing Workshop.  
D. Reading Report Show.

**3. Which kind of reading material does NOT count in this event?**

A. Poem collections.  
B. Science books.  
C. Biographies.  
D. Magazines.

**4. What will the "Lucky Participants" get?**

A. A gift of 5 new books.  
B. A class name on the library wall.  
C. A free ticket to the city book fair.  
D. A one-month free lunch in the dining hall.

**5. What should students do before January 28?**

A. Finish all their English reports.  
B. Pay money to the library teacher.  
C. Get a Reading Passport from the library desk.  
D. Write 10 new English poems.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "February is our school's Reading Month."  
【解析】整个 2 月均为读书月。A、B、D 与时间段不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 Week 3 "English Poem Writing Workshop | Room 205"。  
【解析】205 教室为英语诗歌写作工作坊。其余活动地点不同。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "comic books and magazines do not count."  
【解析】漫画与杂志均不算。A、B、C 均允许。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Lucky Participants: 20 students who join three or more activities will get a free ticket to the city book fair in March."  
【解析】幸运参与者可获城市书展免费门票。A 为前三名；B 为最佳读书会；D 无依据。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Visit the library desk before January 28 to get your Reading Passport."  
【解析】1 月 28 日前需去图书馆前台领取阅读护照。A、B、D 均无依据。故选 C。
