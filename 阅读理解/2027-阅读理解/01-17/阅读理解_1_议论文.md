# 阅读理解（议论文） **Morning Exercise or More Sleep: Which Matters More?**

① Many middle-school students face the same question every morning: should I get up early to exercise, or should I sleep a little longer? Some teachers love to tell students, "Early to bed and early to rise makes a person healthy and wise." However, some parents and doctors say that enough sleep is just as important for a growing body. The question is worth thinking about carefully.

② People who support morning exercise have strong reasons. A short run, a few jumps or a group of push-ups can wake up the whole body. After exercise, the heart beats faster, blood flows better, and the brain gets more oxygen. Many students say that they feel more awake in the first class after morning exercise. Besides, exercising in the cool morning air can help build a strong habit of moving every day.

③ On the other hand, people who prefer more sleep also make good points. Most middle-school students are between the ages of 12 and 15, and their bodies are changing quickly. Doctors say that teenagers need about nine hours of sleep each night. Students who go to bed late but get up very early may feel tired, sleepy and even a little sad during the day. A tired student cannot exercise well and cannot learn well either.

④ In my opinion, the answer is not "exercise or sleep," but "enough of both." Students should try to finish their homework earlier, turn off their phones before bed, and go to sleep by ten o'clock. Then they can get up around six forty, do ten to twenty minutes of light exercise, and still have enough sleep for their growing bodies. Health is a long race, not a short fight. Balance is the smartest plan.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main question the writer discusses?**

A. Whether students should eat breakfast every day.  
B. Whether morning exercise or more sleep is more important.  
C. Whether students should go to school alone.  
D. Whether parents should help with homework.

**2. According to Paragraph ②, what can morning exercise do for students?**

A. Make them lose weight in one week.  
B. Replace their breakfast in a hurry.  
C. Help the brain get more oxygen and feel awake.  
D. Stop them from catching colds forever.

**3. How many hours of sleep do most middle-school students need each night?**

A. About five hours.  
B. About seven hours.  
C. About nine hours.  
D. About twelve hours.

**4. What does the writer suggest students do at night?**

A. Play mobile games until very late.  
B. Finish homework early and go to sleep by ten.  
C. Watch a long movie with the whole family.  
D. Begin heavy exercise after dinner.

**5. Which sentence best shows the writer's main idea?**

A. "Early to bed and early to rise makes a person healthy and wise."  
B. "A tired student cannot exercise well and cannot learn well either."  
C. "Health is a long race, not a short fight. Balance is the smartest plan."  
D. "Many students say that they feel more awake in the first class."

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段 "should I get up early to exercise, or should I sleep a little longer?"  
【解析】作者核心在讨论晨练与睡眠之争。A、C、D 均偏离主题。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "blood flows better, and the brain gets more oxygen."  
【解析】晨练让大脑获得更多氧气并使人清醒。A 一周减肥、B 代替早餐、D 永不感冒均属过度推断。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "teenagers need about nine hours of sleep each night."  
【解析】青少年每晚约需 9 小时睡眠。其他数字与原文不符。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Students should try to finish their homework earlier... go to sleep by ten o'clock."  
【解析】作者建议尽早完成作业并十点前睡觉。A、C、D 与建议相反。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】写作意图/主旨题  
【定位】第④段末句 "Health is a long race, not a short fight. Balance is the smartest plan."  
【解析】此句最准确地表达作者"平衡"的主张。A 为引言；B 为反方论据；D 为局部例子。故选 C。
