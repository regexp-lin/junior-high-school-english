# 阅读理解（议论文） **Group Trip or Family Trip: Which Is Better for Students?**

① During the winter and summer holidays, many middle-school students travel. Some of them go with a school group or a travel company's youth tour. Others take trips with their parents. Both ways can be fun, but which is really better for students?

② Supporters of group trips say that travelling with classmates has special value. A group trip usually has a clear plan, young leaders and many team activities. Students can practise English with foreign friends, try new food together, and learn how to share a room or a bus with others. These are important social skills. If a problem happens, a young leader is there to help quickly. Parents also feel safer when a trained guide is with the group.

③ However, family trips are also very meaningful. With parents, a trip is not only about seeing new places but also about spending real time together. During the busy school year, students and parents often leave home before dawn and come back tired. A trip can be a rare chance to talk, play and even argue about small things, like what to eat for dinner. Children also learn to read maps, carry their own bags and help parents solve small problems.

④ Of course, each type of trip has some weak points. A group trip may feel too busy. You may eat too fast and then rush to the next spot. A family trip may feel a little boring for students who want to make new friends, especially if they are the only child.

⑤ In my opinion, students should try both kinds of trips during their middle-school years. A family trip helps us love our home more; a group trip helps us step into the bigger world. The "best" trip is not a fixed answer, but the mix that brings both warm memories and new growth.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main question of this passage?**

A. Which country is the most popular for Chinese tourists.  
B. Whether group trips or family trips are better for students.  
C. How to save money for winter holidays.  
D. Why airports are always crowded.

**2. Which of the following is mentioned as a good point of group trips?**

A. Students can skip all their homework.  
B. Students can practise English and learn to share with others.  
C. Students get free lunch every day.  
D. Students can choose any hotel they like.

**3. Why may a family trip be especially important for students?**

A. Because parents always pay for expensive toys.  
B. Because it gives a rare chance to spend real time with parents.  
C. Because it lets students skip school for a month.  
D. Because it is always cheaper than a group trip.

**4. What weak point of a group trip does the writer mention?**

A. The hotels are too dirty.  
B. Students eat too fast and rush from spot to spot.  
C. The buses are always late.  
D. Parents never allow such trips.

**5. What is the writer's final opinion?**

A. Only family trips can really help students grow.  
B. Group trips and family trips should both be tried.  
C. Middle-school students should not travel at all.  
D. Students must travel only with foreign friends.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段末句 "which is really better for students?"  
【解析】文章围绕团游与家游的比较。A、C、D 均偏离主题。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Students can practise English... learn how to share a room or a bus with others."  
【解析】团游能锻炼英语和分享能力。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "A trip can be a rare chance to talk, play and even argue..."  
【解析】家游提供与父母相处的宝贵时间。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "A group trip may feel too busy. You may eat too fast and then rush..."  
【解析】团游节奏紧凑。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑤段 "students should try both kinds of trips during their middle-school years."  
【解析】作者主张两类旅行都应体验。A、C、D 与原文相反。故选 B。
