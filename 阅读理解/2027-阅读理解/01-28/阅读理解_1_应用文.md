# 阅读理解（应用文） **Spring Break Photo Contest — Call for Works**

① Sunshine English Weekly is happy to invite all middle-school students to take part in our **Spring Break Photo Contest**. Use your phone or a simple camera to catch a beautiful moment during the Spring Festival holiday, and share the story in your own English words.

② **Main Theme:** *A Warm Moment in My Holiday*

③ **Rules**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Who | Students from Grade 7 to Grade 9 |
| Works | 1 – 3 photos per student |
| Size | Photos should be clear; no less than 1 MB each |
| Words | An English title (≤ 8 words) & a short story (40 – 80 words) |
| Send before | **February 20** (the contest starts February 6) |

④ **Do & Don't**

- Photos should be taken by the student himself/herself. Internet photos are **not accepted**.  
- You may edit the light or cut the edges, but **do not** change people's faces or add unreal objects.  
- Please **do not** use other people's faces clearly without their permission.  
- Stories should be your own writing. You can ask a teacher to check grammar, but not to write for you.

⑤ **Prizes**

- **Golden Lens (1 winner):** a new camera bag and 1,000 yuan book card.  
- **Silver Lens (3 winners):** a photography book and 500 yuan book card.  
- **Bronze Lens (6 winners):** a set of postcards and 200 yuan book card.  
- **Popular Choice (10 winners):** chosen by online readers — a small gift.

⑥ **How to Join**

Send your photos (.jpg) and story (.doc) in one email to **photo@sunshine-en.com**. Please use your real name, class and school as the email subject. The top 50 works will be shown on our website in March. For questions, call Miss Chen at **010-6789-2345** on weekdays.

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the theme of this contest?**

A. The most expensive meal at the New Year dinner.  
B. A warm moment during the Spring Festival holiday.  
C. A special trip to a foreign country.  
D. A game between two football teams.

**2. How many photos can each student send?**

A. Only one.  
B. One to three.  
C. Four to six.  
D. As many as possible.

**3. Which of the following is NOT allowed?**

A. Using a phone to take photos.  
B. Changing the faces in a photo.  
C. Writing a 60-word story.  
D. Asking a teacher to check grammar.

**4. What will a Silver Lens winner receive?**

A. A new camera bag.  
B. A photography book and 500 yuan book card.  
C. Only a set of postcards.  
D. A trip to the countryside.

**5. When is the last day to send the works?**

A. February 6.  
B. February 14.  
C. February 20.  
D. March 1.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Main Theme: A Warm Moment in My Holiday."  
【解析】主题为"假期中的温暖时刻"。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Works: 1 – 3 photos per student."  
【解析】每人 1-3 张。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "do not change people's faces or add unreal objects."  
【解析】禁止修改人脸。A 合规；C 40-80 词范围内；D 允许老师检查语法。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Silver Lens (3 winners): a photography book and 500 yuan book card."  
【解析】银奖为摄影书和 500 元书卡。A 为金奖；C 为铜奖；D 无依据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Send before: February 20."  
【解析】2 月 20 日前提交。A 为开赛日；B、D 均不符。故选 C。
