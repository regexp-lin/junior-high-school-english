# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Planting a Willow Tree with Grandpa at Qingming**

① Every Qingming, our family goes back to the small village where Grandpa grew up. This year, on a soft, rainy morning, Grandpa woke me up with a quiet smile. "Lele, put on your old shoes. Today we are not only going to sweep the family graves. Today we are going to **plant a tree**." I was half asleep, but his voice was warm, so I got up and followed.

② Grandpa carried a **young willow tree** on his shoulder. It was thin, still a baby, with a small ball of earth around its roots. I carried a shovel bigger than my forearm, and a bottle of rice water that Mum said was "**the kindest food for any new plant**". A soft rain wet our jackets, but neither of us opened an umbrella. Grandpa said, "Good rain for planting. We should not block the sky's gift."

③ We walked along a quiet path by the stream until we reached the family burial ground on a low hill. The old cypresses stood tall and silent. Grandpa placed his hand on one gravestone for a long minute. Then he turned to me and said, "Your great-grandfather planted this cypress. He was my age when I was as young as you. Today, we plant a new one, so that in fifty years, another young hand can touch it."

④ Grandpa picked a spot near a small clearing. He showed me how to **dig a hole** wider than the root ball, how to loosen the dirt at the bottom, and why we should not press the soil too hard. "Trees, like people, need room to breathe. If we hold them too tight, they cannot grow strong." My palms got dirty. My knees got muddy. But I felt a kind of quiet joy I could not yet name.

⑤ When the willow was standing straight, Grandpa tied a bamboo stick beside it with a soft cloth. "This is his first friend," he said. "When the wind is too strong, they will lean on each other." I poured the rice water slowly, letting it sink into the earth. The rain stopped for a moment. A ray of pale sunlight came through the clouds and rested on the young leaves. It felt as if the tree was quietly saying, "**Hello, world.**"

⑥ On the way home, Grandpa told me old stories I had never fully listened to before: how he and his brothers crossed this hill every spring to collect wild vegetables; how one winter the old willow in the village square saved a neighbour's roof from a heavy snow; how trees, in our family, have always been a **silent way of remembering people**. My mind was full of pictures I had never had before.

⑦ Back at the village house, I wrote one line in my diary that evening: *"Today I planted a tree. But really, Grandpa planted a piece of memory inside me."* Qingming is often about **missing** those who are gone. But this year, for the first time, I felt it was also about **passing on** — the stories, the hands that dig, the rain that falls, and the small willows that one day will stand tall for someone else.

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What did Grandpa want to do with the writer this Qingming?**

A. Visit a big city museum.  
B. Plant a young willow tree together.  
C. Buy new shoes at the market.  
D. Take photos at the river.

**2. Why did Grandpa and the writer not open an umbrella in the rain?**

A. They left the umbrella at home.  
B. The rain was very heavy.  
C. Grandpa said the rain was a gift for planting.  
D. They were running very fast.

**3. What lesson did Grandpa give about planting trees?**

A. Trees grow faster when the soil is very tight.  
B. Trees, like people, need room to breathe.  
C. Only boys can plant trees.  
D. Trees should be planted only in autumn.

**4. Why did Grandpa tie a bamboo stick beside the young tree?**

A. To make the tree look taller in photos.  
B. So the tree and the stick can lean on each other in strong wind.  
C. To scare away birds.  
D. To show the way to Grandpa's old school.

**5. What did the writer learn about Qingming from this experience?**

A. It is only about visiting graves.  
B. It is also about passing on stories and memories to the young.  
C. It is a good day for shopping.  
D. It must be celebrated alone.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "Today we are going to plant a tree"  
【解析】爷爷计划种柳树。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Good rain for planting. We should not block the sky's gift"  
【解析】爷爷称好雨是天赐礼物。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "need room to breathe"  
【解析】树与人一样需要呼吸空间。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "When the wind is too strong, they will lean on each other"  
【解析】大风时互相依靠。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨归纳题  
【定位】第⑦段 "also about passing on — the stories... the small willows..."  
【解析】清明也关乎传承。故选 B。
