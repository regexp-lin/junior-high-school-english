# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Rainy Night Our Power Went Out**

① It was a Tuesday evening in late May. A heavy rainstorm began at dinner time. The wind pushed the rain flat against our kitchen window, and the trees outside bent like old men. I was finishing my math homework, while Mum was washing the bowls. Just then — *click* — every light in our building disappeared at the same moment. We were left in **thick, soft darkness**, with only the sound of rain.

② My little sister Niu Niu, who is six, grabbed my arm. "Jie jie, the night has swallowed the house!" she whispered. Mum laughed gently. "Don't worry," she said. "Old Mr. Rain is only playing a little joke. Sunny, please bring the big torch from the drawer." I felt my way along the wall, found the drawer handle, and opened it. My fingers touched the cold metal torch — and, to my surprise, also three small candles and a box of matches. Mum had prepared them long before.

③ Mum lit two candles on the dining table. The tiny yellow flames pushed the darkness back into the corners. For the first time in many evenings, the three of us really sat **together**, without TV, without homework, without phones. Shadows danced softly on the wall. Niu Niu took Mum's hand and said, "This is more beautiful than our big lamp." I thought so too, but I did not say it aloud.

④ Just then, we heard a soft knock. It was our neighbour Grandma Zhou, who lives alone next door. She was holding a wet umbrella. "Child, do you also have no power?" she asked. Mum quickly opened the door wider. "Please come in, Aunt Zhou. We have candles and hot water." I pulled out a warm chair for her. We shared a small plate of peanuts and two red apples. Outside the window, thunder rolled far away. Inside, the three adults talked slowly about Shanghai thirty years ago, when power failures used to last all night.

⑤ Around nine o'clock, I suggested we play a shadow game. I made a rabbit, Mum made a butterfly, Niu Niu made something that looked like "a panda sitting on a pineapple", and Grandma Zhou made a perfect flying goose. Niu Niu laughed so hard that she slid off her chair. It turned out, I realised, that we did not need WiFi to be happy.

⑥ At ten past ten, the lights suddenly came back, bright and cold. We all blinked at each other, almost disappointed. Mum blew out the candles softly. Grandma Zhou said goodbye and walked to her own door, which was only two steps away. Before closing her door, she turned and said, "Tonight was the warmest rainy night I have had in a long, long time. Thank you." I understood her feeling. Some nights teach us more in darkness than other nights do in light.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What was the weather like that Tuesday evening?**

A. Sunny and warm.  
B. There was a heavy rainstorm, with strong wind.  
C. It was snowing.  
D. It was dry and cold.

**2. What did the writer find in the drawer besides the big torch?**

A. A book and a pencil.  
B. Three small candles and a box of matches.  
C. A box of sweets.  
D. A pair of shoes.

**3. Why did Grandma Zhou come to their house?**

A. She wanted to borrow money.  
B. Her house also had no power and she came to check with them.  
C. She wanted to sell peanuts.  
D. She wanted to clean their window.

**4. What shadow animal did Grandma Zhou make on the wall?**

A. A rabbit.  
B. A butterfly.  
C. A perfect flying goose.  
D. A panda sitting on a pineapple.

**5. What does the last sentence "Some nights teach us more in darkness than other nights do in light." mean?**

A. Nights without electricity are dangerous and we should avoid them.  
B. Some special dark nights let us learn more — about family and love — than normal bright nights.  
C. People should never turn on lights at night.  
D. Light is less useful than darkness.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "A heavy rainstorm... The wind pushed the rain flat against our kitchen window..."  
【解析】暴风雨。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "three small candles and a box of matches."  
【解析】三支小蜡烛和一盒火柴。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Child, do you also have no power?"  
【解析】邻居同样停电来查看。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Grandma Zhou made a perfect flying goose."  
【解析】祖母周做了大雁。A 作者；B 妈妈；D 小牛妞。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题／主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑥段末  
【解析】某些特别的夜晚让我们学到比平常更多的温情。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
