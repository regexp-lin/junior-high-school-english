# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Young River Guardians" — City Lake Clean-Up Programme**

**CITY LAKE PROTECTION OFFICE**
*Clean water makes a kinder city.*

---

**Recruitment Notice — Summer 2027**

Every summer our office welcomes a small team of middle school students to help clean the banks of **South Lake**, in the centre of our city. This short, half-day programme is called "**Young River Guardians**". It is a great way to learn about water, the environment, and team work.

**Programme dates and choices**

| Team | Date | Time | Max. students |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| T-1 | 11 July 2027 | 07:30 – 12:00 | 30 |
| T-2 | 18 July 2027 | 07:30 – 12:00 | 30 |
| T-3 | 25 July 2027 | 07:30 – 12:00 | 30 |
| T-4 | 8 August 2027 | 07:30 – 12:00 | 30 |

Please pick only **one** team.

**Who can join?**

- Any Grade 7, 8 or 9 student who lives in the city.
- You should be able to walk 2 km on a gravel path.
- No experience needed — we will train you on the day.

**What we do on the day**

| Time | Activity |
| --- | --- |
| 07:30 – 08:00 | Arrive at South Lake East Gate, sign in, get gloves, bag and grabber |
| 08:00 – 08:30 | 30-minute training (water facts, safety rules, basic first aid) |
| 08:30 – 11:00 | Clean-up walk along the lake in 5 small groups |
| 11:00 – 11:45 | Sort rubbish (paper / plastic / glass / other) at the collection point |
| 11:45 – 12:00 | Short talk + group photo + certificate |

**What we provide**

- Thick cotton gloves and a long rubbish-grabber stick.
- A blue "Young River Guardian" vest you can keep as a gift.
- A small boxed lunch (sandwich, banana and water).
- A paper certificate with your group leader's signature.

**What to wear and bring**

- Sports clothes with long sleeves and long trousers (to protect skin from plants and insects).
- Comfortable running shoes, NOT slippers.
- A hat and sunscreen.
- One small reusable water bottle (1 L).

**What you MUST NOT do**

1. Do not walk into the water at any place along the lake. Even shallow water has sudden steep parts.
2. Do not pick up glass, dead animals, or anything sharp with your hand. Use the grabber; if unsure, call a group leader.
3. Do not feed fish, ducks, or any other animal from the lake.
4. Do not bring big electronic items — no drones, no skateboards, no wireless speakers.
5. Do not wander away from your group. If you feel tired or unwell, stop and tell a leader.

**How to sign up**

1. Ask your homeroom teacher for a green card. Fill in your name, team choice, and your parent's phone number.
2. Return the card to the school office **before Friday 2 July, 17:00**.
3. The winners will be announced on **Tuesday 6 July**, outside Classroom 3-4.
4. On the day, bring the card to the East Gate for sign-in.

**Contact**
City Lake Protection Office · 021-xxxx-4409 · guard@citylake.cn
*Clean hands. Clean lakes. Thanks for helping.*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Please pick only one team"  
【解析】每人只能选一组。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"What we provide" 列表  
【解析】午餐盒+手套+夹子+马甲。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Sports clothes with long sleeves..."  
【解析】长袖长裤+运动鞋。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"Do not walk into the water..."  
【解析】禁止下水。A/C/D 均允许或要求。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"before Friday 2 July, 17:00"  
【解析】7 月 2 日 17:00 前。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。
