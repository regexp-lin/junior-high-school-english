> **01-05 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Grandma's Kitchen**

① Every winter holiday, Zhang Hao went to his grandma's house in the countryside. It was a small village with only thirty families. There were no tall buildings, no shopping malls, and no cinemas. But Zhang Hao loved it there more than anywhere else, because of Grandma's kitchen.

② Grandma's kitchen was old but warm. There was a big wood-burning stove in the corner. When Grandma cooked, the whole house filled with wonderful smells. She made everything from scratch — noodles, steamed buns, and dumplings. Her hands moved quickly, rolling the dough and filling each dumpling with care. Zhang Hao liked to sit on a small wooden stool and watch her work.

③ "Grandma, why don't you use a machine? It would be much faster," Zhang Hao once asked. Grandma laughed softly. "Machines are fast, but they don't have love in them. When I make food by hand, I think about each person who will eat it. That makes it taste better."

④ One afternoon, Grandma taught Zhang Hao how to make dumplings. At first, his dumplings looked terrible — too fat, too thin, or falling apart. Grandma didn't laugh at him. She held his hands gently and showed him again. "Don't rush," she said. "Good things take time." By the evening, Zhang Hao had made twenty dumplings that looked almost perfect. He was so proud.

⑤ Years later, Zhang Hao grew up and moved to a big city. He could buy any food he wanted from restaurants and apps. But nothing ever tasted as good as Grandma's dumplings. Every time he missed her, he would go to the kitchen, take out some flour, and try to make dumplings by hand. They were never quite the same, but the warm feeling always came back.

**注释：** stove 炉子；dough 面团；stool 凳子；flour 面粉

---

**Questions:**

1. Where did Zhang Hao go every winter holiday?
   A. To a big city.
   B. To his grandma's house in the countryside.
   C. To a summer camp.
   D. To a foreign country.

2. Why did Grandma prefer making food by hand?
   A. Because she did not have a machine.
   B. Because she believed hand-made food has love in it and tastes better.
   C. Because machines were too expensive.
   D. Because she wanted to be slow.

3. What happened when Zhang Hao first tried to make dumplings?
   A. He made perfect dumplings right away.
   B. His dumplings looked terrible — too fat, too thin, or falling apart.
   C. He refused to try.
   D. Grandma was angry with him.

4. What does Grandma's advice "Good things take time" suggest?
   A. You should never cook.
   B. Patience and care lead to good results.
   C. Cooking is a waste of time.
   D. Only grandmothers can cook well.

5. Why did Zhang Hao make dumplings by hand in the city?
   A. Because city food was too expensive.
   B. Because he missed his grandma and the warm feeling of her kitchen.
   C. Because he wanted to open a restaurant.
   D. Because he had no money for food.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Every winter holiday, Zhang Hao went to his grandma's house in the countryside."

**【解析】** 每个寒假张浩都去乡下奶奶家。A 项大城市、C 项夏令营、D 项外国均非原文所述。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "When I make food by hand, I think about each person who will eat it. That makes it taste better."

**【解析】** 奶奶认为手工做的食物带着对家人的爱，味道更好。A 项没有机器、C 项机器太贵均无依据；D 项想要慢与原意不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "At first, his dumplings looked terrible — too fat, too thin, or falling apart."

**【解析】** 张浩第一次包饺子很不成功，形状各异、会散开。A 项一开始就完美、C 项拒绝尝试、D 项奶奶生气均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Don't rush. Good things take time."

**【解析】** 奶奶的话意味着耐心和用心才能做出好东西。A 项不该做饭、C 项做饭浪费时间、D 项只有奶奶会做饭均是曲解。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Every time he missed her, he would go to the kitchen, take out some flour, and try to make dumplings by hand. They were never quite the same, but the warm feeling always came back."

**【解析】** 张浩想念奶奶时就亲手包饺子，重温温暖的感觉。A 项城里食物太贵、C 项想开餐厅、D 项没钱买饭均属无中生有。故选 B。
