# 阅读理解（说明文）

**How Do Animals Survive the Cold Winter?**

① When winter comes, humans put on warm clothes and turn on the heating. But what about animals? They don't have coats or blankets. Yet most animals survive even the harshest winters. How do they do it?

② Some animals hibernate. Hibernation is a deep sleep that lasts for weeks or even months. During hibernation, an animal's body temperature drops, its heartbeat slows down, and it uses very little energy. Bears are famous for hibernating. Before winter, a bear eats a lot of food to store fat in its body. Then it finds a safe den and sleeps through the cold months. Frogs and some snakes also hibernate, often underground or at the bottom of ponds.

③ Other animals migrate. Migration means moving to a warmer place. Many birds fly south for the winter. For example, wild geese travel thousands of kilometres to reach warm areas. They fly in a V-shape to save energy. When spring comes, they fly back home. Some fish and butterflies also migrate.

④ Some animals neither hibernate nor migrate. Instead, they adapt to the cold. Squirrels collect and store nuts and seeds in autumn. They hide them in different places and find them later when food is hard to get. Arctic foxes grow thicker fur in winter. Their white fur also helps them hide in the snow. Penguins huddle together in large groups to share body heat.

⑤ Each animal has its own special way to deal with winter. Whether it is sleeping, travelling, or preparing, these survival strategies have developed over millions of years. They remind us how amazing nature really is.

**注释：** hibernate 冬眠；den 巢穴；migrate 迁徙；huddle 挤在一起；strategy 策略

**Questions:**

1. What happens to a bear's body during hibernation?
   A. Its temperature rises and it eats more.
   B. Its body temperature drops, heartbeat slows, and it uses little energy.
   C. It runs around to keep warm.
   D. It moves to a warmer country.

2. Why do wild geese fly in a V-shape?
   A. Because it looks beautiful.
   B. To save energy during their long journey.
   C. Because they are following a leader.
   D. To scare away other animals.

3. How do squirrels prepare for winter?
   A. They hibernate underground.
   B. They fly to a warmer place.
   C. They collect and store nuts and seeds in autumn.
   D. They build a fire.

4. What is special about the Arctic fox's fur in winter?
   A. It becomes thinner.
   B. It turns black.
   C. It grows thicker and turns white to hide in the snow.
   D. It falls off completely.

5. What is the best title for this passage?
   A. Why Bears Sleep So Long.
   B. How Animals Survive the Cold Winter.
   C. The Life of Wild Geese.
   D. Why Winter Is Dangerous.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "an animal's body temperature drops, its heartbeat slows down, and it uses very little energy."

**【解析】** 冬眠时熊的体温下降、心跳减慢、能量消耗极少。A 项体温升高和吃更多与原文相反；C 项跑来跑去、D 项迁徙均非冬眠行为。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "They fly in a V-shape to save energy."

**【解析】** 大雁以 V 字形飞行是为了节省能量。A 项好看、C 项跟随领导者、D 项吓跑其他动物均非原因。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Squirrels collect and store nuts and seeds in autumn."

**【解析】** 松鼠秋天收集并储存坚果和种子为冬天做准备。A 项冬眠、B 项迁徙、D 项生火均不是松鼠的方式。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Arctic foxes grow thicker fur in winter. Their white fur also helps them hide in the snow."

**【解析】** 北极狐冬天皮毛变厚且变白以在雪地中隐蔽。A 项变薄、B 项变黑、D 项完全脱落均与原文相反。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文分别介绍了冬眠、迁徙和适应三种动物过冬方式。

**【解析】** 文章围绕"动物如何度过寒冷的冬天"展开，B 项最能概括全文。A 项只涉及熊，以偏概全；C 项只涉及大雁；D 项"冬天为什么危险"非文章重点。故选 B。
