# 阅读理解（记叙文） **My First Camping Night With Dad**

① Last summer, Dad suddenly said at dinner, "Liang, next weekend, you and I will camp on Green Valley Hill — just the two of us." My spoon stopped in mid-air. I had never slept in a tent in my life. The idea was half exciting and half scary. Mum just smiled and said, "Take a thick jacket. Mountains are cool at night."

② We got to the hill at four in the afternoon. Dad taught me how to **pitch** the tent. I thought it would be easy — just push up a stick and throw a cloth over it. I was wrong. The poles kept slipping, the door kept facing the wrong way, and once the whole tent fell on my head like a **green jellyfish**. Dad laughed so hard that his glasses slid down. After forty minutes of sweating, we finally made a small blue home standing by a pine tree.

③ At sunset, we cooked noodles on a tiny gas stove. The smoke got in my eyes, my fingers got oil on them, and the noodles were too hot to swallow. Yet that simple bowl tasted better than any fancy dinner in town. While we ate, Dad pointed at the sky and said, "Look, the first star." I looked up — and then I saw them, one, two, and soon hundreds, brighter than anything I had seen in the city.

④ Trouble came at night. Around ten o'clock, the wind grew strong and the tent began to shake. Somewhere in the bushes, a small animal made a strange, short cry. My heart jumped. I moved closer to Dad and whispered, "Is it a wolf?" Dad smiled in the dim light and said quietly, "It's only a small deer calling its mother. Listen carefully — it is not angry, just a bit lost." I listened again. He was right. The cry sounded almost like a question.

⑤ I could not sleep for a long time. I kept thinking of Mum at home, of the warm bed in my room, of my Monday English test. Then Dad gently said, "Fear is not a bad thing, Liang. It just means you are **growing into a new place**. Breathe out slowly, listen to the wind, and feel the ground under your back." I did what he told me. Little by little, the tent stopped feeling strange. It became a small soft boat floating on the hill.

⑥ When I woke up at six, the whole valley was **silver with dew**. A bird sang somewhere above the pine tree. I opened the tent door and saw Dad already making tea. He handed me a warm cup and said, "You did well, my boy." I did not say much, but I knew something inside me had quietly changed. Home was still the safest place, but the world outside my door was a little less scary than before.

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How did the writer feel when Dad first suggested the camping trip?**

A. Totally calm and ready.  
B. Both excited and a little afraid.  
C. Bored and unwilling to go.  
D. Worried that Mum would not allow it.

**2. What happened when they tried to pitch the tent?**

A. They finished it in just a few minutes.  
B. Dad did all the work while the writer watched.  
C. They met many small problems before the tent stood up.  
D. The tent was broken and could not be used.

**3. Why does the writer say the noodles "tasted better than any fancy dinner in town"?**

A. The noodles were made with very expensive food.  
B. The writer had not eaten the whole day.  
C. They had a chef cooking for them.  
D. The simple meal in nature with Dad gave it a special flavour.

**4. What did Dad say about fear when the writer could not sleep?**

A. Fear is shameful and should be hidden.  
B. Fear means you are weak and should go home.  
C. Fear shows you are growing into a new place.  
D. Fear should be fought with loud shouting.

**5. What does the last paragraph mainly tell us?**

A. The writer decided never to camp again.  
B. The writer became braver and saw the outside world in a new way.  
C. The writer worried about the English test even more.  
D. Dad taught the writer how to make perfect tea.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "half exciting and half scary"  
【解析】既兴奋又有点害怕。A 与 "scary" 矛盾；C、D 无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段描述 "poles kept slipping"、"door wrong way"、"tent fell on head"、最后 "forty minutes of sweating"  
【解析】遇到诸多小麻烦后才搭起帐篷。A、B、D 与原文冲突。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第③段烟熏眼、油手指，但 "that simple bowl tasted better"  
【解析】强调与父亲在自然中共享的简单食物特别美味。A、B、C 无依据。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "It just means you are growing into a new place"  
【解析】恐惧意味着正在成长。A、B、D 与原文不符。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨归纳题  
【定位】第⑥段 "something inside me had quietly changed", "world outside... less scary"  
【解析】"我"变得更勇敢，看待世界的方式有所改变。A、C 与正面情绪相反；D 是细节。故选 B。
