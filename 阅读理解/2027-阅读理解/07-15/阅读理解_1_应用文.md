# 阅读理解（应用文） **Blue Ocean Aquarium — Summer Pass & Guide**

**BLUE OCEAN AQUARIUM**
*Under one roof, the whole sea.*

---

**Summer Pass Brochure — 2027**

Welcome to Blue Ocean Aquarium, the largest aquarium in our province! From **1 July to 31 August 2027**, our "Summer Pass" lets you visit as many times as you like — perfect for curious students.

**Opening hours (summer)**

| Day | Opening | Last entry |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tuesday – Friday | 09:30 – 18:00 | 16:30 |
| Saturday – Sunday & public holidays | 09:00 – 21:00 | 19:30 |
| Monday | Closed for cleaning | — |

**Summer Pass prices**

| Pass type | Visits | Price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Single-entry ticket | 1 | ¥100 (adult) / ¥60 (student) |
| Day Pass | 1 day unlimited | ¥120 / ¥70 |
| Week Pass | 7 days (continuous) | ¥240 / ¥150 |
| Summer Pass | 62 days (1 July – 31 August) | ¥380 / ¥220 |
| Family Summer Pass | 2 adults + 2 students, 62 days | ¥950 (best value!) |

*Students must show a valid school ID. Children under 110 cm enter free with an adult pass.*

**Must-see halls**

| Hall | Highlight |
| --- | --- |
| Hall A — *Sunlight Reef* | Colourful tropical fish, living corals, feeding show at 11:00 |
| Hall B — *Deep Canyon* | Giant octopuses, jellyfish, blue neon glow |
| Hall C — *Cold Waters* | Penguins, sea otters, snow machine, short talk at 14:30 |
| Hall D — *River Meets Sea* | Freshwater turtles, mangrove models, touch pool for children |
| Hall E — *Ocean Classroom* | Fossil corner, small lab, free workshops every afternoon |

**Daily schedule of key activities**

| Time | Activity | Place |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 10:30 | Morning show — dolphins and friends (20 min) | Main pool |
| 11:00 | Feeding show: tropical fish | Hall A |
| 14:00 | Penguin parade (they walk past visitors!) | Hall C |
| 15:30 | Workshop: "Make a mini jellyfish with soft paper" (20 spots) | Hall E |
| 17:00 | Talk: "Plastic in our seas — what you can do" | Ocean Theatre |

**Rules for a great visit**

1. **Do not knock on the glass.** Loud noise frightens the fish and causes real harm over time.
2. **No flash photos**, even with a phone. Animals may become disoriented.
3. **Food and drink** only in the Ocean Cafe on the first floor. Water in a closed bottle is fine.
4. In Hall D, children may touch the starfish in the shallow pool **with one clean finger**. No grabbing.
5. **Do not feed the animals**. Wrong food makes them sick.
6. Keep small children within arm's reach at all times. Strollers allowed except in Hall E.

**Tips from our staff**

- Weekend afternoons are crowded; the quietest time is Tuesday to Thursday before 11:00.
- If you love penguins, come on Saturday — the penguin cake is sold at the cafe.
- Bring a light jacket for Hall C — it is kept at about 10 °C to match penguin weather.
- Combine your visit with the nearby City Harbour Park for a full day out; just 10 minutes' walk.

- Email: summer@blueocean.cn

*Ocean lovers, see you beneath the waves!*

**4. What does the aquarium advise visitors to bring for Hall C?**

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Monday — Closed for cleaning"  
【解析】周一闭馆清洁。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Week Pass... ¥240 / ¥150"  
【解析】学生周票 150 元。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Hall D... touch pool for children"  
【解析】D 馆浅池可触摸。A/B/C 均错。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Bring a light jacket for Hall C"  
【解析】C 馆 10℃需保暖。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"Do not feed the animals"  
【解析】禁止喂食。A/B/D 均允许。故选 C。
