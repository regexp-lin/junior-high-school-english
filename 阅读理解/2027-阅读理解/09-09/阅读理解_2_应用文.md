# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — "Morning Breeze" Student Newspaper 2027 Autumn Recruitment**

> **Lihua Middle School · Student Newspaper Committee**
> **"Morning Breeze" (晨风报) — 2027 Autumn Recruitment Notice**
> **Open to every student of Grades 7–9**

---

### 1. About "Morning Breeze"

**Morning Breeze** is our school's student-run monthly newspaper. It has been running for **eleven years**. The paper has **four pages**, printed in both **Chinese and English**, and is read by more than **1,500 students** every month. Every word in the paper — except the weather box — is written, edited or drawn by students.

This autumn, we are rebuilding the team and inviting new members to join.

### 2. What We Print Every Month

- **Page 1 — Campus News**: important activities, short interviews with teachers and students.
- **Page 2 — Learning Corner**: study tips, book reviews, English language columns.
- **Page 3 — Voices**: student opinion pieces, letters to the editor, debate on campus topics.
- **Page 4 — Bright Side**: student art, short stories, poems, comic strips, and photos.

### 3. Open Positions

| Position | Main Work | Expected Time per Week |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Reporter (6)** | Interview teachers and students; write short campus-news pieces | 3–4 hours |
| **Editor (3)** | Read, improve and shorten student drafts; check facts | 4–5 hours |
| **English Columnist (2)** | Write one English learning column per month | 2–3 hours |
| **Illustrator (2)** | Draw small comics and page art | 2–4 hours |
| **Photographer (2)** | Take campus photos and short portrait pictures | 2–3 hours |
| **Designer (1)** | Lay out pages with the student design tool | 3–4 hours |

Every member promises to work quietly, to **respect all interviewees**, and to **never publish names or photos without permission**.

### 4. Who Can Apply?

- Any **Grade 7–9 student** of Lihua Middle School.
- No paper awards or published work required. **First-time writers are especially welcome.**
- Students whose average grade has fallen below **65** in the last big exam are asked to join only the **Illustrator** or **Photographer** positions this term, to keep study in balance.

### 5. How to Apply

Please hand in **two items** at Room 212 of Teaching Block B **or** on the school portal → "Morning Breeze 2027":

1. A **short application form** (downloadable online) with your name, class, chosen position, and one paragraph (about 80 words) on why you want to join.
2. **One short sample work**:
   - Reporters & Columnists: a 150–250 word article on any school-related topic.
   - Editors: a short rewrite of a given 300-word rough draft (file available online).
   - Illustrators: one simple campus comic or sketch.
   - Photographers: three campus photos you have taken yourself.
   - Designers: one short A4 page you have designed in any tool (including paper).

### 6. Timeline

- **Thursday 11 September** — applications open on the school portal.
- **Friday 26 September 18:00** — applications close.
- **Saturday 27 September** — first reading by senior editors.
- **Tuesday 30 September 16:30** — short 10-minute chat with shortlisted students in Room 212.
- **Friday 3 October** — final list put on the school portal and on the noticeboard of Teaching Block B.

### 7. What You Will Get

- A small **member card** with your name, class and role.
- Your name **printed** on each paper you have worked on.
- A monthly **editing workshop** led by Ms Hou Qing (a journalist) from October.
- A quiet skill in writing, editing and designing that stays with you for years — and, some past members say, a lifetime.

### 8. Three Gentle Rules of the Newsroom

1. **Never cheat** facts. If unsure, ask again.
2. **Never publish** a person's face, grade, or private story without written permission.
3. **Never mock** a classmate in our pages, even as a "joke".

### 9. Contact

- **Ms Hou Qing**, student newspaper teacher, Teaching Block B, Room 212.
- **Lin Xinhe**, student editor-in-chief, Grade 9 Class 2.
- Email: `morningbreeze2027@lihuaschool.cn`

> **"A good school newspaper is not a mirror that makes us look better; it is a quiet window that lets us see each other more clearly."**
> — "Morning Breeze" Editorial Team

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many pages does the "Morning Breeze" newspaper have each month?**

A. Two pages.  
B. Four pages.  
C. Eight pages.  
D. Sixteen pages.

**2. Which of the following positions is open on Page 4 ("Bright Side")?**

A. English Columnist.  
B. Reporter.  
C. Illustrator (drawing small comics and page art).  
D. Head of the Maths Department.

**3. According to the notice, who is asked to apply **only** for the Illustrator or Photographer positions this term?**

A. Students who write in Chinese.  
B. Students whose average grade has fallen below 65 in the last big exam.  
C. Students in Grade 9 only.  
D. Students who already have published work.

**4. What is the deadline for submitting the application?**

A. Thursday 11 September.  
B. Friday 26 September, 18:00.  
C. Tuesday 30 September.  
D. Friday 3 October.

**5. Which of the following is **NOT** one of the "three gentle rules of the newsroom"?**

A. Never cheat facts — if unsure, ask again.  
B. Never publish a person's face, grade or private story without written permission.  
C. Never mock a classmate in our pages, even as a "joke".  
D. Never write any article shorter than one thousand words.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 条 "The paper has four pages"  
【解析】报纸共四版。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 条 "Page 4 — Bright Side: student art, short stories, poems, comic strips, and photos"；第 3 条 "Illustrator (2) Draw small comics and page art"  
【解析】第 4 版属插画师负责范围。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 条 "Students whose average grade has fallen below 65 ... only ... Illustrator or Photographer"  
【解析】成绩低于 65 分的仅限申请这两类岗位。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 6 条 "Friday 26 September 18:00 — applications close"  
【解析】9 月 26 日 18:00 截止。A 开启；C 面试；D 公布最终名单。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节判断题（否定型）  
【定位】第 8 条列出三条编辑部守则  
【解析】未要求每篇稿件至少 1000 字。A、B、C 均为守则。故选 D。
