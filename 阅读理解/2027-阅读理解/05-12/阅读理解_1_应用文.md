# 阅读理解（应用文） **Young Chef Cook-Off 2027 — Registration Notice**

> **CITY YOUTH KITCHEN**  
> **"YOUNG CHEF COOK-OFF 2027"**  
> *A family cooking contest for middle school students*  
> Saturday, 6 June 2027 · City Youth Kitchen, 88 Orange Street

---

Dear students and parents,

The **City Youth Kitchen** is proud to hold the **Young Chef Cook-Off 2027**. This year's theme is **"One Healthy Plate from Home"**. Middle school students from Grades 6–9 are invited to cook a home-style dish at our big kitchen stage, under the watchful eyes of our safety team. Parents are welcome to sit in the audience and cheer.

### Contest Rules

1. Each student must cook **one main dish**, using at least **three** kinds of fresh vegetables.  
2. Cooking time is **30 minutes**, from 10:00 to 10:30. Preparation (washing, cutting) may start at 09:45.  
3. Students may use only the tools placed on each workstation: a gas stove, one pan, one pot, one knife, one chopping board, one colander, and a set of plates and spoons.  
4. Students must wear a **clean apron** and **tie back long hair**. Slippers and sandals are NOT allowed.  
5. Parents must stay in the audience area. Help from parents during cooking will lead to score loss.

### Judging

| Item | Score | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Taste | 40 | How delicious is the dish? |
| Healthy idea | 25 | Use of vegetables, little oil and salt |
| Kitchen safety | 20 | Handling knife, stove, fire; clean workstation |
| Creativity | 15 | Clever use of simple ingredients |

### Awards

- **Gold Chef (3 students):** 500 yuan book gift card + chef-coat trophy  
- **Silver Chef (6 students):** 200 yuan book gift card  
- **Bronze Chef (10 students):** 100 yuan book gift card  
- **Little Health Star (special award for the healthiest dish):** a free one-day summer cooking camp

Every student who finishes the dish will receive a **Certificate of Participation** and a small cotton apron as a keepsake.

### How to Register

- **Step 1:** Visit **www.cykitchen.cn/cookoff2027** and fill in the online form.  
- **Step 2:** Upload a 1-minute video of your dish idea at home (telephone video is fine).  
- **Step 3:** Pay the **50-yuan** material fee online. Students from families with a Youth Care Card pay only **10 yuan**.  
- **Deadline:** 25 May 2027, 20:00. The contest will take only **40 students**, chosen after video review.

### Food Safety

- All fresh food used on the day is provided by the Youth Kitchen.  
- Students with food allergies must write the details clearly on the form; the kitchen team will prepare safe ingredients.  
- Spicy food, raw seafood and nuts are **not allowed** in this contest.

For questions, email **cookoff@cykitchen.cn** or call **020-5511-6688** (Mon–Fri, 10:00–17:00).

**Cook with care, share with joy, and make one healthy plate for your family.**

— Young Chef Cook-Off Team

**根据通告内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is this year's theme?**

A. "Fancy Desserts Only."  
B. "One Healthy Plate from Home."  
C. "Around the World Spices."  
D. "Fastest Instant Noodles."

**2. How many vegetables must a student use at least?**

A. One.  
B. Two.  
C. Three.  
D. Five.

**3. Which of the following is NOT allowed during the contest?**

A. Wearing a clean apron.  
B. Tying back long hair.  
C. Getting help from parents while cooking.  
D. Using the provided knife and chopping board.

**4. What is the top award for the 3 Gold Chefs?**

A. 10 yuan only.  
B. A 500 yuan book gift card and a chef-coat trophy.  
C. A free year of noodles.  
D. A bicycle.

**5. Who can pay only 10 yuan for the material fee?**

A. Students from families with a Youth Care Card.  
B. Students who are late for the deadline.  
C. Students who bring their own knife.  
D. Students over 20 years old.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开头段 "This year's theme is 'One Healthy Plate from Home'."  
【解析】比赛主题即该句。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Contest Rules 第1条 "at least three kinds of fresh vegetables"  
【解析】至少 3 种蔬菜。A/B/D 均不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Contest Rules 第5条 "Help from parents during cooking will lead to score loss."  
【解析】烹饪过程中禁止家长帮忙。A/B/D 都是允许或要求的行为。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Awards "Gold Chef: 500 yuan book gift card + chef-coat trophy"  
【解析】金牌厨师奖品。A/C/D 与原文不符。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How to Register Step 3 "Students from families with a Youth Care Card pay only 10 yuan."  
【解析】持 Youth Care Card 家庭可仅付 10 元。B/C/D 均无据。故选 A。
