# 阅读理解（应用文） **Sunrise Bookshop — Summer Book-Exchange Fair**

**SUNRISE BOOKSHOP**
*Read more. Spend less. Share better.*

---

**Summer Book-Exchange Fair 2027**
Every July our small bookshop organises a free fair where students can trade the books they have already read for new ones. No money is needed. You only bring the books you want to share.

**Where and when**

- **Sunrise Bookshop**, No. 22 Linden Road (3-minute walk from Linden metro station).
- **Friday 16 July – Sunday 18 July 2027**, 10:00 – 18:00 each day.
- The exchange corner will be open in the big hall on the second floor.

**How the exchange works**

1. Bring between **3 and 12 books** to the front desk.
2. Our staff will quickly check the condition of the books and give you a colour ticket for each book:
   - **Green** — classic novels, award-winning books;
   - **Blue** — new-looking textbooks or reference books;
   - **Yellow** — children's picture books or comics in good shape.
3. Use your tickets to pick books in the exchange corner. One ticket equals one book of the **same colour**. You may not mix colours.
4. Remaining tickets can be kept for the fair next year.

**What we accept**

- Books that are clean, complete, with no missing pages.
- Books printed within the last 15 years (we want modern knowledge).
- Textbooks only from Grade 3 primary up to Grade 12 senior high.

**What we do NOT accept**

- Books with strong smells, wet pages, or drawing over the text.
- Magazines older than two years.
- Workbooks already filled with answers.

**Special events**

- **Saturday 17 July, 14:00** — Writer **Lin Qiu** will share the story behind her new book *Paper Moon*. Free, but seats are limited to 60.
- **Sunday 18 July, 10:30** — Children's storytelling hour for readers aged 6-10, led by volunteers from the city library.
- **Friday – Sunday, all day** — Drawing wall: any visitor can leave a short review (no more than 30 words) of one book they love.

**Kind reminders**

- Please carry your books in a cloth bag. We do not offer plastic bags.
- The bookshop is small; please keep your voice low.
- Food and drinks are not allowed in the exchange corner.

**Contact**
Sunrise Bookshop · 010-xxxx-3381 · fair@sunrisebooks.cn
*More joy. Less waste. See you in July!*

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How much does a student need to pay to join the fair?**

A. 50 yuan.  
B. 100 yuan.  
C. Nothing — the fair is free.  
D. 20 yuan per book.

**2. A student brings 5 picture books. What colour tickets will he receive?**

A. Green.  
B. Blue.  
C. Yellow.  
D. White.

**3. Which book will NOT be accepted at the fair?**

A. A clean novel printed in 2018.  
B. A Grade 8 maths textbook printed in 2022.  
C. A magazine printed in 2019.  
D. A workbook already filled with a student's answers.

**4. When will writer Lin Qiu talk about her new book?**

A. Friday 16 July, morning.  
B. Saturday 17 July, 14:00.  
C. Sunday 18 July, 10:30.  
D. Sunday 18 July, evening.

**5. What should visitors bring to carry their books?**

A. A plastic bag from the bookshop.  
B. A cloth bag.  
C. A small box.  
D. A metal basket.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"a free fair"  
【解析】完全免费，以书换书。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Yellow — children's picture books or comics"  
【解析】绘本对应黄票。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"Workbooks already filled with answers"  
【解析】已填答案的练习册不收。A/B/C 均在接受范围。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Saturday 17 July, 14:00 — Writer Lin Qiu..."  
【解析】周六下午 2 点。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"please carry your books in a cloth bag"  
【解析】请用布袋。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
