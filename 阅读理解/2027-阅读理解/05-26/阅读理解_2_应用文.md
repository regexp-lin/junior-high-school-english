# 阅读理解（应用文） **Starlight Middle School Robotics Club — Recruiting New Members!**

**STARLIGHT MIDDLE SCHOOL · ROBOTICS CLUB**
*"Think it. Build it. Test it."*

---

**Who are we?**
We are a student-led club of about 40 middle school students. Every week we design, build and program small robots. Some robots pick up ping-pong balls; some follow a black line on the floor; our newest robot can even sort toy bottles by colour!

**Who can join?**

- Any student in **Grade 7 – Grade 9** at Starlight Middle School.
- **No experience is needed.** If you are curious and ready to try, we welcome you!
- Both girls and boys are equally welcome. About half of our current members are girls.

**What will you learn?**

1. Basic mechanical parts — motors, wheels, gears.
2. Simple block-based programming (we start with a kids' programming tool, then move to Python for senior members).
3. Teamwork, problem-solving and how to give a short project talk.

**When and where do we meet?**

- **Every Wednesday, 16:30 – 18:00** — Robotics Lab, Room 305 (Building B).
- **Every Saturday, 9:00 – 11:30** — Optional deeper practice during exam-free weeks.

Snacks are provided at every Saturday session.

**How to join**

**Step 1.** Fill in the green paper form at the **school gate notice board** or download it at *starlight-school.cn/robotics*.
**Step 2.** Ask your homeroom teacher to sign the form.
**Step 3.** Return the form to **Mr. Li Wei** in Room 305, **before Friday 5 June, 2027**.
**Step 4.** Come to the "Welcome Night" on **Tuesday 9 June, 18:30 – 19:30** to meet your new team.

**Membership fee**
**Free.** All materials and tools belong to the school. Members must, however, promise to handle tools and robots carefully.

**Special chances this year**

- **City Robotics Carnival** — Saturday 24 October, 2027. Top teams will be chosen to represent our school.
- **Summer "Robot for a Farmer" project** — July 2027. Our club will design a small robot to help pick weeds in a school vegetable garden.

**A few important rules**

- Safety glasses must be worn when using the glue gun or soldering iron.
- **Never** take any tools or robot parts home unless Mr. Li has signed a note.
- If you miss three meetings in a row without a good reason, your place may be given to a student on the waiting list.

**Questions?**
Talk to **Mr. Li Wei** (Room 305), or send an email to *robotics@starlight-school.cn*.

*We can't wait to see what you will build!*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Who can join?" 部分  
【解析】7–9 年级都可申请，无需经验。A/C/D 均不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"When and where do we meet?" 部分  
【解析】固定周三 16:30–18:00 在 305。A/C/D 均不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Step 3." 部分  
【解析】第 3 步为交表给李老师。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Membership fee · Free."  
【解析】免费会员。A/B/C 均错。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"A few important rules · If you miss three meetings in a row without a good reason, your place may be given to a student on the waiting list."  
【解析】位置可能被等候者占用。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。
