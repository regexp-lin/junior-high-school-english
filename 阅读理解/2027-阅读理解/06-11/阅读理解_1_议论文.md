# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should Schools Keep Weekly Handwritten Diaries?**

In recent years, many middle schools in China have asked students to keep a **handwritten diary** once a week. Some parents say this is old-fashioned: "We already have phones and apps." A few students complain, too. But I believe handwritten weekly diaries are very useful and should be kept in our schools.

First, writing by hand slows the mind down in a good way. When we type on a phone, our fingers fly across the screen and our thoughts rush with them. We can write a long message in two minutes without really thinking. When we write by hand, however, each word takes time. We have to choose our words more carefully, and we naturally stop and think. Teachers in my school often say that a simple 300-word handwritten diary gives them a clearer picture of a student's week than 3,000 words in a group chat.

Second, a handwritten diary helps us to know ourselves. Life in Grade 8 is busy: tests, training, chatting, gaming. One week flies by and we can hardly remember what we felt on Monday. But if we sit down on Sunday evening and write about the week — the happy moments, the small worries, even the things we are afraid to say out loud — we slowly learn about our own feelings. A famous Chinese writer once said, "The pen is the mirror of the heart." This is especially true for teenagers, whose feelings change very quickly.

Some students worry that handwriting is "a waste of time" and "not useful in the future". I do not agree. Of course, we will use keyboards and voice messages more and more. But handwriting is not only a way to record information; it is a kind of training for **patience**. A student who can sit quietly and finish a 300-word diary, week after week, is also learning how to stay with small tasks that are not exciting. This patience is very useful for studying maths, learning a musical instrument, or building any long-term skill.

Of course, the diary should not become heavy homework. Teachers should only read it to support students, not to give a score. Parents should never open it without permission. And students should feel free to write in their own way — short lists, small drawings, even poems. With these simple rules, the weekly handwritten diary is not a burden but a quiet friend on the school desk.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the author's main opinion?**

A. Handwritten diaries are old-fashioned and not useful.  
B. Handwritten weekly diaries are useful and should be kept in schools.  
C. Schools should let students write diaries only on phones.  
D. Diaries should be checked and scored by parents every week.

**2. According to paragraph 2, why is writing by hand useful?**

A. Because it is much faster than typing.  
B. Because it slows the mind down and makes us think more carefully.  
C. Because it never has spelling mistakes.  
D. Because teachers have more free time.

**3. What does the Chinese writer mean by "The pen is the mirror of the heart"?**

A. Only writers own good pens.  
B. Pens are more expensive than mirrors.  
C. Writing by hand can help us see our true feelings.  
D. Mirrors can help us write better sentences.

**4. What does the author say about patience?**

A. Handwriting cannot help patience at all.  
B. Finishing a weekly diary can train patience, which is useful for other long-term skills.  
C. Only maths exercises can train patience.  
D. Patience has nothing to do with schoolwork.

**5. What rule is suggested for parents?**

A. Parents should open the diary every day.  
B. Parents should grade each diary.  
C. Parents should never open the diary without permission.  
D. Parents should write the diary for the student.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】观点态度题  
【定位】第 1 段末句  
【解析】作者支持保留周记。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 段 "slows the mind down..."  
【解析】手写使思维放慢、思考更细。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第 3 段"The pen is the mirror of the heart"  
【解析】笔能映照内心。A/B/D 均偏题。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 段 "a kind of training for patience"  
【解析】每周坚持写日记是对耐心的训练，有利于长期学习。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】末段 "Parents should never open it without permission"  
【解析】未经允许，家长不可查看。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。
