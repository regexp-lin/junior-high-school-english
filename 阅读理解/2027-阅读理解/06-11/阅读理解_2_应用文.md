# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Star Watchers" Summer Astronomy Camp — Information Sheet**

**BLUE OBSERVATORY SCIENCE CENTRE**
*Where young eyes meet old stars.*

---

**About the camp**
Every August, our small observatory opens a 4-day astronomy camp for middle school students. Campers live in a simple mountain lodge, learn to use telescopes, and spend two nights watching the night sky together.

**Key information**

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Dates** | Monday 9 August – Thursday 12 August 2027 |
| **Place** | Blue Mountain Observatory, 60 km north of the city |
| **For** | Students aged 12 – 15 (with a school proof) |
| **Size** | 24 campers only (3 small groups, 8 per group) |
| **Fee** | 900 yuan (transport, food, stay, telescope rental) |
| **Languages** | Chinese; simple English for science terms |

**Daily plan (example — Day 2)**

- 07:30 Breakfast in the lodge.
- 08:30 Science class: *How far is a "light year"?*
- 10:30 Outdoor walk: reading the sky with the naked eye.
- 12:00 Lunch + long rest (under strong mid-day sun).
- 15:00 Workshop: make your own simple star map.
- 18:00 Light supper in the garden.
- 21:30 – 24:00 Night sky watching (warm jacket needed).

**What you will learn**

- How to find the **North Star**, the **Big Dipper** and five other easy groups of stars.
- How to use a small telescope safely — never point it at the sun, even for "one quick second".
- How to take simple photos of the Moon with a phone and a cheap adapter.
- How to record a "star journal" every night, in your own language.

**What to bring**

1. A warm jacket (nights are about 12 °C, even in August).
2. Long trousers + comfortable walking shoes.
3. A paper notebook, a pencil, and a torch with a red cover (normal white light hurts night vision).
4. Basic toothbrush and a 1-litre water bottle.

**Safety rules**

- Always stay with your group leader during night walks; the mountain road has some steep parts.
- Do not drink the stream water on the mountain, even if it looks clean.
- Switch your phone to silent after 21:30 so other campers can concentrate on the sky.
- If a camper feels sick, the lodge doctor is on duty 24 hours a day.

**How to apply**

1. Fill in the online form at www.bluestars-camp.cn before **Sunday 4 July 2027**.
2. Upload a short letter (150 words) about why you want to join.
3. A teacher from our centre will call selected campers before **20 July** for a 10-minute chat.
4. If you are selected, pay the full fee before **28 July**.

**Cancellation**

- Before 20 July: full refund.
- 21 – 28 July: 50 % refund.
- After 28 July: no refund, but we can move your place to next summer.
