# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Lost in the Old Town**

① Last October, I travelled with my parents to a small old town called Wuzhen. On the second morning, I woke up earlier than they did and decided to walk along the narrow stone lanes by myself. I was thirteen, and I wanted to feel like a grown-up for once. I took only my camera, a small map and a red paper umbrella that Mom had bought for me the day before.

② The morning was misty. Grey stone bridges, wooden houses and quiet rivers all blended into soft grey and white, like an old Chinese ink painting. I walked slowly, taking photos and turning left and right without thinking. After perhaps thirty minutes, I suddenly realized that I could not see our hotel, the high red lamps or even the bus stop any more. The little map in my hand looked like a pile of broken lines. My heart started to beat quickly.

③ I tried to stay calm. I walked toward the sound of running water, thinking the river would lead me somewhere familiar. Instead, I came to a tiny market street where old people were just opening their shops. An old grandmother was wiping a wooden chair in front of a tea shop. When she saw me standing still with wet shoes and a pale face, she smiled and said softly, "My dear child, are you lost?"

④ I nodded. Tears almost rolled down. The grandmother poured me a small cup of warm brown-sugar water and asked about my hotel. I showed her a photo of the gate on my camera. "Ah, the Moon-Gate Hotel. It's not far," she said, putting down her cloth. She walked out with me into the rain, holding a black umbrella higher than her own head, just to keep my head dry. I walked on her left, and she pointed to every little sign — a red flag, a stone lion, a bakery — so that I could find my way alone next time.

⑤ When we reached the hotel, my parents were already standing at the gate, their phones in their hands. They ran toward me, half angry and half relieved. Before I could say anything, the grandmother put her warm hand on my shoulder and said to my mother, "She is a brave little girl. Please do not be too hard on her." Then she turned, nodded politely, and disappeared into the grey lanes.

⑥ That afternoon, Mom bought a box of tea biscuits from that little shop and gave it to me. "Keep them," she said, "as a reminder." Even today, every time I see a grey stone bridge in a travel book, I remember the old tea shop, the brown-sugar water and a tall black umbrella — and I understand that a small town, even when you are lost, can be full of warm hearts.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the writer go out alone that morning?**

A. Because his/her parents were ill.  
B. Because he/she wanted to feel like a grown-up.  
C. Because the hotel was too noisy.  
D. Because the tour guide asked him/her to do so.

**2. What did the writer do first when he/she realized he/she was lost?**

A. Sat on the ground and cried loudly.  
B. Walked toward the sound of running water.  
C. Called the police from a shop.  
D. Took a bus to the train station.

**3. How did the grandmother help the writer?**

A. She gave the writer a big sum of money.  
B. She guided the writer to the hotel with an umbrella.  
C. She called the writer's parents on the phone.  
D. She carried the writer on her back.

**4. What can we learn about the grandmother from Paragraph 5?**

A. She was angry with the writer's parents.  
B. She spoke kindly for the writer.  
C. She was too shy to speak.  
D. She wanted the writer to stay in her shop.

**5. What is the main message of this passage?**

A. Travelling alone is always dangerous.  
B. A small town can be full of warm hearts.  
C. Umbrellas are important on rainy days.  
D. Children should never go out without a map.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "I wanted to feel like a grown-up for once"  
【解析】作者想像大人那样独自走走。其他选项无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "I walked toward the sound of running water"  
【解析】作者迷路后先向流水声走去。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "She walked out with me into the rain, holding a black umbrella"  
【解析】奶奶撑伞送作者到酒店。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Please do not be too hard on her."  
【解析】奶奶替作者说好话。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑥段 "a small town... can be full of warm hearts"  
【解析】主题是小镇处处有温情。A 绝对，C、D 偏题。故选 B。
