# 阅读理解（应用文） **City-Wide English Speaking Contest — "Our World in 3 Minutes"**

Hello, young speakers!

The **City Education Bureau** and **Blue Lake English Centre** are proud to announce the **2027 English Speaking Contest** for middle-school students. The theme this year is warm, open, and close to your daily life: **"Our World in 3 Minutes."** Tell us a real story, an honest opinion, or a small dream — in just **3 minutes of English**.

---

### Schedule

| Round | Date | Who | Place |
|-------|------|-----|-------|
| School Round | Sept 10 – Oct 10, 2027 | All Grade 7–9 students in each school | Each school's hall |
| City Semi-Final | Saturday, Nov 7, 2027 | Top 2 from each school | Blue Lake Concert Hall (South Gate) |
| City Final | Sunday, Nov 22, 2027 | Top 30 from the Semi-Final | Blue Lake Concert Hall (Main Stage) |

---

### Rules (Please Read Carefully)

**1. Speech length**  
- **3 minutes**, plus or minus 10 seconds.  
- A soft bell will ring at 2:50. A second bell at 3:10 ends the speech.

**2. Topic**  
- Anything that matches our theme "Our World in 3 Minutes":  
    - a family story,  
    - a childhood memory,  
    - a small invention for your city,  
    - a problem you care about (kind, not heavy),  
    - a dream for your country in 2037.  
- **Political debates and religious topics** are not allowed.

**3. Language**  
- English only, from start to finish.  
- You may use **one short quote in Chinese or another language** (up to 10 seconds), if it helps the story — please translate it.

**4. Help and honesty**  
- The speech must be **your own words**. Teachers and parents may give ideas, but AI-written speeches are not accepted. A judge will ask two short questions after each speech to check understanding.  
- You may use **one small paper card** (A5 size) with key words, but not a full script.  
- **No slides, videos, or music** on the stage.

**5. Dress**  
- School uniform or simple neat clothes. Hats must be removed.

**6. What judges look for**  
| Part | Marks | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Content & story | 40 | Original, interesting, close to the theme |
| Language | 25 | Clear grammar, suitable vocabulary |
| Pronunciation | 20 | Easy to understand, natural rhythm |
| Stage presence | 15 | Eye contact, confident voice, appropriate gestures |

### How to Sign Up

1. Tell your English teacher **by Friday, September 3, 2027**, that you wish to take part in the School Round.  
2. Submit a short (100-word) English summary of your speech topic on the School Round day.  
3. If you win the School Round, your English teacher will upload your information to **www.cityespeak2027.cn** by **October 15**.  
4. Three days before each round, you will receive a WeChat message with your speaker number and time slot.

### Student Guidance — Small Tips

- **Tell, don't recite.** Speak as if you are telling one friend.  
- **Short sentences beat long ones.** If a sentence is longer than 15 words, think about cutting it in two.  
- **Practise to a timer.** Record yourself, listen once, change one thing, and record again.  
- **Breathe.** Before you start, take two slow, quiet breaths. The room will wait for you.
