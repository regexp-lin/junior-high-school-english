# 阅读理解（应用文） **Books for Mountain Friends — Charity Book Donation**

① Dear students and parents, our school is working with the **Blue Sky Education Charity** to support a small middle school in the **Wuyi Mountains**. Many children there love to read but do not have enough books. Let us share some of ours, so their small library can grow greener this spring.

② **Why we need your help**

- The mountain school has only about **600 books** for **300 students**, most of them over 10 years old.  
- Last year, with the help of four city schools, we sent them **4,200 books** — they built a new reading corner!  
- This year, our target is **1,500 books** from our own school community.

③ **Books We Are Looking For**

| Type | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| Story books | short stories, fairy tales, simple novels |
| Science books | about space, animals, simple experiments |
| English readers | readers with pictures, Level 1 – Level 4 |
| Dictionaries | English-Chinese, Chinese-Chinese (clean copies) |
| Magazines | no older than 3 years, in good shape |

④ **Books We Cannot Accept**

- Books with torn pages, strong smell or heavy water damage.  
- Textbooks for a certain city's exam only.  
- Books with pictures that do not fit young readers.  
- Bought-recently-from-an-adult-bookshop "study material" (too hard for mountain classes).

⑤ **How to Donate**

1. Put your books into a clean paper bag or cardboard box.  
2. Stick a small **yellow note** on the bag with your class and name.  
3. Drop the bag at the **Book Collection Point** — the long table beside the school library door.  
4. Collection time: **Monday 22 March – Friday 26 March, 08:00 – 18:00**.

⑥ **What Happens Next**

- From **29 March**, all books will be checked by our student librarians.  
- Good books will be packed and shipped to the mountain school before **15 April**.  
- Each donor will receive a **reading certificate**, and the top three classes (by weight of books) will get a **class field trip** to the City Library in May.

⑦ **Extra Ways to Help**

- Write a short letter in English or Chinese to the mountain students. Put it inside your donated book.  
- Sign up to be a **book-checker**: two hours on Saturday 27 March, light snacks provided.  
- Parents may donate a small gift such as a **bookmark set** or a **clean writing pen**.

⑧ **Contact**  
- Ms. Xu (school library) — library@bs-ms.cn  
- Phone (12:00 – 13:30 on school days): 027-8855-1980  
- Blue Sky Charity website: www.bluesky-edu.org

**A good book is a small boat. Let's send ours across the mountains. — School Library & Blue Sky Charity**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many books does the mountain middle school have now?**

A. About 1,500.  
B. About 600.  
C. About 4,200.  
D. About 300.

**2. Which of the following books CAN students donate?**

A. A textbook for a certain city's own exam.  
B. A science book about animals, in good shape.  
C. A magazine from 2015 with water damage.  
D. An adult study book too hard for mountain students.

**3. Where should students leave their book bags?**

A. At the school main gate.  
B. In the PE office.  
C. At the long table beside the school library door.  
D. In Ms. Xu's personal office.

**4. How can students get a class field trip to the City Library?**

A. Their class must donate the heaviest total weight of books (top three).  
B. Every student must donate at least 20 books.  
C. Their class must write a play about the mountain school.  
D. Their class teacher must pay 500 yuan.

**5. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as an extra way to help?**

A. Writing a letter to the mountain students.  
B. Signing up to be a book-checker on Saturday.  
C. Donating a bookmark set or a clean pen.  
D. Travelling to the mountain school to teach.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段第 1 条 "only about 600 books for 300 students"  
【解析】山区学校现有约 600 本书。A 是目标；C 是去年送出数；D 是学生数。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Science books ... about space, animals" + 第④段排除项  
【解析】科学类动物书符合要求。A、C、D 均在不接受之列。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段第 3 条 "the long table beside the school library door"  
【解析】捐赠点在图书馆门旁长桌。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段第 3 条 "top three classes (by weight of books) will get a class field trip"  
【解析】按重量前三的班级可获参观。其他选项均无依据。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑦段列出的三条额外方式  
【解析】写信、当图书检查员、送小礼物均提及；去山区支教未提。故选 D。
