# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — Science Fair 2027**

---

## **Ask, Build, Wonder — Join the Fair!**

Dear Grade 7–9 students,

Our school's **12th Science Fair** will take place on **Friday, November 26, 2027** in the Sports Hall. This year the theme is **"Small Hands, Big Questions"**, and for the first time, student teams from three **partner schools** will join us. It is a great chance to show your own science project, meet new friends and perhaps win a spot in the City Youth Science Contest next March.

---

## **Key Dates**

| Stage | Date | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Team sign-up opens | Sep 5, Mon | Online form at `science.lihua-ms.cn` |
| Sign-up closes | Sep 30, Tue | No late teams |
| Project proposal due | Oct 17, Fri | One-page English summary |
| School pre-check | Nov 6, Thu, 15:00 | Room 401, Science Building |
| **Main Fair Day** | **Nov 26, Fri, 9:00–17:00** | Sports Hall |
| Results announced | Nov 28, Sun | School website & class groups |

---

## **Who Can Join**

- **Team size:** 2 to 4 students.  
- **Grade mix:** Grade 7, 8 and 9 students may join the same team.  
- **Teacher guide:** Each team must have **one teacher guide** (PE and music teachers may also sign up).  
- Students who joined last year's fair **may** join again, but not with the same project.

---

## **Project Groups**

Choose **one** group only:

| Group | Example Projects | Time Needed |
|---|---|---|
| **A. Daily Life Science** | A better school-bag shape; a low-cost water filter | 6–8 weeks |
| **B. Environment & City** | Noise under a bridge; city bird count; food waste study | 8–10 weeks |
| **C. Build & Code** | Small robot for rubbish sorting; weather box with sensors | 10–12 weeks |
| **D. Mini-Experiment** | Plants in different light colours; salt and water test | 4–6 weeks |

---

## **Project Requirements**

1. **Real question:** Every project must begin with **one clear "why" question** that can be tested. "How fast does ice melt" is OK; "what is the best thing in the world" is not.  
2. **Simple materials:** No chemicals from outside the school lab. No animal experiments that could harm any living thing.  
3. **Safety first:** Scissors, small batteries, and low-heat tools are fine under teacher guidance. Fire, sharp blades and strong magnets must stay in the lab.  
4. **Display board:** One poster board, **60 cm × 80 cm**, showing question, method, result and drawing.  
5. **Short talk:** On Main Fair Day, each team gives a **3-minute English talk** to the judges.

---

## **Prizes and Chances**

- **Top 10 Teams:** Each member gets a science water bottle and a certificate; the team enters the **City Youth Science Contest** next March.  
- **Most Creative Project:** One team wins a 500-yuan bookshop voucher.  
- **Best "Small Hands" Award** (for Grade 7 teams only): a small robotics kit for the classroom.  
- **Friendship Award:** For the school that shows the most help to other teams on the day.  

> *All teams* that pass the school pre-check get **10 service-learning hours** on their record.

---

## **Fair Day Rules**

- Please wear your **school uniform** and bring your **student ID**.  
- Each team has **one table** in the Sports Hall; tables will be numbered at the door.  
- Judges will visit between **10:00 and 15:00**; please keep at least **one member** at the table at all times during this window.  
- **No food or drink** on the display tables; there is a tea station near the east door.  
- Photos are welcome, but please **do not touch** other teams' displays unless invited.

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When will the Main Fair Day be held in 2027?**

A. September 30.  
B. October 17.  
C. November 26.  
D. November 28.

**2. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed for a team?**

A. Three Grade 7 and one Grade 9 student on the same team.  
B. A team with only one student working alone.  
C. Having a PE teacher as the team's guide teacher.  
D. A student taking part again this year with a different project.

**3. A Grade 8 group wants to study **noise levels under a city bridge**. Which group should they sign up for?**

A. Group A: Daily Life Science.  
B. Group B: Environment & City.  
C. Group C: Build & Code.  
D. Group D: Mini-Experiment.

**4. Which of the following is **against** the project requirements?**

A. Using school-lab chemicals under a teacher's guidance.  
B. Bringing in strong magnets and sharp blades for the display table.  
C. Making a 60 × 80 cm poster showing question, method, and result.  
D. Writing a real "why" question that can be tested.

**5. A team passes the school pre-check but does not reach the Top 10. What do its members still get?**

A. Nothing at all.  
B. A 500-yuan bookshop voucher.  
C. 10 service-learning hours on their school record.  
D. A free trip to another country.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Key Dates 表格 "Main Fair Day — Nov 26, Fri, 9:00–17:00"  
【解析】A 是报名截止，B 是方案截止，D 是公布结果日。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Who Can Join "Team size: 2 to 4 students"  
【解析】团队人数需 2–4 人，不能独自组队。A、C、D 均符合规定。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Project Groups 表 "B. Environment & City — Noise under a bridge"  
【解析】研究桥下噪音属于 B 组的示例。A、C、D 与主题不对应。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Project Requirements 第 3 条 "Fire, sharp blades and strong magnets must stay in the lab"  
【解析】强磁铁、利刃不可带上展示台。A、C、D 均合规。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】Prizes and Chances 末注 "All teams that pass the school pre-check get 10 service-learning hours"  
【解析】只要过预审，不必进入前 10，即可获得 10 小时学时。A、B、D 均与规则不符。故选 C。
