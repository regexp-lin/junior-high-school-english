# 阅读理解（应用文） **Bright Future Middle School — Spring Sports Meeting**

① Dear students, the **27th Bright Future Middle School Spring Sports Meeting** is coming! This is our biggest sunny day of the term. All students from Grade 7 to Grade 9 are welcome to take part. Whether you are a fast runner or a gentle cheerleader, there is a place for you on the **Red Field** this spring.

② **Basic Information**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Date | **Friday, 16 April – Saturday, 17 April 2027** |
| Place | Red Field (main stadium) |
| Morning start | **08:00** sharp (opening ceremony) |
| Ticket | No ticket needed for students; **parents pay 5 yuan** at the East Gate |
| Food | Canteen open both days, 11:30 – 13:30 |

③ **Programme (Friday)**

- **08:00 – 08:45**　Opening ceremony, national flag rising, student march  
- **09:00 – 10:30**　100 m, 200 m, 400 m (boys & girls)  
- **10:45 – 12:00**　Long jump, high jump  
- **13:30 – 15:00**　Relay races (4 × 100 m)  
- **15:15 – 16:30**　Tug-of-war (class vs. class)

④ **Programme (Saturday)**

- **09:00 – 10:30**　1,500 m and 3,000 m runs  
- **10:45 – 12:00**　Shot put, javelin (Grade 9 only)  
- **13:30 – 15:00**　Basketball and volleyball finals  
- **15:30 – 16:15**　Fun races (3-leg race, sack race, egg-on-spoon race)  
- **16:30 – 17:00**　Closing ceremony and prize-giving

⑤ **Rules & Tips**

1. Every student may enter **up to 3 individual events** and any team event.  
2. Please wear the school **PE uniform** and clean **sports shoes** (no leather shoes).  
3. Drink water, not sports drinks with too much sugar.  
4. Please hand your phone to the class teacher before events. We do not want phones falling on the track!  
5. If it rains heavily, the meeting will move to **20–21 April** (same programme).

⑥ **How to Sign Up**

- Get the **green sign-up form** from your class teacher.  
- Fill in your name, class, and the events you want to join.  
- Return the form before **9 April, 17:00**.  
- A list of all athletes will be on the **notice board outside the PE office** on **12 April**.

⑦ **Cheer Squad**

Every class will pick **8 – 10 students** as the class **Cheer Squad**. They will prepare short cheers, colourful flags and small music instruments. The three best squads will win a **"Best Cheer Squad"** award and a class pizza party. Interested? Talk to your PE teacher first.

**Strong bodies, warm hearts, bright futures! — Bright Future PE Department**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When will the Spring Sports Meeting take place?**

A. On 9 April 2027.  
B. On 12 April 2027.  
C. On 16 – 17 April 2027.  
D. On 20 – 21 April 2027 only.

**2. Which of the following events will be held on Friday?**

A. The 1,500 m run.  
B. Shot put.  
C. Relay races.  
D. Basketball final.

**3. How many individual events can one student join at most?**

A. 1.  
B. 2.  
C. 3.  
D. 5.

**4. Which item below is NOT a rule for students?**

A. Wear the school PE uniform.  
B. Drink sweet sports drinks instead of water.  
C. Hand phones to the class teacher before events.  
D. Clean sports shoes are needed.

**5. How can a student join the Cheer Squad?**

A. Buy a special ticket at the East Gate.  
B. Talk to the PE teacher first.  
C. Pay 5 yuan to the class teacher.  
D. Write a report about cheering.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Date: Friday, 16 April – Saturday, 17 April 2027"  
【解析】明确日期为 4 月 16–17 日。D 项为雨天备用日，不是正式日期。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 13:30 – 15:00 "Relay races (4 × 100 m)"  
【解析】接力赛在周五下午。A、B、D 均在周六。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段第 1 条 "up to 3 individual events"  
【解析】最多 3 项个人项目。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段第 3 条 "Drink water, not sports drinks with too much sugar"  
【解析】禁止喝高糖运动饮料。A、C、D 均为规定内容。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑦段末句 "Interested? Talk to your PE teacher first."  
【解析】感兴趣需先找体育老师。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。
