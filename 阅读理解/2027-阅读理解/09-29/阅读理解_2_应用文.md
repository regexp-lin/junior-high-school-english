## 2. Three Daily Rules of Our Eyes

To keep our eyes healthy, every student is asked to try to follow **three small rules** every day.

**(1) Daylight outdoor time ≥ 2 hours**
- Short breaks in the sunlight help slow the growth of short-sightedness.
- Even 10 minutes of light outdoor play during each break helps.

**(2) Reading distance ≥ 30 cm · Reading time ≤ 40 min per session**
- Keep eyes **at least 30 cm** from paper books and **50 cm** from computer screens.
- Every 40 minutes of near work, take a short rest with the **20-20-20** rule.
- Do not read in a moving bus or while lying down.

**(3) Sleep ≥ 8 hours · Screen time for fun ≤ 1 hour on weekdays**
- Sleep rests and repairs the eye muscles.
- Fun screen time (short videos, games) for weekdays should be kept to **one hour or less**.

## 3. Free Eye Check — Important Notes

- The free check covers **basic sight testing**, not full diagnosis.
- Students who already wear glasses **should bring their glasses** to the check.
- Results will be given in a small yellow card within three working days.
- If the nurse suggests a full check at the city hospital, **please go with a parent within two weeks**; do not ignore the card.
- **No eye check** will be done for students with red, swollen eyes or a current eye infection; visit a doctor first.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】正文 "from Monday 12th October to Friday 23rd October 2027"  
【解析】活动起止日期明确。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 部分 "should bring their glasses ... small yellow card within three working days"  
【解析】已有眼镜需带上参加检查。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】D

【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题
