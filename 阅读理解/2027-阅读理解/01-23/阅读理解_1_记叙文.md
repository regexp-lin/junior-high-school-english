# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Fixing Dad's Old Bike**

① Dad has ridden the same black bike to work for almost ten years. Last Saturday morning, he took it out of the shed, but the chain kept slipping off every time he turned the pedals. "This old friend needs a little care," he said with a smile. "Would you like to help me fix it?"

② I used to watch Dad work on his bike from our window, but I had never really helped. First, we turned the bike upside down and put it on an old blanket in the yard. Dad gave me a small box of tools and said, "Never be afraid of getting your hands dirty. Just ask if you are not sure." I liked the heavy feel of the tools.

③ Following Dad's directions, I loosened the back wheel and moved it a little to the front. I cleaned the chain with an old tooth brush and some special oil. Dad showed me how to check the air in the tyres and how to make the brakes tighter. At one point, I got black oil on my white sleeve. Mum would not be very happy, but Dad and I laughed about it.

④ After about an hour, we put everything back together. Dad turned the pedals slowly. The chain moved smoothly, and the wheels spun like new. "Now you try it!" he said. I rode the bike along our small yard. It felt light and steady. More importantly, I felt a kind of pride that only fixing something with your own hands can give you.

⑤ Over lunch, Dad told me that when he was my age, his own father had also taught him to fix a simple bike. "Some small skills pass from one pair of hands to another," he said. I am going to remember this Saturday for a long time. A single black bike had become more than a way to go to work — it was a quiet lesson about learning from family.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What problem did Dad's bike have at the beginning?**

A. The bell did not ring any more.  
B. The chain kept slipping off.  
C. The seat was broken.  
D. One of the wheels was missing.

**2. Why did Dad and the writer put the bike on an old blanket?**

A. Because the blanket could make the bike warm.  
B. Because they did not have a workshop at home.  
C. Because the blanket could keep the bike off the hard ground.  
D. Because the blanket was too old to use anywhere else.

**3. What did the writer do to the chain?**

A. He painted it a new colour.  
B. He cleaned it with an old tooth brush and some oil.  
C. He cut it into three small pieces.  
D. He wrapped it with paper.

**4. How did the writer feel after riding the fixed bike?**

A. Tired and hungry.  
B. Afraid and shy.  
C. Proud and happy.  
D. Angry and sad.

**5. What is the deeper meaning of the last paragraph?**

A. Old bikes are always better than new ones.  
B. Small skills can be passed down through family.  
C. Fathers should repair bikes alone.  
D. Lunch tastes better after fixing a bike.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "the chain kept slipping off every time he turned the pedals."  
【解析】链条总掉。A、C、D 均不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第②段 "we turned the bike upside down and put it on an old blanket in the yard."  
【解析】铺毯子是为了避免车子与硬地直接接触。A 保暖无依据；B 原因不是因为没工坊；D 毯子年份是次要信息。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "I cleaned the chain with an old tooth brush and some special oil."  
【解析】用旧牙刷和油清洗链条。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "I felt a kind of pride that only fixing something with your own hands can give you."  
【解析】作者感到自豪而开心。A、B、D 情绪不符。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段 "Some small skills pass from one pair of hands to another."  
【解析】作者感悟：家庭中小技能代代相传。A 与内容无关；C 与合作经历相反；D 为表面细节。故选 B。
