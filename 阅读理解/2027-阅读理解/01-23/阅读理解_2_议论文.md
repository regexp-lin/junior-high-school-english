# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should Middle-School Students Start a Second Foreign Language?**

① English is the first foreign language in most Chinese middle schools. In recent years, some schools and parents have begun to talk about a second foreign language — Japanese, Spanish, French or Russian. They ask: should middle-school students start a second foreign language so early?

② Those who support the idea mention three main reasons. First, young teenagers learn new sounds and grammar quickly. The earlier we start, the easier it is for us to get a natural pronunciation. Second, a second language opens a door to another culture. We can read stories, watch films and make friends that we could not reach with only Chinese and English. Third, it may help future studies and jobs. In many international companies, workers who know three languages are more welcome.

③ However, some teachers and parents worry about the problems. Middle-school students already have many subjects and exams. Adding a new language may make them too tired and put more pressure on their sleep. If English itself is not yet strong, two foreign languages may confuse students. Besides, not every student is interested in language. For a child who loves sports or science, extra language classes may be less useful than a robotics club or a football practice.

④ In my view, a second foreign language can be a gift, but only if it is offered in the right way. Schools should set it as a club or a selective course, not a required exam subject. Students who truly enjoy the language will choose to join, and those who prefer other activities can use their time on their own strengths. We should also keep a good English base first, so that a second language becomes a new window, not a heavy bag.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main question in this passage?**

A. Whether English should be dropped from middle school.  
B. Whether middle-school students should start a second foreign language.  
C. Why Japanese is more popular than French.  
D. How to teach grammar in high school.

**2. According to Paragraph ②, why can young teenagers learn a new language easily?**

A. Because they never forget anything.  
B. Because they learn new sounds and grammar quickly.  
C. Because they usually travel to other countries.  
D. Because they have no homework at all.

**3. Which of the following is a reason AGAINST starting a second foreign language?**

A. Teenagers have too many holidays.  
B. Parents cannot read the new textbooks.  
C. Adding a new language may tire students and confuse English learning.  
D. Languages are not taught well in any country.

**4. What does the writer suggest schools do?**

A. Make the second language a required exam subject.  
B. Stop teaching English for one year.  
C. Offer the second language as a club or a selective course.  
D. Only teach a second language in university.

**5. What does the writer mean by "a new window, not a heavy bag"?**

A. The second language should feel too easy.  
B. The second language should be learned only in spring.  
C. The second language should bring joy and new views, not heavy pressure.  
D. The second language should replace English.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段末句 "should middle-school students start a second foreign language so early?"  
【解析】全文围绕是否学二外。A、C、D 均偏离主题。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "young teenagers learn new sounds and grammar quickly."  
【解析】少年学习发音和语法快。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Adding a new language may make them too tired... two foreign languages may confuse students."  
【解析】会使学生疲惫并混淆英语。A、B、D 均不符原文。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Schools should set it as a club or a selective course, not a required exam subject."  
【解析】作者建议以社团或选修方式开设。A、B、D 与原文相反。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】词义/句义猜测题  
【定位】第④段 "a second language becomes a new window, not a heavy bag."  
【解析】比喻：第二外语应带来新视野，而不是负担。A、B、D 为字面误读。故选 C。
