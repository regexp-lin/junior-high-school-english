# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua School — Autumn Charity Book Sale 2027**

---

## **What Is the Book Sale?**

On **Saturday, October 23, 2027**, Lihua Middle School will hold its 7th **Autumn Charity Book Sale** in the Big Playground, from **9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.** Every student and teacher is warmly invited to bring along their **second-hand books**, sell them at a very small price, and give **all the money** to the **Dandelion Education Fund**, which builds small libraries for schools in the mountains of western China.

In 2026, our sale raised 18,500 yuan and helped one mountain school build a reading room with 900 new books.

---

## **Who Can Sell?**

- **Any student** from Grade 7 to Grade 9 at our school.  
- **Teachers and parents** are welcome to set up their own small table too.  
- Each student can take only **one shared booth** with at most **3 classmates**.  
- **Primary-school siblings** are welcome, but an older student must stay with them at the booth.

---

## **What You Can Bring**

| Welcome | Not Allowed |
|---|---|
| Storybooks, novels, comics (in Chinese or English) | Textbooks printed after 2024 (students still need them) |
| Old but clean workbooks with **no answers filled in** | Workbooks **already filled in** with answers |
| Magazines and picture books | Books with damaged or missing pages |
| Foreign-language readers (graded readers) | Books with badly torn covers or water stains |
| Maps, atlases, art books | Photocopies or pirated books |

---

## **Price Rules**

1. Paperback novels and comics: **3 – 8 yuan** each.  
2. Hardcover books: **6 – 20 yuan** each.  
3. Magazines and thin picture books: **1 – 3 yuan** each.  
4. Book sets (2 or more books together): may be sold as a package; please mark the price clearly.  
5. **Free books** for mountain-school friends: if you decide not to sell a book, you may put it in the **pink "Free Box"**, and we will send it to a mountain school directly.

Please do **not** sell a single paperback for more than 8 yuan. Our aim is to make good books easy to buy, not to earn a big profit.

---

## **How to Set Up Your Booth**

- Come to the Big Playground between **8:00 – 8:40 a.m.** on Oct 23.  
- Find your booth number (your teacher will tell you this on Oct 21).  
- Each booth has **one desk and two stools**. You may bring a small tablecloth, a price board, and a decoration (no balloons with helium).  
- **Electronic payment is allowed**, but please keep a **printed record** of every sale of 5 yuan or more, so we can check the total.  
- At **4:15 p.m.**, all money must be handed in to the student volunteers at the red tent near the gate.

## **Rewards for Students**

- **"Reading Star" certificate** for every student who donates **10 books or more**.  
- **Top 5 selling booths**: a hand-made wooden book-stand, made by the Grade 9 craft club.  
- **Most Creative Booth** (chosen by teachers): a one-year free membership of the school reading club.  
- Any student who donates **more than 30 books** will have their name in the school's yearly "Kind Readers" list.

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main purpose of the Lihua Book Sale?**

A. To sell new textbooks to other schools.  
B. To sell second-hand books cheaply and give all the money to the Dandelion Education Fund to help mountain-school libraries.  
C. To buy expensive hardcover books at a high price.  
D. To replace the school library with a new one.

**2. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed at the sale?**

A. Old but clean workbooks with no answers filled in.  
B. Textbooks printed after 2024 which students still need.  
C. A graded English reader.  
D. A picture book in good condition.

**3. What is the maximum price of a single paperback novel or comic?**

A. 3 yuan.  
B. 8 yuan.  
C. 20 yuan.  
D. 50 yuan.

**4. A Grade 9 student, Lin, wants to share a booth with her classmates. What is the largest number of students allowed in one booth?**

A. Up to 2 students.  
B. Up to 4 students (Lin and 3 classmates).  
C. As many students as want to join.  
D. Only Lin alone.

**5. Ben donates 32 books and sells many of them during the day. Which rewards can he probably win?**

A. Only a wooden book-stand.  
B. A "Reading Star" certificate + his name in the school's yearly "Kind Readers" list, plus any other prize his booth earns.  
C. Nothing, because only top 5 booths get rewards.  
D. Free textbooks for two years.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】What Is the Book Sale?  
【解析】卖二手书筹款捐给蒲公英基金会。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】What You Can Bring 表  
【解析】2024 年后教材不允许出售。A/C/D 均允许。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Price Rules 第1条与下方说明  
【解析】单本平装最多8元。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Who Can Sell?  
【解析】一个摊位最多4人（1+3）。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】Rewards for Students  
【解析】捐 10+ 拿证书；30+ 上"善读者榜"；摊位奖另计。A/C/D 均偏。故选 B。
