# 阅读理解（应用文） **Student Tour Guides Wanted — Bright Future Campus Open Day**

① On **Saturday, 24 April 2027**, our school will hold its yearly **Campus Open Day** for parents, new students, and friends from the community. More than 800 visitors are expected. To welcome them warmly, the Students' Union is looking for **30 student tour guides** from Grade 7 and Grade 8. Could that be you?

② **What Will a Tour Guide Do?**

- Greet visitors at the school main gate with a smile.  
- Take small groups (up to **10 people each**) along one of two routes:  
  - **Green Route**: classrooms → library → art rooms → sports ground.  
  - **Blue Route**: science labs → maker space → music hall → rooftop garden.  
- Each tour lasts **40 minutes**; explain the main stops in simple English or Chinese.  
- Answer short questions; for harder ones, kindly lead the visitor to the **Help Desk** in the main hall.

③ **Who Can Apply?**

- Any student in Grade 7 or Grade 8.  
- Friendly, clear speaking voice in Chinese **OR** English.  
- Tidy school uniform on the day; short hair or a neat ponytail.  
- Able to stand and walk for **at least 2 hours**.  
- No serious cold or fever on the open day.

④ **Key Dates**

- **31 March**　Sign-up opens on the Students' Union website.  
- **14 April**　Sign-up closes at 18:00.  
- **16 April**　Short voice test in the music room (2 minutes each).  
- **18 April**　Name list posted on the **red notice board**, Floor 1 of the Office Building.  
- **22 April**　Group training: route walk-through + Q&A practice, 15:00 – 16:30.  
- **24 April**　Open Day! Arrive by **8:15 a.m.** at the Students' Union office.

⑤ **What You Get**

- A special blue **"Student Guide" badge** to keep.  
- A small gift bag: school notebook, pen, and a lemon drink.  
- Community-service points, which will be signed by the head teacher.  
- Lunch box at 12:00 in the canteen, free for all volunteers.  
- A group photo in front of the main gate at 16:30.

⑥ **Simple Rules**

1. Be patient with older visitors; speak slowly and clearly.  
2. **Do not run** in the corridors; walk on the right side.  
3. Help any visitor who looks lost, even if he or she is not in your group.  
4. No snacks in the library; remind visitors kindly.  
5. If you feel tired or unwell, tell Mr. Guan at the Help Desk at once.

⑦ **Contact**  
- Students' Union Secretary, Jin Xinyue — xy.jin@bfms.cn  
- Mr. Guan, Students' Union Teacher — Room 112, Office Building, 12:20 – 13:00

**Come, show your school with your own words — become a friendly bridge for every visitor that day!**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many student tour guides does the school want to choose?**

A. 10.  
B. 20.  
C. 30.  
D. 40.

**2. Which of the following places is on the Blue Route?**

A. The library.  
B. The art rooms.  
C. The science labs.  
D. The sports ground.

**3. What will happen on 16 April?**

A. The open day will be held.  
B. The sign-up will open on the website.  
C. A short voice test will take place in the music room.  
D. All visitors will receive a free lemon drink.

**4. Which of the following is NOT listed as a rule for tour guides?**

A. Walk slowly and on the right side of the corridor.  
B. Let visitors eat snacks freely in the library.  
C. Help lost visitors even if they are not in your group.  
D. Be patient and speak slowly with older visitors.

**5. Who should students contact if they have more questions about the open day?**

A. The school gate guard.  
B. The canteen chef.  
C. Mr. Guan or Jin Xinyue, Students' Union.  
D. The main hall cleaner.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "looking for 30 student tour guides"  
【解析】招募 30 名。A、B、D 不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】路线匹配题  
【定位】第②段 Blue Route: science labs → maker space → music hall → rooftop garden  
【解析】蓝色路线含科学实验室。A、B、D 属绿色路线。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】日期定位题  
【定位】第④段 "16 April — Short voice test in the music room"  
【解析】4 月 16 日是声音测试。A 为 24 号；B 为 31 号；D 为活动当天礼品。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】否定细节题  
【定位】第⑥段第 4 条 "No snacks in the library"  
【解析】图书馆禁零食。A、C、D 都是规则。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】联系人查找题  
【定位】第⑦段联系信息  
【解析】应联系学生会秘书金心月或管老师。A、B、D 无依据。故选 C。
