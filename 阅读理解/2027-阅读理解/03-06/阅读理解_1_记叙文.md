# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The New Classmate from the Grasslands**

① On a cold Monday morning in October, our head teacher Ms. Huang opened the classroom door and walked in with a tall boy. He stood beside her quietly, holding a small blue bag with a sheep picture on it. "Class," she said, "this is Batu. His family just moved from Inner Mongolia. Please be friendly." We said hello, but Batu only gave a small nod. He sat down alone at the empty desk beside the window.

② At lunch, most of us pulled out the canteen rice box, but Batu took out a thin flatbread and some pale yellow cheese. A few boys at the back giggled. Batu's ears turned red. He put the cheese back into the bag and ate only the flatbread. I felt bad, but I did not dare to say anything.

③ The next day in PE class, our teacher said, "Today we run 1,000 metres." Most of us moaned, but Batu's eyes lit up. When the whistle blew, he flew out like a small horse. Halfway, he was already fifty metres ahead of everyone else. At the finish line, he was not even out of breath. Suddenly, no one was laughing any more; we were all asking, "How can you run so fast?"

④ That afternoon, during the ten-minute break, I walked over to his desk. I pointed at the sheep picture on his bag. "Is it from your hometown?" I asked. Batu looked up, and for the first time, his eyes smiled. "Yes. My grandfather is a **herdsman**," he said. "I used to run from one sheep to another every morning. That's how I learned to run." He took out a small photo of a white lamb and a green sky that seemed bigger than any sky I had ever seen.

⑤ Soon the classroom was full of voices. "Can you show us tomorrow?" "Did you ride horses?" Batu answered one question after another, his face shining. When the bell rang, he gave me half of his cheese. It tasted salty and a little sour, but warm, like a small gift from a far-away field.

⑥ Now Batu is our class champion in every running race. But what I remember best is not his running. It is the moment I stopped watching him from my seat and walked to his desk. Ms. Huang later told us, "Being kind is not hard. Sometimes it is just taking **three steps** to say hello." Three small steps, I learned, can carry a friendship from the grasslands all the way to our classroom.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How did Batu look when he first entered the classroom?**

A. Loud and excited.  
B. Quiet and a little shy.  
C. Angry and tired.  
D. Sleepy and hungry.

**2. Why did Batu put the yellow cheese back into his bag at lunch?**

A. He did not like the taste of it.  
B. Some boys laughed at him and his ears turned red.  
C. He wanted to save it for his mother.  
D. The canteen rules did not allow cheese.

**3. What happened in the 1,000-metre run?**

A. Batu got tired at the halfway point.  
B. Everyone ran faster than Batu.  
C. Batu finished far ahead and was not even out of breath.  
D. The PE teacher stopped the race.

**4. What do we know about Batu's life back in Inner Mongolia?**

A. He never went outside in winter.  
B. He lived in a big city centre.  
C. He used to run between sheep at his grandfather's home.  
D. His family owned a big cheese factory.

**5. What does the writer mainly want to tell us?**

A. Running is the best sport for students.  
B. Kindness is simple — sometimes it is just a few steps to say hello.  
C. Cheese from the grasslands is better than city food.  
D. PE teachers should test new students first.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "stood beside her quietly... only gave a small nod... sat down alone"  
【解析】Batu 安静、仅点头、独自坐下，体现腼腆安静。A、C、D 均与描述不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "A few boys at the back giggled. Batu's ears turned red. He put the cheese back"  
【解析】因男生嘲笑而感到难为情。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "fifty metres ahead... not even out of breath"  
【解析】Batu 遥遥领先且不喘气。A、B 与原文相反；D 项老师停赛无依据。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "My grandfather is a herdsman... I used to run from one sheep to another"  
【解析】他曾在爷爷的牧群间奔跑。A、B、D 均无依据。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑥段末两句 "Being kind is not hard. Sometimes it is just taking three steps to say hello."  
【解析】作者借 Ms. Huang 的话点出"善意其实很简单"的主旨。A、C、D 均偏题。故选 B。
