# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The 800-Metre Race I Feared**

① The spring sports meeting came around again in our school. For most of my classmates, it was a happy day — lots of cheering, lots of ice pops, no classes. For me, it was a **quiet nightmare**. Our PE teacher, Mr. Kong, had written my name on the list of **800-metre runners**. I had asked him three times to change it; he only smiled and said, "Trust me, Xiaoyu. Running 800 metres will not kill you. But running away from it will make you smaller every year."

② On the morning of the race, my legs felt like two pieces of wet bread. The playground looked **twice as long** as usual. I could see my family in the stand — Mum holding a small paper flag, Dad filming with his phone, and little brother Dabao waving a plastic whistle he had made from an empty water bottle. Their smiles did not make me braver; they made me more afraid of losing face.

③ "On your marks… set… go!" The whistle cut the air. We flew out of the starting line. In the first 300 metres, I pushed hard, my breath short, my shoes slapping the red track. Soon three students were ahead of me; then four; then five. A sour pain rose from my side like a slow fire. I thought, **I will just quit. Who cares about the 800?** My feet nearly slowed down.

④ Then I heard it — a small, sharp sound from the stand. Dabao was blowing his home-made whistle. It sounded silly, almost like a baby duck. "Go, Jiejie! Slow is okay! You are still running!" he shouted. For a moment, I could not believe he, a seven-year-old, was cheering in **English**. He had been practising this sentence for two weeks. I suddenly felt something warm come up from my chest to my throat.

⑤ I did not run faster. In fact, I let myself **breathe more**. Two boys passed me; I did not try to keep up. I only promised myself one thing: **I would not stop until I reached the finish line.** The last 200 metres were the longest. My legs burned. My face was wet with sweat — and maybe a few tears, though I could not tell. I crossed the line in **7th place out of 10**. My time was far behind the first runner.

⑥ After the race, I half-lay on the grass. I expected Mum to say, "It's okay, next time." Instead, Dad came first, pulled me up gently, and said, "You did the **hardest part** today, Xiaoyu. You stayed in." Dabao rushed over with the whistle and said proudly in English, "Good job, Jiejie." I laughed until my side hurt. That evening, I wrote in my diary: "Today I was slow. But today I did not run away. And that, maybe, is the first real victory of my life."

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the writer fear the 800-metre race?**

A. She had never run on a playground before.  
B. She believed she was not good at running long distances.  
C. Her PE teacher refused to teach her any running skills.  
D. The weather that day was very bad.

**2. What did the writer think when five students ran ahead of her?**

A. She wanted to run even faster and catch up quickly.  
B. She wanted to stop and quit the race.  
C. She wanted to cheat and cut the track.  
D. She wanted to shout at her little brother.

**3. What did Dabao do that warmed the writer's heart?**

A. He gave her a bottle of cold water.  
B. He ran beside her for 100 metres.  
C. He shouted a sentence of English cheer with his home-made whistle.  
D. He waved a giant school flag for all runners.

**4. What place did the writer finally get in the race?**

A. 1st out of 10.  
B. 3rd out of 10.  
C. 7th out of 10.  
D. Last place.

**5. What is the main idea of the last paragraph?**

A. The writer was sad that she did not win a medal.  
B. Winning is the only thing that matters in sport.  
C. Not running away and finishing the race was her first real victory.  
D. The writer decided never to enter a race again.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "For me, it was a quiet nightmare... asked him three times to change it"  
【解析】自认不擅长而惧怕。A、C、D 无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "I will just quit..."  
【解析】想放弃。A、C、D 与原文不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 Dabao 用自制哨子并用英语喊 "Go, Jiejie!"  
【解析】英语加油+哨声温暖了"我"。A、B、D 无依据。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "7th place out of 10"  
【解析】第 7 名。A、B、D 不符。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨归纳题  
【定位】第⑥段 "did not run away... first real victory"  
【解析】坚持到底本身就是胜利。A、B、D 与主旨不符。故选 C。
