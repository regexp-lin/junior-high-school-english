# 阅读理解（应用文） **School Open Day — Parents' Schedule & Rules**

**LONGYUAN MIDDLE SCHOOL**
*Open Day for Parents — Thursday 24 June 2027*

---

**Dear Parents,**
Thank you for coming to our school. This sheet tells you what happens during the day and what we ask you to do or not to do. Please read it carefully before you arrive. Keep this sheet with you; you may need it at the gate.

**Schedule**

| Time | Place | Activity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 08:00 – 08:30 | Main gate | Parents register and get a blue visitor badge |
| 08:30 – 09:15 | Hall A | Welcome talk by the headmaster |
| 09:30 – 11:00 | Classrooms | Watch 2 classes (one is your child's class) |
| 11:15 – 12:00 | Library | Science project show by Grade 8 |
| 12:00 – 13:00 | Small dining hall | Free simple lunch (rice, two dishes, soup, fruit) |
| 13:30 – 15:00 | Art room | Talk with homeroom teachers (one by one) |
| 15:30 – 16:00 | Playground | Closing — student choir |

**Registration**

- Bring **one** adult ID card (身份证).
- Only **one parent** per student may enter. Younger brothers or sisters cannot come in (for safety reasons).
- Please arrive before **8:30**. Late parents will join from 09:00 outside Classroom 3-5.

**Basic rules inside the school**

1. Please wear your blue visitor badge on your jacket at all times.
2. Turn your phone to **silent mode** before entering any classroom.
3. During class watching, sit at the back of the classroom. Do not walk around, take photos or whisper with your neighbour.
4. During the talk with the homeroom teacher, each parent has **8 minutes**. If more time is needed, please book a second meeting at the teacher's office (Room 205) after 16:00.
5. Please do not enter the computer rooms, the gym, or the teachers' office upstairs. These are not part of the Open Day.

**Food and drinks**

- The free lunch is only for the parents who have registered at the main gate.
- Please do not bring hot drinks into the library or the art room.
- Tap water and hot water are free at the water station outside Hall A.

**Leaving early**

- If you must leave before the closing ceremony, please return the visitor badge to the security desk at the gate. If you forget, please post it back within three days.

**Questions on the day**

- For small questions, ask any teacher with a yellow armband. They are "Open Day helpers".
- For urgent family calls, please use the quiet phone room (next to Hall A). Do not make long calls in the corridor.

**Before you arrive, please**

1. Read your child's class number carefully (it is on the bottom of this sheet).
2. Print and bring this page; we do not provide extra copies.
3. Dress simply and walk quietly — our students have tests that afternoon in the same building.

**Thank you for your support. We look forward to seeing you.**
Teaching Office, Longyuan Middle School · 021-xxxx-6690 · office@longyuan.cn

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 08:00 – 08:30 "Parents register... Main gate"  
【解析】正门登记。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Only one parent per student may enter"  
【解析】每生仅一名家长。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】第 3 条规则  
【解析】听课时不得走动、拍照、交头接耳。A/B/D 均为允许/要求。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"each parent has 8 minutes"  
【解析】8 分钟。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Leaving early"  
【解析】归还徽章或三日内寄回。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
