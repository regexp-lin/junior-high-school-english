# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Old Man Who Fed the Birds**

Every morning on my way to school, I passed a small park called Willow Garden. Next to the stone bench near the entrance, an old man was always there, sitting very still with a cloth bag on his knees. Inside the bag was a pile of yellow rice. Ten, twenty, sometimes more than fifty birds surrounded him — sparrows, pigeons, sometimes even a few small crows. He threw the rice gently, handful after handful. The birds did not seem afraid of him at all.

I passed him for over a year without ever speaking to him. His face was brown and lined like the bark of an old tree. He wore the same grey coat every day, winter or summer. I thought he was a bit strange. One cold morning in January, I walked past, and he was not there. The birds were, but they were flying in circles above the stone bench, as if waiting.

The next day, and the day after, he still did not come. The birds became fewer and fewer. By the end of the week, only three or four sparrows remained, looking thin.

That Friday, my grandmother, who lived near Willow Garden, told me something new. "Did you hear about Old Master Feng? The one who used to feed the birds? He fell on the ice and broke his leg last Monday. He's in the hospital now." I was surprised. I did not know his name until that moment.

My grandmother added quietly, "His son died young. His wife passed away ten years ago. Feeding the birds has been his only work for the last five winters." I held my schoolbag a little tighter and said nothing.

That Saturday, Grandma and I bought a small bag of yellow rice and took bus 7 to the hospital. Old Master Feng was alone in a small white room. He did not know us. But when Grandma said, "We saw your birds. We want to help while you are here," his eyes became wet. He could not speak for a minute. Then he pointed at a drawer beside the bed, where there was an old map of Willow Garden, with every favourite tree of every bird group marked.

For the next six weeks, Grandma and I walked to Willow Garden almost every morning. We used his map. At first, only two sparrows came. Then five, then fifteen. By the day Old Master Feng was able to leave the hospital, there were more than forty birds waiting for him on the stone bench, exactly as before.

When we took him back to the park in a wheelchair, he did not say much. He only touched the cloth bag in his lap, looked up at the trees, and began, again, to throw the rice gently. The birds rushed down around him. I stood behind the bench, and for a long time, I could not see clearly. There was too much sunlight in my eyes — or maybe it was something else.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Where did the old man usually feed the birds?**

A. In the main square of the city.  
B. In the school playground.  
C. Near a stone bench at the entrance of Willow Garden.  
D. On the roof of an old building.

**2. Why was the old man absent one January morning?**

A. Because the weather was too warm for birds.  
B. Because he fell on the ice and broke his leg.  
C. Because he had moved to a new city.  
D. Because he was angry with the narrator.

**3. How did the narrator find out the old man's name?**

A. From a teacher at school.  
B. From a small paper note on the bench.  
C. From his grandmother, who lived near the park.  
D. From a newspaper article.

**4. What did the narrator and his grandmother do while the old man was in hospital?**

A. They asked the police to close the park.  
B. They started feeding the birds in Willow Garden using his old map.  
C. They taught the birds how to fly south.  
D. They sold the yellow rice in the market.

**5. What is the best title for the story?**

A. My New School Bag.  
B. The Old Man Who Fed the Birds.  
C. A Cold Winter in the City Hospital.  
D. How to Make a Bird Map.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 段  
【解析】柳园入口石凳旁。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 段 "He fell on the ice and broke his leg"  
【解析】摔倒骨折住院。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 段 "my grandmother... told me something new"  
【解析】从奶奶处得知。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 7 段 "walked to Willow Garden almost every morning. We used his map"  
【解析】替老人每日喂鸟。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】标题归纳题  
【定位】全文  
【解析】围绕喂鸟老人展开。A/C/D 均片面。故选 B。
