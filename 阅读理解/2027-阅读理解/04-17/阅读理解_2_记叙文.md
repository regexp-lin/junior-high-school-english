# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Tiny Stray Cat Who Chose Our Doorstep**

① One cold Wednesday evening, I came home from cram school and found a **tiny, grey cat** sitting on the mat outside our flat door. Its fur was wet from the rain, and one of its back legs looked hurt. The moment I stopped in front of it, two round **green eyes** looked straight up at me, without fear, as if saying, "I have been waiting for you." I quickly opened the door, and the cat **limped in** as easily as an old friend visiting after a long trip.

② Mum was not happy. "Yawei, we cannot keep a cat. Grandpa is allergic, and your exams are one month away." I knew she was right. But the cat was shivering and had not eaten for days. Dad gently brought a small box, a warm towel, and a saucer of warm milk. "Tonight only," he said, "then tomorrow we find a better home." That night, I sat on the floor beside the box and watched the cat slowly sip the milk, the way a quiet, hopeful child might sip a long-awaited bowl of soup.

③ The next morning, the cat was still weak. I named him "**Grey Button**" because of the grey colour and his small, button-like eyes. His back leg had a bite from another animal. I used a clean cloth to wipe it, the way Grandma taught me on my own knee last year. He did not move. He did not cry. He only looked at me with those two quiet green circles. Somehow, in that moment, I knew I could not send him away that day.

④ That evening, I walked in a soft rain to the neighbourhood **animal clinic**, carrying Grey Button in a paper box. Dr. Xu, a kind young vet, said the wound was small, and gave me a tiny tube of medicine. "Keep him warm, clean the wound once a day, and give him a little rice with chicken for three days," she said. The clinic cost was 45 yuan. I had saved 50 from my New Year money. I paid with pride, feeling **five yuan richer than the whole world**.

⑤ For the next ten days, Grey Button stayed in a corner of our kitchen. Before school, I cleaned his wound; after school, I played with him using a string. Slowly, he began to hop around on three legs, then four. Mum, who had said "No cat", suddenly started calling him by name. One Saturday morning, she even cooked a tiny piece of fish for him, pretending it was "left over from our own breakfast". Dad and I looked at each other and smiled quietly.

⑥ A full month later, my mid-term exams finished. When I came home, Grey Button had not only healed — he had a new **small blue collar** that Mum had bought, and a bowl with his name painted on it. Grandpa, who sat far on the sofa with his allergy medicine beside him, said, "He is a polite cat. He keeps his distance and does not make me sneeze. He is welcome to stay — if Yawei remembers to clean up after him."

⑦ Grey Button still follows me to the lift every morning, waits quietly at the window when I leave for school, and jumps onto my desk at night. Sometimes I think he found us, not the other way round. A small wet cat on a cold doorstep taught me something no textbook ever could: **kindness is not a big action; it is a small daily choice, made again and again, with warm hands and an open door.**

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Where did the writer first see the cat?**

A. In a park near the school.  
B. Outside the flat door on the mat.  
C. In the animal clinic.  
D. On the city bus.

**2. Why was Mum NOT happy at first?**

A. The cat was too noisy.  
B. Grandpa was allergic and the writer's exams were coming.  
C. The cat was too old.  
D. Dad already kept five cats.

**3. Why did the writer name the cat "Grey Button"?**

A. Because the cat liked buttons.  
B. Because of its grey colour and button-like eyes.  
C. Because the cat was very small.  
D. Because a neighbour called it so.

**4. How much did the clinic visit cost?**

A. 30 yuan.  
B. 45 yuan.  
C. 50 yuan.  
D. 80 yuan.

**5. What lesson did the writer learn from this experience?**

A. Cats always bring illnesses to a family.

D. Students should spend all their money on pets.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "on the mat outside our flat door"  
【解析】家门口门垫。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Grandpa is allergic, and your exams are one month away"  
【解析】过敏+期中考。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段命名原因  
【解析】灰色+扣子般眼睛。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "45 yuan"  
【解析】45元。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨归纳题  
【定位】第⑦段末  
【解析】善良是日常小选择。故选 B。
