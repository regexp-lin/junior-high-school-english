# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Charity Run 2027 — 5 km & 10 km for Rural Schools**

Dear friends and neighbours,

The **Blue River City Charity Run** will return this autumn for its tenth year. Every kilometre you run will help build reading corners in **rural primary schools** across our province. Runners of all ages are welcome — from parents with strollers to grandparents with walking sticks, and of course, our energetic middle-school students.

---

### Key Information

- **Date:** Sunday, October 17, 2027  
- **Place:** Starts at Blue River Park (North Gate) and ends at Music Square  
- **Routes:**  
    - 5 km "Sunrise Route" — flat, along the river  
    - 10 km "Forest Route" — mixed, with two small hills  
- **Start times:**  
    - 5 km Group: 07:30  
    - 10 km Group: 08:00  
- **Finish cut-off:** 11:00 (everyone must cross the finish line or ride the support bus)

---

### Who Can Join

| Age group | Event options | Special notes |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| 6 – 11 | 5 km only, with a parent or an adult family member | Must wear a yellow bib |
| 12 – 15 | 5 km or 10 km | Middle-school runners get free juice after the race |
| 16 – 59 | 5 km or 10 km | — |
| 60 and above | 5 km only | Must bring a recent health check within 3 months |

Pregnant participants and people who have had a cold for more than 3 days are asked to cheer this year instead of running.

---

### Entry Fee & Charity

- 5 km: ¥30 per person  
- 10 km: ¥60 per person  
- Students aged 12–15: **half price** with a student card

**Every yuan goes to the charity.** Running costs (water, medical team, finish bag) are paid by our business partners. In 2026, runners raised ¥880,000, which built **22 reading corners** in mountain schools.

---

### What You Will Get

- A cotton T-shirt (2027 green design, choose size at registration)  
- A paper number bib with a timing chip on the back  
- A cloth finish bag with water, a banana, a small towel, and a bookmark made by the rural students you are helping  
- A digital certificate by email within two days

---

### Safety Rules

1. **Walking is fine.** The slogan this year is: "Cross the line any way you can."  
2. Headphones with music are **NOT** allowed on the route, to keep ears free for warnings.  
3. Keep to the **right side** of the road; runners who need to pass should wave a hand and call out.  
4. First-aid stations are set up every 2 km; blue flags mark their location.  
5. In case of heavy rain or thunder, the event will be postponed to the following Sunday (October 24).

### How to Sign Up

- Online: **www.bluerivercharityrun.cn** from now until **11:59, Friday, October 8, 2027**.  
- On paper: collect a form from any City Post Office; hand it in by **October 1**.  
- Pay the fee online or at the post office. A confirmation message will be sent within 24 hours.

### Sign-Up in Teams

A "School Team" needs **4 or more students from the same school**, plus one teacher as the team leader. Teams share an extra benefit: a group photo at the finish and a thank-you letter signed by students from the rural schools you help.

### Contact

- **Phone:** 0571-8899-1017 (Mon–Fri, 10:00–17:00)  
- **Email:** run@bluerivercharity.cn  
- **WeChat:** search "BlueRiverRun2027"

**Run, walk, smile, help — every step you take will become a shelf of books.**

*— Blue River Charity Foundation, July 2027*

**1. What is the main purpose of the 2027 City Charity Run?**

B. He pays ¥30 (half price with a student card) and can also get free juice after the race.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】开头"Every kilometre you run will help build reading corners..."  
【解析】募款为乡村小学建阅读角。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】费用表 + Who Can Join 表  
【解析】12–15 岁学生半价 30 元，赛后免费果汁。A 错：可参加；C 错：不需健康证明；D 错：无此规定。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Safety Rules 第 2 条 "Headphones with music are NOT allowed..."  
【解析】比赛中不允许戴耳机听音乐。A/C/D 均允许。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Safety Rules 第 5 条 "postponed to the following Sunday..."  
【解析】延期至 10 月 24 日。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Sign-Up in Teams "a group photo... thank-you letter..."  
【解析】团体合照与感谢信。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
