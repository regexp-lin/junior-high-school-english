> **01-13 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Shopping with Grandma Liu**

① Grandma Liu lives next door to me. She is almost eighty years old and her legs are not strong, but she still likes to cook fresh food herself. Last Saturday morning, I saw her standing at the gate with an empty basket. "Lily," she said with a smile, "could you please come to the vegetable market with me?" I said yes at once.

② The market was busy and full of noise. Sellers were shouting about fresh fish and cheap apples. Grandma Liu held my arm tightly. We walked slowly from one stall to another. I helped her choose green vegetables, two tomatoes and a small bag of eggs. She told me how to tell whether eggs were fresh by holding them up to the light. I had never known that before.

③ At the fruit corner, a young seller gave us a wrong bill. He said the apples were 35 yuan, but my quick calculation showed it should be only 28. Grandma Liu was confused and ready to pay more. I politely pointed out the mistake. The seller checked again, thanked me, and gave us the right price. Grandma Liu patted my shoulder and said, "Math is really useful in daily life, isn't it?"

④ On our way home, Grandma Liu could not carry the heavy basket, so I took it for her. My arms became sore, but I felt proud. When we got back, she asked me to stay for lunch. She cooked egg-tomato noodles, and the smell filled her small kitchen. It was one of the best noodles I had ever tasted.

⑤ Before I left, Grandma Liu held my hand and thanked me again. I told her it was nothing — just a short walk to the market. But on my way upstairs, I realized that helping an old neighbor was more than shopping. It made two lonely mornings turn into one warm Saturday for both of us.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did Grandma Liu ask Lily to go to the market with her?**

A. Because her legs were not very strong.  
B. Because she did not know the way to the market.  
C. Because she wanted to buy a present for Lily.  
D. Because the market was holding a special sale.

**2. What new thing did Lily learn at the market?**

A. How to cook egg-tomato noodles.  
B. How to choose fresh eggs.  
C. How to bargain for cheap fish.  
D. How to grow green vegetables.

**3. Why did the young fruit seller give the wrong bill?**

A. He was trying to cheat old people on purpose.  
B. He could not speak Chinese very well.  
C. It seemed to be a careless mistake in calculation.  
D. He wanted to give Lily a special discount.

**4. How did Lily feel on her way home?**

A. Tired but proud.  
B. Angry and sad.  
C. Cold and hungry.  
D. Afraid and nervous.

**5. What is the main message of the passage?**

A. Young people should avoid busy markets.  
B. Math is only useful inside the classroom.  
C. Small kind acts can warm hearts on both sides.  
D. Grandmothers should do all shopping by themselves.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "her legs are not strong... could you please come to the vegetable market with me?"  
【解析】因为腿脚不便，所以请 Lily 陪同。B、C、D 文中无依据。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段末句 "She told me how to tell whether eggs were fresh by holding them up to the light. I had never known that before."  
【解析】Lily 第一次学会如何分辨鸡蛋新鲜度。A 面条是回家后吃的；C 讨价还价文中未提；D 种菜无依据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第③段 "The seller checked again, thanked me, and gave us the right price."  
【解析】卖家核对后致谢并更正，可见是计算失误而非故意欺骗。A 故意骗老人属过度推断；B 语言问题无依据；D 折扣与原文相反。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "My arms became sore, but I felt proud."  
【解析】原文直接指出她"累但自豪"。B、C、D 情绪不符。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段 "helping an old neighbor... made two lonely mornings turn into one warm Saturday for both of us."  
【解析】作者强调一个小小的善举让两代人都感到温暖。A 避开菜市场片面；B 与文中计算经历矛盾；D 与"请求陪同"矛盾。故选 C。

**词汇注释（超纲词）**  
- **stall** [n.] 货摊  
- **sore** [adj.] 酸痛的
