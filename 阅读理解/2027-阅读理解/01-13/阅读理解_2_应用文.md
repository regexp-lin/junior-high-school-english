# 阅读理解（应用文） **Green Hearts Community Tree-Planting Day**

① Green Hearts Community is pleased to invite all residents and middle-school volunteers to join our yearly **Community Tree-Planting Day**. Together, we can add more color to our streets and bring cleaner air to our homes.

② Please read the following information carefully before signing up.

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date & Time | Saturday, March 14, 8:30 a.m. – 11:30 a.m. |
| Place | West Hill Park (North Gate) |
| For whom | Community residents & students aged 12–18 |
| Bring | Gloves, a hat, a water bottle, and a lunch box |
| Tools offered | Shovels, small trees, watering cans (all free) |
| Max. volunteers | 120 people (first come, first served) |

③ On that day, each volunteer will plant **2 to 3 small trees** with the help of our park workers. You will also learn the correct way to dig holes, set a young tree, and water it so that it has the best chance to grow well. Short breaks with fruit and warm tea will be offered at 10:00 a.m.

④ Students who finish the activity will receive a **three-hour community service certificate**, which is useful for their school records. Families that take part together will get a small gift – a pack of flower seeds for the balcony. All trees will be marked with the names of the volunteers, and you can come back in autumn to see how big "your" tree has grown.

⑤ To sign up, please scan the QR code on the community notice board, or send your name, age and phone number to **green.hearts@mail.com** before **March 10**. If the weather is bad on March 14, the event will move to the following Saturday. We look forward to seeing your green footprints in our park!

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main aim of the Tree-Planting Day?**

A. To sell trees to the residents at a low price.  
B. To invite people to plant trees and make the area greener.  
C. To choose the best gardener in the community.  
D. To test how fast students can dig holes.

**2. Which of the following is TRUE according to the table?**

A. The activity will last for six hours on Saturday.  
B. Only adults over 30 can take part.  
C. Volunteers need to bring their own shovels.  
D. Gloves and a water bottle should be brought by the volunteers.

**3. What will students get after they finish planting?**

C. A job at West Hill Park.

A. The event may be moved if the weather is bad.  
B. Latecomers will still be welcome any time in March.  
C. People must pay a fee when they sign up.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段 "add more color to our streets and bring cleaner air to our homes."  
【解析】活动目的是植树、增加绿色、改善空气。A 卖树、C 评选园丁、D 比赛挖坑均与内容不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Bring: Gloves, a hat, a water bottle, and a lunch box."  
【解析】手套和水壶确实需自带。A 活动只有 3 小时；B 接受 12–18 岁学生；C 铲子由主办方提供。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Students who finish the activity will receive a three-hour community service certificate."  
【解析】学生获得三小时社区服务证书。A 只提供茶点；C 工作、D 科技馆门票无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑤段 "If the weather is bad on March 14, the event will move to the following Saturday."  
【解析】天气不好时活动会改期。B 报名有 3 月 10 日截止日期；C、D 均无依据。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】写作出处/意图题  
【定位】全文面向社区居民及 12–18 岁学生的志愿活动通知。  
【解析】最可能吸引希望参加社区服务的学生。A、C、D 与通知主题不符。故选 B。
