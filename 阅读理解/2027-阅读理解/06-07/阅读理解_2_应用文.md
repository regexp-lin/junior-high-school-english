# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Green Village" — Summer Rural Volunteer Programme (Recruitment)**

**CITY YOUTH VOLUNTEER CENTRE · "GREEN VILLAGE" PROGRAMME**
*Six days in a small village. Six days close to the earth.*

---

**What is Green Village?**
A simple, short rural experience for city middle school students. Volunteers live with village families, help teachers at a small village primary school, and take part in farm work. We believe that one week in a rice field is more valuable than one hundred videos about the countryside.

**Dates and place**

- **Sunday 19 July – Friday 24 July, 2027** (6 days / 5 nights).
- **Qinghe Village**, two hours by bus from the city.
- A round-trip coach will be arranged by our centre.

**Daily activities (sample)**

- 06:30 – 07:00 Morning exercise (tai chi with village grandparents).
- 07:00 – 08:00 Breakfast with the host family.
- 08:30 – 11:30 Volunteer work (one of: teaching English at the village primary school / picking beans in a farmer's field / painting a community wall).
- 12:00 – 13:30 Lunch and short rest.
- 14:00 – 16:30 Skill classes (basket-weaving, paper-cutting, river-safety class).
- 17:00 – 18:30 Dinner + free time.
- 19:30 – 21:00 Evening circle (songs, stories, reflection).

**Eligibility**

- Any middle school student in **Grade 7 – Grade 9**.
- Must have a **basic health check** done in the past 6 months.
- Must be able to walk **3 km** and sit on a small wooden stool without problems.
- Students with serious pollen allergy should think carefully, because the village has many wild flowers.

**Cost**

- **600 yuan** per student (transport + food + stay + materials).
- **Reduced price 300 yuan** for students from low-income families (apply with a paper letter from your school).
- Extra free seats: **10 places** will be given each year to students whose parents work outside our city, so they can experience a different village life during summer.

**What to bring**

1. Personal clothes (short-sleeve shirts, long trousers for mosquitoes in the evening).
2. Rubber boots or old sports shoes for the rice field.
3. A reusable water bottle, a hat, and some sunscreen.
4. A small notebook and a pen — you will need them for the evening reflection.

**What NOT to bring**

- Expensive clothes, jewellery or brand-new shoes.
- Gaming devices, drones or speakers.
- More than 200 yuan in cash (we already cover meals).

**How to sign up**

1. Ask your homeroom teacher for a paper recommendation note.
2. Fill in the form at the City Youth Volunteer Centre, Room 401, **before Friday 26 June**.
3. Attend a 90-minute training on **Saturday 5 July, 14:00**, in the centre's hall.

**Safety & behaviour rules**

- Always stay with your partner during farm and river activities.
- Do not go to the village river alone at any time; even shallow water can be dangerous for new swimmers.
- Treat your host family's home as your own — keep it clean, and say "thank you" every meal.
- If you feel unwell or homesick, please speak to the team leader **Ms. Lin Xi** at any time. Leaving the programme early is allowed and free.

**Contact**
Ms. Lin Xi · 021-xxxx-7002 · greenvillage@cyvc.cn

*See you on a summer morning in Qinghe Village.*

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"6 days / 5 nights."  
【解析】6 天 5 夜。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"Volunteer work" 列表三项  
【解析】未提开拖拉机。A/B/C 均列出。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"600 yuan per student..."  
【解析】常规 600。A 为低收入减免价。C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"What NOT to bring · Gaming devices, drones or speakers."  
【解析】禁带游戏机、无人机、音箱。A/B/D 均允许或要求。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Do not go to the village river alone at any time..."  
【解析】任何时间禁独自前往。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
