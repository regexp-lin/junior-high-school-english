# 阅读理解（说明文） **Why Do Leaves Change Colour in Autumn?**

Every autumn, parks and forests in many parts of China put on a big show. Green trees turn yellow, orange, bright red, or even dark purple. Many students enjoy these colours, but few of them ask a simple question: **why** do leaves change colour at all? The answer brings together two sciences — plant biology and a little bit of chemistry.

During spring and summer, tree leaves are busy doing one important job: **photosynthesis**. They use sunlight, water from the ground and gas from the air to make food for the tree. To do this, leaves contain a green colour called **chlorophyll**, which catches sunlight well. There is so much chlorophyll in the leaves that we see only the green colour, even though the leaves also contain other colours inside.

When autumn comes, the days become shorter and the nights become cold. The tree senses these changes. It begins to pull its energy back into the trunk and roots, getting ready for winter. At the same time, the tree stops sending water and "food" to the leaves. Without enough water and sunlight, the leaves cannot keep making chlorophyll, and the green colour quickly breaks down and disappears.

Now the other colours can finally show. Some leaves turn **yellow** because of a colour called **carotene**, the same colour that is found in carrots. This yellow colour was in the leaves all summer, but it was hidden under the green. In some trees, such as maple, a red colour called **anthocyanin** is also made in the cool autumn days, especially when the weather is bright and the nights are cold. That is why the most famous red leaves in China, on Xiang Shan near Beijing, often look brightest after a cold, clear night.

Finally, when the tree no longer needs the leaves, a thin wall of cells grows at the base of each leaf. This small wall cuts the leaf off gently. A light wind or a morning rain then pulls the leaf to the ground. It is not the death of the tree — it is the tree's way of saving energy so that it can wake up again next spring.

So the next time you walk through an autumn park and kick a golden leaf, remember: that small coloured paper on the ground has been through a whole season of sunlight, water, and quiet change.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is photosynthesis, according to the passage?**

A. The way a tree sleeps in winter.  
B. The way a leaf uses sunlight, water and gas to make food.  
C. The red colour on the maple leaves.  
D. The small wall at the base of a leaf.

**2. Why do leaves look green in summer?**

A. Because there is no carotene in the leaves.  
B. Because chlorophyll is very strong and covers the other colours.  
C. Because the summer sun is green.  
D. Because the tree grows inside a green forest.

**3. Why do leaves stop making chlorophyll in autumn?**

A. Because they are attacked by insects.  
B. Because the tree starts to save energy and stops sending water and "food" to the leaves.  
C. Because farmers cut off the water.  
D. Because rain washes away the green colour.

**4. According to paragraph 4, why are the red leaves on Xiang Shan often brightest after a cold, clear night?**

A. Because cold, bright weather helps the tree make more anthocyanin.  
B. Because the leaves are painted by park workers at night.  
C. Because tourists like red and the tree knows it.  
D. Because cold wind always turns yellow into red.

**5. Why does the tree drop its leaves in autumn?**

A. To frighten small animals.  
B. To save energy so it can wake up again next spring.  
C. To make the forest look prettier.  
D. To help the wind become stronger.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】词义理解题  
【定位】第 2 段 "photosynthesis. They use sunlight, water..."  
【解析】光合作用即利用阳光、水和气制造食物。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 段 "so much chlorophyll... we see only the green"  
【解析】叶绿素太多，遮盖其他颜色。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 段 "stops sending water and 'food' to the leaves"  
【解析】秋季保存能量，不再给叶供水供养。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 段 "especially when the weather is bright and the nights are cold"  
【解析】冷而晴朗的天气促成花青素。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 5 段 "saving energy so that it can wake up again next spring"  
【解析】节能越冬。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
