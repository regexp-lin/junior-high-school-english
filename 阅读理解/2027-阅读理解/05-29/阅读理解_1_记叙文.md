# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Old Flower Seller Outside the Train Station**

① Every Friday afternoon I pass the west gate of the train station on my way home from the science museum. On the corner, there is always a very old woman who sells roses from a small plastic bucket. Her back is bent, her glasses are broken at one corner, and she wears the same blue cotton jacket, summer and winter. For almost a year I had walked past her without paying much attention.

② One rainy Friday, I got off the bus ten minutes earlier because the science class had finished sooner. The rain was light, but cold. As I turned the corner, I saw the flower seller trying to hold an umbrella over her bucket and her shoulders at the same time. Every time she moved, a few cold drops fell on her grey hair. My bus pass was still in my hand, and my feet stopped almost by themselves.

③ "Grandma," I said softly, "let me hold the umbrella for you, only for a while." She looked up, surprised, and her tired eyes smiled. "Thank you, child. You have your school bag; it is heavy." But she did not say "no". I moved closer, lifted the umbrella higher, and kept it over the flowers and her head. She began to talk quietly. She told me that she was seventy-one, that her son had been working in Guangzhou for six years, and that she sold flowers because "sitting at home makes the bones rust".

④ We stood there for almost fifteen minutes. Only three passers-by looked at her flowers; none of them bought. When the rain became lighter, she pointed to a white rose with a tiny pink edge. "Take this one. It cannot be sold tomorrow; by then it will open too much." I tried to refuse, but she already put it into my hand. Her fingers were cold, but very steady.

⑤ That night, I put the white rose into an empty mineral-water bottle on my desk. It sat quietly beside my English book. When I looked at it, I remembered not only the light rain, but also the flower seller's broken glasses and her small wet shoulders. I felt a strange, full feeling — a mixture of sadness, warmth and something close to **gratitude**.

⑥ The next Friday, I took a bigger umbrella with me from home. The old woman was there again. This time, she saw me from far away and raised her hand. I smiled and raised mine. Some meetings in life are only fifteen minutes long, but they can gently change the way we look at a whole street, and perhaps, the whole world.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What does the writer notice about the old flower seller?**

A. She is a very young girl.  
B. Her back is bent, her glasses are broken at one corner, and she always wears the same blue cotton jacket.  
C. She sells fruit, not flowers.  
D. She is always talking to many customers.

**2. Why did the writer stop to help on that rainy Friday?**

A. He wanted to buy a rose.  
B. He saw the old woman trying to hold an umbrella over her flowers and herself at the same time, and cold drops were falling on her hair.  
C. He was looking for his lost keys.  
D. He wanted to ask the time.

**3. Why does the old woman keep selling flowers, according to what she said?**

A. She wants to be rich.  
B. Her son asks her to do it.  
C. She says that "sitting at home makes the bones rust".  
D. She wants to travel around China.

**4. Why did the old woman give the writer a white rose at the end?**

A. Because he paid double for it.  
B. Because the rose would open too much the next day and could not be sold.  
C. Because it was the writer's birthday.  
D. Because she did not want it any more.

**5. What does the writer mean in the last paragraph?**

A. The old woman will move away soon.  
B. Some short meetings can gently change the way we look at a street and even at the world.  
C. Roses are the most expensive flowers.  
D. The writer will never pass the station again.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "Her back is bent, her glasses are broken... the same blue cotton jacket..."  
【解析】正面描写。A/C/D 均不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "the flower seller trying to hold an umbrella over her bucket and her shoulders at the same time..."  
【解析】老人遇冷雨，作者停下帮忙。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "sitting at home makes the bones rust"  
【解析】她借口原话。A/B/D 均无据。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "It cannot be sold tomorrow; by then it will open too much."  
【解析】第二天已不能卖。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题／主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑥段末  
【解析】短暂的温暖相遇可改变视角。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
