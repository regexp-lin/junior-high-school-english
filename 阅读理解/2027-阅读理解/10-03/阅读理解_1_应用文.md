# 阅读理解（应用文） **Riverside City Science Museum — October "Little Engineer" Weekend Workshops**

---

> **RIVERSIDE CITY SCIENCE MUSEUM**  
> **"Little Engineer" Weekend Workshops — October 2027**  
> **For students in Grade 6 – Grade 9**

Do you enjoy taking things apart and putting them back together? This October, our museum opens **four hands-on weekend workshops**. Each workshop lasts **2 hours**, and you will go home with your own working model!

---

## 🔧 Workshop Timetable

| Date | Title | Time | Room |
|------|-------|------|------|
| **Sat, Oct 10** | **Paper Bridges That Hold a Bottle of Water** | 9:30 – 11:30 | Maker Lab A |
| **Sun, Oct 11** | **Build a Mini Wind-Turbine (with a real LED light)** | 14:00 – 16:00 | Maker Lab A |
| **Sat, Oct 17** | **A Hand Warmer You Can Use Tonight** (safe chemistry) | 9:30 – 11:30 | Chemistry Lab |
| **Sun, Oct 25** | **Your First Line-Following Robot** (coding + soldering-free kit) | 14:00 – 16:30 | Robotics Lab |

> 🔎 *Please arrive 10 minutes before start time. Late students may not be able to keep up with the safety briefing.*

---

## 💰 Fees & Places

- Each workshop: **60 yuan / student**, **materials included**.
- Robot workshop (Oct 25): **95 yuan**, because the kit is more expensive.
- **Places are limited**: Maker Lab A – 24 seats; Chemistry Lab – 18 seats; Robotics Lab – 16 seats.
- **Family discount:** if an older brother or sister signs up together with a younger one, the second student pays **half price**.

---

## 📝 How to Book

1. Open the museum's WeChat mini-app **"Riverside Science"**, tap the **"Weekend Workshops"** banner.
2. Choose the date, write the student's name, grade and parent's phone number.
3. Booking opens **Monday 6 October, 10:00** and closes **three days before each workshop**.
4. Full refund is possible up to **48 hours** before the class; after that, the fee can only be **moved to another date**, not returned.

---

## 👷 What to Bring

- A bottle of water and a small towel.
- For the hand-warmer class: long sleeves and closed shoes (no sandals).
- For the robot class: a notebook — you will write down your own code changes.
- **No extra tools needed.** All hammers, scissors, hot-glue guns and batteries are provided by the museum and used under teacher supervision.

---

## ❗ Safety First

- Every workshop has **one main teacher + two student helpers** from a local university.
- Parents of Grade 6 students may stay and watch from the **family seats** at the back.
- If you feel unwell during a workshop, raise your hand — a first-aid nurse is on duty on the 1st floor every weekend.

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many "Little Engineer" weekend workshops are offered in October 2027?**

A. Two  
B. Three  
C. Four  
D. Five

**2. Which workshop costs more than 60 yuan?**

A. Paper Bridges That Hold a Bottle of Water  
B. Build a Mini Wind-Turbine  
C. A Hand Warmer You Can Use Tonight  
D. Your First Line-Following Robot

**3. A student wants to attend the class on Saturday, October 17. What is the latest time they can still book it?**

A. Any time before Saturday, October 17, 9:30.  
B. Three days before the workshop, that is by **Wednesday, October 14**.  
C. Only on October 6.  
D. Only at the door on the day of the class.

**4. If a student has booked the paper-bridge workshop but cannot go, what is true?**

A. They will always lose all their money.  
B. They can get a full refund if they cancel more than 48 hours before the class; otherwise the fee can only be moved to another workshop.  
C. They can transfer the seat to any friend without telling the museum.  
D. They can get a refund any time during the workshop.

**5. Which of the following is **NOT** required of students attending a workshop?**

A. Arriving 10 minutes before start time.  
B. Bringing a bottle of water and a small towel.  
C. Bringing their own hammer and hot-glue gun.  
D. Raising a hand if they feel unwell.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Timetable 表格共列出 4 个日期（Oct 10 / 11 / 17 / 25）  
【解析】表格明确有四个活动。A、B 少算、D 多算。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Fees & Places "Robot workshop (Oct 25): 95 yuan"  
【解析】机器人课 95 元，其余均 60 元。A、B、C 均为 60 元。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】How to Book 第 3 条 "closes three days before each workshop"  
【解析】10 月 17 日前 3 天即 10 月 14 日截止报名。A 过晚、C 仅开放日、D 门口报名均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】How to Book 第 4 条 "Full refund is possible up to 48 hours before ... after that, the fee can only be moved to another date"  
【解析】48 小时外可退款，之后只能改期。A 绝对化、C "转让朋友"无依据、D "任何时间退款"均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】What to Bring "No extra tools needed. All hammers, scissors, hot-glue guns ... provided by the museum"  
【解析】工具由博物馆提供，学生无需自带。A、B、D 都是合理要求。故选 C。
