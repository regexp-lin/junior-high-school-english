# 阅读理解（应用文） **Night Star-watching Event — Invitation**

① Dear students and parents, the **Bluewater Middle School Astronomy Club** is happy to invite you to our spring event: **"A Night with the Stars"**. Come and enjoy a quiet evening under the open sky, far away from the bright city lights. No experience is needed — all you need is warm clothes and a curious heart.

② **When & Where**

| Item | Information |
| --- | --- |
| Date | **Saturday, 20 March 2027** |
| Time | 19:00 – 22:00 |
| Place | **Green Hill Park (North Gate)** |
| Meeting point | Parking area near the lake |
| Number limit | **80 people** (first come, first served) |

③ **What You Will Do**

1. **Sky tour (19:10 – 20:00)** — Our teacher Mr. Lin will use a laser pointer to show you the spring stars: the **Big Dipper**, **Leo the Lion**, and the bright planet **Jupiter**.  
2. **Telescope time (20:00 – 21:00)** — Two school telescopes will be set up. You will see the **craters** on the moon and the cloud bands on Jupiter.  
3. **"Star story" corner (21:00 – 21:45)** — Listen to traditional Chinese star stories, such as *Niulang and Zhinü*, told by student hosts around a warm lantern.  
4. **Group photo (21:45 – 22:00)** — Take a memory photo with your own constellation drawing.

④ **How to Join**

- Scan the QR code at the school gate **before 18 March** and fill in the online form.  
- Pay a **small fee of 10 yuan** to cover paper star maps and hot drinks.  
- Students under 12 must be accompanied by a parent.

⑤ **Helpful Tips**

- Bring a **torch with a red film**; white light hurts night eyes.  
- Wear a thick coat — March evenings can still be cold.  
- Do **not** bring laser pointers, loud speakers or drones.  
- If the weather is rainy or very cloudy, the event will be moved to **Saturday, 27 March**, same time and place.

⑥ The stars have waited for us for millions of years. On that Saturday evening, let us lift our eyes together, breathe the cool spring air, and fall in love with the quiet beauty of our night sky. **See you under the stars!**

**— Bluewater Middle School Astronomy Club**
