# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**The Unexpected Gift**

① Last Friday was my twelfth birthday. My parents gave me a new bike, and my classmates came to sing and play games with me in the yard. I felt very happy that day. However, my best friend Lily could not come to my party because she was sick and had to stay in bed.

② The next morning, I visited Lily with some fruit from home. She looked pale but smiled when she opened the door. "I'm sorry I missed your party," she said in a low voice. I told her it was fine and that I only wanted her to get better soon. We talked for a while, and I helped her copy the English notes she had missed.

③ On Monday, Lily ran to me at school with a small box in her hands. "Open it," she said, her eyes shining. Inside, there was a handmade card and a photo of us from last year's sports day. On the card she had written, "True friends are never apart."

④ I was surprised and deeply moved. The gift did not cost much money, but I knew it <u>carried her true feelings</u>. That afternoon, we walked home together and shared stories about our weekend. I realized that friendship is not always shown by expensive presents. It is more about caring, remembering, and being there for each other.

⑤ From that day on, I learned to notice small acts of kindness. Whenever I see the card on my desk, I feel warm inside. Lily taught me that the best gifts sometimes arrive quietly, when we least expect them, and that a true friend is a gift in itself.

---

**一、选择题（共5小题，每小题2分）**

**1.** Why couldn't Lily come to the writer's birthday party?

A. She had to study for a test.  
B. She was visiting her grandparents.  
C. She was ill and had to rest at home.  
D. She forgot the time of the party.

**2.** What did the writer do the day after the birthday?

A. He/She went to see Lily and helped with schoolwork.  
B. He/She rode the new bike around the neighborhood.  
C. He/She bought a gift for Lily at a shop.  
D. He/She stayed at home to clean the yard.

**3.** We can infer from Paragraph ④ that the writer believes ______.

A. expensive gifts are more polite  
B. friendship needs honest communication only  
C. sports day photos are the best presents  
D. care and being present matter more than price  

**4.** The underlined part "carried her true feelings" in Paragraph ④ is closest in meaning to ______.

A. made her feelings heavy  
B. showed her real care and love  
C. hid her secret worries  
D. carried her books to school  

**5.** What is the writer's main purpose in writing this passage?

A. To share a lesson about friendship and unexpected kindness.  
B. To explain how to plan a successful birthday party.  
C. To advertise handmade cards for students.  
D. To compare bikes with photos as birthday gifts.  

---

**二、答案与解析**

**1.**

**【答案】** C  

**【题型】** 细节理解  

**【定位】** 第①段："However, my best friend Lily could not come to my party because she was sick and had to stay in bed."  

**【解析】** 题干问莉莉为何没来参加生日聚会。原文明确说明她生病、需要卧床休息，故 C 正确。A 项“备考”、B 项“看望祖父母”、D 项“忘记时间”在文中均未提及，属于无中生有，应排除。  

---

**2.**

**【答案】** A  

**【题型】** 细节理解  

**【定位】** 第②段："The next morning, I visited Lily with some fruit from home." 以及 "I helped her copy the English notes she had missed."  

**【解析】** 生日次日作者去看望莉莉并帮她抄写错过的英语笔记，与 A 项“看望莉莉并帮助功课”一致。B 项骑新车、C 项商店买礼物、D 项在家打扫院子均未在该段出现，故排除。  

---

**3.**

**【答案】** D  

**【题型】** 推理判断  

**【定位】** 第④段："I realized that friendship is not always shown by expensive presents. It is more about caring, remembering, and being there for each other."  

**【解析】** 从第④段可推断，作者认为友谊重在关心、记挂与陪伴，而非礼物价格，D 项“关心与在场比价格更重要”符合推理。A 与作者观点相反；B 缩小为“仅需要坦诚沟通”，片面；C 把照片当作“最好礼物”，而文中强调心意与关系，故排除 A、B、C。  

---

**4.**

**【答案】** B  

**【题型】** 词义猜测  

**【定位】** 第④段："The gift did not cost much money, but I knew it carried her true feelings." 结合后文 "caring, remembering, and being there"。  

**【解析】** 礼物不贵，却表达真心；下文强调关心与陪伴，故 "carried her true feelings" 指“体现她真挚的关心与爱”，B 正确。A 按字面解为“使情绪沉重”，不符；C“隐藏忧虑”、D“把书带到学校”均脱离语境，排除。  

---

**5.**

**【答案】** A  

**【题型】** 写作意图  

**【定位】** 全文，尤其第④段关于友谊的感悟与第⑤段 "learned to notice small acts of kindness" 与 "a true friend is a gift itself"。  

**【解析】** 作者通过记叙生日与意外礼物，点明友谊与善意的主旨，写作目的是分享关于友谊与意外温暖的感悟，A 正确。B 侧重聚会策划、C 推销卡片、D 单纯比较礼物类型，均非本文主要目的，排除。  

---

**注释：** pale 苍白的；deeply 深深地；realize 意识到
