# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Students Wear School Uniforms?**

① In many middle schools, students are asked to wear the same clothes on weekdays. This rule is simple, but opinions about it are not. Some people strongly support it, while others think students should choose their own clothes. The debate is common in school meetings and family talks.

② Supporters say uniforms save time in the morning. Students do not need to worry about what to wear, so they can focus on breakfast and homework checks. Uniforms can also reduce unnecessary comparisons about brands and styles. In addition, they help teachers quickly tell who belongs to the school when students are on trips or in crowded places.

③ However, <u>opponents</u> argue that uniforms may limit self-expression. Teenagers are learning who they are, and clothing can be a quiet way to show hobbies and personality. They also say buying both uniforms and weekend clothes can cost more for some families, even if each uniform item looks cheap on its own.

④ A balanced view is that rules should match the school's goals. If the main aim is safety, unity, and fewer distractions, uniforms make sense. Schools can still allow small choices, such as different ties or shoes within clear rules, so students feel a sense of freedom without breaking order.

⑤ In my opinion, wearing uniforms on school days is reasonable for most students, as long as the school listens to their ideas and keeps the dress code fair and comfortable. Education is not only about books; it is also about learning to live and work together. A clear uniform policy can support that aim when it is explained with respect.

**一、选择题（共5小题，每小题2分）**

**1.** What is the main idea of the passage?

A. It presents different views on school uniforms and the writer's own idea.  
B. Schools should stop using uniforms at once.  
C. Weekend clothes are always more expensive than uniforms.  
D. Teachers dislike students who wear colorful shoes.  

**2.** According to Paragraph ②, what do supporters say uniforms do on busy mornings?

A. They help teachers pick out students on school trips.  
B. They make sure every student gets higher grades.  
C. They take the place of all other school rules.  
D. They save time because students need not choose what to wear.  

**3.** It can be inferred that opponents care most about ______.

A. shorter morning routines  
B. stronger school unity on trips  
C. personal identity and family cost  
D. teachers' safety in crowded places  

**4.** The underlined word "opponents" in Paragraph ③ most probably means people who ______.

A. design new school uniforms  
B. disagree with the uniform rule  
C. sell uniforms at a low price  
D. organize sports trips for schools  

**5.** Which statement would the writer most likely agree with?

A. Uniforms are useless if some students dislike them.  
B. Students should never show hobbies through what they wear.  
C. Schools should only care about exam scores, not dress codes.  
D. A fair, respectful uniform policy can help students learn to live and work together.  

---

**二、答案与解析**

**1.**

**【答案】** A  

**【题型】** 主旨大意  

**【定位】** 第①段提出论题；第②③段分别陈述支持与反对；第④段提出折中思路；第⑤段表明作者观点——全文围绕“是否穿校服”的不同看法及作者态度展开。  

**【解析】** A 项概括“呈现不同观点并给出作者看法”，与全文结构一致。B 与作者“多数情况下认为穿校服合理”相反；C 把第③段中的部分信息夸大为全文中心；D 文中未提及，属无中生有。  

---

**2.**

**【答案】** D  

**【题型】** 细节理解  

**【定位】** 第②段："Supporters say uniforms save time in the morning. Students do not need to worry about what to wear, so they can focus on breakfast and homework checks."  

**【解析】** 支持者指出校服可节省早晨时间、不必纠结穿什么，D 项与此一致。A 虽在②段后半出现，但题干限定 “on busy mornings”，应选“省时”这一细节；B“保证提分”、C“取代所有校规”均无依据，排除。  

---

**3.**

**【答案】** C  

**【题型】** 推理判断  

**【定位】** 第③段："Uniforms may limit self-expression." 以及 "buying both uniforms and weekend clothes can cost more for some families"。  

**【解析】** 反对者强调自我表达受限与家庭开支，可推断其关注身份认同与费用，C 正确。A、B、D 均为支持方理由或无关信息，与反对者立场不符。  

---

**4.**

**【答案】** B  

**【题型】** 词义猜测  

**【定位】** 第②段 "Supporters say..." 与第③段 "However, opponents argue..." 形成对比。  

**【解析】** "However" 表明与 supporters 对立，opponents 指反对校服规定的人，B 正确。A“设计校服”、C“售卖”、D“组织出游”均与 “argue that...” 的论辩角色不符。  

---

**5.**

**【答案】** D  

**【题型】** 写作意图（观点态度）  

**【定位】** 第⑤段："as long as the school listens to their ideas and keeps the dress code fair and comfortable" 以及 "learning to live and work together" 与 "A clear uniform policy can support that aim when it is explained with respect."  

**【解析】** 作者认为在公平、尊重的沟通下，校服政策有助于学生学会共同生活与合作，D 项与之一致。A 与作者认为校服“reasonable”矛盾；B、C 过于绝对或与末段“教育不止书本”相悖，排除。  

---

**注释：** opponent 反对者；self-expression 自我表达；policy 政策
