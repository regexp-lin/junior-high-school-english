# 阅读理解（应用文） **Art in the Park — Weekend Painting Festival**

> **CITY PARK COMMITTEE**  
> **"ART IN THE PARK" — WEEKEND PAINTING FESTIVAL**  
> *Saturday & Sunday, 24–25 May 2027 · South Bank of Willow Lake*

---

Dear students, families and art lovers,

On the last weekend of May, the **Willow Lake South Bank** will turn into a lively open-air studio. Join our two-day outdoor painting festival — come to watch, come to paint, or both. The event is **free** for everyone who lives in the city, but some workshops require a small material fee.

### Two Main Activity Zones

| Zone | Who May Join | Activities |
|------|--------------|------------|
| **Zone A (Family Zone)** | All ages | Big chalk-on-ground drawings, face painting, children's paper-plate animals |
| **Zone B (Student Zone)** | Grades 3–9 | Watercolour workshops, sketching the lake, mini portrait contest |

### Painting Workshops (Zone B)

| Workshop | Day | Time | Material Fee |
|----------|-----|------|--------------|
| **Watercolour Flowers** | Sat | 09:30–11:00 | 15 yuan |
| **Sketching Willow Trees** | Sat | 14:30–16:00 | 10 yuan |
| **Lake Sunset Watercolour** | Sun | 15:00–16:30 | 15 yuan |
| **Mini Portrait Contest** | Sun | 10:00–11:30 | Free |

*All brushes, paper and simple aprons are included. Students only need to bring a bottle of water and a happy heart.*

### Mini Portrait Contest (Sunday, 10:00)

- Paint a small portrait (A5 size, 15 cm × 21 cm) of a family member or a close friend in 90 minutes.  
- Judges: two art teachers, one local artist.  
- Top 3 winners: **100-yuan art shop gift cards**.  
- All painters receive a small art badge.

### Rules and Safety

1. Please do **not** climb trees, walk on the flower beds, or enter the lake water.  
2. Keep your painting waste in the provided bags. Do not put paint brushes into the lake.  
3. Children under 10 must come with an adult.  
4. If it rains heavily, the workshops will move to the covered pavilion near Gate C.  
5. Please say **hello** to passing visitors — this is an open festival, and questions are welcome.

### How to Join a Workshop

- **Online:** www.citypark.cn/artinthepark2027 (choose workshop → pay fee → receive QR code)  
- **On site:** Registration desk at the festival's main gate from 08:30 each day.  
- **Deadline for online booking:** Friday, 23 May, 18:00.

Each workshop has **20 seats** only. First come, first served on site.

### Bring-a-Friend Bonus

If you sign up with a friend online, you will each get a **free small sketchbook** on the day of your workshop. One friend, one sketchbook — simple and kind.

**Let's fill the park with colour.**

— City Park Committee, Youth Arts Office

**根据通告内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the festival's main activity?**

A. A two-day outdoor painting festival with workshops and contests.  
B. A swimming contest.  
C. A photography show.  
D. A music concert.

**2. Which workshop is FREE?**

A. Watercolour Flowers.  
B. Sketching Willow Trees.  
C. Lake Sunset Watercolour.  
D. Mini Portrait Contest.

**3. Which of the following is NOT allowed at the festival?**

A. Drawing on the ground with chalk in Zone A.  
B. Climbing trees and walking on flower beds.  
C. Taking part in a workshop with 15-yuan material fee.  
D. Saying hello to passing visitors.

**4. What will each of the top 3 Mini Portrait winners receive?**

A. 100-yuan art shop gift cards.  
B. Free family dinner.  
C. A new phone.  
D. 1,000 yuan in cash.

**5. What will a student get for signing up online together with a friend?**

A. A free small sketchbook each on the day of the workshop.  
B. A free bicycle.  
C. A free summer camp.  
D. Nothing extra.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】开头 "two-day outdoor painting festival"  
【解析】活动是为期两天的户外绘画节。B/C/D 均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Painting Workshops 表 Mini Portrait Contest "Free"  
【解析】迷你肖像比赛免费。A/B/C 均需材料费。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules and Safety 第1条 "Please do not climb trees, walk on the flower beds..."  
【解析】禁止爬树、踩花坛。A/C/D 均被允许或鼓励。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Mini Portrait Contest "Top 3 winners: 100-yuan art shop gift cards."  
【解析】前三名各获 100 元艺术用品礼券。B/C/D 均无据。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Bring-a-Friend Bonus "free small sketchbook on the day of your workshop"  
【解析】与朋友同报名可各获一本速写本。B/C/D 均无据。故选 A。
