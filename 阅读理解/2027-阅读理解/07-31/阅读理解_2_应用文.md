# 阅读理解（应用文） **Family Sports Day — "Move Together 2027"**

**Riverside Stadium · Sunday, September 28, 2027**

Hello families!

The City Health Bureau and the Parent-Teacher Union invite every family with a middle-school student to our **Family Sports Day 2027**. One cool autumn Sunday. One big stadium. Lots of easy, silly, happy games designed for **parents and children to play together**. No medals. No winners and losers. Just warm hearts, tired legs, and a full afternoon of laughter.

---

### The Day at a Glance

- **Date:** Sunday, September 28, 2027  
- **Time:** 13:30 – 17:30  
- **Place:** Riverside Stadium, West Entrance, 6 Riverside Road  
- **Who can join:** **one middle-school student + one or two family members** (parent, grandparent, uncle, aunt, older brother or sister). At least one adult must be with the student.  
- **Cost:** free  
- **Numbers:** 500 families, first come first serve

---

### Activities (choose any 4 out of 6)

**① Three-Legged Walk (50 m)**  
One student + one adult, two inner legs tied together with a soft cloth. Walk (not run!) to the finish line.

**② Family Rope Skip (2 min)**  
The adult turns a long rope, the student jumps in, then out, then in again. Counter counts each good jump; the family with the biggest smile wins. (Not the most jumps!)

**③ Shared Shooting (basketball, 3 min)**  
Family takes turns shooting a soft sponge basketball into a low basket from 2 metres. Counts total successful shots.

**④ Back-to-Back Ball Walk (30 m)**  
One student and one adult stand back-to-back with a soft volleyball between their shoulders. Walk sideways 30 m. If the ball drops, pick it up and keep going.

**⑤ Memory Relay**  
Four short memory cards are hidden around the track. Family members find them in order. The student writes down the four words at the end. No phones or paper during the search!

**⑥ Paper Aeroplane Challenge**  
Family works together (max. 10 minutes) to build one paper aeroplane from a piece of A4 paper. Then the student throws it. Longest straight flight wins the "Golden Wing" sticker — only for fun.

---

### Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|------|---------|
| 13:00 – 13:25 | Arrival; pick up bib number and schedule slip at the West Entrance |
| 13:30 – 13:45 | Opening ceremony + simple warm-up dance on the main lawn |
| 13:50 – 16:30 | Families move between the 6 activity stations (choose any 4) |
| 16:45 – 17:15 | "Calm-down" picnic on the south lawn (families bring their own snacks and water; a free water-refill station is open) |
| 17:15 – 17:30 | Closing circle: every family shares in ONE sentence a happy moment from the day |

---

### What to Bring

- Comfortable sports shoes (not sandals)  
- A thin jacket (September evenings are cool)  
- A water bottle (re-fill station available)  
- A light snack for the picnic  
- A small, soft towel

### What We Provide

- All equipment (ropes, balls, cards, paper)  
- A "Move Together 2027" cotton headband for every participant  
- Free juice and one banana per person at the picnic corner  
- A small first-aid team in the central tent

### Safety Rules

1. No running inside the corridor area of the stadium; save running energy for the games.  
2. If anyone in the family feels unwell (fast heart, dizziness), please stop and walk to the first-aid tent.  
3. Families with a member over 65 or with any heart condition should tell the welcome desk at arrival.  
4. Please keep the lawn clean. Take your picnic rubbish with you.

### Sign Up

1. Visit **www.citysports2027.cn/family** before **23:59, Monday, September 15, 2027**.  
2. Fill in each family member's name and age, and tick the activities you want to try.  
3. Once your form is accepted, you will receive a confirmation SMS within 24 hours, with your family number and a small map of the stadium.  
4. **Cancellation:** please cancel online at least **2 days before**, so another family can take your place.

### Contact
