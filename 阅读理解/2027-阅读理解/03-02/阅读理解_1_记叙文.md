> **03-02 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Day I Tried Rock Climbing**

① Last Saturday our PE teacher, Mr. Chen, took ten of us to a new indoor climbing wall in our city. When I stood at the bottom and looked up, my heart started beating like a drum. The wall was about eight metres high, and the colourful holds looked so small from below. I am not afraid of most sports, but heights have always scared me a little.

② Mr. Chen smiled at us and said, "Today, nobody has to reach the top. Your only job is to be one step braver than yesterday." He asked Liu Yang, who already had some experience, to climb first. Liu Yang moved up the wall like a lizard, quick and light, and finished in two minutes. Everyone clapped. Then it was my turn. My hands were sweating before I even put on the safety belt.

③ I took a deep breath and started. The first three holds were easy, but when I was about three metres up, my right foot slipped. I dropped a little before the rope caught me. I hung in the air, swinging slowly, too afraid to move. Below me, Li Na shouted, "Don't look down! Look at the next blue hold!" Her voice sounded so sure that I decided to trust her.

④ I reached out to the blue hold and pulled myself up. Then I found another good hold for my foot. Step by step, I climbed higher. At about six metres, my arms felt as soft as noodles, but I could see a little red flag at the top. I told myself, "Just three more holds. Just three." And then I touched it.

⑤ When I came down, my legs were shaking, but I could not stop smiling. Mr. Chen gave me a small sticker that said **"Braver than Yesterday"**. On the bus home, I looked out of the window and suddenly understood something: the wall had not grown shorter, and I was not suddenly stronger. What had changed was the voice inside my head. It used to say "I can't"; now it quietly said, **"Let's try one more hold."**

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How did the writer feel when he first saw the climbing wall?**

A. Excited and confident.  
B. Bored and tired.  
C. Nervous because of the height.  
D. Angry at the PE teacher.

**2. What did Mr. Chen ask the students to do on that day?**

A. To reach the top of the wall.  
B. To be one step braver than yesterday.  
C. To learn how to tie the safety rope.  
D. To finish the climb within two minutes.

**3. What happened when the writer was about three metres up?**

A. He hit his head on a hold.  
B. His right foot slipped and the rope caught him.  
C. He saw a red flag and touched it.  
D. He climbed past Liu Yang.

**4. Who helped the writer by shouting advice from below?**

A. Mr. Chen.  
B. Liu Yang.  
C. Li Na.  
D. His father.

**5. What does the writer mainly want to tell us at the end of the story?**

A. Climbing walls are not safe for middle school students.  
B. Practice is the only way to become a good climber.  
C. Real change starts with the voice inside our own head.  
D. Teachers should give out more stickers as rewards.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段末句 "heights have always scared me a little"  
【解析】原文说站在下面抬头看时心跳加速，并明确"heights have always scared me a little"，体现因高度紧张。A 项"兴奋自信"与"heart beating like a drum"矛盾；B 项"无聊疲惫"文中无依据；D 项"对老师生气"属于无中生有。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段第 2 句 "Your only job is to be one step braver than yesterday."  
【解析】老师明确说目标不是登顶，而是比昨天勇敢一步。A 项"到达顶端"与"nobody has to reach the top"相反；C 项"学系安全绳"文中未提；D 项"两分钟内完成"是 Liu Yang 的用时，张冠李戴。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段第 2 句 "my right foot slipped. I dropped a little before the rope caught me."  
【解析】作者三米高处右脚打滑，绳子接住了他。A 项"撞头"未提；C 项"摸红旗"发生在登顶时；D 项"超过 Liu Yang"无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段末 "Below me, Li Na shouted..."  
【解析】明确是李娜在下面喊话指引。其他三人均未在此情节中发话。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑤段最后两句  
【解析】作者点明"真正改变的是脑中那个声音"，由"I can't"变成"Let's try one more hold"，强调内心信念的转变。A 项与文意相反；B 项偏离主旨；D 项以偏概全。故选 C。

- **lizard** [n.] 蜥蜴  
- **sticker** [n.] 贴纸
