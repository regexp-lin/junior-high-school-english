# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Clearing Snow for Old Mr. Li**

① It snowed heavily on Friday night. When I opened the curtains on Saturday morning, the whole street was white. Roofs, trees and cars all wore a thick white coat. My little sister Amy cheered loudly and wanted to run out right away, but Mom stopped us at the door. "Before you play," she said kindly, "please go and clear the snow in front of old Mr. Li's gate. He lives alone and his leg still hurts."

② Mr. Li used to be a math teacher in our neighborhood school. After his wife passed away two years ago, his children moved to another city for work. Every spring, he shares red flowers from his small yard with the children on our lane. So Amy and I agreed at once. We put on thick gloves, took two small shovels, and walked through the quiet snow.

③ When we reached his gate, the snow was almost up to our knees. At first, we were full of energy. Amy pushed the snow aside and kept singing. I made a narrow path along the wall. After ten minutes, however, my back began to ache and my fingers felt cold inside the wet gloves. Amy said quietly, "It's harder than I thought." I smiled and told her, "Let's rest for a while, then go on."

④ Just as we sat on the stone step, the old wooden gate opened. Mr. Li came out slowly with a cup of hot ginger water in each hand. "My dear children," he said, his eyes shining. "I saw you from the window. You must warm yourselves first." His smile was gentle, like a warm candle in the cold wind.

⑤ After we finished, Mr. Li took out an old black-and-white photo. It showed him as a young man, standing with my grandpa in front of a small village school. "Your grandpa and I cleared snow together on such a morning almost forty years ago," he said, his voice trembling a little. "Kindness in our family never grows old."

⑥ Walking home, I held Amy's hand tightly. My shoulders still ached, but my heart felt surprisingly light. A small act of kindness, I suddenly understood, could connect two generations and warm a whole winter morning.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did Mom ask the writer to clear the snow at Mr. Li's gate?**

A. Because Mr. Li paid them for the work.  
B. Because Mr. Li lived alone and his leg still hurt.  
C. Because the writer was punished for playing too much.  
D. Because the school asked every family to do it.

**2. What did Mr. Li used to do for a living?**

A. He was a farmer.  
B. He was a soldier.  
C. He was a math teacher.  
D. He was a bus driver.

**3. How did the writer feel in Paragraph 3?**

A. Excited all the time.  
B. Tired but still willing to go on.  
C. Angry with the little sister.  
D. Disappointed with Mr. Li.

**4. Why did Mr. Li show the old photo to the children?**

A. To prove how rich his family used to be.  
B. To ask them to clean the yard too.  
C. To remind them of a family tradition of kindness.  
D. To teach them how to use an old camera.

**5. What does the writer mainly want to tell us?**

A. Winter snow brings a lot of trouble to the elderly.  
B. A small act of kindness can warm people across generations.  
C. Children should play in the snow as much as possible.  
D. Old photos always tell the saddest stories.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "He lives alone and his leg still hurts."  
【解析】李爷爷独居且腿伤，妈妈请孩子们帮忙。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Mr. Li used to be a math teacher"  
【解析】他以前是数学老师。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第③段 "my back began to ache... 'Let's rest for a while, then go on.'"  
【解析】作者虽累，仍坚持完成。A 持续兴奋错，C、D 无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Kindness in our family never grows old."  
【解析】老照片传递家族善意的传统。A、B、D 均不符原意。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑥段 "A small act of kindness... could connect two generations"  
【解析】主题是小小善举温暖两代人。A 片面，C 无关主旨，D 错误。故选 B。
