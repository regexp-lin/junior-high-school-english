# 阅读理解（记叙文） **A Rainy Afternoon at the Bus Stop**

It was the last day before the summer holiday. The sky had been grey since noon, but the rain did not start until I left the school gate. I opened my old blue umbrella and stepped onto the wet pavement, feeling happy that final exams were finally over.

At the bus stop, I saw an old woman standing very still under the small roof. She had a cloth bag full of vegetables. Water was dripping from the edge of the roof right onto her left shoulder. She did not move, but I could see she was trying to make her body small. She had no umbrella. For a moment I thought: someone else will help her. Then I told myself, "No. You are the someone else."

I walked over. "Grandma, please share my umbrella. It's quite big." She looked up. Her face was tired but kind. "Thank you, child, but I am fine. The bus is coming soon."

But the bus was **not** coming soon. After a few minutes, the rain turned heavy, and cars were moving more slowly than ever. The grandma finally said, "Yes, thank you," and stepped half a step closer to me. I moved the umbrella over her head. My left shoulder quickly became wet, but her vegetables — a small bunch of spring onions, some tomatoes, and a big white cabbage — were dry.

We did not talk for a long time. Then, slowly, she began. She told me that she had bought the vegetables for her grandson, who was coming to stay with her for the summer. "He loves my tomato noodles," she said, smiling. "But the market far away has better tomatoes, so I take two buses to get there."

I listened. I did not know what to say, so I just smiled too. After about fifteen minutes, the bus finally arrived. She tried to give me five yuan "for the trouble". I held her hand gently and said, "Please. My grandma is also somewhere. Maybe today someone is helping her too."

She looked at me for a long moment. Then she got on the bus and waved from the window. I stood in the rain, watching the bus disappear into the grey street. My shoulder was soaked, but I felt strangely warm inside.

That evening, when I took off my wet jacket at home, I found a small thing stuck in the pocket — a single, perfect tomato. I laughed, and then my eyes got a little wet. Summer holidays had started, and already I had learned one small thing: sometimes the best gift is not the one you give, but the one you receive back without asking.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When did the story happen?**

A. On the first day of the school year.  
B. In the middle of a maths exam.  
C. On the last day before the summer holiday.  
D. At Spring Festival.

**2. Why didn't the old woman accept the umbrella at once?**

A. Because she had her own umbrella.  
B. Because she thought the bus would come very soon.  
C. Because she did not like strangers.  
D. Because she was on the phone.

**3. What did the grandma buy at the far market?**

A. New clothes.  
B. Schoolbooks for her grandson.  
C. Vegetables, including spring onions, tomatoes and a big white cabbage.  
D. Medicine for her husband.

**4. Why did the narrator refuse the five yuan?**

A. Because the money was too little.  
B. Because he did not like money.  
C. Because he hoped that someone would also be helping his own grandma somewhere.  
D. Because the grandma only had paper money.

**5. What did the narrator find in his jacket pocket at home?**

A. A small piece of cake.  
B. A letter of thanks.  
C. A single, perfect tomato.  
D. Five yuan.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 段 "the last day before the summer holiday"  
【解析】暑假前一天。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 段 "The bus is coming soon."  
【解析】以为公交很快来。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 段描述  
【解析】葱、番茄、大白菜。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第 6 段 "Maybe today someone is helping her too"  
【解析】以爱传递为由谢绝。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】末段 "a single, perfect tomato"  
【解析】口袋里一枚番茄。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。
