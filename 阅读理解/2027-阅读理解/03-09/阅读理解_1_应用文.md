# 阅读理解（应用文） **Spring Cherry Blossom Festival — Park Visit Guide**

① Dear students and parents, the **Willow Lake Park Cherry Blossom Festival** is back for its 12th year. This spring the park has opened **six new flower tunnels**, planted with pink, white and even green cherry trees. The festival runs from **Saturday, 20 March** to **Sunday, 10 April 2027**. Come and enjoy a slow walk, a warm lunch and the sound of spring!

② **Park Hours & Tickets**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Opening time | **08:00 – 18:30** (last entry 17:30) |
| Adult ticket | **40 yuan** |
| Student ticket (ages 7–18, with ID) | **15 yuan** |
| Family ticket (2 adults + 1 student) | **80 yuan** |
| Under 6 / over 70 | **Free** (with ID of an adult) |

③ **Four Main Spots**

1. **North Gate Tunnel** — the most famous pink tunnel, 220 metres long.  
2. **Sakura Island** — a small round island in the lake, reached by a wooden bridge.  
3. **Night Light Garden** — open 18:00 – 21:00 on **Friday–Sunday**, with soft lights under the trees.  
4. **Children's Painting Yard** — free painting papers and pencils on weekends.

④ **Tips for Your Visit**

- On sunny weekends the park can be very crowded. Try **weekday mornings** (8:00 – 10:30) for quieter flowers.  
- The ground is flat but may be slippery after rain. Please wear **comfortable shoes**.  
- Many snacks are sold near the East Gate, but you may also bring your own **light picnic**.  
- Free drinking water is offered at **Station A, C and F**. Please take your own bottle to help us save paper cups.

⑤ **Special Events (Weekends Only)**

| Date | Event | Place | Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 21 March | Opening music night | Central Lawn | 19:00 – 20:30 |
| 28 March | Kite-making class for kids | Sakura Island | 10:00 – 11:30 |
| 4 April | Tea culture show | Tea Pavilion | 14:00 – 16:00 |
| 10 April | Photo contest prize-giving | Art Centre | 15:00 – 16:00 |

⑥ **Please DO NOT**

- Pick any flower or shake any branch.  
- Fly drones in the park (for everyone's safety).  
- Bring pets (except guide dogs).  
- Play loud music near the lake.

⑦ **Contact**  
- Park Service Centre: Building 1, East Gate  
- Phone (daily 9:00 – 17:00): 020-5588-2027  
- Email: spring-info@willowlakepark.cn  
- Lost-and-found: Room 106, North Gate Office

**Let the pink tunnels carry your smile into spring — Willow Lake Park**

**1. How long does the Cherry Blossom Festival last this year?**

A. About one week.  
B. Three days.

**2. How much does a family ticket cost for two adults and one student?**

A. 40 yuan.

**3. Where can visitors see the pink tunnel that is 220 metres long?**

A. Sakura Island.  
B. North Gate Tunnel.  
C. Night Light Garden.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed in the park?**

A. Taking photos of the flowers.  
B. Bringing a light picnic.  
C. Playing loud music near the lake.  
D. Wearing comfortable shoes.

**5. What event will be held on 28 March?**

A. The opening music night.  
B. A kite-making class for kids.  
C. A tea culture show.  
D. The prize-giving of the photo contest.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段第 3 句 "from Saturday, 20 March to Sunday, 10 April 2027"  
【解析】节期 3 月 20 日至 4 月 10 日。A、B、D 与原文不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段表格 "Family ticket ... 80 yuan"  
【解析】家庭票 80 元。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段第 1 条 "North Gate Tunnel — the most famous pink tunnel, 220 metres long"  
【解析】北门隧道长 220 米。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段第 4 条 "Play loud music near the lake"  
【解析】禁止在湖边大声播放音乐。A、B、D 均被允许。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段表格 28 March 行 "Kite-making class for kids"  
【解析】3 月 28 日为儿童风筝制作课。其他选项是其他日期的活动。故选 B。
