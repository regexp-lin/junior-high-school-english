# 阅读理解（应用文） **Teen Photography Exhibition 2027 — "My City, My Home"**

---

## **About the Exhibition**

The **City Youth Art Centre** and the **City Library** will hold the 9th **Teen Photography Exhibition** from **November 6 to November 30, 2027**. The theme this year is **"My City, My Home"** — we want teenagers in our city to show us the streets, people, lights, trees, and small moments that make this place feel like home.

The exhibition is free and open to everyone. Last year, more than **7,000 visitors** came to see 180 student photos. This year, we plan to choose **220 photos** out of all the entries.

---

## **Who Can Enter?**

- **Age:** Students born between **Jan 1, 2010** and **Dec 31, 2014** (roughly Grade 7 to Grade 9, plus some senior Grade 6 students).  
- **City:** You must live or study in our city.  
- **Skill level:** No prizes or training are needed. A clear photo taken with a phone is very welcome.

---

## **Submission Rules**

1. **Your photo must be taken by you**, within the city, between **Jan 1, 2026** and **Sep 30, 2027**.  
2. **Each student can send in up to 3 photos.** Groups of two students are allowed, but count as one "entry".  
3. Photos must be **at least 3 megapixels (MP)** and no larger than **20 MB** per file. JPG or PNG only.  
4. **No heavy editing.** You may slightly adjust brightness or cut edges, but you may **not** add sticker frames, painted effects, or cartoon filters. We want photography, not digital painting.  
5. **Each photo needs a title (in English, up to 10 words) and a short note (up to 80 words)** explaining why this corner of the city feels like home to you.  
6. If there are **clearly visible faces** in the photo, you must get permission from those people (or from their parents, if they are under 14). A simple signed note is enough.

---

## **Five Suggested Topics**

| No. | Topic | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Morning in the City** | a street food stall, a sunrise cleaner, parents walking children to school |
| 2 | **Quiet Corners** | an old bookshop, a small park bench, a lamp by a river |
| 3 | **People Who Keep the City Going** | a bus driver, a nurse, a neighbourhood worker |
| 4 | **Nature in the City** | a tree growing out of a wall, birds on a wire, flowers between old bricks |
| 5 | **Night Lights** | a late-night bakery, a bus stop under the rain, fireworks for a festival |

You may also submit a photo on a **different topic**, as long as it fits "My City, My Home".

## **Prizes**

- **Grand Prize (1 photo):** a new mirrorless camera + a photography workshop  
- **Gold Prizes (3 photos):** 1,000-yuan bookshop voucher + a framed print  
- **Silver Prizes (6 photos):** 500-yuan bookshop voucher + a framed print  
- **Public Choice (1 photo):** free 3-month membership at the City Library  
- **All 220 exhibited photos** will be printed free of charge for the students.
