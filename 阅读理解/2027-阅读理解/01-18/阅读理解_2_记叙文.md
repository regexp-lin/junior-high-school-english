# 阅读理解（记叙文） **A Cake for Mum**

① My mother's birthday was on Sunday. For many years, my father and I simply bought her a store cake with white cream and red cherries. This year I wanted to do something different. I decided to bake a cake by myself for the first time.

② On Friday evening after school, I went to a small bakery near our building. The owner, Aunt Zhou, was a kind woman with a soft voice. She agreed to teach me a simple recipe for butter cake. "Baking is not just about sugar and eggs," she said. "It's about patience." I listened carefully and wrote every step in my notebook.

③ On Saturday afternoon, I started the big job in our kitchen. My little brother Tom was very excited and wanted to help. We carefully weighed the flour, the sugar and the butter. Tom broke two eggs into a bowl, but one piece of shell fell in. I spent five minutes picking it out. Then we mixed everything together and poured the thick yellow cream into a round pan.

④ While the cake was baking in the oven, a sweet smell filled the house. Tom could not stop jumping around. Forty minutes later, I opened the oven. The cake had risen up, golden and soft. But when I tried to take it out, the pan was hotter than I expected. I put on thick gloves and moved more slowly. The cake landed safely on the table, without a single crack.

⑤ On Sunday morning, I put the cake on a blue plate and wrote "Happy Birthday, Mum" with chocolate sauce. Mother's eyes became wet when she saw it. She said it was the most beautiful cake she had ever received. I knew that my cake was not as perfect as the ones in bakeries, but it carried something a store cake could never have — my own time, care and love.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the writer decide to make a cake this year?**

A. Because store cakes were too expensive.  
B. Because he wanted to do something different for his mother.  
C. Because his mother asked him to do so.  
D. Because Aunt Zhou told him to.

**2. Who taught the writer the recipe?**

A. His mother.  
B. His father.  
C. His little brother Tom.  
D. Aunt Zhou at a small bakery.

**3. What problem did the writer meet when breaking the eggs?**

A. One egg was not fresh.  
B. A piece of shell fell into the bowl.  
C. All the eggs rolled off the table.  
D. His brother ate all the eggs.

**4. Why did the writer put on thick gloves?**

A. Because the kitchen was too cold.  
B. Because he wanted to look like a chef.  
C. Because the baking pan was very hot.  
D. Because his hands were dirty.

**5. What does the writer really want to tell us in the last paragraph?**

A. Only handmade cakes taste really good.  
B. A homemade cake carries care and love that a store cake cannot.  
C. Store cakes should never be bought for birthdays.  
D. Mothers prefer chocolate sauce to cream.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "This year I wanted to do something different. I decided to bake a cake by myself for the first time."  
【解析】作者想今年做点不一样的事。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "The owner, Aunt Zhou... agreed to teach me a simple recipe for butter cake."  
【解析】周阿姨教他烘焙。A、B、C 均与原文不符。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Tom broke two eggs into a bowl, but one piece of shell fell in."  
【解析】蛋壳掉入碗中是主要问题。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第④段 "the pan was hotter than I expected. I put on thick gloves..."  
【解析】烤盘很烫，所以戴厚手套。A、B、D 均与原文不符。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段 "my cake... carried something a store cake could never have — my own time, care and love."  
【解析】作者强调手作蛋糕蕴含爱与心意。A 片面；C 与"商店蛋糕"本身无关；D 仅为表面细节。故选 B。

---

**词汇注释（超纲词）**  
- **bakery** [n.] 面包房；烘焙店  
- **recipe** [n.] 食谱  
- **oven** [n.] 烤箱
