# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Is It Better to Live in the City or the Countryside?**

① China has changed a lot in recent years. Many people have moved from the countryside to big cities for better jobs and education. But is city life really better than life in the countryside? Let's look at both sides.

② City life has many advantages. Cities have better schools, hospitals, and public transport. There are more job opportunities. You can find museums, cinemas, libraries, and shopping centres. Young people enjoy the excitement and convenience of city life. If you need something, there is always a shop or a service nearby. Cities also offer a wider choice of food, entertainment, and activities.

③ However, city life also has problems. The air is often polluted because of cars and factories. The streets are crowded, especially during rush hour. Housing is very expensive. A small flat in a big city can cost more than a large house in the countryside. Many city people feel stressed and lonely. They work long hours and have little time for family or nature. The fast pace of life can be tiring.

④ Countryside life is quite different. The air is fresh, and the environment is quiet and peaceful. People know their neighbours and help each other. Food is often fresher because it comes straight from the farm. The pace of life is slower, and people have more time to relax. Children can play freely outdoors. Many old people prefer the countryside because it is calm and good for health.

⑤ But the countryside also has disadvantages. There are fewer schools and hospitals. Job choices are limited. Young people may feel bored because there is less entertainment. The internet may be slower, and some modern services are not available. This is why many young people choose to move to cities.

⑥ In my opinion, there is no perfect answer. It depends on what you value most. If you want convenience and career opportunities, the city may be better. If you prefer a peaceful and healthy life, the countryside might be the right choice. The most important thing is to find a place where you feel happy.

**注释：** opportunity 机会；polluted 受污染的；pace 节奏/步伐

---

**Questions:**

1. Why have many people moved from the countryside to cities?
   A. Because the countryside is too hot.
   B. For better jobs and education.
   C. Because the government asked them to.
   D. Because they don't like farming.

2. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a problem of city life?
   A. Air pollution.
   B. Expensive housing.
   C. Too many trees.
   D. Crowded streets.

3. What is one advantage of living in the countryside?
   A. There are more cinemas.
   B. The air is fresh and the environment is quiet and peaceful.
   C. There are more jobs.
   D. The internet is faster.

4. Why do many young people leave the countryside?
   A. They want to become farmers.
   B. There are fewer schools, hospitals, and jobs, and less entertainment.
   C. Their parents force them to leave.
   D. The countryside has too many people.

5. What is the writer's opinion?
   A. Everyone should live in the city.
   B. Everyone should live in the countryside.
   C. There is no perfect answer; it depends on what you value most.
   D. It doesn't matter where you live.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Many people have moved from the countryside to big cities for better jobs and education."

**【解析】** 人们进城是为了更好的工作和教育。A 项太热、C 项政府要求、D 项不喜欢务农均无依据。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段提到了空气污染、街道拥挤、住房昂贵等问题。

**【解析】** 文中提到城市问题包括空气污染（A）、住房贵（B）、街道拥挤（D），但从未提到"太多树"是城市问题。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The air is fresh, and the environment is quiet and peaceful."

**【解析】** 农村空气新鲜、环境安静祥和是其优点。A 项更多电影院、C 项更多工作、D 项网速更快均是城市特点或与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "There are fewer schools and hospitals. Job choices are limited. Young people may feel bored because there is less entertainment."

**【解析】** 农村学校和医院少、工作选择有限、娱乐少，导致年轻人离开。A 项想当农民、C 项父母逼迫、D 项农村人太多均不正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "there is no perfect answer. It depends on what you value most."

**【解析】** 作者认为没有完美答案，取决于个人看重什么。A 项都该住城市、B 项都该住农村过于片面；D 项住哪都无所谓并非作者态度。故选 C。
