> **01-06 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Lost and Found Notice Board — Sunshine Middle School**

① The following notices were posted on the school notice board this week.

---

**LOST**

**Item:** A blue pencil case with a cartoon cat on it
**Owner:** Li Xiaomei, Class 3, Grade 8
**When lost:** Monday, January 5, during lunch break
**Where:** Possibly in the dining hall or on the playground
**Contact:** Room 305 or call 138-XXXX-5678
**Note:** There are two pens, a ruler, and a USB drive inside. The USB drive has my homework on it. Please return it if you find it!

---

**FOUND**

**Item:** A pair of black sports shoes, size 39
**Found by:** Mr. Chen, the PE teacher
**When found:** Tuesday, January 6, after the basketball game
**Where:** Left in the boys' changing room
**Pick up:** Please come to the PE office (Room 108) with proof before Friday.

---

**LOST**

**Item:** A library book — "Robinson Crusoe"
**Owner:** Wang Tao, Class 1, Grade 7
**When lost:** Wednesday, January 7
**Where:** Somewhere between the library and the classroom building
**Contact:** Room 201 or send a message to 156-XXXX-8899
**Note:** The book is due on January 15. I will have to pay for it if I can't find it.

---

**FOUND**

**Item:** A silver watch
**Found by:** Zhang Yue, Class 5, Grade 9
**When found:** Thursday, January 8, morning
**Where:** Near the school gate
**Pick up:** Come to Class 5, Grade 9, or call 139-XXXX-2233. Please describe the watch to prove it is yours.

---

**Questions:**

1. What is inside Li Xiaomei's lost pencil case?
   A. Only pens.
   B. Two pens, a ruler, and a USB drive with her homework.
   C. A library book.
   D. A pair of sports shoes.

2. Where were the black sports shoes found?
   A. In the dining hall.
   B. Near the school gate.
   C. In the boys' changing room.
   D. In the library.

3. Why is Wang Tao worried about losing the library book?
   A. The book is very expensive.
   B. The book is due on January 15, and he will have to pay if he can't find it.
   C. His teacher will be angry.
   D. He has already read it.

4. If you find a silver watch, who should you contact?
   A. Li Xiaomei.
   B. Mr. Chen.
   C. Wang Tao.
   D. Zhang Yue in Class 5, Grade 9.

5. What do the "found" notices both require for pick-up?
   A. A student ID card.
   B. Proof that the item belongs to you.
   C. A letter from a teacher.
   D. Payment of a fee.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第一则 LOST 启事 "There are two pens, a ruler, and a USB drive inside."

**【解析】** 文具盒里有两支笔、一把尺子和一个存有作业的U盘。A 项只有笔不完整；C 项图书馆书和 D 项运动鞋是其他启事的内容。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第一则 FOUND 启事 "Left in the boys' changing room."

**【解析】** 黑色运动鞋是在男生更衣室找到的。A 项食堂、B 项校门口、D 项图书馆均非发现地点。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第二则 LOST 启事 "The book is due on January 15. I will have to pay for it if I can't find it."

**【解析】** 王涛担心的是书1月15日到期，找不到就要赔偿。A 项书很贵、C 项老师生气、D 项已经读过均非原文说的担忧原因。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第二则 FOUND 启事 "Found by: Zhang Yue, Class 5, Grade 9" 和 "Come to Class 5, Grade 9, or call 139-XXXX-2233."

**【解析】** 银色手表是张悦发现的，应联系她。A 项李小梅是丢文具盒的；B 项陈老师是捡到运动鞋的；C 项王涛是丢书的。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第一则 FOUND 启事 "come to the PE office with proof" 和第二则 FOUND 启事 "describe the watch to prove it is yours."

**【解析】** 两则"找到"启事都要求领取人提供证明物品属于自己的证据。A 项学生证、C 项老师信、D 项缴费均未提及。故选 B。
