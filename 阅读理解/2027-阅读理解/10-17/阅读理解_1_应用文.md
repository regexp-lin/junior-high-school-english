# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School "Eco-Makers" Autumn Workshop — Call for Student Members**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Science Club + Art Club**  
> **2027 Autumn "Eco-Makers" Workshop**  
> **Turn school waste into something useful, one small idea at a time**

Our school throws away about **18 kg of paper, plastic and metal every day**. This autumn, we want a different story. The Science Club and the Art Club are joining hands to open an **"Eco-Makers" workshop** for students in Grade 7 and Grade 8, so that we can turn a part of that waste into **real, useful things for the school**.

---

## 🧰 About the Workshop

- **Dates:** **Saturday afternoons from 24 October to 19 December** (nine weeks).  
- **Time:** 14:00 – 16:30.  
- **Place:** Labour Education Room (Building B, Room 105) + outdoor yard behind the canteen (for painting and drying).  
- **Team size:** **30 students**, working in six teams of five.  
- **Teachers on site:** Mr. Zhou (physics), Ms. Lin (art), and one visiting engineer from a local recycling company.

---

## 🛠 What We Will Make — Four "Mini-Missions"

**Mission 1 — Paper Stools (Weeks 1–3)**  
Folded cardboard stools strong enough for one student to sit on. **Target: 20 stools** for the school reading corner.

**Mission 2 — Glass-Jar Lamps (Weeks 4–5)**  
Clean glass jars from the canteen become small bedside lamps with a 3 V battery and warm LED. **Target: 15 lamps**, donated to a nearby primary school.

**Mission 3 — Plastic-Bottle Planters (Weeks 6–7)**  
Turn 500 mL plastic bottles into hanging planters for small herbs. **Target: 60 planters** for the school corridor.

**Mission 4 — Open Project (Weeks 8–9)**  
Each team chooses its own waste material and its own eco-product, then presents the idea at the final show on **19 December**.

---

## 📋 How to Apply

1. Fill in the green form at the **Science Office (Room 401)**, or on the **school mini-app** → "Clubs" → "Eco-Makers".  
2. Write **one short paragraph (60–80 words)** about a piece of waste in your daily life and how you might give it a second life.  
3. Tell us **which two tools** you already know how to use safely (scissors, hot-glue gun, ruler, saw, screwdriver, etc.).  
4. **Application closes Wednesday 22 October, 18:00.** Results will be posted at the bulletin board near the library **on Friday 24 October morning**.

---

## 🧷 Safety and Gear

- The workshop provides **gloves, aprons, goggles and all power tools**; you don't need to buy anything.  
- **No hot-glue gun is used by students alone** — always with a teacher watching.  
- **Long hair must be tied back**; please wear **flat shoes** (no high heels, no sandals).  
- Students with a skin reaction to paint or glue should mention this on the form; we will arrange safe tasks for you.

---

## 🌱 What You Will Get

- A handmade **"Eco-Maker" badge** after you finish any three missions.  
- **Two volunteer-service hours per session**, stamped into your school record.  
- Three **"Top Design" teams** at the December show will have their work displayed in the school hall for the whole of next term.  
- More important, you will leave the workshop with a skill most textbooks don't teach: **turning waste into quiet, useful beauty.**

---

*Planet Earth is our first classroom. See you on Saturday.*

*Lihua Middle School, Science Club + Art Club*  
*Posted 17 October 2027*
