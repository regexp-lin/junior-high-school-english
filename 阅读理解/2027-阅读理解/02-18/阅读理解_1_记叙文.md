# 阅读理解（记叙文） **Teaching Grandma to Use a Smartphone**

① My grandma is seventy-two years old. For years, she has used a small grey phone that can only make calls. Last Spring Festival, all the uncles and aunts decided to give her a surprise — a shining new smartphone. When they placed it in her hands, Grandma smiled politely, but I noticed her fingers trembling. "This thing is too clever for me," she whispered to me later that evening. "Perhaps your uncles should take it back."

② I promised that I would teach her. I was thirteen, and her youngest grandchild. On Sunday morning, we sat at the old wooden table by the window. Sunlight fell across a plate of cut apples. I opened the phone, smiled and said, "Don't worry, Grandma. I will go very slowly." She laughed a little. "Then you must be more patient than the math teacher of your father."

③ The first lesson was about the small "circle" at the bottom of the screen. I taught her to press it to go back to the main page. In the first hour, I must have said "press the round button" at least fifty times. Grandma pressed the side buttons, the volume keys, even the little camera lens by mistake. Each time, she apologised, "Sorry, sorry, my old eyes are not bright enough." I only smiled and said, "Grandma, your eyes taught me how to read. Now it's my turn."

④ After lunch, I began to teach her WeChat. I drew two little pictures on a paper napkin: one for "send a message" and one for "listen to a voice." When the first "hello" from her old friend Aunt Hua arrived with a soft *ding*, Grandma's eyes grew wider than usual. "She is really in the phone!" she laughed. Then she pressed the microphone and said loudly, word by word, "Hua-jie-hao. Today-I-learn-this-new-friend." Her voice trembled with joy, as if she was on the radio.

⑤ By the end of the day, Grandma had learned to send a short voice message, take a photo of her potted flower, and watch a short video of mountain streams. I felt proud, but even more I felt something warm. In my whole life, Grandma had taught me how to hold chopsticks, how to make a dumpling, how to sing a lullaby. For the first time, I was handing a small gift back to her, and I realised teaching feels just as sweet as being taught.

⑥ Before I went home that evening, Grandma held my hand and said, "Xiao Fang, thank you for not laughing at me." I shook my head and said, "Grandma, you were the best student I've ever had." That night, she sent me her first WeChat voice message. It said simply, "Good night, my little teacher. I love you." I listened to it three times, and quietly kept it in my phone, next to a photo of our family taken twenty years before I was even born.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the writer's uncles and aunts give Grandma a smartphone?**

A. Because her old grey phone was broken.  
B. Because they wanted to give her a Spring Festival surprise.  
C. Because the school asked every grandparent to have one.  
D. Because Grandma asked for it many times.

**2. What did Grandma say when she first received the phone?**

A. "It is exactly what I wanted."  
B. "This thing is too clever for me."  
C. "I will give it to my neighbour."  
D. "Please teach me WeChat only."

**3. What did the writer use to help Grandma understand WeChat?**

A. Two little pictures on a paper napkin.  
B. A long English book.  
C. A special pair of glasses.  
D. A card printed by a teacher.

**4. How did Grandma feel when Aunt Hua's voice message arrived?**

A. She was suddenly very sleepy.  
B. She was surprised and happy.  
C. She was a little angry with her friend.  
D. She was afraid and wanted to stop.

**5. What does the writer learn from this experience?**

A. Old people cannot really learn new things.  
B. Teaching someone can feel just as sweet as being taught.  
C. Smartphones are better than family meetings.  
D. Grandmothers should only listen to the radio.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "give her a surprise... a shining new smartphone"  
【解析】长辈们想给奶奶春节惊喜。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "This thing is too clever for me"  
【解析】奶奶说手机太聪明。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "I drew two little pictures on a paper napkin"  
【解析】用餐巾上的小图示。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第④段 "Grandma's eyes grew wider... trembled with joy"  
【解析】她惊讶又兴奋。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑤段 "teaching feels just as sweet as being taught"  
【解析】教人与被教一样温暖。故选 B。
