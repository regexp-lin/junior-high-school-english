# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — Autumn Sports Meet 2027**

---

## **Run, Jump, Cheer — See You on the Playground!**

Dear students, teachers and parents,

Lihua Middle School is happy to invite everyone to our **37th Autumn Sports Meet**, held from **Thursday, October 14** to **Saturday, October 16, 2027**. This year we have added **two new team events** and, for the first time, a **Parent–Child Relay** on the afternoon of Day 3. Our theme is **"Move Together, Grow Together"**. All Grade 7–9 students are encouraged to join.

---

## **Main Schedule**

| Day | Date | Morning (8:00–11:30) | Afternoon (14:00–17:00) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 1 | Oct 14, Thu | **Opening Ceremony** + 100m, 400m, long jump | High jump, 4×100m relay |
| Day 2 | Oct 15, Fri | 200m, 800m (girls) / 1,500m (boys), shot put | **Tug-of-War (NEW)** — class teams |
| Day 3 | Oct 16, Sat | 3-Legged Race, Basketball Shooting | **Parent–Child Relay (NEW)** + Closing Ceremony |

> **Rainy-day plan:** If it rains heavily, outdoor events move to **Sunday, Oct 17**, morning only. Indoor events (shot put, shooting) go on as planned.

---

## **How to Sign Up**

1. **Solo events (100m, 200m, 400m, 800m/1,500m, long jump, high jump, shot put):** Each class may enter up to **2 students** per event. Class teachers send the final list to PE office by **Sep 30**.  
2. **4×100m relay & Tug-of-War:** Each class forms **one team**; sign up on class notice board before **Oct 5**.  
3. **Parent–Child Relay:** **One student + one parent**. Scan the QR code in the class group by **Oct 8**. The first **30 family pairs** only.  
4. **3-Legged Race & Basketball Shooting:** Free for any student; come to the starting area **15 minutes before** the event.

---

## **Rules and Notes**

- **Sports uniform** and **sports shoes** are a must for every event. Slippers and skirts are not allowed on the track.  
- **No eating or drinking** (except water) inside the track area.  
- Every **athlete** must bring the **"Health Check Card"** signed by parents; students without the card cannot run long-distance events.  
- Please **do not leave rubbish** on the playground; each class has its own rubbish bag area.  
- The **school hospital** will stay open during all three days for small injuries; the school bus will **not** run on Day 3 afternoon because of the closing ceremony.

---

## **Cheering Squad & Volunteers**

- Each class forms a **cheering squad** of 10–15 students; best squad wins a class trophy.  
- **Volunteers** (help with scores and water): sign up in Room 102 before **Sep 28**; 12 hours of service will be added to your record.  
- Class boards near the east gate will be used for the **cheering poster contest** — one poster per class, A2 size, hand-drawn only.

---

## **Prizes**

- **Individual events:** Top 3 in each grade receive a certificate, a sports water bottle and score points for the class.  
- **Team events:** The winning class keeps the "Golden Shoe" trophy for one year.  
- **Best Sportsmanship Award:** Not by speed, but by fair play and help to other athletes, chosen by all PE teachers.  
- **Cheering Squad Trophy & Best Poster:** Announced at the closing ceremony.

---

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When will the Autumn Sports Meet be held in 2027?**

A. October 14–15.  
B. October 14–16, with October 17 as a rainy-day backup.  
C. The whole month of October.  
D. Only on the afternoon of October 16.

**2. On which day will the 1,500m boys' race be held?**

A. Day 1, in the morning.  
B. Day 2, in the morning.  
C. Day 2, in the afternoon.  
D. Day 3, in the afternoon.

**3. Xiao Ming wants to enter the 100m and also join the Parent–Child Relay with his mother. What should he do?**

A. Do nothing; every student is automatically entered.  
B. Let the class teacher send his name for 100m by Sep 30, and scan the QR code for the Parent–Child Relay by Oct 8.  
C. Sign up for both events by Oct 5 on the class notice board.  
D. Go to Room 102 before Sep 28 for both events.

**4. Which of the following is **allowed** on the track during the sports meet?**

A. Wearing slippers and a long skirt while running 400m.  
B. Eating hot noodles inside the track area.  
C. Drinking water during events.  
D. Long-distance running without the signed Health Check Card.

**5. How is the **Best Sportsmanship Award** chosen?**

A. By who runs the fastest.  
B. By fair play and help to other athletes, chosen by all PE teachers.  
C. By which class has the loudest cheering squad.  
D. By a lucky draw on the closing day.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开头 & Rainy-day plan  
【解析】主赛期 10/14–10/16，雨天顺延至 10/17 上午。A、C、D 均不全或不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Main Schedule Day 2 Morning 栏  
【解析】1,500m (boys) 安排在 Day 2 上午。A、C、D 均与课表不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】How to Sign Up 第 1 条（9/30 前报 100m）& 第 3 条（10/8 前扫码报亲子接力）  
【解析】两项规则分别说明两事的报名方式与截止日。A、C、D 均与规则不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules and Notes 第 1、2 条 "water" 是例外  
【解析】赛道区除水之外禁食禁饮，但水是被允许的。A 违反着装要求；B 违反饮食规定；D 违反健康卡规定。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Prizes Best Sportsmanship Award 条  
【解析】此奖靠 fair play 和帮助他人，由 PE 教师组投选。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。
