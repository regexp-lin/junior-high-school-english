> **10-15 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文） **The Last Saturday at Grandpa's Watch Shop**

① For thirty-one years, my grandpa ran a tiny **watch-repair shop** at the corner of Xinhua Street. The shop was only six square metres. A single wooden chair stood behind the counter, and a yellow lamp hung low so that its light fell right on the small tools. My mother said the lamp had never moved, not even when I was born.

② Last Saturday was **the last day the shop would open**. The building was going to be pulled down, and the corner would become part of a new metro station. Mum called me after breakfast. "Xiao Yu, go and help Grandpa pack. He may not say it, but he needs company."

③ When I arrived, the street was already half-gone. Two shops on the left had closed. Grandpa's blue sign still hung above the door — "**时光修理铺 · Time Repair Shop**" — the English part added by my cousin who studies at university. Grandpa was sitting on his wooden chair, cleaning a small silver watch with a soft brush. He did not look up. "Sit down," he said. "There is still work today."

④ A thin woman came in with a child's plastic watch. "My son cried all morning," she said. "The hand won't move." Grandpa nodded, took a tiny screwdriver, and opened the back. In two minutes the hand was ticking again. He refused her money. "Today is a free day," he said. The woman's eyes grew red. She held the watch with both hands and left without speaking.

⑤ By noon the shop was full of people. A retired teacher brought his old Shanghai-brand watch — Grandpa had repaired it in 1998. A taxi driver came with a watch his wife had given him for their wedding. A high-school boy, shy and quiet, placed on the counter a broken digital watch that once belonged to his grandfather, who had passed away last spring. Grandpa fixed them one by one. He did not charge anyone. **Some people paid with tea, some with apples, some with quiet tears.**

⑥ At five o'clock, the sun turned the street orange. Grandpa took out a small box and opened it in front of me. Inside was a watch I had never seen — simple, silver, with blue hands. "This was my father's," he said. "He gave it to me the day I opened this shop. Now you are fifteen. It is yours." I tried to say something but my voice would not come.

⑦ We closed the door together. Grandpa locked it twice. Then he slowly took down the yellow lamp, wrapped it in an old newspaper, and put it in my backpack. "A lamp for small, careful work," he said. "Wherever you study, let the light be low and steady. That is enough."

⑧ Walking home, I kept touching the watch in my pocket and the lamp on my back. The shop was gone, but **time had not stopped** — it had only moved into my own hands.

---

**注释：** watch-repair 修表；screwdriver 螺丝刀；steady 平稳的；metro station 地铁站

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why was last Saturday special for Grandpa's shop?**

A. It was Grandpa's birthday.  
B. It was the last day the shop would open before the building was pulled down.  
C. New workers were joining.  
D. Grandpa was moving to a new house.

**2. What did Grandpa charge the woman with the child's plastic watch?**

A. Ten yuan.  
B. Fifty yuan.  
C. Nothing; he said it was a "free day".  
D. A box of milk.

**3. Which of the following best describes the atmosphere in the shop at noon?**

A. Empty and cold.  
B. Angry and loud.  
C. Full of customers, quiet feelings, and simple gifts like tea and apples.  
D. Crowded and hurried, with everyone shouting.

**4. What did Grandpa give to the writer in the end?**

A. The silver watch that once belonged to Grandpa's father, and the yellow lamp.  
B. A bicycle.  
C. The wooden chair and a large clock.  
D. Only the blue sign.

**5. What does the writer mean by saying "**time had not stopped — it had only moved into my own hands**" in the last paragraph?**

A. The clocks were broken.  
B. She bought a new watch.  
C. Grandpa's work and memory will continue through her, even though the shop is gone.  
D. She wants to open a new shop tomorrow.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "the last day the shop would open ... pulled down ... new metro station"  
【解析】拆迁前最后一天。A、C、D 无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "He refused her money. 'Today is a free day,' he said."  
【解析】当日免费修理。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑤段 "Some people paid with tea, some with apples, some with quiet tears."  
【解析】描写安静温情的氛围。A、B、D 均与文意相反。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段 "This was my father's ... it is yours"；第⑦段 "took down the yellow lamp ... put it in my backpack"  
【解析】银表与黄灯双物件。B、C、D 均错。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题／主旨理解题  
【定位】第⑦段传承寓意 + 第⑧段结尾句  
【解析】表与灯象征祖父技艺与品格的延续。A、B 字面化，D 偏离。故选 C。
