# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Neighbourhood — Community Tree-Planting Day 2027**

---

## **A Green Morning, A Greener Street**

Dear neighbours and young friends,

This year, our neighbourhood is joining the city-wide **"Trees for Tomorrow"** project. On **Saturday, March 14, 2027**, we will hold our own **Community Tree-Planting Day** at the South Side Park. Last year, 180 families planted 220 young trees along Riverside Walk. This year, we hope to welcome **even more middle-school students** — the trees we plant today are for the school gates of 2037.

- **Date:** Saturday, March 14, 2027  
- **Time:** **8:30 – 11:30** (please arrive before 8:20)  
- **Where:** South Side Park, East Gate (next to Bus Stop "Lotus Road")  
- **Organised by:** Lihua Neighbourhood Council + Green City Volunteers

---

## **Programme of the Morning**

| Time | Activity | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 8:20–8:30 | Sign-in & get a green armband | Bring your ID card |
| 8:30–8:40 | Opening talk by Mr. Zhang, city forest officer | In front of the stage |
| 8:40–9:00 | Short "How to Plant" demo by volunteers | One demo tree per group |
| 9:00–11:00 | Planting in five small groups (see below) | Follow your group leader |
| 11:00–11:20 | Watering + labelling young trees | Label must be clear |
| 11:20–11:30 | Group photo & closing words | Near the stone bench |

> **Free light snacks** (fruit, water, bread) at the Rest Point after 10:30.

---

## **Sign-Up**

1. Scan the QR code on the yellow poster at the neighbourhood notice board, **or** visit `green.lihua-nb.cn`.  
2. Deadline: **March 7, 2027, 23:59**. First **200 people** only.  
3. Middle-school students (Grade 7–9) may sign up **alone**, **with a classmate**, or **with a parent**.  
4. Students under 13 years old must come with an adult.  
5. After sign-up, you will receive a short email two days before, telling your group number (1–5) and meeting point.

---

## **What to Bring and What to Wear**

**Please bring:**
- A pair of clean **cotton gloves** (we have extra pairs, but not many).  
- An **empty water bottle** (we will share tap water at the Rest Point).  
- A **hat** and **sun cream**.  
- A small **towel** for your hands.

**Please wear:**
- Long trousers and **closed shoes** (no sandals, no skirts).  
- A **dark, old T-shirt** — the soil will leave marks.

**Please do NOT bring:**
- Very young pets.  
- Expensive cameras near the planting area.  
- Sharp tools from home — all tools are provided.

---

## **Rules on the Day**

- Always **listen to your group leader**. Do not start digging alone.  
- Do not step inside the **yellow-tape safety line** when the digging machine is working.  
- If you feel dizzy or unwell, please go straight to the Rest Point and tell a volunteer.  
- Every student who plants at least **one tree with a proper label** will earn **3 green service hours** (verified by your school).  
- Please **do not pull out** any young tree after it is planted — even if you think it looks crooked. Our gardener will fix it later.

---

## **Prizes and Surprises**

- **Youngest Planter Prize:** the youngest student to plant a tree wins a "Little Gardener" cloth bag.  
- **Best Tree Label:** vote at the end of the morning.  
- **Family Green Award:** for the most family members planting in one group.  
- **Lucky Draw:** 10 lucky participants will win a one-year free pass to the City Botanic Garden. Winners will be announced on our neighbourhood WeChat group before March 18.

---

**1. When and where is the Community Tree-Planting Day 2027 held?**

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开头 "Saturday, March 14, 2027 — 8:30 – 11:30 — South Side Park, East Gate"  
【解析】原文明确日期、时间、地点。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Sign-Up 第 4 条 "Students under 13 years old must come with an adult"  
【解析】11 岁需由大人陪同。A、C、D 均与规则不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Please do NOT bring "Sharp tools from home"  
【解析】不得自带利器。A、B、D 均为建议携带的物品。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules on the Day "Every student who plants at least one tree with a proper label will earn 3 green service hours"  
【解析】原文明确奖励 3 小时。A、C、D 均无文中依据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Prizes and Surprises "Winners will be announced on our neighbourhood WeChat group before March 18"  
【解析】通过社区微信群公布。A、B、D 均与原文不符。故选 C。
