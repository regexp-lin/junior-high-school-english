# 阅读理解（应用文） **Ditan Spring Festival Temple Fair — Visitor's Guide**

① The **Ditan Spring Festival Temple Fair** is one of the oldest and most popular fairs in the city. This year's fair will be held in Ditan Park from **the first day to the fifth day** of the lunar new year. It is a great chance for both local people and visitors to feel the warm and colourful mood of a traditional Chinese New Year.

② **Opening Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Dates | Lunar Day 1 – Day 5 |
| Time | 8:30 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. |
| Place | Ditan Park (South Gate entry only) |
| Ticket | 10 yuan for adults, 5 yuan for students |
| Free entry | Children under 1.2 m, visitors over 60 |

③ **Main Activities**

- **Dragon and Lion Dance Show:** Main Stage, 10:00 a.m. & 2:00 p.m. every day.  
- **Folk Music Corner:** East Lawn, 11:00 a.m. – 12:30 p.m.  
- **Paper Cutting & Calligraphy Workshop:** Workshop Tent, 1:00 p.m. – 3:00 p.m. (small paid activity).  
- **Traditional Snack Street:** More than 30 shops. Try sugar paintings, candied fruit, hot soup and rice cakes.  
- **Old Toys Corner:** Bamboo dragonflies, shuttlecocks and small wooden horses for children to play.

④ **Useful Tips**

- Please **do not bring pets or big cameras** into the park.  
- **Rubbish bins** are placed every 50 metres. Please help keep the park clean.  
- The East Gate is **exit only**. To get to the metro, follow the blue signs.  
- On cold days, wear warm clothes and hats; the lawn can be windy in the afternoon.  
- A **first-aid point** is set up next to the Workshop Tent.

⑤ For group visits (more than 20 people), please call **010-6457-9988** three days in advance. The fair team wishes you a warm, happy and lucky Chinese New Year!

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many days does this year's Ditan Temple Fair last?**

A. Three days.  
B. Five days.  
C. Seven days.  
D. Fifteen days.

**2. Which visitors can enter the park for free?**

A. All Chinese adults.  
B. Foreign tourists with passports.  
C. Visitors over 60 years old.  
D. Any student under 18.

**3. When can people watch the Dragon and Lion Dance Show?**

A. At 10:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m. every day.  
B. Only at 5:00 p.m. on Day 1.  
C. At midnight on the last day.  
D. In the Workshop Tent all morning.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed in the park?**

A. Wearing warm hats.  
B. Bringing pets or big cameras.  
C. Trying sugar paintings.  
D. Playing with shuttlecocks in the toy corner.

**5. What should a group of 30 people do before the visit?**

A. Send emails to every teacher.  
B. Call 010-6457-9988 three days in advance.  
C. Buy a map at the East Gate only.  
D. Write a 1,000-word report first.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "from the first day to the fifth day of the lunar new year."  
【解析】庙会从正月初一到初五，共 5 天。A、C、D 与原文不符。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Free entry: Children under 1.2 m, visitors over 60."  
【解析】60 岁以上老人免票。A、B、D 均不符。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Dragon and Lion Dance Show: Main Stage, 10:00 a.m. & 2:00 p.m. every day."  
【解析】每日两场舞龙舞狮。B、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Please do not bring pets or big cameras into the park."  
【解析】宠物与大相机不得入园。A、C、D 均允许。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第⑤段 "For group visits (more than 20 people), please call 010-6457-9988 three days in advance."  
【解析】20 人以上团体需提前 3 天致电预约，30 人的团队属此类。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。
