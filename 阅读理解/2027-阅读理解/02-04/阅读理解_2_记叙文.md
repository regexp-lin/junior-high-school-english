# 阅读理解（记叙文） **My First Time Making Dumplings**

① The New Year was only two days away. I had watched Grandma make dumplings every winter, but this year, I decided I was old enough to make them by myself. "Grandma," I said proudly, "this year, I want to make the dumplings for the family dinner." She smiled, wiped her hands on her blue apron and said, "Then let's start right now, my little chef."

② We stood at the long kitchen table. Grandma placed a big bowl of flour in front of me and taught me to add water slowly. "Too much water, and the dough will be sticky. Too little, and it will break." My first try was terrible. The dough stuck to my fingers like glue. I pulled, I pushed, I almost cried. Grandma laughed gently, washed the flour off my hands, and guided my movements with her own warm fingers. Little by little, a smooth round ball of dough appeared under our four hands.

③ Next came the pork and cabbage filling. I thought cutting cabbage would be easy, but my knife moved in unsteady lines. Green juice ran across the wooden board. My younger brother Bob, who had come to watch, giggled loudly. "Your dumplings will run away!" I felt my face turn as red as the lucky paper on the window. Still, Grandma said kindly, "A cook who is afraid of a knife can never cook a good meal."

④ Finally, it was time to wrap. I held a thin round wrapper in my left hand and placed a spoon of filling in the middle. I tried to press the edges together, but the wrapper kept opening, like a small mouth refusing to close. My first three dumplings were flat, my fourth looked like a little sheep lying on its back, and my fifth broke in the middle with filling running out. Bob laughed until he fell off his chair.

⑤ But I did not give up. By the twentieth dumpling, my fingers had learned the small movements — fold, press, close — just like a secret song. When Mom and Dad came home from work, Grandma held up my plate with great pride. "Look! Your daughter made these tonight." Dad looked at the dumplings carefully, some fat, some thin, some standing, some sleeping. Then he picked one up, tasted it slowly, and said, "The best dumplings of my life."

⑥ That night, the whole family ate from my plate. The kitchen was full of steam, laughter and the soft sound of chopsticks. I understood at last what Grandma had often said: "A family is not only held together by blood, but also by the warm food made with our own hands."

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did the writer want to make dumplings this year?**

A. Because Grandma could no longer move her fingers.  
B. Because the writer thought he/she was old enough.  
C. Because there was no money to buy dumplings.  
D. Because the writer wanted to sell them on the street.

**2. What problem did the writer meet while making the dough?**

A. The dough turned black.  
B. The flour fell on the floor.  
C. The dough stuck to the writer's fingers.  
D. Grandma forgot to bring water.

**3. What does the underlined sentence in Paragraph 3 mean?**

A. A real cook must own a sharp knife.  
B. A cook who fears a knife cannot cook well.  
C. A good cook never uses a knife.  
D. A kitchen knife must be very small.

**4. How did the writer's first dumplings look?**

A. All of them were round and perfect.  
B. Some were flat or broken, not all perfect.  
C. They were all the same size.  
D. They were the best in the neighborhood.

**5. What does the writer mainly learn from the experience?**

A. Making dumplings is a waste of time.  
B. Grandmas always make the best food.  
C. Warm food made by family members holds a family together.  
D. Younger brothers are always annoying.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "this year, I decided I was old enough"  
【解析】作者认为自己长大了，能独自包饺子。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "The dough stuck to my fingers like glue."  
【解析】面团粘手。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】句意理解题  
【定位】第③段 "A cook who is afraid of a knife can never cook a good meal."  
【解析】怕刀者成不了好厨师。A、C、D 均曲解。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "My first three dumplings were flat... fifth broke"  
【解析】最初饺子有扁有破。A、C、D 相反或无据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第⑥段 "held together by... warm food made with our own hands"  
【解析】家人亲手做的饭菜维系家庭。A、B、D 均偏题。故选 C。
