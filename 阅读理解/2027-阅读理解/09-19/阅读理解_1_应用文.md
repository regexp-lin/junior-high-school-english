# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — 2027 Autumn Pen-pal Exchange Programme "Letters Across the Sea" (with Thames Middle School, the UK)**

---

> **Lihua Middle School — Foreign Languages Department**
> **Programme Notice | Application Form Inside**
> Released: **12th September 2027**

**Hello, students of Grade 7 to Grade 9!**

We are happy to announce that our school's **Pen-pal Exchange Programme** with **Thames Middle School in Bristol, the United Kingdom** will start again this autumn. The new round, **"Letters Across the Sea 2027"**, will run from **Monday 11th October 2027** to **Friday 19th March 2028**.

Over these **five months**, every student in the programme will write four real handwritten letters to a British pen-pal of similar age, exchange two small cultural gifts, and join three online video talks.

---

## Part 1 — What the Programme Includes

| Item | Details | Time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Letter 1 | Self-introduction + your family + your city | Posted by **25 Oct** |
| Letter 2 | A day in your school life | Posted by **30 Nov** |
| Gift Box A | Small Chinese cultural gift (book-mark, paper-cut, tea bag, etc. — total value under ¥30) | Posted **1–10 Dec** |
| Video Talk 1 | Live video meeting in English | **Sun 14 Dec**, 20:00 |
| Letter 3 | Traditional festival — Spring Festival story | Posted by **25 Jan** |
| Video Talk 2 | Live video meeting | **Sun 8 Feb**, 20:00 |
| Letter 4 | Your hopes for the next 5 years | Posted by **5 Mar** |
| Gift Box B | Hand-written scroll or painting (optional) | Posted **1–10 Mar** |
| Video Talk 3 | Final group meeting | **Sun 15 Mar**, 20:00 |

> All letters will be **read by one English teacher** before posting, but **the content stays yours** — teachers correct obvious spelling and grammar, not ideas.

---

## Part 2 — Who Can Join?

- Any current **Lihua Middle School** student in **Grade 7 / 8 / 9**.
- Term-one English score of **80 or above** *(Grade 7: 75 or above)*.
- Able to **attend all three video talks at 20:00 Beijing time** on the dates listed above.
- Willing to **post real paper letters** (stamps paid by student, about ¥6.5 per letter).

---

## Part 3 — How to Apply

1. **Sign up online** at **exchange.lihuams.edu.cn** between **15th Sep and 28th Sep**.
2. In the form, write **a 120-word introduction in English** about yourself and why you want a British pen-pal.
3. Final list of **60 students** will be posted on the **school website** on **Friday 3rd Oct**.
4. Those chosen must come to **Lecture Hall B on Sunday 5th Oct at 14:30** for a short briefing and to receive the first address card.

---

## Part 4 — Important Rules

- **Do not share** your home address, phone number, ID number, or parents' bank information in any letter.
- Cultural gifts must **not** include food with meat, live plants, or anything forbidden by UK customs.
- Students who miss **two video talks** without a good reason will be **removed** from the programme.
- All letters must be in **English**; small Chinese phrases with English translation are allowed.

---

## Part 5 — Teacher Support

- **Head teacher:** Ms Zhou (Room 308, Foreign Languages Office)
- **Email:** penpal@lihuams.edu.cn
- **Help sessions:** every **Wednesday, 16:00–17:00**, Room 308, starting from 15th October.

---

>

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long will the 2027 Pen-pal Exchange Programme last?**

A. About one month, from October to November 2027.  
B. About five months, from Monday 11th October 2027 to Friday 19th March 2028.  
C. About one full year, from September 2027 to September 2028.  
D. Only three weeks during the Spring Festival holiday.

**2. According to Part 2, which of these students is NOT allowed to apply?**

A. A Grade-8 girl whose English score in last term was 82.  
B. A Grade-7 boy whose English score in last term was 78.  
C. A Grade-9 girl whose English score was 85, who can join all three video talks.  
D. A Grade-8 boy whose term-one English score was 70 and who often has late family activities on Sunday evenings.

**3. What will happen to a chosen student who misses TWO video talks without a good reason?**

A. Nothing — absences do not matter.  
B. The student will be removed from the programme.  
C. The student must pay a fine of ¥200.  
D. The student must write two extra letters.

**4. On which date should chosen students attend the short briefing and receive their first address card?**

A. Sunday 28th September at 14:30.  
B. Friday 3rd October at 20:00.  
C. Sunday 5th October at 14:30 in Lecture Hall B.  
D. Monday 11th October at 14:30.

**5. Which of the following is FORBIDDEN in a letter or gift, according to Part 4?**

A. A small Chinese bookmark worth ¥10.  
B. A short sentence in Chinese followed by its English translation.  
C. The student's home address and parents' bank information.  
D. A self-introduction written in English.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】正文 "from Monday 11th October 2027 to Friday 19th March 2028 ... five months"  
【解析】项目为期约 5 个月。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节判断题  
【定位】Part 2 要求 "English score of 80 or above (Grade 7: 75 or above) ... attend all three video talks"  
【解析】D 中 8 年级 70 分未达 80，且周日晚常有活动无法参加视频，故不符合。A、B、C 均符合。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 4 "miss two video talks without a good reason will be removed"  
【解析】无正当理由缺席两次视频将被取消资格。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 3 第 4 条 "Lecture Hall B on Sunday 5th Oct at 14:30"  
【解析】10 月 5 日周日 14:30 在 B 报告厅开会。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Part 4 "Do not share your home address, phone number, ID number, or parents' bank information"  
【解析】地址和父母银行信息严禁。A、B、D 均允许。故选 C。
