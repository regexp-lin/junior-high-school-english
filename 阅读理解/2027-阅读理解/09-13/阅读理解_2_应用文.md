# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — National Day Documentary Film Week 2027**

> **Lihua Middle School · Student History & Film Club**
> **"Our Country, Our Ordinary Heroes" — National Day Documentary Film Week 2027**
> **A Warm Invitation to Students, Teachers and Parents**

---

### 1. About This Week

From **Monday 28 September** to **Wednesday 30 September 2027**, and on the afternoon of **National Day, Thursday 1 October**, our school will hold a four-day **documentary film week**, open to all students of Grades 7–9. The theme this year is: **"Our Country, Our Ordinary Heroes — how everyday Chinese people quietly shape our shared home."**

We will not watch loud action films or new movies from the cinema. We will watch four carefully chosen documentaries about **real people and real work** — and we will talk, in small groups, about what we saw.

### 2. When & Where

- **Day 1 — Mon 28 Sep (16:30–18:00)** — Hall B, Room 305
- **Day 2 — Tue 29 Sep (16:30–18:00)** — Hall B, Room 305
- **Day 3 — Wed 30 Sep (16:30–18:00)** — Hall B, Room 305
- **Day 4 — Thu 1 Oct (14:00–17:00)** — Main Auditorium (open to families)

Each day includes one documentary + a 20-minute small-group discussion in Chinese and English.

### 3. The Four Films

| Day | Film Title | Runtime | Main Topic |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **"The Early Train"** | 45 min | A retired railway driver on his last winter run |
| 2 | **"Blue Coats"** | 48 min | A rural clinic doctor and her 300 patients |
| 3 | **"Rice at Six"** | 50 min | A young farmer returning to her home village |
| 4 | **"Letters to Beijing"** | 70 min | Teachers in a mountain school for 30 years |

All films include **English subtitles**. A printed vocabulary sheet will be given before each film, with **10 key words** to help younger students follow the story.

### 4. Who Can Join?

- All **Grade 7, 8 and 9 students** of Lihua Middle School.
- **Teachers** and **parents** are welcome on Day 4 only; please sign in at the auditorium door by 13:40.
- Students with health needs (noise sensitivity, fear of loud voices) should tell Ms Yu Jia in advance; a quieter seat will be saved.

### 5. How to Sign Up

- **Online**: school portal → "Film Week 2027" → select the days you wish to attend.
- **Offline**: fill in a small paper slip at Teaching Block B, Room 305, **16:00–16:25**, Monday to Friday.
- Sign-up closes at **Friday 26 September, 17:00**.
- No ticket, no money; attendance is **free**.

### 6. Small-Group Discussions

After each film, we will form **small discussion groups of 6–8 students** at round tables. Each group will answer three simple questions together:

1. **Who** was the "ordinary hero" in today's film, and what did he or she do?
2. **Which moment** moved you most? Why?
3. **Do you know someone in your own family** who quietly does similar work?

Student leaders of each group will write 5–8 short lines on a large paper sheet; all sheets will be shown on the wall of Hall B during the following week. No student's name will appear without permission.

### 7. Behaviour Rules

- Please **arrive on time** — no late entry once the film has started.
- **No phone use** during the films (silent mode is not enough).
- **No food or drinks** inside the hall, except the free warm water at the back of the room.
- Please **clean your seat** before leaving and put any waste paper into the green bins at the door.

### 8. A Reflection Task (Optional)

After the film week, interested students may write a **short 300-word reflection** (Chinese or English) on any one of the four films, and send it by Sunday 5 October to `film2027@lihuaschool.cn`. The best reflections will be shared, with permission, on the "Morning Breeze" school newspaper.

### 9. Contact

- **Ms Yu Jia**, Student History & Film Club teacher, Teaching Block B, Room 305
- **Xu Ziyan**, student organiser, Grade 9 Class 4
- Emergency on the day: the blue-vest help team at Hall B entrance

> **"A country is not only built by famous names in textbooks. It is quietly built every day by ordinary hands we may never meet."**
> — Lihua Student History & Film Club

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 1 条 "The theme this year is: 'Our Country, Our Ordinary Heroes — how everyday Chinese people quietly shape our shared home.'"  
【解析】主题明确。其余均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 条 "Each day includes one documentary + a 20-minute small-group discussion"  
【解析】四部纪录片、每场 20 分钟小组讨论。A、C、D 均错。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 条 "Teachers and parents are welcome on Day 4 only"；第 2 条 "Day 4 — Thu 1 Oct ... Main Auditorium (open to families)"  
【解析】Day 4 开放家长、教师入场。A、B、C 均仅限学生。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节判断题（否定型）  
【定位】第 7 条 "No phone use during the films (silent mode is not enough)"  
【解析】观影期间禁止使用手机。A、B、D 均被允许或要求。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨/写作意图题  
【定位】结尾语 "A country ... is quietly built every day by ordinary hands we may never meet"  
【解析】国家由无数默默无闻的普通人共同建设。A、C、D 均不合本意。故选 B。
