# 阅读理解（应用文） **Morning Glory School Garden — Volunteer Sign-Up**

> **MORNING GLORY MIDDLE SCHOOL**  
> **SCHOOL GARDEN VOLUNTEER TEAM · SUMMER 2027**  
> *"Small hands, big garden. Grow with us."*

---

Dear students,

Welcome to the tenth summer of our **School Garden Volunteer Team**. Last year, twenty-four students harvested more than 80 kilos of tomatoes, cucumbers and bean sprouts, which were donated to the school kitchen and to a nearby elderly home. This summer, we invite **30 new student volunteers** to join us from **1 June to 26 August 2027**.

### Main Tasks

| Day | Morning Tasks (7:30–9:00) | Afternoon Tasks (16:30–18:00) |
|-----|----------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Mon** | Watering and leaf check | Weeding Area A |
| **Tue** | Watering and soil loosening | Building bamboo supports |
| **Wed** | Watering and pest check | Composting leaves and shells |
| **Thu** | Watering and picking ripe fruit | Weeding Area B |
| **Fri** | Washing and packing for donation | Cleaning tools and storage |
| **Sat** | Family Open Garden 9:00–11:00 | — |

Each volunteer will pick **one morning shift** and **one afternoon shift** per week.

### What You Will Learn

- Safe use of simple tools: watering can, small spade, soft gloves.  
- How to read plant leaves and spot problems early.  
- Basic composting: turning kitchen waste into soil food.  
- Friendly teamwork in a real working garden.

### Who Can Join

- Students in **Grades 6–9** of any middle school in our city.  
- Students must be able to bend, kneel and carry up to **5 kg** safely.  
- Students with serious grass or pollen allergies are kindly asked to wait for our autumn indoor projects.

### Rules to Remember

1. Wear long sleeves, long trousers and closed shoes. **No sandals or flip-flops.**  
2. Bring a water bottle, a straw hat, and clean gloves if you have them.  
3. Do not eat fruit picked straight from the plants before washing.  
4. Listen to the **Garden Captain** and do not touch garden tools until the short safety talk is finished.  
5. If you feel tired, dizzy or too hot, sit in the shade and tell a teacher at once.

### Rewards

- Weekly garden diary + one piece of fresh produce to take home (mid-July to late August).  
- **Volunteer Certificate** and **40 community service hours**.  
- The three most active volunteers will win a free seat at the city's "Young Green Scientists" weekend camp (September).

### How to Apply

- **Online form:** www.morningglorymid.edu.cn/garden2027  
- **On paper:** Ask the Moral Education Office, Room 205, Building B.  
- **Deadline:** 28 May 2027, 17:00.

For any question, please write to **garden@morningglorymid.edu.cn** or find Mr. Liu (garden teacher) in the school greenhouse during recess.

**Small hands, big garden. See you in the soil.**

— Morning Glory School Garden Team

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】开头段 "we invite 30 new student volunteers..."  
【解析】邀请 30 名新志愿者。A/B/D 均不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格周三下午 "Composting leaves and shells"  
【解析】周三下午堆肥。A 为早班任务、C/D 为其他日。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules 第1条 "No sandals or flip-flops."  
【解析】禁穿凉鞋/拖鞋。A/B/D 均符合规则。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Who Can Join "Students with serious grass or pollen allergies are kindly asked to wait..."  
【解析】严重花草过敏者建议等秋季室内项目。A/C/D 均符合或无关。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rewards 第3条 "The three most active volunteers will win a free seat..."  
【解析】前三名活跃志愿者获得夏令营免费名额。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。
