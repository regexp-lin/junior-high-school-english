> **YOUTH CODE CLUB**  
> **SUMMER WORKSHOPS 2027**

Dear students and parents,

The **Youth Code Club** will open its doors for four weeks of small-group **coding workshops** this summer. All classes are taught in **English and Chinese**. No prior coding experience is needed for the Starter courses. Every student will bring home a **digital project folder** and a paper **Certificate of Completion**.

### Courses

| Code | Title | Age | Level | Days | Time |
|------|-------|-----|-------|------|------|
| **A1** | Block Coding with Scratch | 10–12 | Starter | Mon & Wed | 09:00–11:00 |
| **A2** | Make Your First 2-D Game | 12–14 | Starter+ | Tue & Thu | 09:00–11:00 |
| **B1** | Tiny Robots (LEGO Spike) | 11–13 | Starter | Mon & Wed | 14:00–16:00 |

*Each course runs for 4 weeks, from **7 July to 3 August 2027**.*

### Fees and Discounts

- **One course:** 480 yuan (4 weeks).  
- **Two courses together:** 880 yuan (save 80 yuan).  
- **Free seats:** 5 free seats per course for students from families with a City Care Card. Apply before 20 June.

### How to Sign Up

1. Scan the QR code on our school notice board, **or**  
2. Send an email to **youthcodeclub@greenhill.edu.cn** with your name, age, school, chosen course code, and parent's phone number.  
3. Payment link will be sent only after we confirm your seat.

### Important Rules

- Each class has **only 12 seats**, first come, first served.  
- Bring your own **water bottle** and a **notebook**. A laptop is NOT required — computers are provided.  
- **Parents** may stay in the waiting area but not inside the classroom.  
- If you cannot come, please tell us at least **24 hours** in advance.

**根据通告内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Which course is suitable for a 10-year-old total beginner?**

A. A1 Block Coding with Scratch.  
B. B2 Python Basics for Beginners.  
C. C1 Weekend Maker Lab.  
D. A2 Make Your First 2-D Game.

**2. How much can a student save by signing up for two courses together?**

A. 480 yuan.  
B. 880 yuan.  
C. 80 yuan.  
D. 160 yuan.

**3. Which of the following is TRUE according to the programme?**

A. Students must bring their own laptop to class.  
B. Each class has only 12 seats.  
C. Parents can sit inside the classroom.  
D. No free seats are offered to any student.

**4. When does the summer programme run?**

A. From 7 July to 3 August 2027.  
B. From 1 June to 30 June 2027.  
C. Every Sunday of May.  
D. Only on 1 July.

**5. Who would probably find this programme MOST useful?**

A. University physics students.  
B. Young children under 8.  
C. Middle school students interested in coding, games or robots.  
D. Retired workers looking for a sports club.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Courses 表 A1 行 "Age 10–12, Starter"  
【解析】A1 适合 10–12 岁零基础学生。B2 为 Python 中级班（13–15 岁）；C1 适合 12–15 岁；A2 年龄也不匹配。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Fees and Discounts "Two courses together: 880 yuan (save 80 yuan)"  
【解析】两门一起报名可省 80 元。A 单科价、B 合并价、D 无依据。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Important Rules 第1条 "Each class has only 12 seats"  
【解析】每班仅 12 个座位。A 原文明确"laptop is NOT required"；C 家长不能进教室；D 文中提供 5 个 City Care Card 免费名额。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Courses 表下方说明 "from 7 July to 3 August 2027"  
【解析】7 月 7 日至 8 月 3 日。B/C/D 均与原文不符。故选 A。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】文末 "We believe every student can build something cool" 结合年龄段（10–15 岁）  
【解析】课程面向对编程、游戏或机器人感兴趣的中学生。A 大学生、B 8 岁以下、D 退休工人均不在年龄与目标群体范围内。故选 C。
