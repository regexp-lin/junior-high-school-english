# 阅读理解（应用文） **Blue Wind Middle School — Spring Sports Day Notice**

> **Blue Wind Middle School**  
> **Notice — Spring Sports Day 2027**  
> **Posted by:** Student Union Office  
> **Date:** 20 April 2027

---

**Dear students and parents,**

We are happy to announce our **Spring Sports Day 2027**. All students from Grade 7 to Grade 9 are warmly welcomed. Please read the information below carefully.

---

### 1. Date & Place

- **Date:** Saturday, 8 May 2027 (rain date: Sunday, 9 May)  
- **Opening time:** 8:00 a.m.  
- **Closing time:** 4:30 p.m.  
- **Place:** Blue Wind Middle School Main Playground (North Gate A)

### 2. Events You Can Join

| Group | Event | Who Can Join |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| Track | 100 m, 400 m, 4 × 100 m Relay | All students |
| Field | Long Jump, High Jump, Shot Put (Grade 8–9 only) | By grade |
| Fun   | Tug-of-war, 30-person Skipping, Classmate Carry | Class teams |
| Family| Parent-Child 50 m Run | 1 parent + 1 student |

Each student **must choose at least 1** event, but **no more than 3** (Relay counts as 1). Sign up on your class wall before **Friday, 30 April**, 4:00 p.m.

### 3. What to Bring

- **Sports clothes** of your grade colour (Grade 7 blue, Grade 8 green, Grade 9 red)  
- **Clean sports shoes** (no slippers)  
- **Water bottle** with your name  
- **Sun hat** and **light jacket** (the weather can change)  
- **Class flag**, drums, small posters (please do not use loudspeakers)

### 4. Safety Rules

1. Running students **must have a health card** from the school doctor.  
2. Students who are ill or injured should **tell their teacher first**.  
3. No food is allowed **on the running track**.  
4. Only parent volunteers with **yellow wristbands** may enter the field.

### 5. Awards

- **Gold**, **silver**, **bronze** medals for each event  
- **Spirit Cup** for the class with the best cheering and tidiest seats  
- **Sunshine Family Prize** for 3 parent-child teams

All winners will be shown in our school WeChat account on **Monday, 10 May**.

For questions, please call **Mr Lin (Student Union Office)** at **020-8812-6677**, or e-mail **sports@bluewind.edu.cn** before 30 April.

**Let's play hard and play fair. See you on the playground!**

**Student Union Office**

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Section 1 "8 May 2027"  
【解析】运动会定于5月8日。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Section 2 "no more than 3"  
【解析】最多三项。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Section 3 "please do not use loudspeakers"  
【解析】不允许用大扩音器。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Section 4 第4条  
【解析】只有黄色腕带家长志愿者可进赛场。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Section 5 末句  
【解析】获奖者通过学校微信公众号公布。故选 A。
