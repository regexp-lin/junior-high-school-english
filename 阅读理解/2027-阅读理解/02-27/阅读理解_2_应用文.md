# 阅读理解（应用文） **Tree-Planting Day — Volunteer Call**

① Dear Grade 7 – 9 students, spring is coming and so is our **"Green Campus, Green Future"** tree-planting programme. The **Greenhill Environmental Club** together with the **City Forestry Station** warmly invites you to join us on our yearly tree-planting day. Let us turn one bare hill slope into a small forest that will grow with you.

② **Event Information**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Date | **Sunday, 12 March 2027** (Planting Day) |
| Time | 8:00 – 12:30 |
| Meeting place | Main gate of **Willow Lake Park** |
| Planting site | **East Slope of Mount Willow** (15 min by bus) |
| Volunteers needed | **120 students**, 60 parents |

③ **What you will do**

1. **Morning briefing (8:00 – 8:30):** Forestry officer Mr. Huang will teach us the correct way to dig a hole, hold a sapling, and water it.  
2. **Planting (8:30 – 11:00):** Work in small groups of 4. Each group will plant and label **three young cherry or pine trees**.  
3. **Name card (11:00 – 11:30):** Write your group name on a wooden card. In June, you can come back to see how your trees have grown.  
4. **Clean-up & picnic (11:30 – 12:30):** Collect rubbish along the path. Enjoy a simple bread and juice snack before going home.

④ **What to bring**

- Old clothes and shoes (you will get muddy!)  
- **Gloves** and a small towel  
- Reusable water bottle  
- Sunhat and **sunscreen**  
- Your student ID card

⑤ **What NOT to bring**

- Glass bottles  
- Any toy lighter or fire-starter  
- Drone or loud speaker

⑥ **How to register**

- Step 1: Scan the QR code at the notice board outside the school library.  
- Step 2: Fill in the online form **before 5 March**. Late forms cannot be accepted.  
- Step 3: Each volunteer will receive a **green cotton cap** on the event day.  
- Certificates will be sent to the class teacher one week after the event.

⑦ **Contact**  
- Ms. Zhou (Greenhill Club) — Room 312  
- Email: greenhill@willow-ms.edu.cn  
- Phone: 0571-8820-1234 (14:00 – 17:00 on school days)

**Let's plant a small tree today and watch a greener future grow with us! — Greenhill Environmental Club**
