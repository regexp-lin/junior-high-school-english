# 阅读理解（应用文） **Grade 8 Science Fair — Call for Projects**

① Dear Grade 8 students, the **River City Middle School Science Fair** is back! This year's theme is **"Small Ideas, Big Changes"**. We welcome any project that makes daily life a little better, greener or safer. You may work alone or in a team of up to three students. All projects will be shown in the school gym on **Sunday, 18 April 2027**.

② **Basic Information**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Open to | Grade 8 students only |
| Team size | 1 to 3 people |
| Sign-up deadline | **25 March, 18:00** |
| Project submission | **12 April, 17:00** |
| Fair day | **18 April 2027**, 9:00 – 15:30 |
| Place | School Gym, Hall B |

③ **Topic Examples**

- A **smart plant pot** that tells you when the flower needs water.  
- A **mini rain collector** made of plastic bottles for the school garden.  
- A **hand-warmer** that uses a chemical reaction (with a teacher's help).  
- A **health quiz app** built with Scratch.  
- A **noise alarm** for the library that turns red when the room is too loud.  
*(You may also choose your own topic — just make sure it is safe!)*

④ **Rules**

1. **Safety first.** No open fire, no strong chemicals, no electric voltage higher than 24 V.  
2. Your poster must not be larger than **90 cm × 120 cm** — we need enough space for all teams.  
3. Every team must bring a **3-minute talk** in English or Chinese to explain how and why the project works.  
4. **No ready-made kits** bought online may be used as the whole project. You must build or rebuild at least **half** of the parts by yourselves.  
5. Projects that are not your own (copied from the Internet) will not be judged.

⑤ **Prizes**

- **Gold Idea Prize** (1 team) — 500-yuan book card + trophy + photo with the principal  
- **Green Planet Prize** (1 team, most eco-friendly) — 300-yuan book card  
- **Best Teamwork Prize** (2 teams) — 200-yuan book card each  
- **Young Maker Prize** (5 teams) — a nice science notebook each  
- Every team that takes part will receive a **certificate**.

⑥ **Judges**

- Mr. Han, Physics teacher  
- Ms. Yu, Chemistry teacher  
- Prof. Lin, guest from City University of Engineering  
- Two parent engineers from the "Parents-on-Campus" project

⑦ **How to Sign Up**

- Scan the **yellow QR code** on the science lab door.  
- Fill in team name, members, and a short idea description (no more than 80 Chinese characters).  
- Any question? Email **science-fair@rc-ms.edu.cn** before **23 March**.

**Every big change starts from one small idea — yours could be next! — Science Club**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is this year's theme of the science fair?**

A. "Robots Around Us".  
B. "Small Ideas, Big Changes".  
C. "Green Future, Green Home".  
D. "Back to Nature".

**2. Which of the following is NOT allowed at the fair?**

A. A smart plant pot made by students.  
B. A noise alarm for the library.  
C. A project that uses 220 V electricity.  
D. A health quiz app built with Scratch.

**3. Who will NOT be one of the judges?**

A. Mr. Han, a physics teacher from the school.  
B. Prof. Lin, a guest from a university.  
C. Two parent engineers.  
D. A Grade 9 student from a top school.

**4. What will every team that takes part receive?**

A. A trophy.  
B. A 500-yuan book card.  
C. A certificate.  
D. A photo with the principal.

**5. What is the last day for students to sign up?**

A. 18 April 2027.  
B. 25 March, 18:00.  
C. 12 April, 17:00.  
D. 23 March, all day.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段第 2 句 "This year's theme is 'Small Ideas, Big Changes'."  
【解析】原文明示。A、C、D 无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段第 1 条 "no electric voltage higher than 24 V"  
【解析】220 V 明显超过 24 V 的安全限制。A、B、D 均为示例中的可行项目。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段列出的 4 类评委  
【解析】评委是两位校内老师、大学教授、家长工程师；并未列出九年级学生。故选 D。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段末句 "Every team that takes part will receive a certificate."  
【解析】参与即获证书。A、B、D 均为具体奖项，非人人都有。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段表格 "Sign-up deadline 25 March, 18:00"  
【解析】报名截止为 3 月 25 日 18:00。A 是展会日，C 是作品提交日，D 是咨询邮件截止日。故选 B。
