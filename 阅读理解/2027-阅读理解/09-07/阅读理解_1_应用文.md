# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — "Safe Campus" Emergency Drill Week 2027**

> **Lihua Middle School · Student Safety Office**
> **"Safe Campus" Emergency Drill Week — 2027**
> **An Important Notice to All Students and Staff**

---

### 1. Why This Week?

Every term, the Safety Office runs one full **drill week** to make sure that every student of Lihua Middle School knows **what to do in the first minute** of a fire, an earthquake or a medical emergency. This term, we also add a new item: **"Safe Internet Behaviour"**, because accidents today can begin on a screen, not only on a staircase.

### 2. When & Where

- **Dates**: Monday **22 September** – Friday **26 September 2027**
- **Main drill area**: Teaching Block A, Teaching Block B, Central Lawn
- **Indoor theory room**: School Hall B, 3F, Rooms 303 – 305
- **Time**: during normal school hours; one drill or short lesson per day.

### 3. Five Daily Themes

| Day | Date | Theme | Main Activity |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mon | 22 Sep | **Fire Evacuation** | Whole-school fire drill, 10:15; exit by the nearest stairway |
| Tue | 23 Sep | **Earthquake Response** | "Drop, cover, hold on" practice, 10:15; 60-second hide-under-desk test |
| Wed | 24 Sep | **First Aid Basics** | Small-group training: how to stop bleeding, how to help someone who faints |
| Thu | 25 Sep | **Road & Cycling Safety** | A 40-minute talk + cycling safety check at the south gate |
| Fri | 26 Sep | **Safe Internet Behaviour** | A new theme: online scams, cyber-bullying, password care |

### 4. The "Three Bells" Rule

Every drill is announced by one of three bell sounds:

- **Long single bell (one long "beep")** → Fire drill. Leave the classroom; do NOT go back for school bags.
- **Short double bell (two quick "beeps")** → Earthquake drill. Stay inside; drop under the desk, cover the head, hold on.
- **Short triple bell (three quick "beeps")** → Gathering signal at the end of a drill. Walk in two lines to the Central Lawn.

Please do NOT use these bell patterns for jokes among classmates. Anyone caught imitating drill bells will help clean safety equipment for one week.

### 5. What to Wear

- Students **must wear** full school uniform and flat shoes during the drill week.
- **No high heels, no slippers, no loose scarves, no long decorative strings** — they are dangerous on stairs.
- Bring a **small cotton mask** in your pocket (the school will also hand out masks on Monday for the fire drill).

### 6. First Aid Training (Wednesday)

- Each class will be split into **four small groups**, each led by a nurse or PE teacher.
- Training includes: **bleeding control with a clean bandage**; **how to call 120**; **recovery position** for a fainting classmate.
- Please tell your teacher in advance if you have any medical conditions (asthma, food allergy, heart problem) so that the drill fits you safely.

### 7. Safe Internet Class (Friday)

- A 40-minute class for every grade, plus a free one-hour Q&A after school from 16:15 to 17:15 in Hall B.
- Topics: how to recognize online scam messages; what "cyber-bullying" looks like; why **never** to send your ID photo over chat; how to set a strong password.
- Parents are warmly invited to join the Q&A session.

### 8. Rules & Safety Reminders

- During any drill, **do not run, do not push, do not speak loudly**.
- If you drop a pen, a book or even your glasses, **leave them**. Pick them up only after reaching the lawn.
- Students with **injured legs, asthma or other medical needs** should tell their head teacher **by Friday 19 September**. A separate quiet drill route will be arranged.
- **No one may film classmates** during medical role-plays. Respect each other's privacy.

### 9. Contact

- **Ms Zhao Hongmei**, Safety Office, Ground Floor, Teaching Block A, Room 108.
- Emergency: dial **9** on any school phone; outside campus, dial **119 (fire)**, **120 (medical)**, **110 (police)**.
- Email: `safety2027@lihuaschool.cn`

> **"A drill is a quiet rehearsal for a loud day. We practise now so that no one has to think twice later."**
> — Lihua Middle School Safety Office

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long is the "Safe Campus" Emergency Drill Week?**

A. One day only.  
B. Two days.  
C. Five days (Monday to Friday).  
D. Two weeks.

**2. On which day will students practise first aid skills such as stopping bleeding and helping a fainting classmate?**

A. Monday 22 September.  
B. Tuesday 23 September.  
C. Wednesday 24 September.  
D. Friday 26 September.

**3. What should students do when they hear **one long bell** during drill week?**

A. Stay inside, drop under the desk and cover the head.  
B. Walk in two lines to the Central Lawn.  
C. Leave the classroom for a fire drill, and do NOT go back for school bags.  
D. Lie down and sleep on the floor.

**4. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed during the drill week?**

A. Wearing flat shoes and a full school uniform.  
B. Bringing a small cotton mask in your pocket.  
C. Filming classmates during medical role-plays.  
D. Telling your head teacher about an asthma problem by 19 September.

**5. What is the main message of the drill week, as shown in the final quotation?**

A. Drills are loud, so students can show off.  
B. Drills are pointless as long as students are careful.  
C. Drills are a quiet rehearsal so that, in a real emergency, students do not have to think twice.  
D. Drills are only for the teachers, not for the students.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 条 "Monday 22 September – Friday 26 September 2027"  
【解析】共五天。A、B、D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 3 条 "Wed 24 Sep First Aid Basics"  
【解析】周三为急救基础。A、B、D 均为其他主题。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 4 条 "Long single bell ... Fire drill. Leave the classroom; do NOT go back for school bags"  
【解析】长鸣一声表示火警演练。A 为两声；B 为三声；D 无据。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节判断题（否定型）  
【定位】第 8 条 "No one may film classmates during medical role-plays"  
【解析】禁止在急救角色扮演中录制同学。A、B、D 均被允许或要求。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨/写作意图题  
【定位】第 9 条末 "'A drill is a quiet rehearsal for a loud day. We practise now so that no one has to think twice later.'"  
【解析】演练旨在让真正事发时可立即反应。A、B、D 均不符本意。故选 C。
