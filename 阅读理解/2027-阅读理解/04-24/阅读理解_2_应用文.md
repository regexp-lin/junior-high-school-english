# 阅读理解（应用文） **Email — Student Radio Station "Young Voices" Is Looking for New Hosts**

> **From:** song_ms@sunlightms.edu.cn  
> **To:** all_students@sunlightms.edu.cn  
> **Subject:** Join Us! "Young Voices" Radio Station Is Recruiting — Closing 5 May  
> **Date:** Saturday, 24 April 2027, 9:10 a.m.

---

Dear students,

Our campus radio station, **"Young Voices"**, is looking for **new student hosts, reporters, and editors** for the new term. This is your chance to make your voice known, try new skills, and meet wonderful friends across different grades. Please read this email carefully before sending in your form.

### 1. Positions and Numbers

| Position     | Number | Main Work                                       |
|--------------|--------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Morning Host | 2      | Read news and play music, 7:25–7:45 a.m.         |
| Noon Host    | 2      | Host the "Lunch Talk" show, 12:10–12:30 p.m.     |
| Reporter     | 3      | Write and record short school news reports       |
| Editor       | 2      | Plan topics, check scripts, and arrange music    |

### 2. Who Can Apply

- Students from **Grade 7 and Grade 8** only (Grade 9 students are focusing on the big exam).  
- Grades in Chinese and English must both be **70 or above** in the last term.  
- You must be **ready to work at least 2 afternoons a week** in the radio room.

### 3. How to Apply

1. Download the form from our school website under **"Clubs → Young Voices"**.  
2. Fill in your name, class, the position you want, and a short paragraph (80–120 words) in English telling us **why you should be chosen**.  
3. Record a 60-second audio clip of yourself reading any piece of news in **Chinese or English**.  
4. Send the form and audio file together to **radio@sunlightms.edu.cn** before **Wednesday, 5 May 2027, 18:00**.

### 4. Skills Test (by Invitation)

- A short list of applicants will be invited to a **skills test** on **Saturday, 9 May, 14:00**, in the Multimedia Room (Building C, Floor 3).  
- The test has three small parts: **cold-reading a news sheet**, a **live interview with a teacher**, and **a 3-minute free talk** about one of these topics: *My favourite festival*, *A book that changed me*, or *Why I love our school*.  
- Wear **smart school clothes**. Please bring your student card.

### 5. Training and Rules

- All chosen students will receive **two weeks of free training** (micro-writing, clear pronunciation, studio rules).  
- You must **not** read political jokes or unfinished news on air.  
- If you miss three turns without a good reason, your place will be given to another student.

For any questions, please reply to this email, or knock on my door in **Office 212** between 4:00 p.m. and 5:00 p.m. on weekdays.

Good luck!

**Ms Song Li**  
Teacher in Charge, "Young Voices" Radio Station  
Sunlight Middle School
