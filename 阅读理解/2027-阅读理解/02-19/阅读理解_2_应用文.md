# 阅读理解（应用文） **Charity Walk for Mountain Children — Invitation**

① Dear students and parents, the **Sunshine Charity Foundation** is proud to hold the **9th Charity Walk for Mountain Children** this May. Every step you take will help one student in a faraway mountain school get new books, warm coats and fresh pencils. We warmly invite middle school students and their families to walk with us.

② **Event Information**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date | Sunday, May 2, 2027 |
| Start Time | 8:30 a.m. |
| Start Point | South Gate of Central Park |
| End Point | Riverside Square (about 6 km walk) |
| Open to | Students (Grade 5+) with at least one parent |
| Registration Fee | ¥50 per person (all money goes to mountain schools) |

③ **What You Will Get**

- A bright yellow T-shirt with the event logo (please wear it during the walk).  
- A small cotton bag, a water bottle and a healthy snack box.  
- A paper certificate and **10 community service hours** for every student who finishes the whole 6 km.  
- One letter from a mountain child, written in simple Chinese or drawing.

④ **How to Sign Up**

- **Online:** Open the QR code on the back of this notice and fill in the form before **Wednesday, April 21**.  
- **At school:** Hand in a paper form to Ms. Xu (Room 108) during lunch break.  
- Each family may register up to **4 people**. If a student is younger than Grade 5, please wait for next year.

⑤ **Important Rules**

- All walkers must finish a **short warm-up** at the Start Point before 8:25 a.m.  
- Please **do not** run or push on the road. The walk is a slow, happy movement.  
- Pets are **not** allowed, as the walk passes through several busy streets.  
- Each student must walk with a parent or a registered adult at all times.  
- Please throw rubbish into the yellow bags provided; do not leave anything on the road.

⑥ **Surprise Performance**

At the end point, at about 11:30 a.m., a short concert will be held. Children from a mountain school will sing two of their favourite songs by video call, and students from our city middle schools will reply with a song in English. Families are welcome to stay, clap and enjoy lunch in the square.

⑦ **If It Rains**

If there is light rain, the event goes on — please bring a raincoat (no umbrellas on the road). If there is heavy rain or thunder, the event will move to **Sunday, May 16**. A short message will be sent to all families by Saturday evening.

⑧ For more help, please email **walk@sunshinefoundation.org** or call **4008-123-567** on working days. Together, our small steps can climb a high mountain.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "about 6 km walk"  
【解析】路程约 6 公里。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段礼品列表  
【解析】T 恤、包、水瓶和零食盒。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑥段 "children from a mountain school will sing two of their favourite songs by video call"  
【解析】视频连线合唱。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤⑦段 "no umbrellas on the road"  
【解析】路上不许打伞。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑦段 "will move to Sunday, May 16"  
【解析】顺延至 5 月 16 日。故选 B。
