# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Spring Marathon 2027 — Student Volunteer Guide**

> **CITY SPORTS OFFICE**  
> **SPRING MARATHON 2027 — STUDENT VOLUNTEER GUIDE**  
> *Sunday, 7 June 2027 · Start: Central Square · Finish: Riverside Park*

---

Dear student volunteers,

Thank you for signing up for the **Spring Marathon 2027** volunteer team. Your help makes the race warmer and safer for more than 8,000 runners. This guide tells you where to go, what to do, and how to stay safe on the day.

### Arrival and Check-In

- **Date:** Sunday, 7 June 2027  
- **Check-in time:** 05:30–06:00 (race starts at 07:00)  
- **Check-in place:** Volunteer Tent, North Gate of Central Square  
- Please bring your **ID card** and **Volunteer QR code** (sent to your email on 5 June).  
- You will receive your **blue volunteer vest**, a hat, gloves and a simple breakfast box.

### Volunteer Roles

| Role Code | Main Duty | Time |
|-----------|-----------|------|
| **A-1 Water Station** | Hand out water cups at Water Point 3 (km 10) | 06:45–10:30 |
| **A-2 Banana Station** | Hand out fruit and energy bars at Point 5 (km 20) | 07:30–11:00 |
| **B-1 Route Guard** | Stand at corners and guide runners with arrows | 06:15–10:30 |
| **B-2 First-Aid Helper** | Work with medical team; hand out ice bags | 06:30–11:30 |
| **C-1 Finish Line** | Give medals, cold towels and a smile | 07:30–12:30 |
| **D-1 Language Helper** | Help foreign runners with directions (English or Japanese) | 06:00–11:00 |

### What You Must Do and Must NOT Do

**Must do:**
- Arrive **on time**. Late arrival means losing your place on the team.  
- Keep your volunteer vest on during your whole shift.  
- Follow the orders of the **Station Captain** (a staff with a yellow armband).  
- Keep the water and food areas **clean**; put cups in the yellow bag.

**Must NOT do:**
- Do NOT run on the race route with the runners.  
- Do NOT take professional photos or selfies with runners during their running; photos at the finish area are allowed.  
- Do NOT give any drug or medical treatment yourself — call a first-aid helper instead.  
- Do NOT talk loudly into mobile phones near the race route.

### Safety Rules

1. If a runner falls or looks ill, **call the red-cross team** on channel 6 of the walkie-talkie, then stay with the runner until help arrives.  
2. Drink water yourself every 30 minutes, and sit in the shade during your break.  
3. Report any damaged barrier or lost child to the Station Captain at once.  
4. If the weather changes suddenly (heavy rain, strong wind), wait for the Captain's signal before you move.

### Reward for Volunteers

- Each student volunteer will receive a **Certificate of Volunteer Service**, a **sports towel**, and **30 community service hours**.  
- The "Best Student Volunteer" (chosen by Station Captains) will win a free one-day ticket to the City Science Museum.

For any question, please email **marathon.volunteer@citysport.cn** or call **020-8899-1100** (Mon–Fri, 09:00–17:00).

**Run kindness, not only distance. See you on 7 June.**

— City Sports Office, Spring Marathon Committee

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Arrival and Check-In "Check-in time: 05:30–06:00"  
【解析】签到时间 05:30–06:00。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Volunteer Roles 表 A-1 "Water Point 3 (km 10)"  
【解析】A-1 在第 3 水点约 10 公里处。故选 A。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Must NOT do 第1条 "Do NOT run on the race route with the runners."  
【解析】志愿者不得与选手一同跑。A/B/D 均是应做之事。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Safety Rules 第1条 "call the red-cross team on channel 6... stay with the runner..."  
【解析】呼叫红十字队并陪在选手身边。A/C/D 均错或不安全。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Reward "a free one-day ticket to the City Science Museum"  
【解析】最佳志愿者获科学博物馆一日票。A/C/D 均无据。故选 B。
