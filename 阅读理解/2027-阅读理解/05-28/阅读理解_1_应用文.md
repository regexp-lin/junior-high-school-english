# 阅读理解（应用文） **Happy Neighbours — Senior Home Visit Programme (Volunteer Sign-Up)**

**HAPPY NEIGHBOURS · SENIOR HOME VISIT PROGRAMME**
*Bringing a cup of tea and a young smile to the elderly in our community.*

---

**What is it?**
Every weekend, small teams of middle school volunteers visit the homes of elderly neighbours who live alone. A typical visit lasts **50–60 minutes**. During the visit, volunteers will:
- chat with grandparents about the week;
- read a short newspaper article or a poem aloud;
- help with one **simple task**, such as watering plants, tidying a table or checking a smartphone problem.

**Please note: volunteers do NOT cook, take medicine out, or move heavy furniture.** Health and safety come first.

**Who can join?**

| Group | Age / Grade |
| --- | --- |
| Junior volunteers | **Grade 7 – Grade 9** |
| Family pairs | One student + **one parent** (recommended for first-time volunteers) |

You must have a valid student ID card and parents' written permission.

**Schedule — June to August 2027**

- **Saturday visits**: 10:00 – 11:00  
- **Sunday visits**: 15:00 – 16:00  
- Each volunteer is asked to join **at least 2 visits per month**, so that we become familiar faces to our senior friends.

**How to sign up**

1. Fill in the **online form** at *happyneighbours.cn/2027* before **Thursday 4 June**.
2. Attend a **short training session** (only 90 minutes) on **Friday 5 June, 18:30**, City Youth Centre, Hall 2.
3. After the training, you will receive a team and your first visit date by text message.

**What to bring to a visit**

- Your yellow volunteer badge (we give it to you after training).
- A clean, quiet voice — remember that many grandparents hear less well.
- One small, **homemade** gift if you like: a drawing, a paper flower, or a short handwritten note. **Please do NOT bring expensive gifts.**

**Important rules**

1. Always visit **in pairs**. Never enter a senior neighbour's home alone.
2. Stay in the living room; do not walk into bedrooms or kitchens without being invited.
3. If an elderly person shows any sign of illness (sudden pain, strong tiredness, or trouble breathing), call **120** and inform the programme leader right away.
4. After each visit, send a short report (50 words is enough) in the volunteer group chat within the same evening.

**Contact**

- Programme leader: **Ms. Zhao Yi**, 138-xxxx-5630  
- Office: **Room 214, City Youth Centre**, open Mon.–Fri., 09:00 – 18:00.

*Small visits, warm lives. We hope to see you soon.*

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long does a typical visit last?**

A. 10 – 20 minutes.  
B. 30 minutes.  
C. 50 – 60 minutes.  
D. The whole afternoon.

**2. Which of the following tasks are volunteers NOT allowed to do?**

A. Chat with grandparents about the week.  
B. Read a short newspaper article aloud.  
C. Cook, take medicine out, or move heavy furniture.  
D. Water plants.

**3. When should volunteers finish the online sign-up form?**

A. Before Thursday 4 June.  
B. Before Friday 5 June.  
C. Before Sunday 7 June.  
D. Any time in August.

**4. Which gift does the programme suggest volunteers bring?**

A. An expensive box of imported candy.  
B. A small homemade gift such as a drawing, paper flower, or handwritten note.  
C. A new smartphone.  
D. A large plant pot.

**5. What should volunteers do if an elderly person suddenly has strong pain or trouble breathing?**

A. Leave the house quietly and not tell anyone.  
B. Call 120 and inform the programme leader right away.  
C. Wait until the next visit.  
D. Ask a neighbour to take a photo.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"A typical visit lasts 50–60 minutes."  
【解析】时长 50–60 分钟。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Please note: volunteers do NOT cook, take medicine out, or move heavy furniture."  
【解析】禁做烹饪、拿药、搬重家具。A/B/D 均允许。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Fill in the online form... before Thursday 4 June."  
【解析】线上报名截止 6 月 4 日。B 为培训日；其他均无据。故选 A。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"One small, homemade gift..."  
【解析】建议自制小礼物，不带贵重物品。A/C/D 均不符。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"call 120 and inform the programme leader right away."  
【解析】紧急情况拨 120 并告知负责人。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
