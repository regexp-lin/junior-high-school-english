# 阅读理解（议论文） **Should Middle Schools Start Classes Later in the Morning?**

① In many Chinese cities, the first class in a middle school begins at 07:30 or even 07:20. Students wake up at 06:10, wash quickly, push a cold bun into their mouths on the bus, and arrive at the classroom with sleepy eyes. In recent years, more and more doctors and parents have been asking: **should middle schools let classes start later, say at 08:15 or 08:30?** I believe they should.

② First, science clearly shows that teenagers' bodies need **more sleep** than children's bodies. Doctors recommend 8–10 hours of sleep every night for students aged 12–15. If homework ends at 22:00 and the alarm rings at 06:10, most middle school students sleep only seven hours. Too little sleep hurts memory, slows thinking and weakens the immune system. No amount of hard work can fix a tired brain.

③ Second, a slightly later start helps **safety** on the roads. At 07:00, the sky in winter is still dark, and parents who drive their children to school are often tired themselves. Small drowsy drivers, big sleepy buses and cold fog create a dangerous mix. Pushing the start time back by only 45 minutes would move many school journeys into clearer, lighter, safer morning hours.

④ Third, a later start allows time for a **real breakfast**. A piece of bread eaten on the way is not breakfast — it is an emergency. When students can sit down for ten quiet minutes at home with a bowl of porridge, a boiled egg and some fresh fruit, they enter school with fuel, not with hunger. Good breakfast is linked with better attention in the first two classes, which happen to be the most important ones of the day.

⑤ Some teachers worry that if school starts later, it will also end later, and students will have less time for homework, sports or family. This is a fair concern. However, research from several Beijing and Shanghai pilot schools shows a surprising result: when the first class is moved from 07:30 to 08:15 and the last class is pushed only 20 minutes later (the rest of the time is saved by shortening the morning break), students finish homework **faster** in the evening because they concentrate better. Thus the total "free" time is almost the same.

⑥ A small change of 45 minutes in the morning can be a large gift for a young life. More sleep, safer streets, better breakfasts, clearer minds — all without new buildings or expensive equipment. I hope more schools will try this simple change, and I hope one day, waking up at 06:10 will be only a story that today's middle school students tell their grandchildren.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main opinion of the writer?**

A. Middle schools should start classes at 06:30 instead.  
B. Middle schools should let classes start later, for example at 08:15 or 08:30.  
C. Students should not sleep on school nights.  
D. Students should stop eating breakfast.

**2. How many hours of sleep do doctors recommend for students aged 12–15?**

A. 5–6 hours.  
B. 6–7 hours.  
C. 7–8 hours.  
D. 8–10 hours.

**3. According to paragraph ③, why does a later start help road safety?**

A. Because buses will be cleaner.  
B. Because schools will have fewer buses.  
C. Because many school journeys will move into clearer, lighter, safer morning hours.  
D. Because drivers will all be replaced by robots.

**4. What does the writer say about breakfast?**

A. Breakfast is unnecessary for teenagers.  
B. A piece of bread on the way is not breakfast; a sit-down breakfast helps attention in the first two classes.  
C. Breakfast should only include meat.  
D. Breakfast should be skipped on exam days.

**5. How does the writer answer teachers who worry about less time for homework?**

A. Students will just sleep less at night.  
B. Pilot schools show that with better concentration, students finish homework faster; total free time is almost the same.  
C. Schools must cancel all sports activities.  
D. Parents must hire tutors for every child.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段末及全文  
【解析】作者主张推迟上课。A/C/D 均与观点相反。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "Doctors recommend 8–10 hours of sleep every night for students aged 12–15."  
【解析】8–10 小时。A/B/C 均错。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "move many school journeys into clearer, lighter, safer morning hours."  
【解析】更亮更安全。A/B/D 均无据。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段  
【解析】匆忙的面包不是早餐，好早餐提高前两节课的专注力。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "students finish homework faster in the evening because they concentrate better."  
【解析】专注度提升，作业更快完成。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
