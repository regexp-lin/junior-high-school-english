# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Junior Museum Week 2027 — Visitor Guide**

---

## **Step Inside History, Find Yourself There**

Dear Grade 7–9 students,

For one special week in September, our city opens the doors of four city museums to middle-school students with the **Lihua Junior Museum Week Pass**. Bring your curiosity, your camera and this small guide — and let's make museums feel less like old buildings and more like good friends.

- **Dates:** **Monday Sep 13 – Sunday Sep 19, 2027**  
- **Pass:** Free for Lihua students with a student card + the green Junior Week sticker  
- **Contact:** Ms. Sun, Room 304, Teaching Block A, 8822-5566

---

## **The Four Museums**

| No. | Museum | Open Hours | Highlight This Year | Age Note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **City History Museum** | 9:00–17:00 (Mon–Sun) | New hall: "Our Streets in 1957" | All ages |
| 2 | **Science and Nature Museum** | 9:30–17:30 (Tue–Sun) | Giant **panda** photo wall + hands-on lab | Grade 7+ |
| 3 | **Ancient Art Museum** | 10:00–17:00 (Wed–Sun) | Live **calligraphy** show, 14:00 daily | All ages |
| 4 | **City Railway Museum** | 9:00–16:30 (Thu–Sun) | Climb inside an 80-year-old steam train | All ages |

> **Closing day:** The Science Museum does not open on Monday. Please plan carefully.

---

## **Special Student Activities (Free, sign-up only)**

1. **"My Favourite Object" Mini-Lecture** — Each day, 10:30 at Museum 1. A young museum guide talks for 15 minutes about one old street object (an old iron, a paper ticket, a wooden lunch box...). Sit or stand; take notes.  
2. **Family Science Saturday** — Sep 18, 14:00–16:00, Museum 2. Parents welcome. Sign up at `museum.city-edu.cn/junior` by **Sep 11**. First **60 families** only.  
3. **Children's Calligraphy Try-Out** — Sep 14 & 17, 15:00, Museum 3. Real brushes, real ink, real paper. Bring an apron.  
4. **Night at the Railway Museum** — Sep 16, 18:30–20:30, Museum 4. Torch tour, old-train stories. Grade 8+ only. Sign up at the school library desk by **Sep 10**.  
5. **Junior Museum Notebook** — You can collect **one coloured stamp** at each museum you visit. Bring your notebook to school on Sep 22; top 20 complete notebooks get a **Lihua Museum Friend Card** (free museum entry for one year).

---

## **Rules for Visitors**

- No food or drinks (except bottled water) inside the halls.  
- Flash photography is **not** allowed in any museum. You may take photos without flash in open areas.  
- Please stay **60 cm** away from the objects; do not touch unless the sign says "Please touch".  
- Loud voices and running are not allowed inside. Phone calls — please step outside.  
- Students under 13 may enter the Science and Nature Museum only with a parent or teacher.

---

## **Useful Notes**

- Every museum has a **quiet reading room** on the top floor — a nice place to write your notebook notes.  
- If you lose your student card during the visit, please ask the staff at the information desk; we will call your class teacher.  
- For any question about the Junior Week, email `junior@lihua-ms.cn` or talk to Ms. Sun.

> *"Every old object in a quiet room still has something to say. Come and listen."*

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. On which day is the Science and Nature Museum **closed**?**

A. Monday.  
B. Tuesday.  
C. Saturday.  
D. Sunday.

**2. Little Li is only 12 years old. Which rule applies to his visit to the Science and Nature Museum?**

A. He cannot enter at all.  
B. He can enter only if a parent or teacher comes with him.  
C. He must stay in the quiet reading room the whole time.  
D. He has to pay an extra fee of 20 yuan.

**3. If you want to attend "Family Science Saturday", by what date must you sign up online?**

A. Sep 10.  
B. Sep 11.  
C. Sep 18.  
D. Sep 22.

**4. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed inside the museum halls?**

A. Drinking bottled water.  
B. Taking photos without flash in open areas.  
C. Taking photos with a bright flash.  
D. Quietly walking around.

**5. What will happen if you bring a complete Junior Museum Notebook (with stamps from all four museums) to school on Sep 22?**

A. You will be given free museum entry for one year if you are in the top 20.  
B. You will have to give the notebook to the teacher forever.  
C. You will receive a fine of 50 yuan.  
D. You will be asked to leave the school.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】The Four Museums 表格第 2 行 "Tue–Sun" & Closing day 提示 "The Science Museum does not open on Monday"  
【解析】表格及小字均指出周一不开放。B、C、D 均在开放时间内。故选 A。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules for Visitors 末条 "Students under 13 may enter the Science and Nature Museum only with a parent or teacher"  
【解析】12 岁 <13 岁，需家长或老师陪同。A 过度禁止；C、D 属无中生有。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Special Student Activities 第 2 条 "Sign up at ... by Sep 11"  
【解析】在线报名截止 9/11。A 为活动 4 的截止日；C 为活动日；D 为交本子日。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】Rules for Visitors 第 2 条 "Flash photography is not allowed"  
【解析】馆内禁止闪光拍照。A、B、D 均为允许行为。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】Special Student Activities 第 5 条 "top 20 complete notebooks get a Lihua Museum Friend Card (free museum entry for one year)"  
【解析】完成 notebook 且位列前 20 即可获得一年免费参观卡。B、C、D 均与原文冲突。故选 A。
