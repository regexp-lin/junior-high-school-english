# 阅读理解（应用文） **City History Museum — Student Visit Guide**

① Welcome to the City History Museum! We have four main halls on three floors. The table below shows the topics and opening times so that students can plan a short but useful visit.

② **Hall Guide**

| Floor | Hall | Topic | Opening Hours |
|-------|------|-------|---------------|
| 1F | Ancient Life Hall | Tools, pots and clothes from 2,000 years ago | 9:00 – 17:00 |
| 1F | River & Bridge Hall | Boats, old bridges and the story of our river | 9:00 – 17:00 |
| 2F | Silk Road Hall | Trade, maps and stories between East and West | 9:30 – 16:30 |
| 3F | Modern City Hall | Railways, schools and city changes in the last 100 years | 10:00 – 16:00 |

③ **Suggested Route for Students**

- **Step 1 (30 min):** Start on 1F with the *Ancient Life Hall*. See how people cooked and dressed long ago.  
- **Step 2 (20 min):** Move next door to the *River & Bridge Hall*. Try the "Build a Wooden Bridge" mini game.  
- **Step 3 (30 min):** Go up to 2F and explore the *Silk Road Hall*. Don't miss the giant map with touch screens.  
- **Step 4 (40 min):** Finish at the *Modern City Hall* on 3F to see how our city has grown.

④ **Useful Information**

- Tickets: **Free** for students with a valid school card.  
- **No food or drinks** are allowed inside the halls.  
- Taking photos is OK, but please **turn off the flash**.  
- Group visits (10+ students) should book by phone at **010-5588-1234** at least one day in advance.  
- The museum is **closed every Monday** for cleaning and repair.

⑤ If you have questions during your visit, please look for staff wearing yellow name cards near each hall door. We hope you enjoy our city's long and exciting story!

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How many main halls are there in the museum?**

A. Two.  
B. Three.  
C. Four.  
D. Five.

**2. Which hall is on the third floor?**

A. Ancient Life Hall.  
B. River & Bridge Hall.  
C. Silk Road Hall.  
D. Modern City Hall.

**3. According to the suggested route, what will students do at Step 2?**

A. Read books about old trains.  
B. Play the "Build a Wooden Bridge" mini game.  
C. Watch a short film about the Silk Road.  
D. Paint a picture of the ancient river.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed in the halls?**

A. Taking photos without flash.  
B. Reading a printed map.  
C. Eating snacks quietly.  
D. Speaking in a low voice.

**5. What can be inferred from the guide?**

A. The museum is open every day of the year.  
B. Students need to pay a high ticket price.  
C. A large school group should call before coming.  
D. Flash photos are welcome to make the photos brighter.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "We have four main halls on three floors."  
【解析】共 4 个主展厅。其他选项与数字不符。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "3F | Modern City Hall"。  
【解析】3 楼是现代城市厅。A、B 在 1F；C 在 2F。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 Step 2 "Move next door to the River & Bridge Hall. Try the 'Build a Wooden Bridge' mini game."  
【解析】第二步为在河桥厅体验搭木桥小游戏。A、C、D 与 Step 2 内容不符。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "No food or drinks are allowed inside the halls."  
【解析】展厅内禁止饮食。A 允许无闪光拍照；B、D 未被禁止。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第④段 "Group visits (10+ students) should book by phone... at least one day in advance."  
【解析】大型团体需提前电话预约。A 与"周一闭馆"矛盾；B 学生免费；D 要求关闭闪光。故选 C。
