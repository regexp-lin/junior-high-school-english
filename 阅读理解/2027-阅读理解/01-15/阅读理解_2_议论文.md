# 阅读理解（议论文） **Are Smart Watches a Good Choice for Middle-School Students?**

① These days, more and more middle-school students are wearing smart watches. Some parents are happy to buy them, because the watches can call home, count steps and even help children find their way. Others worry that smart watches may cause new problems. So, are smart watches really a good choice for students?

② Smart watches do bring clear advantages. First, safety is a big reason. Parents can see where their children are, which is helpful when students walk home or take the bus alone. Second, the watch can remind students of tests, after-school clubs or family dinners, so that they do not forget important things. Third, a health app can check heart rate and walking steps. Students who sit at a desk all day will be pushed to stand up and move more often.

③ However, smart watches can also be used in the wrong way. Some students play small games or send messages during class. This makes it hard for them to focus on the teacher. A few watches can even take short videos, which may create problems for classmates' privacy. Besides, students often compare the price and the brand of their watches. A simple tool becomes a reason for showing off.

④ In my view, smart watches are tools, not toys. Students can use them, but there must be clear rules. Parents should buy watches with necessary functions such as calls and steps, not watches full of games. Schools can ask students to keep their watches silent during class, or to place them in a box at the classroom door. In this way, a smart watch stays smart, and students will not be controlled by it.

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the writer's main question in the passage?**

A. Which brand of smart watch is the cheapest?  
B. Whether smart watches are a good choice for middle-school students.  
C. How many hours a day students should play games.  
D. Why adults also need smart watches.

**2. According to Paragraph ②, a smart watch can help students ______.**

A. do all their homework automatically  
B. remember tests, clubs and family dinners  
C. cook a simple meal at home  
D. teach English songs to younger students

**3. Which of the following is a problem mentioned in Paragraph ③?**

A. Watches are too heavy for young wrists.  
B. Watches can make students cold in winter.  
C. Students may play games or send messages in class.  
D. Watches sometimes fly away in strong wind.

**4. What does the writer suggest schools do?**

A. Buy a smart watch for every student.  
B. Ban all kinds of watches on campus.

D. Give a prize to the most expensive watch.

**5. The main idea of the last paragraph is that ______.**

A. smart watches are more important than lessons

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第①段末句 "So, are smart watches really a good choice for students?"  
【解析】作者主要探讨智能手表是否适合中学生。A、C、D 偏离主题。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "the watch can remind students of tests, after-school clubs or family dinners."  
【解析】手表可提醒学生考试、社团和家宴。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "Some students play small games or send messages during class."  
【解析】上课玩游戏或发消息是文中提到的问题。A、B、D 均未提及。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段 "Schools can ask students to keep their watches silent during class, or to place them in a box at the classroom door."  
【解析】学校应要求手表静音或放入教室门口的盒中。A、B、D 均不是作者建议。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第④段 "smart watches are tools, not toys... there must be clear rules."  
【解析】作者强调手表是工具，需有明确规则。A 与"工具而非玩具"矛盾；B 并未完全禁止；D 价格贵并非作者立场。故选 C。
