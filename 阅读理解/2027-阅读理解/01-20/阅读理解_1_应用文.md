# 阅读理解（应用文） **Spring Classes at the Youth Activity Centre**

① Welcome to Riverside Youth Activity Centre! Our **Spring Term** opens on **March 2** and lasts for ten weeks. The following classes are open to students from Grade 6 to Grade 9.

② **Class List**

| Class | Day | Time | Teacher | Fee (10 weeks) |
|-------|-----|------|---------|----------------|
| Chinese Paper Cutting | Tuesday | 18:30 – 20:00 | Miss Liu | 200 yuan |
| Stage Drama (English) | Wednesday | 18:30 – 20:00 | Mr. Brown | 280 yuan |
| Robotics Beginner | Friday | 19:00 – 20:30 | Mr. Zhao | 350 yuan |
| Table Tennis Team | Saturday | 9:00 – 11:00 | Coach Lin | 180 yuan |
| Nature Photography | Sunday | 9:30 – 11:30 | Miss Chen | 240 yuan |

③ **Important Notes**

- Each student may sign up for **up to two classes** this term.  
- All class fees include **materials** such as paper, glue or robot parts. For Nature Photography, students need to bring their own phone or simple camera.  
- Students who miss four or more classes will not receive the end-of-term certificate.  
- **Family discount**: Brothers and sisters from the same family get a **10% discount** on the second class.

④ **How to Sign Up**

1. Fill in the **Spring Class Form** (green paper) at the front desk before **February 22**.  
2. Pay the fee in cash or by mobile phone at the same desk.  
3. Check your name on the class list at the notice board on **February 25**.  
4. If a class has fewer than eight students by February 22, the class will be changed to another time.

⑤ **Open House Day** — On **Saturday, February 15**, all classes offer **free trial lessons** from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Parents are welcome to visit with their children. For more help, call our service line at **010-8866-4321** or visit our website: **www.youthcentre-rs.com**.

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When does the Spring Term begin?**

A. February 15.  
B. February 22.  
C. March 2.  
D. March 10.

**2. Which class is held on Saturday morning?**

A. Chinese Paper Cutting.  
B. Stage Drama (English).  
C. Robotics Beginner.  
D. Table Tennis Team.

**3. According to the notes, what is TRUE about the fees?**

A. Students must pay extra for paper and glue.  
B. The fees include most materials for each class.  
C. Only cash is accepted at the front desk.  
D. Every student must pay the same fee for all classes.

**4. What will happen if a class has only five students by February 22?**

A. The fee will be doubled.  
B. The class will be changed to another time.  
C. The class will start without any teacher.  
D. All students will get their money back and leave the centre.

**5. On February 15, what can parents and students do?**

A. Attend free trial lessons.  
B. Watch a city football match.  
C. Buy used robot parts at a low price.  
D. Take photos of famous stars.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "Our Spring Term opens on March 2."  
【解析】春季学期 3 月 2 日开学。A、B、D 为其他日期。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Table Tennis Team | Saturday | 9:00 – 11:00"。  
【解析】乒乓球队周六上午开课。A 周二、B 周三、C 周五。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段 "All class fees include materials such as paper, glue or robot parts."  
【解析】学费已含大部分材料。A 与原文相反；C 还可用手机支付；D 费用各班不同。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段第 4 条 "If a class has fewer than eight students by February 22, the class will be changed to another time."  
【解析】人数不足 8 人将改期。A、C、D 无依据。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "On Saturday, February 15, all classes offer free trial lessons."  
【解析】当天可免费试听。B、C、D 均无依据。故选 A。
