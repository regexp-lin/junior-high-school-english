# 阅读理解（应用文） **City Youth Library — Summer Reading Passport**

**CITY YOUTH LIBRARY**
*Read together. Grow together.*

---

**Summer Reading Passport 2027 — Information for Middle School Students**

Every summer, our library gives free paper "Reading Passports" to young readers. Students use the passport to write down the books they have read, collect stamps, and get small rewards at the end of the holiday. This year's theme is "**Stories Make Minds**".

**Who can get a passport?**

- Middle school students aged 12 – 16.
- You must already be a member of our library; joining is free for school students with a valid student card.

**When and where to pick up your passport**

- **From Wednesday 1 July 2027.**
- **Children's Desk**, second floor of the main library.
- Please bring your student card. One student may pick up only one passport.

**How the passport works**

1. For each book you finish, ask any librarian at the Children's Desk to stamp one square in your passport.
2. The librarian may ask you **two simple questions** about the book — for example, "Who is the main character?" or "What did you learn?"
3. Each book can be recorded only once, even if you read it twice.
4. Comics and magazines do not count this summer. Picture books count if they have more than 300 words.
5. You may record up to **three books a day**, no more.

**Rewards**

| Stamps | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 5 | A small bookmark with a hand-painted drawing |
| 10 | A cotton reading bag from the library |
| 15 | A free ticket for the "Writer Meets Reader" event on 26 August |
| 20 | A special "Top Summer Reader" paper certificate, signed by the chief librarian |
| 25 or more | A private 15-minute book-list talk with a children's author |

**"Writer Meets Reader" event**

- **Date:** Thursday 26 August 2027, 14:30 – 16:30.
- **Place:** Meeting Hall, 3rd floor.
- **Guests:** author **Han Yi** and illustrator **Wu Lei**.
- **Free**, but you must have at least 15 stamps on your passport.

**Rules and kind advice**

- Please do not read more than three hours without a break; short walks and water are good for your eyes.
- The library is not responsible for lost passports. If you lose yours, we cannot write the old stamps back.
- Parents may sit next to their child in the reading area, but they should not tell the child the answer to the librarian's questions.
- Eating inside the library is not allowed. Water in a closed bottle is fine.

**Closing of the programme**

- The programme ends on **Sunday 29 August 2027, 20:00**.
- All rewards must be collected before **Saturday 4 September**. After that, we keep them for next year.

**Contact**
City Youth Library · 0572-xxxx-2310 · passport@cyl.cn
*See you among the shelves.*

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When can a student pick up the Summer Reading Passport?**

A. Tuesday 30 June.  
B. From Wednesday 1 July 2027.  
C. From Saturday 29 August.  
D. Only on the last day of summer.

**2. Which of the following books will count towards the stamps?**

A. A comic read in one hour.  
B. A weekly magazine from last month.  
C. A picture book with more than 300 words.  
D. A TV programme watched online.

**3. What is NOT a rule of the programme?**

A. You may record up to three books a day.  
B. The librarian will ask two simple questions about the book before giving a stamp.  
C. You can get a stamp for the same book twice.  
D. Each student can pick up only one passport.

**4. What reward does a student with 20 stamps receive?**

A. A bookmark.  
B. A cotton reading bag.  
C. A special "Top Summer Reader" paper certificate signed by the chief librarian.  
D. A private 15-minute talk with a children's author.

**5. What must a student have to attend the "Writer Meets Reader" event?**

A. A parent's letter.  
B. At least 15 stamps on the passport.  
C. Payment of 50 yuan.  
D. An official invitation letter.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"From Wednesday 1 July 2027."  
【解析】7 月 1 日起领取。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Picture books count if they have more than 300 words."  
【解析】超 300 词绘本计入。A/B/D 均不算。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题（反向）  
【定位】"Each book can be recorded only once"  
【解析】同一本书不可计两次。A/B/D 均为正确规则。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】奖励表 "20 / Top Summer Reader..."  
【解析】20 章奖总馆长签名证书。A/B/D 均错。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Free, but you must have at least 15 stamps"  
【解析】至少 15 枚章。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。
