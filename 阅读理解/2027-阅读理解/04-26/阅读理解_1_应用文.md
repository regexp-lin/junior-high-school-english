# 阅读理解（应用文） **Summer Nature Camp — "Forest Friends" 5-Day Programme (Brochure)**

> **Green Pine Nature School**  
> **Summer Programme 2027 – "Forest Friends"**  
> *"Five days, a hundred questions, one forest."*

---

### About the Camp

"Forest Friends" is a **5-day summer nature camp** for middle school students who love animals, plants, and a little bit of mud. The camp is held deep in the **Green Pine National Park**, 80 km north of our city. Each group has **12 students** and **2 guide teachers**. No phones are used during the programme (emergency phone with teachers only).

- **Dates:** Monday 19 July – Friday 23 July 2027  
- **For:** Students aged **12–15**  
- **Cost:** 1,200 yuan (includes beds, meals, all activities, and a small backpack kit)  
- **Group size:** Maximum 48 students in total

---

### What We Will Do

| Day | Morning | Afternoon | Evening |
|-----|---------|-----------|---------|
| Mon | Meet your team & safety training | Forest walk – recognising 15 plants | Tent assembly practice |
| Tue | Birdwatching at Sunrise Hill (5:30 start) | Drawing nature journals | Night sounds listening game |
| Wed | River study: water quality & small fish | Tree climbing (with ropes) | Story night with Ranger Wang |
| Thu | All-day mountain walk (10 km) | (see "All-day") | Stargazing on Meadow Square |
| Fri | Group project presentations | Camp clean-up & closing ceremony | Return to city, 6 p.m. |

---

### What to Bring

1. Sleeping bag and small pillow  
2. Warm jacket, sun hat, rain coat  
3. **Long trousers** (not shorts) for forest walks  
4. Strong walking shoes, two pairs of socks  
5. Water bottle, small notebook, pencil  
6. Personal medicine (give it to the nurse on arrival)  
7. One book of your choice for the Story Night  

*Please do NOT bring: video games, large snacks, open fire items, or any knives longer than 8 cm.*

---

### Safety Rules

- Always walk in a group; never leave your team.  
- Listen to the guide teacher and Ranger Wang.  
- Wild berries, mushrooms, and animals must **not be touched or eaten**.  
- In a thunderstorm, move away from tall trees and gather at the Main Cabin.  
- A school nurse, **Mr Gao**, stays at the camp 24 hours.

---

### How to Sign Up

1. Parents please fill in the **"Parent Agreement" form** (two pages).  
2. Students fill in the **"Health Statement"** on page 3.  
3. A **non-swimming note from the doctor** is needed if your child cannot swim, even for river study.  
4. Send all forms to **camp@greenpine.edu.cn** or hand them to Ms Han in Room 105 **before Saturday, 5 June 2027**.  
5. Fees should be paid by WeChat or at the school bank counter **within 5 days** after accepting your place.

Places are limited to **48 students**. Early birds will get the Sunrise Hill team.  
For more information, call **0591-8822-3344** or visit www.greenpine.edu.cn/camp.
