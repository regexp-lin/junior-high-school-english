# 阅读理解（应用文） **Spring Charity Run 5K — "Steps for the Children's Hospital"**

---

**Blue Star Middle School × City Children's Hospital**  
*Every step you take buys a book, a warm blanket, or a smile for a hospital child.*

---

**1. The Event**

A **5-kilometre charity run** along the Green River Ring Road. All fees will be donated to the **City Children's Hospital**, to support small patients with long-stay illnesses. Last year the event raised **68,000 yuan** and brought books, toys, and new pillows to over **200 children**. This year, our goal is **100,000 yuan**.

---

**2. Date & Place**

- **Date:** Sunday, 25 April 2027  
- **Start:** 7:30 a.m. at the North Gate of Green River Park  
- **Finish:** Children's Hospital front square, 3 Flower Street  
- **Weather plan:** If heavy rain, the run moves to Saturday, 2 May.

---

**3. Who Can Join?**

- **Family Group:** at least one parent and one child aged 6+  
- **Student Group:** Grade 7–9 students, in pairs  
- **Teacher Group:** teachers, alone or in small teams  
- **Cheer Squad:** younger children (under 6) and grandparents — they walk, clap, and hand out water  
- *No runners under 6 years old may run the full 5 km.*

---

**4. Fee & What You Get**

| Item | Price |
|------|-------|
| Early sign-up (before 15 April) | **60 yuan / person** |
| Normal sign-up (15–22 April) | 80 yuan / person |
| Family pack (2 adults + 1 child) | 150 yuan |

Every runner gets:

- A **blue T-shirt** with our event logo  
- A cloth bag with water and two energy bars  
- A small **thank-you letter** from a hospital child (after the run)

---

**5. Run-Day Timeline**

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 6:45 a.m. | Gather at North Gate, pick up bib numbers |
| 7:00 a.m. | Warm-up dance led by PE teachers |
| 7:30 a.m. | Start horn — run begins |
| 9:00 a.m. | Finish line at the Hospital Square |
| 9:15 a.m. | Short ceremony: donation cheque handed to the hospital director |
| 9:30 a.m. | Family snack time & photo wall |

---

**6. Rules & Reminders**

1. **Safety first** — no earphones while running; keep the path clear for others.  
2. Do not wear new shoes; **broken-in trainers** are best.  
3. If you feel dizzy, please walk to the nearest water point (every 1 km).  
4. All runners must **bring their ID card or student card** to the event.  
5. Reusable water bottles are highly welcome. **No plastic cups** will be given out.

---

> To sign up, scan the QR code on the school notice board or visit **run.bsms.edu.cn**. Contact: Coach Yang, 138-0055-6677.

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Where will all the money raised go?**

A. To the Blue Star Middle School library.  
B. To a city marathon club.  
C. To the City Children's Hospital.  
D. To the PE teachers' salaries.

**2. Who is NOT allowed to run the full 5 km?**

A. A Grade 8 student.  
B. A parent and a 10-year-old child.  
C. A child under 6 years old.  
D. A group of PE teachers.

**3. How much does an early sign-up cost per person (before 15 April)?**

A. 30 yuan.  
B. 60 yuan.  
C. 80 yuan.  
D. 150 yuan.

**4. What happens at 9:15 a.m. on the event day?**

A. The warm-up dance begins.  
B. The run starts.  
C. A short ceremony with a donation cheque to the hospital.  
D. Lunch is served at a restaurant.

**5. Which rule about the event is CORRECT?**

A. Runners must wear brand-new shoes.  
B. Runners are welcome to use their own reusable water bottles.  
C. Plastic cups will be handed out at every kilometre.  
D. Earphones are required for safety.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第1节 "donated to the City Children's Hospital"  
【解析】款项捐给市儿童医院。故选 C。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第3节 "No runners under 6 years old"  
【解析】6岁以下不得跑全程。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第4节表格 "Early sign-up... 60 yuan"  
【解析】早鸟价60元。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第5节 "9:15 a.m. ... donation cheque"  
【解析】移交捐款支票仪式。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第6节 "Reusable water bottles are highly welcome"  
【解析】鼓励自带水瓶。故选 B。
