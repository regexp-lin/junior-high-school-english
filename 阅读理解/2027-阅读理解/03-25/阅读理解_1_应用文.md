# 阅读理解（应用文） **Spring Kite Festival — South River Park**

① Hello, friends of South River Park! Spring is here, and the **wind above our river has never been softer**. This April, our park and the City Youth Centre will hold the **10th Spring Kite Festival**. Bring your family, bring your colours, and let your kite fly high above the willows.

② **Basic Information**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Date | Saturday, 17 April 2027 |
| Time | 9:00 – 16:00 |
| Place | North Grassland, South River Park (main gate 2) |
| Entry | **Free** for everyone |
| Organiser | South River Park + City Youth Centre |

③ **Main Activities**

- **9:00 – 10:00**　Opening ceremony & guest kite show (giant dragon kite from Weifang).  
- **10:00 – 12:00**　Free kite flying. Bring your own kite OR try a small paper one at the **Beginner Table** (free, 6 to 12 years old).  
- **12:00 – 13:00**　Lunch break; food trucks on the east path.  
- **13:00 – 14:30**　**Kite-Making Workshop** in the pine pavilion (bamboo sticks, silk paper, glue provided; 40 seats, first come first served).  
- **14:30 – 15:30**　**Kite Contest** — categories: Highest, Longest Tail, Most Creative Shape.  
- **15:30 – 16:00**　Prize-giving & family photo time.

④ **Kite Contest Rules**

- **Two groups**: Junior (6 – 12) and Teen (13 – 18). No adult group.  
- One person, one kite per category. You can join more than one category.  
- Only **hand-held strings** are allowed; no large motor wheels.  
- Each flight will be timed for up to 10 minutes. Highest kite is judged by height; Longest Tail by measurement on the ground; Most Creative Shape by three guest judges.  
- Prizes: a set of art pens, a book about Chinese traditional crafts, and a small trophy.

⑤ **Safety Tips**

1. Flying area is marked with **yellow flags**; do not fly kites outside them.  
2. Stay **at least 20 metres** away from the river and the tall pine trees.  
3. No metal string. Dry cotton string is strongly advised.  
4. Please watch small children closely; a free "Kid Wristband" with parents' phone number is offered at the info desk.  
5. No flying near power lines; park staff will stop any unsafe flight.

⑥ **Easy Rules for Visitors**

- Bring your own water bottle — the park has free water refills at two points.  
- Please carry your rubbish to the green bins.  
- Pets are welcome on a short lead; please clean up after them.  
- Drones, balloons, or open fires are **not** allowed inside the park today.

⑦ **Contact**  
- South River Park Service Centre — 0571-8866-2100 (8:30 – 16:30)  
- City Youth Centre, Mrs. Ouyang — ouyang@cyc.org  
- Event WeChat service account: "SRP-Kite" (QR code on all park posters)

**See you where the sky meets the willow trees!**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Where will the Spring Kite Festival be held?**

A. In the school playground.  
B. At the North Grassland of South River Park.  
C. Outside the City Youth Centre.

**2. At what time will the Kite-Making Workshop start?**

A. 9:00.

C. 13:00.

**3. Which of the following is TRUE about the Kite Contest?**

A. Adults can join the contest together with children.  
B. Each person can join only one category.  
C. There are three categories: Highest, Longest Tail, and Most Creative Shape.  
D. The kite flight can last for a whole afternoon.

**4. What should you NOT bring into the park on that day?**

A. A water bottle.  
B. A small pet on a short lead.  
C. Rubbish to throw in the green bin.  
D. A drone or an open fire.

**5. How can people get more information about the festival?**

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "North Grassland, South River Park"  
【解析】风筝节在南河公园北草地。A、C、D 无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】日程定位题  
【定位】第③段 "13:00 – 14:30 Kite-Making Workshop"  
【解析】工坊 13:00 开始。A、B、D 为其他时段。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段三项类别与规则  
【解析】三个类别匹配。A 明文拒绝成人组；B 错（可跨类）；D 错（每次 10 分钟）。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节否定题  
【定位】第⑥段 "Drones, balloons, or open fires are not allowed"  
【解析】禁止无人机和明火。A、B、C 都可或鼓励。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】联系信息题  
【定位】第⑦段服务电话与微信公众号  
【解析】拨打服务中心电话或关注公众号。A、C、D 无依据。故选 B。
