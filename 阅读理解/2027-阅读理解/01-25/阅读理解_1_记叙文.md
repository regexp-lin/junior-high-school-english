# 阅读理解（记叙文） **My Brother's First Day Alone on the Bus**

① My little brother, Kai, is in Grade 3. Every day, my mother used to take him to school by bike. But last Monday, Mum had to leave home very early for a business trip. She said, "From this week on, Kai can take Bus 28 alone. Lu Lu, can you help him get ready?" I am in middle school now, and Kai is my only brother. I nodded seriously.

② On Sunday evening, we practised together at home. I drew a simple map on a piece of paper. I marked the bus stop, the two stops on the way, and the school gate. We also put a small paper card in Kai's pocket. On it, I wrote my phone number, Mum's number, and our home address. Kai kept looking at the card and smiling. He said, "I'm a big boy now."

③ On Monday morning, I got up earlier than usual. I made toast and milk for Kai. Then I walked him to the bus stop ten minutes away. The morning wind was cold, and Kai's little hand held mine very tightly. When Bus 28 arrived, I let him get on first. "Stay near the driver," I whispered. "Get off at the third stop. And don't forget to smile at the guard at the school gate." He waved at me through the window. The bus slowly moved away.

④ I stood at the stop for a minute, feeling a strange kind of pain in my chest. Was I too worried? Should I have taken him to school myself? I tried to shake these thoughts away and hurried to my own school.

⑤ At noon, Kai called me from a teacher's phone. His voice was full of excitement. "Sister! I did it! I got off at the right stop and even said hello to the driver!" I laughed and almost cried at the same time. From that day, I understood that loving someone often means letting them try — and trusting them to be braver than we think.

---

**根据短文内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why did Mum ask Kai to take the bus alone from last Monday?**

A. Because she was angry with him.  
B. Because she had to go on a business trip.  
C. Because the bike was broken.  
D. Because Kai did not like riding a bike.

**2. What did Lu Lu write on the small paper card in Kai's pocket?**

A. A funny story about animals.  
B. Her phone number, Mum's number and the home address.  
C. A list of subjects in middle school.  
D. A math problem for Kai to solve.

**3. How did Kai feel on the bus stop on Monday morning?**

A. He was relaxed and carefree.  
B. He was angry and sad.  
C. He held his sister's hand tightly, feeling a little nervous.  
D. He ran away from the bus stop.

**4. What did Kai say when he called at noon?**

A. He got off at the wrong stop.  
B. He missed the school bus.  
C. He got off at the right stop and said hello to the driver.  
D. He lost the small paper card.

**5. What is the deeper meaning of the last paragraph?**

A. Older sisters should never trust their brothers.  
B. Loving someone often means letting them try and trusting them.  
C. Taking a bus is the most dangerous part of growing up.  
D. Children should always walk to school with their parents.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段 "Mum had to leave home very early for a business trip."  
【解析】妈妈要出差。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第②段 "I wrote my phone number, Mum's number, and our home address."  
【解析】卡上写明电话和家庭地址。A、C、D 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】推理判断题  
【定位】第③段 "Kai's little hand held mine very tightly."  
【解析】紧握姐姐手说明紧张但信任。A、B、D 均不符。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段 "I got off at the right stop and even said hello to the driver!"  
【解析】Kai 在正确站点下车并问好司机。A、B、D 均不符。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】末段 "loving someone often means letting them try — and trusting them to be braver than we think."  
【解析】作者感悟："爱"意味着让其尝试并信任。A 与"信任"相反；C 片面；D 与放手教育相反。故选 B。
