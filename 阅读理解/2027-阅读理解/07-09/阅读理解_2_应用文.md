# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Green Campus" — School Recycling Competition**

**GREENWOOD MIDDLE SCHOOL · STUDENT UNION**
*Less waste. More life.*

---

**Event Handbook — August 2027**

The Student Union is excited to launch **"Green Campus"**, a four-week recycling competition for all students. The aim is simple: reduce waste on campus and let students experience how small daily actions can protect the environment.

**Key dates**

- **Start:** Monday, 16 August 2027
- **End:** Friday, 10 September 2027 (17:00)
- **Award ceremony:** Tuesday, 14 September 2027, at the flag-raising spot

**Teams**

- Students take part by **class** (not individual).
- Each class picks two "Green Leaders" to plan, count and report.
- Classes in Grades 7 and 8 are in **Group A**; Grade 9 classes are in **Group B**, so each grade level competes fairly.

**How do you earn points?**

| Item | Points per kilogram |
| --- | --- |
| Paper (books, notebooks, cardboard) | 6 |
| Plastic bottles (rinsed) | 10 |
| Aluminium cans (crushed) | 15 |
| Old clothes (clean, folded) | 8 |
| Used batteries (inside a sealed bag, important!) | 25 |
| Kitchen waste from the canteen | — (not counted, handled by staff only) |

Teachers will weigh items every Friday at 15:30 at the bicycle shed behind the library.

**Extra bonus tasks**

- **Poster of the Week (5 points):** The student union picks the best eco-poster from each class every Friday.
- **"Zero Paper Day" (10 points):** A class that uses no paper for notes on a Wednesday (everything on tablets or blackboard) gets 10 bonus points.
- **Refill Challenge (8 points per class):** Each student who brings a reusable bottle for a full week earns 2 points; the class total up to 8 points per week.

**Prizes**

| Place | Group A (Grade 7–8) | Group B (Grade 9) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | 300 library points + green banner + cake party | 300 library points + green banner + free books |
| 2nd | 200 library points + cloth bag | 200 library points + cloth bag |
| 3rd | 100 library points + seed pack | 100 library points + seed pack |
| All classes joining | "Green Class" paper certificate | "Green Class" paper certificate |

**Rules**

1. Items must be **clean and dry**. Wet paper or unwashed bottles will NOT be counted.
2. **No buying recyclables from outside** (e.g. asking a parent to bring a bag of cans from a shop). If checked, the class will lose 50 points.
3. **Do not bring glass.** Glass may break and hurt students; our school will not collect it.
4. **Weighing sessions** are only on Fridays. Other days, place items in the labelled green corner of the classroom.
5. Any argument or unfairness must be reported to the head of the student union, Wang Min, Class 9-1.

**Contact**
Student Union, Room 102 · Email greencampus@greenwood.edu · Notice board: main hall, left wall.

*Our planet has only one Earth. Let's carry her lightly.*

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. How long does the competition last?**

A. About one week.  
B. From 16 August to 10 September 2027.  
C. Only on Fridays.  
D. The whole year.

**2. Which item earns the highest points per kilogram?**

A. Paper.  
B. Plastic bottles.  
C. Aluminium cans.  
D. Used batteries (in a sealed bag).

**3. What can a class do to earn extra points?**

A. Run around the playground for 10 minutes.  
B. Hold a "Zero Paper Day" on a Wednesday.  
C. Bring glass bottles to school.  
D. Eat lunch outside the canteen.

**4. What happens if a class asks a parent to bring a bag of cans from a shop?**

A. The class gets more points.  
B. The class loses 50 points.  
C. The parent joins the competition.  
D. Nothing happens.

**5. Where do Grade 7 and Grade 8 classes compete?**

A. In Group A.  
B. In Group B.  
C. In a separate city event.  
D. Only at night.

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Start / End" 日期  
【解析】8 月 16 日至 9 月 10 日。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】表格 "Used batteries... 25"  
【解析】电池每千克 25 分最多。A/B/C 均错。故选 D。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Zero Paper Day... 10 bonus points"  
【解析】无纸日加 10 分。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"the class will lose 50 points"  
【解析】扣 50 分。A/C/D 均错。故选 B。

**5.**  
【答案】A  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】"Grades 7 and 8 are in Group A"  
【解析】在 A 组。B/C/D 均错。故选 A。
