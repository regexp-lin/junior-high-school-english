# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — "Welcome Back" Second-Hand Book Fair 2027**

> **Lihua Middle School · Library Council**
> **"Welcome Back" Second-Hand Book Fair — 2027 Autumn Term**
> **Notice to Students, Parents and Teachers**

---

### 1. Why a Second-Hand Book Fair?

Every new term, many families **buy more reading books** than they will ever need, while other families find that good books are **hard to afford**. At the same time, thousands of books sit unread in our school's cupboards. Our Library Council believes that every good book deserves **at least three owners**. The second-hand book fair is a simple way to move books from silent shelves back into busy hands.

### 2. When & Where

- **Date**: Saturday, **25 September 2027**
- **Time**: 09:00 – 16:00 (one day only)
- **Place**: School Inner Playground (south side) + Teaching Block A, 1st floor corridor
- **Set-up**: Friday, 24 September, 16:30 – 18:00 (sellers only)

If it rains, the fair will move fully to Teaching Block A. A short notice will be sent in the school app **by 07:30 on the day**.

### 3. Who Can Take Part?

- **Every student** of Lihua Middle School (Grades 7–9) may sell **up to 25 books**.
- **Parents and teachers** may join, but they should share a stall with their own child or class.
- **Outside visitors** are welcome **only as buyers**, with a short sign-in at the school gate.

### 4. What Kind of Books Are Welcome?

✔  Storybooks, novels, poems, classic literature.  
✔  English readers, vocabulary books, graded readers.  
✔  Popular science and "how-things-work" books.  
✔  Clean, well-kept textbooks **from last year**, with no full answers written inside.

✘  Test-answer booklets and hot-sell answer keys.  
✘  Books with broken covers, missing pages, or water damage.  
✘  Magazines older than three years, unless they are special collections.

### 5. How to Set a Price

- Use a small **yellow price tag** (free at the library counter).
- Suggested price: **20 % – 40 %** of the original price; classic literature may be priced a bit higher.
- **Swap** is also allowed: mark your book "**Swap Only**" and choose one book of equal value in return.

### 6. The "Second Pocket" Rule

The fair encourages a quiet kindness. Any money you make on the day can be put into **two pockets** of your choice:

1. **Pocket A — Your Pocket**: You may keep **up to 70 %** of the money for yourself.
2. **Pocket B — The Mountain Classroom Pocket**: The rest (**at least 30 %**) will be collected by the Library Council and used to buy English readers for our **partner mountain school** in Yunnan.

**No one is allowed to put 0 % into Pocket B.** Kindness is not a choice; it is the price of joining the fair.

### 7. On the Day — What to Bring

- Your books, neatly tied with string (no more than 25).
- A small **cardboard sign** with your name, class, and table number.
- A **reusable bag** (no single-use plastic bags; the school will offer cloth bags at cost).
- A smiling face (free).

### 8. Sign-Up

- **Who**: any Grade 7–9 student who wants to sell.
- **When**: **1–20 September 2027**, on the school portal → "Events" → "Book Fair".
- **What you get after sign-up**: a table number + a reminder on the day before.

### 9. Contact

- **Mr Li Tianyu**, Library Council Teacher, Library 2F, Room 203.
- WeChat school account: "Lihua Library Corner".
- For lost-and-found on the day: front desk, Playground Entrance B.

> **"A book you have finished is not an old friend; it is a friend ready to meet someone new."**
> — Lihua Library Council

**根据通知内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. What is the main purpose of the "Welcome Back" Second-Hand Book Fair?**

A. To sell new books at a lower price.  
B. To help books move from silent shelves back into busy hands, and to help a partner mountain school.  
C. To recruit new teachers for the library.  
D. To train students as professional sellers.

**2. Where will the fair be held if it rains on 25 September?**

A. Only on the outer playground.  
B. In the school canteen.  
C. Fully in Teaching Block A, with a notice sent by 07:30 on the day.  
D. The fair will be cancelled completely.

**3. Which of the following is **NOT** allowed for sale at the fair?**

A. Last year's clean textbooks without full answers written inside.  
B. English graded readers.  
C. Test-answer booklets and hot-sell answer keys.  
D. Popular science books about how things work.

**4. According to the "Second Pocket" rule, what must every seller do with at least 30 % of the money earned?**

A. Give it to the school head teacher.  
B. Spend it on new games in the school shop.  
C. Put it into Pocket B, which will be used to buy English readers for a partner mountain school.  
D. Return it to the buyers.

**5. What is the deadline for signing up to **sell** at the fair?**

A. 25 September.  
B. 24 September.  
C. 20 September.  
D. 1 September.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】主旨大意题  
【定位】第 1 条 "every good book deserves at least three owners"；第 6 条 "buy English readers for our partner mountain school in Yunnan"  
【解析】目的是让旧书重新流转并资助山区学校。A、C、D 均不合原意。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 2 条 "the fair will move fully to Teaching Block A. A short notice will be sent in the school app by 07:30"  
【解析】雨天移至教学楼 A，并会在 7:30 前发布通知。A、B、D 均与原文相悖。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节判断题（否定型）  
【定位】第 4 条 "✘ Test-answer booklets and hot-sell answer keys"  
【解析】答案书与热销答案册明确被禁止。A、B、D 均为欢迎品类。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 6 条 "Pocket B — The Mountain Classroom Pocket ... at least 30% ... used to buy English readers for our partner mountain school"  
【解析】至少 30% 进入 B 口袋，用于资助山区学校。A、B、D 均无依据。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第 8 条 "When: 1–20 September 2027"  
【解析】报名窗口 9 月 1 日至 20 日。A 为活动日；B 为布展日；D 为报名开启日。故选 C。
