# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School — Autumn Club Fair 2027**

Hi everyone,

The new school year is just around the corner, and it is time to find the club that fits you! On **Saturday, September 6, 2027**, our school will hold the **Autumn Club Fair** on the playground and in the main hall. Twenty-two student-run clubs will open their doors to you. Come, ask, try, and then choose your favourite.

---

### Quick Facts

- **Date:** Saturday, September 6, 2027  
- **Time:** 09:00 – 16:00  
- **Place:** Playground (outdoor booths) + Main Hall 1F (indoor booths)  
- **Who can join:** all Grade 7–9 students + their parents (welcome)  
- **Cost:** free to visit; some clubs ask for a small yearly fee (¥30–¥80)

---

### Featured Clubs (2027 Highlights)

**① Robot Makers Club**  
Build small moving robots with wood, plastic, and simple code.  
Meets: Tuesday & Thursday, 16:30–18:00, Tech Room 303.  
Yearly fee: ¥80 (for parts).

**② Green Campus Team**  
Plan recycling days, plant trees, and cook plant-based lunches once a month.  
Meets: Wednesday, 16:30–17:30, Biology Lab + Back Garden.  
Yearly fee: none.

**③ English Drama Circle**  
Read, act, and perform short English plays. End-of-term show on stage!  
Meets: Friday, 17:00–18:30, Music Room.  
Yearly fee: ¥50 (for costumes).

**④ Camera Walk Club**  
Walk in the city and take photos on phones or cameras; monthly small exhibition.  
Meets: Saturday, 10:00–12:00, different city spots.  
Yearly fee: ¥30.

**⑤ Chess & Board Games**  
Chinese chess, international chess, Go, and strategy board games.  
Meets: Monday, 16:30–17:30, Library Reading Room B.  
Yearly fee: none.

**⑥ Young Reporters**  
Run the school blog; write simple news, interviews and book reviews.  
Meets: Tuesday, 12:10–13:00, Computer Room 2.  
Yearly fee: none.

**⑦ Running & Health Club**  
Easy runs 3 km/5 km, weekend hikes, healthy-food talks.  
Meets: Monday & Thursday, 17:00–18:00, Playground.  
Yearly fee: ¥30 (T-shirt).

**⑧ Maths Puzzle Club**  
Weekly fun puzzles, maths tricks, Olympic-style problems — no pressure.  
Meets: Wednesday, 16:30–17:30, Classroom 8-4.  
Yearly fee: none.

*(Other 14 clubs — art, calligraphy, music, dance, cooking, model UN, animals-care, etc. — will be listed on the posters at the school gate from August 25.)*

---

### How the Day Works

1. Enter through the west gate. Pick up a free **"Club Passport"** with a map.  
2. Visit any booth. Members will show their work, answer your questions, and let you try a short sample activity.  
3. If you like a club, get your passport stamped by the club leader.  
4. **By Monday, September 8, 23:59**, submit your passport online at **www.lihua-ms.edu.cn/clubs**, choosing up to **two** clubs. If a club is full, you will be moved to the waiting list.

### House Rules

- Running in indoor halls is **not allowed**.  
- Please bring your own water bottle; a refill station is near the playground gate.  
- No food or drinks inside the main hall except plain water.  
- Parents are welcome but please stay in the visitor area near the booth.

### Notes for Grade 9 Students

Because of the higher study pressure, Grade 9 students may choose **only one** club, and meetings must not go beyond 17:30 on weekdays. Clubs marked with the symbol "★" (Chess, Young Reporters, Maths Puzzle, Green Campus) are specially recommended for Grade 9, as they are low-hour and close to study interests.

### Contact

- **Student Union Office:** Room 216, Main Building  
- **Phone:** 0571-8831-0906 (Mon–Fri, 16:30–17:30)  
- **Email:** clubs@lihua-ms.edu.cn  
- **WeChat:** search "Lihua Clubs 2027"

**See you on September 6. A whole new year of friendships and fun is waiting.**

*— Student Union, Lihua Middle School, July 2027*
