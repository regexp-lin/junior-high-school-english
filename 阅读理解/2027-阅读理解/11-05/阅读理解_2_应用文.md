# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Sounds of November": Autumn Soundscape Collection 2027**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · Music Department + Literature Club**  
> **Autumn Soundscape Project 2027 — "Sounds of November"**  
> **Not every beautiful thing in school is something you can see.**

A school is full of **sounds** that we never stop to notice: the slow click of a broken radiator; the paper cups stacked at the north canteen door; the exact second when the chalk hits the blackboard in Mr. Han's Chinese class. In November, before the coldest wind flattens everything into one quiet grey, we would like to **record** these small sounds — and build our first-ever **Autumn Soundscape Album**.

---

## 🎧 What Is a "Soundscape"?

A soundscape is a **short audio recording**, usually no longer than **30 seconds**, of the sounds of a single place at a single moment — no voice-over, no added music, no stitching together. Just one small, honest sound-picture.

For example:
- 30 seconds of **leaves** being stepped on by early-morning students on the south path.  
- 30 seconds of the **school radio's opening music** from inside the third-floor corridor.  
- 30 seconds of the **water boiler** filling the teachers' thermos flasks.  
- 30 seconds of **rain** on the art-classroom skylight.

## 🎙 How to Record

- **Phone is fine.** No special microphone is needed. But please use the full, un-shortened audio app (not a chat app that shortens sound).  
- Keep the microphone about **20 – 30 cm** from the source.  
- **Turn off ringtones and notification sounds** on your phone first.  
- Please record in one go; no cut-and-paste.  
- File name: `YOURCLASS_YOURNICKNAME_PLACE.m4a`, for example: `8-2_AN_STAIR_WEST.m4a`.

---

## 📝 Please Attach a Short Note

With each upload, add a small form:

1. **Place** (where the sound was made).  
2. **Time** (date + hour + minute).  
3. **One sentence**, in Chinese or English, about what this sound means to you.  
4. **Your name** (or a nickname — anonymous is OK).

Short note example:  
> *Place: West staircase, second floor. Time: 7:22 a.m., 13 Nov 2027. About this sound: the first class monitor of the day turning on the long lamp; a tiny click that says "school begins now".*

---

## 🚫 Sounds We Cannot Use

- Any voice-only recording longer than 5 seconds of a classmate or teacher's clear words (privacy).  
- Sounds that could hurt an animal or a person.  
- Copyrighted music or film dialogue (we want **real** school sounds).  
- Very loud, harsh sounds that may damage listeners' ears.
