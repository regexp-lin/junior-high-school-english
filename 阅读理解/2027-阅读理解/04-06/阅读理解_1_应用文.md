# 阅读理解（应用文） **"Little Einsteins" Campus Science Fair — Spring 2027**

---

**Bright Future Middle School**  
*Science is not just in textbooks. It is in the air, the kitchen, and the playground.*

---

**1. About the Fair**

The Science Department is organising the **11th "Little Einsteins" Campus Science Fair**. Students are invited to design a small experiment, poster, or model that answers one simple question: **"Why does this happen in daily life?"**

---

**2. Date & Place**

| Item | Information |
|------|-------------|
| Opening ceremony | Friday, 23 April, 3:30 p.m. |
| Main fair | Saturday, 24 April, 9:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m. |
| Venue | School Sports Hall (1F) + Science Building (2F) |
| Visitors | All students, teachers, parents, and the Grade 6 pupils from our sister primary school |

---

**3. Groups**

- **Group A — Kitchen Science**: e.g. why does rice stick together? Why does bread rise?  
- **Group B — Green Earth**: e.g. how to save water in daily life, how rubbish is sorted.  
- **Group C — Space & Sky**: e.g. how a simple paper rocket flies; why the sky turns red at sunset.  
- **Group D — Free Topic**: any clever idea you can test or show safely.

Each project must be prepared by a **team of 2 – 4 students** from the same grade.

---

**4. Important Rules**

1. Projects with **open fire, strong acids, or dangerous animals** are not allowed.  
2. Posters should be **A1 size (594 × 841 mm)** and include clear diagrams.  
3. Each team is given a **1-metre table**; please do not bring huge models that cannot fit.  
4. During the fair, at least **one team member** must stay at the stall to answer questions.  
5. Mobile phones may be used for videos, but **do not play music loudly**.

---

**5. How to Join**

- Step 1: Download the **Project Plan Form** from the school website.  
- Step 2: Fill in the form with your team name, question, and method.  
- Step 3: Email it to **science@bfms.edu.cn** before **Monday, 12 April, 8:00 p.m.**  
- Step 4: Wait for the teacher's feedback within 3 school days.  
- Step 5: Pick up your table number and name card from the Science Office on Thursday, 22 April.

---

**6. Prizes**

- **Gold Einstein Award:** 3 teams (one per grade) — free visit to the City Science Museum.  
- **Silver Einstein Award:** 6 teams — a book gift set and **"Future Scientist"** certificate.  
- **Best Poster Award:** 4 teams — a beautiful handmade **microscope bookmark**.  
- **Audience Star:** 1 team chosen by visitors' votes — featured in the school magazine.

---

> **Remember: A good science question is always more valuable than a fancy answer. See you at the fair!**

---

**根据文章内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. When is the main science fair held?**

A. Friday, 23 April, 3:30 p.m.  
B. Saturday, 24 April, 9:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.  
C. Sunday, 25 April, all day.  
D. Thursday, 22 April, afternoon.

**2. Which of the following projects would BEST fit into Group A (Kitchen Science)?**

A. Why does ice melt in the sun?  
B. Why does bread rise?  
C. Why does the sky turn pink at sunset?  
D. How to sort plastic bottles.

**3. How many students are needed in one team?**

A. Only one, working alone.  
B. 2 to 4 students from the same grade.  
C. At least 5 students.  
D. One student and one parent.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed at the fair?**

A. Using a mobile phone for videos.  
B. Using A1 posters with clear diagrams.  
C. Bringing open fire or strong acids.  
D. Having a team member at the stall to answer questions.

**5. What is the prize for the "Audience Star" team?**

A. A trip to the City Science Museum.  
B. A handmade microscope bookmark.  
C. A book gift set.  
D. Being featured in the school magazine.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第2节表格 "Saturday, 24 April, 9:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m."  
【解析】主场在周六。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第3节 Group A "why does bread rise?"  
【解析】面包为何会发酵属厨房科学。故选 B。

**3.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第3节 "team of 2 – 4 students"  
【解析】2–4 人同年级组队。故选 B。

**4.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第4节 "open fire, strong acids..."  
【解析】明火与强酸被禁。故选 C。

**5.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第6节 "Audience Star... featured in the school magazine"  
【解析】观众之星登校刊。故选 D。
