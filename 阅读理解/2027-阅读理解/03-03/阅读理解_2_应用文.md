# 阅读理解（应用文） **Campus Radio Host Auditions — Sign-up Notice**

① Dear students, the **Starlight Campus Radio Station** will open its microphones again this new term. After three wonderful years on air, most of our Grade 9 hosts will leave in June, so we need **fresh voices**! If you love talking, reading, music or storytelling, this is your chance.

② **About the Station**

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Started in | **2024** |
| On-air time | Every school day, **12:20 – 12:50** (lunch break) |
| Listeners | About **1,800** students and teachers |
| Programmes | English Corner, Campus News, Music Box, Story Time |

③ **Who can sign up?**

- Any **Grade 7 or Grade 8** student.  
- Grade 9 students are **not** accepted this term because of their busy exams.  
- You do not need to be a top English student — clear speech and a warm heart matter more.

④ **Audition Steps**

1. **Sign-up (before 10 March, 17:00)** — Scan the QR code on the red poster at the school library door.  
2. **Round 1 — Reading test (13 March, Saturday, 9:00 – 11:30, Classroom 3-202)**: Read a 1-minute Chinese passage and a 1-minute English passage given on the day.  
3. **Round 2 — Free topic (17 March, Wednesday, 16:30 – 18:00, Radio Room 105)**: Talk about any topic you like for 2 minutes. No slides or music are needed.  
4. **Final — Team interview (21 March, Sunday, 10:00 – 12:00, Art Centre Room 206)**.

⑤ **What to Prepare**

- A **smile** and a **glass of warm water** (the room can be dry).  
- A short self-introduction (about **30 seconds**).  
- Comfortable clothes — no school uniform needed for Round 2 and the Final.  
- **Do not** bring any pre-written scripts or big toys to the audition room.

⑥ **Tips from last year's hosts**

> *"Don't speak too fast. Slow down, breathe, smile — the mic will hear your feelings."* — Wu Lan, Grade 9  
> *"Choose a topic that you really care about. Real feelings always sound better than big words."* — Chen Yi, Grade 9

⑦ **Results & Training**

- Results will be sent by school email on **25 March, before 18:00**.  
- New hosts must join the **spring training camp** from **6 April to 28 April**, every Wednesday 16:30 – 18:00.  
- Any question? Please come to Radio Room 105 on Monday or Friday lunch break, or write to starlight@bluesky-ms.cn.

**We are waiting for your voice on the wave! — Starlight Campus Radio Team**

**根据材料内容，选择最佳答案。**

**1. Why is the Radio Station looking for new hosts this term?**

A. The old hosts have broken the microphones.  
B. Most Grade 9 hosts will leave the station in June.  
C. The school has bought a new radio room.  
D. The programmes have been cancelled by teachers.

**2. Which group of students CANNOT sign up this term?**

A. Grade 7 students.  
B. Grade 8 students.  
C. Grade 9 students.  
D. Students who are not top in English.

**3. What do students need to do in Round 1 of the audition?**

A. Talk about any topic for 2 minutes.  
B. Have a team interview with the teachers.  
C. Read a Chinese passage and an English passage on the day.  
D. Prepare their own slides and music.

**4. Which of the following is NOT allowed in the audition room?**

A. A smile.  
B. A glass of warm water.  
C. A short self-introduction.  
D. A pre-written script.

**5. When should students who pass the audition start training?**

A. 10 March.  
B. 21 March.  
C. 25 March.  
D. 6 April.

---

## 答案与解析

**1.**  
【答案】B  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第①段第 2 句 "most of our Grade 9 hosts will leave in June"  
【解析】原因是九年级主持人 6 月离开，故需新声音。A、C、D 均无依据。故选 B。

**2.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第③段第 2 条 "Grade 9 students are not accepted this term"  
【解析】明确本学期不接受九年级学生。A、B 明确可以报名；D 项"不是英语尖子"文中说"matter more"的不是顶尖水平，不构成拒绝。故选 C。

**3.**  
【答案】C  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第④段第 2 条 "Read a 1-minute Chinese passage and a 1-minute English passage given on the day"  
【解析】第一轮是当场给稿各读 1 分钟中英短文。A 项是第二轮；B 项是决赛；D 项与"No slides or music are needed"相反。故选 C。

**4.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑤段末句 "Do not bring any pre-written scripts or big toys"  
【解析】禁止带事先写好的稿件。A、B、C 均属于"要准备"的清单。故选 D。

**5.**  
【答案】D  
【题型】细节理解题  
【定位】第⑦段第 2 条 "spring training camp from 6 April to 28 April"  
【解析】通过面试的新主持人 4 月 6 日开始春季培训。A 是报名截止日；B 是决赛日；C 是出结果日。故选 D。
