# 阅读理解（应用文） **Lihua Middle School Winter Morning-Light Run 2027**

---

> **Lihua Middle School · PE Department + Student Health Club**  
> **Winter Morning-Light Run 2027** — *"Run Gently Into the Short December Sunrise."*  
> **Dates**: **Mon 14 Dec 2027 – Fri 15 Jan 2028 (five weeks, Mon – Fri)**  
> **Time**: **07:05 – 07:35 each weekday morning** (30 minutes, before breakfast)  
> **Venue**: **Lihua 400-m outdoor running track + inside ring road on heavy-smog days**

---

The sun rises this month between **07:24 and 07:29** in our city. This is a short, beautiful window in which the sky turns pink, then orange, then a pale clean winter blue. Our new Morning-Light Run is a **gentle, non-competitive, optional** 30-minute activity for Grades 7–9 students who want to start the school day with warm muscles, open eyes, and a real live sky above them. There is no time-trial, no ranking, no speed requirement. You come. You run or walk. You see the sun. You go to breakfast.

---

## 🕒 Daily Schedule (typical morning)

| Time | What | Where |
|---|---|---|
| 07:00 – 07:05 | Sign-in at the track gate with your school ID | Track gate |
| 07:05 – 07:10 | **Warm-up** (led by student captains) | Track centre grass |
| 07:10 – 07:25 | **Main run / walk** (3 lanes: light / medium / brisk) | 400-m track outer lanes |
| 07:25 – 07:30 | **Cool-down & stretch** | Track centre grass |
| 07:30 – 07:35 | Walk slowly to the cafeteria (warm drink + breakfast) | Main building |

---

## 🏃 The Three Lanes (choose your own, change any day)

| Lane | Approximate pace | Who it suits |
|---|---|---|
| **Light** (inner-outer lane 5) | Walking / very slow jogging (~ 7 min/km and slower) | Students new to running, cold mornings, days when you are tired |
| **Medium** (lane 6) | Easy jog (~ 5:30 – 7 min/km) | Most students, most days |
| **Brisk** (lane 7) | Faster run (~ 5 min/km and under) | Students in the track team, training days |

- **You may change lanes** any morning; no approval required.  
- **Fast runners**, please stay in lane 7; passing on the inside is forbidden for safety.  
- **Walking is welcome in the Light lane every single day.** Walking is also a real winter exercise.

---

## 🧥 Clothing & Warm-up Advice

- **Three thin layers** (thermal base + mid-layer fleece + windbreaker) are warmer than one thick coat. Layers trap small pockets of warm air.  
- **Covered ears** (a thin knitted hat): 30% of our body heat escapes from the head and neck area in cold still air.  
- **Thin gloves** for the run, thick gloves in the pocket for the cool-down.  
- **Clean running shoes** with flexible soles; football studs and leather shoes are NOT allowed on the tartan track.  
- **Do NOT wear headphones** — you need to hear the captain's voice and the small station bell.

---

## 🩺 Health & Safety Rules

- **Eat before you run**: one small banana or one slice of bread, 15 minutes before warm-up. **No full breakfast before running** — this causes stomach discomfort.  
- **Breathing**: breathe in through the nose, out through the mouth. A thin cotton scarf over the mouth on very cold days (below −3 °C) is recommended.  
- **Asthma / bronchitis / any lung condition**: please see the school infirmary first; a **blue asthma inhaler** is allowed in your pocket.  
- **Injury report**: any small fall must be reported to the sign-in captain immediately; the school nurse Ms Kang is on duty in the infirmary at 07:00 every morning.  
- **Air quality rule**: if the local AQI is **above 150** at 06:30, the run moves indoors to the ring corridor on the 2nd floor of Block A. An announcement will appear on the school mini-app by 06:35.

| Day | Small theme | Idea |
|---|---|---|

Sign-up: school mini-app → PE → Morning-Light Run captains. **Closing: Thu 10 December, 17:30.**

## 🍵 Warm Breakfast at the Cafeteria (07:35 – 07:55)

- **Ginger-honey tea** at the entrance counter (free to all runners that morning).  
- Normal cafeteria breakfast continues.  
- Runners get a small **green stamp** on their meal card (one per day; not for any ranking — only for the end-of-winter small thank-you postcard).

---

## ❗ Important Quiet Rules

- **This is NOT a competition.** No timings are recorded, no ranking is published.  
- **No mobile phones on the track.** Leave them in the classroom locker.  
- **If you miss a morning, simply come the next day.** Nobody keeps attendance as a pressure tool.  
- **Grade 9 students preparing for senior-high exams** are specifically invited; a gentle morning run improves cognitive test scores by about 8% in the same afternoon (University of Illinois 2021, 1,200 teenagers).  
- **Students with fever above 37.5 °C, a cold, or other illness**: please rest at home or in the dormitory. Your classmates and captains will miss you kindly for one morning.

The Morning-Light Run is a small invitation. **Come stand on a tartan track, with your friends, for thirty minutes, once a winter morning.** Run gently. Walk gladly.
