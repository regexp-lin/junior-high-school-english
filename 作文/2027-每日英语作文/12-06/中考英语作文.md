# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你最近养了一只宠物。请你写一篇短文介绍它，内容包括：

① 你的宠物是什么、叫什么名字；  
② 它的外貌和性格；  
③ 养宠物给你带来了什么。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 说明文（介绍类） |
| 话题 | 介绍宠物 |
| 人称 | 第一/第三人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

I have a pet cat named Mango. She joined our family two months ago, and she has already become my best companion.

Mango is a small orange tabby with bright green eyes and soft, fluffy fur. She loves sleeping in sunny spots and chasing butterflies in the garden. Although she looks lazy during the day, she becomes incredibly playful at night, running around the house like a little furry tornado.

Having a pet has changed my life in many ways. When I feel stressed after a long day of studying, Mango curls up on my lap and purrs softly. That sound is the most calming thing in the world. She has also taught me about responsibility — I feed her, clean her space and take her to the vet regularly.

Pets may not speak our language, but they understand our hearts.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *best companion* — 最好的伙伴  
2. *orange tabby* — 橘色虎斑猫  
3. *a little furry tornado* — 一个毛茸茸的小旋风  
4. *curls up on my lap* — 蜷缩在我腿上  
5. *purrs softly* — 轻声打呼噜  
6. *understand our hearts* — 懂得我们的心  

---

## 全文翻译

我有一只叫"芒果"的宠物猫。她两个月前加入了我们的家庭，已经成了我最好的伙伴。

芒果是一只小巧的橘色虎斑猫，有着明亮的绿色眼睛和柔软蓬松的毛。她喜欢在有阳光的地方睡觉，也喜欢在花园里追蝴蝶。虽然白天看起来很懒，到了晚上她就变得超级好动，在家里跑来跑去，像一个毛茸茸的小旋风。

养宠物在很多方面改变了我的生活。当我学了一天感到疲惫时，芒果就蜷缩在我腿上，轻轻打着呼噜。那声音是世界上最治愈的东西。她还教会了我责任——我每天喂她、打扫她的空间、定期带她去看兽医。

宠物也许不会说我们的语言，但它们懂得我们的心。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| companion | 伙伴 | A pet is a wonderful companion. |
| curl up | 蜷缩 | The cat curled up by the fireplace. |
| incredibly | 难以置信地 | She is incredibly playful. |
| teach sb. about | 教某人关于 | Pets teach us about responsibility. |
| in many ways | 在很多方面 | Having a pet changed my life in many ways. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 伙伴 | company ✅ | companion / company（意思略有不同） |
| 蓬松 | fluffy ✅ | fluffy（注意拼写） |
| 兽医 | animal doctor | vet / veterinarian |
| 定期 | regular ✅ | regularly（副词） |
| 呼噜 | purr ✅ | purr（猫打呼噜的专用词） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 外貌 | She has green eyes and soft fur. | Her green eyes glow like two tiny lanterns, and her orange fur is softer than a cotton cloud. |
| 性格 | She is playful at night. | By day she is a sleeping princess; by night she transforms into a furry hurricane that no furniture is safe from. |
| 感悟 | Pets make us happy. | A purring cat on your lap is nature's way of saying: you are loved, you are warm, and everything is going to be fine. |
