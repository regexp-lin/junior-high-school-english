# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，上个月你过了一个特别的生日。请你写一篇短文记录这次经历，内容包括：

① 你的生日是怎么过的；  
② 让你最感动的一个瞬间；  
③ 这次生日让你领悟到了什么。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 记叙文 |
| 话题 | 难忘的生日 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般过去时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Last month, I turned fifteen. I thought it would be just an ordinary day, but my friends and family turned it into a birthday I will never forget.

In the morning, I found a handwritten card on my desk at school. It was from my classmates — each of them had written a sentence about what they liked about me. Some were funny, some were sweet, and all of them made me smile.

When I got home, Mum had baked a chocolate cake from scratch. As she lit the candles, she said quietly, "I remember the day you were born — you were so tiny, and now look how much you've grown." Her words brought tears to my eyes.

That birthday taught me that the best gifts are not wrapped in boxes. They come in the form of kind words, warm hugs and the love of people who truly care about you.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *turned fifteen* — 满了十五岁  
2. *a handwritten card* — 一张手写的卡片  
3. *baked a chocolate cake from scratch* — 从零开始烤了一个巧克力蛋糕  
4. *lit the candles* — 点燃蜡烛  
5. *brought tears to my eyes* — 让我热泪盈眶  
6. *wrapped in boxes* — 包在盒子里  

---

## 全文翻译

上个月我满十五岁了。我以为只是普通的一天，但朋友和家人把它变成了一个我永远不会忘记的生日。

早上，我在学校的课桌上发现了一张手写的卡片。那是我的同学们写的——每人写了一句他们喜欢我的什么。有些搞笑，有些温馨，全都让我忍不住微笑。

回到家，妈妈从零开始烤了一个巧克力蛋糕。她点燃蜡烛时，轻声说："我记得你出生那天——你那么小，现在看看你长了多少。"她的话让我热泪盈眶。

那次生日教会了我，最好的礼物不是包在盒子里的。它们以善意的话语、温暖的拥抱和真心关爱你的人的爱的形式出现。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| turn + 年龄 | 满了……岁 | She will turn sixteen next month. |
| from scratch | 从零开始 | Mum made the cake from scratch. |
| bring tears to one's eyes | 让人热泪盈眶 | His speech brought tears to our eyes. |
| in the form of | 以……的形式 | Love comes in the form of daily care. |
| I will never forget | 我永远不会忘记 | That moment is something I will never forget. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 满……岁 | was fifteen | turned fifteen（更生动） |
| 手写的 | hand-written ✅ | handwritten（一个词） |
| 点蜡烛 | lighted | lit（light过去式常用lit） |
| 热泪盈眶 | bring tear to | bring tears to（复数） |
| 从零开始 | from zero | from scratch（固定搭配） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 惊喜 | I found a card on my desk. | Thirty handwritten sentences, thirty different reasons to smile — that card was a birthday present no shop could sell. |
| 感动 | Mum's words made me cry. | Her voice was steady, but her eyes told a fifteen-year story of sleepless nights, early mornings and unconditional love. |
| 感悟 | The best gifts are not things. | The best birthday gift has no price tag — it is the feeling that you are loved, remembered and never alone. |
