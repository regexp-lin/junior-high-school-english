# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，圣诞节来临之际，你的英国笔友 Lily 邀请你参加她们学校的圣诞派对。你无法到场，请写一封回信，内容包括：

① 感谢她的邀请并解释无法参加的原因；  
② 简单介绍你这边的节日氛围或安排；  
③ 表达祝福并期待下次见面。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 参考范文

Dear Lily,

Thank you so much for inviting me to your school's Christmas party! I really wish I could be there, but unfortunately I can't make it because my school is having final exams that week.

Although Christmas is not a traditional Chinese holiday, many young people here enjoy the festive atmosphere. Shops play Christmas songs, and streets are decorated with colourful lights. On Christmas Eve, my friends and I usually exchange small gifts and share apples — in China, apples on Christmas Eve symbolise peace because the Chinese word for "Christmas Eve" sounds like "peaceful night".

I hope your party is full of laughter and joy. Merry Christmas, Lily! Please send my greetings to your family. When summer comes, maybe you can visit China and we'll have our own celebration!

With warm wishes,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *I can't make it* — 我去不了  
2. *festive atmosphere* — 节日氛围  
3. *exchange small gifts* — 互换小礼物  
4. *symbolise peace* — 象征平安  
5. *full of laughter and joy* — 充满欢笑和喜悦  
6. *with warm wishes* — 带着温暖的祝愿  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的Lily，

非常感谢你邀请我参加你们学校的圣诞派对！我真的很想去，但可惜那周我学校在期末考试，没办法参加。

虽然圣诞节不是中国传统节日，但这里很多年轻人喜欢节日的氛围。商店里放着圣诞歌，街道上装饰着五颜六色的灯。在平安夜，我和朋友们通常交换小礼物并分享苹果——在中国，平安夜送苹果象征"平安"，因为"平安夜"的"平"和苹果的"苹"发音相似。

希望你的派对充满欢笑和快乐。圣诞快乐，Lily！请代我向你的家人问好。等到夏天，也许你可以来中国，我们一起庆祝！

带着温暖的祝愿，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| can't make it | 去不了 | Sorry, I can't make it to the party. |
| festive atmosphere | 节日氛围 | The city is full of festive atmosphere. |
| exchange gifts | 交换礼物 | We exchange gifts on Christmas Day. |
| send one's greetings | 代某人问好 | Please send my greetings to your mother. |
| with warm wishes | 带着温暖的祝愿 | With warm wishes for the new year! |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 去不了 | can't go it | can't make it |
| 氛围 | atmosphere ✅ | atmosphere（注意拼写和发音） |
| 象征 | symbolize ✅ | symbolise (英) / symbolize (美) |
| 问好 | say my hello | send my greetings |
| 装饰 | decorated with ✅ | be decorated with |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 遗憾 | I'm sorry I can't come. | My heart will be at your party even if my body is stuck in an exam hall six thousand miles away. |
| 描写 | The streets look nice at Christmas. | Even without snow, my city dresses up in red and gold, and carols drift out of every shopfront like invisible invitations to celebrate. |
| 祝福 | Merry Christmas! | May your Christmas be wrapped in warmth, filled with laughter and sprinkled with just enough magic to last until we meet again. |
