# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，即将升入高中。请你写一封信给未来的自己，内容包括：

① 回顾初中三年你最难忘的经历；  
② 你对未来高中生活的期待；  
③ 你想对未来的自己说的话。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（书信） |
| 话题 | 写给未来自己的信 |
| 人称 | 第一/第二人称 |
| 时态 | 一般过去时+一般将来时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Future Me,

As I write this letter, I am about to leave middle school and step into a brand-new chapter of my life.

Looking back, the past three years have been unforgettable. I still remember the English speech contest where I stood on stage trembling with fear but finished my speech with a loud round of applause. That moment taught me that courage beats fear every time.

I am both excited and nervous about high school. I look forward to making new friends, exploring new subjects and becoming a more independent person. I know there will be challenges, but I believe I can handle them.

So, Future Me, here is my message to you: never stop learning, never stop dreaming, and never forget the brave teenager who once dared to step outside his comfort zone.

Stay strong and keep smiling!

With love,  
Li Hua from 2027

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *step into a brand-new chapter* — 踏入全新的篇章  
2. *trembling with fear* — 因恐惧而颤抖  
3. *a loud round of applause* — 热烈的掌声  
4. *courage beats fear* — 勇气战胜恐惧  
5. *a more independent person* — 更独立的人  
6. *step outside his comfort zone* — 走出舒适区  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的未来的我，

写这封信时，我即将离开初中，踏入人生全新的篇章。

回首过去三年，那些日子令人难忘。我仍然记得那次英语演讲比赛——我站在舞台上因害怕而发抖，但最终在热烈的掌声中完成了演讲。那一刻教会了我：勇气每次都能战胜恐惧。

对于高中，我既兴奋又紧张。我期待交到新朋友、探索新学科、成为一个更独立的人。我知道会有挑战，但我相信自己能应对。

所以，未来的我，这是我想对你说的：永远不要停止学习，永远不要停止梦想，也永远不要忘记那个曾经勇敢走出舒适区的少年。

保持坚强，继续微笑！

来自 2027 年的李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| a brand-new chapter | 全新的篇章 | High school is a brand-new chapter in life. |
| courage beats fear | 勇气战胜恐惧 | Remember, courage always beats fear. |
| step outside one's comfort zone | 走出舒适区 | Growth begins when you step outside your comfort zone. |
| look forward to | 期待 | I look forward to the new adventure ahead. |
| never stop dreaming | 永不停止梦想 | No matter how old you are, never stop dreaming. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 即将 | be about ✅ | be about to + 动词原形 |
| 回首 | look back ✅ | look back / looking back（分词结构） |
| 掌声 | a applause | a round of applause（量词） |
| 舒适区 | comfortable zone | comfort zone |
| 独立 | independant | independent |

### 升格建议（3条）

|------|----------|----------|
| 回顾 | Middle school was great. | Middle school was a garden where friendships bloomed and lessons grew roots. |
