# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，新年即将来临，你校英语广播站邀请你分享"新年愿望"。请你写一篇广播稿，内容包括：

① 回顾过去一年中一件让你成长的事；  
② 你的新年愿望（至少两个）；  
③ 对同学们的新年寄语。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（广播稿） |
| 话题 | 新年愿望 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般过去时+一般将来时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Good afternoon, everyone! This is Li Hua from Class 5.

As the new year approaches, I'd like to share my reflections and wishes.

Looking back, the moment that helped me grow the most was losing the school debate competition in March. I had prepared hard but still lost in the semi-finals. It was painful, but it taught me that losing gracefully is just as important as winning.

For the new year, I have two wishes. First, I want to improve my time management — less scrolling, more reading. Second, I want to become a kinder person by doing at least one good deed every week.

To all my schoolmates, here is my new year message: don't be afraid to set big dreams, but don't forget to take small steps every day. The person you become at the end of next year depends on the habits you build today.

Happy New Year to everyone! Let's make it our best year yet!

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *as the new year approaches* — 随着新年临近  
2. *losing gracefully* — 优雅地面对失败  
3. *less scrolling, more reading* — 少刷手机，多读书  
4. *at least one good deed* — 至少一件好事  
5. *the person you become* — 你成为的那个人  
6. *our best year yet* — 到目前为止最好的一年  

---

## 全文翻译

大家下午好！我是五班的李华。

新年即将来临，我想分享一下我的回顾和心愿。

回顾过去，让我成长最多的时刻是三月份输掉学校辩论赛。我准备得很充分，但在半决赛中失利了。那很痛苦，但它教会了我，优雅地面对失败和赢得比赛同样重要。

新年里我有两个愿望。第一，我想改善时间管理——少刷手机，多读书。第二，我想成为一个更善良的人，每周至少做一件好事。

给所有同学的新年寄语：不要害怕设立远大梦想，但也不要忘记每天走出小小的一步。明年年底你成为什么样的人，取决于你今天养成的习惯。

新年快乐！让我们一起把它变成迄今为止最好的一年！

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| as … approaches | 随着……临近 | As winter approaches, the days get shorter. |
| lose gracefully | 优雅地面对失败 | A true winner knows how to lose gracefully. |
| set big dreams | 设立远大梦想 | Don't be afraid to set big dreams. |
| take small steps | 走小步 | Great journeys begin with small steps. |
| make it count | 让它有价值 | We only have one chance — make it count. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 临近 | approach ✅ | approaches（三单）/ as … approaches |
| 优雅地 | graceful (副词) | gracefully |
| 至少 | at least ✅ | at least + 数词 |
| 取决于 | depend on ✅ | depends on（三单） |
| 到目前为止 | yet ✅ | yet（用在最高级后面） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 回顾 | I lost a debate and learned a lot. | Losing that debate was like swallowing bitter medicine — unpleasant going down, but exactly what I needed to grow. |

| 寄语 | Work hard next year. | A new year is a blank notebook — write bravely, edit kindly, and by December, you'll have a story worth telling. |
