# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，英语课上老师请同学们以"我最想感谢的人"为题写一篇短文。请你写一篇，内容包括：

① 你最想感谢的人是谁；  
② 他/她为你做了什么让你感动的事；  
③ 你想对他/她说什么。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 记叙文（感恩类） |
| 话题 | 感恩 |
| 人称 | 第一/第三人称 |
| 时态 | 一般过去时+一般现在时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

The person I want to thank most is my father.

My father is a quiet man who doesn't talk much, but he shows his love through actions. Every morning, he wakes up at five o'clock to prepare breakfast for me before I go to school. Every evening, he waits at the school gate no matter how cold or rainy it is. He never complains, and he never asks for anything in return.

One moment I will never forget was the night before my final exam. I was too nervous to sleep, so my father sat beside me and told me stories about his childhood. His gentle voice calmed me down, and I fell asleep feeling safe and loved.

Dad, I may not say it often, but I am grateful for everything you do. You are the silent hero of my life, and I love you more than words can say.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *shows his love through actions* — 用行动表达爱  
2. *no matter how cold or rainy* — 无论多冷多大的雨  
3. *never asks for anything in return* — 从不求回报  
4. *too nervous to sleep* — 紧张得睡不着  
5. *calmed me down* — 让我平静下来  
6. *more than words can say* — 无法用语言表达  

---

## 全文翻译

我最想感谢的人是我的爸爸。

爸爸是一个沉默寡言的人，但他用行动表达爱。每天早上他五点就起床给我准备早餐。每天傍晚，不管天多冷、雨多大，他都在校门口等我。他从不抱怨，也从不求回报。

有一个时刻我永远不会忘记——期末考试前一天晚上。我紧张得睡不着，爸爸就坐在我身旁给我讲他小时候的故事。他温柔的声音让我平静下来，我在安心和被爱的感觉中睡着了。

爸爸，我也许不常说，但我感激你所做的一切。你是我生命中无声的英雄，我爱你，胜过言语所能表达。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| show love through actions | 用行动表达爱 | Actions speak louder than words. |
| no matter how | 无论多么 | He comes no matter how far it is. |
| in return | 作为回报 | He never expects anything in return. |
| too … to | 太……以至于不能 | I was too tired to move. |
| more than words can say | 无法言表 | My gratitude is more than words can say. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 感激 | grateful for ✅ | grateful for sth. / grateful to sb. |
| 太……而不能 | too nervous to sleeping | too nervous to sleep（to+原形） |
| 无论 | no matter ✅ | no matter how / what / where + 从句 |
| 求回报 | ask for return | ask for anything in return |
| 起床 | wake up ✅ | wake up / get up（注意区别） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 描写 | My father is kind and quiet. | My father's love is like the morning sun — warm, steady and always there before I even open my eyes. |
| 事件 | He told me stories at night. | That night, his stories became a lullaby, and his presence became a blanket that wrapped me in safety. |
| 感恩 | Thank you, Dad. | Dad, you carry the weight of our family on your shoulders and never let us see you sweat — that is the bravest kind of love. |
