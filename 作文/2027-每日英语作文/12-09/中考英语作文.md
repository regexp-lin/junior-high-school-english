# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，英语课上老师让大家写一篇"我最喜欢的季节"的短文。请你写一篇，内容包括：

① 你最喜欢的季节是什么；  
② 这个季节有哪些特点、你通常做什么；  
③ 你喜欢这个季节的原因。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 说明文+记叙文 |
| 话题 | 最喜欢的季节 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Among the four seasons, autumn is my favourite.

In autumn, the weather is cool and comfortable — neither too hot nor too cold. The leaves turn golden, red and orange, painting the world like a watercolour masterpiece. The air smells of ripe fruits and fallen leaves, and the sky is usually clear and blue.

I love what autumn lets me do. On weekends, I go hiking with my family in the nearby mountains. Walking through a path covered with colourful leaves feels like stepping into a fairy tale. I also enjoy eating seasonal fruits like oranges, persimmons and chestnuts.

Autumn is a season of harvest. It reminds me that hard work in spring and summer always leads to something beautiful. Just as farmers collect their crops, we should look back and appreciate the progress we have made.

That is why autumn holds a special place in my heart.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *neither too hot nor too cold* — 不太热也不太冷  
2. *painting the world like a watercolour masterpiece* — 像水彩画一样描绘世界  
3. *smells of ripe fruits* — 闻起来有成熟水果的味道  
4. *stepping into a fairy tale* — 走进童话  
5. *a season of harvest* — 收获的季节  
6. *holds a special place in my heart* — 在我心中有特殊位置  

---

## 全文翻译

四季之中，我最喜欢秋天。

秋天天气凉爽舒适——不太热也不太冷。树叶变成金色、红色和橙色，把世界描绘成一幅水彩画杰作。空气中弥漫着成熟水果和落叶的气息，天空通常清澈湛蓝。

我喜欢秋天能做的事情。周末我和家人去附近的山上徒步。走在铺满五彩落叶的小路上，感觉像走进了童话世界。我也喜欢吃应季水果，比如橘子、柿子和栗子。

秋天是收获的季节。它提醒我，春夏的辛勤劳动总会带来美好的成果。就像农民收获庄稼一样，我们也应该回头欣赏自己取得的进步。

这就是秋天在我心中有特殊位置的原因。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| neither … nor | 既不……也不 | The weather is neither too hot nor too cold. |
| smell of | 闻起来有……味道 | The kitchen smells of fresh bread. |
| step into | 走进 | It felt like stepping into a dream. |
| a season of harvest | 收获的季节 | Autumn is a season of harvest and gratitude. |
| hold a special place in | 在……有特殊位置 | This song holds a special place in my heart. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 既不……也不 | neither…or | neither … nor |
| 树叶变 | leaves become golden | leaves turn golden（颜色变化用turn） |
| 闻起来 | smells like ✅ | smells of / smells like（用法不同） |
| 应季的 | season fruit | seasonal fruit |
| 在……之中 | in the four seasons | among the four seasons |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 描写 | The leaves turn colourful. | Autumn dresses the trees in gold and crimson, like a painter who cannot choose just one colour. |
| 活动 | I go hiking in autumn. | Walking through autumn mountains is like turning the pages of nature's most beautiful picture book. |
| 感悟 | Autumn is a harvest season. | Autumn teaches a gentle lesson: every fruit on the tree was once a flower that trusted the sun and waited through the rain. |
