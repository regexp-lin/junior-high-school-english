# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校正在开展"感恩父母"主题活动。请你用英语写一篇短文，内容包括：

① 父母为你做了哪些事（至少两件）；  
② 描述一件让你最感动的事；  
③ 你打算如何回报父母。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

## 参考范文

Parents are the greatest people in the world. They give us love without asking for anything in return.

My parents do a lot for me every day. My mum gets up early to cook breakfast, and my dad drives me to school no matter how bad the weather is. They always put my needs before their own.

I will never forget one night last winter. I had a high fever and could not stop shaking. My mum sat beside my bed the whole night, checking my temperature again and again. When I woke up the next morning, I saw her tired eyes and felt my own eyes fill with tears.

From now on, I will study harder and help with housework. Most importantly, I want to spend more time with them and say "I love you" more often. Actions speak louder than words, and I hope my love can warm their hearts.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面
父母付出（做早饭、接送上学）；感动的事（发烧妈妈整夜照顾）；回报（学习、家务、陪伴），三点齐全。

### 结构方面
总起—日常付出—感动事件—回报计划，层次递进，感情真挚。

### 语言方面
1. *without asking for anything in return* — 不求任何回报  
2. *no matter how bad the weather is* — 无论天气多糟  
3. *put my needs before their own* — 把我的需求放在自己之前  
4. *could not stop shaking* — 止不住地发抖  
5. *felt my own eyes fill with tears* — 感到自己的眼睛充满泪水  
6. *Actions speak louder than words* — 行动胜于言辞  

---

## 全文翻译

父母是世界上最伟大的人。他们给予我们爱，却从不要求任何回报。

我的父母每天为我做了很多事。妈妈早起做早饭，爸爸无论天气多糟糕都开车送我上学。他们总是把我的需求放在自己之前。

我永远不会忘记去年冬天的一个夜晚。我发了高烧，止不住地发抖。妈妈整夜坐在我的床边，一遍又一遍地给我量体温。第二天早上醒来，我看到她疲惫的双眼，感到自己的眼眶湿润了。

从现在起，我要更加努力学习，帮忙做家务。最重要的是，我想多花时间陪伴他们，更常说"我爱你"。行动胜于言辞，我希望我的爱能温暖他们的心。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| without asking for anything in return | 不求回报 | They help me without asking for anything in return. |
| no matter how | 无论怎样 | I will support you no matter how hard it is. |
| fill with tears | 充满泪水 | Her eyes filled with tears of joy. |
| from now on | 从现在起 | From now on, I will be more responsible. |
| actions speak louder than words | 行动胜于言辞 | Remember, actions speak louder than words. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 感恩 | thanks for parents | be grateful to parents / thank parents |
| 发烧 | I got a high fever. ✅ | I had a high fever. |
| 整夜 | the all night | the whole night / all night |
| 回报 | return them | repay them / do something in return |
| 说"我爱你" | say "I love you" to them more often ✅ | say "I love you" more often |
