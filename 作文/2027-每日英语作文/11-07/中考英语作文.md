# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校英语报刊正在征稿，主题为"怎样交到好朋友"。请你写一篇短文投稿，内容包括：

① 好朋友应该具备什么品质；  
② 你是怎么交到你最好的朋友的；  
③ 你对友谊的理解。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 议论文+记叙文 |
| 话题 | 交友 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般过去时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

What makes a good friend? In my opinion, a good friend should be honest, kind and supportive. Someone who listens when you are upset and cheers you up when you feel down — that is a true friend.

I met my best friend, Xiao Wei, on the first day of middle school. We were both shy and sat alone at lunch. I offered to share my snacks, and he smiled. That small moment was the beginning of a friendship that has lasted three years.

Since then, we have studied together, laughed together and even argued a few times. But we always make up quickly because we respect each other.

I believe true friendship is not about being perfect — it is about being real. A friend who stays through the storms is worth more than a hundred who only come for the sunshine.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *honest, kind and supportive* — 诚实、善良、互相支持  
2. *cheers you up* — 让你振作起来  
3. *that small moment* — 那个小小的瞬间  
4. *make up quickly* — 很快和好  
5. *being real* — 真实地做自己  
6. *stays through the storms* — 在风雨中留下来  

---

## 全文翻译

什么样的人才是好朋友？在我看来，好朋友应该诚实、善良、互相支持。当你难过时愿意倾听，当你低落时给你打气——那才是真正的朋友。

我在初中开学第一天认识了我最好的朋友小伟。我们都很害羞，午饭时独自坐着。我主动分享了我的零食，他笑了。那个小小的瞬间就是一段持续三年友谊的开始。

从那以后，我们一起学习、一起大笑，甚至吵过几次架。但我们总是很快和好，因为我们互相尊重。

我相信真正的友谊不在于完美——而在于真实。一个在风雨中留下的朋友，胜过一百个只为阳光而来的人。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| cheer sb. up | 使某人振作 | A good friend always cheers you up. |
| make up | 和好 | We argued but soon made up. |
| respect each other | 互相尊重 | Friends should respect each other. |
| last (v.) | 持续 | True friendship lasts forever. |
| be worth | 值得 | A loyal friend is worth everything. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 互相 | each others | each other（无复数） |
| 和好 | make up ✅ | make up（注意与 make up for 区别） |
| 持续 | has lasted for three years ✅ | has lasted three years（for 可省略） |
| 值得 | is worth than | is worth more than |
| 吵架 | argued with a few times | argued a few times |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 品质 | A good friend is kind. | A good friend is a mirror that shows you who you truly are and still chooses to stand beside you. |
| 经历 | We became friends at school. | Friendship found us in the most ordinary moment — a shared snack and an unspoken promise to never eat lunch alone again. |
| 感悟 | Friends are important. | True friendship is not a shelter from the storm — it is two people learning to dance in the rain together. |
