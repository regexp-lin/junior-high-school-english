# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校正在开展"绿色校园"环保活动。请你写一篇倡议书，内容包括：

① 校园中常见的环境问题（如浪费水电、乱扔垃圾等）；  
② 你认为应该怎样做来改善这些问题（至少两条建议）；  
③ 号召同学们一起行动。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 参考范文

Dear fellow students,

Our school is launching the "Green Campus" campaign, and I'd like to share some thoughts with you.

At present, there are several environmental problems on campus. Some students leave the lights and fans on when they leave the classroom. Others throw rubbish on the ground instead of putting it in the bins. Water is often wasted in the washrooms because taps are left running.

To build a greener campus, I have the following suggestions. First, turn off the lights and electrical appliances before leaving any room. Second, sort your rubbish into recyclable and non-recyclable categories. Third, use reusable water bottles instead of plastic ones.

Small changes can make a big difference. Let's start today and turn our school into a place we can all be proud of!

Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *launching the "Green Campus" campaign* — 发起"绿色校园"活动  
2. *instead of putting it in the bins* — 而不是扔进垃圾桶  
3. *taps are left running* — 水龙头一直开着  
4. *electrical appliances* — 电器  
5. *recyclable and non-recyclable* — 可回收和不可回收  
6. *a place we can all be proud of* — 一个我们都能引以为豪的地方  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的同学们，

我校正在开展"绿色校园"活动，我想和大家分享一些想法。

目前，校园里存在一些环境问题。有些同学离开教室时不关灯和风扇。还有人把垃圾扔在地上而不是放进垃圾桶。洗手间的水龙头经常没关，导致浪费水资源。

为了建设更绿色的校园，我有以下建议。第一，离开任何房间前关掉灯和电器。第二，把垃圾分类为可回收和不可回收。第三，使用可重复使用的水壶代替塑料瓶。

小小的改变能产生大大的不同。让我们从今天开始，把学校变成一个我们都能引以为豪的地方！

李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| make a big difference | 产生很大影响 | Recycling can make a big difference. |
| instead of | 而不是 | Walk instead of driving to reduce pollution. |
| be proud of | 为……自豪 | We should be proud of our green campus. |
| turn off | 关掉 | Always turn off the lights when you leave. |
| sort rubbish | 垃圾分类 | We should sort rubbish into different bins. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 垃圾 | rubbish ✅ | rubbish (英) / garbage (美) |
| 垃圾桶 | rubbish box | rubbish bin (英) / trash can (美) |
| 代替 | instead + 名词 | instead of + 名词/动名词 |
| 水龙头 | water tap ✅ | tap / faucet |
| 可回收 | can recycle | recyclable（形容词） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|

| 号召 | Let's protect the environment. | The greenest campus begins with the smallest habit — and that habit begins with you, right now. |
