# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校英语社团举办了一次英语演讲比赛。请你写一篇短文记录这次经历，内容包括：

① 比赛的主题和你的准备过程；  
② 比赛当天发生的事以及你的表现；  
③ 你从这次比赛中学到了什么。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |

| 话题 | 一次英语演讲比赛 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |

| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Last month, our English club held a speech contest with the theme "The Power of Kindness". I decided to take part, even though public speaking terrified me.

I spent two weeks preparing my speech. I wrote draft after draft, practised in front of the mirror and asked my English teacher for feedback. The night before the contest, I rehearsed ten times until I could deliver the speech without looking at my notes.

On the day of the contest, my heart was pounding as I walked onto the stage. My voice trembled for the first few seconds, but once I made eye contact with a smiling friend in the audience, I relaxed. I spoke clearly, paused at the right moments and ended with a powerful quote. I won second place!

This experience taught me that courage is not the absence of fear — it is choosing to act despite the fear. I'm already looking forward to the next contest.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *terrified me* — 让我害怕  
2. *draft after draft* — 一稿又一稿  
3. *my heart was pounding* — 心怦怦跳  
4. *made eye contact* — 进行了眼神交流  
5. *paused at the right moments* — 在恰当的时候停顿  
6. *the absence of fear* — 恐惧的缺失  

---

## 全文翻译

上个月，我们英语社团举办了一次演讲比赛，主题是"善良的力量"。我决定参加，尽管公开演讲让我害怕。

我花了两周准备演讲稿。我写了一稿又一稿，对着镜子练习，还向英语老师请教。比赛前一晚，我排练了十遍，直到不用看稿子也能完整演讲。

比赛当天，走上台时我心怦怦跳。前几秒我的声音在颤抖，但当我和观众中一位微笑的朋友眼神交流后，我放松了下来。我表达清晰，在恰当的时候停顿，最后以一句有力的名言结尾。我获得了第二名！

这次经历教会了我，勇气不是没有恐惧——而是在恐惧中选择行动。我已经在期待下一次比赛了。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| take part in | 参加 | I took part in the writing contest. |
| my heart was pounding | 心怦怦跳 | My heart was pounding before the interview. |
| make eye contact | 眼神交流 | Making eye contact shows confidence. |
| the absence of | ……的缺失 | Silence is not the absence of sound. |
| look forward to | 期待 | I'm looking forward to the school trip. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 参加 | take part of | take part in |
| 颤抖 | trembled ✅ | trembled（过去式） |
| 眼神交流 | made eye contact with ✅ | make eye contact with sb. |
| 停顿 | paused ✅ | paused（注意发音） |
| 尽管 | even though ✅ | even though / although |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 准备 | I practised many times. | I rehearsed until my reflection in the mirror could have given the speech on its own. |
| 表现 | I was nervous but I did well. | Fear held the microphone first, but courage gently took it from fear's shaking hands. |
| 感悟 | I learned to be brave. | A stage is just a few square metres of floor — but standing on it taught me more about myself than a year of textbooks ever could. |
