# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你班正在开展"我的梦想职业"主题班会。请你写一篇发言稿，内容包括：

① 你的梦想职业是什么；  
② 你为什么选择这个职业；  
③ 你打算怎样为实现梦想而努力。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（发言稿） |
| 话题 | 梦想职业 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般将来时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Good afternoon, everyone!

My dream job is to become a doctor. It has been my dream ever since I was a child.

When I was seven, my grandmother was seriously ill and stayed in hospital for two months. I watched the doctors and nurses take care of her patiently every single day. Thanks to them, she recovered. At that moment, I told myself that I wanted to be someone who saves lives and brings hope to families.

To achieve this dream, I know I need to work hard. First, I will study science and biology carefully in school. Second, I will read more books about medicine. Third, I will keep a healthy body so that I have the energy to help others in the future.

I believe that with hard work and determination, my dream will come true one day.

Thank you!

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *ever since I was a child* — 从小就  
2. *seriously ill* — 病得很重  
3. *patiently every single day* — 每一天都耐心地  
4. *saves lives and brings hope* — 救死扶伤、带来希望  
5. *achieve this dream* — 实现这个梦想  
6. *with hard work and determination* — 凭借努力和决心  

---

## 全文翻译

大家下午好！

我的梦想职业是成为一名医生。这是我从小就有的梦想。

七岁那年，奶奶生了重病，住了两个月的院。我看着医生和护士每天耐心地照顾她。多亏了他们，奶奶康复了。那一刻，我告诉自己，我要成为那种能拯救生命、为家庭带来希望的人。

为了实现这个梦想，我知道我必须努力学习。首先，我会在学校认真学习科学和生物。其次，我会多读关于医学的书。第三，我会保持健康的身体，这样将来才有精力去帮助别人。

我相信凭借努力和决心，我的梦想终有一天会实现。

谢谢大家！

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| ever since | 从……以来 | I have loved music ever since I was little. |
| thanks to | 多亏了 | Thanks to his help, I passed the exam. |
| achieve one's dream | 实现梦想 | Work hard and you will achieve your dream. |
| come true | 实现 | My wish finally came true. |
| determination | 决心 | Success requires both talent and determination. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 从小 | since a child | since I was a child |
| 生重病 | was serious ill | was seriously ill（副词修饰形容词） |
| 实现 | My dream will be come true | My dream will come true（不用被动） |
| 多亏 | thanks for | thanks to（表原因） |
| 决心 | determination ✅ | determination（注意拼写） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 引入 | I want to be a doctor. | If I could choose one superpower, it would not be flight or invisibility — it would be the power to heal. |
| 原因 | Doctors help sick people. | A doctor does more than cure diseases — a doctor mends the broken hopes of a worried family. |
| 努力 | I will study hard. | Every page I turn in my textbook today is one step closer to the white coat I dream of wearing tomorrow. |
