# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你的英国笔友 Tom 想来中国旅游，请你给他写一封邮件介绍你的家乡，内容包括：

① 你的家乡在哪里、有什么特色；  
② 推荐一两个值得参观的景点或体验；  
③ 你希望他来的时间以及原因。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

## 参考范文

Dear Tom,

I'm so glad to hear that you want to visit China! Let me introduce my hometown to you.

I live in Guangzhou, a beautiful city in southern China. It is famous for its delicious food, such as dim sum and roast goose. People often call it the "City of Flowers" because there are flowers blooming all year round.

If you come, I strongly recommend visiting the Canton Tower at night — the view of the city lights is absolutely breathtaking. You should also take a boat ride along the Pearl River to enjoy the cool breeze and beautiful scenery.

I suggest you visit in October or November, when the weather is warm and comfortable — perfect for sightseeing.

I can't wait to show you around!

Yours,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *famous for its delicious food* — 以美食闻名  
2. *flowers blooming all year round* — 四季鲜花盛开  
3. *absolutely breathtaking* — 绝对令人叹为观止  
4. *take a boat ride along the Pearl River* — 沿珠江乘船游览  
5. *perfect for sightseeing* — 非常适合观光  
6. *can't wait to show you around* — 迫不及待地带你参观  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的Tom，

听说你想来中国旅游，我太高兴了！让我来介绍一下我的家乡。

我住在广州，一座中国南方的美丽城市。广州以美食闻名，比如早茶点心和烧鹅。人们常常称它为"花城"，因为这里四季鲜花盛开。

如果你来，我强烈推荐晚上去参观广州塔——城市灯光的景色绝对令人叹为观止。你也应该沿珠江乘船游览，享受凉爽的微风和美丽的风景。

我建议你在十月或十一月来，那时天气温暖舒适——非常适合观光。

我迫不及待地想带你四处逛逛！

你的，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| be famous for | 以……闻名 | Hangzhou is famous for its West Lake. |
| all year round | 一年四季 | The park is open all year round. |
| breathtaking | 令人叹为观止的 | The sunset view was breathtaking. |
| show sb. around | 带某人参观 | Let me show you around our campus. |
| can't wait to | 迫不及待 | I can't wait to see you again. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 以……闻名 | famous of | famous for |
| 建议 | suggest you to visit | suggest you visit（虚拟语气） |
| 风景 | sceneries | scenery（不可数） |
| 迫不及待 | can't wait to do ✅ | can't wait to + 动词原形 |
| 南方 | south China ✅ | southern China / south China |
