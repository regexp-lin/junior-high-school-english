# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，冬天来了，你的英国笔友 Emma 想知道中国学生在冬天通常做些什么。请你写一封回信，内容包括：

① 你们那里的冬天天气怎样；  
② 你在冬天最喜欢的活动（至少两个）；  
③ 邀请她有机会来体验。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（回信） |
| 话题 | 冬天的活动 |
| 人称 | 第一/第二人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般将来时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Emma,

Thanks for your letter! I'd love to tell you about winter in my city.

Winter here in northern China is very cold. The temperature can drop to minus ten degrees, and we often get heavy snowfall. But the cold doesn't stop us from having fun!

My favourite winter activity is ice skating on the frozen lake in our city park. Gliding across the smooth ice under a clear blue sky feels absolutely wonderful. I also love making snowmen with my little sister. We spend hours rolling giant snowballs and decorating them with scarves, carrots and buttons. After playing outside, we warm up with a bowl of hot dumplings — that's the best part!

If you ever visit China in winter, I'll take you to try all of these. Trust me, Chinese winter fun is something you don't want to miss!

Yours,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *drop to minus ten degrees* — 降到零下十度  
2. *heavy snowfall* — 大雪  
3. *gliding across the smooth ice* — 在光滑的冰面上滑行  
4. *rolling giant snowballs* — 滚巨大的雪球  
5. *warm up with* — 用……暖身  
6. *something you don't want to miss* — 你不想错过的事  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的Emma，

谢谢你的来信！我很乐意告诉你我们这里的冬天。

中国北方的冬天非常冷。气温可以降到零下十度，经常下大雪。但寒冷挡不住我们的乐趣！

我最喜欢的冬天活动是在城市公园的结冰湖面上滑冰。在清澈的蓝天下，在光滑的冰面上滑行，感觉棒极了。我也喜欢和妹妹一起堆雪人。我们花好几个小时滚巨大的雪球，用围巾、胡萝卜和纽扣装饰。在外面玩完之后，我们用一碗热腾腾的饺子暖身——这是最棒的部分！

如果你冬天来中国，我会带你体验这一切。相信我，中国的冬日趣事你绝对不想错过！

你的，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| drop to | 降到 | The temperature can drop to zero. |
| glide across | 滑过 | We glided across the frozen lake. |
| warm up with | 用……暖身 | Let's warm up with some hot cocoa. |
| spend time doing | 花时间做 | I spent hours making a snowman. |
| something you don't want to miss | 不想错过的事 | The show is something you don't want to miss. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 零下 | under zero | below zero / minus（更常用） |
| 大雪 | big snow | heavy snowfall / heavy snow |
| 滑冰 | ice skate ✅ | ice skating（名词）/ go ice skating |
| 堆雪人 | make a snowman ✅ | make / build a snowman |
| 气温 | temperature ✅ | temperature（注意拼写） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 天气 | It is very cold in winter. | Winter wraps our city in a white blanket and paints every rooftop with frost. |
| 活动 | I like ice skating. | Skating on a frozen lake is like dancing with the winter wind — cold on the outside, warm on the inside. |
| 邀请 | I hope you can come. | Pack your thickest coat and warmest smile — Chinese winter has adventures that no amount of snow can bury. |
