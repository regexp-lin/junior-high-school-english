# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

如今越来越多的中学生拥有手机。假设你是李华，请以"Should Middle School Students Use Mobile Phones?"为题写一篇短文。内容包括：

① 手机给中学生带来的好处  
② 手机可能带来的问题  
③ 你的观点和建议  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；可适当发挥；不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 参考范文

**Should Middle School Students Use Mobile Phones?**

Nowadays, more and more students own mobile phones. Opinions are divided on whether this is a good thing.

On one hand, mobile phones bring us many conveniences. We can keep in touch with our parents easily and search for information to help with our study. Many useful learning apps are also available on phones.

On the other hand, phones can cause problems too. Some students spend too much time on social media or games, which badly affects their study. Staring at the screen for long also damages our eyesight.

In my opinion, students can use phones, but we must use them wisely. We should set time limits and never use phones during class. After all, it's not the phone that's the problem — it's how we use it.

（约125词）

## 全文翻译

**中学生应该使用手机吗？**

如今，越来越多的学生拥有手机。对于这是否是好事，人们看法不一。

一方面，手机给我们带来了很多便利。我们可以方便地与父母保持联系，还能搜索信息帮助学习。手机上还有很多有用的学习应用。

另一方面，手机也会带来问题。有些学生花太多时间在社交媒体或游戏上，严重影响了学习。长时间盯着屏幕也会损害视力。

在我看来，学生可以使用手机，但必须明智地使用。我们应该设定时间限制，上课时绝不使用手机。毕竟，问题不在于手机本身，而在于我们怎么使用它。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| Opinions are divided on | 对……看法不一 | Opinions are divided on this issue. |
| keep in touch with | 与……保持联系 | We can keep in touch with parents easily. |
| badly affects | 严重影响 | Phone addiction badly affects study. |
| set time limits | 设定时间限制 | We should set time limits for phone use. |
| it's how we use it | 关键在于怎么用 | It's not the phone — it's how we use it. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| opinion 搭配 | Opinions are different about | Opinions are divided on（divided on 更地道） |
| keep in touch | keep touch with | keep in touch with（in 不可省） |
| affect 词性 | badly effect our study | badly affect our study（affect 是动词，effect 是名词） |
| available 位置 | available apps are many | Many apps are available（available 放 be 动词后） |
| stare at 搭配 | stare on the screen | stare at the screen（stare at 固定搭配） |
