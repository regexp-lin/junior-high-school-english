# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你所在的社区正在招募青少年志愿者，帮助改善社区环境。请你写一封申请信，内容包括：

① 你想参加的原因；  
② 你能为社区做什么（至少两个方面）；  
③ 你对社区服务的看法。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（申请信） |
| 话题 | 为社区做贡献 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般将来时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Community Centre,

I am Li Hua, a Grade 9 student. I am writing to apply for the youth volunteer position in our neighbourhood.

I want to join because I believe every young person should contribute to the community they live in. As the saying goes, "A community is only as strong as its members."

If selected, I can help in several ways. First, I am good at organising events, so I could assist with weekend clean-up activities and recycling drives. Second, I enjoy working with children and the elderly, so I could read to kids at the community library or help elderly neighbours with their shopping.

I believe community service builds a sense of belonging and teaches us to care for those around us. It is not just about giving — it is about growing together.

I look forward to your reply. Thank you for considering my application!

Yours sincerely,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *apply for* — 申请  
2. *contribute to* — 为……做贡献  
3. *as the saying goes* — 俗话说  
4. *recycling drives* — 回收活动  
5. *a sense of belonging* — 归属感  
6. *growing together* — 共同成长  

---

## 全文翻译

尊敬的社区中心，

我是李华，一名初三学生。我写信申请我们社区的青少年志愿者岗位。

我想加入是因为我相信每个年轻人都应该为自己所在的社区做贡献。俗话说："一个社区的力量取决于它的成员。"

如果被选中，我能在几个方面提供帮助。第一，我擅长组织活动，可以协助周末的清洁活动和回收活动。第二，我喜欢与孩子和老人相处，可以在社区图书馆给小朋友读书或帮助老年邻居购物。

我相信社区服务能建立归属感，教会我们关爱身边的人。这不仅仅是付出——更是共同成长。

期待您的回复。感谢考虑我的申请！

您真诚的，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| apply for | 申请 | I want to apply for this job. |
| contribute to | 贡献 | Everyone can contribute to society. |
| a sense of belonging | 归属感 | Volunteering gives me a sense of belonging. |
| if selected | 如果被选中 | If selected, I will do my best. |
| care for | 关爱 | We should care for the elderly. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 申请 | apply to a position | apply for a position |
| 贡献 | contribute for | contribute to |
| 擅长 | good at organise | good at organising（介词+动名词） |
| 归属感 | belonging sense | sense of belonging |
| 考虑 | consider ✅ | consider + 名词/动名词 |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 原因 | I want to help the community. | I grew up on these streets, and now it's my turn to give something back to the neighbourhood that shaped me. |
