# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你们班正在讨论"中学生该不该带手机上学"。请你写一篇短文表达你的观点，内容包括：

① 手机给中学生带来的好处；  
② 手机带来的问题或隐患；  
③ 你的建议或看法。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 参考范文

Should middle school students bring mobile phones to school? This is a hot topic in our class.

Some students think phones are helpful. They can use educational apps to study, look up new words in dictionaries and contact their parents in emergencies. I agree that phones can be useful tools.

However, the problems are hard to ignore. Many students secretly play games or scroll through social media during class, which seriously affects their learning. Some even stay up late chatting, leading to poor sleep and low grades. In addition, expensive phones may cause comparison and jealousy among classmates.

In my view, students can bring phones to school but should hand them in to the teacher before class and collect them afterwards. This way, we can enjoy the benefits of phones without being distracted by them.

Technology is a good servant but a bad master — the key is self-control.

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *a hot topic* — 热门话题  
2. *secretly play games* — 偷偷玩游戏  
3. *scroll through social media* — 刷社交媒体  
4. *seriously affects their learning* — 严重影响学习  
5. *cause comparison and jealousy* — 引起攀比和嫉妒  
6. *a good servant but a bad master* — 好仆人但坏主人  

---

## 全文翻译

中学生该不该带手机上学？这是我们班的热门话题。

一些同学认为手机有帮助。他们可以用教育类APP学习、查词典里的生词、紧急情况下联系父母。我同意手机可以是有用的工具。

然而，问题也难以忽视。很多学生上课偷偷玩游戏或刷社交媒体，严重影响了学习。有些人甚至熬夜聊天，导致睡眠差、成绩低。此外，昂贵的手机可能在同学间引起攀比和嫉妒。

在我看来，学生可以带手机到学校，但上课前应该交给老师，课后再取回。这样我们就能享受手机的好处而不被它分心。

科技是好仆人但坏主人——关键在于自控力。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| a hot topic | 热门话题 | Climate change is a hot topic worldwide. |
| hard to ignore | 难以忽视 | The problems are hard to ignore. |
| in emergencies | 在紧急情况下 | Call 120 in emergencies. |
| self-control | 自控力 | Using phones wisely requires self-control. |
| be distracted by | 被……分心 | Don't be distracted by social media. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 影响 | effects their learning | affects their learning（动词用affect） |
| 导致 | leading to ✅ | leading to + 名词/动名词 |
| 嫉妒 | jealousy ✅ | jealousy（名词）/ jealous（形容词） |
| 取回 | collect them ✅ | collect / pick up |
| 偷偷地 | secret play | secretly play（副词） |
