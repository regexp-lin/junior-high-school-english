# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你最近看了一部让你印象深刻的电影。请你写一篇短文推荐给同学们，内容包括：

① 电影的名称和大致故事情节；  
② 你最喜欢的角色或场景；  
③ 你推荐的理由。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 说明文（推荐类） |
| 话题 | 推荐一部电影 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般过去时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

I'd like to recommend a film called "Coco", a Disney-Pixar animated movie.

The story follows Miguel, a Mexican boy who dreams of becoming a musician. But his family has banned music for generations. On the Day of the Dead, Miguel accidentally enters the Land of the Dead, where he meets his ancestors and discovers a hidden family secret.

My favourite scene is the ending, where Miguel sings "Remember Me" to his great-grandmother, and she finally remembers him. I cried because it showed the deep bond between family members that not even death can break.

I recommend this film because it reminds us to cherish our families while we can. The animation is stunning, the music is beautiful and the message is unforgettable: the real death is not when our bodies stop — it is when no one remembers us.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *dreams of becoming* — 梦想成为  
2. *banned music for generations* — 几代人禁止音乐  
3. *the Land of the Dead* — 亡灵之地  
4. *a hidden family secret* — 一个隐藏的家族秘密  
5. *the deep bond between* — 之间深厚的纽带  
6. *cherish our families while we can* — 趁我们还能时珍惜家人  

---

## 全文翻译

我想推荐一部电影叫《寻梦环游记》，是一部迪士尼·皮克斯的动画电影。

故事讲的是一个叫米格的墨西哥男孩，他梦想成为音乐家。但他的家族几代人都禁止音乐。在亡灵节那天，米格意外进入了亡灵之地，在那里他遇到了祖先并发现了一个隐藏的家族秘密。

我最喜欢的场景是结尾，米格对曾祖母唱了《请记住我》，她终于想起了他。我哭了，因为它展现了家人之间连死亡都无法割断的深厚纽带。

我推荐这部电影因为它提醒我们趁还能的时候珍惜家人。动画精美，音乐动听，传达的信息令人难忘：真正的死亡不是身体停止——而是没有人再记得我们。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| dream of | 梦想 | She dreams of travelling the world. |
| not even | 甚至不 | Not even distance can break our friendship. |
| cherish | 珍惜 | Cherish every moment with your family. |
| stunning | 令人惊叹的 | The animation in this film is stunning. |
| unforgettable | 令人难忘的 | The movie's message is unforgettable. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 梦想 | dream to become | dream of becoming |
| 禁止 | banned ✅ | banned / forbidden |
| 意外地 | accidently | accidentally（注意拼写） |
| 纽带 | bond ✅ | bond（注意搭配：bond between） |
| 珍惜 | cherish ✅ | cherish + 名词（直接加宾语） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 剧情 | A boy enters the world of the dead. | A guitar, a forgotten photo and a boy's forbidden dream open a door between the living and the dead. |
| 场景 | The ending made me cry. | When Miguel's trembling voice sang "Remember Me" and his great-grandmother's eyes lit up with recognition, every person in the cinema reached for a tissue. |
| 推荐 | You should watch this movie. | Watch "Coco" with your family — and when it ends, hug them a little tighter, because the film will remind you how precious they are. |
