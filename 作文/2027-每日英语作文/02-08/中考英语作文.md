# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，学校将举行"How to Be a Good Student"主题班会。请你写一篇英语演讲稿。内容包括：

① 一个好学生应该具备哪些品质  
② 在学习和生活中应该怎么做  
③ 呼吁大家努力成为好学生  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；演讲稿格式；不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 写作思考

### 审题

- **情境**：主题班会演讲
- **身份**：初中学生李华
- **核心任务**：列举品质 → 说明做法 → 发出呼吁
- **限制**：80-100词，演讲稿格式

### 结构安排

| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 问候+开头 | 引出话题 | 12-15词 |
| 正文 | 品质和做法 | 50-60词 |
| 结尾 | 呼吁+致谢 | 15-18词 |

### 表达准备（三要点中英文）

| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达参考 |
|------|------|--------------|
| ① 品质 | 勤奋、诚实、尊重他人、有责任心 | hardworking; honest; respect others; have a sense of responsibility |
| ② 做法 | 认真听课、按时完成作业、积极参加活动、帮助同学 | listen carefully in class; hand in homework on time; take part in activities; help classmates |
| ③ 呼吁 | 从小事做起、成为更好的自己 | start from small things; become a better version of ourselves |

### 可加分亮点（4条）

1. 用 **Dear classmates** 得体称呼
2. 用 **Being a good student is not only about grades** 突破传统观念
3. 用 **It also means...** 拓展内涵
4. 用 **Let's work together and become better every day** 号召

---

## 参考范文

Dear classmates,

Today I'd like to talk about how to be a good student.

Being a good student is not only about getting high grades. It also means having good qualities and habits. First, we should be hardworking and honest. Cheating may bring short-term success but will hurt us in the long run. Second, we should respect our teachers and classmates. A kind word and a friendly smile can make a big difference. Third, we need to have a sense of responsibility. We should hand in our homework on time, keep our classroom clean and take part in school activities actively.

Besides studying, we should also care about others. Helping a classmate with a difficult problem or sharing an umbrella on a rainy day are all examples of being a good student.

Let's work together and become a better version of ourselves every day!

Thank you!

（约135词）

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面

- 三要点完整：① 勤奋、诚实、尊重他人、有责任心 ② 不作弊、尊重师生、按时交作业、积极参加活动 ③ 呼吁共同进步
- 突破"好成绩=好学生"的传统观念，视角新颖

### 结构方面

- 演讲稿格式正确
- 用 Not only about... It also means... 展开
- 用 First / Second / Third / Besides 有序分层
- 结尾号召有力

### 语言方面

- **is not only about getting high grades** — 不仅仅是取得高分
- **in the long run** — 长远来看
- **a sense of responsibility** — 责任感
- **make a big difference** — 产生很大的影响
- **a better version of ourselves** — 更好的自己

## 全文翻译

亲爱的同学们，

今天我想谈谈如何做一个好学生。

做一个好学生不仅仅是取得高分。它还意味着拥有良好的品质和习惯。首先，我们应该勤奋和诚实。作弊可能带来短期的成功，但长远来看会伤害我们。其次，我们应该尊重老师和同学。一句善意的话和一个友好的微笑能产生很大的影响。第三，我们需要有责任感。我们应该按时交作业、保持教室整洁、积极参加学校活动。

除了学习，我们还应该关心他人。帮同学解答难题、下雨天分享一把伞，这些都是做好学生的体现。

让我们一起努力，每天都成为更好的自己！

谢谢大家！

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| is not only about | 不仅仅是关于 | Being a good student is not only about grades. |
| in the long run | 长远来看 | Cheating will hurt us in the long run. |
| a sense of responsibility | 责任感 | We should have a sense of responsibility. |
| make a big difference | 产生很大的影响 | A kind word can make a big difference. |
| a better version of ourselves | 更好的自己 | Let's become a better version of ourselves. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| not only about | not only about get grades | not only about getting grades（about + doing） |
| in the long run | in long run | in the long run（需要冠词 the） |
| hand in 搭配 | hand in homework in time | hand in homework on time（on time 按时） |
| take part in | take part of activities | take part in activities（take part in 固定搭配） |
| become 搭配 | become to better | become better（become 后直接加形容词） |

### 升格建议（3条）

| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. 用 not only about 打破常规 | A good student gets good grades. | Being a good student is not only about getting high grades. |
| 2. 用 in the long run 补充论证 | Cheating is bad. | Cheating may bring short-term success but will hurt us in the long run. |
| 3. 用 a better version of ourselves | We should be better. | Let's become a better version of ourselves every day. |
