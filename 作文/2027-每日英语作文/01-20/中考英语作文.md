# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

"低碳生活"是当下的热门话题。假设你是李华，请以"Let's Live a Low-carbon Life"为题写一篇短文，内容包括：

① 什么是低碳生活  
② 作为中学生，我们可以做些什么来践行低碳生活（至少三条）  
③ 你对低碳生活的看法  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；可适当发挥；不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 写作思考

- **情境**：写关于低碳生活的倡议文

- **核心任务**：解释低碳生活 → 提出做法 → 表达看法

## 参考范文

**Let's Live a Low-carbon Life**

Nowadays, low-carbon life has become a hot topic. It means we should try to reduce energy use and protect our environment in daily life.

As students, there are many things we can do. First, we can walk or ride bikes to school instead of asking our parents to drive us. Second, remember to turn off the lights and the tap when we don't need them. Third, we should use fewer disposable products such as plastic bags and paper cups. We can bring our own water bottles and shopping bags. Finally, sorting rubbish is also a good habit to develop.

I believe every small action counts. If everyone does a little, it will make a big difference. Let's work together for a greener future!

（约120词）

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面

- 三要点完整：① 低碳生活即减少能源使用、保护环境 ② 四条做法（绿色出行、节水节电、少用一次性物品、垃圾分类） ③ 小事也重要
- 做法具体可行，贴近中学生生活

### 结构方面

- 开头解释概念
- 用 First / Second / Third / Finally 有序展开
- 用 I believe 结尾表态
- Let's 号召有力

### 语言方面

- **a hot topic** — 热门话题
- **instead of asking our parents to drive us** — 而不是让父母开车送
- **disposable products** — 一次性产品
- **every small action counts** — 每个小行动都有意义
- **make a big difference** — 产生很大的影响

## 全文翻译

**让我们过低碳生活**

如今，低碳生活已成为一个热门话题。它意味着我们在日常生活中应该尽量减少能源使用，保护我们的环境。

作为学生，我们能做的事有很多。首先，我们可以步行或骑自行车上学，而不是让父母开车送。其次，不需要的时候记得关灯和关水龙头。第三，我们应该少用塑料袋、纸杯等一次性产品。可以自带水壶和购物袋。最后，垃圾分类也是一个值得培养的好习惯。

我相信每个小行动都有意义。如果每个人都做一点，就会产生很大的影响。让我们一起为更绿色的未来而努力！

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| a hot topic | 热门话题 | Low-carbon life has become a hot topic. |
| instead of | 而不是 | Walk to school instead of asking parents to drive. |
| disposable products | 一次性产品 | We should use fewer disposable products. |
| every small action counts | 每个小行动都有意义 | I believe every small action counts. |
| make a big difference | 产生很大的影响 | If everyone helps, it will make a big difference. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| fewer/less | use less plastic bags | use fewer plastic bags（bags 可数用 fewer） |
| instead of | instead of drive | instead of driving（instead of + doing） |
| turn off 搭配 | close the light | turn off the light（灯用 turn off） |
| remember to | remember turning off | remember to turn off（记住要做用 to do） |
| sort 搭配 | sort the rubbish by class | sort the rubbish / sort rubbish into categories |

### 升格建议（3条）

| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. 用 instead of 对比 | Don't let parents drive you. Walk. | Walk to school instead of asking parents to drive. |
| 2. 用 every action counts 强调 | Small things are also important. | Every small action counts. |
| 3. 用 make a big difference 升华 | It will be helpful. | It will make a big difference. |
