# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你的英国笔友 Peter 想了解中国学生的体育锻炼情况。请你回复邮件，内容包括：

① 中国学生常见的运动方式；  
② 你最喜欢什么运动以及原因；  
③ 你对体育锻炼重要性的看法。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（邮件） |
| 话题 | 体育锻炼 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |

| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Peter,

Thanks for your email! I'd love to tell you about how Chinese students exercise.

Chinese students enjoy many kinds of sports. Running, basketball and badminton are the most popular. Many schools also have morning exercises and eye exercises as part of the daily routine. During PE class, we usually run laps, play ball games or practise rope skipping.

My favourite sport is badminton. I play it with my friends every weekend. I love it because it is fast, exciting and a great way to relieve stress after a busy week of studying.

I think exercise is just as important as studying. A healthy body is the foundation of everything. As the saying goes, "All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy."

What sports do you like? Write back soon!

Yours,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *as part of the daily routine* — 作为日常的一部分  
2. *run laps* — 跑圈  
3. *relieve stress* — 缓解压力  
4. *just as important as* — 和……一样重要  
5. *the foundation of everything* — 一切的基础  
6. *All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy* — 只学不玩，聪明孩子也变傻  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的 Peter，

谢谢你的邮件！我很乐意告诉你中国学生的锻炼情况。

中国学生喜欢很多种运动。跑步、篮球和羽毛球是最受欢迎的。很多学校还把早操和眼保健操作为每天的常规活动。体育课上，我们通常跑圈、打球或练跳绳。

我最喜欢的运动是羽毛球。我每个周末都和朋友一起打。我喜欢它是因为它快速、刺激，是忙碌一周学习后缓解压力的好方法。

我认为锻炼和学习一样重要。健康的身体是一切的基础。正如谚语所说："只学不玩，聪明孩子也变傻。"

你喜欢什么运动？快回信告诉我！

你的，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| relieve stress | 缓解压力 | Exercise is a good way to relieve stress. |
| just as important as | 和……一样重要 | Sleep is just as important as exercise. |
| the foundation of | ……的基础 | Health is the foundation of a happy life. |
| daily routine | 日常活动 | Morning reading is part of my daily routine. |
| run laps | 跑圈 | We run laps every PE class. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 羽毛球 | badminton ✅ | badminton（不加 s） |
| 跳绳 | skip rope | skip / jump rope / rope skipping |
| 眼保健操 | eye exercise ✅ | eye exercises（复数） |
| 和……一样 | as important than | as important as |
| 体育课 | PE class ✅ | PE class / physical education class |
