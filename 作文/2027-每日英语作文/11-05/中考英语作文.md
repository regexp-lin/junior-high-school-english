# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，暑假期间你和家人一起去了一个你从未去过的地方旅行。请你写一篇短文记录这次旅行，内容包括：

① 你们去了哪里、怎么去的；  
② 旅途中印象最深的一件事或一个场景；  
③ 这次旅行给你带来的收获或感悟。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 记叙文 |
| 话题 | 一次难忘的旅行 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般过去时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Last summer, my family and I took a trip to Guilin by high-speed train. It was my first time visiting this beautiful city, and everything amazed me.

The most unforgettable moment was a bamboo raft ride on the Li River. As we floated slowly along the water, green mountains reflected on the surface like a painting. The air was fresh, and the only sounds were birdsong and the gentle splash of the water. I felt as if I had stepped into a Chinese ink painting.

This trip taught me something important: the best scenery is not always found on a screen — it is waiting outside, ready to take your breath away.

I hope to visit more places in the future and see the beauty that our country has to offer.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *took a trip to* — 去……旅行  
2. *everything amazed me* — 一切都让我惊叹  
3. *reflected on the surface like a painting* — 倒映在水面上如同一幅画  
4. *stepped into a Chinese ink painting* — 走进了一幅中国水墨画  
5. *take your breath away* — 令人叹为观止  
6. *the beauty that our country has to offer* — 我们国家的美景  

---

## 全文翻译

去年暑假，我和家人乘高铁去了桂林。这是我第一次去这座美丽的城市，一切都让我惊叹。

最难忘的时刻是在漓江上坐竹筏。我们沿着江面缓缓漂流，翠绿的山倒映在水面上如同一幅画。空气清新，耳边只有鸟鸣和水花轻轻拍打的声音。我感觉自己仿佛走进了一幅中国水墨画。

这次旅行教会了我一件重要的事：最好的风景并不总在屏幕上——它在外面等着你，随时让你叹为观止。

我希望将来能去更多的地方，看看我们国家的美景。

---

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| take a trip to | 去……旅行 | We took a trip to Shanghai last year. |
| for the first time | 第一次 | I saw the sea for the first time. |
| as if | 仿佛 | I felt as if I were in a dream. |
| take one's breath away | 令人叹为观止 | The sunrise took my breath away. |
| has to offer | 所拥有的 | There is so much this city has to offer. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 第一次 | It was my first time to visit ✅ | It was my first time visiting / to visit |
| 倒映 | reflected in ✅ | reflected on the surface / in the water |
| 仿佛 | as if I stepped | as if I had stepped（虚拟语气） |
| 风景 | sceneries | scenery（不可数） |
| 乘坐 | by the high-speed train | by high-speed train（交通方式不加the） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 场景 | The mountains were very beautiful. | The mountains rose like sleeping giants draped in emerald green. |
| 感受 | I was very happy. | Happiness floated in the air like the morning mist over the Li River. |
| 感悟 | Travelling is good for us. | Travel is the only thing you buy that makes you richer — richer in memories, richer in wonder. |
