# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校开展了"我的成长故事"英语写作活动。请你写一篇短文，内容包括：

① 你在成长过程中养成的一个好习惯是什么；  
② 你是如何养成这个习惯的；  
③ 这个习惯对你的影响。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 记叙文（成长类） |

| 人称 | 第一人称 |

| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

## 写作思考

### 审题
- **情境**："我的成长故事"写作活动。
- **身份**：李华。
- **核心任务**：好习惯是什么、怎么养成的、影响。
- **限制**：80-100 词、无真实姓名校名。

### 结构安排
| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第一段 | 习惯是什么——早起跑步 | 约20词 |
| 第二段 | 如何养成 | 约40词 |
| 第三段 | 对我的影响 | 约35词 |

### 表达准备（三要点中英文）
| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达参考 |
|------|------|--------------|
| ① | 每天早起跑步 | get up early and run every morning |
| ② | 爸爸带我跑、一开始很难、坚持21天 | Dad took me running, hard at first, kept going for 21 days |
| ③ | 更精力充沛、学习效率高、更自律 | more energetic, study more efficiently, more self-disciplined |

### 可加分亮点（4条）
1. *A good habit can change your life.*  
2. *The first week was the hardest.*  
3. *Now it has become a part of who I am.*  
4. *Good habits are like seeds — the earlier you plant them, the stronger they grow.*

## 参考范文

A good habit can change your life. For me, that habit is getting up early and going for a morning run.

It all started two years ago when my dad suggested we run together before breakfast. The first week was the hardest. My alarm rang at six, and all I wanted to do was stay in bed. But Dad always knocked on my door with a smile and said, "Let's go!" After about three weeks, I no longer needed his help — running had become a natural part of my morning.

This habit has changed me greatly. I feel more energetic throughout the day and can concentrate better in class. It has also taught me self-discipline and the power of persistence.

Good habits are like seeds — the earlier you plant them, the stronger they grow. I am glad I planted mine two years ago.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面
习惯（早起跑步）；养成过程（爸爸建议、第一周很难、坚持三周后成自然）；影响（精力充沛、集中注意力、自律），三点齐全。

### 结构方面
引出—起因—过程—影响—感悟，叙事完整。

### 语言方面
1. *suggested we run together* — 建议我们一起跑（suggest + 虚拟语气）  
2. *all I wanted to do was stay in bed* — 我只想赖在床上  
3. *knocked on my door with a smile* — 微笑着敲我的门  
4. *a natural part of my morning* — 早晨自然的一部分  
5. *self-discipline and the power of persistence* — 自律和坚持的力量  
6. *Good habits are like seeds* — 好习惯如同种子  

---

## 全文翻译

一个好习惯能改变你的人生。对我来说，那个习惯就是每天早起晨跑。

两年前，爸爸建议我们一起在早饭前跑步。第一周是最难的。闹钟在六点响，我只想赖在床上。但爸爸总是微笑着敲我的门说："走吧！"大约三周后，我不再需要他的帮助了——跑步已经成为我早晨自然的一部分。

这个习惯极大地改变了我。我一整天都更有精力，上课也更专注了。它还教会了我自律和坚持的力量。

好习惯如同种子——越早播种，它们就长得越强壮。我很庆幸两年前种下了我的那颗种子。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| self-discipline | 自律 | Self-discipline is the key to success. |
| the power of persistence | 坚持的力量 | He showed us the power of persistence. |
| concentrate on | 专注于 | I can concentrate on my study better now. |
| a natural part of | ……自然的一部分 | Exercise has become a natural part of my life. |
| plant a seed | 播下种子 | Every good habit plants a seed for the future. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 建议 | suggested me to run | suggested (that) I (should) run |
| 早起 | get up early ✅ | get up early / wake up early |
| 习惯 | used to ✅ | get used to（习惯于） vs used to（过去常常） |
| 自律 | self-discipline ✅ | self-discipline（名词）/ self-disciplined（形容词） |
| 坚持 | insist running | keep running / persist in running |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 起因 | Dad asked me to run with him. | Dad's morning invitation to run became the most life-changing wake-up call I ever received. |
| 过程 | I didn't want to get up. | The warm blanket fought against my willpower every single morning. |
| 影响 | I feel better now. | That daily run gave me more than fitness — it gave me the belief that I can conquer anything, one step at a time. |
