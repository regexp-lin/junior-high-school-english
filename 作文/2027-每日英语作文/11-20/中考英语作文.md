# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，最近你和妈妈因为使用手机的问题发生了争吵。请你写一篇日记，内容包括：

① 事情的经过（起因、争吵过程）；  
② 事后你冷静下来后的想法；  
③ 你打算如何与父母更好地沟通。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（日记） |
| 话题 | 与父母沟通 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |

| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Wednesday, November 20th                                                    Sunny

Something unpleasant happened today. Mum asked me to put down my phone during dinner, but I was in the middle of chatting with my friends and didn't want to stop. She got angry and took my phone away. I shouted, "You never understand me!" and ran back to my room.

After calming down, I felt ashamed of what I said. Mum wasn't trying to control me — she just wanted us to enjoy dinner together as a family. I was the one being rude and unreasonable.

I've decided to apologise to her tonight. In the future, I will try to see things from my parents' point of view before reacting. I'll also set screen-time limits for myself so the same argument doesn't happen again.

Communication is a two-way street. If I want to be understood, I need to learn to understand first.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *in the middle of* — 正在……中间  
2. *took my phone away* — 把我的手机拿走  
3. *felt ashamed of* — 感到羞愧  
4. *rude and unreasonable* — 无礼且不讲理  
5. *see things from one's point of view* — 从某人的角度看问题  
6. *a two-way street* — 双向的  

---

## 全文翻译

11月20日 星期三                                                              晴

今天发生了一件不愉快的事。妈妈让我在吃饭时放下手机，但我正在和朋友聊天，不想停下来。她生气了，把我的手机没收了。我冲她喊："你从来不理解我！"然后跑回了房间。

冷静下来后，我为自己说的话感到羞愧。妈妈不是想控制我——她只是想让我们一家人好好吃顿饭。无礼且不讲理的人是我。

我决定今晚向她道歉。以后，我会在反应之前试着从父母的角度看问题。我也会给自己设定屏幕时间限制，这样同样的争吵就不会再发生了。

沟通是双向的。如果我想被理解，我需要先学会理解别人。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| in the middle of | 在……过程中 | I was in the middle of reading when he called. |
| feel ashamed of | 为……感到羞愧 | I felt ashamed of my behaviour. |
| from one's point of view | 从某人的角度 | Try to see it from her point of view. |
| calm down | 冷静下来 | Take a deep breath and calm down. |
| a two-way street | 双向的 | Respect is a two-way street. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 羞愧 | ashamed about | ashamed of |
| 没收 | took away my phone ✅ | took my phone away / took away my phone |
| 角度 | in one's view ✅ | from one's point of view |
| 道歉 | apologise for her | apologise to her |
| 不讲理 | unreasonable ✅ | unreasonable（注意拼写） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 经过 | I was angry and shouted. | My words flew out like arrows, and the moment they left my mouth, I wished I could catch them back. |
| 反思 | I was wrong. | The silence in my room was louder than our argument — it gave me time to hear the voice of regret. |
| 感悟 | We should communicate better. | A family is not a courtroom where someone wins and someone loses — it is a garden where patience and understanding help love grow. |
