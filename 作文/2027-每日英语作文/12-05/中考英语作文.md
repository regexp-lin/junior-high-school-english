# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校正在开展关于"如何学好英语"的经验交流会。请你写一篇发言稿，内容包括：

① 你在学英语过程中遇到的困难；  
② 你使用过的有效学习方法（至少两个）；  
③ 你对学好英语的建议。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 参考范文

Good morning, everyone!

Learning English is a long journey, and I'd like to share some of my experiences.

When I first started learning English, my biggest problems were poor pronunciation and a small vocabulary. I felt embarrassed every time I spoke in class.

To improve, I tried two methods that really helped. First, I started listening to English songs and podcasts every day. This trained my ears and made me more familiar with natural pronunciation. Second, I began keeping a vocabulary notebook. Every time I learned a new word, I wrote it down with an example sentence and reviewed it before bed.

My advice is: don't be afraid of making mistakes. Every mistake is a chance to learn. Also, practise a little every day rather than cramming before exams. Language learning is like building a house — one brick at a time.

Thank you!

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *a long journey* — 一段漫长的旅程  
2. *trained my ears* — 训练了我的耳朵  
3. *more familiar with* — 更熟悉  
4. *a vocabulary notebook* — 词汇本  
5. *cramming before exams* — 考前临时抱佛脚  
6. *one brick at a time* — 一次一块砖

## 全文翻译

大家早上好！

学英语是一段漫长的旅程，我想分享一些我的经验。

刚开始学英语时，我最大的问题是发音差和词汇量小。每次在课堂上开口说英语我都觉得很尴尬。

为了提高，我尝试了两个真的很有帮助的方法。第一，我开始每天听英文歌和播客。这训练了我的耳朵，让我更熟悉自然的发音。第二，我开始记词汇本。每学一个新词，我就把它和例句一起写下来，睡前复习。

我的建议是：不要害怕犯错。每个错误都是一次学习的机会。还有，每天练一点点比考前临时抱佛脚好得多。语言学习就像盖房子——一次一块砖。

谢谢大家！

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| be familiar with | 熟悉 | I am familiar with this grammar point. |
| cram for exams | 考前突击 | Cramming for exams is not effective. |
| a chance to learn | 学习的机会 | Every mistake is a chance to learn. |
| rather than | 而不是 | Practise daily rather than cramming. |
| one brick at a time | 一步一步来 | Success is built one brick at a time. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 熟悉 | familiar to ✅ | familiar with（人作主语时） |
| 害怕 | afraid of make | afraid of making（介词+动名词） |
| 而不是 | rather than to cram | rather than cramming / rather than cram |
| 发音 | pronunciation ✅ | pronunciation（注意拼写） |
| 词汇量 | vocabulary ✅ | vocabulary（既可指单个词也可指词汇量） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 困难 | My English was poor. | My tongue tripped over every English word like a toddler learning to walk. |
| 方法 | I listen to English songs. | English songs turned my commute into a classroom and my earphones into a patient teacher. |
| 建议 | Practise every day. | Water a plant once a month and it dies; water it a little every day and it blooms — English works the same way. |
