# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，学校正在开展"节约用水"主题教育活动。请你写一篇倡议书，内容包括：

① 水资源面临的问题；  
② 日常生活中节约用水的方法（至少两条）；  
③ 号召同学们行动起来。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 参考范文

Dear schoolmates,

Water is essential to life, but it is not unlimited.

Although 70% of the earth's surface is covered by water, only about 1% of it is fresh water available for daily use. In many parts of the world, people still don't have access to clean drinking water. Even in China, some areas face serious water shortages, especially in the north and west.

Here is what we can do every day. First, turn off the tap while brushing your teeth — this alone can save litres of water each month. Second, take shorter showers. Five minutes is enough to get clean. Third, report any leaking taps or pipes at school immediately.

Water seems free, but it is priceless. Every drop we save today is a gift to future generations.

Let's turn off the tap and turn on our awareness. Save water, save life!

Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *essential to life* — 对生命至关重要  
2. *fresh water available for daily use* — 可供日常使用的淡水  
3. *face serious water shortages* — 面临严重缺水  
4. *report any leaking taps* — 报告漏水的水龙头  
5. *seems free, but it is priceless* — 看似免费却无价  
6. *turn on our awareness* — 提高我们的意识

## 全文翻译

亲爱的同学们，

水对生命至关重要，但它不是无限的。

虽然地球表面70%被水覆盖，但只有约1%是可供日常使用的淡水。世界上很多地方的人还无法获得干净的饮用水。即使在中国，一些地区也面临严重的缺水问题，尤其是北方和西部。

以下是我们每天都能做的事。第一，刷牙时关掉水龙头——仅此一项每月就能节省好几升水。第二，缩短淋浴时间。五分钟足以洗干净。第三，在学校发现漏水的水龙头或水管要立即报告。

水看似免费，却是无价之宝。今天我们节省的每一滴水都是送给后代的礼物。

让我们关掉水龙头，打开我们的意识。节约用水，守护生命！

李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| essential to | 对……至关重要 | Water is essential to all living things. |
| have access to | 能够获得 | Everyone deserves access to clean water. |
| water shortage | 缺水 | Many regions face water shortages. |
| leaking tap | 漏水的水龙头 | A leaking tap wastes thousands of litres a year. |
| priceless | 无价的 | Clean water is truly priceless. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 至关重要 | essential for ✅ | essential to / essential for（均可） |
| 淡水 | fresh water ✅ | fresh water / freshwater |
| 缺水 | short of water ✅ | water shortage（名词短语更正式） |
| 升 | liter ✅ | litre (英) / liter (美) |
| 漏水 | leaking ✅ | leaking / dripping（后者指滴水） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 问题 | Many people don't have enough water. | Somewhere right now, a child is walking miles for a cup of water that we let run down the drain while brushing our teeth. |
| 方法 | Turn off the tap when brushing teeth. | Closing the tap for two minutes costs you nothing, but it gives the planet something it desperately needs. |
| 号召 | Save water. | Water is the silent hero of our survival — let us be the hero who saves it in return. |
