# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，请你写一篇短文分享你的一次失败经历，内容包括：

① 事情的经过（你做了什么，结果如何）；  
② 失败后你的心情和反思；  
③ 这次失败教会了你什么。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

## 参考范文

Last term, I entered the school English spelling bee with high confidence. I had practised for weeks and felt sure I would win.

In the first three rounds, I did well and answered every word correctly. But in the final round, the judge gave me the word "necessary". My mind went blank. I spelled it as "n-e-c-e-s-s-e-r-y" and was eliminated immediately. I stood there, frozen, while the audience clapped for the next contestant.

After the competition, I felt embarrassed and upset. I kept blaming myself — how could I miss such a common word? But after a few days, I calmed down and realised something important: failure is not the end; it is feedback.

Now I keep a "mistake notebook" where I write down every word I get wrong. That one painful moment has made me a more careful and humble learner. Sometimes you have to lose a battle to win the war.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *with high confidence* — 满怀信心  
2. *my mind went blank* — 大脑一片空白  
3. *was eliminated immediately* — 立即被淘汰  
4. *failure is not the end; it is feedback* — 失败不是终点，是反馈  
5. *a mistake notebook* — 错误本  
6. *lose a battle to win the war* — 输掉一场战斗才能赢得整场战争  

---

## 全文翻译

上学期，我满怀信心地参加了学校英语拼写大赛。我练习了好几个星期，觉得自己一定会赢。

前三轮我表现很好，每个单词都拼对了。但在最后一轮，评委给了我"necessary"这个词。我大脑一片空白。我拼成了"n-e-c-e-s-s-e-r-y"，立刻被淘汰了。我站在那里，呆住了，观众为下一位选手鼓掌。

比赛结束后，我觉得很丢脸、很难过。我不停地责怪自己——我怎么会拼错这么常见的词？但几天后，我冷静下来，意识到了一个重要的道理：失败不是终点，而是反馈。

现在我有一个"错误本"，每个拼错的词我都会记下来。那个痛苦的瞬间让我成为了一个更细心、更谦逊的学习者。有时候你必须输掉一场战斗，才能赢得整场战争。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| one's mind goes blank | 大脑一片空白 | When I saw the question, my mind went blank. |
| be eliminated | 被淘汰 | He was eliminated in the second round. |
| blame oneself | 责怪自己 | Don't blame yourself for one mistake. |
| calm down | 冷静下来 | I calmed down and thought carefully. |
| lose a battle to win the war | 以退为进 | Sometimes you lose a battle to win the war. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 大脑空白 | my mind went empty | my mind went blank |
| 淘汰 | was eliminated ✅ | was eliminated（被动语态） |
| 责怪 | blame for myself | blame myself |
| 常见的 | common ✅ | common（注意不要拼成 comon） |
| 拼写 | spell ✅ | spell — spelled/spelt — spelled/spelt |
