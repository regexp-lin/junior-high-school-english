# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，教师节快到了，请你给你最喜欢的一位老师写一封感谢信，内容包括：

① 你最感谢的老师是谁，教什么科目；  
② 他/她做过什么让你印象深刻的事；  
③ 你想对他/她说的话。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（感谢信） |
| 话题 | 感谢老师 |
| 人称 | 第一/第二人称 |
| 时态 | 一般过去时+一般现在时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Mr. Zhang,

As Teachers' Day is coming, I want to express my heartfelt thanks to you.

You are my English teacher, and you have made English my favourite subject. I remember when I first entered middle school, I was afraid of speaking English because I always made mistakes. Instead of criticising me, you smiled and said, "Mistakes are the footprints on the road to success." From that day on, I was no longer afraid to raise my hand in class.

You also stayed after school many times to help me with my reading and writing. Your patience and encouragement gave me the confidence to keep trying.

Thank you for believing in me when I didn't believe in myself. You are not just a teacher — you are a light that guides me forward.

Happy Teachers' Day!

Your student,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *express my heartfelt thanks* — 表达衷心的感谢  
2. *instead of criticising me* — 没有批评我  
3. *the footprints on the road to success* — 通往成功路上的脚印  
4. *raise my hand in class* — 在课上举手  
5. *patience and encouragement* — 耐心和鼓励  
6. *a light that guides me forward* — 引领我前行的一盏灯  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的张老师，

教师节快到了，我想向您表达衷心的感谢。

您是我的英语老师，是您让英语成了我最喜欢的学科。我记得刚进初中时，我害怕说英语，因为总是犯错。您没有批评我，而是微笑着说："错误是通往成功路上的脚印。"从那天起，我不再害怕在课上举手了。

您还多次放学后留下来帮我辅导阅读和写作。您的耐心和鼓励给了我继续尝试的信心。

谢谢您在我不相信自己的时候相信了我。您不只是一位老师——您是引领我前行的一盏灯。

教师节快乐！

您的学生，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| express one's thanks | 表达感谢 | I want to express my thanks to you. |
| instead of | 而不是 | He praised me instead of blaming me. |
| no longer | 不再 | I am no longer afraid of speaking English. |
| believe in | 相信（某人的能力） | Thank you for believing in me. |
| guide sb. forward | 引领某人前行 | Good teachers guide us forward with love. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 教师节 | Teacher's Day | Teachers' Day（复数所有格） |
| 不再 | no more afraid | no longer afraid |
| 而不是 | instead of criticise | instead of criticising（介词+动名词） |
| 衷心的 | heartful | heartfelt |
| 相信 | believe me ✅ | believe in me（信任我的能力） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 感谢 | Thank you for helping me. | Your words did not just teach me English — they taught me that I am braver than I think. |
| 事迹 | You helped me after school. | While other classrooms went dark, yours stayed bright — and so did my hope. |
| 寄语 | You are a good teacher. | You planted a garden of confidence in my heart, and its flowers bloom every time I open my mouth to speak. |
