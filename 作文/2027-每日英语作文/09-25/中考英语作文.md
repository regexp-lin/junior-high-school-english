# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校英语报正在征集以"My Favourite Animal"为主题的短文。请你写一篇投稿，内容包括：

① 你最喜欢的动物是什么；  
② 描述它的外观和习性；  
③ 你为什么喜欢它以及我们应该如何保护动物。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

## 参考范文

Among all the animals, my favourite is the giant panda. It is the national treasure of China and loved by people around the world.

Giant pandas have black and white fur, round faces and dark circles around their eyes. They look like big cuddly toys! They live mainly in the mountains of Sichuan and spend most of their day eating bamboo. Although they look lazy, they are actually good at climbing trees.

I love pandas because they are gentle and adorable. Watching them play always makes me smile.

Sadly, pandas are still in danger because of habitat loss. It is our duty to protect wildlife by planting more trees and saying no to pollution. Only when we live in harmony with nature can all animals have a safe home.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面
最喜欢的动物（大熊猫、国宝）；外观习性（黑白毛、吃竹子、爬树）；喜欢原因（温和可爱）与保护呼吁，三点齐全。

### 结构方面
引出—描写—原因—呼吁，四段式完整。

### 语言方面
1. *the national treasure of China* — 中国的国宝  
2. *dark circles around their eyes* — 眼睛周围的黑眼圈  
3. *look like big cuddly toys* — 看起来像大号毛绒玩具  
4. *good at climbing trees* — 善于爬树  
5. *It is our duty to* — 这是我们的责任  
6. *Only when … can …* — 只有……才能……（倒装句）

## 全文翻译

在所有动物中，我最喜欢的是大熊猫。它是中国的国宝，受到全世界人民的喜爱。

大熊猫有着黑白相间的毛、圆圆的脸和眼睛周围的黑眼圈。它们看起来就像大号毛绒玩具！它们主要生活在四川的山区，一天中大部分时间都在吃竹子。虽然看起来很懒，但它们其实很善于爬树。

我喜欢大熊猫，因为它们温和而可爱。看它们玩耍总能让我微笑。

遗憾的是，由于栖息地的减少，大熊猫仍然处于危险之中。保护野生动物是我们的责任——多种树、对污染说不。只有当我们与自然和谐相处，所有动物才能拥有一个安全的家。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| national treasure | 国宝 | The giant panda is a national treasure of China. |
| habitat loss | 栖息地丧失 | Many animals are in danger because of habitat loss. |
| live in harmony with | 与……和谐相处 | We should live in harmony with nature. |
| It is our duty to | 这是我们的责任 | It is our duty to protect the earth. |
| be good at | 善于 | Pandas are good at climbing trees. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 大熊猫 | big panda | giant panda |
| 国宝 | national treasure ✅ | the national treasure of China |
| 竹子 | eat bamboos | eat bamboo（不可数） |
| 善于 | be good in | be good at |
| 倒装 | Only when we … all animals can … | Only when we … can all animals … |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 外观 | They are black and white. | Their black-and-white coat looks like a perfectly painted tuxedo. |
| 习性 | They eat bamboo all day. | A panda's world revolves around bamboo, crunching through about 12 hours a day. |
| 呼吁 | We should protect them. | Every bamboo forest we save is a promise to the next generation of pandas. |
