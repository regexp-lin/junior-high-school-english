# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，上周你在公交车上帮助了一位外国游客。请你写一篇短文记录这次经历，内容包括：

① 事情的经过（你遇到了什么情况、做了什么）；  
② 对方的反应和你的感受；  
③ 这次经历给你的启发。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 记叙文 |
| 话题 | 帮助他人 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |

| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

## 参考范文

Last Saturday, I was riding the bus when I noticed a foreign tourist looking confused and worried. He kept checking his phone and glancing at the bus map, but he clearly couldn't read the Chinese characters.

I walked over and asked in English, "Do you need any help?" His face lit up. He told me he was trying to get to the City Museum but had taken the wrong bus. I helped him get off at the next stop, showed him which bus to take and even wrote down the Chinese name of his destination on a piece of paper.

He shook my hand and said, "Thank you so much! Chinese people are so kind." I felt a warm glow inside — knowing that my English skills and a few minutes of my time had made a real difference.

This experience reminded me that kindness needs no passport. A helping hand can cross any language barrier.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *looking confused and worried* — 看起来困惑又着急  
2. *his face lit up* — 他的脸亮了起来  
3. *taken the wrong bus* — 坐错了车  
4. *a warm glow inside* — 内心的温暖感觉  
5. *made a real difference* — 真正产生了影响  
6. *kindness needs no passport* — 善意不需要护照  

---

## 全文翻译

上周六，我坐公交车时注意到一个外国游客看起来困惑又着急。他不停地看手机和公交线路图，但明显看不懂中文字。

我走过去用英语问："你需要帮助吗？"他的脸一下亮了起来。他告诉我他想去市博物馆，但坐错了车。我帮他在下一站下车，告诉他该坐哪路车，甚至把目的地的中文名写在一张纸上。

他握了握我的手说："非常感谢你！中国人太好了。"我心里涌起一股温暖——知道我的英语技能和几分钟的时间真正帮到了别人。

这次经历让我想到，善意不需要护照。一只伸出的援手可以跨越任何语言障碍。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| one's face lit up | 脸上露出光彩 | Her face lit up when she heard the news. |
| take the wrong bus | 坐错车 | I took the wrong bus and got lost. |
| make a difference | 产生影响 | Small acts of kindness make a difference. |
| language barrier | 语言障碍 | A smile can break any language barrier. |
| a helping hand | 援手 | He offered me a helping hand. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 亮起来 | face lighted up | face lit up（light的过去式常用lit） |
| 坐错车 | take wrong bus | take the wrong bus |
| 产生影响 | make difference | make a difference（加冠词） |
| 语言障碍 | language barrier ✅ | language barrier |
| 握手 | shook my hand ✅ | shook hands / shook my hand |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 经过 | I saw a tourist who needed help. | He stood at the bus stop like a character dropped into a story written in a language he couldn't read. |
| 感受 | I felt happy to help. | That handshake carried more warmth than any words could — it was the universal language of gratitude. |
| 启发 | Helping others is good. | Kindness has no nationality, no language and no expiry date — it simply travels from one heart to another. |
