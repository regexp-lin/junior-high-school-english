# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，英语老师让你写一篇关于"我最敬佩的人"的短文。请你写一篇，内容包括：

① 你最敬佩的人是谁（可以是历史人物或身边的人）；  
② 他/她做了什么令你敬佩的事；  
③ 他/她对你的影响。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 记叙文（人物描写） |
| 话题 | 我最敬佩的人 |
| 人称 | 第一/第三人称 |
| 时态 | 一般过去时+一般现在时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

The person I admire most is Helen Keller.

Helen Keller lost her sight and hearing at the age of nineteen months due to a serious illness. Living in a world of darkness and silence, most people would have given up. But Helen refused to surrender. With the help of her teacher, Anne Sullivan, she learned to read, write and even speak. She graduated from college and went on to become a famous author and activist who inspired millions.

What I admire most about her is her unbreakable spirit. She once said, "Although the world is full of suffering, it is also full of the overcoming of it." This quote has become my motto whenever I face difficulties.

Helen Keller taught me that our limits are not set by our bodies but by our minds. If she could light up the world without seeing it, surely I can light up my own path with hard work and determination.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *lost her sight and hearing* — 失去了视力和听力  
2. *refused to surrender* — 拒绝投降  
3. *went on to become* — 后来成为  
4. *unbreakable spirit* — 不屈的精神  
5. *full of the overcoming of it* — 也充满了对苦难的克服  
6. *our limits are not set by our bodies but by our minds* — 限制不在身体而在心灵  

---

## 全文翻译

我最敬佩的人是海伦·凯勒。

海伦·凯勒在19个月大时因重病失去了视力和听力。生活在黑暗和无声的世界里，大多数人会选择放弃。但海伦拒绝屈服。在老师安妮·沙利文的帮助下，她学会了阅读、写字甚至说话。她大学毕业，后来成为著名的作家和社会活动家，激励了数百万人。

我最敬佩她的是她不屈不挠的精神。她曾说："虽然世界充满了苦难，但也充满了对苦难的克服。"每当我遇到困难时，这句话就成了我的座右铭。

海伦·凯勒教会了我，我们的限制不在于身体而在于心灵。如果她在看不见世界的情况下依然能照亮世界，我一定也能用努力和决心照亮自己的道路。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| refuse to | 拒绝 | She refused to give up her dream. |
| go on to | 后来继续 | He went on to become a great scientist. |
| unbreakable | 不可摧毁的 | Her spirit is truly unbreakable. |
| motto | 座右铭 | This quote has become my motto. |
| light up | 照亮 | Her story lights up my path. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 失去视力 | lost her see | lost her sight |
| 拒绝 | refused + doing | refused to do（后接不定式） |
| 座右铭 | motto ✅ | motto（注意拼写） |
| 不屈的 | unbreakable ✅ | unbreakable / indomitable |
| 在……帮助下 | with the help from | with the help of |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 介绍 | She couldn't see or hear. | The world locked her in darkness and silence, but Helen Keller found a key that no disability could take away — her will. |
| 成就 | She became a famous writer. | From a little girl who could not say a single word to an author whose words moved nations — that is the miracle of determination. |
| 影响 | She inspires me. | Helen Keller did not just overcome her own darkness — she lit a torch that still guides the world a century later. |
