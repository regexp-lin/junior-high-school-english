# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，最近你感到学业压力很大。你的英语老师让大家写一篇关于"如何应对学习压力"的短文。请你根据自身经历来写，内容包括：

① 你目前面临的学习压力有哪些；  
② 你尝试过的缓解压力的方法；  
③ 你对同龄人的建议。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 说明文（经验分享） |
| 话题 | 应对学习压力 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般过去时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

As a Grade 9 student, I often feel stressed about schoolwork. The final exam is getting closer, and there is so much to review. Sometimes I lie awake at night worrying about my grades.

To deal with the pressure, I have tried several things that really work. First, I go jogging every evening for about twenty minutes. Exercise clears my mind and helps me sleep better. Second, I talk to my parents when I feel overwhelmed. They always remind me that doing my best is good enough. Third, I break big tasks into small steps so they don't feel so scary.

My advice to other students is simple: don't fight stress alone. Share your feelings, move your body and remember — one step at a time is still moving forward. Pressure is not the enemy; giving up is.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *lie awake at night* — 夜里睡不着  
2. *clears my mind* — 让头脑清醒  
3. *feel overwhelmed* — 感到不堪重负  
4. *doing my best is good enough* — 尽力就好  
5. *break big tasks into small steps* — 把大任务拆成小步骤  
6. *one step at a time* — 一次一步  

---

## 全文翻译

作为一名初三学生，我经常为学业感到压力。期末考试越来越近，要复习的内容太多了。有时候我晚上躺在床上睡不着，担心成绩。

为了应对压力，我尝试了几个真正有效的方法。第一，我每天晚上慢跑大约二十分钟。运动能让我头脑清醒，睡得更好。第二，当我感到不堪重负时，我会和父母聊聊。他们总是提醒我，尽力就好。第三，我把大任务拆成小步骤，这样就不会觉得那么可怕。

我对其他同学的建议很简单：不要独自面对压力。分享你的感受，活动你的身体，记住——一次走一步，也是在前进。压力不是敌人，放弃才是。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| feel stressed about | 对……感到有压力 | I feel stressed about the coming exam. |
| clear one's mind | 使头脑清醒 | A walk in nature can clear your mind. |
| feel overwhelmed | 感到不堪重负 | Don't feel overwhelmed — take it slowly. |
| break … into | 把……拆成 | Break your work into small tasks. |
| one step at a time | 一步一步来 | Just take it one step at a time. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 越来越 | more and more closer | getting closer（用比较级） |
| 睡不着 | can't asleep | lie awake / can't fall asleep |
| 提醒 | remind me + that ✅ | remind sb. that / remind sb. to do |
| 不堪重负 | overwhelmed ✅ | overwhelmed（注意拼写） |
| 敌人 | the enemy ✅ | the enemy（注意冠词） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 压力 | I have a lot of homework. | My desk is a battlefield of textbooks and past papers, and the final exam is the ticking clock on the wall. |
| 方法 | Running helps me feel better. | Twenty minutes of jogging is my daily reset button — it turns a foggy mind into a clear sky. |
| 建议 | Don't give up. | Pressure is just a heavy backpack on a long hike — you can set it down to rest, but you don't have to throw it away to keep walking. |
