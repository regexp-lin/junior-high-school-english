# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你的班主任让每位同学写一封信给三年后的自己。请你写一封，内容包括：

① 你对目前生活的感受；  
② 你希望三年后自己变成什么样；  
③ 你对未来自己的叮嘱。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（书信） |
| 话题 | 给未来自己的信 |
| 人称 | 第一/第二人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般将来时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Future Me,

If you're reading this, three years have passed. I wonder: have you changed as much as I hope?

Right now, I'm a Grade 9 student preparing for the high school entrance exam. Life is busy, tiring and sometimes stressful. But it is also full of small joys — a funny joke from a friend, a perfect score on a quiz and the taste of Mum's hot soup after a cold day.

Three years from now, I hope you're a high school student who is not afraid to speak up, who reads for fun every week and who still calls Mum and Dad even when you're busy.

Here is my advice to you: stay curious, stay humble and never stop being kind. The world will test you, but don't let it harden your heart. Remember the boy you are today — he worked hard, believed in you and sent you this letter across time.

Be brave. Be you.

With love,  
Li Hua, 2027

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *I wonder: have you changed* — 我想知道：你变了吗  
2. *full of small joys* — 充满小小的快乐  
3. *not afraid to speak up* — 不怕发言  
4. *harden your heart* — 让你的心变硬  
5. *sent you this letter across time* — 穿越时光寄给你这封信  
6. *Be brave. Be you.* — 勇敢。做你自己。  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的未来的我，

如果你正在读这封信，三年已经过去了。我想知道：你是否像我期望的那样改变了？

现在的我是一名初三学生，正在准备中考。生活忙碌、疲惫，有时有压力。但也充满小小的快乐——朋友的一个有趣笑话、一次测验的满分、以及冷天回家后妈妈的热汤。

三年后，我希望你是一个不怕发言的高中生，每周为兴趣而读书，即使忙也还是会给爸妈打电话。

给你的建议是：保持好奇、保持谦虚、永远不要停止善良。这个世界会考验你，但不要让它使你的心变硬。记住今天的那个男孩——他努力学习、相信你、跨越时光给你寄了这封信。

勇敢。做你自己。

带着爱，  
2027年的李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| speak up | 大胆说出 | Don't be afraid to speak up in class. |
| full of | 充满 | Life is full of surprises. |
| harden one's heart | 使心变硬 | Don't let failure harden your heart. |
| stay curious | 保持好奇 | Stay curious and keep learning. |
| across time | 跨越时光 | This letter travels across time to find you. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 我想知道 | I wonder + 直接问句 | I wonder: have you …（冒号后接完整句） |
| 即使 | even when ✅ | even when / even if |
| 使变硬 | harden ✅ | harden（使动用法） |
| 跨越 | across ✅ | across（介词） |
| 充满 | full with | full of |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 现在 | I'm busy preparing for exams. | My desk is buried under textbooks, my alarm rings before the sun rises, and coffee has become my best study partner. |
| 期望 | I hope you've grown up. | I hope the years have given you wrinkles from laughter, not from worry — and stories worth telling around a dinner table. |
| 叮嘱 | Be a good person. | When the world pushes you to be sharp, choose to stay soft — that is not weakness, it is the bravest thing a heart can do. |
