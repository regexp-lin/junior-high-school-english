# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你班正在讨论"中西方文化对比"。请你写一篇短文，内容包括：

① 选一个方面（如教育、饮食、礼仪等）进行中西对比；  
② 举具体例子说明差异；  
③ 你对文化差异的态度。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 参考范文

Today I want to compare Chinese and Western dining culture, because food is one of the best ways to understand a culture.

In China, meals are usually shared. Dishes are placed in the centre of the table, and everyone picks from the same plates. This reflects the Chinese value of togetherness and generosity. In contrast, Western meals are typically individual — each person has their own plate with a set portion.

Another interesting difference is the use of tools. Chinese people use chopsticks, which require skill and patience, while Westerners use knives and forks, which are more about cutting and precision.

Neither way is better or worse — they simply reflect different values. Chinese dining focuses on sharing and warmth, while Western dining values independence and order.

I believe cultural differences should be respected and celebrated. After all, it is our differences that make the world interesting.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *meals are usually shared* — 饭菜通常是共享的  
2. *reflects the Chinese value of* — 体现了中国人的价值观  
3. *in contrast* — 相比之下  
4. *a set portion* — 固定的分量  
5. *neither … is better or worse* — 两者都没有更好或更差  
6. *respected and celebrated* — 被尊重和赞赏  

---

## 全文翻译

今天我想比较一下中西方的饮食文化，因为食物是了解一种文化的最好方式之一。

在中国，饭菜通常是共享的。菜放在桌子中间，大家从同样的盘子里夹菜。这反映了中国人注重团聚和慷慨的价值观。相比之下，西方的用餐通常是个人化的——每个人有自己的盘子和固定分量。

另一个有趣的区别是餐具的使用。中国人用筷子，需要技巧和耐心，而西方人使用刀叉，更注重切割和精准。

两种方式都没有更好或更差——它们只是反映了不同的价值观。中式用餐注重分享和温情，西式用餐看重独立和秩序。

我认为文化差异应该被尊重和赞赏。毕竟，正是我们的不同让这个世界变得有趣。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| in contrast | 相比之下 | In contrast, Western culture values privacy. |
| reflect | 反映 | Food reflects a nation's culture. |
| neither … nor | 两者都不 | Neither is better nor worse. |
| celebrate differences | 赞赏差异 | We should celebrate our differences. |
| after all | 毕竟 | After all, variety makes life interesting. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 相比之下 | by contrast ✅ | in contrast / by contrast |
| 反映 | reflect ✅ | reflect + 名词（直接加宾语） |
| 筷子 | chopstick | chopsticks（总用复数） |
| 刀叉 | knife and fork ✅ | knives and forks（复数）/ a knife and fork |
| 两者都不 | neither is ✅ | neither … is（单数谓语） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 对比 | Chinese people share food. | A Chinese dinner table is a stage where generosity performs — the best dish is always pushed towards the guest. |
| 差异 | They use different tools. | Chopsticks dance with food; knives and forks command it — both get the job done, just with different rhythms. |
| 态度 | Differences are OK. | Culture is not a competition with a winner — it is an art gallery where every room offers a masterpiece worth admiring. |
