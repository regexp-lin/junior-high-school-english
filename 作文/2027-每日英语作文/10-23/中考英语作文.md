# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你的班级正在讨论"AI（人工智能）会取代老师吗"。请你写一篇发言稿，内容包括：

① AI 在教育中的应用举例；  
② AI 能否完全取代老师，说明理由；  
③ 你对 AI 与教育未来的看法。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

## 参考范文

Good morning, everyone! Today's topic is "Will AI replace teachers?"

AI is already being used in education. For example, smart apps can correct our homework, recommend learning materials and even answer simple questions. Some students use AI tools to practise English speaking at home.

However, I don't think AI can fully replace teachers. A teacher does much more than deliver knowledge. Teachers encourage us when we fail, share life experiences and understand our feelings. A machine cannot give a warm hug or a patient smile when we are struggling.

I believe the best future is one where AI and teachers work together. AI handles repetitive tasks while teachers focus on inspiring, guiding and caring for students.

Technology can help, but only a human heart can truly educate.

Thank you!

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *is already being used in* — 已经被应用于  
2. *recommend learning materials* — 推荐学习材料  
3. *does much more than deliver knowledge* — 远不止传授知识  
4. *a warm hug or a patient smile* — 一个温暖的拥抱或耐心的微笑  
5. *handles repetitive tasks* — 处理重复性任务  
6. *only a human heart can truly educate* — 只有人心才能真正教育人  

---

## 全文翻译

大家早上好！今天的话题是"AI会取代老师吗？"

AI 已经在教育中得到应用。例如，智能应用可以批改作业、推荐学习材料，甚至回答简单问题。有些同学在家用 AI 工具练习英语口语。

然而，我认为 AI 无法完全取代老师。老师做的远不止传授知识。老师在我们失败时鼓励我们，分享人生经验，理解我们的感受。当我们在挣扎时，机器无法给予一个温暖的拥抱或耐心的微笑。

我相信最好的未来是 AI 与老师合作。AI 处理重复性任务，而老师专注于激励、引导和关爱学生。

技术能帮忙，但只有人心才能真正教育人。

谢谢！

|--------|--------|--------|
| 人工智能 | AI ✅ | AI / artificial intelligence |
| 取代 | replace ✅ | replace / take the place of |
| 远不止 | do more than ✅ | do much more than（加 much 强调） |
| 处理 | deal with ✅ | handle / deal with |
| 重复的 | repeat tasks | repetitive tasks |
