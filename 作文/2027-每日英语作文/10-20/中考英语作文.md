# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，上周你第一次自己做了一顿饭。请你写一篇短文记录这次经历，内容包括：

① 你为什么决定自己做饭；  
② 你做了什么菜、过程如何；  
③ 这次经历给你的感受或启发。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |

| 体裁 | 记叙文（生活经历） |
| 话题 | 第一次做饭 |

| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Last Sunday, my parents went out for the whole day, so I decided to cook a meal for myself for the first time.

I chose to make fried rice because it seemed simple. I cooked some rice first, then cut ham, eggs and vegetables into small pieces. When I heated the oil, it splashed everywhere and I almost burned my hand! After a few nervous minutes, I mixed everything in the pan and added salt and soy sauce. It smelled great, but the rice stuck to the bottom of the pan a little.

Although the fried rice was not perfect, it was the most delicious meal I had ever had — because I made it myself.

This experience taught me that cooking is not as easy as it looks. I now understand how much effort my parents put into every meal. From now on, I will treasure every dish they make.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *for the first time* — 第一次  
2. *it splashed everywhere* — 油溅得到处都是  
3. *the rice stuck to the bottom of the pan* — 米饭粘锅  
4. *the most delicious meal I had ever had* — 我吃过的最美味的饭  
5. *not as easy as it looks* — 不像看起来那么简单  
6. *treasure every dish they make* — 珍惜他们做的每一道菜  

---

## 全文翻译

上周日，父母外出了一整天，所以我决定自己第一次做一顿饭。

我选择做炒饭，因为看起来很简单。我先煮了些米饭，然后把火腿、鸡蛋和蔬菜切成小块。当我加热油时，油溅得到处都是，我差点烫伤手！紧张了几分钟后，我把所有东西混在锅里，加了盐和酱油。闻起来很香，但米饭有点粘锅。

虽然炒饭不太完美，但它是我吃过的最美味的一顿饭——因为是我自己做的。

这次经历让我明白，做饭并不像看起来那么简单。我现在理解了父母在每顿饭中付出的心血。从今以后，我会珍惜他们做的每一道菜。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| for the first time | 第一次 | I cooked for the first time last week. |
| not as … as it looks | 不像看起来那样 | Swimming is not as easy as it looks. |
| splash | 飞溅 | The water splashed all over the floor. |
| stick to | 粘在 | The noodles stuck to the pot. |
| treasure | 珍惜 | We should treasure everything we have. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 炒饭 | fry rice | fried rice |
| 切 | cut into small piece | cut into small pieces |
| 粘锅 | stick on the pan | stick to the bottom of the pan |
| 差点 | almost burned ✅ | almost burned / nearly burned |
| 酱油 | soya sauce ✅ | soy sauce / soya sauce |

|------|----------|----------|
