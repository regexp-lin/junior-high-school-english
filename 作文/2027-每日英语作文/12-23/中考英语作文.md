# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校英语节即将举行。你的好友小明被选为班级代表参加英语才艺展示，但他非常紧张。请你写一封鼓励信，内容包括：

① 你对他被选中的祝贺；  
② 你认为他为什么能做好（至少两个优点）；  
③ 鼓励他自信发挥。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（鼓励信） |

| 人称 | 第一/第二人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般将来时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Xiao Ming,

Congratulations on being chosen to represent our class at the English Festival! I know you're feeling nervous, but trust me — you've got what it takes.

First, your pronunciation is one of the best in our class. Every time you read aloud, it sounds natural and confident. Second, you're creative. Remember the funny English skit you performed at the class party? Everyone loved it! These qualities will definitely help you shine on stage.

Here's my advice: don't aim for perfection — just be yourself. The audience wants to enjoy your performance, not judge every word. Take a deep breath, smile and have fun.

No matter what happens, the whole class will be cheering for you. You're not just performing for yourself — you're performing for all of us. Go out there and make us proud!

Your friend,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *you've got what it takes* — 你有能力做到  
2. *sounds natural and confident* — 听起来自然又自信  
3. *shine on stage* — 在台上闪耀  
4. *don't aim for perfection* — 不要追求完美  
5. *cheering for you* — 为你加油  
6. *make us proud* — 让我们骄傲  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的小明，

恭喜你被选为我们班在英语节的代表！我知道你很紧张，但相信我——你有这个能力。

首先，你的发音是我们班最好的之一。每次你朗读时，听起来自然又自信。其次，你很有创意。还记得班会上你表演的那个搞笑英语小品吗？大家都喜欢！这些品质一定会帮你在台上闪闪发光。

我的建议是：不要追求完美——做你自己就好。观众想享受你的表演，而不是评判每一个字。深呼吸，微笑，享受过程。

不管结果如何，全班都会为你加油。你不只是为自己表演——你是代表我们所有人。上台去，让我们骄傲！

你的朋友，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| have got what it takes | 有能力做到 | You've got what it takes to succeed. |
| shine on stage | 在舞台上闪耀 | Practice hard and you will shine on stage. |
| aim for | 以……为目标 | Don't aim for perfection; aim for progress. |
| cheer for | 为……加油 | We'll always cheer for you. |
| make sb. proud | 让某人骄傲 | Go out there and make your parents proud. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 恭喜 | congratulation | congratulations（总用复数） |
| 被选中 | be chosen to ✅ | be chosen to + 动词原形 |
| 为……加油 | cheer for ✅ | cheer for / root for |
| 追求 | aim to perfection | aim for perfection |
| 不管 | no matter what ✅ | no matter what + 从句 |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 祝贺 | Well done on being selected. | Of all the students in our class, the spotlight chose you — and it chose wisely. |
| 优点 | Your English is very good. | When you speak English, it doesn't sound like a student reading — it sounds like someone telling a story they believe in. |
| 鼓励 | Don't be nervous. | Butterflies in your stomach are not a sign of weakness — they are your body's way of saying, "Something exciting is about to happen." |
