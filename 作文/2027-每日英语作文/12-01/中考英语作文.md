# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校英语社团将在本周六举办英语角活动。请你写一封邮件邀请你的好友王明参加，内容包括：

① 活动的时间、地点和主要内容；  
② 你认为参加这个活动的好处；  
③ 你希望他能来并期待他的回复。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（邀请信） |
| 话题 | 邀请朋友参加活动 |
| 人称 | 第一/第二人称 |
| 时态 | 一般将来时+一般现在时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Wang Ming,

How are you? I'm writing to invite you to our English Corner this Saturday.

The activity will be held in the school library from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. This week's theme is "My Dream Trip". We will share travel plans in English, play word games and have a short debate. There will also be two foreign teachers joining us, so it's a great chance to practise speaking with native speakers.

I think you will enjoy it a lot. It's a fun and relaxing way to improve your English, and you might make some new friends too. Plus, snacks and drinks will be provided!

I really hope you can come. Please let me know by Friday. Looking forward to seeing you there!

Yours,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *I'm writing to invite you* — 我写信是为了邀请你  
2. *will be held in* — 将在……举办  
3. *a great chance to practise* — 练习的好机会  
4. *native speakers* — 以英语为母语的人  
5. *a fun and relaxing way* — 一种有趣且放松的方式  
6. *snacks and drinks will be provided* — 会提供零食和饮料  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的王明，

你好吗？我写信是想邀请你参加本周六的英语角。

活动将在学校图书馆举行，时间是下午两点到四点。本周的主题是"我的梦想旅行"。我们将用英语分享旅行计划、玩单词游戏、进行一场小型辩论。还有两位外教会加入我们，这是一个和母语者练习口语的好机会。

我觉得你会很喜欢的。这是一种有趣又放松的提高英语的方式，你还可能交到新朋友。而且，会提供零食和饮料哦！

我真的希望你能来。请在周五前告诉我。期待在那里见到你！

你的，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I'm writing to | 我写信是为了 | I'm writing to ask for your advice. |
| will be held | 将举办 | The meeting will be held next Monday. |
| a great chance to | 一个……的好机会 | It's a great chance to learn new things. |
| native speaker | 母语者 | She speaks like a native speaker. |
| look forward to | 期待 | I look forward to your reply. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 举行 | be held in ✅ | be held in + 地点 / on + 时间 |
| 机会 | chance to practise ✅ | chance to + 动词原形 |
| 期待 | look forward to see | look forward to seeing（to为介词） |
| 提供 | will be provide | will be provided（被动语态） |
| 告诉我 | let me to know | let me know（let后接原形） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 邀请 | Please come to our English Corner. | This Saturday, the library will turn into a little English-speaking world — and there's a seat saved just for you. |
| 好处 | You can improve your English. | Think of it as a gym for your tongue — two hours of English workout with laughs, snacks and zero homework. |
| 期待 | I hope you can come. | Don't let me be the only one cheering in the word game — I need my best teammate by my side! |
