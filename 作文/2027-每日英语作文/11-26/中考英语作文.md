# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，上周你们班参观了市博物馆。请你写一篇短文，内容包括：

① 参观的时间、地点和主要展品；  
② 你印象最深的一件展品或一个展区；  
③ 这次参观给你的启发。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

| 项目 | 内容 |

## 参考范文

Last Wednesday, our class visited the City Museum as part of a history field trip. We spent the whole morning there, and it was an eye-opening experience.

The museum had four main sections: ancient history, modern history, science and technology, and local culture. What impressed me most was a 2,000-year-old bronze sword displayed in the ancient history section. Despite its age, the blade was still sharp and shiny. It was hard to believe that people could make such a fine weapon so long ago.

Standing in front of that sword, I felt a deep connection to the past. History is not just dates and facts in a textbook — it is the story of real people who lived, struggled and created before us.

This trip reminded me that understanding the past helps us build a better future.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *an eye-opening experience* — 一次大开眼界的体验  
2. *despite its age* — 尽管年代久远  
3. *sharp and shiny* — 锋利且发亮  
4. *a deep connection to the past* — 与过去的深层联系  
5. *lived, struggled and created* — 生活、奋斗、创造  
6. *understanding the past helps us build a better future* — 了解过去帮助我们建设更好的未来  

---

## 全文翻译

上周三，我们班参观了市博物馆，作为历史实地考察的一部分。我们在那里度过了整个上午，这是一次大开眼界的体验。

博物馆有四个主要展区：古代史、近代史、科技和本土文化。给我印象最深的是古代史展区里一把有2000年历史的青铜剑。尽管年代久远，剑刃仍然锋利且发亮。很难相信那么久以前的人就能制造出如此精良的武器。

站在那把剑前，我感受到与过去的深层联系。历史不只是课本上的日期和事实——它是那些在我们之前生活、奋斗和创造的真实人类的故事。

这次参观让我认识到，了解过去有助于我们建设更好的未来。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| eye-opening | 大开眼界的 | The museum visit was truly eye-opening. |
| despite | 尽管 | Despite the rain, we had a great time. |
| be impressed by | 被……打动 | I was deeply impressed by the exhibit. |
| a connection to | 与……的联系 | Music gives me a connection to my culture. |
| remind sb. that | 提醒某人 | The trip reminded me that history matters. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 尽管 | despite of | despite（不加of）/ in spite of |
| 展品 | exhibit ✅ | exhibit / display |
| 令人印象深刻 | impressed me most ✅ | impressed me the most / most（均可） |
| 联系 | connection with ✅ | connection to / with（均可） |
| 实地考察 | field trip ✅ | field trip（两个单词） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 描写 | The sword was old and beautiful. | Two thousand years of time had polished the bronze into a golden whisper of a forgotten warrior. |

| 感悟 | History is important. | Every museum display is a time capsule — crack it open, and the past spills out in colours you never imagined. |
