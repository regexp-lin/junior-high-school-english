# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校图书馆正在开展"好书推荐"活动。请你写一篇推荐文，向同学们推荐一本你喜爱的书，内容包括：

① 书名、作者以及大致内容；  
② 这本书吸引你的原因；  
③ 你推荐它的理由。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 说明文（推荐类） |
| 话题 | 推荐好书 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般过去时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

I'd like to recommend a book called "Wonder" by R.J. Palacio.

The story is about a boy named August who was born with a facial difference. When he starts going to a regular school for the first time, he faces stares, whispers and even bullying. But with the help of his family and a few brave friends, he proves that what truly matters is not how you look, but who you are inside.

What attracted me most is the book's warm and powerful message: "When given the choice between being right or being kind, choose kind." This line changed the way I treat the people around me.

I recommend this book because it teaches empathy in a simple yet touching way. Whether you finish it in one sitting or over a week, I promise it will stay in your heart for a long time.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *born with a facial difference* — 天生面部与众不同  
2. *stares, whispers and even bullying* — 注视、议论甚至霸凌  
3. *what truly matters* — 真正重要的是  
4. *choose kind* — 选择善良  
5. *in a simple yet touching way* — 以简单却感人的方式  
6. *stay in your heart* — 留在你心中  

---

## 全文翻译

我想推荐一本书，叫《奇迹男孩》，作者是R.J. 帕拉西奥。

故事讲的是一个叫奥吉的男孩，他天生面部与众不同。当他第一次进入普通学校时，面对的是注视、议论甚至霸凌。但在家人和几位勇敢朋友的帮助下，他证明了真正重要的不是你的外表，而是你的内心。

最吸引我的是书中温暖而有力的信息："当你在'正确'和'善良'之间做选择时，选择善良。"这句话改变了我对待身边人的方式。

我推荐这本书，因为它以简单却感人的方式教会我们同理心。不管你是一口气读完还是用一周的时间慢慢读，我保证它会在你心中留下很久。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| what truly matters | 真正重要的是 | What truly matters is your effort, not the result. |
| choose kind | 选择善良 | When in doubt, always choose kind. |
| in a simple yet touching way | 简单却感人地 | She expressed her love in a simple yet touching way. |
| stay in one's heart | 留在心中 | This movie will stay in my heart forever. |
| in one sitting | 一口气 | I finished the book in one sitting. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 推荐 | recommend sb. to read | recommend sth. to sb. / recommend that sb. read |
| 天生 | born of | born with |
| 重要的是 | what matters is ✅ | what matters is + 名词/从句 |
| 不管 | no matter ✅ | whether … or …（此处更合适） |
| 简单却 | simple but touching ✅ | simple yet touching（yet更正式） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 介绍 | It's about a boy who looks different. | It's the story of a boy whose face tells one story, but whose heart tells a far more beautiful one. |
| 吸引 | The book has a good message. | "Choose kind" — two small words that landed on my heart like a seed and have been growing there ever since. |
| 推荐 | You should read this book. | Open this book with your eyes, and you will close it with a bigger heart. |
