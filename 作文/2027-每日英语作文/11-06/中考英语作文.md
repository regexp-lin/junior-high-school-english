# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，学校正在征集学生对校规的看法和建议。请你写一封建议信给校长，内容包括：

① 你认为目前校规中哪些是合理的；  
② 你认为哪一两条校规需要改进，并说明原因；  
③ 你的具体改进建议。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（建议信） |
| 话题 | 校规建议 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般将来时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Headmaster,

I'm writing to share my thoughts on our school rules.

First of all, I think most of the rules are reasonable and necessary. For example, the rule that requires us to wear school uniforms helps create a sense of equality and belonging. The rule against using mobile phones in class also helps us focus on learning.

However, I feel that the lunchtime rule could be improved. At present, we have only thirty minutes for lunch, which is too short. Many students have to rush through their meal and cannot rest properly. I suggest extending the lunch break to forty-five minutes so that we can eat calmly and take a short walk afterwards.

I believe a well-rested student learns better. Thank you for listening to our voices!

Yours sincerely,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *a sense of equality and belonging* — 平等感和归属感  
2. *focus on learning* — 专注学习  
3. *rush through their meal* — 匆匆吃完饭  
4. *extending the lunch break* — 延长午休时间  
5. *eat calmly* — 从容地吃饭  
6. *listening to our voices* — 倾听我们的声音  

---

## 全文翻译

尊敬的校长，

我写信想分享一下对我校校规的看法。

首先，我认为大多数校规是合理且必要的。例如，要求我们穿校服的规定有助于营造平等感和归属感。上课禁止使用手机的规定也帮助我们专注于学习。

然而，我觉得午餐时间的规定可以改进。目前我们只有三十分钟吃午饭，时间太短了。很多学生不得不匆匆吃完，无法好好休息。我建议把午休延长到四十五分钟，这样我们可以从容地吃饭，饭后还能散散步。

我相信休息好的学生学习效率更高。感谢校长倾听我们的声音！

您的学生，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| a sense of belonging | 归属感 | The team gave me a sense of belonging. |
| focus on | 专注于 | We should focus on our study. |
| rush through | 匆匆完成 | Don't rush through your homework. |
| suggest doing | 建议做 | I suggest starting earlier. |
| well-rested | 休息充分的 | A well-rested mind works more efficiently. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 建议 | suggest to extend | suggest extending（suggest + 动名词） |
| 合理的 | reasonable ✅ | reasonable（注意拼写） |
| 要求 | require us wear | require us to wear |
| 太短 | too short ✅ | too short（注意 too…to 结构） |
| 之后 | after ✅ | afterwards / after that |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 认同 | Wearing uniforms is a good rule. | School uniforms are the great equaliser — they remind us that we are judged by character, not clothing. |
| 问题 | Thirty minutes is too short. | Swallowing lunch in thirty minutes is like reading a novel in five — you miss all the good parts. |
| 建议 | We need more time for lunch. | A longer lunch break is not a luxury; it is an investment in an afternoon of sharper minds and happier hearts. |
