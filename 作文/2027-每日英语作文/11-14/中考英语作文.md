# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你在网上认识了一位来自澳大利亚的笔友 David。这是你第一次给他写信，请你写一封自我介绍信，内容包括：

① 你的基本情况（年龄、爱好等）；  
② 你的校园生活；  
③ 你希望通过通信了解什么。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（书信/自我介绍） |
| 话题 | 写给笔友 |
| 人称 | 第一/第二人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear David,

I'm so happy to write to you! My name is Li Hua, and I'm a 15-year-old student from China.

I love playing basketball and drawing in my free time. I'm also a big fan of science fiction — my favourite book is "The Three-Body Problem". At school, my favourite subject is English because it opens a window to the outside world.

My school life is busy but colourful. We have classes from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., and I'm a member of the art club. Every Friday afternoon, we paint together and sometimes display our works in the school hall.

Through our letters, I'd love to learn about Australian culture, especially your school life and the animals there. I've always been curious about kangaroos and koalas!

I hope we can become good friends. Looking forward to your reply!

Yours,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *a big fan of* — ……的忠实粉丝  
2. *opens a window to the outside world* — 打开一扇通向外面世界的窗户  
3. *busy but colourful* — 忙碌但丰富多彩  
4. *display our works* — 展示我们的作品  
5. *through our letters* — 通过我们的书信  
6. *curious about* — 对……感到好奇  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的David，

我很高兴给你写信！我叫李华，是一名来自中国的15岁学生。

我在空闲时间喜欢打篮球和画画。我也是科幻小说的忠实粉丝——最喜欢的书是《三体》。在学校，我最喜欢的学科是英语，因为它打开了一扇通向外面世界的窗户。

我的校园生活忙碌但丰富多彩。我们从早上8点上课到下午5点，我是美术社团的成员。每周五下午我们一起画画，有时还在学校大厅展示作品。

通过书信，我很想了解澳大利亚文化，特别是你的校园生活和那里的动物。我一直对袋鼠和考拉很好奇！

希望我们能成为好朋友。期待你的回信！

你的，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| a big fan of | 忠实粉丝 | I'm a big fan of Harry Potter. |
| open a window to | 打开一扇窗 | Reading opens a window to new ideas. |
| in one's free time | 在空闲时间 | I play guitar in my free time. |
| be curious about | 对……好奇 | Children are curious about everything. |
| look forward to | 期待 | I look forward to hearing from you. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 15岁 | 15-years-old | 15-year-old（作定语时不加s） |
| 期待 | look forward to hear | look forward to hearing（to为介词） |
| 在空闲时间 | in free time | in my free time |
| 好奇 | curious of | curious about |
| 成为朋友 | become good friend | become good friends（复数） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 自我介绍 | I'm a student from China. | Somewhere in a bustling Chinese city, a boy with a paintbrush in one hand and a basketball in the other is writing you this letter. |
| 校园生活 | My school life is busy. | My days are a patchwork quilt of textbooks, art supplies and laughter in the hallways. |
| 期望 | I want to know about Australia. | I picture Australia as a land where the sky meets the ocean and kangaroos share the road with cars — tell me if I'm right! |
