# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，今天是冬至，你的美国笔友 Mike 好奇中国人在冬至这天做什么。请你写一封回信，内容包括：

① 冬至是什么时候、有什么含义；  
② 冬至的习俗（如吃饺子、汤圆等）；  
③ 你和家人是怎么过冬至的。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（回信） |
| 话题 | 冬至/传统节气 |
| 人称 | 第一/第二人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时+一般过去时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

Dear Mike,

Thanks for asking about the Winter Solstice! It falls around December 21st or 22nd every year and marks the shortest day and the longest night of the year.

In China, the Winter Solstice is almost as important as a festival. In the north, families make and eat dumplings together. There is even a saying: "If you don't eat dumplings on the Winter Solstice, your ears will freeze off!" In the south, people prefer eating tangyuan — sweet rice balls that symbolise family reunion.

In my family, we gather in the kitchen to make dumplings from scratch. Mum prepares the filling while Dad and I roll the dough. We talk, laugh and sometimes compete to see who can make the prettiest dumpling. After dinner, we drink warm ginger tea and watch TV together.

For us, the Winter Solstice is less about the cold and more about the warmth of being together.

Yours,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *marks the shortest day* — 标志着最短的一天  
2. *your ears will freeze off* — 耳朵会冻掉（谚语）  
3. *symbolise family reunion* — 象征家庭团圆  
4. *make dumplings from scratch* — 从零开始包饺子  
5. *roll the dough* — 擀面皮  
6. *less about … more about* — 与其说是……不如说是

## 全文翻译

亲爱的Mike，

谢谢你对冬至的好奇！冬至大约在每年12月21日或22日，标志着一年中最短的白天和最长的夜晚。

在中国，冬至几乎像节日一样重要。在北方，家人一起包饺子吃。甚至有句俗话说："冬至不吃饺子，耳朵会冻掉！"在南方，人们更爱吃汤圆——甜甜的糯米圆子，象征团圆。

在我家，我们在厨房里一起从零开始包饺子。妈妈准备馅料，爸爸和我擀面皮。我们一边聊天、大笑，有时还比赛看谁包得最漂亮。晚饭后，我们一起喝热姜茶、看电视。

对我们来说，冬至与其说是关于寒冷，不如说是关于团聚的温暖。

你的，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| fall on | 在（某日期） | Christmas falls on December 25th. |
| mark | 标志着 | This day marks the start of winter. |
| symbolise | 象征 | Red symbolises luck in Chinese culture. |
| from scratch | 从零开始 | We made everything from scratch. |
| less about … more about | 与其说……不如说 | The festival is less about food and more about family. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 冬至 | Winter Solstice ✅ | Winter Solstice（首字母大写） |
| 象征 | symbolise ✅ | symbolise (英) / symbolize (美) |
| 馅料 | stuffing ✅ | filling / stuffing |
| 擀面 | roll the dough ✅ | roll the dough / roll out the dough |
| 汤圆 | tangyuan ✅ | tangyuan / sweet rice balls |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 含义 | It is the shortest day of the year. | On the darkest day of the year, Chinese families light up the longest night with laughter, dumplings and love. |
| 习俗 | We eat dumplings together. | The kitchen becomes the warmest room in the house — not because of the stove, but because of the family standing around it. |
| 感受 | Winter Solstice is warm and happy. | The Winter Solstice whispers a comforting truth: even the longest night must give way to a new, brighter day. |
