# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你的英国笔友 Amy 想了解中国和西方在节日方面的文化差异。请你写一封回信，内容包括：

① 简单介绍一个中国传统节日及其习俗；  
② 提到一个西方节日（如万圣节），比较两者的异同；  
③ 你对文化差异的看法。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 参考范文

Dear Amy,

Thanks for your letter! I'd love to share some thoughts on festivals in China and the West.

In China, the Mid-Autumn Festival is one of the most important holidays. Families get together, eat mooncakes and admire the full moon. It is a time for reunion and gratitude.

I know that Halloween is popular in the UK. Children dress up in costumes and go trick-or-treating. It is more about fun and excitement.

Although these two festivals are quite different, they both bring people closer. The Mid-Autumn Festival focuses on family warmth, while Halloween celebrates community and creativity. I think cultural differences make the world more colourful. Instead of seeing them as strange, we should respect and learn from each other.

Looking forward to your reply!

Yours,  
Li Hua

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *admire the full moon* — 赏满月  
2. *a time for reunion and gratitude* — 团圆和感恩的时刻  
3. *dress up in costumes* — 穿上戏装  
4. *go trick-or-treating* — 去"不给糖就捣蛋"  
5. *bring people closer* — 拉近人们的距离  
6. *respect and learn from each other* — 互相尊重和学习  

---

## 全文翻译

亲爱的Amy，

谢谢你的来信！我很乐意分享一些关于中西方节日的想法。

在中国，中秋节是最重要的节日之一。一家人团聚在一起，吃月饼，赏满月。这是团圆和感恩的时刻。

我知道万圣节在英国很受欢迎。孩子们穿上各种服装，去"不给糖就捣蛋"。它更多的是关于乐趣和刺激。

虽然这两个节日截然不同，但它们都拉近了人们的距离。中秋节注重家庭温暖，而万圣节庆祝社区和创意。我认为文化差异让世界更加丰富多彩。与其觉得它们奇怪，不如互相尊重和学习。

期待你的回信！

你的，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| get together | 团聚 | We get together for Spring Festival every year. |
| dress up | 装扮 | Children love to dress up on Halloween. |
| bring people closer | 拉近距离 | Festivals bring people closer to each other. |
| instead of | 而不是 | Instead of complaining, let's take action. |
| learn from each other | 互相学习 | Different cultures can learn from each other. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 最……之一 | one of the most important holiday | one of the most important holidays（复数） |
| 团聚 | get together ✅ | get together / have a reunion |
| 虽然……但是 | although…but | although…,（不与but连用） |
| 月饼 | moon cake | mooncake（一个词） |
| 而不是 | instead to | instead of + doing |
