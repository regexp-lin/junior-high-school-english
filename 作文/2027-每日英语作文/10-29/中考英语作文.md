# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校心理健康社团正在征稿，主题为"如何保持心理健康"。请你写一篇短文投稿，内容包括：

① 中学生常见的心理压力来源；  
② 保持心理健康的两到三个方法；  
③ 你对心理健康的看法。  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；文中不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 说明文（投稿类） |
| 话题 | 心理健康 |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 参考范文

As middle school students, we often face a lot of pressure from exams, homework and even friendships. If we don't deal with it properly, it may affect our health and happiness.

Here are some ways to stay mentally healthy. First, talk to someone you trust — a parent, a teacher or a close friend. Sharing your feelings can make a big difference. Second, exercise regularly. Running, swimming or even a short walk can help reduce stress. Third, make time for hobbies you enjoy, such as drawing, reading or listening to music.

In my opinion, mental health is just as important as physical health. A healthy mind helps us think clearly, study better and enjoy life more. Let's take care of our minds the way we take care of our bodies.

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 语言方面
1. *deal with it properly* — 正确地处理它  
2. *make a big difference* — 产生重大影响  
3. *reduce stress* — 减轻压力  
4. *just as important as* — 和……一样重要  
5. *think clearly* — 清晰地思考  
6. *take care of our minds* — 照顾我们的心灵  

---

## 全文翻译

作为中学生，我们经常面临来自考试、作业甚至友谊的很大压力。如果我们不正确处理，可能会影响我们的健康和快乐。

以下是一些保持心理健康的方法。第一，和你信任的人倾诉——父母、老师或亲密的朋友。分享你的感受能产生很大的不同。第二，定期锻炼。跑步、游泳甚至短暂的散步都能帮助减轻压力。第三，为你喜欢的爱好腾出时间，如画画、阅读或听音乐。

在我看来，心理健康和身体健康一样重要。健康的心灵帮助我们清晰思考、更好地学习、更享受生活。让我们像照顾身体一样照顾心灵吧。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）
| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| deal with | 处理 | We should learn to deal with pressure. |
| make a big difference | 产生重大影响 | A kind word can make a big difference. |
| reduce stress | 减轻压力 | Listening to music helps reduce stress. |
| just as … as | 和……一样 | Sleep is just as important as food. |
| take care of | 照顾 | We should take care of ourselves. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）
| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 压力 | pressure ✅ | pressure / stress（注意语境选词） |
| 影响 | effect (动词) | affect（动词）/ effect（名词） |
| 和……一样 | as important than | as important as |
| 定期 | regular (副词) | regularly（副词形式） |
| 享受 | enjoy to do | enjoy doing（后接动名词） |

### 升格建议（3条）
| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 引入 | Students have a lot of pressure. | Behind every calm face in the classroom, there may be a storm of worry that no one sees. |
| 方法 | Talking to friends is helpful. | A conversation with a trusted friend is like opening a window in a stuffy room — fresh air rushes in. |
| 总结 | Mental health is important. | A strong body with a tired mind is like a sports car with a flat tyre — it cannot go far. |
